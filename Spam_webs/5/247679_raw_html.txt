           <p>
            B2B marketers are becoming more savvy with
            <a href="http://business.linkedin.com/marketing-solutions/content-marketing?src=s-bl" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             content marketing
            </a>
            , embracing more channels and investing in new content and tactics. In fact, more marketers are now allocating larger budgets and time to implement and experiment with content marketing strategies than ever before.
           </p>
           <p>
            Our friends over at
            <a href="http://www.marketingprofs.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             MarketingProfs
            </a>
            and the
            <a href="http://contentmarketinginstitute.com/2013/10/2014-b2b-content-marketing-research/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Content Marketing Institute
            </a>
            recently released their annual
            <a href="http://contentmarketinginstitute.com/2013/10/2014-b2b-content-marketing-research/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             report
            </a>
            uncovering insights into how B2B marketers are using content marketing techniques and the biggest challenges they face.
           </p>
           <p>
            Were thrilled that the survey indicates that LinkedIn is the leading social media site for content distribution and that SlideShare has shown the biggest surge in usage, growing to 40 percent from 23 percent last year.
           </p>
           <p>
            The following infographic provides a visual interpretation by breaking down the tactics, platforms, and trends that are proving successful.
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="1972" marginheight="0" marginwidth="0" scrolling="no" src="http://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/embed_code/27864071?rel=0" style="border: 1px solid #CCC; border-width: 1px 1px 0; margin-bottom: 5px;" width="520">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <p>
            Theres no doubt that its a good time to be a content marketer. Confidence is high regarding its effectiveness as a strategy, and marketers are becoming more savvy with the tactics they are using to distribute their content.
           </p>
           <p>
            <i>

              @LinkedInMktg
             </a>
             .
            </i>
           </p>

                console.log('overswipe left');
                adjustment = xPos + listWidth + distance - rightBoundary;
              }

              // max the bounce at either end of the list to 30px
              if (adjustment &gt; 30) {
                tempXPos = xPos + distance - adjustment + 30;
              }
              else if (adjustment &lt; -30) {
                tempXPos = xPos + distance - adjustment - 30;
              }
              else {
               tempXPos = xPos + distance;
              }
              xPos  = xPos + distance - adjustment;
              $list.animate({'left': tempXPos}, 400).animate({'left': xPos}, 200);
              console.log('new left coord: ' + xPos);
          },
          threshold: 0,
          excludedElements:"button, input, select, textarea, .noSwipe"
        });
      };
