       <h1>
        Cant Wait for Your Baltimore Orioles Fix?
       </h1>
       <div class="entry">
        <p>
         With the final touchdowns of football season already scored, Baltimore sports fans are looking forward to the next opportunity they will have to scream themselves silly for Charm City: Baseball season! The Orioles have just recently announced their spring training schedule and plans for the upcoming season, which is great news for fans. If you are a Federal Hill resident looking forward to baseball season, read on to find out more!
        </p>
        <h2>
         <a href="http://www.1111lightstreet.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/rsz_baltimore-orioles.jpg">
          <img alt="baltimore-orioles" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-483" height="199" src="http://www.1111lightstreet.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/rsz_baltimore-orioles-300x199.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <strong>
          Baltimore Orioles Announce Spring Training Schedule
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
         </strong>
         The Os will be flying south for this last bit of winter, returning to Sarasota, Floridas Ed Smith Stadium to carry out their spring training. The Orioles have trained at this location for five seasons now and given it the affectionate nickname, Birdland South. Their work there has seemed to be paying off; the Orioles were just a heartbeat away from the World Series last season and no doubt hope to go all the way in 2015!
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Baltimore Orioles News to Watch For
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
         </strong>
         While spring training continues and the Os prepare to give Baltimoreans an unforgettable season, there are a few stories that all Orioles fans will surely be following, including:
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          New Season, Familiar Faces.
         </strong>
         Os fans will no doubt be happy to see the return of top-notch players Manny Machado and Matt Wieters, both of whom were recovering from injuries for the majority of last season.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          The New Guys.
         </strong>
         As some favorites return, other players will be leaving the Os, including Nelson Cruz and Nick Markakis. Who will be taking over for these star     players? Only time will tell.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Winning It All.
         </strong>
         First and foremost on any fans mind will of course be whether or not the Orioles will be able to push just a bit further to win the World Series this season. Take advantage of your Federal Hill location to check out the games and see for yourself when the Orioles start out their season officially this spring!
        </p>
        <p>
         Keep checking in with 1111 Light Street to learn about life in Baltimore/Federal Hill! Were not just about the luxury apartment lifestyle, were about the fabulous location that comes with it!
        </p>
