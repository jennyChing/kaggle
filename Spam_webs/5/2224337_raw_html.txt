           <p>
            Now I know that now everyone is as relaxed as I am when it comes to kids painting and the mess it can create. But the summer is a great time to try and lower your worry factor and get painting outdoors.
           </p>
           <p>
            The key to being more relaxed is preparation, so get a big bowl of soapy water and a towel ready for cleanups. I have even been known to take the hosepipe to the boys to clean them up or bath them in the paddling pool.
           </p>
           <p>
            We are big fans of the large rolls of paper that you can get from Ikea.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.muminthemadhouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Great-Kids-Outdoor-Painting-Activities.jpg">

            </a>

           </p>

             </a>
             you are introducing new textures to your child.

             . it would be fab to cover the fence with paper and it would be great fun too.

             <a href="http://artfulparent.com/2010/05/ball-painting-in-the-swimming-pool.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://artfulparent.com/2010/05/ball-painting-in-the-swimming-pool.html', 'ball painting in the paddling pool']);" target="_blank">
              ball painting in the paddling pool
             </a>
             . This is on our to do list for the summer holidays.

             <a href="http://www.muminthemadhouse.com/2009/07/27/pavement-paint/" target="_blank">

            </li>

             <a href="http://crayonboxchronicles.com/2013/09/04/bouncy-ball-art/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://crayonboxchronicles.com/2013/09/04/bouncy-ball-art/', 'bouncing balls']);" target="_blank">

             to paint with. This is another activity on our summer to do list. It is a great skill to pick up wet and slippy bouncing balls.
            </li>
            <li>
             My boys love to feel paint between their toes and we often make
             <a href="http://www.muminthemadhouse.com/2009/11/23/crafting-with-children-make-your-own-wrapping-paper/" target="_blank">
              footprint wrapping paper
             </a>
             .
