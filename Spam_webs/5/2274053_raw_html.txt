      <h2>
       2015 LA Juried Exhibition
      </h2>
      <p>
       Los Angeles Municipal Art Gallery
       <br/>
       2015 Los Angeles Juried Exhibition
       <br/>
       Jul 26  September 20, 2015
      </p>
      <p>
       Opening Reception July 26, 2 to 5 pm
       <br/>
       Awards Announced July 26 at 3 pm
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        THE 2015 LOS ANGELES JURIED EXHIBITION IS A BIENNIAL EXHIBITION PROVIDING ARTISTS WITH A COMPETITIVE OPPORTUNITY TO EXHIBIT THEIR WORK.
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       Artists
      </p>
      <p>
       Lillian Abel, Susan Amorde, A.S. Ashley, Anne Blecksmith, Ray Bravo, Elizabeth Chandler, Mark Crase, Andrew K. Currey, Laura Darlington, Kristin du Charme, Shannon Durbin, Paul Emberger, Michael Floeck , Kathi Flood, David Fobes, Sarajo Frieden, Curtis Green, Carole Goldman, Matthew Miles Grayson,Julie Green, Michael Haasis, Emily Halpern, Mark Hayward, Chris Hopkins, Walter Impert, Aazam Irilian, Louis Jacinto, Robert Klewitz, Russell Knight, Sheku Kowai, Joslyn Lawrence, Brian Kuhlman, Dave Lefner, Edward Lightner, Bruce Litz, Patricia Liverman, Erika Lize, Ralph Loynachan, Dion Macellari,Louisa Miller, Jason Manley, Chris Mathis, Chrystal McConnell, Mike McLain, Zoran Milosavljevic, Stephen Morrison, Djibril NDoye, Jim Newberry, Brooke Nasser, Jon Ng, Michael Ochinero,Monica Orozco, Thibault Pelletier, Susan Price, Antonio Pelayo, Melissa Reischman, Lynda A.N. Reyes, Todd B. Richmond, Regine Rode, Karrie Ross, Carol Roullard, Kerri Sabine-Wolf, Nairi Safaryan,Larisa Safaryan, Ilya Schar, Dorothy Shepherd,
       <a name="14e6475c9fcaa505__GoBack">
       </a>
       Mahara Sinclaire, Olga Seem, Lidia Shaddow, Aren Skalman, Baby Smith, Cheryl Smith, Kymm Swank, Stephanie Sydney, Lisa Talbot, Roger Thomas, Tiensirin Tienngern, Sue Tuemmler, Richard Turner, Emmalyn Tringali, Frank Ventrola, Art Venti, Dana L. Walker, Cecily Willis, Wendy Widell Wolff, Tomi Yang, Peggy S. Zask
      </p>
      <p>
       JURORS
      </p>
      <p>
       Tomas Benitez,Fatemeh Burnes,Peter Frank
      </p>
      <p>
       Awards Announced July 26 at 3 pm
       <br/>
        Best of Show $1,000
       <br/>
        Second Place $500
       <br/>
        Third Place $300
       <br/>
        Honorable Mentions (4) $50 each
      </p>
      <p>
       EXHIBITION SITE
      </p>
      <p>
       Los Angeles Municipal Art Gallery (LAMAG)
       <br/>
       Barnsdall Park
       <br/>
       4800 Hollywood Boulevard
       <br/>
       Los Angeles, CA 90027
      </p>
      <p>
       ELIGIBILITY
       <br/>
       ARTISTS MAY ENTER A MAXIMUM OF 4 WORKS
       <br/>
        Open to all artists residing in Southern California
       <br/>
       (Santa Barbara to San Diego).
       <br/>
        Works must be original and have been completed after January 2014.
       <br/>
        City of Los Angeles Department of Cultural Affairs employees are ineligible.
      </p>
      <p>
       SUBMISSION
       <br/>
        Submit a CD or USB flash drive containing up to 4 artworks, numbered 1 through 4.
       <br/>
        Artworks must be in JPEG format (no Tiffs, PDFs, PowerPoints, etc.).
       <br/>
        Image files should have a maximum image size of 2400 pixels x 3000 pixels each.
       <br/>
        Complete entry form (see link above) number entries 1  4 to correspond with your CD.
       <br/>
        Submission of work does not guarantee acceptance into the exhibition. Works are selected solely at the jurors discretion.
      </p>
      <p>
       FEE
       <br/>
       $20 per work (check, cashiers check or money order should be make payable to the City of Los Angeles). Cash entries accepted only if hand-delivered to LAMAG.
       <br/>
       Entry fee is non-refundable.
      </p>
      <p>
       CATEGORIES:
       <br/>
        Painting (encaustic, oil, water-based media, canvas or panel)
       <br/>
        Sculpture (three-dimensional works: pedestal, floor or wall-hung works)
       <br/>
        Works on Paper (oil, water-based media, collage, photography, digital imagery or printmaking)
       <br/>
        Mixed Media/Assemblage (works formed by combining different materials, also includes fiber and textile works)
       <br/>
        Video (DVD)
       <br/>
        Installation
       <br/>
        Performance Art
      </p>
      <p>
       DISPLAY REQUIREMENTS:
      </p>
      <p>
       Two-dimensional work
       <br/>
        All works must be ready to hang with wire and eye hooks.
       <br/>
        Works on paper must be covered by glass or Plexiglas.
       <br/>
        Maximum size: 132 x 132 inches  Minimum size: no less than 10 X 10 inches, including frame..
       <br/>
        Plexiglas or glass may not be used with metal/clip frames if any one side exceeds 25 inches.
      </p>
      <p>
       Three-dimensional work
       <br/>
        Applicants may use up to 3 images to represent 1 work (front, back, side).
       <br/>
        Maximum size: 118 X 64 inches.
       <br/>
        Minimum size: no smaller than 10 X 10 inches.
       <br/>
        Weight maximum: 150 lbs. Artist must provide pedestals as necessary.
      </p>
      <p>
       Video
       <br/>
        For works longer than three minutes, DVDs should include a sample of up to 3 minutes in length along with the complete video.
      </p>
      <p>
       Installation works
       <br/>
        A narrative, 500 words or fewer, along with 3 images addressing the contents and components of the proposed work.
       <br/>
        Installation artists must install their own work and provide all the necessary equipment.
      </p>
      <p>
       Performance Art
       <br/>
        Send one DVD showing artists works presented within the last two years to allow the jurors to assess performers past work.
       <br/>
        A narrative, 500 words or fewer, describing the contents of the proposed work.
      </p>
      <p>
       INADMISSIBLE WORK
       <br/>
       Unfinished, incomplete or wet works, and any work considered unsafe will not be accepted. No live animals, no hazardous materials and no perishables.
      </p>
      <p>
       SALES
       <br/>
       Inquiries for purchase will be directed to the artist or their representative. The Los Angeles Municipal Art Gallery does not handle sales of artwork.
      </p>
      <p>
       CHECK LIST
       <br/>
       1. Fee: $20 per work. ($80 total) Check, cashiers check or money order should be made payable to the City of Los Angeles. Cash will be accepted only if hand delivered to gallery staff.
      </p>
      <p>
       2. Optional, include self-addressed stamped envelope (4 x 9  inch) for return of CD or USB flash drive, otherwise CD will be discarded.
       <br/>
       3. Completed entry form signed by artist. Incomplete entries will not be accepted.
      </p>
      <p>
       4. Entries (4 Maximum)
      </p>
      <p>
       5. Performance DVDs written statement.
      </p>

       6. Please do not include resumes.
