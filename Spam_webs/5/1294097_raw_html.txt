       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Grouting and Cleaning Brick Floor Tiles
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.tilecleaning.co.uk/grouting-and-cleaning-brick-floor-tiles/" rel="bookmark" title="5:11 pm">
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-12-07T17:11:47+00:00">
           December 7, 2014
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.tilecleaning.co.uk/author/abzdominise/" rel="author" title="View all posts by AbzDominise">
            AbzDominise
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         The owner of this house in
         <a href="http://www.henleystandard.co.uk/news/news_district.php?district=Peppard" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.henleystandard.co.uk/news/news_district.php?district=Peppard', 'Peppard Common']);" target="_blank">
          Peppard Common
         </a>
         near Henley on Thames was keen to have the grout completely removed from this brick paved floor in the Kitchen and re-done.  This is a lot of work and re-colouring the grout with a
         <a href="http://www.tiledoctor.co.uk/GroutColourant.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.tiledoctor.co.uk/GroutColourant.html', 'Grout Colourant']);" target="_blank">
          Grout Colourant
         </a>
         kit would have been faster and easier however it is something we can do if required.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img alt="Brick Paved Floor Before Regrouting and Cleaning Peppard" height="650" src="http://oxfordshire.tiledoctor.biz/wp-content/uploads/sites/23/2014/12/Brick-Paved-Floor-Before-Regrouting-and-Cleaning-Peppard.jpg" title="Brick Paved Floor Before Regrouting and Cleaning Peppard" width="520"/>
        </center>
        <h2>
         Removing Grout
        </h2>
        <p>
         I started by removing the kick boards around the base of the kitchen units then to remove the grout I had to cut along all the grout lines with a hand held multi tool; as you can imagine this work is quite painstaking and slow however I persisted and eventually completed the task which took me the whole day.  After this I came back the next day and grouted the whole floor polishing off the excess grout as I went.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Cleaning Brick Paving
        </h2>
        <p>
         On the third day I gave the floor a clean with
         <a href="http://www.tiledoctor.co.uk/TileGroutCleaner.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.tiledoctor.co.uk/TileGroutCleaner.html', 'Tile Doctor Pro Clean']);" target="_blank">
          Tile Doctor Pro Clean
         </a>
         diluted with water.  The customer did not want a full scrub as the brick floor had a nice patina to it and a full scrub would have made the brick floor more of an orange colour.  The last step was to give the floor a thorough rinse with water and then use a wet vacuum to extract as much water from the floor as possible.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img alt="Brick Paved Floor After Regrouting and Cleaning Peppard" height="650" src="http://oxfordshire.tiledoctor.biz/wp-content/uploads/sites/23/2014/12/Brick-Paved-Floor-After-Regrouting-and-Cleaning-Peppard.jpg" title="Brick Paved Floor After Regrouting and Cleaning Peppard" width="520"/>
        </center>
        <p>
         In total the job took three days to complete and it now looks much cleaner and fresher.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Source:
         <a href="http://oxfordshire.tiledoctor.co.uk/brick-paved-floor-re-grouted-and-cleaned-in-peppard/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://oxfordshire.tiledoctor.co.uk/brick-paved-floor-re-grouted-and-cleaned-in-peppard/', 'Tile, Stone and Grout Cleaning in Oxfordshire']);">
          Tile, Stone and Grout Cleaning in Oxfordshire
         </a>
        </p>
