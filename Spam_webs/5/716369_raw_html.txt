       <a href="http://blog.foxsoccer.com/post/100183506447/crazy-brawl-breaks-out-in-argentinas-fourth">
        Crazy brawl breaks out in Argentinas fourth division
       </a>
      </h1>
     </header>
     <div class="body copy">
      <date>
       October 16th, 2014
      </date>
      <div class="by">
       <a href="http://twitter.com/hautmannonfox" target="_blank">
        <span>
         by
        </span>
        Thomas Hautmann
       </a>
      </div>
      <p>
       <img alt="image" src="http://38.media.tumblr.com/cbaba43cfe0ade12c51b07fcd1da5b66/tumblr_inline_ndk15kXZXH1ryfaxj.png"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Its been a while since the last full-fledged melee out of Argentina has come to our attention. Frankly, its been quiet. Too quiet.
      </p>
      <p>
       But sadly, we knew that eventually the silence wouldnt last. Enter fourth-division sides Deportivo Roca and Cipolletti.
      </p>
      <p>
       The two teams got involved in a massive brawl on Thursday after a foul at one end of the pitch quickly spilled over to the other end, where all 22 players and dozens of fans took part in a
       <span id="__caret">
        frenetic free-for-all:
        <br/>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <iframe frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/QuhqFVuNesw" width="560">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span>
        The match was suspended after only twenty minutes of play.
       </span>
      </p>
