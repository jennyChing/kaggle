             <img alt="Do you to remember a time when you believed in something" class="attachment-large wp-post-image" height="788" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/712/96DE7/rU9Oq8ZqiMHVCMoJ51nm2g/Do-you-to-remember-a-time-when-you-believed-in-something.png" width="940"/>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              Do you remember a time when you believed in something and that actually happen?
             </p>
             <p>
              Because belief is everything in life. Everything we have in life comes because of our belief that they are possible.
             </p>
             <p>
              If you dont believe in yourself how can you expect others to believe in you.
             </p>
             <p>
              In this post I thought Ill share with you 3 moments in my life that I believed I was going to achieve and that actually happen so that you too can think about similar moments in your life and how you could carry forward those same beliefs to the future so that you can achieve greater success in life.
             </p>
             <ul>
             </ul>
             <ol class="number-list">
              <li>
               <h3 class="itemTitle">
                My Big test in college
               </h3>
               <div class="item itemTool">
                <p>
                 Let me take you back to my college days.
                </p>
                <p>
                 This was the first big hurdle I had to jump in life after spending close to 11 years in college. This was the ultimate examination known as the G.C.E ordinary level or in abbreviated form known as O/Ls
                </p>
                <p>
                 <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/712/96E15/puDvkptSvTVZtYGzZHMgpw/oedinary-level-exam-results.jpg">
                  <img alt="oedinary level exam results" class="alignnone wp-image-183 size-thumbnail" height="150" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/712/96E16/tglQ5bYMyO_9V29PhRaKow/oedinary-level-exam-results-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Because I remember my teachers telling us in class that if we dont get through this exam, we wont be able to move forward in life like getting into higher studies, finding a suitable job as good grades in this exam was considered as entry points to all of this.
                </p>
                <p>
                 So as a student sitting for this exam there was lots of pressure to pass this exam with good grades not only from teachers but also from my parents as well.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The thing is I was never a bright student. And I could hardly even get 2 distinctions from the 8 subjects I had to do even throughout my term exams.
                </p>
                <p>
                 And I remember my sister whos two years younger to me use to always get distinctions for all the subjects she did and I was no where near her :)
                </p>
                <p>
                 But when it mattered most I delivered, I believed in myself that I could do way better than the results I was getting in the term exams throughout the years
                </p>
                <p>
                 And I worked hard studying intensely and the day I went to college to check my results for the G.C.E O/L exam What I saw on the results sheet under my name was
                </p>
                <p>
                 4 Distinctions, 3 Credits &amp; 1 simple pass for the 8 subjects I had taken.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The simple pass was for science, this was one subject I use to hate in school :)
                </p>
                <p>
                 This was the best results I had ever achieved throughout the exams I sat in college.
                </p>
                <p>
                 So if you believe that you can you actually can.
                </p>
               </div>
              </li>
              <li>
               <h3 class="itemTitle">
                My real exposure to the corporate world
               </h3>
               <div class="item itemTool">
                <p>
                 Ever since I had completed my higher studies the next challenge was to find suitable job.I went for so many interviews but I was not selected.I was feeling quite frustrated that I wasnt able to find a suitable job.
                </p>
                <p>
                 But one day I heard from one of my friends there was a vacancy in the marketing department in a multinational FMCG company.
                </p>
                <p>
                 I applied sent in my CV and I was called in for an interview.
                </p>
                <p>
                 When I was called for the 1
                 <sup>
                  st
                 </sup>
                 interview and when I faced it I knew that I will be called for the second interview as I was really confident about the first.
                </p>
                <p>
                 I wanted this job badly as I didnt have a job and also it wasnt easy getting into this company as you are being screened though so many applicants.
                </p>
                <p>
                 But I always had the belief Ill get the job.
                </p>
                <p>
                 So in the final interview from ten applicants two were selected. And I was one of them.
                </p>
                <p>
                 So I went from a place of not having a job, frustrated about not being selected at interviews, not sure of my future and somehow because of I believed that what happened in the past doesnt matter for the future I was able to get selected to the company I was looking for.
                </p>
                <p>
                 These are some pics below me speaking at company events
                </p>
                <p>
                 <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/712/96E94/ZfgSVNMODHdBJAOQRTwZeQ/speaking-in-events.png">
                  <img alt="speaking-in-events" class="alignnone wp-image-185 size-large" height="442" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/712/96E97/6jiCcPe70WJy3Ue_lhG2YQ/speaking-in-events-1024x442.png" width="1024"/>
                 </a>
                </p>
               </div>
              </li>
              <li>
               <h3 class="itemTitle">
                How I became a better badminton player
               </h3>
               <div class="item itemTool">
                <p>
                 I was involved in many sports during my school days.But badminton was a sport that I had never played during my school days.And this was a sport that I got exposed to few years back because of my dad who was playing this sport for a very long period of time and also he had won tournaments at the national level.
                </p>
                <p>
                 So when I first got started I never thought I could reach the skill level that I have now
                </p>
                <p>
                 As this is a sport that you need to be really technically sound.
                </p>
                <p>
                 But as I started to play over the years I really got the hang of this sport and also I was able to improve my skill level in order to be really competitive.
                </p>
                <p>
                 By no means I would say I am great at it but as I really believed in myself that I can improve and be more competitive I only keep getting better every time I get to the court
                </p>
                <p>
                 This is one of the courts where I play.
                </p>
                <p>
                 <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/713/96F12/01jF8gCXWnIb988KTvaPNg/20141021_202615.jpg">
                  <img alt="20141021_202615" class="alignnone wp-image-186 size-medium" height="225" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/713/96F14/pl5eebyeu8xnDSfsStyo0g/20141021_202615-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p>
                </p>
               </div>
              </li>
             </ol>
             <p>
              Therefore its about having faith, and faith gets stronger when you remember a time when you believed in something that already happen as you wished.
             </p>
             <p>
              This habit of going back to a moment where you had obstacles but came on top definitely does increase your belief levels going to the future as life is definitely going to present you various problems from time to time.
             </p>
             <p>
              Its important to always believe in your vision; believe that your vision is important, its important to believe that your future can be great.
             </p>
             <p>
              By believing that you can find the solutions to a problem, you will be definitely be more excited to work towards it.
             </p>
             <p>
              If we doubt our ability, then we have already lost the battle. Belief is a very strong medicine and it works in wonderful ways.
             </p>
             <p>
              So always believe that you will be able to find out all solutions. Believe that you will overcome all problems. Believe that you will emerge successful. Believe that you are a winner. Believe that you deserve success. Believe that you have the mental and physical strength to fight against all the odds.
             </p>

              These are the same beliefs that I took forward when I got started with my online home business.
