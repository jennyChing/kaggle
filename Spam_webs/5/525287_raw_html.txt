            Advances in education and knowledge delivery are crucial because today's students do not learn like they did 20 years ago. With the nearly inescapable saturation of technology, students today are practically hardwired to excel while learning through a web-based interface.
           </p>
           <p>
            The following infographic covers educational advances over the past hundred years. From simple tools like the #2 pencil to elaborate high-powered computing systems, like tablets and laptops - this infographic seeks to depict just how far "better education" has come.
           </p>
           <p>
            The infographic shares some of the amazing ways we have evolved over the past hundred years, but it also hints at the exciting future possibilities in the field of education.
