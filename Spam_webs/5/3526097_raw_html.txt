        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Costa Rica is really a tiny state  only Seventy five miles sets apart the Pacific Ocean out of the Carribbean Sea in the narrowest area  Costa Rica continues to have probably the most diversified landscapes on the planet. Its shorelines possess bothmarshy swamp sand sandy beaches , when its central is filled with volcanoes, thick with rainforests and full of uncommon plants.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         When you plan your Panama And Nicaragua , holiday, be sure to go to locations likeTortuguero National Park,Corcovado National Park, Monteverde Cloud Forest or Manuel Antonio National Park. Additionally, youll take pleasure in Costa Rica holiday, an array of venture vacation fun-based activities, such as deep-sea diving, climbing across the Arenal Volcano region, as well as going through the thick exotic jungles.

        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-564" height="675" src="http://www.mousetravel.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Costa_Rica_.jpg" title="Costa_Rica_" width="900"/>

        <p>
         Where to start? Where you should go? Travel throughout the rain-forest canopy. Go river rafting? Ascend a volcano? Relax on a sand beach front inside manuel antonio and guanacaste national park on the pacific coastline or Limon within the carribbean coastline? Take a look at vibrant sea life when scuba diving? Or perhaps attempt to hook a fish on the deep-sea angling adventure?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-565" height="676" src="http://www.mousetravel.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Costa_Rica_1.jpg" title="Costa_Rica_1" width="900"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Costa Rica delivers lots of enjoyable alternatives such as these, particularly if you are interested in the wilderness and open-air activities. That is why more and more people have chosen to travel there: During the past 10 years, the quantity of guests has quadrupled, and also travel and leisure has turned into a major portion of the countrys economic system. Costa Rica is virtually synonymous with holidays  journey which features education and learning concerning the natural environment and stimulates preservation of all-natural sources.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-566" height="675" src="http://www.mousetravel.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Costa_Rica_2.jpg" title="Costa_Rica_2" width="900"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The nation contains a great number of national parks and the wilderness protects which provide an abundant variety of wild birds, animals, {serpent|reptile} and rain- forest vegetation. The plethora of wild birds, especially, is incredible: Some Eight hundred fifty species are loaded right into a fairly tiny space, a bird watcher heaven. Around 25% of the states territory has been put aside inside preserved places, making Costa Rica a popularity being an eco-friendly delicate state as well as leader with regard to environmental preservation.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-567" height="900" src="http://www.mousetravel.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Costa_Rica_3.jpg" title="Costa_Rica_3" width="900"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The region contains superb types of conditions for a variety of excitement sports activities, particularly surfing, scuba diving,river rafting, mountaineering and , wind surfing .People who want a significantly less intense holiday can look at over 60 volcanoes (Poas volcano,Arenal volcano Rincon de la Vieja volcano, Irazu volcano) take charter boat journeys straight down rainforest streams and flow throughout the treetops within the planets very first Rainforest Aerial Tram, check out La Paz Fountain Landscapes, Tortuga Isle,The Cafe Britt Flavored coffee excursion
