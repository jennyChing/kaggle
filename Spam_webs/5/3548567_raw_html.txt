            <p>
             WATKINS GLEN, N.Y. -- Four-time Sprint Cup champion
             <a href="http://sports.espn.go.com/rpm/driver?seriesId=2&amp;driverId=67">
              Jeff Gordon
             </a>
             will have Drive to End Hunger back on the hood of his No. 24 Chevrolet in 2015.
            </p>
            <aside class="inline inline-photo float-l">
             <figure>
              <picture>
               <!--[if IE 9]&gt;&lt;video style="display: none;"&gt;&lt;![endif]-->
               <source data-srcset="http://a.espncdn.com/combiner/i?img=%2Fphoto%2F2014%2F0713%2Frpm_a_jeffgts_1296x729.jpg&amp;w=267, http://a.espncdn.com/combiner/i?img=%2Fphoto%2F2014%2F0713%2Frpm_a_jeffgts_1296x729.jpg&amp;w=534 2x" media="(min-width: 376px)">
                <source data-srcset="http://a.espncdn.com/combiner/i?img=%2Fphoto%2F2014%2F0713%2Frpm_a_jeffgts_1296x729.jpg&amp;w=375, http://a.espncdn.com/combiner/i?img=%2Fphoto%2F2014%2F0713%2Frpm_a_jeffgts_1296x729.jpg&amp;w=750 2x" media="(max-width: 375px)">

              <figcaption class="photoCaption">
               For the fifth consecutive season, AARP and its Drive to End Hunger campaign will return as the primary sponsor for Jeff Gordon in 2015.
               <cite>
                AP Photo/Jim Cole
               </cite>
              </figcaption>
             </figure>
            </aside>
            <p>
             Hendrick Motorsports announced Friday that AARP and AARP Foundation's nationwide campaign to address the problem of older adult hunger will return as a primary sponsor of Gordon for 13 races, including the Daytona 500.
            </p>
            <p>
             It marks AARP's fifth year as a primary sponsor of the No. 24 team, unprecedented in NASCAR for a social cause. The organization will be an associate sponsor in all other events.
            </p>
            <p>
             Drive to End Hunger is supported through donations and has donated more than 30 million meals since its launch in February 2011.
            </p>
            <p>
             Gordon says the relationship has been "an extremely rewarding and humbling experience."
            </p>
