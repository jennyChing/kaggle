           <div class="content-text">
            <p>
             <strong>
              Here are things we all know about
             </strong>
             <a href="http://www.redbull.com/en/athletes/1331580314963/john-wall" target="_blank">
              <strong>
               John Wall
              </strong>
             </a>
             <strong>
              :
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             -Born in September 6, 1990, Walls full name is Johnathan Hildred Wall, Jr. He played at the
             <a href="http://www.ukathletics.com/sports/m-baskbl/" target="_blank">
              <strong>
               University of Kentucky
              </strong>
             </a>
             for one year before being chosen first overall in the 2010 NBA Draft by the
             <a href="http://www.nba.com/wizards/" target="_blank">
              <strong>
               Washington Wizards
              </strong>
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             -In his first season in the NBA, Wall was the third youngest player to hit a triple double. He was also named Rookie Game MVP during the 2011
             <a href="http://www.nba.com/allstar/" target="_blank">
              <strong>
               All-Star weekend
              </strong>
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             -Known as being one of the quickest, most athletic point guards in the league, Walls famous for his explosiveness. So much so, his clothes blew off when he appeared butt-nekkid alongside athletes such as Tarah Gieger for
             <a href="http://espn.go.com/espn/photos/gallery/_/id/9428872/image/5/john-wall-2013-body-issue-bodies-want-espn-magazine" target="_blank">
              <strong>
               ESPNs famed 2013 Body Issue
              </strong>
             </a>
             .
            </p>
           </div>
           <div class="content-text">
            <p>
             <strong>
              Now heres what we dont know:
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             -Precisely how fast is John Wall? Thoroughbred horse-fast or are we talking cheetah-like speeds?
            </p>
            <p>
             -More appropriately, is John Wall fast enough to alley-oop a basketball by chucking it full-court, then sprinting to the other side quick enough to catch his own ball and make the basket? Check out the video
             <a href="http://dimemag.com" target="_blank">
              <strong>
               Dime Magazine
              </strong>
             </a>
             posted to find out.
            </p>
