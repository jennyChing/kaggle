        <h1>
         <a href="http://www.themoviefiftyshadesofgrey.com/50-shades-of-grey-movie-stars-dakota-johnson-and-jamie-dornan-shooting-scenes/">
          50 Shades of Grey Movie stars Dakota Johnson and Jamie Dornan shooting scenes
         </a>
        </h1>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Jamie Dornan and Dakota Johnson in Fifty Shades Movie" class="aligncenter wp-image-1363" height="460" src="http://www.themoviefiftyshadesofgrey.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Jamie-Dornan-and-Dakota-Johnson-in-Fifty-Shades-Movie.jpg" title="Jamie Dornan and Dakota Johnson photo" width="558"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         After much anticipation about the fate of the movie Fifty Shades of Grey, the love story written by
         <strong>
          E.L. James
         </strong>
         and its adaptation for the big screen, directed by
         <strong>
          Sam Taylor Johnson
         </strong>
         , the fans and the followers of the 50 Shades Movie trilogy can now heave a sigh of relief.
         <a href="http://www.themoviefiftyshadesofgrey.com/" target="_blank" title="fifty shades film">
          <strong>
           The movie Fifty Shades of Grey
          </strong>
         </a>
         will finally see the light of day and will be released on the
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          13
          <sup>
           th
          </sup>
          of February 2015
         </span>
         . But why did this movies fate hang in the balance? Though it is now known to all that the cast of the movie comprises
         <a href="http://www.themoviefiftyshadesofgrey.com/category/jamie-dornan/" target="_blank" title="Jamie Dornan">
          Jamie Dornan
         </a>
         as the sadistic pleasure loving
         <strong>
          Christian Grey
         </strong>
         and
         <a href="http://www.themoviefiftyshadesofgrey.com/category/dakota-johnson-anastasia-steele/" target="_blank" title="Dakota Johnson">
          Dakota Johnson
         </a>
         as his submissive partner, many may not know that this coveted role, that of the self made billionaire, first went to Charlie Hunnam, the noted cast member of the television series Sons of Anarchy. But citing date problems and his commitment to the television series, Hunnam had to walk out after only a few weeks.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          <img alt="Jamie Dornan and Dakota Johnson with Marcia gay Harden and Rita Ora" class="wp-image-1364 aligncenter" height="318" src="http://www.themoviefiftyshadesofgrey.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Jamie-Dornan-and-Dakota-Johnson-with-Marcia-gay-Harden-and-Rita-Ora.jpg" title="Jamie Dornan and Dakota Johnson with Marcia gay Harden and Rita Ora" width="508"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          Post that, it was a trying time for the casting team, as they scouted every possible opportunity to find the next
          <a href="http://www.themoviefiftyshadesofgrey.com/category/christian-grey/" target="_blank" title="Christian Grey">
           Christian Grey
          </a>
          and start shooting. After dispelling rumours that the film would be shelved for lack of an actor who would play the role of the dynamic businessman and entrepreneur, the casting team and
         </span>
         E.L. James
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          welcomes Irish actor and model,
          <strong>
           Jamie Dornan
          </strong>
          as the new (and might we say improved) Christian Grey. Soon after, the cast and the crew packed their bags and were off to Canada to start shooting this February 2015 release. The cast, which now sports an Oscar winner and a singer/songwriter, is now ready to shift base to Canada, just like their
          <a href="http://www.themoviefiftyshadesofgrey.com/category/anastasia-steele-50-shades-movie/" target="_blank" title="Anastasia Steele">
           Anastasia Steele
          </a>
          and Christian Grey have already done, and continue shooting.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          <img alt="dakota-johnson-jamie-dornan Shooting of Fifty Shades of Grey Movie" class="aligncenter wp-image-1360" height="468" src="http://www.themoviefiftyshadesofgrey.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/dakota-johnson-jamie-dornan-Shooting-of-Fifty-Shades-of-Grey-Movie.jpg" title="dakota-johnson-jamie-dornan Shooting of Fifty Shades of Grey Movie" width="468"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Another scene which has been in the news ever since it was shot was the bike scene where the 31 year old hunk, rescues Ana, as they are walking down the road and a speeding bike rushes towards Ana. The scene is important not only because it shows the chemistry between the lead pair but also because the feelings that the couple has, for each other, other than the BDSM fantasies, are explored for the first time. As they leave the cafe, Ana and Grey, engrossed in talk, walk along the road and suddenly out of nowhere a biker emerges and almost hits Ana, when the knight in shining armour, Grey, jumps in and pulls her out of harms way. As she coils in his arms, their relationship seems to go through a transformation and when he pushes a strand of her hair away from her face and behind her eyes, we are really forced to think about the future of this relationship.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="dakota-johnson-jamie-dornan-set-50-shades-of-grey-Movie" class="aligncenter wp-image-1361" height="302" src="http://www.themoviefiftyshadesofgrey.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/dakota-johnson-jamie-dornan-set-50-shades-of-grey-Movie-1024x652.jpg" title="Dakota Johnson kissing Jamie Dornan" width="475"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Among the first scenes that were shot in Canada, was the contract scene. For those who have read the trilogy or even the first part, will know that the pair embarks on a kinky and BDSM inspired relationship, which is just like a business arrangement and a deal, replete with non-disclosure agreements and contracts and when the businessman Grey, gives his docile partner, the virginal college graduate Ana a copy of the contract in a brown envelope, the fans could only see the palpable chemistry between the two, as the Ana walked away with her copy, dressed in red, wearing a camel coloured belted trench coat. But the scene did not end there, as she turned towards the Irish hunk one more time and before driving off in her car, she gently planted a kiss on his cheeks.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Christian Grey and Anastasia Steele" class="aligncenter wp-image-1359" height="277" src="http://www.themoviefiftyshadesofgrey.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Christian-Grey-and-Anastasia-Steele.jpg" title="Fifty Shades movie cafe scene" width="444"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The coffee scene, which was but of course shot in a cafe, is important as it shows Greys genuine interest in
         <strong>
          Anastasia Steele
         </strong>
         as he quizzes her about her life and parents and actually underneath all the facade, actually wants to know whether she is in a relationship or not. To get the look and the expressions correct in this scene, the director had reportedly asked the cafe owner to serve as many as 44 cups of lattes. Talk about perfection, anyone! Lets raise a cuppa to that!
        </p>
