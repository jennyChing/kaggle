       <h1>
        New Cincinnati Cyclones Logo, Uniforms Unveiled
       </h1>
      </div>
      <div id="post-date">
       Written By:
       <a href="http://news.sportslogos.net/author/ccreamer/" rel="author" title="Posts by Chris Creamer">
        Chris Creamer
       </a>
       Friday, August 29, 2014
      </div>
      <div id="video_holder" style="display:none;">
       <p>
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="pure_content">
       <p>
        <img alt="Cyclones new Logo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16472" height="375" src="http://content.sportslogos.net/news/2014/08/Cyclones-new-Logo.jpg" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         UPDATE
        </strong>
         The Cyclones have now officially unveiled their new logo and uniforms. The new logo is the same as what we posted earlier. Ouroriginal story below:
       </p>
       <p>
        The
        <a href="http://www.sportslogos.net/logos/list_by_team/1930/Cincinnati_Cyclones/">
         Cincinnati Cyclones
        </a>
        are set to make a big announcement tonight at 8pm ET, toutingit as a coming Winds of Change on their Twitter account.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/CincyCyclones/status/505361862100525056">
         <img alt="Cyclones winds of change" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-16473" height="462" src="http://content.sportslogos.net/news/2014/08/Cyclones-winds-of-change-590x462.jpg" width="590"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Well, if a trademark application for a new logo is any indicator, theyll be showing off their swell new look.
       </p>
       <p>
        Shown at the top of this post, the image which accompanies USPTO trademark serial#
        <span style="color: #000000;">
         86135240 shows a large black and white letter C with a red, black, and silver cyclone in the middle. The trademark was registered by Arena Management Holdings of Cincinnati, Ohio (the same group which owns the Cyclones existing trademarks) and was filed December 3, 2013.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        Heres the probable new mark compared to what theyd been using for the past decade:
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_16471" style="width: 600px">
        <img alt="Current on the left, possibly new on the right" class="size-large wp-image-16471" height="266" src="http://content.sportslogos.net/news/2014/08/Cyclones-Old-vs-New-590x266.jpg" width="590"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Current on the left, possibly new on the right
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Upgrade.
       </p>
       <p>
        The Cincinnati Cyclones are a member of the ECHL, the current franchiserelocated from Miami and played their first season in 2001.Currently they are an affiliate of both the NHLs Florida Panthers and Nashville Predators, they are two-time ECHLs Kelly CupChampions winning in 2008 and 2010.
       </p>
       <p>
        Teams using the Cyclones name have existed since 1990 playing previously in both the ECHL and the old International League where they used this, at one time, worst logo in the history of anything:
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.sportslogos.net/logos/view/xxf4virfop8xitms8nl968c32/Cincinnati_Cyclones/1993/Primary_Logo">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="222" src="http://content.sportslogos.net/logos/15/2964/full/xxf4virfop8xitms8nl968c32.gif" width="250"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <strong>
          UPDATE (Aug 29/14 8:03pm ET):
         </strong>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        The Cincinnati Cyclones have officially unveiled the logo we posted earlier today, an excerpt from their official release which you can
        <a href="http://cycloneshockey.com/news/2014/08/new-look-same-fun" target="_blank">
         read in full here
        </a>
        :
       </p>
       <p>
        In partnership with Cincinnati-based agency, LPK, the Cyclones have unveiled new branding in conjunction with a complete uniform overhaul. The updated colour scheme features red, black, and gunmetal grey, which will be highlighted on the teams three uniform combinations.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://news.sportslogos.net/2014/08/29/is-this-the-new-cincinnati-cyclones-logo/screen-shot-2014-08-29-at-20-04-47/" rel="attachment wp-att-16479">
         <img alt="Cincinnati Cyclones New Uniform and Logo 2014-15" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-16479" height="309" src="http://content.sportslogos.net/news/2014/08/Screen-shot-2014-08-29-at-20.04.47--590x309.png" width="590"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://news.sportslogos.net/2014/08/29/is-this-the-new-cincinnati-cyclones-logo/cyclones-black-jersey/" rel="attachment wp-att-16480">
         <img alt="Cyclones Black Jersey" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16480" height="282" src="http://content.sportslogos.net/news/2014/08/Cyclones-Black-Jersey.jpg" width="260"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://news.sportslogos.net/2014/08/29/is-this-the-new-cincinnati-cyclones-logo/cyclones-grey-jersey/" rel="attachment wp-att-16481">
         <img alt="Cyclones Grey Jersey" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16481" height="282" src="http://content.sportslogos.net/news/2014/08/Cyclones-Grey-Jersey.jpg" width="260"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://news.sportslogos.net/2014/08/29/is-this-the-new-cincinnati-cyclones-logo/cyclones-logo-and-wordmark/" rel="attachment wp-att-16482">
         <img alt="Cyclones Logo and Wordmark" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16482" height="85" src="http://content.sportslogos.net/news/2014/08/Cyclones-Logo-and-Wordmark.png" width="325"/>
        </a>
