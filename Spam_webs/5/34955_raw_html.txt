           <a href="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/?p=10748" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Model Lisa Fonssagrives was Haute Couture Personified">
            Model Lisa Fonssagrives was Haute Couture Personified
           </a>
          </h1>
          <div class="art-PostHeaderIcons art-metadata-icons">
           February 4th, 2011 | Author:
           <a href="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/?author=6" title="Posts by Ali Basye">
            Ali Basye
           </a>
           |
           <a href="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/?p=10748#comments">
            Post Comment
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="art-PostContent">
           <p>
            <span>
             <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-668" height="42" src="http://otdif.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/SBTS_HEADER_1png1.png" title="SBTS_HEADER_1png" width="560"/>
             <a href="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/?p=10748">
              <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10749" height="370" src="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Lisa-Fonssagrives-wine-bottle.jpg" title="Lisa-Fonssagrives-wine-bottle" width="560"/>
             </a>
             <strong>
              On this day in 1992, Swedish beauty Lisa Fonssagrives died in Long Island.
             </strong>
             She is often called the worlds first supermodelalthough
             <a href="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/?p=201" target="_blank">
              Dorian Leigh
             </a>
             ,
             <a href="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/?p=7385" target="_blank">
              Suzy Parker
             </a>
             and Janice Dickenson would happily argue that point to the graveearning more than $40 an hour when other models were striking a pose for half that amount or even less. Fonssagrives began modeling for
             <a href="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/?p=3971" target="_blank">
              Horst P. Horst
             </a>
             in 1936, but her heyday was from the late 1940s into the 1950s, when
             <a href="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/?p=8822" target="_blank">
              Christian Diors New Look
             </a>
             ushered in an era of grace and nobility. Her figureone both simultaneously willowly and angularand aristocratic looks
             <span id="more-10748">
             </span>
             beautifully translated
             <a href="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/?p=8822" target="_blank">
              Diors vision of
              <em>
               Corelle
              </em>
             </a>
             . At a time when most models careers lasted a few short years, her age-defying features allowed hers to flourish into her 40s.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            Fonssagrives can also take credit for creating the clichd fashion fairy tale: In 1950 she married
            <a href="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/?p=6480" target="_blank">
             her favorite photographer, Irving Penn
            </a>
            , and the two lived a long and happy life together until her death in New York. The self-described good clothes hanger was a frequent subject of Penns photographs for
            <a href="http://onthisdayinfashion.com/?p=8847" target="_blank">
             <em>
              Vogue
             </em>
             magazine
            </a>
            hows that for a marriage perk?particularly his studies of French haute couture.
            <strong>
             Her pert nose sloped as dramatically as her waistline
            </strong>
            , making for irresistible profile shots and, as you can see in the images below, many pictures of Fonssagrives are inevitably captured from the side. 
            <em>
             Ali Basye
            </em>
            <br/>
