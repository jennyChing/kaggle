&lt;p&gt;&lt;em&gt;The high heeled Staphorster clog is handpainted in an old traditional dotted craftwork pattern by an elderly woman from Staphorst. The wooden clog was manufactured by Jos Hogenkamp.&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Clogs have been a popular footwear in the &lt;a href="http://creativeroots.org/?cat=999"&gt;Netherlands&lt;/a&gt; since Dr. Scholl successfully branded them in 1882. In 2007 &lt;a href="http://www.viktor-rolf.com/index.htm" target="_blank"&gt;Viktor &amp; Rolf&lt;/a&gt; came up with their own collection to honor this traditional footwear.
