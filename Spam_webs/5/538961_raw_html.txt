      <p>
       MTV India in Partnership with Swipe Telecom has come up with a budget voice calling Tablet named the
       <b>
        Swipe MTV Slash 4X
       </b>
       . The device can be Pre-booked with e-Retailer
       <a href="http://www.ebay.in/itm/Swipe-MTV-SLASH-4X-Tablet-Android-4-2-Dual-SIM-Wi-Fi-3G-7-Touch-Screen-/171219116964?pt=IN_iPads_Tablets&amp;hash=item27dd7463a4" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        eBay
       </a>
       for Rs. 8,990 with delivery starting from January 27, 2014 however, it is said that the actual price of the device will be higher upwards Rs. 9,999 after the Pre-Order ends.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.newzars.com/intex-aqua-curve-key-features/" title="Intex Aqua Curve with Quad Core CPU goes official: Key Features">
         Recommended: Intex Aqua Curve with Quad Core CPU goes official: Key Features
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Swipe MTV Slash 4X
       </b>
       claims to be Indias first Co-Branded 7-Inch Quad Core Tablet. Coming to the specifications, The Tablet comes with Dual SIM with 3G voice calling Support. The device features 7-Inch IPS Display with OGS Technology having 1024 x 600 Pixel Resolution. The tablet gets its processing power from 1.3GHz Quad Core Chipset coupled with 1GB of DDR3 RAM for smooth performance.
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Swipe MTV Slash 4x
       </b>
       Runs Android 4.2.2 Jelly Bean OS and comes with 4GB of in-built storage which is further expandable up to 32GB. There is a front VGA camera for video calling along with a 2 Mega Pixel camera on the back. To power up the device, Company has managed a 3000 mAh Battery. As far as connectivity goes, the device will have all the Major connectivity options like Wi-Fi, MicroUSB, Bluetooth, A-GPS etc.
      </p>
