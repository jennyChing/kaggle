             <p>
              The weather in South Florida is the perfect companion for a glass of fresh squeezed juice. Juice bars are popping up all over Florida, and Juicernet is happy to provide the equipment from our CJ for citrus juicing to our Ceado ES-700 for fruit and vegetables to the MJ575 for wheatgrass. We even stock the Vitamix blenders for the smoothies. We love helping our local juice bars and supermarkets serve up glasses of fresh squeezed juice daily. The excitement that is building around the entire juicing industry is a great move for everyone. Adding just one juice a day will give a boost to your health and energy level. Juicernet is located in Jupiter, Florida a little bit north of Palm Beach and about 2 hours from Miami. We do have customers all over North America and the Caribbean.
             </p>
             <p>
              Our typical juice bar package includes the
              <a href="http://juicernet.com/portfolio/vegetable/ceado-es-700commercial-fruit-and-vegetable-juicer/" title="Ceado ES-700 Commercial Fruit and Vegetable Juicer">
               Ceado
              </a>
              <a href="http://juicernet.com/portfolio/vegetable/ceado-es-700commercial-fruit-and-vegetable-juicer/" title="Ceado ES-700 Commercial Fruit and Vegetable Juicer">
               ES-700
              </a>
              for all of your on demand fruit and vegetable drinks. If your juice bar wishes to add citrus we recommend our CJ by JBT Fresh n Squeeze. Most juice bars include wheatgrass shots on their menu. To process those you will need the MJ575. The
              <a href="http://juicernet.com/portfolio/wheatgrass/" title="Wheatgrass Juicers">
               MJ575
              </a>
              is a masticating slow juicer that can process ginger and wheatgrass shots as well as other greens. If you are including smoothies on your menu then you will also need one of our blenders, both by
              <a href="http://juicernet.com/blenders/" title="Blenders">
