         <p>
          <span style="color: #008000;">
           <em>
            * Mom Central received products to facilitate this review. All opinions are our own.
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Spending time outdoors in the summer is a given.We love feeling the fresh warm air whether we are strolling in the park or visiting the beach. Where the warmer temperatures certainly lighten our mood, they can also do a number on our hair and skincare routine, and weve discovered a few new products this year that have helped us maintain a healthy look all season-long.
         </p>
         <h3>
          <span style="font-weight: bold;">
           <em>
            For Our Hair:
           </em>
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Infiniti Pro by Conair Finishing Brush with One n Only Replaceable Argan Oil Treatment Strip
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          This brush helps our hair get the ultimate finished look  and very simply! The Infiniti Pro by Conair Finishing Brush with One n Only Replaceable Argan Oil Treatment Strip has a built-in argon oil treatment strip. The argon oil offers great benefits like less frizz, more conditioning, and more shine  all by just brushing our hair. I typically blow out my hair with a round brush and then go over it with my favorite straightener from
          <a href="http://www.ghdhair.com" target="_blank">
           ghd
          </a>
          . This brush really helps to make the look smooth.
         </p>
         <p>
          A tip for care  be careful when putting the plastic covering back over the strip. If you are over-zealous like me, it could potentially break if you are too forceful with it. Luckily
          <a href="http://www.conair.com/infiniti-pro-by-conair-finishing-brush-one-n-only-replaceable-argan-oil-treatment-strip-p-1547-1_124_188.html" target="_blank">
           you can get replacement strips here
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <center>
          <br/>
          <img alt="Infiniti Pro by Conair Finishing Brush" src="/wp-content/uploads/u8/conair-hairbrush.jpg" style="width: 235px; height: 436px; border-width: 1px; border-style: solid; margin: 10px;"/>
         </center>
         <b>
          One n Only Argan Oil Color Oasis Clarifying Shampoo
         </b>
         <p>
          One n Only has created several products that help with our summer hair. There are plenty of products in this line that contain argon oil, which again lead to those great frizz-free benefits! One of our favorite products in this line is the
          <a href="http://www.one-n-only.com/catalog.php?pcID=4_1&amp;products_id=41" target="_blank">
           One n Only Argan Oil Color Oasis Clarifying Shampoo
          </a>
          . Being out in the sun definitely can affect my highlights, and I tend to color my hair more often in the summertime to combat fading hues. Use this shampoo before you plan to color, as it can help strip away build-up and leave the hair more prone to absorb the new, fresh color.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Brazilian Tech Keratin Deep Penetrating Conditioning Treatment
          </b>
         </p>
         <p>
          We wouldnt dream of skipping conditioner when washing our hair, and its even more necessary when sun and frequent dips in the pool or ocean can leave our hair particularly dry.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Brazilian-Penetrating-Conditioning-Treatment-Ounce/dp/B007ZYYT8S" target="_blank">
           Brazilian Tech Keratin Deep Penetrating Conditioning Treatment
          </a>
          infuses hair with keratin, vitamins and proteins to make hair smooth, soft and strong. Its specifically for those of us with color-treated hair, and protects against our heat styling tools, too!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <center>
          <br/>
          <img alt="Brazilian Tech Keratin Deep Penetrating Conditioning Treatment" src="/wp-content/uploads/u8/brazilian-tech-keratin.jpg" style="width: 214px; height: 411px; border-width: 1px; border-style: solid; margin: 10px;"/>
         </center>
         <b>
          Baby Bliss Pro MiraCurl
         </b>
         <p>
          I try and embrace my natural waves as much as possible during the summer, and the
          <a href="http://miracurlpro.com/styling-products.php?product=4" target="_blank">
           Baby Bliss Pro MiraCurl leave-in detangler and curl prep
          </a>
          is the perfect formula to help. It doesnt weigh hair down, so curls can maintain their bounce, and I love the boost of moisture it provides. It certainly helps give us beach waves in our hair!
         </p>
         <h3>
          <em>
           <span style="font-weight: bold;">
            For Our Skin:
           </span>
          </em>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Burts Bees Brightening Daily Facial Cleanser and Radiance Toner
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          We cant forget about keeping our skin feeling fresh, bright and clean in the summer months when we are exposed to more time outdoors and of course are prone to get a bit sweaty. We love the
          <a href="http://www.burtsbees.com/Brightening-Daily-Facial-Cleanser/02288-00,default,pd.html" target="_blank">
           Brightening Daily Facial Cleanser
          </a>
          from
          <a href="http://www.burtsbees.com" target="_blank">
           Burts Bees
          </a>
          to help our skin feel renewed all summer long. The cleanser uses daisy extract, and has a light floral scent that is not overbearing. I love how rich the cream is. Its definitely more luxurious to apply than other cleansers I have tried!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <center>
          <br/>
          <img alt="Burt's Bees Brightening Daily Facial Cleanser" src="/wp-content/uploads/u8/burts-bees-cleanser.jpg" style="width: 200px; height: 444px; border-width: 1px; border-style: solid; margin: 10px;"/>
         </center>
         We also love
         <a href="http://www.burtsbees.com/Radiance-Toner/00961-03,default,pd.html" target="_blank">
          Burts Bees Radiance Toner
         </a>
         . Its a great way to remove excess dirt and make-up and clear up our pores.
         <p>
          <strong>
           Smashbox Full Exposure Palette
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <center>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           <img alt="Smashbox Full Exposure Palette" src="/wp-content/uploads/u8/smashbox-full-exposure-pale_2.jpg" style="width: 300px; height: 206px; border-width: 1px; border-style: solid; margin: 10px;"/>
          </strong>
         </center>
         For a finishing touch, we love the
         <a href="http://www.smashbox.com/product/6109/28675/Palettes-Kits/FULL-EXPOSURE-PALETTE/New/index.tmpl" target="_blank">
          Smashbox Full Exposure Palette
         </a>
         , which combines our favorite nudes and shimmers in a convenient palate that is easy to store and take with you on the go!
