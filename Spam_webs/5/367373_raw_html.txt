           <p>
            http://ansys.com/Industries/Aerospace+&amp;+Defense
           </p>
           <p>
            The modern aircraft is an engineering marvel. The epitome of a smart system. An intricate balance of hardware and software technologies operating across extreme environments.
            <br/>
            Performing safely, reliably and predictably. Performingas PROMISED.
           </p>
           <p>
            To reach this level of performance, the aerospace industry demands accurate fast and reliable simulation technology supported by scalable and cost effective IT solutions that leverage high performance computing as well as cloud and mobile technology to empower robust design methodologies offered in an integrated multiphysics environment allowing aerospace engineers to collaborate across disciplines departments and even continents.
           </p>
           <p>
            Aerospace has led the way with simulation-driven design processes amplified through automation customization and a seamless user experience empowering engineers of ALL experience levels to truly extend the boundaries of innovative design.
           </p>
           <p>
            They have trusted the most advanced simulation tools for both single physics and full mechatronic system modeling.
           </p>
           <p>
            Tools that are accurate fast. AND reliable
           </p>
           <p>
            The technology, vision and strategy of ANSYS stand alone as the leading provider of critical modeling and robust simulation solutions for the aerospace industry.
           </p>
           <p>
            Enabling our customers to consistently realize their product promise.     (pick up)
            <br/>
            Enabling you to realize your product promise.
           </p>
           <p>
            How exactly does the aerospace industry rely on ANSYS simulations?
           </p>
           <p>
            In more ways than you would think.
           </p>
           <p>
            Today, fuel and environmental performance have become mission-critical in our industry.
           </p>
           <p>
            Starting with engine performance, ANSYS provides critical simulation best practices in the areas of flow path aerodynamics aeromechanics thermal design and combustion processes.
           </p>
           <p>
            Specific solutions include stress and modal analysis heat transfer for high temperature operation rotor dynamics compressor aerodynamics and turbine aerodynamics and blade row aeromechanics.
           </p>
           <p>
            In reducing the weight of the aircraft, ANSYS provides simulation capabilities for advanced composite materials that are faster than traditional methods and allow for thermal and electrical properties to address the challenge of multifunctional composites.
           </p>
           <p>
            ANSYS continues to participate in industry leading consortia to advance aerodynamic design.
           </p>
           <p>
            A portfolio of coupled physics enables multi-disciplinary optimization to deliver higher fidelity understanding of aircraft performance throughout the flight envelope.
           </p>
           <p>
            With the increase of electronic systems, ANSYS is focused on providing simulation best practices for electric machine design antenna design mechatronics and thermal management.
           </p>
           <p>
            We recognize that with more electronic systems comes the dramatic rise in the number of embedded software lines of code.
           </p>
           <p>
            Through the SCADE family of products ANSYS provides software that delivers FAA certified embedded code that is automatically generated.
           </p>
           <p>
            This can reduce the overall time to deliver certified code by 50% compared with manual or non-certified code generation methods.
           </p>
           <p>
            Beyond performance, passenger safety and comfort are paramount concerns for any airline.
           </p>
           <p>
            Whether its for icing blade impact and damage bird strike lightning strike or crashworthiness ANSYS provides a portfolio of technology to support the development of the safest aircraft.
           </p>
           <p>
            As a member of the Cabin Air Reformative Environment or (CARE) consortium, ANSYS is using modeling and simulation to improve the in-cabin air quality and control the spread of disease in flight.
           </p>
           <p>
            ANSYS also provides a suite of aeroacoustic and vibroacoustic solvers to significantly reduce noise comply with industry regulations and improve the passenger experience.
           </p>
           <p>
            Highly complex. Intricately interconnected  the modern aircraft is a smart system unlike any other.
           </p>
           <p>
            Requiring the most advanced hardware and software system simulation technologies available.
           </p>
           <p>

How exactly does the aerospace industry rely on ANSYS simulations? &lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
In more ways than you would think.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
Today, fuel and environmental performance have become mission-critical in our industry.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
Starting with engine performance, ANSYS provides critical simulation best practices in the areas of flow path aerodynamics... aeromechanics... thermal design and combustion processes.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
Specific solutions include stress and modal analysis... heat transfer for high temperature operation... rotor dynamics... compressor aerodynamics... and turbine aerodynamics and blade row aeromechanics.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
In reducing the weight of the aircraft, ANSYS provides simulation capabilities for advanced composite materials that are faster than traditional methods and allow for thermal and electrical properties to address the challenge of multifunctional composites.  &lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
ANSYS continues to participate in industry leading consortia to advance aerodynamic design. &lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
A portfolio of coupled physics enables multi-disciplinary optimization to deliver higher fidelity understanding of aircraft performance throughout the flight envelope. &lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
With the increase of electronic systems, ANSYS is focused on providing simulation best practices for electric machine design... antenna design... mechatronics... and thermal management.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
We recognize that with more electronic systems comes the dramatic rise in the number of embedded software lines of code. &lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
Through the SCADE family of products ANSYS provides software that delivers FAA certified embedded code that is automatically generated. &lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
This can reduce the overall time to deliver certified code by 50% compared with manual or non-certified code generation methods.&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;

&lt;br /&gt;

&lt;br /&gt;
ANSYS also provides a suite of aeroacoustic and vibroacoustic solvers to significantly reduce noise... comply with industry regulations... and improve the passenger experience. &lt;br /&gt;

Highly complex. Intricately interconnected - the modern aircraft is a smart system unlike any other.&lt;br /&gt;
