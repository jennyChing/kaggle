         Get the Look: After the introduction of Prince George, we introduce our Georgia collection!
        </h1>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         For the last 9 months, the world has waited patiently with baited breath for the birth of the first child of TRH William and Kate, Duke and Duchess of Cambridge. Last week, on the 22nd July 2013, Prince George Alexander Louis was born, 8 pounds and 6 ounces and became the third in line to the British throne.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.77diamonds.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Prince-George.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4388" height="465" src="http://www.77diamonds.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Prince-George.jpg" title="William and Kate with their new son Prince George" width="700"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         At
         <a href="http://www.77diamonds.com">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <strong>
            77 Diamonds
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         , we love anything Royal and anything with diamonds, so what better way to celebrate such a historic moment than with our new diamond and sapphire collection, the
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          <strong>
           Georgia
          </strong>
         </span>
         collection. Inspired by the engagement ring of HRH Duchess of Cambridge and featuring a central blue sapphire surrounded by white diamonds we have created both earrings and pendant of this exquisite design.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.77diamonds.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/MG_0009.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4389" height="467" src="http://www.77diamonds.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/MG_0009.jpg" title="Our Georgia Collection" width="700"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         The pendant features 11 diamonds surrounding a sublime sapphire set in white gold that is on an 18 inch chain of the same metal.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.77diamonds.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/MG_0016.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4390" height="467" src="http://www.77diamonds.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/MG_0016.jpg" title="Georgia Pendant" width="700"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         The earrings are of matching design with 10 diamonds surrounding the sapphires with an additional 3 diamonds on the drop of the delicate hoop of the earring.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.77diamonds.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/MG_0017.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4391" height="467" src="http://www.77diamonds.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/MG_0017.jpg" title="Our Georgia Earrings" width="700"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         For more information regarding our Georgia Collection, please do contact our sales team at:
         <a href="http://www.77diamonds.com/contact-77diamonds.html">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <strong>
            sales@77diamonds.com
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <span style="clear:both;display:none;">

        </span>
