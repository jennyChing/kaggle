         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          <img alt="Duffle bag tutorial with free pattern on believeninspire.com" class="alignnone wp-image-3540 size-full" height="895" src="http://believeninspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Duffle-bag-tutorial-with-free-pattern-on-believeninspire.com_.jpg" width="650"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          Sew a
          <a href="http://believeninspire.com/2014/07/duffle-bag-from-pillow-cover-free-sewing-pattern.html" target="_blank">
           duffle bag
          </a>
          for their sleepovers or for gym.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          <img alt="Notebook cover Tutorial - 10 Back to School Sewing Ideas with Free Patterns on believeninspire.com" class="alignnone wp-image-4348 size-full" height="600" src="http://believeninspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/720f5680a7e4055e32feb5fa006eee891.jpg" width="389"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          Or make a
          <a href="http://www.theribbonretreat.com/blog/fabric-covered-notebook.html" target="_blank">
           fabric coverednotebook
          </a>
          cover to make the books stand out.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          <img alt="Vera bradley inspired cosmetic bags - 10 Back to School Sewing Ideas with Free Patterns on believeninspire.com" class="alignnone wp-image-3719 size-full" height="975" src="http://believeninspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/vera-bradley-inspired-cosmetic-bags.jpg" width="650"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          Another thing you can sew is a
          <a href="http://believeninspire.com/2014/07/vera-bradley-inspired-quilted-cosmetic-bags-tutorial.html" target="_blank">
           cosmetic/toiletries pouch
          </a>
          . It would be a perfect to carry to gym and swimming.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          <img alt="DIY Laptop Sleeves - 10 Back to School Sewing Ideas with Free Patterns on believeninspire.com" class="alignnone wp-image-4350 size-full" height="850" src="http://believeninspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/DSC_01832.jpg" width="650"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          Or surprise your child with a new laptop cover that goes with their style. Find the sewing tutorial at
          <a href="http://believeninspire.com/2014/04/diy-laptop-sleeves-computer-bags.html" target="_blank" title="DIY Laptop Sleeves (Computer Bags)">
           DIY Laptop Sleeves (Computer Bags).
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          <img alt="DIY Manicure Pouch (Sewing Ideas) on believeninspire.com - 10 Back to School Sewing Ideas with Free Patterns on believeninspire.com" class="alignnone wp-image-3774 size-full" height="975" src="http://believeninspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/DSC_0630.jpg" width="650"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          You can also sew a gift for their teachers. A cute manicure wallet would be a great gift. You can get the free pattern and tutorial
          <a href="http://believeninspire.com/2014/08/diy-manicure-pouch-sewing-ideas.html" target="_blank" title="DIY Manicure Pouch (Sewing Ideas) Back to School Sewing Ideas">
           here
          </a>
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          <img alt="Kids lunch box pattern and tutorial - 10 Back to School Sewing Ideas with Free Patterns on believeninspire.com" class="alignnone wp-image-4351 size-full" height="896" src="http://believeninspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Kidslunchboxpatternandtutorial.png" width="600"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
          Lastly, the most important thing for the kiddoes, a perfect lunch bag. Find the tutorial at
          <a href="http://www.skiptomylou.org/2013/08/24/kids-lunch-box-pattern-tutorial/" target="_blank">
           Kids Lunch Box Pattern &amp; Tutorial
          </a>
