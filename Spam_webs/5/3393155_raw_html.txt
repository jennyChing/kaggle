      <h1>
       Top 5 Tuesdays:
       <strong>
        The Best Superhero Cars
       </strong>
      </h1>
      <p class="timestamp">
       August 12, 2014
      </p>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        <img alt="Top5Tuesdays" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7279" height="200" src="https://www.thecarloanwarehouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Top5Tuesdays.png" width="615"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        We all have our answers ready when asked what our dream super power would be  but were more interested in the machine behind the man. If you were a superhero, which super-car would you pick? For this weeks Top 5 Tuesday, weve picked the best crime-fighting vehicles around, so you can save the day in style!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Black Beauty  Green Hornet (2011)
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="Superhero Movie Cars: Black Beauty Green Hornet" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7588" height="377" src="https://www.thecarloanwarehouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/BlackBeautyGreenHornet.png" width="615"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        The beast that is Black Beauty, Green Hornets supercar, is a modified 1965 Chrysler Imperial. If its rocket launchers and machine guns arent enough to deter the baddies, it also features a spiked wheels and even a flamethrower! Its clear from its wide array of weaponry that the Black Beauty takes no prisoners. In the 2011 film version of Green Hornet, Black Beauty is the star of the show, overshadowing every other character in the superhero blockbuster.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Fantasticar  Fantastic Four: Rise of the Silver Surfer (2007)
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="Superhero Movie Cars: The Fantastic Four, Fantasticar" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7589" height="332" src="https://www.thecarloanwarehouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Fantasticar.png" width="615"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        The Fantastic Fours super vehicle made its big screen debut in the 2007 Fantastic Four sequel Fantastic Four: Rise of the Silver Surfer. Although its named the Fantasti
        <i>
         car
        </i>
        , its really more of a plane, flying the clan from New York to Siberia in a matter of minutes. The sleek design also boasts the ability to split into multiple sections  because nobody likes to car share.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Mist Mobile  Kick Ass (2009)
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="Superhero Movie Cars: The MistMobile, Kick Ass" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7590" height="314" src="https://www.thecarloanwarehouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/MistMobileKickAss.png" width="615"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Kick Ass and Red Mist may just be ordinary kids trying to be superheroes, but Red Mists ride is far from ordinary. Its not just a modded-up Ford Mustang, says Mist: Its got sat-nav, its got my iPhone so I can check the websites for emergencies when Im driving around, its got a little bit of mood lighting right there, a rear-view camera  no big deal  oh, and check this, youre going to love this the mist. Yep, it can even fire smoke from its bonnet  whats a superhero without his superhero gimmick?
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        The Incredicar  The Incredibles (2004)
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="Superhero Movie Cars: The Incredibles, Incredicar" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7591" height="283" src="https://www.thecarloanwarehouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Incredicar.png" width="615"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        The car driven by the Incredibles family patriarch, Mr Incredible, during his superhero hay day has the ability to disguise itself, changing to stealth mode in order to go about day-to-day life undetected. The jet turbine changes into a spare tire and the hood retracts to look like a normal sedan  but changing appearance isnt even the most impressive feat of the Incredicar. Along with all the standard gizmos and gadgets  an ejector seat for example  the car even has the ability to dress Mr Incredible in seconds, which comes in very handy (especially when hes late for his own wedding)!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        The Batmobile
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="Superhero Movie Cars: Batman Begins, the Batmobile" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7592" height="316" src="https://www.thecarloanwarehouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/BatmanBeginsBatmobile.png" width="615"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        This has to be everyones number one. Its the ultimate superhero car. Batmans famous car has gone through its fair share of incarnations over the years, from the sleek art deco look Tim Burton gave the car in Batman (1989) and Batman Returns (1992) to the most recent army tank style that Christopher Nolan opted for in The Dark Knight Trilogy (2005  2012). Nolans beefed-up Batmobile boasts autocannons, rocket launchers and an impenetrable armoured exterior.
       </p>
       <p>
        Most impressive, though, is the Batpod. If the car gets damaged, the two front wheels can detach to form a motorbike while the rest of the car self-destructs. The legendary vehicle is set to return, with a new look and even more new features, in Zach Snyders upcoming Batman V Superman: Dawn of Justice.
       </p>
       <p>
        You may not have superpowers, but you can still have your dream car. The Car Loan Warehouse can help you get the best deal on
        <a href="http://www.thecarloanwarehouse.com/apply-online/">
         car finance
        </a>

        <a href="http://www.thecarloanwarehouse.com/car-loan-calculator/">
         car finance calculator
        </a>
        for an instant car finance estimate.
       </p>
