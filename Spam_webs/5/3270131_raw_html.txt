           <h2 class="blue">
            Making a better MICE event with social media
           </h2>
           <script src="/typo3conf/ext/news/Resources/Public/JavaScript/jquery.bxslider.min.js">
           </script>
           <script src="/typo3conf/ext/news/Resources/Public/JavaScript/blog-image.js">
           </script>
           <!-- media files -->
           <div class="news-img-wrap">
            <div class="mediaelement mediaelement-image">
             <img src="/uploads/tx_news/Blog24.jpg" style="max-height: 300px" width="100%"/>
            </div>
           </div>
           <!-- teaser -->
           <div class="teaser-text">
            The common goal of MICE planning is to create an event, make an impact among participants and fulfill its marketing objectives; and social media is highly effective in accomplishing this. Social networks like Youtube, Facebook and Twitter have drastically changed our everyday lives. Photo sharing sites Instragram, Pinterest, and Flickr are key social media tools that help inspire new ideas for business events, while LinkedIn can connect you to other professionals around the globe.
           </div>
           <!-- main text -->
           <div class="news-text-wrap">
            <div>
             <p>
              The content-, promotion- and activity-related social tools found on social network sites like these have become the essential backbone of todays successful business event.
             </p>
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             <p>
              So how is it that social networks like these are so popular and effective at things like boosting ticket sales and engaging event participants? They simply provide the perfect medium for the two major features that attract people to events: networking opportunities and educational content.
             </p>
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             <p>
              <b>
               Here are a few most-used social media sites for supporting MICE events:
              </b>
             </p>
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             <p>
              <b>
               Facebook
              </b>
               The worlds most popular and populous social network is super-effective for promoting your event and creating a buzz that will grab attention. Facebook is also where to go for information on latest promotions, contests and
              <a class="external-link-new-window" href="http://businesseventsthailand.com/event-calendar/highlights/" title="Opens external link in new window">
               upcoming events
              </a>
              .
             </p>
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             <p>
              <b>
               Twitter
              </b>
               Perfect for easy and effective real-time updates, announcements and live streaming during the event, or engage potential attendees by running contest using your Twitter feed. For example, post a marketing trivia question and offer free event registration to the person who answers correctly.
             </p>
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             <p>
              <b>
               Pinterest, Flickr, and Instragram
              </b>
              - can be used for uploading photos and videos, which assure your event continues generating interest and contacts well after its over. With
              <b>
               Flickrs creative commons feature
              </b>
              , links and embeds are mirrored on other sites like Wikipedia Commons, while the new
              <b>
               Video on Instagram
              </b>
              feature lets users create and upload a 15-second, filter-enabled, editable video. Meanwhile,
              <b>
               Pinterst
              </b>
              makes it possible to provide true value to your communities with a blend of utility, inspiration, and product information.
             </p>
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             <p>
              <b>
               Youtube
              </b>
              - The biggest player in online videos,
              <b>
               YouTube
              </b>
              lets you upload spots of up to 15 minutes, adding a personal touch to your marketing in a way thats easy to adapt to your clients needs. Create an office tour, give a behind-the-scenes look at an event or produce video biographies of people in your business.
             </p>
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             <p>
              <b>
               LinkedIn
              </b>
               The most popular professional online network is now having surpassed over 300M users. You can use your companys page to promote your event by updating and adding events to the page. Also, you can join up some LinkedIn Groups to stimulate a discussion around one of topics that you are planning to cover in your conference.
             </p>
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             <p>
              Its easy to gain big benefits with social media as part of your event strategy, by taking full advantage of the free listing and social tools you can find on these sites. Just create official web pages for your event on each site to enjoy the huge variety of event-enhancing features they offer.
             </p>
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             <p>
              To avoid communication problems that are known to plague live feeds, designate a person who is familiar with the tone and manner of your brand to manage your social network channels. They should be able to respond with authority to queries and comments, manage feedback online and have experience with a wide variety of social tools.
             </p>
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             <p>
              See all the
              <a class="external-link-new-window" href="http://www.businesseventsthailand.com/" title="Opens external link in new window">
               latest updates about TCEB
              </a>
              and stay informed about MICE activities in Thailand via our
              <a class="external-link-new-window" href="http://www.businesseventsthailand.com/news-download/our-social-networks/" title="Opens external link in new window">
               social media
              </a>
              . Get started now  see how TCEB can help
              <a class="external-link-new-window" href="http://www.businesseventsthailand.com/plan-your-events/find-your-venue/convention-and-exhibition-centers/search/" title="Opens external link in new window">
               plan your event
              </a>
              and assure you find the perfect destination, ideal venue and reliable support services you need for a successful, profile-boosting MICE event.
             </p>
            </div>
