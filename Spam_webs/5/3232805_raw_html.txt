         <div class="article-body" itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           It's long been rumored that red cars get stopped the most by police. Although that's proved to be an
           <a href="http://www.snopes.com/autos/law/redcars.asp">
            urban legend
           </a>
           , it turns out the vehicle you're in may play a part in how often you're pulled over, according to a study from
           <a href="http://Insurance.com">
            Insurance.com
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           The car insurance comparison-shopping website looked at data from 526 models and more than 557,000 recent customers to determine which vehicles received the most traffic violations and the Subaru WRX sped to the top of the list. The study found a whopping one in three drivers of the turbocharged sport-compact has a recent traffic violation, ranking it the most-ticketed car overall.
          </p>
          <p>
           Cars dont get tickets, drivers do  but those drivers like the WRX,
           <a href="http://Insurance.com">
            Insurance.com
           </a>
           Managing Editor Des Toups noted in a statement about the study.
          </p>
