            <h1 class="arw-postheader">
             <span class="arw-postheadericon">
              Are you looking for some theme ideas for your next event?
             </span>
            </h1>
            <div class="arw-postheadericons arw-metadata-icons">
             <span class="arw-postdateicon">
              <span class="date">
               Published
              </span>
              <span class="entry-date" title="2:14 am">
               February 12, 2014
              </span>
             </span>
             |
             <span class="arw-postauthoricon">
              <span class="author">
               By
              </span>
              <span class="author vcard">
               <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.stepandrepeatla.com/author/jewel/" title="View all posts by Jewel">
                Jewel
               </a>
              </span>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div class="arw-postcontent">
             <!-- article-content -->
             <p>
              So it is up to you and your team to come up with the next great idea for your upcoming event.
             </p>
             <p>
              Coming up with fresh ideas for an event theme can sometimes be a challenge.
             </p>
             <p>
              I thought maybe I could help prime the pumps in the idea factory
             </p>
             <p>
              One of my favorite techniques is to mix two ideas together and see where it leads. This technique can sometimes create some pretty wacky concepts, but if you keep at it some great ideas can come out of it..
             </p>
             <p>
              So here we go
             </p>
             <p>
              Randomly pick any two items from this list and put them together
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               VALENTINES
              </li>
              <li>
               NASCAR
              </li>
              <li>
               HOBBITS
              </li>
              <li>
               WIZARDS
              </li>
              <li>
               LATIN MUSIC
              </li>
              <li>
               PROTEST MARCH
              </li>
              <li>
               MEXICAN FIESTA
              </li>
              <li>
               MEDIEVAL
              </li>
              <li>
               MONTE CARLO
              </li>
              <li>
               SPEAKEASY
              </li>
              <li>
               SUPER BOWL
              </li>
              <li>
               DANCING
              </li>
              <li>
               COFFEE
              </li>
              <li>
               BROOKLYN
              </li>
              <li>
               BEVERLY HILLS
              </li>
              <li>
               TWENTIES
              </li>
              <li>
               PICNIC
              </li>
              <li>
               HIP HOP
              </li>
              <li>
               CERAMICS
              </li>
              <li>
               LAS VEGAS  CASINO
              </li>
              <li>
               FUTURISTIC
              </li>
              <li>
               CHICAGO
              </li>
              <li>
               CRUISE SHIP
              </li>
              <li>
               PUDDING
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p>
              So you can see that a wizard pudding may not be a great theme idea
             </p>
             <p>
              But choosing another pair  futuristic cruise ship could be a lot of fun.
             </p>
             <p>
              If Hip Hop Hobbits isnt your fancy, maybe a Valentines Picnic could be just the thing.
             </p>
             <p>
              You get the idea
             </p>
             <p>
              Use this list or make up your own. Mixing two different ideas can make coming up with the next event theme into a fun event in itself.
             </p>
             <p>
              Once you have found that ideal theme for your event, we can help you with your backdrops and other theme props. Besides, we would love to hear what you came up with! Give us a call!
             </p>
