         Capture the Colour
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">

         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Travel Blogger Photo Contest
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im teaming up with
         <a href="http://www.travelsupermarket.com/">
          TravelSupermarket.com
         </a>
         as one of the judges in their very cool Capture the Colour bloggers photo contest.
        </p>
        <p>
         To participate you have to be a blogger and we are requesting that each blogger submit a blog post with photos from your trips around the world that showcase the following colours:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Red
         </li>
         <li>
          White
         </li>
         <li>
          Blue
         </li>
         <li>
          Green
         </li>
         <li>
          Yellow
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Take a look at the example post I showcase here to get an idea of what to write and what kind of colour scheme youll need for your photos. Be creative and original. The whole photo does not have to be in one tone, but there should be an eye catching element represented by that colour.
        </p>
        <div>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_8347" style="width: 938px">
         <img alt="Red Canoes" class="size-full wp-image-8347" height="673" src="http://blog.kenkaminesky.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Kaminesky-Blog-RED.jpg" title="Kaminesky-Blog-RED" width="928"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          RED  Lake Louise in Banff National Park
         </p>
        </div>
        <h2>
         RED:
        </h2>
        <p>
         As a photographer I tend to get up before the sun rises in many of the places I visit so that I can capture some amazing places that would otherwise be crawling with tourists. This is a perfect example of why I rise so early. The light can be magical and the silence deafening. Try it for yourself sometime!
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_8348" style="width: 938px">
         <img alt="Rockefeller Plaza" class="size-full wp-image-8348" height="673" src="http://blog.kenkaminesky.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Kaminesky-Blog-YELLOW.jpg" title="Kaminesky-Blog-YELLOW" width="928"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          YELLOW  New York City taxis at Rockefeller Plaza
         </p>
        </div>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          YELLOW:
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         One of my all time favourite shots of mine from New York City. This one really captures the energy of the place and the iconic yellow cabs make it oh so New York. New York City is probably my all time favourite city in the world for so many reasons. I never get tired of shooting there and look forward to being back sometime this autumn.
        </p>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_8349" style="width: 938px">
         <img alt="Arlington National Cemetery" class="size-full wp-image-8349" height="673" src="http://blog.kenkaminesky.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Kaminesky-Blog-WHITE.jpg" title="Kaminesky-Blog-WHITE" width="928"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          WHITE  Arlington National Cemetery at Christmas
         </p>
        </div>
        <h2>
         White:
        </h2>
        <p>
         Sometimes travel is fun and exciting, yet at other times it can be somber and rather melancholy. This was a touching moment in my travels and a morning Ill never forget. Being alone amongst countless graves and wreaths was eerie, yet in a way it was also peaceful.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_8350" style="width: 938px">
         <img alt="La Defence in Paris" class="size-full wp-image-8350" height="673" src="http://blog.kenkaminesky.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Kaminesky-Blog-BLUE.jpg" title="Kaminesky-Blog-BLUE" width="928"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          BLUE  La Defence in Paris
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Blue:
        </h2>
        <p>
         La Defense in Paris is a lesser know treasure in the city of light  but one well worth exploring. As a huge fan of both art and architecture I was able to blend those two elements into one fun image. Sacr Bleu indeed!
        </p>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_8351" style="width: 938px">
         <img alt="Aspen trees in Banff National Park" class="size-full wp-image-8351" height="673" src="http://blog.kenkaminesky.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Kaminesky-Blog-GREEN.jpg" title="Kaminesky-Blog-GREEN" width="928"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          GREEN  Aspen trees in Banff National Park
         </p>
        </div>
        <h2>
         Green:
        </h2>
        <p>
         Ahhh Banff! I absolutely adore this Canadian national treasure. If you have not visited Banff National Park in Alberta, Id suggest you add it to the top of your list of must see locations. This part of Canada is the most jaw droppingly beautiful part of the nation in my humble opinion. Its been several years since I was in Alberta and I hope that I get a chance to visit again very soon.
        </p>
        <p>
         Sometimes there is beautystaring you in the face in an unexpected place like these aspen trees that were literally fifty meters away from a parking area. I took a little stroll around and was really thrilled when I came upon a scene so captivating after only a few steps.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>

        <h2 style="text-align: left;">
         Now for some info about the Capture the Colour Contest
        </h2>
        <p>
