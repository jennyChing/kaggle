       <p>
        In order to serve its rapidly expanding population of more than five million, the ArRiyadh Development Authority has commissionedZaha Hadid Architectsto construct the new King Abdullah Financial District (KAFD) Metro Station in its capital city ofRiyadh, Saudi Arabia.
       </p>
       <p>
        With six platforms over four public floors and two levels of underground car parking, the KAFD Metro Station will be integrated within the urban context of the financial district, while responding to the functional requirements for a multimodal transport centre and the districts future vision. The project extends beyond the simple station typology to emphasize the buildings importance as a dynamic, multi-functional public space; not only an intermediate place perceived through quick transitions, but also a dramatic public space for the city.
