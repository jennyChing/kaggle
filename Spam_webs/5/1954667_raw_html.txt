        <h1>
         25 Reasons You're Glad You're Not Going To Burning Man
        </h1>
        <style media="screen" type="text/css">

       <div class="entry-body">
        <p>
         <span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;">
          <div class="image-none">
           <img alt="burning-man-traffic.jpg" height="437" src="http://sfist.com/attachments/SFist_Jay/burning-man-traffic.jpg" width="640"/>
           <br/>
           <i>
            Ugh, right? Photo:
            <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/snype451/3937900025/sizes/o/in/photolist-6ZYLu2-72NaMD-72Nefp-72RTcS-72RXky-72S4zC-769AL7-77g9qF-79BWkU-7gHBkt-amH8DB-aj5fTs-aj2rYH-8BXLtt-8C1SaG-8C14ZC-8BXaZa-8C2jE5-8BYe7n-8C1mff-8BXbUk-8BX2nV-8C2iqm-8BZTHE-8C1tBm-8BXXga-8BXugX-8BXfYg-bBGuDE-bBGuLN-bBGuWY-bBGzru-8zdUSS-8zaNQe-8zaKW2-8zdXA7-8zaLRx-8zdWL7-8zdWsN-8zdWmf-8zaKtc-8zaKac-8zaMqp-8zdW2S-8zaNqz-8zdUGW-8zaJmH-8zaNnT-8zaML2-8zdSZG-8zdYwj/">

        <p>
         It's that time of year when many of your friends are making daily runs to hardware stores and ill-advised clothing stores and REI in preparation for their annual pilgrimage to the playa. Whether you've joined them in the past and just can't hack it this year, or whether you've really, constitutionally, never been able to fathom the whole thing, we stand with you in solidarity this year as non-Burners. We know, it's a terrific, mind-altering party that requires many months of
         <a href="http://www.burningman.com/blackrockcity_yearround/special_events/decompression/">
          decompression
         </a>
         and therapy afterward, and anyone who isn't going is so missing out. Well, whatever. To paraphrase the great Peggy Lee, the reaction of the harder-to-please and more frat-party-averse among us is more likely to be "Is that all there is to Burning Man?"
        </p>
        <p>
         Below, some thoughts to comfort yourself with if you think you're missing out, or to validate your choice of never going.
        </p>
        <p>
         <big>
          <strong>
           1. When seen from the air, it just looks like a giant desert trailer park.
          </strong>
         </big>
         <br/>
         <span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;">
          <div class="image-none">
           <img alt="blackrockcity_michaelholden.jpg" height="426" src="http://sfist.com/attachments/SFist_AndrewD/blackrockcity_michaelholden.jpg" width="640"/>
           <br/>
           <i>
            Black Rock City, circa 2006. Shot by
            <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/michaelholden/238050722/">
             Michael Holden
            </a>
           </i>
          </div>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          <big>
           2. You actually have time to do stuff this month, like go to the beach/park/wine country, instead of sitting in your garage with your campmates constructing pieces of your elaborate theme camp.
          </big>
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image" style="display: inline;">
          <div class="image-none">
           <img alt="roller-disco.jpg" height="480" src="http://sfist.com/attachments/SFist_Jay/roller-disco.jpg" width="640"/>
           <br/>
           <i>
            Photo:
            <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/alison_faith/3908815008/sizes/z/in/photolist-6XpGwd-6XxWDa-6XByub-6ZYLu2-72NaMD-72Nefp-72RTcS-72RXky-72S4zC-7gHBkt-amH8DB-aj5fTs-aj2rYH-8BXLtt-8C1SaG-8C14ZC-8BXaZa-8C2jE5-8BYe7n-8C1mff-8BXbUk-8BX2nV-8C2iqm-8BZTHE-8C1tBm-8BXXga-8BXugX-8BXfYg-bBGuDE-bBGuLN-bBGuWY-bBGzru-8zdUSS-8zaNQe-8zaKW2-8zdXA7-8zaLRx-8zdWL7-8zdWsN-8zdWmf-8zaKtc-8zaKac-8zaMqp-8zdW2S-8zaNqz-8zdUGW-8zaJmH-8zaNnT-8zaML2-8zdSZG-8zdYwj/">
             Alison Faith
            </a>
           </i>
          </div>
         </span>
