          <p>
           <strong>
            Best Women Clothing Brands
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           The cosmetic products and clothing brands knew an incredible ascension during the last decades, many of those positioning as representative for certain categories of clients.
          </p>
          <div class="code-block code-block-2" style="margin:8px 0px;">
           <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
           </script>
           <!-- Neinki Content Ads -->
           <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-4450452389882772" data-ad-format="auto" data-ad-slot="9341477649" style="display:block">
           </ins>
           <script>
            (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
           </script>
          </div>
          <p>
           This is why we have brands for mature men, for teenagers, and brands designed exclusively for women. During the next years, the young consumers will invest more in how they look, even if they will not have more incomes than the ones of the past generations.
          </p>
          <p>
           The millennium generation is formed of people born between 1980 and 2000, with extremely selective consumers, and for those, the big brands will have to invest more in publicity. In the same generation, women are even more selective than men.
          </p>
          <p>
           A study made on 1200 young women on 350 clothing brands revealed which are the most loved brands of clothes. However, this is not a top of the most expensive brands. On the contrary, it is a top of the brands that are attractive simply because the quality of clothes matches the expectations and budgets of clients.
          </p>
          <p>
           1.
           <strong>
            Forever 21
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Forever 21 takes the first place in this top by far. It is the only American retailer that had an immense success in Europe and Middle East. Moreover, the clothes made by this company are not only for the millennium generation, but also for teenagers and women over the first youth. Overall, it is the retailer that can offer elegance without ruining the budgets of consumers.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <div class="wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper">
           <img alt="Best Women Clothing Brands" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1746" height="419" src="http://www.newinki.com/wp-content/uploads/forever211.jpg" width="404"/>
          </div>
          <p>
           2.
           <strong>
            Victorias Secret
           </strong>
           <br/>
           One of the oldest but most established brands in the world remains Victorias Secret. Maybe the original concept of a secret revealed only to people that are really interested kept the interest for the brand alive.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <div class="wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper">
           <img alt="Best Women Clothing Brands" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1747" height="520" src="http://www.newinki.com/wp-content/uploads/vs-angels-victorias-secret-angels-31368855-720-720-e1402182836119.jpg" width="520"/>
          </div>
          <p>
           3.
           <strong>
            The Pink line
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Is another brand from Victorias Secret, which was launched in 2002. It is designed especially for teenage girls, it comes with happy colors, and this is why it is more popular lately even than the mother brand.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <div class="wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper">
           <img alt="Best Women Clothing Brands" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1748" height="673" src="http://www.newinki.com/wp-content/uploads/tumblr_lehxv93mv91qzdzve.jpg" width="500"/>
          </div>
          <p>
           4.
           <strong>
            Sephora
           </strong>
           <br/>
           The famous brand with perfumes and beauty products launched in 1969 is also in this top. The French chain made it big in Eastern Europe and even Asia lately, imposing itself as a classy brand that does not necessarily kills the appetite for buying.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <div class="wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1749" height="261" src="http://www.newinki.com/wp-content/uploads/sephora_9.jpg" width="300"/>
          </div>
          <p>
           5.
           <strong>
            H&amp;M
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Also known as Hennes and Mauritz is also a part of this top. The brand attracts with its good prices, but also with the quality of products. What is special about it is that it is considered as a common brand in Western Europe, while in the East, it is considered a luxury line. Even so, H&amp;M can be considered one of the most successful brands in the world, especially when it comes to womens clothes.
           <br/>
           This top might not be approved by some of women, because they would like other brands in this top. It is a general top made considering the preference of women from all over the world, and we are sure that you all have your preferences. Just because your favorite brand is not in this list does not mean that it is a bad one, so feel free to tell us what your favorite names are when it comes to clothes.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
