        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Coke Marinated Steak Bites
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-01-21T10:55:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 21, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.busymomshelper.com/author/danielle" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Danielle
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.busymomshelper.com/2015/01/coke-marinated-steak-bites.html#comments">
           2 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.busymomshelper.com%2F2015%2F01%2Fcoke-marinated-steak-bites.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.busymomshelper.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2015%2F01%2FCoke-Main.jpg&amp;description=Coke%20Marinated%20Steak%20Bites" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <em>
          This shop has been compensated by Collective Bias, Inc. and its advertiser. All opinions are mine alone.#PrepareToParty #CollectiveBias
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre like us, the
         <a href="http://www.homebowl.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Coke Home bowl">
          Big Game
         </a>
         is all about the food, and lots of it! With friends and family visiting to join in the fun, youll need to be ready. While youre busy preppingto feed all those hungry bellies for your get-together, dont forget these deliciously amazing
         <strong>
          Coke Marinated Steak Bites
         </strong>
         !
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-Main.jpg">
          <img alt="Coke Marinated Steak Bites / by Busy Mom's Helper #PrepareToParty #Ad #steak #BigGame" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-38734" height="905" src="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-Main.jpg" width="590"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I doubt Im the only one who loves a good meat option during the Big Game, especially with a juicy and flavorful marinade! Mix it up with some veggies and youve got a fabulous appetizer to serve up to your guests!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-6.jpg">
          <img alt="Coke Marinated Steak Bites / by Busy Mom's Helper #PrepareToParty #Ad #steak #BigGame" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-38727" height="446" src="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-6.jpg" width="590"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The main thing that really makes this marinade perfect is the
         <a href="http://www.homebowl.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Coke Home Bowl">
          Coca-Cola
         </a>
         . Seriously, it wouldnt be near as tasty without it! So be sure to pick some up at Walmart, and you can easily find it with their Big Game display! Pick up some
         <a href="http://www.ritzcrackers.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Ritz Crackers">
          Ritz Crackers
         </a>
         while youre there to complete your food options!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-11.jpg">
          <img alt="Coke Marinated Steak Bites / by Busy Mom's Helper #PrepareToParty #Ad #steak #BigGame" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-38732" height="999" src="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-11.jpg" width="590"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Ingredients
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          2 lbs. Thin-sliced Steak
         </li>
         <li>
          1 cup Teriyaki Sauce
         </li>
         <li>
          1 cup Coca-Cola
         </li>
         <li>
          2 tbsp. Garlic, minced
         </li>
         <li>
          2 tbsp. Red Wine Vinegar
         </li>
         <li>
          Sliced Potatoes (small red and gold)
         </li>
         <li>
          Other Veggies: green peppers, mushrooms, onions, tomatoes, pineapples
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h2>
         Directions
        </h2>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Preheat oven to 350 Degees F. Mix the teriyaki sauce, Coke, garlic and red wine vinegar together. Be sure you measure the Coke after the bubbles settle, or you want have a full cup.
          <a href="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke.jpg">
           <img alt="Coke Marinated Steak Bites / by Busy Mom's Helper #PrepareToParty #Ad #steak #BigGame" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-38735" height="433" src="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke.jpg" width="590"/>
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          In a large baking dish, spread a layer of the thin steak slices, covering with a bit of marinade.
          <a href="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-2.jpg">
           <img alt="Coke Marinated Steak Bites / by Busy Mom's Helper #PrepareToParty #Ad #steak #BigGame" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-38723" height="393" src="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-2.jpg" width="590"/>
          </a>
          Add the rest of the steak slices, cover with more marinade, then cover with foil.
         </li>
         <li>
          Slice your potatoes and lay on a cookie sheet covered in foil. Drizzle marinade over them.
          <a href="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-3.jpg">
           <img alt="Coke Marinated Steak Bites / by Busy Mom's Helper #PrepareToParty #Ad #steak #BigGame" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-38724" height="397" src="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-3.jpg" width="590"/>
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          Bake the meat for about 15-30 minutes (or until cooked through) and the potatoes about 25-45 minutes, or until they reach desired softness.
         </li>
         <li>
          Use toothpicks to stick strips of steak, potato slices and other veggies together. You can even add flags to the top of the toothpicks with your teams colors!
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-5.jpg">
          <img alt="Coke Marinated Steak Bites / by Busy Mom's Helper #PrepareToParty #Ad #steak #BigGame" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-38726" height="452" src="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-5.jpg" width="590"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Super fun, right?! We gobbled these up so fast, we had to whip up some more really quickwhich was nice and easy to pull off!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-9.jpg">
          <img alt="Coke Marinated Steak Bites / by Busy Mom's Helper #PrepareToParty #Ad #steak #BigGame" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-38730" height="531" src="http://www.busymomshelper.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Coke-9.jpg" width="590"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         What are YOUR favorite Big Game foods?

    } else {
        var index = -1;

        try {

            if (parts[1]==undefined){
