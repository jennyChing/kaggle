         <em>
          An easy 4-ingredient recipe for healthy snacks packed with protein. They taste like fudgy bites of pure dark chocolate!
         </em>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1690-labeled.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">

        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         While visiting my parents for the holidays, we drove past my old high school on our way back from our favorite
         <a href="http://instagram.com/p/xDgQ1TGSXh/" target="_blank">
          brunch restaurant
         </a>
         . As the traffic lights changed, my mom turned around and asked my brother and I in the backseat, Do you have mostly happy memories from high school?
         <strong>
          <span id="more-12078">
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         My brother paused, but I immediately answered yes, definitely! Despite the long days (from 7 am until 3 pm) and at least 4 hours of homework every night, I rarely hated it. Classes lasted less than an hour; I spent time with my band friends before school, during orchestra, and at lunch; and my electives of French and photography were a breeze.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1778.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Dark Chocolate Strawberry Energy Bites -- these 4-ingredient snacks taste like fudgy brownies! Barely 100 calories &amp; 10+ grams of protein!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12084" height="675" src="http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1778.jpg" width="450"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://amyshealthybaking.com/blog/2015/01/07/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites/&amp;media=http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1778.jpg&amp;description=Dark Chocolate Strawberry Energy Bites')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Besides We had snack time! The school tacked on an extra 15 minutes to the start of 3
         <sup>
          rd
         </sup>
         period, originally intended for the video production class to present a daily bulletin, but almost all of the teachers allowed us to eat during that segment. I always looked forward to that!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Nearly every day, I found a granola bar in my brown bag lunch, usually a toss-up between
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B005ER15G2/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B005ER15G2&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=amysheabak-20&amp;linkId=JULKAACYKTKUS6K4" target="_blank">
          Quakers chewy bars
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00IJNZ5Q4/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00IJNZ5Q4&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=amysheabak-20&amp;linkId=OBQLS6CHPOGA5AXD" target="_blank">
          Nutri-Grain fruity ones
         </a>
         . Although my friends normally plowed through their snacks at light speed, I slowly nibbled on mine to make them last the full 15 minutes. It was one of my favorite routines for all 4 years of high school!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1763.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Dark Chocolate Strawberry Energy Bites -- these 4-ingredient snacks taste like fudgy brownies! Barely 100 calories &amp; 10+ grams of protein!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12083" height="675" src="http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1763.jpg" width="450"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://amyshealthybaking.com/blog/2015/01/07/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites/&amp;media=http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1763.jpg&amp;description=Dark Chocolate Strawberry Energy Bites')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now that I work from home,
         <em>
          any
         </em>
         time is snack time, which can be a little dangerous with so many
         <a href="http://amyshealthybaking.com/blog/2015/01/03/apple-pie-oatmeal-cookies/" target="_blank" title="Apple Pie Oatmeal Cookies">
          cookies
         </a>
         lying around! So to start the New Year off on the right foot, I mixed up a batch of these
         <span style="color: #dc143c;">
          <strong>
           Dark Chocolate Strawberry Energy Bites
          </strong>
         </span>
         . Theyre so rich that they practically taste like little balls of pure dark chocolate!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         This simple recipe contains only
         <span style="color: #dc143c;">
          <strong>
           4 ingredients
          </strong>
         </span>
         , most of which you probably stash in your pantry already. As opposed to other energy bite recipes, these are actually packed with protein and held together with pure fruit, not peanut or almond butter. So yes, theyre nut-free!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1741.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Dark Chocolate Strawberry Energy Bites -- these 4-ingredient snacks taste like fudgy brownies! Barely 100 calories &amp; 10+ grams of protein!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12082" height="675" src="http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1741.jpg" width="450"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://amyshealthybaking.com/blog/2015/01/07/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites/&amp;media=http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1741.jpg&amp;description=Dark Chocolate Strawberry Energy Bites')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The base starts with a touch of oats, a generous amount of cocoa powder, and a lot of protein powder. I prefer
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008NCUSMW/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B008NCUSMW&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=amysheabak-20&amp;linkId=QEZNNZDK4C6OL3EO" target="_blank">
          Plan Fusion Vanilla Bean
         </a>
         (no, this isnt a sponsored postI just really, really love it!). Its gluten-free and vegan, and it doesnt have any strange flavors. If youre sensitive to the taste of whey like me, I guarantee youll like this better!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #6a5acd;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Note
          </span>
          :
         </span>
         Not all protein powders absorb liquids equally. Ive only tested these energy bites with Plan Fusion; if you use a different protein powder, the texture may vary.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         I used
         <span style="color: #dc143c;">
          <strong>
           frozen strawberries
          </strong>
         </span>
         as the glue to hold together the dry ingredients. Just thaw them in the microwave, drain off any liquid, and pure them. You want to make the pure as smooth as possible because that will ensure that the bites are moist and fudgy. No big chunks please!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #6a5acd;">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Note
          </span>
          :
         </span>
         The strawberries old hold together the bites. You cant really taste them because the chocolate flavor is so rich.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1690.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Dark Chocolate Strawberry Energy Bites -- these 4-ingredient snacks taste like fudgy brownies! Barely 100 calories &amp; 10+ grams of protein!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12080" height="675" src="http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1690.jpg" width="450"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://amyshealthybaking.com/blog/2015/01/07/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites/&amp;media=http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1690.jpg&amp;description=Dark Chocolate Strawberry Energy Bites')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Oh my goodness, you guys I snuck one of these
         <span style="color: #dc143c;">
          <strong>
           Dark Chocolate Strawberry Energy Bites
          </strong>
         </span>
         nearly every time I walked into the kitchen! Which was a lot more frequently than I care to admit. They didnt last more than a day or two Especially since they tasted so rich and fudgy!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://amyshealthybaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dark-chocolate-strawberry-energy-bites_1713.jpg">

          Dark Chocolate Strawberry Energy Bites

          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">

         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          These protein-packed energy bites taste like fudgy spheres of pure dark chocolate! Theyll last for at least a week if stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSServes">
           Yields:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            30 energy bites
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 scoops (60g)
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008NCUSMW/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B008NCUSMW&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=amysheabak-20&amp;linkId=QEZNNZDK4C6OL3EO" target="_blank">
             Plant Fusion Vanilla Bean protein powder
            </a>
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             c (60g) unsweetened cocoa powder (
            <a href="http://amyshealthybaking.com/blog/2013/09/17/baking-basics-101/" target="_blank" title="Baking Basics 101">
             measured correctly
            </a>
            )
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             c (50g) old-fashioned oats (
            <a href="http://amyshealthybaking.com/blog/2013/09/17/baking-basics-101/" target="_blank" title="Baking Basics 101">
             measured correctly
            </a>
            and
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002TXT502/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B002TXT502&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=amysheabak-20&amp;linkId=ZLONPAOTRF4HNDB4" target="_blank">
             gluten-free
            </a>
            if needed)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1  c (210g) frozen unsweetened strawberries, thawed and drained
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a large bowl, mix together the protein powder, cocoa powder, and oats.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Pure the strawberries until completely smooth. Add the pure to the cocoa mixture, and stir until all of the dry ingredients have been incorporated. (If necessary, stir in 1 teaspoon of water at a time if the mixture is too dry to hold together.) Shape into 30 small balls. Place in an airtight container, and refrigerate until ready to eat.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSNotesDiv">
          <div class="ERSNotesHeader">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSNotes">
           <strong>
            Notes:
           </strong>
           I have only tested this recipe with
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008NCUSMW/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B008NCUSMW&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=amysheabak-20&amp;linkId=QEZNNZDK4C6OL3EO" target="_blank">
            Plant Fusion protein powder
           </a>
           . Other protein powders may absorb liquid differently. If your mixture is too wet, try chilling for an hour, or add an additional 1 teaspoon of protein powder at a time until the mixture is fudgy enough to shape.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The strawberries merely act as the glue to hold together the dry ingredients. You cant actually taste them.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           These are meant to taste very dark, similar to 72% dark chocolate. If you prefer a sweeter taste, add your preferred granulated (or solid) sweetener to taste.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <em>
            {gluten-free, vegan, clean eating, low fat, low calorie, high protein}
           </em>
