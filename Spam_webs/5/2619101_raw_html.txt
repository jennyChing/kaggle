       <div class="txt step-body">
        1/2 c. butter, melted
        <br/>
        1/2 c. applesauce (you can use 1 pack of those single serving applesauce containers)
        <br/>
        1 Tbsp. vanilla extract
        <br/>
        1 1/2 c. brown sugar
        <br/>
        1 1/2 c. white sugar
        <br/>
        3 eggs
        <br/>
        1 1/2 c. flour
        <br/>
        1 c. unsweetened cocoa powder
        <br/>
        1 tsp. salt
        <br/>
        <br/>
        1 c. of your choice of mix-ins (mini marshmallows, chopped candy bars (Snickers, York peppermint patties, Reese's peanut butter cups, etc.), chopped nuts, dried fruit, chopped caramels, etc.)
        <strong>
         OR
        </strong>
        you can just use 1 c. semisweet chocolate chips for plain chocolate brownies
        <strong>
         OR
        </strong>
        for cheesecake brownies follow directions through pouring batter into pan (using 1 c. semisweet chips as mix-in) then beat together 1 package softened cream cheese (8 oz.), 1 egg, 1/2 tsp. vanilla, and 1/2 c. sugar. Swirl over top of brownies and bake as instructed in recipe.
