       <p>
        I was determined to stay awake on my recent flight from Amsterdam to Seattle. Ive flown this route many times before but this was the first time that I had my camera right next to me, in the hope of getting some good shots of the icy lands of Greenland and northeastern Canada. The weather was great, the sky was clear and my decision to stay up was rewarded. The flight route took us past Iceland, Greenland and across to
        <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=Baffin+Island,+Nunavut,+Canada&amp;hl=en&amp;ll=66.231457,-79.804687&amp;spn=27.635244,158.027344&amp;sll=37.0625,-95.677068&amp;sspn=26.316024,79.013672&amp;z=3" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://maps.google.com/maps?q=Baffin+Island,+Nunavut,+Canada&amp;hl=en&amp;ll=66.231457,-79.804687&amp;spn=27.635244,158.027344&amp;sll=37.0625,-95.677068&amp;sspn=26.316024,79.013672&amp;z=3', 'Baffin Island']);" target="_blank">
         Baffin Island
        </a>
        in Canada. The aerial views of the mountains, ice caps and frozen shores of Baffin Island were simply stunning!
