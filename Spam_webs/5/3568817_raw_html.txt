         <h1>
          Stop Me If Youve Heard This One
         </h1>
         <p class="post-meta">
          Jan 25, 2010 |  |
          <span class="comments-number">
           <a href="http://hijinksensue.com/comic/stop-me-if-youve-heard-this-one/#respond">
            51 comments
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            Ill be at
            <a href="http://www.scifiexpo.com/DCC/dallascomiccon.html" target="_blank" title="http://www.scifiexpo.com/DCC/dallascomiccon.html">
             <strong>
              Dallas Comic Con
             </strong>
            </a>
            this weekend. Come say hi!
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.scifiexpo.com/DCC/dallascomiccon.html" target="_blank">
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1266" height="66" src="http://hijinksensue.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/01/dallascomiccon.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" title="dallascomiccon" width="250"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://store.hijinksensue.com/product/team-edward-james-olmos-t-shirt" target="_blank">
            <img alt="" class="alignleft" height="150" src="http://www.hijinksensue.com/assets/verts/store/hijinks-ensue-edward-shirt-250.png" style="margin: 5px;" title="Team Edward Shirt - Twilight Edward James Olmost Shirt" width="150"/>
           </a>
           I wrote this comic after viewing the
           <em>
            <strong>
             Star Trek TNG
            </strong>
           </em>
           episode 
           <a href="http://memory-alpha.org/en/wiki/The_Icarus_Factor_%28episode%29" target="_blank" title="http://memory-alpha.org/en/wiki/The_Icarus_Factor_%28episode%29">
            <strong>
             The Icarus Factor
            </strong>
           </a>
           . In it,
           <strong>
            Wesley Crusher
           </strong>
           throws Worf a surprise Klingon birthday party on the Holodeck, complete with a Painstick Pinnata. Also Riker worked out his daddy issues by beating the crap out of his old man American Gladiators style. What does this have to do with anything? Nothing. I just like making comics about
           <em>
            <strong>
             Star Trek
            </strong>
           </em>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           The actual comic inspiration came from the fact that Wesley clearly didnt realize that everyone on the ship was punking him when they encouraged him to sneak around and try to plan
           <strong>
            Worfs KPlahmitzvah
           </strong>
           .
           <span id="more-1265">
           </span>
           <strong>
            Geordi and Data
           </strong>
           encouraged Wes to pry deeper into Worfs personal bid-ness despite his obvious increased aggression and agitation. No doubt they were hoping that Worf would remove the young Ensigns spine through his nipples. Genius he may have been, Wesley just didnt fit in. He was clearly too young and naive to be gallivanting through the galaxy on life and death missions with cynical space-adults that clearly didnt have his best interests in mind.
          </p>
          <p>
           The best line of the episode went to Papa Riker who pleaded with his emotionally blockaded son to 
           <strong>
            Let your shields down, Wil
           </strong>
           . Those are metaphorical shields hes talking about. Heart shields. The idea of using 24th century metaphors in family arguments lead to these tweets:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <a href="http://twitter.com/hijinksensue/status/8060467076" target="_blank" title="http://twitter.com/hijinksensue/status/8060467076">
             Lower your shields son! Let my emotional away team beam down to your hearts surface!
            </a>
           </li>
           <li>
            <a href="http://twitter.com/hijinksensue/status/8060481741" target="_blank" title="http://twitter.com/hijinksensue/status/8060481741">
             You were always my commanding officer Dad, when you should have been my ships counselor!
            </a>
           </li>
           <li>
            <a href="http://twitter.com/hijinksensue/status/8060534381" target="_blank" title="http://twitter.com/hijinksensue/status/8060534381">
             Im hailing you on all frequencies, Dad, but your communication systems have been malfunctioning since Mom left!
            </a>
           </li>
           <li>
            <a href="http://twitter.com/hijinksensue/status/8060626225" target="_blank" title="http://twitter.com/hijinksensue/status/8060626225">
             YOU CANT SOLVE EVERYTHING WITH AN INVERSE TACHYON PULSE, DAD!!!
            </a>
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            <strong>
             So, whats the best line of shitty space-soap opera dialog you can come up with?
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            <span style="color: #000000;">
