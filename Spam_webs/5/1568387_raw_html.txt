         <h1 class="entry-title post-title">
          NASA parodies Meghan Trainor with All About That Space
         </h1>
         <article>
          <div class="st-format-standard-holder">
           <img alt="nic_print3600x2993ppi" class="size-original featured-image wp-post-image" data-hidpi="http://www.shinyshiny.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/nic_print3600x2993ppi.png" height="565" src="//www.shinyshiny.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/nic_print3600x2993ppi.png" width="679"/>
          </div>
          <div id="article">
           <div addthis:title="NASA parodies Meghan Trainor with All About That Space " addthis:url="http://www.shinyshiny.tv/2014/12/nasa-parodies-meghan-trainor-space.html" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style addthis_32x32_style">
            <a class="addthis_button_preferred_1">
            </a>
            <a class="addthis_button_preferred_2">
            </a>
            <a class="addthis_button_preferred_3">
            </a>
            <a class="addthis_button_preferred_4">
            </a>
            <a class="addthis_button_compact">
            </a>
            <a class="addthis_counter addthis_bubble_style">
            </a>
           </div>
           <p>
            Everyone loves the catchy beat of Meghan Trainors smash hit All About That Bass, but not everyones been
            <a href="http://www.slate.com/blogs/browbeat/2014/09/05/meghan_trainor_s_new_song_title_is_just_as_anti_feminist_as_all_about_that.html">
             quite as keen on the lyrics
            </a>
            . Luckily, NASAs here to save us with their science-themed parody, All About That Space, and we love it.
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/kHy08ZPav88" width="560">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <p>
            Yes, some of the dancings a bit dodgy and yes, it goes on too long, but we think its a lot more positive than Trainors lambasting of skinny bitches and tracing everything back to dudes opinions of her backside.
            <a href="http://www.shinyshiny.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/nic_print3600x2993ppi.png">
             <br/>
            </a>
            Yay, science!
           </p>
