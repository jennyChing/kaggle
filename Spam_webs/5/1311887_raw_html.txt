        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          <strong style="font-size: 110%;">
           Wife Swap: Caddel vs. Lawrence
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="font-size: 90%;">
          Below you will find the episode of the television show,
          <em>
           Wife Swap
          </em>
          , that our family was featured on. We filmed this in 2006 and it originally aired in January of 2007. When we were recruited for the show, the producers were interested in many aspects of our life, so we were uncertain what the focus of the program would be when we got to the filming stage. As we quickly learned, the centralization seemed to be directed toward our differences in faith and beliefs. It also touches on our dissimilarities in parenting styles, lifestyles, and appearances. It was an interesting experience and we surely learned how skilled television editors can be; as the 200+ hours of film footage between the two homes were chopped down to 44 minutes of show and certainly relied on the dramatic moments to highlight what happened within the two week timeframe. But alas, that is the "reality" of reality tv, eh? I hope you enjoy the show and have a few laughs throughout. Thanks for your interest!
         </span>
        </p>
