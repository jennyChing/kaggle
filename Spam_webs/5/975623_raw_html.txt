          <h1 class="entry-title">
           Chocolate Chip Muffin Recipe
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <div class="meta-info-wrap">
           <span class="sep posted-on">
            Posted on
           </span>
           <a href="http://theknitwitbyshair.com/2014/04/chocolate-chip-muffin-recipe.html" rel="bookmark" title="11:26 am">
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-04-02T11:26:58+00:00">
             April 2, 2014
            </time>
           </a>
           <span class="by-author">
            <span class="sep">
             by
            </span>
            <span class="author vcard">
             <a class="url fn n" href="http://theknitwitbyshair.com/author/shairbearg" rel="author" title="View all posts by KnitWitShair">
              KnitWitShair
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>

            April 2, 2014
           </span>
          </div>
          <!-- .entry-meta-icons -->
         </div>
         <!-- .entry-meta -->
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content cf">
         <p>
          <a href="http://theknitwitbyshair.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Chocolate-Chip-Muffin-Recipe.jpg">
           <img alt="Easy Muffin Recipe" class="size-full wp-image-13412 aligncenter" height="516" src="http://theknitwitbyshair.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Chocolate-Chip-Muffin-Recipe.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
          I had my wisdom teeth out recently, and one side was very bad and my surgeon told me to be very careful what to eat for 3 weeks, and even 3 months I cant eat anything too hard. So I one of the days I was really sick of the same old mush, oatmeal, and yogurt. One of my friends posted that she was making some muffins. Before she sent me the link to the recipe (
          <a href="http://www.canadianmomscook.com/2012/05/aunties-amazing-banana-muffins.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.canadianmomscook.com/2012/05/aunties-amazing-banana-muffins.html', 'Aunties Amazing Banana Muffins Recipe)');" target="_blank">
           Aunties Amazing Banana Muffins Recipe)
          </a>
          I looked up some chocolate chip muffin recipes myself.
         </p>
         <p>
          I found this recipe Chocolate Chip Muffin Recipe on
          <a href="http://allrecipes.com/Recipe-Tools/Print/Recipe.aspx?recipeID=7906&amp;origin=detail&amp;servings=12&amp;metric=false" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://allrecipes.com/Recipe-Tools/Print/Recipe.aspx?recipeID=7906&amp;origin=detail&amp;servings=12&amp;metric=false', 'AllRecipes.com.');" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           AllRecipes.com.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          There arent a lot of pictures of this recipe because I was working in adverse conditions! LOL Not only am I still in some pain, my kitchen is all ripped apartmy counter is a card table my friend
          <a href="http://www.bewilderedbug.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bewilderedbug.com/', 'Bewildered Bug');" target="_blank">
           Bewildered Bug
          </a>
          gave me before she moved to the UK LOL
         </p>
         <p>
          So on with the recipe!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://theknitwitbyshair.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_1336.jpg">
           <img alt="Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13413" height="404" src="http://theknitwitbyshair.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/IMG_1336.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          2 cups all-purpose flour
          <br/>
          1/2 cup white sugar
          <br/>
          3 teaspoons baking powder
          <br/>
          1/2 teaspoon salt
          <br/>
          3/4 cup milk
          <br/>
          1/3 cup vegetable oil
          <br/>
          1 egg
          <br/>
          3/4 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
          <br/>
          3 tablespoons white sugar
          <br/>
          2 tablespoons brown sugar
         </p>
         <h1>
          Directions:
         </h1>
         <p>
          1. Preheat oven to 400. Place baking cups in muffin tin, or grease just the bottoms.
          <br/>
          2. In a medium bowl, combine all dry ingredients. Mix well.
          <br/>
          3. In a large bowl, put in all wet ingredients; blend well.
          <br/>
          4. Dump all dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.
          <br/>
          5. Mix together until just moistened.
          <br/>
          6. Spoon batter into the tins, about 2/3rds full.
          <br/>
          7. Sprinkle tops of muffins with the sugar and brown sugar mixture, although I will probably skip this next time. I found it to be a bit too sweet.
          <br/>
          8. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
          <br/>
          9. Cool 1 minute before removing from pan. Serve warm.
         </p>
