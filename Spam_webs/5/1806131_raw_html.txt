           <p>
            If you are looking for some great fitness apps this season, to keep those diet and fitness goals on track before the holiday craze and binge eating sets in, here are a few mobile apps to try to keep your fitness goals in check.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             CODY
            </strong>
            : This is a new app with a funny name. The Cody workout app offers you a way to connect to an active fitness community where they work out together, discover brand new fitness routines, and engage in beneficial sharing sessions. Friends can like or comment on the workouts they do and it keeps you motivated to reach your fitness goals. In addition, users can track progress on a timeline. It also features high-quality stats and highlights from previous workouts. It is free and available on iOS platform.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             FITSTAR
            </strong>
            : As its name implies, Fitstar features a fit star in the NFL, Tony Gonzalez, who leads you through a complete fitness workout routine and serves as your personal trainer. The app utilizes high-quality videos, showing shots with different angles that feature highly customizable workouts that help users reach their fitness goals. It also calculates your progress as you go along, and revs up your workout to help you meet new and exciting challenges as you reach each level. This gamification of the fitness process adds to the excitement, and even makes it fun. Available for iOS.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             PACT
            </strong>
            : This app, which is formerly known as GymPact has users pledge a set number of days they will work out with a set amount of cash. Users pay for each day they miss so they lose money if they miss too many. This app uses the value of negative reinforcement (a basic human psychology principle), which occurs when the desired behavior is not met. The app now features a motion tracker, and also allows users to track winnings and connect with other tracking apps. There are some other added features in this new edition, including the option to commit to eating healthy or pay up. Free to iOS and Android smart device users.
           </p>
           <p>
            These are just 3 of the many great fitness apps available for your mobile devices. Try these iOS apps and Android workout apps and see if they can help you move closer to your fitness goals this holiday season, and beyond!
