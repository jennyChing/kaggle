      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        Turkish Coffee Set Design by Esli Alovi from Israel
       </p>
       <p>
        This coffee set was designed by Esli Alovi for DesignBooms
        <a href="http://www.designboom.com/contest/winner.php?contest_pk=21" target="_blank">
         Dining in 2015 Design Contest
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Turkish Coffee Set Design" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-359" height="635" src="http://www.turkishcoffee.us/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/turkish-coffee-set-design-1.jpg" title="Turkish Coffee Set Design" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Turkish Coffee Set Design" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-360" height="700" src="http://www.turkishcoffee.us/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/turkish-coffee-set-design-2.jpg" title="Turkish Coffee Set Design" width="579"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Turkish Coffee Set Design" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-361" height="384" src="http://www.turkishcoffee.us/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/turkish-coffee-set-design-3.jpg" title="Turkish Coffee Set Design" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        This is one of the coolest designs weve ever seen.  Usually, a
        <a href="http://www.turkishcoffee.us/products/turkish-coffee-sets/">
         Turkish Coffee Set
        </a>
        has a traditional design. But this modern design takes to a totally new level.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Esli Alovis own words about the design:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        A modern interpretation for a turkish coffee set which still maintains its traditional values. the tray integrates everything required for the preparation of the coffee, from cooking to serving, all in the dining area. the materials used are copper and ceramic.
       </p>
