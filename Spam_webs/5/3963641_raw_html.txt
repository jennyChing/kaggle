       <h1 class="entry-title single-title">
        <span>
         Night Time Routine
        </span>
       </h1>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-media">
      </div>
      <div class="wrapper">
       <div class="grids">
        <div class="grid-8">
         <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content">
          <p>
           Ever wish you could wake up without having messy bed head hair?Waking up with your style maintained andsinging I woke up like this like Beyonce? According to Afro Hair Specialist, Nita from The House of Rush, wrapping the hair is a great way to protect it and your style whilst sleeping at night and should be part of your night time routine.
          </p>
          <p>
           Nita shared 5 simple steps of how to wrap your hair overnight.
          </p>
          <p>
           Things you will need:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Silk head scarf
           </li>
           <li>
            Kirby grips
           </li>
           <li>
            Small brush (bristle) / comb
           </li>
           <li>
            Mizani Gloss Veil Shine Spray
           </li>
           <li>
            Mizani Shine Extend Spritz
           </li>
           <li>
            Mizani Supreme Oil
           </li>
          </ul>
          <h3>
           Step 1:
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://shoprush.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/image101.jpg">
            <img alt="image101" class=" size-medium wp-image-1074 aligncenter" height="300" src="http://shoprush.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/image101-225x300.jpg" width="225"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Leaving your hair in the styled parting, spray a little of
           <a href="http://shoprush.com/mizani-thermasmooth-shine-extend-anti-humidity-spritz-100ml.html">
            Mizani Thermasmooth Shine Extend Anti-Humidity Spritz
           </a>
           and comb the hair forward, wrapping it round the head.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Step 2:
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://shoprush.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/image202.jpg">
            <img alt="image202" class=" size-medium wp-image-1078 aligncenter" height="300" src="http://shoprush.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/image202-225x300.jpg" width="225"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Secure any loose or short hair with kirby grips. Nita shared her expert tip of positioning the grips so that you are able to easily remove them once you have tied the scarf to avoid leaving an indent.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Step 3:
           <a href="http://shoprush.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/image404.jpg">
            <img alt="image404" class=" size-medium wp-image-1081 aligncenter" height="300" src="http://shoprush.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/image404-225x300.jpg" width="225"/>
           </a>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Once the hair is neatly wrapped round the head, finish off with
           <a href="http://shoprush.com/mizani-gloss-veil-shine-spray-250ml.html">
            Mizani Gloss Veil Shine Spray
           </a>
           for extra shine ensuring that you do not overload with product.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Step 4:
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://shoprush.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/head-trio.jpg">
            <img alt="head trio" class="aligncenter wp-image-1084 size-large" height="173" src="http://shoprush.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/head-trio-1024x256.jpg" width="690"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Fold your silk scarf in half and place it round the head and firmly double knot the scarf. Gently take out the kirby grips making sure the scarf is still tight around the head.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Step 5:
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://shoprush.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/PicMonkey-Collage.jpg">
            <img alt="PicMonkey Collage" class="aligncenter wp-image-1086 size-large" height="345" src="http://shoprush.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/PicMonkey-Collage-1024x512.jpg" width="690"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           After removing the scarf in the morning add a pea size amount of
           <a href="http://shoprush.com/mizani-supreme-oil-122ml.html">
            Mizani Supreme Oil
           </a>
           to the hair (mainly lengths and ends) and gently brush out to your desired style, if you prefer a thicker product use
           <a href="http://shoprush.com/mizani-spradiance-high-gloss-serum-148ml.html">
            Mizani Spraidance High Gloss Serum
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Although this process is slightly time consuming and you may need to give it a couple of tries for you to get the hang of it, wrapping the hair is perfect for those that have Afro-Caribbean, frizzy and tight curly hair to keep your hair moisturised and speed up the styling process in the morning. Keep your eyes peeled for Rush Shop offers that includes a FREE Mizani silk night scarf!
          </p>
          <p>
           Pros:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Protects hair during sleeping preventing any friction on the hair.
           </li>
           <li>
            Keeps the moisture in the hair
           </li>
           <li>
            Easy and quick styling in the morning
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           Have you tried this at home? Tell us what you think and how it transformed your hair!
          </p>
