           <h2 style="text-align: left;">
            Moving Critical Supplies to Cut-Off Places
           </h2>
           <div class="clearfix content-separator" style="margin-bottom:30px">
           </div>
           <p>
            Solar Ship Inc. builds aircraft to service remoteareas.Most of these areas haveno roads.
           </p>
           <p>
            Thesolarship is a hybrid aircraft, gaining lift from both buoyant gas and aerodynamics.
           </p>
           <p>
            Itswing-shipdesign allowsfor extreme short take-off andlanding (XSTOL), such as in asoccer field.
           </p>
           <p>
            Its designalso providesa large surface area for solar electric power, allowing long, self-sufficient range.
           </p>
           <p>
            The solarship is a new mode of transportationthat does not depend on fossil fuels, roads,or runways.
           </p>
