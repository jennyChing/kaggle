       <div class="section">
        <h3>
         Here's the Dish
        </h3>
        <div class="entry">
         <p>
          IabsolutelyLOVE eating my veggies this way! The parmesan cheese adds thesaltiness and the honey makes them slightly sweet. Talk about the perfect combination of flavors! I can almost gaurantee that you can get even the pickiest of eaters to not only
          <em>
           eat
          </em>
          but to
          <em>
           love
          </em>
          their veggies! If youve never tried brussels sprouts (or have tried and didnt like them), I promise this is going to make you a fan of this green powerhouse veggie!
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="section">
        <h3>
         Method
        </h3>
        <p class="cook_time">
         The estimated total time to make this recipe is
         <span>
          20-25 minutes
         </span>
         .
        </p>
        <div class="step clearfix">
         <span class="num">
          1
         </span>
         <div class="text entry">
          <p>
           Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Line a sheet pan with foil and spray with non-stick cooking spray. (I used a cooling rack to get brussels sprouts evenly cooked on every side, and sprayed this with cooking spray as well).
           <span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 19px;">
            Microwave brussels sprouts in bag (or in bowl covered with saran wrap, pierced with a knife to let steam out) for 5 minutes.
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
         <img alt="IMG_6334" class="attachment-medium" height="825" src="https://dashingdish.com/files/2013/03/IMG_6334.jpg" title="" width="550"/>
        </div>
        <div class="step clearfix">
         <span class="num">
          2
         </span>
         <div class="text entry">
          <p>
           Place brussels sprouts on prepared pan and spray with cooking spray, (this helps seasonings to stick). Season with salt, pepper, and garlic and onion powder. Divide parmesan cheese evenly over brussels sprouts.
          </p>
         </div>
         <img alt="IMG_6336" class="attachment-medium" height="825" src="https://dashingdish.com/files/2013/03/IMG_6336.jpg" title="" width="550"/>
        </div>
        <div class="step clearfix">
         <span class="num">
          3
         </span>
         <div class="text entry">
          <p>
           Roast brussels sprouts for 12-15 minutes, or until parmesan cheese becomes lightly golden brown. (I broiled mine for the last minute to ensure the parmesan cheese gets nice and golden!)
           <span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 19px;">
            Remove from the oven and drizzle honey evenly over brussels sprouts and enjoy!
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
