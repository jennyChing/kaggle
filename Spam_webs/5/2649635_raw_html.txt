           his 
is Tootsie our little tortoiseshell, double-maned, purebred
Lionhead. She was three months old in this picture. No matter where she
is, she is always posing! Especially when she sees the camera! She is
such a little ham!
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Tootsie just went to her first Bunny Show and came away with Best Of
Breed for her class! We were thrilled! Tootsie really charmed the judge
as well, with her terrific personality and straight upright pose! She
behaved like an experienced pro even though she's still a baby! But I
guess she is a professional at winning hearts, she's been doing that
with us since we first met. She's such a character, and a real sweetie, too.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Tootsie enjoys her Blue Seal Show Hutch Deluxe pellets, and occasionally
she gets treats of banana slice, parsley, sliced orange, and apple
slices. She only gets sweet treats moderately since bunnies have a sweet
tooth, and their tummies don't tolerate sugar very well.
