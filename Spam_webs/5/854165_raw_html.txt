       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         How A Misanthrope Sees the Superbowl
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-01-31T21:26:25+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 31, 2015
         </time>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Field.png">
          <img alt="How A Misanthrope Sees the Superbowl" class="alignnone wp-image-1398 " height="424" src="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Field.png" width="753"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         misanthrope
        </p>
        <p>
         mis()nTHrp,miz()nTHrp/
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          noun
         </em>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          a person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         Let me start off by saying, I dont dislike humankind or avoid human society. Yes I am a crazy cat lady but, I generally like other humans. I just question mankind once every yearSuperbowl. Every year around December I get a tense feeling of dread and anxiety. I worry. I fret. Then I start looking at calendars. Why, you may ask, is she going crazy? Every year is the Superbowl. And every year I wonder if it will be on my birthday. Call me self centeredbut I hate when a game with men throwing a ball overshadows
         <span style="color: #f9aa89;">
          <strong>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            my
           </span>
          </strong>
         </span>
         birthday. My birthday is February 7
         <sup>
          th
         </sup>
         so the Superbowl has landed right on my birthday before or the weekend of. One year I sat in my room while everyone else was watching the game. Oi. Not every year has been bad. My friends(who live near the stadium the Superbowl is being held at this year) throw an annual party. One year they got me a cake.:)
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/DSCN4498.jpg">
          <img alt="Harry Potter Cake" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1396" height="225" src="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/DSCN4498-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/DSCN4512.jpg">
          <img alt="Harry Potter Plays Football" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1397" height="225" src="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/DSCN4512-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So obviously my sentiments about the Superbowl are a littlebiased/resentful/whatever. I wanted to convey my feelings(that I am sure others have) about the Superbowl, but then I realizedpictures tell a thousand words. So here is how a misanthropesees the Superbowl.
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Oh yay the game has finally started.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/giphy-4.gif">
          <img alt="giphy (4)" class="alignnone wp-image-1384 size-full" height="225" src="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/giphy-4.gif" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Yay! For reals.
         <span style="text-decoration: line-through;">
          Adele Dazeem
         </span>
         Idina Menzel is singing the National Anthem!
         <a href="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/giphy-10.gif">
          <img alt="giphy (10)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1390" height="279" src="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/giphy-10.gif" width="495"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Shit. Now I want to watch
         <em>
          Glee
         </em>
         . Or
         <em>
          Frozen
         </em>
         . Or anything else.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
          <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="390" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/MplzKZ4Tius?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Seriously. We are five minutes in and there is already a commercial. They are usually funny though.
        </p>
        <p>
         No. Now I am just in tears.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/giphy-6.gif">
          <img alt="giphy (6)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1386" height="282" src="http://themodernmusings.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/giphy-6.gif" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Fuck. Game has started again. Going to eat some buffalo chicken dip. Its the crack of Superbowl.
        </p>
