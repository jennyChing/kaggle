        <p>
         After a long days work, theres nothing like lighting up scented candles that will cause inexplicable trauma and horror as you unwind to its relaxing aroma. Etsy store uncannyartshop created these horrific, realistic scented candles that look like melting, dismembered body parts.
         <!--more-->
        </p>
        <p>
         Available for $18 apiece, these candles come in 15 different scents and 3 different styles: ears, happy mouth, and disgusted mouth. If you want your candles to look their best for your guests, they can be customized to come with lipstick! Disgusting yet fancy, I like it!
        </p>
        <p>
         You can get yourself a bodily candle over on

          Etsy
         </a>
         .
