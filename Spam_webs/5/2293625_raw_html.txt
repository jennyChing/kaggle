             <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
              Warning:
             </h3>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              AndroidXDA.net will not be responsible for any kind of lose or damage occurred to your Samsung Galaxy S5 (Sprint). Proceed at your own risk.
             </p>
             <h3>
              <strong>
               Before Getting Started:
              </strong>
             </h3>
             <ul>
              <li>
               Always remember to take a proper backup of your Android device before starting the updating/installing or rooting etc process. Such as backup of necessary of your SMS, MMS, Call Logs, Contacts and Media Files to the internal/external SD Card.
               <strong>
                <br/>
               </strong>
              </li>
              <li>
               Download &amp; Install Samsung Galaxy S5 G900P USB Drivers from
               <strong>
                <a class="ext-link" href="http://www.samsung.com/us/support/owners/product/SM-G900PZKASPR" rel="external nofollow" title="">
                 here
                </a>
               </strong>
              </li>
              <li>
               Download the firmware .zip file
               <strong>
                <a class="item file ext-link" href="http://rwilco12.com/Files/Devices/Samsung%20Galaxy%20S5%20%28Sprint%29%20%28SM-G900P%29/Stock%20ROMs/NKD/Full%20Restore/G900P_NKD_FullRestore.exe" rel="external nofollow" title="">
                 G900P_NKD_FullRestore
                </a>
               </strong>
               for Samsung Galaxy S5 (Sprint) (SM-G900P)
              </li>
              <li>
               Download Odin 3.09 Flashing Tool from
               <strong>
                <a class="ext-link" href="https://www.mediafire.com/?efr676hpa7i5m6l" rel="external nofollow" title="">
                 this link
                </a>
               </strong>
              </li>
              <li>
               Enable USBDebuggingMode option on your Samsung Galaxy S5.
              </li>
              <li>
               Make sure that the battery level of your Galaxy S5 is above 70% before starting updating.
              </li>
             </ul>
             <h2>
              <strong>
               Steps to Update Galaxy S5 with Android 5.0 Lollipop:
              </strong>
             </h2>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Step No 1:-
              </strong>
              First of all extract both the files which youve already downloaded from the above links on the desktop of your PC.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Step No 2:-
              </strong>
              Run Odin Flashing Tool on PC as an Administrator.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Step No 3:-
              </strong>
              Boot your Galaxy S5 into the Download Mode, to do so first
              <strong>
               Turn Off
              </strong>
              the device and then press &amp; hold the
              <strong>
               Volume Down + Home
              </strong>
              button at the same time. Switch ON your Galaxy S5 while holding these both buttons to enter into the Download Mode.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Step No 4:-
              </strong>
              Now connect your device to the PC via its original USB data cable.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Step No 5:-
              </strong>
              On the Odin Tool click on
              <strong>
               PDA
              </strong>
              and select the firmware file from your desktop.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Step No 6:-
              </strong>
              Just click on the Start button on Odin to begin the firmware updating process.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Step No 7:-
              </strong>
              Wait for the Firmware update process to be completed successfully as it may take around 3 to 4 minutes. Your device will be automatically rebooted once the firmware updating/installing process is finished.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Step No 8:-
              </strong>
              Navigate to Settings &gt; About Phone to check the the firmware version after rebooting the device.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Tip:-
              </strong>
              Repeat all the above steps if your Galaxy S5 gets in the boot loop mode.
             </p>
