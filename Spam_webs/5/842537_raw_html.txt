             SNEAK PEEK OF JUSTIN BIEBER ON THE SET OF ZOOLANDER 2!
            </span>
           </h3>
           <p>
            <a href="http://lauraramargosian.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/2015-04-303.png">
             <img align="right" alt="2015-04-30" border="0" height="385" src="http://lauraramargosian.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/2015-04-30_thumb1.png" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: right; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; border-left: 0px; display: inline; padding-right: 0px" title="2015-04-30" width="377"/>
            </a>
            Justin Bieber and Ben Stiller have been working their asses off in Rome, filming Zoolander 2. It was announced earlier this week that Ben Stiller added one special person to the cast list, Justin Bieber. And so far it looks like the Biebs is having a lot of fun on setand if you look to the right, you can see Justin Bieber looking very curious on set.
           </p>
           <p>
            I wonder what scene this will be, dont you? J-Dawg uploaded the picture to Fahlo tagging it So intrigued!! [happycry emoj] and #Zoolander2 Justin Bieber is the master at teasing his fans and getting them all prepped for surprises. The picture revolves around Derek and Hansel and, of course their modeling. But what happens when your competition, attempts to take them out of business?
           </p>
           <p>
            Im sure the film is going to be excellent. And did you know that Ben Stiller is also directing Zoolander 2? Pretty amazingbecause he is hilarious and if youre looking for humor, Ben Stiller will knock it out of the ball park. Ben has also cast a few other well-known celebrities. Some of them are Penelope Cruz, Olivia Munn, Will Ferrell, Kristen Wiig and even Kanye West.
           </p>
           <p>
            The production began on the first of April and there are currently no release datesbut keep checking back and we will keep you updated on your favorite stars. What do you guys think about the new film Zoolander 2? What do you think of Justin Bieber On The Set Of Zoolander 2 ? If you havent seen Zoolander, then scroll down, baby because we have a trailer for you watchit might just get you pumped up for the second film!
