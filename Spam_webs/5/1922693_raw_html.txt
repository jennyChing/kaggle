        <p>
         Hello Everyone. This weekend, I decided to make myself an overnight duffel bag. This is a simple duffel pattern that has oval sides with pockets accented with wrap around straps. The cool thing about this pattern is that you dont have to line the bag or cover seams with binding! The tutorial is written so that when you are done, you bag will look stunning inside and out without the the trouble of adding lining. This fabric is from Joanns Fabric Store. I have no idea what its called but I was looking for purple coordinating fabrics and these showed up during my search. The bag measures about 20 x 10 which makes it the perfect size for an overnight trip. Enjoy the pattern and tutorial.
        </p>

         I decided to add a bottom panel with heavy weight interfacing to make the bottom sturdy. Love how it turned out!

        <p>
         <strong>
          Your Supplies
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          1 yard cotton for outer
         </li>
         <li>
          1 yard cotton for inner
         </li>
         <li>
          1 yard medium weight interfacing
         </li>
         <li>
          1/4 yard heavy weight interfacing
         </li>
         <li>
          1/4 yard light batting for straps
         </li>
         <li>
          20 zipper
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         You can download your
         <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B7GumdKUnNPkUVNyOFc2bG82blE/view?usp=sharing">
          free pattern HERE
         </a>
         . This PDF downloads from Google Docs. You will need Adobe to view the PDF. At 100%, the bag ends up being about 2010 in size. Scale according to your desired sizing. If you cannot download it, please email me and I will send you a PDF copy.
        </p>
        <p>
         Lets Start.
        </p>
        <p>
         Note: 1/2 seam allowance is used for this pattern. A stitch length of 3-3.5 is recommended since it is heavy weight.
        </p>
        <p>
         First, iron on your interfacing to the wrong side of your inner fabric pieces. Cut all the interfacing so that it sits about 1 away from the edges in the center of your pieces. Add the heavy weight interfacing to your bottom piece. You will have 2 side ovals, 2 side main and 1 bottom. (Picture below does not show pocket pieces and strap pieces)
        </p>

          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6446" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2194" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6446-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6446-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Next, take your main (outer) pieces and sew to the interfaced inner pieces right sides together. Turn right side out. Top-stitch your opening closed. Iron neatly. Remember to do this for all your pieces.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6447.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6447" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2195" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6447-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6447-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6448.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6448" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2196" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6448-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6448-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6449.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6449" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2197" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6449-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6449-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         To make your side pockets, take your piping and insert it in between your two pocket pieces with the raw edge aligning with the top right side raw edges of your two pocket pieces. Sew all the way around leaving the bottom open. Turn inside out on the bottom. You dont need to sew the bottom closed at this point.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6450.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6450" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2198" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6450-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6450-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6451.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6451" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2199" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6451-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6451-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6452.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6452" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2200" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6452-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6452-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Next, take your pocket piece and baste stitch the sides and bottom to the outer part of your pocket pieces. Repeat for both pockets.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6453.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6453" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2201" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6453-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6453-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         Now lets do the zipper. Take one of your main pieces and pin the zipper right side down on the right side. Sew. Next, take the other main piece and place right side together and sew down as well. You can top-stitch the sides of the zipper if needed.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6463.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6463" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2202" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6463-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6463-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6464.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6464" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2203" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6464-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6464-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6465.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6465" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2204" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6465-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6465-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         At this point, you will want to add your straps. To make the straps, cut 2 pieces 4 x20 of fabric. You only need to cut 2 x20 of batting. You may need to sew pieces together to get this length. Fold the sides in about 1/4. Fill in the batting in one side. Fold over the fabric and sew down. Sew about 4 straight lines to quilt.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6469.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6469" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2208" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6469-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6469-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6470.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6470" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2209" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6470-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6470-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6471.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6471" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2210" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6471-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6471-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I made a mistake and forgot to add my straps to the sides at this point. Instead I had to go back at the end and do it. Ideally, this is where you want to add your straps to the sides. Again, this picture shows it done after the bag was put together. I recommend doing it at this step. Pin and sew 5 from each side of the bottom of the main pieces on the right side. Leave about 3 from the top of the bag when you sew down the straps.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6472.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6472" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2211" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6472-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6472-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6473.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6473" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2212" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6473-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6473-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Next, take your bottom piece and sew right side together with the bottom of one of your side piece. Bring the other main piece and sew to the other side of the bottom piece.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6466.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6466" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2205" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6466-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6466-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6467.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6467" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2206" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6467-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6467-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         Next, take your side ovals with your pockets attached and pin and sew all the way around, right side together.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6468.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="IMG_6468" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-2207" height="225" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6468-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://thestitchingscientist.com/2015/05/free-pattern-overnight-duffel-bag.html&amp;media=http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_6468-300x225.jpg&amp;description=Free Pattern Overnight Duffel Bag')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats it, your bag is done and you dont have to do lining or bind your inner raw edges!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/duffel.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="duffel" class="aligncenter wp-image-2217" height="818" src="http://thestitchingscientist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/duffel-625x1024.jpg" width="499"/>

         </a>
        </p>

         Thanks for visiting and Happy Sewing,
