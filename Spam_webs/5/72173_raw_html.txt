        <p>
         <a href="/files/uploads/2012/08/bz-panel-08-09-122.jpg" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-7342" height="514" src="/files/uploads/2012/08/bz-panel-08-09-122.jpg" width="432"/>
         </a>
         <strong>
          Bizarro is brought to you today by
          <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_Wqo9IVCWV5E/SU1H3o214hI/AAAAAAAABrU/pr_tz2AX9KI/s1600-h/Freaky+Cartoon+Cover.jpg" target="_blank">
           <em>
            <span style="color: #3366ff">
             Super Schlong.
            </span>
           </em>
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         This clever little bit of wordplay is a collaboration with my known associate, Wayno of Pittsburgh. Wayno is a good friend and he and I have been working together on cartoons for a few years now. In fact, this one represents his 100th appearance in Bizarro. Hes a joy to work with, a great gag writer, and a darned dandy artist, to boot. I also suspect hes a good husband and member of his community but youll have to ask his wife and neighbors for confirmation on that.
         <a href="http://waynocartoons.blogspot.com/2012/08/weapons-of-self-destruction-number-100.html" target="_blank">
          <span style="color: #3366ff">
           Heres what Wayno has to say about our anniversary of sorts.
          </span>
         </a>
         <span id="more-7292">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/files/uploads/2012/08/bz-panel-08-10-121.jpg" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-7322" height="514" src="/files/uploads/2012/08/bz-panel-08-10-121.jpg" width="432"/>
         </a>
         I have a friend who is a Grammy Award winning percussionist and he loved this cartoon so Im going to consider it a success. I like it, too, but I dont have
         <em>
          half
         </em>
         as many Grammys to my name as he does.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/files/uploads/2012/08/bz-panel-08-11-121.jpg" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-7332" height="514" src="/files/uploads/2012/08/bz-panel-08-11-121.jpg" width="432"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         And now some silliness with Humpty Dumpty. I used to do a very surreal Humpty Dumpty routine in my one-man show. Humpty sang a strange, bittersweet song about lost love (he was gay, in love with Eggs Benedict) while his arms wafted back and forth musically as he emoted through the ballad. It was a real tear jerker.
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-4_H-FO4QCKo/UCaxlJkHcVI/AAAAAAAAHXE/0br5gRyS_JI/s1600/Dan+as+Humpty.jpg" target="_blank">
          <span style="color: #3366ff">
           Heres me as Humpty around 2001.
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/files/uploads/2012/08/bz-panel-08-08-121.jpg" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-7302" height="514" src="/files/uploads/2012/08/bz-panel-08-08-121.jpg" width="432"/>
         </a>
         Lastly, I offer you a super hero I could really use. I despise blister packs and wish we as a species would outlaw them. Apart from them being an environmental nightmare (I assume) they are also dangerous and frustrating. I dont know how many fingers Ive cut off accidentally while trying to get into one of those things. (Okay, I just counted and I havent actually lost any fingers, but its only a matter of time.)
        </p>

             More super hero cartoons can be found in my latest book!
            </span>
