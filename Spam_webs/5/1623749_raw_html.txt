         Tis giveaway season! Its a strange and wonderful situation to be in when Im having trouble finding a way to schedule in multiple giveaways before I leave on Christmas holidays next week. I hope you dont mind my RSS feed bearing a concentration of
         <a href="http://foodess.com/10571-holiday-gifts-foodies-2014.html" target="_blank">

        <p>
         This is my new baby, the Vitamix S30. He lives on my counter beside his big brother, the Pro 750, and together were a
         <a href="http://instagram.com/p/v4GsYrllHi/?modal=true" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://instagram.com/p/v4GsYrllHi/?modal=true', 'happy little family');" target="_blank">

        </p>
        <p>
         Hes got the same high-power motor I lovein the rest of the Vitamix family, but with a few key differences  it takes up much less counter space (6 inches, to be precise), it has two interchangeable canisters (one of which is an insulated to-go cup that will keep cold beverages cold and hot beverages hot  great for on-the-go smoothie lovers), its dishwasher safe (!) and its perfectfor small tasks. I adore my Vitamix 750, but its just not the ideal machine for doing very small amounts (the blades wont reach the food once its hitsthe walls of the canister if there isnt enough volume to keep it circulating).
        </p>
        <p>
         I use the S30 for
         <a href="http://instagram.com/p/ve6f93llBu/?modal=true" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://instagram.com/p/ve6f93llBu/?modal=true', 'peanuts');" target="_blank">
          peanuts
         </a>
         afternoon
         <a href="http://foodess.com/10028-banana-kale-green-smoothie.html" target="_blank">
          green
         </a>
         <a href="http://foodess.com/4643-avocado-banana-smoothie.html" target="_blank">
          smoothie
         </a>
         , because I only do a small amount for his tinytoddler tummy.Its also the tool I reach for when I want to finely chop just a handful of nuts or any other ingredient. But dont get me wrong, its not just for mini-tasks thelarger containeris 40 ounces, which is plentysubstantial  thats three filling smoothies. Im super excited that I have one to give to you, too!
