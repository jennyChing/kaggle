					&lt;h1 class="entry-title"&gt;Piata-inspired animal charms&lt;/h1&gt;
				
				&lt;div class="article-header"&gt;
					
	&lt;span class="hover-effect"&gt;
		&lt;img class="article-photo" src="http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/giraffe550.jpg-890x395_c.jpg" alt="Piata-inspired animal charms" /&gt;	&lt;/span&gt;

					&lt;div class="article-meta"&gt;
						&lt;div class="meta-date updated"&gt;
															&lt;span class="date"&gt;16&lt;/span&gt;
								&lt;span class="month"&gt;Apr, 2014&lt;/span&gt;
							 
														&lt;span class="author"&gt;by 
								&lt;a  href="http://www.craftychica.com/author/craftychica/" title="Posts by Kathy Cano-Murillo" rel="author"&gt;Kathy Cano-Murillo&lt;/a&gt;							&lt;/span&gt;
													&lt;/div&gt;
						&lt;div class="meta-tools"&gt;
															&lt;a href="http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/print" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;i class="fa fa-print"&gt;&lt;/i&gt;Print this article&lt;/a&gt;
														&lt;span&gt;&lt;i class="fa fa-text-height"&gt;&lt;/i&gt;Font size &lt;span class="f-size"&gt;&lt;a href="#font-size-down"&gt;-&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span class="f-size-number"&gt;16&lt;/span&gt;&lt;a href="#font-size-up"&gt;+&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;

				&lt;div class="addtoany_share_save_container addtoany_content_top"&gt;&lt;div class="a2a_kit addtoany_list a2a_target" id="wpa2a_3"&gt;&lt;a class="a2a_button_facebook_like addtoany_special_service" data-href="http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/"&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;a class="a2a_button_twitter_tweet addtoany_special_service" data-count="none" data-url="http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/" data-text="Piata-inspired animal charms"&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;a class="a2a_button_google_plusone addtoany_special_service" data-annotation="none" data-href="http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/"&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;a class="a2a_button_pinterest_pin addtoany_special_service" data-pin-config="none" data-url="http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/"&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;a href="https://www.addtoany.com/share_save" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.addtoany.com/share_save', 'Share']);" class="a2a_dd addtoany_share_save"  style="background:url(http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/plugins/add-to-any/favicon.png) no-repeat scroll 4px 0px;padding:0 0 0 25px;display:inline-block;height:16px;vertical-align:middle"&gt;&lt;span&gt;Share&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div class='shareaholic-canvas' data-app-id='84416' data-app='share_buttons' data-title='Piata-inspired animal charms' data-link='http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/' data-summary=''&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charms620.jpg.jpg"&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img src="http://i0.wp.com/www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charms620.jpg.jpg?resize=620%2C441" alt="plastic-animal-necklace.jpg" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13694" data-recalc-dims="1" /&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/&amp;media=http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charms620.jpg.jpg&amp;description=Piata-inspired animal charms')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Plastic animals are just too fun to resist. They&#8217;ve been gilded, glittered, blinged, and chopped. I went the easy route and used old-school paint and varnish. The result? These awesome charms that can be used for bracelets or plastic animal necklaces. I love all things Mexi-style, so I went with bright and bold colors, kind of like a piata!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Supplies:&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Plastic animals&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Handheld rotary tool&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Eye pin, needle nose pliers (jump rings and chain for necklace)&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.ilovetocreate.com/Tulip-Soft-Fabric-Paint-222" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.ilovetocreate.com/Tulip-Soft-Fabric-Paint-222', 'Tulip Soft Fabric Paint']);" target="_blank"&gt;Tulip Soft Fabric Paint&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.ilovetocreate.com/Aleenes-Paper-Glaze-57" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.ilovetocreate.com/Aleenes-Paper-Glaze-57', 'Aleene&#8217;s Paper Glaze']);" target="_blank"&gt;Aleene&#8217;s Paper Glaze&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Head pin, thin liner brush&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmspaint1.jpg.jpg"&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img src="http://i1.wp.com/www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmspaint1.jpg.jpg?resize=620%2C477" alt="charmspaint1.jpg" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13696" data-recalc-dims="1" /&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/&amp;media=http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmspaint1.jpg.jpg&amp;description=Piata-inspired animal charms')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The first step is to use your rotary tool to make a hole through each animal. Next, base coat each one with paint.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmpaint1A.jpg.jpg"&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img src="http://i2.wp.com/www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmpaint1A.jpg.jpg?resize=620%2C714" alt="charmpaint1A.jpg" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13697" data-recalc-dims="1" /&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/&amp;media=http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmpaint1A.jpg.jpg&amp;description=Piata-inspired animal charms')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Let dry, then use the head pin to add smaller details. The animals are so small, make sure to have patience and use good lighting!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/chamrpaint2.jpg.jpg"&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img src="http://i2.wp.com/www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/chamrpaint2.jpg.jpg?resize=620%2C559" alt="chamrpaint2.jpg" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13698" data-recalc-dims="1" /&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/&amp;media=http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/chamrpaint2.jpg.jpg&amp;description=Piata-inspired animal charms')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Once they are all painted, apply a coat of Aleene&#8217;s Paper Glaze to make them nice and glossy. Let dry.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmbull.jpg.jpg"&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img src="http://i2.wp.com/www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmbull.jpg.jpg?resize=620%2C414" alt="charmbull.jpg" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13699" data-recalc-dims="1" /&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/&amp;media=http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmbull.jpg.jpg&amp;description=Piata-inspired animal charms')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Use your needle nose pliers to insert the eye pin through the animal and create a secure loop at the top. Add a jump ring and attach your charm to whatever you want. They can be charms, ornaments, pendants, whatever you want! &lt;strong&gt;NOTE:&lt;/strong&gt; My first thought was to add beads or tassels from the bottom, but I changed my mind. If you don&#8217;t want to hang anything from under the animal, use a head pin instead of an eye pin.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmgiraffe1.jpg1.jpg"&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img src="http://i2.wp.com/www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmgiraffe1.jpg1.jpg?resize=580%2C386" alt="charmgiraffe.jpg" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-13700" data-recalc-dims="1" /&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/&amp;media=http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmgiraffe1.jpg1.jpg&amp;description=Piata-inspired animal charms')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/a&gt; &lt;a href="http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmsB620.jpg"&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img src="http://i1.wp.com/www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmsB620.jpg?resize=580%2C580" alt="charmsB620" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-13701" data-recalc-dims="1" /&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/&amp;media=http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmsB620.jpg&amp;description=Piata-inspired animal charms')"&gt;

		&lt;img src="http://i1.wp.com/www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmsingle.jpg.jpg?resize=580%2C386" alt="charmsingle.jpg" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-13702" data-recalc-dims="1" /&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.craftychica.com/2014/04/pinata-inspired-animal-charms/&amp;media=http://www.craftychica.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/charmsingle.jpg.jpg&amp;description=Piata-inspired animal charms')"&gt;
