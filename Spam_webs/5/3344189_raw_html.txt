       <h1>
        Ties with Tradition
       </h1>
       <h2>
        Macedonian apron designs
       </h2>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       This exhibition showcases a selection of
Macedonian aprons, traditionally worn for special
occasions and everyday use.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       A powerful juxtaposition of colours, patterns,
and materials, these hand-woven aprons tell
the stories of women's lives and creativity, their
villages and traditions.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       For the women of the Illawarra their journey of
migration to Australia transformed their aprons
from an integral part of daily dress into a lasting
memento of a former way of life.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Our thanks to the women and their families who
have shared their aprons and their stories to
make this exhibition possible.
