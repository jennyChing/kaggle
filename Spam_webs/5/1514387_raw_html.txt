      <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
       2015 Ferrari California T Convertible Exterior and Interior Design
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       You will feel different sensation with this car. Maybe you will not look sporty to drive with
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        <strong>
         2015 Ferrari California T Convertible
        </strong>
       </span>
       but you still can be the center of attention on the city streets. Car has higher dimension with Chrome Satin Grille. Car is using Power Retractable Hard Top that can flip automatically. And on the interior you can sit in the Premium Cloth and Suede Leather that cover all interior. on the dashboard you will find many in car entertainment system that will entertain you during your trip. Ferrari provides Bluetooth Connection that can be connected with the phone.
      </p>
      <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
       2015 Ferrari California T Convertible Engine and Specifications
      </h3>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       You will drive in classy car with powerful engine because Ferrari gives you 3.9L V8 Turbocharge engine. This is different kind of engine that will bring you to the new level of driving. The engine is supported with Two IHI Twin-scroll Turbo that can deliver more power. Ferrari also provides ECU Adjusts Boost that will blend with the engine combustion system so you will reach the top speed in 7-speed Automatic Transmission. And you can go fast and stable at the same time because car has Stability Control and Traction Control that will ensure your safety when driving
       <em>
        <strong>
         2015 Ferrari California T Convertible.
        </strong>
       </em>
      </p>
