        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="emma holten" src="http://i.huffpost.com/gen/2460004/thumbs/o-EMMA-HOLTEN-570.jpg?2"/>

        <p>
         Holten told
         <a href="http://www.elle.com/news/lifestyle/fighting-revenge-porn-with-nude-images?src=spr_TWITTER&amp;spr_id=1448_127541959&amp;linkId=11555326" target="_hplink">
          Elle magazine
         </a>
         that she was only able to participate in such a shoot due to her support system and understanding boss. She recognizes that this avenue is not open to many victims of revenge porn -- but hopes that her actions will help some of them regardless.
        </p>
        <p>
         "I say to victims: You are right, even if you're alone!," Holten told Elle. "You live in a patriarchal culture that seeks to target you for being you, and THEY'RE wrong, and you're right!         </p>

        <p>
         Holten's experience, while upsetting, is not uncommon. According to a 2013
         <a href="http://www.mcafee.com/us/about/news/2013/q1/20130204-01.aspx" target="_hplink">
          MacAfee survey
         </a>
         , 10 percent of ex-partners have threatened to release intimate photographs -- and 60 percent of those people follow through on said threats. An estimated 90 percent of
         <a href="http://www.endrevengeporn.org/revenge-porn-infographic/" target="_hplink">
          revenge porn victims
         </a>
         are women.
        </p>
        <p>
         Activists are pushing to make revenge porn punishable by law worldwide, as is already the case in
         <a href="http://www.economist.com/news/international/21606307-how-should-online-publication-explicit-images-without-their-subjects-consent-be" target="_hplink">
          13 U.S. states
         </a>
         . Holten hopes that her photographs will contribute to the battle against revenge porn by forcing people to consider the difference between these images, willingly posed for and shared as a celebration of her body, and revenge porn, shared without a subject's consent with the intention of humiliating them.
        </p>
        <p>
         "There is no sexual, visual, acceptable picture without my consent," Holten told
         <a href="http://www.elle.com/culture/career-politics/news/a19783/fighting-revenge-porn-with-nude-images/?src=spr_TWITTER&amp;spr_id=1448_127541959&amp;linkId=11555326" target="_hplink">
          Elle
         </a>
         . "Without my consent there is only violation."
