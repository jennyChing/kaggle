         <a class="vt-p" href="http://green.thefuntimesguide.com/tag/food" target="_blank">
          health and nutrition
         </a>
         are a pretty big deal to me.
        </p>
        <p>
         Now dont hear me saying that you shouldnt indulge in a guilty pleasure now and again, but controlling what goes into your body is something that is paramount if you want to live a healthy life.
        </p>
        <p>
         Unfortunately, the task of controlling what goes into our bodies isnt up to anyone but ourselves  because it sure isnt up to the FDA, regardless of what good intentions they might tout.
        </p>
        <p>
         (What are those things that are said to pave the road to Hell? Oh yeah, good intentions)
        </p>
        <p>
         The green and health worlds talk a lot about organic foods, fruits, veggies, and the like, but theres sadly little awareness of food additives that slip under the radar.
        </p>
