         <p>
          A bit of something different for todays blog post, and were thinking about possibly doing this on a monthly basis. No set dates or anything  you know us  but as were all over social media, we thought wed start pulling out some of our favourite bits from each month and then post them to the blog.
         </p>
         <p>
          Today, were concentrating on our Twitter profile. Lots of people tell us they cant really get into Twitter, so, if youre one of those people, maybe these Twitter posts will help to change your mind. Some will be Tweets weve sent and some will be from other people who have been in conversation with us.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Its Friday so weve kept it random  just a bit of fun, really. And if you do use Twitter, these Tweets are interactive so you can follow the people we mention directly from hereand theyre all people worth following.
          </b>
