      <h2>
       Wall Tiles - Strip Planking Meshed Wall Tiles
      </h2>
      <p>
       Be it interior or exterior, Strip Cladding is your answer. The tiles are made of solid stone and every stone is hand-picked for its texture and color. The tile creates an amazing effect which changes subtly with both natural and artificial lighting. 
                  This tile creates a refreshing new design element to your environment and is ideal for feature walls, in particular. Depending on the design you desire the tile can be placed horizontally or vertically, further more it can be installed grout-free or with traditional grout joints. These tiles are precisely cut to facilitate butt jointing. We are also supplying these strips in interlocking mesh backed pattern for ease of installation. This cladding is available in varying lengths / widths and in almost all the stone in which we deal. The new concept of variable finishes like honed, shot blasted, flamed , Brushed etc. in different colours can also be ordered.
      </p>
