       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-04-29T07:32:41+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          April 29, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/author/sherri-hagymas" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Sherri
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/chicken-lazone-with-mushrooms.html#comments">
           11 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
         <br/>
         This post may contain an affiliate link.  Read my
         <a href="/disclosure-policy/" target="_blank" title="to simply inspire disclosure policy">
          Disclosure Policy here
         </a>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <em>
          This simple yet fabulous Chicken Lazone recipe is loaded with flavor. The added mushrooms make it even that more scrumptious!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Chicken-Lazone-with-Mushrooms2.jpg">
          <img alt="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" class="aligncenter wp-image-46404 size-full" height="627" src="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Chicken-Lazone-with-Mushrooms2.jpg" title="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Have you ever heard of Chicken Lazone? I certainly had not. It is originally from the restaurant in New Orleans called Brennans. It is so simple yet incredibly flavorful!
        </p>
        <p>
         It requires such common ingredients that you would never think to would create the flavor it goes.
        </p>
        <p>
         I changed things up a little bit. The recipe called for heavy cream and I really didnt want to use that myself. So I simply used 2 cups of fat free milk and 2 tablespoons of corn starch to thicken the sauce. It worked just a good! Yay for less fat!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          INGREDIENTS
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         2 lb chicken tenders
        </p>
        <p>
         8 oz of white mushrooms, sliced
        </p>
        <p>
         2 TBSP of coconut oil
        </p>
        <p>
         1 small onion, chopped
        </p>
        <p>
         2 gloves of garlic
        </p>
        <p>
         1 1/2 tsp chili powder
        </p>
        <p>
         2 tsp garlic powder
        </p>
        <p>
         1 1/2 tsp onion powder
        </p>
        <p>
         1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
        </p>
        <p>
         1/4 cup coconut oil
        </p>
        <p>
         2 cups milk
        </p>
        <p>
         2 Tablespoons of corn starch or flour
        </p>
        <p>
         Salt and Pepper to taste
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Chicken-Lazone-with-Mushrooms.jpg">
          <img alt="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" class="aligncenter wp-image-46405 size-full" height="367" src="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Chicken-Lazone-with-Mushrooms.jpg" title="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          DIRECTIONS
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Saute the mushrooms,onions and garlic in the 2 TBSP of coconut oil until tender.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chicken-lazone2.jpg">
          <img alt="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" class="aligncenter wp-image-46408 size-full" height="333" src="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chicken-lazone2.jpg" title="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Combine salt, chili powder, onion powder, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper. Sprinkle over both sides of chicken tenders.
        </p>
        <p>
         In large saute pan, melt 1/4 cup of coconut oil over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken tenders for about 8 minutes or until done.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chicken-lazone1.jpg">
          <img alt="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" class="aligncenter wp-image-46407 size-full" height="333" src="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chicken-lazone1.jpg" title="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chicken-lazone3.jpg">
          <img alt="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" class="aligncenter wp-image-46409 size-full" height="333" src="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chicken-lazone3.jpg" title="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mix the corn starch or flour with cold milk in a bowl.
        </p>
        <p>
         Pour the milk mixture into the skillet.
        </p>
        <p>
         Add the mushrooms and onions.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chicken-lazone4.jpg">
          <img alt="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" class="aligncenter wp-image-46410 size-full" height="333" src="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chicken-lazone4.jpg" title="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Lower heat and simmer until the sauce thickens, about 5-7 minutes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chicken-lazone5.jpg">
          <img alt="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" class="aligncenter wp-image-46411 size-full" height="333" src="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chicken-lazone5.jpg" title="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Serve chicken and sauce over quinoa or pasta
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Chicken-Lazone-with-Mushrooms1.jpg">
          <img alt="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" class="aligncenter wp-image-46406 size-full" height="825" src="http://www.tosimplyinspire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Chicken-Lazone-with-Mushrooms1.jpg" title="Chicken Lazone with Mushrooms" width="550"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
        </p>

           </li>

           </li>
