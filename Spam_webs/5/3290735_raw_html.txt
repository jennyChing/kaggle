            <p>
             Comedian Hannibal Buress is facing quite a bit of
             <a href="http://www.phillymag.com/ticket/2014/10/17/hannibal-buress-bill-cosby-rapist/#ZfGw7D5EsvUTRQ5l.01" target="_blank">
              backlash
             </a>
             after referring to Bill Cosby as a rapist during a stand-up routine in Cosbys hometown of Philadelphia.
            </p>
            <p>
             During his late night show at the Trocadero in Philadelphia last week, Buress went on a rather lengthy rant about the legendary comedian. Not only does Buress call out Cosbys perceived self-righteousness, he also calls him out for his checkered past concerning sexual assault.
            </p>
            <p>
             Check out Hannibal Buresss comments on Bill Cosby below:
            </p>
            <p>
             <iframe frameborder="0" height="437" scrolling="no" src="http://bostonmagazine.magnify.net/video/Hannibal-Burresss-Bill-Cosby-/player?layout=&amp;read_more=1" width="616">
             </iframe>
            </p>
            <blockquote>
             <p>
              <em>
               Bill Cosby has the f***ing smuggest old black man public persona that I hate. Pull your pants up, black people. I was on TV in the 80s. I can talk down to you because I had a successful sitcom. Yeah, but you raped women, Bill Cosby. So, brings you down a couple notches. I dont curse on stage. Well, yeah, youre a rapist, so Ive done this bit on stage, and people dont believe. People think Im making it up That s**t is upsetting. If you didnt know about it, trust me. You leave here and Google Bill Cosby rape. Its not funny. That s**t has more results than Hannibal Buress.
              </em>
             </p>
            </blockquote>
            <p>
             Buress referring to Cosby as a rapist has led to a lot of outrage on social media sites, especially Twitter, where some people are calling for Buresss head:
            </p>
            <p>
             <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
             </script>
            </p>
            <p>
             <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
             </script>
            </p>
            <p>
             Others have been quick come to Buresss defense and say that people are mad at the wrong person:
            </p>
            <p>
             <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
             </script>
            </p>
            <p>
             <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
             </script>
            </p>
            <p>
             After Buresss comments about Cosby started making waves around the web, he posted the following comment on Twitter:
            </p>
            <p>
             <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
             </script>
            </p>
            <p>
             Unfortunately, where theres smoke there is fire in Bill Cosbys case. According to
             <em>
              <a href="http://mic.com/articles/101918/13-women-have-accused-bill-cosby-of-rape-so-why-has-america-forgiven-him" target="_blank">
               Mic
              </a>
             </em>
             , Cosby has been accused of rape by 13 different women. Even though Cosby hasnt been convicted of anything, he did settle a lawsuit brought forth by an alleged victim in 2006.
            </p>
            <p>
             Cosby has not commented on Buresss rape accusations thus far.
            </p>

jQuery.fn.nospam = function(settings) {
    settings = jQuery.extend({
        replaceText: false, // optional, accepts true or false
        filterLevel: 'normal' // optional, accepts 'low' or 'normal'
    }, settings);

    return this.each(function() {
        e = null;
        if (settings.filterLevel == 'low') { // Can be a switch() if more levels added
            if (jQuery(this).is('a[rel]')) {
                e = jQuery(this).attr('rel').replace('//', '@').replace(/\//g, '.');
            } else {
                e = jQuery(this).text().replace('//', '@').replace(/\//g, '.');
            }
        } else { // 'normal'
            if (jQuery(this).is('a[rel]')) {
                e = jQuery(this).attr('rel').split('').reverse().join('').replace('//', '@').replace(/\//g, '.');
            } else {
                e = jQuery(this).text().split('').reverse().join('').replace('//', '@').replace(/\//g, '.');
            }
