       <h3>
        I Love This Gluten Free Cereal Recipe!
       </h3>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        So if you have been around my blog much, you might have noticed that I pretty much live a gluten free life and I often eat grain free as well.
       </p>
       <p>
        Notice that I said pretty much! I believe in balance. Sometimes I will eat a gluten free cereal recipe like this one, and other times I might whip up a batch of my
        <a href="http://wholelifestylenutrition.com/recipes/paleo-cereal-recipe-cranberry-almond-crunch-gluten-free/" target="_blank">
