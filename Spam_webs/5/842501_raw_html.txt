       <h1 class="entry-title single-title">
        <span>
         Amazon Confirms a New Beyonc Album Is Dropping This Month
        </span>
       </h1>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-media">
      </div>
      <div class="wrapper">
       <div class="grids">
        <div class="grid-8">
         <div class="single-box entry-media">
          <img alt="Amazon-Confirms-New-Beyonce-Project" class="attachment-big-size wp-post-image" height="654" src="http://stshd.translationllc.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Amazon-Confirms-New-Beyonce-Project.jpg" width="1050"/>
         </div>
         <!-- start byline -->
         <span class="entry-author">
          By
          <a href="http://thestashed.com/author/kaz/" rel="author">
           Kazeem Famuyide
          </a>
         </span>
         <!-- end byline -->
         <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content">
          <p>
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Beyonc
           </span>
           season is coming sooner rather than later. Over the weekend, a proposed tracklist for a brand-new
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Beyonc
           </span>
           album hit the net. The leaked list featured songs that included
           <strong>
            Nicki Minaj, Rihanna
           </strong>
           and
           <strong>
            Justin Timberlake.
           </strong>
           Given how secretive the Knowles-Carter clan is when it comes to music, especially when it came to the release of
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Beyoncs
           </span>
           self-titled project last year, the supposed new info was taken with a grain of salt.
          </p>
          <p>
           Apparently, we might be wrong.
          </p>
          <p>
           Amazon has confirmed that a new
