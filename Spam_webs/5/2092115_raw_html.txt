        <header>
         <h1 class="title entry-title">
          Creative Advertising at the Edinburgh Fringe
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="post-meta">
         <span class="small">
          By
         </span>
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/author/miranda-and-dylan/" rel="author" title="Posts by Miranda">
            Miranda
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="small">
          on
         </span>
         <abbr class="date time published updated" title="2013-08-16T22:51:00+0100">
          August 16, 2013
         </abbr>
         <span class="small">
          in
         </span>
         <span class="categories">
          <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/places/europe/" title="View all items in Europe">
           Europe
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/places/europe/scotland/" title="View all items in Scotland">
           Scotland
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <section class="entry">
         <div class="seriesmeta">
          
          <a class="series-106" href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/series/edinburgh-fringe-festival-2013/" title="Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2013">
           Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2013
          </a>
          #3 
         </div>
         <div class="seriesbox">
          <div class="center">
           <a class="series-106" href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/series/edinburgh-fringe-festival-2013/" title="Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2013">
            Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2013
           </a>
          </div>
          <ul class="serieslist-ul">
           #1
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/edinburgh-fringe-festival-2013-opening-weekend/" title="Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2013: Opening Weekend">
            Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2013: Opening Weekend
           </a>
           <br/>
           #2
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/edinburgh-fringe-week-1/" title="Edinburgh Fringe: Week 1">
            Edinburgh Fringe: Week 1
           </a>
           <br/>
           #3 Creative Advertising at the Edinburgh Fringe
           <br/>
           #4
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/the-end-off-the-ed-festival-fireworks/" title="Edinburgh Fireworks Concert Wraps Up The Festival [VIDEO]">
            Edinburgh Fireworks Concert Wraps Up The Festival [VIDEO]
           </a>
           <br/>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="17665770" data-link="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/creative-advertising-at-the-edinburgh-fringe/" data-summary="The Edinburgh Fringe can be a competitive environment when it comes to attracting audiences. A showcase of the best creative advertising used to draw people in." data-title="Creative Advertising at the Edinburgh Fringe">
         </div>
         <div itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
          <p>
           With over 2500 shows being put on during the Edinburgh Fringe Festival, theres no way for someone to attend every one of them. Competition is fierceamong performers bidding for revelers attention. Walking down the Royal Mile involves jostling through a gauntlet of leafleters looking to offload their advertisements. The first time we ventured here during the Fringe, we were bombarded with flyers, quickly accumulating handfuls of them.
          </p>
          <p>
           Of course, with such a competitive environment, youll get those who resort to creative advertising. And leave it to theatre folk to do it with flare. Besides standing out, pre-show advertising of this sort gives potential audiences the opportunity to sample a bit of what will be in the show; whether it be a prop, costume, or gimmick, you can bet that youll find something similar if you do choose to spend your afternoon watching their act.
          </p>
          <p>
           This type of advertising is definitely effective. Those people lying in the street,covered in blood? Thatll stick with me for awhile. That guy who crammed a leaflet into my chest as I walked by Who was that again?
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1975" style="width: 595px">
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/murdered.jpg" rel="lightbox">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Leafleters covered in blood on Royal Mile" class="size-medium wp-image-1975" height="390" src="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/murdered-585x390.jpg" width="585"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/creative-advertising-at-the-edinburgh-fringe/&amp;media=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/murdered-585x390.jpg&amp;description=Creative Advertising at the Edinburgh Fringe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Nothing gets peoples attention like lying on the ground covered in blood. Especially when theyre trying to walk where youre lying.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1974" style="width: 595px">
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/free_hugs.jpg" rel="lightbox">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Guys holding free hugs signs" class="size-medium wp-image-1974" height="390" src="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/free_hugs-585x390.jpg" width="585"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/creative-advertising-at-the-edinburgh-fringe/&amp;media=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/free_hugs-585x390.jpg&amp;description=Creative Advertising at the Edinburgh Fringe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            The question is, do you HAVE to hug them if you want a flyer?
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1971" style="width: 595px">
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/whitedress.jpg" rel="lightbox">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Burlesque advertisement fringe" class="size-medium wp-image-1971" height="390" src="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/whitedress-585x390.jpg" width="585"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/creative-advertising-at-the-edinburgh-fringe/&amp;media=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/whitedress-585x390.jpg&amp;description=Creative Advertising at the Edinburgh Fringe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            The sign the man is holding says: My wife is on vacation from 31 Jul till 26 Aug. Id like to meet with with a young girl every day at 2:30pm on Chambers St.
            <br/>
            Funny, thats also when the Fringe is running until.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1973" style="width: 595px">
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/fiddle_dance.jpg" rel="lightbox">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Creative fiddle dance at the Fringe" class="size-medium wp-image-1973" height="390" src="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/fiddle_dance-585x390.jpg" width="585"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/creative-advertising-at-the-edinburgh-fringe/&amp;media=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/fiddle_dance-585x390.jpg&amp;description=Creative Advertising at the Edinburgh Fringe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Its not a festival till you have a fiddle dance in the middle of the street.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1972" style="width: 595px">
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/coffin.jpg" rel="lightbox">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Man lying in coffin" class="size-medium wp-image-1972" height="390" src="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/coffin-585x390.jpg" width="585"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/creative-advertising-at-the-edinburgh-fringe/&amp;media=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/coffin-585x390.jpg&amp;description=Creative Advertising at the Edinburgh Fringe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Props like this coffin always attract attention. Mostly because they take up so much space. We actually went and saw
            <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/edinburgh-fringe-festival-2013-opening-weekend/" title="Edinburgh Fringe Festival 2013: Opening Weekend">
             this play  its called Life Sentence
            </a>
            .
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1976" style="width: 595px">
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/remove_gag.jpg" rel="lightbox">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Gagged man handing out leaflets" class="size-medium wp-image-1976" height="390" src="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/remove_gag-585x390.jpg" width="585"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/creative-advertising-at-the-edinburgh-fringe/&amp;media=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/remove_gag-585x390.jpg&amp;description=Creative Advertising at the Edinburgh Fringe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            We had to ungag this one to hear his spiel.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1978" style="width: 595px">
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/silver_paint.jpg" rel="lightbox">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="People covered in silver paint" class="size-medium wp-image-1978" height="390" src="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/silver_paint-585x390.jpg" width="585"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/creative-advertising-at-the-edinburgh-fringe/&amp;media=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/silver_paint-585x390.jpg&amp;description=Creative Advertising at the Edinburgh Fringe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            We see the strategy here. Cover yourself in silver paint and hope the glare attracts some attention.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1977" style="width: 595px">
           <a href="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/salt_bath.jpg" rel="lightbox">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Girl in tub of salt holding flyer" class="size-medium wp-image-1977" height="390" src="http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/salt_bath-585x390.jpg" width="585"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/creative-advertising-at-the-edinburgh-fringe/&amp;media=http://www.endlesslychanginghorizon.com/wp-content/uploads/salt_bath-585x390.jpg&amp;description=Creative Advertising at the Edinburgh Fringe')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Sitting in a tub of salt. Anything for your art, right?
           </p>
          </div>
          <div style="background-color: #c7e6e5; margin-bottom: 10px; padding: 10px;">
           <strong>
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             Update
            </span>
            August 18/13:
           </strong>
           <a href="http://laurenjunorcameron.tumblr.com" target="_blank" title="Lauren Junor Cameron's Website">
            Lauren Junor Cameron
           </a>
           , a freelance stage manager who is currently working the Edinburgh Fringe,
           <a href="http://www.reddit.com/r/Theatre/comments/1kish3/theatre_troops_have_to_resort_to_some_pretty/" target="_blank" title='Reddit discussion on "Creative Advertising"'>
            weighed in via Reddit
           </a>
           on this practice:
          </div>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <span style="font-size: 48px; color: #888888;">
            
           </span>
           Having just worked on a show at the Fringe (and about to do another for the last week), and had to flyer for it, let me tell you, its HARD trying to get an audience for your show, especially if youre one of the smaller productions (we were a two woman operation  the actor and I).
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           If youre not in one of the Big 4 venues, you have hardly anything for promotion  just posters, flyers, t-shirts, blood, sweat and tears. There is so much competition from so many shows, is it any wonder that people go to extreme measures to even be noticed by the public?
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           To be fair though, I think actually just talking to people about your show is more likely to get them in rather than lying in the street, holding flyers and expecting people to come take them from you.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           The people in white and covered in blood were nice though, they were in our venue before us and came to see our show. Most troupes knew how important it was to support each other. So I guess, dont judge until you work at the Fringe  it is a uniquely insane experience.
           <span style="font-size: x-large;">
            <br/>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <strong>
            Do you think creative adverting at the Fringe is necessary? Or does it go too far? Chime in below.
           </strong>
          </p>
