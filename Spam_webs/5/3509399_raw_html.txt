De-cluttering your home will bring innumerable benefits in the New Year and you will be surprised with the positive flow of energy this change will bring to your life. Prepare yourself for a serene year ahead with Feng Shui for your home.
           </p>

            Avoid the undesirable clutter of shoes, umbrellas and loose keys by making use of storage ideas on the entrance. Also allow a proper place designated for shoes (or better yet, a shoes cabinet!).Your guests will feel the super-charge of happiness and inner serenity this way.

           </p>

            Keep your kitchen tidy especially your stove. In Feng Shui, the kitchen stove represents health for you and other dwellers of the home. If this is not possible for you to place your workstations this way, then the next best thing you can do to reap the rewards of these two locations is to position your desk facing either of the two locations.
