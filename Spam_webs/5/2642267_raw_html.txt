        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Fruit Kebabs: A Sweet Take on Grilling
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-07-16T09:00:59+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          July 16, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://momstart.com/author/beeb/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Beeb
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://momstart.com/2014/07/fruit-kebabs-sweet-take-grilling/#comments">
           4 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <img alt="Fruit Kebabs" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-48735" height="365" src="http://momstart.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/FRUIT-KEBABS-3.jpg" title="Fruit Kebabs" width="550"/>
        </center>
        When you think of BBQ season, sweet treats arent necessarily the first thing that pops to mind. So I love this recipe that Maria Zoitas shared! Maria, creator of
        <a href="http://www.wmarketnyc.com/maria/index.html?jmid=1518&amp;j=308679723" target="_blank">
         Marias Homemade
        </a>
        , is a chef with some great ideas. This one is for grilling up pieces of fruit to make a dessert kebab:
        <br/>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <link href="http://momstart.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/FRUIT-KEBABS-3.jpg" itemprop="image"/>
         <div class="ERSSavePrint">
          <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
           <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://momstart.com/easyrecipe-print/48734-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Print
           </a>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Fruit Kebabs
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSDetails">
          <div class="ERSAuthor">
           Author:
           <span itemprop="author">
            Maria Zoitas
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSHead">
           Recipe type:
           <span itemprop="recipeCategory">
            Dessert
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSTimes">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1Lb. Watermelon cut into one inch cubes
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1Lb. Honeydew melon cut into one inch cubes
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1Lb. Cantaloupe cut into one inch cubes
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1Lb. Pineapple cut into one inch cubes
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             Cup of honey
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             Cup of unsalted pistachio shelled nuts, chopped roughly.
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            6 pieces wood skewers (6" long, soaked in water for 2-3 hours before use)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat grill at 400F.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Pass through the wood skewers the cubed fruit in whichever order you want and place them. onto the hot grill to create grill lines for about 20 seconds each side.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove and place them in an oval platter, drizzle the kebabs with honey and sprinkle the chopped pistachios on top and around the grilled fruit kebabs.
           </li>
          </ol>
