         <p>
          Things are getting so intense on
          <em>
           <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/abcs-secrets-and-lies" target="_blank" title="ABCs Secrets and Lies">
            ABCs Secrets and Lies
           </a>
          </em>
          and it seems like the evidence is pointing one way and then things shift for
          <b>
           Ben
          </b>
          (
          <strong>
           Ryan Phillippe
          </strong>
          ) and a new twist is brought to our attention. We had the big bomb of that video footage from
          <strong>
           Det. Cornell
          </strong>
          (
          <strong>
           Juliette Lewis
          </strong>
          ) last week, so where did things go from there? Ben and
          <strong>
           Christys
          </strong>
          marriage is falling apart and that takes center stage tonight on
          <em>
           Secrets and Lies
          </em>
          2015! Check out the details from The Son tonight on
          <em>
           Secrets and Lies
          </em>
          on ABC below in our
          <em>
           ABCs Secrets and Lies
          </em>
          recap!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/70494/abcs-secrets-and-lies-recap-the-son-ben-recalls-that-night/ryan-phillippe-4" rel="attachment wp-att-70562">
           <img alt="ABC's Secrets and Lies Spoilers - Ryan Phillippe" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-70562" height="333" src="http://gossipandgab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/ABCs-Secrets-and-Lies-Spoilers-Ryan-Phillippe1-500x333.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/69797/abcs-secrets-and-lies-recap-the-cop-ben-is-finally-arrested" target="_blank" title="ABCs Secrets and Lies Recap: The Cop  Ben Is Finally Arrested?">
           <strong>
            Last week on
            <em>
             Secrets and Lies
            </em>
            2015
           </strong>
          </a>
          , Ben was on a mission to find out dirt on Cornell. It appeared to be that Cornell was on a mission to get him to admit he was guilty, but it was all a coincidence. Nothing ever leads in the direction Ben needs it to go, as the reporter was just playing him and ended up getting arrested himself! Christy and Ben got into a huge fight and he told her to get out and she did! Then the episode ended with Cornell coming over and showing video footage from the taxi cab that had Ben walking over to Jess place the night of the murder, but Ben has always denied seeing Jess that night!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <em>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            <strong>
             Warning: This post contains spoilers from Secrets and Lies tonight on ABC, so stop reading now if you dont want to know what happened!!!
            </strong>
           </span>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          The next day Ben goes to see his attorney on Christmas Day and he said that Ben has to figure this out, as the video allows them to charge Ben with murder and if he cant figure out what happened then he cant testify.
         </p>
         <p>
          Now Ben will be on the hunt to figure out what happened, so he goes to
          <strong>
           Jess
          </strong>
          house to see if there is anything she is not telling him. Her sister is there and wants to go. Jess said there was something about her ex, but her sister interrupts and Jess said it is probably nothing.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Natalie
          </strong>
          calls Ben and said things are terrible and she wants to come home, but her mother interrupts. Christy talks to Ben and he wants to see the girls, but she is not working with him and does not want to see him. Then
          <strong>
           Dave
          </strong>
          comes over and he has some gifts for Ben, which happens to be the flashlight Ben ditched in the woods! He thought something might come out when Christy saw him, so he went and got it before the cops could find it! They go back to the spot that Ben was tortured and toss the flashlight, but is it for good this time???
         </p>
         <p>
          While they are out, Ben wants to see his kids and goes to Christys brothers house. Christy comes out and it gets ugly. She calls him a murder suspect and he said she is using the girls as a way of revenge. She calls the cops, but then they notice the girls up on the roof deck and
          <strong>
           Abby
          </strong>
          is crying. Way to make a scene, Christy!
         </p>
         <p>
          Back at home, Ben and Dave are talking and Ben is trying to remember what happened that night. We flashback to his argument with Christy that night and her asking if she is cheating on her with Jess. He now questions why she would ask that night. Dave said at Thanksgiving and playing football and they were touchy feely and he is dumb and even he noticed!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/70494/abcs-secrets-and-lies-recap-the-son/ryan-phillippe-dan-fogler" rel="attachment wp-att-70554">
           <img alt="RYAN PHILLIPPE, DAN FOGLER" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-70554" height="333" src="http://gossipandgab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/ABCs-Secrets-and-Lies-Spoilers-Ryan-Phillippe-and-Dan-Fogler-500x333.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Dave said he is all alone, but Ben said he will always be there for him. Dave said not for long and he did something and has been wanting to tell him, but hasnt because of all Ben is going through. What is it???? As it turns out, Dan thought that Ben was in a bad way that night and the alcohol was not working. Ben went to the bathroom and Dave slipped a drug in his drink. He didnt think the reaction would be as bad as it was. Ben is angry, but he said his heart was in the right place even if his head was up his ass.
         </p>
         <p>
          They flashback to the night and Dave said he was not anything like normal. Ben is normally quiet and together, but not that night. Ben was being drunk and unruly and said he was going home to fix it with Christy. Dave put him in a cab and let him go, but what happened when he got home???
         </p>
         <p>
          Dave makes a good point, as he said he went to Jess house and did not have his flashlight! I am thinking Christy was so pissed about Jess and figured it out about Tom before Ben found out and she did it.
         </p>
         <p>
          To help Ben remember, Dave and him go back to the bar to try to recreate things. The lady he was rude to that night is there and he buys her an apology drink. She says that Ben was waving a picture around oh his son, which happened to be the ultrasound of the baby that Christy aborted! Det. Cornell came to the bar and talked to people, so she thought the photo was of Tom and not the unborn baby. Ben then remembers calling Jess to warn her about Christy maybe being on the prowl the next day, which is the voicemail that Cornell played for him!
         </p>
         <p>
          The pieces are starting to come together, but then Ben said time to move on and they leave the bar. Ben said he needs to go alone now and then finds a tooth in his jacket. Um, what??? Dave said it is a small tooth, so is it Toms???
         </p>
         <p>
          Ben and his attorney are at the police station and he is there to tell Det. Cornell his side of the story, as the tooth jogged his memory on
          <em>
           ABCs Secrets and Lies
          </em>
          . He saw Toms bike in the driveway, so he went to go get it and then warn Jess about Christy. The door was unlocked and Jess was sleeping on the couch. Tom came out, so Ben went in to put him to bed. He lost a tooth and wanted to see if the tooth fairy came yet! So, Ben talks with him and then takes the tooth and puts money under the pillow for him. He then went home and fell asleep on the couch. However, they zoomed in on him locking and unlocking the door at Jess place, so that has to be something. Cornell said this doesnt eliminate him as a suspect, but Ben said that is fine and he knows he did not do it!
         </p>
         <p>
          We now see Ben go tell Jess about going there that night and it was him that put the money under Toms pillow, so it was not
          <strong>
           Scott
          </strong>
          , like she thought it could be.
         </p>
         <p>
          Back at home, Bens attorney calls and said he has a plan for their defense and they just need to have reasonable doubt. Christy knew something, since she asked about Jess that night. She knew it was six years ago. The attorney thinks Christy did it and Ben said it cant be, but the attorney said he even had doubt about it and a jury will too. He said that Christy is the one that paid the $25,000 to get him as Bens attorney and she will destroy him in divorce court because she has to have a lot more money.
         </p>
         <p>
          This puts Ben on the hunt and indeed he finds it, as he finds a safe in the attic and it is loaded with stacks of cash. There will be more twists, because it cant be Christy that did it with two episodes remaining! However, it looks like Cornell doesnt even think Ben did it from the previews for next week and she is on the prowl!
         </p>

          What did you think of tonights episode?
