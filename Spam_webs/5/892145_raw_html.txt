                    <meta content="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-YNaHswK2_WM/VGFdIFBN49I/AAAAAAAAFVc/MIRIXNoAPR8/s1600/banana%2Balmond%2Bquick%2Bbread.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Banana Almond Quick Bread Recipe
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                     <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="13108282">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4535930151399302859" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <span style="background-color: white; font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: xx-small;">
                      This post containsaffiliate
                     </span>
                     <span style="background-color: white; font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: xx-small;">
                      links. For more info, click
                     </span>
                     <a href="http://www.hoteatsandcoolreads.com/p/disclosure-and-privacy-policy.html" style="background-color: white; color: #f4794e; font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: small; text-decoration: none;" target="_blank">
                      here
                     </a>
                     <span style="background-color: white; font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: xx-small;">
                      !
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      Banana bread always reminds me of my childhood. Whenever we had ripe bananas around, my Mom always made a couple loaves. I never liked it with any kind of nuts when I was a kid, but now I love making different ways, with differentingredientsadded in.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-YNaHswK2_WM/VGFdIFBN49I/AAAAAAAAFVc/MIRIXNoAPR8/s1600/banana%2Balmond%2Bquick%2Bbread.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-YNaHswK2_WM/VGFdIFBN49I/AAAAAAAAFVc/MIRIXNoAPR8/s1600/banana%2Balmond%2Bquick%2Bbread.jpg" width="456"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <a data-pin-color="red" data-pin-config="above" data-pin-do="buttonPin" data-pin-height="28" href="https://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.hoteatsandcoolreads.com%2F2014%2F11%2Fbanana-almond-quick-bread-recipe.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2F3.bp.blogspot.com%2F-YNaHswK2_WM%2FVGFdIFBN49I%2FAAAAAAAAFVc%2FMIRIXNoAPR8%2Fs1600%2Fbanana%2Balmond%2Bquick%2Bbread.jpg&amp;description=Great%20breakfast%20or%20snack!%20Banana%20Almond%20Quick%20Bread%20from%20Hot%20Eats%20and%20Cool%20Reads!">
                       <img src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pinit_fg_en_rect_red_28.png"/>
                      </a>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                      I love the almonds and almond extract that's added in this loaf. I like adding almond into any baked goods. It adds such a wonderful flavor. This bread is best with some butter slathered on top for breakfast or snack! Enjoy!
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      Banana Almond Quick Bread (
                      <b>
                       <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/hoteatsandcoolreads/banana-almond-quick-bread-from-hot-eats-and-cool-reads?tmpl=%2Fsystem%2Fapp%2Ftemplates%2Fprint%2F&amp;showPrintDialog=1" target="_blank">
                        click here to print
                       </a>
                      </b>
                      )
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      What you need:
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      1 cup sugar
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      1/2 cup butter, softened
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      2 large eggs
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      3 tablespoons buttermilk
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      1 teaspoon almond extract
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      3 large bananas
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      1 teaspoon baking soda
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      1 teaspoon baking powder
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      2 cups flour
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      1/2 cup sliced almonds
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      Directions:
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      Cream together the sugar, butter and eggs until combined. Then mix in the buttermilk, almond extract and bananas until combined. After that, mix in the soda, powder and flour until combined. Last, mix in the almonds.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      Pour into a greased bread pan and bake for 1 hour, until toothpick inserted comes out clean. Cool then remove from pan.
                     </span>
