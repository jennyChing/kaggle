&lt;p&gt;Day 2 of the carpet tear-out revealed much nicer wood floors in our living room. The carpet removal is the easy part to this job. Once the carpet is gone, you are left with the tack strips that run along the wall and the staples that were holding down the carpet pad. Oh and while I was there, I took down the wall paper. All the paper on this wall came off in about 1 minute. Luckily for us, this was the only wall in the house with wall paper.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Below is what a tack strip looks like nice and new!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.homedepot.com/webapp/wcs/stores/servlet/ProductDisplay?storeId=10051&amp;productId=100204165&amp;langId=-1&amp;catalogId=10053&amp;ci_sku=100204165&amp;ci_src=14110944&amp;cm_mmc=shopping-_-googlebase-_-D23X-_-100204165&amp;locStoreNum=3810&amp;marketID=139" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6496" title="ROBERTS Extra-Wide Carpet Tack Strip" src="http://www.handmadespark.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/tack-strip.png" alt="ROBERTS Extra-Wide Carpet Tack Strip" width="350" height="288" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Old tack strips!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;br class="spacer_" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;br class="spacer_" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;a href="http://makemethod.vox.com/library/post/diy-how-to-rip-up-old-carpeting.html" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="size-full wp-image-6497" title="tack strip" src="http://www.handmadespark.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/tack1.jpg" alt="tack strip" width="500" height="332" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;br class="spacer_" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;br class="spacer_" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;So here is what the floors looked like after we pulled the carpet.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.handmadespark.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/0609001702.jpg"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-6499" title="living room | DIY Home Renovations" src="http://www.handmadespark.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/0609001702-1024x768.jpg" alt="living room | DIY Home Renovations" width="504" height="378" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This floor will be sanded, stained then finished with a clear polyurethane.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Here was my favorite tool of the day! Cat&#8217;s Claw / Molding Prybar&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.acehardwareoutlet.com/productdetails.aspx?sku=2122257&amp;source=GoogleBase" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6502" title="Dasco 101-0 Molding Claw Bar 10&quot;" src="http://www.handmadespark.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/Cats-Claw.png" alt="Dasco 101-0 Molding Claw Bar 10&quot;" width="371" height="492" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This tool is great for pulling the nails from the tack strip as you can see from the picture below, but it also does a number on getting under the tack strip and pulling off larger sections at a time.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.handmadespark.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/cats.png"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6500" title="living room | DIY Home Renovations" src="http://www.handmadespark.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/cats.png" alt="living room | DIY Home Renovations" width="416" height="455" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
