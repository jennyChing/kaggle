     <h1>
      Comfortable and Stylish Travel Clothes for Women (awesome travel pants!)
     </h1>
     <div class="meta">
      <span>
       By
       <a href="http://www.ytravelblog.com/author/cazcraig/" rel="author" title="Posts by Caz">
        Caz
       </a>
      </span>
      <span class="date">
       Wednesday, August 13th, 2014
      </span>
      <span class="comments">
       26 Comments
      </span>
     </div>
     <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
     <p style="text-align: right;">
      This post is brought to you by Anatomie Style
     </p>
     <p>
      Ive not been one for fashion  not because I dont want to look a million dollars, but because I dont know how.
     </p>
     <p>
      Im clueless when it comes time to choose clothes that match, or fit, my body style. Dressing for me has never been a priority for looking good  merely a practicality for getting myself around the world.
     </p>
     <p>
      But, the longer Ive lived this life (I dont want to say the older Im getting) the more I want to break free and try something new actually wearing clothes that maybe look good on me, but more importantly feel good  and are still practical for travel.
     </p>
     <p>
      I did not understand just how uncomfortable, heavy and suffocating my pair of black jeans were until I put on my new pair of black pants. I cant believe I put my body through wearing them for so long!
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.ytravelblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Travel-clothes-for-women1.jpg">
       <img alt="Travel clothes for women" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-44265" height="710" src="http://www.ytravelblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Travel-clothes-for-women1-532x710.jpg" width="532"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <h2>
      Skyler Travel Pants
     </h2>
     <p>
      Im now in love with these pants.
     </p>
     <p>
      I could barely feel them when I put them on. I didnt get muffin tops from them, I could freely move around, and they were so soft and lightweight.
     </p>
     <p>
      I walked straight over to my black jeans and trashed them. There was no way I could ever go back to them after putting on these black pants from
      <a href="https://anatomiestyle.com/shop/?ap_id=ytravelblog">
       Anatomie.
      </a>
      Itd be like going back to milkshakes after youve had a nourishing Mother Earth smoothie. They just dont compare and I wont put my body through that discomfort anymore.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.ytravelblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Anatomie-style-white-travel-dress.jpg">
       <br/>
      </a>
      <a href="http://www.ytravelblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Anatomie-travel-pants.jpg">
       <img alt="Anatomie travel pants" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-44230" height="710" src="http://www.ytravelblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Anatomie-travel-pants-473x710.jpg" width="473"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      When Anatomie contacted me about reviewing the travel pants, I didnt quite understand the excitement over them  theyre just a pair of pants. And then I wore them and I realised the difference quality fabric and fitting makes.
     </p>
     <h4>
      Heres what I love about the
      <a href="https://anatomiestyle.com/shop/skyler-skinny-pants/?ap_id=ytravelblog">
       Anatomie Skyler travel pants
      </a>
     </h4>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Theyre lightweight and ultra thin.Made from super-light-weight Nylon-Lycra Micro Fiber fabric.Jeans weigh a ton, especially when wet.
      </li>
      <li>
       Thefabric is water resistant.
      </li>
      <li>
       They are just super comfortable  breathable and strechy
      </li>
      <li>
       Thetravel pantscome out of the washing machine feeling almost dry. They only took 1-2 hours to dry hanging up inside!
      </li>
      <li>
       They also come out of the washing machine wrinkled, yet when I put them on the wrinkles fell out pretty quickly.
      </li>
      <li>
       They dont give muffin tops, or wedgies, nor make my but feel like its Kim Kardashian size. It feels more compact and firmer. (Note  I either messed up with my measurements (likely) or I lost a bit of weight between measuring and receiving the pants as they are a little big on me (possible  I fluctuate a lot!). Still super comfortable and I love them, but theyd be better a bit closer fitting. measure yourself correctly and youll be fine!)
      </li>
      <li>
       Theyre long legged. Are there any tall people in the house who feel my pain? Damn I hate finding a nice pair of pants that stop just above my ankle. These pants fold comfortably over my shoes  love it.
      </li>
      <li>
       They dont take up any space in my luggage  unlike the jeans which take up almost half a packing cube.
      </li>
      <li>
       Theyre really smooth and silky to touch.
      </li>
      <li>
       Theyre multi- purpose. The travel pants have replaced my tracksuit pants for lazing around in on a cold evening  yes even though these travel pants are light weight, they are deceptively warm  not like jeans that let the cold seep through. I wear them out during the day for sight seeing  perfectly comfortable and breathable. I can dress the travel pants up of an evening with a pair of heels and a nice shirt. The pants are what I usually reach for for a day of A to B travelling  nothing worse than feeling uncomfortable in your clothes when youre already uncomfortable sitting all day in a plane, car or bus.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      They are expensive, but if you can, the investment is worth it.
     </p>
     <p>
      You get a pair of pants that will suit many different outfits, they look great, they feel even better on, and they take up less space in your suitcase.
     </p>
     <p>
      The only thing I didnt like was they seemed to catch the dirt quite easily, but could be easily wiped off with a baby wipe. The material makes for easy cleaning. Mind you I did wear them out walking in the rain, not realizing it was going to be really muddy and Savannah jumped up on me with muddy shoes. So I dont think that would be a normal catch the dirt thing!
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Undara lava tubes (1)" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-44231" height="473" src="http://www.ytravelblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Undara-lava-tubes-1-710x473.jpg" width="710"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      They arecomfortable to hike in, more so than my made for hiking Kathmandu pants! (I wouldnt wear them for hiking though, this was a caught-unawares, unplanned short hike during transit. Id definitely keep them for the nice, more comfortable sight-seeing experiences.)
     </p>
     <p>

     <p>
      Anatomie has a range of different travel pant styles to suit your body shape and likesand has a great feature to help you choose the right pants style to match your body type.

     <h2>
      Monika Ballerina Dress
     </h2>
     <p>
      <img alt="Anatomie style white dress" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-44232" height="504" src="http://www.ytravelblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Anatomie-style-white-dress-710x504.jpg" width="710"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Whos afraid of wearing a white dress?
     </p>
     <p>
      After what I did to my wedding dress, Ive avoided wearing white. Theres not a drop of red wine, or grass that doesnt want to stain me. And then theres the whole thing about white making you look bigger.
     </p>
     <p>
      But, my friend Brenda, a stylist whos been in the fashion industry for years, picked it as something I needed to wear. I was a little worried, but I have to say she knows what shes talking about. It really hides those not so flattering parts of my body!
     </p>
     <p>
      I really love
      <a href="https://anatomiestyle.com/shop/monika-ballerina-dress/?ap_id=ytravelblog">
       this dress
      </a>
      . Its perfect for those evening dinners. I wear my yellow sunflower flats for something a bit casual, or dress it up with heels for a little more fancy. Its perfect for tropical sunsets or even lunches on the Riviera or some shopping.
     </p>
     <p>
      Its perfectly paired with a glass of white wine and an outback view dont you think?
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.ytravelblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Anatomie-style-white-travel-dress.jpg">
       <img alt="Anatomie style white travel dress" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-44229" height="710" src="http://www.ytravelblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Anatomie-style-white-travel-dress-499x710.jpg" width="499"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      I sometimes leave the family to speak at conferences and events. This dress will now be my new speakers outfit. When youre travelling its important to pack minimal clothes that have multiple uses.
     </p>
     <p>
      Just like the pants, its made from super comfortable fabric  jersey cotton knit and I dont feel frumpy in it.
     </p>
     <h2>
      Isadora Cotton Tank
     </h2>
     <p>
      Again this
      <a href="https://anatomiestyle.com/shop/isadora/?ap_id=ytravelblog">
       travel cotton tank
      </a>
      wins on comfort hands down. The material is so soft and smooth. Theres no scratchiness or shirt crawling up your back. It just sits to the shape of your body.
     </p>
     <p>
      This top really suits more active exploration or just a laze around comfort afternoon. I LOVE the bright yellow colour and it goes so well with my sunflower flats and travel pants.
     </p>

     <h2>
      About Anatomie Style
     </h2>
     <p>
      I like
      <a href="https://anatomiestyle.com/shop/?ap_id=ytravelblog" style="color: #2a6496;">
       Anatomie
      </a>
      .Its a designer brand range, somight be outside some budgets. Itsvery well suited to business travellers and those who leisure seekers who love to look good  especially if theyre walking the streets of Paris or Rome.
     </p>
     <p>
      The designers do have a background in designing for athletes so I love how a lot of their range does also suit the adventurous or sporty travellers.Its probably on par with what youd spend buying quality clothes from Kathmandu or North Face. But I like the style and comfort of these clothes better.
     </p>
     <p>
      The clothes feel very European in style and Im thinking some of these outfits will go really well when we explore New York City on our US road trip next year!
     </p>
     <p>
      Instead of stuffing my suitcase with a bunch of clothes I dont really like and avoid wearing because theyre uncomfortable, Im going to do the less is more approach.
     </p>
     <p>
      Buy clothes that look and feel better, that last longer and can mix and match well. It might cost me more initially but will save me more in the long run as Ill have quality clothes that take up less space and hassle in my suitcase.
     </p>

      Ive now got my eye on a gorgeous coat and another travel dress thats super pretty and yellow!
