        My favorite grocery shopping app + a $50 Winco giveaway!
       </h1>

         April 30, 2014
        </span>

        <span class="author vcard">

        </p>
        <p>
         Do you remember what your favorite cereal was when you were a kid?
         <br/>
         I still remember how good a box of Trix smelled (like sweet lemons!) when you opened them,
         <br/>
         and reading the back of the box, while devouring my breakfast.
         <br/>
         I think my all time favorite has to be Cheerios there was just something
         <br/>
         about a bowl of cereal that always started the day right. My kids love them now too, but
         <br/>
         its a close tie with Lucky Charms depending on who you ask!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-He65p_OeIZY/U0v5Q-YYDmI/AAAAAAAAKVE/p93qB5GP9aw/s1600/cheerio+post+1.jpg">
          <img alt="" border="0" data-orig-src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-He65p_OeIZY/U0v5Q-YYDmI/AAAAAAAAKVE/p93qB5GP9aw/s1600/cheerio+post+1.jpg" height="571" src="https://images-blogger-opensocial.googleusercontent.com/gadgets/proxy?url=http%3A%2F%2F2.bp.blogspot.com%2F-He65p_OeIZY%2FU0v5Q-YYDmI%2FAAAAAAAAKVE%2Fp93qB5GP9aw%2Fs1600%2Fcheerio%2Bpost%2B1.jpg&amp;container=blogger&amp;gadget=a&amp;rewriteMime=image%2F*" width="468"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         I learned about the
         <a href="https://ibotta.com/app/retailer/39">
          Ibotta
         </a>
         app recently, which saves time looking for coupons and deals, plus it
         <br/>
         pays me back every time I use it. Featured on shows like Good Morning America, ABC News &amp; Katie Couric,
         <br/>
         this cool app is the #1 choice for savvy shoppers looking to earn rebates, rewards, coupons, deals and
         <br/>
         discounts that can all be turned into cash, with the ease of using your mobile device.
         <span id="more-1089">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         My weekend routine includes a stop at my favorite place to shop for groceries, WinCo.
         <br/>
         This week I took a selfie to show you where I was and tell you more about how much I saved.
         <br/>
         Check out Ibotta on your mobile phone for weekly General Mills offers at
         <a href="http://wincofoods.com/">
          WinCo
         </a>
         . Each week from
         <br/>
         now until 5/25 there will be a featured cereal of the week that you can redeem for $1.50. If you stock up
         <br/>
         and buy 5 cereals you can redeem an additional $2.50! Cereals included in this offer are Cinnamon Toast
         <br/>
         Crunch, Golden Grahams, Dark Chocolate Multigrain Cheerios, Honey Nut Cheerios, Lucky Charms, and
         <br/>
         Fiber One Protein. Save $14.50 on your favorite cereals, not bad! For more information visit
         <br/>
         Ibottas blog at:
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          <span style="font-size: 9.0pt; font-family: 'Calibri','sans-serif'; color: #4180ff;">
           <a href="https://ibotta.com/app/retailer/39" target="_blank">
            https://ibotta.com/app/
            <wbr>
            </wbr>
            retailer/39
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-SsCrm9CMGcw/U0uEuqZrWXI/AAAAAAAAKU0/rF_3yL9mYbk/s1600/winco+1.jpg">
          <img alt="" border="0" data-orig-src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-SsCrm9CMGcw/U0uEuqZrWXI/AAAAAAAAKU0/rF_3yL9mYbk/s1600/winco+1.jpg" height="400" src="https://images-blogger-opensocial.googleusercontent.com/gadgets/proxy?url=http%3A%2F%2F1.bp.blogspot.com%2F-SsCrm9CMGcw%2FU0uEuqZrWXI%2FAAAAAAAAKU0%2FrF_3yL9mYbk%2Fs1600%2Fwinco%2B1.jpg&amp;container=blogger&amp;gadget=a&amp;rewriteMime=image%2F*" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         And when we arrived home, and all the groceries were put away, we sat down and each had
         <br/>
         a mid-afternoon snack that hit the spot. Do you ever get busy and end up having a bowl of
         <br/>
         cereal on those nights that dinner is just not going to happen? We do sometimes, and cereal
         <br/>
         seems to be the food my family asks for most often. Which makes me feel good, knowing that
         <br/>
         they are getting fiber, and calcium while they are enjoying their favorite cereal too.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-nfkn3dv6fiU/U0v5cihb6uI/AAAAAAAAKVM/LPk3g3UtF_Y/s1600/cheerio+post+2.jpg">
          <img alt="" border="0" data-orig-src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-nfkn3dv6fiU/U0v5cihb6uI/AAAAAAAAKVM/LPk3g3UtF_Y/s1600/cheerio+post+2.jpg" height="640" src="https://images-blogger-opensocial.googleusercontent.com/gadgets/proxy?url=http%3A%2F%2F3.bp.blogspot.com%2F-nfkn3dv6fiU%2FU0v5cihb6uI%2FAAAAAAAAKVM%2FLPk3g3UtF_Y%2Fs1600%2Fcheerio%2Bpost%2B2.jpg&amp;container=blogger&amp;gadget=a&amp;rewriteMime=image%2F*" width="468"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So would you love to win a $50 giftcard for WinCo?
         <br/>
         Just use the rafflecopter below to enter!
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>

         and
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/winco.official.page">
          facebook,

         and save the Ibotta app too for your next trip to the store!!
