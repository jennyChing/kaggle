          Featured Artist: Kali Jani Song: Permission to Go @Kali_Jani
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="post-image">
         <img alt="KJ5" class="attachment-full wp-post-image" height="1280" src="http://www.phoenixstar9online.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/KJ5.jpeg" width="855"/>
        </div>
        <div class="entry clearfix">
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <span class="author-name">

          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.phoenixstar9online.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/KJ5.jpeg">
            <img alt="KJ5" class="aligncenter wp-image-4639" height="599" src="http://www.phoenixstar9online.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/KJ5.jpeg" width="400"/>

          <p>
           I REALLY love Kalis style from the music to the fashion. The track Permission To Go is very different from the majority of these songs I have been hearing lately. The video visual itself is enough to make you sit down to hear what she is singing about,but the life she brings to the song is what makes you really listen.
          </p>
          <p>
           Website:http://www.kalijani.com/
          </p>
          <p>
           Twitter: http://www.twitter.com/kali_jani
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-weight: normal; color: #222222;">
            <strong>
             About the Artist:
            </strong>
            <em>
             Kali Jani is the hot new singer on the scene out of Orlando, Florida, who recently released her debut single Permission to Go, a lyrics driven track with a hot mixture of pop and urban flavor.
            </em>
           </span>
           <br style="font-weight: normal; color: #222222;"/>
           <br style="font-weight: normal; color: #222222;"/>
           <em>
            <span style="font-weight: normal; color: #222222;">
             Born Simone Farrell in Boston Massachusetts, she grew-up in Poinciana, Florida, and earned a BA in Criminology from the University of South Florida, in 2012.
            </span>
           </em>
           <br style="font-weight: normal; color: #222222;"/>
           <br style="font-weight: normal; color: #222222;"/>
           <em>
            <span style="font-weight: normal; color: #222222;">
             The stage name Kali Jani has significant meaning. Kali means lively and fierce, while Jani translates into God has answered. Jani believes that now is her time.
            </span>
           </em>
          </p>
