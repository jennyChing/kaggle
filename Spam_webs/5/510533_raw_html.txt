                  <img alt="iNEMI Roadmap Webinar - North America and Europe Regions" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2654" height="216" src="http://www.pannam.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/iNEMI-Roadmap-Webinar-North-America-and-Europe-Regions.png" width="500"/>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p>
                 The International Electronics Manufacturing Initiative (iNEMI) is a non-profit research and development consortium comprised of leading electronics manufacturers, suppliers, associations, government agencies, and universities. iNEMIs mission is to forecast and accelerate improvements in the electronics manufacturing industry for a sustainable future, and one of the ways they address this mission is by hosting roadmap webinars featuring prominent OEMs to forecast their business and technical needs in various product sectors. This webinar, to be held April 9 at 11AM EDT, is 90 minutes in length and features speakers Bill Bader, Fred Sporon-Fiedler, Jim Spall, Dan Gamota, Michael Gaitan, and Matthew Chalkley. Participate in the webinar and access forecasts relating to membrane switch technology needs.
