        <p>
         <em>
          A Pioneer Woman Parody by
         </em>
         <a href="http://www.minivan-momma.com/" target="_blank">
          Heather Davis
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5703" height="400" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/eg-1024x768.jpg" style="padding: 5px; margin: 0 0 8px 0; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="eg" width="520"/>
         <br/>
         On Saturday mornings, when we raise our heads at the crack of dawn (
         <em>
          or about 3 hours afterwards
         </em>
         ), we like to have a nice family breakfast (
         <em>
          when were lucky enough to have all the ingredients together  otherwise its each man for himself!
         </em>
         ). We start with gathering the eggs. Expiration dates? Did you know there are expiration dates on eggs? Really? You did? I had no clue. But I say, screw it! Theyre pasteurized, right? Besides, if I get sick, the stay in the hospital will be a nice break  Im just keeping it real, here!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="sausage" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5714" height="395" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/sau.png" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="sausage" width="520"/>
         <br/>
         Lucky us! We found some sausage! The expiration date is very important on sausage; however, it was on the part of the package that is no longer with the sausage. There were no fumes or green tints, so we used it. This sausage is not organic or freshly ground. I dont believe in actually making my own sausage. I do believe in buying the cheapest, fattiest sausage I can find at Hellmart, which I live just down the street from. Im just keeping it real here!
         <span id="more-5698">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5724" height="375" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/bac.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="bac" width="500"/>
         <br/>
         I must say that I am totally loyal to this brand of bacon. Honestly. Its the cheapest stuff out there. As long as I cannot find anything else cheaper, this will be my brand of choice. Nothing but the best for my family, I always say. Unless the best costs more than I can save using a coupon, then all bets are off. They dont need good bacon. I mean, theyre just going to eat it. Im just keeping it real here!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="biscuits" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5723" height="374" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/bc.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="biscuits" width="500"/>
         <br/>
         My hubbys in charge of the biscuits (
         <em>
          and the whole rest of the meal because he really has been up since dawn
         </em>
         ). I would totally prepare the entire meal for my family, but that would require a couple of things that Im just not willing to give up: 1. Im not willing to wake up that early to make breakfast at breakfast time. And 2. If I were to spend all that time cooking breakfast, Id lose valuable Facebook time. Madmens status update will not like itself. But, just so you know, my hubby and I both know how to make REAL biscuits. But, seriously, why waste that time and effort MAKING the darned things when you can pop open the can and VOILA! BISCUITS!?!? Im just keeping it real!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5713" height="356" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/lo.png" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="lo" width="491"/>
         <br/>
         Making our meals is a family affair, and the kids love to get involved. My youngest daughter, for example, likes to be right in the mix of our family activities. On this particular morning, she crawled right up on the counter and dragged her nail polish with her. My husband was all like, No. No fingernail polish on the counter. JeezCould you image, though, breakfast without your nails done? (
         <em>
          Notice the nice pattern: pink, pink, purple, purple, pink
         </em>
         .) Sometimes, my hubby is a killjoy. Who doesnt just melt at the aroma of nail polish mixed with canned biscuits  MMMmmmmmmmmmm  it makes my belly wiggle  it really doesnt take much to do that, though. A good laugh, moving quickly, picking up socks from the floor. All those make my belly wiggle, too. Im just keeping it real here!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5727" height="375" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/bot.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="bot" width="500"/>
         <br/>
         Back to breakfast: OK  its really important to get the can of 10-biscuits on the baking stone before you do anything else so that they can be in your way while youre doing everything else. Just set them right in the middle of your stove and forget about em! Except when you keep bumping stuff into the pan. Its a good vocabulary lesson: Teaching your children fifty different versions of dammit first thing in the morning is just good learning. Im just keepin it real, babies!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5722" height="372" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/sp.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="sp" width="496"/>
         <br/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5711" height="399" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/pp.jpg" style="padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="pp" width="400"/>
         <br/>
         And, please, for the love of all thats rich: Do not waste your money on new pans. When one has a handle to break off, just head over to my moms house. She has
         <span style="font-size: 160%;">
          1,000s
         </span>
         of pans and eats out for every meal. Shell never know when one is missing. Then, just take it home and let your kids scratch the hell out of it with metal utensils. Shell never believe it was hers. Besides, Teflon is not bad for you, right? What? It is? Who says and since when? What the hell Im just keeping it real!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5730" height="300" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/mh.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="mh" width="400"/>
         <br/>
         Next thing you need to do is set the biscuits out on the stone. What? Theyre already on the stone? OK, then. Just take a moment to look at that hand. Its a mans hand. Theres nothing sexier than a man who cooks breakfast. BUT, youve got to keep your night gown on while taking pictures. Trust me  if youre all skin exposed then you could get bacon greased popped onto placed where bacon grease should not be popped. Even EL James drew the line at bacon grease in her bestseller
         <em>
          Fifty Shades of Emo Sex
         </em>
         . Plus, if youre naked in the kitchen, your daughter may just paint pictures on your skin with her fingernail polish. I would assume. Not that Ive ever had a heart painted on my ass. BUT, if you do stay in your nightgown, your hubby will think its foreplay and go along with your little shenanigans. Hell probably cook the whole meal if you just keep saying things like, Mmmmm Baby knows I like it scrambled. Im just keeping it real here, peeps!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5719" height="464" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/rw.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="rw" width="348"/>
         <br/>
         Occasionally, just turn the water on and let it run. Thats right. For no good reason. Its a natural resource. Its not like well run out or anything, right? Ive seen the water towers in town and they still look full to me. Also, keep a bottle of dish soap by your sink. It will give the impression that you actually do the dishes. And impressions are so much more important than actual deeds.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5731" height="360" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/sau-h.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="sau-h" width="480"/>
         <br/>
         Since theres not much sausage left, be sure to fashion them into weird shapes so your children wont eat them. That way there will be more for you and your sexy man whos cooking you breakfast. Thats right, work that sausage Im just keeping it real, honey!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5732" height="480" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/cs.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="cs" width="360"/>
         <br/>
         Again. Im totally a name-brand freak in that why in the WORLD would I pay one whole dollar more (
         <em>
          Im being serious here
         </em>
         !) for the EXACT SAME no-stick spray? So, I dont. I buy the Great Value brand because, well, its a great value. And, well, Im totally cheap. Also, that whole aerosol vs the ozone business: Its not my problem. I mean Im just one person and I only use the spray when Im cooking. Lets face it its not THAT often.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5733" height="312" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/lo2.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="lo2" width="416"/>
         <br/>
         See, again, breakfast is a family affair. My first born smelled the bread and meandered into the kitchen. Bread, after all, is her food group of choice AND, shes out to prove that man CAN INDEED live on bread alone. And jelly. Bread and jelly. Eventually her eating habits will land her on an episode of 20/20. Im just keeping it real!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5721" height="372" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/cb.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="cb" width="496"/>
         <br/>
         See this cutting board? I got it at IKEA. It cost $3. I didnt pay for it; my mom did. My mom  she rocks that way. See, we had my daughters with us and, as most people know, my daughters are good only in small doses. So by the time we had found our way from the ultra cool kitchen gadgets to the exit, my mom was pretty much done with the constant Can we have this? How does this work? Is it supposed to bend this way? that she told me to just take the kids and go find some traffic to play in. I tried to give her some money, but she said it was totally worth it for me to just go. Of course, I didnt just leave her to fend for herself; I tossed a couple of bamboo breakfast trays in the cart before chasing the girls through Ikeas grocery section. Im just keeping it real.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5734" height="372" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/bf.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="bf" width="496"/>
         <br/>
         Finally, breakfast is on the table. The bacon apparently magically cooked itself. I think I might have been watching reruns of
         <em>
          Friends
         </em>
         at the time. Breakfast was taking so long to cook and I was getting weak. I cant be expected to actually stay standing up after Ive just slept 10 straight hours. Also, I think its important for every family to keep their piggy banks and finger nail polish on the table for most meals. Try it. I think youll find it surprisingly in your way! Im just keeping it real!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5720" height="448" src="http://funnynotslutty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/rw2.jpg" style="margin: 19px 0 8px 0; padding: 5px; border: 1px solid #ddd;" title="rw2" width="336"/>
         <br/>
         Again, dont forget to randomly run water.
        </p>
