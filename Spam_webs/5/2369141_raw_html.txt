         </a>
        </h1>
        <div class="post-meta">
         Posted by
         <a href="/blog/category/contributors/allison-norton/">
          Allison Norton
         </a>
         <time class="post-time" datetime="2013-10-04T04:00:00+00:00">
          October 4th, 2013
         </time>
        </div>
        <div class="post-copy">
         <p>
          If theres anything Team LC loves in a fall outfit, its a touch of personality that makes it unique. We admire a look that has an interesting twistwhether that means wearing a special piece of jewelry thats been passed down through your family or mixing unexpected prints and patterns. Well, today were spotlighting one LaurenConrad.com member who knows how to add some personality to her outfit of the day
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          Congrats to
          <a href="http://laurenconrad.com/user/1336612/profile" target="_blank">
           The Mrs. Gibby
          </a>
           We love your foxy fall outfityou are our newest
          <a href="/blog/category/member-spotlight" target="_blank">
           <em>
            Chic of the Week
           </em>
          </a>
          !
          <br/>
          <img alt="Chic of the Week: A Foxy Fall Outfit" class="aligncenter" height="775" src="/wp-content/uploads/s3photo/zNJmNFgUW7dNTLWVumzWEvop.jpeg" title="Chic of the Week: A Foxy Fall Outfit" width="500"/>
          <br/>
          This member added all the right touches to her transitional outfit. Heres what she said about this look:
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           We first saw the animal sweater trend last fall and its back again this year. The fox print sweaters get me every time and this one from Ann Taylor may be the cutest one I have seen yet.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          We adore our
          <em>
           Chic
          </em>
          s foxy sweaterits the perfect unique touch to a summer-to-fall outfit! Congrats!
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Do you have what it takes to be our next
           <em>
            Chic of the Week
           </em>
           ?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>

          <br/>
