        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <img alt="" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-9499" height="223" src="http://www.shibleysmiles.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/Screen-Shot-2011-09-17-at-10.05.42-PM-300x239.png" title="Screen Shot 2011-09-17 at 10.05.42 PM" width="281"/>
           Pumpkin Face Mask
          </span>
         </strong>
         <br/>
         2 tsp canned pureed pumpkin
         <br/>
         1/2 tsp honey
         <br/>
         1/4 tsp heavy whipping cream
         <br/>
         Mix all ingredients and apply to face. Leave on for 10 min and rinse
        </p>
        <p>
         Why: Pumpkin is rich in vitamin A, which is very healing to the skin and is essential to maintain and repair skin tissue. It also helps reduce lines and wrinkles. Honey is a humectant. The cream moisturizes and helps flaky skin.
        </p>
