       <h1 class="title posttitle">
        Party Pleasing Apple Recipe Roundup
       </h1>
       <!-- Following line modified by MSC to add author profile link -->
       <div class="post-meta">

        <h2>
         Apple Recipes for Your Next Party
        </h2>
        <h2>
         <img alt="Apple-Cinnamon-Sugar-Cookie-Bars-2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13477" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Apple-Cinnamon-Sugar-Cookie-Bars-2.jpg" width="601"/>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Earlier this autumn, my roommates and I went apple picking. Since were not always the greatest of planners, we picked approximately 55 pounds of apples between the four of us. For the next few weeks, the apples took over our entire kitchen, and the question became,
         <em>
          what on earth do we do with nearly 60 pounds of apples?
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         The answer came to us in pieces  some we gave away, some went into pies, many of them found a home in a delicious apple crumble, we snacked on some, a few even went into potato latkes, and we used some to create an improvised sangria. In the end, we used all but 11 apples, which reached the end of their useable life before we were able to eat them.
        </p>
        <p>
         So now, its your turn. With the holiday season fast approaching, were sure youll soon be headed to plenty of family gatherings and holiday parties. And that, of course, means taking enough dessert to feed a large group of people. So check out these tasty, shareable apple desserts and get inspired to do some holiday season baking.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://www.lemonsforlulu.com/2013/09/03/apple-cinnamon-sugar-cookie-bars/" style="color: #bf8221;">
            Apple Cinnamon Sugar Cookie Bars
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via Lemons for Lulu
        </p>
        <p>
         These dessert bars from our blogger friend Tanya shown above, are perfect for sharing. These are light, apple-y, and delicious! We love that the base is a sugar cookie in bar form.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="cider_muffins_2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13478" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/cider_muffins_2.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://www.kailleyskitchen.com/2014/09/10/sparkling-cider-cinnamon-chip-muffins/" style="color: #bf8221;">
            Sparkling Cider Cinnamon Chip Muffins
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via Kailleys Kitchen
        </p>
        <p>
         You wont find bits of apple in these tiny muffins  they feature sparkling cider  but they look perfect for a sweet, mini morsel!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="apple-fritter-bake-101" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13479" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/apple-fritter-bake-101.jpg" width="461"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://wonkywonderful.com/1/post/2014/04/apple-fritter-breakfast-casserole.html" style="color: #bf8221;">
            Apple Fritter Breakfast Casserole
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via Wonky Wonderful
        </p>
        <p>
         My birthday falls right around Thanksgiving, and my mom is always scrambling to find something to serve for breakfast brunchI know what shes making this year! This casserole combines flaky croissants, apple butter and fresh apples. Sweet glaze optional.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Serioulsy Delish- Apple Crisp_2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11133" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Serioulsy-Delish-Apple-Crisp_2.jpg" width="601"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         We love
         <strong>
          Jessica Merchant
         </strong>
         s sweet and savory
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://www.bakepedia.com/white-cheddar-apple-crisp/" style="color: #bf8221;" target="_blank" title="White Cheddar Apple Crisp">
            White Cheddar Apple Crisp
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         . Like apple pie with a cheddar crust but so much easier to put together  and she puts the cheese in the filling so it gets melty and gooey! Bake in a pretty casserole dish to take to a party.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="AppleStreuselBread" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13480" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/AppleStreuselBread.jpg" width="284"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://www.runningtothekitchen.com/2013/09/apple-streusel-bread/" style="color: #bf8221;">
            Apple Streusel Bread
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via Running to the Kitchen
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre looking for something thats on the healthier side, this is the perfect recipe for you. The recipe incorporates flax seed, whole wheat pastry flour healthy coconut oil and is gently sweetened. We have to do what we can to try to avoid putting on all those extra holiday pounds, right?
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Apple-Platz-15" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13481" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Apple-Platz-15.jpg" width="284"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://www.foodfaithfitness.com/apple-dessert-recipe/" style="color: #bf8221;">
            Grandmas Apple Dessert Recipe (Platz)
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via Food Faith Fitness
        </p>
        <p>
         How delicious does that crumbly golden crust look? Dont forget to double the recipe if youre trying to feed a really big family! Works with fruit other than apples, too.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Chewy-Apple-Peanut-Butter-Bars-1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13482" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Chewy-Apple-Peanut-Butter-Bars-1.jpg" width="491"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://www.sugardishme.com/2014/09/04/chewy-apple-peanut-butter-bars/" style="color: #bf8221;">
            Chewy Apple Peanut Butter Bars
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via Sugar Dish Me
        </p>
        <p>
         More apple bars! The texture of the peanut butter chips and the tartness of the apples make these bars absolutely delicious.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Apple-Cake-3" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13489" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Apple-Cake-3.jpg" width="267"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://www.lemontreedwelling.com/2014/08/apple-snack-cake.html" style="color: #bf8221;">
            Apple Snack Cake
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via Lemon Tree Dwelling
        </p>
        <p>
         Im so in love with the brown-sugar pecan topping on this cake, but you can of course leave it off if someone in your family has a nut allergy! Blogger Cathy uses a blend of apples for the maximum apple experience.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Apple-Blondies-2-1024x682" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13484" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Apple-Blondies-2-1024x6821.jpg" width="601"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://dinnersdishesanddesserts.com/apple-blondies-w-cinnamon-brown-sugar-frosting/" style="color: #bf8221;" target="_blank">
            Apple Blondies With Cinnamon Brown Sugar Frosting
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via Dinners, Dishes, and Desserts
        </p>
        <p>
         One of my friends has quoted me as saying, Ive never met a blondie I didnt like, and these look like no exception. What a delightful soft, sweet, shareable treat! Super easy to make; the batter uses melted butter.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="apple-turnovers" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13485" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/apple-turnovers.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://www.errenskitchen.com/apple-turnovers/" style="color: #bf8221;">
            Apple Turnovers
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via Errens Kitchen
        </p>
        <p>
         To keep things fresh, take these unglazed turnovers to your holiday party and add the glaze when its time to serve them. And if you have a trusty puff pastry recipe, dont be afraid to use that instead of the recommended time-saving store bought.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="caramel-apple-cookies_5894" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13486" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/caramel-apple-cookies_5894.jpg" width="267"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://amyshealthybaking.com/blog/2014/06/15/caramel-apple-cookies/" style="color: #bf8221;">
            Caramel Apple Cookies
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via Amys Healthy Baking
        </p>
        <p>
         In my experience, cookies are always a crowd pleaser. How can you go wrong with some soft, caramel apple cookies to go with after-dinner coffee, tea, or cocoa? These feature fried apples  and follow the tip to slightly under-bake so that they stay chewy.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="apple-crisp-bars7" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13487" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/apple-crisp-bars7.jpg" width="601"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://www.ihearteating.com/2013/02/25/salted-caramel-apple-crisp-bars/" style="color: #bf8221;">
            Salted Caramel Apple Crisp Bars
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via I Heart Eating
        </p>
        <p>
         I love the serving directions on this recipe: serve warm. Whos going to say no to some warm apple crisp with salty caramel sauce drizzled over top? Certainly not me.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Apple-Half-Slab-Pie-6091" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-13488" height="400" src="http://www.bakepedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Apple-Half-Slab-Pie-6091.jpg" width="601"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #bf8221;">
           <a href="http://www.chocolatemoosey.com/2014/08/26/apple-half-slab-pie/" style="color: #bf8221;">
            Apple Half-Slab Pie
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         via Chocolate Moosey
        </p>
        <p>
         And, of course, a more traditional apple recipe for those of you dying for apple pie. The half-slab makes this particular pie ideal for sharing!
        </p>
