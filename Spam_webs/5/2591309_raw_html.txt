        <p>
         Id like to thank everyone who participated and either failed miserably, causing me to laugh hysterically, or succeeded, causing me to laugh hysterically.  Honorable mentions will receive TFM koozies for everyone in the photo, and winners will receive TFM koozies as well as Merica tee shirts from
         <a href="http://frocketees.com/products/merica-shirt/">
          Frocketees.com
         </a>
         for everyone in the photo.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Honorable Mentions
        </h2>
        <p class="top-photo">
         <img class="re-size border" src="http://d3imyo1kk0rcam.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/21e017878999948ff63114347c569ec42139006349.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <small class="caption">
          Skiing penguins.
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          This picture earned points because it involves awesome penguin costumes, partaking in unsafe activities, and it was actually taken in a fraternity house (see composites on walls).
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="top-photo">
         <img class="re-size border" src="http://d3imyo1kk0rcam.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/85fdd4547cbff533cf0c0a84eb3de49f672068653.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <small class="caption">
          Mr. McGibblets.  What more can be said?
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          I had no idea who the fuck Mr. McGibblets was until I did a little research, but honestly it wouldnt have mattered.  Full body costumes of this nature show incredible dedication (or orders from an active) and the name Mr. McGibblets is funny as fuck.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="top-photo">
         <img class="re-size border" src="http://d3imyo1kk0rcam.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/da1b9638bf0662c47a14893cfc17bf601357330789.JPG"/>
         <br/>
         <small class="caption">
          Every girl is a unique TSM.  Here are 27 of them
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          This was a no brainer for honorable mention, but if it had been 60+ girls all in heels, unique TSM dresses, AND pearls, then they wouldve made it to the winners circle.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="top-photo">
         <img class="re-size border" src="http://d3imyo1kk0rcam.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/876de90640cf107d87913b9b2f55be691106764705.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <small class="caption">
          Its all in the lean.
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          The comments on this one alone were enough to merit an honorable mention.  The delay before people realized there were females in this photo was priceless.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="top-photo">
         <img class="re-size border" src="http://d3imyo1kk0rcam.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/d450c5dbcc10db0749277efc32f15f9f1957075420.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <small class="caption">
          I was going to dress up like a GDI, but then I realized I wouldnt get laid. TFM.
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          In a perfect world youd have to stay home if you cant or dont want to pull off a scantily clad Halloween costume.  We dont live in a perfect world.  Is that a skiing penguin with his arm around her?
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="top-photo">
         <img class="re-size border" src="http://d3imyo1kk0rcam.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/f7223462c0cb28e746b22e55f08d580f1951347399.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <small class="caption">
          Natty Knight.
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Ive seen variations of this costume, but there can only be one true Natty Knight.  This stout fucker would unhorse all others.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="top-photo">
         <img class="re-size border" src="http://d3imyo1kk0rcam.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/9e65506d7a82e20a4978762b318f495860090925.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <small class="caption">
          One: cut a hole in the box.
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Weve seen variations of this one as well, but the facial expressions, sunglasses and caption got this particular Dick in a Box a spot under the tree.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="top-photo">
         <img class="re-size border" src="http://d3imyo1kk0rcam.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/908a0da311dd1474df1d2d6bfffb3102829368803.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <small class="caption">
          Business Cowsual for Halloween.
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Classic wordplay with utters and a blazer earns you a spot in the honorable mentions section.  For the record, those of you who were lucky enough to see Utter Oral before it was removed by the submitters request, that wouldve taken this spot.
         </em>
        </p>
        <h2>
         WINNERS
        </h2>
        <p class="top-photo">
         <img class="re-size border" src="http://d3imyo1kk0rcam.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/5ae0c1c8a5260bc7b6648f6fbd115c351710133486-e1320186188559.JPG"/>
         <br/>
         <small class="caption">
          Pledge fire.
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          This one really had it all:  use of pledge, entertainment for others at the party, cleverness, and costume execution.  Flawless.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="top-photo">
         <img class="re-size border" src="http://d3imyo1kk0rcam.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/03401ed8aac56a2fa8219b7466f093001121168691.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <small class="caption">
          Deltas Angelsthank you Halloween.
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Many people submit photos with the caption No caption necessary but this is the first time it wouldve been acceptable.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p class="top-photo">
         <img class="re-size border" src="http://d3imyo1kk0rcam.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/a56218a2264e056d1b07224ca5aa3b98516107198.JPG"/>
         <br/>
         <small class="caption">
          Slap the bag
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          I had never seen this costume attempted and it easily couldve resulted in
          <em>
           What are you supposed to be?
          </em>
          failure.  These girls executed the idea very well, included all three flavors, and I would slap all three bags.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out all of the submissions for the
         <a href="http://totalfratmove.com/tfm-halloween-costume-contest-2011">
          TFM Halloween Costume Contest 2011
         </a>
         and the rest of the
         <a href="http://totalfratmove.com/tfm-halloween-costume-contest-2011?picks=1">
          finalists
         </a>
         that didnt quite make the cut.
        </p>
