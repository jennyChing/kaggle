         <h1 class="art-postheader">
          Good Relationship vs Bad Relationships- Is Your relationship too bad to be good
         </h1>
         <div class="art-postheadericons art-metadata-icons">
          <span class="art-postdateicon">
           <span class="date">
            Published
           </span>
           <span class="entry-date" title="5:22 pm">
            October 21, 2013
           </span>
          </span>
          |
          <span class="art-postauthoricon">
           <span class="author">
            By
           </span>
           <span class="author vcard">
            <a class="url fn n" href="http://psychiclifecoach.com/author/lifecoach" title="View all posts by lifecoach">
             lifecoach
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="art-postcontent clearfix">
          <p>
           Good relationships, like all relationships are a mixture of good and bad, but the good far outweighs the bad. Although we should focus on the good, we cannot simply choose to ignore the bad in our relationships. When we focus too much on the good, we can not see clearly just how bad our relationship is. If we cannot get a grip on reality and make excuse after excuse we tend to shove the problems in our relationship under the rug. When we do that, the problems only get bigger and the problems breed and branch out. A psychic life coach can help you get a better perspective on your relationship, both the good and the bad. A psychic life coach can see if you are making too big a deal and sweating the small stuff instead of counting your blessings. No one is perfect, so there has to be some bad in every relationship. As long as the bad is relatively small in proportion to the good, the relationship can sail along towards happier times. If the bad is allowed to grow and gain strength, there may come a time where the damage done to the relationship is far to great for it to be salvageable. If you are picking your good relationship apart by focusing on every little negative thing, that negativity will poison your good relationship to the point where it can no longer survive.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_378" style="width: 160px">
           <a href="http://psychiclifecoach.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/533457_10150824445096296_1257056387_n.jpg">
            <img alt="Good Relationship vs Bad Relationships- Is Your relationship too bad to be good" class="size-thumbnail wp-image-378" height="150" src="http://psychiclifecoach.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/533457_10150824445096296_1257056387_n-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Good Relationship vs Bad Relationships- Is Your relationship too bad to be good
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Judging a good relationship or a bad relationship may be hard for those within it. A psychic life coach and tell you what problem areas your relationship suffers from, and offer solutions as well. Relationships take work, and we all need teachers to help guide us through the tough times and challenges. Psychic life coaches can offer you assistance with creating an environment for positive change. We learn bad habits, behaviors and skills from past relationships. Where can one turn when they wish to seek more productive than destructive behaviors, methods and tools for their relationships? Psychic life coaches can help you work more effectively at keeping your relationship properly balanced and your bad behaviors in check. Psychic life coaches can also help you assist the one you love to help them create change for the better.
          </p>
          <p>
           No one sets out to be trapped in a bad relationship, yet so many people find themselves in one. These same people also have a hard time letting go or making the relationship better. A psychic life coach can be there for you if it is time to let go, and if there is a way to salvage what you have. We all want to be happy, and good relationships go a long way towards making us live a happy life. The same goes for the reverse. A bad relationship makes for a lot of unhappiness, makes you feel weak, embarrassed and ruins your self esteem. Let a psychic life coach help lift you up, life your spirits up, and get you back on the right track.
          </p>

          Astrology and How it Forms our Lives
         </div>
         <div class="art-postcontent clearfix">
          <div class="textwidget">

            <a href="http://psychiclifecoach.com/?attachment_id=25" rel="attachment wp-att-25">

            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Astrology Defines Who We Are
            </p>
           </div>
           Since the dawn of time mankind has tried to understand how the stars and planets influence their lives. From earliest civilizations, including Stonehenge, New Grange as well as the pyramids of Egypt and the Yucatan, we have used the position of the stars and planets not only as a calendar but also as a way to explain events of our lives. Economic hardship, crops cycles, changing seasons, wars and omens were all based on the astrological influences occurring at any given point in the heavens. The result is a combination of science and religion, which has become Astrology. Astrology comes from the Greek term science of the stars" and embodies the art and science of studying the heavens, the motion of the planets and the stars to determine how they influence human personality and behavior. People have been seeking astrologers for thousands of years to receive predictions for future events delivered through an astrology reading. An astrology reading can help you see how the planets have affected your life from the day you were born. When you get an astrology reading, the astrologer will need to cast your birth, or natal, chart. This chart, or road map of where the planets were on the day you were born, is the cornerstone of your individual astrology reading and the foundation the astrologer will use to predict what you can expect in the months to come. Your birth chart shows where all the planets were on the day you were born and what influences will be affecting your personality, character and traits based upon those influences. During an astrology reading you will learn how the planets have influenced the development of your personality and character. An astrology reading can be used to help you better understand yourself. During your astrology reading you will also be able to understand why you do the things you do, why you like certain types of music, furniture or clothing, where the best place to live is and your best career choices. In other words, the astrology reading, will show you why you are the way you are.
