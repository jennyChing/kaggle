            <a class="lightbox_link" href="http://seventy2minutes.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Andrea-Balency-Volcano.jpg">
             <img alt="Andrea Balency Volcano" class="attachment-big-thumb-hd wp-post-image" height="510" src="http://seventy2minutes.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Andrea-Balency-Volcano-765x510.jpg" width="765"/>

          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Andrea-Balency-Heather-Sommerfield-9434-1" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4517" height="393" src="http://seventy2minutes.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Andrea-Balency-Heather-Sommerfield-9434-1-590x393.jpg" width="590"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Compared, in sound, toBat for Lashes, FKA twigs, and Bjrk, Andrea Balency shares the stream for her new single; Waterfalls, a beautiful paean delivered over a beat that paces like ones own heart.
          </p>
          <p>
           <iframe frameborder="no" height="450" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?url=https%3A//api.soundcloud.com/tracks/187294243&amp;auto_play=false&amp;hide_related=false&amp;show_comments=true&amp;show_user=true&amp;show_reposts=false&amp;visual=true" width="100%">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           Franco-Mexican artist, singer, instrumentalist and producer
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <a href="http://www.andreabalency.com/" style="color: #0000ff;" target="_blank" title="http://www.andreabalency.com/">
             ANDREA BALENCY
            </a>
           </span>
           follows her breakthrough 2014 with a brand new EP entitled
           <span style="color: #0000ff;">
            <a href="https://soundcloud.com/tags/volcano%20ep" style="color: #0000ff;" target="_blank" title="https://soundcloud.com/tags/volcano%20ep">
             Volcano
            </a>
           </span>
           , demonstrating a growth in style, experience and quality beyond that which she has already earned. Having moved from Paris where she originally settled to write her debut EP Walls (released summer 2014 via Bataille); she now resides in London for an expanded approach to her classically trained electronic compositions
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/fr/album/volcano-ep/id962843855" target="_blank" title="https://itunes.apple.com/fr/album/volcano-ep/id962843855">
            Pre-order

             <strong>
              Volcano
             </strong>
            </span>
            on iTunes
           </a>
