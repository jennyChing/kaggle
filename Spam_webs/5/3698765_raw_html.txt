        <p>
         <a href="http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File%3AKue_Moci_Kacang.jpg" title="By Gunawan Kartapranata (Own work) [CC BY-SA 3.0 (http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0)], via Wikimedia Commons">
          <img alt="Kue Moci Kacang" class=" alignleft" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6f/Kue_Moci_Kacang.jpg/512px-Kue_Moci_Kacang.jpg" width="512"/>
         </a>
         While American women are actively avoiding
         <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snacking" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Snacking">
          snack foods
         </a>
         to enhance their outer beauty. Asian women are taught that eating snack foodsleads to long lasting inner beauty.
        </p>
        <p>
         Over the last 10 years the idea ofsnack foods in the US has become increasingly associated with health problems such as obesity, diabetes, and
         <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inborn_error_of_metabolism" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Inborn error of metabolism">
          metabolic disorders
         </a>
         . To counteract this trend public health officials have attempted to discourage people fromsnack foodsaltogether. However some nutrition experts believe that snacking is not the problem. But ratherits the quality of snack foods consumed in the US that continues to be a health problem.
        </p>
        <h2>
         When most Americans think of snack foods
        </h2>
        <p>
         the list often includes things like candy bars, chips, and soft drinks. But to many Asian womensnack foods are associated with nutritionally dense foods such as
         <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coconut_milk" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Coconut milk">
          coconut juice
         </a>
         ,
         <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mung_bean" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Mung bean">
          mung bean
         </a>
         cakes, green tea with walnuts, and
         <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mochi" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Mochi">
          Mochi
         </a>
         . Foods which have been praised for their health and beauty promoting properties. Some of the best snack foods are found at your local Asian Market.
        </p>
