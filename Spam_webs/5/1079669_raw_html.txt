           </h2>
           <h3 class="chapter_title">

              <div id="ch-1">

              <p>
               Globally, India has one of the oldest traditions of CSR. But it was seen that CSR was not actively pursued by corporates across the country. Much has been done in recent years to make Indian Entrepreneurs aware of social responsibility as an important segment of their business activity. The Companies Act got the corporates approach in line with CSR activities in India. It changed their attitude towards setting clear objectives, undertaking potential investments, measuring and reporting performance publicly in terms of CSR projects that the company undertook.
              </p>
