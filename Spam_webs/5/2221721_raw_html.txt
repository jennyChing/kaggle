      <div class="post-body">
       <p>
        <a href="http://static02.mediaite.com/themarysue/uploads/2014/10/dany1.jpg">
         <img alt="Game of Thrones Season 3 Review" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-254116" height="380" src="http://static02.mediaite.com/themarysue/uploads/2014/10/dany1.jpg" width="612"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        So far weve seen some good(
        <a href="http://www.themarysue.com/game-of-thones-doran-martell-set-pics/">
         Alexander Siddig as Doran Martell
        </a>
        ) and some bad (
        <a href="http://www.themarysue.com/first-look-at-game-of-thrones-sand-snakes-reveals-nipple-boob-armor-why/">
         Sand Snakes with nipple armor
        </a>
        ) from
        <em>
         Game of Thrones
        </em>
        season five set pics. Now its time for some the hell? Warning, front and center. This post discusses major plot pointsfor season five of
        <em>
         Game of Thrones
        </em>
        and
        <em>
         A Dance with Dragons
        </em>
        . Abandon spoilerphobia, all ye who enter here.
       </p>
       <p>
        Heres whats going down in
        <em>
         A Dance with Dragons
        </em>
        for Daenerys and Tyrion Lannister: Dany, having found that
        <em>
         keeping
        </em>
        Meereen isnt as easy as
        <em>
         capturing
        </em>
        Meereen, has to marry sleazy yet oddly appealing (
        <a href="http://www.themarysue.com/got-the-laws-of-gods-and-men-favorite-moments/slide/11/">
         in the show, anyway
        </a>
        ) one-percenterHizdahr zo Loraq so that she can hang onto even the barest shred of loyalty fromthe Meereeneese nobility. Thepost-wedding celebrations take place at what a gladitorial arena. Meanwhile, Tyrions met up with Jorah Mormont, and both of them have joined the sellsword company The Second Sons with the aim of eventually meeting and joining forces with Dany. As of the end of
        <em>
         A Dance with Dragons
        </em>
        , their paths have not yet converged.
       </p>
       <p>
        So lets discuss this pic where Tyrion and Dany are chilling at the latters wedding:
       </p>
       <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en">
        <p>
         Season 5 photo! Omg!! Tyron and I together gives me chills!

       <p>
        Look at
        <a href="http://io9.com/game-of-thrones-season-5-just-made-another-major-change-1648831088">
         this other pic
        </a>
        of Jorah participating in the games as a competitor, get your keysmashing out, and then lets try and sort through this. Pretty much all of
        <em>
         ADwD
        </em>
        is Tyrion and Jorahs Adventures In Being Drunk Sad Sacksso is the show skipping a good chunk of that? Id say not
        <em>
         necessarily
        </em>
        , because the post-wedding scene has some dragon-shaped stuff that makes it deal for a finale or near-finale. Theres room for a few scenes of Tyrion and Jorah co-starring in the most depressing buddy comedy ever.
       </p>
       <p>
        But where does that leave fan favorite Penny, a dwarf girl whom Tyrion meets in his travels? (She was also one of the dwarves who performed at
        <a href="http://www.themarysue.com/game-of-thrones-the-lion-and-the-rose-favorite-moments/">
         Joffrey and Margaerys wedding
        </a>
        . Oh, and she wants to have sex with Tyrion, whoaccording to fan theoriesmay or may not be her father.
        <em>
         Game of Thrones
        </em>
        , man.) Like Arianne Martell, theres been nary a whisper of her being cast, so Im guessing she wont be.
       </p>
       <p>
        I understand the need to simplify
        <em>
         A Song of Ice and Fire
        </em>
        s frankly hellaciously complicated narrative for a different medium, but that leads us to the elephant in the room: the show catching up with the books. Fannish consensus generally regards Daenerys
        <em>
         ADwD
        </em>
        plotline as being boring as fuck,
        <em>
         until
        </em>
        this scene happens near the end. It sends her story in a brand new direction. A brand new direction that has like one chapter so far. Seriously. We dont know what happens after Tyrion and Daenerys finally meet,
        <em>
         because it hasnt happened in the books yet
        </em>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Tyrions in Meereen.
        <a href="http://www.themarysue.com/game-of-thones-doran-martell-set-pics/">
         Jaimes in Dorne
        </a>
        .
        <a href="http://www.themarysue.com/game-of-thrones-omission-reveal/">
         Lady Stoneheart is MIA
        </a>
        . This may be an unpopular opinion, but I kind of like not knowing whats going on. The books are the books. The show is the show. Chop away, HBO. Well always have Strong Belwas in our hearts.
       </p>
