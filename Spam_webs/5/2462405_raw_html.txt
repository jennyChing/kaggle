             <h2 class="no-marg-top">
              $5 Friday: 5 Beauty Picks for $5 (or less!)
             </h2>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Richel Newborg
              </strong>
              <br/>
              <em>
               July 11, 2014
              </em>
             </p>
             <img alt="Five Dollar Friday" class="alignnone img-responsive wp-image-5510" src="http://blog.chippmunk.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Five-Dollar-Friday.jpg"/>
             We've come across a number of awesome picks for our $5 Friday. This week we were able to shop all of these deals from one store, Forever 21. These are some awesome beauty picks and we love that you can score some of them for just about $14 plus tax! What a steal!



Here's your shopping list:
             <h4>
              Day Dreamer Hair Brush, $4
             </h4>
             You can
             <a href="http://chipp.us/r/richelSite/fc0/" target="_blank">
              grab this in pink or blue
             </a>
             and it's awesome for long hair, too. I love the day dreamer message on the back.
             <h4>
              Trio of Headbands "Textured Elastic Headband Set", $2
             </h4>
             These
             <a href="http://chipp.us/r/richelSite/fc0/" target="_blank">
              are perfect for summer
             </a>
             and personally, I use them when I wash my face and when I apply my makeup. You get three and one of them is a little sparkly so that is perfect for wearing out.
             <h4>
              Gold Eyelash Curler, $4
             </h4>
             These are so cute, and love that they come in two colors, too.
             <a href="http://chipp.us/r/richelSite/fc2/" target="_blank">
              The gold tone
             </a>
             makes them look extra fancy.
             <h4>
              Forever 21 Tweezer Duo, $3
             </h4>
             I love these tweezers. They are
             <a href="http://chipp.us/r/richelSite/fc3/" target="_blank">
              perfect for plucking
             </a>
             those stray brow hairs (or random chin hair) and they look great, too. You get two in a pack which is a total steal for just $3.
             <h4>
              Soft Knit Head Wrap, $2
             </h4>
             Love, LOVE, LOVE.
             <a href="http://www.forever21.com/Product/Product.aspx?BR=f21&amp;Category=acc_hair&amp;ProductID=1000059243&amp;VariantID=&amp;siteID=Hy3bqNL2jtQ-SFYMBWbMY.f3rAfpWDEuQw&amp;ls_affid=Hy3bqNL2jtQ&amp;utm_campaign=Hy3bqNL2jtQ&amp;utm_source=affiliatetraction&amp;utm_medium=ls" target="_blank">
              These are stylish
             </a>
             for wearing out or just when you want to flop around the house. It's soft knit and it's perfect for long or short hair.



What do you think of $5 Friday? Let us know by leaving a comment below or better yet, post a pic on Facebook, Twitter or Instagram with the #ShopChippmunk.

Want more deals? Check out the
             <a href="http://eepurl.com/YCm5D" target="_blank">
              best deals this week right here
             </a>
