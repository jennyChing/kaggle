       <h2 class="post-title">
        Kaligrafiti Akmnn Yaratcs Shoe, stanbula Nefis Bir Mural Brakt!
       </h2>
       <div align="right">
        <a class="twitter-share-button" data-hashtags="iyikafa" href="https://twitter.com/share">
         Tweet
        </a>

      <div class="post-content">
       <p>
        Kaligrafi ve grafitiyi birletiren kaligrafiti akmnn yaratcs, dnyaca nl sanat Niels Shoe Meulman, bilinen adyla Shoe, stanbuldayd.
       </p>
       <p>
        Biz de Shoenun Tophanedeki Krampf Gallerynin d cephesine yapt enfes mural batan sona izleme imkan bulan birka ansl kiiden biriydik.
       </p>
       <p>
        yi Kafa, stanbulun tarihi dokusundan izler tayan bu muraln taze fotoraflarn ve hyperlapse yapm videolarn iftiharla sunar.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Kaligrafiti ustas Shoe stanbul'da" class="alignnone" height="987" src="http://i.imgur.com/9t8u5oF.jpg" width="1000"/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="Kaligrafi Grafiti Shoe stanbul" class="alignnone" height="987" src="http://i.imgur.com/MczWBXB.jpg" width="1000"/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="Calligraffiti master Shoe Istanbul" class="alignnone" height="987" src="http://i.imgur.com/NOVVDAz.jpg" width="1000"/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="Calligraffiti Niels Shoe Meulman stanbul Amsterdam" class="alignnone" height="987" src="http://i.imgur.com/wyEPfDu.jpg" width="1000"/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="Calligraphy Graffiti Shoe Istanbul Amsterdam" class="alignnone" height="987" src="http://i.imgur.com/kVM3Pls.jpg" width="1000"/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="Kaligrafitinin yaratcs Niels Shoe Meulman stanbul Amsterdam" class="alignnone" height="987" src="http://i.imgur.com/i2JFMea.jpg" width="1000"/>
        <br/>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="349" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/yZuiRcxExx8?rel=0&amp;vq=hd720" width="620">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        Biz tam mural bitti derken, Shoe frasn yeniden eline alp merdivenin altnda kalan ksma yle bir ustalkla stanbul yazverdi ki, kendisine bir kez daha hayran kaldk
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="349" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/l4O2iyHuoi4?rel=0&amp;vq=hd720" width="620">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.krampfgallery.com/" target="_blank">
         Krampf Gallery
        </a>
        de yakaladmz Shoeyu, nmzdeki ay da
        <a href="http://contemporaryistanbul.com/tr.htm" target="_blank">
         Contemporary stanbul
        </a>
        da ve dahi
        <a href="https://290sqm.com/location-istanbul" target="_blank">
         290 Square Meters
        </a>
        daki solo sergisinde takipte olacaz.
       </p>
       <p>
        Shoe ve kaligrafiti ilgili detayl bilgiye
        <a href="http://www.calligraffiti.nl/" target="_blank">
         sitesinden
        </a>
        ulaabilir,
        <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/Calligraffiti/349261328247" target="_blank">
         facebook
        </a>
        sayfasndan sanatnn gncel ilerini takip edebilirsiniz.
       </p>
