     <h2 class="blog-post">
      15 New Secret Starbucks Drinks for Fall and Winter
     </h2>
     <div class="post-meta-bayside">
      <span class="date-bayside">
       Posted on
       <a href="http://jolt24.com/2013/11/" rel="bookmark" title="11:01 pm">

     <p>
      Fall and winter Starbucks drinks to feed your addiction! These drinks are created by enthusiastic baristas and customers from across the world. If you want a drink you see here, ask for it
      <strong>
       by recipe
      </strong>
      , not by name. The names are not known universally and most of them are only known in small regions. But as long as you know the recipe, you can order it anywhere.
     </p>
     <p>
      Please keep in mind that many of these drinks have seasonal ingredients in them that wont be available year round (gingerbread syrup, pumpkin spice flavoring, and more). Also, because some of these can be complex, it would be totally awesome of you if you were to tip your barista when asking for one!
     </p>
     <h2>
      1. Pumpkin Pie Frappuccino
     </h2>
     <p>
      <a href="http://jolt24.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/pumpkinpiefrapp.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto-img">
       <img alt="pumpkinpiefrapp" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16329" height="522" src="//jolt24.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/pumpkinpiefrapp.jpg" width="378"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Not a big fan of the pumpkin spice frappuccino? Maybe youd prefer something more like pumpkin pie? Try ordering a cream-based pumpkin spice frappuccino and add:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Whipped cream blended into the frappuccino
      </li>
      <li>
       Cinnamon dolce syrup (3 pumps venti, 2 pumps grande, 1 pump tall)
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      And get it topped with whipped cream and cinnamon topping. Or with spiced whipped cream, if available.
     </p>
     <h2>
      2. Hot Apple Chai
     </h2>
     <p>
      <a href="http://jolt24.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/hotapplechai.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto-img">
       <img alt="hotapplechai" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16313" height="611" src="//jolt24.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/hotapplechai.jpg" width="470"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      A hot version of the
      <a href="http://jolt24.com/2013/09/04/35-secret-starbucks-drinks-you-didnt-know-you-could-order/" target="_blank" title="35 Secret Starbucks Drinks You Didnt Know You Could Order">
       iced apple chai
      </a>
      . Its prepared similarly to their caramel apple spice drink. Order:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Chai syrup (5 pumps venti, 4 pumps grande, 3 pump tall)
      </li>
      <li>
       Steamed apple juice
      </li>
      <li>
       Whipped cream + caramel drizzle (optional)
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      Or just ask for their caramel apple spice drink, with chai syrup instead of caramel.
     </p>
     <p>
      This one is extremely easy to make at home, too. Just buy some apple cider and add in either chai tea concentrate or syrup (either that you made yourself or bought in the store). Apple juice works, too, if you find the taste of cider to be too tart. If you buy chai in the store, make sure its not vanilla-flavored chai. The plain chai concentrates taste much better with apple than the vanilla.
     </p>
     <h2>
      3. Hot Butterbeer Latte
     </h2>
     <p>
      <a href="http://jolt24.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/hotbutterbeer.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto-img">
       <img alt="hotbutterbeer" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16317" height="428" src="//jolt24.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/hotbutterbeer.jpg" width="557"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Too cold for a
      <a href="http://jolt24.com/2013/09/04/35-secret-starbucks-drinks-you-didnt-know-you-could-order/4" target="_blank" title="35 Secret Starbucks Drinks You Didnt Know You Could Order">
       butterbeer frappuccino
      </a>
      ? Warm up with this drink! Order:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Steamed whole milk (or steamed heavy cream if you want something richer)
      </li>
      <li>
       Toffee nut syrup (4 pumps venti, 3 pumps grande, 2 pumps tall)
      </li>
      <li>
       Caramel syrup (4 pumps venti, 3 pumps grande, 2 pumps tall)
      </li>
      <li>
       Cinnamon dolce syrup (4 pumps venti, 3 pumps grande, 2 pumps tall)
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      Try getting it with whipped cream, and ask for either the caramel brulee chips (from their caramel brulee drinks), salted caramel chunks (from their salted caramel mocha drinks), or the caramel crunch bits (from their caramel ribbon crunch drinks) on top, depending on availability.
     </p>
     <h2>
      4. Pumpkin Chai Frappuccino
     </h2>
     <p>
      <a href="http://jolt24.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/pumpkinchaifrappuccino.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto-img">
       <img alt="pumpkinchaifrappuccino" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16321" height="375" src="//jolt24.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/pumpkinchaifrappuccino.jpg" width="500"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Many local coffee shops have pumpkin chai-flavored drinks (as does Starbucks competitor Caribou coffee). But if youre away from your local place and need your fix, you can get it at Starbucks. Order:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       A pumpkin spice frappuccino
      </li>
      <li>
       With chai syrup (4 pumps venti, 3 pumps grande, 2 pumps tall)
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      Alternatively, you could try asking for:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       A chai tea frappuccino
      </li>
      <li>
       with pumpkin spice syrup (4 pumps venti, 3 pumps grande, 2 pump tall)
      </li>
      <li>
       And an extra pump of chai syrup
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      With or without whipped cream, its a delicious treat! The pumpkin spice topping works well here, too.
     </p>
     <h2>
      5. Pumpkin Chai Latte
     </h2>
     <p>
      <a href="http://jolt24.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/pumpkinchailatte.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto-img">
       <img alt="pumpkinchailatte" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16325" height="559" src="//jolt24.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/pumpkinchailatte.jpg" width="418"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      The hot version of the pumpkin chai frappuccino. Ask for:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       A chai tea latte
      </li>
      <li>
       Pumpkin spice syrup (3 pumps venti, 2 pumps grande, 1 pump tall)
      </li>
      <li>
       With an additional pump of chai syrup
      </li>
      <li>
       Get it with whipped cream and the pumpkin spice topping
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      Or, you could flip it. Try ordering:
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       A pumpkin spice latte
      </li>
      <li>
       Chai syrup (4 pumps venti, 3 pumps grande, 2 pump tall)
      </li>
      <li>
       With whipped cream and the pumpkin spice topping to top it off
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      Additionally, you can try adding either mocha syrup or caramel syrup for more of a flavor twist. Both chocolate and caramel go well with the spicy chai-flavored pumpkin. Get 3 pumps of syrup for venti, 2 pumps grande, or 1 pump for a tall.
     </p>
