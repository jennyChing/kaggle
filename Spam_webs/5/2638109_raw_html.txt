           <h1>
            1. The Hulk
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://i0.wp.com/nerdswole.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Template_Bruce.jpg">
             <img alt="Bruce Banner Tinder Profile" class="aligncenter wp-image-3536 size-large" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/nerdswole.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Template_Bruce.jpg?resize=671%2C1024"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Naturally, we had to imagine that Dr. Bruce Banner wanted a very relaxed relationship/hookup. No surprises there. Although he might run into an issue with girls being disappointed and wanting to see some Hulking action downstairs.
            <em>
             If you know what I mean.
            </em>
           </p>
           <h1>
            2. Hawkeye
           </h1>
           <p>
            <a href="http://i2.wp.com/nerdswole.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Template_Clint.jpg">

            </a>
           </p>
           <p>

Although, it could just as easily have been claiming he was cupid and had the ability to shoot an arrow through your heart (and mean it). Or it could have been a really long description about how much he loves long walks on the beach, Clint Barton is justso easy to poke fun at, but isnt that why we love Hawkeye?
           </p>

            3. Black Widow

            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Natasha is obviously ultra confident and knows she could have any dude she ever wanted.
           </p>

            4. Captain America

            5. Thor

           <p>
            <a href="http://i0.wp.com/nerdswole.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Tindder-Thor.jpg">
