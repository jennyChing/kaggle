        <p>
         Business over the internet and its survival has become tough and a huge competition has developed over internet among all business. Online businesses are increasing frequently and it has become very difficult for new business to beat their competitors that have become a brand over the internet. There are some major steps which can help you to beat your competitors easily without investing lot of money. These steps are as followings.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Business Website
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         If your business is online it means you have your business website as like other business competitors over the internet. Its a very general factor that new websites dont have so much traffic from relevant audience and they are not easily accessible by searching keywords on top pages of search engines. Now you are thinking about how to bring your website on top pages of search engines (Google, Yahoo, Bing etc.) quickly with specific keywords that drive relevant traffic to business websites and business grow quickly. In fact its not possible to bring your website within few days in all search engines top pages. You have to consider all those factors and parts of websites which help to get top SERPs (Search Engine Ranking Positions) in search engines and help to get organic traffic from these search engines. For getting top rankings in major search engines for your business websites is not very difficult but it takes some time to get top and stable keywords rankings of your business website. At this stage you have to be patience and follow search engine optimization (SEO) recommendations given by all those search engines in which you wanted to get top keywords rankings. Mostly all search engines requirements and recommendations are same such as
         <a href="http://moz.com/learn/seo/on-page-factors" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Onpage
          </strong>
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://moz.com/ugc/21offpage-seo-strategies-to-build-your-online-reputation" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Offpage
          </strong>
         </a>
         (
         <a href="http://searchengineland.com/seotable" target="_blank">
          APeriodic Table of SEO Success Factors
         </a>
         ) of website should be natural and well optimized with all those primary and secondary keywords which will help you to get relevant organic traffic from search engines. Once you got top rankings in search engines than dont stop optimization of your website and keep doing quality of work of SEO and improve all those fields and factors which seem that should be improved. Following are some main things which you must focus on them.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         1- Is your website is conveying a message to site visitors?
        </p>
        <p>
         2- Is your website user friendly?
        </p>
        <p>
         3- Is your website page loading speed really fast?
        </p>
        <p>
         4- Are navigation options providing to visitors a right path to reach their required page?
        </p>
        <p>
         5- Is your website is compatible for all devices and browsers?
        </p>
        <p>
         6- Is your website accessible on all mobile devices without any kind of error and bug?
        </p>
        <p>
         7- Is your website has returning visitors? And how much?
        </p>
        <p>
         8- Is your payment system is user friendly and is it one step checkout?
        </p>

         . These companies followed latest trends and started their business through mobile applications and now these companies have become brand.
