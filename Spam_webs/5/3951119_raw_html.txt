            Located in one of the most picturesque areas of South Maui, the
            <a href="http://www.grandwailea.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.grandwailea.com/', 'Grand Wailea Resort');" target="_blank">
             Grand Wailea Resort
            </a>
            is no small attraction. While its grandiosity is on par with that of a Las Vegas resort, we assure you its free of slot machines and full of luxury. The beautiful resort sprawls across 40 acres of beachfront Maui property, seeing the entire resort is a challenge all by itself. We were lucky enough to review the property and have highlighted some of our favorite aspects below.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            <a href="http://www.prideofmaui.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/grand-wailea-visual-guide.jpg" target="_blank">
             <img alt="Grand Wailea Property Map" class="alignright wp-image-2625" height="766" src="http://www.prideofmaui.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/grand-wailea-visual-guide.jpg" width="680"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h2>
            Grand Wailea Property Tour
           </h2>
           <h3>
            Lobby
           </h3>
           <p>
            One of our favorite things about this resort is how bright and open it is. From the gorgeous floral arrangements to the artistic statues and outrigger canoes, the lobby is a spectacle all its own. As you approach the hotel entrance, bypassing a giant welcome waterfall and amazing landscaping, Grand Waileas friendly front-of-house staff are there to greet you with a lei and a smile.
           </p>
           <img alt="Grand Wailea lobby" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2631" height="312" src="http://www.prideofmaui.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/grand-wailea-lobby.jpg" width="680"/>
           <h3>
            Hibiscus Pool
           </h3>
           <p>
            Named for the huge tiled Hibiscus flower on the bottom of the 4,850 square foot pool, the Hibiscus Pool is reserved for adults 18 and over. Featuring two jacuzzis and surrounded by a mix of lounge chairs, cabanas and casabellas (available for reservation), this pool is the perfect place to enjoy an afternoon cocktail in the warm Maui sunshine.
           </p>
           <img alt="Hibiscus Pool" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2632" height="356" src="http://www.prideofmaui.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/hibiscus-pool.jpg" width="685"/>
           <h3>
            Wailea Canyon Activity Pool
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="pools at Grand Wailea" class="alignright size-full wp-image-2633 hide-on-mobile" height="450" src="http://www.prideofmaui.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/pools.jpg" width="325"/>
            If youre looking for a pool with every amenity and perk you can imagine, look no further. The Wailea Canyon Activity Pool is technically nine pools on six levels, all connected by a river that carries swimmers through a series of intertwining slides, whitewater rapids slide, Tarzan pool with rope swing, sand beach, six waterfalls, caves, three jacuzzis, infant pool, lava slide, Ana Puka slide, swim-up bar and the worlds first water elevator. Whoa. If you cant find your perfect pool hangout in this 25,700 square foot water world, it probably doesnt exist.
           </p>
           <p>
            For an extra thrill, check out the Fishpipe, the worlds first rotating barrel ride. 90 seconds in the Fishpipe is like going down a mile long water slide, and we cant wait to try it on our next visit!
           </p>
           <img alt="Wailea Canyon Activity Pool" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2634" height="779" src="http://www.prideofmaui.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/wailea-canyon-activity-pool.jpg" width="679"/>
           <h3>
            Wailea Beach
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="Wailea Beach Hawaii" class="size-full wp-image-2636 alignright hide-on-mobile" height="275" src="http://www.prideofmaui.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/wailea-beach-hawaii.jpg" width="325"/>
            Not much of a pool person? No worries. The
            <strong>
             Grand Wailea fronts the stunning Wailea Beach
            </strong>
            , where ocean seekers can enjoy the warm breeze, clear water, gently sloping golden sand, palm trees and sunshine.The resort also offers several options for beach activities, including stand up paddleboard (SUP) and kayak rentals, boogie boards, snorkel gear and scuba diving programs at the only resort-designated scuba training pool in Hawaii. Complimentary beach umbrellas are available for use upon request.
           </p>
           <img alt="wailea beach" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-2645" height="335" src="http://www.prideofmaui.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/wailea-beach.jpg" width="680"/>
           <h3>
            Activities
           </h3>
           <p>
            If youre in search of an excellent golf experience, youre in luck. Wailea is home to 3 championship, 18-hole
            <a href="http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/golf.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/golf.html', 'Golf Courses');" target="_blank">
             Golf Courses
            </a>
            , all located only minutes away from the resort. Take in the views and play a round at Wailea Blue Course, Gold Course or Emerald Course, all designed by the worlds best golf architects.
           </p>
           <img alt="Wailea Activities" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-2640 hide-on-mobile" height="672" src="http://www.prideofmaui.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/wailea-activities.jpg" width="300"/>
           <p>
            Less than a mile away also lies the
            <a href="http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/wailea-tennis-club.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/wailea-tennis-club.html', 'Wailea Tennis Club');" target="_blank">
             Wailea Tennis Club
            </a>
            , featuring 11 courts, equipment shop and professional instructors available for clinics, lessons and game-matching services.
           </p>
           <p>
            An onsite, 24 hour
            <a href="http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/fitness-center.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/fitness-center.html', 'Fitness Center');" target="_blank">
             Fitness Center
            </a>
            is also available for use, equipped with the latest weight training and cardiovascular equipment as well as an air-conditioned half court for racquetball, squash, basketball, wallyball or handball. Wellness consultations are available on request and a variety of fitness classes, including complimentary morning beach yoga, total body conditioning and spin classes, are offered on a daily basis.
           </p>
           <p>
            The
            <a href="http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/island-activities.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/island-activities.html', 'Concierge Desk');" target="_blank">
             Concierge Desk
            </a>
            provides direct booking for some of the islands most popular activities and excursions, including bike rides down Haleakala Volcano, helicopter tours, snorkeling at Molokini Crater, whale watching (during season) and an authentic Hawaiian luau. You can even learn how to scuba dive in their diving pool (pictured.)
           </p>
           <p>
            Check the
            <a href="http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/calendar-of-events.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/calendar-of-events.html', 'Calendar of Events');" target="_blank">
             Calendar of Events
            </a>
            for a current list of unique opportunities and activities, including outdoor photography classes, outdoor painting, sand sculpture, beginning watercolor painting classes and more.
           </p>
           <h3>
            The Rock
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="Camp Grande" class="alignright size-full wp-image-2642" height="775" src="http://www.prideofmaui.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/camp-grande.jpg" width="300"/>
            If you plan on bringing the kiddos to paradise, youll be happy to know that the Grand Wailea offers a friendly mini-resort for children of all ages.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             The Spot
            </strong>
            : A Teen Lounge for guests 13 to 17 years of age, The Spot includes a snack bar, pool table, ping pong table, air hockey, foosball table and video game room withKinnect, Playstation 3, Wii and multiple game options. Open daily from 9am to 10pm.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Ohana Hale
            </strong>
            : Enjoy this family-friendly room, featuring a karaoke lounge, lego room, soft toy room and game lounge withXBOX 360, Wii, Playstation 3 and Rock Band. Parental supervision required for children 12 and younger.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Camp Grande
            </strong>
            :
            <a href="http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/camp-grande.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.grandwailea.com/pages/vacation-and-getaways/activities/camp-grande.html', 'Camp Grande');" target="_blank">
             Camp Grande
            </a>
            offers a variety of fun activities duringMorning Sessions, Afternoon Sessions and Night Camp, including sand sculpting contests, guided tide pooling, relay races, games, arts &amp; crafts, scavenger hunts, movies, pool activities, walkie-talkie tag, crab hunting and glow-in-the-dark parties. Reservations are recommended and children under 13 must be accompanied by an adult.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Poolside Workshops
            </strong>
            : Daily workshops are held by the Poolside Activity Desk, including Hawaiian Puka Shell Necklace Making, Fresh Orchid Lei Making, Hawaiian Wood Creations, Kukui Nut Lei Making and T-Shirt Tye Dye, perfect for all ages.
           </p>
           <p>
            Of all the resorts, The
            <strong>
             Grand Wailea is probably the most kid friendly
            </strong>
            . Its pretty much our version of Disneyland, Dave and Busters, and a water park all wrapped up in one.
           </p>
