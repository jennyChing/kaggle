         <h1 class="post-title">
          <a href="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/?p=22544" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Joel Osteen Prosperity Gospel Revival Show Hits $850 For Top Ticket Price">
           Joel Osteen Prosperity Gospel Revival Show Hits $850 For Top Ticket Price
          </a>
         </h1>
         <div class="meta top">
          <span class="meta-author">
           <a href="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/?author=1274" rel="author" title="Posts by Geoffrey Grider">
            Geoffrey Grider
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="meta-date">
           | June 10, 2014
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="fb-social-plugin fb-like" data-href="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/?p=22544" data-layout="button_count" data-ref="above-post" data-share="true" data-width="450">
         </div>
         <h4>
          Living his best life now for sure
         </h4>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           
          </span>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            And unto the angel of the church of the Laodiceans write; These things saith the Amen, the faithful and true witness, the beginning of the creation of God;I know thy works, that thou art neither cold nor hot: I would thou wert cold or hot.So then because thou art lukewarm, and neither cold nor hot, I will spue thee out of my mouth.
           </span>
           <a href="http://www.blueletterbible.org/Bible.cfm?b=rev&amp;c=3" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/www.blueletterbible.org/Bible.cfm?b=rev&amp;c=3');" target="_blank" title="Church of Laodicea">
            Revelation 3
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Whether you are a fan of the motivational speaker from Texas or not, one thing is absolutely for certain. Joel and Victoria Osteen are, without a doubt, living their best life now.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_22545" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.joelosteen.com/Pages/NightOfHope.aspx" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/www.joelosteen.com/Pages/NightOfHope.aspx');" rel="attachment wp-att-22545" target="_blank" title="Joel Osteen Night of Hope">
           <img alt="joel-osteen-night-of-hope-christian-con-artists-church-laodicea-great-falling-away-best-life-now" class="wp-image-22545 size-full" height="465" src="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/joel-osteen-night-of-hope-christian-con-artists-church-laodicea-great-falling-away-best-life-now.jpg" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Because thou sayest, I am rich, and increased with goods, and have need of nothing; and knowest not that thou art wretched, and miserable, and poor, and blind, and naked: Revelation 3
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          In the days before Christianity became the Church of Laodicea prophesied in the 3rd chapter of the book of Revelation, there were mighty men of God, great, Holy Spirit filled preachers who traveled the world setting up tents and seeing souls get saved. Men like
          <a href="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/pages/preachers/dwight-moody.htm" target="_blank" title="DL Moody">
           DL Moody
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/pages/preachers/billy-sunday.htm" target="_blank" title="Billy Sunday Sawdust Trail">
           Billy Sunday
          </a>
          who pounded the sawdust trail looking for lost sinners.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           That day is
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <em>
             not
            </em>
           </span>
           our day. Not even close.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Joel Osteens version of the old-fashioned tent revival meetings are glitzy affairs attended by the upper-crust of society. With tickets prices being (yes, they charge you to come) starting at over $100.00 and going all the way up to $850.00, you would have to be pretty well off to afford to even walk through the door. Osteen likes to play good cop, bad cop. He makes a small bunch of tickets available through his site for cheap, but does nothing as various ticket agencies jack up the price through the roof. The old my hands are tied ruse doesnt cut it. But again, our issue is not what the dollar amount is for
          <em>
           any
          </em>
          of the tickets, but that he is charging people to hear the gospel.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_22546" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.photosnack.com/FA875FD9E8C/pdx96ill" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/www.photosnack.com/FA875FD9E8C/pdx96ill');" rel="attachment wp-att-22546" target="_blank">
           <img alt="joel-osteen-night-of-hope-christian-con-artists-church-laodicea-great-falling-away-best-life-now-ticket-prices-1" class="wp-image-22546 size-full" height="368" src="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/joel-osteen-night-of-hope-christian-con-artists-church-laodicea-great-falling-away-best-life-now-ticket-prices-1.jpg" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           What is my reward then? Verily that, when I preach the gospel, I may make the gospel of Christ without charge, that I abuse not my power in the gospel. 1 Corinthians 9:18
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           <strong>
            This is light years removed from the day where the preacher raised all the money for the revival and depended completely on the love offerings from Gods people to pay the expenses.
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_22547" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.photosnack.com/FA875FD9E8C/pdx96ill" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/www.photosnack.com/FA875FD9E8C/pdx96ill');" rel="attachment wp-att-22547" target="_blank">
           <img alt="joel-osteen-night-of-hope-christian-con-artists-church-laodicea-great-falling-away-best-life-now-ticket-prices-2" class="wp-image-22547 size-full" height="368" src="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/joel-osteen-night-of-hope-christian-con-artists-church-laodicea-great-falling-away-best-life-now-ticket-prices-2.jpg" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           What is my reward then? Verily that, when I preach the gospel, I may make the gospel of Christ without charge, that I abuse not my power in the gospel. 1 Corinthians 9:18
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          It is well within your right to be a fan of this mans message, such as it is. Also within your rights to spend your hard-earned money to increase the size of his bank accounts. But do not call this man a preacher of the Gospel of Jesus Christ. That he absolutely is not. He is nothing more than Tony Robbins with a Jesus fish in a $4,000 suit.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_22557" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/?attachment_id=22557" rel="attachment wp-att-22557">
           <img alt="joel-osteen-night-of-hope-christian-con-artists-church-laodicea-great-falling-away-best-life-now-ticket-prices-3" class="wp-image-22557 size-full" height="620" src="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/joel-osteen-night-of-hope-christian-con-artists-church-laodicea-great-falling-away-best-life-now-ticket-prices-3.jpg" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           After the original posting of this story on 6.10.14 (yesterday), many of you attacked us saying that the ticket prices were no where near the $850 we claimed. Well, this iPhone screengrab was sent in by Kristin Greene who took it upon herself to investigate our claims. Mind. Blown.
          </p>
         </div>
         <blockquote>
          <p>
           The Apostle Paul said that to charge people to hear and receive the Gospel is an abuse of the preachers power. But Joel Osteen can breathe easy on that count  he doesnt preach the Gospel. No, where he will be held accountable will be for leading millions of people astray and to Hell with the false Prosperity Gospel.
          </p>
         </blockquote>
