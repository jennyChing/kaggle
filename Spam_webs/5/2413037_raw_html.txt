       <h2>
        A Walk Around The Block by Bill Harley
       </h2>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="337" mozallowfullscreen="" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/86270470?byline=0&amp;portrait=0&amp;color=ff9933" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="600">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        "A Walk Around the Block", is the result of my collaboration with two time Grammy award winner and childrens entertainer Bill Harley. Inspired by the imagination of young children and poking fun and kids driving their parents crazy, "Walk around the block" takes you on a trip inside the mind of a little boy. used a mix of hand drawn / painted illustrations animated in after effects and animated shape layers. I Hope you enjoy watching as much as I enjoyed the making of
        <br/>
        "Walk" has been a big hit on Sirius/Xm KidsPlace for the past couple of years (so popular it was voted off the air for a week by moms who were sick of hearing!).
       </p>
