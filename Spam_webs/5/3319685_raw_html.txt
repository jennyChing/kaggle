        <p>
         Now, is possible find the best Luxury residential projects in New York reported here! With modern residential design you could have different ideas for your house or only see the trends about residential. Today, you can see here the different materials that was used during the construction of a swimming pool house: natural stone and Ipe (Brazilian walnut).
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bestdesignprojects.com/luxury-residential-projects-swimming-pool-house-by-hariri-and-hariri/minimalist-pool-house-design-by-hariri-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-10363">

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Hairiri &amp; Hariri architecture has completed this structure in 2007 and is located in Wilton, CT. This architects say that inside the building are usual elements for entertainment and relaxing: the bar, kitchen, bathroom and and small terraces.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bestdesignprojects.com/luxury-residential-projects-swimming-pool-house-by-hariri-and-hariri/outstanding-swimming-pool-house-design-10/" rel="attachment wp-att-10360">
          <img alt="outstanding swimming pool house design 10 Luxury residential projects: swimming pool house by Hariri and Hariri" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10360" height="563" src="http://bestdesignprojects.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/outstanding-swimming-pool-house-design-10.jpg" title="Luxury residential projects: swimming pool house by Hariri and Hariri" width="884"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now, you have the possible find the best Luxury residential projects in New York, the poolhouses architecture is in total harmony with the pool and its surroundings.
        </p>
        <p>
