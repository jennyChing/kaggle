        <p>
         Certain characters have been around for centuries, but that doesnt mean they cant be reimagined.Talented US-based artist
         <a href="http://www.dhellandbrand.com/home.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.dhellandbrand.com/home.html', 'Damon Hellandbrand ');" target="_blank">
          Damon Hellandbrand
         </a>
         decided to twist up the modern characters associated with each zodiac sign, turning them into monstrous creatures from your scariest nightmares.
        </p>
        <p>
         Hellandbranduses a mix of drawings, watercolors, and digital illustration programs to bring his art to life. In an interview with Bored Panda Hellandbrand revealed, I have a fondness for anything fantasy related or anything that doesnt exist in reality,and mythology certainly has that in spades.
        </p>
        <p>
         Read on to seethe 12 zodiac signs reborn as horrifying monsters imaginedby Damon Hellandbrand.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Aries
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.astrology.com.au/astrology/12-signs-of-the-zodiac/aries.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.astrology.com.au/astrology/12-signs-of-the-zodiac/aries.html', 'Aries');" target="_blank">
          Aries
         </a>
         is symbolizedby The Ram, and is said to represent a group of born leaders with lively, fearless qualities.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="zodiac-monsters-fantasy-digital-art-damon-hellandbrand-1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11216" height="1184" src="http://cdn.earthporm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/zodiac-monsters-fantasy-digital-art-damon-hellandbrand-1.jpg" width="880"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Hellandbrand used his knowledge about each zodiac sign to influence the final product. I basically took what I knew the signs represented in terms of their name (i.e. Cancer = Crab, Leo = Lion, Pisces = Fish etc.) and proceeded to illustrate them as human/animal hybrids.
        </p>

         Taurus

          Taurus
         </a>
         is represented by the bull and stands for strength, tenacity, power, and virility.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="zodiac-monsters-fantasy-digital-art-damon-hellandbrand-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11217" height="1045" src="http://cdn.earthporm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/zodiac-monsters-fantasy-digital-art-damon-hellandbrand-2.jpg" width="880"/>
        </p>

         Gemini
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.astrology.com.au/astrology/12-signs-of-the-zodiac/gemini.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.astrology.com.au/astrology/12-signs-of-the-zodiac/gemini.html', 'Gemini');" target="_blank">
          Gemini
         </a>
         , represented by The Twins, issaid to signalintelligence, communication, and ability to express ones self fully.
        </p>

         <img alt="zodiac-monsters-fantasy-digital-art-damon-hellandbrand-3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11218" height="1235" src="http://cdn.earthporm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/zodiac-monsters-fantasy-digital-art-damon-hellandbrand-3.jpg" width="880"/>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Cancer
        </h3>
        <p>
         The Crab represents
         <a href="http://www.astrology.com.au/astrology/12-signs-of-the-zodiac/cancer.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.astrology.com.au/astrology/12-signs-of-the-zodiac/cancer.html', 'Cancer');" target="_blank">
          Cancer
         </a>
         ,this water sign is said to be highly emotional, caring, generous, and intuitive.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="zodiac-monsters-fantasy-digital-art-damon-hellandbrand-4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11219" height="1047" src="http://cdn.earthporm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/zodiac-monsters-fantasy-digital-art-damon-hellandbrand-4.jpg" width="880"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Hellandbrand employs a number of different methods to create his final product. This particular image was created by black and white pencil drawing on paper, which was then scanned into a drawing/painting program known as Corel Painter.Using this program, Hellandbrand addedhis finishing touches.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Leo

         <a href="http://www.astrology.com.au/astrology/12-signs-of-the-zodiac/leo.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.astrology.com.au/astrology/12-signs-of-the-zodiac/leo.html', 'Leo');" target="_blank">

         Scorpio

        <p>

         <img alt="zodiac-monsters-fantasy-digital-art-damon-hellandbrand-8" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11223" height="629" src="http://cdn.earthporm.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/zodiac-monsters-fantasy-digital-art-damon-hellandbrand-8.jpg" width="880"/>

        <p>
         Hellandbrand has always had an obsession with art, as a child he would spend endless hours working to recreate art by Walt Disney. As a kid artists like Boris Vallejo, Frank Frazetta, and Ralph McQuarrie inspired Hellandbrand. Today he is interested by all types of art, from the great masters of the past to the current masters of the present, as well as the amazing works of mother nature!
        </p>
        <h3>
         Sagittarius
        </h3>
        <p>
         The Centaur is the Zodiac symbol for
         <a href="http://www.astrology.com.au/astrology/12-signs-of-the-zodiac/sagittarius.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.astrology.com.au/astrology/12-signs-of-the-zodiac/sagittarius.html', 'Sagittarius');" target="_blank">
          Sagittarius
         </a>
