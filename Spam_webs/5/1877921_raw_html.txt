               <h2>
                Cast &amp; Characters of Boxtrolls
               </h2>
              </div>
              <div class="col-sm-6">
               <img class="full-width" src="images/cast-eggs.jpg"/>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Isaac Hempstead-Wright
                </strong>
                <br/>
                (as Eggs)
                <br/>
               </p>
               <hr/>
               A feral 11-year-old boy who has been raised underground by the Boxtrolls. He's dirty, eats bugs and he loves to build machines out of junk. He even thinks he is a Boxtroll. Unlike his timid Boxtroll brothers, he is brave and a little bit impetuous. Eggs realizes he is actually a human boy when he goes above ground to search for his kidnapped Boxtroll friend Fish.
              </div>
              <div class="col-sm-6">
               <img class="full-width" src="images/cast-winnie.jpg"/>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Elle Fanning
                </strong>
                <br/>
                (as Winnie)
                <br/>
               </p>
               <hr/>
               A precocious little girl who is morbidly obsessed with grotesque stories about the Boxtrolls kidnapping children. Despite the pretty bows and frilly dresses, there is nothing delicate about this sassy bruiser. The only child of Lord and Lady Portley-Rind, she is lavished with gifts but starved of attention. When she is pulled into the Boxtrolls' fantastical underworld, she is forced to re-think everything she's ever believed.
              </div>
              <div class="col-sm-12">
               <hr/>
              </div>
              <div class="col-sm-6">
               <img class="full-width" src="images/cast-archibald.jpg"/>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Ben Kingsley
                </strong>
                <br/>
                (as Archibald Snatcher)
                <br/>
               </p>
               <hr/>
               A lowly pest exterminator who dreams of becoming an aristocrat. He lives in many extravagant states of denialhe fancies himself a connoisseur of fine cheeses despite the fact that his severe lactose intolerance causes him to swell up to monstrous proportions. He is the leader of a gang of Victorian guttersnipes called the Red Hats. Denied the life of privilege he feels entitled to, Snatcher will resort to anything to succeed, regardless of the trail of wreckage he leaves behind. No one is safe from this deviously cunning villain.
              </div>
              <div class="col-sm-6">
               <img class="full-width" src="images/cast-trout.jpg"/>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Nick Frost
                </strong>
                <br/>
                (as Trout)
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="col-sm-6">
