         <p>
          The wedding favor. Typically, its more of a headache than anything else. Its a ubiquitous, but often overlooked, part of the wedding
          <a href="http://weddingpartyapp.com/blog/2014/05/08/fun-wedding-bar-ideas/">
           reception
          </a>
          and ceremony. In the grand scheme of things, it may seem like a minor, meaningless part of your wedding day. But the wedding favor serves a greater purpose than you might think. It helps to commemorate your special day. It can be used as a way of thanking your guests for joining you in your celebration. It serves as a keepsake for the future.
         </p>
         <p>
          The perfect wedding favor, then, should be symbolic of your special bond and the commitment you and your fianc have made to each other. Thankfully,you dont REALLY have to break the bank on wedding favors. For weddings with:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           &lt;100 guests  you can afford to spend around $3.00 or more per favor
          </li>
          <li>
           100-150 guests  maybe $2-3 per favor
          </li>
          <li>
           150+ guests  keep it lower than $2 to prevent yourself from giving yourself a budget aneurysm
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          But your wedding favorsshould also be thoughtful, unique and indispensablea token by which your guests can think back fondly on your big day for years to come. After all, the last thing you want is for everyone to guiltily throw away the wedding favor you put so much time and thought into.
         </p>
         <p>
          And thats where Wedding Party comes in. To help facilitate you in your pre-wedding day planning and preparations, we have put together this helpful list of wedding favor ideas that all of your
          <a href="http://weddingpartyapp.com/blog/2013/06/14/10-thoughtful-and-easy-tips-that-will-make-your-guests-love-your-wedding/">
           guests
          </a>
          are sure to love.
         </p>
