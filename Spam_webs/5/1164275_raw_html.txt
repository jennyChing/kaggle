        <p>
         Last week, Canadian cops arrested more than 30 people operating a massive 
         <a href="http://www.durhamregion.com/news/crime/article/1497112--durham-cops-bust-international-crime-ring">
          international crime ring
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         A police spokesman
         <a href="http://www.sunnewsnetwork.ca/video/search/gypsy/the-jew-vs-the-gypsies/1824793737001">
          carefully described those charged as Roma,
         </a>
         but the
         <a href="http://www.sunnewsnetwork.ca/sunnews/canada/archives/2012/09/20120905-203437.html">
          <em>
           Toronto Sun
          </em>
         </a>
         tabloid wasnt shy about employing what will no doubt soon come to be known as the G-word: Gypsies.
        </p>
        <p>
         The accused thieves are Hungarian refugees who easily lured fellow lowlifes over from the old country by touting Canadas generous welfare programs and soft-touch refugee system. Yet the days of
         <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Displaced_persons_camp">
          the DPs
         </a>
         are long gone. Exactly what are residents of a modern liberal democracy such as Hungary refugee-ing
         <em>
          from
         </em>
         ?
        </p>
        <p>
         The answer: Nazis such as you.
        </p>
        <div class="pullquote">
         If the only free thing Gypsies dont want to exploit in their adopted countries is an education, they deserve being called inferior.
        </div>
        <p>
         At least thats the answer if youre Bernie Farber, whos still one of
         <a href="http://ezralevant.com/2009/11/calgarys-official-jews-as-stup.html">
          Canadas Official Jews
         </a>
         despite the demise of his once-mighty Canadian Jewish Congress (
         <a href="http://takimag.com/article/the_great_white_dope_kathy_shaidle#axzz264TvMLji">
          I helped
         </a>
         ).
        </p>
        <p>
         From
         <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.ca/bernie-farber/roma-refugee_b_1673647.html">
          his unpaid perch at
          <em>
           The Huffington Post
          </em>
         </a>
         , Farbers playing the worlds smallest (Gypsy) violin on behalf of another allegedly persecuted ethnic constituency: the Roma currently flooding into Canada. They
         <a href="http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/story/2012/09/05/distraction-theft-ring.html">
          cost taxpayers millions of dollars
         </a>
         in
         <a href="http://www.sunnewsnetwork.ca/video/search/gypsy/significant-criminal-activity-among-gypsies/1817002690001">
          police stings
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.sunnewsnetwork.ca/video/search/gypsy/the-jew-vs-the-gypsies/1824793737001">
          bureaucratic paper-shuffling
         </a>
         , new inclusive
         <a href="http://www.tdsb.on.ca/about_us/media_room/Room.asp?show=allNews&amp;view=detailed&amp;self=33290">
          school-board lesson plans
         </a>
         , and all that finger-lickin social assistanceincluding the welfare checks some Gypsies were
         <a href="http://www.torontosun.com/2012/08/31/deported-refugee-claimants-still-collecting-welfare">
          still receiving after being deported
         </a>
         . Those millions are a pittance next to the
         <a href="http://www.thestar.com/article/694977--gypsies-caught-in-political-crossfire">
          $17 billion
         </a>
         the EU has set aside for something called the 
         <a href="http://www.romadecade.org/">
          Decade of Roma Inclusion
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Gypsies, you see, were murdered by Hitler, just like Jews! Since Farbers true religion seems to be what I call Holocaust-ianity, such historical coincidences are supposed to shame the rest of us into embracing these leeches. If we remain unmoved, were obviously right-wing racist scum.
        </p>
        <p>
         (Thousands of Canadian
         <em>
          goyim
         </em>
         were also murdered by Hitler while fighting the Nazis during that same war. Oddly enough, that fact has never managed to endear white working-class Canuck males to Farber, who is
         <a href="http://www.fivefeetoffury.com/2012/06/22/i-want-bernie-farber-charged-with-cliche-speech/">
          proud of the career hes made
         </a>
         helping censor
         <a href="http://www.freedomsite.org/legal/">
          precisely that group
         </a>
         of citizens.)
        </p>
        <p>
         These Roma refugees
         <em>
          arent
         </em>
         citizens, but for a well-to-do urban liberal such as Farber, thats a feature, not a bug. When your career is conspicuous compassion, the more exotic ones new pet, the better. So Farber has courageously vowed to stand shoulder to shoulder with his freshly adopted victim group.
        </p>
        <p>
         I hope they lift his wallet.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         If they do, itll probably be on a Friday. According to a friend who now finds himself living in one of Torontos rapidly metastasizing Gypsy enclaves, local teachers have given up expecting Roma kids to attend class on Friday because its their designated Stealing Day.
        </p>
        <p>
         This invasion wasnt supposed to happen. Ottawa re-imposed visa requirements in 2009 specifically to keep out Gypsies.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats according to
         <a href="http://www.thestar.com/article/694977--gypsies-caught-in-political-crossfire">
          a piece
         </a>
         penned that year by Rosie DiManno, bleeding-heart conservative
         <em>
          emeritus
         </em>
         at Canadas largest newspaper.
        </p>
        <p>
         DiManno writes that throughout history, Gypsies have been enslaved and ostracized, subjected to pogroms and genocide simply for being what they were, dark-skinned and perceived as alien, inferior, parasitic.
        </p>
        <div style="float:left;margin-right:7px">
         <div id="div-gpt-ad-1348014429997-3" style="width:300px; height:250px;">
          <script type="text/javascript">
           googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('div-gpt-ad-1348014429997-3'); });
          </script>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         Sheesh. Roma are no more dark-skinned than DiMannos fellow Italians, who, while
         <a href="http://takimag.com/article/why_i_hate_italians#axzz264TvMLji">
          not always warmly embraced by us mangiacake Canadians
         </a>
         , never made anybody want to (seriously) gas them in droves.
         <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-1316139/France-faces-massive-fines-Roma-gipsy-crackdown.html#ixzz260ZKdSQU">
          Overwhelming
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-1389282/Benefits-boulevard-Built-Romania--YOUR-money.html#ixzz260Ze7SEB">
          evidence
         </a>
         suggests that parasitic sounds about right. Alien, too, since by definition, a Gypsy is a rootless wanderer by choice. As for inferior, well, DiManno insists that while Some [Roma] do, in fact, keep their children out of school, this might be because they see no profit in education [and/or] cant even afford to buy their kids shoes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Lawyers call such alibis admissions against interest. If the only free thing Gypsies dont want to exploit in their adopted countries is an education, they deserve being called inferior.
        </p>
        <p>
         British sympathizers have obediently dubbed the Roma travellers, which, like most liberal euphemisms, is the precise antonym for whatever it is supposed to be describing. (See gay and diverse.) In this instance, travellers are more accurately described as immovable squatters, such as the ones
         <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2026723/Gypsies-immigration-officers-home-Proms.html#ixzz260ZtJVqb' ">
          who moved into a British immigration officers (!) house
         </a>
         while she was on vacation.
        </p>
        <p>
         What makes this politically correct terminological nitpickery all the more urgent on the Gypsies part is the inconvenient fact that, unlike almost every real victim group, they arent members of a race or a religion. In the words of
         <a href="http://bcove.me/0b81efxv">
          a decidedly unimpressed Canadian pundit
         </a>
         , they are the medieval prototype of the Occupy Wall Street movement: a shiftless group of hobos that doesnt believe in property rights for themselves (theyre nomads) or for others (they rob people blind.)
        </p>
        <p>
         Or, as I like to think of Gypsies, a sort of Down Syndrome Mafia.
        </p>
        <p>
         So what is to be done? The good news is that someone finally figured out a way to gyp the Gypsies: The producers of British televisions
         <em>
          Big Fat Gypsy Weddings
         </em>
         stand
         <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2106498/Big-Fat-Gypsy-goldmine-How-TV-company-exploited-travellers-make-rich-indeed.html#ixzz25ztZdUg6">
          accused
         </a>
         of conning their stars into performing for free.
        </p>
        <p>
         But as amusing as that is, it clearly isnt a viable long-term strategy. My solution to the Gypsy problem? Start a rumor that they draw cartoons of Muhammad.
        </p>
