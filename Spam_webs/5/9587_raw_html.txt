        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          Your tastebuds will flip out over the deep chocolate and mint flavors in every bit of this Double Mint Chocolate Poke Cake.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3849/15016789366_94701a5a6d_z.jpg">
          <img alt="Double Mint Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com" class="aligncenter" height="640" src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3849/15016789366_94701a5a6d_z.jpg" width="427"/>

        <p>
         Is anyone else counting down the days until the weekend? I live for my 3-day weekends! Sleeping, eating and hanging out. I love me some weekends. Usually I am rushing around to complete as much baking and photographing on the weekends as possible. However, having a 3-day weekend means I can take a day and not worrying about doing anything!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3885/14853069949_5ae9fa6f24_z.jpg">
          <img alt="Double Mint Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com" class="aligncenter" height="640" src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3885/14853069949_5ae9fa6f24_z.jpg" width="427"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The problem is, when I make things like this
         <strong>
          Double Mint Chocolate Poke Cake.
         </strong>
         I am a crazy mint chip FREAK. It mind boggling that I dont have more mint chip recipes for you guys, but that is about to change! This cake has to be one of my favorite recipes I have made to date and thats a big statement!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://farm6.staticflickr.com/5575/15016788416_f16000382d_z.jpg">
          <img alt="Double Mint Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com" class="aligncenter" height="640" src="https://farm6.staticflickr.com/5575/15016788416_f16000382d_z.jpg" width="427"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         You guys know what a poke cake is right? I have SO many versions on my blog:
         <a href="http://beyondfrosting.com/2013/12/08/triple-chocolate-poke-cake/" title="Sinful Triple Chocolate Poke Cake">
          triple chocolate,
         </a>
         <a href="http://beyondfrosting.com/2013/10/01/double-pumpkin-poke-cake/" title="Double Pumpkin Poke Cake">
          pumpkin
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://beyondfrosting.com/2013/08/18/funfetti-poke-cake/" title="Funfetti Poke Cake">
          funfetti
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://beyondfrosting.com/2014/04/18/triple-coconut-poke-cake/" title="Triple Coconut Poke Cake">
          coconut
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://beyondfrosting.com/2013/07/05/mudslide-poke-cake/" title="Mudslide Poke Cake">
          mudslide
         </a>
         and more! You basically bake a cake, poke some holes in the top, fill it with some pudding and add your toppings. I am certainly not the first person to do a mint poke cake, but I can say this is out of this world amazing.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3868/15016788926_ab17d17da9_z.jpg">
          <img alt="Double Mint Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com" class="aligncenter" height="640" src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3868/15016788926_ab17d17da9_z.jpg" width="427"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This is a chocolate cake that has been drenched in hot fudge and filled with chocolate pudding. The topping is a mint whipped cream that will make you want to lick the spatula clean. Since I like a little crunch, I topped this cake with Andes mints and mini chocolate chips. You can drizzle it with a little added hot fudge too!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3838/14853245047_53c0f884ed_z.jpg">
          <img alt="Double Mint Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com" class="aligncenter" height="640" src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3838/14853245047_53c0f884ed_z.jpg" width="427"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Seriously, every bite of this cake was a dream. The chocolate cake is rich in flavor. I prefer dark chocolate, so I used a dark chocolate hot fudge sauce and dark chocolate pudding. First I poured the hot fudge and let it set. The pudding layer is added after the hot fudge. Its important to pour the pudding over the cake BEFORE it sets. That way when you put it in the fridge, the pudding gets cold.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3908/15016789226_75b46abb6b_z.jpg">
          <img alt="Double Mint Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com" class="aligncenter" height="640" src="https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3908/15016789226_75b46abb6b_z.jpg" width="427"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         My favorite thing about this cake? Make it ahead of time and leave it in the fridge. It will be the hit of the party! You dont even need to make it all pretty on a plate, spoon it into a cup and go to town. For reals. Theres no shame in that game.
        </p>

         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Double Mint Chocolate Poke Cake
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSDetails">
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSTimes">
           <div class="ERSHead">
            Prep time:
            <time datetime="PT20M" itemprop="prepTime">
             20 mins
            </time>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSHead">
            Cook time:
            <time datetime="PT22M" itemprop="cookTime">
             22 mins
            </time>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSHead">
            Total time:
            <time datetime="PT2H30M" itemprop="totalTime">
             2 hours 30 mins
            </time>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSHead">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            12-16 slices
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          A rich chocolate cake filled with hot fudge and chocolate pudding. Topped with a fresh mint whipped cream.
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Box Chocolate Cake Mix
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Ingredients listed on the box (eggs, oil, water)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Jar (13oz) Hot fudge sauce
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 pkg (3.4oz) Chocolate pudding mix
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 C Milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            15 Andes candy bars, crushed
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Mini Chocolate Chips
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           For the whipped cream
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 C Heavy whipping cream
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             C Powdered sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp Mint extract
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 350F.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Combine cake mix according to the ingredients on the box. Beat on medium speed until well combined.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake cake in 9x13 for 20-22 minutes. Check for doneness by inserting a toothpick into the middle of the cake. If the toothpick comes out clean, you cake is done. Allow to cool for 10 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            While the cake is still warm, take the end of a wooden spoon or another round object and poke holes all over the top of your cake.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Heat the jar of hot fudge sauce in the microwave for about 30 seconds until you can stir it and thin it out. You may need an additional 30 seconds. Pour the jar of hot fudge sauce on the top of cake (reserve  C to drizzle over top of the cake) and allow it to absorb in the cake until cake is cooled. Let set until completely cooled.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            When the cake has cooled, prepare the pudding layer by mixing a package of instant dark chocolate pudding with 2 cups of cold milk. Before the pudding sets, pour it over the top of the cake and put the cake in the fridge to let the pudding set, about 15 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Prepare the whipped cream. Put the bowl and whisk attachment in the freezer for 5-10 minutes to get them really cold (this step is optional, but a common practice I use.). In cold mixing bowl, pour in heavy whipping cream and beat on medium speed for about 3 minutes. Add powdered sugar and mint extract and increase speed to medium high. Beat until stiff peak form.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spread whipped cream over the top of cake. You can top you cake with a hot fudge drizzle and some crushed Andes Mints and mini chocolate chips.
           </li>
          </ol>
