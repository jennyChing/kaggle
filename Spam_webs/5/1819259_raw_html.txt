           From short to hot!!!
          </span>
         </h1>
         <p class="post-meta">
          <span class="post-meta-author">
           <i class="fa fa-user">

           <a href="/author/vyoma/">

         <div class="entry">
          <p>
           We all know there are certain things that simply cant be changed-as much as you might want to. One great example: your height. When you are short, figuring out what to wear can be pretty difficult  especially if you want to make yourself look a bit taller than you really are. Well, the most important thing for you to keep in mind is that it is possible for you to make yourself look taller than you really are, according to the way that you dress.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Black is back
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-black.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-black" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3258" height="819" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-black-1024x819.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-black-1.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-black-1" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3259" height="1024" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-black-1-682x1024.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Wearing black is a secret that women have long used to look both taller and slimmer. The idea is that you are presenting a unified look, and when others see the uninterrupted lines of dark colors, it gives an impression of length. Be sure to wear heels or wedges with a monochromatic ensemble and add a few pops of color in your shoes or jewelry to avoid looking too sombre. If wearing one color is too much for you, simply match your shoes to your pants to elongate your legs.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Dont forget maxi!
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-maxi.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-maxi" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3260" height="481" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-maxi.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-maxi-1.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-maxi-1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3261" height="852" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-maxi-1.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Its common fashion folklore that small women simply cannot wear floor-grazing skirts and dresses, but luckily thats been proven entirely false. In fact, a well-cut maxi skirt can actually give you the appearance of looking taller.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Be short and mini
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-mini.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-mini" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3262" height="1024" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-mini-802x1024.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-mini-1.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-mini-1" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3263" height="1024" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-mini-1-802x1024.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           When youre short, its just a fact thatwhen it comes to skirts and dressesyou should either go super-long, or super-short (avoid calf-length and knee-lengths). Mini-skirts show more skin, which creates the illusion of a longer leg.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Pick one color and stick to it!
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-one-color.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-one-color" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3264" height="992" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-one-color.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Wear monochrome outfits because youll create one long line of color that will make you look taller. Wearing lots of drastically different colors show exactly where each body part begins and ends and can actually make you look shorter. If wearing one color seems a little dull, mix up the textures to make your outfit pop.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Heels can solve can problem
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-heels.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-heels" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3265" height="600" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-heels.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Obviously wearing high heels can make you taller but only if youre wearing the right shoe. A nude shoe is the best choice to show off legs that seem to go on forever. Strappy ankle heels or booties will make some short look choppy regardless of the color.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Go high and go wide
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-high.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-high-wide" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3266" height="918" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-high.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-high-1.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-high-wide-1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3267" height="900" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-high-1.jpg?04ccd3" width="000"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Flared, high waist pants give the illusion of longer legs. Flare jeans are flattering because they elongate your legs when paired with chic heels. The higher the better! Avoid three-quarter style pants or cropped shorts because their edgy cut make your legs look clunky. Choose pants that skim the top of your shoes. If you can make them touch the floor, even better!
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Pointy flats! Yes!!
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-pointy-flats.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-pointy-flats" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3268" height="501" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-pointy-flats.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-pointy-flats-2.jpeg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-pointy-flats-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3269" height="431" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-pointy-flats-2.jpeg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Since nobody can be expected to wear sky-high heels every day, short girls should invest in a pair of pointy flats. Why? Because its a known fact that shoes with pointed toes lengthen your leg line.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Stick to skinny belts
           </strong>
           <br/>
           When it comes to belts, short girls should always opt for a skinny versionitll define your natural waist in the most flattering way possible, but wont cut you in half the way a super-thick belt can, which causes you to look shorter.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            VVV-necks!
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-vneck.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-vneck" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3270" height="1024" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-vneck-787x1024.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-vneck-1.jpg?04ccd3">
            <img alt="dresses-to-look-tall-vneck-1" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3271" height="1024" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/dresses-to-look-tall-vneck-1-787x1024.jpg?04ccd3" width="700"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           Did you know that wearing V neck tops can elongate the body also? Whether you go with a subtle V neck top or a plunging neckline, a V neck gives off the impression of a longer more flattering torso. So keep that in mind when youre shopping for new tops this season!
          </p>
          <p>
           These are these tips and very easy things that you can do when dressing in order to help yourself look taller! Remember that being short is not always a bad thing though! Being happy with who you are and showing confidence will help you look taller in itself! 
          </p>
          <p>
           
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Vyoma Vyas
           </strong>
          </p>
