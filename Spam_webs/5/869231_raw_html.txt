     <h1>
      Welcome to Quilt in a Day
     </h1>
     <p>
      Since 1978 with the publication of my first quilt book
      <em>
       Make a Quilt in a Day   Log Cabin Pattern
      </em>
      , I have written over 100 quilt books and quilt patterns.  My
      <a href="http://www.quiltinaday.com/shoponline/books.asp">
       quilt books &amp; patterns
      </a>
      teach a wide variety of quilt techniques: strip piecing, applique, scrap quilting, crazy quilts, paper piecing, piecing stars, triangles, and squares. You will find numerous quilt border treatments, methods to bind a quilt, tie a quilt, layer a quilt, miter a binding corner, and so much more.
      <em>
       <img align="right" alt="Eleanor Burns" src="imgs/homepage/el.jpg" width="200"/>
      </em>
      Many of my quilt books and quilt patterns contain yardage and cutting instructions for baby quilts, lap quilt, twin quilts, queen quilts, and king quilts. On my Quilt in a Day website  you will find everything you need to make a quilt, whether you are a beginning quilter or advanced quilter. You will find quilt books,
      <a href="http://www.quiltinaday.com/shoponline/patternst.asp">
       quilt patterns
      </a>
      , specialty
      <a href="http://www.quiltinaday.com/shoponline/rulers.asp">
       quilting rulers
      </a>
      , quilting DVD s,
      <a href="http://www.quiltinaday.com/shoponline/notions.asp">
       quilting notions
      </a>
      , quilt novels, quilt related gifts and hundreds of bolts of
      <a href="http://www.quiltinaday.com/shoponline/fabric.asp">
       quilt fabric
      </a>
      ! If you are a beginning quilter, I suggest my books
      <em>
       Make a Quilt in a Day Log Cabin Pattern
      </em>
      ,
      <em>
       Flying Geese Quilt in a Day
      </em>
      ,
      <em>
       Sunbonnet Sue Visits Quilt in a Day
      </em>
      , and
      <em>
       It s Elementary
      </em>
      to get you started. Or try my quilt pattern
      <em>
       Tossed Nine Patch
      </em>
      ,
      <em>
       Tee Shirt Quilt
      </em>
      , or
      <em>
       Pinwheels &amp; Rectangles
      </em>
      . A wide variety of quilt kits are available to make your quilting adventure even easier to get started! Looking for
      <a href="http://www.quiltinaday.com/freepattern/">
       free quilt block patterns
      </a>
      ? You will find a variety of them here on the website. Also feel free to watch my 200+
      <a href="http://www.quiltinaday.tv/tv/default.asp">
       quilting videos
      </a>
      of my television series and our live filmed
      <a href="http://www.quiltinaday.tv/tv/default.asp">
       quilting block party
      </a>
      for free!
      <br/>
      <br/>
      I invite you in to kick off your shoes and take a tour around my website to find everything a quilter needs, wants, and more!
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <em>
       Eleanor Burns
      </em>
      <br/>
      <img alt="Eleanor Burns" height="35" src="imgs/homepage/el_sig.jpg" width="183"/>
     </p>
