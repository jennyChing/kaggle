        <img alt="Married &amp; More With Michelle; Photography by Laurel McConnell" src="http://o.aolcdn.com/smp/cache/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/last2.jpg" title="Married &amp; More With Michelle; Photography by Laurel McConnell" width="600"/>

       <p>

        <a href="http://www.farmkitchen.com/" target="_blank" title="Farm Kitchen ~ Wedding and Event Venue">
         Farm Kitchen

       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Married &amp; More With Michelle; Photography by Laurel McConnell" src="http://o.aolcdn.com/smp/cache/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/last-b.jpg" title="Married &amp; More With Michelle; Photography by Laurel McConnell" width="602"/>
       </p>
       <p>

        <a href="http://www.stylemepretty.com/2009/04/21/real-wedding-country-chic-dinner-party-at-the-farm-kitchen-darcy-matt/" target="_blank" title="Leaf Favors">

         </a>
        </em>
        , or the scrumptious
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <a href="http://www.stylemepretty.com/2009/04/21/real-wedding-darcy-matt-at-the-farm-kitchen-iii/" target="_blank" title="Herban Feast Catering; Photography by Laurel McConnel">

        </span>
