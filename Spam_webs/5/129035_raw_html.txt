        <h1 class="entry-title">
         9 Ways To Stay Organized In Small Apartment
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <div align="center">
         <script type="text/javascript">

        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         When you live in a small apartment, the most important thing is to stay organized. Otherwise, your place will look like a junkyard. You dont want that, right? We have some great ideas for you that will help you stay organized in small apartment.
        </p>
        <h2>
         1. Wall Shelf Units
         <br/>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Wall-Shelf-Units.jpg">
          <img alt="Wall Shelf Units" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5828" height="966" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Wall-Shelf-Units.jpg" width="736"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         If you dont have much space to organize all your things, why not go with wall shelf units? They look great and you can use them to store all those belongings you dont know where else to put. It will look awesome if you put them around the door. Color combination is just brilliant!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.ikea.com/us/en/catalog/products/20163779/?cid=us|af|pinterest.com|20163779_20130716" target="_blank" title="Wall Shelf Units">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Source
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         2. A Charging Station
         <br/>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/A-Charging-Station.jpg">
          <img alt="A Charging Station" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5831" height="733" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/A-Charging-Station.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         The biggest problem in apartments are often our electronics. So many cords all around the place, how to keep them in control? Why dont you build a charging station? Looks great, dont you agree?
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bhg.com/decorating/storage/organization-basics/savvy-ways-to-stay-organized/#page=4" target="_blank" title="A Charging Station">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Source
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         3. Study Room
         <br/>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Study-Room.jpg">
          <img alt="Study Room" class="aligncenter wp-image-5827" height="379" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Study-Room.jpg" width="309"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         You dont have to have big apartment to have a little corner for working or studying. You can have an organized study place in a small apartment too. Put organizers on your wall to keep the clutter away!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/388717011560402256/" target="_blank" title="Study Room">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Source
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         4. An Over-the-Faucet Shelf
         <br/>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/An-Over-the-Faucet-Shelf.jpg">
          <img alt="An Over-the-Faucet Shelf" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5829" height="625" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/An-Over-the-Faucet-Shelf.jpg" width="625"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         This is a great way to use the space that is otherwise completely wasted. The plastic trays are removable and easy to clean, so you dont have to worry about that.
        </p>
