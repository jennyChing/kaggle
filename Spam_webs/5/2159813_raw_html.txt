        <h1>
         Minor League Transactions: January 16th  22nd
        </h1>
       </div>
       <div class="entry">
        <a href="http://prosportsextra.com/2014/01/26/minor-league-transactions-january-16th-22nd/">
        </a>
        <div class="clear">
        </div>
        <p>
         Last week there were quite a few minor league transactions around baseball. In case you missed them, here is
         <a href="http://www.baseballamerica.com/" target="_blank">
          Baseball Americas
         </a>
         full list of transactions made in the minors from January 16th to January 22nd:
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Arizona Diamondbacks
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
         </b>
         <b>
          Signed:
         </b>
         1B Shelley Duncan
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Baltimore Orioles
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Signed:
         </b>
         1B Carlos Andres Diaz, 2B Alexi Casilla (re-signed)
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Chicago Cubs
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         1B Lars Anderson (released by White Sox, July 18), OF Chris Coghlan (non-tendered by Marlins)
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Chicago White Sox
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         RHP Brian Omogrosso (re-signed)
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Cincinnati Reds
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         2B Hernan Iribarren
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Cleveland Indians
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         OF Nyjer Morgan (Yokohama (Japan))
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Kansas City Royals
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         RHP Guillermo Mota (did not play in 2013), RHP Brad Penny (did not play in 2013)
         <br/>
         <b>
          Released:
         </b>
         RHP Harol Rosario
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Los Angeles Angels
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         RHP Lianmy Galan, LHP Mark Mulder (has not played since 2008), C Anderson de la Rosa, SS John McDonald
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Los Angeles Dodgers
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         RHP Mark Pope, C Miguel Olivo, 2B Ryan Adams (did not play in 2013), OF Trayvon Robinson
         <br/>
         <b>
          Released:
         </b>
         RHP Simon Binns
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Miami Marlins
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         OF Angel Morales
         <br/>
         <b>
          Released:
         </b>
         RHP Junior Rincon, LHP Patrick Merkling, OF Connor Burke, OF Ryan McIntyre
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Milwaukee Brewers
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         1B Lyle Overbay, 1B Mark Reynolds
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          New York Mets
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         RHP Giancarlo Alvarado (re-signed), LHP John Lannan, SS Omar Quintanilla (non-tendered by Mets)
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Oakland Athletics
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         C Dusty Brown (did not play in 2013)
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Pittsburgh Pirates
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         C Erick Fernandez (released by Nationals back on July 22)
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          San Diego Padres
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         OF Travis Buck (re-signed)
         <br/>
         <b>
          Traded:
         </b>
         RHP Matt Andriese, RHP Brad Boxberger, RHP Matt Lollis, 2B Logan Forsythe and 2B Maxx Tissenbaum to Raysfor RHP Jesse Hahn and LHP Alex Torres
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          San Francisco Giants
          <br/>
          Released:
         </b>
         LHP Randy Zeigler
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Seattle Mariners
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         C Manny Pina
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Tampa Bay Rays
          <br/>
          Traded:
         </b>
         RHP Jesse Hahn and LHP Alex Torres to Padresfor RHP Matt Andriese, RHP Brad Boxberger, RHP Matt Lollis, 2B Logan Forsythe and 2B Maxx Tissenbaum
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Texas Rangers
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         LHP Rafael Perez
         <br/>
         <b>
          Released:
         </b>
         RHP Tyler Tufts
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Toronto Blue Jays
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         RHP Jose Diaz, RHP Luis Miguel Gomez, RHP Jeffry Martinez, RHP Luis Miguel Sanchez, RHP Thony Vinicio, LHP Erick Hurtado (released by Yankees back on June 17), SS Junior Vasquez, OF Francisco Javier Rodriguez
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Washington Nationals
          <br/>
          Signed:
         </b>
         RHP Clay Hensley, LHP Zach Jackson
        </p>
