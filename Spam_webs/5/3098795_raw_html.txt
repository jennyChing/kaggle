       <h1 class="entry-title">
        White Chocolate GHOSTIE Spoons
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2012-09-09T15:00:00+00:00">
         September 9, 2012
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://yesterdayontuesday.com/author/malia/" rel="author">
           Malia
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">

          18 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="6817727" data-link="http://yesterdayontuesday.com/2012/09/white-chocolate-ghostie-spoons/" data-summary="" data-title="White Chocolate GHOSTIE Spoons">
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Theres nothing frightful about these little Ghostie Spoons!
         <br/>
         <img alt="White Chocolate Ghostie Spoons - Yesterday on Tuesday" src="//yesterdayontuesday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/6a0120a538ca82970c017744900a96970d.jpeg" title="White Chocolate Ghostie Spoons - Yesterday on Tuesday"/>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         You can pull them together quicker than you can say Trick or Treat. Ghostie Spoons are fun on their own and particularly delicious in a hot drink.
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Ghostie Spoon Supplies - Yesterday on Tuesday" src="//yesterdayontuesday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/6a0120a538ca82970c017c31b251fd970b.jpeg" title="Ghostie Spoon Supplies - Yesterday on Tuesday"/>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         1 package Bakers White Chocolate (or 6 oz white chocolate)
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.wilton.com/store/site/product.cfm?sku=710-0017" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.wilton.com/store/site/product.cfm?sku=710-0017', 'Wilton Candy Eyeballs']);" target="_self">
          Wilton Candy Eyeballs
         </a>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         12 white or clear spoons
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         12 large white cupcake wrappers
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         scissors + double sided tape
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         large plastic Ziploc bag
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Plastic Spoons - Yesterday on Tuesday" src="//yesterdayontuesday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/6a0120a538ca82970c017c31b257eb970b.jpeg" title="Plastic Spoons - Yesterday on Tuesday"/>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Line up the spoons with the handles resting on a baking pan (so the chocolate doesnt spill over).
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Spoons in Action - Yesterday on Tuesday" src="//yesterdayontuesday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/6a0120a538ca82970c017c31b261b0970b.jpeg" title="Spoons in Action - Yesterday on Tuesday"/>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Put 6 oz of white chocolate the microwave a microwave safe bowl and pop in the microwave, stirring at 1 minute and then 30 second intervals until its melted. Pour into a plastic Ziploc bag and snip off the end.
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Spoons Hardening - Yesterday on Tuesday" src="//yesterdayontuesday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/6a0120a538ca82970c017744902456970d.jpeg" title="Spoons Hardening - Yesterday on Tuesday"/>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Squeeze chocolate onto spoon and add eyes. Let the chocolate set for at least half and hour. To make the paper outfit cut into the cupcake liner and cut out the center. Put a small piece of tape on the back of the spoon and tightly wind the liner around, secure with another piece of tape at the end.
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Ghostie Spoon Display - Yesterday on Tuesday" src="//yesterdayontuesday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/6a0120a538ca82970c017c31b2721d970b.jpeg" title="Ghostie Spoon Display - Yesterday on Tuesday"/>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Ghostie Spoons make a tasty in a cup of hot chocolate EVEN BETTER!
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Ghostie Spoon Hot Chocolate  - Yesterday on Tuesday" src="//yesterdayontuesday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/6a0120a538ca82970c017d3be105b0970c.jpeg" title="Ghostie Spoon Hot Chocolate  - Yesterday on Tuesday"/>
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         Arent they cute? You gotta love a treat with personality!
        </div>
        <div style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="White Chocolate Ghostie Spoons Lustreware - Yesterday on Tuesday" src="//yesterdayontuesday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/6a0120a538ca82970c017d3be112e7970c.jpeg" title="White Chocolate Ghostie Spoons Lustreware - Yesterday on Tuesday"/>
        </div>
