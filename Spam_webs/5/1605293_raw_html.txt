       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Be Quick or Be Squashed
       </h1>
       <!-- .entry-title -->
       <div class="entry-content clearfix">
        <p style="text-align: right;">
         <a class="btn btn-info medium square" href="http://www.sahmreviews.com/disclosures/non-monetary-compensation" target="_blank">
          Disclosure
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I get embarrassed when I have to admit it sometimes takes us a long time to publish a game review. The delays can be for a number of reasons: Our calendar is full, there was an issue with the game, or maybe we just didnt have much good to say. In the case of our latest featured game, Squashed, I can proudly say the reason was none of the above. The real reason? The game is selling so well that PlaSmart keeps running out and hasnt been able to get us a copy!
        </p>
        <p>
         So lets back up a little. Last fall we attended the Chicago Toy &amp; Game Fair and met our friends from PlaSmart. They had a number of new offerings they were showcasing (see
         <a href="http://www.sahmreviews.com/2014/01/highlights-chitag-2013-morphology.html" target="_blank">
          Morphology
         </a>
         &amp;
         <a href="http://www.sahmreviews.com/2013/12/highlights-chitag-2013-drop-shot.html" target="_blank">
          Drop Shot
         </a>
         ) and provided us with copies at the show. We also had the opportunity to interview the inventor of Squashed, Mr. Nick Metzler, who was kind enough to take questions from one of our budding reporters.
        </p>
        <div class="video-shortcode clearfix">
         <div class="video-post-widget">
          <iframe src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/r3m-6dVIaWU?autohide=1&amp;showinfo=0">
          </iframe>
         </div>
         <!-- /video-post-widget -->
        </div>
        <!-- /video-shortcode -->
        <p>
         The publics reaction to the game at ChiTag was so great they were worried they didnt bring enough of them to sell (they were correct). After restocking (then running out again) and restocking once more our copy finally arrived last week. We couldnt wait to tear into it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://amzn.to/1ytzNcf" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Squashed is a new game from PlaSmart that combines strategy with good old smashing! - SahmReviews.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5725" height="404" src="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Squashed-Box.jpg" width="712"/>
         </a>
         I guarantee you have never played a game like this one. Sure there are plenty of land on your opponent and send them back to start games out there, but none that let you physically SMASH your opponents piece off of the game board.
        </p>
        <p>
         The premise is simple  be the last man standing. This is King of the Mountain in a 3D cube. Each player (up to 4) starts with four pieces placed on each horizontal side of the cube.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://amzn.to/1ytzNcf" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Squashed is a new game from PlaSmart that combines strategy with good old smashing! - SahmReviews.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5726" height="404" src="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Squashed-Starting.jpg" width="712"/>
         </a>
         The King piece is place on the top and play begins. Each person rolls a special die numbered from 1-3 and moves a piece (or pieces by splitting the roll) the matching number of holes. You can move in any direction horizontally, vertically, around a corner, etc.  every direction except diagonally. You can even change direction in a move.
        </p>
        <p>
         In this game you are trying to land on another player to squash them into the center of the cube and out of play.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://amzn.to/1ytzNcf" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Squashed is a new game from PlaSmart that combines strategy with good old smashing! - SahmReviews.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5727" height="404" src="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Squashed-Squishing.jpg" width="712"/>
         </a>
         This squashing part is actually quite fun, you put your piece on top of the other one, push hard and they pop through the cube, landing in the middle.
        </p>
        <p>
         Heres the catch. You can also land in one of the four gray spaces surrounding the king. This allows you to rotate the cube on one side and squashing EVERYONE on that face of the cube!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://amzn.to/1ytzNcf" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Squashed is a new game from PlaSmart that combines strategy with good old smashing! - SahmReviews.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5728" height="404" src="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Squashed-Squashing.jpg" width="712"/>
         </a>
         In this scenario our youngest eliminated one piece from each of her opponents in one move! After they are all Squashed, the king is removed from the side and placed back at the top of the cube. Play continues until there is only one piece left!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://amzn.to/1ytzNcf" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Squashed is a new game from PlaSmart that combines strategy with good old smashing! - SahmReviews.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5729" height="404" src="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Squashed-Winning.jpg" width="712"/>
         </a>
         Each game only takes 5-10 minutes at best (including setup) which makes it perfect for a quick game or two before bedtime. The entire unit is well-built from hard plastic and holds up well to the squashing. Rated for ages 6+, our smaller-than-normal 9-yr-old did have some difficulty removing the pieces from the board to move them, but it didnt sour her from enjoying the game.
        </p>
        <p>
         Very simply, this is a FUN game and one we really cant compare to anything else in our collection. Highly-rated on
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00EC901O0/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00EC901O0&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=lawheecomusedveh" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Amazon
         </a>
         (and almost out of stock there again!), this is definitely one that should be on your wish list. PlaSmart has a number of other great products available on their
         <a href="http://plasmarttoys.com/home.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          website
         </a>
         , and be sure to check out their
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/plasmart" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Facebook
         </a>
         and
         <a href="https://twitter.com/plasmart" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Twitter
         </a>
         pages to keep tabs on what they have in store for us this year. With the weather finally warming up Im sure youll be seeing their line of
         <a href="http://plasmarttoys.com/product-plasmacar.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          PlasmaCars
         </a>
         on your neighborhood streets!
        </p>
