          You know whats cool?
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           This:
          </strong>
          Learning that a toddler can dance better than I could ever hope to pretend to even remotely dream of accidentally falling on myself in a way that looks like its almost a dance move.
         </p>
         <p>
          This is unreal.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone" height="240" src="http://media.tumblr.com/tumblr_me9akw54rB1rwt0uq.gif" title="Tony Stark is freaking out" width="279"/>

         <p>
          I present to you
         </p>
         <h2>
          <strong>
           THE COOLEST TODDLER EVER
          </strong>
         </h2>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="431" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/pceio4hOmFQ" width="575">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          My wife wants to put Finn into dance classes immediately. I was on the fence before but this kid has a freeride to getting attention from WHOEVER and WHATEVER he wants. The world is his disco-ball-enshrined oyster. So, maybe Im caving on it.
         </p>
         <p>
          ::busts a super white-boy move::
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone" height="143" src="http://i.imgur.com/DYuUc.gif" title="Bored Cat" width="210"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Or maybe I should stick to nodding my head to music.
         </p>
         <p>
