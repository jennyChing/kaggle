         <h2>
          List Of The Biggest Libraries In The World
         </h2>
         <h3>
          With the great advancement of the Internet today, we do not depend much on visiting a local library to do actual researches from various books or reading materials. Unfortunately, we must also remember that a library is the most useful reference when searching for complicated word, pieces of codes and complete data from the different parts of the world. Because of this, we really have to spend a little time to discover some of the biggest libraries in the world and reveal what they can offer us.
         </h3>

            <b>
             The Library of Congress
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The Library of Congress is one of the biggest and amazing research libraries in the world. It is actually the national library of America located in Washington D.C. If you get the chance to visiting this library, it boasts some of the biggest and rarest book collections in the world. Likewise, the Library of Congress features some the best series of books produced by famous authors. This library has over 151. 8 million different reading materials and if you visit here, you will definitely feel the satisfaction.

           <p class="listDes">
            <b>
             The British Library
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The British Library is one of the biggest libraries in the world and it is located in London, United Kingdom. This is UKs national library and boasts more than 14 million different books in various languages and impressive patterns from the different parts of the world. It has over 170 millions combined collections of numerous books, journals, periodicals, maps, databases, drawings and a lot more inside this huge library. This is the reason why the British Library is one of the most respected libraries worldwide.

           <p class="listDes">
            <b>
             The New York Public Library
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The New York Public Library is one of the biggest libraries in the world and it is worth mentioning this remarkable library. It features more than 11 million books from numerous authors worldwide. Being a public library in New York City, it has the capacity to provide the best histories, researches and information to the public visiting here. Because it has other branches all over New York, it is easier for the people to gain access to the numerous books it has. Both affluent and private individuals funded this library for the past years of its existence.

           <p class="listDes">
            <b>
             The Russian State Library
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The Russian State Library is Russias very own national library located in the beautiful place of Moscow. It is one of the biggest libraries in the world and the largest library in Russia with more than 17.5 million of different book collections. This remarkable library boasts more than 43 million of various items from almost 247 languages in the different parts of the world. When we talk about the available foreigner books inside this huge library, they represent around 29 percent of the total book collections.
            <br/>
           </p>

           <p class="listDes">
            <b>
             The National Diet Library
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The National Diet Library is Japans only available national library and recorded as one of the biggest libraries in the world. In 1948, they established this huge to provide the best assistance for the members of the Diet of Japan. These people are doing various research matters of the exact public policy. This huge library has more than 36 million of book collections and has the same purpose like the Library of Congress in the United States. According to some reports, the National Diet Library is a very strict place because it does not allow people bringing their cameras inside.
           </p>

            <b>
             The National Library of China
            </b>
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The National Library of Chine is one of the worlds biggest libraries founded in 1909, which is located in Beijing. In 1912, this huge library welcomed the public and presented more than 26.3 million collections of different books that can definitely stun countless of professional average readers around the world. The most interesting available books here are the best and historical Chinese writings, besides revealing the greatest Dynasty stories, literature, ancient documents, discovered bones and shells all over China.
