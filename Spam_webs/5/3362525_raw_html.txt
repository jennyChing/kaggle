       <div id="ssm_node_title">
        9 TIME-SAVING BEAUTY SHORTCUTS
       </div>
       <div class="jcarousel jcarousel-skin-wh">
        <ul data-nextss="/beauty/natural-beauty-tips" data-prevss="" id="ssmax">
         <li class="slide" data-body="&lt;p&gt;If your daily hair and makeup regimen rivals Beyonces, its time to lighten your load: An overblown beauty routine could be making you late in the morning and keeping you up longer at night. Use these time-saving tricks to look gorgeous in a flash.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a onclick=&quot;$('.jcarousel-next').click()&quot; style=&quot;cursor:pointer;cursor:hand&quot;&gt;&lt;img src=&quot;http://www.womenshealthmag.com/files/best-summer-bodies/images/summer-bodies-start.gif&quot; /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
" data-body-bottom="" data-title="">
          <div class="slide-image" style="width:500px;;">
           <a class="img-slide">
            <img src="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/files/wh6_uploads/images/beauty-shortcuts-intro.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <!-- /image -->
         </li>
         <li class="slide" data-body='&lt;p&gt;Seriously, we wont tell. If youre in a rush and have zero time to wash and blow dry, spray on dry shampoo instead, says New York City makeup artist Mari Shten. Apply at the roots then blow dry for a minute or two to get a little volume. And for those really bad hair days, try this &lt;a href="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/beauty/how-to-do-a-sock-bun" target="_blank"&gt;crazy-simple, J-Lo-inspired sock bun&lt;/a&gt;.&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
' data-body-bottom="" data-title="Skip the shampoo">
          <div class="slide-image" style="width:500px;;">
           <a class="img-slide">
            <img src="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/files/wh6_uploads/images/02-dry-shampoo.jpg"/>
           </a>
           <div class="image-credit">
            Shutterstock
           </div>
          </div>
          <!-- /image -->
         </li>
         <li class="slide" data-body='&lt;p&gt;Even when youre low on time, you shouldnt skip this stepespecially during the &lt;a href="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/beauty/winter-skin-care-tips" target="_blank"&gt;dry winter months&lt;/a&gt;. Just choose a lighter, water-based formula since these will absorb into your skin faster, says Shten.&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
' data-body-bottom="" data-title="Speed up your moisturizer">
          <div class="slide-image" style="width:500px;;">
           <a class="img-slide">
            <img src="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/files/wh6_uploads/images/03-speed-up-moisturizer.jpg"/>
           </a>
           <div class="image-credit">
            Shutterstock
           </div>
          </div>
          <!-- /image -->
         </li>
         <li class="slide" data-body="&lt;p&gt;If youre really in a hurry, mix your moisturizer with a little concealer in your hands to make a tinted formula, says Shten. Apply it evenly on your face and you may not even need foundation!&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
" data-body-bottom="" data-title="Combine two steps">
          <div class="slide-image" style="width:500px;;">
           <a class="img-slide">
            <img src="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/files/wh6_uploads/images/04-combine-steps.jpg"/>
           </a>
           <div class="image-credit">
            Shutterstock
           </div>
          </div>
          <!-- /image -->
         </li>
         <li class="slide" data-body="&lt;p&gt;Theres nothing more genius than a makeup palette you can use for your eyes, lips, and face, says Shten. They may cost a little more, but having this all-in-one tool will streamline your beauty routine. And dont be afraid to get creativeyou can blend a creamy lip color on your cheeks for blush and use a light, shimmery eye shadow as a highlighter, says Shten.&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
" data-body-bottom="" data-title="Look for one-stop products">
          <div class="slide-image" style="width:500px;;">
           <a class="img-slide">
            <img src="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/files/wh6_uploads/images/05-one-stop-products.jpg"/>
           </a>
           <div class="image-credit">
            Shutterstock
           </div>
          </div>
          <!-- /image -->
         </li>
         <li class="slide" data-body='&lt;p&gt;Unless youre a professional, you probably spend more time fumbling with all those makeup brushes than actually using them. Shten suggest sticking with &lt;a href="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/beauty/cream-makeup" target="_blank"&gt;cream formulas&lt;/a&gt; (for everything from eye shadow and blush to foundation) when youre in a hurry since theyre way easier to blend. Just use a makeup sponge or your fingertips!&lt;/p&gt;
' data-body-bottom="" data-title="Opt for creams">
          <div class="slide-image" style="width:500px;;">
           <a class="img-slide">
            <img src="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/files/wh6_uploads/images/06-opt-for-creams.jpg"/>
           </a>
           <div class="image-credit">
            Shutterstock
           </div>
          </div>
          <!-- /image -->
         </li>
         <li class="slide" data-body='&lt;p&gt;When you have, like, 10 seconds to get ready, dont try to rush through your usual makeup routineyoull probably look like a sloppy, morning-after version of yourself. Instead, Shten suggests three simple steps for the biggest impact: under-eye &lt;a href="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/beauty/anti-aging" target="_blank"&gt;concealer&lt;/a&gt;, a swipe of mascara, and a touch of blush. Youll brighten up your eyes and look totally fresh faced in no time.&lt;/p&gt;
' data-body-bottom="" data-title="Focus on your cheeks and eyes">
          <div class="slide-image" style="width:500px;;">
           <a class="img-slide">
            <img src="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/files/wh6_uploads/images/07-focus-cheeks-eyes.jpg"/>
           </a>
           <div class="image-credit">
            Shutterstock
           </div>
          </div>
          <!-- /image -->
         </li>
         <li class="slide" data-body="&lt;p&gt;Spray bottles arent just for sunscreen anymore: St. Ives and Vaseline now make spray-on body lotion. The next time youre running late, just spray some on your arms and legs, rub it in, and youre good to go.&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
" data-body-bottom="" data-title="Apply lotion faster than ever">
          <div class="slide-image" style="width:500px;;">
           <a class="img-slide">
            <img src="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/files/wh6_uploads/images/08-apply-lotion-faster.jpg"/>
           </a>
           <div class="image-credit">
            Shutterstock
           </div>
          </div>
          <!-- /image -->
         </li>
         <li class="slide" data-body="&lt;p&gt;We know, it sounds like trap, but theres a speedier way. Grab some makeup remover wipes instead, says Shten. Theyll wisk away dirt, oil, and even that heavy-duty waterproof mascara.&lt;/p&gt;
" data-body-bottom="" data-title="Skip your nightly face wash">
          <div class="slide-image" style="width:500px;;">
           <a class="img-slide">
            <img src="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/files/wh6_uploads/images/09-skip-face-wash.jpg"/>
           </a>
           <div class="image-credit">
            Shutterstock
           </div>
          </div>
          <!-- /image -->
         </li>
         <li class="slide" data-body='&lt;p&gt;Youre tired enough at night so dont overcomplicate your evening skincare routine. There are a ton of two-in-one products that pack great benefits in less time (and take up less space). Check out these &lt;a href="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/beauty/eye-makeup" target="_blank"&gt;two-in-one eye treatments&lt;/a&gt; and other &lt;a href="http://blog.womenshealthmag.com/beauty-style-buzz/beauty-products-that-work-overtime/" target="_blank"&gt;multi-use beauty products&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
' data-body-bottom="" data-title="Get two treatments in one">
          <div class="slide-image" style="width:500px;;">
           <a class="img-slide">
            <img src="http://www.womenshealthmag.com/files/wh6_uploads/images/10-two-treatments.jpg"/>
           </a>
           <div class="image-credit">
            Shutterstock
           </div>
          </div>
          <!-- /image -->
         </li>
        </ul>
