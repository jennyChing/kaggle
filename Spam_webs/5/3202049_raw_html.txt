            <p>
             <a href="http://www.visualnews.com/?p=18335">
              <img alt="" src="http://cdn.visualnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Natalie_Irish_4.png"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p style="text-align: justify;">
             <strong>
              <a href="http://www.natalieirish.com/Home_Page.php">
               Natalie Irish
              </a>
              likes art so much, she just cant help kissing it!
             </strong>
             Using just her lips as her brush and lipstick as paint, shes been creating some bold portraits of famous icons like Marilyn Monroe and Jimi Hendrix. Check out the video below to see her tender technique and be sure to catch the end: her lipstick covered face is priceless!
             <span id="more-18335">
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="366" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/0ra5kRL6iuk?rel=0" width="590">
             </iframe>
             <br/>
             <img alt="" src="http://cdn.visualnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Natalie_Irish_1.png"/>
             <br/>
             <img alt="" src="http://cdn.visualnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Natalie_Irish_2.png"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             Before painting with her lips, Natalie did some finger-painting: the great portrait of Roy Rogers below, was made using only thumbprints.
             <br/>
             <img alt="" src="http://cdn.visualnews.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Natalie_Irish_3.png"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             Update: Natalie released a new video creating her painting Influence  an uncanny representation of Jimi Hendrix (thanks to DB for the tip!)
