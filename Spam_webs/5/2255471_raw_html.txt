          <p>
           In this episode of "Ask the Expert" we talk with fitness training guru, Lori Delf, AFAA about KIDS ZUMBA! Lori holds classes in the Auburn, NY area and has a large following - and for good reason, she's an AWESOME instructor!
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Certified in Zumba Sentoa, Aqua Zumba, Kids Zumba, PiYo, Turbo Kick, and Insanity, Lori draws people of all ages and fitness levels and helps them get to the next level of fitness.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Watch and learn from Lori as she shares some of the benefits of KIDS ZUMBA including kids getting active, falling in love with exercise, learning healthy exercise &amp; nutrition habits, and social interaction. In addition, Lori makes her Kids Zumba classes an educational experience.
           <br/>
