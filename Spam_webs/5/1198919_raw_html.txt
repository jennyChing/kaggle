     <h1>
      101 Important Questions To Ask Yourself
     </h1>

       <p>
        The key to wisdom is knowing all the right questions.  John Simone
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       What questions do you ask yourself every day?
      </p>

      <p>
       Believe it or not, the kind of questions you ask determine the kind of life you lead. Thats because your questions trigger its own set of answers, which lead to certain emotions, which then lead to certain actions (or inactions), followed by results. If you ask yourself limiting questions, youll get limited results. If you ask yourself mind-opening, forwarding questions, youll gain a lot more out of them.
      </p>
      <p>
       Some people like to ask questions like Why am I so unlucky?, What if I had done this earlier? and Why am I always in such a situation?. Because these questions are backward looking, the answers you get with them are naturally disempowering. On the other hand, questions like What can I learn from this experience?, What can I do differently move forward? and What am I grateful for? are empowering. They provoke you and get you thinking, putting you on the path to a better life.
      </p>
      <p>
       If youre someone who normally doesnt self-reflect, you might be stumped by questions. However, this is perfectly normal. I remember when I was a small primary school student, the teacher asked us how we would describe ourselves. The question got me stumped, because I had never thought much about it. It took me some time to find some meaningful adjectives to describe myself, but even then they didnt seem right. That was one of the starting points of my self-exploration journey. Somewhere along the way, I became a big fan of introspection, enjoying the process of self-reflecting and thinking over questions.
      </p>
      <p>
       Today, I believe questions are a key to self-awareness and personal growth. In my 1-1 coaching sessions, I enable my clients to discover the answers to their goals/questions by asking them the right questions. Itd be easy if I just tell my coachees what they should do and what they should not do. But thats not the point. Its about empowering my clients to discover themselves through asking the right guiding questions, catered to their situation and their profiles. The right questions will help them untangle the blockages in them and
       <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/finding-your-inner-self/">
        connect with their inner self
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       It doesnt matter if you get stumped in the beginning, or if you dont know the answers to the questions at first. Our minds are boundless. Just because you dont get an answer immediately, it doesnt mean there are no answers. The answers are there  its just that your mind is too foggy since youve not consulted it before. The more you ask, the clearer your mind will be, and the more ready youll be to receive the answers.
      </p>
      <p>
       Here are 101important questions for your self-reflection, by yours truly. Manyare questions that I ask my 1-1 clients during our coaching sessions to help them uncover their inner self. These questions will help you focus on whats important to you, and what you want. They are not meant to be answered all at one shot  they are to be reflected duly over time, as part of your growth journey. Pick out a few questions to reflect on today, then bookmark and check back a week later to think over the others. Check back regularly to reflect on the questions.
      </p>
      <p>
       Remember, there is no one final answer. Its a continuous discovery process. Your answers to the questions today will be different from your answers one month, three months, six months, and one year down the road. Add these important questions to your
       <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/goal-achievement-review/">
        weekly review sessions
       </a>
       . This is a highly important article for your growth journey.
      </p>

       </li>
       <li>
        What is your ideal diet?
       </li>
       <li>
        What do you need to do to achieve your ideal diet?
       </li>
       <li>
        What is your ideal home like?
       </li>
       <li>
        What do you need to do to achieve your ideal home?
       </li>
       <li>
        What is your
        <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/weight-loss-tips/">
         ideal physical look
        </a>
        ?
       </li>
       <li>
        What do you need to do to achieve your ideal physical look?
       </li>
       <li>
        What is your ideal life?
       </li>
       <li>
        What can you do to start living your ideal life?
       </li>
       <li>
        What would you want to
        <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/letter-to-future-self/">
         say to yourself 1 year in the future
        </a>
        ?
       </li>
       <li>
         3 years?
       </li>
       <li>
         5 years?
       </li>
       <li>
         10 years?
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="300" src="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/images/birthday.jpg" width="500"/>
       </li>
       <li>
        What do you
        <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/why-overcome-fear/">
         fear
        </a>
        most in life?
       </li>
       <li>
        Is there anything you are
        <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/escapism/">
         running away from
        </a>
        ?
       </li>
       <li>
        Are you settling for less than what you are worth? Why?
       </li>
       <li>
        What is your inner dialogue like? (Day 26 of
        <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/30bbm/">
         Be a Better Me in 30 Days Program
        </a>
        is about uncovering your limiting thoughts.)
       </li>
       <li>
        What limiting beliefs are you holding on to?
       </li>
       <li>
        Are they helping you achieve your goals?
       </li>
       <li>
        If not, is it time to let them go?
       </li>
       <li>
        What empowering beliefs can you take on to help you achieve your goals?
       </li>
       <li>
        What bad habits do you want to break?
       </li>
       <li>
        What
        <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/21-day-trial/">
         good habits
        </a>
        do you want to cultivate?
       </li>
       <li>
        What are the biggest actions you can take now to create the biggest results in your life?
       </li>
       <li>
        Where are you living right now  the past, future or present?
       </li>
       <li>
        Are you living your life to the fullest right now?
       </li>
       <li>
        What is the meaning of life?
       </li>
       <li>
        What is
        <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/life-purpose/">
         your purpose in life
        </a>
        ? Why do you exist? What is your mission?
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="300" src="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/images/mission.jpg" width="500"/>
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        How can you make your life more meaningful, starting today?
       </li>
       <li>
        What drives you?
       </li>
       <li>
        What are the times you are most inspired, most motivated, most charged up?
       </li>
       <li>
        What did you do during those times?How can you do more of that starting today?
       </li>
       <li>
        How can you change someones life for the better today?
       </li>
       <li>
        Who are the 5 people you
        <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/average-of-5-people/">
         spend the most time with
        </a>
        ?
       </li>
       <li>
        Are these people enabling you or holding you back?
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="300" src="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/images/people.jpg" width="500"/>
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        What qualities do you want to embody? (Refer to Q7: Values and Q11: Ideal Self)Where can you
        <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/new-friends/">
         meet people
        </a>
        who embody these qualities?
       </li>
       <li>
        Who inspire you the most?
       </li>
       <li>
        How can you be like them?
       </li>
       <li>
        What is your ideal life partner like?
       </li>
       <li>
        Where can you find him/her?
       </li>
       <li>
        How can you get to know him/her?
       </li>
       <li>
        Are you
        <a href="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/why-overcome-fear/">
         afraid
        </a>
        of letting others get close to you?
       </li>
       <li>
         Why?
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="300" src="http://personalexcellence.co/blog/images/fear.jpg" width="500"/>
        </p>
       </li>
       <li>
        Who is/are the most important person(s) to you in the world?
       </li>
       <li>
        Are you giving them the attention you want to give?
       </li>
       <li>
        How can you spend more time with them starting today?
       </li>
       <li>
        What kind of person do you enjoy spending time with?
       </li>
       <li>
        How can you be this person to others?
       </li>
       <li>
        Who do you want to be like in 1 year?
       </li>
       <li>
         3 years?
       </li>
       <li>
         5 years?
       </li>
       <li>
         10 years?
       </li>
       <li>
        Who are your mentors in life? (formal or informal)
       </li>
       <li>
        What is one thing youre going to do differently after reading this article?
       </li>
      </ol>
