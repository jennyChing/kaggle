         <h2 class="inner_heading_bg">
          <span>
           Late Push Leaves the US Stock Market Higher
          </span>
         </h2>
         <!-- .entry-meta -->
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <a href="http://onlineroboticstocktrader.com/">
           <img alt="Stock Trading Robot" class="alignleft wp-image-539" height="368" src="http://onlineroboticstocktrader.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/4-29-2014-5-52-46-PM.png" width="641"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          There was a probability that the health care industry in the US could bear witness to a merger. At the same time, the Bank of America had some bad news to offer. Amidst such highs and lows, traders were able to keep their enthusiasm in balance and this is the key reason behind the stocks ending up in higher values.You can manage this with the help of
          <strong>
           <a href="http://onlineroboticstocktrader.com/cooltrade/features/" target="_blank">
            Best Stock Trading Robots
           </a>
           .
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          It is important that traders refrain from acting hastily in light of circumstances indicative of high benefit or considerable loss. By maintain composure and a balanced approach, traders can easily survive the rapidly fluctuating dynamics of the market. Additionally, this subtle reaction would also prevent the market from plunging into dire conditions. Panic selling or over enthusiastic buying has always been the precursor of major market mishaps.
         </p>
         <p>
          Whenever any news of paramount importance is received, the trader should conduct analysis of the market based on his understanding and then the results should be correlated to arrive at an apt conclusion. Acting hastily on the news could result in loss. Similarly, the news of mergers should also be taken with appropriate caution.
         </p>
         <p>
          There is a possibility, that the merger could either be stopped at the last minute or the benefits of the merger would not be realised. In both of these situations, it is the investor which has the most to lose. So it is best to check out the finer details of the information before any investment is made. This would prove to be a life saver in the long run.
         </p>
         <p>
          Here are some interesting statistics that are shaping the market as we speak.
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <i>
            The Dow Jones industrial average rose by 0.5% or 87 points with the closing value standing at 16,448 on Monday.
           </i>
          </li>
          <li>
           <i>
            The Standard &amp; Poors 500 index rose by 0.3% or 6 points to 1869. The NSDAQ composite, however, came down by a single point to 4074. The most of the 61 point loss was erased by the index.
           </i>
          </li>
          <li>
           <i>
            Pfizer had made another effort to acquire its rival, AstraZeneca, with an offer standing at $100 billion. This resulted in the stock value of Pfizer rising by over 4%.
           </i>
          </li>
          <li>
           <i>
            As a result of the occurrence of a financial error, the Bank of America was forced to suspend its plans for increasing its dividend value and purchasing as many stocks as possible out of its own offerings. This resulted in decline in valuation by over 6%.
           </i>
          </li>
          <li>
           <i>
            The bond prices have also exhibited a decline. The yield that was reaped from the 10 year old Treasury Notes have increased substantially to over 2.70%
           </i>
          </li>
         </ul>
