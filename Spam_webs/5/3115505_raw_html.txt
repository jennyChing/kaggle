             Normally a bite doesn't mean love, a bite normally means they have had too much attention. Even if it's just one pet and they bite, or you pet them in a wrong place that is sore or uncomfortable or it's a trained behavior that they know of. Watch to see if he bites because of a length of time that you pet him, or that you pet him in a spot that cats hate, or a hand shaking or playfulness he may misinterpret. Sometimes you have to act like an older act and when he bites, make a yipping sound and swat. NOT HIT. Don't think I mean hit. It's just a way for mother cats to let their kits know that it hurts. That's all. it's really effective!
