       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Whats a Little Arts &amp; Crafts Without Some String Theory?
       </h1>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-content">
       <hr class="single-hr"/>
       <div class="single-meta">
        <!-- call year / month for archive link -->
        <a href="http://www.thinkdope.com/author/garett/">
         Garett Southerton
        </a>
        <span class="seper">
         |
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.thinkdope.com/2014/09/" title="Posts This Month">
         September 10th, 2014
        </a>
        <span class="seper">
         |
        </span>
        <a href="#commentsarea" title="Scroll to Comments">

         </span>
        </a>
       </div>
       <hr class="single-hr"/>
       <p>
        Zach Strop is a 20 year old producer from Kokomo, Indiana. Known musically as Stropulous Beats, Zach just dropped his
        <a href="https://soundcloud.com/stropulous/sets/arts-crafts-ep" target="_blank" title="Stropulous Beats - Arts &amp; Crafts EP">
         <em>
          Arts &amp; Crafts EP
         </em>
        </a>
        full of instrumentals including this dope rap/EDM crossover track called
        <em>
         String Theory
        </em>
        .
        <br/>
        <span id="more-3139">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        What I like most about
        <em>
         String Theory
        </em>
        is the way the strings and vocal sample mesh. Its reminiscent of a 90s hip hop track with a little bit of a modern pop vibe. Its some cool stuff. Listen to the full track below;
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe frameborder="no" height="400" scrolling="no" src="https://w.soundcloud.com/player/?visual=true&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fapi.soundcloud.com%2Ftracks%2F160279904&amp;show_artwork=true&amp;maxwidth=500&amp;maxheight=750&amp;in=stropulous%2Fsets%2Farts-crafts-ep" width="500">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        If you like
        <em>
         String Theory
        </em>
        you can check out the rest of
        <a href="https://soundcloud.com/stropulous/sets/arts-crafts-ep" target="_blank" title="Stropulous Beats - Arts &amp; Crafts EP">
         Arts &amp; Crafts EP on Zachs SoundCloud
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        What do you think of Zach Strops
        <em>
         String Theory
        </em>
        ? I want to hear your thoughts in the comments below!
       </p>

         <h2 style="color: #fff;">
          3 Songwriting Tips For Anyone Struggling
         </h2>
         <div>
          <p>
           Alright! Heres my quick perspective on songwriting. There isnt any one way or secret that one can bring about the process of penning a great song or lyric on command. If there were, its probably locked up somewhere in a chest at the bottom of the ocean surrounded by great white sharks and deadly algae. I wish I wrote nothing but absolute gold all the time, but this isnt always the case (and thats okay). Ive compiled 3 songwriting tips for anyone struggling to find value and confidence in their work. Sometimes songwriters need that reminder!
