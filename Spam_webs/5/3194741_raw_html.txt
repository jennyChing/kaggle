           <p>
            <img alt="resize 7" class="alignright wp-image-9183" height="405" src="http://doterrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/resize-71.jpg" style="margin: 10px;" width="270"/>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             There is no need to pull out your rubber gloves and surgical masks when you clean your home. It might surprise you to know that you can concoct simple and inexpensive non-toxic cleaners from ingredients already lying around your house. Using essential oils to create your own cleaning spray is not only safe on the environment, but is safe for your family as well. This spray is perfect for cleaning hard surfaces in the kitchen, bathroom, etc.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: large;">
             <strong>
              All Purpose Disinfecting Spray:
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             <strong>
              What You Need:
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             16 oz. glass spray bottle (you can purchase one
             <a href="https://www.aromatools.com/Glass_Plastic_s/3.htm">
              here
             </a>
             )
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             1/4 cup white vinegar
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             1 3/4 cups water
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             30 drops d
            </span>
            
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             TERRA essential oil (see suggestions below)
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             1 teaspoon borax
            </span>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <img alt="resize 1" class="size-full wp-image-9184 aligncenter" height="428" src="http://doterrablog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/resize-11.jpg" width="644"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             <strong>
              Essential oils to try:
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             A combination of any of the above essential oils would work great in your homemade cleaning spray. Experiment and find what works best for you.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             <strong>
              Some recommended combinations include:
             </strong>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             15 drops each of Lavender and Lemon
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             10 drops each of Eucalyptus, Peppermint, and Wild Orange
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             30 drops On Guard blend
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="font-size: medium;">
             15 drops each of Grapefruit and On Guard
            </span>
           </p>
