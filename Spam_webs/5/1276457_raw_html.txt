       <div class="post-content">
        <p>
         The Otomi people, descendants of the Mayan Olmec and Toltecs of Mexico, prophesized the
         <a href="http://indigenousaction.blogspot.com/2008/02/8000-drums-on-march-21-at-noon-central.html" target="_blank">
          healing of Mother Earth
         </a>
         will begin when 8,000 sacred drums are played together. This inscription was found in a cave located on an Otomi sacred ceremonial site. The Otomi people consider sound as a form of prayer and a basis for their spiritual practices.  In 2004 the first modern ceremony was held and the prophecy of healing began. To continue the energy of this
         <a href="http://www.universidadindigena.org/uii/id70.html" target="_blank">
          healing ceremony
         </a>
         , indigenous peoples and followers of indigenous traditions worldwide will drum together on Friday, March 21 at 2 p.m. EST.
        </p>
        <p>
         You can participate by drumming alone or with others, while holding the intent of Earth healing.
        </p>
