          <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
           Homemade Crisps
          </h1>
          <p class="entry-meta">
           <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-02-26T14:52:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
            February 26, 2013
           </time>
           by
           <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
            <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.alifeofgeekery.co.uk/author/vicki" itemprop="url" rel="author">
             <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
              Vicki Higham
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="entry-comments-link">
            <a href="http://www.alifeofgeekery.co.uk/2013/02/homemade-crisps.html#comments">
             29 Comments
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
         </header>
         <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
          <div>
           <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-rnAzaLNkKTg/USzGMfJ8DBI/AAAAAAAABNc/Mij4kktpJbo/s1600/homemadecrisps.jpg">
            <br/>
            <img alt="" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/4.bp.blogspot.com/-rnAzaLNkKTg/USzGMfJ8DBI/AAAAAAAABNc/Mij4kktpJbo/s640/homemadecrisps.jpg?resize=800%2C535"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <p>
           It hit lunch time today and I had a massive craving for a good bag of crisps. I opened the cupboard to a grim scene; no crisps, not even a cracker. Whats a girl to do?
          </p>
          <p>
           If you know me well enough you know Ill have a go at anything. So today it was time try my hand at making crisps. I dont have the energy to mess around with frying things, I need things to be quick and simple, so armed with two small
           <a href="http://albertbartlett.co.uk/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Albert Bartlett
           </a>
           Rooster potatoes, here is how I did it:
          </p>
          <p>
           Thinly slice your lovely reds (or whites if you fancy) on a mandolin  if you dont have one, your cheese grater might have a slice function.
          </p>
          <div>
          </div>
          <div>
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.alifeofgeekery.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/blogger/-7UJLi0aw5XE/USzGOm1KTJI/AAAAAAAABNk/syme5NCI04A/s1600/potatoslices.jpg">
            <img alt="" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.alifeofgeekery.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/blogger/-7UJLi0aw5XE/USzGOm1KTJI/AAAAAAAABNk/syme5NCI04A/s640/potatoslices.jpg?resize=800%2C535"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <p>
           Blot the starchy liquid off between sheets of kitchen paper and spread around the outside of a microwavable plate.
          </p>
          <div>
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.alifeofgeekery.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/blogger/--30OVhHinvs/USzGL6XC0pI/AAAAAAAABNM/S9x7Rq1vhxo/s1600/onplate.jpg">
            <img alt="" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.alifeofgeekery.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/blogger/--30OVhHinvs/USzGL6XC0pI/AAAAAAAABNM/S9x7Rq1vhxo/s640/onplate.jpg?resize=800%2C535"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div>
          </div>
          <p>
           Ping for 4mins, turn and repeat until just slightly golden. Youll need to keep an eye on them, mine take 6mins at 750w.
          </p>
          <p>
           Transfer to a cooling rack to really crisp up.
          </p>
          <div>
           <a href="http://i1.wp.com/www.alifeofgeekery.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/blogger/-yjTvBsH1kZo/USzGL66I9uI/AAAAAAAABNQ/mkXbY3RWQyw/s1600/oncoolingtray.jpg">
            <img alt="" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.alifeofgeekery.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/blogger/-yjTvBsH1kZo/USzGL66I9uI/AAAAAAAABNQ/mkXbY3RWQyw/s640/oncoolingtray.jpg?resize=800%2C535"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <p>
           Now its time to season. I kept things simple with just salt and pepper and boy were they good! My cute little crisp packet is just made from folded up baking paper  perfect to shake them around in.
          </p>
          <p>
           Youre done, probably under 10mins. They havent seen a drop of fat or any sneaky flavourings but youd never know that these werent the real deal.
          </p>
          <p>
           If youre counting calories, or just looking to rustle up a quick snack, I dare you not to love these.
          </p>
          <div>
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.alifeofgeekery.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/blogger/-O4FqllNayNc/USzGQHEuPwI/AAAAAAAABNw/Fkj3hxYj7DI/s1600/bagofcrisps.jpg">
            <img alt="" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.alifeofgeekery.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/blogger/-O4FqllNayNc/USzGQHEuPwI/AAAAAAAABNw/Fkj3hxYj7DI/s640/bagofcrisps.jpg?resize=800%2C535"/>
           </a>
          </div>
