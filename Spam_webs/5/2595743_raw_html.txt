       <p style="color: #333333;">
        Safer Roads for Pedestrians requires Fairer Compensation
       </p>
       <p style="color: #333333;">
        <strong>
         Road traffic victims call for civil justice reform
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="color: #333333;">
        Whilst most efforts this week will focus on reducing the risk of death or injury to those walking, the European Federation of Road Traffic Victims Association (FEVR) and Roadpeace are highlighting what can be done to reduce the toll on those injured whilst walking and cycling. They have collected information from their members and organisations representing cyclists on how national civil compensations systems treat pedestrians and cyclists injured in a crash.
       </p>
       <p style="color: #333333;">
        press release:
        <a href="http://www.fevr.org/new/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/RP-FEVR-Stricter-Liablity-briefing-press-release-1c.pdf">
