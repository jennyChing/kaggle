         <h1>
          Which states are motorcycle helmets required by law?
         </h1>
         <p>
          State Laws vary with their requirements concerningwhetheror not a rider is capable ofdeterminingif it he or she should wear a helmet while on a motorcycle. Some states have and agerequirementof 21, while others is 18.
          <strong>
           A few states have no motorcycle helmet laws or requirements at all.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          To easilynavigatethese laws, Ive put together an interactive chart, at the bottom of the page, with all 50 state helmet laws, andCanada, Mexico, andEurope. (Europe, I am still a littlefuzzyon, but I hope to get someanswerscleared up and posted here in the future). Thisevolvedfrom my own need to know which state has what laws when I started planning a 50 states in 50 days road tripacrossthe entire US.
          <em>
           More to come on that later.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Sons of Anarchy infographic on motorcycle helmet laws by the badasshelmetstore.com" class="size-full wp-image-363 aligncenter" data-lazy-src="http://badasshelmetstore.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Sons_of_Anarchy_infographic-by-the-badass-helmet-store.jpg" height="4089" src="http://badasshelmetstore.com/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="623"/>
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Sons of Anarchy infographic on motorcycle helmet laws by the badasshelmetstore.com" class="size-full wp-image-363 aligncenter" height="4089" src="http://badasshelmetstore.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Sons_of_Anarchy_infographic-by-the-badass-helmet-store.jpg" width="623"/>
         </noscript>
         <p>
          A partial law refers to states that require additional medical coverage if you choose not to wear a helmet. This also varies from state to state and may change. Your motorcycle insurance provider will notify you if this is adjusted and/or revised. States with partial laws have aminimumage requirement for this option.
         </p>
