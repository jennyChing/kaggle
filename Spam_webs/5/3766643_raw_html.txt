             <h3>
              Managing Chronic Fatigue Syndrome Symptoms with the Trivedi Effect
             </h3>
             <em>
              January 18th, 2014
             </em>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p align="JUSTIFY">
              <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif;">
               <span style="font-size: medium;">
                <span style="color: #333333;">
                 Do you feel tired most of the time? Even a short nap or rest does not take away the fatigue? Do you think you are suffering from chronic fatigue syndrome symptoms?Medical experts explain that tiredness or fatigue is one of the most common symptoms for a diverse range of illnesses.
                </span>
                <a href="http://www.trivedieffect.com/get-relief-from-chronic-fatigue/">
                 Chronic fatigue syndrome symptoms
                </a>
                <span style="color: #333333;">
                 can seriously affect a persons life and as yet are not fully understood by science. No definitive way of managing this condition is currently available as symptoms can be different for each person.
                </span>
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p align="JUSTIFY">
              <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif;">
               <span style="font-size: medium;">
                <img align="BOTTOM" alt="Chronic Fatigue" border="0" class="aligncenter" height="200" name="graphics1" src="http://www.trivedieffect.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/chronic-fatigue_001.jpg" width="300"/>
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p align="JUSTIFY">
              <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
               <span style="font-size: medium;">
                A wellness phenomenon that is changing the world has been reported to have brought successful transformation to people known to have been suffering from this condition. This phenomenon known as The Trivedi Effect has already helped tens of thousands of people to improve the quality of their lives.
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p align="JUSTIFY">
              <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
               <span style="font-size: medium;">
                For many suffering from Chronic fatigue syndrome symptoms life can become a struggle. Fatigue can be so intense that even everyday tasks can become insurmountable. Relationships, career, finances, education, sexual, physical, mental and emotional wellbeing can all be severely impacted by this condition.
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p align="JUSTIFY">
              <span style="color: #333333;">
               <strong>
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  What are CFS symptoms?
                 </span>
                </span>
               </strong>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p align="JUSTIFY">
              <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
               <span style="font-size: medium;">
                Medical experts identify chronic fatigue syndrome when a person suffers from consistent tiredness; not just the tiredness one would feel after a full day of work or poor quality sleep.
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p align="JUSTIFY">
              <span style="color: #333333;">
               <strong>
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  Symptoms include:
                 </span>
                </span>
               </strong>
              </span>
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  feeling of severe exhaustion and sickness following physical or mental activities
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  Disturbed sleep
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  Poor concentration and memory retention
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  Headaches
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  Experiencing pain in muscles or joints
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  Swollen lymph nodes in the armpit and neck
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  sore throat
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  Mental Fogginess
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  Inability to remain standing for long
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  Urge to faint
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  Blurred or distorted vision
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
              <li>
               <p align="JUSTIFY">
                <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
                 <span style="font-size: medium;">
                  Depression and panic attacks
                 </span>
                </span>
               </p>
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p align="JUSTIFY">
              <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
               <span style="font-size: medium;">
                <b>
                 Current ways of managing Chronic Fatigue
                </b>
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p align="JUSTIFY">
              <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
               <span style="font-size: medium;">
                Conventional methods of managing
               </span>
              </span>
              <a href="http://www.trivedieffect.com/chronic-fatigue-syndrome/">
               <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif;">
                <span style="font-size: medium;">
                 c
                </span>
               </span>
               <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif;">
                <span style="font-size: medium;">
                 hronic fatigue syndrome
                </span>
               </span>
              </a>
              <span style="font-family: FreeSans,sans-serif; color: #333333;">
               <span style="font-size: medium;">
                symptoms include the use of prescribed medication e.g. antidepressant medicines combined with psychotherapy treatments like cognitive behavior therapy or counseling. Relaxation strategies like meditation, yoga, music therapy are often utilized as well as other therapies like reiki healing, hypnotherapy, acupuncture, qi gong etc. Often people with this condition are given nutritional advice and are encouraged to follow small exercise regimes.
