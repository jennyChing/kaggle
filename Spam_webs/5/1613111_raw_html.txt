            <h1>
             Content Management System
            </h1>
            <p>
             Content management system (CMS) is a software that assist you to maintain the track of each and every piece of your content on the website.
            </p>
            <p>
             Web Content can be simple text, photos, music, video, documents, or just about anything you can think of. A major advantage of using a Content Management System(CMS) is that it requires almost no technical skill or knowledge to manage. Since the CMS manages all your content for you, you don't have to.
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div id="po" style="height:875px;">
           <div class="subnav-2">
            <p style="margin-top:15px;">
             Corporate or Enterprise Content Management (ECM) enables extraction, management, storage and delivery of content or documents related to processes of organization by using various tools. We offer content management solutions to help organizations associate diverse content into a single centrally managed store with perfect data categorization which helps enhance operational efficiency.
            </p>
            <h2 style="margin-bottom: 0in">
             Types of content management :
            </h2>
            <ul style="margin-bottom: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             <li>
              Documents and records management (DMS)
             </li>
             <li>
              Media asset management
             </li>
             <li>
              Web content management
             </li>
            </ul>
            <h2 style="margin-top: 0.3in">
             WHY Ketu software Content Management System (CMS)?
            </h2>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             Navigate - Edit - Save:
            </h3>
            <p>
             Updating your website with Ketu software (CMS)Content Management System is very instinctive. All pages are organized in a  hierarchical tree that reflects the site navigation, so the page you want to edit can be found easily, make the changes and click Save. It's that simple!
            </p>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             "What you see is what you get" Editor:
            </h3>
            <p>
             The built-in editor provides editors with all tools they need to create and update content:
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Text formatting (bold, italics, color, font, headings, etc.)
             </li>
             <li>
              Insert image
             </li>
             <li>
              Insert movie
             </li>
             <li>
              Insert YouTube video
             </li>
             <li>
              Create link
             </li>
             <li>
              Insert on-line form, poll or other dynamic object
             </li>
             <li>
              Spell checker and many others
             </li>
            </ul>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             Images and Media:
            </h3>
            <p>
             Inserting an image into the text is as easy as clicking, selecting the image and resizing the image as you need. Even if image comes directly from your camera, no worries, since Ketu software will automatically resize it for the required dimensions. You can also upload music, publish movies and YouTube videos.
            </p>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             Built-in Image Editor:
            </h3>
            <p>
             Ketu software Content Management System(CMS) comes with a built-in image editor that allows you to quickly rotate, resize or crop image and even convert it to different format. Means you don't have to use an external image editor for basic image manipulation.
            </p>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             Widgets:
            </h3>
            <p>
             Widgets enable the content editors, so that they can variate the layout of the page and add dynamic features, such as forms, boxes, banners with latest news, etc. It provides them more elasticity since the editors can make these changes by themselves without waiting for a developer.
            </p>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             Rank Higher with
             <a href="/it-services/business-software-applications/search-engine-optimization.aspx">
              Search Engine Optimization (SEO):
             </a>
            </h3>
            <p>
             Ketu software allows you to build websites that are highly optimized for search engines like Google, Yahoo &amp; Bing and improve ranking in the search engine result pages due to:
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              SEO friendly URLs
             </li>
             <li>
              Customizable page title, keyword and descriptions
             </li>
             <li>
              Google Sitemap
             </li>
             <li>
              Customizable ALT and TITLE attributes
             </li>
             <li>
              Headings H1, H2, etc. supported in the editor
             </li>
             <li>
              Clean (X)HTML code and CSS-based menu
             </li>
             <li>
              Fast page load
             </li>
            </ul>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             Delegate Content Authoring without Losing Control:
            </h3>
            <p>
             With Ketu software CMS, you can represent the content management to other people while keeping control over what is published. It allows you to describe custom workflows for particular site sections and content types and grant users with permissions for the chosen documents. All changes are tracked and you can easily figure out who modified the content. These features allow you to keep control over content quality and brand consistency.
            </p>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             Versioning, Archiving:
            </h3>
            <p>
             Ketu software keeps track of all versions for the updated pages, so you can roll back to a previous version or compare versions. Removed pages can be archived so that they are no longer displayed and you can view them whenever you want or re-publish them.
            </p>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             Go Global with Multilingual Content:
            </h3>
            <p>
             Ketu software CMS helps you to easily create websites in multiple languages. Different languages are no problem due to the support for UNICODE encoding. Also, Ketu Software CMS is translated into many languages, so your colleagues in other countries can use it, too.
            </p>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             Structured Content and Content Re-Use:
            </h3>
            <p>
             Looking forward to publish structured documents with certain fields, such as press releases or product specifications, you can use custom document types. They give you full control over fields and their types and allow you to easily re-use the content across the website and display it using various transformations.
            </p>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             Manage All Your Websites from a Single Point:
            </h3>
            <p>
             With Ketu software CMS, you can direct all your websites from a single point. Whether you need a corporate site, on-line store, microsite or on-line community, you can quickly launch new sites, maintain brand consistency and share content across sites.
            </p>
            <br/>
            <p>
             With Ketu software multi-site management, you can manage all your sites from a single place and redistribute new sites in the matter of couple of hours.
            </p>
            <h3 style="font-weight: bold;margin-left: 0in;margin-top:0.2in">
             Web Content Scheduling:
            </h3>
            <p>
