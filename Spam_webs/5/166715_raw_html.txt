                              <h1 class="entry-title">
                               Paleo Strawberry Crumble
                              </h1>
                             </div>
                             <div class="metabar">
                              <div class="metabar-pad">
                               <em>
                                <time class="date time published updated sc" datetime="2015-02-16T05:00:00+00:00">
                                 Feb 16, 2015
                                </time>
                               </em>
                              </div>

                           <div class="entry_content">
                            <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
                            <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="15439516" data-link="http://fakeginger.com/2015/02/16/paleo-strawberry-crumble/" data-summary="" data-title="Paleo Strawberry Crumble">
                            </div>
                            <p>
                             <img alt="" class=" aligncenter" height="800" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7417/16542702182_3e6eb8b9e1_c.jpg" width="536"/>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Well guys, its time to start another week. Hopefully you at least got to sleep a little late today. And I hope you had a wonderful Valentines Day.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             We spent our Valentines morning at the park. The husband took the kids to the playground and I took the dogs to the dog park, like we usually do. And then we spent a couple hours texting each other photos of the kids and the dogs and others peoples dogs. #crazydoglady
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             I always forget how awkward the dog park is. Like, Im bad at small talk to begin with but small talk while also picking up poop and politely looking away when someones dog tries to get frisky?
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             The worst.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             We went to our
                             <a href="http://instagram.com/p/zGfcWlPlsW/?modal=true">
                              fave Mexican place
                             </a>
                             for dinner and then I forced my husband to go to REI to get new running shoes because hes been complaining about his since before this last deployment. A full year. Of hating his running shoes. And telling me about it.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             So he got new shoes and then we got Dairy Queen because I haaaad to try the Red Velvet Blizzard before it went away.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             It was pretty perfect.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             After all the Mexican food and the mojitos and the ice cream and the entire box of Ferrero Rocher chocolates, I need a small detox. But I cannot detox without at least a few treats in there and this Paleo Strawberry Crumble is amazing for situations like that.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Theres nothing bad in it and its absolutely scrumptious. I cant get enough coconut flour so I used that in the topping and then some shredded coconut. The top gets all crunchy and the strawberries get juicy and delicious.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Also, why are the strawberries so good so early in the year? We cant get enough!
                            </p>

                                <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
                                 Paleo Strawberry Crumble
                                </div>
                               </div>
                               <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
                                <div class="fl-l width-50">
                                </div>

                                </div>
                               </div>
                               <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
                                Ingredients
                               </p>
                               <span id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 4 cups strawberries, halved
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 2 tablespoons arrowroot starch
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1 teaspoon vanilla
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1 tablespoon lemon juice
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1 tablespoon maple syrup
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5">
                                 For the topping:
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1/2 cup coconut flour
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1/4 teaspoon salt
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 3 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 3 tablespoons maple syrup
                                </div>
                                <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
                                </div>
                               </span>
                               <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
                                Instructions
                               </p>
                               <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
                                <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                 Preheat oven to 350F. Spray an 8x8-inch pan with coconut oil.
                                </li>
                                <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                 In a large mixing bowl, toss together strawberries, arrowroot starch, vanilla, lemon juice, and maple syrup. Pour into prepared pan.
                                </li>
                                <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                 In a separate bowl, stir together coconut flour, salt, coconut oil, and maple syrup until it sticks together. Stir in shredded coconut. Sprinkle the topping over the strawberries, making sure to cover most of the strawberries.
                                </li>
                                <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                 Bake for about 20 - 25 minutes or until the topping is browned and the strawberries are bubbly.
                                </li>
                                <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                 Let stand about 5 minutes before serving.
                                </li>
                               </ol>
