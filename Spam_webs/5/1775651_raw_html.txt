               </iframe>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
                By presenting this video in its entirety, you may choose to see it from start to finish or skip through it to watch the segments that interest you.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
                In almost all American communities, behavioral standards for the community and amongst police officers are set by the executives in the top level management positions. The City Manager, the Mayor and the Council members are a few that set policy and the Chief of Police usually enforces laws they establish.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <code>
                <a href="http://click.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/click?id=SZuAXQ01aPk&amp;offerid=336475.79&amp;subid=0&amp;type=4">
                 <img alt="Wigsbuy.com" border="0" class="alignleft" data-lazy-src="http://ad.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/show?id=SZuAXQ01aPk&amp;bids=336475.79&amp;subid=0&amp;type=4&amp;gridnum=13" height="250" src="http://snn.bz/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="300"/>
                 <noscript>
                  <img alt="Wigsbuy.com" border="0" class="alignleft" height="250" src="http://ad.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/show?id=SZuAXQ01aPk&amp;bids=336475.79&amp;subid=0&amp;type=4&amp;gridnum=13" width="300"/>
                 </noscript>
                </a>
               </code>
               <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
                But in Ferguson, Missouri like in many unfortunate municipalities, the Police have taken on such a powerful role that their leadership is ahead of the other executives and carries more weight on the streets. They actually carry out and display more silent power than the other Executives who after some time, basically follow.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
                This awkward imbalance of power is one of the reasons explaining why many citizens of Ferguson, Missouri are afraid of the police. It is suspected that there are many citizens that have cell phone videos of the struggle at the squad car between Officer Darren Lewis and Michael Brown. Being fearful of the police is the reason they have not come forward.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
                Right after the shooting of Michael Brown, police were dispatched to visit homes in the immediate area and when they knocked on doors, they were met by mostly women. The police officers questions surrounded whether or not the men in those homes had Facebook accounts. Thats an unusual question but the police thought that those that had filmed the event probably couldnt resist being the first to upload the video to their Facebook accounts.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <code>
                <a href="http://click.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/click?id=SZuAXQ01aPk&amp;offerid=332712.88&amp;subid=0&amp;type=4">
                 <img alt="SuperStarTickets" border="0" class="alignleft" data-lazy-src="http://ad.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/show?id=SZuAXQ01aPk&amp;bids=332712.88&amp;subid=0&amp;type=4&amp;gridnum=9" height="600" src="http://snn.bz/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="160"/>
                 <noscript>
                  <img alt="SuperStarTickets" border="0" class="alignleft" height="600" src="http://ad.linksynergy.com/fs-bin/show?id=SZuAXQ01aPk&amp;bids=332712.88&amp;subid=0&amp;type=4&amp;gridnum=9" width="160"/>
                 </noscript>
                </a>
               </code>
               <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
                In this video, Daniel Page says hes a killer. And that if you dont want to be killed  you should not go near him. He says he has killed many people and that hell kill many more because hes not a coward. That is the personality and behavior displayed by Daniel Page. It is impossible to have worked side-by-side with him and not know this about him.
                <br/>
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
                This same Officer, Page, was the Crowd Control Officer in Ferguson, Missouri for years. He was in charge of controlling crowds during the Michael Brown protests.
