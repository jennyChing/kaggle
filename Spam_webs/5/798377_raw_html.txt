    <br/>
    <h4>
     "Through dedication to our mission of quality patient care and commitment to our employees, clients and 
	  communities, it is our goal to gain the trust and loyalty of those associated with Suwannee Medical Personnel".
    </h4>
    <br/>
    <br/>
    <h4>
     Our Mission
    </h4>
    <p style="padding:6px 25px 0 265px;">
     Suwannee Medical Personnel is committed to being the premier provider of the highest quality supplemental medical staff in all of the markets we service. We are dedicated to improving the quality of patient care.
    </p>
    <br/>
    <br/>
    <h4>
     Our Values
    </h4>
    <p style="padding:6px 25px 0 265px;">
     Customer Service: Superior! You will find our administrative team to be a talented, highly motivated group of professionals that are customer service driven internally and externally.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Our local and traveling teams of medical professionals are valued and respected. We appreciate and honor our employees for their continuing contributions to the communities we serve.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     We are focused on building relationships and partnering with our clients to create flexible solutions to meet a wide range of diverse staffing situations. We understand the challenges healthcare administrators face. We know the devastating effects of the shortage of professional care givers and the pitfalls associated with as-needed staffing. We know your needs are critical! We commit to prompt service, attention to detail, quality candidates, and a customized staffing solution to meet your organizations specific needs.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Our family instilled values of respect, compassion, and integrity have been the key to our success since our establishment in 1988. We will continue operating our business ethically and professionally.
    </p>
