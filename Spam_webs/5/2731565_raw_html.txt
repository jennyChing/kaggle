            Striving for Kindness in 2015
           </a>
          </h1>
          <ul class="post_meta">
           <li class="post_date">

           </li>

             Kate Summers

           <div class="singleimgbox">
            <p>
             The beginning of a new year can be really rough. January and February arent really known for having the best weather, unless your idea of great weather is snow and freezing rain. In addition, after all the excitement and joyous celebration of the winter holidays, the beginning of the year can seem dull at best and bleak at worst. It seems like the only thing there is to look forward to is the eventual thawing of the bad weather into the warmer spring and summer months.
            </p>
            <p>
             But there are some things to make a new year worthwhile. For one, the new year brings with it new years resolutions, which challenge us to become our idealized versions of ourselves. Across the world, people make commitments with themselves to lose weight, to quit smoking, to get themselves out there and make new friends or find that significant other theyve been waiting for. Valentines Day is another highlight, of course.
            </p>
            <p>
             Something you might not know about the beginning of the year is that it is also host to Random Acts of Kindness Day, which takes place on February 17. Random acts of kindness are exactly what they sound like  a kind gesture, with no expectation of reciprocation, given to someone whose path you cross, either directly or indirectly. RAKtivists have started chains of drive-through customers paying for the Starbucks order of the car behind them; they have also left dollar bills in places for others to find, especially those who might need them. Considering the bleakness of winter, this is a great time to give the people around you a reason to smile.
            </p>
            <p>
             If youd like to make performing random acts of kindness a habit,
             <a href="http://www.cleverpedia.com/help-make-2015-year-kindness/">
              Cleverpedia put together the Kindness Challenge
             </a>
             to help motivate you throughout the course of the entire year. Simply print the checklist and work through the 50 kindness prompts at your own pace. The challenge is set up as a years challenge, but you can push yourself to complete it sooner. You can even challenge your friends  who will complete the challenge first?
            </p>
            <p>
             At the end of the challenge, you have the satisfaction of knowing that your actions made 50 different people happy. And what can be better than that?
            </p>
            <p>
             <img alt="kindness" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4849" height="1008" src="http://howtogetarelationship.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/kindness.jpg" width="620"/>
            </p>
            <p>
             The Kindness Challenge was created by
             <a href="http://www.cleverpedia.com/">
              Cleverpedia
             </a>
             . You can follow Cleverpedia on
             <a href="https://www.facebook.com/cleverpedia">
              Facebook
             </a>
             or
             <a href="https://twitter.com/cleverpedia">
              Twitter
             </a>
              be sure to share your challenge progress!
            </p>
