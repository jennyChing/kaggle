           Lighthouses are lonely places. Abandoned lighthouses are the loneliest of the lonely. This derelict lighthouse off the coats of South Wales can be reached at low tide.
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           Its naked, bolted ribs are reminiscent of a design by Lebbeus Woods, the inspiration behind the setting of Alien III and the source of the plagiary in 12 Monkeys.
