           <p>
            <font size="2">
             The first Canadian MBA fair, being organised by the High Commission      of Canada in India in association with the Indo-Canadian Business Chamber      (ICBC) will be held from September 20 to 27, 2014 in Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad      and Mumbai. 11 of Canadas leading business schools will showcase MBA      opportunities during their four-city MBA Showcase Tour to India from September      20 to 27. New Delhi will be the first city to host Canadian MBA fair on September      20, 2014.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span style="font-weight: bold;">
              Participating Canadian Universities
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             The participating universities from Canada include: University of Alberta      (Alberta School Of Business), McGill University (Desautels Faculty of Management),      HEC Montral ,University of Toronto (Rotman School of Management),      University Of British Columbia (Sauder School Of Business),Queen's University      (Queen's School Of Business), University of Western Ontario (Richard Ivey      School of Business),York University (Schulich School Of Business), Concordia      University (John Molson School Of Business), Saint Mary's University (Sobey      School Of Business) and Ryerson University (Ted Rogers School of Management).
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Participation is free of charges but registration to participate is mandatory      and can be done by visiting www.icbc.org.in
            </font>
           </p>
           <p>
            <font size="2">
             <font size="3">
              <a href="http://www.mbauniverse.com/forums/forum/mba-from-abroad-choose-b-school-in-canada/3171-edu-canada-mba-showcase-tour" target="_blank">
               <strong>
                <font color="#009100">
                 MBA      from Abroad: Choose B School in Canada
                </font>
               </strong>
              </a>
             </font>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span style="font-weight: bold;">
              Good Opportunity for MBA aspirants
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             The event will provide an excellent opportunity for young professionals, entrepreneurs      and students aspiring for a foreign management degree. It will enable them      to meet with representatives of Canadian institutions and learn about Canadian      MBA programs. Over the course of this event, attendees can take part in interactive      sessions on a range of subjects such as admissions, visa procedures, and career      opportunities.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span style="font-weight: bold;">
              Canada universities offer quality MBA programmes
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Canadian MBA is considered to be of high quality with credentials that      are globally recognized for providing a world-class education.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             The delegates representing Canadas elite MBA programs as well as representatives      from the High Commission of Canada in India will be available to interact      with MBA aspirants.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span style="font-weight: bold;">
              Destination Canada; choice of many
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Interestingly, Indian students are increasingly seeing Canada as a destination      for a high quality education at an affordable cost, in a safe, welcoming and      multicultural environment. Last year, approximately 14 000 Indian students,      chose Canada for their post-secondary studies.
