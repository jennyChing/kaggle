                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_18o8A2sFF8/VXoeCyrgNdI/AAAAAAAAK5Q/yRBZhp0ZBPE/s1600/1.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_18o8A2sFF8/VXoeCyrgNdI/AAAAAAAAK5Q/yRBZhp0ZBPE/s1600/1.gif"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       Many a times we are confused about the role of a concealer. The way I look at makeup is to balance the colors of our face. Even out the tone and have a smooth evenness. The idea is to have our natural beauty out and not let the distractions be in the way!
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-yVa9wZmGWzQ/VXoeBtDelDI/AAAAAAAAK5A/65HsUHmlOMs/s1600/2.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-yVa9wZmGWzQ/VXoeBtDelDI/AAAAAAAAK5A/65HsUHmlOMs/s1600/2.gif"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       We all have been through the most trying age and that has left its signs on our face. As they say, everything leaves their signs on the face. It sometimes it nice to have a little layer to lighten them.
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-dNIiOsYRmOI/VXoeB_MZKMI/AAAAAAAAK5E/Rnh54ld7AWo/s1600/3.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-dNIiOsYRmOI/VXoeB_MZKMI/AAAAAAAAK5E/Rnh54ld7AWo/s1600/3.gif"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       Especially when it comes to photography, bright eyes look great. It perks up the eyes, and makes it stand out and bright.
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-0ELVwQMB-a0/VXoeE7gndpI/AAAAAAAAK5Y/c18aCD90yCE/s1600/4.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-0ELVwQMB-a0/VXoeE7gndpI/AAAAAAAAK5Y/c18aCD90yCE/s1600/4.gif"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       This is another form of shading just like the eyes. The corners of our nose are usually bit darker or appear so due to the way light falls on our face. By applying the concealer here, it looks even.
                      </div>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     <a href="http://imyownmuse.blogspot.com/2015/06/how-to-start-blog.html">
                      How To Start A Blog
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-1883594580526997066" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-geYvZxUxJuw/VYggU4kajoI/AAAAAAAAK7E/xvdtrma2uzo/s1600/Untitled-1.gif" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-geYvZxUxJuw/VYggU4kajoI/AAAAAAAAK7E/xvdtrma2uzo/s1600/Untitled-1.gif"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      When I wanted to started a blog for the first time I thought and contemplated and pondered and pondered and spent, what looked like an entire lifetime! But the second time around I just did it, and since then I never quit. This has been a process of discovery and learning which I have enjoyed and love. I know that I will continue to do so for a very long time!
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       What you need to know?
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      o Do you really want to do this? It takes up time and effort like you would to raise a baby. You need  to be involved, passionate and your heart right there.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      o What is your blog going to be about? Do you like fashion, cooking, watching TV or just reading blogs? Thats the fun part of it you can write whatever you feel for. But it must be something that you love.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      o Is your blog going to make money? there are success stories all over where bloggers make so much money that its worth quitting a 9-5 job. But the point is whether thats what you want to do. Because if thats what it is, then you have to put in that much effort. You have to have a clean blog and have good content regularly. Because that means traffic.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       So how do we start?
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      o Choose a platform. I like blogger.com as I have complete flexibility to build from scratch. But you should seriously consider Tumblr too, as it has the option to share and like. However I know bloggers who are completely smitten by Wordpress. Choice is yours. If you want help building up a pretty blog, try Wix.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      o Choose a name. Keep it Simple Fun &amp; Meaningful.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      o Create a neat layout. I am permanently re-layouting my blog and playing with designs &amp; elements. Its important to have a neat looking layout because people like to scroll through a page which has things organized. And more importantly it will keep your mind sorted, and know what exactly are you unto with the blog. Apart from that, brands like blogs which are well looked after.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      o Try to optimize the quality of your photos. Today with great internet speed &amp; rich media people are rather used to seeing content with great visual quality. The pictures might be from internet, but improvise it in that case or if it your's try and use simple tools like the Picasa. Its simple and great for pictures. If you are feeling a bit more confident, opt for adobe Illustrator and Photoshop and you can get online courses too!
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      At the end of it, its about the content. Thats why people are there, and thats the reason it means so much to you. This is where your passion lies and it shows. Hope you found this write up useful. If you have any questions, or want to add something then I would appreciate your comments down there in the comment box.

                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       Trust me. It took me a while to cut down to this. I like to carry my brushes and eye curlers, but lets face it. We travel by airplanes with rules and have no servant to carry our stuff around, not that I want one. So here's the breakdown of what I need. A basic coverage- foundation, spot corrections- concealer, blush, powder, eye shadow, eyeliner, mascara and finally the lipstick. I carry 2. The Elizabeth Arden 8 Hour Lip Balm serve as a regular Lip Balm, that gives me a great tint and a darker shade of either red or pink. If you want to carry lesser liquid than this but still skip the brush use the NARS Multiple for cheeks and Tarte Concealer for concealing. Even less? use Lancome's
                       <a href="http://www.lancome-usa.com/Miracle-Cushion/1010654,default,pd.html?dwvar_1010654_color=220%20Buff%20(C)&amp;cgid=makeup-foundation#start=1" target="_blank">
                        Miracle Cushion
