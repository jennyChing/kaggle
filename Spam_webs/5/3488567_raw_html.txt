        Operators
       </h2>

        <div class="slide_operators" id="slide-montagne">
         <div class="home_operators_artwork">
          <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/R6-operators-montagne_211283.png"/>

          </span>
          <h3>
           Montagne
          </h3>
          <p>
           A veteran of the National Gendarmerie, Gilles Tour is the longest-serving GIGN operative. Fiercely protective of his teammates, this gentle giant would not hesitate to throw himself in front of a bullet if it meant protecting his fellow operators from harm.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_operators" id="slide-twitch">
         <div class="home_operators_artwork">

         </div>
         <div class="home_operators_desc">
          <span class="ico">
           <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/Twitch_Badge_211297.png"/>
          </span>
          <h3>
           Twitch
          </h3>
          <p>
           Emmanuelle Pichon is an expert technologist with a penchant for using gadgetry to aid her squad. She enters operations with a small drone capable of delivering disabling electric shocks to hostile forces and their traps.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_operators" id="slide-doc">
         <div class="home_operators_artwork">
          <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/R6-operators-doc_211282.png"/>
         </div>
         <div class="home_operators_desc">
          <span class="ico">
           <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/Doc_Badge_211294.png"/>
          </span>
          <h3>
           Doc
          </h3>
          <p>
           Gustave Kateb is cut from a rare cloth: a genuine altruist, a true believer in making the world a safer place for his fellow man. A volunteer for Doctors Without Borders and medical officer for the Brigade des Forces Speciales Terre, he is a proficient field medic who carries a Stim Pistol allowing him to revive downed teammates from a distance.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_operators" id="slide-rook">
         <div class="home_operators_artwork">
          <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/R6-operators-rook_211284.png"/>
         </div>
         <div class="home_operators_desc">
          <span class="ico">
           <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/Rook_Badge_211296.png"/>
          </span>
          <h3>
           Rook
          </h3>
          <p>
           Julien Nizan is the deliverer of the GIGN's famous trust exercise for new recruits: taking a bullet fired by one of your colleagues with only a small armor plate to protect you. He carries these same armor plates into combat, dropping them for his allies to reinforce their defenses.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_operators" id="slide-thermite">
         <div class="home_operators_artwork">
          <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/R6-operators-thermite_196405.png"/>
         </div>
         <div class="home_operators_desc">

          </span>
          <h3>
           Thermite
          </h3>
          <p>
           Thermite is a prominent demolitions expert recruited from the ranks of the FBI SWAT. He carries a thermite breach charge capable of burning through the most resilient barriers.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_operators" id="slide-castle">
         <div class="home_operators_artwork">
          <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/R6-operators-castle_196404.png"/>
         </div>

          </span>
          <h3>
           Castle
          </h3>
          <p>
           A third generation law enforcement officer, Castle brings an expertise in defense and reinforcement to Rainbow squad, ever ready to deploy his signature reinforced barricades.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_operators" id="slide-ash">
         <div class="home_operators_artwork">
          <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/R6-operators-ash_196403.png"/>

          </span>
          <h3>
           Ash
          </h3>
          <p>
           Upon joining the FBI SWAT, Ash brought her knowledge of demolitions with her in producing a weapon-mounted breach charge capable of blowing open a door or wall at a distance.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_operators" id="slide-pulse">
         <div class="home_operators_artwork">
          <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/R6-operators-pulse_202498.png"/>
         </div>
         <div class="home_operators_desc">
          <span class="ico">
           <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/Pulse_Badge_202497.png"/>
          </span>
          <h3>
           Pulse
          </h3>
          <p>
           A former intern for the FBI biometrics program, Pulse's ability to read people makes him a natural negotiator. He was the first to use the Heartbeat Sensor prototype in the field.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_operators" id="slide-sledge">
         <div class="home_operators_artwork">
          <img src="http://static9.cdn.ubi.com/resource/en-US/game/rainbow6/siege/R6-operators-sledge_196202.png"/>
         </div>
         <div class="home_operators_desc">

          </span>
          <h3>
           Sledge
          </h3>
          <p>
           Sledge is typically found at the front lines of siege operations, ready to create a path for his teammates with one swing of his signature breaching hammer.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_operators" id="slide-mute">
         <div class="home_operators_artwork">

          </span>
          <h3>
           Mute
          </h3>
          <p>
           Mute is a signals intelligence specialist. He uses a tool which allows him to jam all communications in a set area, preventing all remote detonations and usage of drones.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_operators" id="slide-smoke">

           Smoke
          </h3>
          <p>
           Smoke is a fearless thrill seeker in battle and can be a great asset for defensive maneuvers with his detonatable chemical gas charge that deals damage to anyone in the smoke.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
