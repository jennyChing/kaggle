        Comedy Spotlight: Craig Robinson
       </h3>
       <p class="blogdate">

     <div class="entrybody">
      <div class="page-container clearfix">
       <p>
        Craig Robinson is instantly recognizable for his roles on
        <em>
         The Office
        </em>
        and in such movies as
        <em>
         Hot Tub Time Machine
        </em>
        and
        <em>
         Zack and Miri Make A Porno
        </em>
        . However, what you may not know is that Robinson started out as a stand-up comic and he studied improv and acting at The Second City in Chicago. He and fellow comedian Jerry Minor also performed as a musical comedy duo known as L. Witherspoon and Chucky, and the two had performed on HBOs sketch pad and on Comedy Central.
