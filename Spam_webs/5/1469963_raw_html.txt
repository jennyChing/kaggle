           <h2>
            Location
           </h2>
          </div>
          <div class="" id="ownermap">
           <div id="ownermap-mapwrap">
            <div id="ownermap-map" style="position: relative; background-color: rgb(229, 227, 223); overflow: hidden; -webkit-transform: translateZ(0);">
            </div>
            <div class="ownermap-info-box" id="ownermap-infobox" style="display: none;">
             <div class="ownermap-place">
             </div>
             <div id="ownermap-infobox-arrow">
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
           <p>
            Established in 1711, historic downtown Beaufort boasts a scenic waterfront park with an array of alfresco dining options--locally caught fresh seafood, steaks, Lowcountry barbeque, burgers and gourmet flatbread pizza. Relax on the riverfront green space with its swinging benches for a great view of the river and the marina. Sit a spell and watch the bridge swing open for the tall masts of sail boats gliding past on the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, see porpoises play on the tides of the river, and witness breathtaking coastal sunsets over the water.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            To learn about Beaufort's 300-year heritage, take a boat tour along the river, travel through the city's historic streets in a quaint horse-drawn carriage or walk the streets with a guide dressed in period garb. Linger in the quaint downtown shops loaded with antiques, clothing, gifts, furnishings and more. Peruse the many art galleries featuring the works of local Lowcountry artisans.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            Beaufort is the ideal base for all of your Lowcountry and Coastal Empire excursions. The Sea Islands and Atlantic Ocean are just a few miles from downtown, so be sure to visit Hunting Island with its historic lighthouse and sunny ocean beaches. And Beaufort is ideally located for day-trips to Charleston, Savannah and Hilton Head Island--only an hour or so from each destination.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            In Beaufort, you can sit and enjoy the sun and water while you sip your favorite beverage, or you can fill your days with boating, kayaking, beachcombing, fishing, sightseeing and history--it's your choice. Welcome to Beaufort, South Carolina's coastal paradise!
           </p>
