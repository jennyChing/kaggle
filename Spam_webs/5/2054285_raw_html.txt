        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Home Brew
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="posted-on">
          Posted on
          <a href="http://thehappiestsoul.com/home-brew/" rel="bookmark">

           <time class="updated" datetime="2015-05-10T03:33:59+00:00">
            May 10, 2015
           </time>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="byline">
          by
          <span class="author vcard">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <img alt="A Trying Tryst" class="attachment-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" height="200" src="http://thehappiestsoul.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/poem-header.jpg" width="1800"/>
        <p>
         <em>
          by RJ Parker
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Its caught in the lint filter
         <br/>
         so I shook the bottle a little
         <br/>
         got the main part to clear.
        </p>
        <p>
         Its a shook up series of sandstorms
         <br/>
         blowing through the mid-western
         <br/>
         part of the desert, you know
        </p>
        <p>
         An aimless heart captivates worn women
         <br/>
         graven masks carry on though
         <br/>
         seventy-two tanks seek riches
        </p>
        <p>
         All the shallow kids end up digging
         <br/>
         proud ditches and shallow sorrows
         <br/>
         amphitheatre addiction, sufficient.
        </p>
        <p>
         Crawling up and siphoning out are ways
         <br/>
         taken or dealt with, surmised.
         <br/>
         all jump on the cattle call, amy.
        </p>
       </div>
