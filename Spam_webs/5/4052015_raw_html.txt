                   <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
                    <meta content="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-9bFOfzQAAGo/VKIIkJoaQqI/AAAAAAAAJVE/LLXzvhbMA1A/s1600/Southwest_Scramble_Title1.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Southwest Scramble
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7514170937165407880" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-9bFOfzQAAGo/VKIIkJoaQqI/AAAAAAAAJVE/LLXzvhbMA1A/s1600/Southwest_Scramble_Title1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-9bFOfzQAAGo/VKIIkJoaQqI/AAAAAAAAJVE/LLXzvhbMA1A/s1600/Southwest_Scramble_Title1.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Like most of you, my fridge is still full of those decadent holiday leftovers. And like most of you, I am trying find new and creative ways to use them up. That is why tonight I decided I was going to make some delicious omelets. Everything was going swimmingly until it came time for the fold. *sigh* Alas the non-stick coating in my pan is not as non-stick as it used to be, so I had to improvise, but I am so glad I did. What came of this near kitchen disaster was the most delicious
                       <b>
                        Southwest Scramble
                       </b>
                       I have ever tasted. I know you are going to love it so let's get started!
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <a name="more">
                      </a>
                      <u>
                       <b>
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         Southwest Scramble:
                        </span>
                       </b>
                      </u>
                      <br/>
                      <u>
                       <b>
                        *Prep Time: 10 min. *Cook Time: 10 min. *Difficulty: Easy *Servings: 4
                       </b>
                      </u>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <u>
                        <b>
                         <br/>
                        </b>
                       </u>
                       <u>
                        <b>
                         Ingredients:
                        </b>
                       </u>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-guTGqk-DxxY/VKII6G6aqkI/AAAAAAAAJVU/KJA7h-yQ0Yo/s1600/DSC_0903.NEF" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-guTGqk-DxxY/VKII6G6aqkI/AAAAAAAAJVU/KJA7h-yQ0Yo/s1600/DSC_0903.NEF" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <ul style="text-align: left;">
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         1 Dozen Eggs
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         2 c. of Finely Diced Ham
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         2 c. Mixed Pepper, Diced
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         8 Scallions, Sliced
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         4 tbsp. Unsalted Butter
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         1/4 tsp. Garlic Salt
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         1/8 tsp. Chipotle Powder
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         2 c. Cheese, Shredded
                        </span>
                       </li>
                      </ul>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-xGKKEQBY9pY/VKIJFWCGC8I/AAAAAAAAJVc/JIg7OaqkoWo/s1600/DSC_0904.NEF" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-xGKKEQBY9pY/VKIJFWCGC8I/AAAAAAAAJVc/JIg7OaqkoWo/s1600/DSC_0904.NEF" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       This is a quick cooking meal so before you even turn on the stove, take the time to prep all of your meat, cheese, veggies, eggs, and butter.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-4_90hoCiNIA/VKIJKgWNCYI/AAAAAAAAJVk/jKbX62ZYRc0/s1600/DSC_0907.NEF" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-4_90hoCiNIA/VKIJKgWNCYI/AAAAAAAAJVk/jKbX62ZYRc0/s1600/DSC_0907.NEF" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Next, place a skillet over medium/low heat to get hot.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-zvBI6u-Lf6M/VKIJVhXxAUI/AAAAAAAAJVs/uJCuk41ww-8/s1600/DSC_0908.NEF" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-zvBI6u-Lf6M/VKIJVhXxAUI/AAAAAAAAJVs/uJCuk41ww-8/s1600/DSC_0908.NEF" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Add the butter to the skillet and let it melt.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-w9piiAOKpTs/VKIJgiBaXwI/AAAAAAAAJV0/tJ4gZHbJpKI/s1600/DSC_0910.NEF" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-w9piiAOKpTs/VKIJgiBaXwI/AAAAAAAAJV0/tJ4gZHbJpKI/s1600/DSC_0910.NEF" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Next add the ham, scallions, peppers, garlic salt, and chipotle power to the skillet and saute until heated through.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-rjGt5-Fdt_E/VKIJk9xlf0I/AAAAAAAAJV8/m_eQYZnVdng/s1600/DSC_0911.NEF" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-rjGt5-Fdt_E/VKIJk9xlf0I/AAAAAAAAJV8/m_eQYZnVdng/s1600/DSC_0911.NEF" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Now you can pour the beaten eggs over the sauteed veggies and ham.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-KEPb79E8jnQ/VKIJo8w0r2I/AAAAAAAAJWE/_YRCr4agROY/s1600/DSC_0912.NEF" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-KEPb79E8jnQ/VKIJo8w0r2I/AAAAAAAAJWE/_YRCr4agROY/s1600/DSC_0912.NEF" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       With your spatula, slowly stir until the egg whites are fully cooked and the eggs appear creamy.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       I actually add a little shredded cheese to mine as well.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-a4QptrXLDS8/VKIImN8uPYI/AAAAAAAAJVM/Q8J0BFsi9GA/s1600/Southwest_Scramble_Finish.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-a4QptrXLDS8/VKIImN8uPYI/AAAAAAAAJVM/Q8J0BFsi9GA/s1600/Southwest_Scramble_Finish.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Plate them up, garnish with a little of the scallions, and ENJOY!
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       You see, dinner doesn't always have to be big, heavy meals to be good! Something as simple as a few eggs, some leftover ham, and pantry staples can all come to create a culinary delight in your mouth! This
                       <b>
                        Southwest Scramble
                       </b>
                       is creamy, savory, spicy, everything you want in a hearty weeknight meal and I know you are going to love it!
                      </span>
                      <br/>

                      <u>
                       <b>
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         Southwest Scramble:
                        </span>
                       </b>
                      </u>
                      <br/>
                      <u>
                       <b>
                        *Prep Time: 10 min. *Cook Time: 10 min. *Difficulty: Easy *Servings: 4
                       </b>
                      </u>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <u>
                        <b>
                         <br/>
                        </b>
                       </u>
                       <u>
                        <b>
                         <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/thekitchenwife/southwest-scramble?tmpl=%2Fsystem%2Fapp%2Ftemplates%2Fprint%2F&amp;showPrintDialog=1" target="_blank">
                          PRINTABLE RECIPE:
                         </a>
                        </b>
                       </u>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <u>
                        <b>
                         <br/>
                        </b>
                       </u>
                       <u>
                        <b>
                         Ingredients:
                        </b>
                       </u>
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <div>
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                       <ul>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-size: large;">
                          1 Dozen Eggs
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-size: large;">
                          2 c. of Finely Diced Ham
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-size: large;">
                          2 c. Mixed Pepper, Diced
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-size: large;">
                          8 Scallions, Sliced
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-size: large;">
                          4 tbsp. Unsalted Butter
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-size: large;">
                          1/4 tsp. Garlic Salt
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-size: large;">
                          1/8 tsp. Chipotle Powder
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-size: large;">
                          2 c. Cheese, Shredded
                         </span>
                        </li>
                       </ul>
                       <div>
                        <span style="font-size: large;">
                         Directions:
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div>
                        <ol style="text-align: left;">
                         <li>
                          <span style="font-size: large;">
                           Prep all of the ham, cheese, eggs, and vegetables.
                          </span>
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <span style="font-size: large;">
                           Place skillet over medium/low heat and allow skillet to get hot.
                          </span>
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <span style="font-size: large;">
                           Add butter to the skillet and allow it to melt.
                          </span>
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <span style="font-size: large;">
                           Add the ham, vegetables, and spices and saute until heated through.
                          </span>
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <span style="font-size: large;">
                           Next pour the beaten eggs over the ham and vegetable mixture.
                          </span>
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <span style="font-size: large;">
                           With a spatula, slowly stir the egg mixture until the egg whites are fully cooked and the scrambled eggs are creamy.
                          </span>
                         </li>
                         <li>
                          <span style="font-size: large;">
                           Serve and Enjoy!
                          </span>
                         </li>
                        </ol>
                        <div>
