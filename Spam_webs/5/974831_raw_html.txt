          <p>
           Fred Armisen stopped by
           <em>
            The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon
           </em>
           to visit with his old pal from
           <em>
            SNL
           </em>
           . They talked about Freds new role as bandleader for
           <em>
            Late Night with Seth Meyers
           </em>
           and why his fear of heights may have made everyone on the set of
           <em>
            Modern Family
           </em>
           think he was a big old weirdo.

            The Ed Sullivan Show
           </em>
           and reveal just how ahead of the curve the band really was (and not just musically speaking):
          </p>
