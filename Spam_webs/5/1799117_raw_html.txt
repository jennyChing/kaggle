            An Aside | Steampunk Star Wars
           </a>
          </h2>
          <div class="post-info">
           <span class="byline">
            By Aidan Moher
           </span>
           <i class="fa fa-calendar">
           </i>

        <section class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
         <p>
          Okay, this is seriously cool. An artist named Sillof has recreated the
          <strong>
           Star Wars
          </strong>
          cast in a Steampunk style. Click the pictures to embiggen.
         </p>
         <a href="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/sp_z-group-hero1.jpg" rel="lightbox[post-1780]">
          <img alt="Steampunk Star Wars | Heroes" class="center" height="313" src="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/sp_z-group-hero1-500x313.jpg" title="Steampunk Star Wars | Heroes" width="500"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/sp_z_group-villain.jpg" rel="lightbox[post-1780]">
          <img alt="Steampunk Star Wars | Villains" class="center" height="348" src="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/sp_z_group-villain-500x348.jpg" title="Steampunk Star Wars | Villains" width="500"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/Steam_Wars_Jabba__s_Court_by_sillof.jpg" rel="lightbox[post-1780]">
          <img alt="Steampunk Star Wars | Jabba's Court" class="center" height="501" src="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/Steam_Wars_Jabba__s_Court_by_sillof-500x501.jpg" title="Steampunk Star Wars | Jabba's Court" width="500"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/Steam_Wars_Bounty_Hunters_by_sillof.jpg" rel="lightbox[post-1780]">
          <img alt="Steampunk Star Wars | Bounty Hunters" class="center" height="319" src="http://aidanmoher.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/Steam_Wars_Bounty_Hunters_by_sillof-500x319.jpg" title="Steampunk Star Wars | Bounty Hunters" width="500"/>
         </a>
         <div class="quote">
          <p>
           This line is my attempt to redesign the Star Wars universe in an antiquated Victorian style.
          </p>
          <p>
           This line is a three part series.  It features my favorite aestheticold.   I love the clunky old-fashioned look of the industrial era.  It is kind of steampunk, but not really, which is why I changed the name of the line.  It is influenced by Jules Verne, HG Welles, Terry Gilliam, Guillermo Del Toro, etc.   But I feel it has my own unique sensibilities as well.
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          There are detail shots of all of the characters over at
          <a href="http://www.sillof.com/C-Steampunk-SW.htm">
           Sillofs Workshop
          </a>
          , the website of the artist behind the conversions. Even more is available on his
          <a href="http://sillof.deviantart.com/gallery/">
           deviantART Gallery
          </a>
          .
         </p>
