       <h3>
        Description
       </h3>
       <div>
        <p>
         Meet Curly Chops, I think he might be the cutest little ram in the world (and I not just saying that because I made him). He is a cuddly little ram with a very gentle temperament, he won't try to butt your legs and he plays well with others.
        </p>
        <p>
         Approximately 5.5 inches from his bottom to the top of his head and is about 4 inches tubby. His horns are soft and squidgy and around 2-3 inches in width each. He has a soft patch of faux fur on his face that is appliqud on with a machine.
        </p>
        <p>
         His eyes are plastic safety eyes so will not pull off easily and are suitable for 3 years and up.
        </p>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="inspiration">
       <h3>
        Inspiration
       </h3>
       <p>
        Eejits are my babies, each one is made with care and attention to detail and I love giving them new homes to live in.
       </p>
       <p>
        The pattern is one I designed and reuse to create the signature Eejit shape but every time I make one I use different materials so that each individual Eejit is entirely unique. Once they are finished I decide on a name based on what they look like.
       </p>
