        <a href="http://www.howfo.com/residential-solar-panels/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to The Best Residential Solar Panels">
         The Best Residential Solar Panels
        </a>
       </h2>
       <hr/>
       <div class="postmeta">
        <div class="postdate">
         April 10, 2015
        </div>
        <div class="postcats">
         Filed In
         <a href="http://www.howfo.com/category/energy-efficiency/" rel="category tag">
          Energy Efficiency
         </a>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div style="clear:both">
       </div>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr">
        Solar energy provides countless benefits for a lot of homeowners across the globe. The ultimate and the most beneficial among individuals is that it is able to lower the high cost of monthly electric bill by just supplementing the electricity consumed from the local electric company. This kind of energy system promise to stop a dreadful climate change from happening and will be able to provide efficient electricity without any harm done on the environment and on peoples health.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr">
        <a href="http://www.egsolar.com.au/solahart-solar-panels/" target="_blank">
         Solar panel
        </a>
        for home have dropped down its installation cost to 75% which will enable more household to install their own solar panel at their rooftop and enjoy solar energy while spending less of what they have been spending for their monthly electric bill. But the choice of the right solar panel can somehow become a tedious and overwhelming task. Solar cells are the most critical component of a solar panel. It converts the light coming from the sun via photovoltaic materials like silicon.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr">
        The process is like this, when the sunlight hit these photovoltaic materials, the light energy is captured and that energy causes electrons within the material to escape from their position in the atom of silicon or other photovoltaic material. These released electrons are captured to produce an electric current.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr">
        A lot of things must be considered. Things like, the number of appliance that will be used, the habit of using them, the source sunlight, the amount of sunlight and the area where there is abundant sunlight and the total amount of energy consumed in a day. These things are the most basic in order to determine the appropriate solar panel needed to be installed.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr">
        Hiring a solar power installer company will do a lot of help with your quest of the perfect solar panel for your home. They will be able to conduct inspection and assessment to give you an idea of the exact dimension and where to install the solar panel.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr">
        Installation of residential solar panel does not only provide the owner clean, safe and cost-effective electricity but also it adds value to your house. It will also provide a maximum return on investment since installation will require you of a one-time installation expenses but will last for a decade or even more years.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr">
        Residential solar power is the fastest growing solar power category since most household eats up a lot of energy but usually intends to spend less.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr">
        <em>
         When looking for
         <a href="http://www.egsolar.com.au/" target="_blank">
          solar panels installer Bairnsdale
         </a>
         , make sure to get only the company that has built a good name in what they do: installing only top-quality and efficient solar power or hot water systems. Bairnsdale is a perfect place for on-grid solar power systems, which promises lower energy consumption costs by day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Enjoy the state of Victorias rebates too!
        </em>
       </p>
