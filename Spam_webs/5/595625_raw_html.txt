         <p>
          Bond, Im going to need you to be on your toes for this next assignment. This group is very dangerous.
         </p>
         <p>
          Whatever you say, M.
         </p>
         <p>
          Im serious, Bond. Cat ladies are the real deal. Theyve been known to actually persuade spies to abandon their missions and join the cat crusade. They will not stop until the world goes full-on feline!
         </p>

         <p>
          Sodoes that mean I have to keep my paws to myself?
         </p>
         <div style="text-align:center;">
          . . .
         </div>
         <p>
          Yeah, so if the curmudgeonly cat lady thing doesnt pan out for me over the next couple of years, I might just become a full-time, self-employed Bond girl. Seriously! Id have no problem slinking around the house by myself all day long looking all mysterious and sultry like Savarine, the Bond girl from
          <em>
           Skyfall
          </em>
          , the latest installment in the series.
         </p>
         <p>
          Id be totally fine with that.
         </p>
         <p>
          In the movie, which opens this Friday across North America, Savarine is played by French actress Brnice Marlohe. Ive forbidden myself from reading any spoilers, even though I desperately want to (just two more days!), but movie stills are okay, especially when the makeup, hair and nails are to die for, like they are in these pics of Savarine.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="bond girls severine" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-124885 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/bond-girl-makeup-looks-inspired-by-the-opi-skyfall-collection-from-skyfall-severine/" height="407" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/bond-girls-severine.jpg" title="bond girls severine" width="610"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Ooh, I
          <img alt="love" class="pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/bond-girl-makeup-looks-inspired-by-the-opi-skyfall-collection-from-skyfall-severine/" height="17" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/07/summer-heart-tiny.jpg" style="margin-bottom:-3px;" title="love" width="20"/>
          that gray eye look Its kinda smokey, and I dig the cat eye shape.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="bond girls severine" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-124884 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/bond-girl-makeup-looks-inspired-by-the-opi-skyfall-collection-from-skyfall-severine/" height="275" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/bond-girls-severine-2.jpg" title="bond girls severine" width="610"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          See how the shadow slightly wings out at the ends? I feel like it elongates the eye. Oh, and peep the inner corners, and how her eyeshadow tracks her tear duct, ending in a slightly pointed tip. Makes it look even
          <em>
           more
          </em>
          feline,
          <em>
           meow!
          </em>
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/bond-girl-makeup-looks-inspired-by-the-opi-skyfall-collection-from-skyfall-severine/" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          I was really inspired today by Savarines look, so I thought Id give it a try this afternoon and wear it with OPIs dark plummy red
          <a href="http://www.opi.com/">
           Skyfall
          </a>
          nail polish.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-124887 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/bond-girl-makeup-looks-inspired-by-the-opi-skyfall-collection-from-skyfall-severine/" height="407" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/opi-skyfall.jpg" title="opi-skyfall" width="610"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-124886 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/bond-girl-makeup-looks-inspired-by-the-opi-skyfall-collection-from-skyfall-severine/" height="432" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/opi-skyfall-swatch.jpg" title="opi skyfall swatch" width="610"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          The eyes: step by step
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span id="more-124878">
          </span>
          <br/>
          <img alt="bond girls severine skyfall look eye tutorial" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-124879 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/bond-girl-makeup-looks-inspired-by-the-opi-skyfall-collection-from-skyfall-severine/" height="451" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/bond-girls-severine-skyfall-look-eye-tutorial.jpg" title="bond girls severine skyfall look eye tutorial" width="610"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Mostly, for my eyes, I used a mix of products from new MAC, NARS and Hourglass fall/holiday makeup collections   products like the MAC Extra Dimension Eye Shadows in
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/product-reviews/neither-long-nights-nor-wig-snatching-tabbies-can-stop-the-new-mac-extra-dimension-glamour-daze-eye-shadows/">
           Evening Gray and Tall, Dark &amp; Handsome
          </a>
          ; the
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/daily-beauty-cat/art-is-what-you-can-get-away-with/">
           NARS Self Portrait 2 Eyeshadow Palette from the Andy Warhol
          </a>
          collection; and the
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/product-reviews/peanut-butter-and-jelly-meet-black-liner-and-red-lipstick-another-power-couple-the-new-hourglass-icon-collection-and-film-noir-kohl-eye-pencil-in-eclipse/">
           Hourglass Film Noir Kohl Eye Pencil in Eclipse
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          But you could do something very similar to this with related colors.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           1.
          </strong>
          First things first, pat a light gray eyeshadow on your lids, slightly winging it out at the ends to create the overall shape of a cat eye.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           2.
          </strong>
          Next, layer a darker gray eyeshadow directly on top of your lighter gray, concentrating most of the color on the outer wing.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           3.
          </strong>
          Then, line your upper and lower lash and water lines with a black liner, and soften the appearance of the liner along your lash lines by smudging it. Now, with an angled brush, pat your darker gray eyeshadow directly on top of your smudged liner, and create a soft point in the inner corners of your eyes. Finish by filling in your brows and applying a few coats of mascara.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="bond girls severine skyfall look" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-124882 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/bond-girl-makeup-looks-inspired-by-the-opi-skyfall-collection-from-skyfall-severine/" height="434" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/bond-girls-severine-skyfall-look-3.jpg" title="bond girls severine skyfall look" width="610"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          Cheeks, lips and nails
         </h3>
         <p>
          <img alt="bond girls severine skyfall look" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-124883 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/bond-girl-makeup-looks-inspired-by-the-opi-skyfall-collection-from-skyfall-severine/" height="462" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/bond-girls-severine-skyfall-look-4.jpg" title="bond girls severine skyfall look" width="610"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Peachy pink cheeks and lips serve to offset the smokey factor from the eyes and balance the overall look.
         </p>
         <p>
          Here Im wearing the new
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/product-reviews/radiant-peachy-pink-cheeks-are-easy-with-the-right-blush-mac-glamour-daze-powder-blush-in-easy-manner/">
           MAC Easy Manner Blush
          </a>
          on the apples of my cheeks.
         </p>
         <p>
          On my lips, Im wearing
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/product-reviews/lighten-your-load-and-perfect-your-pout-with-too-faced-perfect-lips/">
           Too Faced Perfect Lips Liner in Perfect Pink
          </a>
          , topped with
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/product-reviews/say-high-shine-these-full-coverage-lip-creams-from-too-faced-are-la-creme-of-the-crop/">
           Too Faced La Creme Lipstick in Naughty Nude
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/mac-makeup/mac-is-making-holidays-happier-in-10-different-ways-glamour-daze-all-for-glamour-primped-out-and-guilty-passions-pics-and-swatches-for-mac-holiday-2012/">
           MAC Lipglass in Talk to Me Softly
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          And then two coats of OPI Skyfall nail polish tie everything together.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="My Bond girls Severine Skyfall look" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-124905 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/bond-girl-makeup-looks-inspired-by-the-opi-skyfall-collection-from-skyfall-severine/" height="460" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/bond-girls-severine-skyfall-look2.jpg" title="My Bond girls Severine Skyfall look" width="610"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          Umwhere can I get one of these dancing horses!?
         </h3>
         <p>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="343" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/AGLCZSwYfDo?rel=0" width="610">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          If anyone knows, HOLLA, because I want to go to there.
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/bond-girl-makeup-looks-inspired-by-the-opi-skyfall-collection-from-skyfall-severine/" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          The video is from OPIs website. Basically, a dancing horse battles one-on-one against other dancers.
         </p>
         <p>
          The horse does a great job, too. Its very compelling, LOL!
         </p>

          Okay, thats enough crazy talk for one evening
