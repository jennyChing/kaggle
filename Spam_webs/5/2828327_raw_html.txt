    <article class="article post-1368 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-beauty tag-bobbi-brown tag-cleanser tag-dry-skin tag-skincare" id="post-1368" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Article">

      <time class="post-date" datetime="March 7, 2013">

      </time>
      <h1 class="post-title">
       <a href="http://www.simplystine.com/bobbi-brown-hydrating-cleanser/" rel="bookmark" title="Bobbi Brown Hydrating Cleanser">
        Bobbi Brown Hydrating Cleanser
       </a>
      </h1>
      <p>
       By
       <a href="http://simplystine.com/About" rel="author external" title="Visit Christines website">
        Christine
       </a>
      </p>
     </header>
     <p>
      <!-- BEGIN THEIA POST SLIDER -->
     </p>
     <p>
      I love Bobbi Brown cosmetics, but I never got around to trying any of her skincare. I recently came across her Hydrating Cleanser and fell in love. This is an
      <a href="http://www.temptalia.com/2012-editors-choice-awards" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.temptalia.com/2012-editors-choice-awards', 'award winning']);">
       award winning
      </a>
      cleanser great for removing all traces of dirt and makeup from the skin, but its not too harsh on the face. This is a very creamy, thick cleanser, but for folks who prefer a foaming cleanser, this isnt for you. The trick here is that most cleaning foamers (not all) can make your skin even drier. They sometimes remove and strip essential oils from the skin that are necessary, especially when you have dry skin.
     </p>
     <div>
      <a class="colorbox" href="http://simplystine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_61231.jpg" imageanchor="1" rel="colorbox">
       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://simplystine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_6123.jpg" width="240"/>
      </a>
     </div>
     <div>
     </div>
     <p>
      I loved this product. I felt that the Avocado Butter and Grape seed Oil really moisturized my skin, but didnt leave it filling stripped and dry. It removed my makeup nicely and the scent was refreshing. This is a definite cream cleanser. Its not milky or foamy at all. I used this AM/PM and could probably use this all season, as long as my skin wasnt super oily.
     </p>
     <p>
      This cleanser retails for $28.00 and can be purchased
      <a href="http://www.bobbibrowncosmetics.com/product/2558/19431/Skincare/CleanseTone/Cleansers/Hydrating-Rich-Cream-Cleanser/New/index.tmpl" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bobbibrowncosmetics.com/product/2558/19431/Skincare/CleanseTone/Cleansers/Hydrating-Rich-Cream-Cleanser/New/index.tmpl', 'online']);">
       online
      </a>
      or at any department store that carries Bobbi Browns products. I picked mine up at Macys!
     </p>
