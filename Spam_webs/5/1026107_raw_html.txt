									Kitchen Remodel 1000								&lt;/h2&gt;
																	&lt;span class="post-author"&gt;Written by &lt;a href="http://blog.builddirect.com/author/rojon/" title="Posts by Rob Jones" rel="author"&gt;Rob Jones&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt;

							&lt;p&gt;So, let&#8217;s say you&#8217;ve decided that a kitchen remodel doesn&#8217;t have to be this immediate, overnight transformation like you see on TV. Let&#8217;s assume that you&#8217;re siding with the tortoise, and not so much with the hare.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Let&#8217;s say that you&#8217;re totally on board with the idea that kitchen remodels can take the form of a gradual transformation; a color scheme change, a decorative house plant or two, the addition of new artwork, or a new way of storing your cooking implements.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;But, maybe that was a while ago.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Since then, you&#8217;ve set aside a larger budget for your kitchen remodel. You&#8217;ve &lt;em&gt;prioritized&lt;/em&gt;. And now, you&#8217;ve got twice the amount to spend than you had &#8211; $1000.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Following a similar path that you did with your&lt;a title="Kitchen Remodel 500" href="http://blog.builddirect.com/kitchen-remodel-500/" target="_blank"&gt; $500 kitchen remodel&lt;/a&gt;, where does twice the budget get you? Well, take a look at this graphic that shows you the possibilities of a kitchen remodel for a whole grand.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/downloads/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Budget_Remodel_1000_Kitchen.jpg');"  href="http://blog.builddirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Budget_Remodel_1000_Kitchen.jpg"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-19609" title="Budget_Remodel_1000_Kitchen" src="http://blog.builddirect.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Budget_Remodel_1000_Kitchen.jpg" alt="" width="604" height="1192" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;Kitchen remodel 1000: pushing the boat out a bit further&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;When you&#8217;ve got $1000 instead of $500, it&#8217;s not time to lay out solid gold flooring and a platinum stove.Where $500 let you add some subtle additions, twice that amount on a kitchen remodel still puts you solidly in accessories and subtle texture country.But, you can expand your horizons a little bit more.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;With $1000, the additions can be a bit more obvious &#8211; a new &lt;a onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/www.builddirect.com/Glass-Tile.aspx?utm_source=BDBlog&amp;utm_medium=Social&amp;utm_campaign=BDBlog_HowTo');" title="Glass tile from BuildDirect"  href="http://www.builddirect.com/Glass-Tile.aspx?utm_source=BDBlog&amp;utm_medium=Social&amp;utm_campaign=BDBlog_HowTo" target="_blank"&gt;backsplash&lt;/a&gt;, new window coverings, a new pot rack, even a wine cooler!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;Expanding on what you created before&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Another aspect of all of this of course is a steady design evolution in your kitchen. You had the budget before to choose a tasteful color for the walls. Now, maybe you can afford to &lt;a title="The Power of the Accent Wall!" href="http://blog.builddirect.com/the-power-of-the-accent-wall/" target="_blank"&gt;create an accent wall&lt;/a&gt; to bring out the subtleties a bit more. Maybe there&#8217;s a textural aspect to that wall as well. Toward the same goal, maybe your earthenware and crockery has been replaced to be a part of that new color dynamic. And perhaps too, that brown crown molding adds more value than it would have before without those other color elements in place.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;When you&#8217;ve got $1000 to spend, thinking about expanding on what was there before when you invested $500 makes the whole thing easier. It reveals a path that maybe you were trying to define all along. Or maybe it reveals something that comes as a pleasant surprise, a new vista for the design possiblilities&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;Kitchen remodel 1000: a chance to learn about what you really want&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;When you&#8217;ve got a grand budgeted for a kitchen remodel in 2012, it&#8217;s often a cosmetic project rather than reconstructive surgery. This is not to de-value it in any way. It&#8217;s a &lt;em&gt;start&lt;/em&gt;, and a greater one than you had a while back when you could only devote $500 to it. It offers you a chance that perhaps a bigger budget might not allow you to see as clearly. This is a chance to ramp up to your dream kitchen. It&#8217;s a chance to play around with color, and get a sense of how your walls, art, countertop accessories, and other details look in various seasons, and at various times of day. It&#8217;s a chance for you to learn more about your space, and collect more ideas.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Who knows? Maybe this $1000 investment in your kitchen can actually help you to re-think some of the things you thought you wanted in a kitchen. Maybe this one-grand project can help you focus on a bigger spend, and a bigger scale of project later on. Intelligence, especially when it comes to bigger projects, is like gold. This cosmetic stage of a kitchen remodel can help you get there.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;Some questions for you&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
&lt;li&gt;What would you do with a $1000 budget in your kitchen?&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;What do you think are the advantages of a gradual kitchen remodel project?&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Would you throw caution to the wind and just invest in one big change?&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;What obvious thing did we miss in the above graphic?&lt;/li&gt;
