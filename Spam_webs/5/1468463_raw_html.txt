        <p>
         I have probably made three bundt cakes in my life, and I totally plan on rectifying that this year. I declare 2014 the YEAR OF THE BUNDT!
        </p>
        <p>
         My bundt cakes will be overly a-bundt-ant.
        </p>
        <p>
         Ha, ha, see what I did there??
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://iambaker.net/vanilla-bundt-cake-with-vanilla-frosting/flower-surprise-inside-cake-1/" rel="attachment wp-att-44242">
          <img alt="Vanilla Bundt Cake on MacKenzie-Childs Cakestand" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-44242" data-pin-description="Vanilla Bundt Cake with Vanilla Frosting ~ http://iambaker.net" height="400" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/flower-surprise-inside-cake-1.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         This is simply a vanilla bundt cake with vanilla glaze. Well, maybe there is a bit more to it than that
        </p>
        <p>
         But what I really want to talk about here is that cake stand. I am completely smitten with it! The designer is
         <a href="http://www.mackenzie-childs.com/" target="_blank">
          MacKenzie-Childs
         </a>
         , and this particular stand is called
         <a href="Vanilla%20Bundt Cake on MacKenzie-Childs Cakestand" target="_blank">
          Flower Market
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://iambaker.net/vanilla-bundt-cake-with-vanilla-frosting/mackenzie-childs-cake-stand-fb/" rel="attachment wp-att-44245">
          <img alt="MacKenzie-Childs Flower Market Cake Stand" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-44245" data-pin-description="Vanilla Bundt Cake with Vanilla Frosting ~ http://iambaker.net" height="345" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/mackenzie-childs-cake-stand-FB.jpg" width="345"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         There is something so beautiful to me about the bold geometric design paired with the delicate yet colorful beautiful flowers. This cake stand is one of my most prized! (It is available in two sizes and both are wonderful!)
        </p>
        <p>
         I really wanted to make sure that the cake could stand up to the eleganceof it; that the cake was subtle in beauty, but delicious and bold in flavor.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://iambaker.net/vanilla-bundt-cake-with-vanilla-frosting/flower-surprise-inside-cake-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-44246">
          <img alt="Vanilla Bundt on MacKenzie-Childs Cake Stand" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-44246" data-pin-description="Vanilla Bundt Cake with Vanilla Frosting ~ http://iambaker.net" height="308" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/flower-surprise-inside-cake-2.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>

           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup granulated sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, softened
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 eggs, at room temperature
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             3/4 cup milk
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 teaspoons vanilla extract
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 cups all-purpose flour
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 teaspoons baking powder
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 teaspoon salt
            </li>
            <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8">
             Vanilla Glaze
            </div>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 cups powdered (confectioners) sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
             2-4 tablespoons milk
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tsp lemon juice
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-12" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 tsp vanilla extract
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Preheat oven to 350F. Grease and flour Bundt pan.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Beat granulated sugar, butter, and eggs in stand mixer at medium speed until well blended. Change speed to low and add milk and vanilla; change back to medium speed and mix well. Add flour, baking powder and salt; beat about 1 minute or until smooth.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Pour into prepared pan.* Bake 30 minutes or until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean. Gently loosen cake from pan with knife and turn out onto wire rack; cool completely.
            </li>
            <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3">
             Vanilla Glaze
            </div>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Put powder sugar, 2 tablespoons milk, lemon juice, and vanilla extract in medium bowl and mix until fully incorporated.  If glaze is not runny enough, add more milk.  You will want glaze to be thick enough to hold it's "drip" on the cake, but runny enough to pour.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             *If making surprise-inside cake see additional directions in blog post.
            </li>
           </ol>
