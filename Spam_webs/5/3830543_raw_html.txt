        <p>
         Before they first met, Juan Camilo spotted Ana from across the room and knew instantly that they were meant to be. They became friends, and weeks later, when he told her how he felt, she was resistant; having just ended a long-term relationship, she didn't feel ready for anything more.
         <em>
          "Just let yourself go with the flow..."
         </em>
         , he said, and she gave it a small shot. That night spent chatting, laughing and getting to know each other was all it took for Ana to believe.
        </p>

         He left my apartment and my heart started beating as if it would come out from my chest and in that moment, I understood why I should give this relationship a chance... Ever since we have spent six amazing years of love, dreams, commitment, joy, friendship, complicity, trust and harmony together.
