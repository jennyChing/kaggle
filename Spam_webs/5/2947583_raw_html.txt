        <h2 class="headline">
         Lamb Of God Vocalist Randy Blythe Reportedly Arrested For Manslaughter In Prague
        </h2>

        <div id="mainarticlephoto">
         <img alt="Photo of Lamb of God" class="photo" src="/images/bandphotos/Lamb_of_God_photo.jpg" title="Photo of Lamb of God"/>
         <p>
          Band Photo: Lamb of God
          <span>
           <a href="/help/faq/index.cfm?#faq-oldbandphoto" title="Old/wrong photo?">

        <p>
         As reported by MetalSucks.net, citing an article on Novinky.cz, Lamb Of God frontman Randy Blythe was arrested on charges of manslaughter stemming from a "tragic incident" in Prague at a Lamb of God performance on May 24, 2010, which led to a death.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.novinky.cz/krimi/271872-zpevaka-americke-metalove-skupiny-v-praze-zatkli-a-obvinili-ze-zabiti.html">
          The article
         </a>
         states that Lamb Of God performance scheduled tonight at Club Cafe in Prague was cancelled by the band's management, which identified no specific reason to local promoting agency Obscure.
        </p>
        <p>
         The band was scheduled to perform with All Shall Perish and Skeletonwitch. Stay tuned for more information on this breaking story.
        </p>
