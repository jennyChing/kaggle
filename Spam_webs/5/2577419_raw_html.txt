           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://tothemotherhood.com/2014/05/the-rules-of-being-a-kid/&amp;media=http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Daugher-in-Kid-Trax-VW-Beetle-Convertible-12-Volt-Battery-Powered-Ride-On.jpg&amp;description=The Rules of Being a Kid  #PopsicleRules')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         As I miss and reminisce the halcyon days of my youth, I can now live vicariously live through my children. They remind me how fleeting life is and to enjoy every single moment of it, however monumental or mundane it may be. There are days when they keep me on my toes, but they keep me young and happy, as there is hardly a day without joy and laughter. Of course, there may be something that they do that either be frustrating or confusing; I never forget that I was once a child, too. Now that I am a mother with two children of my own, I understand what my mom means when she says that when theyre young, theyll tug on your apron strings and when theyre older, theyll tug on your heartstrings. How true that statement is! Therefore, I cherish their limited years of childhood, as I know that someday theyll be on their own, venturing adulthood. And, Ill miss those days that they were mine.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/My-Son-at-the-Park.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest pibfi_float_center">
           <img alt="My Son at the Park" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19056" height="450" src="http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/My-Son-at-the-Park.jpg" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://tothemotherhood.com/2014/05/the-rules-of-being-a-kid/&amp;media=http://tothemotherhood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/My-Son-at-the-Park.jpg&amp;description=The Rules of Being a Kid  #PopsicleRules')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <br/>
         <h1>
          The Popsicle Rules of Being a Kid
         </h1>
         <p>
         </p>
        </center>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
          Although its the most commonly used adage; be true to yourself, while accepting others for who they are.
         <p>
           Even though it can be fun staying up at times, try to go to bed at a reasonable time as much as possible. Once youre an adult, youll cherish and value every moment of sleep, whereas late nights will become a rarity or obsolete altogether.
         </p>
         <p>
           Popularity is overrated. Take the time and reach out to the underdog, because they need a friend as the next person.
         </p>
         <p>
           Keeping that in mind, remember that youre no better than the next person. We are all created equal,
          <em>
           yet
          </em>
          there are some people who think otherwise, and try to inflate their value.
         </p>
         <p>
           Video games cant take the place of the sheer beauty of being outdoors. Skip a stone. Use chalk to create a masterpiece on the sidewalks or drive. Fly a kite. Play Frisbee. Go treasure hunting with your metal detector. Enjoy nature, because it is irreplaceable.
         </p>
         <p>
           Dont idolize celebrities, because theyre people, too, and theyre prone to make mistakes. In fact, dont idolize anyone, for that matter! Everyone makes mistakes, thus theyre bound to disappoint.
         </p>
         <p>
           Dont wish to be older, because youll someday miss your childhood.
         </p>
         <p>
           Theres nothing wrong with taking pride in your appearance, but enjoy being a child. Dont try to grow up too fast!
          <br/>
         </p>
