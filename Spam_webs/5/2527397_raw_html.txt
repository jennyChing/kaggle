               PURE ADRENALINE

             <h3 style="text-align: center;">
              <span style="color: #ff00ff;">
               FEELING WILD &amp; ADVENTUROUS? BUNGEE JUMP LIKE JAMES BOND IN SWITZERLAND
              </span>
             </h3>
             <p>
              <span id="more-5694">
              </span>
             </p>
             <h3 style="text-align: left;">
              Live like 007for a day and bungee jump off Switzerlands infamous Verzasca Dam, just like 007 did in the opening sequence of the 1995 James Bond movie, GoldenEye.

             <h3 style="text-align: center;">
              THE HEIGHT OF INSANITY
             </h3>
             <span class="su-dropcap su-dropcap-style-flat" style="font-size:2em">
              O
             </span>
             ne of the highest stationary bungee jumps in the world and undoubtedly the most famous, Verzasca, or Contra Dam as its also called, lures flocks of adrenaline junkies and adventure seekers who drive the winding roads of the southern alps to this scenic alpine setting to experience the 007 thrill. Or prove their insanity, depending on your definition of wild.
             <br/>
             <a href="http://www.backtobuckley.com/?attachment_id=6060" rel="attachment wp-att-6060">
              <img alt="Bungee jump like james bond in switzerland" class="aligncenter wp-image-6060" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.backtobuckley.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Bungee-jump-like-james-bond-in-switzerland-5.jpg?resize=700%2C208"/>
             </a>
             <p style="text-align: left;">
              Why the heck not? This 220m drop towards the rocky river bed below comes complete with a 7.5 second free-fall in extremely close proximity to what is, despite its Hollywood fame and Golden Eye glory, essentially just a bloody mammoth rock-solid cement wall.This one is serious bucket list material If your must-do-before-I-die list doesnt have height restrictions or a phobia of your face being spontaneously rearranged.
             </p>
             <blockquote>
              Step up to the edge with your toes just over the lip and look at the top of the cliffs straight ahead. Try not to look down
             </blockquote>
             <!-- blockquote (end) -->
             Dont look down?! Just the type of sterling advice youd expect from someone who didnt wake up this morning with the intention of throwing themselves headfirst over the edge of an epic precipice, tied to a rubber slim line that despite the promises of highly skilled safety instructors, still looked suspiciously like my dogs leash. Excellent.

             <h3 style="padding-left: 30px; text-align: right;">
              <blockquote>
               If James Bond did it, you can too!
              </blockquote>
              <!-- blockquote (end) -->
             </h3>
             <p style="text-align: left;">
              Really? Is that the pep talk? Right. One minute then, looks like I forgot to grab my invincibility smash-proof face mask from the glove box of the Aston Martin I drove here.
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: left;">
              Can you tell As grand an idea as it was, the second I saw the behemoth water-blocker that is the Verzasca Dam, my desire to wannabe-anything that involved having the balls to fly off that thing disappeared I didnt actually jump, Im not the first soul to opt out either. But as I watched those who did manage to soar, or in some cases simply squat and plop off the platform, I was in awe.
             </p>
