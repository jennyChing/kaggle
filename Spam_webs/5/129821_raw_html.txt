      Gre

     Bana gre da
     Yalnzdr
     Uykusuzumdur
     Daa gre ben

     Ben
     Daa
     Gre
     Deliyim

     Da
     Bana
     Gre
     A

     Daa gre ben
     Uzanamam
     Ulaamaz
     Da bana gre
     </pre>

     <pre>
      Relatively

     To me the mountain
     Is lonesome.
     I am sleepless
     According to the mountain.

     To
     The mountain
     I am
     Insane.

     To
     Me
     The mountain
     Is hungry.

     According to the mountain
     I cannot reach out.
     The mountain cannot arrive
     According to me.
     </pre>
