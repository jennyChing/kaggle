          <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
           Tea Light Melted Snowman
          </h1>
          <p class="entry-meta">
           <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-12-10T07:00:57+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
            December 10, 2013
           </time>
           by
           <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
            <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.madetobeamomma.com/author/madetobeamomma" itemprop="url" rel="author">
             <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
              Katie
             </span>
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="entry-comments-link">
            <a href="http://www.madetobeamomma.com/2013/12/tea-light-melted-snowman.html#comments">
             4 Comments
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
         </header>
         <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
          <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
          <p>
           <img alt="Tea Light Melted Snowman Made to be a Momma" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2396" height="872" src="http://www.madetobeamomma.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Tea-Light-Melted-Snowman-Made-to-be-a-Momma.jpg" width="700"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           I LOVE Christmas time! I love all the decorations that it brings; however, after a month of the Christmas tree being up its about time I need to take it down. Having little ones that are constantly pulling at the ornaments probably attribute a lot to that feeling. I love having little decorations that can stay up ALL winter long though. We all know that winter lasts months past Christmas day and the first of January is a little too early to bring out the pink hearts!
           <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.madetobeamomma.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
           This little snowman tea light is the perfect little decoration that you can keep out all winter long! Turn on his light and his nose looks just like a carrot!
           <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.madetobeamomma.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
           <em>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <em>
            What kind of decorations do you have that can stay up all winter long??
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Tea light snowma collage Made to be a Momma" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2397" height="880" src="http://www.madetobeamomma.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Tea-light-snowma-collage-Made-to-be-a-Momma.jpg" width="700"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           1. Gather your supplies. You will need: White paper, black paper, red ribbon, a tea light, permanent marker.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           2. Draw a snowman face on your tealight
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           3. Cut out two melted snow pieces of white paper. Cut out a black hat and attach to your tea light.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           4. Tie your ribbon around your tie light. Use a small amount of glue to hold in place.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           5. Cut a small hole on the back of melted snow so you can easily turn your tea light on and off.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           6. Using either hot glue or tape, secure your tea light to your melted snow attach a magnet to the back and put it on your fridge!
           <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.madetobeamomma.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           ENJOY!
          </p>
