           <span class="first-paragraph">
            <span class="paragraph-0">
             <p class="p1">
              Football is months away, but Game of Thrones is off to a bloody start. Fill the void in your heart from the absence of fantasy football and basketball with Fantasy Game of Thrones.
             </p>
            </span>
           </span>
           <span class="paragraph-1">
            <p class="p1">
             <strong>
              INSTRUCTIONS
             </strong>
            </p>
           </span>
           <ol>
            <li class="p3">
             Get your Game of Thrones viewing party together and split into teams.
            </li>
            <li class="p3">
             Each person picks one character (or two if you have a small party) from the series.
            </li>
            <li class="p3">
             Each time one of the below things happens to a character, either add or subtract points from the team.
            </li>
            <li class="p3">
             The team with the most points at the end of the season (or episode for a shortened version) wins the Fantasy Iron Throne.
             <hr/>
            </li>
           </ol>
           <p class="p3">
            <strong>
             POINT BREAKDOWN:
            </strong>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li class="p1">
             <span class="s1">
              <strong>
               Your character mentions dragons +1 *
              </strong>
             </span>
            </li>
            <li class="p1">
             <span class="s1">
              <strong>
               Your character draws their sword +1
              </strong>
             </span>
            </li>
            <li class="p1">
             <span class="s1">
              <strong>
               Your character says their house words +3
              </strong>
             </span>
            </li>
            <li class="p1">
             <span class="s1">
              <strong>
               Your character orders someone to be killed +3
              </strong>
             </span>
            </li>
            <li class="p1">
             <span class="s1">
              <strong>
               Your character kills someone +5**
              </strong>
             </span>
            </li>
            <li class="p1">
             <span class="s1">
              <strong>
               Your character has sex +5
              </strong>
             </span>
            </li>
           </ul>
