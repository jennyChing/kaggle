        <h1 class="postitle">
         Repurposed Dynamite Stick Patriotic Party Favors
        </h1>
        <div class="imagess_box">
        </div>
        <div class="single_metainfo">
         by Dinah Wulf
        </div>
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="15198337" data-link="http://www.diyinspired.com/repurposed-dynamite-stick-patriotic-party-favors/" data-summary="" data-title="Repurposed Dynamite Stick Patriotic Party Favors">
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Repurposed-Dynamite-Stick-Patriotic-Party-Favors-5.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Repurposed Dynamite Stick Patriotic Party Favors (5)" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16040" height="424" src="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Repurposed-Dynamite-Stick-Patriotic-Party-Favors-5.jpg" width="650"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.diyinspired.com/repurposed-dynamite-stick-patriotic-party-favors/&amp;media=http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Repurposed-Dynamite-Stick-Patriotic-Party-Favors-5.jpg&amp;description=Repurposed Dynamite Stick Patriotic Party Favors')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
          Thank you to
          <a href="http://sweetworks.net/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           SweetWorks, Inc.
          </a>
          , for sponsoring the candy for this post,
          <em>
           Repurposed Dynamite Stick Patriotic Party Favors.
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
          If you know me, you know that I work out often. When I do, I bring bottled water with a vitamin B12 energy powder mixed in. The powder comes in these cute little cases that I have been saving because Im a Craft Hoarder (thats rightI admit it!). They come in several colors and I have a ton of them because I buy them at Costco! Arent they cool?
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Repurposed-Dynamite-Stick-Patriotic-Party-Favors-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Repurposed Dynamite Stick Patriotic Party Favors (2)" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16037" height="444" src="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Repurposed-Dynamite-Stick-Patriotic-Party-Favors-2.jpg" width="650"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.diyinspired.com/repurposed-dynamite-stick-patriotic-party-favors/&amp;media=http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Repurposed-Dynamite-Stick-Patriotic-Party-Favors-2.jpg&amp;description=Repurposed Dynamite Stick Patriotic Party Favors')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
          When I got these cute little red, white, and blue Sweet Shapes stars from SweetWorks, Inc., I thought Id make some candy filled patriotic party favors that look like little dynamite sticks with my containers. If you dont have these containers, you can make larger favors with toilet paper rolls.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Repurposed-Dynamite-Stick-Patriotic-Party-Favors-1.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Repurposed Dynamite Stick Patriotic Party Favors (1)" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16036" height="432" src="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Repurposed-Dynamite-Stick-Patriotic-Party-Favors-1.jpg" width="650"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.diyinspired.com/repurposed-dynamite-stick-patriotic-party-favors/&amp;media=http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Repurposed-Dynamite-Stick-Patriotic-Party-Favors-1.jpg&amp;description=Repurposed Dynamite Stick Patriotic Party Favors')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
          <strong>
           Materials:
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
           Plastic Energy Powder Containers (or something similar)
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
           SweetWorks Sweet Shapes Candies
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Have-a-Blast-Printable.pdf" target="_blank">
           <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
            FREE Have a Blast Printable
           </span>
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
           Decorative Paper
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
           Black Cardstock
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
           Scissors
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
           Double-Sided Tape
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
           Tape
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
          I made sure each container was clean and dry and filled them with the red, white, and blue, star pressed candies.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.diyinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Repurposed-Dynamite-Stick-Patriotic-Party-Favors-3.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
