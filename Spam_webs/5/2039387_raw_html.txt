         15,000 Delta Medallion Qualifying Miles (MQMs) added to my SkyMiles account today  Thank you AMEX! How to claim them.  Rookie Wednesday post.
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <a class="entry-date" href="http://deltapoints.boardingarea.com/2015/03/04/15000-delta-medallion-qualifying-miles-mqms-added-to-my-skymiles-account-today-thank-you-amex-how-to-claim-them-rookie-wednesday-post/" rel="bookmark" title="10:30">

        </div>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-11383" height="100" src="http://boardingarea.com/deltapoints/files/2012/10/dl-points-rookie-300x100.jpg" width="300"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Welcome to a weekly feature on the Delta Points blog. Each week this series covers in a rookie way either a Delta or travel related theme and attempts to break down to a basic level each topic. You can read up on all the previous posts
          <span style="color: #800000;">
           <a href="http://boardingarea.com/deltapoints/2013/05/29/rookie-wednesday-a-look-back-on-what-we-have-covered-so-far-and-beyond/" target="_blank">
            <span style="color: #800000;">
             <strong>
              HERE
             </strong>
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
          . Now on to this weeks feature.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://deltapoints.boardingarea.com/2015/03/04/15000-delta-medallion-qualifying-miles-mqms-added-to-my-skymiles-account-today-thank-you-amex-how-to-claim-them-rookie-wednesday-post/my-numbers-b4-amex/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="my numbers b4 amex" class="aligncenter wp-image-25140 size-medium" height="158" src="http://deltapoints.boardingarea.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/my-numbers-b4-amex-300x158.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the absolute best features of the
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.deltapoints.com/dlgold" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.deltapoints.com/dlgold', 'Delta AMEX cards');" target="_blank">
           Delta AMEX cards
          </a>
          <span style="color: #800000;">
           &lt;-LINK
          </span>
         </strong>
         especially in this case my Delta Reserve card, is being able to earn 15,000 MQMs by spending $30,000 on the card in a calendar year and again once I hit $60,000.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://deltapoints.boardingarea.com/2015/03/04/15000-delta-medallion-qualifying-miles-mqms-added-to-my-skymiles-account-today-thank-you-amex-how-to-claim-them-rookie-wednesday-post/bonus-skymiles-from-30000-spend-on-delta-reserve-card-delta-points/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="bonus skymiles from 30000 spend on delta reserve card delta points" class="aligncenter wp-image-25141 size-medium" height="51" src="http://deltapoints.boardingarea.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bonus-skymiles-from-30000-spend-on-delta-reserve-card-delta-points-300x51.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I often get the question, just when can I get these MQMs? Is it when I meet the spend or when the statement closes or just when? The simple answer is what you see above. You ALSO get 15,000 bonus SkyMiles for meeting these spend goals. A few days after your statement closes you will see at Delta.com the bonus SkyMiles post to you account. THAT is the key event to look for to know you are ready to redeem or gift the bonus MQMs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://deltapoints.boardingarea.com/2015/03/04/15000-delta-medallion-qualifying-miles-mqms-added-to-my-skymiles-account-today-thank-you-amex-how-to-claim-them-rookie-wednesday-post/step-one-get-mqms/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="step one get mqms" class="aligncenter wp-image-25142 size-medium" height="233" src="http://deltapoints.boardingarea.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/step-one-get-mqms-300x233.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Take a look at the screen shot of my current points for this year including my million miler status. Now it is time to use
         <a href="https://www.delta.com/deltareservemqm/mbrLanding.action" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.delta.com/deltareservemqm/mbrLanding.action', 'THIS');" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           THIS
          </strong>
         </a>
         link, after I am logged in to Delta.com, and go to choose what to do with my MQMs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://deltapoints.boardingarea.com/2015/03/04/15000-delta-medallion-qualifying-miles-mqms-added-to-my-skymiles-account-today-thank-you-amex-how-to-claim-them-rookie-wednesday-post/mqms-added-to-my-skymiles-account/" target="_blank">

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Then I simply pick if I am redeeming the MQMs from either a personal or business
         <a href="http://www.deltapoints.com/bpc" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.deltapoints.com/bpc', 'Delta AMEX Reserve card');" target="_blank">
          Delta AMEX Reserve card
         </a>
         (in my case it is a business card  you can hold BOTH cards). I also choose if I want to keep the MQMs for myself or gift them to anyone else with a SkyMiles account.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://deltapoints.boardingarea.com/2015/03/04/15000-delta-medallion-qualifying-miles-mqms-added-to-my-skymiles-account-today-thank-you-amex-how-to-claim-them-rookie-wednesday-post/mqms-added-to-my-skymiles-account-instantly-updated-on-delta-com/" target="_blank">

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         And that is all there is to it. It is just that simple and Delta tells me I have successfully redeemed the points.         </p>
        <p>
         It really is SO
         <a href="http://deltapoints.boardingarea.com/2015/01/05/2015-earn-delta-mqd-exempt-waver-avoid-mqd-spend-requirement-medallion-status/" target="_blank">
          simple to meet the spend
         </a>
         , not just to be MQD exempt from Delta each year, but also as a way to offset the $450 fee for the card. Since you can spend the SkyMiles bonus points at 1 cent each with the PWM option, you are earning after spending $60,000 in a calendar year 30,000 SkyMiles on top of the MQMs worth at least $300 making the annual fee much more reasonable. I also greatly value my MQMs for status and this card is a great way to earn a bunch without even stepping on a Delta jet!  Ren
