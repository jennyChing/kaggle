       <p>
        Ever why you cant watch the BBC in America or American television networks in another country?
       </p>
       <p>
        Many times you never even get the chance to see the content that is out there. Sometimes it simply just doesnt work. Most people think it is just a connection problem. Far from it the BBC has plenty of bandwidth.
       </p>
       <p>
        When you browse the internet your location servers can find out where you are located because you need to have anIP address. for those of you that dont know an IP Address is just like a phone number and you can use that information to lookup where you are located.
       </p>
       <p>
        Do to content restrictions most Televisions Networks are restricted to showing shows only in their own countries.
       </p>
       <p>
        This is what happens when I click on the BBC live news feed.
