           Cocktails and Mini Herb Crystals &amp; Mini Flower Crystals  A Perfect Pair
          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="post-details">
          Monday, September 08, 2014
         </div>
         <div class="post-body">
          <p itemprop="description">
           Creating a cocktail is just as much about pleasing the eyes as it is pleasing the pallet. Enter our
           <a href="/mixology.htm">
            Mini Herb Crystals &amp; Mini Flower Crystals
           </a>
           , the vibrant colors draw attention and the crystals fresh flavors are captivating to the taste buds. The uses are varied and can be incorporated into something as subtle as being a different substitute for plain white sugar, adding a unique sweetness to any drink, or as an eye-catching rim on a cocktail.
          </p>
          <p>
           The
           <a href="/mixology.htm">
            Mini Crystals
           </a>
           are a smaller, more granular than the regular Crystals, making them more adhesive and ideal for rimming cocktail glasses. Check out
           <a href="/mixology.htm">
            Mixologists
           </a>
           like Junior Merino with his The Big Apple cocktail. Rimming his cocktail with Herb Crystals Fennel, and garnishing with our Apple Mint Tops. Check out his recipe:
          </p>
          <img alt="" src="/images/cocktail-with-mint-herb-crystals.jpg" style="border-width: 1px; border-style: solid; float: left; margin-right: 10px; margin-top: 5px;"/>
          <p>
           2 oz Hudson Whiskey
           <br/>
           1/2 oz grilled apple juice
           <br/>
           1/2 oz cinnamon syrup
           <br/>
           3/4 oz fresh lime juice
           <br/>
           1/4 oz Xtabentun Mayan Liqueur
           <br/>
           1 dash of Junior Merino Roasted Apple &amp; Toasted Spices Bitters
          </p>
          <p>
           Pour all ingredients into a mixing glass. Add ice and strain into a rocks glass rimmed with
           <a href="/mixology.htm">
            Flower Crystals
           </a>
           Fennel and garnish with an Apple Mint top.
          </p>
          <p>
