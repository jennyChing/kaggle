             Japan was the victim of the Tohoku earthquake in 2011  an earthquake that caused a tsunami which crashed into the Fukushima power plant. This resulted in shutting down of three nuclear reactors. A total of 300,000 people had to evacuate the towns in the vicinity. Although no lives were lost to this incident, the earthquake and tsunami claimed 1,600 lives.
