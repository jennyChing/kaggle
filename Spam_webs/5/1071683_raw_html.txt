         <p>
          Little League champion Mone Davis is having a Disney movie made about her! That makes sense: Davis
          <a href="http://gokicker.com/2014/08/18/shutout/" target="_blank">
           is the first girl
          </a>

         <p>

           Joey Casselberry, you're a certified douchebag for this and you deserved to be kicked off the team.

            pic.twitter.com/Y97hkaVCSY
           </a>

            codymullis (@cmull43)
           <a href="https://twitter.com/cmull43/status/579733771055812609">
            March 22, 2015
           </a>
          </p>
         </blockquote>
         <p>
          <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
          </script>

         </p>

           Bloomsburg Univ is deeply saddened by what was written about
           <a href="https://twitter.com/hashtag/MoneDavis?src=hash">
            #MoneDavis
           </a>
           by one of our student-athletes. His words do not represent us.
          </p>
          <p>
            BU Huskies (@GoBUHuskies)
           <a href="https://twitter.com/GoBUHuskies/status/579424305001455616">
            March 21, 2015
           </a>

          <p>
            BU Huskies (@GoBUHuskies)

            March 21, 2015
           </a>

         </blockquote>
