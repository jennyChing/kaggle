       <p>
        A United States Postal Service (USPS) worker tossed mail into a dumpster in Bond Hill, Ohio Wednesday, rather than delivering the letters, paychecks and notices to their rightful owners.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        The female worker was caught on tape disposing of the mail, and she could soon face federal charges. She drops the first load at 00:15 and the second load at 1:20.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        This is clearly unacceptable behavior that does not reflect the efforts of the thousands of professional, dedicated carriers in our workforce," USPS spokesman David Van Allen said.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        "The Postal Services OIG (Office of Inspector General) is currently investigating this incident.
