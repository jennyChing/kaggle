     <h1>
      Revolver shells upcycled into cufflinks!
     </h1>
     <p>
      Cartridges (the metal casing that houses the bullet, gunpowder, and primer) are not designed for reuse. The "hot brass" that is ejected from a pistol after firing is typically thrown away.
     </p>
     <p>
      As it turns out, that doesn't have to be the case. Colt .45 Cufflinks are made from these discarded shells.
     </p>
     <p class="text-align-center">
