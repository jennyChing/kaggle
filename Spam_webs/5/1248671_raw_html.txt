         <p>
          Now heres something you dont see everyday. Have you ever wanted to use your PS4 controller on your Wii or Wii U? Now you can, thanks to an adapter manufactured by Cyber Gadget. The adapter allows you to use a PS4, PS3, or USB PC controller on your Wii and Wii U. Currently this item is only available in Japan for the asking price of 2894. The adapter will be available on November 30th in Japan.
