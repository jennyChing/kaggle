                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                     <a href="http://www.easypeasyorganic.com/2010/12/basket-of-googies.html">
                      A Basket of 'Googies'
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <p>
                     Little Red Riding Hood lives at my house. No matter where you are - she'll find you, with her basket of 'googies' in tow. An imaginary wolf hides behind the couch, or under the dining table - always ready to leap out and frighten our little heroine away. But she persists. She's very stubborn, our Little Red.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                    </p>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_NLUJGAFQKAg/TPG2cqeOBDI/AAAAAAAABPQ/2R6r4f3RlbY/s1600/_DSC9522.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_NLUJGAFQKAg/TPG2cqeOBDI/AAAAAAAABPQ/2R6r4f3RlbY/s640/_DSC9522.jpg" width="424"/>
                     </a>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     And if I really
                     <i>
                      was
                     </i>
                     Granny, and I really
                     <i>
                      was
                     </i>
                     sick in bed, I think these googies (er - cookies) might just do the trick.A touch of lime ... and cumin ... it sounds like a strange combination, but it really works! A little tangy, a little sweet, a little savoury, and oh so buttery. What's not to love about these cookies?
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_NLUJGAFQKAg/TPG2vWPHWgI/AAAAAAAABPc/lxS09ChSYp8/s1600/_DSC9519.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_NLUJGAFQKAg/TPG2vWPHWgI/AAAAAAAABPc/lxS09ChSYp8/s640/_DSC9519.jpg" width="424"/>
                     </a>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: x-large;">
                      <b>
                       ***********
                      </b>
                     </span>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: x-large;">
                      <b>
                       Cumin and Lime Cookies
                      </b>
                     </span>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <i>
                      makes ~ 20
                     </i>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <i>
                      adapted from
                     </i>
                     Marie Claire
                     <i>
                      <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Zest-Michele-Cranston/dp/1558687734?ie=UTF8&amp;tag=having-20&amp;link_code=btl&amp;camp=213689&amp;creative=392969" target="_blank">
                       Zest
                      </a>
                      <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=having-20&amp;l=btl&amp;camp=213689&amp;creative=392969&amp;o=1&amp;a=1558687734" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important; padding: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
                     </i>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <b>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="color: #990000;">
                       The time
                      </span>
                     </b>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     10 min prep + 12 min baking
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <b>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="color: #990000;">
                       The ingredients
                      </span>
                     </b>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     1/2 c softened butter
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     3/4 c raw sugar
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     1 organic egg
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     3 Tbs lime juice
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     1 tsp vanilla
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     1 tsp cumin powder
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     1 1/2 c + 1 Tbs wholemeal spelt flour*
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     1 tsp baking powder
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     4 dried figs, chopped finely (optional)
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     + yogurt and honey (optional)
                     <i>
                      to serve
                     </i>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <i>
                      <br/>
                     </i>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <i>
                      *
                     </i>
                     You can substitute regular wholemeal flour here, but I do recommend trying spelt. It's lovely and soft and has a slightly different nutritional profile from regular wheat. And we like variety!
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     **Try to use organic ingredients when you can, particularly for the eggs.
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <span class="Apple-style-span" style="color: #990000;">
                      <b>
                       The process
                      </b>
                     </span>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     1. Preheat the oven to 180C or 350F (160C fan-forced). Line a large cookie sheet or two with baking paper.
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     2. Cream together the butter and sugar, then add the egg, lime, vanilla, and cumin. Mix well.
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     3. Add the flour and baking powder to the wet ingredients and mix well. Transfer tablespoon-sized portions of dough onto the cookie sheets, leaving a 5cm (2") between them.
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     4. Bake for about 12 min, until golden. Remove from the oven, cool on the sheet for about 5 min and then transfer the cookies from the sheet to a wire rack for the remainder of the cooling process.
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <b>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span" style="color: #990000;">
                       These cookies came out to be $0.20 each, or $4 for the whole batch.
                      </span>
                     </b>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_NLUJGAFQKAg/TPG3FHpEd3I/AAAAAAAABPs/3oOvef15Jzg/s1600/_DSC9552.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img border="0" height="640" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_NLUJGAFQKAg/TPG3FHpEd3I/AAAAAAAABPs/3oOvef15Jzg/s640/_DSC9552.jpg" width="424"/>
                     </a>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     And by the way, that wolf wasn't really all that bad. I mean, I'm going to give him the benefit of the doubt. I'm sure he was just after that basket of 'googies', right?
                    </div>
                    <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     <br/>
