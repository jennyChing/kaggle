       <span itemprop="description">
        <p>
         Freddie Prinze Jr. has undergone surgery for some "disc issues" but is recovering well. The 'Scooby Doo' actor has been keeping fans up to date on his progress via Twitter.
        </p>
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="EchoTopic">
       <span id="intelliTxt">
        <span class="articleBody" itemprop="articleBody">
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/freddie-prinze-jr">
           Freddie Prinze Jr
          </a>
          . is recovering well after undergoing spinal surgery.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Freddie Prinze Jr." height="750" src="http://www.contactmusic.com/pics/ld/24_premiere_2_140110/freddie_prinze_jr_5415497.jpg" style="vertical-align: top; border: 1px solid black;" title="Freddie Prinze Jr." width="500"/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: x-small;">
           Freddie Prinze Jr. has undergone spinal surgery but is recovering well.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Read More:
           <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/news/she-s-had-a-boy-sarah-michelle-gellar-gives-birth-to-first-son-with-freddie-prinze-jr_3300662">
            It's A Boy! Sarah Michelle Gellar Gives Birth To First Son With Freddie Prinze Jr.
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The 38-year-old actor has undergone surgery after encountering some "disc issues". Prinze Jr. has kept his fans up to date with his progress via
          <a href="https://twitter.com/realfpjr" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Twitter
          </a>
          since undergoing surgery on 20th November.
         </p>
         <p>
          Prinze first alerted his fans to the news on 20th November when he uploaded pictures on Twitter before and after his surgery. The first "before" photo showed Prinze on the operating table but still smiling, the "after" picture was taken in a car and showed the actor wearing a neck brace.
         </p>
         <p>
          Prinze, although wearing a neck brace initially, also shared photographs of his operation scar. The scar appears to be a couple of inches across his neck and covered by transparent gauze. The
          <em>
           Delgo
          </em>
          actor has been optimistic about his operation and recovering, even nicknaming himself "Robo-bro", "Brobot" and "the Iron Android".
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <center>
          <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en-gb">
           <p>

            Freddie Prinze Jr (@RealFPJr)

           </a>
          </blockquote>
          <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
          </script>
         </center>
         <p>
          Fortunately, only a few days after undergoing surgery, Prinze was well enough to start exercising. Four days after Prinze's operation, on Monday (24th November), he tweeted a photograph of himself with a neck brace and added the caption "Already getting my miles in". Later the same week, Prinze Jr. tweeted that he was making further progress, writing: "#KeepItPlayful Learnin [sic] to walk again is like learning jiu-jitsu - 1 technique at a time."
         </p>
         <p>
          Media sources have seemingly been widely speculating about Prinze's progress since his operation. In response, Prinze confirmed on Saturday (29th November) that he had simply had "some disc issues that have been resolved."
         </p>
         <p>
          Get well soon Freddie Prinze Jr.!
