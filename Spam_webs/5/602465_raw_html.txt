       <div class="post-content">
        <p dir="ltr">
         Summer scarves? I know. Its only March, and some parts of the country are still blanketed in snow.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         But here in Portland, its sunny and 61 degrees! It feels like the whole city is smiling. Here in rainy Portland, basically anything above 60 degrees is fair game for breaking out the shorts and Birkenstocks (with socks, obviously).
        </p>
        <p>
         Im joking, but spring and summer fashion is on our minds. In that spirit, Ive put together a little guide with 5 summer scarves to help you pick the perfect one.
        </p>
        <h2 dir="ltr" id="docs-internal-guid-5a0698c6-04e0-48a8-f4aa-0b90634060f3">
         If You Need a Beach Cover-Up: Endless Summer Scarf
        </h2>
        <p>
         Im in love with our
         <a href="http://www.yoursummerskin.com/accessories/sun-protective-scarves/sun-protective-scarf-black-mariner-stripe.html">
          Endless Summer Scarf
         </a>
         ! It comes in11 colors, and the fabric is the softest bamboo/cotton blend. You can wear it endless different ways (as a scarf, skirt, halter top, shoulder wrap, etc). The ultraviolet protection factor (UPF) is the icing on the cake  it blocks 98% of UV rays, keeping your skin safe and youthful.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Endless Scarf" class="size-full wp-image-3043 aligncenter" height="338" src="http://yoursummerskin.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Endless-Scarf.jpg" width="505"/>
         <br/>
         If youre a solid-color type of gal, mix things up with the
         <a href="http://www.yoursummerskin.com/accessories/sun-protective-scarves/sun-protective-scarf-black-mariner-stripe.html">
          black-and-white striped
         </a>
         one. If your tops are usually splashed with colorful prints, try solid
         <a href="http://www.yoursummerskin.com/accessories/sun-protective-scarves/sun-protective-scarf-hot-pink-os.html">
          pink
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.yoursummerskin.com/accessories/sun-protective-scarves/sun-protective-scarf-kiwi-os.html">
          kiwi
         </a>
         , or
         <a href="http://www.yoursummerskin.com/accessories/sun-protective-scarves/sun-protective-scarf-orchid.html">
          orchid
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <h2 dir="ltr" id="docs-internal-guid-709d65e9-04e1-2541-c195-98bba1ab258f">
         If Your Closet Needs a Pick-Me-Up: Animal Print
        </h2>
        <p>
         Feel like the winter blahs have taken over your wardrobe? This Kate Spade
         <a href="http://shop.nordstrom.com/s/kate-spade-new-york-white-tiger-infinity-scarf/3999513">
          printed viscose scarf
         </a>
         ($98) is a jolt of caffeine! The simple color scheme makes it versatile, whether youre wearing a
         <a href="http://www.yoursummerskin.com/sun-protective-tops/dolmans/dolman-top-black.html">
          comfy black dolman top
         </a>
         or bright, summery blouse. And the jungle print adds a subtle edge without going full-on rock n roll.
        </p>
        <h2 dir="ltr" id="docs-internal-guid-c408df11-04e1-a7c9-a36b-dcca39d64ae0">
         If Youre Feeling a Little Bohemian: Pastel Pom-Poms
        </h2>
        <p>
         The
         <a href="http://www.canvashomestore.com/collections/scarves/products/bauble-scarf-seagreen">
          bauble scarf
         </a>
         from Canvas Home ($98) is a silk-cotton blend, making it comfy and lightweight for warm days. I love the on-trend pastel mint color! And dont little pom-poms make everything more fun? Pair it with cream and dark chocolatey browns if your skin tone is warmer, or wear it with bold black and white if you look best in cooler colors. Either way, Im sure youll be doing something artsy like brewing homemade iced tea.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Pom Pom Scarf" class="size-full wp-image-3044 aligncenter" height="340" src="http://yoursummerskin.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Pom-Pom-Scarf.jpg" width="422"/>
        </p>
        <h2 dir="ltr" id="docs-internal-guid-8e1e4952-04e2-2516-6646-7c4f4a6fd04f">
         If You Need a Wardrobe Staple: Classic Denim
        </h2>
        <p>
         Everyone loves blue. Its universally flattering! And a denim-hued scarf is an effortless match for your favorite jeans. This
         <a href="http://www.scotch-soda.com/us/en/men/accessories/scarves/washed-indigo-scarf-/15051270301.html?dwvar_15051270301_color=dessin%20B">
          washed indigo scarf
         </a>
         from Scotch &amp; Soda ($69) fits the bill perfectly. The gentle, wavy pattern provides interest without being too distracting. Perfect for a crisp evening. (Im picturing marshmallows around a campfire.) Wear this with your classic crisp, white button-down, some tailored shorts, and metallic strappy sandals.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Denim Scarf" class="size-full wp-image-3045 aligncenter" height="336" src="http://yoursummerskin.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Denim-Scarf.jpg" width="297"/>
        </p>
        <h2 dir="ltr" id="docs-internal-guid-d58302b2-04e3-62b3-865e-846ffe70c02b">
         If You Want Something Cozy: Feathery Cashmere
        </h2>
        <p dir="ltr">
         The hand feel of fabric is extremely important. The older I get, the more I value soft, quality material (bye bye, scratchy polyester!). So this
         <a href="http://shop.nordstrom.com/s/halogen-crinkled-cashmere-wrap/3517146">
          gauzy cashmere scarf
         </a>
         ($74) is a dream come true. Pair this cream one with a flowing rose top or a sky blue cotton shirt. If whisper-gray is more your color try pairing it with blues and lilacs. Either way, itll keep you from freezing in an air-conditioned movie theater or when the temperature drops at the beach.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         <img alt="Feather Scarf" class="size-full wp-image-3046 aligncenter" height="320" src="http://yoursummerskin.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Feather-Scarf.jpg" width="449"/>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         OK, your turn! Which of these summer scarves would you wear? Do you already have a go-to scarf for summer nights? Let us know in the comments below!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
