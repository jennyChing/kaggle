Candidates those who qualify in all the tiers will be able to take admission in IITs, ISMs and CSFTIs all over the country. Though the candidates can only appear in JEE Main and take admission in one of the various options available for the same. Though there are various details related to the exam but here we are focusing on providing the details for JEE Main 2015 Answer Key only. Then they must check each and every answer of their with the answer provided in the answer key. Depending on whether the answer is correct or incorrect they must mark themselves. Negative marking criteria must also be followed to get more precise total. After repeating these steps for all the questions, add the marks at the end. These marks are the approximate marks calculated using answer key. But till the declaration of official result, candidates must not consider these as final marks.
