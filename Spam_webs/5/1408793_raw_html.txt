         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 20px;">
           <strong>
            Description:
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Have you ever wished you could take multiple materials and make alloys out of them? Well now you can! This mod adds alloys to Minecraft.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 20px;">
           <strong>
            Features:
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Alloys: Iron Gold Alloy
          </li>
          <li>
           Tools: Iron Gold Alloy Sword, Iron Gold Alloy Pickaxe, Iron Gold Alloy Axe, Iron Gold Alloy Shovel, Iron Gold Alloy Hoe
          </li>
          <li>
           Armor: None (Yet!)
          </li>
          <li>
           Blocks: Iron Gold Alloy Block
          </li>
          <li>
           Other: Iron Gold Alloy Mix
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 20px;">
           <strong>
            Recipes:
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           1. Iron Gold Alloy Mix: 1 iron ingot and 1 gold ingot. Gives you 2 Iron Gold Alloy Mix
          </li>
          <li>
           2. Put Iron Gold Alloy Mix in a furnace to get 1 Iron Gold Alloy.
          </li>
          <li>
           3: Iron Gold Alloy Block: Fill the entire crafting space in a crafting table with Iron Gold Alloy
          </li>
          <li>
           4. Iron Gold Alloy Pickaxe: Make an iron pickaxe, but use Iron Gold Alloy instead of iron.
          </li>
          <li>
           5. Iron Gold Alloy Axe: Make an iron axe, but use Iron Gold Alloy instead of iron.
          </li>
          <li>
           6. Iron Gold Alloy Sword Make an iron Sword, but use Iron Gold Alloy instead of iron.
          </li>
          <li>
           7. Iron Gold Alloy Shovel: Make an iron shovel, but use Iron Gold Alloy instead of iron.
          </li>
          <li>
           8. Iron Gold Alloy Hoe: Make an iron hoe, but use Iron Gold Alloy instead of iron.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>

          <span style="font-size: 20px;">
           <strong>
            How to installAlloys Mod:
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Download and install
           <a href="http://www.modsdownloads.com/minecraft-forge-2/" target="_blank" title="Minecraft Forge">
            Minecraft Forge
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           Download the mod
          </li>
          <li>
           Drag and drop the downloaded jar file into
           <ul>
            <li>
             C:UsersYOURUSERNAMEAppDataRoaming.minecraftmods folder
            </li>
            <li>
             /Users/[Your user]/Library/Application Support/minecraft/mods on Mac folder
            </li>
           </ul>
          </li>
          <li>
           If one does not exist you can create one
