        <p style="text-align: left;">
         St. Patricks Day is almost here, so I thought this would be the perfect time to gather all of my 29 St Patricks Day Recipes together and share them with you. Each year I try to add a few more of my favorites, and as of today I am up to 29 recipeswoo hoo! My Irish ancestors should be proud, lol.
         <span id="more-18204">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://cookingwithcurls.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/My-favorite-29-St.-Patricks-Day-Recipes.....so-far-cookingwithcurls.jpg">
          <img alt="My favorite 29 St. Patrick's Day Recipes.....so far | cookingwithcurls" class="aligncenter wp-image-18207 size-full" height="1000" src="http://cookingwithcurls.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/My-favorite-29-St.-Patricks-Day-Recipes.....so-far-cookingwithcurls.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Some are traditional Irish recipes that my great grandmothers would have made {by hand of course}, and some use Irish ingredients like Guinness and BAILEYS. The Boozy Shamrock Shake is a St. Patricks Day tradition, simply because it is green! I think thats why St. Patricks Day is so much fun, there are no rules, and there are no gifts to buy!!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         So whether you are having a party, or just want to celebrate this fun holiday, you are sure to find some new favorites in this collection. All of these recipes have been
         <del>
          tried out on
         </del>
         served to my family multiple time over the years.The images from my first posts need to be redone {sorry about that}, but the recipes themselves are delicious!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         How do you celebrate St. Patricks Day? What recipes are missing from my list that you think I should try next? Im not a fan of corned beef, so we can go ahead and skip those. 
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Enjoy!!
        </p>
