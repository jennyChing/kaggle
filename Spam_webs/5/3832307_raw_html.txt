        <h1 class="entry-title">
         DADA GANA NATIONAL INDIGENOUS LEADERSHIP PROGRAM!
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <!-- Facebook Like Button Vivacity Infotech BEGIN -->
        <fb:like action="like" colorscheme="light" href="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/dada-gana-national-indigenous-womens-leadership-program-2014/" layout="standard" show_faces="false" width="450">
        </fb:like>
        <!-- Facebook Like Button Vivacity Infotech END -->
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/eaCbabUwpa0" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Dada-logo-vector-V1.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-2455 size-medium" height="300" src="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Dada-logo-vector-V1-289x300.jpg" width="289"/>
         </a>
        </h1>
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #ff6600;">
           Strong Hearing, Strong Thinking, Strong Understanding!
          </span>
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Yurra, Yurra, welcome,welcome! WATCH this space for when the NEXT Dada Gana National Indigenous Leadership Programis OPEN!
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
        </h3>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          DOWNLOAD
          <a href="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Dada-Gana_Information_document.pdf">
           Dada Gana_Information_document
          </a>
          FOR MORE INFORMATION
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Dada Gana recognises and respects the complex histories and modern day challenges of leadership in the 21st century that are unique to First Nationspeoples. The uncertainty of the currentclimate within Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Affairs is causing instability and havoc for First Nations peoples. As a result our children, Old People, organisationsand communities are suffering greatly and often silently.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/IMG_3081.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_3081" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-2880" height="200" src="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/IMG_3081-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/IMG_4759.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_4759" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-2882" height="200" src="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/IMG_4759-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          We all feel this suffering deeply within our hearts, and even though our spirits may be strong, there are times when emotionally, physically and mentally we too are suffering in silence. On top of this wehave continue to soldier on trying to stay strong for our Mob, whilst juggling work, family, cultural responsibilities,and community expectations.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #993366;">
          <em>
           <strong>
            
           </strong>
           <strong>
            <strong>
             Yes
            </strong>
            Dada Gana is truly out of the box and in my work experience of training it was the best medicine both culturally and as a woman. With my eyes and earsopen feeling my strength in my culture, my ancestors and family with me I can achieve so much more in my personal and professional life. My thinking and my approach to situations has changed quite remarkable.
           </strong>
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <address style="text-align: center;">
         Margaret Grenfell Sustainability Support Officer  Culture and Community Straddie Camping
        </address>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
        </h3>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          DOWNLOAD
          <a href="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Dada-Gana_Information_document.pdf">
           Dada Gana_Information_document
          </a>
          FOR MORE INFORMATION
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          As leaders who aspire to make a real difference weneed new practical and simple skills, new thinking, and new ways of being highly effective in this fast changing world if we are going to successfully lead in the changes we are here to make, keep our own well-being and health strong, and pave the way for others
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/IMG_4992.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_4992" class=" size-medium wp-image-2879 aligncenter" height="200" src="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/IMG_4992-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #993366;">
          <strong>
           WHAT MAKES DADA GANA SO EFFECTIVE
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #808080;">
           Having the program run over 7 months allowed us time to practice the learningsby usingthem inreal life situations Teena Akiba
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #808080;">
           Having the 2nd Leadership School at Minjerribah as a residential and sharing an apartment with my sista-gal group worked well. This workshop brought us closer together as a sista group and on a friendship level. Cheryl Harward
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #808080;">
           Being in a group of strong women empowers you.You want to go that one step further and better yourself rather than staying in your comfort zone. Amy Blow
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #808080;">
           I love the way we are able to have interaction with the leadership team and hear their personal stories. You feel equal amongst the group. Sonia Minniecon
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #993366;">
          <strong>
           HERE IS WHAT PEOPLE GOT FROM PARTICIPATING
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #808080;">
           <strong>
            feeling and knowing they are strong in their culture, identity and spirit;
           </strong>
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #808080;">
           <strong>
            freed up from the constraints of a colonial mindset;
           </strong>
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #808080;">
           <strong>
            growth in their emotional fitness, well-beingand resilient;
           </strong>
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #808080;">
           <strong>
            masterful communicatorswho know how to have difficult conversations with ease;
           </strong>
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #808080;">
           <strong>
            highly skilled in building and sustaining good healthy relationships;
           </strong>
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #808080;">
           <strong>
            confident in how they can turn an idea into a real life result
           </strong>
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          Dada Gana leadership focuses on educating and training you in how to apply all the knowledge and skills in the program immediately. We want you to be able to use and optimise your learnings, and increase your performance so that you can produce real-time personal and professional results.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #993366;">
          Its one of a kind and brings out the best in you. Loran Bowie
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #993366;">
          Be prepared to be shocked. Be prepared to want to change your leadership. Be prepared for the best conversations. Suzanne Connoll-Andrews
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #993366;">
          Be ready for an awakening of heart, mind, and spirit  its worth it. It changes your life choices. great support and friendships. Margie Grenfell
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          DOWN
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <a href="http://www.starsleadershipinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Dada-Gana_Information_document.pdf">
            Dada Gana_Information_document
           </a>
           <a href="/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Dada-Gana_Information_document.pdf" style="color: #ff0000; text-decoration: underline;" target="_blank">
            FOR
           </a>
          </span>
          MORE INFORMATION
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          The National Dada Gana Indigenous Leadership program is sponsored by the Australian Governments Department of the Prime Minister and Cabinet and designed and lead by the STARS Institute of Learning and Leadership.
         </span>
        </p>
