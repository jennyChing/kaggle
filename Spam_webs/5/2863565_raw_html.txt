       <h2 style="text-align: left;">
        <span style="color: #d51e3a;">
         HAPPY 2014 ALL!
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        The New Years come around alreadyand weve all come back from our wonderfully lazy and food-filled breaks raring to go  what better way to ease your brain cells into the new projects for 2014 than with a little bit of Trivia to get those neurones firing?
       </p>
       <p>
        Coincidentallyits also
        <a href="http://www.holidayinsights.com/other/trivia.htm" title="National Trivia Day">
         National Trivia Day
        </a>
        in the US today  so were kicking off our brand new music trivia series:
       </p>
       <h2>
        Trivia Tuesday!
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="Trivia Tuesday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3274" height="267" src="http://blog.ricall.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Trivia-Tuesday-image-600.png" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        You may have noticed us sneakily posting photos to our facebook page with trivia attached to them  take a moment to have a look through if you havet already  and each week well be asking a trivia question based on the music acts in our catalogue.
       </p>
       <p>
        Dont worry if you dont know the answer off the bat  theyre all tucked away in our photo albums alongside each artist.
       </p>
       <h2>
        But thats not all!
       </h2>
       <p>
        <img alt="Trivia Tuesday Discount" class="wp-image-3276 alignleft" height="196" src="http://blog.ricall.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Trivia-Tuesday-Discount-280.png" style="border: 1px solid #777777;" width="196"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Well also be adding the tracks relating to each weeks trivia to our Trivia Tuesday playlist and offering a spectacular:
       </p>
       <h3 style="text-align: center;">
        <span style="color: #d51e3a;">
         10% offthe license fee for each and every one of the tracks on the playlist*!
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p>
        First one is coming up on Tuesdayand as its the opener, well give you a little hint in advance: the first artist youre looking for isalso on
        <a href="http://ricall.com/search/express/playlist/80s+Rock+%26+Punk/5501" title="License Music - 80s Rock &amp; Punk">
         <em>
          this
         </em>
         <em>
          playlist
         </em>
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Look out for our first Trivia Tuesday question every Tuesday on
        <a href="http://www.facebook.com/RicallExpress" title="Facebook - Ricall Express">
         Facebook
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.twitter.com/RicallExpress" title="Twitter - Ricall Express">
         Twitter
        </a>

        <a href="https://www.facebook.com/RicallExpress/photos_albums" title="Facebook - Trivia Galleries">
         photo galleries
        </a>
        for a sneak peak at the facts!
