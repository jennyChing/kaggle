       <p>
        Spring. This means dragging your bike out of the garage or basement and hitting the road (or trail), begrudgingly putting on miles while you suffer through the last of the cold weather and potential snow storms. If youre not out there, your friends surely are, and theyre dropping you on the proverbial ladder of fitness. The key to riding in the spring is being comfortable and proper bike prep. Unless you live in Southern California, the desert, or Florida (basically any retirement destination) being prepared will pose a challenge. Weve outlined some of the top items to have in your quiver for the ever changing elements.
       </p>
