      <p>
       Theres a lot to love about summer in New York  the
       <a href="http://www.upout.com/blog/cool-nyc-rooftops/" target="_blank">
        rooftops
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="http://www.upout.com/blog/best-beaches-near-nyc-that-dont-require-a-car/" target="_blank">
        beaches
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="http://www.upout.com/blog/best-ice-cream-shops-in-nyc/" target="_blank">
        ice cream
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="http://www.upout.com/blog/new-york-city-pool-parties/" target="_blank">
        pool parties
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="http://www.upout.com/blog/the-great-escape-10-weekend-getaways-to-take-a-break-from-nyc/" target="_blank">
        weekend getaways
       </a>
        but like any other time in this city, its not all rainbows and puppies and cupcakes and
       <a href="http://forums.pelicanparts.com/uploads21/3741358429061.jpg" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://forums.pelicanparts.com/uploads21/3741358429061.jpg', 'puppies eating rainbow cupcakes');" target="_blank">
        puppies eating rainbow cupcakes
       </a>
       . In fact, sometimes it downright
       <em>
        sucks.
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       Year after year, we swear were going to move to Alaskabut year after year we stay, trapped in a giant urban furnace, wondering if somehow global warming is happening here faster than anywhere else.Theres at least 93 awful things about summer in New York, but well condense it down to 15 because if we have to type any more than that, well probablydie from heat exhaustion.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>

      </p>
      <p>
       Oh, so you had the brilliant idea of enjoying the weather with a picnic in the park? So did
       <em>
        literally everyone else.
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>

       <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-3571 size-full" height="465" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/10993445723_06df5cdce4_z.jpg?679039" title="10993445723_06df5cdce4_z" width="620"/>

      <p style="text-align: left;">
       Theres probably a New York City street under there. Well never know.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #4 The smelly people on the subway
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="245" src="http://ed_wp-content_v2.s3.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/tumblr_medz4n222o1qaqsz7.gif" title="Emma-Stone" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Make that the more than usually smelly people on the subway. Public transportation is never the most enjoyable olfactory experience, but throw on all those layers of summer sweat andnext stop, Vomittown. Transfer available to the A, C, E, and Bleurghhhh.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #5 Constantly needing to remember sunglasses, sunscreen, and an umbrella
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="625" src="http://s3-ec.buzzfed.com/static/2014-07/14/13/enhanced/webdr04/anigif_enhanced-27766-1405358688-6.gif" title="Rain in NYC" width="625"/>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: right;">
       <em>
        Image:
        <a href="http://www.buzzfeed.com/nathanwpyle/7-important-rules-for-umbrella-owners-in-nyc" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.buzzfeed.com/nathanwpyle/7-important-rules-for-umbrella-owners-in-nyc', 'Nathan W. Pyle/BuzzFeed');" target="_blank">
         Nathan W. Pyle/BuzzFeed
        </a>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       To be honest, after a weekend binge of Netflix and ordering Seamless, were lucky we can even remember pants when we leave the house. Also, everyone needs to read
       <a href="http://www.buzzfeed.com/nathanwpyle/7-important-rules-for-umbrella-owners-in-nyc" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.buzzfeed.com/nathanwpyle/7-important-rules-for-umbrella-owners-in-nyc', 'this');" target="_blank">
        this
       </a>
       before well even consider going outside.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #6 The mosquitos
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-3575 size-full" height="391" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/giant_mosquito_attack.jpg?679039" title="giant_mosquito_attack" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       This is the actual size of a New York City mosquito. And you thought the rats were bad
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #7 The scorching hot subway platforms
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="193" src="http://s3-ec.buzzfed.com/static/enhanced/webdr02/2013/7/5/16/anigif_enhanced-buzz-24569-1373056031-0.gif" title="Raiders Of The Lost Ark" width="450"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       If we wanted to steam in a sauna, wed go to a spa. And get a massage first. And not be anywhere near someone who thinks a saw is an instrument.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #8 The freezing cold buildings
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="275" src="http://i.imgur.com/jbKzaJ2.gif?1" title="Community" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Cant we be comfortable for just one single second of our day?
       <em>
        Is that too much to ask?
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #9 The overzealous cyclists in Central Park
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-3576 size-full" height="465" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/5873957782_23f823efb0_z.jpg?679039" title="5873957782_23f823efb0_z" width="620"/>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: right;">
       <em>
        Image:
        <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/24415055@N00/5873957782" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.flickr.com/photos/24415055@N00/5873957782', 'Charles Smith');" target="_blank">
         Charles Smith
        </a>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       This is not the Tour de France. You are not Lance Armstrong. If you dont calm down, we
       <em>
        will
       </em>
       remove a testicle out of spite.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #10 The droplets of air conditioner juice falling on your face
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="260" src="http://anonymousasta.files.wordpress.com/2013/12/anigif_enhanced-buzz-17589-1374245907-29.gif" title="Oh My God" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       At least we hope thats what it is.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #11Trying to figure out how to get an air conditioner up to your apartment
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-3582 size-full" height="237" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/summer-air-conditioner-falling-not-having-ac-funny-ecard-IyV.png?679039" title="summer-air-conditioner-falling-not-having-ac-funny-ecard-IyV" width="425"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Not to mention trying to figure how how the hell that thing is supposed to stay in a window.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #12Not being able to cook because any use of the stove or oven turns your apartment into a blistering hellscape
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="281" src="http://gifrific.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/sizzling-bacon-cinemagraph.gif" title="Bacon Sizzling" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       So
       <em>
        this
       </em>
       is what they mean by Hells Kitchen. If you need us, well be
       <a href="http://img.thesun.co.uk/aidemitlum/archive/01520/fridge532main_1520072a.jpg" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://img.thesun.co.uk/aidemitlum/archive/01520/fridge532main_1520072a.jpg', 'here');" target="_blank">
        here
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #13 The kids arent in school
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-3583 size-full" height="294" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/quotes-not-having-children.png?679039" title="quotes-not-having-children" width="420"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Not only does that mean our once-safe streets and beloved parks are now overrun by ill-behaved brats, it also means its time for
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #14Family vacations
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-3584 size-full" height="300" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/tourists.jpg?679039" title="tourists" width="400"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       MAKE IT STOP.
       <br/>
      </p>
      <h3>
       #15 The fact that we really dont hate it at all
      </h3>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-3586 size-full" height="415" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/11818533295_8fa2282359_z.jpg?679039" title="11818533295_8fa2282359_z" width="620"/>
      </p>

       <em>

        <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/vauvau/11818533295" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.flickr.com/photos/vauvau/11818533295', 'Clemens v. Vogelsang');" target="_blank">

        </a>

       <a href="http://www.onecoolthingaday.com/today/2011/1/27/star-wars-hoth-battle-in-nyc-blizzard-2010.html" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.onecoolthingaday.com/today/2011/1/27/star-wars-hoth-battle-in-nyc-blizzard-2010.html', 'like this');" target="_blank">
        like this

       again.
