        <p>
         <em>
          Disclosure:I am part of the Blue Dragon Ambassador program with Mom Central Canada and I receive special perks as part of my affiliation with this group. The opinions on this blog are my own.

        <p>
         Entertaining outdoors means we're trying lots of tasty treats on the grill. Mike has been sweet and has let me try lots of flavor experiments lately. I've created some new tasty
         <a href="http://spaceshipsandlaserbeams.com/kids-party-food-ideas" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           party food ideas
          </strong>
         </a>
         that we've enjoyed immensely. These wasabi plum grilled mushrooms are a winner that you will want to enjoy more than once this summer!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Wasabi Plum Grilled Mushrooms" src="http://spaceshipsandlaserbeams.com/content/blog-posts/party-food/@content/bbq-wasabi-plum-grilled-mushrooms.jpg.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Create this delicious dish for yourself:
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="grilled mushroom recipe" src="http://spaceshipsandlaserbeams.com/content/blog-posts/party-food/@content/grilled-mushroom-recipe.jpg.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         1. Soak bamboo skewers for at least one hour.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="asian mushroom recipe" src="http://spaceshipsandlaserbeams.com/content/blog-posts/party-food/@content/asian-mushroom-recipe.jpg.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         2. Thread on fresh white mushrooms and lay on a piece of plastic wrap.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="wasabi plum blue dragon mushroom" src="http://spaceshipsandlaserbeams.com/content/blog-posts/party-food/@content/wasabi-plum-blue-dragon-mushroom.jpg.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="blue dragon wasabi plum mushroom recipe" src="http://spaceshipsandlaserbeams.com/content/blog-posts/party-food/@content/blue-dragon-wasabi-plum-mushroom-recipe.jpg-1.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         3. Spread wasabi plum sauce liberally over mushrooms and massage gently.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="bbq mushroom recipe" src="http://spaceshipsandlaserbeams.com/content/blog-posts/party-food/@content/bbq-mushroom-recipe.jpg.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         4. Wrap mushrooms and marinate for two hours.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="grilled mushrooms" src="http://spaceshipsandlaserbeams.com/content/blog-posts/party-food/@content/grilled-mushrooms.jpg.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         5. Grill for approximately 20 minutes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="wasabi plum mushroom on the grill" src="http://spaceshipsandlaserbeams.com/content/blog-posts/party-food/@content/wasabi-plum-mushroom-on-the-grill.jpg"/>
        </p>
