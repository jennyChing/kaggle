                <p>
                 For some unknown reason, many folks who have cats as pets have more than one cat in their home. In fact, it is not uncommon for cat lovers to own several cats. So how do you keep track if you have several cats and still want to keep a tidy and organized home? Well, I think it is pretty darn simple. You should take the basic principles of organization and simply apply it to your cats. But to help make it clear to those of us without the necessary imagination to figure it out on our own, I did some basic web research. After many minutes of scouring the web, here are my tips and ideas on the
