        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Importance Of Briquetting Plant
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://blog.jaykhodiyar.in/importance-of-briquetting-plant/" rel="bookmark" title="11:04 am">
          <span class="entry-date">
           November 24, 2014
          </span>
         </a>
         <span class="meta-sep">
          by
         </span>
         <span class="author vcard">
          <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.jaykhodiyar.in/author/admin/" title="View all posts by admin">
           admin
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Great eco friendly features and cost saving features have made briquettes a perfect source of energy as well as an alternative of traditional fuel sources. Ever increasing prices of fuel, decreasing resources and increasing risk to environment has compelled everyone to look for better options and briquettes have proved to be the best invention in this direction.
         </p>
         <p>
          Increasing demand of briquettes as fuel has also resulted in mushrooming of manufacturers of different briquetting machines. A manufacturer of briquetting plants deals in a wide range of machines required to convert waste into briquettes.
         </p>
         <p>
          There benefits are not just limited to money saving, rather there is a long list of advantages of owning a briquetting plant. Lets have a look at few of these:
         </p>
         <p>
          For farmers: As these machines are the perfect solution to recycle different kinds of wastes like agriculture, forestry and industries, this proves to be a great solution for farmers. They have a lot of waste material after cultivation of the crop. With
          <a href="http://www.jaykhodiyar.in/super65-model.php" target="_blank">
           Super 65 machines
          </a>
          they can not only reuse the waste, but can also save money on buying fuel. Briquetting machines convert waste into cylindrical logs by leaving heavy pressure on these.
         </p>
         <p>
          CSR: By getting a briquetting plant installed in the company, organizations can also perform their corporate social responsibility. How?
         </p>
         <p>
          They can not only give their contribution in saving environment, but can also find economic source for electricity production. Various industries are blamed for polluting the environment by emitting poisonous gases during their production process. By using briquettes, they can get rid of this stigma.
         </p>
         <p>
          Environment friendly: In normal conditions, the waste of different industries is either dumped into rivers or burnt inefficiently. Both the ways drastically spoil environment and make this earth an unsafe place to live. Briquetting machines are the perfect option to minimize this risk and re-use the waste for better purposes. The great thing is that, no chemical is used in manufacturing of briquettes.
         </p>
         <p>
          Natural Resources: Renewable energy is the topic of concern for every country. Briquettes work as a great resource for electricity generation with no fear of falling short or getting over. These work as a useful bio fuel for various industries like textile, electricity etc.
         </p>
         <p>
          Government subsidy: Setting a briquetting plant is now much easier as looking at the benefits of it; government has also started supporting the same with subsidies. Poor people with no income source can now look for this option. It is a profitable venture with less investment and high returns.
         </p>
         <p>
          There are a number of
          <a href="http://www.jaykhodiyar.in/" target="_blank">
           briquetting plants manufacturers
          </a>
          in the market. Be careful while choosing one. Ensure that you carry out a thorough research on internet and get best option at affordable rates.
         </p>
        </div>
