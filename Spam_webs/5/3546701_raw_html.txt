         <p>
          An entire
          <a href="http://www.ktul.com/story/28824541/body-found-in-car-in-tulsa-walmart-parking-lot" target="_blank">
           slew of mysterious deaths surround Jade Helm 15 state Wal-Marts
          </a>
          where
          <a href="https://www.google.com/search?client=ubuntu&amp;channel=fs&amp;q=Decomposing+Bodies+Found+In+Vehicles+at+4+Different+Walmarts&amp;ie=utf-8&amp;oe=utf-8" target="_blank">

          </a>
          s period of time in
          <a href="http://www.khou.com/story/news/local/2015/03/15/mans-body-found-inside-car-in-wal-mart-parking-lot/24831869/" target="_blank">
           Texas
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://kfor.com/2015/03/20/osbi-two-bodies-discovered-behind-walmart-in-oklahoma-town/" target="_blank">
           Oklahoma in at least 5 different incidents
          </a>
          . The latest case getting mainstream media attention from
          <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-3043548/Colorado-father-five-missing-13-days-seen-Walmart-buying-300-items.html" target="_blank">
           this Daily Mail story

           shares

          of a Colorado father of five who 
          <span class="IL_AD" id="IL_AD7">
           disappeared
          </span>
           after spending $300 at a southern California Wal-Mart. With so many mysterious deaths surrounding
          <span class="IL_AD" id="IL_AD3">
           Jade
          </span>
          <span class="IL_AD" id="IL_AD2">
           Helm
          </span>
          15
          <span class="IL_AD" id="IL_AD8">
           states
          </span>
          Wal-Marts in only the last month, were offered a conspiratorial look into a mysterious Wal-Mart tunnel system
          <span class="IL_AD" id="IL_AD5">
           detailed
          </span>
          below that lead us to ask if these tunnel systems will soon be used to quickly make people disappear out of public eye, to never be seen again, giving 
          <span class="IL_AD" id="IL_AD10">
           black sites
          </span>
           a whole new meaning as the red list is taken underground? If they check you in, can you ever leave?
          <strong>
           <img alt="poster-0" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-46816" height="360" src="http://freepatriotpost.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/poster-0.jpg" width="640"/>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           This quote from a former US Navy SEAL gives us
           <span class="IL_AD" id="IL_AD6">
            even more
           </span>
           to go by.: The tunnels are already in place. They are using the 6 months, which brings us into September, to clear the places out and configure them for supply depots, communication hubs and pre-processing centers.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          With Wal-Marts in several
          <a href="http://www.allnewspipeline.com/What_Martial_Law_Will_Look_Like.php" target="_blank">
           Jade Helm 15 states mysteriously closing for at least 6 months
          </a>
          and the mystery now deeper as we learn of an
          <a href="http://www.oocities.org/walmarttunnels/" target="_blank">
           underground Wal-Mart tunnel system project
          </a>
          launched along with the
          <a href="http://www.nbcnews.com/id/40552073/ns/us_news-security/t/homeland-security-taps-new-partner-terror-fight-wal-mart/?hc_location=ufi#.VTBcseQ57yz" target="_blank">
           Department of Homeland Security
          </a>
          and NSA involving hundreds of stores and the creation of a system of underground tunnels throughout the country which they say were designed to further protect the homeland and ensure the safety of its citizens in case of nuclear, biological or chemical attacks.
         </p>

           CICs

         <p>
          Paulding County, Georgia, is said to be the central location of the project. Yorkville, Georgia is to be a terminus of sorts for the hubs.
         </p>
         <p>
          Wal-Marts involvement in the project has not yet been officially commented on, but it is generally percieved that Wal-Marts in these areas are staging areas for the tunnel project.
         </p>
         <p>
          It is to be noted that this project is under a great deal of scrutiny by those who live near the ongoing construction project. Analysts do not believe the project will be available for public use, and will not be publicly acknowledged (rumors of usage by NASA for its
          <span class="IL_AD" id="IL_AD9">
           installation
          </span>
          in Huntsville, AL have been found to be true).
         </p>
         <p>
          While the Official Wal-Mart Tunnels of Paulding County Investigation tells us that Wal-Marts involvement in the project had yet to be officially commented on, a number of Wal-Marts throughout the South were to be hubs of the project as shared in the new video below from

Are they expecting some kind of WMD event?
