      <p>
       <font face="arial, helvetica, sans-serif">
        2. The Local Bar &amp; Grill
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font face="arial, helvetica, sans-serif">
        Formerly known as Brussels, this bar and restaurant has changed its name to The Local. They've updated their cocktails and changed the food menu, but it's still the same welcoming pub environment as it was before.
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font face="arial, helvetica, sans-serif">
        With over 400 square meters across two floors, The Local also functions as an events space, with the second floor often being rented out while the first floor continues to serve as a pub for regular customers. The Local also caters for parties and can provide bartenders and alcohol for your own events. Multiple flat screen televisions and a large, 3 meter projection screen makes The Local an ideal space for watching sports, screening movies and showing presentations.
       </font>
      </p>
      <table align="center" border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0">

      <br/>
      <p>
       <font face="arial, helvetica, sans-serif">
        The Local has a wide range of Belgian beers: think Westmalle, Kwak and Lindemans. Most of these 33cl bottles go for 40 RMB, but you can buy three for 100 RMB or seven for 200 RMB. There are a number of French and German bottled beers too, plus draught lagers (Stella for 30 RMB; Tiger for 25 RMB), house wines (35 RMB a glass) and a range of cocktails (40 RMB).
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font face="arial, helvetica, sans-serif">
        This bar is a great place to watch sport. It may currently lack a killer buzz, but for those who simply want to watch and drink, its perfect: the team here is keen to please and can seemingly show anything.
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font face="arial, helvetica, sans-serif">
        Open: 4:00 p.m. - 3:00 a.m. Mon-Thur; 4:00 p. m. - 4:00 a.m. Fri; 2:00 p.m. - 4:00 a.m. Sat; 2:00 p.m. - 3:00 a.m. Sun
       </font>
      </p>
      <p>
       <font face="arial, helvetica, sans-serif">
        Add: 4 Gongti Bei Lu, Chaoyang District, Beijing
       </font>
      </p>
