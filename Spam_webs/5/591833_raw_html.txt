         Loving Parents..Love them BACK
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="meta-data">

        </span>

       <div class="entry">
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          <img alt="" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_RG9_WyRUwAE/TF0zHUA7WuI/AAAAAAAACdk/LLjnDZyJ3sM/s400/dad.jpg" style="width: 328px; height: 384px; cursor: hand;"/>
          <img alt="" border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_RG9_WyRUwAE/TF0zHNlm9FI/AAAAAAAACdc/A9J9XMUKm9k/s400/LovingParents.jpg" style="width: 250px; height: 333px; cursor: hand;"/>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           An 80 year old man was sitting on the sofa in his house along with his 45 years old highly educated son. Suddenly a crow perched on their window.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           The Father asked his Son,What is this?
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           The Son replied It is a crow.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           After a few minutes, the Father asked his Son the 2nd time, What is this?
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           The Son said Father, I have just now told you It's a crow.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           After a little while, the old Father again asked his Son the 3rd time,
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           What is this?
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           At this time some ex-pression of irritation was felt in the Son's tone when he said to his Father with a rebuff. It's a crow, a crow.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           A little after, the Father again asked his Son t he 4th time, What is this?
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           This time the Son shouted at his Father, Why do you keep asking me the same question again and again, although I have told you so many times IT IS A CROW. Are you not able to understand this?
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           A little later the Father went to his room and came back with an old tattered diary, which he had maintained since his Son was born. On opening a page, he asked his Son to read that page. When the son read it, the following words were written in the diary :-
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Today my little son aged three was sitting with me on the sofa, when a crow was sitting on the window. My Son asked me 23 times what it was, and I replied to him all 23 times that it was a Crow. I hugged him lovingly each time h e asked me the same question again and again for 23 times. I did not at all feel irritated I rather felt affection for my innocent child.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           While the little child asked him 23 times What is this, the Father had felt no irritation in replying to the same question all 23 times and when today the Father asked his Son the same question just 4 times, the Son felt irritated and annoyed.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           So..
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           If your parents attain old age, do not repulse them or look at them as a burden, but speak to them a gracious word, be cool, obedient, humble and kind to them. Be considerate to your parents.From today say this aloud, I want to see my parents happy forever. They have cared for me ever since I was a little child. They have always showered their selfless love on me.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           They crossed all mountains and valleys without seeing the storm and heat to make me a person presentable in the society today.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Say a prayer to God, I will serve my old parents in the BEST way. I will say all good and kind words to my dear parents, no matter how they behave.
          </span>
          <br/>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Lovely Thoughts for Lovely People Just Like You
          </span>
