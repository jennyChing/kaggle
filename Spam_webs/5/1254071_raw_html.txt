              West Roxbury Moving Made Simple - Boston
             </a>
            </h2>

             <p itemprop="description">
              West Roxbury is kind of the suburb of the city. So, it has a bit of a suburban feel to it. The typical home in this part of town is a standard colonial.
             </p>
             <p>
              A
              <strong>
               Colonial style home is probably one of the easiest homes to move into architecturally
              </strong>
              . In general,
              <a href="http://marathonmoving.com/local-residential-moving">
               moving in West Roxbury
              </a>
              tend to be the easiest moves around the city itself. Colonial style floor plans are the easiest homes to maneuver. There are some challenges, as there always are when
              <a href="http://marathonmoving.com/local-residential-moving">
               moving in Boston
              </a>
              , and this is the age of the homes. Many of these Colonials were built well before the average height of man was 5'10". However, the access to the homes in West Roxbury is probably the best in the city.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               This is also one of the few areas of the city where you don't have to overly concern yourself with parking permits or street access for trucks
              </strong>
              . There are only a few streets in town where you would have to arrange for a parking permit. However, if you are
              <a href="http://marathonmoving.com/interstate-moving">
               moving long distance
              </a>
              and have a tractor trailer coming to town, this can be a little bit challenging. West Roxbury does have a lot of Parkways that lead to the town or through the town, and tractor trailers are not allowed on them.
             </p>
             <p>
              For more information on moving and storage around Boston,
              <a href="http://marathonmoving.com/contact-us">
               contact Marathon Moving
              </a>
              .
