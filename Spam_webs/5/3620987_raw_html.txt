        <h1 class="feature-title-article">
         <a href="https://tropicalogy.com/19-things-to-do-in-barbados-from-a-cruise-ship/">
          19 Things To Do In Barbados From A Cruise Ship
         </a>
        </h1>
        <p class="meta">
         By Billy Walsh   Updated:
         <span class="post-date">
          July 22, 2014
         </span>
        </p>
        <div class="desc-article">
         <img alt="things to do in barbados from a cruise ship" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7723" height="300" src="https://tropicalogy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/things-to-do-in-barbados-from-a-cruise-ship1.jpg" width="650"/>
         <div id="photo-credit">
          Photo by
          <a href="https://tropicalogy.com/19-things-to-do-in-barbados-from-a-cruise-ship/#paddlebarbados">
           Paddle Barbados
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         Theres certainly no shortage of things to do in Barbados from a cruise ship, but you only get so much time. We want you to get the most out of your cruise stop, so weve come up with a highlight list of must do activities and weve organized it in a way to help you easily plan your day. Weve talked to the locals of Barbados and gotten traveler feedback on the tours, excursions, food, shopping, cultural attractions, and beaches in easy walking or driving distance from the Barbados Cruise Terminal / port. You also might save a little money by booking directly with some of these organizations.
         <p>
         </p>
         <div id="blog-pink-title">
          <h2>
           TOURS &amp; EXCURSIONS
          </h2>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div id="paddlebarbados">
         </div>
         <div id="bold-title">
          <h3>
           PADDLE BARBADOS
          </h3>
         </div>
         <br/>
         <img alt="paddle barbados sup lessons and tours carlisle bay" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7529" height="300" src="https://tropicalogy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/paddle-barbados-sup-lessons-and-tours.jpg" width="650"/>
         <div id="photo-credit">
          Photo by Paddle Barbados
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <b>
          Location:
         </b>
         Bridgetown, Carlisle Bay
         <br/>
         <b>
          Drive time from cruise ship:
         </b>
         approx. 5 mins
         <br/>
         <b>
          Time needed for activity:
         </b>
         1.5 hrs
         <br/>
         <b>
          Price:
         </b>
         $60
         <br/>
         <p>
         </p>
         If you havent ever paddle boarded, youre missing out on one of the great vantage points to experience the ocean, and Carlisle Bay of Barbados is an amazing spot to do it. With Paddle Barbados, youll learn SUP quickly, or if you already know how, you can start exploring the glowing blue coastlines of Barbados right away through their rentals and tours including SUP &amp; Snorkel, SUP Surfing, and more.
         <br/>
         <a href="https://tropicalogy.com/paddle-barbados/">
          Learn More
         </a>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div id="agapey">
         </div>
         <div id="bold-title">
          <h3>
           AGAPEY CHOCOLATE FACTORY TOUR
          </h3>
         </div>
         <br/>
         <img alt="agapey chocolate factory tour rum bar" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7534" height="300" src="https://tropicalogy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/agapey-chocolate-factory-tour-rum-bar.jpg" width="650"/>
         <div id="photo-credit">
          Photo by Agapey Chocolate
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <b>
          Location:
         </b>
         Bridgetown
         <br/>
         <b>
          Drive time from cruise ship:
         </b>
         approx. 5 mins
         <br/>
         <b>
          Time needed for activity:
         </b>
         1 hr 15 mins
         <br/>
         <b>
          Price:
         </b>
         $18
         <br/>
         <p>
         </p>
         Step off the ship and into a chocolate lovers paradise. During the Agapey Chocolate Factory tour youll taste dark chocolate and Barbadian Plantation Reserve cane sugar. Youll also become a chocolate history buff and learn about the rare bean to bar process. Make sure you book a tour ahead of time as they are so busy they cant accept walk-ins.
         <br/>
         <a href="https://tropicalogy.com/agapey-chocolate-factory-tour/">
          Learn More
         </a>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div id="predator">
         </div>
         <div id="bold-title">
          <h3>
           PREDATOR SPORTFISHING CHARTERS
          </h3>
         </div>
         <br/>
         <img alt="predator sportfishing barbados fishing charter billfish and marlin" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7736" height="300" src="https://tropicalogy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/predator-sportfishing-barbados-fishing-charter.jpg" width="650"/>
         <div id="photo-credit">
          Photo by Predator Sportfishing Charters
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <b>
          Location:
         </b>
         Bridgetown
         <br/>
         <b>
          Drive time from cruise ship:
         </b>
         6 mins
         <br/>
         <b>
          Time needed for activity:
         </b>
         5 or 8 hr trips; duration can also be customized
         <br/>
         <b>
          Price:
         </b>
         from $500
         <br/>
         <p>
         </p>
         To the devoted fishermen, or those aspiring to be one, theres no sweeter sound than that of a screaming reel as a determined fish zips line away from the boat. Youre likely to hear this sound with Predator Sportfishing Charters as they certainly know how to find and catch fish. Most of your trip will be devoted to fishing, but a portion is also reserved for enjoying the natural scenery.
         <br/>
         <a href="https://tropicalogy.com/barbados-fishing-charters/">
          Learn More
         </a>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div id="calabaza">
         </div>
         <div id="bold-title">
          <h3>
           CALABAZA SAILING CRUISES
          </h3>
         </div>
         <br/>
         <img alt="swim with turtles calabaza sailing cruises barbados catamaran" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7536" height="300" src="https://tropicalogy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/calabaza-sailing-cruises-barbados-catamaran.jpg" width="650"/>
         <div id="photo-credit">
          Photo by Calabaza Sailing Cruises
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <b>
          Location:
         </b>
         Bridgetown
         <br/>
         <b>
          Drive time from cruise ship:
         </b>
         6 mins
         <br/>
         <b>
          Time needed for activity:
         </b>
         5 hr lunch, 3 hr sunset, and customized cruises
         <br/>
         <b>
          Price:
         </b>
         $110
         <br/>
         <p>
         </p>
         Relax, snorkel, and swim among the turtles, reefs, and gorgeous aqua blue waters of Barbados. Calabaza Sailing Cruises offers private and personalized charters with no more than 12 guests. As if thats not enough, youll also enjoy an excellent local Bajan lunch, snacks, cakes, and their tasty rum punch.
         <br/>
         <a href="https://tropicalogy.com/calabaza-sailing-cruises/">
          Learn More
         </a>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div id="bold-title">
          <h3>
           ECO DIVE BARBADOS
          </h3>
         </div>
         <br/>
         <img alt="cruise ship stop scuba eco dive barbados" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7766" height="300" src="https://tropicalogy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/cruise-ship-stop-scuba-eco-dive-barbados.jpg" width="650"/>
         <div id="photo-credit">
          Photo by Andrew Western of Eco Dive Barbados
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <b>
          Location:
         </b>
         Bridgetown
         <br/>
         <b>
          Drive time from cruise ship:
         </b>
         6 mins
         <br/>
         <b>
          Time needed for activity:
         </b>
         4 hours, including prep
         <br/>
         <b>
          Price:
         </b>
         from $75/person
         <br/>
         <p>
         </p>
         Cruise ship cocktails, deck chairs, and dinners are all quite nice, but what about the beauty under your cruise ship? Explore the deeper waters of Barbados and its enchanting wildlife including turtles, seahorses, lobsters, rays, barracuda, and lively coral reef ecosystems. Eco Dive Barbados is a highly rated and praised dive tour you wont want to miss.
         <br/>
         <a href="https://tropicalogy.com/eco-dive-barbados/">
          Learn More
         </a>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div id="naturally">
         </div>
         <div id="bold-title">
          <h3>
           BARBADOS NATURALLY  ISLAND TOUR
          </h3>
         </div>
         <br/>
         <img alt="naturally island tour barbados, historic, chalky mount pottery" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7538" height="300" src="https://tropicalogy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/naturally-island-tour-barbados.jpg" width="650"/>
         <div id="photo-credit">
          Photo by Eco Adventures Barbados
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <b>
          Location:
         </b>
         Dover, Christ Church
         <br/>
         <b>
          Drive time from cruise ship:
         </b>
         13 mins
         <br/>
         <b>
          Time needed for activity:
         </b>
         3, 4, or 6 hr shore excursions
         <br/>
         <b>
          Price:
         </b>
         prices vary
         <br/>
         <p>
         </p>
         Looking for a break from floating in the water? You wont want to miss Eco Adventures Barbados Naturally  Island Tour. Through the ultimate cultural tour of Barbados, youll explore historic Speightstown, a farm with indigenous Blackbelly sheep, the 100% functional local art of Chalky Mount Pottery, and the impressive and rugged eastern coastline of Bathsheba.
         <br/>
         <a href="https://tropicalogy.com/barbados-naturally-island-tour/">
          Learn More
         </a>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div id="ridethetide">
         </div>
         <div id="bold-title">
          <h3>
           RIDE THE TIDE SURF SCHOOL
          </h3>
         </div>
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/84atDK-0WVo?start=29&amp;feature=oembed" width="640">
         </iframe>
         <p>
         </p>
         <b>
          Location:
         </b>
         Atlantic Shores, Christ Church
         <br/>
         <b>
          Drive time from cruise ship:
         </b>
         21 mins
         <br/>
         <b>
          Time needed for activity:
         </b>
         2hrs 20mins
         <br/>
         <b>
          Price:
         </b>
         Normally $60/head, but they run specials
         <br/>
         <p>
         </p>
         If you love the ocean, why wouldnt you want to feel the natural push of its waves gliding you across its surface on a surfboard? Take a short lesson with Ride The Tide surf school on a gorgeous beach that is perfect for beginner surfers. Or if youre already an expert, rent a board or take an adventurous surf tour off the beaten path of Barbados coast.
         <br/>
         <a href="https://tropicalogy.com/ride-the-tide-barbados-surf-school/">
          Learn More
         </a>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div id="foursquare">
         </div>
         <div id="bold-title">
          <h3>
           FOURSQUARE RUM FACTORY &amp; HERITAGE PARK
          </h3>
         </div>
         <br/>
         <b>

          <h2>
           TRANSPORTATION SERVICES
          </h2>
         </div>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Taxis
         </b>
          Taxis are available outside the cruise terminal. If you would like to prearrange a regular or luxury taxi for a tour or a direct ride to where you want to go, try
         <a href="http://www.suntoursbarbados.com/" target="_blank">
          Sun Tours Barbados
         </a>
         .
         <br/>
         <b>
          Tour Operators
         </b>
          If youre taking a tour or excursion, you can contact your chosen tour operator directly for transportation options.
         <br/>
         <b>
          Car Rentals
         </b>
          If you feel you have enough time to rent a car and find your own way around the island, here is a list of
         <a href="http://www.barbados.org/tours/barbados_car_rentals.htm#.U4StQ5RdXGI" target="_blank">
          car rental options
         </a>
         .
         <br/>
         <b>
          ZR Vans
         </b>
          An option for cheap and quick stops around the island are the privately owned ZR Vans (ZR on license plate). Make sure youre ready for a
         <a href="https://tropicalogy.com/island-transport-with-character/">
          thrill ride
         </a>
         if you choose this option.
