       Our Services

          <div class="gdl-page-content">
           <p>
            <img alt="laser" class="bordered alignleft size-medium wp-image-2883 colorbox-2607" height="199" src="http://www.hebertdental.com/files/2013/04/laser-300x199.jpg" width="300"/>
            Hebert Dental takes pride in offering a wide range of dental services.
           </p>
           <p>
            Whether you need complex restorative procedure or simple teeth cleaning, you may expect a great experience with us. Our practice boasts of advanced tools and equipment, and a relaxing environment.
           </p>
           <p>
            Our
            <b>
             Eau Claire dentists
            </b>
            also offer experience and expertise in different fields of dentistry. They may provide the quality healthcare and treatment you deserve.
           </p>
           <p>
            We offer the following dental services:
           </p>
           <p>
            <br class="clear"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Preventive Dentistry
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Our dentists believe that prevention is always better than cure. This is why we offer comprehensive dental screenings and laser teeth cleanings. We will take excellent care of your teeth and gums to keep them strong and healthy. Our practice also takes pride in offering mercury-free dental fillings to restore you teeths natural form and functioning.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Cosmetic Dentistry
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            We understand how your smile affects your confidence. If your teeth are chipped, overcrowded, crooked, or discolored, drop by our officefor cosmetic dentistry procedures. Our most popular is in-office teeth whitening, specifically the KoR and Zoom! whitening solutions. They can get rid of any deep-seated stains and give you a perfectly white smile.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Restorative Dentistry
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Losing a tooth can be a big problem  especially if its located at the front. This may make you feel uncomfortable and give you low self-esteem. If you lose your teeth because of an accident or periodontal problem, you may always head to our office for an implant dentistry procedure. This dental solution will permanently replace your missing teeth and restore your mouths natural appearance and functioning.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Sedation Dentistry
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Invasive dental procedures often cause fear and anxiety to our patients. These in turn keeps them from getting the proper healthcare and treatment they need. If youre one those patients who feel uncomfortable sitting on a dental chair, you will be glad to know that we offer sedation dentistry for your convenience. You may always count on our fast and effective relaxation solutions whether youre afraid of simple teeth cleaning or anxious about the pain involved in having a root canal.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Orthodontics
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Having crooked, overcrowded, and widely gapped set of teeth can take a toll on your self-esteem. In some cases, it may even affect your eating habits and your overall lifestyle. If you want to have your teeth straightened in the most effective way possible, we have a wide range of orthodontic treatments you can choose from. Our solutions basically include conventional braces and clear aligners, also known as Invisalign. These two treatments offer different levels of comfort and convenience, but they will definitely correct your malocclusion.
           </p>
           <p>
            Hebert Dental offers all of these dental healthcare and treatments. If you want to know more about them or want to have a treatment program customized for your special needs, call us at 715-318-8893 and set an appointment. We look forward to seeing youat our
            <b>
             Eau Claire dental
            </b>
            clinic.
           </p>
