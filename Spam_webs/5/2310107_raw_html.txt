              <h1 class="title" itemprop="title headline">
               How Happy Are Designers?
              </h1>
             </div>
             <span class="deck pull-three" itemprop="deck description">
              <p>
               The glass is half full, and then some.
              </p>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div class="tools row collapse">
             <div class="twelve columns hide-on-print">

           <div class="row collapse">
            <div class="body prose twelve columns" itemprop="body articleBody">
             <p>
              Creative people are pretty damn happy at work according to a new study by
              <a href="http://www.adobe.com/#acrobat" target="_blank">
               Adobe
              </a>
              . After interviewing more than 1,000 people, Adobe found that the glass is more than half full for the country's designers, illustrators, and photographers.
             </p>
             <p>
              Here, Co.Design information design editor
              <a class="profile" href="//www.fastcompany.com/person/lily-tidhar">
               Lily Tidhar
              </a>
              visualizes the most intriguing findings: 93% of the respondents felt valued at work, 84% recognized the value of their creative passion, and 88% thought their industry's best days lie ahead. Such optimism!
             </p>
             <figure class="inline-large inline">
              <img alt="" src="//b.fastcompany.net/multisite_files/fastcompany/imagecache/inline-large/inline/2014/07/3032565-inline-i2-adobecreatives2.png"/>
              <figcaption>
               Click to view larger. Credit: Lily Tidhar
              </figcaption>
             </figure>
             <p>
              Read
              <a href="http://www.adobe.com/content/dam/Adobe/en/solutions/digital-media/pdfs/adobe-new-creatives-report.pdf" target="_blank">
               Adobe's full report here
              </a>
              .
             </p>
