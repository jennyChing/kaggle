        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Oreo Sundae Pie
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2014-06-13T05:30:46+00:00">
          June 13, 2014
         </span>
         By
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://bitzngiggles.com/author/saraannzenner@yahoo.com" rel="author">
            Sara
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="post-comments">
          <a href="http://bitzngiggles.com/2014/06/oreo-sundae-pie.html#comments">
           19 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="6639177" data-link="http://bitzngiggles.com/2014/06/oreo-sundae-pie.html" data-summary="" data-title="Oreo Sundae Pie">
         </div>
         <p>
          <!-- BEGIN THEIA POST SLIDER -->
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Sundae-Pie.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-2279">
           <img alt="Oreo Sundae Pie" class="alignnone wp-image-2279" height="900" src="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Sundae-Pie-682x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Did you know that I dont like pie? Let me clarify. I dont like fruit pie. The chunks of fruit just arent my thing. Gag. Id rather just eat the crust with some ice cream and call it good. Crust a la mode. If given the choice between a fruit pie and a Snickers bar on the bottom of a shoe that was lightly stepped on Id take the Snickers.
         </p>
         <p>
          Now, I dont feel that way about ALL pie. Throw in some chocolate, cookies&amp; cream. . .and now were talking. THISOreo Sundae Pie is
          <em>
           my
          </em>
          kind of pie.It boastsan Oreo crust and cookies &amp; cream filling, with chocolate swirled throughout.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie2.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-2277">
           <img alt="Oreo Sundae Pie - Bitz &amp; Giggles" class="wp-image-2277 aligncenter" height="900" src="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie2-682x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Am I serious? Heck yeah, Im serious. I wouldnt kid about deliciousness. I truly believe fruit pies are a bit over-rated. For one thing, they take forever to make. Who wants to sit and peel a million apples? Not me. I dont have time for that. I need quick and I need delicious. Lets be honest, isnt that what everybody wants?
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie1.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-2276">
           <img alt="Oreo Sundae Pie - Bitz &amp; Giggles" class="wp-image-2276 aligncenter" height="900" src="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie1-682x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I suppose fruit pies have their time and their place. Their place just isnt in
          <em>
           my
          </em>
          kitchen. I apologize if Ive offended you. Ill like you whether or not
          <em>
           you
          </em>
          like fruit pies. Just dont make one for
          <em>
           me
          </em>
          when I come over. Deal?
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie3.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-2278">
           <img alt="Oreo Sundae Pie - Bitz &amp; Giggles" class="wp-image-2278 aligncenter" height="900" src="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie3-682x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Ok, Im not that rude. If you went out of your way to make me dessert and it turned out to be a fruit pie, just make sure you have some ice cream on hand so I can choke down a bite or two. Shudder. Gag.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie-4.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-2270">
           <img alt="Oreo Sundae Pie - Bitz &amp; Giggles" class="wp-image-2270 aligncenter" height="900" src="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie-4-682x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          On the other hand, if you invite me over and you make this Oreo Sundae Pie, I may never leave. So, take note, I like really warm showers andcant stand hand towels that smell like mildew. Ick. Have you ever gone over to someones house who had stinky hand towels? Like after you wash your hands, they smell worse than before? Is the problem with their water? Is the washing machine gunked up? Do they even wash their towels?
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie-5.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-2271">
           <img alt="Oreo Sundae Pie - Bitz &amp; Giggles" class="wp-image-2271 aligncenter" height="900" src="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie-5-682x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Ill admit. I do have some issues. But dont we all? I mean, what fun would it be to be perfect? Boring!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie-6.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-2272">
           <img alt="Oreo Sundae Pie - Bitz &amp; Giggles" class="wp-image-2272 aligncenter" height="900" src="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie-6-682x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I really feel like weve gotten toknow one another better after this post. Ok, well, maybe you know more about
          <em>
           me
          </em>
          . I would love to know more about you! What kind of pie is your favorite? Are you a fruit pie lover? Can you relate to my obsession over clean, fresh-smelling hand towels? Do you think Im officially crazy?
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie-7.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-2273">
           <img alt="Oreo Sundae Pie - Bitz &amp; Giggles" class="wp-image-2273 aligncenter" height="900" src="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie-7-682x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I am. But thats why we have fun around here. We look at good pictures of food, share secret confessions and tell crazy stories! Im so glad were friends. We are friends, right? Hold that thought and just make some of this pie already! My entire family gives this recipe a BIG thumbs up!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie-Final.jpg" rel="attachment wp-att-2275">
           <img alt="Oreo Sundae Pie - Bitz &amp; Giggles" class="wp-image-2275 aligncenter" height="900" src="http://cf.bitzngiggles.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Oreo-Pie-Final-682x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>

           <div class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-section" yumprintsection="0">
            <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
             Ingredients
            </div>
            <ol class="blog-yumprint-ingredients">
             <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="0">
              10-12 crushed Oreo cookies
             </li>
             <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="1">
              1 tub  (8 oz.) COOL WHIP Whipped Topping, thawed
             </li>
             <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="2">
              1 pkg.  (3.4 oz.) JELL-O Vanilla Flavor Instant Pudding
             </li>
             <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="3">
              1 packaged Oreo pie crust
             </li>
             <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="4">
              1 cup cold milk
             </li>
             <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="5">
              2 oz.  BAKER'S Semi-Sweet Chocolate, melted
             </li>
             <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="6">
              1/3 cup  sweetened condensed milk
             </li>
             <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="7">
              Extra Oreos, chocolate and caramel syrup for garnish (optional)
             </li>
            </ol>
           </div>
           <div class="blog-yumprint-method-section" yumprintsection="1">
            <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
             Instructions
            </div>
            <ol class="blog-yumprint-methods" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="0">
              Whisk pudding mix and milk in mixing bowl until pudding has thickened.
             </li>
             <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="1">
              Mix in the crushed Oreos.
             </li>
             <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="2">
              Stir in the Cool Whip and spoon into the Oreo crust.
             </li>
             <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="3">
              Mix melted chocolate and condensed milk in a separate bowl; spoon over pie. Swirl gently with knife.
             </li>
             <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="4">
              Freeze 4 hours or until firm. Let pie soften at room temperature for about 10 minutes before serving. Top with extra Oreos and drizzle with chocolate and caramel syrup.
             </li>
            </ol>
