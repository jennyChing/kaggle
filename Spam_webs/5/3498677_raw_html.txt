           <h1 class="entry-title">
            7 Ways to Style Your Classic Crisp White Shirt
           </h1>
           <div class="entry-meta">
            <span class="entry-date">
             <a href="http://www.oasisshirts.com/7-ways-to-style-your-classic-crisp-white-shirt/" rel="bookmark">
              <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-11-11T07:34:44+00:00">
               November 11, 2014
              </time>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="byline">
             <span class="author vcard">
              <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.oasisshirts.com/author/martin/" rel="author">
               Martin
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
            <span class="comments-link">
             <a href="http://www.oasisshirts.com/7-ways-to-style-your-classic-crisp-white-shirt/#respond">
              Leave a comment
             </a>
            </span>
           </div>
          </header>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <p align="justify">
            What, according to you, is the most versatile wear of all? If you ask us, wed undoubtedly go for shirts. No matter whether it is a lazy Sunday afternoon brunch with your friends or a Saturday night party with your colleagues or a meeting at your office, a proper shirt can make you look a million dollars everywhere. And, if you find it difficult to agree with us, heres furnishing you with a few tips on how to wear your basic white shirt, and change them into eye-catchers everywhere
           </p>
           <p align="justify">
            <a class="http://www.oasisshirts.com/product-category/women-shirts/" href="http://www.oasisshirts.com/product-category/women-shirts/" rel="http://www.oasisshirts.com/product-category/women-shirts/" target="_blank">
             <img alt="Crisp White Shirt" class="Crisp White Shirt aligncenter wp-image-1214 size-full" height="300" src="http://www.oasisshirts.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/crispwhiteshirt.jpg?3ea08f" title="Crisp White Shirt" width="677"/>
            </a>
            <br/>
            1. For that special meeting at your office, you may simply wear a full sleeved crisp white shirt and team it with a well tailored skirt or a pair of pants. A pair of tights over a crisp white tucked in shirt topped with an open buttoned leather tailor jacket is also a great option if youre comfortable in pulling yourself stylishly at your office.
           </p>
           <p>
            2. A loose white classic shirt over a pair of tights and suede boots or stilettos are great for that shopping spree with your girl friends.
           </p>
           <p>
            3. If its a proper evening event, you may wear your  sleeved classic black sweater dress over a
            <a href="http://www.oasisshirts.com/shop/crisp-white-shirts/">
             crisp white shirt
            </a>
            and wrap up the ensemble with a sleek but catchy neck piece in silver or gold. Ah! no one can stop the diva in you to play a sneak-peak!
           </p>
           <p>
            4. So, youre wearing a flowy skirt or a grand palazzo for the day out, and are simply clueless about how and what to accentuate it with? Simple; wear a classic white shirt, roll up the sleeves and keep the collar buttons open. And, youre done.
           </p>
           <p>
            5. For those boring college lectures, simply wear a colorful tank top and top it off with an open buttoned, rolled-up sleeved classic white shirt. Wear this with your ripped jeans, and youre simply great to go!
           </p>
           <p>
            6. Team your denim hot shorts with a stylish classic white shirt, and throw it on just casually. Accentuate the look with an aviator and a pair of casual slippers. You, my lady, have just hit a master stroke!
           </p>
           <p>
            7. If youve got a knee length loose fitted classic white shirt, pair it with tan slippers and let a thin tan belt wrap it up over your waist line, and youre good to go! Take a giant tote and a wear mirrored aviators to finish the look!
           </p>
