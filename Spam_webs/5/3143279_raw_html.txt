           <h1 class="entry-title">
            <a href="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/index.php/gerald-the-lazerian-dog-and-christian-montenegro/" title="Gerald the Lazerian dog and Christian Montenegro">
             Gerald the Lazerian dog and Christian Montenegro
            </a>
           </h1>
           <div class="whitespace">
            <div class="date-title">
             <ul class="breadcrumbs">
              <li>
               <a href="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk">

               Gerald the Lazerian dog and Christian Montenegro
              </li>
             </ul>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="right-float">

          <div class="accent">
          </div>
          <div class="post-content">
           <!-- Post content -->
           <div id="fb_share_1" name="fb_share" style="float: right; margin-left: 10px;;width: 55px;">
            <div id="fb-root">
            </div>
            <script src="http://connect.facebook.net/en_US/all.js#appId=125029517579627&amp;xfbml=1">
            </script>
            <fb:like font="arial" href="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/index.php/gerald-the-lazerian-dog-and-christian-montenegro/" layout="button_count" send="false" show_faces="false" width="55">
            </fb:like>
           </div>
           <p>
            We are very happy to have
            <a href="http://www.christianmontenegro.com.ar/" target="_blank">
             Christian Montenegro
            </a>
            on board to customise a Gerald the dog for the exhibition.
            <strong>
            </strong>
            Christian Montenegro is an Argentinean illustrator. First, he studied comics in Alberto Breccias atelier and later Graphic design in the Buenos Aires University.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists7.jpg">
             <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1252" height="390" src="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists7.jpg" title="christian montenegro gerald exhibition artists7" width="390"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span id="more-1237">
            </span>
            Heres a small selection of Christans work.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists6.jpg">
             <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1251" height="390" src="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists6.jpg" title="christian montenegro gerald exhibition artists6" width="390"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Since the year 2002 hes worked with the digital media, mixing concepts  from design with his previous experience in the comics.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists5.jpg">
             <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1250" height="585" src="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists5.jpg" title="christian montenegro gerald exhibition artists5" width="390"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The style could  be compared with a Lego system, simple pieces are combined in a more  complex organization.
            <br/>
            <a href="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists4.jpg">
             <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1249" height="585" src="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists4.jpg" title="christian montenegro gerald exhibition artists4" width="390"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            His works has been published in Argentina and all over the world.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists2.jpg">
             <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1247" height="503" src="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists2.jpg" title="christian montenegro gerald exhibition artists2" width="390"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists1.jpg">
             <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1246" height="570" src="http://blog.lazerian.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/christian-montenegro-gerald-exhibition-artists1.jpg" title="christian montenegro gerald exhibition artists1" width="390"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            We look forward in working with Christian and seeing how he adds his characteristic style to Gerald.
           </p>
