         <p>
          <b>
           Dr. Jemma Simmons
          </b>
          is a bio-chemist who works for
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/S.H.I.E.L.D." title="S.H.I.E.L.D.">
           S.H.I.E.L.D.
          </a>
          undercover with
          <a href="/wiki/HYDRA" title="HYDRA">
           HYDRA
          </a>
          . Shespecializes in life sciences (both human and alien) and is generally the partner of Agent
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Leo_Fitz" title="Leo Fitz">
           Leo Fitz
          </a>
          , an engineer.
         </p>

         <h2>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="Personality">
           Personality
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Jemma is usually highly enthusiastic and has a bright personality. She has a deep passion for science. She is also very accepting and forgiving (examples include forgiving Skye for betraying the team in "
          <a href="/wiki/Girl_in_the_Flower_Dress" title="Girl in the Flower Dress">
           Girl in the Flower Dress
          </a>
          "). Being merciful is another of her traits (examples include not wanting to kill
          <a href="/wiki/Centipede" title="Centipede">
           Centipede
          </a>
          operatives in "
          <a href="/wiki/The_Magical_Place" title="The Magical Place">
           The Magical Place
          </a>
          ".) Jemma can usually be found bickering with her best friend and partner, engineer Leo Fitz, but they are almost always found together. They are so close, Jemma and Fitz are often referred to as Fitz-Simmons, a gestalt entity. Despite Jemma's kind calm nature, she cannot lie. When she tries she always ends up giving herself away, a bad habit. When pressured she panics. She protested in the "
          <a href="/wiki/Pilot" title="Pilot">
           Pilot
          </a>
          " that she is "not Hermione", but, in "
          <a href="/wiki/Seeds" title="Seeds">
           Seeds
          </a>
          ", Leo claims that she's so smart "because she loves homework more than life itself".

          Jemma is stated to be a genius bio-chemist. She is so incredibly talented at her job that she graduated 3 years early and is becoming a star in S.H.I.E.L.D.'s research division. She has more than two PhDs.
          <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-0">
           <a href="#cite_note-0">
            [1]
           </a>
          </sup>
         </p>
         <h2>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">
           Biography
          </span>
         </h2>
         <h3>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="Early_Life">
           Early Life
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          A bio-chem genius with two PhDs, Jemma Simmons attended the S.H.I.E.L.D. training academy with her best friend, engineering genius
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Leo_Fitz" title="Leo Fitz">
           Leo Fitz
          </a>
          . Among their instructors were Professor Vaughn
          <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Agents2_1-0">
           <a href="#cite_note-Agents2-1">
            [2]
           </a>
          </sup>
          and Professor
          <a href="/wiki/Franklin_Hall" title="Franklin Hall">
           Hall
          </a>
          , who taught chemical kinetics during their second year.
          <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Agents3_2-0">
           <a href="#cite_note-Agents3-2">
            [3]
           </a>
          </sup>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Wanting to see more than the inside of a lab, and against the advice of Professor Vaughn, Jemma convinced Leo to seek out a field assignment.
         </p>
         <h3>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="Field_Work">
           Field Work
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
          Jemma and Fitz were recruited by agent
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Phil_Coulson" title="Phil Coulson">
           Phil Coulson
          </a>
          to be the science crew for a new team he was putting together, although it has since been claimed by
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Melinda_May" title="Melinda May">
           May
          </a>
          that she was, in fact, responsible for their inclusion. Stationed on the
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bus" title="Bus">
           Bus
          </a>
          , the pair have designed specialized equipment and compounds on the fly in various emergency situations.
         </p>
         <p>
          Their first foray into the field involved the case of
          <a href="/wiki/Michael_%22Mike%22_Peterson" title='Michael "Mike" Peterson'>
           Mike Peterson
          </a>
          , a man in danger of exploding due to his use of the volatile
          <a href="/wiki/Centipede" title="Centipede">
           Centipede
          </a>
          formula. Peterson was saved in part by Jemma's efforts to create a Centipede counter-agent for Peterson.
          <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Agents1_3-0">
           <a href="#cite_note-Agents1-3">
            [4]
           </a>
          </sup>
         </p>
         <p>
          Jemma first faced danger in the field in
          <a href="/wiki/Peru" title="Peru">
           Peru
          </a>
          , where the team was investigating an
          <a href="/wiki/0-8-4_(code)" title="0-8-4 (code)">
           0-8-4
          </a>
          . She and the team escaped Peruvian rebels only to face an armed takeover of the Bus. Working with the team, she helped take back the Bus and secure the old
          <a class="external text exitstitial" href="http://marvel.com/universe/Hydra" rel="nofollow">
           Hydra
          </a>
          weapon.
          <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Agents2_1-1">
           <a href="#cite_note-Agents2-1">
            [2]
           </a>
          </sup>
         </p>
         <p>
          While investigating a mysterious death in Pennsylvania, Jemma was infected with a fatal alien virus passed along from a
          <a href="/wiki/Chitauri_helmet" title="Chitauri helmet">
           Chitauri helmet
          </a>
          . After what appeared to be a final failed attempt at a cure, Jemma threw herself from the Bus in flight in order save her team from the virus' effect. Fortunately for her, the cure was actually effective and teammate
          <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grant_Ward" title="Grant Ward">
           Grant Ward
          </a>
          caught her in mid-flight, injected her with the antiserum, and parachuted them to safety.
          <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Agents6_4-0">
           <a href="#cite_note-Agents6-4">
            [5]
           </a>
          </sup>
         </p>
         <p>
          When the team stopped at the
          <a href="/wiki/Hub" title="Hub">
           Hub
          </a>
          , Jemma and Fitz were forced to separate when Fitz and Grant went into the field. She was shown to fuss over Fitz a lot before he went, and made him a sandwich.
          <a href="/wiki/Skye" title="Skye">
           Skye
          </a>
          convinced Jemma to help her hack SHIELD, at great risk to Jemma's good-girl reputation. While accessing a wall panel, she was confronted by
          <a href="/wiki/Jasper_Sitwell" title="Jasper Sitwell">
           Sitwell
          </a>
          , a superior officer. After many lame excuses and failed sweet talking, Jemma panicked and used the
          <a href="/wiki/Night-Night_Gun" title="Night-Night Gun">
           Night-Night Gun
          </a>
          to shoot Sitwell in the chest. Coulson managed to get Jemma out of any serious trouble, luckily. The team rescued Fitz and Grant with perfect timing, and Fitzsimmons were very happy to be reunited. After Fitz bragged about how he had Grant's back, Jemma excitedly told him about how she had shot a superior officer in the chest, only to see his face fall.
         </p>
         <p>
          In the begining of
          <a href="/wiki/The_Well" title="The Well">
           The Well
          </a>
          , Jemma and the others are cleaning up after the events of the latest Thor movie. She ducks a call from her parents because she's been yet to tell them the events of
          <a href="/wiki/FZZT" title="FZZT">
           FZZT
          </a>
          . A bit later, when the team goes to investigate a chopped down tree, Ward helps Jemma scale the tree, because she still has a fear of falling to certain death.
         </p>
         <p>
          As of "
          <a href="/wiki/Providence" title="Providence">
           Providence
          </a>
          ", Simmons appears to have the least faith of any of the original members in her colleagues, showing any real degree of trust only in Fitz.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <span class="mw-headline" id="Season_2">
           Season 2
          </span>
         </h4>
         <p>
          In "
          <a href="/wiki/Shadows" title="Shadows">
           Shadows
          </a>
          " Coulson reveals that she has left the team, at least in part in the hope that forcing Fitz to recover without her will speed the process. It has, unfortunately, had the opposite effect, and her former partner is hallucinating her continuing presence. Despite appearing to have quit the team, she in fact was sent by Coulson to infiltrate HYDRA.
         </p>
