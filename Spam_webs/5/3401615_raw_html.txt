         <h4 class="inline">
          Trivia:
         </h4>
         The title of his autobiography, "Bring on the Empty Horses", is taken from a command given by director
         <a href="/name/nm0002031?ref_=nm_dyk_trv1">
          Michael Curtiz
         </a>
         during the filming of
         <a href="/title/tt0027438?ref_=nm_dyk_trv2">
          The Charge of the Light Brigade
         </a>
         (1936) in which Niven was an extra. Curtiz, notorious for his poor command of English, wanted a lot of riderless horses in the background of the climactic charge.
