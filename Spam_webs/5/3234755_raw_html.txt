        Advice from a White Girl in Jeans
Holy shit, y'all. I had my birthday this week and it got me thinking about all kinds of shit: life goals, space travel, gluten and DATING. Specifically, dating age-appropriately, a hot topic amongst all kinds of
        <a href="http://www.psychologytoday.com/blog/head-games/201308/when-it-comes-dating-do-age-differences-matter">
         scientists and also my mom.
        </a>
        Dating around my age is something I strive for but you can bet your FACES I've dabbled in some age-inappropriateness (it's called COLLEGE, and I got an EDUCATION). However, my teenage ass dating 30-year-old creeps is a story for a different column. For now, let's field some AGE APPROPRIATE DATING QUESTIONS.
        <strong>
         I'm 22. What should my age range be on my OKCupid Profile?
        </strong>
        Hey sexy 22-year-old, here's a tip: adhere to the "dating age range formula," for this kind of thing, especially at your tender age. I don't know where this handy rule came from, but it's been pretty good to me thus far. Any dum-dum can do the math: take your current age, subtract 7, and multiply it by 2. So, 30 is as ickily old as you'll want to date. The low range is easy, because dating under 18 is illegal. NO MATH NEEDED, CREEP-O.
        <strong>
         What's the biggest difference between dating in your teens, twenties and thirties?
        </strong>
        WOAH WOAH WOAH I'm so young it's hard to speak about this, but I'll DO MY BEST, for YOU, dear readers.
In your teens, you are figuring shit out in the most basic, rudimentary way. What you like, what YouPorn channel's your thing, etc. etc. etc. You're finding your footing in a lot of ways, but mostly it's just...not being completely fucking mortified by everything you do, say and are. It's the very beginnings of dating, knowing who you are, and expressing yourself, but primarily managing your boner. You know, teen stuff.
In your twenties, you've established what you like and you're trying to explore it, amongst people that you don't completely hate and copious amounts of boozedrugs. It's still all about self-discovery, but hopefully you've shaved that soul patch and have learned not to choke on your retainer while taking a beer bong. You got this, bro.
In your 30's, you're kinda over some of the bullshit that you got off on in your 20's, e.g. dating hot psychos, passive aggressive texting, and pleasing others a ton in order to have sex/feel acceptance/have interesting blog content. You're doing YOU, and are trying to find people who compliment your own weirdness. I think? Who knows, I'm only JUST 18! (Weeps).
        <strong>
         I just bought a Groupon for a hot air balloon ride. This isn't a question, just something cool that I did.
        </strong>
        Awesome. Keep up the good work!
