      <p>
       The folks at
       <a href="http://goo.gl/19mRGe" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://goo.gl/19mRGe', 'Veggie Grill']);" target="_blank">
        Veggie Grill
       </a>
       have a new Spring Menu and they invited me out to one of their Los Angeles locations to give it a try.It didnt take much convincing to get me to spend an afternoon trying out their new recipes.Ive been a fan of their food ever since I tasted their buffalo wings, and later found out the entire restaurant is a meat-free zone.You know you found a winner when the wings youre eating arent chicken, and yet, are some of thebest youve ever tasted.
      </p>
      <p>
       Veggie Grills new Spring Menu adds five new dishes, coveringeverything from appetizer to dessert; asparagus to rhubarb.Seewhat their chefs cooked up below:
      </p>
      <h2>
       Fresh Pea + Asparagus Soup
      </h2>
      <p>
       Made from scratch using fresh-market peas, asparagus, chopped mint, and cracked pepper.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="http://goo.gl/19mRGe" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://goo.gl/19mRGe', '']);" target="_blank">
        <img alt="Veggie Grill Spring LTO Menu - Fresh Pea + Asparagus Soup" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-37721" height="425" src="http://www.joesdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Spring-LTO-fresh-pea-and-asparagus-soup.jpg" width="525"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Bnh Mi Salad
      </h2>
      <p>
       Glazed organic tofu, chargrilled eggplant, house-pickled carrots, daikon, white cabbage, fresh jalapos, basil, cilantro, and marinated kale.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="http://goo.gl/19mRGe" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://goo.gl/19mRGe', '']);" target="_blank">
        <img alt="Banh Mi Salad - Veggie Grill Spring LTO Menu" class="alignnone wp-image-37723" height="565" src="http://www.joesdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Spring-LTO-banh-mi-salad.jpg" width="620"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Spice Market Bowl
      </h2>
      <p>
       Grilled tofu seasoned with Moroccan-spices, sauted asparagus, sweet peppers and leeks, roasted corn, farro, swiss chard, beets, golden raisins, and preserved lemon vinaigrette.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://goo.gl/19mRGe" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://goo.gl/19mRGe', '']);" target="_blank">
        <img alt="Spice Market Bowl - Veggie Grill Spring LTO Menu" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-37724 aligncenter" height="466" src="http://www.joesdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Spring-LTO-spice-market-bowl-.jpg" width="560"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Chargrilled Asparagus
      </h2>
      <p>
       Seasoned with sea salt and drizzled preserved lemon vinaigrette.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="http://goo.gl/19mRGe" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://goo.gl/19mRGe', '']);" target="_blank">
        <img alt="Chargrilled Asparagus - Veggie Grill Spring LTO Menu" class="alignnone wp-image-37725" height="608" src="http://www.joesdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Spring-LTO-chargrilled-asparagus-.jpg" width="620"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Strawberry + Rhubarb Parfait
      </h2>
      <p>
       Crushed ginger snaps mixed in a parfait of fresh starberry and rhubarb compote, and strawberry crema.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="http://goo.gl/19mRGe" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://goo.gl/19mRGe', '']);" target="_blank">
        <img alt="Strawberry and Rhubarb Parfait - Veggie Grill Spring LTO Menu" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-37726" height="426" src="http://www.joesdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Spring-LTO-strawberry-rhubarb-parfait-.jpg" width="455"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       I brought my friend Lander along to help try out a few of the new menu items. We ordered theBnh Mi Salad, Spice Market Bowl, and the Strawberry + Rhubarb Parfait, along with a few Veggie Grill favorites Ive had before. You MUSTtrytheir crispy cauliflower and buffalo wings. Each dish was as good as they sound, but my personal favorite had to be the Bnh Mi Salad. The glazed organic tofuwent perfectly on top the bed of marinated kale. Delicious!
      </p>
      <p>
       Even though Veggie Grills menu is all about veggies, grains and nuts,that doesnt stop them from bringing vegetables to the center of your plate in a tasteful, yet healthy fashion. You dont have to be seeking an uber-healthy mealto enjoy your trip to VG.Their new spring menu, and spring menus past, continue to impress even the most skeptical vegetable eaters. I for one fancy myself as a hamburgerconnoisseur, but leave that title at the door, as well as my guilt.
      </p>
      <p>
       Visit
       <a href="http://goo.gl/19mRGe" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://goo.gl/19mRGe', 'VeggieGrill.com']);" target="_blank">
        VeggieGrill.com
       </a>
       to find nutritional facts and their entire menu.
      </p>
