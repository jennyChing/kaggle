        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Asian Cole Slaw Recipe
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2014-06-16T00:01:40+00:00">
          June 16, 2014
         </span>
         <span class="categories">
          Filed Under:
          <a href="http://lilluna.com/category/recipes/" rel="category tag">
           Recipes
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://lilluna.com/category/recipes/salads/" rel="category tag">
           Salads
          </a>
         </span>
         by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://lilluna.com/author/kristynm/" rel="author">
            Lil' Luna
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="post-comments">
          <a href="http://lilluna.com/asian-cole-slaw-recipe/#comments">
           10 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="14173655" data-link="http://lilluna.com/asian-cole-slaw-recipe/" data-summary="" data-title="Asian Cole Slaw Recipe">
         </div>
         <p>
          Today, I want to share with you an AMAZING recipe that is one we love to make this time of year  Asian Cole Slaw. It is super easy to throw together and is one of the side dishes/salads that is perfect for holidays or get togethers. It takes Cole Slaw to a whole new level and is a great summer time recipe to try out.
         </p>
         <p>
          Everyone who has had it loves it, and I think youll like it too!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://lilluna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/asian-cole-slaw-1.jpg">
           <img alt="A delicious Asian version of Cole Slaw - so simple and perfect for the summer! { lilluna.com }" class="aligncenter wp-image-49273 size-full" height="900" src="http://lilluna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/asian-cole-slaw-1.jpg" width="643"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <center>
         </center>
         If youve ever had salads with ramen noodle bits in it, then you know they add a crunchy and delicious touch to the dish. This cole slaw is no different and will be a hit at your next party!!
         <p>
          Heres theAsian Cole Slaw recipe:
          <br/>
         </p>
         <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
          <link href="http://lilluna.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/asian-cole-slaw-1.jpg" itemprop="image"/>
          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://lilluna.com/easyrecipe-print/45901-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Print
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
           Asian Coleslaw Recipe
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSDetails">
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
           <div class="ERSTimes">
           </div>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSIngredients">
           <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
            Ingredients
           </div>
           <ul>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 Ramen Noodle packages (without seasoning packets)
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup butter
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup slivered almonds
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup sunflower seeds
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             11/2cups cooked chicken (cubed)
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup rice vinegar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup olive oil
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 TB soy sauce
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 TB mustard (optional)
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 package of cole slaw
            </li>
           </ul>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSInstructions">
           <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
            Instructions
           </div>
           <ol>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Break up Ramen Noodles into small pieces and place in a pan. Add  cup butter, almonds, and sunflower seeds and cook for 8-10 minutes on low-medium heat.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In a large bowl, add coleslaw, ramen noodle mixture and chicken.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Make dressing by combining rice vinegar, sugar, olive oil, soy sauce, and mustard in a small bowl.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Add dressing and mix well. Serve coleslaw chilled.
            </li>
           </ol>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
          </div>
