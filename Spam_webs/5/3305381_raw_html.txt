        <p>
         While commuting to work everyday, this bride-to-be crocheted her own lace wedding dress on the bus for about 5 months. She designed her own dress pattern from a commonly used pineapple stitch and the materials she used cost a mere $30. The amazing DIY project is also completely washable and re-usable, and
         <a href="http://www.reddit.com/user/alkikat" target="_blank">
          Reddit user alkikat
         </a>
         says she is wearing it again with linings of different colors and with the train bustled up in different ways.
        </p>
