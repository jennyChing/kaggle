        Most people who are not aware of mobile application development feel that is a piece of cake to make mobile applications. In fact, developing mobile applications can be as difficult as developing computer applications. In simple terms, mobile application development is all about developing software applications for mobile phones that run on operating systems such as Android, Symbian, iOS and so on. You will find many mobile applications on the internet, but they may be for generic purposes. You may or may not find any use for them.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Although there are commercial mobile applications for the general public, you will find that mobile applications are also developed for industrial and medical purposes. Most people feel that there would be very app development companies that would cater to the industrial and medical fields. Well, that is not the case at all. In fact, the number of app development companies for all industries is growing leaps and bounds. The mobile app market is considered to be one of the fastest growing markets in the world. In fact, the mobile app market has surpassed the social networking and the internet boom of the 1990s.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Smartphones dominate the consumer and commercial mobile markets around the world. The distribution of mobile applications is mainly controlled by big companies such as Apple, Google, Amazon, Blackberry, and Microsoft. Apple has its iTunes App Store, Google has its Google Play, Amazon has Amazon Mobile, Blackberry has AppWorld, and Microsoft has Microsoft App Store.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Among these, you will find that android apps development has taken the lead in most countries. Mobile app development for commercial and consumer market entails creating mobile applications that target a specific platform. Going by this logic, if you want a mobile application for your iPhones, iPads, or iPods, then you will need the iOS operating system platform along with its Cocoa Touch framework and Objective-C programming.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        On the other hand, if you want to develop mobile applications for Android smart phones, you will need app developers who can create android applications using Android SDK or NDK. Similarly, if you want mobile applications for Windows smartphones, you will need an app developer who works on Microsoft .Net framework for mobile.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        For Blackberry apps development, you will have to check out the app developers who are experts in native C or Java SDKs. The consumer market for mobile applications is mainly dominated by the games applications. There are other productivity apps that are also in great demand in the consumer market. The mobile applications that are used for the consumer market are generally standalone mobile applications and are used on a single smartphone.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Standalone mobile applications are considered to be simple, but the development effort for such applications is not less. The standalone mobile applications do not interface with back-end systems like ERPs or database servers. Hence, these mobile applications don't have much of dependency on external data.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        If you want to get an app developed for your mobile phone, you will have to first check out the online market. You can get lucky if they are freely available on the internet.
