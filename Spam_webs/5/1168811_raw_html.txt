         Contact Centers serve as the primary link between a business and their customers acting as a customer service facility that coordinates all inbound and outbound communication between an organization and the public. Having the perfect solution for your business is a need in todays fast paced market, but often businesses simply dont know what best for their business. The infographic below outlines the many differences found when comparing Cloud and Premise based contact centers.
