        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         Kt Tunstall Lights Up Pixie Hollow
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://smslwithheidi.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/unnamed1.png">
          <img alt="Kt Tunstall Lights Up Pixie Hollow" class="size-full wp-image-127708 aligncenter" height="104" src="http://smslwithheidi.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/unnamed1.png" width="267"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <!-- Please call pinit.js only once per page -->
         <br/>
         <script async="" data-pin-hover="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/js/pinit.js" type="text/javascript">
         </script>
         <br/>
         Kt Tunstall Lights Up Pixie Hollow on March 3rd 2015 ON BLU-RAY
         <br/>
         Singer/songwriter/musician KT Tunstall heads to Pixie Hollow to help bring animal fairy Fawns story to life in Tinker Bell and the Legend of the NeverBeast. Tunstall performs three new songs in the film, including Float, Strange Sight and 1000 Years.
         <br/>
         KT is a celebrated songwriter and musician, says director Steve Loter. She reminds me of Fawn in a way with her big heart and this amazing voice that has a little bit of edge and a tremendous amount of appeal.
        </p>
        <p>
         The all-new Tinker Bell film is about the relationship that develops between Fawn, a compassionate animal fairy who thinks with her heart, and Gruff, a massive beast with glowing green eyes who seems to be on a mission. Its a real action-adventure movie, says Tunstall. The characters are so cool. I was over the moon to take part. Disney Animation has such an incredible heritage of work, spanning decades, and its really special to be part of that.
         <br/>
         Tunstall had fun channeling the films heroine. Float is Fawns opening theme, she says. Its fantastic how she spends her days looking after all the animals around Pixie Hollow.
        </p>
        <p>
         The song says, Ill just float. She doesnt have to have a reason, she just really loves having a pair of wings, so shes going out for a fly, getting into trouble and mischief.
         <br/>
         The artist also helped introduce the NeverBeast. Strange Sight is heard when Fawn discovers this beast living in the woods near Pixie Hollow, says Tunstall. Hes a huge, scary-looking guy, but she isnt afraid. She wants to help him and make friends with him, but cant work out what he is or what hes doing with the rocks hes collecting.
         <br/>
         The third song in Tunstalls lineup, which she sings with singer/songwriter Bleu, is an emotional song that can be heard during the films end credits.
        </p>
