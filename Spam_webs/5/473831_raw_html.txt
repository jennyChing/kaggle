         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Cascade Microtech in the News
         </h1>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.oregonlive.com/silicon-forest/index.ssf/2014/09/cascade_microtech_brings_human.html">
            <img alt="Cascade Microtech brings human touch to electronics production" class="alignright size-full wp-image-2723" height="233" src="http://www.cascademicrotech.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/production.jpg" width="350"/>
           </a>
           If you happen to read The Oregonian or visit Oregon Live, you may have seen a recent story featuring Cascade Microtech. The piece, titled
           <a href="http://www.oregonlive.com/silicon-forest/index.ssf/2014/09/cascade_microtech_brings_human.html" target="_blank" title="Cascade Microtech brings human touch to electronics production">
            <strong>
             Cascade Microtech brings human touch to electronics production
            </strong>
           </a>
           , paints a nice picture of who we are and how weve grown over the last 10 years.
          </p>
          <p>
           As the article points out:
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <em>
            Thirty years after its founding, and 10 years after its initial public offering, Cascade Microtech is finally thriving as the electronics industry races toward wireless technology, fueling demand for the Beaverton companys tools.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <em>
            Revenues hit a record $120 million last year and were up 16 percent through the first half of this year. Net income doubled last year, topping $13 million and the company boasts a gross profit margin north of 50 percent.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           Since 2010, weve been able to maintain strong growth in part because of a strong team and in part because of the position of the company relative to emerging technologies. Our specialization in
           <a href="http://cascademicrotech.com/products/probes/rf-microwave/rf-microwave-probes" target="_blank" title="RF Microwave Probes">
            <strong>
             radio frequency (RF) technology
            </strong>
           </a>
           and the growth of mobile technologies, combined with new trends such as the Internet of Things (IoT) has positioned us nicely for growth.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <em>
            Cascades success relies on human touch and customization, and a stroll through the factory floor of its Beaverton headquarters finds employees at lab benches, peering through microscopes refining wafer probe tips. Across the hall, workers are customizing a million-dollar probing system to meet a clients specifications. In a separate clean room, Cascade Microtech has invented its own process for creating microscopic probe tips for clients working on especially tiny products.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           With a strong investment in R&amp;D spending, we look forward to sharing new products and solutions to meet customer needs, and you can bet well be blogging about those developments in the months and years ahead, so come back often!
          </p>
