        <p>
         Whoa  this is a beautiful town house! Found at
         <a class="external" href="http://www.struttandparker.com/residential/featured-properties/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.struttandparker.com']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Strutt and Parker">
          Strutt and Parker
         </a>
