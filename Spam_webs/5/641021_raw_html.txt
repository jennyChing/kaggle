         </a>
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <ul class="meta-list">
         <li class="meta-cat">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11919" height="338" src="http://www.wiretotheear.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Elgam.jpg" title="Elgam" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="338" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/zBGouZ45_u4" width="600">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="450" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/vDd9sSOIRw4" width="600">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         I just set up an eBay alert for an
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vDd9sSOIRw4" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vDd9sSOIRw4', 'Elgam Carousel']);">
          Elgam Carousel
         </a>
         . You could really call this a vintage groovebox. It would go nicely with my Omnichord! Pretty machine.
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          The CAROUSEL is an interesting analog preset rhythmbox with accompaniment made by Elgam, Italy. It has rhythm (like the Elgam Rhythm Match series), bass, chord and 3 arpeggiators! Rare, obscure and very interesting! 
          <a href="http://www.youtube.com/user/AnalogAudio1?feature=watch" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.youtube.com/user/AnalogAudio1?feature=watch', 'AnalogAudio1']);">
           AnalogAudio1
          </a>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         For more info:
         <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Groovebox" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Groovebox', 'wikipedia.org/wiki/Groovebox']);">
          wikipedia.org/wiki/Groovebox
         </a>
        </p>
