          What is? Balk Ekmek
         </h1>

         <div class="box success">
          <em>
           Balk ekmek,
          </em>
          or fish in bread, is a grilled fish fillet sandwich often served as a street food in Istanbul.
         </div>
         <p>
          The perfect meal. Two fatpiecesof fresh, crunchy on the outside chewy on the inside, with a freshly grilled fish fillet inside. Onions, lettuce, maybe a small leaf of parsley for garnish. Lemon juice and salt as a sauce. Optional pickles called
          <a href="http://sirkecirestaurants.com/what-is-tursu-pickles-sirkeci-restaurants-istanbul/" title="What is? Turu (pickles)">
           turu
          </a>
          (soldseparately.)
         </p>
         <p>
          Available throughout Istanbul, and usually ranging in price from about 4tl from a street seller to 10tl in a restaurant, a fishsandwichnever disappoints. Find street sellers next to any ferry station or in Sirkeci to the left of the Galata Bridge there is a row of
          <a href="http://sirkecirestaurants.com/balik-ekmek-half-a-century-old-historical-flavor/" title="BALIK EKMEK: Half A Century Old Historical Flavor">
           boats
          </a>
          grilling and selling fresh fish breads all day every day.
         </p>
