       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Enroll our son for the youth hockey
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.sportyjess.com/enroll-our-son-for-the-youth-hockey/" rel="bookmark" title="7:38 pm">

          February 26, 2015
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Finally my husband decided to enroll our son to a
         <strong>
          youth hockey
         </strong>
         for the spring through summer. He loves to play hockey but only by our pond. My husband thinks that our son has an amazing leg reflex. My son loves to play as a goalie.
        </p>
        <p>
         We were very excited for him. We cannot wait to see him practice and play with other kids. I remind him to stay humble and avoid full of himself. We paid $250 for 14 practices and ten games. The practice starts around April and will end in August. All the practices will start every Saturday from nine in the morning to 12 in the afternoon.
        </p>
        <p>
         The arena is new to our city. It is only eight minutes drive from where we live. We have not been there. We will take a ride to check the new skating rink. There is also available ice skating for the public.
        </p>
        <p>
         We are so happy and excited for our son. This will motivatehim to do well in school.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
