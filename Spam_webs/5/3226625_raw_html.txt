           <div class="title_fader">
           </div>
           <div class="edit" id="43617">
            <a href="/collections/the-beginning-1">
             The Beginning
            </a>
           </div>
          </h1>
          <p class="collection_type" id="collection_type">
           Portfolio by
           <a class="nru" href="/yuliakopr" onmouseover="display_user_preview(this, 'http://s3.amazonaws.com/glossom-main/avatars/0004/6041/avatar_small_1422019063.75.jpg', 'Yulia Kopr', 'Graphic Designer, Photographer', 'Vienna - Austria', 'collection_sidebar','')">
            Yulia Kopr
           </a>
          </p>
          <p class="created_on">
           Created on 26/07/14
          </p>
         </div>
         <div style="clear:left">
         </div>

        <div class="item_description collection_description">
         <p class="sidebar_section_title">
          Collection Description
         </p>
         <div class="edit-desc" id="43617">
          <pre>
           A series of ten works under the theme of human passions. Passion here as affect paralyzes the human mind and conformable it to a single idea. Through the symbolic act of death I wanted to show how a man, absorbed by his passion, loses connection with reality and with life itself. The name The Beginning" of this concept is taken as a symbol of human capabilities. This is the point, which can be located at any moment of time and space. Death, life, the beginning and the end - the multifaceted subjective conceptions for each person. Why death cant be the beginning?
          </pre>
