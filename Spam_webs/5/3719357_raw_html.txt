       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Videos Can Be Used To Market Your Business
       </h1>
      </header>
      <!-- .entry-header -->
      <div class="entry-content">
       <div style="float: left; padding: 10px;">
        <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/toprankblog/">
         <img border="0" src="http://c2.staticflickr.com/6/5219/5473593476_9924f0ef6c_n.jpg" style="max-width:325px;"/>
        </a>
       </div>
       <p>
        The article that follows contains creative advice about video marketing that can take your business.
       </p>
       <p>
        Do not be intimidated by video marketing scare you. A tripod and a good quality camera and mount are all you need in order to make your own videos.
       </p>
       <p>
        Video marketing allows you to stay in contact with your targeted customer base.
       </p>
       <p>
        If you want to use YouTube for your videos you need to use the editing they have avaiable. You can add comments and text in your video. Use this feature to add some information, share a coupon code or a link to your site.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div style="padding: 5px 5px 5px 5px; border-style: dashed none dashed none; border-width: 1px;">
        <div style="background-color: #FFFFCC; color: #000000;">
         <small>
          <font color="#FF0000">
           <b>
            TIP!
           </b>
          </font>
          They wont necessarily come just because you build it. You still have to promote your videos.
         </small>
        </div>
       </div>
       <p>
        Dont think that customers will watch a 20 minute video. You can go that long only if you are showing your audience how a very in-depth how-to video. If youre just updating folks, stay under 10 minutes.
       </p>
       <p>
        Video marketing doesnt give you a short time to capture attention. The first 20 seconds of your video are crucial. You should put a small tease in for the duration of the video so that your customers stay interested.
       </p>
       <p>
        Remember that coming up with your video is just a part of the battle. You have to promote your videos. By promoting your videos, you can experience much more traffic. You can produce great content, but people must know it is there!
       </p>
       <p>
        An excellent method of creating video content is working collaboratively with others. You dont need coworkers. You could use friends, family or your organizations. Give your team members their few minutes of fame by listing their names in the credits at the end of the video.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div style="padding: 5px 5px 5px 5px; border-style: dashed none dashed none; border-width: 1px;">
        <div style="background-color: #FFFFCC; color: #000000;">
         <small>
          <font color="#FF0000">
           <b>
            TIP!
           </b>
          </font>
          Of all web searches, directions on how you do something rank highest. Having tutorial videos creates an avenue for those who search in your niche to find you.
         </small>
        </div>
       </div>
       <p>
        Make sure your videos remain on-topic and on topic.It is easy to go off topic when you are not prepared properly. Try making a simple outline of what you want your video. You will have greater success with video marketing efforts if you keep to the topic at hand.
       </p>
       <p>
        Use a consistent tone in each of your videos. You could choose to be very serious and create educational videos or you could make fun of yourself. You must be considering the product and your target audience.
       </p>
       <p>
        You can talk with an expert or simply document your experience at this event. If you are offering a speech, dont forget to ask someone to record that for you.
       </p>
       <p>
        If customers struggle to use your product in a certain way, create a video to show them how to get the job done. You will be solving the problem for those that have asked about it and for people that are going to be using it in the future as well.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <div style="padding: 5px 5px 5px 5px; border-style: dashed none dashed none; border-width: 1px;">
        <div style="background-color: #FFFFCC; color: #000000;">
         <small>
          <font color="#FF0000">
           <b>
            TIP!
           </b>
          </font>
          Dont forget to add a form to opt into your emailing list on the sites page where the videos are. If viewers want to learn more about the videos topic, they can sign up.
         </small>
        </div>
       </div>
       <p>
        Video content analytics are a must-have to get the most from your marketing videos. Youll learn about whos watching your video and where they come from. You can use all of this information to your advantage to promote your business to new outlets.
       </p>
       <p>
        There are a lot of things video marketing can do for you and your business. Gaining an online presence and reputation are just a few ways. Get your plan on paper today so you can move forward on your launch. This will provide you with great rewards.
       </p>
      </div>
