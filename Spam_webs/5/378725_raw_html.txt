        <p>
         <strong>
          Yes, you read that right. You want to be a male cheerleader and you never even knew it. Dont believe us? Well, let us enlighten you.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you like the idea of getting hench by tossing beautiful young women in the air and catching them by their buttocks, then male cheerleading might just be for you.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-3968">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Heres a little video demonstration of whats involved:
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="383" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/kdsioXA9rAQ" width="680">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         That was Daytona Stuntfest and those guys and gals are the best of the best US college cheerleaders. Every year, once the Nationals competition is over, the cheerleaders unwind on Daytona beach with some freestyle stunting.
        </p>
        <p>
         The guy and girl teams usually dont know each other. Stuntfest is about finding a new partner and hooking up for some stunt speed dating. Pretty awesome, huh?
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Benefits
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3970" style="width: 690px">
         <a href="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bigstock-Closeup-rear-view-midsection-o-48931370.jpg">
          <img alt="Close up rear female cheerleaders" class="size-full wp-image-3970" data-lazy-src="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bigstock-Closeup-rear-view-midsection-o-48931370.jpg" height="453" src="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="680"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Close up rear female cheerleaders" class="size-full wp-image-3970" height="453" src="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bigstock-Closeup-rear-view-midsection-o-48931370.jpg" width="680"/>

        </p>
        <p>
         	Youll learn how to throw gorgeous women 30ft in the air and catch them safely.
         <br/>
         	It will get you in seriously good shape.
         <br/>
         	Youll be able to perform some pretty cool stunts.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Training
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3972" style="width: 690px">
         <a href="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bigstock-Group-of-cheerleaders-26248631.jpg">
          <img alt="Group of cheerleaders" class="size-full wp-image-3972" data-lazy-src="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bigstock-Group-of-cheerleaders-26248631.jpg" height="458" src="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="680"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Group of cheerleaders" class="size-full wp-image-3972" height="458" src="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bigstock-Group-of-cheerleaders-26248631.jpg" width="680"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Image source:
          <a href="http://www.bigstockphoto.com/image-26248631/stock-photo-group-of-cheerleaders" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bigstockphoto.com/image-26248631/stock-photo-group-of-cheerleaders', 'PDB1']);">
           PDB1
          </a>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Got a spare 20 hours a week to train?
          </strong>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Male elite cheerleaders spend around 20 hours a week training. Coed stunting  the gawp-worthy trickery featured in the Stuntfest clip  requires strength and endurance training.
        </p>
        <p>
         Cardio training like running or skipping will give you the endurance you need to get through those high intensity routines.
        </p>
        <p>
         To get the upper body strength youll need to lift your flyer (the girl youre throwing and catching), do chin-ups, bench presses and push-ups. And for strong legs, lunges, squats and leg presses are great.
        </p>
        <p>
         Healthy eating, early gym sessions and the right
         <a href="http://www.supplementcentre.com/">
          supplements
         </a>
         will get you in male cheerleader shape.
         <a href="http://buzz.bournemouth.ac.uk/male-cheerleaders/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://buzz.bournemouth.ac.uk/male-cheerleaders/', 'Dan Wake, Portsmouth Phoenix cheerleader, says']);">
          Dan Wake, Portsmouth Phoenix cheerleader, says
         </a>
         : I like to link in cheer with my weightlifting. The benefit I get from being stronger from the weight makes it easier for me to lift girls  they just help each other.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Get started
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3971" style="width: 690px">
         <a href="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bigstock-Two-strong-male-hands-holding-18842531.jpg">
          <img alt="Get Started" class="size-full wp-image-3971" data-lazy-src="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bigstock-Two-strong-male-hands-holding-18842531.jpg" height="338" src="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" width="680"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Get Started" class="size-full wp-image-3971" height="338" src="http://blog.supplementcentre.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bigstock-Two-strong-male-hands-holding-18842531.jpg" width="680"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Image source:
          <a href="http://www.bigstockphoto.com/image-18842531/stock-photo-two-strong-male-hands-holding-one-female-foot-teamwork%2C-support%2C-competition-concept" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bigstockphoto.com/image-18842531/stock-photo-two-strong-male-hands-holding-one-female-foot-teamwork%2C-support%2C-competition-concept', 'Alex Max']);">
           Alex Max
          </a>
          <br/>

           Find our where to learn the ropes.
          </strong>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Most coed stunts rely on the strength and ability of just one male cheerleader. If youre unprepared or not strong enough, your flyer is likely to fall.
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre going to be a male cheerleader you must be on top of your game in terms of fitness and skill. So it goes without saying that you need to learn from an experienced, certified coach.
        </p>
        <p>
         Most universities in the UK have at least one cheerleading squad. There are also hundreds of cheerleading teams throughout the UK. Youll need to join a seniors team and work your way up the skills and fitness ladder before youre at full-gorgeous-women-tossing level.
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out the
         <a href="https://www.cheerleading.org.uk/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.cheerleading.org.uk/', 'British Cheerleading Association']);">
