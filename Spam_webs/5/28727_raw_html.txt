        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <img alt="mockupsMobile-iPhone-Android" class="alignright wp-image-54897" height="394" src="http://www.3cx.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/mockupsMobile-iPhone-Android.png" width="300"/>
         </strong>
         3CXPhone is a VoIP phone client for
         <a href="http://www.3cx.com/phone-system/windows-mac-softphone/">
          Windows, Mac
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.3cx.com/phone-system/android-ios-voip">
          Android and iOS
         </a>
         . Its a softphone that is integrated in 3CX Phone System and by connecting it toa
         <a href="http://www.3cx.com/voip/ip-phone-help/voip-provider/" title="VoIP Providers">
          VoIP Provider
         </a>
         users can make calls to any mobile or landline number.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The VoIP phone clientcan be installed on laptops, PCs, smartphones and tablets boosting yourcompanys productivity and mobility while at the same time slashing your
         <a href="http://www.3cx.com/phone-system/cut-costs-ip-telephony/">
          telecommunications costs
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Installing 3CXPhone is easy and only takes a few minutes. There is no licensing fee and you are not tied to a particular VoIP provider.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          3CXPhone Features
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          Available for Android, iOS,Windows and Mac
         </li>
         <li>
          Several popular phone interfaces to choose from
         </li>
         <li>
          Automatic provisioningofsettings
         </li>
         <li>
          Blind Transfers
         </li>
         <li>
          Record calls
         </li>
         <li>
          Shows personal call log/history
         </li>
         <li>
          Supports G.711, GSM and Speex codecs
         </li>
         <li>
          Supports standard USB and JABRA headsets (Windows)
         </li>
         <li>
          Works with popular
          <a href="http://www.3cx.com/pbx/sip-trunking/">
           VoIP Providers/SIP Trunks
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
