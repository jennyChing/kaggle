      <h1>
       the fashion
      </h1>
      <p>
       In the autumn of 2008 I resolved to never again sacrifice my morals for the sake of fashion  and to never sacrifice looking great, for anything.  Really, why sacrifice at all?!   Fashion is fun!  Clothing is expressive, artistic, intellectual, and always couples with cultural revolutions.  Use the lists and links below to learn how to
       <b>
        be an ethical sartorialist  and look impeccable doing it!
       </b>
