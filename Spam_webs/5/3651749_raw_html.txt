            <p>
             With the cost of living beginning to climb, everyone should make an effort to save money when possible. By making a few practical changes, your monthly energy bill can be dramatically lowered.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.thewomentalk.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/5-Simple-Ways-to-Reduce-Energy-Costs1.jpg">
              <img alt="5 Simple Ways to Reduce Energy Costs" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5543 nob" height="422" src="http://www.thewomentalk.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/5-Simple-Ways-to-Reduce-Energy-Costs1.jpg" width="550"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Here are five simple ways to reduce energy costs.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Install motion sensors
            </h2>
            <p>
             In an effort to enhance their safety at night, some homeowners choose to turn on the porch light. Unfortunately, the light can consume a great deal of electricity when left on for long periods of time.
            </p>
            <p>
             The most efficient approach is to install a motion detector on all of the outdoor light fixtures. Motion sensors are also great for use in garages and storage rooms.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Programmable thermostat
            </h2>
            <p>
             A programmable thermostat could possibly save you over $200 a year. Unlike a traditional thermostat, a programmable thermostat can be programmed to automatically adjust the temperature at different times during the day. This is a big advantage to the people who are away from home for a significant portion of the day.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Use the ceiling fan effectively
            </h2>
            <p>
             Contrary to popular belief, a ceiling fan can be effectively used throughout the year. However, the homeowner must remember to adjust the direction of the fan according to the current season.
            </p>
            <p>
             Allowing the ceiling fan to rotate in a clockwise direction will keep warm air from rising to the ceiling in the winter. When summer arrives, the rotation of the ceiling fan will need to be reversed.
            </p>
            <h2>
             The dwelling needs to be well-insulated
            </h2>
            <p>
             In order for a dwelling to remain at a constant temperature, it needs to be properly insulated. A lack of insulation can reduce a homes energy efficiency by as much as 30 percent. To inspect the amount of insulation in a wall, remove the cover from one of the outlets. However, remember to first shut off the electricity. Companies such as
             <a href="http://www.reitzel.ca/blown-insulation-service" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
              Reitzel Insulation Co Ltd
             </a>
             can actually add insulation without tearing apart the walls.
            </p>
            <h2>
             Install weather stripping around the windows
            </h2>
            <p>
             Although energy-efficient windows work very well, many people will find the cost of replacing their old windows to be a tad bit too expensive.
            </p>
            <p>
             A more affordable alternative is to install new weather stripping around your old windows. This will help to keep drafts from circulating around the window seals.
            </p>
            <p>
             These five energy-reduction strategies will certainly save you some extra cash. Even small changes can make a big improvement in your homes energy efficiency.
            </p>
