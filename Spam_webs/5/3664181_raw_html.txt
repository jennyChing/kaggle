         Laptop Lifestyle  What Is It About The Laptop Lifestyle
        </div>
        <div itemprop="articleBody">
         <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_3452" style="width: 330px">
          <a href="http://bruno-buergi.com/entrepreneur/" target="_blank">
           <img alt="Laptop Lifestyle" class="size-full wp-image-3452 " height="240" src="http://bruno-buergi.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Laptop-Lifestyle1.jpg" width="320"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Laptop Lifestyle
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Laptop Lifestyle
          </strong>
          was the one thing that attracted me to the online business. The thing that you can build a business with only a laptop wherever you are in the world. Its an interesting concept and since I heard it the first time the thought about it never left me.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h2 itemprop="alternativeHeadline">
          What Is The Laptop Lifestyle?
         </h2>
         <p>
          For me the laptop lifestyle is a way to earn money with only using your laptop. There are many ways you can do this. For me it is the internet online business Im attracted to. My main business are at the moment affiliate business and network marketing. Both business I market through online medium.
         </p>
         <p>
          The key of the
          <em>
           laptop lifestyle
          </em>
          is that you dont have to be at a certain place at a certain time like in the traditional business. When you have a job then you have to be at a certain time for a certain time in your company to get paid. The laptop lifestyle business is different. You learn how to make money online and it doesnt matter where you are and at what time you do your work. There is no one who controls how many hours you work and if you are on time at work.
         </p>
         <p>
          With the
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           laptop lifestyle
          </span>
          you are your own boss. That means you have the full control over your business and yourself. On the other side you are responsible for your own results. You cant complain about your boss or the company. Its your responsibility. Nothing more, nothing less.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h2>
          How Do You Create The Laptop Lifestyle
         </h2>
         <p>
          How to create the
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           laptop lifestyle
          </span>
          ? Its a good question and as I see it in this industry, not so many people achieve it. The biggest obstacle on the way to the laptop lifestyle is inside you. You have to make the shift from an employee to an entrepreneur. Do not underestimate this shift. You have to learn to think different and as I mentioned above you have to take the full responsibility for your results.
         </p>
         <p>
          Now, how to start it. From my experience it is the best way to start to join a company or a community where you learn the skills you need to build a business. It also should be something you are passionate about. For example you are passionate about nutrition and weight loss then you have to learn the skills to market products and or services to the public to make a living.
         </p>
         <p>
          The good thing today is that you can learn all the skills you need to succeed through online courses and communities. Beside that you need products and services to sell. Nowadays this is easy. You can join different online platform where you can find products or services to sell. Or you already have a product you can sell.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3453" style="width: 330px">
          <a href="http://bruno-buergi.com/entrepreneur/" target="_blank">
           <img alt="Enjoying the Laptop Lifestyle" class="size-full wp-image-3453 " height="240" src="http://bruno-buergi.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Enjoing-the-laptop-lifestye.jpg" width="320"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Enjoying the Laptop Lifestyle
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          It has never been easier to build your own business and with the internet the art of business changed also dramatically. The things that makes the difference to achieve the laptop lifestyle are your willingness to learn the skills, work hard, persistence, discipline and also luck. The reason why most people do not achieve the
          <a href="http://bruno-buergi.com/making-money-at-home/" target="_blank" title="laptop lifestyle">
           <em>
            laptop lifestyle
           </em>
          </a>
          is that they are not willing to do all these points I mentioned and go beyond their self limitation.
         </p>
         <p>
          One thing that I can recommend to achieve the
          <strong>
           laptop lifestyle
          </strong>
          is to join a community where you learn all these skills you need and where you also get support on the way. This community is the
          <em>
           <strong>
            Six Figure Mentors
           </strong>
          </em>
          . When you want to know more about
          <em>
           <strong>
            the Six Figure Mentors
           </strong>
          </em>
          and how they can help you to achieve the
          <strong>
           laptop lifestyle
          </strong>
          the just
          <a href="http://bruno-buergi.com/entrepreneur/" target="_blank" title="laptop Lifestyle">
           <strong>
            click here!
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
