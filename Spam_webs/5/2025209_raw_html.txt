         <p>
          This is the thirteenthin a series I bring to you each Friday, where I pick a city and share with you some of their strangest (and sometimes hilarious) Craigslist.com postings. I figure the content is endless, so why not!
         </p>
         <p>
          This week I zeroed in on my stomping grounds, Washington, DC. I grew up in, and am moving back to, northern Virginia next week and we fall under the Washington, DC Craigslist.
         </p>
         <p>
          Lets begin!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.peckedtodeathbychickens.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-12-at-6.29.35-AM.png">
           <img alt="Hookah Pipe" class="wp-image-1202 aligncenter" height="393" src="http://www.peckedtodeathbychickens.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-12-at-6.29.35-AM.png" width="650"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           <em>
            <strong>
             The hookah itself is not extremely unusual, but that fact that they will consider trading for high quality smoke machine is amusing. Isnt that pretty much what a hookah is?
            </strong>
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.peckedtodeathbychickens.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-11-at-9.30.31-PM1.png">
           <img alt="Chewbacca Costume" class="wp-image-1204 aligncenter" height="392" src="http://www.peckedtodeathbychickens.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-11-at-9.30.31-PM1.png" width="650"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <em>
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #0000ff;">
             Hands not furry?  Then costume not Chewbacca.
            </span>
           </strong>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.peckedtodeathbychickens.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-12-at-6.32.25-AM.png">
           <img alt="Stripper Pole" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1205" height="541" src="http://www.peckedtodeathbychickens.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-12-at-6.32.25-AM.png" width="515"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <em>
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #0000ff;">
             A)Is the color of a stripper pole really important to anyone? I dont think so.
            </span>
           </strong>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <em>
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #0000ff;">
             B)Im pretty sure I know what the person bartering for thiswill have in mind
            </span>
           </strong>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.peckedtodeathbychickens.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-12-at-6.38.30-AM.png">
           <img alt="Hulk Sweat" class="alignnone wp-image-1206" height="451" src="http://www.peckedtodeathbychickens.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-12-at-6.38.30-AM.png" width="750"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <em>
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #0000ff;">
             Just when I thought the CraigsList wacko bin might be scraping the bottom, my faith is restored.
            </span>
           </strong>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.peckedtodeathbychickens.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-12-at-6.57.18-AM1.png">
           <img alt="Screen Shot 2014-06-12 at 6.57.18 AM" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1208" height="409" src="http://www.peckedtodeathbychickens.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-12-at-6.57.18-AM1.png" width="640"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           <em>
            <strong>
             Sweet! I have been thinking about replacing my lucky rabbits foot and this might be just the thing! It has lovely toenails and hairy bristles is a real selling point for me.
            </strong>
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">

         <p style="text-align: center;">

           </strong>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.peckedtodeathbychickens.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-12-at-3.12.16-PM.png">
