       <p>
        <a href="http://www.contactmusic.com/angelina-jolie">
         Angelina Jolie
        </a>
        talks about her eponymous character in the fairytale flick 'Maleficent' in a short interview which sees her discuss her fascination with the character, how she relates to her and working with co-star Elle Fanning.
       </p>
       <p>
        'She was actually somebody who was quite fierce and loyal and a fighter for what she believed in when she was young', Angelina explains of Maleficent. 'When she gets wounded she loses that and does the absolute most unjust, horrible thing, to curse an innocent person.' She goes on to talk about Elle Fanning in the role of Princess Aurora. 'There's a lot of humour to be had and charm in the combination of these two because for as frightening as I can be, her kindness and sweetness and openness is frightening to me and Maleficent', she reveals.
