                      <a href="http://www.theslowroasteditalian.com/2013/01/Ladies-Night-Cocktail-Party001.html" target="_blank">
                       Chocolate Cherry Cordial Cocktail
                      </a>
                     </b>
                     - Who needs a box of chocolates when you can have this luscious, sweet, chocolaty, cherry cordial in a glass.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://www.theslowroasteditalian.blogspot.com/2012/05/lemon-cream-pie-martini.html" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;" target="_blank">
                       <img border="0" height="424" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-W2kTL1YIev0/UKR7VryZUtI/AAAAAAAAHP4/IQCIFbhoNzA/s640/lemon-cream-pie-martini.jpg" width="640"/>

                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      This luscious
                      <b>
                       <a href="http://www.theslowroasteditalian.blogspot.com/2012/05/lemon-cream-pie-martini.html" target="_blank">
                        Lemon Cream Pie Martini
                       </a>
                      </b>
                      is the perfect cocktail to celebrate spring. Just like the dessert it is lip smacking good, tart, sweet &amp; creamy! Actually reminds me of Lemon Drops too!
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <b>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <span class="st">
                        <span class="st">
                         <span class="st">
                          <span style="color: black;">
                           Snickers Martini
                          </span>
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </b>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                      <span class="st">
                       <span class="st">
                        <span class="st">
                         <span style="color: black;">
                          <i>
                           serves 2 (large martinis)
                          </i>
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     3 ounces Divinci caramel syrup (or your favorite)
                     <br/>
                     4 ounces creme de cacao (white)
                     <br/>
                     2 ounces amaretto liqueur
                     <br/>
                     2 ounces hazelnut liqueur
                     <br/>
                     2 ounces International Delight French Vanilla Creamer
                     <br/>
                     3 tablespoons peanut butter
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add peanut butter, creme de cacao, amaretto and hazelnut liqueurs to the shaker. Shake for about a minute until the peanut butter is mostly dissolved (alternately you can stir in the peanut butter.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Add caramel syrup and creamer. Shake to combine. Strain into cocktail glasses.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Serve and enjoy!
