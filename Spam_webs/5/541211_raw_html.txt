              <p>
               No matter your age, most people will admit to wanting to look their best when they know that they will be having sexual intercourse. One of the most common complaints that people make is that they do not like going to a gym to tone and build muscle strength. While your body may look younger and stronger, if you do not like where you are working out you are less likely to continue. There are some sexy exercises that can be performed during sexual intercourse, not only will this help you to shed a few extra pounds, both you and your partner will enjoy the workout.
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <b>
                The Best and Sexiest Ways to Burn Calories during Sex
               </b>
               <br/>
               <br/>
               These are a few calorie burning tricks that will not only help you to tone muscles, but it will also be appreciated by your partner. It is not often you find a way to exercise that also stimulates and satisfies your partner. While this should not replace regular workouts, it is a fun way to stay in shape and look great.
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <b>
                Kiss Like You Did In High School
               </b>
               <br/>
               <br/>
               Some research has shown that during a thirty minute having make-out session it is possible to burn up to 240 calories. While most couples do not kiss for a full half hour, even in high school it does increase heart rate and a persons libido. Kissing in a hot room can also increase the number of calories burned, along with frequently changing position and moving around a room. If you are serious about burning off some excessive weight during sexual intercourse, the most important thing is to keep moving. Once the heart rate has decreased, it can be difficult to
               <b>
                <a href="https://www.performanceinsiders.com/natural-ways-to-increase-stamina.html">
                 build up the level of stamina
                </a>
               </b>
               . Without any exertion, you will not burn enough calories to lose weight nor will your partner be satisfied sexually.
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <b>
                Go Down on Them
               </b>
               <br/>
               <br/>
               Both women and men can burn up to 100 calories by performing oral sex on their partners, though this is based on thirty minutes of sexual activity. For many couples thirty minutes of oral sex can seem like an incredibly long time, but you can mix in other sexual tricks that you might have. Remember the more activity you put into the sexual act, the higher your rate of physical exertion and the number of calories you are burning. Some tips to try include using stomach muscles to pull yourself into a sitting position, or using your hands to do a pushup. While some women may not appreciate that you are trying to get in shape for her, most will applaud you and hope that you continue exercising while performing oral sex.
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <b>
                On Top During Sex
               </b>
               <br/>
               <br/>
               Most men never considered that the missionary position can feel good and count as an exercise. Research has clearly shown that the sexual position does matter, especially when you are trying to calculate how many calories you can burn during intercourse. Not only will you exert more energy, you can also tone your muscles. While you wont get the same workout during sex as you would at the gym, it is more enjoyable and can have the added benefit of strengthening your relationship.
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <b>
                Summary
               </b>
               <br/>
               <br/>
               There is nothing wrong with wanting to look your best physically, especially if you know that you will be showing your body off during sex. Regular exercise and workouts are the best way for you to get in shape and tone major muscle groups, but there is nothing wrong with including
               <b>
                <a href="http://www.examiner.com/article/why-sex-count-as-the-best-form-of-exercise-1">
                 sexual activity in your exercise routine
                </a>
               </b>
               . Whether you spend a lot of time kissing or having oral sex, you can enjoy the added benefit of burning a few extra calories. If you are planning on using sex as part of an exercise routine, you will want to discuss it with your partner. No one wants to feel like they are simply part of an exercise program and not part of the relationship. If you do happen to burn a few calories during sexual intercourse than you should be happy, but you should never try to have sex just to get in a daily workout. It is still an intimate act between two people, and not part of a weight loss or muscle toning program.
