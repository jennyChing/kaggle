        <h1 class="entry-title">
         <a href="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/review-swatches-sinfulcolors-full-throttle-collection/" rel="bookmark" title="">
          Quick Review &amp; Swatches: SinfulColors Full Throttle Collection
         </a>
        </h1>
        <h2 class="entry-meta">
         <time class="published updated" datetime="2014-07-16">
          July 16, 2014
         </time>
         
         <a href="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/review-swatches-sinfulcolors-full-throttle-collection/#comments">
          <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="5924 http://www.slashedbeauty.com/?p=5924">
           32 Comments
          </span>
         </a>
        </h2>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div id="greet_block">
        </div>
        <a class="maxbutton-1" href="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/disclosures" rel="nofollow">
         PR Sample
        </a>
        <p>
         Ive been dabbling in textured polishes lately, and while Im not sure I prefer them over a smooth, shiny finish, they definitely add some variety to my manicures. If youre looking for some textured polishes to try on a dime, perhaps youd enjoy the Full Throttle collection from SinfulColors for Summer 2014. These have a rubberized texture that looks like subtle, small dimples on the surface.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Sneak Peak Swatches: SinfulColors Full Throttle Collection - Burn Rubber" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5925" height="430" src="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/burnrubber.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Burn Rubber
         </strong>
         is a safety-cone orange. It reminds me very much of an actual orange (like, the fruit) because of the bumpy texture.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Sneak Peak Swatches: SinfulColors Full Throttle Collection - Race Rubber" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5926" height="428" src="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/racerubber.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Race Rubber
         </strong>
         is a deep, bold burgundy shade. This one was a bit tricky, because the shade deepens with each layer, so you have to be pretty precise each time to get an even application.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Sneak Peak Swatches: SinfulColors Full Throttle Collection - Rubber Ball" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5927" height="429" src="http://www.slashedbeauty.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Rubberball.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Rubber Ball
         </strong>
         is a vibrant, mid-toned pink. This shade applied with full coverage in just one layer.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im not sure if this texture is what I think about when I imagine rubberized. I think it resembles concrete more, no? Either way, the shades are vibrant and still maintain a shiny finish. I found it much more suitable to apply in thin layers, otherwise the polish has a hard time settling into the texture and drying down.
        </p>
        <p>
         Find these shades and the rest of the SinfulColors Full Throttle collection at Rite Aid for $1.99 each throughout July.
        </p>
        <p>
         Whats your favorite variation of nail polish texture?
        </p>
