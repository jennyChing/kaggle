     <h1 class="grub-topOfColumn">
      9 Simple Vegetarian Recipes for the Perfect Easter Brunch
     </h1>
     <div class="grub-byline">
      Blog Post by
      <a href="https://plus.google.com/113274240511284571445" rel="author">
       Jerry James Stone
      </a>
       March 30, 2013
     </div>
     <div class="grub-mainPostContent">
      <p>
       <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="1000" grub-popupphotopath="/blog/2013/03/9-simple-vegetarian-recipes-for-the-perfect-easter-brunch/easter-brunch/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/easter-brunch" rel="attachment wp-att-2162">
        <img alt="Easter Brunch" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2162" height="600" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Easter-Brunch-600x600.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       If you are hosting Easter Brunch tomorrow or just want to wow your family, I know the perfect thing. Well, I know the perfect 9 things! These recipes are big on flavor and easy on time. I think youre gonna love them. Heck, youll probably even make them past Easter.
      </p>
      <p>
       Lets start with the booze, shall we?
      </p>
      <h2>
       <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/cucumber-green-tea-mojito/">
        Cucumber &amp; Green Tea Mojito
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       I originally created this for St. Patricks Day but you know, it works for Easter too. Its fresh and crisp and just sings of spring.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="724" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/cucumber-green-tea-mojito/cucumber-green-tea-mojito-main/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/cucumber-green-tea-mojito-main" rel="attachment wp-att-1860">
        <img alt="Cucumber &amp; Green Tea Mojito Main" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-1860" height="434" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Cucumber-Green-Tea-Mojito-Main-600x434.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/frozen-avocado-margarita/">
        Frozen Avocado Margarita
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       This drink is nothing like youd expect. Full of flavor and the fresh tarragon keeps it super bright. Its really yummy and will wow your guests for sure.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="802" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/frozen-avocado-margarita/frozen-avocado-margarita-close-up/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/frozen-avocado-margarita-close-up" rel="attachment wp-att-2063">
        <img alt="Frozen Avocado Margarita Close Up" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2063" height="481" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Frozen-Avocado-Margarita-Close-Up-600x481.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/broccoli-gouda-stuffed-cornbread/">
        Broccoli &amp; Gouda Stuffed Cornbread
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       We could all use a little more broccoli on our plates, right? Now, the vegetable is wonderful just on its own-or wonderful with a little lemon and butter-but the more places I can sneak in broccoli, the better! This isnt the first thing Ive snuck into a corn muffin. For example, it is great with an egg inside. But today is about broccoli.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="678" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/broccoli-gouda-stuffed-cornbread/broccoli-gouda-stuffed-cornbread-main/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/broccoli-gouda-stuffed-cornbread-main" rel="attachment wp-att-1053">
        <img alt="Broccoli &amp; Gouda Stuffed Cornbread Main" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-1053" height="406" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Broccoli-Gouda-Stuffed-Cornbread-Main-600x406.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/spiked-deviled-eggs-for-easter-sunday/">
        Spiked Deviled Eggs
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Theyre the perfect deviled egg for Easter, and theyre easier to make than they look! I promise. The brine is quite simple and once you make it, you just have to store some eggs in it. After the eggs pickle, it is business as usualexcept with more vodka!
      </p>
      <p>
       <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="666" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/spiked-deviled-eggs-for-easter-sunday/spiked-deviled-eggs-main/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/spiked-deviled-eggs-main" rel="attachment wp-att-2124">
        <img alt="Spiked Deviled Eggs Main" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2124" height="399" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Spiked-Deviled-Eggs-Main-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/amazing-brussels-sprout-latkes/">
        Amazing Brussels Sprout Latkes
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       My affinity for Brussels sprouts is no secret. I mean, have you seen my
       <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/balsamic-glazed-brussels-sprouts-with-crushed-pine-nuts-and-parmesan/" title="Balsamic-Glazed Brussels Sprouts with Crushed Pine Nuts and Parmesan">
        Balsamic-Roasted Brussels Sprouts
       </a>
       ? Or what about my
       <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/garlic-and-herb-stuffed-brussels-sprouts/" title="Garlic and Herb Stuffed Brussels Sprouts">
        Stuffed Brussels Sprouts
       </a>
       ?! So when came time to experiment with latkes, you just know I had to add Brussels sprouts, right?
      </p>
      <p>
       <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="666" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/amazing-brussels-sprout-latkes/brussels-sprout-latke/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/brussels-sprout-latke" rel="attachment wp-att-660">
        <img alt="Brussels Sprout Latke" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-660" height="399" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Brussels-Sprout-Latke-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/chocolate-balsamic-roasted-beets/">
        Chocolate &amp; Balsamic Roasted Beets
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Did you know that you can make Red Velvet Cake with beets? I made one recently and it was quite good. The photos sucked so I need to redo the photo shoot but the cake itself was scrumptious. That was the inspiration behind this dish.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="916" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/chocolate-balsamic-roasted-beets/chocolate-balsamic-roasted-beets-top/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/chocolate-balsamic-roasted-beets-top" rel="attachment wp-att-2020">
        <img alt="Chocolate Balsamic Roasted Beets Top" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2020" height="549" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Chocolate-Balsamic-Roasted-Beets-Top-600x549.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/brussels-sprout-tangerine-couscous-salad/">
        Brussels Sprout &amp; Tangerine Couscous Salad
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Toasted Israeli couscous with roasted brussels and crisp tangerine make this salad the perfect representation of winter meets spring.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="1128" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/brussels-sprout-tangerine-couscous-salad/brussels-sprout-tangerine-couscous-full/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/brussels-sprout-tangerine-couscous-full" rel="attachment wp-att-2093">
        <img alt="Brussels Sprout &amp; Tangerine Couscous Full" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2093" height="676" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Brussels-Sprout-Tangerine-Couscous-Full-600x676.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/artichoke-baked-egg/">
        Artichoke Baked Egg
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       I never get tired of baking an egg inside something so it should be of no surprise that I put one in a roasted artichoke. Yum! It is quite good and I think you will enjoy it as much as I did.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="666" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/9-healthy-eats-drinks-for-st-patricks-day/artichoke-baked-egg-2/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/artichoke-baked-egg-2" rel="attachment wp-att-1910">
        <img alt="Artichoke Baked Egg" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-1910" height="399" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Artichoke-Baked-Egg-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/strawberries-stuffed-with-honey-lavender-cream-cheese/#recipe">
        Strawberries Stuffed with Honey-Lavender Cream Cheese
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Youre gonna be stuffed but still, no excuse for skipping dessert. And this one is so light and easy to enjoy that you can have as many or as little as you want.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="666" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/strawberries-stuffed-with-honey-lavender-cream-cheese/strawberry-stuffed-with-honey-lavender-cream-cheese-final/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/strawberry-stuffed-with-honey-lavender-cream-cheese-final" rel="attachment wp-att-1955">
        <img alt="Strawberry Stuffed with Honey-Lavender Cream Cheese Final" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-1955" height="399" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Strawberry-Stuffed-with-Honey-Lavender-Cream-Cheese-Final-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
