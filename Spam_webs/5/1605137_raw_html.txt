        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Krympta barnteckningar 
         <br/>
         <i>
          Shrinked kids drawings
         </i>
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/2012/08/26/shrinkartwork/" rel="bookmark" title="21:48">
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2012-08-26T21:48:53+00:00">
           2012/08/26
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Helena, Craft and Creativity">
            Helena, Craft and Creativity
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>

       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing5.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone" height="630" src="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing5.jpg" title="Shrink kids' artwork - by Craft &amp; Creativity" width="450"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Krymplast r verkligen i ropet igen och jag har sett mnga fina exempel, bland annat hos
         <a href="http://www.pastill.nu/search/label/krympplast" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.pastill.nu/search/label/krympplast', 'Pastill.nu']);" target="_blank">
          Pastill.nu
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://pysselbolaget.se/2012/04/24/kuliga-krympisar/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://pysselbolaget.se/2012/04/24/kuliga-krympisar/', 'Pysselbolaget']);" target="_blank">
          Pysselbolaget
         </a>
         och
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/minifanfan/sets/72157623540489606/detail/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.flickr.com/photos/minifanfan/sets/72157623540489606/detail/', 'Minifanfan']);" target="_blank">
          Minifanfan
         </a>
         . Nu har ven jag blivit smittad och mitt frsta projekt blev att gra hngen av min dotters fina teckningar. Samma teckningar som min mamma
         <a href="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/2012/05/16/childdrawings/" target="_blank">
          omvandlade till virkade dockor
         </a>
         fr ett tag sedan. Jag garanterar att det kommer dyka upp fler krympplastprojekt p bloggen i framtiden, fr s roligt var det!
         <br/>
         -
         <br/>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Shrink plastic seems really popular at the moment and Ive seen many great examples, for example at
           <a href="http://www.pastill.nu/search/label/krympplast" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.pastill.nu/search/label/krympplast', 'Pastill.nu']);" target="_blank">
            Pastill.nu
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://pysselbolaget.se/2012/04/24/kuliga-krympisar/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://pysselbolaget.se/2012/04/24/kuliga-krympisar/', 'Pysselbolaget']);" target="_blank">
            Pysselbolaget
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/minifanfan/sets/72157623540489606/detail/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.flickr.com/photos/minifanfan/sets/72157623540489606/detail/', 'Minifanfan']);" target="_blank">
            Minifanfan
           </a>
           . Ive also been smitten and my first project was to make pendants out of my daughters drawings. Actually the same drawings as my mother
           <a href="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/2012/05/16/childdrawings/" target="_blank">
            adapted into crocheted dolls
           </a>
           , a while back. I can guarantee that you will see more shrink plastic projects being featured here in the future, thats how much fun it was
          </span>
         </em>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           !
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing1.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4272" height="769" src="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing1.jpg" title="Shrink kids' artwork - by Craft &amp; Creativity" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Jag ritade av barnteckningarna p vit krympplast (man kan sjlvklart lta barnen sjlva rita direkt p plasten). Jag anvnde mig av vanliga frgpennor/akvarellpennor. Sedan klippte jag ut figurerna och gjorde hl med hltngen (gr skert ven bra med hlslag). Hlet ska vara ganska stort eftersom det krymper rejlt. In i ugnen i 160C. Plasten rullar ihop sig nr den krymper men efter en stund sltar den till sig igen och blir platt. D r det dags att ta ut den ur ugnen.
         <br/>
         
         <br/>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           I re-drew my daughters drawings on white shrink plastic sheets (of course you can let the kids draw directly on the plastic instead). I used regular colored pencils / watercolor pencils. Then I cut out the figures and made holes with a hole puncher. The hole should be quite large because it will shrink considerably. Place the objects in the oven, at 320
           <em>
            F
            <em>
             (160C). The plastic will curl up while shrinking, but after a while they will flatten out. Then its time to remove them from the oven.
            </em>
           </em>
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing4.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone" height="385" src="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing4.jpg" title="Shrink kids' artwork - by Craft &amp; Creativity" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Vi kpte o-ringar och gjorde hngen av krympplasten. /
         <br/>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           We bought round jump rings and made pendants out of the shrink plastic.
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing6.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4279" height="356" src="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing6.jpg" title="Shrink kids' drawings by Craft &amp; Creativity" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         S hr ser min dotters originalteckningar ut. Jag tycker de r s hrliga! /
         <br/>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           This is what my daughters original drawings look like. I think theyre so great!
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing2.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4273" height="275" src="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing2.jpg" title="Shrink kids' artwork - by Craft &amp; Creativity" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Och s hr blev de som krympplast. /
         <br/>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           And this is what they look like in shrink plastic.
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing3.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4274" height="630" src="http://craftandcreativity.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/shrink_drawing3.jpg" title="Shrink kids' artwork - by Craft &amp; Creativity" width="450"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ett konstverk i miniatyr. /
         <em>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Aminiature artwork.
          </span>
         </em>
