     <h2 class="storytitle" id="post-1364">
      Wives of Spanking Husbands Club
     </h2>
     <span class="posted">
      26 Jan 2008 /
      <a href="http://eppsnet.com/author/pe-2/" rel="author" title="Posts by Paul Epps">
       Paul Epps
      </a>
     </span>
     <div class="storycontent">
      <p>
       From
       <a href="http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/thedailymirror/files/1938_0126_cover.jpg" rel="external">
        the front page of the
        <em>
         Los Angeles Times
        </em>
       </a>
       70 years ago today, Jan. 26, 1938:
      </p>
      <blockquote class="quoted smaller">
       <h2>
        Wives of Spanking Husbands
        <br/>
        Form Girls Auxiliary to Club
       </h2>
       <p>
        SIOUX FALLS (S. D.) Jan. 25 (AP)  Wives of Spanking Husbands Club, organized in Sioux City, Iowa, and parent organization of fifty-nine such clubs throughout the nation according to its own figuresreached out for another slice of territory today.
       </p>
       <p>
        The Iowa housewives who consider it a mark of esteem for their husbands to wield a disciplinary hairbrush once in a while, announced plans today for a junior auxiliaryDaughters of Spanking Parents.
       </p>
       <h4>
        ELIGIBLE GIRLS
       </h4>
       <p>
        A letter received here from Sioux City and signed Rita Rae, general delivery, told of plans for the new organization for which she claimed an initial membership of seventeen. Any girl above the age of 11 years is eligible to join, Mrs. Rae wrote.
       </p>
       <p>
        We think all parents should spank their daughters when they dont behave, Mrs. Rae wrote. Some girls wont admit it, but the really know it is better to get spanked than scolded and nagged. Spanking creates a better understanding between parents and daughters.
       </p>
       <h4>
        MERELY SPANKED
       </h4>
       <p>
        Mrs. Rae is the president of the Wives of Spanking Husbands, which was organized last June 26 under the broad-minded slogan Spare the hairbrush and spoil the wife.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our husbands dont beat us, the Sioux City woman was careful to explain. They just turn us over their knees and give us a good sound spanking.
       </p>
      </blockquote>
