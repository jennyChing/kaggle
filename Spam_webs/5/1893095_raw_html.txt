        <h1>
         The List: 8 Hot Tubs in Cool Places
        </h1>
        <div class="contributor-info user-level-10">
         <div class="contributor-description">
          - Joy Pecknold, Passport blog editor
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="entry">
         <p>
          Baby, its getting colder outside, but thats nothing a little hot tub cant fix. Theres no shortage of pools and Jacuzzis to be found around the globe, but for those in search of more spectacular onesbe it en suite or under the starsthis is your list.
          <span id="more-30725">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/007435-26-Penthouse-Bath-lake-okanagan-view.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/007435-26-Penthouse-Bath-lake-okanagan-view.jpg', '']);">
           <img alt="007435-26-Penthouse-Bath-lake-okanagan-view" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-30733" height="402" src="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/007435-26-Penthouse-Bath-lake-okanagan-view.jpg" width="714"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           British Columbia:
           <a href="http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/sparkling-hill-resort-and-wellness-hotel" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/sparkling-hill-resort-and-wellness-hotel', 'Sparkling Hill Resort']);">
            Sparkling Hill Resort
           </a>
          </strong>
          <br/>
          With an astounding 40,000-square-foot spa on-site, this Swarovski crystal-inspired resort has quite a few pools, but one neednt leave their room to indulge. The bathtubs placed in front of floor-to-ceiling picture windows offer a front row seat to the sun setting on Lake Okanagan and dipping behind the Monashee Mountains.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Post-Ranch-Inn-000068-06.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Post-Ranch-Inn-000068-06.jpg', '']);">
           <img alt="Post Ranch Inn-000068-06" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-30729" height="402" src="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Post-Ranch-Inn-000068-06.jpg" width="714"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           California:
           <a href="http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/post-ranch-inn " onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/post-ranch-inn ', 'Post Ranch Inn']);">
            Post Ranch Inn
           </a>
          </strong>
          <br/>
          In beautiful Big Sur, the Post Ranch Inn has not one but two cliff-top heated infinity spas to soak in whilst soaking up an unhindered view of the Pacific Ocean out front. For those who permit themselves just one vacation Instagram to make friends back home jealous, a snap from either tub will do it.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Dunton-Hot-Springs.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Dunton-Hot-Springs.jpg', '']);">
           <img alt="Dunton Hot Springs" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-30737" height="402" src="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Dunton-Hot-Springs.jpg" width="714"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Colorado:
           <a href="http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/dunton-hot-springs-resort" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/dunton-hot-springs-resort', 'Dunton Hot Springs']);">
            Dunton Hot Springs
           </a>
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Known for their therapeutic powers, hot springs are the ultimate hot tub (Mother Nature knows best), and here they have sixfour under the stars dotted across the property, one within the Well House cabin and one in the restored 19th century Bathhouse.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/000595-17-RS521_Amangani-Swimming-Pool-in-Winter-scr.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/000595-17-RS521_Amangani-Swimming-Pool-in-Winter-scr.jpg', '']);">
           <img alt="000595-17-RS521_Amangani - Swimming Pool in Winter-scr" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-30732" height="402" src="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/000595-17-RS521_Amangani-Swimming-Pool-in-Winter-scr.jpg" width="714"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Wyoming:
           <a href="http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/amangani " onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/amangani ', 'Amangani']);">
            Amangani
           </a>
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Atop a butte looking down on an alpine meadow where elk graze with the snow-capped peaks of Grand Teton National Park over yonder, theres Amanganis superb square-shaped hot tub. Needless to say, its sublime.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/006556-04-hotel-le-crystal-spa-hot-tub.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/006556-04-hotel-le-crystal-spa-hot-tub.jpg', '']);">
           <img alt="006556-04-hotel le crystal-spa-hot tub" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-30730" height="402" src="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/006556-04-hotel-le-crystal-spa-hot-tub.jpg" width="714"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Montreal:
           <a href="http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/hotel-le-crystal " onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/hotel-le-crystal ', 'Hotel Le Crystal']);">
            Hotel Le Crystal
           </a>
          </strong>
          <br/>
          The best way to unwind after a day gorging on Montreals magnificent cuisine is a dip in Hotel Le Crystals rooftop terrace hot tub which affords a sparkling city view. The icing on the cake would be if snow started to fallcome winter, theres a pretty good chance of that.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Hotel-le-Saint-Roch.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Hotel-le-Saint-Roch.jpg', '']);">
           <img alt="Hotel le Saint Roch" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-30735" height="402" src="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Hotel-le-Saint-Roch.jpg" width="714"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           France:
           <a href="http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/hotel-le-saint-roch" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/hotel-le-saint-roch', 'Hotel le Saint Roch']);">
            Hotel le Saint Roch
           </a>
          </strong>
          <br/>
          In Courchevel, part of the worlds biggest ski area, Trois Valles, Saint Roch has awe-inspiring alpine views but the artful indoor setting for its warm wading pool is equally impressivea spectacular sculpture of spheres hangs above.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Nevai.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Nevai.jpg', '']);">
           <img alt="Nevai" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-30736" height="402" src="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Nevai.jpg" width="714"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Switzerland:
           <a href="http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/nevai" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/nevai', 'Neva']);">
            Neva
           </a>
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Situated in the Swiss Alps, the two penthouse suites of this design hotel offer private outdoor Jacuzzis with panoramic views of Verbier and the peak of Petit Combin. Inside, a fireplace keeps the cozy going.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Queenstown-Hilton.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Queenstown-Hilton.jpg', '']);">
           <img alt="Queenstown Hilton" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-30734" height="402" src="https://blog-cdn.kiwicollection.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Queenstown-Hilton.jpg" width="714"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           New Zealand:
           <a href="http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/queenstown-hilton" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.kiwicollection.com/hotel-detail/queenstown-hilton', 'Queenstown Hilton']);">
            Queenstown Hilton
           </a>
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Any shiver-inducing evening in Queenstown is remedied by staying in one of this Hilton propertys Relaxation Rooms which boast private decks with hot tubs for soaking in the surroundingsthat being the mountain-framed shores of Lake Wakatipu.
         </p>
