         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Abundance Is A State of Mind
         </h1>
         <div class="meta-below-title entry-meta clearfix clearfix-title">
          <div class="left">
           Written on
           <span class="entry-date published">
            October 25, 2013
           </span>
           by
           <a class="author-link fn nickname url" href="http://www.alchemy4thesoul.com/author/magnet" rel="author" title="View all posts by Kath Roberts">
            Kath Roberts
           </a>
           in
           <a href="http://www.alchemy4thesoul.com/category/law-of-attraction" rel="category tag">
            Law of Attraction
           </a>
           ,
           <a href="http://www.alchemy4thesoul.com/category/wealth-creation-2" rel="category tag">
            Wealth Creation
           </a>
          </div>
          <!-- .left -->
         </div>
         <!-- .entry-meta -->
         <div class="entry-content">
          <div class="lr_horizontal_share" data-counter-url="http://www.alchemy4thesoul.com/law-of-attraction/abundance-state-of-mind" data-share-url="http://www.alchemy4thesoul.com/law-of-attraction/abundance-state-of-mind">
          </div>
          <h2 style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <a href="http://www.alchemy4thesoul.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/shutterstock_158133149.jpg" rel="nofollow">
            <img alt="abundance is a state of mind, vibration of abundance, growing a successful business" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-8217" height="200" src="http://www.alchemy4thesoul.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/shutterstock_158133149-300x200.jpg" title="" width="300"/>
           </a>
           How to Develop Abundance in Your Business
          </h2>
          <p>
           I meet many peoplein my work who are looking for the answers to build a successful business. In many ways theyre
           <em>
            <strong>
             DOING
            </strong>
           </em>
           everything theyve been led to believe that will make their businesses grow, namely activities such as marketing, networking, building social media links, selling and building collaborative relationships with other business owners that will catapult their growth.
          </p>
          <p>
           Theyre putting in all the effort, the hours and the commitment in learning the how to of business.
          </p>
          <p>
           Often theyre looking for short-cuts, formulas and breakthroughs to get to the gold as quickly as possible.
          </p>
          <p>
           In many regards though, the business of
           <em>
            <strong>
             DOING
            </strong>
           </em>
           is overshadowing and neglecting the importance of
           <em>
            <strong>
             BEING
            </strong>
           </em>
           and thats frequently the missing ingredient that makes all the difference.
          </p>
          <p>
           Whilst I appreciate that we all need to make a living from our work there is a better way than just doing more and working longer.
          </p>
          <p>
           Whilst youre looking outside of yourselves the answers will always allude you.
          </p>
          <p>
           We know that customers are more discerning today than ever before . Theyare interested in ethical products as well ascompanies that have noble causes and credible trustworthy leaders.
          </p>
          <p>
           They want value, authenticity and principle centred leadership which create win wins for all concerned.
           <em>
            <strong>
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           The rules of doing business have changed for good!
          </p>
          <p>
           CHARACTER and what you stand for is more important than ever before and that only comes with self reflection and going inside a while to seek that deeper wisdom. When we start with
           <em>
            <strong>
             Being
            </strong>
           </em>
           we automatically do less and achieve far more.
          </p>
          <p>
           The harvest firstbegins by planting the right intentions and weeding out those emotions that dont serve you. Long before the ears of corn ever surface there is a complexprocess unfolding under ground. Perfect corn needs the right conditions and so do you if you want to run a successful business.
          </p>
          <p>
           Learn to take care of your own emotional, spiritual, mental and physical needs and youre there.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Abundance is a state of mind
          </h3>
          <p>
           To create a healthy balance sheet and steady stream of customers we must first get into the
           <a href="http://www.stevepavlina.com/blog/2009/02/shifting-your-vibration-to-manifest-your-desires/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="vibration of abundance">
            <em>
             <strong>
              vibration of abundance
             </strong>
            </em>
           </a>
           . Seeing is not believing but the other way round ironically. When we work with love rather than fear and eradicate the self -limiting beliefs that hold us back we become a magnet to attract money, precisely because that hasnt been our singular objective.
          </p>
          <p>
           To be a receiver however you also need to be a giver.
          </p>
          <p>
           So the basis of growing a successful business starts with service and giving and that means that youre happy and open to sharing your wisdom and helping others. Thats avery different propositionfrom giving with the expectation to receive. There is often a desperation attachment to that way of working which starts with words like need to/should/take and thats a prettyunattractive vibe for finding clients.
          </p>
          <p>
           So first of all establish whether youve been unconsciouslymatching with that expectation in mind or whether youve been blatantly taking. In my own experience true givers are a very rare and specialbreed!
          </p>
          <p>
           <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="356" marginheight="0" marginwidth="0" scrolling="no" src="http://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/embed_code/24379947?rel=0" style="border: 1px solid #CCC; border-width: 1px 1px 0; margin-bottom: 5px;" width="427">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 5px;">
           <strong>
            <a href="https://www.slideshare.net/NowPosible/the-wisdom-of-giving" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="The Wisdom of Giving">
             The Wisdom of Giving
            </a>
           </strong>
           from
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.slideshare.net/NowPosible" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Bruce Kasanoff
            </a>
           </strong>
          </div>
          <p>
           We cant give what we dont have so the more we can invest our energy in feeling good ourselves and clearing our own deficiency needs as well as developing deep-seated self acceptance, the quicker we can get into the vibration of abundance. Abundance on the inside will always result in abundance on the outside.
          </p>
          <p>
           Heres a parable which explains beautifully what I m saying;
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Precious Gift
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           A wise woman who was traveling in the mountains found a precious stone in a stream.
          </p>
          <p>
           The next day she met another traveler who was hungry,
           <br/>
           and the wise woman opened her bag to share her food.
          </p>
          <p>
           The hungry traveler saw the precious stone and asked the woman to give it to him. She did so without hesitation.
          </p>
          <p>
           The traveler left rejoicing in his good fortune.
           <br/>
           He knew the stone was worth enough to give him
           <br/>
           security for a lifetime. But, a few days later,
           <br/>
           he came back to return the stone to the wise woman.
          </p>
          <p>
           Ive been thinking, he said. I know how valuable this stone is,
           <br/>
           but I give it back in the hope that you can give me something
           <br/>
           even more precious. Give me what you have within you that
           <br/>
           enabled you to give me this stone.
          </p>
          <p>
            Sometimes its not the wealth you have
           <br/>
           but whats inside you that others need.
          </p>
          <p>
           To your abundance!
          </p>
