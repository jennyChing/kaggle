        <p>
         Bookshelves dont have to be used just for books storing. You can use them in bunch of really creative ways that will make your apartment look modern and unique.
        </p>
        <h2>
         1. Bookcase Dcor
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Bookcase-Decor-mod.jpg">
          <img alt="Bookcase Decor mod" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5189" height="720" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Bookcase-Decor-mod.jpg" width="960"/>

        </p>
        <p>
         If youre aiming for a modern look, then you will love this awesome looking bookshelf. Arrange your books by color and it will look even better (if that is even possible).
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.elkaniho.com/cool-bookshelf-design-ideas/bookworm-bookshelf-design/" target="_blank" title="Bookcase Dcor mod">

        <h2>
         2. Turning a bookshelf into a bar
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/turning-a-bookshelf-into-a-bar-mod.jpg">
          <img alt="turning a bookshelf into a bar mod" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5183" height="480" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/turning-a-bookshelf-into-a-bar-mod.jpg" width="720"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Guys will love this! Turn a simple bookshelf into a cool looking bar. Show your collection of drinks to everyone! Perfect piece of furniture for a man cage.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://9am-3pm.com/2012/03/15/bookshelf-to-bar/" target="_blank" title="Turning a bookshelf into a bar mod">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Source
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         3. Bookseat
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Bookseat-mod.jpg">
          <img alt="Bookseat mod" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5177" height="604" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Bookseat-mod.jpg" width="605"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This bookseat looks so simple but at the same time is amazingly creative. Store your books at the same place where you sit and read them. Awesome!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://rilane.com/decorating-ideas/15-insanely-creative-bookshelves-designs/" target="_blank" title="Bookseat mod">

        <h2>
         4. Headboard Bookshelf
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/As-a-headboard-mod.jpg">
          <img alt="As a headboard mod" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5186" height="662" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/As-a-headboard-mod.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Why dont you put a bookcase behind your bed? You can use it as a one large cushion and your favorite books will always be near you when you want to read them before sleep.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.architectureartdesigns.com/27-cool-ideas-for-your-bedroom/" target="_blank" title="Headboard Bookshelf mod">
