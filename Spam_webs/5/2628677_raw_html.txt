         <p>
          While I still think that
          <em>
           90 Day Fiance
          </em>
          Season 2 is just trashy and I cant believe they have me hooked and watching every week, I will admit that I found myself crying several times in tonights episode! Hey, I am a huge fan of both
          <strong>
           Amy
          </strong>
          and
          <strong>
           Yamir
          </strong>
          and they both had good times during tonights episode of
          <em>
           90 Day Fiance
          </em>
          2014! Check out the highlights from tonight below in our
          <em>
           90 Day Fiance
          </em>
          Season 2 Recap!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/58534/90-day-fiance-season-2-recap-week-6-wedding-dress-shopping/90-day-fiance-season-2-spoilers-week-6-recap-chelsea-and-yamir" rel="attachment wp-att-58535">
           <img alt="90 Day Fiance Season 2 Spoilers - Week 6 Recap - Chelsea and Yamir" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-58535" height="303" src="http://gossipandgab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/90-Day-Fiance-Season-2-Spoilers-Week-6-Recap-Chelsea-and-Yamir-460x303.jpg" width="460"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/58087/90-day-fiance-season-2-recap-week-5-no-nude-magazines" target="_blank" title="90 Day Fiance Season 2 Recap: Week 5  No Nude Magazines!">
           <strong>
            Last week on
            <em>
             90 Day Fiance
            </em>
            Season 2
           </strong>
          </a>
          , it was all about trust and being honest with each other. We saw
          <strong>
           Cassia
          </strong>
          find some Playboys that
          <strong>
           Jason
          </strong>
          was hiding, so that went well.
          <strong>
           Danielle
          </strong>
          was hiding financial issues from
          <strong>
           Mohamed
          </strong>
          and their phones were turned off, so she finally had to tell him!
          <strong>
           Yamirs
          </strong>
          manager told him his band was done without him!Check out a recap of all the couples from tonight on
          <em>
           90 Day Fiance
          </em>
          Season 2 here:
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Chelsea &amp; Yamir 
          </strong>
          Oh Yamir, how I am such a fan of you! He met with some music producers in Chicago and they liked his energy and told him he needs to move to Chicago. Chelsea seemed hesitant and shes been moving all around the past few years and wants to be settled. Well, settle in Chicago, which is only three hours from your parents! I think she is going to do it though because she wants Yamir to be happy.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Danielle &amp; Mohamed 
          </strong>
          She is still trying to pay her phone bill, so they werent shown tonight!
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Brett &amp; Daya 
          </strong>
          Tonight seemed to focus on Daya and her relationship with Bretts daughter, who is not a fan of Daya, but is anyone? Brett works all the time, so apparently he wants a nanny and not a wife? Daya has troubles to start and gets locked out of the house by his daughter and she calls her a brat. Well, by the end of the episode she actually tried being nice to the daughter and paying attention to her and the daughter warmed up.imagine that!
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Danny &amp; Amy 
          </strong>
          I love Amy! Lots of attention on her tonight, as she got a little bachelorette party getaway with Dannys sisters. It was so sweet that they did it for her and they know she loves sunsets, so they setup a picnic in a field to see the sunset and eat and do gifts (yeah, I cried when Amy cried)! Then her Mom came and I love them both! Her mom seems so classy and gives off such a positive vibe. They went dress shopping together and Amy found the one, which made her mother cry and then made me cry!
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Justin &amp; Evelin 
          </strong>
          They had dinner with Justins brother and sister-in-law, who is a bitch and I want to slap the look off her face at times, but Justin is a douche. He tells them he wants to elope and just him and Evelin on the beach. They are not happy and say his mother will not be happy either. Justin doesnt seem to care!
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Jason &amp; Cassia 
          </strong>
          These two are just awkward together and bother me so much. To prove his love for her, he takes her to the aquarium and gets down on one knee and proposes. She said yes, but she has to say yes to get that green card! There is no connection between them and I cringe watching them together on
          <em>
           90 Day Fiance
          </em>
          Season 2!
         </p>
