               <h1 class="entry-title">
                On-Site Haiti: Discovering Bamboo Beach Bar in Le Nord
               </h1>
               <div class="single-share-holder">
                <div class="share-button-single">
                </div>
                <div class="share-button smaller">
                 <div data-text="On-Site Haiti: Discovering Bamboo Beach Bar in Le Nord" data-title="share" data-url="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/2013/10/02/on-site-haiti-discovering-bamboo-beach-bar-in-le-nord/" id="post-18524">
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
            </row>
           </div>
           <div class="entry-content">
            <row>
             <div class="span1 author-info smallest">
              <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/author/sbpr/">
               <img alt="Steve" src="http://uncommoncarib.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/themes/uncommon_caribbean_v2_0_27_modal/assets/img/authors/Steve.jpg" width="150px"/>
               Steve
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="span7 article-content">
              <div class="wps-seo-booster-headline" itemprop="headline">
               On-Site
               <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/destination/haiti/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/destination/haiti/', 'Haiti']);" title="Haiti">
                Haiti
               </a>
               : Discovering Bamboo
               <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/beach-bar/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/beach-bar/', 'Beach Bar']);" title="Beach Bar">
                Beach Bar
               </a>
               in Le Nord
              </div>
              <div itemprop="articleBody">
               <p>
                Beach Bars and Haiti dont go together too well in the minds of most people, but on my latest visit here just this past weekend, I discovered that they should.
               </p>
               <p>
                I made my discovery while entering the bay pictured above. I was on a charter boat with a group of other journalists exploring the lush, untamed northern coast of the country. Conditions were calm, though overcast and intermittently drizzly adding the type of
                <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/exotic/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/exotic/', 'exotic']);" title="exotic">
                 exotic
                </a>
                , sub-tropically sleepy and alluring feel you might sooner expect to find in Tahiti, or some other such Southeast Asia paradise.
               </p>
               <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_18527" style="width: 610px">
                <a href="http://uncommoncarib.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bb3.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bb3.jpg', '']);">
                 <img alt="Le Nord du Haiti | SBPR" class="size-full wp-image-18527" height="407" src="http://uncommoncarib.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bb3.jpg" width="610"/>
                </a>
                <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
                 Le Nord du Haiti | SBPR
                </figcaption>
               </figure>
               <p>
                This, however, is Haiti.
               </p>
               <p>
                So is this
               </p>
               <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_18528" style="width: 610px">
                <a href="http://uncommoncarib.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bb4.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bb4.jpg', '']);">
                 <img alt="Le Plage du Cadrasse, Northern Haiti | SBPR" class="size-full wp-image-18528" height="407" src="http://uncommoncarib.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bb4.jpg" width="610"/>
                </a>
                <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
                 Le Plage du Cadrasse, Northern Haiti | SBPR
                </figcaption>
               </figure>
               <p>
                You come to expect surprises adventuring around Haiti, but this beach, and more to the point, the tiny little beach bar on it, were right out of the blue for me.
               </p>
               <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_18529" style="width: 610px">
                <a href="http://uncommoncarib.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bb5.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bb5.jpg', '']);">
                 <img alt="Bamboo Bar on Cadrasse Beach, Haiti | SBPR" class="size-full wp-image-18529" height="407" src="http://uncommoncarib.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bb5.jpg" width="610"/>
                </a>
                <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
                 Bamboo Bar on Cadrasse Beach, Haiti | SBPR
                </figcaption>
               </figure>
               <p>
                Bamboo Bar is located right in the sand on Cadrasse Beach in the heart of the pristine and
                <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/secluded/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/secluded/', 'secluded']);" title="secluded">
                 secluded
                </a>
                bay pictured in the lead
                <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/photo/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/photo/', 'photo']);" title="photo">
                 photo
                </a>
                . Fringed by tall peaks tumbling straight down to the sea, the area is solely accessible by boat, so youre not apt to find too many folks usually
               </p>
               <p>
                You see, Bamboo Bar is not so uncommon to the more than 600,000 Royal
                <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/destination/caribbean/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/destination/caribbean/', 'Caribbean']);" title="Caribbean">
                 Caribbean
                </a>
                cruise passengers who stop off in nearby
                <a href="http://www.royalcaribbean.com/findacruise/ports/group/home.do?portCode=LAB" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.royalcaribbean.com/findacruise/ports/group/home.do?portCode=LAB', 'Labadee']);" target="_blank">
                 Labadee
                </a>
                each year. One of the more popular shore excursions ferries cruisers over to Cadrasse
                <em>
                 (Theyre told the name is Paradise Beach)
                </em>
                for some fun at Bamboo.
               </p>
               <p>
                No doubt, the uncommon traveler will better enjoy this place without the cruise crowds, as I was lucky enough to do during my visit. Our bartender, No-No, was friendly and happy to have a few stray visitors on a slow and dreary day. Prestige Beers were less than US$1 each, and the rum/fresh coconut
                <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/water/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/water/', 'water']);" title="water">
                 water
                </a>
                combo wasnt much more than that, leaving us in no mood to leave anytime soon.
               </p>
               <p>
                There were only us four visitors and No-No, liming, laughing,
                <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/dancing/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/dancing/', 'dancing']);" title="dancing">
                 dancing
                </a>
                , and drinking. The whole visit lasted less than 30 minutes, but it felt much longer for all the right reasons.
               </p>
               <p>
                Best way to get to Bamboo Bar is through our friends at
                <a href="https://www.facebook.com/sandbarisland" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.facebook.com/sandbarisland', 'Sandbar Island Tours']);" target="_blank">
                 Sandbar Island Tours
                </a>
                . They can accommodate groups large and small in one of two
                <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/boats/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/boats/', 'boats']);" title="boats">
                 boats
                </a>
                , and are a lot of fun to hang out with. There are also
                <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/local/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/tag/local/', 'local']);" title="local">
                 local
                </a>
                taxi boats that leave from Labadee to areas all along the north coast, though those overladen vessels appear to have more of a
                <a href="http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/2013/06/12/uncommon-attraction-the-lavishly-decorated-and-unsafe-tap-tap-buses-of-port-au-prince/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.uncommoncaribbean.com/2013/06/12/uncommon-attraction-the-lavishly-decorated-and-unsafe-tap-tap-buses-of-port-au-prince/', 'Tap-Tap']);" target="_blank">
                 Tap-Tap
                </a>
                feel that youll likely want to avoid
               </p>
              </div>
