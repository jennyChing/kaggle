       <h1 class="">
        Saturday-Night Hair: Sexy Summer Waves, Inspired by Khloe Kardashian
       </h1>
       <div class="byline">
        BY
        <a href="">
        </a>
        - 
                                June 7, 2013
       </div>
       <div class="heart-count" data-glm-id="2000000002295124" data-glm-stat="likes" data-glm-value="2">

       <div class="article">
        <div class="article-text">
         <p>
          Whether or not you
          <a href="http://www.glamour.com/about/keeping-up-with-the-kardashians">
           keep up with the Kardashians
          </a>
          , you have to admit the girls have some pretty amazing
          <a href="http://www.glamour.com/beauty/hair">
           hair
          </a>
          . Khloe Kardashian has been killing it in the style department lately (remember when we spotted her
          <a href="http://www.glamour.com/fashion/blogs/dressed/2013/05/khloe-kardashians-flaunting-he.html">
           looking next-level good in a Clover Canyon
          </a>
          dress?). I've been loving her wavy-hair looks lately, so I thought I'd share this how-to for sexy summer waves.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="khloe-kardashian-wavy-hair-1" data-dam-id="2000000002295074" src="http://www.glamour.com/images/beauty/2013/06/khloe-kardashian-wavy-hair-1-w352.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Here's how to get the look:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="wavy-hair-step-1" data-dam-id="2000000002295076" src="http://www.glamour.com/images/beauty/2013/06/wavy-hair-step-1-w352.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 1:
          </strong>
          Starting at the bottom, section your hair from side to side. Then take a smaller section and mist with a medium-hold hairspray.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="wavy-hair-step-2" data-dam-id="2000000002295118" src="http://www.glamour.com/images/beauty/2013/06/wavy-hair-step-2-square-w352.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 2:
          </strong>
          Wrap the section around a clampless curling iron, twisting the section as you go (this makes the wave more pronounced).
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="wavy-hair-step-3" data-dam-id="2000000002295075" src="http://www.glamour.com/images/beauty/2013/06/wavy-hair-step-3-square-w352.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 3:
          </strong>
          Now the fun part! Continue this exact technique all the way up your head, making sure to not disturb the sections that are cooling. When you reach the top, create a deep side part and then curl.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="wavy-hair-step-4" data-dam-id="2000000002295128" src="http://www.glamour.com/images/beauty/2013/06/wavy-hair-step-4-square-w352.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 4:
          </strong>
          After you have curled all your hair, brush it out starting underneath.
         </p>
         <p>

         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 5:
          </strong>
          Tuck the short side back, using a few bobby pins to secure.
