                  <div class="post-outer">
                   <div class="post hentry uncustomized-post-template" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
                    <meta content="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=kimskandkrea-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B00BI1PSGG" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Tie Dye Cupcakes
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-340731656159288162" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     We went shopping last week for a birthday cake for Mini Me's big day yesterday. He originally wanted a yellow cake with Funfetti frosting. Then he decided he wanted a chocolate cake with a yellow layer of cake on top. But when we got to the store, he saw the new
                     <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00BI1PSGG/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00BI1PSGG&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=kimskandkrea-20&amp;linkId=25VNYHUBRMBTW3VD" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                      Duff Goldman's Tie-Dye  Cake Mix
                     </a>
                     <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=kimskandkrea-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B00BI1PSGG" style="border: currentColor !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
                     , and decided that was, for sure this time, what he wanted.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-wXl9AUy35V0/U875MB6_ZZI/AAAAAAAALXo/RcbN5qp7EG0/s640/blogger-image-701290152.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="299" src="https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-wXl9AUy35V0/U875MB6_ZZI/AAAAAAAALXo/RcbN5qp7EG0/s400/blogger-image-701290152.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     The cake was a pretty cool design, but I just couldn't swallow paying $4.00 for a box of cake mix. That's crazy. Instead, we purchase a white cake and decided to DIY our own Tie Dye Cupcakes.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-fPXGQ5wkJ9s/U87Pa4N8UtI/AAAAAAAALXE/9jfG2qD61d8/s1600/Tie+Dye+Cupcake+DIY.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="White Cake Mix used for Tie Dye Cupcakes from Kandy Kreations" border="0" height="266" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-fPXGQ5wkJ9s/U87Pa4N8UtI/AAAAAAAALXE/9jfG2qD61d8/s1600/Tie+Dye+Cupcake+DIY.JPG" title="White Cake Mix used for Tie Dye Cupcakes from Kandy Kreations" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     We started by making a white cake mix using the egg whites to keep the white color pure for dying. I'm not sure what would happen if we used a yellow cake or used the whole cake recipe on the white mix. I may give it a try sometime, but we didn't here.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-3bYlwKdqtRA/U87PkHah3zI/AAAAAAAALXM/fxI9Snwlafg/s1600/Tie+Dye+Cupcake+Colors.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Dyeing Cake Mix colors for Tie Dye Cupcakes from Kandy Kreations" border="0" height="400" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-3bYlwKdqtRA/U87PkHah3zI/AAAAAAAALXM/fxI9Snwlafg/s1600/Tie+Dye+Cupcake+Colors.JPG" title="Dyeing Cake Mix colors for Tie Dye Cupcakes from Kandy Kreations" width="266"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     After the cake was mixed, we separated the batter into several different bowls. I let Mini Me play with the food coloring to dye to batter any color he wanted. He ended up making 8 different colors.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-gaYGsBp0hSk/U87QC8jrQ8I/AAAAAAAALXU/T0BooXEop-U/s1600/Tie+Dye+Cupcake+Batter.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Tie Dye Cupcakes from Kandy Kreations" border="0" height="266" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-gaYGsBp0hSk/U87QC8jrQ8I/AAAAAAAALXU/T0BooXEop-U/s1600/Tie+Dye+Cupcake+Batter.JPG" title="Tie Dye Cupcakes from Kandy Kreations" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     We lined the cupcake tins and proceeded to start adding blobs and dots and layers of colored batter to the cupcakes. I think this was Mini Me's favorite part. We had a lot of fun mixing the different colors.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-QzPQSAiy71A/U87QIb-a78I/AAAAAAAALXc/qnyQjYZZ3kc/s1600/Tie+Dye+Cupcakes.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Tie Dye Cupcakes from Kandy Kreations" border="0" height="266" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-QzPQSAiy71A/U87QIb-a78I/AAAAAAAALXc/qnyQjYZZ3kc/s1600/Tie+Dye+Cupcakes.JPG" title="Tie Dye Cupcakes from Kandy Kreations" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     Once the cupcakes were about 3/4 of the way full (we only got 18 cupcakes doing it this way), we cooked them as directed on the box mix.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     They turned out so cool!
