       <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog/author/farrahhedwat/">
        <img alt="Farrah Hedwat" src="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Farrah-100x100.jpg"/>
       </a>
       <p>
        <span>
         by
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog/author/farrahhedwat/" rel="author" title="Posts by Farrah Hedwat">
         Farrah Hedwat
        </a>
       </p>

      <!-- Categories + Comment Count -->
      <header>
       <p>
        <span>
         <i class="fa fa-folder-open">
         </i>
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog/destinations/usa/new-york/" rel="tag">
         New York
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog/tips/planning/" rel="tag">
         Planning
        </a>
       </p>
      </header>
      <div class="content">
       <!-- Content -->
       <p>
        <b>
         <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/thingstodo/new-york" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/thingstodo/new-york', 'New York']);" title="Things">
          New York
         </a>
         is amazing all year round, but around Christmas the magic goes into serious overdrive!
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        If youre jetting off to the Big Apple in the run-up to Christmas, there are some sights you just HAVE to get a snap of. Trust me, youll regret it if you go home without them!
       </p>
       <h3>
        The Christmas Trees
       </h3>
       <p>
        New York has some of the BEST Christmas trees youll ever see. Check out the gigantic one at the
        <b>
         Rockefeller Center Plaza
        </b>
        , the Wicked, Wild, and Wonderful origami tree at the
        <b>
         Museum of Natural History
        </b>
        and the
        <b>
         New York Stock Exchange
        </b>
        tree on Wall Street!
       </p>
       <h3>
        Macys Santaland
       </h3>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/macys.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/macys.jpg', '']);">
         <img alt="macys" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1031" src="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/macys.jpg"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Fan of the film Miracle on 34th Street? Dont miss the opportunity to have your photo taken with Santa at
        <b>
         Macys Santaland
        </b>
        .
       </p>
       <h3>
        The bright lights of Broadway
       </h3>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/bright.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/bright.jpg', '']);">
         <img alt="bright" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1032" src="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/bright.jpg"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Youre in New York  a photo of the bright lights of
        <b>
         Broadway
        </b>
        is compulsory! And while youre there, why not get ultra-festive by going to see a Christmas-themed show?
       </p>
       <h3>
        The Big Piano
       </h3>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/big.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/big.jpg', '']);">
         <img alt="big" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1033" src="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/big.jpg"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        The famous toy shop
        <b>
         FAO Schwarz
        </b>
        is a wonderland of Christmas shopping for kids (and big kids!)  Dont forget to have your taken on the Big Piano from the film Big.
       </p>
       <h3>
        That world-famous skyline
       </h3>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/skyline.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/skyline.jpg', '']);">
         <img alt="skyline" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1034" src="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/skyline.jpg"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Travel up to the
        <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/tickets/top-of-the-rock" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/tickets/top-of-the-rock', 'Top of the Rock']);" target="_blank" title="Top of the Rock">
         Top of the Rock
        </a>
        , for one of the best views you could get of that incredible skyline and the twinkling Christmas lights below.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Ice Skaters
       </h3>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/ice.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/ice.jpg', '']);">
         <img alt="ice" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1035" src="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/blog2/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/ice.jpg"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        The iconic
        <b>
         Rockefeller ice rink
        </b>
        looks stunning, but is so busy you might just need to watch and snap some pics. If you actually want to get in on the action, try the
        <a href="http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/tickets/wollman-rink-ice-skating-central-park" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.attractiontix.co.uk/tickets/wollman-rink-ice-skating-central-park', 'Wollman Rink']);" target="_blank" title="Wollman Rink">
         Wollman Rink
        </a>
        in Central Park.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Send us your New York holiday snaps and WIN a NYC Goody Bag!
       </h2>
       <p>
        These are our favourite NYC snaps  but do you think you can do better??
       </p>
       <p>
        Send us your favourite photo from the Big Apple, telling us where it was taken, and you could win a fab New York goody bag!
       </p>
       <h3>
        Whats in the bag?
       </h3>
       <p>
        <b>
         Your goody bag prize will contain:
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        3 DVDs (set in New York of course!):
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Miracle on 34th Street
        </li>
        <li>
         Sex and the City: The Movie
        </li>
        <li>
         I Am Legend
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        A New York 2014 Wall Calendar
       </p>
       <p>
        An I Heart NY Coffee Mug
       </p>
       <p>
        Plus a 25 AttractionTix voucher!
       </p>
       <h3>
        How to enter:
       </h3>
       <p>
        Send your photos by email to
        <em>
         competitions@attractiontix.co.uk
        </em>
        by 12th December.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our favourites will be featured on our Facebook page in the coming weeks, and well announce our overall winner on 13th December 2013.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Good Luck!
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>

         See the

          Competition terms and conditions
