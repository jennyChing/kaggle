       <h2>
        <span class="mw-headline" id="Cirque_du_Soleil_Performer_Job_Overview">
         Cirque du Soleil Performer Job Overview
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        The job of a Cirque du Soleil performer involves learning complex choreography that accompanies the company's signature performances. In each show, the kind of performance can vary according to the theme. Artists who perform in a musical show may have to learn specific dances. For example, the Cirque du Soleil show "La Nouba", which is performed at
        <a class="external text" href="http://www.besttravelcouponguide.com/Walt_Disney_World_Deals" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Walt Disney World
        </a>
        in Orlando, Florida, highlights clowns, while the show "One" is set to the music of Michael Jackson.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
         <a href="#cite_note-3">
          [3]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </p>
       <p>
        The schedule for a Cirque du Soleil performer can be rigorous. Some professionals may have to perform in as many as 475 shows per year.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cnn_4-0">
         <a href="#cite_note-cnn-4">
          [4]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </p>
       <h3>
        <span class="mw-headline" id="Types_of_Cirque_du_Soleil_Performers">
         Types of Cirque du Soleil Performers
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p>
        Cirque du Soleil employs several kinds of performers. Along with acrobats, the circus features balancing artists, contortionists, actors,
        <a href="/How_to_Become_a_Singer" title="How to Become a Singer">
         singers
        </a>
        , musicians, dancers, clowns, and athletes. Cirque du Soleil circus performer job openings include positions in aerial arts, wheel performing, juggling, and hula hooping.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5">
         <a href="#cite_note-5">
          [5]
         </a>
        </sup>
        The circus also hires individuals who have other talents, such as martial arts, skipping, and experience in extreme sports.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6">
         <a href="#cite_note-6">
          [6]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </p>
       <p>

       <h2>
        <span class="mw-headline" id="Cirque_du_Soleil_Performer_Education">
         Cirque du Soleil Performer Education
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Becoming a Cirque du Soleil performer involves learning how to perform the show's particular style of circus arts. After choosing a specific discipline, an aspiring Cirque du Soleil performer must complete years of education in their chosen field. The company allows interested individuals to apply online for their desired discipline.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7">
         <a href="#cite_note-7">
          [7]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </p>
       <p>
        Once hired, performers must learn the company's performing numbers, including any choreography and costume changes that are involved. Artists must also learn how to apply their own
        <a href="/How_to_Become_a_Makeup_Artist" title="How to Become a Makeup Artist">
         makeup
        </a>
        before each performance.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8">
         <a href="#cite_note-8">
          [8]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span class="mw-headline" id="Cirque_du_Soleil_Performer_Training">
         Cirque du Soleil Performer Training
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Cirque du Soleil performers train extensively for each performance. Performers are required to attend mandatory training sessions and they do cross-training several times a week to maintain their overall physical strength. They also complete a lengthy warm-up before the shows.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9">
         <a href="#cite_note-9">
          [9]
         </a>
        </sup>
        Many Cirque du Soleil performers also practice nutrition habits such as clean eating and gymnastic training.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10">
         <a href="#cite_note-10">
          [10]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </p>
