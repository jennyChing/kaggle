         <p>
          Bad news, folks. The
          <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/aponline/2015/04/14/world/europe/ap-eu-odd-netherlands-chimp-drone-buster.html?smid=tw-share&amp;_r=0">
           inevitable ape uprising
          </a>
          Hollywood has been warning us about since 1968 has begun.
         </p>
         <p>
          First blood has been drawn by a chimpanzee named Tushi, currently in custody at the Dutch Burgers Zoo.
         </p>
         <p>
          The 23-year-old ape bided her time and calmly waited for the zoo-operated drone to get within striking distance. When it flew within reach, she brought it down with a stick.
         </p>
         <p>
          Clearly our advanced technology will be useless in the war to come. Better just surrender now. Maybe theyll go easy on us.
         </p>
