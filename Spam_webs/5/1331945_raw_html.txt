          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.migrationideas.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/inner_banner_open.jpg">
            <img alt="Canada Work Permit " class=" wp-image-403 alignleft" height="75" src="http://www.migrationideas.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/inner_banner_open-300x75.jpg" style="margin: 5px;" title="Canada Work Permit" width="303"/>

           Canada is a leading country that offers excellent employment to overseas nationals to live and work in Canada in order to meet the labor shortages in Canada. Canadas Citizenship and Immigration Department has initiating several programs to entice foreign employees to work in Canada. Every year, nearly 200,000 overseas skilled employees arrive to work in Canada on temporary work permits.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Canada Work Permit:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Canada Work Permit program approves work permits to entitled overseas employees who desire to temporarily work in Canada, offers details regarding application process, and grants visitor records to business travelers. Canada Work Visa is a temporary visa, structured for global skilled employees with a valid employment offer from Canadian employer.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Canada Work Visa:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Canada Work Visa allows overseas nationals to enter and work in Canada for a specific period of time i.e. till the employment offer is valid or employment agreement is intact.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <strong>
            Canada Work Permit Requirements:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           In order to obtain Canada Work Permit applicants need to fulfill the necessary requirements such as:
          </p>
          <ul style="text-align: justify;">
           <li>
            Applicants should have valid passport
           </li>
           <li>
            Employment offer letter in prior to the visa application
           </li>
           <li>
            Employer should hold a labor market opinion (LMO)
           </li>
           <li>
            Applicants should also meet the required health and character requirements
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           After fulfilling the necessary requirements applicants need can apply for the Canada Work Visa by following all the Canada immigration rules. If immigration process is completed successfully then the Canada Work Visa will be issued.
           <br/>
           <strong>
            <br/>
            Canada Work Permit Features:
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul style="text-align: justify;">
           <li>
            Visa holders are permitted to Canada Work Visa
           </li>
           <li>
            Eligible to engage in study courses
           </li>
           <li>
            Visa holders are allowed to travel throughout the Canada
           </li>
           <li>
            Canada Work Visa holder can sponsor his or her spouse and dependent children to join in Canada.
           </li>
          </ul>
