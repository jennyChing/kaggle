       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Valentines Day  Chevron &amp; Sparkle Wreath (tutorial)
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2012-01-17T01:11:08+00:00">
         January 17, 2012
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="pw-widget pw-size-large pw-horizontal" pw:image="" pw:title="Valentines Day  Chevron &amp; Sparkle Wreath (tutorial)" pw:url="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html">

        <input data-frizzlyhovercontainer="" data-frizzlypostcontainer="" data-frizzlyposttitle="Valentines Day  Chevron &amp; Sparkle Wreath (tutorial)" data-frizzlyposturl="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html" type="hidden" value=""/>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html/chevron-sparkles-chevron-wreath" rel="attachment wp-att-31343">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/tatertotsandjello.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Chevron-Sparkles-Chevron-Wreath-.jpg?resize=576%2C386" title="Chevron &amp; Sparkles Chevron Wreath"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          I love making wreaths!!
         </strong>
         Maybe its a sickness. But each year I want to make a new wreath for each holiday. And
         <span style="color: #ff00ff;">
          <strong>
           Valentines Day 
          </strong>
         </span>
         is becoming one of my favorite holidays to decorate for.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html/valentine-chevron-and-glitter-wreath" rel="attachment wp-att-31342">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/tatertotsandjello.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Valentine-Chevron-and-Glitter-Wreath.jpg?resize=576%2C386" title="Valentine Chevron and Glitter Wreath"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         In the fall I went crazy one night and ordered every single color of chevron I could find. And one of my favorite ones was the
         <span style="color: #ff00ff;">
          <strong>
           Hot Pink Chevron
          </strong>
         </span>
         
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html/pink-and-white-chevron-material" rel="attachment wp-att-31333">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-31333" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/tatertotsandjello.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/pink-and-white-chevron-material.jpg?resize=300%2C240" title="pink and white chevron material"/>
         </a>
         from
         <a href="http://premierprintsfabric.com/zzgcpw.shtml">
          <strong>
           Premier Fabrics
          </strong>
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          To make this wreath:
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <span id="more-31332">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I started off with a Dollar Store Wreath
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html/dollar-store-wreath-form" rel="attachment wp-att-31334">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-31334" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/tatertotsandjello.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/dollar-store-wreath-form.jpg?resize=300%2C200" title="dollar store wreath form"/>
         </a>
         Then I wrapped some quilt batting around the form. And cut a 3-inch wide strip of chevron fabric and wrapped it around the wreath form.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html/wrap-the-fabric-around-wreath" rel="attachment wp-att-31335">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-31335" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/tatertotsandjello.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/wrap-the-fabric-around-wreath.jpg?resize=420%2C281" title="wrap the fabric around wreath"/>
         </a>
         Then I took one of those foam packages of hearts  they are about $2 at your craft store.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html/foam-hearts" rel="attachment wp-att-31336">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-31336" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/tatertotsandjello.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/foam-hearts-.jpg?resize=420%2C533" title="foam hearts"/>
         </a>
         I bought two different sizes of the hearts  one small and the other a little bigger. Then to make them 3-D I folded them a little bigger and sewed them.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html/sew-the-hearts-in-half" rel="attachment wp-att-31337">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-31337" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/tatertotsandjello.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/sew-the-hearts-in-half.jpg?resize=480%2C717" title="sew the hearts in half"/>
         </a>
         Then I glued them on the wreath. I started with the big hearts and then filled in with the smaller ones.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html/sew-hearts-for-a-3-d-effect" rel="attachment wp-att-31338">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-31338" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/tatertotsandjello.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/sew-hearts-for-a-3-d-effect-.jpg?resize=420%2C627" title="sew hearts for a 3-d effect"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html/chevron-and-sparkle-wreath-from-the-side" rel="attachment wp-att-31339">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-31339" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/tatertotsandjello.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/chevron-and-sparkle-wreath-from-the-side.jpg?resize=600%2C402" title="chevron and sparkle wreath from the side"/>
         </a>
         Then I took an old window that I had and taped some polka-dotted cellophane to the back.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html/tape-wrapping-paper-to-the-back-of-the-window" rel="attachment wp-att-31341">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-31341" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/tatertotsandjello.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/tape-wrapping-paper-to-the-back-of-the-window.jpg?resize=600%2C402" title="tape wrapping paper to the back of the window"/>
         </a>
         You could also add some colored paper to the back. Or even some doilies or fabric for color.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/01/valentines-day-%e2%99%a5-chevron-sparkle-wreath-tutorial.html/chevron-and-sparkle-valentine-wreath" rel="attachment wp-att-31340">
          <img alt="" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/tatertotsandjello.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/chevron-and-sparkle-valentine-wreath.jpg?resize=480%2C717" title="chevron and sparkle valentine wreath"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Its a really easy and fun wreath for the
         <span style="color: #ff00ff;">
          
         </span>
         Holiday!
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #ff00ff;">
          <strong>
           Have a Lovely Day!!
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         xoxxoox
        </p>
