                 <h2 class="date-header">
                  <span>
                   8/14/2014
                  </span>
                 </h2>
                 <div class="date-posts">
                  <div class="post-outer">
                   <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
                    <meta content="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-8UI2NsulxpE/U-vI8vXqVcI/AAAAAAAAAPI/HNyRp6My21g/s1600/%7B9138CEB0-4242-43B4-8C9C-54D6ACDD11EB%7D.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Cheesecake ice cream cake
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="addthis_sharing_toolbox">
                     </div>
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-624419005940555135" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div dir="rtl" style="text-align: right;" trbidi="on">
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <strong>
                         "
                        </strong>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-8UI2NsulxpE/U-vI8vXqVcI/AAAAAAAAAPI/HNyRp6My21g/s1600/%7B9138CEB0-4242-43B4-8C9C-54D6ACDD11EB%7D.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-8UI2NsulxpE/U-vI8vXqVcI/AAAAAAAAAPI/HNyRp6My21g/s1600/%7B9138CEB0-4242-43B4-8C9C-54D6ACDD11EB%7D.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       I love ice cream cakes very much and I truly enjoy those rich and full of taste cheesecakes.
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       I saw a recipe of a cake with cream cheese and whipping cream and really wanted to make it, however I didn't had all the ingredients so I just threwthe ingredients I had to a bowland this refreshing
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       and so tasty dessert came out.
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                       </span>
                       <a name="more">
                       </a>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-AvmjhMAaY4k/U-vJEcjdunI/AAAAAAAAAPQ/kIqUTy8_Eqs/s1600/processimg.asp.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="298" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-AvmjhMAaY4k/U-vJEcjdunI/AAAAAAAAAPQ/kIqUTy8_Eqs/s1600/processimg.asp.jpg" width="400"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       The recipe is very easy and the only thing that you need is time for it to freeze and some patience :-)
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <strong>
                        Ingredients
                       </strong>
                       :
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       1 cup (180 ml)
                       <span class="short_text" id="result_box" lang="en">
                        <span class="hps">
                         Rice Krispy's
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span class="short_text" lang="en">
                        <span class="hps">
                         250ml sweetened whipping cream (or whipping cream and sugar to taste)
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span class="short_text" lang="en">
                        <span class="hps">
                         1 Heaping Tbsp. instant pudding powder vanilla flavored
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span class="short_text" lang="en">
                        <span class="hps">
                         2 Tbsp. cream cheese
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span class="short_text" lang="en">
                        <span class="hps">
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span class="short_text" lang="en">
                        <span class="hps">
                         <strong>
                          Preparation
                         </strong>
                         :
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span class="short_text" lang="en">
                        <span class="hps">
                         Whip together the whipping cream and instant pudding.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span class="short_text" lang="en">
                        <span class="hps">
                         Add the cream cheese and whip again.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span class="short_text" lang="en">
                        <span class="hps">
                         Sprinkle half the amount of the rice Krispy's on the bottom of a small English cake pan.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span class="short_text" lang="en">
                        <span class="hps">
                         Pour the cheese mixture to the pan andSprinkleit with the rest of the rice Krispy's.
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
                       <span class="short_text" lang="en">
                        <span class="hps">
                         <span style="font-size: large;">
                          <span style="font-size: small;">
                           Put in the freezer until all set.
                          </span>
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
