         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Top 7 DIY Trends To Look Out For in 2015
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <span class="posted-on">
           Posted on
           <a href="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/top-7-diy-trends-for-2015/" rel="bookmark">
            <time class="entry-date published" datetime="2015-02-09T16:55:56+00:00">
             February 9, 2015
            </time>
           </a>
          </span>
         </div>
         <!-- .entry-meta -->
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p class="Standard">
          <b>
           <span style="font-size: 15.0pt;">
            Top DIY trends to look out for in 2015
           </span>
          </b>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          If youre thinking of changing things up in your home this year, its a good idea to check out the trends in the home decor world for inspiration. Some trends lack longevity and could be a risk but we think these are the best of the bunch. Of course you cant predict it all and theres every chance the shag pile and lava lamp will make a triumphant return but lets hope not. Heres ourTop 7 DIY Trends To Look Out For in 2015.
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <b>
           1. Upcycling and recycling
          </b>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          This has never gone away among the more thrifty and diligent amongst us but this year will see many people roll up their sleeves and attempt some form of upcycling. Whether you decide to upholster an old armchair or fashion a table out of old wooden crates, make it a resolution to attempt some masterpiece to show off in your home however small. The quirkier the better.
         </p>
         <p class="Standard" style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Upcycling.jpg">
           <img alt="Upcycling" class="aligncenter wp-image-2269" height="376" src="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Upcycling.jpg" style="border: 2px solid #000000;" width="501"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard" style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://data.whicdn.com/images/3786640/upcycled-guitar-case-bookshelf_large.jpg" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://data.whicdn.com/images/3786640/upcycled-guitar-case-bookshelf_large.jpg', 'Image Source');" rel="nofollow">
           Image Source
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <b>
           2. Knitting
          </b>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          Knitting is a hobby that has been steadily growing followers over the years, at times popping up in the form of retro-chic evening classes and many appearances of handmade scarves at Christmas. You dont have to stop at a scarf though you could try blankets, cushions or wall art. If you made it yourself its impressive basically and nobody is allowed to say otherwise.
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <a href="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Knitting.jpg">
           <img alt="Knitting" class="aligncenter wp-image-2265" height="785" src="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Knitting.jpg" style="border: 2px solid #000000;" width="503"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard" style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://data3.whicdn.com/images/97508229/large.jpg" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://data3.whicdn.com/images/97508229/large.jpg', 'Image Source');" rel="nofollow">
           Image Source
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <b>
           3. Wallpaper
          </b>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          The DIY trend for painting has dominated over the past few years with every wannabe decorator trying their hand. As a result the notion of wallpapering seemed too retro and got pushed into the shadows. But its coming back. Just papering one wall in a room is a cool compromise and will add texture to a room.
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <a href="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Wallpaper.jpg">
           <img alt="Wallpaper" class="aligncenter wp-image-2261" height="375" src="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Wallpaper.jpg" style="border: 2px solid #000000;" width="501"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard" style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.apartmenttherapy.com/uimages/chicago/4-1-08wallpaper1.jpg" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.apartmenttherapy.com/uimages/chicago/4-1-08wallpaper1.jpg', 'Image Source');" rel="nofollow">
           Image Source
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <b>
           4. Mid-century Modern
          </b>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          The movement in architecture and furniture spanned the1930s-1960s and was born out of a lack of materials and a need to be innovative. Its never really gone away but is more popular than ever as people more and more are opting to flavour their homes with a touch of nostalgia. Watch Mad Men for inspiration and try and get your hands on something like the famous Eames Lounge Chair or an Egg Chair.
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <a href="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Mid-Century-Modern.jpg">
           <img alt="Mid-Century Modern" class="aligncenter wp-image-2266" height="383" src="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Mid-Century-Modern.jpg" style="border: 2px solid #000000;" width="510"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard" style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.evokemodern.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/EVOKE_credenza.jpg" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.evokemodern.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/EVOKE_credenza.jpg', 'Image Source');" rel="nofollow">
           Image Source
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <b>
           5. Gold
          </b>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          Gold is back in a big way and who doesnt like gold? Copious amounts of gold decorating in 80s may have scared some people off for good but touches of gold, gold leaf and brassier, warmer hues about the home are set to be a good look this year. Think bathroom fittings and intricate gold light fittings.
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <a href="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/gold-bathroom-accessories.jpg">
           <img alt="gold-bathroom-accessories" class="aligncenter wp-image-2264" height="599" src="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/gold-bathroom-accessories.jpg" style="border: 2px solid #000000;" width="449"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard" style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/gold-bathroom-accessories.jpg" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/gold-bathroom-accessories.jpg', 'Image Source');" rel="nofollow">
           Image Source
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <b>
          </b>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <b>
           6. Bookshelves
          </b>
          <b>
          </b>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          While DVDs are a thing of the past and many people reach for the Kindle before bed, books are still going to be around for a long time and there is nothing more impressive than a large collection of books in a home. Makes you look intelligent if nothing else.
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <a href="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Bookshelves.jpg">
           <img alt="Bookshelves" class="aligncenter wp-image-2262" height="375" src="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Bookshelves.jpg" style="border: 2px solid #000000;" width="520"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard" style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://cdn.designhomes.pics/design/home4lifenow.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/build-a-bookshelf.jpg" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://cdn.designhomes.pics/design/home4lifenow.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/build-a-bookshelf.jpg', 'Image Source');" rel="nofollow">
           Image Source
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard" style="text-align: left;">
          <b>
           7. Natural and sustainable
          </b>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard" style="text-align: left;">
          Think green. Its never been more urgent than now to look towards sustainable living for inspiration. Expect to see an emergence of natural materials, recycled/reclaimed materials and a lot more greenery indoors.
         </p>
         <p class="Standard">
          <a href="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Sustainable-living.jpg">
           <img alt="Sustainable living" class="aligncenter wp-image-2267" height="338" src="http://www.chadwicks.ie/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Sustainable-living.jpg" style="border: 2px solid #000000;" width="520"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="Standard" style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://energetechs.com/ener_get473/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/sustianable-interior2-600x390.jpg" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://energetechs.com/ener_get473/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/sustianable-interior2-600x390.jpg', 'Image Source');" rel="nofollow">
           Image Source
          </a>
         </p>
