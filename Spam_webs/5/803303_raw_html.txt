             <section class="article-content">
              <p>
               If your homepage just loads to
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/google/">
                Google
               </a>
               , you're missing a trick. You could start every day by learning something new, getting motivated by the creative arts or enjoying an inspiring visual.
              </p>
              <p>
               We've found some awesome options to save as your homepage, so hit up your browser's settings and get galvanized each and every time you go online.
              </p>
              <div class="see-also">
               <p>
                See also:
                <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2014/10/24/time-saving-apps/">
                 8 Apps to Help You Get More Done in Less Time
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <p>
               Take a look through our suggestions below. Do you Internet with something even more awesome? Share it in the comments.
              </p>
              <h3>
               1.
               <a href="http://www.merriam-webster.com/word-of-the-day/" target="_blank">
                Merriam-Webster's "Word of the Day"
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
               <figure class="image">
                <br/>
                <img alt="w3" class="" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="" data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!5c75" data-image="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzlkL3czLjJmZjhmLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/6826d671/74d/w3.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="691" src="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzlkL3czLjJmZjhmLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/6826d671/74d/w3.jpg" title="w3"/>
                <br/>
               </figure>
               <br/>
              </center>
              <p>
               Any language-lovers might want to consider a "Word of the Day" option. Increase your vocab by checking in with Merriam-Webster every morning.
              </p>
              <h3>
               2.
               <a href="https://www.google.com/doodles" target="_blank">
                Google Doodles
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
               <figure class="image">
                <br/>
                <img alt="w4" class="" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="" data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!4212" data-image="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzNlL3c0LmM2MTY5LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/0c90565c/7ef/w4.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="1048" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzNlL3c0LmM2MTY5LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/0c90565c/7ef/w4.jpg" title="w4"/>
                <br/>
               </figure>
               <br/>
              </center>
              <p>
               If, like us, you love Google Doodles, you can hit up the dedicated page to see what Google is celebrating each day.
              </p>
              <h3>
               3.
               <a href="http://apod.nasa.gov/apod/astropix.html" target="_blank">
                Astronomy Picture of the Day
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
               <figure class="image">
                <br/>
                <img alt="w5" class="" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="" data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!a8fb" data-image="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzIzL3c1LmJmNjE0LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/58aec3c8/2af/w5.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="1021" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzIzL3c1LmJmNjE0LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/58aec3c8/2af/w5.jpg" title="w5"/>
                <br/>
               </figure>
               <br/>
              </center>
              <p>
               Star-gaze from the comfort of your desk with a fresh astronomical image  complete with explanation from pro astronomer  delivered to your desktop each morning.
              </p>
              <h3>
               4.
               <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:Random" target="_blank">
                Wikipedia's "Random Article"
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
               <figure class="image">
                <br/>
                <img alt="w1" class="" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="" data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!688b" data-image="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2L2E5L3cxLmFiNzE1LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/43a0802d/a48/w1.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="1045" src="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2L2E5L3cxLmFiNzE1LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/43a0802d/a48/w1.jpg" title="w1"/>
                <br/>
               </figure>
               <br/>
              </center>
              <p>
               If you like a little randomness in your day, why not add some arbitrary chance with Wikipedia's "Random Article" option?
              </p>
              <h3>
               5.
               <a href="http://www.thecurrent.org/collection/song-of-the-day" target="_blank">
                <em>
                 The Current
                </em>
                's "Song of the Day"
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
               <figure class="image">
                <br/>
                <img alt="w6" class="" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="" data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!851b" data-image="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzhhL3c2LmY2YzRhLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/fc94d184/dd6/w6.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="968" src="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzhhL3c2LmY2YzRhLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/fc94d184/dd6/w6.jpg" title="w6"/>
                <br/>
               </figure>
               <br/>
              </center>
              <br/>
              <p>
               Minnesota's
               <em>
                The Current
               </em>
               offers a new, independent and unreleased song every weekday. Tune in and start your day with a musical twist.
              </p>
              <h3>
               6.
               <a href="http://www.randomyoutubecomment.com/" target="_blank">
                Random YouTube Comment
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
               <figure class="image">
                <br/>
                <img alt="w10" class="" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="" data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!42da" data-image="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2Lzg2L3cxMC42ZjZlYy5qcGcKcAl0aHVtYgkxMjAweDk2MDA-/2c8ace18/e25/w10.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="1318" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2Lzg2L3cxMC42ZjZlYy5qcGcKcAl0aHVtYgkxMjAweDk2MDA-/2c8ace18/e25/w10.jpg" title="w10"/>
                <br/>
               </figure>
               <br/>
              </center>
              <p>
               Strangely fascinating, this site loads up a random YouTube comment every time it refreshes. Warning  you'll find yourself clicking through a few before you start your online day.
              </p>
              <h3>
               7.
               <a href="http://www.poets.org/poetsorg/poem-day" target="_blank">
                Poets.Org's "Poem-a-Day"
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
               <figure class="image">
                <br/>
                <img alt="w9" class="" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="" data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!d27e" data-image="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2L2VmL3c5LjZhZDM0LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/e2859600/9dc/w9.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="748" src="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2L2VmL3c5LjZhZDM0LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/e2859600/9dc/w9.jpg" title="w9"/>
                <br/>
               </figure>
               <br/>
              </center>
              <p>
               Ideal for anyone who wants to read more poetry but can't find the time, this will bring you an original, previously unpublished poem on weekdays and classic poems on weekends.
              </p>
              <h3>
               8.
               <a href="http://www.todayifoundout.com/index.php/2009/12/click-to-go-to-a-random-interesting-fact-article/" target="_blank">
                <em>
                 Today I Found Out
                </em>
                's "Surprise" Article
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
               <figure class="image">
                <br/>
                <img alt="w8" class="" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="" data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!fcac" data-image="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzJiL3c4LjQwOWY4LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/065587b3/84d/w8.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="590" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzJiL3c4LjQwOWY4LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/065587b3/84d/w8.jpg" title="w8"/>
                <br/>
               </figure>
               <br/>
              </center>
              <p>
               Perfect for fans of factoids, you can find out something new every darn day of the week with "Today I Found Out."
              </p>
              <h3>
               9.
               <a href="https://photography.nationalgeographic.com/photo-of-the-day/" target="_blank">
                <em>
                 National Geographic
                </em>
                's Photo of the Day
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
              </center>
              <figure class="image">
               <p>
                <img alt="w7" class="" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="" data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!c58e" data-image="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzNjL3c3LmMwYzJlLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/5f410c86/341/w7.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="698" src="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzNjL3c3LmMwYzJlLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/5f410c86/341/w7.jpg" title="w7"/>
               </p>
              </figure>
              <p>
               Give your eyeballs a treat every time you log on with good old
               <em>
                National Geographic
               </em>
               's "Photo of the Day."
              </p>
              <h3>
               10.
               <a href="http://dailykitten.com/" target="_blank">
                Daily Kitten
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
              </p>
              <center>
               <figure class="image">
                <br/>
                <img alt="w2" class="" data-caption="" data-credit-link="" data-credit-name="" data-credit-provider="" data-fragment="m!321f" data-image="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzYyL3cyLmRiMDE2LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/ebb55bbf/38f/w2.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="900" src="http://rack.1.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDE1LzAxLzE2LzYyL3cyLmRiMDE2LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/ebb55bbf/38f/w2.jpg" title="w2"/>
                <br/>
               </figure>
               <br/>

               Finally, you can enjoy a regular dose of cuteness with the excellent Daily Kitten site. It does what it says, delivering a fresh kitty for your delectation and delight every morning at 10:07 a.m. ET.
