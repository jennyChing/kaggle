      <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
       <span itemprop="name">
        10 Most Tallest Buildings of World
       </span>
      </h1>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        There are so many beautiful and high buildings in the world when we see all these we are shocked. Here 10 tallest buildings of world are described.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_144" style="width: 1930px">
        <a href="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/burj_khalifa_wallpaper_Most-Tallest-Buildings-of-World.jpeg">
         <img alt="burj_khalifa_wallpaper_Most Tallest Buildings of World" class="size-full wp-image-144" height="1200" src="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/burj_khalifa_wallpaper_Most-Tallest-Buildings-of-World.jpeg" width="1920"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Burj Khalifa
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Burj Khalifa
        </strong>
        is the tallest building in Dubai in United Arab Emirates, whos designed, was to be made by Skidmore. Height if this building is 828 meter, floors are 163.This is the popular building in Dubai and most of the people visit this building. Construction of this building was started in 2013.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/shanghai-tower-wallpaper.jpg">
         <img alt="shanghai tower wallpaper" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-146" height="2048" src="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/shanghai-tower-wallpaper.jpg" width="2048"/>
        </a>
        <strong>
         Shangai Tower
        </strong>
        It is at number 2 in the world and was to be expected that it is to be open in 2015.It is situated in Shangai. Its construction was to be started in 2008.This is 632 meters high. It is less high to the Burj Hlaifa. It has completed its destination
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Makah-Clock-Royal-Tower-Pictures.jpg">
         <img alt="Makah Clock Royal Tower Pictures" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-148" height="680" src="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Makah-Clock-Royal-Tower-Pictures.jpg" width="917"/>
        </a>
        <strong>
         Makah Clock Royal Tower
        </strong>
        Third tallest building of the world is Makah Clock Royal Tower. Clock is so large you can see it from 25 kilometers distance. Its height is 601 meter and was completed in 2012.This building is in Saudi Arabia.
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/world-trade-center-now_wallpapers.jpg">
         <img alt="world trade center now_wallpapers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-149" height="1257" src="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/world-trade-center-now_wallpapers.jpg" width="1692"/>
        </a>
        <strong>
         One World Trade Center
        </strong>
        It is at the top four and tallest building. Its height is 531.It is affected in 2001 by terrorist attacks. It was to be decided to build again. This building has 104 stories. David Childs is the designer of this building.
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/ctf-finance-center.jpg">
         <img alt="ctf finance center wallpapers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-150" height="699" src="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/ctf-finance-center.jpg" width="980"/>
        </a>
        <strong>
         CTF Finance Center
        </strong>
        It is on the number of fifth and skyscraper. It is to be situated in Guangzhou. Its height is 530 meters, 500 apartments and 350 room hotels. Its height is less then Taipei.
        <br/>
        Taipei
        <br/>
        Height of Taipei is 509 meters. It is at the number if six in the tallest buildings. It was the tallest building in 2004 to 2007.This building has won the award Emporis Skyscraper. Construction was to be started in 2009 and was opened in 2014.
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/shanghai-world-finance-center-wallpapers.jpg">
         <img alt="shanghai world finance center wallpapers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-151" height="1228" src="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/shanghai-world-finance-center-wallpapers.jpg" width="1024"/>
        </a>
        <strong>
         Shangai World Finance Center
        </strong>
        Construction was to be started in 1997 and visitors were gone to see this building in 2008.It has lees height then Taipei. This building is famous because of its shape and design.
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/international-commerce-center.jpg">
         <img alt="international commerce center pictures" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-155" height="1038" src="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/international-commerce-center.jpg" width="912"/>
        </a>
        <strong>
         International Commerce Center
        </strong>
        It is in the Hong Kong. Height of this building is 484 meters. It has 13 floors and tallest building in Hong Kong.
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/petronas_towers_kuala_lumpur_wallpapers.jpg">
          <img alt="petronas_towers_kuala_lumpur_wallpapers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-152" height="1152" src="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/petronas_towers_kuala_lumpur_wallpapers.jpg" width="1152"/>
         </a>
         Petronas towers
        </strong>
        It is situated in Kuala Lumpur. In Malaysia it is the popular building. It was best skyscraper since 2003.This building is elegant. In three years this was to be completed.1.6 billion dollars are its cost.
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/zifeng-tower.jpg">
         <img alt="zifeng tower wallpapers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-153" height="1917" src="http://www.picnwallpapers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/zifeng-tower.jpg" width="1200"/>
        </a>
        <strong>
         Zifeng Tower
        </strong>
        It has 89 story building. It is situated in china. In five years from 2005 to 2010 was completed. Its height is 450 meters and, 66 floors and five basements.
       </p>
