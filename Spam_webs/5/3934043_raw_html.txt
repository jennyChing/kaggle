         <br/>
         <h2 id="about">
          About
         </h2>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Redneck Randal
          </strong>
          is an
          <a href="/memes/advice-animals">
           advice animal
          </a>
          image macro series centered around a picture of a mustached young man wearing a sleeveless American pride t-shirt and posing with two thumbs up. The captions typically involve poking fun at the lifestyle of so-called American rednecks
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr2">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn2">
            [2]
           </a>
          </sup>
          , a derogatory slang term for people from the southern United States associated with unabashed American pride, beer drinking, and rural life.
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr3">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn3">
            [3]
           </a>
          </sup>
         </p>
         <h2 id="origin">
          Origin
         </h2>
         <p>
          The earliest known instance of the image macro was shared on Reddit
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr4">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn4">
            [4]
           </a>
          </sup>
          on March 4th, 2011. The caption alludes to an incestual relationship between the character and his daughter, a common trope employed in redneck humor.
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr5">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn5">
            [5]
           </a>
          </sup>
          Originally uploaded via Quickmeme
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr1">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn1">
            [1]
           </a>
          </sup>
          , there are 7891 instances of the series on the site as of May 2012.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <center>
          <a href="/photos/302028">
           <img data-src="http://i2.kym-cdn.com/photos/images/newsfeed/000/302/028/f06.png" height="300" src="http://a.kym-cdn.com/assets/blank-b3f96f160b75b1b49b426754ba188fe8.gif"/>
          </a>
          <a href="/photos/301982">
           <img data-src="http://i0.kym-cdn.com/photos/images/newsfeed/000/301/982/235.jpg" height="300" src="http://a.kym-cdn.com/assets/blank-b3f96f160b75b1b49b426754ba188fe8.gif"/>
          </a>
         </center>
         <br/>
         <p>
          Following the blog coverage of Redneck Randal, a Memebase
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr6">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn6">
            [6]
           </a>
          </sup>
          user commented on May 11th, 2011 that the identity of the man in the photo is a Texas arcade owner named Cory Clinkscales.
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr7">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn7">
            [7]
           </a>
          </sup>
          Meanwhile, several other commenters claimed that the photo is of a Mississippi resident named Hunter Smith. The images original site of origin still remains unclear.
         </p>
         <h2 id="spread">
          Spread
         </h2>
         <p>
          A compilation of Redneck Randal images were featured on internet humor blog The Bigster
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr10">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn10">
            [10]
           </a>
          </sup>
          on May 22nd, 2011. Two additional sets from the series can be found on image macro gallery site #1 Meme Universal Trend in 2011.
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr13">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn13">
            [13]
           </a>
          </sup>
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr14">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn14">
            [14]
           </a>
          </sup>
          In 2012, another compilation was hosted on humor blog Runt of the Web.
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr16">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn16">
            [16]
           </a>
          </sup>
          On Reddit, a Redneck Randal image post
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr8">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn8">
            [8]
           </a>
          </sup>
          earned 1172 points on April 12th, 2012. The next month, an instance with the caption Then I told the teacher lady I aint no homosapien, Im a heterosapien was featured as the Meme of the Week in the Atlanta magazine GA Voice.
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr15">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn15">
            [15]
           </a>
          </sup>
          As of June 2012, there are more than 480 instances
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr9">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn9">
            [9]
           </a>
          </sup>
          of the series on Reddit and more than 10,000 instances on Quickmeme.
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr1">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn1">
            [1]
           </a>
          </sup>
          Redneck Randal has continued to spread on Memebase
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr11">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn11">
            [11]
           </a>
          </sup>
          and Tumblr.
          <sup class="footnote" id="fnr12">
           <a class="footnote-superscript" href="#fn12">
            [12]
           </a>
          </sup>
         </p>

         <h4 id="almost-politically-correct-redneck">
          Almost Politically Correct Redneck
         </h4>
         <p>
          In May 2012, Redditor IanL27 submitted an image macro to the /r/adviceanimals subreddit in a post titled I present to you: Somewhat Politically Correct Redneck, which featured a photo of a young shirtless man with a mullet hairstyle standing next to a pickup truck and the caption Not against gay marriage / unless the couple is colored. The post was well received on /r/adviceanimals subreddit, spawning a significant volume of image macros that became known as
          <a href="http://knowyourmeme.com/memes/almost-politically-correct-redneck">
           Almost Politically Correct Redneck
          </a>
          , portraying the man as a slightly more tolerant peer of Redneck Randal.
         </p>
