         <h1>
          The Overland Track, Tasmania, Australia
         </h1>
         <div class="caption">
          <p>
           This destination is a hikers dream. A demanding mountain walk through a wide array of Australian ecosystems, the Overland Track is a world away from the hustle and bustle of Sydney. The six-day, 40-mile trek winds through some beautiful and rugged Tasmanian terrain, and has become increasingly popular as one of the areas most authentic wilderness bushwalks.
