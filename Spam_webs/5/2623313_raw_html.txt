     <div class="post-3668 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-shop-showrooms">
      <h6>
       SHOWROOM | MADE.COM IN LONDON SOHO
      </h6>
      <div class="post-featured">
       <a class="thumb" href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/shop-showrooms/made-com-showroom-in-london-soho/">
        <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-6" class="alignleft wp-post-image" height="420" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-6-640x420.jpg" width="640"/>
       </a>
      </div>
      <div class="content post-inner-single clearfix">
       <div class="cat-post-single">
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/category/shop-showrooms/" rel="category tag">
         SHOPS &amp; SHOWROOMS
        </a>
       </div>
       <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
       <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="8143824" data-link="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/shop-showrooms/made-com-showroom-in-london-soho/" data-summary="" data-title="SHOWROOM | MADE.COM IN LONDON SOHO">
       </div>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-6.jpg">
         <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-6" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3674" height="457" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-6.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        One of the most popular online homeware retailers among Design Lovers just opened a second Physical Shop right in the heart of London.
        <a href="http://www.made.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.made.com/', 'Made.com']);">
         Made.com
        </a>
        is not only a click away but has conquered Londons Soho, featuring a shopfront based on a pin-art toy and a series of domestic sets combined with full-scale video projections and slideshows.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-3.jpg">
         <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-3" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3671" height="457" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-3.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        For those that were used to buy online this showroom is just a realscale representation of their website. They already had a similar approach on their first ever showroom in the ninth floor of a west London office tower, were theyfeatured QR codes and miniature 3D-printed models of furniture.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-1.jpg">
         <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3669" height="640" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-1.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Designed by London studio
        <a href="http://www.b-de-c.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.b-de-c.com/', 'Bureau de Change']);">
         Bureau de Change
        </a>
        , you can actually relive the all online experience in this physical shop. The
        <a href="http://www.made.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.made.com/', 'Made.com']);">
         Made.com
        </a>
        showroom is used to present the products available to order via the companys website.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-10.jpg">
         <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-10" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3678" height="964" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-10.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Using video projection alongside the physical products, many of the products are simply displayedagainst colourful settings and in this way the customers canpair up different itemsto understand how they might work together in the home.
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-11.jpg">
         <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-11" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3679" height="457" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-11.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-8.jpg">
         <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-8" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3676" height="457" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-8.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-9.jpg">
         <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-9" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3677" height="457" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-9.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        There is also a curving white screens thatcreatesa useable surfacefor projection. Another feature of the space is a wall of framed postcards, indicating where customers can help themselves to material swatches.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-5.jpg">
         <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-5" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3673" height="457" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-5.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-4.jpg">
         <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-4" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3672" height="457" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-4.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-2.jpg">
         <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3670" height="457" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-2.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        It took a lot of ingenuity and design flare from the design studio
        <a href="http://www.b-de-c.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.b-de-c.com/', 'Bureau de Change']);">
         Bureau de Change
        </a>
        to come up with this spatial solution. One of our favourite displays of this space is the actual window shop. Inspired by the pin-art toy, so popular and familiar to hundreds of generations, the shopfront is covered in 40,000clear plastic rods that puncture a row of10 windows leaving the impression of a human size toy.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-7.jpg">
         <img alt="Lodon Design-MADE.com-Showroom-7" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3675" height="457" src="http://www.londondesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/Lodon-Design-MADE.com-Showroom-7.jpg" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Some of the most popular items of the brand where then pressed against this wall and the final outcome is something to delight.
       </p>

        As seen in

          Dezeen
         </a>
        </strong>
