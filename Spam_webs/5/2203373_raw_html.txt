      <h1 class="entry-title single-title">
       <span>
        DIY Felt Crowns // Party Hats for Pets or Kids
       </span>
      </h1>
     </header>
     <div class="entry-media">
     </div>
     <div class="wrapper">
      <div class="grids">
       <div class="grid-8">
        <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content">
         <div align="justify">
          <center>
           <img alt="Birthday crowns for Frenchies -DIY Party hats" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-21020" height="638" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/Birthday-crowns-for-Frenchies1.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <p>
           <b>
            <a href="http://www.lovemaegan.com/category/the-little-lords" target="_blank">
             Trevor and Randy
            </a>
            turned ONE on January 19th
           </b>
           and just to commemorate their birthday, I thought Id make them little crowns, which of course, was more for my own amusement than for theirs, BUT they got plenty of treats for being such good models, so it was a win win.
          </p>
          <p>
           The little crowns are easy and quick to whip up and make the perfect little birthday or party hats for pets or kiddos. In fact, if you use regular glue instead of hot glue, it could be a fun project with your kiddos as well as cute party hats and/or party favors. But if youre looking for a more sophisticated crown, you can easily stitch up the edges on a sewing machine. One piece of felt is about .45cents and trim is optional, making this project completely affordable and fun for all.
          </p>
          <h4>
           Heres What Youll Need
          </h4>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <img alt="DIY Felt crows for pets or kids-1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20944" height="637" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/DIY-Felt-crows-for-pets-or-kids-1.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Felt
           </li>
           <li>
            Scissors
           </li>
           <li>
            Hot glue gun + glue
           </li>
           <li>
            Elastic
           </li>
           <li>
            Fancy trim
           </li>
          </ul>
          <h4>
           Steps
          </h4>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <img alt="DIY Felt crows for pets or kids-2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20945" height="411" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/DIY-Felt-crows-for-pets-or-kids-2.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <p>
           Fold your felt in half long-ways {end to end} and glue it together. It will be 12 long.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <img alt="DIY Felt crows for pets or kids-3" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20946" height="379" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/DIY-Felt-crows-for-pets-or-kids-3.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <p>
           Create the top of your crown by cutting triangles from the folded side.
          </p>
          <p>
           NOTE: cut each end first so they line up correctly.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <img alt="DIY Felt crows for pets or kids-4" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20947" height="780" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/DIY-Felt-crows-for-pets-or-kids-4.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <p>
           Glue together all openings and gaps, top and bottom, to create a seamless two-layer felt textile.
          </p>
          <p>
           NOTE: You can stitch this if you prefer.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <img alt="DIY Felt crows for pets or kids-5" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20948" height="797" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/DIY-Felt-crows-for-pets-or-kids-5.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <p>
           Hold your ends together to create your crown and decide where your elastic band will line up by centering it on each side between the front and back.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <img alt="DIY Felt crows for pets or kids-6" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20949" height="398" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/DIY-Felt-crows-for-pets-or-kids-6.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <p>
           Mark your spots with a pen and poke holes with your scissors.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <img alt="DIY Felt crows for pets or kids-7" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20950" height="808" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/DIY-Felt-crows-for-pets-or-kids-7.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <p>
           Slip your elastic through the holes and tie tight knots
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <img alt="DIY Felt crows for pets or kids-8" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20951" height="351" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/DIY-Felt-crows-for-pets-or-kids-8.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <p>
           Trim your excess elastic.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <img alt="DIY Felt crows for pets or kids-9" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20952" height="430" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/DIY-Felt-crows-for-pets-or-kids-9.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <p>
           Glue on your pretty trim {which also hides your elastic knots} and add any other cute details if you wish
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <img alt="DIY Felt crows for pets or kids-10" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20953" height="756" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/DIY-Felt-crows-for-pets-or-kids-10.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <p>
           And finally, glue your ends together creating your crown.
          </p>
          <p>
           Once it has dried, trim away any areas that dont line up just to finish it off.
          </p>
          <h4>
           And Voila!
          </h4>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <img alt="DIY Felt crowns for dogs or kids" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20943" height="456" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/DIY-Felt-crowns-for-dogs-or-kids.jpg" width="640"/>
          </center>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>
           <br/>
           <h4>
            Happy Birthday Boys!
           </h4>
           <p>
           </p>
          </center>
          <p>
          </p>
          <center>

          </center>
          <p>

          </center>
          <p>

          <center>
           <img alt="Pied French Bulldog with crown" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-20958" height="957" src="http://www.lovemaegan.com/wp-content/uploads/Pied-French-Bulldog-with-crown.jpg" width="640"/>
