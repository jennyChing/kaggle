       Thomas Becker and Sebastian Stahlhofen are two young German guys who are fascinated with everyday ordinary things, but especially movement and precision. The video is 2 minutes long, but had 5 hours of outtakes (not included). The idea behind A Normal Day is that seemingly impossible events are perfectly normal.
