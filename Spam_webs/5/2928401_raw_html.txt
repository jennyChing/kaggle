          <p>

           </span>
          </p>

          </p>

          <p>
           They also filmed at the Bay Bridge, inside Oracles Redwood Shores headquarters, on Mission Street between First and Second Street, as well as some scenes on California and Powell.
          </p>

           The cast of the film includes Khaleesi  er, we mean Emilia Clarke  playing the role of Sarah Connor, Jai Courtney as Kyle Reese, Jason Clarke as John Connor, and of course, our former governor Arnold Schwarzenegger as the Terminator.
