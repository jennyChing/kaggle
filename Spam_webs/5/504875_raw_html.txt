       <h1 class="hLargeTitle">
        Top 5: Badass Literary Figures
       </h1>
       <h2 class="hSubTitle">
       </h2>
       <div class="author clearfix">
        <div class="authorInfo">

       History has certainly seen its share of authors and poets who are also tough guys, but you generally don't associate literary men with the same level of machismo as, say, guys who pull trucks with their teeth.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       For every Joseph Conrad -- gunrunner and Master Mariner -- you have hordes of foppish, decadent romantics who spent their careers writing on pink paper about nymphs and pretty shepherd girls.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       However, a few men really managed to stand out from the crowd, a few men so fearless or indestructible that it would be a crime against masculinity if their stories went untold. What follows is a list of the top five badass literary figures, all well-known or well-respected authors (rather than guys who just happened to write books after a lifetime of
       <a href="http://www.askmen.com/money/how_to_300/370_how_to.html">
        wrestling bears
       </a>
       ).
