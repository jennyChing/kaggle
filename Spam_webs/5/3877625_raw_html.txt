         <h3>
          Do you rememberthe near-perfect film
          <a href="http://www.fiz-x.com/back-to-the-future-behind-the-scenes-set-photos/" target="_blank" title="BACK TO THE FUTURE Behind the Scenes Set Photos">
           <i>
            Back to the Future
           </i>
          </a>
          . But did you know the classic 1985 film has one of the best Easter eggs ever?
         </h3>
         <h3 style="color: #121212;">
          Near the beginning of the film, Marty is woken up by a phone call from Doc Brown, who wants to meet him at the mall to film an experiment.
         </h3>
         <div class="buzz_superlist_item buzz_superlist_item_image buzz_superlist_item_wide image_hit " id="superlist_3347955_3188895">
          <h3 style="color: #121212;">
           The mall that Marty meets Doc at is named Twin Pines Mall.
          </h3>
          <div class="sub_buzz_content">
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-29338-1403296927-15.png">
            <img alt="Back to the Future" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-49508" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-29338-1403296927-15.png?resize=620%2C330"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="sub_buzz_source_via buzz_attribution" style="font-style: italic; color: #999999;">
           Universal Pictures / Via
           <a href="http://cineliterarte.files.wordpress.com/2012/12/backtothefuture11.png" style="color: #999999;" target="_blank">
            wordpress.com
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="buzz_superlist_item buzz_superlist_item_image buzz_superlist_item_wide image_hit " id="superlist_3347955_3189059">
          <h3 style="color: #121212;">
           The mall is also the location where Marty time travels back to 1955 from, after Doc Brown is killed by Libyan terrorists.
          </h3>
          <div class="sub_buzz_content">
           <a href="http://i1.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-20501-1403297474-8.jpg">
            <img alt="enhanced-20501-1403297474-8" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-49514" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-20501-1403297474-8.jpg?resize=600%2C397"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="sub_buzz_source_via buzz_attribution" style="font-style: italic; color: #999999;">
           Universal Pictures
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="buzz_superlist_item buzz_superlist_item_grid_row buzz_superlist_item_wide " id="superlist_3347955_3189012">
          <h3 style="color: #121212;">
           In 1955, the location where Twin Pines Mall would be is a ranch owned by a man named Mr. Peabody, who happens to mistake Marty and the DeLorean time machine for a Martian and a UFO.
          </h3>
          <div class="grid_row two_pl grid_height_m">
           <div class="grid_cell cell_1">
            <div class="grid_cell_image_wrapper">
             <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/The-Thing-You-Missed-In-Back-To-The-Future-Custom.jpg">
              <img alt="The Thing You Missed In Back To The Future!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-49519" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/The-Thing-You-Missed-In-Back-To-The-Future-Custom.jpg?resize=600%2C300"/>
             </a>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="buzz_superlist_item buzz_superlist_item_image buzz_superlist_item_wide image_hit " id="superlist_3347955_3188887">
          <h3 style="color: #121212;">
           As Marty flees Mr. Peabodys ranch  so that he wouldnt be killed  he happens to run over one of two pines that sit near the Peabodys house.
          </h3>
          <div class="sub_buzz_content">
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-20514-1403296879-21.png">
            <img alt="The Thing You Missed In Back To The Future!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-49511" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-20514-1403296879-21.png?resize=620%2C326"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="sub_buzz_source_via buzz_attribution" style="font-style: italic; color: #999999;">
           Universal Pictures / Via
           <a href="http://cineliterarte.files.wordpress.com/2012/12/backtothefuture11.png" style="color: #999999;" target="_blank">
            wordpress.com
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="buzz_superlist_item buzz_superlist_item_image buzz_superlist_item_wide image_hit " id="superlist_3347955_3188872">
          <h3 style="color: #121212;">
           The ranch happened to be named after those two pines: Twin Pines Ranch.
          </h3>
          <div class="sub_buzz_content">
           <a href="http://i1.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-11351-1403296828-13.png">
            <img alt="Back to the Future" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-49510" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-11351-1403296828-13.png?resize=620%2C328"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="sub_buzz_source_via buzz_attribution" style="font-style: italic; color: #999999;">
           Universal Pictures / Via
           <a href="http://cineliterarte.files.wordpress.com/2012/12/backtothefuture11.png" style="color: #999999;" target="_blank">
            wordpress.com
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="buzz_superlist_item buzz_superlist_item_image buzz_superlist_item_wide image_hit " id="superlist_3347955_3189207">
          <h3 style="color: #121212;">
           While in 1955, Marty is told by Doc that any actions he takes will have serious consequences and could potentially change the future.
          </h3>
          <div class="sub_buzz_content">
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-15670-1403298623-1.jpg">
            <img alt="The Thing You Missed In Back To The Future" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-49520" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-15670-1403298623-1.jpg?resize=620%2C465"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="sub_buzz_source_via buzz_attribution" style="font-style: italic; color: #999999;">
           Universal Pictures
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="buzz_superlist_item buzz_superlist_item_image buzz_superlist_item_wide image_hit " id="superlist_3347955_3189150">
          <h3 style="color: #121212;">
           When Marty eventually goes back to 1985, he sets the time machine back a few minutes earlier, to give him time to get to the mall and try to stop Doc from being killed.
          </h3>
          <div class="sub_buzz_content">
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-31022-1403298285-7.jpg">
            <img alt="The Thing You Missed In Back To The Future!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-49515" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-31022-1403298285-7.jpg?resize=620%2C357"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="sub_buzz_source_via buzz_attribution" style="font-style: italic; color: #999999;">
           Universal Pictures. / Via
           <a href="http://earnthis.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/lightning-delorean.jpg" style="color: #999999;" target="_blank">
            earnthis.net
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="buzz_superlist_item buzz_superlist_item_image buzz_superlist_item_wide image_hit " id="superlist_3347955_3188885">
          <h3 style="color: #121212;">
           But, Marty doesnt go back to the same mall. In fact, there was a big clue in front of him that he had already altered the future. The mall was now called Lone Pine Mall.
          </h3>
          <div class="sub_buzz_content" style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-30881-1403296849-24.png">
            <img alt="Back to the Future" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-49509" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-30881-1403296849-24.png?resize=620%2C311"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="sub_buzz_source_via buzz_attribution" style="font-style: italic; color: #999999; text-align: left;">
           Universal Pictures / Via
           <a href="http://cineliterarte.files.wordpress.com/2012/12/backtothefuture11.png" style="color: #999999;" target="_blank">
            wordpress.com
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="buzz_superlist_item buzz_superlist_item_image buzz_superlist_item_wide image_hit " id="superlist_3347955_3188865">
          <h3 style="color: #121212;">
           And there you go, Marty and us viewers got an early clue that he had changed the future. This is one insane Easter Eggfrom the movie after all this time. Back To The Future wont stop surprising us.
          </h3>
          <div class="sub_buzz_content" style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://i1.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-12161-1403296223-13.png">
            <img alt="The Thing You Missed In Back To The Future!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-49512" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.fiz-x.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/enhanced-12161-1403296223-13.png?resize=620%2C248"/>
           </a>
          </div>
