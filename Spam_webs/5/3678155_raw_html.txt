        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Pomegranate Lemonade
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-05-25T19:00:26+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          May 25, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://totallythebomb.com/author/mary" itemprop="url" rel="author">

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input data-frizzlyhovercontainer="" data-frizzlypostcontainer="" data-frizzlyposttitle="Pomegranate Lemonade" data-frizzlyposturl="http://totallythebomb.com/pomegranate-lemonade" type="hidden" value=""/>
        <p>
         My local grocery store just got in a fresh shipment of pomegranates and now Im craving pomegranate everything. The other day I had pomegranate jewels over my ice cream, and today Im making this
         <strong>
          pomegranate lemonade
         </strong>
         . It doesnt take hardly any time at all to make, and its such a refreshing summer twist on traditional lemonade. Plus, the beautiful pink of the pomegranate juice just looks so pretty up against that lemony yellow. This is such a fun treat to share with friends, and Im so glad to be sharing it will all of you. Enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="pomegranate lemonade" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28680" height="450" src="http://cdn1.totallythebomb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/summer-drink1.jpg" width="700"/>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         What You Need To Make Pomegranate Lemonade:
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          2 1/2 cups water
         </li>
         <li>
          1 3/4 cups pomegranate juice
         </li>
         <li>
          1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
         </li>
         <li>
          1/4 cup sugar
         </li>
         <li>
          crushed ice
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <img alt="pomegranate lemonade" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28683" height="1000" src="http://cdn1.totallythebomb.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/pomegranate-lemonade.jpg" width="700"/>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <h2>
         How To Make Pomegranate Lemonade:
        </h2>
        <p>
         In a large pitcher, stir all ingredients until the sugar dissolves.
        </p>
        <p>
         Serve immediately over crushed ice. Enjoy!
        </p>
        <div class="code-block code-block-4" style="text-align:center;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;margin-top:8px;margin-bottom:8px;">

        <p>
         <strong>
          Pro Tip:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         For a fun twist, blend all ingredients together in a blender until it is the texture of a smoothie. You can also add yogurt or milk to make it a creamy, delicious treat. Enjoy!
        </p>

The other day I had pomegranate jewels over my ice cream, and today I'm making this pomegranate lemonade. It doesn't take hardly any time at all to make, and it's such a refreshing summer twist on traditional lemonade. Plus, the beautiful pink of the pomegranate juice just looks so pretty up against that lemony yellow. This is such a fun treat to share with friends, and I'm so glad to be sharing it will all of you.
