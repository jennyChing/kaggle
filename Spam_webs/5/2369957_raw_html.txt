        DIY Valentines Day Easy Massage Oil Recipe

         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Homemade Valentine's Day Gift Idea - DIY Sensual Massage Oil Recipe" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-4603" height="374" src="http://soapdelinews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/diy-valentines-day-sensual-massage-oil-recipe-500x374.jpg" width="500"/>

        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         If youre still meshing out plans for Valentines Day on Friday, then you may want to give this easy massage oil recipe for a sensual Valentines Daytreat. It makes a romantic and intimate homemadeValentines Day gift for couples who could use a little more stress free time together.
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
         Valentines Day EasyMassage Oil Recipe
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         
         <a href="http://www.soapdelicatessen.com">
          Rebeccas Soap Delicatessen
         </a>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
         Ingredients:
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         16 grams
         <a href="http://edge.affiliateshop.com/public/AIDLink?AID=021074&amp;Redirect=/products/macadamia-nut-oil/profile">
          macadamia nut oil
         </a>
         <br/>
         7 grams
         <a href="http://edge.affiliateshop.com/public/AIDLink?AID=021074&amp;Redirect=/products/sweet-almond-oil/profile">
          sweet almond oil
         </a>
         <br/>
         4 grams
         <a href="http://edge.affiliateshop.com/public/AIDLink?AID=021074&amp;Redirect=/products/sesame-oil/profile">
          sesame oil
         </a>
         <br/>
         1 gram
         <a href="http://edge.affiliateshop.com/public/AIDLink?AID=021074&amp;Redirect=/products/shea-butter/profile">
          shea butter
         </a>
         <br/>
         6 drops
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002RTIMZW/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B002RTIMZW&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=rebeccassoapd-20">
          Edens Garden Aphrodisiac Synergy Blend Essential Oil
         </a>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=rebeccassoapd-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B002RTIMZW" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="How to Make Natural Sensual Massage Oils for Valentine's Day" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-4604" height="352" src="http://soapdelinews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/how-to-make-massage-oil-for-a-romantic-valentines-day-gift-500x352.jpg" width="500"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://soapdelinews.com/2014/02/homemade-diy-valentines-day-massage-oil-recipe.html&amp;media=http://soapdelinews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/how-to-make-massage-oil-for-a-romantic-valentines-day-gift-500x352.jpg&amp;description=DIY Valentines Day Easy Massage Oil Recipe')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
         Instructions:
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Using a
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001N07KUE/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B001N07KUE&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=rebeccassoapd-20">
          digital kitchen scale
         </a>
         weigh out the oils in a
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008VEHLBI/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B008VEHLBI&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=rebeccassoapd-20">
          small glass beaker
         </a>
         and then warm slightly. Weigh out the shea butter and stir into oils until it melts completely, then add the essential oil blend. Pour into a
         <a href="edge.affiliateshop.com/public/AIDLink?AID=021074&amp;Redirect=/catalog/containers/bottles">
          1 oz. amber glass bottle
         </a>
         and cap.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="DIY Homemade Sensual Valentine's Day Massage Oil Recipe - DIY Valentine's Day Gift Idea" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-4602" height="387" src="http://soapdelinews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/homemade-valentines-day-gift-massage-oil-recipe-500x387.jpg" width="500"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://soapdelinews.com/2014/02/homemade-diy-valentines-day-massage-oil-recipe.html&amp;media=http://soapdelinews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/homemade-valentines-day-gift-massage-oil-recipe-500x387.jpg&amp;description=DIY Valentines Day Easy Massage Oil Recipe')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         You can then decorate your bottle as desired. For my bottle I used a
         <a href="http://www.worldlabel.com">
          blank sticker label
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://www.cutetape.com">
          washi tape
         </a>
         . I covered a section of the label with washi tape then cut it out into the shape of heart. I then cut out a smaller heart shape from the blank label sheet and adhered it to the front of the washi tape covered heart and wrote out my message. Finally the I adhered the entire heart to the bottle.
        </p>
