        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Salmone alle erbe aromatiche in crosta di pane e semi di papavero
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="bl_categ">
          <i class="icon-folder-open icon-metas" title="Categorie">
          </i>
          <a href="http://claudiopucci.com/category/secondi-piatti" rel="tag">
           Secondi piatti
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://claudiopucci.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/salmone-1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
            <img alt="" border="0" height="400" src="http://claudiopucci.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/salmone-1.jpg" width="640"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 10px;">
          </div>
          <div style="font-family: 'Courier New'; text-align: center;">
           <span style="font-size: large;">
            Salmone alle erbe aromatiche in crosta di pane e semi di papavero
           </span>
          </div>
          <div style="font-family: 'Courier New'; text-align: center;">
           <span style="font-size: large;">
           </span>
          </div>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <span style="font-family: 'Courier New';">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Categoria: Secondi di pesce
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Ingredienti per 5 persone:
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           500 g. Salmone fresco intero pulito a baffe
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           2 g. Timo liofilizzato Wiberg
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           10 g. Prezzemolo tritato
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           10 g. Sale profumato
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           20 g. Senape
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           20 g. Grana Padano
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           1 conf. Pane tramezzini
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           20 g. Semi di papavero
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Procedimento:
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Tagliare la baffa di salmone in 5 pezzi. Spennerlarli con la senape da ambo le parti,aggiungere il timo, il prezzemolo tritato e spolverare col grana grattugiato.
          </p>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://claudiopucci.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_2411.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
            <img alt="" border="0" height="240" src="http://claudiopucci.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_2411.jpg" width="320"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Stendere su un piano le fette di pane per tramezzini e appiattirle col mattarello,arrotolare il salmone e spennellare con luovo sbattuto e spolverare con i semi di papavero. Cuocere in forno a 180 gradi per 11 minuti.
          </p>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://claudiopucci.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_2412.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
            <img alt="" border="0" height="320" src="http://claudiopucci.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_2412.jpg" width="240"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://claudiopucci.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_2414.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
            <img alt="" border="0" height="240" src="http://claudiopucci.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_2414.jpg" width="320"/>
           </a>
          </div>
          <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
          </div>
          <div style="text-align: center;">
           <span style="font-family: Courier New, Courier, monospace; font-size: large;">
            Su piatto Optik (Square) by
