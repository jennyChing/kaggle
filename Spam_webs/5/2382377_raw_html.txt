                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                     Maple-Bacon Fruit Dip
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3366928171022846248">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      It's no secret that I love maple. Just love it. I've posted recipes for
                      <a href="http://www.reneeskitchenadventures.com/2014/06/maple-walnut-granola.html" target="_blank">
                       Maple Walnut Granola
                      </a>
                      ,
                      <a href="http://www.reneeskitchenadventures.com/2014/04/maple-walnut-banana-bread-with-maple.html" target="_blank">
                       Maple Walnut Banana Bread with Maple Glaze
                      </a>
                      , and
                      <a href="http://www.reneeskitchenadventures.com/2011/12/roasted-brussels-sprouts-wmaple.html" target="_blank">
                       Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Maple-Balsamic Drizzle
                      </a>
                      , just to name a few. So when
                      <a href="http://www.lorannoils.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                       LorAnn Oils
                      </a>
                      offered to send members of the OBC14 three flavors to try, and I saw Canadian Maple as one of the choices, I jumped for joy!
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Of course, at the time, I had no idea what I'd make! But then I got an idea...
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       Maple-Bacon Fruit Dip
                      </b>
                      !
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Fl4FrAFSwAI/VBjk5cLb-sI/AAAAAAAAG58/N1t2f3jCpNo/s1600/Maple-Bacon%2BFruit%2BDip%2Blong%2Btext.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Maple-Bacon Fruit Dip featuring LorAnn Oils and Flavorings " border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Fl4FrAFSwAI/VBjk5cLb-sI/AAAAAAAAG58/N1t2f3jCpNo/s1600/Maple-Bacon%2BFruit%2BDip%2Blong%2Btext.jpg" title="Maple-Bacon Fruit Dip featuring LorAnn Oils and Flavorings "/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     I'll be honest, I was not familiar with these oils before I was sent the samples to try. (
                     <i>
                      Disclosure: I was sent product of LorAnn Oils to try. All opinions and photos are 100% mine.)
                     </i>
                     Most people in the OBC group were very excited to hear we were being offered this product, so I figured I must have been missing out on something good.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      And you know what?
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     I was right! Wow. These super-concentrated oils and bakery emulsions are amazing! Just a little goes a long way in the flavor department. Unlike ordinary extracts, these oils can be used in baking, crafting, and cooking, as well as candy making and added to chocolates without making the chocolate seize! The bakery emulsions are water based, so they are formulated to keep the true flavor even after baking.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-AJ-M3uVu5hw/VCLGqyA-7WI/AAAAAAAAG_M/69YdChhdmBY/s1600/maple%2Bbacon%2Bfruit%2Bdip%2B3.1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Maple-Bacon Fruit Dip with apples! " border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-AJ-M3uVu5hw/VCLGqyA-7WI/AAAAAAAAG_M/69YdChhdmBY/s1600/maple%2Bbacon%2Bfruit%2Bdip%2B3.1.jpg" title="Maple-Bacon Fruit Dip with apples! "/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     This easy
                     <b>
                      Maple-Bacon Fruit Dip
                     </b>
                     is a perfect dip for all the apples coming to season right now. It's sweet and maple-y with the saltiness of bacon which pairs beautifully with fresh apples or those cute mini toaster waffles. I used reduced fat cream cheese and fat free Greek yogurt in the dip to help keep it somewhat figure friendly...
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-5TXSEVf3emc/VBjl8HgrxvI/AAAAAAAAG6U/Wvgy5Asd-2c/s1600/lorann%2Boils.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="LorAnn Oils Canadian Maple Flavor" border="0" height="270" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-5TXSEVf3emc/VBjl8HgrxvI/AAAAAAAAG6U/Wvgy5Asd-2c/s1600/lorann%2Boils.jpg" title="LorAnn Oils Canadian Maple Flavor" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      And of course,
                      <b>
                       LorAnn's Canadian Maple oil
                      </b>
                      for tons of maple flavor!
                     </div>
                     <br/>

                      <h1 class="fn">
                       Maple Bacon Fruit Dip
                      </h1>
                      <label>
                       By
                      </label>
                      <span class="author">
                       Renee's Kitchen Adventures
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <img alt="Maple Bacon Fruit Dip" class="photo" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-GU7dgi_-c9c/VCGEvmEv4gI/AAAAAAAAG98/RnICqlAE7zw/s1600/maple%2Bbacon%2Bfruit%2Bdip%2Bsqu.jpg" style="border-width: 0px; height: 140px; width: 140px;"/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="summary">
                       Maple flavored fruit dip with the saltiness of bacon.  Featuring LorAnn Canadian Maple oil.
                      </div>
                      <h2>
                       Ingredients
                      </h2>
                      <ul>
                       <li class="ingredient">
                        8 oz. reduced fat cream cheese, softened
                       </li>
                       <li class="ingredient">
                        1/2 cup Fat free vanilla flavored Greek Yogurt
                       </li>
                       <li class="ingredient">
                        1/2 tsp. to 1 tsp. LorAnn Canadian Maple flavored oil
                       </li>
                       <li class="ingredient">
                        3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
                       </li>
                       <li class="ingredient">
                        4 strips of crispy cooked bacon, crumbled
                       </li>
                       <li class="ingredient">
                        Assorted cut up apples, shortbread cookies, or mini waffles
                       </li>
                      </ul>
                      <h2>
                       Instructions
                      </h2>
                      <ol>
                       <li class="instructions">
                        In small bowl, combine softened cream cheese, vanilla Greek yogurt, LorAnn Canadian Maple flavored oil (start with 1/2 tsp. and increase if stronger maple flavor is desired), and 3/4 cup brown sugar.  Mix well to combine.
                       </li>
                       <li class="instructions">
                        Add all but 1 TBS. of crumbled bacon to the dip.  Stir to combine.  Top the dip with reserved TBS bacon bits.
                       </li>
                       <li class="instructions">
                        Serve with cut up apples, shortbread cookies, mini waffles, or whatever you like.
                       </li>
                      </ol>
                      <span style="font-weight: bold;">
                       Yield:
                      </span>
                      <span class="yield">
                       Approx. 2 cups
                      </span>
                      <br/>

                       Prep Time:
                      </span>
                      <span class="preptime">
