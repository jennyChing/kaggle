      <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
       In one of 
        history's more absurd acts of totalitarianism, China has banned Buddhist 
        monks in Tibet from reincarnating without government permission.
      </font>
     </p>
     <p>
      <font face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" size="2">
       According 
        to a statement issued by the State Administration for Religious Affairs, 
        the law, which goes into effect next month and strictly stipulates the 
        procedures by which one is to reincarnate, is "an important move 
        to institutionalize management of reincarnation."
      </font>
     </p>
