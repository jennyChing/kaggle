       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Healthy Homemade Spaghettios
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2012-10-24T17:29:08+00:00">
         October 24, 2012
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://too-much-time.com/2012/10/healthy-homemade-spaghettios.html#comments">
          24 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://too-much-time.com/?attachment_id=4404" rel="attachment wp-att-4404">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4404" height="600" src="http://too-much-time.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Homemade-Spaghettios-from-Too-Much-Time-On-My-Hands-4-copy.jpg" title="Homemade Spaghettios from Too Much Time On My Hands 4 copy" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://too-much-time.com/2012/10/healthy-homemade-spaghettios.html&amp;media=http://too-much-time.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Homemade-Spaghettios-from-Too-Much-Time-On-My-Hands-4-copy.jpg&amp;description=Healthy Homemade Spaghettios')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I try really, really, really hard to feed my kids nutritious and delicious food. However, I am not perfect. I have been known to succumb to a box of Kraft Macaroni and Cheese and a can of Spaghettios in my time.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Then, last week at my son's school as I worked on a yearbook project interviewing three year olds about their likes and dislikes , I learned my sons favorite foods are, "bacon and meatballs." It was a proud moment! I would have guessed broccoli, my famous homemade pizza, or the air popped popcorn he eats by the barrel-full. But alas, it is bacon and meatballs.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         On our way home that afternoon, we stopped at my very favorite European market, where the make their own mozzarella every morning, sell their secret recipe sauces, and offer imported delights I find hard to resist. I spied this bag of pasta and decided I would do my darndest to create a healthy version of Spaghettios and Meatballs for my son.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://too-much-time.com/2012/10/healthy-homemade-spaghettios.html/homemade-spaghettios-from-too-much-time-on-my-hands-1" rel="attachment wp-att-4408">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-4408" height="483" src="http://too-much-time.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Homemade-Spaghettios-from-Too-Much-Time-On-My-Hands-1-768x1024.jpg" title="Homemade Spaghettios from Too Much Time On My Hands 1" width="362"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I used organic tomato sauce and paste and pureed the carrots myself. I also used bison meat, but use whatever you enjoy.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://too-much-time.com/2012/10/healthy-homemade-spaghettios.html/healthy-spaghettios-and-meatballs-recipe-from-too-much-time-on-my-hands" rel="attachment wp-att-4410">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4410" height="406" src="http://too-much-time.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Healthy-Spaghettios-and-Meatballs-Recipe-from-Too-Much-Time-On-My-Hands.jpg" title="Healthy Spaghettios and Meatballs Recipe from Too Much Time On My Hands" width="628"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://too-much-time.com/2012/10/healthy-homemade-spaghettios.html&amp;media=http://too-much-time.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Healthy-Spaghettios-and-Meatballs-Recipe-from-Too-Much-Time-On-My-Hands.jpg&amp;description=Healthy Homemade Spaghettios')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         This made a lot. I froze the leftovers in a bunch of containers and am so happy to have them waiting for me when I need a quick and healthy meal for the kids that I know they will enjoy.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://too-much-time.com/2012/10/healthy-homemade-spaghettios.html/homemade-spaghettios-from-too-much-time-on-my-hands-2-copy" rel="attachment wp-att-4402">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-4402" height="442" src="http://too-much-time.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Homemade-Spaghettios-from-Too-Much-Time-On-My-Hands-2-copy.jpg" title="Homemade Spaghettios from Too Much Time On My Hands 2 copy" width="589"/>
         </a>
