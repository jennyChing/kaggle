     <h1>
      Maps of the Seven Deadly Sins
     </h1>
     <div class="meta-bar">
      Posted to
      <a href="http://flowingdata.com/category/visualization/mapping/" rel="category tag">
       Mapping
      </a>
     </div>
     <div class="entry">
      <p>
       Geographers from
       <a href="http://www.k-state.edu/geography/">
        Kansas State University
       </a>
       map the spatial distribution of the seven deadly sins in the United States. These types of maps are always kind of iffy as they draw from data from various sources gathered with different methods and usually use some kind of researcher-defined metric. Still interesting though... right?
       <br/>
       <span id="more-1551">
       </span>
      </p>

      </p>

      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1554" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/flowingdata.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/05/lust.png?resize=545%2C418" title="lust"/>

      <p>
