        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Shrimp and Crab Quesadillas
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-03-17T11:00:41+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          March 17, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://jeffersonparishparent.com/author/cara/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Cara
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p style="color: #444444;">
         Quick an easy meal for any time, but especially during lent!
        </p>
        <p style="color: #444444;">
         <img alt="gluten-free-shrim-crab-quesadillas" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4820" height="400" src="http://jeffersonparishparent.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/gluten-free-shrim-crab-quesadillas-600x400.png" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="color: #444444;">
         So you need, some shrimp, some crab (lump or imitation), cilantro, garlic, onion, lime juice, olive oil, cheese and your wraps. I am using the Toufayan Gluten Free Wraps which are one of my favorite gluten free foods.
        </p>
        <p style="color: #444444;">
         <img alt="gluten-free-shrimp-crab-wraps" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4821" height="400" src="http://jeffersonparishparent.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/gluten-free-shrimp-crab-wraps-600x400.png" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="color: #444444;">
         First, I put a little olive oil in a skillet and add the shrimp, crab, 1/2 an onion, 1 tsp of lime juice, and cilantro and garlic to taste (I like a lot of garlic, you may not). You can also add other seasonings you would like here such as salt or a spice mix. Mix it all together and cook it for about 10 minutes, so everything kind of marries together. If you are using uncooked shrimp you want to cook this until your shrimp are at least mostly cooked and pinkish.
        </p>
        <p style="color: #444444;">
         <img alt="gluten-free-shrimp-crab-wraps-3" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4823" height="400" src="http://jeffersonparishparent.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/gluten-free-shrimp-crab-wraps-3-600x400.png" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="color: #444444;">
         While this is all cooking, put your oven on broil and brush a bit of olive oil on one side of one of your wraps. Place it oil side down on a baking sheet. When your seafood mixture is ready put a scoop of it into the middle of your wrap, top it with cheese and then place another wrap on top, oil side up.
        </p>
        <p style="color: #444444;">
         <img alt="gluten-free-shrimp-crab-wraps-6" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4825" height="400" src="http://jeffersonparishparent.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/gluten-free-shrimp-crab-wraps-6-600x400.png" width="600"/>
         <img alt="gluten-free-shrimp-crap-wraps-4" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4826" height="400" src="http://jeffersonparishparent.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/gluten-free-shrimp-crap-wraps-4-600x400.png" width="600"/>
         <img alt="gluten-free-shrimp-crab-wraps-5" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4824" height="400" src="http://jeffersonparishparent.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/gluten-free-shrimp-crab-wraps-5-600x400.png" width="600"/>
         <img alt="gluten-free-shrimp-crap-wraps-8" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4828" height="400" src="http://jeffersonparishparent.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/gluten-free-shrimp-crap-wraps-8-600x400.png" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="color: #444444;">
         Put them in the broiler until they are golden brown and the cheese is melted, put them on a plate, cut them using a pizza cutter and serve with your favorite toppings. I personally like a little taco sauce, guacamole and sour cream with mine.
        </p>
        <p style="color: #444444;">
         <img alt="gluten-free-shrimp-crap-wraps-7" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4827" height="400" src="http://jeffersonparishparent.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/gluten-free-shrimp-crap-wraps-7-600x400.png" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="color: #444444;">
        </p>
