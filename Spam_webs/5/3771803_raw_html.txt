         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          Spicy Shrimp &amp; Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-07-12T12:11:46+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
           July 12, 2013
          </time>
          by
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.simplystacie.net/author/simplystacienet/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             Stacie
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="entry-comments-link">
           <a href="http://www.simplystacie.net/2013/07/spicy-shrimp-vegetable-stir-fry-recipe/#comments">
            48 Comments
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <p>
          This summer Im participating in the Simply Asia Recipe Challenge. I was asked to create an entree, side dish and appetizer for the challenge using
          <a href="http://www.simplyasia.com/" target="_blank">
           Simply Asia
          </a>
          products that were provided to me. For my entree, I made this Spicy Shrimp &amp; Vegetable Stir-Fry. Im a seafood fanatic and love shrimp in just about anything. I used Simply Asia Sweet &amp; Spicy Saigon Seasoning &amp; Spicy Kung Pao Stir-Fry Sauce mixed in with fresh veggies and shrimp. To add another kick, I put the veggie &amp; shrimp mixture over a bed of Singapore Street Noodles in Classic Curry. I added cashews for an extra bit of crunch!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.simplystacie.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Spicy-Shrimp-Stir-Fry1.jpg">
           <img alt="Spicy Shrimp Stir Fry" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-86076" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.simplystacie.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Spicy-Shrimp-Stir-Fry1.jpg?resize=566%2C377"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
          <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
           Spicy Shrimp &amp; Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSTopRight">
           <img data-recalc-dims="1" itemprop="image" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.simplystacie.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Spicy-Shrimp-Stir-Fry1.jpg?w=205"/>
           <div class="ERSSavePrint">
            <span class="ERSSaveBtnSpan">

           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="divERSHeadItems">
           <div class="ERSAuthor">
            Author:
            <span itemprop="author">
             Simply Stacie
            </span>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSIngredients">
           <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
            Ingredients
           </div>
           <ul>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tbsp. olive oil
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup carrots, sliced
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 cups broccoli florets
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 red pepper, chopped
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup snow peas, trimmed
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 green onions, chopped
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 cups mushrooms, sliced
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             2  cups shrimp, uncooked
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tsp Simply Asia Sweet and Spicy Saigon Seasoning
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              cup cashews, plain
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 package Simply Asia Spicy Kung Pao Stir-Fry Sauce
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 packages of Simply Asia Singapore Street Noodles in Classic Curry (or your favorite flavor)
            </li>
           </ul>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSInstructions">
           <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
            Instructions
           </div>
           <ol>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Heat oil in a large frying pan (or wok) over medium heat. Add broccoli florets and carrots and saute, stirring occasionally, for about 5 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Add red pepper, snow peas, green onions, mushrooms and Sweet &amp; Spicy Saigon Seasoning to the pan. Continue to saute for another 7 minutes, until vegetables are softened.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Add in the shrimp and cook for another 5 minutes, or until shrimp turns pink. Add in the Spicy Kung Pao Stir-Fry Sauce and the cashews and stir.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Prepare Singapore Street Noodles according to package directions.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             To serve, add the noodles to a plate and top with shrimp &amp; veggie mixture. Serve hot.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Serves 4.
            </li>
           </ol>
           <div class="ERSClear">
