            <p>
             <em>
              <strong>
               Breakups suck.
              </strong>
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
             Before you can even think about moving on theres a period where all you want to do is cry. Your heart feels like its been ripped out, your future pulled away from under youand you wonder how youre ever going to feel normal again. Breakups hurt like hell.
            </p>
            <p>
             The only thing that will help? A major sob fest. You probably dont need much help in the tears department but theres nothing like some of the worlds most depressing and heart wrenching songs all out into a breakup playlist to make those tears come thick and fast.
            </p>
            <p>
             Do a Bridget Jones  pull the curtains, put on your pjs and blare those depressing songs full blast. Dont feel guilty. Its healthy to embrace the fact that right now, at this second, life is shit. It will get better, you probably know that very deep down. The Im so over you songs will come down the line but for now GET IT ALL OUT!
            </p>
            <h2>
             <span style="color: #0a6584;">
              Breakup Playlist
             </span>
            </h2>
            <p>
             <strong>
              1) 3 Doors Down  Here Without You
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              2) Ne-Yo  So Sick
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              3) Luther Vandross  If only for one night
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              4) Snow Patrol  Chasing Cars
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              5) Not like the movies  Katy Perry
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              6) Sinead OConnor  Nothing Compares 2 U
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              7) Adele  Someone Like You
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              8) Etta James  Id Rather Go Blind
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              9) Lady Antebellum  Need You Know
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              10) Teardrops on my guitar  Taylor Swift
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              11) Aerosmith  I dont want to miss a thing
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              12) Evanescence  My Immortal
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              13) Mariah Carey  Without You
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              14) Michelle Featherstone  Coffee and Cigarettes
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Finally, no breakup playlist would be complete without..
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              15) Whitney Houston  I will Always Love You
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Got a song you think should be on the breakup playlist? As always, please leave a comment below..
            </p>
            <p>
             Much Love
            </p>
