        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Felt Cookies  Busy Bag Idea
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-12-21T05:00:55+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          December 21, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/author/blogging" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Stacey
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/felt-cookies-busy-bag-idea.html#respond">
           Leave a Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="mailmunch-forms-before-post" style="display: none !important;">
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/felt-cookies-busy-bag-idea.html/cookie_busy_bag_cover_1" rel="attachment wp-att-3805">
          <img alt="Felt Cookies - Busy Bag Idea" class="aligncenter wp-image-3805" height="600" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/cookie_busy_bag_cover_1-682x1024.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         It wouldnt be Christmas without making cookies, right? But who wants the hassle of bringing out the mixer, the ingredients and possibly even dealing with a messy preschooler in the kitchen? Not me  thats for sure! So I recently made these mess-free {&amp; guilt-free}
         <em>
          <strong>
           Felt Cookies
          </strong>
         </em>
         for my aspiring little baker. Lets just say, they were a BIG hit in the imagination department!
        </p>
        <p>
         Not only can he bake them over and over again  but this busy bag idea gives me that mess-free kitchen I oh-so-love and the temptation of eating leftover cookies, is gone too! I even have a sneaky plan to leave these out for Santa this year {jokes on him, right?}
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/felt-cookies-busy-bag-idea.html/cookie_busy_bag_1" rel="attachment wp-att-3798">
          <img alt="Felt Cookies - Busy Bag Idea" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3798" height="400" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/cookie_busy_bag_1.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h1>
         <em>
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #339966;">
            Supplies:
           </span>
          </strong>
         </em>
        </h1>
        <p>
         * Colored Felt {tan being the most important!}
         <br/>
         * Scissors
         <br/>
         * Circle Template
         <br/>
         * Sharpie
         <br/>
         * Puffy Paint
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/felt-cookies-busy-bag-idea.html/cookie_busy_bag_2" rel="attachment wp-att-3799">
          <img alt="Felt Cookies - Busy Bag Idea" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3799" height="400" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/cookie_busy_bag_2.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/felt-cookies-busy-bag-idea.html/cookie_busy_bag_3" rel="attachment wp-att-3800">
          <img alt="Felt Cookies - Busy Bag Idea" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3800" height="400" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/cookie_busy_bag_3.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/felt-cookies-busy-bag-idea.html/cookie_busy_bag_4" rel="attachment wp-att-3801">
          <img alt="Felt Cookies - Busy Bag Idea" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3801" height="400" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/cookie_busy_bag_4.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
        </div>
        <h1>
         <em>
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #339966;">
            Directions:
           </span>
          </strong>
         </em>
        </h1>
        <p>
         * Using a template  trace &amp; cut out several circle shapes from your tan felt. This is the base of your cookies. You will also want to make smaller circles out of your colored felt to represent the top frosting of the cookies.
         <br/>
         * Using puffy paint, decorate the smaller felt circles however you like. Set aside to let them dry completely.
         <br/>
         * With the leftover felt, cut out small squares to use as sprinkles for the cookies.
         <br/>
         * Put all the finished pieces in a pile and let your childs imagination go wild! Go ahead and even grab some cookie sheets, cookie cutters etc  to really get creativity juice going!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/2014/12/felt-cookies-busy-bag-idea.html/cookie_busy_bag_7" rel="attachment wp-att-3804">
          <img alt="cookie_busy_bag_7" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3804" height="400" src="http://gluedtomycraftsblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/cookie_busy_bag_7.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
