       <div class="ERSIngredients">
        <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
         Ingredients:
        </div>
        <ul>
         <li class="ingredient">
          6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          2 teaspoons kosher salt
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          1 teaspoon ground black pepper
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          1 large onion, finely chopped (white or yellow onion works)
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          1 10.5 ounce can of cream of chicken condensed soup
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient">
          16 ounces freshly grated Colby Jack cheese
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient last-list-item">
          1 pound frozen shredded hashbrowns (thawed)
         </li>
        </ul>
        <div class="ERSClear">
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="ERSInstructions">
        <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
         Instructions:
        </div>
        <ol>
         <li class="instruction">
          Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction">
          Spray a 3 quart casserole dish with cooking spray.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction">
          In a very large bowl, stir together butter, salt, pepper, onion, cream of chicken soup, and cheese. Gently toss the hashbrowns in the mixture until its all coated with the mixture.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction">
          Bake uncovered for 35-40 minutes or until mixture is bubbly and cooked through.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction">
          To get a nice crust on top, place the casserole under a broiler for 5 minutes  watch it carefully!!
         </li>
        </ol>
