        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Parsnips vs Chocolate
        </h1>
        <p class="headline_meta">
         by
         <span class="author vcard fn">
          Heather
         </span>
         on
         <abbr class="published" title="2010-04-16">
          April 16, 2010
         </abbr>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="format_text entry-content">
        <p>
         <strong>
          I WIN!!!
         </strong>
         The vote was rocked 14 to 9 by people who do make or want to make the bed every day. Take that Nate
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.heathersdish.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         <strong>
          I can here it now
         </strong>
         , It just doesnt make sense to make it if youre going to get back in it! But you see, dear, more people disagree, and
         <strong>
          therefore I win
         </strong>
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.heathersdish.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Lunch today includes a little gem of a recipe I made up last weekend that Ive been keeping from yall since weve been so busy this week and didnt have a chance to try it:
         <strong>
          Slow-Cooker Veggie Pasta Sauce!
         </strong>
         I served it over some stuffed shells with a big salad for lunch:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-324.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-324.jpg', '\n		\n		\n			\n			\n		\n	']);">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1659" height="334" src="http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-324.jpg" title="food-3" width="500"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.heathersdish.com/uncategorized/parsnips-vs-chocolate/&amp;media=http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-324.jpg&amp;description=Parsnips vs Chocolate')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         Its seriously sooooooo good. We also had some bagel with it to wipe the sauce off the plate and make sure every drop got in our mouths
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.heathersdish.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-419.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-419.jpg', '\n		\n		\n			\n			\n		\n	']);">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1660" height="334" src="http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-419.jpg" title="food-4" width="500"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.heathersdish.com/uncategorized/parsnips-vs-chocolate/&amp;media=http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-419.jpg&amp;description=Parsnips vs Chocolate')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         Heres the recipe:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          4 large carrots, shredded
         </li>
         <li>
          1 large onion, shredded
         </li>
         <li>
          3 stalks celery, shredded
         </li>
         <li>
          3 zucchini, shredded
         </li>
         <li>
          4 cloves garlic, minced
         </li>
         <li>
          2 cups sliced mushrooms
         </li>
         <li>
          2 28-oz cans crushed tomatoes
         </li>
         <li>
          salt &amp; pepper to taste
         </li>
         <li>
          3 Tbsp dried basil
         </li>
         <li>
          1 Tbsp dried oregano
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 Tbsp crushed red pepper flakes
         </li>
         <li>
          1 tsp dried rosemary
         </li>
         <li>
          1 tsp thyme
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         I used my food processor to shred all of the veggies; however, you could chop them all and still have a delicious sauce. I was wanting something less chunky though, so shredded worked for me! Combine all ingredients in your slow cooker, and set on low for 6-7 hours, or high for 3-4 hours. I froze most of mine for delicious dinners down the road!
        </p>
        <p>
         On my lunch break I was not feeling well, and it started raining, so I decided to skip the walk and go to Marshalls and Whole Foods instead. I was planning on getting some parsnips for fries after
         <a href="http://ohsheglows.com/2010/04/16/these-fries-will-change-your-life/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://ohsheglows.com/2010/04/16/these-fries-will-change-your-life/', 'Angelas rave review of parsnip fries']);">
          Angelas rave review of parsnip fries
         </a>
         , but Whole Foods didnt have parsnips! So
         <strong>
          I did the only natural thing and bought chocolate instead
         </strong>
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.heathersdish.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-58.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-58.jpg', '\n		\n		\n			\n			\n		\n	']);">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1661" height="334" src="http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-58.jpg" title="food-5" width="500"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.heathersdish.com/uncategorized/parsnips-vs-chocolate/&amp;media=http://heathersdish.files.wordpress.com/2010/04/food-58.jpg&amp;description=Parsnips vs Chocolate')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         I had a row of squares, which is about 1/2 of a serving, and died and went to heaven.
         <strong>
          I adore Chocolove!
         </strong>
         If only they read the blog and wanted to offer a bar of every flavor for review  Kidding. Sort of.
        </p>
        <p>
         This weekend should be funwere doing some kid portraits for some friends and hopefully just relaxing the rest of the time! If its not TOO rainy then we might get out into the yard for some yardwork, but Im not counting on that just yet. Thanks to all of you for participating in the
         <strong>
          Marriage Ref Friday
         </strong>
         vote! In honor of that, I want to know about yalls silly relationship arguments/disagreements:
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Whats something silly that you and your spouse/boyfriend/significant other argue about thats completely silly?
         </strong>
         Obviously we have pretty silly disagreements: first the toilet paper, now the bed-making. I would say that how we load the dishwasher is another funny one, but well save that for another time 
         <strong>
          Happy weekend everyone!
         </strong>
        </p>
