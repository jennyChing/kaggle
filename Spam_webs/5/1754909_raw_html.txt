                say it with flowers
               </h1>
               <span class="itemDateCreated">

                </time>

              <div class="itemFullText" itemprop="articleBody">
               <p>
                Flowers are a classic go-to when you want to send a message, like 'high five for your new digs!', 'sorry I cocked up' or 'I like you even more than a Frosty Fruit on a muggy day'. But instead of asking your special someone to read between the lines (or artfully placed stems, as it were), this easy-peasy DIY quite literally spells out what you want to say.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                Just remember to keep your blooming memo short and sweet, or you'll wind up spending more time foraging for flowers than putting your floral letters together! Find the full step-by-step instructions
                <a href="http://honestlyyum.com/8713/diy-floral-letters/" target="_blank">
                 here
                </a>
                .
               </p>
