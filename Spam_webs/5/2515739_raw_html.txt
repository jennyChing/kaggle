           <h2 class="title" style="margin-top: 0px !important;">
            <a href="http://www.torontobrigantine.org/summer-program/2015-adult-sails/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to 2015 Adult Sails">
             2015 Adult Sails
            </a>
           </h2>
           <div class="entry">
            <p>
             We have an exciting opportunity to offer this summer for Adults!
            </p>
            <p>
             Trip to Cape Vincent  (21+)              $375+HST
            </p>
            <p>
             You will join Pathfinder on Thursday June 25th at her dock in Toronto. From there the ship will sail to Cape Vincent, NY-The only town in the US where you can enjoy both the St Lawrence and Lake Ontario at the same time- for the Historical Festival. An event with tons of activities including music, battle skirmishes, reenactors AND our sister ship St Lawrence II. Saturday avenue and Sunday will be spent in Cape Vincent. The weekend will conclude with a sail to Kingston with SLII.
            </p>
            <p>
             Thursday June 25th to Sunday June 28th. Toronto to Kingston with a visit to Cape Vincent, NY. Please note that return Transportation from Kingston is not provided.
            </p>
            <p>
             REGISTER
             <a href="http://www.torontobrigantine.org/registration/summer-registration/">
              HERE
             </a>
            </p>
