       <p>
        <em>
         This feel good, tastes good, and do some good in the world post has been brought to you by Karma Kisses Tea House.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/KarmaKisses-Rice-Tea.jpg">
         <img alt="Karma Kisses Rice Tea" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7845" height="450" src="//BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/KarmaKisses-Rice-Tea.jpg" width="600"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        I had never heard of rice tea before. That is until recently, when I was asked if Id like to try some rice tea from
        <a class="external" href="http://www.karmakisses.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Karma Kisses Tea House
        </a>
        . Honestly, I was intrigued by not only rice tea, but also by the companys name.
       </p>
       <p>
        What a great name is Karma Kisses? I know, right?
       </p>
       <p>
        First let me tell you a little about Karma Kisses:
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         Our mission at KarmaKisses is to create high-performance natural foods and drinks that are powered by nature and supported by science. We use potent plants, organic ingredients and a healthy dose of love for what we do. We are committed to helping you maintain your very best health, at the same time as helping those in need and protecting the planets resources. Our commitment is backed by action, with earth- and animal-friendly practices.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        I highly recommend you read their
        <a href="www.karmakisses.com/2013/08/our-story.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         story
        </a>
        , I think, like me, youll be impressed with Karma Kisses Tea House.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Karma-Kisses-Yummy-Young-Rice-Tea.jpg">
         <img alt="Karma Kisses Rice Tea Yummy Young" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7846" height="450" src="//BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Karma-Kisses-Yummy-Young-Rice-Tea.jpg" width="600"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        I tried these 3 teas:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         <a class="external" href="http://www.karmakisses.com/2013/08/yummy-young-rice-tea_1.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Yummy Young Rice Tea
          </strong>
         </a>
         
         <i>
          Apple blossoms in the spring A sweet lively awakening Yummy Young Rice Tea releases the goodness of organic rice, apple and a combination of fruit and berry extracts into your cup. It delivers a potent dose of antioxidants, a powerful blend to repair and defend your body  to help you fight the daily stresses of aging
         </i>
        </li>
        <li>
         <a class="external" href="http://www.karmakisses.com/2013/08/sweet-peace-rice-tea.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Sweet Peace Rice Tea
          </strong>
         </a>
         
         <i>
          Flow away from the daily stresses of life and take a deep breath to release your tensions Enjoy the calming, magical power of the Irish Sea. The precious, organic Irish red seaweed combines with organic rice to relax the mind and stay the troubles of the soul. It helps promote a restful state of peace with all the natural seaweed minerals and fruit nutrients found in abundance beneath the water.
         </i>
        </li>
        <li>
         <a class="external" href="http://www.karmakisses.com/2013/08/silky-skin-rice-tea.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Silky Skin Rice Tea
          </strong>
         </a>
         
         <i>
          From high in the Tibetan Himalayas, where time is still being born, comes Sea Buckthorn. Not to be confused with common buckthorn, this is one of the most nutritious fruits on the planet. Combined with organic rice, it delivers unique nutrients to nourish the skin, hair and nails. The precious fruit oils found in Sea Buckthorn, including Omegas 3, 6, and 9, and the elusive essential fatty acid, Omega-7 Silky Skin Rice Tea is a powerful beverage for your healthy youthful glow.
         </i>
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <a href="http://BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Karma-Kisses-Rice-Tea-Sweet-Peace.jpg">
         <img alt="Karma Kisses Rice Tea Sweet Peace" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-7847" height="450" src="//BlogByDonna.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Karma-Kisses-Rice-Tea-Sweet-Peace.jpg" width="600"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        I found all the teas to be rather sweet. If you sweeten your teas, you may find the rice teas perfect. My husband does. Karma Kisses rice teas dont have sugar in them, though. They are sweetened from the all-natural
        <a class="external" href="http://www.karmakisses.com/2013/08/organic-ingredients.html?m=0#.UpS5mI02b-Y" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         sweet leaf
        </a>
        (Stevia). But since I dont sweeten my tea though, I found them just a bit too sweet for my taste. To remedy that, I just added more warm water.
       </p>
       <p>
        The temperature of the water is key, too. You dont want the water too hot because it can break down the nutrients. The tea doesnt come in bags, but in powder form. I found it best to just stir it really well. I plan on experimenting with them in some smoothies. With all the health benefits and all-natural ingredients, I think I could come up with some tasty and healthy smoothies.
       </p>
       <p>
        I love that they only use organic, non-GMO ingredients in their rice teas. I can feel good about my family and I drinking them.
       </p>
       <p>
        Another thing that I LOVE about Karma Kisses Rice Tea is their Good Karma Fund. With every cup of KarmaKisses Rice Tea you drink, you are not only taking care of yourself, but also, supporting underprivileged children, organic farmers and the environment. Now that some good karma!
       </p>
       <p>
        From their site:
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <div>
         We have committed 20% of all profits to our GoodKarmaFund. This dedicated fund supports charities for children care, organic farming and environmental protection. It might also respond to international emergency or poverty relief when needed.
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         We have undertaken to support only outstanding charities with a great cause and a strong proven track record  where the money is put to the best use possible.
        </div>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        Check out the
        <a class="external" href="http://www.karmakisses.com/2013/08/good-karma-fund.html?m=0#.UpS6cI02b-Y" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         charities that Karma Kisses Tea House supports
        </a>
        . Im a firm believer in karma  What goes around, comes around.
       </p>
       <p>
        If you are a tea drinker or know someone who is, and you want to support good health, organic farmers, our environment, and some worthwhile charities, you can purchase
        <a class="external" href="http://www.karmakisses.com/2013/08/menu.html?m=0#.UpS8dY02b-Y" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Karma Kisses Rice Teas
        </a>
        and have them delivered right to your door.
       </p>
