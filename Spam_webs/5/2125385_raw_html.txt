       <p>
        Voici la nouvelle
        <a href="http://www.lc470.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.lc470.com']);" target="_blank">
         Lucra LC 470
        </a>
        , la bi-place qui s'inspire des classiques Europennes des annes 50. Avec son moteur V8 d'origine GM, elle acclre de 0  60 miles en 3,4 sec. pour une vitesse dpassant les 257 km/h. Construite  Los Angeles, elle allie confort de conduite moderne, maniabilit exceptionnelle et superbes formes arodynamiques. La LC 470 est vendue  partir de 85 000 $.
        <span id="more-28788">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28789" height="168" src="http://viacomit.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/Lucra-LC470-pic-01.jpg" title="Lucra-LC470-pic-01" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="color: #888888;">
         <a href="http://www.lc470.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.lc470.com']);" target="_blank">
          Lucra Cars
         </a>
         , based out of Los Angeles, have revealed the specifications and details of their 2010 LC470 sports/racing car. A one-piece carbon fiber body fits the tubular steel chassis like a glove. Independent suspension all round smoothes the ride yet allows the car to pull 1.3 lateral G through high speed curves and tight corners. Depending on the choice of GM motors mated to a close-ratio Tremec gearbox, the LC470 offers 0-60 in 3.4 seconds, a standing quarter mile in 9.1 secs, 150 mph over the  mile line and top speeds above 180 mph. Performance and handling of a true supercar at prices starting well below $100k. With an eye to comfort and control, brakes, clutch, gas pedals and the gearshift position can be set to suit the driver.
        </span>
       </p>
