        <h1>
         <img alt="Amethyst" src="http://www.mamacreepy.com/images/amethyst.jpg" style="width:26px;height:25px;border:0;box-shadow:none;vertical-align:middle;"/>
         <span style="color:rgb(125,86,129);">
          Welcome to Mama Cs!
         </span>
        </h1>
        Thank you for stopping in to our home on the web! Please take a moment to read our Policies page to insure a pleasant shopping experience.
        <p>
         We carry tools to support many spiritual and magickal traditions. Whether you practice Buddhism, Santeria, Wicca, Witchcraft, Asatru, Druidry, Norse, Germanic, or Reconstructionist Paganism, Goddess spirituality or another path, we have something just for you! No matter if you are an experienced Priestess or a beginner, we have what you need to help you along your way.
        </p>
        <p>
         We carry a huge selection of both the magickal and the mundane. Oils and oil warmers, resin, stick and cone incense and vegan perfumes serve to create an aromatic environment. Books of Shadows and journals allow you to record recipes, spells, thoughts and dreams. For those who practice divination, we carry a wide range of Tarot cards, pendulums, crystal balls, and exquisite scrying mirrors. To store them safely, we have boxes and bags of all sizes!
        </p>
        <p>
         Symbols such as the Celtic Triquetra, Om, Pentagram and Triple Moon grace cauldrons, rings and candleholders. From small chime candles to large Seven Day candles, we have selections for every need.
        </p>
