                Choosing The Right Shelter Dog  an essential guide for anyone looking to adopt a rescue dog.
               </h1>
               <div class="post-meta the-icons pmeta-alt">
                <span class="post-author vcard">

                  April 2, 2015
                 </abbr>
                </span>
                <span class="meta-no-display">
                 <a href="http://www.thehouseofdog.co.uk/choosing-the-right-shelter-dog-an-essential-guide-for-anyone-looking-to-adopt-a-rescue-dog/" rel="bookmark">
                  Choosing The Right Shelter Dog  an essential guide for anyone looking to adopt a rescue dog.

                <span class="date updated meta-no-display">

              <div class="post-content">
               <div class="entry-content">
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 <a href="http://493170518.r.lightningbase-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/2449_CHOOSING-THE-RIGHT-SHELTER-DOG-COVER.jpeg" rel="nofollow">
                  <img alt="CHOOSING-THE-RIGHT-SHELTER-DOG-COVER" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-2450" height="320" src="http://493170518.r.lightningbase-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/2449_CHOOSING-THE-RIGHT-SHELTER-DOG-COVER.jpeg" width="207"/>
                 </a>
                 Author and dog trainer Jill Keaton is pleased to announce the release of her book
                 <em>
                  
                  <strong>
                   Choosing The Right Shelter Dog
                  </strong>
                 </em>
                 <strong>
                  .
                 </strong>
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 <em>
                  <strong>
                  </strong>
                 </em>
                 <em>
                  <strong>
                   Choosing The Right Shelter Dog
                  </strong>
                 </em>
                 is a must read for anyone looking to adopt a rescue or shelter dog.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 While your heart may be in the right place, if you are unprepared, or allow your emotions to cloud the decision process,
                 <img alt="" class="alignright" height="268" src="http://493170518.r.lightningbase-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/2449_Jill-Ducati-Photo.jpg" width="320"/>
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 you could wind up taking home the
                 <em>
                  wrong dog.
                 </em>
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 <strong>
                  <em>
                   Choosing The Right Shelter Dog
                  </em>
                 </strong>
                 is chock full of valuable information such as:
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 How to choose the breed of dog that is right for you and your environment.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 What supplies to get before bringing home your shelter dog.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 How to prepare your home for the arrival of your shelter dog.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 How to test the temperament of a shelter dog&amp; much more!
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 <img alt="" class="alignleft" height="284" src="http://493170518.r.lightningbase-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/2449_Jill-Keaton-Photo.jpg" width="320"/>
                 The information in this easy to read guide will not only help set you up for success, but give you the tools to make the best decision possible and serve as your road map as you journey down the path to find your four-legged soul mate.
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 <em>
                  As an in-home dog trainer/behaviorist, Jills mission is to help owners better understand the motivation behind their dogs actions in order to make change happen. Jill has helped thousands of owners improve communication with their dogs and in turn, improve the quality of life they share together.
                 </em>
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 <em>
                  Jill has been working with dogs and their owners for over 13 years and is a member of The Pet Professional Guild and APDT. As a positive reinforcement trainer, Jill is a firm believer in using force-free training methods in order to achieve long-term success.
                 </em>
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 <em>
                  Jills goal is to teach owners how to transform their dogs behaviors by becoming better teachers, leaders and communicators.
                 </em>
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">
                 <em>
                  Jill currently lives in Littleton, MA, USA and is the owner of
                  <a href="http://www.thesocialcanine.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                   The Social Canine doggie daycare
                  </a>
                  .
                 </em>
                </p>
                <p style="text-align: left;">

                  (in paperback only).
                 </em>
