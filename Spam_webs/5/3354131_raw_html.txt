        <h1 title="Skin Tightening Options Portland">
         What skin tightening options are available to Portland area patients?
        </h1>
        Nobody enjoys the signs of aging. Skin laxity, which can result in fine lines and wrinkles, are often the first sign of an aging body. After patients turn 30, they begin to lose collagen, which gives volume and youthfulness to the skin. Collagen loss results in loose skin on the face, which can contribute to the development of fine lines and wrinkles. While some Portland area patients are happy with the use of dermal fillers, injectables, and even collagen stimulating products on the face, others want a more long-term result that doesn't require surgical intervention. This is when Dr. Adam Maddox speaks with patients about their skin tightening options, and may often recommend a treatment called Exilis.
        <br/>
        <br/>
