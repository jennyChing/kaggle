       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Chiffon Cake
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.zrecipes.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/chiffon-cake-zr.jpg">
          <img alt="Chiffon Cake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-304" height="423" src="http://www.zrecipes.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/chiffon-cake-zr.jpg" width="636"/>
         </a>
         This
         <strong>
          Chiffon Cake recipe
         </strong>
         is a true delight! A delicious cake!
        </p>
        <div style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-4947814141550636" data-ad-slot="8616354987" style="display:inline-block;width:336px;height:280px">
         </ins>
         <script>
          (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
         </script>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          6 ounces (175 grams) flour
         </li>
         <li>
          7 eggs
         </li>
         <li>
           a teacup powdered chocolate
         </li>
         <li>
          7 ounces (200 grams) sugar
         </li>
         <li>
           a teacup oil
         </li>
         <li>
          3 teaspoons yeast
         </li>
         <li>
           a teaspoon bicarbonate
         </li>
         <li>
          1 teaspoon vanilla extract
         </li>
         <li>
          A pinch of salt
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          How to make Chiffon Cake
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Start by separating the egg yolks from the whites and melt the chocolate in some hot water.
         </li>
         <li>
          Then, mix the flour very well with the yeast, the sugar, the vanilla extract, the egg yolks, the oil and the chocolate.
         </li>
         <li>
          On the side, whip the egg whites stiff, along with the bicarbonate. Then, mix it softly with the dough.
         </li>
         <li>
          Pour the dough in a baking pan powdered with flour and greased.
         </li>
         <li>
          It takes about an hour for the chiffon cake to be ready.
         </li>
        </ul>
