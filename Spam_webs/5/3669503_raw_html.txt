                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Number 84 is made up of the vibrations and energies of number 8 and number 4.
                       <a href="http://numerology-thenumbersandtheirmeanings.blogspot.com/2011/05/number-8.html" target="_blank">
                        Number 8
                       </a>
                       resonates with the attributes of
                       <b>
                        <a href="http://bodysoulmind.net/attracting-and-manifesting-what-you-want-into-your-life" target="_blank">
                         manifesting abundance and prosperity
                        </a>
                       </b>
                       ,inner-strength and
                       <b>
                        <a href="http://bodysoulmind.net/the-inner-voice-of-wisdom" target="_blank">
                         inner-wisdom
                        </a>
                       </b>
                       , patience, self-confidence, giving and receiving, achieving success, employment, practicality, and
                       <b>
                        <a href="http://hubpages.com/hub/KARMA-What-Is-It-And-How-Does-It-Work" target="_blank">
                         karma
                        </a>
                       </b>
                       - the
                       <b>
                        <a href="http://universalspirituallaws.blogspot.com.au/2010/09/spiritual-law-of-karma.html" target="_blank">
                         Universal Spiritual Lawof Cause and Effect
                        </a>
                       </b>
                       .
                       <a href="http://numerology-thenumbersandtheirmeanings.blogspot.com/2011/02/number-4.html" target="_blank">
                        Number 4
                       </a>
                       carries the vibrations of hard work and building solid foundations, effort and application, devotion, practicality, honesty and integrity,endurance and determination, dependability and responsibility, stability and ability, passion and drive.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Angel Number 84 is a message from the
                       <b>
                        <a href="http://psychicjoanne.blogspot.com/2010/02/angels.html" target="_blank">
                         angels
                        </a>
                       </b>
                       that you need to see yourself in a higher light (as your true self as a Divine being). You have a lot of work to do andmuch to achieve in this lifetime and your angels fully encourage and support you. Your
                       <b>
                        <a href="http://hubpages.com/hub/The-Purpose-of-Your-Life-Your-Life-Mission" target="_blank">
                         Divine life mission
                        </a>
                       </b>
                       requires that you be your authentic self and work at your mostbrilliance in order to achieve success on all levels.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Angel Number 84 indicates that the discipline and hard work you have put into achieving your goals and aspirations have been recognized and acknowledged. Yourdetermination and efforts have been worth your while as work done well in the past is bringing you rewards today. Your angels encourage you to keep up the great workin order to continue to achieve and attain your desired results and outcomes. As you work diligently towards achieving your goals you will
                       <b>
                        <a href="http://bodysoulmind.net/6-steps-to-successful-manifestation" target="_blank">
                         manifest success
                        </a>
                       </b>
                       on manylevels.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       The repeating Angel Number 84 is also a message from your angels that an important cycle or phase in your life is about to end. Your angels want to assure you thatthese changes are necessary and will usher in positive opportunities and situations that will be better suited to your wants, needs and
                       <b>
                        <a href="http://psychicjoanne.blogspot.com.au/2012/03/our-life-purpose.html" target="_blank">
                         Divine life purpose
                        </a>
                       </b>
                       and
                       <b>
                        <a href="http://bodysoulmind.net/discover-your-souls-mission" target="_blank">
                         soulmission
                        </a>
                       </b>
                       .  This may be in relation to your career and/or income earning capacities.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       If you are being prompted to begin or expand a
                       <b>
                        <a href="http://hubpages.com/hub/How-To-Turn-Your-Spirituality-Into-Your-Career" target="_blank">
                         spiritually-based practice, career and/or profession
                        </a>
                       </b>
                       , Angel Number 84 tells you that it is a great time to look to pursuingthis avenue of work. If feeling any fears, doubts or concerns,
                       <b>
                        <a href="http://bodysoulmind.net/guidance-from-the-angels" target="_blank">
                         call upon the angels for guidance
                        </a>
                       </b>
                       and support.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>

                      <a href="http://numerology-thenumbersandtheirmeanings.blogspot.com/2011/02/number-3.html" target="_blank">

                      (8+4=12, 1+2=3) and
                      <b>
                       <a href="http://sacredscribesangelnumbers.blogspot.com/2011/06/angel-number-3.html" target="_blank">
                        Angel Number 3
                       </a>
                      </b>
                      .
                     </span>
