    <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-rail-leftRailSpanner" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
     <h1 class="grub-topOfColumn" itemprop="name" style="font-size: 27px;">
      Fresh Mozzarella Marinated in Basil &amp; Lime
     </h1>
     <img height="487" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Fresh-Mozzarella-with-Basil-and-Lime-Close-Up-790x487.jpg" width="790"/>
     <div class="yui3-g">
      <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-rail-well">
       <div class="grub-byline" style="margin-top: 1em;">
        Recipe by
        <a href="https://plus.google.com/113274240511284571445" rel="author">
         Jerry James Stone
        </a>
         April 26, 2013
       </div>
       <div class="grub-mainPostContent" itemprop="recipeInstructions" not-style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 23px;">
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="617" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/fresh-mozzarella-marinated-in-basil-lime/fresh-mozzarella-with-basil-and-lime-close-up/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/fresh-mozzarella-with-basil-and-lime-close-up" rel="attachment wp-att-2735">
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Marinated mozzarella, it reminds me of warm summer days and picnics. It can be such a treat. I cannot tell you how many delicatessens have taken my money for this stuff. Its so damn good but so damn pricy.
        </p>
        <p>
         Then it occurred to me that I could make my own! Sometimes I am a bit slow. This mozzarella is crisp and savory. Perfect for your next caprese salad. Thats a hint by the way.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="450" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/WLJK1Jx4fwo" width="600">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <div class="yui3-g" id="grub-recipeSummary">
         <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-recipeSummary-left">
          <h2>
           Ingredients
          </h2>
          <ul id="grub-ingredients">
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
             Pound fresh mozzarella
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Tablespoon Cilantro, coarsely chopped
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Tablespoon Basil, coarsely chopped
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
             Teaspoon Tarragon, coarsely chopped
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
             Cup Olive Oil
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            3 lime peels
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 clove of
            <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/ingredient/garlic/">
             garlic
            </a>
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Teaspoon Peppercorns
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
             Teaspoon Salt
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-recipeSummary-right">
          <h2>
           <a class="grub-button" href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/fresh-mozzarella-marinated-in-basil-lime/printable/" target="_blank">
            <span class="grub-printIcon">
            </span>
            Printable View
           </a>
          </h2>
          <h2>
           Yield:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            1/2 pound of marinated mozzarella
           </span>
          </h2>
          <meta content="PT6H10M" itemprop="totalTime"/>
          <h2>
           Total Time
           <div style="float: right;">
            6 hours, 10 minutes
           </div>
          </h2>
          <meta content="PT10M" itemprop="prepTime"/>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
           Prep time
           <div style="float: right">
            10 minutes
           </div>
          </div>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
           Inactive time
           <div style="float: right">
            6 hours
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="grub-recipeTaxonomyList">
           <h2>
            Recipe Type:
           </h2>
           Appetizer
          </div>
          <div class="grub-recipeTaxonomyList">
           <h2>
            Main Ingredient:
           </h2>
           Mozzarella
          </div>
          <div class="grub-recipeTaxonomyList">
           <h2>
            Diet:
           </h2>
           Vegetarian
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         1. Take the 1/2 pound of fresh mozzarella and cut it into slices. You could also do cubes, circles or unicorns if you wanted. Just depends on what you plan to use it for. I am using this for a caprese salad so I want sheets of mozzarella for it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="666" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/caprese-salad-with-bourbon-peaches-mozzarella/fresh-mozzarella/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/fresh-mozzarella" rel="attachment wp-att-2354">
          <img alt="Fresh Mozzarella" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2354" height="399" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Fresh-Mozzarella-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Place the mozzarella in a dish and add the tablespoon of cilantro, tablespoon of basil, 1/4 teaspoon tarragon, 3 limes peels, one clove of garlic, teaspoon peppercorns, and 1/4 teaspoon salt.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="722" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/fresh-mozzarella-marinated-in-basil-lime/marinated-mozzarella/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/marinated-mozzarella" rel="attachment wp-att-2739">
          <img alt="Marinated Mozzarella" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2739" height="433" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Marinated-Mozzarella-600x433.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="726" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/fresh-mozzarella-marinated-in-basil-lime/basil-mozzarella/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/basil-mozzarella" rel="attachment wp-att-2738">
          <img alt="Basil Mozzarella" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2738" height="435" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Basil-Mozzarella-600x435.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Now add the 1/2 cup olive oil. It is really important to use a good olive oil for this because your mozzarella is gonna taste like it after. I used this lovely
         <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biodynamic_agriculture">
          biodynamic
         </a>
         olive oil from
         <a href="http://www.ambythestate.com/">
          AmByth Estate
         </a>
         . If you like good wine, I suggest checking them out. More importantly, if you live in Californiavisit the Paso Robles area and check them out. They are by appointment only but they have a very charming operation.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="666" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/fresh-mozzarella-marinated-in-basil-lime/fresh-mozzarella-with-basil-and-lime-main/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/fresh-mozzarella-with-basil-and-lime-main" rel="attachment wp-att-2736">
          <img alt="Fresh Mozzarella with Basil and Lime Main" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2736" height="399" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Fresh-Mozzarella-with-Basil-and-Lime-Main-600x399.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         4. Now that the mozzarella is covered with yummy olive oil, fresh herbs and spiceswe are going to let it sit in the fridge for about 6 hours to infuse it with all those flavors. Enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="1443" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/fresh-mozzarella-marinated-in-basil-lime/fresh-mozzarella-with-basil-and-lime-full/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/fresh-mozzarella-with-basil-and-lime-full" rel="attachment wp-att-2737">
          <img alt="Fresh Mozzarella with Basil and Lime Full" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2737" height="865" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Fresh-Mozzarella-with-Basil-and-Lime-Full-600x865.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
