    <p>
     The color blue, in many cultures, symbolizes wisdom, trust, depth, intelligence, confidence, faith, and truth. Its the color of the sky and the ocean, and so its also often associated with tranquility and calmness. Below are 50 examples of blue logos. Notice the variety of blues, from lapis lazuli to sky blue, used in these logos.
