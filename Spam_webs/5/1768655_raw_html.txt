          Listen: Original Demo of Sepulturas Roots Bloody Roots

         <div class="entry clearfix">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.revolvermag.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-06-at-3.22.56-PM.png">
            <img alt="sepultura 1996" class="size-full wp-image-60732 alignleft" height="287" src="http://www.revolvermag.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Screen-Shot-2014-06-06-at-3.22.56-PM.png" width="294"/>

           Gloria Cavalerawife and manager of the bands founding frontman Max Cavalerahas posted the original demo track for Sepulturas classic song Roots Bloody Roots on her website
           <a href="http://www.gloriacavalera.com/2014/06/who-wrote-rootsyou-decide.html" target="_blank">
            GloriaCavalera.com
           </a>
           ; Max, of course, is currently in Soulfly, Killer Be Killed, and the Cavalera Conspiracy. Check it out, and read what Gloria has to say about the track below or on
           <a href="http://www.gloriacavalera.com/2014/06/who-wrote-rootsyou-decide.html" target="_blank">
            her site
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            As I promised once, I will let you all hear the early tape Max made when he was writing at the kitchen table when I was pregnant with Igor. Enjoy and have a good discussion, my Students! I am sure there will be many comments around the classroom..
           </p>
           <p>
            After Max wrote a big portion of the album, Roadrunner owner and CEO, Cees Wessel flew Max and myself to Amsterdam for a meeting with the marketing team. Max laid out his ideathe title, artwork and concept along with the music on his machine. We all had a laugh when the label suggested the title may be confusing to the fans and they will think it an R&amp;B release!
           </p>
           <p>
            Since we were in Europe and the band still in Brazil, Max and I took a train to Vienna and then on to Salzburg, Austria, for a breather. I was quite pregnant at the time.
           </p>
           <p>
            When the band finished their months long break and came to Phoenix to jam, Max had his idea fully planted. Have fun with this class, Studentsthe truth be told
