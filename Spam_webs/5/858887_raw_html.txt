        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Nutella Black and White Sandwich Cookies
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-02-05T09:28:10+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          February 5, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://momentswithmandi.com/author/mandi/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Mandi Welbaum
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://momentswithmandi.com/nutella-black-and-white-sandwich-cookies/#comments">
           3 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <a href="http://momentswithmandi.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Nutella-Black-and-White-Sandwich-Cookies.jpg">
          <img alt="Nutella Black and White Sandwich Cookies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23849" height="435" src="http://momentswithmandi.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Nutella-Black-and-White-Sandwich-Cookies.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Who doesnt love Nutella? Did you know that there is a WORLD NUTELLA DAY? Im not kidding! Its time to break the golden seal on that jar and make these delicious
         <strong>
          Nutella Black &amp; White Sandwich Cookies
         </strong>
         ! This is an easy recipe to follow and the results are amazing. Youll enjoy these with a big glass of ice cold milk, and they make a great little treat to give to a neighbor or a friend. Nutella makes everything better, am I right?
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">

         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Nutella Black and White Sandwich Cookies
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSDetails">
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSTimes">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          These cookies are easy and delicious!
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 C. Nutella
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tbs. sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 egg
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             C. flour + 1 tbs.
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            8-10
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            silicone baking cups
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            or a 12ct. muffin tin
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            8-10 black &amp; white sandwich cookies
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             C. chocolate chips (optional)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Combine the Nutella, sugar, egg and flour and mix well.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Lay 1 sandwich cookie into the bottom of muffin tin or silicon baking cup. I love the silicone baking cups  no mess and they peel right off the cookies! ;)
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Scoop about 2 tbs. of the Nutella cookie dough onto the top of each sandwich cookie.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place a few chocolate chips onto the top of each cookie (optional).
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake 10-15 minutes on 350 degrees. Insert a toothpick into the Nutella cookies and when it comes out 'dry' they are done.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Store in an airtight container 1-2 weeks. Makes 8-10 servings. 1 cookie = 1 serving.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
