             <section class="article-content">
              <p>
               Get ready to join the no 'poo movement  no, not
               <em>
                that
               </em>
               kind of movement.
              </p>
              <p>
               Plenty of people have switched over to the
               <a href="http://www.nopoomethod.com/" target="_blank">
                no 'poo
               </a>
               lifestyle, which involves cleansing your hair without a commercial shampoo. The all-natural alternative boasts healthy
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/hair/">
                hair
               </a>
               , but also helps the environment by using less plastic and washing less chemicals down the drain.
              </p>
              <div class="see-also">
               <p>
                See also:
                <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2014/07/21/diy-bobby-pins/">
                 9 Practical Uses for Bobby Pins That Don't Involve Hair
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <p>
               It's time to unlock the natural luster of your locks without dropping a ton of cash on expensive, harmful treatments. Turning heads has never been this easy.
              </p>
