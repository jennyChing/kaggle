        <p>
         <img alt="Fall Theatre Season - What's on with Mirvish" class="alignleft wp-image-1982" height="197" src="http://amextappedin.ca/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Fall-Theatre-Season-Whats-on-with-Mirvish.jpg" width="298"/>
         The fall theatre season is one of the best times of year to escape dropping temperatures and get swept away by a live stage production. With the busy summer season now past and the family back into a routine, September and October are the perfect months to plan a theatre show with the family before the Christmas holidays.
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out some of the top performances playing at Mirvish theatres in Toronto this fall:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mirvish.com/shows/cats">
          <b>
           CATS
          </b>
         </a>
         (
         <i>
          Extended through September 22
          <sup>
           nd
          </sup>
          , 2013
         </i>
         )
        </p>
        <p>
         Be one of the first to experience an all-new Canadian production of CATS. For the first time in over 25 years, an all-Canadian cast graces the Toronto stage with Andrew Lloyd Webbers beloved musical masterpiece.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mirvish.com/shows/lesmiserables" target="_blank" title="Les Miserables">
          <b>
           Les
          </b>
         </a>
         <b>
          <a href="http://www.mirvish.com/shows/lesmiserables" target="_blank" title="Les Miserables">
           Misrables
          </a>
         </b>
         (
         <i>
          Begins September 27
          <sup>
           th,
          </sup>
          2013)
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         Cameron Mackintosh presents his acclaimed new production of Boublil &amp; Schnbergs legendary musical, Les Misrables, with glorious new staging and dazzlingly reimagined scenery inspired by the paintings of Victor Hugo. This new production has been acclaimed by critics, fans and new audiences and is breaking box office records wherever it goes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mirvish.com/shows/ilovelucyliveonstage">
          <b>
           I Love Lucy Live on
          </b>
         </a>
         <b>
          Stage
         </b>
         (
         <i>
          Begins October 15
          <sup>
           th
          </sup>
          , 2013
         </i>
         )
        </p>
        <p>
         I Love Lucy is the brand-new hit stage show adapted from one of the most beloved programs in television history. Americas favorite foursome  Lucy, Ricky, Fred and Ethel  is live on stage and in color for the very first time in Canada.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://amex.co/ZHJJy2" target="_blank" title="American Express Cardmembers">
          American Express Cardmembers
         </a>
         can get have reserved access to a great block of seats for these shows. Make sure to use your
         <a href="http://amex.co/12wg8xL" target="_blank">
          American Express Card
         </a>
         to purchase tickets through
         <a href="https://www316.americanexpress.com/iFormsSecure/un/iforms.do?cuid=fotl_en_CA&amp;evttype=0&amp;evtsrc=link" target="_blank" title="FOTL">
          Front Of The Line
         </a>
         to get early ticket access. Then sit back, relax and enjoy the show!
        </p>
