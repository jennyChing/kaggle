            Adult Desserts:  Strawberry-Watermelon Popsicles &amp; Champagne
           </a>
          </h1>
          <div class="meta single">
           <span class="meta-author">
            <a href="http://www.zomppa.com/author/patty/" rel="author" title="Posts by Patty">

          <p>
           Remember popsicles?
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.zomppa.com/2011/07/07/adult-desserts-strawberry-watermelon-popsicles-champagne/watermelon/" rel="attachment wp-att-16602">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-16602" height="459" src="http://www.zomppa.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/watermelon-1024x765.jpg" title="watermelon" width="614"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           When was the last time you had a popsicle? One of those sticky, succulent, sweet and refreshing icons most generally associated with summertime heat and good times? My sister and I had a popsicle at least once a day during the summer. With the cacophonous sounds of crickets chirping in our backyard and the hazy orange-red sun settling into the horizon, my sister and I would steadily slurp on our sticky-sweet treats, hoping we could catch the popsicle drips before they oozed all over our chins and palms.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           <a href="http://www.zomppa.com/2011/07/07/adult-desserts-strawberry-watermelon-popsicles-champagne/watermelong-ii/" rel="attachment wp-att-16605">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-16605" height="342" src="http://www.zomppa.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/watermelong-II-1024x570.jpg" title="watermelong II" width="614"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Sigh. Those were the days.
          </p>
          <p>
           At some point, I stopped eating/enjoying a good old-fashioned popsicle. I cant remember the exact age at which the popsicle became obsolete in my world, but I suspect it was sometime before I hit the age of 13.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           It was then that I reluctantly understood that sucking on a popsicle as a full grown adult was slightly inappropriatemaybe even a little obscene. Anytime I think of someone licking/sucking on a popsicle, the scene usually involves an extraordinarily gorgeous female performing tongue acrobatics with a red (most likely strawberry flavored) popsicle. Somehow, overnight, I understood that the popsicle was synonymous with sex.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           <a href="http://www.zomppa.com/2011/07/07/adult-desserts-strawberry-watermelon-popsicles-champagne/watermelon-i/" rel="attachment wp-att-16606">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-16606" height="460" src="http://www.zomppa.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/watermelon-I-1024x767.jpg" title="watermelon I((" width="614"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           How did this happen?? I doubt I am the only one who feels a little dirty when it comes to enjoying a popsicle. When was the last time you sucked on a popsicle in public? Outside the privacy of your own home? Years, right? Maybe decades? Yes, same here.
          </p>
          <p>
           When did it become inappropriate for adults to suck on popsicles? More importantly, why did this happen? Sure, the popsicle is ever so slightly phallic in nature, but so is the cucumber? Why dont I have the same strange, big-brother-is-watching-you effect when I chomp down on a cumber?? Hummm.
          </p>
          <p>
           In any case, I am here to tell you that popsicles are very much retro-chic and that you should not be deterred by whatever inhibitions youve been conditioned to harbor. These
           <strong>
            Strawberry-Watermelon
           </strong>
           popsicles served with champagne are an excellent summertime treat for any and all adults. Minus the champagne, they are an excellent and nutritious mid-day pickme-up on a hot summers day.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.zomppa.com/2011/07/07/adult-desserts-strawberry-watermelon-popsicles-champagne/watermelong-iv/" rel="attachment wp-att-16607">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-16607" height="459" src="http://www.zomppa.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/watermelong-IV-1024x765.jpg" title="watermelong IV" width="614"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Strawberry-Watermelon Popsicles in Champagne
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           1 pint of strawberries
          </p>
          <p>
            a watermelon
          </p>
          <p>
           1 Bottle of Champagne (optional)
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Directions
           </em>
           :
          </p>
          <p>
           Blend, in a blender, the strawberries and watermelon.
          </p>
          <p>
           Pour into popsicle molds and follow mold instructions.
          </p>
          <p>
           Once completely froze, place popsicles in wine glasses wide enough to fit the popsicles and pour in champagne. I used the cheap kind of champagne, but feel free to use the more expense stuff!
          </p>
