      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        Are you ready for a transformation?
       </p>
       <p>
        Life changing income?
       </p>
       <p>
        A solid, lucrative system for making money online?
       </p>
       <p>
        Congratulations! You are in the right place, which youll soon see as you read on.
       </p>
       <p>
        Whether you are that person who is struggling to be successful in a home based business; or a seasoned, successful business builder looking for a way to take your business online; or someone looking to cut through the layers of hype, fluff and bogus information and systems, you will love what you find here.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our vision at Pure Leverage is quite different than what you may have found in other companies, and other systems. We are different because we are truly committed in not only providing you with top notch products to market, sell and profit from; a lucrative pay plan where you keep most all of the profits; but also the training and mentoring you need to be successful.
       </p>
       <p>
        We offer something that works for the little guy; the part timer; the stay home mom; the non guru who doesnt have time to figure out complicated systems, set up and marketing.
       </p>
       <p>
        We also offer something for the full time, home business internet marketer who wants to add a primary or additional income stream.
       </p>
       <p>
        If youve looked around, or been involved in some other programs, you know what I am talking about.
       </p>
       <p>
        The confusion, the frustration is overwhelming, and causes most people to throw up their hands in despair. Learning how to market; be an entrepreneur; and finding real success can be challenging.
       </p>
       <p>
        We are here to change that for you and anyone looking for a change.
       </p>
       <p>
        Here are some statistics youll find interesting:
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         Every 11 seconds, someone starts a new home based business.
        </p>
        <p>
         Over 70 Million People earn an income in Home Based Internet and Network Marketing and Direct Sales.
        </p>
        <p>
         Sadly, fewer than 5% people in this industry do it full time.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        And the reason? Because they cant afford to do it full time. In the past, there just has not been enough time in the day to work a full time job, care for a family and other obligations and then try to build a side business and create the income needed to quit the job.
       </p>
       <p>
        The reality is, many of the old methods of building a leveraged income takes way too long. Calling family and friends; begging people who have no interest to take a look; scheduling home meetings and constantly looking for new people to show your business to  its a long, slow, painful process.
       </p>
       <p>
        Not any more!
       </p>
       <p>
        The internet changed everything.
       </p>
       <p>
        With over 3 million people a DAY logging on and looking for a way to make money, doesnt it make sense to put yourself out there, with your business, so that some of those people can find you?
       </p>
       <p>
        Our
        <strong>
         #1 priority
        </strong>
        is to help you grow and prosper; not only financially, but personally, and give you the ability to not only create positive change in your own life, but also the lives of others, all around the globe.
       </p>
       <p>
        Weve built our system around the average, normal, non-guru individual, who wants to learn how to make an income, online, without home parties, hotel meetings, grinding it out on the phones, calling leads and people who arent really interested or motivated.
