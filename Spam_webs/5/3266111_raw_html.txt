       <h1 class="entry-title">
        All-Time Favorite Snack as a Grab N Go Breakfast: Apple Peanut Butter Smoothie (and win a hamper of Pip &amp; Nut all-natural nut butters)
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2015-04-09T16:40:20+00:00">
         April 9, 2015
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://www.theworktop.com/author/the-worktop/" rel="author">
           Tina Jui
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://www.theworktop.com/breakfast-brunch-recipes/apple-peanut-butter-smoothie/#comments">
          8 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <em>
          This Apple and Peanut Butter Smoothie takes an all-time favorite snack and turns it into a grab n go breakfast! Its delicious, super easy to make, and will give you a great start to the morning! Its also a great afternoon pick-me-up, or a perfect snack to have before hitting the gym.
         </em>
         <span id="more-4035">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Apple Peanut Butter Smoothie" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4039" height="417" src="http://www.theworktop.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Apple-Peanut-Butter-Smoothie-Feature.jpg" width="625"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Heres theclassic combination of apples and peanut butter in a drinkable grab n go form. Its perfect for those busy mornings when you dont have the time to sit down or make ahearty
         <a href="http://www.theworktop.com/breakfast-brunch-recipes/savory/pulled-pork-omelette/" target="_blank" title="Pulled Pork Omelette For Those Hungry Mornings">
          pulled pork omelette
         </a>
         , but it will still fill you up with a lot of goodness.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Apple Peanut Butter Smoothie" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4038" height="937" src="http://www.theworktop.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Apple-Peanut-Butter-Smoothie-3.jpg" width="625"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Besides being entirely delicious and simple to make, this Apple Peanut Butter Smoothiechecks off everything you might want in a breakfast:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Two types of fruit  remember the saying, an apple a day keeps the doctor away? With this smoothie, you not onlygetan apple, but alsoa banana (whoo a bonus!).
         </li>
         <li>
          A good glob of all-natural peanut butter  a great source of protein and vitamin E. Make sure youuse an all-natural peanut butter so theres no added sugar in it. I used my favorite peanut butter by Londons
          <a href="http://www.pipandnut.com/" target="_blank">
           Pip &amp; Nut
          </a>
          , and its so good that Ive teamed up with them to give you a chance to
          <span style="color: #3366ff;">
           win your own jars below
          </span>
          ! Pip pip horray!
         </li>
         <li>
          A generous pourof oat milk  you can use almond milk or regular cows milk, but oat milk offersmore fiber and beta-glutens (which can
          <a href="http://www.eufic.org/page/en/show/latest-science-news/fftid/Eating-oat-beta-glucan-regularly-helps-maintain-normal-blood-cholesterol/" target="_blank">
           help control blood cholesterol level
          </a>
          ) than other milk alternatives, so its a great choice to usein this quick breakfast.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <img alt="Apple Peanut Butter Smoothie" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4037" height="938" src="http://www.theworktop.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Apple-Peanut-Butter-Smoothie-2.jpg" width="625"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Peanut butter on apple slices have always been a favorite of mine, and when I love something, I normally just keep having itover and over again. But Im so glad I madethis smoothie  it was one of those eye-opening 
         <a href="http://www.theworktop.com/breakfast-brunch-recipes/skillet-potatoes-eggs/" target="_blank" title="Skillet Potatoes and Eggs">
          yumm-mmm
         </a>
          moments! Now, whenever I crave apples and peanut butter, I never know whether I should have it the classic way, or in this new Apple Peanut Butter Smoothie. Its a good problem to have.
        </p>
