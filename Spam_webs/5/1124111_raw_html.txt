        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          <strong>
           Could someone please tell me what happened to October?!
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         It seems that
         <em>
          I blinked
         </em>
         and
         <em>
          I missed
         </em>
         it.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         But I am seriously glad that November will be here tomorrow.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          I cant wait to host
          <em>
           Thanksgiving
          </em>
          dinner!
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         And Im already playing around with the table a bit.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Im thinking something naturaland full of dried hydrangeaand acornsand leaveswith a touch of gold.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Here is one idea for setting a pretty fall table.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/setting-a-fall-table.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="setting a fall table" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10772 pin-it" height="459" src="http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/setting-a-fall-table.jpg" width="644"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://daisymaebelle.com/setting-pretty-fall-table/&amp;media=http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/setting-a-fall-table.jpg&amp;description=Setting a Pretty Fall Table')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span id="more-10767">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/fall-table-setting-ideas.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="fall table setting ideas" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10768 pin-it" height="429" src="http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/fall-table-setting-ideas.jpg" width="644"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://daisymaebelle.com/setting-pretty-fall-table/&amp;media=http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/fall-table-setting-ideas.jpg&amp;description=Setting a Pretty Fall Table')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I started with my centerpiece.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         A giant concrete urn filled with some of my dried hydrangea on top of my
         <a href="http://daisymaebelle.com/no-sew-burlap-table-runner/" target="_blank" title="how to make a burlap table runner">
          burlap table runner.
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/natural-table-decorations.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="natural table decorations" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10771 pin-it" height="966" src="http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/natural-table-decorations.jpg" width="644"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://daisymaebelle.com/setting-pretty-fall-table/&amp;media=http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/natural-table-decorations.jpg&amp;description=Setting a Pretty Fall Table')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Ive had these blooms for a couple of years.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         They started off a pale green but have turned to this beautiful golden color over the years.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/natural-fall-table-decorations.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
           <img alt="natural fall table decorations" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10770 pin-it" height="429" src="http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/natural-fall-table-decorations.jpg" width="644"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://daisymaebelle.com/setting-pretty-fall-table/&amp;media=http://daisymaebelle.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/natural-fall-table-decorations.jpg&amp;description=Setting a Pretty Fall Table')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I scattered some fake acorns around to lend to the natural feel.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
