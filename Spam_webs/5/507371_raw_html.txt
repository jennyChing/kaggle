             <div class="article-main-body row" data-component="lazyloadImages" data-use-autolinker="true" itemprop="articleBody">
              <p>
               Do you like to keep your horizontal toolbar free of clutter, but still want quick access to print preview, sync, downloads, and other options? Try using a simple vertical toolbar.
              </p>
              <p>
               The
               <a data-is-autolink="true" href="/tags/firefox/">
                Firefox
               </a>
               extension is called Vertical Toolbar, and you can get it
               <a data-component="externalLink" href="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/vertical-toolbar" target="_blank">
                here
               </a>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               The toolbar displays on the left edge of the browser by default, but can be moved to the right edge as well. Right-clicking on either the horizontal or vertical toolbars brings up more options, including "Vertical Toolbar Options."
              </p>
              <figure class="image image-large pull-none" section="shortcodeImage">
               <span class="imageContainer">
                <img alt="Vertical toolbar options" class="" itemprop="image" src="http://cnet3.cbsistatic.com/hub/i/r/2011/10/18/d16a6c34-fdc4-11e2-8c7c-d4ae52e62bcc/resize/620x/dbbe7de1e5c55ef37b9b99c840a4cfb4/Vertical_toolbar_options.png"/>
               </span>
               <figcaption>
                <span class="credit">
                 Screenshot by Ed Rhee
                </span>
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
              <p>
               To add or remove Firefox buttons, click on the "Customize" button.
              </p>
              <figure class="image image-large pull-none" section="shortcodeImage">
               <span class="imageContainer">
                <img alt="Customize toolbar" class="lazy " data-original="    http://cnet2.cbsistatic.com/hub/i/r/2011/10/18/d170eee5-fdc4-11e2-8c7c-d4ae52e62bcc/resize/620x/b3916dec6d48ea68e8deddfa3507fe7f/Customize.png" src=""/>
                <noscript>
                 <img alt="Customize toolbar" class="" itemprop="image" src="http://cnet2.cbsistatic.com/hub/i/r/2011/10/18/d170eee5-fdc4-11e2-8c7c-d4ae52e62bcc/resize/620x/b3916dec6d48ea68e8deddfa3507fe7f/Customize.png"/>
                </noscript>
               </span>
               <figcaption>
                <span class="credit">
                 Screenshot by Ed Rhee
                </span>
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
              <p>
               Now you can drag and drop items from the selection box over to the vertical toolbar.
              </p>
              <figure class="image image-large pull-none" section="shortcodeImage">
               <span class="imageContainer">
                <img alt="Drag and drop" class="lazy " data-original="    http://cnet1.cbsistatic.com/hub/i/r/2011/10/18/d1767dd6-fdc4-11e2-8c7c-d4ae52e62bcc/resize/620x/8505ef906b42e376911623a9c541f6ac/Drag_and_drop.png" src=""/>
                <noscript>
                 <img alt="Drag and drop" class="" itemprop="image" src="http://cnet1.cbsistatic.com/hub/i/r/2011/10/18/d1767dd6-fdc4-11e2-8c7c-d4ae52e62bcc/resize/620x/8505ef906b42e376911623a9c541f6ac/Drag_and_drop.png"/>
                </noscript>
               </span>
               <figcaption>
                <span class="credit">
                 Screenshot by Ed Rhee
                </span>
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
              <p>
               That's it. If you want more functionality than what's available in the Vertical Toolbar, try the
               <span class="link" section="shortcodeLink">
                <a href="/news/featured-freeware-all-in-one-sidebar/">
                 All-in-One Sidebar
                </a>
               </span>
               Firefox extension instead.
              </p>
