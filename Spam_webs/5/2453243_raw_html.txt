            <article class="general-post">
             <div class="pf-content">
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://thegospelcoalition.org/blogs/trevinwax/files/2013/06/west-virgina-misty-morning-sunrise.jpg">
                <img alt="" class="wp-image-17197 alignnone" height="245" src="http://thegospelcoalition.org/blogs/trevinwax/files/2013/06/west-virgina-misty-morning-sunrise-1024x682.jpg" style="margin-top: 2px; margin-bottom: 2px; margin-left: 3px; margin-right: 3px;" title="west-virgina-misty-morning-sunrise" width="368"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               Lord God,
               <br/>
               almighty and everlasting Father,
               <br/>
               you have brought me in safety to this new day:
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               Preserve me with your mighty power,
               <br/>
               that I may not fall into sin,
               <br/>
               nor be overcome by adversity;
               <br/>
               and in all I do direct me to the fulfilling of your purpose;
               <br/>
               through Jesus Christ my Lord. Amen.
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               -
               <em>
                Book of Common Prayer
               </em>
              </p>
