        <div id="article">
         <img alt="A New Workout Apparel Set For Fall" class="full-thumbnail wp-post-image" height="400" src="http://www.shopfinal.com/wp-content/uploads/shopfinal/2013/08/a-new-workout-apparel-set-for-fall.jpg" width="400"/>
         <p>
          Workout clothes dont last forever  and if you exercise as much as we do, youre probably due for a new set of athletic clothes! Whether youre into running, yoga, Zumba, Soul Cycle, Cross Fit, hiking, Pilates, strength training or one of those crazy workout trends (is cardio pole dancing still at thing?), weve found some stylish and affordable options in activewear to keep you covered for both indoor and outdoor workouts.
         </p>
         <p>
          Perhaps the most stylish and trendy workout pants are the ones from Lululemon! Forget boring black pants  with the
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B00DG8JHMA/ref=asc_df_B00DG8JHMA2588279?smid=A2U6T1OL7CB1QF&amp;tag=pgmp-1585-01-20&amp;linkCode=asn&amp;creative=395133&amp;creativeASIN=B00DG8JHMA" target="_blank">
           Lululemon Womens Presence Yoga Pants
          </a>
          , you can work out in white, bleached coral or fresh teal. Available in womenss sizes 4-12, these straight-leg style comfortable and functional yoga pants are made of Luon fabric, which is breathablee and features a 4-way stretch that moves with you. The medium rise, crossover waistband also ensures that you can fold forward with ease  no worrying about muffin top or other parts popping out! These yoga workout pants are gusset designed for greater range of movement and comfort, while the pants flat seams offer plenty of chafe-resistance and comfort. Youll also be glad to know that theyre preshrunk so that theyll keep the same size and shape after you wash them.
