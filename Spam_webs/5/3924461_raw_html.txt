     <p>
      Ive recently been looking for a simple to use, easy to install shopping cart for the Dot Design website.
     </p>
     <p>
      This site is built on WordPress and it would be handy if I could find a plugin that could easy integrate into my current site design smoothly.
     </p>
     <p>
      Ive been considering
      <strong>
       <a href="http://www.vendr.com/features/" target="_self">
        Vendr.com
       </a>
      </strong>
      because of it simplicity and that fact that other then adding a red button to the top of your website it doesnt change anything hence no need for structure changes or fiddling about. But its not free (though this isnt a huge problem if its any good) and its independent of the site, meaning it cant be styled.
     </p>
     <p>
      So question is, are you currently using a shopping cart for your blog or WordPress site?
      <br/>
      If so would you recommend it and why?
     </p>
