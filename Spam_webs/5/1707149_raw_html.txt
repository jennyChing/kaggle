         <h1 class="actualpost">
          DIY - Urban Gardening with Recycled Items
         </h1>
         <div class="float_right_post">
         </div>
         <div class="clearing">
         </div>
         <p>
          <img alt="urban-gardening-IG1000" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-789" src="http://www.purelyshutters.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/urban-gardening-IG10001.jpg" width="850"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          Share this Image On Your Site
         </h3>
         <p>
          <textarea onclick="this.focus();this.select()" style="width:850px;height:125px">
          </textarea>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.purelyshutters.co.uk/blog/purely-shutters/diy-urban-gardening-recycled-items/">
           <img alt="DIY Urban Gardening with Recycled Items" border="0" src="http://www.purelyshutters.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/urban-gardening-IG10001.jpg" width="600px"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Grow your own vegetables, save money and save Mother Earth at the same time! Recycle old junk from around your house and create stylish, compact garden features that are perfect for any space.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Shutters
          </b>
         </p>
         <p>
          Hang small buckets, pots or even old shoe holders onto a shutter to create an attractive display of herbs or flowers. If youre using a wooden shutter, try adding a light stain to help tie the finished piece together with your existing dcor.
         </p>
         <p>
          Love the look of this hanging shutter garden? These are handmade by Elizabeth Kate and can be found at her Etsy shop: https://www.etsy.com/shop/ElizabethKateDecor
         </p>
         <p>
          Stapling landscaping fabric to the back of a plantation-style shutter creates a customizable, visually appealing planter that accommodates herbs, lettuces, strawberries, creeping perennials, succulents, and more.
         </p>
         <p>
          Protip  if you set up a plantation shutter planter indoors, keep an absorbent mat underneath to catch any dripping water.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Ladders
          </b>
         </p>
         <p>
          Ladders can be transformed to create gorgeous tiered gardens. Repurpose old flower pots, watering cans, or even tin candle buckets for a quaint look!
         </p>
         <p>
          Protip  when reusing old containers, make sure you put in drainage holes.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           More Inspiration
          </b>
         </p>
         <p>
          Spruce up a boring wall with a combination of these upcycled planters.
         </p>
         <p>
          Protip  repaint containers for a bright new look that coordinates with your current dcor.
         </p>
         <p>
          Youre limited only by your imagination. Practically anything can be upcycled into a garden! Birdcages, chairs, wine crates, boats and even license plates  plenty of unlikely items can be new life in an upcycled garden.
         </p>
         <p>
          Want a license plate planter?
          <a href="http://erikrdavis.com/" rel="nofollow">
           http://erikrdavis.com
          </a>
          can custom-make you one of these charming upcycled gardening planters.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Raised Bed Planters
          </b>
         </p>
         <p>
          Like this beautiful planter? It can be purchased
          <a href="http://rollingplanter.com/" rel="nofollow">
           http://rollingplanter.com
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Raised bed planters make it easy to turn a little lumber into a viable garden  convenience and control all in one.
         </p>
         <p>
          Protip  Wheels make for easy relocation in case you move to a new home or experience inclement weather.
         </p>
