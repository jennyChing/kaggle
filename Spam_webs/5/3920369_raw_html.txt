Safari Sundays handled every aspect of the brand re-launch; including brand identity, key communications, collateral, packaging, point of sale and digital assets.

             <div class="sqs-block-content">
              <p>
               "Launched in 2009 as the only brand in the category created by moms for moms, dapple co-founders Tamar Rosenthal and Dana Rubinstein emphasized the importance of a 100% natural and baby-safe cleaner. Within the crowded cleaners category, dapples vision was to deliver a line of products that tackle baby-specific messes and eliminate toxins, synthetic ingredients, and residue &amp; odors. Safari Sundays was challenged to modernize the now outdated identity and create a design solution to segment the growing product line and support new product development. The result is a revitalized pack architecture that simply differentiates product categories through bold graphic icons and a refined system for core messaging communications that is seamlessly integrated into the design.
              </p>
              <p>
               The team is planning a global market launch for the reinvented brand in mid 2014."
              </p>
