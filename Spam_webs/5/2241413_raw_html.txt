        <p>
         When the Emmy come just give them all to
         <a href="http://www.hbo.com/vice#/">
          Vice on HBO
         </a>
         and call it a day Well them and of course American Crime This weeks episodes on Vice are  A Prayer for Uganda with correspondent Isobel Yeung and Kidneyville with correspondent Vikram Gandhi will have you speechless. After I watched A Prayer for Uganda I had to Tweet Isobel Yeung and tell her how powerful that episode was. Homophobia is on the rise in Uganda. The president of Uganda signed into law an anti-homosexuality bill which went as far as mandating the death penalty for being gay. It really wasnt until the missionaries started to invade Uganda preaching hatred &amp; fear that gay bashings and killings became the norm.
         <strong>
          Republican
         </strong>
         Senator Jim Inhofe from Oklahoma, traveled to Africa over 100+ time on taxpayers money. Inhofe is an elected public official who goes to Uganda spreading anti gay rhetoric on our dime. This is another great example as to why we need to VOTE these types of people out of public office. Im on my soap box so please just click and watch VICE.I need to calm down!
