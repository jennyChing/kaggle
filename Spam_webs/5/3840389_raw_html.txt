        Quest Nutrition Chocolate Peanut Butter Protein Shake #15SecondRecipe
       </h1>

      <p class="p1">
       Is there a more perfect pairing then chocolate and peanut butter? Try this deliciously creamy and nutritious Quest Nutrition Chocolate Peanut Butter Protein Shake #15SecondRecipe. Easy to make with a whopping 24.5g of protein!
      </p>
      <p class="p1">
       Yields: 2 servings. Serving size: 12 shake. Prep time: 5 min.
      </p>
      <p class="p1">
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        <strong>
         Ingredients:
        </strong>
       </span>
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li class="p1">
        1 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
       </li>
       <li class="p1">
        12 cup water
       </li>
       <li class="p1">
        2 cups ice
       </li>
       <li class="p1">
        1 scoop
        <a href="http://www.questnutrition.com/protein-powders/?utm_source=blog%20post&amp;utm_medium=link&amp;utm_campaign=quest%20protein%20powder%20blog%20post%20chocolate%20peanut%20butter%20shake" target="_blank">
         Quest Peanut Butter Protein Powder
        </a>
       </li>
       <li class="p1">
        12 scoop
        <a href="http://www.questnutrition.com/protein-powders/?utm_source=blog%20post&amp;utm_medium=link&amp;utm_campaign=quest%20protein%20powder%20blog%20post%20chocolate%20peanut%20butter%20shake" target="_blank">
         Quest Chocolate Milkshake Protein Powder
        </a>
       </li>
       <li class="p1">
        2 tablespoons peanuts
       </li>
       <li class="p1">
        1 12 tablespoons zero-calorie sweetener
       </li>
       <li class="p1">
        1
        <a href="http://www.questnutrition.com/quest-cravings/?utm_source=blog%20post&amp;utm_medium=link&amp;utm_campaign=quest%20cravings%20blog%20post%20chocolate%20peanut%20butter%20shake" target="_blank">
         Quest Cravings Peanut Butter Cup
        </a>
        , chopped and divided
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p class="p1">
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        <strong>
         Method:
        </strong>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p class="p1">
        Pour the milk, water and ice into a blender. Add the
       <a href="http://www.questnutrition.com/protein-powders/?utm_source=blog%20post&amp;utm_medium=link&amp;utm_campaign=quest%20protein%20powder%20blog%20post%20chocolate%20peanut%20butter%20shake" target="_blank">
        protein powders
       </a>
       , peanuts, zero-calorie
      </p>
      <p class="p1">
       sweetener and half the chopped
       <a href="http://www.questnutrition.com/quest-cravings/?utm_source=blog%20post&amp;utm_medium=link&amp;utm_campaign=quest%20cravings%20blog%20post%20chocolate%20peanut%20butter%20shake" target="_blank">
        peanut butter cups
       </a>
       . Blend until smooth and thick. Pour into
      </p>
      <p class="p1">
       two glasses. Sprinkle the tops with remaining peanut butter cups pieces.
      </p>
      <p class="p1">
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        <strong>
         Nutrition Info per serving (makes two):
        </strong>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p class="p1">
       Calories: 205. Protein: 24.5g. Net Carbs: 3.5g. Fat: 10g.
      </p>
