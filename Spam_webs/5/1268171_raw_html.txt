        <p>
         In the wake of the earthquake in Japan I decided to pull a card from my Earth Magic deck. I figured Mother Earth was definitely trying to tell us something. The card I picked was
         <em>
          <strong>
           Tsunami
          </strong>
         </em>
         wake-up call.
        </p>
        <p>
         In many ways our world is experiencing a wake-up call to change our ways. Many people are still in denial about climate change on this planet, or if they accept the premise that the worlds climate is changing, they believe it is a natural cycle an that man plays no part in what is going on. Even though some of the change is cyclical, that doesnt mean that we (people) are not responsible for our share of the problem. There are too many of us on the planet, making too much waste and using up too many resources in inexcusable ways. These natural disasters that we experience are not a punishment for poor caretaking of the Earth, but rather a symptom of it. Its as if Mother Earth is telling us that we have gotten on her last nerve and its time to back off.
        </p>
        <p>
         On a personal level, consider what you have been in denial about? What signs in your life have you been ignoring?  In what ways do you need to change in order to ward off a self-distruct? In what areas and in what ways are your energies better spent than what you are doing now?
        </p>
