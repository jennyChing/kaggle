       <div class="article-header-title">
        Superfood Chocolate Sesame Snack Squares
       </div>
       <div class="article-content">
        <p>
         We are loving this ingenious flavor combination from Jessie Snyder at
         <a href="http://faring-well.com/" target="_blank">
          Faring Well
         </a>
         . Tahini, honey, and chocolate combine for the perfect treat when you need something new, but don't have time to make a special grocery trip for exotic ingredients. These sesame snack bars are great as a midafternoon snack or as an easy dessert.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Ingredients
          <img alt="" src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/aloha-production/pictures/ck/360/content_aloha-chocolate-sesame-squares-2-embed.jpg" style="float:right; height:267px; margin:5px; width:400px"/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         1 1/4 cups gluten-free rolled oats
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2 cup white sesame seeds, hulled
        </p>
        <p>
         Good pinch of salt
        </p>
        <p>
         1/4 cup tahini
        </p>
        <p>
         5 tablespoons raw, unpasteurized honey
        </p>
        <p>
         1
         <a href="https://aloha.com/shop/superfood-chocolate" target="_blank">
          ALOHA Superfood Chocolate bar
         </a>
         , chopped
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Preparation
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Toss the oats, sesame seeds, and salt in a medium mixing bowl. Add the tahini and raw honey, mix well. Add the chopped ALOHA Superfood Chocolate bar last and mix until evenly incorporated. Press evenly into a parchment-paper-lined 9x5 loaf pan. Place in freezer to set for 15 minutes. Remove from pan by pulling up on the parchment paper. Slice into 8 squares and store in an airtight container in the refrigerator until ready to serve. Best if eaten within one week.
        </p>
