        <h1 class="entry-title">
         New from Southern Living, The Slim Down South Cookbook
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2014-02-14T05:52:10+00:00">
          February 14, 2014
         </span>

         <span class="author vcard">

        <div class="entry-content">
         <div>
          <div>
           <div>
            <a href="http://www.havesippywilltravel.com/2014/02/new-southern-living-slim-south-cookbook.html/pic-1-287" rel="attachment wp-att-16262">
             <img alt="pic 1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16262" height="661" src="//www.havesippywilltravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/pic-17.jpg" width="519"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <div>
            As a Northern girl who loves Southern food a little too much, this was a welcome addition to my kitchen. Nothing much beats a good pecan pie or batch or biscuits, and you bet your sweet tea that I love my time below the Mason-Dixon line even if it tends to work my waistband a little bit
            <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="//www.havesippywilltravel.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
            Apparently, I can make some of my favorites at home, and with less calories as well. Who knew? Read on for more info- then go
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Southern-Living-Slim-South-Cookbook/dp/0848742826">
             get this cookbook
            </a>
            and start cooking
            <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="//www.havesippywilltravel.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div>
            Can Southern food be satisfying and healthy? You bet your biscuits!
            <b>
             THE SLIM DOWN SOUTH COOKBOOK:
             <i>
              Eating Well &amp; Living Healthy in the Land of Biscuits and Bacon
             </i>
             ,
            </b>
            a newbook written by registered dietitian and award-winning journalist Carolyn ONeil, serves up delicious recipes for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks while teaching you an easy-to-follow philosophy for living a healthy lifestyle  without giving up the Southern food you love.
           </div>
           <p>
            <span id="more-16261">
            </span>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div>
          <div>
           <div>
            <div>
             <div>
              <p>
               <span style="font-family: Calibri; font-size: large;">
                Southern culture certainly tempts with the deviled eggs, layer cakes and all things deep-fried, but Southern food can also be good for you. Peaches, pecans, okra, greens and sweet potatoes fill Southern pantries with good health and great taste. And
                <b>
                 THE SLIM DOWN SOUTH COOKBOOK
                </b>
                also includes traditional dishes with nutritious makeovers. Yes, you can enjoy
                <i>
                 Pecan Pancakes, Baked Cheese Grits, Fried Green
                </i>
                <i>
                 Tomato Sliders, Skillet Fried Chicken,
                </i>
                and
                <i>
                 Key Lime Pie
                </i>
                as part of a slim and healthy lifestyle.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-family: Calibri; font-size: large;">
                Slimming down with
                <b>
                 THE SLIM DOWN SOUTH COOKBOOK
                </b>
                is also as much about the grit and grace as it is about the groceries. Living and eating like a true Southerner can put you on the path to good health and it comes down to four easy SLIM strategies that work whether youre in South Carolina or South Dakota:
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-family: Calibri; font-size: large;">
                <i>
                 <b>
                   S
                 </b>
                 avor the South 
                </i>
                Discover the healthy variety of Southern foods.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-family: Calibri; font-size: large;">
                <i>
                 <b>
                   L
                 </b>
                 inger Longer 
                </i>
                Take your sweet time to enjoy meals.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-family: Calibri; font-size: large;">
                <i>
                 <b>
                   I
                 </b>
                 ndulge a Little 
                </i>
                Incorporate little splurges that keep you satisfied.
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span style="font-family: Calibri; font-size: large;">
                <i>
                 <b>
                   M
                 </b>
                 ake it Happen!
                </i>
                 Use Southern grit and grace to stay the course.
                <br/>
               </span>
              </p>
             </div>
