         Kramer On Veritas: Roots Of Hidden Reality
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-info">
        
        <abbr class="published" title="2014-07-17T20:29:52+00:00">
         07/17/2014
        </abbr>
        <span class="entry-cat">
         <strong>
          Posted in:
         </strong>
         <a href="http://neilkramer.com/category/interviews">
          Interviews
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content clearfix">
        <p>
         <strong>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4523" height="225" src="http://neilkramer.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/nkv3.jpg" title="Kramer On Veritas" width="576"/>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Neil Kramer talks to Mel Fabregas, host of Veritas Radio. We talk about how practical mysticism and philosophy help us to engage with the hidden layers of reality.
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Topics of discussion include: the great ontological questions, who-what-where-why?, outlining the root teachings, fulfillment from metaphysical inquiry, making friends with death, three kinds of happiness, the fulfillment-distraction equation, the drug of phenomenal information, universal safety valve of ego positioning, governmental authority starting to fail, philosophically there are no rights, where are the wise elders, spiritual development determines wholeness and fragmentation.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #800000;">
           <a href="http://www.veritasradio.com/guests/2014/07jul/VS-140717-nkramer.php">
            <span style="color: #800000;">
             CLICK HERE
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </strong>
         for the interview. You can stream it online or download an MP3. Theres also a second part for Veritas members, where we dig deeper into some of the above issues and more.
        </p>
