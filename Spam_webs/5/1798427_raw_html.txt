          <h3 style="text-align: center;">
           This Weeks Tasty Travel Tip
          </h3>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Tasty Travel Tip #3: Get a Visitors Guide.
           </strong>
           The Catalina Island Chamber of Commerce released their
           <a href="http://online.publicationprinters.com/launch.aspx?eid=c2c25923-95c0-4519-b207-b2b21ff3cd07&amp;_ga=1.13336372.267137172.1421394464">
            2015 Catalina Island Visitors Guide
           </a>
           this week. It is the best item to have when it comes to events, lodging, transportation and info regarding Catalina Island. Would you rather have the hard copy to make notes on? Just click here to
           <a href="http://web.catalinachamber.com/cwt/external/wcpagesnew/wcwebinput/webinputpage.aspx">
            fill out the form
           </a>
           and they will mail you a Visitors Guide for free! The photos in this years guide are gorgeous! Many of the winners from their 2014 Summer Fun Photo Contest are featured. Just think  you could be featured in next years guide!
          </p>
         </div>
         <div>
          <hr/>
          <a href="http://www.catalinafoodtours.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Avalon-Catalina-Island.png">

          </a>
          <p>
           Looking for something fun to do on Catalina Island? Join us on tour! We offer the
           <a href="http://www.catalinafoodtours.com/tours/avalon-tasting-and-cultural-walking-tour/">
            Avalon Tasting &amp; Cultural Walking Tour
           </a>
           every day of the week, year-round. Our
           <a href="http://www.catalinafoodtours.com/tours/catalina-happy-hour-tour/">
            Catalina Happy Hour Tour
           </a>
           runs on certain days, March  October. We cant wait to eat and drink our way through Avalon with you!
