      <h2>
       2G, 3G and 4G  whats the difference?
      </h2>
      <div class="lowerboxLeft">
       <h3>
        What is 2G?
       </h3>
       <p>
        The 2G network allows you make calls, send a texts and picture messages, access emails and browse the internet.
       </p>
       <p>
        2G is shown on the map as
        <strong>
         Voice, text
        </strong>
        and
        <strong>
         email
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="lowerboxRight">
       <h3>
        Now tell me about 3G
       </h3>
       <p>
        3G lets you browse the internet, stream music and send videos to friends or family.
       </p>
       <p>
        3G is shown on the map as
        <strong>
         Mobile internet and voice
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="lowerboxRight">
       <h3>
        And now weve got O2 4G too?
       </h3>
       <p>
        O2 4G lets you download music, stream videos and play games faster than ever before. But remember, youll still be using 3G for your calls  4G is for mobile internet only.
       </p>
       <p>
        4G is shown on the map as
        <strong>
         Mobile internet
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
