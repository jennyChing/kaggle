        <p>
         I have alright eyes, although for many years I did wear glasses. Several years ago I began feeling as though I wanted my eyes to be better, and I didnt have thousands of dollars to spend on laser surgery. So I began practicing healing my eyes with will alone, and some various ingredients that I had picked up along the way.
        </p>
        <h3>
         1) De-Stress
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27264" height="407" src="http://thespiritscience.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/stressss.jpg" width="610"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The basis is to recognize that all of the strain and stress in your eyes is not solely related to your eyes, but the stress that you are carrying around in your body, particularly in your face, which relates heavily to your brain as well. Beginning by realizing the stress in your face  and relaxing the muscles around your eyes is the first step of this, and understand that healing is a process which can take some time, especially if your external environment is a particularly stressful one. I always notice that my eyesight worsens when I am more busy and stressed out in my head.
        </p>
        <h3>
         2) Sun Gaze
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27265" height="450" src="http://thespiritscience.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/sun-gazing.jpg" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Close your eyes gently and look into the sun absorbing the light (also while relaxing the muscles). This can facilitate a calm, warming soothing feeling across your whole face and even the rest of your body, and can greatly help relax. This is good to do for a few seconds while the sun is rising and when it is setting because the suns rays are less powerful during those times, and will be less harmful to your eyes. This will also help remove a layer of mucous from the top of your eyeball.
        </p>
        <h3>
         3) Eat The Right Foods
        </h3>
        <p>
         There is also food that is great for your eyes, such as Carrots, Eggs, and Almonds! (Just make sure its organic). Any fruits and vegetables will be good for your eyesight, especially ones containing vitamin E and zinc.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27262" height="418" src="http://thespiritscience.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/foods-to-improve-eyesight.jpg" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         So a few days ago I stumbled upon this video below and I got super excited about it! The results that ive had have been noticeable to me, but I had never been able to put it into words or describe it as im able to now.
        </p>
        <p>
         Upon watching the video, I found myself pleasantly surprised. This guy is a legitimate doctor, who has used a method from a
         <em>
          renegade optometrist
         </em>
         from about a hundred years ago who put together a solid formula for healing your eyes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-19287">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="480" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/itfuva1xgEg" style="line-height: 1.5;" width="640">

        <center>
         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="5111570" data-link="http://thespiritscience.net/2014/02/18/heal-your-eyesight-naturally/" data-summary="" data-title="How To Heal Your Eyesight Naturally">
         </div>
        </center>
        <p>
         Everything he says really resonates with me, and I think even though theres a lot more to it, it sounds a lot like what ive been practicing. For example, as I wrote earlier when I wake up in the morning I like to go outside and just close my eyes gently and stare into the sun. It not only helps my eyes wake up, but my whole mind wakes up! Not to mention, my eyes lose their morning grogginess completely, and my eyesight seems to be really great, much better than had if I did not do it.
        </p>
        <p>
         Likewise, the whole
         <em>
          dont squint
         </em>
         thing is another practice, and it totally makes sense! Like, if youre whole face is squinting and stressed out all of the time, then thats constricting the way your face and head receives energy  both with light through our eyes, energy in our 3rd eyes, or smells through our noses.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
          And if theres no more strain, then your brain, eyes, and facial muscles can all function with ease and clarity. Energy can flow smoothly, and it only makes sense that your eyes will naturally improve as well
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://thespiritscience.net/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         For all of us with glasses, lets give these tips a practice and see how we do!
        </p>
