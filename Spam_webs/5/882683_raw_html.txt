         <h3>
          <strong>
           Xbox One is the Place for Games
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.homeonderanged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/XBOX.jpg">
           <img alt="Fun gifts for gamers, music fans &amp; travel lovers at Best Buy this #HintingSeason on Home on Deranged @homeonderange @bestbuy #tech #gadgets #ad #gamers #musicfans #holidaygiftguide #Christmasgiftideas " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3084" height="386" src="http://www.homeonderanged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/XBOX.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           What is Xbox One? Good question!
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Xbox One was built by gamers, for gamers, and as my gamer husband would say, thats how you know its awesome. Theyve got the best exclusive titles (you know Halo and Sunset Overdrive, right?), pluse the biggest blockbusters (youve seen the posters for Assassins Creed and Call of Duty!). Plus, those super hot sports franchises of Madden 15, FIFA 15 and NBA 2K15. Big hits with the guys!
         </p>
         <p>
          If thats not enough, theres the fun selectionsyou can add to your family game night, like Fantasia: Music Evolved, Just Dance 15 and Skylanders. Xbox One is taking gaming to a whole new level.
         </p>
         <p>
          We know its a great gaming console, but did you know it keeps you connected unlike any other device? Xbox One enables access to the latest TV shows and movies through your favorite apps. Youve heard of streaming TV, right? With Xbox One, all of your media is at your fingertips when you want it.
         </p>
         <p>
          Ready to gitcha one?
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.bestbuy.com/site/video-games/xbox-one/pcmcat300300050002.c?id=pcmcat300300050002&amp;DCMP=rdr107719" target="_blank">
            Check out all the details on Xbox One at Best Buy
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <h3>
          <strong>
           JBL for Music Lovers
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.homeonderanged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/JBL_Holiday.png">
           <img alt="Fun gifts for gamers, music fans &amp; travel lovers at Best Buy this #HintingSeason on Home on Deranged @homeonderange @bestbuy #tech #gadgets #ad #gamers #musicfans #holidaygiftguide #Christmasgiftideas " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3085" height="260" src="http://www.homeonderanged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/JBL_Holiday.png" width="460"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Who doesnt love music? We may not all have the same taste in tunes, but we love to crank up our jam. Thats where JBL comes in, and it comes in mighty fine when youre ready to listen. Check out these options:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <strong>
            JBL Charge 2
           </strong>
           Between the twin passive radiators for deep bass, the massive 6000mAh battery and the hands-free conference speakerphone the key benefit is the Bluetooth Social Mode which allows up to three different users to take their turns to play their own playlists on a single speaker.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            JBL Flip 2
           </strong>
           Aside from the dual performance-tuned JBL drivers and a built-in bass port, the key benefit of the JBL Flip 2 is the SoundClear echo and noise cancellation combined with the state-of the art microphone system to pick up calls on the speaker.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            JBL Reflect Earbud Headphones
           </strong>
           Sweat proof ergonomic design. With a 5 hour rechargeable battery and a reach of about 40 feet, the JBL Reflect BTs key benefit is the design of the ergonomic eartips along with the built-in magnets which keep your buds on standby without loose cables.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          Want to see them for yourself?
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.bestbuy.com/site/promo/jbl-holiday-130158" target="_blank">
            Check out all the specs at Best Buy
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <h3>
          <strong>
           Garmin and TomTom
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.homeonderanged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/GO-50-S-lifestyle.jpg">
           <img alt="Fun gifts for gamers, music fans &amp; travel lovers at Best Buy this #HintingSeason on Home on Deranged @homeonderange @bestbuy #tech #gadgets #ad #gamers #musicfans #holidaygiftguide #Christmasgiftideas " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3086" height="350" src="http://www.homeonderanged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/GO-50-S-lifestyle.jpg" width="470"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Alright, navigating the roads gets more and more complicated. Between new highways and closed highways and traffic jams and weather hazards, driving can be stressful.
         </p>
         <p>
          How about adding a GPS device to your car? Theyre all souped-up these days, with Bluetooth connectivity for hands-free calling, traffic and weather updates, plus lane assist and lifetime traffic and map updates. Oh, theyve got voice recognition technology. How cool is that??? Ive highlighted a few that lead the pack.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Garmin 52 LM and/or the Garmin 2597 LMT
          </strong>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           5.0 display
          </li>
          <li>
           Maps of 49 U.S. States, plus lifetime map updates
          </li>
          <li>
           Lane assist with junction view
          </li>
          <li>
           Displays speed limit and accurate arrival time
          </li>
          <li>
           Easily finds addresses and millions of points of interest
          </li>
          <li>
           Spoken turn-by-turn directions
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.homeonderanged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/GO50S_300px.jpg">
            <img alt="Fun gifts for gamers, music fans &amp; travel lovers at Best Buy this #HintingSeason on Home on Deranged @homeonderange @bestbuy #tech #gadgets #ad #gamers #musicfans #holidaygiftguide #Christmasgiftideas " class="alignright size-full wp-image-3088" height="294" src="http://www.homeonderanged.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/GO50S_300px.jpg" width="300"/>
           </a>
           TomTom Go 50S
          </strong>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Lifetime TomTom Traffic: Drive with TomTom Traffic for the lifetime of your device
          </li>
          <li>
           Lifetime Maps (US, Canada &amp; Mexico): Always drive with the latest map
          </li>
          <li>
           3D Maps: See buildings and landmarks in stunning 3D
          </li>
          <li>
           5 touchscreen with advanced lane guidance
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          Need a few more details to decide?
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.bestbuy.com/site/promo/gps-holiday" target="_blank">

           </a>
