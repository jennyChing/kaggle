        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Cutting Out Meat  One Day at a Time
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Have you thought about how much meat you eat? Back in 2003 the phenomenon known as
         <strong>
          Meatless Monday
         </strong>
         started to help people cut back on their meat intake by one day a week.By cutting back on meat one day a week, you are cutting down your
         <strong>
          saturated fat intake by 15%
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Now there are more than
         <strong>
          30 countries
         </strong>
         who participate in Meatless Monday. Read more about the
         <strong>
          impact meat has on your health
         </strong>
         , the history of Meatless Monday and the future of
         <strong>
          meat consumption
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
