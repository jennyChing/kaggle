     <p class="desc">
      Luka's dad says: Luka was seven weeks when he picked us to come
and live with. He was a little underweight and very quiet at this
point. A month later he is filling out nicely and much more lively!
As a border collie, he is born to herd. We don't walk him; he walks
us, nudging us along from behind. Everyone who has met him has
adored him, just as we do. His favourite toys are small plastic
flower pots. They have a life of their own...
