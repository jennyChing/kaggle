    <h1>
     New York Citys floating concert hall
    </h1>
    <p>
     Moored in  Brooklyn just under the Brooklyn Bridge,
     <span class="big">
      Bargemusic
     </span>
     presents
  great music up to five days a week, every week of the year. Walk across the gangplank of a renovated coffee barge into a
     <span class="big">
      wonderfully
    intimate wood-paneled room with thrilling views of lower Manhattan and excellent
     acoustics.
     </span>
     Experience why critics call Bargemusic
     <span class="big">
      the
  perfect chamber-music hall
     </span>
     and why artists say it is
     <span class="big">
      unlike
  any other place in the world to perform.
     </span>
     <br/>
    </p>
