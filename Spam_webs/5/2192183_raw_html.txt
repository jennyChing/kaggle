       The Toy Fair
      </h1>

       <div class="post__body">
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19866" height="384" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/1businessmen-640x384.jpg" title="1businessmen" width="640"/>
          The Toy Fair isnt for kids. The shows held yearly at the Javits Center, Manhattans main convention facility (a.k.a. massive gray box), and its full of serious adults inbusiness suits with corporate accounts. Its not supposed to be fun. Well see about that!
        </p>
        <p>
          Toy Fair badges are only available for pros, so my boyfriends mom generously registered me and my friend Tim as employees of her chia seed company. My badgesays CHIA POWER/Assistant Buyer. Well avoid walking by chia products for fear of having to hold our own in a chia conversation.
         <span id="more-19865">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-19867" height="1024" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/2hippityhop-470x1024.jpg" title="2hippityhop" width="470"/>
        </p>
        <p>
          I want to pretend were here for legitimate reasons, so Tim and I work out a cover story, we distribute chia products, but were looking to branch out into toys andathletics. That totally sounds like a thing, right?
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19868" height="570" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/3tim.jpg" title="3tim" width="598"/>
        </p>
        <p>
          Tim is my quintessential New York Friend: erudite, droll, always up for hijinks.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19869" height="651" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/4timglitter.jpg" title="4timglitter" width="462"/>
        </p>
        <p>
          As soon as we get past security, Tim points to the bedazzling station and says, Im going to let one of those pretty girls give me a glitter tattoo. Sure, no harm inouting ourselves as ultra-eager Toy Fair participants!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19870" height="214" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/5glittertats.jpg" title="5glittertats" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
          Freshly glittered, we take a look around the main exhibition hall. These vendors have wildly differing ideas on what makes for appropriate convention wear, Tim says.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19871" height="457" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/6conventionoufits-640x457.jpg" title="6conventionoufits" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
          A lot of businessmen are whizzing around the aisles on little scooters and things. The ones selling miniature vehicles seem to be having more fun than everyone else.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19883" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/7scooterguy-339x640.jpg" title="7scooterguy" width="339"/>
        </p>
        <p>
          The most uptight vendors are the German artisanal stuffed animal salesmen.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19884" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/8stuffedanimals-621x640.jpg" title="8stuffedanimals" width="621"/>
        </p>
        <p>
          I do a double-take at the Teddy Tanks booth, where live sh are swimming inside teddy bears. A young, bored guy gives us a spiel, We put the animate in theinanimate. Thats our concept. We bring it to life.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19885" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/9teddytanks-519x640.jpg" title="9teddytanks" width="519"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         He demonstrates how to pour sh food into the stuffed bears mouth, then repeats, We put the animate in the inanimate. WHAT. Never mind that its a terrible slogan,doesnt anyone realize that to a child, a teddy bear
         <em>
          is
         </em>
         animate? And wont it be horrifying when encasing a live sh tank in Teddys plush esh causes him to rot from theinside?
        </p>
        <p>
          A lot of these toys could benet from being workshopped with actual children.
        </p>
        <p>
          We circumvent educational toys until I spot a faux archaeological Digasaurs sandbox with fake dinosaur bones and brushes. How cool! Bone brushing has to be thebest part of being a paleontologist, I bet paleontologists are constantly ghting over who gets to brush bones.
        </p>
        <p>
          I hope the owner of Royal Bobbles got his booth space at a discounted rate.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19887" height="521" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/10bobbleheads-640x521.jpg" style="border-style: initial; border-color: initial;" title="10bobbleheads" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <div>
          Ooh look, its a giant inatable shark! Tim and I both covet Sharky, but who could t him in their apartment and afford his constant diet of fresh Helium?
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19888" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/11inflatableshark-576x640.jpg" title="11inflatableshark" width="576"/>
         </p>
         <p>
           Heres an uncomfortable observation: I notice my attraction to certain toys feels kind of  sexual. A lot of these toys have voluptuous and irtatious designs, forexample the My Little Ponies have big soft rumps and perfumed hair. It makes me uneasy.
         </p>
         <p>
           Im sneering at the nerdy collectible gurines section, when I see a limited edition Red Stay Puft and feel thunderstruck. He looks angry, yet supple and inviting. Idont want to say he looks like a dildo, but  well.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19889" height="504" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/12staypuft.jpg" title="12staypuft" width="286"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          I also like this 4D Shark Puzzle; I think hed make a ne friend for Red Stay Puft.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19890" height="258" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/13sharkanatomy.jpg" title="13sharkanatomy" width="449"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          I want this fashionable black and neon globe, too, but its not quite a toy. Maybe I could get creative and incorporate it into some playtime with the other guys?
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19891" height="360" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/14globe.jpg" title="14globe" width="356"/>
         </p>
         <p>
           Heres a toy I cant imagine anyone wanting: a creepy blonde doll crying semen-like tears and clutching a smaller doll of her own.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19892" height="606" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/15creepydoll.jpg" title="15creepydoll" width="451"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          I approach Dolly for a closer inspection and nod at the owner, a midwestern Mom type. Shes lovely, I say as I snap a picture.
          <em>
           Those tears really look like jizz.
          </em>
          Isntshe stunning? Just beautiful.
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           
          </em>
          Theres a lot of candy at this convention. Im doing a lot of pretending to be interested in toys so that I can eat pieces of candy.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19893" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/16icecreamorsoap-607x640.jpg" title="16icecreamorsoap" width="607"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           
          </em>
          Its important to remember we can make a swift exit if things get awkward. The vendors are somewhat glued to their booths and wont pursue us very far into the aisle.
         </p>
         <p>
           I leave Tims side to go investigate a salesman wearing suggestive red shorts and playing hacky sack with a badminton birdie. During his demo, he leans in to conde,Im REALLY hungry. I offer him a banana, he says, Im really not allowed to eat your banana, and its a million times more awkward than it should be. Why cant hejust take the fruit and switch it out with the birdie for a minute?
          <em>
           Is he a prisoner?!
          </em>
          I walk away before Birdie Bananas can involve me in an escape plan.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19894" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/17bananaguy1-386x640.jpg" title="17bananaguy1" width="386"/>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19895" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/18bananaguy2-411x640.jpg" title="18bananaguy2" width="411"/>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19896" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/19bananaguy3-300x640.jpg" title="19bananaguy3" width="300"/>
         </p>
         <p>
           I nd Tim in the Star Trek cologne booth, and bleccch! It smells cheap and repugnant. Shirtless Kirk is especially disappointing, not even worth it as a gag gift.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19897" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/20startrekcologne-369x640.jpg" title="20startrekcologne" width="369"/>
         </p>
         <p>
           But heres something even less desirable: Abundant Harvest, a board game for teaching children and adults how to make wise life choices. Sample scenarios includeYour spouse is not putting effort into maintaining a strong marriage and Your friend is passing around a marijuana cigarette.
          <strong>
           <em>
            FUN!
           </em>
          </strong>
          The booth is deserted, like eventhe salespeople couldnt take it anymore.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19898" height="282" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/21abundantharvestgame-640x282.jpg" title="21abundantharvestgame" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
           I squint at an Americoob banner just to make sure  no, it doesnt say AmeriBOOB. The booth guy explains the toy is based on the viking game,
          <em>
           Kubbspel
          </em>
          , and heinvites me to wear a plastic viking helmet while trying out a game of Americoob in the aisle.
         </p>
         <p>
          I knock a block (coob?) over with a differently shaped block (or is this one the coob?), I yell COOB! and it feels good. It has a bare bones throw-a-thing-at-a-thingcharm. Still, I wonder if the whole ancient Norse warriors vibe couldnt be incorporated more into gameplay.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its hard to associate these game pieces
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-19899" height="326" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/22Americoob.jpg" title="22Americoob" width="288"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          with my mental picture of a viking:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19900" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/23viking-438x640.jpg" title="23viking" width="438"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Then again?
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19901" height="557" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/24vikingscoobing-640x557.jpg" title="24vikingscoobing" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
           We head upstairs, where all the heavy hitters and big brands went nuts designing elaborate booths. The already confusing trade show social dynamic changes uphere; instead of being courted by desperate small business owners, were met with icy suits at podiums requesting vendor account numbers and appointments. I realizeIm the villain and these people are just trying to sell wholesale goods, but I still feel entitled  whats the harm in letting me check out the newest pirate ship play set orwhatever? Sure, I just sell chia products, but Im looking to branch out.
         </p>
         <p>
           Reactions to my chia afliation have been mostly neutral or oh, cool, like the Chia Pets, but the women guarding the Playmobil compound are total dicks about it.
         </p>
         <p>
          One of them says Chia?!, and turns to her coworker with a look of contempt. Then both of them laugh in our faces. Im still fuming ten minutes later, who doesPlaymobil think they are? Theyre no Lego, thats for sure.
         </p>
         <p>
           Lego wont even let us into their booth. You need an appointment, and the receptionist wont let us peek even though theres nobody else around. At Comic-con, theypile Legos on the oor and let you swim in them! Just sayin.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19902" height="477" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/25Legobooth-640x477.jpg" title="25Legobooth" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
           Thank you, Uncle Milton Industries, for letting us enjoy your Tarantula Planet critters. Im a sucker for ugly animals dressed up in costumes, and hey, weird, thesoccer-themed tarantula is wearing the same outt as that Birdie Bananas guy from earlier!
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19903" height="354" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/26spidertoys-640x354.jpg" title="26spidertoys" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Too late, Birdie, I already ate my banana!
         </p>
         <p>
           I spy plastic horses from across the room and get all screechy.
          <strong>
           <em>
            Breyers
           </em>
          </strong>
          . I corner a saleslady, Oh my god, Im such a Breyer fan! I sold most of my collection when Imoved to New York, but still keep a bunch in the closet at my parents house!! I nerd out over their displays for awhile.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19904" height="452" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/27mebreyers-640x452.jpg" title="27mebreyers" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
           Next we check out model airplanes so Tim can take a turn geeking. I admire the planes for their beauty, particularly a woodgrain space shuttle with movingcompartments, but theyre no match for acetate equines.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19905" height="553" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/27timplanes-640x553.jpg" title="27timplanes" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
           Im going to get a little nostalgic for a moment, so bear with me, but do you remember your favorite toys as a child? And maybe you wondered why adults didnt seemas enchanted with your toys as you were? I used to swear Id never lose interest in my gurines and dolls, I feared it would mean losing my entire sense of self. But ofcourse I grew older and started leaving those beloved things behind.
         </p>
         <p>
          I can pick up a Breyer horse, admire the smooth plastic and artful details, but  thats it. Im not going to sit on the oor with it for hours, making it talk to other toys,acting out my latest conicts and fantasies, forming pretend relationships. I wish I could enjoy toys on that level again.
         </p>
         <p>
           We wander around for a few more minutes before admitting were worn out, we cant play with toys all day like we used to. Outside the Javits Center, we see a mancontrolling a large toy car and I ask, Hey, can you give me a ride? He pretends not to hear me. Hes
          <em>
           very
          </em>
          serious.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19906" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/28toycarguy-328x640.jpg" title="28toycarguy" width="328"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          The end.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-19907" height="640" src="https://awlnetwork-static-theawl.netdna-ssl.com//wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2012/04/29weddingstaypuft-518x640.jpg" title="29weddingstaypuft" width="518"/>

           Previously

           <em>
            War Horse
           </em>
           , an Illustrated Review.
          </a>
         </p>
