         <p>
          The fine folks at
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/agencekerozen">
           Kerozen
          </a>
          have created the most horrifying thing to ever penetrate the eyes of unsuspecting readers. Using photographs of members of their design team, Kerozen artist JC Debroize fashioned letters featuring their flesh, eyes, and hair. And its the worst thing in the world, and I will never get over it.
         </p>
         <p>
          Heres an E with a soul patch, just in caseyou thought you would eversafely dream again:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="This human flesh font is the worst thing to ever happen to your psyche" class="alignnone" height="600" src="https://m1.behance.net/rendition/modules/81432955/disp/67e6f0f7aa7c8ae06e2f23863a652719.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Heres a K to follow you in back alleys forever:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="This human flesh font is the worst thing to ever happen to your psyche" class="alignnone" height="600" src="https://m1.behance.net/rendition/modules/81432953/disp/1fd2cec227df71b7a5cbf874e52d9f3e.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          An O that will make you check behind the shower curtain every time you use the bathroom, just to make sure its not lurking there with a knife:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="This human flesh font is the worst thing to ever happen to your psyche" class="alignnone" height="600" src="https://m1.behance.net/rendition/modules/81432959/disp/13d5cdb29a63f521485ec25643bd3eb5.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Another E, just to drive home the fact that youre never going to be safe again:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="This human flesh font is the worst thing to ever happen to your psyche" class="alignnone" height="600" src="https://m1.behance.net/rendition/modules/81432963/disp/eaa4123c03e7b12ad4aa6275bc84e822.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          An N to never let you fully enjoy anything good in this world:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="This human flesh font is the worst thing to ever happen to your psyche" class="alignnone" height="600" src="https://m1.behance.net/rendition/modules/81432965/disp/1a1599f3fadbdf369f49899b4475b3fa.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          A letterZ that will judge your every move, no matter what you do, forever:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="This human flesh font is the worst thing to ever happen to your psyche" class="alignnone" height="600" src="https://m1.behance.net/rendition/modules/81432961/disp/b73b3e2a43b4f16488b6209f9f0d5f85.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          And a letter R to follow you around and remind you that youll never be good enough for love, no matter how hard you try:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="This human flesh font is the worst thing to ever happen to your psyche" class="alignnone" height="600" src="https://m1.behance.net/rendition/modules/81432957/disp/147e90ed1f122040f49438eb85def66e.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          And, just in case youre wondering what all of these letters spell, here they are together, forming the design teams name:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="This human flesh font is the worst thing to ever happen to your psyche" class="alignnone" height="241" src="https://m1.behance.net/rendition/modules/81432967/disp/0af6187eb5da6721485ac2f63b5c43d1.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          THEY WILL WATCH YOU FOREVER.
         </p>
