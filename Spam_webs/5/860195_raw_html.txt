          <strong>
           Summary:
          </strong>
          A coronal mass ejection hit Earth on Dec. 14th, sparking a severe 
              (Kp=8) geomagnetic storm and auroras seen as far south as Arizona. 
              The source of the CME was an X3-class explosion from sunspot 930 
              on Dec. 13th.
         </font>
