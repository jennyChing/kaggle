      <h1 itemprop="headline">
       MLB All-Star Game Tickets Cheapest Since 2011 On Secondary Market
      </h1>
      <!-- Make individual group author appear -->
      <div class="mninst">
      </div>
      <div class="comment_links">
       <a class="scroll_to post_your_comment" href="#comment_reply">

       </a>
       <div class="comments_follow followable_block">
        <div class="followable_block initializing" data-object-json='{"title":"MLB All-Star Game Tickets Cheapest Since 2011 On Secondary Market","avatar_src":"","follow_count":""}' data-target-id="blogAndPostId/blog/post/2683-5590" data-target-name="" data-target-type="article" data-tracking-link-name="articleLink_ArticleCommentsFollow" data-type-advoice="false">

     <div class="article_body article" itemprop="articleBody">
      <div class="body">
       <div class="body_inner">
        <p>
         The MLB All-Star game returns to Minnesota on July 15 for the first time since 1985. Itll also be the first time the game is hosted at
         <a href="http://www.tiqiq.com/seating-chart/target-field">
          Target Field
         </a>
         , the new ballpark of the Minnesota Twins that opened in 2010. On Sunday ESPN revealed the All-Star lineups and the Twins had two players make the American League squad with lefty reliever Glen Perkins and catcher Kurt Suzuki.
        </p>
        <p>
         But the game of course is about more than Minnesota and the Twins. The best players from the league will be on display for the event, and fans are willing to dole out money to see these players share the field together. According to TiqIQ,
         <a href="http://www.tiqiq.com/mlb/mlb-all-star-game-tickets">
          2014 MLB All-Star tickets
         </a>
         have an average price of $752.34 on the secondary market, over 1,000% more expensive than the average price for
         <a href="http://www.tiqiq.com/mlb/minnesota-twins-tickets">
          Twins tickets
         </a>
         at home this season. While that certainly seems like a high price, especially relative to a regular season game, the average price was as high as $1,096 for the game back in May.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="985ec8300a77895237429c2e628f90b6" class=" size-full wp-image-5591" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/jesselawrence/files/2014/07/985ec8300a77895237429c2e628f90b6.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         In fact, the average is less than its been over the last couple seasons. Last year the average price was $883.44 for the game at
         <a href="http://www.tiqiq.com/seating-chart/citi-field">
          Citi Field
         </a>
         , and in 2012 it was $863.61 at Kauffman Stadium. Its not until you go back to 2011 at Chase Field that you find a cheaper average. That game had an average price of just $351.02, which is an anomaly when you consider the average was $601.63 just the season before at Angel Stadium.
        </p>
        <p>
         While Minnesota has two players in the game, none of them were voted in as starters for the American League. The starting lineup for the AL is Salvador Perez (replacing an injured Matt Wieters) at catcher, Miguel Cabrera at first base, Robinson Cano at second, Derek Jeter at short, Josh Donaldson at third, Jose Bautista, Mike Trout and Adam Jones in the outfield, and Nelson Cruz as the designated hitter. The National League will have Yadier Molina at catcher, Paul Goldschmidt at first, Chase Utley at second, Troy Tulowitzki at short, Aramis Ramirez at third and Andrew McCutchen, Carlos Gomez and Yasiel Puig in the outfield.
        </p>
        <p>
         There are plenty of big and impressive names on that list, but the name that sticks out will be Jeter. Its not because hes having a particularly good year, but because this will be the last All-Star Game of his career with him announcing this is his final season prior to Opening Day. It will be Jeters 14th appearance in the game, and hell likely be celebrated during the game like Yankees closer Mariano Rivera was last year. For
         <a href="http://www.ticketsnow.com/new-york-yankees-tickets/">
          New York Yankees fans
         </a>
         , it will be 14.84% cheaper to see Jeters final All-Star Game than Riveras.
        </p>
        <p>
         The All-Star break is fast approaching, and once again baseballs biggest names and greatest players will share the field in a battle for league supremacy, and home field advantage in the World Series. It wont be cheap to see the leagues top players all gather at Target Field in Minnesota, but prices have dropped noticeably in the last two months, as the average price is now the lowest its been for an All-Star game since 2011.
        </p>
