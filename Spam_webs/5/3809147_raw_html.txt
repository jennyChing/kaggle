          We are doing a lot of alphabets activities to keep my preschoolers alphabet interest satisfied. She sees alphabets in every thing. Whenever we say a word, she tries to find out the letters in the word. She tries to read the title of her sisters book. It is great to see her interest towards letters. We made this alphabet necklace, a simple alphabet learning activity, thatsheenjoyed thoroughly.

         <p>
          3.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000F9XBQQ/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000F9XBQQ&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=eduathom04-20&amp;linkId=DX2G4GF7RZUBY4OX" rel="nofollow">
           Sharpie markers
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           <strong>
            Preparation:
           </strong>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          1. Cut out a small piece of masking tape and attach it to one side of the paper clips.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          2. Write the alphabets in the attached tape with sharpie.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://sparklingbuds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Alphabet-activities-for-preschoolers.jpg">
           <img alt="Alphabet activities for preschoolers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1004" height="450" src="http://sparklingbuds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Alphabet-activities-for-preschoolers.jpg" width="597"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           <strong>
            How to play:
           </strong>
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          There is no rule to play. Ask the preschooler to pick a clip, call out the letters written on it. Attach the paper clipstogether to make a necklace.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://sparklingbuds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Fine-motor-activities-for-preschoolers.jpg">
           <img alt="Fine motor activities for preschoolers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1006" height="450" src="http://sparklingbuds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Fine-motor-activities-for-preschoolers.jpg" width="675"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          My preschooler wanted to attach the paper clips in the alphabetical order. She needed help to attach the paper clips sometimes. But she was determined to finish the necklace. She was proudly wearing the necklace she had made!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://sparklingbuds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Alphabet-learning-activities-for-preschoolers.jpg">
           <img alt="Alphabet learning activities for preschoolers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1005" height="450" src="http://sparklingbuds.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Alphabet-learning-activities-for-preschoolers.jpg" width="666"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          This is a very simple alphabet activity that gives work to her fine motor skills too. Bonus!
         </p>
