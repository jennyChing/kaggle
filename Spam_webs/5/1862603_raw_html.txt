When you vape, some smoke does get released, but as evidenced by crafty inhalers such as
                <a href="https://youtu.be/JZO8opyg14k?t=3m23s" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://youtu.be/JZO8opyg14k?t=3m23s', 'this guy');">

                 <h1>
                  6 Situations Where Portable Vapes are Lifesavers
                 </h1>
                 <p>
                  By Smokazon
                 </p>
                 <p>
                 </p>
                </heading>
                <ul>
                 <li>
                  <h2>
                   When Your Tinder Date is Fizzling
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                   By Smokazon
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Fizzling first dates can be awkwardunless you haveyour trusty portable vaporizer. If theres no connection and youve decided to stop caring about making a good impression, simply excuse yourself and hit the washroom for a few minutes. Youll either come back in a better frame of mind oryoull blurt out something ridiculous and your date will hastily conclude the night.
                  </p>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <h2>
                   At the Office
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                   By Smokazon
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Weve all been theremany of us are there right now. Its around 2 in the afternoon and time is moving slowly. You just want to head home, but still have hours of work ahead of you. Instead of sulking, pop your vaporizer in your pocket and head for the corner stall of the washroom. A couple of short puffs and youre good to go.
                  </p>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <h2>
                   At the Back of Your Lecture Hall
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                   By Smokazon
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   This one is a no-brainer, literally. Your coffee cant diffusethe deadly combination oflast nightsdouble-kegger and this mornings9am lecture on War and Peace.So take a seat at the very back, pull out your vaporizer (pen vape is ideal) and slink down in your chair. All will be right with the world once again.Protip: Take a
                   <a href="http://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=ghost+hit" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=ghost+hit', 'ghost hit');">
                    ghost hit
                   </a>
                   .
                  </p>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <h2>
                   Visiting Your Grandparents
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                   By Smokazon
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Theyre sweet and you love them, but these types of visits tend to drag on. Imagine how fun a visit to Grandma and Grandpas would be if you could duck-out onto their patio and spend a few minutes with your vaporizer. Just pretend youre taking a phone call. With any luck, that roast beef dinnerwill be on the table by the time you get back.
                  </p>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <h2>
                   On Public Transit
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                   By Smokazon
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   Considering transit-userscan bepretty self-interested individuals, its unlikely that anyone will take notice of the puffs of smoke coming fromyour general direction. Get high and enjoy your commute homejust try not to forgetwhich stop is yours.
                  </p>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <h2>
                   At a Sporting Event or Concert
                  </h2>
                  <p>
                   By Smokazon
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   There will already be such a plethora of sights, smells and sounds at a game or concert, that no one will even notice that youre putting a vape to your lips. Or that your eyes are as red as your teams jerseys. The days of getting busted in your seats for smoking up are overgo team.
                  </p>
                 </li>
                </ul>
