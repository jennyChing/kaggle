            <div class="textilized" id="project-content">
             <p>
              When I first posted the
              <a href="http://lumberjocks.com/projects/17273">
               Knothead Box
              </a>
              I didnt realize at the time, but it seemed to be a fairly unique joint and the comments were pretty positve, so I decided to try another one. I wanted to try this one without the legs to see how it looked. This one is mostly for practice to try and hone the joint a bit. The joint didnt need the splines for strength as its strong, but I wanted to break up all of the red in the Padauk a little. It gave me some more practice on the multi colored splines too that Ive been trying out.
             </p>
             <p>
              The woods used are Padauk, Zebra, Wenge, Walnut and Maple and the bottom and tray are lined with tan suede. Thanks for taking the time to look and any and all comments and feedback are appreciated.
             </p>

              -- Brad, Oregon- The things that come to those who wait, may be the things left by those who got there first.
