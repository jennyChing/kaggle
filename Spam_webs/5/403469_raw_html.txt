         <h1>
          Learn If a Senior Life Settlement is Right For You!
         </h1>
         <p class="info">
          <strong class="date">
           <a href="http://www.sellyourinsurance.com/senior-life-settlements/" rel="bookmark">
            January 14th, 2015
           </a>
          </strong>
          by Lee Berman
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="content">
         <p>
          Are you a senior citizen aged 75 or older, or do you have someone in your family who is 75 or older? Are you also strapped for cash? Then its time you start learning about possible options to help yourself and your loved ones. If you have a life insurance policy and are 75 or older you may be able to sell your life insurance policy for a value greater than the cash value.
         </p>
         <p>
          It is normal for many people to have purchased life insurance policies, especially at times when they were raising a family because of the financial obligations. If you were to suddenly pass away you would want to leave your family with money so to cover expenses and help them. You may be familiar with the fact that you can quit your life insurance policy by not paying the premiums and letting it lapse, or take the cash value that was built up in the policy. While this will give you some quick cash, your policy is actually worth a lot more than this, and thats why information and news
          <a href="http://www.sellyourinsurance.com/seniors/">
           about life settlements for seniors is finally getting out there.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          So lets glance at a quick example.
         </p>
         <p>
          Age/Gender: 75/Male
         </p>
         <p>
          Policy Type: Universal Life
         </p>
         <p>
          Face Amount: $100,000
         </p>
         <p>
          Annual Premium: $1,250
         </p>
         <p>
          Cash Surrender Value: $7,000
         </p>
         <p>
          Purchase Price: $18,000
         </p>
         <p>
          If this elderly man had decided to just surrender his plan he would have only received $7,000 cash, which was not enough to help him with his needs, but by selling his plan he was able to receive over two times the surrender value. What many of us have not realized is that by selling and surrendering our plan to a third-party we can make a lot more money on an unwanted life insurance policy. Given difficult economic times created the Great Recession it is understandable that many of us are in need of cash now.
         </p>
         <p>
          You shouldnt feel bad if you are 75 or older and feel that you no longer want your life insurance policy and want to make a life settlement. 75 years ago the life expectancy for people wasnt as high as it is today, and if we look back through history we will see that even though it has steadily gone up being able to properly appropriate the right amount of money for retirement is almost impossible. So if you are in financial need and are considering a life settlement be sure to educate yourself on all available options before making a decision.
         </p>
