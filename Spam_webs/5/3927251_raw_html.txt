                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     5 Ways to Attract People You Want In Your Life
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-2379272607244990226" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <!-- Go to www.addthis.com/dashboard to customize your tools -->
                     <br/>
                     <div class="addthis_native_toolbox">
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      I came across an old friend a few weeks ago. She was clearly frustrated, and kept saying, "I don't like my circle of friends, but I still hang out with them as they are only people I know.I feel drained when I'm around them. Their talks doesn't interest me. Their visions and views are different to mine, yet I cannot seem to let go of them".
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      She prompted me to write this post. I am certain, many of you could be feeling the same right now. If you do, no need to panic. The fact that you have been able to recognize your social circle isn't doing you any good is proof enough that you want to change things.
                      <br/>
                      I can help you with that. Read on!
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      How many times have you felt stuck, surrounded by the 'wrong' people?
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      Let me make myself clear here. When I say 'wrong' I don't mean 'bad'. 'Wrong' in the sense that some people are simply not meant to be a part of your life. They're a mismatch for you. As the well known proverb goes 'birds of the same feather flock together'....Think about it. Makes complete sense right? We humans are the same. We usually gravitate towards those we feel comfortable with. Just like when we're ready to be in a serious relationship, we look out for our soulmate, likewise as we grow older we realize the importance of having REAL friends as opposed to those who simply say a 'hi and a 'bye'. We become increasingly selective and look for those who we truly want in our lives.
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ufdz2tK-aho/VO38VjInccI/AAAAAAAAgUE/hnud_0SKFRU/s1600/people.png" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="266" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ufdz2tK-aho/VO38VjInccI/AAAAAAAAgUE/hnud_0SKFRU/s1600/people.png" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                      I am truly pleased with my circle of friends both in real life and on social media. As I get to know myself better, I find it easier to attract people I need in my life and I don't hesitate in letting go of those who bring nothing but negativity.
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      Believe it or not, our success is partly determined by the people we are surrounded with on a daily basis.
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      We all want people whose aspirations are similar to ours, those who inspire and believe in us when we doubt ourselves. Unfortunately, attracting the 'right' people doesn't happen by chance and certainly not overnight. It requires a fair bit of personal effort and belief that you can attract who you want in your life.
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      Here's 5 ways to attract the people you want in your life.
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <b>
                       <span style="color: #741b47;">
                        1. Be True To Yourself
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      I can't stop myself from emphasizing how important being 'REAL' truly is. I've often seen people letting go of their true selves just to fit in. If you can't be your own true self around those you mingle with, time to reconsider your circle of friends. Being true to yourself sounds simple, but there's a lot of courage and will power involved. You might annoy a few and it could cost you a few friends but it will worth it in the long run. Fact of life:- You can't have everything. You have to lose something to get something. People are usually attracted to those who are true to themselves. Hence whatever you do, be your best version of yourself. You have nothing to lose and everything to gain in doing so.
                     </div>
                     <blockquote class="tr_bq">
                      <span style="color: #134f5c;">
                       <b>
                        "To be yourself in a world that is constantly trying to make you something else is the greatest accomplishment"
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      Ralph Waldo Emerson
                     </blockquote>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-5hWsjmGWjKE/VO3u_67-tWI/AAAAAAAAgT0/G2emEYr4UQI/s1600/2015-02-25%2B15.16.15.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-5hWsjmGWjKE/VO3u_67-tWI/AAAAAAAAgT0/G2emEYr4UQI/s1600/2015-02-25%2B15.16.15.jpg" width="240"/>
                      </a>
                      <span style="color: #741b47;">
                       <b>
                        2. Toot your horn
                       </b>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      Yes! You heard me right. Toot your horn. Promote yourself. You are your own brand. If you don't, who will? You probably aren't crossing paths with people you want in your life simply because you aren't advocating for yourself. Remember though, that there's a fine line on calling to get noticed and seeking attention in an obnoxious manner. Get that right and you'll attract who you want and be able to make a difference. Display your work. Get recognised for what you do. Be known. Use social media to your advantage here but don't overdo it.
                     </div>
                     <blockquote class="tr_bq">
                      <span style="color: #134f5c;">
                       <b>
                        "If you don't toot your own horn don't complain that there's no music"
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      Guy Kawasaki
                     </blockquote>
                     <br/>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <span style="color: #741b47;">
                       <b>
                        3. Know what you want and stick to it
                       </b>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      People often struggle to find the 'right' company because they still haven't been able to figure out what they want or they probably know what they want but can't commit. That's a dilemma.
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      The moment you know exactly what you want that's when everything else starts falling into place. Let's take my instance here. (tooting my horn...haha) Let's get serious!
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      After spending years being confused and dazed about what I wanted, I realized I wanted to make a difference in people's lives. I began by offering support and advice to anybody feeling under the weather, post positive messages on Facebook encouraging people to see the brighter side of life on a daily basis, which then led to blogging. While blogging about my experiences and thoughts on positivity, I happen to come across people who were 'right' for me. People I really wanted to mingle with. People I felt comfortable discussing my dreams and aspirations with.
                     </div>
                     <blockquote class="tr_bq">
                      <span style="color: #134f5c;">
                       <b>
                        "If you don't know what you want, you'll end up with a lot you don't"
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      Chuck Palahnluk
                     </blockquote>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <b>
                       <span style="color: #741b47;">
                        4. Say 'NO'
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      Tough isn't it? It is for many. Being unable to say 'NO' can screw your life badly. You need to bring yourself to say 'NO' to a lot of things and people who just don't fit in your life. You certainly don't have to feel guilty about it. If it's going to cost you more than you can afford, say NO. It's your life. It's your choice. Do what you want. No need to explain yourself at all times. Remember 'NO' is a complete sentence.
                     </div>
                     <blockquote class="tr_bq">
                      <b>
                       <span style="color: #134f5c;">
                        "Saying NO means saying 'YES' to something more desirable"
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      Unknown
                     </blockquote>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <b>
                       <span style="color: #741b47;">
                        5. Find like minded people
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      <b>
                       <span style="color: #741b47;">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      If you're looking to surround yourself with people who challenge you, join clubs or take part in seminars and workshops where you get to mingle with people whose tastes are similar to yours. You don't need me to tell you the power of social media. The world is literally at our finger tips. Finding like minded people is a breeze. You could easily join forums, groups, communities you're interested in, share your views and listen to what others have to say. You could also attend webinars, skype sessions or google hangouts and very likely come across people who share your vision and passion. Isn't it better to hang out with people who are miles away from you, yet fully relate to you than being close to those who literally make you feel drained?
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                      Start choosing wisely and carefully.
                     </div>
                     <div dir="ltr">
                     </div>
                     <blockquote class="tr_bq">
                      <b>
                       <span style="color: #134f5c;">
                        "Who you hang out with determines what you dream about and what you collide with. And the collisions and the dreams lead to your changes. And the changes are what you become. Change the outcome by changing your circle"
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      Seth Godin
                     </blockquote>
                     <blockquote class="tr_bq">
                      <b>
                       <span style="color: #134f5c;">
                        "Evaluate people in your life; then promote, demote or terminate. You're the CEO of your life"
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      Unknown
                     </blockquote>
                     Your turn. How do you attract people you want in your life? Do you find it easy to do so? Are you happy with your circle of friends? I would love to hear from you:)
