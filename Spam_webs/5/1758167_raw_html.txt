        <h2 align="LEFT" style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          <strong>
           Steve Spring helps youtransform yourgoals fromdreams to reality.
          </strong>
         </em>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         ___
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         Do you go about your daily life just getting by? Do you save your dreams and goals for
         <i>
          someday?
         </i>
         Wouldnt you love to turn that
         <i>
          someday
         </i>
         into today?
        </p>
        <div style="border-left: 2px solid; padding-left: 15px; margin: 0px 15px 15px 20px; float: right; clear: both; width: 350px;">
         <table align="right" border="0.5" cellpadding="0" width="350">
          <tbody>
           <tr>
            <td>
             <div>
              <span style="font-size: 20px; font-family: georgia; color: #307d7e; line-height: 125%;">
               Whats your biggest reason for putting off your goals until a later date?
              </span>
             </div>
            </td>
           </tr>
          </tbody>
         </table>
        </div>
        <p align="LEFT">
         Your personal goals
         <i>
          can
         </i>
         become reality sooner than you think! By believing in yourself and taking decisive action, even if its one small step at a time, you can wake upone day soon and find that youre living the life of your dreams.
         <span id="more-1601">
         </span>
         Whats your biggest reason for putting off your goals until a later date? Is it because your goals are unattainable, or require a tremendous change from the life youre living now? The good news is, even with large goals,
         <i>
          <b>
           you can get yourself on the right track
          </b>
         </i>
         by consistently allocating a little free time toward these goals.
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <b>
          Here are some tips to help you achieve your goals:
         </b>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <p align="LEFT">
           <b>
            Clarify your goals.
           </b>
           Instead of thinking of your goals as vague ideas, determine exactly what you want. If you want money, how much do you want? If you want to lose weight, how much?
           <i>
            <b>
             Specify your goal in a measurable format.
            </b>
           </i>
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <p align="LEFT">
           <b>
            Make a solid plan.
           </b>
           The planning phase is very important. This is where you figure out how youre going to get what you want. Its as simple as choosing a path toward your goal and writing down each step youll need to take to achieve it.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <p align="LEFT">
           <b>
            Hold yourself to deadlines.
           </b>
           Give yourself a reasonable amount of time to achieve each step and hold yourself accountable.
           <i>
            <b>
             When the going gets tough, just picture that big payoff
            </b>
           </i>
           youll enjoy when you get past your hurdles and achieve your goal.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <p align="LEFT">
           <b>
            Be prepared to deal with setbacks.
           </b>
           Expect the unexpected. Sometimes, things go awry. When facing a setback, learn what you can from the situation and then fight your way back into the game.
           <i>
            <b>
             Most importantly, continue to believe in yourself.
            </b>
           </i>
           Each challenge you overcome makes you stronger.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <p align="LEFT">
           <b>
            Explore your options.
           </b>
           Sometimes when your circumstances in life change, your priorities also change, and thats okay! (
           <a href="http://clicktotweet.com/9P885" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://clicktotweet.com/9P885', 'Tweet this');">
            Tweet this
           </a>
           ) Never be afraid of reevaluating your situation and your goals. If you decide to take a different path in life, its your decision. Make new goals, and corresponding plans, for your new journey.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <p align="LEFT">
           <b>
            Be serious about your goals.
           </b>
           When you feel that your goals are a priority, itll be easier for you to avoid procrastination. Taking your wants and needs seriously could make all the difference between reaching your goals or not.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <p align="LEFT">
           <b>
            Break your goal into smaller pieces.
           </b>
           This tip is especially important when youre pursuing a large goal. For example, if your goal is to go after a career that involves a great deal of specialized training, then make each type of training a goal in itself. Celebrate as you complete each step because youre now one step closer to your dream!
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <p align="LEFT">
           <b>
            Get the help you need.
           </b>
           There are many people willing to help you reach your goals in life. Whether its someone to lean on to give you an extra confidence push, or someone to outsource work to, itll show you that you can count on others for help.
           <i>
            <b>
             With help, you can accomplish more.
            </b>
           </i>
          </p>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p align="LEFT">
         It all comes down to your attitude. Once youve got your goals organized, if you have a positive mindset about achieving them, youll also have the drive necessary to keep working until you reach success. Take these strategies to heart, and live the life you desire
         <i>
          and deserve!
         </i>
        </p>
