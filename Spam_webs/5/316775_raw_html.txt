        Innergie PocketCell  powerful portable battery pack for mobile devices
       </h1>

     <p>
      The 2.8-ounce (79-gram) Innergie PocketCell is a little larger than a car key, and allows for hours of extra use of Apple and
      <a href="http://tdevice.net/samsung-galaxy-note-supersizes-smartphone/">
       Android devices
      </a>
      , MP3 players, and a variety of other
      <a href="http://tdevice.net/pda-personal-digital-assistant-future-gadgets-concept/">
       mobile devices
      </a>
      . It comes bundled with the Magic Cable Trio 3-in-1 charging cable, which allows
      <a href="http://tdevice.net/tascam-im2-stereo-microphone-for-idevices/">
       devices
      </a>
      with Apple, mini USB or micro USB connections to plug into a standard USB port. The PocketCell incorporates a 2.1 Amp USB power port and a 3,000 mAh rechargeable lithium-ion battery bank. Once charged, it can power
      <a href="http://tdevice.net/communication-device-for-deaf-people-communicaid/">
       devices
      </a>
      directly, or be used to recharge their batteries. It can be charged via USB from a laptop or desktop computer, or using a USB power adapter.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://tdevice.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/InnergiePocketCellpowerfulportablebatterypackformobiledevices8.jpg">
       <img alt="Innergie PocketCell - powerful portable battery pack for mobile devices8" border="0" height="574" src="http://tdevice.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/InnergiePocketCellpowerfulportablebatterypackformobiledevices8_thumb.jpg" style="display: inline; border: 0px;" title="Innergie PocketCell - powerful portable battery pack for mobile devices8" width="764"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      <span id="more-1624">
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      The one fully-fueled PocketCell could manage two full
      <a href="http://tdevice.net/apples-new-iphone-4s/">
       iPhone 4
      </a>
      charges, or half an iPad charge. A fully-charged PocketCell should be good for about 4.5 hours of iPad use; over 25 hours of talk time on an
      <a href="http://tdevice.net/iphone-5-concept/">
       iPhone
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="http://tdevice.net/htc-rezound-android-smartphone/">
       Android
      </a>
      or Blackberry
      <a href="http://tdevice.net/htc-bleyback-smartphone-concept/">
       smartphone
      </a>
      ; 100 hours of playback time on an iPod or MP3 player; or over 10 hours of playing time on a game console. The PocketCell is available for US$79.99.
     </p>
