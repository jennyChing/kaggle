        <div style="width: 890px; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
         <h1>
          3D Printing Resources
         </h1>
         <p>
          If youre looking for a good place to get started learning about 3D printing, you can use the resources on this page.
         </p>
         <p>
          This 3D printing resource will be updated and added to on a regular basis in order to keep it relevant. If you have any suggestions for resources that should be added, please contact me and let me know.
         </p>
         <p style="margin-bottom: 50px;">
          <span style="color: #dd0000;">
           <em>
            Disclosure:it should be noted that I may make a small commission on some of the items listed below should you purchase them through my affiliate link. These commissions help me keep the site running and allow me to add more features to the site.
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <h3>
          3D Printing Booksto Get You Started
         </h3>
         <p>
          The following is a list of books that will help you get started in understanding what 3D printing is, howthe technologyworks, as well as some of the future implications the technology will likely have. These are good starting points for anyone who isnt familiar with 3D printing and wants to learn more about the technology.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class="easyazon-link" data-cart="n" data-cloak="n" data-identifier="0307720969" data-locale="US" data-localize="y" data-popups="n" data-tag="thowisguy-20" href="http://www.amazon.com/Makers-The-New-Industrial-Revolution/dp/0307720969?tag=thowisguy-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/Makers-The-New-Industrial-Revolution/dp/0307720969?tag=thowisguy-20', '']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <img class="easyazon-image alignleft" height="160" src="http://ecx.images-amazon.com/images/I/51uDsV2tE3L._SL160_.jpg" width="107"/>
          </a>
          <a class="easyazon-link" data-cart="n" data-cloak="n" data-identifier="0307720950" data-locale="US" data-localize="y" data-popups="n" data-tag="thowisguy-20" href="http://www.amazon.com/Makers-The-New-Industrial-Revolution/dp/0307720950?tag=thowisguy-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/Makers-The-New-Industrial-Revolution/dp/0307720950?tag=thowisguy-20', 'Makers: The New Industrial Revolution']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            Makers: The New Industrial Revolution
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="margin-bottom: 38px;">
          In this book,
          <em>
           Wired
          </em>
          magazine editor and bestselling author Chris Anderson gives you a sort of visionary, if not in-depth, look into the 3D printing industry. The book dives into the maker movement and explains how it stacks up against previous industrial revolutions. Overall this is probably one of the best books on 3D printing, especially for anyone who wants to get a look atthe full potential of the industry.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class="easyazon-link" data-cart="n" data-cloak="n" data-identifier="1494213966" data-locale="US" data-localize="y" data-popups="n" data-tag="thowisguy-20" href="http://www.amazon.com/Printing-Technology-Capitalize-Distributed-Manufacturing/dp/1494213966?tag=thowisguy-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/Printing-Technology-Capitalize-Distributed-Manufacturing/dp/1494213966?tag=thowisguy-20', '']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <img class="easyazon-image alignleft" height="160" src="http://ecx.images-amazon.com/images/I/51H%2BaSyDRFL._SL160_.jpg" width="107"/>
          </a>
          <a class="easyazon-link" data-cart="n" data-cloak="n" data-identifier="1494213966" data-locale="US" data-localize="y" data-popups="n" data-tag="thowisguy-20" href="http://www.amazon.com/Printing-Technology-Capitalize-Distributed-Manufacturing/dp/1494213966?tag=thowisguy-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/Printing-Technology-Capitalize-Distributed-Manufacturing/dp/1494213966?tag=thowisguy-20', '3D Printing: The Next Technology Gold Rush']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            3D Printing: The Next Technology Gold Rush
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="margin-bottom: 38px;">
          This book provides a general overview of 3D printing as well as a number of markets that individuals can get into through the use a 3D printer. Ultimately, this is a decent book that will cover the basics of 3D printing, how to get started in it, and it will open your mind to the possibilities of 3D printing from a manufacturing and business point-of-view. A good read for any prospective printers.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class="easyazon-link" data-cart="n" data-cloak="n" data-identifier="1118350634" data-locale="US" data-localize="y" data-popups="n" data-tag="thowisguy-20" href="http://www.amazon.com/Fabricated-The-New-World-Printing/dp/1118350634?tag=thowisguy-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/Fabricated-The-New-World-Printing/dp/1118350634?tag=thowisguy-20', '']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <img class="easyazon-image alignleft" height="160" src="http://ecx.images-amazon.com/images/I/417AfnGpmwL._SL160_.jpg" width="107"/>
          </a>
          <a class="easyazon-link" data-cart="n" data-cloak="n" data-identifier="1118350634" data-locale="US" data-localize="y" data-popups="n" data-tag="thowisguy-20" href="http://www.amazon.com/Fabricated-The-New-World-Printing/dp/1118350634?tag=thowisguy-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/Fabricated-The-New-World-Printing/dp/1118350634?tag=thowisguy-20', 'Fabricated: The New World of 3D Printing']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            Fabricated: The New World of 3D Printing
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="margin-bottom: 38px;">
          This well-written and informative overviewof 3D printing is another good option for anyone looking to go over the basics ofand dive a little bit further into 3D printing. The book covers what is being done in 3D printing today as well as provides a look at what the technology will be capable of in the future. If youre new to 3D printing then this book will catch you up quickly.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class="easyazon-link" data-cart="n" data-cloak="n" data-identifier="148418176X" data-locale="US" data-localize="y" data-popups="n" data-tag="thowisguy-20" href="http://www.amazon.com/3D-Printing-Next-Industrial-Revolution/dp/148418176X?tag=thowisguy-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/3D-Printing-Next-Industrial-Revolution/dp/148418176X?tag=thowisguy-20', '']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <img class="easyazon-image alignleft" height="160" src="http://ecx.images-amazon.com/images/I/51ywuHich8L._SL160_.jpg" width="107"/>
          </a>
          <a class="easyazon-link" data-cart="n" data-cloak="n" data-identifier="148418176X" data-locale="US" data-localize="y" data-popups="n" data-tag="thowisguy-20" href="http://www.amazon.com/3D-Printing-Next-Industrial-Revolution/dp/148418176X?tag=thowisguy-20" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/3D-Printing-Next-Industrial-Revolution/dp/148418176X?tag=thowisguy-20', '3D Printing: The Next Industrial Revolution']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            3D Printing: The Next Industrial Revolution
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="margin-bottom: 38px;">
          Another solid introduction into the world of 3D printing. Like the other books on this list,
          <em>
           3D Printing: The Next Industrial Revolution
          </em>
          will give you a look at what 3D printing is and what it has the potential to become. It also covers who the players in the industry are and goes into a little bit of their backgrounds. Another great read for 3D printing beginners and veterans alike.
         </p>
         <p style="margin-bottom: 38px;">
         </p>
         <hr class=" sb-hr" size="1" style="background:black"/>
         <h3 style="margin-top: 38px;">
          Helpful 3D Printing Websites
         </h3>
         <p>
          3D printing is making waves across the internet. The recent rise in popularity for 3D printing has lead to the establishment of many online sites dedicated to 3D printing. What follows is a list (albeit a short one in the grand scheme of things) of helpful 3D printing sites.
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
           <strong>
            <a href="http://3dforged.com">
             3DForged
            </a>
            :
           </strong>
           Well, I couldnt include a list of 3D printing sites without including my own. With 3DForged.com I have three goals First, to provide as much help as possible to 3D printing beginners, second to help spread the word and get people involved in 3D printing, and third, to develop a strong community.
          </li>
          <li style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.thingiverse.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thingiverse.com/', 'Thingiverse']);">
             Thingiverse
            </a>
            :
           </strong>
           Thingiverse is perhaps the most important 3D printing site on the internet. On Thingiverse you can find thousands of 3D printable things, including trinkets, jewelry, gears, models, figures, etc. Ultimately, if you want to find something cool to print on your new 3D printer, then Thingiverse is the place to go.
          </li>
          <li style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.sculpteo.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.sculpteo.com/', 'Sculpteo']);">
             Sculpteo
            </a>
            :
           </strong>
           Sculpteo and Shapeways are similar in that they provide a platform where anyone can get their ideas professionally printer. So, if you want a prototype of something, or just a cool trinket, Sculpteo will print it for you. For professional designers and printers, Sculpteo offers a platform to sell your designs.
          </li>
          <li style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.shapeways.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.shapeways.com/', 'Shapeways']);">
             Shapeways
            </a>
            :
           </strong>
           As mentioned above, Shapeways offers the same thing as Sculpteo: a platform to get your ideas printed, or to sell your designsto people who want them printed. Both platforms provide consumers with the ability to access professionally-printed 3D objects.
          </li>
