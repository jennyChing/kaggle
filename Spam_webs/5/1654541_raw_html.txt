     <div class="entry-inner">
      <h3 class="entry-header">
       iPhone Dock Lets You Make Calls Like It's 1937
      </h3>
      <div class="entry-content">
       <div class="entry-body">
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/12905355@N05/4542184078/" title="iRetro iPhone Base by Photo Giddy, on Flickr">
         <img alt="iRetro iPhone Base" src="http://farm3.static.flickr.com/2728/4542184078_64c2ab6413_o.jpg" style="width: 485px; height: 373px;"/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <p>
         <strong>
          STYLE GUIDE
         </strong>
         : I'm not exactly sure why I'd want to tether my epic mobile handset to a retro phone base, but Freelandstudios has created just such a monster to make iPhone calling feel like an ancient chore.
        </p>
        <p>
         This cleverly handmade "iRetrofone" dock, borrows inspiration from the prolific industrial designer Henry Dreyfuss who created the iconic type 302 phone back in 1937. The modern recasting is sculpted one at a time from resin with the proper notch provided for your iPhone dock cable.
        </p>
        <p>
         The base is designed to be compatible with all iPhones and retro handset works for calls. Now just add the
         <strong>
          <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/iretrophone-rotary-dialer/id284700702?mt=8" target="_blank" title="http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/iretrophone-rotary-dialer/id284700702?mt=8">
           iRetroPhone Rotary Dialer
          </a>
         </strong>
         app ($.99) into the mix (like we did) and you'll instantly dazzle friends with this authentic piece of modern art for the antique soul.
        </p>
        <p>
         Expect to pay
         <strong>
