       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Arcs 21 [iPhone, Processing, oF]
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-author">
        written by
        <a href="http://www.creativeapplications.net/author/filip/" title="All posts by Filip Visnjic">

       <a href="http://www.creativeapplications.net/processing/arcs-21-iphone-processing-of/" title="Permalink to Arcs 21 [iPhone, Processing, oF]">
        <img alt="arcs2101" class="attachment-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" height="450" src="http://www.creativeapplications.net/wp-content/uploads/2009/12/arcs2101.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </div>
      <!-- end .entry-thumbnail -->
      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-wVAP]" href="http://www.creativeapplications.net/wp-content/uploads/2009/12/arcs2101.png">
         <img alt="arcs2101" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-6350" height="480" src="http://www.creativeapplications.net/wp-content/uploads/2009/12/arcs2101-640x480.png" title="arcs2101" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Arcs 21
        </em>
        is the latest application for the iPhone by the Austrian generative artist
        <a href="http://www.liaworks.com/">
         Lia
        </a>
        . Originally created in
        <a href="http://processing.org/">
         Processing
        </a>
        , which you can
        <a href="http://liaworks.com/projects/arcs21/">
         view here
        </a>
        , Arcs 21 has been ported to the iPhone using
        <a href="http://www.openframeworks.cc/">
         openFrameworks
        </a>
        . The app includes controllable parameters allowing you to influence the sculpting the development of the artwork on your screen. No single images is the same as any other.
       </p>
       <p>
        See this
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/lia_lia/sets/72157612528559730/">
         flickr group
        </a>
        for images created using the Processing app.
       </p>
       <p>
        From December 2009 until April 2010 the original Arcs 21 can be viewed in the Victoria and Albert Museum as a part of
        <a href="http://www.vam.ac.uk/exhibitions/future_exhibs/Decode/">
         Decode exhibition
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Platform: iPhone
        <br/>
        Version: 1.0
        <br/>
        Cost: Free
        <br/>
        Developer:
        <a href="http://www.liaworks.com/theprojects/arcs21/">
         Lia
        </a>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/arcs-21/id338741179?mt=8">

        </a>
       </p>
