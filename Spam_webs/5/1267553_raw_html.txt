        <h2 class="skin_details_bottom_titles">
         Description:
        </h2>
        Agret WhatPulse User Info is a script which connects to the WhatPulse Web API and downloads your current stats. Your stats are then accessible for use in your Personal Message and inside conversations through variables.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Version 1.2 addeds 2 missing variables to the cheat sheet, adds some default quick texts &amp; numbers are now comma delimited.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        How to use this script:
        <br/>
        - Make sure you have Live Messenger and Messenger Plus! Live installed.
        <br/>
        - Download the script, open it, accept the warning and press Import.
        <br/>
        - Open a conversation window.
        <br/>
        - Click on the Messenger Plus! logo in your conversation window.
        <br/>
        - Click on WhatPulse User Info, and choose Cheat Sheet.
        <br/>
        - Use any of the tags listed there in your messages and they will automatically be replace by your WhatPulse stats.
