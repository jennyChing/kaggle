        <h3 class="hidden title">
         FAQ
        </h3>
        <b>
         What platforms can I play The Sims 4 on?
        </b>
        <br/>
        The Sims 4 can be played on PC and Mac. And because this title supports dual entitlement, you can play on either platform with a single purchase.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <b>
         Is a persistent online connection required to play The Sims 4?
        </b>
        <br/>
        Youll need to be online to start The Sims 4 for the first time. Once you're all set up, you can take advantage of Origin's Offline Mode and play The Sims 4 without an internet connection.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <b>
         Will I be able to bring any of my Sims or content from The Sims 3 into The Sims 4?
        </b>
        <br/>
        The Sims 4 has been developed from the start to take advantage of new and unique features that were not possible in past editions of The Sims. The Sims 4 cannot use characters or content from previous versions of The Sims.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <b>
         Will I be able to use SimPoints to purchase additional content in The Sims 4?
        </b>
        <br/>
        SimPoints are used to purchase content in The Sims 3 and you can continue to buy content for that version of the game. The Sims 4 does not use SimPoints for in-game purchases.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <b>
         What is the Sims 4 Rewards Program?
        </b>
        <br/>
        If you own The Sims 3 and any of its Expansion Packs, you may register them to unlock bonus digital content in The Sims 4. Its our way of thanking you for your continuing dedication to The Sims!
