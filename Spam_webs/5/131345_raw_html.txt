        <h1 class="title" itemprop="name headline">
         The Ultimate Guide to Hashtags #hashtagprimer
        </h1>
        <div class="post-meta clearfix">
         <p class="meta-info">
          Posted  by
          <a href="http://socialmediasun.com/author/contributor/" rel="author" title="Posts by Contributor">
           Contributor

        <div class="entry-content">
         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="12207281" data-link="http://socialmediasun.com/the-ultimate-guide-to-hashtags-hashtagprimer/" data-summary="" data-title="The Ultimate Guide to Hashtags #hashtagprimer">
         </div>
         <p>
          <img alt="hashtag-infographic" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3382" height="789" src="http://socialmediasun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/hashtag-infographic.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          The answer to What the hecks a hashtag? Infographic explains what a hashtag is, how to use it across multiple social networks, the etiquette for each social network, and examples of popular hashtags.
         </p>
         <p>
          Offers 64 tactics to using hashtags, organized in 8 categories, for a potential 4 million possible combinations of how to use hashtags to market your brand. An excellent primer for brands using Twitter, Facebook, Google , Instagram &amp; Tumblr.
         </p>
         <p>
          Includes examples from brands including the NFL, Gilette, Audi, McDonalds, Coca-Cola, Oprah, Lancome, Dove, and more.
         </p>
         <p>
          Excellent resource and great for printing, as is 8.5 x 11.
         </p>
         <p>
          It gives brands a roadmap for success when using hashtags, as many social media managers find hashtags &amp; Twitter intimidating. This guide helps brands avoid social media gaffes, increasing their brand reputation and visibility.
         </p>
         <p>
          Use the infographic as a checklist for how to use hashtags properly and great brands to watch for inspiration.
         </p>
