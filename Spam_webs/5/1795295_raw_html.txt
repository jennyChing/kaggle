         <h1 id="page-title">
          Family Travel Forum Teen Travel Writing Scholarship 2014
         </h1>
        </header>
        <!-- !Main Content -->
        <div class="region" id="content">
         <div class="block block-system no-title odd first last block-count-8 block-region-content block-main" id="block-system-main">
          <article about="/Teen_travel_writing" class="node node-page article odd node-full clearfix" id="node-67" role="article" typeof="foaf:Document">
           <div class="node-content">
            <span class="print-link">
            </span>
            <!-- google_ad_section_start -->
            <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden view-mode-full">
             <div class="field-items">
              <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
               <p>
                Do you have fun travel stories to share? We bet you do! Our 8th annual simple-to-apply for teen travel blogging scholarship grants cash prizes for great writing.
               </p>
               <p>
                THE 2014 FTF TEEN TRAVEL WRITING SCHOLARSHIP IS NOW CLOSED.
                <a href="/content/61983-family-travel-forum-congratulates-winners-2014-teen-travel-writing-scholarship" rel="nofollow" target="-blank">
                 READ THE WORK OF OUR SCHOLARSHIP WINNERS.
                </a>
               </p>
               <p>
                For 2014,
                <a href="http://www.familytravelforum.com" rel="nofollow">
                 Family Travel Forum
                </a>
                (FTF), the trusted vacation planning resource, and the
                <a href="http://www.satw.org" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                 Society of American Travel Writers
                </a>
                (SATW), a non-profit organization that promotes responsible journalism, are offering scholarships to encourage teens to share travel experiences in words and images.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                Have you ever taken an unexpectedly wonderful -- or terrible but funny -- trip with your family? Or have you journeyed somewhere with a school group or sports team and have some advice on how to make the most of your experience or destination? Have you gone somewhere -- near of far -- that opened up new ways of looking at the world?
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Family Travel Forum
                </strong>
                and the
                <strong>
                 Society of American Travel Writers
                </strong>
                invite you to submit travel blogs that are serious, funny or contemplative, as long as they are informative.
               </p>
               <p>
                The top winners will receive cash awards. Twenty-five finalists will win honorable mention awards and travel gifts such as passes to
                <strong>
                 Ripley's Believe It or Not!
                </strong>
                attractions. Ten lucky finalists will win a pair of
                <strong>
                 CityPASS
                </strong>
                ticket booklets containing prepaid admission to the top attractions in their choice of 10 North American destinations: New York City, Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Hollywood, Houston, Philadelphia, San Francisco, Seattle or Toronto.
               </p>
               <p>
                Watch our video on how to blog and then please read the Rules below carefully to ensure eligibility.Good Luck!
               </p>
               <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/536lS534o7k?rehttp://myfamilytravels.com/content/60536-i-love-new-york-teen-travel-writing-scholarship=0" width="560">
               </iframe>
               <p>
               </p>
               <h3>
                FTF Teen Travel Writing Scholarship
               </h3>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 First Place: $1,000
                </li>
                <li>

                </li>

                 <strong>
                 </strong>
                </li>
               </ul>
               <ul>
                <li>

                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
                All winners will receive a Family Travel Forum Certificate of Commendation, and selected submissions will be published on Family Travel Forum's
                <strong>
                 <a href="http://www.MyFamilyTravels.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  MyFamilyTravels.com
                 </a>
                </strong>
                website.
               </p>
               <p>
                FTF Teen Travel Writing Scholarship Rules
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Eligibility for FTF Teen Travel Writing Scholarship
                </strong>
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 All applicants must sign up for the
                 <strong>
                  <a href="http://www.MyFamilyTravels.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                   MyFamilyTravels.com
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 community prior to submitting an essay.
                </li>
                <li>
                 All applicants must complete a profile on
                 <strong>
                  <a href="http://www.myfamilytravels.com" rel="nofollow">
                   MyFamilyTravels.com
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 and include a photograph of themselves
                </li>
                <li>
                 Applications will be accepted between April 1, 2014 and August 1, 2014.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Applicantsmust be ages 13-18 and attending grades 8-12 as of June 27, 2014. Graduating seniors are
                 <em>
                  only
                 </em>
                 eligible if you submit your completed travel blog on or before June 27, 2014.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Applicants must be attending junior high or high school in the United States or Canada, or an American international school abroad, or be enrolled in a U.S. homeschool program.
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Submission Requirements for FTF Teen Travel Writing Scholarship
                </strong>
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 Travel blogs must be no more than 600 words in length.
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Entries must include text and be illustrated by at least one photo, digital artwork or video. (In addition to the personal photograph on your profile).
                 </strong>
                </li>
                <li>
                 Travel essays must include practical details so that others can follow in your footsteps. Details should include names of attractions, museums, themeparks, resorts, restaurants, the local beach, etc. with URLs as appropriate.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Travel blogs must be submitted online using the
                 <strong>
                  <a href="/node/add/scholarship" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                   FTF Teen Travel Scholarship Essay Submission Form
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 . Please create a strong, descriptive blog title, and use the editing tools provided to make paragraph breaks or add punctuation, and to upload your photos and or video.
                </li>
                <li>
                 All applications must be received no later than 11:59pm ET on August 1, 2014. Late entries will not be considered.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Your Travel blog must be your original creation and must not have been previously published.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Multiple submissions are not only.
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
                If you are notified that you are a Finalist, you will be contacted after September 5, 2014 and asked to send a copy of your transcript from your school, as well as a permission letter from your parents if you are under 18. More information about the Parent Permission Letter is available on the
                <strong>
                 <a href="http://myfamilytravels.com/node/51" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  FTF Teen Travel Writing Scholarship FAQ
                 </a>
                </strong>
                page. These documents must be received by email, fax or snail mail by September 26, 2014.
               </p>
               <h3>
               </h3>
               <h3>
                Judging the FTF Teen Travel Writing Scholarship
               </h3>
               <p>
                Travel blogs will be judged on originality, quality of expression, and a sense of place, as well as in accordance with standard rules of English grammar, mechanics and spelling. A panel of esteemed professional writers and editors from the
                <strong>
                 Society of American Travel Writers
                </strong>
                will make the final selection of winners.
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 All applicants will be contacted by email when the Judges have completed selecting the Quarter-Final round of essays and posted them online. Please update your online Account Info if your email address changes during the scholarship period.
                </li>
                <li>
                 Only Finalists will be contacted for follow up documents to verify their eligibility.
                </li>
               </ul>
