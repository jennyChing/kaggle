            <p>
             And its every bit as wondrous as you imagined.
            </p>
            <h6>
             You have to admit, graduation gowns are lame and kind of ridiculous anyway.
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="This Awesome College In Japan Allows Students To Wear Whatever They Want To Graduation. 0.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/This-Awesome-College-In-Japan-Allows-Students-To-Wear-Whatever-They-Want-To-Graduation.-0.jpg?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <h6>
             It makes more sense to go to graduation dressed as the instrument your parents forced you to practice for 30 minutes EVERY DAY because COLLEGE APPLICATIONS!!!
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="This Awesome College In Japan Allows Students To Wear Whatever They Want To Graduation. 1.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/This-Awesome-College-In-Japan-Allows-Students-To-Wear-Whatever-They-Want-To-Graduation.-1.jpg?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <h6>
             Or as a box robot. Because Box Robots are AWESOME.
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="This Awesome College In Japan Allows Students To Wear Whatever They Want To Graduation. 2.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/This-Awesome-College-In-Japan-Allows-Students-To-Wear-Whatever-They-Want-To-Graduation.-2.jpg?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <h6>
             Accepting your diploma dressed as your #1 go-to device for procrastination makes sense.
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="This Awesome College In Japan Allows Students To Wear Whatever They Want To Graduation. 3.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/This-Awesome-College-In-Japan-Allows-Students-To-Wear-Whatever-They-Want-To-Graduation.-3.jpg?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <h6>
             Vegan activist, or just someone who really loves packaged meat? You decide.
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="This Awesome College In Japan Allows Students To Wear Whatever They Want To Graduation. 4.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/This-Awesome-College-In-Japan-Allows-Students-To-Wear-Whatever-They-Want-To-Graduation.-4.jpg?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <h6>
             Your parents dont have to film graduation when you ARE the camera. (Just kidding, they will still be embarrassing you with their video camera. Theyre parents.)
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="This Awesome College In Japan Allows Students To Wear Whatever They Want To Graduation. 5.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/This-Awesome-College-In-Japan-Allows-Students-To-Wear-Whatever-They-Want-To-Graduation.-5.jpg?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <h6>
             With honors.
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="This Awesome College In Japan Allows Students To Wear Whatever They Want To Graduation. 6.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/This-Awesome-College-In-Japan-Allows-Students-To-Wear-Whatever-They-Want-To-Graduation.-6.jpg?w=950"/>
            </p>
            <h6>
             Robo-graduate!
            </h6>
            <p>
             <img alt="This Awesome College In Japan Allows Students To Wear Whatever They Want To Graduation. 7.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/This-Awesome-College-In-Japan-Allows-Students-To-Wear-Whatever-They-Want-To-Graduation.-7.jpg?w=950"/>

             Anime characters are obviously represented

             <img alt="This Awesome College In Japan Allows Students To Wear Whatever They Want To Graduation. 8.jpg" class="aligncenter size-full" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/lewl.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/This-Awesome-College-In-Japan-Allows-Students-To-Wear-Whatever-They-Want-To-Graduation.-8.jpg?w=950"/>
