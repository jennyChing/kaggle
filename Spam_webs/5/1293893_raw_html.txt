        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Seven Free Things to Do in London
        </h1>

        <p>
         Being Scottish, Im always on the lookout for free things to do when I visit London. Here are my travel tips for seven free things to do in London.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Watch a Free Show at Covent Garden
        </h3>
        <p>
         There were two guys pedaling unicycles with a young member of the audience throwing skittles for them to catch, when I was at Convent Garden. The best place to view the show is from the first floor terrace of the Punch and Judy pub. However only over 18s are allowed out onto the terrace.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/2013/03/25/free-things-to-do-london/covent-garden-free-entertainment/" rel="attachment wp-att-37164">
          <img alt="covent garden free entertainment" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-37164 frizzly-group-37128" height="375" src="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/covent-garden-free-entertainment.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Free entertainment at Londons Convent Garden
         </em>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Be Dazzled by Colour at Neals Yard
        </h3>
        <p>
         Id repinned photos of Neals Yard on to my
         <a href="https://pinterest.com/europealcarte/london/">
          London board on Pinterest
         </a>
         . I happened to notice Neal St as I was heading north from Convent Garden. I assumed that they Yard would be close by. Sure enough, Neals Yard was up an alleyway. It was late afternoon on a cold March weekday, with very few people around, so I had one of the circular benches in the courtyard to myself.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/2013/03/25/free-things-to-do-london/neals-yard/" rel="attachment wp-att-37169">
          <img alt="neals yard" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-37169 frizzly-group-37128" height="375" src="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/neals-yard.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Neals Yard
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-37128">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Admire the Works at the National Portrait Gallery
        </h3>
        <p>
         The National Portrait Gallery in Trafalgar Square was founded in 1856. The emphasis has always been on the person featured in the portrait, rather than the artistic merit of the piece.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/2013/03/25/free-things-to-do-london/national-portrait-gallery/" rel="attachment wp-att-37165">
          <img alt="national portrait gallery" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-37165 frizzly-group-37128" height="375" src="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/national-portrait-gallery.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          National Portrait Gallery in Trafalgar Square
         </em>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Tea Tasting at Twinings
        </h3>
        <p>
         You can get a free cuppa at the Twinings Tea Room at 216 The Strand, directly opposite the Royal Courts of Justice. The closest Tube station in Temple. Theres history of the Twinings Tea, from the time Thomas Twining bought the Tea Room in 1706 to the present day.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/2013/03/25/free-things-to-do-london/twinings-tea-tasting/" rel="attachment wp-att-37166">
          <img alt="twinings tea tasting" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-37166 frizzly-group-37128" height="375" src="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/twinings-tea-tasting.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Tea tasting tables at Twinings Tea Room in the Strand
         </em>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Learn About Money at the Bank of England Museum
        </h3>
        <p>
         I spent three nights at Travelodge London Bank, which is a three miunute walk from the Bank of England Museum. As Im also a personal finance blogger, the history of the Bank of England and the national currency was very interesting to me. Unfortunately, youre not allowed to take photos in the museum.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/2013/03/25/free-things-to-do-london/bank-of-england-museum/" rel="attachment wp-att-37167">
          <img alt="bank of england museum" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-37167 frizzly-group-37128" height="351" src="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/bank-of-england-museum.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Bank of England Museum
         </em>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Do Some Dragon Spotting in the City
        </h3>
        <p>
         The
         <a href="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/2013/03/14/dragon-boundary-mark-city-london/">
          Dragon boundary marks
         </a>
         sculptures guard the entrance to the City of London. The one in the photo below is on Victoria Embankment on the Thames Path.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/europealacarte/8564774636/" title="Dragon boundary mark on Victoria Embankment London by Karen V Bryan, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Dragon boundary mark on Victoria Embankment London" class="frizzly-group-37128" height="500" src="http://farm9.staticflickr.com/8518/8564774636_24e0d38ded.jpg" width="375"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Dragon boundary mark in Victoria Embankment
         </em>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Learn about Medicine &amp; the Human Body at the Wellcome Collection
        </h3>
        <p>
         Ive been meaning to visit the Wellcome Collection, near Euston Station, since I read about it the article on
         <a href="http://www.europealacarte.co.uk/blog/2009/06/25/guest-post-quirky-collections-london-free-entry/">
          Londons Quirky Collections with Free Entry
         </a>
         . I decided to visit on a Thursday evening, as the venue is open until 22.00, so I could do other things in the daylight.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/europealacarte/8558504001/" title="Female body model at Wellcome Collection London by Karen V Bryan, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Female body model at Wellcome Collection London" class="frizzly-group-37128" height="500" src="http://farm9.staticflickr.com/8244/8558504001_98621cd2a2.jpg" width="375"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Transparent model of female body
         </em>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Accommodation in London
        </h3>
        <p>
         Theres no denying that it can be expensive to find decent accommodation in London.Although its tempting to save money by going for a cheap hotel, I wouldnt advise this tactic. I had an
         <a href="http://www.helpmetosave.com/2012/11/saving-money-good-idea/">
          unpleasant stay at the County Hotel
         </a>
         in Woburn Place, where a single room with a shared bathroom cost 50 a night. I think its better to spend more money to get a reasonable standard of accommodation.
        </p>
