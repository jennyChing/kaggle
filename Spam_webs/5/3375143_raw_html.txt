    <h1>
     Switzerland
    </h1>
    <p>
     This small country in the Alps is famous for watches, chocolate and the Swiss Army Knife. It is a beautiful, but expensive country that is a popular spot for backpackers to persue adventure activities and extreme sports such as rafting , bungy jumping, skiing or anything else to do with the great outdoors. Switzerland also offers breathtaking alpine scenery dotted with cute little villages and criss-crossed by hiking trails.
    </p>
