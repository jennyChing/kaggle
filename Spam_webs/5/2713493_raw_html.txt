          New Years Resolutions
         </a>
        </h1>

        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mummysblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/to-do.jpg">
          <br/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.mummysblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/todo.jpg">
          <img alt="todo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8911" height="760" src="http://www.mummysblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/todo.jpg" width="2000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         A new year for me, means making new years resolutions. I make them every year without fail, sometimes I dont last a week, sometimes I do actually stick to them! This year I feel quite optimistic and motivated. I have a list of things I want to achieve this year which is as long as my arm, but I thought I would share some of the main ones here
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Lose Weight
         </strong>
         Probably quite a common resolution for many people, but this is the first time post-baby, that I have really felt 100% serious about doing it. My goal is to lose a stone by the end of March, which I feel should be quite achievable. Once I hit that target, I would probably like to lose maybe another half stone, then re-assess the situation at that point to see if I am happy at that weight or if I want to keep going, as Im not totally sure what my happy weight is!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Be More Organised
         </strong>
         I feel my mind has been quite chaotic recently and Im also a champion procrastinator, so I want 2015 to be the year of getting things done and being more organised is the first step for me!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Meal Plan
         </strong>
         I have been meal planning on and off, but this year I really want to do it and stick to it. We spend far too much money on food, so only buying what we actually need should help our bank balance and hopefully my waistline, if it means Im not buying lots of naughty treats!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Decorate the living room and our bedroom
         </strong>
         We havent decorated either of these rooms since we moved in  every time weve got close something has got in the way. Ive lived with it for years and it didnt actually bother me until I had the baby  I think when you stay at home and have to look at it all day, it gets to you! Both of these rooms are big jobs, the living room especially because it needs plastered, but if I have both of these rooms done by the end of 2015 Ill be happy.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Read more books
         </strong>
         I dont really get the chance to read much now, but I have so many unread books, its crazy. I ideally want to get through all my paperbacks this year, then only buy books for Kindle from then on, so they dont take up so much space!
        </p>
        <p>
         Those are my main resolutions this year. I think it will be interesting to come back at the end of the year and see how many I have stuck to!
        </p>
