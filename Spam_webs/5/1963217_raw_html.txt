        Welcome 
            to the Colorado Dances of Universal Peace Web site! On this site we 
            offer information and opportunities to experience the Dances of Universal 
            Peace and related events on the Front Range.
        <p>
         Colorado is home to the largest weekly gatherings of this nature 
              in the country, and the dances have served as a hub of connection 
              to people who value tolerance, diversity, peace, the sacredness 
              of Life, and enjoy celebrating this Life with others.
        </p>
        <p>
         You will find a community events calendar, descriptions of the 
              dances and a little bit of history on where this all came from. 
              Also, we've written a little on what you can expect at a dance event, 
              and some answers to commonly asked questions. You'll find contact 
              information as well as location addresses and maps. We sincerely 
              hope you will move more
         <img align="right" height="160" src="images/maypole1.jpg" width="210"/>
         than 
              your mouse and join us at a dance circle very soon.
        </p>
        <p>
         A picture is worth a thousand words, but to move and sing - that's 
              how you know about dance.
        </p>
