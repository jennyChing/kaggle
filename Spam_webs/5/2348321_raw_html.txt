        <h1 class="entry-title">
         16 Awesome Headboard Ideas You Can Do By Yourself
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <div align="center">
         <script type="text/javascript">

        <p>
         If you have some free time on your hands, why dont you try doing something creative? For example, have you ever thought of making your own headboard? Dont worry, that is really easy to do and there are plenty of themes you can use! Here you will see the best ideas we have found! What is your favorite?
        </p>
        <h2>
         1. Shelves Headboard
         <br/>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Shelves-Headboard.jpg">
          <img alt="Shelves Headboard" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5475" height="594" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Shelves-Headboard.jpg" width="549"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         This one maybe isnt so easy to make, but its possible. You just need some free time and enough motivation. After all, you just need a couple of shelves and a modern looking bed. It would be great if you paint a bed with a bright color to make the right contrast. Modern and sleek!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.hometalk.com/1052623/modern-bed" target="_blank" title="Shelves Headboard">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Source
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         2. Curtain Headboard
         <br/>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Curtain-Headboard.jpg">
          <img alt="Curtain Headboard" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5470" height="533" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Curtain-Headboard.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         You can use a simple curtain panel as a headboard. Boring walls behind your head will never look the same. Simply install a curtain rod on the wall above your bed and hang a curtain with an awesome design.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/439663982343422363/" target="_blank" title="Curtain Headboard">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Source
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         3. Wall of Frames Headboard
         <br/>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Wall-of-Frames-Headboard.jpg">
          <img alt="Wall of Frames Headboard" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5472" height="733" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Wall-of-Frames-Headboard.jpg" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         This is simple and cheap idea! You can use picture frames as a headboard for your bed. Choose a simple ones and put some nice artsy photographs inside  it will look modern and gorgeous!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/101401429084216598/" target="_blank" title="Wall of Frames Headboard">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Source
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         4. Shower Curtain Artwork
         <br/>
         <a href="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Shower-Curtain-Artwork.jpg">
          <img alt="Shower Curtain Artwork" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5468" height="534" src="http://apartmentgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Shower-Curtain-Artwork.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Artwork doesnt have to be expensive. You can even make an art piece with a simple shower curtain. Use the one with nice design and frame it. It looks awesome as a headboard!
        </p>
        <p>
