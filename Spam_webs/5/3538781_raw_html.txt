         <h2 class="title">
          <a href="http://poleates.tumblr.com/post/103792061444/pole-dance-academies-helping-in-reinventing">
           Pole Dance Academies Helping in Reinventing Identity
          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="body-text">
          <p>
           How about an effective workout regimen to burn the excess calories that is wrapped in sheer fun and thrill and is guaranteed to make you satisfied and joyous right in your mind? It surely sounds amazing! However, as it should be clarified before, this entire package is not going to be available in any traditional gym. Popularity of
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.truelocal.com.au/business/poleates/blacktown" target="_blank">
             pole dance academy
            </a>
           </strong>
           is spreading like wildfire across contemporary Australia. As the dance is ideal for all age groups and equally beneficial for all the genders thus, one gets to see a thick stream of ever-increasing crowd in front of pole studios these days.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Pole Dancing Classes Parramatta" src="http://38.media.tumblr.com/d79e6b27947ae038936942a37f6ce632/tumblr_inline_nfqp7qrViq1sjqcs4.jpg" title="Pole Dancing Classes Parramatta"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           This craze stands in stark contrast to the severe taboo and the stigma that were invariably associated to the dance in the bygone eras. Dancing along the pole actually originated in seedy strip clubs. However, considering the overwhelming benefits that it provides, the dance has been widely accepted in the mainstream society. As a matter of fact, the dance is more suitable for those who are daring and athletic by nature. In contrast to monotonous exercise regimens practised in regular health clubs, pole dance offers unique thrill that is hard to ignore.
          </p>
          <p>
           Latest breakthroughs in the world of medical sciences have confirmed acute physical exhaustion resulting from dancing along the pole helps improving Serotonin and Endorphin hormones in our body. These naturally secreted hormones are responsible for triggering euphoric feeling and thus, individuals who attend
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.poleates.com.au/pole-dancing/classes/" target="_blank">
             pole dancing classes in Parramatta
            </a>
           </strong>
           on regular basis are the happier and blissful lot!
          </p>
          <p>
           The dance is even helpful in increasing both physical tenacity and mental perseverance of individuals. It proves effective in boosting ones self esteem and redefines ones overall personality. Severe cardiovascular exercise, in form of pole dancing, helps in keeping an array of chronic medical ailments at bay. These include high blood sugar, high blood pressure and sleeplessness or insomnia, etc.
          </p>
