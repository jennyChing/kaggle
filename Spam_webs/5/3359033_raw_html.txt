             <section class="article-content">
              <p>
               On Friday morning, many in Europe will be fortunate enough to view a
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/total-solar-eclipse/">
                total solar eclipse
               </a>
               .
              </p>
              <div class="see-also">
               <p>
                See also:
                <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2015/03/16/total-solar-eclipse-2015/">
                 When and how to watch Friday's total solar eclipse
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <p>
               So we put together a
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/music-monday/">
                Music Monday
               </a>
               playlist full of interstellar songs to celebrate the occasion. You may belt "Total Eclipse of the Heart" while watching the eclipse at your own risk.
              </p>
              <p>
               <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" height="380" src="https://embed.spotify.com/?uri=spotify%3Auser%3Amashablehq%3Aplaylist%3A759czCI2UcPWrIm1x94PSK" width="300">
               </iframe>
              </p>
              <p>
               Have something to add to our playlist? Share with us in the comments, below, or on
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/spotify/">
                Spotify
               </a>
               , and we'll add it in.
              </p>
              <h3>
               How to share your song with us
              </h3>
              <p>
               1. To follow
               <em>
                Mashable
               </em>
               on Spotify, click
               <a href="http://open.spotify.com/user/mashablehq" target="_blank">
                here
               </a>
               . If a popup window asks you to launch an external application, accept. This just means the browser will open up Spotify.
              </p>
              <p>
               2. Follow
               <em>
                Mashable
               </em>
               , or send us music by clicking "Send Music."
               <br/>
               <img alt="" data-width="352" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA0LzE1LzA5L1NjcmVlblNob3QyLmUyMTUwLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/e03563cc/7e3/Screen-Shot-2013-04-15-at-2.58.53-PM.jpg" title="Screen Shot 2013-04-15 at 2.58.53 PM"/>
              </p>
              <p>
               3. To send music while browsing tracks within Spotify,
               <a href="http://open.spotify.com/user/mashablehq" target="_blank">
                follow the MashableHQ account
               </a>
               . Then right-click (ctrl + click) on the song you want to send, and click the "Share" option.
               <br/>
               <img alt="" data-fragment="m!7693" data-image="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA0LzE1LzA5L1NjcmVlblNob3QyLmU2YzlhLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/29182612/bd2/Screen-Shot-2013-04-15-at-1.57.37-PM-640x484.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="640" src="http://rack.0.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA0LzE1LzA5L1NjcmVlblNob3QyLmU2YzlhLmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/29182612/bd2/Screen-Shot-2013-04-15-at-1.57.37-PM-640x484.jpg" title="Screen Shot 2013-04-15 at 1.57.37 PM"/>
              </p>
              <p>
               4. By default, Spotify pulls up the "Post to Feed" option. Instead, select the "Send to Friend" tab, type in "MashableHQ" and hit the blue "Send" button.
               <br/>
               <img alt="" data-fragment="m!34c1" data-image="http://rack.3.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA0LzE1LzA5L1NjcmVlblNob3QyLjViZDQ2LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/6f45c0af/0a6/Screen-Shot-2013-04-15-at-1.59.50-PM.jpg" data-micro="1" data-width="637" src="http://rack.3.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEzLzA0LzE1LzA5L1NjcmVlblNob3QyLjViZDQ2LmpwZwpwCXRodW1iCTEyMDB4OTYwMD4/6f45c0af/0a6/Screen-Shot-2013-04-15-at-1.59.50-PM.jpg" title="Screen Shot 2013-04-15 at 1.59.50 PM"/>
              </p>
              <p>
               If "MashableHQ" doesn't show up when you're trying to send a track, make sure you're following the account. If you're still having problems sending a song after that, let us know in the comments, below, and we'll help you fix the problem.
              </p>
