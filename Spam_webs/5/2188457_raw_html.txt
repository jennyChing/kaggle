        <p>
         This Black Friday
         <strong>
          MAC
         </strong>
         is offering up an exclusive online-only edition of
         <strong>
          Veluxe Pearlfusion Shadow
         </strong>
         in
         <strong>
          Smoky Black Friday
         </strong>
         . This trio of shades are all you need for the perfect, smokiest eye ball in all of the shopping land. Just add mascara.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.blushingnoir.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/MAC-Smoky-Black-Veluxe-Pearlfusion-Shadow.jpg">
          <img alt="MAC Smoky Black Veluxe Pearlfusion Shadow" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-34281" height="501" src="http://www.blushingnoir.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/MAC-Smoky-Black-Veluxe-Pearlfusion-Shadow.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.blushingnoir.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/MAC-Smoky-Black-Friday.jpg">
          <img alt="MAC Smoky Black Friday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-34282" height="522" src="http://www.blushingnoir.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/MAC-Smoky-Black-Friday.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Veluxe Pearlfusion Shadows can be applied dry (for a lightweight, not so intense look) or wet (which gives them a more saturated and metallic look). I find them soft and easy to use either way, but for a really smoky look applying them with a damp brush is the way to go. Smoky Black Friday has an icy white, a metallic silver that almost boasts a hint of lilac and a black shade which also appears to swatch with a bit of purple, though that doesnt translate so much to the eye. Below the shades are swatched dry on the right and wet on the left.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.blushingnoir.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/MAC-Smoky-Black-Friday-Eye-Shadow-Swatches.jpg">
          <img alt="MAC Smoky Black Friday Eye Shadow Swatches" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-34283" height="522" src="http://www.blushingnoir.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/MAC-Smoky-Black-Friday-Eye-Shadow-Swatches.jpg" width="584"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         In the eye look below Im wearing UDPP as a base with the black shade on the lid and into the crease, the silver in the crease blended up and the white to highlight. All shades were used slightly dampened. I took a bit more of the black shade to wing out the look at the outer corner and then smudged a bit of the black and silver mixed under the lower eye lid. I only have liner on the bottom waterline which is
         <a href="http://www.blushingnoir.com/2013/07/urban-decay-foxy-four-way-double-ended-247-glide-on-eye-pencil-set-swatches-review/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Urban Decay 24/7 Glide-On Eye Pencil in Perversion
         </a>
         . The mascarais
         <a href="http://www.blushingnoir.com/2014/03/milani-total-lash-cover-mascara-swatches-review/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Milani Total Lash Cover
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://www.blushingnoir.com/2014/08/tarte-rainforest-dark-fall-2014-swatches-review/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Tarte Lights Camera Lashes
         </a>
         layered and the Tarte alone on the bottom.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.blushingnoir.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/MAC-Smoky-Black-Friday-EOTD.jpg">
           <img alt="MAC Smoky Black Friday EOTD" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-34284" height="522" src="http://www.blushingnoir.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/MAC-Smoky-Black-Friday-EOTD.jpg" width="584"/>
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          MAC Smoky Black Friday
         </strong>
         retails for $25.00 and is available exclusively on
         <a href="http://www.maccosmetics.com/index.tmpl" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          maccosmetics.com
         </a>
         on Black Friday (aka November 28, 2014).
        </p>
