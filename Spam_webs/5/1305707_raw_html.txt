               <h2 class="blog-title">
                <a class="blog-title-link blog-link" href="http://www.simplysoccerpro.com/blog/how-to-finish-in-soccer-tips-on-how-to-score-in-soccer">
                 How To Finish In Soccer - Tips On How To Score In Soccer
                </a>
               </h2>
               <p class="blog-date">
                <span class="date-text">
                 2/9/2015
                </span>
               </p>
               <p class="blog-comments">
                <a class="blog-link" href="http://www.simplysoccerpro.com/blog/how-to-finish-in-soccer-tips-on-how-to-score-in-soccer#comments">
                 0 Comments
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <div class="blog-separator">
              </div>
              <div class="blog-content">
               <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
                <font color="#2a2a2a">
                 <strong>
                  Let's face it, the main objective in soccer or football is to score more goals than your opponent. That is essentially how you win matches and titles.
                 </strong>
                </font>
                <br/>
                <br/>
                <br/>
                <font color="#2a2a2a">
                 <strong>
                  This is why goalscorers are always the main footballers that are appearing in the headlines. If you score 20 or more league goals a season, you are suddenly your teams super star.
                 </strong>
                </font>
                <br/>
                <br/>
                <br/>
                <font color="#2a2a2a">
                 <strong>
                  Professional teams will pay good money for a player that will bag them plenty of goals.
                 </strong>
                </font>
                <br/>
               </div>
               <div>
                <!--BLOG_SUMMARY_END-->
               </div>
               <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
                <font color="#2a2a2a">
                 <strong style="">
                  Players today like Ronaldo, Suarez, Bale, Costa, and Neymar to name a few, command such huge price tagsbecausein the end they score goals, or create goals.
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <strong style="">
                  I want to draw your attention to a player who has retired recently, but in his prime, was the best goal scorer in the world.
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <strong style="">
                  What made him such a great goalscorer was his incredible ability to finish. He always seemed to know how to finish in every situation.
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <strong style="">
                  I am of course talking about the Arsenal legend, Thierry Henry.
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <strong style="">
                  Look no further than King Henry if you want to improve your finishing. Henry had a knack of getting himself into the right positions or creating chances himself in order to score.
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <strong style="">
                  And when he had a chance to score, you better believe that he would.
                 </strong>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                </font>
                <strong style="">
                 <font color="#2a2a2a">
                  Here is a great video of the man himself giving some tips on how to finish at speed.
                 </font>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div class="wsite-youtube" style="margin-bottom:10px;margin-top:10px;">
                <div class="wsite-youtube-wrapper wsite-youtube-size-auto wsite-youtube-align-center">
                 <div class="wsite-youtube-container">
                  <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/d3CpiT6p8UE?wmode=opaque">
                  </iframe>
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
               <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
                <strong>
                 <font color="#2a2a2a">
                  Great advice, no? Be sure to also look at some footage of his playing days at Arsenal in order to get an idea of how good a finisher he really was. Here's one of my favorites to get you started.
                 </font>
                </strong>
               </div>
               <div class="wsite-youtube" style="margin-bottom:10px;margin-top:10px;">
                <div class="wsite-youtube-wrapper wsite-youtube-size-auto wsite-youtube-align-center">
                 <div class="wsite-youtube-container">
                  <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/TTbAU6Km3CQ?wmode=opaque">

                 <strong>
                  Pure class that is. Also remember everyone that to become a great finisher it will take much practice.
                 </strong>
                </font>
