         <p>
          Remember when Blizzard was working on
          <em>
           StarCraft Ghost
          </em>
          , a third person shooter that would give the
          <em>
           StarCraft
          </em>
          universe an entirely new perspective? It didnt quite pan out, with Blizzard canceling the project after years of confused messages to the public. However, thanks to the modding community, players will get to experience the
          <em>
           StarCraft
          </em>
          universe from a third person perspective after all.
         </p>
         <p>
          This comes in the form of a custom map for
          <em>
           StarCraft II
          </em>
          , titled
          <em>
           Psionic Warfare: Total Destruction
          </em>
          . The map is available for
          <a href="http://www.sc2mapster.com/forums/resources/project-workplace/76270-psionic-warfare-total-destruction-trailer-included/#p1">
           free download
          </a>
          from the Blizzard
          <a href="http://us.battle.net/arcade/en/">
           Arcade
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          In this map, up to four players take on the roles of a squad of Dominion marines who have psionic abilities thanks to a XelNaga artifact. They must defend themselves against waves and waves of Zerg in a cooperative Last Stand format.
         </p>
         <p>
          You wouldnt know that
          <em>
           Psionic Warfare
          </em>
          was a mod just by looking at it, as it has enough features and graphical fidelity to qualify as a standalone release. It features upgraded graphics, functional third person shooter mechanics, five different classes, four game modes, four maps, and full voice acting.
         </p>
         <p>
          Because of its enhanced visuals, youll need a beefier system to run it smoothly. If thats beyond your ability right now, then the below gameplay video will have to suffice.
