        <h1 class="entry-title">
         10 Worst Ways to Open a Conversation
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="date">
          <a href="http://specialkindofstupid.com/2008/06/09/10-worst-ways-to-open-a-conversation/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to 10 Worst Ways to Open a Conversation">
           <time class="entry-date" datetime="2008-06-09T09:34:57+00:00">
            June 9, 2008
           </time>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="categories-links">
          <a href="http://specialkindofstupid.com/category/users/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Guest Rants">
           Guest Rants
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="author vcard">
          <a class="url fn n" href="http://specialkindofstupid.com/author/kathyf/" rel="author" title="View all posts by KathyF">
           KathyF
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>

       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <a id="dd_start">
        </a>
        <p>
         <em>
          The following is a guest post from one of my favorite bloggers, the witty KathyF. Check her out at the insanely popular
          <a class="external" href="http://www.junkdrawerblog.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           The Junk Drawer
          </a>
          .
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignleft" height="225" src="http://www.emcoscotland.co.uk/images/bubble.jpg" style="float: left; border: 0; margin: 0px;" width="225"/>
         <span>
          Years ago, I worked with a socially inept fellow who used half  these conversation starters on me. Usually, he was met with stunned  silence.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span>
          He no longer works in our office, but oh, how we  miss the fun of watching him get all twisted up in knots every time he opened  his mouth. He was a one-man sideshow freak who gave us years of  entertainment.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span>
          Here are some less than engaging ways to start a  conversation. Use them at your own peril.
         </span>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          <span>
           1.  I know you didnt ask for my opinion, but
           <strong>
            <em>
             Yeah, so shut it.
            </em>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
           2.  Im not a racist, but
           <strong>
            <em>
             You sure about  that?
            </em>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
           3.  Long story short
           <strong>
            <em>
             Long story to  follow.
            </em>
            (OK. I admit it. Im guilty of this one.)
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
           4.  Promise you wont laugh?
           <strong>
            <em>
             No can  do.
            </em>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
           5.  Can I tell you something and you wont get  mad?
           <strong>
            <em>
             I dare you.
            </em>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
           6.  I overheard your conversation. Can I just say  
           <strong>
            <em>
             No. It wasnt a threesome.
            </em>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
           7.  Dont take this the wrong way
           <strong>
            <em>
             Already  there
            </em>
            .
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
           8.  With all due respect
           <strong>
            <em>
             Doubtful.
            </em>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
           9.  I was trying to wait for the right time to  tell you this.
           <strong>
            <em>
             Wait longer.
            </em>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span>
           10. Can I have the last donut?
           <strong>
            <em>
             Not if you  still need those fingers.
            </em>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
