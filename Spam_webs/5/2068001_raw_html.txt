            Weekly Favourites: Urban Walks and Cult Beauty Goody Bag
           </h3>
           <hr/>

          <div class="content-post">
           <input data-frizzlyhovercontainer="" data-frizzlypostcontainer="" data-frizzlyposttitle="Weekly Favourites: Urban Walks and Cult Beauty Goody Bag" data-frizzlyposturl="http://pawsandprada.uk/weekly-favourites-graffiti-cult-beauty-goody-bag/" type="hidden" value=""/>
           <p>
            This week Bruce and I have spent a lot at home, chilling out, working hard and being very unsociable indeed. Well, Im nothing if not honest.
           </p>
           <h3>
            Bruces Favourite  Urban Walks
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="Dog in the city" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-684" data-recalc-dims="1" src="//i2.wp.com/pawsandprada.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Bruce-graffiti.jpg?resize=960%2C1326"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Seeing as we havent been doing anything particularly exciting this week, Ive been taking Brucington Bear exploring around the neighbourhood on his walks. He still gets a daily visit to the park to terrorise, sorry I mean
            <em>
             play with
            </em>
            his canine companions, but on his other walk weve been going on little adventures. I like to give Bruce these walks around the streets of London because it stops him developing fears of loud or unusual noises like sirens, alarms, traffic and motorbikes, but he loves it because he gets to take in new sights, sounds and smells. A sniff around an unknown London street will stimulate and entertain him more than a romp around the park, any day of the week.
           </p>
