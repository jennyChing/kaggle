        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Rudolphs Snack Mix
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.sunshineandhurricanes.com/author/mama24cubs/" itemprop="url" rel="author">

            Michelle Myers
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input data-frizzlyhovercontainer="" data-frizzlypostcontainer="" data-frizzlyposttitle="Rudolphs Snack Mix" data-frizzlyposturl="http://www.sunshineandhurricanes.com/rudolphs-snack-mix/" type="hidden" value=""/>
        <p>
         <img alt="Rudolph's Snack Mix" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-18136" height="1024" src="http://www.sunshineandhurricanes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/rudolphsnack1-971x1024.jpg" width="971"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         A couple of my kiddos came up with a simple snack idea and wanted me to write a post about their invention. This is so simple but adorable so here we go!
        </p>
        <p>
         1 box of Cinnamon Sugar Pita Chips
        </p>
        <p>
         Pretzels
        </p>
        <p>
         Chocolate Chips
        </p>
        <p>
         Red M&amp;Ms
        </p>
        <p>
         I bought a bag of holiday M&amp;Ms and they were happily sorted by my little elves. We dumped everything into a bowl, gently stirred it up and voila, a snack is born! The thing my kiddos were most excited about was the fact they could play with their food! Following the photo above, your little elves can create an entire team of reindeer for Santas sleigh.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Rudolph's Snack Mix" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-18138" height="923" src="http://www.sunshineandhurricanes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/rudolphsnack2-1024x923.jpg" width="1024"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This mix looks cute in a pretty bowl and the adults thought it was pretty yummy as well; cant go wrong with sweet &amp; salty! After munching while watching a Christmas movie, I bagged up the leftovers for lunch boxes or treats on the go. This simple mix would be easy for class parties, maybe a jar full for a neighbor, or great to have on hand when friends drop by.
        </p>
