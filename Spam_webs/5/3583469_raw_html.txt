       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Design  Logo for Gubbi Labs
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Gubbi in Kannada language means House Sparrow. Incidentally it is also the name of a place in Karnataka, India. One of my friends contacted me for designing a logo for 
         <a href="http://www.gubbilabs.in" target="_blank">
          Gubbi Labs
         </a>
           a private research enterprise that works on a host of domains ranging from sustainable ecosystems to livable cities.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Design Brief
          <br/>
         </strong>
         The design brief was simple: The logo should look like a house sparrow.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-976" height="100" src="http://fullfx.antzfx.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/gubbi_antzFx0.jpg" title="gubbi_antzFx0" width="400"/>
         <strong>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span id="more-982">
          </span>
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Ideation
         </strong>
         <br/>
         I gave three options for the logo.
         <br/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-977" height="108" src="http://fullfx.antzfx.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/gubbi_antzFx1.jpg" title="gubbi_antzFx1" width="300"/>
         The logo given above is an artistic monogram that directly shows an association with knowledge. The logotype is cursive (running writing/joint writing) and looks as if written by hand. This logo is a direct communication of the themes rare knowledge and human touch.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-978" height="126" src="http://fullfx.antzfx.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/gubbi_antzFx2.jpg" title="gubbi_antzFx2" width="200"/>
         A silhouette of sparrow is used as the logo. The name Gubbi appears on the body. The dot of the alphabet i is placed such that it also represents the eye of the bird. This logo clearly says that Gubbi = Common house sparrow.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-979" height="143" src="http://fullfx.antzfx.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/gubbi_antzFx3.jpg" title="gubbi_antzFx3" width="200"/>
         The sparrow is made of three overlapping pieces of brown cellophane sheets. An egg shape can be seen on its body formed by the overlap. This egg represents an idea that is going to hatch. The font for Gubbi Labs is free flowing without any sharp corners. It appears that the bird is perched on the alphabets bb of Gubbi. This logo directly communicates the central themes of genesis of an idea and futuristic solutions.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Refined Concept
          <br/>
         </strong>
         We can clearly identify a pelican from its silhouette. But a sparrow does not have a unique shape that clearly tells that it is a sparrow. A silhouette of a sparrow just communicates that it is a bird.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-981" height="185" src="http://fullfx.antzfx.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/gubbi_antzFx5.jpg" title="gubbi_antzFx5" width="200"/>
         <em>
          Photo courtesy: Sudhira
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         So the client asked me to put a one to one mapping of the colors of a sparrow to the logo.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Final Logo
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.gubbilabs.in" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-980" height="143" src="http://fullfx.antzfx.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/gubbi_antzFx4.jpg" title="gubbi_antzFx4" width="200"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         The logo represents a male sparrow perched on the bbs of Gubbi. Bauhausm Medium is the font used in the design.
         <br/>
         I really liked the outcome. Any comments? The space below is all yours.
        </p>
        <p>
         Software: Photoshop, Illustrator
         <br/>
         Design Date: 01-Feb-2010
        </p>
       </div>
