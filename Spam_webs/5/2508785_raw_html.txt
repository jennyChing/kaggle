         <h1 class="entry-title">
          How to Make An Orange Peel Face Mask
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <span class="entry-date">
           <a href="http://wandercharm.com/beauty/orange-peel-face-mask/" rel="bookmark">
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-06-18T07:25:48+00:00">
             June 18, 2014
            </time>
           </a>
          </span>
          by
          <span class="byline">
           <span class="author vcard">
            <a class="url fn n" href="http://wandercharm.com/author/rosanie-tunacao/" rel="author">
             rosanie.tunacao
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="sep">
           
          </span>
          <span class="comments-link">
           <a href="http://wandercharm.com/beauty/orange-peel-face-mask/#comments" title="Comment on How to Make An Orange Peel Face Mask">
            2 Comments
           </a>
          </span>
         </div>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Okay,so maybe a face mask made of orange peel sounds a bit queer but do you know that the peels actually come with more nutrient than the fruit itself?
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Abundant in Vitamin C and other antioxidants, the peels are proven to be effective against warding off free radicals and especially helpful in maintaining skins health and young look. Peels are also high in citric acid content which works on exfoliating and brightening skin.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Powder from orange peels often employed in skin remedies involving control of acne, cleaning and opening of pores, reduction of oiliness, treatment of blackheads and increase of skin radiance. Combine this with yogurt and honey to complete this DIY face mask and youll get even more added benefits.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Yogurt is rich in lactic acid, a mild AHA that aids in skin exfoliation through dead skin removal, stimulates lightening of skin, helps to fade dark spots and other blemishes, promotes production of collagen and stops premature aging.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Moreover, yogurt hydrated and moisturizes skin, giving it much nourishment. Its zinc content is a necessary component that stimulates regeneration of skin and likewise functions as an agent to counter inflammation.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Honey helps maintain skins moisture and at the same time, functions as a natural humectant. As mother natures cleanser, it helps to keep skin clear and glowing. Abundant in antioxidants and antibacterial properties, it wards off acne and delays skins aging process.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The ingredients to prepare for this recipe are 3 oranges, honey, yogurt, a bowl, a blender, a mixing spoon and some measuring spoons.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Heres how to prepare it:
         </p>
         <ul style="text-align: justify;">
          <li>
           Takeoutthepeelsfromall3oranges.
          </li>
          <li>
           Tearthepeelintolittlepieces.Washthereafterandspreadthemouttodry.
          </li>
          <li>
           Letthemdryunderthesunfor3daysstraight.
          </li>
          <li>
           Gatherthenowdriedpeelsandplaceitablender.
          </li>
          <li>
           Grindthepeelsathighspeedinordersoastopowderizethemwell.
          </li>
          <li>
           Combinetwoteaspoonsoftheorangepeelpowderandyogurteachwithoneteaspoonofhoney.
          </li>
          <li>
           Storewhatsleftoftheorangepeelpowderinacleananddryglassjar.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Now, youre all done and set to use your newly-made homemade recipe. To do so, cleanse your face. Then, spread the orange peel mask on both your face and neck, massaging then gently in circular movements. Allow to let it stay on your skin for a duration of fifteen to twenty minutes then rinse off with water thats lukewarm. After which, wash your face with cold water to close pores. Apply this Recipe twice to thrice every week.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          A wise tip to follow would be to steam your face up to open its pores prior to applying the face mask. And if you want to have a natural cleanser made of orange peels, mix it with a little water until it forms into a paste. Spread evenly on skin and let stay there until completely dry. Then wash off with water thats lukewarm.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Another way to keep your skin young and glowing is through the use of Prodermagenix Anti-Aging Serum. With the combining power of skin-firming peptides, moisturizing agents and vitamins that penetrate skin at a cellular level, an action that mimics a surgical face life happens.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          This, in turn, works on reducing fine lines, wrinkles and age spots on both your face and neck; thereby revealing a more supple, smoother skin that takes ten years off your age. Read
          <b>
           Prodermagenix reviews
          </b>
          to know what this breakthrough product us all about and the wonders it can do in restoring your skins former glory.
         </p>
