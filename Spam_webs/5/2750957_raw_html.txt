      <h3>
       Niko  Hendrickx (1974) is a visual artist who lives and works in Lommel (Belgium). His  art focuses on change in the broadest sense, hence the delta symbol in his  logo. He uses traditional media such as oil, drawing and sculpture, as well as  (and often combined with) 3D animation and video.
