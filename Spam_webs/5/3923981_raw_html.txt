          <p>

            <img alt="Tangle Teezer" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3776 pin-it" height="584" src="http://www.boemagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Tangle-Teezer.jpg" width="957"/>

          <p>
           Thanks to Shaun P who created the Tangle Teezer, detangling your hair has never been easier. The original professional detangling hairbrush that has been referred to as the miracle brush delivers impressive pain free results on both wet and dry hair.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Tangle Teaser is designed with revolutionary teeth to help minimise hair breakage as well as produce speedy and gentle results. This is ergonomically designed for a palm friendly, non-slip fit.
          </p>
          <p>
           The colour, shape and size of the brush appeals not only to all ages but to men and women alike. The Tangle Teezer can be used on all types of hair, making it one of the most popular and innovative hairbrushes on the shelves today.
          </p>
          <p>
           Your future to healthy looking hair starts right now from just 10.50 and can be found from anywhere from Boots to Selfridges.
          </p>
          <p>
           The tangle teaser is available in an array of bold colours. As well as the original styles, the brushes now come in a compact size, perfect for your handbag. The compact size includes a removable cover that protects the teeth from damage and keeps them clean.
