       <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
        Hot Dogs with Krautslaw
       </h1>
       <div class="post-meta clearfix">
        <span class="printonly">
         Categories:
        </span>
        <p class="meta_categories">
         <a href="http://www.fabfood4all.co.uk/category/bbq/" rel="category tag">

        <p>
         <img alt="Warburtons,German, snack, world cup" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5879" height="651" src="http://www.fabfood4all.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hotdogs-4-Custom.jpg" width="1024"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         In case you hadnt notice were in the grip of World Cup fever and to mark the occasion Warburtons are challenging everyone over on their facebook page to enter their competition 
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/Warburtons?fref=ts" rel="nofollow" title="Warburtons Facebook">
          Cook Off Before Kickoff
         </a>
          where they want fans to come up with match day snacks to enjoy during key games to win prizes! I was asked to devise a recipe to enjoy during the Germany v USA match which is due to kick off on 26 June. Warburtons sent me a German themed mystery box of ingredients and lots of their bread products with which to make my creation!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Warburtons, sub roll, German, sub roll, snack, world cup" class="size-medium wp-image-5880 aligncenter" height="206" src="http://www.fabfood4all.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hotdogs-1-Custom-300x206.jpg" width="300"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I immediately honed in on the sauerkraut as I have heard about it for so many years and yet I have never tasted it. So I set about researching uses and how to eat it. You can eat it hot or cold, in its juice, drained or rinsed depending on your preference. I had no idea that sauerkraut was used as a hot dog topping and therefore decided to make a zesty slaw for mine by adding some carrot, apple and mayonnaise (its a sneaky way of getting your men to eat some of their 5 a day at least). A slice of pickled gherkin and a squeeze of German mustard were the finishing touches. Just like any football match theres usually a substitution so I substituted Hot Dog Rolls for
         <a href="http://www.warburtons.co.uk/products/rolls/white/2pk-white-batch-sub-rolls" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Warburtons">
          Warburtons Sub Rolls
         </a>
         which were just the perfect size for the large German frankfurters I was sent.
         <img alt="Warburtons, sub roll, German, snack, world cup, frankfurters" class="size-medium wp-image-5877 aligncenter" height="267" src="http://www.fabfood4all.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hotdogs-3-Custom-300x267.jpg" width="300"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         These hot dogs went down really well with me and the boys (I include my hubby in that category) but they werent to my 10 year old daughters liking as pickles are a taste she has not yet acquired! The subs were light and airy and made a great home for the frankfurters so I would use these again!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Cook off before kickoff" class="size-medium wp-image-5882 aligncenter" height="300" src="http://www.fabfood4all.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Cook-off-before-kickoff-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I think this is a great quick snack which you can easily knock up before kick off or during the match and would be fab washed down with some German beer. Now its your turn to think of a snack using Warburtons bread!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Hot Dogs with Krautslaw" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5878" height="679" src="http://www.fabfood4all.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hotdogs5-Custom.jpg" width="1024"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Some Warburtons products which might inspire you!
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_5876" style="width: 1034px">
         <img alt="Warburtons,German, snack, world cup" class="size-full wp-image-5876" height="601" src="http://www.fabfood4all.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Hotdogs-2-Custom.jpg" width="1024"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          What snack could you come up with?

            Mix the drained sauerkraut with the grated carrot, apple and enough mayonnaise to coat. Set aside in the fridge.

           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Fry the frankfurters in the oil in a griddle pan according to their instructions.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Side slice the sub rolls and fill with the krautslaw, sliced gherkin and frankfurters.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
