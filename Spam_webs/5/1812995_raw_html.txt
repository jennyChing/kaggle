         <p>
          We sell Beekeeping supplies to Hobbyist Beekeepers. Our blog and podcast help you to keep bees in urban and rural locations.
         </p>
         <p>
          We are here to support you in keeping and saving the humble honey bee 
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_honey_bee" target="_blank" title="Western honey bee">
           Apis mellifera
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          We are beekeepers from the hills of West Auckland, We share Beekeeping information and news via our website and podcast. You will read our blog
          <a href="../blog/" title="blog">
           HERE
          </a>
          and you can listen to our podcasts
          <a href="../category/podcast/" title="Podcasts">
           HERE
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>

          <a href="http://kiwimana.co.nz/shop/" title="kiwimana Beekeeper Supplies Shop">
           beekeeping supplies online shop

          <a href="../contactus/">
           touch
          </a>
          if we dont stock what you need.
