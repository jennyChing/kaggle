       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         40+ Road Trip Snacks + SoFabU Phoenix Trip with Mitsubishi!
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-04-08T04:00:51+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          April 8, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/author/jennifer" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Jen
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/2015/04/40-road-trip-snacks.html#comments">
           12 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-color="gray" data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.yummyhealthyeasy.com%2F2015%2F04%2F40-road-trip-snacks.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.yummyhealthyeasy.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2015%2F04%2Fkid-friendly-road-trip-snacks-collage.jpg&amp;description=40%2B%20Road%20Trip%20Snacks%20%2B%20SoFabU%20Phoenix%20Trip%20with%20Mitsubishi%21" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <em>
          Going on a road trip soon? Check out these 40+ easy and kid-friendly road trip snacks! Ideas from granola bars, nut balls, and snack mixes. Plus a recap of my awesome trip to Arizona in a brand-new Mitsubishi Outlander! (I was provided car and othertravel accomodations for this post. All opinions are my own.)
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/kid-friendly-road-trip-snacks-collage.jpg">
          <img alt="Going on a road trip soon? Check out these 40+ easy and kid-friendly road trip snacks! Ideas from granola bars, nut balls, and snack mixes. " class="aligncenter wp-image-7281 size-full" height="1144" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/kid-friendly-road-trip-snacks-collage.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you follow me on
         <span style="color: #000080;">
          <a href="https://instagram.com/jen_yummyhealthyeasy/" style="color: #000080;" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            Instagram
           </strong>
          </a>
         </span>
         , you know that I had the privilege to drive this super cute car below to Arizona to go to the SoFabU On The Road Conference!
        </p>
        <p>
         I was initially supposed to drive there with my blogging bud, Kristin (
         <a href="http://www.yellowblissroad.com/" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Yellow Bliss Road
          </strong>
         </a>
         ) but she unfortunately got the stomach flu the night before we were supposed to leave for the conference.
         <img alt=":(" class="wp-smiley" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/frownie.png"/>
         I was hesitant to go by myself but then thought,(being amom of four) alone doesnt happen often, right?! So I decided I couldnt
         <em>
          not
         </em>
         go!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/mitsubishi-outlander-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Recap of my fun road trip to Arizona in a brand-new Mitsubishi Outlander! #DriveMitsubishi" class="aligncenter wp-image-7275 size-full" height="800" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/mitsubishi-outlander-1.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So let me get to all the fun details of my trip. First of all, the car.I was really excited when I found out that I got to drive a Mitsubishi car.
         <a href="http://www.mitsubishicars.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #000080;">
            Mitsubishi
           </span>
          </strong>
         </a>
         is known to have quality cars for great prices. They drive almost like a race car, the way theyre lower to the ground and quickly maneuver. Theyre easy to turn and navigate, too.
        </p>
        <p>
         I have absolutely nothing bad to say about this cute
         <span style="color: #000080;">
          <a href="http://www.mitsubishicars.com/outlander?gclid=CJ7u-ZXi5cQCFcKHaQodvKkAGg" rel="nofollow" style="color: #000080;" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            Mitsubishi Outlander
           </strong>
          </a>
         </span>
         ! I would honestly buy this car. Plain and simple.
        </p>
        <p>
         I love that it has a 3rd row that easily pops up and down. I love that it drives like a pro and can turn on a dime. I love the touch screen radio and navigation. I love that this car has the ability to be a mom car but is totally sporty too.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/mitsubishi-5.jpg">
          <img alt="Recap of my fun road trip to Arizona in a brand-new Mitsubishi Outlander! #DriveMitsubishi" class="aligncenter wp-image-7277 size-full" height="640" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/mitsubishi-5.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Iput the sunroof down and zipped through the desert terrainwith theSirius XM radio up as loud as I wanted (no kids, remember?!). Lets just say 5 1/2 hours went by
         <em>
          fast
         </em>
         . Pretty much it was a dream drive. Oh, and I forgot to mention that I didnt even need to fill the gas tank on my drive!
        </p>
        <p>
         I had to stop a few times just to take some fun pictures of my little red car. I made a pit stop at the Cabazon Outlets, because, duh. Outlets.  And of course the windmills, which make a pretty rad backdrop. Driving through the desert, I felt like a superstar. Such a fun car to drive! I made it to Arizona in record time.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/SoFabU-conference-7.jpg">
          <img alt="SoFabU On The Road Conference! Lots of great information learned here. Plus a recap of my entire road trip." class="aligncenter wp-image-7278 size-full" height="640" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/SoFabU-conference-7.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         After a refreshing night in my hotel room, I woke up ready for the
         <span style="color: #000080;">
          <a href="http://ontheroad.socialfabric.us/" rel="nofollow" style="color: #000080;" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            SoFabU On the Road Conference
           </strong>
          </a>
         </span>
         ! One of the things I loved about this conference was how intimate it was. There were probably only 30 bloggers there, which made it a comfortable setting. Ive been to other conferences that have so many people that its overwhelming.
        </p>
        <p>
         I was able to meet and hang out with
         <a href="http://www.eazypeazymealz.com/" target="_blank">
          <span style="color: #000080;">
           <strong>
            Rachael from Eazy Peazy Mealz
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         . LOVE her. She is seriously the sweetest! I really enjoyed hearing fromAliza Sherman, who taught a class about Social Media Strategy and Storytelling. We got to hear from a couple of cool brands and bloggers about the best ways to work together. There was also a peer review session, where we had a fellow blogger look over one or more of our posts and they could tell us what we could do to improve.
        </p>
        <p>
         But overall, my favorite class of the day was taught by a professional food stylist (Kim Krejca) and professional food photographer (Rick Gayle). Chef Steven Fowler cooked an amazing Southwestern menu and Kim and Rick worked together to photograph each course, going through each step to teach us. It was absolutely incredible!
        </p>
        <p>
         There are so many secrets I learned from them about styling and photography. I put some of their tips to use as soon as I got home. Rachael and I were lucky enough to stay after the conference and chat with them one on one. They looked at my pictures here on my blog and gave me some great constructive criticism. I feel so lucky that I was able to meet them and learn so much from them.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/SoFabU-conference-5.jpg">
          <img alt="Wonderful lessons learned from a professional food stylist and food photographer! Loved my road trip to SoFabU On the Road Phoenix!" class="aligncenter wp-image-7279 size-full" height="853" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/SoFabU-conference-5.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Overall, I think the SoFabU OTR Conference was a smashing success. I was sad to leave and have to give back my car but I had such a great experience.
        </p>
        <p>
         Besure to check out all of
         <a href="http://www.mitsubishicars.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #000080;">
            Mitsubishis
           </span>
          </strong>
         </a>
         fabulous cars and then stop bythe
         <a href="http://ontheroad.socialfabric.us/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="color: #000080;">
           <strong>
            SoFabU website
           </strong>
          </span>
         </a>
         to see where theyre heading next and if theyll be visiting your area soon!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/SoFabU-conference-6.jpg">
          <img alt="SoFabU On The Road Conference! Lots of great information learned here. Plus a recap of my entire road trip." class="aligncenter wp-image-7280 size-full" height="853" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/SoFabU-conference-6.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Just for fun, I thought it would be great to roundup recipes from some of my blogger friends of easy road trip snacks. I dont know about you, but Im always looking for great snacks to take on the road. And these arent just road trip snacks, theyre snacks you can make anytime! Thanks for reading  see you Friday!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/kid-friendly-road-trip-snacks-collage.jpg">
          <img alt="Going on a road trip soon? Check out these 40+ easy and kid-friendly road trip snacks! Ideas from granola bars, nut balls, and snack mixes. " class="aligncenter wp-image-7281 size-medium" height="600" src="//www.yummyhealthyeasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/kid-friendly-road-trip-snacks-collage-336x600.jpg" width="336"/>
         </a>
         <br/>

        </p>
