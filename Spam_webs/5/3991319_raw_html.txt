           <h1 class="post-title">
            Windows 10 Preview: Cortana, Spartan, HoloLens, Surface Hub
           </h1>
           <p class="post-byline">
            by
            <a href="http://themobiguru.com/author/pradeep-kumar/" rel="author" title="Posts by Pradeep Kumar">
             Pradeep Kumar
            </a>
             January 24, 2015
           </p>
           <div class="clear">
           </div>
           <div class="entry">
            <div class="entry-inner">
             <p>
              Windows 10 is an operating system developed by Microsoft. The OS is currently in beta testing. The Windows 10 is unveiled on Sept, 30 2014 and is scheduled to be release in 2015. This is designed to make the experience of operating system smoother.
             </p>
             <p>
              Microsoft will provide free upgrade for Windows 10 for the customer running Windows 7, Windows 8, Windows 8.1 and Windows Phone 8.1, who upgraded in the first year after launch. And this is not one time upgrade, the upgrade support is for life time of the device at no cost.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Cortana: Personal Digital Assistant
             </h3>
             <p>
              Microsofts virtual assistant in Window Phone 8.1 is now being available in Windows 10. Cortana looks pretty handy. Interaction with Cortana is easy via talking or typing like a mobile device.
              <a href="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_cortana.jpg">
               <img alt="cortana" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-783" height="568" src="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_cortana.jpg" width="1022"/>
              </a>
              The personal assistant will get its place next to the start button in the desktop taskbar. She will be able to search local storage, OneDrive, or business network to respond the queries.
              <br/>
              As Cortana learns from your preferences of choice, it will become more useful and handy with time.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Spartan: Next Generation Browser
             </h3>
             <p>
              The new browser includes some features which make it far different from other browsers. This includes a note-taking mode that let you add motes or comment to a webpage, and then share it with friends using Windows 10.
              <a href="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_spartan.jpg">
               <img alt="spartan browser" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-784" height="670" src="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_spartan.jpg" width="1255"/>
              </a>
              A clipping tool in Spartan lets you save a portion of website directly to OneNote. Spartan includes a Reading Mode which skips out all the ads and sidebar from a webpage. Reading Mode formats the article to appear similar to a book. Articles can be saved for offline reading.
              <br/>
              Cortana is also being integrated into the Spartan. This integration makes the finding, searching and doing things faster.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Universal Applications
             </h3>
             <p>
              This offers the experience consistent on all the devices. Offering include for Photo, Videos, Music, Maps, Messaging, Mail, and Calendar. These apps are designed in Windows 10 to give same look and feel in all the devices. The content is synced through OneDrive. Notification removed from any of the Window 10 devices will be cleared from all your Windows 10 devices.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Windows 10 for Phones
             </h3>
             <p>
              Windows 10 in phones basically act as an extension of your Windows 10 PC. Microsoft will give free copy of Office to Windows 10 phones and tablets. Small screen Windows 10 devices will have Skype build into the default text messaging app. This helps users to switch between Skype and messaging app without leaving one app.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Continuum Mode: UI Switching
             </h3>
             <p>
              <a href="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_continuum.png">
               <img alt="windows 10 continuun" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-786" height="299" src="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_continuum.png" width="500"/>
              </a>
              <br/>
              This mode detects whether you are using a PC or touch-enabled devices. The Windows 10 easily detects the transition of mouse and keyboard and decides to move between. This interface is considered on 2in1 convertible devices like
              <a href="http://www.microsoft.com/surface/en-us/products/surface-pro-3">
               Surface Pro 3
              </a>
              <a>
               .
              </a>
             </p>
             <h3>
              Xbox Experience:
             </h3>
             <p>
              Games can access the expansive Xbox Live games on both Windows 10 PC and Xbox One.
              <a href="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_xbox_video.jpg">
               <img alt="windows 10 xbox" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-782" height="325" src="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_xbox_video.jpg" width="579"/>
              </a>
              New DirectX 12 in Windows 10 will improve the speed, graphics and efficiency of games. Players can stream games from their Xbox console to their Windows 10 phones and tablets, within their home. Players can capture and share their moment to their friends with Game DVR.
             </p>
             <h2>
              New Microsoft Windows 10 Devices
             </h2>
             <p>
              Windows 10 not only delivers the great software experience in PCs, phones and tablets but support the all new devices from Microsoft like Microsoft HoloLens and Microsoft Surface Hub.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Microsoft HoloLens:
             </h3>
             <p>
              HoloLens is the craziest hardware announced by the Microsoft. With Microsoft HoloLens, Windows 10 becomes the worlds first holographic computing platform.
              <a href="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_microsoft_hololens.jpg">
               <img alt="microsoft hololens" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-781" height="800" src="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_microsoft_hololens.jpg" width="1200"/>
              </a>
              Developers are able to create holographic experience in the real world with the all new complete set of APIs. Windows universal apps can work as holograms. Microsoft HoloLens, a headset for viewing and interaction with 3D images. The headset will be completely wireless. The headset will have its own CPU and GPU.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Microsoft Surface Hub: Enables Group Computing
             </h3>
             <p>
              Microsoft Surface Hub is another unexpected hardware announcement made by Microsoft. Surface Hub is a 4k, touch enabled display for offices.
              <a href="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_surface_hub.jpg">
               <img alt="surface hub" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-780" height="321" src="http://themobiguru.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/windows_10_surface_hub.jpg" width="580"/>
              </a>
              The display will be available in two sizes 55 and 84. Surface Hub removes the current limitations of current traditional conference system. Surface Hub has Bluetooth, Camera, Speakers, Microphone, WiFi, and a Motion sensor. The display is integrated with the Skype and OneNote to make business more interesting. With Surface Hub the teams can create best work together. Multiple people can share and comment on the screen from any device.
              <br/>
             </p>
