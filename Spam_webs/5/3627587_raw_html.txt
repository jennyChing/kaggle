       <h2 class="subtitle">

       </h2>
      </div>
     </div>
    </div>

        <a class="themewich-lightbox" href="http://aegis-strife.net/illustration/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Suspense-by-Mario-Snchez-Nevado.jpg">
         <img alt="Suspense by Mario Snchez Nevado" class="normal size-full wp-image-8736" height="707" src="http://aegis-strife.net/illustration/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Suspense-by-Mario-Snchez-Nevado.jpg" width="1000"/>

        <p class="wp-caption-text caption-normal">
         <span>
          Suspense
         </span>
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        I finished this photo manipulation circa June, 2014. With all the readjustments of the website, I have been waiting for the right moment to publish it. Its way simpler than my usual stuff, a thing that Ive been trying to achieve for quite a long time.
       </p>
       <p>
        I ignore if this illustration will be a step forward in my artistic evolution or if it will remain as a simple experiment, but I like the way it turned out!
       </p>
       <p>
        Its about being emotionally blind, but having exploding feelings inside. Like having a hard time to communicate something
