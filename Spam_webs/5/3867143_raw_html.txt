     <p>
      Having
      <strong>
       Elements Home Remodeling
      </strong>
      replace your old windows with new, energy-efficient replacement windows is one of the
      <em>
       best ways to make your home look great, enhance your familys comfort and safety, and reduce your monthly energy bills.
      </em>
     </p>
     <p>
      If your homes windows are more than 10 years old, it is likely that they are very inefficient at stopping energy loss. Older windows were not designed to prevent energy transfer, and are generally constructed with materials that simply do not offer sufficient insulating properties.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       The U.S. Department of Energy estimates that as much as 30% of a homes energy can be lost through inefficient doors and windows.
      </strong>
      Thats like leaving a window wide open all winter long!
      <strong>
       Elements Home Remodeling
      </strong>
      solves this problem by installing thermally-efficient windows in Alexandria, VA homes, and in homes across Northern Virginia.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       How can replacing your windows save you money?
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      When energy escapes through your old, inefficient windows, your homes heating system and air conditioning units have to work extra hard to maintain the desired comfort level. That means burning more fuel or using more electricity. If you have ever noticed your homes furnace kicking on frequently during the winter, youll understand how unnecessary energy loss can quickly add up to higher utility bills.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       By creating an effective thermal barrier, replacement windows from Elements Home Remodeling can virtually eliminate this energy loss.
      </strong>
      The warm air that keeps your family cozy during the cold weather remains inside your home without dissipating. During the hot summer months, cool comfortable air does not escape and hot, humid air is kept from entering your home.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Elements Home Remodeling
      </strong>
      installs windows manufactured by  one of the pioneers in the development of energy-efficient replacement windows. Fairfax, VA homeowners and customers in other Northern Virginia towns have come to realize that our windows offer more than superior energy savings  they are also beautiful, easy to operate, and deliver many years of reliable service.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Dont go through another season of excessive energy bills
      </strong>
      and constant fluctuations in your homes interior temperatures. Get comfortable and save money by calling on
      <strong>
       Elements Home Remodeling
      </strong>
      for new replacement windows.
