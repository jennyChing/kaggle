       <h5>
        March 10, 2014
       </h5>
       <h1>
        Bossys Ban On Ban Bossy
       </h1>
      </div>
      <!-- End Post Header -->
      <!-- Begin Post Body -->
      <div id="entry">
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="290" mozallowfullscreen="" msallowfullscreen="" oallowfullscreen="" src="https://www.flickr.com/photos/8127276@N05/13072297004/player/0a13bd639a" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="425">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        As some of you may have heard because a
        <em>
         Ban Bossy
        </em>
        avalanche fell on your Monday morning, Sheryl Sandberg launched a campaign to
        <strong>
         murder a word
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="418" mozallowfullscreen="" msallowfullscreen="" oallowfullscreen="" src="https://www.flickr.com/photos/8127276@N05/13071942255/player/18a3f45509" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="325">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        If you dont know Sheryl Sandberg because youre buried under an avalanche, heres a brief tutorial: she was always at the top of her class, graduating from college with the highest distinction. She served as Chief of Staff for the Treasury and then Vice President of Operations at Google where she initiated the sites philanthropic arm. Now she serves as the Chief Operating Officer of a little online project that rhymes with SpaceTook, and has firmly landed on the Time 100 list of influential people, her estimated worth at over one billion dollars owing to various stock holdings.
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="350" mozallowfullscreen="" msallowfullscreen="" oallowfullscreen="" src="https://www.flickr.com/photos/8127276@N05/13072818714/player/cf7f13fb00" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="350">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        Compare, if you will,
        <em>
         Bossy
        </em>
        , who was also at the top of her class, where
        <strong>
         top
        </strong>
        equals
        <em>
         peering down
        </em>
        from her near six-foot frame. And like Sandberg, Bossy graduated from college with
        <em>
         distinction
        </em>
        , which in this case refers to the distinction of not finishing her Bachelors degree until seventeen years after she began chasing those bachelors.
       </p>
       <p>
        Bossy then launched her own online project that should have been named
        <em>
         I Am Boozy
        </em>
        , where Bossy also initiated the sites philanthropic arm, referring specifically to her
        <em>
         right arm
        </em>
        <a href="http://www.iambossy.com/fambly/2008/01/29/dont-quit-your-day-job-bossys-ass/" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          holding the paint brush
         </strong>
        </a>
        that paid for her
        <strong>
         wine
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Bossy also firmly landed on the Time 100 influential list, by which she means
        <strong>
         the 100 times Bossy landed firmly while under the influence
        </strong>
        <em>
         (see wine).
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Like Sandberg, Bossy also served as Vice President of Operations, including
        <a href="http://www.iambossy.com/bossy-moments-throughout-history/2010/02/08/varicose-barbie/" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          one operation to remove varicose veins
         </strong>
        </a>
        and one
        <a href="http://www.iambossy.com/fambly/2011/01/17/operation-dysfunctional/" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          Operation Dysfunctional
         </strong>
        </a>
        . And finally, Bossys estimated worth is
        <em>
         also
        </em>
        owing to her various stocks, which mostly
        <strong>
         boils down
        </strong>
        to
        <em>
         chicken
        </em>
        stock, although doesnt completely rule out
        <em>
         vegetable
        </em>
        stock.
       </p>
       <p>
        So what is the Ban Bossy campaign about? According to Sheryl Sandberg, when a little girl is
        <em>
         assertive
        </em>
        , she is called
        <strong>
         bossy
        </strong>
        . But when a little
        <em>
         boy
        </em>
        is assertive, he is called
        <strong>
         leader
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="357" mozallowfullscreen="" msallowfullscreen="" oallowfullscreen="" src="https://www.flickr.com/photos/8127276@N05/13072270914/player/ee6820425e" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="350">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        Bossy took great interest today in all of the little girls
        <strong>
         now women
        </strong>
        who are standing with Sheryl Sandberg in admonishment of having been referred to as bossy, including
        <em>
         three world leaders, two Supreme Court Justices, two Presidential candidates, two members of Congress, a CEO, a First Lady, a successful publisher,
        </em>
        and
        <strong>
         Beyonc
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Looking at the lineup of women, Bossy wondered how many
        <em>
         other
        </em>
        adjectives they have in common. Because if
        <strong>
         bossy
        </strong>
        is a
        <em>
         descriptor
        </em>
        these powerhouses share  a confidence of personality that propelled them to such greatness  then maybe the more liberating thing isnt
        <em>
         running
        </em>
        from a word, but owning the
        <strong>
         grit
        </strong>
        in its meaning.
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="395" mozallowfullscreen="" msallowfullscreen="" oallowfullscreen="" src="https://www.flickr.com/photos/8127276@N05/13072272894/player/e1fd084225" webkitallowfullscreen="" width="299">
        </iframe>
       </p>
      </div>
