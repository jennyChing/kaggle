       <div id="audata">

        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="background-color:#dda0dd;">
          <span style="font-size: 14px;">
           <span style="color: rgb(128, 0, 128);">
            Relationship Advice Tips from Dr. Jeanette Raymond, Ph.D.
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size:16px;">
          Are you ready to give up on your relationship because your
          <a href="http://losangeleswestsidetherapy.com/intimacy/partner-choice/#.U6nenbGmWkg">
           partner
          </a>
          shuts you out and keeps you out each time you try to connect?
          <br/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size:16px;">
          Are you fed up with being treated like a monster instead of a lover and a loving partner?
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size:16px;">
          Maybe you are wondering what on earth is making your partner push you away and spurn your efforts to get
          <a href="http://losangeleswestsidetherapy.com/intimacy/emotional-intimacy/#.U6nfPLGmWkg">
           emotionally intimate
          </a>
          !
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size:16px;">
          This video is the first in a series that takes you behind the curtains and into the soul of your partner  where you can see all the murky
          <a href="http://losangeleswestsidetherapy.com/insecurity-and-stress/worry-and-fear/#.U6nffbGmWkg">
           fears
          </a>
          that make it hard to
          <a href="http://losangeleswestsidetherapy.com/intimacy/trust-and-betrayal/#.U6nfWLGmWkg">
           trust
          </a>
          you and let you in.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size:16px;">
          Yes, you can save your relationship by tuning in and understanding what's going on with your partner.
          <a href="http://losangeleswestsidetherapy.com/therapy-services/married-and-cohabiting-couples/#.U6ngEbGmWkg">
           Couples counseling
          </a>
          isn't the only alternative.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/k1NSwd1MQWc" width="420">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="font-size:16px;">
          You will be amazed but enlightened, realizing that it's not your fault. Then you can hang on in there and use the methods I outline in
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Now-You-Want-Dont-relationship/dp/0991518004/ref=sr_1_2?s=books&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1404051853&amp;sr=1-2&amp;keywords=Now+You+want+Me%2C+Now+You+don">
           <span style="background-color:#add8e6;">
            '
            <em>
             Now You Want Me, Now You Don't! Fear of Intimacy: Ten ways to recognize it and ten ways to manage it in your relationship'
            </em>
            ,
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         to make your partner feel safer.
        </p>
