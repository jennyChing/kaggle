      <h2 class="single-entry-title">
       Bruno Engagement Macaron Cake
      </h2>
      <div class="linebreak">
      </div>
      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/63438696@N02/5818113225/" title="Macaron tree 1 by bigboyosven02, on Flickr">
         <img alt="Macaron tree 1" height="753" src="http://farm3.static.flickr.com/2441/5818113225_49575620e9_b.jpg" width="500"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        I had been busy away from my kitchen this few weeks as I was baby sitting my nephew and niece during this school holidays. But I did sneak into the kitchen to bake some request. One was Brunos wedding solemnisation macaron cake.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span id="more-8194">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/63438696@N02/5818113097/" title="Macaron tree by bigboyosven02, on Flickr">
         <img alt="Macaron tree" height="753" src="http://farm6.static.flickr.com/5026/5818113097_ddb2004c0f_b.jpg" width="500"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Bruno was swifted, he emailed me what he wanted. A photo of a tree filled with macarons. I baked heart shape macarons came in two colours. Baked a round cake and fondant it, built a tree.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/63438696@N02/5818113335/" title="Macaron tree 2 by bigboyosven02, on Flickr">
         <img alt="Macaron tree 2" height="753" src="http://farm6.static.flickr.com/5067/5818113335_7bd29c67fe_b.jpg" width="500"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        It was awesome building this macaron cake. Another project accomplished and will be planning another macaron project next week. I will definitely be having macaron class this June while cake baking in July ! .
        <strong>
         . . . . . . . . Macaron-ing with me, Sunny Yaw
        </strong>
       </p>
