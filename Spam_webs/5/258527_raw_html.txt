       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         A Yacht, a Mountain, and Americas Coolest Small Town
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Mark
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/everest-beaufort-accommodations/#respond">
           Leave a Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_8698-634-2.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_8698-634-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1688" height="365" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_8698-634-2.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
         <em>
          Looking towards Town Marsh at sunrise
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         At first glance, Beauforts accommodation choices are pretty limited: a few small hotels, inns, bed &amp; breakfasts, and a few vacation rental homes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Earlier this year we started compiling notes taken over the years on places to stay in and around Beaufort.
        </p>
        <p>
         Weve writtenabout some in our
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/the-beaufort-accommodations-guide-a-quick-introduction/" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Beaufort Accommodations Guide  A Quick Introduction
          </strong>
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/the-beaufort-accommodations-guide-part-ii-a-guide-to-beauforts-small-hotels/" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           Beaufort Accommodations Guide Part II  A Guide to Beauforts Small Hotels
          </strong>
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         We had planned on posting a few moreparts, maybe compiling it all on one page, and wrapping it up.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          As we started to dive a little deeper into Beauforts accommodation choices, we discovered that the choices are anything but limited, and represent an eclectic mix of great places to stay!
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Were going to start featuring some of thosechoices.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Today well begin with one of the most interesting accommodation choices in town:
         </strong>
         <span id="more-1678">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          The Everest!
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/7315310b-50a4-4a24-a2b4-6687f0ea7f38-634x360.jpg">
          <img alt="7315310b-50a4-4a24-a2b4-6687f0ea7f38-634x360" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1681" height="360" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/7315310b-50a4-4a24-a2b4-6687f0ea7f38-634x360.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Docked at the west end of Front Street just across from the Duncan House, the Everest is an 80-foot Italian-made San Lorenzo Motor Yacht. Her Captain, Dan Meggitt, and her first mate, Kelly Gordon, operate the Everest as the equivalent of a small floating inn or bed &amp; breakfast.
        </p>
        <p>
         Last week, Dan and Kelly were kind enough to ask me aboard for a tour and some great conversation about the Everest, Beaufort, and the future of Americas coolest small town.
        </p>
        <p>
         Guests board Everest via a small set of steps near the rear of the yacht.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/804431e1-87d5-4f6e-b245-aa7dab301f81-634.jpg">
          <img alt="804431e1-87d5-4f6e-b245-aa7dab301f81-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1691" height="405" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/804431e1-87d5-4f6e-b245-aa7dab301f81-634.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
         <em>
          Aft lounge area
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         When you step on deck, youll find the aft area a perfect spot for lounging with a half-circle of red-cushioned wicker couches, and plenty of room for fishing.
        </p>
        <p>
         Theres also a small ladder to climb down to a swimming platform(that is if you can resist jumping from the deck!).
        </p>
        <p>
         With the Everests distinctive blue lights just below the waterline, the platform becomes a great spot for after-dark fish watching.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/10428543_664386713638217_748561904869015777_n-634.jpg">
          <img alt="10428543_664386713638217_748561904869015777_n-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1692" height="423" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/10428543_664386713638217_748561904869015777_n-634.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
         <em>
          Those cool blue lights
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         As you move inside on the main level youll find a roomy salon with plenty of comfortable seating.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/a19fe41b-6ac0-4b49-8193-522f28304cd1-634.jpg">
          <img alt="a19fe41b-6ac0-4b49-8193-522f28304cd1-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1694" height="423" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/a19fe41b-6ac0-4b49-8193-522f28304cd1-634.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
         <em>
          The main salon
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         As you enter the salon from aft, you see a set of descending steps to starboard. These lead to one of Everests four bedroomsactually this oneismore of a stateroom and is considered the VIP suite.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/55ca8964-e5c2-4f66-89a4-201a9cb70743.1-1.jpg">
          <img alt="55ca8964-e5c2-4f66-89a4-201a9cb70743.1 (1)" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1696" height="767" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/55ca8964-e5c2-4f66-89a4-201a9cb70743.1-1.jpg" width="511"/>
         </a>
         <em>
          Got to love that shower
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         This room has a double bed and the coolest bathroom aboard, with a glass-encased shower stall. It may be the most private spot on board, as it is separate from the remainder of the sleeping quarters.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/8f4eb58b-b888-45e6-9799-66b1264f7c75-634.jpg">
          <img alt="8f4eb58b-b888-45e6-9799-66b1264f7c75-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1697" height="423" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/8f4eb58b-b888-45e6-9799-66b1264f7c75-634.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
         <em>
          More seating in the main salon
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Returning up to the main salon and moving forward, youll find a comfortable, classy, well-decorated, but not at all flashy or fragile interior.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/9cd2ef54-0a4a-4a8f-8b9d-97430c24429c-634.jpg">
          <img alt="9cd2ef54-0a4a-4a8f-8b9d-97430c24429c-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1698" height="423" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/9cd2ef54-0a4a-4a8f-8b9d-97430c24429c-634.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
         <em>
          In the main salon
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Better than that, it feels pretty homey and easy to slide into.
        </p>
        <p>
         Forward of the main salon is the galley.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/9af0b18d-37e4-467a-9860-a773503103ff.jpg">
          <img alt="9af0b18d-37e4-467a-9860-a773503103ff" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1699" height="767" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/9af0b18d-37e4-467a-9860-a773503103ff.jpg" width="511"/>
         </a>
         <em>
          The galley
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Its a beautiful combination of wood and steel and is a step up from many home kitchens.
        </p>
        <p>
         Youll love the refrigerator, too.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/172117e1-e493-4ec2-9e84-14be345423d0.jpg">
          <img alt="172117e1-e493-4ec2-9e84-14be345423d0" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1700" height="767" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/172117e1-e493-4ec2-9e84-14be345423d0.jpg" width="511"/>
         </a>
         <em>
          Another shot of the galley
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Just forward of the galley is Everests main bridge.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_8707-634.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_8707-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1702" height="514" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_8707-634.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         When nobodys looking you probably wont be able to resist a quick test drive of the captains chair. I couldnt.
        </p>
        <p>
         Even with the galley on both sides of the Everest are doors to the deck passageways that lead both forward and aft.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_7031-634.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_7031-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1703" height="291" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_7031-634.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Heading forward to Everests bow, you find a great place to enjoy the scenery but also a wonderfully cushioned built-in sun bathing spot.
        </p>
        <p>
         We also understand from past guests that its an amazing spot to enjoy the stars after sunset.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_8735-634-2.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_8735-634-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1704" height="356" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_8735-634-2.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Youll also enjoy great views of Gallants Channel.
        </p>
        <p>
         Back inside, next to the galley theres a set of steps leading to the upper deck.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/31e85723-11eb-49e7-a831-14bfe93941cf-634.jpg">
          <img alt="31e85723-11eb-49e7-a831-14bfe93941cf-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1705" height="423" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/31e85723-11eb-49e7-a831-14bfe93941cf-634.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
         <em>
          The top deck
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         The views are some of the best in Beaufort.
        </p>
        <p>
         Theres afly bridgearea, but also a steel Jenn-Air grill, plenty of seating, and a large table to enjoy a meal with a view.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/fcf12c86-59c6-4484-8979-a1d64fe47a90-634.jpg">
          <img alt="fcf12c86-59c6-4484-8979-a1d64fe47a90-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1709" height="536" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/fcf12c86-59c6-4484-8979-a1d64fe47a90-634.jpg" width="804"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/10492301_664384826971739_6647441054463108143_n-634.jpg">
          <img alt="10492301_664384826971739_6647441054463108143_n-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1710" height="634" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/10492301_664384826971739_6647441054463108143_n-634.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Heading back down to the main level, youll see another set of steps that leads to the lower deck and the three other bedrooms on board.
        </p>
        <p>
         As you reach the bottom of the stairway, take a right and youll find the master stateroom.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/abf67574-4ffa-44c3-b4c9-d27857f5d921-634.jpg">
          <img alt="abf67574-4ffa-44c3-b4c9-d27857f5d921-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1707" height="370" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/abf67574-4ffa-44c3-b4c9-d27857f5d921-634.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         It is unbelievably large with a king-sized bed, plenty of seating, and another extremely roomy bath with a shower.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/8c3f7cc6-776c-44e1-afea-421b1c384ef9.jpg">
          <img alt="8c3f7cc6-776c-44e1-afea-421b1c384ef9" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1711" height="767" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/8c3f7cc6-776c-44e1-afea-421b1c384ef9.jpg" width="511"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Moving forward from the master suite and to the left just past the stairway is another bedroom with two singles and a bathroom with a shower  perfect for two kids.
        </p>
        <p>
         Moving left from that bedroom, you pass a bath with a shower in the hallway and into the forward sleeping quarters, a comfy cabin with a double bed that will easily sleep 2.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/6fd35642-c4f1-49cf-a489-f9b0811389fa-634.jpg">
          <img alt="6fd35642-c4f1-49cf-a489-f9b0811389fa-634" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1708" height="423" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/6fd35642-c4f1-49cf-a489-f9b0811389fa-634.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         These photos show you much of what you might expect from a yacht of this size and quality, but what they dont show you is the feel of the Everest.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          The Everest is Dans home, and while it is every bit as luxurious as it looks in the photos  more actually  the Everest has a lived-in, home-like feel that goes perfectly with Beauforts relaxed culture.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Dan and Kelly are also the perfect hosts.
        </p>
        <p>
         They will welcome you on-board, get you oriented to the Everest and to Beaufort.
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre lucky, you might even get a few stories from Dan.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_7016-634-2.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_7016-634-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1716" height="423" src="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_7016-634-2.jpg" width="634"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Both Dan and the Everest have great stories.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Dan isa modern-day adventurer. He has sailed he world solo and summited 5 of the 7 highest mountains on each of the Earths continents.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         He acquired the Everest after his successful climb of the highest mountain in the world, Nepals Mount Everest. Dan named the vessel for the mountain.
        </p>
        <p>
         After a few minutes on board, Dan and Kelly will feel like old friends.
        </p>
        <p>
         However, once youre settled in on the Everest they will slip off-board and allow you to begin getting the most from your time on Everest and in Beaufort.
        </p>
        <p>
         To learn more about the Everest you can check out listings on
         <strong>
          <a href="https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/2366963" target="_blank">
           Airbnb
          </a>
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.vrbo.com/597063" target="_blank">
           VRBO
          </a>
         </strong>
         , and
         <strong>
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/EverestMaritimeLlc" target="_blank">
           Facebook
          </a>
         </strong>
         . Make sure to check out the reviews from previous guests  theyre some of the best weve seen online.
        </p>
        <p>
         You can enjoy a stay on Everest for $400 per night for a couple, with an extra $50 per person up to 8 guests. Use of Everests kayaks and snorkel gear is included.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Convinced?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ready for a night on Everest?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         You can reach out to Dan and Kelly through one of their profiles or you can drop
         <strong>
          <a href="http://southerngirltravel.com/" target="_blank">
           Jennifer
          </a>
         </strong>
         and I an
         <strong>
          <a href="http://bringinghomebeaufort.com/contact/" target="_blank" title="Contact">
           email, or give us a call
          </a>
          .
         </strong>
         Well get the ball rolling for you!
        </p>
        <p>
         And dont forget to:
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Relaxyou CAN get to Beaufort from here!
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">

          Full disclosure: Dan and Kelly were kind enough to offer me a night aboard the Everest when I visited last week.
         </em>
