      <h1 class="entry-title">
       How to Configure Voice VLAN for Cisco Switches?
      </h1>
      <div class="entry-meta">
       <span class="meta-author">
        <a href="http://www.3anetwork.com/blog/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by 3Anetwork">
         3Anetwork
        </a>
       </span>
       /
       <span class="meta-date">
        December 9, 2014
       </span>
       /
       <span class="meta-comment">
        <a href="http://www.3anetwork.com/blog/how-to-configure-voice-vlan-for-cisco-switches/#respond" title="Comment on How to Configure Voice VLAN for Cisco Switches?">
         No Comments
        </a>
       </span>
      </div>
      <!-- .entry-meta -->
      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        The voice VLAN feature enables access ports to carry IP voice traffic from an IP phone. The switch can connect to a
        <em>
         <strong>
          Cisco7960 IP Phone
         </strong>
        </em>
        and carry IP voice traffic. Because the sound quality of an IP phone call can deteriorate if the data is unevenly sent, the switch supports quality of service (QoS) based on IEEE 802.1P class of service (CoS). QoS uses classification and scheduling to send network traffic from the switch in a predictable manner.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.3anetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/configure-voice-vlan-for-cisco-devices1.jpg">
         <img alt="configure-voice-vlan-for-cisco-devices" class="alignnone wp-image-1810" height="350" src="http://www.3anetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/configure-voice-vlan-for-cisco-devices1.jpg" width="408"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Note
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        The routers here can be all Cisco routers, like
        <a href="http://www.3anetwork.com/cisco-1900-series-routers-price_c12">
         Cisco 1900
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.3anetwork.com/cisco-2900-series-routers-price_c13">
         Cisco 2900 series routers
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.3anetwork.com/cisco-3900-series-routers-price_c14">
         Cisco 3900 routers
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.3anetwork.com/cisco-1800-series-routers-price_c15">
         Cisco 1800
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.3anetwork.com/cisco-2800-series-routers-price_c16">
         Cisco 2800
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.3anetwork.com/cisco-3800-series-routers-price_c17">
         Cisco 3800
        </a>
        etc.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Configuration of the Switch
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Switch&gt;
       </p>
       <p>
        Switch&gt; en
       </p>
       <p>
        Switch#conf t
       </p>
       <p>
        Switch(config)#no ip domain look
       </p>
       <p>
        Switch(config)#user cisco pr 15 pass cisco
       </p>
       <p>
        Switch(config)#ho SW1
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config)#line con 0
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-line)#exec-tim 0 0
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-line)#logg syn
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-line)#exit
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config)#line vty 0 4
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-line)#logg syn
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-line)#login loc
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-line)#exec-tim 30 0
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-line)#exit
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config)#vlan 20
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-vlan)#name Data-VLAN
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config)#vlan 150
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-vlan)#name Voice-VLAN
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config)#int f0/1
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-if)#switchport mode access
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-if)#switchport access vlan 150
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config)#int f0/2
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-if)#switchport mode access
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-if)#switchport access vlan 20
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-if)#switchport voice vlan 150
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config)#int f0/3
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-if)#switchport mode access
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-if)#switchport access vlan 20
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config)#mls qos
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config)#int f0/2
       </p>
       <p>
        SW1(config-if)#mls qos trust cos
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Configuration of R1
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Router&gt;en
       </p>
       <p>
        Router#conf t
       </p>
       <p>
        Router(config)#no ip domain-lookup
       </p>
       <p>
        Router(config)#ho R1
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config)#usern cisco pr 15 pass cisco
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config)#line c 0
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config-line)#logi loca
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config-line)#logg sy
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config-line)#exec-tim 0 0
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config-line)#exit
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config)#line vty 0 4
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config-line)#logi loca
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config-line)#logg sy
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config-line)#exec-tim 10 0
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config-line)#exit
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config)#int f0/0
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config-if)#ip add 172.16.150.1 255.255.255.0
       </p>
       <p>
        R1(config-if)#no sh
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Configuration of R2
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Router&gt;en
       </p>
       <p>
        Router#conf t
       </p>
       <p>
        Router(config)#no ip domain-lookup
       </p>
       <p>
        Router(config)#ho R2
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config)#usern cisco pr 15 pass cisco
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config)#line c 0
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-line)#logi loca
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-line)#logg sy
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-line)#exec-tim 0 0
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-line)#exit
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config)#line vty 0 4
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-line)#logi loca
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-line)#logg sy
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-line)#exec-tim 10 0
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-line)#exit
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config)#int f0/0.20
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-subif)#encapsulation dot1Q 20 native
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-subif)#ip add 172.16.20.2 255.255.255.0
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config)#int f0/0.150
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-subif)#encapsulation dot1Q 150
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-subif)#ip add 172.16.150.2 255.255.255.0
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config)#int f0/0
       </p>
       <p>
        R2(config-if)#no sh
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Configuration of R3
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Router&gt;en
       </p>
       <p>
        Router#conf t
       </p>
       <p>
        Router(config)#no ip domain-lookup
       </p>
       <p>
        Router(config)#ho R3
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config)#usern cisco pr 15 pass cisco
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config)#line c 0
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config-line)#logi loca
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config-line)#logg sy
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config-line)#exec-tim 0 0
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config-line)#exit
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config)#line vty 0 4
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config-line)#logi loca
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config-line)#logg sy
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config-line)#exec-tim 10 0
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config-line)#exit
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config)#int f0/0
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config-if)#ip add 172.16.20.3 255.255.255.0
       </p>
       <p>
        R3(config-if)#no sh
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>

        <strong>
         Experimental Debugging
