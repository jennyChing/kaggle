     <h1>
      <a class="posttitle" href="http://scaryjane.com/broken-glass-cupcakes.html" rel="bookmark">
       Broken Glass Cupcakes
      </a>
     </h1>
     <div class="postbody">
      <p>
       Heres a popular dessert idea that I have used at my party quite a few times over the years. Make some red velvet cupcakes with white cream cheese frosting. Now make a batch of clear sugar glass. If you dont know how to do that its really quite easy. Find a recipe
       <a href="http://www.cooks.com/recipe/315fv5lt/glass-candy.html" title="Sugar Glass recipe">
        here
       </a>
       . After thats all done break the glass into pieces. I usually do this by dropping the sheet of glass while its still in the baking pan onto the floor. It will break into a bunch of glass like shards. Take the broken pieces and randomly stick them into the cupcakes then sprinkle edible blood (see recipe below) over the glass and cupcakes. They turn out yummy and look awesome.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        1 tbsp golden syrup
       </strong>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        1 tbsp maple syrup
       </strong>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        1 tsp red food coloring
       </strong>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Few drops blue food coloring
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       If the mixture is too thin stir in a teaspoon of corn starch, whisking rapidly to thin out any lumps or you can leave it to settle for a while and skim the lumps off the top. Obviously you can up the recipe if you are making lots of cupcakes, but this will get you started.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://scaryjane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/IMG_0227.png">
        <img alt="IMG_0227.PNG" class="alignnone " height="439" src="http://scaryjane.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/IMG_0227.png" width="326"/>
       </a>
      </p>
