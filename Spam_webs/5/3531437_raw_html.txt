       <h2>
        How to Choose your Perfect Luxury Apartment
       </h2>
       <div class="entry">
        <div addthis:title="How to Choose your Perfect Luxury Apartment " addthis:url="http://harborsidevillage.com/blog/best-harford-county-apartments/how-to-choose-your-perfect-luxury-apartment/" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style ">
         <a class="addthis_button_facebook_like" fb:like:layout="button_count">
         </a>
         <a class="addthis_button_tweet">
         </a>
         <a class="addthis_counter addthis_pill_style">
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_496" style="width: 310px">
         <a href="http://harborsidevillage.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/luxury-apartment-choices.jpg">
          <img alt="Luxury apartment choices" class="wp-image-496 size-medium" height="200" src="http://harborsidevillage.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/luxury-apartment-choices-300x200.jpg" title="Luxury apartment choices" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Choosing the right luxury apartment for you can be tough. Youll make some compromises, but in the end youll be happy.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         When you set out on your apartment hunting adventure, youll see a lot of duds. Youve likely got a wish list in mind, and its rare to find one apartment that has absolutely everything you want. As the saying goes, nobody is perfect, and the same can be said of your potential apartments. So how are you supposed to choose? How can you find that one apartment that comes closest to meeting all of your needs and all of your desires? The luxury apartments at Harborside Village might be just the thing you were looking for.
         <span id="more-495">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h2>
         The Landlord or Property Management
        </h2>
        <p>
         One thing a lot of people forget to consider when choosing between a few apartment candidates is the landlord or property management company with whom youll be working. It is so important to have confidence in your landlord. If a problem arises, your apartment will be in their hands. Make sure you are comfortable with potential landlords and the terms of the lease. If you are stuck between two apartments, a good landlord is a great reason to choose one over the other.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Location, Location, Location
        </h2>
        <p>
         When it comes to finding your dream luxury apartment, it isnt uncommon to find yourself choosing between the perfect location and the perfect home. Ultimately its about what is more important to you. With that said, an ideal location is a great reason to make some compromises. Consider your proximity to social areas, great views, and convenient shopping. These perks can easily make up for a few unchecked boxes on your checklist.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Non-negotiable Features
        </h2>
        <p>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
          Everybody has a few things that they just dont want to live without. For some its a dishwasher and for others it is outdoor space. Find out what you absolutely must have, and prioritize those features. If you find a place that has them all, you have probably found your home. Remember to stick to your non-negotiables in the face of luxury items that might sway you their way. At the end of the day, youll be more comfortable in a space that fulfills this requirement.
         </span>
        </p>
