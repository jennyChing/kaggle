         <p>
          Rock band The Replacements, who got their start in the 1980s, performed live for the first time in 22 years at
          <a href="http://www.mxdwn.com/2013/06/13/news/riot-fest-toronto-announces-2013-lineup-featuring-the-replacements-iggy-and-the-stooges-and-rocket-from-the-crypt/">
           Torontos Riot Fest
          </a>
          last Sunday.
          <br/>
          <span id="more-38383">
          </span>
          <br/>
          <img alt="thereplacementsfeaturegilles" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-38384" height="385" src="http://www.mxdwn.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/thereplacementsfeaturegilles-580x385.jpg" title="thereplacementsfeaturegilles" width="580"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          The Minneapolis-based group
          <a href="http://www.mxdwn.com/2013/06/13/topstory/the-replacements-announce-official-reunion-and-first-shows-in-22-years/">
           announced a reunion
          </a>
          in June, and they plan to play sets at Riot Fest Chicago and Riot Fest Denver in September.
          <a href="http://consequenceofsound.net/2013/08/listen-to-a-recording-of-the-replacements-first-performance-in-22-years/">
           Consequence of Sound reported
          </a>
          that The Replacements Live Archive Project has uploaded a recorded stream of the entire performance.
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe frameborder="no" height="450" scrolling="no" src="http://w.soundcloud.com/player?url=http%3A%2F%2Fapi.soundcloud.com%2Fplaylists%2F9510369" width="100%">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          The Replacements guitarist, Slim Dunlap, had a stroke last year, and the reason the band decided to reunite was to show support for Dunlap. The
          <a href="http://www.mxdwn.com/2013/06/15/news/the-replacements-auctioning-track-featuring-john-doe-deer-tick-vanessa-carlton-and-scott-lucas-in-songs-for-slim-project/">
           non-profit fundraiser Songs for Slim
          </a>
          had artists covering Dunlaps songs and auctioning them to the public, then giving the proceeds to Dunlaps family.
         </p>
         <p>
          The reunion isnt the only exciting news from the rock veterans  their last album,
          <em>
           All Shook Down
          </em>
          , will finally be
          <a href="http://www.mxdwn.com/2013/08/25/news/the-replacements-announce-final-studio-album-all-shook-down-to-receive-first-ever-us-vinyl-pressing-for-reissue-to-be-released-november-2013/">
           pressed to vinyl
          </a>
          in the U.S. and reissued in November. The album was originally released in 1990.
         </p>
