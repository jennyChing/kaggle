             <p>
              <a data-rel="lightbox-0" href="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/26-Horror-Flix-Netflix.jpg">
               <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112880 aligncenter" height="359" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/26-Horror-Flix-Netflix-650x359.jpg" width="650"/>

             <p>
              Halloween might be done and over with, but that doesnt mean you have to stop watching horror flicks! Keep the scary and ghostly alive through November and catch the latest horror flicks that are streaming on Netflix. Itll be like Halloween never ended! Just remember to leave a night light on cause some of these flicks will scare the living sh*t out of you!
             </p>

             <h3>
              26. Session 9
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112751 aligncenter" height="929" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/SESSION_9-650x929.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              As a psychological horror film,
              <em>
               Session 9
              </em>
              will scare you and mess with your head. Its mainly about an asbestos removal crew who works at an abandoned mental asylum, which obviously means crazy ass ghosts!
             </p>
             <h3>
              25. The Fly
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112752 aligncenter" height="1027" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/fly_poster-650x1027.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              Brilliant scientists are always crazy and this one turns himself into a hideous fly after an experiment goes wrong. Gross and horrifying in one flick! Be afraid, be very afraidand have a fly swatter handy just in case!
             </p>
             <h3>
              24. Interview with a Vampire
             </h3>
             <h3>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112753 aligncenter" height="487" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Interview-with-the-vampire-vampires-25076180-1024-768-650x487.jpg" width="650"/>
             </h3>
             <h3>
              Twilight has nothing on this flick that stars Brad Pitt, Tom Cruise and Christian Slater in a very intriguing vampire flick full of horror, sex and more!
             </h3>
             <h3>
              23. Lost Boys
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112754 aligncenter" height="406" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Lost-Boys-Wallpaper-the-lost-boys-movie-1969044-1280-800-650x406.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               Ahh
              </em>
              , an 80s classic about vampires that are cool and a nerdy brother and his friends who try to help out an older bro from becoming one of the undead. Not so much horror, but definitely lots of fun!
             </p>
             <h3>
              22. Let the Right One In
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112755 aligncenter" height="963" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/let_the_right_one_in_poster-650x963.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              Another vampire love storyor is it? Watch to find out!
             </p>
             <h3>
              21. Night of the Living Dead
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112756 aligncenter" height="487" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/night-of-the-living-dead-1-650x487.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              The original 1968 black-and-white zombie flick will scare the living day light out of youand make you start thinking about a zombie survival kit!
             </p>
             <h3>
              20. Paranormal Entity
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112757 aligncenter" height="941" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/ParanormalEntity-650x941.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              Hmmm, were not sure about the claim on actual footage from the supernatural events leading up to the 2008 murder of Samantha Finley, but its still some scary sh*t!
             </p>
             <h3>
              19. Monkey Shines
             </h3>
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-112758" height="744" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/monkey-shines-mid.jpg" title="monkey-shines-mid" width="500"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              This movie will have you thinking twice about owning your own pet monkey. Not all play nice.
             </p>
             <h3>
              18. Werewolf in London
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112759 aligncenter" height="513" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/poster-werewolf-of-london-1935_03-650x513.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              An oldie but goodie, but dont be too disappointed on the special effectsit was 1935 for goodness sakes!
             </p>
             <h3>
              17. From Dusk till Dawn
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112760 aligncenter" height="943" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/From_Dusk_Till_Dawn_poster_by_smalltownhero-650x943.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              When Quentin Tarantino and Robert Rodriguez team up, you know youll in for a movie treat. The babalicious Selma Hayek is an added bonus to this vampire satire.
             </p>
             <h3>
              16. Wake the Witch
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-112761 aligncenter" height="472" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/WakeTheWitch.jpg" width="629"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              This movie has a lesson to it: dont piss off witches cause theyll come back from the dead to kill your ass!
             </p>
             <h3>
              15. They Live
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112762 aligncenter" height="967" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/they-live-poster-650x967.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              Sort of sci-fi and horror, a drifter discovers that a pair of sunglasses he found allows him to see that aliens have taken over the Earth.
             </p>
             <h3>
              14. The Crazies
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112764 aligncenter" height="520" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/the_crazies_wallpaper_3_1024-650x520.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              The name should speak for itself! When a small town is plagued by a mysterious toxin, all hell breaks lose with crazies!
             </p>
             <h3>
              13. The Ghouls
             </h3>
             <p>
              <span style="text-align: center; direction: ltr;">
               <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112988 aligncenter" height="908" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/the-Ghouls-650x908.jpg" title="the Ghouls" width="650"/>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="text-align: center; direction: ltr;">
               A power hungry journalist learns that theres something beneath the streets of L.A. and they dont play nice!
              </span>
             </p>
             <h3>
              12. Paranormal Activity 2
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112766 aligncenter" height="925" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/936full-paranormal-activity-2-cover-650x925.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              While still not real, its scary as hell and will have you on the edge of your seat and maybe under your covers when you go to bed.
             </p>
             <h3>
              11. The Evil Dead
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112767 aligncenter" height="857" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/evil_dead_blu-ray1-650x857.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              Five friends travel to a cabin in the woods, where they unknowingly release flesh-possessing demonslesson here is dont release anything that you think is scary.
             </p>
             <h3>
              10. Puppet Master
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-112768 aligncenter" height="927" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/228026.1020.A.jpg" width="580"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              Puppets are scary on their own, but when they are cursed, theyre even worse. Put away your dolls after watching this one.
             </p>
             <h3>
              9. Human Centipede: First Sequence
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-112769 aligncenter" height="800" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Human-Centiped-poster.jpg" width="600"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              All we have to say is that its seriously some disturbing stuff that will totally mess with your mind. Gross and soooooo horrible!
             </p>
             <h3>
              8. The Others
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112770 aligncenter" height="921" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/the-others-original-650x921.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              Haunted houses always make the best horror flicks!
             </p>
             <h3>
              7. Trick r Treat
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112771 aligncenter" height="487" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Trick-r-treat-trick-r-treat-9240010-1280-960-650x487.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              Four interwoven stories that occur on Halloween and each one is scary and disturbinga horror flick at its best!
             </p>
             <h3>
              6. Halloween: Resurrection
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112773 aligncenter" height="969" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/halloween_resurrection_ver2_xlg-650x969.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              Just when you thought Michael Myers was dead
             </p>
             <h3>
              5. Saw: The Final Chapter
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112774 aligncenter" height="762" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/saw-the-final-chapter-blu-ray-cover-art-1-650x762.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              Jigsaws brutal legacy continues.
             </p>
             <h3>
              4. The Last Exorcism
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112776 aligncenter" height="487" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Last-Exorcism-final-UK-quad-650x487.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              A troubled evangelical minister agrees to let his last exorcism be filmed by a documentary crew. Dont see this alone cause you wont sleep.
             </p>
             <h3>
              3. The Exorcist
             </h3>
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112777 aligncenter" height="487" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/exorcist-650x487.jpg" width="650"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              How can we forget this horror classic! Makes you think what if you were possessed!?
             </p>
             <h3>
              2. Childs Play
             </h3>
             <p>
              <a data-rel="lightbox-1" href="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Childs-Play.jpg">
               <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-112881 aligncenter" height="447" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Childs-Play.jpg" width="649"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p>
              Dolls arent always nice to play withtake Chuckie, for example.
             </p>
             <h3>
              1. Poltergeist II
             </h3>
             <p>
              <span style="direction: ltr;">
               <img alt="" class="size-large wp-image-112986 aligncenter" height="519" src="http://gadgetreview.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Poltergeist-2-650x519.jpg" title="Poltergeist 2" width="650"/>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="direction: ltr;">
               Still one of the scariest films ever made and part II is even scarier!
              </span>
             </p>

		var regex = new RegExp("[\\?&amp;]" + name + "=([^&amp;#]*)"),
		results = regex.exec(location.search);
		return results === null ? "" : decodeURIComponent(results[1].replace(/\+/g, " "));
	}
