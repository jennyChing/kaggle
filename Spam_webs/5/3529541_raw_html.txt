        <p>
         <img alt="core workout" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-7991" height="409" src="http://classpass.wpengine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/core_switchuproutine-640x409.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         You probably know that the key to being fit is eating healthy and exercising. However, many people struggle with not losing weight despite frequent gym visits. The gym can be great for a run or another machine workout. The challenge is that we often find ourselves stuck in a routine. We do the same things at the gym every time. The problem isyour body gets used to it and you reach a plateau.
        </p>
        <p>
         The key to losing weight is not just regular exercise, but varied exercise.
         <b>
          Switch it up!
         </b>
         By using different muscles and working different parts of your body, your system gets shocked and the pounds melt off! It worked for me, and it will for you too if you give it a try.
         <a href="http://classpass.com/?utm_source=facebook&amp;utm_medium=cpc&amp;utm_campaign=bos&amp;utm_content=1675">
          ClassPass has made me enjoy working out again!
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <a href="http://classpass.com/?utm_source=facebook&amp;utm_medium=cpc&amp;utm_campaign=bos&amp;utm_content=1675">
           Charlestown Cycling Club
          </a>
         </b>
         is a phenomenal workout. You can jam out to a great beat while getting your heart beating too. I left my last session feeling energized and ready to take on the world. Who can complain about a workout like that?
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <a href="http://classpass.com/?utm_source=facebook&amp;utm_medium=cpc&amp;utm_campaign=bos&amp;utm_content=1675">
           Boston Body
          </a>
         </b>
         is a killer pilates class. The last one I went to was early one morning before work, so a small class. We spent the whole time on foam rollers, pulling a contraption out I never even knew existed. My balance and coordination were tested and my muscles worked with every move.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you can resist a cannoli before headed to
         <b>
          <a href="http://classpass.com/?utm_source=facebook&amp;utm_medium=cpc&amp;utm_campaign=bos&amp;utm_content=1675">
           Exhale Battery Wharf
          </a>
         </b>
         , youre in for a supreme yoga class. Your week of stress will quickly become your hour of relaxation, and whats better  youll take that feeling with you the whole day.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Lose weight and have fun by trying something new10 classes for $99/month!
         </b>
        </p>
