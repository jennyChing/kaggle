        <a href="http://notalwaysworking.com/sending-her-anxiety-the-way-of-the-dinosaurs/37645" rel="bookmark">
         Sending Her Anxiety The Way Of The Dinosaurs
        </a>
       </h3>
       <div class="post_header">
        <a href="http://notalwaysworking.com/tag/cafe" rel="tag">
         Cafe
        </a>
        | England, UK |
        <a href="http://notalwaysworking.com/category/awesome-workers" rel="category tag">
         Awesome Workers
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://notalwaysworking.com/category/family-kids" rel="category tag">
         Family &amp; Kids
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://notalwaysworking.com/category/food-drink" rel="category tag">

       <div class="storycontent">
        <div class="at-above-post addthis-toolbox at-wordpress-hide" data-title="Sending Her Anxiety The Way Of The Dinosaurs" data-url="http://notalwaysworking.com/sending-her-anxiety-the-way-of-the-dinosaurs/37645">
        </div>
        <div class="at-above-post-recommended addthis-toolbox at-wordpress-hide">
        </div>
        <p>
         <i>
          (Im a young adult with severe anxiety issues, so I always get a bit nervous going out in public. Once a week I meet my sister at a local cafe, but because I have to wait for her to arrive Im always worried Ill get called out for not having ordered anything when I sit down. I also always carry a stuffed toy dinosaur with me for comfort. When a server comes over to me, I think Im about to be kicked out, so I probably look terrified.)
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Server:
         </b>
         <i>
          *speaking gently*
         </i>
         You know, I see you come in here a lot, and I just wanted to say I love your dinosaur! Seeing him always makes me want to work extra hard. He makes me so happy.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Me:
         </b>
         Well he is a carnivore, so if you slack off hell probably eat you.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Server:
         </b>
         Oh, consider myself warned! And I just wanted you to know you dont have to be nervous, dear. We know your friend is coming. Is she buying you cake today?
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Me:
         </b>
         <i>
          *nods*
         </i>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Server:
         </b>
         Good for you. Id better get back to work before I get eaten!
        </p>
        <p>
         <i>
          (She smiles at me every time I come in now, and Im no longer nervous about sitting and waiting for my sister!)
         </i>
        </p>
