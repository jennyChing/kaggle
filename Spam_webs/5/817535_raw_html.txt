        <div class="scrolling_cont" id="conta" style="position:absolute; width: 284px; left: 0px; top: 0px;">
         <p>
          Lifestyle Homes is a group housing project spread across 12 acres in Sector 83, Gurgaon. Its architecture blends elegance with contemporary styling, in an innovative design that resonates perfectly with the modern, urban way of life.
         </p>
         <p>
          This residential gated community is a combination of high-rise and mid-rise structures. A wide range of compact, efficient and stylish homes is available, ranging from single-bedroom apartments to three-bedroom apartments with store and servant room.
         </p>
         <p>
          The layout of the project has a large Central Park at its heart, and its design is such that each residence receives plenty of light and cross-ventilation. An exceptional living experience is at the heart of the projects design, making this one of the most interesting residential projects in Gurgaon. The three-bedroom units enjoy a garden view as well as a boulevard view, and all balconies are furnished with screens for shade and extra privacy.
         </p>
         <p>
          Residents will enjoy a rich and vibrant lifestyle, with an accent on community living. There is a school inside the project premises, and the Lifestyle Homes community will have all the amenities for a contented and contemporary lifestyle  a retail facility, a modern gym, a swimming pool, a multi-purpose hall and play areas for children.
         </p>
