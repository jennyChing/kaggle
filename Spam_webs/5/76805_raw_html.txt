           <h1>
            Waynes Comics Podcast #161: Jeremy Haun
           </h1>
           <span class="author">
            by
            <span itemprop="author">
             Wayne Hall
            </span>
           </span>
           <div class="featured-panel">
            <div class="inner-content">
             <div class="featured-image-wrapper">
              <div class="featured-image-inner">
               <img alt="Wayne's Comics, Wayne Hall, Jeremy Haun, Bad Karma, Dino Day Art Book, Constantine, dinosaur, Batwoman" class="attachment-single-790 wp-post-image" height="414" itemprop="image" src="http://majorspoilers.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/HaunBanner1.jpg" title="Waynes Comics Podcast #161: Jeremy Haun" width="750"/>
              </div>

           <div class="the-content">
            <p>
             This week, youll enjoy the return of Jeremy Haun, probably best known for his work on DCs
             <em>
              Constantine
             </em>
             and
             <em>
              Batwoman
             </em>
             as well as the
             <em>
              Bad Karma
             </em>
             volume!
            </p>
            <p>
             Jeremys in the middle of a Kickstarter project called Dino Day Art Book. This excellent volume started out as a way for Mr. Haun to share his work with his young sons, who both love dinosaurs. Each Wednesday on his blog, Jeremy posted a new dinosaur illustration. Now, the entire years artwork is being collected into a hardcover volume you can only obtain by supporting this project. To contribute, be sure to go to
             <a href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/jerhaun/dino-day-art-book-by-jeremy-haun">
              this link
             </a>
             ! We also get an update about Mr. Hauns current and future projects, so be sure not to miss what he has to say!
            </p>
