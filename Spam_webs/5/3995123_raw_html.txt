      <h1>
       <a href="http://www.skiptomylou.org/2008/12/31/how-to-make-newspaper-party-hats/">
        How to Make Newspaper Party Hats
       </a>
      </h1>
      <a class="surprise_me" href="/?random">
       <span>
        Surprise Me!
       </span>
      </a>
     </header>
     <p>
      Here is a quick way to make last minute party hats for New Years Eve. We made two different styles of newspaper hats.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/kids-in-hats.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5139" height="479" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/kids-in-hats.jpg" title="kids-in-hats" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Childs sized Newspaper Party Hat
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Use a whole sheet of newspaper
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-1.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5140" height="560" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-1.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-1" width="420"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Fold in half
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-2.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5141" height="428" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-2.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-2" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Fold each of the top corners to the center
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-3.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5142" height="343" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-3.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-3" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Fold top layer at bottom up
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-4.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5143" height="574" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-4.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-4" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Fold it up again
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-5.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5144" height="385" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-5.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-5" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Turn newspaper over and fold up bottom
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-6.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5145" height="534" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-6.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-6" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Fold it up again (like other side)
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-7.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5146" height="486" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-7.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-7" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Finished!
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-8.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5147" height="362" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-8.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-8" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Bishops Hat Folded from Newspaper
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Use one whole sheet of newspaper
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-2a.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5148" height="237" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-2a.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-2a" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Bring top corners to center
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-3a.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5149" height="231" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-3a.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-3a" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Fold top layer at the bottom up one time
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-4a.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5150" height="248" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-4a.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-4a" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-5a.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5151" height="224" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-5a.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-5a" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Turn newspaper over and bring corner of hat to the center
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-7a.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5152" height="545" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-7a.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-7a" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Bring other corner to the center
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-6a.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5153" height="319" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-6a.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-6a" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Fold piece at bottom up
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-8a.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5154" height="445" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-8a.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-8a" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Finished!
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-9a.jpg">
       <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5155" height="485" src="http://assets.skiptomylou.org/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/newspaper-hat-9a.jpg" title="newspaper-hat-9a" width="425"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Now youre ready to party!
     </p>
