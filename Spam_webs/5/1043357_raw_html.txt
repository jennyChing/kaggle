             <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
              Flower Shop Dramatic Play
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5113073790740228763" itemprop="articleBody">
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-lw-kpur6HS0/VT2azLkE08I/AAAAAAAAIKk/Hnl4QjLo-B4/s1600/0%2BFlower%2BShop.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Play to Learn Preschool" border="0" height="640" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-lw-kpur6HS0/VT2azLkE08I/AAAAAAAAIKk/Hnl4QjLo-B4/s1600/0%2BFlower%2BShop.jpg" title="Flower Shop Dramatic Play Center" width="448"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              Each month we like to spruce up our
              <a href="http://playtolearnpreschool.blogspot.com/2014/08/kitchen-refresh.html" target="_blank">
               basic dramatic play center
              </a>
              and make it into something wonderful. To celebrate spring, we've transformed it into a Flower Shop.
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-RneUN_QXmGY/VT2azStcZ1I/AAAAAAAAIKo/5gBFauNRRDk/s1600/20150419_165449.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-RneUN_QXmGY/VT2azStcZ1I/AAAAAAAAIKo/5gBFauNRRDk/s1600/20150419_165449.jpg" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              Of course, I headed right to the dollar store to get all the supplies that we would need! I chose 4 different types of flowers. They come in bunches of 6 stems for $1. You can't beat that! I cut them apart and reinforced the flowers and leaves with a dot of super glue.
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-F3tBVl5HmoE/VT2azacvTRI/AAAAAAAAIKs/lbxoao-jDIA/s1600/20150419_172736.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-F3tBVl5HmoE/VT2azacvTRI/AAAAAAAAIKs/lbxoao-jDIA/s1600/20150419_172736.jpg" width="534"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               I also picked up some plastic pots and foam circles. The foam fits perfectly into the bottom of the pots.
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-bvZI3r1CEf0/VT2a2Wt9utI/AAAAAAAAILY/liYnw3TVyaA/s1600/IMG_9563.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-bvZI3r1CEf0/VT2a2Wt9utI/AAAAAAAAILY/liYnw3TVyaA/s1600/IMG_9563.JPG" width="426"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               We labeled each pot of flowers with a price tag for the store.
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-IrPL22gX2cI/VT2a0tnlynI/AAAAAAAAIK4/XCso_AeuMhU/s1600/IMG_9524.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="350" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-IrPL22gX2cI/VT2a0tnlynI/AAAAAAAAIK4/XCso_AeuMhU/s1600/IMG_9524.JPG" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              One of the things that really makes our dramatic play centers work well is the use of role playing name tags. For the flower shop, we need a florist, a delivery driver, a cashier, a gardener, and a bunch of customers!
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-SBA9TbT4HTU/VT2a0zfb-zI/AAAAAAAAIK8/FpxBfV2VA74/s1600/IMG_9526.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="426" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-SBA9TbT4HTU/VT2a0zfb-zI/AAAAAAAAIK8/FpxBfV2VA74/s1600/IMG_9526.JPG" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               Almost all of the supplies that we need came from the dollar store: watering cans,
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-6OuufHtzR1A/VT2a1W8VggI/AAAAAAAAILE/sj7So-B9dOo/s1600/IMG_9550.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="470" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-6OuufHtzR1A/VT2a1W8VggI/AAAAAAAAILE/sj7So-B9dOo/s1600/IMG_9550.JPG" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              ribbon, tissue paper,
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-UBlxAB3zelc/VT2a1UePM9I/AAAAAAAAILI/BJdvre8LEwU/s1600/IMG_9552.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="352" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-UBlxAB3zelc/VT2a1UePM9I/AAAAAAAAILI/BJdvre8LEwU/s1600/IMG_9552.JPG" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              and plastic "fancy" vases.
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-z1N2MYnZVfE/VT2a1l_3kiI/AAAAAAAAILM/smiFnByXPHo/s1600/IMG_9554.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="508" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-z1N2MYnZVfE/VT2a1l_3kiI/AAAAAAAAILM/smiFnByXPHo/s1600/IMG_9554.JPG" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               It's important to include many opportunities for reading and writing in dramatic play. At the flower shop, students can write down their orders before heading into the store. Our florist also uses these forms for phone orders!
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-X0zYD_QhY2A/VT2a1G7dRJI/AAAAAAAAILA/JJDVZ2Fyd-M/s1600/IMG_9537.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="440" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-X0zYD_QhY2A/VT2a1G7dRJI/AAAAAAAAILA/JJDVZ2Fyd-M/s1600/IMG_9537.JPG" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               Would you like these wrapped into a bouquet or arranged in a vase?
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-8gCiyqNJs2g/VT2a0MxhvhI/AAAAAAAAIK0/L_iM0SounJ8/s1600/IMG_9444.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-8gCiyqNJs2g/VT2a0MxhvhI/AAAAAAAAIK0/L_iM0SounJ8/s1600/IMG_9444.jpg" width="496"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               What color ribbon would you like for your bouquet?
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-X3wTKnii2hk/VT2a0J4qvHI/AAAAAAAAIKw/dBa7tC3tWHE/s1600/IMG_9366.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="486" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-X3wTKnii2hk/VT2a0J4qvHI/AAAAAAAAIKw/dBa7tC3tWHE/s1600/IMG_9366.jpg" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              Finally, we added a variety of blank cards to the writing center. They are the perfect addition to a dozen roses for Mother's Day!
