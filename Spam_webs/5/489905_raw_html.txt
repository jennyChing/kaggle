               <div style="text-align: center;">
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #e06666; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <b style="margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">

                   <br/>
                  </span>
                 </b>
                </span>

               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="https://images-blogger-opensocial.googleusercontent.com/gadgets/proxy?url=http%3A%2F%2F2.bp.blogspot.com%2F-WV1OdcevsKY%2FU6Th7M8RhuI%2FAAAAAAAAAcg%2FWjTu0PhQ6Nk%2Fs1600%2FSketch%2BUp%2BSaturday.png&amp;container=blogger&amp;gadget=a&amp;rewriteMime=image%2F*" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                 </span>
                </a>
               </div>
               <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #e06666; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <b style="margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                  What is Sketch-Up-Saturday?
                 </b>
                </span>
                <br style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;"/>
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #666666; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 It's basically us fangirls sketching up our own drawing and imagination of the characters, from a book we recently fell in love with! And then, after the sketching, we showcase it on our blog once a week, every Saturday.
                </span>
                <br style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;"/>
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #666666; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <br style="margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;"/>
                </span>
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px;">
                </span>
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #666666; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 Simple, right? Simple? Yes?
                </span>
                <br style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;"/>
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #666666; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <br style="margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;"/>
                </span>
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px;">
                </span>
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #666666; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 So hop right in and join if you're interested :)
                </span>
                <br style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;"/>
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #666666; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 Or if you don't, well, I'll be sobbing in the back, but feel free to browse my creation here!
                </span>
                <br style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;"/>
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #666666; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <br style="margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;"/>
                </span>
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px;">
                </span>
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <b style="margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                  <span style="color: #e06666; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                   This week on Sketch-Up-Saturday, I'll be featuring one of this book that has a crazy amount of feeling on it.
                  </span>
                 </b>
                 <span style="color: #666666; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                  Again like last week, it's retelling, with a more ferocious kind of story compared to the original, but still perfect nonetheless.
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
               <br/>
               <span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #666666; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                  Wanna guess what it is? Here's a little teaser :
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <div style="text-align: center;">
                <span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <span style="color: #666666; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                  <img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ezxBdvPXo0c/U6TjZFf9VGI/AAAAAAAAAc8/6cONSJPOP9A/s1600/4.jpg"/>

                </span>
                <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-u8BppUmg7LQ/U6TjaKVTxGI/AAAAAAAAAdE/KATR-69AdhA/s1600/5.jpg"/>

               <span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">

                  <br/>

               </span>
               <span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #666666; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                  Does that tiny little winged thing, completed with all the sparkly feeling reminds you of something?
                  <br/>
                  Today's lucky book is:
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <div style="text-align: center;">
                <span style="background-color: white; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <span style="margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                  <b>
                   <span style="color: #e06666; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                    TIGER LILY by Jodi Lynn Anderson
                   </span>
                  </b>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </div>
               <span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #666666; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
               <span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #666666; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                  I'm never a big fan of Peter Pan myself, but this book managed to make me completely fallen in love with the story, to the point I even put the quote on my slider up there permanently :D
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
               <br/>
               <span style="background-color: white; color: #444444; line-height: 20.399999618530273px; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                <span style="color: #666666; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px;">
                 <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                  This time, I draw our lovely couple that I'll ship forever, Tiger Lily and Peter Pan, all with just mechanic pencil again.
                 </span>
                </span>

                 I got a little lazy with their tiny little fingers, so I left it out altogether. And I'm actually not so good at drawing male, but I think this Peter Pan turns out quite nicely haha. And oh, I also decide to use a different approach on Tiger Lily's face, making it softer and all.
