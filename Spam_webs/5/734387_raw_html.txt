             <section class="article-content">
              <p>
               Last week, we asked for
               <em>
                Mashable
               </em>
               readers to vote in polls to decide the best
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/12/09/vote-for-song-of-2013/">
                song
               </a>
               ,
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/12/10/best-tv-shows-2013/">
                TV show
               </a>
               ,
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/12/11/best-movie-of-2013/">
                movie
               </a>
               ,
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/12/12/vote-best-sports-moment-2013/">
                sports moment
               </a>
               , and
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/12/14/vote-for-video-game-2013/">
                video game
               </a>
               of 2013.
              </p>
              <p>
               After 30,000 votes across the polls, the results are in. Here are this year's champions of entertainment, according to your votes.
              </p>
              <h2>
               Song: 'Get Lucky' by Daft Punk feat. Pharrell
              </h2>
              <p>
               <iframe class="vine-embed" frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/h0Z1p26D2v9/embed/simple" width="600">
               </iframe>
              </p>
              <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.vine.co/static/scripts/embed.js">
              </script>
              <p>
               How big a hit was Daft Punk's "Get Lucky" when it came out? Spotify's
               <a _target="blank" href="https://www.spotify.com/us/2013/" target="_blank">
                Year in Review
               </a>
               reports that it was streamed 1.5 million times in a day. The combination of Pharrell Williams' smooth vocals and the feel-good beat gave the song mass appeal, and it has remained a staple of 2013 all year long. Here are the top 5 vote-getters in our poll:
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <p>
                 "Get Lucky" by Daft Punk feat. Pharrell Williams (8.8%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 "Royals" by Lorde (6.7%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 "Roar" by Katy Perry (5.6%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 "Wake Me Up" by Avicii (5.2%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 "Wrecking Ball" by Miley Cyrus (5.1%)
                </p>
               </li>
              </ul>
              <h2>
               TV Show: 'Breaking Bad'
              </h2>
              <p>
               <iframe class="vine-embed" frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/h050HPzVPjB/embed/simple" width="600">
               </iframe>
              </p>
              <p>
               While it didn't capture a pure 99.1% of the vote,
               <em>
                <a href="http://mashable.com/category/breaking-bad/">
                 Breaking Bad
                </a>
               </em>
               won the poll for best TV show by a wide margin. After the show took over
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/10/29/walter-white-chemistry-jokes-meme/">
                pop culture
               </a>
               and saw over 10 million people tune into its thrilling conclusion, this result didn't come as much of a surprise to us. The shows that received the most votes were:
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  Breaking Bad
                 </em>
                 (19.0%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  Game of Thrones
                 </em>
                 (11.0%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  The Walking Dead
                 </em>
                 (7.9%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  House of Cards
                 </em>
                 (5.6%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  Modern Family
                 </em>
                 (4.9%)
                </p>
               </li>
              </ul>
              <h2>
               Movie: 'Gravity'
              </h2>
              <p>
               <iframe class="vine-embed" frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/h0pmmbPTtz9/embed/simple" width="600">
               </iframe>
              </p>
              <p>
               Alfonso Cuarn's
               <em>
                <a href="http://mashable.com/category/gravity/">
                 Gravity
                </a>
               </em>
               achieved the dual success of being a
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/10/07/gravity-box-office/">
                record-breaking blockbuster
               </a>
               and garnering critical acclaim across the board. Viewers were held breathless as they watched the stressful space venture, and it looks to be a frontrunner in several categories at the Oscars. Rounding out the top 5 were:
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  Gravity
                 </em>
                 (13.5%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  The Hunger Games: Catching Fire
                 </em>
                 (11.2%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  Iron Man 3
                 </em>
                 (6.8%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  Star Trek Into Darkness
                 </em>
                 (5.8%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  Man of Steel
                 </em>
                 (5.2%)
                </p>
               </li>
              </ul>
              <h2>
               Sports Moment: Auburn's Game-Winning Field Goal Return
              </h2>
              <p>
               <iframe class="vine-embed" frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/h0226KApMMV/embed/simple" width="600">
               </iframe>
              </p>
              <p>
               This was the play that took over the Internet, had non-sports fans talking about it and turned a previously unknown player into a
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/12/02/auburn-football-chris-davis-class-twitter/">
                campus celebrity
               </a>
               . What originally looked to be nothing more than a missed field goal turned into one of the most improbable endings to a game in sports history, and it saw Alabama lose the No. 1 ranking they had held all season long. Here are the five moments that got the most votes:
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <p>
                 Auburn's Game-Winning FG Return vs. Alabama (26.3%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 Andy Murray Becomes First British Man to Win Wimbledon in 77 Years (15.6%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 David Ortiz's Grand Slam Against Tigers in ALCS (7.8%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 Ray Allen's Buzzer-Beater in Game 6 of NBA Finals (7.6%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 Bruins Crowd Sings National Anthem at First Game After Boston Bombing (7.0%)
                </p>
               </li>
              </ul>
              <h2>
               Video Game: 'Grand Theft Auto V'
              </h2>
              <p>
               <iframe class="vine-embed" frameborder="0" height="600" src="https://vine.co/v/hEB6h3JexWO/embed/simple" width="600">
               </iframe>
              </p>
              <p>
               We expected
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/grand-theft-auto-v/">
                Grand Theft Auto V
               </a>
               to be the frontrunner in the gaming category, but we did not foresee it collecting nearly half of the overall votes. Rockstar's latest installment in the highly popular franchise broke
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2013/10/08/guinness-gta-v-guinness-records/">
                several Guinness World Records
               </a>
               within days of its release, and it won its category by the largest margin of victory out of all the polls. The top 5 games were:
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  Grand Theft Auto V
                 </em>
                 (44.5%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  The Last of Us
                 </em>
                 (9.6%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  Battlefield 4
                 </em>
                 (7.0%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag
                 </em>
                 (5.8%)
                </p>
               </li>
               <li>
                <p>
                 <em>
                  Bioshock Infinite
                 </em>
                 (5.5%)
                </p>
               </li>
              </ul>
