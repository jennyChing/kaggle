                             I posted this in my other thread, but I figured it was worth sharing.                              <br/>

                             <br/>
                             Day 6  P90X Kenpo X or Insanity Max Cardio Circuit (Optional)
                             <br/>

                             Phase 3 (4 Weeks + 1 Recovery Week)
