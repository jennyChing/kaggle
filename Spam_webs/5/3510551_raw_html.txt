      <h2>
       How to make the Droid sound
       <span class="date">
        SEP 17 2010
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       If you've got a Mac, the "droid" sound that Android phones make -- yep, the one from
       <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cmk4V6pe5Oc">
        the commercials
       </a>
       -- can be produced in the following manner:
      </p>
      <p>
       1. Open Terminal.app
       <br/>
       2. Type
       <em>
        say -v "Cellos" "droid"
       </em>
       at the prompt
       <br/>
       3. Experiment:
       <em>
        say -v "Cellos" "droid. sucks."
       </em>
       <br/>
       4. Or
       <em>
        say -v "Cellos" "droid want to be iphone when droid grow up"
       </em>
       <br/>
       5. And finally,
       <em>
        say "i am trying to unlock the mysteries of the universe, like how the big bang happened and where all the lost socks go after being in the dryer that really makes me mad"
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       (via
       <a href="http://twitter.com/mikeysan/status/24719627536">
        @mikeysan
       </a>
       )
      </p>
