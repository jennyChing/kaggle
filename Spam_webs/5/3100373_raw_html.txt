          <p>
           Just say no to
           <a href="http://www.fda.gov/food/recallsoutbreaksemergencies/safetyalertsadvisories/ucm405787.htm" target="_blank">
            pure powdered caffeine
           </a>
           , the FDA warned, citing the risk  --  especially to those with a pre-existing heart condition  --  and ease of overdose.
          </p>
          <p>
           Even
           <a href="http://content.onlinejacc.org/article.aspx?articleid=1889060" target="_blank">
            moderate amounts of alcohol boost atrial fibrillation risk
           </a>
           , although that has to be balanced against the other heart benefits of a little wine, according to a study in the
           <em>
            Journal of the American College of Cardiology
           </em>
           .
          </p>
