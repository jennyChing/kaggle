         PRESS RELEASE
         <br/>
         <br/>
         American Atheists, Inc.
         <br/>
         <a class="external" href="https://atheists.org/www.atheists.org" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          www.atheists.org
         </a>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         For Immediate Release: April 23, 2013
         <br/>
         <br/>
         For More Information Contact:
         <br/>
         David Silverman, President 908-276-7300 x5,
         <a href="mailto:dsilverman@atheists.org">
          dsilverman@atheists.org
         </a>
         <br/>
         Dave Muscato, Public Relations Director 908-276-7300 x7,
         <a href="mailto:dmuscato@atheists.org">
          dmuscato@atheists.org
         </a>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          Atheists Denounce Religion as Reason for Boston Terror
         </span>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Cranford, NJAmerican Atheists today condemns suspect Dzhokhar Tsarnaevs statement to police that defending Islam was the motivating factor behind the bombings during the Boston Marathon. Of the two suspects identified by police, the surviving younger brother, Dzhokhar, has communicated to police that the motivation for the attacks was his older brother Tamerlans desire to defend his faith.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         There are no religious reasonsonly religious excuses. The excuse of religion does not make people less accountable for their actions and in no way justifies harming innocent people. American Atheists joins our brothers and sisters worldwide in condemning these attacks, said President David Silverman. Let us not forget what religion can doit helps bad people convince themselves that bad actions are good if they are committed in the name of their god.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         AMERICAN ATHEISTS is a national 501(c)(3) organization that defends civil rights for atheists, freethinkers, and other nonbelievers; works for the total separation of religion and government; and addresses issues of First Amendment public policy. American Atheists celebrates its 50th anniversary this year.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         American Atheists, Inc.
         <br/>
         P.O. Box 158
         <br/>
         Cranford, NJ 07016
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Tel: (908) 276-7300
         <br/>
         Fax: (908) 276-7402
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Keywords: American Atheists, Boston, marathon, Boston Marathon, explosion, attack, Islam, Muslim, terrorism, faith, atheism, religion
