       <p>
        <span style="color: #ff0000;">
         <strong>
          DiversityJobs.coms scholarshipprogram is open to all diversity and minority students
         </strong>
        </span>
        <span style="color: #000000;">
         , including Hispanics, African Americans, Asian Americans, Native Americans, military veterans, women, people with disabilities, and members of the LGBT community. DiversityJobs.com awards $3,000 annually
        </span>
        to one student per year.  Applicants must be currently enrolled in an undergrad or graduate program at one of our registered schools in the US. The next application deadline is April 30, 2016. There is NO application fee. Click here to see
        <a href="http://streetsmart.diversityjobs.com/c/scholarship/">
         past award finalists
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <img alt="diversity training" class="alignright" height="190" src="http://academy.justjobs.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/diversity-training.png" title="diversity training" width="365"/>
         For students to be eligible, their school must be registered.
        </strong>
        Check our
        <a href="http://streetsmart.diversityjobs.com/registered-schools/">
         registered schools list
        </a>
         if your school is not registered, just ask your schools Financial Aid office to register today (its quick and easy). Please also
        <a href="mailto:scholarships@diversityjobs.com">
         contact us
        </a>
        with any questions.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Eligibility
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Students must:
       </p>
       <ol>
        <li>
         Be enrolled in AND currently attending a school that has registered forDiversityJobs scholarship program. Check our
         <a href="http://streetsmart.diversityjobs.com/registered-schools/">
          registered schools list
         </a>
         .
        </li>
        <li>
         Be a full-time student when applying for the scholarship.
        </li>
        <li>
         Like us
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/DiversityJobscom" target="_blank">
          on Facebook
         </a>
         .
        </li>
        <li>
         Complete the
         <a href="http://streetsmart.diversityjobs.com/scholarship-application/">
          scholarship application
         </a>
         .
        </li>
       </ol>
       <p>
        Schools must:
       </p>
       <ol>
        <li>
         Be registered forDiversityJobs scholarship program and appear on our
         <a href="http://streetsmart.diversityjobs.com/registered-schools/">
          registered schools list
         </a>
         .
        </li>
       </ol>
       <p>
        <img alt="dj_schol_sentence" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1419" height="44" src="http://streetsmart.diversityjobs.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/dj_schol_sentence.png" width="743"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Award Criteria
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        In the weeks following the scholarship deadline, finalists will be chosen and their essays published on
        <a href="http://streetsmart.diversityjobs.com/blog/" target="_blank">
         our blog
        </a>
        for the final selection process. The scholarship award winner will be determined by the DiversityJobs Scholarship Committee and announced within 2 months of the scholarship deadline.
       </p>
