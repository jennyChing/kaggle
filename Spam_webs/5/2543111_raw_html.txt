      <a id="dd_start">

      <p>
       Gmail is awidely used email service provided by Google. Its popularity and simplicity makes handling emailing easy.
      </p>
      <p>
       Even though it is so popular and a cakewalk experience to use, it lacks some functionality and attributes which could make your mailing job, quite quick and simpler. Many independent developers came up with some excellent Gmail Extension for the web browsers to give a fantabulous experience to the Gmail users.
      </p>
      <p>
       Mentioned below are five new Gmail extensions, you should try to make your mailing easy, quick and hassle free.
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Framebench
       </b>
       : It is one of the most amazing Gmail extensions to communicate with your client and online team. Framebench gives you the ability to visually interact with your team to get their feedback. It supports file formats such as images, videos and PDFs.
      </p>
      <p>
       It prevents you from the mess of going through different email threads to understand their review and feedback, as Framebench gives you advantage to interact with your peers on real time basis through the chatbox while they give feedback with marking changes required on your creative. It provides you with the option of annotate, draw, pan and zoom on your design.
      </p>
      <p>
       Framebench makes your communication with your team, an action-oriented task in spite of just a mere conversation.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/annotate-attachments-in-g/ggfddmmbggnlhnelhlkbgaapbidkhcfa">
        <img alt="annotate gmail attachments using Frajmebench" class="alignnone wp-image-1612 size-large" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/blog.framebench.com/wp-content/uploads/Screenshot-from-2014-09-03-145142.png?resize=1024%2C529"/>
       </a>
       <a href="chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/annotate-attachments-in-g/ggfddmmbggnlhnelhlkbgaapbidkhcfa" title="Annotate Gmail Attachments using Chrome Extension">
        Get the Extension
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Cloudy
       </b>
       <b>
        :
       </b>
       In this era of multimedia, we keep our files on different cloud services and social media platforms. Google also provide cloud service with Google Drive, but different people use different cloud services to keep their pictures and files.
      </p>
      <p>
       This Gmail Extension- Cloudy gives you leverage to add a file toyour mail through various cloud services such as Facebook, Dropbox, SkyDrive, Box, Instagram, Flickr, Gmail attachments, FTP, Evernote, WebDav, Picasa, Github, Web Images. Apart from various online cloud services it allows you to attach files through your computer, you can directly record videos and take pictures in just one click.

       </a>
       <a href="https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/cloudy-for-gmail/fcfnjfpcmnoabmbhponbioedjceaddaa" onclick="ga('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/cloudy-for-gmail/fcfnjfpcmnoabmbhponbioedjceaddaa', 'Get the Extension');">
        Get the Extension
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Right Inbox
       </b>
       : Imagine you have a long day ahead, and you need to send some important mails to people during that day, what about sending them a night prior.
      </p>
      <p>
       If youre looking for such an app, Right Inbox is the thing for you  an awesome Gmail extension to schedule your emails for a future date. Once you set the date and time for your email, it will automatically get delivered at the specified time. Best part of the extension is that, it also works with your Gmail App. You can always track important conversations, with Remind Me feature.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://i2.wp.com/blog.framebench.com/wp-content/uploads/Right-Inbox.png" onclick="ga('send', 'event', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://blog.framebench.com/wp-content/uploads/Right-Inbox.png', '');">
        <img alt="right inbox gmail extension" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1594" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/blog.framebench.com/wp-content/uploads/Right-Inbox.png?resize=1024%2C575"/>
       </a>
       <a href="https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/right-inbox-for-gmail/mflnemhkomgploogccdmcloekbloobgb" onclick="ga('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/right-inbox-for-gmail/mflnemhkomgploogccdmcloekbloobgb', 'Get the Extension');">
        Get the Extension
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Screenleap:
       </b>
       This Gmail extension gives you leverage to share your screen instantly in one click to other people whom you send request. You can share your screen to PCs, tablets and smartphones, almost to every computing device available which can browse web.
      </p>
      <p>
       The most important thing is other user have to just click on link to view screen, no login or account registration is required for them. It also gives you option to share entire screen or part of a screen which you select. It is so instant, that you save lots of your time. A must chrome extension for people who spend considerable time in sharing screens.
       <a href="http://i1.wp.com/blog.framebench.com/wp-content/uploads/Screenleap-screenshot.png" onclick="ga('send', 'event', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://blog.framebench.com/wp-content/uploads/Screenleap-screenshot.png', '');">

        Get the Extension
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <b>
        Powerbot:
       </b>
       Integrate your Gmail account with Dropbox and Evernote with the help of this excellent Chrome extension. Powerboat allows you to insert your notes from Evernote, Dropbox and OneNote, directly into composing message of your Gmail.
      </p>
      <p>
       Also you can save your email thread into your Dropbox or Evernote account into the specified notebooks and folders, so that it can be easily pull out for the next time. This will not only save your time, but will also make your mails error free.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://i0.wp.com/blog.framebench.com/wp-content/uploads/Power-Boat.jpg" onclick="ga('send', 'event', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://blog.framebench.com/wp-content/uploads/Power-Boat.jpg', '');">
