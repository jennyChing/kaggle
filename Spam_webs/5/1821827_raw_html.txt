     <h1 class="article-headline wordy no-hero">
      Hero Showdown: Master Chief vs. Samus Aran
     </h1>
     <div class="article-content" id="article-content">
      <div class="article-subhead">
       <div class="article-share-count-container">
        <span class="icon icon-sharrow">

       It's Halo vs. Metroid for all the marbles.
      </div>
      <span class="article-byline icon-pointy">
       By Chris Carle
      </span>
      <aside class="ign-ad ign-ad-300x250 no-hero">
       <div class="sugarad" id="sugarad-300x250">

                &lt;p&gt;Here at the newly-formed IGN Stars, we like to pose the kind of questions that you and your friends are already arguing about and open them up to the world for resolution once and for all. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt; In the world of videogames, there is nothing more polarizing than console loyalty&amp;#Array; and that extends to mascots. In the realm of sci-fi shooters, there are no bigger players than Metroid's Samus and this week's biggest star, &lt;a class='autolink' href='http://stars.ign.com/objects/961/961983.html'&gt;Master Chief&lt;/a&gt;. Both have tenacity, both have proved themselves in the sales arena, both have withstood the test of time. Both are set up as shining examples of the companies they represent: Chief for &lt;a class='autolink' href='http://games.ign.com/objects/025/025169.html'&gt;Microsoft&lt;/a&gt; and Samus for &lt;a class='autolink' href='http://games.ign.com/objects/025/025047.html'&gt;Nintendo&lt;/a&gt;.  &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt; With the latest installments of their respective franchises recently on store shelves, we thought it the perfect time to ask: Who would win in a knock-down, drag-out space brawl to the death? Would it be the crafty, stoic Samus or the resourceful, hard-edged Master Chief? We decided to let you, the readers battle it out. Scan the information below then visit the poll and let your voice be heard. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;!-- poilib start --&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="accentDivider"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;!-- poilib end --&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt; &lt;strong&gt;&lt;a href="http://stars.ign.com/objects/961/961983.html"&gt;Master Chief&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;br /&gt; Ever since &lt;a class="autolink" title="Master Chief" href="http://www.ign.com/characters/master-chief" data-legacy-id="961983"&gt;Master Chief&lt;/a&gt; came on the scene in the original Halo, the Xbox faithful had their hero&amp;#Array; and one hell of a hero he is. Adhering to the philosophy that less is more (at least in the talking department), Chief is a man of few words but a lot of action. Whether dual-wielding some of the sickest weapons ever created, piloting a Warthog through the rough-and-tumble wastelands, or generally setting the universe a-cheese with his advanced bulletry, Chief is a cool customer&amp;#Array; and the perfect face for a new generation of shooter fans. Never at a loss for the best strategy and armed to the teeth&amp;#Array; is Master Chief ready for his greatest challenge yet in &lt;a href="http://xbox360.ign.com/halo3launch/"class='autolink' href='http://stars.ign.com/objects/959/959384.html'&gt;Samus Aran&lt;/a&gt;? &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt; &lt;strong&gt;&lt;a &gt;Check the &lt;a class="autolink" title="Halo 3" href="http://www.ign.com/games/halo-3/xbox-360-872443" data-legacy-id="872443"&gt;Halo 3&lt;/a&gt; launch center for treasure troves of information.&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;!-- poilib start --&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="imageWithCaption"&gt;&lt;img src="http://starsmedia.ign.com/stars/image/article/823/823148/hero-showdown-master-chief-vs-samus-aran-20070926030341203-000.jpg"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;!-- poilib end --&gt;
 &lt;br /&gt; &lt;strong&gt;&lt;a href="http://stars.ign.com/objects/961/961983.html"&gt;Click here to read Master Chief's full profile.&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;br /&gt; While you're there, be sure to rate Master Chief as a character, contribute trivia, quotes and more! Show everyone just how much you know about Master Chief. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;!-- poilib start --&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="accentDivider"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;!-- poilib end --&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt; &lt;strong&gt;&lt;a href="http://stars.ign.com/objects/959/959384.html"&gt;Samus Aran&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;br /&gt; Samus has been cruising the cosmos significantly longer than Master Chief. Arriving on the scene in 1986, the no-nonsense bounty hunter has been featured in nine games, and is a staple in an impressive arsenal of Nintendo characters. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt; Although her identity was obscured in the original Metroid until beating the game, Samus has since been immortalized in fanboy lore as the ultimate galaxy-hopping femme fatale. With high-tech weaponry, vast intelligence and unparalleled knack for puzzle-solving, Samus has gotten herself out of many a tough scrape, whether against Mother Brain or a horde of bloodthirsty space pirates. She always lives to fight again&amp;#Array; Will she survive Master Chief? &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt; &lt;strong&gt;&lt;a href="http://games.ign.com/articles/815/815011p1.html"&gt;Check out the comprehensive history of Metroid for more information.&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;!-- poilib start --&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="imageWithCaption"&gt;&lt;img src="http://starsmedia.ign.com/stars/image/article/823/823148/hero-showdown-master-chief-vs-samus-aran-20070926030330516-000.jpg"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;!-- poilib end --&gt;
 &lt;br /&gt; &lt;strong&gt;&lt;a href="http://stars.ign.com/objects/959/959384.html"&gt;Click here to read &lt;a class="autolink" title="Samus Aran" href="http://www.ign.com/characters/metroid-samus-aran" data-legacy-id="959384"&gt;Samus Aran&lt;/a&gt;'s full profile.&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;  &lt;br /&gt; While you're there, be sure to rate Samus as a character, contribute trivia, quotes and more! Show everyone just how much you know about Samus. &lt;/p&gt;

Cast your vote at the poll to the right and then check back next Wednesday to see who took the ultimate crown!&lt;/p&gt;
