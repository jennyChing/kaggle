          <span class="lessontext">
           Hello GMC fellows, and welcome to my contribution in the Neo-Classical style. I called it Extreme Neo-Classic because it's pretty fast and includes lots of speed picking, sweeping etc.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Actually I stared working on it at 120bpm but I ended on 150bpm, just sounds more powerful in my opinion.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           As always I'm giving you TONS of backings, from 70 up to 150bpm, pick the tempo that fits you nicely! :)
           <br/>
           <br/>
           So it's kind a standard Neo-Classical piece, key of Am, we have few diminished chords here etc.
           <br/>
           Scales I used are A harmonic minor, D harmonic minor and B dominant phrygian.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Once more, don't push yourself too hard on this one, just play it slow and clean, speed will come naturally, with hard work of course.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Hope you like it,ask if you need any help!
           <br/>
           <br/>
           Muris Varajic
