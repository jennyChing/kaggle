          Chocolate Mug of Sadness
         </a>
        </h1>
        <div class="meta clearfix">
         Posted

        <p>
         No story today. In this case, truth is stranger than fiction.
         <a href="http://somekitchenstories.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Chocolate_mug_cake_1.jpg">
          <br/>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <br/>
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-350" height="456" src="http://somekitchenstories.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Chocolate_mug_cake_1.jpg" title="Chocolate_mug_cake_1" width="705"/>
           <br/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somekitchenstories.com/2011/06/20/chocolate-mug-of-sadness/&amp;media=http://somekitchenstories.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Chocolate_mug_cake_1.jpg&amp;description=Chocolate Mug of Sadness')">
            <br/>
           </span>
           <br/>
          </span>
          <br/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im a little bit horrified by this thing.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://somekitchenstories.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Chocolate_mug_cake_2.jpg">
          <br/>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <br/>
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-351" height="456" src="http://somekitchenstories.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Chocolate_mug_cake_2.jpg" title="Chocolate_mug_cake_2" width="705"/>
           <br/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somekitchenstories.com/2011/06/20/chocolate-mug-of-sadness/&amp;media=http://somekitchenstories.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Chocolate_mug_cake_2.jpg&amp;description=Chocolate Mug of Sadness')">
            <br/>
           </span>
           <br/>
          </span>
          <br/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Which isnt to say its not
         <em>
          good
         </em>
         .  It is. Its rich and dense  and very chocolatey. Its cake, you guys- I dont mean to sound like it ate my family or anything. We made a batch for the  kids at the office- lined  up a bunch of our company branded mugs and  spooned in the ingredients,  launched the microwave and then dropped them  off at peoples desks.  They ate them. They were pleased, very pleased, and amused by the novelty of it all. But I stood back  and watched, not able to  hide the horror on my face.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://somekitchenstories.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Chocolate_mug_cake_3.jpg">
          <br/>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <br/>
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-352" height="456" src="http://somekitchenstories.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Chocolate_mug_cake_3.jpg" title="Chocolate_mug_cake_3" width="705"/>
           <br/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somekitchenstories.com/2011/06/20/chocolate-mug-of-sadness/&amp;media=http://somekitchenstories.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/Chocolate_mug_cake_3.jpg&amp;description=Chocolate Mug of Sadness')">
            <br/>
           </span>
           <br/>
          </span>
          <br/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Theres just something so sad about chocolate cake for one. In a mug. So not right. It should not be this easy to make your own cake in the microwave, for one thing. Maybe thats what bothers me- how close I am to making chocolate cake. Every. Day. Just for myself. Just for myself in my apartment. Using my
         <em>
          Sleepless in Seattle
         </em>
         mug. Swarmed by cats as I dig in, all hunched over it, wrapped in a shawl I knit for myself. Plucking a stray piece of cat hair off my Chocolate Mug Cake for One. Feeling the thick cake hitting the bottom of my empty, loveless womb. Waiting for death.
        </p>
        <p>
         So, you know, ENJOY MAKING THIS RECIPE.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.livelovepasta.com/2011/05/nutella-mug-cake/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.livelovepasta.com/2011/05/nutella-mug-cake/', 'Eclectic Recipes ']);" target="_blank">

         <a href="http://www.livelovepasta.com/2011/05/nutella-mug-cake/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.livelovepasta.com/2011/05/nutella-mug-cake/', ' Live Love Pasta']);" target="_blank">
          Live Love Pasta
         </a>
         (who did not have the same existential concerns. She just ate hers like a normal, functioning, food blogging human being.
         <a href="http://www.livelovepasta.com/2011/05/nutella-mug-cake/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.livelovepasta.com/2011/05/nutella-mug-cake/', ' ']);" target="_blank">
         </a>
         Also, her blog is pretty, so pretty.)
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Chocolate Mug of Sadness
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Makes 1 large mug of sadness or 2 slightly less sad servings
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients
         </strong>
         <br/>
         4 TBsp flour
         <br/>
         4 TBsp sugar
         <br/>
         1 egg
         <br/>
         3 TBsp cocoa powder
         <br/>
         3 TBsp Nutella
         <br/>
         3 TBsp milk
         <br/>
         3 TBsp vegetable oil
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Directions
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Combine all ingredients in a large coffee mug (or 2).
        </p>
        <p>
         Whisk with fork  until smooth.
        </p>
        <p>
         Microwave on high for 1 3 minutes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Sprinkle with chocolate chips. Or, halfway through baking time, drop in a dollop of peanut butter. (Baking time depends on  microwave minutes. Ours took about 2 minutes.)
        </p>
