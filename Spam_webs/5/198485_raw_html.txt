             <p>
              Do you know how far a dogs loyalty will go?You may have heard of the now legendary story of Hachiko, the dog who stood by its owners grave. You may know about the St. Bernards/ alpine mastiffs, and the many stories of them rescuing humans in the Alps. You may also know about Balto, whose legendary trup from Nenana to Nome, Alaska in 1925 became the basis for the Iditarod.
              <br/>
              However, one could say a heroic dog is born everyday. In this list we have stories of ten different dogs, in different circumstances, doing things that will astound and warm the hearts of many an ordinary person. If youre a dog owner yourself, you may find yourself wanting to hug them today, after you read about these amazing signs of loyalty and fidelity these dogs show to their owners, friends, and fellow dogs.
