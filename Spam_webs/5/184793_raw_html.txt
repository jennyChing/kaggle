     <h1 class="entry-title">
      5 Unique Ways to Display Your Kids Artwork
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">
      <time class="published" datetime="2014-05-22T12:09:03+00:00">
       May 22, 2014
      </time>
     </div>
    </header>
   </div>
   <div class="wrap">
    <div class="container" role="document">
     <div class="content row">
      <main class="main " role="main">
       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         You love showcasing your little artistes masterpieces. But what do you do after youve filled every inch of the fridge door with magneted-on drawings?
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont despair. Plain, fancy or in-between, these ideas for ever-changing displays fit the bill. You and your kids will be proud to show off both the art and how its presented when relatives and friends come to visit.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Hang it Up
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Things youll need:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Twine
         </li>
         <li>
          Hooks or nails
         </li>
         <li>
          Clothespins
         </li>
         <li>
          Glue
         </li>
         <li>
          Curtain rod system
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Hang art from a clothesline. Tie some twine between two hooks or nails on the wall and hang the pictures with clothespins. You can dress up the display by gluing decorations such as buttons to the nailheads. Get your little ones input on which decorations go with their favorite artwork
         <b>
          .
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Display Your Kids' Artwork Things You'll Need" class="alignnone" height="534" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/02_Unique_Ways_Display_Your_Kids_Artwork-blog140522" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Or use one of those nifty curtain rod systems that come with clips that slide on the rod. Just mount the rod to a wall, thread the clips on the rod and hang your childs pictures from the clips. Any way you want to hang it, you can add or change pictures in a flash.
         <b>
          <br/>
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Unique Ways to Display Your Kids Artwork  hang it up" class="alignnone" height="534" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/03_Unique_Ways_Display_Your_Kids_Artwork-blog140522" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Repurpose Wooden Hangers
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Things youll need:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Wooden hangers
         </li>
         <li>
          Paint
         </li>
         <li>
          Decorations such as fabric, buttons, sequins or glitter
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Wooden hangers with clips can hold more than pants and skirts! Attach the top of a drawing or painting to the clips and hang the hanger from a hook mounted on a wall, pegboard or bulletin board. Leave the hangers wooden frame plain for a natural look, or decorate with paint and your childs name or initial.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Unique Ways to Display Your Kids Artwork repurpose hangers" class="alignnone" height="534" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/04_Unique_Ways_Display_Your_Kids_Artwork-blog140522" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Get fancy with decoupage, by pasting on scrapbook paper or fabric. To add sparkle, spread decoupage medium on the hanger and coat with glitter, or wrap with shiny sequin strands.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Unique Ways to Display Your Kids Artwork " class="alignnone" height="534" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/05_Unique_Ways_Display_Your_Kids_Artwork-blog140522" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Make a Mug
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Print
         <a href="http://gifts.personalcreations.com/gifts/Photo-Latte-Mug-30029301" target="new">
          a mug
         </a>
         with your childs artwork. Scan the masterpiece or photograph it with a good digital camera. For best results, use photo-editing software to get a crisp, clear image with bright colors.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Unique Ways to Display Your Kids Artwork " class="alignnone" height="534" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/06_Unique_Ways_Display_Your_Kids_Artwork-blog140522" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The possibilities are endless  how about a set of personalized mugs for each member of the family? Or serve seasonal drinks in matching mugs  autumn leaves with hot cider, for example, or hot chocolate from within a snow scene.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Print a Welcome Mat
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Welcome guests with an artistic expression. Commission your little Michelangelo to produce a large, high-resolution image that you can reprint on a doormat. You might even want to have your childs drawing or painting professionally scanned before uploading to get the best possible quality copy on your
         <a href="http://gifts.personalcreations.com/gifts/Custom-Photo-Doormat--17x27-30022020" target="new">
          one-of-a-kind doormat
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignnone" height="534" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/07_Unique_Ways_Display_Your_Kids_Artwork-blog140522" title="Unique Ways to Display Your Kids Artwork " width="800"/>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Keep a Time Capsule
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Things youll need:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Keepsake box
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Store your childs artwork in a time capsule. Tuck small art pieces into
         <a href="http://gifts.personalcreations.com/gifts/hands-down-keepsake-box-30028372" target="new">
          a personalized keepsake box
         </a>
         to open at a special time, such as a milestone birthday years from now. Have your child decorate a greeting card or tag and list her favorite things. Take photos of your child holding larger art pieces and print them in a size that will fit in the box.
        </p>
        <p>
         Include crafts and keepsakes, such as a handmade ornament, lanyard or friendship bracelet. Once your keepsake box is full, store it in a safe place  in a location youll remember. You and your child will get a kick out of opening the box years from now and reliving the memories.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Unique Ways to Display Your Kids Artwork keepsake box" class="alignnone" height="534" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/08_Unique_Ways_Display_Your_Kids_Artwork-blog140522" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <b>
          Share Your Ideas
         </b>
        </h3>
        <p>
         From mini-canvases to fancy frames, there are many more ways to display kids art around your home. However you choose to transform the artwork, what really matters is that your little artist gets to show it off around the house. Post your thoughts and discoveries in the comments and share the inspiration.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="nique Ways to Display Your Kids Artwork May 22, 2014" class="alignnone" height="534" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/09_Unique_Ways_Display_Your_Kids_Artwork-blog140522" width="800"/>
        </p>
       </div>
