        <h2>
         In this free cdn wordpress list we have collected free cdn hosting content delivery network websites for wordpress, and that are premium and free services.
        </h2>
        <p>
         A content delivery network (CDN) can speed up your site and help to protect it against traffic spikes.
         <br/>
         On designrazzi, weve also looked at types of CDNs, easy integration in WordPress with W3 Total Cache, and weve compared some of the larger content delivery networks.
         <br/>
         The Internet was designed according to the end-to-end principle.
        </p>
        <p target="_blank">
         <img alt="Best Free CDN Services for WordPress" src="http://cdn.designsrazzi.com/images/2014/10/Best_Free_CDN_Services_for_WordPress.jpg" title="20+ Best free cdn services for wordpress websites"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This principle keeps the core network relatively simple and moves the intelligence as much as possible to the network end-points: the hosts and clients.
         <br/>
         As a result the core network is specialized, simplified, and optimized to only forward data packets.
        </p>
        <h3>
         What Files can be Hosted on a CDN?
        </h3>
        <p>
         Most CDNs are used to host static resources such as images, videos, audio clips, CSS files and JavaScript.
         <br/>
         Youll find common JavaScript libraries, HTML5 shims, CSS resets, fonts and other assets available on a variety of public and private CDN systems.
        </p>
        <h3>
         CDN Services
        </h3>
        <p>
         There are a number of free CDNs offered by Google, Microsoft, Yahoo and other large web organizations. For example, few people host their own videos when YouTube and Vimeo offer amazing free services. Content Delivery Networks augment the end-to-end transport network by distributing on it a variety of intelligent applications employing techniques designed to optimize content delivery. The resulting tightly integrated overlay uses web caching, server-load balancing, request routing, and content services. These techniques are briefly described below.
        </p>

         <p>
          Nothing in this world is free
