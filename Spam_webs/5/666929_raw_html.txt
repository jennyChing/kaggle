  decisions, says Huffington Post founder Arianna Huffington.

        <div id="mainBodyArea" itemprop="articleBody">
         <div class="firstPar">
          <p>
           Arianna Huffington, founder of online newspaper The Huffington Post and one of the most famous female entrepreneurs in the US, has called on leaders of governments and businesses alike to sleep more, and place less emphasis on the old-fashioned metrics for success, fame and power
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="secondPar">
          <p>
           Defining success in terms of just money and power is like a two-legged stool, she told
           <i>
            The Telegraph
           </i>
           at the One Young World Summit in Johannesburg. You see a lot of famous and successful people toppling off. 
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="thirdPar">
          <p>
           Ms Huffington, who is a councillor for One Young World, which hopes to promote and create the next generation of global leaders, took to the stage to talk to the assembled delegates about her third metric for success: This consists of wellbeing, wisdom, wonder, and giving, she said.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fourthPar">
          <p>
           Wellbeing is essential to being a good leader, she explained. If youre not feeling well, you arent going to be as effective, or as creative, or as productive.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fifthPar">
          <p>
           This issue is even more important for women she argued. New data shows that women in stressful, successful jobs have a 40pc greater chance of heart attacks and heart disease and 60pc greater incidence of diabetes.
          </p>
         </div>

         <div class="body">
          <p>
           Guys can deal with it much better, she said. You can go and watch a football game and forget about it but women want to wrestle it into the ground. If you go into any corporation, with the exception of a few pioneering organisations, you see people burned out and exhausted and as a result making terrible decisions.
          </p>
          <p>
           Sleep is the key to being successful, she said, but it was a lesson she learned the hard way. In 2007, in the middle of building the Huffington Post, I fainted from exhaustion, she said. I hit my head on my desk, I broke my cheekbone and got four stitches on my right eye. Dont wait until you get a rude awakening like that.
          </p>
          <p>
           Ms Huffington then rediscovered sleep. She went from sleeping just four hours a night to seven or eight. And these have been my best years, she said. I have never been more creative, more productive, less reactive.
          </p>
          <p>
           You know all those clichs? That sleep is for losers, or Ill sleep when I die, forget that, she continued. Sleep deprivation is for losers. Sleep deprived people make the wrong decisions. They miss the whispers and the signs and red flags.
          </p>
          <p>
           Ms Huffington has installed nap rooms at her online newspaper to encourage her writers to take a rest rather than rely on coffee and cinnamon buns to keep them going. Except the other day I saw two people coming out, she laughed. Oh well. Whatever it takes to help them feel recharged.
          </p>
          <p>
           Ms Huffington is on a mission to make people slow down, sleep more, and be more present in the moment. Multitasking is a disease, she said. Im here to celebrate uni-tasking. Miracles happen when we give our full attention.
          </p>
          <p>
           You walk down these beautiful streets and you see people on their iPhones with their iPods in their ears, she added. Is anybody present? Is anybody really living their lives? Dont miss the moment. A lot of the anxiety we have is because we are either living in the past, reliving our mistakes, or were living in the future, being anxious about what is going to happen.
          </p>
          <p>
           Her warnings about the dangers of work stress and exhaustion resonated with the crowd. One delegate, in tears, admitted she experienced her first burn out at just 28.
          </p>
          <p>
           Ms Huffingtons final word as she finished her address was a call to arms for the young people in the room. You know the days when you wake up feeling really great? You can deal with anything, she said. Sleep your way to the top!
          </p>

                        '&lt;iframe style="border:none;position:absolute;top:0;left:0; height:100%;width:100%;z-index:-1;" id="iefix" src="about:blank"&gt;&lt;/iframe&gt;' + //fix for IE bug
                        '&lt;/div&gt;';
                return template;
            };


            var init = function(currentArticleId, articleList, displayThumbnail) {

                settings.articleList = articleList;
                settings.currentArticleId = currentArticleId;
                settings.displayThumbnail = displayThumbnail;

                settings.articleListToSkip = unescape($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName)).split(',');

                if ($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName) != null) {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    updateCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                } else {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    createCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                }


                if (settings.articleId != "") {

                    $(renderTemplate()).appendTo("body");

                    $("#nextArticleWrapper a, #readNextOpenInner a").click(function () {
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_headline', 'version_1']);

                        getNavPos($(this).attr("href"), "wayfarerReadNext");
                    });

                    bindUIActions();

                    var displayed = false;

                    setTimeout(function () {
                        if (!displayed) {
                            displayed == true;
                            readNextWidget.display();
                        }

                    }, 10000);

                    $(window).scroll(function () {
                        if (isScrolledIntoView("#tmg-related-links")) {
                            if (!displayed) {
                                displayed == true;
                                readNextWidget.display();
                            }
                        }

                        if(isScrolledIntoView("#shareBottom") || isScrolledIntoView("#newGigyaShareBottom")) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }

                        if($(window).scrollTop() + $(window).height() == $(document).height()) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }
                    });
                }
            };

            var display = function () {
                $(settings.widgetContainer).fadeIn(2000, function () {
                    //animation complete
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_closed', 'version_1']);
                });
            };

            var bindUIActions = function () {
                $(settings.openButton).mouseenter(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'hover_closed', 'version_1']);
                    openArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.widgetContainer).mouseleave(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    closeArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.closeButton).click(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_close', 'version_1']);
                    updateCookie(settings.articleId, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    closeArticleContainer();
                    settings.autoOpen = false;
                });
            };

            var openArticleContainer = function () {
                if (!settings.panelOpen) {
                	$("#readNextArticleContainer").show();//show/hide because height is calculated incorrectly for elements with display:none
                    $("#readNextArticleContainer").hide();
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_open', 'version_1']);
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                    settings.autoOpen = false; //set auto open to false once user has viewed this once
                }
                return false;
            };

            var closeArticleContainer = function () {
                if (settings.panelOpen) {
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                }
                return false;
            };


            var isScrolledIntoView = function(elem) {
                if($(elem).length) { //check if element exists
                    var docViewTop = $(window).scrollTop();
                    var docViewBottom = docViewTop + $(window).height();

                    var elemTop = $(elem).offset().top;
                    var elemBottom = elemTop + $(elem).height();

                    return ((elemBottom &lt;= docViewBottom) &amp;&amp; (elemTop &gt;= docViewTop));
                } else {
                    return false;
                }
            };

            var unique = function(array) {
                return array.filter(function (el, index, arr) {
                    return index == arr.indexOf(el);
                });
            }

            var createCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        articleListToSkip[0] = currentArticleId;
                        $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        updateCookie(currentArticleId, articleListToSkip);
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookie = function(articleIdToSkip, articleListToSkip) {
                articleListToSkip.push(articleIdToSkip);
                articleListToSkip = unique(articleListToSkip);
                $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
            }

            var chooseNextArticle = function (currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if ((article.id != currentArticleId) &amp;&amp; ($.inArray(article.id, articleListToSkip) === -1)) {

                        //set article
                        settings.articleTitle = article.title;
                        settings.articleUrl = article.url;
                        settings.articleId = article.id;
						settings.articleImage = article.imgUrl;
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            //public API
            return {
                settings: settings,
                init: init,
                display: display,
                openArticleContainer: openArticleContainer,
                closeArticleContainer: closeArticleContainer
            }
        })();

    };
  </script>
