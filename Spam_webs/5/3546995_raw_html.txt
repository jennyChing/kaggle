       <h1 class="post-title">
        <a href="http://urbanbushbabes.com/beauty-diy-activated-charcoal-teeth-whitener-face-mask/">
         Beauty DIY: Activated Charcoal Teeth Whitener &amp; Face Mask
        </a>
       </h1>
       <p class="post-author">
        <em>
         Posted by
        </em>
        <a href="http://urbanbushbabes.com/author/nikisha/" rel="author" title="Posts by Nikisha Brunson">
         Nikisha Brunson
        </a>

       <!-- /post-meta -->
       <figure class="post-image ">
        <a href="http://urbanbushbabes.com/beauty-diy-activated-charcoal-teeth-whitener-face-mask/">
         <img alt="Beauty DIY: Activated Charcoal Teeth Whitener &amp; Face Mask" src="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/finalcollage.jpg" width="548"/>

       <div class="post-content">
        <p>
         I know some of you may be thinking where the heck do you buy activated charcoal, never mind using it as part of your beauty regimen?! Well ladies, I have added activated charcoal to my beauty regimen, using it as a teeth whitener and a face mask and Im loving it! Activated charcoal is carbon thats treated with oxygen. Its used to treat gastrointestinal issues and counteracts ingestion of poisons and drugs. It makes a great face mask because its very porous and absorptive drawing substances to itself. These qualities make it great for clearing up blemishes and cleansing your skin of toxins. You may have even heard of the activated Charcoal mask from the brand
         <span style="color: #33cccc;">
          <a href="http://www.sephora.com/clear-improvement-active-charcoal-mask-to-clear-pores-P297524?om_mmc=Googlepla,_requestid=42976,cm_mmc=us_search-_-GG-_-pla-_-,ci_src=17588969,ci_sku=1375773" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.sephora.com']);">
           <span style="color: #33cccc;">
            Origins
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         . The carbon also cleans teeth like no other by removing acidic plaque which whitens and brightens. The beautiful thing about incorporating charcoal into your beauty regimen is its all natural and safe!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         You can find activated charcoal capsules at your local
         <span style="color: #33cccc;">
          <a href="http://www.vitaminshoppe.com/store/en/browse/sku_detail.jsp?id=VS-2626&amp;sourceType=sc&amp;source=FG&amp;adGroup=60-80&amp;keyword=VS-2626&amp;cm_mmc=Google+Shopping-_-Product+Listing+Ads-_-60-80-_-VS-2626&amp;gclid=CLKMpMaZoLYCFaGpPAod51gAZw#.UVShNxlpfoA" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.vitaminshoppe.com']);">
           <span style="color: #33cccc;">
            Vitamin Shoppe
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         (where I purchased mine), CVS, or online.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         ***Somethings to keep in mind when dealing with activated charcoal: It can be a bit messy and stain things. Other than that its easily rinsed off with water from skin and non porous surfaces, and can be washed out of clothing. When using it as a teeth whitener limit use to 1-2 times a week to avoid wear on tooth enamel.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          For teeth whitener you will need:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0058_600.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[23785]">
          <img alt="IMG_0058_600" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23796" height="400" src="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0058_600.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         A tooth brush, tooth paste, and an activated charcoal capsule.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0030cropped-e1364579987761.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[23785]">
          <img alt="IMG_0030cropped" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23823" height="378" src="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0030cropped-e1364579987761.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         Carefully open activated charcoal capsule.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0035_600.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[23785]">
          <img alt="IMG_0035_600" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23824" height="400" src="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0035_600.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Apply toothpaste to the toothbrush and dip it into the charcoal powder completely covering the toothpaste. Begin to brush your teeth as usual. You will notice your mouth turn black which is pretty cool, you can brush with just water to get it completely out of your mouth.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          For the face mask you will need:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0065_600.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[23785]">
          <img alt="IMG_0065_600" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23799" height="400" src="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0065_600.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         1 teaspoon activated charcoal (in powder form)
        </p>
        <p>
         1 teaspoon aloe vera gel or juice
        </p>
        <p>
         1 teaspoon water or rosewater
        </p>
        <p>
         5 drops tea tree oil
        </p>
        <p>
         1 pinch sea salt
        </p>
        <p>
         Small container
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div>
         Mix all the ingredients together.
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <a href="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0051_600.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[23785]">
          <img alt="IMG_0051_600" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23825" height="366" src="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0051_600.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         Apply a thin even layer all over your face. Allow the mask to dry and then wash off.
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <a href="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0050_600.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[23785]">
          <img alt="IMG_0050_600" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23827" height="383" src="http://urbanbushbabes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/IMG_0050_600.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         This mask is safe for most skin types, but you should first do a spot test to make sure this mask is right for you. Apply to a small patch of skin, let dry, wash off. Wait 24 hours to see how your skin reacts.
         <div>
         </div>
         <div>
         </div>
