        <h1 class="entry-title">
         The Easiest (and Coolest) Way To Cut A Watermelon
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         I saw an article the other day that showed the right way to cut and cube a watermelon.
        </p>
        <p>
         Um..no. THIS way is magic! Once someone showed me this, I never went back to buying pre-packaged, cubed melon. It is so easy and saves you a ton of money if you do it yourself. Did I mention, it is also very quick and low mess? I Know! Amazing, right?
        </p>
        <p>
         Heres how you do it:
        </p>
        <p>
         1. Cut your watermelon in half. Then cut each half in half until you have a quarter that looks like this (below.) Using a big knife, slice 1 inch thick lines into one side. You can feel when the knife is hitting the rindjust glide the knife against it.
        </p>
        <p>
         Now turn it the other way and repeat on the other side.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://mollyandmolly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/How-To-Cut-A-Watermelon-5.jpg">
          <img alt="How To Cut A Watermelon-5" class="alignleft wp-image-179 size-full" height="433" src="http://mollyandmolly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/How-To-Cut-A-Watermelon-5.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Next, cut crosswise through the melon, gliding the tip of your knife against the back of the rind, just like you did before. Make your cuts about an inch apart here too.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://mollyandmolly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/How-To-Cut-A-Watermelon-6.jpg">
          <img alt="How To Cut A Watermelon-6" class="alignnone wp-image-180 size-full" height="433" src="http://mollyandmolly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/How-To-Cut-A-Watermelon-6.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://mollyandmolly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/How-To-Cut-A-Watermelon-7.jpg">
          <img alt="How To Cut A Watermelon-7" class="alignnone wp-image-181 size-full" height="433" src="http://mollyandmolly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/How-To-Cut-A-Watermelon-7.jpg" width="650"/>
          <br/>
         </a>
         3. now cut along the rind, letting your knife go about halfway through. Turn it and do the same on the other side.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://mollyandmolly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/How-To-Cut-A-Watermelon-9.jpg">
          <img alt="How To Cut A Watermelon-9" class="alignnone wp-image-183 size-full" height="494" src="http://mollyandmolly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/How-To-Cut-A-Watermelon-9.jpg" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now flip it over a big bowl and enjoy your perfect melon cubes!
         <a href="http://mollyandmolly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/How-To-Cut-A-Watermelon-11.jpg">
          <img alt="How To Cut A Watermelon-11" class="alignnone wp-image-185 size-full" height="433" src="http://mollyandmolly.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/How-To-Cut-A-Watermelon-11.jpg" width="650"/>
          &gt;
         </a>
        </p>
