       <section class="entry fix">
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <a href="http://bigvoicepictures.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Writing-your-documentary-media-or-video-proposal.png">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Writing Your Media, Video or Documentary Proposal" class="aligncenter wp-image-1151" height="475" src="http://bigvoicepictures.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Writing-your-documentary-media-or-video-proposal.png" width="317"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://bigvoicepictures.com/blog/2014/10/21/steps-to-planning-your-media-video-and-documentary-project/&amp;media=http://bigvoicepictures.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Writing-your-documentary-media-or-video-proposal.png&amp;description=Steps to Planning your Media, Video and Documentary Project')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           For those of you wanting to launch a media, photography, book, documentary or video project, Im certain my own experience planning and producing documentaries will help you unfold your dream project.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           Do you have what it takes? Read on.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           When I first started developing
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           our documentary,
           <a href="http://bigvoicepictures.com/boys-and-men-healing/">
            <i>
             Boys and Men Healing,
            </i>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           I first applied to the International Documentary Association for fiscal sponsorship in order to apply for grants from foundations. This entailed writing a lengthy proposal detailing my plans for pre-production, production, fundraising, and outreach and distribution. It took me over a year from start to completion to finish the proposal..
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           Dont be scared you off! In this blog, Ill offer you the Cliff Note version of my proposal writing strategy.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           Looking back, the laborious proposal writing
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           process
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            was necessary
           </span>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           . It helped me synthesize my research, discussions with experts in the field, content ideas, and creative vision. It forced me to
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           define my project from A-Z. When finished,
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            The Proposal
           </b>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           became my trusted road map for producing the film. Later it became my GPS for navigating distribution and outreach worldwide.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://bigvoicepictures.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Map-for-documentary-Proposal-e1413846825889.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Media and documentary Proposals map" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1152" height="333" src="http://bigvoicepictures.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Map-for-documentary-Proposal-e1413846825889.jpg" width="500"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://bigvoicepictures.com/blog/2014/10/21/steps-to-planning-your-media-video-and-documentary-project/&amp;media=http://bigvoicepictures.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Map-for-documentary-Proposal-e1413846825889.jpg&amp;description=Steps to Planning your Media, Video and Documentary Project')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            The Proposal
           </b>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           guided me in creating a successful project that made an impact. Without it, I doubt I would have reached the finish line.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           Like most big projects, during the often arduous creative process, we face times when we lose steam and focus.
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            The Proposal
           </b>
           became my
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           best friend, guiding me back on the high road to progress when I felt lost.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           Below is a handy outline for writing
           <i>
            your
           </i>
           <strong>
            Proposal
           </strong>
           , a Cliff Notes sort of version. I use it to sketch outlines for my writing and documentary projects. The outline will guide you in developing your heart projectwhether your project is a a book, photography or media project, or a video or documentary. You might start small by answering #1-2, and later move to the following points. Each point will get you thinking, helping you funnel your ideas into the in-progress
           <strong>
            Proposal 
           </strong>
           your creative well.
           <br/>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           Remember, creativity needs ample time to take shape, so dont rush through your
           <strong>
            Proposal
           </strong>
           . Take lots of walks, put your project aside for weeks, if needed. I took six months off midway through writing my
           <strong>
            Proposal
           </strong>
           for
           <em>
            <strong>
             Boys
            </strong>
            <strong>
             and Men Healing
            </strong>
           </em>
           to allow my ideas to brew. This down time became vital for blossoming my ideas, and for the films vision taking shape.
           <br/>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <a href="http://bigvoicepictures.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Creative-Process.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="the creative process " class="aligncenter wp-image-1155" height="367" src="http://bigvoicepictures.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Creative-Process-300x200.jpg" width="550"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://bigvoicepictures.com/blog/2014/10/21/steps-to-planning-your-media-video-and-documentary-project/&amp;media=http://bigvoicepictures.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Creative-Process-300x200.jpg&amp;description=Steps to Planning your Media, Video and Documentary Project')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           So, give yourself time to address each of these points. Revisit each one until you feel youve defined each point. Perfection isnt necessary. Feeling good about what you wrote is.
           <br/>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            1. Statement of Purpose/Objectives:
           </b>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           a short paragraph or sentence stating why the project is important. Explain if and how your project will solve a social conflict, reveal and/or meet a need.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            2. A visual synopsis or treatment of the proposed project including (1- 4pages):
           </b>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           A clear statement/paragraph about what your project is about.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           How you will illustrate this story visually to tell a compelling story? (film making, photography, documentary, etc?) What style is your project? (for example, for a documentary, is it a historical documentary with archival footage, or cinema verite? For a photography project, is it black and white photography, and why this choice? Are you writing a memoir or a non-fiction book?) Include specific creative elements, themes, story structure, media elements, and details youll incorporate. Describe your project in a clear way that will help the reader visualize your project.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
             If relevant, describe any characters or on-camera participants, and what role they play in your project. For instance, for a main character in a film you might address struggles and triumphs they face, and possible outcomes of their story in relationship to your projects message. Or an interview with an expert will support specific main topic points your video project addresses.
            </span>
            <br/>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            3. Bios of your team with specific roles for your project.
            <br/>
           </b>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            4. Budget for Project from start to finish.
           </b>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            5. Fundraising Strategy
           </b>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           (if relevant)
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            6. Status of the Project
           </b>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           (what phase of the project are you in now, what youve done so far)
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            7. Timeline for Pre-production and Production
           </b>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           (week by week details of every task needed toward completion). Be sure to check them off each time..trust me, it gives you hope that this long-winded project will someday be completed!
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            8. Distribution Strategy.
           </b>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           Who is your audience? Where will you distribute the project? (for example, a documentary can be distributed through DVDs to libraries and educational organizations, ancillary, theatrical, broadcast, etc.)
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            9. Publicity and Social Network Strategy
            <br/>
           </b>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            10. Timeline for Distribution, Social Network/Publicity
           </b>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT" style="padding-left: 30px;">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           <b>
            11. Outreach/Audience Engagement Strategy
           </b>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           : What specific plans do you have for reaching your audience? How will you engage communities and your audience? (Examples: Film screenings and panel discussions, community media exhibits, speaking at colleges,etc., webinars, pod-casts)
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           Although writing a media proposal can be challenging, it will inspire you to move your project forward.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           Was the above outline helpful in getting you started on your dream project? Or did it refuel an idea youve put aside?
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="LEFT">
         <span style="color: #000000; font-size: 14pt;">
          <span style="font-family: Arial,serif;">
           Let us know how we help you further define your project!
           <br/>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
