      <h1 class="inner">
       What Methods of Teeth Whitening are Available?
      </h1>
      <div class="para">
       Having a whiter, brighter smile is the dream of many patients that visit Dr. John Aurelia's practice. Thanks to professional teeth whitening, Rochester Hills' patients can enjoy a smile that rivals that of their favorite Hollywood stars.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Teeth can become dull and discolored over time thanks to what we drink, eat, and our own personal habits. With teeth whitening, patients can reverse stains and discoloration that may have occurred and can bring forth whiter enamel.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       There are many options available for patients who are considering teeth whitening with Dr. John Aurelia.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        In-Office Whitening
       </strong>
       <br/>
       For those who want the fastest results, Dr. John Aurelia offers in-office teeth whitening called Zoom! whitening. In as little as one hour, patients can have a whiter, brighter smile! The dentist will paint the bleaching solution onto the patient's teeth, and a specialized light is used to activate the whitening solution. When the treatment is complete, the whitening gel is removed from the teeth and the patient is ready to go about their day with a smile that can be as much as eight shades whiter!
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Whitening Trays
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Dr. John Aurelia offers whitening trays, which can be used to whiten teeth at home. The trays are made with impressions of the patient's teeth in order to get the proper fit, and are fabricated from medical-grade plastic. Whitening gel is sent home with the patient to use with the trays and whiten regularly in the comfort of their own home. This allows a patient to whiten over a period of several weeks and gives the patient more control over their results.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Whitening Strips
       </strong>
       <br/>
       Professional grade whitening strips are also available from Dr. John Aurelia. These strips are placed over the teeth and left on for a specific period of time to whiten teeth in several weeks. The whitening strips are safe and easy to use for patients of all ages.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Dr. John Aurelia loves to see his patients smile, and enjoys seeing the boost of self-confidence that they receive when they whiten their teeth. Are you considering doing the same? Call his practice today to schedule a consultation appointment to determine which whitening solution is best for you!
