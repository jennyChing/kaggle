                     Holiday Gift Guide ~ Day 2 ~ Wisconsin Cheeseman
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3184043098839276715" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-3PAdjnjFskg/VFrp7NtKqRI/AAAAAAAAK1c/MkYPgPCX6Zo/s1600/imageedit_37_8522491220.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                      I'm so excited to have Wisconsin Cheeseman joining our Holiday Gift Guide again this year! We recently were sent The Big Show to review!
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://www.wisconsincheeseman.com/" target="_blank">
                       Wisconsin Cheeseman
                      </a>
                      was started in 1947 and has been one of the leading food gift companies in the US. They offer a nice variety of cheeses, meats, chocolates and more! We have been getting their catalogs for as long as I can remember and we always place an order or two during the holiday season! I really like the gifting ability with them because you can choose your mailing date so you can make sure your gift arrives right on time ~ do it early and no more forgetting gifts!
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">

                       The Big Show
                      </a>
                      was no different!
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-c2qZrqs1yUU/VFrp7GCSc7I/AAAAAAAAK1Y/sY6bb2x21jk/s1600/photo%2B(1).JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-c2qZrqs1yUU/VFrp7GCSc7I/AAAAAAAAK1Y/sY6bb2x21jk/s1600/photo%2B(1).JPG"/>
                      </a>
                      What's all included in this gift set?
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <div>
                       ~ Mellow &amp; Sharp Cheddar, Brick, Monterey Jack, Colby cuts; Cheddar, Brick, Mont. Jack, Colby wedges
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       ~ 10 All-Beef Sausages (Garlic and Original)
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       ~ Cheddar spread, tangy cheese spreads &amp; mini spreads
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       ~ Mini Mint Tortes
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       The sausages were our favorites and definitely was the first item gone from the tray. It was my first time trying the Garlic Sausage and it was just as tasty as the original! Colby was the next cheese that was gone and the Bacon Cheddar Spread. We just received our new catalog and it's on our list to get again closer to Christmas!
