             <h1>
              Inspiration
             </h1>
             <p>
              Just like all great innovations, there were others before us that paved the way and inspired our creation. Here are just a few:
             </p>
             <p>
              Steen Strand of
              <a href="http://www.freebord.com" target="_blank">
               Freebord
              </a>
              is the godfather of alternative ways to snowboard, and was a huge inspiration in developing this product. My
              <a href="http://www.metro-board.com" target="_blank">
               Metroboard
              </a>
              showed me how fun andexhilarating electric skateboards can be, and that this is an excellent option for commuting.The
              <a href="http://www.boostedboards.com" target="_blank">
               Boosted Board
              </a>
              showed everyone that electric skateboards can be beautifully designed despite all of the bulky components that go inside.
             </p>
             <p>
              The result of our intensive development process, driven by our experiences, inspiration and personal knowledge, led to the development of new and original designs that made this product possible - such as the invention of the world's first tandem-rotating motorized caster wheel, motor assemblies, battery configurations and more.
             </p>
             <p>
              We are excited for the future, and we can't wait to see the LEIF project develop alongside other visionaries that are inspired in the same ways!
             </p>

             <p>
              The LEIF is an advanced product and getting into production is never easy.

             </p>

              Certification can take only a few days or much longer if designs need to be modified. We will be working with testing labs that can help with this process.

              <ul class="faqs">
               <li class="faq" id="project_faq_101322">
                <div class="faq-question">
                 <a class="js-faq-question-toggle" href="#project_faq_101322" name="project_faq_101322">
                  <span class="ss-icon ss-navigateright">
                  </span>
                  <span class="question">
                   Why is the price set at $1,300?
                  </span>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div class="faq-answer">
                 <p>
                  The technology required to make the LEIF powered and move just like a snowboard is much more complicated than any other electric skateboard. The tech behind our tandem-rotating caster wheel requires a lot of custom and expensive components. We are also using the best batteries on the market, since they will go 16,000 miles before needing to be replaced!
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  This tech, as well as the fact the board needs 2 sets of high-end motors and custom components, are why the price is $1,300. It seems high at first, but it is a bargain compared to other high-end electric skateboards like the Boosted Board ($2,000) or Evolve ($1,500) that don't have the snowboard capabilities.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  It burns our hearts to have the price where it is, but this will change over time. This project is simply about having the capabilities of being able to snowboard everywhere. We are hoping to connect with others that feel the same way about snowboarding everywhere through this KickStarter campaign. Maybe we'll find enough like-minded people to get funded, maybe not. But our hearts compel us to try our hardest to make this happen!
