      <div class="post-header">
       <h2 class="post-title">
        <a href="http://www.makeandtakes.com/rudolph-the-red-nose-reindeer-thumbprint-cards" rel="bookmark" title="Rudolph the Red Nose Reindeer Thumbprint Cards">
         Rudolph the Red Nose Reindeer Thumbprint Cards
        </a>
       </h2>
      </div>
      <div class="post-content">
       <div class="at-above-post addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide" data-title="Rudolph the Red Nose Reindeer Thumbprint Cards" data-url="http://www.makeandtakes.com/rudolph-the-red-nose-reindeer-thumbprint-cards">
       </div>
       <div class="at-above-post-recommended addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide">
       </div>
       <p>
        These cute little cards are perfect for the holidays. You can gift these to friends or create 8 little reindeer to display on your mantle for a fun decoration. And its a great activity for kids, theyll love to stamp their thumbs!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18078" height="367" src="//www.makeandtakes.com/wp-content/uploads/Thumbprint-Stamped-Reindeer-Card.jpg" title="Thumbprint Stamped Reindeer Card" width="550"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Supplies for Reindeer Thumbprint Cards
        </strong>
        :
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         cardstock paper
        </li>
        <li>
         brown stamping ink
        </li>
        <li>
         brown pipe cleaner
        </li>
        <li>
         scissors
        </li>
        <li>
         red pom pom
        </li>
        <li>
         googly eyes
        </li>
        <li>
         glue
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18079" height="367" src="//www.makeandtakes.com/wp-content/uploads/Thumbprint-Reindeer-Card-Supplies.jpg" title="Thumbprint Reindeer Card Supplies" width="550"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        1. Create a small folded card from your cardstock. My card measures 34 when folded.
       </p>
       <p>
        2. Press a thumb into the brown ink and stamp the thumb onto the center of the folded card.
       </p>
       <p>
        3. With a pair of scissors, pierce 2 holes into the paper, on the top sides of the thumbprint, where the antlers will go. NOTE: this part is for an adult. Please be careful, its sharp!
       </p>
       <p>
        4. Cut your pipe cleaner into a 3-4 inch piece. Thread it through one hole and back out through the second hole. Bend the pipe cleaner to give it a bended antler look. And you can add a drop of glue to the back of the antlers to hold them in place if they start to fall forward when the card is upright.
       </p>
       <p>
        5. Glue on 2 googly eyes and a red pom pom nose.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18080" height="367" src="//www.makeandtakes.com/wp-content/uploads/Rudolph-the-Reindeer-Thumbprint-Card.jpg" title="Rudolph the Reindeer Thumbprint Card" width="550"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        6. You can add a fun saying at the bottom of your card. I added the words, Ho Ho Ho, and used small green pom poms for the o letter!
       </p>
       <p>
        Now Rudolph the Red Nose Reindeer is ready to light up the night!
       </p>
