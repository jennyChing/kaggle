           <h2>
            The Importance of Hand Protection
           </h2>
           <div class="entry">
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.gmservices.ws/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Hand-Protection.jpg">
              <img alt="Hand-Protection" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-638" height="300" src="http://www.gmservices.ws/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Hand-Protection-172x300.jpg" width="172"/>
             </a>
             Those who work within the construction field are continuously using their fingers and hands to do their job. Should an injury occur to this area of their body, the construction worker will not be able to continue working, possibly ever again depending on the damage. With so many risks present on a construction site, it is imperative that construction workers use proper hand protection while on the job. Glove manufacturers have taken notice in the need for proper hand protection on construction sites, and are producing a higher quality product for the construction industry. So why are gloves so important for hand protection?
            </p>
            <p>
             <span id="more-637">
             </span>
            </p>
            <ol>
             <li>
              Lengths of Wear- Gloves are being created with a stronger, yet lighter material to help with the length of wear. Gloves are needed at all times on the job to protect your hands and fingers from the elements of the job. If your gloves are weak, or gloves arent worn at all, you are leaving your hands vulnerable to serious injury.
             </li>
             <li>
              Durability- Gloves are being made to be strong against the harsh elements of a construction site. This means they will be able to handle being brushed against concrete and wood without breaking down and leaving your hands exposed. Your bare fingers and hands would be unable to function properly if put under extreme stress for long periods of time.
             </li>
             <li>
              Chemical Protection- If you are handling dangerous chemicals, hand protection is essential. Your hands and fingers could become seriously damaged should a harsh chemical come within direct contact of your bare skin. Chemicals can affect your skin, blood, and nerves depending on their strength and composition.
             </li>
             <li>
              Resistance- Gloves are also being created to provide hand protection against cuts. The materials being used are cut resistant, meaning should you slip with a sharp tool; your hands will be protected.
             </li>
             <li>
              Grip- Using your bare hands to grip certain types of material can be almost impossible. Hand protection allows you to grip and move materials efficiently and safely.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             G &amp; M Services is proud of our employees and the dedication they have for safety in the work place. Our employees are also rewarded for positive safety practices. We also incorporate weekly and monthly discussions and meetings to ensure that all involved are aware of how to handle equipment and potential situations that could occur during a project.
            </p>
