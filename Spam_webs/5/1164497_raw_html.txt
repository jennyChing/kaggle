          <p>
           <img alt="amazing-beaches-glass-beach-california" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-644" height="768" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/amazing-beaches-glass-beach-california.jpg" width="1024"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           The
           <a href="http://www.boredpanda.com/glass-beach-in-california/" target="_blank">
            Glass Beach
           </a>
           is located in MacKerricher State Park in California and is famous for its sea glass beachfront that was created from years of garbage-dumping in the coastline near the northern part of the town.
          </p>
          <h2>
           The Hidden Beach of Marieta, Mexico
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="article-0-1A2CE9E0000005DC-259_964x665" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-645" height="665" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/article-0-1A2CE9E0000005DC-259_964x665.jpg" width="964"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           The area was once a secluded strip of sand formed after it was used by the
           <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2336764/Worlds-idyllic-bomb-site-Hidden-beach-created-giant-blast-Mexican-government-target-practice-stunning-strip-sand.html" target="_blank">
            Mexican government
           </a>
           as a target practice for military exercises. Now, its an attraction of clear waters, and heaven-looking beaches.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Glowing Beach, Maldives
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="A Maldives Beach That Looks like an ocean lightened with blue stars 5" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-646" height="800" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/A-Maldives-Beach-That-Looks-like-an-ocean-lightened-with-blue-stars-5.jpg" width="1200"/>
           <img alt="o-BIO-facebook" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-647" height="1000" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/o-BIO-facebook.jpg" width="2000"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           A fairy-tale-ish view, the Maldives beach is one of the most magical places to visit on Earth; but dont get misguided, there is some science behind this.
          </p>
          <p>
           According to
           <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2014/05/14/maldives-beach-bioluminescence_n_5309928.html" target="_blank">
            James Morin of Cornell,
           </a>
           this phenomena is brought about by organisms called
           <em>
            ostracod crustaceans
           </em>
           . These animals emit light for a longer time than bioluminescent phytoplanktons which were thought to be responsible for the glowing beach.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Pink Sand Beach of Bahamas
          </h2>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <img alt="Viajar-a-Pink-Sand-Beach" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-649" height="866" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Viajar-a-Pink-Sand-Beach.jpg" width="1300"/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Pink-Sands-Beach-Bahamas" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-648" height="1199" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Pink-Sands-Beach-Bahamas.jpg" width="1600"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           The pink sand of this Bahamas beach is not really sand but washed-up coral remains.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Maho Beach, Saint Martin
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="airplanefb" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-650" height="3264" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/airplanefb.jpg" width="4928"/>
          </p>
          <div class="wpInsert wpInsertInPostAd wpInsertMiddle" style="margin: 0 auto; padding: 10px;background:#eaeaea;">
           <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
           </script>
           <!-- Danglemouse-New-Mid-of-post -->
           <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-9358606161538649" data-ad-format="auto" data-ad-slot="6730836418" style="display:block">
           </ins>
           <script>
            (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
           </script>
          </div>
          <p>
           This beach is so close to the airport runway that you can actually see the planes close up! Airplanes arriving the airport must touch down as close as possible to the runway because it is relatively short. This results to the aircraft flying closer to the beach at minimal altitude.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Green Sand Beach,French Guiana
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="Green_sand_in_Kourou" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-651" height="2736" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Green_sand_in_Kourou.jpeg" width="3648"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           This beach is one of only four green beaches in the world: Talofofo, Punta Cormorant and Hornidalsvatnet. The green color comes from
           <em>
            olivine.
           </em>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Punaluu Black Sand Beach, Hawaii
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="Punaluu_Black_Sand_Beach,_Hawaii,_USA6" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-652" height="2592" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Punaluu_Black_Sand_Beach_Hawaii_USA6.jpg" width="3888"/>
           <img alt="Punaluu5" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-653" height="1200" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Punaluu5.jpg" width="1600"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           This beach is popular for its
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Punalu'u_Beach" target="_blank">
            black sand
           </a>
           that comes from basalt, created by lava flowing into the oceans and explodes as it reaches the ocean and cools. This beach is also home to the endangered Hawksbill and Green turtles.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Red Sand Beach, Rabida, Galapagos
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="Red Sand Beach" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-655" height="768" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Red-Sand-Beach.jpg" width="1024"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           The red color of the ground on Rabida The color of the beach oin this island is due to the oxidization of lava that flowed through the beachfront. However, some argue that its because of dead corals.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Pfeiffer Purple Sand Beach, California
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="DSC_0063-1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-657" height="1071" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/DSC_0063-1.jpg" width="1600"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           While
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            most sand in beaches come from Quartz, a translucent mineral that gives the sand a light color, Pfeiffers sand is made up of manganese garnet.
           </span>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Cave Beach, Portugal
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="2048" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-658" height="1365" src="http://danglingmouse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/2048.jpg" width="2048"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           A beach in a cave? Yes way!
          </p>
