                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Every Man Wants A Canon!!
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5219276247475583736" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      As the post title suggest, I think it would be quite hard to find a man who doesn't want a cannon! However, I'm not advocating buying a weapon of immense destruction, something a bit less intimidating comes to mind.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       I think there is something very special about photography, whether it's the pursuit of that perfect shot, or the capturing of memories, it can be breathtaking. Now I know that not everyone is going to share my passion for photography, butnonetheless, I believe a camera can make a fantastic gift for the man in your life.
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       The
                       <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004J3V90Y/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B004J3V90Y&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=gifome07-20">
                        Canon EOS Rebel T3i
                       </a>
                       <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=gifome07-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B004J3V90Y" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
                       is a great camera to start off with, offering a fantastic still image quality, along with decent video capabilities.
                      </span>
                      <br/>
                      <a name="more">
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                      <tbody>
                       <tr>
                        <td style="text-align: center;">
                         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-AlL7M1-9gvI/UVwyCvngWcI/AAAAAAAAAHQ/dHQ2boTfP7Y/s1600/81K9ypOYNDL._AA1500_.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                          <img border="0" height="400" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-AlL7M1-9gvI/UVwyCvngWcI/AAAAAAAAAHQ/dHQ2boTfP7Y/s400/81K9ypOYNDL._AA1500_.jpg" width="400"/>
                         </a>
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                          The Canon 600D
                         </span>
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                      </tbody>
                     </table>
                     <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <div>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       Before I go into any details about thecameralet me tell you it is alsoreferred to as the
                       <a href="http://www.amazon.co.uk/gp/product/B004MPQXZ0/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1634&amp;creative=19450&amp;creativeASIN=B004MPQXZ0&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=gifome-21">
                        Canon EOS 600D Digital SLR Camera (inc. 18-55 mm f/3.5-5.6 IS II Lens Kit)
                       </a>
                       <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://www.assoc-amazon.co.uk/e/ir?t=gifome-21&amp;l=as2&amp;o=2&amp;a=B004MPQXZ0" style="border:none !important; margin:0px !important;" width="1"/>
                       . With a resolution of 18 megapixels and High Definition recording capabilities this camera is sure to get your man off ontheright foot. Prices vary so it's worth shopping around. The lowest I found was $469.99, and the highest $748.99! If you throw in a few accessories such as a carry bag or extra lenses, you will have a great starter camera package! I think it's also worth noting that this camera has an LCD flip-out screen that's ideal for capturing videos orcontinuousstill shooting.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <h3>
                       <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                        Pros :
                       </span>
                      </h3>
                      <div>
                       <ul>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                          Great for both still pictures and videos
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                          Reasonably priced for the quality
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                          Stylish Canon design, attractive camera
                         </span>
                        </li>
                       </ul>
                       <h3>
                        <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                         Cons :
                        </span>
                       </h3>
                      </div>
                      <div>
                       <ul>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                          You may need to buy some accessories (Flash, Bag etc.)
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                          Photography isn't for everyone! Be sure before you buy
                         </span>
                        </li>
                       </ul>
                       <span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                        I hope you enjoyed the latest installment in my
                        <a href="http://idealgiftsformen.blogspot.ie/">
                         Gifts For Men
                        </a>
                        series, I'll be back with more great gift ideas soon!
