        <h1 itemprop="headline">
         Ooh, The Porsche Is Coming To Forza 4
        </h1>
        <div class="meta-wrap">
         <div class="meta">
          <a class="tracking_article_author" data-tracking-name="Mark Serrels" href="/author/mark-serrels/" rel="author">
           <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-24 photo" height="24" src="http://0.gravatar.com/avatar/e1854dd0df67d612eedb27bdc6f33fb7?s=24&amp;d=http%3A%2F%2F0.gravatar.com%2Favatar%2Fad516503a11cd5ca435acc9bb6523536%3Fs%3D24&amp;r=G" width="24"/>
           <span itemprop="author">
            Mark Serrels
           </span>
          </a>
          <time datetime="2012-03-12T09:15:12+11:00" itemprop="datePublished">
           12 March 2012 9:15 AM
          </time>
         </div>
         <div class="social tracking_article_social">
          <a class="btn btn-mini share anchor-link" data-target="#post-495053 .collapse" data-toggle="collapse" data-tracking-name="Share" href="#share" rel="nofollow" title="Share">
           <i class="icon-share-alt">
           </i>
           Share
          </a>
          <a class="btn btn-mini discuss anchor-link" data-tracking-name="Discuss" href="#comments" rel="nofollow" title="Discuss">
           <i class="icon-comment">
           </i>
           Discuss
          </a>
          <a class="btn btn-mini bookmark disabled" data-post-id="495053" data-target="/wp-admin/admin-ajax.php?id=login_to_bookmark" data-toggle="modal" data-tracking-name="Bookmark" title="Bookmark this post">

        <div id="content_post" itemprop="articleBody">
         <div class="media_wrap image legacy center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/k/2012/03/Release_Image_pbs_WM_352012.png" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_legacy" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/k/2012/03/Release_Image_pbs_WM_352012.png"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          Normally new car announcements for video games are hardly worth writing about  particularly for me, a man with no interest in cars or racing games  but after years of wrangling, it looks as though the Porsche is finally coming to the Forza Motorsport series.
          <span id="more-495053">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          So what was the delay? Apparently there were licensing issues surrounding the Porsche, but now an upcoming piece of DLC will feature 30 new Porsche cars for afficiendos to get all sploongy about.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its a pretty extensive update  in addition to the cars themselves, 20 Porsche centric events are being added to the game. Once downloaded players can expect to see Porsches hooning around in other events as well.
         </p>
         <p>
          The upcoming Porsche Expansion Pack for Forza Motorsport 4 is great news for racing fans, said Turn 10 Creative Director Dan Greenawalt, and Im pleased that weve been able to work closely with EA on an agreement that brings one of the worlds most iconic automotive brands to Forza 4.
         </p>
         <p>
          Im assuming this of interest to car people, but I wouldnt know. I still drive a Hyundai Excel. And that doesnt embarrass me.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://forzamotorsport.net/en-us/en-us/porsche_expansion_announce/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://forzamotorsport.net']);">
           Porsche Expansion Pack
