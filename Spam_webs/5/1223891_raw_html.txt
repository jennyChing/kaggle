        <p>
         Turkish artistSeluk Ylmaz has created anexceptional lion sculpture from almost 4,000 pieces of scrap metal. TitledAslan (Turkish for Lion), the sculpture took 10 months to complete and weighs roughly550 pounds (250kg).Selukhand-cut and hammered every piece by himself, and metal-work is not easy.
         <em>
          It needs patience and we have to know pain,
         </em>
         said the artist on DeviantArt.
        </p>
