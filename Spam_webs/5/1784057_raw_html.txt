       <div class="post_content entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          <strong>
           Kingzone Z1 Smartphone
          </strong>
          : You may not much familiar with Kingzone mobile company but you should know that this is most famous mobile manufacturing company in China. Now
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <em>
            <strong>
             Kingzone Z1 Smartphone
            </strong>
           </em>
          </span>
          is new version launched by this company this year. Z1 Smartphone runs on latest version of
          <b>
           Android v4.4.4 KitKat
          </b>
          operating system and based on 64 Bit MT6752 Processor.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.techsradar.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Kingzone-Z1-Smartphone-Features.jpg">
          <img alt="Kingzone Z1 Smartphone" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1085" height="300" src="http://www.techsradar.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Kingzone-Z1-Smartphone-Features-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Kingzone Z1 Smartphone Features
           </span>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          Z1 Smartphone features all the latest technology which are in need for current generation smartphones. As mentioned above Z1 features brand new
          <b>
           64 Bit Octa Core MT6752 Processor
          </b>
          with clock rate at
          <b>
           1.7GHz
          </b>
          . it will be complemented with
          <b>
           2GB RAM
          </b>
          on board along with
          <b>
           16GB ROM
          </b>
          facility which is further expandable upto 64GB via MicroSD card. It also sports with
          <b>
           5.5 inch HD display
          </b>
          with 720p resolution and protected by Gorilla Glass Screen.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>

        <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           <span style="font-size: medium;">
            Kingzone Z1 Smartphone Camera and Connectivity
           </span>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          Turning back, we have
          <b>
           13 MP Primary
          </b>
          camera along with AF and Flash and
          <b>
           8MP Secondary
          </b>
          camera. Well this is a budgeted smartphone but it do support 4G LTE networks. But other supported brands are WCDMA 850/900/2100MHz, FDD-LTE 800/1800/2100/2600MHz, GSM 850/900/1800/1900MHz. Apart from these feature, it is
          <b>
           Dual SIM
          </b>
          supported with
          <b>
           Dual Standby
          </b>
          facility. Bluetooth v4.0, GPS and Wi-Fi are some general connectivity specs available in it. The main and remarkable feature available in Z1 Smartphone is its large battery of 3500 mAh capacity which is expected to sustain up to 8 Days durations. As obvious it will runs on Android v4.4.4 KitKat Operating system.
         </span>
        </p>
