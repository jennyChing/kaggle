                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
                       <a href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/f4/Baby_elephants3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="262" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/f4/Baby_elephants3.jpg" width="320"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elephant">
                        Image Source
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <ul style="text-align: left;">
                       <li>
                        Elephants are strong animals. Their trunk can hold up to 600 pounds and carry 20,000 pounds.
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        Female Elephants get males to fight for them and she gives birth to a 140kg baby after a 22-month pregnancy  the longest gestation period in the animal kingdom.
                       </li>
                      </ul>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <b>
                         <span>
                          6.) Gorilla
                         </span>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
                       <a href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/18/Gorilla_gorilla08.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="214" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/18/Gorilla_gorilla08.jpg" width="320"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gorilla">
                        Image Source
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <ul style="text-align: left;">
                       <li>
                        Gorillas are strong creatures. They can lift 4,600 pounds which is equivalent to two family cars.
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        In the movie King Kong, he is depicted as a carnivore but in reality, gorillas are strictly herbivorous.
                       </li>
                      </ul>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <b>
                         <span>
                          7.) Naked Mole Rat
                         </span>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
                       <a href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cf/Naked_Mole_Rat_Eating.jpg/713px-Naked_Mole_Rat_Eating.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="269" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cf/Naked_Mole_Rat_Eating.jpg/713px-Naked_Mole_Rat_Eating.jpg" width="320"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Naked_mole_rat">
                        Image Source
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <ul style="text-align: left;">
                       <li>
                        Naked Mole rats teeth are very tough. Its incisor bite can go through a cement wall little by little.
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        Like ants and termites, one queen Naked Mole Rat, controls hundreds of Naked Mole Rats.
                       </li>
                      </ul>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <b>
                         <span>
                          8.) Orangutan
                         </span>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
                       <a href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/0b/Orangutan.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="320" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/0b/Orangutan.jpg" width="218"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orangutan">
                        Image Source
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <ul style="text-align: left;">
                       <li>
                        Mother Orangutans are very caring and passionate mothers. They build beds every night for them and their youngster.
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        They swings over 61m high in the trees on a limb while baby Orangutan hangs on their mother for dear life.
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        Orangutans are smart animals. They have found a way to escape the rain by putting a large leaf on top of their heads.
                       </li>
                      </ul>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <b>
                         <span>
                          9.) Platypus
                         </span>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
                       <a href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/f2/Platypus.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="215" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/f2/Platypus.jpg" width="320"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Platypus">
                        Image Source
                       </a>

                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>

                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <ul style="text-align: left;">
                       <li>
                        Platypuses have beaks of ducks, flippers of Otters and tails of beavers.
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        They can detect the electrical impulses given off by its prey.
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        Male Platypuses hind limbs are venomous.
                       </li>
                      </ul>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>

                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-size: large;">
                        <b>
                         <span>
                          10.) Hippopotamus
                         </span>
                        </b>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
                       <a href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/33/Hippo_walking.jpg/800px-Hippo_walking.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="226" src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/33/Hippo_walking.jpg/800px-Hippo_walking.jpg" width="320"/>
                       </a>

                       <br/>

                      <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      </div>
                      <ul style="text-align: left;">
                       <li>
                        Male Hippopotamuses are yucky animals. They fling dung or spread their poops everywhere with their tail.
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        They are mighty biters. They can open their jaw up to 170 and snap a crocodile in half.
                       </li>
                      </ul>
