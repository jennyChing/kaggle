        <h2>
         <span style="color: #ff6433;">
          Interesting facts about some of theGame of Thrones characters
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff6433;">
          <strong>
           Daenerys Targaryen
          </strong>
         </span>
         Mother of Dragons istheyoungest survivor of the Targaryen family. Daenerys marriedKhal Drogobut theirlove story did not last that long with Drogo dying (like everyone else). Daenerys had to start alone with her 3 dragons and her brave, promising leadership attributes. She is said to be much younger in the books than she appears on the series.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff6433;">
          <strong>
           Khal Drogo
          </strong>
         </span>
         The Dothraki leader never lost a battle as displayed by his long hair (they put bells for every victory and cut their hair if they lose). Silent, tough, strong and respected; its a shame his character did not last long as viewers wanted.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff6433;">
          <strong>
           Tyrion Lannister
          </strong>
         </span>
         has always been a favorite. He is not tall, dark and handsome; in fact he is a dwarf but he is powerful because of his intelligence. He is funny and seems to take things lightly but he always gets the best out of a bad situation. He is an underdog that everyone adores.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff6433;">
          <strong>
           Sandor Clegane
          </strong>
         </span>
         The Hound is atough, heavily scarred guy with his left side of face ruined by fire. From that incident, he takes it as a lesson that violence is the only power in the world. Hes not all evil as we later witnessed how he looked after and protected Arya.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff6433;">
          <strong>
           Jon Snow
          </strong>
         </span>
         has always been pertained to as the bastard of the Starks but also is one of the most loved characters. He is the commander of the Nights Watch and the only older Stark family member left. He is kind, just, silent and even was once romantic.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #ff6433;">
          <strong>
           Eddard Stark
          </strong>
         </span>
         -the perfect dad who has been kind to all of his children and treats them equally. Short role but also one of the early favorites. Loyal friend, great leader.
        </p>
