              No-Bake Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Truffles
             </a>
            </h1>
            <p class="byline">
             Written on
			August 29, 2013			at
			8:30 am			, by
             <a href="http://www.bhg.com/blogs/delish-dish/author/bhgguestblogger/" rel="author" title="Posts by BHG Guest Blogger">

          <p>
           Confession: I am a cookie dough lover. Obsessed actually. I know youre not supposed to eat the dough because of the raw egg, etc. but it is way too tempting. When I saw these no-bake chocolate chip cookie dough truffles on
           <a href="http://www.bhg.com/recipe/cookies/chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-truffles/" target="_blank">
            BHG
           </a>
           that contained no egg I was sold. This recipe is super easy to make and is quite the crowd pleaser. I made them for a few friends and they were gone within minutes!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.bhg.com/recipe/cookies/chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-truffles/">
            <img alt="No-bake chocolate chip cookie dough truffles" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3436" height="896" src="http://blogs.mydevstaging.com/blogs/delish-dish/files/2013/08/Cookie-dough-truffles.jpg" width="640"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Here is the list of ingredients you will need to make these yummy truffles.
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            1/3cupbutter, softened
           </li>
           <li>
            1/3cuppacked brown sugar
           </li>
           <li>
            1tablespoonvanilla
           </li>
           <li>
            1cupall-purpose flour
           </li>
           <li>
            1 cupmilk chocolate chips
           </li>
           <li>
            2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (for dipping)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div>
           Find the instructions for this recipe
           <a href="http://www.bhg.com/recipe/cookies/chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-truffles/" target="_blank">
            here
           </a>
           .
          </div>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.bhg.com/recipe/cookies/chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-truffles/">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3439" height="427" src="http://blogs.mydevstaging.com/blogs/delish-dish/files/2013/08/cookie-dough-truffles-3.jpg" width="640"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           These truffles only took about 5 minutes to whip up and then 30 minutes to freeze. Seriously perfect for a last minute treat.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.bhg.com/recipe/cookies/chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-truffles/">
            <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3441" height="427" src="http://blogs.mydevstaging.com/blogs/delish-dish/files/2013/08/cookie-dough-truffles-4.jpg" width="640"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Once they were frozen I decided to make things easy and melt chocolate chips in the microwave. The trick to getting the chocolate smooth is to hold the ball with a fork and tap off the excess chocolate on the side of the bowl. Then lay them on wax paper and allow them to harden.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.bhg.com/recipe/cookies/chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-truffles/">
            <img alt="No-bake chocolate chip cookie dough truffles" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3443" height="896" src="http://blogs.mydevstaging.com/blogs/delish-dish/files/2013/08/cookie-dough-truffles-2.jpg" width="640"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           I topped these truffles off with a little sea salt which added the perfect touch. Seriously divine! Throw these truffles in a ziplock bag (if there is any left) and place them in the freezer for whenever you need a little late night snack. ;)
          </p>
          <p>
           These no-bake chocolate chip cookie dough truffles will definitely be made again. Easy and deliciouscant beat that! Find the full recipe
           <a href="http://www.bhg.com/recipe/cookies/chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-truffles/" target="_blank">
            here
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Heres a pinnable image so you can save this recipe.
          </p>

           <img alt="No-bake chocolate chip cookie dough truffles" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3457" height="800" src="http://blogs.mydevstaging.com/blogs/delish-dish/files/2013/08/No-bake-cookie-dough-truffles-336x800.jpg" width="336"/>
          </p>

           Happy baking! xo-
           <a href="http://www.iheartnaptime.net/" target="_blank">
            Jamielyn
           </a>
