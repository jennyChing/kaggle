       <p>
        Faced with new spaces, new forms of expression. Creativity is endless and every opportunity is good to express something with it.
        <b>
         An interesting example has emerged recently in the new Facebook timeline cover, since its release a few months ago.
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        Playing with the elements and combinations the structure provides, we can bring new perspectives. The vast imagination of some users seems to have no limits.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         And you, why not try and 'create' a previously unimagined cover?
        </b>
        The friends who visit your profile will see you in a new light.
       </p>

        Here are some examples.
