          <p>
           Its no secret; Teresa Giudice of The Real Housewives of New Jersey is in deep legal trouble. Were not talking a few parking tickets that went unpaid or even a DUI, which is pretty serious. No, this is serious41 counts of fraud for a variety of different things, all related to money. But, the New York Times bestselling author is back for another season, and her bestie Dina Manzo is back too.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://dailytwocents.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/rhonj-teresa-retreat1.png">
            <img alt="Teresa Giudice" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-57032" src="//dailytwocents.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/rhonj-teresa-retreat1.png"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Were not really sure what fireworks to expect this season. Since Caroline Manzo and Jacqueline Laurita have moved on to other projects and cousin Kathy Wakile has been relegated to friend of the Housewives status, three of the four women who have targeted Teresa in past seasons wont be around to kick her while shes down. Sources day that Teresa and her sister-in-law Melissa Gorga have mended fences, somewhat.
          </p>
          <p>
           Teresa has said she felt compelled to return for the sixth season of RHONJ because thats her job and shes going to keep doing it until they haul her off to prison. How much of the Giudices legal problems will make the air remain to be seen when the new season starts on Sunday, July 13.
          </p>
          <p>
           But we do know this  Teresa is glad Dina is back and Dina is glad to BE back and shes GOT Teresas back. Its a sisterhood Dina says in one of the promos for the show. Ummmokayis that why you dont speak to your sister Caroline or your sister-in-law Jacqueline? And rumors say you dont speak to ANY of your 11 siblings? Lets hope siding with Teresa Giudice doesnt come back to bite Dina in the butt.
          </p>
          <p>
           Are you ready for the new season of RHONJ? I am! Let the games begin! Pop the corn and lets go!
          </p>
