         Hi Friends, Welcome to Tech Freshers Point a site with all information. Most of the Bloggers are failing to optimize their website on search engines, and unable to get the traffic from various search engines. Here in this article, I am reviling the tips and tricks to optimize your website on search engines like Google, Yahoo, Bing and many more
        </p>

        <h1>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          TIPS &amp; TRICKS TO OPTIMIZE YOUR SITE ON SEARCH ENGINES
         </span>
        </h1>
        <h2>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          1. Use Keywords on OnPage
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Try to use keywords using Google Adwords on your article. Actually Google Adwords tool is designed for advertisers on Google. But this is most useful for writing articles on our websites. Try to use keyword in less no. of times. Dont use keywords more no. of times in your article. Google will find out each and every task in your website.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          2. Use More Internal Links
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Try to use more no. of Internal Links in your webpage. This will help search engine to get more traffic. Always use Long tail keywords to internal links.
        </p>

        <h2>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          3. Give External links to Trusted Sites only
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         You can give external links to other websites also. Make sure that your external link is given to trusted site only. Please dont give external links to unknown websites. Depending upon the Alexa Rank you can give external link to those website. All of the bloggers think that, if you give external link to other website our page rank flow will be less. Make sure bloggers google is not going to track page rank now a days already announced by google.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          4.Write more no. of Articles
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         If you write more no. of articles on your blog you will be get succeeded. Most of the blogger write less no. of articles and they feel that they are getting success and they will stop writing articles(blogging career). Remember friends write more no.of articles as much as you can. Minimum of 200 articles will be get succeeded in blogging field.
        </p>

        <h2>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          5. Tips and Tricks to write Articles
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Bloggers, I am going toreviling most most important topic here ie., how to get top in search engines.
        </p>

        <ul>
         <li>
          First Step: Choose what type of Article that you are going to write on your blog.
         </li>
         <li>
          Dont write all the information related to that topic on single article.
         </li>
         <li>
          By writing all the information related to that topic, some times you may get top insearch engines and some times you may not get top ie., even below the 100th search
         </li>
         <li>
          For Example: If you are writing an article related to FACEBOOK
         </li>
         <li>
          Dont write complete information of FACEBOOK on single article
         </li>
         <li>
          Divide those Facebook information into multiple parts ie., for each part write an individual article.
         </li>
         <li>
          Why we need to write multiple article for single topic
         </li>
         <li>
          Generally we write an article in 500 words.
         </li>
         <li>
          If you write multiple articles you will get more no. of words ie., thousand of words
         </li>
         <li>
          With in this thousands of words search engines will get the search data from any of these articles.
         </li>
         <li>
          So, dont write all the information in single article.
         </li>
        </ul>
