     WTC Fire-Proofing -
     <br/>
     Cover for Conspiracy?
    </b>
   </font>
  </p>
  <div align="center">
   <center>
    <table border="0" bordercolor="#111111" cellpadding="7" style="border-collapse: collapse" width="630">
     <tr>
      <td>
       <p align="left">
        Kevin Ryan, formerly of UL, made a post back in January of 2008 
at 9/11 Blogger showing that the floors of the WTC that had the fire-proofing 
upgraded, matched almost exactly to the floors that were impacted and failed in 
both towers:
       </p>
       <p align="center">
        <a href="../IMAGES/wtc_correlation.gif" target="_blank">
         <img alt="Click for full size" border="0" height="363" src="../IMAGES/wtc_correlation.gif" width="630"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.911blogger.com/node/13272" target="_blank">
         www.911blogger.com...
        </a>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        It now turns out the company that did those upgrades is Turner Construction 
company.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://i224.photobucket.com/albums/dd121/88Badmachine88/1-7.jpg" target="_blank">
         i224.photobucket.com...
        </a>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Turner Construction Company also helped plan and oversee the demolition of the 
Seattle Kingdome in 2000:
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://web.archive.org/web/20021016043840/http:/seattlepi.nwsource.com/local/dome04.shtml" target="_blank">
         seattlepi.nwsource.com...
        </a>

        <a href="http://www.stadium.org/news/171.asp" target="_blank">

        <br/>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_tenants_in_One_World_Trade_Center" target="_blank">
         en.wikipedia.org...

steel wreckage of the WTC towers following September 11, 2001:
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_tenants_in_One_World_Trade_Center" target="_blank">
         en.wikipedia.org...
        </a>

        <br/>
        Aidan credits (and I concur) Kevin Ryan &amp; James Gourley:
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Without Kevin Ryan's discovery of the remarkable relationship between the common 
re-fireproofed floors, aircraft impact floors and failure floors, one might not 
have considered the role that this contract work may or may not have played in 
the WTCs demise.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        And without James Gourley's willingness to provide substantial payment for 
access to the referred-to NIST records, the potential leads contained within 
these records might not be known.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        With continued research and investigation, the truth will continue to emerge.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        - EDIT TO ADD - In case it hasn't "clicked" in peoples' minds, alleged plane 
impact at the Pentagon in the area being upgraded. Both towers had impacts of 
planes in the areas that had fireproofing upgraded. What are the odds?
