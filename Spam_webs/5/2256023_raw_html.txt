         <p>
          <em>
           Smooth and creamy dark chocolate hazelnut spread made easily at home. Vegan, gluten-free and even more delicious than the store bought kind!
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://arismenu.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Homemade-Vegan-Nutella1.jpg.jpg">
           <img alt="Homemade Nutella: Vegan, gluten-free and even more delicious than the store bought kind!" class="alignnone wp-image-7611" height="648" src="http://arismenu.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Homemade-Vegan-Nutella1.jpg.jpg" width="432"/>

         </p>
         <p>
          GUESS WHAT????? ITS MY BIRTHDAY!!!!!!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://arismenu.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/nutella-32.jpg">
           <img alt="Homemade Nutella: Vegan, gluten-free and even more delicious than the store bought kind!" class="alignnone wp-image-7618" height="648" src="http://arismenu.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/nutella-32.jpg" width="432"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Okay, so maybe its a little narcissistic, but I freaking love birthdays. Not just mine, but everyones. We all need a day to celebrate our lives, the people in it, and all that jazz. Thats what birthdays are to me, anyway. A chance to celebrate the fact that I have a freaking awesome life filled with freaking awesome people who make me feel like a million bucks.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://arismenu.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/nutella-7.jpg">
           <img alt="Homemade Nutella: Vegan, gluten-free and even more delicious than the store bought kind!" class="alignnone wp-image-7612" height="648" src="http://arismenu.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/nutella-7.jpg" width="432"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          So to celebrate, I made you guys Nutella. Its one of my very favorite things, so i just figured you guys would feel the same way. Ive been obsessed with Nutella since the first time I tasted it, and ya know what? I am even
          <em>
           more
          </em>
          obsessed with this homemade variety. Im pretty sure its the dark chocolate. It just makes it richer and even more chocolatey!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://arismenu.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Homemade-Vegan-Nutella.jpg.jpg">
           <img alt="Homemade Nutella: Vegan, gluten-free and even more delicious than the store bought kind!" class="alignnone wp-image-7610" height="454" src="http://arismenu.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Homemade-Vegan-Nutella.jpg.jpg" width="454"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Its actually super simple, and when you make your own you get to be the one controlling what goes in. Just real ingredients here with a slightly heavy hand on the dark chocolate because, lets be honest, Nutella is totally, 100% dessert. Its not a healthy spreadits made to be a treat, and it delivers!
         </p>

             </p>
             <div id="zlrecipe-summary" itemprop="description">
              <p class="summary italic">
               Smooth and creamy dark chocolate hazelnut spread made easily at home. Vegan, gluten-free and even more delicious than the store bought kind!
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
             Ingredients
            </p>
            <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 cup toasted hazelnuts
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
              2-3 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
              12 ounces dark chocolate, melted and cooled
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 teaspoon vanilla extract
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
              pinch sea salt
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
             Instructions
            </p>
            <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Place hazelnuts in food processor or Vitamix and blend until broken down and nuts start to form a big mound. Continue to blend and stream in coconut oil 1 tablespoon at a time until nut butter consistency. Add melted chocolate, vanilla and sea salt and blend until well combined. Store in an airtight container for 2-3 weeks.
             </li>
