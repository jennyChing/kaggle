       <p>
        Remember way back when we used to go outside and play with our friends? Its the time of the year when most people who go to school or work just want to get away from everything and go offline  as scary as that may sound to some.You are probably checking your pockets right now to make sure your phone or Nintendo is safe! Most of us want to go to the beach, enjoy ourselves and have amazing times with our friends! But apart from talking to each other, what else can you doto entertain yourself and your friends when you get together? What if I tell you, there are games that are not only fun but also develop your skills? Why not try some of these old-school style holiday games?
        <span id="more-75">
        </span>
       </p>
       <h2>
        # Jungle Speed
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 510px">
        <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/juanpablo-santos/3904893478" title="jungle speed by juan pablo santos rodrguez, on Flickr">
         <img alt="Jungle Speed - Holiday Games" height="375" src="https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2564/3904893478_8b66aefd65.jpg" width="500"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/juanpablo-santos/">
          juan pablo santos rodrguez
         </a>
         /
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/juanpablo-santos/3904893478/in/photolist-6X4AMC-5oyfgS-4Dy6pF-4DfCwL-6chyCi-csyX2u-csyVNC-5ibwKA-5ibumb-5i7a9x-6JUneH-6rrFi-aQ1vSH-4S4npd-2iRdkK-6gdWJR-4rxVew-7wRzpW-nJs1Ag-nYU9RW-o1amoC-nJsd47-7FSdkW-o4Z5gF-7GjR9C-o3cMQ2-oq2N7-o1WDhM-avAndt-aahUM3-vkwDG-vku2B-6chxTR-6cmGeG-4DCnSS-ayAv4u-7j2RNi-4DCm5W-fKMBS-9BTEk3-4gfaQx-fAYq4-7Fc6vu-9UtTDN-6isoS3-9Ur5Xc-9UuaZf-9LhhVN-7LAaQ-kZy65X">

        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        If youve never encountered this game before you should give it a go as soon as possible! Aside from being extremely fun, the game is highly competitive and it could definitely inspire some friendly rivalrybetween players. That makes
        <a href="http://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/8098/jungle-speed">
         Jungle Speed
        </a>
        extremely fun to play  you get hooked so quickly you dont notice the time flying. How to play Jungle Speed? You can play it with as many people youd like  the more the merrier. You divide the cards and put the totem in the middle of the table; then everyone starts flipping a card in a clockwise direction, once you see that your top face-up cards has the same symbol as someone elses, you have a go at grabbing the totem first. That way you get rid of all your face-up cards and your opponent gets them all. Whoever ends up with no cards first, wins! In order to make the game more difficult than it already is, there are a number of special cards that make the fun last longer! You think Jungle Speed is only for geeks? The gamecould be played before going out and you can also make up new rules to suit the current moment. Every time someone grabs the totem, they win a penny or a candy out of a box. Aside from the fun factor, the game is extremely good for you because it helps you develop faster reflexes and a quick attention to detail  most of the cards are really similar and the colors could distract you.
       </p>
       <h2>
        # Cards Against Humanity
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 510px">

         <img alt="Day 24/365  01/24/2014: Cards Against Humanity" height="168" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7431/12144081385_244ea0fd9c.jpg" width="500"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/mlcastle/">

         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/btnphoto/12144081385/in/photolist-jv8Aix-bwUPbN-fJtvE3-fJtcqf-bMRwz2-ecEwZG-ecyTTZ-gaRHXF-dN58hN-aywpKt-aywprH-aywpy2-aywpDa-dd6onV-e9HnAD-dWXEBt-dEWKxo-iQYuqK-e9P4es-e9P2J3-e9Hnuk-e9HnHt-e9Ho8k-e9P3SA-e9P3e1-e9P3su-e9P3nY-e9P2fS-e9HmE4-e9P2nJ-e9Hmzt-e9Hn92-e9P33E-e9P42G-e9P2U1-e9P2b9-e9P3D7-e9HnYi-e9Hnn4-e9P2NG-e9HmPX-e9HmKn-d9JSoj-nCsZZ7-ddagEk-emohaG-dkAER1-dhZ8uR-ejVCxg-ipP4X7">

       <p>
        This is definitely the most provocative card game that you will ever face. The games slogan Cards Against Humanity is a party game for horrible people, stays extremely close to the truth. The more awkward or geekier you and your friends are, the more fun you are probably going to have, or at least thats what the creators suggest. How do you play Cards Against Humanity? Its extremely easy  each player is given a number of white answer cards. A designated person chooses the black question card, and waits until people decide which white card theyd like to use. Then the answers get shuffled and read out loud! The funniest, and usually the most obnoxious answer, wins an awesome point! Whoever has the most awesome points by the time there are no white cards left, wins! The popularity of this game is evident  it occupies the top spots in many online retailers charts, among which,
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/bestsellers/toys-and-games/ref=pd_dp_ts_t_1">
         Amazon
        </a>
        . The game has also received numerous positive reviews, including from
        <a href="http://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/50381/cards-against-humanity">
         Board Game Geek
        </a>
        . The games main incentive is simple  to have fun. However, it also could improve your simultaneous action selection and has positive social impact by making it easier to relax. Cards Against Humanity is a fun game you can play anywhere  on the beach, at a picnic with your friends, and before going out, too.
       </p>
       <h2>
        # The Memory Ball Game
       </h2>

       <p>
        If you dont want to do too much physical activityor if volleyball is not your favorite sport, this beach game will definitely keep you and your friends entertained. The only thing you need for the memory game is, like you would assume, one or more balls and a couple of friends. The game is based on the memory games that some of us used to play as kids. The rules are simple  throw just one beach ball and ask everyone to remember who you threw it to. The point of the game the ball needs to bethrown at everyone just once until it is given back to the first person who threw it. You can then start throwing the ball in the same order while including extra beach balls. Apart from being extremely fun and nerve-wrecking, the memory ball game is a great one to improve on your, you probably guessed right memory skills! Not hard or challenging enough? Pause the game at any particular space of time and ask a person to revise the order of people the ball is thrown to. If they get it wrong, you have one less person to worry about. Do you think you will be the last one standing?

       <p>
        This is probablythe ultimate family and party game. Why not give it a shot while on a holiday? The perfect time to play it would be before going out or during a lazy relaxing afternoon! The reason why charades never gets boring is because there are so many different ways you can play  Word Charades, Movie Charades, Celebrity Charades to name a few. Rules are simple, you are given something to impersonate by the opposite team and you have a limited time to explain it to your team, without using any words! Want to improve on those acting or guessing skills? Then this is the game for you. Plus, this fun-tested game will make you laugh, as well!
