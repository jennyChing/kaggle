            <h2 class="entry-title">
             <a href="http://thegreendivas.com/2014/06/10/earth-focus-explores-chemicals-everyday-products-accelerate-every-disease-youve-heard/">
              Exactly How Chemicals in Everyday Products Accelerate Every Disease Youve Heard Of
             </a>
            </h2>
            <div class="meta-box custom-post">
             <span class="entry-date">
              <i class="icon-calendar">
              </i>
              June 10, 2014
             </span>
             <span class="entry-author">
              <i class="icon-user">
              </i>

           <p style="text-align: right;">
            <a href="http://thegreendivas.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Untitled-design-1-640x480.jpg">
             <img alt="chemicals in every day products" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13700" height="480" src="http://thegreendivas.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Untitled-design-1-640x480.jpg" width="640"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: right;">
            <em>
             Written by
             <a href="http://ecowatch.com/2014/06/01/earth-focus-chemicals/" target="_blank">
              Brandon Baker
             </a>
             from
             <a href="http://ecowatch.com" target="_blank">
              EcoWatch
             </a>
             . Published with permission.
             <br/>
            </em>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Soup of chemicals
           </h3>
           <p>
            The recent
            <a href="http://ecowatch.com/2014/05/27/march-against-monsanto-boycott-gmos/" target="_blank">
             March Against Monsanto
            </a>
            proves that our
            <a href="http://ecowatch.com/2013/10/31/sean-penns-human-experiment-toll-toxic-chemicals/" target="_blank">
             awareness of chemicals
            </a>
            has ballooned over the years, but do we truly understand how many
            <a href="http://thegreendivas.com/2014/05/26/michael-green-protecting-us-toxic-chemicals/" target="_blank" title="Michael Green, Protecting Us from Toxic Chemicals">
             chemicals
            </a>
            we put in our bodies and just how unavoidable many of them are?
           </p>
           <p>
            This
            <a href="http://www.linktv.org/earthfocus" target="_blank">
             <em>
              Earth Focus
             </em>
            </a>
            report discusses the soup of chemicals our bodies take in every day from food,
            <a href="http://thegreendivas.com/2014/04/22/3-ways-nanomaterials-may-harm-us/" title="4 Ways Nanomaterials May Harm Us">
             cosmetics
            </a>
            or simply breathing. Even worse, the 84,000
            <a href="http://thegreendivas.com/2014/05/28/detox-the-box-parody-music-video-takes-on-toxic-chemicals-in-feminine-care-products/" target="_blank" title="Hilarious Music Video: Detox the Box Takes on Toxic Chemicals">
             chemicals
            </a>
            that are legal for commerce in the U.S. are accelerating every disease or condition youve ever heard ofdiabetes, asthma, cancer, a slew of birth defects and so much more.
           </p>
           <h4>
            Watch
            <em>
             UNSAFE: The Truth Behind Everyday Chemicals
            </em>
            in its entirety
           </h4>
           <p>
            <span id="more-13589">
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/4VAUh9QtjDE?rel=0" width="560">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <h4>
            There are 13,000 chemicals in
            <a href="http://thegreendivas.com/2014/05/15/5-easy-diys-for-natural-beauty/" target="_blank" title="4 Easy DIYs for Natural Beauty">
             cosmetics
            </a>
            , but only 10 percent of them have been evaluated for safety.
           </h4>
           <p>
            Erin Switalski, executive director of
            <a href="http://www.womensvoices.org/" target="_blank">
             Womens Voices for the Earth
            </a>
            , estimates that there are 13,000 chemicals in
            <a href="http://thegreendivas.com/2014/05/15/5-easy-diys-for-natural-beauty/" target="_blank" title="4 Easy DIYs for Natural Beauty">
             cosmetics
            </a>
            , but only 10 percent of them have been evaluated for safety. The 27-minute report features several other examples that touch every aspect of our lives.
           </p>
           <p>
            The chemicals industry earned $763 billion in sales in 2011 and provides about 3 million jobs. Those figures likely explain how the industry goes largely unregulated, even in the face of the deterioration of human health.
           </p>
           <p>
            The episode goes on to explore how low-dose exposures to different chemicals can quickly add up, the increased chemical
            <a href="http://www.myearth360.com/2014/03/19/toxic-chemicals-ending-products/" target="_blank">
             risk
            </a>
            experienced by low-income communities and whatif anythingcan be done about all of this.
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             EARTHFOCUSairs every Thursday at 9 p.m. ET on Link TV. Episodes are also available to watch online at
             <a href="http://linktv.org/earthfocus" target="_blank">
              linktv.org
             </a>
             .
            </em>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Bonus:
           </h3>
           <p>
            Listen to this excellent
            <a href="http://www.spreaker.com/user/greendivas" target="_blank">
             Green Divas podcast
            </a>
            featuring Susan Freinkel talking about how pesticides impact our children, and also some new segments.
            <br/>
            <iframe frameborder="0" height="150" scrolling="no" src="//www.spreaker.com/embed/player/standard?autoplay=false&amp;episode_id=4477953" style="width: 100%; height: 131px; min-width: 400px;" width="300">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            Watch
            <a href="http://thegreendivas.com/2014/02/26/unacceptable-levels-must-see-film-about-the-toxic-chemicals-in-our-daily-lives/" target="_blank" title="Unacceptable Levels: Must-See Film about Toxic Chemicals in Our Daily Lives">
             <em>
              Unacceptable Levels
             </em>
            </a>
            , a documentary about Director
            <a href="http://thegreendivas.buzzsprout.com/2640/164713-green-dudes-ed-brown-filmmaker-unacceptable-levels" target="_blank">
             Ed Brown

            s quest to learn about toxic chemicals in everyday products and they might be impacting his familys health.
