&lt;h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'&gt;
DIY. Plain tank into grommet racer back.
&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;div class='post-header'&gt;
&lt;div class='post-header-line-1'&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-5463610081261466237' itemprop='description articleBody'&gt;
&lt;div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on"&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-WKJjGQcGdKY/T8Phzfdzg5I/AAAAAAAAB6c/mKEtz7hfBUc/s1600/ellr.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"&gt;&lt;img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-WKJjGQcGdKY/T8Phzfdzg5I/AAAAAAAAB6c/mKEtz7hfBUc/s640/ellr.jpg" height="524" width="640" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
DIY:&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
To view a video go &lt;a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S7VQvQeBW9E"&gt;here:&nbsp;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-6SyBCT6vAPk/T8P2oaHsC7I/AAAAAAAAB7U/3t0VGPcJOgY/s1600/PicMonkey+Collsssage.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"&gt;&lt;img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-6SyBCT6vAPk/T8P2oaHsC7I/AAAAAAAAB7U/3t0VGPcJOgY/s640/PicMonkey+Collsssage.jpg" height="320" width="640" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-0Nz6JNFBKSc/T8P2tMxr56I/AAAAAAAAB7c/O_GH-vFdXUs/s1600/PicMonkey+Collsssassge.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"&gt;&lt;img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-0Nz6JNFBKSc/T8P2tMxr56I/AAAAAAAAB7c/O_GH-vFdXUs/s640/PicMonkey+Collsssassge.jpg" height="320" width="640" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
1) Pin grommet trim starting from back strap and wrapping around the front collar and down the other strap to the back again. Top stitch left side (so you can tie in the back). &nbsp;If you have grommet pliers, just &nbsp; do that all the way around. &nbsp;Tie some string in the back to create a racer back. I like the front as well with the grommet, gives it an edgy look.&lt;br /&gt;
