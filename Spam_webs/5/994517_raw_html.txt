       <h1>
        Super Scandi: Kauniste A/W 2014 Collection
       </h1>
      </div>
      <div class="post-entry">
       <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-cushions.jpg">
         <img alt="kauniste-cushions" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5744" height="346" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-cushions.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        Each time I see a new collection from
        <a href="http://www.kauniste.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.kauniste.com/', 'Kauniste');" target="_blank">
         Kauniste
        </a>
        my decor-loving heart beats that little bit faster: I just *know* its going to be good. Their previous collections (blogged
        <a href="http://www.brightbazaarblog.com/2013/01/scandinavian-design-collective-kauniste.html" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.brightbazaarblog.com/2013/04/a-take-it-easy-weekend-new-kauniste-finds.html" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        ) offered beautiful pieces to layer into the home, and their latest offering is no different. Lets take a look
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-tray.jpg">
         <img alt="kauniste-tray" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5750" height="347" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-tray.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-tea-towels.jpg">
         <img alt="kauniste-tea-towels" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5747" height="389" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-tea-towels.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-tea-towels-1.jpg">
         <img alt="kauniste-tea-towels-1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5746" height="595" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-tea-towels-1.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        Kaunistes new pattern Konfetti is a festive play of simple shapes and pastel colours; it has a touch of lightness and randonmess of everyday life. The pattern was designed by artist Hanna Konola, who is originally from a small village in north of Finland, but she now lives and works in Helsinki. I think there is a really nice contrast between the modern pattern &amp; colours and the more traditional linen material it is printed on.
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-apron.jpg">
         <img alt="kauniste-apron" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5742" height="346" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-apron.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-textiles.jpg">
         <img alt="kauniste-textiles" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5748" height="346" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-textiles.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-throw.jpg">
         <img alt="kauniste-throw" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5749" height="346" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-throw.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-bedspread.jpg">
         <img alt="kauniste-bedspread" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5743" height="346" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-bedspread.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        The brand are also carrying a new 100% pure wool blanket called the Swans  doesnt it look a treat in this gorgeous green colourway? For this item Kauniste collaborated with Elisabeth Dunker from Swedish brand,
        <a href="http://www.finelittleday.com" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.finelittleday.com', 'Fine Little Day');" target="_blank">
         Fine Little Day
        </a>
        (and whose summer house is in the
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1250042011/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1250042011/', 'Bright.Bazaar book');" rel="noskim" target="_blank">
         Bright.Bazaar book
        </a>
        !). Which pieces are catching your eye, folks?
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-design-homewear.jpg">
         <img alt="kauniste-design-homewear" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5745" height="346" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/kauniste-design-homewear.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
       </div>
