         <img alt="mr four fingers - Tessa_2_LR" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8490" height="742" src="http://skullappreciationsociety.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/mr-four-fingers-Tessa_2_LR.jpg" width="600"/>

        <p>
         With 16 days to go untilour
         <a href="http://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/celebrabis-vitae-skull-art-exhibition-31st-october-2014-tickets-12502099115" target="_blank" title="Skull Art Exhbition">
          inaugural skull exhibition
         </a>
         I thought it best to do some serious self promotion, and that is by announcing the involvement of our very own art brand,
         <a href="http://shop.mrfourfingers.com/" target="_blank" title="MR FOUR FINGERS SHOP">
          MR FOUR FINGERS
         </a>
         , in this massive collection of skullart. We are honoured, and bit star struck, to be included among some of our favourite artists and we hope you can join us, and them, for a celebration of life and art through in appreciation of this iconic symbol.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          CELEBRABRIS VITAE Exhibition Details:
         </strong>
        </p>

          <sup>
           st

          October 2014

           th
          </sup>
          November, which falls on a First Thursday

         <li>
          <a href="https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/celebrabis-vitae-skull-art-exhibition-tickets-12502099115">
           Info &amp; Tickets for opening night are available here
