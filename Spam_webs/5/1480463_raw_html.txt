           Mad Max Game now I remember growing up watching the Mad Max flims and instantly loving them, who cant forget Tina Turner as a young kid in that outfit ohhhh my, well Im super excited for 2014 with the released of Mad Max Soul of Man being released around the 30th of April, give yourself a mental note as this is one game i cant wait to buy.
          </p>

           <a href="http://www.playstation4.co.uk/mad-max-game-soul-of-man/madmaxgame2" rel="attachment wp-att-355">

          <p>
           For those to young to remember Mad Max here is a little back story Play as Mad Max, a lone warrior who must embark on a dangerous journey after his Interceptor is stolen by a deadly gang of marauders. A reluctant hero with an instinct for survival, Max wants nothing more than to leave the madness behind and find solace in the storied Plains of Silence.
