             <a href="http://www.visualnews.com/2014/09/19/japanese-spa-proves-coffee-wine-green-tea-sake-arent-just-drinking/" rel="bookmark">
              This Japanese Spa Proves Coffee, Wine, Green Tea, &amp; Sake Arent Just For Drinking
             </a>
            </h1>
            <div class="entry-meta">
             on September 19, 2014 by Paul Caridad
            </div>
            <!-- .entry-meta -->
           </header>
           <!-- .entry-header -->
           <div class="entry-content">
            <p>
             <strong>
              While weve heard about the antioxidant benefits of drinking a glass of red wine per day, there are other ways of using the reservatol to reduce environmental damage to your skin.
             </strong>
             In fact, Cleopatra, Queen of Egypt is rumored to have bathed in wine, and at a resort in Hakone, Japan, you can also receive the royal treatment. Yunessun Spa Resort is the largest wine spa in the world and offers a variety of liquid soaks for various skin benefits.
             <span id="more-113385">
             </span>
            </p>
            <div class="see-also">
             <a href="http://www.visualnews.com/2012/05/04/interactive-learn-good-french-wines-like-a-pro/" title="Interactive: Learn Good French Wines Like a Pro">

               Interactive: Learn Good French Wines Like a Pro
              </a>
             </p>
             <div class="clearfix">
             </div>
            </div>
            <p>
             They boast a hot green tea pool with a 2 meter tall teapot that is said to reverse aging. They brew coffee straight from the natural hot springs to reduce cellulite and puffiness in another pool. There is another pool filled with sake to minimize the appearance of age and sun spots. All of these beverage pools also invite you to work on the benefits from the inside out as they keep your glasses full of the drink of your choice. So on your next vacation to Japan, be sure to pamper yourself at Yunessun Spa Resort for a very unique experience!
            </p>
