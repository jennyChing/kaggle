              Last April 11, I received a message from Jojo A., the host of "The Goodnight Show"--a local late night talk show ala Conan / Letterman.  He asked me if he could use one of my YouTube music videos for his show.  I was so surprised to get such a message from a cult classic!  So, I gave him the green light to use my video, because I felt that this could be instrumental in my quest for rockstardomand dear dad.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              It was the 30th of April when I finally saw myself on TV.  I was so excited, I texted all my friends.  (I just hope they got my message early enough for them to get a glimpse of me on the telly!)
              <br/>
              <br/>
              I wished that night would never end.
