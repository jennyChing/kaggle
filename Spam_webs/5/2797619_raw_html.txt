     <p>
      Choosing the right font for a marketing piece is about more than finding an attractive typeface. Just the act of looking at a certain font can stir up powerful emotions and make people feel everything from anxious to nostalgic. This is why a font like Comic Sans can be so hated by designers, yet so widely usedits a font that was designed to be fun and playful, which is appealing to many audiences.
     </p>
     <p>
      If you want to choose a font that creates an emotional connection between you and your audience, you first have to understand the psychology of fonts. Read more about how to use the power of fonts to create personality and stir emotions.
