        <p>
         <b>
          Delivery Time Frame
         </b>
         <br/>
         First deliveries of production PlexiDrones are set to begin
         <b>
          <u>
           September 2015
          </u>
         </b>
         . This will coincide with delivery to major retailers as well as to our IndieGoGo backers. After first delivery, PlexiDrone will no longer be offered through the IndieGoGo InDemand platform.
        </p>
        <p>
         Depending on which perk package you are considering, retail pricing will be between 40%-60% more than the current campaign prices.

        <p>
         <img src="https://images.indiegogo.com/file_attachments/1056940/files/20141129234600-FAQ-Header.jpg?1417333560" style="max-width:620px;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          Can I use a standard RC transmitter?
         </span>
         <br/>
         Yes! But you can also use an iOS, Android, or windows device to control and co-ordinate one or MANY PlexiDrones!
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          GPS Follow Me sounds AWESOME! How does it work?
         </span>
         <br/>
         GPS Follow Me works using a smartphone or tablet running iOS, Android, or Windows.  Simply press the "Follow Me" button, and our systems does the rest.  You can also specify, follow behind, in-front, above, left, and right.
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          What About GPS Position Hold?
         </span>
         <br/>
         To use GPS Position Hold, just flick a switch on your standard RC transmitter, or even better, use PlexiGCS to simply press "GPS POS HOLD".
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          How does PlexiGCS let me plan my flight?
         </span>
         <br/>
         Just click on a point on the map to set way-points, altitude, distance from pilot (or target), speed between way-points, time spent at way-point, and much more!
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          Does PlexiDrone have GPS home features?
         </span>
         <br/>
         This can be done with either an RC transmitter or PlexiGCS.  Just flick a switch on your RC transmitter or press "Go Home" on the PlexiGCS interface.
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          What kind of Gimbal and/or camera can I use with Plexi?
         </span>
         <br/>
         PlexiDrone's quick release payload mechanism allows you to use almost any gimbal + camera combination that weighs less than 2.2 lbs (1000 grams). For optimal performance we recommend under 1.1 lbs (500 grams)
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          Is the PlexiDrone Water Resistant?
         </span>
         <br/>
         Plexi is water resistant with fully enclosed electronics.  It is not waterproof, but we are working to increase water resistance.
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          What are the maximum wind conditions that the PlexiDrone can fly in?
         </span>
         <br/>
         The PlexiDrone has been flown in winds up to 12.5 MPH (~20 KPH) with easy control.
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          I'm buying accessories for the PlexiDrone, do I have to pay shipping twice?
         </span>
         <br/>
         PlexiDrone accessories cannot be shipped in the same package as a PlexiDrone- there just isn't enough room! It is less expensive for you to ship them as two separate packages. However, if you order more than one accessory package, those can be bundled together!
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          Is there a display? What is shown on the display?
         </span>
         <br/>
         The PlexiGCS (Ground Control Station) display shows all standard  data, including:
         <br/>
         - Live telemetry
         <br/>
         - Battery status
         <br/>
         - GPS coordinates
         <br/>
         - 3D Map Display
         <br/>
         - Sensor Data
         <br/>
         - Payload Data
         <br/>
         - Camera View (GoPro, Sony ActionCam, BublCam)
         <br/>
         - Flight Planner
         <br/>
         - And more!
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          What flight controller does the PlexiDrone use?
         </span>
         <br/>
         The PlexiDrone uses DreamQii's custom electronics and software.  We do not use any off-the-shelf options.
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          Can you control the camera position via the PlexiGCS App?
         </span>
         <br/>
         Yes, you can control the gimbal position with some pretty awesome and simple controls on the PlexiGCS or using your standard RC transmitter.
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          What do you mean by 360 degree unobstructed field of view?
         </span>
         <br/>
         On other lesser drones, you will notice that propellers and landing gear constantly get in the camera frame and ruin your beautiful aerial shot.  With the PlexiDrone this problem is solved with retractable landing gear, careful camera positioning, and pitch and roll control of the camera itself via the gimbal.
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          Will the charger work for 110V/220V?
         </span>
         <br/>
         Yes, the charger is universal and will work globally.
        </p>
        <p style="">
         <span style="font-weight: bold; color: #006699; font-size: 16px;">
          When do I pay the rest for Save Now perks?
         </span>
         <br/>
         The second half of your payment will be due before we ship. Likely sometime in June/July. We will keep you informed via e-mail as the date approaches.
        </p>
