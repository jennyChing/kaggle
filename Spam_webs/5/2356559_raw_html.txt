                     Sounds from Down Under Blog Series: Olivia Newton-John
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4184396216804961594" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     Sounds from Down Under Series: Day 1
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-OnUYahcFVRk/VLbVqJemDyI/AAAAAAAACMI/OKFWxkyIXKg/s1600/olivia-newton-john.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Olivia Newton-John is a British-born Australian singer, songwriter, and actress.http://www.jinglejanglejungle.net/2015/01/onj.html #OliviaNewtonJohn" border="0" height="200" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-OnUYahcFVRk/VLbVqJemDyI/AAAAAAAACMI/OKFWxkyIXKg/s1600/olivia-newton-john.jpg" title="Olivia Newton-John is a British-born Australian singer, songwriter, and actress.http://www.jinglejanglejungle.net/2015/01/onj.html #OliviaNewtonJohn" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <b>
                      Olivia Newton-John
                     </b>
                     is a British-born Australian singer, songwriter, and actress. She is a four-time Grammy award winner who has amassed five No. 1 and ten other Top Ten Billboard Hot 100 singles and two No. 1 Billboard 200 solo albums. Eleven of her singles (including two platinum) and 14 of her albums (including two platinum and four double platinum) have been certified gold by the RIAA. Her music has been successful in multiple genres including pop, country, and adult contemporary, and has sold an estimated 100 million records, making her one of the world's best-selling artists of all time. She co-starred with John Travolta in the film adaptation of the Broadway musical Grease, which featured one of the most successful soundtracks in Hollywood history.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-5Oz0esYAgAk/VLbVqMYlKpI/AAAAAAAACMM/4V_EJW7OYKc/s1600/Olivia-Newton-John1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Olivia Newton-John is a British-born Australian singer, songwriter, and actress.http://www.jinglejanglejungle.net/2015/01/onj.html #OliviaNewtonJohn" border="0" height="320" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-5Oz0esYAgAk/VLbVqMYlKpI/AAAAAAAACMM/4V_EJW7OYKc/s1600/Olivia-Newton-John1.jpg" title="Olivia Newton-John is a British-born Australian singer, songwriter, and actress.http://www.jinglejanglejungle.net/2015/01/onj.html #OliviaNewtonJohn" width="215"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     Newton-John has been a long-time activist for environmental and animal rights issues. Since surviving breast cancer in 1992, she has been an advocate for health awareness becoming involved with various charities, health products and fundraising efforts. Her business interests have included launching several product lines for Koala Blue and co-owning the Gaia Retreat &amp; Spa in Australia.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Newton-John has been married twice. She is the mother of one daughter, Chloe Rose Lattanzi, with her first husband, actor Matt Lattanzi. Her second husband is John Easterling.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://youtu.be/DJpyG3PXoKw" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                      "Hopelessly Devoted to You"
                     </a>
                     , is a song written by John Farrar. It was originally performed by Olivia Newton-John in the film version of Grease. The song received an Oscar nomination as Best Original Song, losing to "Last Dance" from Thank God It's Friday at the 51st Academy Awards.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Halfway through shooting the movie, Newton-John's contractually-entitled vocal solo had still not been written. Farrar, Newton-John's personal producer, wrote the song and submitted it to the film's production team. Though reluctant at first, they eventually approved it; shooting and recording took place after the rest of the film had been completed.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://youtu.be/DJpyG3PXoKw" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                      http://youtu.be/DJpyG3PXoKw
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" rel="nofollow" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/DJpyG3PXoKw" width="420">
                     </iframe>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://youtu.be/vWz9VN40nCA" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                      "Physical"
                     </a>
                     is a song for Olivia Newton-John's twelfth studio album Physical. It was released in September 1981, by MCA Records as the lead single from the project. The song was written by Steve Kipner and Terry Shaddick, who originally intended to offer it to British singer-songwriter Rod Stewart, while production was handled by John Farrar.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     The song was an immediate success, shipping 2 million copies in the United States, being certified Platinum, and spending 10 weeks at number one on the Billboard Hot 100, ultimately becoming Newton-John's biggest American hit. The song reached number 7 on the UK chart in November. The song was nominated for the Grammy Award for Best Female Pop Vocal Performance and won the Billboard Award for Top Pop Single.
