     <span>
      We
     </span>
     are all on a spiritual journey whether we are aware of it or not. Our lives seem to be beautifully organized to teach us the life lessons we need to individually learn. Graciously there is space for us to make mistakes only to find they point to something else. There is an incredible intelligence to everything that lovingly prepares us to see trough the falsities of our identification with thought.
    </p>
    <p>
     <span>
      Conscious-Yes
     </span>
     points to stepping out of judgment of every single moment and in to love by giving an inner yes to whatever arises in your conscious field. This inner subtle yes understands that we do not have to judge and measure each moment to the world of ideas that the ego has created for itself.
    </p>

     <span>
      Can you
     </span>
     be content with whatever each moment brings? And give yourself a break from the world of the mind made self and rest in that which is true and eternal?
    </p>
    <p>
     <span>
      Are you
     </span>
     ready to tell the truth? A ruthless honesty and sincerity about the minds motives and agendas will enrich your live with an incredible beauty and depth of intelligence. Have you exhausted yourself yet by all the demands that the mind has laid upon you. Perhaps they are spiritual ideas or ideas about monetary success that can keep you captive day in day out.
    </p>
    <p>
     <span>
      The Conscious-Yes teachings
     </span>
     offered by Gerben are here to assist you on your spiritual journey by helping you with an intuitive understanding of the minds mechanisms to open yourself up, find the answers that the mind needs to satisfy its need to know and to finally go beyond all this and deepen the spiritual experience of your life.
    </p>
