            Rubro Cafe  Centro, Rio de Janeiro
           </h1>
           <p class="post-byline">

            <div class="entry-inner">
             <p>
              <img alt="Rubro Caf Centro - Matriz Rua da Quitanda, 191  Centro - Rio de Janeiro" class="lazy aligncenter wp-image-349 size-full" data-original="http://coffeebean.guru/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/RubroWall.jpg" height="240" src="http://coffeebean.guru/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="720"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Rubro Caf Centro - Matriz Rua da Quitanda, 191  Centro - Rio de Janeiro" class="aligncenter wp-image-349 size-full" height="240" src="http://coffeebean.guru/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/RubroWall.jpg" width="720"/>

             <blockquote>
              <p>
               Its where we stop, whenever we have a chance!
              </p>
             </blockquote>
             <p>
              In an alley, in downtown Rio de Janeiro, there is a small cafe we always stop in when we have an opportunity. Not a place you would seek as a tourist, but if you are seeking the genuine Brazilian coffee flavor, maybe you should do just that. Find a Rubro Caf.
             </p>
             <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_350" style="width: 310px">
              <img alt="Rubro Caf Centro - Matriz Rua da Quitanda, 191  Centro - Rio de Janeiro" class="lazy wp-image-350 size-medium" data-original="http://coffeebean.guru/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/RubroCafe-300x246.jpg" height="246" src="http://coffeebean.guru/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="300"/>

              </noscript>
              Rubro Caf Centro  Matriz
              <br/>
              Rua da Quitanda, 191
              <br/>
              Centro  Rio de Janeiro
             </div>
             <p>
              There are more than one Rubro cafe in Rio, but we have only visited this one, which is among our favorites.
             </p>
             <p>
              They have
              <a href="http://www.rubrocafe.com.br/" target="_blank" title="Rubro Caf - Rio de Janeiro">
               a website
              </a>
              with a list of cafes that are scattered in Rio de Janeiro.
             </p>
             <p>
              You can be sure to get a premium coffee or something so rare, in Brazil, as a
              <a href="http://coffeebean.guru/rubro-cafe-rio-de-janeiro-ice-coffee/" title="Rubro Caf (Rio de Janeiro)  Ice Coffee">
               perfect iced coffee
              </a>
              .
             </p>
