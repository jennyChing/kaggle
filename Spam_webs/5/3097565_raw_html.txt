         <h4>
          Enjoy the bold graphic colors and expressive lines and patterns of Edyta Salaks acrylic paintings. To see more of her work, please visit her
          <a href="http://www.edytaart.com/" target="_blank" title="Edyta Salak">
           website
          </a>
          .
         </h4>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_31771" style="width: 510px">
          <img alt="BELLA" class="size-full wp-image-31771" height="312" src="http://www.artsyshark.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/BELLA-by-Edyta-Salak-30x40-acrylic-on-canvas-2.jpg" width="500"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           BELLA Acrylic on Canvas, 30 x 40 x 1.5
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          I come from a long line of talented artistic women. My mother was a painter and my grandmother a dancer. Even my father played piano and sang opera.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_31772" style="width: 510px">
          <img alt="Gemini Girl with Moon" class="size-full wp-image-31772" height="374" src="http://www.artsyshark.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Gemini_Girl_with_Moon-_by_Edyta_Salak-_40x30x1.5_acrylic_on_canvas__-2.jpg" width="500"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Gemini Girl with Moon Acrylic on Canvas, 40 x 30 x 1.5
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          My professional art career started in my first year of college when a major clothing manufacture in Los Angeles hired me as a junior textile designer and soon had me heading the art department. Four years later I launched out on a freelance career and never looked back.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_31779" style="width: 510px">
          <img alt="Green Roses Girl" class="size-full wp-image-31779" height="499" src="http://www.artsyshark.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Green_Roses_Girl_by-_edyta_salak_48x48x1.5_acrylic-on-canvas-2.jpg" width="500"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Green Roses Girl Acrylic on Canvas, 48 x 48 x 1.5
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          In my current series of paintings I call MODERN MOODS; I deconstruct and then reconstruct my subject matter. I pull out the essence of shape and form and rid the subject of inexpressive line and shadow.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_31780" style="width: 510px">
          <img alt="Turban Girl" class="size-full wp-image-31780" height="499" src="http://www.artsyshark.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Turban_Girl_by-_edyta_salak_36x36x1.5_acrylic_canvas_-2.jpg" width="500"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Turban Girl Acrylic on Canvas, 36 x 36 x 1.5
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          I use color that fits the feeling and personality of what Im painting.I strip away the non-essential and am left with the simple mood of each subject.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_31869" style="width: 510px">
          <img alt="Moon Feathers" class="size-full wp-image-31869" height="450" src="http://www.artsyshark.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Moon.jpg" width="500"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           left: Moon Feathers Girl Acrylic on Canvas, 40 x 30 x 1.5; right: Moon Feathers Acrylic on Canvas, 40 x 12 x 1.5
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Many of my pieces have an accompanying piece that brings in a pattern that compliments its partner. Each may remain separate and appealing so they can stand on their own or they may hang well together. This is my surface design experience coming into play.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_31865" style="width: 510px">
          <img alt="Orchid Girl" class="size-full wp-image-31865" height="394" src="http://www.artsyshark.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Orchid-Collage.jpg" width="500"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           left: Orchid Girl Acrylic on Canvas, 24 x 24 x 1.5; right: Orchid Stripe Acrylic on Canvas, 40 x 12 x 1.5
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          I have been in the professional business of art for over 30 years but I have always known art was my calling since I was knee-high to Toulouse Lautrec; however, over the years, Ive always worked for others, painting and creating according to their agenda.I have both public and private art installed in several Town centers from Tahiti to Santa Barbara, California, and permanent installations in many hospitals, shopping centers, businesses and private residences across the United States.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_31781" style="width: 510px">
          <img alt="Breezy Dog Day" class="size-full wp-image-31781" height="398" src="http://www.artsyshark.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/rsz_breezy_dog_day-_by_edyta_salak_60x48x15_acrylic_canvas_-2.jpg" width="500"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Breezy Dog Day Acrylic on Canvas, 60 x 48 x 1.5
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          I also have extensive experience as a professional surface and textile designer and taught my craft to many College students and professionals at Otis College of Art and Design.I finally reached a crossroad and decided that was then and this is now.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_31782" style="width: 314px">
          <img alt="Edyta with MOD painting at soho show" class="size-full wp-image-31782" height="500" src="http://www.artsyshark.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Edyta_with_MOD-painting-at-Solo_show-2.jpg" width="304"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Edyta with MOD painting at Solo show
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          I am grateful for the years of learning countless techniques and styles of art by painting for others. Life, however, is too short to not do the thing you love. Now I am only painting what I want to paint. I can only hope to do just THAT for years to come!
         </p>
