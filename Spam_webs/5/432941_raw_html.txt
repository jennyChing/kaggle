           <h2>
            About PokerListings Poker Room Reviews
           </h2>
          </div>
          <div class="bd">
           <div class="fs-m">
            <p>
             At its very core PokerListings is a database of online poker room reviews and poker site rankings. Thats how its been since the very beginning and our editors are all very experienced online poker players with countless hours logged on
             <a href="http://www.pokerlistings.com/online-poker-rooms" target="_blank">
              online poker sites
             </a>
             like PokerStars, PartyPoker, Titan Poker and more.
            </p>
            <p>
             The objective of our top-rated poker room reviews is to give our visitors all the information necessary to make an informed decision about where to find the best online poker site. Below is an explanation of some of the main factors our poker room reviews are based on.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Software
            </h3>
            <p>
             A poker rooms online client obviously has a huge effect on the overall user experience.
            </p>
            <p>
             Many poker rooms share common software platforms and we meticulously detail the pros and cons of playing on certain networks compared to others.
            </p>
            <p>
             Playing on a poker site with buggy and generally unappealing software is something that no poker fan should be subjected to. Some software is simply sluggish and bloated while other software is lightweight and lightning fast.
            </p>
            <p>
             It also depends what kind of player you are because certain software is much better for tournaments while other software excels in running cash games. There are some features that can only be found at certain sites like four-color decks, re-sizable tables or the ability to play more than four tables at once.
            </p>
            <p>
             There are also questions about compatibility because some sites only run on Windows computers and not Apple devices. We take all of these factors into account when we are reviewing the best poker software.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Traffic
            </h3>
            <p>
             It goes without saying that player site traffic is very important. You want someone to play against right? There are sites where there are games going off 24/7 and then there are sites where you may have to wait an hour just to play a specific type of tournament. Player volume is the biggest factor in deciding how fast youll be able to find a game.
            </p>
            <p>
             Keep in mind that some sites offer more tournaments while others are focused on cash games.
            </p>
            <p>
             Its also worth noting that while a lot of poker sites offer less popular games like Seven-Card Stud and Draw Poker that doesnt necessarily mean they have traffic in those games. When we review online rooms we take into account traffic in every game a particular site offers.
            </p>
            <p>
             You should also remember that some sites hit peak traffic during European hours while others are more focused on North American time zones.
            </p>
            <p>
             Also some sites get almost all their traffic in micro to small stakes while others offer legitimate high-stakes volume.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Player Skill
            </h3>
            <p>
             This isnt something that every new player immediately thinks about but when it comes to poker the vast majority of people would rather play against bad players because making money is fun.
            </p>
            <p>
             The average skill level varies considerable from site to site and we try to guide our players to the softer sites or at least let them know where theyll find the tougher competition.
            </p>
            <p>
             It can be tough to quantify which sites have the worst players but we take into account the average amount of cash contributed to a pot pre-flop to help find loose players.
            </p>
            <p>
             Sometimes youll find the softest sites are actually the lesser-known ones. Were always hunting for the next hidden gem. Whatever you do, dont discount player skill when it comes to signing up for a new poker site.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Game Variety
            </h3>
            <p>
             If you only want to play
             <a href="http://www.pokerlistings.com/texas-holdem" target="_blank">
              No-Limit Texas Holdem
             </a>
             , this isnt a huge issue. But even if youre the most steadfast Holdem purist, we recommend trying your hand at games like
             <a href="http://www.pokerlistings.com/pot-limit-omaha" target="_blank">
              Pot-Limit Omaha
             </a>
             and Seven-Card Stud.
            </p>
            <p>
             You might be surprised how much you enjoy a little variety. Some sites are vastly superior when it comes to their selection of games.
            </p>
            <p>
             Dont like any of the basic games? Youll be happy to know there are plenty of sites that offer even more obscure games like 2-7 Lowball, Razz, Badugi and Five-Card Draw.
            </p>
            <p>
             There are even some sites that spread gambling favorites like Open-Face Chinese Poker.
            </p>
            <p>
             Even beyond all the traditional games there are sites that offer poker variants they invented. These can be a welcome change of pace to standard No-Limit Holdem and you simply wont find them anywhere else.
            </p>
            <p>
             You might find an edge in those games, considering everyone is still trying to figure out the best strategy.
            </p>
            <p>
             For a full rundown of the rules for most poker variations, visit our
             <a href="http://www.pokerlistings.com/poker-rules" target="_blank">
              poker rules
             </a>
             page.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Bonuses and Promotions
            </h3>
            <p>
             You may or may not know that the online poker industry is highly competitive. In order to attract new players online poker sites will quite often host free tournaments (called freerolls) or straight up offer bonus cash for a deposit.
            </p>
            <p>
             If you like free money then youll want to play close attention to the various bonuses and promotions on each poker site. At PokerListings.com we pride ourselves on securing the
             <a href="http://www.pokerlistings.com/poker-bonus" target="_blank">
              best poker bonuses
             </a>
             in the industry.
            </p>
            <p>
             One of the most popular online poker site promotions is the deposit match, where the site will match whatever you deposit - provided you play enough hands on the site. Some of the top deposit matches go up to 200% and will match up to a staggering $2,000.
            </p>
            <p>
             You might be surprised to learn that some of the best deals are located on the smaller sites that have to market their sites aggressively.
            </p>
            <p>
             Some players base their entire game on finding the best bonuses and running up a bankroll from there. At PokerListings we save you time by compiling a comprehensive list of the various online poker sites and the sign-up bonuses they offer.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Support and Security
            </h3>
            <p>
             Never play online poker on a site you dont trust. This is perhaps the most important feature of all.
            </p>
            <p>
             You absolutely have to feel safe on the poker site you chose to play at. PokerListings.com does in-depth security analysis and only lists sites that have achieved online certification.
            </p>
            <p>
             It helps that we communicate regularly with online poker sites to get a good idea of whats going on behind the scenes.
            </p>
            <p>
             Equally important to software security is having solid customer support options in case something should go awry and you need to talk to someone.
            </p>
            <p>
             Most top-tier online poker sites now have instant chat options to speak with a customer support agent live immediately.
            </p>
            <p>
             When reviewing online poker sites we carefully take into account the security of an online poker client as well as the variety of support options and the quality of said options.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Note About Law &amp; Legislation
            </h3>
            <p>
             PokerListings.com is an international brand and were always tracking the legality of online poker in various countries around the globe. Our toplists can be filtered by country to give you an idea of whats legal in the country where you reside. Of course online poker law is always changing and we frequently update our lists to reflect that.
            </p>
