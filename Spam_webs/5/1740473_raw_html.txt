         Healthy use of fume cupboards
        </h1>

        <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;
tab-stops:315.0pt">
         <span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:
"Arial","sans-serif"'>
          We are living in such a world where perhaps each day we
have to face various chemical products or we need to visit some area where
chemicals are used in large proportions. It may be a chemical lab, or
factories. In various factories, different types of chemical are used to
produce medicinal and other chemical products. As different chemicals are used
in these factories and labs, naturally there exist inhalable substances, which
are harmful for the human being. In such situation, there we need to take some
precautionary measures so that persons working in such factories and labs
cannot inhale that poisonous air. Hence, fume cupboard is necessary to get rid
of this situation.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph">
         <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">
          <span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:
115%;font-family:"Arial","sans-serif"'>
           Types and Purposes of using these
cupboards
          </span>
         </b>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph">
         <span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Arial","sans-serif"'>
          In
the recent world, there are many countries having a large number of chemical
labs and chemical factories. The responsible authority of these factories
should take appropriate measure keep the atmosphere healthy. Hence, they must
use specially made cupboards to extract the fume inside the lab or factory.
          <span style="color:black;background:white">
           The purpose of a fume extraction cupboard
is to pull vapors up. We can avail generally two types of cupboards to extract
the fume. One is, fixed cupboard to extract the fume and another is a semi
mobile cupboards. By fixed cupboard, we mean the cabinet, which is fixed on a
particular place. Seethe are extracted with the help of PVC ductwork and a unit
of remote fan. This is a safe technique to take out all the harmful elements
within the air in the laboratory. Another type of cupboard is a semi movable
cupboard. These are designed quite same as fixed cupboards, but these are
movable up to 2 meters with the help of castors. The primary advantages that we
can get from these fume cupboards can be stated below:
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="margin-left:.75in;mso-add-space:auto;
text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;text-indent:-.25in;mso-list:

         <span lang="EN-IN" style="font-size:10.0pt;
line-height:115%;font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol">
          <span style="mso-list:Ignore">
           
           <span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span lang="EN-IN" style='font-size:10.0pt;
line-height:115%;font-family:"Arial","sans-serif"'>
          Using of these cupboards are
not complicated at all and installation process is very much easy
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle" style="margin-left:.75in;mso-add-space:
auto;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;text-indent:-.25in;
mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1">
         <span lang="EN-IN" style="font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:
Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:Symbol">
          <span style="mso-list:Ignore">
           
           <span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span lang="EN-IN" style='font-size:10.0pt;
line-height:115%;font-family:"Arial","sans-serif"'>
          These cupboards save cost
and energy as air conditioning system is not removed from ere.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle" style="margin-left:.75in;mso-add-space:
auto;text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;text-indent:-.25in;
mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1">
         <span lang="EN-IN" style="font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:
Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:Symbol">
          <span style="mso-list:Ignore">
           
           <span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span lang="EN-IN" style='font-size:10.0pt;
line-height:115%;font-family:"Arial","sans-serif"'>
          The exhaust air filtration
work satisfactorily and pure air is circulating back into the laboratory.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpLast" style="margin-left:.75in;mso-add-space:auto;
text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph;text-indent:-.25in;mso-list:
l0 level1 lfo1">
         <span lang="EN-IN" style="font-size:10.0pt;
line-height:115%;font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol">
          <span style="mso-list:Ignore">
           
           <span style='font:7.0pt "Times New Roman"'>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span lang="EN-IN" style='font-size:10.0pt;
line-height:115%;font-family:"Arial","sans-serif"'>
          There is no chance that any
contaminated air can get back into the laboratory again.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:.25in;text-align:justify;text-justify:
inter-ideograph">
         <span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:
"Arial","sans-serif"'>
          In spite of having these advantages, we sometimes face
some disadvantages of these cupboards. These disadvantages can be like need to
regular replacement of filters, the efficiency of carbon filter must be checked
using gas tubes. There is also another disadvantage of chemical exposure.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph">
         <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">
          <span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:
115%;font-family:"Arial","sans-serif"'>
           Services in extraction of fume in
Ireland
          </span>
         </b>
        </p>
        <p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify;text-justify:inter-ideograph">
         <span style='font-size:10.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Arial","sans-serif"'>
          In
Ireland, this
          <a href="http://www.haroldeng.ie/products/air-compressor/air-compressors.htm" target="" title="Fume Extraction">
           <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">
            fume extraction
           </b>
          </a>
          service has
become a popular one. Citizens of Ireland want to keep their atmosphere healthy
and that is the reason that many companies here are emerging as popular in
extraction of fume. The companies provide services in fume and dust extraction
from any lab or complex or factory. They are involved in producing customer
service 24x7 and provide services from installation to the extraction. These
companies also offer maintenance service of these cupboards. Again, in Ireland
you will also find some companies, which are providing services only related to
designing and consultation. Dust extraction machines are manufactured by these
companies, and different cupboards like dust extraction cupboards, bag filters,
scrubbing extraction etc.
          <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">
          </span>
          Apart from all
these services, some famous companies are there to provide timely maintenance
of these extraction machines and testing process be taken before installing.
         </span>
        </p>
