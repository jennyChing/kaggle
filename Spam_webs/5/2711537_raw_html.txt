            <p>
             Nothing can surpass a beauty of a merry room than a room donned with a supple and dazzling hand knotted Oriental rug. In fact, authentic Oriental Rugs not only bring sound proofing, warmth and color to a room, but they are
             <a href="http://blog.rugknots.com/oriental-rug-tips/10-reasons-you-should-consider-buying-an-oriental-rug/">
              also a durable and a prudent investment for your home
             </a>
             . Persian rugs are considered superior in quality compared to the choices that are marketed as
             <em>
              hand loomed or hand tufted
             </em>
             . These terms normally refer to a rug that was machine made. The hand-knotted character of a rugwhether specifically Persian or more generally Orientalis
             <a href="http://blog.rugknots.com/oriental-rug-tips/why-quality-matters-when-it-comes-to-oriental-rugs/">
              what matters the most when it comes to quality
             </a>
             , intricacy, and overall durability.
            </p>
            <p>
             There are so many Oriental Rugstyles, designs, motifs and colors that finding the perfect match for your space can bereally quite delightful! In this post, we offer 24 of our merriest rooms and the rug styles that make them irresistible.
