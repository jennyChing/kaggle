         <a href="http://www.coastalaccents.org/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.coastalaccents.org/', 'Coastal Accents']);" target="_blank">

          the Cotton Gin

         <a href="http://www.ambrosefurniture.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.ambrosefurniture.com/', 'Ambrose Furniture']);" target="_blank">
          Ambrose Furniture

         </a>

        </p>
        <p>
          Feeling a little non-traditional this season? Try take out! Bring home some fresh seafood from
         <a href="http://www.frogislandseafood.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.frogislandseafood.com/', 'Frog Island']);" target="_blank">

          El Potrillo
         </a>
         or even Chinese from Mr. Lu! Make this a memorable non-traditional holiday altogether, or just add some whacky additions to the traditional meal.
        </p>
        <p>
          Dine in local restaurants. Just relax and let someone else do the cooking for a change this year. There are so many choices! Have a relaxing dinner at
         <a href="http://www.coinjockmarina.com/restaurant/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.coinjockmarina.com/restaurant/', 'Coinjock Marina']);" target="_blank">
          Coinjock Marina
         </a>
         or
         <a href="http://www.crabbiesrestaurant.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.crabbiesrestaurant.com/', 'Crabbies']);" target="_blank">
          Crabbies
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.southlandtrade.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.southlandtrade.com/', 'Southland']);" target="_blank">

         </a>
         or the
         <a href="http://www.weepingradish.com/index.php?option=com_content&amp;view=article&amp;id=51" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.weepingradish.com/index.php?option=com_content&amp;view=article&amp;id=51', 'Weeping Radish']);" target="_blank">
          Weeping Radish
         </a>
          Go someplace that youve never tried. You may find a new favorite!
        </p>
        <p>
          Give a gift certificate for your favorite local restaurant or shop. Your neighbors and friends will love your thoughtfulness!
        </p>
        <p>
          Take a mini vacation and really enjoy your family! Stay in a vacation rental home or hotel in Corolla the rates are great and the weather is still nice. Enjoy the beach! Relax!
        </p>
        <p>
          Attend local events! Have some fun with the family! There are many local events going on every weekend. Go to a festival like Silver Bells, a parade or the Currituck tree lighting ceremony. Be sure to check the events calendar at
         <a href="http://www.buylocalcurrituck.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://www.buylocalcurrituck.com', 'www.buylocalcurrituck.com']);" target="_blank">

         . Catch up with your neighbors and friends!
        </p>

          Save your Buy Local Currituck receipts and enter the contest to win prizes. This quarter promises to be one of the best ones yet! Stay tuned for the prize list. Spoiler alert, prize list includes a vacation rental package and over $2,000 in total prizes.
