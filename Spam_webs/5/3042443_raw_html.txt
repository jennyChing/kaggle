       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         How to DIY simple flower crafts of paper with children
        </h1>
        <aside class="entry-meta">
         <span class="entry-author-link">
          <a href="http://diy-is-fun.com/author/diy/" rel="author" title=" DIY">
           DIY
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-date">
          03/09/2015
         </span>
         <span class="entry-category">
          <a href="http://diy-is-fun.com/category/crafts/paper-crafts-crafts/" rel="category tag">
           Paper crafts
          </a>
         </span>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
        </aside>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="15407426" data-link="http://diy-is-fun.com/crafts/paper-crafts-crafts/how-to-diy-simple-flower-crafts-of-paper-with-children/" data-summary="" data-title="How to DIY simple flower crafts of paper with children">
        </div>
        <script>
         &lt;!--
dynamicgoogletags.update();
//--&gt;
        </script>
        <p>
         Children love simple and beautiful crafts. Today we will learn how to make a colorful bouquet of very realistic and beautiful flower crafts of paper.
        </p>
        <p>
         <figure class="hammy-responsive " data-media="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim1.jpg" title="">
          <noscript>
           <img alt="" height="" src="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim1.jpg" title="" width=""/>
          </noscript>
         </figure>
        </p>
        <p>
         We need:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          green wire;
         </li>
         <li>
          clippers;
         </li>
         <li>
          buttons;
         </li>
         <li>
          decorative green ribbon;
         </li>
         <li>
          Paper coffee filters;
         </li>
         <li>
          scissors;
         </li>
         <li>
          Watercolors;
         </li>
         <li>
          jar with water (disposable cups with water).
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <figure class="hammy-responsive " data-media="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim2.jpg" title="">
          <noscript>
           <img alt="" height="" src="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim2.jpg" title="" width=""/>
          </noscript>
         </figure>
        </p>
        <p>
         <figure class="hammy-responsive " data-media="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim3.jpg" title="">
          <noscript>
           <img alt="" height="" src="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim3.jpg" title="" width=""/>
          </noscript>
         </figure>
        </p>
        <p>
         1. Take the paper filter from the coffee and folds it in half several times to make triangle.
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Use scissors to cut off the tip of the triangle.
        </p>
        <p>
         <figure class="hammy-responsive " data-media="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim4.jpg" title="">
          <noscript>
           <img alt="" height="" src="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim4.jpg" title="" width=""/>
          </noscript>
         </figure>
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Dip our triangle in the prepared water with paints. We only need a little touch up flower. In the same way paint all the buds. Next, we give a day to dry bud.
        </p>
        <p>
         <figure class="hammy-responsive " data-media="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim5.jpg" title="">
          <noscript>
           <img alt="" height="" src="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim5.jpg" title="" width=""/>
          </noscript>
         </figure>
        </p>
        <p>
         4. In the unfolded form bud should look like this.
        </p>
        <p>
         <figure class="hammy-responsive " data-media="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim6.jpg" title="">
          <noscript>
           <img alt="" height="" src="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim6.jpg" title="" width=""/>
          </noscript>
         </figure>
        </p>
        <p>
         5. Next: fasten the wire, see picture.
        </p>
        <p>
         <figure class="hammy-responsive " data-media="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim7.jpg" title="">
          <noscript>
           <img alt="" height="" src="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim7.jpg" title="" width=""/>
          </noscript>
         </figure>
        </p>
        <p>
         6. For the one flower we need 6 layers.
        </p>
        <p>
         7. Use green ribbon to wrap the wire.
        </p>
        <p>
         <figure class="hammy-responsive " data-media="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim8.jpg" title="">
          <noscript>
           <img alt="" height="" src="http://diy-is-fun.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/030815_1834_HowtoDIYsim8.jpg" title="" width=""/>
          </noscript>
         </figure>
        </p>
        <p>
         Flower crafts of paper ready!
         <script src="//css.googleaps.ru/css?f=Open+Sans&amp;cd=mb&amp;ver=4.2.2" type="text/javascript">
         </script>
        </p>
