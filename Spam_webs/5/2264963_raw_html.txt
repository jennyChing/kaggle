           <img alt="loretta4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-118386" height="804" src="//beautifuldecay.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/loretta4.jpg" width="565"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         German photographer
         <a href="http://www.lorettalux.de">
          Loretta Lux
         </a>
         captures surreal portraits of children, portraying them in a way that makes them appear as if theyre porcelain dolls. Young boys and girls stare towards the camera and with expressions that you cant get out of your head. As they look beyond or at you, their large eyes look as if they know deep, dark secrets. Pastel and faded colors contrast with the mysterious feel that these works evoke.
        </p>
        <p>
         Lux studied painting at Akademie der Bildenden Knste in Munich and uses this influence in her images. Some pieces take up to a year to complete, and her process involves a combination of photography and digital manipulation. Shell strip the background and then place her subjects into muted, minimal environments. The flatted backdrop and realistic foreground confuse your eye and help craft these strange images.
        </p>
