          <p>
           It is safe to say that food and drink festivals have come a long way. Gone are the days of table filled hotel ballrooms. Todays events have become one-in-a-lifetime events from from the slowfood movement to street food and wine. Lobster love-in, garlic galore or a good cocktail? You decide. Now, in writing this post, it was extremely hard to narrow it down. So, I narrowed it down by the not so popular to the big names.
          </p>
          <p>
           Mark your calendars and prepare your stomach  here are some of the 12best food and drink festivals waiting for you (and your stomach.) You might want to bring elastic waist pants!
          </p>
          <h3>
           1. Msamerica Festival
