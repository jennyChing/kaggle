         <b>
          MEAT isn't Murder - Murder is Murder
          <br/>
         </b>
        </font>
        <font face="Arial,Helvetica,Univers,Zurich BT" size="-1">
         How many times have we heard a man referred to as "beefy" or a woman as a "hot bucket of chicken"? Or disgusting euphemisms for the sexual act like, "I'd love to pork her", "I am bopping the baloney" or "Who's grilling the liverworst?" This is blatant propaganda by Vegetarians to discredit the joy of
         <b>
          Flesh!
          <br/>

         </b>
        </font>
        <font face="Arial,Helvetica,Univers,Zurich BT">

          <br/>
         </b>
        </font>

         <br/>
         <br/>
        </font>
        <font face="Arial,Helvetica,Univers,Zurich BT">
         <b>
          You are not alone.
          <br/>
         </b>
        </font>
        <font face="Arial,Helvetica,Univers,Zurich BT" size="-1">

         <b>
          eat a giraffe
         </b>
         .

         <br/>
        </font>

         <b>
          So who is really the Killer?
