        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Low Calorie Summer Cocktails
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-07-14T09:18:16+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          July 14, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/author/healthperch/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Health Perch
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>

        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-header.jpg">
          <img alt="Low Calorie Summer Cocktails" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1025" height="319" src="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-header.jpg" width="1068"/>
         </a>
        </h2>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         Tips On How To Make Your Favorite Cocktails Lighter
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         For many of us, a picture perfect summer day equals sunshine, good friends, a barbecue, and, of course, a cool drink. The calories in that thirst quencher are often the last thought on our mindsbut liquid calories can add up fast and may be a disaster for a healthy diet.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Consider this: A single chocolate martini has
         <a href="http://www.webmd.com/diet/features/low-calorie-cocktails" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.webmd.com/diet/features/low-calorie-cocktails', 'more calories']);">
          more calories
         </a>
         than a fast food grilled chicken sandwich. Alcohol has seven calories per gram; one gram of either a protein or a carbohydrate has only four. And the higher the alcohol content, the higher the calories.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-001.jpg">
          <img alt="An Alcohol-Food Calorie Comparison" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1036" height="409" src="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-001.jpg" width="1068"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         And alcohol is only one ingredient in many summertime beverages. Mix-insfruit juices, soft drinks, sugary syrupscan make a drinks calorie count skyrocket. In fact, these summer drink mix-ins had so many calories and so few nutrients, they were the least recommended in a study
         <a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16522898" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/16522898', 'published']);">
          published
         </a>
         in the
         <em>
          American Journal of Clinical Nutrition
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Try these
         <a href="http://www.webmd.com/diet/features/low-calorie-cocktails" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.webmd.com/diet/features/low-calorie-cocktails', 'tips']);">
          tips
         </a>
         to easily cut down on the calories in your summertime cocktails:
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Use fewer ingredients.
         </li>
         <li>
          Use no- or low-calorie mix-ins such as club soda, or those with better nutritional profiles, such as 100 percent fruit juice.
         </li>
         <li>
          Serve only recommended portion sizes.
         </li>
         <li>
          Drink a glass of water between each alcoholic beverage to stay hydrated.
         </li>
         <li>
          Serve drinks over ice.
         </li>
         <li>
          Flavor drinks with zero or low-calorie garnishes such as mint, basil, or frozen fruit pieces.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         TIP: To track your liquid calories from alcohol, try using this online
         <a href="http://rethinkingdrinking.niaaa.nih.gov/toolsresources/caloriecalculator.asp" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://rethinkingdrinking.niaaa.nih.gov/toolsresources/caloriecalculator.asp', 'alcohol calorie counter']);">
          alcohol calorie counter
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-002.jpg">
          <img alt="Portion Size For Alcoholic Beverages" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1023" height="983" src="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-002.jpg" width="1068"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-mojito.jpg">
          <img alt="Mojito Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1049" height="141" src="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-mojito.jpg" width="1068"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.fitsugar.com/Mojito-Makes-Good-Low-Calorie-Cocktail-10155793" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.fitsugar.com/Mojito-Makes-Good-Low-Calorie-Cocktail-10155793', 'Mojito']);">
           Mojito
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Mojitos are one of the easiest cocktails to lower calories. Try no-calorie soda water, fresh fruit, and herbs to make this a refreshing and healthier treat on a hot day. Original Mojito
         <a href="http://www.fitsugar.com/Mojito-Makes-Good-Low-Calorie-Cocktail-10155793" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.fitsugar.com/Mojito-Makes-Good-Low-Calorie-Cocktail-10155793', 'recipe']);">
          recipe
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-PinaColada-.jpg">
          <img alt="Pina Colada Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1050" height="141" src="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-PinaColada-.jpg" width="1068"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://carrotsncake.com/2011/07/skinny-pia-colada.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://carrotsncake.com/2011/07/skinny-pia-colada.html', 'Pia Colada']);">
           Pia Colada
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Blended cocktails are traditionally high in calories and heavy on sugar. For pia colada fans, try a version that uses coconut water instead of coconut cream to cut calories. Original Pina Colada
         <a href="http://carrotsncake.com/2011/07/skinny-pia-colada.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://carrotsncake.com/2011/07/skinny-pia-colada.html', 'recipe']);">
          recipe
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-margarita.jpg">
          <img alt="Margarita Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1048" height="141" src="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-margarita.jpg" width="1068"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://cookieandkate.com/2012/fresh-margaritas/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://cookieandkate.com/2012/fresh-margaritas/', 'Margarita']);">
           Margarita
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Forget those sugar and calorie-laden margarita mixes; make your own from scratch without added preservatives and hard-to-pronounce ingredients. This six-ingredient recipe has only 184 calories and comes with variations. Original Margarita
         <a href="http://cookieandkate.com/2012/fresh-margaritas/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://cookieandkate.com/2012/fresh-margaritas/', 'recipe']);">
          recipe
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          White Wine and Champagne
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         If youre a wine lover, keep an eye on the alcohol content in your favorite bottle. Wine and champagne with a higher alcohol content will have a higher calorie count. Generally speaking, white wines have slightly fewer calories than red. Shoot for around 140 calories or less for a
         <a href="http://www.shape.com/blogs/fit-foodies/best-wines-your-waistline" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.shape.com/blogs/fit-foodies/best-wines-your-waistline', 'six-ounce pour of white']);">
          six-ounce pour of white
         </a>
         . Light whites with lower alcohol contents, such as Riesling and Pinot Grigio, usually have fewer calories as well. To calculate the calorie count, use this formula:
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-003.jpg">
          <img alt="Calorie Count Formula" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1027" height="409" src="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-003.jpg" width="1068"/>
         </a>
         For a sipping wine on a patio, try
         <a href="http://www.entwine-wines.com/our-wines/pinot-grigio" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.entwine-wines.com/our-wines/pinot-grigio', 'Entwines 2010 Pinot Grigio']);">
          Entwines 2010 Pinot Grigio
         </a>
         . A six-ounce pour of this crisp, citrusy white wine has just 125 calories.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         If youre thinking about celebrating summer with a glass of champagne, WineFolly.com recommends a bottle with no added sugar. Look for Brut Nature or Brut Zero on the label.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-GinTonic.jpg">
          <img alt="Gin Tonic Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1028" height="141" src="http://www.northwestpharmacy.com/healthperch/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/low-calorie-summer-cocktails-GinTonic.jpg" width="1068"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
