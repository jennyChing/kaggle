        <h1>
         <a href="http://gevrilgroup.com/watchbrands/ferragamo-gancino-chic-watch-collection/" title="Ferragamo Gancino Chic Watch Collection">
          Ferragamo Gancino Chic Watch Collection
         </a>
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-date">
         March 21, 2015
        </div>
        <p>
         <!--Skip a Line-->
        </p>
        <div align="center">
         <table cellpadding="0" cellspacing="2">
          <tr>
           <td>
            <img alt="Ferragamo Gancino Chic Watch Collection - FID05 0015" src="http://gevrilgroup.com/images/salvatore-ferragamo-watches/luxury-watches/Ferragamo Gancino Chic Watch Collection - FID05 0015.jpg" style="border: solid 1px #a9a9a9;" title="Ferragamo Gancino Chic Watch Collection - FID05 0015" vspace="4" width="522"/>
           </td>
          </tr>

         <p>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         by
         <a href="http://gevrilgroup.com/john-sealander/" target="_blank">
          John Sealander
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         When you combine Swiss watchmaking expertise with fine Italian leatherwork, you get the Salvatore Ferragamo Gancino Chic.
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gevrilgroup.com/salvatore-ferragamo-timepieces/" target="_blank">
          <img align="left" alt="Ferragamo Watches" height="38" hspace="10" src="http://gevrilgroup.com/images/salvatore-ferragamo-watches/luxury-watches/Salvatore Ferragamo.jpg" title="Ferragamo Watches" vspace="10"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.ferragamo.com/" target="_blank">
          Salvatore Ferragamo
         </a>
         made his reputation as one of the worlds finest shoemakers. His unique artistry with leather has seldom been equaled. When the renowned fashion house decided to add fine timepieces to its repertoire, they turned to the Swiss, who had a similar reputation for exemplary craftsmanship. Together, these artisans have created a masterpiece: the stunning new Ferragamo Gancino Chic.
        </p>
        <p>
         Making its debut at
         <a href="http://gevrilgroup.com/baselworld/tag/baselworld-2015/" target="_blank">
          Baselworld 2015
         </a>
         , this beautifully executed 34 mm ladies timepiece is a work of art. The unique cuff style band is a tribute to the fine leather craftsmanship that Salvatore Ferragamo has become famous for. This supple
         <a href="http://gevrilgroup.com/watchrepair/leather-watch-straps-textile-watch-straps-advantages/" target="_blank">
          topstitched strap
         </a>
