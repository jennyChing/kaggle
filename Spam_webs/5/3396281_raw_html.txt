      <p>
       This Thanksgiving, weve put together a list of 10 of the most awesome characters that we all know and love, who have appeared as larger-than-life-sized balloons.
      </p>
      <h2>
       1. Shrek  Shrek (2001)
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="" class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source/Shrek.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <small class="source">
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/musicwala/5209749894/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Flickr
        </a>
       </small>
      </p>
      <p>
       In 2007, Macys Thanksgiving Day Parade used the most lovable ogre in the world!
      </p>
      <h2>
       2. Bart Simpson  The Simpsons (1989 )
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="bart.jpg" class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source/bart.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <small class="source">
        <a href="http://www.jfnphoto.com/index.php#mi=2&amp;pt=1&amp;pi=10000&amp;s=0&amp;p=14&amp;a=0&amp;at=0" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         jfnphoto
        </a>
       </small>
      </p>
      <p>
       Americas favorite 10 year-old, Bart Simpson featured in the 1990 parade.
      </p>
      <h2>
       3. Horton  Horton Hears a Who! (2008)
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="horton.jpg" class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source/horton.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <small class="source">
        <a href="http://www.upi.com/News_Photos/Entertainment/Macys-82nd-Thanksgiving-Day-Parade/fp/1275/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         UPI
        </a>
       </small>
      </p>
      <p>
       One of the stars of the 2008 parade, Horton the elephant was received with massive applause.
      </p>
      <h2>
       4. Pikachu  Pokmon (1998 )
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="Pikachu.jpg" class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source/Pikachu.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <small class="source">
        <a href="http://www.gameinformer.com/b/news/archive/2010/11/23/pikachu-celebrates-ten-years-in-macy-s-thanksgiving-day-parade-by-inviting-friends.aspx" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         gameinformer
        </a>
       </small>
      </p>
      <p>
       Featured in both 2001 and 2006, Pikachu is one of the most endearing and popular characters on TV.
      </p>
      <h2>
       5. Scooby Doo  Scooby Doo, Where Are You! (1969  1972)
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="Scooby.jpg" class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source/Scooby.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <small class="source">
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/markhurst/68389879/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Flickr
        </a>
       </small>
      </p>
      <p>
       Part of the 2005 edition of the parade, Scooby Doo is easily Americas favorite dog. Crowds rushed to see the greatest Dane in cartoon history.
      </p>
      <h2>
       6. Snoopy  The Charlie Brown and Snoopy Show (1983  1985)
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="macys-parade.jpg" class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source/macys-parade.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <small class="source">
        <a href="http://jobspapa.com/tilted-tens-best-parade-balloons.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         JopsPapa
        </a>
       </small>
      </p>
      <p>
       A recurring character, Snoopy has been featured in the 1968, 1972, 1987, 1988, 1999, 2006, and 2013 Macys parades. As to his popularity, the facts speak for themselves.
      </p>
      <h2>
       7. Po  Kung Fu Panda (2008)
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="Po.jpg" class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source/Po.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <small class="source">
        <a href="http://www.newyorkfamily.com/tips-from-the-experts-the-best-spots-to-stand-at-the-macys-day-parade/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         NewYorkfamily
        </a>
       </small>
      </p>
      <p>
       America has a lot of favorite animals, but this Panda is one of the highest on that list. Po appeared in the Parade in 2010. Skadoosh!
      </p>
      <h2>
       8. Buzz Lightyear  Toy Story (1995)
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="Buzz-lightyear.jpg" class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source/Buzz-lightyear.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <small class="source">
        <a href="http://www.ibtimes.com/macys-thanksgiving-day-parade-2013-route-map-start-time-live-stream-video-best-viewing-spots-1486924" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         ibtimes
        </a>
       </small>
      </p>
      <p>
       Neil Armstrong who? Buzz Lightyear, everyones favorite astronaut, featured in the parade in 2008.
      </p>
      <h2>
       9. Garfield  Garfield (2004)
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="garfield.jpg" class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source/garfield.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <small class="source">
        <a href="http://www.catsparella.com/2011/11/cat-balloons-of-macys-thanksgiving-day.htmlhttp:/www.catsparella.com/2011/11/cat-balloons-of-macys-thanksgiving-day.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         catsparella
        </a>
       </small>
      </p>
      <p>
       The laziest cat in the world flew around the Big Apple twice for Macys Thanksgiving Day Parade, once in 1984 and then in 2003.
      </p>
      <h2>
       10. SpongeBob SquarePants  SpongeBob SquarePants (1999 )
      </h2>
      <p>
       <img alt="Spongebob.jpg" class="img-responsive" src="http://images.bidnessetc.com/content/uploads/images/source/Spongebob.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <small class="source">
        <a href="http://darkroom.baltimoresun.com/2013/11/a-look-back-at-past-macys-thanksgiving-day-parade/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         BaltimoreSun
        </a>
       </small>
      </p>
      <p>
       Who lives in a pineapple under the sea? SpongeBob SquarePants came to shore and took to the skies in 2004 and 2013.
      </p>
