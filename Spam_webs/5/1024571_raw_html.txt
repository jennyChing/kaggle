         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           Islami Bank New Job Circular 20015
          </strong>
          . IBBL is the 1st shariah Bank in Bangladesh. A lot of branches are operated by this shariah bank. This Bankhas recently published a job circular on their official website and in the Daily Star too. The posts are such as-AVP/PO as Information System (IS) Auditor, AVP/SPO for R &amp; DD and IBTRA, PO/SO for R &amp; DD &amp; IBTRA, SO/Officer (Engineer) for any Department, Security Officer (Post/Rank is negotiable), Assistant Security Officer, Fire Safety Officials, Official for Call Center/Khidmah Card Marketing, Messenger Cum Guard and Security Guard. For details go through the below given circular. You have to apply through online.
          <strong>
           Last date
          </strong>
          of application is
          <strong>
           March 31, 2015.
          </strong>
         </p>
