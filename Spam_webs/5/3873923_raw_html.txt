               <p>
                Selling your house is a great way to aquire a lot of digital coins, so why not? If the Canadian owners manage to sell this house, they will be 6500 Bitcoins richer.
               </p>
               <h2>
                4.2011 Cirrus SR22 Turbo (LCE)  The IFR Beast: 8300 BTC
               </h2>
               <p>
                <a href="http://factaholics.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2011-Cirrus-SR22-Turbo-Plane.jpg">
                 <img alt="2011-Cirrus-SR22-Turbo-Plane" class="alignnone wp-image-1713" height="629" src="http://factaholics.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2011-Cirrus-SR22-Turbo-Plane.jpg" width="1013"/>
                </a>
               </p>
               <p>
                Yes, Bitcoins also buys you a plane! This one could be yours for 8300 BTC.
               </p>
               <h2>
                3.Downtown house in Taiwan: 11,000 BTC
               </h2>
               <p>
                <a href="http://factaholics.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Downtown-House-in-Taipei-City-Taiwan.jpg">
                 <img alt="Downtown-House-in-Taipei-City-Taiwan" class="alignnone wp-image-1715" height="512" src="http://factaholics.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Downtown-House-in-Taipei-City-Taiwan.jpg" width="898"/>
                </a>
               </p>
               <p>
                Located in Taipei City, this is the most expensive house on our list that you can buy with Bitcoins. Youre able to set up your own dinner party (actually, buy the whole house) for as much as 11,000 BTC.
               </p>
               <h2>
                2.Peter Beard original photograph: 15,100 BTC
               </h2>
               <p>
                <a href="http://factaholics.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/A-Peter-Beard-Orphaned-Cheetah-Cubs-Original.jpeg">
                 <img alt="A-Peter-Beard-Orphaned-Cheetah-Cubs-Original" class="alignnone wp-image-1716" height="719" src="http://factaholics.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/A-Peter-Beard-Orphaned-Cheetah-Cubs-Original.jpeg" width="1058"/>
                </a>
               </p>
               <p>
                This is a once in a lifetime opportunity, because theres only one piece of this photograph available, created by the famous contemporary photographer Peter Beard. This unique item could be yours for 15,100 BTC.
               </p>
               <h2>
                1. Trump SoHo Hotel Condominium: 25,500 BTC
               </h2>
               <p>
                <a href="http://factaholics.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Trump-SoHo-Hotel-Condominium.jpg">
                 <img alt="Trump-SoHo-Hotel-Condominium" class="alignnone wp-image-1717" height="579" src="http://factaholics.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Trump-SoHo-Hotel-Condominium.jpg" width="932"/>

When the ad for this apartment was published, the owner was asking for $1.9 million. The funny thing here is that he only accepted Bitcoins!
