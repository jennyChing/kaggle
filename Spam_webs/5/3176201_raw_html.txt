       <a href="http://chrmdpoet.tumblr.com/post/98503084454/some-news">
        Some News
       </a>
      </h2>
      <div class="textpostbody">
       <p>
        On that note, I suppose now is as good a time as ever to make a little surprise announcement.
       </p>
       <p>
        I have been offered and have accepted a publishing deal for the original adaptation of
        <em>
         Popcorn Love.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        I have been adapting the story into an original novel (of the same name), which will be published and released in the latter half of 2015.
       </p>
       <p>
        There will be some differences, plot-wise, as in a
        <strong>
         tad
        </strong>
        more angst, and a few moments that were not seen in the fanfiction version. Other than that, it will be the same story that so many of you fell in love with and so kindly supported (but with new, original characters).
       </p>
       <p>
        I hope you all will join me for the original ride when it releases next year. I so appreciate your love and support. It has meant and continues to mean more to me than words can say.
       </p>
       <p>
        Love to you all, XO.
       </p>
