           </h1>
           <div class="h4-clone">

           <section class="content__raw-html">
            <img alt="Max Verstappen" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16902" data-id="16902" height="667" src="http://cdn.wp.sunmotors.co.uk/get/2014/08/verstappen.1000x667.jpg" width="1000"/>
            <p class="image-caption">
             Prodigy  Max Verstappen gets Formula One seat
            </p>
            <p>
             DUTCH teenager Max Verstappen will race in Formula One next season  despite not being old enough to drive on the roads.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              SIXTEEN-YEAR-OLD
             </strong>
             Max will become the youngest driver in the sports history when he lines up for the Toro Rosso team in the 2015 championship.
            </p>
            <p>
             The baby-faced racer, who is the son of former F1 driver Jos, signed for the Red Bull Junior Team earlier this month following a string of wins in the European F3 Championship  and impressed bosses have now decided to promote him further.
            </p>
            <hr/>
            <p style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="/used-cars/" title="Used cars for sale">
              Search for and buy used cars on sunmotors.co.uk
             </a>
            </p>
            <hr/>
            <p>
             The sports youngest driver to date is Spains Jaime Alguersuari, who made his debut with the same Red Bull-owned team aged 19 and 125 days in 2009  but Verstappen will be two years younger when he takes his place on the grid for the opening race in Melbourne.
            </p>
            <img alt="Max and Jos Verstappen" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-16904" data-id="16904" height="667" src="http://cdn.wp.sunmotors.co.uk/get/2014/08/verstappens.1000x667.jpg" width="1000"/>
            <p class="image-caption">
             Like father, like son  Max with racer dad Jos Verstappen
            </p>
            <p>
             Interestingly Verstappen will not be legally be able to drive a car on his own in his native Netherlands until over half-way through the F1 season.
            </p>
            <p>
             A delighted Verstappen said: Ever since I was seven years old Formula One has been my career goal, so this opportunity is truly a dream come true.
            </p>
            <p>
             Toro Rossos team principal, Franz Tost, added: We consider Max to be one of the most skilled young drivers of the new generation and we believe he has the necessary maturity and mental strength to take on this challenge successfully.
            </p>
