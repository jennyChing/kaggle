        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Colorful Bohemian Ranch Wedding: Danielle + Adam
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2014-10-27T06:00:46+00:00">
          Monday, October, 27, 2014
         </span>
         at
         <span class="date published time" title="2014-10-27T06:00:46+00:00">
          6:00 am
         </span>
         //
         <span class="post-comments">
          <a href="http://greenweddingshoes.com/colorful-bohemian-ranch-wedding-danielle-adam/#comments">

         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="Flying Caballos Guest Ranch" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-212118" height="450" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/daniellepoff-wedding-01.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          For those of you who dont yet know her,
          <a href="http://www.daniellepoff.com/" target="_blank">
           Danielle Poff
          </a>
          is one of our fave wedding photogs here at GWS. Weve featured her beautiful work more times than we can count+ her taste is always seriously spot-on. When we heard she was getting hitched herself, naturally we knew her celebrationwould for sure be a must-share!
         </p>
         <p>
          She + her beau, Adam, wanted to plan their day based around their favorite things, and that meant forgoing elements that are sometimessynonymous with weddings. She tells us,
          <em>
           Our inspiration came from the fact that we love antiquing, I love Free People, Adam loves wood working, and we just really like the unique, eclectic look. We have been shopping at antique stores and thrift stores for the last 3 years to collect all of the dcor that you see on our tables. We really wanted our wedding to reflect us and not just the wedding tradition. We felt like things should be done because we wanted to do them, not because it was a wedding.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Big hive five to that! Thanks so much to
          <a href="http://www.jen-rodriguez.com/" target="_blank">
           Jen Rodriguez Photography
          </a>
          , who documented this day just perfectly, and congrats to the newlyweds, Danielle + Adam!
         </p>
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="styled invitation" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-212119" height="531" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/daniellepoff-wedding-02.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Invite love + wedding ring envyOh, and Adam made his own wooden wedding ring. So rad, right?!
         </p>
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="halo engagement ring and wood ring" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-212121" height="429" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/daniellepoff-wedding-03.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="free people bridesmaid dresses" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-212123" height="850" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/daniellepoff-wedding-04.jpg" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Danielle on her dress,
          <em>
           I chose to wear a
           <a href="http://rstyle.me/n/p8uuqyvw" target="_blank">
            Free People
           </a>
           dress instead of a real wedding dress because itwas way more me. It was also cheaper, more unique and way easier to move in which was awesome!! I then let my bridesmaids each choose a Free People dress based on pre-selected ones I chose and it came together so well; I love the mismatched look.
          </em>
         </p>
