       <p>
        There's no word yet on whether the cheerleader herself will be appearing in
        <a href="http://www.today.com/entertainment/nbc-relaunching-heroes-2015-2D12156579" target="_blank">
         "Heroes Reborn,"
        </a>
        a miniseries reboot of the sci-fi drama "Heroes." But the cheerleader's dad? He's a lock, according to a
        <a href="https://twitter.com/heroes/status/478685115053133824" target="_blank">
         tweet sent out by the show
        </a>
        Monday.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <div class="widget tweetembed">
        <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en">

           pic.twitter.com/7LenkJ0Nao
          </a>
         </p>
          Heroes Reborn (@heroes)
         <a href="https://twitter.com/heroes/statuses/478685115053133824">
          June 16, 2014
         </a>
        </blockquote>
        <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
        </script>
       </div>
       <p>
        Jack Coleman played Noah Bennett (aka "HRG" or "Horn-Rimmed Glasses") in the original series, which ran from 2006 to 2010. Bennett, a former Company agent, was the father of super cheerleader Claire, played by Hayden Panettiere. The "Nashville" actress
        <a href="http://www.cosmopolitan.com/celebrity/exclusive/hayden-panettiere-neutrogena-interview" target="_blank">
         recently told Cosmopolitan
        </a>
        that she had not (yet) been asked to rejoin the series, speculating that the show may return with "a whole new set of characters."
       </p>
       <p>
        Not
        <em>
         all
        </em>
        new, it seems.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://secure.nbcnews.com/_tps/_accounts/msnbc/newsletters" target="_blank">
         Sign up for top Entertainment news direct to your inbox
        </a>
       </p>
       <div class="inline-img-container">
        <figure class="entry-img small-image retina-enabled">

         <small class="entry-img-credit">

         <div class="entry-img-caption">
          "Heroes" writer Jeph Loeb and show actors Adrian Pasdar, Hayden Panettiere, Jack Coleman and actor Masi Oka in 2007.
         </div>
        </figure>
       </div>
       <p>
        Coleman's a familiar face to TV fans, appearing as Caroline's father on "The Vampire Diaries" and Senator Bracken on "Castle." He's also racked up a few parts as closeted gay men, as on "The Office" (Angela's senator husband) and "Scandal" (Sally's husband).
       </p>
       <p>
        The original "Heroes" followed the adventures of ordinary folks who found themselves mysteriously possessed of supernatural powers. Creator Tim Kring is also behind the 13-episode reboot.
       </p>
       <p>
        "Heroes Reborn" is set to air in 2015 on NBC.
        <br/>
