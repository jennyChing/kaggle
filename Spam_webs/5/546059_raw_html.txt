        <p>
         Designer Steve Kuhl of
         <a href="http://www.kuhldesignbuild.com/">
          Kuhl Design
         </a>
         is looking like the father of the year after building his son an awe inspiring pirate themed bedroom.
        </p>
        <p>
         The room has everything youd expect with this motif, including a pirate ship hull/clubhouse, a rope bridge to access the hull, nautical accoutrements, a crows nest, and best of all a
         <em>
          freaking slide
         </em>
         that goes down an entire floor. Luckiest kid ever? We think so. Check out the incredible pics below.
