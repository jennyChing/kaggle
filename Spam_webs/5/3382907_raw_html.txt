        <h1 class="full_story_title">
         One with the Water Receives GuideStar Exchange Gold Participation Level as a Transparent Non-Profit
        </h1>
        <p class="DateAndAuthor">
         Oct 27 2013, 3:16am CDT | by PR.com
        </p>
        <h2 class="StoryIntro">
         One with the Water today received the GuideStar Exchange Gold participation level, a leading symbol of transparency and accountability. This level demonstrates One with the Waters deep commitment to nonprofit transparency and accountability.
        </h2>
        <div style="float:left;margin:6px;">
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <!-- Balla Story -->
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-8330330673974283" data-ad-slot="6654334554" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
         </ins>
         <script>
          (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
         </script>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="/category/los-angeles">
          Los Angeles
         </a>
         , CA,  October 27, 2013 --(
         <a href="http://www.pr.com/" rel="nofollow">
          PR.com
         </a>
         )-- One with the Water today received the
         <a href="/category/guidestar">
          GuideStar
         </a>
         Exchange Gold participation level, a leading symbol of
         <a href="/category/transparency">
          transparency
         </a>
         and accountability provided by GuideStar
         <a href="/category/usa">
          USA
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="/category/inc-0">
          Inc
         </a>
         ., the premier source of nonprofit information. This level demonstrates One with the Water's deep commitment to nonprofit transparency and accountability. In order to be awarded the GuideStar Exchange logo, One with the Water was required to fill out every required field of the nonprofit report page on www.guidestar.org for the Gold level of participation.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         "We have worked hard to showcase our progress toward our mission, and our long-held belief in being transparent about our work, to our constituents," said
         <a href="/category/kenneth-rippetoe">
          Kenneth Rippetoe
         </a>
         , Founder and Executive Director. "As a GuideStar Exchange participant, we use their platform to share a wealth of up-to-date information about our work to our supporters and GuideStar's immense online audience of nonprofits, grant makers, individual donors, and the media."
         <br/>
         <br/>
         "I encourage you to check out our profile on GuideStar to see what we're
         <a href="/category/all-0">
          all
         </a>
         about," added Kenneth Rippetoe. "We are engaged in exciting initiatives, and we are thrilled to have another platform for communicating our advancement and progress."
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The GuideStar Exchange is an initiative designed to connect nonprofits with current and potential supporters. With millions of people coming to GuideStar to learn more about nonprofit organizations, the GuideStar Exchange allows nonprofits to share a wealth of up-to-date information with GuideStar's
         <a href="/category/many">
          many
         </a>
         audiences. Becoming a GuideStar Exchange participant is free of charge. To join, organizations need to
         <a href="/category/update">
          update
         </a>
         their report pages, completing all required fields for participation. The GuideStar Exchange level logos, acknowledged as symbols of transparency in the nonprofit sector, are displayed on all Exchange participants' nonprofit reports.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         As a
         <a href="/category/non-profit">
          non-profit
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="/category/grants">
          grants
         </a>
         and donations to One with the Water pay exclusively for pool space, insurance, and swim lessons for children, Veterans and families with
         <a href="/category/limited">
          limited
         </a>
         financial means. Since its founding, One with the Water has given out over $51,000 in
         <a href="/category/scholarships">
          scholarships
         </a>
         to needy students and changed the lives of over 700 swimmers. One with the Water is proud to offer special needs swim classes for persons with
         <a href="/category/disabilities">
          disabilities
         </a>
         , or mental or physical challenges. Swimming lessons are available year-round with swim classes designed to help swimmers learn and improve skills, maintain physical
         <a href="/category/fitness">
          fitness
         </a>
         , and achieve success.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Tax-deductible donations to One with the Water, a 501(c)(3), can be made at the organization's website: OneWithTheWater.org/donate.aspx.
        </p>
