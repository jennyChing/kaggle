More often than not, Tatum will train with William J. Harris, a New York based fitness instructor. William says that in order to achieve this body type, you only have to be working out 30-45 minutes a day. But of course, the workouts are of a high intensity!&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;For the film The Fighter, Tatum was set a circuit of 4 exercises a day. However he had to continuously do the same circuit for 30 minutes. This circuit has a 5 minute warm up and a 10 minute cool down period.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Or course for different films Tatum will include different warm ups and different additional exercise each day. For The Fighter, it was fitting for him to include 2 hours a day of boxing along side his circuit training. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt; &lt;hr style="width:100%;clear:both;visibility:hidden;"&gt;&lt;/hr&gt;  &lt;div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;"&gt;The Channing Tatum workout routine is a 6 day plan with only 1 day of rest. You can afford to workout for 6 days because the workouts are only 30-45 minutes long each. He warms up on a treadmill for 5 minutes then jumps straight into his circuit routine;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Jump Rope Skips x 100&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Military Press x 25&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Bench Press x 25&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Push Ups x 25&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;Tatum has 30 minutes to complete the circuit as many times as possible using a heavy weight for each of the presses. Each time you do this circuit you can change to a decline press or raised press to hit different muscle fibres. Make sure you keep the intensity up and move straight onto the next exercise without any&nbsp;time in between. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;Once this circuit has been doe for 30 minutes, cool down with stretching and a slow jog. &lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt; &lt;hr style="width:100%;clear:both;visibility:hidden;"&gt;&lt;/hr&gt;  &lt;h2 style="text-align:left;"&gt;Channing Tatum Workout: Tuesday&lt;/h2&gt;  &lt;span class='imgPusher' style='float:left;height:0px'&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span style='z-index:10;position:relative;float:left;;clear:left;margin-top:0px;*margin-top:0px'&gt;&lt;a&gt;&lt;img src="/uploads/1/4/4/6/14463812/7279279.jpg?289" style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 10px; border-width:1px;padding:3px;" alt="Picture" class="galleryImageBorder" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span style="display: block; font-size: 90%; margin-top: -10px; margin-bottom: 10px; text-align: center;" class="wsite-caption"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;display:block;"&gt;On Tuesday, Tatum focuses on burning off body fat whilst strengthening his core. This circuit is quite demanding and requires you to push yourself for the full 30 minutes. You will need some dumbbells to perform some the circuit.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Burpees x 25&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Squats x 25&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Walking Lunges x 25&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Hanging Leg&nbsp;Lifts x 25&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Make sure you do this for the full 30 minutes. Each workout is designed to push you to your limits. Once you start felling tired, make sure that you keep good form even if you&nbsp;have to go&nbsp;slower. The Burpees are particularly demanding, especially if you're not used to doing them. However this workout is very rewarding and can torch a huge amount of fat if done for the whole 30 minutes. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Cool down after by stretching properly for 10 minutes. &lt;/div&gt; &lt;hr style="width:100%;clear:both;visibility:hidden;"&gt;&lt;/hr&gt;  &lt;h2 style="text-align:left;"&gt;Channing Tatum Workout: Wednesday&lt;/h2&gt;  &lt;span class='imgPusher' style='float:left;height:0px'&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span style='z-index:10;position:relative;float:left;;clear:left;margin-top:0px;*margin-top:0px'&gt;&lt;a&gt;&lt;img src="/uploads/1/4/4/6/14463812/6889651.jpg?290" style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 10px; border-width:1px;padding:3px;" alt="Picture" class="galleryImageBorder" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span style="display: block; font-size: 90%; margin-top: -10px; margin-bottom: 10px; text-align: center;" class="wsite-caption"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;display:block;"&gt;Channing Tatum's workouts revolve around building lean muscle and burning off fat. On Wednesday, Tatum goes on to do another fat burning workout. These types of workouts help him to build his abdominal muscles whilst burning off fat around his core. You can use your dumbbells to do the kettle bell swings involved. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Sprints x 1 lap (Or 1 minute on treadmill high speed)&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Kettle Bell Swings x 25 &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Mountain Climbers x 30&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Sit Ups x 50&nbsp;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;br /&gt;Make sure that you complete this circuit back to back for 30 minutes. Make sure that you don't have a break in between each exercise to keep your metabolism up and the fat burning. This cardio workout is another short 30 minute workout but it's extremely powerful at helping you lean down whilst strengthening your core. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Cool down by stretching you arms and legs for 10 minutes after. &lt;/div&gt; &lt;hr style="width:100%;clear:both;visibility:hidden;"&gt;&lt;/hr&gt;  &lt;h2 style="text-align:left;"&gt;Channing Tatum Workout: Thursday&lt;/h2&gt;  &lt;span class='imgPusher' style='float:left;height:0px'&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span style='z-index:10;position:relative;float:left;;clear:left;margin-top:0px;*margin-top:0px'&gt;&lt;a&gt;&lt;img src="/uploads/1/4/4/6/14463812/3422565.jpg?288" style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 10px; border-width:1px;padding:3px;" alt="Picture" class="galleryImageBorder" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span style="display: block; font-size: 90%; margin-top: -10px; margin-bottom: 10px; text-align: center;" class="wsite-caption"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;display:block;"&gt;&nbsp;Thursdays workout is the same as Mondays workout. (Refer back to Monday.)&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;font size="5"&gt;CT: "&lt;em&gt;T&lt;/em&gt;&lt;em&gt;his is how I approach my work out: I want to be the best athlete I can possibly be. If I can out-perform some of the better athletes then I'm happy. When I look at the NFL or the NBA, these guys look how I want to look - it's useable, functional muscle.&nbsp; I don't work out because [puts on best meathead voice] "I want to get my chest bigger! I want to get huge arms..."&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt; &lt;hr style="width:100%;clear:both;visibility:hidden;"&gt;&lt;/hr&gt;  &lt;h2 style="text-align:left;"&gt;Channing Tatum Workout: Friday&lt;/h2&gt;  &lt;span class='imgPusher' style='float:left;height:0px'&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span style='z-index:10;position:relative;float:left;;clear:left;margin-top:0px;*margin-top:0px'&gt;&lt;a&gt;&lt;img src="/uploads/1/4/4/6/14463812/2993082.jpg?287" style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 10px; border-width:1px;padding:3px;" alt="Picture" class="galleryImageBorder" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span style="display: block; font-size: 90%; margin-top: -10px; margin-bottom: 10px; text-align: center;" class="wsite-caption"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;display:block;"&gt;Friday is Tatum's day off. Make sure you give yourself a day off as the workouts are demanding and you need to let your muscles recover. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Channing uses his day off to spend time with his family and rehearse his lines. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;em&gt;&lt;font size="5"&gt;"The method I use with all of my clients is to simply measure their daily activity level and fuel their bodies accordingly. So for example, Channing&rsquo;s morning started with 2 hours of fight training followed by our workout, then script readings, rehearsals, and meetings &lt;br /&gt; that didn&rsquo;t require much physical strain for the rest of the day." - William J Harris (Channing Tatum's instructor)&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt; &lt;hr style="width:100%;clear:both;visibility:hidden;"&gt;&lt;/hr&gt;  &lt;h2 style="text-align:left;"&gt;Channing Tatum Workout: Saturday&lt;/h2&gt;  &lt;span class='imgPusher' style='float:left;height:0px'&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span style='z-index:10;position:relative;float:left;;clear:left;margin-top:0px;*margin-top:0px'&gt;&lt;a&gt;&lt;img src="/uploads/1/4/4/6/14463812/5988460.jpg?286" style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 10px; border-width:1px;padding:3px;" alt="Picture" class="galleryImageBorder" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span style="display: block; font-size: 90%; margin-top: -10px; margin-bottom: 10px; text-align: center;" class="wsite-caption"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;display:block;"&gt;Channing Tatum's Saturday workout is the same as Tuesdays workout. (Refer back to Tuesday.)&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Every exercise in Tatum's workouts are helping to work each muscle group. There are zero isolation exercises which helps to keep his metabolism revving and fat burning. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;This helps to give more of a testosterone boost to his muscles which helps them to stimulate and grow. Each workout is designed to target everything which is why this type of training is so effective at also burning fat. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Isolation exercises are great for pumping up muscles and concentrating grow on each individual muscle but like Tatum says himself, he does not want to get huge, nor was that ever his aim in any film role.&nbsp;It us just a preference of his to&nbsp;look this way.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Because&nbsp;this&nbsp;type of training is different to most, you must&nbsp;make sure that you perform for the whole 30 minutes to get the full effect of each workout. You need to&nbsp;do each exercise back to back with no rest.&nbsp;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt; &lt;hr style="width:100%;clear:both;visibility:hidden;"&gt;&lt;/hr&gt;  &lt;h2 style="text-align:left;"&gt;Channing Tatum Workout: Sunday&lt;/h2&gt;  &lt;span class='imgPusher' style='float:left;height:0px'&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span style='z-index:10;position:relative;float:left;;clear:left;margin-top:0px;*margin-top:0px'&gt;&lt;a&gt;&lt;img src="/uploads/1/4/4/6/14463812/7533713.jpg?298" style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 10px; none;" alt="Picture" class="galleryImageBorder" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span style="display: block; font-size: 90%; margin-top: -10px; margin-bottom: 10px; text-align: center;" class="wsite-caption"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;display:block;"&gt;Tatum's Sunday workout is the same as Wednesdays workout. (refer back to Wednesday.)&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;em&gt;&lt;font size="5"&gt;"We used absolutely NO machines, simply dumbbells,   a medicine ball, and a jump rope!&hellip;     While Channing&rsquo;s workouts did   systematically allow for proper cycles of work, rest, and recovery, there was   not a set &ldquo;routine&rdquo;. Routine is the enemy! I say that because in order to   maintain consistent change and progress, you can not allow the body to   plateau." - William J. Harris (Channing Tatum's instructor.)&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/em&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt; &lt;hr style="width:100%;clear:both;visibility:hidden;"&gt;&lt;/hr&gt;  &lt;h2 style="text-align:left;"&gt;The Channing Tatum Diet: The Food Used To Fuel The Lean Body&lt;/h2&gt;  &lt;span class='imgPusher' style='float:left;height:0px'&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span style='z-index:10;position:relative;float:left;;clear:left;margin-top:0px;*margin-top:0px'&gt;&lt;a&gt;&lt;img src="/uploads/1/4/4/6/14463812/9366494.jpg?280" style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 10px; border-width:1px;padding:3px;" alt="Picture" class="galleryImageBorder" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span style="display: block; font-size: 90%; margin-top: -10px; margin-bottom: 10px; text-align: center;" class="wsite-caption"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt; &lt;div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;display:block;"&gt;&lt;li style="left: 0px; top: 1px;"&gt;Channing Tatum's diet is nothing out of the ordinary. It all comes down to understanding what the body type he wants requires and then fuelling it with the right foods at the right time;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/li&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;li style="left: 0px; top: 1px;"&gt;&lt;font size="3"&gt;&lt;em&gt;&ldquo;Once someone understands the function of protein, carbohydrates, fats and why they should or should not eat them in relation to their goals, it makes healthy eating relatively easy. I recommend reading up on the Paleo Diet and creating your personal plan around that &lt;br /&gt; philosophy. I also recommend finding a local organic farmer and butcher, and&nbsp;centering your diet around poultry, fish, eggs, lean meats plenty of green veggies, and plenty of fruit. &lt;br /&gt;Furthermore, learning to prepare your favourite dishes in a healthier way as opposed to eradicating them altogether helps eliminate those &ldquo;cravings&rdquo; which will derail even those of us with the strongest of willpower. In my opinion, the only limitations and restrictions that need to be put in place are on salt, sugar, and refined carbs." - William J. Harris.&lt;/em&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/li&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;li style="left: 0px; top: 1px;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;Tatum has 6 meals a day. This is what&nbsp;a typical days meals amount to;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/li&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Breakfast &ndash;&lt;/strong&gt; veggie omelette, multigrain toast, Morningstar vegetarian sausage patties, and orange slices.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;strong&gt;AM snack &ndash;&lt;/strong&gt; apple slices and unsalted mixed nuts.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Lunch &ndash;&lt;/strong&gt; grilled bbq chicken breast, spinach, and a sweet potato.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Afternoon snack &ndash;&lt;/strong&gt; string cheese&nbsp;and unsalted mixed nuts.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Dinner &ndash; &lt;/strong&gt;a turkey burger,&nbsp;broccoli, and carrots.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;strong&gt;PM snack &ndash;&lt;/strong&gt; yogurt and unsalted&nbsp;almonds (and a jell-o cup if something sweet was&nbsp;desired).&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;em&gt;&lt;font size="3"&gt;"He simply ate a clean and well balanced diet. Again, that&rsquo;s not exactly what he ate every day, but that &lt;br /&gt; was the basis of his diet. Channing also ate whole wheat pizza topped with grilled chicken and broccoli, turkey lasagne made with multi-grain pasta and especially made sauces and cheeses, turkey bacon cheeseburgers with sweet potato, and other specially made dishes which broke up the monotony and made his diet enjoyable&hellip;which is also a key component of maintaining healthy eating habits. It&rsquo;s all about learning to season your foods without loading them up with salt, sugar, and excess calories.&rdquo; - William J.
