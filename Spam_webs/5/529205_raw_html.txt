      <p>
       *This post may contain affiliate links
       <a href="http://i2.wp.com/cheshirecatlookingglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMGA0684.jpg">
        <img alt="Microbiome Plus+GI Probiotic" class="aligncenter wp-image-12515" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/cheshirecatlookingglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMGA0684.jpg?resize=417%2C476"/>

       After the holidays I like to do a cleanse, the best follow up is a probiotic to help balance you out. Microbiome Plus+GI offered to let me test their product. Having used probiotics in the past, I was curious how this would stack up.
       <span id="more-12513">
       </span>
       As you can see in the above photo, there are 4 boxes, inside of each box are 2 foils of the pills. They are nicely lined up to match our 2 pills per meal and could easily be cut to separate them.
       <a href="http://i2.wp.com/cheshirecatlookingglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMGA0710.jpg">
        <img alt="Microbiome Plus+GI 1 month probiotic supply" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12518" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/cheshirecatlookingglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMGA0710.jpg?resize=570%2C480"/>
       </a>
       They suggest that you take one of each capsule and tablet twice daily with meals. Most probiotics are 1 pill once or twice a day, so this is a bit different. If you already take morning and evening pills and keep them in an organizer/container then putting these in will be easy. Otherwise I can easily see forgetting some.
       <b>
        Ingredients
       </b>
       : PROBIOTIC Capsule: Lactobacillus reuteri NCIMB 30242 3.5 billion CFU. Other Ingredients: microcrystalline cellulose, silicon dioxide, magnesium stearate, vegetable capsule (hypromellose, titanium dioxide). PREBIOTIC scFOS TAblet: scFOS (Short-chain fructo oligosaccharides) 600mg. Other Ingredients: microcrystalline cellulose, dicalcium phosphate, stearic acid, magnesium stearate, croscarmellose sodium and silicon dioxide. About Lactobacillus reuteri NCIMB 30242: Lactobacillus reuteri NCIMB 30242 is a probiotic bacteria of the genus and species Lactobacillus reuteri. It acts through its capacity to produce bile salt hydrolase (BSH) enzyme and reduces cholesterol levels by decreasing absorption of dietary cholesterol and increasing metabolic elimination of cholesterol from the blood. Lactobacillus reuteri NCIMB 30242 has been shown to effectively reduce total cholesterol (TC), low-density lipoprotein cholesterol (LDL-C), apolipoprotien B 100 (ApoB-100), and non-high density lipoprotein cholesterol (non-HDL-C) in human clinical trials. In addition to its cholesterol-lowering properties, the strain provides traditional probiotic benefits such as improved digestive health and immune function. Quite simply most of the ingredients I did not understand, but they dont look to be too natural. These are available on

        Amazon.
