        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Meet The Zelfs: Toy Review
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2013-10-17T18:04:45+00:00">

         </span>

         <span class="author vcard">

         <h4>
          <span style="color: #dc143c;">
           I received a complimentary product in exchange for my honest review. All opinions expressed here are 100% mine. This post may contain affiliate links. Please see my
           <a href="http://www.mommyrunsit.com/about/disclosure-policy/" title="Disclosure Policy">
            <span style="color: #dc143c;">
             Disclosure Policy
            </span>
           </a>
           for more information.
          </span>
         </h4>
         <h3>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Zelfs? What on earth are Zelfs?
          </span>
         </h3>
         <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_482" style="width: 490px">
          <img alt="zelfs" class=" wp-image-482 " height="480" src="http://www.mommyrunsit.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/zelf1.jpg" width="480"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           This is a Zelf.
          </p>
         </div>
         <h6>
          <a href="http://thezelfs.com/us/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="source">
           source
          </a>
         </h6>
         <p>
          At first glance, you might think youre looking at a troll doll (assuming, of course, that you were born before 1995). And while the resemblance is undeniable, the
          <a href="http://thezelfs.com/us/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Zelfs">
           Zelfs
          </a>
          are whimsical little creatures with a fun story that makes them unique. At twilight, the Zelfs emerge from the secret garden of Zardenia, where they live in different fabitats  trees, mounds of rocks, even dark corners. They get together to chat at Buttershys Flitterpad and grab a potion and a bite to eat at the Witchbrew Bistro. Each colorful, wild-haired creature has its own unique persona, a special Zelf power (represented on their bellies by a Zelfmark), and matching charms and hair accessories.
         </p>
         <p>
          The
          <a href="http://thezelfs.com/us/" target="_blank" title="Zelfs">
           Zelfs
          </a>
          come in 3 different sizes. The largest is about 4 inches tall, and the smallest (the lil Zelfs) are about one inch tall. I was sent a lil. Zelf to review. These are really quite tiny, but at a retail price of only $1.99 and 36 to choose from, you can purchase several and create a tiny Zelf family.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_484" style="width: 370px">
          <img alt="Meet the Zelfs | Mommy Runs It" class=" wp-image-484 " height="270" src="http://www.mommyrunsit.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/zelf2.jpg" width="360"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           The Lil Zelfs
          </p>
         </div>
         <h6>
          <a href="http://thezelfs.com/us/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="source">
           source
          </a>
         </h6>
         <p>
          Our Zelf arrived in her own planter. She is the perfect size to play in the dollhouse with the princesses, and she also makes a great pencil topper! Her hair is super-soft and fun to twirl and comb. Heres what my 5 year old daughter has to say about her new Zelf: I love her. Shes pretty. I like the color of her hair and her wings. Her hair is very long and I can put skirts on her, but not shirts. And she fits on top of my pencil. I like playing with her.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Meet the Zelfs | Mommy Runs It" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-486" height="1024" src="http://www.mommyrunsit.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/zelf-collage-395x1024.jpg" width="395"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          To learn more about the Zelfs, visit them
          <a href="http://thezelfs.com/us/" target="_blank" title="online">
           online
          </a>
          .
         </p>
