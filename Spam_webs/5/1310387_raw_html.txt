           <h2 class="post-title">
            Sporty in Seattle: Sporting Events That Give You a Reason to Stay in Seattle
           </h2>
          </a>
          <div class="post-author">
           posted by Davis Moon, Rooms Division Manager, October 06, 2014;
           <span class="tags" rel="108012">
            Tags:
            <a href="?tag=554">
             Local Attractions
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="?tag=552">
             Recreation
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="post-intro">
           <p>
            Seattle has a wider array of sport offerings throughout the city and the fans in Seattle are truly the best. They are loyal to their teams win or lose which makes sporting events here so much more enjoyable. The Woodmark Hotel is a gathering place for many of these fans whether to catch a game on t.v. in
            <a href="http://www.destinationhotels.com/admin/thewoodmark.com/beachcafe" target="_blank" title="Beach Cafe Kirkland">
             Beach Caf
            </a>
            or catch ride over to a game on our Husky Cruises. Keep reading for the big sports to catch while you're in Seattle.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="post-description-open">
           <p>
            Right now, the biggest Seattle team in the news is the Seattle Seahawks as they just came off their Super Bowl season and are already showing signs of a repeat this season. The number 12th men has grown drastically and you can see by simply observing those around you near Seattle. Jerseys, shirts, flags, posters, and many other signs of being a fan populate the streets leading up to each week's game. At the Woodmark Hotel in Kirkland right outside of Seattle, we show our support for the Hawks each week. Our bartenders at
            <a href="http://www.destinationhotels.com/admin/thewoodmark.com/beachcafe" target="_blank" title="Beach Cafe Kirkland">
             Beach Caf
            </a>
            serve 12th Man drink products and show every game on the TV's in the bar. Many of our employees put hints of blue and green in their attire to show their support on the clock as well. Our Director of Barketing even sports a 12th man scarf or jersey in support too! The biggest benefit of being a part of this movement is the community it builds. The Woodmark has always valued being a part of the Greater Seattle community and this gives another way to do so.
           </p>
           <p>
            The Seattle Mariners recently wrapped up their season just one game away from making the playoffs this year. Though that doesn't sound successful the previous years have been nowhere near that mark and the fans have taken notice. You could feel the buzz among our guests and the surrounding city members. Moving to next season there will be excitement and hope for the fans so make sure you join in to witness it. Being right off the freeway on your way to Seattle, The Woodmark is here for you to return to after the game!
           </p>
           <p>
            Lastly, we have the Seattle Sounders FC club that has brought soccer to life in the Seattle area. Our controller, Jac Cooper is a huge Sounders fan and is the beginning of their support in our Woodmarkteam. Being right near 520 is a great excuse for Sounders fans to make a staycation out of their next MLS experience with us. After the game they can grab some snacks from our Raid the Pantry amenity and then retire to their Napa Black Label Pillow Top Bed for a resting sleep.
           </p>
           <p>
            There's always a reason to celebrate, whether it's a win or just a great experience at one of Seattle's sporting events. When you find that reason, the Woodmark Hotelnear Seattle is ready to make that celebration special.
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             Call Reservations at 888.329.7553 to schedule your sporting experience today!
            </em>
           </p>

			$('#dhr-brandtile-bar a#dhr-brandtile-trigger').removeClass('bt-active');
			$('#dhr-brandtile-content').slideUp(300);
		});

		$('.col2-m').wrapAll( '&lt;div class="medium-6 columns"/&gt;' );

		$(".bt-florida, .bt-hawaii, .bt-louisiana, .bt-new-mexico, .bt-new-york").addClass('col2-bt');
		$(".bt-north-carolina, .bt-oregon,  .bt-south-carolina, .bt-texas, .bt-vermont,  .bt-virginia").addClass('col3-bt');
		$(".bt-washington, .bt-washington-dc").addClass('col4-bt');

		$('.col1-bt').wrapAll( '&lt;div class="large-6 columns"/&gt;' );
		$('.col2-bt').wrapAll( '&lt;div class="large-6 columns"/&gt;' );
		$('.col3-bt').wrapAll( '&lt;div class="large-6 columns"/&gt;' );
		$('.col4-bt').wrapAll( '&lt;div class="large-6 columns"/&gt;' );

		// build UTM Tracking URL

		var curDomain = document.domain;
		curDomain = curDomain.replace('www.', '');
		$('#dhr-brandtile-properties ul li a').each(function() {
			var curHREF = $(this).attr('href');
			var campaign = $(this).text();
			campaign = campaign.replace('-', '');
			campaign = campaign.split(' ').join('_');
			campaign = campaign.split('.').join('_');
			campaign = campaign.split(',').join('_');
			var utmURL = '?utm_source=' + curDomain + '&amp;utm_medium=referral' + '&amp;utm_campaign=' + campaign + '_brand_tile';
			$(this).attr('href', curHREF + utmURL);
		});
	});
