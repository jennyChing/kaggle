       <noscript>
        <img alt="camel desert" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15758" height="683" src="http://9227-presscdn-0-11.pagely.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_1414.jpg" width="1024"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        Three years ago, when my site was still fairly new, I traveled to a remarkable country in the Middle East for the first time, Jordan. That trip was an important one personally. It helped changed some opinions I had of the region and developed a deep love for the people (and food) of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. Ive wanted to return for a long time and starting next week, Ill get that chance.
       </p>
       <p>
        Starting Monday, October 13, I will fly to Amman, Jordan where Ill be meeting up with other bloggers and writers to start an exciting trip, the #GoJordan adventure. The goal is to once again see some of the best Jordan has to offer, but also to go a little deeper than I did the first time. This time the people of Jordan will be a big focus as I meander around the country. Whether its learning basic Arabic in Amman or discovering what Bedouin life is like in Wadi Rum, this time I will explore Jordan through the eyes of her people and I frankly cant wait to get started.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Wadi Rum, Jordan" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10992" data-lazy-src="http://9227-presscdn-0-11.pagely.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Wadi.jpg" height="550" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="940"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="Wadi Rum, Jordan" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10992" height="550" src="http://9227-presscdn-0-11.pagely.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Wadi.jpg" width="940"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        As you may know, Jordan is home to some of the most exciting and beautiful sites in the world from Petra to the deserts of Wadi Rum and even the warm waters of the Red Sea. Theres a lot to see and do here, especially for the luxury adventure traveler. Thats the perspective from which Ill be exploring Jordan this time. Its my usual mode of travel and thankfully there are plenty of opportunities for both in Jordan. So, what will I be doing? Some of it Im keeping secret for now, but a few of the highlights include:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Hiking through Dana Biosphere Reserve and staying at the Feynan Ecolodge, one of the top ecolodges in the world
        </li>
        <li>
         Embracing my inner Indiana Jones at Petra, hiking through the Siq to the Treasury and on to the Monastery.
        </li>
        <li>
         Follow the footsteps of Lawrence of Arabia in Wadi Rum desert and sleep under the stars at a Bedouin camp
        </li>
        <li>
         Return to one of my favorite places in the world, MaIn Hot Springs and their famous hot waterfalls
