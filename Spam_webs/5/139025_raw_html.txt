        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Rosemary Hummus Cranberry Turkey Wraps
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-11-16T11:23:43+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          11.16.14
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://shewearsmanyhats.com/author/ajohnson/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Amy Johnson
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://shewearsmanyhats.com/rosemary-hummus-turkey-wraps/#comments">
           21 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         These
         <strong>
          <em>
           Rosemary Hummus Cranberry Turkey Wraps
          </em>
         </strong>
         are a great way to use leftovers.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://shewearsmanyhats.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Rosemary-Hummus-Turkey-Cranberry-Wrap-5new.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="These Rosemary Hummus Cranberry Turkey Wraps are like Thanksgiving dinner all wrapped up in a tasty little package." class="aligncenter wp-image-22186 size-full" height="967" src="http://cf.shewearsmanyhats.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Rosemary-Hummus-Turkey-Cranberry-Wrap-5new.jpg" width="680"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://shewearsmanyhats.com/rosemary-hummus-turkey-wraps/&amp;media=http://shewearsmanyhats.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Rosemary-Hummus-Turkey-Cranberry-Wrap-5new.jpg&amp;description=Rosemary Hummus Cranberry Turkey Wraps | http://shewearsmanyhats.com/rosemary-hummus-turkey-wraps/')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Our fridge is always stocked with one or two containersofhummus. Although wevebeen eating hummus daily as a snack or light lunch with chips or vegetable crudits foryears, I had not thought about using it other than as a dip or as an ingredient in a recipe until enjoying a light veggie-filled wrap this past summer that included hummus. It was the perfect combination and no other condiment was needed. It seemed so obvious. Why I had not considered using hummus that way before? Such is life, right?
         <span id="more-13949">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://shewearsmanyhats.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Rosemary-Hummus-Turkey-Cranberry-Wrap-3new.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="These Rosemary Hummus Cranberry Turkey Wraps are like Thanksgiving dinner all wrapped up in a tasty little package." class="aligncenter wp-image-22184 size-full" height="1350" src="http://cf.shewearsmanyhats.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Rosemary-Hummus-Turkey-Cranberry-Wrap-3new.jpg" width="590"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://shewearsmanyhats.com/rosemary-hummus-turkey-wraps/&amp;media=http://shewearsmanyhats.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Rosemary-Hummus-Turkey-Cranberry-Wrap-3new.jpg&amp;description=Rosemary Hummus Cranberry Turkey Wraps | http://shewearsmanyhats.com/rosemary-hummus-turkey-wraps/')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I was originally going to share a recipe I found over at Sabra for
         <strong>
          Stuffed Chicken Breast
         </strong>
         , but began thinking about all the turkey leftovers that will pop up throughout the holidays. Thanksgiving leftovers can get boring, but Ive got a plan of attack thanks to the handy dandy wrap and some Sabra Hummus:
         <strong>
          <em>
           Rosemary Hummus Cranberry Turkey Wraps
          </em>
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         A little turkey (or chicken), hummus, cranberry sauce (or your favorite preserve) all wrapped snug in a soft tortilla, and youve got a quick meal thats pretty healthy too.
        </p>
        <p>
         Sabra offers a huge variety of hummus flavors.
         <strong>
          <a href="https://sabra.com/products/Roasted-Pine-Nut-Hummus" rel="nofollow">
           Roasted Pine Nut Hummus
          </a>
         </strong>
         is our all-time favorite, with a close second going to
         <strong>
          Jalapeo Hummus
         </strong>
         for me (if you can find it, try it). They also feature special flavors at certain times of the year.
        </p>
        <p>
         Sometimes Sabra creates seasonal favorites like
         <em>
          Hummus with Rosemary, Olive Oil and Sea Salt
         </em>
         , which I thought would be excellent in this wrap with leftover turkey or chicken. I think any hummus will work great with turkey, but do keep on the lookout for seasonal special combinations. If Rosemary Hummus isnt available, dried rosemary may be added, if youd like.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://shewearsmanyhats.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Rosemary-Hummus-Turkey-Cranberry-Wrap-4new.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="These Rosemary Hummus Cranberry Turkey Wraps are like Thanksgiving dinner all wrapped up in a tasty little package." class="aligncenter wp-image-22185 size-full" height="452" src="http://cf.shewearsmanyhats.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Rosemary-Hummus-Turkey-Cranberry-Wrap-4new.jpg" width="680"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://shewearsmanyhats.com/rosemary-hummus-turkey-wraps/&amp;media=http://shewearsmanyhats.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Rosemary-Hummus-Turkey-Cranberry-Wrap-4new.jpg&amp;description=Rosemary Hummus Cranberry Turkey Wraps | http://shewearsmanyhats.com/rosemary-hummus-turkey-wraps/')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         After all the major holiday cooking, these
         <strong>
          <em>
           Rosemary Hummus Cranberry Turkey Wraps
          </em>
         </strong>
         couldnt be simpler, AND tastier. I think Im actually looking forward to having some leftover turkey this year.
        </p>
        <p>
         You may also enjoy these easy hummus recipes:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://shewearsmanyhats.com/hummus-olive-goat-cheese-flatbread-recipe/" title="Hummus Olive Goat Cheese Flatbread Recipe">
          Hummus Olive Goat Cheese Flatbread Recipe
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://shewearsmanyhats.com/easy-healthy-power-salad-recipe/" title="Easy Healthy Power Salad Recipe">
          Easy Healthy Power Salad Recipe
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://shewearsmanyhats.com/tomato-cucumber-hummus-snack-recipe/" title="Easy Tomato Cucumber Hummus Snack">
          Easy Tomato Cucumber Hummus Snack
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Enjoy! And happy holidays yall!
         <br/>
        </p>

          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">

          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            soft tortillas/wraps
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Sabra's Season's Finest Hummus with Rosemary, Olive Oil and Sea Salt (or your favorite hummus)*
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">

           </li>

           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spread hummus on soft tortillas, add turkey, and top with cranberry sauce. Salt and paper to taste. Gently roll up and enjoy!
           </li>
          </ol>
