     <h1 class="entry-title">
      Interview: Cake Pop Artist Marsha Talks Spring
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">
      <time class="published" datetime="2014-03-06T08:56:49+00:00">
       March 6, 2014
      </time>
     </div>
    </header>
   </div>
   <!-- /.hero -->
   <div class="wrap">
    <div class="container" role="document">
     <div class="content row">
      <main class="main " role="main">
       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/bookmarklet/?media=http://images.proflowers.com/rtimages/SharisBerries/custom/blog/2014/3/3.6_cakepop_blog5.jpg&amp;url=http://www.berries.com/blog/spring-cake-pop-collection&amp;description=Spring%20%20Collection:%20An%20Interview%20With%20Our%20Resident%20Cake%20Pop%20Artist" target="new">
          <img alt="Easter berries anc cake pops" height="395" src="http://images.proflowers.com/rtimages/SharisBerries/custom/blog/2014/3/3.6_cakepop_blog5.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Our resident cake pop artist Marsha Johnston has one of the sweetest jobs in the company. Shes the mastermind behind this decadent favorites craftsmanship and design. Besides honing our process  all our cake pops are decorated and dipped by hand  she also creates fresh designs for each season. Recently, we had a chance to chat with her about the inspiration behind our spring collection.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/bookmarklet/?media=http://images.proflowers.com/rtimages/SharisBerries/custom/blog/2014/3/3.6_cakepop_blog1.jpg&amp;url=http://www.berries.com/blog/spring-cake-pop-collection&amp;description=Spring%20%20Collection:%20An%20Interview%20With%20Our%20Resident%20Cake%20Pop%20Artist" target="new">
          <img alt="St. Patrick's Day Cake Pops" height="395" src="http://images.proflowers.com/rtimages/SharisBerries/custom/blog/2014/3/3.6_cakepop_blog1.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          The Spirit of St. Patricks Day
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         St. Paddys day is always associated with shamrocks and good luck. So we transformed those themes into a
         <a href="http://products.berries.com/chocolate/6-St-Patricks-Day-Cake-Pops-30065257" target="new">
          delicious cake pop
         </a>
         that makes people smile. After all, not everyone gets into the spirit by drinking green beer (that includes me).
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/bookmarklet/?media=http://images.proflowers.com/rtimages/SharisBerries/custom/blog/2014/3/3.6_cakepop_blog3.jpg&amp;url=http://www.berries.com/blog/spring-cake-pop-collection&amp;description=Spring%20%20Collection:%20An%20Interview%20With%20Our%20Resident%20Cake%20Pop%20Artist" target="new">
          <img alt="Easter Cake Pops" height="395" src="http://images.proflowers.com/rtimages/SharisBerries/custom/blog/2014/3/3.6_cakepop_blog3.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          From the Easter Bunny
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Our
         <a href="http://products.berries.com/chocolate/Handmade-Easter-Cake-Pops-30100227" target="new">
          Easter pops
         </a>
         were designed with children in mind  a gift for Grandma to send when she doesnt live close, complete with fudgy bunnies, chicks and hand-painted eggs. I also love to give my neighbors gifts for every holiday they celebrate; this seems like the perfect way to let them know Im thinking of them. I have a gallery of photos with kids of all ages enjoying Easter pops Ive created.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/create/bookmarklet/?media=http://images.proflowers.com/rtimages/SharisBerries/custom/blog/2014/3/3.6_cakepop_blog4b.jpg&amp;url=http://www.berries.com/blog/spring-cake-pop-collection&amp;description=Spring%20%20Collection:%20An%20Interview%20With%20Our%20Resident%20Cake%20Pop%20Artist" target="new">
          <img alt="Mother's Day Cake Pops" height="395" src="http://images.proflowers.com/rtimages/SharisBerries/custom/blog/2014/3/3.6_cakepop_blog4b.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          Moms Favorite Things
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         As for Mothers Day  we love anything feminine and played around with hats, shoes and purses of all sorts. These pops were a tribute to mom and her favorite accessories.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          Spring for it!
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Embrace the season and
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/spring-gifts-s2e">
          send a taste of spring
         </a>
         to someone you love.
        </p>
       </div>
