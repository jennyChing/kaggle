          <h2>
           Advanced warfare gameplay Induction
          </h2>
          <p>
           E3 has officially started and Microsoft was the first of the three Major console manufacturers to hold their press conference.
          </p>
          <p>
           Call of Duty has been a Microsoft exclusive for many years at E3 and Sledgehammer Games kept up the tradition by showing exclusive Advanced Warfare Gameplay During there press conference .
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="mceItemHidden">
            The advanced warfare Gameplay shown to us was a campaign level called Induction, which takes place in Seoul, South Korea and follows Pvt. Jack Mitchell, Pvt. Will Irons and Sgt. Cormack who get dropped into the city.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="mceItemHidden">
            During the Gameplay you can see Mitchells IMR (Integrated Munitions Rifle) print its own bullets on the fly we see The exoskeletons features, the boost jump which allows you to maneuver like never seen in a Call of Duty game to date.
            <br/>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           During the presentation we can also see the soldiers exoskeleton suit uses Augmented Reality to display tactical info such as ammo and grenade counters.
          </p>
          <p>
           Checkout the full 9 minute advanced warfare Gameplay presentation below:
