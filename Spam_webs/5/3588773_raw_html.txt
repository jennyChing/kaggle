       <p>
        While we were
        <a href="http://www.makingmine.com/texas-adventures/" target="_blank" title="texas adventures">
         vacationing in Texas
        </a>
        , we had the privilege of celebrating my niece Mahalas 4th birthday! Like most little ladies her age, M is obsessed with all things Frozen! My sister planned the most adorable Frozen party for our very own Princess Elsa, and shewas absolutely delighted!
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/mbday2.jpeg">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="princess cupcake dress" class="alignnone wp-image-6214" height="541" src="http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/mbday2-1024x1024.jpeg" width="541"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.makingmine.com/frozen-birthday-princess-cupcake-dress-tutorial/&amp;media=http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/mbday2-1024x1024.jpeg&amp;description=frozen birthday {+ princess cupcake dress tutorial}')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <br/>
        </a>
        My
        <a href="http://www.makingmine.com/12-months-series/" target="_blank" title="12 months of">
         personal obsession with cupcakes
        </a>
        left me in charge of Ms birthday cake (a task I was more than happy to take on)! We decided to make a Frozen inspired princess cupcake dress! It turned out really cute and was very simple to assemble  check it out!
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/princesscupcakedress.jpg">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="princess cupcake dress" class="alignnone wp-image-6211" height="711" src="http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/princesscupcakedress-775x1024.jpg" width="538"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.makingmine.com/frozen-birthday-princess-cupcake-dress-tutorial/&amp;media=http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/princesscupcakedress-775x1024.jpg&amp;description=frozen birthday {+ princess cupcake dress tutorial}')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <br/>
        </a>
        Mahala and I whipped up 2 batches of
        <a href="http://sallysbakingaddiction.com/2013/04/20/very-vanilla-cupcakes/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://sallysbakingaddiction.com/2013/04/20/very-vanilla-cupcakes/', 'these tasty vanilla cupcakes']);" target="_blank">
         these tasty vanilla cupcakes
        </a>
        . I forgot the milk in the first batch (whoops!). They turned out a little dry, but still remarkably good (see the top half of the dress). Then I got to work assembling the dress
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/cupcakecollage.jpeg">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="princess cupcake dress" class="alignnone wp-image-6212" height="762" src="http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/cupcakecollage-731x1024.jpeg" width="544"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.makingmine.com/frozen-birthday-princess-cupcake-dress-tutorial/&amp;media=http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/cupcakecollage-731x1024.jpeg&amp;description=frozen birthday {+ princess cupcake dress tutorial}')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <br/>
        </a>
        First I covered a flattened cardboard box in aluminum foil for my base. I then arranged 26 cupcakes into a dress shape. You can really make this as large or as small as you like. This number fit perfectly on our board!
       </p>
       <p>
        Next, I swirled frosting on each individual cupcake before going back and filling in the gaps. I then blotted the frosting with the tip of the dispenser to create a more textured look.
       </p>
       <p>
        For the larger holes, I placed marshmallows in the gaps to support the frosting (see photo #3 above).
       </p>
       <p>
        Last, Mahala helped me create the candy sash and sequin sprinkles!
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/mbday.jpeg">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="princess cupcake dress" class="alignnone wp-image-6213" height="387" src="http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/mbday-1024x472.jpeg" width="840"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.makingmine.com/frozen-birthday-princess-cupcake-dress-tutorial/&amp;media=http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/mbday-1024x472.jpeg&amp;description=frozen birthday {+ princess cupcake dress tutorial}')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </a>
        <br/>
        For a finishing touch, Rachel added a tiara and wand fit for a princess! Obviously, the frosting HAD to be licked off ;)
       </p>
       <p>
        Rachel also made some beautifully BLUE ice cream to enjoy with our cake  perfect for the Frozen occasion!
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/frozenicecream.jpg">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="princess cupcake dress" class="alignnone wp-image-6217" height="780" src="http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/frozenicecream-710x1024.jpg" width="541"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.makingmine.com/frozen-birthday-princess-cupcake-dress-tutorial/&amp;media=http://www.makingmine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/frozenicecream-710x1024.jpg&amp;description=frozen birthday {+ princess cupcake dress tutorial}')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <br/>
        </a>
        Jeff and I were SO happy to celebrate with Mahala! How blessed are we for getting to enjoy
        <em>
         BOTH
        </em>
        of our nieces big days?! And lets be real, another chance to eat cake and ice cream is always welcomed ;)
       </p>
