         <h2>
          A selection of drawings and paintings by artist and graphic designer Fatma Al-Remaihi.
         </h2>
         <p>
          Fatma Al-Remaihi is a creative graphic designer and artist from Qatar. She studied at the Virginia Commonwealth University and the School of the Art Institute of Chicago. In addition to her work as graphic designer, she uses drawing and painting as an outlet for her creative ideas. Her diverse portfolio includesexperimental self-portraits created with oil orseemingly surreal patterns drawn with ballpoint pen on paper.
         </p>
         <p>
          Below you can see some examples of her work. If you want to know more about this creative designer and artist fromQatar, feel free and visit her website:
          <a href="http://fatmaalremaihi.com/" target="_blank">
           fatmaalremaihi.com
          </a>
         </p>
