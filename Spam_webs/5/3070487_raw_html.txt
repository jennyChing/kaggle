      <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
       <span itemprop="name">
        Victory Kitchens Recalls Chicken Noodle Soup
       </span>
      </h1>
      <p class="post-meta">
       <span>
        By
        <a href="http://goldshieldgroupllc.com/news/author/social-media-coordinator/" title="">
         Alexandra Pavek
        </a>
       </span>
       <span>
        May 7, 2015
       </span>
      </p>
      <div class="clear">
      </div>
      <div class="entry">
       <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="14775566" data-link="http://goldshieldgroupllc.com/news/victory-kitchens-recalls-chicken-noodle-soup/" data-summary="" data-title="Victory Kitchens Recalls Chicken Noodle Soup">
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_2573" style="width: 310px">
        <img alt="Victory Kitchens is recalling thousands of pounds of frozen chicken noodle soup." class="lazy lazy-hidden size-medium wp-image-2573" data-lazy-src="http://goldshieldgroupllc.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Chicken-Noodle-Soup-300x199.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="199" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="300"/>
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Victory Kitchens is recalling thousands of pounds of frozen chicken noodle soup." class="size-medium wp-image-2573" height="199" src="http://goldshieldgroupllc.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Chicken-Noodle-Soup-300x199.jpg" width="300"/>
        </noscript>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Victory Kitchens is recalling thousands of pounds of frozen chicken noodle soup.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        May 1, 2015  Victory Kitchens is
        <a href="http://www.fsis.usda.gov/wps/portal/fsis/topics/recalls-and-public-health-alerts/recall-case-archive/archive/2015/recall-073-2015-release" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.fsis.usda.gov']);" target="_blank">
         recalling
        </a>
        4,572 pounds of chicken noodle soup that contains chicken from a county that is not eligible to send products to the United States. The chicken comes from Thailand and uses poultry ingredients that are not produced under equivalent inspection.
       </p>
       <p>
        The frozen chicken noodle soup products were made on February 5, 2014 and April 1, 2014. Boxes of chicken noodle soup with the product code VK-HALCHX. Each box contains 4 units.
       </p>
       <p>
        The issue was found when a FSIS import inspector observed a shipment with poultry ingredients from Thailand.
       </p>
       <p>
        No adverse reactions have been reported due to the consumption of these products.
       </p>
       <p>
        Consumers and media with questions about the recall can contact Allan Kliger, President of Victory Foods Ltd., (416) 766-5848.
       </p>
      </div>
