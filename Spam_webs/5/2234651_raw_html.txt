       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Pancakes
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-02-15T03:00:58+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          February 15, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.garnishwithlemon.com/author/admin2/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Lisa
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.garnishwithlemon.com/oatmeal-chocolate-chip-pancakes/#comments">
           11 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <a data-pin-color="white" data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" data-pin-height="20" data-pin-lang="en" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/">
         <img src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pinit_fg_en_rect_white_20.png"/>
        </a>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.garnishwithlemon.com/2014/02/15/oatmeal-chocolate-chip-pancakes/">
          <img alt="Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Pancakes " class="size-full wp-image-4323 aligncenter" data-pin-description="Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Pancakes ~ http://www.garnishwithlemon.com" height="818" src="http://www.garnishwithlemon.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Oatmeal-Chocolate-Chip-Pancakes-1-of-2.jpg" title="Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Pancakes " width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 16px;">
          Pancakes are the most requested weekend breakfast from my kids, which is really kind of ironic because my kids dont like syrup on their pancakes. Ever. They are perfectly happy with chocolate chips in the batter and a little butter on top. Seriously. They think that every pancake should have chocolate chips in them. In fact, Greta will protest loudly when pancakes are served without chocolate. (Not that I blame her.)
          <span id="more-4330">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 16px;">
          I have a buttermilk pancake recipe that is, hands down, my daughters all-time favorite pancake. But of course, I cant leave well enough alone and just make those. I need to try new recipes. Thats what food bloggers do, and you have to take the good with the bad. Gretas pancake critiques always start with, These are ok, but your
          <em>
           regular ones
          </em>
          are my favorite. However, I made some headway with these
          <strong>
           Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Pancakes
          </strong>
          . She actually gave them a double thumbs up. Seriously ground breaking progress.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 16px;">
          This Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Pancake recipe makes me happy because they have a little more staying power than straight buttermilk pancakes. I took out half of the buttermilk, added Greek yogurt (love the protein) and subbed some of the all-purpose flour for oat bran (good source of whole grains). And I still got great tasting, fluffy pancakes. No sacrifice of flavor in these beauties.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 16px;">
          Are my kids spoiled for always getting chocolate chips in their pancakes? Maybe. But life is short, so why not enjoy chocolate chips in your flapjacks?
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.garnishwithlemon.com/2014/02/15/oatmeal-chocolate-chip-pancakes/">
          <img alt="Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Pancakes " class="size-full wp-image-4324 aligncenter" data-pin-description="Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Pancakes ~ http://www.garnishwithlemon.com" height="651" src="http://www.garnishwithlemon.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Oatmeal-Chocolate-Chip-Pancakes-2-of-2.jpg" title="Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Pancakes " width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Pancakes
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTopRight">
          <img itemprop="image" src="http://www.garnishwithlemon.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Oatmeal-Chocolate-Chip-Pancakes-1-of-2-220x300.jpg" width="205"/>
          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://www.garnishwithlemon.com/easyrecipe-print/4330-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Print
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTimes">
          <div class="ERSTime">
           <div class="ERSTimeHeading">
            Prep time
           </div>
           <div class="ERSTimeItem">
            <time datetime="PT10M" itemprop="prepTime">
             10 mins
            </time>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSTime ERSTimeRight">
           <div class="ERSTimeHeading">
            Cook time
           </div>
           <div class="ERSTimeItem">
            <time datetime="PT4M" itemprop="cookTime">
             4 mins
            </time>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSTime ERSTimeRight">
           <div class="ERSTimeHeading">
            Total time
           </div>
           <div class="ERSTimeItem">
            <time datetime="PT14M" itemprop="totalTime">
             14 mins
            </time>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClearLeft">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          A fluffy pancake made with Greek yogurt and oatmeal.
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSAuthor">
           Author:
           <span itemprop="author">
            adapted from epicurious
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSCategory">
           Recipe type:
           <span itemprop="recipeCategory">
            Breakfast
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSServes">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            4-6
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            6 ounces plain Greek Yogurt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup buttermilk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 large eggs, lightly beaten
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon vanilla
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoons baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon baking soda
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tablespoons packed brown sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup chocolate chips (optional)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup all purpose flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup oat bran (ground oatmeal)
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place yogurt, buttermilk and beaten eggs in a small bowl and whisk until combined. Add vanilla and mix until incorporated. Set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a medium size bowl, add dry ingredients and whisk to combine. Add yogurt mixture to dry mixture and stir with a wooden spoon to combine. There should still be a few lumps.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spray griddle with cooking spray or brush with melted butter; heat over medium heat. Pour batter on griddle in  cupfuls and sprinkle chocolate chips on top of batter, if desired. Cook until bottoms are golden brown and the edges have quit bubbling. (About 2 minutes) Flip and cook until done.
           </li>
          </ol>
