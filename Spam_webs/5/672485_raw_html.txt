           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/almond-cocoa-frosting-grow-google-circles-hop/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/almond-cocoa-frosting1.jpg&amp;description=Almond Cocoa Frosting and Grow Your Google+ Circles Hop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #800000;">
          <em>
           <strong>
            Almond Cocoa Frosting:
           </strong>
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="color: #737373; text-align: center;">
         3 cups confectioners sugar
        </p>
        <p style="color: #737373; text-align: center;">
         1/3 cup real butter (softened)
        </p>
        <p style="color: #737373; text-align: center;">
         3 Tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
        </p>
        <p style="color: #737373; text-align: center;">
         2 tablespoons milk (may need a little more)
        </p>
        <p style="color: #737373; text-align: center;">
         1 tsp pure almond extract
        </p>
        <p style="color: #737373; text-align: center;">
         1/2 tsp pure vanilla
        </p>
        <p style="color: #737373; text-align: center;">
         Using the whisk on your mixer, blend confectioner sugar, cocoa, and butter.
        </p>
        <p style="color: #737373; text-align: center;">
         Add milk, almond extract, and vanilla. mix until light and creamy.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://wp.me/p2LxGy-25d">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="almond cocoa frosting" class="aligncenter wp-image-7872 size-full" height="699" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/almond-cocoa-frosting.jpg" width="680"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/almond-cocoa-frosting-grow-google-circles-hop/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/almond-cocoa-frosting.jpg&amp;description=Almond Cocoa Frosting and Grow Your Google+ Circles Hop')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
