       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Game Preview: Green Bay Packers Week Two vs New York Jets
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium; color: #000080;">
          <strong>
           Green Bay Packers vs. New York Jets at Lambeau Field  4:25PM
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Television
         </span>
         :
         <em>
          CBS will broadcast the game to a regional audience. Play-by-play man Greg Gumbel joins color commentator
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/G/GreeTr00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Trent Green
           </a>
          </strong>
          and sideline reporter Evan Washburn. DIRECTV subscribers can watch the game on channel 716.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Radio
         </span>
         :
         <em>
          Sports USA will air the game across the country. Adam Amin (play-by-play) and
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/P/PlumJa00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Jake Plummer
           </a>
          </strong>
          (analyst) will call the action, with Mike Bloomberg reporting from the sidelines.
         </em>
         <br/>
         <em>
          The broadcast is also available on Sirius Satellite Radio (WTMJ feed) as part of the networks NFL Sunday Drive.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div style="background-color: #fff; display: inline-block; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue',Arial,sans-serif; color: #a7a7a7; font-size: 11px; width: 100%; max-width: 594px;">
         <div style="overflow: hidden; position: relative; height: 0; padding: 72.390572% 0 0 0; width: 100%;">
          <iframe frameborder="0" height="430" scrolling="no" src="//embed.gettyimages.com/embed/454655794?et=-nZTVpS-Sjp85Q90xsFbXQ&amp;sig=Fin-rtjY3K8KxpmGSEiFl04qFXh0yLiP3QhFdHzcO5k=" style="display: inline-block; position: absolute; top: 0; left: 0; width: 100%; height: 100%;" width="594">
          </iframe>
         </div>
         <div style="padding: 0; margin: 0 0 0 10px; text-align: left;">
          <a href="http://www.gettyimages.com/detail/454655794" style="color: #a7a7a7; text-decoration: none; font-weight: normal !important; border: none; display: inline-block;" target="_blank">
           #454655794
          </a>
          /
          <a href="http://www.gettyimages.com" style="color: #a7a7a7; text-decoration: none; font-weight: normal !important; border: none; display: inline-block;" target="_blank">
           gettyimages.com
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         Fresh off a disappointing season opener in Seattle in week one, the Green Bay Packers face another top defense of the NFL as the New York Jets travel to historic Lambeau Field in week two. They face a Jets squad that dominated the lowly Oakland Raiders in week one. The Jets defense beat-up Oaklands rookie quarterback
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/C/CarrDe02.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Derek Carr
          </a>
         </strong>
         and only another multi-turnover performance by quarterback
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/S/SmitGe00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Geno Smith
          </a>
         </strong>
         kept the Raiders in the game.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Jets will find that
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/R/RodgAa00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Aaron Rodgers
          </a>
         </strong>
         is a little more of a challenge to their aggressive defense.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Seahawks spanked the Packers on Thursday Night Football to kick-off week one of the NFL season. Rodgers had already conceded half the field that game not even attempting a pass towards All-Pro cornerback
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/S/SherRi00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Richard Sherman
          </a>
         </strong>
         . That lack of aggressiveness showed and set the scene for the evening. Fortunately for Rodgers, the Jets have gone from having the top duo of cornerbacks two years ago with
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/R/ReviDa99.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Darrelle Revis
          </a>
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/C/CromAn20.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Antonio Cromartie
          </a>
         </strong>
         to a weak secondary that also faces injury issues in New York.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Jets invested a high first-round draft pick last season in cornerback
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/M/MillDe00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Dee Milliner
          </a>
         </strong>
         . Injuries early in his rookie season slowed Milliner, though he showed dramatic improvement in the last four games of the season against Oakland, Carolina, Cleveland, and Miami. Of course, last week saw Milliner sidelined to start 2014. Although expected to play Sunday,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/W/WallDa00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Darrin Walls
          </a>
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/A/AlleAn02.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Antonio Allen
          </a>
         </strong>
         , and slot cornerback
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/W/WilsKy99.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Kyle Wilson
          </a>
         </strong>
         should start and get the majority of the work against the Packers dangerous offense.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Jets strength on defense is their ultra-talented duo of young defensive linemen
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/R/RichSh00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Sheldon Richardson
          </a>
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/W/WilkMu00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Muhammad Wilkerson
          </a>
         </strong>
         . The duo combined to harass Oakland with 2 quarterback hits, 4 hurries, and 3 batted passes while tying up offensive linemen to let linebackers
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/P/PaceCa20.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Calvin Pace
          </a>
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/C/CoplQu00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Quinton Coples
          </a>
         </strong>
         , and newcomer
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/B/BabiJa20.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Jason Babin
          </a>
         </strong>
         clean up. With underrated run stuffing nose tackle
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/H/HarrDa03.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Damon Harrison
          </a>
         </strong>
         and inside linebackers
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/D/DaviDe00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Demario Davis
          </a>
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/H/HarrDa99.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           David Harris
          </a>
         </strong>
         slowing the running game, the Jets have been able to make do with the weak secondary to date.
        </p>
        <p>
         If the Packers can protect Rodgers they should be in good shape to put some points up on the board. Wide receivers
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/N/NelsJo00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Jordy Nelson
          </a>
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/C/CobbRa00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Randall Cobb
          </a>
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/B/BoykJa00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Jarrett Boykin
          </a>
         </strong>
         , and tight end
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/Q/QuarAn00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Andrew Quarless
          </a>
         </strong>
         have the talent to make big plays when they get the ball in space. Whether
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/L/LacyEd00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Eddie Lacy
          </a>
         </strong>
         or
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/S/StarJa00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           James Starks
          </a>
         </strong>
         is carrying the ball, the Packers are not planning to run the ball to beat New York.
        </p>
        <p>
         On defense, the Packers must be ecstatic not to see
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/L/LyncMa00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Marshawn Lynch
          </a>
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/H/HarvPe00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Percy Harvin
          </a>
         </strong>
         , and
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/W/WilsRu00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Russell Wilson
          </a>
         </strong>
         this week. Instead Geno Smith,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/D/DeckEr00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Eric Decker
          </a>
         </strong>
         , and
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/player_search.fcgi?search=Chris+Johnson&amp;utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Chris Johnson
          </a>
         </strong>
         should make for an easier matchup on Sunday. The Jets will rotate in quarterback
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/V/VickMi00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Michael Vick
          </a>
         </strong>
         for special packages, give running back
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/I/IvorCh00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Chris Ivory
          </a>
         </strong>
         a lot of playing time, and run out
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/player_search.fcgi?search=David+Nelson&amp;utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           David Nelson
          </a>
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/S/SalaGr00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Greg Salas
          </a>
         </strong>
         at wide receiver as well as tight ends
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/A/AmarJa00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Jace Amaro
          </a>
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/C/CumbJe00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Jeff Cumberland
          </a>
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         The Packers need their defense to first stop the Jets rushing attack and hope the offense put up points quickly to get the Jets defense on their heels. Forcing Smith to try to win the game in the air should allow the Packers to unleash outside linebackers
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/P/PeppJu99.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Julius Peppers
          </a>
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/player_search.fcgi?search=Clay+Matthews&amp;utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Clay Matthews
          </a>
         </strong>
         to put pressure on the Jets quarterback and force turnovers and easy points. With top cornerbacks
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/S/ShieSa00.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Sam Shields
          </a>
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.pro-football-reference.com/players/W/WillTr99.htm?utm_campaign=Linker&amp;utm_source=direct&amp;utm_medium=linker-firststopfantasy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Tramon Williams
          </a>
         </strong>
         neutralizing Decker, the defense should be able to ground the Jets on Sunday.
        </p>
