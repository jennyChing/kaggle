           good morning everyone! well although
           <b>
            <a class="text" href="http://americanidol.com/" target="_blank">
             american idol
            </a>

           runner-up
           <b>
            <a class="text" href="http://www.adamofficial.com/" target="_blank">

           <b>
            drake labry
           </b>
           (hes twenty-four years old and is an artist / interior designer) the couple was hanging at
           <b>
            guys and dolls
           </b>
           nightclub here in los angeles!
           <a class="text" href="http://www.nypost.com/seven/05292009/gossip/pagesix/coming_out_171520.htm" target="_blank">
            recent rumors are suggesting
           </a>
           mr. lambert might soon be coming out on the cover of
           <b>
            <a class="text" href="http://rollingstone.com/" target="_blank">
             rolling stone
            </a>
           </b>
           magazine  finally putting all the speculation to rest! im still feeling a little bitter adam didnt win idol this year but i know he has an amazing career ahead of him  i seriously cant wait for him to begin work on his debut album  i know its going to be a hot one! popbytes over &amp; out for now  xoxo
