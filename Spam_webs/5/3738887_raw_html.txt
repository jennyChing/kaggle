          </h1>

           <img alt="Saleslogix can also be accessed via other popular mobile devices, such as a tablet, shown here." class="inline-3" height="231" id="Asset_a695ae3ba26c4b2eda83cad026c03cbe" name="Asset_a695ae3ba26c4b2eda83cad026c03cbe" src="http://www.dmcsoftware.co.uk/Assets/GenericImages/SLXTablet.png?1409574418" width="300"/>
           Infor Mobile CRM version v3.2 extends
           <a href="http://www.dmcsoftware.co.uk/InforCRM/">
            Infor CRM
           </a>
           capabilities to smartphones and tablets, giving your mobile workforce instant access to vital customer information. With a user friendly application across multiple mobile platforms, this application is designed to improve efficiency and productivity. It empowers users, allowing them to respond quickly to new opportunities or customer issues, giving your businesses a competitive edge.
          </p>
          <h2>
           What's New?
          </h2>
          <p>
           The Infor Mobile CRM application allows users to access menu items that offer global, context, and quick actions, along with contextualised KPIs and charts, ensuring the information you view is most relevant. Infor Mobile also enables image, signature and attachment support for emails.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Mobile Only
          </h3>
          <p>
           Infor Mobile CRM is the first solution to offer mobile only CRM user access, which can help keep costs down, with mobile only users at a lower price point. Existing and new users can mix and match license types to suit their needs. Whats more, it uses a subscription pricing model, which enables you to add users when required.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Features and Benefits
          </h2>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Monitor Customers-
           </strong>
           Create, view, and edit key customer information, including accounts, contacts, leads, opportunities, and tickets. The system also offers contextualised KPIs and charts to ensure the information you view is relevant.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Organised Data-
           </strong>
           Mobile users can view and schedule activities, record important notes, and access detailed history records. Users can also log email and phone interactions to the notes and history sections while on the move, with image, signature and attachment support for emails.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Time Management-
           </strong>
           Easily manage meeting schedules, personal activities, phone calls, and to-do lists to ensure time is managed correctly and nothing is missed.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Continual Access-
           </strong>
           Configure Infor Mobile CRM to meet your individual business needs and working styles to maximise returns, with options for Mobile CRM users to work online or offline and receive updates automatically.
          </p>
