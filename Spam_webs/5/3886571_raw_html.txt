      <div class="entry-content aesop-entry-content" id="content">
       <p class="p1">
        <strong>
         Happy New Year guys! I hope youre not too bored already with all the end of year/new year -inspired posts that are being published all over the place, because heres another one.
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p class="p1">
        During the new years holiday Jempi and I hopped over to Dublin to check out the New Years Festival. Now we all know Dublin is already Irelands most vibrant city, but when you add a three days festival filled with fun activities, music, art and food the place makes you go double wow.
       </p>
       <p class="p1">
       </p>
       <div class="aesop-article-chapter-wrap default-cover aesop-component has-chapter-image aesop-chapter-full " id="chapter-unique-5607-1">
        <div class="aesop-article-chapter clearfix" style="background:url('http://travelboulevard.be/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dublinnyf15010.jpg');background-size:cover;background-position:center center;">
         <h2 class="aesop-cover-title" data-title="About the festival" itemprop="title">
          <span>
           About the festival
          </span>
          <small>
           New Year in Dublin
          </small>
         </h2>
        </div>
       </div>
       <p class="p1">
        New Years Festival in Dublin, Ireland, is brand new. In the past it used to be just New Years Eve but the Dubliners thought it would be fun to spice things up with a festival that runs across the city over three days (from 30 December 2014 straight to 1 January 2015), hence the New Years Festival (NYF). It was the biggest New Years celebration Dublins ever seen.
       </p>
       <p class="p1">
        The program was a mix of the best what Dublin has to offer packed in three days and its impossible to sum up all the activities, so here are just some of the highlights:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li class="p1">
         live gigs across the city at the
         <a href="http://nyfdublin.com/nyf-music-trail/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://nyfdublin.com/nyf-music-trail/', 'NYF Music Trail');" target="_blank">
          NYF Music Trail
         </a>
        </li>
        <li class="p1">
         a
         <a href="http://nyfdublin.com/procession-of-light/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://nyfdublin.com/procession-of-light/', 'Procession of Light');" target="_blank">
          Procession of Light
         </a>
        </li>
        <li class="p1">
         3D animation from
         <a href="http://nyfdublin.com/luminosity/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://nyfdublin.com/luminosity/', 'Luminosity');" target="_blank">
          Luminosity
         </a>
        </li>
        <li class="p1">
         the
         <a href="http://nyfdublin.com/category/multiple/love-dublin/spoken-word-festival/30th-spoken-word-festival/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://nyfdublin.com/category/multiple/love-dublin/spoken-word-festival/30th-spoken-word-festival/', ' Spoken Word Festival');" target="_blank">
          Spoken Word Festival
         </a>
        </li>
        <li class="p1">
         the
         <a href="http://nyfdublin.com/category/multiple/love-dublin/nyf-art-trail/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://nyfdublin.com/category/multiple/love-dublin/nyf-art-trail/', 'NYF Arts Trail');" target="_blank">
          NYF Arts Trail
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://nyfdublin.com/category/multiple/love-dublin/dublin-discovery-trail/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://nyfdublin.com/category/multiple/love-dublin/dublin-discovery-trail/', 'Dublin Discover Trail');" target="_blank">
          Dublin Discover Trail
         </a>
          an
         <a href="http://nyfdublin.com/nyf-food-village/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://nyfdublin.com/nyf-food-village/', 'NYF Food village');" target="_blank">
          NYF Food village
         </a>
         in the grounds of Dublin Castle
        </li>
        <li class="p1">
         the
         <a href="http://nyfdublin.com/nyf-countdown-concert/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://nyfdublin.com/nyf-countdown-concert/', 'NYF Countdown Concert');" target="_blank">
          NYF Countdown Concert
         </a>
         at College Green with the popular Irish band Kodaline (and special guest Vincent McMorrow)
        </li>
        <li class="p1">
         <a href="http://nyfdublin.com/resolution-day/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://nyfdublin.com/resolution-day/', 'Resolution day');" target="_blank">
          Resolution day
         </a>
         with NYF Fun Run
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p class="p1">
        All the ingredients for an unforgettable festival experience were present, but in the best thing  in our opinion  was that there was something for everyone: fun families activities, workshops for culture seekers, parties, concertsyou name it.
       </p>
       <h3 class="p1">
        <span style="color: #008080;">
         Flying to Dublin
        </span>
       </h3>
       <div class="aesop-component aesop-image-component " id="aesop-image-component-5607-1">
        <div class="aesop-content">
         <figure class="aesop-image-component-image aesop-component-align-left aesop-image-component-caption-left" style="max-width:500px;">
          <a class="aesop-lightbox" href="http://travelboulevard.be/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dublinnyf15001.jpg" title="">
           <p class="aesop-img-enlarge">
            <i class="aesopicon aesopicon-search-plus">
            </i>
            Enlarge
           </p>
           <img alt="dublin aerlingus" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/travelboulevard.be/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/dublinnyf15001.jpg?w=900"/>
          </a>
         </figure>
        </div>
       </div>
       <p class="p1">
        <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
         Flying from Brussels to Dublin takes only 1h45 minutes. We flew with
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.aerlingus.com/en-BE/home/index.jsp" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.aerlingus.com/en-BE/home/index.jsp', 'Aer Lingus');" style="line-height: 1.5;" target="_blank">
         Aer Lingus
        </a>
        <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
         , the national flag carrier of Ireland and were happy to arrive at
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.dublinairport.com/home.aspx" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.dublinairport.com/home.aspx', 'Dublin Airport');" style="line-height: 1.5;" target="_blank">
         Dublin Airport
        </a>
        <span style="line-height: 1.5;">
         on time (even though is was Tuesday 30th december, a busy day). A fun taxi ride later (for the record: the Irish taxi drivers are the best in the world. They welcome you as if you were their long-lost friend and always have something interesting to say) we checked in at the Morrison Hotel and around noon we were already all set to hit the streets of Dublin. Talk about a smooth trip.
        </span>
       </p>
       <h3 class="p1">
        <span style="color: #008080;">
         Our stay: the Morrison Hotel
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p class="p1">
        The next three days the
        <a href="http://www.morrisonhotel.ie/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.morrisonhotel.ie/', 'Morrison Hotel');" target="_blank">
         Morrison Hotel
        </a>
        was going to be our home and we couldnt be happier with that choice. The 4star hotel is located right in the heart of Dublin city, on the banks of the River Liffey, just a couple of footsteps away from all the places-to-be in the city.
       </p>

       <h3>

       <p class="p1">
        People were applauding and cheering and although we had so much fun it all made us feel a bit humble because, well,
        <span class="Apple-converted-space">
        </span>
        what had we ever done to deserve an honor like this? There we were, taking part in a magical event, waving a lantern, dancing and singing, feeling blessed to have met so many new friends from all over the world (check out fellow bloggers Pat &amp; Dave from
        <a href="http://hungrytravelers.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://hungrytravelers.com/', 'Hungry Travelers');" target="_blank">
         Hungry Travelers
        </a>
        !), and even when it started to rain and we arrived totally wed, cold and tired at Dublin Castle, we were still high on the event.
       </p>
       <h3 class="p1">
        The party &amp; the countdown concert
       </h3>
       <p class="p1">
        Time to party! We gathered at the
        <a href="http://www.discoverireland.ie/Activities-Adventure/dublin-discover-ireland-centre-suffolk-street/73794" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.discoverireland.ie/Activities-Adventure/dublin-discover-ireland-centre-suffolk-street/73794', 'Discover Ireland Center');" target="_blank">
         Discover Ireland Center
        </a>
        in Andrew St Church on Suffolk Street, just a couple of steps away from College Green where the big Countdown Concert  with headliner
        <a href="http://www.kodaline.com/home" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.kodaline.com/home', 'Kodaline');" target="_blank">
         Kodaline
        </a>
         took place. It rained for about half of the concert, but it looked like not one of the 12.000 people attending the concert seemed to mind: there was some serious sing along going on. But I have to be honest: Ive missed most of the concert since it was just so much fun just hanging around in the Discover Ireland Center. I mean: there was a photo boot, so whats not to like?
       </p>

       <p class="p1">
        A couple of minutes before midnight we decided to join the crowd at the concert for the actual countdown. We headed straight to the VIP-area and maybe that was a little mistake. Yes, there were drinks and all, but absolutely no view at the stage because the area was just a bit too close to the side. But no complaints here: the atmosphere was fantastic and all the new years kisses we got at the stroke of 12 were fab as well.

       <h3 class="p1">
        <span style="color: #008080;">
         New Years Day
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p class="p1">
        Our plan was to kick-start 2015 by taking part in the 2,5 km
        <a href="http://nyfdublin.com/nyf-fun-run/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://nyfdublin.com/nyf-fun-run/', 'NYF Fun Run');" target="_blank">
         NYF Fun Run
        </a>
        . Did we? No, of course we didnt. We
        <em>
         couldnt.
        </em>
        Too many Guinness the night before, too many miles of walking around the city, too many impressionswe were beat! We desperately needed to recover from the day(s) before so we swapped the run for a really (really!) long brunch.
       </p>
       <div class="aesop-component aesop-image-component " id="aesop-image-component-5607-9">

       <p class="p1">
        In the afternoon we felt energized and explored Dublin on our own, doing touristic things like taking Hop-on Hop-off bus en just wandered around the city. Still, there was one more event to attend and we had no idea what to expect: Resolution in
        <a href="http://christchurchcathedral.ie/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://christchurchcathedral.ie/', 'Christchurch Cathedral');" target="_blank">
         Christchurch Cathedral
        </a>
        , presented by the
        <a href="http://www.thetrailblazery.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.thetrailblazery.com/', 'Trailblazery');" target="_blank">
         Trailblazery
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://firstfortnight.ie/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://firstfortnight.ie/', 'First Fortnight ');" target="_blank">
         First Fortnight
        </a>
        and NYF Dublin. Not sure how to explain this on: lets say it was a venue with lots of music (an amazing choir!) and people sharing their personal journey about challenges, courage and compassion. All in all a perfect way to reflect on the past and looking forward to new things to come.
       </p>
       <h3 class="p1">
        <span style="color: #008080;">
         See you next year (or sooner)?
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p class="p1">
        So, here you have it: this is how New Years Festival in Dublin looked like. I can honestly say it was the best NY we ever had, maybe were biased because we simply fell in love with the city and its people, but: Dublin just does it better than any other city. If you havent made any resolutions for 2015, let us inspire you and plan your next end-of-year-trip to Dublin, it may be one of the best places to celebrate the new year. Were already counting down to the next NYF. So, how about it, will we see you in Dublin next New Years Eve?
       </p>
