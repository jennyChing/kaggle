twine, and about 30 minutes. Ready? Gather: HP Officejet Pro 8600 AiO
Printer + Original HP Ink HP Premium Inkjet Matte Presentation Paper
Scissors Tape Twine Directions: Step 1 Print out my Halloween

of your silhouettes, tape on the front and back of the paper and cut out
the shape. Step 3 After all of your items are cut out, it's time to put
them up in the window. I chose the window right at our front door so
that the trick-or-treaters are sure to see it. Use the clear tape to put
them up in whatever arrangement you like best. Step 4 To make it all
seem a little more &quot;real,&quot; I added strands of twine to each silhouette
to make them look like they are hanging. That's it! You are done. Now it

                                 <div class="lia-message-body-content">
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="middle" alt="Halloween Window Silhouette" border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/7117i171455550CBEF2DE/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="Halloween Window Silhouette"/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Halloween is around the cornerhave you put up your decorations? No? What?! It's ok, don't worry... I'm here to help. I'd like to share a very quick and easy DIY to create some creepy silhouettes for your window. What you will need: a printer, 15 sheets of paper, clear tape, twine, and about 30 minutes. Ready?
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Gather:
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <ul>
                                   <li>
                                    <a href="http://www.shopping.hp.com/en_US/home-office/-/products/Printers/HP-Officejet-Pro/CM749A?jumpid=SC_R11513_Li_HPCreate_officejet-rpo" rel="nofollow" style="font-size: 14px;" target="_blank">
                                     HP Officejet Pro 8600 AiO Printer
                                    </a>
                                    <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 16px;">
                                     +
                                    </span>
                                    <a href="http://shopping.hp.com/en_US/home-office/-/products/Ink_Toner_Paper/Ink_Toner_Paper?jumpid=sc_R11513_Li_HPCreate_Ink_Toner_Paper" rel="nofollow" style="font-size: 14px;" target="_blank">
                                     Original HP Ink
                                    </a>
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    <a href="http://www.shopping.hp.com/en_US/home-office/-/products/HP-Paper/Brochure-and-Presentation-paper/D0Z55A?HP-Premium-Inkjet-Matte-Presentation-Paper-120-gsm-100-sht-Letter-8.5-x-11-in&amp;jumpid=sc_R11513_Li_HPCreate_Premium_Inkjet_Matte_Presentation_Paper" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
                                     HP Premium Inkjet Matte Presentation Paper
                                    </a>
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Scissors
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Tape
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Twine
                                   </li>
                                  </ul>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    <em>
                                     Directions:
                                    </em>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    <img align="middle" alt="Step 1" border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/7119i4799219063CFCACD/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="Step 1"/>
                                    <br/>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 1
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Print out my Halloween Silhouettes pack here
                                   <em>
                                    (see below).
                                   </em>
                                   Each silhouette is printed on 2 to 3 sheets of paper.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    <div class="video-embed-center video-embed-youtube">
                                     <iframe allowfullscreen="true" frameborder="0" height="557" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/MfD4BaZrvVY" width="625">
                                     </iframe>
                                    </div>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 2
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   To assemble the silhouettes, line up the edges of your silhouettes, tape on the front and back of the paper and cut out the shape.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="middle" alt="Step 3" border="0" height="985" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/7121i883E8B78ADDF1BA9/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="Step 3" width="627"/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 3
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   After all of your items are cut out, it's time to put them up in the window. I chose the window right at our front door so that the trick-or-treaters are sure to see it. Use the clear tape to put them up in whatever arrangement you like best.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="middle" alt="Step 4" border="0" height="687" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/7123iD2FBC716DDD11A40/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="Step 4" width="629"/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 4
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   To make it all seem a little more "real," I added strands of twine to each silhouette to make them look like they are hanging.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   That's it! You are done.Now it looks like I actually have a cleaver and axe hanging in my home.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>

                                    Idea Exchange
                                   </a>
