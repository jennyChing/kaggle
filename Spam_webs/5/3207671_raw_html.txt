        <p>
         New Delhi, India, January 10, 2014: Spectranet and BoxTV have exclusively tied up to offer free movies to all the customers of Spectranet Broadband powered by FOX (Fiber Optic Experience) for watching movies, shows and more on BoxTV.com.
        </p>
        <p>
         Customers of Spectranet Broadband powered by FOX will get a full month of BoxTVs premium service absolutely free and on confirmation of subscription will be billed directly by Spectranet as part of their monthly plan. This will enable them to sign up for the premium service without any hassles of providing credit card or any other payment information.
        </p>
        <p>
         In addition to this, customers of Spectranet Broadband powered by FOX will be pre-registered for the offer and can avail the same using their existing Spectranet login credentials.
        </p>
        <p>
         In this offer, customers of Spectranet Broadband powered by FOX will get extra 8GB data volume bundled free of cost with their monthly BoxTV subscription plan purchased through Spectranet. Customers will be able to watch as many as 10 movies or shows in great quality every month without consuming their monthly plan volume. Post which the customers can continue watching BoxTV on their regular data plan. Spectranet Broadband powered by FOX offers amazing internet experience with download speeds of 12 to 16Mbps and lets its customers carry forward their unused data volume to the next billing cycles.
        </p>
        <p>
         Business Head BoxTV.com Pandurang Nayak said, We are very glad to announce this offer which is a great step ahead for Indian Internet consumers. Spectranet is a one-of-its-kind ISP in India offering great value plans for customers with really high speeds and high data volumes at very affordable pricing. Given their customer-centric focus, we were very happy to enter into this partnership to offer their customers a great digital entertainment offering without pinching either their data or pocket.
        </p>
        <p>
         On behalf of Spectranet, Rajat arora, Chief Commercial Officer, Spectranet said  BoxTV.com has been instrumental in leading the premium video streaming space in India with great quality Hollywood, Bollywood and regional content. We already see that reflecting in great demand for the service among our existing customers. We are adding convenience to our commitment of continuously enhancing the lives of our consumers by delivering the most amazing internet experience.
        </p>
