               <img alt="Face Technical Interview 4.jpg" height="426" src="http://pad1.whstatic.com/images/b/b9/Face-Technical-Interview-4.jpg" width="629"/>
              </noscript>
             </div>
             <div class="step_num">
              4
             </div>
             <div class="step">
              <b class="whb">
               Look professional.
              </b>
              Dress is another important aspect for facing interview. Always look professional and smart as your first impression is going to make a way for you.
             </div>
             <div class="clearall">
             </div>
            </li>
            <li>
             <div class="mwimg largeimage underwidth floatcenter " style="max-width:629px">
              <a class="image lightbox" data-href="/Image:Face-Technical-Interview-5.jpg?ajax=true" href="#/Image:Face-Technical-Interview-5.jpg">
               <img alt="Face Technical Interview 5.jpg" data-src="http://pad2.whstatic.com/images/3/32/Face-Technical-Interview-5.jpg" height="426" id="55a424c2a344a" src="" width="629"/>

               </script>
              </a>
              <noscript>
               <img alt="Face Technical Interview 5.jpg" height="426" src="http://pad2.whstatic.com/images/3/32/Face-Technical-Interview-5.jpg" width="629"/>
              </noscript>
             </div>
             <div class="step_num">
              5
             </div>
             <div class="step">
              <b class="whb">
               Ask questions.
              </b>
              Whatever queries you have in your mind, ask directly so that you can decide if this is the right company for you. Also, it shows that you are interested.
             </div>
             <div class="clearall">
             </div>
            </li>
            <li class="final_li">
             <div class="mwimg largeimage underwidth floatcenter " style="max-width:629px">

&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="step_num"&gt;6&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div class="step"&gt;
&lt;b class="whb"&gt;Dont be talkative.&lt;/b&gt; Make sure you are giving the required information only. Dont talk extra as it can give you minus points. Remember, interview is a professional meeting.
