       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Upcycle a Glass Bottle Into a Lamp
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/author/savedbylovecreations" rel="author">
           Johnnie Collier
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <center>
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <!-- Mid_Post_1_ATF_336x280 -->
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-8307284746414467" data-ad-slot="8817930017" style="display:inline-block;width:336px;height:280px">
         </ins>
         <script>
          (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});

        </center>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper-shortcode">
         <a data-pin-color="gray" data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fsavedbylovecreations.com%2F2014%2F03%2Fupcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fsavedbylovecreations.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F03%2FHow-to-make-a-lamp-from-a-bottle2.png&amp;description=How%20to%20make%20a%20lamp%20from%20a%20bottle%20%40savedbyloves%20with%20%40modpodgerocks%20adhesive%20stencils%20%40plaidcrafts" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-make-a-lamp-from-a-bottle2.png">
          <img alt="How to make a lamp from a bottle @savedbyloves with @modpodgerocks adhesive stencils @plaidcrafts" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-make-a-lamp-from-a-bottle2.png" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="559" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-make-a-lamp-from-a-bottle2.png" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper-shortcode">
         <a data-pin-color="gray" data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fsavedbylovecreations.com%2F2014%2F03%2Fupcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fsavedbylovecreations.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F03%2FHow-to-make-a-lamp-from-a-bottle2.png&amp;description=How%20to%20make%20a%20lamp%20from%20a%20bottle%20%40savedbyloves%20with%20%40modpodgerocks%20adhesive%20stencils%20%40plaidcrafts" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         If you have ever thought it would be too hard to turn a bottle into a lamp, I am here to change your mind about that. Not just any lamp, mind you, but a sparkly, polka-dot spangled, fabulous lamp thanks to
         <a href="http://www.plaidonline.com/mod-podge-rocks/brand/detail.htm">
          Mod Podge Rocks adhesive stencils
         </a>
         from Amy Anderson and Plaid Crafts! Read on to see how you can make your very own in less than an hour. It is true.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         SUPPLIES:
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Mod-Podge-Rocks-Adhesive-Stencils-@plaidcrafts.png">
          <img alt="Mod Podge Rocks Adhesive Stencils @plaidcrafts" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12937" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Mod-Podge-Rocks-Adhesive-Stencils-@plaidcrafts.png" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="421" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Mod-Podge-Rocks-Adhesive-Stencils-@plaidcrafts.png" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Glass Bottle
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.plaidonline.com/mod-podge-rocks-peel-stick-stencils-dot-pattern/188/25089/product.htm">
          Mod Podge Rocks Adhesive Stencils (Polka Dot Pattern)
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Podge-CS11202-Original-16-Ounce-Finish/dp/B00178QQJ8?psc=1&amp;SubscriptionId=AKIAJ63VQUTYHKYRPBVA&amp;tag=saved0a-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="">
          Mod Podge Gloss Finish
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Foam Spouncer
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Podgeable Glitters
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/GE-Bottle-Lamp-White-50961/dp/B002DNK0SY?SubscriptionId=AKIAJ63VQUTYHKYRPBVA&amp;tag=saved0a-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="">
          GE Bottle Lamp Kit
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Lamp Shade
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Light Bulb
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Drill and
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Diamond-Tipped-Metal-Drill-Ceramic/dp/B00880CFJS?psc=1&amp;SubscriptionId=AKIAJ63VQUTYHKYRPBVA&amp;tag=saved0a-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="">
          1/2 inch Diamond Hole Drill Bit
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Lint free towel and rubbing alcohol
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Dust mask
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Protective Gloves
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Protective Eyewear
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Container of water with a a soaked towel or rag
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         TUTORIAL:
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Drill-A-Hole-In-a-Glass-Bottle.jpg">
          <img alt="How to Drill A Hole In a Glass Bottle" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12930" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Drill-A-Hole-In-a-Glass-Bottle.jpg" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="497" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Drill-A-Hole-In-a-Glass-Bottle.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Wearing gloves, mask and protective eyewear, drill a hole in the bottle where you want the cord to exit your finished lamp (in the back near the bottom). You will be using a 1/2 inch hole saw diamond bit.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Come in at an angle to start the notch in the glass, then you will be able to come in at a 90 degree angle to continue drilling.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Drill-in-Glass-Bottle.jpg">
          <img alt="How to Drill in Glass Bottle" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12932" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Drill-in-Glass-Bottle.jpg" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="465" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Drill-in-Glass-Bottle.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Here I am drilling at 90. You can see the dust from the glass being drilled. About every 2o seconds of drilling, take the rag from the water and drip it onto the drilled area and the drill bit to cool things down and get rid of the glass dust.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         * Some people do the entire thing in water, but I have found that to be inconvenient to pull off with just one person.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         *Dont press on the drill. Let the drill bit do the work or you risk breaking the glass. Dont forget to cool things off with water. Again, the glass will break if things get to hot.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Drill-hole-in-a-Glass-Bottle.jpg">
          <img alt="How to Drill hole in a Glass Bottle" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12931" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Drill-hole-in-a-Glass-Bottle.jpg" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="399" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Drill-hole-in-a-Glass-Bottle.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Here you can see the hole is complete, and the glass circle cut out is now inside the bottle (on the left). Clean away the debris.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Stenciling-on-Glass.png">
          <img alt="Stenciling on Glass" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12939" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Stenciling-on-Glass.png" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="518" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Stenciling-on-Glass.png" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Clean the glass well with rubbing alcohol and a lint free towel before stenciling. Once it is dry, place stencil and apply Mod Podge Gloss generously with a foam spouncer.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Paint-Glass.png">
          <img alt="How to Paint Glass" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12936" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Paint-Glass.png" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="399" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-Paint-Glass.png" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Apply Podgeable Glitter immediately after applying Mod Podge. Remove stencil and let dry!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Bottle-Lamp-Kit-Tutorial.png">
          <img alt="Bottle Lamp Kit Tutorial" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12927" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Bottle-Lamp-Kit-Tutorial.png" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="382" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Bottle-Lamp-Kit-Tutorial.png" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I picked up this kit at Walmart for I think $6.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-make-a-bottle-lamp.png">
          <img alt="How to make a bottle lamp" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12933" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-make-a-bottle-lamp.png" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="484" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-make-a-bottle-lamp.png" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         It comes with 3 different sized adapters for various bottle opening sizes. It turned out that all of them were to small for my bottle, so I dug through my stash and found a big cork that fit perfectly.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Drill-a-hole-in-bottle-cork.png">
          <img alt="Drill a hole in bottle cork" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12928" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Drill-a-hole-in-bottle-cork.png" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="482" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Drill-a-hole-in-bottle-cork.png" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I found a drill bit just smaller than the threaded rod I needed to insert in the cork and drilled a hole for the rod.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-make-a-lamp.png">
          <img alt="How to make a lamp" class="aligncenter wp-image-12935" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-make-a-lamp.png" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="595" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/How-to-make-a-lamp.png" width="571"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I inserted the rod and assembled the lamp according to the instructions, running the wire through the hole drilled in the back of the bottle.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Upcycle-Glass-Bottles.png">
          <img alt="Upcycle Glass Bottles" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12941" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Upcycle-Glass-Bottles.png" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="405" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Upcycle-Glass-Bottles.png" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I found a burlap lampshade at Walmart as well. For this kit, you need one attaches at the base of the bulb (the lamp kit doesnt come with a harp attachment). Mine was $8. I added a burlap flower to finish, and I am in love with my new lamp.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Recycled-Bottled-Art.png">
          <img alt="Recycled Bottled Art" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12943" data-pin-media="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Recycled-Bottled-Art.png" data-pin-url="http://savedbylovecreations.com/2014/03/upcycle-a-glass-bottle-into-a-lamp.html" height="632" src="http://savedbylovecreations.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Recycled-Bottled-Art.png" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         These stencils are so easy to use! They give great lines and come in 6 hip designs. Head to Michaels to grab your own, or visit
         <a href="http://www.plaidonline.com/mod-podge-rocks-peel-stick-stencils-dot-pattern/188/25089/product.htm">
          Plaid Online
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.plaidonline.com/mod-podge-rocks/brand/projects.htm">
          Here are more awesome projects using Mod Podge Rock Stencils
         </a>
         . Enjoy!!
        </p>
