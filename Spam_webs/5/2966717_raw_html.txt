       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         When you own luxury property in Miami, you are not part of an elite group. In fact, there are thousands of home and property owners just like you on
         <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4XaU0T1A_Cg">
          South Beach and in downtown Miami
         </a>
         . This means that there are also a number of well-equipped and knowledgeable luxury real estate agents in the area. These individuals can help ensure the selling of your luxury property is seamless and meet the unique challenges that this type of sale entails.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Experience of the Agent
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         When selling this type of top real estate, no matter where in Miami you are located, it is a good idea to work with someone experienced in the field, rather than someone new in the industry. This is because there are a number of unique challenges associated with marketing, pricing and dealing with those who are interested in purchasing this type of property. The fact is that this is not something you should allow a newbie to handle for you.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Custom Design Specifications
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the most unique challenges you will face when selling luxury property is that a luxury home is not a tract home. They are not ones that are mass-produced by one, single developer. Most of the homes in this part of the market in Miami are custom built and designed by architects. Additionally, if you are selling in the Art Deco Historic district in Miami, you will find even more challenges, since the design and structure is even more unique, making the pricing that much more difficult to determine.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Pricing Considerations
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         When pricing luxury property, you have to consider the actual structure, as well as a number of intangibles, such as access or views of the beach, privacy and the history of the property. In most cases, these factors will impact the price. Also make sure that the agent you use actually visits the property. You cannot really get a feel for any luxury property without seeing it in person.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Marketing the Luxury Real Estate
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Once a price is determined, you are still faced with the unique challenges of marketing the property to those who may be interested. This means that you need to
         <a href="http://sterlingluxurygroup.com/miami-luxury-real-estate-foreign-buyers-dubai-uk-russia-purchasing-miami-beachs-oceanfront-porsche-design-tower-condominiums/">
          find a luxury real estate agent
         </a>
         with a large index of contacts. This includes past celebrity clients, attorneys, other luxury agents and even high end public relations. It is important they have these relationships so that your Miami luxury property is marketed to the right group of people.
        </p>
        <p>
         When you are looking for a luxury real estate company in Miami Beach, FL it is essential that you take the time to find one with the proper experience to get the job done properly. Luxury real estate is much different than traditional real estate, which is why it requires that you hire a special type of agent. If you fail to put in the time and effort, then you may wind up accepting much less than your Miami Beach, ocean front condo is really worth.
        </p>
