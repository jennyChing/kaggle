         <p>
          Have you noticed how many makeup brushes are needed by makeup artists and beauty gurus, before they achieve the final flawless look we all want to see? Are you wondering why? The answer is very simple  the brushes help create a flawless finish to your makeup. Makeup brushes are indispensable weapon for any lady who is interested to get the best out of makeup! With their help, you can achieve better coverage and create a wide range of different looks just let your imagination be your guide!
         </p>
         <p>
          There are many different types of brushes and each perform a different task. Makeup brushes can be made of synthetic or real animal hair. Foundation brushes can be one of two shapes, they can be flat or round shaped. With a flat brush it is much easier to get used to appling foundataion, especially if you are a beginner.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMG_1955.jpg">
           <img alt="IMG_1955" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-175" height="225" src="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMG_1955-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Round fluffy brushes are also very easy to use and they gives better results. I really like my Real techniques stippling brush for that.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMG_1957.jpg">
           <img alt="IMG_1957" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-176" height="225" src="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMG_1957-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Yes, it does the job very well!
         </p>
         <p>
          Before I started using foundation brushes, I always applied foundation with fingers but in my opinion using a brush is the best way to achieve an ideal finish.
         </p>
         <p>
          Concealer brushes are usually of the same structure but a lot more smaller. They are used to cover smaller areas, such as spots and dark circles.
         </p>
         <p>
          Very important are also contour brushes since if you really want to look like a cover girl you need contour! So do not overlook contour bruses, you can find many video tutorials and advices on how to apply contour according to your face shape and this will make you look very different and stunning, trust me
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Powder brushes have longer bristles, they have an oval shape and depends on your personal preferences you can choose a long or short handle.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMG_1959.jpg">
           <img alt="IMG_1959" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-177" height="225" src="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMG_1959-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Blusher brushes are usually with the same shape but they are smaller, because they need to cover smaller sections of the face.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMG_1963.jpg">
           <img alt="IMG_1963" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-178" height="225" src="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMG_1963-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          If you want to use a highlighter there is a special brush you can use just for that. Apply small amount to the cheekbone,in the center of your forehead and in the middle of your chin. The special brushes for highlighter look like that:
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMG_1961.jpg">
           <img alt="IMG_1961" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-179" height="225" src="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMG_1961-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          And of course last but not least are the makeup brushes for the eyes! There is a huge variety of eye makeup brushes, because this is the area that make up is applied most, starting with the eyebrow brush, helping perfect the shape of your brows.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="result_box" lang="en">
           <span class="hps">
            For applying
           </span>
           <span class="hps">
            eye shadow
           </span>
           <span class="hps">
            brushes
           </span>
           <span class="hps">
            diversity
           </span>
           <span class="hps">
            is most important. There are various eye shadow brushes for applying makeup in different zones of the eye. These are:
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="result_box" lang="en">
           <span class="hps">
            -pencil brush- helps to apply eyeshadow to the top or bottom lash lines, to highlighting inner corner of the eyes or softening pencil lines and it helps create smoky eyes.
            <br/>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          -blending brush- this is a small fluffy brush with an oval shape which helps you shade and blend the product so you can avoid abrupt boundaries. This brush is very important in the process of creating smoky eyes.
         </p>
         <p>
          -eye liner brush- precision is important when applying eye liner.
         </p>
         <p>
          -brush for lashes-
          <span id="result_box" lang="en" tabindex="-1">
           <span class="hps">
            used
           </span>
           <span class="hps">
            to avoid
           </span>
           <span class="hps">
            compaction
           </span>
           <span class="hps">
            of
           </span>
           <span class="hps">
            mascara
           </span>
           <span class="hps">
            by separating
           </span>
           <span class="hps">
            each
           </span>
           <span class="hps">
            lash.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          There are also many many other eye shadow brushes but these are the most important one if you are looking to achieve the best results with your make up and deffinitely worth the investment.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.beautylovesme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/IMG_1965.jpg">

There are many ways to clean brushes- you can purchase a special cleaner spray for makeup brushes. Many cosmetic brands offer this product now. There is another way for that, which I use very often at home. You can simply wash your brushes with soft shampoo and a few drops of extra virgin olive oil.
