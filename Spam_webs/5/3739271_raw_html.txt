         <span class="post-meta-separator">
          
         </span>
         <a class="post-date updated" href="http://www.cxmagazine.com/team-gates-carbon-drive-showcases-singlespeed-carbon-drive-bikes-spot" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Team Gates Carbon Drive Showcases New Singlespeed Carbon Drive Bikes from Spot">
          October 2, 2013
         </a>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="post-content clearfix">
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_47276" style="width: 600px">
         <a href="http://cx.cxmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Rallye-Complete-yellow-line-1024x681.jpg">
          <img alt="The new team bike for the Gates Carbon Drive team: the Spot Rallye. " class="size-medium wp-image-47276" height="392" src="http://cx.cxmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Rallye-Complete-yellow-line-1024x681-590x392.jpg" width="590"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          The new team bike for the Gates Carbon Drive team: the Spot Rallye.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Singlespeeders, start your engines. Since the
         <a href="http://www.cxmagazine.com/product-spotlight-centertrack-carbon-belt-drive-gates" title="New Product Spotlight: CenterTrack Carbon Belt Drive from Gates">
          Gates Carbon Drive
         </a>
         made its way onto the cyclocross scene a couple of years ago, the brand has formed a team and pushed the limits of singlespeed racing on new bikes each year. For 2013, theyve introduced the new Team Gates Carbon Drive cyclocross bike, the Rallye from Spot Brand.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_47274" style="width: 600px">
         <a href="http://cx.cxmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Rallye-brake-levers-745x1024.jpg">
          <img alt="TRP's Hylex shifters are designed for singlespeeding. " class="size-medium wp-image-47274" height="810" src="http://cx.cxmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Rallye-brake-levers-745x1024-590x810.jpg" width="590"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          TRPs Hylex shifters are designed for singlespeeding.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Based in Colorado, Team Gates Carbon Drive launches into its fourth cross season with plans to once again belt the competition and dominate the singlespeed race category in the Rocky Mountains and beyond. The goal: be ambassadors for SS cross and spread the gospel of belt drive. The team roster includes Cristina Begy, Brian Hutchison, Taylor Jung, Ryan McFarling, Taylor Nye, Jesse Swift, Derek Strong and Mitch Westall.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_47273" style="width: 600px">
         <a href="http://cx.cxmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Rallye_front-sprocket-1024x681.jpg">
          <img alt="Of course, the bike is carbon-drive-equipped. " class="size-medium wp-image-47273" height="392" src="http://cx.cxmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Rallye_front-sprocket-1024x681-590x392.jpg" width="590"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Of course, the bike is carbon-drive-equipped.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         The team bike is equipped with Stans Iron Cross Pro wheels,
