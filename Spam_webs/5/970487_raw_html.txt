        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Hamburger Quiche
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-12-23T00:00:19+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          12/23/2013
         </time>
         Posted by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
           Kelly Rowe
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         What dishes come to mind when you hear the words comfort food? Pizza? Hamburgers? Pasta? Your favorite dessert? A lot of my favorite dishes come to mind and quiche is towards the top. My mom made lots of quiches when we were growing up  and, well, those recipes have to stay in the family! I have quite a few and I cant seem to keep track of what ones Ive made or not  I mean, theyre all dee-lish. Recently, I partnered with Kraft and made this Hamburger Quiche and apparently I hadnt mad this one yet. My husband flipped! He took leftovers to work the next day and text me,
         <em>
          I could eat quiche every day!
         </em>
         Ha! Of course I was glowing. We always like to hear that our meals are family approved. So, today Im sharing this family favorite that Ive been enjoying for years: Hamburger Quiche.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="A family favorite!  The best Hamburger Quiche with livelaughrowe.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18817" height="654" src="http://www.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/A-family-favorite-The-best-Hamburger-Quiche-with-livelaughrowe.com_.jpg" width="525"/>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <link href="http://www.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/A-family-favorite-The-best-Hamburger-Quiche-with-livelaughrowe.com_.jpg" itemprop="image"/>
         <table border="0" class="ERHDTable">
          <tr>
           <td>
            <span class="item ERName" itemprop="name">
             Hamburger Quiche
            </span>
           </td>
           <td align="center" valign="top">
           </td>
           <td class="ERHDPrint" valign="top">
            <a href="http://livelaughrowe.com/easyrecipe-print/18827-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             <div class="btnERPrint">
              Print
             </div>
            </a>
           </td>
          </tr>
         </table>
         <div class="ERClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERHead">
          Serves:
          <span itemprop="recipeYield">
           6 Servings
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="ERIngredients">
          <div class="ERIngredientsHeader ERHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul class="ingredients">
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1- 9" Pie Shell
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             lb. Ground Beef
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup Miracle Whip Dressing
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup Whole Milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tbsp Parsley
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 Eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1-1/2 cups Kraft Sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup chopped onion
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Salt and Papper, to taste
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <div class="ERInstructions">
          <div class="ERInstructionsHeader ERHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol class="instructions">
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Brown meat and pour off excess fat.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add Miracle Whip, whole milk, parsley and eggs.  Mix until smooth.
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Ingredients-for-Hamburger-Quiche.jpg"/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add grated cheese, onion and salt/pepper.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Pour into pie shell.
            <br/>
            <img src="http://www.livelaughrowe.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Yummy-Hamburger-Quiche-ready-for-the-oven.jpg"/>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake at 350 for 45 minutes (or until knife inserted comes out clean)
           </li>
          </ol>
         </div>
         <div class="endeasyrecipe" style="display: none" title="style000">
          3.3.3077
         </div>
        </div>
