                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Waiting Rooms Should Contain Classic Literature Not Lame Entertainment Magazines
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-1522038104695456684" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      We've all had the experience of sitting in some waiting room while we anticipate our turn to have our transactions. It can get very boring so we often try to find something to do to pass the time. Most waiting rooms have either newspapers or magazines to help keep us waitees from losing our minds. The problem is that these magazines are often crap.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      It would be much better if waiting rooms have great literature books instead of crappy magazines. Just imagine walking into a dentist's office and seeing Edgar Allan Poe or George Orwell titles stacked in the waiting room's center table. Now that would be magnificent. For example, the automotive shop pictured below has Leo Tolstoy's
                      <i>
                       War and Peace
                      </i>
                      . As a book lover, I wouldn't mind waiting for a long time as my car gets fixed if waiting means reading one of the greatest books ever penned.
                     </div>
