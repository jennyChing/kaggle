                   Last month, developers presented plans for a duplex with off-street parking at
                   <a href="https://www.google.com/maps/place/439+Belgrade+St,+Philadelphia,+PA+19125/data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x89c6c84022752b43:0x62e272e5860a7b20?sa=X&amp;ved=0CB0Q8gEwAGoVChMI66GnxoHKxgIVDD4-Ch1a8A8d">
