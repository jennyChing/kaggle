            <p>
             You guys. Today Im sharing my first video (I guess the word would be vlog?). Im kinda nervous, but its a super fun and festive DIYand a video just made more sense than photos (though Ive included a few photos below too). Check out the video below, which included a step-by-step tutorial on creating your own decorative holiday cones (mini trees, as I like to call them). Perfect for a centerpiece for Christmas dinner (or about a thousand other uses!). Happy DIYing, yall!
