      <p class="intro">
       Finally the count down is over  the most anticipated Guinness World Records 2015 edition is out now. The book released on Wednesday, September 10th encompass many amusingly strange and crazy world records.
      </p>
      <p>
       This edition is loaded with unique and hilarious records, ranging from the tallest teenager to the shortest cat  it covers everything even the largest Yo Yo and the longest golf club.
      </p>
      <p>
       The edition comprise a few fresh faces, whereas some veteran champions are also there. Watch all of them in their quirky, world-dominating glory in the gallery below.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Largest Disco Ball  Bestival
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records" class="aligncenter wp-image-39082" height="312" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records.jpg" width="596"/>
       </a>
       Its a gigantic disco ball that measures
       <strong>
        10.33 meters (about 33 feet) in diameter
       </strong>
       . Engineers wearing space-suits pulled together to hang this giant twinkling sphere above the U.K. music festival.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Longest Tongue  Nick Stoeberl
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records2.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records2" class="aligncenter wp-image-39083" height="312" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records2.jpg" width="596"/>
       </a>
       Where a normal human tongue measures just 8.5cm (3.3in)  Stoeberls tongue measures 10.1cm (3.97 inches) from the tip to the middle of the closed lip. This comedian from California is rightly nicknamed The Lick.
      </p>

      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Longest Jump by a Cat  Alley
      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: center">
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records3.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records3" class="aligncenter wp-image-39084" height="310" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records3.jpg" width="592"/>
       </a>
       The Super cat from Austin, Texas has the skill to jump up to 6 feet high.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       First Musical Act to Perform a Concert on Every Continent  Metallica
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records4.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records4" class="aligncenter wp-image-39085" height="312" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records4.jpg" width="596"/>
       </a>
       The metal group travelled to Carlini Station in Antarctica to entertain 120 scientists and competition winners. They performed 10 tracks to set a record in December 2013.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Largest Yo-Yo  Beth Johnson
      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: center">
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records5.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records5" class="aligncenter wp-image-39086" height="312" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records5.jpg" width="596"/>
       </a>
       A giant toy it measures 11 feet, 9 inches and weighs a whopping 4,620 pounds. It took just a year and a half to build, and is fully functional.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Most Apples Crushed With the Biceps in One Minute  Linsey Lindberg
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records6.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records6" class="aligncenter wp-image-39087" height="787" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records6.jpg" width="595"/>
       </a>
       Lindberg  nicknamed Mama Lou has the most craziest world record to his name. As per Guinness, she squashed eight apples in Los Angeles, California on June 26, 2013.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Shortest Cat  Lilleput
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records7.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records7" class="aligncenter wp-image-39088" height="312" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records7.jpg" width="596"/>
       </a>
       This nine-year-old munchkin cat measures just .25 inches from the floor to the shoulders. Shes rightly the tiniest cat in the world  who lives in Napa, California with her owner Christel Young.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Largest Collection of Comic Books  Bob Bretall
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records8.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records8" class="aligncenter wp-image-39089" height="311" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records8.jpg" width="594"/>
       </a>
       Bretell a collector of books has so far collected 94,268 comic books  hes been doing this since he was 8 years old. His collection almost weighs up to 8.3 tons.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Largest Collection of James Bond Memorabilia  Nick Bennett
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records9.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records9" class="aligncenter wp-image-39090" height="313" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records9.jpg" width="598"/>
       </a>
       This UK native has a massive collection of 12,463 pieces of James Bond Memorabilia. His collection is stored in a secret place, away from the peeping eyes of friends.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Longest Golf Club  Karsten Maas
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records10.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records10" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-39091" height="440" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records10.jpg" width="840"/>
       </a>
       This man from Denmark built the worlds longest usable golf club, measuring 14 feet, 5 inches in length. As per Guinness, he uses his gigantic club to send a ball flying 542 feet, 10.62 inches.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Smallest Caravan  Yannick Reads QTvan
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records11.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records11" class="aligncenter wp-image-39092" height="311" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records11.jpg" width="594"/>
       </a>
       This tiny caravan packs a full size bed, lighting, a TV, a sink and a kettle into a vehicle that measures only 7 feet, 10 inches in length, 5 feet, .24 inches in height and 2 feet, 7.9 inches in width.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Largest Electric Guitar  Scott Rippetoe
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records12.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records12" class="aligncenter wp-image-39093" height="307" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records12.jpg" width="586"/>
       </a>
       This enormous instrument is playable. It measures 43 feet, 7.5 inches in height, 16 feet, 5.5 inches in width and weighs 2,000 pounds. Pupils from Conroe Independent School District Academy of Science and Technology in Texas  sculpted this instrument after a 1967 Gibson Flying V. Just $3,000 were spent to build the piece.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Fastest Dog on Two Paws
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records13.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records13" class="aligncenter wp-image-39094" height="311" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records13.jpg" width="594"/>
       </a>
       This little Pomeranian has two records to his name; fastest time to cover 10 meters on his hind legs and fastest time to cover five meters on his front legs.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Tallest Teenager  Broc Brown
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records14.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records14" class="aligncenter wp-image-39095" height="876" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records14.jpg" width="597"/>
       </a>
       This high school student from Michigan is exactly 7 feet, 1.5 inches tall. He cannot play basketball, however, Nike has supplied him with size 24 athletic sneakers, which as per Guinness, is larger than those worn by Shaquille ONeal.
      </p>
      <h2 class="Heading2" style="text-align: center">
       Longest Fingernails  The Dutchess
      </h2>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records15.jpg">
        <img alt="Weirdest Records15" class="aligncenter wp-image-39096" height="312" src="http://www.todayoutlook.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Weirdest-Records15.jpg" width="596"/>
       </a>
       The Dutchess named Christine Walton from Las Vegas, Nevada, holds the top spot when it comes to long fingernails. Last measured, her nails were 23 feet, 11 inches long.
      </p>
