     <strong>
      Fantasy Player of the Week
     </strong>
    </p>
    Eric Decker: 10 receptions, 221 receiving yards, and 1 TD for 28.1 fantasy pointsIt was the final week of the regular season and also the final week of fantasy football. In some leagues Week 17 was the championship week while it others it was meaningless. Eric Decker was last weeks fantasy player of the week with a surprising 28 fantasy point performance against the Dolphins. This was Deckers best performance of what turned out to be a relatively disappointing fantasy season. Decker led all receivers in fantasy scoring during Week 17 but ends the year with just 962 yards and 5 TDs  his worst line in the last three seasons.
    <strong>
     Surprising Performance of the Week
    </strong>
    Geno Smith: 358 passing yards, 3 passing TDs, 18 rushing yards, and 1 fumble for 26.12 fantasy pointsGeno Smith also had a surprisingly good game against what has been an otherwise good Miami Dolphins defense. This was just the first time all season that Smith broke 20 fantasy points in a week (many weeks he was lucky to even put up double digit points) and it is all the more surprising to see the game come against Miami and without the teams best wide receiver, Percy Harvin. Smith was the highest scoring QB in Week 17.
