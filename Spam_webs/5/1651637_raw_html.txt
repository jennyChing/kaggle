       <h1 class="entry-title">
        31 Days of Lovely Limitations Day 8 :: Use Your Mad Skilz
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2011-10-08T00:01:09+00:00">
         October 8, 2011
        </span>
        <span class="categories">
         Filed Under:
         <a href="http://www.thenester.com/category/31-days-of-lovely-limitations" rel="category tag">
          31 Days of Lovely Limitations
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-24216" height="450" src="http://www.thenester.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/ft_sept05msl15_xl.jpg" title="no sew seat cover" width="360"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.marthastewart.com/photogallery/chair-projects#slide_6">
          Martha
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         You know that chair with the broken seat and the ugly fabric? You dont have to wait till you learn how to sew, buy a yard of fabric and figure out how to use the jigsaw you dont own, to cut a new board for the bottom.
        </p>
        <p>
         Refusing to learn a new skill but being open to using the skills you have in a new way is one method to get things done.
        </p>
        <p>
         I dont know how to reupholster,
         <a href="http://www.thenester.com/2011/03/fur-chair-no-sew-reupholstery.html">
          but I know how to hot glue.
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont focus on what you dont know how to do, instead think about how you can use what you know for a different purpose.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-24297" height="293" src="http://www.thenester.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/Picture-121.png" title="Picture 12" width="530"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
