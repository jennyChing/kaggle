          <h1 class="entry-title">
           Asphalt Art: AKs Top 25 Asphalt Arts And Laughs
          </h1>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <span class="posted-on">

         <div class="entry-content">
          <a id="dd_start">
          </a>
          <p>
           Because asphalt is my work and my passion, I love to look at the amazing asphalt art that is out there. From linestriping humor to bollard art to pothole filling, these artists have gone the extra pavement mile to make us laugh, ogle, think, and enjoy. Nothing brightens the day better than humor that tickles your work funny-bone. Heres our top 25 Asphalt Arts And Laughs. Enjoy!
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1245" style="width: 394px">
           <a href="http://www.streetartutopia.com/?attachment_id=6012" rel="attachment wp-att-1245" target="_blank">

           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://www.streetartutopia.com/?attachment_id=6012" rel="attachment wp-att-1245" target="_blank">
             Piano Keys Pavement Art
            </a>
           </p>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Wouldnt this be the perfect parking lot crosswalk for a business with music lessons?
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1253" style="width: 419px">

           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://www.streetartutopia.com/?attachment_id=6011" rel="attachment wp-att-1245" target="_blank">
             Pac-man Sidewalk Art
            </a>
           </p>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Or how about this pedestrian crossing for a gaming store?
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1255" style="width: 420px">
           <a href="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/2013/04/street-artist-roadsworth-transforms-the-streets-of-montreal-into-a-visual-playground/" target="_blank">
            <img alt="Dandelion Street Art" class=" wp-image-1255 " height="614" src="http://www.asphaltkingdom.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/dandelion-street-art.jpg" width="410"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/2013/04/street-artist-roadsworth-transforms-the-streets-of-montreal-into-a-visual-playground/" rel="attachment wp-att-1245" target="_blank">
             Dandelion Street Art
            </a>
           </p>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            These parking lot lines ending in dandelion fluff are fanciful, artistic, and open up a whole host of ideas for linestriping art.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1257" style="width: 458px">
           <a href="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/2013/04/street-artist-roadsworth-transforms-the-streets-of-montreal-into-a-visual-playground/" target="_blank">

           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/2013/04/street-artist-roadsworth-transforms-the-streets-of-montreal-into-a-visual-playground/" rel="attachment wp-att-1245" target="_blank">
             Playful Street Graffiti
            </a>
           </p>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Cant you just see using this idea in your next parking lot job like this? For instance at the exit when theres multiple check out lanes would be perfect.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1258" style="width: 650px">

           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://www.thisiscolossal.com/2013/04/street-artist-roadsworth-transforms-the-streets-of-montreal-into-a-visual-playground/" rel="attachment wp-att-1245" target="_blank">
             3D Street Art
            </a>
           </p>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            This is an intersection where its impossible to miss the crossing, and looks great too.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1260" style="width: 610px">

           <p class="wp-caption-text">

            </a>
           </p>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            What better way to mark a no-parking zone than with sharks, using fin-bollards?
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1261" style="width: 560px">

           <p class="wp-caption-text">

             Sidewalk Art
            </a>
           </p>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Never would have imagined a coughing sewer drain, but what a fun way to mark it.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1262" style="width: 745px">

           </p>
