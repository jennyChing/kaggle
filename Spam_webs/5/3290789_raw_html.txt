         <h2>
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           Soul Shriven in Coldharbour Walkthrough
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          This is the introductory quest that opens up the main story arc. It starts automatically after character creation at the Wailing Prison in Molag Bals Plane of Oblivion. Simply approach your cell door and speak with the Prophet. Another prisoner will open your cell to allow you to proceed.
         </p>
         <p>
          From here, proceed with the only path available until you reach a weapons room. Select a weapon and walk through the next door where you will encounter skeletons. Defeat them to earn your first level up. A brief conversation with Titanborn will lead to a progress in your quest. Your target will be to reach the Tower of Eyes as you defeat skeletons on your way.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.itzwuteva.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Soul_Shriven_in_Coldharbour.png">
           <img alt="Soul_Shriven_in_Coldharbour" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2523" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.itzwuteva.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Soul_Shriven_in_Coldharbour.png?resize=620%2C339"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The next mission is to destroy a Sentinel. However this is a good chance to know your class well in terms of how it will play as you level up in the process. Fight plenty of monsters, collect plenty of items and follow the marker up the small tower and smash the eye to proceed.
         </p>
         <p>
          You will be required to reach the Prophets cell. Follow the maker and avoid the traps. Defeat the enemy and open chests. Two switches need opening at the end to enter the final area. Defeat the Anchor Guardian to finish.
         </p>
