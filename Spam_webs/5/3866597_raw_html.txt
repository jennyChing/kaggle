        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         Homemade Puppy Float!
        </h1>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         For the drink itself I used
         <strong>
          Low Sodium Beef Broth (Organic)
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         For the Magic Topping (looks like Whipped Cream) (Dog Friendly)
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          1 can Garbanzo Beans (Drained)
         </strong>
         (Save the juice) Super Important
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          1/2 Cup Honey
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Add all of the Bean Water (Drained off Garbanzo Beans)
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          1/2 Cup Honey
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li style="text-align: center;">
          I put both ingredients in my kitchen aid on low. Beat on low until you see it start turning white.
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: center;">
          Increase your speed slowly til your whipping it. Like Whipped Cream only healthier!
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: center;">
          I promise it turns into this amazing white fluff!
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           Beat until Stiff!
          </strong>
         </li>
         <li style="text-align: center;">
          Thats it!! Ready to use
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Spoon onto your Beef Float. Top with a
         <strong>
          Pedigree Dentastix Beef Flavor
         </strong>
         and you now have a Puppy Float ready for Puppy consumption!
        </p>
