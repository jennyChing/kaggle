        <aside class="img-box bulge first-image org-size tiny-size ">
         <img alt="Raver down at Electric Daisy Carnival in 2008." src="http://images1.laweekly.com/imager/raver-down-at-electric-daisy-carnival-in-2/u/original/2417096/edc_2008_thumb_220x186.jpg"/>

          <table>
           <tbody>
            <tr>
             <td>
              <div class="cap">

              </div>
             </td>
            </tr>
           </tbody>
          </table>
         </div>
        </aside>
        <p>
         One of the more bizarre aspects of officials
         <a href="http://blogs.laweekly.com/informer/2011/02/rave_commission_edc_1.php" target="_blank">
          sanctioning raves
         </a>
         at the publicly owned L.A. Coliseum and Sports Arena is that Los Angeles County health authorities have actually embarked on a campaign to teach young people how to take ecstasy safely.
        </p>
        <div class="ads middle" data-vmg-ad="Middle" data-vmg-format="normal">
        </div>
        <p>
         Under the strategy of "harm reduction" -- if you're going to get wasted, mind as well help you do so without killing yourself -- the county this year will distribute fliers at raves that give tips that essentially amount to advice on how not to O.D. on this illicit,
         <a href="http://www.justice.gov/dea/pubs/scheduling.html" target="_blank">
          schedule 1
         </a>
         drug.
        </p>
        <p>
         Strange, because ...
        </p>
        <div class="img-box org-size ">
         <div class="inner-img-box">
          <img alt="Your tax dollars at work: L.A. County wants you to roll safely." src="http://images2.laweekly.com/imager/your-tax-dollars-at-work-la-county-want/u/original/2417093/county_ecstasy_flier.jpg"/>
          <div class="insert">
           <table>
            <tbody>
             <tr>
              <td>
               <div class="cap">
                Your tax dollars at work: L.A. County wants you to roll safely.
               </div>
              </td>
             </tr>
            </tbody>
           </table>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         ... the Coliseum Commission, which runs those venues, seems to be bending over backwards to allow ecstasy-fueled raves to happen four times a year at the sites.
        </p>
        <div class="ads yieldmo ym" data-vmg-ad="Yieldmo" data-yieldmo-id="927393268424711308" id="ym_927393268424711308">
        </div>
        <p>
         California drug-abatement law might have a different view of the parties:
        </p>
        <blockquote class="city-back-superlight">
         "Every building or place used for the purpose of unlawfully selling, serving, storing, keeping, manufacturing, or giving away any controlled substance, precursor, or analog specified in this division, and every building or place wherein or upon which those acts take place, is a nuisance which shall be enjoined, abated, and prevented, and for which damages may be recovered, whether it is a public or private nuisance."
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         But L.A. County says, hey, if you're going to party, here's how:
        </p>
        <blockquote class="city-back-superlight">
         <p>
          -Take frequent breaks.
         </p>
         <p>
          -Let your body cool and rest.
         </p>
         <p>
          -Stay hydrated.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         This part is really interesting, because doctors say ecstasy can rob your body of electrolytes and cause you to shut down and end up in a hospital.
        </p>
        <blockquote class="city-back-superlight">
         -Sip or take small gulps of water ... and non-caffeinated sports-drinks like Gatorade (
         <strong>
          to replenish electrolytes
         </strong>
         ). (Emphasis ours).
        </blockquote>
        <div class="img-box org-size ">
         <div class="inner-img-box">
          <img alt="Only you can prevent e-tards." src="http://images2.laweekly.com/imager/only-you-can-prevent-e-tards/u/original/2417094/the_more_you_know.jpg"/>
          <div class="insert">
           <table>
            <tbody>
             <tr>
              <td>
