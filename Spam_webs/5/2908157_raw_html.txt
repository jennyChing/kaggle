         <h1>
          Apocalyptic Warhorse
         </h1>
         <div class="gallery-image-info">
          Visions of our impending doom here in the final days of Western existench: gnashing greedy teeth, snarling warhorse, mass graves, mountains of tires, industrial decay, guns, ruins, American missiles (coming soon to a country near you). ...seriously though, our world is being destroyed literally right now, because of psychotic war criminal tyrants like Bush and Cheney.  We are in the final era of our American empire, spreading our forces too thin, creating unrest and rebellion in all corners of the globe, just like Rome, just like Great Britain (and all the other empires that destroyed themselves)...and our collective apathy is going to seal our fate. So EDUCATE YOURSELF, GET PREPARED, STOP LOOKING AT MY TATTOOS AND GET OFF YOUR ASS AND START DOING SOMETHING ABOUT IT.  Or if you're in too much of a consumerist daze to even know of anything to do, start learning at infoshop.org, crimethinc.com, indymedia.org, truthout.org, etc etc...
