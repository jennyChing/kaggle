        <h2 class="date-header">
         Mar 04, 2015
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-category-music entry-author-household_name entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00e5502b6df4883401b8d0e26c1f970c">
         <div class="entry-inner">
          <h3 class="entry-header">
           <a href="http://householdname.typepad.com/my_weblog/2015/03/pandora-pays-convicted-paedophiles.html">
            Pandora Pays Convicted Paedophiles
           </a>
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://householdname.typepad.com/.a/6a00e5502b6df4883401b8d0e26bbb970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Gary_Glitter_Pandora_Paedophile" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e5502b6df4883401b8d0e26bbb970c img-responsive" src="http://householdname.typepad.com/.a/6a00e5502b6df4883401b8d0e26bbb970c-800wi" title="Gary_Glitter_Pandora_Paedophile"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13pt; font-family: helvetica;">
              Wrote to Pandora asking them why they're still hosting "Gary Glitter Radio" and writing him royalty checks.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13pt; font-family: helvetica;">
              They replied:
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13pt; font-family: helvetica; color: #888888;">
              Hi Adrian,
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Thanks very much for writing. I definitely understand your concern. As far as our collection goes, we carry any artist that is relevant and has a significant fanbase. We're interested in offering music and comedy that listeners will want to hear, regardless of genre, historical context, or other classification. That said, we are sensitive to individual taste, so we do offer you the ability to remove any track that you don't like from your stations.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              If you give consistent Thumbs-down to an artist on a given station, Pandora should learn to stop playing that artist on that station.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Of course, if you have added this artist or one of their songs as a seed on that station, or ever Thumbed them up on that station, you would have to remove these seeds or Thumbs first.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Thanks again for your feedback and for listening!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Best,
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13pt; font-family: helvetica; color: #888888;">
              Warren | Pandora
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #888888;">
              <span style="font-size: 13pt; font-family: helvetica;">
               Artist Advocate
              </span>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13pt; font-family: helvetica;">
              So Pandora employees will continue to derive
             </span>
             a
             <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: 13pt;">
              percentage of their pay check directly from the sale of songs that benefit a man who raped a 12 year old girl.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: 13pt;">
              There's CSR for you!
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>

        <div class="entry-author-household_name entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00e5502b6df4883401a73de41978970d">
         <div class="entry-inner">
          <h3 class="entry-header">
           <a href="http://householdname.typepad.com/my_weblog/2014/07/razzle-dazzle-camouflage.html">
            Razzle Dazzle Camouflage
           </a>
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://householdname.typepad.com/.a/6a00e5502b6df4883401a511d8ac48970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="RAZZLE_DAZZLE_SHIPS" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e5502b6df4883401a511d8ac48970c image-full img-responsive" src="http://householdname.typepad.com/.a/6a00e5502b6df4883401a511d8ac48970c-800wi" title="RAZZLE_DAZZLE_SHIPS"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13pt; font-family: helvetica;">
              Credited to British artist, Norman Wilkinson (1878-1971) the concept of 'Razzle Dazzle' camouflage 'Is essential to break up the regularity of outline and this can be easily effected by strongly contrasting shadesa giraffe or zebra or jaguar looks extraordinarily conspicuous in a museum but in nature, especially when moving is wonderfully difficult to pick up.'
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13pt; font-family: helvetica;">
              When a German submarine spotted one of these flamboyant warships in their primitive rangefinders, it became extremely difficult to judge the speed and heading of the vessels course.
             </span>
             <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: 13pt;">
              This made it nearly impossible to accurately make a successful torpedo hit.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://householdname.typepad.com/.a/6a00e5502b6df4883401a73de418f6970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="RAZZLE_DAZZLE_PAINTING" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e5502b6df4883401a73de418f6970d image-full img-responsive" src="http://householdname.typepad.com/.a/6a00e5502b6df4883401a73de418f6970d-800wi" title="RAZZLE_DAZZLE_PAINTING"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: 13pt;">
              Razzle Dazzle was applied to over 2,000 warships and 4,000 British merchant ships in both the World Wars.
              <br/>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://householdname.typepad.com/.a/6a00e5502b6df4883401a73de41894970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="RAZZLE_DAZZLE_INFINITI" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e5502b6df4883401a73de41894970d image-full img-responsive" src="http://householdname.typepad.com/.a/6a00e5502b6df4883401a73de41894970d-800wi" title="RAZZLE_DAZZLE_INFINITI"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="font-size: 13pt; font-family: helvetica;">
              And its still used today on car prototypes to prevent competitors getting a clear view of a new cars lines.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
