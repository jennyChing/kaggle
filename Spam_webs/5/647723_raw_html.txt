             <h2>
              What Are The Odds?
             </h2>
             <p class="transcript-value">
              What are the odds?
              <br/>
              <br/>
              What are the odds that you exist, as you, today?
              <br/>
              Author Ali Binazir attemps to quantify the probability that you came about and exist as you today, and reveals that the odds of you existing are almost zero.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              LET'S START SMALL
              <br/>
              What is the probability of your dad meeting your mom?
              <br/>
              Though the world was smaller 20 years ago, your dad could have met almost 200 million of its women (go dad!)
              <br/>
              But over 25 years, he probably met around 10,000 women.
              <br/>
              So the odds that your mom was in this small group and met your dad is: 1 in 20,000
              <br/>
              <br/>
              BUT WE KNOW HOW TRICKY LOVE CAN BE.
              <br/>
              What is the probability that they stay together long enough to have kids?
              <br/>
              It is a 1 in 10 chance that they talk to each other.
              <br/>
              Also a 1 in 10 chance that they go on a second date.
              <br/>
              Another 1 in 10 chance that they keep dating for a while.
              <br/>
              And a coin toss if they stay together long enough for offspring.
              <br/>
              Thus, the odds that your parents' meeting results in kids is: 1 in 2,000
              <br/>
              <br/>
              So far, the combines odds of you being here are: 1 in 40,000,000 That is about the size of the population of California.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              NOW THINGS ARE GOING TO GET PRETTY INTERESTING
              <br/>
              Why? Because we are about to deal with eggs and sperm, which come in large numbers.
              <br/>
              Mom has about 100,000 eggs in her lifetime.
              <br/>
              Dad makes about 4 trillion sperm during the years you could have been born.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              What are the odds that the 1 egg... (Sally)...
              <br/>
              ...met the 1 sperm, which together made you(and not your brother)? (Harry)
              <br/>
              1 in 400,000,000,000,000,000 (1 in 400 quadrillion)That is approximately the volume in cubic meters of the Atlantic Ocean (3,236x10 17 cubic meters).
              <br/>
              <br/>
              BUT WE'RE JUST GETTING STARTED
              <br/>
              Because your existence here, now, and on planet earth presupposes another supremely unlikely and utterly undeniable chain of events. Namely, that every one of your ancestors lived to reproductive age- going all the way back not just to the first Homo sapiens, first Homo erectus and Homo habilis, but all the way back to the first single-celled organism. You are a representative of an unbroken lineage of life going back 4 billion years.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              What are the odds that you lineage remained unbroken for the length of human existence?
              <br/>
              (You.Aw.)
              <br/>
              1 in 2 odds that a child will be born, grow, and reproduce per generation...
              <br/>
              ... for 150,000 generations
              <br/>
              <br/>
              1 in 10  45,000
              <br/>
              That's a 10 with 45,000 zeroes after it.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              How large is that number?
              <br/>
              That number is not just larger than all the particles in the universe-it is larger that all the particles in the universe if each particle were itself a universe.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              THAT'S PRETTY BIG, BUT WAIT A MINUTE.
              <br/>
              The right sperm also had to meet the right egg for every single one of those ancestors.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              (Weeeee!)
              <br/>
              ODDS OF THE RIGHT SPERM, MEETING THE RIGHT EGG 150,000 TIMES?
              <br/>
              1 in 10 2,640,000
              <br/>
              That is 1 quadrillion multiplied by 1 quadrillion for every generation.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Let's add it all up
              <br/>
              <br/>
              10 2,640,000 x 10 45,000 x 2000 x 20,000= 1 in 10 2,685,000
              <br/>
              <br/>
              BY COMPARISON:
              <br/>
              The number of atoms in the body of an average male (80kg. 175 lb) is about: 10 27
              <br/>
              The number of atoms making up the earth is about: 10 50
              <br/>
              The number of atoms in the known universe is estimated at: 10 80
              <br/>
              <br/>
              THINK OF IT LIKE THIS:
              <br/>
              It is the probability of 2 million people getting together (about the population of San Diego) each to play a game of dice with trillion-sided dice. They each roll the dice, and they all come up with the exact same number-for example, 550,343,279,001.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              SO, THE ODDS THAT YOU EXIST AT ALL ARE:
              <br/>
              BASICALLY ZERO.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              A miracle is an event so unlikely as to be almost impossible. By that definition, we have just shown that you are a miracle.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Now go forth and feel and ac like the miracle that you are.
             </p>
