        A list of five beauty secrets that include only natural substances.
       </p>
       <div class="KonaBody">
        <p>
         Everyone has something that they dont like about themselves and they always try different things to fix the problems. Mostly they are factory made items that are full of man made chemicals that can be harsh on your body that can cause more harm than help. Here are five beauty remedies that will come in handy with even the worse beauty situations.
        </p>

        <p>
         1. Banish Under eye Circles.
        </p>
        <p>
         Using a banana you can get rid of these awful dark marks that live under your beautiful eyes. The bananas contain enzymes that turn brown when the banana is cut, but when applied to your skin it does the opposite by lightening dark patches.
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Zap Away Blemishes and Pimples.
        </p>
        <p>
         Honey, the kind you put on Grandmas homemade butter biscuits? It can help renew your skin and get rid of those pesky pimples faster than any Proactive. For the best results, apply a drop of honey to pimples twice a day.
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Give Your Hair A Glossy Shine.
        </p>
        <p>
         You know that Rosemary Tea that your mother drinks every morning? Take it and spritz it onto your hair and give it a gloss that resembles and rivals the highest paid French model. Boil 2 Tbs. fresh rosemary and 2 cups of water. Simmer it for 3 minutes and then let it cool down. Pour it into a spray bottle and you have a hairspray that is completely natural.
        </p>

         4. Smooth Away Cellulite.
        </p>
        <p>
         While looking in mirror after a shower, you discover that there seems to be a dent in your left thigh and cheek. You go hysterical and go to town to find some treatment lotion and you totally pass by the papayas in your refrigerator. Scoop out the flesh of the papaya and massage it into places on your person that already have cellulite and the prone areas. Then rinse it off.
        </p>
        <p>
         5. Have Beautiful Healthy Nails.
        </p>
        <p>
         You can have shiny, healthy nails by using Neem Oil. The women in India use this and it may not be familiar to some of us. You can order it from Amazon or you can go to a health-food store. Massaging one or two drops into your nails and cuticles can help turn those claws into princess hands. And its all natural.
