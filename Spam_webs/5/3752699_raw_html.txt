         <p>
          Anthony Robbins, the famous motivational speaker and writer says this about NLP: "NLP is like the nuclear physics of the mind. Physics deals with the structure of reality, the nature of the world. NLP does the same thing for your mind. It allows you to break things down into the component parts that make them work. People have spent a lifetime trying to get to find a way to be loved. They've spent fortunes trying  to get to "know themselves" with analysts and read dozens of books on how to succeed. NLP gives us the technology to accomplish these and many other goals elegantly, efficiently, and effectively- now!" NLP is a new revolution about how the human mind operates to achieve certain desired results.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          It is about change.Many people long for change in their lives. It could be the desire to be a better parent,  lover, teacher,  chess player, an athlete or simply to enjoy better health mentally and physically. Many people fail dismally to change for the better because they simply do not have the skills of how to go about effecting the desired change in their lives. NLP is the answer. Briefly NLP can be defined as programming our brains in certain particular ways to achieve  a desired result. Say for example you wish to wake up feeling energetic and soon start being busy with a days plan of action.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          However no matter how hard you try, you always feel tired and lethargic long after getting out of bed. Even though you don't drink alcohol and eat well and take enough exercise, you still can't overcome your lerthargy after waking. What do you do then to overcome this problem? One of the ways NLP can be of benefit to you is MODELLING. You have to observe what a person who wakes up energetic does and model your behaviour accordingly. Perhaps you will observe that the person meditates for fifteen minutes every evening before bedtime and evry morniing  just after waking. Perhaps you will also observe that the person does some five minutes of breathing exercises  before taking a shower. Then he has a diet of fruits for breakfast and smiles when he greets people.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          He also hums a tune and walks upright around the house. This is modelling. The are other interesting techniques you can employ to overcome you lerthargy after getting out of bed. Note that neuro stands for brain and linguistic stands for language. Your inner speech affects how your brain responds and this can truly affect how good you feel each time you get out of bed.
         </p>
