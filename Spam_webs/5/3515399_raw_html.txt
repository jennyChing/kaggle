        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Gouda Jalapeno Roasted Red Pepper Grilled Cheese
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-04-04T11:09:02+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          April 4, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/author/jenniferbullistrongmail-com/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Jenny B
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/gouda-jalapeno-roasted-red-pepper-grilled-cheese/#comments">
           29 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.linqia.com/">
          <img alt="" class=" aligncenter" data-jpibfi-description="" src="http://linqia.ooh.li/disclosure/121972000.png"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Turn a kid-friendly classic sandwich into an adult masterpiece with this recipe for a
          <strong>
           gouda jalapeo roasted red pepper grilled cheese sandwich
          </strong>
          !
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://ooh.li/e3a926b" rel="nofollow">
          <img alt="Gouda Jalapeno Roasted Red Pepper Grilled Cheese Sandwich #GooeyGoodness Naturally Delicious Grilled Cheese" class="alignnone wp-image-9963 size-large" data-jpibfi-description="Gouda Jalapeno Roasted Red Pepper Grilled Cheese Sandwich #GooeyGoodness Naturally Delicious Grilled Cheese" height="972" src="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/gouda-jalapeno-roasted-red-pepper-grilled-cheese-sandwich1-680x972.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I love a good grilled cheese sandwich. There is something about gooey meltedcheese that makes me all warm and fuzzy inside. Thank goodness April is National Grilled Cheese Month and Im celebrating with
         <a href="https://ooh.li/e3a926b" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Arla Dofino
         </a>
         !
        </p>
        <p>
         I was going through my recipe archives the other day and I realized that I havenevershared a grilled cheese recipe of my own. Obviously, that is a problem with an easy solution. As a kid, I ate a lot of grilled cheese sandwiches. I think a lot of kids did  its one of those classic foods and it is kid-friendly. Well, Im not a kid anymore and I want a grilled cheese sandwich that is more than white bread and yellow cheese.
         <span id="more-9958">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://ooh.li/e3a926b" rel="nofollow">
          <img alt="Gouda Jalapeno Roasted Red Pepper Grilled Cheese Sandwich #GooeyGoodness Naturally Delicious Grilled Cheese" class="alignnone wp-image-9967 size-large" data-jpibfi-description="Gouda Jalapeno Roasted Red Pepper Grilled Cheese Sandwich #GooeyGoodness Naturally Delicious Grilled Cheese" height="928" src="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/gouda-jalapeno-roasted-red-pepper-grilled-cheese-sandwich-2-680x928.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I started with good bread and delicious cheese, like the naturally goodcheeses from
         <a href="https://ooh.li/e3a926b" target="_blank">
          Arla Dofino
         </a>
         . Arla Dofino cheeses have been traditionally crafted in Wisconsin for more than 100 years. The cheese is free of added hormones, artificial flavors and preservatives, and crafted with quality, natural ingredients. Also, it tastes really good!
        </p>
        <h2>
         Gouda Jalapeno Roasted Red Pepper Grilled Cheese Sandwich
        </h2>
        <p>
         I chose the
         <a href="https://ooh.li/e3a926b" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Arla Dofino gouda
         </a>
         for my grilled cheese recipe. Its buttery, creamy and oh, so delicious.Then, things get crazyI addedspicy jalapeos paired with smoky roasted red peppers. I figure if I am turninga kid-friendly food into an adult-minded sandwich, you need to add adult flavors.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://ooh.li/e3a926b" rel="nofollow">
          <img alt="Gouda Jalapeno Roasted Red Pepper Grilled Cheese Sandwich #GooeyGoodness Naturally Delicious Grilled Cheese" class="alignnone wp-image-9970 size-large" data-jpibfi-description="Gouda Jalapeno Roasted Red Pepper Grilled Cheese Sandwich #GooeyGoodness Naturally Delicious Grilled Cheese" height="1020" src="http://www.honeyandbirch.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/gouda-jalapeno-roasted-red-pepper-grilled-cheese-sandwich-3-680x1020.jpg" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I bought a wedge of gouda instead of slices, so I shredded itwhich I have found makes it melt pretty well. Then its time to get cooking. Start will two pieces of well buttered bread and a hot pan or skillet. Place one piece of bread, butter side down, on the pan. Sprinkle the shredded gouda evenly on top of the bread, being careful not to spill too much cheese on the pan.
        </p>
        <p>
         Put the jalapeo and roasted red pepper on top of the cheese and then sprinkle some more gouda over that. You can never have too much gouda. Then place the second piece of bread, butter side up, on top of the cheese. Use a spatula to push down on the bread so the cheese and ingredients all stick together. When the bottom piece of bread is toasted, carefully flip the sandwich over and cook the other side. Before you know it, you are going to have a melted, gooey mess of a delicious sandwich that you will want to start eating immediately.
        </p>
        <p>
         And you should. Cut it in half, and take a big bite when its cooled down just a little. Enjoy!
        </p>
