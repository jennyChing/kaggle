       <h1 class="entry-title">
        99 Links to Learn Any Language for Free (Updated for 2012)
       </h1>
       <hr class="clearer"/>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         They say love is the universal language, but when youre in a Chinese market trying to haggle over a fake Rolex, love probably wont help you get the deal done. With all the resources freely available, theres no reason to not at least brush up on the native language of a country youll be visiting or to simply expand your horizons. A few years back we brought you a
         <a href="http://www.onlinecollege.org/2009/08/16/101-tools-to-learn-any-foreign-language-for-free/">
          list of 101 online tools
         </a>
         for learning any foreign language on the arm. We thought it was time for a tune-up. Andale!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>

           <a href="http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/German">
            German
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           This book is under development but can still serve as a handy guide to your German instruction.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://archive.org/details/rosettaproject_abq_morsyn-2">
            Abaza Grammar
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Fewer than 50,000 people in the world speak this language of the Caucasus Mountain region, so youll be in an exclusive club if you learn it with the help of this book.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Afrikaans">
            Afrikaans
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Work your way through the South African language with this free textbook.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Belarusian">
            Belorusian
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Join the 9 million or so people who speak Belorusian by reading up on the language with this textbook.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Albanian Basic Course Vol. I:
          </strong>
          <p>
           The scan could be better quality, but this is the first of 10 volumes for sinking your teeth into the language of Albania.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Arabic">
            Arabic
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           If youre interested in this geopolitically important language, learn the pronouns, definite articles, descriptors, and more, here.
          </p>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Translation
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/togawanderings/6416695905/">
          <img src="http://www.onlinecollege.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/101languagelinks5.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Use them as a supplement to your studies or just type a word in and memorize what comes out.
        </p>
        <ol class="list-continue" start="66">
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.free-translator.com/">
            Free-Translator
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           The text box has room for your Twitter character limit and then some for easy translation into scores of languages.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Xanadu:
          </strong>
          <p>
           This sites made for traveling, with a currency converter and text translation accompanying the glossaries and reference materials for six different languages.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://translate.google.com/">
            Google Translate
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Its quick, clean, and efficient for translating dozens of languages to and from English.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.freelang.net/">
            Freelang
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Choose from 265 bilingual dictionaries and get your human translation here.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.bing.com/translator/">
            Bing Translator
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Formerly Babbel Fish, this is Microsofts text and web translator for the Google haters.
          </p>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Communities
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/smbcollege/5510561294/">
          <img src="http://www.onlinecollege.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/101languagelinks6.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Harness the power in numbers by connecting with other language learners.
        </p>
        <ol class="list-continue" start="71">
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://livemocha.com/pages/the-livemocha-community/">
            Livemocha
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Through this language sites community you can have exercises reviewed by other users, practice your conversation, and more.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.italki.com/">
            italki
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Have your questions answered, your writing corrected, and above all, communicate on this global language network.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://lingopass.com/">
            Lingopass
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Jump into group discussions or start up a one-on-one in a chat room through this site.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.unilang.org/">
            UniLang
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Uniting language lovers, UniLangs forums are a great place to practice that new tongue youve been learning.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.mylanguageexchange.com/">
            My Language Exchange
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Join the community of more than 1 million users from more than 130 countries and practice a foreign language with a native speaker.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.palabea.com/">
            Palabea
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           The Facebook of language networking, Palabea lets you upload video and audio, record, translate, and of course, dialogue.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://community.skype.com/t5/Language-learning/bd-p/Languages">
            Skype Community Language Learning
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           The popular video-calling software has a section for users to ask and answer questions and make connections for conversations.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.language-exchanges.org/">
            The Mixxer
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Similar to My Language Exchange, Mixxer is Dickinson Colleges platform for language learners to be both teachers and students.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.myhappyplanet.com/">
            My Happy Planet
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Members can create their own lessons for others or just communicate with them directly on this learning community site.
          </p>
         </li>
        </ol>

         <strong>
          Tools
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/hanse/799801895/">

        </p>
        <p>
         These high-tech resources will streamline your language learning experience.

         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.babbel.com/">
            Babbel
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>

         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.byki.com/">
            Byki
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Available as a free download or paid upgrade, Byki is a solid bit of language-learning, flash card-based software.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://lingro.com/">
            Lingro
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           Lingro is a slick tool that works like Rikai except it works with 12 languages, making any word on a web page clickable to see its definition.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.arizona-software.ch/provoc/">
            ProVoc
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           ProVoc ups the ante on flash card learning with features like variable difficulty and the ability to create your own cards.
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/one-world-dictionary/id464285660?mt=12">
            One World Dictionary
           </a>
           :
          </strong>
          <p>
           This program by Ascendo works on Macs to bring you 100,000 translations into Spanish, French, Italian, and German.
          </p>
         </li>
        </ol>

           This highly-rated iPad app helps you practice Spanish pronunciation and vocab based on conversational lessons.

           <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=aephid.cueBrainLite&amp;feature=more_from_developer#?t=W251bGwsMSwxLDEwMiwiYWVwaGlkLmN1ZUJyYWluTGl0ZSJd">
