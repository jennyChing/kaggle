       <section class="post-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
        <div id="nrelate_flyout_placeholder">
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.woolffgallery.co.uk/" target="_blank">
           <span class="post-first-letter">
            W
           </span>
           oolff Gallery
          </a>
          , London, presents a dynamic new series of paintings by artist
          <a href="http://www.oonahassim.com/paintings.html" target="_blank">
           Oona Hassim
          </a>
          . Her solo exhibition will open on 26 January, 2012, ending March, 15, 2012.Her unique style of painting lies between figurative and abstract. Her works reflect the citys atmosphere, from benign marches to reactive demonstrations, harnessing the power of mass movement and the mood of its people.
         </p>
         <div class="img-center">
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_7656">
           <a href="http://artboom.info/painting/oona-hassim-at-woolff-gallery-london.html/attachment/painting-on-canvas-by-oona-hassim" rel="attachment wp-att-7656">
            <img alt="Oona Hassim Student Demo Nov 22nd Oil on canvas 498x496 Oona Hassim  at Woolff Gallery, London" class="size-large wp-image-7656" height="496" src="http://cdn.artboom.info/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Oona-Hassim-Student-Demo-Nov-22nd-Oil-on-canvas-498x496.jpg" title="painting on canvas by Oona Hassim" width="498"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            'Student Demo Nov 22nd', Oil on canvas
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="img-center">
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_7657">
           <a href="http://artboom.info/painting/oona-hassim-at-woolff-gallery-london.html/attachment/painting-on-canvas-by-oona-hassim-2" rel="attachment wp-att-7657">
            <img alt="Oona Hassim Anti cuts demo March 2011 Piccadilly for web 498x375 Oona Hassim  at Woolff Gallery, London" class="size-large wp-image-7657" height="375" src="http://cdn3.artboom.info/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Oona-Hassim-Anti-cuts-demo-March-2011-Piccadilly-for-web-498x375.jpg" title="painting on canvas by Oona Hassim" width="498"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Anti-cuts demo March 2011, Piccadilly
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p>
          <span id="more-7653">
          </span>
          Focusing on random crowds and the passage of life that traverses the city of London, her distinctive palette of softgraysjuxtaposed against colorful crowds and neon lights.
         </p>
         <div class="img-center">
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_7660">
           <a href="http://artboom.info/painting/oona-hassim-at-woolff-gallery-london.html/attachment/urban_rhythms2" rel="attachment wp-att-7660">
            <img alt="urban rhythms2 498x372 Oona Hassim  at Woolff Gallery, London" class="size-large wp-image-7660" height="372" src="http://cdn2.artboom.info/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/urban_rhythms2-498x372.jpg" title="urban_rhythms2" width="498"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Leicester Sq. series - 2, 2005
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p>
          The potent emblem of a city is its crowds. Her new works explore an explosion of recent activity and unrest on the streets. Posing as the flaneur, the impartial spectator, allows Hassim to observe the vast charge of changing energy within the urban environment.
         </p>
         <div class="img-center">
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_7658">
           <a href="http://artboom.info/painting/oona-hassim-at-woolff-gallery-london.html/attachment/trafalgar_square" rel="attachment wp-att-7658">
            <img alt="trafalgar square 498x374 Oona Hassim  at Woolff Gallery, London" class="size-large wp-image-7658" height="374" src="http://cdn.artboom.info/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/trafalgar_square-498x374.jpg" title="trafalgar_square" width="498"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Trafalgar Square, 2009
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p>
          One of her recent works, explore the power of crowds at public demonstrations such as the March 2011 protest rallyagainst the coalition governments cuts within the public sector. Here she examines the UKs largest union-organised march since the Second World War, reflecting upon the manner in which passionate crowds can never move freely due to the constraint of the city landscape and event marshaling.
         </p>
         <div class="img-center">
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_7659">
           <a href="http://artboom.info/painting/oona-hassim-at-woolff-gallery-london.html/attachment/painting-on-canvas-by-oona-hassim-3" rel="attachment wp-att-7659">
            <img alt="24.Occupy London 5th November 2011 498x376 Oona Hassim  at Woolff Gallery, London" class="size-large wp-image-7659" height="376" src="http://cdn.artboom.info/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/24.Occupy-London-5th-November-2011-498x376.jpg" title="painting on canvas by Oona Hassim" width="498"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Occupy London 5th November 2011
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p>
          In contrast, Hassims expression of Aprils 2011 Royal Wedding, portrays regimented patriot colours of an ordered, exuberant, yet controlled city. Oona Hassim records these historic events in the traditional and timeless medium ofoils  the result is both tempestuous and emotive.
         </p>
         <div class="img-center">
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_7661">
           <a href="http://artboom.info/painting/oona-hassim-at-woolff-gallery-london.html/attachment/covent_garden_dta" rel="attachment wp-att-7661">
            <img alt="covent garden dta 498x371 Oona Hassim  at Woolff Gallery, London" class="size-large wp-image-7661" height="371" src="http://cdn2.artboom.info/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/covent_garden_dta-498x371.jpg" title="covent_garden_dta" width="498"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Covent Garden during the act
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p>
          As one of the contemporary artists who continues to express concern about the city as a paradigm for society, Oona Hassim depicts its paradox of positive and negative energies and comments.
         </p>
         <blockquote>
          <p>
           Perhaps to fully understand the city, we need to grasp at all of its trajectories, its intimate corners and blinding glitter, and somehow attempt to weave together these disparate qualities.
          </p>
         </blockquote>
         <p>
          Heres also a short film made at Occupy London by the
          <a href="http://www.woolffgallery.co.uk/" target="_blank">
           Woolff Gallery
          </a>
          .
         </p>
