               <h2 class="blog-title">
                <a class="blog-title-link blog-link" href="http://www.ripleymcfly.com/articles-and-recipes/november-22nd-2014">
                 17 Ridiculously Dumb things I have done
                </a>
               </h2>
               <p class="blog-date">
                <span class="date-text">
                 11/22/2014
                </span>
               </p>
               <p class="blog-comments">

                 <a>
                  <img alt="Picture" src="/uploads/3/1/6/8/31684703/1416689635.png" style="width:auto;max-width:100%"/>

               <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
                1. Rear ended a cop
                <br/>
                <br/>
                2. Rear ended some lady while rooting around for that elusive Chicken McNugget
                <br/>
                <br/>
                3. Walked into a light pole because I was too busy talking to someone to notice (3 times)
                <br/>
                <br/>
                4. Hit some sort of oil patch or something while on the highway and did a 180 (don't ask me how I'm still alive because I don't know)
                <br/>
                <br/>
                5. Invested some money (not much fortunately) in penny stocks
                <br/>
                <br/>
                6. Locked myself inside my own car (twice) (sighs) long story
                <br/>
                <br/>
                7. Loaned friends money and thought I'd actually see it again
                <br/>
                <br/>
                8. Grabbed a broom sideways in a hurry while I was standing between 2 open doors and then bolted (I now know what cartoon characters feel like, my stomach was in agony for hours)
                <br/>
                <br/>
                9. Believed the tire salesman (multiple times) was telling the truth when he was selling me tires
                <br/>
                <br/>
                10. Actually thought the guy would call me afterwards
                <br/>
                <br/>
                11. Up until I was about 13, thought Harrison Ford was a car dealership
                <br/>
                <br/>
                12. In high school dated a guy who got arrested on what would have been our first date (he was 15, told me he was 16 and like an ass I never thought twice about it. On his way to pick me up, a cop noticed that his brake lights were out. He then began a mini high speed chase, but got caught because he turned into a dead end road. This was well before cellphones were popular, so I was left waiting and wondering what happened to him)
                <br/>
                <br/>
                ...and yes, I still dated him for a little bit after the matter (What? He was really cute)
                <br/>
                <br/>
                13. Once forgot my own name. I was running for student government in 6th grade and forgot my name in front of the entire school. The best part was: it was caught on tape. I still wonder if the school still has a copy.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                14. Made a few jokes about my boss while she was standing right behind me
                <br/>
                <br/>
                15. Failed my first driving test when I blew through the very first red lightof the test(in under 60seconds) because I was daydreaming about how I was such an awesome driver
                <br/>
                <br/>
                16. Left my purse in a shopping cart and drove off (I actually managed to get it back, unharmed and nothing missing)
                <br/>
                <br/>
                17. Somehow managed to get an orange peel stuck up my nose for who knows how long when I was a kid (when the doctor extracted it, lord only knows how much later, it was apparently green and moldy)
                <br/>
                <font size="3">
                 <span style="background-color: initial;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </font>
                <br/>
                <font size="3">
                 <span style="background-color: initial;">
                  ***For those wondering how I managed to lock myself in my own car well, i
                 </span>
                 <span style="background-color: initial;">
                  t involves a bunch of factors that have to be combined in just the right way.
                 </span>
                </font>
                <br/>
                <br/>
                <br/>
                <font size="3">
                 To begin:
                </font>
                <br/>
                <ol style="">
                 <li style="">
                  <span style="font-size: small; background-color: initial;">
                   My door handle is broken, I have to open the door to my car by rolling down the window and opening it from the outside (I don't want to pay to have it fixed, it's costly and I'm cheap)
                  </span>
                  <br/>
                 </li>
                 <li style="">
                  <font size="2">
                   <span style="background-color: initial;">
                    I had left the lights on to my car the night before and the battery had died
                   </span>
                   <br/>
                  </font>
                 </li>
                 <li style="">
                  <font size="2">
                   <span style="background-color: initial;">
                    the locks are electrical, except when you open them with a key from the
                   </span>
                   <strong style="background-color: initial;">
                    <em>
                     outside
                    </em>
                   </strong>
                   <br/>
                  </font>
                 </li>
                 <li style="">
                  <font size="2">
                   <span style="background-color: initial;">
                    I got into the car and shut the door
                   </span>
                   <br/>
                  </font>
                 </li>
                 <li style="">
                  <span style="font-size: small; background-color: initial;">
                   and then I realized that I couldn't open the door because of the door handle not working, I couldn't roll down the window to open it from the outside and I couldn't even unlock the locks, because from the inside of the car they are electrically powered
                  </span>
                  <br/>
                 </li>
                </ol>
                <br/>
                And that is how someone can lock themselves inside their own car
                <br/>
                <br/>
