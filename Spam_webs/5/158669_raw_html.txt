      <h2>
       The Royal beauty  Ruby Briolette engages everyone eyes with its unique glow
      </h2>
      <div class="byline">
       Posted on
        Mar. 13th, 2014        by
       <span class="author">

      <div class="entry">
       <div align="justify">
        The Ruby Briolette stones are specially designed with delicate cut and minute hand work crafted for perfection. The Ruby Briolette is the symbol for elegance and simplicity which gets the attention of
        <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="daa913b8-e5f9-4ac8-b38f-2b1a5a3d152d">
         every one
        </span>
        easily.
        <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="52607d40-c929-483a-ba16-6125a996bd7a">
         Briolette
        </span>
        was invented 800 years ago in India and the specialty of this stone is that it can be viewed at all angles. This Ruby Briolette stone allows the reflected light to pass
        <span class="GINGER_SOFTWARE_mark" id="59b8f974-0279-4888-aa1e-f6b201383571">
         through which
        </span>
        increases the refraction level of the stone. This
        <a href="http://beacab.com/ruby/ruby-beads.html" title="ruby briolette">
         <strong>
          Ruby Briolette
         </strong>
        </a>
        is most beautiful to watch on. The beauty of this stone can flatter anyone and its vibrant color makes it look Royal.
