         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Chocolate Egg Basket Tartelettes for Spring &amp; Easter Holidays
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <span class="posted-on">
           Posted on
           <a href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/chocolate-egg-baskets/" rel="bookmark">
            <time class="entry-date published" datetime="2015-04-02T22:48:43+00:00">
             2 April, 2015
            </time>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="byline">
           by
           <span class="author vcard">
            <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/author/margot/">
             Margot
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="posted_in">
           in
           <a href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/category/food/classics/">
            Classics
           </a>
          </span>
         </div>
         <!-- .entry-meta -->
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Despite complicated look those egg basket tartelettes / tartlets are quite quick and easy to make, all you need is some shortcrust pastry, condensed milk and decorations.
         </p>
         <p>
          The condensed milk filling is adaptation of
          <a href="http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/almond-cranberry-mazurek/">
           Polish mazurek recipe
          </a>
          I made last year and the idea for the baskets is based on traditional Polish Easter braided bread bakes.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="how to make tartelettes" class="alignnone wp-image-31384 size-large" data-sizes="(max-width: 499px) 100vw, 499px" height="750" sizes="499px" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-tarlettes-499x750.jpg" srcset="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-tarlettes-213x320.jpg 213w, http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-tarlettes-499x750.jpg 499w, http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-tarlettes.jpg 798w" title="Chocolate Egg Basket Tartelettes for Spring &amp; Easter Holidays" width="499"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          INGREDIENTS
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <em>
            for the shortcrust
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           3cups / 500 gplain flour
          </li>
          <li>
           bar / 250 g unsalted butter(softened)
          </li>
          <li>
           cup golden caster sugar
          </li>
          <li>
           2egg yolks
          </li>
          <li>
           1 whole egg
          </li>
          <li>
           2 tbs natural yoghurt or soured cream
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <em>
            for thecondensed milk filling
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           2 x397 g tins of sweetened condensed milk
          </li>
          <li>
           4 tbs cocoa powder 
           <em>
            optional
           </em>
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <img alt="how to make baskets from shortcrust pastry" class="alignnone wp-image-31367 size-large" data-sizes="(max-width: 750px) 100vw, 750px" height="500" sizes="(min-width: 800px) 750px, 100vw" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-easter-egg-basket-tarts-750x500.jpg" srcset="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-easter-egg-basket-tarts-320x213.jpg 320w, http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-easter-egg-basket-tarts-750x500.jpg 750w, http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-easter-egg-basket-tarts.jpg 1200w" title="Chocolate Egg Basket Tartelettes for Spring &amp; Easter Holidays" width="750"/>
         </p>

           </ins>

           </script>

         </div>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <em>
            for the topping
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           colourful fondant icing to make sugar flowers and butterfliesor ready (shop bought) sugar decorations
          </li>
          <li>
           sugar coated chocolate eggs
          </li>
          <li>
           icing sugar + hot water (to stick the decorations)
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <img alt="traditional Polish Easter bakes recipe" class="alignnone wp-image-31362 size-large" data-sizes="(max-width: 499px) 100vw, 499px" height="750" sizes="499px" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-tarts-499x750.jpg" srcset="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-tarts-213x320.jpg 213w, http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-tarts-499x750.jpg 499w, http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-tarts.jpg 798w" title="Chocolate Egg Basket Tartelettes for Spring &amp; Easter Holidays" width="499"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          EQUIPMENT
         </h3>
         <ul>
          <li>
           16  18 mini
           <a href="https://www.amazon.co.uk/Kitchen-Craft-10cm-Loose-Base/dp/B0001IWWUC/ref=as_sl_pc_ss_til?tag=coffandvani-21&amp;linkCode=w01&amp;linkId=UUQYB7P5JXSWS7MI&amp;creativeASIN=B0001IWWUC" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            fluted quiche
           </a>
           or
           <a href="https://www.amazon.co.uk/Premier-Housewares-Flute-Tart-Tin/dp/B005CVLZWE/ref=as_sl_pc_ss_til?tag=coffandvani-21&amp;linkCode=w01&amp;linkId=VH34EUXSO62GHVCV&amp;creativeASIN=B005CVLZWE" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            tartlet tins
           </a>
           (we have used 8 cm and 10 cm tins)
          </li>
          <li>
           mixing bowl
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://www.amazon.co.uk/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1634&amp;creative=19450&amp;field-keywords=plunger%20cutters&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;rh=n%3A11052681%2Ck%3Aplunger%20cutters&amp;tag=coffandvani-21&amp;url=search-alias%3Dkitchen" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            plunger cutters
           </a>
           to make sugar decorations
          </li>
          <li>
           double boiler to make thefilling
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <img alt="chocolate egg baskets for Easter" class="alignnone wp-image-31365 size-large" data-sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" height="750" sizes="500px" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-mini-tarts-500x750.jpg" srcset="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-mini-tarts-213x320.jpg 213w, http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-mini-tarts-500x750.jpg 500w, http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/chocolate-egg-basket-mini-tarts.jpg 800w" title="Chocolate Egg Basket Tartelettes for Spring &amp; Easter Holidays" width="500"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          METHOD
         </h3>
         <ol>
          <li>
           If you are making your own sugar decorations prepare them in advance, even days before.
          </li>
          <li>
           On the day of making tartelettes in a mixing bowl combine all ingredients for the shortcrust base and knead until well combined.
          </li>
          <li>
           Fill each tart tin with some dough, make sure to cover the edges properly.
          </li>
          <li>
           Use leftover dough to make sure basket handles, baby animalsor what ever you fancy we have used silicone chocolate trays to make our chicks and bunnies.
          </li>
          <li>
           Pierce couple of times, with wooden skewer, bottom of eachpastry to prevent from bulging during baking.
          </li>
          <li>
           Bake in the oven preheated to 200C (392F)for about 15  20 minutes or until the dough is golden brown.
          </li>
          <li>
           When tartelette bases areready pour condensed milk into double boiler, add also cocoa powder if using and heat over low fire constantly stirring until it will start bubbling and getting thicker.
          </li>
          <li>
           Pour filling intothe bases.
          </li>
          <li>
           While still soft and sticky decorate with basket handles, eggs, flowers,butterflies, chicks and bunnies.
          </li>
          <li>
           Use icing sugar mixed with few drops of hot water as a glue to stick butterflies to the basket handles.
          </li>
          <li>
           Leave aside to set.
          </li>
          <li>
           Once completely cooled down store in airtight containerfor a day or two.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <em>
            Makes about16  18tartelettes.
           </em>
          </strong>
         </p>

          <img alt="sugar butterflies and short crust pastry baskets" class="alignnone wp-image-31360 size-large" data-sizes="(max-width: 563px) 100vw, 563px" height="750" sizes="563px" src="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/sugarart-butterflies-563x750.jpg" srcset="http://coffee.416studios34.netdna-cdn.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/sugarart-butterflies-240x320.jpg 240w, http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/sugarart-butterflies-563x750.jpg 563w, http://www.coffeeandvanilla.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/sugarart-butterflies.jpg 900w" title="Chocolate Egg Basket Tartelettes for Spring &amp; Easter Holidays" width="563"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          NOTES
         </h3>
         <p>
          I used half of the coco powder with 1 tin of the milk and left the other half of the filling white.
         </p>
         <p>
          To make butterflies out of the icing use the cutter or plunger and then place them on curved surface, wehave used chocolate egg mould for this (pictureabove) but small bowl would work fine here as well.
         </p>
