             Now, on to MY comments: I give this Award Winning Tiny House a B+. Im mad for all the windows and the wee balconycharming. I adore the brick finish to the kitchen walls for two reasons: Ive never seen that application in another tiny home and I also grew up with brick walls in our kitchen as a child.
