        <p>
         About
         <del>
          8 months
         </del>
         a year ago, I started a new site about my new life with my soon to be husband, Mr. Vines. The site was originally started as a personal blog on Blogger. I just posted my raw thoughts about things that were happening. It began as kind of an online diary. Several months after I started the site, I decided to toy around with the idea of reinventing myself. I like LOVE the name Melissa Say What?, but I was never as successful as I wanted to be as far as reaching readers who could identify with me. Honestly, it wasnt about the address or the site name. I was posting crappy content.
        </p>
        <p>
         Part of the reason I didnt reach the success level I had hoped is because I was in a TERRIBLE relationship with someone else for a LONG time, longer than THIS SITE has existed. Then, as a result, my content was all over the place, mediocre at best (when there was actually more than just a few sentences) and quite frankly, I wasnt being myself.
        </p>
        <p>
         Things really changed when I met Travis, aka Mr. Vines, for both me and my teenage son, Nicholas. Were approaching our 1 year anniversary in about 5 weeks and its time to retire Melissa Say What?
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Without further adieu, Im pleased tointroduce you to
          <a href="http://keepingupwiththevines.com" target="_blank" title="Keeping Up With The Vines">
           Keeping Up With The Vines
          </a>
          !
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://keepingupwiththevines.com" target="_blank" title="click here to visit our new blog - Keeping Up With The Vines!">
          <img alt="Keeping Up With The Vines" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10764" height="79" src="http://melissasaywhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/rsz_1rsz_1keeping_up_with_the_vines_header_20140906-2.jpg" width="426"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         (Ugh. I cant even stand the fonts on this site any more).  Thats another thing. I spent so much time trying to focus on the appearance of the site and not enough content. I started Keeping Up With The Vines in August of 2014 and when I moved it to WordPress a few months later, I found a theme and designed my blog ONCE. Im happy with it and have no desire to change it.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im more focused on content and bringing you recipes, product reviews you can use and other information, written in my own words.the real me.somewhere I lost the Melissa Say What in me on this site.
        </p>
        <p>
         So, head over to our new site,
         <a href="http://keepingupwiththevines.com/" target="_blank" title="Keeping Up With The Vines">
          Keeping Up With The Vines
         </a>
         to meet us (again).
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre a current email subscriber of Melissa Say What?, you can subscribe to our new site
         <a href="http://eepurl.com/bbqAQ1" target="_blank" title="Subscribe to the Weekly Newsletter for Keeping Up With The Vines">
          here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Thanks for reading!
