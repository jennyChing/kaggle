       <p>
        Google has earned its title of the most incredible search engine in the whole world, second only to Rajnikanth. I mean, were used to it helping us source just about any information we want but thats usually in a more textual sense. In India, googles managed to go a few hundred steps further. Google Earth helped Brierley to locate his birthplace and reunited him with his family after 25 years!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="google1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3933" height="600" src="http://74.117.181.42/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/google1.jpg" width="800"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        His story is certainly an incredible one. He explained that he once overslept on a train journey and lost his way home. He ended up going through many hardships before an Australian couple finally adopted him. When he moved to Tasmania for business he began searching for his family again and with some luck plus a stable internet connection he managed to locate his village. He flew down to the place and knocked every door till he finally found his family. Never underestimate the power of the internet to induce Bollywood-like endings.
       </p>

        <img alt="google3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3935" height="754" src="http://74.117.181.42/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/google3.jpg" width="800"/>
