        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         The Lessons We Teach Our Kids When We Buy Fair Trade
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-02-10T22:03:48+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          February 10, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://wearethatfamily.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Kristen
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         We stood at the mirror and I brushed her blonde hair into a ponytail. She still lets me fix her hair most mornings. I told her to grab a headband from the cabinet. Its her signature school hairdo since shes been growing her bangs out.
        </p>
        <p>
         I tucked her hair beneath the new blue corded band and tied it under her ponytail. Do you know who made your headband? I asked.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://wearethatfamily.com/2015/02/lessons-teach-kids-buy-fair-trade/fullsizerender-11/" rel="attachment wp-att-18583">
          <img alt="FullSizeRender" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-18583" height="553" src="http://wearethatfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/FullSizeRender1-e1423660703523-600x553.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Who, mom? Our eyes met in the mirror. It was one of those obvious questions we dont always ask.
        </p>
        <p>
         And so I told her about the woman in Haiti who became an amputeein the earthquake that devastated the country 5 years ago. Theres
         <a href="http://www.3cordshaiti.com">
          an organization
         </a>
         who helps women with prosthetics and they teach them how to sew, I told her.
        </p>
        <p>
         My answer opened up a meaningful conversation with my 8 year old. For the next 10 minutes, I answered questions about earthquakes and amputations, prosthetics and mostly,
         <em>
          hope
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://wearethatfamily.com/2015/02/lessons-teach-kids-buy-fair-trade/img_1977_copy_grande/" rel="attachment wp-att-18570">
          <img alt="IMG_1977_copy_grande" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-18570" height="400" src="http://wearethatfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_1977_copy_grande.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I walkedover tothedrawer that holds our headbands and held up a bright turquoise one made by
         <a href="http://therefugeeproject.net">
          my refugee friends
         </a>
         from Burma and Nepal. I smiled remembering the day we prayed wed have enough yarn. I looked a little closer at the kitenge headbandfrom Rwanda, thinking about the girls at
         <a href="http://www.no41.org">
          No.41
         </a>
         who are given sewing jobs instead of the street once they age out of the orphanage they grew up in. I ended up choosing mychevron print hairband for my hair. It was made by
         <a href="http://joynindia.com">
          womeninIndia
         </a>
         , women who are no longer subject to the horrors of trafficking. Every one of these
         <a href="http://fairtradefriday.club">
          Fair Trade Friday
         </a>
         partners do much more than make cute things that provide jobs for poor women
         <em>
          they do it in the name of Jesus.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         When we buy fair trade, we do so much more than add another headband to our accessory pile or another beautiful paper bead necklace to our jewelry box. We offer more than a fair wage to a woman in an oppressed country. We get the opportunityto tell astory that is begging to be told.
        </p>
        <p>
         When need to knowthe little bags that hold our fair trade items each month means food on the table for families in Kenya, Costa Rica and Zambia. And thats why we include them- not just for reusable packaging, but for life.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="fair trade bags" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-18571" height="600" src="http://wearethatfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/fair-trade-bags-518x600.jpg" width="518"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         We all know we cant always buy fair trade. And even when we want to, its sometimes a challenging, time-consuming option.
         <em>
          But sometimes we can.
         </em>
         When we give agift that empowers a woman, we are giving much more than something nice. We are giving something deeply important and receiving something even more.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="IMG_8130" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-18575" height="600" src="http://wearethatfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_8130-e1423627076642-450x600.jpg" width="450"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The other day Terrell and I looked at a small warehouse space down the street from us that we thought might be the answer to the
         <a href="http://fairtradefriday.club">
          Fair Trade Friday
         </a>
         product that has taken over home and life. We have nearly 700 in our monthly club now and at just .33 cents a square foot, we had to consider this unexpected God-nod. When we told the property owner what we would do with the space, he asked, What does fair trade mean?
        </p>
        <p>
         It means a child wasnt chained to a chair to sew your clothes.
        </p>
        <p>
         It means a woman can feed her family.
        </p>
        <p>
         It means an amputee can work again.
        </p>
        <p>
         It means hope for the hopeless.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Because its about the story behind the new blueheadband.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          The one our kids need to hear.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://wearethatfamily.com/2015/02/lessons-teach-kids-buy-fair-trade/img_8131/" rel="attachment wp-att-18574">
          <img alt="IMG_8131" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-18574" height="600" src="http://wearethatfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/IMG_8131-e1423627344168-450x600.jpg" width="450"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Join the monthly
          <a href="http://fairtradefriday.club">
           Fair Trade Friday Club
          </a>
          ! February is full, but we are now taking names on our
          <a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1lW-zwqLvYz67BsMhBqVjFNSYU-9I3TxrtlsDcXEgAdE/viewform">
           March Wait List.
          </a>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          But we do have One-Time Trial or Gift Fair Trade bags and todayyou can get $5 off.Check out our newest options for
          <a href="http://shop.mercyhousekenya.org/product/new-fair-trade-friday-home-box">
           Home
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://shop.mercyhousekenya.org/product/new-fair-trade-friday-kid-s-box">
           Kids
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://shop.mercyhousekenya.org/product/fair-trade-friday-men-s-boxes">
           Mens
          </a>
          (with Limited Edition Mothers Day boxes coming soon!) Or buy our
          <a href="http://shop.mercyhousekenya.org/product/fair-trade-friday-gift-box">
           Original One Time Trial or Gift Box
          </a>
          with code:
         </em>
         <strong>
          4hope
         </strong>
        </p>

  extend(Mimi.Signups.EmbedValidation.prototype, {

          validEmail = /.+@.+\..+/;

      }
    },

    textFieldError: function(input) {
      input.className   = 'required invalid';
      input.placeholder = input.getAttribute('data-required-field');
    },

    removeTextFieldError: function(input) {
      input.className   = 'required';
      input.placeholder = '';
    },

    onSelectCallback: function(input) {
      if(typeof Widget == 'undefined' ||
        Widget.BasicDropdown == undefined) return;

      var dropdownEl = input.parentNode,
          instances  = Widget.BasicDropdown.instances;

      for(var i = 0; i &lt; instances.length; ++i) {
        var instance = instances[i];
        if(instance.wrapperEl == dropdownEl) {
          instance.onSelect = this.validate.bind(this);
        }
      }
    },

    updateFormAfterValidation: function() {
      this.form.className   = this.setFormClassName();
      this.submit.value     = this.submitButtonText();
      this.submit.disabled  = !this.isValid;
      this.submit.className = this.isValid ? 'submit' : 'disabled';
    },

    setFormClassName: function() {
      var name = this.form.className;

      if(this.isValid) {
        return name.replace(/\s?mimi_invalid/, '');
      } else {
        if(name.indexOf('mimi_invalid') == -1) {
          return name += ' mimi_invalid';
        } else {
          return name;
        }
      }
    },

    submitButtonText: function() {
      var invalidFields = document.querySelectorAll('.invalid'),
          text;

      if(this.isValid || invalidFields == undefined) {
        text = this.submit.getAttribute('data-default-text');
      } else {
        if(invalidFields.length &gt; 1 || invalidFields[0].className.indexOf('checkgroup') == -1) {
          text = this.submit.getAttribute('data-invalid-text');
        } else {
          text = this.submit.getAttribute('data-choose-list');
        }
      }
      return text;
    },

    submitForm: function() {
      this.formSubmitting();

      var _this = this;
      window[this.callbackName] = function(response) {
        delete window[this.callbackName];
        document.body.removeChild(script);
        _this.onSubmitCallback(response);
      };

      var script = document.createElement('script');
      script.src = this.formUrl('json');
      document.body.appendChild(script);
    },

    formUrl: function(format) {
      var action  = this.form.action,
          divider = action.indexOf('?') &gt;= 0 ? '&amp;' : '?';

      if(format == 'json') action += '.json';

      return action + divider + 'callback=' + this.callbackName + '&amp;' + serialize(this.form);
    },

    formSubmitting: function() {
      this.form.className += ' mimi_submitting';
      this.submit.value = this.submit.getAttribute('data-submitting-text');
      this.submit.disabled = true;
      this.submit.className = 'disabled';
    },

    onSubmitCallback: function(response) {
      if(response.success) {
        this.onSubmitSuccess(response.result);
      } else {
        top.location.href = this.formUrl('html');
      }
    },

    onSubmitSuccess: function(result) {
      if(result.has_redirect) {
        top.location.href = result.redirect;
      } else if(result.single_opt_in || result.confirmation_html == null) {
        this.disableForm();
        this.updateSubmitButtonText(this.submit.getAttribute('data-thanks'));
      } else {
        this.showConfirmationText(result.confirmation_html);
      }
    },

    showConfirmationText: function(html) {
      var fields = this.form.querySelectorAll('.mimi_field');

      for(var i = 0; i &lt; fields.length; ++i) {
        fields[i].style['display'] = 'none';
      }

      (this.form.querySelectorAll('fieldset')[0] || this.form).innerHTML = html;
    },

    disableForm: function() {
      var elements = this.form.elements;
      for(var i = 0; i &lt; elements.length; ++i) {
        elements[i].disabled = true;
      }
    },

    updateSubmitButtonText: function(text) {
      this.submit.value = text;
    },

    revalidateOnChange: function() {
      var fields = this.form.querySelectorAll(".mimi_field.required");

      for(var i = 0; i &lt; fields.length; ++i) {
        var inputs = fields[i].getElementsByTagName('input');
        for(var j = 0; j &lt; inputs.length; ++j) {
          inputs[j].onchange = this.validate.bind(this);
        }
      }
    }
  });

  if (document.addEventListener) {
    document.addEventListener("DOMContentLoaded",
      new Mimi.Signups.EmbedValidation);
  }
  else {
    window.attachEvent('onload', function() {
      new Mimi.Signups.EmbedValidation();
    });
  }
})(this);
         </script>
