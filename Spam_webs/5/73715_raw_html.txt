      <h2 class="title">
       Movie Review: The Worlds End  The Three Flavours Cornetto trilogy comes to an end
      </h2>
      <div class="postmeta-primary">
       <span class="meta_date">
        July 20, 2013
       </span>
       <span class="meta_categories">
        <a href="http://blazingminds.co.uk/category/reviews/film-reviews/" rel="category tag">
         Film Reviews
        </a>
       </span>
       <span class="meta_comments">
        <a href="http://blazingminds.co.uk/movie-review-the-worlds-end/#comments">
         <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="17226 http://blazingminds.co.uk/?p=17226">
          7 Comments
         </span>
        </a>
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="entry clearfix">
       <img alt="The World's End" class="alignleft featured_image wp-post-image" height="224" src="http://blazingminds.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/TheWorldsEnd-300x224.jpg" width="300"/>
       <p>
        <em>
         <strong>
          The Worlds End is the final film starring
          <a class="zem_slink external" href="http://twitter.com/simonpegg" rel="twitter nofollow" target="_blank" title="Simon Pegg">
           Simon Pegg
          </a>
          and
          <a class="zem_slink external" href="http://www.rottentomatoes.com/celebrity/nick_frost" rel="rottentomatoes nofollow" target="_blank" title="Nick Frost">
           Nick Frost
          </a>
          in theThree Flavours Cornetto trilogy(also known as theBlood and Ice Cream trilogyor theCornetto trilogy)
         </strong>
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="The World's End" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-17237" height="449" src="http://blazingminds.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/TheWorldsEnd-600x449.jpg" width="600"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Yes folks weve got to the end of the trilogy and what an exciting ride its been, the previous movies were all time classic comedies and Shaun of the Dead has to be my favourite of the previous movies, but would The Worlds End stand up and be worthy of the trilogy!
       </p>
       <h3>
        Its a Pub Crawl To The Worlds End With A Difference
       </h3>
       <p>
        So we have a group of blokes, led by Simon Pegg, reattempting an epic pub crawl during an alien invasion, what could possibly go wrong.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a class="zem_slink external" href="http://edgarwrighthere.com/" rel="homepage nofollow" target="_blank" title="Edgar Wright">
         Edgar Wright
        </a>
        has done a superb job with the movie, it has everything you could want in a film, pub crawling, mails bonding, romance, hand to hand combat (some with bar stools as boxing gloves) and the fate of the world in the hands of a bunch of drunks.
       </p>
       <p>
        Despite the slow start of the film, which is mainly introductions to the characters and bit of a back story to quest for 60 pints in 12 pubs and making it all the way to The Worlds End.
       </p>
       <p>
        Wright and Pegg have done a brilliant job with the writing and the characters Gary King (Simon Pegg) and Andrew Knightly (Nick Frost) are a change from the previous films as Frost is more the straight man and Pegg is the complete fool, this works very well.
       </p>
       <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
       <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
        <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
        </script>
        <!-- Blazing Minds Responsive -->
        <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-4686115569321190" data-ad-format="auto" data-ad-slot="9174884163" style="display:block">
        </ins>
        <script>
         (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
        </script>
       </div>
       <p>
        With its one liners and some rather funny set action pieces, the film has proven that the team behind the Cornetto Trilogy, really know how to pull the punches and have the audience laughing out loud.
       </p>
       <p>
        There were plenty of people laughing on the night I saw the film.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
         <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="312" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/7ibQvQUpMTg?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </span>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Overall Thoughts For The Worlds End
       </h3>
       <p>
        A great end to the Three Flavours Cornetto trilogy, its funny, daft, well written and also has a good soundtrack. Even if youre not a big fan of Shaun of the Dead and
        <a class="zem_slink external" href="http://www.amazon.com/Fuzz-Ultimate-Blu-ray-Simon-Pegg/dp/B002HR1W1K%3FSubscriptionId%3D0G81C5DAZ03ZR9WH9X82%26tag%3Dblazingminds-20%26linkCode%3Dxm2%26camp%3D2025%26creative%3D165953%26creativeASIN%3DB002HR1W1K" rel="amazon nofollow" target="_blank" title="Hot Fuzz (Ultimate Edition) [Blu-ray]">
         Hot Fuzz
        </a>
        , youll enjoy this one.
       </p>
       <p>
        Just sit back and enjoy the greatest Barmageddon!
       </p>

       </p>
