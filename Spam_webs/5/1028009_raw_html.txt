          <p>
           Just how big is EDM in year 2013? Try $100,000 a night big, and in some cases, upwards of $500,000  $1 million gargantuan. At least thats according to Celebrity Net Worth, who compiled a list of this years Richest Electronic DJs in the World based on all music sales, merchandise, touring revenue, licensing, endorsements, real estate, royalties and more.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span id="more-31106">
           </span>
           Holding down the top spot is
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/tiesto" target="_blank">
            Tiesto
           </a>
           , who upped his net worth from $65 million in 2012 to $75 million in 2013. Exploding into the second and third slots this year were Guy-Manuel de Homem-Christo and Thomas Bangalter of
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/daft-punk" target="_blank">
            Daft Punk
           </a>
           , who, thanks to the record-breaking success of
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/2013/05/daft-punk-random-access-memories-album-stream.html" target="_blank">
            <em>
             Random Access Memories
            </em>
           </a>
           , both checked in at a cool $60 million. Other names in the top 10 included trance heavyweights Paul van Dyk and
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/armin-van-buuren" target="_blank">
            Armin van Buuren
           </a>
           , along with the face of BBC Radio 1
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/pete-tong" target="_blank">
            Pete Tong
           </a>
           and 2001s top rated DJ, John Digweed (all of whom ranked in
           <a href="http://www.celebritynetworth.com/articles/entertainment-articles/30-richest-djs-world/" target="_blank">
            2012s top 10
           </a>
           ).
          </p>
          <p>
           Of course, it has to be noted that DJ Pauly D came in at #21 with a net worth of $15 million. Sadly, the Jersey Shore star is actually one of the most in-demand DJs out right now, booking over 130 gigs a year and raking in six figure salaries per set. Among other frequently hated names, we had
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/david-guetta" target="_blank">
            David Guetta
           </a>
           at $30 million,
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/steve-aoki" target="_blank">
            Steve Aoki
           </a>
           at $28 million,
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/deadmau5" target="_blank">
            Deadmau5
           </a>
           at $18 million,
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/skrillex" target="_blank">
            Skrillex
           </a>
           at $16 million (doubled from last year),
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/calvin-harris" target="_blank">
            Calvin Harris
           </a>
           at $13 million,
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/avicii" target="_blank">
            Avicii
           </a>
           at $12 million,
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/afrojack" target="_blank">
            Afrojack
           </a>
           at $11 million, and the recently discontinued trio of
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/swedish-house-mafia" target="_blank">
            Swedish House Mafia
           </a>
           at $10 million a pop.
          </p>
          <p>
           Some surprising names excluded from this list include mainstage regular
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/fedde-le-grand" target="_blank">
            Fedde Le Grand
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/2013/02/the-10-best-zedd-clarity-remixes.html" target="_blank">
            <em>
             Clarity
            </em>
           </a>
           megastar
           <a href="http://soundisstyle.com/tag/zedd" target="_blank">
            Zedd
           </a>
           (among others Im sure). Check out the full top 50 below and leave your thoughts in the comments.
          </p>
