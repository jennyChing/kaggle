      <h1>
       Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels
      </h1>
      <span class="post-date">
       March 22, 2011
      </span>
      <div class="postcontenter">
       <p>
        Loud patterns, bright color shades, thick textures, and quiet results. No, this is not a standardized test question asking which doesnt fit. For acoustic wall and ceiling panels, all of the above are breaking into the design scene. This week, our Top Ten list adds acoustics to your cutting edge interior projects by offering the latest in cork, breathable felt, molded polyester fibers, and wood veneers that reflect and absorb sound with no exceptions.
        <span id="more-45069">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         1.
         <a href="http://www.designerpages.com/products/1043202-100-Wool-Felt-Perforated-Panels">
          100% Wool Felt Perforated Panels
         </a>
        </strong>
        <br/>
        MANUFACTURER:
        <a href="http://www.designerpages.com/products/manufacturer/filzfelt">
         FilzFelt
        </a>
        <br/>
        PRICE: $695- 1,395
        <br/>
        <em>
         Dimensions: 34W x 96H x 3/16D, 64W x 96H x 3/16D or custom
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Wool felt panels made from 100% Merino Wool are ideal for their high quality, textured look and sound absorption. In bright colors and patterns - or in a company logo if you like - FilzFelt designs the acoustic paneling in two standard sizes with perforation to allow for natural light and air to flow through.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-45121" src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/13.jpg" title="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" width="546"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         2.
         <a href="../2010/10/13/starsilent-recycled-soundboard/">
          StarSilent Sound Absorbing Panel
         </a>
        </strong>
        <br/>
        MANUFACTURER:
        <a href="http://www.starsilent.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.starsilent.com']);">
         StarSilent
        </a>
        <br/>
        PRICE: Available upon request
        <br/>
        <em>
         Dimensions: 47.5W x 95H x 5/8D
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Looking a bit like a plain white gypsum or plastered surface, the  StarSilent absorbing panels are meant to blend in. Without showing off  its technical qualities, the ceilings can be used anywhere, on flat or  curved surfaces. It is currently one of the most environmentally  friendly applications on the planet that is made using 96% post consumer  recycled glass that is then coated with a sound permeable plaster.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-45122" src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/23.jpg" title="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" width="546"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         3.
         <a href="http://www.designerpages.com/products/65058-Sono-Wall-Panel">
          Sono
         </a>
        </strong>
        <br/>
        DESIGNER:
        <a href="http://www.ckr.se/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.ckr.se']);">
         CKR Architects
        </a>
        <br/>
        MANUFACTURER:
        <a href="http://www.designerpages.com/products/manufacturer/hightower">
         Hightower
        </a>
        <br/>
        PRICE: $1,150- 1,195
        <br/>
        <em>
         Dimensions: 47-1/4H x 23-1/2W x 3-1/8D
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Designed by the well-known architecture studio in Sweden, CKR Architects, the Sono acoustical wall panels by Hightower are both decorative and purposeful. Made from bentwood  birch wood and recyclable polyester fiber, the sound from room to room is absolutely absorbed in a modern, natural fashion.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-45123" src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/33.jpg" title="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" width="546"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         4.
         <a href="http://www.designerpages.com/products/38633-Wall-Panels">
          Acoustical Panel
         </a>
        </strong>
        <br/>
        MANUFACTURER:
        <a href="http://www.designerpages.com/products/manufacturer/decoustics">
         Decoustics
        </a>
        <br/>
        PRICE: Available upon request
        <br/>
        <em>
         Dimensions: up to 48W x 120H with 1/2- 2 thickness
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Decoustics acoustic panels are constructed from a medium density  fiberglass core and are recommended for areas that will not be subjected  to abuse or impact. These general purpose acoustical wall panels are  manufactured to suit individual project requirements including, where  required, special shapes, sizes, curves, thicknesses and finishes.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-45124" src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/43.jpg" title="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" width="546"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         5.
        </strong>
        <strong>
         <a href="../2010/10/05/9-wood-acoustic-wood-ceilings/">
          3100 Acoustic Planks
         </a>
        </strong>
        <br/>
        MANUFACTURER:
        <a href="http://9wood.com/series/index/3000" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://9wood.com']);">
         9Wood
        </a>
        <br/>
        PRICE: Available upon request
        <br/>
        <em>
         Dimensions: 7-9/16W x 4-8L
        </em>
        <em>
         x 3/4D
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Using thin linear grooves inspired by Alvar Aaltos use of wood  ceilings in modern architecture, the 3100 Acoustic Plank wood ceiling  offers acoustics to a space using engineered and veneered wood  composites. This refined appearance can be used on the ceiling or walls  to absorb sound and create a warm environment.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-45125" src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/53.jpg" title="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" width="546"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         6.
         <a href="http://azure-usa.com/miscellaneous/scrunch" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://azure-usa.com']);">
          Scrunch
         </a>
        </strong>
        <br/>
        MANUFACTURER:
        <a href="http://www.designerpages.com/products/manufacturer/azure">
         Azure
        </a>
        <br/>
        PRICE: $135/unit
        <br/>
        <em>
         Dimensions: 22.8W x 22.8H x 3.1D
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Lightweight sound absorbers  in the upper frequency range (500 Hz and  above), the Scrunch is a beautiful way to eliminate voices and sounds  from other rooms. Made from a molded polyester fiber in a beautiful 3-D  design, they are offered in grey, anthracite, and off-white shades.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-45126" src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/63.jpg" title="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" width="546"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         7.
         <a href="http://www.designerpages.com/products/1103932-Touch-Wall-Panels-new-colors">
          Touch Wall Panels
         </a>
        </strong>
        <br/>
        MANUFACTURER:
        <a href="http://www.nunoerin.com/">
         NunoErin
         <br/>
        </a>
        PRICE: $100/sq. ft
        <br/>
        <em>
         Dimensions: 12 x 12, 18 x 18, and 24 x 24 sizes
         <br/>
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Modular, interactive panels that can be touched to shift colors in response to body heat, the Touch Wall Panels by NunoErin use an acoustic foam core to control the sound. Equipped with a magnetic hanging system, the panels can be moved and shifted around to your hearts content. Choose between green that turns to yellow, purple to pink, orange to yellow, black to green and blue along with custom color options for larger projects.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-45127" src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/73.jpg" title="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" width="546"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         8.
         <a href="http://3rings.designerpages.com/?s=acoustic+panel">
          Cork Surface
         </a>
        </strong>
        <br/>
        DESIGNER:
        <a href="http://www.yemiawosile.co.uk/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.yemiawosile.co.uk']);">
         Yemi Awosile
        </a>
        <br/>
        PRICE: Available upon request
        <br/>
        <em>
         Dimensions: varies
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        A London-based designer who has mastered the naturally sound-absorbing art of cork, Yemi Awosile was a subject of our blogging last year who received massive praise for her cork surface that appears to be a flexible panel of mosaic-style cork tiles. Lightweight and laser-cut, the checkered cork surface is made using a woven elastane and re-composited cork that comes right from the wine industry.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-45128" src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/83.jpg" title="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" width="546"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         9.
        </strong>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.designerpages.com/products/64020-Soundwaves">
          Soundwave Flo
         </a>
        </strong>
        <br/>
        DESIGNER:
        <a href="http://www.karimrashid.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.karimrashid.com']);">
         Karim Rashid
        </a>
        <br/>
        MANUFACTURER:
        <a href="http://www.offecct.se/_eng/produkter.asp?FAM=5">
         Offecct
         <br/>
        </a>
        PRICE: Available upon request
        <br/>
        <em>
         Dimensions: 23H x 23W x 2.35D
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Created using a molded recyclable polyester fiber, the lightweight  Soundwave Flo panels come in four shades  that range from hot fuchsia to  plain white. Designed by Karim Rashid for the Swedish company, Offecct,  the wave-like design of Flo dampens frequencies beyond 500 Hz and is  ideal for the contemporary dweller.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-45129" src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/93.jpg" title="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" width="546"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         10.
         <a href="http://www.designerpages.com/products/50467-Texaa-Panels">
          Texaa Panels
         </a>
        </strong>
        <br/>
        MANUFACTURER:
        <a href="http://www.designerpages.com/products/manufacturer/robin-reigi">
         Robin Reigi
        </a>
        <br/>
        PRICE: Available upon request
        <br/>
        <em>
         Dimensions: 2 x 4, 4 x 4, or 4 x 8; all are 2 thick
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Using the knit fabric, Aeria, with acoustic transparency woven within  and aluminum framing, Robin Reigi created the Strato and Stereo  acoustic ceiling panels. In single units of three standard sizes, you  can mix and match a variety of colors to match your interior projects  while keeping noise at bay.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-45130" src="http://3rings.designerpages.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/102.jpg" title="Top Ten: Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels" width="546"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Hope you enjoyed our roundup of the Top Ten Fashion-Forward Acoustic Panels!
       </p>
