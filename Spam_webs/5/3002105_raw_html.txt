            Summer is here, let's fire this thing up!
           </a>
          </span>
         </h2>
         <h5 class="date-header font-dateheader">
          <span class="entry-date">
           <a href="http://cleosday.grouchypuppy.com/2009/06/summer-is-here-lets-fire-this-thing-up.html">
            06/14/2009
           </a>
          </span>
         </h5>
         <div class="entry-body font-entrybody">
          <p>
           <a href="http://grouchypuppy.typepad.com/.a/6a01053649b34f970b0115701aff9b970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
            <img alt="DSC00603" border="0" class="at-xid-6a01053649b34f970b0115701aff9b970c image-full " src="http://grouchypuppy.typepad.com/.a/6a01053649b34f970b0115701aff9b970c-800wi" title="DSC00603"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Our grill may be on its last legs but still I am waiting for PL to fire it up and grill some meat, then let a little drop or hand me a taste - covertly. My PL might spoil me
           <span style="text-decoration: line-through;">
            a bit
           </span>
           a lot and my female is always -
           <em>
            all up in my grill
           </em>
           - about my eating and snacking too much. Grrrr.
          </p>
