          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Frequently Asked Questions:
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           1. Q: I cant skin the bear / The Bears always kill me / etc.Why?
          </p>
          <p>
           A: Short answer: Its a trap.
           <br/>
           Long answer: Its deliberate, based on the original source video from Blizzards April fools joke. Their purpose is to trick you once, and be avoided essentially. You cannot kill them.
          </p>
          <p>
           2. Q: I keep dying when I try to fight ______ boss, my health just keeps draining! Whats happening?
          </p>
          <p>
           A: You are fighting the bosses out of order! Minus 10 DKP!
           <br/>
           For help with this, find a guide online about the actual Molten Core raid dungeon, a map with boss names and an order should steer you straight.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
          </p>
          <p>
           3. Q: What happened to the Experience and Timbermaw Rep bars?
          </p>
          <p>
           A: Well, I debated with myself for a long time about whether or not they should be used. In hindsight, it may
           <br/>
           have been an easier way of indicating how capable you were of fighting the next boss which is now expressed in the final game as the fixed boss order (fight out of order and you cannot win) but it really boiled down to me asking myself would an Atari be capable of tracking that? there is no such thing as an Atari game with game-saving capabilities to my knowledge, and while someone may be able to do such a thing today, it didnt seem very authentic to me. And, at the risk of being un-authentic to the original Blizzard video, I left it out.
          </p>
          <p>
           4. Q: Why would you do something like this?
          </p>
          <p>
           A: For the fun of it, honestly. I am a big WoW fanatic and this is something of a loveletter to Blizzard for their
           <br/>
           fantastic games over the years. Also, I wanted to see if I could raise any money through donations for my upcoming wedding (on 11/30/08). But mostly, I wanted to learn and become more proficient with Multimedia Fusion 2 Developer, (and of course, become an internet superstar. ) 
          </p>
          <p>
           5. Q: I have a great idea for a game!! Will you make it??
          </p>
          <p>
           A: Well, I cant guaruntee anything. But if you email me at Endymian@gamersuniversity.com we can chat about what you have in mind. I have a lot of great ideas already and not enough time as it is though!
           <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.gamersuniversity.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           6. Q: Do you actually play World of Warcraft?
          </p>
          <p>
           A: Yep, you can find yours truly on the Arathor server (used to be on Argent Dawn, but I moved recently)
           <br/>
           My alliance paladins name is, of course, Endymian. This is not my only character, but it is my only character named Endymian, so if youre talking to an Endymian on another servernot the real Slim Shady.
           <br/>
           Edit 2/4/2010: Ive moved my Paladin, Endymian, to Ravencrest. Not on Arathor anymore. Come harass me there!
          </p>
          <p>
           7. Q: This was posted at _________ forums by someone called Jemak, who is that?
          </p>
          <p>
           A: Its me. Old name that I havent been able to or gotten around to updating. I go by Endymian now at 95% of places I go. Same person.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
          </p>

           Keep em coming. Send questions, comments, or whatever to Endymian@gamersuniversity.com Thanks!
