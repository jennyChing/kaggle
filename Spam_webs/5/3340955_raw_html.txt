         <p>
          Halloween is coming soon, but you still have not found a beautiful costume for your little princess? There is one costume that is very interesting to try to offer to your little angel. Floral print shift dress will bring your daughter to be a pretty simple princess that dreamed of by every little girl in the world. This gorgeous dress will also be in love if it is equipped with a sling bag with a beautiful and cute floral too.
         </p>
         <p>
          Halloween of course can not be separated from the totality of which showed that for the day that he was not himself, but the character he was portraying. Therefore, make-up and accessories are in use there must be total. With the concept of simple princess who wears a floral print shift dress, make-up done on your little princesses do not be too flashy, but can naturally be added the beautiful mask with feathers beautiful there in his eyes. And also pretty magic wand like a flower with the ability to beautify the world. With such you may also have instilled a sense of affection for the environment on your daughter yourself early on.
         </p>
         <p>
          As for the shoes, as they may in this event will occur romp candy scramble among children for beautify the floral print shift dress and also ensure that your child does not fall while running you can put him with beautiful boots with motifs and colors the same as the dress she was wearing. For decoration you might be able to give her a digital clock and beautiful flowers for her strawberry ring. Then for the necklace, you can give a small necklace with a beautiful pendant key that may be made of silver in anticipation of losing a lot of money when a necklace belonging to your child lost in the play.
