        <header>
         <h1 class="title entry-title">
          7 of The Best Wildlife Experiences in The World
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="post-meta">
         <span class="small">
          By
         </span>
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/author/abi/" rel="author" title="Posts by Abi King">
            Abi King
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="small">
          on
         </span>
         <abbr class="date time published updated" title="2015-03-09T00:07:21+0100">
          March 9, 2015
         </abbr>
         <span class="small">
          in
         </span>
         <span class="categories">
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/category/fresh/7-of-the-best/" title="View all items in 7 of The Best">
           7 of The Best
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/category/fresh/" title="View all items in Show Me">
           Show Me
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <section class="entry">
         <input data-frizzlyhovercontainer="" data-frizzlypostcontainer="" data-frizzlyposttitle="7 of The Best Wildlife Experiences in The World" data-frizzlyposturl="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/7-of-the-best-wildlife-experiences-in-the-world/" type="hidden" value=""/>
         <p>
          <img alt="7 best wildlife experiences" class="alignleft size-large wp-image-26616" height="900" src="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/7-best-wildlife-600x900.png" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Were back with the latest
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/category/fresh/7-of-the-best/" target="_blank">
           7 of the best series,
          </a>
          this time taking a look at
          <strong>
           7 of the best wildlife experiences that the world has to offer,
          </strong>
          from far away to close to home. Not since I wrote
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/7-of-the-best-travel-photo-blogs/" target="_blank">
           7 of the best travel photo blogs
          </a>
          have I struggled so much with the pick. But thats the beauty of writing 7 of the best, right? I can always come back with seven moreespecially after
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/off-to-the-galapagos-islands-plus-amazing-travel-prizes/" target="_blank">
           this Galapagos trip
          </a>
          and my recent adventures with the
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/the-orangutans-of-borneo-rainforest/" target="_blank">
           king of the swingers
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          So, this shortlist highlights the unusual and the beautiful of the big wide world out there. Enjoy!
         </p>
         <h2>
          7 of the Best Wildlife Experiences
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="Sukau Rainforest Lodge Night Safariin Borneo from @insidetravellab" class="alignleft size-large wp-image-25605" height="400" src="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Sukau-Rainforest-Lodge-32-600x400.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           1) Take a night safari in Borneo
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Its not every day you find a baby crocodile swishing through the water by your side. But it is something that happens every night, or thereabouts, on
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/borneo-night-safari/" target="_blank">
           a night safari from
           <strong>
            Borneos
           </strong>
           <strong>
            Sukau Rainforest Lodge.
           </strong>
          </a>
          Up above, amid the branches and the shadows and the tricks of the mindare monkeys. Many, many monkeys.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Leopard on safari at Makanyane Safari Lodge in Madikwe South Africa - Great for safari from @iinsidetravellab" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-22348" height="438" src="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/InsideTravelLab1310146.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           2) Catch the Big Five in South Africa
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          The plane trembles like a bumblebee with vertigo as it lilts and lifts through the sky cavity around Joburg. The ground, a blurred marble of ochre and olive, continues on. And on. And on.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its only a short hop by air from Johannesburg to the
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/makanyane-safari-lodge-review-south-africa/" target="_blank">
           Madikwe Game Reserve
          </a>
          in African terms, but of course, for those of us from smaller isles, its hard to believe just how much space there is out there on the ground.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_12843" style="width: 610px">
          <img alt="Reef sharks one of the best wildlife experiences in the Maldives from @insidetravellab" class="size-large wp-image-12843" height="403" src="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Maldives-wildlife013-600x403.jpg" width="600"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Were worse
          </p>
         </div>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           3) Swim withsharks in The Maldives
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Sometimes, you have to work hard to see wildlife. And when I say sometimes, I mean most of the time. You have to creep past snakes in the darkness and risk frostbite
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/safari-tiger/">
           to glimpse a tiger.
          </a>
          You have to play basketball with the base of your spine along a rickety dirt track
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/photo-friday-a-zebra-in-the-moremi-game-reserve-botswana/">
           to see a zebra, lion or rhino.
          </a>
          You have to hike for days to see a condor,
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/kayaking-in-alaska-alone-in-the-wild/">
           paddle for miles to see a seal
          </a>
          and at the absolute minimum jump into the sea to
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/turtle-safaris-saltwater-scepticism/">
           eyeball a turtle.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Not so in the Maldives, a chain of one thousand or so islands with a reputation for showing guests the luxurious side of life. The diving grabs all the headlines, requiring paperwork to prove that you know how to breathe, but you dont even need to go that far.
         </p>
         <p>
          You can swim with sharks at the shore. Or
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/deckchair-safari-maldvies/" target="_blank">
           sit back on a deckchair and watch
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Seeing wild puffins on Skomer Island Pembrokeshire from @insidetravellab" class="alignleft size-large wp-image-18298" height="390" src="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Puffin-PemLab-600x390.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           4) Admire the Puffins on Skomer Island, Wales
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Roam around the raw and rugged cliffs of Britains only Coastal National Park in Pembrokeshire, West Wales. The Puffins on
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/a-puffin-on-skomer-island/">
           <strong>
            Skomer Island
           </strong>
          </a>
          are so beautiful youll want to get up close and give them a
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/tooty-down-for-a-cwtch-and-other-welsh-words-of-love/">
           <strong>
            cwtch
           </strong>
          </a>
          (actually, probably best to just admire them from afar).
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_2896" style="width: 650px">
          <img alt="The stunning Okavango Delta in Botswana from @insidetravellab" class="size-full wp-image-2896" height="427" src="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/okavango-delta-pic-resized.jpg" width="640"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           The Okavango Delta From Above
          </p>
         </div>
         <h4>
          5) Arrange a Mokoro Safari in Botswanas Okavango Delta
         </h4>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/okavango-delta-safari-botswana/">
           <strong>
            The Okavango Delta in Botswana
           </strong>
          </a>
          (the largest delta in the world) is home to elephants, impala, giraffe, hippos and warthogs aplenty. Its watery nature means that there are virtually no roads  so the only ways of getting around are by boat or by plane. Both versions will give you a truly amazing experience: either
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/okavango-delta-safari-botswana/">
           <strong>
            up close with the reeds and the animals in a traditional
           </strong>
           <em>
            <strong>
             mokoro
            </strong>
           </em>
          </a>
          , or
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/the-okavango-delta-from-the-air/">
           <strong>
            gazing down at the swirls of the delta from the sky
           </strong>
           .
          </a>
         </p>
         <h4>
          <img alt="Spectacular sighting of a whale from @insidetravellab in Alaska" class="alignleft size-large wp-image-26619" height="373" src="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Whale-tail-from-@insidetravellab-600x373.jpg" width="600"/>
         </h4>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           6) Go whale watching in Alaska
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          The tail, that majestic symbol of all the monsters of the deep, rises, pauses, holds, waits, lingers above the water before plunging down again, throwing up a signature of spray. Very few animals have the ability to stir emotions in us with their everyday movements. The
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/whale-watching-in-alaska/">
           <strong>
            humpback whale
           </strong>
          </a>
          is undoubtedly part of this very special group.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Buffalo and yachts hang out together on the beaches of Hong Kong - from @insidetravellab Kong" class="alignleft size-large wp-image-16288" height="400" src="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Buffalo-Beach-Hong-Kong004-600x400.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           7) Hang out with Buffalo in Hong Kong
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          On
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/the-beach-with-the-buffalo-hong-kong/">
           <strong>
            Lantau Island
           </strong>
          </a>
          at the mouth of the Pearl River, the buffalo come to sleep. Twilight drifts over the
          <a href="http://www.insidethetravellab.com/the-road-sort-of-a-little-bit-toward-mandalay/">
           <strong>
            neon lights, the gleaming spires and the synthetic shopping malls elsewhere in Hong Kong.
           </strong>
          </a>
          But in this part, at least, twilight signals a change of pace. A string of fairy lights sums up the illumination on the beach, where wild buffalo scuff the sand and settle down as the light fades for a snooze with an oceanfront view.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h3>
          So, what do you think? Which wildlife experiences should make the best in the world list?
         </h3>
         <p>
