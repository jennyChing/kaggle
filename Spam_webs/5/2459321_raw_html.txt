     <h2>
      PSYCH 9A - Psychology Fundamentals
     </h2>
     <p>
      <b>
       PSYCH 9A: Psychology Fundamentals (Winter 2014, UC Irvine)
      </b>
      . Instructor: Professor Michael D'Zmura. This course is designed to provide freshman Psychology majors with an in-depth survey of general psychology. Topics include biological bases of 
				behavior, sensation, perception, cognition, development, personality, psychopathology, and social psychology.
      <i>
       PSYCH 9A
      </i>
      discusses topics: communication and control using brain waves, genetic and evolutionary roots of behavior, brain and nervous system, 
				sensation, color opponency, perception, and consciousness.
     </p>
