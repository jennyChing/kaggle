        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Wet Black Bean Burritos
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-05-20T07:33:31+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          May 20, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://lifecurrents.dw2.net/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Debi
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://lifecurrents.dw2.net/wet-black-bean-burritos/#comments">
           8 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         These Wet Black Bean Burritos make a great Brown Bag lunch! Theyre super easy to make, and really yummy! They would also make a fabulous Meatless Monday dinner dish!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifecurrents.dw2.net/wet-black-bean-burritos/wet-black-bean-burritos-horizontal/" rel="attachment wp-att-14481">
          <img alt="Wet Black Bean Burritos make a great Brown Bag lunch or Meatless Monday dinner! by Life Currents" class="aligncenter wp-image-14481 size-full" height="433" src="http://lifecurrents.dw2.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Wet-Black-Bean-Burritos-horizontal.jpg" title="Wet Black Bean Burritos make a great Brown Bag lunch or Meatless Monday dinner! by Life Currents" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I often like to make Mexican bowls for my husband for lunch: a little rice (this
         <a href="http://lifecurrents.dw2.net/chipotle-seasoned-rice/" target="_blank">
          Chipotle Seasoned Rice
         </a>
         was delicious with them!), the main ingredient, some salsa. So, this totally works. I place the rice in the bottom of the
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00LN810PM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00LN810PM&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=lifecurr-20&amp;linkId=4W6NRGQ226RX3NUU">
          glass to-go container
         </a>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=lifecurr-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B00LN810PM" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
         , and top it with the Wet Black Bean Burrito. Voila! Mexican lunch!
        </p>
        <p>
         Use your favorite salsa for this dish. Remember if youre making this for brown-bagging, the desk jockey probably doesnt want anything too hot. Its no good to sit at your desk with your brow sweating and your tongue tingling. But,
         <a href="http://lifecurrents.dw2.net/double-salsas/" target="_blank">
          homemade salsa
         </a>
         or jarred salsa, its going to be great either way.
        </p>
        <p>
         When I processed the black beans in the food processor I found that they stuck a little, so I added a spoonful or two of sour cream to loosen the mixture up a bit. Ill definitely be making this one again, and I think Id like to try adding a can of green chilies and some chopped zucchini to the filling before baking.
        </p>
        <p>
         Make your own Chipotle Paste, its super easy.
         <a href="http://lifecurrents.dw2.net/chipotle-paste/" target="_blank">
          Youll find the instructions here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lifecurrents.dw2.net/wet-black-bean-burritos/wet-black-bean-burritos-vert/" rel="attachment wp-att-14484">
          <img alt="Wet Black Bean Burritos make a great Brown Bag lunch or Meatless Monday dinner! by Life Currents" class="aligncenter wp-image-14484 size-full" height="650" src="http://lifecurrents.dw2.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Wet-Black-Bean-Burritos-vert.jpg" title="Wet Black Bean Burritos make a great Brown Bag lunch or Meatless Monday dinner! by Life Currents" width="433"/>
         </a>
        </p>

          <div class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-section" yumprintsection="0">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Ingredients
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-ingredients">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="0">
             1 (15-ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="1">
             1 teaspoon chipotle paste
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="2">
             1 teaspoon chili powder
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="3">
              teaspoon cumin
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="4">
              cup sour cream
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="5">
             1 cup frozen corn, thawed
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="6">
             4 (6 to 8-inch) flour tortillas
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="7">
             1 cup salsa
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="8">
              cup shredded Pepper Jack cheese
            </li>
           </ol>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-method-section" yumprintsection="1">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Instructions
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-methods" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="0">
             Preheat oven to 350 F.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="1">
             Place half of beans, chipotle paste, chili powder, and cumin in the bowl of a food processor; process until finely chopped, scraping down the sides of the bowl a few times. Add a little of the sour cream if necessary to loosen the mixture up.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="2">
             Stir together the pureed black beans, remaining whole beans, sour cream, and corn in a large bowl.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="3">
             Spoon  cup bean mixture down the center of each tortilla. Roll up tortillas; place, seam side down, in an 11 x 7-inch baking dish coated with cooking spray. Spread salsa over tortillas; sprinkle with cheese. Cover and bake at 350 F for 20 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
            </li>
           </ol>
