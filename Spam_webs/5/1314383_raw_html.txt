       <img alt="Espresso la chaise  caf par Giuseppe Formica" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25162" height="525" src="http://www.blog-espritdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Espresso-la-chaise-caf-Giuseppe-Formica-design-chair-italie-blog-espritdesign-1.jpg" title="Espresso la chaise  caf par Giuseppe Formica" width="700"/>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <strong>
        Giuseppe Formica
       </strong>
       , jeune designer italien exil au Danemark nous prsente
       <strong>
        Espresso
       </strong>
       , une chaise  destination des bars et restaurants.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       Une structure lgre en htre stratifi permettant en un seul geste de la faire tenir en quilibre sur une petite table et permettre le nettoyage du sol. Ralis en collaboration avec la marque
       <em>
        Midform
       </em>
       , sa finesse et facilit de montage / transport sont galement trs intressantes.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <img alt="Espresso la chaise  caf par Giuseppe Formica" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25168" height="525" src="http://www.blog-espritdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Espresso-la-chaise-caf-Giuseppe-Formica-design-chair-italie-blog-espritdesign-7.jpg" title="Espresso la chaise  caf par Giuseppe Formica" width="700"/>
       <img alt="Espresso la chaise  caf par Giuseppe Formica" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25169" height="525" src="http://www.blog-espritdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Espresso-la-chaise-caf-Giuseppe-Formica-design-chair-italie-blog-espritdesign-8.jpg" title="Espresso la chaise  caf par Giuseppe Formica" width="700"/>
       <img alt="Espresso la chaise  caf par Giuseppe Formica" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-25170" height="525" src="http://www.blog-espritdesign.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Espresso-la-chaise-caf-Giuseppe-Formica-design-chair-italie-blog-espritdesign-9.jpg" title="Espresso la chaise  caf par Giuseppe Formica" width="700"/>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       
       <em>
        Espresso is a chair for caf, particularly addressed to waiters. Through a simple function, which ease the movement of putting the chair on the table for cleaning, the profile changes radically. The challenge is to have a structure which is light but strong enough, and this is possible thanks to the techniques of laminated beech veneer, that give an excellent resistance. The final surface treatment is soaping, to preserve a natural look.
       </em>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       <em>
        Project realized in collaboration with Midform, danish furniture supplier and manufacturer.
       </em>
       
      </p>
