     Mumbai is a city today has become synonymous with money, glamour, bollywood and fast paced life. These very characteristics have been part of Mumbais folklore since time immemorial and have created enigmatic aura of the city around the world. But, defying these clichd perceptions are plethora of art galleries in Mumbai. Yes, there are indeed plenty of art galleries in Mumbai.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     <div style="text-align:center">
      <img alt="Art Galleries in Mumbai" height="265" src="http://im.hunt.in/cg/mumbai/City-Guide/Art%20Galleries%20in%20Mumbai.jpg" title="Art galleries are living testimony of the fact that citizens of Mumbai do have a fair share of interest in all the important forms of art like painting, sculpture and handicraft" width="540"/>
     </div>
     <br/>
     In fact, some of the art galleries located in Mumbai are counted amongst the best in the entire country. These galleries also defies the notion that Mumbai people aren't that "Art-Appreciating" crowd and cities like
     <a href="http://www.delhionline.in/" target="_blank">
      Delhi
     </a>
     and
     <a href="http://www.kolkataonline.in/" target="_blank">
      Kolkata
     </a>
     are much better in that matte. But not many know that Mumbai houses some of the best lot of amateur and mixed material artist in the entire country.
