          <h1>
           World's Most Expensive Watch Ever, Will Cost $6.5 Million
          </h1>
          <p>
           Posted by
           <a href="http://stylecrave.com/author/alexion/" rel="author" title="Posts by Alex Ion">
            Alex Ion
           </a>
          </p>
         </header>
         <div class="post-content">
          <p>
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1713" height="550" src="http://stylecrave.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/10/vacheron_constatin_watch_commission.jpg" width="550"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           An unnamed European billionaire has authorized Swiss watchmaker Vacheron Constantin to design a one of a kind timepiece that will cost the buyer a whooping
           <em>
            $6.5 million
           </em>
           , making it the worlds most expensive watch ever to have been created to date.
          </p>
          <p>
           The watch is expected to take years to complete and after reading the feature list, we can understand why. This timepiece will fit a calendar, a start chart, a celestial annual calendar, a tourbillon regulator, a sunrise and sunset indicator and a hell of a lot more into its display. The buyer has definitely found an innovative way of putting his pouring millions into good use, hasnt he!? Wed love to meet this fucker in a dark alley [via
           <a href="http://www.affluentpageluxuryindex.com/news-and-noteworthy/tycoon-commissions-65-million-vacheron-constantin/" target="_blank">
            AffluentPageLuxuryIndex
           </a>
           ]
          </p>
