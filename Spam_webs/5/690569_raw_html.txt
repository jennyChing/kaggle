        <p>
         Gridiron, smack talk, and beers are flying at 10am in Surry Hills. I am sitting writing this column among a group of guys with a beer to the right of my laptop and breakfast nachos to the left.
        </p>
        <p>
         I'm clearly ignoring health and wellness on this Super Bowl Monday. It's noon somewhere in the world, but I'm still glad I went for an early morning jog.
        </p>
        <p>
         Seeing the brawned-up football players on screen leads me to ask the group about the latest tribe to join the man jungle. "What do you guys think of the death of the metrosexual and the birth of the 'spornosexual'?"
        </p>
        <figure class="media media--portrait-photo">
         <img alt="Adult star James Deen scrubs up well in clothes." src="/content/dam/images/1/3/5/2/9/0/image.related.articleLeadNarrow.300x0.1352xj.png/1423006508721.jpg" title=""/>
         <figcaption class="media__caption">
          Adult star James Deen scrubs up well in clothes.
          <cite>
           Photo: Pascal Le Segretain
          </cite>
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.theage.com.au/executive-style/fitness/boot-camp">
           <b>
            More Boot Camp blogs from fitness expert Michael Jarosky
           </b>
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         The ensuing chat flows like this:
        </p>
        <h3>
         What is a spornosexual?
        </h3>
        <p>
         Metrosexuals are last year's news. The spornosexual is the
         <a href="http://www.theage.com.au/executive-style/culture/forget-metro-man-meet-the-spornosexual-20140612-39ytd.html">
          fusion of three different types
         </a>
         - metrosexuals, porn stars, and sports icons. A spornosexual aspires to obtain a body like Maroon 5's Adam Levine, meets adult performer James Deen, meets soccer legend Cristiano Ronaldo. The 'look' is more important than the quality of air being breathed - shaved, tattooed, body beautiful, with perfect hair the norm.
        </p>
        <figure class="media media--portrait-photo">
         <img alt="Adam Levine rocks spornosexual appeal." src="/content/dam/images/1/3/5/2/7/k/image.related.articleLeadNarrow.300x0.1352xj.png/1423006509170.jpg" title=""/>
         <figcaption class="media__caption">
          Adam Levine rocks spornosexual appeal.
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <h3>
         How's their health?
        </h3>
        <p>
         Sporno guys are fit. Everything that goes into the body is pure, organic, and probably Paleo. Ask a sporno about carbohydrates and he'll laugh so hard his six-pack will bust through his Ed Hardy t-shirt.
        </p>
        <p>
         The sporno body is a temple with no room for deviant delicacies such as bread, rice, or chocolate. The sporno exercise regime includes plenty of weights and hypertrophy training, yet they would also be able to pull off four rounds of a CrossFit-esque WOD (that's Workout of the Day) that would mirror this:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          100 Skipping Ropes (double unders)
         </li>
         <li>
          20 Pull-Ups
         </li>
         <li>
          50 Push-Ups
         </li>
         <li>
          20 Kettlebell Swings
         </li>
         <li>
          20 Box Jumps
         </li>
         <li>
          20 Burpees
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Any spornosexual that can pull off that workout is without question, physically fit.
        </p>
        <h3>
         What's good about it?
        </h3>
        <p>
         If spornos are doing it naturally, they are healthy and balanced. They build strength, move with speed and power, and can last the distance. From plyometrics to stair running, to heavy lifting, spornos can do it all.
        </p>
        <p>
         Aesthetically speaking, they have bodies that are to be admired because they aren't massive, yet they aren't skinny either. They look good in workout gear, and they fill out a tailored suit or jeans and a t-shirt with masculine confidence.
        </p>
        <h3>
         What's bad?
        </h3>
        <p>
         Some spornos would rather build self-admiration and hold a selfie stick than the hand of a cool girl. To decrease caloric intake, they'll choose party drugs at a dance festival festival over a beach beer, Aussie style.
        </p>
        <p>
         If they take shortcuts, their refrigerators are more likely to be filled with protein shakes, 'roids and HGH instead of salmon, steak, and spinach.
        </p>
        <h3>
         My take?
        </h3>
        <p>
         I'm at an age where my arsenal to meet women is a healthy body, some French brie, a bottle of pinot noir, and my forgettable sense of humour. The
         <a href="http://www.theage.com.au/executive-style/fitness/boot-camp/the-problem-with-being-awesome-20150120-12tglz.html">
          Stereosonic-stereotype sporno body
         </a>
         is a bit too much, and cheating your way to a fit body ain't healthy.
        </p>
        <p>
         Maybe I'm just getting older and all this emo-to-metro-to-sporno trend stuff is best left for the younger generation. More importantly, I wonder if guys are going sporno for themselves, for the women/men they want to attract, or even for their workout buddies to admire/envy?
        </p>
        <p>
         Something about spornos is a little too
         <i>
          Jersey Shore
         </i>
         and not enough Bronte Shore.
        </p>
        <p>
         And just as I think the sporno is the all the rage, my good mate Jeffnudges me and says, "Mate, the lumbersexual is next."
        </p>
        <p>
         That's well-groomed style from the rugged woods that is taking over men's closets. Oh my.
        </p>
        <p>
         Okay. My Super Bowl nachos have been down an hour, and I've gotta go. I REALLY gotta go. The last health message for this column is: don't eat breakfast nachos unless the bathroom is close by.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Men, do you want the sporno look? Ladies, do you like it?
         </b>
        </p>

         </b>
        </p>
