     <div class="artist-overview__about-wrap">
      <h1 class="heading--large artist-overview__name">
       Andrew Stellitano
      </h1>
      <div class="artist-overview__about-copy">
       <p>
        Andrew Stellitano is a designer and art director based in London, whose conceptually driven work draws on his fascination with exploring communication and narrative through and across disciplines. Andrew's distinct aesthetic is sensorially engaging and often otherworldly, with an emphasis on tactility through his experimental use of materials and processes.
       </p>
       <p>
        Clients: Absolut, AnOther Magazine, Audi, Nike, Selfridges, Stella Artois, Vogue, The Victoria &amp; Albert Museum, Wallpaper* magazine and Warner Bros.
       </p>
