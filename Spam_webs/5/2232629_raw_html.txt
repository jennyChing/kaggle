           <a href="http://www.cafepress.com/beartoons/9326201" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.cafepress.com/beartoons/9326201', 'first tshirt design']);" target="_blank">

            So here it is
           </a>
           along with other holiday designs. So when it is time for Oktoberfest whether in Munich or Zinzinnati (Cincinnati), it is time to Grab Your Sausage and Have Fun Because what is funnier than a man in Lederhosen grabbing an oversized chunk of meat.
          </p>
          <p>
           Of course saying Wienerschnitzel instead of Sausage would have been funnier but they are two completely different things.
          </p>
          <p>
           In addition to my cafepress store, I also added this design to my

            Society6 site
