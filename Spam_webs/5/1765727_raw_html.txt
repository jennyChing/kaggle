           <h1 class="update__title update__title--large">
            Stormtroopers at sci-fi funeral for Star Wars fan
           </h1>
          </div>
         </header>
         <figure class="update__hero-image">
          <div class="fluid-media-wrapper">
           <div class="fluid-media-wrapper__media">
            <span class=" responsive-image" data-alt="Storm Trooper Funeral" data-height="null" data-picture="true" data-width="null">
             <span data-src="http://news.images.itv.com/image/file/591759/article_update_img.jpg">
             </span>
             <span data-media="(min-width: 321px)" data-src="http://news.images.itv.com/image/file/591759/stream_img.jpg">
             </span>
             <!--[if (lt IE 9) &amp; (!IEMobile)]&gt;
    &lt;span data-src="http://news.images.itv.com/image/file/591759/article_update_img.jpg"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
  &lt;![endif]-->
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Storm Trooper Funeral" src="http://news.images.itv.com/image/file/591759/article_update_img.jpg"/>
             </noscript>
            </span>
           </div>
          </div>
          <figcaption class="update__hero-image__caption">
           <cite class="update__hero-image__cite">
            Photo: Wales News Service
           </cite>
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <div class="item__content-block item__content-block--markdown">
          <p>
           A Star Wars fan had an out-of-this world send off at his funeral with an honour guard of Stormtroopers.
          </p>
          <p>
           Father-of-four Gordon Deacon, 58, had his coffin flanked by the white-armoured soldiers from his favourite sci-fi movie.
          </p>
          <p>
           He was brought to the church by horse drawn coffin with Darth Vader's henchmen leading the funeral procession from a galaxy far, far away.
          </p>
          <p>
           Gordon's friends and family were welcomed at the service by the Death Star troopers in helmets and guns - but Darth Vader was banned because no black was allowed.
          </p>
         </div>
         <figure class="item__content-block item__content-block--with-caption item__content-block--image">
          <div class="fluid-media-wrapper">
           <div class="fluid-media-wrapper__media">
            <span class=" responsive-image" data-alt="Gordon Deacon" data-height="null" data-picture="true" data-width="null">
             <span data-src="http://news.images.itv.com/image/file/591765/article_update_img.jpg">
             </span>
             <span data-media="(min-width: 321px)" data-src="http://news.images.itv.com/image/file/591765/stream_img.jpg">
             </span>
             <!--[if (lt IE 9) &amp; (!IEMobile)]&gt;
    &lt;span data-src="http://news.images.itv.com/image/file/591765/article_update_img.jpg"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
  &lt;![endif]-->
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Gordon Deacon" src="http://news.images.itv.com/image/file/591765/article_update_img.jpg"/>
             </noscript>
            </span>
           </div>
          </div>
          <figcaption class="item__content-block__media-info">
           Father-of-four Gordon Deacon of Cardiff was a Star Wars fan.
           <cite>
            Credit: Wales News Service
           </cite>
          </figcaption>
         </figure>
         <div class="item__content-block item__content-block--markdown">
          <p>
           His widow Marilyn, 54, said he was mad on Star Wars ever since he went to see the first film in 1977.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="item__content-block item__content-block--quote newsapp-icon newsapp-icon-ui_icon-15">
          <div class="quote">
           <div class="quote__content">
            <blockquote>
             <p>
              We used to go to shows and he had a signed photograph of bounty hunter Boba Fett on the wall. He had Star Wars lightsabers, figurines, cookie jars, the lot.
My husband didn't want people to wear black, his wish was to have a different funeral. People can either wear Star Wars costumes, but not Darth Vader
I think he would be over the moon about the theme of his funeral.
             </p>
            </blockquote>
           </div>
           <div class="quote__cite">
            <cite class="quote__cite-text">
              Marilyn Deacon
            </cite>
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="item__content-block item__content-block--markdown">
          <p>
           The space soldiers stood with their heads bowed in mourning as Gordon's coffin was carried into the church.
          </p>
          <p>
           Gordon, of Llanedeyrn, Cardiff, died last month after a battle with pancreatic and liver cancer.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dozens of mourners turned out in force at St Margaret's Church in Roath, Cardiff, for hospital housekeeper Gordon's Star Wars send-off.
          </p>
         </div>
