      <p>
       <a href="http://tilt.lt/TiltDonateButton" target="_blank">
        <img alt="Screen Shot 2014-12-01 at 11.52.46 AM" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1886" height="616" src="http://blog.tilt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Screen-Shot-2014-12-01-at-11.52.46-AM.png" width="1024"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Last Saturday, I read a heartbreaking article on Facebook about a little girl fighting a life-threatening skin condition. Her family wasbeing bankrupted by herexpensive medical care.
      </p>
      <p>
       Its the kind of article that makes you feel helpless  until you reach the end and see that theres a page where you can help out! Opening the familys fundraisingsite, I tapped the 
       <em>
        donate
       </em>
        button, then made my way through a gauntlet to complete my contribution  five different pages, an account creation, and 22 different text fields stood in my way and I was trying to complete this on my smartphone.
      </p>
      <p>
       Partially due to the poor reception on my bus home, this became a zen-esque test of patience. Those fields and pages stoodin the way of completing what should have been a quick and simpledonation.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Simplified, Powerful Donations, in Two Lines of Code.
      </h2>
      <p>
       So today, Im incredibly excited to unveil
       <a href="http://tilt.lt/TiltDonateButton" target="_blank">
        Tilts new Donate button
       </a>
       .In a timely coincidence, just weeks prior to this experience we had charged our team with building a
       <strong>
        simple,powerful, and flexible donation button
       </strong>
       to enabledonations on any site in
       <strong>
        minutes
       </strong>
       . This button, powered by our API and sitting within our suite of enterprise tools on
       <a href="http://open.tilt.com" target="_blank">
        Tilt/Open
       </a>
       , makes that possible.
      </p>
      <p>
       Its pretty magical. We hope you check it out the next time youneeddonations on your site  whether its to support a professional effort, to collect funds for aregistered non-profit, to launch apolitical fundraiser, or to funda side project or charitablecause.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <strong>
        How it Works
       </strong>
      </h2>
      <p>
       The Tilt Donate button is the
       <em>
        only
       </em>
       button where you cancopy and paste two lines of code into any site (WordPress, Squarespace, NationBuilder, personal site, etc) toimmediately activate donations.
      </p>
      <p>
       In under five minutesyoucan:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Accept donations from any card inany country  instantly
       </li>
       <li>
        Customize the look and feel of your donations
       </li>
       <li>
        Get anoptimized and seamless mobile/tablet experience
       </li>
       <li>
        Add extra information collection fields
       </li>
       <li>
        Automatically issue tax-deductible receipts (for U.S. registered non-profits)
       </li>
       <li>
        Manage contributions, donor info, and refunds through our dashboard
       </li>
       <li>
        Stay protected with 24/7 algorithmic and manual fraud prevention
       </li>
       <li>
        Keep safewithTilts robustpayments infrastructure
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       Weve packed a lot of power into these two lines, and we hope you
       <a href="http://tilt.lt/TiltDonateButton" target="_blank">
        give it a try
       </a>
       or
       <a href="http://tilt.lt/TiltDonateButton" target="_blank">
        recommend it
       </a>
       to a friend!
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Tilt/Open Donations" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1882" height="768" src="http://blog.tilt.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Zidisha_donate.png" width="1024"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       In thelast two years of building crowdfunding tools at Tilt, weve seen firsthand the tremendous increase inconversion that happens when you remove as many steps as you can. Weve taken that knowledge and optimization to simplify fundraising to one simple step.
      </p>
      <p>
       And in 2014, 55% of web browsing and email consumption occured
       <a href="http://money.cnn.com/2014/02/28/technology/mobile/mobile-apps-internet/" target="_blank">
        on your visitors phone or tablet
       </a>
       . Ourelegant, seamless user flow onthese devices will help you multiplyyour conversion rates.
      </p>
      <p>
       We hope you like it! As a special gift from our team for #GivingTuesday, were covering 100% of credit card processing fees for your first $10,000 in donations.Simplyaccept yourfirst dollar before December 31st. Youll keep 100% of the donations you receive! You can start
       <a href="http://tilt.lt/TiltDonateButton" target="_blank">
        here
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Let us know what you think, and spread the word!
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
