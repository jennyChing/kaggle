        <p>
         I love sweet potatoes but have had a hard time getting my family to eat them until I created this Apple Cider
         <strong>
          Roasted Sweet Potato Recipe
         </strong>
         . They think its like getting dessert for dinner. What they dont realize is that this recipe is really pretty healthy. They are easy to make and a great side dish for any fall meal.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.thismamaloves.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Apple-Cider-Roasted-Sweet-Potatoes.jpg">
          <img alt="Apple Cider Roasted Sweet Potatoes" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-56953" height="1024" src="http://www.thismamaloves.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Apple-Cider-Roasted-Sweet-Potatoes-682x1024.jpg" width="682"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Apple Cider Roasted Sweet Potato Recipe
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          2-3 large sweet potatoes
         </li>
         <li>
          cinnamon
         </li>
         <li>
          nutmeg
         </li>
         <li>
          12  15 oz apple cider
         </li>
         <li>
          1 tbsp butter
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Instructions:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
        </p>
        <p>
         Wash the sweet potatoes well. The skin will be left on but not eaten.
        </p>
        <p>
         Slice the potatoes into medallions approximately 1/2 thick. If they are too thick, they will take a very long time to cook.
        </p>
        <p>
         Lay the sweet potato slices in a single layer on a non stick baking sheet.
        </p>
        <p>
         Pour the apple cider over the potatoes. They should be 3/4 covered with cider.
        </p>
        <p>
         Add a dab of butter to the top of each potato.
        </p>
        <p>
         Sprinkle the potatoes with cinnamon and nutmeg to taste.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.thismamaloves.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/roasted-sweet-potato-recipe.jpg">
          <img alt="roasted sweet potato recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-56956" height="1023" src="http://www.thismamaloves.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/roasted-sweet-potato-recipe.jpg" width="682"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Roast in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Turn the potatoes, add butter to the side that is now up, and sprinkle with cinnamon and nutmeg. Return to oven.
        </p>
        <p>
         Bake potatoes for an additional 20 minutes or until they are soft.
        </p>
        <p>
         Serve the roasted sweet potatoes with the skin on. The skin will easily peel away with a fork.
         <br/>
        </p>
