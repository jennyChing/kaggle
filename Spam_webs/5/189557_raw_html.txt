      <h1 itemprop="headline">
       Increase Your Productivity By Eliminating DAWAL
      </h1>
      <!-- Make individual group author appear -->
      <div class="mninst">
      </div>
      <div class="comment_links">
       <a class="scroll_to post_your_comment" href="#comment_reply">
        Comment Now
       </a>
       <div class="comments_follow followable_block">
        <div class="followable_block initializing" data-object-json='{"title":"Increase Your Productivity By Eliminating DAWAL","avatar_src":"","follow_count":""}' data-target-id="blogAndPostId/blog/post/2305-15370" data-target-name="" data-target-type="article" data-tracking-link-name="articleLink_ArticleCommentsFollow" data-type-advoice="false">
         <div class="follow_unireg">
          <a class="follow_unireg_link" href="javascript://follow">

          <span class="loadingstate">

     <div class="article_body article" itemprop="articleBody">
      <div class="body">
       <div class="body_inner">
        <p>
         <span>
          Were all affected by one of those slow work days once in a while. We spend all day being active, yet at the end of the day, none of our tasks are complete. What happened? And what ifheaven forbidyou are doing this to yourself? Earlier this week I discussed this topic with marketing strategist Michael Maven, author of
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Scientific-Marketing-Codex-Increasing-Strategies/dp/1782802525/ref=sr_1_3?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1422711859&amp;sr=8-3&amp;keywords=Marketing+Codex">
           <em>
            The Scientific Marketing Codex
           </em>
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Get-Better-Customers-Now-Strategies/dp/1782804013/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1422711949&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=Michael+Maven+Get+Better+Customers+Now">
           <em>
            Get Better Customers Now
           </em>
          </a>
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span>
          In an interview, Maven introduced me to DAWAL (the Destructive Attention Without Awareness Loop), a concept he identified and teaches to clients of his profit enhancement firm,
          <a href="http://www.progressyourprofits.com/">
           Carter &amp; Kingsley
          </a>
          . The ability to master DAWAL produces measurable productivity results.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span>
          DAWAL is a growing cause for concern that Maven believes is ruining the productivity of a generation. It typically occurs when we are near any device that produces alerts, with cell phones and email programs being two of its primary culprits.
         </span>
        </p>
        <div style="padding: 20px 0pt;margin: 20px 0pt;border-bottom: 1px solid #DDDDDD;border-top: 1px solid #DDDDDD">
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Beyond-PR-Communicate-Champ-Digital-ebook/dp/B00TZGMK30/?utm_source=article&amp;utm_medium=direct&amp;utm_campaign=beyondprbook" style="text-decoration:none;color:#900;font: bold 20px/26px Georgia">
          Beyond PR: An eBook From Forbes
         </a>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Beyond-PR-Communicate-Champ-Digital-ebook/dp/B00TZGMK30/?utm_source=article&amp;utm_medium=direct&amp;utm_campaign=beyondprbook" style="text-decoration:none;font: 15px/18px  Georgia;color:#000">
          Old-fashioned PR is dead. Discover how to communicate with purpose and passion today.
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span>
           How DAWAL Works
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span>
          Youre working on a report whenbeepa message arrives on your cell phone. What do you do? Most of us instantly stop what were doing, reach for the phone and check the new message.
          <a href="/companies/next/">
           Next
          </a>
          <span class="quotecard_hook" data-exchange="null" data-naturalid="fred/company/3064" data-quotes-closing="0.0" data-quotes-now="0.0" data-ticker="null" data-type="organization">
           <span class="wrapper">
            <a href="/companies/next/">
             Next
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
          we see the other person is online and we spend a few minutes to chat via text. No worries, right? Wrong.
         </span>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_15371" style="width: 650px">
         <img alt="Marketing strategist Michael Maven is the author of Get Better Customers Now (Image courtesy of Carter &amp; Kingsley, www.ProgessYourProfits.com) " class="size-full wp-image-15371" src="http://blogs-images.forbes.com/cherylsnappconner/files/2015/01/MichaelLarge1.jpg"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Marketing strategist Michael Maven is the author of Get Better Customers Now (Image courtesy of Carter &amp; Kingsley, www.ProgessYourProfits.com)
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <span>
          Studies by Carter &amp; Kingsley show that when you are focused on a task and get distracted, it takes a full 17 minutes to get fully focused again. 17. Minutes. Now think how many times a day youre distracted like this. Its like trying to go for a run while stopping every two minutes to re-tie your laces, Maven says. Youre burning effort but but never moving forward by far. Worse still, when you think youre paying attention, its actually
          <em>
           Destructive Attention
          </em>
          . Your awareness is all over the map.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <span>
           DAWAL Self-Perpetuates
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
