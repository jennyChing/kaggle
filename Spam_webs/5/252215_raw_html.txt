     <p>
      <span style="font-size: x-large;">
       <strong>
        cat teepee
        <br/>
       </strong>
      </span>
      the native american teepee for fluffy little critters
      <br/>
      by loyal luxe, canada
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" height="29" src="/cms/images/designboom_shop/25usd.gif" title="" width="57"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" height="631" src="/cms/images/designboom_shop/teepee03.jpg" title="" width="818"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       cat teepee now available on the designboom shop!
      </strong>
      <br/>
      after the success of the
      <a href="/shop/cat_cabin.html" target="_blank">
       canadian cat cabin
      </a>
      , design duo loyal luxe have created a second model of cat houses made of 100% recycled cardboard:
      <br/>
      a native american teepee. this fun modern take on another classic symbol of canadian iconography distinguishes itself through its humorous theme,
      <br/>
      this time translated from amerindian culture and the collective imagination associated with it.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" height="615" src="/cms/images/designboom_shop/teepee07.jpg" title="" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-size: xx-small;">
       interchangeable ornaments  clockwise from the top left:
       <br/>
       bulls head, name banner, the tomahawk, the feather, the eagle
      </span>
      ,
      <span style="font-size: xx-small;">
       dream catcher
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      made entirely of corrugated cardboard and sold flat, the teepee is available with 6 interchangeable ornaments:
      <br/>
      the dream
      <br/>
      catcher, the feather, the tomahawk, the eagle, the bulls head and the banner in which the name of the animal can be written.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" height="502" src="/cms/images/designboom_shop/teepee04.jpg" title="" width="818"/>
      <br/>
      <span style="font-size: xx-small;">
       expandable door entry
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       dimensions:
      </strong>
      <br/>
      22 X 22 X 26 in., door opening once expanded: 9.5 in.
      <br/>
      (approximate)
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       material:
      </strong>
      <br/>
      one flat, foldable piece of corrugated cardboard.
      <br/>
      assembly instructions included.
      <br/>
      it requires no glue or tools.
     </p>
     <p>
      please note,
      <br/>
      there is a
      <strong>
       USD 11
      </strong>
      shipping and handling cost for this product.
      <br/>
      at the moment we ship to USA / canada only.
      <br/>
      <em>
      </em>
      <strong>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      cat teepee
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" height="29" src="/weblog/images/images_2/anita/SHOP/soldout.gif" title="" width="59"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <span style="font-size: medium;">
       <strong>
        sorry, this item is currently sold out.
       </strong>
      </span>
      <br/>
      USD 25 product + USD 11 shipping and handling = USD 36 total
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" height="844" src="/cms/images/designboom_shop/teepee06.jpg" title="" width="818"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      since the love for paper and cardboard is innate to cats, your pet will
      <br/>
      surely adopt their new cabin and will soon call it home sweet home!
      <br/>
      the teepee is also suitable for small furry friends like domestic rabbits, ferrets and small dogs.
     </p>
