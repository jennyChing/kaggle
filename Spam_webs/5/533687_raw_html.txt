        <h2 align='\"JUSTIFY\"'>
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          <strong>
           How To Use Olive Oil For Hair Growth
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://vitalbynature.net/olive-oil-for-hair-growth/olive_oil/" rel="attachment wp-att-737">
          <img alt="use olive oil for hair repair and hair regrowth" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-737" height="297" src="http://vitalbynature.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/olive_oil-300x297.png" title="olive_oil" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          It is natural for men and women, alike, to lose several strands of hair in a day. However, if it is more than usual and you notice your thinning hair, it is about time to have your scalp examined. This may be blamed on genetics or some underlying health conditions or perhaps, the result of using harmful hair products. Whatever the reason for losing hair, you can use
          <a href="http://vitalbynature.net/olive-oil-for-hair-growth/">
           <strong>
            <a href="http://vitalbynature.net/olive-oil-for-hair-growth/" title="olive oil for hair growth">
             olive oil for hair growth
            </a>
           </strong>
          </a>
          . Studies prove that it not only prevents hair loss but also promotes its growth.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          <a href="http://vitalbynature.net/olive-oil-facts/" title="Olive Oil Facts: History And Quality">
           Olive oil
          </a>
          it has been around for more than 6000 years. It originated in Asia Minor and spread from Palestine, Syria and Iran to the rest of the countries in the Mediterranean. During the ancient times, it was used for anointing those belonging to royalty. For this reason, it was considered as sacred. As a matter of fact, anyone who cut an olive tree was punished by death.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          At present, the production of
          <a href="http://vitalbynature.net/category/food-drink/olive-oil/" title="olive oil">
           olive oil
          </a>
          is still centered in the Mediterranean region, with Spain and Italy being the first 2 largest manufacturers, respectively. Because of the value it brings, millions of trees have been planted all over the world.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          There is a lot to benefit from the use of olive oil. Since then till now, its wide range of applications include cooking, medicines, soaps, beauty treatment products, lamps and candles, to name a few. With regards to health and beauty, it is now regarded as one of the effective ways to treat hair loss and promote hair production. This starts with a
          <a href="http://vitalbynature.net/healthy-hair/" title="healthy hair">
           healthy hair
          </a>
          and scalp.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         Heres a helpful video that shows you how to condition your hair with Hot Olive Oil Treatment (its fantastic)
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe frameborder="0" height="315" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/1iCweXzvLLA?rel=0" width="420">
         </iframe>

        <h3 align='\"JUSTIFY\"'>
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          <strong>
           Ways on Achieving Healthy Hair with Olive Oil
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://vitalbynature.net/olive-oil-for-hair-growth/olive_oil_for_hair_growth/" rel="attachment wp-att-734">
          <img alt="Olive oil can balance scalp and hair" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-734" height="300" src="http://vitalbynature.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/olive_oil_for_hair_growth-298x300.png" title="olive_oil_for_hair_growth" width="298"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <strong>
          <span style="font-size: small;">
           It helps maintain balance in the scalp
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          When there is an excess of the DTH hormone in the scalp, hair may begin to fall. DTH is a hormone that reduces hair follicle shaft. Applying olive oil stops DTH production, thus, solving the problem of hair loss.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <strong>
          <span style="font-size: small;">
           It acts as a scalp disinfectant.
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          <a href="http://vitalbynature.net/olive-oil-benefits-for-skin/" title="Olive Oil Benefits For Skin, Hair And Body">
           Olive oil has natural antioxidants
          </a>
          that provide a healthy scalp. It helps in removing impurities found in it. It can treat a dry scalp, dandruff and lice. It can, also, prevent infections.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          Hair damage brought about by harmful hair products and excessive hair treatments can be treated with olive oil.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <strong>
          <span style="font-size: small;">
           It functions as a natural conditioner.
          </span>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          Applying olive oil to the hair can make it feeling soft, manageable and beautiful.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 align='\"JUSTIFY\"'>
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          <strong>
           Steps in Applying Olive Oil
          </strong>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://vitalbynature.net/olive-oil-for-hair-growth/olive_oil_fingers/" rel="attachment wp-att-735">
          <img alt="olive oil to strengthen hair and nails" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-735" height="284" src="http://vitalbynature.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/olive_oil_fingers.png" title="olive_oil_fingers" width="194"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          1. Pour some warm olive oil in the dish.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          2. Dip your fingers in it.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          3. Gently massage into the scalp in a circular motion.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          Doing the above mentioned will benefit both your nails and hair. Both will become more moisturized. Olive oil can serve as you new hair conditioner that can be used between shampoos.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          Dry hair will benefit from using a lot while oily hair can use a minimal amount. . Just a little may go a long, long way to having beautiful and healthy hair.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="JUSTIFY">
         <span style="font-size: small;">
          Regardless of your age or gender, you can enjoy the benefits of
          <em>
           olive oil for hair growth
          </em>
          .
         </span>
        </p>
