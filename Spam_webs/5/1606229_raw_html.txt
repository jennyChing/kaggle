      <p>
       Drama over powered the
       <em>
        Big Brother 2014
       </em>
       house this week. However, one of the light moments of the week involved Team America bonding together to serve us. On Thursdays eviction episode of
       <em>
        Big Brother
       </em>
       16, we saw the first member of Team America,
       <strong>
        Joey Van Pelt
       </strong>
       leave the house. Also new member
       <strong>
        Donny Thompson
       </strong>
       took his spot as the second member of it. Tonight
       <em>
        Big Brother
       </em>
       2014 reveals the final members. If you cant wait to find out who joins Team America, weve got you covered.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/49446/big-brother-2014-spoilers-team-america-selected-did-we-pick-the-right-people/bb16-frankie-photobooth-week-2" rel="attachment wp-att-49448">
        <img alt="BB16-Frankie Photobooth Week 2" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-49448" height="291" src="http://gossipandgab.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/BB16-Frankie-Photobooth-Week-2-460x291.jpg" width="460"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        Big Brother
       </em>
       16 spoilers below, read with caution.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Derrick Levasseur
       </strong>
       and
       <strong>
        Frankie Grande
       </strong>
       complete Team America.
      </p>
      <p>
       Were not surprised that Frankie joined the team, but that he wasnt the first member of it.
       <strong>
        Ariana Grande
       </strong>
       s celebrity power holds a lot of weight. So its kind of shocking that her fans didnt secure his spot earlier.
      </p>
      <p>
       Currently, Derrick is playing one of the best games of
       <em>
        Big Brother
       </em>
       16. Hes on our shortlist of possible winners this year. However we believe Derrick won this vote because of the general population of
       <em>
        Big Brother
       </em>
       fans
       <em>
        ,
       </em>
       the casual viewers. His police sergeant status probably convinced them that he could do a good job in a secret mission like Team America.
      </p>
      <p>
       We like Donny and Derrick as parts of Team America, but we kind of hoped the team could be better-rounded with a female player instead of Frankie. He is going to get lots of America based prizes. So wed love to see
       <strong>
        Jocasta Odom, Victoria Rafaeli
       </strong>
       or
       <strong>
        Brittany Martinez
       </strong>
       as part of Team America. These women, with the possible exception of Brittany, are likely to fade to the background and float to the finals. Something like Team America would force them to really get involved and play the game.
      </p>
      <p>
       What do you think of Team America on
       <em>
        Big Brother
       </em>
       2014? Who should have been on it?
      </p>
