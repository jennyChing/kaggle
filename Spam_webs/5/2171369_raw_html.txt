                   Saturday, 12 July 2014

                 </h2>

                  <div class="post-outer">
                   <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     How Can We Do SEO?
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3316169487959526665" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      <span style="background: white; font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                       Trends keep changing in this highly
c
                      </span>
                      <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                       ompetitive world. Every guideline
will be applicable to only a certain time. The trend is important; not only in
the fashion world, but also in technology. Each year there are new set of rules
and techniques to stay in the top ranking in all the search engines.Unique and high
quality content are a must to be at the top in SERP. But there are other tips
to conquer
                      </span>
                      <b>
                       <span style='background: white; font-family: "Times New Roman","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                        SEO and content marketing.
                       </span>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                       <span style='font-family: "Times New Roman","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-fareast-font-family: "Times New Roman";'>
                        Here are
some
                        <b>
                         SEO
                        </b>
                       </span>
                       <b>
                        <span style='background: white; font-family: "Times New Roman","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                         techniques for 2014
                        </span>
                       </b>
                       <span style='background: white; font-family: "Times New Roman","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                        
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal">
                      </div>
                      <ul style="text-align: left;">
                       <li>
                        <span style="background: white; font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         Choose quality over quantity. It is not true that if you have
more backlinks your SEO is successful.
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         Purchasing backlinks is quick and cheap. But your site can
be ruined if the site that links is penalized or has a low DA.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt; line-height: normal;">
                         </span>
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         The link building will always remain
important. However, any SEO value from link building depends upon the authority
and validity of the site that is sending the links.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt; line-height: normal;">
                         </span>
                        </span>
                        <span style="background: white; font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         Guest blogging is no longer a table turner. People do not get
so excited about guest blogging any longer. True, people can still have guest
blogs, but be aware of
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         spammy
guest blogging.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         Do not optimize anchor texts. It is an extremely risky process.
Optimized anchor texts can ruin a sites SEO through gradual algorithmic
penalization. You can use safe anchors such as naked URLs, branded URLs and
long phrases.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         Create useful, information-rich
content and avoid key word stuffing. Keyword stuffing is the practice of
loading a webpage with keywords or numbers to manipulate a sites ranking in
search results. Covering pages with keywords or numbers results in a negative
user experience and can harm your sites ranking. Create keywords appropriately
and to the context.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         Social bookmarking is necessary. It
is a process of saving a picture, page or video to the web. It can be shared with
your friends and family. There are many websites like Digg, LinkedIn, Reddit,
Fark where you can bookmark your content by submitting. These bookmarking sites
are very useful to get traffic to your website. Links can be shared or
organized to suit your needs.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-family: Wingdings; font-size: 10pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt; line-height: normal;">
                         </span>
                        </span>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         PDF files are as important as html
files. Create a search engine optimized PDF file for your business website and
submit it to PDF submission websites. Getting a backlink here will get your
keywords optimized and bring more traffic.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         Do not ignore infographics. Instead
of writing long article or blog post that can bore and confuse readers, you can
just create an Infographics which is entertaining.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         Reviews are important in
                        </span>
                        <b style="text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         <span style='background: white; font-family: "Times New Roman","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                          Local SEO
businesses.
                         </span>
                        </b>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         Search
engines show reviews in several places. They are also used to rank your website.
Ask your clients to write a review of your business in major business review
sites like Google+, Shvoong and Stylefeeder.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%; text-indent: -0.25in;">
                         Multimedia is the trend of 2014
Off-page SEO. Add interesting videos, images and presentations about your
products. Use picture and video sharing sites like YouTube, Flickr, Slideshare,
Pinterest, and Instagram.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                       <li>
                        <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                         In the present
age, achieving a strong online presence requires a multifaceted SEO strategy. Follow
the above rules and enjoy maximum attention.
                        </span>
                       </li>
                      </ul>
