          Using risk to create stability
         </h1>
         <!-- End Title -->
         <!-- Start Post Meta -->

        <div class="post-single-content box mark-links">
         <p>
          The risk of losing your job and/or financial stability is more real than ever in todays world where steady employment, stable economies and governments all but disappeared. What if you could use risk to create a more stable financial future for yourself?
         </p>
         <p>
          I had this thought when I was reading to my son. I bought him a new Dr. Seuss book, which is translated into Afrikaans, and so I will re-translate back into English, not having a clue what the original English would have read:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <em>
           And when things happen
          </em>
          <br/>
          <em>
           Do not worry or complain
          </em>
          <br/>
          <em>
           Happen with them
          </em>
          <br/>
          <em>
           Ride the cusp of the wave
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          Risk can be defined as a treat or something that presents a danger. I am a husband and father and I have fears about risks and it is natural if you look at how the world treats and teaches children about risk everywhere. I was brought up to avoid risk and always try to steer towards to more predictable path and stable path.
         </p>
         <p>
          That is a good strategy but how is it relevant in our current world? Not much. More people are getting fired, more companies are using non-permanent labour and it seems that the world is changing faster and faster.
         </p>
         <p>
          You need to be open to surprises and see where they might lead you and in surprises I mean risks. Look at some of the cool products that came from surprises  Post-it Notes, Viagra (not that I am using it :D) and even the microwave oven  when a radar range melted the chocolate bar in a scientist.s pocket.
         </p>
         <p>
          So look at yourself and think of a surprise that can come out of you! Have you ever imagined the hidden skills and abilities you have within you or are you just hammering away at those you know in order to ensure a stable financial future?
         </p>
         <p>
          Do the following exercise: Imagine the risk what you are avoiding, such as losing your job or financial stability and what you can do. Not what you
          <em>
           will
          </em>
          do  because that defaults you back to what you have always done in life. Think about what you CAN do. Can you bake? Are you good with your hands? What do you love reading about what you can teach others?
         </p>
         <p>
          Ask friends and family what about you as a person stands out for them. It may be your ability to listen and advise people. People pay large sums of money to have people listen to them.
         </p>
         <p>
          Make a list of these things that you CAN do and go and see what the people are paying for such services. You will be amazed at what money you can make by applying the things you would normally just see as you being yourself.
         </p>
         <p>
          This exercise can lead you to discovering new opportunities and directions for your life. You can even launch a new career.
         </p>
         <p>
          You can choose to do this exercise now when things are still stable or be forced to do this exercise like I did in September when I had to survive change and replace the bulk of my income in 30 days.
         </p>
         <p>
          The risks we avoid can be the door to a new and exciting future we would not otherwise have. Learn how to embrace that which you fear and use it to grow and not to diminish yourself.
         </p>
