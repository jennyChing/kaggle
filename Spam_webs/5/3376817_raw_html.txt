         <p>
          December 13, 2014 - Coventry, West Midlands (LITERUM NEWS) -- A toothbrush is designed, primarily, to clean the gums and teeth with a scrubbing action.  Whether it is electric toothbrush  (
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Electric-Toothbrush-Battery-operated-replacement/dp/B00OCHAS9U/m=A2WR2ANBQG4DXZ&amp;keywords=electric+toothbrush" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          </a>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Electric-Toothbrush-Battery-operated-replacement/dp/B00OCHAS9U/" rel="nofollow">
           http://www.amazon.com/Electric-Toothbrush-Battery-operated-replacement/dp/B00OCHAS9U/
          </a>
          ) or manual that is their basic function.  Manufacturers of electric toothbrushes over the last few years, have added an assortment of other actions to this basic design but the bristle motion of the new TempIR Turbo toothbrush produces 30,000 brush strokes per minute producing better dental health  (
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Electric-Toothbrush-Battery-operated-replacement/product-reviews/B00OCHAS9U/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          </a>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Electric-Toothbrush-Battery-operated-replacement/product-reviews/B00OCHAS9U/" rel="nofollow">
           http://www.amazon.com/Electric-Toothbrush-Battery-operated-replacement/product-reviews/B00OCHAS9U/
          </a>
          ).
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The pressure built up from this high-speed brushing action forces the saliva in the mouth against the surfaces of the tooth and gums where plaque has had the chance to settle.  The amount of pressure that builds up means that plaque can even be reached up to 4mm past the actual brushing area of the  toothbrush bristles.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Sales Manager Aidan Wade explains: "The Turbo electric toothbrush produces such a high number of brush strokes per minute it agitates the saliva in the mouth to the extent that it disturbs any dental plaque in the mouth even where the bristles of the brush don't touch.  On the back of the brush head there is a tongue cleaner which helps with oral hygiene as it helps in the removal of bacteria.  Dentist prefer moderate softness for toothbrush bristles to avoid any undue damage to the gums so our toothbrush has been designed with the moderate softness bristles as well as ergonomic design of the handle making it much more comfortable to hold than standard brushes".
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The TempIR Turbo Electric toothbrush is battery operated, using non-rechargeable batteries thus avoiding the need for a charger but they do last for hundreds of uses.  The toothbrush comes in a pack of two, has four spare brush heads and is backed by a Lifetime No Hassle Free Product Replacement Guarantee.
