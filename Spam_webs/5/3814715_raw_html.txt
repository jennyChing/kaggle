               <span class="user-name-print">
                <span class="ljuser i-ljuser i-ljuser-type-P " lj:user="taylorgeiger">
                 <a class="i-ljuser-profile" href="http://taylorgeiger.livejournal.com/profile" target="_self">

              There are plenty of successful weight loss stories that you can relate with. As modern innovation progresses, our lifestyle also changes. Each of us becomes obsessive in finding pleasures in material things, unhealthy hobbies and foods. We usually buy things which are not necessary. We excessively eat foods, without moderation. And because of this, we learn to forget to live a healthy lifestyle. Unknowingly little by little we gain weight, we become heavy and eventually we suffer from obesity.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              How can we live a healthy lifestyle and be physically healthy? Regardless of weight size, you can definitely have your own successful weight loss stories. To monitor your calorie diet and exercise are only few guidelines that you need to follow. However, for long term goal, you must adhere to these following tips for you to lose weight:
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <b>
               Be Committed
              </b>
              <br/>
              Losing weight can't be done overnight. Therefore, it takes time and incredible effort. Maintaining your daily diet and exercise routine are not easy to do-list. Self- discipline is part of your difficult test that you need to overcome. Stay committed to your short term goal.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <b>
               Be Consistent
              </b>
              <br/>
              Follow your daily routines. For your diet, it is best to eat different meals in a day or two. But always stick to your diet plans. Temptations are everywhere! It surrounds you just right in front of your computers, phones, magazines and many other food stores. Stay away from them as much as possible.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <b>
               Go on a Cheat Day
              </b>
              <br/>
              It is very common that we normally crave for food -- this applies to everyone from children to elderly people. It's a normal process because we are only human being. You can give yourself a break. Don't be too hard on yourself. Eat ice creams, cake or drink sodas. However, it is ONLY allowed in a DAY -- not in consecutive days, weeks or months because for sure you'll not lose weight. Your diet and workout will be useless.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <b>
               Start eating right
              </b>
              <br/>
              Avoid fatty and high calorie foods. Most processed foods like chips, sodas are unhealthy food. In addition, you can gain weight for that type of food. Always eat a balanced meal, eat vegetables and eat fruits.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <b>
               Find an exercise buddy
              </b>
              <br/>
              Your friend, mom, dad, brother or sister can be your exercise buddy. You can also try joining in groups. Go in a membership gym and sign up for group exercises. Nowadays, Zumba exercise is very popular. You can enroll to it. Or if you don't like in a crowded place, then you can
              <a href="http://taylorgeiger.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               hire personal trainer
              </a>
              to help you train and guide you to lose weight. Having an exercise buddy excites you, motivates you and inspires you to work hard in losing weight. Your exercise buddy is also your great motivator.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <b>
               Be positive
              </b>
              <br/>
              Think positive. You CAN do it! You can definitely achieve the body that you want and release all those toxins inside your body. Every hard work will be paid at the right time. Don't give up with your exercises, dietary meals and positive outlook about losing weight. You'll be achieving your great body in time!
