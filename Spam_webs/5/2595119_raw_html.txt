            <h2>
             <span style="color: #333399;font-size: 14pt">
              <strong>
               What is domestic violence?
              </strong>
             </span>
            </h2>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #000000;font-size: 14pt">
              <span style="color: #800000">
               The Belgian College of Prosecutors General uses the following definition
              </span>
              :
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #000000;font-size: 14pt">
              any form of physical, sexual, psychological or economic violence between spouses, cohabitants or persons who have cohabited and between whom a sustainable affective and sexual band exists or existed.
              <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/6B2/865D8/W_31Lj5Y79ZDuXMrnIGyTg/warning-abuse-signs-of-violence-in-family.jpg">
               <img alt="partner violence emotion financial sexual Why people have so many problems with Partner violence ?" class="alignright wp-image-2329 size-medium" height="225" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/6B2/865DA/dQQ79mMrrMxqZHbeJbhIJw/warning-abuse-signs-of-violence-in-family-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #000000;font-size: 14pt">
              Partner violence includes a set of behaviors, actions, and attitudes of one of the (ex) partners that aim to control and dominate the other.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #000000;font-size: 14pt">
              It includes physical, sexual and economic aggression, threats or acts of violence that can repeat and repeat itself or that the integrity of the other and even its socio-professional integration tarnish.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #000000;font-size: 14pt">
              This violence affects not only the victim, but also other family members, particularly the children.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #000000;font-size: 14pt">
              It is a form of domestic violence.Usually the perpetrators are men and the victims are women.Violence in intimate relationships in the private sphere is the expression of the unequal power relations that in our society still exist between men and women .
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #000000;font-size: 14pt">
              The big difference between partner violence and a banal quarrel  homely  is the power difference.Each couple makes arguments.But when one of the two partners ever wants to be the boss and the other wants to verify under the thumb, and before that conflict and violence used, we speak of partner violence.(vzwzijn.be Partner violence)
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #000000;font-size: 14pt">
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #800000;font-size: 14pt">
              According to the European Commission the following is meant by intimate partner violence:
