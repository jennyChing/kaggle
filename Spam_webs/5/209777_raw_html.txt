         <p>
          Two of Urban Ghosts most popular articles to date have delved into the hidden history of the
          <a href="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2013/11/13-abandoned-stations-disused-platforms-london-underground/" target="_blank">
           London Undergrounds abandoned stations and ghost platforms
          </a>
          (check them outhere 
          <a href="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2013/11/13-abandoned-stations-disused-platforms-london-underground/" target="_blank">
           Part 1
          </a>
          &amp;
          <a href="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2014/09/abandoned-london-underground-10-derelict-tube-stations-deserted-platforms/" target="_blank">
           Part 2
          </a>
          ). And that doesnt include the Post Offices
          <a href="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2013/09/mail-rail-exploring-the-deserted-tunnels-of-londons-post-office-railway/" target="_blank">
           abandoned Mail Rail
          </a>
          line or its eccentric predecessor, a long forgotten
          <a href="http://www.urbanghostsmedia.com/2013/12/subterranean-forgotten-london-pneumatic-dispatch-railway/" target="_blank">
           pneumatic dispatch railway
          </a>
          system designed to ferry parcels in capsule-like trains beneath the streets of inner London.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://usvsth3m.com/post/52135944891/ghost-stations-of-the-london-underground-on-the-classic" target="_blank">
           Us vs th3m
          </a>
          teamed up with Twitter Tube fan
          <a href="https://twitter.com/DylanMaryk" target="_blank">
           @DylanMaryk
          </a>
          to create this awesome schematic map of the London Underground with only its ghost stations included. And as you can see, there are plenty of them!
         </p>
         <p>
          Over the decades myriad London Underground stations have been abandoned or relocated for various reasons. Some never saw many passengers and were thus not economically viable. Other underground stations, meanwhile, remain open but hide forgotten ghost platforms behind locked gates. Click the above image to load a larger map.
         </p>
