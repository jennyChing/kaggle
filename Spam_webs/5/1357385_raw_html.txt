                                10 Ways to Cook with Chai Plus Chai Shortbread Cookies with Sea Salt
                               </a>
                              </h1>
                             </div>
                             <div class="metabar">
                              <div class="metabar-pad">

                           <div class="entry_content">

                            <p>
                             <!-- BEGIN THEIA POST SLIDER -->
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Chai-Shortbread-Cookie-FoodieCrush.jpg">
                              <img alt="Chai Shortbread Cookie from FoodieCrush.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2582" height="800" src="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Chai-Shortbread-Cookie-FoodieCrush.jpg" title="Chai Shortbread Cookie from FoodieCrush.com" width="600"/>
                             </a>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Goodbye summers afternoon diet coke, hello chai latt de fall.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Now that my coffee shop is just 18 strides from my office deskwhen you work at home you have the luxury of doing things like actually counting how far the kitchen is from the computerIm finding that my afternoon routine has made a major switcheroo.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Lately Ive been craving a different afternoon pick me up rather than the cold diet soda I sometimes wished could be pumped straight into my veins. Ive been craving a cuppa something hot.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Chai-Shortbread-Cookie-FoodieCrush-1.jpg">
                              <img alt="Chai Shortbread Cookie from FoodieCrush.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2583" height="800" src="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Chai-Shortbread-Cookie-FoodieCrush-1.jpg" title="Chai Shortbread Cookie from FoodieCrush.com" width="600"/>
                             </a>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             I tend to get all wrapped up in what Im working on, so lately Ive been making it a point to get up from my chair, move away from the computer screen, stretch my legs and clear my head with a 5-minute afternoon timeout.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             My new favorite ritual is partaking of a moment in the waning afternoon sun, sitting on my deck with my chihuahua in my lap and a simple coffee drink in my hand. Lately my favorite has been an afternoon latte. Warm, milky and creamy.
                             <span id="more-2566">
                             </span>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Ive been making them for myself the past few days and adding a dose of
                             <strong>
                              <a href="http://www.tipuschai.com/" target="_blank">
                               Tipus Chai Now
                              </a>
                             </strong>
                             , an instant Chai mix from a sweet little company based in Montana. Chai tea in Montana? Apparently so. And no, theres nary a sign of steers or roping cowboys on their packaging.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Chai-Shortbread-Cookie-FoodieCrush-4.jpg">
                              <img alt="Chai Shortbread Cookie from FoodieCrush.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2586" height="800" src="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Chai-Shortbread-Cookie-FoodieCrush-4.jpg" title="Chai Shortbread Cookie from FoodieCrush.com" width="600"/>
                             </a>
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Savoring the warming flavors of Chaicinnamon, allspice, nutmeggot me thinking I should be cooking something tasty that included the same flavors I love drinking.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             And then Smudge started asking me for cookies. And then I put two and two together and waah-laah, Chai Shortbread Cookies were being created in my kitchen.
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             Oddly, I was compelled to add chocolate and sea salt. Im sure youll understand.
                            </p>
                            <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
                             <link href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Chai-Shortbread-Cookie-FoodieCrush-2.jpg" itemprop="image"/>
                             <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
                              Chai Shortbread Cookies with Sea Salt
                             </div>
                             <div class="ERSClear">
                             </div>
                             <div class="ERSTopRight">
                              <div class="ERSSavePrint">
                               <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
                                <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/easyrecipe-print/2566-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                                 Print
                                </a>
                               </span>
                              </div>
                             </div>
                             <div class="divERSHeadItems">
                             </div>
                             <div class="ERSIngredients">
                              <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
                               Ingredients
                              </div>
                              <ul>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                2 sticks butter
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 cup sugar
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                2 cups all-purpose flour
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 teaspoon salt
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 teaspoons vanilla
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 tablespoons
                                <a href="http://www.http://www.tipuschai.com" target="_blank">
                                 Tipu's Chai Now mix
                                </a>
                                or 1 tablespoon chai tea, ground fine
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                1  cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                 cup cream
                               </li>
                               <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                                1 tablespoon sea salt
                               </li>
                              </ul>
                              <div class="ERSClear">
                              </div>
                             </div>
                             <div class="ERSInstructions">
                              <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
                               Instructions
                              </div>
                              <ol>
                               <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
                               </li>
                               <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar together until fluffy. Add vanilla and blend well. In a separate bowl combine flour, salt and Chai mix and fluff with a fork or whisk. Add dry ingredients to butter mixture and mix until ingredients come together. Mixture will be crumbly. Mold dough into an 8-inch log, cover tightly with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least one hour or 20 minutes in the freezer.
                               </li>
                               <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                With a serrated bread knife cut logs into -inch thick discs and place 2 inches apart on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 12 minutes. Cool on a baking sheet.
                               </li>
                               <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                While cookies are baking, prepare the chocolate chip ganache. In a small saucepan add 1-inch of water, place a glass or heat-proof bowl filled with chocolate chips over the saucepan and bring water to a boil. Stir chocolate chips often as they melt. Add cream and mix until smooth. Remove from heat.
                               </li>
                               <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                                Dip cookies halves into chocolate mixture and lightly sprinkle with sea salt.
                               </li>
                              </ol>

                            <p>
                             The feeling of fall leaves us plenty of room for more chai-inspired recipes, and theres even one you can make for dinner!
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/PumpkinChaiCupcakesFinalforsite2.jpg">
                              <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2569" height="777" src="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/PumpkinChaiCupcakesFinalforsite2.jpg" title="PumpkinChaiCupcakesFinalforsite2" width="600"/>
                             </a>
                            </p>
                            <p style="text-align: center;">
                             Christina makes it impossible to pass up these gorgeous little creamy Chai peaks in
                             <em>
                              <strong>
                               Dessert for Twos
                              </strong>
                             </em>
                             Pumpkin Chai Cupcakes
                            </p>
                            <p>
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Gluten-Free-Carrot-Bread2.jpg">
                              <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2573" height="800" src="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Gluten-Free-Carrot-Bread2.jpg" title="Gluten-Free-Carrot-Bread2" width="600"/>
                             </a>
                            </p>
                            <p style="text-align: center;">
                             Karen did it again! Nutty brown rice flour gives sweet carrots the perfect lift in
                             <em>
                              <strong>
                               Gluten-Free Goddess
                              </strong>
                             </em>
                             Gluten-Free Carrot Bread with Chai Spices
                            </p>
                            <p style="text-align: center;">
                             <a href="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/chai-latte-marshmallows_25.jpg">
                              <img alt="" height="429" src="http://www.foodiecrush.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/chai-latte-marshmallows_25.jpg" title="chai-latte-marshmallows_25" width="600"/>
                             </a>
                            </p>
