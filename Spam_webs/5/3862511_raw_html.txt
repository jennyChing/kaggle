        <p>
         <a href="http://lifecurrents.dw2.net/easy-homemade-pizza-sauce/pizza-sauce-main/" rel="attachment wp-att-13913">
          <img alt="Easy Homemade Pizza Sauce is a house favorite!  by Life Currents http://lifecurrents.dw2.net" class="aligncenter wp-image-13913 size-full" height="433" src="http://lifecurrents.dw2.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/pizza-sauce-main.jpg" title="Easy Homemade Pizza Sauce is a house favorite!  by Life Currents http://lifecurrents.dw2.net" width="650"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I realized the other day that my favorite pizza sauce didnt have a post of its own. I mean, Ive shared the recipe before, but only on other pizza recipes. And, since its such a favorite in this house now, I thought it deserved a nice little space all its own!
        </p>
        <p>
         We use it all the time. If there isnt a jar in the fridge, Ive probably stashed it in the freezer. Just thaw it in the microwave, or take it out of the freezer the day before you want to use it.
        </p>
        <p>
         Its thick and rich, with a little sweetness and a little heat. Its filled with herbs and spices, and requires no cooking! Just stir it all together. Super easy and yummy! And, it goes great on lots of different pizzas.
