            <p>
             <strong>
              Pregnancy
             </strong>
             can be a wonderful, exciting and difficult time for a woman. As her body shape and size starts changing rapidly, she may no longer feel like her old self.
            </p>
            <p>
             Her current clothes do not fit anymore. As the time progresses, she has to start looking for the right maternity clothes.
            </p>
            <p>
             Many women wonder if they will look good in maternity clothing, and worry that it will take an extensive wardrobe and expensive shopping to do so.
            </p>
            <p>
             Maternity clothes have never been so stylish but you need to figure out how many do you need.
            </p>
            <p>
             After all, you will be using them for only a few months. When it comes to maternity clothing, you need to consider affordability, style and comfort. It is important to find out the right style and support for your changing body.
            </p>
            <h2>
             What should I buy when going out for Maternity Clothes Shopping?
            </h2>
            <p>
             Here are the basic must haves during pregnancy that offer comfort, practicality and affordability to your about to be changing wardrobe.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Maternity Bras
            </h3>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.thewomentalk.com/aff-bravado-body-silk-seamless-nursing-soft-cup-bra" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Bravado Body Silk Seamless Nursing Soft Cup Bra">
              <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
               Bravado! Body Silk Seamless Maternity &amp; Nursing Bra
              </span>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             The very first signs of pregnancy are swollen and sore breasts, so get yourself a good fitting bra. It is important that you are measured to feel comfortable. Your bra size can change up to four times during your pregnancy.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Soft Fabric Outfits
            </h3>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.thewomentalk.com/aff-maternal-america-womens-maternity-scoop-neck-front-tie-dress" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Maternal America Women's Maternity Scoop Neck Front Tie Dress">
              <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
               Womens Maternity Scoop Neck Front Tie Dress
              </span>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Pregnancy may make your skin sensitive and your body temperature higher than normal. Avoid fabrics which could be irritating to your skin. Choose soft natural fabrics that are cool and comfortable. You can also wear soft fabrics in the form of shawls and wraps. Velvet ones are specially comfy.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Leggings
            </h3>
            <p>
             <a class="broken_link" href="http://www.thewomentalk.com/aff-cotton-spandex-jersey-leggings" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Cotton Spandex Jersey Leggings">
              <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
               Cotton Spandex Jersey Leggings
              </span>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             A pair of stretchy black leggings keeps you comfy and stylish. You can pair them with knee-high boots in winters or flats in summers. You can get away with non-maternity leggings, by just pushing the waistband beneath your bump. Maternity leggings stretchable belly band grows with your belly making them one of the comfortable and stylish pregnancy outfits.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Maternity Support Band
            </h3>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.thewomentalk.com/aff-boppy-maternity-support-band" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Boppy Maternity Support Band">
              <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
               Boppy Maternity Support Band
              </span>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             As your pregnancy progresses, your abdomen begins to expand to accommodate your growing baby. An aching in your back and pelvic region can begin to develop as the muscles and ligaments begin to soften in preparation for childbirth. Maternity support band or belt offer supportive relief for women. So invest in a maternity support band that you can strap around your bump. They also help to take the strain from your lower back.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Slip On Shoes
            </h3>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.thewomentalk.com/aff-studded-round-tow-basic-slip-on-flat-shoes" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Studded Round Tow Basic Slip On Flat Shoes">
              <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
               Studded Round Toe Basic Slip On Flat Shoes
              </span>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Slip in and out of your shoes with comfort and style. Since you will be avoiding bending over your growing belly anyways, you will feel comfortable wearing shoes with no laces and buckles. Plus, you will be able to wear them after you have the baby as well.
            </p>
            <h3>
             Post Pregnancy Dresses
            </h3>
            <p>
             <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
              Stylish Loose Fit Shirring Dresses
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             Dont waste money on clothes that you can only wear when you are pregnant. You will most likely have a little postnatal tummy after the baby is born so its often not possible to start wearing your pre-pregnancy wardrobe right away. Find clothes that will stretch to accommodate your expanding figure and then shrink back as you lose the postnatal weight.
            </p>
            <p>
             It is important to enjoy while being
             <strong>
              pregnant
             </strong>
             . Try to experiment with a whole new world of fashion and style. It is totally up to you to decide what style is best for you and what you will feel most comfortable in. There are dresses; pants, skirts, and even lingerie that will make any women feel more confident and happier with their body when they are pregnant.
            </p>
            <p>
             Heres wishing you a
             <strong>
              Happy and Healthy Pregnancy
             </strong>

            </p>
