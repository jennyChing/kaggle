              On the recent edition of Teens React from popular Youtubers The Fine Brothers, a group of teens viewing some of Nirvanas most popular performances and voicing their real-time opinions on them. Most of the group appreciate Cobain or at least have heard or Nirvana however as you would expect there are some that arent just familiar with the band but grunge music in general. You can watch the video above. We finally must be experiencing a generation gap. I assume their reactions would be if you ask those in their thirties  forties their opinions on Imagine Dragons or Bastille.
