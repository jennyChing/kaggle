&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-64941" src="http://cdn.medgadget.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/covidien-nellcor-portable-spo2.png" alt="covidien-nellcor-portable-spo2" width="585" height="534" /&gt;&lt;img class="size-full wp-image-64946 alignright" src="http://cdn.medgadget.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/spo2-nellcor.png" alt="spo2-nellcor" width="330" height="631" /&gt;The FDA has given &lt;strong&gt;Covidien &lt;/strong&gt;clearance to introduce its Nellcor Portable SpO2 Patient Monitoring System the U.S. Though the device can be used in clinical environments, its particularly beneficial for at-home monitoring and for conducting sleepstudies outside the clinic.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The device comes with a standard finger clip that allows tracking of the pulse rate and blood oxygenation while the patient is ambulatory. While at home, the person using the portable monitor can check their own readings, in the meantime preserving the data for clinicians to review during an office visit.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The device is compatible with all of Nellcor OxiMax sensors and offers SatSeconds alarm management, pleth waveform, blip bar, and tabular trend information. Additionally, itcomes with a sleep mode that will dim the three-inch color LCD screen and silence the alarms to let patients get their z&#8217;s while the device is quietly monitoring the vitals.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Some of the applications of the device according to the announcement:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Six Minute Walk Test  This test evaluates the response of all systems involved during exercise,&lt;br /&gt;
including the pulmonary and cardiovascular systems, systemic circulation, peripheral circulation,blood, neuromuscular units, and muscle metabolism. The Six Minute Walk Test provides anobjective measurement of a patients exercise capacity.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Critical Congenital Heart Disease (CCHD) Screening  Every year, about 4,800 (or 11.6 per10,000) babies in the United States alone are born with CCHD. These babies are at significantrisk if this condition goes undiagnosed.&lt;br /&gt;
Since 1993, Nellcor pulse oximetry technology has been utilized on more than 33,000 newborns spanning five separate clinical studies evaluating the use of pulse oximetry for critical congenital heart disease screening.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Car Seat Challenge Test  According to the American Academy of Pediatrics guidelines, as partof the discharge process, each preterm infant born less than 37 weeks gestational age should havea period of observation in a car safety seat before hospital discharge to monitor for possibleapnea, bradycardia or oxygen desaturation.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;/blockquote&gt;
