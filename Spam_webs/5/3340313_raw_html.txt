        <h2>
         <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/top-49-2015-cloud-conferences-and-expos-in-the-u-s/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Top 49 2015 Cloud Conferences and Expos in the U.S.">
          Top 49 2015 Cloud Conferences and Expos in the U.S.
         </a>
        </h2>
       </div>
       <!--/post-title -->
       <div class="post-date">
        <em>
         On 01.20.15, In
         <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/cloud-computing-news/" rel="category tag">
          Cloud Computing
         </a>
         , by
         <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/author/william/" rel="author" title="Posts by William Toll">
          William Toll
         </a>
        </em>
       </div>
       <p>
        2015 may have just started, but dozens of technology companies are gearing up for their cloud conferences and expos that will take place throughout the year across the United States. Fromexploring how cloud-based technology affects business and communication, to understanding security in the age of the cloud, the topics at this years cloud conferences and events are sure to be of interest to anyone looking to deepen their understanding ofcloud environments and their benefits. While we realize there are some excellent cloud events being held outside of the US this year, we decided to focus this list on eventstaking place within the fifty states.You may just save enough money by attending some of these domestic conferences that you will be able to attend more events than in years past!
       </p>
       <p>
        To make this list, the cloud events must include well-respected speakers, innovative ideas and agendas, and the most buzzed about topics. Conferences and expos that include track options, leading information sources, and unique activities also made the cut. Check out our top 49 2015 cloud conference and expo picks below, and start planning your conference calendar before one of the events passes you by. Keep in mind, our top conferences and expos are listed in no particular order. Keep track of events ProfitBricks is attending, speaking, sponsoring and exhibiting at
        <a href="https://www.profitbricks.com/press-and-info-center/events" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         1.
         <a href="http://www.ciocloudsummit.com/" target="_blank">
          CIO Cloud Summit
          <br/>
         </a>
        </strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/CDMmedia" target="_blank">
         @CDMmedia
         <br/>
        </a>
        June 25-26
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Location TBD
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/CIO-Cloud-Summit.png">
         <img alt="CIO Cloud Summit" class="aligncenter wp-image-4719 size-full" height="194" src="https://blog.profitbricks.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/CIO-Cloud-Summit.png" width="500"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        CIOs have embraced the could but still have questions about data security and efficiently managing cloud environments. The goal of CIO Cloud Summit is to help C-level executives understand cloud computing, its capabilities, and the opportunities it offers. Featuring a blend of CIO Think Tanks, Executive Visions panel discussions, Analyst Q&amp;A sessions, Keynote Presentations, and time to network, CIO Cloud Summit ensures C-level executives will spend their time well and leave with ideas and strategies to better their businesses.
       </p>
