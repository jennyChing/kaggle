           <p>
            Following the completion of the Super Rugby play offs between the Blues and Waratahs and the Crusaders and Sharks the Super Rugby Semifinalists have been confirmed.
           </p>
           <p>
            The Blues beat the Waratahs 26-13 and qualified for the Semi-finals and then on Saturday the Crusaders beat the Sharks 36-8.
           </p>
           <p>
            Interestingly the Reds hosted the Blues in Maythis year and won 37-31 and the Stormers hosted the Crusaders alsoin May andlost 14-20.
           </p>
           <p>
            Both Semi-finals will be played on Saturday the 2nd of July 2011.
           </p>
           <p>
            The 2011 Super Rugby Semi-Final fixtures are confirmed as follows:
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Reds vs Blues
            </strong>
            <br/>
            Suncorp Stadium, Brisbane
            <br/>
            19h40 (21h40 NZ/11h40 SA/10h40UK)
            <br/>
            Referee: Jonathan Kaplan Both
            <br/>
            AR 1: Stuart Dickinson
            <br/>
            AR 2: James Leckie
            <br/>
            TMO: George Ay oub
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Stormers vs Crusaders
            </strong>
            <br/>
            Newlands, Cape Town
            <br/>
            17h05 (01h05 Aus/03h05 NZ/16h00UK)
            <br/>
            Referee: Craig Joubert
            <br/>
            AR 1: Jaco Peyper
            <br/>
            AR 2: Stefan Breytenbach
            <br/>
            TMO: Shaun Veldsman
            <br/>
           </p>
