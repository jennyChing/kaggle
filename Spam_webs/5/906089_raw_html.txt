         <h2>
          <span style="color: #3366ff;">
           Cloud Computing Advisory Service Offered to SMEs
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: right;">
          By
          <a href="http://ezinearticles.com/?expert=Andy_Tierney">
           Andy Tierney
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           IT company offer Cloud Computing Advisory service to SMEs
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Throughout this article we will give advice to SMEs on advantages and disadvantages of Cloud Computing.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           To find out why SMEs should embrace this service to their business, take a look at the information provided below.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Does Cloud Computing apply to SME businesses?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Is there a need for SMEs to embrace it?
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          There are certain answers out there, but its up to each company to decide for themselves. Of course experts agree that Cloud Computing is the future, not only for SMEs, but for other companies, too.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_615" style="width: 274px">
          <a href="http://www.thecloudcomputingaustralia.com/2012/06/27/cloud-computing-advisory-service-offered-to-smes/the-cloud-computing-australia-094/" rel="attachment wp-att-615">
           <img alt="Cloud Computing Advisory Service Offered to SMEs" class="size-full wp-image-615" height="191" src="http://www.thecloudcomputingaustralia.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/The-Cloud-Computing-Australia-094.jpg" title="Cloud Computing Advisory Service Offered to SMEs" width="264"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Cloud Computing Advisory Service Offered to SMEs
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Most SMEs still see this improved services as something new and strange, not as an opportunity to progress.
         </p>
         <p>
          There are certain security issues that we cannot avoid when it comes to this service. Those issues are:
         </p>
         <ol>
          <li>
           Privacy Issues
          </li>
          <li>
           Legal Issues
          </li>
          <li>
           Availability Issues
          </li>
          <li>
           Loss of control over data Issues
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p>
          IT engineers and experts are working hard to solve those problems and to improve the security. These were the main issues why SMEs and other companies avoided Cloud Computing.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           How would I deploy it
          </span>
          ?
         </p>
         <p>
          There are different ways to deploy this service into your business. It all depends on your business requirements. Four main cloud computing deployment models are:
         </p>
         <ol>
          <li>
           Private
          </li>
          <li>
           Public
          </li>
          <li>
           Community
          </li>
          <li>
           Hybrid
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Private
          </span>
          - is the infrastructure deployed for specific organization. Deployment of private cloud is mainly in-house operation. A third party can be included into the private cloud if they are on the premises.
         </p>
         <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
         <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
          <script type="text/javascript">
           &lt;!--

          </script>
          <script src="http://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/show_ads.js" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
         </div>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Public
          </span>
          - is a commercial basis operation available to the public. This infrastructure enables consumer to develop and deploy different services in the cloud. Public clouds are serviced and managed by a cloud service provider.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Community
          </span>
          - is the shared cloud computing infrastructure among a number of organizations with similar interests. To keep the price of this cloud service low, users (companies involved) share that between themselves.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Hybrid
          </span>
          - is the mix of private and public clouds. This cloud infrastructure has the ability to move data from one type of cloud to another, through different applications.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           How much would it cost
          </span>
          ?
         </p>
         <p>
          One of the first reasons why SMEs should embrace this improved IT service would be price.
         </p>
         <p>
          Usually the cost of cloud IT service is cheaper than traditional computing.
         </p>
         <p>
          By introducing cloud IT service to your business venture, you can save money in your existing IT department.
         </p>
         <p>
          Advisory service on Cloud Computing shouldnt cost a lot, and the best way for the customer is to contact providers customer service department for more information.
         </p>
