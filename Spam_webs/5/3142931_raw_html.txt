        <p>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          So youve got a headache. Whatever the cause  too much looking at screens, too much whisky, your needy cat spent the night showing how important you are to her by walking across your pillow and purring as hard as she possibly could  the throbbing in your cranium now threatens your ability to be productive for the rest of the day. While you might normally take an ibuprofen and be done with it, what do you do if theres no magical headache pill to be found? How are you going to get out of this mess?
         </span>

        <p>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          In this article, wediscuss10different natural remedies that can reduce headache pain and tension. No chemicals or caffeine to worry about. Its all natural and really helps fight the awful pain. Beside the fresh air, rest enough, drinking lot room temperature water, there are also some foods and liquids and other techniques that will regenerate you brain and you will feel like you have been reborn.
         </span>

        <h3>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          1.Crush It With Cayenne
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          <img alt="Crush It With Cayenne" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-25003" data-wp-pid="25003" height="877" src="http://www.topinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Crush-It-With-Cayenne.jpg" width="736"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          <strong>
           via
           <span style="color: #333333;">
            <a href="http://everydayroots.com/headache-remedies" target="_blank">
             everydayroots.com
            </a>
           </span>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Utilizing something spicy may not sound headache helpful, but cayenne is somewhat revered in the kingdom of natural remedies to treat pain and inflammation. The secret behind its success lies in an ingredient called capsaicin, which inhibits something in our body that is one of the main elements in pain perception called Substance P. In short, Substance P is part of what makes us feel pain, and the capsaicin depletes it.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          2. Go Nuts
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          <img alt="Go Nuts" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-25005" data-wp-pid="25005" height="965" src="http://www.topinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Go-Nuts.jpg" width="736"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          <strong>
           via
           <span style="color: #333333;">
            <a href="http://everydayroots.com/headache-remedies" target="_blank">
             everydayroots.com
            </a>
           </span>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          Instead of popping a pill when you get a headache, toss back some almonds. For everyday tension-type headaches, almonds can be a natural remedy and a healthier alternative to other medicine. It acts as a pain reliever because it contains something called salicin, which is also an agent in popular over the counter killers. Try eating a handful or two of these wholesome nuts when you feel the ache start to set in.
         </span>
        </p>

         <span style="color: #333333;">
          3.Potato
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          <img alt="Potato" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-25006" data-wp-pid="25006" height="490" src="http://www.topinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Potato.jpg" width="736"/>
         </span>

          <strong>
           via
           <span style="color: #333333;">

        <p>
         <span style="color: #333333;">
          After a night out, you may think that a plate of greasy fries is the way to go; forget the french fries and go for a baked potato instead. Foods high in potassium have proven to help alleviate bad headaches, and a baked potato with its skin offers up to a whopping 600 mg.
         </span>
        </p>
