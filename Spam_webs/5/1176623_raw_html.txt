      <div class="heading alignleft">
       <h2>
        <a href="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/2014/04/11/diy-floral-party-hats/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to DIY floral party hats">
         DIY floral party hats
        </a>
       </h2>
       <small>
        categories
        <a href="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/category/diy-wedding/" rel="category tag">
         DIY Wedding
        </a>
       </small>
      </div>
      <div class="clear">
      </div>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-71417" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-1.jpg" height="854" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-71417" height="854" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-1.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        You wanna know why we love party hats, Cakies? You put on a party hat and you cant help but feel festive. Plus they make for some
        <i>
         way
        </i>
        cute
        <a href="http://instagram.com/100_layercake/" target="_blank">
         Instagram ops
        </a>
        (#adorbs). But today were sharing a little something from
        <a href="http://cambriagracephotography.blogspot.com/" target="_blank">
         Cambria Grace Photography
        </a>
        that goes beyond any party hats weve ever seen before. For this DIY,
        <a href="http://www.bostonpollen.com/" target="_blank">
         Pollen Floral Design
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://laurenwellsevents.com/" target="_blank">
         Lauren Wells Events
        </a>
        fashioned party hats out of natural-colored cardboard and the most stunning fresh blooms. Just gorgeous.
       </p>
       <p>
        Check out the tutorial below, and then after you do that, pop on over to our sister site,
        <a href="http://www.100layercakelet.com/" target="_blank">
         Cakelet
        </a>
        , to see the equally adorable
        <a href="http://www.100layercakelet.com/2014/04/11/diy-confetti-party-hats/" target="_blank">
         confetti party hat DIY
        </a>
        that were sharing today. Now heres
        <a href="http://www.bostonpollen.com/" target="_blank">
         Pollen Floral Design
        </a>
        with the step-by-step instructions
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-71418" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-2.jpg" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-71418" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-2.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        <b>
         Supplies:
         <br/>
        </b>
        - party hats
        <br/>
         hot glue gun
        <br/>
         fresh flowers
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-71421" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-5.jpg" height="500" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-71421" height="500" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-5.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        <b>
         Instructions:
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        1. Assemble the party hat. (To achieve a natural cardboard look as shown in our photos, we bought party store hats and just turned them inside out).
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-71423" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-7.jpg" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-71423" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-7.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        2. Clip off all flower stems, so only flower heads remain.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-71419" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-3.jpg" height="854" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-71419" height="854" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-3.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        3. Using the hot glue gun, place a dollop of glue on a flower head, and then place the flower on the hat.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-71424" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-8.jpg" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-71424" height="400" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-8.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        4. Use a mixture of smaller blooms to fill in the spaces between the larger flowers.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-71420" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-4.jpg" height="854" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-71420" height="854" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-4.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        5. Flowers will begin to wilt quickly after your hat is assembled. You can place your hat in the refrigerator to help them last longer. For best results, make your hat an hour or two before you plan to wear it.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="lazy alignnone size-full wp-image-71422" data-original="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-6.jpg" height="380" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" width="570"/>
       </p>
       <noscript>
        <img alt="DIY floral party hats | Photo by Cambria Grace Photography | Design by  Lauren Wells Events | 100 Layer Cake" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-71422" height="380" src="http://www.100layercake.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/DIY-floral-party-hats-6.jpg" width="570"/>
       </noscript>
       <p>
        6. Celebrate!
       </p>
       <p>
