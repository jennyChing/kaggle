    <p>
     <br/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      british family is living the highlife in treehouses by blue forest
     </strong>
     <br/>
     all images courtesy of blue forest
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      british design firm
      <a href="http://www.blueforest.com/" target="_blank">
       blue forest
      </a>
      has developed living the highlife, which is comprised of two treehouses, with one designated for children and the other for adults.
     </strong>
     the elevated dwellings are linked among a network of rope bridges, which accesses the adventure play area and assault course also located on the site. the aerial complex provides a playful getaway for the owners family, and is able to accommodate guests as well.
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="blueforest living highlife treehouse designboom" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-199162" height="818" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/blueforest-living-the-highlife-treehouse-designboom-02.jpg" width="818"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     the roof of the childrens treehouse is comprised of three pointed tower forms
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      the childrens treehouse is characterized by its tall pointed towers, which reference medieval architectural forms.
     </strong>
     inside, a concealed hatch in the upper floor leads to a game room above. additionally, the complex includes an 80-yard zip line, which accesses the assault course on the forest ground.
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="blueforest living highlife treehouse designboom" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-199163" height="484" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/blueforest-living-the-highlife-treehouse-designboom-03.jpg" width="818"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     the elevated complex is connected by a series of rope bridges
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      the adult dwelling features a low conical roof, with walls clad in hand-split oak shingles and cedar tongue-and-groove boards.
     </strong>
     the interior is composed of a kitchen, bathroom, and large living area, with a thick tree passing through the center of the space.
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="blueforest living highlife treehouse designboom" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-199164" height="610" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/blueforest-living-the-highlife-treehouse-designboom-04.jpg" width="818"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="blueforest living highlife treehouse designboom" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-199165" height="545" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/blueforest-living-the-highlife-treehouse-designboom-05.jpg" width="818"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     a concealed hatch leads to a game room in the upper storey of the childrens treehouse
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="blueforest living highlife treehouse designboom" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-199166" height="609" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/blueforest-living-the-highlife-treehouse-designboom-06.jpg" width="818"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     the pointed tower forms produce high interior spaces
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="blueforest living highlife treehouse designboom" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-199167" height="818" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/blueforest-living-the-highlife-treehouse-designboom-07.jpg" width="818"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     the adult dwelling features a low conical roof
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="blueforest living highlife treehouse designboom" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-199168" height="545" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/blueforest-living-the-highlife-treehouse-designboom-08.jpg" width="818"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     a tree passes through the living room
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="blueforest living highlife treehouse designboom" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-199169" height="545" src="http://www.designboom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/blueforest-living-the-highlife-treehouse-designboom-09.jpg" width="818"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     the adult dwelling features a large open room able to accommodate dinner parties
    </p>
