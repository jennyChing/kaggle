         Nail Art DIY: How To Do Marble Nails
        </h1>

        <p>
         At first glance, marble nail art looks like something youd need to have professionally done. Not true; this is something really easy to do on your own  not to mention its really fun. Anything that allows me to play with different colors and get my hands dirty, Im in. That said, you dont have to get your hands dirty to do this. Heres how to do marble nail art!
         <span id="more-62380">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Jewelry pictured:
          <a href="http://www.freepeople.com/accessories-the-jewelry-box-rings/star-signet-ring/?cm_sp=Blog-_-Q32012-_-120828starsignetring" target="blank">
           Star Signet Ring
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.freepeople.com/accessories-the-jewelry-box-rings/dion-ring/?cm_sp=Blog-_-Q32012-_-120828dionring" target="blank">
           Dion Ring
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.freepeople.com/motif-midi-ring-25902453/?cm_sp=Blog-_-Q32012-_-120828motifmidiring" target="blank">
           Motif Midi Ring
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.freepeople.com/accessories-the-jewelry-box-rings/dree-eternal-ring/?cm_sp=Blog-_-Q32012-_-120828dreeeternalring" target="blank">
           Dree Eternal Ring
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.freepeople.com/accessories-the-jewelry-box-rings/fairfax-ring-brass/?cm_sp=Blog-_-Q32012-_-120828fairfaxbrassring" target="blank">
           Fairfax Brass Ring
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.freepeople.com/accessories-jewelry-bracelets/stone-wrap-braclet/?cm_sp=Blog-_-Q32012-_-120828stonewrapbracelet" target="blank">
           Stone Wrap Bracelet
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.freepeople.com/accessories-jewelry-bracelets/mixed-bead-wrap/?cm_sp=Blog-_-Q32012-_-120828mixedbeadwrap" target="blank">
           Mixed Bead Wrap
          </a>
          .
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0630.jpg">
          <img alt="diy marble nails" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-62382" height="387" src="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0630.jpg" title="diy marble nails" width="580"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          What you need:
         </strong>
         different colors of nail polish  I recommend 3-5 different colors. I chose a really soft, muted palette because I dont like when my nails are too bright. Youll also need a cup  any kind of plastic cup will work, but these little shot-glass sized Solo cups are perfect. Lastly youll need some water to put in the cup and a few toothpicks. Be sure to use room temperature water  if it is too cold, the nail polish will harden quickly.
        </p>
        <p>
         Note: Be sure to do this project over paper towels or a surface you dont mind getting dirty! It can get a bit messy.
        </p>
        <p>
         Also, if you prefer to not get your hands dirty (since you will be dipping your fingers in nail polish) you can coat your fingers (not the nails!) with Vaseline first so the polish doesnt stick to your skin, or you could try putting tape around your fingertips, leaving the nails exposed. If you just dip your fingers in the polish, like I did, you just need to wait until it dries and then carefully remove it from your skin using nail polish remover and cotton swabs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0651.jpg">
          <img alt="diy marble nails" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-62383" height="416" src="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0651.jpg" title="diy marble nails" width="580"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         To get marble nails, you simply pour a small amount of polish, one color at a time, into your cup. As you add more colors it will form a bulls-eye shape.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/marble1.jpg">
          <img alt="diy marble nails" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-62384" height="180" src="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/marble1.jpg" title="diy marble nails" width="580"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0693.jpg">
          <img alt="diy marble nails" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-62385" height="391" src="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0693.jpg" title="diy marble nails" width="580"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Do this as quickly as possible so that the polish doesnt start to harden in the water. When youre finished it should look something like this:
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0656.jpg">
          <img alt="diy marble nails" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-62386" height="381" src="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0656.jpg" title="diy marble nails" width="580"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now use the toothpick and make your design of choice in the water. I simply swirled it around a bit to get the marbled effect.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0696.jpg">
          <img alt="diy marble nails" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-62387" height="399" src="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0696.jpg" title="diy marble nails" width="580"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Once youre happy with your design, start dipping your fingers straight into the polish, one by one. The best part about this is if youre normally not very good at painting your nails (I am not) it wont matter, because youre just dipping your nails!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0697.jpg">
          <img alt="diy marble nails" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-62388" height="389" src="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0697.jpg" title="diy marble nails" width="580"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         When you dip your nails in, the polish will stick, leaving you with a pretty marbled polish on your fingernail! I love that each nail is slightly different, as well.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0705.jpg">
          <img alt="diy marble nails" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-62389" height="401" src="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0705.jpg" title="diy marble nails" width="580"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Once the polish is dry, use nail polish remover and cotton swabs to clean the polish off your skin, and voila! Marble nails.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0674.jpg">
          <img alt="diy marble nails" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-62390" height="394" src="http://blog.freepeople.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/IMG_0674.jpg" title="diy marble nails" width="580"/>
