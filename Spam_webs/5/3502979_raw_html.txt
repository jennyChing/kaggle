       <h2 class="post-title">
        MemoBottle, the flat water bottle
       </h2>
      </div>
      <!-- /post-header -->
      <div class="post-content">
       <p>
        This is some interestingdesign; so simple, yet wasnt previously conceived.
       </p>
       <p>
        We are all busy people, running around, and some of us carry water bottles with us to keep hydrated on the go. All water bottles are round to be ergonomic, easy for the hand to hold, but is bulky. In comes the MemoBottle.
       </p>
       <p>
        Its flat-packed, BPA-free, slim, dishwasher-friendly, reusable water bottle that is available in different paper sizes, ranging from Letter to A5. Certain comments have been saying this is an idea that is less successful that the conventional bottle due to its shape, and some agree that it is an interesting concept and makes it convenient to slip in with books, laptop, or any other flat-packed objects. What do you think?
       </p>
