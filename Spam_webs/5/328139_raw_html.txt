      <h2 class="titlepost">
       Adventures Over Appliances: Ask for The Gift of Cash with Envelope Registry
      </h2>
      <hr/>
      <!--start post content-->
      <div class="postcontent">
       <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        A few weeks ago, my fianc and I embarked on the journey of starting our wedding registry. At first, I was just thrilled to play with the scan gun. I felt like a space soldier saving the world one crystal wine glass barcode at a time. But, upon uploading said list to our wedding website, I immediately feltdiscombobulated. It felt awkward, providing our guests with a list of acceptable gifts and to be honestdo I really need a new toaster? Is this juicer symbolic of our ever-lasting love? Do these serving bowls make me weak in the knees?
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Nope.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://onthegobride.com/13ua">
         <img alt="Envelope Registry" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-21704" height="475" src="http://onthegobride.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/devices-1024x660.jpg" width="738"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Im 25. Hes 30. Were young, hopeful, and full of wanderlust. We have an insatiable desire to see everything, experience everything, do everything We value memories and experiences above any material item and we want our friends and family to be allowed the option to contribute to that dream if they wish to.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://onthegobride.com/44le">
         <img alt="Envelope Registry" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-21706" height="873" src="http://onthegobride.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/ipad-portrait-865x1024.jpg" width="738"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        So, upon discovering
        <a href="http://onthegobride.com/e53g" target="_blank">
         Envelope Registry
        </a>
        , I fell in love. Envelope allows your guests to gift experiences instead of appliances. It saves you the struggle of hinting to your cousin, that while the sterling silver salad-spinner is lovely, youd really prefer cold hard cash.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://onthegobride.com/sfaq">
         <img alt="Envelope Registry" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-21708" height="551" src="http://onthegobride.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/iphone-macbook-1024x765.jpg" width="738"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        When I set up my profile on Envelope Registry I also discovered that they currently are running a competition in which one lucky couple will have their registry value doubled. Theyll match the total your friends and families gifts to you up to $5,000. Thats a whole lot of amazing experiences. To enter you just need to be in the first 100 couples to sign up. So be quick!
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://onthegobride.com/vwk5">
         <img alt="Envelope Registry" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-21707" height="551" src="http://onthegobride.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/iphone-1024x765.jpg" width="738"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Setting up my account was so easy, and you can check it out
        <a href="http://onthegobride.com/uoxo" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        (arent we gorgeous?). I love that its displayed in such a beautiful and classy way and allows our guests to pick what adventure theyd like to support. Not to mention, I can now focus on more pressing matterslike, what in the world am I doing with my hair??
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://onthegobride.com/ulax">
         <img alt="Envelope Registry" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-21705" height="551" src="http://onthegobride.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/ipad-1024x765.jpg" width="738"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         This lovely sponsored post was written by our very own Sarah Pabon. Sarah is currently launching her crafts blog, and is transitioning into the On The Go Bride team as a writer. Watch for more of her elegantly written, mildly sassy posts in the near future.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
