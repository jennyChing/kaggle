      <h1 class="page-title">
       Thank You For Calling MEDIGO: Our Patient Care Team
      </h1>
      <div class="thb-text post-413 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-meet-the-medigo-team tag-care-team tag-medical-travel tag-medigo tag-patient-news-2">
       <p>
        Medical travel made simple sounds crazy to a lot of people who have tried to book a medical procedure in another country before. We understand that planning medical care in another country can be a confusing and overwhelming project for many patients. Once they find MEDIGO and search for their procedure they could be faced with dozens of clinics in different countries that offer the care they need. How do you know which clinic is right for you?
       </p>
       <p>
        Enter MEDIGOs Patient Care Team. At the time of writing, they hail from all over the globe; Canada, the US, France, Italy, Thailand, Germany, South Africa, and Sierra Leone. Help is available in English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Filipino(Tagalog), Pidgin, Mende, and Creole. The Care Team is available almost 24 hours per day to make sure everything runs smoothly for patients and clinics. I caught up with Melanie, who specializes in helping French and North American patients
       </p>
       <h3>
        Hey Melanie, thanks for taking the time out to talk to me. Why dont you start with a little about yourself?
       </h3>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Im from Toronto, Canada, but Ive lived all over Europe as well. I studied International Business and languages in school, and I was caught up in Berlins startup scene. I wanted to work for a small and growing company where I could make a difference.
        </strong>
       </p>
       <h3>
        What does the Patient Care Team do for patients?
       </h3>
       <p>
        <strong>
         We help patients find the best clinic for their needs based on their personal requirements. We help them find the best price and procedure for what they need and then find a clinic in a location that works for them. We make sure the clinic speaks their language, and can accommodate everything they need. Some patients have trouble choosing a clinic, and we get a lot of feedback from patients about different clinics so we can recommend a clinic based on what theyre asking for.
        </strong>
       </p>
       <h3>
        What Can Care Team Do For A Patient Having Trouble With A Clinic?
       </h3>
       <p>
        <strong>
         We have direct contacts we work with at our clinics so we can call our patient coordinator there and get clear answers for the patient. And of course, if they have any issues while traveling they can call one of our international numbers and we will help any way we can.
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Most importantly, patients think theyre doing a great job, too
        </strong>
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         I decided to undergo an
         <a href="https://www.medigo.com/en/plastic-and-cosmetic-surgery/otoplasty" title="otoplasty with MEDIGO">
          otoplasty
         </a>
         procedure while on vacation in
         <a href="https://www.medigo.com/en/no-results/thailand" title="MEDIGO's partners in Thailand">
          Thailand
         </a>
         but had no idea where to start looking due to the great language barrier that I would undoubtedly face not speaking Thai. Melanie from Medigo was unbelievably helpful and walked me through every step of the way. I am sincerely thankful and would choose Medigo again in a heartbeat. -Shea
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         MEDIGOs Patient Care team can always be reached through our website, by email at careteam@medigo.com, or on twitter @medigocareteam
        </em>
       </p>
