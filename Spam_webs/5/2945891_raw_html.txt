            <h2>
             <span class="wsite-text wsite-headline">
              <strong>
               <a href="/privat-livemont.html" title="">
                Privat Livemont
               </a>
              </strong>
             </span>
            </h2>
            <p>
             <span class="wsite-text wsite-headline-paragraph">
              <strong>
               <a href="http://affiliates.art.com/get.art?Search_String=Privat+Livemont&amp;A=945490&amp;L=12&amp;P=0&amp;S=3&amp;Y=1" target="_blank">
                Privat Livemont
               </a>
              </strong>
              s exquisite posters depict beautiful women clutching glasses of
              <strong>
               <a href="http://affiliates.art.com/get.art?Search_String=Absinthe&amp;A=945490&amp;L=12&amp;P=0&amp;S=3&amp;Y=1" target="_blank">
                absinthe
               </a>
              </strong>
              as though bestowed from the gods.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              Hailing from Belgium, Livemont (1861  1936) was known for his absinthe-themed
              <strong>
               <a href="http://affiliates.art.com/get.art?Search_String=Art+Nouveau&amp;A=945490&amp;L=12&amp;P=0&amp;S=3&amp;Y=1" target="_blank">
                Art Nouveau
               </a>
              </strong>
              ads.
              <br/>
              Originally a painter, he taught himself lithography after a poster design he exhibited won first prize.
              <br/>
              Livemonts distinctive artworks are exhibited in museums and galleries worldwide.
             </span>
            </p>
