          <h1>
           DIY ART GIFT FOR MOM
          </h1>
          <div class="metadata">
           <div class="published">
            Apr 16 2014 11:39 AM
           </div>
          </div>
         </header>
         <div class="sharebar-wrapper">
          <div class="sharebar">
           <!-- AddThis Button BEGIN -->
           <div addthis:title="DIY ART GIFT FOR MOM" addthis:url="http://www.blacks.ca/inspiration/home/mothers-day-diy" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style">

           <!-- AddThis Button END -->
          </div>
         </div>
         <p class="taxonomy-field">
          <span class="name">
           Pick  Section:
          </span>
          <a href="/inspiration/section/everyday-moments">
           Everyday Moments
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="/inspiration/section/seasonal">
           Seasonal
          </a>
         </p>
         <div style="width: 100%; position: relative; float: left; margin: 0 0 1em;">
          <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/inspiration/jennifer">
           <img height="44" src="/inspiration/Media/Default/SmallBloggerImage/jennifer.jpg" style="float: left;" width="44"/>
          </a>
          <div style="float: left; padding-left: 0.8em; font-size: 1.077em;">
           <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/inspiration/jennifer">
            <span style="color: #333;">
             <span style="font-weight: bold;">
              Jennifer Flores,
             </span>
             Toronto, ON
            </span>
           </a>
           <br/>
           <span style="color: #a1a1a1;">
            <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/inspiration/jennifer">
             Blogger Since: March, 2014
            </a>
            |
            <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/inspiration/jennifer">
             View Jennifer's Bio
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
           I have a five-year-old daughter Chloe, and like most children, she is a prolific artist. On a daily basis, shell come home from school with artwork: large paintings, crafty art made of paper plates or pipe cleaners, even scrap pieces of paper decorated with her latest masterpiece.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span class="Apple-tab-span" style="white-space: pre;">
           <span face="Arial, sans-serif" style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
           </span>
          </span>
          <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
           As a mom, I want to save all these papers and tuck them away as mementoes of her childhood  but I know we dont have enough room to store all of these treasures. So for this Mothers Day I made a gift for me: a Photobook of my childs artwork. This is a perfect gift for Moms  youll have a keepsake of your childs work and theres no guilt when you have to throw out the originals!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
           <img height="1674" src="/inspiration/Media/Default/Mother's%20Day/mothers-day-diy-1.jpg" width="2500"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <!--more-->
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
           BLACKS has Photobooks that are "Made for Instagram" so putting this book together was very easy: I could download photos right from my Instagram account or take them from my phone. If youd like to make your own photo book, you can do it in one quick in-store visit even right from your phone. The BLACKS Photo App makes it easy to create prints, cards or Photobooks in only five minutes! To create this Photobook using your phone, just follow these simple steps:
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          1.
          <span style="font-size: 7pt; line-height: normal; font-family: 'Times New Roman';">
          </span>
          <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/Blacks_photo_app" target="_blank">
           Download the BLACKS Photo App
          </a>
          from iTunes or Google Play.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          2.Using your smartphone, take photos of your childs artwork.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          3.The Photobooks are 21 pages and print one photo per page so ensure you have enough photos. Your book doesnt have to be all artwork  I mixed in photos of me and my daughter with the art.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
           <img height="1674" src="/inspiration/Media/Default/Mother's%20Day/mothers-day-diy-3.jpg" width="2500"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="margin-left: 0in; mso-add-space: auto;">
          4.From the BLACKS Photo App menu, select BOOKS.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="margin-left: 0in; mso-add-space: auto;">
          5.Add photos by simply tapping the photos youd like to add to the book. You can change photos by tapping on the page.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="margin-left: 0in; mso-add-space: auto;">
          6.Once youre happy with the book layout, you can select the cover colour and add text.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          In 7-10 business days, your printed book will be ready for pick up. This is a great gift for Dads to make for Moms. You could have the kids gather their artwork and surprise Mom with a book from each child. Or make a gift for Grandma. Fill it with artwork and photos from all of the grandkids for a truly memorable, one of a kind Mothers Day keepsake.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
           <img height="1674" src="/inspiration/Media/Default/Mother's%20Day/mothers-day-diy-2.jpg" width="2500"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">
           Im so happy with how this little BLACKS book turned out. The photos are such a joyful reminder of childhood. Its definitely a gift this mother loves!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
