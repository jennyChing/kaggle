        <h2>
         <strong>
          Celebrity Stretch Marks
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Not all of us are made perfect. No matter how hard stars try to look flawless in every single shot, no amount of Photoshopping can cover the fact that celebrity moms get stretch marks just like regular people. While some stars fork up a huge amount of dough just to get those marks out of the way as soon as the baby comes, some honorable actresses wear them with pride wherever they go. Instead of being hidden in shame, these celebrity stretch marks are out there for all the world to see:
        </p>

        </h2>

        <p>
         After giving birth to her first child in 2008, Halle Berry has lived her life without a care in the world about her celebrity stretch marks. Hollywood may be all about going under the knife just to keep everything smooth and tight, but this former fashion model knows that shes got to be proud of how she looks if she wants to be happy with her work. And if all her great movies arent testament enough to that mindset, we dont know what is!
        </p>

          Kate Winslet
         </strong>

          <img alt="Kate Winslet" class="size-medium wp-image-1018" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/spoiledcelebrity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/3120399003_a30dbdc089_o.jpg?resize=300%2C181"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          As someone who has a tendency to get naked in films, Kate Winslet is unafraid to bare her stretch marks.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Kate Winslet not only knows how to bare her soul in her emotionally gripping movies, but also knows how to bare her true body. In her film Little Children, media critics gave her high praise not only for her performance but also for baring her celebrity stretch marks that are completely natural. Every mother has them, and why should she be any different?
        </p>

         <strong>
          Angelina Jolie

        </h2>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1017" style="width: 310px">

          <img alt="Angelina Jolie" class="size-medium wp-image-1017" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/spoiledcelebrity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/610462898_67cac91032_z.jpg?resize=300%2C225"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Angelina Jolie loves her mommy life.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         As if power actress and all-around mom
         <a href="http://www.babble.com/celebrity/celebrity-moms-who-have-stretch-marks-and-dont-care-photos/">
          Angelina Jolie
         </a>
         doesnt have enough on her platewhat with her award-winning roles and her media presence seen left and rightshe also has to deal with celebrity stretch marks, too? Of course she does, and shes proud of them. I love it. It makes me feel like a woman. It makes me feel that all the things about my body are suddenly there for a reason. It makes you feel round and supple, and to have a little life inside you is amazing, she says. With a line like that, all her kids are definitely beaming with pride.
        </p>

         <strong>
          Jennifer Lopez

        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1020" style="width: 310px">

          <img alt="celebrity stretch marks" class="size-medium wp-image-1020" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/spoiledcelebrity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/4095588841_fa83c9f96d_o.jpg?resize=300%2C225"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Nothing makes a woman more beautiful than being a mom, and Jennifer Lopez knows it.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Sure, shes got the body of a smoldering hot dancer, but shes a mommy, too, and proud of it. I remember my flabby belly hanging out right after I gave birth, she says. I kind of laughed and said to Marc, Look, baby! Isnt this crazy? I just didnt care. She may not care, but the rest of the world sure doesand everyone is happy she bared her true self. You get to the point where youre like, Where am I? What happened to me? I got to get myself together for my kids, you know? They need to know what I really look like.
        </p>

         <strong>
          Jessica Alba
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1019" style="width: 310px">
         <a href="http://i0.wp.com/spoiledcelebrity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/3209476930_2bcebdd7b6_o.jpg">
          <img alt="Jessica Alba" class="size-medium wp-image-1019" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/spoiledcelebrity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/3209476930_2bcebdd7b6_o.jpg?resize=300%2C225"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Nothing can stop this star from looking like an angel, not even Jessica Albas stretch marks.
         </p>

        <p>
         This petite beauty is not one to hide her celebrity stretch marks. Even though some might consider them a flaw, Ive learned to love my stretch marks. Pregnancy was the most incredible experience Ive ever had. So Ill take the stretch marks. Ill take the cellulite I can never get rid of, she says. Dont worry, Jessica; we still think youre flawless.
