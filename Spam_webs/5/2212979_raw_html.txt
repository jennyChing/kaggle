           <h2>
            Product Description
           </h2>
           <p>
            This scope comes from one of the top companies in the world for AR Optics. While only a relatively new company, they have quickly found their way to being one of the leaders in the scope world, providing some of the best scopes to customers across the planet.
           </p>
           <h2>
            Functionality
           </h2>
           <p>
            One of their best scopes is in their Viper line, with the HS-T 4-16x44mm scope. This scope retails for an inexpensive rate, putting it in the price range of many hobbyists and enthusiasts. No matter where you are shooting the gun, the Viper HS-T is going to help you hit that target every single time. The HS-T, which stands for Hunting Shooting Tactical, design is excellent and consists of an extremely strong 30mm one-piece tube that provides you with excellent adjustment abilities for the wind, the elevation and for optics. With the scope, you will hit that target 200 or 300 yards away quite easily, with little effort and little chance of missing.
           </p>
           <h2>
            Optical Zoom
           </h2>
           <p>
            The 4X zoom range provides you with excellent versatility in terms of magnification, and the advanced optics system gives you a clear shot in even the harshest of conditions. In addition, the eye relief has been designed so that shooters can be on target quickly, and easily, with little time needed for getting comfortable looking through the scope. When you see the target, you can be shooting seconds later and hitting the target right where you want to hit it. Whether you are hitting a paper target, or hunting in the bush, this scope is going to give you that extra edge you have been looking for.
           </p>
           <p>
            This incredibly precise scope is excellent for anyone on a budget, who wants to still get a good scope for their shooting activities. Durable and light, it is easy to carry, easy to store and easy to attach, no matter what you are doing.
           </p>
