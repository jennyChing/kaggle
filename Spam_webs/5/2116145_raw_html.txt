        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Homemade Summer Skin Care
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="entry-date">
          <a href="http://thelunabird.com/homemade-summer-skin-care/" rel="bookmark">
           <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-05-16T11:26:41+00:00">
            May 16, 2014
           </time>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="byline">
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://thelunabird.com/author/the-luna-bird/" rel="author">
            The Luna Bird
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="comments-link">
          <a href="http://thelunabird.com/homemade-summer-skin-care/#respond">
           Leave a comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25">
        </span>
        <p>
         Summer is almost here. Yeah!
        </p>
        <p>
         When it gets warmer, we get happier and spend more time outside.
        </p>
        <p>
         We expose more skin, we love our summer dresses and shorts and spending time at the pool. And although our skin and bodies love the Vitamin D it soaks up in the sun, too much exposure to the sun can be bad for our skin.
        </p>
        <p>
         But that doesnt mean we cant enjoy the sunshine. We just need to follow a few tips to ensure our skin is healthy and ready for the sun.
         <br/>
         <span id="more-799">
         </span>
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <h1>
         Homemade Summer Skin Care Tips and Recipes
        </h1>
        <p>
         These easy tips and homemade summer skin care recipes will help your skin stay fresh, healthy and hydratedall summer long.
        </p>
        <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_838" style="width: 300px;">
         <a href="https://farm7.staticflickr.com/6143/5951666839_91fe88b141_b.jpg">
          <figure class="hammy-responsive wp-image-838 size-medium" data-media="http://thelunabird.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/summer-time-300x203.jpg" title="">
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Image: https://farm7.staticflickr.com/6143/5951666839_91fe88b141_b.jpg" height="203" src="http://thelunabird.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/summer-time-300x203.jpg" title="" width="300"/>
           </noscript>
          </figure>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
          Image: https://farm7.staticflickr.com/6143/5951666839_91fe88b141_b.jpg
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <hr/>
        <h2>
         Summer Skin Care Tips
        </h2>
        <h3>
         Stay Hydrated  Your Skin Is Thirsty Too:
        </h3>
        <p>
         During the summer we sweat a lot and loosing all that water leaves less water for our skin. Staying well hydrated keeps our skin hydrated too and helps it keep in moisture. So drink lots of water!
        </p>
        <h3>
         Moisturize Your Skin:
        </h3>
        <p>
         No need to buy expensive lotions. Try melting some coconut oil between your fingers and adding some
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/search/?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;index=aps&amp;keywords=Essential%20Oils&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;linkId=5RCE52ZOEQKPUUQ2&amp;tag=thlubi0e-20&amp;linkId=M67PFPIZZEM57HLU" target="_blank">
          Essential Oils
         </a>
         <figure class="hammy-responsive " data-media="https://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=thlubi0e-20&amp;l=ur2&amp;o=1" title="">
          <noscript>
           <img alt="" height="1" src="https://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=thlubi0e-20&amp;l=ur2&amp;o=1" title="" width="1"/>
          </noscript>
         </figure>
         to make your own body lotion.
        </p>
        <p>
         Oil goes deeper into the skin and especially after a shower helps lock in moisture. Let it all sink in before heading out into the sun though. Otherwise you might get a sunburn. The opposite of healthy skin.
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <h2>
         Summer Skin Care Recipes
        </h2>
        <p>
         These recipes make larger batches. For a serving size of 1-2 use half the amount of each ingredient.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Strawberry Facial Mask
        </h3>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Mix 1/ cup strawberries with
         </li>
         <li>
          1 tsp
          <a href="http://thelunabird.com/12-uses-for-honey-amazing-health-and-beauty-benefits-hidden-in-honey/" target="_blank" title="12 Uses for Honey  Amazing Health and Beauty Benefits Hidden in Honey">
           honey
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          1 tsp cornstarch (or for an extra exfoliating effect use sugar)
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thelunabird.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/SDC11884.jpg">
          <figure class="hammy-responsive aligncenter wp-image-830 size-medium" data-media="http://thelunabird.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/SDC11884-300x225.jpg" title="">
           <noscript>
            <img alt="" height="225" src="http://thelunabird.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/SDC11884-300x225.jpg" title="" width="300"/>
           </noscript>
          </figure>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Massage and spread over face, leave on for about 10-20 minutes and rinse with warm water, then pat the skin dry.
        </p>
        <p>
         The wonderful strawberry scent will remind you of picking berries in the summer time and will immediately lift your spirits.
        </p>
        <p>
         Strawberries can help clear blemishes and battle acne. This nifty berry also helps reduce oiliness and makes the skin look younger. This face mask will also exfoliate and moisturize the skin. A perfect combination for the summer.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Chocolate Coconut Sugar Scrub
        </h3>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Mix 1 cup brown sugar with
         </li>
         <li>
           cup lightly melted
          <a href="http://thelunabird.com/12-uses-for-coconut-oil/" target="_blank" title="12+ Uses for Coconut Oil">
           coconut oil
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          1/3 cup olive oil
         </li>
         <li>
          2-3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thelunabird.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/SDC11888.jpg">
          <figure class="hammy-responsive aligncenter wp-image-840 size-medium" data-media="http://thelunabird.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/SDC11888-300x225.jpg" title="">
           <noscript>
            <img alt="" height="225" src="http://thelunabird.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/SDC11888-300x225.jpg" title="" width="300"/>
           </noscript>
          </figure>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Combine all ingredients in a bowl until all chunks are gone. You dont want the coconut oil to be melted, but if it is too stiff, melt it between fingers or heat in microwave for only a few seconds before adding the other ingredients.
        </p>
        <p>
         Scrub over body and massage your skin gently. Rinse and wash.
        </p>
        <p>
         You will absolutely love this body scrub. There is no need to moisturize afterwards as the coconut oil does that for you.
        </p>
        <p>
         The sweet chocolaty scent is just intoxicating. The cocoa powder and sugar exfoliate the skin and make it super smooth.
        </p>
        <p>
         Feel refreshed and happy after this wonderful body treat.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thelunabird.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/SDC11893.jpg">
          <figure class="hammy-responsive aligncenter wp-image-841 size-medium" data-media="http://thelunabird.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/SDC11893-225x300.jpg" title="">
           <noscript>
            <img alt="" height="300" src="http://thelunabird.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/SDC11893-225x300.jpg" title="" width="225"/>
           </noscript>
          </figure>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Refreshing Body Water Spray
        </h3>
        <p>
         Boil 1-2 cups of water. Pour over 2 tsp of dried lavender or mix with a few drops of
         <a href="http://thelunabird.com/5-reasons-to-love-lavender/" target="_blank" title="5 Reasons to Love Lavender">
          lavender oil
         </a>
         . Add2 tsp of rosemary leaves or rosemary oil. Steep for 30 minutes and strain.
        </p>
        <p>
         Mix in a spray bottle with  cup apple cider vinegar. Keep in fridge and mist on body on hot days. It will refresh and moisturize your skin as well as help you cool down when it gets too hot.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          What is your favorite way to keep your skin fresh and glowing this summer? Please leave a comment below.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
