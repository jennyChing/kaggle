           <h1>
            Glitter Tip Nails
           </h1>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h2>
            Items used for Glitter Tip Nails:
           </h2>
           <p>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             OPI Nail Envy
            </li>
            <li>
             Le Chat Dare To Wear Strip-Tease
            </li>
            <li>
             Models Own Disco Inferno
            </li>
            <li>
             Seche Vite Top Coat
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            After applying OPI Nail Envy I painted three coats of Le Chat Dare To Wear Strip-Tease to all nails. When this was dry I dabbed Models Own Disco Inferno in a curved shape onto Tips of nails using the brush in the bottle. This was done freehand but you could use Tip Guides to help with the ease of application. I finished with one coat of Seche Vite Top Coat.
           </p>
