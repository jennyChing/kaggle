         <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
          <span itemprop="name">
           How Would Sony be Impacted by the New PlayStation?
          </span>
         </h1>
         <div class="entry">
          <div class="wp_social_bookmarking_light">
           <div class="wsbl_linkedin">
            <a class="wp_social_bookmarking_light_a" href="http://www.linkedin.com/shareArticle?mini=true&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fus.zuuonline.com%2Farchives%2F984&amp;title=How%20Would%20Sony%20be%20Impacted%20by%20the%20New%20PlayStation%3F" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Share on LinkedIn">

          <br class="wp_social_bookmarking_light_clear"/>
          <p>
           <span style="line-height: 1.6em">
            The thing with every technology related firm is the fact that its products and services become obsolete very rapidly. As a result, for the company to grow and expand and for it to be able to give its investors good returns, it is pivotal that it manages to introduce something new frequently.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           With the cutthroat competition that prevails in this field, it is of utmost importance to be in the lead or, at least, be amongst the top few companies to be able to lure investors. Sony Corporation (
           <a href="https://www.google.com/finance?cid=33095" target="_blank">
            NYSE:SNE
           </a>
           ) has realized the importance that is associated with constant innovation owing to which it has decided to build a future around its most popular consumer product: the PlayStation.
          </p>
          <div>
           <p style="text-align: center">
           </p>
           <div>
            <img alt="sony" class="size-medium wp-image-985 aligncenter" height="201" src="http://cdn-zuu-online-us.s3-website-ap-northeast-1.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/9/2014/08/sony-300x132.jpg" title="" width="456"/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
           Source: Yahoo Finance-Sony One Year Chart
          </p>
          <h2>
           <b style="line-height: 1.2em">
            New Company Strategy
           </b>
           <br/>
          </h2>
          <p>
           The plan, however, involves an idea which focuses on how to make money with old titles as well without having to come up with brand new games in order to make money. Sony Corp has revealed plans to
           <a href="http://www.thestreet.com/story/12824253/1/will-sony-sne-stock-be-helped-by-new-playstation-plan.html" target="_blank">
            recover the losses
           </a>
           of around $10 billion that it has had to face over the past 6 years via a whole new PlayStation plan. This plan will obviously have impacts on the companys stocks which is why I will be discussing this move made by the company.
          </p>
          <p>
           The PlayStation 4 proved to be a huge success for the company as it was evident that the company had learned from its mistakes with the previous PlayStation models to create an error free model of the PlayStation. Since the PlayStation competes directly with the Xbox, comparing the PlayStation 4 with the Xbox 1 gives us a fairly good idea of how well designed the PlayStation 4 is. Sony has used a lower pricing policy in order to attract more gamers. Of course, this means that the company had to leave out the camera as a pack-in, however, most gamers didnt seem to have an issue with the absence of the camera.
          </p>
          <p>
           Despite the fact that both PS4 and Xbox one have AMDs latest technology in them, the PlayStation 4 comes with a processing speed that is roughly 40% higher. Sony Corp faced a boost in its earnings after the launch of the PS4. Gaming related sales for the company
           <a href="http://www.engadget.com/2014/05/23/sony-hirai-ps4-profit/" target="_blank">
            increased by 53%
           </a>
           . The company is expected to continue performing well with the arrival of PlayStation Now and the companys new cloud based TV streaming service. This service obviously competes directly with Apple; it is evident that Sony is prepared to take over the market and compete with bigger players in the market.
          </p>
          <p>
           After the company made this decision, the companys revenue growth exceeded the industry average of 11%. However, this did not result in higher earnings per share for the company. Even so, at the same time, the companys net operating cash flow has improved significantly by 59.15% as a result of which it can be seen that the company has not been facing any liquidity problems in its operations. This is a good sign for investors as this promises future growth that is sustainable and even though the companys earnings per share may not be increasing at present, with improvements in the companys free cash flow, the EPS of the company will eventually increase thus proving to be beneficial for the investors.
          </p>
          <p>
           With this move, Sony does not only intend to compete with its major rival Apple but also plans on
           <a href="http://www.deadline.com/2014/07/sony-video-game-playstation-now-network-open-beta/" target="_blank">
            crushing its direct competitor Nintendo
           </a>
           if all things go as planned. This will significantly increase the companys sales revenue which will result in higher earnings per share if the company manages to control its expenses at the same time.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <b>
            What does this mean for the investors?
           </b>
           <br/>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Sony is at a risky stage where if its plan succeeds, it can manage to significantly increase its profitability. However, failure can lead to the company facing losses resulting in even lower earnings per share than what it has at present. For investors, the main factor determining whether or not they should invest in Sony is how risk averse the investors are. Those investors who prefer riskier investments that promise higher returns should invest in this stock because if Sonys plan works, it will gain a lot financially thus leading to an increase in the returns that the investors get.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <b>
            So what can be concluded?
           </b>
           <br/>
          </h2>
          <p>
           Sony is trying to use a new approach in the world of technology  a place, where everything becomes obsolete within the blink of an eye. Sony is trying to ensure that the rate at which things become obsolete is slowed down. At the same time, it is trying to ensure that old games can be used to generate cash and is trying to
           <a href="http://arstechnica.com/gaming/2014/07/sony-working-on-subscription-plan-for-playstation-now-streaming/" target="_blank">
            eliminate the need for constant innovation
           </a>
           . If the company manages to succeed in its plans, it will become a market leader leaving both Apple and Nintendo behind. This will definitely add to the value of the company as a result of which investors will be able to enjoy the perks associated with investing in the company.
          </p>
