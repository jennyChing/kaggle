           <p>
            Three years ago, the artist Adonna Khare estranged from the world of arts, first off on maternity leave, and then sitting on a steady paying job at the school. And last year, she won the Kare ArtPrize in Grand Rapids, Michigan, United States, taking the award in the Peoples Choice and a prize of 200,000 dollars. The victory brought her  elephant size 10 m wide and 2 m high. This drawing was completely done in pencil, and the detail is phenomenal.
