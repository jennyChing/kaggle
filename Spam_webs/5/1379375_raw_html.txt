      <div class="content-oh-my-disney" id="article-content">
       <p>
        The world of dating is a strange place where otherwise normal human beings seemingly forget how to be human.
        <span id="more-52739">
        </span>
        The world of dating a Disney fan can be a much more extraordinary placeonce you learn that us Disney fans are maybe a bit eccentric, and a whole lot obsessed with all things Disney. Here are a few things that you might learn when dating one of us Disney fans:
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        1. A Trip to a Disney Park is Serious Business
        <br/>
        <img alt="signs you're dating a disney fan - disneyland" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-52743" height="569" src="http://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/05/signs-youre-dating-a-disney-fan-disneyland.jpg" width="850"/>
        <br/>
        You had better pick up the pace because we are on a tight schedule that weve most likely been planning for at least a month. We have to FASTPASS Indiana Jones before we go on Haunted Mansion. So just smile, nod, and try to keep up.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        2. We Will Sing Along to Every Disney Movie We Watch
        <br/>
        <img alt="signs you're dating a disney fan - scuttle" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-52749" height="467" src="http://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/05/signs-youre-dating-a-disney-fan-scuttle.png" width="850"/>
        <br/>
        So get on board, because you are in for a full concert every time we pop in a Blu-ray. This also applies to car rides, because we obviously have all the soundtracks to our favorite Disney movies at the ready.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        3. Halloween Costumes Will Be Disney Themed
        <br/>
        <img alt="signs you're dating a disney fan - the lion king" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-52750" height="477" src="http://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/05/signs-youre-dating-a-disney-fan-the-lion-king.jpg" width="850"/>
        <br/>
        This is not up for debate, will be couples themed, and is an unspoken rule for the rest of your natural life.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        4. We Will Speak in Disney Quotes
        <br/>
        <img alt="signs you're dating a disney fan - cinderella" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-52742" height="628" src="http://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/05/signs-youre-dating-a-disney-fan-cinderella.jpg" width="850"/>
        <br/>
        Whats this? Its the circle of life. I wonder Have faith in your dreams. Entire conversations will pass in this fashion. Sobe prepared.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        5. Theres Only One Option for Holiday Gifts
        <br/>
        <img alt="signs you're dating a disney fan - lady" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-52746" height="686" src="http://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/05/signs-youre-dating-a-disney-fan-lady.jpg" width="850"/>
        <br/>
        We dont even care what holiday it is. Arbor Day? We want a Disney gift. National Chocolate Chip Cookie Day? Disney gift. You get the drift.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        6. We Will Never Not Make a Mickey-Shaped Pancake
        <br/>
        <img alt="signs you're dating a disney fan - mickey pancake" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-52747" height="608" src="http://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/05/signs-youre-dating-a-disney-fan-mickey-pancake.jpg" width="850"/>
        <br/>
        We hope you like the mouse with the most because we will find a way to make almost everything we cook in his likeness.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        7. Well Inevitably Be Dating You Because You Remind us of a Disney Prince
        <br/>
        <img alt="signs you're dating a disney fan - flynn rider" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-52745" height="477" src="http://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/05/signs-youre-dating-a-disney-fan-flynn-rider.jpg" width="850"/>
        <br/>
        Most likely our favorite Disney prince. We might not admit this to you, but well probably drop subtle hints like, youre exactly like Flynn Rider! Just go with it. Its a compliment.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        8. Baby Name Discussions Will Include Disney Names
        <br/>
        <img alt="signs you're dating a disney fan - Belle" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-52741" height="486" src="http://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/05/signs-youre-dating-a-disney-fan-Belle.jpg" width="850"/>
        <br/>
        Aurora, Belle, Ariel, Flynn, Eric, Kristoff. These are all great name options that we are serious about bestowing on our kids, so start brainstorming now.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        9. We Wont Conceal our Disney Love
        <br/>
        <img alt="signs you're dating a disney fan - Elsa" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-52744" height="350" src="http://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/05/signs-youre-dating-a-disney-fan-Elsa.jpg" width="850"/>
        <br/>
        So dont ask. Were a proud group. Here we stand, in the light of day. Let the storm rage ooooooon! The cold never bothered us anyway. (See point #4.)
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        10. Were Pretty Great
        <br/>
        <img alt="signs you're dating a disney fan - mickey, donald, goofy" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-52748" height="566" src="http://a.dilcdn.com/bl/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/05/signs-youre-dating-a-disney-fan-mickey-donald-goofy.jpg" width="850"/>
        <br/>
        Us Disney fans are a special group who believe that dreams can come true, and anything is possible. So if youre lucky enough to be dating one us, keep your hands and arms inside the vehicle at all times and enjoy the ride!
       </p>
