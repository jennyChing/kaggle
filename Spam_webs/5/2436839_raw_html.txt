         <a href="http://pvs.kcc.hawaii.edu/ike/hookele/hawaiian_lunar_month.html" target="_hplink">
          the 10-day phase of the full moon
         </a>
         , meant abundance -- it was a time of the year when the
         <a href="http://www.wpcouncil.org/community/Indigenous_Display-1.pdf" target="_hplink">
          fishing would bring a plentiful harvest
         </a>
         , feeding many mouths and keeping the community alive. But it was also a time of great beauty and wonder, as the mountains and sea were illuminated under the bright light of the moon, and in so many ways, that wonder and beauty still echoes through the islands today.
        </p>
