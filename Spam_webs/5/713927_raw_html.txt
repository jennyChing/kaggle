        <h1 class="entry-title">
         How Numbers Affect Our Lives
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          Numbers have always held a deep significance, in many cultures and societies. Many of us have favourite or lucky numbers which give us reassurance when we encounter them, and were all familiar with the idea of certain numbers, such as the number 13, being unlucky.
         </p>
         <p>
          In myths, legends and traditional sayings, the same few numbers are seen far more than others. We get three wishes from a genie, there are three Fates, and Cerberus, the watchdog of the Underworld, has three heads. There are seven deadly sins and seven virtues, and seven sisters were pursued by Orion. There are nine muses, Odin spent nine days hanging on the tree Yggdrasil, and a stitch in time saves nine.
         </p>
         <p>
          In our daily lives, we constantly refer to numbers in reference to ourselves; our birth dates and National Insurance or social security numbers, for example.
