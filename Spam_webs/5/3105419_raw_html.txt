       <h1 class="entry-title">
        RECPhilly announced an all-Philly SXSW showcase and its pretty awesome
       </h1>
       <time class="published" datetime="2015-03-03T09:49:18+00:00">
        March 3rd, 2015 | 9:49AM |
       </time>
       By
       <a class="fn" href="http://thekey.xpn.org/author/jvettese/" rel="author">
        John Vettese
       </a>
       <!-- This was enclosing author name

&lt;p class="byline author vcard"&gt;&lt;/p&gt; 

-->
       <!-- this was the featured category links -->
      </header>
      <div class="entry-content">
       <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="14020987" data-link="http://thekey.xpn.org/2015/03/03/recphilly-philly-sxsw-moosh-and-twist/" data-summary="" data-title="RECPhilly announced an all-Philly SXSW showcase and it's pretty awesome">
       </div>
       <figure class="thumbnail wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_134693" style="width: 620px">
        <img alt="Moosh and Twist | Photo by Rachel Del Sordo | racheldelsordo.tumblr.com" class="size-large wp-image-134693" height="413" src="http://thekey.xpn.org/aatk/files/2014/08/MIA-254-620x413.jpg" width="620"/>
        <figcaption class="caption wp-caption-text">
         Moosh and Twist rocking Made In America last summer. They play an all-Philly showcase at SXSW | Photo by Rachel Del Sordo |
         <a href="http://racheldelsordophotography.com" target="_blank">
          racheldelsordophotography.com
         </a>
        </figcaption>
       </figure>
       <p>
        Its something Ive been saying for years: SXSW needs a Philly-centric showcase, and it needs it in the center of the action. Ive been going to the massive music industry rodeo in Austin for a few years now, and while there have been well-curated Philly unofficial Philly parties in the past 
        <a href="http://philadelphia.thedelimagazine.com/node/4876" target="_blank">
         Yvynyl and XPNs showcase
        </a>
        in 2011,
        <a href="http://thekey.xpn.org/2012/03/16/sxsw-dispatch-greetings-from-the-bitby-backyard/" target="_blank">
         BITBYs showcase
        </a>
        in 2012,
        <a href="http://thekey.xpn.org/2013/03/15/sxsw-dispatch-a-philly-centric-showcase-with-drgn-king-dangerous-ponies-blayer-pointdujour-and-more/" target="_blank">
         Bonfires showcase
        </a>
        in 2013  all took place a modest drive or a long walk from Austins bustling downtown, which is kind of where the musicians need to be to make an impact.
       </p>
       <p>
        So big shoutouts this year to the RECPhilly crew and their partners at
        <a href="http://whatscene.net/" target="_blank">
         WhatScene?
        </a>
        for putting together a Friday night show at SXSW 2015 that is (a) official and listed in the festival guide, (b) right in the thick of things on 6th Street and (c) all-Philly.
        <span id="more-153386">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        Performing at Old School Bar and Grill at 6th and Trinity on Friday, March 20th is a solid, mostly hip-hop lineup of up-and-comers and established Philly names: West Philly hero
        <a href="https://www.facebook.com/phillyfreezer" target="_blank">
         Freeway
        </a>
        , infectious party rockers
        <a href="http://www.mooshandtwist.com/" target="_blank">
         OCD: Moosh &amp; Twist
        </a>
        , the amazing
        <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/Tunji-Ige/238486586358503" target="_blank">
         Tunji Ige
        </a>
        , the suave
        <a href="http://www.chillmoody.com/" target="_blank">
         Chill Moody
        </a>
        , the soulful
        <a href="http://miltonmusiq.com/" target="_blank">
         Milton
        </a>
        , blues-rock power trio
        <a href="https://www.facebook.com/illfatednatives" target="_blank">
         Ill Fated Natives
        </a>
        and many more.
       </p>
       <p>
        Check out the full lineup in the show flyer below, and congrats again to the RECPhilly crew for putting this killer party together.
       </p>
       <p>

       </p>
