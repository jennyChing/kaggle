       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         The hungry hounds
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="cat">
          <span class="entry-cat-bg main-color-bg cat93-bg">
           <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/category/animals/">
            Animals
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="entry-cat-bg main-color-bg cat794-bg">
           <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/category/life/">
            Life
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="date post-date updated">
          January 12, 2014
         </span>
         <span class="sep">
          -
         </span>
         <span class="vcard author post-author">
          <span class="fn">
           Paul Steele
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="sep">
          -
         </span>
         <span class="comments">
          <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/2014/01/12/the-hungry-hounds/#comments">
           7 comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
       </header>
       <div class="thumbnail single-post-thumbnail section">
        <img alt="P7160032" class="attachment-wt550_300 wp-post-image" height="385" src="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P7160032.jpg" width="550"/>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         The Basset Hounds, Monty and Jasmine are here to say hello again. This time with pre dinner antics. As I have said before in
         <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/tag/basset-hounds/" target="_blank">
          previous articles
         </a>
         they will eat anything and everything they can smell and find. And oh yes they can smell the smallest scrap of a thing for miles
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/DSC_0585.jpg">
          <img alt="The hungry hounds" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-15286" height="559" src="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/DSC_0585.jpg" title="The hungry hounds" width="798"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         They know dinner time is getting close. Afternoon walk is underway, their body clocks telling them that once this is over they will be fed. They may look a dumb dog but I can assure you they are the sneakiest and clever manipulators around here. As soon as we turn the corner to head homeward that is it come on Baldy lets go faster, food now please. Back home it becomes a game.. Monty gets more and more excited as he cant control himself Jasmine, never keeping her eye off me pretends to not be bothered but stares, shaking with excitement
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1080012.jpg">
          <img alt="The hungry hounds" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-15293" height="559" src="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1080012.jpg" title="The hungry hounds" width="798"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1090021.jpg">
          <img alt="The hungry hounds" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-15292" height="559" src="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1090021.jpg" title="The hungry hounds" width="798"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         As I go get the bowls Monty gets more and more animated, shaking, singing, barking Jasmine gives up and sniffs the ground everywhere I normally step with the bag of food, the clever one of the twins.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1080015.jpg">
          <img alt="The hungry hounds" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-15288" height="559" src="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1080015.jpg" title="The hungry hounds" width="798"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P8010021.jpg">
          <img alt="The hungry hounds" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-15285" height="559" src="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P8010021.jpg" title="The hungry hounds" width="798"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1080013.jpg">
          <img alt="The hungry hounds" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-15289" height="559" src="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1080013.jpg" title="The hungry hounds" width="798"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1080021.jpg">
          <img alt="The hungry hounds" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-15290" height="559" src="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1080021.jpg" title="The hungry hounds" width="798"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now comes the only time you can ever get them to sit. I have food in my hand and it is theirs. Suddenly I have their full attention and suddenly they listen. Both shaking excitedly and looking with begging eyes before me. I prepare myself for the onslaught as I put the bowls down. It is a messy affair, they do not mess around. Slobber, ears, food and water everywhere. Yes I do feed them outside, if you had watched them you would know why bless em 
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1080023.jpg">
          <img alt="The hungry hounds" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-15291" height="559" src="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P1080023.jpg" title="The hungry hounds" width="798"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P8010024.jpg">
          <img alt="The hungry hounds" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-15287" height="559" src="http://www.baldhiker.com/wp-content/uploads/P8010024.jpg" title="The hungry hounds" width="798"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         It had been a while since an update on the hounds. They have a few adventures with us coming up so watch this space as they will be woofing through these pages again real soon.
        </p>
       </div>
