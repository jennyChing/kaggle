                 <h2>
                  <a href="/event/budapest-meeting-missions-habitable-worlds">
                   Budapest meeting: Missions to Habitable Worlds
                  </a>
                 </h2>
                </header>
                <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                 <div class="field-items">
                  <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                   <p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 10px 10px 0px; line-height: 24px; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); font-family: 'Open Sans', HelveticaNeue, 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px;">
                    The aim of the two days long conference (
                    <strong style="box-sizing: border-box;">
                     28-29 October
                    </strong>
                    ) is to provide forum for trans domain interaction in astrobiology connected to next missions - thus the organizers expect contribution from biologists, chemists, Earth scientists, astronomers, philosophers etc. On the first day mission PIs will present the astrobiology relevant aspects of ExoMars, JUICE, and next space telescope missions.
                   </p>
                   <p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 10px 10px 0px; line-height: 24px; color: rgb(102, 102, 102); font-family: 'Open Sans', HelveticaNeue, 'Helvetica Neue', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px;">
                    On the second day a "Trans-domain view of habitability" session will take place, where biologist, chemist, Earth scientist and astronomer will present how they see the term habitability. A panel discussion will follow on he habitability issue - here contribution, suggestions from anybody is welcomed.
                   </p>
