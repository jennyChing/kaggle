          <h2 class="av-special-heading-tag" itemprop="headline">
           <span class="special_amp">
            
           </span>
           Myths of the Belize Offshore company
           <span class="special_amp">
            
           </span>
          </h2>
          <div class="special-heading-border">
           <div class="special-heading-inner-border">
           </div>
          </div>
         </div>
         <section class="av_textblock_section" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/BlogPosting">
          <div class="avia_textblock " itemprop="text">
           <h4>
           </h4>
           <h4>
            1.Illegal:
           </h4>
           <p>
            This myth is so nonsensical, why? because international trade in widely advertised as a good thing, yet when someone opens a branch or moves his company over to an offshore, he becomes a traitor. Also the
            <a href="http://belizebusinessformations.com/belize-company-formation/" target="_blank">
             Belize offshore company
            </a>
            is simply saving on taxes because by law of that country the offshore location allows it and taxes earned outside the USA are not taxable in most case.
           </p>
           <h4>
            2.Evading Taxes:
           </h4>
           <p>
            If a business/company is not located in your country, why should the government be envious and feel entitled to tax it? Are citizens not allowed to spent their money as they pleased?
           </p>
           <h4>
            3.High Maintenance:
           </h4>
           <p>
            There is nothing high maintenance about an
            <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Offshore_company" target="_blank">
             offshore company
            </a>
            , unless you hire a crappy manager! Between the exchange rate, extremely low cost of wages, and tax exemption, your profits (comparing your company in the US and company in Belize) will definitely be much better here.
           </p>
           <h4>
            4.Difficult process:
           </h4>
           <p>
            If you had thought it out and worked with a registered agent, you will not be faced with this issue. Having a registered agent means there is someone who knows how to get the job done just as he/she knows the lines in the palm of his/her hand. Which means, you get to relax at home or in office while they wait in a line somewhere for their turn to get your documents approved/signed.
           </p>
           <h4>
            5.For the rich and wealthy:
           </h4>
           <p>
            This is a great option for medium to large businesses. However, even if you are just an ordinary person who has no business to own or no asset to protect, you can open a bank account for about $500usd and you will not be question as long as your transfers does not exceed $10,000 per month. To be on the safe side, please do not transfer $9500 and try to claim its logical because its under the limit.
           </p>

    var d = new Date();
    d.setTime(d.getTime() + (exdays*24*60*60*1000));
    var expires = "expires=" + d.toGMTString();
    document.cookie = cname+"="+cvalue+"; "+expires;
}

function csbwfsGetCookie(cname) {
    var name = cname + "=";
    var ca = document.cookie.split(';');
    for(var i=0; i&lt;ca.length; i++) {
        var c = ca[i];
        while (c.charAt(0)==' ') c = c.substring(1);
        if (c.indexOf(name) != -1) {
            return c.substring(name.length, c.length);
        }
    }
    return "";
}
