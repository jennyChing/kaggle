       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Home
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Welcome to the Public Index, a site to study and discuss the Google Books litigation.  This site, which is no longer actively maintained, includes a comprehensive collection of
         <a href="http://www.thepublicindex.org/settlement">
          legal documents
         </a>
         relating to the settlement.  Click
         <a href="http://www.thepublicindex.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/19/docs/amended_settlement/opinion.pdf">
          here
         </a>
         to read Judge Chins March 2011 opinion rejecting the proposed settlement. We also have a
         <a href="http://www.thepublicindex.org/documents/amended_settlement/opinion-rejecting-the-amended-settlement">
          web version
         </a>
         with links to the filings it discusses.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Events and Deadlines
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.thepublicindex.org/documents/post-rejection">
           Authors Guild v. Google
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Class certification appeal fully argued, awaiting decision
         </li>
         <li>
          All other proceedings currently stayed
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.thepublicindex.org/documents/cases/asmp">
           ASMP v. Google
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Fact discovery completed by May 17, 2013
         </li>
         <li>
          Expert discovery completed by July 15, 2013
         </li>
         <li>
          All other aspects stayed pending Second Circuits decision in
          <em>
           Authors Guild v. Google
          </em>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.thepublicindex.org/documents/cases/hathitrust">
           Authors Guild v. HathiTrust
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          May 28, 2013: Defendants file responses brief in appeal
         </li>
        </ul>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         The Public Index was a project of the Public-Interest Book Search Initiative and the
         <a href="http://www.nyls.edu/centers/harlan_scholar_centers/institute_for_information_law_and_policy">
          Institute for Information Law and Policy
         </a>
         at
         <a href="http://www.nyls.edu/">
          New York Law School
         </a>
         .  The Public Index was compiled by a group of professors, students, and volunteers who strongly felt that the Google Book Search lawsuit and settlement deserved a full, careful, and thoughtful public discussion.  The Public Index was a site for people from all points of view to learn from each other about the settlement and join together to make their voices heard in the public debate.
        </p>
        <p>
         Note: The Public Index is no longer actively maintained.
        </p>
       </div>
