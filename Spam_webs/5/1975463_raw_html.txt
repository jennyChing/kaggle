         <p>
          25 awesome Easter recipes! That s what I wanted to find. So, I asked all the moms at dance last week what they ate for Easter. I got a lot of basic brunch food and ham and green bean casserole answers. So, I decided to come up with a list that was a little out of the ordinary because it seems we all eat the same old boring stuff. I turned to
          <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/debclembuckert" target="_blank">
           Pinterest
          </a>
          to find great recipes by bloggers that I found interesting and would want to try myself (and of course, I threw in one of my own just for fun).
         </p>
         <p>
          So, here it is. Its got a little bit of everything, starting with brunch (which seems to be quite popular after church), into dinner and then dessert. I even threw in a couple of Spring cocktails that sounded totally yummy. There are also paleo and gluten free selections for my clean eating friends. Hope this gives you a little inspiration for your Easter meal. Im planning to try everything on the list. Just not all at once.
