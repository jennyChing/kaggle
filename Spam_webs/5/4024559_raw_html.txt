     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      Students from the iSEDE team are preparing their experiment for integration on their CubeSat as part of the Balloon-borne EXperiments for University Students (
      <a href="http://www.rexusbexus.net/index.php?option=com_content&amp;view=article&amp;id=77&amp;Itemid=55">
       BEXUS
      </a>
      ) project.
     </p>
     <div align="center">
      <script type="text/javascript">

     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      The REXUS/BEXUS program allows students from universities and higher education colleges across Europe to carry out scientific and technological experiements on research rockets and balloons. Each year, two rockets and two balloons are launched carrying up to 20 experiements designed and built by student teams.
     </p>
