          Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Energy Bites

        <p>
         Id like to restate a popular theory:
         <em>
          there is nothing new in the world today
         </em>
         . But yet, somehow, we trudge on. Go figure. Sorry for the negativity. Its just that when I set out to make these
         <em>
          Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Energy Bites
         </em>
         , I thought it was a concept that I had seen once or twice. You know, a smidge? But when I did a quick search before publishing this post, I saw that there are exactly 3.39 gazillion different energy bite recipes online. So, here I am sharing 3.39 gazillionand one. Enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Peanut Butter Energy Bites with mini chocolate chips" class="lazy lazy-hidden aligncenter size-full wp-image-16010" data-lazy-src="//namelymarly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Peanut-Butter-Energy-Bites-12.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="899" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="596"/>
        </p>
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Peanut Butter Energy Bites with mini chocolate chips" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16010" height="899" src="//namelymarly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Peanut-Butter-Energy-Bites-12.jpg" width="596"/>
        </noscript>
        <h3>
         Marly and the Caped Super Eater
        </h3>
        <p>
         Sometimes I like to go to a coffee shop to get some work done. I dont know why I can be more productive in a noisy room full of people, but it works every time. So on Friday when I found myself in Starbucks waiting for my Chai Tea with Steamed Soy (oh yes, its a thing), I sawa notebook on the counter. I said the barista, I think someone forgot their notebook.
        </p>
        <p>
         He responded, Nope. We put that there. Every now and then we like to ask our patrons a fun question. People come up and write their answers if they want.
        </p>
        <p>
         I pretended, at least momentarily, to be uninterested. What kind of stupido would jump to a notebook and answer a question from a stranger?
        </p>
        <p>
         But I kind of moseyed my way up to the counter and peaked over at the notebook. The question scrawled at the top of the page was, If you could have any kind of super power, what would it be?
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, thats too easy, I thought to myself. I know exactly what super power I want. Ive even plotted it for years, but could I be brave enough to write it down for others to see?
        </p>
        <p>
         Just then my little Bren Brown angel appeared on my shoulder, Dont be afraid to be vulnerable, Marly Bren whispered in my ear. Oh, and your chai tea is ready. I smiled. That Brensure is thoughtful.
        </p>
        <p>
         So, I grabbed my chai tea, picked up the pen and wrote,
         <strong>
          My super power would be Super Eater, eating my way through any and all tragedies, and yetstill able to wear a bikini.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Oh, and a cape. All super power people need a cape.
        </p>
        <p>
         Hey. A girls gotta dream.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Peanut Butter Energy Bites are made with honey, peanut butter, chocolate chips, and more healthy goodness!" class="lazy lazy-hidden aligncenter size-large wp-image-16011" data-lazy-src="//namelymarly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Peanut-Butter-Energy-Bites16-637x960.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="960" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="637"/>
        </p>
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Peanut Butter Energy Bites are made with honey, peanut butter, chocolate chips, and more healthy goodness!" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-16011" height="960" src="//namelymarly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Peanut-Butter-Energy-Bites16-637x960.jpg" width="637"/>
        </noscript>
        <p>
         <img alt="Ground golden fax seeds are the secret healthy ingredient in these Peanut Butter Energy Bites" class="lazy lazy-hidden aligncenter size-large wp-image-16012" data-lazy-src="//namelymarly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Peanut-Butter-Energy-Bites18-637x960.jpg" data-lazy-type="image" height="960" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="637"/>
        </p>
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Ground golden fax seeds are the secret healthy ingredient in these Peanut Butter Energy Bites" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-16012" height="960" src="//namelymarly.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Peanut-Butter-Energy-Bites18-637x960.jpg" width="637"/>
        </noscript>
        <p>
         It wasnt long after I wrote that down, that I heard people reading my line out loud. One barista even read it out loud to another barista and they laughed. It wasnt a isnt that stupid kind of laugh. It was a isnt that too funny kind of laugh.
        </p>
        <p>
         I like making people laugh. Now I just need a hill to stand triumphantly on while my cape bustles in the wind.
        </p>
        <h3>
         What I love About these Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Energy Bites
        </h3>
        <p>
         I first learned about the idea of Energy Bites at
         <a href="http://choppedcon.com" target="_blank" title="Chopped Conference">
          Chopped Conference
         </a>
         . We invited local chefs to share their favorite appetizers during one of the receptions and
         <a href="https://twitter.com/kylewilliams923" target="_blank" title="Chef Kyle, Chef Kyle Williams">
          Chef Kyle
         </a>
         made these delicious, bite-size balls he called Energy Bites. I was so busy with everything that day I hardly had anything to eat, so when the reception started, I grabbed one (or two) just about every time I walked by his table. Yumminess!
        </p>
        <p>
         These Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Energy Bites are:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          super easy to make  less than 10 ingredients and 10 minutes!
         </li>
         <li>
          super healthy  theyre packed with Omega 3s thanks to the ground flax seeds
         </li>
         <li>
          lightly sweetened, so if youre trying to reduce sugar, these are a perfect lightly sweet treat
         </li>
         <li>
          with the addition of dark chocolate chips, theyre so satisfying
         </li>
         <li>
          perfect for a light pick-me-up snack or even for breakfast with a cup of hot tea!
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Need I say more?
        </p>

          <div class="sr-ingredients-wrap">
           <h3>
            Ingredients
           </h3>
           <div class="sr-ingredients">
            <ul>
             <li>
              <span itemprop="ingredients">
                cup peanut butter
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span itemprop="ingredients">
                cup agave, maple syrup, or honey
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span itemprop="ingredients">
                teaspoon vanilla
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span itemprop="ingredients">
               1 cup oatmeal, uncooked
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span itemprop="ingredients">
                cup coconut flakes
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span itemprop="ingredients">
                cup golden flax seeds, ground
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span itemprop="ingredients">
                mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
              </span>
             </li>
            </ul>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="sr-instructions-wrap">
           <h3>
            Instructions
           </h3>
           <div class="sr-instructions">
            <span itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             <ol>
              <li>
               Place everything except the chocolate chips in a food processor. Pulse for a few seconds and push down the ingredients and pulse again until the ingredients are combined.
              </li>
              <li>
               Add the chocolate chips and give it one more very quick pulse to combine. I like to reserve a few chocolate chips to roll the finished energy bites to make them look irresistible.
              </li>
              <li>
               Roll the dough into bite-size balls, roll in extra chocolate chips if you'd like (and who doesn't?), and then store in the fridge. Allow the energy bites some time to chill in the fridge for about 5-10 minutes before serving to allow them to firm up or eat them in their ooey gooey room temperature state.
              </li>
             </ol>
            </span>
