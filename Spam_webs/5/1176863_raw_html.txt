        <p>
         <strong>
          EARLIER:
         </strong>
         A FACEBOOK page exposing Toowoomba's worst motorists has ended much the way it started - in a storm of controversy.
        </p>
        <p>
         Toowoomba's Bad Parkers and Drivers has closed down.
        </p>
        <p>
         The page's demise started at the weekend when administrators took aim at their critics.
        </p>
        <p>
         In a lengthy post, they claimed a "gang" of people opposed to Toowoomba's Bad Parkers and Drivers had "grown out of control across multiple Facebook hate pages".
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.thechronicle.com.au/news/parker-gets-no-apology-for-facebook-shaming/2053474/" target="_blank">
          <strong>

          </strong>

         Administrators claimed the opponents had incorrectly "directed all their rage" at a person believed to be behind the page.
        </p>
        <p>
         "They have harassed him at home while his family is there," the Facebook post read.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.thechronicle.com.au/news/online-crusader-exposes-locations-city-speed-camer/2103073/" target="_blank">
