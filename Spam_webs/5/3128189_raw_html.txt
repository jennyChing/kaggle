        <div class="blog-heading">
         <h2>
          <span>
           Welcome to On the Vine at Richmond Farms!
          </span>
         </h2>
        </div>
        <div class="blog">
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">
           <strong style="font-size: 1.6em;">
            <span style="color: #008000;">
             NOW TAKING 2015 CSA MEMBERS!
            </span>
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="fontSize3" style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
           Located on about 4 acres, we pride ourselves on the quality and freshness of our organically grown vegetables and herbs, the vibrancy of our flowers, and the neighborly presence of our farm.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="fontSize3" style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
           Our CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) offers membership to the farm, providing you with a closer relationship to your food and its production. You can receive a discount on your membership by becoming a "working member" and enjoy the hands-on experience of working the land to bring seedlings to saut!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="fontSize3" style="font-family: tahoma,arial,helvetica,sans-serif;">
           We are also a market farm, providing farm fresh items to stop-in customers. No need to get up early on Saturday for a Farmers' Market -- we have everything you need on a daily basis. Check us out during the growing season any time you see our roadside signs!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
