     <h2 class="posttitle">
      Flatbread with Hummus and Vegetables
     </h2>
     <div class="postmeta">
      <em>
       posted on
      </em>
      September 25, 2014
      <em>
       by
      </em>
      Rachel Gurk
      <div class="sep">
      </div>
     </div>
     <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
     <p>
      <em>
       This flatbread with hummus and vegetables is the perfect appetizer or meal. Filling, healthy, delicious and easy to make.
      </em>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.rachelcooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/silk-almond-milk-600-1-of-4.jpg">
       <img alt="Hummus Flatbread Pizza - a great appetizer or a meal! On RachelCooks.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9408" height="741" src="http://www.rachelcooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/silk-almond-milk-600-1-of-4.jpg" width="600"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      I recently had the absolute pleasure of touring the
      <a href="http://sabra.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
       Sabra
      </a>
      * headquarters in Richmond, Virginia and also getting a tour of the chickpea fields.
     </p>
     <p>
      Hey, look, its me on a chickpea farm! Farming chickpeas. Clearly. Wearing boots that took up half my suitcase. Necessities for chickpea farms.
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe frameborder="0" height="710" scrolling="no" src="//instagram.com/p/qwYZeNk5zo/embed/" width="612">
      </iframe>
      <span id="more-9022">
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      And here are the little lovelies right off the plantwe even tried a few in this state. Chewy but tasty!
     </p>
     <p>
      <iframe frameborder="0" height="710" scrolling="no" src="//instagram.com/p/qwZmsNk51V/embed/" width="612">
      </iframe>
     </p>
     <p>
      I came back inspired to create all sorts of
      <a href="http://sabra.com/products/Classic-Hummus" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
       hummus
      </a>
      recipes (not to mention all their other great products  the yogurt dips are my favorite!). This recipe may seem simple but its a great lunch or a fun appetizer. Plus, lets be real, we all know simple recipes are my favorite kind of recipes. This flatbread is easy to make, healthy, and so delicious. And its totally customizable like many of my recipes are.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.rachelcooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/silk-almond-milk-600-3-of-4.jpg">
       <img alt="Hummus Flatbread Pizza - a great appetizer or a meal! On RachelCooks.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9410" height="707" src="http://www.rachelcooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/silk-almond-milk-600-3-of-4.jpg" width="600"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Bake up your favorite pizza crust (bonus points for whole wheat!). Spread on your favorite variety of Sabra hummus. Top with fresh, crunchy vegetables. Serve.
     </p>
     <p>
      EASY.
     </p>
     <p>
      I likened mineto one of my favorite Lebanese salads  fattoush. I mean, its not a perfect match to the salad of course, but I topped the Sabra Classic Hummus with cucumbers, tomatoes, radishes and parsley. Its beautiful, healthy and clearly tastes wonderful. If youre in a hurry and dont want to bake a pizza crust, this whole shebang would be great on top of a big ol pita.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://www.rachelcooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/silk-almond-milk-600-2-of-4.jpg">
       <img alt="Hummus Flatbread Pizza - a great appetizer or a meal! On RachelCooks.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9409" height="406" src="http://www.rachelcooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/silk-almond-milk-600-2-of-4.jpg" width="600"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      What veggies would you throw on your flatbread pizza?
     </p>
     <div class="recipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
      <meta content="http://www.rachelcooks.com/2014/09/25/flatbread-with-hummus-vegetables/" itemprop="url"/>
      <h2 itemprop="name">
       Flatbread with Hummus and Vegetables
      </h2>
      <div class="time">
       <p>
        <strong>
         Yield:
        </strong>
        <span itemprop="recipeYield">
         12 appetizer servings
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Prep Time:
        </strong>
        <meta content="PT15M" itemprop="prepTime"/>
        15 minutes
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Total Time:
        </strong>
        <meta content="PT15M" itemprop="totalTime"/>
        15 minutes
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="summary" itemprop="description">
       <p>
        This flatbread with hummus and vegetables is the perfect appetizer or meal. Filling, healthy, delicious and easy to make.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="sep">
      </div>
      <img align="right" class="photo nopin" height="125" itemprop="image" nopin="nopin" src="http://www.rachelcooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/silk-almond-milk-600-1-of-4-180x180.jpg" width="125"/>
      <h3 id="ingredients">
       Ingredients:
      </h3>
      <div class="ingredients">
       <ul>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 pizza crust, cooked and cooled
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         10 ounce package of Sabra Classic Hummus
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1/4 of an English cucumber, cut into bite-size pieces
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         3 radishes, thinly sliced or julienned
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
        </li>
        <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
         about 1/4 cup fresh parsley, torn or chopped
        </li>
       </ul>
      </div>
      <h3 id="directions" style="clear:left">
       Directions:
      </h3>
      <div class="instructions" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
       <p>
        Make sure your baked pizza crust is cooled completely. Spread evenly with hummus. Top with vegetables. Cut and serve. Best served immediately, but store any leftovers in refrigerator.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="notes">
       <p>
        Note: Feel free to sub out Classic hummus for your favorite Sabra variety.
       </p>
       <p>
        Note: Have fun changing up this pizza with different vegetables.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="printbutton">
       <a class="print" href="http://www.rachelcooks.com/2014/09/25/flatbread-with-hummus-vegetables/print/" title="Print Recipe">
        Print Recipe
       </a>
      </div>
     </div>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Verdict:
      </strong>
      Loved this as a healthy, filling, easy meal.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Husbands take:
      </strong>
      He loved this too!
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Changes I would make:
      </strong>
      None are necessary, but as I mentioned, have fun with the toppings and the hummus flavor!
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Difficulty:
      </strong>
      Easy peasy. And if it isnt easy enough, you can always pick up a pre-baked pizza crust.
     </p>

      <p>
