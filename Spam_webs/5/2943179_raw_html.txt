         <p>
          <a href="http://www.funkyspacemonkey.com/supercharge-ios-7-spotlight-search/search_ios_cydia_fsmdotcom" rel="attachment wp-att-46498">
           <img alt="search+_iOS_Cydia_FSMdotCOM" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-46498" height="612" src="http://cdn.funkyspacemonkey.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/search+_iOS_Cydia_FSMdotCOM.jpg" width="700"/>
          </a>
          Search+ is a new jailbreak tweak by developer Ma Jun., that uses Activator to make Spotlight more accessible and supercharges it with many new features. Search+ will also force Spotlight to support landscape orientation.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-46497">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          After installing Search+, you can access Spotlight from anywhere using Activator. Once installed, just assign an Activator action to the tweak and you invoke Spotlight even if youre in an app.
         </p>
         <p>
          With Search+ installed you can quickly call and/or send a text/iMessage to a contact by tapping and holding on the contacts name in the search results until an Action Menu pops up.
         </p>
         <p>
          The tweak also blurs out the UI behind Spotlights search interface and will allow you to clear recent search history from the Spotlight Search.
         </p>
         <p>
          The tweak comes with its own dedicated preference pane in the Settings.app where you can configure all the options that Search+ has to offer  enable/disable the tweak, choose your Activator/QuickDo triggers, enable/disable Seatch+ on the Lock Screen and more
         </p>
         <p>
          Search+ is available for $0.99 on Cydia Store via BigBoss repo ( includes a 3-day free trial )
         </p>
