       <p>
        In a satured domain, its very hard to come with something new, something that can inspire others to build new designs. Searching over and over the internet, I found a talented guy who succed creating a inspiring collection of instagram experiments. His work demonstrates creativity.
        <span id="more-85815">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        The author,
        <a class="external" href="http://www.behance.net/cintascotch" id="profile-username-text" rel="nofollow">
         Javier Perez
        </a>
        , isa talented graphic designer / audiovisual producer from Guayaquil-Ecuador. Hiswork is very simple and minimal. He wants that the person can take a break of the saturation of photos in general.
