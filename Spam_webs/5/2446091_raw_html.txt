       <h1 class="entry-title full-title">
        <a href="http://www.geekytattoos.com/sherlock-holmes-in-text/" rel="bookmark" rev="post-1843" title="Permanent link to Sherlock Holmes In Text">
         Sherlock Holmes In Text
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-content full-content">
        <div class="image">
         <a href="http://www.geekytattoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/Sherlock.png">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1844" height="749" src="http://www.geekytattoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/Sherlock-500x749.png" title="Sherlock Holmes" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I think most people would agree with me when I say that Sherlock Holmes was a geek.  Come on, think about it for a minute.  No, stop thinking about Robert Downey Jr and how he made Sherlock Holmes all cool and stuff.  The old school Sherlock Holmes that could deduce your entire life story from a small smudge of mud on your pants leg.
        </p>
        <p>
         Allie, a graphic designer from Boston, sent in her tattoo of Sherlock Holmes complete with pipe and magnifying glass.  What makes this extra special geeky is that its all done with the letters in his name with different fonts.  Pretty awesome, eh?
        </p>
