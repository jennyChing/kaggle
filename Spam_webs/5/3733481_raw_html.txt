          Is The Bebecar Ip-Op The Ideal Stroller For You?
         </h1>

          <p>
           The Bebecar Ip-Op pushchair is amongst the top strollers on the market today, combining a compact, easy folding chassis with ultra-modern design. As well as being extremely stylish, the Ip-Op is also equally excellent in terms of build quality, comfort and safety.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Why opt for the Ip-Op?
           </strong>
           <br/>
            Assembling the Bebecar Ip-Op is really simple. Due to its chassis with a specially designed easy-lock attachment system, it is easy to fit or remove each of the available seating options, allowing you to switch from pushchair seat to carrycot, or infant car seat by simply squeezing on either side of the seating unit and then lifting it off of the chassis.
           <br/>
            Folding this Bebecar stroller is also quite simple. It can be folded with a seat, carrycot or infant car seat still attached, and features a one handed scissor folding mechanism, meaning that it is convenient for busy parents, holding their child with one arm, and quickly folding with the other. Once folded, the Bebecar Ip-Op chassis is completely freestanding and fits easily into a medium sized car.
           <br/>
            Whilst not the lightest strollers, Bebecar pushchairs are extremely robust and hardwearing, making them equally as ideal for a newborn as a boisterous toddler, and suitable for mothers and fathers that do a lot of walking or travel on public transport.
           <br/>
            One of the most attractive features to parents that love to shop is that storage under the seating unit is very well designed. As the shopping basket is lower at the front, it is easy for parents to access this to put down their bags, or retrieve them without disturbing their baby. In many strollers, it is often difficult to get to under-seat storage space without having to move a footrest, or even the seating unit, which could understandably wake up a sleeping child.
           <br/>
            All seating options can be either forward or rear facing and are easily adjustable.
           <br/>
            The Bebecar Ip-Op is designed with a strong focus on comfort, both for the parent and passenger. For the parent, the handle height is completely adjustable, through little more effort than the click of a button. Adjustable rear suspension and easy swivel wheel systems make sure that the parent can manoeuvre their stroller with ease, whilst the child rides in comfort. In addition to providing a smooth ride for parent and child, the Bebecar Ip-Op features soft padded chest and buckle pads; keeping the child safe and secure without affecting their comfort.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_849" style="width: 305px">

           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Bebecar Ip-Op
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Suitable from birth, the Ip-Op by Bebecar is the perfect stroller for on the go parents that want a highly stylish and fashionable pushchair that is also robust enough to keep up with their busy schedule. Although heavier than some similar strollers, the Bebecar Ip-Op folds easily and is ideal for public transport.
          </p>
