          <h1>
           Alter Bridge Wins Top Guitar Solo [VIDEO]
          </h1>
          <div class="post_meta">
           <span class="author_avatar">
            <a href="http://q103albany.com/author/robdawes/" title="WQBK">
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="the_author">
            <span>
             By
            </span>
            <a href="http://q103albany.com/author/robdawes/" title="WQBK">
             WQBK
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="the_date">
            March 24, 2011 11:17 AM
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="social-group-top large">
           <div class="social_icons size_large">
            <a class="facebook share_link" href="http://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?u=http%3A%2F%2Fq103albany.com%2Falter-bridge-wins-top-guitar-solo%2F%3Ftrackback%3Dfbshare_top&amp;t=Alter+Bridge+Wins+Top+Guitar+Solo+%5BVIDEO%5D" target="_blank" title="Share on Facebook">
             <div class="social_icon_large">
             </div>
             <span class="social_icon">
              Share on Facebook
             </span>
            </a>
            <a class="twitter share_link" href="http://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Check+out+Alter+Bridge+Wins+Top+Guitar+Solo+%5BVIDEO%5D+http%3A%2F%2Fq103albany.com%2Falter-bridge-wins-top-guitar-solo%2F%3Ftrackback%3Dtwitter_top+%40q103albany" target="_blank" title="Share on Twitter">
             <div class="social_icon_large">
             </div>
             <span class="social_icon">
              Share on Twitter
             </span>
            </a>
            <span class="social-submenu">
            </span>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="the_content clearfix">
           <p>
            A recent poll to find the Greatest Guitar Solo in historyhas Alter Bridge guitarists Mark Tremonti andMyles Kennedy as number one.
            <span id="more-10654">
            </span>
           </p>
           <figure class="alignleft" id="attachment_10659">
            <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-10659" height="195" src="http://wac.450F.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/q103albany.com/files/2011/03/Alterbridge-300x195.jpg" title="Alterbridge" width="300"/>
            <figcaption style="width:300px;">
             Photo by Frazer Harrison/Getty Images
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            Guitarist Magazine took nominations from readers andnarrowed them down to the most popular twenty beforeopening it to thepublic.
           </p>
           <p>
            When told of the accomplishment Tremonti and Kennedy wereblown away:
           </p>
           <p>
            Thank you! Really honored to have even been included among such iconic solos. A little uncomfortable with the idea that it was voted the best. I dont necessarily agree that our solo deserves that title  at least my half
           </p>
           <p>
            Tremonti also thanked thesupport of fans in getting the top spot:
           </p>
           <p>
            It was an immense honour to be nominated in this poll. Looking at the other listed nominees it seemed absolutely impossible to win. We definitely feel unworthy and want to thank our die-hard fan base for voting!
           </p>
           <p>
            When asked who they liked forfavorite solo both picked  David Gilmours Comfortably Numb, which came in at #5
           </p>
           <p>
            The top twenty solos of all time reads
           </p>
           <p>
            1. Alter Bridge  Blackbird (Mark Tremonti/Myles Kennedy)
            <br/>
            2. Van Halen  Eruption (Eddie Van Halen)
            <br/>
            3. Guns N Roses  Sweet Child O Mine (Slash)
            <br/>
            4. Guns N Roses  November Rain (Slash)
            <br/>
            5. Pink Floyd  Comfortably Numb (David Gilmour)
            <br/>
            6. Dream Theater  Hollow Years (John Petrucci, Live At Budokan)
            <br/>
            7. Dream Theater  Under A Glass Moon (John Petrucci)
            <br/>
            8. Led Zeppelin  Stairway To Heaven (Jimmy Page)
            <br/>
            9. Lynyrd Skynyrd  Free Bird (Gary Rossington/Allen Collins)
            <br/>
            10. Megadeth  Tornado Of Souls (Marty Friedman)
            <br/>
            11. Jimi Hendrix  Voodoo Child (Slight Return)
            <br/>
            12. Steve Vai  For the Love Of God
            <br/>
            13. Jimi Hendrix  All Along The Watchtower
            <br/>
            14. The Eagles  Hotel California (Joe Walsh/Don Felder)
            <br/>
            15. Dire Straits  Sultans Of Swing (Mark Knopfler)
            <br/>
            16. Led Zeppelin  Since Ive Been Loving You (Jimmy Page)
            <br/>
            17. Gary Moore  Still Got The Blues
            <br/>
            18. Michael Jackson  Beat It (Eddie Van Halen)
            <br/>
            19. Manic Street Preachers  Motorcycle Emptiness (James Dean Bradfield)
            <br/>
            20. Jethro Tull  Aqualung (Martin Barre)
           </p>
           <p>
            <iframe frameborder="0" height="390" scrolling="auto" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/uv-EZx-qI_8" width="480">
            </iframe>
           </p>
           <p>
            Q103 presents Alterbridgelive in concert at
            <a href="http://www.northernlightslive.com">
             Northern Lights
            </a>
            in Clifton Park on May 18th, tickets are still available.
           </p>

            Keep listening to Q103 to win your pair including exclusive meet n greets.
