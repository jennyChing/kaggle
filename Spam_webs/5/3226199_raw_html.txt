         <div id="description-text">
          George Washington Dollar Coin Ring
          <br/>
          <br/>
          This coin ring is a beautiful handcrafted and hand polished George Washington ring in sizes 7-15. These rings feature a high polished finish with beautifully raised details both inside and outside of the ring.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Coin rings make a great gift for those that are hard to buy for.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Each ring is crafted from a circulated coin retaining its individual characteristics both inside and out.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          All coin ring orders come with a nice velvet drawstring gift bag.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          All coin ring orders are shipped USPS so that you can have your handcrafted coin ring as quickly as possible.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Material : Copper with manganese brass cladding
          <br/>
          US ring size: 7-15
          <br/>
          <br/>
          I really want you to be 100% satisfied with your purchase so please have your finger professionally sized before placing an order if you are not sure about your ring size.
