        <p>
         <em>
          Recipe brought to you in collaboration with Cobblestone Bread Co.
         </em>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/Roasted-Tomato-Yellow-Fin-Tuna-Panini-Melt.jpg">
          <img alt="Roasted Tomato &amp; Yellow Fin Tuna Panini Melt" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-49161" height="1157" src="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/Roasted-Tomato-Yellow-Fin-Tuna-Panini-Melt-725x1157.jpg" width="725"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Roasted Tomato &amp; Yellow Fin Tuna Panini Melt is a step beyond the classic tuna melt sandwich we are all familiar with. Its been kicked up a notch to make it gourmet Super Bowl fare worthy. Lots of flavor, but not fishiness. Great ingredients make it easy.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/Roasted-Tomato-Yellow-Fin-Tuna-Panini-Melt1.jpg">
          <img alt="Roasted Tomato Yellow Fin Tuna Panini Melt" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-49162" height="484" src="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/Roasted-Tomato-Yellow-Fin-Tuna-Panini-Melt1-725x484.jpg" width="725"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The sandwich starts with
         <a href="http://cobblestonebreadco.com/recipes/?utm_source=mom_foodie_blog&amp;&amp;utm_medium=content&amp;&amp;utm_campaign=gameday" target="_blank">
          Cobblestone Bread Co.
         </a>
         s Knead for Seed Wheat Bread. My daughter and I love this bread. I actually had to hide a loaf so I would have it to make this recipe. I selected Yellowfin tuna packed in water (can or jarred), for great flavor without the off-putting fishiness of many tunas. Rather than the old school mayonnaise, I felt the Yellowfin deserved something better. Flavorful roasted tomatoes and the oil they were packed in become both a dressing and primary ingredient. Finally, a nice sharp aged cheddar gives the panini a great punch of flavor.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Making the Roasted Tomato &amp; Yellowfin Tuna Panini Melt
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/roasted-tomatoes-and-yellowfin-tuna.jpg">
          <img alt="roasted tomatoes and yellowfin tuna" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-49163" height="483" src="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/roasted-tomatoes-and-yellowfin-tuna-725x483.jpg" width="725"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Mix together roasted tomatoes (recipe below sandwich recipe, if you need it) and Yellowfin tuna.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/sharp-cheddar-knead-for-seed-bread-and-tuna.jpg">
          <img alt="sharp cheddar, knead for seed bread and tuna" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-49164" height="483" src="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/sharp-cheddar-knead-for-seed-bread-and-tuna-725x483.jpg" width="725"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Cut sharp cheddar, arrange on
         <a href="http://cobblestonebreadco.com/recipes/?utm_source=mom_foodie_blog&amp;&amp;utm_medium=content&amp;&amp;utm_campaign=gameday" target="_blank">
          Knead for Seed Wheat Bread
         </a>
         and top with tuna-tomato mixture. Place another slice of bread on top.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/pressing-the-panini.jpg">
          <img alt="pressing the panini" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-49165" height="478" src="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/pressing-the-panini-725x478.jpg" width="725"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Apply butter and give it a hot pressing.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/Roasted-Tomato-and-Yellowfin-Tuna-Panini.jpg">
          <img alt="Roasted Tomato and Yellowfin Tuna Panini Melt" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-49169" height="483" src="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/Roasted-Tomato-and-Yellowfin-Tuna-Panini-725x483.jpg" width="725"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/tomato-tuna-panini.jpg">
          <img alt="tomato tuna panini" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-49167" height="1114" src="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/tomato-tuna-panini-725x1114.jpg" width="725"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
        </p>
