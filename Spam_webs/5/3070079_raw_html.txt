       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Packing 20Kg/ Pail
       </p>
       <p>
        Air Setting Mortar Product is general purpose high strength mortar. Air setting mortar is an air setting refractory mortar aluminum comes in a plastic, ready to use form. it is usually used as a clustering mortar for low or high alumina dense refractory brickwork.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Air Setting Mortar Classification :
        </strong>
        It is not advisable to Air Setting Mortar above a maximum temperature of 1600  C (= MST) to use.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Air Setting Mortar How to use
       </h3>
       <p>
        So easy to use Air Setting Mortar because it is a ready to use product. Air setting mortar mix well before use. Blade can be when the mortar frozen. It has to be covered after use when it starts on contact with air. Only 1/16 to 1/8 connection, the. Performed by applying on a flint and 35 to 40 pounds should be straight pieces (depending on the total thickness) is used on hundreds of 94  x2  .
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Composition :
        </strong>
        Air Setting Mortar a mortar on the aluminum with a typical aluminum oxide content of 35.0% to 70.0% and Silicon dioxide (SiO2) of 15.0% to 55.0%.
       </p>
