      <h1>
       Income generating secrets Wall Street doesn't want you to know
      </h1>
      <div class="author-full-name">
       by Uncle Bob Williams
      </div>
      <div id="main">
       <p>
        Most Americans have at least some assets tied up in the stock market.  Too many of them let their mutual funds or brokers handle their accounts; they simply check their statements quarterly to see how much money they made or lost.  As a result, most are sorely disappointed with their returns over the past few years.
       </p>
       <p>
        So it isn't surprising that more people are taking a personal interest in learning how financial markets work and are making some or all of their own financial decisions.  And this trend is being driven by on-line trading companies that make it easy and inexpensive to trade in various financial markets.
       </p>
       <p>
        But
        <b>
         <i>
          easy-to-trade
         </i>
        </b>
        and
        <b>
         <i>
          easy-to-make a profit
         </i>
        </b>
        are rarely two sides of the same coin.  Trading on tips or on stock movements is usually no better than gambling in Vegas: the odds are steeply stacked against the player.   Frequently , individual traders lose.  But for every loser there is a winner: usually a savvy financial trading firm using techniques the average trader doesn't know about. Common techniques include clever ways to move the odds just a little in the trader's favor.  This means following a consistent conservative strategy and leveraging tools and techniques developed over many years which, for a variety of reasons, aren't in the hands of the individual trader.
       </p>
       <p>
        One of the ways that professional trading firms make consistent profits, regardless of the market's direction, is through options trading.  By structuring and timing options trades with non-directional strategies, professional traders consistently make money in up
        <b>
         <i>
          and
         </i>
        </b>
        down markets.
       </p>
       <p>
        But are these techniques destined to remain the sole secret of the financial trading firms? Fortunately, the answer is no - thanks to Uncle Bob's Money.
       </p>
       <p>
        Uncle Bob's Money teaches individual investors how to take advantage of the same conservative options trading strategies the pros use.  By explaining the concepts in simple straightforward English so no one needs a finance degree to understand them, and by developing highly effective on-line tools that let non-professionals leverage the same techniques that the pros use -- like trading calendars and model portfolios -- Uncle Bob's Money allows you to practice until they feel completely proficient before they jump into making live trades.
       </p>
       <p>
        Best of all, the information is completely free. The step-by-step instructions on the website cover everything you need to trade options successfully including which market conditions to monitor before entering into each strategy, when to start, and the specific rules they follow.  It also teaches which specific indicators are most important for each strategy, the rules for exiting a position early, and how to do it without spending your every waking hour in front of a computer.
       </p>
       <p>
        Uncle Bob's also offers a subscription-based opportunity. By subscribing you'll gain immediate access to Advanced Tools and Private Membership emails.  At this level of engagement, the advanced trading tools do all the hard work for you and make trading a breeze. And Uncle Bob's tremendously-valuable e-letters -- available to subscribers only -- offer the trade alerts, advice, news and information you'll need to make wise, timely decisions. At UncleBobsMoney.com you can find all the tools you need to succeed.
       </p>
       <p>
        You have nothing to lose and wealth to win. Welcome aboard!
       </p>
