         <div class="twelve columns">
          <h1 class="post-title">
           Hottest Hairstyles for Spring 2014
          </h1>
          <div class="post-meta-container">
           <ul class="post-meta">
            <li>
             on February 24, 2014 10:00 am
            </li>
            <li>
             <a href="http://phyrra.net/author/oicheghealai">
              Phyrra
             </a>
            </li>
            <li>
             <ul class="post-meta-categories">
              <li>
               <a class="" href="http://phyrra.net/category/hair-2">
                Hair
               </a>
              </li>
             </ul>
            </li>
            <li>
            </li>
           </ul>
          </div>
          <div class="post-content">
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Hottest Trendy Spring 2014 Hairstyles" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-53163" height="700" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Hot-Trendy-Spring-2014-Hairstyles.png" width="600"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://phyrra.net/2014/02/hottest-hairstyles-spring-2014.html&amp;media=http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Hot-Trendy-Spring-2014-Hairstyles.png&amp;description=Hottest Hairstyles for Spring 2014')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: right;">
            I have nothing to disclose.
            <a href="http://www.phyrra.net/ftc">
             <img alt="" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Affiliate-Links.png"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Variety is the trend for spring 2014 hairstyles. From up-dos to letting it all hang out, youll easily find the perfect trendy style to step out in come spring. Just thing fun, creative and sexy and youve got the trends covered. Theres something for every hair length and color, though red heads will definitely be feeling the love this season. Do some hairstyle spring cleaning and embrace some of the latest trendy 2014 hairstyles.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Messy Updo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-53154" height="600" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Messy-Updo.png" width="600"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://phyrra.net/2014/02/hottest-hairstyles-spring-2014.html&amp;media=http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Messy-Updo.png&amp;description=Hottest Hairstyles for Spring 2014')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <b>
             Messy Up-dos
            </b>
            <br/>
            Forget about putting every hair in its place. Grab some hair pins and sweep your hair up in a messy up-do, leaving some wavy tendrils hanging. You can have some fun with this style by adding in braids, a bun, a french twist or even turning long hair into a temporary bob. Basically, make it look effortless and like youve been riding with the top down.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_53164" style="width: 610px">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Messy Braided Updo" class="size-full wp-image-53164" height="600" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Messy-Braided-Updo.png" width="600"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://phyrra.net/2014/02/hottest-hairstyles-spring-2014.html&amp;media=http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Messy-Braided-Updo.png&amp;description=Hottest Hairstyles for Spring 2014')">
             </span>
            </span>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             I love this messy braided updo!
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <b>
             Braids Are Back
            </b>
            <br/>
            Braids are not only classic, but theyre a staple in spring 2014 hairstyles. Whether you combine them with other trends or make them the focal point, you cant go wrong with braids. However, you should make them look a little messy, instead of super sleek. Think of how a braid looks after a day at the beach and youll get the perfect look. Try mixing it up with different types of braids, from clunky to fishtail and everything in between.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Pretty Braids" class="aligncenter" height="600" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/More-Braids.png" width="600"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://phyrra.net/2014/02/hottest-hairstyles-spring-2014.html&amp;media=http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/More-Braids.png&amp;description=Hottest Hairstyles for Spring 2014')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            As always, I urge you to visit
            <a href="http://www.hairromance.com/" target="_blank">
             Hair Romance
            </a>
            for the best in hair tutorials! Christina has many unique braids and hairstyles to share. Youll never have to wear the same style twice, unless you want to!
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Low Pony Tail" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-53156" height="400" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Low-Pony-Tail.png" width="600"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://phyrra.net/2014/02/hottest-hairstyles-spring-2014.html&amp;media=http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Low-Pony-Tail.png&amp;description=Hottest Hairstyles for Spring 2014')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <b>
             The Low Pony
            </b>
            <br/>
            Super high cheerleader ponytails are out and sexy, low ponies are in. For best results, start the pony no higher than the nape of your neck. These usually look best with straight hair to avoid messy frizzing above the pony. Thick bands are the perfect match and keep the pony in place all day.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Straight Hair" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-53157" height="600" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Straight-Hair.png" width="600"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://phyrra.net/2014/02/hottest-hairstyles-spring-2014.html&amp;media=http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Straight-Hair.png&amp;description=Hottest Hairstyles for Spring 2014')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <b>
             Go Straight
            </b>
            <br/>
            Even though messy is all the rage, super straight hair is just as trendy. For girls who are always trying to get more body in their locks, now is your time to shine. The stick straight style is a must have. Just make sure you add a little shine serum so your locks really stand out.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Beach Babe Hair" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-53158" height="736" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Beach-Babe.png" width="600"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://phyrra.net/2014/02/hottest-hairstyles-spring-2014.html&amp;media=http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Beach-Babe.png&amp;description=Hottest Hairstyles for Spring 2014')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <b>
             Beach Babe
            </b>
            <br/>
            Waves always seem to be in and spring 2014 is no exception. Give you hair that beach babe look with soft waves. Mix this with a partial messy up-do for a stunning look.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Mix It Up Hair" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-53159" height="387" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Mix-It-Up.png" width="600"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://phyrra.net/2014/02/hottest-hairstyles-spring-2014.html&amp;media=http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Mix-It-Up.png&amp;description=Hottest Hairstyles for Spring 2014')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <b>
             Mix It Up
            </b>
            <br/>
            Waves, straight hair, messy hair  its all trendy, but which should you choose? Mixed texture hair is a major trend for spring hair. Go half straight, half wavy or half messy, half straight. Whatever you do, just mix it up a bit.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Severe Side Part" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-53160" height="636" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Severe-Side-Part.png" width="600"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://phyrra.net/2014/02/hottest-hairstyles-spring-2014.html&amp;media=http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Severe-Side-Part.png&amp;description=Hottest Hairstyles for Spring 2014')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <b>
             Severe Side Part
            </b>
            <br/>
            Most of the trendy 2014 hairstyles have one thing in common  a severe side part. Part your hair two inches or more from your normal center part. You can mix this style with pretty much any other or just go natural with a different part.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Layered Hair" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-53161" height="780" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/body.png" width="600"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://phyrra.net/2014/02/hottest-hairstyles-spring-2014.html&amp;media=http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/body.png&amp;description=Hottest Hairstyles for Spring 2014')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            The main overall trend for spring 2014 hairstyles is relaxed and simple. Your hair shouldnt look over done. Have fun and play around with accessories, especially gold ones as these not only stand out, but are huge on the runways right now. Of course, I prefer my
            <a href="http://www.phyrra.net/2014/02/bunny-paige-hair-accessories.html" target="_blank">
             spiked and gemmed
            </a>
            accessories, like the ones I purchased from Bunny Paige. Hair of all lengths is in, so feel free to experiment with all the different trends to find which ones work best for you.
           </p>
