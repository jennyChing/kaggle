        <p>
         <font size="2">
          <b>
           Facts and Commentary about the List:
          </b>
         </font>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <font size="2">
           The newspaper asked 
            50 or more distinguished film critics, including Molly Haskell, Jonathan 
            Rosenbaum, Andrew Sarris and others, to vote in their film poll.
          </font>
          <br/>
         </li>
         <li>
          <font size="2">
           One 
            of the film poll categories asked the participants to rank their top 
            ten best films of the century.
          </font>
          <br/>
         </li>
         <li>
          <font size="2">
           The results were compiled from the poll's voting and published in the January 4, 
          2000 edition of
           <i>
            The Village Voice
           </i>
           .
