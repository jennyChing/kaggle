           Lemaitre Singularity New EP Out July 22 On Astralwerks
          </h2>

          <div class="video-block bio inner">
           <a>
            <img alt="Lemaitre Singularity New EP Out July 22 On Astralwerks" src="http://www.astralwerks.com/wp-content/files_mf/singularitycoverartlowres.jpg"/>
           </a>
           <p align="center" style="text-align: center;">
            <b>
             SINGULARITY NORTH AMERICAN HEADLINE TOUR LAUNCHES JULY 22
            </b>
           </p>
           <p align="center" style="text-align: center;">
            <b>
             ON TOUR WITH PORTER ROBINSON (WORLDS NORTH AMERICAN TOUR) BEGINNING AUG 28
            </b>
           </p>
           <p>
            Astralwerks announces Singularity the brand new EP from Lemaitre out July 22
            <sup>
             nd
            </sup>
            . The four-track EP sees the band (aka Norwegian duo Ulrik Denizou Lund and Ketil Jansen) deliver the guitar-driven, disco-infused electronic indie pop that has earned Lemaitre its global fan following as it incorporates elements of hip hop and soul into its signature sound.
           </p>
           <p>
            <i>
             We grew up listening to a lot of different music. A lot of jazz and fusion. A lot of French music, especially Serge Gainsbourg and electronic music like Daft Punk, Justice, Phoenix and Basement Jaxx,
            </i>
            explains Ulrik who goes on to cite hip-hop, funk, soul, RnB and the film scores of composer Hayao Miyazaki among the pairs shared influences referenced on the upcoming EP.
           </p>
           <p>
            <i>
             Its pretty varied, but still very Lemaitre-ish,
            </i>
            he continues.
            <i>
             We worked with a female singer for the first time (Tennessee-born vocalist Lolo who previously featured on Panic! at the Discos Miss Jackson). We also got a feature from one of our favorite rappers Chuck Inglish from The Cool Kids.
            </i>
           </p>
           <p>
            The four-track EP includes the critically acclaimed single High Tide(Interview Magazine calls the track crisply produced and beautiful while Earmilk calls its chord progressions unrivalled and its production value out of this world) plus three brand new tracks; the disco-infused Wait feat.
            <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lauren_Pritchard_(singer)">
             LOLO
            </a>
            , the danceable soul-guided funk of All I Need feat
            <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chuck_Inglish">
             Chuck Inglish
            </a>
            , and the playful pop of Go which features bouncing synths across gliding guitars.
           </p>
           <p>
            In addition to the EP release, Lemaitre is set to play a series of headline North American club dates beginning July 22
            <sup>
             nd
            </sup>
            in Washington D.C. The bands live show is as ambitious as its music; consisting of three self-designed light installations complete with 3D animations. Following the headline dates, Lemaitre will join Astralwerks label-mate Porter Robinson as support for the massive 40+ date Worlds North American tour beginning August 28
            <sup>
             th
            </sup>
            . A full list of dates can be found below.
           </p>
