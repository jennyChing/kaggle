       <h1 class="entry-title">
        5 Must-Visit Destinations for Extreme Sports Fans
       </h1>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        Our recent
        <a href="http://bit.ly/1dwtIGz">
         Wisconsin snowmobiling episode
        </a>
        has us in the mood for another adrenaline rush. Lucky for us, when it comes to heart-pounding activities, Wisconsin delivers. Here are just a handful of places in Wisconsin to get your fix:
       </p>
       <p>
        1.
        <a href="http://www.diveout.com/" target="_blank">
         AtmosphAir Skydiving Center  Fort Atkinson
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-WKvM]" href="http://bobber.discoverwisconsin.com/media/Skydiving.jpg">
         <img alt="AtmosphAir Skydiving Center  Fort Atkinson" class="aligncenter img-responsive wp-image-821" height="385" src="http://bobber.discoverwisconsin.com/media/Skydiving.jpg" width="576"/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        Looking for the ultimate rush? Its hard to top jumping out of a plane! And thats exactly what you can do at the AtmosphAir Skydiving Center in Fort Atkinson. Owners Bo Babovic and Alex Kolacio have more than 16,000 jumps under their belt, so you can breathe a little easier knowing youre in safe hands during this extreme adventure.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        2.
        <a href="http://www.commandopaintballsports.com/" target="_blank">
         Commando Paintball Sports  Little Suamico
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-WKvM]" href="http://bobber.discoverwisconsin.com/media/paintball.jpg">
         <img alt="Paintballing at Commando Paintball" class="aligncenter img-responsive size-full wp-image-827" height="267" src="http://bobber.discoverwisconsin.com/media/paintball.jpg" width="440"/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        If you prefer having two feet on the ground, consider rounding up your pals for some paintball action. Wisconsins largest paintball field, located just 20 minutes north of Green Bay, boasts three fields and numerous two-story forts. Rest assured: A trained referee is always on hand to safeguard the combat zone and prevent potentially illegal battle ploys.
       </p>
       <p>
        3.
        <a href="http://www.divepointscuba.com/" target="_blank">
         Divepoint Scuba Center  Stevens Point
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-WKvM]" href="http://bobber.discoverwisconsin.com/media/Scuba_Tom-Rutledge.jpg">
         <img alt="Scuba Diving near Sheboygan" class="aligncenter img-responsive wp-image-822" height="395" src="http://bobber.discoverwisconsin.com/media/Scuba_Tom-Rutledge.jpg" width="595"/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        Scuba diving in Wisconsin? Its trueits a real thing. You can experience the underwater world at the Divepoint Scuba Center in Stevens Point, where theres a Professional Association of Diving Instructors (PADI) certified dive center. Classes are offered for beginner and advanced divers, and the trained instructors at Divepoint will help you find the best equipment, too. Once youre certified, explore the many shipwrecks found in our Great Lakes and other inland lakes throughout Wisconsin.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        4.
        <a href="http://www.usabmx.com/tracks/1741" target="_blank">
         Toads Cove BMX  La Crosse
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-WKvM]" href="http://bobber.discoverwisconsin.com/media/toad-bmx.jpg">
         <img alt="Theres a track for every skill level at Toads Cove in La Crosse. With courses available for practice and racing events, you can be a spectator and join in on the fun if you want. They even have a new indoor course so you can feel the thrill all year round. " class=" wp-image-828 img-responsive aligncenter" height="470" src="http://bobber.discoverwisconsin.com/media/toad-bmx.jpg" width="627"/>
        </a>
