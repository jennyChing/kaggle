              <div style="white-space: pre-line; margin-top: 0;">
               The 1995 co-headlining tour by
               <b>
                David Bowie
               </b>
               and
               <b>
                Nine Inch Nails
               </b>
               was one for the ages, with each act playing a full set that was bridged nightly when the
               <b>
                Thin White Duke
               </b>
               and his bandmates joined
               <b>
                Trent Reznor
               </b>
               and Co.  touring in support of their epic
               <b>
                The Downward Spiral
               </b>
               to perform a handful of
               <b>
                Bowie
               </b>
               tunes and a couple NIN songs together, before segueing into Bowie's full set.

For years, fans have clamored for high-quality video from the trek, and as fan site The NIN Hotline notes, some finally emerged two months ago on the Vimeo page of David Williams, a film editor who apparently worked on a pro-shot film of the Bowie/NIN tour (and, among his other credits, edited Anton Corbijn's video for Depeche Mode's "World In My Eyes").

Below you'll see a superb clip of Bowie and Reznor playing NIN's "
               <b>
                Hurt
               </b>
               " (with a killer guitar intro by Reeves Gabrels), part of the transition between the two sets; clips of Nine Inch Nails and Bowie performing the latter's "
               <b>
                Hallo Spaceboy
               </b>
