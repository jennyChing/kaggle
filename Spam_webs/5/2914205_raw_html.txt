          </h2>

          <a href="http://go.tipjunkie.com/hm/1250/spunkyjunky.blogspot.com/2011/03/tutorial-tuesday-nifty-leather-flower.html" target="_blank" title="Leather Flower Tutorial">
           Leather Flower Necklace Tutorial
          </a>
          ~Leather scraps and scrapbooking flowers make up this fabulous necklace. This is a great solution for when you need that perfect shade that you cant seem to find at the store...
          <img alt="Brooch Ribbon Necklace" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-41071" height="334" src="http://tj-prod-media.s3.amazonaws.com/2012/06/Screen-Shot-2012-06-05-at-1.02.00-PM.png" title="Brooch Ribbon Necklace" width="331"/>

          <a href="http://go.tipjunkie.com/hm/532/www.marthastewart.com/how-to/chic-strands-brooch-ribbon-necklace?backto=true" target="_blank" title="Brooch Ribbon Necklace">
           Brooch Ribbon Necklace
          </a>
          ~Take old and broken brooches, or even buy some inexpensive ones, antique them with a bit of spraypaint, link them together and attach to a ribbon. Voila  a fab necklace that is reminiscent of Anthropologie, but is closer to a Target price tag.
