       <h2>
        Birthday Cake Parfait
       </h2>
       <div class="postmeta">
        <em>
         posted by
        </em>
        Beth @ The First Year
        <em>
         on
        </em>
        July 14, 2014
        <div class="ccount">
         <a href="http://thefirstyearblog.com/birthday-cake-parfait/#comments">
          30
          <em>
           comments 
          </em>
         </a>
        </div>
       </div>
      </div>
      <p>
       <img alt="Birthday Cake Parfait | thefirstyearblog.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6730" height="700" src="http://thefirstyearblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Birthday-Cake-Parfait-7.jpg" width="465"/>
       <br/>
       Today is a perfect day for a birthday cake parfait because its my birthday! I am now a quarter century old, woohoo! Actually, turning 25 is a tad bit scary.. I feel old and it feels like I should have my life figured out, or at least own a pet or a house.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Birthday Cake Parfait | thefirstyearblog.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6729" height="700" src="http://thefirstyearblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Birthday-Cake-Parfait-5.jpg" width="465"/>
       <br/>
       When I feel unsettled about my future I like to remind myself of what God says about my future.
      </p>
      <p>
       Proverbs 91:21 (The Message)  We humans keep brainstorming options and plans, but Gods purpose prevails.
      </p>
      <p>
       Psalm 119:105 (The Message)  By Your words I can see where Im going; they throw a beam of light on my dark path.
      </p>
      <p>
       I find great comfort in knowing that the King of Creation already knows my future, I just need to trust Him!
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Birthday Cake Parfait | thefirstyearblog.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6726" height="700" src="http://thefirstyearblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Birthday-Cake-Parfait-1.jpg" width="465"/>
       <br/>
       For my past couple birthdays Ryan and I would make ice cream cake loaded with oreos, coffee ice cream, fudge, nuts and cool whip. Well, there isnt any ice cream cake this year, at least that I know of! But theres a new birthday dessert in town.
      </p>
      <p>
       This birthday cake parfait has vanilla cupcake and chocolate cupcake pieces (frosting and all), vanilla pudding, whip cream and of course, sprinkles. The addition of pudding makes it rich and delicious. If you like mixing your cake with your ice cream (I do!), then you will surely love this. This birthday parfait is also great for those of us who cant decide between vanilla and chocolate cake.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Birthday Cake Parfait | thefirstyearblog.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6728" height="700" src="http://thefirstyearblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Birthday-Cake-Parfait-4.jpg" width="465"/>
       <br/>
       <span style="font-size: smaller;">
        Dont forget, you can save our recipes to your Recipe Box and create meal plans by clicking Save Recipe.
       </span>
       <br/>
      </p>

         <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
          Ingredients
         </p>
         <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
          <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
           Vanilla cupcakes
          </li>
          <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
           Chocolate cupcakes
          </li>
          <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
           1 package of vanilla pudding
          </li>
          <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
           Whip cream
          </li>
          <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
           Sprinkles
          </li>
          <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
           *the amount of each item depends on how many you plan to serve, but I suggest using 1 and 1/2 cupcakes per parfait
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
          Instructions
         </p>
         <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
          <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           Prepare the pudding as directed on the package, allow it to set in the refrigerator.
          </li>
          <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           To assemble the parfaits: cut up or use your finger to break the cupcakes into smaller pieces, frosting and all. Using small parfait glasses or small drinking glasses, place some of the cupcake pieces into the bottom of the glass. Add the pudding, enough to cover the tops of the crumbled cupcake pieces. Add more crumbled cupcake pieces. Top with whip cream and sprinkles. Serve immediately.
          </li>
         </ol>

      <br/>
      If I had to pick just one other dessert from my blog that I would want for my birthday I would pick our
      <a href="http://thefirstyearblog.com/out-of-the-pan-triple-berry-pie/" target="_blank">
       out of the pan triple berry pie
      </a>
      . In that post I talked about how I wanted that for my birthday this year. I suppose this whole week is my birthday week.. so I am sure I can find time to make it!
      <p>
       I am curious to know, do you have a birthday dessert that you have each year? Growing up I would always ask for an ice cream cake from Dairy Queen, and since we have made homemade ice cream cake several times for my birthday, I would say that mine is typically ice cream cake.
      </p>
