      <section class="post_content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
       <div class="pinterest-wrapper">
        <a class="pin-it-button" count-layout="horizontal" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://ifoodreal.com/meatless-monday-spinach-lentil-soup-recipe/&amp;media=http://ifoodreal.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/NEW-Spinach-Lentil-Soup.jpg&amp;description=Spinach Lentil Soup Recipe -- Vegan and gluten free soup with green lentils, spinach and diced tomatoes.        <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Vegan &amp; gluten free soup with lentils, spinach &amp; diced tomatoes. Perfect for Meatless Mondays.
        </em>
       </p>
       <img alt="NEW-Spinach-Lentil-Soup" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10610" src="http://ifoodreal.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/NEW-Spinach-Lentil-Soup.jpg"/>
       <p>
        Happy Meatless Monday everyone! I made this soup last night and updated the photos of this recipe from last year. My kids ate all of it, even picky 3 year old.:) Enjoy!
       </p>
       <p>
        I have a perfect vegan, vegetarian and gluten free soup recipe for you today. Virtually 0 fat, low in sodium, cholesterol and full of complex carbs. Make for dinner tonight and have lunch ready for tomorrow.
       </p>
       <p>
        Meatless Monday is an international campaign that began in 2003 with a goal to encourage people to not eat meat on Mondays. Focusing on your own health in turn improves the health of the planet. Im in! Are you?
       </p>
       <p>
        I already had a personal
        <a href="http://www.ifoodreal.com/healthy-cheesy-green-beans-cauliflower-recipe/" target="_blank" title="Healthy &amp; Cheesy Green Beans &amp; Cauliflower Recipe">
         Meatless Friday
        </a>
        after weeks of irresponsible indulgement of
        <a href="http://www.ifoodreal.com/a-weekend-in-seattle-piroshky/" target="_blank" title="A Weekend in Seattle &amp; Piroshky">
         piroshky
        </a>
        and other unaudiable goodies during my moms visit. Embarrassing? Yes. Guilty? No.:)
        <span id="more-2702">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        I could call this soup Italian as it is my neighbours, Liliana, recipe. She is Italian and possibly one of the best cooks in the world.
       </p>
       <p>
        I have tried this soup before and loved it right away. She was kind enough to share the recipe with me last week while we were watching the hockey game and trying my dessert experiments.
       </p>
       <img alt="NEWCloseup-Spinach-Lentil-Soup" src="http://ifoodreal.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/NEWCloseup-Spinach-Lentil-Soup.jpg"/>
       <p>
        One of them was
        <a href="http://www.ifoodreal.com/easy-skinny-pumpkin-black-bean-cake-recipe/" target="_blank" title="Easy &amp; Skinny Pumpkin &amp; Black Bean Cake Recipe">
         Easy &amp; Skinny Pumpkin &amp; Black Bean Cake
        </a>
        . By the way, the original recipe is hers as well. It was interesting to watch her face trying this cake as it tasted different from the original LOL.
       </p>
       <p>
        The other one was Marthas
        <a href="http://www.marthastewart.com/313319/chocolate-pear-tart" target="_blank">
         Chocolate Pear Tart
        </a>
        . Of course I have changed half of the ingredients. I will post it once Im done experimenting. I decided to stop baking for a bit, you know why.:)
       </p>
       <p>
        Going back to soupIt super easy to make. What else? I always rinse my grains, meat, veggies, fruits including melons and bananas. So, rinse your lentils.
        <img alt="NEWFar-Spinach-Lentil-Soup" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10615" src="http://ifoodreal.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/NEWFar-Spinach-Lentil-Soup.jpg" style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', 'Bitstream Charter', Times, serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        I used organic green lentils and
        <a href="http://www.superiortouch.com/retail/products/better-than-bouillon/all-natural-reduced-sodium/45/chicken-base-reduced-sodium" target="_blank">
         Better than Bouillon low sodium organic chicken base
        </a>
        to make the stock. Lentils are so good for you and are a great source of vegetarian protein. With about 30% of their calories from protein, lentils have the third-highest level ofproteinof any legume or nut, aftersoybeansandhemp.
       </p>
       <p>
        Lentils are also full of fiber, folate, vitamin B1 and a good source of iron. They do not require much soaking like many other beans, which makes them convenient for quick night vegetarian meals.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>

           </p>

          <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
           Ingredients
          </p>
          <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
            2-3 garlic cloves, crushed
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 large onion, finely diced
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 large carrots, diced
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 celery stalks, diced
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups green lentils, rinsed
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
            14 oz can diced tomatoes (with liquid)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
            6 cups vegetable broth, low sodium
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups water
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 bay leaves
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
            1/2 tsp salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
            1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 handfuls of spinach, roughly chopped
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-12" itemprop="ingredients">
            Cooking spray (I use
            <a class="ingredient-link" href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/offer-listing/B00004SPZV/sr=8-2/qid=1392492899/?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;colid=&amp;coliid=&amp;condition=new&amp;creative=390957&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;me=&amp;qid=1392492899&amp;seller=&amp;sr=8-2&amp;tag=if0466-20" target="_blank">
             Misto
            </a>
            )
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
           Directions
          </p>
          <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
           <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat large pot on medium heat. Spray with cooking spray and add garlic and onions. Saute until translucent. Add carrots and celery and saute for 5 more minutes. Add lentils and saute for another 3-5 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, water, bay leaves, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, cover and cook on low for 30 minutes. Add spinach, remove from heat and let stand covered a few minutes. Serve warm garnished with Parmesan cheese if desired.
           </li>
           <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2">
            Storage Instructions: Refrigerate covered for up to 3 days or freeze for up to 3 months.
           </div>
          </ol>
