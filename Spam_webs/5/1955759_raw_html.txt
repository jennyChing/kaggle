     </p>

      We're a hub for several of London's most exciting annual theatre festivals:  a founding venue of the
      <a href="http://www.camdenfringe.com">
       Camden Fringe
      </a>
      , home of the
      <a href="http://www.londonhorrorfestival.com">
       London Horror Festival
      </a>
      , creator of the Black Box Festival, and hosts of an Edinburgh  Fringe preview season that frequently attracts some of the biggest names  in stand-up and sketch comedy.
     </p>

      Our programme spans across everything from award winning theatre to  thrilling new writing; cabaret, magic, comedy and musicals - no matter what kinds of shows excite you, you'll find them at the Etcetera.
