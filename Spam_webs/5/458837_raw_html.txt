     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        Tensai Bit-Kun: Gramon Battle
       </b>
      </i>
      is a
      <a href="/wiki/Tactical_role-playing_game" title="Tactical role-playing game">
       tactical role-playing
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Video_game" title="Video game">
       video game
      </a>
      released in 2003 by
      <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Garakuta-Studio&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Garakuta-Studio (page does not exist)">
       Garakuta-Studio
      </a>
      . The game was released only in
      <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">
       Japan
      </a>
      .
     </p>
