          <p>
           <a href="http://swns.com/tag/beyonce">
            Beyonce
           </a>
           s husband Jay-Z is a lucky man  as well as being a multimillionaire and married to one of the worlds most beautiful women he is now the proud owner of a 1.3 million Bugatti Veyron Grand Sport, the worlds fastest convertible.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Beyonce buys Jay-Z 1.3 million Bugatti Veyron Grand Sport " src="http://swns.com/wp-content/themes/wp-clear/scripts/timthumb.php?src=http://swns.com/images/stories/veyrongrandsport/veyrongrandsport1.jpg&amp;w=460&amp;h=345&amp;zc=1"/>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center">
           <em>
            The Bugatti Veyron Grand Sport
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           The RnB star had to wait a year after ordering the car as a present for her hip-hop mogul hubbys 41st birthday. It can reach speeds of up to 253 miles an hour and goes from zero to 60 miles in just 2.5 seconds.
          </p>
          <p>
           Jay-Z already has an impressive car collection, including a Maybach 62S, a Ferrari F430 Spider and a 1959 Rolls Royce Convertible which he purchased for $1 million.
          </p>
          <p>
           A source told
           <em>
            The Sun
           </em>
           : Jay-Z has one of the most enviable collections of bling motors in the world  but even for him the Bugatti is special.
          </p>
          <p>
           Obviously the money isnt a factor for Beyonce, but Jay will be touched that out of everything she could have bought him, she chose something hell treasure for years. Hes in for the ride of his life.
          </p>
