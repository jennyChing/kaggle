         <div class="item-description text-content">
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Celebrate that great British ritual of tea drinking with this Alice in Wonderland Tea Selection. The set contains 3 of Whittards most popular loose leaf blended black teas presented in keepsake tins printed with John Tenniels illustrations for the Mad Hatters Tea Party scene in Alice in Wonderland.
          </p>
          <div>
           Whittards English Breakfast is an exceptional breakfast blend loved by its customers. It combines fine black teas from across the globe to create a familiar, comforting taste that is just right to start off the day.
          </div>
          <div>
          </div>
          <div>
           Afternoon Tea is just what it says  the perfect tea to enjoy in the afternoons. It is one of Whittards original blends, created by Walter Whittard in the 1940s to enjoy on long, lingering afternoons when tea taken on the lawn was the most British of rituals.
          </div>
          <div>
          </div>
          <div>
           Earl Grey is a classic English tea. It is an enticingly light blend of fine black teas with just the right amount of bergamot to give it the exotic flavour Whittard customers love.
          </div>
          <div>
          </div>
          <div>
           Quintessentially English, the teas make the perfect gift for any discerning tea drinker. The blends have been created by Whittard specially for its customers to enjoy at those traditional tea drinking times of day, when nothing but the best will do.
          </div>
