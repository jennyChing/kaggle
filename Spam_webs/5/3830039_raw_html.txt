         <h1 class="wa-postheader">
          Ways to reduce energy consumption and costs!
         </h1>
         <div class="wa-postheadericons wa-metadata-icons">
          <span class="wa-postdateicon">
           <span class="date">
            Published
           </span>
           <span class="entry-date" title="11:52 am">
            08/29/2014
           </span>
          </span>
          |
          <span class="wa-postauthoricon">
           <span class="author">
            By
           </span>
           <span class="author vcard">
            <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.technolamp.com/author/techno1/" title="View all posts by techno1">
             techno1
            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="wa-postcontent wa-postcontent-0 clearfix">
          <h5 style="text-align: center;">
           Simple tips on how you can save energy and money at the same time. Follow these minor adjustments at home, office, or on vacation and you will greatly contribute to our environment as well as to your electric bill.
          </h5>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.technolamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Dollarphotoclub_58372218.png">
            <img alt="Dollarphotoclub_58372218" class="alignnone wp-image-1280" height="92" src="http://www.technolamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Dollarphotoclub_58372218-264x300.png" width="81"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Change to
            <a href="http://www.technolamp.com/shop/A60-LED-Bulb-110-220V-3000K-Non-Dimm-E27" target="_blank">
             LED bulbs
            </a>
            , these use up 85% less energy for the same light output and last 25 times longer than incandescent lamps.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Try using dimming technology, adjust the brightness and light output when you dont need that much light. We recommend our
            <a href="http://www.technolamp.com/shop/Wifi-Set-2-Bulbs-1-Controller-E27-86-265V-3000-6500K" target="_blank">
             Led wifi controller set
            </a>
            , night light mode consumes only 0.4W.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.technolamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Screen-Shot-2014-08-29-at-4.58.46-PM.png">
            <img alt="Screen Shot 2014-08-29 at 4.58.46 PM" class="alignnone wp-image-1288" height="124" src="http://www.technolamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Screen-Shot-2014-08-29-at-4.58.46-PM-300x151.png" width="246"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <ul style="text-align: center;">
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            Turn off the lights when you are not using a room.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <ul style="text-align: center;">
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            For reading, try using low power task light and avoid using the general lighting. We can recommend our
            <a href="http://www.technolamp.com/shop/Table-Lamp-4W-100-240V-50-60Hz-3000K" target="_blank">
             LED desk lamp
            </a>
            , with only 4W you can focus light where you need it.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.technolamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Screen-Shot-2014-08-29-at-4.50.30-PM.png">
            <img alt="Screen Shot 2014-08-29 at 4.50.30 PM" class="alignnone wp-image-1289" height="117" src="http://www.technolamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Screen-Shot-2014-08-29-at-4.50.30-PM-300x144.png" width="244"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <ul style="text-align: center;">
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            Use natural light during the day, take advantage and leave shades and blinds open during daylight.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <ul style="text-align: center;">
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            Use sensor bulbs where you dont go that often. For example: in your garage, hallway or home entrance. We recommend our
            <a href="http://www.technolamp.com/shop/special-line/LED-IR-Motion-Sensor-Bulb-3000K-560LM-E27" target="_blank">
             LED IR sensor bulb
            </a>
            . This bulb will automatically turn on and off when detecting the human body and when the illumination is less than 10 lux (not during daytime).
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.technolamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Screen-Shot-2014-08-29-at-4.53.47-PM.png">
            <img alt="Screen Shot 2014-08-29 at 4.53.47 PM" class="alignnone wp-image-1291" height="192" src="http://www.technolamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Screen-Shot-2014-08-29-at-4.53.47-PM-300x293.png" width="196"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <ul style="text-align: center;">
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            Unplug appliances when they are not needed; chargers, TV, computers, table lamps, radios, etc. Or use power strips to switch off when you are not using them.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <ul style="text-align: center;">
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            Set computers to sleep or hibernate during inactivity. Turn them off when not in use.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <ul style="text-align: center;">
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            Wash full loads in dish and clothes washers. Best would be to use cold or warm setting for clothes washers and using the eco or energy saving setting for your dishwasher.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <ul style="text-align: center;">
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            Dont preheat the oven more than necessary.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <ul style="text-align: center;">
           <li style="text-align: left;">
            Use less heating and cooling energy when nobody is home or office.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.technolamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Vector-Meter-Icons-copy.jpg">
            <img alt="Vector Meter Icons" class="alignnone wp-image-1285" height="141" src="http://www.technolamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Vector-Meter-Icons-copy-300x300.jpg" width="141"/>
           </a>
          </p>
