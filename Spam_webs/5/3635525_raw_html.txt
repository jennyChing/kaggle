             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://travelmasticom.blogspot.com/2010/09/munnar-holiday-packages-unleashing.html">
               Munnar Holiday Packages - Unleashing Natural Splendour In and Around Munnar
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header-line-1">
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TId6KHJ-TXI/AAAAAAAAAYc/1kGU1R0PO04/s1600/Gopika-in-Munnar-001.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="266" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TId6KHJ-TXI/AAAAAAAAAYc/1kGU1R0PO04/s400/Gopika-in-Munnar-001.jpg" width="400"/>

              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               The land of winding lanes, lush greenery and sprawling tea plantations, Munnar is an oasis in the state of Kerala.  Located on the confluence of Muthirappuzha, Nallathanni and Kundaly rivers, Munnar is a premium hill station in Kerala.  Draped in verdant foliage, Munnar is beautifully placed in the Idduki district of Kerala in the south Western Ghats.  After the heat of lowlands, the pristine views, craggy peaks, invigorating mountain air of Munnar strikes as a refreshing hideaway for the visitors.
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TId58YfcM-I/AAAAAAAAAYU/2_SQbfpDBWY/s1600/munnar.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="300" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TId58YfcM-I/AAAAAAAAAYU/2_SQbfpDBWY/s400/munnar.jpg" width="400"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               One look at the town reminds of those picture book towns described in fictional books.  On
               <b>
                Munnar holiday packages
               </b>
               one can expect to witness angelic beauty extracted out of ones fantasy and put to life.  An amalgamation of natures bounty and captivating wildlife, Munnar tour packages offer a world of serenity and serendipity to each of its visitors.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               The main attractions of
               <a href="http://www.travelmasti.com/Munnar-Holiday-Packages.html">
                <b>
                 Munnar Holiday Packages
                </b>
               </a>
               include:
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TId6iuPir6I/AAAAAAAAAYk/ZK_ZMU7Nw4c/s1600/Mattupetty.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="300" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TId6iuPir6I/AAAAAAAAAYk/ZK_ZMU7Nw4c/s400/Mattupetty.jpg" width="400"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="color: magenta; text-align: justify;">
               <b>
                Mattupetty
               </b>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Perched on an altitude of 1700 m above sea level, Mattupetty Lake and Dam is a pristine picnic spot.  The scenic views surrounding are elegantly wrapped with stunning views of manicured tea plantations and the serene lake. Here boating at the crystal clear waters you can enjoy the warmth and tranquillity that the region exudes.  The region is also famed for its highly specialized dairy farm- the Indo-Swiss Live Stock project which is worth a visit.  Also one can indulge in activities like trekking and bird watching at Shola forests.
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TId8GvbgGUI/AAAAAAAAAYs/hdVryamOpv4/s1600/Munnar-1137_1.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="300" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TId8GvbgGUI/AAAAAAAAAYs/hdVryamOpv4/s400/Munnar-1137_1.JPG" width="400"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               The region is beautifully adorned with rivulets crisscrossing across the landscape filled with gripping greenery creating ideal habitat for variety of species of birds.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <b>
                <span style="color: magenta;">
                 Atukkad Waterfalls
                </span>
                <br/>
               </b>
               The scenic waterfalls situated amidst hills and jungles of Munnar create mesmerising scenery.  The stunning waterfalls are ideal for a picnic amidst caresses of Mother Nature.  Enhance your photography skills at the gorgeous falls to capture some rejuvenating memories of your most eventful
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TIeABevQ3NI/AAAAAAAAAZE/f6fQQaraOgU/s1600/Pothamedu+View+Point.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="300" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TIeABevQ3NI/AAAAAAAAAZE/f6fQQaraOgU/s400/Pothamedu+View+Point.JPG" width="400"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               and exciting
               <b>
                Munnar holiday packages
               </b>
               .
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div style="color: magenta; text-align: justify;">
               <b>
                Pothamedu View Point
               </b>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               Situated short drives away from Munnar, Pothamedus rolling hills are draped in emerald hues of verdant tea plantations.  The hills exude pleasant aroma of tea, coffee and cardamom whose plantations here together create a soothing sight.  While athletic travellers push on trekking tours others prefer simple nature walks to set their eyes on tranquilizing views of Pothamedu.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div style="color: magenta; text-align: justify;">
               <b>
                Eravikulam National Park
               </b>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TIeCegzBE6I/AAAAAAAAAZU/Sl8xIDy1ui4/s1600/Tourist-Attraction-Sites-Munnar-Eravikulam-National-Park-22.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="300" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TIeCegzBE6I/AAAAAAAAAZU/Sl8xIDy1ui4/s400/Tourist-Attraction-Sites-Munnar-Eravikulam-National-Park-22.jpg" width="400"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               The national park nestled along the Western Ghats in Idukki district of Kerala, Eravikulam National Park is situated a short distance from Munnar.  The park consist of high altitude grasslands and numerous beauteous perennial streaks criss-crossing the entire terrain.  The park has a recorded population of Nilgiri Tahr, Indian Muntjac, Gaur, Sambar Deer, Golden Jackal, Tigers, Leopards and more.
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <br/>
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <a href="http://www.travelmasti.com/Munnar-Holiday-Packages.html">
                <b>
                 Munnar Holiday Packages
                </b>
               </a>
               offer great escapes to the lap of nature.  Cascades and lush foliage and breathtaking
              </div>
              <div style="text-align: justify;">
               <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TIeAK-Kx2eI/AAAAAAAAAZM/xAWAki0Z-1E/s1600/Pothamedu+View+Point1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="300" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_xzYPy_7eP2M/TIeAK-Kx2eI/AAAAAAAAAZM/xAWAki0Z-1E/s400/Pothamedu+View+Point1.jpg" width="400"/>
                </a>
               </div>
               vistas awaits you on visit to the region.  The beauty of the land captivates while the adventure options thrills and the fragrance of aromatic valleys intoxicates here in Munnar.  A journey to this pretty hamlet is sure to sedate all your senses leaving you behind with a rejuvenating experience which stays for long.
              </div>
