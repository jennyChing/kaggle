           <div itemprop="item" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/CreativeWork">
            <a class="step-image-link" data-gallery="#blueimp-gallery" href="http://images.coplusk.net/projects/3400/steps/8842/full_8842.jpg" itemprop="image">

            <div class="body small">
             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 1
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">
              <p>
               Ice cubes
               <br/>
               1 old pot
               <br/>
               Empty juice or milk cartons
               <br/>
               Camping-gas or other hitting device
               <br/>
               1 lighter
               <br/>
               Some pegs
               <br/>
               Some bamboo sticks
               <br/>
               Scented candles (same height with the cartons or higher)
               <br/>
               Paraffin flakes (or old used candles)
               <br/>
               and
               <br/>
               Scissors or/and Knife
               <br/>
               Newspapers
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </li>
          <li class="instruction step with-image" itemprop="itemListElement" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ListItem">

             <img alt="How to make a candle. Ice Cube Candles - Step 2" class="step-image" height="153" src="http://images.coplusk.net/projects/3400/steps/8843/normal_8843.jpg" width="400"/>
            </a>
            <div class="body">
             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 2
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">
              <p>
               We need the wick of the candles, so we try to chop gently the scented candles with hands so that the wicks are separated out of the candles. If this is not possible, you must buy some wick.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </li>
          <li class="instruction step with-image" itemprop="itemListElement" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ListItem">
           <div itemprop="item" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/CreativeWork">
            <a class="step-image-link" data-gallery="#blueimp-gallery" href="http://images.coplusk.net/projects/3400/steps/8844/full_8844.jpg" itemprop="image">
             <img alt="How to make a candle. Ice Cube Candles - Step 3" class="step-image" height="278" src="http://images.coplusk.net/projects/3400/steps/8844/normal_8844.jpg" width="400"/>
            </a>
            <div class="body">
             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 3
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">
              <p>
               Cut the carton so as its top part is 5-10cm shorter than the wick.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </li>
          <li class="advert-well">

              Step 4
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">

            </a>

             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 5

            <div class="body">
             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 6
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">

            <div class="body">
             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 7
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">
              <p>
               Add the paraffin flakes (or pieces of some cropped used candles)
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </li>

            <div class="body">
             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 8
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">
              <p>
               Stir with a bamboo stick until all the pieces of the paraffin and the candles melt.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </li>
          <li class="instruction step with-image" itemprop="itemListElement" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ListItem">

            <div class="body">
             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 9
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">
              <p>
               Pure some of the liquid (the melted paraffin and candles) into the carton just fill the carton about 2 cm.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </li>
          <li class="instruction step with-image" itemprop="itemListElement" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ListItem">

             <img alt="How to make a candle. Ice Cube Candles - Step 10" class="step-image" height="150" src="http://images.coplusk.net/projects/3400/steps/8851/normal_8851.jpg" width="400"/>

            <div class="body">
             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 10
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">
              <p>
               Leave the paraffin to cold and become solid.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </li>
          <li class="instruction step with-image" itemprop="itemListElement" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ListItem">

            </a>
            <div class="body">
             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 11
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">
              <p>
               Pour the liquid into the carton. If the paraffin started to cold, just hit it up for a second before pouring.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </li>
          <li class="instruction step with-image" itemprop="itemListElement" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ListItem">

            <div class="body">
             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 12
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">
              <p>
               Put the ice cubes into the carton; try to have the wick always at the center of the carton.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </li>
          <li class="instruction step with-image" itemprop="itemListElement" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ListItem">

            <div class="body">
             <span class="number" itemprop="position">
              Step 13
             </span>
             <div itemprop="text">
              <p>
               After some hours, when the paraffin is solid, you can tear the carton. Just take under consideration that there is some water (from the ice cubes) inside the carton.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
           </div>
          </li>
          <li class="instruction step with-image" itemprop="itemListElement" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ListItem">

               Cut the wick 2-3 cm long.
              </p>
