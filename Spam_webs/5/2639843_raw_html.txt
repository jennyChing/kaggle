         <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
          The Mark Carson Brand Is on Its Way to producing a New Hawaiian Collection!
         </h1>
         <p class="post-meta">
          <span>
           Posted by:
           <a href="http://www.300magazine.com/author/300magazine/" title="">
            300Magazine
           </a>
          </span>
          <span>
           in
           <a href="http://www.300magazine.com/watch-brands/" rel="category tag">
            Watch Brand
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="post-date">
           July 22, 2014
          </span>
          <span class="post-comments">
           <a href="http://www.300magazine.com/watch-brands/mark-carson-brand-way-producing-new-hawaiian-collection/#respond" title="Comment on The Mark Carson Brand Is on Its Way to producing a New Hawaiian Collection!">
            0
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="post-views">
           8341 Views
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <div class="entry">
          <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            The Hawaii-based company Individual Design also known under the trade name Mark Carson has announced the creation of a revolutionary individual collection of Ka La GMT watches, which is indeed a festivity for true fans of the individual, authentic Hawaiian design of Mark Carson luxury watches. The appearance of Ka La watches is filled with the Hawaiian hotness and exotics, so everyone wishing to have a piece of Hawaii with him or her should try the Ka La
            <a href="http://300watches.com/" target="_blank" title="high end watches">
             <strong>
              high-end watches
             </strong>
            </a>
            by Mark Carson for sure.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/pin/390194755185871175/" target="_blank" title="high end watches">
            <img alt="Mark Carson watches" class="alignnone" height="407" src="http://media-cache-ec0.pinimg.com/736x/2c/d1/b5/2cd1b526702b6ea74815d9939ef76a07.jpg" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            The specific feature of this new Ka La GMT collection by Mark Carson is in its very limited numbers; the first set of only five watches was created with consecutive serial numbers, which makes these watches indeed unique in the realm of other luxury watches produced in great numbers worldwide every year. The Ka La GMT watch with Reference 10274 is equipped with a black sunray dial and a patented SuperLuminova covering for the dial and hands of this attractive, original watch. The stainless steel case of this model is covered with black PVD, which adds both elegance and restraint to the watch. Other technical complications include the textured black silicon rubber strap with blue stitching, and a traditional-looking black deployment buckle typical for Mark Carson watch
           </span>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Though Mark Carson luxury watches are less known than other luxury watch brands, they still have much to offer to even the most demanding experts in luxury, high-quality, and functional watches. This new Ka La GMT watch, for instance, has a dual time zone display that may be controlled by the watchs wearer with a 24-hour GMT hand. The watch is distinguished with a unique, original, high-visibility design, and can boast the original Hawaiian assembly by the WOSTEP-trained watchmaker, thus guaranteeing the quality of the watch not losing positions in comparison with other luxury watches producers. The watch is equipped with a unique serial number, which ensures that you will have a unique luxurious accessory, and automatic winding that powers the whole mechanism. This distinguished model also has a date display and a Swiss-made ETA 2893-2 Dual Time/GMT movement. These features coupled with the stop-seconds time setting function create the original appearance and top-notch performance of the Ka La GMT watch by Mark Carson.
           </span>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            This model of luxury watches is an interesting novelty of this brand with a 43 mm stainless steel case possessing 12.5 mm of thickness. It has a domed top sapphire crystal with a fashionable and technologically savvy anti-reflective coating. Those living in Hawaii or attending the place often with a passion for diving will obviously appreciate this watchs water resistance up to 50 meters, which makes the opportunities for enjoying the favorite water sports virtually endless without the need to part with your favorite accessory!
           </span>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
