        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         headshots of an aspiring actor
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-10-25T18:18:58+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 25, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.sharpfocusphoto.com/author/admin-91/" itemprop="url" rel="author">

            admin-91

        </p>

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="clear-review">
         <a href="http://www.sharpfocusphoto.com/headshots-aspiring-actor/thomas-actor-0073/" rel="attachment wp-att-10029">
          <img alt="actor's headshot of a man with a paper" class="alignnone wp-image-10029 size-thumbnail" height="272" src="http://www.sharpfocusphoto.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/10/thomas-actor-0073-200x272.jpg" width="200"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.sharpfocusphoto.com/headshots-aspiring-actor/thomas-actor-0112/" rel="attachment wp-att-10030">
          <img alt="actor's headshot of a man leaning forward" class="alignnone wp-image-10030 size-thumbnail" height="272" src="http://www.sharpfocusphoto.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/10/thomas-actor-0112-200x272.jpg" width="200"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.sharpfocusphoto.com/headshots-aspiring-actor/thomas-actor-0226/" rel="attachment wp-att-10031">
          <img alt="actor's headshot of a man with a checkered shirt holding a paper" class="alignnone wp-image-10031 size-thumbnail" height="272" src="http://www.sharpfocusphoto.com/wp-content/uploads//2014/10/thomas-actor-0226-200x272.jpg" width="200"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p style="color: #232323;">
         thomas is a versatile artist who told me that he needed some actors headshots to take to casting calls.he had an interestinglook that wasnt quite dramatic but hecould only be featured in tv roles or movies. I met him when he was working on his website but I didnt know he was interested in acting. I think that as an artist you are onlylimited to what you think you can do. the power is within, you can express it through playing music, playing a characteror expressing it through choreography or even designing.
        </p>
        <p>
         we set up a date for the shoot and he
         <a href="http://www.sharpfocusphoto.com/wear-headshots-photography/">
          brought afull wardrobeassortment
         </a>
         for different looks. though we had worked together before, it was only remotely, so I was meeting him in real life for the first time when he walkedinto the studio. I had always thought that he was a dead serious kind of guy you couldnt get a laugh out of.hessmaller than I thoughtbut actors come in all sizes. after introductions, a little conversation to ease him up, it was light, camera, action. as I was taking more pictures, he was feelingrelaxed enough to strike different posesfrom dramatic to formal, to even comical. that allowed us to cover enough looks for differentcharacters. though people usually think of acting with regard to the big screen, theres a growing demand of talent for tv and advertising. I thought he was well qualified to play in both. we took sover 100 pictures out of which he selected 12. I touched them up in photoshop and delivered to his satisfaction.
        </p>
