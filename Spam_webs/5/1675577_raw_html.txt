       <h1 class="headline">
        6 Things To Do When Your Motivation Tank Hits Empty
       </h1>
      </div>
     </header>
     <div class="content-item-text content-item inner links-main" itemprop="text">
      <div class="block-full-content clear">
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         Motivation. Sometimes you have it, and sometimes you dont. Inspiration isnt known for being predictable.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         One moment youre sitting on top of the world, the next youre struggling to simply exist.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         Regardless of how positive you may be, everyone needs to refill their motivation tank sooner or later.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         Luckily, filling up your inspiration tank is less difficult than it seems.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         Filling up your tank has never been so easy, and many times filling up is free.
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.aboundlessworld.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          Refilling Your Motivation Tank
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         <strong>
          Breathe:
         </strong>
         When was the last time you consciously chose to breathe? Purposeful breathing is by far the best thing you can ever do for yourself. Take some time and just breathe. Really, thats it.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         Breathe in out.. in.. out.. Try taking 30 breathes and as you do so , feel your awareness forming in the present moment. Feels good doesnt it?
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         <strong>
          Find Your Fuel:
         </strong>
         With the internet its easier than ever to find inspiration. Search for some
         <a href="http://www.inspirational-quotes.info/">
          motivational quotes
         </a>
         . Watch some
         <a href="http://www.aboundlessworld.com/10-inspirational-youtube-videos/">
          inspiring videos
         </a>
         . Read a powerful book. Surround yourself with uplifting stimuli and watch your motivational tank fill up.
         <br/>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         <strong>
          Connect:
         </strong>
         Humans are indeed social creatures, and many times thats all we need. Surround yourself with people you love and genuinely care about.Its remarkable how quickly your well-being changes when you surround yourself with tons of positive energy.
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.aboundlessworld.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         <strong>
          Disconnect:
         </strong>
         The fact that we live in a world of constant stimuli can be tremendously overwhelming.Every now and then make a deliberate effort to
         <a href="http://ivancampuzano.com/permission-to-disconnect/">
          disconnect
         </a>
         . Go on a walk outside. Find a quiet place and just be. Dont talk just listen. The sounds of the universe the cure.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         <strong>
          Be Lazy:
         </strong>
         Who says you have to be productive everyday? Take sometime and enjoy not doing
         <em>
          anything.
         </em>
         Watch some T.V take a nap or two. Sit on your butt and do nothing. Recharge, you deserve it.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         <strong>
          Laugh:
         </strong>
         If youre not laughing youre not living. Watch a funny movie. Surround yourself with people that make you laugh. Better yet go to a comedy club. Theres an abundance of
         <a href="http://www.aboundlessworld.com/finding-humor-in-your-mistakes/">
          humor
         </a>
         in everyday life as long as youre open to receive.
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.aboundlessworld.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          The Road Is Waiting
         </span>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         Feeling unmotivated? Worthless?
         <br/>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         Dont let lack of motivation hinder your ability to live fully. Your purpose has yet to manifest.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: medium;">
         Fill up your tank, get back on the road. Bliss boulevard is straight ahead.
        </span>
       </p>
      </div>
