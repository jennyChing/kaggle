           <a href="http://changingminds.org/techniques/language/modifying_meaning/generalization.htm" rel="nofollow">
            generalization

           <a href="http://changingminds.org/techniques/language/modifying_meaning/emphasis.htm" rel="nofollow">
            emphasis

            Taking the actions of one terrorist as representing the beliefs of everyone they claim to represent.
