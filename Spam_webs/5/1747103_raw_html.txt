         Soccer fandom isnt just for regular folks. There are a whole host of celebritiesincluding some of the biggest names in Hollywoodwho follow the beautiful game and support a team. Were not just talking about the celebs who send a Go USA! tweet once every four years here. The names on this list are people whove gone out of their way to connect with the team they follow, and weve got the photos to prove it.
        </p>
