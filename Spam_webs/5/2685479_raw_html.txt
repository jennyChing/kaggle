        <h2>
         Highlights from #PTC15
        </h2>
        <p>
         February 6, 2015 | Written by Jessica Smith
         <br/>
         <a href="/">
          HOME
         </a>
         
         <a href="/BLOG/">
          BLOG
         </a>
          Highlights from #PTC15
        </p>
       </div>
       <div itemprop="articleBody">
        <div class="u-bigText">
         <p>
          <img alt="PTC" src="/files/images/blog/PTC-2015.png" style="width: 975px; height: 650px;"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          The flip-flops are back in the closet, the tans have (mostly) faded, and weve traded sunny beaches for snowy streets, but were happy to say that this years
          <a href="https://www.ptc.org/ptc15.html">
           Pacific Telecommunications Council conference
          </a>
          (#PTC15) was a resounding success!
         </p>
         <p>
          As we have in previous years, several members of the Telx team made the trip out to Hawaii for this great conference. As always, catching up with our international friends in sunny Hawaii was a highlight and a great way to start off our year!
         </p>
         <p>
          With Anthony &amp; Chris
          <a href="http://www.telx.com/blog/telxs-anthony-rossabi-elected-to-pacific-telecommunications-council-ptc-boa">
           being part of the Board of Governors and Advisory Council
          </a>
          , we were actively involved with the #PTC15 programand were also proud to be a part of the future of PTC as an organization. It was also great to learn about
          <a href="http://www.ptc.org/community/initiatives.html">
           all of the work PTC does to support our growing community
          </a>
          , from the PTC Young Scholar program to providing research and support to projects benefiting telecommunications in the Asia/Pacific region.
         </p>
         <p>
          A big highlight for the football fans among us was the NFC Championship game between the Seahawks and Packers. Over 170 people turned up to join us for networking and football, which was unsurprisingly a great time! Even though the Seahawks couldnt pull out a win in the Super Bowl, it was great cheering them on with many other fans.
         </p>
         <p>
          Finally, we couldnt leave out our meetings with a range of customers and prospects in our suite overlooking Waikiki beach. We covered our
          <a href="http://www.telx.com/cloud-services/cloud-xchange">
           Global Cloud Xchange
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.telxmarketplacelive.com/">
           Telx MarketplaceLIVE
          </a>
          , the
          <a href="http://map.telx.com/NYC-Trifecta.html">
           NYC Trifecta
          </a>
          , and more, ensuring that everyone we talked to was up to date on the latest happenings throughout our company. Who wouldnt love networking overlooking a beautiful beach?
         </p>
         <p>
          Needless to say, we had a great time at #PTC15and we know many others did, too! Dont forget to save the date and meet with us again next year for #PTC16 from January 17-20, 2016. We hope to see you there!
         </p>
