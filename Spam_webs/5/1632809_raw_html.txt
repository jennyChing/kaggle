        Forming Recap: Episodes 1-20
       </a>
      </div>
      <div class="rightclearingfooter">
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="post-body">
      <p>
       Central golden boy Mithras, lands in Atlantis around 10,000 B.C. Within a hundred years he has transformed the Atlantean population into a slave force to mine Earths precious minerals.  He has children with human labor captain Gaia because the freaky star-shaped birthmark on her face is a sign of the elements.  Their offspring are gifted with awesome abilities but the children, in general, creep Mithras out.
      </p>
      <p>
       One such child, Arges, stumbles across the head of a dead dog in the forest.  The head is possessed by a channeling entity. He warns, You can no longer trust your father.
       <br/>
       Thats what my sister said. Arges replies.
      </p>
      <p>
       In Canaan another alien, Serapis the Androgyne, arrives with a group
       <br/>
       of creatures cloned from his DNA, The Nephalim Guard.  They encounter
       <br/>
       Adam &amp; Eve and begin a period of observation and stealth known as
       <br/>
       OPERATION: HEAVENLY SWORD.
      </p>
      <p>
       These Adam and Eve communicate with telepathic pictures as the
       <br/>
       Atlanteans did before Mithras taught them speech but on the seventh
       <br/>
       day of OPERATION HEAVENLY SWORD when Serapis the Androgyne tries to reveal himself to them, their communication hacks his transgender super mind causing epic barfs.  Serapis and the guard retreat into
       <br/>
       invisibility (which may or may not work) but by the 60th day he is
       <br/>
       with Adam and Eve routinely.
      </p>
      <p>
       The dog head reveals  to Arges a battle at the dawn of time where The Adversary aka Lucifer separated himself from the universal consciousness (Ain Soph the Absolute) and was pursued by Michael the archangel for
       <br/>
       this crime.  The ensuing energies from their billion-year-battle gave
       <br/>
       form to the tangible universe, Lucifers plan all along.  Infuriated,
       <br/>
       Michael unseals his Unblockable Strike and defeats Lucifer, popping
       <br/>
       the emerald crown jewel from Lucifers forehead.  Both Lucifer and the
       <br/>
       emerald fall to primeval earth. Lucifer gets trapped between the two
       <br/>
       magnetic poles.  The crown jewel, Lapisexilis, is lost.
       <br/>
       Victorious, Michael observes, Even my most powerful attack could not
       <br/>
       eliminate his illusion of self. As it turns out, the voice from the dog head is the voice of Lucifer.
      </p>
      <p>
       On the 60th day of OPERATION: HEAVENLY SWORD Serapis the Androgyne orders his men to destroy their communication unit and cease
       <br/>
       communication with the home world and Emperor Angra Maniyu.
      </p>
      <p>
       Mithrass child, Rhea, tall naked and first to hit her growth spurt
       <br/>
       throws a tantrum and reveals her feelings for her brother, Cronus.
      </p>
      <p>
       Gaia meets with her other lover, secret father of Themis and Lapetus,
       <br/>
       Noah.  She begs him to take her away but he reminds her they must
       <br/>
       yield to the proper sequence of events.  He communes with Ain Soph and
       <br/>
       receives visions of (among many things) bees and his inevitable ark.
      </p>
      <p>
       Themis reveals to her mother that Cronus has been in communication
       <br/>
       with Gob (king of the Gnomes) and Gaia remembers Gobs instruction to bear Mithrass children so they would grow up to drive Mithras out.
       <br/>
       You bear the mark. Gob warns her. Your genetic makeup is the
       <br/>
       gateway to superhuman transfiguration.
      </p>
      <p>
       Mithras communicates with his friend Nommo back on Planet Dogon and they discuss their dreams.  Nommo leaves work and vows to spend more
       <br/>
       time on his sculptures.  That night he dreams he travels to earth,
       <br/>
       fights Mithras and falls to the center of the earth where he
       <br/>
       encounters a giant gold serpent.
       <br/>
       Eat the scroll, the serpent orders and when Nommo awakens in his
       <br/>
       room on Planet Dogon, he holds the scroll in his hand.
      </p>
      <p>
       -Suzette Smith
      </p>
