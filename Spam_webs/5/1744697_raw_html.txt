           <div class="group-content group-review-content field-group-div" id="node-review-full-group-review-content">
            <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
             <div class="field-items">
              <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
               <p class="MsoNormal">
                You really dont know what to expect when you hear a band whose name is The Real Nasty. The three member band combines a mix of country and rock to bring an old school feeling to their music. This means jamming guitar solos, classic vocal tracks, and a softer tone than what todays hard rockers head bang to.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p class="MsoNormal">
                Dirty Dollars has the feel of real music. It consists of simple vocals with simple guitars and other musical instruments. Theres no crazy auto-tune or overbearing synthesizers that mask the actual talent this trio has.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p class="MsoNormal">
                Some may find this type of music to boring or simple, but The Real Nasty manage to put an album together thats a refreshing break from the norm. It isnt the best thing out there but Dirty Dollars works well at keeping you in a peaceful mood. This is the kind of music you want to listen to when you just want to take a break from it all.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p class="MsoNormal">
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <p>
                Perhaps the best thing about the album is that you can stream the entire thing from
                <a href="http://deli.therealnasty.com/dirty-dollars">
                 The Real Nastys official website
                </a>
                . You can also download it at whatever price you wish. Want to pay $15 for it? Go ahead. Feel that its better at $0? Thats fine too. Regardless, this is definitely one album worth listening to.
               </p>
               <p>
                <a href="http://www.twitter.com/Majiesto">
                 Follow me on Twitter @Majiesto
                </a>
               </p>
