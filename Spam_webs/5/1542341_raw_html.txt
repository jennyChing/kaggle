     <div class="entry-content">
      <p>
       Recently the Obama Administration proposed specific regulatory changes that, among other restrictions, would virtually ban the possession of otherwise legal National Firearms Act (NFA) products for many law-abiding citizens. Specifically, the proposal targets NFA Trusts  a common way for Americans to legally obtain NFA items.
      </p>
      <p>
       Advancement Armament Corps (AAC) is taking this issue very seriously and is encouraging all gun owners and Second Amendment advocates to contact their U.S. Senators and Representative and voice their concerns about this anti-gun proposal.
      </p>
      <p>
       Heres how you can help!
      </p>
      <p>
       Step 1: Become a gun rights
       <b>
        ADVOCATE
       </b>
       :
       <a href="http://takeastandnow.com/contact-your-legislator/">
        <b>
         Click here
        </b>
       </a>
       to send a prepared email to your elected officials.
      </p>
      <p>
       Step 2: Become a gun rights
       <b>
        AMBASSADOR
       </b>
       : E-mail this link to your friends and family. Urge them to stand up to those attempting to curtail our Right to Keep and Bear Arms.
      </p>
