      <p>
       The King of Pop allegedly used sex-related code words and secret hand signals with an underage boy who's now suing his estate.
      </p>
      <p>
       James Safechuck, 36, filed his original molestation claim in May but added disturbing details of Michael Jackson's alleged abuse in new paperwork filed this week.
      </p>
      <p>
       He claims Jackson, who died in 2009, started abusing him when he was 10 years old and taught him to refer to semen as "duck butter" and an erection as "bright light, brick city," the documents obtained by TMZ.com reportedly say.
      </p>
      <p>
       A lawyer for Jackson's estate blasted Safechuck's accusations and said his lawsuit should be scrapped.
      </p>
      <div class="a-module">
       <div class="a-video">
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="357" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/NHoW2vsk5lA" width="635">
        </iframe>
       </div>
      </div>
      <p>
       "Mr. Safechuck's request to file a late claim against the Jackson Estate so he can recover money from Michael's beneficiary should be rejected," lawyer Howard Weitzman said in a statement obtained by the Daily News.
      </p>
      <p>
       "This is a person that made his claim five years after Michael died, more than 20 years after the incidents supposedly happened and who has given sworn testimony that Michael never did anything inappropriate to him," Weitzman said.
      </p>
      <p>
       In the paperwork obtained by TMZ, Safechuck says Jackson would scratch the inside of his hand with a finger as a signal he wanted to have sex.
      </p>

      <p>
       Safechuck claims Jackson even performed a secret wedding ceremony with him complete with a wedding ring and fake marriage certificate.
      </p>
      <p>
       He said Jackson molested him at multiple locations, including a place called "The Hideout" in Century City, where they would drink sweet pink wine and watch porn, including movies of children masturbating, TMZ reported.
      </p>
      <p>
       Safechuck reportedly claims in the new paperwork that he once confessed to Jackson he had a crush on the singer Sheryl Crow, and Jackson was so jealous, he showed Safechuck unflattering photos of Crow without makeup.
      </p>
      <div class="a-module" itemid="http://assets.nydailynews.com/polopoly_fs/1.1896292!/img/httpImage/image.jpg_gen/derivatives/article_635/jacko9n-10-web.jpg" itemprop="associatedMedia" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">

       Safechuck, who first crossed paths with Jackson while filming a 1987 Pepsi ad, claims the abuse stopped after he reached puberty.
      </p>
      <p>
       He filed his claim following a similar molestation lawsuit brought by choreographer Wade Robson against the estate last year.
      </p>
      <p>
       In his highly redacted filing, Robson says the pop star invited him to Neverland Ranch in early 1990 - when he was 7 years old - and quickly initiated the abuse.
      </p>
