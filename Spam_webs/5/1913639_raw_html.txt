      <h1>

      <p class="meta">
       Posted April 30, 2014 in

      <div class="desc">
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.deringhall.com/designers/la-cienega-design-quarter-lcdq">
         La Cienega Design Quarter
        </a>
        (LCDQ), the premier design district in Los Angeles, is holding its sixth annual
        <a href="http://lcdqla.com/legends-2014/">
         Legends
        </a>
        event from May 7th-9th.  In addition to panels, speakers, and social events, over forty top interior designers will create showroom window vignettes inspired by great novels. Ranging from the serenity of The Secret Garden to the modernity of Fahrenheit 451, here are seven looks inspired by the book themes featured at
        <a href="http://www.deringhall.com/designers/jean-de-merry">
         Jean de Merry
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.deringhall.com/designers/dragonette-ltd">
         Dragonette Ltd.
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.deringhall.com/designers/downtown">
         Downtown
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.deringhall.com/designers/harbinger">
         Harbinger
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.deringhall.com/designers/suzanne-rheinsteinhollyhock">
         Hollyhock
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.deringhall.com/designers/waterworks">
         Waterworks
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://www.deringhall.com/designers/nanz">
         Nanz
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.deringhall.com/designers/remains-lighting">
         Remains Lighting
        </a>
        .
       </p>
