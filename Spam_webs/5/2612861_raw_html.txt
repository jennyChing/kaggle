        <p>
         I was given the challenge to mix things up with
         <a href="https://ooh.li/7c43b93" target="_blank">
          Progresso Bread Crumbs
         </a>
         and it couldnt have come at a better time nor a better day.  I love to cook from scratch but I didnt have time for chopping my own herbs or for making my own bread crumbs today.  I had to get up bright and early to be at the school for Abbys honors program.  By the time it was over, I barely had time to fix lunch but I knew I had to because Ive got to go to graduation tonight and I wanted to fix lunch and dinner at the same time.  On top of that, Abs teacher said she could go home early if she wanted to after the awards program and of course she did.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://ooh.li/7c43b93" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Progresso Bread Crumbs at Publix" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5180" height="427" src="http://shabbychicboho.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_8344-640x427.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I had taken out lean ground beef for a homestyle meatloaf with Progresso Bread Crumbs but I knew that wasnt going to go over with Abby at all.  She dont like onions and I like a lot of them, so, I had to dash in and grab some chicken and while I was standing in line to check out I decided I could save time by breading the chicken with my Progresso Italian Style Bread Crumbs and popping it in the oven with the meatloaf.  Quick and easy.  Ive used bread crumbs in my meal preparation before but this was the first time I used
         <a href="https://ooh.li/7c43b93" target="_blank">
          Progresso Italian Style Bread Crumbs
         </a>
         and I really wasnt sure that the chicken or even the meatloaf would taste good with it but I decided to give it a try anyhow.
        </p>
        <p>
         They. Tasted. Amazing.
        </p>
        <p>
         Tiffany popped in for lunch and the three of us couldnt believe how delicious everything was with the bread crumbs.  I will NEVER run out of them.  I go to Publix a couple of times a month and I plan to stock up on
         <a href="https://ooh.li/7c43b93" target="_blank">
          Progresso Bread Crumbs at Publix
         </a>
         from here on out.  In fact, I was so impressed with the taste that Id love to try all of their flavors.
        </p>
        <p>
         For my meatloaf, I used 1 lb of lean ground beef, 1 egg, 1/2 diced small onion, dash of ketchup, salt and pepper and the bread crumbs.  I didnt measure it but if I was to guess, I used about 1/4 to 1/3 cup of the Italian style bread crumbs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://ooh.li/7c43b93" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Progresso Italian Style Bread Crumbs " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5175" height="427" src="http://shabbychicboho.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_8350-640x427.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://ooh.li/7c43b93" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Progresso Italian Style Bread Crumbs" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5176" height="427" src="http://shabbychicboho.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_8354-640x427.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         For my chicken, I used chicken breasts, dipped them in milk, then the bread crumbs.  It was that easy to have a meal that was so tasty, youd of thought you were at a restaurant somewhere.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://ooh.li/7c43b93" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5178" height="427" src="http://shabbychicboho.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/IMG_8356-640x427.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         COUPON ALERT: Save $1.00 ON TWO when you buy TWO any variety
         <a href="https://ooh.li/7c43b93" target="_blank">
          Progresso Bread Crumbs at Publix
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Since I was in a hurry, I preheated the oven to 400 (I usually cook at 350) and let the meatloaf and the chicken cook for about 45 minutes, then I popped in my jiffy corn muffins.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://ooh.li/7c43b93" target="_blank">

         </a>
