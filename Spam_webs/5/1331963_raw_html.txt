       <p>
        Children of Lesotho by Jason vd Merwe.
       </p>
       <p>
        I went to Lesotho on an outreach/ mission with a Church based in in Stellenbosch Kruiskerk together with a group of Students from the Stellenbosch University. Before embarking on the trip I knew I was in for a treat into an endless opportunity of photography possibilities. If I had the time to do photography. My inspiration behind the photos are purely my love for children, photography and to inspire people to go out and help. I went there for three weeks, we worked everyday helping these people work on their crops etc. We did Months of work for them in a matter of days and the happiness that brought to them wasspeechless. More relevant to the photos when they were taken there is a massive communication block between me and them I cant speak their language. So then firstly making them understand what my camera is was quite a challenge with some of them as they arent as privileged as you and I to be in contact with technology on a daily basis. A lot of them were afraid at first so I had to snap a quick shot and then show them the back of the screen. After doing that a few times and then playing  with them they got more and more use to it and enjoyed it and that is when you capture real emotion in them.
       </p>
       <p>
        You can see more at www.jasonvdmerwephotography.com
       </p>
