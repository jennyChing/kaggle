        <h1>
         Romantic surprises for Your Beloved
        </h1>
        <ul class="navigation byline">
         <li>
          <a class="nolink">
           Posted by
          </a>
          <a href="/profile/Angara">
           Angara Inc
          </a>
          <a class="nolink">
           on September 30, 2014 at 1:17am
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          <a class="xg_sprite xg_sprite-view" href="http://www.bridaltweet.com/profiles/blog/list?user=2suqiaxi5jxga">
           View Blog
          </a>
         </li>
        </ul>
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="xg_module_body">
       <div class="postbody">
        <div class="xg_user_generated">
         <div align="justify">
          Life is too short to regret the things that had never happened; it is better not get a chance to remorse especially when it is a husband-wife or boyfriend-girlfriend relationship. People are so hooked up with their everyday responsibilities that they dont get leisure time to relish with family and friends. Everyone finds it difficult to keep up the spirit of love and friendship alive and thus, God has given us reasons to celebrate and make merry in the form of festivals.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          The last quarter of the year is reserved for conviviality and commemoration, wherein you get a break from your mundane life. This is the time to beat the drum and honor each others presence, without which survival would be highly troublesome. To make each day of the celebration season rejoicing, do something out of the blue. Surprise your soul mate with some romantic and creative ideas as mentioned. This is sure to rejuvenate the essence of your love affair.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Plan an exciting vacation
          </strong>
         </div>
         <div align="justify">
          <a href="http://api.ning.com:80/files/aDnEqQYbuL8*YefBczw6gJ8FgawfuVmjT*Fpjd9JioqoOSzm17mCwq90kKkLNWJhRrmrJwtNEr4KFwGAnVw4cuE88HIkAFmn/145.jpg" target="_self">
           <img class="align-center" src="http://api.ning.com:80/files/aDnEqQYbuL8*YefBczw6gJ8FgawfuVmjT*Fpjd9JioqoOSzm17mCwq90kKkLNWJhRrmrJwtNEr4KFwGAnVw4cuE88HIkAFmn/145.jpg" width="615"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          Sometimes it becomes really important to get a layoff from the boredom of routine life. Not a very long, but a short weekend trip in the arms of the natural world will unwind the tangles as developed in your relationship. Take her to a lovely park where you both can cherish the beautiful woodland paths and divine colors of Fall or go for an adventurous voyage where you can engage in recreation and exploratory sports activities.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Say Thank You
          </strong>
         </div>
         <div align="justify">
          <a href="http://api.ning.com:80/files/aDnEqQYbuL*u*qS2Wt0miXe-OvkuIkEVKVK5FNcTgrnrB9Jc2Fn2aJpZDCctoJoqcOxHldjIYZjweYu9UDCfMBxq6ndy-uQ0/saythankyou.jpg" target="_self">
           <img class="align-center" height="408" src="http://api.ning.com:80/files/aDnEqQYbuL*u*qS2Wt0miXe-OvkuIkEVKVK5FNcTgrnrB9Jc2Fn2aJpZDCctoJoqcOxHldjIYZjweYu9UDCfMBxq6ndy-uQ0/saythankyou.jpg?width=750" width="545"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          You become so busy in your regular and methodical days that you overlook the little things that she does for you. However, none of her efforts goes unrecognized; its just your over-occupancy that keeps you mute at times. Saying a simple thank you can delight her so much so that she experiences this feeling until the next thank you note comes to her. You can even hide some romantic notes in different corners of the house and let her play the heart-filling treasure hunt. This can be a dramatic idea to appreciate her companionship in all sweet and bitter moments.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Buy her sparkling gifts
          </strong>
         </div>
         <div align="justify">
          <strong>
           <a href="http://api.ning.com:80/files/aDnEqQYbuL*YRfAZYniqkCtEV3QZWe4wfIkH4h1k*zBcVU*TPyp6IkVzsKw77fKe68LTi-QDVk83LIPIW3CVx3ziP*IpO8bU/DiamondRingGiftsforwife.jpg" target="_self">
            <img class="align-center" src="http://api.ning.com:80/files/aDnEqQYbuL*YRfAZYniqkCtEV3QZWe4wfIkH4h1k*zBcVU*TPyp6IkVzsKw77fKe68LTi-QDVk83LIPIW3CVx3ziP*IpO8bU/DiamondRingGiftsforwife.jpg" width="670"/>
           </a>
          </strong>
         </div>
         <div align="justify">
          <br/>
          Even though a fresh bouquet of flowers can please and touch the chords of a girl, but I believe adorning her with some sparkles is a much more pleasant way of valuing her presence. Shop for a jewelry ensemble that she adores to don; it can be a
          <a href="http://www.angara.com/earrings/diamond-earrings.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           dainty pair of diamond stud earrings
          </a>
          , birthstone ring, or an elegant and charming necklace for her ravishing neckline. Aside from regarding the gorgeous gift, she will admire your endeavors for her whole life.
         </div>
