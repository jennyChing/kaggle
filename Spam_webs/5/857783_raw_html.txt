      <article class="clearfix post-67378 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-art category-art-crush-art category-interviews-2 tag-art-basel-2014 tag-interview tag-jen-stark tag-proust-questionnaire tag-wetransfer" id="post-67378" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting" role="article">
       <header class="article-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title single-title" itemprop="headline">
         <span>
          Jen Starks Proust Questionnaire
         </span>
        </h1>
        <div class="byline vcard">
         <div class="author-wrap">
          words by
          <span class="author">
           <a href="http://livefastmag.com/author/vivianne/" rel="author" title="Posts by Vivianne Lapointe">
            Vivianne Lapointe
           </a>
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="posted-on">
          on
          <time class="updated" datetime="2014-12-10" pubdate="">
           December 10, 2014
          </time>
         </div>
         <hr class="retina"/>
         <section class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
          <figure class="main-image">
           <img alt="Whirl-side-02-lores" class="attachment-full wp-post-image" height="667" src="http://files.livefastmag.com.s3.amazonaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Whirl-side-02-lores.jpg" width="1000"/>
          </figure>
          <p>
           Contemporary artist
           <a href="http://www.jenstark.com/" target="_blank">
            Jen Stark
           </a>
           is a Miami native whose specialty is incredibly intricate paper sculptures. At first glance it is psychedelic, on second take it is brilliant. Inspired by nature and the concepts of replication and infinity, Stark recreates the repetitive patterns with various mediums  paper, wood, and mirrors are all common materials found in her art. Aside from Starks hypnotic art featured at Eric Firestones gallery as a part of Untitled, the young artist made a huge splash with her
           <a href="http://jenstark.com/news/?p=1832" target="_blank">
            teepee project
           </a>
           installation at the Mondrian, and kept the party going with Friends with You and Misaki Kawaii as they released a limited edition collection of pool toys at the Soho House. After such a non-stop week, you wont be surprised to find outshes chilling in the Keys right nowSee below for Jens ProustQuestionnaire, brought to you by
           <a href="http://wetransfer.info/artbasel/" target="_blank">
            WeTransfer
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           1. What is your idea of perfect happiness?
          </p>
          <p>
           A nice view, no stress, good friends and family!
          </p>
          <p>
           2. What is your greatest fear?
          </p>
          <p>
           To be eaten by a monster
          </p>
          <p>
           3. What is the trait you most deplore in yourself?
          </p>
          <p>
           When I meet someone, I can never remember their names or faces. It takes me at least a few times of meeting them, and makes for some embarrassing moments.
          </p>
          <p>
           4. What is the trait you most deplore in others?
          </p>
          <p>
           Greed
          </p>
          <p>
           5. Which living person do you most admire?
          </p>
          <p>
           My mom
          </p>
          <p>
           6. What is your greatest extravagance?
          </p>
          <p>
           Sparkling water
          </p>
          <p>
           7. What is your current state of mind?
          </p>
          <p>
           Pretty stressed and busy due to Art Basel Miami, but having fun.
          </p>
          <p>
           8. What do you consider the most overrated virtue?
          </p>
          <p>
           I cant think of one virtue thats overrated.
          </p>
          <p>
           9. On what occasion do you lie?
          </p>
          <p>
           To make someone feel better.or to get myself into a party Im not on the list for. Haha
          </p>
          <p>
           10. What do you most dislike about your appearance?
          </p>
          <p>
           I wish I could have a tan year round. Im pretty pale.
          </p>
          <p>
           11. Which living person do you most despise?
          </p>
          <p>
           Meter maids
          </p>
          <p>
           12. What is the quality you most like in a man?
          </p>
          <p>
           The ability to make people laugh
          </p>
          <p>
           13. What is the quality you most like in a woman?
          </p>
          <p>
           Confidence
          </p>
          <p>
           14. Which words or phrases do you most overuse?
          </p>
          <p>
           Cool, Awesome, OK
          </p>
          <p>
           15. What or who is the greatest love of your life?
          </p>
          <p>
           My hubby Alvaro and my art!
          </p>
          <p>
           16. When and where were you happiest?
          </p>
          <p>
           I think every new moment I am happier than I was before. Life keeps getting better and better.
          </p>
          <p>
           17. Which talent would you most like to have?
          </p>
          <p>
           The ability to do amazing high-dives.
          </p>
          <p>
           18. If you could change one thing about yourself, what would it be?
          </p>
          <p>
           Id want to have a Jamaican accent.
          </p>
          <p>
           19. What do you consider your greatest achievement?
          </p>
          <p>
           A large scale art installation I completed last year at Chicago Outlet malls. I created a 20ft x 8ft hanging sculpture, as well as a huge 30ft x 40ft hand painted mural on an escalator in 1 month.
          </p>
          <p>
           20. If you were to die and come back as a person or a thing, what would it be?
          </p>
          <p>
           Probably would want to come back as an astro physicist or someone who studies the universe. I would finally figure out rhyme and reason to it all.
          </p>
          <p>
           21. Where would you most like to live?
          </p>
          <p>
           On an island in a hut somewhere far away.
          </p>
          <p>
           22. What is your most treasured possession?
          </p>
          <p>
           My pup Zelda and jewelry from my grandma.
          </p>
          <p>
           23. What do you regard as the lowest depth of misery?
          </p>
          <p>
           Not being able to do what you love everyday.
          </p>
          <p>
           24. What is your favorite occupation?
          </p>
          <p>
           Spelunker
          </p>
          <p>
           25. What is your most marked characteristic?
          </p>
          <p>
           My blonde hair and my nose that tends to point up a bit haha
          </p>
          <p>
           26. What do you most value in your friends?
          </p>
          <p>
           Generosity
          </p>
          <p>
           27. Who are your favorite writers?
          </p>
          <p>
           David Sedaris &amp; Michael Pollan
          </p>
          <p>
           28. Who is your hero of fiction?
          </p>
          <p>
           Jesus
          </p>
          <p>
           29. Which historical figure do you most identify with?
          </p>
          <p>
           Darwin
          </p>
          <p>
           30. Who are your heroes in real life?
          </p>
          <p>
           My best friends &amp; family
          </p>
          <p>
           31. What are your favorite names?
          </p>
          <p>
           Stella, Sterling, Jackson
          </p>
          <p>
           32. What is it that you most dislike?
          </p>
          <p>
           Pretentious people who treat you differently if they think youre important/famous or not.
          </p>
          <p>
           33. What is your greatest regret?
          </p>
          <p>
           Not being able to go to my grandpas funeral (was overseas studying abroad). He was one of the biggest influencers of me becoming an artist.
          </p>
          <p>
           34. How would you like to die?
          </p>
          <p>
           In little or no pain around loved ones, lucid, and able to say goodbye.
          </p>
          <p>
           35. What is your motto?
          </p>
          <p>
           Work hard play hard.
          </p>
         </section>
