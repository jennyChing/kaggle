         <p>
          The music world is mourning the death of legendary blues guitarist
          <a href="http://countrymusicnation.com/tag/b-b-king" title="B.B. King">
           B.B. King
          </a>
          , who died in Las Vegas at the age of 89, as confirmed by his lawyer.
         </p>
         <p>
          Attorney Brent Bryson tells The Associated Press that King died peacefully in his sleep at 9:40 p.m. PDT Thursday (May 14th) at his home in Las Vegas. Funeral arrangements are currently pending.
         </p>
         <p>
          Although King continued to perform well into his 80s, the 15-time Grammy winner suffered from diabetes and his health had been declining during the last year. He collapsed during a concert in Chicago last October, after complaining of dehydration and exhaustion. The blues icon had been in hospice care at his Las Vegas home in recent months.
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           This is a developing story. More details to come.
          </em>
