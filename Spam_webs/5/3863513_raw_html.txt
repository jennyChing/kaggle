         <p>
          Typography in web design is the most trending topic among designer community. Typography is the base of any websites look. A better typography would let visitors feel comfortable. While on other side, a nasty typography will help to ruin your website completely. Previously I have written about
          <a href="http://inspirationmind.com/typography-websites-how-to-use-typography-in-web-design/" title="Typography Websites  How to use Typography in Web Design? + Typography Tools &amp; Inspirations">

          </a>
          . Today, Im going to explore effective typography in web design with 20+ inspiration examples and 20 handwritten font styles.
         </p>
         <p>
          This article will feature
          <strong>
           websites with handwritten fonts
          </strong>
          . Mostly, kids and comic niche websites use handwritten fonts. But there is no restriction; you can also use handwritten fonts for your blog or website. Handwritten fonts are compiled to cursive style also which is well known font style.
         </p>
         <p>
          Handwritten fonts give your website typography a traditional look. Following 20+ inspiration examples are collected for their handwritten typography. These will lead you to the use of effective typography in web design. Moreover, you can download 20 handwritten free fonts as well to use with your web designs.
         </p>
