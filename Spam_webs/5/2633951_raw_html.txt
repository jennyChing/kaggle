                <br/>
                The biggest daily newspaper in  Switzerland is calling her the most successful DJane of Switzerland.  But the former international topmodel wants to make an impact with her  music and not her looks. During six years in the music business she  achieved more than this. Today Tanja La Croix is internationally highly  demanded as a DJ and producer with gigs at the hot spots of the party  world. She is the testimonial of Mercedes Benz Switzerland and  successfully released her new CD HOUSE KISS.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                Tanja La Croix  alias Tanja Wettach, born April 8th 1982, made her first musical  appearance in the clubs throughout Switzerland in 2005. The DJ from St.  Gallen loves House and Electronic music so much, she isnt satisfied to  just DJing and started to produce her own music early in her career.  Already in 2007 she released her debut album A TRIP TO BIKINI ISLAND and  gained chart position 49 in the official Swiss album charts with it.  What followed were tons of successful singles, remixes, EPs and  compilations.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                I cannot be a single day without House music.
                <br/>
                In the summer of 2010 Tanja La Croix landed the next big coup in her  career and became the face of the Street Parade. As the musical  ambassador of the biggest Techno-Party of the world she was mixing the  official Street Parade compilation and was celebrating with a half a  million fans on her own Love-Mobile.
                <br/>
                <br/>
                In 2011 the musician  released her fourth compilation HOUSE KISS and became the testimonial of  the luxury automobile brand Mercedes Benz Switzerland. Thats not all.  In the new funny Mercedes campaign she proved her driving skills and  delivered with the own production Star the adequate soundtrack for it.  The official Mercedes Benz Switzerland song appeared with other own  productions like Hard To Handle feat. Andy P. on her current release.  The compilation reached immediately position 11 of the official Swiss  compilation charts and received a great deal of attention on the most  important online portal,

                 beatport.com.
