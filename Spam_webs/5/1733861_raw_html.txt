       <h1 itemprop="name">
        Top 10 Beautiful Waterfalls of Iceland
       </h1>
       <h2>
        <small>
         Text by:
         <span itemprop="author">
          Einar Steinn Valgarsson
         </span>
        </small>
       </h2>
       <div class="social-media-sharing">
        <div class="facebook col-xs-5ths">

      <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="281" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/_henIN18dz0#t=15" width="500">

     <p>
      <strong>
       Iceland is a country of many amazing waterfalls. Below are some of the most beautiful waterfalls in Iceland.For more waterfalls see also:
       <a href="https://guidetoiceland.is/nature-info/waterfalls-in-iceland">
        Waterfalls in Iceland
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/about-iceland/article/waterfalls-in-iceland">
        WaterfallTips &amp; Tours
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="https://guidetoiceland.is/book-trips-holiday/nature-tours/golden-circle" style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">
        Golden circle tours
       </a>
       or the best attractions by
       <a href="https://guidetoiceland.is/best-of-iceland/best-attractions-by-the-ring-road-of-iceland" style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">
        Route 1 in Iceland
       </a>
       .
      </strong>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       Kirkjufellsfoss
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a class="fancybox" href="/image/4928/x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-1.jpg" rel="article-image">
        <img alt="Kirkjufellsfoss is located on the north side of Snaefellsnes peninsula in west Iceland" height="588" src="/image/4928/883x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-1.jpg" title="Kirkjufellsfoss is located on the north side of Snaefellsnes peninsula in west Iceland" width="883"/>
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      This small and charming waterfall is situated near the impressive mount Kirkjufell, at Grundarfjordur, on the
      <a href="/talk-to-locals/article/regina/the-magical-snaefellsnes-peninsula">
       Snaefellsnes peninsula
      </a>
      . For photographing, it is particularly scenic when you capture the fall, the clearness of the water and the mountain in the background, best experienced in the light of the
      <a href="https://guidetoiceland.is/nature-info/midnight-sun-in-iceland">
       midnight sun
      </a>
      in midsummer.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       Hraunfossar
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a class="fancybox" href="/image/4929/x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-2.jpg" rel="article-image">
        <img alt="Hraunfossar falls are located in Borgarfjordur in West Iceland" height="573" src="/image/4929/883x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-2.jpg" title="Hraunfossar falls are located in Borgarfjordur in West Iceland" width="883"/>
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      These stunningly beautiful falls, located in
      <a href="/travel-iceland/attraction/borgarfjordur">
       Borgarfjordur
      </a>
      in West Iceland, are formed by rivulets flowing at the edge of the Hallmundarhraun lava field, and pour into the glacier river Hvita(not to be confused with the river of the same name in Arnessysla, home to the Gullfoss waterfall, see below). These falls are widely considered some of the most spectacular in Iceland.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       Bruarfoss
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a class="fancybox" href="/image/4930/x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-3.jpg" rel="article-image">
        <img alt="Bruarfoss is located in Bruara river, in Southwest Iceland" height="1325" src="/image/4930/883x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-3.jpg" title="Bruarfoss is located in Bruara river, in Southwest Iceland" width="883"/>
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      This splendid series of small waterfalls is located in Bruara river, in the area of Grimsnes in Southwest Iceland, and considered something of a hidden gem. Watching the river falling in thousands of small runlets and the stark blue colour as the water falls into a deep gap makes it a fascinating scene and ideal for
      <a href="https://guidetoiceland.is/nature-info/photography-in-iceland">
       photographing
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       Svartifoss
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <a class="fancybox" href="/image/4931/x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-4.jpg" rel="article-image">
       <img alt="Svartifoss waterfall is located in Skaftafell National Park in Southeast Iceland" height="1325" src="/image/4931/883x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-4.jpg" title="Svartifoss waterfall is located in Skaftafell National Park in Southeast Iceland" width="883"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      This fascinating fall is located in the
      <a href="https://guidetoiceland.is/book-trips-holiday/day-tours/from-skaftafell">
       Skaftafell
      </a>
      preservation area in Southeast Iceland, that is a part of
      <a href="/about-iceland/article/national-parks-in-iceland">
       Vatnajokull National Park
      </a>
      . The name Svartifoss means Black Waterfall.A dramatic contrast is to be seen in the white water of the fall and the surrounding dark hexagonal basalt columns. At the base of the fall are sharp rocks that have broken from the columns. This picturesque scenery further provided inspiration for the columnar architecture of
      <a href="/travel-iceland/attraction/hallgrimskirkja">
       Hallgrimskirkja church
      </a>
      in Reykjavik, the ceiling of the National Theater and sculptor Richard Serras Milestones, located in
      <a href="/about-iceland/article/best-islands-to-visit-in-iceland">
       Videy island
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       Hrafnabjargafoss
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a class="fancybox" href="/image/4932/x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-5.jpg" rel="article-image">
        <img alt="Hrafnabjargafoss is located in Skjalfandafljot glacier river in North Iceland" height="587" src="/image/4932/883x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-5.jpg" title="Hrafnabjargafoss is located in Skjalfandafljot glacier river in North Iceland" width="883"/>
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Hrafnabjargafoss is a beautiful waterfall that is locatedin the mighty
      <a href="/travel-iceland/attraction/skjalfandafljot">
       Skjalfandafljot
      </a>
      glacier river in North Iceland, the first of three successive ones, the other two being Aldeyjarfoss and Godafoss.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       Aldeyjarfoss
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a class="fancybox" href="/image/4933/x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-6.jpg" rel="article-image">
        <img alt="Aldeyjarfoss is located in Skjalfandafljot in North Iceland" height="588" src="/image/4933/883x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-6.jpg" title="Aldeyjarfoss is located in Skjalfandafljot in North Iceland" width="883"/>
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      This is the second in a successive row of beautiful falls in Skjalfandafljot river and has a 20 metrecascade. As with Svartifoss, here youllsee a fascinating contrast between the white water of the fall and dark basalt columns, perfect scenery for the
      <a href="https://guidetoiceland.is/connect-with-locals/rarinnJnsson/a-little-photography-advice">
       avid photographer
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       Godafoss
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a class="fancybox" href="/image/4934/x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-7.jpg" rel="article-image">
        <img alt="Godafoss is located in Skjalfandafljot in North Iceland" height="588" src="/image/4934/883x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-7.jpg" title="Godafoss is located in Skjalfandafljot in North Iceland" width="883"/>
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      This waterfall, 12 meters high and 30 meters wide, is at once the most famous of the Skjalfandafljot waterfalls and one of the most famous in North Iceland and the country at large. According to the sagas, lawspeaker Thorgeir Ljosvetningagodi settled a religious crisis in Iceland by throwing the idols of the old Nordic gods into the fall, wherefrom it gets its name The waterfall of the gods. Certainly, those who witness the sheer beauty of the fall will agree that the name is fitting.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       Gullfoss
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a class="fancybox" href="/image/4935/x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-8.jpg" rel="article-image">
        <img alt="Gullfoss belongs to the famous Golden Circle in south Iceland" height="588" src="/image/4935/883x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-8.jpg" title="Gullfoss belongs to the famous Golden Circle in south Iceland" width="883"/>
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Widely considered the most beautiful of Icelandic waterfalls, the aptly named Gullfoss (The Golden Waterfall) belongs to the famous 
      <a href="/talk-to-locals/article/regina/the-golden-circle-tour-in-iceland">
       Golden Circle
      </a>
       of essential attractions, along with the Geysir geothermal area and
      <a href="/travel-iceland/attraction/thingvellir">
       Thingvellir National Park
      </a>
      . This thunderous waterfall, located in the mighty Hvita glacier river in Arnessysla county in South Iceland, drops down 32 meters into a narrow river gorge and is every photographers dream. You can even walk near enoughto feel the water spray on your face!
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       Skogafoss
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a class="fancybox" href="/image/4936/x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-9.jpg" rel="article-image">
        <img alt="Skogafoss is located on the South Coast of Iceland close to the Skogar area" height="588" src="/image/4936/883x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-9.jpg" title="Skogafoss is located on the South Coast of Iceland close to the Skogar area" width="883"/>
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Skogafoss is a popular destination when travelling along the
      <a href="/tour/category/waterfalls/south-coast--waterfalls">
       South Coast
      </a>
      of Iceland. It is located close to the area of Skogar. 60 meters wide and 25 high, it is one of the greatest and most beautiful of all waterfalls in Iceland. Photographers should particularly note that on sunny days it may produce a single or even a double rainbow, due to the strong waterflows interplay with the sunlight.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       Seljalandsfoss
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <strong>
       <a class="fancybox" href="/image/4937/x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-10.jpg" rel="article-image">
        <img alt="Seljalandsfoss waterfall is located on the South Coast of Iceland" height="588" src="/image/4937/883x/0/top-10-beautiful-waterfalls-of-iceland-10.jpg" title="Seljalandsfoss waterfall is located on the South Coast of Iceland" width="883"/>
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Seljalandsfoss is another of widely popular falls when travelling the south coast. This is a narrow and tall fall, with a drop of 63 meters, and it has the rare distinction that you can actually walk behind it, a spectacular photographic angle. This is indeed considered one of Icelands most picturesque waterfalls, and absolutely essential to visit when travelling in the country.
     </p>
