          <a href="http://cigarettezoom.com/emily-scott-selfie-stirs-resentiment/">
           Emily Scott Selfie Stirs Resentiments
          </a>
         </h2>
         <div class="single_metainfo">
          by Jenny Novac on July 23, 2014
         </div>
         <p>
          <img alt="Emily Scott Selfie with Cigarette" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6613" height="505" src="http://cigarettezoom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/emily-scott-selfie-smoking.jpg" width="500"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Latest image of Australian model Emily Scott aroused resentment on the web as she appears on a selfie with a cigarette. Blonde girlfriend of Shane Warne, the famous Australian cricketer, posted this week on her Instagram an image where she holds a cigarette in her hand. The photo appeared with the next caption: Smoke break #winter #sydney #sun.
         </p>
         <p>
          The image gathered a lot of likes, however, it also gathered many negative comments from fans who remain shoked with it. The majority of fans definitely disliked the fact that their favorite beautiful model smokes and posts images with
          <a href="http://cigarette-deals.com/galaxy">
           discount cigarettes
          </a>
          on web.
         </p>
         <p>
          Under the photo users left next comments:
         </p>
         <p>
          Nothing hot about this
         </p>
         <p>
          Thats pretty poor, my dad has lung cancer Ill send you a pic of what he like after he has treatment
         </p>
         <p>
          Pretty poor role model. Gross
         </p>
         <p>
          Not all comments had same negative message, however, later Emily Scott deleted the image from the Instagram.
         </p>
         <p>
          Shane Warne and Emily Scott became the official couple from this month.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Shane Warne and Emily Scott" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6616" height="1000" src="http://cigarettezoom.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/shane-warne-and-emily-scott.jpg" width="650"/>
