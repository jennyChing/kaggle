              <b>

              <br/>
              There are many android games and apps freely available,many of us are interested to play android games.But sad thing is these games are only available for android.

              <br/>
              If you don't have android still you can play those games on Pc or laptop using A software called
              <b>
               Bluestacks
              </b>
              .

              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <br/>
              There are many
              <b>
               software
              </b>
              's available but my choice is
              <b>
               Bluestacks
              </b>
              because it is best among them.
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <br/>
              <h4 style="text-align: left;">
               Steps
              </h4>
              <div style="text-align: left;">
               <br/>
               1.Go to
               <a href="http://www.bluestacks.com/" target="_blank">
                <b>
                 bluestacks
                </b>
               </a>
               to download software.
               <br/>
               2.After downloading it double click on bluestacks so that it will be installed on your or laptop.
              </div>
              <br/>
              Now you can play Android games on it.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

                google play
