       <h2>
        <span class="mw-headline" id="Sierra_Trading_Post_Coupons_and_Codes">
         Sierra Trading Post Coupons and Codes
        </span>
       </h2>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Sierra Trading Post offers free shipping and percentages off discounts on orders over a specified amount at various times throughout the year. These discounts are published on the store's official website in the form of coupon codes that can be entered upon checkout.
         <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-official_1-0">
          <a href="#cite_note-official-1">
           [1]
          </a>
         </sup>
         In addition, the store also offers a
         <b>
          Coupons
         </b>
         page featuring the most recent discounts and deals.
         <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
          <a href="#cite_note-2">
           [2]
          </a>
         </sup>
        </li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Coupons and codes for Sierra Trading Post are sometimes offered through third-party coupon websites and include percentages off savings and dollars off shipping discounts.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li>
         The Sierra Trading Post offers a 20 percent off coupon code to its
         <a href="/Facebook_Freebies" title="Facebook Freebies">
          Facebook fans
         </a>
         and may occasionally offers additional discounts, deals, and store news throughout the year.
         <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
          <a href="#cite_note-3">
           [3]
          </a>
         </sup>
        </li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li>
         The Sierra Trading Post offers shoppers the
         <b>
          Give $10, Get $10
         </b>
         program. Once a customer refers his or her friend (who must be a first time customer) to the store, the Sierra Trading Post will send that friend a $10 coupon to spend. Customers that referred the friend to the store will in turn receive a
         <a href="/Free_Gift_Card_Offers" title="Free Gift Card Offers">
          free $10 gift card
         </a>
         after their friend makes their first purchase.
         <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">
          <a href="#cite_note-4">
           [4]
          </a>
         </sup>
        </li>
       </ul>
       <ul>
        <li>
         The Sierra Trading Post notifies subscribers of its e-mail
         <b>
          Deal Flyer
         </b>
         of giveaways, exclusive monthly free shipping deals, additional discounts of up to 25 percent off, and information on "secret sales." Upon enrollment, new subscribers receive a 25 percent off coupon code when they spend $75 or more.
         <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5">
          <a href="#cite_note-5">
           [5]
          </a>
         </sup>
        </li>
       </ul>

       <h2>
        <span class="mw-headline" id="About_Sierra_Trading_Post">
         About Sierra Trading Post
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Sierra Trading Post is a retail and online store that offers overstock and closeout items at 35 percent to 70 percent off the regular retail price. The first Sierra Trading Post location opened in Reno, Nevada in July 1986. Today the company has four retail stores and two call centers. The store's corporate headquarters are located in Cheyenne, Wyoming.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6">
         <a href="#cite_note-6">
          [6]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </p>
       <p>
        The Sierra Trading Post offers a variety of items that include men, women, and children's clothing and footwear, sporting goods items, home decor items, and outdoor gear. The store features brand name items from Smartwool, Keen, Carhatt, and Dansko. The Sierra Trading Post also provides customers with services such as secure online shopping, order tracking, live online chat, size charts, recall information, buying guides, and
        <a href="/Free_Gift_Card_Offers" title="Free Gift Card Offers">
         gift cards
        </a>
        .
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-official_1-1">
         <a href="#cite_note-official-1">
          [1]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </p>
