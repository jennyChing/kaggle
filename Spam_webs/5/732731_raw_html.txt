             <p class="police_note">
              December 8, 2014
             </p>
             <p>
              Attracting visitors to your website can be a daunting task, especially if youre unfamiliar with search engine optimization techniques and search engine advertising.
             </p>
             <p>
              Without the appropriate guidance and tools, you can find yourself throwing good money after bad in a vain effort to attract more traffic.
             </p>
             <p>
              So its important to have a general understanding of how
              <a href="/EN/blog/Pages/seo-basics-entrepreneurs-easy-tips-optimizing-website.aspx">
               search engine optimization
              </a>
              (SEO) works and how you can use lowcost Internet tools to improve your efforts.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Why competitive intelligence matters
             </h3>
             <p>
              To successfully use SEO techniques and online ads, you need to know your competition. This will give you a better idea of how much money and effort will be needed to compete against them or find opportunities where they are not present.
             </p>
             <p>
              Free and lowcost tools such as
              <a href="https://moz.com/researchtools/ose/" target="_blank">
               OpenSiteExplorer.org
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://www.spyfu.com/" target="_blank">
               SpyFu.com
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://www.semrush.com" target="_blank">
               SEMRush.com
              </a>
              and
              <a href="https://adwords.google.com/KeywordPlanner" target="_blank">
               Google Keyword Planner
              </a>
              can help you in this process.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Assessing your SEO success
             </h3>
             <p>
              You may have heard the term
              <strong>
               organic traffic
              </strong>
              before. It refers to visits to your website that you did not pay for with online advertising.
             </p>
             <p>
              The more organic traffic your website attracts, the better, because youre not paying per click or impression. Tools like SEMRush.com and Spyfu.com provide you with a dollar estimate of the value of this organic traffic and the actual keywords that have brought it to your site.
             </p>
             <p>
              At the same time, you can observe which keywords are driving free traffic to your competitors websites and begin targeting the same keywords or identify other highvalue keywords that your competitors have missed.
             </p>
             <p>
              Below, you can see the performance of Company XYZs website, using SEMRush.com. The tool returned 1,176organic keywords that contributed to monthly traffic volume on Company XYZs website. This list also shows the position (Pos) of their website with respect to a specific keyword, the number of expected visits per month (volume) and the costperclick (CPC) in dollars should we want to pay Google to show up in searches for that specific keyword.
             </p>
             <div class="centre">
              <img alt="Performance with SEMRush.com" class="resize" src="/PublishingImages/blog/competitive-intelligence-digital-world-organic-keywords.jpg" style="margin-bottom:15px" title="Performance with SEMRush.com"/>
             </div>
             <p>
              Additionally, the tool identifies competitors who are ranking for the same keywords by showing common keywords that both Company XYZ and the competitor rank for in organic searches. Among other things, this feature allows you to identify competitors you might not have been aware of.
             </p>
             <p>
              Next, we took the first three competitor websites and used another tool called OpenSiteExplorer.org to look at each sites domain authority, a prediction (expressed as score out ofa100) of how a website will perform in search engine rankings.
             </p>
             <div class="centre">
              <img alt="Performance of competitors with OpenSiteExplorer.org" class="resize" src="/PublishingImages/blog/competitive-intelligence-digital-world-competitors.jpg" style="margin-bottom:15px" title="Performance of competitors with OpenSiteExplorer.org"/>
             </div>
             <p>
              With this tool, you can identify which sites are linking to the websites of your competitors (backlinks). This allows you to contact these linking sites and ask them to link to your site as well.
             </p>
             <p>
              Backlinks and keywords are the backbone of a strong search engine presence for any website. The stronger and more relevant the backlinks, the better your domain authority is going to be.
             </p>
             <h3>
              Using tools to improve your online ad campaigns
             </h3>
             <p>
              By combining tools like SEMRush and Google Keyword Planner, you can identify who is competing with your paid search ads and which keywords they are using in their campaigns.
             </p>
             <p>
              You can then piggyback on their keyword selections and be inspired by their approach to advertising messages to create your own marketing communications and ads that are strong and unique to your business.
             </p>
             <div class="centre">
              <img alt="Ads performance" class="resize" src="/PublishingImages/blog/competitive-intelligence-digital-world-organic-keywords.jpg" style="margin-bottom:15px" title="Ads performance"/>
             </div>
             <p>
              By using various web tools, you can learn more about your competitors and their online activities. You will save time and money by avoiding keywords that are ineffective for your business, focusing instead on keywords that have already been tested by other companies. This is especially true for keywords used to structure search engine ads.
             </p>
             <p>
              Tools like SEMRush.com, OpenSiteExplorer.org, SpyFu.com and Google Keyword Planner can help you focus on profitable opportunities while also exploring unchartered waters in your
              <a href="/EN/consulting/internet-consulting/Pages/internet-strategy.aspx">
               digital marketing
              </a>
              efforts.
             </p>
             <p class="intro">
              Do you have questions regarding how to analyze your competitors? Feel free to post them in the comments section below and will get back to you as soon as we can.
             </p>
