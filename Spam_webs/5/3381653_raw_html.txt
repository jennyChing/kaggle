        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <img alt="2015 Astrology Forecast" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-49443" height="515" src="http://1vlvnaa07ry3zp66wnhasyel1.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2015a.jpg" width="300"/>
          The astrology forecast for the year 2015,
         </strong>
         like last year, starts with a roller coaster but then smoothes out into an easier cosmic cruise through April to September. Winter however will give us some turbulence. Mercury goes retrograde through all the air signs this year and spending more time there should bring some interesting innovation and inventions. Mercury loves to fly through the air, so new ideas and thought forms should connect with lightening speed throughout the planet. Great ideas will go viral at the drop of a hat. Maybe we will feel like we can trust our intuition a lot more, as we find ourselves somehow able to communicate and grasp complex concepts without words. Instead we get symbols, dreams and rapid downloading of gnosis from the ether. We will learn things more rapidly than usual so its a good year for study, especially if you are very airy or if Mercury is strong in your chart.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Winter could be the most shocking in terms of global events. In our personal lives the volatile, but powerful energy from Octoberonwards could translate as breakthroughs. Of course there is also the possibility of breakdown, so be kind to yourselves. We have freewill so its up to you how you weld these powerful transits of change. Think of it as the cosmos is handing you a virtual sharp knife. You can be successful if you are decisive with it. This means cutting out all the parts of your life that are making you unhappy or ill. Otherwise you could just self-harm with it. Do realize the universe will see this as a cry for attention and will respond! If you take control of the cutting then you are less likely to attract unwanted attention, or worse, attract someone who grabs the knife from your dithering hands and starts hacking away without any anaesthetic!
        </p>
