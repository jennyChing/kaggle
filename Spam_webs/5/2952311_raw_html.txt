                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     <a href="http://ten-paces.blogspot.com/2012/03/happy-anniversary-ten-paces.html">
                      Happy Anniversary Ten Paces
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5270488914883329169" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <a href="http://www.tenpacesanddraw.com/" target="_blank">
                      <img height="275" src="http://www.tenpacesanddraw.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/celebrate.jpg" width="550"/>
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Hooray! Did you know that April 3rd marks Ten Paces and Draw year anniversary! This blog and community has grown so much in the past year, and we've gone from a few friends doing swaps to a contributors list over 120 talented artists. In honor of this milestone, and due to our snowballing project we've decided to make the switch from a blogspot to a brand new website!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     I've been teasing everyone with "big news" for a few month now, but the cat's out of the bag! You can visit
                     <a href="http://www.tenpacesanddraw.com/">
                      www.tenpacesanddraw.com
                     </a>
                     to check out our new website - please update your bookmarks! Ten-Paces.blogspot.com will make the official switch with next week's Disney swap, and this blog will be no longer accessible. All the past swaps will be archived on our brand new
                     <a href="http://tenpacesanddraw.tumblr.com/">
                      tumblr
                     </a>
                     , which you should follow if you're not already!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Thanks everyone for all the support over the past year, we couldn't have done this without are awesome artists and all of the fans of Ten Paces! Let us know what you think of the new site @tenpacesanddraw - we hope you love it! A super special shout out to the amazing
                     <a href="http://christophermuccioli.com/">
                      Christopher Muccioli
                     </a>
                     for his time and talent in getting this project off the ground. He is the creative mastermind behind our new branding, and our new website. Thanks Chris! Thanks everyone!
