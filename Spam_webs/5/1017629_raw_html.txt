     <h1 class="entry-title">
      Treats on a Stick, 29 Ways
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">
      <time class="published" datetime="2014-06-10T15:18:04+00:00">
       June 10, 2014
      </time>
      <p class="byline author vcard">
       By 
			Shari's Berries
      </p>
     </div>

   </div>
   <!-- /.hero -->
   <div class="wrap">
    <div class="container" role="document">
     <div class="content row">
      <main class="main " role="main">
       <p class="lead">
        Cake pops. Caramel apples. Cookies. Everything's better on a stick. How else could you eat pie at a cocktail party, with your hands, and not look like you have the table manners of a kindergartner? Plus they make treats fun and portable enough to eat anywhere: the beach, your backyard, on a train, on a plane ... If Dr. Seuss were still writing, we're pretty sure he'd write a book about treats on a stick. So without further ado, here are 29 of our favorite recipes.
       </p>
       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">

        <p>
         <strong>
          Ice Cream Cookie Pops
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/ice-cream-cookie.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick, Ice Cream Cookie Pops" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1946" height="532" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/ice-cream-cookie.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         A tried-and-true combination, ice cream and chocolate chip cookies go hand in hand when snack time rolls around. See how to make these ice cream sandwich pops at
         <a href="http://www.pnpflowersinc.com/2010/07/in-my-continuous-search-for-mini.html">
          Pen and Paper Flowers
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Mini Caramel Apples on a Stick
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/caramel-apples.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Mini Caramel Apples " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1934" height="777" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/caramel-apples.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The mini version of this party favorite has all the crisp, candy taste that everyone loves plus the earthy style of actual sticks gathered from the yard  scrubbed and cleaned, of course. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://allysonbakerdesign.blogspot.com/2010/11/twig-mini-caramel-apples.html">

         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Bunny Cookie Lollies
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>

          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Bunny Cookie Lollies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1942" height="594" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/easter-pops.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>

        <p>

         <a href="http://cakejournal.com/tutorials/how-to-make-a-bunny-cookie-lolly/">
          Cake Journal
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Cupcake Pops
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/cupcake-bites.jpg">

         </a>

        <p>

          Bakerella
         </a>

         <strong>
          Oreo Pops
         </strong>

        <p>

          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Oreo Pops" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1950" height="533" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/oreo-pops.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This recipe reinvents the popular cookie by dipping it in white chocolate and topping it with sprinkles. See how to make it at

          See Vanessa Craft

         <strong>
          Smores on a Stick
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/smore-pops.jpg">

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>

         <a href="http://melaniescrafts.blogspot.com/2010/06/smores-on-stick.html">

         </a>
         .

         <strong>

         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/butterfinger-pops.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Butterfinger Pops" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1933" height="502" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/butterfinger-pops.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Transform the crunchy candy bar into pops or even suckers! Theyre best served after having been frozen. See how to make them at

          Veronicas Cornucopia

         <strong>
          Watermelon Krispie Treats
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>

          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Watermelon Krispie Treats" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1959" height="628" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/watermelon-krispie-treats.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Its amazing what some food coloring can do. This summery snack is easy to make and now that youve put it on a stick, you dont have to worry about your hands becoming sticky. See how to make it at

          Glorious Treats
         </a>

        <p>
         <strong>
          Hot Chocolate on a Stick
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/hot-chocolate.jpg">

        <p>

         </a>

         <strong>
          Mini Buttermilk Cinnamon Bun Pops
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/cinnamon-pops.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Mini Buttermilk Cinnamon Buns" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1938" height="597" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/cinnamon-pops.jpg" width="900"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This breakfast mainstay just got even better, especially when you enjoy as much or little of the glaze as you like when they are served in dipping
         <br/>
         bowls. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://www.icecreambeforedinner.com/2011/06/mini-buttermilk-cinnamon-bun-pops.html">
          Ice Cream Before Dinner
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Skittles Pops
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/skittles-pops.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Skittles Pops" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1956" height="599" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/skittles-pops.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Step one: get Skittles. Step two: eat some of them. Step three: get marshmallows. Step four: eat some of those. Step five: crush skittles and sprinkle them onto marshmallow. Start enjoying this candy dream in just five easy steps. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://numstheword.com/skittles-marshmallow-pops/">
          Nums the Word
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Pancake Pops
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/pancake-pops.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Pancake Pops" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1951" height="533" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/pancake-pops.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Pancakes are always good, no matter what time of day it is. These bite-sized versions are no different. Top them with blueberries or dip them in maple syrup; anything goes once you take them off the griddle. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://tatertotsandjello.com/2012/05/make-pancake-pops-perfect-fathers-day-breakfast-recipe-tutorial.html">
          Tater Tots and Jello
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Frozen Grapes on a Stick
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/frozen-grapes.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Frozen Grapes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1943" height="533" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/frozen-grapes.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         It is just as easy to make as it sounds. This crisp-sweet summer snack only requires a couple of hours in the freezer. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://heritageschoolhouse.blogspot.com/2011/06/cool-snack-treat.html">
          Heritage School House
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Cookie Dough Pops
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/cookie-dough.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Cookie Dough Pops" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1939" height="600" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/cookie-dough.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         No baking required. Enjoy this beloved indulgence on a stick, one bite at a time. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://www.bakedperfection.com/2009/04/cookie-dough-pops.html">
          Baked Perfection
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Bite-Sized Baked Brie
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/baked-brie.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Baked Brie" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1930" height="534" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/baked-brie.jpg" width="800"/>

        </p>
        <p>

         <a href="http://joythebaker.com/2011/12/bite-sized-baked-brie/">
          Joy the Baker
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Caramel Popcorn Balls
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/popcorn-balls.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Caramel Popcorn Balls" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1954" height="531" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/popcorn-balls.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Whether youre about to curl up with a movie or are preparing treats for your kids birthday party, these peanut butter and chocolate-dipped popcorn balls are irresistible. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://dineanddish.net/2011/06/on-a-stick-by-matt-armendariz-recipe-caramel-popcorn-balls-on-a-stick/">
          Dine and Dish
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Bacon and Cheese Pops
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/bacon-cheese.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Bacon Cheese Pops" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1929" height="706" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/bacon-cheese.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         You could put bacon on pretty much anything and it would taste good. But put it on cream cheese and make a lollipop out of it? Now that is a treat impossible to beat. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://distractedcrafter.com/2011/06/cheese-pops/">
          Distracted Crafter
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Handmade Marshmallow Pops
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/marshmallow-pops.jpg">

        <p>

         </a>
         .

         <strong>

         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/mashed-potato.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Mashed Potato Pops" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1949" height="800" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/mashed-potato.jpg" width="800"/>

        </p>
        <p>
         Creamy, cheesy mashed potatoes have always been a popular side dish. With this recipe, you can let your creativity loose. Fill them with whatever you love, roll in breadcrumbs, fry and this side dish is sure to steal the spotlight. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://www.inerikaskitchen.com/2012/12/idaho-mashed-potato-pops.html">
          In Erikas Kitchen
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Twisted Shamrocks
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/pretzel-stick.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Twisted Shamrocks" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1955" height="667" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/pretzel-stick.jpg" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         In true St. Patricks Day fashion, these pretzels are joined by chocolate to form a four-leaf clover. Plus, the stick used is just another pretzel. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://pennysfoodblog.com/2014/03/16/twisted-pretzel-shamrocks/">
          Pennys Food Blog
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Easy Easter Cookie Pops
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/cookie-pops.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Cookie pops" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1940" height="809" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/cookie-pops.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont be fooled by the name; these treats can be made any time of the year. After spreading peanut butter between two buttery crackers, let that freeze so the stick wont fall off when dipping into white chocolate. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://dailycrave.wordpress.com/2012/04/03/easy-easter-cookie-pops/">
          Daily Crave
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Pizza on a Stick
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/pizza-stick.jpg">
          <img alt="Treats on a Stick | Pizza on a Stick" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1953" height="600" src="http://www.berries.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/pizza-stick.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Pizza is good anywhere, anytime. Of course, some people dislike the mess it can make, or the amount of grease on the slice. But homemade pizza rolls on a stick? Perfection. See how to make them at
         <a href="http://www.artisanbreadinfive.com/2009/09/01/pizza-on-a-stick">
          Artisan Bread in Five
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Chocolate-Covered Banana Split Bites

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         No spoon required. Let the splits sit in the freezer for a bit after dipping in chocolate sauce, then theyre ready to enjoy. See how to make it at
         <a href="http://fooddoodles.com/2012/06/11/chocolate-covered-banana-split-bites/">
          Food Doodles
         </a>
         .
