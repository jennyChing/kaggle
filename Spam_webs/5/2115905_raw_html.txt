        <p>

        <p>
         It is said in the tech market that with the Nokia Lumia 1520, Windows phone has never looked this great. Boasting of a vibrant display, the Nokia Lumia has a top of the shelf hardware. The imaging experience that users get from this phone is extraordinary and has people lining up to get a piece of the action. The build quality is great and this is a definitely plus in an age where manufacturers are compromising on the quality to ensure that it lies within the cost.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are the specs for the Nokia Lumia 1520 as seen on their official website.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Display size: 15.24cm
         </li>
         <li>
          Display resolution: Full HD (1920 x 1080)
         </li>
         <li>
          Processor name: Qualcomm Snapdragon 800
         </li>
         <li>
          Maximum talk time (3G): 25.1h
         </li>
         <li>
          Battery capacity: 3400mAh
         </li>
         <li>
          Wireless charging: Yes
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Dimensions
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Height: 162.8 mm
         </li>
         <li>
          Width: 85.4 mm
         </li>
         <li>
          Thickness2: 8.7 mm
         </li>
         <li>
          Weight: 209 g
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Display and User Interface
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Display size: 15.24 cm
         </li>
         <li>
          Display resolution: Full HD (1920 x 1080)
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Display features: Brightness control, Orientation sensor, High brightness mode, Refresh rate 60 Hz, Corning Gorilla Glass 2, Easy to clean, Nokia Glance screen, Lumia Color profile, Wide viewing angle, Sunlight readability enhancements
        </p>
        <p>
         Display colors: TrueColor (24-bit/16M)
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Aspect ratio: 16:9
         </li>
         <li>
          Pixel density: 368 ppi
         </li>
         <li>
          Display technology: ClearBlack, IPS LCD
         </li>
         <li>
          Touch screen technology: Super sensitive touch
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Sensors: Ambient light sensor, Accelerometer, Gyroscope, Proximity sensor, Magnetometer
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the best litmus test for any phone is to know how much it can last through various functions. Heres the claim for the Nokia Lumia 1520.
        </p>
        <p>
         Power Management
        </p>

         <li>
          Battery model: BV-4BW
         </li>
         <li>
