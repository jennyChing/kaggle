       <h2 class="container-header">
        About this job
       </h2>
       <div class="content">
        <div class="description-section">
         <h3 class="sub-header">
          Job description
         </h3>
         <div class="rich-text" itemprop="description">
          <p>
           We are looking to fill a few, very exciting Product Manager roles across a several of our product lines; hearing, gloves, clothing, respiratory and footwear. A product manager for one of these product lines is responsible for managing product marketing related activities for their portfolio and will be accountable for profitable growth of their relevant business.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Growth drive by innovation is critical to the deliver on our vision and strategy for the business, and New Product Introduction (NPI) is at the very core of everything we do as a team, our objective is to deliver product innovation to delight our customers and keep workers safe, every minute of every day.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           This is an impactful role for a broad based, business thinker with analytical, strategic thinking, project management and communication skills who can collaborate effectively across the entire business (marketing, engineering, R&amp;D, manufacturing, sourcing, sales and all relevant business leaders). The product manager position reports directly to the Product Marketing Leader for the business.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           The successful candidate will possess a strong business acumen with knowledge of product marketing and portfolio management, will have strong customer oriented skills and even stronger desire to get results to maximize growth, profitability and return on investment for the business.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are a dynamic, successful and driven professional with a proven track record of getting results then Honeywell is the company that will further your experience and career growth.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           The role is located in Bucharest, Romania, however, Brno (Czech Republic) is the preferred location. The candidates who are willing and interested in relocating will have a considerable advantage.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="skills-section">
         <h3 class="sub-header">
          Desired Skills and Experience
         </h3>
         <div class="rich-text">
          <p>
           <strong>
            <span style="text-decoration:underline">
             Position Requirements
            </span>
           </strong>
           <strong>
            :
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           The successful candidate will be expected to be a high-energy, creative and resourceful self-starter who demonstrates leadership skills and instincts. He/she is highly intelligent and enthusiastic with a commitment to excellence. In terms of personal competencies, the successful candidate must be a hands-on person who possesses the best combination of strategic thinking and drive to achieve tangible business results. The candidates must be able to balance theoretical thought with practical. Successful candidate will have string influence skills and will have proven they can lead and drive results within a cross-functional team. In addition, he/she should possess the following competencies:
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Strategic Marketing Skills:
            </strong>
            Deep understanding and experience in microeconomics, finance, VOC, research and analysis and basic marketing toolkit [market definition and sizing, segmentation, value management, targeting and positioning, pricing] with the ability to practically apply. Must link strategies to financial results.
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Program / Project Management:
            </strong>
            Have a working knowledge of project management techniques; utilize project management tools to load/balance resources, define critical paths and risk mitigation plans
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Leadership:
            </strong>
            A confident, mature person with the ability to connect with others. A proven track record of leading projects and teams that successfully achieve milestones and complete deliverables
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Results-Oriented:
            </strong>
            A driver who possesses the ability to take actions and implement effective solutions in a timely manner
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Problem Solver:
            </strong>
            A creative yet pragmatic problem solver. Methodical and hands-on as well as detail-oriented
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Analytical Thinking and Decision-Making:
            </strong>
            A conceptualizer of enterprise and market trends/issues who can then integrate that thinking into marketing and business strategies. Decisive and logical at thoroughly evaluating issues. Excellent planning, execution and project-management skills.
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Teamwork and Interpersonal Skills:
            </strong>
            A team player and builder, receptive to ideas from others. Shares information and keeps team members and partners informed. Works effectively with others to identify and resolve issues. Excellent interpersonal skills and an ability to interact successfully with all levels of management as well as a diverse work force.
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Ethics:
            </strong>
            Highest level of professional integrity and honesty as well as personal credibility.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <span style="text-decoration:underline">
            </span>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <span style="text-decoration:underline">
             Experience and Educational Requirements
            </span>
           </strong>
           <strong>
            :
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Minimum 3 to 5 years work experience with proven track record of success with demonstrable results
           </li>
           <li>
            Experience in industrial or consumer business (by specific product line) is desirable
           </li>
           <li>
            Must have demonstrated success leading marketing, strategic planning, product management, engineering
           </li>
           <li>
            Successful candidate must possess proven marketing, analytical, program/project management and implementation skills.
           </li>
           <li>
            Undergraduate degree in an analytical / technical (e.g. engineering, economics etc) discipline is required. An MBA or equivalent is preferred.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
