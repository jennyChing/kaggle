           <h3>
            <strong>
             Top Exercises For Bodybuilding
            </strong>
           </h3>
           <h4>
            <strong>
             Whole Body Exercises
            </strong>
           </h4>
           <p>
            If you are a beginner, the whole body exercises performed three times per week is the ideal routine for you. People who perform whole body exercises usually
            <strong>
             choose Monday, Wednesday and Friday as their workout days.
            </strong>
            These days are evenly spaced with break days, providing adequate rest to your body for muscle growth. Concentrate on compound exercises to involve more than one muscle at the time of execution.
           </p>
           <p>
            The common compound exercises involve overhead press, bench press, deadlifts, bent-over rows and squats. These exercises target hips, chest, lower back, upper back, legs and shoulders.
            <strong>
             Perform three sets with ten to twelve repetitions each.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <div style="width: 100%;margin-bottom:15px;">
            <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
            </script>
            <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-9763352381900777" data-ad-slot="2113351946" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
            </ins>
            <script>
             (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
            </script>
           </div>
           <p>
            According to Mile Mentzer and Arnold Schwarzenegger, compound exercises like squats, deadlifts and bench press are the fundamental workouts that helped them in achieving dense muscularity and improving strength.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <span class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9079 pib-pinterest" style="width: 450px; height: auto;">
             <a class="pib-pin" href="#" onclick='window.open("http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.bodybuildingestore.com%2Fbest-exercises-for-bodybuilding%2F&amp;media=http://www.bodybuildingestore.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/excercise1.jpg&amp;description=BodyBuilding eStore - http%3A%2F%2Fwww.bodybuildingestore.com%2Fbest-exercises-for-bodybuilding%2F","Pinterest","scrollbars=no,menubar=no,width=600,height=380,resizable=yes,toolbar=no,location=no,status=no");return false;'>

             </a>
            </span>
           </p>
           <h4>
            <strong>
             Isolation Exercises
            </strong>
           </h4>
           <p>
            The isolation exercises are solely meant for the intermediate and the advanced bodybuilders. Target the chest muscles and the triceps on Monday with normal bench presses, close grip bench presses, bar dips, triceps pushdowns and inclined dumbbell presses. On Tuesday, focus on your legs and perform squats, leg extension, leg curls and standing calf raises.
            <strong>
             Set Wednesday as the rest day.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <div style="width: 100%;margin-bottom:15px;">
            <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
            </script>
            <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-9763352381900777" data-ad-slot="3590085147" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
            </ins>
            <script>
             (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
            </script>
           </div>
           <p>
            On Thursday, perform overhead presses, lateral raises, dumbbell presses and barbell shrugs to focus on the trapezius and the shoulder muscles. On Friday, you can pump up your biceps and upper back muscles by performing pull ups, bent over rows, barbell curls and alternate dumbbell curls.
           </p>
           <p>
            Make sure you perform eight to ten repetitions per set for each exercise and do not allow more than two minutes rest in between the sets. Fewer exercises, intense workouts with heavy weights and three sets for each exercise are the three crucial factors related to
            <strong>
             muscle building exercises for bodybuilding.
            </strong>
           </p>

           </p>
           <h4>
            <strong>
             Super-sets
            </strong>
           </h4>
           <p>
            Adding super-sets to your exercise routine not only breaks the monotony, but also intensity of the workouts. In super-sets, you
            <strong>
             perform two exercises back-to-back without any rest in between the sets
            </strong>
            . While performing super-sets, make sure you do not exceed more than 10 repetitions per set.
           </p>
           <p>
            Some of the common super-sets pairs are lateral raises and shoulder presses for shoulders, flat bench flies and bench presses for chest, lying triceps extension and barbell curls for arms and leg extensions and leg presses for legs.
           </p>

            <span class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9081 pib-pinterest" style="width: 450px; height: auto;">

             </a>
            </span>
           </p>
           <h4>
            <strong>
             Drop Set Exercises
            </strong>
           </h4>
           <p>
            These are the
            <strong>
             super intensity exercises and are solely meant for the advanced bodybuilders
            </strong>
            who are unable to achieve muscle growth even after intensive training. After you have completed three sets of a particular exercise, strip the weight gradually and perform drop sets till complete muscle failure.
           </p>
