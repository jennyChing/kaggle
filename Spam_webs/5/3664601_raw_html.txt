       <p>
        Sometimes you just need to redecorate your home. Instead of heading down to your local IKEA and picking up furniture being held together with toothpicks, check out some of this furniture and get your geek on.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Binary Doormat
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1157" style="width: 410px">
        <a href="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/geek-furniture/binary-mat/" rel="attachment wp-att-1157">
         <img alt="Binary Mat" class="size-full wp-image-1157" height="262" src="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Binary-Mat.jpg" title="Binary Mat" width="400"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         It either says "welcome" or "no solicitors"
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        We start at the front door and while Im a bit rusty on my binary, Im assuming that this says Welcome or something to that effect.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Blank CD Chair
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1158" style="width: 367px">
        <a href="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/geek-furniture/blank-cd-chair/" rel="attachment wp-att-1158">
         <img alt="Blank CD Chair" class="size-full wp-image-1158" height="365" src="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Blank-CD-Chair.jpg" title="Blank CD Chair" width="357"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         It's either going to massage your back or rip it to shreds.  Good luck!
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        While Im not going to comment on how comfortable I believe this chair to be, you have to at least admire the resourcefulness of it. Im sure Im not the only one with spindle upon spindle of blank CDs and DVDs that I dont use anymore thanks to the rise in USB flash drives.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Bracket Shelving
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1159" style="width: 450px">
        <a href="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/geek-furniture/bracket-shelving/" rel="attachment wp-att-1159">
         <img alt="Bracket Shelving" class="size-full wp-image-1159" height="440" src="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Bracket-Shelving.jpg" title="Bracket Shelving" width="440"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Remember your rules for which item you have to count first.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Nice to see somebody incorporating brackets and parentheses into their wall shelving. Not only does this let you separate items on the shelf, it lets everybody that looks at it know that youre a geek.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Geek Clock
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1160" style="width: 511px">
        <a href="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/geek-furniture/geek-clock/" rel="attachment wp-att-1160">
         <img alt="Geek Clock" class="size-full wp-image-1160" height="414" src="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Geek-Clock.jpg" title="Geek Clock" width="501"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         The left hand is on the g, the right hand is on the square root of 4
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Not everybody wears a watch or has their cell phone on them to know what time it is. Hopefully even with this clock, theyll be able to tell what time it is. Some are obvious, others not so much.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Frozen in Carbonite Desk
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1161" style="width: 370px">
        <a href="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/geek-furniture/han-in-carbonite-desk/" rel="attachment wp-att-1161">
         <img alt="Han in Carbonite Desk" class="size-full wp-image-1161" height="400" src="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Han-in-Carbonite-Desk.jpg" title="Han in Carbonite Desk" width="360"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Leia unfreezing him is not going to help your work-flow
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Welcome to my office, where I can have a constant reminder of one of the great geek movies of all time. Ok, so I dont actually have this in my office, but how cool would it be (and what a great talking piece) to have this in you office?
       </p>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         iPhone Coffee Table
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1162" style="width: 510px">
        <a href="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/geek-furniture/iphone-coffee-table/" rel="attachment wp-att-1162">
         <img alt="iPhone Coffee Table" class="size-full wp-image-1162" height="377" src="http://www.digitalbusstop.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/iPhone-Coffee-Table.jpg" title="iPhone Coffee Table" width="500"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Sorry, you can only use one coaster at a time.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Ill be the first to say that Im not a fan of the iPhone (Android all the way!), but I do like this coffee table. To add to the geek cred, the app icons can be removed and used as coasters.
       </p>
       <h2>
        <strong>
         Keyboard Chair
        </strong>
       </h2>
