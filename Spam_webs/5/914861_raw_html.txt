      <div style="float: left; padding-bottom: 10px; word-wrap:break-word; display: block; width: 100%; margin-bottom: 20px; border-bottom: 1px solid #ddd;">
       <p>
        This month is National Get Organised Month and Easyshed know how much clutter and stuff family homes have so why not go to your garden workshop, get some tools and transform an unused cupboard or wardrobe into a space you can actually use  go on, you know you want to!
       </p>
       <ol>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Make a homework space
         </strong>
        </li>
       </ol>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-17276-1415654973-12.jpg">
          <img alt="enhanced-17276-1415654973-12" class="alignnone wp-image-4208 size-full" height="640" src="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-17276-1415654973-12.jpg" width="427"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Start with a cupboard with sliding doors preferably. Paint it using chalkboard paint then added some storage. Then build a floating desk and add some wall mounted lighting. After adding some office accessories from home craft and DIY stores, you are done!
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Design an invisible office
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-27749-1415718636-24.png">
          <img alt="enhanced-27749-1415718636-24" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4209" height="639" src="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-27749-1415718636-24.png" width="500"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Create a modern, out-of-the-way office that disappears when you want it to. Hang pretty curtains along the closet rod to cover up the space when youre ready to relax.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Make a workshop
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-9737-1415720926-13.jpg">
          <img alt="enhanced-9737-1415720926-13" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4203" height="1032" src="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-9737-1415720926-13.jpg" width="625"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
        Find a permanent home for bulky sewing machines inside an organized closet. Stow fabrics in woven bins and learn how to make a DIY pegboard
        <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://www.honeybearlane.com/2013/01/extra-large-pegboard-for-craft-room-organization.html" rel="external nofollow" title="">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Create a changing station
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-12096-1415821867-4.jpg">
          <img alt="enhanced-12096-1415821867-4" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4206" height="941" src="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-12096-1415821867-4.jpg" width="625"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Push bulky furniture out of the way and save space in the nursery.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Create extra book storage
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/comp.png">
         <img alt="comp" class="alignnone wp-image-4200 size-full" height="606" src="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/comp.png" width="541"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
        To store extra volumes while still keeping them on display, place books on the top shelf of the closet. If youre a real bookworm, add a few more shelves and turn the space into a mini library.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Enjoy a wine cellar
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-4571-1415832843-14.jpg">
         <img alt="enhanced-4571-1415832843-14" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4202" height="726" src="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-4571-1415832843-14.jpg" width="555"/>
        </a>
        <strong>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Install a wine refrigerator in your closet or just stack a few wine racks to store your precious vino.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Make a super storage system
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-17275-1415654703-12.jpg">
          <img alt="enhanced-17275-1415654703-12" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4207" height="777" src="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-17275-1415654703-12.jpg" width="518"/>
         </a>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
        Keep important papers organized with a series of shelves and a rolling file cabinet. If youre a photo junkie, create your own picture library with labelled boxes and rows of photo albums.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Read in a little library
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-10217-1415891541-1.jpg">
          <img alt="enhanced-10217-1415891541-1" class="alignnone wp-image-4204 size-full" height="675" src="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-10217-1415891541-1.jpg" width="450"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Build a simple bench, add a cushion, and arrange your childs favourite books to create the perfect reading nook. Get the full tutorial
        <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://thriftydecorchick.blogspot.com/2011/05/closet-turned-book-nook.html" rel="external nofollow" title="">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         DIY A Canopy Bed
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-32618-1415816349-4.jpg">
          <img alt="enhanced-32618-1415816349-4" class="alignnone wp-image-4210 size-full" height="640" src="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-32618-1415816349-4.jpg" width="427"/>
         </a>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Remove closet doors and push the crib against the inside wall. Perfect for kids that share a room.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Create a sleeping nook
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-993-1415816201-5.jpg">
         <img alt="enhanced-993-1415816201-5" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4201" height="937" src="http://www.easyshed.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/enhanced-993-1415816201-5.jpg" width="625"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Move your bed into the closet (just make sure you measure it correctly before moving). If you have to ditch the headboard, pick up some bed legs.
       </p>
