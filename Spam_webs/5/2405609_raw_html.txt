      <div class="post-content">
       <div class="inner-content clearfix">
        <div class="rw-right">
         <div class="rw-ui-container rw-class-blog-post rw-urid-10430" data-img="http://windowswebhostingreview.com/wp-content/uploads/no-e1428899492169.jpg">

        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="11275222" data-link="http://windowswebhostingreview.com/reasons-why-you-should-avoid-free-hosting/" data-summary="" data-title="Reasons Why You Should Avoid FREE Hosting">
        </div>
        <p>
         We believe that some of you want to use FREE hosting and dont want to spend money to build your site. But, the fact that you will face any problem while you use FREE hosting. In this article, we will show you the reasons why never go with free hosting. Nowadays, there are many cheap hosting provider that offer reliable hosting services.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="no" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1044" height="183" src="http://windowswebhostingreview.com/wp-content/uploads/no-e1428899492169.jpg" width="276"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are few reasons you should not go for FREE web hosting providers:
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1. Many Advertisement to Your Site
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         There are no free lunch in this world, you must surely pay in one way or the other. You may get FREE hosting on their server, but they will also put FREE banner advertisement on your site. You will see popup adds, popunder ads, affiliate links, banner links etc appear on your website or blog while using afree hosting service because this is one way to make you pay for their free server space.
        </p>
        <p>
         And the amazing thing that sometimes the banner ads is popup is your competitor and it will hurt to your business. Avoid FREE hosting!!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          2. You Cant Use Your Domain
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Sometimes, free hosting providers dont let you to purchase your own domain name. They will give you custom domain name for you. Why? With custom domain, they wish to promote their business by forcing you to use a lengthy domain like
         <a href="http://www.yoursite.freehostingprovider.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.yoursite.freehostingprovider.com', 'www.yoursite.freehostingprovider.com']);">
          www.yoursite.freehostingprovider.com
         </a>
         . We dont want to mention the name of the hosting provider but we believe that you might experience with this hosting provider if you use free hosting.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you are serious in business online, such domain name makes your business look unprofessional.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          3. The Performance is SUCKS
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont hope they will promise to give you 100% uptime guarantee. They wont!!! Your site will be up and down like rollercoaster. They will put all eggs on same bucket. One server can host thousand sites.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          4. There is no Guarantee about Customer Support
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="no-support" class="aligncenter wp-image-1043 size-full" height="180" src="http://windowswebhostingreview.com/wp-content/uploads/no-support-e1428899368151.jpg" width="180"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Free hosting is actually synonymous to no customer support for you. Just imagine how they hire people to answer your problem while they offer free hosting for you. You are not paying any cent from them, they have many servers that need to pay every month. How can you expect they will give you support? Although they have customers support, they actually would barely respond to your needs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          5. The Servers run Incredibly Slow
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Why? It is because they will put your site with a thousand sites on the same server. That makes your site often goes up and down like rollercoaster. This would definitely break the operation of several websites hosted on their servers. Andgetting your business offlinecould be disastrous at most times, especially if you own an eCommerce site.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          6. Your Data is not SAFE
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Please keep in mind that they can go bankrupt anytime as they cant afford to pay running cost every month. So, how is your data? We believe that you can answer it, all your data will be lost. You need to start from beginning again
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          Conclusion
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p>
         If you wish to run a successful website and online company, avoiding thefree web hosting companiesis one step in the right direction. There are many cheap and reliable hosting provider now, you may check the
         <a href="http://windowswebhostingreview.com/best-asp-net-hosting-awards-2015/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://windowswebhostingreview.com/best-asp-net-hosting-awards-2015/', 'hosting recommendation here']);">
          hosting recommendation here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
