            <h2 class="post-title">
             <a href="http://www.whompcomic.com/2015/06/09/the-end-of-video-game-streams/">
              The end of video game streams
             </a>
            </h2>
            <span class="post-author">
             by
             <a href="http://www.whompcomic.com/author/admin/">
              Ronnie
             </a>
            </span>

          <div class="entry">
           <p>
            Hi there~
           </p>
           <p>
            Most of you probably dont know or care (and thats perfectly okay!) that I used to stream a lot of video games.
           </p>
           <p>
            When I started drawing bonus comics and Otaku Dad, I lost a lot of the free time I used to stream.
           </p>
           <p>
            A happy alternative, of course, was streaming for just a couple-few hours whenever I felt like it.
           </p>
           <p>
            Unfortunately, streaming has turned into a frustrating experience, and I have no idea why.
           </p>
           <p>
            Its no ones fault but my own. My chat is almost entirely friendly and civil, but I get upset and tired easily when playing games. I frequently look at the clock to see how many hours Ive been streaming and if I can stop playing yet.
           </p>
           <p>
            The most egregious sin I commit is getting snippy with my chatters, and Im just not that guy. I want to be friendly and nice all of the time, but for some reason, that ability becomes lost to me after only short time after going live.
           </p>
           <p>
            I dont really know why it happens, but I dont want anyone to remember me as angry mean fellow playing the video games. Ive tried hard to enjoy streaming again, and for some reason I cant make it work.
           </p>
           <p>
            I want to thank everyone who made streaming possible. When I started, a donation drive helped me buy a capture unit so that I was able to properly stream Silent Hill 2 and some other console games. These were extremely fun experiences, and Im blessed to have been able to share them with people.
           </p>
           <p>
            Thank you for making it a greatly enjoyable time for me. Maybe someday Ill find joy in it again, but until then, Im not going to force it.
           </p>
           <p>
            I hope you have a wonderful day.
           </p>
           <p>
             Ronnie
           </p>
           <p>
            P.S. This does not affect Patreon reward drawing streams, which I still find to be a fun way to draw while interacting with people.
           </p>

           <p>
            Theres been a slight change in scheduling for the release of Patreon content!
           </p>
           <p>
            The eBooks of Volumes 1, 2 and 3 will be distributed on Feb 1st.
            <br/>
            The first Patreon-supported Whomp! will go live Feb 7th, along with the creation video with the Special Thanks at the end.
            <br/>
            The second Patreon-supported Whomp! will go live Feb 14th.
           </p>

           </p>

            </strong>

Before the session goes live, $10+ patrons will receive a message in Patreon with a unique session link. Expect the streams to last 2-4 hours.

           </p>

           </p>

            If youre not a patron, its not too late to get in on the hot February content!

            <h2 class="post-title">
             <a href="http://www.whompcomic.com/2015/01/04/prepare-yourself-for-2-extra-whomp-comics-in-february/">
              Prepare yourself for 2 extra Whomp! comics in February!
             </a>
            </h2>
            <span class="post-author">
             by
             <a href="http://www.whompcomic.com/author/admin/">
              Ronnie
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="posted-on">
             on
            </span>
            <span class="post-date">
             January 4, 2015
            </span>
            <span class="posted-at">
             at
            </span>
            <span class="post-time">
             11:36 am
            </span>
            <div class="post-cat">
             Posted In:
             <a href="http://www.whompcomic.com/category/uncategorized/" rel="category tag">
              Uncategorized
             </a>
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="clear">
          </div>
          <div class="entry">
           <p>
            The
            <a href="http://www.patreon.com/whomp">
             Patreon
            </a>
            has reached $600 monthly!
           </p>
           <p>
            Things that happen on Feb 1st:
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Release an extra comic that will appear on the Patreon page. I will link it from this blog post and my twitter. (Along with the PSD files you can download and do whatever you want with!)
            </li>
            <li>
             Release a video of the process of making the first comic while walking you through the process. (Also, special thanks to everyone on the $5 tier and above on the video!)
            </li>
            <li>
             All $5 patrons will get the eBooks of all three currently existing Whomp! volumes. Suitable for reading comics on mobile without website bloat~
            </li>
            <li>
             Contact patrons for their photos or other source material for the caricatures!
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            Things that happen later:
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Release an extra comic on Feb 7th in the same place with the same bonuses as the first one!
            </li>
            <li>
             Start giving people who were $40 patrons their caricatures!
            </li>
            <li>
             Hold two drawing streams a week where patrons of the $10 tier can join and chat! (I will set up a schedule some time this month)
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            I dont even have to mention the $100 tier because we are already in touch, and that is a cool person!
           </p>
           <p>
            What I would like to do:
            <br/>
            I want to think of more ideas that will make the $40 tier more exciting for repeat patrons. Not everyone wants a drawing every month, so Id like to think of something else I can do. Many people suggest putting patrons in the background of comics, but I feel like modifying the comic at all because of a differing source of income can indirectly change the work itself, and I want it to remain as pure as possible for everyone.
           </p>
           <p>
            Im also hesitant to add any more I will draw things rewards, mainly because I draw very slowly, and if we reach the higher tiers, I will already be drawing during most waking hours.
           </p>
           <p>
            If you have any ideas, feel free to send them at my twitter
            <a href="https://twitter.com/RonnieFilyaw">
             @RonnieFilyaw
            </a>
            , but PLEASE dont feel bad if I reject them.  I am super duper picky and annoying, but I absolutely love you for your effort, no matter what! So if theres anything in particular youve ever wanted, maybe let me know!
           </p>
