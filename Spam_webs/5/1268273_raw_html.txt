          <p>
           Have you ever wondered if anything in your house could cause you to die early? Most people have this particular substance in the home that can cause an early death.
          </p>

           <center>
            <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

          <p>
           Milk is one of the only beverages that are still being pushed on children as a healthy food when it is the exact opposite. Drinking pasteurized milk is not as good for general health or bones as the dairy industry has made it out to be. A lot of independent scientists and researchers have just about had enough of the propaganda.
          </p>
          <p>
           A large-scale study of thousands of Swedish people, cows milk has a deteriorating effect on health when being consumed long term. Research was published and they tracked 61,433 women who were aged from 39-74 over 20 years, and 45,339 men of similar age for 11 years. They found that the more cows milk that people drink, the more likely they were to die or experience a bone fracture during the study.
          </p>
          <p>
           Women who drank 3 or more glasses of milk a day had just about doubled the chance of dying during the study period as those who reported drinking only one glass. A glass of milk is defined as a 200 milliliter serving that has a 16 percent high chance of getting one fracture.
