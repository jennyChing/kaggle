Today we found some great articles on tips to become a better listener, ways to eat less sugar, and goals for a more organized life.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h2&gt;Top 5 Articles&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.gretchenrubin.com/happiness_project/2015/01/5-tips-for-becoming-a-better-listener/"&gt;5 Tips for Becoming a Better Listener&lt;/a&gt; &mdash; Ask open-ended questions and make &quot;listening noises&quot; to encourage the other person to elaborate. [Gretchen Rubin]&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://parentingsquad.com/new-years-resolution-eat-less-sugar-and-9-ways-to-get-it-done"&gt;New Year's Resolution &mdash; Eat Less Sugar and 9 Ways to Get It Done&lt;/a&gt; &mdash; You can have better control of your sugar intake by buying unsweetened foods and adding natural sweeteners and flavors to taste. [Parenting Squad]&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.popsugar.com/smart-living/New-Year-Resolutions-Organization-36367042"&gt;17 Goals For a More Organized Life in 2015&lt;/a&gt; &mdash; Want to be more organized? Section off your drawers with plastic dividers &mdash; or simply cut up cardboard boxes and pop them in! [PopSugar Smart Living]&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.shebudgets.com/diy-projects/10-steps-creating-easiest-outdoor-herb-garden/52784"&gt;10 Steps for Creating the Easiest Outdoor Herb Garden&lt;/a&gt; &mdash; There's no need to start from scratch. Buy already-grown plants so you can see what you're cultivating and know if it's a project you want to keep up with. [She Budgets]&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;In case you missed it:&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;a href="http://www.wisebread.com/10-supposedly-healthy-things-that-are-actually-hurting-you"&gt;10 Supposedly Healthy Things That Are Actually Hurting You&lt;/a&gt; &mdash; Be careful of the vitamin levels in your system; overdosing on vitamin supplements can wreak havoc on your body. [Wise Bread]&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h2&gt;Other Essential Reading&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://acultivatednest.com/2015/01/5-apps-to-organize-receipts-free/"&gt;5 Great Free Apps For Organizing Your Receipts&lt;/a&gt; &mdash; Take a picture of your receipt with your smartphone, send it to your email account, and an app like OneReceipt or Itemize will automatically extract the data and itemize it for you. [A Cultivated Nest]&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://moneysoldiers.com/2015/01/15/top-5-resume-mistakes-avoid-interviewing-job"&gt;Top 5 Resume Mistakes to Avoid when Interviewing for a Job&lt;/a&gt; &mdash; If you say that you know a foreign language, be sure to also include your level of fluency. [Money Soldiers]&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://moneypantry.com/make-money-with-pinterest/"&gt;How to Make Money with Pinterest&lt;/a&gt; &mdash; Services like ShopSense will pay you for driving traffic to certain retailers. [Money Pantry]&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.onesmartdollar.com/4-ways-to-use-the-cloud-to-save-money/"&gt;4 Ways to Use the Cloud to Save Money&lt;/a&gt; &mdash; Make your important documents easily accessible, wherever you are, by storing them onto a secure cloud service. [One Smart Dollar]&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.listenmoneymatters.com/goals-versus-dreams/"&gt;Goals Versus Dreams&lt;/a&gt; &mdash; Goals and dreams are not the same thing, but you can set goals (which include a certain level of practicability) to achieve your dreams. [Listen Money Matters]&lt;/p&gt;
