        <article class="et_pb_post post-5298 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-craft category-notices" id="post-5298">
         <h1>
          Beginners Crochet and Knitting Tutorials Poll
         </h1>
         <p class="post-meta">
          by
          <a href="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/author/Violet/" rel="author" title="Posts by Violet">
           Violet
          </a>
          | Jun 3, 2011 |
          <a href="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/category/craft/" rel="category tag">
           Craft
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/category/notices/" rel="category tag">
           Notices
          </a>
          |
          <span class="comments-number">
           <a href="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/2011/06/beginners-crochet-and-knitting-tutorials-poll/#respond">
            <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="5298 http://www.violetlebeaux.com/?p=5298">
             0 comments
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
          <p>
           Ive been promising to do a basic how to knit and crochet tutorial for quite a while now and here I am with the first step.
          </p>
          <p>
           What I want to do is rather than just have the standard how to that you could find anywhere online is that I want to show how to actually make something useful so its more interactive like a big knit-a-long and by the end of the tutorial youve got something nice to show for it. I wanted to make something that you guys would actually like so rather than picking myself I figured we would have a poll and whatever gets the most votes is what we will make for the project ^_^
          </p>
          <p>
           Im not sure whether more people are interested in crochet or knitting so thats the first section of the poll. All of these projects can easily be done with either knitting or crochet so it doesnt matter.
          </p>
          <p>
           These are the projects and their positives and negatives, you can cast your vote below after you read through.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/gallery/Tutorials and Craft/knitting-and-crochet-tutorial/scarfimagefromartstudio-copy.jpg">
            <img alt="Beginners Knitting and Crochet Ideas" class="ZenphotoPress_thumb aligncenter" src="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/gallery/albums/Tutorials%20and%20Craft/knitting-and-crochet-tutorial/scarfimagefromartstudio-copy.jpg" title="Beginners Knitting and Crochet Ideas"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           #1. Striped scarf with pockets on the end.
           <br/>
           Most beginners start with a scarf, I started making scarves for my dolls when I was 3 in England. This one is super easy but still has cute elements to make it interesting. It can be as long as you have patients for and there are pockets at the ends for your hands. Depending on the yarn and needle size you use you can make it suitable for most seasons. The downside to this one is that it does take a very long time to make, this isnt a quick project if you are a beginner! While its an impressive end project its very tempting to not bother to finish a project like this when you get half way and realize how long youve been doing it!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/gallery/Tutorials and Craft/knitting-and-crochet-tutorial/untitled-1-copy.jpg">
            <img alt="Beginners Knitting and Crochet Ideas" class="ZenphotoPress_thumb aligncenter" src="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/gallery/albums/Tutorials%20and%20Craft/knitting-and-crochet-tutorial/untitled-1-copy.jpg" title="Beginners Knitting and Crochet Ideas"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           #2. Heart face washer.
           <br/>
           I did a brief tutorial on this one before but it wasnt very detailed. This is very quick to make and doesnt take much yarn so its cheaper too. The end product is cute too because you can use it as a wall decoration to remind you of your first project ever!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/gallery/Tutorials and Craft/knitting-and-crochet-tutorial/untitled-2-copy.jpg">
            <img alt="Beginners Knitting and Crochet Ideas" class="ZenphotoPress_thumb aligncenter" src="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/gallery/albums/Tutorials%20and%20Craft/knitting-and-crochet-tutorial/untitled-2-copy.jpg" title="Beginners Knitting and Crochet Ideas"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           #3. Small basket
           <br/>
           Again on the smaller and quicker side of projects so you wont want to quit half way. This is a very little basket which can be used to hold hair accessories or trinkets. Not much yarn needed here either.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/gallery/Tutorials and Craft/knitting-and-crochet-tutorial/untitled-3-copy.jpg">
            <img alt="Beginners Knitting and Crochet Ideas" class="ZenphotoPress_thumb aligncenter" src="http://www.violetlebeaux.com/gallery/albums/Tutorials%20and%20Craft/knitting-and-crochet-tutorial/untitled-3-copy.jpg" title="Beginners Knitting and Crochet Ideas"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           #4. Bunny Toy
           <br/>
           Lastly we have the bunny toy which would make a really cute present for a friend or just a present for you! While it doesnt have much actual use its still adorable and a good keepsake for remembering learning how to knit. Again this one doesnt take up much yarn so is quick and cheap to knit.
          </p>
          <p>
           All of the projects have the same skill level (they are all basically just knitting squares or rectangles) so just pick the one you like best!
          </p>
