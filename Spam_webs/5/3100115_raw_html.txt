             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6999296425715915748">
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
               <b>
                <i>
                 Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies are the perfect cookie to satisfy your sweet tooth and still get in your daily fiber and fruit requirements...well, sort of. LOL
                </i>
               </b>
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-dv6-nel6Lho/VMK3gtchn9I/AAAAAAAAZhQ/7umUKkn3JF4/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatmeal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies are the perfect cookie to satisfy your sweet tooth and still get in your daily fiber and fruit requirements...well, sort of. LOL" border="0" height="426" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-dv6-nel6Lho/VMK3gtchn9I/AAAAAAAAZhQ/7umUKkn3JF4/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatmeal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B1.jpg" title="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <!-- Ziplist Button code -->
              <script id="wk_script" src="http://www.ziplist.com/javascripts/wk.js" type="text/javascript">
              </script>
              <a class="ziplist-button add-recipe small" href="http://www.ziplist.com/webkitchen/button/add_recipe?as_partner=kudoskitchenbyrenee" target="_blank">
               <img src="http://asset1.ziplist.com/wk/add_recipe-small.png"/>
              </a>

              <br/>
              Yippee! It's finally the weekend, so it's time to take a break from all that healthy eating and be a little decadent, don't you think? Well, maybe don't go totally crazy because you'd hate to undo all your past week's healthy choices (and good on you if you've been making them). But just enough to take a break and indulge a little in something sweet and chocolaty.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <a name="more">
              </a>
              That's where these Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies make their entrance. They're sweet and chocolaty and yet they have heart-healthy oatmeal baked in, along with chopped dried cherries so you can be mindful of your daily fruit intake. Yep, that's a stretch in the "healthy department", but like I said it's the weekend...lets PARTY!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ny5lfFWXLb4/VMK9BopJH3I/AAAAAAAAZhg/nRnsiloVEhI/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatmeal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B6.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies are the perfect cookie to satisfy your sweet tooth and still get in your daily fiber and fruit requirements...well, sort of. LOL" border="0" height="640" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ny5lfFWXLb4/VMK9BopJH3I/AAAAAAAAZhg/nRnsiloVEhI/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatmeal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B6.jpg" title="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies" width="426"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              A few days after I'd made my cookies I saw that King Arthur Flour had named
              <a href="http://www.kingarthurflour.com/recipes/chocolate-chip-oatmeal-cookies-recipe" target="_blank">
               their recipe
              </a>
              for Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies the best recipe of 2015. While I certainly agree that theirs is a wonderful cookie, I'm pretty sure that these cookies can (and will) gently nudge their plain chocolate chip oatmeal cookie into second place.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-0RlbGkHt5UQ/VMK_oMyx3PI/AAAAAAAAZhw/KF2gmEi26Xw/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatmeal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B5.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies are the perfect cookie to satisfy your sweet tooth and still get in your daily fiber and fruit requirements...well, sort of. LOL" border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-0RlbGkHt5UQ/VMK_oMyx3PI/AAAAAAAAZhw/KF2gmEi26Xw/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatmeal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B5.jpg" title="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              If you're a regular reader here then you know that I bring food to my ailing father-in-law on a pretty regular basis. He's now 94 years old and always has (since I've known him) had a huge sweet tooth. Not that long ago one of his nurses had told us that as we age, our taste buds change and our desire for sweet foods intensifies. Which is why you'll notice elderly people in the grocery store, more times than not, will have their carts filled with sweets.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              I find this information fascinating, and very true since I now find myself paying attention to what the senior citizens in front of me at the grocery store place on the conveyor belt. Now that you know that, I dare you not to take notice too. LOL
              <br/>
              <br/>
              The reason I tell you this is to let you know that when we brought these cookies over to my father-in-law's house, I hear that almost all of the 4 dozen we brought to him were gone in a matter of 2 days flat. Guess I need to get busy baking more, huh?
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-fXrL6KcC9_U/VMK_ed8n_LI/AAAAAAAAZho/o2gkPTHgq0g/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatmeal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies are the perfect cookie to satisfy your sweet tooth and still get in your daily fiber and fruit requirements...well, sort of. LOL" border="0" height="426" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-fXrL6KcC9_U/VMK_ed8n_LI/AAAAAAAAZho/o2gkPTHgq0g/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatmeal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B2.jpg" title="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <u style="font-weight: bold;">
               CHOCOLATE CHERRY OATMEAL COOKIES
              </u>
              <b>
               <u>
                <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/kudoskitchenbyrenee/chocolate-cherry-oatmeal-cookies?tmpl=%2Fsystem%2Fapp%2Ftemplates%2Fprint%2F&amp;showPrintDialog=1" target="_blank">
                 {PRINT THIS RECIPE}
                </a>
               </u>
              </b>
              <br/>
              <b>
               <u>
                Yield:
               </u>
              </b>
              5 dozen cookies
              <u style="font-weight: bold;">
               Prep Time:
              </u>
              10 minutes
              <b>
               <u>
                Bake Time:
               </u>
              </b>
              15-17 minutes
              <br/>
              <br/>
              1 cup butter, softened (I use salted butter)
              <br/>
              1 cup brown sugar
              <br/>
              1 cup granulated sugar
              <br/>
              2 large eggs
              <br/>
              1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
              <br/>
              1  cups all purpose flour
              <br/>
              1 teaspoon salt
              <br/>
              1 teaspoon baking soda
              <br/>
              1 cup chopped dried cherries
              <br/>
              1 cup milk chocolate chips
              <br/>
              3 cups quick cooking oats
              <br/>
              <br/>
              -Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
              <br/>
              -In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugars together thoroughly.
              <br/>
              -Blend in the eggs and vanilla.
              <br/>
              -In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt and baking soda.
              <br/>
              -Add the flour mixture into the butter/sugar mixture in thirds, beating between each flour addition.
              <br/>
              -Stir in the dried cherries, chocolate chips and oatmeal until completely combined.
              <br/>
              -Drop the dough by tablespoons onto parchment lined baking sheets (12-15 per sheet).
              <br/>
              -Bake in a preheated oven for 15-17 minutes, making sure to rotate the cookie sheets half way through the baking.
              <br/>
              -Remove and cool completely.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ofBquxIE5V8/VMLKYJsqraI/AAAAAAAAZiA/w-xd9XuN85U/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatemal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B4.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies are the perfect cookie to satisfy your sweet tooth and still get in your daily fiber and fruit requirements...well, sort of. LOL" border="0" height="426" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-ofBquxIE5V8/VMLKYJsqraI/AAAAAAAAZiA/w-xd9XuN85U/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatemal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B4.jpg" title="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ievWq_VqkP8/VMLSe7n4W7I/AAAAAAAAZik/DadoCIWmiFQ/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatmeal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img alt="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies are the perfect cookie to satisfy your sweet tooth and still get in your daily fiber and fruit requirements...well, sort of. LOL" border="0" height="640" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ievWq_VqkP8/VMLSe7n4W7I/AAAAAAAAZik/DadoCIWmiFQ/s1600/chocolate%2Bcherry%2Boatmeal%2B-%2Bfinish%2B-%2B3.jpg" title="Chocolate Cherry Oatmeal Cookies" width="426"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              I don't know anyone who could refuse a cookie (or two). And, since we're indulging how about a nice big mug of milk or hot chocolate to go along with them?
