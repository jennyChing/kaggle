      <h3>
       ArcheAge Leveling Guide: Remote Classes with Vitalism for Beginners
      </h3>
      <div style="margin-left:-5px">
       <span class="st_sharethis_hcount" displaytext="ShareThis">
       </span>
       <span class="st_googleplus_hcount" displaytext="Google +">
       </span>
       <span class="st_facebook_hcount" displaytext="Facebook">
       </span>
       <span class="st_twitter_hcount" displaytext="Tweet">
       </span>
       <span class="st_reddit_hcount" displaytext="Reddit">
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="cf" style="margin:10px 0;">
       <span class="fl tag">
        <b>
         Tag
        </b>
        <em>
         <a href="/tag-archeage-class-guide">
          <b>
           ArcheAge Class Guide
          </b>
         </a>
        </em>
       </span>
       <span class="fr">
        09/01/2014
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="news_font">
       <p>
        <strong>
         <font color="#008000">
          Vitalism
         </font>
        </strong>
        can improve your viability while enhancing combat effectiveness with other skillsin ArcheAge online game, especially in archeage dungeon, it is one of the essential class with vitalism skill. Now, today
        <u>
         recommended
         <font color="#800000">
          3 remote classes with vitalism
         </font>
         skill for beginner leveling fast and easy.
        </u>
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <font color="#008000">
          Ranger
         </font>
        </strong>
        (
        <strong>
         <font color="#800000">
          Vitalism + Archery + Shadowplay
         </font>
        </strong>
        )
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
        Ranger has a high remote attack for single target, but it is not good for group enemies. High attack of archery with escapes of shadowplay and stealth skills, make this class very wretched.
       </p>
       <p>
        Both Archery and Shadowplay have bow based attacks so they are frequently used together. However, you could have a bow based build that uses only archery or only Shadow Play. The second option may not be too feasible, but nonetheless it is possible.
       </p>
       <p>
        Archery revolves around the bow. The vast majority of skills are bow attacks or passives to improve your bow. What's left over is Crowd Control or mobility enhancing.
       </p>
       <p>
        ShadowPlay is on the other hand most associated with the typical rogue class and is a hybrid between three fields: Ranged attacks (Bow), Melee Attacks and Utility. Most notably the skill trees utility revolves around stealth, mobility &amp; Aggro reduction.
       </p>
       <p>
        With Vitalism, can
        <strong>
         <font color="#800000">
          self-buffs, self-heals, group-heals
         </font>
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Combo skill, Toxic Shot + Piercing Shot, piercing shot can inflict additional + 27% damage over time on Poisoned targets, so you must cast toxic shot before piercing shot skill.
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <font color="#008000">
          Shaman
         </font>
        </strong>
        (
        <strong>
         <font color="#800000">
          Witchcraft + Vitalism + Sorcery
         </font>
        </strong>
        )
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
        Witchcraft with a strong ability to control, Sorcery with a remote attack, so Shaman class is very popular in dungeon, especially slay the boss.
       </p>
       <p>
        Solo for low level enemy, Antithesis + Freezing Arrow + Flamebolt + Arc Lightning. Once the enemy nearby to you, use the Insidious Whisper or Lassitude, then continue use above skills.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="ArcheAge Shaman Build for PVE" height="306" src="http://www.sellarcheagegold.com/images/news/ArcheAge%20Shaman%20Build.jpg" width="614"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <font color="#008000">
          Cultist
         </font>
        </strong>
        (
        <strong>
         <font color="#800000">
          Vitalism + Sorcery + Occultism
         </font>
        </strong>
        )
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Leveling guide
        </strong>
        <br/>
        Before level 5, using Antithesis + Bow + Melee, can use its range to attack the target within 25 meter.
       </p>
       <p>
        Level 5, using Antithesis + Mirror Light, mirror light inflicts snare enemies for 3 seconds, if still can not kill them, please cast again Antithesis skill.
       </p>
       <p>
        Level 10, Antithesis + Freezing Arrow + Mirror Light + Flamebolt, try not to be besieged by enemies when you solo.
       </p>
       <p>
        Insulating Lens of Sorcery, is very nice skill, increases your Physical Defense + absorbing damage, you are invincible to deal the normal enemy with the effect of Insulating Lens, so you must cast the insulating lens before kill the enemy.
        <u>
         <font color="#800000">
          Now, this class has been nerfed in 1.2, and become useless.
         </font>
        </u>
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <font color="#008000">
          Equipment and Weapon
         </font>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <strong>
        <font color="#008000">
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
        </font>
       </strong>
       Above 3 classes are rely on the remote to kill enemy, basically, the physical attack of enemy can kill before close to you, so these class also need the Defense to deal remote attack enemy, recommended the
       <strong>
        <font color="#800000">
         light armor
        </font>
       </strong>
       , will has a buff effect when you wear light armors for all equipment.
       <u>
        Weapon, can use one-hand weapon + shield
       </u>
       , as a remote class, it is not much need you melee attack, and the
       <u>
        shield can make up for the lack of Physical Defense.
       </u>
       <u>
       </u>
       <p>
        The Shield is your reliable partner for all time, of course.
        <br/>
         Bonuses available:
        <strong>
         <font color="#800000">
          force, stamina, intelligence
         </font>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <font color="#008000">
          1H Swords
         </font>
        </strong>
        <br/>
         Attack speed: 1.6
        <br/>
         Bonuses available: force, stamina, intelligence
        <br/>
         Damage type: slashing
        <br/>
         Notes:
        <br/>
        - Slashing damage is more effective against targets that use light armor, however, cause less damage to any other type of equipment.
        <br/>
        - When receiving damage there is a chance to parry next attack
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <font color="#008000">
          1H Club
         </font>
        </strong>
        <br/>
         Attack speed: 1.7
        <br/>
         Bonuses available: force, stamina, intelligence + Healing Power
        <br/>
         Damage type: blunt
        <br/>
         Notes:
        <br/>
        Blunt damage is most effective against targets that use heavy armor, I believe this is the best option to invest, after all, covers most of its high-priority targets.
        <br/>
        When inflicting damage there is chance to stun enemy on 1,5 sec.
       </p>
       <p>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         <font color="#008000">
          1H Staff
         </font>
        </strong>
        <br/>
         Attack speed: 1.8
        <br/>
         Bonuses available: intelligence, spirit
        <br/>
         Damage type: blunt
        <br/>
         Notes:
        <br/>
        It has the same kind of damage that the club, however, gives magical attack. Compared with the previous options, staves cause less damage.
        <br/>
        When inflicting magic damage there is chance to heal yourself on certain amount of HP.
       </p>
