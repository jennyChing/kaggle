        </a>
       </h3>
       <h2 class="date-header">
        by Kristen Philipkoski
       </h2>
       <div class="post-body entry-content">
        <p>
         <em>
          Welcome to Bloggers in Denim, where we highlight the best denim looks on our favorite bloggers.
          <br/>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://eatsleepdenim.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/walk-in-wonderland-3.jpg">

         </a>

         <a href="http://www.walkinwonderland.com/2015/07/western-denim-dress.html" target="_blank">
          Walk in Wonderland
         </a>
         , where blogger Sheryl Luke shows how its done.
        </p>

          denim dress
         </a>

        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://eatsleepdenim.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/walk-in-wonderland-2.jpg">
          <img alt="walk in wonderland 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16191" height="993" src="http://eatsleepdenim.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/walk-in-wonderland-2.jpg" width="662"/>

          <img alt="walk in wonderland" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16192" height="993" src="http://eatsleepdenim.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/07/walk-in-wonderland.jpg" width="662"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out
         <a href="http://www.walkinwonderland.com/2015/07/chicago-day-3.html">
          Walk in Wonderland
         </a>
         for more of Lukes great looks!
        </p>

          jeans

      <div class="post-16109 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-uncategorized tag-crop-tops tag-denim-overalls tag-denim-stories tag-favorite-jeans tag-overalls" id="post-16109">
       <h3>
        <a href="http://eatsleepdenim.com/blog/uncategorized/what-are-your-favorite-jeans-denim-overalls-from-1995/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to What Are Your Favorite Jeans? Denim Overalls From 1995">
         What Are Your Favorite Jeans? Denim Overalls From 1995
        </a>
       </h3>
       <h2 class="date-header">
        by Kristen Philipkoski
       </h2>
       <div class="post-body entry-content">
        <p>
         <em>
          Theres always a story behind your favorite jeans, and this is where we celebratethe history of our denim best friends.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="MomJeans-009" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16110" height="800" src="http://eatsleepdenim.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/MomJeans-009.jpg" width="532"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Photos by
         <a href="http://www.delriophotography.com/" target="_blank">
          Maria del Rio
         </a>
         for
         <a href="http://www.mothermag.com/mom-jeans-san-francisco/" target="_blank">
          Mother
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I thought I would kick off this series with my own favorites: these
         <a href="http://www.shopbop.com/actions/viewSearchResultsAction.action?searchButton=Submit&amp;query=denim+overalls&amp;searchSuggestion=false" target="_blank">
          denim overalls
         </a>
         . I bought them at a vintage store circa 1995 (that would be TWENTY years ago but whos counting). If memory serves it was Aardvarks on Haight Street, which sadly closed several years ago. They were certainly dirt cheap, because I had zero dollars to my name at the time. I was working in a coffee shop, which is not only why I was poor, but also why these denim overalls hold a special place in my memory.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="MomJeans-010" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16112" height="426" src="http://eatsleepdenim.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/MomJeans-010.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Much like today,
         <a href="http://www.shopbop.com/clothing-tops-crop/br/v=1/32075.htm" target="_blank">
          crop tops
         </a>
         were all the rage in 1995, and I enthusiastically participated in the trend by wearing mine underneath my overalls. This is where the boy comes in. He was a maniac and a clown. No, for real he was a clownwell he became one eventually. At the time he was more of a stand-up comedian with a Robin Williams vibe. Like, really crazy energy, andI was into it! And he was really into that little flash of skin at my waist between where the crop top left off and my overalls began. And there began a six-year romance that like so many other romances ended in sorrow and my inability to enjoy Cirque du Soleil. But thats a story for another time. Or probably never because it really sucked. I used to reallyenjoy trapeze art.
        </p>
        <p>
         But to this day, every time I lay eyes on these overalls I think of the comedianand coffee. The smell of coffee permeated the fabric of anything I wore, grounds got in my hair and under my fingernailsthere was no escape. Coffee +the comedian=a very fun combination for a while! And what do you know,the jeans outlasted all of it.
        </p>
        <p>
         What are your favorite pair of jeans? Send your stories to eatsleepdenim at gmail dot com!
         <a href="http://eatsleepdenim.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/MomJeans-010.jpg">
          <br/>
         </a>
        </p>
