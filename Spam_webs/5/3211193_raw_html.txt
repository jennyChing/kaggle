         <h1 class="entry-title">
          Florida Man Gets Drunk: Tries To Drive It Off
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div class="meta">
          <span class="meta_posted_by">
           Posted by:
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a href="http://sportsmasher.com/author/sportsmasher/" rel="author">
            SportSmasher
           </a>
          </span>
          ,
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-03-04T09:44:16+00:00">
           March 4, 2014
          </time>
         </div>
         <p>
          <a href="http://sportsmasher.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/20140304-093624.jpg">
           <img alt="20140304-093624.jpg" class="size-full aligncenter" src="http://sportsmasher.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/20140304-093624.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://blogs.tcpalm.com/off_the_beat_will_greenlee_blog/2014/03/michael-moore-said-his-wife.html">
           TCPalm
          </a>
          <em>
           Michael Moore said his wife told him hed been drinking to excess. So, Moore told Stuart police, he decided to drive it off. Moore made the statement around 12:35 a.m. Feb. 13 after an officer noticed his sport utility vehicle appeared to be speeding on U.S. 1, according to recently released records. Police stopped Moore, 61, in the 800 block of Southwest U.S. 1 and reported he smelled of booze, appeared to have glassy eyes and slurred his speech. He told one officer he was coming from home and had a couple of drinks. He said he was en route to a bar for a few more. Moore told another officer he got in an argument with his wife and left for a drive. He also told me that his wife told him that he (had) been drinking too much so he decided to go out and drive it off,' an arrest affidavit states. Some who overindulge in alcohol sleep it off, or go to sleep until sober. Law enforcement officials frown on those who drive after copious quaffing. Moore was given field sobriety exercises, and a breath test measured his blood alcohol content at 0.104 and 0.103  greater than the 0.08 limit. Moore, of the 8100 block of Southeast Peppercorn Court in Hobe Sound, was arrested on a DUI charge.
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          I love this drunk driving excuse by Michael Moore, who drank too much and got kicked out of his house by his wife, so he decided to drive it off. The phrase is, of course sleep it off and the last thing that anyone who is drinking to excess should ever do is obviously get behind the wheel of a car. We have seen several great excuses out of Florida, including last week, when a man was busted for cocaine
          <a href="http://sportsmasher.com/2014/02/27/florida-man-arrested-thought-cocaine-was-legal-in-florida/">
           thought cocaine was legal in Florida.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           This concludes another chapter of Fucking Florida: Tales From Americas Most Fucked Up State for more, click the Fucking Florida tag on this page.
          </em>
         </p>
