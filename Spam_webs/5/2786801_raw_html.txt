                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Vending Machines in China Now Sells Live Crabs
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-8630832539852289808" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <header class="post-header" style="background-color: white; color: #494949; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 22px; position: relative;">
                      <h1 class="post-title entry-title" style="font-family: Raleway, sans-serif; font-size: 45px; font-weight: 400; line-height: 54px; margin: 0px 0px 30px;">
                       <img alt="china vending machine" class="attachment-single-thumb wp-post-image" height="350" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/china-vending-machine-600x350.jpg" style="font-family: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; height: 350px; line-height: 22px; max-width: 100%; width: 798px;" width="600"/>
                      </h1>
                     </header>
                     <div class="post-content" style="background-color: white; color: #494949; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 22px; margin: 0px 0px 30px; overflow: hidden; width: 798px;">
                      <div class="post-content-right" style="float: left; font-size: 15px; line-height: 27px; margin: 0px; width: 608px;">
                       <div style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
                        <a href="http://cdn1.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/In-China-A-Vending-Machine-That-Dispenses-Live-Crabs-3.jpg" rel="fancybox" style="-webkit-transition: all 0.2s ease-out; color: #a81010; transition: all 0.2s ease-out;">
                         <img alt="In China, A Vending Machine That Dispenses Live Crabs-3" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1476" height="610" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/In-China-A-Vending-Machine-That-Dispenses-Live-Crabs-3-610x610.jpg" style="border: none; clear: both; display: block; height: auto; margin: 10px auto; max-width: 100%;" width="610"/>
                        </a>
                        Mr Liu, a businessperson in China, has invented a vending machine that sells live crabs.
                       </div>
                       <div style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
                        The vending machine has been installed in numerous parts of Hangzhou, the east of Zhejiang province of China. The price of these crabs has been set from US$3-US$9. Ginger and vinegar are also dispensed by this machine for those who like to have condiments.
                       </div>
                       <div style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
                        <a href="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/In-China-A-Vending-Machine-That-Dispenses-Live-Crabs-2.jpg" rel="fancybox" style="-webkit-transition: all 0.2s ease-out; color: #a81010; transition: all 0.2s ease-out;">
                         <img alt="In China, A Vending Machine That Dispenses Live Crabs-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1475" height="400" src="http://cdn.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/In-China-A-Vending-Machine-That-Dispenses-Live-Crabs-2.jpg" style="border: none; clear: both; display: block; height: auto; margin: 10px auto; max-width: 100%;" width="309"/>
                        </a>
                        The moisture and temperature in these vending machines has been attuned in order to keep the seafood fresh. But once the crabs have been bought, they melt down and can move as well.
                       </div>
                       <div style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
                        <a href="http://cdn1.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/In-China-A-Vending-Machine-That-Dispenses-Live-Crabs-1.jpg" rel="fancybox" style="-webkit-transition: all 0.2s ease-out; color: #a81010; transition: all 0.2s ease-out;">
                         <img alt="In China, A Vending Machine That Dispenses Live Crabs-1" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1474" height="820" src="http://cdn1.wonderfulengineering.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/In-China-A-Vending-Machine-That-Dispenses-Live-Crabs-1-610x820.jpg" style="border: none; clear: both; display: block; height: auto; margin: 10px auto; max-width: 100%;" width="610"/>
                        </a>
                        During Chinas Mid-Autumn Festival, Mr Liu is planning to cash in due to the call for crabs and expects to publicize these vending machines at supermarkets and subway stations across the country.
                       </div>
                      </div>
