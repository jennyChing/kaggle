        <h1 class="cat_article_title">
         Experience music on mobile
        </h1>
        <div class="article_meta">
         <span class="meta_author">
          Posted by:
          <a href="http://www.mindcron.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by MindCron">
           MindCron
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="meta_date">
          Posted date:
          <strong>
           December 23, 2013
          </strong>
         </span>
         <span class="meta_cat">
          In:
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.mindcron.com/category/corporate-story/" rel="category tag">
            Corporate
           </a>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="single_article_content" id="article_content">
         <p>
          <span style="color: #333399; font-size: 14px;">
           The chartbuster application brings along slick new features defining a new landscape for experiencing music on mobile.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           Gaana.com launches Version 3.0 apps for an all new listening buzz !
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mindcron.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Gaana-App.jpg">
           <img alt="Gaana App" class="alignright wp-image-2301" height="512" src="http://www.mindcron.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Gaana-App.jpg" width="279"/>
          </a>
          <span style="color: #333399;">
           <strong>
            Gaana.com, Indias No. 1 online music streaming service
           </strong>
          </span>
          has launched Gaana 3.0 apps for Android and iOS to take its listeners on a high musical note. The updated version of the app comes loaded with lots of new features to provide users an unparalleled music listening experience.
         </p>
         <p>
          The app with a refreshing new look is packaged with a
          <span style="color: #333399;">
           faster search engine
          </span>
          , which makes it easier to browse, discover and favorite music from a large collection of over 3 Million songs. The available music catalogue is spread across Bollywood, International and Regional spanning 18 languages and multiple genres. The new app boasts thousands of editorially curated playlists, professionally programmed radio streams and a host of social music discovery &amp; personalization features.
         </p>
         <p>
          Enhanced features include
          <span style="color: #333399;">
           curated streams, social music discovery and personalization
          </span>
          , which position Gaana app as the one stop destination for users with varying music tastes and preferences. The new Discover Section in the app showcases cherry picked playlists to suit every mood, occasion and genre, be it romance or dance, happy or sad, party or devotional.
         </p>
         <p>
          The app offers listeners an opportunity to tune in to a variety of
          <span style="color: #333399;">
           pre-programmed radio streams
          </span>
          (Radio Mirchi streams &amp; Gaana Radios), featuring popular Radio Jockeys bytes, Celebrity favorites and loads of curated music. The main attraction being 
          <span style="color: #333399;">
           <em>
            One Touch Radio
           </em>
          </span>
           which plays continuous music based on a users music taste. The recommendation improves with increased listens by a user on the service. Deep integration with users social network enables the users to not only discover new music through friends but to form a stronger bond with friends with similar music tastes.
         </p>
         <p>
          Users can download the songs within the app for offline play and save on data costs along with a high quality ad  free music experience, all at an even lower price of USD1.99 per month in India.
         </p>
         <p>
          Speaking on the occasion, Pawan Agarwal, Business Head, Gaana said 
          <em>
           <span style="color: #333399;">
            We are totally focused on evolving an incredible mobile experience for music. The new apps, packed with, Professional curation, social discovery and personalization take us a step ahead in delivering a world-class, engaging, and easy-to-use service that caters to the varying music needs of our audience across the globe.
           </span>
          </em>
          
         </p>
         <p>
          The music service currently boasts 7.5million monthly active users, and is available on the web, iOS, Android, Blackberry, Windows and J2ME platforms.
         </p>
         <p>
          To download the app simply sms 
          <span style="color: #333399;">
           <em>
            <strong>
             Gaana
            </strong>
           </em>
          </span>
           to
          <span style="color: #333399;">
           <em>
            <strong>
             58888
            </strong>
           </em>
          </span>
          or visit the app store.
         </p>
