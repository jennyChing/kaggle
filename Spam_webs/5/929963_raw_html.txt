        <div id="mainBodyArea" itemprop="reviewBody">
         <div class="firstPar">
          <p>
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/cars/bmw/" target="_blank" title="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/cars/bmw/">
             BMW
            </a>
           </strong>
           s product range is growing faster the geraniums at the end of my garden. Before you know it, the bloody things will have taken over completely.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="secondPar">
          <p>
           Dont get me wrong; I like geraniums as much as the next mildly indifferent gardener, but unless somebody goes out and chops them back once every other week theres a very real risk theyll swamp the little crocuses and bluebells that bring vibrancy, colour and variety to our tiny patch of Surrey. Thank goodness then, for my father-in-law who seems only to happy to wield the shears against the crafty cranesbills. Which makes this analogy not only tortuous but ironic too, because I bet hed love a 4-series Gran Coup.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="thirdPar">
          <p>
           Oh yes, the
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/cars/" target="_blank" title="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/cars/">
             car
            </a>
           </strong>
           . Really very good. It arrives in a period of unprecedented expansion for the Munich-based car maker. Indeed, there are so many new model lines sprouting that BMW is having to launch them to the worlds press two at a time (hence yesterday's
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/motoring/car-manufacturers/bmw/10858754/BMW-X4-review.html" target="_blank">
             review of the new X4
            </a>
           </strong>
           ). One PR told me, with a look of terror in their eyes, that there are 15 new BMWs due this year alone. It makes Ford look like a boutique manufacturer.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fourthPar">
          <p>
           <img caption="" declared-caption="" height="306" name="filename.ext" photographer="Chris Knapman" refid="2924464" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/02924/filename_ext_2924464c.jpg" version="c" width="460"/>
           <br/>
           <sub>
            The 4-series Gran Coupe is a smaller version of the 6-series of the same name
           </sub>
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fifthPar">
          <p>
           Such is the obscurity of reasoning behind many of these new cars, the origins of the 4-series Gran Coup make, if nothing else, for a pretty amusing explanation. It might go something like this: New car, Barry?
          </p>
         </div>

         <div class="body">
          <p>
           Indeed.
          </p>
          <p>
           BMW, I see. What one did you go for?
          </p>
          <p>
           A 4-series Gran Coupe.
          </p>
          <p>
           And whats one of those when its at home?
          </p>
          <p>
           Well obviously its a five-door version of the 4-series two door coup and convertible that are themselves two door versions of a four-door 3-series saloon and five-door Touring and GT, not to be confused with the new four-door M3 and two-door M4, although its almost certainly a prelude to a five-door M4 Gran Coup.
          </p>
          <p>
           I see, Barry. Well, as long as youre happy.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <img caption="" declared-caption="" height="306" name="filename.ext" photographer="Chris Knapman" refid="2924465" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/02924/filename_ext_2924465c.jpg" version="c" width="460"/>
           <br/>
           <sub>
            The 4-series Gran Coupe shares its dash layout with the 4-series coupe
           </sub>
          </p>
          <p>
           For now, the engine range is identical to that of the 4-series coupe, with four-cylinder petrol and diesel models, along with a couple of six-cylinder flagships. But machine such as this are catnip to fleet buyers and so the bulk will be fitted with the ubiquitous 2.0-litre diesel - which inevitably meant the only model available to test at launch was the petrol-powered 428i.
          </p>
          <p>
           This light yet powerful turbocharged engine is doing the rounds in everything from the 5-series executive saloon to the Z4 sports car and, aside from a slightly anodyne soundtrack for a purportedly sporty engine, its a fine thing. With enough mid-range torque from the turbocharger to make it feel game, plus a reasonably strong top end, it makes for a swift car (0-62mph is dispatched in 6sec), especially if you splash out on BMWs sublime eight-speed automatic gearbox.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img caption="" declared-caption="" height="306" name="filename.ext" photographer="Chris Knapman" refid="2924466" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/02924/filename_ext_2924466c.jpg" version="c" width="460"/>
           <br/>
           <sub>
            The boot of the 4-series Gran Coupe is the same size as that of the 3-series
           </sub>
          </p>
          <p>
           Being based on the same wheelbase as the 4-series coup (the only real difference is that the roofline has been extended, plus the two frameless rear doors added), it also steers and handles beautifully, with a fine balance and a playful nature. There are few other cars that can go quite so convincingly from refined motorway cruiser to grab-it-by-the-scruff-of-the-neck sports car. And if the rides a touch busy, even with 550s worth of Variable Damper Control fitted, its by no means a disaster.
          </p>
          <p>
           Of course, you could say all of this about any 3-series-based model. And while the 4-series is slightly more sporting in its setup, its pretty marginal. Equally, although more expensive than a 3-series, the 4-series is also better equipped, with even entry-level SE cars getting trinkets such as an automatic tailgate, dual-zone climate control and heated seats as standard. Dont get me wrong, youll still spend a fortune on options (including four-wheel drive if you want it), but hey, youre buying a BMW, what did you expect?
          </p>
          <p>
           <img caption="" declared-caption="" height="306" name="filename.ext" photographer="Chris Knapman" refid="2924467" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/02924/filename_ext_2924467c.jpg" version="c" width="460"/>
           <br/>
           <sub>
            Rear-seat accomodation is improved compared with the coupe, but headroom is still a bit tight
           </sub>
          </p>
          <p>
           Which means the choice comes down to your preferred bodystyle, and the caveat that if you go for this version its not quite as easy to get into the back as the 3-series saloon, nor is there quite as much headroom once youre there. The boot capacity is identical though (as well as 35 litres larger than the 4-series coup), as well as more useful on account of the hatchback opening.
          </p>
          <p>
           Personally, I think this Gran Coup a rather handsome beast, as well as different enough to seem that bit more exclusive. In terms of being all things to all men  green-fingered father-in-laws included  its possibly the best BMW of the lot.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            THE FACTS
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           BMW 428i M-sport
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Tested:
           </strong>
           1,997cc four-cylinder turbocharged petrol engine, 8-spd automatic transmission, rear-wheel drive
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Price/on sale:
           </strong>
           Range from 29,420/June 21
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Power/torque:
           </strong>
           245hp @ 5,000rpm/280lb ft @ 1,250rpm
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Top speed:
           </strong>
           155mph
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Acceleration:
           </strong>
           0-62mph in 6.0sec
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Fuel economy:
           </strong>
           42.8mpg (EU Combined)
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            CO2 emissions:
           </strong>
           147g/km
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            VED band:
           </strong>
           F (145)
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Verdict:
           </strong>
           Youll want a diesel engine, but otherwise this is a surprisingly desirable if utterly confusing edition to the 4-series line-up with great handling and a usefully more spacious cabin than the equivalent coup
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Telegraph rating:
           </strong>
           Four out of five stars
          </p>

        var readNextWidget = (function(){

            var settings = {
                articleImage: "",
                articleTitle: "",
                articleUrl: "#",
                articleId: "",
                currentArticleId: "",
                articleList: [],
                articleListToSkip: [],
                readNextCookieName: "tmg_read_next",
                openButton: "#readNextOpen a",
                closeButton: "#closeBtn a",
                articleContainer: "#readNextArticleContainer",
                widgetContainer: "#readNextWidget",
                displayThumbnail: false,
                panelOpen: false,
                autoOpen: true
            };

            var renderTemplate = function() {
                var template = '&lt;div id="readNextWidget" style="display:none"&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="readNextOpen"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div id="readNextOpenInner"&gt;&lt;a href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;span&gt;Next Story&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span class="icn"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +

                        '&lt;div id="nextArticle"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticleWrapper"&gt;'+
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticleInnerWrapper"&gt;'+
                        '&lt;p id="readNextLabel"&gt;MOST VIEWED IN NEWS&lt;/p&gt;';
                        
				if(settings.articleImage &amp;&amp; settings.articleImage!="null" &amp;&amp; settings.displayThumbnail){
					template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageThumbnail" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;img src="'+ settings.articleImage +'"/&gt;&lt;/a&gt;';
				}
                        
				template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageTitle" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;span&gt;' + settings.articleTitle + '&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="closeBtn"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;a href="#"&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;iframe style="border:none;position:absolute;top:0;left:0; height:100%;width:100%;z-index:-1;" id="iefix" src="about:blank"&gt;&lt;/iframe&gt;' + //fix for IE bug
                        '&lt;/div&gt;';
                return template;
            };


            var init = function(currentArticleId, articleList, displayThumbnail) {

                settings.articleList = articleList;
                settings.currentArticleId = currentArticleId;
                settings.displayThumbnail = displayThumbnail;

                settings.articleListToSkip = unescape($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName)).split(',');

                if ($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName) != null) {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    updateCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                } else {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    createCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                }


                if (settings.articleId != "") {

                    $(renderTemplate()).appendTo("body");

                    $("#nextArticleWrapper a, #readNextOpenInner a").click(function () {
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_headline', 'version_1']);

                        getNavPos($(this).attr("href"), "wayfarerReadNext");
                    });

                    bindUIActions();

                    var displayed = false;

                    setTimeout(function () {
                        if (!displayed) {
                            displayed == true;
                            readNextWidget.display();
                        }

                    }, 10000);

                    $(window).scroll(function () {
                        if (isScrolledIntoView("#tmg-related-links")) {
                            if (!displayed) {
                                displayed == true;
                                readNextWidget.display();
                            }
                        }

                        if(isScrolledIntoView("#shareBottom") || isScrolledIntoView("#newGigyaShareBottom")) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }

                        if($(window).scrollTop() + $(window).height() == $(document).height()) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }
                    });
                }
            };

            var display = function () {
                $(settings.widgetContainer).fadeIn(2000, function () {
                    //animation complete
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_closed', 'version_1']);
                });
            };

            var bindUIActions = function () {
                $(settings.openButton).mouseenter(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'hover_closed', 'version_1']);
                    openArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.widgetContainer).mouseleave(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    closeArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.closeButton).click(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_close', 'version_1']);
                    updateCookie(settings.articleId, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    closeArticleContainer();
                    settings.autoOpen = false;
                });
            };

            var openArticleContainer = function () {
                if (!settings.panelOpen) {
                	$("#readNextArticleContainer").show();//show/hide because height is calculated incorrectly for elements with display:none
                    $("#readNextArticleContainer").hide();
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_open', 'version_1']);
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                    settings.autoOpen = false; //set auto open to false once user has viewed this once
                }
                return false;
            };

            var closeArticleContainer = function () {
                if (settings.panelOpen) {
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                }
                return false;
            };


            var isScrolledIntoView = function(elem) {
                if($(elem).length) { //check if element exists
                    var docViewTop = $(window).scrollTop();
                    var docViewBottom = docViewTop + $(window).height();

                    var elemTop = $(elem).offset().top;
                    var elemBottom = elemTop + $(elem).height();

                    return ((elemBottom &lt;= docViewBottom) &amp;&amp; (elemTop &gt;= docViewTop));
                } else {
                    return false;
                }
            };

            var unique = function(array) {
                return array.filter(function (el, index, arr) {
                    return index == arr.indexOf(el);
                });
            }

            var createCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        articleListToSkip[0] = currentArticleId;
                        $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        updateCookie(currentArticleId, articleListToSkip);
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookie = function(articleIdToSkip, articleListToSkip) {
                articleListToSkip.push(articleIdToSkip);
                articleListToSkip = unique(articleListToSkip);
                $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
            }

            var chooseNextArticle = function (currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if ((article.id != currentArticleId) &amp;&amp; ($.inArray(article.id, articleListToSkip) === -1)) {

                        //set article
                        settings.articleTitle = article.title;
                        settings.articleUrl = article.url;
                        settings.articleId = article.id;
						settings.articleImage = article.imgUrl;
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            //public API
            return {
                settings: settings,
                init: init,
                display: display,
                openArticleContainer: openArticleContainer,
                closeArticleContainer: closeArticleContainer
            }
        })();

    };
