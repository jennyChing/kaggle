          </a>
         </p>

           tissue paper

          ,

           scissors

          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=ohhada-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B001685PEQ" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>

           paint

          ,
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B007C7XPVA/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B007C7XPVA&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=ohhada-20&amp;linkId=L5MCXP2N2CUQCJLL">
           paintbrush
          </a>
          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=ohhada-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B007C7XPVA" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000UJ0Z10/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000UJ0Z10&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=ohhada-20&amp;linkId=HPABFGY7A2MMKUVW">
           bamboo skewers
          </a>
          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=ohhada-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B000UJ0Z10" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Step 1: Stick the foam ball on the skewer, paint it and let dry.
          <br/>
          Step 2: Cut a 3 x  piece of tissue paper, fold in half lengthwise, and cut fringe into the strips. Set aside.
          <br/>
          Step 3: Fold a cupcake liner in half, then fold in half again to find the midpoint. Open liner, dab fringe strips with a small amount of glue, and attach to the midpoint.
          <br/>
          Step 4: Gently twist the liner into a hat shape and secure it with hot glue. Repeat 5 more times, to create 6 hats.
          <br/>
          Step 5: With hot glue, attach the hats to each side of the Styrofoam ball.
          <br/>
          Step 6: Cut a 4 x  strip of tissue paper. Fold in half lengthwise, and fringe the short side.With hot glue, attach fringed strip around base of hat, where it attaches tothe Styrofoam ball. Repeat for each hat.
         </p>
         <p>
          Additional materials needed for Mini Piata Mobile:
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004KYSGAI/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B004KYSGAI&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=ohhada-20&amp;linkId=ZIAN2YAWWQAY6VDB">
           10 embroidery hoop
          </a>
          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=ohhada-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B004KYSGAI" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000YQLVN0/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000YQLVN0&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=ohhada-20&amp;linkId=BWSGDEP43EZVS6A6">
           yarn
          </a>
          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=ohhada-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B000YQLVN0" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Step 1: Create 3 mini piatas using the instructions above.
          <br/>
          Step 2: Attach piatas to hoop with yarn.
          <br/>
          Step 3: Attach yarn to top of hoop for hanging.
          <br/>
          Step 4: Hang well out of reach of baby or toddler hands!
         </p>
