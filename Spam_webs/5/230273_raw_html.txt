       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Organic produce delivery in Amsterdam
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.groent.org/blog/food/organic-produce-delivery-in-amsterdam/" rel="bookmark" title="21:14">
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2013-04-03T21:14:51+00:00">
           April 3, 2013
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.groent.org/blog/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by admin">
            admin
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         As you can see on my blog there is a lot of organic, local fruit an vegetables for sale in Amsterdam. But it does take time and inspiration to go to the farmers market or an organic supermarket and think up a recipe. If you like to be surprised you might like to try a subscription to a weekly crate / box / bag full of fresh vegetables or fruit or even more.
        </p>
        <p>
         I have gathered the suppliers of these delivery systems in Amsterdam. You find the links in this post, on the
         <a href="http://www.groent.org/blog/links/" target="_blank">
          links page
         </a>
         and Ive made a
         <a href="https://twitter.com/groent_org/organic-crates-amsterdam" target="_blank">
          twitter list
         </a>
         you can follow to help you choose. Please let me know if I missed a supplier!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.ruudmaaz.nl/" target="_blank">
          Ruud Maaz
         </a>
         <br/>
         This online supermarket offers all organic grocerys, preferably made by local producers. It also offers a weekly crate that contains fruits, vegetables, meat, fish, cheese, bread, juice etc. Price range 34,14  75,00.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.dekrat.nl/ " target="_blank">
          De krat
         </a>
         <br/>
         De krat offers a weekly or two-weekly crate of organic fruits and vegetables, meat, bread and a snack. Vegetarian crate is optional. Price range 26,00  72,00.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.odin.nl/ " target="_blank">
          Odin
         </a>
         <br/>
         Odin offers bags with organic vegetables or fruits or both. You pick up the bag at Estafette or other organic stores. Price range 8,75  15,75.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.proef.nu/proef-vitatas/ " target="_blank">
          Vitatas
         </a>
         <br/>
         Proef sells these subscription bags full of vegetables, fruits or only Dutch vegetables (and sometimes fruits). You pick them up at Ekoplaza or organic shops. Price range 7,50  12,00.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.hellofresh.nl/ " target="_blank">
          Freshbox
         </a>
         <br/>
         Hello Fresh sells the freshbox with fruit, or everything you need for 3 or 5 meals for 1, 2, 3 or 4 people. The box for vegetarians is for 2 people 3 meals and costs 39,00. Most products are organic. They deliver at home on Monday evening. Price range 14,00  89,00.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.thegreenbee.nl/beebox/" target="_blank">
          Beebox
         </a>
         <br/>
         The Beebox contains fruit, vegetables and bread for 2 or 4 persons, including recipes for minimum 3 meals. Thats the only vegetarian option. The Family box adds juice, eggs and meat, the Box for Cooks adds cheese and a special product. Products come (generally?) from farms in the centre of the country and most are organically produced. Price range 25,00  60,00.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.streekbox.nl/" target="_blank">
          De streekbox
         </a>
         <br/>
         De streekbox sells boxes including meat (1-2 or 3+ persons) or vegetarian (1-2 or 4 persons). They include all ingredients for 2 meals and one or more side dishes or lunches. Almost completely organic and the produce comes from farms in het Groene Hart. Delivery at home in Amsterdam Friday evening or Saturday morning.
         <br/>
         Price range: 32,50  47,50.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.ekonoom.nl/" target="_blank">
          EKOnoom
         </a>
         <br/>
         EKOnoom delivers to Amsterdam by PostNL delivery only (4,95). That why they have to add coolers to the package and you never know when it will arrive. They run a full organic webstore, so you can order anything you need. The weekly packages contain fruits and / or vegetables. The smallest is for 1 person, 3 or 4 days. The familypackage for 4 persons, 3 or 4 days.
         <br/>
         Price range: 10,25  18,50.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.beemsterbezorgservice.nl/" target="_blank">
          Beemster bezorgservice
         </a>
         <br/>
         I cant tell you much about what the Beemster Bezorgservice has on offer. Their website just says that if youre interested you can send an e-mail to info@beemsterbezorgservice.nl and theyll send you a list to order from. They do deliver in Amsterdam. On their
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/Beemster-Bezorgservice/205770936163465" target="_blank">
          Facebook page
         </a>
         they publish very pretty pictures of the organic produce from the Beemster area.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.hofwebwinkel.nl" target="_blank">
          Hofwebwinkel
         </a>
         <br/>
         De Warmonderhof in Dronten is an ecological farm and educational institute. You can order from their web shop online and have it delivered at home (5,-) or pick it up at one of their pick-up points (1,50) every Saturday. They dont have a subscription system, you have to choose yourself!
        </p>
        <p>
         Update January 2014, because last year a new box was introduced:
         <br/>
         <a href="https://www.foodwelove.nl/" target="_blank">
          FoodWeLove
         </a>
         <br/>
         This box contains only delicacies with an interesting story behind the product. It is delivered monthly, a subscription costs 24,95 per month. A proefbox is 27,50. There is no vegetarian option and no remarks in the FAQ, so I guess youll have to wait and see what the selection brings. On the website you find the contents of the boxes of last year.
        </p>
       </div>
