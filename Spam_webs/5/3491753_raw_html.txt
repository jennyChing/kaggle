                  <span>
                   Monday, 10 March 2014
                  </span>
                 </h2>
                 <div class="date-posts">
                  <div class="post-outer">
                   <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     field sales agents
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6146225461012685493" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="p1">
                      Multi-Link Group is a distributor of clean cookstoves.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p2">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      We are looking for
                      <span class="s1">
                       <b>
                        field sales agents
                       </b>
                      </span>
                      in Kisumu, Eldoret, Laikipia and Nairobi.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p3">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      Field Sales Agents will travel regularly to host community sales events and develop relationships with our customers.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p3">
                     </div>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="p1">
                      Reporting to the Regional Sales Manager, the Field Sales Agent will be responsible for leading and executing direct group sales events and managing product delivery and customer service to our customers.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      The successful sales agent will directly represent Multi-link brand to its customers.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p3">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      As such, candidates must demonstrate strong interpersonal skills and an aptitude for working with low-income customers, with an emphasis on women consumers.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p3">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      <b>
                       Knowledge, Skills and Experience Required
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <ul class="ul1">
                      <li class="li1">
                       Excellent customer service and interpersonal skills
                      </li>
                      <li class="li1">
                       Excellent communication skills
                      </li>
                      <li class="li1">
                       Self driven and confident
                      </li>
                      <li class="li1">
                       A team player
                      </li>
                      <li class="li1">
                       Drives for results and is action-oriented
                      </li>
                      <li class="li1">
                       Demonstrated ability to plan, organize, and effectively manage time while paying attention to the details
                      </li>
                      <li class="li1">
                       Computer skills (email and MS office)
                      </li>
                     </ul>
                     <div class="p1">
                      Applications should be submitted by email to hr@multilink-kenya.com with a CV and cover email stating your name, relevant past experiences, languages spoken and preferred work location by 14th March 2014.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p3">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p1">
                      Only selected candidates will be contacted.
                     </div>
                     <div class="p4">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p4">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="p4">
                      <br/>
