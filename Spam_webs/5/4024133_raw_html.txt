       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Appliances We Cant Live Without.?
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://www.onlineworld-glan.com/shopping-blog-51/" rel="bookmark" title="1:23 pm">
         <span class="entry-date">
          February 6, 2014
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.onlineworld-glan.com/author/admin/" title="View all posts by George Maliakal">
          George Maliakal
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25">
        </span>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignleft wp-image-1995" height="112" src="http://www.onlineworld-glan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/SB_post_51_9.jpg" width="163"/>
         Some appliances, which were considered as luxury items till the recent past are now become the most essential items for a home. From cooking or keeping foods from spoiling, to helping us complete laundry tasks, we cannot be without some of these appliances. And all of them certainly make your living more comfortable.
        </p>
        <p>
         But appliances require an investment, which can be considerable if you want the best or the most convenient. All these appliances are now available with new technology, new features in different price range. And you can choose the right appliance that suits your home needs and fits within your budget. Now lets have a look at these appliances.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <img alt="" class="wp-image-1996 alignright" height="104" src="http://www.onlineworld-glan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/SB_post_51_1.jpg" width="142"/>
          Refrigerator:
         </b>
         Refrigerator, once considered as one of the luxury item has now become an essential appliance and a part of the modern home. A refrigerator is a long-term investment and youll want to carefully consider various refrigerator styles and features to ensure that it meets your familys needs now and for the next few years. Whether youre buying a refrigerator for the very first time or replacing an older refrigerator model, some tips will certainly help you to shop for better a refrigerator for your home.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <img alt="" class="wp-image-1997 alignright" height="163" src="http://www.onlineworld-glan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/SB_post_51_2.jpg" width="122"/>
          Washing Machine:
         </b>
         Now, well take a look at the next essential appliance, the washing machine. As long as people have been wearing clothes, theyve had to come up with ways to clean them. The biggest benefit of a washing machine is the convenience of being able to do your washing at home. You dont have to spend two hours a day scrubbing dirty clothes, straining your back and ruining your washing machine, you can do some other activities.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <img alt="" class="wp-image-1998 alignright" height="96" src="http://www.onlineworld-glan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/SB_post_51_3.jpg" width="153"/>
          Microwave Oven:
         </b>
         With the introduction of the domestic microwave oven, the notion of meals in minutes has become a reality. The average microwave can cook meat six times faster than a conventional oven. Microwave ovens use a magnetron tube that generates high-energy, short radio waves that agitate the water molecules in food, cooking it faster and more evenly. And the multi-heating system is designed to cook healthier, tastier, gourmet-standard food in a microwave oven by letting food retain its natural flavors and goodness. That means you spend less time in the kitchen. Microwave oven has become a blessing for the families, especially for the working couples.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <img alt="" class="wp-image-1999 alignright" height="134" src="http://www.onlineworld-glan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/SB_post_51_4.jpg" width="114"/>
          Dish washer:
         </b>
         Dishwasher is yet another essential appliance that has proved to be very helpful. Dishwasher saves a lot of your time and effort. Youll be surprised to know how much better newer models can clean your dishes. Many new dishwashers come with innovative features such as third racks, adjustable racks, sanitizing options, and other improvements that have increased their functionality. When looking for a new dishwasher, you should choose one with the right combination of features, whether you need just the basics or a whole array of cycles and options.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <img alt="" class="wp-image-2000 alignright" height="134" src="http://www.onlineworld-glan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/SB_post_51_5.jpg" width="134"/>
          Television:
         </b>
         Music clearly speaks to something deep in the human consciousness, and wherever we go, we take our music with us. Imagine a world in which there was no way to record sound. The only music would be live, created for the moment and then gone forever. And now, if you can add some pictures to the sound  that brings us another essential appliance, Television. It brings news and entertainment world to you home. New television technologies are vying with one another for supremacy, and the potential for the viewer to access and control the viewing experience is almost unlimited.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <img alt="" class="wp-image-2001 alignright" height="87" src="http://www.onlineworld-glan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/SB_post_51_6.jpg" width="137"/>
          Air Conditioner:
         </b>
         In the middle of a summer heat wave, its great to have air conditioning. Before air conditioner was invented, it was a challenge to stay comfortable in hot weather. A nice, cold drink was helpful, and some homes were designed to make the most of a light breeze. Even a nice, big tree for shade was great. But now the technology has advanced so much that today we have different types of air conditioners available for home and for commercial use, making our living more comfortable both during extreme cold or hot climate.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <img alt="" class="wp-image-2002 alignright" height="111" src="http://www.onlineworld-glan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/SB_post_51_7.jpg" width="126"/>
          Phone:
         </b>
         Communication has become part n parcel of our lives. And the cellphones has taken the world by surprise. Today, cell phones are quickly replacing landline telephones. Mobile/ cellphone culture has grown so much that almost everyone in a family owns one to communicate with other family members, friends and for official communication. Today the market is flooded with numerous models with new technology and with a wide price range.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          <img alt="" class="wp-image-2003 alignright" height="89" src="http://www.onlineworld-glan.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/SB_post_51_8.jpg" width="131"/>
          Computer:
         </b>
         Although fairly new, compared to some other appliances, computer has become incredibly popular in our daily life both at home and at work. And many major technological developments and the mobile phone revolution have further made it easy for the people to access computers. Online shopping, which is becoming the call of the day, has further attracted more n more people to the internet, social networking sites and for doing their online shopping. Thus Computers has become another most essential appliance you cant live without.
        </p>
