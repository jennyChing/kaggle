         Is Your Password Really Protecting You?
        </h1>

          <p>
           Technology is the cornerstone of the modernized world, and these days, our entire lives are protected by passwords. We use these super-secret codes multiple times a day to access our private information, but how can we be sure that our passwords are secure? Well, it turns out that the majority of tech users arent aware of what makes a password strong, and therefore many people are setting themselves up to be targeted by hackers.
          </p>
          <p>
           Hackers operate in various ways, but theyre all motivated by personal gain, and the telltale aspect of personal gain is money  your money. Whether theyre targeting your bank accounts, email addresses, or ATM pin numbers, the first step in their shady process is to crack your passwords. Cyber criminals are becoming more and more sophisticated, and hacking software can crack the average password in a matter of minutes.
          </p>
          <p>
           So, the ultimate goal when creating a password is to make sure its strong enough to foil even the most experienced hacker. Check out the infographic below to get the lowdown on passwords, as well as some tips and tricks you can use to make sure your passwords are doing their job, and really protecting you.
          </p>
