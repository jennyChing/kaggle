      <p>
       Movement across the North American Craton is obvious today.
      </p>
      <p>
       Beginning with a 6.0M earthquake occurring at the very far Northwest tip of the edge of the craton, in Alaska,
       <a href="http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/359560/Malaspina-Glacier">
        at the Malaspina Glacier
       </a>
       ice / rock flows.
      </p>
      <p>
       The large earthquake was followed by dozens of mid 3.0M events.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://i2.wp.com/dutchsinse.tatoott1009.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/alaska-glacier-earthquakes-july-17-2014.jpg">
        <img alt="alaska glacier earthquakes july 17 2014" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11716" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/dutchsinse.tatoott1009.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/alaska-glacier-earthquakes-july-17-2014.jpg?w=670"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/eventpage/ak11328120#summary">
        http://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/eventpage/ak11328120#summary
       </a>
      </p>
      <div class="event-times">
       <h3>
        Event Time
       </h3>
       <ol>
        <li>
         2014-07-17 11:49:37 UTC
        </li>
        <li>
         2014-07-17 04:49:37 UTC-07:00
         <abbr title="Time where earthquake occured">
          at epicenter
         </abbr>
        </li>
        <li>
         2014-07-17 06:49:37 UTC-05:00
         <abbr title="Timezone your computer is configured to use">
          system time
         </abbr>
        </li>
       </ol>
      </div>
      <div class="event-location">
       <h3>
        Location
       </h3>
       <p>
        60.217N 140.550W depth=14.8km (9.2mi)
       </p>
      </div>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       Here is a diagram of the North American Craton for comparison of the activity vs. the location of the plate edge:
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://i1.wp.com/dutchsinse.tatoott1009.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/300px-North_america_craton_nps.gif">
        <img alt="300px-North_america_craton_nps" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11718" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/dutchsinse.tatoott1009.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/300px-North_america_craton_nps.gif?resize=300%2C351"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       In addition to the earthquake in Alaska, there were also several mid-3.0 magnitude earthquakes which occurred further South along the edge of the craton, at Yellowstone supervolcano, Kansas / Oklahoma fracking injection operation, and at the active Southern California
       <a href="https://www.google.com/search?num=50&amp;client=firefox-a&amp;hs=TLd&amp;rls=org.mozilla%3Aen-US%3Aofficial&amp;channel=sb&amp;biw=1920&amp;bih=920&amp;q=salton+sea+volcanic+buttes&amp;oq=salton+sea+volcanic+buttes&amp;gs_l=serp.3...1695.3226.0.3511.9.9.0.0.0.0.103.759.8j1.9.0....0...1c.1.49.serp..3.6.498.JAAFb_As5-Q">
        Salton Sea volcanic buttes
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://i2.wp.com/dutchsinse.tatoott1009.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/salton-sea-volcano-july-17-2014.jpg">
        <img alt="salton sea volcano july 17 2014" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11720" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/dutchsinse.tatoott1009.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/salton-sea-volcano-july-17-2014.jpg?resize=670%2C344"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <hr/>
      <p>
       A newly active volcano in Southern California? Yes.
      </p>
      <p>
       <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="270" src="//www.dailymotion.com/embed/video/x1k6wad" width="480">
       </iframe>
