       <h2>
        K.W.Doggett
       </h2>
       <div class="row row-pt content">
        <div class="col-md-6">
         <p class="feature-text">
          K.W.Doggett Fine Paper offer fine quality paper stocks to high-end print and design clients Australia wide. For their Buffalo brand, they wanted a promotional piece to work as both a demonstration and an advertisement for this fine food-grade board.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          To emphasise the boards food-based application, we originally developed a series of five recipe cards all based on traditional buffalo mozzarella as a subtle reinforcement of the brand.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="col-md-6">
         <p class="p1">
          The recipe cards were then put together in a stylish folder with edgy, somewhat irreverent copy, crafted to appeal to K.W.Doggett designer clients. To complete the visual feast, wedirected a photo-shoot of mouth-watering food in collaboration with one of our favourite Italian restaurants, Ladro.
         </p>
         <p class="p1">
          Recently the promotion extended to include two differentrecipe cards that showcase the stock through clean illustration and clever print processes. Its fair to say that the result resonated with designers and printers, and became an item worth keeping, as both a paper sample and a welcome addition to recipe collections.
         </p>
