         <div id="description-text">
          Purple Stuffed Cat Doll Plush.
          <br/>
          In my lifetime I have had various cats, in different moments and circumstances, each one has a story and a particular place in my heart. With this Cat Doll I decided to make a very special blend of all those furry friends in just one, creating each one with different colors and combinations of cotton fabrics to represent the different personalities and memories I have of them. I called this design The NY Calico Cat because I designed it here in NYC and calico because it's made of different patches and patterns to make each one a one of a kind, like any calico out there, completely unique. Once you adopt one of them you can name him or her with any name you wish and to keep forever close to your heart.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Description:
          <br/>
          Each stuffed cat is made of cotton designer fabrics, machine sewn and with a bit of a rustic style to look more unique. The eyes are handmade of glass cabochon, and the scarf is hand crocheted.
          <br/>
          16" tall from ears to toes and 6" wide head from ear to ear. Hand stuffed with hypoallergenic fill and hand stitched between the torso and the legs. From the sketch board to the the selection of each piece of fabric, each one of them take time and dedication from start to finish, adding to it the element of "made with love" quiet literally and quality control. No piece leaves my shop without the proper inspection.
          <br/>
          Before I cut the fabrics, I tumble dry them for a few minutes with bounce sheets to take away the new fabric smell (which I really don't like) and after cutting, ironing, sewing and airing there is no smell anymore.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          This toy's filling  is 100%polyester silky fiber, completely non-Allergenic, handwashable and maintains resiliency.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Care:
          <br/>
          I recommend hand wash the stuffed cat in cold water or spot clean with a soft detergent, (no Bleach) I also don't recomend you put the plushie  in the washer machine or the dryer. Better air dry. The more gently you care for it, the more it will last for years 
          <br/>
          <br/>
          SAFETY: Dolls and animal toys with buttons or glass cabochon eyes are not suitable for little babies or children under 3 years. Upon request I will replace them with soft wool felt hand stitched eyes. You can buy this particular one and send me a note at checkout to let me know if you want to change the glass eyes for felt ones.(price won't go up nor down).
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Extras at no cost:
          <br/>
          Let me know if you are sending your order directly to someone else as a gift and if you want a personal note included in the package.
          <br/>
          All of my items come from a non-smoking cat friendly environment (extremely clean space)
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Have fun selecting and buying your favorite one. Any questions please let me know.
          <br/>
          Peace and Love
