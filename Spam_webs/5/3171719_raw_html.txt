       <h2>
        Well share a modern home Casa Blanca in Lima, Peru which is designed by Dulanto Sangalli. Its Awe-inspiring bright orange stairs can be seen from the outside and staircase are the most eye-catching part of the house.

       <p>
        This beautiful modernhomeCasa Blanca is located in Lima, Peru. This house is really beautiful, attractive and striking. The most eye-catching thing is its bright orange staircase that can be seen outside from the home. It gives fabulous look to see it. The house has three different levels, the basement, first floor and the terrace. Architecture Martin Dulanto Sangalli designed this modern, outstanding and stunning home with intrepid structural piece which is perceptible from the outside.
