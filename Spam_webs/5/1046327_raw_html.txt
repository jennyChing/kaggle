             <p>
              Our team has experience in designing wearable technology products. We have used our design experience and consumer insights to develop a product that is aesthetically elegant, remarkably light and works seamlessly to provide all the features in a simple and natural manner.
             </p>
             <p>
              Glance has been designed to match the curvature of your wrist, and comes in three sizes to fit small, medium or large wrists. Glance has been worn by many people from concept to final design, and each prototype has been repeatedly improved to ensure a comfortable fit.
             </p>
             <p>
              The display shows you text messages and notifications as they come through your smartphone. We have partnered with speed reading technology company Spritz that enables stable text reading at upto 450 words per minute. Spritz's patent-pending technology is already being used by thousands of developers world-wide, and Glance is bringing the same technology to your wrist.
             </p>
             <p>
              A single full colour LED lets you be notified when an email, text message or group message comes in. Three white LEDs show you how many messages have cued up for your email, phone calls or instant messages.
             </p>
             <p>
              Use your hand as a pointer on your screen to control your mouse. Make a simple thumb tap gesture with your fingers to simulate a click when using the air mouse mode.
             </p>
             <p>
              Two tactile buttons to allow you to control the three modes of the device:
             </p>

              <li>
               phone notifications
              </li>
              <li>
               activity tracking
              </li>
              <li>

             <h1>
              Production Updates
             </h1>
             <p>
              We have already completed an initial production run with our local manufacturer, and are fixing the bugs to be in a production ready state. We have gone through a number of design revisions are looking to move to a flexible PCB design such to fit the curvature of your wrist.
             </p>

Here we will move into the larger production run for the first 1000 beta devices. Those of you lucky enough to be part of this group will have the first access to Glance and help perfect the product for those who receive the next group.
             </p>
             <p>

             <h3 class="normal mb4">
              Risks and challenges
             </h3>
             <p>
              In order to tackle the many challenges with bringing a new device to the market we have partnered with one of the leading embedded systems companies in Canada (Rowebots Inc.). We have partnered with a local manufacturer to produce our initial prototypes and helps us with our design review process. The close proximity helps us keep a close feedback loop for reliability and testing.
             </p>
             <p>
              To prepare for a smooth rollout we are staging our delivery of devices, the first 1000 units will have a finalized version of hardware with upgradeable software.
             </p>
             <p>
              We are also working with a Fortune 500 manufacturing company to build and distribute our product at large (greater than 10,000 units) volumes.
             </p>
             <p>
              With your support, we can bring a Glance to be worn worldwide. While we're close to entering production, your contribution will help fund:
             </p>
             <p>
              - Production tooling
              <br/>
              - Large component ordering
              <br/>
              - Production ready real-time operating system
              <br/>
              - Miniaturization of current board to provide a smaller and more reliable form factor
              <br/>
              - Global Bluetooth certification
             </p>
             <p>
              We are focused on developing a great wearable product that provides information you care about. We aim to provide information to let you live a happier, healthier and more productive life. We were founded in 2013, and Glance is our second product.
