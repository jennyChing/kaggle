         <h2 id="@3">
          Interpretation
         </h2>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h3 id="@4">
          Goals
         </h3>
         <p>
          Finding new directions, new goals through immersion. Pisces is linked to the collective subconscious, or at least your own
          <a href="/right_and_left_brain" title="Right And Left Brain">
           subconscious
          </a>
          where everything is processed at once so time and reality take a back seat to intuition and the unknown. The answers at this time do not come through logical analysis, one has to connect with 'the force' and once connected, you will know what needs to be done.
         </p>
         <p>
          We experience the results of our actions and are once again aware that our goals did not take us to the utopian result we had envisioned. We can become inslaved by these detrimental factors or we can find new directions and continue to build toward the future we desire.  There is an urge to escape and sometimes a rest will allow you to let go of the "logic" to see, feel and experience the current reality. This stepping back will allow you to see what needs to be done, what comes next.
         </p>
         <p>
          Reap the rewards, take full advantage of the social opportunities to bargain and trade go with the flow to experience and reap the rewards you can. In capricorn one solidifies the structures, in aquarius there is analysis and logical thinking, and pisces is about experiencing the reality before beginning again. Hard work got you where you are today, the experiencing allows your subconscious to catch up with your logic. You need both working together or the subconscious will derail even your best efforts. Pay attention to both the good and the undesirable, but fight tendencies to wallow in either.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h3 id="@5">
          General Meaning
         </h3>
         <p>
          sensitive, labile, dreamily, passive, unsure, cool, uncertain, dependent, relaxed, self-sacrificing, suggestible, medial, easily unsettled or overwhelmed, romantic, imaginative, flexible, opportunist, intuitive, impossible to categorized, irrational, seductive, placid, secretive, introverted, pleasant, artistic, and charming, indecisive, moody, confused, wavering, lazy, scatterbrained, vulnerable, unpredictable and gullible.
         </p>
         <p>
          In seasons, this is the time to prepare seeds for planting in spring. Fantasy as a source of insight and wisdom. Emotional, intuitive and insecure.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Seeks connections, preferably those which are intangible or unseen.
          </li>
          <li>
           Haunted, haunting by the intangible or unseen.
          </li>
          <li>
           Purging, cleansing.
          </li>
          <li>
           There is no way to know or be secure within the current illusion
          </li>
          <li>
           Highly sensory
          </li>
          <li>
           Release old habits or be stuck, Metamorphosis
          </li>
          <li>
           Pierce illusion
          </li>
          <li>
           Peace makers, artists, those who understand at a spiritual level.
          </li>
          <li>
           Vague, inconsistent, lost or frantic. Thinks in broad strokes, difficulty with the concrete.
          </li>
          <li>
           The least concrete of all the signs, difficult to pin down because of constant fluctuations.
          </li>
          <li>
           I sense.  I love.
          </li>
          <li>
           Instinctively effective or the victim prone to panic attacks.
          </li>
          <li>
           Charming, agreeable, accommodating chameleon like facade can hide someone with a cruel streak beneath.
          </li>
          <li>
           You want to appear selfless with enormous sympathy for all. Difficulty in setting limits can result in coldness or harshness when you attempt it.
          </li>
          <li>
           At worst self-pitying, malleable, without drive or direction, lazy and impractical, unstable, hypochondriac and dependent.
          </li>
         </ul>
