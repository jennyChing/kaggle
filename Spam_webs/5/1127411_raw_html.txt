       </h1>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <h2>

        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignleft" height="200" src="http://www.davidvansant.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/shutterstock_128031956411.jpg" style="border: 5px solid white;" width="300"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         A traumatic brain injury is caused by a force to the head that results in impairment. It can involve the actual fracturing of the skull or other types of trauma, such as violent shaking. Various
         <a href="http://davidvansant.com/Personal-Injury/Types-of-Accidents">
          types of accidents
         </a>
         can cause a traumatic brain injury, including
         <a href="http://davidvansant.com/Personal-Injury/Motorcycle-Accidents">
          motorcycle accidents
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://davidvansant.com/Personal-Injury/Car-Accidents">
          car accidents
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://davidvansant.com/Personal-Injury/Truck-Accidents">
          truck accidents
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://davidvansant.com/Personal-Injury/Types-of-Accidents/Pedestrian-Accidents">
          pedestrian accidents
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://davidvansant.com/Personal-Injury/Premises-Liability/Slip-and-Fall-Accidents">
          falls
         </a>
         . An Alpharettapersonal injury attorney should be consulted immediately if you or a loved one has suffered a traumatic brain injury in an accident caused by another persons
         <a href="http://davidvansant.com/Grounds-for-Personal-Injury-Claims/Negligence">
          negligence
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         You will need competent advice from a qualified attorney regarding your legal options. A lawyer that has experience in these extremely complex cases can review the accident and discuss how best to proceed in filing a
         <a href="http://davidvansant.com/Personal-Injury">
          personal injury
         </a>
         claim. Personal injury
         <a href="http://davidvansant.com/Grounds-for-Personal-Injury-Claims/Financial-Compensation">
          compensation
         </a>
         can provide critical financial resources to help cover injury-related expenses and losses, including home care, pain, suffering and emotional trauma.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          A traumatic brain injury can have many severe and adverse consequences, including causing:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Impairment of motor functions
         </li>
         <li>
          Impairment of speech
         </li>
         <li>
          Emotional imbalance
         </li>
         <li>
          Difficulty sleeping
         </li>
         <li>
          Learning difficulties
         </li>
         <li>
          Problems concentrating
         </li>
         <li>
          Memory problems
         </li>
        </ul>
