        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         TuVous: Ground Floor Independent Fashion Consultant Opportunity
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
           Angie
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <center>
         <i>
          Links on our site may lead to consultants ready to help you start a home business. Looking to advertise your own opportunity? See our Promote Your Business page for more information.
         </i>
        </center>
        <p>
         TuVous is a new direct sales company currently in pre-launch phase. The company, when fully launched, will offer fashion-forward items. I recently held an interview with the TuVous PresidentRyan Daley to find out a little more about their upcoming business opportunity.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <img alt="TuVous Independent Fashion Consultant Opportunity" class="alignright size-full wp-image-2392" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/thebestdirectsalescompanies.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/tuvous_IFC_profile-01.png?resize=333%2C333"/>
          What commission can consultants expect to make?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         A Consultant at a party, show, or through their online sales will be able to make 20-55% retail commissions from the sale of TuVous fashion products. Those who build downline organizations, and help build success will be able to participate in a Compensation Plan which will pay an up to an additional 45% of Sales Volume.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          I see interested parties can already reserve a starter kit. Can you provide us with a little information on what those will include?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Each Starter Kit will include a selection of TuVous inventory at a significant discount (30-55%), as well as other training material. The purchase of a Starter Kit will also waive the $60 enrollment fee, and some of the Kit include free tickets to our Convention.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Will personal URLs be available to reps? Will there be a monthly maintenance cost involved?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Each Consultant will have their own business replicated retail shopping cart. On that cart they will be able to have a selection of inventory that represents the same inventory that they have personally carried. Every time they purchase inventory for their parties, they will also be able to have that same piece reflected on their online retail cart. There will be no additional monthly fees involved in the replicated shopping cart.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Will a company-sponsored hostess program be offered?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         There will be a company supported hostess program, however, that program is not expected to be launched until near the end of the summer of 2014.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          What are the requirements for signing up? (age, location, etc)
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Each Independent Fashion Consultant account needs to have the main applicant be at least 18 years old, and currently in the United States.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          What is your anticipated launch date?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         We are currently in pre-enrollment stage, allowing those who want to be first in TuVous and be the first ones in their area with the Limited Launch Line to sign up and put a deposit on their Start Kit. Those first orders will start to ship in June 2014.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thanks Rick, for the interview!
         <a href="http://www.tuvous.com/">
          You can find out more about TuVous here.
         </a>
        </p>
