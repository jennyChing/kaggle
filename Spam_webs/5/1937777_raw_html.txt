       <div class="title mobile">

       <img alt="Rachel Roy" class="polaroid headshot" src="http://www.blogcdn.com/www.stylelist.com/media/2013/07/rachel-roy-280x280_280x280.png"/>
       <h1 class="name hide-on-mobile">
        Rachel Roy
       </h1>
       <div class="title hide-on-mobile">
       </div>
       <p class="bio">
       </p>
       <p class="desc">
       </p>
       <p>
        It was a honor when StyleList asked me to be a guest editor in July  it's thetime of year to celebrate everything that exists around us, the great outdoors, our best friends, and great food andwine --all (hopefully)done while relaxing under the stars.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        This month Ive shared some of my favorite parts of summer with StyleList  from great finds like Missonis reversible bikini to my fabulous RACHEL Rachel Roy striped espadrilles,my pages are full of must-have beauty products, summer essentials and splurge worthy pieces that youll have for years to come.
       </p>
       <blockquote class="center">
        <p>
         Fashion is more than adornment, its a powerful way to communicate who you are. Hollywood's fascinated with telling stories, which allows for a fashion script writer like myself to paint vivid pictures that are loudly heard across many mediums.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        While summer is a time we can easily get caught up in our own plans, its important to take a minute to think about what we can do for the planet, which of course, lead back to us as we are all connected. It can be as simple as using a FEED bag like the one I created with Lauren Bush Lauren next time youre at the beach or leaving the car in the garage and riding your bike to work  every act of generosity no matter how big or small makes a difference.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Im also excited to share with you a video interview with celebrated fashion writer Melissa Magsaysay who dishes on her career path and shares her tips for the next generation of writers looking to crack the industry.I think it is our responsibility to share what we know and provide insights tothose who want to work on their dreams as well.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        This month, make every day count by living with intent and purpose! Have a beautiful relaxing restful summer that recharges you. I cant wait to hear from you!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        xo Rachel
       </p>
