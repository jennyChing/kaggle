         Through the watch interface, apps will be able to take advantage of heightened user convenience thanks to the accessible wrist-mounted interface and at-a-glance information. The Watch is also equipped with a myriad of sensors which can be utilized in creative ways by apps which pursue the concept of the Quantified Selfgiving customers access to actionable data about themselves and their daily lives.
        </p>
        <p>
         In support of such innovative Apple Watch app ideas, SDI is quickly building up an internal knowledge-base for Apple Watch app development, and adapting their iOS expertise to this new device. This month Apple released a beta, first-phase launch of the WatchKit API, with which SDIs iOS team has begun to work with clients to flesh out their Apple Watch app designs and start programming prototypes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Looking to the future, Apple has announced an early 2015 release for the Watch, which some speculate may come as early as February. This is an incredibly important moment for entrepreneurs who are interested in pursuing Apple Watch app development, as early movers will have the unique opportunity to go to market ahead of the competition.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         When the Watch launches, having an app on the store will provide unique access to a large numbers of Watch early adopters.
        </p>

An early start on development is extremely valuable, as it ensures that there is adequate time for a full design, development, and testing cycle prior to launch.
