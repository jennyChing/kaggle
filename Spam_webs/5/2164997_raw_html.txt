           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            Yesterday and today the Pound has been stronger then ever against the Euro (at least considering the last few years): 1 Pound is around 1.33/1.34 Euro.
           </p>
           <p>
            You might not have realised the sort of impact would that mean on your property purchase in Italy, lets have a look to one example.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" height="467" src="/images/stories/images/Blog/dsc_0006.jpg" width="700"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            If you bought one of our properties, say
            <a href="http://en/property-search/43-tuscan-lajatico-pi/170-ville-degli-olivi-timo" target="_blank">
             Timo
            </a>
            (an image above), a couple of years ago, you would have paid around 504,000 Pounds (plus transaction costs, tax and an FP).
           </p>
           <p>
            If you buy it today the price in pounds (identical price in Euro) would be circa 435,000 Pounds (plus transaction costs, tax and an FP) with a reduction of a massime 69,000 pounds equal to nearly 92,000 Euro.
           </p>
           <p>
            Working out the difference as a percentage of the asking price, we are talking about a nearly 16% reduction!
           </p>
           <p>
            It is an unprecedentedly good time to act, take advantage of this exceptional value for your money now.
           </p>
           <p>
            Feel free to contact me direct to learn more.
           </p>
           <p>
            All the best,
           </p>
           <p>
            Luca Catalano -
            <a href="mailto:l.catalano@realinvest.co.uk">
             l.catalano@realinvest.co.uk
            </a>
           </p>
