       <h2>
        January 31, 2005
       </h2>
       <h3>
        Fug Night
       </h3>
       <p>
        Okay, who let Lil' Kim design a prom dress?
       </p>
       <p>
        <img src="http://img.photobucket.com/albums/v719/gofugyourself/fugnight.jpg"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        This is, apparently, an actual dress, made by a Texas company that has advertised it successfully in teen magazines like
        <em>
         YM
        </em>
        and
        <em>
         Seventeen
        </em>
        . And the model is
        <em>
         not
        </em>
        , apparently, wearing it backwards.
       </p>
       <p>
        Now, I would maybe expect to see something like this on
        <em>
         The O.C.
        </em>
        , just because if any show is likely to have a complete break with fashion reality, that is the one. But ...
        <em>
         really
        </em>
        ? This guy has actually sold some of these. If I had come down the stairs in that thing, my father would have locked me inside the house, burned all my clothes, and replaced them with billowing muumuus --
        <em>
         if
        </em>
        he was able to retain hold of his consciousness.
       </p>
       <p>
        What is
        <em>
         wrong
        </em>
        with people? It's a
        <em>
         school dance
        </em>
        . Do you really want your geometry teacher to know the exact diameter of your breasts? Are you
        <em>
         really
        </em>
        that interested in rendering your English teacher speechless, or perhaps reducing him/her to speaking in tongues? Are you this hell-bent on becoming a stripper?
       </p>
       <p>
        Sweet God. To quote my esteemed colleague Jessica, "I need to lie down."
       </p>
