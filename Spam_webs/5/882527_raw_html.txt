         VH1: I love Dance With Me, it has such a joyous retro clubvibe. Its one of my favorite cuts on the album.
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Me too, thank you! I had that song since the
        <em>
         Greatest Hits
        </em>
        album but I kept it behind. I just felt like itsong was a little too progressive so I was saidwe need to hold it for the next album. I love that song, becauseI just love the whole connection of music. It doesnt matter who you are or where you come fromeverybody relates to music. At the end of the day, everyone dancing in that club hastheir own life going on and their own ups and downs. But it doesnt matter at that point, because youre just having a good time and releasing. Thats why I use music, its release and aform of escapism. So thats why I love that song. Its kind of a fresh sound right now, too.
       </p>
