                  <img alt="Luxury" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hubfs/174483/Luxury.jpg?t=1435180077561"/>
                  <p>
                   Trophy has become the new buzz-word in luxury real estate. According to the latest issue of
                   <a href="http://www.christiesrealestate.com/eng/luxury-defined" target="_blank">
                    <em>
                     Luxury Defined
                    </em>
                   </a>
                   <span>
                    our
                   </span>
                   just-released study on the global prime property marketmore properties than ever before were both sold and listed for sale at US$100 million or higher in 2014. As the number of billionaires globally reached new heights, so too did the price benchmarks for their trophy homes, as the worlds most affluent focused more and more on acquiring collectible residential assets in prized locations across the globe. US$100 million is now firmly established as the
                   <strong>
                    billionaire benchmark
                   </strong>
                   for ultra-prime property.
                  </p>
                  <!--more-->
                  <p>
                   At the more traditional luxury residential real estate price segments, the overall market experienced steady growth in 2014, with US$1 million plus home sales up by an average of 16 percent over 2013 in the 80 luxury residential markets surveyed for the report.After an explosion in urban luxury home sales growth in 2012-2013, the story in 2014 was that
                   <strong>
                    second-home resort markets were red hot
                   </strong>
                   , leading the growth in luxury property sales.'Jet set destinations soared 28 percent over the prior year
                   <strong>
                    ,
                   </strong>
                   fueled by attractive investment opportunities, below market peak prices, and an emerging confidence that the market has bottomed and a recovery is well under way.
                  </p>
                  <div style="padding-left: 30px;">
                   <strong>
                    <a href="/hubfs/market-for-high-end-homes.html" target="_blank">
                     WHAT DEFINES LUXURY AROUND THE WORLD? SEE THE INFOGRAPHIC
                    </a>
                   </strong>
                  </div>
                  <p>
                   <span>
                    As millennials grow up and baby boomers transition into life as empty nesters, these buyers are increasingly seeking urban amenities. This helped fuel growth in high-value urban market sales, particularly in metropolitan cities, which jumped by 15 percent over 2013.
                   </span>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   In the top global economic hub cities,
                   <strong>
                    Toronto
                   </strong>
                   ranked as the worlds hottest luxury market in 2014, and was the only top city to see a faster year-on-year pace with a 37 percent increase in luxury home sales.
                   <strong>
                    London
                   </strong>
                   topped the list of the world's most luxurious cities for prime propertymeasured bytop sales prices, high average pricesper square foot, and number of luxury salesfollowed closely by New York.
                  </p>
                  <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
                   <strong>
                    <a href="http://www.christiesrealestate.com/eng/luxury-defined" target="_blank">
                     DOWNLOAD THE COMPLETE
                     <em>
                      LUXURY DEFINED
                     </em>
                     2015 REPORT
                    </a>
                   </strong>
                  </p>
                  <p>
                   More than ever before, we are focusing on the purchasing patterns of ultra-affluent individuals
                   <span>
                    
                   </span>
                   as well as whats driving their investments across the globe  and why the acquisition of luxury real estate has become increasingly important to their portfolios, notes Dan Conn, CEO, Christies International Real Estate. Whether for safe storage of wealth, lifestyle upgrades or the pure passion of the investment, the intrinsic recognition of the sheer value of prime property ownership could not be stronger among these consumers.
                  </p>
                  <p style="padding-left: 30px;">

                   <a href="http://www.christiesrealestate.com/eng/luxury-defined" target="_blank">
                    <em>
                     Luxury Defined
                    </em>
                   </a>
                   , the third annual edition of the research paper not only examines the worlds top 10 cities for prime property, but also analyzes an additional 70 key regional markets, exploring the dynamics and drivers shaping the globes high-end real estate market.
