     <a class="post-title" href="http://johnnylists.com/post/115123717919/10-tips-for-first-time-solo-travelers">
      <h2>
       10 Tips for First Time Solo Travelers
      </h2>
     </a>
     <p align="center">
      by
      <a href="http://twitter.com/johnny_webber">
       Johnny Webber
      </a>
     </p>
     <br/>
     <p>
      <b>
       1. Bring a journal.
      </b>
      Write, draw, get inspired.
      <br/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       2. You should probably pack less than youre planning.
      </b>
      You really dont need as much stuff as you think. Pack light to stay mobile.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       3. Follow the people.
      </b>
      If you meet someone, and they invite you somewhere go. If the locals tell you to do something, do it.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       4. See less places, and do more at each.
      </b>
      Invest time in places to get to know them better.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       5. Book your hostels in advance.
      </b>
      Hostels are amazing. They are cheap, and you get to meet many like-minded travelers. Since they are so awesome, they get booked fast. Dont get stuck without a bed.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       6. Dont wear white clothes.
      </b>
      They are gonna get really really dirty.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       7. Embrace mistakes.
      </b>
      They will happen. Laugh them off, try to learn something, and move on.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       8. Stray from the beaten track.
      </b>
      Spend some time outside the tourist cities. Try to get to know the local culture a little better.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       9. Pack a first aid kit.
      </b>
      Bring some band aids and extra Immodium. Foreign food can do unpredictable things to an unfamiliar stomach.
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       10. Never keep your wallet in your back pocket.
      </b>
      Youre just asking to be pickpocketed if you do that.
     </p>
