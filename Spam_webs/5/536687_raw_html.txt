         <h2 class="post-title">
          10 Money Saving Tips for the Summer
         </h2>
        </div>
        <div class="clear">
        </div>
        <div class="post-content-content">
         <p>
          The summer is here, and it can become expensive getting through it, especially for those with kids. With that in mind, here are some easy money saving tips that can help ease the financial burden.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-405">
          </span>
         </p>
         <h3>
          1. Children Eat For Less, or Free
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a data-rel="lightbox-0" href="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kids-eat-free.jpg">
           <img alt="Kids Eat Free" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-469" data-lazy-src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kids-eat-free-300x140.jpg" height="140" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="300"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Kids Eat Free" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-469" height="140" src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kids-eat-free-300x140.jpg" width="300"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
          Treating the whole family to a meal in a restaurant can be one of the most costly parts of any day trip.
         </p>
         <p>
          Fortunately, some places have special deals where the kids meals will be heavily discounted when eating with a full-paying adult.
         </p>
         <p>
          Start the day at Beefeater Grill, where up to two children can get a free breakfast when an adult buys one for full price.
         </p>
         <p>
          On Sundays, Slug and Lettuce offer a 1 childrens meal when an adult buys something from the main menu.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.myvouchercodes.co.uk/restaurants-takeaways-bars" target="_blank">
            MyVoucherCodes.co.uk
           </a>
          </strong>
          have a great list of the latest voucher codes and offers for restaurants and places to eat, including deals from Pizza Express, Dominos, Hungry House, Zizzi and more.
         </p>
         <p>
          You can find a list of locations offer for
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.hotukdeals.com/search?action=search&amp;keywords=kids+eat+free&amp;category=&amp;type=0&amp;date_range=&amp;sort_type=relevance" target="_blank">
            free kids meals on HotUKeals.com
           </a>
          </strong>
          or another good site to find
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.moneysavingexpert.com/deals/cheap-restaurant-deals" target="_blank">
            restaurant money off vouchers and deals
           </a>
          </strong>
          is MSE.
         </p>
         <h3>
          2. Cinema Mornings
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a data-rel="lightbox-1" href="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/two-people-cinema.jpg">
           <img alt="Couple at cinema" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-471" data-lazy-src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/two-people-cinema-300x246.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="180"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Couple at cinema" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-471" src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/two-people-cinema-300x246.jpg" width="180"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
          Although not the most traditional time to go and see a film, it is often the cheapest.
         </p>
         <p>
          Various cinemas offer discounted tickets for children on weekend or holiday mornings.
         </p>
         <p>
          Meanwhile, Orange or EE pay-as-you-go customers can also take advantage of Orange Wednesday to get 2 for 1 tickets.
         </p>
         <p>
          Check for more cheap cinema deals
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.moneysavingexpert.com/deals/cheap-cinema-tickets" target="_blank">
            here
           </a>
          </strong>
          or
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.groupon.co.uk/vouchers/tickets/cinema-tickets" target="_blank">
            here
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <h3>
          3. Free Festivals
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a data-rel="lightbox-2" href="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/festival-scene.jpg">
           <img alt="Festival" class="alignright wp-image-477" data-lazy-src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/festival-scene-300x199.jpg" height="177" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="267"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Festival" class="alignright wp-image-477" height="177" src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/festival-scene-300x199.jpg" width="267"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
          The big festivals are getting more expensive every year, but there are a host of free ones that you can go to, and they arent limited to just music.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.brixtonsplash.org/" target="_blank">
            Brixton Splash
           </a>
          </strong>
          features arts, crafts, theatre, and music.
         </p>
         <p>
          The
          <strong>
           Riverside Festival
          </strong>
          in Nottingham is a street fair mixed with musical performances.
         </p>
         <p>
          The
          <a href="http://www.edinburghjazzfestival.com/" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            Edinburgh Jazz and Blues Festival
           </strong>
          </a>
          features both free and ticketed events. Wherever you are, there will be something near you.
         </p>
         <p>
          You can find a great list of free festivals in the UK on
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.moneysavingexpert.com/deals/free-uk-festivals" target="_blank">
            Martins Money Saving Expert here
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <h3>
          4. Book A Flight While Theyre On Sale
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a data-rel="lightbox-3" href="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kids-having-fun-on-beach-medium.jpg">
           <img alt="Kids having fun on the beach" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-480" data-lazy-src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kids-having-fun-on-beach-medium.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" style="width: 100%;"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Kids having fun on the beach" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-480" src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kids-having-fun-on-beach-medium.jpg" style="width: 100%;"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          If youre planning to go somewhere a little further and havent booked yet, be sure to check out
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.quidco.com/user/3193000/1865008/british-airways-flights-and-holidays/" target="_blank">
            British Airways
           </a>
          </strong>
          and Virgins summer sales.
         </p>
         <p>
          Until July 20th, you can get return flights to Europe and beyond for less.
         </p>
         <p>
          Be sure to check their last minute deals too, and always use a comparison site like Skyscanner to check if anybody else can give you a better deal.
         </p>
         <p>
          You can find a great list of flight comparison sites in the UK
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.telegraph.co.uk/travel/travel-advice/8250012/Travel-advice-the-best-flight-comparison-websites.html" target="_blank">
            here
           </a>
          </strong>
          , or why not try
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.quidco.com/user/3193000/1865007/booking-buddy/" target="_blank">
            Booking Buddy via Quidco
           </a>
          </strong>
           you get 2p for every time you search!
         </p>
         <h3>
          5. Dont Change Your Money Too Often
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a data-rel="lightbox-4" href="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/holiday-money.jpg">
           <img alt="Holiday money" class="alignleft wp-image-482" data-lazy-src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/holiday-money-300x200.jpg" height="174" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="261"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Holiday money" class="alignleft wp-image-482" height="174" src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/holiday-money-300x200.jpg" width="261"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
          Of course, when travelling outside of the UK you will need to change some of your hard-earned pounds to the local currency of your chosen destination.
         </p>
         <p>
          While most people know never to change money at the airport, you could be paying an unnecessary commission wherever you make the transaction.
         </p>
         <p>
          Ask your friends and colleagues who may have recently been to the same destination if they have any leftover currency that you could swap for pounds.
         </p>
         <p>
          And if you return with leftover currency yourself, keep it.
         </p>
         <p>
          Especially if you plan to go back to the same place again in the future.
         </p>
         <h3>
          6. Buy A Train Ticket
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a data-rel="lightbox-5" href="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/train-station-brighton.jpg">
           <img alt="Train station, Brighton" class="alignright wp-image-486" data-lazy-src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/train-station-brighton-300x223.jpg" height="187" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="252"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Train station, Brighton" class="alignright wp-image-486" height="187" src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/train-station-brighton-300x223.jpg" width="252"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
          The Days Out Guide website offers vouchers, redeemable after buying a National Rail ticket, that offer discounts on entry to certain attractions, such as Drayton Manor or Madame Tussauds.
         </p>
         <p>
          You need a ticket to the closest station to the attraction, but it can work out cheaper than paying full price, even if you dont travel by train.
         </p>
         <p>
          Did you know you can use
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/reviews/review-is-quidco-worth-it-or-a-con/">
            Quidco
           </a>
          </strong>
          to get a little cash-back when you buy train tickets trough
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.quidco.com/user/3193000/1863270/thetrainline/" target="_blank">
            TheTrainLine.com
           </a>
          </strong>
          ? Now you do.
         </p>
         <h3>
          7. Rent A Dress For The Big Day
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a data-rel="lightbox-6" href="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/wedding-cost.jpg">
           <img alt="Wedding cost" class="alignleft wp-image-488" data-lazy-src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/wedding-cost-300x213.jpg" height="151" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="213"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Wedding cost" class="alignleft wp-image-488" height="151" src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/wedding-cost-300x213.jpg" width="213"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
          If youre going to a wedding or other posh event, and dont like to be seen in the same dress twice, you can save money by hiring your outfit, rather than buying.
         </p>
         <p>
          There are plenty of websites offering this service, including
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.girlmeetsdress.com/" target="_blank">
            Girl Meets Dress
           </a>
          </strong>
          and
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.wishwantwear.com/" target="_blank">
            Wish Want Wear
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          You can also rent a hat or fascinator to top it off.
         </p>
         <h3>
          8. Staying In
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a data-rel="lightbox-7" href="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/family-watching-tv.jpg">
           <img alt="Family watching tv" class="alignright wp-image-489" data-lazy-src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/family-watching-tv-300x205.jpg" height="177" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="259"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Family watching tv" class="alignright wp-image-489" height="177" src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/family-watching-tv-300x205.jpg" width="259"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
          Despite the rise in popularity of online streaming services like
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.netflix.com/" target="_blank">
            Netflix
           </a>
          </strong>
          , it is still possible to rent DVDs.
         </p>
         <p>
          Love Film packages start from as low as 3.99 a month, and even send you a prepaid envelope for when you want to return the DVD.
         </p>
         <p>
          This can be whenever you like, as there are no late fees. Another similar UK based service is
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.quidco.com/user/3193000/1863334/blinkbox/" target="_blank">
            Blinkbox
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Check out this list of
          <a href="http://www.thesimpledollar.com/100-things-to-do-during-a-money-free-weekend/" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            100 things to do staying in for no money
           </strong>
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h3>
          9. Free Museums
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a data-rel="lightbox-8" href="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kids-at-mueum.jpg">
           <img alt="Kids at museum in London, Photo by Angela (Angie) Accarrino" class="alignleft wp-image-492" data-lazy-src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kids-at-mueum.jpg" height="125" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="188"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Kids at museum in London, Photo by Angela (Angie) Accarrino" class="alignleft wp-image-492" height="125" src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kids-at-mueum.jpg" width="188"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
          The UK has some of the best museums in the world, and a lot of them are free.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.britishmuseum.org/" target="_blank">
            The British Museum
           </a>
          </strong>
          , the
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.npg.org.uk/" target="_blank">
            National Portrait Gallery
           </a>
          </strong>
          , and the
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.nhm.ac.uk/" target="_blank">
            Natural History Museum
           </a>
          </strong>
          are some of Londons best, but you dont have to travel there.
         </p>
         <p>
          Every part of the UK has a selection of
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.moneysavingexpert.com/deals/free-museums-and-art-galleries" target="_blank">
            free museums
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <h3>
          10. Frozen Fruit For Smoothies
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a data-rel="lightbox-9" href="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/smoothie.jpg">
           <img alt="Blueberry doughboy Smoothie - Photo by miriamwilcox" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-494" data-lazy-src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/smoothie.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" style="width:100%;"/>
           <noscript>
            <img alt="Blueberry doughboy Smoothie - Photo by miriamwilcox" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-494" src="http://www.dumbfunded.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/smoothie.jpg" style="width:100%;"/>
           </noscript>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The cheapest way to enjoy smoothies this summer is to make them yourself.
         </p>
         <p>
          Buying frozen fruit and berries will ensure they wont go bad if you dont use them quickly, but you can also freeze your own fresh fruit.
         </p>
         <p>
          Bananas in particular can easily be frozen and used later.
         </p>
         <p>
          Making smoothies with frozen fruit also means you dont need to add ice.
         </p>
         <p>
          Check out this list of
          <a href="http://www.nursingdegree.net/blog/38/100-delicious-easy-to-make-smoothies-that-work-wonders-for-your-health/" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            100 healthy smoothie recipes
           </strong>
          </a>
          !
         </p>
