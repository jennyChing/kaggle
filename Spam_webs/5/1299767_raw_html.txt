          <p>
           <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/category/tags/staff">
            <b>
             By Debbie Emery
            </b>
           </a>
           
           <span style="color:#777;">
            <i>
             Radar Reporter
            </i>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Even in
           <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/exclusives/2012/04/newt-gingrich-bitten-penguin-zoo" target="_blank">
            the animal kingdom
           </a>
           , nothing lasts forever!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/category/tags/kim-kardashian" target="_blank">
            <b>
             Kim Kardashian
            </b>
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/category/tags/kris-humphries" target="_blank">
            <b>
             Kris Humphries
            </b>
           </a>
           couldnt make it past
           <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/exclusives/2011/10/kim-kardashian-officially-files-divorce" target="_blank">
            72 days of marriage
           </a>
            and after a relationship that lasted longer than any Hollywood union, a turtle couple have waddled their separate ways  after 115 years of marriage.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/photos/image/149778" target="_blank">
            PHOTOS: Worlds Most Stunning Wild Animals Strike A Pose
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Giant turtles
           <b>
            Bibi
           </b>
           and
           <b>
            Poldi
           </b>
           have spent more than a century by each others side, rubbing shells, holding flippers and swapping flirtatious looks across their enclosure, but have now decided that enough is enough, reported
           <i>
            the Austrian Times
           </i>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           We get the feeling they cant stand the sight of each other anymore, revealed
           <b>
            Helga Happ
           </b>
           of the Klagenfurt zoo, where the pair have lived for the past 36 years.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/photos/image/152741" target="_blank">
            PHOTOS: Super Cute Porkers Show Off Their Best Sides
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           They are both 115 years old  they have been together since they were young and grew up together, eventually becoming a pair. But for no reason that anyone can discover they seem to have fallen out, they just cant stand each other.
          </p>
          <p>
           Bibi isnt just giving her husband the cold shoulder  like many
           <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/exclusives/2012/06/kim-kardashian-kris-humphries-divorce-trial-deposition-date" target="_blank">
            human marriage meltdowns
           </a>
           , this one has gotten violent! The disgruntled 220-pound female recently attacked her former lover and bit off a chunk of his shell with her powerful jaws.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/photos/image/170846/2012/03/meet-prince-harry-hippo" target="_blank">
            PHOTOS: Prince Harry The Pygmy Hippo Loves To Bathe!
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Further angry alterations eventually persuaded zoo keepers that they needed to be separated before a reptilian War Of The Roses erupted.
          </p>
          <p>
           Even relationship counseling has so far been futile, despite coaxing with aphrodisiacs and encouraging them to play games together.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.radaronline.com/photos/image/159176" target="_blank">
            PHOTOS: Baby Elephant Throws Tantrum At Weigh-In
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           We have staff talking to and trying to engage the two in interacting and we hope that they might find their harmony again, explained Happ.
          </p>
          <p>
           We were told that its very rare that after so many years animals who are a pair will fall apart, but thats where we are. We hope though we can bring round a reconciliation.
          </p>
