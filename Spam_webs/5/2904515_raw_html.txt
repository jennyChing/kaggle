        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Times-Square-and-Cab.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14848" height="300" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Times-Square-and-Cab-225x300.jpg" title="Times Square and Cab" width="225"/>

        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #0000ff;">
          <em>
           ** This post contains a lot of photos. You can click on the photos to get a larger view **
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Im aNew Yorker, bred and born. My family resides about 35 minutes (depending on traffic) from New York City. We try and get down to the city as often as we can. Even though we have seen many parts of the Big Apple, there are still plenty of places we havent seen, and experience yet to be had. My kids have never been to the Empire State Building or on the Circle Line around the Statue of Liberty. Even though we are New Yorkers were a lot like tourists when we visit the city.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         This past weekend we were invited down to New York City to experience one of New Yorks hottest new attractions  The Ride.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Ride debuted in New York City this past fall. The Ride is a custom made tour bus outfitted with stadium seating, state of the art lighting and sound and windows that extend from the floor up to the ceiling. The Ride takes patrons on a 4.5 mile journey around Manhattan. Make no mistake. This is NOT just a tour of New York City. The Ride will have you experiencing the city in ways you never imagined.
        </p>
        <p>
         My family was thrilled that the weather couldnt have been anymore perfect when we went down to the city to experience The Ride. We arrived in the city early enough to walk around a bit and to have lunch before we departed on The Ride. FYI if you plan on eating at the TGI Fridays restaurant in Times Square be prepared to take out a second mortgage to pay your dining bill. Lunch for the four of us came to $126.70, not including tip. OUCH! Cheeseburgers were nearly $20.00 each. LESSON LEARNED! The next time well just grab a hot dog from a street vendor. The food was great though.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Marriott-Marquis.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14805" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Marriott-Marquis-300x225.jpg" title="Marriott Marquis" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Ride departs from the side of the Marriott Marquis Hotel right in the heart of Times Square. We love Times Square. Not only the bright lights, billboards and beautiful buildings, but also because you can find some pretty interesting characters in the Times Square area. Case in point  The Naked Cowboy and the Naked Cowgirl. Of course you cant run around the streets of New York City butt naked, well, legally anyway. So the naked couple were actually dressed in a bikini and briefs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Naked-Cowboy.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14806" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Naked-Cowboy-300x225.jpg" title="Naked Cowboy" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Naked-Cowgirl-and-Rob.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14807" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Naked-Cowgirl-and-Rob-300x225.jpg" title="Naked Cowgirl and Rob" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         My husband was more then happy to pose with the Naked Cowgirl. LOL!
        </p>
        <p>
         Not far from the guitar playing naked duo were some students looking to raise awareness and funds for Alabama and Japan.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Japan-Relif-2.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14808" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Japan-Relif-2-300x225.jpg" title="Japan Relif 2" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I felt really bad for the students. Everyone was surround the naked couple and giving them money but no one was paying much mind to these young people. I gave my son some $$$ and told him to bring it over to them.
        </p>
        <p>
         We had to meet up with the rest of the people who were going on The Ride with us inside the Marriott Marquis. I have never been inside that hotel before. Wow! Its HUGE! The elevators remind me of little rocket ships. They are made of glass so you can watch yourself zooming up and down the hotel.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here is a photo of the inside looking up. Im assuming these are all the hotel rooms.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Marriott-Marquis-Inside.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14809" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Marriott-Marquis-Inside-300x225.jpg" title="Marriott Marquis - Inside" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Marriott Marquis is HUGE!
        </p>
        <p>
         We met up with the group of people we were taking The Ride with. Once we had all gathered we went downstairs and outside to the side of the Marriott Marquis. We waited in line for a little while while The Ride was prepared for us.
        </p>
        <p>
         While we were in line a few Ride employees were going around with iPads asking people if they could answer a few questions. I honestly thought it was just so they could get a demographic of the people who are taking The Ride. It wasnt until after I answered some questions, as did my daughter, that we realized that they MIGHT use this information as part of The Ride experience (more on that in a bit). That left me and my daughter wondering what we got ourselves into. LOL!
        </p>
        <p>
         The Ride is HUGE inside. Its hard to believe you are on a bus. Its not an ordinary bus either. I believe its the largest bus allowed in New York City. Its 13 high, 45 long and 8 wide. Its a monster bus!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-Front-of-Bus.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14812" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-Front-of-Bus-300x225.jpg" title="The Ride - Front of Bus" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-Back-of-Bus.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14813" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-Back-of-Bus-300x225.jpg" title="The Ride - Back of Bus" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-Side-View-of-Bus.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14814" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-Side-View-of-Bus-300x225.jpg" title="The Ride - Side View of Bus" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Inside youll find three rows of seats, all facing to the one side of the bus where the windows are located. The seats are set up in a stadium seating fashion which means every seat on the bus provides you with a great view.
        </p>
        <p>
         There are more then 40 video screens on The Ride and 3,000 LED lights. This is one high tech bus! It can even emulate a New York City Subway or the inside of a night club.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/On-The-Ride-1.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14810" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/On-The-Ride-1-300x225.jpg" title="On The Ride 1" width="300"/>

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-Display.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14825" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-Display-300x225.jpg" title="The Ride Display" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I found the seats to be comfy too.
        </p>
        <p>
         My family and I sat in the front row. It was interesting to see all the way to the ground and way above to the roof tops of the skyscrapers. You could literally see everything that was in front of you, below you or on top of you.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Ride has two hosts, a driver and a mystery voice which is suppose to be the voice of The Ride bus. Think
         <em>
          Night Rider
         </em>
         (the talking car David Hasselhoff drove in the 80s).
        </p>
        <p>
         Our hosts were really funny. They made everyone laugh. Even the voice of The Ride was funny. It made it even better that we had a very lively group on board with us.
        </p>
        <p>
         The interesting thing about The Ride is that not only can you see out, but people on the streets of New York can see you too! Initially my kids were mortified by that idea, especially my teen daughter (got to love teenagers). She was so embarrassed when I would wave back at the people on the street that were waving at us. Personally I think that really adds to the laughter and fun of The Ride.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/TheRide_XSML_white_Logo.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14851" height="63" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/TheRide_XSML_white_Logo-300x63.jpg" title="TheRide_XSML_white_Logo" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Our tour guides mixed a stand up comedy routine that engaged the audience along with some really interesting facts about the places we were seeing in the city. It was great to see places in New York City that I have only heard about. Sometimes images would also appear on one of the 40 screens around the bus also contributing to the tour with more stats and information (many of which was very funny).
        </p>
        <p>
         You dont have to wait long to see why The Ride is a very unique experience. Along the tour the bus stops and out on the side walk youll see a performer that actually interacts with the people on the bus. Not only are they entertaining to watch but the reactions from the people on the street are priceless. Sometimes we were more in awe of the ordinary people on the street then the performer themselves. Some people had looks of total shock and disbelief on their faces. You can also easily spot the jaded New Yorkers from the tourists because the hardcore New Yorker wouldnt bat an eyelash at the performers and just kept on their merry way.
        </p>

          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14829" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-MM-Store-300x225.jpg" title="The Ride - M&amp;M Store" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Restaurant.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14830" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Restaurant-300x225.jpg" title="Restaurant" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Random-NYC-Shot.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14831" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Random-NYC-Shot-300x225.jpg" title="Random NYC Shot" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/NYC-Radio-City-Music-Hall.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14832" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/NYC-Radio-City-Music-Hall-300x225.jpg" title="NYC Radio City Music Hall" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Times-Square-2.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14833" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Times-Square-2-300x225.jpg" title="Times Square 2" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Personally one of the best parts of The Ride came at the end. The Ride is fitted with outside speakers, as well as inside speakers. Before we made it back to the Marriott Marquis The Ride started to play the song New York, New York. Not only did The Ride guests get to hear the song, but so did the people of New York City. As you can image in a very busy place like Times Square hearing New York, New York being blasted from a bus really captures peoples attentions. The people on board The Ride were all singing and waving our hands in the air. We even had a kick line going on in one section of the bus! The best part though was seeing the people on the streets watching us and singing and dancing right along with us. Oh my goodness!!!! Talk about a blast. That was an awesome experience! That was so much fun. For me that was the cheery on top of the sundae as far as this unique New York experience was concerned.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-NYC-Sidewalk-3.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14834" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-NYC-Sidewalk-3-300x225.jpg" title="The Ride - NYC Sidewalk 3" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-NYC-Sidewalk-2.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14835" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-NYC-Sidewalk-2-300x225.jpg" title="The Ride - NYC Sidewalk 2" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-NYC-Sidewalk.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14836" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-NYC-Sidewalk-300x225.jpg" title="The Ride - NYC Sidewalk" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         In the photos above the lady in the pink scarf (bottom photo) was doing her best Radio City Rockettes kick dance. And the lady in the top picture with the pink pants was having a grand old time dancing along with the music.
        </p>
        <p>
         FUN! FUN! FUN!!!!!!!!!
        </p>
        <p>
         We were all shocked that The Ride was over. It was actually 75 minutes long but seemed like it went by quickly. I guess that is because we were having so much fun.
        </p>
        <p>
         Once we got off The Ride we were able to speak with a few of the actors and tour guides. I was able to speak with the rapper for a few minutes and he told us about what he does, how he comes up with his rap songs, what he does when there is no bus coming and many other questions. It was nice to get a change to interact with them too.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-Actors.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14839" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Ride-Actors-300x225.jpg" title="The Ride Actors" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         After we were finished with The Ride we headed on over to the Hershey Store and the M&amp;M World Store. They were packed! Especially the M&amp;M World Store (they are across the street from each other).
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Giant-Hershey-Chocolate-Bar.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14837" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Giant-Hershey-Chocolate-Bar-300x225.jpg" title="Giant Hershey Chocolate Bar" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Wall-of-MMs-at-the-MM-World-Store.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14838" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Wall-of-MMs-at-the-MM-World-Store-300x225.jpg" title="Wall of M&amp;Ms at the M&amp;M World Store" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/MM-World-Store-NYC.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14840" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/MM-World-Store-NYC-300x225.jpg" title="M&amp;M World Store NYC" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         We were sorry that it was time to leave. We were having such a perfect day.
        </p>
        <p>
         On our way back home we stopped off at a scenic vista to take a few more photos ofNew York City,theBronx and the Hudson River. What a perfect way to end a perfect day.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Bronx-at-Sunset.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14841" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/The-Bronx-at-Sunset-300x225.jpg" title="The Bronx at Sunset" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Hudson-River-The-Bronx.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14842" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Hudson-River-The-Bronx-300x225.jpg" title="Hudson River - The Bronx" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Hudson-River-NYC.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14843" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Hudson-River-NYC-300x225.jpg" title="Hudson River - NYC" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Kids-and-NYC.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14844" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Kids-and-NYC-300x225.jpg" title="Kids and NYC" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/NYC-and-the-Hudson-River.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14845" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/NYC-and-the-Hudson-River-300x225.jpg" title="NYC and the Hudson River" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Viewing-NYC.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-14846" height="225" src="http://www.shescribes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Viewing-NYC-300x225.jpg" title="Viewing NYC" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you are ever in New York City and want to experience The Ride for yourself you canpurchase tickets and learn moreby visiting
         <a href="http://www.ExperienceTheRide.com">
          www.ExperienceTheRide.com
         </a>
         . The Ride runds364 days out of the year (its onlyclosed on Christmas day).Times vary depending on the month/season. Some rides start as soon as noon. Some rides start as late as 9 PM. Special pricing applies to holidays and group discount rates are available.
        </p>
        <p>
         To be perfectly honest with you the ONLYdownside to The Rideis the price. Regular ticket pricing is$65 per person (peak) and $59 per person (non-peak). There are no discounted children or senior tickets.A family of four during peakhours will cost you $260.00, which is very steep. My husband said if the cost was$30 per person ($120 for a family of four) then he would do it again ina heartbeat.You also have to consider things like parking, tolls, train, subway and/or taxi costs too.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Ride was an experiencemy entire family willnever forget. It made us see our beloved city in a whole new way.
        </p>

         </a>
        </p>
