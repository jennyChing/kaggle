     <h1 class="grub-topOfColumn" itemprop="name" style="font-size: 27px;">
      Grilled Peppers with Lime &amp; Salt
     </h1>
     <img height="516" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Grilled-Peppers-with-Lime-Salt-790x516.jpg" width="790"/>
     <div class="yui3-g">
      <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-rail-well">
       <div class="grub-byline" style="margin-top: 1em;">
        Recipe by
        <a href="https://plus.google.com/113274240511284571445" rel="author">
         Jerry James Stone
        </a>
         May 1, 2013
       </div>
       <div class="grub-mainPostContent" itemprop="recipeInstructions" not-style="font-size: 16px; line-height: 23px;">
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="653" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/grilled-peppers-with-lime-salt/grilled-peppers-with-lime-salt/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/grilled-peppers-with-lime-salt" rel="attachment wp-att-2859">
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I am always looking for fun and exciting ways to add heat to a meal. I put it in my sangrias,
         <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe-type/popsicles/">
          popsicles
         </a>
         and pretty much everywhere. Well, sometimes I just want to go straight to the source.
        </p>
        <p>
         These lovely grilled Padron peppers are the perfect example. Sweet, slightly spicy and very tastythey were made to be grilled! You can eat them straight up or put them on a taco, sandwich or how about a grilled cheese?
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="450" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/j2bROPLUKwE" width="600">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <div class="yui3-g" id="grub-recipeSummary">
         <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-recipeSummary-left">
          <h2>
           Ingredients
          </h2>
          <ul id="grub-ingredients">
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Pound Padron Peppers
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Tablespoon Lime Juice
           </li>
           <li itemprop="ingredients">
             Teaspoon Sea Salt
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <div class="yui3-u" id="grub-recipeSummary-right">
          <h2>
           <a class="grub-button" href="http://cookingstoned.tv/recipe/grilled-peppers-with-lime-salt/printable/" target="_blank">
            <span class="grub-printIcon">
            </span>
            Printable View
           </a>
          </h2>
          <meta content="PT15M" itemprop="totalTime"/>
          <h2>
           Total Time
           <div style="float: right;">
            15 minutes
           </div>
          </h2>
          <meta content="PT5M" itemprop="prepTime"/>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
           Prep time
           <div style="float: right">
            5 minutes
           </div>
          </div>
          <meta content="PT10M" itemprop="cookTime"/>
          <div style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
           Cook time
           <div style="float: right">
            10 minutes
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="grub-recipeTaxonomyList">
           <h2>
            Recipe Type:
           </h2>
           Appetizer
          </div>
          <div class="grub-recipeTaxonomyList">
           <h2>
            Main Ingredient:
           </h2>
           Chili Peppers
          </div>
          <div class="grub-recipeTaxonomyList">
           <h2>
            Diet:
           </h2>
           <a href="http://cookingstoned.tv/diet/vegan/">
            Vegan
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         1. Toss the Padron peppers with one tablespoon of olive oil and lime juice.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="644" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/grilled-peppers-with-lime-salt/peppers/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/peppers" rel="attachment wp-att-2863">
          <img alt="Peppers" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2863" height="386" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Peppers-600x386.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Lay them out on the grill, preferably such that each pepper is in contact with the grill. Placing them in a pile will only take longer to grill them.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="1349" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/grilled-peppers-with-lime-salt/grilled-peppers/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/grilled-peppers" rel="attachment wp-att-2861">
          <img alt="Grilled Peppers" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2861" height="809" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Grilled-Peppers-600x809.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Grill them until they are charred and blistering on each side. They will even wilt a little. While you are doing this, take time to enjoy the sound of those peppers sizzling. Such a lovely sound! It makes me hungry just hearing it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="735" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/grilled-peppers-with-lime-salt/grilling-peppers/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/grilling-peppers" rel="attachment wp-att-2862">
          <img alt="Grilling Peppers" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2862" height="441" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Grilling-Peppers-600x441.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         4. Transfer the peppers back to the same bowl you originally tossed them in and now add the salt, tossing them again.
        </p>
        <p>
         Enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="712" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/grilled-peppers-with-lime-salt/grilled-peppers-close-up/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/grilled-peppers-close-up" rel="attachment wp-att-2857">
          <img alt="Grilled Peppers Close Up" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2857" height="427" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Grilled-Peppers-Close-Up-600x427.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="grub-popupPhotoLink" grub-popupphotoheight="1270" grub-popupphotopath="/recipe/grilled-peppers-with-lime-salt/grilled-peppers-full/" grub-popupphotowidth="1000" href="#photo/grilled-peppers-full" rel="attachment wp-att-2858">
          <img alt="Grilled Peppers Full" class="alignnone size-grub-wellWidth wp-image-2858" height="762" itemprop="image" src="http://cookingstoned.tv/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Grilled-Peppers-Full-600x762.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
