         <p>
          <img alt="spiral-cafe1.jpg" src="http://coolboom.net/en/wp-content/uploads/2008/01/spiral-cafe1.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          The Spiral Cafe, designed by
          <a class="external" href="http://www.marksbarfield.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           Marks Barfield Architects
          </a>
          , is a competition winning design that combines art and commerce to bring life to a new public space.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-2784">
          </span>
          The building form is inspired by the 13th century mathematician Leonardo Fibonacci who identified the natural patterns of growth found throughout the universe.
         </p>
         <p>
          Like a shell, the exterior of the cafe is rough, rugged and durable patinated copper, whereas the inside is smooth and precious and lit by pearl-like glass spheres.
         </p>
