         <h3>
          <b>
           What Is An Anti Rust Paint?
          </b>
         </h3>
         <p>
          It is a common belief that paints are primarily used to protect metal surfaces from corrosion. However,experts have noted that there are specific chemical compounds that react better to specific materials.
         </p>
         <p>
          For instance, not all oil-based paints are anti rust paints because they are more commonly applied as decorative paints that offer partial protection against climatic corrosion. In short, they are not effective in protecting against drastic corrosion secondary to industrial chemicals and gases.
         </p>
         <p>
          Anti rust paint have three main components:
         </p>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <b>
            The Pigments
           </b>
            these are finely ground organic or inorganic powders that give color, film, cohesion, opacity, and corrosion inhibition.
          </li>
          <li>
           <b>
            The Binder
           </b>
            typically oils or resins but can also be soluble silicates. The binders act as the film forming component.
          </li>
          <li>
           <b>
            The Solvent
           </b>
            liquefies the binder and assists paint application. It can be any organic liquid or water.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p>
          At present, approximately 15 million known chemicals of inorganic or organic origin can cause corrosion. Hence, most corrosion engineers believe that no anti rust paint or coating method can singlehandedly mitigate corrosion resulting from this myriad of chemicals.
         </p>
         <h3>
          <b>
           Application Of Effective Anti Rust Paint From Cor-Pro Systems
          </b>
         </h3>
         <p>
          The corrosion industry continues to grow as novel discoveries such as high performance anti rust paint is now available to help prevent corrosion in submerged equipment. Most of these anti rust paints are environment-friendly and are more advantageous than solvent based counterparts.
         </p>
         <p>
          One of the methods of application of anti rust paint is through airless sprays. Airless spray is rapidly becoming the method of choice for application of anti rust paint to structural steel works.
         </p>
         <p>
          In this method, hydraulic compression of paint occurs before releasing it through a small opening in an airless spray gun. Variation in the opening size also changes the hydraulic pressure that results to a wide range of paint consistencies.
         </p>
         <p>
          For superior results, Cor-Pro applies airless spraying while working incontrolled conditions. The company has a 155,000 square foot, enclosed, weatherproof metallic building that stands on 4.5 acres. Thclimate controlled facilities are manned byhighly trained NACE-certified professionals.
         </p>
