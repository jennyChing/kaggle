       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Salmon Cakes for the one you LOVE!
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://presleyspantry.com/2012/01/08/salmon-cakes-recipe/" rel="bookmark" title="12:23 am">
         <span class="entry-date">
          January 8, 2012
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://presleyspantry.com/author/admin/" title="View all posts by Nicole">
          Nicole
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fpresleyspantry.com%2F2012%2F01%2F08%2Fsalmon-cakes-recipe%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fpresleyspantry.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2012%2F01%2FIMG_1252.jpg&amp;description=Salmon%20Cakes%20for%20the%20one%20you%20LOVE%21" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1252.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-3687" height="426" src="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1252-1024x682.jpg" title="oh how I love you" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The gorgeous salmon cake takes the spotlight. This is the dish you pull out when you simply want to show someone you love them. Packed with omega 3s, and one of the tastiest little cakes known to man. This cake also works well as an apology. Oh let me count the ways to express my heartfelt sentiments. Place a top a salad for a heart healthy meal, or gobble them down fresh out of the pan. My honey bunny likes me to make him french fries on the side. Either way. its all going to be a great meal. Cheers to your love with salmon cakes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #808080;">
          All photos taken by: Nicole Presley
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <strong>
           <em>
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             <a href="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1216.jpg">
              <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-3688" height="426" src="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1216-1024x682.jpg" title="the fun stuff" width="640"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
            </span>
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <strong>
            Ingredients for salmon cakes:
           </strong>
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         2 1/2  cups poached and shredded salmon
         <a href="http://presleyspantry.com/2012/01/05/how-to-make-poached-salmon/">
          (click here to see poached salmon instructions)
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2  cup diced yellow bell pepper
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2  cup grated carrot
        </p>
        <p>
         1/4  cup chopped green onion
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2  cup chopped mushrooms
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2  cup diced red onion
        </p>
        <p>
         1/4  teaspoon dil
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2  teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2  teaspoon garlic powder
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2  teaspoon black pepper
        </p>
        <p>
         1  teaspoon Lawrys seasoning salt
        </p>
        <p>
         1  heaping teaspoon spicy brown mustard
        </p>
        <p>
         2  eggs
        </p>
        <p>
         4  tablespoons mayonnaise
        </p>
        <p>
         1/4  cup bread crumbs
        </p>
        <p>
         2  tablespoons vegetable oil
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <strong>
            Dipping cream for salmon cakes:
           </strong>
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2  cup sour cream
        </p>
        <p>
         1  tablespoon spicy brown mustard
        </p>
        <p>
         In a large bowl combine shredded salmon, yellow bell pepper, grated carrot, green onion, mushrooms, and red onion. Mix well to combine.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1222.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3689" height="200" src="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1222-300x200.jpg" title="put it in a bowl" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Then add in the flavorful stuff: dill, sesame seeds, garlic powder, black pepper, Lawrys, and spicy brown mustard. Mix well.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1224.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3690" height="200" src="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1224-300x200.jpg" title="mix mix mix" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Lets add the stuff that will make it all come together. Eggs, and mayonnaise. Mix that in then add in the bread crumbs. Presto. We are ready to fry.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1228.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3691" height="200" src="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1228-300x200.jpg" title="I &lt;3 eggs" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1232.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3692" height="200" src="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1232-300x200.jpg" title="ready for the pan" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         In a large sauce pan over a medium heat add vegetable oil and allow to get hot. Then take 1/4 cup of salmon mixture and pat in your hands to form a patty. Place in the pan and cook on either side for 5 minutes or until golden brown.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1238.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-3693" height="426" src="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1238-1024x682.jpg" title="half way there" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Make the dipping cream:
         </strong>
         Mix sour cream and mustard together. Serve atop salmon cakes for a sensational experience. ENJOY!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1252.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-3687" height="200" src="http://presleyspantry.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/IMG_1252-300x200.jpg" title="oh how I love you" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
