     <h2>
      Chapter 22
     </h2>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="1" name="1">
       1
      </a>
      My
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/god.html" target="_blank">
       God
      </a>
      , my
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/god.html" target="_blank">
       God
      </a>
      , why hast thou forsaken me? why art thou so far from helping me, and from the words of my roaring?
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="2" name="2">
       2
      </a>
      O my
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/god.html" target="_blank">
       God
      </a>
      , I cry in the
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/day.html" target="_blank">
       day
      </a>
      time, but thou hearest not; and in the night season, and am not silent.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="3" name="3">
       3
      </a>
      But thou art holy, O thou that inhabitest the praises of Israel.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="4" name="4">
       4
      </a>
      Our
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/father.html" target="_blank">
       fathers
      </a>
      trusted in
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/thee.html" target="_blank">
       thee
      </a>
      : they trusted, and thou didst deliver them.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="5" name="5">
       5
      </a>
      They cried unto
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/thee.html" target="_blank">
       thee
      </a>
      , and were delivered: they trusted in
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/thee.html" target="_blank">
       thee
      </a>
      , and were not confounded.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="6" name="6">
       6
      </a>
      But I am a worm, and no man; a reproach of men, and despised of the people.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="7" name="7">
       7
      </a>
      All they that see me laugh me to scorn: they shoot out the lip, they shake the head, saying,
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="8" name="8">
       8
      </a>
      He trusted on the LORD that he would deliver him: let him deliver him, seeing he delighted in him.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="9" name="9">
       9
      </a>
      But thou art he that took me out of the womb: thou didst make me
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/hope.html" target="_blank">
       hope
      </a>
      when I was upon my mother's breasts.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="10" name="10">
       10
      </a>
      I was cast upon thee from the womb: thou art my
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/god.html" target="_blank">
       God
      </a>
      from my mother's
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/belly.html" target="_blank">
       belly
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="11" name="11">
       11
      </a>
      Be not far from me; for trouble is near; for there is none to help.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="12" name="12">
       12
      </a>
      Many bulls have compassed me: strong bulls of
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/bashan.html" target="_blank">
       Bashan
      </a>
      have beset me round.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="13" name="13">
       13
      </a>
      They
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/gap.html" target="_blank">
       gaped
      </a>
      upon me with their mouths, as a ravening and a roaring lion.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="14" name="14">
       14
      </a>
      I am poured out like water, and all my bones are out of joint: my heart is like wax; it is melted in the midst of my
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/bowels.html" target="_blank">
       bowels
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="15" name="15">
       15
      </a>
      My strength is dried up like a
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/potsherd.html" target="_blank">
       potsherd
      </a>
      ; and my tongue cleaveth to my jaws; and thou hast brought me into the dust of
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/death.html" target="_blank">
       death
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="16" name="16">
       16
      </a>
      For dogs have compassed me: the assembly of the wicked have inclosed me: they pierced my hands and my feet.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="17" name="17">
       17
      </a>
      I may tell all my bones: they look and stare upon me.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="18" name="18">
       18
      </a>
      They part my garments among them, and cast lots upon my vesture.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="19" name="19">
       19
      </a>
      But be not thou far from me, O LORD: O my strength, haste
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/thee.html" target="_blank">
       thee
      </a>
      to help me.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="20" name="20">
       20
      </a>
      Deliver my soul from the sword; my
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/darling.html" target="_blank">
       darling
      </a>
      from the power of the dog.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="21" name="21">
       21
      </a>
      Save me from the lion's mouth: for thou hast heard me from the horns of the unicorns.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="22" name="22">
       22
      </a>
      I will declare thy name unto my brethren: in the midst of the congregation will I praise
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/thee.html" target="_blank">
       thee
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="23" name="23">
       23
      </a>
      Ye that fear the LORD, praise him; all ye the seed of Jacob,
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/glorify.html" target="_blank">
       glorify
      </a>
      him; and fear him, all ye the seed of Israel.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="24" name="24">
       24
      </a>
      For he hath not despised nor
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/abhor.html" target="_blank">
       abhorred
      </a>
      the affliction of the afflicted; neither hath he hid his face from him; but when he cried unto him, he heard.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="25" name="25">
       25
      </a>
      My praise shall be of
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/thee.html" target="_blank">
       thee
      </a>
      in the great congregation: I will pay my vows before them that fear him.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="26" name="26">
       26
      </a>
      The meek shall eat and be satisfied: they shall praise the LORD that seek him: your heart shall live for ever.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="27" name="27">
       27
      </a>
      All the ends of the world shall remember and turn unto the LORD: and all the kindreds of the nations shall worship before
      <a class="1" href="/dictionary/thee.html" target="_blank">
       thee
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="28" name="28">
       28
      </a>
      For the kingdom is the LORD's: and he is the governor among the nations.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a class="verse" id="29" name="29">
       29
      </a>
      All they that be fat upon earth shall eat and worship: all they that go down to the dust shall bow before him: and none can keep alive his own soul.
