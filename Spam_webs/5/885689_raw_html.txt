      <h2 class="entry-header">
       TFTNs Top 11 Baby Product Picks in 2011
      </h2>
      <div class="entry-meta meta-detail">
       <img alt="" src="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/wp-content/themes/initiator/images/author-icon.png"/>
       Posted by
       <a href="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/author/darcy/" rel="author" title="Posts by Darcy Zalewski">
        Darcy Zalewski
       </a>
       
       <img alt="" src="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/wp-content/themes/initiator/images/date-icon.png"/>
       January 11, 2012 
       <img alt="" src="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/wp-content/themes/initiator/images/folder-icon.png"/>

      <!-- .entry-meta -->
      <div class="entry-content">
       <p>
        This is long overdue, but I must share with you our top 11 baby products that we used in 2011. Most of these items we received for review  thankfully!  or we may not have given them a whirl. Who knows for sure, right? What I can say is that these products are my favorites and I recommend them for anyone with a baby! Some of these are better suited for under 12 months old, while (most) others are still seeing plenty of use even today.
       </p>
       <p>
        These are not listed in any particular order:
        <span id="more-9192">
        </span>
        <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-9749" height="241" src="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/20120111-024037.jpg" title="20120111-024037.jpg" width="180"/>
       </p>
       <ol>
        <li>
         <a href="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/2011/08/13/ergobaby-carrier-review-abe2/" target="_blank" title="ERGObaby Carrier Review #ABE2">
          ERGObaby Carrier
         </a>
          We bought this but I wrote a review.
        </li>
        <li>
         <a href="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/2011/08/12/amy-michelle-lotus-diaper-bag-review-abe2/" target="_blank" title="Amy Michelle Lotus Diaper Bag Review #ABE2">
          Amy Michelle Diaper Bag
         </a>
        </li>
        <li>
         BabyLegs  Weve bought several pairs and we are asked about them all the time!
        </li>
        <li>
         <a href="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/2011/05/13/toyconomy-toy-rental-service-review/" target="_blank" title="TOYconomy ~ Toy Rental Service Review">
          Toyconomy
         </a>
          This is a little different since its a toy rental service, but we love it!
        </li>
        <li>
         Infant to Toddler Rocker  We received one as a gift for Rissas 1st Christmas from her Great-Grandma T. She still sits in it! (In the past she also slept and ate in that chair. Its made its way into a few pictures around here!) Mariah wrote a good review of it
         <a href=" http://formulamom.com/things-we-love-thursday-infant-to-toddler-rocker/" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         .
        </li>
        <li>
         <a href="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/2011/07/29/playtex-the-first-lil-gripper-straw-trainer-cup-review/" target="_blank" title="Playtex The First Lil Gripper ~ Straw Trainer Cup Review">
          Playtex Lil Gripper
         </a>
          Awesome for straw training!
        </li>
        <li>
         <a href="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/2011/09/12/snuzzle-me-7-in-1-baby-cover-review-giveaway-uscan-926/" target="_blank" title="Snuzzle Me {7-in-1 Baby Cover} Review &amp; Giveaway (Closed)">
          Snuzzle Me 7-in-1 Baby Cover
         </a>
        </li>
        <li>
         <a href="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/2011/11/21/12-days-of-gifts-galore-halo-sleepsack-review/" target="_blank" title="12 Days of Gifts Galore: Halo SleepSack Review">
          Halo Sleepsacks
         </a>
        </li>
        <li>
         <a href="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/2011/12/05/12-days-of-gifts-galore-kidzmed-veratemp-no-contact-thermometer/" target="_blank" title="12 Days of Gifts Galore: KidzMed Veratemp No Contact Thermometer">
          Kidzmed Veratemp No Contact Thermometer
         </a>
        </li>
        <li>
         <a href="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/2011/11/18/12-days-of-gifts-galore-douglas-cuddle-toy-review/" target="_blank" title="12 Days of Gifts Galore: Douglas Cuddle Toy Review">
          Douglas Cuddle Toy
         </a>
          The cat we got is Rissas BFF.
        </li>
        <li>
         <a href="http://www.darcyandbrian.com/2011/10/20/in-honor-of-the-tatas-lovable-labels-review/" target="_blank" title="In Honor of the Tatas: Lovable Labels Review">
          Lovable Labels
         </a>
          I never knew what I was missing out on until I finally reviewed a set!
