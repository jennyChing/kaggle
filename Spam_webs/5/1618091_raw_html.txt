     <div class="about_row">
      <h1 class="entry-title">
       Serious and complex Fraud investigation
      </h1>
      <a href="http://www.omegainvestigations.co.nz/serious-and-or-complex-fraud/">
       <div class="pic">
        <img alt="Serious and complex Fraud investigation" class="attachment-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" height="87" src="http://www.omegainvestigations.co.nz/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/FRAUD-Serious-and-Complex-fraud-shutterstock_13470619-1.jpg" width="125"/>
       </div>
      </a>
      <!-- .entry-header -->
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       Though they just sound like adjectives, serious and complex fraud is not the same as normal fraud, these cases involve incidents of fraud made against the government. Serious and complex fraud cases are considered to be the most damning of all the instances of fraud because these inflict the biggest financial burden on both public and private sectors.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       The country has its own sector that specifically deals with cases such as this, but do not close their doors on private agencies that can aid them in their investigations. Omega Investigations is an agency that has worked alongside authorities to crack financial offences that threaten to undermine the governments system.
      </p>
