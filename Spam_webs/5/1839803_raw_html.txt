     <h3>
      7 Next Gen Audio Gadgets for the Music Obsessed
     </h3>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="aligncenter" src="//cdn.thinglink.me/api/image/466464557892632578/1024/10/scaletowidth#tl-466464557892632578;1043138249" style="max-width: 100%;"/>
      <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//cdn.thinglink.me/jse/embed.js" type="text/javascript">
      </script>
     </p>
     <p>
      This holiday season is different from the rest when it comes to shopping for the music-obsessed person in your life. Thanks to the rise of wireless speakers and high-def components in a wide range of new gadgets, shopping for the always-on listener in your life couldnt be easier. If its still a bit perplexing to you, let us dispel the mystery with seven excellent suggestions for this holiday season.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Sonos Play:1 Wireless Speakers
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      The people that brought wireless HiFi to the masses have a very special wireless speaker available for holiday shoppers this season. The
      <a href="http://www.bestbuy.com/site/play1-wireless-speaker-for-streaming-music/1648801.p?id=1219058144905&amp;skuId=1648801&amp;st=1648801&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=1" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bestbuy.com/site/play1-wireless-speaker-for-streaming-music/1648801.p?id=1219058144905&amp;skuId=1648801&amp;st=1648801&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=1', 'Sonos Play:1');">
       Sonos Play:1
      </a>
      ($199) delivers deep, crystal clear HiFi sound in a compact unit that can be placed just about anywhere at home. It can access nearly limitless music from any device it is connected to from a wireless network. Its also modular, so if its a big hit for the music-obsessed person in your life, they can add more Play:1 units later as they please.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Beats Audio Pro Studio Headphones
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Every discerning listener needs a good pair of over-the-ear studio headphones. They deliver a bass response that just cant be matched by earbuds, and theres a bonus they keep your ears warm in the cold months as well. The
      <a href="http://www.bestbuy.com/site/beats-studio-over-the-ear-headphones/1710138.p?id=1219061145203&amp;skuId=1710138&amp;st=1710138&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=1" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bestbuy.com/site/beats-studio-over-the-ear-headphones/1710138.p?id=1219061145203&amp;skuId=1710138&amp;st=1710138&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=1', 'Beats Audio Pro Studio Headphones');">
       Beats Audio Pro Studio Headphones
      </a>
      ($299) are a great option for shoppers this season, and its hard to beat anything with the Drs logo on it.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Philips Shoqbox SB7200
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Bluetooth speakers have been a big hit in recent years, but few are designed to handle the rigors of outdoor use. The
      <a href="http://www.bestbuy.com/site/philips-shoqbox-sb7200-wireless-portable-speaker-for-most-bluetooth-enabled-devices-black/6767293.p?id=1218806152466&amp;skuId=6767293" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bestbuy.com/site/philips-shoqbox-sb7200-wireless-portable-speaker-for-most-bluetooth-enabled-devices-black/6767293.p?id=1218806152466&amp;skuId=6767293', 'Philips Shoqbox SB7200');">
       Philips Shoqbox SB7200
      </a>
      ($104.99) is built with rubber sealants and shock protection so it can handle spills, drops and other issues that only seem to happen outdoors. Itll deliver crystal clear sound in a compact package, but its one of the few that is designed to do so on the beach, at the campsite and elsewhere outdoors.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Google Chromecast
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Priced to be the perfect stocking stuffer,
      <a href="http://www.bestbuy.com/site/chromecast-hdmi-streaming-media-player/9071056.p?id=1219013308425&amp;skuId=9071056&amp;st=9071056&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=1" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bestbuy.com/site/chromecast-hdmi-streaming-media-player/9071056.p?id=1219013308425&amp;skuId=9071056&amp;st=9071056&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=1', 'Googles Chromecast');">
       Googles Chromecast
      </a>
      ($35) is designed to bring video and music from your mobile devices or your computer to your TV. It connects to the HDMI port on the back of your television, and it streams music and video from the source of your choice. Its compatible with Pandora, effectively turning your television into a wall-mounted stereo with endless music.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Audioengine Premium Powered Speakers
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Most computer speakers are underwhelming. This pair of
      <a href="http://www.bestbuy.com/site/2-2-0-channel-premium-powered-speaker-system-2-piece/3919143.p?id=1218443574077&amp;skuId=3919143&amp;st=categoryid$abcat0515039&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=3" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bestbuy.com/site/2-2-0-channel-premium-powered-speaker-system-2-piece/3919143.p?id=1218443574077&amp;skuId=3919143&amp;st=categoryid$abcat0515039&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=3', 'Audioengine Premium Powered Speakers');">
       Audioengine Premium Powered Speakers
      </a>
      ($199) are an entirely different beast, a set of high-power speakers with silk dome tweeters, 2.75-inch Kevlar woofers and a bass port for a fine representation of the full audio spectrum. These are the kind of speakers you might see in a home music studio, but theyre built for HiFi music, video gaming and more.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Bowers and Wilkins  C5 In-Ear Headphones
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      The Cadillac of earbuds, these
      <a href="http://www.bestbuy.com/site/c5-in-ear-headphones/3197299.p?id=1218384143401&amp;skuId=3197299&amp;st=categoryid$pcmcat143000050007&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=4" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bestbuy.com/site/c5-in-ear-headphones/3197299.p?id=1218384143401&amp;skuId=3197299&amp;st=categoryid$pcmcat143000050007&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=4', 'Bowers and Wilkins  C5 In-Ear Headphones');">
       Bowers and Wilkins  C5 In-Ear Headphones
      </a>
      ($179) are amongst the best you can buy for premium music listening. With a tungsten-balanced design and micro porous filtering, the technology around the interior speakers is as impressive as the kickers themselves. Your music-obsessed loved one will practically be hearing their favorite music for the first time with a set of these.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Samsung 2.1-Channel Soundbar
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      If the music-obsessed person in your life is watching concerts on TV with the standard speakers, this is an excellent upgrade. The
      <a href="http://www.bestbuy.com/site/2-1-channel-soundbar-with-wireless-subwoofer/7905043.p?id=1218862381353&amp;skuId=7905043&amp;st=7905043&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=1" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.bestbuy.com/site/2-1-channel-soundbar-with-wireless-subwoofer/7905043.p?id=1218862381353&amp;skuId=7905043&amp;st=7905043&amp;cp=1&amp;lp=1', 'Samsung 2.1-Channel Soundbar');">
       Samsung 2.1-Channel Soundbar
      </a>
      ($249) provides vibrant, well-balanced sound from an array of soundbar-based speakers and a wireless subwoofer. Its the ideal upgrade from the tinny speakers that come with most modern televisions.
     </p>
