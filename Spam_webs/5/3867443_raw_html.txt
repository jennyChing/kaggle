          <h5>
           The Washington Post was shameless again in the way it tried to hide its latest Washington Post-ABC News poll that showed a trend against President Obama and in favor of the Republicans.
          </h5>
          <p>
           (For their part, ABC only mentioned it for 17 seconds on Wednesday morning.)
          </p>
          <p>
           The story was placed below the fold on page A4 on Thursday, headlined: Voter enthusiasm points to GOP advantage, poll finds.
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            Heading into the final weeks of the midterm campaign, the political landscape continues to tilt in favor of the Republican Party, with President Obamas overall approval rating at the lowest level of his presidency and GOP voters signaling greater likelihood than Democrats that they will cast ballots, according to a new Washington Post-ABC News poll.
           </p>
          </blockquote>
