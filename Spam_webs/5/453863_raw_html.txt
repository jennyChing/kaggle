        <div class="article-content clearfix">
         <p>
          After social media unanimously cried out for the blood of unabashed animal poacher
          <a href="http://www.deathandtaxesmag.com/223936/teen-hunter-sparks-outrage-by-posing-with-animals-she-killed/">
           Kendall Jones
          </a>
          , it wasnt long until the Internet Outrage Industrial Complex
          <a href="http://dangerousminds.net/comments/steven_spielberg_animal_killer_inhumane_prick">
           set sights on a new target
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Were not sure if
          <a href="http://i.imgur.com/StNDD7q.jpg">
           this Facebook user has the driest sense of humor
          </a>
          on the planet or if hes just that unaware of the fact that the Triceratops hasnt existed for tens of millions of years,
          <a href="http://gizmodo.com/5601514/the-triceratops-never-existed-it-was-actually-a-young-version-of-another-dinosaur">
           if it even existed at all
          </a>
          . Either way, the caption and some of the subsequent comments are gold.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Internet angry at Steven Spielberg for murdering triceratops" class="alignnone" height="300" src="http://images.dangerousminds.net/uploads/images/steven1dfdfdddg_n.jpg" width="465"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i.imgur.com/hkufKlf.jpg">
           This
          </a>
          is, by far, the best screed in the comments section, and involves the commenter calling Steven Spielberg a disgusting, inhumane prick for his cold-blooded murder of a prop from the early 90s blockbuster Jurassic Park. Bravo, Internet!
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Internet angry at Steven Spielberg for murdering triceratops" class="alignnone" height="465" src="http://images.dangerousminds.net/uploads/images/stevensdfdfdgdfdfg.jpg" width="465"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          If that pic of the dead dinosaur enough to get people up in arms, then lets hope animal lovers dont see this candid shot of the time Spielberg sexually harassed a poor innocent shark. Talk about a disgusting, inhumane prick.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Internet angry at Steven Spielberg for murdering triceratops" class="alignnone" height="362" src="http://38.media.tumblr.com/9cyPFQbgCmo08ohfSHQmqt96o1_500.jpg" width="500"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Will this mans blood lust ever be sated?
         </p>
