        <h2 class="">
         11 Free Must-Have Apps When You Move Abroad
        </h2>
        <p class="preview">
        </p>
        <p>
         Movingabroadis always difficult. Not only do we leave our loved ones back home but many practical daily issues arise that we did not think of before moving. Thankfully we live in an age of smartphones and applications that are designed to make to our lives easier. Here is a list of freeapps that you must-have when you decide to move abroad:
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          FINANCE:
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Whether you like it or not money matters
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="381" src="http://blog.rebtel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/ToshL+XE.png" width="596"/>
         <br/>
         <em>
          <a href="https://toshl.com/">
           Toshl Finance
          </a>
         </em>
         assistsyou in putting your expenses in a budgetso that you know where your money is going.
         <br/>
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.xe.com/apps/">
           XE currency
          </a>
         </em>
         , an app converting any currency on-the-go, great to get you familiarized with the currency of your new country.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          LANGUAGE:
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          What does that mean?
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="381" src="http://blog.rebtel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/GoogleT+Duolingo.png" width="596"/>
         <br/>
         <em>
          <a href="https://www.google.se/mobile/translate/">
           Google Translate
          </a>
         </em>
         can help you translate some words on the spot, can come in handy at any moment.
         <br/>
         <em>
          <a href="https://www.duolingo.com/mobile">
           Duolingo
          </a>
         </em>
         makes learning the basics of a second language fun and interactive.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          PRACTICAL:
          <br/>
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Be aware of what you can do in your surroundings
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="381" src="http://blog.rebtel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/aroundme+wififinder.png" width="596"/>
         <br/>
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.aroundmeapp.com/">
           AroundMe,
          </a>
         </em>
         whether you are looking for the closest ATM or find a coffeehouse, find all interests points in your vicinity.
         <br/>
         <em>
          <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.jiwire.android.finder">
           Wi-Fi Finder
          </a>
          (click
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/en/app/wi-fi-finder/id300708497?mt=8">
           here
          </a>
          for iPhone),
         </em>
         finds the nearest paid and free Wi-Fi spots around you.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          WHERE TO SLEEP:
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          If youre in a pickle and need a place to crash
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="381" src="http://blog.rebtel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/hostelw+couchS.png" width="596"/>
         <br/>
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.hostelworld.com/mobile-app">
           Hostelworld
          </a>
          and
          <a href="https://www.couchsurfing.org/n/mobile">
           Couch Surfing
          </a>
         </em>
         ,find aplace to stay for a couple nights and meet new people.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          HEALTH:
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          You know what they say, safety first!
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <img alt="" height="600px" src="http://blog.rebtel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/medibabble.png" width="300px"/>
         <br/>
         <em>
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/medibabble-translator/id355398880?mt=8">
           Medibabble
          </a>
         </em>
         (only iOS),anapp designed for doctors and patients speaking different languages to communicate better.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          JUST FOR FUN:
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <strong>
          Because Everybody needs to relax or make the time pass while commuting.
         </strong>
         <br/>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="381" src="http://blog.rebtel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Candy+Timberman.png" width="596"/>
         <br/>
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.candycrushsaga.com/">
           Candy Crush
          </a>
         </em>
         , by now you should know this casual gaming classic and match the candies together.
         <br/>
         <em>
          <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.dm.timber&amp;hl=en">
           Timberman
          </a>
         </em>
         (click
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/timberman/id871809581?mt=8">
          here
         </a>
         for iPhone), as addictive as angry birds, chop down a tree to get the highest score possible.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
