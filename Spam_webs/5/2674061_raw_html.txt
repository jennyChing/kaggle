        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Are you wondering what
         <strong>
          clean eating
         </strong>
         is?Would you be surprised to hear that there really arent any
         <strong>
          health goals
         </strong>
         you can achieve without it? You just cannot get
         <strong>
          healthy
         </strong>
         without eating
         <strong>
          healthy
         </strong>
         . You can exercise the most of your time, but the food is the most important thing for a
         <strong>
          good health
         </strong>
         .If you eat whole,
         <strong>
          healthy
         </strong>
         and fresh foods, your body, skin and soul will glow with
         <strong>
          health
         </strong>
         that gives you endless energy.Check out our list of
         <strong>
          TOP10 clean eating recipes!
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <em>
          Over weeks and months you will get addicted to the awesome taste of healthy foodand start wondering why you never did it earlier!
         </em>
         <br/>
          The Fitness Doc
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          A must see list
         </strong>
         :
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.topinspired.com/top-10-clean-eating-meal-plans-for-better-living/" target="_blank">
           TOP 10 CLEAN EATING MEAL PLANS FOR BETTER LIVING
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <hr/>
        <h3 style="text-align: left;">
         1. Cauliflower Tabbouleh Tabouli Salad
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Cauliflower Tabbouleh Tabouli Salad" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-23955" data-wp-pid="23955" height="1104" src="http://www.topinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Cauliflower-Tabbouleh-Tabouli-Salad.jpg" width="736"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <em>
          via
          <strong>
           <a href="http://gourmandeinthekitchen.com/2014/cauliflower-tabbouleh-recipe/" style="color: #000000;" target="_blank">
            gourmandeinthekitchen.com
           </a>
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         This meal comes originally from the
         <strong>
          Middle East
         </strong>
         . Its not fancy or flashy but it comes together in no time at all, and conveniently keeps its crunch for days in the fridge. It containschoppedcauliflowerfor an entirely raw,grain-freeand vegetable filled version, and ousted the not quite yet in season tomatoes for some thinly sliced celery and tangygreen olivesinstead.
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: left;">
         2. Cilantro Lime Cucumber Salad
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Cilantro Lime Cucumber Salad" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-23956" data-wp-pid="23956" height="1196" src="http://www.topinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Cilantro-Lime-Cucumber-Salad.jpg" width="736"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <em>
          via
          <strong>
           <a href="http://thefoodcharlatan.com/2014/01/06/cilantro-lime-cucumber-salad/" style="color: #000000;" target="_blank">
            thefoodcharlatan.com
           </a>
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         This
         <strong>
          cucumber salad
         </strong>
         is light, just as you would expect anything with
         <strong>
          cucumbers
         </strong>
         to be.You can serve it immediately after you make it, or let it marinate in the fridge for a few hours. The flavors will be a little stronger if you let it sit.
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: left;">
         3. Quinoa Kale Tomato Corn Salad
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Quinoa Kale Tomato Corn Salad" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-23957" data-wp-pid="23957" height="1046" src="http://www.topinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Quinoa-Kale-Tomato-Corn-Salad.jpg" width="736"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <em>
          via
          <strong>
           <a href="http://jeanetteshealthyliving.com/2014/03/quinoa-kale-tomato-corn-salad.html" style="color: #000000;" target="_blank">
            jeanetteshealthyliving.com
           </a>
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         This
         <strong>
          Quinoa Kale Tomato Corn Salad
         </strong>
         is
         <strong>
          clean eating
         </strong>
         and its very good! It is packed with a lot of nutrients.Theres nothing fancy about this salad  its just made with really fresh ingredients, lemon juice and good olive oil. Adding some cheese is totally optional.
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: left;">
         4. Roasted Shrimp and Green Beans
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Roasted Shrimp and Green Beans" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-23958" data-wp-pid="23958" height="441" src="http://www.topinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Roasted-Shrimp-and-Green-Beans.jpg" width="736"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <em>
          via
          <strong>
           <a href="http://cleananddelicious.com/2012/01/09/clean-eating-roasted-shrimp-and-green-beans/" style="color: #000000;" target="_blank">

           </a>
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         The
         <strong>
          shrimps
         </strong>
         can be prepared on different ways. You can adda few dried seasonings with the green beans and the shrimp, and toss them all in the oven for a quick roast.This recipe you can serve over some quinoa or brown rice and youve got your self a super-quick, super-easy, clean and delicious meal.
        </p>

         5. Cauliflower Soup with Garlic Scapes
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Cauliflower Soup with Garlic Scapes" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-30928" data-wp-pid="30928" height="892" src="http://www.topinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Cauliflower-Soup-with-Garlic-Scapes.jpg" width="736"/>

         6. Vegetable Tian

        <p style="text-align: left;">

        <p style="text-align: left;">

For making this meal, you need to slice thin vegetables, season them only with little salt, pepper, and thyme, then you will topped them with small amount of flavorful cheese. Roasting the vegetables magnifies their flavor and gives them just a hint of sweetness.
        </p>

         7. Purple Cabbage Slaw with Hung Curd Dressing
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Purple Cabbage Slaw with Hung Curd Dressing" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-23961" data-wp-pid="23961" height="904" src="http://www.topinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Purple-Cabbage-Slaw-with-Hung-Curd-Dressing.jpg" width="736"/>

        <p style="text-align: left;">
         While
         <strong>
          green cabbage
         </strong>
         is the most commonly eaten variety of cabbage, we highly recommend trying
         <strong>
          red cabbage
         </strong>
         because of its nutritional benefits and its robust hearty flavor. The antioxidants in the purple cabbage are easier for your body to absorb from a cooked state. For the best benefit, eat a mixture of raw and cooked purple cabbage.
        </p>

         8. Blackberry Arugula Salad with Citrus Vinaigrette
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Blackberry Arugula Salad with Citrus Vinaigrette" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-23962" data-wp-pid="23962" height="1104" src="http://www.topinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Blackberry-Arugula-Salad-with-Citrus-Vinaigrette.jpg" width="736"/>

          clean eating recipes

        <h3 style="text-align: left;">
         10. Avocado Potato Salad
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Avocado Potato Salad" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-23964" data-wp-pid="23964" height="552" src="http://www.topinspired.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Avocado-Potato-Salad.jpg" width="736"/>

         <em>
          via
          <strong>

And what about the potatoes?Whether mashed, baked or roasted, people often consider potatoes as comfort food. It is an important food staple and the number one vegetable crop in the world.
