                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Back Home Indiana: An Essay by Returning Artist, Mychael Lee.
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5885948813349727465" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       Like myself, our guest contributor, Mychael Lee, has recently journeyed "Back Home Indiana" and has been rediscovering his native Hoosier land.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       I've known Mychael through many moons, many seasons and phases, and now, even, a couple decades. Mychael
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                        has ventured out to the forests and 
mountains and deserts and oceans of our country to cultivate something 
special to bring back to our cozy little farm land, appreciation.
                       </span>
                       As
                       <a href="http://www.brainyquote.com/quotes/quotes/n/nelsonmand107690.html" target="_blank">
                        Nelson Mandela
                       </a>
                       once said, "There is nothing like returning to a place that remains unchanged to find the way in which you yourself have altered."
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                       <b>
                        Mychael Lee:
                       </b>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
                      <span style="font-size: large;">
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                      I find myself living in an ambiguous area between St. Joseph and Marshall counties now. Farm country. My studio is on one of those farms, tucked into a sizable island of massive oak trees that is surrounded on all sides by corn. A wall of it. Its a place where you dont really need to build a fence because you have that wall of corn. And while we really ought to, Indiana doesnt have a town called Cornwall.
                     </span>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-Qa3Ev8jKBOg/U_iqzgZQP2I/AAAAAAAAIWo/iXqfrjR2XQI/s1600-h/1%252520Cornwall%25255B18%25255D.jpg">
                      <img alt="1 Cornwall" border="0" height="640" src="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-lANnspr6Bdg/U_iq0bZNwkI/AAAAAAAAIWs/E6hkrjzi2Uc/1%252520Cornwall_thumb%25255B15%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-color: -moz-use-text-color; border-style: none; border-width: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="1 Cornwall" width="481"/>
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: small;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                      There are no mountains nearby. There is no ocean nearby. There is a city and a river an hours bike ride north. On a quiet back roadits awesome (Im a cyclist). But it usually takes me longer because I get so distracted by all the tiny moments happening around me. Beautiful, necessary moments.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-mqKaCKwzQWA/U_iq0yZjEgI/AAAAAAAAIW4/iKxTszPVxbo/s1600-h/2%252520Mowing-1%25255B8%25255D.jpg">
                      <img alt="" border="0" src="http://lh3.ggpht.com/-NresiaIXW7Y/U_iq2KT2WNI/AAAAAAAAIXA/5dDqM_bVXaI/2%252520Mowing-1_thumb%25255B5%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-color: -moz-use-text-color; border-style: none; border-width: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title=""/>
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                      Its a slower pace here, but some things happen too fast to capture in a photo; like the two rabbits that leapt from the grass onto the road and ran concentric figure-eights in front of me before disappearing back into that grass. Luckily some of the critters arent in such a damn hurry.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-B-t8W1l-5WQ/U_iq2sSnbRI/AAAAAAAAIXI/ofM0kbJ-mB0/s1600-h/3%252520Katydid%25255B8%25255D.jpg">
                      <img alt="" border="0" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-LKw8qCx7XBI/U_iq3YtrScI/AAAAAAAAIXQ/_1DOY_I0_ZI/3%252520Katydid_thumb%25255B5%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-color: -moz-use-text-color; border-style: none; border-width: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title=""/>
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                      I dont know which has more Indiana Sexythe katydid, or the cicada:
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-TqaH1up88rw/U_iq4OHBXQI/AAAAAAAAIXY/rQ8Nrl5NsrY/s1600-h/4%252520Green%252520Cicada%25255B9%25255D.jpg">
                      <img alt="" border="0" src="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-0n-ESHSlONE/U_iq46IA69I/AAAAAAAAIXg/Ped1WtVSfMY/4%252520Green%252520Cicada_thumb%25255B6%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-color: -moz-use-text-color; border-style: none; border-width: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title=""/>
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: small;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                      Theres such an abundance of wildlifestill, after weve done so much damage to the land with farming and agriculture. Just on this farm alone Ive seen snakes, deer, squirrels, crows and ravens, hawks, turkey vultures, feral cats, bats, robins, bluebirds, toads, big insectsand elsewhere gophers and rabbits and turkeys and on and on. Theres always something going on somewhere. I find a lot of inspiration. Riding along these back roads in the mists of sunrise is an amazing way to wake up.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: small;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-Qy3ucWnHReQ/U_iq5k1VNKI/AAAAAAAAIXo/nRoS6ob67bM/s1600-h/5%252520Misty%252520Morning%252520Canal%25255B9%25255D.jpg">
                      <img alt="5 Misty Morning Canal" border="0" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-Kc8V6LgjslY/U_iq6FvYuLI/AAAAAAAAIXw/kqLmWPHHPqI/5%252520Misty%252520Morning%252520Canal_thumb%25255B6%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-color: -moz-use-text-color; border-style: none; border-width: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="5 Misty Morning Canal"/>
                     </a>
                     <span style="font-size: large;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                      If the time is right, and youre standing in the right place facing the right direction, you can find pathways to magical places. And thats no exaggeration. When I take the time to acknowledge where I am, I receive these experiences. The two bumblebees wrestling and falling out of a chicory flower; the paper wasp that is slowly eating the guard rail, but doing it so fast I can barely see him; the shaft of light leading to this primrose.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: small;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/-95Zl02f4DQA/U_iq6qknLXI/AAAAAAAAIX4/lEunCaJ0gnw/s1600-h/6%252520Primrose%25255B8%25255D.jpg">
                      <img alt="" border="0" src="http://lh4.ggpht.com/-5_RWRtQ1Ft0/U_iq7WLe1wI/AAAAAAAAIYA/fBk8QoW5atQ/6%252520Primrose_thumb%25255B5%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-color: -moz-use-text-color; border-style: none; border-width: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title=""/>
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: small;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                      I love riding along these roads, but its the farms they lead to that are the real treat. Its a vibrant and fertile place. You can grow some damn good food in this soil, and that is seriously sexy.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: large;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Verdana; font-size: small;">
                     </span>
                     <br/>

                      <img alt="7 Tomato" border="0" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-VsmpHmJslRU/U_iq8g5efvI/AAAAAAAAIYM/Qdh7-kMrjCg/7%252520Tomato_thumb%25255B5%25255D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-color: -moz-use-text-color; border-style: none; border-width: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px;" title="7 Tomato"/>
