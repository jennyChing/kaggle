      <h1 class="title" itemprop="name">
       Midday movers: Aaron's, Campbell Soup, Intel &amp; More
      </h1>
      <div class="source" itemprop="author" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
       <a href="/rich-fisherman/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
        Rich Fisherman

          <div class="group" itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            <em>
             Take a look at some of Tuesday's midday movers:
            </em>
           </p>
           <p>
            Social Media and Biotechs -  The
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="151687" data-inline-quote-symbol="IBB" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/IBB" target="_self">
             iShares Nasdaq Biotech ETF
            </a>
            and
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="39761258" data-inline-quote-symbol="SOCL" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/SOCL" target="_self">
             Global X Social Media Index ETF
            </a>
            dropped after Federal Reserve raised concerns that prices in both sectors remained high.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="136279" data-inline-quote-symbol="AAN" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/AAN" target="_self">
             Aaron's
            </a>
            - The electronics retailer slid after reducing its outlook for the second quarter.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="13942" data-inline-quote-symbol="CPB" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/CPB" target="_self">
             Campbell Soup
            </a>
            - The producer of convenience foods declined after Goldman Sachs cut its rating to sell from neutral.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="group-container last">
          <div class="group" itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="71569" data-inline-quote-symbol="CIT" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/CIT" target="_self">
             CIT Group
            </a>
            - The financial-holding company gained after increasing its quarterly dividend 50 percent to 15 cents a share.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="20154" data-inline-quote-symbol="HPQ" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/HPQ" target="_self">
             Hewlett-Packard
            </a>
            - The computer maker edged lower. Interim Chairman Ralph Whitworth resigned from the board due to health issues.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="21400" data-inline-quote-symbol="INTC" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/INTC" target="_self">
             Intel
            </a>
            - The chip manufacturer declined ahead of its second-quarter earnings report after the bell.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="23523" data-inline-quote-symbol="LGF" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/LGF" target="_self">
             Lions Gate Entertainment
            </a>
            - The film studio rose after it and Alibaba said they would join forces on a subscription streaming service for mainland China.
            <br/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="42765" data-inline-quote-symbol="MGM" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/MGM" target="_self">
             MGM Resorts International
            </a>
            - The casino operator declined as did
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="23998" data-inline-quote-symbol="LVS" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/LVS" target="_self">
             Las Vegas Sands
            </a>
            after Sterne Agee raised its estimates for both companies.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="140847" data-inline-quote-symbol="KORS" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/KORS" target="_self">
             Michael Kors Holdings
            </a>
            - The clothing retailer fell for the second session as Sterne Agee and Barclays both reduced their price targets for the firm.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="28738" data-inline-quote-symbol="PLUG" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/PLUG" target="_self">
             Plug Power
            </a>
            - The maker of fuel cells climbed after FBR Capital Markets began coverage with an outperform rating.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="44730" data-inline-quote-symbol="ROC" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/ROC" target="_self">
             Rockwood Holdings
            </a>
            - The lithium producer jumped after chemicals maker
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="8988" data-inline-quote-symbol="ALB" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/ALB" target="_self">
             Albemarle
            </a>
            agreed to buy the company for $6.2 billion.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="35655" data-inline-quote-symbol="USNA" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/USNA" target="_self">
             USANA Health Sciences
            </a>
            -  The provider of nutritional products fell after CNBC contributor Herb Greenberg suggested the company may be violating Chinese direct-sales rules.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="37038" data-inline-quote-symbol="WWW" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/WWW" target="_self">
             Wolverine World Wide
            </a>
            - The footwear maker dropped after cutting its forecast for full-year earnings.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="37243" data-inline-quote-symbol="YHOO" href="http://data.cnbc.com/quotes/YHOO" target="_self">
             Yahoo
            </a>
            - The online search engine fell ahead of its quarterly earnings report after the bell.
           </p>
