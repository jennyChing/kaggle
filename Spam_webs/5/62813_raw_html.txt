                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Topps metal signs pay tribute to classic Star Wars trading cards
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-6218732195305026815" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-bbn8qZFYZqk/VVRB1m67hUI/AAAAAAAAG0g/ZNIsMmAhtc4/s1600/toppsswsigns.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

These 10X14 metal signs are numbered to 99 and feature every wrapper design ever made for the first three films. They aren't cheap - starting at $69.99 each, but with less than 100 made of each, these will undoubtedly be a unique piece in your collection.
