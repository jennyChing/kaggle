         <div class="inner">
          <h2>
           Beings of the Otherworldly Kind
          </h2>
          <div class="details">
           <ul>
            <li class="comments-link">
             <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/2014/02/beings-of-the-otherworldly-kind/#comments">
              <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="28015 http://mysteriousuniverse.org/?p=28015">
               <strong>
                4
               </strong>
               Comments
              </span>
             </a>
            </li>
            <li>
             <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/2014/02/04/">
              <span class="icon-3">
              </span>
              February 4, 2014
             </a>
            </li>
            <li>
             <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/author/nredfern/" rel="author">
              <span class="icon-4">
              </span>
              Nick Redfern
             </a>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <div class="cl">
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
           <strong>
            As youll know, every so often Ill review here, at Mysterious Universe, a new book. And its that time again. Today, its
            <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1578594553/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=1578594553&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=mysteruniver-20">
             <em>
              Real Encounters, Different Dimensions, and Otherworldly Beings
             </em>
            </a>
            by Brad Steiger and his wife, Sherry Hansen Steiger. Neither Brad nor Sherry needintroductions. But they are going to get them anyway!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Brad has now penned somewhere close to 200 books, on all manner of paranormal topics, including UFOs, life after death, and zombies.
           <a href="http://www.goodreads.com/author/list/50210.Sherry_Hansen_Steiger">
            Sherry has authored
           </a>
           /co-authored more than 40 books, including the best-selling
           <em>
            Miracles
           </em>
           series. And, Im pleased to say, that even with so many titles under their belts, the dynamic duo continue to put out quality material.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/real-encounters.jpg">
            <img alt="real-encounters" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-28140" height="738" src="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/real-encounters-570x738.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Published by
           <a href="http://www.visibleinkpress.com/">
            Visible Ink Press
           </a>
           ,
           <em>
            Real Encounters
           </em>
            is a book that, as its title suggests, exposes the reader to an amazing number of strange entities that have for so long interacted with, helped, plagued, manipulated, taught, deceived, and downright baffled the Human Race. They are thecreatures of other realms of existence; those beings that suddenly pop into our reality, do their weird thing, then pop out of it again  just as suddenly.
          </p>
          <p>
           Some of them are from the depths of space, others come from the depths of the planet and the oceans. More than a few seem to have the uncanny ability to negotiate dimensions aseasily as we go from room to room in our own homes. Then there are those intelligences that appear to come from the land of the dead; that mysterious place (or places) to which some essence of us travels once physical life is at an end.
          </p>
          <p>
           Its important to note  with so many different critters, creatures and things under the Steiger microscope  that
           <em>
            Real Encounters
           </em>
           is not some sterile, seen-it-all-before reference-type book. No. This is a first-class study of a large body of data and varied entities, and one whichprovides the reader with a wealth of thought-provoking data  and all of a notable nature.
          </p>
          <p>
           Take, for example, the section of the book titled
           <em>
            Contact with Space Brothers and Sisters
           </em>
           . Yep, were talking about the controversial Contactee movement, one which really caught major attention in the 1950s, thanks to the likes of
           <a href="http://obscurantist.com/oma/orfeo-m-angelucci/">
            Orfeo Angelucci
           </a>
           , George Adamski, George Van Tassel, Truman Bethurum, and George Hunt Williamson. For themost part, the UFO research community has been content toplace such cases into several discernible categories: real, hoax, and fantasy. Brad and Sherry take us down a very different path, however.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/alien-lady.jpg">
            <img alt="alien-lady" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-28142" height="633" src="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/alien-lady-570x633.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           They carefully and astutely note the undeniable similarities betweenthe messages received from the Space Brothers and Sisters and those that are channeled by spirit mediums. This leads the pair to comment that perhaps they are all part of the same chameleon-like phenomenon that changes to reflect the belief structures of the time. In other words, this is bloody good stuff! It challenges the accepted wisdom and theories for who or what the Space Buddies are, and pushes us down a far more satisfying and likely pathway.
          </p>
          <p>
           One of the things that has long-fascinated me is the notion of highly advanced beings coming not from
           <em>
            outer
           </em>
           -space, but from
           <em>
            inner
           </em>
           -space. As in far below our feet. Yep, we are talking about creatures of the underworld. Such theories have been advanced, to varying degrees,by
           <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/2012/09/the-strange-saga-of-richard-shaver/">
            Richard Shaver
           </a>
           , Mac Tonnies, and Wm. Michael Mott. In fact, the reason why the subject so fascinates me is because of astorythat originated in my home county of Staffordshire, England  its a 1770-era saga revolving around an old, underground chamber and a menacing, hooded character.
          </p>
          <p>
           Our fearless authors address the story, along with many others; all of which suggest that many of the near-magical beings that interact with us come from a place far closer to home than we might think, or they may wish us to believe.Tales of Old Ones, Elder Gods, the occult-driven beliefs of the Nazis, and ancient legends of highly advanced races inhabiting subterranean realms, abound in what is an undeniably great chapter.
          </p>
          <p>
           Whenever people mention the word fairy, it inevitably conjures up pop-cultureimagery of small, winged, sparkling figures; the kind most often represented in childrens books and on Christmas cards and such like. The reality behind the fantasy and the folklore is verydifferent, however. Brad and Sherry share with us  in a chapter titled
           <em>
            The Wee People: Guardians of the Earth Mother
           </em>
            that the phenomenon
           <a href="http://paranormal.about.com/od/othercreatures/a/little-people.htm">
            of the little people
           </a>
           has a significant place in reality.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/abducted-by-fairies.jpg">
            <img alt="abducted-by-fairies" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-28143" height="421" src="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/abducted-by-fairies-570x421.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           One gets a distinct feeling from reading this particular chapter that the veil betweenour reality and that of the wee folk is one that, on far more than a few occasions, becomes definitively blurry. Thats when magical encounters occur some of which (maybe even
           <em>
            all
           </em>
           ) clearlytake placewhen the witness is plunged into a distinctly altered state of mind. Such creatures  it is made
           <em>
            very
           </em>
           obvious to us are not to be trifled with. Yes, they can be friendly. On other occasions, however,they are trickster-like. Sometimes they appearto be nothing less thanoutright malevolent and hostile.
          </p>
          <p>
           I was very pleased to see that one particular issue that gets good, solid coverage in
           <em>
            Real Encounters
           </em>
            is the work of
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s?ie=UTF8&amp;field-author=Trevor%20James%20Constable&amp;page=1&amp;rh=n%3A283155%2Cp_27%3ATrevor%20James%20Constable">
            Trevor James Constable
           </a>
           , the brains behind the theory that at least
           <em>
            some
           </em>
           UFOs are plasmic living organisms native to our atmosphere. If you arent already aware of Constables work, the Steigers will open your eyes to a whole new world  almost literally!
          </p>
          <p>
           The domain of the afterlife gets much coverage, too, in the book. Were talking aboutsinister and violent poltergeists,
           <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.com/alejandro-rojas/electronic-voice-phenomenon_b_1024541.html">
            electronic voice phenomena
           </a>
           , the world of the medium, shadow people, and numerous other issues that suggest there are things out there  on the other side of the veil, so to speak  with whom we can interact. When we do interact with them, however, we should take great care. In many cases, deception and manipulation are their calling cards.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dreams that may be far more than normal dreams, ones in which people have reportedly traveled to worlds and realities far beyond our own, abound in the pages ofthe latest Steiger-Steiger book. As do shape-shifters,extraterrestrial kidnappers, and benevolent and angelic entities.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/dark-forest.jpg">
            <img alt="dark-forest" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-28144" height="358" src="http://mysteriousuniverse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/dark-forest-570x358.jpg" width="570"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           And, there is something else too: my favorite chapter of the book. It goes by the title of
           <em>
            Meeting Strange Creatures in Dark Forests
           </em>
           . Large, shadowy and creepy woods curiously provoke wonder, intrigue and mystery in all of us  even if were not overly surewhy. We imagine them to be filled with mysterious creatures; those diabolical half-human monsters, winged and nightmarish beasts, and paranormalanimals that lurk in the shadows of the thick trees.
          </p>
          <p>
           Well, thats
           <em>
            exactly
           </em>
           what you get in this particular chapter: a fine study of how easy it is to leave civilization behind and suddenly become plunged into a very different world; a world of the primitive, of the malevolent, and of the abominable.
          </p>
          <p>
           Thisisa book that skillfully demonstrates the world we see around usdoes not amount to all that there is to see. There are domains anddimensions  call them what you will  that are inhabited by supernatural intelligences that we do not fully understand,and which we may
           <em>
            never
           </em>
           fully understand. In a strange and symbiotic fashion, however, its as if they
           <em>
            need
           </em>
           to be seen by us, just as much as we crave the answers to what they are and what they want from us. There are doors to be opened and forces and forms to be reckoned with.
          </p>

            <em>
             Real Encounters, Different Dimensions, and Otherworldly Beings
            </em>

           opens those doors andunleashes those forces and forms upon us  and then some!
          </p>
          <p>
           Finally, I should add that here in Dallas, Texas the weather is usually warm to scalding.Over the last couple of weeks, however, the temperature has plunged, the skies have darkened ominously, and the wind has been powerful and howling. It was against this chilly, atmospheric backdrop that I eagerly read
           <em>
            Real Encounters
           </em>
            And, for full effect,I recommend that you do likewise.
