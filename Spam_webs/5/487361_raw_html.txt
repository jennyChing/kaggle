     <section class="subsection">
      <p>
       Modeled after the dessert they were named for, pineapple upside-down cake drinks look just as good as they taste. A clever combination of cake-flavored vodka and pineapple juice results in a fruity and brilliantly yellow drink. Adding a splash of grenadine lends a pop of contrasting color as it settles on the bottom, creating a lovely red hue that mimics the appearance of the baked dessert.
      </p>
     </section>

     <section class="subsection">
      <h2 class="headline3 Heading2 head-alternate mg-4 block">
       Ingredients
      </h2>
      <ul class="markdown-ul">
       <li>
        1 1/2 ounces cake-flavored vodka
       </li>
       <li>
        3 1/2 ounces pineapple juice
       </li>
       <li>
        Splash of grenadine
       </li>
       <li>
        Ice
       </li>
       <li>
        Maraschino cherry, for garnish
       </li>
      </ul>
     </section>
     <section class="subsection">
      <div class="step">
       <h3 class="head-alternate headline5 mg-05">
        Step 1
       </h3>
       <p>
        Combine ice, vodka and pineapple juice in a cocktail shaker and shake for 20 to 30 seconds, until well chilled.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="step">
       <h3 class="head-alternate headline5 mg-05">
        Step 2
       </h3>
       <p>
        Strain into a cocktail glass.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="step">
       <h3 class="head-alternate headline5 mg-05">
        Step 3
       </h3>
       <p>
        Add a splash of grenadine and drop a single maraschino cherry into the glass to consume when the drink is finished.
       </p>
      </div>
     </section>
     <section class="subsection">
      <h2 class="headline3 Heading2 head-alternate mg-4 block">
       Sweet Substitutions
      </h2>
      <p>
       <strong class="markdown-strong">
        I
       </strong>
       f you dont have grenadine on hand, you can replace it with a variety of
       <strong class="markdown-strong">
        juices or syrups
       </strong>
       , since its addition is cosmetic and wont effect the resulting flavor. Substitute the grenadine with pomegranate juice, pomegranate molasses, maraschino syrup or liqueur, raspberry syrup or even
       <a href="http://www.seriouseats.com/2014/10/best-creme-de-cassis-brands-how-to-drink-it-cocktail.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        creme de cassis
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       If cake-flavored vodka is difficult to locate, you can replace it with
       <strong class="markdown-strong">
        whipped cream-flavored vodka or vanilla vodka.
       </strong>
       You can even substitute with plain vodka; just add a splash of vanilla extract for sweetness.
      </p>
     </section>
