     <h1 class="detail_title">
      How to Make a Pair of Handmade Beaded Flower Earrings with Beads
     </h1>
     <p class="detail_subtitle">
      Summary: In this tutorial, I will show you a pair of handmade beaded flower earrings. Follow my instruction and try to make one, hope you can enjoy the process.
     </p>
     <div class="Art_detail_banner">
      <img alt="How to Make a Pair of Handmade Beaded Flower Earrings with Beads" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/article_main_image/2389/628a4c1c-a8c9-4e0e-b5c6-31be629d9e9e-680-330.jpg" title="How to Make a Pair of Handmade Beaded Flower Earrings with Beads"/>
     </div>
     <div id="articleProductList">
     </div>
     <div class="art_detail_text">
      <div class="art_detail_content">
       <p>
        Have you ever try to wear some handmade earrings? It's very boring to wear the same style earrings. Today, I will tell you how to make a new kind of handmade beaded flower earrings. Look at the picture, do you want to own one? It's a good choice to make one for yourself and your best friend.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Materials and tools needed in the handmade beaded flower earrings:
       </h2>
       <p>
        Tiger Wires
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.pandahall.com/electroplate-glass/243-1.html">
         Electroplate Glass Beads
        </a>
        Strands
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.pandahall.com/normal-glass/209-1.html">
         Glass Beads
        </a>
        Strands
        <br/>
        Crystal Glass Beads Strands
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.pandahall.com/round-seed-beads/229-1.html">
         Glass Seed Beads
        </a>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://KK-EC221-NFS-NF">
         Ear Wires
        </a>
        <br/>
        8mm White Pearl Beads
        <br/>
        2mm White Pearl Beads
        <br/>
        Needle Nose Plier
        <br/>
        DIY Jewelry Tool Sets
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="How to Make a Pair of Handmade Beaded Flower Earrings with Beads" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/description/83809024-a4e2-4fea-bfab-88a45d6c79dc.jpg" style="height:400px; width:600px"/>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Instructions on how to make the handmade beaded flower earrings:
       </h2>
       <h3>
        Step 1: Thread the 3 main beads of the earring
       </h3>
       <p>
        1st, string a piece of 2mm pearl bead on the tiger wire, and fold the tiger wire, then string a piece of 8mm pearl bead on it
        <br/>
        2nd, part the folded tiger wire, and string a piece of 2mm pearl bead on the one tiger wire, then thread the other across the 2mm pearl bead.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="How to Make a Pair of Handmade Beaded Flower Earrings with Beads" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/description/600c510d-8d12-4b81-b5c8-8dcb7c822838.jpg" style="height:400px; width:600px"/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Step 2Keep threading beads for the center part of the earring
       </h3>
       <p>
        1st, pull the tiger wire and fix the 3 pearl beads, then string 3 pieces of 2mm pearl beads on the tiger wire;
        <br/>
        2nd, thread the tiger wire across the pearl bead as the picture shows, then string 4 pieces of 2mm pearl beads on the tiger wire.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="How to Make a Pair of Handmade Beaded Flower Earrings with Beads" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/description/682118ed-82e2-42f1-b1b5-5ad3e9ee17c3.jpg" style="height:400px; width:600px"/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Step 3: Finish the center part of the earring
       </h3>
       <p>
        1st, thread the tiger wire across the first 2mm pearl bead, now you can see there are two parted tiger wires;
        <br/>
        2nd, string a blue glass bead and a seed bead on the one tiger wire, and thread the tiger wire back across the blue glass bead, then thread the tiger wire across the next 2mm pearl bead;
        <br/>
        3rd, string blue glass bead and seed bead, then do the same steps I showed before, you can see a final look of a flower.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="How to Make a Pair of Handmade Beaded Flower Earrings with Beads" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/description/dd7e1ae0-9a72-4109-8a96-970d3a3cfe29.jpg" style="height:400px; width:600px"/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Step 4: Make the chains for the earring
       </h3>
       <p>
        1st, pick up a tiger wire and string a crystal glass bead on it, fold the tiger wire then string 8 pieces of glass beads on it;
        <br/>
        2nd, repeat the steps and string 4 pieces of beaded chains, then hang a chain on one of the middle 2mm pearl bead.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="How to Make a Pair of Handmade Beaded Flower Earrings with Beads" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/description/f81240f2-649f-4d59-a6f1-3b7b687b0497.jpg" style="height:400px; width:600px"/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Step 5Hang on the chains and add ear wire to the earring
       </h3>
       <p>
        1st, hang the other 3 chains on the 3 middle 2mm pearl beads;
        <br/>
        2nd, add an ear wire to the top side, and you finish one earring, then repeat the steps and try to make another one.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <img alt="How to Make a Pair of Handmade Beaded Flower Earrings with Beads" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/description/84b110d5-b77f-48d4-ba81-b42186d61337.jpg" style="height:400px; width:600px"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        The final look of the handmade beaded flower earring:
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="How to Make a Pair of Handmade Beaded Flower Earrings with Beads" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/lc_images/description/9bbeb6b7-62e8-4531-8088-18b95c82facf.jpg"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Such a beautiful earring, I can't wait to wear it. Yeah, it makes me so pretty. Give me another 10 minutes and I will make another one. Then I can wear this pair of earrings to see my best friend, Im sure she will like them. Girls, follow my instruction and try to make one.
       </p>
