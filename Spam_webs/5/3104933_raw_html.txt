         Hollywood actor Antonio Banderas and his actress wife Melanie Griffith have both decided to call it quits and get a divorce after 18 years of marriage.
        </p>
        <p>
         The couples separation came to light after website TMZ.com published a news story saying that Griffith on Friday filed for a divorce from her husband.
        </p>
        <p>
         According to reports from TMZ.com, in filing for the divorce, 56-year-old Griffith cited irreconcilable differences as the reason behind her filing for divorce. She is also reported to have asked for spousal support (financial support) from 53-year-old Banderas.
        </p>

         TMZ.com says that the divorce documents that they obtained also indicate that Griffith wants joint legal custody of their teenage daughter, Stella. Their daughter Stella will turn 18 in September.
        </p>
        <p>
         According to a joint statement released by the couple, the split is amicable and that they are both happy and thankful for the beautiful time they had both spent together as husband and wife. The Hollywood stars added that the decision to split was taken in a loving and friendly manner.
        </p>
        <p>
         Banderas and Griffith got married in 1996.
        </p>
        <p>
         This becomes Banderass second divorce. His first divorce came after he split from actress Ana Leza.
        </p>
        <p>
         Griffith on the other hand, was married thrice and divorced thrice before her marriage to Banderas.
        </p>
        <p>
         Banderas is best known for playing the lead role in the film The Mask of Zorro whereas Griffith is also best known for the 1998 film Working Girl in which she was nominated for an Oscar.
