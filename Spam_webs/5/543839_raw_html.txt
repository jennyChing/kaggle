        <h2 class="post-title" itemprop="name">
         <a href="/fitness-center-watertown-ma/push-ups-for-charity-at-forever-fit-in-watertown-help-raise-money-for-our-vets" title='"Push-Ups for Charity" at Forever Fit in Watertown: Help Raise Money for Our Vets'>
          "Push-Ups for Charity" at Forever Fit in Watertown: Help Raise Money for Our Vets
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="post-details">
         Monday, April 20, 2015
        </div>
        <div class="post-body">
         <p itemprop="description">
          On
          <strong>
           Saturday, May 16th at 11:00 AM
          </strong>
          ,
          <a href="http://stayforeverfit.com/">
           Forever Fit
          </a>
          Owner,
          <strong>
           Jim Morando
          </strong>
          and his wife Linda will be hosting a very important fundraising event. We will be joining the FOREVER FIT Push-up Team to do as many push-ups as possible in 90 seconds to raise money for the Boot Campaign.
         </p>
         <p>
          All proceeds are tax deductible and go directly to our veterans.
         </p>
         <p>
          We are asking for as many people as possible to join us and our team as we perform
          <strong>
           <strong>
            P
           </strong>
           ush-
           <strong>
            U
           </strong>
           ps for
           <strong>
            C
           </strong>
           harity
          </strong>
          . You can do the push-ups or you can choose to make a donation, your help is greatly needed. You can make a difference in the lives of the people who have put their lives on the line for our country.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Here are the simple steps you can follow to make a life changing impact, and it takes less than a minute
          </strong>
          :
         </p>
         <ol>
          <li>
           To Donate: Click
           <a href="https://www.crowdrise.com/jimmorando-pushupsforcharity20/fundraiser/jimmorando" rel="nofollow" target="_new">
            here
           </a>
           . Click the donate button. Choose a team member and make your donation.
          </li>
          <li>
           To Join our Pushups Team: Select participant. Click next step and Forever Fit will appear as your team. Click next step and then click Sign up. You will then be prompted to set up your donation page.
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p>
          There will be food, beverages, entertainment, events for the kids, raffles and a silent auction. We hope to see you all on May 16th at 11am!
         </p>
