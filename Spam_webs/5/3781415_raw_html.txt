          <p class="BodyCopyWhite">
           The ND-3 Laser Designator uses patented technology to create true night vision and turns your scoped rifle into a night hunter. Combining a powerful, green laser diode and a precision optical collimator, the ND-3 pairs with your scope and allows you to cast a bright beam of green laser light to fully illuminate targets up to 250 yards. Rotating the collimator ring to minimum creates a bright micro beam of green laser light visible up to 3 miles and rotating the same collimator to a wider setting it can be used as a light source in close quarters or following a trail in conditions with low natural light.
