        <p>
         Ah, the desert. A place of simplistic beauty, peace and oh yeah, wild adventures, safaris, amazing spas and seriously good food and wine.
        </p>
        <p>
         Although the deserts of the world have remained largely the same for centuries, the accommodations available within them have come a long way. But whether youre looking for the good old fashion serenity that can only be found in these barren landscapes or something a little more refined, these five incredible desert getaways spanning five continents have got you covered.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <span style="font-size: 1.17em;">
          1) Amangiri (Utah, United States)
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Worlds-Best-Desert-Resorts-Amangiri-Utah.png">

         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Worlds-Best-Desert-Resorts-Amangiri.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Amangiri | Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3203" height="599" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Worlds-Best-Desert-Resorts-Amangiri.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Amangiri | Trevor Morrow Travel" width="734"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Amangiri-Collage.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Amangiri | Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3212" height="601" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Amangiri-Collage.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Amangiri | Trevor Morrow Travel" width="799"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Have you ever wanted to feel far away? Really, really far away. But the thing is, you dont have time to fly to the other side of the world this week. Good news: theres a place called
         <a href="http://www.amanresorts.com/amangiri/home.aspx" target="_blank">
          Amangiri
         </a>
         and its an other worldly oasis in the desert of southern Utah.
        </p>
        <p>
         Meaning
         <em>
          peaceful mountain
         </em>
         , Amangiriis a member of the ultra-luxurious Aman Resorts family and one of only two Aman outposts in the United States. The seductive resort which is built around a pool that hugs a natural rock formation, effortlessly blends into the stark yet beautiful setting. A visit to Amangiri should be comprised of nothing but relaxing by the pool, eating, and trips to the spa for someflotationtherapy (Im not exactly sure what that is but it sounds awesome).
        </p>
        <h3>
         2) Explora Atacama (Chile)
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Explora-Atacama.png">
           <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Explora Atacama | Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3195" height="482" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Explora-Atacama.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Explora Atacama | Trevor Morrow Travel" width="724"/>
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Explora-Atacama1.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Explora Atacama | Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3215" height="490" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Explora-Atacama1.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Explora Atacama | Trevor Morrow Travel" width="723"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Explorea-Atacama-Collage.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Explora Atacama | Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3216" height="635" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Explorea-Atacama-Collage.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Explora Atacama | Trevor Morrow Travel" width="807"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre looking to conquer the deserts of South America, well youre too late, Spaniard Diego de Almagroalready did that a few hundred years ago. But to fully understand, appreciate, and explore Northern Chiles incredibleAtacama Desert in a style that would make de Almagro proud, youll need to head for
         <a href="http://www.explora.com/explora-atacama/" target="_blank" title="Explora Atacama">
          Explora Atacamas Hotel de Larache
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Luxury and authenticity are balanced at this low-lying and rugged 50-room hotel with views of theAtacama and its volcanic mountains. But simply staring out at the view wont do, so head over to the stables and choose from one of the hotels 26 horseswhich have been specifically trained to handle the local terrain. As you sip South American wine and night begins to fall, youll notice that the Atacama is home to some of the clearest skies in the world. Good thing Explora Atacama has outfitted the property with its very own observatory. Enjoy.
        </p>
        <h3>
         3) The Fort (Namibia)
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/The-Fort-Namibia.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3196" height="483" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/The-Fort-Namibia.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Trevor Morrow Travel" width="727"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Worlds-Best-Desert-Resorts-The-Fort-Namibia.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3205" height="485" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Worlds-Best-Desert-Resorts-The-Fort-Namibia.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Trevor Morrow Travel" width="729"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/The-Fort-Namibia-Collage.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3213" height="577" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/The-Fort-Namibia-Collage.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Trevor Morrow Travel" width="939"/>
         </a>
        </h3>
        <p>
         If you prefer your deserts with more lions, leopards, and elephants, then
         <a href="http://www.onguma.com/about-us/onguma-the-fort.htm" target="_blank" title="The Fort">
          The Fort
         </a>
         in Namibias Onguma Private Game Reserve is for you. With 84,000 private acres of Namibian wilderness at your disposal, youll spend your days stalking (or being stalked by) the local wildlife. Morning game drives are followed by sunset game drives which are followed by night game drives, all of which are punctuated with fine meals and time spent staring out at the captivating dry plains and camel thorn trees that surround you.
        </p>
        <p>
         The African, Moroccan and Indian inspired compound is comprised of 12 private, free standing suites, each with an indoor, and most importantly, outdoor shower. Its time to become one with nature.
        </p>
        <h3>
         4) Banyan Tree Al Wadi (United Arab Emirates)
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Banyan-Tree-Al-Wadi.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Banyan Tree Al Wadi | Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3199" height="486" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Banyan-Tree-Al-Wadi.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Banyan Tree Al Wadi | Trevor Morrow Travel" width="727"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Worlds-Best-Desert-Resorts-Banyan-Tree-Al-Wadi.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Banyan Tree Al Wadi | Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3207" height="491" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Worlds-Best-Desert-Resorts-Banyan-Tree-Al-Wadi.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Banyan Tree Al Wadi | Trevor Morrow Travel" width="736"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Banyan-Tree-Al-Wadi-Collage.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Banyan Tree Al Wadi | Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3210" height="599" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Banyan-Tree-Al-Wadi-Collage.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Banyan Tree Al Wadi | Trevor Morrow Travel" width="835"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Lets say youre in Dubai with a few buddies, one of whom happens to be a member of the royal family (and damn does that dude know how to have a good time). After a few days in the city, youre now looking for less skyscrapers and more sand, so you head an hour northwest to
         <a href="http://www.banyantree.com/en/al_wadi/" target="_blank" title="Banyan Tree Al Wadi">
          Banyan Tree Al Wadi
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         While youre here, you and your compadres can relax by a pool, take advantage of the spa, or ride camels.
         <em>
          OR
         </em>
         you could hone your falconry skills with the Middle Easts first and only falconry course, then perfect your aim over at the archery range.Yeah, that sounds good.
        </p>
        <h3>
         5) Longitude 131 (Australia)
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Longitude-131.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Longitude 131 |Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3219" height="487" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Longitude-131.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Longitude 131 |Trevor Morrow Travel" width="730"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Longitude-131-dinning.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Longitude 131 |Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3220" height="487" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Longitude-131-dinning.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Longitude 131 |Trevor Morrow Travel" width="731"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Longitude-131-Collage.png">
          <img alt="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Longitude 131 |Trevor Morrow Travel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3221" height="653" src="http://trevormorrowtravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Longitude-131-Collage.png" title="Best Desert Resorts in the World | Longitude 131 |Trevor Morrow Travel" width="924"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ah the Outback,
         <span style="text-decoration: line-through;">
          blooming onions, steak and shrimp,
         </span>
         Australias red and wild interior. Home to fascinating Aboriginal culture, the famous Ayers Rock, a variety of strange animals that can probably kill you and one luxury resort:
         <a href="http://www.longitude131.com.au/" target="_blank" title="Longitude 131">
          Longitude 131
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         If youre looking to take your mate on a romantic escape and the typical beachside resort wont cut it this time,Longitude 131s 15 eco-sensitive tents might just be what the love doctor ordered. During the day youll spend hours discovering thelocal flora and fauna with expert guides, and at night youll be left to enjoy three course meals and fine Australian wine under a blanket of stars. After that, its off to the real blankets.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
