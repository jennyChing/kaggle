      <h1 class="pink header-spacer reduced-header bold">
       8 Most Expensive Romantic Gestures Ever
      </h1>
      <div class="addthis_sharing_toolbox">
      </div>
      <h2>
       1. Taj Mahal - Cost: 20 years, 20,000 workers, 1000 elephants.
      </h2>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <img alt="Taj Mahal" height="343" src="https://www.freedating.co.uk/images/articles/8-most-expensive/taj-mahal.jpg" width="500"/>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-dtuE6LPY9K8/TuXSQynOmJI/AAAAAAAAAGg/GFs7fMsjCS0/s1600/Taj-Mahal-Photos-3-%255Bawesomewallpaperspictures.blogspot.com%255D.jpg">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       Some would say that having the Taj Mahal constructed in your name would make you a pretty lucky lady. Others may point out that having given birth to 14 children means you're probably due something special. Yet others may even say that Shah Jahan (the guy who built it) loses brownie points for forgetting to start it until after his devoted wife had passed away.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <img alt="Lego Taj Mahal" height="362" src="https://www.freedating.co.uk/images/articles/8-most-expensive/lego-taj-mahal.jpg" width="500"/>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       If you want to build your own romantic gesture, look no further...
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://www.hardwaresphere.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/lego-taj-mahal-box.jpg">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       Small details aside, he
       <i>
        did
       </i>
       still get it built, and it was a very romantic gesture. And yes, it was also
       <i>
        very
       </i>
       expensive. How expensive? Well, it took twenty thousand workers over twenty years to build it. Over a thousand elephants were used to fetch materials. Twenty eight types of precious and semi-precious stone were used, including turquoise from Tibet, jade from China and sapphire from Sri Lanka.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       Justin Beiber, eat your heart out. The most romantic gesture in history belongs to Mughal emperor Shah Jahan.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <img alt="Shah Jahan" height="726" src="https://www.freedating.co.uk/images/articles/8-most-expensive/shah-jahan.jpg" width="500"/>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <b>
        True Player
       </b>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/84/%27Jujhar_Singh_Bundela_Kneels_in_Submission_to_Shah_Jahan%27,_painted_by_Bichitr,_c._1630,_Chester_Beatty_Library.jpg">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taj_Mahal#Construction">
        Source
       </a>
       ,
       <a class="hover" href="http://www.ohglobe.com/2007/12/10-most-romantic-gestures-of-all-time.html">
        source
       </a>
       ,
       <a class="hover" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mumtaz_Mahal">
        source
       </a>
      </div>
      <h2>
       2. Richard Burton's Massive Diamond - Cost: $15,200,000
      </h2>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <img alt="Elizabeth Taylor diamond" height="375" src="https://www.freedating.co.uk/images/articles/8-most-expensive/elizabeth-taylor-diamond.jpg" width="500"/>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       Big enough to
       <i>
        actually
       </i>
       be her best friend
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://media.tumblr.com/tumblr_lp0lai4djz1qd8di0.jpg">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       It didn't take twenty years and umpteen elephants to build, but it still stands as an
       <i>
        enormously
       </i>
       expensive romantic gesture. Before this diamond was sold, the most expensive diamond in the world had sold for $300,000 - Richard Burton paid $1.1 million (Over $15 million in today's dollars) for the Taylor-Burton diamond, dwarfing that figure.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <img alt="Richard Burton" height="450" src="https://www.freedating.co.uk/images/articles/8-most-expensive/richard-burton.jpg" width="360"/>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       When not buying enormous diamonds, Burton was fond of staring meaningfully into space
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://www.leninimports.com/richard_burton_gallery_new_8.jpg">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       Elizabeth Taylor, a star graduate of the 'bigger is better' school of thought, once quipped that 'big girls need big diamonds'. Apparently, modesty isn't all that easy when you've got a 69 carat diamond weighing you down. In Taylor's defence, she did sell it for $5 million, and built a hospital in Botswana with the funds.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       Who says enormously expensive romantic gestures can't be a force for good in the world?
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://www.toptenz.net/top-10-quotes-by-elizabeth-taylor.php">
        Source
       </a>
       ,
       <a class="hover" href="http://www.dameelizabethtaylor.com/tb_diamond.html">
        source
       </a>
       ,
       <a class="hover" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taylor-Burton_Diamond">
        source
       </a>
      </div>
      <h2>
       3. Jay-Z Buys an Island, Beyonce Buys a Car - Cost: $20,000,000
      </h2>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <img alt="Beyonce" height="400" src="https://www.freedating.co.uk/images/articles/8-most-expensive/beyonce.jpg" width="400"/>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       Beyonce's thinking face, mid-Sudoku
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://www.songslover.com/tracks/images/Beyonce.jpg">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       Some things strike us as so ostentatious as to be plain vulgar. We have no idea where (or whether) Beyonce wrote 'Boyfriend', but we hope it wasn't on the island Jay-Z reportedly bought for her for a stonking $20,000,000. Quite why the singer is in need of an island (being neither an explorer nor a horticulturalist) isn't clear, but it definitely counts as a very expensive romantic gesture.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <img alt="Island" height="316" src="https://www.freedating.co.uk/images/articles/8-most-expensive/island.jpg" width="400"/>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       This isn't the island Jay-Z bought for Beyonce. And boy was she mad about it.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://smsread.com/blog/home/smsreadc/public_html/blog/wp-content/uploads/HLIC/picturrs.com/files/funzug/imgs/travel/hrt_shape_places_05.jpg">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       Never one to look a gift horse - or Jay-Z - in the mouth, Beyonce promptly spent $2,000,000 on a brand spanking new Bugatti Veyron for her lover. Maybe he'll drive it around her island.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://www.zimbio.com/Justin+Bieber/articles/r6YCI5fPj0p/Justin+Bieber+Rents+Arena+More+Celebs+Over">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <h2>
       4. The Oldest Champagne on Earth - Cost: $39,000
      </h2>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <img alt="Champagne" height="287" src="https://www.freedating.co.uk/images/articles/8-most-expensive/champagne.jpg" width="500"/>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       The world's oldest bottle of Champagne nestles next a small jar of gherkins.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://www.letitflow.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/worlds-most-expensive-champagne.jpg">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       As a rule, don't give your significant other something that you've just found lying around. Unless, of course, it is a 170 year old - yet still drinkable - bottle of Champagne.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <img alt="Veuve Clicquot" height="600" src="https://www.freedating.co.uk/images/articles/8-most-expensive/veuve-clicquot.jpg" width="490"/>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       It's sure to pop their cork.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://www.imbecile.me/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/amazing-champagne-popping.jpg">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       Ravi Viswanathan reportedly heard about the Champagne at the auction, and thought that it would "be a fitting gift for the occasion [as] this year's actually our tenth year wedding anniversary".
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       The bottle of Veuve Clicquot was found in a shipwreck at the bottom of the Adriatic Sea and sold for a whopping 30,000.
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://www.letitflow.com/worlds-most-expensive-and-oldest-champagne-sold-for-record-e30000/">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <h2>
       5. A Fragrance Fit for a Footballer - Cost: $50,000
      </h2>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <img alt="David Beckham" height="385" src="https://www.freedating.co.uk/images/articles/8-most-expensive/david-beckham.jpg" width="336"/>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       All he needs now is a super-exclusive perfume...
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0; text-align: center;">
       <a class="hover" href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-_LnuvXB8rqs/Tlai8Sb0PRI/AAAAAAAAFXQ/rklmQR6A2rI/s1600/david-beckham.jpg">
        Source
       </a>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:0 0 15px 0;">
       Posh spice, super millionaire, ex-spice girl and fashion 'expert', spent $50,000 on a bottle of Clive Anderson No. 1 fragrance for her well toned, football-kicking hubby. The perfume is completely exclusive, taking a staggering 6 months to prepare.
      </div>
