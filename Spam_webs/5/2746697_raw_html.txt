        <h1 class="title __pt_title">
         Boost Lashes With Bimatoprost!!
        </h1>
        <i>
         by
         <a class="user" href="/user/bestpharmacystore">
          Sophie Powell
         </a>
         <span class="headline">

        <div class="authorphoto">
         <span class="__pt_photo">
          <a class="user" href="/user/bestpharmacystore">
           <img class="avatar1 " height="96" src="/m_photo/profile/20140920/1411205493940928.jpg" width="96"/>

        <div style="float:right;width:210px;margin-left:10px;">
         <a href="http://www.millionmarketers.com/view/apsense" target="_blank" title="Global Marketers Directory">

        <p align="justify" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
         <span style="font-size:14.0pt;
line-height:115%">
          This is the active ingredient and the generic name of the
popular eyelash growth stimulator latisse.
          <a href="http://www.remedymart.com/Product/50-Latisse" target="_blank" title="Glaucoma treatment with bimatoprost ">
           <i>
            <b>
             Glaucoma treatment with bimatoprost
            </b>
           </i>
          </a>
          <i>
           had been its primary objective
          </i>
          , but doctors had noticed a growth in the length
and density of the lashes. Since then the generic element came to be marketed
as bimatoprost latisse in the market for the growth of eyelashes in individuals
who suffer from hypotrichosis.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="justify" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
         <b>
          <span style="font-size:14.0pt;
line-height:115%">
           What should you know about the product?
          </span>
         </b>
        </p>
        <p align="justify" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
         <span style="font-size:14.0pt;
line-height:115%">
          Before buying the product you should keep a few things in
mind. Firstly, bimatoprost is an eye drop which has to be used with an
applicator. Consult your doctor if you are suffering from any eye condition.
Secondly, if you have undergone an eye surgery or are planning to undergo one
soon, it is better to not use this. Thirdly, find out if you are allergic to
any of the elements of this. If you are, ask your doctor to suggest some
alternative of this. Fourthly, you remove all makeup and your lens, if you wear
one in order to apply latisse. Do not wear your lens before 15 minutes of
applying. This may cause darkening of the eyelids and the eye lashes which are
reversible after discontinuation of the medication. However, it may cause
permanent pigmentation of the iris. In case of any new condition after applying
the medicine consult your doctor immediately.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="justify" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
         <b>
          <span style="font-size:14.0pt;
line-height:115%">
           How does latisse works?
          </span>
         </b>
        </p>
        <p align="justify" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
         <span style="font-size:14.0pt;
line-height:115%">
          The exact mechanism as to how it works is not exactly not
known. However, it is known that bimatoprost latisse lowers the intraocular
pressure of the eyes which stimulates the follicles at the lash line. Hence the
follicles produce hairs which are longer and thicker than the earlier lash
hairs. It also reduces the fall of lash hair at each follicular hair cycle and
thereby helps you to possess hair which are longer and thicker and frame your
eyes in the most beautiful way.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p align="justify" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:justify">
         <b>
          <span style="font-size:14.0pt;
line-height:115%">
           How to use latisse?
          </span>
         </b>
        </p>
        <div align="justify">
         <span style='font-size:14.0pt;line-height:115%;font-family:"Calibri","sans-serif";
mso-ascii-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-fareast-font-family:Calibri;mso-fareast-theme-font:
minor-latin;mso-hansi-theme-font:minor-latin;mso-bidi-font-family:"Times New Roman";
mso-bidi-theme-font:minor-bidi;mso-ansi-language:EN-US;mso-fareast-language:
EN-US;mso-bidi-language:AR-SA'>
          This is an applicator based medication. A batch
of applicators is provided with the medication and a drop of the medication has
to be applied on the applicator. Then, with the help of this applicator the
medication has to be applied at the base of the lash line. It should be applied
only on the upper lash and on the lower lash it is automatically applied when
you close your eyes after application. It has to be applied only once daily and
for a time period of about 3-4 months. You can notice desirable results only
after a period of 2 months. If you notice any harmful side effects then it is
better to consult a physician and use the medicine as prescribed. Even after
you get noticeable results it is important to note that once you stop using
this, your lashes will go back to their former condition. So, to keep the
effects permanent, it is important to keep using the medication at least on a
weekly basis. Take care not to pour the medication in the yes as it may cause
irritation or redness of the yes.
         </span>
        </div>
