       <h1 class="post-title post-title-single" itemprop="headline">
        The Great Irony
       </h1>
       <div class="post-meta post-meta-single">
        <div class="post-meta post-meta-primary">
         <div class="author" itemprop="author">
          <a href="http://hadhhogayi.in/author/nikhil/" rel="author" title="Posts by Nikhil Kumar">
           Nikhil Kumar
          </a>
         </div>
         <time class="article-time" datetime="2015-02-03T23:36:45+00:00">
          | 3 February, 2015 at 23:36
         </time>
        </div>
        <div class="post-meta post-meta-secondary">
         <div class="views">
          <i class="fa fa-eye">
          </i>
          239
         </div>
         <div class="comments">
          <i class="fa fa-comment">
          </i>
          <a href="http://hadhhogayi.in/the-great-irony/#respond" title="Comment on The Great Irony">
           0
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <section class="article-content" itemprop="articleBody">
        <table class="rw-rating-table rw-ltr rw-left rw-no-labels">
         <tr>
          <td>
           <nobr>
           </nobr>
          </td>
          <td>
           <div class="rw-left">
            <div class="rw-ui-container rw-class-blog-post rw-urid-1430" data-img="http://hadhhogayi.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/the-great-irony-e1423421825810.jpg">
            </div>
           </div>
          </td>
         </tr>
        </table>
        <p>
         <a href="http://hadhhogayi.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/the-great-irony.jpg">
          <img alt="Poem on irony of life" class="aligncenter wp-image-145 size-full" height="330" src="http://hadhhogayi.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/the-great-irony-e1423421825810.jpg" width="613"/>
         </a>
         Earlier In her class she got a weekend project,
        </p>
        <p>
         To know about the poor and their life in every aspect
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         She picked up her Gucci bag and wore her classy shoes
        </p>
        <p>
         And set out to know about their problems and rue
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         She roamed around the bus stand and searched every corner and nook
        </p>
        <p>
         But then somebody told her, youd findem where you dont often look,
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         That was pretty helpful and then she could find
        </p>
        <p>
         A family of five near the slums, where the whole developing world turns blind
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         At first she was reluctant to go
        </p>
        <p>
         But then she thought about the project and said NO!!!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The weightage of the project is too huge to ignore
        </p>
        <p>
         And I have to beat the other girl, Ill face whatever is in store
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         She went over to the family and said Hi !!!!!
        </p>
        <p>
         And all they could bear was an expression of WHY???????
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         She sat with the lady and inquired about her life
        </p>
        <p>
         She said her daughter has Dengue and great is her strife
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The girl sat patiently, pretending to hear every word she said
        </p>
        <p>
         But couldnt stop wondering what it would take for them to install a fan over their head
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The lady opened up a little and then cried as hard as she could
        </p>
        <p>
         The girl wondered what shed done to encourage this outburst for me its no good
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         She was just there to finish her project, not as much to care
        </p>
        <p>
         No need to cry in front of a stranger with your whole soul bare
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         But the lady couldnt stop, not very sophisticated you see
        </p>
        <p>
         And sat their wailing and walloping letting herself free
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The girl looked at the daughter, suffering she was..,
        </p>
        <p>
         Or is the disease a blessing in disguise??
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         For she would have had to live her life like her mother
        </p>
        <p>
         If the disease would get good of her she would at least leave without anyone to bother
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Eventually the girl stood up said her job here is done,
        </p>
        <p>
         The lady now understood that she only was
        </p>
        <p>
         One more of those unscrupulous ones
        </p>
        <p>
         The girl presented her project and stunned were all
        </p>
        <p>
         Gave example of Ambulance/ Pizza delivery paradox and how Government should take a call
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         And ended by saying we need to take a stand as soon as Possible
        </p>
        <p>
         Or Fate of the poor would be even more horrible
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         She got A+ and Rs. 3000, which was the first prize
        </p>
        <p>
         Irony, WTH, project on poverty and reward in Grands How Nice!!!!!!!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Meanwhile the daughter of the lady passed away due to low count of blood platelets
        </p>
        <p>
         And another surprise her blood group was also A+ like the girls grade was
        </p>
        <p>
         One was more important than the other thats for all to see
        </p>
        <p>
         But which one is more essential for you, is the key
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Both reached their fate in completely different ways
        </p>
        <p>
         One had the limelight over her, and the other left the worlds gaze
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Not the fault of the girl, she did what everyone else does
        </p>
        <p>
         Use others lives for selfish gains and thats they say how life goes
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
