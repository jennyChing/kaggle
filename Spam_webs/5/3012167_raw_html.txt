     </span>
     <br/>
     <br/>

     <a href=" http://www.bigweekends.com/splash-pages/legends-of-soul-mh.aspx" target="_blank">
      Butlins Legends of Soul Weekender
     </a>
     . To celebrate this, we decided to take a look at nine unforgettable soul hits and explore how they successfully crossed over into other genres of music.
