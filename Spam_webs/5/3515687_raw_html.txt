              Chapter:
              <a href="http://www.kri-comic.com/chapter/adjustment/" rel="tag">
               Chapter 2: Adjustment
              </a>

          <div class="entry">
           <p>
            We get a little bit of back story on how Tina happened to awaken. Not much, but enough to sate your appetites maybe? Will we go more into it later? Only time will tell.
           </p>
           <p>
            In other news, some stuff thats been going on. First off, as I mentioned last week, I put us up on
            <a href="http://www.thewebcomiclist.com/" target="_blank">
             The Webcomic List
            </a>
            as a featured comic. That has managed to skyrocket our ranking it seems. Were now keeping steady around 150. Thats insane. You guys are awesome readers.
           </p>
           <p>
            Secondly, Ive also been teasing on social media about a big announcement. If youre here before ~8 A.M. or so on Monday, youre seeing it before everyone else. For those keeping track, were exactly 7 weeks away from our 1 year anniversary. We may have had a bumpy time in the middle and missed a few updates, but somehow were managing to even make our 52nd comic on our birthday as well. In celebration of this, were proud to announce our first
            <a href="http://www.kri-comic.com/contests/" target="_blank">
             CONTEST
            </a>
            ! Its going to run from now until our birthday, February 2nd, and as the description states, all manner of submissions are welcome. We want to not only have this a celebration of us, but a celebration of
            <strong>
             you
            </strong>
            . Without you guys, we wouldnt be here. So it is with great pride, that I announce the start of the
            <strong>
             <a href="http://www.kri-comic.com/kriative-anniversary/" target="_blank">
              Kriative Anniversary
             </a>
            </strong>
            !
           </p>
