         UK Could Face 300m Over Air Pollution
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content content">
        <p>
         <img alt="smog" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-4176" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.traderiverfinance.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/smog-150x150.jpg?resize=150%2C150"/>
         The UK is facing fines of up to 300m a year over its failure to cut levels of a gas that are believed to cause premature death and affect the growth of childrens lungs.
        </p>
        <p>
         The European Commission said that it was taking legal action against Britain because it had not come up with a plan to get the amount of nitrogen dioxide  produced mainly by diesel vehicles  below agreed limits.
        </p>
        <p>
         Campaigners said people had the right to breathe clean air and that the UK had some of the highest levels of the gas in Europe.
        </p>
        <p>
         Do you think the fines are extreme, or well deserved? Will it affect the UK economy adversely if the fines are applied?
        </p>
        <p>
         Let us know in the comments!
