                     </p>
                     <blockquote>
                      <p>
                       Why prefer blue skies over grey? Because you can sing and play the blues.
                       <br/>
                       ~ Stan Stewart
                      </p>
                     </blockquote>
                     <p>
                      After the opening, at times disjointed
                      <a href="http://muz4now.com/2014/cracked-open-blues-part-1-piano-improv/" target="_blank" title="Cracked Open Blues, part 1  #piano #improv">
                       part one of Cracked Open Blues
                      </a>
                      comes this very bluesy improv. If you start to dance, be ready for some changes in the count (time signature)! This spontaneous composition bounces around from 3 to 5 to 4 and back again.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      No need for more words. Heres part two of Cracked Open Blues from the Painting Piano series of piano improvisations. Enjoy!
