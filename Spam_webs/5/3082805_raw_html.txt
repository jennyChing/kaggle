        <p class="recipe_comments">
         
         <br/>
         Alternate spellings: Yookaejang, Yukejon, Yuhkejahng, Yukaejang
         <br/>
         However you spell it, Yukgaejang is one of the more popular Korean Spicy Soups.
         <br/>
         Yukgaejang is a spicy, soup-like Korean dish made from shredded beef, scallions, fern sprouts, bean sprouts, sweet potato starch noodles, and other ingredients, which are simmered together for a long time.
         <br/>
         Yukgaejang is a variety of gomguk (thick soup) and was originally served in Korean royal court cuisine.
        </p>
