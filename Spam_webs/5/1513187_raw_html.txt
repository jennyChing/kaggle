              </h3>
              Choosing the Right innerwear 
In todays fashion conscious world we have seen people around us putting in some extra time 
for choosing the right pair of shoes the right handbag the right shirt etc. But the item that 
deserves the most importance which is often the most ignored garment in our wardrobes. The 
Innerwear Never have we thought how important it can be to choose the right size and style of 
innerwear that not only suits our body type but keeps us comfortable too.  According the 
greatest fashion designers of all times it is the right innerwear that can make or break an outfit.
             </div>
             <br/>
             <div>
              <h3 class="h312">
               slide 3:
              </h3>
              Fabric that cares 
Many fashion enthusiasts often compromise the comfort part while choosing the innerwear. All 
they want is to put their fashionable foot forward and they choose the fashion rich but low on 
comfort things. But one must not forget that our inner wear do enhances our confidence level.  
The inner wear which is our second skin do have influence and enhances our overall beauty.
             </div>
             <br/>
             <div>
              <h3 class="h312">
               slide 4:
              </h3>
              The first and foremost thing one needs to consider is the fabric of the inner wear. It should be 
something that suits your skin type. With advancement in the fashion industry many brands 
have come up with stretch fibre in their garments that give you enough room to breathe in and 
supports you at all the essential places.
             </div>
             <br/>
             <div>
              <h3 class="h312">
               slide 5:
              </h3>
              Comfy styling 
Now when we talk about comfortable clothing it is a general mind-set that comfortable clothes 
are not fashionable. Brands like Inviya is out with fashion fabrics keeping in mind the basic 
requirements of the inner wear.   Now choosing the right inner wear has been made easier for 
you rather than choosing the not so comfortable fashion brands one can opt for the comfortable 
yet fashionable inner wear. Remember not all innerwear is made for everyone.
             </div>
             <br/>
             <div>
              <h3 class="h312">
               slide 6:
              </h3>
              Next time before you buy innerwear there are certain things to be taken care of first and 
foremost knowing your body type. Then the material of the innerwear as it is one of the thing 
that will flatter your curves.  Spandex is one such material that is used to add comfort and 
adaptability of the garment. The garment made of this material always maintains its original 
shape so in a way it is durable.  It is also the next alternate to natural fibres.  
Choosing the right innerwear for different activities be it your office or your fitness classes can 
save you from the discomfort and many embarrassing moments.
