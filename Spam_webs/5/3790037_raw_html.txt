             <h3 class="title">
              <a href="/entries/gadgets/top-selfie-devices">
               Top Selfie Devices
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post_wrapper">
              <div class="date" title="1413974280000">
               October 22, 2014
              </div>
              <div class="content">
               <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span>
                 Internet and social media have established absolutely brand new communication rules for us. We do not talk too much face to face anymore, but, instead, we do make pictures all the time. Thus, these images become our personal ways of expressing ourselves  telling what we want, look and care for. The appearance of Selfie has become an absolutely logical part of our social-media-oriented life. Today, if you do not have a Selfie, you are just not in circulation. But what else can make a
                </span>
                <strong>
                 Selfie photo
                </strong>
                <span>
                 look better (except for your own face) than the Selfie brilliant and powerful device. See our suggestions for
                </span>
                <strong>
                 top Selfie devices
                </strong>
                <span>
                 and get one of them to stay in touch and in fashion according to the newest communication rules:
                 <!--more-->
                </span>
               </p>
               <ul style="text-align: justify;">
                <li dir="ltr">
                 <p dir="ltr">
                  <span>
                   <strong>
                    Huawei Ascend P7
                   </strong>
                   .
                  </span>
                  <span>
                   The Chinese mobile device producers are totally aware of what the Selfie lovers want and seek for  more and more pixels. They make any face look clearer and more attractive, so if you are in your height of making Selfies, get this stunning 8MPl snapper. Due to its 1080 p screen you can even see your wrinkles and pores, but who cares, when software such as Instagram can correct or erase them in a flash of a second.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                </li>
                <li dir="ltr">
                 <p dir="ltr">
                  <strong>
                   Samsung Galaxy Grand Prime
                  </strong>
                  <span>
                   . This recently announced mobile phone does not have only that complete pack of PC-styled options, but a great Selfie system, as well. Its 5PM front camera is what you need! Turn it at any point you want and shoot yourself in any position. Avoid poses like
                  </span>
                  <a href="http://www.cleaningcarpet-london.co.uk/carpet-cleaning.html" target="_blank">
                   <strong>
                    carpet cleaning
                   </strong>
                  </a>
                  <span>
                   or putting your lipstick, but try to be more original. This is a Selfie master phone after all!
                  </span>
                 </p>
                </li>
                <li dir="ltr">
                 <p dir="ltr">
                  <span>
                   <strong>
                    Nokia Lumia 735
                   </strong>
                   .
                  </span>
                  <span>
                   Most IT reviews and critics have determined this funny and fancy phone as the colourful Microsoft phone that is cheap and cheerful. We will add one other description  it is the most budget-friendly Selfie maker, which can also bring you lots of other interesting and innovative functions for entertainment.
                  </span>
                 </p>
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: justify;">
                <span>
                 So, what is your personal choice for some
                 <strong>
                  Selfie making
                 </strong>
                 ? Whatever you choose, keep in mind that your individual charm, but not a duck face makes the photo really original!
                </span>
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: justify;">
                <span>
                </span>
               </p>
