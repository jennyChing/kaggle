          Cambridge Sound Management Joins Interior Design's Health Care Giants Advisory Council

        <font color="">
         <br/>
         <br/>
         WALTHAM, Mass., Nov. 12, 2014 /PRNewswire/ --Cambridge Sound Management (CSM), a leading provider of sound masking solutions, today announced it has joined Interior Design's Health Care Giants Advisory Council. CSM is the first sound masking firm to join the council.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         "The top health care facility design firms make patient comfort and confidentiality a priority. To this end, sound masking solutions play a major role in the success of design projects in the industry," says David Sholkovitz, Director of Marketing for Cambridge Sound Management. "As a leader in the sound masking space, we are excited about this appointment and welcome the opportunity to continue to drive thought leadership around acoustics and sound masking within the health care facility design community."
         <br/>
        </font>

        <font color="">
         <br/>
         Sound Masking is the addition of an unobtrusive background sound to an environment, similar to the sound of airflow, that reduces the intelligibility of human speech and decreases distractions by "masking" other sounds in the affected environment. The resulting environment is more productive, private and comfortable. The technology can help hospitals increase HCAHPS scores and reduce HIPAA violations, making it a popular solution for health care facility managers in recent years.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         CSM's introduction to the council will be kicked-off at Interior Design's Health Care Giants Celebration at the Fairweather in San Diego on November 16. The Company will also participate in numerous panel discussions and teleconferences for designers and architects throughout the year with the council.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         About Cambridge Sound Management
         <br/>
         Cambridge Sound Management, Inc. is the developer of Quiet Technology and the manufacturer of patented QtPro direct-field sound masking systems which help organizations across multiple industries protect speech privacy, and reduce distractions. Extremely cost-effective, QtPro systems are deployed in hundreds of millions of square feet and are trusted by over 40% of the Fortune 100. QtPro systems are extremely reliable and consume less than 27 watts of power per 72,000 square feet (6,689 m2) of space, are GreenSpec listed, and can contribute to LEED Certifications. For more information please visit

         <br/>
         <br/>
         About Interior Design
         <br/>
         Interior Designis the definitive resource for interior designers, architects, and other design professionals, featuring groundbreaking projects, innovative new products, real-time design industry news, exclusive in-depth research, and more. Offering print, digital and events,Interior Designis the design authority in the B2B design market. Interior Design'sHealthcare Giants advisory council is a year-long design community engagement program and think tank that brings together leaders in healthcare architecture and design to share, debate, and publish research and trends.
