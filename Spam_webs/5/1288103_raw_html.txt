        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         One Time I Secretly Visited the Set of Marvels Ant-Man
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-01-06T21:25:48+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 6, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://7onashoestring.com/author/mommy25/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Staci Salazar
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="pf-content">
         <p>
          <img alt="Disney disclosure" class="aligncenter wp-image-35344" height="45" src="http://7onashoestring.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Disney-disclosure.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <h4>
          So there was this one time, on the coldest day ever in Atlanta, that I was on a secret set visit. We were off the grid for a few days and sworn to secrecy. The big secret? ANT-MAN!
         </h4>
         <p>
          <img alt="Marvel AntMan" class="aligncenter wp-image-49718 size-full" height="1037" src="http://7onashoestring.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Marvel-AntMan-poster.jpg" title="Marvel AntMan" width="700"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          Ant-Man
         </h3>
         <p>
          Okay, so I am still a bit limited on what I can tell you, so lets just get right down to what I
          <em>
           can
          </em>
          share:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           I am not going to lie, one of the most exciting parts of the trip was getting to interview Scott Lang / Ant-Man himself, Mr.
           <strong>
            Paul Rudd
           </strong>
           . I can only say he is just as amazing and nice as everyone says he is!
          </li>
          <li>
           We sat and laughed and talked with
           <strong>
            Judy Greer
           </strong>
           . She is hilarious!
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Abby Ryder Fortson
           </strong>
           , who plays Scott Langs daughter in the movie, had her very 1st interview with us! Too cute for words!
          </li>
          <li>
           After switching shuttles and drivers and signing over our first born, we wereable to tour two sets in Atlanta for the film, as well as the costume department. This was crazy insane. So much fun!
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          And, yep. Thats about all I can say right now. The rest is under lock and key until we get the go-ahead. But its going to be good! You are going to want to watch this space for the rest of the news as soon as I can share.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Ant-Man secret set visit" class="aligncenter wp-image-49709 size-full" height="525" src="http://7onashoestring.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Ant-Man-secret-set-visit.jpg" title="Ant-Man secret set visit" width="700"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          For now, watch this human-size Ant-Man trailer and get ready!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <center>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="394" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/xInh3VhAWs8?rel=0&amp;controls=0&amp;showinfo=0" width="700">
          </iframe>
         </center>
         <p>
          Ant-Man comes to theaters everywhere on July 17th! I cannot wait!
         </p>
