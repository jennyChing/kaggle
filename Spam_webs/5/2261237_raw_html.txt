        <p>

        <p>
         When youre sick, the last thing you want to do is slave away in the kitchen. Why not plug in your
         <a href="http://www.coupons.com/thegoodstuff/?s=crockpot" target="_blank">
          slow cooker
         </a>
         and let it work for you? Toss in your ingredients and let it simmer away for hours.
         <a href="http://www.coupons.com/thegoodstuff/healthy-comfort-foods-5-fall-favorites-get-skinny/" target="_blank">
          Comfort foods
         </a>
         may be just what you need for some TLC, and your
         <a href="http://www.coupons.com/thegoodstuff/?s=slow+cooker" target="_blank">
          slow cooker
         </a>
         can churn out some soul-soothing recipeswith little effort on your part. Weve found six amazing crockpotcomfort foods to make when youre sick, and youll delight in lying on the couch in your jammies as the wonderful aromas waft through your house.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          1. Crockpot Sriracha Ramen
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Crockpot Sriracha Ramen" class="alignnone wp-image-69492" height="887" src="https://cdn.cpnscdn.com/static/blog/2015/02/Crockpot-Sriracha-Ramen.png" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #444444;">
          Find this exclusive to
         </span>
         <strong style="color: #444444;">
          <em>
           The Good Stuff
          </em>
         </strong>
         <span style="color: #444444;">
          recipe
          <a href="http://www.coupons.com/thegoodstuff/crockpot-sriracha-ramen-exclusive-recipe/" target="_blank">
           here
          </a>
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          2. Chicken Noodle Soup
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <img alt="Chicken Noodle Soup" class="alignnone wp-image-69075" height="371" src="https://cdn.cpnscdn.com/static/blog/2015/02/Chicken-Noodle-Soup.jpg" width="600"/>
          Source:
          <a href="http://kitchme.com/recipes/slow-cooker-chicken-noodle-soup" target="_blank">
           kitchme.com
          </a>
         </em>
        </p>
