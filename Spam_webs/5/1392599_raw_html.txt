       <p>
        The property measures 110 feet by 90 feet (33.52 meters by 27.43 meters) offering a total of 9900 square feet / 1100 yards / 919.4 meters. Please see the site plan of the property with the photos. The land is zoned for commercial or residential development and is in a prime location on the South side of Hopkins Village. The property has power and water Connected and benefits from a First Certificate of Title.
       </p>
       <p>
        The village itself is prospering and becoming a perfect Caribbean investment opportunity. Belize as a whole is developing and growing and the country proudly welcomes more and more visitors as well as new businesses and families moving in to the country. There are programs in place to assist with all legalities and procedures there are involved in order to move to Belize and the country also offers an excellent retirement program.
       </p>
       <p>
        The village is known as the safest place in all of Belize. You can walk the streets anywhere in the village alone at any time of day or night without a single worry, and it is only a 30 min boat ride to the reef for snorkeling and diving or floating about in the river and lagoons. The Barrier Reef is a Unesco World Heritage Site where you can dive and snorkel at hundreds of world class spots up and down the coast.
       </p>
       <p>
        the inland sites nearby include, the Cockscomb basin Jaguar sanctuary, The Mayflower Nature reserve, Gales point, Monkey river, Sittee River and much more. There is a vast network of lagoons on the North and the South of the village where there are crocodiles, iguanas and a wide variety of birds and wildlife in general.
       </p>
       <p>
        Belize has an International airport 3 hours drive away and another larger airport in construction which will offer direct flights to Europe as well as The USA and neighboring countries. Belize has paved roads the whole length of the country and Hopkins Village will soon benefit from a new road which is currently being built that connects the village to the Southern Highway. There are plans for further new roads in the Village.
       </p>
