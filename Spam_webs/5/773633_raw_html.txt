                  <h2 class="short-teaser">
                   We take a look back at the best iPod Touch games of 2010
                  </h2>
                  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                   <div class="field-items">
                    <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                     <script type="text/javascript">

&lt;div class="p402_premium"&gt;&lt;p&gt;  Having an iPod Touch is a lot like having an iPhone 4. Its got the same operating system, the same features and masses of storage so you can fill it with apps, music and, most importantly, games from Apples App Store. But what are the best games money can buy on the App Store?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Well, weve got plenty of experience with Apple gaming so instead of trawling the App Store, why not just check out our guide to the best iPod Touch games of 2010?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Angry Birds&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
 Angry Birds needs no introduction and is easily the biggest cross-&lt;a href="/glossary/platform" title=" A mobile platform is also described as an OS or operating system." class="lexicon-term"&gt;platform&lt;/a&gt; game of the past year  maybe even the decade! But if for some reason, you havent heard of Angry Birds, the premise of the game is simple: you have to catapult birds, which have varying special abilities, into buildings that are occupied by evil egg-stealing pigs. The aim of the game is to recover your stolen eggs. Thats it.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Did we mention that this is easily the most addictive game youre ever likely to play?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Cut the Rope&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
 Cut the Rope was Rovios follow-up to the universally popular Angry Birds and while it might not have enjoyed the same level of success and acclaim as its predecessor, its still a massively innovative and addictive game.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Like Angry Birds the premise is simple: you have to cut ropes in a certain way to deliver candy to a cute little critter. The physics of the game play are great and the actual game play is something that can be picked up by anyone. Needless to say, Cut the Rope and Angry Birds have secured Rovio a place in the gaming worlds hall of fame.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Game Dev Story&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
 Ever wondered what it would be like to run your own game development studio? Well, wonder no more this is exactly what you do in Game Dev Story. During the game you must assemble your crack team of developers, sound engineers, story writers and programmers and guide them all through to the finished article  your amazing game.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Sounds interesting, right? It is  plus, its also a lot of fun too.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;N.O.V.A 2&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
 N.O.V.A 2 (AKA: the Halo of the iPhone) is one hell of a first person shooter. Not only does it feature some of the best graphics weve seen to date on the iPod but its game play is staggering  and thats just the single player mode.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Take N.O.V.A 2 online and youre in a world of multiplayer awesomeness unlike anything youve experienced on a mobile device  youll see what we mean once you get involved. In fact, well probably see you in there.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;N.O.V.A 2 is one of the best games around and if youve got an iPod Touch and dont have it you are a very silly individual  this is definitely a must-have game.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Asphalt 6&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
 Asphalt 6: Adrenaline, like all good games, throws realism in the bin and opts, instead, for a face-melting speeds and insane racing. The main premise of the game is to build your car into an environment-killing monster and race the crap out of it across some of the coolest courses weve seen on an iPod-racer.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This is by far and away our personal favourite racing game currently available on the iOS platform  it leaves Real Racing and Need for Speed, quite literally, in its rear view mirror.&lt;/p&gt;
