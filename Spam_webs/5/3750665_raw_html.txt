     <p>
      <b>
       HD 76700 b
      </b>
      is an
      <a href="/wiki/Exoplanet" title="Exoplanet">
       exoplanet
      </a>
      discovered orbiting the star in 2002. The planet is a
      <a href="/wiki/Hot_Jupiter" title="Hot Jupiter">
       hot Jupiter
      </a>
      with a mass at least 0.233 times that of
      <a href="/wiki/Jupiter" title="Jupiter">
       Jupiter
      </a>
      . It orbits very close to its parent star and completes one orbit in less than four
      <a href="/wiki/Day" title="Day">
       days
      </a>
      .
