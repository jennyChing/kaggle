        <h2 class="date-header">
         May 11, 2010
        </h2>
        <div class="entry-category-fun entry-category-garnish entry-author-camper_english entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a00e553b3da2088340133ed71c849970b">
         <div class="entry-inner">
          <h3 class="entry-header">
           The Olive Centipede: The Frightening New Release in the Torture Garnish Genre
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p style="text-align: right;">
             <em>
              (this post is a joke about
              <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Human_Centipede_%28First_Sequence%29" target="_blank" title="human centipede cocktail garnish">
               this movie
              </a>
              )
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
             The Olive Centipede was created by Dr. Heiner, a disturbed German bartender formerly famous for his flair garnishing techniques. The evil Dr. Heiner decided to create a garnish centipede, made from sewing three olives together along the olives' digestive tracts, pit-to-pimento.
            </p>
            <div style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://alcademics.typepad.com/.a/6a00e553b3da2088340133ed71b729970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Olivecentipededrawing" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e553b3da2088340133ed71b729970b " src="http://alcademics.typepad.com/.a/6a00e553b3da2088340133ed71b729970b-500wi" style="width: 480px;"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
            </div>
            <p>
             Unfortunately for the olives, the surgery was a success. A hideous, hideous success.
            </p>
            <div style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://alcademics.typepad.com/.a/6a00e553b3da2088340133ed71b962970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Olivecentipede4s" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e553b3da2088340133ed71b962970b " src="http://alcademics.typepad.com/.a/6a00e553b3da2088340133ed71b962970b-500wi" style="width: 480px;"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
            </div>
            <p>
             The poor olives tried to escape Dr. Heiner's laboratory/ultra-lounge.
            </p>
            <div style="text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://alcademics.typepad.com/.a/6a00e553b3da208834013480a54d51970c-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Olivecentipede3s" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00e553b3da208834013480a54d51970c " src="http://alcademics.typepad.com/.a/6a00e553b3da208834013480a54d51970c-500wi" style="width: 480px;"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
            </div>
            <p>
             But were unsuccessful.
            </p>
