         #BudgetingDivas: Why I Love The Ibotta App
        </h1>

        <p>
         I found out about the
         <a href="https://www.ibotta.com/r/fded9w" target="_blank" title="Ibotta">
          Ibotta app
         </a>
         about a year ago, but have recently begun to utilize it regularly as part of my frugal shopping for groceries and personal items.I love this app!
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          What is Ibotta?
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.ibotta.com/r/fded9w" target="_blank" title="Ibotta">
          Ibotta
         </a>
         is a free mobile application that you can download on your smartphone or tablet.
        </p>
        <p>
         You can find rebates on grocery items, restaurants, apparel, health &amp; beauty products, electronics, pets, home improvement and babies &amp; kids! Ibotta works in over 80 stores. To unlock the rebates you will need to do simple tasks like reading fun facts, watching brief videos and occasionally posting to your social media outlets.
        </p>
        <p>
         Once you purchase the featured products, you verify your purchases by scanning the barcode and uploading a copy of your receipt. Ca-ching! Money is added to your account and once you reach at least $10  you can either transfer the cash to your Pay Pal or Venmo account or redeem for gift cards to stores like Starbucks, Regal Cinemas, iTunes and more!
        </p>

         <img alt="Why I Love Shopping and Earning Cash With The Ibotta App #BudgetingDivas" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8085" height="800" src="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Ibotta-App.jpg" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          Ibotta Bonuses
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         <em>
          Team Bonus
         </em>
         : By building a team, you can earn additional bonuses based on the amount of your rebates and your teams total rebates.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          How do you find team members?
         </em>
         Connect your social media accounts. Facebook friends that are already on Ibotta will be added to your team. Share your link with your friends and family and theyll be added to your team. Currently, for each person you refer to Ibotta,
         <a href="https://www.ibotta.com/r/fded9w" target="_blank" title="Ibotta">
          you can earn an additional $5 when they join and redeem their first rebate
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Special Purchases
         </em>
         : You can, also, earn additional bonuses by purchasing certain products and completing tasks. Therell be a trophy icon next to the items that qualify for these bonuses.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.eepurl.com/QDAJv" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Budgeting Divas Newsletter" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8087" height="211" src="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Budgeting-Divas-Newsletter.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.couponaholic.net/tag/ibotta-offers/" target="_blank">
          Couponaholic shares savings and match-ups you can use to maximize your Ibotta earnings!
         </a>
        </h4>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          How I Incorporate Ibotta Into My Shopping
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         With my shopping, I strive not to purchase products that I will not use. I generally dont believe in spending money to save money. I do, however, believe in stocking up on items when they are on sale and I can get a great deal with cash back, rebates, etc. on them.
        </p>
        <p>
         I do spend some time planning out my shopping trips. I usually go shopping with my children and incorporate them into our shopping trip. My three year old counts the items in the shopping cart  mainly because she loves to do it, it helps her with her counting skills and encourages her to stay in the cart rather than walk / run around the store. My six and three year old share the duty of scanning our items if were using mobile apps to get additional savings. My thirteen year old is responsible for double-checking the list and matching any paper coupons we may have.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="https://www.ibotta.com/r/fded9w" target="_blank" title="Ibotta">
          <em>
           <strong>
            Grab the Ibotta App by clicking here.
           </strong>
          </em>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         When we get home, as were putting up our groceries, were scanning and confirming our rebates on our apps and quickly submitting a copy of our receipt. Typically within 24 hours the rebates are confirmed and added to my account. Yes  its really that easy!
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          My Total Ibotta Rebates By Month
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         Are you
         <a href="http://www.instagram.com/divawithapurpose" target="_blank" title="Divas With A Purpose on Instagram">
          connected with me on Instagram
         </a>
         ? I share my Ibotta purchases and rebates on thereas I earn them, too.
