         <h3 class="entry-header">
          <a href="http://basicjuice.blogs.com/basicjuice/2006/09/wine_lent_in_se.html">
           (wine) Lent in September
          </a>
         </h3>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <div class="entry-body">
           <p>
            Who's up for a month's-worth of wine
            <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lent">
             lent
            </a>
            ? From 9/13 to 10/13 I challenge all you pious wine lovers out
            <a href="http://basicjuice.blogs.com/.shared/image.html?/photos/uncategorized/lent.jpg" onclick="window.open(this.href, '_blank', 'width=300,height=400,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0'); return false">
             <img alt="Lent" border="0" height="140" src="http://basicjuice.blogs.com/basicjuice/images/lent.jpg" style="margin: 0px 0px 5px 5px; float: right;" title="Lent" width="105"/>
            </a>
            there to abstain from:
           </p>
           <p>
            Pinot Noir
            <br/>
            Merlot
            <br/>
            Cabernet Sauvignon
            <br/>
            Zinfandel
            <br/>
            Syrah
            <br/>
            Riesling
            <br/>
            Sauvignon Blanc
            <br/>
            Chardonnay
            <br/>
            Pinot Gris/Grigio
            <br/>
            Viognier
           </p>
           <p>
            Still with me? Great. By 'abstain from' I mean any of the varietals listed above cannot comprise more than 50% of your selected wine. So, for example, you could drink a Rhone blend from California that contained 30% Carignane, 25% Mourvedre and 45% Zin and Syrah. However, a white with 60% Sauvignon Blanc and 40% Semillon would be right out (sinner!).
           </p>
           <p>
            The point of this little exercise is to expose ourselves to as many lesser-known wines as possible. If you're down with me, drop a comment and keep us all informed as to what you discover over the course of the month. I'm looking forward to surprising discoveries!
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             [update]
            </strong>
            A few grape varietals to seek out during your lenting
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Carbernet Franc
            </li>
            <li>
             Carignane
            </li>
            <li>
             Carmenere
            </li>
            <li>
             Cinsault
            </li>
            <li>
             Nebbiolo
            </li>
            <li>
             Pinot Meunier
            </li>
            <li>
             Rousanne
            </li>
            <li>
             Marsanne
            </li>
            <li>
             Muscat
            </li>
            <li>
             Furmint
            </li>
            <li>
             Pinot Blanc
            </li>
            <li>
             Pedro Ximenez (it
             <a href="http://basicjuice.blogs.com/basicjuice/2006/03/drink_pedro.html">
              can be found
             </a>
             as a dry white!)
            </li>
            <li>
             &amp; many, many, more
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            Get searching!
           </p>
