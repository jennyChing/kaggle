            <h1 itemprop="name headline">
             Ban on Gay Conversion Therapy Clears N.Y. State Assembly
            </h1>
            <div class="node-teaser" itemprop="about description">
             If approved by the State Senate, the ban would prevent health care professionals from attempting to change the sexual orientation of minors.
            </div>
            <h4 class="clearfix">
             <span class="author">
              <span>
               BY
              </span>
              <span itemprop="author">
               Daniel Reynolds
              </span>
             </span>
            </h4>
            <h3>
             <span itemprop="datePublished">
              June 16 2014 5:26 PM ET
             </span>
            </h3>
            <div class="addthis_top">
             <div class="clearfix">
             </div>
             <div class="addthis_sharing_toolbox">
             </div>
             <a class="print-page" href="/print/politics/2014/06/16/ban-gay-conversion-therapy-clears-ny-state-assembly" onclick="window.open(this.href); return false" rel="nofollow" title="Display a printer-friendly version of this page.">
              <img src="/sites/advocate.com/themes/advocate/images/icon-print.jpg"/>
             </a>
             <a class="addthis_button_email">
              <img src="/sites/advocate.com/themes/advocate/images/icon-email.jpg"/>
             </a>
            </div>
            <div class="body cunlock_main_content">
             <div class="image" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
              <img alt="" itemprop="thumbnailUrl image contentUrl" src="http://www.advocate.com/sites/advocate.com/files/imagecache/stories/NY_ASSEMBYX390_2_0.jpg" title=""/>
             </div>
             <span itemprop="articleBody">
              <p>
               The New York State Assembly passed a ban Monday on the use of gay conversion therapy for minors.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://www.buzzfeed.com/tonymerevick/new-york-state-assembly-passes-ban-on-conversion-therapy-for" target="_blank">
                <em>
                 Buzzfeed
                </em>
                reports
               </a>
               that Assembly members voted 86-28 in favor of a bill that would prohibit state-licensed health care professionals from trying to change the sexual orientation of clients under the age of 18.
              </p>
              <p>
               The legislation, which would add language to an existing law on professional misconduct, awaits a vote by the State Senate, which may take it up this week, as the legislative session ends Thursday.
              </p>
              <p>
               The vote was applauded by Sen. Brad Hoylman, a gay politician and a cosponsor of the bill.
              </p>
              <p>
               Being an LGBT young person isnt an illness that needs to be cured, he said. Banning conversion therapy is a nonpartisan issue that should receive full consideration by my colleagues in the Senate.
              </p>
              <p>
               California and New Jersey have similar laws.
              </p>
             </span>
