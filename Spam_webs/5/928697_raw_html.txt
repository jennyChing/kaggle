        <p style="text-align: center">
         The video posted below shows a very incredible moment bus passengers cheated death as their vehicle was swallowed by a giant crater and spat out into a raging river. The incident is said to have has happened on the Trans-Amazonian highway in northern Brazil which is currently experiencing some of the worst floods ever in the history of the country.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         The passengers were led off the bus in the nick of time as the ground collapsed beneath their transport. One woman was filmed being helped from the sinking vehicle with a baby in her arms seconds before it plunged into the river on Monday. The vehicle was carried underneath what was left of the road by the rushing water just moments after the last of the passengers managed to make it to safety.
        </p>

        <p style="text-align: center">
         Travel on the non-paved stretches of the road is notoriously difficult during the regions rainy season between October and March and cars often get stuck in the poorly-constructed highways.
         <br/>
         The stretch of the road where the minibus was carried away is between the cities of Itaituba and Ruropolis in the state of Para.
         <br/>
         The Brazilian government declared a state of emergency in the area earlier this month and firefighters have been called out to rescue dozens of poisonous snakes which have entered peoples homes to escape the rising water levels.
