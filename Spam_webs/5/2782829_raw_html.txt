        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://worldtruth.tv/the-evidence-based-healing-properties-of-13-common-fruits/attachment/201209200940/" rel="attachment wp-att-20656">

        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Fruit is not only enjoyable to eat  as it should be, considering the very word fruit stems from the Latin word
         <em>
          frui
         </em>
         , meaning to enjoy, use  but it also nourishes and protects the body with powerful, built-in medicinal activity. Fruits are by design a perfect food, intended to entice animals to consume them in order to help disseminate their seeds, for instance. This means that unlike
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.greenmedinfo.com/blog/going-against-grain-towards-better-health" target="_blank">
           grains
          </a>
         </strong>
         , and other lectin- and anti-nutrient-rich organisms, e.g.
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.greenmedinfo.com/page/opening-pandoras-bread-box-critical-role-wheat-lectin-human-disease" target="_blank">
           wheat
          </a>
         </strong>
         , tomato, beans, we humans have chosen to make into our food, fruits are less likely to come equipped with invisible thorns, as they benefit as much in being eaten as we do in eating them. Also, like our now hard-wired
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.greenmedinfo.com/blog/200-reasons-love-vitamin-c" target="_blank">
           biological dependence on obtaining vitamin C
          </a>
         </strong>
         from external sources (unlike most animals we can not produce it from glucose), countless millennia of fruit consumption has left our genetic infrastructure in need of continual resupply of many of the key vitamins and phytocompounds they contain copious quantities of.
        </p>
        <p>
