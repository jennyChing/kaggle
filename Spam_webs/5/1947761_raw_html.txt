           <span style="font-family: 'times new roman', times; font-size: 14pt;">
            <strong>
             Welcome to the Thomas More Law Center
            </strong>
           </span>
          </h4>
          <div style="text-align: justify;line-height:20px;">
           <span style="font-family: 'times new roman', times; font-size: 12pt;">
            Renowned as a national nonprofit public interest law firm, based in Ann Arbor, Michigan, the Thomas More Law Centers mission is to restore and defend Americas Judeo-Christian heritage and moral values, and to preserve a strong national defense, and a free and sovereign United States of America. In courtrooms throughout our Nation, Law Center lawyers fight for the religious freedom of Christians, time-honored family values, the sanctity of human life, and a strong national defense. The Law Center does not charge for its legal services, and relies on tax-deductible donations from concerned patriotic Americans and charitable foundations.
           </span>
