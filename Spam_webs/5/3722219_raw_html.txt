        <p>
         This month has been a very busy month. We have been waiting to hear if we are going to move next month, sadly attending a funeral and enjoying the company of other bloggers at the
         <a href="http://mytwomums.com/winner/" target="_blank" title="And the winner is.">
          Mad Blog Awards
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         We were very lucky to be invited out to lunch this weekend, with my parents and grandparents. M was such a good boy during dinner and then amazed us afterwards, by climbing up the slide without any fear.
        </p>
        <p>
         Weve been trying to make sure we capture our time we spend as a family. So snapped this picture, just as we were leaving the restaurant.
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/mytwomums/10005224874/" title="Casual_me_and_mine_september by My Two Mums, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Casual_me_and_mine_september" class="aligncenter" height="400" src="http://farm4.staticflickr.com/3831/10005224874_eb11144282_o.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Because we didnt get to take our little man to the Mad Blog Awards, we decided to get dressed up and capture some family time looking smart.
        </p>
        <p>
         It was so much fun taking these pictures. M was not playing ball at all. He wasnt sure why we had dressed up all smart, an hour before bed. It was really lovely to get a picture we can look back on and smile, knowing what happened to get the picture. Lots of shaking of keys, bribing with a phone was involved.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/mytwomums/10005348715/" title="Family shot by My Two Mums, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Family shot" class="aligncenter" height="424" src="http://farm8.staticflickr.com/7336/10005348715_b4c7477bae_o.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I think we scrub up well, M looks adorable and my wife is simply stunning. It would be great if next years photo was just as smart.
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/mytwomums/10005247825/" title="Me_and_mine_September by My Two Mums, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Me_and_mine_September" class="aligncenter" height="600" src="http://farm6.staticflickr.com/5332/10005247825_3146a3bb12_o.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         We are so thankful for the linky by Lucy from
         <a href="http://www.dearbeautifulboy.com/" target="_blank">
          Dear Beautiful
         </a>
         , which constantly inspires us to take a family photo each month. Thanks to her and the bloggers who join in, we have a lovely set of family memories to look back at each month. Please go check it out and join in.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
