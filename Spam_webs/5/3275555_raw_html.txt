           <h3 class="title">
            Great Video Resources for New YAL Recruits
           </h3>
           <div class="arrow">
           </div>

            <div class="node-content">
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <object data="http://www.youtube.com/v/TJIMqwJI2uI" height="350" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="425">
               <param name="src" value="http://www.youtube.com/v/TJIMqwJI2uI">
               </param>
              </object>
             </p>
             <p>
              D.C based lawyer and author
              <a href="http://jhhuebert.com/">
               Jacob Huebert
              </a>
              has released a relatively new video explaining the concept of legal plunder in a very entertaining way. The new video is second in a series based on the basic tenets of the liberty philosophy. The expanded edition of Huebert's first video (
              <a href="http://www.yaliberty.org/posts/new-video-on-the-philosophy-of-liberty-who-owns-you">
               previously mentioned on the YAL blog
              </a>
              ) is great as well.
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: center;">
              <object data="http://www.youtube.com/v/AQ5OSwJdvjk&amp;feature" height="350" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="425">
               <param name="src" value="http://www.youtube.com/v/AQ5OSwJdvjk&amp;feature">
               </param>
              </object>
             </p>
             <p>
              These videos are a great resource for YAL members to use with new recruits in the upcoming semester!
             </p>
