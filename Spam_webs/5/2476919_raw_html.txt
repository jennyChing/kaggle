        <p>
         It got really cold here the week before Thanksgiving. I decided to whip up one of my favorite meals that my mom used to make,
         <strong>
          Pizza Casserole with Hormel Pepperoni.
         </strong>
         This meal was a family favorite growing up. It is quick and easy and was always my moms go to meal for busy weeknights. And because its warm and a comfort food I love it on chilly winter nights.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lovepastatoolbelt.com/wp-content/uploads/Pizza-Casserole7-682x10241.jpg">

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Last week I decided to make this pizza casserole recipe because it was a busy week. We were heading out of town on Wednesday and I had a lot of blog work to do, plus packing. It was freezing cold outside and this meal is so warm and perfect for these cold winter days! I headed out to the store to pick up Original
         <a href="http://hormel.com/Brands/Pepperoni.aspx" rel="nofollow">
          Hormel Pepperoni
         </a>
         to include in my recipe! Right now you can save $1.00 on the purchase of any 2 Hormel Pepperoni packages with this
         <a href="http://www.hormel.com/Coupons.aspx" rel="nofollow">
          coupon
         </a>
         .
        </p>

           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 lb. ground beef
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup onion, chopped
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 package spaghetti sauce mix
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             8 oz. sour cream
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 (8oz) package of mozzarella cheese
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 8 oz. can crescent rolls
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             Original Hormel Pepperoni
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Brown ground beef and onion
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Mix together ground beef and onion with tomato sauce and spaghetti sauce mix
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Spread ground beef mixture in bottom of a greased 9x13 pan
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cover ground beef mixture with slices of mozzarella cheese
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cover with the sour cream
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Lay on pepperoni, as many slices as you want
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cover with crescent rolls
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Bake at 375 for 20 minutes
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-8" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             If desired add additional pepperoni slices to the top of the casserole
            </li>
           </ol>
