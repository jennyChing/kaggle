      <h1 class="singlePageTitle">
       Birds In Trouble Game App  iOS
      </h1>
      <p>
       <a href="http://theappmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/birds.png">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-thumbnail wp-image-15472" height="150" src="http://theappmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/birds-150x150.png" title="Birds In Trouble Game App - iOS" width="150"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        App Description:
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span>
        Birds In Trouble, headstrong and undisputed unique!
       </span>
       <span>
        Embark on a wonderful journey through bizarre worlds.
       </span>
       <span>
        Fly through swamps and soar through the dusk of the woods, but beware from the malicious spirits!
       </span>
       <span>
        Use your skill, collect gold coins and diamonds and unlock undiscovered worlds free.
       </span>
       <span>
        Reach you also set a new record!
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Highlights:
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span>
         Endless Flight pleasure with very cute birds
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span>
         Moody atmosphere that a dream is moving
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span>
         Unique, hand-painted characters and worlds
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span>
         Simple but challenging One-Button Game
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span>
         Other unlockable characters
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span>
         Optimized for the iPhone 5
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        The App Magazine Overall Rating:
       </strong>
       5/5 Stars
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        App Store Download Link:
        <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/de/app/birds-in-trouble/id942087723?mt=8" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://itunes.apple.com']);">

         https://www.facebook.com/FlashRomances
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Category:
       </strong>
       Games
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Requirements:
       </strong>
       Available for the iPhone, iPad and iPod. Requires iOS 5.1 or later.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Developer:
       </strong>
       Tobias Folprecht
      </p>
      <p>

        Price:

       FREE!
