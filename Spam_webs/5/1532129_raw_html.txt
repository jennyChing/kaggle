                     <h2>
                      <a href="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/signs_your_man-is_using_you.jpg">
                       <img alt="A couple in a bad relationship" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1485" height="450" src="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/signs_your_man-is_using_you.jpg" width="600"/>
                      </a>
                     </h2>
                     <h2>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        We all would like to experience beautiful love, but the person we are with is not always the right one. Maybe you havent realized yet that the guy you love is actually not that into you; and most despicably he is using and taking advantage of you.
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h2>
                     <h4>
                      A person may start a relationship with you just for some benefits from you  material, sexual, emotional, financial. He may do it subtly so that you have no idea he is freeloading on you. Or, you have already noticed that something is not quite right but you cant define it. Here are a few signs that might prove be the case.
                     </h4>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        1.
                        <b>
                         He is only around you when he needs you.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      He only contacts you when he needs something from you  he needs your help or he just doesnt want to have dinner alone. However, when he isnt in need of anything that you can provide, you can rarely hear from him.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      If he cares about you, he will give take your phone calls or messages no matter hes busy or not. He wont think of you only when he has nothing to do or he has to ask for your help.
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                      <a href="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/sex.jpg">
                       <img alt="All about Sex" class="alignright wp-image-1490" height="241" src="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/sex.jpg" width="161"/>
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        2.
                        <b>
                         He loves your body more than your mind.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      Every time hes with you, he thinks all about sex. You will notice that he has little interest in watching a movie with you, cooking with you or even chatting with you; he only wants to spend time with you in bed.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      <strong>
                       As I said
                       <a href="http://inondate.ie/relationships/how-to-end-a-bad-relationship/" title="How to recognize an unhealthy relationship and the best ways to end it">
                        in my last post
                       </a>
                       , sex is an important part in a relationship but not all of it. A man who is only interested in your body is definitely not that into you; maybe he just desires a sex partner.
                      </strong>
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        3.
                        <b>
                         He has an ambiguous relationship with his ex.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      If you find that he spends lots of time with his ex, you should be careful. It can be understandable that they became friends after breaking up, but too frequent contacts could mean that he cant forget her or he regrets breaking up with her.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      He will keep a certain distance with her if he is serious about you. Perhaps he just attempts to walk away from his last relationship by starting a new one.
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      <a href="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/HowPastorsGetRich_main.jpg">
                       <img alt="A man loves money" class="alignright wp-image-1502" height="232" src="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/HowPastorsGetRich_main.jpg" width="173"/>
                      </a>
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        4.
                        <b>
                         He relies on you for his financial needs.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      Lets suppose he earns less than you. You will realize that you spend much more money than he does  you pay for every dinner, you pay for the house rent, etc. And he makes various excuses to get what he needs such as asking for your sponsorship.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      It seems he has suddenly solved his financial problems since he met you. You should consider the possibility that he likes only your money.
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        5.
                        <b>
                         He refuses to talk about the future.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      Does he try to change the subject when you bring up the topic of your future? If you find that he had never made any plans for your future or he avoids telling you anything about his future plans, you can be fairly sure that he is not willing to maintain the relationship in the long term.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      He will let you know what hes planning for the future if he believes you are the right person; and not surprisingly, you will be included in it. If he doesnt have a long-term plan with you, he may just want to take the advantage of you for a while.
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                     </h3>
                     <h3>
                      <a href="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/89794742.jpg">
                       <img alt="Man with Secrets" class=" wp-image-1478 alignright" height="230" src="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/89794742.jpg" width="171"/>
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        6.
                        <b>
                         He has many secrets.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      Does he always become vague when talking about certain things? Does he avoid picking up phone calls or reading emails when you are around? If you feel that he doesnt openly talk with you and he obviously hides something from you, you should reconsider your relationship.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      He wont keep secrets from you if he trusts you and expects a long-term relationship with you. There must be a reason behind his mysteriousness.
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        7.
                        <b>
                         He always makes excuses.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      He forgot your birthday because he was on a business trip. He get a gift for you because he didnt get time. He cant celebrate Valentines Day with you because he has another priority. He always has excuses and the biggest one is that hes busy.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      If a man loves you with all his heart, he wont ignore you no matter how busy he is. Too many excuses mean hes not into you even if he hasnt planned to break up with you yet.
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        8.
                        <b>
                         He is prone to cancelling dates.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      Does he always cancel your date in the last minute? Would he rather go out with his friends than with you? Its not normal that he constantly turns down your dates and he prefers to be with some else rather than you.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      If you feel that he seems to avoid spending time with you, you should question his motives for being with you.
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                      <a href="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/unhappy-couple-in-bed-18_.jpg">
                       <img alt="An unhappy Couple" class="alignright wp-image-1497" height="243" src="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/unhappy-couple-in-bed-18_.jpg" width="173"/>
                      </a>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        9.
                       </strong>
                       <b>
                       </b>
                       <b>
                        He doesnt care about your feelings.
                       </b>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      He is completely indifferent when you tell him that certain things make you sad or angry. And he never apologizes to you or comforts you after fighting no matter how depressed you are. The only time he will try to make you happy is that he has something he needs help for.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      He is definitely using you! He will be considerate of your feelings if he is afraid of losing you. But he is only afraid of losing your help.
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        10.
                        <b>
                         He rarely shows you affection.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      Its not easy for a man who is not in love with you to show you a lot affection and emotions. Maybe he can pretend for a while, but he will lose the pretense of passion as time passes. Actually you can even sense that something is wrong from hugs, kisses and sex
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      Less and less affection is a warning sign that he is not into you but he just needs you. Just trust your own feelings!
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                      <a href="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/broken-heart-saidaonline_1.png">
                       <img alt="A Cartoon of a girl with a broken heart" class="alignright wp-image-1500" height="249" src="http://inondate.ie/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/broken-heart-saidaonline_1.png" width="166"/>
                      </a>
                     </h3>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        11.
                        <b>
                         He rarely does anything special for you.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      You always prepare gifts for him on his birthday and Valentines Day, and you always plan a surprise for him. However, you have never received any gifts nor nices surprises from him. You can delude yourself that hes not a romantic person, but maybe it means hes not that into you.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      He will attempt to do anything he can to make you happy if he loves you by heart.
                     </p>
                     <blockquote>
                      <p>
                       Dont believe that a man who does nothing for you cares for you  thats bullshit!
                      </p>
                     </blockquote>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        12.
                        <b>
                         He never invites you to join his friends.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      He never brings you out with his friends, or invites you to any social events involving his friends, which means he may not want his friends to know you. You will find that he doesnt expect you to be in touch with any of his friends.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      He will introduce you to his friends if he is comfortable with this relationship. Otherwise, he will separate you from his friends and social life.
                     </p>
                     <h3>
                      <span style="color: #cc0000;">
                       <strong>
                        13.
                        <b>
                         He has no interest in meeting your friends.
                        </b>
                       </strong>
                      </span>
                     </h3>
                     <p>
                      He doesnt want to meet
                      <em>
                       your
                      </em>
                      close friends, which means he may not expect to be involved in any aspect of your life. Its a sign that he avoids having too much intersection with your social sphere so that one day he can easily walk away from your world.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      If he considers you as an important person to him, he will desire to know more people who are important to you. The opposite behaviour could suggest he has no intention to be involved in your life.
                     </p>
                     <p>
                      <strong>
                       For goodness sake, you should leave the guy who is using you ASAP no matter how much you love him and how sad you are. He doesnt deserve your love! And more importantly, you should learn to say no to others  dont allow anyone to simply take advantage of you.
                      </strong>
                     </p>
