        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Want to know how to faux stain a window with Mod podge and Rit dye?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Experiment # 3.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          If you missed my Experiment with rit dye and mod podge on glass
         </strong>
        </p>
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/2013/06/mixing-mod-podge-and-rit-dye-then-painting-on-glass.html" title="Mixing mod podge and rit dye then painting on glass">
           #1
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/2013/07/mixing-mod-podge-and-rit-dye-project-2.html" title="Mixing mod podge and rit dye project #2">
           #2
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h1>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          You may want to catch up first.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Here is the Before.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <div align="center" style="padding-left:5px; padding-right:5px; padding-bottom:5px; padding-top:5px; margin-left:auto; margin-right:auto; ">
         <script type="text/javascript">

        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Certainly too plain for my liking..
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/rit-dye-experiment-3.jpg">
          <img alt="rit dye experiment #3" class="aligncenter wp-image-15168 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/rit-dye-experiment-3.jpg" data-pin-url="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/2013/07/how-to-faux-stain-a-window-with-mod-podge-and-rit-dye.html" height="778" src="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/rit-dye-experiment-3.jpg" width="474"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          I started off with this old window, that a friend of mine actually picked up for me at an antique mall.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          A few weeks back
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          I shared another one I had picked up.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <a href="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/2013/04/how-to-make-an-old-window-into-a-chalkboard-work-of-art.html" title="How to make an old window into a chalkboard work of art">
           re-purposed old window project that I turned into a chalkboard piece of art.
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-004.jpg">
          <img alt="faux stained glass window with rit dye and mod podge 004" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15172 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-004.jpg" data-pin-url="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/2013/07/how-to-faux-stain-a-window-with-mod-podge-and-rit-dye.html" height="467" src="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-004.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          So I mixed all my colors in individual cups with rit dye and mod podge.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          I used the matte finish as I did not want it glossy. All a personal preference.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          There is no secret formula to the mixing ,
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          it is all trial and error more or less to the color you want to achieve.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          More rit, deeper color, less rit, lighter color.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          I only put a bit of mod podge with it per window pane. You really do not need that much.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-001.jpg">
          <img alt="faux stained glass window with rit dye and mod podge 001" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15169 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-001.jpg" data-pin-url="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/2013/07/how-to-faux-stain-a-window-with-mod-podge-and-rit-dye.html" height="467" src="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-001.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          You can see the craft brushes I used here.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-003.jpg">
          <img alt="faux stained glass window with rit dye and mod podge 003" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15171 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-003.jpg" data-pin-url="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/2013/07/how-to-faux-stain-a-window-with-mod-podge-and-rit-dye.html" height="467" src="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-003.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          After I painted each pane,
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          I went back with a paper towel
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          and before the rit and mod podge actually dried I dabbed the entire pane.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          I wanted it to look textured and more like stain glass obviously.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          I went through a lot of paper towels.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Oh and I also have about three coats total of my concoction.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          I let each coat somewhat dry, dab and re applied.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-002.jpg">
          <img alt="faux stained glass window with rit dye and mod podge 002" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15170 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-002.jpg" data-pin-url="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/2013/07/how-to-faux-stain-a-window-with-mod-podge-and-rit-dye.html" height="500" src="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-002.jpg" width="750"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Here is a close up of what it looks like as it is drying.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          I cleaned up the edges even though this is the back, I still dont want it to look sloppy.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-014.jpg">
          <img alt="faux stained glass window with rit dye and mod podge 014" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15173 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-014.jpg" data-pin-url="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/2013/07/how-to-faux-stain-a-window-with-mod-podge-and-rit-dye.html" height="467" src="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-014.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          And here you can see it completely dried and what it looks like from the front.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Pretty cool huh?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-025.jpg">
          <img alt="faux stained glass window with rit dye and mod podge 025" class="aligncenter wp-image-15175 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-025.jpg" data-pin-url="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/2013/07/how-to-faux-stain-a-window-with-mod-podge-and-rit-dye.html" height="841" src="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-025.jpg" width="560"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          And here you have the end result.
         </strong>
        </p>

          Pretty simple, cheap and fun!

          Certainly is one of kind.

          <img alt="faux stained glass window with rit dye and mod podge 022" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15174 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-022.jpg" data-pin-url="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/2013/07/how-to-faux-stain-a-window-with-mod-podge-and-rit-dye.html" height="456" src="http://debbie-debbiedoos.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/faux-stained-glass-window-with-rit-dye-and-mod-podge-022.jpg" width="684"/>
