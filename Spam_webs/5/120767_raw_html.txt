       <p>
        4/29/15 3:42PM
       </p>
       <p>
        This precipitated from a genuine self-evaluation given by the lovely Blazes employer.  Blaze, while great at many a thing, is godawful at accurate (or positive) self-assessment. I proposed that hers and every such evaluation include perforce a question like our last.  Then I realized its paradoxical nature.
       </p>
       <p>
        I approached Will hoping the paradox could stand on its own two comedic legs, but those legs rendered a bunch of other comedic legs until we had a
        <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ooFSFR2s7Ig" target="_blank">
         centipede
        </a>
        of assessment lambasting.  We dissected the juiciest segments, sewed them back together, and sent a comedic dodecapede (or so) back your way.
       </p>
       <p>
        If anyone would care to use our creation at their place of employment, Im sure we could throw together a version without responses.  Id rate its utility right around that of any self-respecting self-assessment.
       </p>
       <p>
        Nipples Out,
        <br/>
        Jesse
       </p>
