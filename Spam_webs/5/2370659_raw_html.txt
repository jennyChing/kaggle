        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Ancient Grains Strawberry Parfait
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-02-06T19:59:55+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          February 6, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://creativesouthernhome.com/author/blogging" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Kallee
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Disclosure: The information, products and Publix gift cards were provided by General Mills through Linqia.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://creativesouthernhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/ancient-grains-2.jpg">
          <img alt="ancient grains 2" class="aligncenter wp-image-2879" height="823" src="http://creativesouthernhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/ancient-grains-2.jpg" width="598"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         For #FitnessFriday today I am sharing with you a great Heart Healthy snack. General Mills has some new items that are Heart friendly and taste great so your family will love them. The new products are:
         <a href="http://ooh.li/0aa4283" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Cheerios +Ancient Grains,
         </a>
         <a href="http://ooh.li/a1f4954" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Gluten Free Chex Granola Mix
         </a>
         , and
         <a href="http://ooh.li/e74c853" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Nature Valley Nut Crisp Bars
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         I was very excited to try new
         <a href="http://ooh.li/0aa4283" target="_blank">
          Cheerios +Ancient Grains
         </a>
         . I love Cheerios anyway, so I was intrigued to find out what these new grains would taste like. Cheerios +Ancient Grains has only 5 grams of sugar which I love.
