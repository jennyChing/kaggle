     <h1 class="post-title">
      Tips and Ideas for Bathroom Renovation
     </h1>
     <div class="post-entry clearfix">
      <p>
       <a href="http://smallhomefurniture.com/tips-and-ideas-for-bathroom-renovation/bathroom-renovation-on-budget/" title="Bathroom Renovation On Budget">
        <img alt=": Bathroom Renovation On Budget" height="500" src="http://smallhomefurniture.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/bathroom-renovation-on-budget-600x500.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Although bathroom only occupy small area in our house, it does not mean that it should not be decorated optimally. We use it every day and it becomes the important aspect in a house. It is not fun to have a boring look in the bathroom and maybe this is the time for having a renovation for that special room. There are some ideas and tips you can consider in order to make the bathroom get more aesthetic value, here they are.
       <br/>
       Floor and Wall
       <br/>
       What are they in stylish fixture? It is about the tiles, both wall and floor tiles. To have more colorful and playful bathroom, in addition to an easier maintenance, you can replace the flooring with the tiles. In choosing the companion, the wall tiles, both should be match in color and pattern. Match must not be the same color and pattern; they can be different but harmonious.
       <br/>
       Besides the tiles used in flooring, you can use other materials like marble flooring. Marble is known so long for the elegant look; therefore it can bring its elegant to the overall look of the bathroom. The most important thing for the marble flooring is the anti-slippery surface. To avoid constant renovation, choose a durable tilesnot only about the looks.
       <br/>
       Bathtub, Shower, and Sink
       <br/>
       If you still have the vanity cabinet inside the bathroom, you can replace it with pedestal sink for a more modern look. While a shower is a must, some people do not place a bath tub in the bathroom. Actually, it is a good idea to have one bath tub though it is not a big one: a 60-inch tub. Having bath tub allows you to have a quality and relax time for yourself, while admiring the interior look of your bathroom.
       <br/>
       Sink
       <br/>
       Commonly the mirror is hanged above the sink, but this idea suggests you to have a beautiful framed mirror to cover its surface. For the tap, you can replace the design and you can have a new look for the sink rather than replacing the whole sink.
      </p>
