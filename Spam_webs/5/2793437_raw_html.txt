        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Brand name carries a lot of weight when it comes to buying a luxury car. People always tend to identify the logo of the company when making purchase decision. Hence millions of buck is spend to build a superlative image to attract clients, so choosing cars on the sheer basis of brand is indeed the obvious choice of most. But studies have witnessed a different turn.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.firstamericanautoglass.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/cars-2014.jpg">
          <img alt="cars 2014" class="wp-image-238 aligncenter" height="453" src="http://www.firstamericanautoglass.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/cars-2014.jpg" title="best cars 2014" width="463"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The Annual Car Brand Perception survey of 2014 shows that Toyota, Ford, Honda, and Chevrolet are some of the top rank holders, claiming clear cut positions in customers lists of favorites. Its good news that these brands continue to retain their top position in the competitive market, but when considered in terms of once unknown brands like the Tesla moving up the rankings charts, this acts as a clear signal for top brands to gear up. This can pit serious competition for them in near future if nothings done soon to retain the fading value.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The most preferred- land rover, rolls-royce, jaguar now occupy distressing positions in the brand listing of cars. Many car expert reviewers claim that this is just a turn of events, but the question is with such great mileage and sturdiness how come these brands turn up in lowest category?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <em>
          <strong>
           So on what basis cars brands are categorized
          </strong>
         </em>
         <br/>
         The perception of best and worst car is based on quality, safety, performance, value, fuel economy, design/style and technology /innovation. It may so happen a distinguished brand that had held a prestigious ranking last year may come staggering down in the charts. Many branded cars in spite following the above attributes fail to impress consumers. Its all about marketing and proper pricing. The gap between the best and worst can go on increasing, or vice versa so generalities of any kind are always misleading. This is a precarious business with no definite rule.
        </p>
