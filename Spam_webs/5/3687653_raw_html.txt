       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Clink-Clink-Clink. A hint of celebration is in the air. Bubbly glasses, mirthy laughter, appropriate music and a promise of a great month  were beginning our month with a boozy treat. No. Wait. 15 different boozy cakes.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://bakerstreet.tv/2012/12/tiramisu-bundt-cake-with-kahlua-mascarpone-glaze/" rel="attachment wp-att-9391">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-9391" height="536" src="http://bakerstreet.tv/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Tiramisu-Bundt-Cake-with-Kahl%C3%BAa-Mascarpone-Glaze1-1024x894.jpg" title="Tiramisu Bundt Cake with Kahla Mascarpone Glaze" width="614"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        I baked a Tiramisu bundt cake with a Kahlua mascarpone glaze for our bundt-a-month theme  Boozy December. The cake captures thebeautifultraditional flavors of the Italian dessert.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://bakerstreet.tv/2012/12/tiramisu-bundt-cake-with-kahlua-mascarpone-glaze/" rel="attachment wp-att-9410">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-9410" height="455" src="http://bakerstreet.tv/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Tiramisu-Bundt-Cake-with-Kahl%C3%BAa-Mascarpone-Glaze3-1024x759.jpg" title="Tiramisu Bundt Cake with Kahla Mascarpone Glaze" width="614"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        You can distinctly taste the espresso layer that blends perfectly with the Kahlua.But the glaze on it  the ultimate treat  a sweet concoction of Kahlua, mascarpone and sugar. To bring this to the right consistency, I added a couple of teaspoons of milk. (Click
        <a href="http://bakerstreet.tv/2012/07/kahlua-mascarpone-frosting/" target="_blank" title="Kahla Mascarpone Frosting">
         <span>
          <em>
           here
          </em>
         </span>
        </a>
        for the recipe).
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://bakerstreet.tv/2012/12/tiramisu-bundt-cake-with-kahlua-mascarpone-glaze/" rel="attachment wp-att-9387">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-9387" height="391" src="http://bakerstreet.tv/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/Tiramisu-Bundt-Cake-with-Kahl%C3%BAa-Mascarpone-Glaze2-1024x652.jpg" title="Tiramisu Bundt Cake with Kahla Mascarpone Glaze" width="614"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        The cake has an even and well-packed crumb. Ive baked it thrice and the recipe has been a hit every single time. The only tricky part of baking this cake is the getting swirl and layering correct. I recommend you be patient and do it slowly.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://bakerstreet.tv/2012/12/tiramisu-bundt-cake-with-kahlua-mascarpone-glaze/" rel="attachment wp-att-9411">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-9411" height="411" src="http://bakerstreet.tv/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Tiramisu-Bundt-Cake-with-Kahl%C3%BAa-Mascarpone-Glaze4-1024x685.jpg" title="Tiramisu Bundt Cake with Kahla Mascarpone Glaze" width="614"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Dont just pour it in chunks and spread with a spatula, instead take small dollops of batter. I used my cookie scoop to ensure an even distribution of the batter. The only layer you dont need to be bothered about is the bottom layer the espresso and Kahlabatters are both have very different consistencies which makes it important to handle them carefully.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://bakerstreet.tv/2012/12/tiramisu-bundt-cake-with-kahlua-mascarpone-glaze/" rel="attachment wp-att-9412">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-9412" height="343" src="http://bakerstreet.tv/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/Tiramisu-Bundt-Cake-with-Kahl%C3%BAa-Mascarpone-Glaze5-1024x571.jpg" title="Tiramisu Bundt Cake with Kahla Mascarpone Glaze" width="614"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Over to the recipe: A moist cake with a tight crumb and a kick of espresso and Kahlua  could this be, the ultimate homemade dessert?
       </p>

        Tiramisu Bundt Cake with Kahla Mascarpone Glaze
       </div>
       <div class="ERSDetails">
        <div class="ERSAuthor">
         Author:
         <span itemprop="author">
          Cake by Baking Bites | Glaze by Baker Street
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="ERSHead">
         Recipe type:
         <span itemprop="recipeCategory">
          Cake, Dessert
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="ERSClear">
        </div>
        <div class="ERSTimes">
        </div>
        <div class="ERSClear">
        </div>
        <div class="ERSHead">
         Serves:
         <span itemprop="recipeYield">
          12
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="ERSClear">
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="ERSIngredients">
        <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
         Ingredients
        </div>
        <ul>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          3 cups all purpose flour
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          2 tsp baking soda
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          1 tsp baking powder
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          1 tsp salt
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
           cup butter, room temperature
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          1 cups sugar
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          3 large eggs
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          1 tsp vanilla extract
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          1 cups milk
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
           cup espresso or very strong coffee
         </li>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
           cup Kahla
         </li>
        </ul>
        <div class="ERSSectionHead">
         For the Kahla-Mascarpone Glaze:
        </div>
        <ul>
         <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
          Click
          <em>
           <a href="http://bakerstreet.tv/2012/07/kahlua-mascarpone-frosting/" target="_blank" title="Wordless Wednesday: Kahla Mascarpone Frosting">
            here
           </a>
          </em>
          for the recipe.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <div class="ERSClear">
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="ERSInstructions">
        <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
         Instructions
        </div>
        <ol>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          Preheat oven to 325F and generously grease and flour a 12-cup bundt pan.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking power and salt. Set aside.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the vanilla extract.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          Working in two or three additions, alternate adding the flour mixture and the milk large bowl, ending with an addition of flour, and mixing just until everything is incorporated.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          Remove 1 cup of the batter into a small bowl. Remove another cup of batter into a second small bowl.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          Add espresso to one of the small bowls and whisk until well-combined.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          Add Kahlua to the second small bowl and whisk until well combined.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          Pour half of the plain batter into the bundt pan and spread into an even layer. Pour the Kahlua batter into an even layer on top of the plain batter.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          Pour espresso batter on top of Kahlua batter. These batters maybe slightly thinner, but do not mix or stir them.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          Carefully spread all remaining plan batter into the bundt pan.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          Bake for 55-60 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
         </li>
         <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          Let cake cool in pan for 15-20 minutes, then turn cake out onto a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.
         </li>
        </ol>
