       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Egg Rolls
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-04-13T07:00:22+00:00">
         April 13, 2014
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://melaniemakes.com/blog/2014/04/peanut-butter-chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-egg-rolls.html#comments">
          74 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <em>
          Rich and melty peanut butter chocolate chip cookie dough is wrapped in a crisp egg roll wrapper and sprinkled with powdered sugar. Dipped in chocolate ganache, these Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Egg Rolls are the excuse youve been waiting for to enjoy cookie dough, bite after decadent bite.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-4478">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Egg Rolls | Melanie Makes melaniemakes.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4483" height="900" src="http://melaniemakes.com/images/2014/04/peanut-butter-chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-egg-rolls-mm.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the first restaurants we went to after moving to Madison had a bit of an eclectic menu. Great food that you werent going to find anywhere else. Their dessert menu was no different. One of my favorites? Their Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Egg Rolls served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and drizzled with chocolate sauce. Talk about amazing!
        </p>
        <p>
         Ive always wanted to recreate this dessert at home and figured that as this weeks Sunday Supper theme was Stuff, Roll and Wrap, it was the perfect opportunity.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Egg Rolls | Melanie Makes melaniemakes.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4479" height="900" src="http://melaniemakes.com/images/2014/04/peanut-butter-chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-egg-rolls-2.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Melty peanut butter chocolate chip cookie dough is wrapped in a crisp egg roll wrapper and sprinkled with powdered sugar. But I didnt stop there! I paired these dessert egg rolls with a rich, chocolate ganache dipping sauce.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Egg Rolls | Melanie Makes melaniemakes.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4481" height="900" src="http://melaniemakes.com/images/2014/04/peanut-butter-chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-egg-rolls-4.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Besides being the epitomy of over the top decadence, they also couldnt be easier to make. A scoop of eggless cookie dough is wrapped and then fried. Dont believe me? Let me show you how!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Egg Rolls | Melanie Makes melaniemakes.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4487" height="914" src="http://melaniemakes.com/images/2014/04/peanut-butter-chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-egg-rolls-step2.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Using a medium cookie scoop, place a scoop of cookie dough near the bottom third of a wonton wrapper.
         </li>
         <li>
          Form dough into the shape of a log.
         </li>
         <li>
          Roll wrapper over dough to center of wrapper.
         </li>
         <li>
          Fold right side of wrapper to center.
         </li>
         <li>
          Fold left side of wrapper to center.
         </li>
         <li>
          Moisten top corner of wrapper with water and roll wrapper closed.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         <img alt="Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Egg Rolls | Melanie Makes melaniemakes.com" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4480" height="900" src="http://melaniemakes.com/images/2014/04/peanut-butter-chocolate-chip-cookie-dough-egg-rolls-3.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Egg Rolls

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup brown sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup peanut butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon vanilla
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tablespoons water
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1-1/2 cups flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3-1/4 cup chocolate chips
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            30 wonton wrappers
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 quarts vegetable oil
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup whipping cream
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            powdered sugar
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In the bowl of an electric mixer, cream together brown sugar, peanut butter, butter and sugar.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add vanilla and water and mix until combined.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add flour and salt to bowl and mix until just combined. Fold in 1-1/4 cups chocolate chips.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add one scoop of cookie dough to each wonton wrapper and roll as shown.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place wontons on a baking sheet and freeze for at least 30 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oil to 350 degrees in large saucepan.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Fry wontons in small batches for 1-2 minutes or until golden brown.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove from oil and drain on a plate lined with paper towels.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            To make chocolate ganache dipping sauce, combine 2 cups chocolate chips and whipping cream in small bowl and microwave for 1 minute.   Stir to combine.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Sprinkle wontons with powdered sugar and serve with chocolate ganache.
           </li>
          </ol>
