              The Allyn Community Association pulls out all the stops for this action-packed weekend of fun on Case Inlet in Allyn. Seafood fans gather to enjoy the legendary alder-smoked salmon dinner (starts at noon on Saturday), a wine and beer garden, live music and dancing, shellfish delicacies galore and, the star  geoduck. Just four miles south of Belfair on Highway 3, Allyn is one of Puget Sounds best kept secrets. Whether kayaking, fishing, enjoying local dining establishments or teeing up at Lakeland Village Golf Course, Allyn is a place for adventurers and families.
