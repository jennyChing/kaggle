                 <h2 class="story-title" style="text-transform: capitalize !important; font-size: 34px; ">
                  Mad Men Classic Cocktails
                 </h2>
                </div>

                 <p>
                  <strong>
                   Drink Like
                   <em>
                    Mad Men
                   </em>
                   : 6 Classic Cocktail Recipes
                  </strong>
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  <strong>
                  </strong>
                  In
                  <a href="http://www.amctv.com/shows/mad-men" target="_blank">
                   <strong>
                    <em>
                     Mad Men
                    </em>
                   </strong>
                  </a>
                  , premiering its sixth season on Sunday, booze pays almost as large a role as the costumes. Every episode is packed with scenes of advertising executives at meetings sipping on glasses vodka or scotch in the office. Off the clock, Don, execs, and the ladies are seen at lounges or at home, enjoying more cocktails and smokes.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  As season six kicks off this weekend, theres plenty more drinking to be expected. And we cant help but wonder: Why not join in? We bet youll impress all your friends at your
                  <em>
                   Mad Men
                  </em>
                  viewing party.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  So we rounded up six great
                  <a href="http://pinterest.com">
                   Pinterest
                  </a>
                  recipes for classic cocktails usually had by Don, Peggy,
                  <a href="http://www.lifestylemirror.com/woman/beauty/mad-mens-jessica-pare/" target="_blank">
                   Megan
                  </a>
                  ,
                  <a href="http://www.lifestylemirror.com/beauty/makeup/get-january-jones-2013-sag-awards-beauty-look/" target="_blank">
                   Betty
                  </a>
                  , and the rest of the gang. Learn how to make Dons favorite
                  <a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/28710516346678087/" target="_blank">
                   <strong>
                    old fashioned cocktail
                   </strong>
                  </a>
                  <strong>
                  </strong>
                  the proper and delicious way. Stir up a cool Mint Julep that both the men and women of
                  <em>
                   Mad Men
                  </em>
                  love. Or let the standby
                  <a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/165859198748730040/" target="_blank">
                   <strong>
                    classic Manhattans
                   </strong>
                  </a>
                  <strong>
                  </strong>
                  and unfailing
                  <a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/165859198748730040/" target="_blank">
                   <strong>
                    martinis
                   </strong>
                  </a>
                  <strong>
                  </strong>
                  get you advertising exec drunk.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  Kick back, take a swill, and toast to yet another season of
                  <em>
                   Mad Men
                  </em>
                  with any of these classic cocktails. 
                  <em>
                   Mina Kang
                  </em>
