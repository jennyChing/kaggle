         <p>
          Nintendos amiibo lineup is getting bigger and better. A few amiibos today have already stopped being produced and are going for quite a pretty penny on ebay with
          <a href="https://www.amazon.com/Marth-amiibo-nintendo-wii-u/dp/B00N4ABOXU/ref=as_sl_pc_ss_til?tag=techologie-20&amp;linkCode=w01&amp;linkId=INDBEDAMM6TOE5YB&amp;creativeASIN=B00N4ABOXU">
           Marth
          </a>
          being the rarest amiibo of them all, selling for up to $200 on some cases. The upcoming amiibo wave includes a few retail exclusives that are also expected to sale out and become rare. Shulk (Gamestop), Rosalina (Target), Lucario (ToysRUs), Meta Knight (Best Buy). Looking at all the past and upcoming exclusives, its easy to see one thing;
          <a href="https://www.amazon.com/NINTENDO-AMIIBO-SHULK/dp/B00S66TYNY/ref=as_sl_pc_ss_til?tag=techologie-20&amp;linkCode=w01&amp;linkId=3IILXYZQ6S3NJXLM&amp;creativeASIN=B00S66TYNY">
           Shulk
          </a>
          is the best looking amiibo of them all!. Take a look at these close up images sharedby reddit user derek66.
