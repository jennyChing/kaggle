        <p>
         Besides writing on this blog, I am also a book addict and a movie fan. Like many book-lovers, I always get lost in the book, forget everything around me and be a part of the world created by the writer, become one of the characters and
         <span id="more-21629">
         </span>
         even fight against the windmills. I prefer actual books (cant get used to e-readers) and I underline everything that strikes or impacts me in a way. For movies, it is kinda different. You cant highlight the lines or keep the scenes that impacts you or takes you back to a moment in your own life. Wish we could though. In digital era, they solve this problem and ease this pain a little with these photo-scenes with lines so that you can collect and save them. Then I thought it would be something quiet different to share with you, I mean my favourite movie-lines in this way. Hope you enjoy them as much as I do.
        </p>
