         BLOG
        </a>
       </div>
       <p>
        This is a recent commission we had to create a wicked vector snake emblem for some badass fellas in far flung Australia. They basically wanted a tribal-esque snake for their band called Kobrakai and this is what we came up with. I found out with this project that I really like to draw snakes! I rarely create celtic knot style drawings and yes this one is very simplistic but I still think it worked out well.
       </p>
       <p>
        Kobrakai (not to be confused with Kobra Kai) are a new collective so I will update this post with news of their progress.
       </p>
