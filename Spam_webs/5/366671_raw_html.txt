        Dr. Loves Couples Guide to Surviving the Jolly Hollerdays With Dr. Jamie Turndorf
       </h1>
       <div id="sm">
        <b>
         Millions of couples end up ringing their hands and each others throats during the holiday season. In this Ask Dr. Love radio show, Dr. Jamie Turndorf discusses why intimate relationships are particularly vulnerable to holiday stresses.
        </b>
       </div>
       <br/>
       <div id="bdab" style="min-height:410px">

         Rather than being a peaceful time, the holidays are more like World War III for many couples.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         In this Ask Dr. Love radio show, Dr. Jamie Turndorf explains why the holidays often turn to hollerdays. It is precisely the extra stress associated with additional chores and shopping, money worries, and time spent with families that expose our underlying relationship fault lines. The stresses cause these fault lines to rupture, creating relationship stress and fighting.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Discover how to take advantage of this time of year to finally resolve the festering relationship issues that the holiday season brings to a head.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Dont give up. There is hope for a cease fire!
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Joining Dr. Turndorf on this weeks show will be Larry James, NY Times Bestselling author, former Associate Producer of Mars &amp; Venus, the nationally syndicated radio show, and President of CelebrateLove.com.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Tune in to hear their proven plans for turning the hollerdays into truly joyous holidays and beyond!
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Hosted on TalkZone.com, AskDrLove with Dr. Jamie Turndorf, is a live one hour Internet radio program airing at 1pm (EST) every Tuesday afternoon. Listeners can also call-in to the show and ask Dr. Turndorf for advice on any of their personal issues. Archived shows are available on TalkZone.com, BlogTalkradio.com, AskDrLove.com, and iTunes.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Dr. Jamie Turndorf is a popular relationship expert and couples therapist, author, and radio show host known to millions around the world as the creator of AskDrLove.com, the Web's first free relationship advice site originally launched in 1996, http:// AskDrLove.com now offers thousands of advice articles on every imaginable relationship, marriage, dating advice question. You can follow Dr. Turndorf on Facebook and on Twitter @askdrlove.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Dr. Tundorf's advice and methods will bring greater romance and intimacy into your love life, so be sure to tune in or call in to the Ask Dr Love radio show and get help from one of today's top relationship experts. As Dr. Turndorf says, Knowledge Is Your Key To Happy Relationships!
