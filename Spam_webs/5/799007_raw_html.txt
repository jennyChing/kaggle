        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         No, Im not throwing a Halloween party any time soon. But I did pick up a big bag of gourmet jelly beans the other day and had a fun idea. I separated all of the orange and yellow jelly beans into a separate bowl (while the kids and I snacked on the other colors), grabbed some white tulle and some orange ribbon, and whipped up some fun little jelly bean party favors.
        </p>
        <p>
         If I had some green ribbon, I could have made them look like pumpkins.but I still think theyre cute. You could customize these for any special occasion  with a childs favorite color, red jelly beans with green ribbon to look like apples or strawberries, etc.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Step 1:
         </strong>
         Separate all of the orange and yellow jelly beans into a bowl (or whichever color you are using):
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://betterinbulk.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Halloween-party-favor-1.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest pibfi_float_center">
           <img alt="Easy Halloween party favor craft" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17727" height="400" src="http://betterinbulk.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Halloween-party-favor-1.jpg" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://betterinbulk.net/2013/09/easy-jelly-bean-party-favors.html&amp;media=http://betterinbulk.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Halloween-party-favor-1.jpg&amp;description=Easy Jelly Bean Party Favors #WW #Craft')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Step 2:
         </strong>
         Cut a square of tulle large enough to fit your jelly beans (I had to double mine up because the tulle I had was only 6 inches wide and I needed a piece larger than 6 inches square). Place jelly beans in the center of the square.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://betterinbulk.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Halloween-party-favor-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest pibfi_float_center">
           <img alt="Easy Halloween party favor craft" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17726" height="400" src="http://betterinbulk.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Halloween-party-favor-2.jpg" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://betterinbulk.net/2013/09/easy-jelly-bean-party-favors.html&amp;media=http://betterinbulk.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Halloween-party-favor-2.jpg&amp;description=Easy Jelly Bean Party Favors #WW #Craft')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Step 3:
         </strong>
         Gather the tulle up around the pile of jelly beans and secure it closed with a ribbon.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://betterinbulk.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Halloween-party-favor-3.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest pibfi_float_center">
           <img alt="Easy Halloween party favor craft" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17725" height="400" src="http://betterinbulk.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Halloween-party-favor-3.jpg" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://betterinbulk.net/2013/09/easy-jelly-bean-party-favors.html&amp;media=http://betterinbulk.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Halloween-party-favor-3.jpg&amp;description=Easy Jelly Bean Party Favors #WW #Craft')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The kids loved them.especially when we realized that that the yellow and orange colored jelly beans are the BEST flavors. We had a little party of our own and dug in!
        </p>
