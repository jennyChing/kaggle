                 <b>
                  Spicy
Fish Kabab
                 </b>
                  a best and tasty sea food recipe. This fried sea food is loved
by almost all non  Vegetarians!! Today, in this post Im going to share with
you a How to cook Spicy Fish Kabab Recipe. Its simple and easy to make dish. The
freshly ground masala applied for marinating gives the authentic taste. With
few and easily available ingredients you can prepare this recipe. This goes
well with hot chapattis, rotis, plain or brown rice!!!!! Enjoy this fried and
spicy non vegetarian sea food.

                <h2 style="text-align: left;">
                 <b>
                  <span style="color: #660000;">
                   Ingredients required
to prepare Spicy Fish Kabab:
                  </span>
                 </b>
                </h2>
                <div style="text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                  
                  <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 Fish 1
kg
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                  
                  <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 Lemon Juice                      
cup
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                  
                  <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 Fresh Ginger (finely grated)           2 tsp
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                  
                  <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 Garlic (crushed)                   3
cloves
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                  
                  <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 Salt 1-
 tsp
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                  
                  <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 Coriander Powder                 4
tsp
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                  
                  <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 Garam Masala                    1
tsp
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                  
                  <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 Chili Powder                      1
tsp
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                  
                  <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 Yoghurt                         1
cup

                 <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                  
                  <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 Plain flour                        2
tbsp
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                  
                  <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                  </span>
                 </span>
                 Cooking Oil                      1
tbsp
                </div>
                <div class="MsoListParagraphCxSpLast" style="mso-list: l2 level1 lfo2; text-indent: -.25in;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <h2 style="text-align: left;">
                  <b>
                   <span style="color: #660000;">
                    How to make Spicy Fish Kabab:
                   </span>
                  </b>
                 </h2>
                 <div style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                   
                   <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  Rinse fish with cold water and pat dry
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                   
                   <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  Now cut into 1 inch cubes and set aside
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                   
                   <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  Mix all the ingredients to make a marinade
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                   
                   <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  Pour this into large bowl and add the fish
cubes
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                   
                   <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  Make sure fish is well covered by marinade
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                   
                   <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  Place bowl in the refrigerator for two hours
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                   
                   <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  Take away fish from marinade
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                   
                   <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  Discard the marinade and assemble fish cubes
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                   
                   <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  Pour oil in a pan, heat it and add fish pieces
in to it
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                   
                   <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  Cook for about 4-6 minutes each side or until
the fish flakes easily with a fork
                 </div>
                 <div style="text-align: left;">
                  <span style="font-family: Symbol; mso-bidi-font-family: Symbol; mso-fareast-font-family: Symbol;">
                   
                   <span style="font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 7pt;">
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  Fish Kebab is ready to be served with hot rice
                 </div>
                </div>
               </div>
