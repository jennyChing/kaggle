       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Coffee. It helps you wake up in the morning, gives you a spark of energy. Did you know that your skin absorbs caffeine in addition to your stomach? You dont have to ingest coffee to get its benefits.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         This
         <strong>
          Cinnamon Coffee Scrub
         </strong>
         is invigorating, smells delicious and is a great perk-me-up.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="DSC_1553 square small" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-65689" height="630" src="http://kidsactivitiesblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/DSC_1553-square-small.jpg" width="650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         This recipe is so easy, to make and only has four ingredients!
        </p>
        <h2>
         To make
         <strong>
          Essential Oil Cinnamon Coffee Scrub
         </strong>
         you will need:
        </h2>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Coffee Grounds  you can use previously used grounds. Great way to recycle what you have!
         </li>
         <li>
          Baking Soda
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.coffeecupsandcrayons.com/young-living-starter-kit/" title="Young Living Starter Kit">
           Cinnamon Essential Oil
          </a>
         </li>
         <li>
          carrier oil (olive, coconut, V-6 etc to dilute the cinnamon oil)
         </li>
         <li>
          Coconut oil
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         **Mix it up! Feel free to add a couple of drops of
         <a href="http://www.coffeecupsandcrayons.com/young-living-starter-kit/" title="Young Living Starter Kit">
          other Essential Oils
         </a>
         too! Weve mixed orange and cinnamon, cinnamon and clove bud, even Ginger or Anise. Yum! All of these essential oils are great for helping detox the body and awaken the mind.**
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Homemade Cinnamon Coffee Scrub with Essential Oils....SO GOOD!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5858" height="975" src="http://www.coffeecupsandcrayons.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Homemade-Cinnamon-Coffee-Scrub-with-Essential-Oils....SO-GOOD.jpg" width="650"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h2>
         How To Make Essential Oil Cinnamon Coffee Scrub:
        </h2>
        <p>
         To start, mix equal parts coffee grounds and baking soda. How much you use is up to your discretion, depending on how much scrub youd like to make! If you need a more exact starting point, try half a cup of both coffee and baking soda. That should give you enough scrub to try it out.
        </p>
        <p>
         Next, dilute your cinnamon oil with whichever carrier oil you choose. Start with one drop of cinnamon oiland test it out to see what will work for you. Add more carrier oil if it seems too strong or you have skin sensitivity. Add your cinnamon oil mixture to the dry ingredients.
        </p>
        <p>
         Stir in a small amount of coconut oil. Slowly increasing the quantity of coconut oil as you stir. You will know youve found the perfect mix when it takes on the consistency of fudge!
        </p>
        <p>
         When you have reached that point, jar it up. You are able to start using the
         <strong>
          essential oil cinnamon scrub
         </strong>
         right away.
        </p>
        <p>
         Another perk they say that this coffee scrub can be good for hair removal. I have thick hair and have not noticed much benefit, but hey, give it a try! Maybe this scrub would be helpful as a fuzz-remover? If it works as a hair removal scrub let us know.
        </p>
