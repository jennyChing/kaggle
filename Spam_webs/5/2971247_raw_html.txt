      <div class="organ article">
       <p class="accentbox">
        I will make it clear from the beginning. I don't like balloons. I simply don't trust anything can grow to ten times it's size. However, I've got a feeling that the people out there who aren't inflataphobic will enjoy this week's article. One of our mentally deranged forum goons by the name of
        <b>
         TwystNeko
        </b>
        has an impressive talent, and isn't afraid to show it. Taking suggestions from other members of the
        <a href="http://forums.somethingawful.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         SA Forums
        </a>
        , TwystNeko attempted to make some balloons that you won't see Jimmy asking for at his 6th birthday party. Unless little Jimmy happens to have a passion for HR Giger's work. Enjoy this week's family friendly Comedy Goldmine!
        <br class="pb"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Believe it or not, Ripley's arch nemesis is here along with a friend. Here's the
        <b>
         Alien
        </b>
        from Alien. It could possibly be the queen, but I haven't had a chance to check between it's legs.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="undefined" src="http://i.somethingawful.com/u/russ/goldmine39/alien.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Here is a
        <b>
         face hugger
        </b>
        , which is another Alien, and possible a stage on the Ladder Theory.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="undefined" src="http://i.somethingawful.com/u/russ/goldmine39/facehugger.jpg" width="375"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        There's also an
        <b>
         angler fish
        </b>
        , which I assume doesn't come with plastic tupperware attached in the wild.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="undefined" src="http://i.somethingawful.com/u/russ/goldmine39/anglerfish.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        It's
        <b>
         Bob from Bobble Bubble
        </b>
        . Or Bub from Bubble Bobble. You get the idea.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="undefined" src="http://i.somethingawful.com/u/russ/goldmine39/bubblebobble.jpg" width="500"/>
        <br/>
       </p>
