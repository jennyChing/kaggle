         How to Make Chocolate Cake in a Mug

        <p>
         Ever go to the grocery store and walk through the bakery with all those beautiful, rich cakes, and start craving a piece of chocolate cake. Not an entire cake, but just ONE piece?
         <span id="more-208">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center; padding-top: 15px;">
         <img alt="How to Make Chocolate Cake in a Mug- my new favorite dessert! So GOOD." class="alignnone size-full wp-image-216" height="640" src="http://misswish.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/chocolatecakeinamug.jpg" width="640"/>
         <br/>
         <a data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonPin" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fmisswish.com%2Fcake-in-a-mug-recipe&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fmisswish.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2013%2F10%2Fchocolatecakeinamug.jpg&amp;description=How%20to%20Make%20Chocolate%20Cake%20in%20a%20Mug-%20my%20new%20favorite%20dessert!%20So%20GOOD.">
          <img src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Well here is a recipe that will make a large batch mix of chocolate cake that you can store and later make single serving sizes, or gift a few to a friend in a mug.
        </p>
        <div class="hr">
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Here is the recipe for the large stock batch recipe:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="font-size: 22px;">
         Chocolate Cake Mix
        </p>
        <p>
         2 cups all-purpose flour
         <br/>
         1 3/4 cups sugar
         <br/>
         1 1/2 cup cocoa
         <br/>
         2 teaspoons baking powder
         <br/>
         1 teaspoon baking soda
         <br/>
         1 teaspoon salt
         <br/>
         1/4 cup vegetable shortening
         <br/>
          cup cornstarch
         <br/>
         1 cup powder sugar
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="cake in a mug ingredients" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-213" height="427" src="http://misswish.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cakeinamugingredients.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p style="font-size: 18px;">
         Directions
        </p>
        <p>
         Mix all the dry ingredients along with the shortening in a food processor to blend the ingredients and to cut the shortening into the mixture. Its that easy! Find a large jar to store the remainder of the mix and store in a cool, dry place.
        </p>
        <p>
         To make a single serving of cake, take a mug and spray the inside with oil. Then place  cup of the dry mixture into the mug. Add 1 TB of water, 1 TB of oil, and 1 egg white into the mug and mix well. Place in microwave for 1 minute.   You can top your cake with fresh strawberries or chocolate syrup, or make a Chocolate glaze mixture.
        </p>
        <p>
         For the chocolate glaze mixture take 1/3 cup of powder sugar and 2 TSP of choco powder and 1  TSP of water. Place all in a plastic bag and with your fingers mix the ingredients together. You can substitute the coco powder with another dry flavoring or even vanilla extract.
        </p>
        <div class="hr">
        </div>
        <p style="font-size: 18px;">
         Glaze Mixture
        </p>
        <p>
         1/3 Powder sugar
         <br/>
         1  TSP water
         <br/>
         2 TSP of coco powder ( or other flavoring)
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="glaze ingredients" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-217" height="427" src="http://misswish.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/glazeingredients.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <div class="hr">
        </div>
        <p style="font-size: 22px;">
         Cake in the mug recipe
        </p>
        <p>
          cup dry mix
         <br/>
         1TB water
         <br/>
         1 TB oil
         <br/>
         1 Egg white
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="cake ingredients" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-214" height="427" src="http://misswish.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cakeingredients.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p style="font-size: 18px;">
         Directions
        </p>
        <p>
         If you want to create a gift for a friend, take some small plastic bags an place  cup of the mixture, and they dry ingredients for the glaze and tie the top with a ribbon. Place it in a mug with the directions for Cake in a mug recipe. And your small single serving cake is ready to give away!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center; padding-top: 15px;">
         <img alt="Chocolate Cake in a Mug- the perfect gift for pretty much everyone." class="alignnone size-full wp-image-215" height="640" src="http://misswish.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/cakemixinamug.jpg" width="640"/>

        </p>
        <p>
         There are other recipes for cake in a mug, but they require one box of cake mix and one box of angel food cake/ or pudding mix. If youre like me, I wanted everything to be homemade without all the additives. If youre in a crunch for time and have both of the mixes available you can totally use that as your large batch cake mix. The large chocolate cake batch recipe is a mix of homemade chocolate cake and homemade chocolate pudding. It was great because I had all those ingredients already in my home and I didnt have to run down to the grocery store to mix everything up.
