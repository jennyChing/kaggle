           <h3 class="entry-header">
            Feelings, Nothing More than Feelings......
           </h3>
           <div class="entry-content">
            <div class="entry-body">
             <p>
              <strong>
               <em>
                Feelings
               </em>
              </strong>
              are defined in the dictionary as emotions or an emotional perception or attitude. They are also called vague impressions of a situation. So feelings have to do with how we react to situations, people, places and things
              <strong>
               <em>
                . Feelings are not fact. Feelings are not intuition.
               </em>
              </strong>
              I say that because as a society we react to situations far more than we act. No other word has been blamed for everything from hurting ourselves or others to actual murder. We consider them valid reasons for all of the above.
             </p>
             <p>
              Even having said all of the above,
              <strong>
               <em>
                feelings are some of the most powerful forces
               </em>
              </strong>
              when you are attempting to change a negative behavior, enact some new behavior, or respond to some situation in your life. Your body and your mind will have a reaction. The reaction will be based on your perception of the situation or your feelings.
             </p>
             <p>
              There is a saying that is factual in my experience,
              <strong>
               <em>
                You live yourself in to better thinking, you dont think yourself into better living.
               </em>
              </strong>
              When I first started to try to stop drinking, I believed if only the events and people in my life would improve, I would be able to change my life. Yet I was trying to use the thinking that got me into trouble to fix my troubles. It just wont work.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               <em>
                The other issue with feelings is they seem to randomly change
               </em>
              </strong>
              <em>
               .
              </em>
              I dont know about you, but I can go through the day with a different feeling every hour. At first I am ticked off about having to get up so early. Then I am really enjoying my first cup of Starbucks, and that great shower. So now my feelings are pretty happy, until the guy cuts me off in traffic, and I hit every red light on my way to an appointment. Then I am irritated. Do you get my drift?
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               <em>
                So remember you have feelings, you are not a feeling
               </em>
              </strong>
              . You are the captain of your ship. When your feelings are running you, get a grip!! There are tools to help you deal with your feelings. Talking to someone, journaling about your feelings, taking action on them, and exercise are just a few ways that successfully help you cope with your feelings. There are as many ways to cope with them as there are people, some people like music, some like to garden. Experiment, until you find what works for you.
             </p>
             <p>
              By the way, your intuition is that still small voice that when your feelings are controlling you, you have difficulty finding. So, work with your feelings when they seem to have the best of you, find a place to center yourself and remember what you are doing. Shake it off and keep moving.
             </p>
