        <p>
         A guest post by Big Sister
        </p>
        <div align="center">
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.tumbleweedcontessa.com/blog/superbowl-and-chili-party/">
           <img alt="2013 Superbowl and Chili" data-recalc-dims="1" src="//i2.wp.com/www.tumbleweedcontessa.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Superbowl-and-Chili-Party-Button.jpg?w=584" style='"border: none;'/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          I cant stand not to get in on this party. My Mom is having so much fun. I am seeing such great stuff that I dont know where to start. So, here is one addition and I have two more coming this week. My neighborhood is having a Super Bowl Party and I will be bringing these things. My neighbors love the spicy stuff so I had to take my regular pimiento cheese and add some more zing to it.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          <a href="http://i1.wp.com/www.tumbleweedcontessa.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Pimiento-Cheese-Stuffed-Peppers.jpg">
           <img alt="Stuffed Peppers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3362" data-recalc-dims="1" src="//i1.wp.com/www.tumbleweedcontessa.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Pimiento-Cheese-Stuffed-Peppers.jpg?resize=584%2C592"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="zlrecipe-container-border" id="zlrecipe-container-58" style="border: 1px dotted;">
          <div class="serif zlrecipe" id="zlrecipe-container" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
           <div id="zlrecipe-innerdiv">
            <div class="item b-b">
             <div class="zlrecipe-print-link fl-r">
              <a class="butn-link" href="javascript:void(0);" onclick="zlrPrint('zlrecipe-container-58', 'http://www.tumbleweedcontessa.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/ziplist-recipe-plugin/'); return false" title="Print this recipe">
               Print
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="zl-recipe-link fl-r zl-rmvd" id="zl-recipe-link-58">
             </div>
             <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
              Pimiento Cheese Stuffed Peppers
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
             </div>
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
             </div>
             <div class="zlclear">
             </div>
            </div>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
             What You Need:
            </p>
            <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
              3 ounces extra-sharp cheddar cheese, freshly grated
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
              3 ounces cream cheese, softened well
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
              3 ounces diced roasted red peppers
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/3 small can of diced green chiles (drained)
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 garlic cloves, minced
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/8 teaspoon fresh ground pepper
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/8 teaspoon cayenne powder
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 or 3 drops hot sauce
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
              pinch sugar
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 12 oz jars of your favorite hollowed out peppers--this week was cherry peppers and peppadews
             </li>
            </ul>
            <span class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
             <p class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Let the cream cheese come to room temperature
             </p>
             <p class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Mix all ingredients but the whole peppers very well.
             </p>
             <p class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Let the pimiento cheese sit in an airtight container in the refrigerator overnight for flavors to develop
             </p>
             <p class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Cut a hole at the top of each pepper and remove the linings and seeds (I found jars where this had already been done)
             </p>
             <p class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Use a teaspoon to fill and top off the peppers
             </p>
            </span>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-notes">
             Notes
            </p>
            <div id="zlrecipe-notes-list">
             <p class="notes">
              For the best flavor, grate the cheddar just before you make the recipe. Store pimento cheese in an airtight container in the refrigerator. A little time to let the ingredients marry makes it taste better
             </p>
            </div>
