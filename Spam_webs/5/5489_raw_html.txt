       <h1>
        Steel Suppliers
       </h1>
       <p>
        Established in 1980, James Dunkerley Steels Ltd was formed
to offer a quality service of providing steel to the local communities of
Lancashire, often delivering on a same day/next day basis.
       </p>
       <p>
        In 1985 and after steady growth, we crossed the Pennines and
opened a regional depot in Dewsbury, West Yorkshire to emulate the success of
our head office efforts.
       </p>
       <p>
        The dedication and commitment shown in those early days is
as strong today as it ever was, which is why we now enjoy an enviable client
base which includes all local builders, developers, engineers, fabricators and
new house builders alike.
       </p>
       <p>
        1997 saw the launch of our national civils division which
has grown to be a leading supplier of new and reusable structural steels to the
vast majority of regional and national civil engineering, construction and
piling/foundation contractors throughout the entire UK and Eire.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our comprehensive stocks include the full range of all
universal beams, RSJs, universal columns, angles, channels, flats, solid
round/square bars, circular, square and rectangular hollow sections, sheets,
plates, road plates, pipes and tubes which are all available for immediate
collection or delivery.
       </p>
       <p>
        Flame cutting to length has always been standard but with
the arrival of our fabrication shop, we have now extended our services to
include sawing, mitre cutting, welding, drilling, painting and all general
light fabrication services, making us the complete one stop supplier.
       </p>
       <p>
        Our resolve is as strong as the steels we sell, which makes
us a must for steel buyers everywhere, so if you require a competitive, fast
reliable and professional service, look no further - were waiting to hear from
you.
       </p>
