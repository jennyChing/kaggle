                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Exotic Contemporary Style House in Bali by World of Mouth
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-7283859933800475354" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-X8JyeXzdVKo/UmVjYKUAXmI/AAAAAAAAbSE/L67-Yaf44oM/s1600/Exotic_Contemporary_Style_House_in_Bali_on_world_of_architecture_01.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Exotic contemporary style house in Bali" border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-X8JyeXzdVKo/UmVjYKUAXmI/AAAAAAAAbSE/L67-Yaf44oM/s1600/Exotic_Contemporary_Style_House_in_Bali_on_world_of_architecture_01.jpg" title="Exotic contemporary style house in Bali"/>
                      </a>

                     <br/>
                     Bali-based design studio with interesting name,
                     <a href="http://www.wordofmouthbali.com/" target="_blank">
                      World of Mouth
                     </a>
                     , designed this contemporary style exotic house inSeminyak, a mixed tourist / residential area on the west coast of Bali just north of Kuta and Legian.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     The house facade is very exotic combination of white concrete and green palm trees around and on the house. Not to mention perfect green grass. There's also light blue swimming pool right next to the house, connected to covered terrace.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Minimalist interiors are painted in white as well, making the house look like it's carved out of one piece. Place looks very fresh, you can almost feel the warm air of Bali just by looking at those photos.
