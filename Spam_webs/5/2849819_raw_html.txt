        Parmesan and herb crusted chicken with garden salad

      <p>
       Recently, I shared my
       <a href="http://dearcrissy.com/baked-parmesan-fish-bites/" title="Baked Parmesan Fish Bites: Kraft Fresh Take">
        baked parmesan fish bites
       </a>
       recipe, a yummy and kid-friendly dish featuring Kraft Fresh Take breadcrumbs and cheese. Today, I have another delicious recipe utilizing
       <a href="http://www.kraftrecipes.com/kraftcheese/freshtake.aspx" title="Kraft Fresh Take">
        Kraft Fresh Take
       </a>
       ; Im featuring my parmesan and herb crusted chicken with garden salad recipe and I think youre really going to love it.
      </p>
      <p>
       This parmesan and herb crusted chicken recipe is quick, easy, and will please your entire family!
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Parmesan Herb Crusted Chicken with Garden Salad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16556" height="434" src="http://dearcrissy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/garden-salad-crusted-chicken.jpg" title="Parmesan Herb Crusted Chicken with Garden Salad" width="650"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       We eat a lot of chicken breast in our home, so Im always looking for great ways to spice-up my go-to chicken dishes. When I received my second inspiration card in the Fresh Take Challenge, I knew that I wanted to do a new spin on a family favorite.
      </p>
      <p>
       <span id="more-16555">
       </span>
      </p>
      <h3>
       My Fresh Take Challenge prompts:
      </h3>
      <p>
       <em>
        <strong>
         1.
        </strong>
        Try a new salad youve been dying to make.
        <br/>
        <strong>
         2.
        </strong>
        Create a weeknight friendly meal.
        <br/>
        <strong>
         3.
        </strong>
        Use Fresh Take in both your main meal and side dish.
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       Whats easier on a weeknight than whipping up a fresh garden salad with all of your familys favorite veggies, and some yummy baked chicken breast?
      </p>
      <p>
       I used Rosemary and Roasted Garlic Kraft Fresh take to make this parmesan and herb crusted chicken breast really specialmy whole family loved the crispy and flavorful coatingit was a huge hit!
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Parmesan Herb Crusted Chicken with Garden Salad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16559" height="974" src="http://dearcrissy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/parmesan-herb-crusted-chicken.jpg" title="Parmesan Herb Crusted Chicken with Garden Salad" width="650"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       I actually reserved a little of the breadcrumbs and parmesan mixture in a separate dish, and sprinkled it over some sliced Italian bread that I baked to go along with our salad. The cheese and herb mixture was delicious on the bread!
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Parmesan Herb Crusted Chicken with Garden Salad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16557" height="434" src="http://dearcrissy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/kraft-fresh-take.jpg" title="Parmesan Herb Crusted Chicken with Garden Salad" width="650"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       You can find Kraft Fresh Take in the dairy aisle, and it comes in several delicious flavors in addition to the Rosemary and Roasted Garlic.
      </p>
      <p>
       What I love most about
       <strong>
        Kraft Fresh Take
       </strong>
       , is that it allows me to add so much flavor and personality to my meals! Plus, its just
       <em>
        so
       </em>
       fast and convenient, I really love it.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Parmesan Herb Crusted Chicken with Garden Salad" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16558" height="440" src="http://dearcrissy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/herb-crusted-chicken-salad.jpg" title="Parmesan Herb Crusted Chicken with Garden Salad" width="650"/>
      </p>
      <p>

       <img align="right" class="photo nopin" height="125" nopin="nopin" src="http://www.dearcrissy.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/parmesan-herb-crusted-chicken-206x206.jpg" width="125"/>

       <span class="item">
        <h2 class="fn">
         Parmesan &amp; herb crusted chicken with garden salad
        </h2>
       </span>
       <div class="time">
       </div>
       <h3 id="ingredients">
        Ingredients:
       </h3>
       <div class="ingredients">
        <p>
         1pkg.  (6 oz.) Kraft Fresh Take Rosemary &amp; Roasted Garlic Cheese Breadcrumb Mix
         <br/>
         4 small boneless skinless chicken breasts
         <br/>
         1pkg. Salad kit OR your own fresh salad veggies
        </p>
       </div>
       <h3 id="directions" style="clear:left">
        Directions:
       </h3>
       <div class="instructions">
        <p>
         Heat oven to 375F.
        </p>
        <p>
         Spray baking sheet with cooking spray. Open Kraft Fresh Take bag; pull apart center seal. Mix cheese and herbs contents together.
        </p>
        <p>
         Moisten chicken breast halves with water; place in bag, lightly press cheese mixture onto both sides of chicken. Place on prepared baking sheet; top with remaining cheese and herb mixture.
        </p>
        <p>
         Bake 30 to 40 min. or until chicken is done (165F).
        </p>
        <p>
         Prepare salad using fresh ingredients of your choice, or use a pre-made bag of salad greens.
        </p>
        <p>
         Add slices of chicken breast to salad and serve with dressing of your choice.
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="notes">
        <p>
         Optional: Reserve a small amount of the cheese and breadcrumbs mixture (before letting it contact raw chicken) to sprinkle over Italian bread or breadsticks. Toast breadsticks in 350 degree oven for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted and bread is beginning to crisp.
        </p>
       </div>
