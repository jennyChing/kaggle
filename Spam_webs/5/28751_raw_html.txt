        <p>
         National Bookstore is one of the happy places that our family loves to go to. I love to read self improvement books, my husband likes arts/designs, crafts and home and living books while my son loves puzzles, comics and activity books.
        </p>
        <p>
         In this digital time, most people are now using kindle or any tablet to read books. My husband prefers it that way because according to him it saves space in the house because you can store a whole volume in your hard disk and you dont need to spend time to take care of it as it will not be crumpled.
        </p>
        <p>
         He has a point, but, I on the other hand still prefer to hold, smell and touch a real book. I tried to read a book in my tablet but I ended up browsing my Facebook or checking the net until I was not able to finish the book I was reading. My son on the other hand, doesnt mind if the book is digital or real. But since we are trying to limit his use of gadgets, I prefer the old school kind of books to give to him.
        </p>
        <p>
         A few weeks ago, we bought a lot of books on sale. Look at what weve got:
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 730px">
         <img alt="" height="586" src="http://i1117.photobucket.com/albums/k591/LevyC1008/national%20bookstore%20sale%201_zpsyktxduvk.jpg" width="720"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Im Ready for My Movie Contract (from P295.00 to P30.00) The Simpsons (from P295.00 to P30.00) Hansel and Gretel 3D P20.00 Wreck it Ralph (from P175.00 to P20.00) Rover to the Rescue (from P145.00 to P20.00)
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <span id="more-4247">
         </span>
         Ren really loves the Im Ready for my Movie Contract. He reads it from the time he wakes up, from the time he arrives from school and after he had finished with his assignments. He also likes the Wreck it Ralph book even though he has watched the movie several times already.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 730px">
         <img alt="" height="579" src="http://i1117.photobucket.com/albums/k591/LevyC1008/national%20bookstore%20sale%202_zps46zrvpwf.jpg" width="720"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          VIKINGS (from P815.00 to P30.00) Marvel Avengers (from P315.00 to P75.00) Hobbit (from P399.00 to P20.00)
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         My husband and I loves the TV series Vikings in History Channel, thats the reason why I grabbed this piece upon seeing it. The Marvel Avengers book is for my husband. It is hardbound and it is a nice book to be added to his collection. The Hobbit book is also a great deal. I am glad I saw all these books.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 730px">
         <img alt="" height="765" src="http://i1117.photobucket.com/albums/k591/LevyC1008/national%20bookstore%20sale%203_zpsi3j9zmsb.jpg" width="720"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Sexy Forever (from P949.00 to P30.00) I havent started reading this yet.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Weve bought all these books for only
         <strong>
          P275.00
         </strong>
         and we have
         <strong>
          SAVED a total of P3,113.00
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         AMAZING!!!!
        </p>
        <p>
         How about you, what do you prefer, a real book or a digital book?
        </p>
