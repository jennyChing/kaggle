           <p>
            Happy Top Ten Tuesday! This time were discussing the best silver and gold makeup. Since I absolutely love love love golds, its really hard for me to narrow this down, but Ive done the best that I can. I gravitate towards gold much more than silver for makeup, which is amusingto me. When it comes to jewelry, I much prefer platinum to yellow gold.
           </p>
           <p>
            Products that I know are vegan are denoted as vegan.

            </strong>
            <a href="http://affiliate.sugarpill.net/l/13/18677" target="_blank">
             Goldilux
            </a>
            (vegan)
            <br/>
            <em>
             Brilliant, highly-reflective metallic gold with breathtaking shine. The brightest gold youve ever seen. The micro-fine particles are super tiny, so this looks amazing sprinkled all over as body shimmer too.
            </em>
            <br/>
            This is one of the prettiest golds ever.
           </p>

            <img alt="L to R - Silk Naturals Spurs, Sugarpill Tiara, Urban Decay Revolt, Makeup Geek Mercury Urban Decay Oz" class="" height="433" src="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Urban-Decay-Revolt-Dupes2.png" width="600"/>

             Tiara
            </a>
            (vegan)
            <br/>

            </em>
            <br/>
            Love this silver!

            <br/>

            <a href="http://www.phyrra.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Metallic2.jpg">
             <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
