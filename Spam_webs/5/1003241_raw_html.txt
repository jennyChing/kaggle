        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Your Taco Fix in Los Angeles
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-05-11T08:00:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          05/11/2015
         </time>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <img alt="hugos-storefront" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3116" height="600" src="http://thecollaboreat.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/hugos-storefront.png" width="800"/>
         <img alt="Hugo's Tacos" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3115" height="906" src="http://thecollaboreat.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/guacamole.png" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Do you suffer from taco cravings? You know what Im talking about  that unexplainable desire for the perfect combination of spicy, chewy, crunchy goodness that fits so easily into the palm of your hand? I dont think its just me.
        </p>
        <p>
         Over the past several years, tacos have become the trendiest of foods here in Los Angeles, creating a seemingly insatiable craving in all its citizens, myself included. Luckily, whenever I get a hankering for taco-y goodness, I know I can always scratch that itch by heading to a neighborhood favorite:
         <a href="http://www.hugostacos.com/" target="_blank">
          Hugos Tacos
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Hugos Tacos is an offshoot of the popular Hugos restaurants in Los Angeles. Like their counterparts, the food is made with fresh, high-quality ingredients and cooked in a healthy way, incorporating recipes from a variety of family sources. In true health-food style, they offer plenty of options for carnivores, vegetarians, vegans, and gluten-free eaters alike.
        </p>
        <p>
         The extensive menu at Hugos Tacos allows countless possibilities to mix and match. You start by picking your filling: chicken, steak, carnitas, al pastor, grilled fish, mixed veggies, or soy chorizo. Then you top it off with one of seven salsas, which are arranged from mild to hot. Not feeling tacos? No problem! You can order tons of other traditional taco stand fare like burritos, burrito bowls, nachos, tortas, tostadas, and quesadillas.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Hugo's Tacos" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3118" height="797" src="http://thecollaboreat.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/taco-pair.png" width="800"/>
         <img alt="Hugo's Tacos" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3117" height="581" src="http://thecollaboreat.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/potato-taco.png" width="800"/>
         <img alt="Hugo's Tacos" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3119" height="612" src="http://thecollaboreat.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/taco-trio.png" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ive tried dozens of combinations at Hugos, and while they have all been delicious, I quickly discovered a few favorites. The al pastor is my preferred filling, and it is particularly tasty when topped with the honey-chipotle salsa. The spicy marinated pork pairs just perfectly with the sweet, tanginess of the salsa. When Im not in the mood for tacos, I love to order this on top of nachos or as a burrito bowl.
        </p>
        <p>
         In addition to the plentiful savory options, Hugos also offers a variety of sweets in the form of agua frescas and desserts. I am absolutely in love with their horchata, which is perfectly sweet with plenty of cinnamon. If you love horchata as much as I do, be sure to order a large, because you may slurp it all down before your food is even ready.
        </p>
        <p>
         Another notable thing about Hugos is their constantly-changing specialty menu. Every few weeks they offer new, seasonal flavors. On our last visit we tried their new potato tacos, a delicious combination of potatoes and other veggies on a bed of black beans and crispy lettuce. During other visits weve tried Jamaica tacos (made with hibiscus flowers) and a delectable rice pudding!
        </p>
        <p>
         Because Hugos Tacos is a taco stand, there is no indoor seating. There is a lovely outdoor patio that is fully shaded, complete with heat lamps for cold days and a cover for rain, plus they even have a standing area available on one side. Most days, however, we order our tacos to-go and bring them home to enjoy while settle down for a bit of binge-watching. We always order ahead using Hugos app so that we can skip the line (which can get quite long during popular hours).
        </p>
        <p>
         Hugos Tacos is an excellent option for vegans, vegetarians, and health-food lovers who crave a high-quality taco. Its also cheap, fast, and completely filling, which is a winning combination in my opinion. If youre in the neighborhood, Hugos Tacos is perfect when you need to scratch that itch for tacos.
        </p>
