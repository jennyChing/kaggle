     <h3 class="search_title">
      Solofy Envy Solar Powered Backpack
     </h3>
     <p>
      <img alt="main_banner_backpack" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-200 img-responsive" src="http://thegeekleak.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/main_banner_backpack.jpg"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      I am a big fan of Kickstarter projects and this one is pretty cool, at its most basic level it is a backpack with a solar panel that will charge your tech while on the move. Look a little deeper at the project and you will see that it will not only charge your tech but do it at the same rate as your wall socket.
     </p>
     <p>
      There are other backpacks on the market that will charge your tech but you will mostly find that these are just not up to the job, the Solfy will charge laptops, smart phones and tablets. If you wanted to charge an iPad Air 2 using less powerful ones this could take anywhere between 2 days and 8 hours, using the Solofy you can charge your iPad in 3-4 hours and using the more powerful one you can quick charge in just 1hr, thats pretty impressive.
     </p>
     <p>
      The Solofy is the brain child of Mark Boda and he has designed an awesome bit of tech that will ensure that you can charge your tech on the go and still carry things around thanks to the generous 12  15 L internal capacity.
     </p>
     <p>
      Go check out Marks Kickstarter project and drop him a message saying The Geek Leak sent you.
     </p>
