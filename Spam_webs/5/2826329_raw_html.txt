          <p>
           Recently
           <a href="http://www.whatsapp.com/" target="_blank">
            Whatsapp
           </a>
           released a tool that allows you to use Whatsapp on your PC (desktop computer or laptop), Mac, or any other device with an internet connection.
          </p>
          <p>
           Right now this feature that lets you use Whatsapp on PC &amp; Mac is only working for
           <a href="https://www.google.com/chrome/" target="_blank">
            Google Chrome
           </a>
           so if you dont have it youll need to install it.
          </p>
          <h2>
           USING WHATSAPP ON PC &amp; MAC
          </h2>
          <p>
           1. Go to
           <a href="http://web.whatsapp.com" target="_blank">
            web.whatsapp.com
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           Theres a QR code on this page:
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://techoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Screen-Shot-2015-05-08-at-1.13.01-PM.png">
            <img alt="How To Use Whatsapp On PC &amp; Mac" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-114" height="621" src="http://techoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Screen-Shot-2015-05-08-at-1.13.01-PM.png" width="858"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           2. Take outyour smartphone  it works with Android, Windows Phone, Blackberry and Nokia models.
          </p>
          <p>
           You must have the latest version of Whatsapp for this to work, so if you dont, go download it from the App Store.
          </p>
          <p>
           3. In the Whatsapp app itself, click the Menu button, youll see an option called Whatsapp Web  click it.
          </p>
          <p>
           4. A QR scanner will come up on the app itself, point it at the QR code on the screen.
          </p>
          <p>
           5. Whatsapp on your desktop PC &amp; Mac immediately identifies it and imports your contacts and chats!
          </p>
          <h2>
           MIRRORED WHATSAPP CONVERSATIONS ON PC &amp; MAC
          </h2>
          <p>
           You can have full conversations with your Whatsapp contacts on PC &amp; Mac in the exact same way that you can with Whatsapp on a mobile device.
           <br/>
           The conversations are fully mirrored just like Google Hangouts and Facebook Messenger do.
           <br/>
           Whatever you type on your Whatsapp desktop PC or Mac youll see on your mobile phone too, and vice versa!
          </p>
          <p>
           If youre still unclear on
           <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AxGW6HtFxEY" target="_blank">
            how to use Whatsapp on PC
           </a>
           or Mac, watch this quick video tutorial:
          </p>
          <p>
           <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/AxGW6HtFxEY" width="560">
