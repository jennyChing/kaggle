                      11 Things You Need To Know Before You Start A Diet
                     </h1>
                     <div class="post-info" style="background-color:transparent;color:#999;font-size:12px;margin:0px 0px 0px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                     </div>
                    </header>
                    <div class="entry-content" ezoic="wrapper" style="-webkit-box-sizing:content-box;background-color:transparent;background-origin:padding-box;border-spacing:0px 0px;clear:none;font-family:noto;font-size:20px;font-weight:normal;line-height:23px;list-style-image:none;list-style-position:outside;list-style-type:disc;position:static;text-indent:0px;vertical-align:baseline;visibility:visible;word-wrap:normal;height:auto;background:transparent;top:auto;right:auto;left:auto;bottom:auto;margin:0px 0px 0px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                     <div ezoic="wrapper" id="post-thumbnail" style="-webkit-box-sizing:content-box;background-color:transparent;background-origin:padding-box;border-spacing:0px 0px;clear:none;font-family:noto;font-size:20px;font-weight:normal;line-height:23px;list-style-image:none;list-style-position:outside;list-style-type:disc;position:static;text-indent:0px;vertical-align:baseline;visibility:visible;word-wrap:normal;height:auto;background:transparent;top:auto;right:auto;left:auto;bottom:auto;margin:0px 0px 0px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">

                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      A diet is more than just following rules. It considers healthy eating plan and regular exercising, but its also more than that. Many start and fail only to realize they need more discipline or motivation, even less self-criticism, or better diet program. But, is there something else you need to know or keep in mind before you start a diet program? Yes, there is. We compiled a list of 11 things one should consider before choosing a diet plan.
                      <span id="more-5561" style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 0px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      </span>
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                     </p>
                     <h2 style="background-color:transparent;color:#444;font-family:Monda;font-size:26px;font-weight:bold;line-height:36px;margin:0px 0px 12px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      #1 Most weight loss programs dont work
                     </h2>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      Is your diet really worth your time and money?
                     </p>
                     <span class="ezoic-ad square-1" style="display:inline-block;width:250px;min-height:270px;float:none;margin:2px 0px!important">
                      <span class="ezoic-ad" id="div-gpt-ad-ecellulitis_com-square-1-0" style="width:250px;position:relative;z-index:501;display:block">
                       <script data-cfasync="false" style="display:none" type="text/javascript">
                        eval(ez_write_tag([[250,250],'ecellulitis_com-square-1']));
                       </script>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      The truth is that until now we had no specific information on the true benefits of most weight-loss programs.
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      Researchers officially announced that
                      <a href="http://annals.org/article.aspx?articleid=2214178" style="background-color:transparent;color:#0645ad;margin:0px 0px 0px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px" target="_blank">
                       most weight-loss programs dont actually work
                      </a>
                      . Only a few dozen of commercial weight-loss programs have showed that users lose more weight comparing to those who dont use such popular programs.
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      These new findings could help physicians guide obese and overweight patients who would want to try popular programs such as Atkins or SlimFast and help them have a better idea what programs they can consider for their patients. Researchers reviewed 4,200 studies to found that out of 32 major commercial weight-loss programs known and marketed around the world, only 11 have been studied and only two programs showed the benefits. This means that participants lost more weight after one year following these two programs.
                     </p>
                     <span class="ezoic-ad square-3" style="display:inline-block;width:250px;min-height:270px;float:none;margin:2px 0px!important">
                      <span class="ezoic-ad" id="div-gpt-ad-ecellulitis_com-square-3-0" style="width:250px;position:relative;z-index:501;display:block">
                       <script data-cfasync="false" style="display:none" type="text/javascript">
                        eval(ez_write_tag([[250,250],'ecellulitis_com-square-3']));
                       </script>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      Researchers looked into programs such as Weight Watchers, Jenny Craig and NutriSystem, low-calorie meal replacement programs like HMR, Medifast and OPTIFAST, and five self-directed programs including Atkins and SlimFast.
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      NutriSystem resulted in more weight loss after three months (although, researchers warn, very low calorie programs like this one carry higher risk of complication). High-fat, low-carb plans like the Atkins Diet, helped people lose more weight after six and 12 months, and Jenny Craig and Weight Watchers are the only programs where participants lost more weight after one yearat least 8, and 15 pounds, on average.
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      The most important thing the researchers highlighted is that the
                      <a href="http://www.ecellulitis.com/diet-and-nutrition/garcinia-cambogia-just-another-suspicious-weight-loss-supplement-or-real-deal/" style="background-color:transparent;color:#0645ad;margin:0px 0px 0px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px" target="_blank">
                       health benefits of weight loss
                      </a>
                      needs to include lower blood pressure, cholesterol and blood sugar, that is, long-term goals, because losing weight then regaining it again has limited health benefits. So, choosing the program for a weight loss that will help you lose weight, keep it off and become healthier is the most important thing.
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                     </p>
                     <h2 style="background-color:transparent;color:#444;font-family:Monda;font-size:26px;font-weight:bold;line-height:36px;margin:0px 0px 12px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      #2 Chocolate aids in weight loss
                     </h2>
                     <p ezoic="wrapper" style="-webkit-box-sizing:content-box;background-color:transparent;background-origin:padding-box;border-spacing:0px 0px;clear:none;font-family:noto;font-size:20px;font-weight:normal;line-height:23px;list-style-image:none;list-style-position:outside;list-style-type:disc;position:static;text-indent:0px;vertical-align:baseline;visibility:visible;word-wrap:normal;height:auto;background:transparent;top:auto;right:auto;left:auto;bottom:auto;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      <span class="pibfi_pinterest" style="margin:0px 0px 0px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                       <img alt="Why You Should Eat Dark Chocolate?" class="aligncenter wp-image-4335 size-full" data-orig-height="565" data-orig-width="848" src="//cdn-0.ecellulitis.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/chocolate_11934775_m.jpg?6f3640" style="max-width:100%;background-color:transparent;width:764px;height:auto!important;margin:20px auto 20px auto;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px"/>

                     </p>
                     <h6 style="text-align:right;background-color:transparent;color:#444;font-family:Helvetica,Arial,sans-serif;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;line-height:16px;margin:0px 0px 12px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      Image credit: 123rf.com
                     </h6>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      In every diet, one of the most important things is the specific combination of foods, that is, nutrients one consumes, and this, logically, brings the confusion in the diet world. Should one lower the consumption of carbohydrates or protein? What about high fat? And what about specific food we enjoy eating, such as chocolate?
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      German researchers looked at the effects of chocolate, which is associated with several positive health impacts. Can chocolate, when consumed in a combination with dietary intervention, have a positive effect or can a diet be more effective?
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      Chocolate, which is rich in bioactive compounds, a group of molecules called flavanoids, can aid weight loss when combined with a low-carb diet. To test this, researchers divided a group of volunteers into three groups. A control group followed their normal diet, one group followed a strict low-carbohydrate diet and the third followed the low-carbohydrate diet, while consuming 42 grams of dark (81%) chocolate per day. They tracked their body weight, measure blood chemistry before, during and after the testing, while participants were also answering questions related to their sleep quality and well-being. The results showed that the group that followed the low-carb diet consuming chocolate every day lost 10% more weight. Moreover, the weight loss persisted, and answered positively to a classic problem in dietary interventions known as the yo-yo effect. The group who ate chocolate reported better sleep and well-being, while their blood cholesterol levels were significantly reduced as well.
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      So, in combination with exercise and low-carb diet, chocolate can be a weight loss accelerator.
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                     </p>
                     <h2 style="background-color:transparent;color:#444;font-family:Monda;font-size:26px;font-weight:bold;line-height:36px;margin:0px 0px 12px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      #3 Diet drinks increase your waistline
                     </h2>
                     <p ezoic="wrapper" style="-webkit-box-sizing:content-box;background-color:transparent;background-origin:padding-box;border-spacing:0px 0px;clear:none;font-family:noto;font-size:20px;font-weight:normal;line-height:23px;list-style-image:none;list-style-position:outside;list-style-type:disc;position:static;text-indent:0px;vertical-align:baseline;visibility:visible;word-wrap:normal;height:auto;background:transparent;top:auto;right:auto;left:auto;bottom:auto;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">

                     </h6>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      Its wrong to think that due to low calorie count diet drinks dont aid in weight gain. On the contrary,
                      <a href="http://www.scientificamerican.com/article/drinking-diet-soda-linked-to-a-widening-waistline-with-age/" style="background-color:transparent;color:#0645ad;margin:0px 0px 0px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px" target="_blank">
                       the new study
                      </a>
                      from the University of Texas found that those who consumed just one can of Diet Coke per day gained an average of three inches on their waistlines.
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      Low calorie fizzy drinks seem to have the opposite effect, researchers concluded. For nine years, scientists examined the lives of 749 participants, and the increases in abdominal fat were more than three times as great in daily diet soda users as in non-users. This is during the very time in life when increasing waist circumference is associated with increased risk of serious medical conditions, and mortality itself. Another study from 2008 found that people who drink diet beverages gained weight in the long-term.
                     </p>
                     <p style="background-color:transparent;margin:0px 0px 20px 0px;padding:0px 0px 0px 0px">
                      Moreover, daily diet soda users have increased long-term risk of diabetes, stroke, heart attack, and other major medical problems. Therefore, think twice before you drink any diet beverage.
