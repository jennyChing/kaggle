            <p class="has-media media-640 has-embed" data-textannotation-id="1a1517398ef2f300aa82f1a20f97a51b">
             <span class="clear-both flex-video widescreen">
              <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" class="youtube" data-chomp-id="iJ6rGOYyBzM" frameborder="0" height="360" id="youtube-iJ6rGOYyBzM" mozallowfullscreen="mozallowfullscreen" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/iJ6rGOYyBzM?wmode=transparent&amp;rel=0&amp;autohide=1&amp;enablejsapi=1&amp;showinfo=0" webkitallowfullscreen="webkitAllowFullScreen" width="640">
              </iframe>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p class="first-text" data-textannotation-id="3441974b1509bcf03c30b47942be9935">
             <span>
              Here at
             </span>
             <a href="http://www.theinfatuation.com/" target="_blank">
              The Infatuation
             </a>
             <span>
              , bourbon warms us up during New York winters, fuels late-night writing sessions, and has even inspired a
             </span>
             <a href="http://gizmodo.com/5970511/come-along-on-a-bourbon-soaked-eating-and-drinking-tour-of-seattle-wild-boar-and-urchin-included//+knobcreekpartnerpage" target="_blank">
              cross-country search
             </a>
             <span>
              for great restaurants serving great bourbon. When we heard there was a
             </span>
             <a href="http://pubads.g.doubleclick.net/gampad/clk?id=127914818&amp;iu=/4246/gm.gizmodo/promotedpost" target="_blank">
              Knob Creek
             </a>
             <span>
              Campout happening down in Kentucky, we had to check it out. We may have watched a few too many
             </span>
             <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deliverance" target="_blank">
              camping-themed horror movies
             </a>
             <span>
              before the trip and underestimated how many layers of adult onesies we'd need to stay warm, but none of that matters when you wake up at a bourbon distillery.
             </span>
            </p>
            <p data-textannotation-id="36104c4d01440909aa072d25e57c146d">
             <span>
              We weren't entirely sure what we were getting ourselves into, but "
             </span>
             <span>
              <a href="http://pubads.g.doubleclick.net/gampad/clk?id=127914818&amp;iu=/4246/gm.gizmodo/promotedpost" target="_blank">
               Knob Creek
              </a>
             </span>
             <span>
              Bourbon" and "pig roast" were all the convincing we needed. And luckily, it's all on film: everything from getting taken to bourbon school by seventh-generation master distiller Fred Noe (heir to
             </span>
             <a href="http://vimeo.com/103395848" target="_blank">
              Booker Noe's
             </a>
             <span>
              bourbon throne and hopefully one of our long-lost relatives) to chatting with bourbon lover and celebrity chef Michael Symon and indulging in his once-in-a-lifetime pig roast with our fellow campers (an impressive group of
             </span>
             <span>
              <a href="http://pubads.g.doubleclick.net/gampad/clk?id=127914818&amp;iu=/4246/gm.gizmodo/promotedpost" target="_blank">
               Knob Creek
              </a>
             </span>
             <span>
              diehards).
             </span>
            </p>
            <p data-textannotation-id="74204a5163396a5e5c1a51ee94e16f5c">
             <span>
              You may find yourself tempted to experience bourbon camp for yourself after watching this video, so we came up with a few veteran pointers to help you prepare.
             </span>
            </p>
            <div class="ad-unit js_ad-unit hide-contents ad-mobile js_ad-mobile">
             <div class="ad-mobile-inner">
              <p class="ad-label proxima">
               <small class=" proxima">
                Advertisement
               </small>
              </p>
              <div class="ad-container js_ad-container" data-zone-type="MOBILE" id="ad-container-36279066">
              </div>
              <p class="ad-label-bottom">
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
            <ol>
             <li data-textannotation-id="18dc1907e040309b5caa1e75a4038d31">
              <span>
               As Fred Noe wisely advised us, camping out at a bourbon distillery is a marathon, not a sprint. And you can't savor those big, full flavors if you burn out on day one.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li data-textannotation-id="f638808ccd62c8faa2a82b2b60630a18">
              <span>
               Pack several layers of long johns. In fact, cover as much of your body in as many layers of clothing and bug spray as possible.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li data-textannotation-id="afe1d71e54a1bce0c688fe9bcbfc06e9">
              You may have crafted countless Old Fashioneds, but trust us, it's different at a bourbon distillery.
             </li>
             <li data-textannotation-id="1ed710ca3f846df1461ebd347703b2e4">
              <span>
               Do not watch any of the following before your trip:
              </span>
              <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OlhLOWTnVoQ" target="_blank">
               a movie in which a hiker has to amputate his own arm
              </a>
              <span>
               to escape a canyon nightmare, a film featuring city boys taking what turns into a
              </span>
              <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deliverance" target="_blank">
               terrifying trip down south
              </a>
              <span>
               , or the one where the plane crashes on a mountain and the
              </span>
              <a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0106246/" target="_blank">
               survivors end up having to eat each other
              </a>
              <span>
               .
              </span>
             </li>
             <li data-textannotation-id="ddfcbcba52c6cb993fd536c07f392970">
              <span>
               WiFi is less available in the woods than you might hope. You'll want to wean yourself off of whatever you're currently binge-watching to avoid withdrawal at bourbon camp.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li data-textannotation-id="86c4b1bca409f6930281c19ee9d4acd3">
              Like most things, breakfast sandwiches can and should be cooked over a bonfire.
             </li>
             <li data-textannotation-id="af14430c8d408e43ead94a3fb574a498">
              <span>
               There is no need to bring your own bourbon when camping at a distillery, but do bring a
               <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Im-Adult-Sippy-Cup-CafePress/dp/B00A7VRUYG?tag=kinja-20&amp;ascsubtag=[b|knobcreek[p|1652279402[a|B00A7VRUYG[au|4541885" onclick="window.ga('send', 'event', 'Commerce', 'knobcreek - Watch NY Food Writers Soak Up Bourbon Camp With Celeb Chef Michael Symon', 'B00A7VRUYG');" target="_blank">
                safe and secure drinking vessel
               </a>
               . Spills are not easy to clean up in a tent.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li data-textannotation-id="2dd1f95012fa7242fd6e0d7a5b3b78b8">
              <span>
