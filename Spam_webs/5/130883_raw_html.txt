          Triple Coconut Poke Cake
         </b>
         is a coconut cake, drenched in coconut cream pudding and topped with a coconut whipped cream. Since I believe most cakes look naked with a topping, I chose to sprinkle this with cake with toasted coconut. I first fell in love with this coconut whipped cream when I made my
         <a href="http://beyondfrosting.com/2014/04/07/coconut-lemon-poke-cake/" title="Coconut Lemon Poke Cake">
          Coconut Lemon Poke Cake
         </a>
         , andI knew I had to make an all coconut version.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7352/13896331376_f3a9148efe_z.jpg">
          <img alt="Triple Coconut Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com | #pokecake #coconut" class="aligncenter" height="640" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7352/13896331376_f3a9148efe_z.jpg" title="Triple Coconut Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com | #pokecake #coconut" width="427"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Some people dislike Cool Whip. Personally, I dont have a problem with it. While this cake has a homemade coconut whipped cream, it can certainly be substituted for Cool Whip. However, you really shouldnt be afraid of homemade whipped cream! It is quite easy and quick to throw together! Plus, you can make so many different flavors! Have you seen my
         <a href="http://beyondfrosting.com/2013/10/04/cinnamon-maple-whipped-cream/" target="_blank" title="Cinnamon Maple Whipped Cream">
          Cinnamon Maple Whipped Cream
         </a>
         or my
         <a href="http://beyondfrosting.com/2012/12/06/hot-chocolate-whipped-cream/" target="_blank" title="Hot Chocolate Whipped Cream">
          Hot Chocolate Whipped Cream
         </a>
         ? How about
         <a href="http://beyondfrosting.com/2013/04/09/cake-batter-whipped-cream/" target="_blank" title="Cake Batter Whipped Cream">
          cake batter flavor
         </a>
         ?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2934/13919499263_8d22709681_z.jpg">
          <img alt="Triple Coconut Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com | #pokecake #coconut" class="aligncenter" height="640" src="https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2934/13919499263_8d22709681_z.jpg" title="Triple Coconut Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com | #pokecake #coconut" width="427"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I have mentioned before that I have a love/hate relationship with Walmart. I love it because they have a giant food section with things I cant find anywhere else- like coconut cream pudding. BUT I hate going there because its always busy and I have no patience. Well I will tell you that this coconut cake is totally worth the trip to Walmart! Plus you can work off the calories from the cake by weaving in and out of people at said Walmart. Although, if I were you, I would totally check your local store first. Just saying. If you cant find coconut cream pudding, you can easily substitute this for vanilla pudding.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="https://farm6.staticflickr.com/5474/13896331746_0217830278_z.jpg">
          <img alt="Triple Coconut Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com | #pokecake #coconut" class="aligncenter" height="640" src="https://farm6.staticflickr.com/5474/13896331746_0217830278_z.jpg" title="Triple Coconut Poke Cake | beyondfrosting.com | #pokecake #coconut" width="427"/>

         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Triple Coconut Poke Cake
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSDetails">
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSTimes">
           <div class="ERSHead">
            Prep time:
            <time datetime="PT15M" itemprop="prepTime">
             15 mins
            </time>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSHead">
            Cook time:
            <time datetime="PT20M" itemprop="cookTime">
             20 mins
            </time>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSHead">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            12-16 pieces
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          For coconut lovers only! A white coconut cake with coconut pudding. Topped with Coconut whipped cream and toasted coconut.
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 Box White cake mix
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 Large egg whites
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             C Oil
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 C Milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 tsp Coconut extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             C Coconut
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           For the topping
          </div>
          <ul>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           2 pkg Instant Coconut pudding (3.4oz)
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 C Milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 C Heavy whipping cream
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             C Powdered sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 tsp Coconut extract
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 350 F.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a mixing bowl, combine white cake mix, egg whites, oil, milk and coconut extract. Mix until all ingredients until thoroughly mixed.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake cake in 9x13 for 20-22 minutes. Check for doneness by inserting a toothpick into the middle of the cake. If the toothpick comes out clean, you cake is done. Allow
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            to cool for 10 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            While the oven is still on, toast the coconut. Spread out on a baking sheet that is lined with parchment paper or baking mat. Bake until coconut starts to turn golden brown. Remove from oven and allow to cool.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            While the cake is still warm, take the end of a wooden spoon or another round object and poke holes all over the top of your cake.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            When the cake has cooled, prepare the pudding layer by mixing instant coconut pudding with 4 cups of cold milk. Before the pudding sets, pour it over the top of the cake slowly and put the cake in the fridge to let the pudding set, about 15 minutes. The longer it sets the better.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Prepare the whipped cream. Put the bowl and whisk attachment in the freezer for 5-10 minutes to get them really cold (this step is optional, but a common practice I use.). In cold mixing bowl, pour in heavy whipping cream and beat on medium speed for about 3 minutes. Add powdered sugar and coconut extract. Mix and increase speed to medium high. Beat until stiff peak form.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spread whipped cream over the top of cake. Sprinkle with toasted coconut. This cake must stay refrigerated.
           </li>
          </ol>
