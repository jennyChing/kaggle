       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Beach Sensory Bin
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <a href="http://3dinosaurs.com/wordpress/index.php/beach-sensory-bin/" rel="bookmark" title="4:00 am">
         <span class="entry-date">
          July 17, 2014
         </span>
        </a>
        <span class="meta-sep">
         by
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <a class="url fn n" href="http://3dinosaurs.com/wordpress/index.php/author/cassie/" title="View all posts by Cassie">
          Cassie
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="37416" data-link="http://3dinosaurs.com/wordpress/index.php/beach-sensory-bin/" data-summary="" data-title="Beach Sensory Bin">
        </div>
        <p>
         It is full on summer here and we are really enjoying it. We love to go to the beach but cant always happen. Today we are bring the beach to us! Check out our fun Beach Sensory Bin! This goes great with the
         <a href="http://3dinosaurs.com/wordpress/index.php/free-beach-pack/">
          Free Beach Pack
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <div align="Center">
         <img alt="Beach Sensory Bin - 3Dinosaurs.com" src="http://www.3dinosaurs.com/images/blog/7-2014/beachsensorybin.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <a data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonPin" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2F3dinosaurs.com%2Fwordpress%2Findex.php%2Fbeach-sensory-bin%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.3dinosaurs.com%2Fimages%2Fblog%2F7-2014%2Fbeachsensorybin.jpg&amp;description=Beach%20Sensory%20Bin%20with%20connectic%20sand%20-%203Dinosaurs.com">
          <img src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pinit_fg_en_rect_gray_20.png"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         With all our sensory bins or activities I really do try and team them up with a book. We read the book:
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1584690348/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=1584690348&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=3dinos-20&amp;linkId=WS5MZH5AVGCNLKCR">
          Seashells by the Seashore
         </a>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=3dinos-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=1584690348" style="border:none !important; margin:0px !important;" width="1"/>
         . I love the idea of looking for shells. This books also has information about different types of shells in the back of the book.
        </p>
        <p>
         What you need for this sensory bin activity:
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00DGXC0A6/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00DGXC0A6&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=3dinos-20&amp;linkId=3OLACD6T5O4EPMS5">
          sand
         </a>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=3dinos-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B00DGXC0A6" style="border:none !important; margin:0px !important;" width="1"/>
         , sea shells, cups,
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000NDRTVW/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000NDRTVW&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=3dinos-20&amp;linkId=7HK33YS4NSCCQ5LJ">
          hermit crab
         </a>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=3dinos-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B000NDRTVW" style="border:none !important; margin:0px !important;" width="1"/>
         and
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000NDTQWW/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000NDTQWW&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=3dinos-20&amp;linkId=LKFHJLDSUAB35374">
          crab
         </a>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=3dinos-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B000NDTQWW" style="border:none !important; margin:0px !important;" width="1"/>
        </p>
        <div align="Center">
         <img alt="Beach Sensory Bin - 3Dinosaurs.com" src="http://www.3dinosaurs.com/images/blog/7-2014/beachsensorybin1.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         The first thing they did was explore what was in the bit. They took out the crabs and went search for all the shells in the bin. They loved the feel of the sand.
        </p>
        <div align="Center">
         <img alt="Beach Sensory Bin - 3Dinosaurs.com" src="http://www.3dinosaurs.com/images/blog/7-2014/beachsensorybin2.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         After they got everything out they just wanted to play with the
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00DGXC0A6/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00DGXC0A6&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=3dinos-20&amp;linkId=3OLACD6T5O4EPMS5">
          sand
         </a>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=3dinos-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B00DGXC0A6" style="border:none !important; margin:0px !important;" width="1"/>
         . This is not just sand it is the connectic sand that is easy to mold and hold its shape and sticks to itself really easy.
        </p>
        <div align="Center">
         <img alt="Beach Sensory Bin - 3Dinosaurs.com" src="http://www.3dinosaurs.com/images/blog/7-2014/beachsensorybin3.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         They made some fun shell imprints into the sand and see how different each of the shells were.
        </p>
        <div align="Center">
         <img alt="Beach Sensory Bin - 3Dinosaurs.com" src="http://www.3dinosaurs.com/images/blog/7-2014/beachsensorybin4.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         They then just had fun with everything. They buried crabs and shells in the sand to find. They made a little while with the cups of the sad and just had fun seeing what they could create and do. They really enjoyed just exploring all parts of the bin that they could do.
        </p>
        <div align="Center">
         <img alt="Beach Sensory Bin - 3Dinosaurs.com" src="http://www.3dinosaurs.com/images/blog/7-2014/beachsensorybin5.jpg"/>
        </div>
