      About
     </h2>

    <br/>
    <h1 class="textesitalic">
     <strong>
      lodie
     </strong>
     works in Paris as a
     <strong>
      fashion illustrator
     </strong>
     since 2010 and developed her own realistic and poetic style.
     <br/>
     Although she prefers using traditional techniques, she enjoys experimenting with new ones regularly to try and inject more soul into her illustrations.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     lodie is fascinated by fashion and have been commissioned for a wide range of clients such as Nina Ricci, Ladure, Rochas, Galeries Lafayette Haussmann.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     She has exhibited in USA, Europe, Australia and her work has appeared in a number of publications including: Elle, Marie-Claire, Cosmopolitan and in several artbook such as 'Curvy 8', 'The Purple book' with Laurence King Publishers and most recently 'Illustration Now ! Fashion' with Taschen'.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     <br/>
     I had the chance to work for...
     <br/>
     <br/>
     <strong>
      Advertising :
     </strong>
     Galeries Lafayette Haussmann - Airbnb - Femme Actuelle - Victoria' s Secret - Bibliothque de Rouen - Rochas - Nina Ricci - Ladure - Labell - Miss Sporty - Casino du Qubec - Target - Open House - Kayra - Vision Plus - New Harbor - L'Oral -
     <br/>
     <br/>
     <strong>
      Press :
     </strong>
     Votre Beaut - Petra - Click - Cosmopolitan France - Marie-Claire Chine - Lufthansa - Ryerson University - Pharmacy Practice - Glamour Espagne - Marie-France magazine - BE - Shape - Elle Qubec - Paperjam - So Chic - Clin d'oeil - Lonsdale - Madame Figaro -
     <br/>
     <br/>
     <strong>
      Publishing :

     Taschen - Rizzoli - Milan - Bayard - Quarry Books - Curvy -
