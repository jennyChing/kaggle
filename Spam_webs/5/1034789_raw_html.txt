          <div class="csc-header csc-header-n3">
           <h1>
            PATTAYA
           </h1>
          </div>
          <h5>
           Thailands Favorite Seaside Resort for Business &amp; Pleasure
          </h5>
          <div class="begin">
           <p>
            Pattaya is about sea and sun and beachside fun, while its nightlife is in a class by itself. This resort town in Chon Buri Province, 90 minutes drive from Bangkoks Suvarnabhumi International Airport, is a place to indulge your hedonistic side, yet at the same time its a friendly, service-oriented destination that hosts countless top business events.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="teaser pull-left">
           <p>
            <img alt="" data-htmlarea-file-table="sys_file" data-htmlarea-file-uid="48730" height="22" src="/uploads/RTEmagicC_teaser1.jpg" title="teaser1.jpg" width="29"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Pattaya is about sea and sun and beachside fun, while its nightlife is in a class by itself.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" data-htmlarea-file-table="sys_file" data-htmlarea-file-uid="48732" height="22" src="/uploads/RTEmagicC_teaser2.jpg" title="teaser2.jpg" width="29"/>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Pattaya is another City of MICE thanks to its incredible variety of accommodations to fit all budgets, the diverse assortment of locations for creative meetings and incentives events, and three convenient and flexible exhibition centers designed with Thailands distinctive culture in mind.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Ambassador City Jomtien Hotel &amp; Convention Centre, Eastern National Sports Centre, and Pattaya Exhibition and Convention Hall (PEACH) combine for over 6,500 square meters of fully-functional business-event space, providing international organizers with versatility and exceptional value for money in a strategic and picturesque location.
          </p>
          <p>
           Pattayas push to become a leading MICE destination in Thailand  and in the ASEAN region  includes special packages for international guests, and has helped attract major events such as the Asia-Pacific Advertising Festival, Money &amp; Banking Magazines Money Expo, and the World Conference on Health Promotion.
          </p>
          <p>
           Award-winning facilities, endless opportunities for creative activities, and the famous Thai service touch are just some of the reasons why Pattaya is so popular with organizers focused on mixing business with pleasure and for creating memorable and productive business events.
          </p>
