        Super Bowl Party Appetizer  Red Velvet Cheese Ball

        <p>
         The Super Bowl is coming up in less than three weeks. Its Sunday, February 3rd,to be exact. Whether youre hosting the big game at your house or watching it somewhere else, finger foods and appetizers are usually the main attraction (besides the commercials, of course!), so I thought Id share a yummy Super Bowl party appetizer for you to try.
        </p>
        <p>
         For New Years Eve we went to a friends house and I made a delicious
         <a class="external" href="http://witandwhistle.com/2012/09/10/red-velvet-cheese-ball/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://witandwhistle.com']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          red velvet cheese ball
         </a>
         that you may have seen on Pinterest. It was a huge hit with everyone, so I thought Id make a variation for the Super Bowl party or
         <em>
          any
         </em>
         football partya football red velvet cheese ball.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://snapcreativity.com/?attachment_id=8356" rel="attachment wp-att-8356">
          <img alt="Superbowl appetizer - Football red velvet cheese ball" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8356" height="396" src="http://snapcreativity.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Football-red-velvet-cheese-ball.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Football Red Velvet Cheese Ball Recipe
        </h3>
        <h4>
         Ingredients:
        </h4>
        <p>
         1 box of red velvet cake mix
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2 cup butter at room temperature
        </p>
        <p>
         8 oz. cream cheese at room temperature
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2 cup powdered sugar
        </p>
        <p>
         2 T. brown sugar
        </p>
        <p>
         3/4 cup mini chocolate chips
        </p>
        <p>
         1 T. white chocolate chips
        </p>
        <p>
         Assorted cookies and crackers
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Football red velvet cheese ball ingredients" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8357" height="440" src="http://snapcreativity.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Football-red-velvet-cheese-ball-ingredients.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Directions:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         1. With an electric mixer, beat the cream cheese and butter until smooth. Add cake mix, powdered sugar and brown sugar. Mix until well blended.
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Turn the mixture out onto a piece of plastic wrap. Form into a football shape, wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 2 hours.
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Unwrap cheese ball and place on serving tray. Use white chocolate chips to make the laces on the football and cover remainder of ball with mini chocolate chips.
        </p>
        <p>
         4. Serve with your choice of cookies or graham crackers.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Football red velvet cheese ball instructions" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8354" height="574" src="http://snapcreativity.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Football-red-velvet-cheese-ball-process.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://snapcreativity.com/?attachment_id=8353" rel="attachment wp-att-8353">
          <img alt="Football red velvet cheese ball with chips" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8353" height="600" src="http://snapcreativity.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/football-red-velvet-cheese-ball-platter.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left">
         I served my red velvet cheese ball with Nilla Wafers and Mini Graham Crackers and both were delish! I guarantee this appetizer will be the hit of the party!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://snapcreativity.com/?attachment_id=8352" rel="attachment wp-att-8352">
          <img alt="Superbowl party appetizer - red velvet cheese ball" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8352" height="757" src="http://snapcreativity.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Football-red-velvet-cheese-ball-2.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>

         </a>
