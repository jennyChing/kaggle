         <br/>
         SUMMER! Summer is probably the best time to party hard. With
         <a href="http://docwalk.files.wordpress.com/2007/03/sleeping-francine1.jpg" target="_blank">
          Students
         </a>
         being off from school and people taking vacations to leave the ordinary routine of their lives, the sunny days in the middle of the year (at least for us) are a time to just go out and do something, anything! These collection of songs are not necessarily about
         <a href="http://www.themusicninja.com/tag/dirty-electro/">
          hard hitting, floor throbbing bass beats and frequency stretching melodies
         </a>
         that are so dirty you just have to mount someone. These collection of songs ARE about having a good time and relaxing at a pool party/bbq with warm electronic melodies that are not being overpowered by filthy and dirty bass beats (not that theres anything with that). Hopefully these songs will get you in the mood to go outside and enjoy your summer with friends as you get drunk over rooting for your country at the
         <img alt="funny-kid" class="alignright size-full wp-image-5934" height="217" src="http://themusicninja.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/funny-kid2.jpg" title="funny-kid" width="270"/>
         <a href="http://www.fifa.com/worldcup/" target="_blank">
          Fifa 2010 World Cup
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://themusicninja.net/newsongs/Kaskade-ft.-Polina-Human-Reactor.mp3" target="_blank">
           Kaskade ft. Polina  Human Reactor
          </a>
         </strong>
        </p>
