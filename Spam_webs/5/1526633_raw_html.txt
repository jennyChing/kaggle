      <h1 class="title">
       <a href="http://blog.abhinav.com/2015/07/interested-in-uk-immigration-choose-entrepreneur-tier-1-visa" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Interested In UK Immigration? Choose Entrepreneur Tier 1 Visa!">
        Interested In UK Immigration? Choose Entrepreneur Tier 1 Visa!
       </a>
      </h1>
      <div class="postmeta-primary">
       <span class="meta_date">
        July 13, 2015
       </span>
       <span class="meta_categories">
        <a href="http://blog.abhinav.com/category/uk-immigration" rel="category tag">
         UK Immigration Visa
        </a>
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="entry clearfix">
       <p>
        The European Union (EU) is in doldrums with Greece, Italy, Spain and many other members witnessing a nose diving economy. However, if you consider three of the economies that are acting as backbone from preventing the collapse of Euro Zone, you will definitely feel that Germany, the UK and France really have the power to make the economy stand.
       </p>
       <p>
        It is pertaining to strong and stable economy that the UK has been attracting maximum immigrants to live in their country and help the economy stand, fight and win.
       </p>
       <p>
        The investors are experiencing the best days in the UK with the government offering them innumerable benefits, like lower restrictions, simple tax payment, expropriation and compensation and more openness to foreign investors.
       </p>
       <p>
        Given this, in case you are looking for a safe and profitable haven for your money, you can always think about the UK in the first place. The
        <strong>
         UK Entrepreneur Tier 1 Visa
        </strong>
        and many other Investor Immigration Programmes provide the right dynamics that the investors need for immigration.
       </p>
       <p>
        If you want the
        <strong>
         UK Entrepreneur Tier 1 Visa
        </strong>
        , then first and foremost thing that would come in your mind would UK be the right choice. Well, let not doubts and scruples hover over your head, take this into consideration if you have any second thoughts.
       </p>
       <p>
        Since being the member of the G8, the British economy is strong and you end up getting more than you invest. At the same time, since it is one of the key members in the EU, you will get better flexibility to streamline the transportation and supply of goods across Europe, as well as in other continents.
       </p>
       <p>
        So, if you are contented and feel that your money will go in the right place, lets
        <a href="http://www.abhinav.com/uk-immigration/entrepreneur-visa.aspx">
         understand the UK Entrepreneur Tier 1 Visa Programme
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Under the scheme, you will have 3 years and 4 months of entry in the nation. During this time period, you can go for investments and look forward to business and revenue generation. The programme seeks for some special requirement and you must have 2, 00,000 Pounds available to invest in the economy of the country.
       </p>
       <p>
        For this programme, you are also asked to appear for a test and score certain points that are the basic need for streamlining the movement. The minimum point that one requires for streamlining the movement is 95. You are also tested on your English proficiency and you must present the best English skills for ensuring that you are able to grab the visa and undertake investment in this economy.
       </p>
       <p>
        Once you get the
        <strong>
         UK Entrepreneur Tier 1 Visa,
        </strong>
        you can bring your family along with dependent children in the nation. There is also possibility of Permanent Residency (PR) that you can claim as per the situation.
       </p>
       <p>
        If you have hired a good immigration attorney, they can help you traverse through everything in the best way. Always make sure that you keep all these things in mind while making a move.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Summary:
        </strong>
        If you are looking forward to the UK, you can always look forward to the UK Tier 1 Visa for movement.
       </p>

      <div class="postmeta-primary">
       <span class="meta_date">

       <p>
        Everyone once might have thought of making his way to the United States, and basically, who wouldnt think that ways reckoning the dynamism, living standards and opportunities that this country hurls at the immigrants. You get better medical facility, splendid education with almost top 5 colleges of the world based in the United States.

      <h1 class="title">
       <a href="http://blog.abhinav.com/2015/07/eb-5-visa-for-usa" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to All You Need To Know About EB-5 Visa For USA">
        All You Need To Know About EB-5 Visa For USA
       </a>
      </h1>
      <div class="postmeta-primary">
       <span class="meta_date">
        July 8, 2015
       </span>
       <span class="meta_categories">
        <a href="http://blog.abhinav.com/category/immigration/us-immigration/eb-5" rel="category tag">
         EB-5 Visa
        </a>
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="entry clearfix">
       <p>
        As per some international observers, perhaps no country in the world matches the credibility of the USA when it comes to investment. If one is looking for a stable economy where he can easily multiply his hard-earned money, then surely he cannot afford to ignore one country and that is the USA. The person should start thinking of applying for EB-5 Visa for the USA.
       </p>
       <h3>
        USA EB-5 Visa
       </h3>
       <p>
        It is basically an employment based category for business investors and widely popular the world over as EB-5 Visa. Till the 1990 Act of Immigration was not introduced, it was not possible for a foreign investor to acquire Permanent Residence (PR) in the country irrespective of the fact he, as an investor, was willing to make a huge investment.
       </p>
       <p>
        The US Congress introduced the EB-5 Visa for the USA with the aim to draw foreign investors who were able to create fresh job opportunities and stimulate the national economy. It is one of the easiest passages for those who have a net worth of at least US $1 million to acquire the much desired US Green Card (Permanent Residency).
       </p>

      <h1 class="title">
       <a href="http://blog.abhinav.com/2015/07/usa-l1-visa-a-fleeting-glance" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to USA L1 VisaA Fleeting Glance">
        USA L1 VisaA Fleeting Glance
       </a>
      </h1>
      <div class="postmeta-primary">
       <span class="meta_date">
        July 7, 2015
       </span>
       <span class="meta_categories">
        <a href="http://blog.abhinav.com/category/immigration/us-immigration" rel="category tag">
         US Immigration Visa
        </a>
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="entry clearfix">
       <p>
        The
        <strong>
         USA L1 Visa
        </strong>
        is a non immigrant visa, and valid for a short period of time, five years, and with extension the maximum stay is fixed to seven years. It is basically an intra company transport visa which allows groups, operating from the US and foreign country, to shift employees of certain specific classes (specialized knowledge stream, executives or managerial) from overseas destinations to the US.
       </p>
       <p>
        The permits are available to the workers of a global organization with bureaus in both the US and out-of-the-country. The permit enables such workers from abroad to move to the corporations US bureau post having offered their professional services for the firm for not less than one nonstop year inside the preceding three before entry in the US.
       </p>
       <p>
        It is mandatory that the US and the non-US recruiters are related in any of the four manners: parent &amp; subsidiary; branch and headquarters; sister firms held by a mutual parent; or affiliates held by the same or individuals in roughly the same proportions.
       </p>
       <p>
        Initially, the
        <a href="http://www.abhinav.com/usa-immigration/l-1-intra-company-transfer-visa.aspx" target="_blank">
         USA L1 visa was valid for large Multi National Companies
        </a>
        to transfer their employees to the country, but later small and startup groups were also included with the aim to expand their business venture and services to the country.
       </p>
       <p>
        In order to become eligible, the employee must have one year working experience in the last three yearseither with a branch office or parent or sister company outside the US.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         USA L1 VisaBasic Requirements
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Aspirants company is responsible for filling the L1 USA Visa and it has to be approved by the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS). Post approval, candidates are not allowed to work for any other company, except their sponsor company. Following are the basic requirements:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         No individual can apply for a visa on your behalf. Your company must apply for a L1 Visa.
        </li>
        <li>
         The visa can be issued to employees of certain class, for example, Manager or Executives.
        </li>
        <li>
         US office must present a letter stating that the services of the candidate are required in US office.
        </li>
        <li>
         The petition must clearly indicate that both the US office and the parent/sister office are legal and qualified organizations.
        </li>
        <li>
         The candidate must have an experience of not less than one year during the last three years, and is ready to take on comparable responsibilities in the US.
        </li>
        <li>
         Managers and Executives are allowed to reside in the country for seven years, and candidates with specialized knowledge are given the right to reside in the nation for five years.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        A candidate can also obtain a L1 visa if he is transferred to establish an office in the US on behalf of his/her company.
       </p>
       <p>
        Following conditions apply:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         The company in question should have proofs of enough capital required to establish a office in the US.
        </li>
        <li>
         To run the business, the company has to submit proofs that it has required infrastructure in the company.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        Now lets define the two categories in detail!
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Managers/Executives:
        </strong>
        The formal definition of the two designations is strict, and at the time of submitting the petition, detailed description of the duties will be required. To be precise, they should be responsible to supervise the staff/department or should be responsible for subdivision of the employer. To begin with, eligible candidates are issued a visa for three years which can be extended for further two years. The maximum number cannot extend seven years.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Specialized Knowledge Staff: I
        </strong>
        t includes candidates with complete knowledge of the firms services/goods, techniques, processes and administration. At first, eligible aspirants are given a L1B visa for a time-frame of three years that can be extended up to five years.
       </p>
       <p>
        What makes L1 visa exclusive and special is that it is a dual intent visa aspirants are not required to maintain a foreign dwelling which anyhow they have no intention of abandoning. The
        <a href="http://www.abhinav.com/usa-immigration/understanding-eb-1c-green-card-conditions-for-l-1a-visa-holders.aspx" target="_blank">
         L1 visa holder may submit an application for a Green Card
        </a>
        , or file for an adjustment of status or else present an application for an immigration visa.
       </p>
