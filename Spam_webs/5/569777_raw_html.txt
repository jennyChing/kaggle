                  <div class="text-content">
                   <p>
                    Spring has sprung! Ive always been a huge fan of the end of winter, with spring come april showers and of course springflowers!  We get to watch flowers bloom, adding a much needed mlange of colors to the drab wintertime palette of grays, we have become accustomed to during the winter.
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Here are some simple spring awaking tips to prep your home for the transition to the warmer weather:
                   </p>
                   <ul>
                    <li>
                     Never underestimate how much fresh flowers can brighten up a room!
                    </li>
                   </ul>
                   <ul>
                    <li>
                     Let the sun shine in; dust off your blinds and open your curtains.
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     If you can, change your bed linens: store your flannel sheets and opt for a more lightweight cotton bedding option.
                    </li>
                   </ul>
                   <ul>
                    <li>
                     Storing winter items: if your winter coats, sweaters, and shawls now need to go into hibernation, make sure to put some cedar wood in the closet to keep the moths away; and lavender to keep them smelling fresh.
                    </li>
                   </ul>
                   <p>
                    April showers bring May flowers, so keep an umbrella handy and have a splendid Spring!
                   </p>
                   <p>
                    Marie-Claire Rensch, Client Account Specialist
                   </p>
