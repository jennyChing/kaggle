            <p>
             <strong>
              To all politicians whove ever tried to block access to voting:
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             What the hell is the matter with you people?! You need to get your head on straight.
            </p>
            <p>
             The cornerstone of this great country is the right to vote  and you should be fighting to make sure that every citizen who can vote, does! But instead
             <em>
              youre creating obstacles for voters?
             </em>
             Well, that makes a lot of sense, doesnt it F*** that!
            </p>
            <p>
             People marched and fought and died for the right to vote. And you want to legislate away that sacrifice? Not on my watch!
            </p>
            <p>
             Your power comes from our votes  its time you started fighting voter discrimination instead of passing laws that disenfranchise more and more people in this country.
            </p>
            <p>
             We wont let you keep getting away with it. So get off your a**es and do the right thing!
            </p>
            <p>
             Love,
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Lewis Black &amp;
              <span id="supporter-count">
              </span>
              ACLU Supporters
             </strong>
            </p>

            </p>
