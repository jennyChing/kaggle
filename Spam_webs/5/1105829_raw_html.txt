       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I am always looking for economical ways to update and decorate our home. Today I am sharing my 12+ Ways to Update Your Home for Under $15. Enjoy as I take you along with my DIYprojects and creativity
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://wp.me/p2LxGy-21b" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="12+ Ways to Update Your Home for Under $15" class="aligncenter wp-image-7776 size-large" height="1024" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/12+-Ways-to-Update-Your-Home-for-Under-15-646x1024.jpg" width="646"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/12-ways-to-update-your-home-for-under-15/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/12+-Ways-to-Update-Your-Home-for-Under-15-646x1024.jpg&amp;description=12+ Ways to Update Your Home for Under $15')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span id="more-7761">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/stenciled-refreshed-rug/" target="_blank" title="Stenciled, Refreshed Rug">
          Paint and Stencil a rug you already own.
         </a>
         It will be refreshed and updated in a flash.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/stenciled-refreshed-rug/" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="painted and stenciled rug" class="aligncenter wp-image-7772 size-full" height="1020" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/painted-and-stenciled-rug.jpg" width="800"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/12-ways-to-update-your-home-for-under-15/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/painted-and-stenciled-rug.jpg&amp;description=12+ Ways to Update Your Home for Under $15')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Make your own
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/my-framed-cardboard-chalkboard/" target="_blank" title="My Framed Cardboard Chalkboard">
          Cardboard Chalkboard
         </a>
         and frame it with a thrifted frame.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/my-framed-cardboard-chalkboard/" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="cardboard chalkboard" class="aligncenter wp-image-7769 size-full" height="690" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/cardboard-chalkboard.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/12-ways-to-update-your-home-for-under-15/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/cardboard-chalkboard.jpg&amp;description=12+ Ways to Update Your Home for Under $15')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/monday-blues-lighting-the-way/" target="_blank" title="Monday Blues-Lighting the Way">
          Buy a string of patio lights
         </a>
         and hang them inside for a nice effect.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/monday-blues-lighting-the-way/" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="String of lights" class="aligncenter wp-image-7773 size-full" height="936" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/String-of-lights.jpg" width="770"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/12-ways-to-update-your-home-for-under-15/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/String-of-lights.jpg&amp;description=12+ Ways to Update Your Home for Under $15')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Head over to your local nursery and buy a few indoor plants. Place them in a decorative pot. Easy peasy.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/plants6.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="plants6" class="aligncenter wp-image-7757 size-large" height="1024" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/plants6-768x1024.jpg" width="768"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/12-ways-to-update-your-home-for-under-15/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/plants6-768x1024.jpg&amp;description=12+ Ways to Update Your Home for Under $15')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Take some time and buy your favorite thrifted plates and saucers. Hang them the easy way, with
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/easy-to-hang-decorative-plates/" target="_blank" title="Easy To Hang Decorative Plates">
          hot glue and a paper clip
         </a>
         . You can even frame them if you want
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/easy-to-hang-decorative-plates/" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Hang Plates with Hot Glue and Paper Clips" class="aligncenter wp-image-7770 size-full" height="842" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/hot-gplates.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/12-ways-to-update-your-home-for-under-15/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/hot-gplates.jpg&amp;description=12+ Ways to Update Your Home for Under $15')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Another thrifting tipBuy
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/use-thrifted-readers-digest-books-to-add-color-to-any-room/" target="_blank" title="Use Thrifted Readers Digest Books To Add Color to Any Room">
          Readers Digest books
         </a>
         in your home colors to add color and depth in your home.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/use-thrifted-readers-digest-books-to-add-color-to-any-room/" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="readers digest decor" class="aligncenter wp-image-7766 size-full" height="717" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/readers-digest-decor.jpg" width="510"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/12-ways-to-update-your-home-for-under-15/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/readers-digest-decor.jpg&amp;description=12+ Ways to Update Your Home for Under $15')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         I love green or blue glass bottles or jars. I purchased each one of these for $2 or less. Over time I have quite a collection. They look just fab in my Grandpas croquet box.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/green-bottles.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="green bottles" class="aligncenter wp-image-7768 size-full" height="501" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/green-bottles.jpg" width="800"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://meeganmakes.com/12-ways-to-update-your-home-for-under-15/&amp;media=http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/green-bottles.jpg&amp;description=12+ Ways to Update Your Home for Under $15')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>

         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/8-thrifted-chandy-makeover/" target="_blank">
          thrifted $8 Chandy
         </a>
         and transforming into something worthing of some crystals. She looks lovely above or dining table.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/8-thrifted-chandy-makeover/" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="$8 Thrifted Chandy Makeover" class="aligncenter wp-image-7767 size-full" height="858" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/chandy.jpg" width="716"/>

         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/diy-book-page-waste-basket/" target="_blank">
          bathroom waste basket I already owned
         </a>
         , I mod podged book pages and a lovely cut out on it. Its a perfect addition to our guest bathroom.

         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/diy-book-page-waste-basket/" target="_blank">

           <img alt="DIY Book Page Waste Basket" class="aligncenter wp-image-7763 size-full" height="1024" src="http://meeganmakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/diy-waste-basket.jpg" title="DIY Book Page Waste Basket" width="684"/>

         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/chari-makeover-chalk-paint-gold-wax/" target="_blank">
          a sample of chalk type paint and painted a $10 thrifted chair.

           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Cut some flowers from right outside your own front door.
         <a href="http://meeganmakes.com/enjoy-your-fresh-cut-flowers/" target="_blank">
          Fresh flowers add a crisp feeling to any home
         </a>
         .

         . It really is so fun and festive any time of year.
