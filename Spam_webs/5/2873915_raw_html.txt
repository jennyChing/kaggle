        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-109398" height="235" src="http://www.filmschoolrejects.com/images/avengers-movie-e1303839073839.jpg" title="avengers-movie" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Prepare yourself to be dazzled by a blurry image of a test poster for
         <strong>
          Joss Whedons
          <em>
           The Avengers
          </em>
         </strong>
         . Taken on the floor of the Licensing Expo in Las Vegas by the folks at
         <a href="http://www.comingsoon.net/news/movienews.php?id=78595" target="_blank">
          Coming Soon
         </a>
         , this first attempt at marketing Marvels big superhero team-up film is a colorful indication of what is to come. Or its just a first crack at it, with an artists representations of what the characters will look like. This includes the Mark Ruffalo Hulk, who looks more like the comic version of Hulk, and will likely not look anything like that in the film. Also, note Captain Americas modern day suit, complete with some brighter colors and that famous winged helmet. Thor is still bearded, still without his winged helmet. It all has an Ultimates vibe, and I dont think theres anything wrong with that.
         <span id="more-114412">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-114414" height="760" src="http://www.filmschoolrejects.com/images/avengers-testposter.jpg" title="avengers-testposter" width="568"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Update:
         </strong>
         The folks at
         <a href="http://www.badassdigest.com/2011/06/14/the-avengers-assembled" target="_blank">
          Badass Digest
         </a>
         have published another version of the poster, which clears things up a bit.
        </p>
