          U.S. Money Concepts
         </a>
        </h1>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <a class="bold" href="http://www.psfk.com/2014/10/reddit-user-kroner-dollar-redesign.html" target="_blank">
          Norwegian Currency Redesign Inspires U.S. Concepts
         </a>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         Last week, the Internet was set ablaze when the
         <a href="http://www.theatlantic.com/entertainment/archive/2014/10/norways-new-banknotes-are-works-of-art/381240/" target="_blank">
          winning designs for the new Norwegian kroner
         </a>
         were released. The central bank of Norway, Norges Bank, had asked for designers to submit proposals for a redesigned currency set for circulation in 2017. The winning proposal features a collaborative Norwegian effort; architecture firm Snhetta and graphic design firm The Metric System are each designing a side.
         <p>
          <img class="pic" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/norwegian-kroner-100.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          While the U.S. has made minor updates to the dollar in recent years for improved security, the basic designs and layout of American currency has remained mostly unchanged over the last 80 years. Until now.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img class="pic noborder" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/US-Currency-front-700x343.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Over Columbus Day weekend, an
          <a href="http://www.reddit.com/r/pics/comments/2iyhpy/the_us_dollar_beautifully_redesigned/" target="_blank">
           anonymous reddit user
          </a>
          posted beautiful new designs for the U.S. $5, $10, $20, $50, and $100 bills. Boasting a layout more closely aligned with currencies seen across Europe, and more than a splash of color to bring out the bold imagery, the redesigned bills are something to behold.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img class="pic noborder" src="/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/US-Currency-back-700x331.jpg"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          One side features intricate close-ups, showcasing everything from constellations and circuit boards to blood platelets and atoms. The reverse side is painted with full-color scenes depicting wide-open fields in the Midwest, skyscrapers, powerful ocean waves, an astronaut, and a mountainous landscape (most likely the Rockies).
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         i absolutely love that norwegian kroner money design  very slick, clean, and modern looking this design concept for the US bank notes are damn cool looking, too.
        </p>
