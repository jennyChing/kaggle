              <h2>
               Why Does My Company Need A Website?
              </h2>
              <p>
               In this era of advanced technology, it should be unheard of for a business to operate without an online presence. This is irrespective of the size of the business; both small and big business ventures ought to have a website.
               <strong>
                A website is an integral part of the companys identity
               </strong>
               in todays dynamic marketplace, and maintaining a strong web presence may make all the difference when it comes to your companys success.
               <a href="http://www.skillfulantics.com" title="Skillful Antics Web Design Orlando">
                <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                 <strong>
                  Skillful Antics
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </a>
               is here to help answering your question below.
              </p>
              <h2>
               A Website Increases Your Companys Credibility
              </h2>
              <p>
               A companys website is the first conveyer of its brand. It is the bearer of your companys image so it is
               <strong>
                important to invest in professional web design services
               </strong>
               . First impressions matter to potential customers, and your website may either attract them or make them look elsewhere. Even a small business with a
               <a href="http://www.skillfulantics.com/services/web-design-orlando-clermont/" title="Web Design by Skillful Antics">
                <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                 <strong>
                  professional and well designed website
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </a>
               can compete on the same level as big time companies; a well executed website makes a level playing field.
              </p>
              <h2>
               A Website is One of Your Most Powerful Marketing Tools
              </h2>
              <p>
               An online presence comes with great advantages. You are able to reach many more people than would be possible with other mainstream media advertising, because the internet is worldwide. It is not affected by time zones, neither is it limited to a particular region.
              </p>
              <p>
               That aside,
               <strong>
                companies that have websites rank higher in customer support services
               </strong>
               . Businesses are able to attend to their customers more quickly and efficiently, and they can receive and respond to feedback. Potential clients can also find and reach you very easily.
              </p>
              <h2>
               Promote and Showcase Your Companys Products on Your Website
              </h2>
              <p>
               The good thing about
               <a href="http://www.skillfulantics.com/services/online-marketing-orlando-clermont/" title="Online Marketing by Skillful Antics">
                <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                 <strong>
                  online marketing
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </a>
               is that it requires very little financial input. For instance, a video tutorial can be used to explain and demonstrate how to use a newly launched product. Company websites are also the best places to showcase your products. Purchases can also be made from your website. A company website gives the ultimate package; cheap advertising as well as increased sales.
              </p>
              <p>
               With a company website, your business gets the chance to stand on a global platform and to compete with other businesses in the same industry on a global scale. In addition to all the above mentioned benefits, you get to earn extra money from ads and merchant links placed on your website.
              </p>
              <p>
               Take a look at

                  Skillful Antics Portfolio

                 <strong>
                  Contact Us
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </a>
               today for a free estimate.
              </p>
