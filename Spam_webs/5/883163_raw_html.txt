          <h1 class="entry-title cb-entry-title cb-single-title" itemprop="headline">
           How I keep fit and healthy while traveling
          </h1>
          <div class="cb-byline">
           <div class="cb-author cb-byline-element">
            <i class="fa fa-user">
            </i>
            <a href="http://www.kwstyle.com/author/kenwenya/">

       <section class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>
         You may have your own planbut im sure there is something you can take from this. How I
         <strong>
          keep fit and healthy while traveling
         </strong>
         isnt as exhausting as it may sound. No, we dont have to log calories of foods that we barely know the name of, no there isnt too much working out. This is just a take on what I do when I travel.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_279" style="width: 310px">
         <a href="http://kwstyle.kwstyle.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/1510703_880002222016877_6588897824398634485_n2.jpg">
          <img alt="Coconut in Bali - Keeping fit and healthy while traveling" class="wp-image-279 size-full" height="400" src="http://kwstyle.kwstyle.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/1510703_880002222016877_6588897824398634485_n2.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Coconut in Bali  Keeping fit and healthy while traveling
         </p>
        </div>
        <h3>
         Look At The Food  Fit and healthy while traveling
        </h3>
        <p>
         Its ridiculous sometimes that people dont even know the basics of nutrition. They know salads can be healthy but then drench the salads in so much dressing, blindly adding a ton of sodium and sugars. Being over in Asia, Ive honestly tried to have anything and everything. That was a little exaggerated. Put it this way, I eat things that arent too
         <em>
          strange
         </em>
         . Im not one to go for insects and fried tarantulas, it just isnt in my taste buds.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Scan The Food  Fit and healthy while traveling
        </h3>
        <p>
         Most foods that you see either on the street or in a restaurant and such can easily be scanned with your own eyes to give you an idea of whats in it. I dont mean the Masterchef take on it, of identifying the herb and spices game. I mean the way it was cooked. Perhaps what it was cooked in. If the food is oily, it was probably fried. Fried foods contain an excess of saturated fat content and are normally better to avoid. Does that mean to avoid them like the plague for the rest of your life? Well, I love fried foods and do eat them on a regular basis.But if I need to prepare myself for a photo shoot and such for modeling work, I will strip all fried foods out of my diet completely. It is the better option for keeping fit and healthy while traveling especially.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_273" style="width: 330px">
         <a href="http://kwstyle.kwstyle.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/429601_414851968531907_561675634_n.jpg">
          <img alt="Keeping fit and healthy while traveling" class="wp-image-273 size-full" height="480" src="http://kwstyle.kwstyle.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/429601_414851968531907_561675634_n.jpg" width="320"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          One of my photo shoots  Keeping fit and healthy while traveling
         </p>
        </div>
        <h3>
         The Things You Cant See  Fit and healthy while traveling
        </h3>
        <p>
         One thing that is great about cooking and eating home meals is knowing exactly what you put inside. A lot of places, Asian especially, use a lot of salt. If it isnt salt, its soy sauce which is filled with sodium. Sodium is a killer. Literally. It can cause high blood pressure issues and so forth. But this is also an ingredient that we cant always see being added in. We may be in a restaurant for instance, and our meals would beprepared in the kitchen. This can create a hassle for someone who is wanting to either maintain themselves overseas or lose weight and come back home looking better than ever, which I highly recommend! So how can we choose our foods to keep fit and healthy while traveling?
        </p>
        <h3>
         Common Sense  Fit and healthy while traveling
        </h3>
        <p>
         A lot of it is common sense. Usually if something looks unhealthy, it is unhealthy. A lot of time and hassles can be done just by using your gut instinct. But sometimes there are those occasions where perhaps youre wanting to eat out socially with a bunch of friends and want to enjoy yourself rather than eating aplain salad. So let me give you an idea of what I do on a daily basis to keep fit and healthy while traveling andjust living in general!
        </p>
        <h4>
         What I look for:
        </h4>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Grilled foods  One of my favourites
         </li>
         <li>
          Steamed / Boiled foods  So healthy
         </li>
         <li>
          Raw foods  The best of the best
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Now dont think that all grilled, steamed and boiled foods are good for you! There are certain other things to take into account like the sauces and perhaps even the marinade used for the chicken and meat before they even cooked it. Try to avoid items on the menu which mention a lot of sauces and things added in. You want your herbs and spices in there, thats terrific flavour. But be wary of saucy items on the menu and also be cautious when adding sauce yourself as well. Tomato sauce isnt as healthy as it sounds!
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_280" style="width: 310px">
         <a href="http://kwstyle.kwstyle.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/1623499_877268688956897_4664055570154637926_n1.jpg">

        <h2>
         The Work Out Plan  Fit and healthy while traveling
        </h2>
        <p>
         I like to keep it simple. Why? Because the last thing you want is a big work out plan and not have access to those same facilities over seas. Hotel gyms are equipped with pretty standard gear. Youll normally have a few treadmills, a bike, a few light dumbbells and a big all-in-one cable machine that can be used for a range of different exercises. Some might have more equipment, some might not even have a gym at all! /facepalm. But keeping it simple is the key for you to stayingmotivated. Otherwise you just wont go.
        </p>
        <h4>
         Gym plan for my weeks:
        </h4>
        <ul>
         <li>
          3 sessions of weight exercises  2 muscle groups per session (ie, chest and triceps / biceps and back, etc)
         </li>
         <li>
          0-3 cardio sessions depending on what youve eaten and what you want to achieve!
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Now if you can find a proper gym to use while youre overseas, thats even better. But for those that dont go to the gym all that often, resort / hotel gyms will do just fine. Now we dont always have to do cardio when were traveling. Some of us are sedentry and like to sit around at the hotel and not do much in terms of movement, but there are also some that do go on tours and hikes on their vacations. Hikes are a great form of exercise and one of the best ways to
         <em>
          keep fit and healthy while traveling
         </em>
         . Cardio exercise is a great way to not only lose weight, but strengthen your heart and lungs. Just because someone is skinny and thin does not mean they have a healthy heart! The benefits of cardio are endless and can only aid into the longevity of your life as a whole.
        </p>
        <h2>
         What is the MOST important part?
        </h2>
        <p>
         In the end, for everything in life, the main thing we need to have is the consistency. You want to arrange your lifestyle to meet your health needs. Arrange it so it is enjoyable and not a chore. Make it a fun thing and let the results motivate you even more. Create that snowball effect of motivation and nothing can stop you!
        </p>
        <p>
         It has been a pleasure.I hope you can take something away from this and stay
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          fit and healthy while traveling
         </span>
         around the world!
        </p>
