        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Cinnamon Yogurt Blueberry Muffins
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-10-08T07:30:18+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 8, 2013
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.acedarspoon.com/author/juliacedar/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Julia
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.acedarspoon.com/blueberry-muffins/#comments">
           16 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="4749521" data-link="http://www.acedarspoon.com/blueberry-muffins/" data-summary="" data-title="Cinnamon Yogurt Blueberry Muffins">
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Hi everyone!
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          This past weekend was a fun filled one. I celebrated being another year older which was fun. My husband and son baked me a cake (German chocolate-yum!). It turned out great even though my son threw a few extra ingredients in! He is quite the boss in the kitchen! 
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/IMG_6202-e1381179316364.jpg">
          <img alt="Cooking" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-9804" height="1024" src="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/IMG_6202-e1381179316364-764x1024.jpg" width="764"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/IMG_6217.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_6217" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-9811" height="764" src="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/IMG_6217-1024x764.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          We went apple picking in Wisconsin which was so much fun. I now have a whole lot of apples to eat and use up! I always love spending a fall day among the apple trees and it was especially fun watching my son experience it and eat apple after apple off of the trees!
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/IMG_6304.jpg">
          <img alt="Apple picking" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-9800" height="1024" src="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/IMG_6304-1024x1024.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/IMG_6306.jpg">
          <img alt="apple picking" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-9801" height="1024" src="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/IMG_6306-1024x1024.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          And he picked out a pretty great pumpkin that needs carving.
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/IMG_6303-e1381178954681.jpg">
          <img alt="pumpkin" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-9799" height="1024" src="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/IMG_6303-e1381178954681-764x1024.jpg" width="764"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Sunday after a busy weekend always calls for a special treat. I love making things like pancakes, waffles, coffee cake or muffins to enjoy with my family on a lazy Sunday morning. These Cinnamon Yogurt Blueberry Muffins hit the spot and were easy to throw together Sunday morning.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Muffins-4.jpg">
          <img alt="Muffins " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9725" height="824" src="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Muffins-4.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I love that they use yogurthelping keep the muffins moist and adding that healthy component to them.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Muffins-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Muffins " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9724" height="523" src="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Muffins-1.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         You could easily substitute the blueberries for any berry that you have on hand and they would turn out great.
         <br/>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" id="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="er-top-box">
          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://www.acedarspoon.com/easyrecipe-print/9719-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Print
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
           Cinnamon Yogurt Blueberry Muffins
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="er-recipe-details">
          <div class="er-half-width er-float-left">
           <p>
            Prep time:
            <span>
             <time datetime="PT15M" itemprop="prepTime">
              15 mins
             </time>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            Cook time:
            <span>
             <time datetime="PT25M" itemprop="cookTime">
              25 mins
             </time>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            Total time:
            <span>
             <time datetime="PT40M" itemprop="totalTime">
              40 mins
             </time>
            </span>
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="er-half-width er-float-left" itemprop="nutrition" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/NutritionInformation">
           <p>
            Serves:
            <span>
             12
            </span>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="er-photo-details">
          <div class="er-photo-container">
           <img itemprop="image" src="http://www.acedarspoon.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/IMG_6202-e1381179316364-764x1024.jpg"/>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
           These Blueberry Cinnamon Muffins make a great breakfast treat or snack with the yogurt creating a moist texture.
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup whole wheat flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups minus 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon baking soda
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Pinch of salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tsp. cinnamon
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp. Nutmeg
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp. allspice
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp. cloves
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup vegetable oil
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 egg
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup low-fat plain yogurt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups fresh blueberries
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tsp. Vanilla Extract
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 385 degrees.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a large bowl sift flour, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a mixer mix together sugar, oil, vanilla, egg, and yogurt. Add the dry ingredients and mix on low until just combined.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add blueberries to mixture and stir for 5 seconds.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add mixture to muffin pan lined with muffin/cupcake liners (12 for large muffins, 18 for small).
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake 20 to 25 minutes (until a toothpick comes out clean) and allow to cool completely.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>

         </div>
        </div>

         </a>
        </p>

         Enjoy!
