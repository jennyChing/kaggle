        <p>
         -----&gt;LEARN ABOUT DRY FOOD&lt;-----

This article lays out some often-ignored principles of feline nutrition and explains why cats have a much better chance at optimal health if they are fed a canned food diet instead of dry kibble. Putting a little thought into what you feed your cat(s) can pay big dividends over their lifetime and very possibly help them avoid serious, painful, life-threatening, and costly illnesses.

        <p>
         ---&gt;LITTER BOX BEHAVIOR&lt;---

Urinating and defecating outside of the litter box, also known as inappropriate elimination, is one of the most common reasons for a cat to be relinquished to a shelter or, in some cases, abused.  
The fault usually involves a dirty box or one that is too small and it is perfectly appropriate for a cat to seek out a cleaner place to do his or her elimination.

         <p>
          My name is Josephine, Im from Sweden and have been very interested in cat behaviour for about 13 years now. I have had 10 cats during this time, and one thing that I always do with my cats, is to get to know them deeply. I do this by placing myself on the same level as them (yes, my floors are often very clean) =) and I just observe them. I LOVE to just watch them; when they eat, sleep, rest, play and even when they go to the bathroom. Because of this, I would like to help others to understand their cats, and learn how to do so.
         </p>
         <p>
          Right now, Im studing for my Diploma in Feline studies at Oxford College, and Im planning to take some more courses and hopefully classes here in Sweden.
          <br/>
          Im also a breeder of Ragdoll and Norwegian forest cat. I learn so much of every litter I have, and I can assist those who maybe find a pregnant stray cat on the streets, and help them with what to expect. I also know how to make everything ready and prepared for the mother to be. I read a lot about cats genetics, behaviour and breeding, all from educational books. I also join online forums where I can take part of other peoples problems and learn from them. That has been a fantastic manner to learn for the past two years.
         </p>
         <p>
          Besides that, Im also a catphotographer (www.kattfotograf.se) and donate a lot of my work to charity, to help cat shelters all over the world.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The idea behind this site:
          </strong>
          <br/>
          As an active member in the cat community, I see a lot of despair from cat owners that are about to put their cat on the streets, because they dont know why the cat is peeing on the bed, or not eating, or biting, etc. I want to help change this, by giving information about how cats behave and want to live. Not many owners know that this problem behaviour is the cats way of telling she is not ok.
          <br/>
          <strong>
           <em>
            <br/>
            <strong>
             Why is this site different from any other?
            </strong>
           </em>
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Simply, I want to do this without charging money for it! I dont work with cats, I live for cats. Why should cats suffer because their owner doesnt have the economics to call a help line that charges by minute? Why should we punish the cats, when they cannot choose their owner. Many might say the owner who doesnt afford to treat or get help for their cat, shouldnt have a cat and that might be true; but how does this help the cat that is sitting in an apartment with an owner that simply cannot afford to get help? Tell me that and I might change my mind on this statement.
         </p>
         <p>
          I will provide an online chat, where you can chat with me to ask about your cat.
          <br/>
          However I cannot answer your medical questions; a vet is more educated to answer and you can always call a vet for free to ask about this. If not, change vet!
          <br/>
          I will also provide links to organizations and funds where you can apply if you need help with paying for an operation or a vet bill in case you just cannot pay it.
          <br/>
          Besides this, I will update the site with new articles to learn about cats behavior. YOUR questions will help me to understand what need to be addressed and learned about.
         </p>
         <p>
          All of this is done by me in my spare time, as an entrepreneur I dont really have too much of that. But, I have put aside as much time as I can for this since I eat, sleep and poop cats !
         </p>
        </div>
