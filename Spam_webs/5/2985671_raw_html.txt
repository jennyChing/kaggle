        <p class="intro">
         Building an app is an ideal avenue for both entrepreneurs and intrapreneurs to achieve goals in areas such as productivity enhancement, user quisition, and revenue generation. One of the great parts about app development is that the investment cost is fairly self-contained. There arent many hidden costs and fees that will unbalance your calculations out of nowhere. How much it ends up costing varies heavily based on the goals of the project, so before we start, its important to note that the only reliable way to estimate cost is to talk through the idea with an expert.
        </p>

        <p>
         That said, lets start narrowing in on some estimates.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Platform Comparison
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Since app development is dominated by two major ecosystems, platform is always a significant factor in any project, and theres no right answer when it comes to choosing one or the other. Here are a few discrepancies that can contribute to costs:
        </p>
        <p>
         A developer account on Google Play costs just $25 up front, considerably less than the annual $99 fee for an equivalent account with Apple. Apart from that both app stores are comparable. Each collects 30% of revenue, which, all things considered, is a fair price for the benefits of using their infrastructure.
        </p>
        <p>
         Android development often comes with a bit more added work for optimizing to the wide variety of devices available. With the latest iPhone release, multi-device optimization has been complicated slightly on iOS, though still not to the same extent as Android. The other possible difference is that the approval process on Google Play is faster and less strict, so you are less likely to be forced into additional changes after you submit to the app store.
        </p>
        <p>
         Overall,

        </p>

        <p>
         <strong>
          Starting Cost
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         SDI offer project based pricing starting at $2000 for Android apps. For longer-term projects, or for clients who value more flexibility, our dedicated programmers can be assigned to your project for $2200 per programmer per month.
        </p>
        <p>
         Its hard to predict an apps cost without talking through the specific goals and features it aims to accomplish. For those of you trying to understand what kind of budget youll need for your app idea, it can be even harder to pinpoint a cost, since you have no precedents. For apps of average complexity, a project cost of $20,000 is a reasonable starting point
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Trying to keep costs low? Here are a few tips:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Design &amp; Develop under the same roof
         </li>
         <li>
          Look for talented programmers overseas
         </li>
         <li>
          Cut extra features in order to focus on the core experience
         </li>
         <li>
          Dont gamble on inexperienced teams. Sure they might lowball the price, but if they fail to deliver youll only end up paying more in the long run
         </li>
        </ul>
