     <h2 itemprop="name">
      QUICK TIPS FOR LASH STRIPS
     </h2>
     <meta content="2012-07-25" itemprop="datePublished"/>
     <div class="entry">
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_7189" style="width: 522px">
       <a href="http://thebeautydepartment.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/TBD-mastering-falsies.jpg">
        <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-7189" height="1175" src="http://97.74.65.162/wp-content/uploads-c/2012/07/TBD-mastering-falsies.jpg" title="TBD mastering falsies" width="512"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        photos + post by amy nadine, graphic design by eunice chun
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       Lash strips arent just for burlesque dancers! I use
       <a href="http://bit.ly/PIODry" target="_blank">
        these delicate ones
       </a>
       on my clients all the time for a look that is very natural and feminine. So today Id love to teach you my favorite tricks for applying them that make it easy and foolproof.
       <span id="more-7188">
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       TOOLS: A
       <a href="http://bit.ly/PIODry" target="_blank">
        pair of lash strips
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://bit.ly/P03nO0" target="_blank">
        lashglue
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://bit.ly/Qjm72j" target="_blank">
        cuticle scissors
       </a>
       , a q-tip and an
       <a href="http://bit.ly/NXBszF" target="_blank">
        eyeliner brush
       </a>
      </p>
      <ol>
       <li>
        Squeeze a dollop of glue onto the back of your hand or a surface that you can wipe it off of after. Wait
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         two minutes
        </span>
        for the glue to get tacky. Dip your eyeliner brush into the glue.
       </li>
       <li>
        Sweep the brush along your upper lashline like you would a gel eyeliner.
       </li>
       <li>
        Place the dry lash strip onto the glue line. By doing it this way instead of applying the glue onto the lash strip, you are
        <em>
         eliminating the chance for error
        </em>
        when you try to pop the lash strip on and it hits other areas of your lid/lashes.
       </li>
       <li>
        With your scissors, cut a q-tip in half and use the cut end to tap along the base of the strip to secure it to the skin.
       </li>
      </ol>
      <p>
       Follow with mascaraor for additional drama, trace over the base of the strip with a black liquid liner. {Thanks to my cuzzie Emily for modeling for me!}
      </p>
