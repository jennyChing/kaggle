              <h1 class="story-title entry-title" itemprop="name headline">
               Actress Neetu Agarwal booked under Sandalwood Smuggling
              </h1>
              <div class="relative" id="right-content">
               <div class="left relative" id="content-area" itemprop="articleBody">
                <div class="relative" id="feat-img-reg">
                 <img itemprop="image" src="http://www.uncutindia.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Neetu-Agarwal-with-Mastan-Vali-1000x600.jpg"/>
                </div>
                <!--feat-img-reg-->
                <div class="hide-title">
                 <span class="entry-title" itemprop="name headline">
                  Actress Neetu Agarwal booked under Sandalwood Smuggling
                 </span>
                </div>
                <!--hide-title-->
                <div id="left-content-mobi">
                 <div class="author-info-mob-wrap">
                  <span class="vcard author">
                   <span class="author-name left fn" itemprop="author">
                    <a href="http://www.uncutindia.com/author/dhana/" rel="author" title="Posts by dhana">
                     dhana
                    </a>
                   </span>
                  </span>
                  <div class="post-date-wrap left relative post-date-mob">
                   <span class="post-date">
                    <time class="post-date updated" datetime="2015-04-23" itemprop="datePublished" pubdate="">
                     Apr 23, 2015
                    </time>
                   </span>
                  </div>
                  <!--post-date-wrap-->
                 </div>
                 <!--author-info-mob-wrap-->
                </div>
                <!--left-content-mobi-->
                <p>
                 Tollywood actress Neetu Agarwal (Prema Prayanam movie fame), is missing ever since the Kurnool police began searching for her over her connection with red Sandalwood smuggler Mastan Vali. The police filed FIR against Neetu Agarwal at Rudravaram in Kurnool district of Andhra Pradesh.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The actress Neetu Agarwal has disappeared since her name has come to light in connection with the case. The police dispatched manhunt in Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, to get information about their whereabouts. Meanwhile, all the bank accounts of the actress are seized.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Red Sandalwood Smuggler Mastanvali entered into filmy business with his smuggling money. He produced love story Prema Prayanam in 2013 which was bombed at Box Office. Mastanvali booked Neetu Agarwal as Heroine for his film. Though the film turned to be huge disastrous, Mastanvali is continuing his living relationship with his actress.
                </p>
                <p>
                 According to the sources, Mastanvali presented 35 lakhs valuable Flat to Neetu Agarwal as a gift in Hyderabad. It came to known that there are many transactions take place between Mastanvali and Neetu Agarwal accounts. Neetu Agarwal was escaped after the enquiry started. Police has seized Neetu Agarwals bank accounts. Red Sandalwood Smugglers gave valuable gifts like ornaments and flats to Telugu and Tamil heroines.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Mastan Vali tried his luck in politics. He spent a large sum on election campaign and was elected as MPTC for YSRCP party. He landed in problem following the change in the state government in 2014.Finally, Mastan Vali was arrested by police in Kurnool district Shirivella. However, he was granted bail later.
                </p>
