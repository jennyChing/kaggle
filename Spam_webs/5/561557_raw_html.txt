        <h1 class="entry-title">
         The DJI Phantom 3 Review:  A first look
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="onDate date updated">
          <i class="icon-time icon-metas" title="Date">
          </i>
          <a href="http://www.thedroneinfo.com/2015/04/15/dji-phantom-3-a-first-look-from-flyt-path/" rel="bookmark">
           April 15, 2015
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <h2>
          Hands on with the DJI Phantom 3
         </h2>
         <p>
          Aldryn Estacio of FLYT PATH wrote this excellent first look at the
          <strong>
           DJI Phantom 3
          </strong>
          . We are republishing this with his permission. We recommend checking out
          <a href="http://flytpath.com/dji-phantom-3-first-look-review/" target="_blank">
           Aldryns work at his site.
          </a>
         </p>
         <div class="wpb_row vc_row-fluid standard_section " id="fws_552e6709cdc90">
          <div class="col span_12 dark ">
           <div class="vc_span12 wpb_column column_container col" data-animation="" data-delay="0">
            <div class="wpb_wrapper">
             <div class="col span_12 section-title text-align-center extra-padding">
              <h2>
               Phantom 3
              </h2>
              <div style="float:right;">
               <h4 style="text-align:center">
                Buy Now
               </h4>
               <p>
                <a href="http://click.dji.com/1mCymhsBczOgFWpd" target="_new">
                 <img class="lazy lazy-hidden" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://www.thedroneinfo.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/phantom3.jpg" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="250"/>
                 <noscript>
                  <img src="http://www.thedroneinfo.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/phantom3.jpg" width="250"/>
                 </noscript>
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <p align="left">
               The Phantom 3 was recently announced and Im excited to share with you my experience with this amazing craft. DJI released two versions of the Phantom 3, the
               <a href="http://store.dji.com/product/phantom-3-professional?position=1" target="_blank" title="http://store.dji.com/product/phantom-3-professional?position=1">
                Professional Version
               </a>
               (4k video) and the
               <a href="http://store.dji.com/product/phantom-3-advanced?position=1" target="_blank" title="http://store.dji.com/product/phantom-3-advanced?position=1">
                Advanced Version
               </a>
               (1080p 60fps video). Ive been test flying the Phantom 3 Advanced now for the past couple weeks and was able to capture some great photos utilizing the new technology integrated into this machine. I currently own a DJI Phantom 2 with H3-3D gimbal (GoPro) and a DJI Inspire 1, so I was so pleased to see that DJI merged all the good features of each into this simple, out of the box solution at a very reasonable price. This first look review is not to go through all the specs of the P3 (DJI.com
               <a href="http://www.dji.com/product/phantom-3" target="_blank" title="http://www.dji.com/product/phantom-3">
                click here
               </a>
               ) but instead I want to show you some features I really liked and how I use the craft.
              </p>
             </div>
             <div class="clear">
             </div>
             <div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
              <div class="wpb_wrapper">
               <p>
                All photos on this Phantom 3 first look review were shot by Aldryn Estacio, please do not edit, copy, or reuse any photos without written permission.
               </p>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="divider">
             </div>
             <p>
              <img alt="DJI Phantom 3" class="lazy lazy-hidden img-with-animation alignnone" data-animation="fade-in" data-delay="150" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://flytpath.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/03.19.15_DJI_P3_Unbox-6.jpg" height="1568" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="2048"/>
             </p>
             <noscript>
              <img alt="DJI Phantom 3" class="img-with-animation alignnone" data-animation="fade-in" data-delay="150" height="1568" src="http://flytpath.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/03.19.15_DJI_P3_Unbox-6.jpg" width="2048"/>
             </noscript>
             <div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
              <div class="wpb_wrapper">
               <p>
                Whats in the box? Dont forget that I received a beta test version so packaging may be different from final product.
                <br/>
                The Phantom 3 Advanced came in an egg crate style package. The box included the Phantom 3, new controller (similar to Inspire 1), 8 self-tightening props that came in a DJI bag, extra dampers, tools, stickers and a charger. You will need to provide your own mobile device in order to connect the P3 to the DJI Pilot app. To view which device DJI recommends,
                <a href="http://www.dji.com/product/phantom-3/spec" target="_blank" title="http://www.dji.com/product/phantom-3/spec">
                 click here
                </a>
                . Final Phantom 3 Advanced package could change by the time of release. There is also a possible DJI backpack that may come out with the Phantom 3 Professional version. We will also see if thats included when released.
               </p>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="divider">
