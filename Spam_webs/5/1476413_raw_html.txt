            <h1 class="arw-postheader">
             <span class="arw-postheadericon">
              The wedding is done and now off to the wedding reception!
             </span>
            </h1>
            <div class="arw-postheadericons arw-metadata-icons">
             <span class="arw-postdateicon">
              <span class="date">
               Published
              </span>
              <span class="entry-date" title="1:05 pm">
               February 12, 2013
              </span>
             </span>
             |
             <span class="arw-postauthoricon">
              <span class="author">
               By
              </span>
              <span class="author vcard">
               <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.stepandrepeatla.com/author/jewel/" title="View all posts by Jewel">
                Jewel
               </a>
              </span>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div class="arw-postcontent">
             <!-- article-content -->
             <p>
              The tables are placed and draped in the brides colors with matching linen and beautiful place settings. The center pieces look exquisite, just the right height so the guests can see and talk with one another.
             </p>
             <div>
              The band is set up and the dance floor shines.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              The wedding cake is set up on its own table with subtle lighting to show off the beautiful design.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              And the step and repeat backdrop, red carpet and special lighting is all arranged just waiting for the fun to begin.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              The guests arrive.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              They look around and find their seats at their tables and the band starts playing softly. The waiters serve drinks while everyone gets situated.
             </div>
             <div>
              As everyone starts to take in all of the special details of the room, they start to notice the banner and red carpet. Whispers and giggles can be heard as the banner is pointed out and little nervous sighs and smiles can be seen on some of the faces.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              The waiters continue to fill glasses with champagne and point out the bar where the guests can order a variety of drinks and are told they can help themselves.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              The Bride and Groom arrive!
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              There is much cheering, applause and hand waving. The couple meet and greet all their guests and all sit down for the meal. Afterward, the toasts are made and the partying begins.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              The bride and groom have their dance and then the bride with her father. Eventually everyone joins in and the couple head on over to the step and repeat backdrop.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              The photographer meets them in front of the banner and the groom picks up the bride for the first photos. Their laughter is contagious and as they start posing and clowning, they start attracting friends and family who come over to watch.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              But not for long. The bride finds her friends and pulls them in front of the backdrop and the cameras click and the videos roll.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              A box is pulled out from behind the banner and crazy hats, boas, mustaches, noses and glasses come out and everyone starts getting into the fun. Pretty soon there is a big crowd and everyone is laughing and having a great time while the photo op continues.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              People are taking pictures with their camera phones and get excited showing off what they captured of their friends and family.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              What a great wedding reception!
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              Everyone had fun and got to participate and now have great mementos of a fabulous time.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              After the cake is cut and good-byes are said, the bridal couple head out for their honeymoonamidstcheers and best wishes.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              A great time was had by all.
             </div>
             <div>
             </div>
             <div>
              Contact Step and Repeat LA for your special backdrop today.
             </div>
             <div>
              May your special day be as great!
             </div>
