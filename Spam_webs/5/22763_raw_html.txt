            <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 150%; text-align: center;">
             <br/>
             <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">
              <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 255);">
               <span new="" style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 150%;" times="">
                Many Mouths - Same Truth
               </span>
              </span>
             </b>
            </p>
            <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;line-height:150%">
             <br/>
             <span new="" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;font-family:" times="">
              Over the years, I have had theprivilegeof witnessing and participating in many communions  physically and metaphysically. Usually, in planes other than earth, the communions are more like a sharing of consciousness or conscious awareness. This means, ones thought becomes everyones understanding. There are no questions and answers because there is no intellect to question. Silence carries the awareness. Here are some excerpts. There are much more. Some day, I hope to share more.
             </span>
             <br/>
            </p>
            <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 150%; text-align: center;">
             <b>
              <span new="" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;font-family:" times="">
               The Power of LOSING
              </span>
             </b>
            </p>
            <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 150%; text-align: left;">
             <br/>
             <span new="" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;font-family:" times="">
              We sat under the tree in a state of absolute bliss and overwhelming gratefulness. I prostrated at His feet mentally. He acknowledged by clasping his hands and extending pranaams. Whenever our eyes locked, we offered pranaams and Namaskaars to each other. The bliss of being with a complete renunciate!!!
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span new="" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;font-family:" times="">
              He smiled internally. It is similar to the smile of very small children during sleep. Nobody can understand the reason behind those smiles and the tears of those tiny babies. He smiled and looked into my eyes again. Then looked away. I was just watching and inhaling the overwhelming presence of a Great Master. About eleven of us sat under the tree, on the banks of Ganges, close to the Avadhoota.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span new="" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;font-family:" times="">
              He said: All of you have come here from afar. Why have you come searching for me? What can I give you which you already do not have? I have nothing to give anyone. All I have, you have too. Why did you have to come spending so much money and time? To see a naked, mad man? and he laughed again. This was all in Hindi.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
            </p>
            <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; line-height: 150%; text-align: center;">
             <br/>
             <span new="" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;font-family:" times="">
              <span style="mso-ignore:vglayout">
               <img alt="" src="/files/blogfiles/images/71262/Slide2(6).jpg" style="width: 367px; height: 275px;"/>
              </span>
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 255);">
              <b style="">
               <span new="" style="font-size: 12pt; line-height: 150%;" times="">
                A Spiritual Emperor
               </span>
              </b>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt;line-height:150%">
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span new="" style="font-size:12.0pt;line-height:150%;font-family:" times="">
              I said: You called us. We came. Without your calling, we cannot come here. All we need is your blessings. He looked into my eyes again. Then looked away, into eternity for sometime. He said: Do you hear the birds singing? How did you hear that? Your mind went there to the tree along with your ears? Mind should not go away from you. It should stay within you. The birds will sing, but, will be within you. There are no birds, outside of you. Everything is within you. Do you understand me? I nodded. He was not talking. He was commanding. His words were commands which must work within all listeners. I felt His power and depth. The authority was astounding. A man who remains unclothed and unattached expressed the conviction and authority of an Emperor. That was his stature.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
