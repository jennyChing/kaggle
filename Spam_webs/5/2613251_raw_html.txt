       <h1 class="margin-bottom20">
        3 Traits Small Business Owners and Olympians Share
       </h1>
       <img alt="Olympian Blog Post_021014" class="full-width block wp-post-image" height="152" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Olympian-Blog-Post_021014.jpg" width="600"/>
       <div class="text-color-pale margin-bottom20 image-excerpt">
       </div>
       <div class="author_wrapper text-color-pale margin-bottom20">
        Eric Ellis
        <br/>
        <img alt="Eric Ellis" class="avatar" height="50" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Eric-Ellis-Profile.jpg" width="50"/>
        on February 10, 2014 - 9:50 am in
        <a href="/blog/category/small-business/" rel="category tag">
         Small Business
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="/blog/category/startups/" rel="category tag">
         Startups
        </a>
       </div>
       <!-- author_wrapper -->
       <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="15962196" data-link="https://ww.deluxe.com/blog/three-traits-small-business-owners-olympians-share/" data-summary="" data-title="3 Traits Small Business Owners and Olympians Share">
       </div>
       <p>
        When you think of small business owners and what it takes to start their own company, there are many traits and characteristics they tend to share. Last year, we conducted a survey of small business owners to uncover what sets them apart from other professions. The study,
        <a href="http://phx.corporate-ir.net/phoenix.zhtml?c=61257&amp;p=irol-newsArticle_Print&amp;ID=1820775&amp;highlight=" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://phx.corporate-ir.net/phoenix.zhtml?c=61257&amp;p=irol-newsArticle_Print&amp;ID=1820775&amp;highlight=', 'The DNA of a Small Business Owner');">
         The DNA of a Small Business Owner
        </a>
        , revealed seven distinct attitudinal clusters ranging from all heart to passionately confident.
        <span id="more-9842">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/DLX_DNA_Survey_Infographic.jpg">
         <img alt="Deluxe DNA Infographic" class="size-large wp-image-9882 aligncenter" height="1024" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/DLX_DNA_Survey_Infographic-341x1024.jpg" width="341"/>
        </a>
        Not surprisingly, Olympians have similar traits to business owners including characteristics such as determination, heart, and perfectionism. Additional
        <a href="http://www.sirc.ca/newsletters/mid-july08/documents/S-840515.pdf" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'download', 'http://www.sirc.ca/newsletters/mid-july08/documents/S-840515.pdf');">
         research
        </a>
        conducted by the Sports Information Resource Centre (S.I.R.C) in Canada uncovered similar information to our study, identifying the following top characteristics shared by Olympians and small business owners:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Genetics
         </strong>
          we found genetics to be a primary indicator on whether or not someone becomes a small business ownerespecially if a family member was a business owner. The same was said about Olympians  that genetics make them predisposed to be a star athlete.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Focus
         </strong>
          We also found that a clear sense of focus is important to own a thriving business and that it takes time to make this a successful formula. Olympians also agreed that becoming a great athlete honing skills worthy of the Olympics also takes time to master.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Self-Development
         </strong>
          Both small business owners and Olympian athletes alike agreed that a period of self-development is important in becoming successful in their craft. Particularly, a period of self-awareness is needed to steer themselves in the right direction.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <em>
          Now the real test!

         DNA of a Small Business Owner, Winter Olympics Edition
        </a>
        quiz.
