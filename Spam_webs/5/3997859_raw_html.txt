         <h2>
          This item is part of the Following Collections
         </h2>
         <div class="collection">
          <h3 class="name">
           <a href="/collections/5">
            The Rose Collection
           </a>
          </h3>
          <div class="description">
           <p>
            Our Rose Garden is, to date, our most popular collection. In the modeling process, each petal is hand-formed, each leaf vein is etched in under magnification. We do all of this work right here in Northern Michigan, where the summers are short and glorious, and we need a bit of floral inspiration to last us through the wicked winters!
           </p>
           <p>
            The petal and leaf components are then sometimes molded, allowing us to rebuild specific rose parts over and over. This helps us keep many items in the Rose collection affordable. Some roses (and other blossoms) are still one-off items, and these are all marked, as they are collector's items. The vine bands and curling trellises of detail are also hand-formed, many of the curls following a natural Fibonacci sequence in diameter and bend.
           </p>
           <p>
            Custom work in the Rose line is welcome, but many custom pieces will cost more than the original items shown. The reason for this is the same as in any design field: ready-to-wear items will always cost less than made-to-order couture. Many items show a starting price for custom, feel free to contact us for more detailed pricing.
           </p>
           <p>
            All designs copyright and exclusive to Wexford Jewelers. To see some of our custom work in this line, visit our Facebook page.
           </p>
