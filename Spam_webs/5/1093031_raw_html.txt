          <div class="entry_widget_large entry_widget_right" id="asset-9258424">
           <span class="adv-photo-large">
            <img alt="mendenhall-run-vert-ap.jpg" class="adv-photo" height="487" src="http://media.cleveland.com/browns_impact/photo/9258424-large.jpg" width="380"/>
            <span class="photo-data">

          <p>
           Pittsburgh Steelers running back Rashard Mendenhall isnt convinced Osama bin Laden was involved in 9/11.
          </p>
          <p>
           Seriously.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7E3oIbO0AWE">
            Apparently, Mendenhall isn't alone.
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Mendenhall also wonders why so many Americans celebrated the news of bin Laden's death.
          </p>
          <p>
           Mendehall
           <a href="http://twitter.com/#%21/R_Mendenhall">
            posted such things on his Twitter
           </a>
           which includes:
          </p>
          <blockquote dir="ltr" style="margin-right: 0px;">
           <blockquote dir="ltr" style="margin-right: 0px;">
            <p>
             <strong>
              We'll never know what really happened. I just have a hard time believing a plane could take a skyscraper down demolition style.
              <br/>
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              I'm not convinced he was even behind the attacks we have really seen no evidence to prove it other than the gov telling us.
              <br/>
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              What kind of person celebrates death? It's amazing how people can HATE a man they have never even heard speak. We've only heard one side.
             </strong>
            </p>
           </blockquote>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           It's bad enough that Mendenhall plays for the Steelers, and of course everyone has the right to their own opinion, but how will these comments fly when the Steelers visit the other eight cities on their schedule?
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
