         <p style="text-align: center;">
          This
          <em>
           Cinnamon Roll Cake
          </em>
          has all the features of your favorite roll and is made entirely from scratch!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Cinnamon Roll Cake from scratch @crunchycreamysw Cinnamon Roll Cake (from scratch) Recipe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10666" height="768" src="http://cdn.crunchycreamysweet.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Cinnamon-Roll-Cake-from-scratch-@crunchycreamysw.jpg" title="Cinnamon Roll Cake (from scratch) Recipe" width="512"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          ~~~A lot of you had questions regarding spreading the cake and how its suppose to look like before and after baking, so Ive updated the post with step-by-step photos. Hope this helps!~~~
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Friends, I am absolutely crazy about this cake. Crazy! I am so excited to share it with you today!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Just like with these
          <a href="http://www.crunchycreamysweet.com/2013/03/07/lemon-burst-cookies-from-scratch/" target="_blank" title="Lemon Burst Cookies {from scratch}">
           <em>
            Lemon Burst Cookies
           </em>
          </a>
          (that most recipes call for cake mix but I was able to make them from scratch), I have been looking for a good recipe for a
          <strong>
           Cinnamon Roll Cake
          </strong>
          . So many recipes use a cake mix, a tube of biscuits or even cinnamon rolls. I dont buy these because I love to make them using real ingredients, like flour and eggs. So I am here to tell you today that it absolutely possible to make a Cinnamon Roll Cake
          <strong>
           from scratch
          </strong>
          , which means that all you need is basic staples and you can make an
          <strong>
           irresistible
          </strong>
          treat without sacrificing the taste or having shattered expectations.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span id="more-4157">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="Cinnamon Roll Cake Cinnamon Roll Cake (from scratch) Recipe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10667" height="768" src="http://cdn.crunchycreamysweet.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Cinnamon-Roll-Cake.jpg" title="Cinnamon Roll Cake (from scratch) Recipe" width="512"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Because this cake
          <strong>
           tastes just like a cinnamon roll
          </strong>
           everyones favorite sweet roll.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Every layer of this cake (the base, the cinnamon filling and the glaze) is made in just minutes using ingredients that you usually have in your pantry. I love it!!
         </p>
         <p>
          <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
          </script>
          <!-- CCSbodylink -->
          <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-1800293909850946" data-ad-slot="6984834510" style="display: inline-block; width: 468px; height: 15px;">
          </ins>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Cinnamon Roll Cake indulgent cake for cinnamon rolls lovers Cinnamon Roll Cake (from scratch) Recipe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-10668" height="768" src="http://cdn.crunchycreamysweet.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Cinnamon-Roll-Cake-indulgent-cake-for-cinnamon-rolls-lovers.jpg" title="Cinnamon Roll Cake (from scratch) Recipe" width="512"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <script>
           // &lt;![CDATA[
(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
// ]]&gt;
          </script>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Its not only made from scratch but so ridiculously easy to make! Everything comes together so quickly and no special equipment is needed. The base is a delicious
          <strong>
           yellow butter cake
          </strong>
          with a perfect moist and delicate crumb. Melts in your mouth! The
          <strong>
           cinnamon filling
          </strong>
          is incredible and as soon as you make it  you will want to put it on everything! Heck, go ahead and smear your morning bagel with it! It is so good! The
          <strong>
           vanilla glaze
          </strong>
