       <div class="photoset" data-entry-id="EKU0CN2S24EXCFBOLK" data-entry-url="/id/The-Best-Damn-Pen-Shooter-Period./step4/Loading-the-ammo/" id="photoset-EKU0CN2S24EXCFBOLK">

            <img alt="PICT0097.JPG" class="photoset-photo" src="http://cdn.instructables.com/FCV/GFPK/PFMEXCFBGS3/FCVGFPKPFMEXCFBGS3.MEDIUM.jpg" style="width:600px;"/>

       <div class="txt step-body">
        Use the ink cartridge as the ammo.
        <br/>
        Insert it into the wide hole of the barrel so that the pointy end goes in last.
        <br/>
        Let it slide all the way to the bottom.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        If you are using an Office Depot pen, the ink cartridge will fit in there very nicely.
