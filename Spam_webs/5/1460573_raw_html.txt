       <p class="author">
        Posted by the Mass Office of Travel and Tourism
       </p>
       <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
       <p>
        Whether youre seeking a maritime, cultural or culinary adventure, you can find it all
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/northBoston/" target="_blank">
         North of Boston
        </a>
        !
       </p>
       <p>
        Here are 10 must-see attractions and activities located in the
        <strong>
         North of Boston region
        </strong>
        that every traveler should have on their list:
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.seethewhales.com/index.php" target="_blank">
         Cape Ann Whale Watch
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Hop aboard for an exhilarating whale watching tour of the Cape Ann and Greater Boston area thats both fun and educational! Youll get the chance to learn about whale ecosystems, protection and the different types of whales in the area, all while enjoying your time on one of the largest and fastest whale watching vessels in Massachusetts. For a whale watching schedule,
        <a href="http://www.seethewhales.com/index.php?link=schedule" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Whale-watch.jpeg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3400" height="419" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Whale-watch.jpeg" title="Whale watch" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.thacherisland.org/climbing.html" target="_blank">
         Twin Lighthouses
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Located on Rockports Thacher Island, the twin lights are among the oldest lighthouses in America. The original 45-foot towers were built all the way back in 1771, while the newer versions finished construction in 1861. The twins are also the only multiple surviving lights in the entire country, and visitors are allowed to climb them. Find out how you can visit Thacher Island
        <a href="http://www.thacherisland.org/howto_gethere.html" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://ghwalk.org/" target="_blank">
         Gloucester Harbor Walk
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        The new harbor walk is a self-guided tour of the Gloucester Harbor, with 42 stories, and two short films that tell the story of Gloucesters history and contemporary culture. The tour can be downloaded as an app for
        <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/walking-cinema-posts-from/id547951147?ls=1&amp;mt=8" target="_blank">
         iPhone
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.untravelmedia.gloucester" target="_blank">
         Android
        </a>
        , or used with a collection of stories and a map, which can be downloaded
        <a href="file://localhost/%5BSalem%20Food%20Tour%5D%20Credit/%20Salem%20Food%20Tours%20Fb%20page">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Gloucester-Harbor.jpeg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3401" height="480" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Gloucester-Harbor.jpeg" title="Gloucester Harbor" width="640"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.pem.org/" target="_blank">
         Peabody Essex Museum
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Salems Peabody Essex Museum has a long background with local historical societies and institutes. With roots reaching all the way back to the 18
        <sup>
         th
        </sup>
        century, the museum has grown into a cultural hub with diverse
        <a href="http://www.pem.org/collections/">
         collections
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.pem.org/exhibitions/" target="_blank">
         exhibits
        </a>
        from all over the globe. Plan your visit
        <a href="http://www.pem.org/visit/" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.fws.gov/northeast/parkerriver/" target="_blank">
         Parker River National Wildlife Refuge
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Plum-Island.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-3402" height="300" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Plum-Island-195x300.jpg" title="Plum Island" width="195"/>
        </a>
        Few places can claim to offer the diverse scenery and vibrant wildlife youll find in Plum Islands Parker River National Wildlife Refuge. Egrets, herons and warblers are just a few of the birds that draw visitors to the park year after year. A remarkable variety of habitats, ranging from beaches to forests, also make the perfect site for a leisurely stroll. To plan your visit, click
        <a href="http://www.fws.gov/northeast/parkerriver/visit.html" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://rockyneckartcolony.org/index.php" target="_blank">
         Rocky Neck Art Colony
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        You can discover one of the oldest continuously operating art colonies in the U.S. nestled in Gloucester. Within the harbor, on a peninsula known for its beautiful views, this community is the perfect spot to find works by both experienced and up-and-coming artists. The
        <a href="http://www.rockyneckarttrail.org/" target="_blank">
         Rocky Neck Art Trail
        </a>
        will show you scenery that has inspired artists for centuries. Check out upcoming exhibitions
        <a href="http://rockyneckartcolony.org/cultural_center_exhibitions.php" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.capeannfoodietours.com/" target="_blank">
         Cape Ann Foodie Tour
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        To find all the great seafood and other cuisine the Cape Ann area has to offer, hop along for a Cape Ann Foodie Tour. Four unique excursions allow you to explore Rockport, Gloucester, and Newburyport, while trying new food and drinks at the same time. On each tour, youll also get to learn a little history about the region. Get more information on individual tours
        <a href="http://www.capeannfoodietours.com/our-tours/" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Cape-Ann-Food.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3403" height="720" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Cape-Ann-Food.jpg" title="Cape Ann Food" width="960"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <em>
         Photo Credit: Cape Ann Food Tour
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/CapeAnnFoodieTours" target="_blank">
          Facebook page
         </a>
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.woodmans.com" target="_blank">
         Woodmans
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        A New England classic awaits you in Essex, home of the original fried clam. Woodmans is an award-winning seafood restaurant and distributor, hosting clambakes and shipping fresh lobsters all over the place. See their menu
        <a href="http://www.woodmans.com/restaurants/menus/" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.duckworthsbistrot.com/" target="_blank">
         Duckworths Bistro
        </a>
        t
       </p>
       <p>
        Gloucesters Duckworths Bistrot is a great place to try fresh, locally sourced, seasonal fare right on the coast. Chef Ken Duckworths goal is to find a taste for every palate by creating quality food that involves plenty of variety, while using local ingredients and seafood. For a look at Duckworth Bistros menu, click
        <a href="http://www.duckworthsbistrot.com/menu.htm" target="_blank">
         here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.salemfoodtours.com/" target="_blank">
         Salem Food Tours
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Cape Ann isnt the only area with tasty food tour options. Salem Food Tours provides a guided walking tour of Salem, complete with selections from the towns finest restaurants. Folks on the tour are also treated to some fun facts about the area. For more information on tickets and reservations,
        <a href="http://www.salemfoodtours.com/reservations/" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Salem-Food-Tour.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3404" height="638" src="/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Salem-Food-Tour.jpg" title="Salem Food Tour" width="960"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <em>
         Photo Credit: Salem Food Tours
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/SalemFoodTours" target="_blank">
          Facebook page
         </a>
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        For more on the North of Boston region, including a number of featured cities and additional attractions,
        <a href="http://www.massvacation.com/northBoston/" target="_blank">
         click here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
