        <span id="WNStoryDateline">
         COLUMBIA COUNTY, GA (WFXG) -
        </span>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         An Evans woman is facing charges after her Alcoholics Anonymous sponsor reported her for having a party that involved alcohol, drugs, sex and minors, according to an incident report from the Columbia County Sheriff's Office
        </p>
        <p>
         Rachel Lehnardt, 35, was arrested Monday and charged with two counts of contributing to the delinquency of a minor.
        </p>
        <p>
         The incident report said the A.A. sponsor reported that Lehnardt allowed her 16-year-old daughter and friends to come to her house "to party." The report said the minors were allowed to drink and smoke marijuana in her home.
        </p>
        <p>
         The sponsor told officials that Lehnardt told her that she had sex with her daughter's 16-year-old boyfriend and with an 18-year-old. Lehnardt also admitted to the sponsor that she played naked Twister with the minors and shared pornography of herself with her 16-year old daughter.
        </p>
        <p>
         Lehnardt told her sponsor her other children ages 4, 6, 8, and 10 were with their father. The sponsor said Lehnardt informed her that her children were taken into custody on April 6 after an incident that occurred at her house.
        </p>
