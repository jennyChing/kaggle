         <a href="http://blog.opticauthority.com/elcan-specterdr-small-arm-optics-review" rel="bookmark" title="Elcan SpecterDR Small Arm Optics Review">
          Elcan SpecterDR Small Arm Optics Review
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="post-meta">
         <span class="post-date">
          <a href="http://blog.opticauthority.com/elcan-specterdr-small-arm-optics-review" title="Elcan SpecterDR Small Arm Optics Review">
           May 8, 2015
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="date-sep">
          /
         </span>
         <span class="post-author">
          <a href="http://blog.opticauthority.com/author/mark_griffin" rel="author" title="Posts by Mark Griffin">
           Mark Griffin
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="date-sep">
          /
         </span>
         <a href="http://blog.opticauthority.com/elcan-specterdr-small-arm-optics-review#respond">

       <div class="post-content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.opticauthority.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Elcan-Specter-DR-scopes.jpg">
          <img alt="Elcan-Specter-DR-scopes" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-117" height="155" src="http://blog.opticauthority.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Elcan-Specter-DR-scopes.jpg" width="750"/>
         </a>
         Raytheon Elcan Optical Technologies is a premier optics supplier for battle tested and approved small arms optics. They supply many elite military groups all over the world with their
         <a href="http://www.opticauthority.com/elcan-specterdr.aspx">
          SpecterDR weapon sight
         </a>
         . The SpecterDR is one of the most durable and accurate small arms rifle scopes on the market today. The Elcan SpecterDR is recognized by military marksmen as being the easiest, most accurate small arms targeting system in the world and is preferred for operational simplicity, rugged performance and precision based optics.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-116">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         The ELCAN SpecterDR Dual Role Optical Sight is a revolution in battlefield optics. This is the worlds first dual field of view combat optical sight. Elcan offers the SpecterDR in both a 1-4x and 1.5-6x variant. The SpecterDR is capable of a quick magnification switch with a simple push of their throw lever. This enables the user to move from a CQB style of magnification to more of a marksman utilization of the scope. This is also an imperative function for identifying targets in the field prior to engagement. The SpecterDR has a 6 MOA dot size in 1 or 1.5x magnification and a 1.5 MOA dot size on 4 or 6x magnifications. The reticles are pre-calibrated for either 5.56 or 7.62 NATO rounds and the user can be choose between full illumination or the crosshairs, or just the center dot. The reticle is also etched in case of illumination failure. The reticle lies in the second focal plane of the optic in order to maintain a consistent size regardless of power. Elcan has also included an integral QD (quick detach) picatinny mount for fast and easy attachment to any standard battle rifle. The
         <a href="http://www.opticauthority.com/elcan-specterdr.aspx">
          SpecterDR
         </a>
         allows for turret adjustments in  MOA clicks and has a 3 eye relief, allowing for both eyes open engagement.
        </p>
        <p>
         Having personally used these scopes, they are head and shoulders above anything else out on the market today. They are the most robust battle optics available and extremely accurate. They are sizeable, but no longer or heavier than a combo hybrid and magnification doubler from EOTech or Aimpoint. Having the versatility provided by the Elcan SpecterDR is essential for any serious shooter. The Elcan Dual Role Optical Sight has changed the standard in battlefield optics; all other sights only perform half the job. If you are a serious operator who needs to trust their life to their gear, a competitor, or serious civilian with defense in mind the Elcan SpecterDR is definitively the tool for the job.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
