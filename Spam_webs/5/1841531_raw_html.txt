                <p>
                 <img alt="" class="alignnone pin-it" height="369" src="http://www.rawstory.com/rs/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Screen-capture-of-Caius-Veiovis-via-WWLP-22-News-615x345.jpg" width="658"/>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Do you see the photo above? Thats Caius Veiovis. As you can see, his face looks a little different than most. He has a 666 tattoo right in the middle of his forehead and horns implanted. Why would he do such a thing to his face? Probably because he was tired of his parents telling him to get a job, so he was like, Ill show them. I will fk up my face so bad that no one will ever hire me.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Anyway, this man is being accused of some pretty sick crimes. Hes accused of assisting two men in the torturing, kidnapping, dismembering and murdering of David Glasser, Robert Chadwell and Edward Frampton back in 2011.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Veiovis lawyer, James G. Reardon, Jr., is concerned that his clients unusual appearance will make it impossible for him to get a fair trial, because the jury will automatically assume hes guilty. Hes petitioned Superior Court Judge C. Jeffrey Kinder to allow him to show potential jurors a photograph of Mr. Veiovis before selecting the jury.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The lawyer also wants the judge to remove photographs taken inside Veiovis apartment. He doesnt want the jury seeing the incriminating photos, which includehatchets, machetes, and baseball bats with nails protruding from them.
                </p>
                <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
                </div>
                <p>
                 Veiovis trial is set to begin September 3, 2014.
                </p>
