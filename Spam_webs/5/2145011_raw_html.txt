     <h3>
      Famous Local Dishes
     </h3>
     <p>
      No trip to Paris is complete without savouring its renowned restaurants, markets and patisseries. Whether you prefer simple, country fare or the exquisite delicacy of haute-cuisine.
     </p>
     <p>
      To ensure you will make the most out of your trip, please make note of our food guide and get some inspiration of what you can do while you visit the city of love. Alternatively we suggest you to take part in some of the amazing food market tours or even cooking classes that are being offered. We highly recommend
      <a href="http://www.audelicious.com" title="Audelicious">
       Audelicious
      </a>
      as the owner (Aude) is a true Parisian chef and passionate about food and its culture.
     </p>
    </div>

        BEEF TARTARE
       </h2>
