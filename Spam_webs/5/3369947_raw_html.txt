     <h2 style="">
      Concerning the Disease
     </h2>
     <h3 style="">

     <p style="">
      <strong style="">
       Alzheimer's Disease
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="">
      I believe progress can be made in memory loss and even Alzheimer's Disease. You can improve and perhaps even regain memory being lost to "brain fog" and too Alzheimer's. While it may seem difficult, if you are beginning that downward spiral doing a few hard things may seem like a bargain. The first thing is never loose hope. Maintain a positive attitude because the very act of depression can cause cognitive function problems.

       [1]
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="">
      <strong style="">
       Walking
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="">
      My first tip on improving memory is that good memory is the product of a healthy body, and walking an hour a day will help in maintaining enough exercise to keep the mind and body in a better state. It has been shown that healthier people do have better memories than those that are physically weak.
     </p>

     <h2 style="">
      Turmeric and Its Effects
     </h2>
     <p style="">
      <strong style="">
       Effects of Turmeric
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="">
      Further, for both body and brain health you need to take in good nutrition, vitamins and minerals. And especially for memory curry and/or turmeric as a supplement and a food. Turmeric acts in so many ways to both prevent memory loss and maintain health. It is also a known cancer fighting agent.
     </p>
     <p style="">
      <strong style="">
       Turmeric Health
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="">
      A detoxification program also helps, and quite frankly this is where we find turmeric again will be quite helpful as it does help detoxify the body.
      <br style=""/>
      Allergens
     </p>
     <p style="">
      Alzheimers victims die consciously slowly while the body follows along afterward more slowly. It is a horrible disease. To avoid and mitigate some of its effects, try removing things from the environment that cause inflammation responses, things like allergens and pollens.
      <br style=""/>
      PH Balance
     </p>
     <p style="">
      You will need to find the proper pH balance for the body as another way to help reduce the inflammation effects on the brain, and here again we find turmeric a great benefit. Is it any wonder India has so few Alzheimers cases.
     </p>
     <p style="">
      <strong style="">
       Acid
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="">
      Acid when in too great a quantity can begin to accumulate and actually becomes a poison to the body.
