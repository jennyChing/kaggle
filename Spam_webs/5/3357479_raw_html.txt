&lt;div class="content post-inner clearfix"&gt;

		

&lt;input class="jpibfi" type="hidden" &gt;&lt;div class='shareaholic-canvas' data-app-id='7789262' data-app='share_buttons' data-title='What to see at Fuorisalone 2015: Brera Design District events' data-link='http://www.milandesignagenda.com/what-to-see-at-fuorisalone-2015-brera-design-district-events/' data-summary=''&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;p&gt;With the upcoming week of &lt;a href="www.fuorisalone.it/" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Fuorisalone 2015&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/a&gt;, from1419 April 2015, &lt;a href="www.milandesignagenda.com" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Milan Design Agenda&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/a&gt; will spot its eyes at the &lt;a href="www.breradesigndistrict.it/" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Brera Design District&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/a&gt;. The district is the creative business core of Milan; it extends along Bastioni di Porta Nuova to Via Broletto, along Via Legnano to &lt;a title="Fendi Milan Boutique at La Rinascente: a newly renovated corner store" href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/fendi-milan-boutique-at-la-rinascente-a-newly-renovated-corner-store/" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Via Montenapoleone&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/a&gt;, sharing the perimeter of Breras DUCs (Urban Business Districts). Last year the Brera Design District totalized over 136 events attended by 140 thousand visitors! What about that to attend at Milan Design Week 2015!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/What-to-see-at-Fuorisalone-2015-Brera-Design-District-events-5.jpg" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone wp-image-3512" src="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/What-to-see-at-Fuorisalone-2015-Brera-Design-District-events-5.jpg" alt="What to see at Fuorisalone 2015 Brera Design District events (5)" width="980" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;Heartened by this great result, this year it will offer a nonstop calendar of events and &lt;b&gt;a format featuring: a themed topic, the Design Lessons Award, a series of meetings, special projects and cultural events.&lt;/b&gt; This year&#8217;s ambassadors: designer &lt;a href="nichettostudio.com/" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Luca Nichetto&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/a&gt;, hosting three events in Brera, artist Patrick Tuttofuoco with his installation in Piazzetta Brera and the architecture firm &lt;a title="Italian Architect 2013 Award goes to Milans Studio Piuarch" href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/italian-architect-2013-award-goes-to-milans-studio-piuarch/"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Piuarch&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/a&gt;, based in Via Palermo, introducing the project Milan and its gardens.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/milan-design-week-2015-tom-dixon-unveils-new-collection-at-fuorisalone-2015/" target="_blank"&gt;See also: Milan Design Week 2015, Tom Dixon unveils new collection at Fuorisalone 2015&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;According to Luca Nichetto, Brera Design District, &lt;em&gt;&#8220;It&#8217;s Fuorisalone&#8217;s most interesting area, for its centers everything in the heart of Milan (&#8230;) not just because i&#8217;m interested in creating an event, but because i really want the public to feel climate and the atmosphere created by the entire district&#8230;&#8221;&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/What-to-see-at-Fuorisalone-2015-Brera-Design-District-events-1.jpg" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone wp-image-3513" src="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/What-to-see-at-Fuorisalone-2015-Brera-Design-District-events-1.jpg" alt="What to see at Fuorisalone 2015 Brera Design District events (1)" width="980" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="tar_text"&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;In continuation of last year, the Brera Design District &lt;b&gt;presents the second edition of the Design Lessons Award&lt;/b&gt;, dedicated to he/she who best interpreted the values standing at the base of the topic chosen. For Project Formation Identity the award goes to &lt;b&gt;Martino Gamper&lt;/b&gt;, a designer whose work integrates art, design and handicraft. Gamper distinguished himself for his unique planning ability, for having transferred a deep knowledge of the history of design into his original creations, by translating the projectbased culture in a well defined identity.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div class="tar_text"&gt;
&lt;div class="tar_text"&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;b&gt;Events and exhibitors&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This year, during the Fuorisalone, designer &lt;b&gt;Luca Nichetto&lt;/b&gt; will be attending the Brera Design District with the Swedish brand &lt;b&gt;Kinnarps&lt;/b&gt;, with an exhibition interpreting the trend report on new ways to live your office space (Largo Treves 5, entrance from Via Solferino). The designer will also introduce BonBon, a set of mirrored glass tables for the Check brand &lt;b&gt;Verreum&lt;/b&gt; (Via DellOrso 12), whilst the&lt;a title="Fall shopping guide into Milan 5 furniture and lighting stores" href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/fall-shopping-guide-into-milan-5-furniture-and-lighting-stores/" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;b&gt;Foscarini Spazio Brera&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/a&gt; showroom (Via Fiori Chiari 28) will display a brand new version of Plass lamps and the new table lamp Kurage, designed with &lt;a title="Maison et Objet Paris 2014: Bourgie table lamp 10th anniversary" href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/maison-et-objet-paris-2014-bourgie-table-lamp-10th-anniversary/" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;b&gt;Nendo&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/a&gt; .&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/milan-design-week-2015-countdown-8-jaw-dropping-design-events-to-see/" target="_blank"&gt;See also : Milan Design Week 2015 countdown: 8 jaw dropping design events to see&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/What-to-see-at-Fuorisalone-2015-Brera-Design-District-events-6.jpg" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone wp-image-3511" src="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/What-to-see-at-Fuorisalone-2015-Brera-Design-District-events-6.jpg" alt="What to see at Fuorisalone 2015 Brera Design District events (6)" width="980" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;Several international exhibitors of collective works have chosen this area: &lt;b&gt;Austrian Design PioneersConsulate General of Austria&lt;/b&gt; (Pelota, Via Palermo 10), &lt;b&gt;Belgium is Design&lt;/b&gt; at the &lt;b&gt;Brera Academy of Fine Arts&lt;/b&gt; (Via Brera 28), &lt;b&gt;Berlin Design SelectionDynamo Design Berlin e.V.&lt;/b&gt; (Via Goito 3); besides international brands such as Japanese &lt;b&gt;Karimoku New Standard&lt;/b&gt; (Via Palemo 1), Brazilian &lt;b&gt;Sollos&lt;/b&gt; (Via Solferino 40), Chinese &lt;b&gt;Pinwo Design Company &lt;/b&gt;(Via del Carmine 9) and &lt;b&gt;Lighting Innovation Design Center&lt;/b&gt; (Via Clerici 10), Swedish &lt;b&gt;Atelj Lyktan&lt;/b&gt; (Corso Garibaldi 117), Dutch &lt;b&gt;eQ+&lt;/b&gt; (Via Del Carmine 11).&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/What-to-see-at-Fuorisalone-2015-Brera-Design-District-events-3.jpg" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone wp-image-3508" src="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/What-to-see-at-Fuorisalone-2015-Brera-Design-District-events-3.jpg" alt="What to see at Fuorisalone 2015 Brera Design District events (3)" width="980" height="653" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;This year the Brera Design District will also be attended by some of the major firms of Italian and international design: &lt;b&gt;Sebastian Herkner, Arik Lev y, Luca Nichetto, Karim Rashid&lt;/b&gt; (Verreum);&lt;b&gt;Emmanuel Babled, Lorenzo Damiani, Francesco Faccin, Diego Grandi, Gordon Guillaumier, Paolo Ulian&lt;/b&gt; (Alfa Marmi); also, some of the designers of &lt;b&gt;Fabrica&lt;/b&gt;, hosting the firm &lt;b&gt;Formafantasma&lt;/b&gt; (Daikin).&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/milan-design-week-2015-patricia-urquiola-and-antonio-citterio-create-new-bb-italia-outdoor-collection/" target="_blank"&gt;See also: Milan Design Week 2015: Patricia Urquiola and Antonio Citterio create new B&amp;B Italia outdoor collection&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class="tar_text"&gt;
&lt;div class="tar_text"&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;b&gt;Also, certain firms have strategically chosen the Brera Design District for their new openings: Kartell by Laufen&lt;/b&gt; (Laufen Pop Up showroom, Via Pontaccio 10), &lt;b&gt;Rubelli&lt;/b&gt; (Via Fatebenefratelli 9), &lt;b&gt;Smeg&lt;/b&gt;(Via Moscova 58), &lt;b&gt;Florim&lt;/b&gt; (Foro Bonaparte 12), &lt;b&gt;Fantini&lt;/b&gt; (Via Solferino 18), &lt;b&gt;Richard Ginori&lt;/b&gt; (Brera ang. Pontaccio), &lt;b&gt;Olivieri&lt;/b&gt; (Largo Treves 2).&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/What-to-see-at-Fuorisalone-2015-Brera-Design-District-events-2.jpg" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone wp-image-3509" src="http://www.milandesignagenda.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/What-to-see-at-Fuorisalone-2015-Brera-Design-District-events-2.jpg" alt="What to see at Fuorisalone 2015 Brera Design District events (2)" width="980" height="653" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p class="tar_text"&gt;&lt;b&gt;At Fuorisalone 2015, the Valcucine showroom will host the installation curated by Maurizio Galante &amp; Tal Lancman, celebrating the art of offering.&lt;/b&gt; Tempting Art is a series of projects created by artists, designers and poets where each work tells a vision, a fully personal story, a hidden memory. A fantastic collection. Works created to satisfy the senses of sight and taste. Valcucine Milano Brera  Corso G. Garibaldi 99&lt;/p&gt;
