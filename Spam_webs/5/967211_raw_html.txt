       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         After the Apple event today, Google had to counter with something new. They are teaming together with Verizon to release the newest version of the Android OS. This version will be better optimized to handle the little annoyances in your life. PhoneTipz managed to acquire a few screenshots before its release.
        </p>
        <p>
         This one comes in handy at the office. Android users have been asking for a
         <strong>
          Tell your boss to piss off
         </strong>
         feature in email for a while now.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt='New "Tell boss to piss off" option in email.' class="alignnone size-full wp-image-666" height="801" src="http://phonetipz.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/10/droid-funny-gmail-menu.png" title="droid-funny-gmail-menu" width="450"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ex-wives are the worst. The new Droid helps you take more control of those calls asking where the child support check is.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Features include Block call, Enable ass mode, Suicide and Deploy nuclear warhead." class="alignnone size-full wp-image-665" height="801" src="http://phonetipz.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/10/droid-funny-call-settings.png" title="droid-funny-call-settings" width="450"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The new Droid will come stock with a new version of Twitter for Android. Below is my favorite feature to auto-unfollow douchebags.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt='Option to unfollow f*cktards and anybody who uses the term "social media".' class="alignnone size-full wp-image-667" height="801" src="http://phonetipz.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/10/funny-twitter-menus.png" style="border: 1px solid black;" title="funny-twitter-menus" width="450"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Note: In no way are these real. They are for humorous purposes only. If you have a problem with this post, tell your shrink that you dont have a sense of humor.
        </p>
