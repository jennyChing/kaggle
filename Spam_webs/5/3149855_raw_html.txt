             <h2 class="field field-name-field-sub-headline field-type-text field-label-hidden">
              As the one-year countdown to 2014 FIFA World Cup Brazil begins, we highlight the Top Ten songs of World Cup history
             </h2>
             <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
              <div class="field-items">
               <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                <p class="p1">
                 The countdown has begun: the 2014 FIFA World Cup begins on June 12, 20014 in Sao Paulo, Brazil. The tournament's slogan is "All in One Rhythm," and we can expect music surrounding the mundial celebration in Brazil to be big, second only to the futbol itself. As the qualifying matches continue, we're awaiting not only FIFA's pick for the official 2014 World Cup theme song(s), but a year in which music and sports could come together like never before.
                </p>
                <p class="p2">
                 Meanwhile, listen to these historic World Cup anthems, some paired with video of footage of beautiful games past. And let us know what your 2014 World Cup anthem would be.
                </p>
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>

                      England 1966
                      <br/>
                     </strong>
                     The King of Skiffle recorded this novelty song dedicated to the mascot of the 1966 World Cup in England.

                 </h3>
                 <div class="field field-name-field-text field-type-text-long field-label-hidden">
                  <div class="field-items">
                   <div class="field-item even">
                    <p>
                     <strong>
                      USA 1994
                      <br/>
                     </strong>
                     We could have expected a little more swing from Daryl Hall, but this metaphorical tribute with Sounds of Blackness doesn't fail to touch the heart.

                  Wavin' Flag - K'naan
                 </h3>
                 <div class="field field-name-field-text field-type-text-long field-label-hidden">
                  <div class="field-items">
                   <div class="field-item even">
                    <p>
                     <strong>
                      South Africa 2010
                     </strong>
                     (Unofficial)
                     <br/>
                     K'Naan's celebratory version of his inspirational song inspired by the stories of refugees was used in Coca-Cola's 2010 World Cup campaign. Not an official World Cup theme, was adopted by football fans around the world.

                 </h3>
