     <h1>
      Paper Craft Idea: Make Your Own Paper Succulents
     </h1>
     <div class="post-detail__author">
      by
      <a href="http://blog.creativelive.com/author/hannaolsen/">
       <span class="upper">
        Hanna Brooks Olsen
       </span>
      </a>
     </div>
     <!-- end .post-detail__author -->
     <time datetime="2014-06-04" pubdate="">
      June 4, 2014
     </time>
     <div class="post-detail__categories">
      <a href="http://blog.creativelive.com/category/topics/maker-craft/" rel="category tag">
       craft &amp; maker
      </a>
     </div>
     <div class="addthis_jumbo_share">
     </div>
     <p style="text-align: left;">
      <a href="http://blog.creativelive.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/paper-succulent.jpg?utm_source=blog&amp;utm_medium=text_link&amp;utm_campaign=paper-craft-idea-diy-paper-succulents">
       <img alt="paper succulent" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6769" height="800" src="http://blog.creativelive.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/paper-succulent.jpg" width="600"/>
      </a>
      <br/>
      Crafting with paper can be a whole lot of fun  as long as youre careful about cuts  but many people dont know that the realm of paper crafting goes way outside of origami and other clever folding techniques. Robert Mahar, who has the envy-inducing title of arts and crafts professional shows us this cool paper craft idea, which leaves you with a beautiful paper succulent that will last much longer than an actual potted plant.
     </p>
     <p>
      Simple green paper goes from a flat sheet to a colorful, decorative element that you can use as a part of a larger project, or by itself. And if you were really getting crafty, you could probably figure out a way to incorporate a tiny one into
      <a href="http://blog.creativelive.com/mothers-day-gift-idea-diy-craft/?utm_source=blog&amp;utm_medium=text_link&amp;utm_campaign=paper-craft-idea-diy-paper-succulents" target="_blank">
       this DIY for a succulent ring
      </a>
      , which calls for the real deal, but could probably be altered to include a longer-lasting paper bud.
     </p>
     <p>
      If you want more amazing paper crafts from Robert, join him for his CreativeLive class,
      <a href="http://cr8.lv/rmpcstyt" target="_blank">
       Paper Crafting: Skills and Technique
      </a>
      , streaming live starting July 14. You can also watch
      <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/craftedwithrobert" target="_blank">
       his YouTube channel
      </a>
      for more paper craft ideas, including a DIY dominos set for dad, and beautiful crepe paper flowers to compliment your succulents.
     </p>
