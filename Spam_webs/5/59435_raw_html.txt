     <div class="description" itemprop="description">
      <h2>
       Dive Dahab with Pospadan
      </h2>
      <p>
       Privately guided dives and safaris to the beautiful reefs of Dahab. Enjoy a diver's freedom, fun and safety with an experienced dive instructor.
      </p>
      <p>
       My name is Medhat A. Moussa, I am working as a CMAS 3*Instructor, Course-Director, Children Instructor Trainer and Examiner in Dahab/Egypt.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        We offer...
       </strong>
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        guided dives
       </li>
       <li>
        introduction dives
       </li>
       <li>
        courses for beginners and experienced divers
       </li>
       <li>
        instructor courses
       </li>
       <li>
        diving for children
       </li>
       <li>
        and a lot more...
       </li>
      </ul>
     </div>
