        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Fable 3 Is Currently Available For Free On Xbox Live
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2013-06-10T09:45:11+00:00">
          June 10, 2013
         </span>

         <span class="author vcard">

          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.techfleece.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/fable-3-logo.jpg">
            <img alt="fable-3-logo" class=" wp-image-15157 aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.techfleece.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/fable-3-logo.jpg?resize=576%2C154" title="fable-3-logo"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Fable 3, the RPG from Lionshead, is currently available to download for free via Xbox Live Marketplace. There is nothing about the offer on the official Lionshead website at the moment, but with E3 kicking off tomorrow, this could very well be the real McCoy and not a glitch.
          </p>

           <em>
            Download To Xbox 360
           </em>
           it will show up as free. Go

          <p style="text-align: left;">
           The story focuses on the player characters struggle to overthrow the King of Albion by forming alliances and building support for arevolution. After a successful revolt, the player becomes the monarch and is tasked with attempting to defend Albion from a great evil. The game was first released on the Xbox 360 on29th October 2010, with the PC version hitting Steam on 20th May 2011.
