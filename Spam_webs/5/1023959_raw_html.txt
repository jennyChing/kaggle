           <img alt="chocolate crepes 1 Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2230" height="1823" src="http://www.crunchycreamysweet.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/chocolate-crepes-1.jpg" title="Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese CrunchyCreamySweet.com" width="1360"/>

         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Welcome to day 7 of our
          <a href="http://www.crunchycreamysweet.com/2013/02/01/chocolate-cupcakes-for-two-valentines-day-celebration-event/" title="Chocolate Cupcakes For Two  Valentines Day Celebration Event">

          </a>
          event! Today we are talking
          <strong>
           breakfast
          </strong>
          . Well, technically it can be
          <strong>
           breakfast or desser
          </strong>
          t. Judge for yourself.
          <img alt="icon smile Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.crunchycreamysweet.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif" title="Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese Filling
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span id="more-2229">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Its pretty amazing what you can do with just
          <strong>
           few simple ingredients
          </strong>
          . And how
          <strong>
           quickly you can create
          </strong>
          a dessert that satisfies
          <strong>
           visually and in taste
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.crunchycreamysweet.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/chocolate-crepes-2.jpg">
           <img alt="chocolate crepes 2 Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2231" height="1885" src="http://www.crunchycreamysweet.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/chocolate-crepes-2.jpg" title="Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese CrunchyCreamySweet.com" width="1360"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          But then, if you ask me, I will say that
          <strong>
           pairing chocolate with fruit
          </strong>
          will always win thumbs up.
          <img alt="icon smile Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.crunchycreamysweet.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif" title="Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese"/>
          My Hubby and our kids were
          <strong>
           scarfing these down
          </strong>
          like nobodys business. These crepes are
          <strong>
           amazing
          </strong>
          ! Filled with sweet
          <strong>
           cream cheese filling
          </strong>
          and
          <strong>
           sliced strawberries
          </strong>
          and dusted with powdered sugar. Now, thats a
          <strong>
           breakfast
          </strong>
          !
         </p>

             <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
              Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
              <p id="zlrecipe-prep-time">
               Prep Time:
               <span content="PT10M" itemprop="prepTime">
                10 minutes
               </span>
              </p>
              <p id="zlrecipe-cook-time">
               Cook Time:
               <span content="PT15M" itemprop="cookTime">
                15 minutes
               </span>
              </p>
              <p id="zlrecipe-total-time">
               Total Time:
               <span content="PT2H25M" itemprop="totalTime">
                2 hours, 25 minutes
               </span>
              </p>
             </div>
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
              <p id="zlrecipe-yield">
               Yield:
               <span itemprop="recipeYield">
                6 to 8 crepes
               </span>
              </p>
             </div>
             <div class="zlclear">
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="img-desc-wrap">
             <p class="t-a-c hide-print">
              <img alt="chocolate crepes 3 Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese" class="photo" itemprop="image" src="http://www.crunchycreamysweet.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/chocolate-crepes-3.jpg" style="width: 100px;" title="Chocolate Crepes with Strawberries and Cream Cheese"/>
             </p>
             <div id="zlrecipe-summary" itemprop="description">
              <p class="summary italic">
               Delicious start to your day. Chocolate crepes filled with cream cheese and strawberries.
              </p>
             </div>
            </div>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
             Ingredients
            </p>
            <ol id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 c cold water
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 c cold milk
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 eggs
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/4 tsp salt
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 c all-purpose flour
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/4 c unsweetened cocoa powder
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/4 c shaved chocolate ( not chocolate chips; optional )
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
              4 Tbsp melted butter or canola oil or coconut oil ( melted )
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
              4 oz. cream cheese
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 c powdered sugar
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 tsp heavy whipping cream
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
              6 - 8 strawberries, washed and sliced
             </li>
            </ol>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
             Instructions
            </p>
            <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Place water, milk, eggs, shaved chocolate and salt in a blender and blend for few minutes until it all comes together.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Add flour and cocoa powder and blend again.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Drizzle in melted butter ( or oil ) and blend for 2 minutes.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Cover and chill the batter for at least 2 hours. I refrigerated it overnight.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              When ready to make crepes, preheat crepe pan.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              In a medium mixing bowl, mix softened cream cheese until creamy. Add powdered sugar and beat until comes together. Add cream and beat again. Set aside.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Using 1/4 c scoops of batter, fry crepes in the preheated pan. Fry for about 1 minute on medium heat. When you see the edges set, try to slide your spatula under the crepe. If it tears, leave of for another 10 seconds and try again.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Flip the crepe and fry on the other side for 10 to 15 seconds. Slide onto a plate. Repeat with remaining batter.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-8" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Spread each crepe with cream cheese filling. Place strawberry slices only on half of the crepe. Fold in half and then fold again. Place on a serving dish. Repeat with remaining crepes.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-9" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Dust with powdered sugar and serve.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-notes">
             Notes
            </p>
            <div id="zlrecipe-notes-list">
             <p class="notes">
              Refrigerating the batter lets it rest and get rid of air bubbles. The batter slightly thickens as it chills. 
The crepes texture is quite delicate so be gentle when flipping them on the other side. Usually, the first crepe won't work and will tear. The second one usually works much better.
             </p>
