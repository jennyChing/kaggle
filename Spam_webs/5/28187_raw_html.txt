			&lt;p&gt;It&#8217;s a bird, it&#8217;s a plane, it&#8217;s&#8230;hundreds of people barreling down a 1,000 foot water slide through the middle of the city. That&#8217;s right, the geniuses behind&lt;a href="http://www.slidethecity.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.slidethecity.com/', 'Slide the City');"&gt;Slide the City&lt;/a&gt; have been setting up their giant waterslide across the nation and this summer they are sliding throughSan Francisco.&lt;span id="more-6271"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Remember back in July 2014 when &lt;a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mampOsVcXIM" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mampOsVcXIM', 'Devin Graham set up a 260-foot Slip &#8216;N Slide');"  target="_blank"&gt;Devin Graham set up a 260-foot Slip &#8216;N Slide&lt;/a&gt; on Potrero Hill? &lt;div class="mceMediaCreditOuterTemp" style="text-align: center;"&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter wp-image-6278 size-bs-biz-size" src="http://www.upout.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/Slide-1-600x450.jpg?679039" alt="" width="600" height="450" /&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;While slip &#8216;nslidesbring back childhood memories,Slide the City has made their event anexperience for all ages (well, everyone over 5) with food, drinks, and live music.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Prices vary depending on if you want to be Single Slider, Triple Slider, or a VIP slider.If you enjoy being a VIP &#8211; not only in the club, but in all aspects of life &#8211; that option is definitely worth checking out. And if slipping and sliding isn&#8217;t your jam, you can watchothers do so for free!&lt;/p&gt;
