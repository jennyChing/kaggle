          <img alt="Dmitri Galcovski" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-134" height="100" src="http://www.theecoacceleratorresourcecenter.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/6/2013/10/DG.jpg" width="122"/>
          by Dmitri Galcovski
          <br/>
          Abookspro Advice: File Your Sales Tax Correctly
          <br/>
          One of the mistakes that I see way too often as an accountant is business owners failing to account for or remit sales tax correctly. Most of those mistakes happen due to a failure to understand what sales tax is and how to calculate it as well as entrepreneurs relying solely on their POS to determine their sales tax amount without ever questioning it or double checking.
          <br/>
          On the other hand, contemporary accounting software could be much more reliable since it calculates sales tax based on the sales that you enter. Therefore, this is probably not such a great idea to change the amount of sales tax in your accounting software based on the amount that your POS is showing. Rather, I would recommend making sure that the sales tax in your POS matches the amount in your accounting software. If it doesnt, then there is probably an error that you need to fix. This is an important correction since it will make a huge difference in your sales tax return, so do not hesitate to consult a professional if you do not seem to be able to manage yourself.
          <br/>
          Another problem that I face quite often are the businesses that charge the prices that already include sales tax amount, for instance bars that charge $4 a drink. Way too many owners fail to remember that this $4 is not a sales price, but rather a sales price plus the sales tax. If you are one of the businesses that practices that, make sure that when you file your sales tax return, you include the amount of sales minus the sales tax on line one  this could make a substantial difference.
          <br/>
          Quite a few business owners have the do-it yourself mentality, but it is quite easy to get confused and make a mess of your books if you are constantly multitasking. If calculating your sales tax is causing you grief, please feel free to contact me and we will get you back on track together.
