         <p>
          <strong>
           Anadolu
          </strong>
          tarih boyunca birok kltrn, medeniyetin beii olmu bir yer. Anadoluda nereye giderseniz gidin farkl kltrlerden birok izler grebilirsiniz. Bu sene ok sk
          <strong>
           Konyaya
          </strong>
          gitme frsatm oldu. Konyada trnn esiz rnekleri olan camiiler var. Bunlardan birisi
          <strong>
           Konyann Beyehir ilesinde
          </strong>
          bulunan
          <strong>
           Erefolu Camiidir.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_11465" style="width: 300px">
          <a href="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/2.jpg">
           <img alt="Erefolu Camii, Beyehir-KONYA" class="size-thumbnail wp-image-11465" height="290" src="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/2-290x290.jpg" width="290"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Erefolu Camii, Beyehir-KONYA
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          <em>
           <strong>
            Erefolu Camii,
           </strong>
          </em>
          Beyehir Glnn hemen yannda bulunmaktadr. Camii ad sylenince akla hemen
          <strong>
           ahap direkli camii
          </strong>
          gelir.
          <strong>
           Anadoluda13. yy.dan itibaren
          </strong>
          ahap direkli camiler yaplmaya balamtr.Aa direkli camiler daha ok Orta Asyada
          <strong>
           Semerkant, Buhara
          </strong>
          gibi yerlerde bulunmaktadr.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_11595" style="width: 490px">
          <a href="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/rsz_111.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="wp-image-11595 size-full" height="288" src="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/rsz_111.jpg" width="480"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Konya Erefolu Camii,  ehnaz nan
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/1.jpg">
           <img alt="1" class="size-thumbnail wp-image-11464 aligncenter" height="290" src="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/1-290x290.jpg" width="290"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Camii
           <span style="color: #252525">
            Beylikler Devrinde
            <strong>
             Erefolu Beyi Sleyman Bey
            </strong>
            tarafndan
           </span>
           <strong>
            1296 -1299
           </strong>
           yllar arasnda yaplmtr.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #252525">
             Anadoludaki ahap direkli camilerin en by ve orijinalidir.
            </span>
           </strong>
          </li>
          <li>
           Camii
           <strong>
            46 ahap stun
           </strong>
           zerinde ykselir. Stunlar
           <strong>
            meeden
           </strong>
           yaplmtr.
          </li>
          <li>
           Ahap stunlar yzyllar boyunca salam kalmtr.
           <strong>
            Bunun sebebi,
            <em>
             cami atsnda biriken kar, yamur atnn ortasndaki boluktan ortadaki havuza aktarlm, yaklan sobalarla birlikte su buhar ortam nemlendirmi ve stunlarn atlamas engellenmitir.
            </em>
           </strong>
          </li>
          <li>
           Mihrab 6 metre yksekliinde olup,
           <strong>
            ini mozaik ile kapldr.
           </strong>
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/3.jpg">
           <img alt="3" class="size-thumbnail wp-image-11466 aligncenter" height="290" src="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/3-290x290.jpg" width="290"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Minberi
           <strong>
            ceviz aacndan
           </strong>
           yaplm olup,
           <strong>
            oymal ve atmal olarak tutkalsz
           </strong>
           yaplmtr. Minber iilii muhteemdir.
          </li>
          <li>
           <strong>
            Seluklu Ulu Camiilerinin btn zelliklerini gsterir:
           </strong>
           <span style="color: #252525">
            oul ahap stunlu, tavan tamamen ahap ve kalem iilii ile sslenmi, minber tamamen ahap ve
            <a href="http://www.mobdizayn.com/kundekari-sabrin-ahsaba-sanatsal-dokunusu/" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              kndekari
             </strong>
            </a>
            teknii ile yaplm, mihrab inilidir.
           </span>
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <span style="font-weight: bold;color: #000000">
           <em>
            Ankara Aslanhane Camisi,Afyon Ulu Camisi,Konya Sahip Ata Camisi
           </em>
          </span>
          gibi camiler ahap direkli camilere rnektir.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Erefolu Camii,
          </strong>
          Konya merkezinden yaklak
          <strong>
           90 kilometre
          </strong>
          olup arala
          <strong>
           1,5 saat mesafededir.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/beyehir-gl.jpg">
           <img alt="beyehir gl" class="size-thumbnail wp-image-11470 aligncenter" height="290" src="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/beyehir-gl-290x290.jpg" width="290"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: left">
          <strong>
           Beyehir gln ziyaret edebilir,
          </strong>
          gl ziyaret ederken
          <strong>
           Erefolu Camiiyi dolaabilirsiniz :)
          </strong>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_11469" style="width: 300px">
          <a href="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/beyehir-gl-2.jpg">
           <img alt="Beyehir Gl" class="size-thumbnail wp-image-11469" height="290" src="http://www.mobdizayn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/beyehir-gl-2-290x290.jpg" width="290"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Beyehir Gl
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Bu yazy yazmaya vesile olan, bilgileri ile bizleri aydnlatan, yollam olduu grseller ile bize destek veren Beyehirde ikamet eden
           <em>
            ehnaz Hanma
           </em>
           ok teekkr ederiz.
          </strong>
         </p>
