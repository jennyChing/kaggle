          <h2 class="subtitle">
           A woman, a rose
          </h2>
          <div class="txtd" id="txtd_24292341" style="">
           <p>
            I compare women with roses, and for this explanation I wrote a hub but I wanted to compare men with something, then came the tree. From the day I saw the comparative I stumbled with things that gave me more understanding as I visualized a man with a tree.
           </p>
           <p>
            Eric wrote a hub titled:
            <a href="http://ericdierker.hubpages.com/hub/Women-is-there-a-man-in-your-life">
             Women is there a man in your life?
            </a>
            and quoted: "
            <em>
             But the point is that somewhere down that road of the 1900's women got brainwashed into thinking they were not women. Do you get my point
            </em>
            "
           </p>
           <p>
            Then I saw the opportunity to put together the comparison of a man, a tree.Of course there is a man in every woman's life ... a father, grandfather, uncle, brother, husband, son, nephew, cousin, boyfriend, admirer, neighbor, a president...heeyy you guys are all over.
           </p>
           <p>
            I love God, and the Bible was written inspired by Him; I do not doubt it, and each day I confirm that knowledge by my own experiences with our Creator, Lord and Savior. It is actually from the Bible that this comparison came of a man, a tree.
           </p>

          </h2>
          <div class="txtd" id="txtd_24291897" style="">

            Who better than our Creator to know the efficiency of a tree planted by water? This importance strives in exactly the words from every text found in the Bible, the Word of God for our joy, living our life.
           </p>
           <p>
            A man, a tree planted by water is an extraordinary habitat for wildlife. Human nature in its wild form needs to be led spiritually, educated, and held to reduce risks of flooding of worldly contamination and stabilizing the banks from vain philosophies. John 7:38 says: "
            <em>
             He who believes in Me, as the Scripture said, 'From his innermost being will flow rivers of living water
            </em>
            .'" A man, a tree than, ought to reflect the source of the tree of life which is Jesus. Revelation 2:7 says: "
            <em>
             He who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the churches. To the one who conquers I will grant to eat of the tree of life, which is in the paradise of God
            </em>
            ."
           </p>
           <p>
            The only way to get in the paradise of God is by Jesus "Jesus said to him, 
            <em>
             I am the way, and the truth, and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me

           Final words
          </h2>
          <div class="txtd" id="txtd_24298710" style="">

           </p>
           <p>
            My second answer was: "Perhaps not 'brainwashed', but circumstances has lead women to take over men's positions, starting with the absence of men to attend war issues. Another fact is, and I am sorry to say it...but where are the men that were men?"

We were created to be men's helpers, not slaves, not their sexual toys, but their helpers; so if the man around your life...or you, the man reading this hub...has a hard time being a man, a tree; let's all put our part and lift up that inner tree to be functional for the peace and joy of the world in God's will.
           </p>
