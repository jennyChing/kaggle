        <p>
         As a stylist we come across a variation of props that captivate our imagination and allow us to execute beautiful imagery. So what do you get when you put together an artist and a prop of a life size moon together? Pure magic. From the creative mind of Russian Artist,
         <a href="http://leonid-tishkov.blogspot.com" target="_blank">
          Leonid Tishkov
         </a>
         brings to the public his 10 years in the making project  Private Moon.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 648px">
         <a href="http://leonid-tishkov.blogspot.com" target="_blank">
          <img alt="leonid-tishkov, hello-moon, photography, series, project, arts, inspiration, russian, private-moon" class=" " height="506" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/06/slide_283611_2164417_free_qtg1b.jpg" width="638"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Leonid Tishkov &amp; Marcus Williams
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
          Private Moon is a collection of documented photographs that captivate Tishkovs life size illuminated crescent moon in different settings around the world. Looking like something out of a fairy tale, take one glance at his collection and you will be captivated in aw (I know I was) in this mysterious love affair portrayed by Leonid and his precious moon.
        </p>
        <p>
         From the rooftops of Paris, to floating in Taiwan, to the shores of the capital of the United States, Tishkovs adventures are endless.
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out a few images from Tishkovs collection below and be amazed but if you would like to see the full series check it out

          here.
