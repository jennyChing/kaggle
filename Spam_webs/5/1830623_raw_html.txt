     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        Look at All the Love We Found
       </b>
      </i>
      is a
      <a href="/wiki/Tribute_album" title="Tribute album">
       tribute album
      </a>
      by various artists dedicated to
      <a href="/wiki/Sublime_(band)" title="Sublime (band)">
       Sublime
      </a>
      , released on June 21, 2005 (see
      <a href="/wiki/2005_in_music" title="2005 in music">
       2005 in music
      </a>
      ). The title comes from a lyric in the song "S.T.P." on Sublime's
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Robbin%27_the_Hood" title="Robbin' the Hood">
        Robbin' the Hood
       </a>
      </i>
      album.
     </p>
