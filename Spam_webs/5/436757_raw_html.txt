       Graduate Jobs
      </h1>
      <div style="float: right; padding: 0 0 20px 10px;">
       <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="230" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/qzzuEw4YN0I?feature=player_detailpage" width="385">

      <p>
       Grad Diary features the largest database of graduate jobs in the UK for sectors including banking, accounting, law, consultancy and finance. We offer a completely free online resource helping students apply for graduate jobs every step of the way. With almost 500 different companies featured on our website, we provide a full description of each company with information on how to apply for their graduate jobs. Simply click on one of the tabs at the top of the page to start your journey.
      </p>
      <p>
       When it comes to applying for graduate programmes, we appreciate that there are a lot of things to take into consideration, such as finding the right company and the right career to pursue. Our long list of companies certainly help make this easier. In addition, we understand that applying for graduate jobs is very competitive, which is why we want to give you an edge over other applicants. Our site offers free numeracy testing and an Application Organiser to help manage all the companies you want to pursue and this free tool has had over 10,000 students sign up since we introduced it in September 2013. To guide you through the process further, we offer hundreds of useful interviews and blog posts with top employers, HR professionals, career advisors and young professionals who have pursued the same graduate jobs.
      </p>
      <p>
       Grad Diary is very proud of the reputation that we have built with our other partners. Other than having a very close relationship with some of the biggest companies in the UK, we are currently working with over 80 societies and careers services and are thrilled that our website has been endorsed and recommended by some of the top Universities in the UK.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Graduate Jobs London
      </h2>
      <p>
       Graduate jobs in London are something that we specialise in. With the head offices of major companies residing in the capital, there are thousands of graduate positions in London. The placements we discuss in the Finance, Accountancy and Banking industries are typically located in The City and Canary Wharf. The big Law firms are generally based in areas such as Fleet Street, Moorgate and Holborn. The Technology and Consultancy jobs that we promote could be based in all of these locations mentioned. With salaries in London said to be slightly higher than the rest of a country, it is not surprising that this is a strong demand for graduate jobs in London from applicants who have just finished University in the UK or overseas. But with a lot of competition means there are less places available which is why we are pleased to offer a service to help young professionals with the entire process from finding companies to passing tests and succeeding in interviews.
