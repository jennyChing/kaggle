               CHIMPORT 2014  The UK Pavilion
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              If your company is is looking to export to China and wants to test the water, find distributors, get product feedback etc.,
              <a href="http://www.channeltrading.co.uk/#chimport-menu" target="_blank">
               then you should exhibit at CHIMPORT 2014
              </a>
              !
             </p>
             <p>
              You will have an unrivaled opportunity to meet potential clients who are looking to import a wide range of quality consumer products.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               By exhibiting at CHIMPORT 2014 you will:
              </strong>
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               Showcase your products
              </li>
              <li>
               Meet hundreds of potential buyers face to face over 3 days
              </li>
              <li>
               Start to build your china face essential for any new product launching in China
              </li>
              <li>
               Collect high quality leads and generate orders
              </li>
              <li>
               Network with other suppliers
              </li>
              <li>
               Raise your profile amongst your competitors
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Exhibitors at this event include:
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              Over 80 country specific pavilions or stands with products in the following sectors:
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               Food
              </li>
              <li>
               Drink
              </li>
              <li>
               Clothing
              </li>
              <li>
               Shoes
              </li>
              <li>
               Jewellery
              </li>
              <li>
               Leather Goods
              </li>
              <li>
               Cosmetics
              </li>
              <li>
               Health products
              </li>
              <li>
               Baby Products
              </li>
              <li>
               Homeware
              </li>
              <li>
               Giftware
              </li>
              <li>
               Tourism destinations
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p>
              <strong>
               THE UK PAVILION
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              The UK Pavilion will be designed to reflect the key elements of heritage, innovation, style, and quality Chinese consumers associate with the UK.
              <br/>
              Taking inspiration from the famous shopping arcades found in our oldest cities  a mix of shell scheme stands and showcases  the buyer will be presented with an array of the finest UK products displayed in a stylish, and undoubtedly British, environment.
             </p>

              To enquire about CHIMPORT,
