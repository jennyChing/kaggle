       <a href="http://lovelypackage.com/chinese-tea-box/#comments" title="Comment on Chinese Tea Box">

       <img alt="tea_box1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4895" height="358" src="http://lovelypackage.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/04/tea_box1.jpg" title="tea_box1" width="538"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Designed by
       <a href="http://carlogiovani.com/info/" target="_blank">
        Carlo Giovani
       </a>
       | Country: Brazil
      </p>
      <p>
       
       <span class="projeto_desc">
        We decided to give to some ordinary packagesa new face based on the product inside, creating a kind of toy-pack, a beautiful object, that people can not put on the trash, but take it to them, reusing the pack. In this case, a beatiful chinese girl opens her mouth to offer you a delicious jasmine tea.
       </span>
      </p>
