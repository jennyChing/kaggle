          <p>
           Just because were young doesnt mean we cant be interested in high culture! Plus, we all know you loved Black Swan, so youll probably love these pictures too.
           <span id="more-6611">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           San Francisco Ballet was founded in 1933, making it the first professional ballet company in the United States. Its home is in the
           <a href="http://sfwmpac.org/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://sfwmpac.org/', 'War Memorial Opera House');" target="_blank">
            War Memorial Opera House
           </a>
           , which has seen some history: the United Nations had their first conference there in 1945 and the
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_San_Francisco" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Treaty_of_San_Francisco', 'Treaty of San Francisco');" target="_blank">
            Treaty of San Francisco
           </a>
           was drafted and signed there in 1951.
          </p>
          <p>
           Big thanksto the
           <a href="https://www.sfballet.org/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.sfballet.org/', 'San Francisco Ballet');" target="_blank">
            San Francisco Ballet
           </a>
           and the
           <a href="http://www.mpdsf.org/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.mpdsf.org/', 'Museum of Performance and Design');" target="_blank">
            Museum of Performance and Design
           </a>
           for these awesome photos ranging from the 1940s to the late 1960s!
          </p>
