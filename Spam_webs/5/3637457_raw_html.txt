       </a>
      </h1>
      <ul class="post-meta">
       <li class="date">

       </li>
       <li class="author">
        <a href="http://punktual-media.com/author/" rel="author" title="Posts by ">
        </a>
       </li>
       <li class="comments">
        <a href="http://punktual-media.com/pop-music-i-like-ed-sheeran-sing/#respond" title="Comment on Pop music I like: Ed Sheeran  Sing">

       </li>
      </ul>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        Besides quietly supporting him as a fellow ginger, I never really paid much attention to Ed Sheeran. Sure, he wrote his own songs and all that but he was a little bit
        <em>
         bland
        </em>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        And then, he only goes and drops this banger
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/tlYcUqEPN58" width="560">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
        Why is this not the official world cup song? That woah chorus is a perfect crowd pleaser. Calling this for summer song of 2014, in my world at least. Break out the convertibles and ice cream. I wish Justin Timberlake still made songs like this.
       </p>
