         <h2 class="date-header">
          Wednesday, February 02, 2011
         </h2>
         <div class="entry-category-author_kevin_michael_murphy entry-category-dance entry-category-music entry-category-viral_videos entry-author-kmp_murphy entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a012876c6c7fb970c0147e21193d1970b">
          <div class="entry-inner">
           <h3 class="entry-header">
            AND THE WORLD SPINS MADLY ON
           </h3>
           <div class="entry-content">
            <div class="entry-body">
             <p>
              <em>
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <span style="font-size: 17px;">
                 A love story told through dance and animation.
                 <br/>
                </span>
               </span>
              </em>
              <strong>
               <span style="font-size: large;">
                <span style="font-size: 17px;">
                 <span style="font-size: 11pt;">
                  By Kevin Michael Murphy(Actor-Voice Teacher)
                 </span>
                </span>
               </span>
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              I am addicted to the r
              <strong>
               ush of emotions that accompany being inspired
              </strong>
              . It's like artistic
              <a href="http://www.google.com/imgres?imgurl=http://i157.photobucket.com/albums/t66/adreamdeferred/dogs%2520and%2520cats%2520funny/Catnip.jpg&amp;imgrefurl=http://www.myspace.com/449946343&amp;usg=__MOpj3LJ7M_n5n10BMeoRLi8bitw=&amp;h=367&amp;w=445&amp;sz=62&amp;hl=en&amp;start=26&amp;zoom=1&amp;tbnid=y5V_KtY-C_toeM:&amp;tbnh=125&amp;tbnw=161&amp;ei=bPlCTYCINYXqgAf6-MWFAg&amp;prev=/images%3Fq%3Dcatnip%26um%3D1%26hl%3Den%26client%3Dsafari%26sa%3DN%26rls%3Den%26biw%3D1024%26bih%3D573%26tbs%3Disch:10%2C1114&amp;um=1&amp;itbs=1&amp;iact=hc&amp;vpx=455&amp;vpy=188&amp;dur=886&amp;hovh=204&amp;hovw=247&amp;tx=110&amp;ty=95&amp;oei=avlCTf7tFIL-8AbSi9G0AQ&amp;esq=3&amp;page=3&amp;ndsp=15&amp;ved=1t:429,r:12,s:26&amp;biw=1024&amp;bih=573" target="_blank">
               catnip
              </a>
              . This piece which blends animation, music, and choreography, is just that. The following animated short, entitled "Thought of You" is by
              <a href="http://conteanimated.com/about-ryan/" target="_blank">
               Ryan Woodward
              </a>
              , based on choreography by
              <a href="http://history.cfac.byu.edu/index.php/Kori_Wakamatsu" target="_blank">
               Kori Wakamatsu
              </a>
              . The
              <strong>
               beautiful
              </strong>
              song is by
              <a href="www.theweepies.com" target="_blank">
               The Weepies
              </a>
              .
             </p>
             <p>
              <object height="306" width="500">
               <param name="movie" value="http://www.youtube.com/e/OBk3ynRbtsw">
               </param>
               <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
               </param>
               <param name="allowscriptaccess" value="always">
               </param>
               <embed allowfullscreen="true" allowscriptaccess="always" height="306" src="http://www.youtube.com/e/OBk3ynRbtsw" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="500">
               </embed>
              </object>
             </p>
             <p>
              The animator's thoughts on this video:
             </p>
             <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
              <em>
               The birth of Thought of Youcame from my desire to unite several of my passions into one art piece. Figurative works, 2d animation, EFX animation, and contemporary dance. Put all three of these forms together to support a theme centered around the complexities of intimate relationshipsand whalaa, Thought of Youis born!
              </em>
             </p>
            </div>
            <a id="more">
            </a>
            <div class="entry-more">
             <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
              <em>
               Rather than creating a narrative animated piece that communicates a well defined story, this piece allows for each individual who views it to to experience something unique and personal that touches their own sensibilities.
              </em>
             </p>
             <p>
              The animator's thoughts on art:
             </p>
             <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
              <em>
               It's one of those things it's tough to answer, when you ask an artist why they decide to be an artist there is this inner beast of creativity, that me personally, will consume me to the point of being miserable if I don't let it out and do something with it.
              </em>
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               <span style="font-style: normal;">
                Word, Ryan Woodward,
                <strong>
                 word.
                </strong>
                Wanna see how they made this whole thing happen? Check it out!
               </span>
              </em>
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               <span style="font-style: normal;">
                <iframe frameborder="0" height="281" src="http://player.vimeo.com/video/16330140" width="500">
                </iframe>
               </span>
              </em>
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               <span style="font-style: normal;">
                All of this collaboration
                <strong>
                 inspires
                </strong>
                me to think outside the box more often, and also makes me
                <strong>
                 sad
                </strong>
                I missed RSO and Matthew Murphy's collaborative mix of photography and song,
                <a href="http://www.crazytownblog.com/crazytown/35mm/" target="_blank">
                 35mm
                </a>
                .
               </span>
              </em>
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               <span style="font-style: normal;">
                Thanks to
                <a href="http://www.myspace.com/barbaraanselmi" target="_blank">
                 Barbara Anselmi
                </a>
                and
                <a href="http://gabyalter.com/" target="_self">
                 Gaby Alter
                </a>
                for introducing this to me!
               </span>
              </em>
             </p>
            </div>
