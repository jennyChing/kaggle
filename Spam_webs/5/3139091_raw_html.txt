      <h1>
       <a href="/blogs/blog/15662628-he-s-playing-where-the-cuse-edition" title="Hes Playing Where? The Cuse Edition">
        Hes Playing Where? The Cuse Edition
       </a>
      </h1>
      <p>
       <em>
        By
        <strong>
         <a href="/blogs/blog/15616136-waterboys-refill" target="_blank" title="Waterboy">
          Waterboy
         </a>
         Sam Meyerkopf
        </strong>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        A crew of former Syracuse hoops stars and role players are droppin dimes and swishin Js across Europe. While it may not be the same as
        <a href="http://youtu.be/HxWv1myZjaM" target="_blank" title="balling in a packed Carrier Dome">
         balling in a packed Carrier Dome
        </a>
        , these guys are getting paid to do what they love on the old continent. Theyre hooping in at least five countries, with a few guaranteed head-to-head alumni matchups.
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.cskabasket.com/team/?player=4526&amp;team=main&amp;season=77&amp;lang=en" target="_blank" title="Demetris Nichols">
         Demetris Nichols  CSKA Moscow (Russia)
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Nichols has reached the Euro basketball mountaintop by signing with
       <a href="http://www.cskabasket.com/" target="_blank" title="CSKA Moscow">
        CSKA Moscow
       </a>
       . They pay the biggest salaries and are a
       <a href="http://www.euroleague.net" target="_blank" title="Euroleague">
        Euroleague
       </a>
       title contender every year.Hell be one of the easiest players to find game footage of as all his
       <a href="http://www.vtb-league.com/en/home/home.htm" target="_blank" title="VTB (Russian) League">
        VTB (Russian) league
       </a>
       games are on their website and all Euroleague games are on
       <a href="http://www.livebasketball.tv" target="_blank" title="Livebasketball.tv">
        LiveBasketball.tv
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.twitter.com/andyrautins1" target="_blank" title="Andy Rautins">
        <strong>
         Andy Rautins  Varese (Italy)
        </strong>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Rautins is a perfect fit for Euro Ball with his ability to play both guard positions, his savvy passing, smart defense, and of course the knockdown jump shot.
      </p>
      <p>
       Whatever he does, you gotta love how juiced up his coach gets after wins!
      </p>
      <p>
       <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/m3GTnVi4lns" width="560">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <em style="background-color: #fce5cd;">
         Want to see a similar write-up of your schools former players? Hit us up on Twitter @point3hoops with hashtag #Waterboy.
        </em>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.twitter.com/bswish20" target="_blank" title="Brandon Triche">
        <strong>
         Brandon Triche  Virtus Roma (Italy)
        </strong>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       After winning the championship in Italys second division last year, Triche got the bump up to the first division. Running point for one of the most historic teams in Italy is a great accomplishment this early in his Euro career. In his first EuroCup game this week he blew up for
       <a href="http://www.eurocupbasketball.com/eurocup/games/results/showgame?gamecode=9&amp;seasoncode=U2014#!boxscore" target="_blank" title="26 points, six rebounds, five assists, three steals">
        26 points, six rebounds, five assists, three steals
       </a>
       , his team won by 29, and he was named
       <a href="http://www.eurocupbasketball.com/eurocup/news/i/142032" target="_blank" title="EuroCup Player of the Week">
        EuroCup Player of the Week!
       </a>
       Talk about a debut.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://web.legabasket.it/player/FLY-JON-89/flynn_jonny" target="_blank" title="Jonny Flynn">
         Jonny Flynn  Upea Capo DOrlando (Italy)
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       The former 6
       <sup>
        th
       </sup>
       overall pick in the NBA draft has landed in Europe after running around in
       <a href="http://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/nba/2012/11/04/jonny-flynn-nbl-melbourne-tigers-timberwolves-pistons/1682449/" target="_blank" title="Australia">
        Australia
       </a>
       and
       <a href="http://www.syracuse.com/orangebasketball/index.ssf/2013/09/ex-syracuse_star_jonny_flynn_h.html" target="_blank" title="China">
        China
       </a>
       . Flynn is on a mid to lower level Italian team but hell be runnin the show for this squad. A duel of Syracuse PGs between Flynn and Triche on October 26 will be must watch for fans of the Orange!
      </p>
      <img alt="" src="//cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0167/2612/files/Euro_Ball_sites_large.png?5650" style="margin-left: 10px; float: right;"/>
      <br/>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.twitter.com/edeven23" target="_blank" title="Eric Devendorf">
        <strong>
         Eric Devendorf  Proger BLS Chieti (Italy)
        </strong>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Devendorf was in Israel and New Zealand 2
       <sup>
        nd
       </sup>
       Divisions last year. It looked like hed gotten his act together professionally but then
       <a href="http://www.mlive.com/sports/bay-city/index.ssf/2014/08/injury_prevents_eric_devendorf.html" target="_blank" title="failed his physical">
        failed his physical
       </a>
       with this squad. Devos getting a real taste of basketball journeymanism now. Well see how this story ends.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.tblstat.net/pd.asp?p=2597" target="_blank" title="Paul Harris">
         Paul Harris  Royal Hali Gaziantep (Turkey)
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       In the Cuse-filled France, Harris had a great season last year for SLUC Nancy and earned a big contract in Turkey. Harris is a unique matchup because of his crazy strength and ability to play inside and out. Sadly though, hes rocking the clean-cut look now, no more braids.
      </p>
      <p>
       One French Fan even made him his own Highlight Hall Of Fame!
      </p>
      <p>
       <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/SMZTWxfl2rs" width="560">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <meta charset="utf-8"/>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.twitter.com/RealJames43" target="_blank" title="James Southerland">
        <strong>
         James Southerland  Limoges CSP (France)
        </strong>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Southerland just signed, as in like two days ago, to the French Champion. Not only is Southerland playing on one of the best teams in France, but
       <a href="http://www.euroleague.net/competition/teams/showteam?clubcode=LMG&amp;seasoncode=E2014" target="_blank" title="Limoges is also in the Euroleague">
        Limoges is also in the Euroleague
       </a>
       . Limoges also has one of the best team
       <a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/LIMOGESCSPSASP" target="_blank" title="Youtube channels">
        Youtube channels
       </a>
       in Europe with incredible game recaps, making Southerland very easy to follow.
      </p>
      <meta charset="utf-8"/>
      <p>
       <em>
        <strong style="background-color: #fce5cd;">
         Want to keep up with future blog posts from Waterboy and get special offers from POINT 3?
         <a href="http://eepurl.com/2RzWT" target="_blank" title="Click here">
          Click here
         </a>
         to sign up for our e-mails.
        </strong>
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.twitter.com/RealKrisJo" target="_blank" title="Kris Joseph">
        <strong>
         Kris Joseph  JDA Dijon Bourgogne (France)
        </strong>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Joseph is starting his second year in France, just on a different team. Similar to when he suited up for the Orange, the question with Joseph is how active will he be? Hell need to be if he wants to continue to progress in his pro career.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.twitter.com/scoopjardine" target="_blank" title="Scoop Jardine">
        <strong>
         Scoop Jardine - UBC Gssing Knights (Austria)
        </strong>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Jardine went to Croatia for a couple weeks last year and just wasnt ready to contribute to a decent European team yet. Rumor was he had signed in Austria, one the lower level leagues in Europe, but through three games he hasnt appeared in any box scores.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.sportando.com/en/europe/france/126939/chalons-reims-tab-darryl-watkins.html" target="_blank" title="Darryl Watkins">
        <strong>
         Darryl Watkins  Champagne Chalons-Reims (France)
        </strong>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Watkins team will struggle to stay in Playoff contention but if he can block shots and finish at the rim offensively, it will provide a huge lift.
      </p>
      <p>
       Shout Outs to
       <a href="https://twitter.com/JoshPace83" target="_blank" title="Josh Pace">
        Josh Pace (New Zealand)
       </a>
       and
       <a href="http://www.syracuse.com/orangebasketball/index.ssf/2014/08/former_syracuse_center_fab_melo_signs_with_pro_team_in_brazil.html#incart_river" target="_blank" title="Fab Melo">
        Fab Melo (Brazil)
       </a>
       plying their trade around the world as well.
