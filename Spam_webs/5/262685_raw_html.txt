      <span>
       Facing Fear When Selling? Check Out These 5 Tips
      </span>
     </h1>
     <hr class="half-bottom"/>
     <div class="date">
      Posted on October 27th, by Stacy Jackson in

        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mtdsalestraining.com/mtdblog/5-essential-steps-eliminate-fear.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.mtdsalestraining.com/mtdblog/5-essential-steps-eliminate-fear.html', '']);" target="_blank">
          <img class="alignleft" src="http://www.leadmanagement.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/4b660bba-81c8-4655-9c33-85cb20dec388.jpg" style="width: 100%"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          Fear is one of the most debilitating emotions a human being can experience. It can stop you emotionally and physically from carrying out even mundane tasks
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         Source:
         <a href="http://www.mtdsalestraining.com/mtdblog/5-essential-steps-eliminate-fear.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.mtdsalestraining.com/mtdblog/5-essential-steps-eliminate-fear.html', 'www.mtdsalestraining.com']);" target="_blank">
          www.mtdsalestraining.com
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you have been in sales for a long time, maybe you dont experience fear anymore while doing your job. For the new sales reps and entrepreneurs out there, selling may still be a fear-inducing activity.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Sean McPheat of MTD Sales Training outlines 5 steps to overcoming fear when selling:
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Think through worst-case scenarios
         </li>
         <li>
          Be realistic  fears are often worse than the reality.
         </li>
         <li>
          Dont try to be perfect
         </li>
         <li>
          Go back to the basics
         </li>
         <li>
          Reward yourself after facing a dreaded call or meeting
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out Seans full post at
         <a href="http://www.mtdsalestraining.com/mtdblog/5-essential-steps-eliminate-fear.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.mtdsalestraining.com/mtdblog/5-essential-steps-eliminate-fear.html', 'http://www.mtdsalestraining.com/mtdblog/5-essential-steps-eliminate-fear.html']);" rel="nofollow">
          http://www.mtdsalestraining.com/mtdblog/5-essential-steps-eliminate-fear.html
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         How do you face your selling fears?
        </p>
