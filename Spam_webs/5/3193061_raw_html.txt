       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         The power of words
        </h1>
        <p class="p-meta">
         <span>
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2014-06-13T10:02:44+00:00">
           June 13, 2014
          </time>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-featured">
        <div class="entry-thumb">
         <img alt="Words matter. Make yours something special." class="attachment-entry-fullwidth wp-post-image" height="600" src="http://www.silouan.com/silouan/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/power-of-words.jpg" width="600"/>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content content">
        <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_3787" style="width: 310px">
         <a href="http://www.silouan.com/silouan/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/power-of-words.jpg">
          <img alt="Words matter. Make yours something special." class="size-medium wp-image-3787" height="300" src="http://www.silouan.com/silouan/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/power-of-words-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Words matter. Make yours something special.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          I was reminded yesterday about the power of words. Meeting with a couple of people who have been helped by and seen the The Ladder UPP change lives, one of them recounted words from family members that had haunted her for years.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          Thunder Thighs. Fat. Youll never amount to anything.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          They were spoken so often, they became her identify. Year after year, beaten down, they were a prophecy to be fulfilled.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          Damn them.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          I thought of the words I heard from my commanding officer when I was broken, PTSD, are you kidding me. Shrink says you are going to kill yourself. I say BS! Youre just whining, a wimp! You are no Marine. Of course, he used much more colorful language!
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.silouan.com/silouan/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          And I believed him. I thought I was pathetic and weak. As someone who had been taught that only the tough survive, I didnt think I deserved to survive. So overwhelmed by the incredible emotion and physical pain I was suffering at the time, I put a gun to my head. This is the power of words.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          Of course, words can have the opposite effect.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          I believe in you.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          You arent a label. You are whatever you dream, what you hope for. You are what you do right now to live. You are the people you love, those that love you.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          Hold me daddy.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          I forgive you.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          I love you, no matter what.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          Im sorry.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          Lets go for a walk in the woods.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          You get the point. Words matter. Just a few can destroy a life, just like a few can save a life.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          Make your words matter in a good way. It doesnt take much to be a light to those around us.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font: 13.0px Arial;">
          Please, take a moment today to tell someone you are sorry. Or to tell someone you love them. Or just say something nice to a stranger who looks like they need it. You might help change a life, or you might just make the world a better place. Either way, sometimes it only takes the right word. Understand the power of words and use them for good. You will help change peoples lives.
         </span>
        </p>
