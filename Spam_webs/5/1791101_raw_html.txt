            The 7 National Parks You Need to Visit
           </h2>

           <p>
            Sometimes it is easy to forget how much natural beauty exists in the U.S. So in honor of National Park Week (April 18-24), weve put together a list of the 7 National Parks we think you need to see. National Park Week is Americas biggest celebration of national heritage. Its all about exploring amazing places, discovering open spaces and enjoying our countrys landscape.
           </p>
           <p>
            And heres the best part: StudentUniverse offers student tour deals to all of them. So check out the list below and start planning your next adventure!
           </p>
           <ol>
            <li>
             <b>
              Yosemite National Park
             </b>
              California
            </li>
           </ol>
           <p>
            Best known for its waterfalls, Yosemite also boasts a vast wilderness area, rolling meadows, sequoias and many deep valleys. Between the impressive granite and towering glaciers at this park, theres no shortage of breathtaking sights. In October of this year, the park will celebrate its 125th anniversary as a national park  even more reason to spend some time indulging in the historic landscape.
           </p>
           <p>
            If youre one for adventure, weve got the perfect opportunity for you. Our 10-day
            <a href="http://www.studentuniverse.com/tour/yosemite-active-adventure/1362">
             Yosemite Active Adventure
            </a>
            trip allows you to camp in the middle of the park itself, raft the rapids of the American River, trek overnight into the high sierra of Yosemite and more. If the beautiful scenery of Napa Valley is more your style, split your time between the two regions on our
            <a href="http://www.studentuniverse.com/tour/yosemite-park-napa-valley/1204">
             Yosemite Park &amp; Napa Valley trip
            </a>
            . On this trip, youll find yourself exploring Yosemites famous waterfalls, paddling the Russian River in Sonoma wine country and camping under the stars.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_5415" style="width: 5806px">
            <img alt="Source: http://st.gdefon.com/wallpapers_original/555308_yosemite_national_park_reka_goryi_skalyi_peyzaj_5796x3531_www.Gde-Fon.com.jpg" class="size-full wp-image-5415" height="3531" src="http://travelblog.studentuniverse.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Yosemite_USA1.jpg" width="5796"/>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Source: http://st.gdefon.com/wallpapers_original/555308_yosemite_national_park_reka_goryi_skalyi_peyzaj_5796x3531_www.Gde-Fon.com.jpg
            </p>
           </div>
           <ol start="2">
            <li>
             <b>
              Yellowstone National Park
             </b>
              Wyoming, Montana &amp; Idaho
            </li>
           </ol>
           <p>
            Home to Old Faithful and the majority of the worlds geysers, you cant go wrong with a visit to Yellowstone. It was established in 1872 as the countrys first national park and has continued to be a destination for nature enthusiasts ever since. It boasts one of the last natural ecosystems on the planet and is a wildland home to grizzly bears, bison, wolves and more.
           </p>
           <p>
            If youre looking to be inspired by the wilderness at its most pure level, you should check out our
            <a href="http://www.studentuniverse.com/tour/camping-yosemite-yellowstone/1207">
             Camping Yosemite and Yellowstone trip
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_5406" style="width: 645px">
            <img alt="1104yellowstoneoldfaithfulhoriz-3112011_panoramic" class="wp-image-5406 size-full" height="345" src="http://travelblog.studentuniverse.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/1104yellowstoneoldfaithfulhoriz-3112011_panoramic.jpeg" width="635"/>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Source: http://images.budgettravel.com/1104yellowstoneoldfaithfulhoriz-3112011_panoramic.jpeg
            </p>
           </div>
           <ol start="3">
            <li>
             <b>
              <strong>
               Death Valley N
              </strong>
              ational Park
             </b>
              California &amp; Nevada
            </li>
           </ol>
           <p>
            Situated between the Great Basin and the Mojave desert, this below-sea-level basin is sure to leave you in awe. The drought-stricken area also surprisingly offers snow-capped mountains, vast fields of wildflowers and oases which support wildlife. If youre interested in experiencing this national park and mixing in the glitz of Las Vegas and Los Angeles along the way, our
            <a href="http://www.studentuniverse.com/tour/sin-surf-sierras/1205">
             Sin, Surf &amp; Sierras trip
            </a>
            is perfect for you.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_5407" style="width: 1930px">
            <img alt="Badwater_Basin_Death_Valley_National_Park_California" class="wp-image-5407 size-full" height="1080" src="http://travelblog.studentuniverse.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Badwater_Basin_Death_Valley_National_Park_California.jpg" width="1920"/>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Source: http://t.wallpaperweb.org/wallpaper/nature/19201080/Badwater_Basin_Death_Valley_National_Park_California.jpg
            </p>
           </div>
           <ol start="4">
            <li>
             <b>
              Grand Canyon National Park
             </b>
              Arizona
            </li>
           </ol>
           <p>
            Its immense size alone is enough to make the Grand Canyon one of the most impressive national parks in the country. After visiting the site, President Theodore Roosevelt said that its, the one great sight which every American should see, and we would certainly have to agree. Check out our
            <a href="http://www.studentuniverse.com/tour/national-parks-bright-lights/639">
             National Parks and Bright Lights trip
            </a>
            , which will lead you through Los Angeles, Monument Valley, the Grand Canyon, Las Vegas and beyond.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_5408" style="width: 1510px">
            <img alt="Source: http://a30de2edae0c4b0bc6ba-a8477a60b1c5d9e290ed2e4c0d53743c.r24.cf1.rackcdn.com/4/5/large.jpg" class="size-full wp-image-5408" height="1000" src="http://travelblog.studentuniverse.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/large.jpg" width="1500"/>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Source: http://a30de2edae0c4b0bc6ba-a8477a60b1c5d9e290ed2e4c0d53743c.r24.cf1.rackcdn.com/4/5/large.jpg
            </p>
           </div>
           <ol start="5">
            <li>
             <b>
              Arches National Park
             </b>
              Utah
            </li>
           </ol>
           <p>
            Dubbed a red rock wonderland, this national park offers a landscape full of contrasting colors and textures unlike anything youve seen before. Find yourself surrounded by over 2,000 natural stone arches and giant balanced rocks. Our
            <a href="http://www.studentuniverse.com/tour/usa-hike-bike-raft/1321">
             USA Hike, Bike &amp; Raft trip
            </a>
            will bring you to this beautiful park, in addition to Zion National Park, Bryce Canyon National Park, Cataract Canyon, The Grand Canyon, Monument Valley and Las Vegas all over a 12-day span. As someone seeking adventure and natural beauty, what more could you ask for?
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_5409" style="width: 610px">
            <img alt="Source: http://images.nationalgeographic.com/wpf/media-live/photos/000/020/cache/arches-camping_2030_600x450.jpg" class="size-full wp-image-5409" height="450" src="http://travelblog.studentuniverse.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/arches-camping_2030_600x450.jpg" width="600"/>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Source: http://images.nationalgeographic.com/wpf/media-live/photos/000/020/cache/arches-camping_2030_600x450.jpg
            </p>
           </div>
           <ol start="6">
            <li>
             <b>
              Denali National Park
             </b>
              Alaska
            </li>
           </ol>
           <p>
            Despite being known for hosting North Americas highest peak, Mount McKinley, this national park has so much more to offer. It is home to 37 different species of mammals and 130 different bird species, so youre destined to see a vast array of wildlife during your visit.
           </p>
           <p>
            Sign up for our
            <a href="http://www.studentuniverse.com/tour/highlights-of-alaska/634">
             Highlights of Alaska trip
            </a>
            and immerse yourself in the park. This trip will be one to remember, allowing you to devour seafood in Anchorage, hike through Denali National Park, hear glaciers groat at Wrangell-St. Elias, marvel at the views of the Kenai Peninsula and more.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_5410" style="width: 910px">
            <img alt="Source: http://images.fineartamerica.com/images-medium-large/denali-national-park-john-hyde.jpg" class="size-full wp-image-5410" height="600" src="http://travelblog.studentuniverse.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/denali-national-park-john-hyde.jpg" width="900"/>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Source: http://images.fineartamerica.com/images-medium-large/denali-national-park-john-hyde.jpg
            </p>
           </div>
           <ol start="7">
            <li>
             <b>
              Shenandoah National Park
             </b>
              Virginia
            </li>
           </ol>
           <p>
            A mere 75 miles outside of Washington D.C., this national park is a perfect escape the hustle and bustle of the city. The park boasts over 500 miles of trails which will lead you anywhere from waterfalls, viewpoints, the wilderness and beyond. If youre looking for a trip that encompasses both city life and a touch of nature, check out our
            <a href="http://www.studentuniverse.com/tour/music-mountains-monuments-eastbound/1215">
             Music, Mountains &amp; Monuments trip
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="virginia-shenandoah-national-park-1-P" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5411" height="539" src="http://travelblog.studentuniverse.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/virginia-shenandoah-national-park-1-P.jpg" width="1038"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Dont stop there  check out the rest of our
            <a href="http://www.studentuniverse.com/tours/search?region=North%20America&amp;q=National%20Parks">
             North America National Parks discounts
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            Want to go beyond these national parks? Check out our
            <a href="http://www.studentuniverse.com/tours/promotions">
             Tours Promo Page
            </a>
            for discounts on even more tours around the world.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
