     <p>
      <i>
       <b>
        Escape from Planet Monday
       </b>
      </i>
      is a
      <a href="/wiki/Drum_and_bass" title="Drum and bass">
       drum and bass
      </a>
      album by former Bad Company member
      <a href="/wiki/DJ_Fresh" title="DJ Fresh">
       DJ Fresh
      </a>
      . The album was released in 2006 to positive reviews.
     </p>
     <p>
      The album has several adjoining tracks such as the intro tracks, and "The Looking Glass".
     </p>
