      <h1 class="entry-title">
       American Horror Story: Freak Show New Details Emerge!
      </h1>
      <p>
       <img alt="American Horror Story" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5336" src="http://www.stonebridgedaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/American-Horror-Story.jpg" width="485"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Get excited
       <strong>
        American Horror Story
       </strong>
       fans because a bunch of new details about season 4 have emerged!
      </p>
      <p>
       First and foremost,
       <strong>
        Ryan Murphy
       </strong>
       has
       <a href="https://twitter.com/MrRPMurphy/statuses/448145257918513153" target="newwindow" title="Ryan Murphy's Twitter">
        revealed via Twitter
       </a>
       that the season theme will be Freak Show!
      </p>
      <p>
       This comes after much speculation online of a carnival or circus theme for the UPCOMING SEASON.
      </p>
      <p>
       And to add to your excitement,
       <a href="http://insidetv.ew.com/2014/03/24/american-horror-story-freak-show-details/" target="newwindow" title="'American Horror Story: Freak Show': Who's in? Where is it set? New season 4 details -- EXCLUSIVE">
        new details surrounding the shows plot
       </a>
       have come to light!
      </p>
      <p>
       Reportedly, series regulars
       <strong>
        Jessica Lange, Evan Peters, Sarah Paulson
       </strong>
       and
       <strong>
        Frances Conroy
       </strong>
       will return alongside newer cast members
       <strong>
        Kathy Bates
       </strong>
       and
       <strong>
        Angela Bassett
       </strong>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Emma Roberts
       </strong>
       and
       <strong>
        Denis OHare
       </strong>
       are  also in talks to return.
      </p>
      <p>
       As for the plot, its being reported that Freak Show will be set in Jupiter, Florida during the 1950s.
      </p>
      <p>
       Lange will be portraying a German-American woman who manages one of the last freak shows in the US.
      </p>
      <p>
       This could very well be their best season yet! Although season 2
       <strong>
        Asylum
       </strong>
       is going to be a tough one to beat!
      </p>
