      <p>
       <img alt="Zeds Dead" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-55091" height="351" src="http://thissongissick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Zeds-Dead.jpg" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Canadian duo and TSIS favorite,
       <strong>
        Zeds Dead
       </strong>
       have been teasing the roll out of their
       <i>
        Somewhere Else
       </i>
       remix EP.We'vealready heard the duo's rework of "Collapse" newly titled
       <a href="http://thissongissick.com/blog/2015/zeds-dead-collapse-2-0-announce-remix-ep/" target="_blank">
        "Collapse 2.0"
       </a>
       , their
       <a href="http://thissongissick.com/blog/2015/zeds-dead-hadouken-vip/" target="_blank">
        VIP of "Hadouken"
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://thissongissick.com/blog/2015/premiere-zeds-dead-bustamove-mscls-remix/" target="_blank">
        MSCLS remix of "Bustamove"
       </a>
       , Sleepy Tom's remix of "Bustamove" and Nebbra's remix of "Collapse."
      </p>
      <p>
       Well the full release is out now and it's full of surprises.
       <em>
        Somewhere Else
       </em>
       packs a rave ready Electro remix of "Hadouken" from LA producer Valentino Khan, a house infused disco remix of "Lost You" from Kove, a heavy future house remix of "Where Are You Now"from fellow Canadian producer Hunter Siegel, a chilled out house remix from Neon Steve, and an alternate remix of"Collapse" from
       <span style="line-height: 20.7999992370605px;">
        Memorecks
       </span>
       . You can stream the all the remixesand pick up on iTunes below.
      </p>
      <p>
       Also, I hope you have marked your calendars for July 2nd when Zeds Dead's return to the iconic Red Rocks Amphitheatrein Colorado for the second edition of DEAD ROCKS with an amazing supporting lineup featuring Dillon Francis, Paper Diamond, Haywyre, Mija and Hunter Siegel. You can grab tickets
       <a href="http://bit.ly/DeadRocksTix" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://bit.ly']);" target="_blank">
        here.
       </a>
