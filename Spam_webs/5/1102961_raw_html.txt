        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Chocolate Chip Banana Bread
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-02-04T03:30:49+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          February 4, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.domestically-speaking.com/author/throughthelens" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            ThroughtheLens
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.domestically-speaking.com/2015/02/chocolate-chip-banana-bread.html#comments">
           1 Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="5531496" data-link="http://www.domestically-speaking.com/2015/02/chocolate-chip-banana-bread.html" data-summary="" data-title="Chocolate Chip Banana Bread">
        </div>
        <p>
         Howdy, everyone! Its Hannah from over at
         <a href="http://throughthelens-blog.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://throughthelens-blog.com/', 'Through the Lens']);" target="_blank">
          Through the Lens
         </a>
         , again. Who else thinks its crazy that its February already?! Today I want to share with you a quick and easy Chocolate Chip Banana Bread recipe that is so moist and yummy!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.domestically-speaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Chocolate-Chip-Banana-Bread-2.jpg">
          <img alt="Chocolate Chip Banana Bread Recipe" class="aligncenter wp-image-12008" height="1080" src="http://www.domestically-speaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Chocolate-Chip-Banana-Bread-2-683x1024.jpg" title="Chocolate Chip Banana Bread Recipe" width="720"/>
         </a>
         I promise you that everyone will love this!
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         Ingredients:
         <br/>
         2 eggs
         <br/>
          cup light brown sugar
         <br/>
          cup white sugar
         <br/>
          cup oil
         <br/>
         1 cup mashed overripe bananas (2 large bananas)
         <br/>
          cup sour cream
         <br/>
         1 tsp vanilla
         <br/>
         1 cup all-purpose flour
         <br/>
         1 tsp baking powder
         <br/>
           1 cup chocolate chips
        </p>
        <p>
         Instructions:
         <br/>
         Mix eggs, white and brown sugar. Add oil, mashed bananas, sour cream, vanilla and mix some more. Add dry ingredients. Gently fold in chocolate chips With a spoon or spatula. Pour into a greased loaf pan. Bake at 350 until a toothpick inserted in the middle of the bread comes out clean, about 50-60 minutes.
        </p>
        <hr/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.domestically-speaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Chocolate-Chip-Banana-Bread.jpg">
          <img alt="Chocolate Chip Banana Bread Recipe" class="aligncenter wp-image-12009" height="480" src="http://www.domestically-speaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Chocolate-Chip-Banana-Bread-1024x683.jpg" title="Chocolate Chip Banana Bread Recipe" width="720"/>
         </a>
         I would love for you to follow along on
         <a href="instagram.com/galloping4god" target="_blank">
          Instagram
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="www.pinterest.com/galloping4god/" target="_blank">
          Pinterest
         </a>
         , and
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/throughthelenshannahdiane" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.facebook.com/throughthelenshannahdiane', 'Facebook']);" target="_blank">
          Facebook
         </a>
         ! Also, keep updated on all my DIY projects, recipes and home decor projects over at
         <a href="throughthelens-blog.com" target="_blank">
          Through the Lens
         </a>
         !
        </p>
        <p>
         I hope you have a good rest of your week!
        </p>
        <p>
         Sincerely,
         <br/>
         Hannah Diane
        </p>
