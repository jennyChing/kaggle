     <h1>
      4 Alternative Sleeping Cycles That You Didnt Know About [infographic]
     </h1>
     <div class="single-post-meta">
      <img alt="" class="avatar avatar-55 photo" height="55" src="http://1.gravatar.com/avatar/55a91885479e60d709560cdfe39d8724?s=55&amp;d=http%3A%2F%2F1.gravatar.com%2Favatar%2Fad516503a11cd5ca435acc9bb6523536%3Fs%3D55&amp;r=G" width="55"/>

         <p>
          Around the world, theres one thing people seem to agree on  that we go to sleep at night and stay awake in the day. But science proves that other sleeping patterns are feasible, and possibly more preferable. Follow the law of contrary public opinion, and try one of these alternative sleeping cycles.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Did you know:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          According to the
          <a href="http://sleepfoundation.org/sleep-topics/napping" target="_blank">
           National Sleep Foundation
          </a>
          , 85% of mammals sleep in polyphasic patterns, meaning that they sleep in portions throughout the day. The vast majority of us, however are monophasic, but is that always best for us? Read on to find out which sleeping pattern youd be best suited to.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you have already tried one and find it more effective than the method of us average humans, let us know in the comments!
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="4 Alternative Sleeping Cycles That You Didn't Know About, an infographic guide." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5859" height="6989" src="http://www.dreams.co.uk/sleep-matters-club/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/4-sleeping-cycles-you-didnt-know-about.jpg" width="800"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Above any unusual sleep patterns, Its massively important that you still get enough sleep, in whatever form that takes. One of our other infographics,
          <a href="http://www.dreams.co.uk/sleep-matters-club/7-sleeping-mistakes-infographic/" target="_blank">
           7 Sleep Mistakes You Dont Know Youre Making and How to Fix Them
          </a>
          , looks at what you can do about the sleep youre getting now, regardless of pattern.
         </p>
