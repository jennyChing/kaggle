          <h2 class="h-custom-headline center-text h2 accent">
           <span>
            <span style="color: #2070e8;">
             Seeking Health Care?
            </span>
           </span>
          </h2>
         </h2>
         <p>
          Today more than ever seniors desire to grow older in the comfort of their own home, to age in place is how one may refer to it. Whether you or a loved one are still living at home, considering moving in with a loved one, or even in to a community, home care may be a great option. Care provided in the home can be provided by a home health agency, home care agency and/or hospice depending you or your loved ones particular needs. Home care agencys employee care assistants (sometimes referred to as caregivers) who assist seniors with activities of daily living (ADLs). These services can include assistance with, but not limited to bathing, dressing, and transportation just to name a few. Home Health agencies consist of licensed medical professionals to aid in skilled services like wound care, physical therapy, IV, and much more. Whether you need assistance short term or long term,
          <strong>
           <em>
            iSeniorSolutions
           </em>
          </strong>
          can provide you with a list of agencies sure to meet your needs and desires.
