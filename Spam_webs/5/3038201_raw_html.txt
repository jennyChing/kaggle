         </a>
         Best Homemade Acne Treatment
        </h2>
        <p>
         Do you, or someone you know, suffer from acne? If so, you (or they) arent alone. In fact, according to
         <a href="http://brown.edu/Student_Services/Health_Services/Health_Education/common_college_health_issues/acne.php" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Brown University
         </a>
         , approximately 17 million Americans suffer from acne. Knowing youve got company probably doesnt make you feel much better about your acne am I right? Well, what if I told you there was an incredible homemade acne spot treatment that could zap your zits like
         <i>
          that
         </i>
         (imagine me snapping my fingers right there
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.freebiefindingmom.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         )? This article details how to make the best homemade acne treatment around.
         <br/>
         <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="https://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=monandsavhel-20&amp;l=ur2&amp;o=1" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
         <br/>
         You may be asking why should I care about good homemade acne treatments when I can just pick something up at the store? Its true. The store shelves are lined with acne treatments all claiming to be the best at zapping zits and soothing skin. However, not only are these store bought goods expensive, theyre filled with unpronounceable ingredients that can be pretty rough on skin.
        </p>
        <p>
         This recipe for the best homemade acne treatment is a great solution for any acne sufferer. It zaps zits without exposing your skin to the harsh chemicals found in many store bought varieties. It is easy to make (just 3 steps!) and doesnt require any expensive or difficult to find ingredients, which is great because you may have an unexpected flare-up where a homemade acne spot treatment is a must-have.
        </p>
        <p>
         Go ahead, give it a try! Youll quickly realize why I labeled this the best homemade acne treatment.
        </p>

          INGREDIENTS

         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          DIRECTIONS
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         1. Choose an airtight, glass jar to hold your zit zapper, these
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00AA2KZPS/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00AA2KZPS&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=monandsavhel-20&amp;linkId=G4K7V2VMN6VNJYGK" target="_blank">
          4 oz. mason jars
         </a>
         are a perfect size.
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Soften coconut oil just slightly in jar (10 to 15 seconds in the microwave should do it).
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.pinterest.com/pin/295971006737959989/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Best Homemade Acne Treatment 2" class="aligncenter wp-image-35766 size-full" height="400" src="http://www.freebiefindingmom.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/best-homemade-acne-treatment2.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Stir in essential oils.
        </p>
        <p>
         4. Place in refrigerator to set.
        </p>
        <p>
         To use: dab a cotton swab in zit zapper and apply a dab to acne in the morning and evening after washing and drying your face well.
        </p>
        <p>
         If coconut oil is in a warm room it may soften, it is still good. You can keep it in refrigerator if you prefer a solid mixture.
        </p>
