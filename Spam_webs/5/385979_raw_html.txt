           Biography:
          </span>
          <span class="author-info-text">
           Hi my name is Andrew Beard I am 33 years old and have been involved in music for over 20 years now. I am a family man with a 7 year old daughter. My music is a huge part of my life and is something I wish to pursue more as a career. I have been singing since the age of 9 being involved in several different amateur dramatic societies from then including ones from the West Midlands to Cornwall my roles include Higgins in My Fair Lady, Jen Val Jean in a Les Mis medley, Tommy in Brigadoon and more recently Curly in Oklahoma. I have been involved in several bands. My first band playing drums in High School, then playing rhythm guitar and singing. After coming to Cornwall I have been in a Blues/Rock band called Blooze as lead singer and guitarist. I am now more freelance and busk when i can but also put in plenty of practice at home singing and learning new songs. I have over 30 different songs which are my own work which one day I hope will give me a major hit. I have a very eclectic taste in music and my influences range from Frank Sinatra to Freddie Mercury. Matt Monroe to MUSE. I am a loving, caring man who dotes on his family and want to give them everything I can. I hope you enjoy my music and I look forward to entertaining you.
