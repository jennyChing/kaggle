     <p>
      Vermont's youth clearly have a better understanding of journalistic integrity than FOX "News."  (Note: in this article, this writer is following the White House's lead in a unique step toward truth, when it
      <a href="http://www.dailykos.com/story/2015/01/20/1359077/-White-House-gives-Fox-accurate-place-card?detail=email#" target="_blank" title="">
       dropped
      </a>
      the word "News" from the SOTU luncheon placecards for FOX, while other placecards included the full terms, "ABC News," "CBS News," and  "NBC News.")
      <br/>
     </p>
     The Quantum Leap Exhibit Program at Mount Anthony Union High School in Bennington, VT analyzed FOX  "News" practices in light of the published
     <a href="http://www.spj.org/ethicscode.asp" target="_blank" title="">
      Ethics of Journalism
     </a>
     per the Society of Professional Journalists.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     The students did their research, found that FOX failed at practicing journalism on every level, and offered a stunning response via video.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Dave Kilby (Liberals Unite)
     <a href="http://samuel-warde.com/2015/01/high-schoolers-destroy-fox-news/" target="_blank" title="">
      says
     </a>
     , "There used to be a time in this country when a propaganda organization wouldn't have been allowed. At the time, all broadcast media had to conform to the 'Fairness Doctrine.'
     <br/>
     <br/>
     "The doctrine 'required the holders of broadcast licenses to both present controversial issues of public importance and to do so in a manner that was, in the Commission's view, honest, equitable and balanced.' "
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Kilby notes that Reagan's F.C.C. removed this safety net in 1985.
