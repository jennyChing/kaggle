        <a href="http://meetmeatmikes.madefreshly.com" target="_blank">

        These courses are one of my very favourite things that I get to do. There is almost nothing I like better than helping people to find their creative community and make the most of their creative talents, bloggy or otherwise. Whether you want to learn to blog, blog better or focus on your creative life and reconnect to the most creative bits of you, I have some smart, useful, practicaladvice and instruction for you. Yep.
       </p>
       <p>
        To celebrate eCoursearama(and just to be exciting, basically), Im giving away an advance copy of 
        <a href="http://www.penguin.com.au/products/9780670076598/craft-soul-how-make-most-your-creative-life" target="_blank">

I will post to anywhere in the world meaning youwill get it WAY before it hits the stores. Cool, right?!
       </p>
       <p>
        Entries close on Friday 6th March at noon, Melbourne time.
        <br/>
        Winner contacted on Monday 9th March.
        <br/>
        I will then post the winner their book straight away.
