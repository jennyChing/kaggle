       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          BMW and Toyota Association: Beyond Belief Sports Car on the Way
         </b>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         There is nothing wrong if it is said that association of two people can do wonders. The reason is that two intelligent brains when work together can give rise to the things that have never seen before. If you are thinking that this is impossible to imagine in Automotive world, then you are wrong as two automotive connoisseurs have joined hands and planned to come up with a new sports car possessing the main features of both the brands. Where Toyota is prominent for its powerful and high performing SUVs, on the other hand,
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.xtralights.com/bmw-1.aspx" target="_blank">
           BMW HIDs
          </a>
         </strong>
         are recognized for its luxury and comfort. Just think of a car having power, performance, luxury and comfort altogether in a single machine. There is nothing better than this can happen, and no one can even think of it. It is simply amazing!!!!!
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <img alt="Supra" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-157" height="500" src="http://blog.xtralights.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Supra.jpg" width="720"/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Although the company announced this thing earlier this year, but they have not yet revealed much about this automobile. They aim at developing a mid-sized sports vehicle that can surprise all car fanatics with its ultimate driving machine. Experts believe that this kind of communally beneficial affiliation will undoubtedly going to produce something that can give birth to new range automobiles. The company owners believe that BMW is famous for developing sensational sports cars while the talent of Toyota is to make environment friendly fuel cell and hybrid automobiles. The outcome is going to be formidable which has never been observed before in the world.
         <span id="more-155">
         </span>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://blog.xtralights.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Toyota-BMW.jpg">
          <img alt="Toyota - BMW" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-158" height="500" src="http://blog.xtralights.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Toyota-BMW.jpg" width="720"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Many automobile professionals believe that this partnership might make a return of Supra, which is a sports car by Toyota. Engineers also believe that there will be a synergy effect that will result in something that has never been done before. Even they suppose that it could be something that will be beyond the expectations of the people and experts. There will be high performing engine equipped with four cylinders, and the platform is going to be stretched providing multiple good options to the driver. This mystery automobile is going to possess electric motors and body shape and size will be different from the vehicles that are already present in the market.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Sources are saying that Toyota is working on an all-wheel-drive GT86 operational with front in-wheel motors. They have plans to make use of this setup to introduce into the upcoming BMW-Toyota sports car. Atmosphere induction is likely to present due to pairing with electric motors. The car might make debut in 2013 Tokyo Motor Show which is going to happen in the end of this year. We are curiously waiting for this mystery automobile that is going to make history sooner or later. We are expecting this vehicle to be a show stopper in 2013 Tokyo Motor Show.
         <br/>
        </p>
