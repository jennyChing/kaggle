       <h1>
        Put away that phone!
       </h1>
       <div class="left">
        <p>
         We
         <span style="font-size: 14px;">
          constantly
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: 14px;">
          talk trough our phones. When were on the bus, at home or at school. Everywhere we go were busy with making contact and share the things we do online. While we do this we forget what happens around us. Instead of starting a conversation with someone across from us, we just sit there typing text messages. Parks and playgrounds are not as full as they where before, now the most kids entertain themselves with a computer or tablet. Just look how much baby toys there are where you have to put a smartphone or tablet in.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14px;">
          This video is a real wake-up call for the technology zombies weve become. With one clear message: put away that phone and enjoy the moment.
