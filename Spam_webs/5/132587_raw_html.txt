         <div class="panel-body panel-text">
          <p class="post-content post-content-justify" itemprop="articleBody">
           If you want to achieve this neutral makeup look, keep reading!
           <br/>
           Step 1: Prime your face with ELF Mineral Infused Face Primer. Let that soak in while we start with the eyes.
           <br/>
           Step 2: Prime your eye lids with Urban Decay Primer Potion with your ring finger. I used the Coastal Scents 88 Warm Palette. For my lid color (Go over 7, Down 5) use over whole lid. Then for my crease I used (Go over 2, Down 8) and make sort of a `V` shape on your outer eye. Then I blended it all together with this golden color (Go over 5, Down 4).
           <br/>
           Step 3: Line your upper lash line with Maybelline Master Precise in Black (it`s a pen liquid eye liner). I lined mine with a medium width line.
           <br/>
           Step 4: We will come back to mascara after were done! Now to the face. I used bareMinerals Ready Foundation in Medium (C25). I brushed it all over my face with a big face brush. After I used a blush brush and swept NYX Mocha (PB01) on my cheeks.
           <br/>
           Step 5: Lastly, I used Maybelline the rocket Volum` Express on my lashes, and to top it off just used chapstick on my lips!
           <br/>
           WAH-LA! The finished makeup look!
