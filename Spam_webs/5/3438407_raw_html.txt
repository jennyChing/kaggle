        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Stream the New King Gizzard &amp; the Lizard Wizard Album Quarters!
        </h1>
        <span class="cat-links">
         <a href="http://reverbparty.com/category/new-music/" rel="category tag">
          New Music
         </a>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         The new King Gizzard &amp; the Lizard Wizard album,
         <em>
          Quarters
         </em>
         ,is now available for streaming via
         <a href="http://kinggizzardandthelizardwizard.com/album/quarters" target="_blank">
          Bandcamp
         </a>
         . The album is coming only six months after their epic,
         <em>
          Im In Your Mind Fuzz
         </em>
         LP was released late last year. Weve already heard two amazing songs (
         <a href="http://reverbparty.com/float-down-the-river-and-trip-out-with-king-gizzard-the-lizard-wizard/" title="Float Down The River and Trip Out With King Gizzard &amp; The Lizard Wizard">
          The River
         </a>
          &amp; 
         <a href="http://reverbparty.com/king-gizzard-cant-stop-wont-stop-putting-out-awesome-psychedelic-music/" title="King Gizzard Cant Stop, Wont Stop, Putting Out Awesome Psychedelic Music">
          God is in the Rhythm
         </a>
         ) from the album, which consists of four 10 minute long tracks.
        </p>
        <p>
         Listen to the full album stream for
