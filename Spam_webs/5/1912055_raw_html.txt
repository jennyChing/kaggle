        <h2>
         Macaroni and Havarti Cheese Recipe
        </h2>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         2cups uncooked elbow macaroni
         <br/>
         4tablespoons butter
         <br/>
         2tablespoons all-purpose flour
         <br/>
         1teaspoon salt
         <br/>
         2cups milk
         <br/>
         2cups shredded
         <a href="http://www.arla.com/en-us/our-brands/brands/arla-dofino/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.arla.com/en-us/our-brands/brands/arla-dofino/', 'Arla Dofino Havarti cheese']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="havarti cheese">
          Arla Dofino Havarti cheese
         </a>
         (8 oz)
         <br/>
         1tablespoon chopped fresh dill weed (optional)
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.longwaitforisabella.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/arla-dofino-havarti-cheese.jpg">
          <img alt="arla dofino havarti cheese" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13879" height="400" src="http://www.longwaitforisabella.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/arla-dofino-havarti-cheese.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Directions:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Cook noodles according to package instructions. Set aside when done. Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan, add flour and whisk together. Add milk and salt, whisking together until boiling. Remove from heat and stir in cheese until melted. Add cooked noodles and mix all together. Serves four.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.longwaitforisabella.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/macaroni-and-havarti-cheese.jpg">
          <img alt="macaroni and havarti cheese" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13880" height="400" src="http://www.longwaitforisabella.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/macaroni-and-havarti-cheese.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         My husband ate his macaroni and Havarti cheese underneath his vegetarian chili and LOVED it. I decided I wanted some fresh dill on top of my serving and I am so glad I did. Dill tastes wonderful with this cheese. {In fact, you can purchase Havarti Dill cheese from Arla Dofino as well.} I love this softer cheese with a mild flavor. It is one of my favorite cheese. The fresh dill complemented it so well, I am going to buy the Havarti Dill
         <a href="http://www.arla.com/en-us/our-brands/brands/arla-dofino/store-locations/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.arla.com/en-us/our-brands/brands/arla-dofino/store-locations/', 'next time I am at the store']);" rel="nofollow nofollow" target="_blank" title="buy arla dofino cheese">
          next time I am at the store
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.longwaitforisabella.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/macaroni-and-havarti-cheese-recipe-2.jpg">
          <img alt="macaroni and havarti cheese recipe 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13881" height="400" src="http://www.longwaitforisabella.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/macaroni-and-havarti-cheese-recipe-2.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          About Arla Dofino
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Arla Dofino has been traditionally crafted in Wisconsin for 100 years. They offer five types of great cheese: Havarti, Gouda, Fontina, Edam and Muenster. All of their cheese are free of added hormones and are crafted in an environmentally friendly way. These cheeses make it easy for busy moms to give their children not only healthy snacks options but also fun and healthy recipe ideas.
