      <h1>
       Best Fitting Jeans for Women
      </h1>
      <p style="float: right;margin: 4px;">
       <script type="text/javascript">
        &lt;!--
google_ad_client = "ca-pub-2654518966293867";
/* Best Fitting Jeans */
google_ad_slot = "7687293858";

      </p>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       Remember one of those confusing days when you were in a rush and couldnt decide on what to wear? Well, this time has been experienced by almost all of us at least once in a lifetime, sometimes more frequently. In such a case the best option that we choose on is a pair of well fitting jeans. Jeans are the favorite attire of millions of women across the world and this is why it has become a staple garment for every womans wardrobe. But, there is no denying the fact that finding the perfectly fitting jeans for you is no less than a herculean task. With the wide range of jeans available in the market, we have got a huge assortment of styles and cuts and fits and patterns when it comes to selecting one pair of jeans. If you are on the lookout for
       <strong>
        best fitting jeans for women
       </strong>
       then you have come at just the right page. After all, no one can look better than a woman clad in a pair of sexily fitting jeans. Give this article a good read and you will get to know that what style of jeans fits best for different body types.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.bestfittingjeansforwomen.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Best-Fitting-Jeans-for-Women.jpg">
        <img alt="Best Fitting Jeans for Women" class="size-full wp-image-50 alignright" height="187" src="http://www.bestfittingjeansforwomen.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Best-Fitting-Jeans-for-Women.jpg" title="Best Fitting Jeans for Women" width="270"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Pear Shaped and the Hourglass figure
      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       The
       <strong>
        best fitting jeans for women
       </strong>
       with a pear shaped body is the one that features a waistband and a high back rise. The waistband will flatter your hips and will help you in sporting a more streamlined appearance. If you are blessed with curves or an hourglass figure then go for boot cuts or the denims that have flared legs. Dark colored denims will never fail to make you look gorgeous and attractive. Also washes that give the jeans a trouser-like look will be a good option.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Apple Shape
      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       The best fitting jeans for women who have an apple shaped figure are those that have a wider waistband. If you have a flabby tummy, then camouflage the bulge with these kinds of jeans that are on the heavier side. You can choose from a number of styles that include boot cut, straight cut, slightly flared, etc. Sport jeans with a yoke seam or flapped rear pockets.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Tall Women
      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       If you are pretty tall in height then finding the right pair of jeans can be troublesome but once you get the knack of it you will always end up being well turned out. Go for styles that feature a long inseam, one which is longer than 36. The best advantage of being tall is that you can easily strut smartly and beautifully in low waist as well as high waist pants. Skinny jeans are the best jeans for you. Also the straight cut and the wide leg style are pop picks for taller girls.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Curvaceous Figure
      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       The
       <em>
        best fitting jeans for women
       </em>
       who are on the heavier side and with a curvaceous figure are the mid waist or high waist jeans. In fact, dark denims suit the curvy woman the best. If you have a curvy body type then the best styles that you can opt for are the bootleg jeans as they are best suited for camouflaging heavy thighs.
      </p>
      <h2>
       The Petite Girl
      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       One of the most difficult body types is that of the petite girl and it really difficult to find the best fitting jeans for women with such a body shape. If you have a petite frame then the best jeans for you will be the one with a curved waist band.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Short Women
      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       Women who are short should opt for jeans that have narrow legs so that they can sport a sleek appearance. The narrow legged jeans call in for a taller look.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Shop Smart
      </h2>
      <p style="text-align: justify;">
       So, now you must have got a clear idea about the various styles of jeans that are meant for different body types. If you know your body type then you can shop smartly at your nearest retail store. Be bold to experiment enough. There are some of the leading jeans brands that sell some of the best jeans in the world. Also, you can make a purchase at any of the online shopping stores so that you can easily have your perfect pair jeans delivered right at your doorstep.
      </p>
