        <div class="post-text-area text-area">
         <p>
          I mean come on, a
          <em>
           burger
          </em>
          resting on
          <em>
           top
          </em>
          of your cocktail? Mind. Blown.
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.metbackbay.com/" target="_blank">
            MET Back Bay
           </a>
          </strong>
          calls it their Crafty Mary, and it should be on everyones must-try listin the
          <em>
           very
          </em>
          near future.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 650px">
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/118658172@N04/16430220771" title="FullSizeRender by BU Spoon University, on Flickr">
           <img alt="FullSizeRender" class="lazy" data-original="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7314/16430220771_2593d7b6ae_z.jpg" height="480" src="http://spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/themes/spoon/images/loading.svg" width="640"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Photo by Bonnie Tynes
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          You can choose your spirit, your rim and your preferred spice level, but the fun part is picking the fixins and garnishes. Err on the side of caution and go for the traditional ones like celery, pepperocini, olives and lime,
          <em>
           or
          </em>
          take a walk on the wild side and get a mini cheeseburger, a salami and cheese skewer or shrimp and lobster atop your cocktail. The possibilities are endless.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 650px">
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/118658172@N04/16430220921" title="IMG_9726 by BU Spoon University, on Flickr">
           <img alt="IMG_9726" class="lazy" data-original="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7436/16430220921_9c1258cd66_z.jpg" height="451" src="http://spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/themes/spoon/images/loading.svg" width="640"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Photo by Bonnie Tynes
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Now anout-of-this-world Bloody Mary bar is enough to sell me on a restaurant, but I do have to admitId rather eat my calories then drink them.
         </p>
         <p>
          With the perfect sized menu (not too short that you feel like the chef is making your diningdecision for you, butnot too long and overwhelming that you cant make a decision), MET Back Bay has great food for a reasonable price for
          <strong>
           <a href="http://metbackbay.com/menu/breakfast">
            breakfast
           </a>
          </strong>
          ,
          <a href="http://metbackbay.com/menu/lunch">
           <strong>
            lunch
           </strong>
          </a>
          ,
          <strong>
           <a href="http://metbackbay.com/menu/dinner">
            dinner
           </a>
          </strong>
          and
          <strong>
           <a href="http://metbackbay.com/menu/saturday-sunday-brunch">
            weekend brunch
           </a>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 650px">
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/118658172@N04/16406220096" title="IMG_8010 by BU Spoon University, on Flickr">
           <img alt="IMG_8010" class="lazy" data-original="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7302/16406220096_204a21f67f_z.jpg" height="480" src="http://spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/themes/spoon/images/loading.svg" width="640"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Photo by Ellen Bartolino
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          My first time there I orderedthe Green Guru Salad:kale, spinach, broccoli, haricots verts, carrots, spiced pumpkin seeds, avocado and salmon in a
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/ina-garten/bibb-salad-with-basil-green-goddess-dressing-recipe.html" target="_blank">
            green goddess dressing
           </a>
          </strong>
          . Amazing! And poking out in the background there is my momscider braised chicken, parmesan Brussels sprouts and sweet potato
          <strong>
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sptzle">
            sptzle
           </a>
          </strong>
          , which was quitetasty as well. (Like I-stole-a-couple-extra-bites-while-she-was-in-the-bathroom tasty.)
         </p>
         <p>
          My sister orderedtheMET rice bowl. Also to die for. Lets just say she was not a happy camper when she went to take her doggy bag to work for lunch and realized I had snagged it to bring back to my dorm room.
         </p>
         <blockquote class="instagram-media" data-instgrm-captioned="" data-instgrm-version="4" style="background: #FFF; border: 0; border-radius: 3px; box-shadow: 0 0 1px 0 rgba(0,0,0,0.5),0 1px 10px 0 rgba(0,0,0,0.15); margin: 1px; max-width: 658px; padding: 0; width: calc(100% - 2px);">
          <div style="padding: 8px;">
           <div style="background: #F8F8F8; line-height: 0; margin-top: 40px; padding: 50% 0; text-align: center; width: 100%;">

            <a href="https://instagram.com/p/vY2sQmH1XQ/" style="color: #000; font-family: Arial,sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: 17px; text-decoration: none; word-wrap: break-word;" target="_top">

           </p>
           <p style="color: #c9c8cd; font-family: Arial,sans-serif; font-size: 14px; line-height: 17px; margin-bottom: 0; margin-top: 8px; overflow: hidden; padding: 8px 0 7px; text-align: center; text-overflow: ellipsis; white-space: nowrap;">

         <p>
          Book early enough and you and your honey can score yourself a Valentines Day
          <strong>
           
           <a href="http://metbackbay.com/special-happenings/valentines-day-2015">
            Table for Two in Rome or Paris
           </a>
           
          </strong>
          without leaving Boston.
         </p>
