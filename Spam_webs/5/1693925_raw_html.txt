             <h2 class="post-title">
              <a href="http://www.flat15.com/2014/11/26/design-inspiration-exposed-wardrobes/">
               Design Inspiration: Exposed Wardrobes
              </a>
             </h2>
             <h6>
              Wednesday November 26, 2014
             </h6>
            </header>
            <!-- Post Content -->
            <div class="post_content">
             <figure class="featured-thumbnail thumbnail large">
              <div class="hider-page">
              </div>
              <img alt="Design Inspiration: Exposed Wardrobes" height="600" src="http://www.flat15.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Open-Closets-Cover--809x600.jpg" width="809"/>

             <!-- google_ad_section_start -->
             <p>
              I am normally trying to cover up my messy closet as much as possible (see it
              <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
               <a href="http://www.flat15.com/2014/11/13/easy-closet-upgrades/" target="_blank">
                here
               </a>
              </span>
              ), but recently I have been inspired by these beautiful rooms which present simple and stylised exposed wardrobes. I have to say that I kind of love this look, although I am on the fence about if this is practical or not? I have put together some of my very favorite rooms with this look and I feel that these exposed rails create a vignette looking area in a room which is both stylish, modern and kind of practical too. I think for me, this look would only work in my guest room with possibly hanging some of my favorite pieces or fancy jackets? ButI do love it. What are your thoughts about thiswould you try this in your home?
             </p>
