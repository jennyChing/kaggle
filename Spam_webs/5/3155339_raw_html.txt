          Lucky Star figures announced, money is already set aside
          <span>
          </span>
         </h1>
         <h6 style="padding:0px; margin:0 0 15px 0; font-size:14px !important;">
          <b>
           11:32 PM

          on 09.09.2007 //
          <a href="/?name=Brad Rice&amp;a=554&amp;start=0">

         <br/>
         <p>
          <a style="font-size: 22px; font-weight:bold; margin:10px 0 15px 0; text-align:left !important;">
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Finally, the Lucky Star figures have been announced
          <a href="http://www.lucky-ch.com/info/info_goods_sp.html" target="_blank">
           over on the Japanese site
          </a>
          . For 4000 yen, you can get either a Kagami or Konata figure, as pictured above. Kagami comes with a miniature Minoru Shiraishi figure, while Konata comes with an Akira-sama statue. Finally, Akira will be getting the attention she craves, being packaged along with the star.
         </p>
         <p>
          The figures are going to be sold in late January of 2008, and are being done by Good Smile Company as part of their Nendoroid line. I will cut you all to get one of these, so don't get in my way. Then again, if you manage to get one, feel free to send it to us here at Japanator so we can...review them. Yeah, that's the ticket.
         </p>

           Heisei Democracy
