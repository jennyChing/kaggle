       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Pimento Cheese Burgers: Low Carb Dinner
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-07-21T01:30:21+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          July 21, 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/author/blogging" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Shirley Wood
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/2014/07/pimento-cheese-burgers-low-carb-dinner.html#comments">
           26 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="pf-content">
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           If you like pimento cheese and you also like burgers, then you should
           <em>
            really
           </em>
           like these Pimento Cheese burgers! Not only are they yummy but they are also great for a low carb diet!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           My husband is the pimento cheese chef at our house. He is actually a talented individual in many ways. Be sure you check out
          </span>
          <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/2014/07/porch-makeover-project-solar-powered-outdoor-chandelier-makeover.html" target="_blank" title="Solar Porch Chandelier">
           this
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           creative
          </span>
          <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/2014/07/porch-makeover-project-solar-powered-outdoor-chandelier-makeover.html" target="_blank" title="Solar Porch Chandelier">
           Solar Porch Chandelier
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           he made for our front porch!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           We normally would grill our burgers outside but we were experiencing a really nice rainy day when we prepared this meal so I prepared them on top of the stove.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Spread-a-dallop-of-the-pimento-cheese-on-top-of-the-burgers-for-about-the-last-minute-of-cook-time.intelligentdomestications.com_.jpg">
           <img alt="Spread a dallop of the pimento cheese on top of the burgers for about the last minute of cook time.intelligentdomestications.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-2846 size-large" height="682" src="http://intelligentdomestications.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Spread-a-dallop-of-the-pimento-cheese-on-top-of-the-burgers-for-about-the-last-minute-of-cook-time.intelligentdomestications.com_-1024x682.jpg" width="1024"/>
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           When your burgers are about a minute away from being done, place a nice dollop of pimento cheese on top and allow it to just begin to melt. Be careful when taking your pimento cheese burgers up so you dont loose any of that yummy stuff!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Pimento-Cheese-Burgers.Low-Carb-when-served-with-no-bun.lowcarb-intelligentdomestications.com_.jpg">
           <img alt="Pimento Cheese Burgers.Low Carb when served with no bun.#lowcarb intelligentdomestications.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-2844 size-large" height="682" src="http://intelligentdomestications.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Pimento-Cheese-Burgers.Low-Carb-when-served-with-no-bun.lowcarb-intelligentdomestications.com_-1024x682.jpg" width="1024"/>
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           We enjoy eating our Pimento Cheese Burgers low carb style without the bread.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Stacy loves Southern Living recipes. Who doesnt? His go-to Pimento Cheese recipe (pictured on the burgers)is available on their site
          </span>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.southernliving.com/food/kitchen-assistant/pimiento-cheese-recipes" target="_blank" title="Basic Pimento Cheese Recipe">
            here.
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           He made the pimento cheese fresh as I was cooking the burgers. Teamwork is great in the kitchen!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           There are only a few ingredients involved in making good pimento cheese. Be sure to spring for the good cheese! Stacy refuses to buy bagged shredded cheese when making this recipe, he buys the blocks and shreds them himself. We live in the deep south where Dukes mayonnaise is heralded as sacred! We wouldnt even think about using any other brand!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Pimento-Cheese-Ingredients.intelligentdomestications.com_.jpg">
           <img alt="Pimento Cheese Ingredients.intelligentdomestications.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-2845 size-large" height="682" src="http://intelligentdomestications.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Pimento-Cheese-Ingredients.intelligentdomestications.com_-1024x682.jpg" width="1024"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           This delicious
          </span>
          <a href="http://www.southernliving.com/food/kitchen-assistant/pimiento-cheese-recipes" target="_blank">
           Basic Pimento Cheese Spread
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           will be even better the next day!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           If its burgers you love with fun toppings, you may like to check out my
           <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/2015/04/tomato-red-onion-jelly.html" target="_blank">
            Tomato &amp; Red Onion Jelly Burger!
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/2015/04/tomato-red-onion-jelly.html" target="_blank">
           <img alt="Who needs ketchup when you can top your burgers with Tomatoe &amp; Onion Jelly. Find the recipe at www.intelligentdomestications.com" class="aligncenter wp-image-6598 size-large" height="861" src="http://intelligentdomestications.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Who-needs-ketchup-when-you-can-top-your-burgers-with-Tomatoe-Onion-Jelly.-Find-the-recipe-at-www.intelligentdomestications.com_-1024x861.jpg" width="1024"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
          Join Stacy and I on our
          <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/hot-dog-tour" target="_blank">
           Hot Dog Tour
          </a>
          across the southeast. We love hot dogs even more than hamburgers, so we are sharing our journey of visiting
          <a href="http://intelligentdomestications.com/hot-dog-tour" target="_blank">
           hot dog joints
          </a>
          on our travels.
         </h4>
         <h3 style="text-align: center;">
          <span style="color: #ff6600;">
           Which one is your favorite, Hot Dogs or Hamburgers?
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
