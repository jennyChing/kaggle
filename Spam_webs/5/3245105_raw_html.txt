     <h3>
      About the Journal
     </h3>
     <p>
      <em>
       <a class="url" href="http://publicculture.org">
        Public Culture
       </a>
      </em>
      is a reviewed interdisciplinary journal of cultural studies, published three times a year in Fall, Winter, and Spring for the Institute for Public Knowledge by
      <a href="http://dukeupress.edu/">
       Duke University Press
      </a>
      . The journal's full archives are available online at
      <a href="http://publicculture.dukejournals.org/">
