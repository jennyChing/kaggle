       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Easy Microwave Caramel Popcorn
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-10-22T06:00:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 22, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/author/mriker/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Melissa
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/2014/10/easy-microwave-caramel-popcorn/#comments">
           2 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="easy microwave caramel corn recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8395" height="900" src="http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/easy-microwave-caramel-corn-recipe.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/2014/10/easy-microwave-caramel-popcorn/&amp;media=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/easy-microwave-caramel-corn-recipe.jpg&amp;description=Easy Microwave Caramel Popcorn')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         I admit it, Im not a lover of popcorn {gasp!} My husband and three sons put down popcorn like its their job and I never touch the stuff, that is, unless I splurge on some sweet caramel corn! This easy to make microwave recipe is not only fast but perfect for gifts or entertaining as well.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         1/2 cup popcorn kernels, air popped plain
         <br/>
         1 cup light brown sugar
         <br/>
         4 tbsp butter or margarine
         <br/>
         1/2 tsp baking soda
         <br/>
         1/4 cup light corn syrup
         <br/>
         1 tsp pure vanilla extract
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="air pop popcorn caramel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8400" height="800" src="http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/air-pop-popcorn-caramel.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/2014/10/easy-microwave-caramel-popcorn/&amp;media=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/air-pop-popcorn-caramel.jpg&amp;description=Easy Microwave Caramel Popcorn')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Air pop your popcorn or, if you dont have an air popper, pop bagged plain popcorn in the microwave. (Avoid highly buttered and salted versions.)
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="microwave caramel corn ingredients" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8399" height="800" src="http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/microwave-caramel-corn-ingredients.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/2014/10/easy-microwave-caramel-popcorn/&amp;media=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/microwave-caramel-corn-ingredients.jpg&amp;description=Easy Microwave Caramel Popcorn')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Combine sugar, butter, vanilla, baking soda and corn syrup in a large microwaveable bowl. Microwave for 3 minutes or until melted. Stir well.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="caramel corn microwave recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8398" height="677" src="http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/caramel-corn-microwave-recipe.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/2014/10/easy-microwave-caramel-popcorn/&amp;media=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/caramel-corn-microwave-recipe.jpg&amp;description=Easy Microwave Caramel Popcorn')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Separate popcorn into two large bowls-the larger the better for ease of stirring. Pour half of the caramel mixture over each bowl of popcorn and stir well.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="making caramel corn" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8397" height="800" src="http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/making-caramel-corn.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/2014/10/easy-microwave-caramel-popcorn/&amp;media=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/making-caramel-corn.jpg&amp;description=Easy Microwave Caramel Popcorn')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Alternate microwaving each bowl for one minute, sitrring thoroughly after each minute of microwaving to help evenly distribute the caramel over the popcorn. I like to stir one while the other bowl is morcrwaving and it usually only takes two times in the microwave to have the popcorn well coated.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="how to make caramel corn easy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8396" height="800" src="http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/how-to-make-caramel-corn-easy.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/2014/10/easy-microwave-caramel-popcorn/&amp;media=http://www.thehappierhomemaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/how-to-make-caramel-corn-easy.jpg&amp;description=Easy Microwave Caramel Popcorn')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Spread popcorn on wax paper or a dish lightly sprayed with nonstick spray until the candy is set (about 5 minutes.)
        </p>
        <p>
         If you want to make caramel popcorn balls simply form the popcorn into balls with your hands (spray with nonstick spray first!) before they are completely cool.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats it! Ten minutes to a sweet treat that most everyone will enjoy!
         <br/>
        </p>

            Total time
           </div>
           <div class="ERSTimeItem">
            <time datetime="PT8M" itemprop="totalTime">
             8 mins
            </time>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClearLeft">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          Did you know you can make caramel corn in the microwave? This fast and easy snack is the perfect dessert for entertaining and can even be bagged up for favors or gifts!
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSAuthor">
           Author:
           <span itemprop="author">
            The Happier Homemaker
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSCategory">
           Recipe type:
           <span itemprop="recipeCategory">
            Dessert
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSCuisine">
           Cuisine:
           <span itemprop="recipeCuisine">
            American
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSServes">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            6
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup popcorn kernels, air popped plain
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup light brown sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 tbsp butter or margarine
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp baking soda
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup light corn syrup
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tsp pure vanilla extract
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Air pop popcorn.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a large microwaveable bowl combine sugar, vanilla, butter, baking soda, and corn syrup.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Microwave for three minutes or until melted.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Divide popcorn into two large bowls and pour half of the caramel over the popcorn in each bowl.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Alternate microwaving popcorn one additional minute and stirring thoroughly until all of popcorn is coated-usually an additional two minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Spread caramel corn over wax paper or pan sprayed lightly with nonstick spray until candy is set.
           </li>
          </ol>
