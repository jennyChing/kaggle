     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.grotraffic.com/connecting-the-pulse-of-your-brand-within-the-hearts-and-minds-of-consumers/" rel="bookmark" title="Connecting The Pulse of Your Brand Within the Hearts and Minds of Consumers">
       Connecting The Pulse of Your Brand Within the Hearts and Minds of Consumers
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      June 20th, 2014
      <strong>
       @
       <a href="http://www.grotraffic.com/author/grotraffic/" rel="author" title="Posts by grotraffic">
        grotraffic
       </a>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <img alt="Guy Kawasaki Profile Picture." class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9184" height="323" src="http://www.grotraffic.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Guy-Kawasaki.png" width="575"/>
      <br/>
      <strong>
       <span style="color: #ff6600;">
        Seize the High Ground
       </span>
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <i>
       <strong>
        If you want to beat your competition, establish an uplifting brand, but dont try to establish a brand based on your silly desire to beat your competition. Guy Kawasaki
       </strong>
      </i>
     </p>
     <p>
      Below is a Slideshare presentation on branding via Guy Kawasaki, but first a few thoughts on the substance of branding
     </p>
     <p>
      Good business is fueled by its underlying purpose for being created, and staying true to the core values that inspired your vision in the first place. This needs to be at the core of the message that drives your brand.
     </p>
     <p>
      If you want your work to make a difference or change the world, it must be motivated by improving lives and the world in which we live.
     </p>
     <p>
      When a business is motivated by doing good or supporting worthy causes in the world, it opens up many more opportunities than if it was established out of greed alone.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       <span style="color: #ff6600;">
        The universe will always make room for good.
       </span>
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Good is established on people. Specifically, improving the lives of people  and its driven by a mindset that tomorrow will be a better day for all in the world.
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Thats what I love about the social web It makes the above statement a reality for inspired business owners who desire to make a difference, and its already happening all around us.
     </p>
     <p>
      On the other hand, greed will always lead to a corrupt heart and devious mind.
     </p>
     <p>
      The latest global financial shakeup in the world was fueled by an out control lust for money and power by those who believe they live above the law And some got away with murder!
     </p>
     <p>
      The old system was not set up for the purpose of preserving and securing your future, but rather it set up by bankers who could manipulate and profit the most from your hard-earned money  and they took huge risks and in many cases broke laws to enrich themselves and their largest shareholders at your expense.
     </p>
     <p>
      What global business needs more than anything right now are leaders who possess a heart and vision for others  coupled with great ideas on how to improve lives through the products and services they provide the marketplace.
     </p>
     <h2>
      <strong>
       <span style="color: #ff6600;">
        Its where the vision, hearts and minds of people come together as one entity to accomplish good things in the world.
       </span>
      </strong>
     </h2>
     <p>
      <strong>
       That sounds like more than just a business to me.
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      Make sure you bake substance into the foundation of your brand  its what consumers will connect with and remember you for.
     </p>
     <p>
      Below are some awesome thoughts on branding by Guy Kawasaki Be inspired!
     </p>
