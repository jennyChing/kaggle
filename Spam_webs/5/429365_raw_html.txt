  <div class="text">
   <p>
    It can be difficult setting a scene well enough that the players instinctively feel that they are within that setting, and not just sitting
around a table in a lounge room.
   </p>
   <p>
    A method that can help is to find photos or artwork of appropriate landscapes and scenery and show them to the players when setting the scene.
Visual aids are powerful tools.
   </p>
   <p>
    Although sometimes nothing short of an IMAX movie will serve to give the correct impression to some players.
   </p>
