class="blog-content-wrapper"&gt;&lt;h1 class="blog-title"&gt;&lt;a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/coconut-oil-trail-mix-cookies/"&gt;Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/h1&gt;&lt;div class="blog-info-wrapper"&gt;&lt;div class="blog-date"&gt;posted on : April 9, 2015&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div class="blog-tag"&gt;&lt;span class="blog-info-head"&gt;tag : &lt;/span&gt;&lt;a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/chocolate-chips/" rel="tag"&gt;Chocolate Chips&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/coconut/" rel="tag"&gt;Coconut&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/coconut-oil/" rel="tag"&gt;Coconut oil&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/mms/" rel="tag"&gt;M&amp;Ms&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/peanut-butter-chips/" rel="tag"&gt;Peanut Butter Chips&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/peanuts/" rel="tag"&gt;Peanuts&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/trail-mix/" rel="tag"&gt;Trail Mix&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div class="blog-author"&gt;&lt;span class="blog-info-head"&gt;by : &lt;/span&gt;&lt;a &lt;/span&gt;&lt;a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/coconut-oil-trail-mix-cookies/#comments"&gt;14&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div class="clear"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div class="blog-content"&gt;&lt;p&gt;These Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies are a thick delicious cookie that&#8217;s crispy on the outside, soft on the inside and packed with your favorite Trail Mix!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-15563" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmixcookies4lead-570x814.jpg" alt="Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies...a thick delicious cookie that is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside packed with your favorite Trail Mix!" width="570" height="814"&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/coconut-oil-trail-mix-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmixcookies4lead-570x814.jpg&amp;description=Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Trail Mix&#8230;it sounds so innocent, doesn&#8217;t it?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;span id="more-15557"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Like if you were to say to me what did you eat today and I said, &#8220;oh just some trail mix&#8221;, you&#8217;d be all, &#8220;That&#8217;s ALL? Wow, you can survive on just nuts and seeds! What a champ!&#8221;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You&#8217;d say that, right?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Well turns out, the trail mix in my brain is a dirty liar. It&#8217;s pretty much the same as eating a Snickers. And since the only real place I&#8217;m hiking is into the kitchen to grab more trail mix, I&#8217;m not necessarily, &#8220;burning off the calories&#8221;. They should really make anything called &#8220;trail mix&#8221; less delicious. It&#8217;s deceiving.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Anyhow, since trail mix is basically a bag of chocolate chips I thought I would give it a proper home.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;In a cookie.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-15560" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmixcookies-570x810.jpg" alt="Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies...a thick delicious cookie that is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside packed with your favorite Trail Mix!" width="570" height="810"&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/coconut-oil-trail-mix-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmixcookies-570x810.jpg&amp;description=Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;I mean, Target calls THIS trail mix&#8230;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-15559" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmix-570x757.jpg" alt="Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies...a thick delicious cookie that is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside packed with your favorite Trail Mix!" width="570" height="757"&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/coconut-oil-trail-mix-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmix-570x757.jpg&amp;description=Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Which is why Target and I are officially bests.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Oh yeah, let me also say that I made these beauties with coconut oil instead of butter. I didn&#8217;t do it for health reasons or whatever&#8230;I did it because it tastes so dang good.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;I like &lt;a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00CPZPYLS?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393177&amp;creativeASIN=B00CPZPYLS&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=cookiesandcup-20&amp;linkId=T5Z3SEQD5HMYMRHU&amp;qid=1428529244&amp;sr=8-1-spell&amp;keywords=carringotn+farms" target="_blank"&gt;Carrington Farms Unrefined Cold Pressed Coconut Oil&lt;/a&gt; best. No, they are not paying me to say this&#8230;I just love it because it tastes and smells like coconut. You can buy other coconut oil that is pretty much flavorless if you&#8217;re not looking for everything you make to taste coconutty&#8230; but for baking I love the mild coconut flavor it gives. That&#8217;s just my 2 cents.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Anyhow, since I used coconut oil, I went ahead and added in some sweetened flaked coconut too. If you don&#8217;t love coconut, you can absolutely sub in butter and absolutely leave out the coconut. I won&#8217;t be mad.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-15564" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmixdough-570x716.jpg" alt="Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies...a thick delicious cookie that is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside packed with your favorite Trail Mix!" width="570" height="716"&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/coconut-oil-trail-mix-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmixdough-570x716.jpg&amp;description=Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;They bake up and stay thick. The edges get slightly crispy, but the inside stays tender.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-15562" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmixcookies3-570x810.jpg" alt="Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies...a thick delicious cookie that is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside packed with your favorite Trail Mix!" width="570" height="810"&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/coconut-oil-trail-mix-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmixcookies3-570x810.jpg&amp;description=Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;They really are a lovely cookie.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
		&lt;span class="pibfi_pinterest "&gt;
		&lt;img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-15561" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmixcookies2-570x763.jpg" alt="Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies...a thick delicious cookie that is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside packed with your favorite Trail Mix!" width="570" height="763"&gt;
			&lt;span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/coconut-oil-trail-mix-cookies/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/trailmixcookies2-570x763.jpg&amp;description=Coconut Oil Trail Mix Cookies')"&gt;
			&lt;/span&gt;
		&lt;/span&gt;
	&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;And just so you know, you don&#8217;t have to use totally trashed out trail mix for these cookies. If you are a little mindful of what you&#8217;re eating, buy a variety without the candies or chips. They make a perfect little lunchbox snack!&lt;br /&gt;

&lt;/div&gt; &lt;div class="ERSIngredients"&gt; &lt;div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading"&gt;Ingredients&lt;/div&gt; &lt;ul&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;&frac34; cup coconut oil, in solid form&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;&frac34; cup light brown sugar&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;1 egg, plus 1 yolk&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;2 teaspoons vanilla&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;1 teaspoon baking soda&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;&frac12; teaspoon kosher salt&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients"&gt;1&frac12; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;In the bowl of your stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment mix the coconut oil and brown sugar together for 2 minutes on medium speed until smooth, scraping the sides as necessary. Add in the egg, yolk and vanilla and continue mixing until smooth.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;With the mixer still on medium add in the baking soda and salt and mix for 30 seconds longer.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;Turn the mixer to low and add in the flour, coconut and oats mixing until incorporated.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;Finally stir in the trail mix.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;Using a 2 tablespoon sized cookie scoop, place the dough onto the prepared baking sheet 2 inches apart.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;Bake for 9-10 minutes until the edges are lightly golden, making sure not to over-bake. The cookies might even seem a tiny bit under-baked, this is fine.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions"&gt;Transfer the cookies to a wire rack to cool.&lt;/li&gt; &lt;/ol&gt; &lt;div class="ERSClear"&gt;&lt;/div&gt; &lt;/div&gt; &lt;div class="ERSNotesDiv"&gt; &lt;div class="ERSNotesHeader"&gt;Notes&lt;/div&gt; &lt;div class="ERSNotes"&gt;Use any kind of trail mix you prefer.
