         <div id="description-text">
          These tiny cat eye earrings are really fun and different. They are made with an original image by Gina M. D'Amico. You will receive 2 pairs of earring in your order, one pair of studs are made with the cat eyes and one pair of black glitter studs. The glitter studs not a deep black.  You can see some of the glitter floating in the dome of the earrings so they may be seen as a very dark gray.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Super tiny, great for big or little ears! These tiny earrings can be deceiving in size in the pictures, at .25"  the stud is about the size of a pencil erasure. They are surgical steel, nickel and lead free, and come with clear rubber ear nuts. I use a professional grade clear acrylic, they are very strong and will not yellow
          <br/>
          <br/>
          If you have multiple holes, these can easily be worn together, apart or matched with a hoop or dangle.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          I wear a pair of these cat eye earrings all the time! They are very easy to mach with blacks, browns and blues.
          <br/>

          I take all the images and make all the items in my tiny Burlington, VT studio. You're earrings will come lovingly gift wrapped.
