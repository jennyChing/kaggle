             <p>
              The case of ace broadcaster, Kwasi Kyei Darkwah, popularly known as KKD, who has been accused of rape, has been adjourned to April 22, 2015.
             </p>
             <p>
              This followed a request by state prosecutors for a one month recess for the court to constitute a jury to hear the popular rape case.
             </p>
             <!-- adanku1 -->
             <div id="adleft">
              <div align="left">
               <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

            <!-- Item fulltext -->
            <div class="itemFullText">
             <p>
              A Kaneshie Magistrate Court on February 19, 2015, committed KKD to stand trial at the High Court over his alleged rape of a 19-year-old student in a hotel.
             </p>
             <p>
              The hearing was expected to
              <a href="/news/general-news/40222-kkd-returns-to-court-on-rape-case.html" target="_blank">
               commence Wednesday March 18
              </a>
              , 2015 at the Fast Track High Court presided over by Mr Justice Bright Mensah.
             </p>
             <p>
              But
              <strong>
               Graphiconlines Nana Konadu Agyeman
              </strong>
              who was in court reported that, when the case was called, the prosecution led by a State Attorney, Ms Malike Woanyo told the court that since it was the first time KKD was standing trial at the High Court, they [prosecution] would like to take a date for the hearing to take place after a jury had been constituted.
             </p>
             <p>
              This, she said the prosecution had considered because it was a trial on indictment.
             </p>
             <p>
              But the legal team of KKD led by Anthony Forson did not take kindly to the prosecutions request.
             </p>
             <p>
              He said she [Woanyo] could have communicated her intentions to him [Forson] early.
             </p>
             <p>
              I dont think I have been treated fairly by my colleague, he said.
             </p>
             <p>
              Mr Forson argued that as a matter of courtesy at the bar, if we cant start the hearing today, she should have whispered to me since we are ready to start.
             </p>
             <p>
              He expressed worry that the accused had been committed since last month and did not see why the state would come for a date for the case to commence.
             </p>
             <p>
              You dont come to court and tell court you want to take a date for hearing.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="KKD, his father and Okyeame Kwame interact at the court premises on Wednesday after proceedings" height="277" src="/images/images/kkd-in-court-wednesday6.png" title="kkd in court wednesday6" width="550"/>
             </p>
             <p>
