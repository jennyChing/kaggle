         <p>
          <a href="http://img.wonkette.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Captain-underpants-2.jpg" rel="prettyphoto[561652]">
           <img alt="Seems fitting for weekend of the Values Voters summit..." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-561653" height="728" src="http://img.wonkette.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Captain-underpants-2.jpg" title="Seems fitting for weekend of the Values Voters summit..." width="500"/>
          </a>
          Can you believe that Banned Books Week is almost over? But you dont have to fear  people are always trying to
          <a href="http://wonkette.com/558193/texas-ministers-will-protect-teens-from-demonic-reading-habit">
           ban something,
          </a>
          <a href="http://wonkette.com/542513/victoria-jackson-freaks-out-over-tiny-cartoony-genitals">
           somewhere,
          </a>
          so you can still enjoy a banned book at your leisure. Heres a shopping list from the American Library Associations list of the 10most banned or challenged books of 2013  and if you buy using the links here, a chunk o the purchase price goes to support Yr Wonkette!
         </p>
         <p>
          This year, the most frequently challenged book is, once again, the various adventures of
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0439417848/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0439417848&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wonkette0f-20&amp;linkId=PSVE6QMJDZQVWJUE" target="_blank">
           Captain Underpants,
          </a>
          Dav Pilkeys hilarious book series for elementary kids (and the 8-year-old booger joke aficionado in all of us). The Waistband Warrior is pretty much just pure silliness, but there are jokes about underpants and bodily functions; some parents also are all aflutter about the books deliberate spelling mistakes, which will promote bad grammar. Go listen to
          <a href="http://www.npr.org/2013/01/15/169326364/hold-on-to-your-tighty-whities-captain-underpants-is-back" target="_blank">
           Pilkeys interview with NPR
          </a>
          from a couple years back, while youre at it. The ALA lists these reasons for objections to poor Captain Underpants: Offensive language, unsuited for age group, violence.
         </p>
         <p>
          And here are a bunch of other banned books, with purchase links for all but one that you know damned well why were not linking (censorship!), along with the reasons they were challenged:
         </p>

            A Bad Boy Can Be Good for A Girl,

          <strong>
           #7:
          </strong>

            Looking for Alaska,

           #8:
          </strong>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1451696191/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=1451696191&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wonkette0f-20&amp;linkId=FROMS3XX3VIIWG6J" target="_blank">

          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0446600253/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0446600253&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wonkette0f-20&amp;linkId=2XJNITOAMCPZZVPW" target="_blank">
