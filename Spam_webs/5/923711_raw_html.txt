         <p>
          QR Codes stands for Quick Response codes. QR Codes are black square shaped dots having white back ground as shown in the below image.You would have seen them many places. Some of us know what it is, some not. Before going to know How To Create Or Generate QR Codes, we will see what exactly QR Code and what it does.
         </p>
         <h3>
          <strong>
           What is a QR Code
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <p>
          In our daily routine if we see some images we will identify what is it for. For example, each and every product has its own image or logo.Most of the times we forget the captions of a product. But never the Logos and Images.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_638" style="width: 590px">
          <img alt="QRCodes" class="size-full wp-image-638" height="580" src="http://www.alltechtricksntips.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/QRCodes.png" width="580"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           QRCodes
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          QR Codes are information in Images. These images can have information about a website,contact details etc. QR Code first used in Japan. But human beings cannot understand QR Codes. A smart phone can scan and give you information of that Image. In QR Codes data can be stored vertically Or horizontallyQR Codes can store up to 7089!. Barcode can store up to 30 numbers.
         </p>
         <h3>
          How To Create QR Codes Using Android Phone Or Online
         </h3>
         <p>
          If you do not have Android phones, do not worry. There are lot of websites which allow you to create QR codes online.
         </p>
         <p>
          We can create our QR Code using Androids
          <strong>
           Barcode Generator/Reader
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Please follow below steps to generate QR Codes
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 1
          </strong>
          : Go to
          <strong>
           Play Store
          </strong>
          and search for
          <strong>
           Barcode Generator/reader
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 2
          </strong>
          : Select the
          <strong>
           Barcode Generator/reader
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 3
          </strong>
          : Click on
          <strong>
           Install
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 4
          </strong>
          : Select what you want to embed in your newly generated barcode. ex: url if you want for a website
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 5
          </strong>
          : Click on
          <strong>
           Generate
          </strong>
          after you typed your site in the text box.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 6
          </strong>
          : Now click on Save the barcode that generated. Click on
          <strong>
           Share
          </strong>
          if you want send it to anyone.
         </p>
