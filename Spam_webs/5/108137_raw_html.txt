         <h2>
          The best scrubs of summer!
         </h2>
         <p>
          Whether youre self-tanning this summer, or just want smooth, soft, and healthy skinthese body exfoliation solutions are the keys to your success. Check out eight of the best and brightest scrubs we could find this season. Fresher, smoother, brighter skin is just a few minutes away with these!
         </p>
         <h3>
          <a href="http://simplesugars.myshopify.com/collections/body-scrubs/products/strawberry-body-scrub" target="_blank">
           Simple Sugars Strawberry Sugar Scrub
          </a>
         </h3>
         <p>
          I knew I had to purchase this product immediately when I saw the teenage founder and creator of
          <strong>
           Simple Sugars
          </strong>
          , Lani Lazzari on ABCs Shark Tank. This savvy teen scored funding from Mark Cuban, and she really knew her stuff! I purchased a few scents, but I fell in love with the
          <strong>
           Strawberry
          </strong>
          it smells like fresh strawberry jam, and the blend of sugar and natural custom-blended oils leaves my skin baby smooth and hydrated. This scrub contains a mild astringent quality due to the strawberries, and the berries are also a natural source of Vitamin C.
          <em>
           $11.95-$16.95,
           <a href="http://simplesugars.myshopify.com/collections/body-scrubs/products/strawberry-body-scrub" target="_blank">
            simplesugars.myshopify.com
           </a>
          </em>
         </p>
         <h3>
          <a href="http://www.lather.com/scripts/prodView.asp?idproduct=731&amp;idCategory=4#" target="_blank">
           LATHER Coconut Foaming Scrub
          </a>
         </h3>
         <p>
          Go coconuts for this deliciously scented body scrub that contains bamboo stem powder, fruit extracts, coconut shell, and cane sugar. When emulsified with water, it slightly foams and gives the perfect amount of friction thats both gentle to the skinbut sloughs off icky dead skin with a quickness. Everything about this body scrub is divine, right down to the tropical coconut and vanilla scent. Paraben and sulfate-free.
          <em>
           $19,
           <a href="http://www.lather.com/scripts/prodView.asp?idproduct=731&amp;idCategory=4#" target="_blank">
            lather.com
            <span id="more-23573">
            </span>
           </a>
          </em>
         </p>
         <h3>
          <a href="http://rstyle.me/n/bbbjgmx2w" target="_blank">
           bliss Super Minty Soap N Scrub
          </a>
         </h3>
         <p>
          There isnt a body scrub thats more refreshing than this one!
          <strong>
           Super Minty Soap N Scrub
          </strong>
          is an invigorating peppermint wash with jojoba beads in a rich paste texture. This leaves my skin feeling super-refreshed, tingly, and incredibly clean. This is the perfect body scrub to wake yourself up on tired mornings, or for cooling down after a workoutwhile exfoliating tough areas like elbows and knees.
          <em>
           $32,
           <a href="http://rstyle.me/n/bbbjgmx2w" target="_blank">
            sephora.com
           </a>
          </em>
         </p>
         <h3>
          <a href="http://rstyle.me/n/bbbk9mx2w" target="_blank">
           alba Botanica Hawaiian Body Polish
          </a>
         </h3>
         <p>
          This sugar cane-based body scrub is composed of natural and 94% certified organic ingredients. The natural enzymes from the sugar cane helps to break down your dullest, driest skin, while the friction and blend of natural oils leave the skin incredibly soft and hydrated. Hawaiian Body Polish contains kukui nut and macadamia nut, along with honey for nourishment.
          <em>
           $10.95,
           <a href="http://rstyle.me/n/bbbk9mx2w" target="_blank">
            drugstore.com
           </a>
          </em>
         </p>
         <h3>
          <a href="http://rstyle.me/~Dgtw" target="_blank">
           Body Drench Raspberry Gelee Body Scrub
          </a>
         </h3>
         <p>
          With walnut powder and raspberry seeds and extract, this intoxicating, sweet scrub smells like fresh raspberry jam. Unlike the other body scrubs that made the list, this product consistency is a little differenta gelee texture which is slightly cooling to the touch. I love that this has a flip-top lid, and I wish more brands would take a page from Body Drenchs book!
          <em>
           $7.49,
           <a href="http://rstyle.me/~Dgtw" target="_blank">
            sallybeauty.com
           </a>
          </em>
         </p>
