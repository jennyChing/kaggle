           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            <strong>
             My Soul Retrieval / Astral Travel/ Out of Body Experiences quest
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            I did my Shamanism in 2010, did many journeys for myself as well for my clients. Since I am a Reiki Grand Master, Past life Therapist and practice many other techniques, I integrate everything in to one package and just let intuition take over and flow with the flow. In essence I am on
            <strong>
             my vision quest
            </strong>
            as I had few memorable Deep meditative experiences during my 
            <strong>
             Self Reiki Healing Sessions in 2005 to 08.
            </strong>
            .Later I came to know that these
            <strong>
             Inner / mystic experiences
            </strong>
            are known by different terms ,like
            <strong>
             Astral travels / journeys/ In between Lives Regressions/Akashic records Readings
            </strong>
            etc etc. These days I am again coming back to Shamanism. I heard few Modern Shamans speak their experience and journey in to Shamanism. As I was doing one of the guided journey there was nothing coming to my mind, which usually happens to me when I am doing any guided meditation. My mind is blank and I have to force myself to imagine the guided visualization . So I kept going along .Then I heard one of the striking sentence ,
            <strong>
             I am your future you, I have come to take you with me
            </strong>
            which brought back the memory of my deep meditative experiences, which I had in 2006, I would like to narrate two such deep meditative experiences .
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            <strong>
             1st-vision
            </strong>
            - In my deep trance I remember my spirit Descending and merging with my body .Then I Ascend along with my soul . I was taken out through the nine planets and then made to land / descend on a place as if below a very huge bridge ,on the edge of the earth connecting to heaven .
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            <strong>
             2nd vision
            </strong>
            - I remember being in my mothers womb chanting this mantra, 
            <strong>
             Yes, I will remember 
            </strong>
            .
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            <strong>
             Excerpt of these two visions , as I experienced in 2005 is given a the end of this article .
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            Comprehending these experiences now that these were my
            <strong>
             future
            </strong>
            <strong>
             vision
            </strong>
            / In between life travels. Since
            <strong>
             supercounscious mind
            </strong>
            doesnt have time sequenced in to past present and future, It only has one time
            <strong>
             The Great Time
            </strong>
            , where everything is happening in the now. So these experiences has been revealed by my subconscious mind once in a while ,when ever I happened to be in
            <strong>
             Trance/ Deep Meditative state
            </strong>
            ,just as guiding messages from my
            <strong>
             Akashic Records
            </strong>
            . When In the future I will know the technique to retrieve my soul fragments ) in vision no two , I would come back as a soul to the same house where my that lifes fragmented part is left behind regretting and crying because it had forgotten its connection to its source and succumb to circumstances and had died accidently. This may be my individual destiny and karma. As in this life there came many events where I would have left my this body due to accidents ,shock ,sickness or just the feeling of not wanting to live any more .
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            The coincidence is that the house where I am living from past seven months is almost the same as it was in my vision ,where that boy (I ) lived. As I remember coming back as a soul, entering at the main door of the house, there is a name plate (though I did not read the name).I go to the last room which incidentally is my Meditation room now. I cry there standing in front of a picture of Monastery on the wall ,saying ,
            <strong>
              yes, I had said that but I could not remember
            </strong>
            . I had myself chosen this house, yet after shifting in to this house I started having again that feeling of not wanting to live more. As if there is nothing more, no interest left in life. Now I healed that fragmented part of myself , assured my support and capability to help
            <strong>
             IT
            </strong>
            and asked
            <strong>
             IT
            </strong>
            to join me so that we are once again
            <strong>
             one and whole
            </strong>
            . Now I feel once again more alive and enthusiastic. My spiritual quest still continues.
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            <strong>
             First vision
            </strong>
            -I have become a healer and am dealing with others for healing, so I am dealing with group karma. So I am reminded to heal all those whose fragmented pieces are left behind and are feeling hugely disconnected from their soul ,that they are born as mentally challenged / differently able . Apart from healing people for various causes generated in their past lives ,I had started working with such kids since then in 2007 in Asha School Kargil (Ladakh )as and when I went there .There after Asha School Hisar and now in Ferozepur Cantt . It cannot be a mere coincident that 2007 In Kargil I had regressed an individual ,and had recorded his session . He was somewhat mentally / physically challenged in his past life , though now he is normal and was hugely fascinated by modern medicine . He wanted to be a doctor/ medical Assistant, something to do in the hospital, which could not be possible but he still works in the hospital as a Religious Teacher. In his session was revealed the cause for his this interest , that due to his weak body and mind, he has been frequently coming in and out of the hospital in his past life till he dies in his mid teens .
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            <strong>
             Details of the Astral Travels
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            <strong>
             Second Vision  Yes, I Will Remember .In MY D
            </strong>
            eep meditation, I felt as if I am sitting in some tub type thing which is circulating fast. Looks like some meditative technique in some gurus ashram. While rotating my conscious went up very high as if into a whirlpool and merged in Unknown Zone.
            <strong>
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            Now I feel I am in a womb, there is grayish fluid, placenta cord, and some leaves type coming out from placenta. (Layers of placenta erupting out from cord looking like leaves)I remember chanting this mantra continuously, yes
            <strong>
             , I will remember
            </strong>
            . Han main yaad rakhunga . I know that I am a boy as I am saying this in Hindi  Rakhunga  And I know that I have to remember this always, this vision. Then I feel some pressure making me breathless and its dark there, I feel I am born now. After some time its light and as if I am being carried to some other place..
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            After few days I am few days old and I want to see how I look?
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            Now I am 8,10 months and again I want to see, how I look ,and I look at myself .I see a boy of 8, 9 months sitting somewhere .
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            Now I am four five years, I have one big brother almost looks same but he is more talkative and sharp. His teeth are protruding out from his lips. He is talking to some body.
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            I think, now that incident of swinging and circulating in the swing happened while playing and (I) the boy  must have died and merged back to All That . Now my conscious is coming back to that same house and I am looking at that house, which looks as if serving soldiers house. Things are decorated, name plate is hanging but I cant read it . I am going from one room to another. In the last room north facing wall, there is one picture of monastery type (which I have not seen so far any where in this life but that time I can recognize it to be some monastery) I am crying seeing this picture and saying, regretfully ,
            <strong>
             yes, I had said that I will remember, but I could not.
            </strong>
             Han maine kha tha, yaad rakhunga, par nahi rakh paya. Now this realization dawns on me that I know, why seeing some idol or Temple , I cry or why others also cry? We had to remember our connection to such things from the past , but we could not , so the soul is reminding us at that time. (today only I had got confirmed through Deepas session, the reason for my crying for the very first time by seeing Durgas idol few years back in kolkatta. In her session when I asked her to see the reason for crying , she said ,  you seem to have become very big as big as the
            <strong>
             idol
            </strong>
            itself and merged in Her.)
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            (May be my soul wanted me to experience it myself the reason for doing so. Yes ,I had to remember, my connection with Her but I could not, the tears were of that repentance) ,Moving forward in this Experience - I go to one office, where my 30- 32 years old Bua, (paternal aunt) is sitting on the chair. But seeing her action or behavior, I have a feeling that she is possessed by some evil spirit. (as if her nose resemble Anjana and I know that she is possessed by spirit) I screamed at her, what are doing here? Hearing this, bua gets up and stands hiding her face from me facing other side, showing me her back. Now I also became forceful and released that soul possessing her. (I am thinking did I have this knowledge and healing power that time also?)
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            Now I feel my elder brother is playing at one place and I happen to meet him there. now I can hear what he is saying, he says, she
            <strong>
             (aunt) is having some pact with uncle
            </strong>
            , (may be against our father (they both want to cheat father in business , capture everything and are hatching some ploy.
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            <strong>
             Lesson-
            </strong>
            1. Previous day I was contemplating on the cause for becoming emotional at some sight of God idol or hearing some Bhajan or prayer of God. This vision was to experience the reason for myself.
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            2 Child can feel things in the womb and is really in meditative mode as described in scriptures and folklore. Soul keeps coming and going and may observe actual birth. In between, The Higher Self comes and have a peep on the activity and overall development. When I had felt that I want to see how I look at this particular age, I had seen and observed for myself that while playing imaginary things children are actually interacting and communicating with
            <strong>
             Spirit Beings.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            3. Life is cut short by any means , accident or illness , if the circumstances are beyond reach for the being to proceed in the direction to full fill the souls plan. I had realized it there that I had to remember something , may be my connectedness to higher power and work in that direction but circumstances may not be conducive to proceed in the right direction.
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            4 crying and becoming nostalgic is also to remind us of our origin and real purpose in this life. Astral body goes backward in time to review life times spent past and can gather what went wrong
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            5. Some souls are possessing living individual and fulfilling their desires of revenge and hatred, making living person dance to their tune and creating discord in their and loved ones lives .
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            Review of Second Vision -
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            <strong>
             2nd June 2006 meditative experience .I was guided to heal wandering souls
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            <strong>
             Astral travel
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p style="margin: 0px;">
            After going deep in meditation, my hands started becoming heavy and big. I had ascended higher and become energy. I could see the planets hanging some thing like a rectangular painting with planets arranged in rows of three. My conscious is being taken from outside of the cosmos and these hanged planets. Then I was made to land below one very big bridge. There are many people standing there, who look as if painted with mud and their eyes are also closed because of that. Which I understood later that these are people who are not in physical body but are not able to reach higher dimension and are earth bound spirits. I was being shown all this with some purpose .I see two boys standing nearby , as I am looking towards their mud painted eyes, I was being prompted to call out his name,, saying his name ,  ,"Ramesh you are healed. As I say so, he opens his eyes and starts looking at me and he feels happy because he is healed. Seeing his reaction, I realize that I have to heal all these people here, that is why I am being shown all this. . But I am in very high speed and by the time I could think and heal some more people, I had already crossed one big long three four storied building. Then there was some empty green place on some hilly place. (This was also an answer for the phenomenon of wandering spirits. Some earth bound spirits out of ignorance ,confusion and fear or excessive material desires make them heavy and earth bound .They are stagnant or scattered and vibrating at a very slow rate making it difficult for higher Energies ( Guides) to collect and carry to their higher destination )
           </p>

      function leaderTabs(tName) {
        if(!$('#'+tName).hasClass('current')) {
            $('.times-tab li').removeClass('current');
            $('#'+tName).addClass('current');
            $('#timestab1-content, #timestab2-content, #timestab3-content').hide();
            $('#'+tName+'-content').show();
        }
    }

    function flipLeaderTab(clsName, tabId) {
        if(!$('#'+tabId).hasClass('weekly_monthly_actbtn')) {
            $('.'+clsName).removeClass('weekly_monthly_actbtn');
            $('#'+tabId).addClass('weekly_monthly_actbtn');
            $('.'+clsName+'-content').hide();
            $('.loader').css('display', 'block');
            setTimeout(function(){
                $('.loader').css('display', 'none');
                $('.'+tabId+'-content').show();
            },500);
        }
    }

    // Load this script once the document is ready
    $(document).ready(function () {
        leaderTabs('timestab2');
        // Get all the thumbnail
        var popdiv = $("div#badgepopup");
        $('div.badgesIMG, div.myprofile-badgeBLK').mouseenter(function(e) {
            $(this).css('z-index','15');
            var cntt = $(this).children("div.tooltip").html();
            popdiv.css({'display': "none"});
            cntt = '&lt;div style="height:10px"&gt; &lt;/div&gt;' + cntt;
            popdiv.html(cntt);
            x = $(this).offset().left + $(this).width()/2 - popdiv.width()/2;
            y = $(this).offset().top + $(this).height() -10;
            popdiv.css({'top': y,'left': x })
            .show();

        }).mouseleave(function() {
            popdiv.hide();
        });
        popdiv.mouseleave(function() {
           popdiv.hide();
        });
        popdiv.mouseenter(function() {
           popdiv.show();
        });

            allErrors[allErrors.length] = "Last Name cannot be empty";
        }else if(last_name.search(fullnameRegEx) == -1){
            allErrors[allErrors.length] = "Special Characters not allowed in last name";

            allErrors[allErrors.length] = "Email cannot be empty";

            allErrors[allErrors.length] = "Please enter a valid email";
