         <p>
          What differentiates contemporary home decor from classic home designs is the way we imagine and choose our furniture. There are many furniture items that have been created keeping in mind of the evolution of the home decor of the modern day. Accent chairs are the best example of such transformations in home decor industry. Before deciding on an accent chair, you need to understand its not just another chair acquired for utility. An accent chair, in fact, combines both style and comfort offering our home a whole new meaning. Hence, choosing the wrong accent chair can badly affect the mood and setting of our home. Here are some ideas to help you make the best choice.
          <span id="more-1102">
          </span>
         </p>
         <h2>
          The Ideal Size of Accent Chair
         </h2>
         <p>
          According to the thumb rule, the size of any furniture items, especially with regards to the size of your room, is critical to the feel they create. And, accent chair is no exception to this rule. The first thing you need to consider while buying an accent chair is its size  is it too high or deep for you to sit on it and feel comfortable? If youre tall, for example, dont go for an accent chair thats low to the ground because your legs will drag on the ground and it will feel absolutely awful sitting in the chair. Likewise, if youre short and choosing a chair thats too high, you will end up having a tough time getting on and off the chair every time. While the choice of an accent chair largely depends on your personal preference, taking the height factor into account can save you much regret later.
         </p>
         <p>

         </p>
         <h2>
          Choosing an Area for Accent Chair
         </h2>
         <p>
          Theres something you must keep in mind  accent chairs are not your regular chairs which can be used in your living room. They are contemporary piece of furniture specifically designed to address our needs in modern home dcor. Therefore, choosing the right area for it holds key to how it impacts the mood of your room. Smart home owners usually prefer to choose a place away from the living room or bedroom so they can relax away from the mundane feeling of the regular ambience of the living room or bedroom. You can also decide to add accessories such as an ottoman or a small side table that not only complements the accent chair but also adds to the comfort and convenience when you sit on it. The side table can be used to keep a small table lamp or books or just about anything you can have easy access to when youre lying on the accent chair relaxing.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.allworldfurniture.com/accent-chairs/329-arne-jacobsen-swan-chair.html">

         </p>
         <h2>
          Accent Chairs with Arms
         </h2>
         <p>
          Although most home owners would prefer choosing an accent chair with arms over an armless accent chair, the decision for either one mostly hinges on the space available for it. As a matter of fact, accent chairs with arms usually take more space as compared to their armless counterparts. While theres a general perception that accent chairs with arms appear more beautiful, its not always true especially when you dont have enough room to accommodate such a chair. It stands to reason that any accent chair will complement your space as long as it doesnt look overwhelming and overstuffed. In fact, there are many armless accent chairs available that are elegant enough despite being armless. Just make sure you keep the space in mind and not just the accent chair that looks appealing inside a store.
         </p>

         </p>
         <h2>
          Accent Chairs as Multipurpose Furniture Pieces
         </h2>
         <p>
          Apart from the design stand point, accent chairs are also different from other chairs in that they cost a lot more than do regular chairs. Therefore, it would be wise to consider buying an accent chair that fits the ambience just about every room at your home. Whether its the color, size, texture of your accent chair, consider investing in a chair that goes with all major rooms in your home. Think of it as multi-purpose furniture item because when it comes to accent chairs, its more about comfort than style.
