           <p>
            Shloer is the sparkling juice drink for any occasion, and it contains no preservatives, artificial colours, sweeteners or flavourings. Now almost 80 years young, Shloer is still based on the original (and very delicious) recipe that was created by Professor Jules Shloer of Switzerland!
