        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Thinking Big Is Okay
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-05-21T06:00:04+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          May 21, 2014
         </time>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div id="greet_block">
         <noscript>
          <div class="greet_block wpgb_cornered">
           <div class="greet_text">
            <div class="greet_image">
             <a href="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/feed/rss/" rel="nofollow">
              <img alt="WP Greet Box icon" src="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/wp-content/plugins/wp-greet-box/images/rss_icon.png"/>
             </a>
            </div>
            Hello there! If you are new here, you might want to
            <a href="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/feed/rss/" rel="nofollow">
             <strong>
              subscribe to the RSS feed
             </strong>
            </a>
            for updates on this topic.
            <div style="clear:both">
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
         </noscript>
        </div>
        <p>
         Welcome to Day 21 of our 31 Days of Embracing Your Divatude Challenge! Today I want you to remind yourself that thinking big is okay! Dont let your negative self-talk or that of others tell you any differently.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you are new to the challenge, you can catch up
         <a href="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/2014/05/embracing-your-divatude-challenge/" target="_blank" title="31 Days of Embracing Your Divatude">
          here
         </a>
         .
         <br/>
         Briefly, the month of May is dedicated to focusing on our Divatude  being driven, inspiring, victorious and called to action in our personal and professional lives. As women, different aspects of divatude impact our daily walk at all times. Our focus with this challenge is taking the time to reflect on our daily attitudes and actions and ensuring we are focusing on what will lift us and others around us up.
         <br/>
         <img alt="Embracing Your Divatude Challenge Recap" class="aligncenter wp-image-6526" height="600" src="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Recap-Day.jpg" width="600"/>
         <span id="more-6563">
         </span>
         Most of us never reach of level of accomplishment that we dream of, mainly because of small thinking.
         <br/>
         <b>
          <i>
           Thinking big requires thick skin.
          </i>
         </b>
         If your plans are significant, plenty of people will do and say little things that can be discouraging.
         <b>
          <i>
           The biggest achievers have to face this challenge all the time.
          </i>
         </b>
         Learn to ignore it or consider keeping your plans to yourself.
         <br/>
         <b>
          If you want to think big, try these tips:
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Consider what will happen if you dont think big.
         </b>
         Youll regret the things you didnt do far more than the things that didnt work out. So take a few minutes and imagine how youll feel if you never even try.
         <b>
          <i>
           Think about how youll feel 10 years from now.
          </i>
         </b>
         Sometimes pain is the greatest motivator. How much pain will you feel if you dont take action today?
         <b>
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Be brave enough to let your creativity shine.
         </b>
         Youre more creative than you realize. Just watch a child play by himself. You still have that ability to create with your mind. But if youre like most adults, youve learned to stifle or ignore it. It might be your greatest strength. Let it out.
         <b>
          <br/>
         </b>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Stretch beyond your comfort zone.
         </b>
         If youre not making yourself at least a little uncomfortable, youre not thinking big.
         <b>
          <i>
           If something seems comfortable to you, youre probably already doing it.
          </i>
         </b>
         If youre already doing it, its not going to take you to the next level.
        </p>
        <p>
         Learning to deal with discomfort is an important part of achieving anything new. If you have enough motivation, anything is possible. Learning to lower your discomfort through healthy means is a valuable skill.
         <br/>
         <b>
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="31 Days of Embracing Your Divatude: Thinking Big Is Okay" class="aligncenter wp-image-6565" height="533" src="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Thinking-Big-Is-Okay.jpg" width="723"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Its easier than it has ever been.
         </b>
         The Internet and other forms of mass communication have made it easier to go global than ever before. All thats required is success on a local level. That success can then be propagated to other places. Think about how difficult it wouldve been to spread your ideas to other countries 50 years ago.
         <br/>
         <b>
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Use your emotions as a guide.
         </b>
         <b>
          <i>
           Youll know youre on to something when youre overwhelmed with positive emotion.
          </i>
         </b>
         Keep thinking big until you come up with an idea that truly moves you. Then keep thinking and see if you can come up with something even better.
         <br/>
         <b>
         </b>
         <br/>
         If youre like most adults, youve become too logical and practical. But you have more capability to do the things that truly excite you than you realize. What do you really want to do? What excites you?
         <b>
          <br/>
         </b>
         <br/>
         <b>
          Spend time each day thinking big thoughts.
         </b>
         Make it a habit to spend a few minutes each day coming up with great ideas. If you already have your great idea, spend the time developing ways to make it a reality. Keep improving your plan.
         <br/>
         <b>
         </b>
         <br/>
         We underestimate ourselves. We worry about failure. We worry about being too successful.
         <b>
          <i>
           But the world needs your big ideas, and you must think big to realize your ultimate potential for your own sake, too.
          </i>
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         Todays challenge is to make thinking big a part of your daily routine. One possibility is to go to bed earlier and spend 30 minutes letting your imagination run wild before you sleep.
        </p>
