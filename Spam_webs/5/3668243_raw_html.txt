     <p>
      The
      <b>
       Black House
      </b>
      is a building that formerly stood at 6114 California St. in
      <a href="/wiki/San_Francisco" title="San Francisco">
       San Francisco
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">
       California
      </a>
      , in the
      <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">
       United States
      </a>
      . The house was used by
      <a href="/wiki/Anton_LaVey" title="Anton LaVey">
       Anton LaVey
      </a>
      as the headquarters of his
      <a href="/wiki/Church_of_Satan" title="Church of Satan">
       Church of Satan
      </a>
      from 1966 until his death in 1997. LaVey conducted Satanic
      <a href="/wiki/Seminar" title="Seminar">
       seminars
      </a>
      and
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rituals" title="Rituals">
       rituals
      </a>
      at the house; one of the most notorious such rituals was the
      <a href="/wiki/Satanism" title="Satanism">
       Satanic
      </a>
      <a href="/wiki/Baptism" title="Baptism">
       baptism
      </a>
      of his daughter
      <a href="/wiki/Zeena_Schreck" title="Zeena Schreck">
       Zeena Schreck
      </a>
      in 1967, punctuated by LaVey speaking the words "Hail
      <a href="/wiki/Zeena_Schreck" title="Zeena Schreck">
       Zeena
      </a>
      !
      <a href="/wiki/Hail_Satan" title="Hail Satan">
       Hail Satan
      </a>
      !"
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        1
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        2
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      over the nude body of a female acting as the 'Satanic Altar'.
     </p>
     <p>
      Public ceremonies were performed at the house until 1972.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
       <a href="#cite_note-3">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        3
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      LaVey lost ownership of the house in 1991 as the result of a court settlement resulting from his separation from
      <a href="/wiki/Diane_Hegarty" title="Diane Hegarty">
       Diane Hegarty
      </a>
      , but LaVey was allowed to reside at the Black House until his death.
     </p>
     <p>
      Following LaVey's death, members of the
      <a href="/wiki/Church_of_Satan" title="Church of Satan">
       Church of Satan
      </a>
      unsuccessfully attempted to raise funds to repurchase the house, and it was demolished on October 17, 2001. A duplex now stands in its place.
     </p>
     <p>
      Though the building is sometimes referred to as a mansion, photographs of the building taken just before its destruction show that it was a moderately sized single family home, considerably smaller than the two small apartment buildings on either side of the property. According to public records, it was listed at 2,205 square feet (204.9m
      <sup>
       2
      </sup>
      ) and constructed in the year 1905.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">
       <a href="#cite_note-4">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        4
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
