       <div class="post-content">
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://growitgirl.com/">
          <img alt="Mia Mariu Review &amp; Giveaway" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1634" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/growitgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Mia-Mariu-Review.png?resize=500%2C375"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: right;">
         <a href="http://growitgirl.com/disclosure-policies/" target="_blank" title="Disclosure/Policies">
          <em>
           *Press Sample
          </em>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Do you smell as good as you look? There is nothing better than a beautiful woman that smells as exquisite as she appears. Have you had a gentleman do a double take and stop you to tell you how beautiful you are and that you smell wonderful? Oh, I have! The look on his face is always priceless.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span id="more-1633">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Mia Mariu has a new fragrance line and I was given the opportunity to
         <a href="http://www.tomoson.com/?code=TOP2a6520d8ec8a410e5380e27c8f72cc26" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.tomoson.com/?code=TOP2a6520d8ec8a410e5380e27c8f72cc26', 'test']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          test
         </a>
         a fragrance of my choice. I couldnt resist! I am a bigger junkie for perfume than I am for make up or hair products. Yes, its true! I may leave home without make up or with my hair in a ponytail, but I NEVER leave home without a spritz or splash of some wonderful fragrance. Its my finishing touch to getting dress for the day.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Ive been like this since junior high. I always say Im scent sensitive. I adore perfumes and colognes. I recently told someone, The right cologne will make a less attractive man handsome. Enough about me and my fragrance addiction. Lets get down to it!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="color: #444444; line-height: 1.7;">
          After reading the description of each fragrance, I chose
         </span>
         Magia
         <span style="color: #444444; line-height: 1.7;">
          . According to the description it contained fragrance notes I knew I loved.
         </span>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p style="text-align: left;">
          With a medley of juicy strawberry, mandarin, peach and pear that play sweetly atop dewy blooms of jasmine, plum, magnolia and flirty frangipaninotes of rich vanilla, musk, tonka bean and warm and enchanting golden amber
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Mia Mariu Review &amp; Giveaway" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1636" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/growitgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/DSCI1300-e1390539273335.jpg?resize=94%2C300"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Tell me that does not intrigue you! Well, I received my bottle of Magia fairly quickly. It is a full size, 1.7oz bottle. On the site, it retails for $42. The price did make me wonder about quality, sillage  projection and longevity.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: left;">
         My Take on Mia Marius Magia!
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I opened the package immediately when I received it in the mail. Yes, I like to smell pretty even when Im relaxing at home. Dont judge me. Anywho, I opened the box to find this hot pink box that just spoke to me. I just knew it was going to be a fragrance that I couldnt resist.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="Mia Mariu Review &amp; Giveaway" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1635" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/growitgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/DSCI1292-e1390539200807.jpg?resize=111%2C300"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         It didnt disappoint at all. It was love at first spray! Yes, seriouslyI squealed! I was beyond excited and couldnt wait to give it a real test drive. Ive worn it regularly over the last couple of weeks and its still a winner for me. Ive received compliments and double sniffs from a few friends.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I will say I wish it was a little less in price but in the end, it is worth the price. The smell does project well and the longevity of the fragrance is great. I did notice that it was still on some of my clothing on wash day. For me, that is a plus.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: left;">

         <img alt="grow it girl siggy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-140" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/growitgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/GG-Siggy1.jpg?resize=328%2C107"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Have you tried any of Mia Mariu products? What is your favorite?
