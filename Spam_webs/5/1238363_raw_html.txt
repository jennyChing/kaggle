            <h1 class="blog-title">
             <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/chicken-stuffing-tenders/">
              Chicken and Stuffing Tenders
             </a>
            </h1>
            <div class="blog-info-wrapper">
             <div class="blog-date">
              posted on : September 2, 2014
             </div>
             <div class="blog-tag">
              <span class="blog-info-head">
               tag :
              </span>
              <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/chicken/" rel="tag">
               Chicken
              </a>
              ,
              <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/tag/stuffing/" rel="tag">
               Stuffing
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="blog-author">
              <span class="blog-info-head">
               by :
              </span>
              <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/author/shelly/" rel="author" title="Posts by Shelly">
               Shelly
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="blog-comment">
              <span class="blog-info-head">
               comment :
              </span>
              <a href="http://cookiesandcups.com/chicken-stuffing-tenders/#comments">
               19
              </a>
             </div>
             <div class="clear">
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="blog-content">
             <p>
              These Chicken and Stuffing Tenders are a fun spin on a chicken tender! The chicken is coated in stuffing mix, so you can have your two favorites in one place!
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Chicken and Stuffing Tenders...chicken tenders coated in Stuffing crumbs and lightly fried to perfection! Two favorites in one bite!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13467" height="750" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/chickenstuffingtenders.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/chicken-stuffing-tenders/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/chickenstuffingtenders.jpg&amp;description=Chicken and Stuffing Tenders')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              So school starts this week for us. I know lots of you guys have been back in the routine for a few weeks already
             </p>
             <p>
              I kind of envy you.
             </p>
             <p>
              The first few days back are the worst. I know it sounds sappy, but I do miss my kids when they go off on that first day.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span id="more-13464">
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              All the thoughts of another summer in the books makes me want to freeze time. Bah.
             </p>
             <p>
              BUT I will tell you that I am happy summer is behind us because lawdy mercy I am flat broke.
             </p>
             <p>
              Keeping kids all busy and stuff requires doing things and going places. And I know there is a whole free stay-cation world out there at my fingertips, but I am lazy and feel oppressed by Pinterest moms.
             </p>
             <p>
              Which causes me to rebel and throw money at the issue.
             </p>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              And easy, Im not talking millions people, but heck, fro-yo aint free.
             </p>
             <p>
              Anyhow, my heart is sad, my wallet is happy.
             </p>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              Which brings me to this recipe. School starting means busy nights, and packed afternoons. So easy meals arent an option in my housethey are a necessity!
             </p>
             <p>
              And these quick and fun Chicken and Stuffing Tenders do the trick.
             </p>
             <p>
              Theyre chicken breasts coated in stuffing crumbs..and you can pan fry them or bake themup to you!
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Chicken and Stuffing Tenders...chicken tenders coated in Stuffing crumbs and lightly fried to perfection! Two favorites in one bite!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13466" height="814" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3769.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/chicken-stuffing-tenders/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3769.jpg&amp;description=Chicken and Stuffing Tenders')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              I grabbed a tub of Stove Top. My kids are obsessed with it. I know I should be ashamed.
             </p>
             <p>
              Anyhow, I pulsed the dry stuffing in the blender until the were fine crumbs
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="IMG_3749" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13469" height="809" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3749.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/chicken-stuffing-tenders/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3749.jpg&amp;description=Chicken and Stuffing Tenders')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              See?
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="IMG_3751" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13470" height="773" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3751.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/chicken-stuffing-tenders/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3751.jpg&amp;description=Chicken and Stuffing Tenders')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Also, FYI, I doubled the recipethats why there is a mountain of chicken. My kids love chicken tenders leftover, so I like to make a bunch and have them ready the next day for lunch!
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Breading the chicken tenders" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13471" height="788" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3752.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/chicken-stuffing-tenders/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3752.jpg&amp;description=Chicken and Stuffing Tenders')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Again, I pan fried mine.., I like how perfectly crispy they get this way, but you can EASILY bake these guys.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Frying the chicken tenders" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13472" height="763" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3759.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/chicken-stuffing-tenders/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3759.jpg&amp;description=Chicken and Stuffing Tenders')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Serve them with a side of Mac and Cheese and some peas and youre good to go!
             </p>
             <p>
              <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
               <img alt="Chicken and Stuffing Tenders" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13468" height="762" src="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3763.jpg" width="570"/>
               <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cookiesandcups.com/chicken-stuffing-tenders/&amp;media=http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_3763.jpg&amp;description=Chicken and Stuffing Tenders')">
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Theyre crunchy, seasoned perfectly and will for sure be a hit in your house!
             </p>
             <p>
              <br/>
             </p>
             <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
              <link href="http://cookiesandcups.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/chickenstuffingtenders.jpg" itemprop="image"/>
              <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
               Chicken and Stuffing Tenders
              </div>
              <div class="ERSClear">
              </div>
              <div class="ERSTopRight">
               <div class="ERSSavePrint">
                <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
                 <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://cookiesandcups.com/easyrecipe-print/13464-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  Print
                 </a>
                </span>
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="divERSHeadItems">
              </div>
              <div class="ERSIngredients">
               <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
                Ingredients
               </div>
               <ul>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 1 pounds boneless chicken breasts, cut into strips
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 2 eggs
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 6 oz seasoned stuffing mix
                </li>
                <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
                 oil for frying
                </li>
               </ul>
               <div class="ERSClear">
               </div>
              </div>
              <div class="ERSInstructions">
               <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
                Instructions
               </div>
               <ol>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Trim fat from chicken breasts and slice into strips.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Beat 2 eggs in a shallow bowl, set aside.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 In blender or food processor pulse stuffing mix until it's a fine crumb and transfer crumbs to a shallow bowl or plate.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Coat each chicken strip in the egg and then with the stuffing crumbs. Repeat until all chicken is coated.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Fill skillet with oil about  inch deep. Heat oil over medium until it begins to ripple or until bubbles form immediately on the back of a wooden spoon inserted into the oil.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Fry for 4 minutes on each side.
                </li>
                <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                 Alternately if you would prefer to bake the chicken, bake at 425 on a foil lined baking sheet, sprayed with cooking spray for 15-20 minutes.
                </li>
               </ol>
