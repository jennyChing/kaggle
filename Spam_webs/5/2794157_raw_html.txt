                 <h1 class="title">
                  Wing Chun Lineage Tree Wall Art
                 </h1>
                 <div class="content-content" id="content-content">
                  <div class="node odd full-node node-type-page" id="node-26535">
                   <div class="inner">
                    <div class="content clearfix">
                     <p>
                      <a href="/lineage-tree-wall-art">
                       Back to lineage tree wall art
                      </a>
                     </p>
                     <div style="background-color:#f9f9f9;padding:10px;margin:auto;margin-bottom:20px;width:900px;">
                      <table>
                       <tr>
                        <td width="600px">
                         This beautiful wall hanging depicts the Ip Man lineage of Wing Chun as it is commonly known. The legend of the Abbess Ng Mui and the young Yim Wing Chun are depicted here as well as the usage of Wing Chun within the Red Boat Opera Troupe.
                         <br/>
                         Each person who helped carry on this art to the next generation has a brief description of who they were and what they accomplished.
                         <br/>
                         Along the left hand side of the image reads, in Chinese "Each generation will reap what the former generation has sown."
                        </td>
                        <td>
                         <center>
                          Size: 12"x36"
                          <br/>
                          Available in a variety of frames.
