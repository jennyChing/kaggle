        <p>
         <em>
          Destiny: House of Wolves
         </em>
         second expansion is a month away and there are a lot of nifty details that have come out.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img class="alignnone" height="253" src=" http://assets.vg247.com/current//2015/04/destiny_house_of_wolves_armor_titan_ursustacticalarms.jpg" width="450"/>
         <br/>
         <em>
          Image source: vg247.com
         </em>
         <br/>
         <span id="more-6845">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         1.	Legendary armor and weapons can now be upgraded to max level with Etheric Light. Exotics can be upgraded separately without resetting progress.
        </p>
        <p>
         2.	New weapons can be reforged with new perks at Gunsmith. This feature was previously only available in Iron Banner, making the Gunsmith useful again for post-Level 20 players.
        </p>
        <p>
         3.	Speaker has an all purpose material exchange including Ascendant and Radiant materials, Glimmer, and Motes of Light
        </p>
        <p>
         4.	New social space: Vestian Outpost. New NPCs to be introduced include:
        </p>
        <li>
         a.	Petra Venj  Main story agent.
        </li>
        <li>
         b.	Variks  Warden of the Prison of Elders
        </li>
        <li>
         c.	Brother Vance  Trials of Osiris Handler
        </li>
        <p>
         5.	Tons of new ships, weapons, and armor, which should introduce greater player variety. Max level cap raised to at least 34.
        </p>
        <p>
         Remember the Bounty Tracker Bot in the Tower? It will also appear on The Reef so that the players can also have the same Vanguard and Crucible bounties, and this will save time to travel from The Tower to The Reef.
        </p>
        <p>
         There are also a lot of reasons why players need to visit The Tower: there will be a lot of new gears and weapons to purchase from The Tower itself. The Reef also has a postmaster and a vault for storing valuable gear that you dont need to use.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Speaker, who is located in the Tower North, will offer material exchanges in the game. Each exchange is a one-for-one deal, so you can hand in a Radiant Shard to get one Radiant Energy, with the Speaker charging a fee of 50 Glimmer for the trade. If you are short of Ascendant or Radiant Shard and you have a lot of Glimmers, you can trade 250 Glimmers to help you grind less on story missions or public events that that offer Ascendant and Radiant Shards. Eris Morn also can exchange Radian Materials if youre Rank 4 or higher. It isnt cheap with Eris Morn, and will cost you 500 Glimmer and one Black Wax Idol.
        </p>
        <p>
         These are the details that you need to know. There will be more weekly livestreams for
         <em>
          House of Wolves
         </em>
         until the expansion comes out on May 19. The next scheduled livestreams will take place on April 29, which will feature Trials of Osiris, and on May 6, featuring The Prison of Elders.
        </p>
        <p>
         Stay tuned for more
         <em>
          Destiny: House of Wolves
         </em>
         updates.
        </p>
