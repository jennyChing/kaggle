      <h2 class="page-title">
       Welcome to StapleGunReviews.com
      </h2>
      <div class="entry clearfix">
       <p>
        <img alt="Stanley TR45K Light Duty Staple Gun" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-72" src="http://staplegunreviews.com/file/2015/01/Stanley-TR45K-Light-Duty-Staple-Gun.jpg" width="350"/>
        <strong>
         Hey There!Welcome to StapleGunReviews.com
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        My name is Austin and I created this site to share everything you need to know about the current staple guns on the market. Whether youre interested in a brand new staple gun, an upgrade from your old beat up one or are simply looking for a few reviews and tips, were here to help! You will find plenty of staple gun reviews for every kind of staple gun ranging from manual, to electric, to pneumatic and more.
       </p>
       <p>
        Choosing a staple gun for whatever project or use you need it for is not that easy. There are hundreds of them out on the market, and they all have their own specific traits and uses. Staple guns come in all different sizes, weights, power types, staple types, quality, durability and more. Without a way to distinguish between them all, you end up lost and confused. Thats where we come in. We provide you with reviews and comparison tables on the best of the best staple guns for every use.
       </p>
       <p>
        When choosing a staple gun, the first place to start would be to determine what you need it for. There are generally three different types of staple guns distinguished by the power source used to operate the gun: manual, electric (From a cord or battery) and pneumatic (Compressed air).
       </p>
       <h3>
        Manual Staple Guns
       </h3>
       <p>
        If you are just doing some light stapling and are looking for a cheap but good quality staple gun, then I would suggest a manual one. Manual staple guns are good for easy tasks around the house like hanging up christmas lights.
       </p>
       <p>
        See our list of the best manual staple guns for 2015:
       </p>
       <p>
        <i class="fa fa-arrow-right">
        </i>
        <a href="http://staplegunreviews.com/best-manual-staple-guns/" title="The Best Manual Staple Guns of 2015">
         The Best Manual Staple Guns of 2015
        </a>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Electric Staple Guns
       </h3>
       <p>
        Electric staple guns are similar to those of manual ones, but they are different in one way. They are electric! That means easy power! Electric staple guns are easy to shoot nails from, and require a lot less strength to do so. They tend to be used for similar uses as manual staple guns.
       </p>
       <p>
        See our list of the best electric staple guns for 2015:
       </p>
       <p>
        <i class="fa fa-arrow-right">
        </i>
        <a href="http://staplegunreviews.com/best-electric-staple-guns/" title="The Best Electric Staple Guns of 2015">
         The Best Electric Staple Guns of 2015
        </a>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Pneumatic Staple Guns
       </h3>
       <p>
        Pneumatic staple guns are great for heavy duty projects or construction jobs. The word pneumatic means that they are air-powered, or powered with an air compressor. These staple guns are powerful and can pretty much send a staple through any reasonable material (Probably not solid rock or steel though).
       </p>
       <p>
        See our list of the best pneumatic staple guns for 2015:
       </p>
       <p>
        <i class="fa fa-arrow-right">
        </i>
        <a href="http://staplegunreviews.com/best-pneumatic-staple-guns/" title="The Best Pneumatic Staple Guns of 2015">
         The Best Pneumatic Staple Guns of 2015
        </a>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Upholstery Staple Guns
       </h3>
       <p>
        Fixing upholstery or reupholstering a piece of furniture is one of the most common things a simple staple gun is used for. Manual or battery powered staple guns make the best upholstery staple guns because they are easy to carry around, and the cord doesnt get in the way.
       </p>
       <p>
        See our list of the best upholstery staple guns for 2015:
