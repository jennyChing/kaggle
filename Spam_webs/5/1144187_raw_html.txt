         <p>
          Here I will countdown top 3

           bananas

          and top 3

           apples
          </a>
          plus fruit pictures, I found about them. I think those are one of the most popular fruits in the world(and orange I guess).
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Top 3
           <a href="http://fruittoplist.com/top-3-bananas-and-top-3-apples/" title="">
            Bananas
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           3. Pisang Raja
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <script src="http://www.everystockphoto.com/widget.php?imageId=21433103&amp;size=large" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
          This is hybrid banana, that is also called Latundan bananas, this type of banana is very popular in Indonesia and Philippines. This types of bananas are used for dessert. This tree of this fruit is not very tall, it no more than 14 feet. The fruits are yellow with thin skin, which splits very easy when they are fully ripe.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           2. Cooking bananas
          </strong>
          <script src="http://www.everystockphoto.com/widget.php?imageId=8059103&amp;size=large" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
         </p>
         <p>
          This type of bananas are used as vegetables, they can be fried, boiled, soups and all kinds of cooking. Also called cooking plantain. This is the only banana that is intended to be eaten after cooking. This type of banana is larger than the Cavendish bananas. And also is not so sweet as the cavendish bananas. And the main reason for that is because this type of banana is used mostly for cooking. When mature yellow banana is fried, it caramelizes, turning golden brown color. The tenth most used food in the world. And in some parts in the world people tend to use this bananas instead potatoes.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           1. Cavendish Bananas
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <script src="http://www.everystockphoto.com/widget.php?imageId=6678800&amp;size=large" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
          This type of bananas are 47% of the global banana production, and are the vast majority of majority of bananas entering the international trade. This type of bananas are eaten raw, rarely baked, but mostly it is used as a dessert, fruit compotes and fruit salads. When the banana is sold to the market it is green and after time it gets ripe. For that reason bananas can be transported to very long distances, and along the way they get ripe and ready for the market.
         </p>

         <p>
          <strong>
           Top 3
           <a href="http://fruittoplist.com/top-3-bananas-and-top-3-apples/" title="">
            Apples
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           3. Honeycrisp apples
          </strong>
          <script src="http://www.everystockphoto.com/widget.php?imageId=5800833&amp;size=large" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
         </p>
         <p>
          This type of apple is with a red color and the flesh of the fruit is from white to light yellow. It is fruit developed in the University of Minnesota. The shelf life of this fruit is really long, it is no more than 3 months. It is best eaten raw, because it is very juicy.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           2. Gala apples
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <script src="http://www.everystockphoto.com/widget.php?imageId=239607&amp;size=large" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
          The gala apple has mild and sweet flavor, and it was ranked number 2 in 2006 in US apple associations list of most popular apples. Gala apples are small and usually red, with small yellow or green part. This apple has thinner skin than most of the apples.Gala apples are sweet and very aromatic.This type of apple can be added to salads or cooked, and are also suitable for making sauces out of them.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           1.Golden delicious
          </strong>
          <script src="http://www.everystockphoto.com/widget.php?imageId=6707406&amp;size=small" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
         </p>
         <p>
          The name says it all. This apples name makes all the marketing for it. It is one of the most popular apple in the US. The Golden delicious is large in size, with yellow-green skin, with very sweet flavor. The inside color can vary from white-green, white-yellow, yellow and green-yellow. It is great eaten raw and it is also great for baking.
         </p>
