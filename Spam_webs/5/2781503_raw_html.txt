       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title">
         The time we painted an arts and crafts house in Cardiff
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-meta heade-entry-meta">
        <span class="posted-on">
         <i class="fa fa-clock-o">
         </i>
         <a href="http://www.neverpaintagain.co.uk/blog/period-property-cardiff-painted/" rel="bookmark">
          <time class="updated" datetime="2015-03-21T15:27:26+00:00">
           21st March 2015
          </time>
         </a>
        </span>
        <span class="byline">
         <span class="author vcard">
          <i class="fa fa-user">
          </i>
          <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.neverpaintagain.co.uk/blog/author/neverpaintagain/">
           neverpaintagain
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="post-thumb blog-thumb">
         <img alt="Cardiff exterior house painting" class="attachment-blog-large wp-post-image" height="300" src="http://www.neverpaintagain.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Cardiff-exterior-house-painting.jpg" width="400"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         This delightful art nouveau house in Cardiff, Wales, was externally refurbished, repaired and painted by NEVER PAINT AGAIN UK in 2012.
        </p>
        <p>
         Great care was taken to protect the artistic windows and features of this wonderful period Welsh home whilst we were working on it and we took some photos to help show you the sort of thing we do when we fix up a house like this.
         <span id="more-24094">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         We always enjoy our work each week as no week is ever the same!
        </p>
        <p>
         We were called by the owners of this lovely Art Nouveau (or arts and crafts) house in the Welsh capital of Cardiff, to help them with the problem of yearly repainting and flaking paint.
        </p>
        <p>
         No matter how many times the owners called the decorators back, and bear in mind there are some very good decorating companies in Cardiff, they just could
         <strong>
          not solve the problem of the paint flaking
         </strong>
         away from the wall each year.
        </p>
        <p>
         They also had issues with trying to keep the house looking white, as most paints start to deteriorate after the 1st winter, leaving the walls an off-white or light grey colour.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          An exterior wall coating and wall restoration project isnt as easy as it looks!
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         As this house is of aesthetic and local value, we had to great care with any work we did, as of course the house is probably under a conservation area,
         <strong>
          so we tread lightly but firmly with this job.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         We got to work doing the repairs on the house, which to be honest were not that bad and it was plainly obvious that the property was of a very good quality build in the first place.
        </p>
        <p>
         The owners had certainly kept the place in good condition too, which does help.
        </p>
        <p>
         The paint flaking off was certainly an issue though.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Heres a shot of the rear with flaking paint.
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_24107" style="width: 593px">
         <img alt="rear before and showing flaking paint" class="wp-image-24107 " height="438" src="http://www.neverpaintagain.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/rear-before3-1024x768.jpg" title="rear before and showing flaking paint" width="583"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Weather eroded paint on this house in Cardiff
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         We carried out a full paint removal by machine and by hand and cleaned the walls of any dust or residue. Once again we had to be careful not to damage the walls, or in fact the flower beds and bushes around the house which had to be covered over by the team so as to not harm the plants etc.
        </p>
        <p>
         You can see what the rear looks like NOW in the following image, which, as luck would have it, brought the sun out with it too.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_24108" style="width: 672px">
         <img alt="looking up at completed rear with wall coating" class="wp-image-24108 " height="497" src="http://www.neverpaintagain.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/looking-up-at-completed-rear-1024x768.jpg" title="looking up at completed rear with wall coating" width="662"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          A high quality exterior wall finish can be achieved on any older home
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         We had to take great care with the very old and valuable original windows to the faade of the house.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Here is a view of a lovely bay window, after the walls had been weatherproofed and painted with our exterior wall coatings. Check out the William Morris style fanlights above each window pane.
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_24110" style="width: 599px">
         <img alt="lovely bay window in arts and crafts style" class="wp-image-24110 " height="442" src="http://www.neverpaintagain.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/lovely-bay-window-1024x768.jpg" title="lovely bay window in arts and crafts style" width="589"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          A lovely bay window in arts and crafts style
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         So anyway, we got to work on the house and applied a special high performance trade only textured wall coating which will last for up to 20 years.
        </p>
        <p>
         Yes the owners do not need to have it repainted again! We have been working in Wales offering exterior wall coatings in Cardiff since the 90s and this was but one of that years projects.
        </p>
        <p>
         The wall coating was applied with a special spray machine which we have to be properly trained to use. Any mess is contained on site and fully removed and cleaned up at the end of each day and of course when the job was finished.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          We turned this house in Cardiff..
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Arts and Crafts house, South Wales" class="aligncenter wp-image-24111 " height="821" src="http://www.neverpaintagain.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/front-of-house-before.jpg" title="Arts and Crafts house, South Wales" width="616"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         with some hard work and elbow grease and some glorious sunny Welsh weather..
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          .into THIS HOUSE in Cardiff!
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Cardiff exterior house painting" class="aligncenter wp-image-24105 " height="507" src="http://www.neverpaintagain.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Cardiff-exterior-house-painting.jpg" title="Cardiff exterior house painting" width="676"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Guaranteed for fifteen years not to fade, crack, chip or peel.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         The paint coating on this house now will NOT fade either and will keep its nice, bright whiteness for many years to come.
        </p>
        <p>
         The long life and high performance wall coating we used on this project, also cuts down on the wind chill factor and therefore owners saw a gradual reduction in their heating bills too!
        </p>
