              <p>
               Every once in a while comes a speech so inspiring, so motivating, that it redefines human existence. This Target employee's
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/black-friday/">
                Black Friday
               </a>
               speech is that moment.
              </p>
              <p>
               Scot, from a Target in Westminster, Maryland, prepared his fellow employees for battle with a speech that may sound familiar  it's a slightly amended version of the "This is Sparta" speech from
               <em>
                300
               </em>
               .
              </p>
              <p>
               "Whatever comes through those gates, you will stand your ground with a smile on your face. They come here with bargains in their heads and fire in their eyes and we shall give those bargains to them."
              </p>
