         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          Dining Room Table Transformation!
         </h1>
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2010-06-19T19:13:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          Jun 19
          <sup>
           th
          </sup>
          , 2010
         </time>
        </div>
        <div class="comments-info clearfix">
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.allthingsthrifty.com/2010/06/dining-room-table-transformation.html#comments">
           51 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="bg-border">
         </span>
        </div>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         Have you ever worked on a project for so long that you forgot when you actually started?
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.allthingsthrifty.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         Ive had this table in my garage for quite some time. She needed extra love and extra effort, but man her transformation has been FUN!
        </p>
        <p>
         It was love at first sight! I knew when I first saw her, that she had potential. I even looked past her mismatched colors. I loved her octagon shape, and I LOVED that she came with two extend-able leaves. One of the leaves matched her and the other was black. She was painted a dark green color, but I had something else in mind!
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1SHFnfPdI/AAAAAAAABMI/0UYi4LD1tD4/s1600/107NCD905.jpg" imageanchor="1" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/3.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1SHFnfPdI/AAAAAAAABMI/0UYi4LD1tD4/s1600/107NCD905.jpg');" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="250" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1SHFnfPdI/AAAAAAAABMI/0UYi4LD1tD4/s400/107NCD905.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I started by lightly sanding the surface to even up the table top. This table had been painted multiple times and although I dont sand furniture often, this one needed it. I didnt sand it down to the wood, but I did sand more than usual.
        </p>
        <p>
         Next, I primed her with KILZ. Im sure you seasoned readers are not surprised. After the KILZ was dry, I sanded her with a high grit sand paper (250) with my electrical sander to remove the sand-paper grit that comes from KILZ at times. Using a high grit sand paper works wonders. It honestly takes the grit right off and leaves the surface oh so smooth. (I did NOT re-prime after thisit only removed the grit and the primer was still perfectly in tact.)
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1R7E2Nh2I/AAAAAAAABMA/Aiv9QUBgGFQ/s1600/Collages1.jpg" imageanchor="1" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/1.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1R7E2Nh2I/AAAAAAAABMA/Aiv9QUBgGFQ/s1600/Collages1.jpg');" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="250" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1R7E2Nh2I/AAAAAAAABMA/Aiv9QUBgGFQ/s400/Collages1.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Then I sprayed her with Krylon Ivory. I chose a gloss sheen because I wanted her to be very durable for her new home. Gloss sheen helps to be able to wipe off the furniture in the future, so since she was a dining room table who will be wiped off A LOT in her life, I went with gloss.
        </p>
        <p>
         Then using the tips from
         <a href="http://www.allthingsthrifty.com/2010/03/glazing-furniture-101.html">
          GLAZING 101
         </a>
         , I glazed her. This is when my husband started to notice her. I kind of want to keep this, he said. Are you sure you dont want this in OUR house?
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1pUapTC1I/AAAAAAAABMQ/ci-x5BcWaBU/s1600/Table+finished+002.JPG" imageanchor="1" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1pUapTC1I/AAAAAAAABMQ/ci-x5BcWaBU/s1600/Table+finished+002.JPG');" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1pUapTC1I/AAAAAAAABMQ/ci-x5BcWaBU/s320/Table+finished+002.JPG"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I LOVE IT WHEN THAT HAPPENS!!!! And to answer his questionof COURSE I want her. In fact I dont think Ive ever wanted to keep a project so bad in. my. life. BUTI am trying to keep focused on our backyard who needs our attention right now. So.
         <span style="color: orange; font-size: large;">
          she is FOR SALE.
         </span>
         Tear.
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1MlcBW4mI/AAAAAAAABLQ/k5F_fXZzXjs/s1600/Dining+Room+Table.jpg" imageanchor="1" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/3.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1MlcBW4mI/AAAAAAAABLQ/k5F_fXZzXjs/s1600/Dining+Room+Table.jpg');" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="400" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1MlcBW4mI/AAAAAAAABLQ/k5F_fXZzXjs/s400/Dining+Room+Table.jpg" width="266"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I love her shape. I love the pedestal bottom. Here is the table without any leaves.
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1LmLsFu0I/AAAAAAAABKw/EF11gc1fx3Q/s1600/Dining+Table.jpg" imageanchor="1" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/1.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1LmLsFu0I/AAAAAAAABKw/EF11gc1fx3Q/s1600/Dining+Table.jpg');" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="265" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1LmLsFu0I/AAAAAAAABKw/EF11gc1fx3Q/s400/Dining+Table.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         And with one leaf.
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1Lx5sJ6II/AAAAAAAABK4/58ou0DhUMTY/s1600/Dining+Room+Table+with+leaf.jpg" imageanchor="1" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1Lx5sJ6II/AAAAAAAABK4/58ou0DhUMTY/s1600/Dining+Room+Table+with+leaf.jpg');" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="201" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1Lx5sJ6II/AAAAAAAABK4/58ou0DhUMTY/s400/Dining+Room+Table+with+leaf.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         ANDwith TWO leaves! Dimensions: 7 feet long by 3 feet 8 inches across when fully extended.
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1MA5fvhQI/AAAAAAAABLA/cbsgK3THgBY/s1600/Dining+Room+Table+with+two+Leaves.jpg" imageanchor="1" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/2.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1MA5fvhQI/AAAAAAAABLA/cbsgK3THgBY/s1600/Dining+Room+Table+with+two+Leaves.jpg');" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="411" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1MA5fvhQI/AAAAAAAABLA/cbsgK3THgBY/s640/Dining+Room+Table+with+two+Leaves.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         And here she is with the chairs from this weeks WEEK of CHAIRS. I. Love. Them.
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1M6q6eLbI/AAAAAAAABLY/Xa84b8niBwY/s1600/Dining+Room+Table+1.jpg" imageanchor="1" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1M6q6eLbI/AAAAAAAABLY/Xa84b8niBwY/s1600/Dining+Room+Table+1.jpg');" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="265" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1M6q6eLbI/AAAAAAAABLY/Xa84b8niBwY/s400/Dining+Room+Table+1.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         But, I am actually selling the set with SIX chairsnot just four!
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1NiTSyF6I/AAAAAAAABLo/zODaDfSUghY/s1600/Dining+room+Table+with+mismatched+chairs.jpg" imageanchor="1" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/3.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1NiTSyF6I/AAAAAAAABLo/zODaDfSUghY/s1600/Dining+room+Table+with+mismatched+chairs.jpg');" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="312" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1NiTSyF6I/AAAAAAAABLo/zODaDfSUghY/s400/Dining+room+Table+with+mismatched+chairs.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         So, e-mail me at allthingsthrifty at gmail dot com if you are interested!
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1NRYdjfFI/AAAAAAAABLg/BLbvHf49HQc/s1600/Dining+Room+Table+with+Colorful+Chairs.jpg" imageanchor="1" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1NRYdjfFI/AAAAAAAABLg/BLbvHf49HQc/s1600/Dining+Room+Table+with+Colorful+Chairs.jpg');" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_mfwl2KBT-r0/TB1NRYdjfFI/AAAAAAAABLg/BLbvHf49HQc/s640/Dining+Room+Table+with+Colorful+Chairs.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>

        <p>
         Back in the
         <a href="http://soyouthinkyoucandecorate.blogspot.com/" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/soyouthinkyoucandecorate.blogspot.com/');">

          mismatched dining room

            I LOVE my table and not just for sentimental reasons, it really is beautiful (it actually looks almost like this table but the pedestal has two posts with a gap between them rather than the one post pedestal). My husband and I have been talking about getting rid of it because of it's poor condition. However, this has given me hope! I have been swooned by your transformation of this table and have decided to seriously convince my husband that our table NEEDS this makeover!
           </p>
           <p>
            Thank you for the inspiration. I am both excited and extremely freaked out to do this, but I know it will look amazing!
