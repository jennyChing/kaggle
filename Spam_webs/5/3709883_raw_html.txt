        <h3>
         <strong>
          What is a Doppelgnger?
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         A doppelgnger is a physical double or look-alike of a living person, making the two appear as if they were the same person or, at least, identical twins. It is possible for two persons to have the same face and yet not be related  and could be living across the world from each other! However, since physical features are taken into account, most doppelgngers belong to the same race or nationality.
        </p>
