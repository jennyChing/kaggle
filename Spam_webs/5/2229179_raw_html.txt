        <p>
         Check out this guys old computer motherboard:
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="greece" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4472" height="332" src="http://geekychristian.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/greece.jpg" width="460"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         It sure looks like
         <strong>
          ancient Greece
         </strong>
         , doesnt it?!
        </p>
        <p>
         But theres something else I see here
         <strong>
          evidence of a Creator!
         </strong>
        </p>
        <h1>
         Evidence of God
        </h1>
        <p>
         This old motherboard, while probably
         <strong>
          not as fancy
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          complex
         </strong>
         as the
         <strong>
          Macbook Air
         </strong>
         Im writing on, is still quite a complex little creation. Leave this complex, organized material alone for
         <strong>
          10 to 15 years
         </strong>
         and what do we have? A piece of machinery that is
         <strong>
          falling apart
         </strong>
         . Its becoming
         <strong>
          more unorganized
         </strong>
         , more
         <strong>
          disorderly
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         This is called the
         <strong>
          Law of Entropy
         </strong>
         (aka The Second Law of Thermodynamics).
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          Nature tends to bring things to disorder. That is, with time, things naturally fall apart. Your car falls apart; your house falls apart; your body falls apart But if the
          <strong>
           universe is becoming less ordered
          </strong>
          , then where did the original order come from? (NormanGeisler,
          <em>
           I Dont Have Enough Faith To Be An Atheist [
          </em>
          Crossway: 2004], 77)
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         In short, the
         <strong>
          universe cannot organize itself
         </strong>
         because its moving towards disorder. Therefore, the universe had a
         <strong>
          beginning
         </strong>
         and a
         <strong>
          Beginner
         </strong>
         who must have done the
         <strong>
          initial organizing
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         And, great news for Christians,
         <a href="http://geekychristian.com/proof-for-god/">
          we have strong evidence
         </a>
         that this designer is exactly the God described in the Bible.
        </p>
        <p>
         Got 10 minutes?
         <a href="http://books.google.com/books?id=FPmV30uX1_4C&amp;lpg=PP1&amp;pg=PA73#v=onepage&amp;q&amp;f=false">
          Read the chapter I quoted from
          <em>
           I Dont Have Enough Faith To Be An Atheist
          </em>
          here
         </a>
         for free.
        </p>
