       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Sugar Skull Decanter Knock-off
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <span class="entry-categories">
          <a href="http://www.homemadeinterest.com/category/crafts/" rel="category tag">
           Crafts
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="5496950" data-link="http://www.homemadeinterest.com/sugar-skull-decanter-knock/" data-summary="" data-title="Sugar Skull Decanter Knock-off">
        </div>
        <p>
         Our
         <strong>
          Sugar Skull Decanter Knock-off i
         </strong>
         s simple to make and a fraction of the price of the original !
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Sugar Skull Decanter Knock-off {www.homemadeinterest.com}" class="aligncenter wp-image-2597 size-full" height="900" src="http://www.homemadeinterest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/SUgar-Skull-Decanter-Knock-off_Pinterest2.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Last year I fell in love with
         <a href="http://www.crateandbarrel.com/halloween/morelia-decanter/s607630" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Crate and Barrell Morelia Decanter">
          Crate and Barrels Morelia Decanter
         </a>
         , but I couldnt justify spending $50 for it when I would only get to use it once a year. When I saw it on their website again this year I decided I had to try to make my own. Im so glad I did. I was able to make make my own version for only $0.99!
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-2609">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Sugar Skull Decanter Knock-off {www.homemadeinterest.com}" class="aligncenter wp-image-2611 size-full" height="600" src="http://www.homemadeinterest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Spooky-Spider-Web-String-Art_sidebyside.png" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Its not exactly the same, but for a decoration Im only going to use during Halloween I think it is a pretty good substitute. It was easy to make, all I needed was:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          A glass bottle = already had
         </li>
         <li>
          Black acrylic paint = already had
         </li>
         <li>
          Etched glass Vinyl (or contact paper) = already had
         </li>
         <li>
          Decorative wine cork = already had
         </li>
         <li>
          Sugar skull design = $0.99
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           Total = $0.99
          </span>
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <img alt="Sugar Skull Decanter Knock-off {www.homemadeinterest.com}" class="aligncenter wp-image-2598 size-full" height="350" src="http://www.homemadeinterest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Sugar-Skull-Decanter-Knock-off_steps.png" width="450"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I usedan empty red wine vinegar bottle that I peeled the label off of and cleaned really well. I poured about 1/3 of a bottle of black paint into the bottle and then slowly turned the bottle until the entire inside was coated. Then I let it dry overnight.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Sugar Skull Decanter Knock-off {www.homemadeinterest.com}" class="aligncenter wp-image-2592 size-full" height="600" src="http://www.homemadeinterest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Sugar-Skull-Decanter-Knock-off_closeup.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The next day I cut out a sugar skull design, that I bought from the
         <a href="http://www.silhouetteonlinestore.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Silhouette online store">
          Silhouette Store
         </a>
         ,with my Silhouette Cameo. I used a small scrap of silver etched vinyl that I already had, but contact paper would also work. Then I put the image on the bottle.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Sugar Skull Decanter Knock-off {www.homemadeinterest.com}" class="aligncenter wp-image-2595 size-full" height="400" src="http://www.homemadeinterest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Sugar-Skull-Decanter-Knock-off_final.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I already had a decorative wine stopper that was perfect so I put it in the bottle and tada! My own sugar skull decanter. I love it :). I cant wait to get my mantel decorated now!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Sugar-Skull-Decanter-Knock-off_featured" class="aligncenter wp-image-2655 size-medium" height="300" src="http://www.homemadeinterest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Sugar-Skull-Decanter-Knock-off_featured-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you liked our Sugar Skull Decanter Knock-off you might also like our
         <a href="http://www.homemadeinterest.com/2014/09/20/halloween-wreath-hack/" title="Halloween Wreath {hack}">
          Halloween Wreath Knock-off
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Halloween Wreath Hack | Home. Made. Interest." class="aligncenter wp-image-2342 size-full" height="300" src="http://www.homemadeinterest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Halloween-Wreath-Hack_grandinroad.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Do you have a project youve done because you just havent been able to bring yourself to spend the money on the real thing? Share you link on our
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/homemadeinterest" target="_blank" title="Home. Made. Interest. Facebook">
          Facebook
         </a>
         page so we can check it out!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="kat" class="noshadow nopin alignleft wp-image-356 size-full" height="48" src="http://www.homemadeinterest.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/kat.png" width="130"/>
