          <h3 class="entry-title">
           4 Proven Strategies to Cope With Small Business Finance Nightmares
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-meta mb30">
           <span class="posted-on">
            Posted on
            <a href="https://www.fundthrough.com/blog/4-proven-strategies-cope-small-business-finance-nightmares/" rel="bookmark">
             <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2014-12-30T08:28:54+00:00">
              December 30, 2014
             </time>
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
          <!-- .entry-meta -->
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-content mb60">
          You might have a groundbreaking idea and a great staff, but your business is never fully immune to experiencing a financial crisis. Managing your money wisely and budgeting correctly are among the top priorities andhardest tasks small business owners deal with.
          <span id="more-564">
          </span>
          You never know when a simple mistake or set of circumstances could leave youin the middle of a finance nightmare. So check out these 4 greatstrategiesyou can implement to turn your nightmare into an opportunity and guarantee working capital for you small business:
          <h2>
           1. Take a Loan
          </h2>
          <figure class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 680px;">

           <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">

             epSos .de /

          One of the most widely recognized methods of financing any type of small business venture is taking a bank loan. Many small businesses experiencing financial difficulty go for this option mainly because its the most obvious. Butis abank loanalways the best option?

Many alternative methods of financing, such as theFundThrough
          <a href="https://www.fundthrough.com/">
           working capital
          </a>
          loans,are sometimes a more appropriate and beneficial option. In comparison to bank loans, FunThroughoffers lines of credit whichmost other financing institution wouldnt, mainly because it doesnt request a financial history report. One of the reasons behind the long and tedious process behind applying and qualifying for a bank loan is the financial check and laborious task of preparing the necessary documents. FundThrough, instead, assesses your application on the outstanding invoices from your customers. This can be done online in only a few minutes.

Some businesses never get approved for a bank loan, meaningtheyve wasted their time.This creates a situation where a delay could only worsen their financial difficulties. Even if their loan isaccepted,bank loans have a much higher interest rate compared to alternative financial institutions. With FundThrough you get todraw money whenever you need without commitment periods or minimum sums.
          <h2>
           2. Embrace Sustainable Business Practices
          </h2>
          <figure class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 680px;">
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/salty_soul/7067187535" title="Behind the Scenes &amp; Documentary Photography - Eco Fashion Week AW 2012 by Jason Hargrove, on Flickr">
            <img alt="working capital" src="https://farm6.staticflickr.com/5032/7067187535_f5f8fdc217.jpg" width="680"/>
           </a>
           <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/salty_soul/">
             Jason Hargrove /
            </a>
            <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/salty_soul/7067187535/in/photolist-bLvbCX-bxAwof-bxAuRE-bxAve7-bxAvUb-bxAvPS-bLv9wK-bxAv8A-bLv7YP-bxAuNo-bLvdFg-bxAs5m-bLveqi-bxAx5d-by7Xqj-by7Vwj-bM2DyT-by7ZGd-by7Xi9-by7Zto-by7Wps-bLsWkZ-bM2FNx-bM2FXp-by7Yxd-by7WJU-a3hPJu-a3hSkj-a3hp33-a3hjAs-a3f2Pt-a3etSg-pkDPPq-9veqRE-9vepP7-9vbp74-9vestL-9vbpL6-9vbrVc-9vepJA-9vepSq-9veqom-9veqD5-9vesW9-9vbphF-9veqih-9vepxQ-9vepDU-9vboCk-9vboHc">
             Flickr
            </a>
            /
            <a href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-nd/2.0/">
             CC BY-NC-ND 2.0
            </a>
           </figcaption>
          </figure>
          Sustainable business practices are not just forsmall startups or huge corporations trying to impress the public with their green outlook. Incorporating sustainable business practices such as
          <a href="http://income.com/2132/how-to-earn-and-save-money-from-sustainable-practices/">
           conducting a green audit
          </a>
          can increase financial returns and reduce risk. This strategy canthen be implemented to help solve financial difficulties.

Of course, you should consider engaging in responsible entrepreneurship not simply for financial reasons.It canalso help out in the long-term by improving customer sales and loyalty in addition to attracting and retaining valuable employees. Its important to note that this solution wont get you out of a financial mess. However, it could improve your overall situation and help you reach your long-term goals.
          <h2>
           3. Work Closely Witha Knowledgeable Accountant
          </h2>
          <figure class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 680px;">
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/teegardin/5913014568" title="Balancing The Account By Hand by Ken Teegardin, on Flickr">
            <img alt="working capital" src="https://farm7.staticflickr.com/6052/5913014568_a1f3148bf7.jpg" width="680"/>
           </a>
           <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/teegardin/">
             Ken Teegardin /
            </a>
            <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/teegardin/5913014568/in/photolist-a1vKe3-99HMHB-iPFPxL-ejYDHb-ejYEPd-6jCNY3-ejYEkq-9VTAtE-cmJFDs-gTocsz-9SowyW-9NUNXV-ejSVeP-9StoR6-dBCVKA-eUjHA-8f4Mzj-6rADtZ-8BtAux-c19wTq-c19wWy-c19wVJ-c19wUw-c19yJh-bpJHfA-8BicGk-FLPq-9Wm6Tn-4nEfgh-9NUQqi-9NXCXw-4dy5nG-oxdnQy-oksUFC-oqGJpn-ejYDSS-7CwmTW-9yxhW1-dE7VWV-6wCBw5-gcBckd-73DiqV-8QkR2w-KTpTu-adPkJR-9NXDJ5-693zHq-9NXDkN-9NXF6d-9WpkUp">

            <a href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/2.0/">

          If your business is approaching a financial cliffwithout an accompanying decrease in sales or customer interest, then you might be dealing with an internal issue. One of the main reasons why most small businesses never manage to expand or end up going bustis the
          <a href="http://thenextweb.com/insider/2014/09/25/top-20-reasons-startups-fail-report/">
           financial neglect or limited financial training
          </a>
          .

But how can you know your finances are being handled in the best way? Working closely with a knowledgeable accountant is how. This is an excellentway to minimize your costs. Combine this with remembering to
          <a href="http://thenextweb.com/insider/2014/09/25/top-20-reasons-startups-fail-report/">
           ask for financial advice
          </a>
          and youve got a great strategy topositively impact your business. Done right, it should guarantee both short and long-term financial stability. So make sure you build a strong relationship with your accountant, even if you dont have an in-house one, that way youll be able to consultthem and get honest insight.
          <h2>
           4. Find Investors and Crowdfunding
          </h2>
          <figure class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 680px;">
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/nicolopaternoster/8127458566" title="Investors meet startups by Nicol Paternoster, on Flickr">

          </figure>
          Finding great external investment is one of the best-case scenarios when experiencing financial difficulties. Pitching your ideas to the right investors and possibly attracting quality partnership for your businessis a great strategy. Where can you meet such investors? Reach out via social media, do your research and connect to promising investors on LinkedIn and get a conversation going. Join networking meetings and ask other small businesses for insight, why not search foran investor-startup/small business meeting events and attend?

The
          <a href="http://www.forbes.com/sites/hsbc/2014/08/05/crowdfundings-untapped-potential-in-emerging-markets/">
           Increasing popularity
          </a>
          ofonline Crowdfunding platforms also provides a huge variety of opportunities for your business. Just design your pitch, make it compelling, and see the great resultscrowdfunding makes possible. Bringing people together to sustain your business and stabilize it, at a low cost no less, is something
          <a href="http://www.entrepreneur.com/article/232234">
           many have managed to succeed in
          </a>
          .

How would you go around securing your working capital? Share your thoughts in the comment section below.
