           <h1 class="title">
            GWA Auto Design &amp; Tuning's Veinte Victorias Concept Studebaker Cooks With Gas
           </h1>
           <!-- google_ad_section_end -->
           <div itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Article">
            <meta content="GWA Auto Design &amp; Tuning's Veinte Victorias Concept Studebaker Cooks With Gas" itemprop="headline"/>

              <div class="KonaBody">
               <span itemprop="articleBody">
                <!--paging_filter-->
                <p>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-_original" height="457" src="http://inventorspot.com/files/images/2015_gwa_studebaker_veinte_victorias-wide_0.jpg" title="" width="640"/>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 <br/>
                 GWA Auto Design &amp; Tuning's proposed
                 <b>
                  Veinte Victorias
                 </b>
                 concept salutes the highly modified 1954 Studebaker Starliner coupes that have won an astounding Twenty Victories since the historic La Carrera Panamericana vintage road race was revived in 1988.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Although Raymond Loewy's groundbreaking (and wind-cheating) design debuted in 1953, the Starliner coupe remains a force to be reckoned with at specialty events such as Bonneville's
                 <a href="/articles/star-cars-speed-week-are-studebakers" target="_blank">
                  SpeedWeek
                 </a>
                 and the revived La Carrera Panamericana.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-_original" height="333" src="http://inventorspot.com/files/images/GWA-Veinte-Victorias-2-700x364.jpg" title="" width="640"/>
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 At the latter, Starliners have reeled off two strings of consecutive wins: 1993 through 2001 and 2007 through 2014. The grand total of 20 wins in 27 seasons have inspired
                 <a href="http://www.gwa-tuning.com/index.html" target="_blank">
                  GWA Auto Design &amp; Tuning
                 </a>
                 of San Antonio, Texas, to propose a unique Starliner fully fifty years after Studebaker threw in the towel as a major automaker.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 GWA's vision would see a limited production of 19 Veinte Victorias painted yellow with La Carrera Panamericana livery, plus a single black-painted vehicle intended to be the event's pace car  pending the agreement of La Carrera Panamericana officials.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 While the Veinte Victorias' bodywork largely evokes Studebaker's 1953 Starliner coupes and hardtops, GWA grace's Loewy's design with prominent tailfins and modern energy-absorbing bumpers. The body is supported by a custom steel tube frame chassis mounting GWA alloy wheels (19x8 front and 19x11 rear), Brembo HPK brakes and race-tuned suspension components.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-_original" height="332" src="http://inventorspot.com/files/images/GWA-Veinte-Victorias-3-700x363.jpg" title="" width="640"/>
                 </span>
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 A supercharged 6.2-liter Chevrolet Corvette LS9 V8 engine rated at 620 hp / 600 lb-feet of torque, a six-speed manual gearbox and a custom Fabspeed exhaust system are the guts that give Veinte Victorias vehicles their go.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 According to GWA,
                 <i>
                  The Veinte Victorias is meant to be a a real fun car to drive, as well as a collectors item for those that had competed or been following the Carrera for all these years.
                 </i>
                 At press time pricing is undetermined but further news and info will be posted at the
                 <a href="http://www.gwa-tuning.com/index.html" target="_blank">
                  GWA website
                 </a>
                 as development progresses.
                 <i>
                  (via
                  <a href="http://blog.hemmings.com/index.php/2015/05/26/gwas-proposed-veinte-victorias-channels-the-1954-studebaker-commander-starliner/#comments" target="_blank">
                   Hemmings Daily
                  </a>
                  )
                 </i>
                </p>
