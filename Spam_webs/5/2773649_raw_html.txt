        </div>
        <p>
         Im a Batman veteran. Since I was a young lad, I have been perfecting my knowledge on all things DC. When I was younger I played the first Lego Batman game created and I loved it. Lego Batman was the game that really intrigued me and led me into the Batman world. Now that Im older, I have moved on to the brilliant Batman Arkham series. However, its still great to see more Lego Batman games coming out for kids as a great introduction to the world of Bruce Wayne and other DC super heroes like Superman, The Flash, and Green Lantern. I also think Lego does a great job at presenting the Jokers complex mind in a friendly way.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="320" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/aVxvKlhZ0JI" width="600">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         In the behind the scenes trailer, Lego shows us that they are really expanding the DC world by adding new characters and environments for kids to explore. They also show the talented cast, including Adam West (Wow, how awesome is that!) and Troy Baker (my all time favorite video game voice actor). I love the way Warner Bros. is collaborating with DC and I cant wait for Batman: Arkham Knight.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://sassymamainla.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Lego_Batman_3.jpg">
          <img alt="Lego Batman 3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12434" height="724" src="http://sassymamainla.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Lego_Batman_3.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         LEGO Batman 3: Beyond Gotham is going to be released this fall on Xbox One, Xbox 360, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 3, PS Vita, Wii U, 3DS and PC.
        </p>
        <p>
         My favorite DC character is Batman. Whos yours?
        </p>
