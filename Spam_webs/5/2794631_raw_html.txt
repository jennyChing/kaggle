&lt;h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'&gt;
Adorable Christmas Tree Ornaments - Made from Buttons
&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;div class='post-header'&gt;
&lt;div class='post-header-line-1'&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-5170085709915525810' itemprop='articleBody'&gt;
&lt;div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on"&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;Hello. And Welcome. I am so glad you are here. I hope you like today's project.&nbsp;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;My six year old granddaughter and I enjoyed creating our very own unique ornaments for the Christmas tree.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;We actually have another ornament to share sometime within the next couple of weeks. I'm looking forward to sharing that.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;If you would like to see last year's ornament please click &lt;a href="http://mylittleshopoftreasures.blogspot.ca/2013/12/hi-another-week-has-come-and-gone.html#.VIDvZzHF-FU" target="_blank"&gt;HERE&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;h2 style="text-align: left;"&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: x-large;"&gt;&lt;i&gt;Button Christmas Tree Ornaments&lt;/i&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-MV-RaslMXBc/VH5m6Hn-y5I/AAAAAAAARe4/xj99MUyAgzc/s1600/Cover%2BPage%2BButton%2BOrnaments.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"&gt;&lt;img alt="Christmas Tree Ornament made from Buttons" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-MV-RaslMXBc/VH5m6Hn-y5I/AAAAAAAARe4/xj99MUyAgzc/s1600/Cover%2BPage%2BButton%2BOrnaments.jpg" height="640" title="DIY Button Christmas Tree Ornament" width="598" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;I had a bit of stretchy cord that's used to string beaded necklaces or bracelets so we used it to make our tree ornaments.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
&lt;a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-AXQ4E-ymzmA/VH5wWyLLVRI/AAAAAAAARfU/7_uo4EegzLM/s1600/Button%2BOrnaments%2B3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"&gt;&lt;img alt="Ornament made from buttons" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-AXQ4E-ymzmA/VH5wWyLLVRI/AAAAAAAARfU/7_uo4EegzLM/s1600/Button%2BOrnaments%2B3.jpg" height="400" title="Christmas Tree Ornament" width="145" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;I decided to make my ornaments look like pine trees. I alternated dark green and light green buttons to add a bit of visual interest. I also added two small dark green buttons to the bottom. I hope they look like the tree trunk.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
&lt;a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-x-CP15xXBeQ/VH5qFalDauI/AAAAAAAARfE/h4jx1zlSoXs/s1600/Button%2BOrnaments.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;"&gt;&lt;img alt="Ornament made from Buttons" border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-x-CP15xXBeQ/VH5qFalDauI/AAAAAAAARfE/h4jx1zlSoXs/s1600/Button%2BOrnaments.jpg" height="400" title="Christmas Tree Ornament" width="115" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;My granddaughter wanted to make something unique. (We love reading Fancy Nancy who is very unique)&nbsp;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;Faith's green buttons aren't in any particular order - free form. She found a shiny red bead and added it to the top. I really like how it turned out. How about you?&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;I love the curly cue ornament hangers that I found at the dollar store. Aren't they pretty?&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;

&lt;a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-j2a7yck9Jiw/VH5xEHoca8I/AAAAAAAARfc/gQYW17sNN_I/s1600/Button%2BOrnaments%2B4.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"&gt;&lt;img alt="Ornament made from buttons" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-j2a7yck9Jiw/VH5xEHoca8I/AAAAAAAARfc/gQYW17sNN_I/s1600/Button%2BOrnaments%2B4.jpg" title="Christmas Tree Ornament" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;span style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: large;"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
