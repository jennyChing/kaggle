        <div class="body " data-context="9188109" data-friendly-name="Article Inline Link Tracking" data-max-times-to-track-links="5" data-widget="tm.omniture.tracking" itemprop="articleBody">
         <p>
          A city centre nightclub boss faces years in jail after being found guilty of raping and sexually assaulting his customers.
         </p>
         <p>
          A judge told Carlos Brown, 53, that three unanimous verdicts against him had he exposed him as a sexual predator.
         </p>
         <p>
          One of Browns victims at his
          <a class="inline-link" data-content-type="news" href="http://www.walesonline.co.uk/news/wales-news/cardiff-nightclub-manager-plied-customer-9131430">
           popular Cardiff bar
          </a>
          Charlie Brown's was a 20-year-old university student and the second was a teenager he suggested might be up for a threesome.
         </p>
         <h3 align="center">
          Old enough to be a grandfather to his victims
         </h3>
         <p>
          Both had been drinking and had been invited to stay behind, with others, after closing time.
         </p>
         <p>
          Brown, of Pengam Green,
          <a class="inline-link" data-content-type="section-topic" href="http://www.walesonline.co.uk/all-about/south-wales-police">
           Tremorfa
          </a>
          , described by prosecutor James Wilson as old enough to be a grandfather to both of them, told
          <a class="inline-link" data-content-type="section-topic" href="http://www.walesonline.co.uk/all-about/cardiff-crown-court">
           Cardiff Crown Court
          </a>
          during a week-long trial that he had done nothing wrong.
         </p>
         <p>
          He said both had given off signals that they were interested in him and the student had asked him to have sex with her.
         </p>
         <p>
          But as the verdicts were announced, the judge, Recorder Paul Hopkins QC told Brown: You have been convicted by this jury of despicable and disgraceful behaviour.
         </p>
         <h3 align="center">
          'A sexual predator'
         </h3>
         <p>
          You have been exposed as a sexual predator of young women whose lair was Charlie Browns.
         </p>
         <p>
          The seven female and five male jurors had to watch CCTV footage of the student being raped on a sofa at the club, the courtroom having been cleared for the viewing on large screens.
         </p>
         <p>
          Police recovered the film after the young woman woke the following morning to find him having sex with her a second time.
         </p>
         <p>
          She was unaware of the first rape,
          <a class="inline-link" data-content-type="news" href="http://www.walesonline.co.uk/news/wales-news/popular-cardiff-nightclub-charlie-browns-8135066">
           following a Halloween celebration last year
          </a>
          , until police told her.
         </p>
         <p>
          At the same time, footage of Brown grabbing the 18-year-old and lifting up her skirt, recorded three weeks earlier, was also recovered.
         </p>
         <p>
          He admitted he had given the teenager Southern Comfort to drink and that she had been interested in and kissing a younger man also having after-hours drinks at the time.
         </p>
         <p>
          But he also said she was flirty and dancing provocatively on the pool table.
         </p>
         <h3 align="center">
          'You were the dominant partner'
         </h3>
         <p>
          I did say Fancy a threesome Brown admitted in court.
         </p>
         <p>
          She was an adult and if someone is interested in you, thats fine.
         </p>
         <p>
          I was trying it on, until she made it clear she wasnt interested.
         </p>
         <p>
          Brown agreed he had tried to kiss her but she pulled away and wiped her hand over her mouth.
         </p>
         <p>
          <figure class="inline-image clearfix ">

            The former Charlie Browns night club on St Mary's Street in Cardiff

         </p>

          If she hadnt wanted to have intimate contact with me, she wouldnt have been there, he told the court.
         </p>
         <p>
          Mr Wilson alleged the woman, seen stumbling earlier, was so drunk she was incapable of giving her consent and told Brown: You were the dominant partner, moving her into the position you wanted and putting her legs up over your shoulders.
         </p>

          'Women in high heels tend to walk funny'
         </h3>
         <p>
          I did (move her legs), yes, but I think she did as well, the club boss replied.
         </p>
         <p>
          She was tipsy drunk but she was fine - women in high heels tend to (always) walk funny.
         </p>
         <p>
          Brown said when the student was sick and accidentally wet her blouse with water while rinsing her mouth, he had taken her to his office and removed her clothing, kissing her breasts, before helping her on with a dry T shirt.
         </p>
         <p>
          But she didnt push me away, he said.
         </p>
         <p>
          There was no conversation but she didnt object.
         </p>
         <p>
          Mr Wilson alleged that was because she was out of it and Brown then took advantage.
         </p>
