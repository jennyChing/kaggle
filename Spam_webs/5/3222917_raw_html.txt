                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5899066679207339882" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     It's been over four months since my last post.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     This break from blogging wasn't desired or planned. Rather, it was unavoidable due to an onslaught of blessing and change in my life that began in August.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Just to give you a little taste, here are a few of the recent happenings.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     In August, my family purchased a new home -- a little fixer-upper across town. We began deep cleaning and moderate remodeling as soon as the sale went through.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Soon after we purchased the house, my family made an offer on 37 acres about 20 minutes away. The offer was accepted, and after a 7-year search, we finally own some acreage.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     The last four months have passed in a flurry of packing, scrubbing, and painting. As the holidays approach and the remodeling continues, I'm not really sure how much writing I'll do in the near future.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     But though I am short on time, I couldn't be more thankful.

      };

        if (window.goog &amp;&amp; window.goog.comments) {
          var holder = document.getElementById('comment-holder');
          window.goog.comments.render(holder, provider);
        }
      };

      // render now, or queue to render when library loads:
      if (window.goog &amp;&amp; window.goog.comments) {
        render();
      } else {
        window.goog = window.goog || {};
