              <div>
               I know most of us think our recipes really are "the best" but I'm not lying when I tell you these really are The BEST Vegan Sugar Cookies! They have that classic flavor and texture of traditional sugar cookies we all remember from our childhood, yet contain fiber-filled flax seeds in place of eggs and cholesterol-free Earth Balance Organic Buttery Spread in place of fat-filled butter. Oh, and they're Santa-approved, of course!

            <div class="recipe-content">
             <h4 class="recipe-section-title recipe-ingredients">
              Ingredients
             </h4>
             <ul class="rp_ingredients">
              <li class="rp_ingredient">
               1 1/4  cups Earth Balance Natural Buttery Spread, original
              </li>
              <li class="rp_ingredient">
               1 cup turbinado sugar
              </li>
              <li class="rp_ingredient">
               1 tablespoon ground flax seeds
              </li>
              <li class="rp_ingredient">
               3  tablespoons warm water
              </li>
              <li class="rp_ingredient">
               2  teaspoons pure vanilla extract
              </li>
              <li class="rp_ingredient">
               4  cups all-purpose flour
              </li>
              <li class="rp_ingredient">
               1 teaspoon baking powder (aluminum-free)
              </li>
              <li class="rp_ingredient">
               2  cups powdered sugar
              </li>
              <li class="rp_ingredient">
               1 tablespoon Earth Balance Organic Buttery Spread, whipped
              </li>
              <li class="rp_ingredient">
               1 tablespoon Earth Balance Organic soy milk, vanilla
              </li>
              <li class="rp_ingredient">
               1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
              </li>
             </ul>
             <h4 class="recipe-section-title recipe-instructions">
              Directions
             </h4>
             <p>
             </p>
             <div class="wdpv_voting">
              <div class="wdpv_vote_up ">
               <input type="hidden" value="9383"/>
               <input class="wdpv_blog_id" type="hidden" value="1"/>

             </div>
             <div class="wdpv_clear">
             </div>
             1. Preheat oven to 350F.
             <br/>
             2. In small cup, mix together ground flax seeds and water to make one
             <br/>
             3. In large bowl, mix together butter and sugar until fluffy using electric beaters. Beat in
             <br/>
             4. Using a large wooden spoon, stir in baking powder and flour, first adding 2 cups, then gradually adding in the last two cups as you continue to stir. The dough will seem a bit dry at first but keep stirring and it will soften up. If youre really struggling, just add a touch more water.
             <br/>
             5. Cover and chill dough for 1 hour.
             <br/>
             6. Once dough is chilled, roll dough out onto floured, flat surface to 1/4
             <br/>
             7. Cut out desired shapes and bake on greased cookie sheet for 8-10 minutes. I like mine a bit softer so I remove them right at the 8-minute mark!
             <br/>
             8. Remove from cookie sheet and allow to cool on wire rack. Be sure to cool completely before frosting. And if youre using sprinkles, add them before you bake the dough!
             <br/>
             9. FOR THE FROSTING: In large bowl, add powdered sugar, then mix in Earth Balance butter by hand with large wooden spoon.
             <br/>
             10. Stir in vanilla. Slowly add soy milk 1 tablespoon at a time.
             <br/>
             11. Continue to stir until there are no lumps. You could make it thicker or thinner by adjusting the powdered sugar/milk ratio but I prefer mine a bit thicker for easier spreading.
