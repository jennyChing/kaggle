         <img alt="A hiker crosses over Franconia Brook on a foot bridge. At the end of this bridge hikers enter into the Pemigewasset Wilderness.        <p>
        <strong>
         Pemigewasset Wilderness, Trestles
        </strong>
         The East Branch &amp; Lincoln Railroad was a logging railroad in operation from 1893-1948 in the towns of Lincoln and Franconia New Hampshire. It was Built by J.E. Henry and then sold to the Parker Young Company and later to the Marcalus Manufacturing Company.
