        <p>
         If you are a photo junkie and spend a lot of time taking really cool, and sometimes outrageous pictures of yourself and your pals, we want you to check out WePose app for iOS devices. The apps aim is to make sure that you dont just shoot photos for your albums, but also to have a lot of fun with it. WePose wants to turn your photo taking experience into a fun game that you can not only enjoy, but also share and enjoy with the rest of the world. Lets check out the app.
        </p>

        <h2>
         WePose App Review
        </h2>
        <p>
         WePose is a pretty simple game to play. Once you download and install the app, you will be asked to give permissions to your photos. Then you will be directed to the Home page where you can start playing the game.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cdn5.theapptimes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/WePose-Home-Page.png?c49e29">
          <img alt="WePose Home Page" border="0" height="534" src="http://cdn5.theapptimes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/WePose-Home-Page_thumb.png?c49e29" style="background-image: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; display: inline; padding-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="WePose Home Page" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Start Posing section contains a series of pose themes, most of which are paid packages costing $0.99 a piece. Among the paid packages are the Disco Lobster pack that features disco dancing lobsters, angry elves, and tiny sharks, while Who Stole My Stapler is an office themed pack that you can play with your coworkers to unwind on a stressful day. Its My Birthday features some insanely funny birthday themed poses that can turn any lame birthday party into a fun event you will remember for years to come. Youll certainly have some unforgettable photos to look back on!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cdn5.theapptimes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/WePose-Themes.png?c49e29">
          <img alt="WePose Themes" border="0" height="534" src="http://cdn5.theapptimes.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/WePose-Themes_thumb.png?c49e29" style="background-image: none; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; display: inline; padding-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="WePose Themes" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         You do have a free pack to try of course, called WePose for All. Hit the button, grant permission to access the camera, and you are ready to roll. At the bottom of the camera screen, you will see an instruction which you will call out to your friends and ask them to pose. You can obviously join in too!
        </p>
        <p>
         Some of the hilarious poses I read out included: You just got punched in the face, Youre an angry little teapot, and Awkward middle school slow dance. You can quickly move to the next pose by clicking on the previous and next arrow buttons at the bottom of the screen.
        </p>
        <p>
         All your fun photo captures are stored in the My Poses section along with the callout pose. You can share these hilarious images on social platforms or via SMS, and email.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Final Thoughts
        </h3>
        <p>
         If you are hanging out with friends, having a reunion or gathering of some sort, or just need to chill out on a stressful day, WePose is an app that is certain to lighten up the day. I am pretty sure that the team will include more and more cool themes in upcoming versions, but for now there are just a handful, paid ones. If you want to have fun with your photos be sure to give the app a try. Its free to download so you have nothing to lose!
        </p>
