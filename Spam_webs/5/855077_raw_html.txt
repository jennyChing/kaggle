          Gun Grabbing Cop Placed on Leave: Connecticut Cop Who Said Hed Love to Bang Down Your Door and Come For Your Gun Placed On Leave
         </h1>
        </header>
        <div class="article-byline">
         Posted on March 11, 2014
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody" style="color: #363636">
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://girlsjustwanna.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/gun-confiscation.jpg" rel="prettyphoto[11010]">
           <img alt="gun-confiscation" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11011" height="538" src="http://girlsjustwanna.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/gun-confiscation.jpg" width="450"/>
          </a>
          As a first step towards gun confiscation, Connecticut is implementing gun registration. That has turned tens of thousands of formerly law abiding citizens in the state whove refused to comply into felons. Now, the state is trying to figure out what to do next. If the next thing officials decide to do is door-to-door gun confiscation, theres
          <a href="http://www.theblaze.com/stories/2014/03/10/officer-reportedly-tells-citizen-i-give-my-left-n-to-bang-down-your-door-and-come-for-your-gun/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.theblaze.com/stories/2014/03/10/officer-reportedly-tells-citizen-i-give-my-left-n-to-bang-down-your-door-and-come-for-your-gun/', 'at least one police officer who wants everyone to know that hell be just following orders when he puts a gun to your head in order to take away your Second Amendment rights');" target="_Top">
           <strong>
            at least one police officer who wants everyone to know that hell be just following orders when he puts a gun to your head in order to take away your Second Amendment rights
           </strong>
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          A Connecticut officer with the Branford Police Department has reportedly been placed on extended leave pending the outcome of an internal investigation after he allegedly told a Facebook user he couldnt wait to bang down your door and come for your gun.
         </p>
         <p>
          Connecticut resident John Cinque, whose passionate remarks to lawmakers on the states new gun registration laws went viral, provided screenshots of a Facebook conversation involving Officer Joseph Peterson to the conservative blog Freedom Outpost. The officer is reportedly a long-time friend of Cinques.
         </p>
         <p>
          The heated debate reportedly began over Connecticuts new gun control law that technically turned gun owners who refuse to register their semi-automatic rifles and/or high-capacity magazines into felons overnight.
         </p>
