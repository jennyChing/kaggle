      <h1 class="title">
       <a href="http://www.techdaily.com.au/b2/linkedin-meets-online-dating/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to LinkedIn meets online dating">
        LinkedIn meets online dating
       </a>
      </h1>
      May 20, 2014
     </div>
     <!-- /.post-meta -->
     <div class="clearfix post-wrap post-3760 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-news tag-linkedin tag-linkedup tag-online-dating tag-tinder" id="post-3760">
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.techdaily.com.au/b2/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/linkedup.jpg">
         <img alt="linkedup" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-3761" height="108" src="http://www.techdaily.com.au/b2/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/linkedup-150x108.jpg" width="150"/>
        </a>
        The professional networking website LinkedIn is a great place to make good business contacts, find your next job or even recruit new employees but now it has also become a useful dating tool.
       </p>
       <p>
        The makers of a a new App called
        <a href="http://www.linkedin.com/company/linkedup-" target="_blank">
         LinkedUP
        </a>
        suggest that as people spend so much of their lives at work theres nothing wrong with looking for love there and have harnessed the power of LinkedIn to help things along.
       </p>
       <p>
        Much like the popular Tinder App users of
        <a href="http://www.linkedin.com/company/linkedup-" target="_blank">
         LinkedUp
        </a>
        will be presented with profile photos which then can then like or reject, but the difference with this App is that youll only be presented with people who share similar professional backgroundsso potentially someone you might also work with.
       </p>
       <p>
        LinkedUp is available free for
        <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/linkedup!/id794546332?mt=8" target="_blank">
         iOS
        </a>
        devices.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
