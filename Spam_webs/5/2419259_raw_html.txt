               <h1 class="title gutter">
                Pavilion for Japanese Art: Paintings in Celebration of Twenty-Five Years
               </h1>
               <div class="content-content static-frame" id="content-content">
                <!-- content region -->
                <div class="region region-content content nested grid16-13" id="content">
                 <div class="content-inner inner" id="content-inner">
                  <div class="block block-system first last odd grid16-13" id="block-system-main">
                   <div class="gutter inner clearfix">
                    <div class="content clearfix">
                     <div class="node node-exhibition odd full-node clearfix" id="node-13553">
                      <div class="inner">
                       <div class="node-top region nested" id="node-top">
                       </div>
                       <div class="content">
                        <div class="field field-name-field-location field-type-text field-label-hidden">
                         <div class="field-items">
                          <div class="field-item even">
                           Pavilion for Japanese Art
                          </div>
                         </div>
                        </div>
                        <div class="field field-type-date field-name-field-exhibition-date">
                         <div class="field-items">
                          April 26, 2014June 22, 2014
                         </div>
                        </div>
                        <div class="field field-name-field-image field-type-image field-label-hidden">
                         <div class="field-items">
                          <div class="field-item even">
                           <img alt="" src="http://www.lacma.org/sites/default/files/styles/Exhibition_Main/public/image/cranes2_0.jpg?itok=hrgpohTW"/>
                          </div>
                         </div>
                        </div>
                        <div class="field field-name-field-description field-type-text-long field-label-hidden">
                         <div class="field-items">
                          <div class="field-item even">
                           <p>
                            This exhibition of paintings from the museum's collection of Japanese art, including personal favorites chosen by LACMA curator and department head Robert T. Singer, celebrates the 25th anniversary of architect BruceGoff's Pavilion for Japanese Art
                            <strong>
                             .
                             <strong>
                             </strong>
                            </strong>
                            Goffinitially conceived of a space designed to meet the unique viewing requirements of traditional Japanese hanging scrolls and folding screens, with natural daylight and each work displayed in its own
                            <em>
                             tokonoma
                            </em>
                            , or alcove.The exterior walls of the Pavilion are made of Kalwall, a translucent material that permits light to enter a room in much the same way a shoji screen does. The gentle, spiraling ramp and petal-like viewing platforms give the viewer a sense of climbing through a garden. Since the building opened in 1988, nearly 20 folding screens and more than 150 hanging scrolls have been added to the museum's collection of Japanese paintings.
                            <strong>
                             <strong>
                             </strong>
                            </strong>
                           </p>
                           <p>
                            <strong>
                             <strong>
                              <a href="http://collections.lacma.org/search/site/?f[1]=im_field_location_search_tag%3A14207">
                               Find out more
                              </a>
                              about other works on view in the Pavilion.
                              <br/>
                             </strong>
                            </strong>
                           </p>
                           <p>
                            <strong>
                             <strong>
                              This exhibition is included in General Admission.
                              <a href="https://www.lacma.org/members">
                              </a>
                              <br/>
                              <a href="https://www.lacma.org/members">
                               Join now
                              </a>
                              and see it free, or
                              <a href="http://tx1.lacma.org/Default.asp">
                               reserve a ticket.
                              </a>
                              <strong>
                               <strong>

                              <br/>
                             </strong>
                            </strong>
                           </p>
                          </div>
