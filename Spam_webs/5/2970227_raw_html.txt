        <div class="mw-content-ltr mw-content-text" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">
         <div class="floatright plainlinks" style="text-align: center;">
          <p>
           <a class="external text exitstitial" href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/timmythesuk/2060272929/in/pool-camerapedia/" rel="nofollow">
            <img alt="2060272929_e8ca5e2ac4_m.jpg" src="http://farm3.static.flickr.com/2347/2060272929_e8ca5e2ac4_m.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <i>
           <a class="external text exitstitial" href="http://www.flickr.com/search/groups/?w=46195334%40N00&amp;q=polaroid+104&amp;m=pool" rel="nofollow">
            Other images
           </a>
          </i>
         </div>
         <p>
          Part of the series of
          <a href="/wiki/Polaroid" title="Polaroid">
           Polaroid
          </a>
          cameras that began with the Automatic Land Camera 100 and finishing at the Automatic Land Camera 450, this camera shares a common set of features with all models in this line:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Folding Bellows
          </li>
          <li>
           Automatic Exposure
          </li>
          <li>
           100-series Packfilm
          </li>
         </ul>
         <div class="floatleft plainlinks" style="text-align: center;">
          <p>
           <a class="external text exitstitial" href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/timmythesuk/2061088316/in/pool-camerapedia/" rel="nofollow">
            <img alt="2061088316_732991d4ee_t.jpg" src="http://farm3.static.flickr.com/2153/2061088316_732991d4ee_t.jpg"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <a class="external text exitstitial" href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/timmythesuk/2061086736/in/pool-camerapedia/" rel="nofollow">
           <img alt="2061086736_79d5fb299e_t.jpg" src="http://farm3.static.flickr.com/2385/2061086736_79d5fb299e_t.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          <br/>
          The 104 was one of the earliest 'consumer' models of the
          <a href="/wiki/Polaroid_100-400_series" title="Polaroid 100-400 series">
           100-400 series
          </a>
          line of folding Packfilm Land Cameras, similar to many other models; this model features a non-folding, Polaroid-designed zone focusing system and a 2 element plastic lens. It was available with a black leather case as pictured.
         </p>
         <p>
          The 104 was produced from 1965 until 1967, retailing on release for $60.
         </p>
         <p>
          <br/>
         </p>
