       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Funfetti Cake Batter White Chocolate Chip Cookie Cake
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2013-05-08T11:14:39+00:00">
         May 8, 2013
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://www.lifeloveandsugar.com/2013/05/08/funfetti-cake-batter-white-chocolate-chip-cookie-cake-2/#comments">
          65 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.lifeloveandsugar.com%2F2013%2F05%2F08%2Ffunfetti-cake-batter-white-chocolate-chip-cookie-cake-2%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.lifeloveandsugar.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2013%2F05%2Ffunfetti_cake_batter_cookie_cake51.png&amp;description=Funfetti%20Cake%20Batter%20White%20Chocolate%20Chip%20Cookie%20Cake" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Is there a rehab for funfetti cake batter addicts? Because if there is, I need to go. I cant seem to get enough. This cookie cake is the latest way that my
         <del datetime="2013-05-08T14:40:48+00:00">
          obsession
         </del>
         love of Funfetti has manifested itself. And boy is it tasty. Everything youd expect. Funfetti in the moistest and chewiest cookie cake form. With icing. Yes, please!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Funfetti Cake Batter White Chocolate Chip Cookie Cake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2517" height="709" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/funfetti_cake_batter_cookie_cake51.png" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ive made it a few times now and it is always a hit. And its not hard to make.
        </p>
        <p>
         The recipe uses an entire box of Funfetti cake mix. I was kind of stubborn about that. I have a number of partially used boxes of cake mix in my pantry and I was determined to not make this another instance where Id use part of a box. Not that there arent plenty of
         <a href="http://lifeloveandsugar.com/?s=funfetti">
          things I could make with it
         </a>
         , I just didnt want to. So there.
         <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Funfetti Cake Batter White Chocolate Chip Cookie Cake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2520" height="702" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/funfetti_cake_batter_cookie_cake2.png" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         However my stubbornness led to one flaw (if you can really call it that) with this cookie cake. It has what I like to call a lip, or a hump, around the edge. Its covered with icing because I like icing on my cookie cake, but if you look at the side you can see it there. I call your attention to it because I dont want you to make this and then freak out about it. Just cover it up. Because you need to make this. This thing is pure Funfetti gold. If gold was edible of course.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Funfetti Cake Batter White Chocolate Chip Cookie Cake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2518" height="480" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/funfetti_cake_batter_cookie_cake4.png" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The directions are pretty straight forward and while I mention this in them, I want to make a point of it here. Most cookie recipes say its best to refrigerate cookie dough for a while, even overnight, before baking. Not everyone does  its a preference as well as a time thing. But I have baked this both after leaving it overnight and after leaving it for just a few hours and I really see a difference (for the better) in the texture of the cookie when its left in the fridge overnight. People still loved it the other way too, but I really noticed it. The flavors and textures come together so much more. I really recommend refrigerating it overnight.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Funfetti Cake Batter White Chocolate Chip Cookie Cake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2521" height="721" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/funfetti_cake_batter_cookie_cake11.png" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         With that said, you cant really go wrong. This is the cookie cake that dreams are made of. So go and make it my friends! You wont regret it!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Funfetti Cake Batter White Chocolate Chip Cookie Cake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2519" height="702" src="http://cf.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/funfetti_cake_batter_cookie_cake3.png" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="zlrecipe-container-border" id="zlrecipe-container-17">
         <div class="serif zlrecipe" id="zlrecipe-container" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
          <div id="zlrecipe-innerdiv">
           <div class="item b-b">
            <div class="zlrecipe-print-link fl-r">
             <a class="butn-link" href="javascript:void(0);" onclick="zlrPrint('zlrecipe-container-17', 'http://www.lifeloveandsugar.com/wp-content/plugins/zip-recipes/'); return false" rel="nofollow" title="Print this recipe">
              Print
             </a>
            </div>
            <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
             Funfetti Cake Batter White Chocolate Chip Cookie Cake
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
            </div>
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
             <p id="zlrecipe-yield">
              Yield:
              <span itemprop="recipeYield">
               one 9-inch cookie cake
              </span>
             </p>
            </div>
            <div class="zlclear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <span id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
             <span class="bold">
              Cookie
             </span>
             <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="//stats.ziprecipes.net/piwik.php?idsite=1&amp;rec=1" style="width: 1px; height: 1px; border:0" width="1"/>
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             3/4 cup butter, softened
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup sugar
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 cup brown sugar
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 large egg
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tsp vanilla
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 box funfetti dry cake mix
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tsp cornstarch
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             2-3 tsp sprinkles
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup white chocolate chips
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
             <br/>
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
             <span class="bold">
              Buttercream
             </span>
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-12" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup butter
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-13" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup shortening
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-14" itemprop="ingredients">
             4 cups powdered sugar
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-15" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tsp vanilla extract
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-16" itemprop="ingredients">
             2-3 tbsp water
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-17" itemprop="ingredients">
             icing color of your choice
            </div>
           </span>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <span class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             1. With a mixer, cream butter and sugars together until light and fluffy.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             2. Add egg and vanilla extract. Mix well.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             3. Add dry cake mix, cornstarch and sprinkles. Mix well. Dough will be thick and sticky.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             4. Stir in white chocolate chips.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             5. Place dough in clear wrap and refrigerate overnight.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             6. When ready to bake, preheat oven to 350 degrees.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             7. Place parchment circle the size of your cake pan in bottom of pan and grease the sides. This will help you remove the cookie cake from the pan after it's baked.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             8. Spread dough evenly in cake pan.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-8" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             9. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until done. The edges should be golden. The center may not seem totally done, but it will firm and cook a little more as it cools. You could cook it for longer, but I like my cookie cake super soft and chewy.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-9" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             10. Allow cookie cake to cool completely in pan before removing. As it cools, it will firm up and you should be able to remove it from the pan without the center breaking (like I said, super moist!). Certainly if you have a spring form pan, that makes it even easier.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-10" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             <br/>
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-11" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             <span class="bold">
              Buttercream
             </span>
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-12" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             1. Beat the shortening and butter until smooth.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-13" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             2. Slowly add 3 cups of powdered sugar. Mix until combined.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-14" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             3. Mix in the vanilla extract and 1 tbsp water.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-15" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             4. Add the rest of the powdered sugar and mix until smooth. Add more water to get the right consistency.
            </div>
            <div class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-16" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             5. Add desired icing color.
            </div>
