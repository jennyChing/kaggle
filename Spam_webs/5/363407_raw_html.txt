       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Junkin around Northwest Arkansas
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-10-07T07:18:13+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 7, 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.gracegritsgarden.com/author/talya" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Talya Tate Boerner
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.gracegritsgarden.com/2014/10/junkin-around-northwest-arkansas.html#comments">
           14 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.gracegritsgarden.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Junkin-around-Northwest-Arkansas.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Junkin around Northwest Arkansas" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4016" height="550" src="http://www.gracegritsgarden.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Junkin-around-Northwest-Arkansas.jpg" width="645"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.gracegritsgarden.com/2014/10/junkin-around-northwest-arkansas.html&amp;media=http://www.gracegritsgarden.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Junkin-around-Northwest-Arkansas.jpg&amp;description=Junkin around Northwest Arkansas')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you know anything about me, you know I cant resist a good flea market, yard sale, vintagestore, dumpster dive. Northwest Arkansas is junkin heaven, Im not kidding. My personal favorites that carry everythingfrom rusted farm equipment and repurposed everythingtoBrady Bunch Seventies are
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/410Vintage" target="_blank">
          410 Vintage Market
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/FayettevillesFunkyYardsale" target="_blank">
          Fayettevilles Funky Yard Sale
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.longagoantiques.net" target="_blank">
          Long Ago Antiques
         </a>
         , and
         <a href="http://daisiesandolives.com" target="_blank">
          Daisies and Olive
         </a>
         (Prairie Grove). Of course technically Ive only been here a few weeks so Im sure there are many I have missed. I will check them all out eventually.
        </p>
        <p>
         Mostly I just browse (I swear). I enjoying lookingat how things are displayed andrepurposed.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are a few pictures from
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/pages/The-Junk-Ranch/418853844884174" target="_blank">
          The Junk Ranch
         </a>
         , a two day event held each fall and springin Prairie Grove, Arkansas. I met up with my writer friend
