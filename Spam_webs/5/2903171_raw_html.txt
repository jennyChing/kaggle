               Adams Rib

              <p>
               <span class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-alignleft">
                <img alt="joke" class="alignleft wp-image-4730 img-downsized img-imagick-downsized" data-src-2x="http://blogitudes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/17-4728-post/AdamTitle.jpg" height="300" src="http://blogitudes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/17-4728-post/AdamTitle(pp_w300_h300).jpg" width="300"/>
                <span class="pp-pinit-overlay alignleft" style="width:300px;height:300px;">
                 <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                  <img alt="" height="20" src="http://blogitudes.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                 </a>
                </span>
               </span>
               Adam was walking around the Garden of Eden feeling very lonely, so God asked Adam, What is wrong with you?
              </p>
              <p>
               Adam said, Lord, I dont have anyone to talk to.
              </p>
              <p>
               God said, Then I will give you a companion, and she will be called a woman. This person will cook for you and wash your clothes, she will always agree with every decision you make. She will bear your children and never ask you to get up in the middle of the night to take care of them. She will not nag you, and will always be the first to admit she was wrong when youve had a disagreement. She will never have a headache, and will freely give love and compassion whenever needed. She will never question your behaviour or the company you keep. She will support you and understand that you have important decisions to make throughout your life and dont have time for nonsense
              </p>
              <p>
               Adam asked God, What will this woman cost?
              </p>
              <p>
               God said, An arm and a leg
              </p>
              <p>
               Adam said, What can I get for just a rib?
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <hr/>
              <p>
              </p>
              <p>
               <span class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-aligncenter">
                <img alt="Silly Sunday" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-878" height="150" src="http://blogitudes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/silly-sunday-badge-250-transparent-150x150.png" width="150"/>
                <span class="pp-pinit-overlay aligncenter" style="width:150px;height:150px;">
                 <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                  <img alt="" height="20" src="http://blogitudes.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                 </a>
                </span>
               </span>
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <p>
               
               <strong>
                Silly Sunday
               </strong>
                is a fun blog hop hosted by
               <strong>
                Sandee
               </strong>
               at
               <strong>
                <a href="http://comedyplus.blogspot.com/" target="_blank" title="Comedy Plus">
                 Comedy Plus
                </a>
               </strong>
               each week and you can click the link to be transported there. Here is how it works:
              </p>
              <ul>
               <li>
                Blog about something funny, silly or laughable  a joke, comic, funny story, etc.
               </li>
               <li>
                To add your funny blog post in the hop, enter your name and your posts URL in the
                <strong>
                 Silly Sunday
                </strong>
                <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                 Linky
                </span>
                thats shown below.
               </li>
               <li>
                To add the Linky to your joke post showing all the
                <strong>
                 Silly Sunday
                </strong>
                joke entries for the week, visit
                <strong>
                 <a href="http://comedyplus.blogspot.com/" target="_blank" title="Comedy Plus">
                  Comedy Plus
                 </a>
                </strong>
                and grab the code that must beinserted in your text editor. Youll find it at the top of Sandees blog under the heading:
                <strong>
                 <a href="http://comedyplus.blogspot.com/p/silly-sunday-code.html" target="_blank" title="Sandee's Silly Sunday Code">
                  Silly Sunday Code
                 </a>
                </strong>
                .
                <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                 The code changes each week.
                </span>
                Simply copy and paste the code for the current week in your text editor where youd like the joke list linky to appear; hit publish and shaazam it appears.
                <span class="pp-pinit-wrap sc pp-pinit-wrap-">
                 <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" height="72" src="http://blogitudes.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;" width="72"/>
                 <span class="pp-pinit-overlay " style="width:72px;height:72px;">
                  <a class="pp-pinit-link" href="">
                   <img alt="" height="20" src="http://blogitudes.com/wp-content/themes/prophoto5/images/pinit-button2.png" width="40"/>
                  </a>
                 </span>
                </span>
               </li>
               <li>
                Hop around (from blog to blog) and enjoy the jokes, etc. posted by other
                <strong>
                 Silly Sunday
                </strong>
                participants and have a lot of fun and laughs today!
               </li>
               <li>
                Oh yeah, and dont forget to comment on the jokes that make you smile or tear up with laughter  because your comment will do the same for the blogger who included and posted the joke. 
               </li>
              </ul>
