It is mainly used inside the engines of automobiles, engineering machinery, agricultural machinery, ships, and airplanes. 
   
 The biggest turbocharger demand in China was none other than auto industry. In 2013, 4.004 million units of automotive turbochargers were sold, accounting for 59.6% of China's turbocharger sales.
