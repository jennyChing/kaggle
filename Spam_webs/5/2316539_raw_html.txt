        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Rainn Dwight K. Schrute Wilson as Xena, Warrior Princess
        </h1>
        <p class="headline_meta">
         by
         <span class="author vcard fn">
          Joey deVilla
         </span>
         on
         <abbr class="published" title="2008-07-04">
          July 4, 2008
         </abbr>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="format_text entry-content">
        <p>
         Well, heres an image thats just burned itself into my brain:
         <strong>
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rainn_Wilson">
           Rainn Wilson
          </a>
          (who plays
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dwight_Schrute">
           Dwight K. Schrute
          </a>
          in
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Office_%28U.S._TV_series%29">
           the American version of
           <cite>
            The Office
           </cite>
          </a>
          ) dressed up as
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xena">
           Xena, Warrior Princess
          </a>
          :
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align:center;">
         <img alt='Rainn \"Dwight K. Schrute\" Wilson, dressed up as \"Xena, Warrior Princess\".' height="825" src="http://globalnerdy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/rainn_wilson_as_xena.jpg" title='Rainn \"Dwight K. Schrute\" Wilson, dressed up as \"Xena, Warrior Princess\".' width="600"/>
         <br/>
         <span class="caption">
          Photo courtesy of Miss Fipi Lele.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Heres the text of the caption:
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          <strong>
           XENA: WARRIOR PRINCESS
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          I always watched
          <cite>
           Xena
          </cite>
          because it was oddly titillating, and I kept wanting to see more cleavage. I wanted it to be
          <cite>
           Baywatch
          </cite>
          with swords, but it never quite went in that direction.
         </p>
         <p>
          Neither did Wilson: Dressed as the warrior princess, he thinks he looks like the chunky girl from Heart.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         For the sake of comparison, heres Xena herself, as played by Lucy Lawless:
        </p>
        <p style="text-align:center;">
         <img alt="Xena, Warrior Princess, played by Lucy Lawless" height="974" src="http://globalnerdy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/xena.jpg" title="Xena, Warrior Princess, played by Lucy Lawless" width="445"/>
        </p>
