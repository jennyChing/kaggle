        <p>
         Ladies and Gentlemen, prepare yourselves 
         <strong>
          Destiny
         </strong>
         Beta and Collectors Edition details have been revealed by Bungie and Activision. Intwoofficial posts on the Bungie.net blog, a whole stack of information relating the forthcoming
         <strong>
          Destiny
         </strong>
         Beta has been given alongside images and information of the finally confirmed Collectors Editions.
        </p>
        <p>
         Regarding the
         <strong>
          Destiny
         </strong>
         Beta, heres the official trailer;
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/TljtC5qnb-s?rel=0" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         Showcasing some of the praise that
         <strong>
          Destiny
         </strong>
         has already received, the trailer gives us a sneak preview of what were likely to access during the Beta, which kicks off in 10 days time on July 17. As well as some of the customization options, online multiplayer and a co-operative strike mission, the trailer clearly shows off a snowy terrain, which means anyone who accesses the Beta will be able to witness a new locale  possibly the same one
         <a href="http://popgeeks.net/2014/07/04/fresh-destiny-panorama-images-and-beta-info/" title="FRESH DESTINY PANORAMA IMAGES AND BETA INFO">
          shown in the recent panoramic images
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Bungie has also kindly provided a timeline for the
         <strong>
          Destiny
         </strong>
         Beta, which can be seen below, and covers the launch dates and maintenance. The
         <em>
          PS3
         </em>
         and
         <em>
          PS4
         </em>
         <strong>
          Destiny
         </strong>
         Beta will open on July 17, whilst
         <em>
          Xbox
         </em>
         owners will need to wait until July 23 on both
         <em>
          Xbox One
         </em>
         and
         <em>
          Xbox 360
         </em>
         . Both Betas will close on the same date, July 27.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_17745" style="width: 584px">
         <a href="http://popgeeks.net/image.php?imageURL=http://popgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bnet.jpg" title="Destiny Beta and Collectors Edition Details">
          <img alt="Destiny Beta Timeline" class="size-600x250 wp-image-17745" height="250" src="http://popgeeks.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/bnet-574x250.jpg" width="574"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          <strong>
           Destiny
          </strong>
          Beta Timeline  Click to expand
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Theres also an FAQ covering a number of topics regarding the Beta on the
         <a href="http://www.bungie.net/7_The-Destiny-Beta/en/News/News?aid=11643" target="_blank" title="The Destiny Beta">
          <strong>
           Destiny
          </strong>
          Beta blog
         </a>
         over at Bungie.
        </p>
        <p>
         Not satisfied with stopping there,
         <em>
          Bungie
         </em>
         finally
         <a href="http://www.bungie.net/7_Destiny-for-Collectors/en/News/News?aid=11642" target="_blank" title="Destiny for Collectors">
          revealed Collectors Editions for
          <strong>
           Destiny
          </strong>
         </a>
          something fans have been waiting on for a long time. Theres three flavors for the Collectors Editions; Limited Edition, Ghost Edition and the Digital Guardian Edition.
        </p>
        <p>
         The
         <strong>
          Destiny
         </strong>
         Limited Edition contains;
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Limited Edition
          <strong>
           Destiny
          </strong>
          steelbook
         </li>
         <li>
          Guardian folo; contains Arms and Armament Field guide, Postcards from the Golden Age, Antique Star Chart
         </li>
         <li>
          Collectors Edition Digital Content; Unique Ghost casing, exclusive player emblem, exclusive player ship skin
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Destiny
          </strong>
          Expansion Pass
         </li>
        </ul>
