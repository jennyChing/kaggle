           <a href="http://www.madeglobal.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/phoenix_rising.jpg">
            <img alt="phoenix_rising" class=" size-medium wp-image-2161 alignleft" height="300" src="http://www.madeglobal.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/phoenix_rising-191x300.jpg" width="191"/>

          <p>
           The last hour of Anne Boleyns life
          </p>
          <p>
           Court intrigue, revenge and all the secrets of the last hour are revealed as one queen falls and another rises to take her place on destinys stage.
          </p>
          <p>
           A young Anne Boleyn arrives at the court of King Henry VIII. She is to be presented at the Shrovetide pageant, le Chteau Vert. The young and ambitious Anne has no idea that a chance encounter before the pageant will lead to her capturing the heart of the king. What begins as a distraction becomes his obsession and leads to her destruction.
          </p>
          <p>
           Love, hate, loyalty and betrayal come together in a single dramatic moment the execution of a queen. The history of England will be changed for ever.
          </p>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            Compelling, captivating and moving
           </strong>
           <br/>
           
           <em>
            Claire Ridgway
           </em>
           , author of The Fall of Anne Boleyn
          </h3>
          <h3>
           <strong>
            An interesting look at the events of May 19, 1536 from an unexpected angle!
           </strong>
           Cyndi Williamson, blogger QueenAnneBoleyn.com
