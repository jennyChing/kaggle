         <p>
          Ive probably approached Twitter marketing in fifty different ways.
         </p>
         <p>
          And in an attempt to find the best way to go forward with
          <em>
           my own
          </em>

          <em>
           start

          </em>

          </span>

          <em>
           exactly
          </em>
          what the experts are doing  day in and day out.

         <h3>
          What is Twitter?
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.commoncraft.com/Twitter">
           Twitter in Plain English
          </a>
          <br/>
          Entertaining and easy to follow, this video from Lee and Sachi LeFever of
          <em>
           Common Craft
          </em>
          show ways of understanding how Twitter works and fits into your daily workflow and lifestyle.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.webinknow.com/2009/12/48-ways-to-explain-twitter-to-skeptics.html">
           48 Ways to Explain Twitter to Skeptics
          </a>
          <br/>
          David Meerman Scott confronts the inevitable questions from the Twitter skeptics. His family asked him to explain Twitter, grilling him on Christmas. Instead of explaining himself, he tweeted the question and posted his replies.
         </p>
         <h3>
          Setting up your Twitter account
         </h3>
         <p>
          <a href="http://michaelhyatt.com/the-beginners-guide-to-twitter.html">
           The Beginners Guide to Twitter
          </a>
          <br/>
          Michael Hyatt shares a tutorial on how to set up Twitter for your cell phone. He also goes over some basics on how to tweet with third-party tools.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://news.cnet.com/newbies-guide-to-twitter/">
           Newbies Guide to Twitter
