         <p>
          Its #NationalBurgerDay! Celebrate with the most delicious-looking burgers on the planet right now.
          <span id="more-805827">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Australia isnt hosting any official National Burger Day events right now, but our friends in both the UK and the US are celebrating, so why shouldnt we?! These are the most delicious burgers ever created by mankind.
         </p>
         <p>
          All of these burgers were created by the geniuses over at
          <a href="http://pornburger.me/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://pornburger.me']);">
           Pornburger
          </a>
          !
         </p>

          <a href="http://www.lifehacker.com.au/2014/08/today-is-national-hamburger-day-celebrate-with-our-tastiest-burger-posts/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.lifehacker.com.au']);">
           Want more burger deliciousness? Check out Lifehackers Taste Tests to see what stacks up!
          </a>
         </h3>
         <h2>
          The Spread Eagle Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger1.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger1.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          This is the the Spread Eagle Burger, with a patty made of 13th Street Meats half smoke grind, Gordys pickle relish, and a DC Brau battered, french fry bun drizzled in mumbo sauce. My level of saliva has increased 550% after looking at that picture and reading the ingredients.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Mexican Muffin Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger2.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger2.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          A toasted English muffin stuffed with seasoned grass fed beef and pork sausage patty, whipped avocado cream, crispy chile ancho rubbed carnitas, purple cabbage slaw and smoky grilled pineapple butter. Get two sunny side up eggs on the side and some fries and youll have the perfect brunch.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Slow-Roasted Lamb Belly Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger3.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger3.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          Slow roasted lamb belly slathered in hot strawberry harissa sauce, balanced by a cool tarragon green goddess, grass-fed beef patty, and a schmear of fresh goat chvre. All of that inside a toasted brioche bun. Im literally salivating right now.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          The Best Burger On A Stick
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger4.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger4.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          Corn dogs? Not a fan. A corn dog-inspired burger-on-a-stick made of pickled beef frank and a slice of crispy pork belly, sandwiched between two smash-cooked beef patties, dipped in a jalapeo, whole kernel corn batter, fried, and drizzled with honey?
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          The Foie-ck A Duck Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger5.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger5.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          The Foie-ck A Duck, ladies and gentlemen: Sous vide duck breast, encrusted in a smattering of peppercorns, smothered in a foie gras cognac cream sauce, and feathered with freshly sliced summer truffle. Im drooling so much right nowreally, actual drool just came out of my mouth. I want this so badly.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          The Liquid Diet Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger6.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger6.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          A White Castle infused Bulleit Rye patty stirred with spicy pickle juice, a splash of tomato water, a dash of liquid smoke that you can suck on with a bacon straw. Now you can have burgers when you go out to dance and drink, and drinks when you go out of for brunch or lunch.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          The Bill Murray Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger7.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger7.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          It doesnt have anything to do with Bill Murray, but I want to eat it anyway: The patty is seared on the outside, medium rare on the inside, with a molten core of melted cheese and creamy sous vide egg yolk. I want to get sucked into a Groundhog Day time warp hole and eat this every day for the rest of eternity.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Wabbit Season Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger8.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger8.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          A delicious rabbit pattyground with dried fig and apricotcovered in melted sheep cheese, quick pickled red onion, mesclun salad dressed in a Dijon vinaigrette and stacked with vermouth braised pear. If youve never tried rabbit meat before this is a good way to start, its wabbit season!!
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          The Holy Cow
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger9.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger9.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          Totalling in at over 2500 calories and costing more than 40 bucks, the Holy Cow burger is basically like sandwiching an entire cow. With 17 different kinds of meat, you cant really go wrong, right?
         </p>
         <p>
          The burger was sold on Fathers Day at Reds True BBQ in Manchester and Leeds UK. Whats exactly in the beeftastic burger?
         </p>
         <p>
           Carpaccio beef and olive
          <br/>
           21-hour Texas-smoked brisket
          <br/>
           Juicy Lucy (melted cheese hidden inside a 100% steak burger)
          <br/>
           A Parmesan and Jacobs cracker-crusted pattie made of pulled oxtail and pulled ox cheek with diced slow-smoked ox heart and ox liver
          <br/>
           Jalapeo and dill pickle relish
          <br/>
           Thickly sliced mustard-pickled tongue
          <br/>
           Unholy sticky beef jus
          <br/>
           Pulled 12hr smoked beef rib
          <br/>
           Blackened Wagyu rib eye steak
          <br/>
           Beef bacon
          <br/>
           Reds Dirty sauce
          <br/>
           Chargrilled red onion ring
         </p>
         <p>
          All that in a pretzel bun. And lest anyone think they forgot about a drink and fries, the burger will come with a beef tomato consomm-based Bloody Mary with jerky shards and thrice cooked beef dripping-fried chips with a pulled beef Ragu dipping sauce. Knowing that this exists, theres no other way to celebrate Fathers Day.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Burger Pop Tarts
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger10.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger10.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          I find pop-tarts really disgusting. But these I Can Haz Pop Tart burgers must be delicious: a smashed grass-fed beef patty, with a bacon onion jam, melty cheddar cheese, in a buttery pop tart pastry. I just want to wake up every day, open my mouth, and have a conveyor belt pushing six of these down my throat.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          The Sookie Monster Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger11.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger11.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          The Sookie Monster Burger is the Southern equivalent of the Asian ramen burger: Instead of ramen as buns, it uses pan-fried pimento cheese grits. Inside: Heirloom tomato, a Louisiana hot link sausage patty, cajun spiced shrimp, smoked jalapeo, and a Sazerac roux.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          The Wake-n Bacon Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger12.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger12.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          The Wake-n-Bacon Burger tries to solve my weekly brunch dilemma: Sweet or savory? The proposal is interesting: Sandwiched between two warm and freshly glazed apple fritters, sits a maple bacon-wrapped beef patty, draped with melty cheddar cheese, and drizzled with a caffeinated maple, espresso syrup.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Bao-sers Castle Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger13.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger13.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          Behold the delicious Bao-sers Castle Burger, a hot steamy bao bun filled with a square, onion soup-braised (and seared) beef tongue patty, nested with green onion slices, quick pickle cucumber, cilantro, grilled pork belly, and a hoisin mayonnaise. The only way this thing could be more awesome is if someone were stuffing two of them in my mouth right now.
         </p>
         <p>
          I know that many of you would probably go eeeewwww about the mention of a tongue patty, but its your loss. I can assure you that its one of the most delicious and delicate meats you can eat (if you ever have the opportunity to try it, you must.) And you just cant go wrong with the rest of the ingredients. This is definitely one tasty burger.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          The Kaleesi Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger14.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger14.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          The Kaleesi Burger (*because its made with kaleget it?) It seems as delicious as the Mother of Dragons: Smokey morsels of crisped shiitake bacon, crown a regal beet gratin, smothered in Red Dragon cheese on top of a crunchy, berberre-rubbed kale patty, heirloom tomato, and slathering of sumac aioli.
         </p>
         <p>
          It doesnt have meat, but it really sounds and looks yummy. I want it.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          Cali-corn-ication Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger15.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger15.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          There are no words to express the lust and gluttony I feel about this breakfast: Pornburger calls it the Cali-corn-ication burger, but its actually eggs Benedict with a chorizo-based hollandaise, a beef patty with huitlacoche (Mexican corn smut), avocado, ancho rubbed corn, and salty pork rind crumble as crunchy seasoning. All on an English muffin. GIMME.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          The Pickleback Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger16.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger16.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          Beer-braised pork cheek, fried breaded pickle, spicy purple cabbage slaw, and cheddar-beer sauce inside a glorious pretzel bread roll. I just had lunch. I dont care. Seamless this thing into my throat, goddamnitI have my Hudson Baby Bourbon ready!
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          The Full Mounty Burger
         </h2>
         <div class="media_wrap image xlarge center">
          <a href="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger17.jpg" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://edge.alluremedia.com.au']);" rel="modal">
           <img alt="" class="size_xlarge" src="http://edge.alluremedia.com.au/m/g/2014/08/burger17.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          A ground short rib patty on a grilled onion roll, with shredded mustard greens, dill pickle slices, black garlic fries, and cheese curds glazed with a bone marrow poutine sauce.
         </p>
         <div class="clear">
         </div>
