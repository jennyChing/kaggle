     <h1>
      Amenities
     </h1>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Lobby House and Provision Co.:
      </strong>
      This is where you check in, gather provisions including beer, wine, snacks, adventure necessities and recuerdos of your Marfa adventure, and hang out. In honor of the occasional need to really get away, this is the only location at El Cosmico with wireless access to internet. Take advantage of the indoor and outdoor lounge areas, or visit our reading room in partnership with Marfa Book Company, which provides open access to approximately 600 books focused largely on art, architecture, philosophy, history, and poetry.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Bath House:
      </strong>
      Our bath house features showers, a tub, toilets, sinks and a few electrical outlets. Camping doesn't have to be dirty. We carry a variety of travel necessities in the lobby store.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Hammock Grove:
      </strong>
      relax in the shade, alone or with friends.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Kitchen:
      </strong>
      Our communal outdoor kitchen has a fridge, sink, several bar-b-cues and picnic tables.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Bikes:
      </strong>
      We rent them. Inquire in the lobby.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Hot Tubs:
      </strong>
      Wood-fired hot tubs are available to rent Wednesday through Saturday nights from 6-10pm.We light and stoke the fire, provide a pile of wood and put it out at 10pm.Call the front desk to reserve. Cost is $85 per tub.
     </p>
