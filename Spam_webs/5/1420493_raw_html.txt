           <p>
            <span class="KonaFilter image-container display-table">
             <span>
              <span class="image on-image" data-post-image="">
               <img alt="The_Price_Is_Right_ __7_000_iPhones___ _YouTube" border="0" class="lazy" data-original="https://static-ssl.businessinsider.com/image/54a87c0969bedd6715a40d7b-950-547/the_price_is_right_-__7_000_iphones___-_youtube.jpg" src="/photo/17981456.cms"/>

              </p>
             </span>
            </span>
            On the New Year's episode of "The Price Is Right" where the show revisits some of the best moments of the previous year's episodes, contestants were asked to guess the price of a
            <span>
             16GB iPhone with a one-year calling plan, 4 gigs of data and unlimited talk and text.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span>
             Off the top of your head, what would your guess be?
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span>
             Well, the contestants had these ideas: The first offered
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
             $7,500 (!), and the second player picked the price of
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
             $7,501.
            </span>
            <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
             The third contestant went with $850 and the last determined the phone package would cost $930.
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
             The actual cost?
             <span>
              $1,969.
             </span>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            Tim Cook, are you watching?
