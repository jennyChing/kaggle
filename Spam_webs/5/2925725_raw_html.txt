         <div class="author-signature">

        <div class="text content-column-right">
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: large;">
           <strong>
            IM-Creator
           </strong>
           is a new way to create your website: choose a design, insert your content and publish it to the world  all in one simple, clear and seamless process.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          We initially built IM-Creator as a solution for our friends &amp; family- they were in need of a website but the process frustrated them.
         </p>
         <p>
          It was just too complicated, expensive and confusing- they spent time &amp; money but many of them gave up even before completing the process.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          We decided to solve it.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          IM-Creator is all about making website building a simple and enjoyable experience, accessible for everyone.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Our team of web-design professionals and developers will make sure you always have the finest collection of web-designs, easiest editing environment and reliable hosting, all so youll have a great sustainable website.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          IM-Creator is powered by Google &amp; Amazon, creating robust and reliable foundations for your website.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="font-size: large;">
            How does it work?
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 1 
          </strong>
          <strong>
           Choose a Design
          </strong>
          : each month we publish dozens of new templates to accommodate your needs: portfolio websites for designers, photographers and artists, websites for business professionals, websites for restaurants and more. Check out our catalog and choose the design that will fit you best. All templates are wisely-structured and come with compelling and relevant content, so theyre pretty close to completion. Our goal is to provide you an almost-done site.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 2 
          </strong>
          <strong>
           Customize:
          </strong>
          after selecting a design, you can start inserting your content  text, pictures, videos etc. Its all very simple. Just click on the element you wish to edit and insert your content. IM-Creator will then adapt your content to the existing design, hassle-free for you.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          All of our designs come with suggested structure and pages. However, you can always change it as you wish and add/delete pages. Simple and flexible.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you encounter any issues when you create your site, click on the support tab and the IM-Creator support team and community will be there to assist you.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Step 3 
          </strong>
          <strong>
           Publish
          </strong>
          : once you finished creating your website, you can connect it to your existing domain or purchase a new one. Its a fast &amp; easy process and at the end youll have a live, easy to update and Google-friendly website (you can even have your own email!)
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://app.imcreator.com/new">
           Start now, creating the website is free.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="font-size: large;">
            IM-Creator for professionals &amp; resellers:
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          IM-Creator allows you to provide a better solution for your clients. Through our tools, you will be able to create websites faster without the need of expensive developers. In addition, you will be able to provide your clients with simple &amp; flexible tools for them to freely update the website. The tools and templates are all provided free for your use.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          With IM Creators White Label, you can offer your clients a one-stop-shop of website, hosting, email and domain  all in one place.
         </p>
         <p>
          IM Creators website builder can be fully integrated into your brand identity, from logos and colors to customized integrations. We will provide you with a branded product, ready for new clients who want to build a website.
         </p>
         <p>
          Whether you are building websites for your clients or have an online business and looking to expand into new areas, IM Creators White Label is the best way to go.
         </p>
         <p>
          In any case, you wont need to deal with HTML coding or any of the technical aspects of hooking up the site to a domain and hosting.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
