                      <h1 id="blog-post-title" itemprop="name">
                       An Infographic Explanation of Non-Rated Bollards
                      </h1>
                      <h2 itemprop="description">
                       Reliance Foundry clearly describes the value that non-rated bollards bring to sites in this straightforward infographic.
                      </h2>
                      <div id="blog-post-details">
                       <span id="blog-post-written">
                        <strong>
                         Written by:
                        </strong>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <span itemprop="articleBody">
                       <p>
                        Bollards are primarily used to prevent vehicle encroachment or to communicate intended traffic routes but many bollards do not have the ability to physically stop a vehicle.  Many are installed solely to act as visual cues or to increase a sites aesthetic value. Other bollards act as markers or as a sacrificial form of defense. Some will even bend upon impact to reduce damage to errant vehicles! The wide variety of applications that bollards are used in inevitably leads to the question 
                        <a href="/blog/non-impact-resistant-bollard-benefits">
                         what good is a bollard that cant stop a car?
                        </a>
                        
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        As one of the sites furnishing industrys foremost suppliers of bollards that are used in non-impact-resistant applications, Reliance Foundry can provide some insight. Non-rated bollards perform many useful functions and they play a key role in the design of many contemporary streetscapes. From architectural highlighting to lane delineation and providing secure bike parking, non-rated bollards do a lot more than simply add beauty to a site. In fact, they can often also help to increase safety on roadways and near pedestrian areas. Reliance Foundry has created an infographic to demonstrate the value that these bollards bring to streetscapes. View it and find out why decorative bollards may be the correct solution for your site:
                       </p>
                       <p>
                        <em>
                         Click on the image below to see a larger view:
                        </em>
                       </p>
                       <div class="content-image-center">
                        <a href="/graphics/non-impact-resistant-bollard-infographic">
                         <img alt="An infographic introduction to the uses and types of non-rated bollards." src="/site/reliance-foundry/images/infographics/infographic-non-impact-bollards-670.png" title="View Reliance Foundrys non-rated bollard infographic to learn the many useful functions they perform on contemporary streetscapes."/>
                        </a>
                       </div>
                       <p>
                        Reliance Foundry supplies a wide variety of bollards that are used to define visual boundaries and accentuate architecture. Over 50 standard models are kept is stock and are available for shipment immediately. Depending on the model selected, a wide assortment of bollard coloring and striping options are also available. Reliance Foundry has employed specialists in bollard design, production and installation since 1927 and offers some of the most competitively-priced bollards on the market. View their
                        <a href="/bollard/all-bollards">
                         online bollards catalogue
                        </a>
                        for more information.
