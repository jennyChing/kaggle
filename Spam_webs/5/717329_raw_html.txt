       <div class="article-main copy">
        <p>
         Tickets are now on sale for the starry Broadway revival of David Mamets
         <a href="http://www.broadway.com/shows/glengarry-glen-ross/">
          <i>
           Glengarry Glen Ross
          </i>
         </a>
         . Directed by Tony winner Daniel Sullivan, the Pulitzer Prize-winning play will feature Oscar winner Al Pacino, Emmy winner Bobby Cannavale, Tony nominee
         <a href="http://www.broadway.com/buzz/stars/jeremy-shamos/profile/">
          Jeremy Shamos
         </a>
         , Emmy winner
         <a href="http://www.broadway.com/buzz/stars/richard-schiff/profile/">
          Richard Schiff
         </a>
         , Tony nominee David Harbour and John C. McGinley.
         <i>
          Glengarry Glen Ross
         </i>
         will begin previews at the Schoenfeld Theatre on October 16 and open on November 11.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Set in a Chicago real estate office,
         <i>
          Glengarry Glen Ross
         </i>
         exposes the depths men will sink to in their cutthroat quest to win a sales contest. Pacino stars as over-the-hill salesman Shelly The Machine Levene, with Cannavale taking on the role of cocky salesman Ricky Roma. The show also features McGinley as Dave Moss, Harbour as John Williamson, Schiff as George Aaronow and Shamos as James Lingk.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The creative team includes set designer Eugene Lee and costume designer Jess Goldstein.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Get your tickets today to see this A-team of stars share the Broadway stage in
         <i>
          Glengarry Glen Ross
         </i>
         .
        </p>
