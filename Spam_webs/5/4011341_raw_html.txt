       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        I tried to mimic the knifeblock Wsthof 7262, and this is what I came up with. To minimize the number of dowels needed,I let all joints that arent visible be attatched with solid wood instead of dowels. The knife block holds knives vertically, 28 cm long in one direction and 24cm in the other.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        The measurements needs to be very precise for this king of build to work, a lot of holes has to line up and all should be parallel. Pallets used are heat treated pallets used for shipping plywood, with beams in beech.
       </p>
