    <h1>
     The most famous island should visit in the Gulf of Thailand
    </h1>
    <p class="post-meta">
     <span class="date">
      September 23, 2013
     </span>
     <span class="author">
      Mayuree
     </span>
     <span class="cats">
      <a href="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/category/travel-guide/thailand-travel-guide/koh-samui-travel-guide/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Koh Samui Travel Guide">
       Koh Samui Travel Guide
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/category/travel-guide/thailand-travel-guide/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Thailand Travel Guide">
       Thailand Travel Guide
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/category/travel-guide/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Travel Guide">
       Travel Guide
      </a>
     </span>
    </p>
    <h2>
     <strong>
      <a href="http://www.hoteltrip.com/">
       <img alt="Nangyuan" class="size-full wp-image-12259 alignleft" height="274" src="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/T-1Nangyuan-1.jpg" title="Koh Nangyuan" width="310"/>
      </a>
      TALAY THAI IS ONE OF THE MOST BEAUTIFUL BEACH DESTINATION IN ASIA.
     </strong>
    </h2>
    <p>
     Thailand is one country located in Southeast Asia. The beautiful land of culture, modern city and nature. Thailand has many beautiful countryside to visit, heres known for the most beautiful beach in the world also such as Phuket island, Phi Phi islands, Lipe island, Similan islands these islands are located in the Andaman coast and from the other side Koh Samui, Koh Toa and Koh Phangan are located in the Gulf of Thailand (East Coast). For visitor who looking for the beautiful beaches that not far from Bangkok, you might check it out these places.
    </p>
    <p>
     <a href="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/T-2Tao-2.jpg">
      <img alt="Koh Tao" class="size-full wp-image-12261 alignright" height="274" src="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/T-2Tao-2.jpg" title="Koh Tao" width="310"/>
     </a>
     Koh Tao Island. Tao is a small island situated on the east coast of Thailand, the island is between Surat Thani Province and Chumphon Province in southern. Tao is known for famous diving sites in the gulf side. There is also popular beach for backpacker a famous island for young adult who looking the beach with cheap
     <a href="http://www.hoteltrip.com/travelblogs/?s=accommodation+" title="Accommodation in Thailand">
      accommodation
     </a>
     to stay.
    </p>
    <p>
     Koh Nangyuan Island. Next to Koh Tao is Nangyuan island, Nangyuan is a small island and known for Talay Waek or the sea divided the island is one of the most unique place to go while visiting Tao island. Visitor can take long trail boat 10 minutes to get there from Tao island
    </p>
    <p>
     Koh Pha Ngan Island Pha Ngan is one of top destination in the east coast. There is known for famous full moon party in Thailand. Many tourists around the world looking for once lifetime experience in Pha Ngan island while they are coming to visit Thailand that Had Rin Beach is popular beach for FMP. but if you looking for quiet beach to stay the other side of Phangan island will be your choice.
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      Location:
     </strong>
     Surat Thani Province
    </p>
