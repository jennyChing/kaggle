                     <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_pVVcdosdNyY/TFGc7hZ8z9I/AAAAAAAAFYI/HXL7Jx6ECLY/s1600/4671064481_bb454cebcb_b.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                     </a>
                     A fellow contact on a social media site, shared this site with me, and it looks interesting enough to share.
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://tokyo-diy-gardening.org/">
                      Tokyo DIY Gardening.org
                     </a>
                     , is a site where anyone can post images from the city of Tokyo, organized by category and theme, to share with an online community. It's an interesting model, to try in any city, and hopefully, someone will try it for mine!
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_pVVcdosdNyY/TFGc_YPE1MI/AAAAAAAAFYM/nuA4tCn3-qg/s1600/tokyo_diy_logo_310_310.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img border="0" height="400" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_pVVcdosdNyY/TFGc_YPE1MI/AAAAAAAAFYM/nuA4tCn3-qg/s400/tokyo_diy_logo_310_310.jpg" width="400"/>
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_pVVcdosdNyY/TFGc_YPE1MI/AAAAAAAAFYM/nuA4tCn3-qg/s1600/tokyo_diy_logo_310_310.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                     </a>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_pVVcdosdNyY/TFGc5c6U95I/AAAAAAAAFYE/m3tzJivHVfw/s1600/4671689104_119e4fb49e_b.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="300" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_pVVcdosdNyY/TFGc5c6U95I/AAAAAAAAFYE/m3tzJivHVfw/s400/4671689104_119e4fb49e_b.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     Oh, those Japanese! Even a simple row of hanging plants can transform a driveway.
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_pVVcdosdNyY/TFGdB0enjlI/AAAAAAAAFYQ/X2WYQrLtlfg/s1600/elementary_school_asagao_na.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="300" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_pVVcdosdNyY/TFGdB0enjlI/AAAAAAAAFYQ/X2WYQrLtlfg/s400/elementary_school_asagao_na.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      In an elementary school, Japanese Morning Glories, (Asagao) grow as both an educational display, and, an ornamental one. No wonder so many Japanese are passionate about certain plants. They learn, early in life to appreciate such things. Culturally, the Asagao is very important to many Japanese people, and even today, the seeds of choice varieties are highly prized.
                     </div>

                      <br/>

                     Anyway, for a hot day like today, it's fun cruising online.
