							&lt;a href="http://www.iraqidinar123.com/niche/banking-finance/"&gt;Banking &amp; Finance&lt;/a&gt; Private Banks will Get U.S Dollars Twice a Month: Finance Committee				&lt;/div&gt;    		&lt;h1 class="entry-title"&gt;Private Banks will Get U.S Dollars Twice a Month: Finance Committee&lt;/h1&gt;				&lt;div class="entry-meta"&gt;			&lt;abbr title="April 8, 2015 at 9:00 pm"&gt;3 months ago&lt;/abbr&gt; by &lt;a href="http://www.iraqidinar123.com/profiles/admin/" title="Posts by Iraq123 News" rel="author"&gt;Iraq123 News&lt;/a&gt; &lt;span class="entry-comment"&gt;&lt;span&gt;Comments Off&lt;span class="screen-reader-text"&gt; on Private Banks will Get U.S Dollars Twice a Month: Finance Committee&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;		&lt;/div&gt; 			    &lt;div class="clear"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;	    	   	&lt;div class="entry"&gt;				   		    	&lt;p&gt;Iraqi Dinar 123 News:  The Iraqi Parliamentary Finance Committee member Masood Haider said that the operation of selling the U.S dollar to the private banks will take place twice a month. He pointed that this method is designed to stop money laundering problem. He further said that the Parliamentary hosted the Directors and Governor of the Iraqi Central Bank to discuss the money laundering issues and increasing price of the U.S dollar exchange rate against the Iraqi dinar. He also said that the bank managers also confirmed that they will take any possible step regarding the money laundering problem. He also added that the new method will be implemented that the U.S dollar will be sold out twice in a month among the private banks.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;He further added that Taxes and Customs fees will be imposed on the companies who deal in import goods and services against the U.S dollar. The commission has formed a committee named Pentagonal Internal Committee to deal in the money laundering case and will meet to discuss the entire case with Integrity Committee. The Parliamentary Finance Committee also discussed with the bank managers regarding maintaining the sale price of the U.S dollar. The Commission also demanded that we should develop a strong policy to control the U.S dollar exchange rate and to stop declining the Iraqi dinar value in the market.&lt;/p&gt;
