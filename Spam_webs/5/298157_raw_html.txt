           <div class="col1">
            <h2 class="page-section-title">
             Ingredients
            </h2>
            <div class="components-container">
             <section class="components-group">
              <ul class="components-list" itemprop="ingredients">
               <li class="components-item">
                Olive oil, for baking sheets
               </li>
               <li class="components-item">
                2 large eggs
               </li>
               <li class="components-item">
                3/4 cup plain dry breadcrumbs
               </li>
               <li class="components-item">
                3/4 cup finely grated Parmesan, plus 2 tablespoons for topping
               </li>
               <li class="components-item">
                1 teaspoon dried oregano
               </li>
               <li class="components-item">
                1/2 teaspoon dried basil
               </li>
               <li class="components-item">
                Coarse salt and ground pepper
               </li>
               <li class="components-item">
                2 large eggplants (2 1/2 pounds total), peeled and sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
               </li>
               <li class="components-item">
                6 cups (48 ounces) store-bought chunky tomato sauce or homemade
                <a href="/339239/chunky-tomato-sauce">
                </a>
                <a href="/339239/chunky-tomato-sauce">
                 Chunky Tomato Sauce
                </a>
               </li>
               <li class="components-item">
                1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella
               </li>
              </ul>
             </section>
            </div>
           </div>
           <!-- Directions -->
           <div class="col2">
            <h2 class="page-section-title">
             Directions
            </h2>
            <section class="directions-group">
             <ol class="directions-list">
              <li class="directions-item">
               <p class="directions-item-text">
                Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Brush 2 baking sheets with oil; set aside. In a wide, shallow bowl, whisk together eggs and 2 tablespoons water. In another bowl, combine breadcrumbs, 3/4 cup Parmesan, oregano, and basil; season with salt and pepper.
               </p>
               <div class="watch-link-container">
                <span class="icon">
                </span>
                <span class="watch-link-blurb">
                 Watch:
                </span>
                <a class="watch-link-title" href="/973728/how-make-dried-breadcrumbs">
                 How to Make Dried Breadcrumbs
                </a>
               </div>
              </li>
              <li class="directions-item">
               <p class="directions-item-text">
                Dip eggplant slices in egg mixture, letting excess drip off, then dredge in breadcrumb mixture, coating well; place on baking sheets. Bake until golden brown on bottom, 20 to 25 minutes. Turn slices; continue baking until browned on other side, 20 to 25 minutes more. Remove from oven; raise oven heat to 400 degrees.
               </p>
              </li>
              <li class="directions-item">
               <p class="directions-item-text">
                Spread 2 cups sauce in a 9-by-13-inch baking dish. Arrange half the eggplant in dish; cover with 2 cups sauce, then 1/2 cup mozzarella. Repeat with remaining eggplant, sauce, and mozzarella; sprinkle with remaining 2 tablespoons Parmesan. Bake until sauce is bubbling and cheese is melted, 15 to 20 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
               </p>
              </li>
             </ol>
            </section>
