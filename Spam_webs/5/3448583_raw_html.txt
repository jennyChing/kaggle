     <h1>
      News Anchors are at War Over Snow Covered Patio Furniture (not really)
     </h1>
     <div class="entry">
      <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
      <p>
       Kyle Clark, News Anchor at Denvers
       <a href="http://www.9news.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.9news.com/', 'KUSA-TV']);" target="_blank">
        KUSA-TV
       </a>
       , went on a rant against people posting pictures of snow covered patio furniture. Kyle wants to end the somber winter spirit and replace it with excitement over theuncelebrated season. The video gained so much attention that it was featured on Today,
       <a href="http://news.yahoo.com/blogs/trending-now/news-anchor-rants-about-viewers--photos-of-snow-covered-patios-182552330.html" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://news.yahoo.com/blogs/trending-now/news-anchor-rants-about-viewers--photos-of-snow-covered-patios-182552330.html', 'Yahoo News']);" target="_blank">
        Yahoo News
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.npr.org/blogs/thetwo-way/2013/11/27/247499306/please-send-this-man-photos-of-snow-of-your-patio-furniture" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.npr.org/blogs/thetwo-way/2013/11/27/247499306/please-send-this-man-photos-of-snow-of-your-patio-furniture', 'NPR']);" target="_blank">
        NPR
       </a>
       , and
       <a href="http://gawker.com/news-anchor-delivers-the-ultimate-takedown-of-snow-cove-1469951503" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://gawker.com/news-anchor-delivers-the-ultimate-takedown-of-snow-cove-1469951503', 'Gawker']);" target="_blank">
        Gawker
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Clarks message is to embrace the beauty of winter instead of thinking of it as a season that limits us to being locked indoors where its nice and toasty.
       <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
        Why not ski, sled, or make snow angels
       </span>
       ! Get out there and have some fun in the snow.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: left;">
       <strong>
        <em>
         Why is it that every time it snows, we whip out photos of our patio sets like were showing off baby photos of our kids.
        </em>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <center>
       <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="358" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/RK75eQ3mMxg" width="600">
       </iframe>
      </center>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <strong>
        <em>
         What a sad way to celebrate the new season.
        </em>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span id="more-8513">
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       Apparently, some people havent got the message as seen during the current winter snow storm in parts of the Northeast.
      </p>
