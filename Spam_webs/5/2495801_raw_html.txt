          My son is a senior in college, and hes getting ready to leave in about two weeks. He has one semester left, and we had been wanting to go to the gorge, which is a park in Scranton, Pennsylvania, before he went back to school so thats exactly what we did.
         </p>

          Its a beautiful place, and boy did we have a good time. The water falls were beautiful, and we enjoyed hiking the trails. The atmosphere was so lovely, and we really enjoyed our time there, and
          <em>
           I got a good work out too
          </em>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          So you need to put this on your to do list for this summer. Go to a park, get out, spend time with your loved ones.
         </p>

         </p>

          So you need to remember the reason why youre working so hard. For most of us its because we want to spend more time with family, friends and loved ones.
         </p>
         <p>
          So remember why youre working so hard, and dont neglect those people on the way up. Dont get so busy with projects that you neglect to take time off and do things with your family.
         </p>
         <p>
          Did you take a vacation or any day trips this summer?
         </p>
         <p>
          Do you have plans to make a special memory? Have you had your nose to the grindstone all summer long? I hope not!
