   <h1>
    Disco Dance Steps
   </h1>
   <p>
    <img src="../images/busstop-r.gif"/>
   </p>
   <p>
    <img src="../images/colorbar.gif"/>
   </p>
   <p>
    What you'll learn here is to dance this basic line dance,
    <br/>
   </p>
   <h1>
    the California Hustle
   </h1>
   <p>
    (
    <b>
     the Bus Stop!
    </b>
    )
    <br/>
    <br/>
    A 16 beats sequence
   </p>
   <p>
   </p>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td height="319" width="100">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="319" src="../images/busstop-b.gif" width="100"/>
     </td>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       Beginning with the right foot, take three steps backwards, right, left, 
								right.
       <br/>
       Hit the left foot against the right foot (don't do this too hard!) and 
								clap your hands.
      </p>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       Starting with the left foot, take three steps forward, left, right, left.
       <br/>
       Hit the right foot against the left foot and clap again.
      </p>
     </td>
     <td height="322" width="100">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="322" src="../images/busstop-f.gif" width="100"/>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <span class="bigbold">
    REPEAT - Once !
   </span>
   <p>
   </p>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td height="139" width="255">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="139" src="../images/busstop-l.gif" width="253"/>
     </td>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       Step to the side with the left foot.
       <br/>
       Cross the right foot behind the left foot.
       <br/>
       Step the the side with the left foot again.
       <br/>
       Hit your right foot against the left foot and clap.
      </p>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <p>
   </p>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       Step to the side with the right foot.
       <br/>
       Cross the left foot behind the right foot.
       <br/>
       Step the the side with the right foot again.
       <br/>
       Hit your left foot against the right foot and clap.
      </p>
     </td>
     <td height="139" width="255">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="139" src="../images/busstop-r.gif" width="247"/>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <p>
    <img height="50" src="../index/trans.gif" width="1"/>
   </p>
   <p>
    <br/>
    <br/>
    <br/>
    OK, Now it's time for your
    <b>
     Saturday Night Fever
    </b>
    sequence...
   </p>
   <p>
   </p>
   <h1>
    the Night Fever Line Hustle
   </h1>
   <p>
    A 46 beats sequence
   </p>
   <p>
   </p>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td height="139" width="400">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="139" src="../images/nflh-1.gif" width="400"/>
     </td>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       <b>
        Beat 1
       </b>
       , begin with the right foot into a 4 beat turn to the right.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 2
       </b>
       , continue the right turn by pivoting on your right foot while moving 
								your left foot.
       <i>
        You should now be facing in the opposite direction
       </i>
       .
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 3
       </b>
       , swing right foot behind while pivoting on the left foot.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 4
       </b>
       , bring left foot around to meet the right. You should now be facing 
								forward again.
      </p>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       <b>
        Beat 5
       </b>
       , now reverse direction - turning to the left. Step left with left 
								foot.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 6
       </b>
       , continue the left turn by pivoting on left foot while moving your 
								right foot.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 7
       </b>
       , swing left foot behind while pivoting on right.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 8
       </b>
       , bring right foot around to meet left. You have now made a full turn.
      </p>
     </td>
     <td height="139" width="400">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="139" src="../images/nflh-2.gif" width="400"/>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td height="312" width="100">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="312" src="../images/nflh-3.gif" width="100"/>
     </td>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       <b>
        Beat 9
       </b>
       , begin to walk backwards, starting with you right foot.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 10
       </b>
       , continue backwards with your left foot.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 11
       </b>
       , step back with right again.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 12
       </b>
       , stop moving backwards by holding your left foot extended in 
								front. Do not step on it.
      </p>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       <b>
        Beat 13
       </b>
       , start moving forward with your left foot.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 14
       </b>
       , continue forward with right foot.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 15
       </b>
       , froward with left.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 16
       </b>
       , finish by touching right foot in front and holding it this beat.
      </p>
     </td>
     <td height="400" width="100">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="400" src="../images/nflh-4.gif" width="100"/>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <p>
    <span class="bigbold">
     REPEAT beats 9 - 16 Once (beats 17-24) !
    </span>
    <br/>
    Except in the last beat, where you bring right foot together to left.
   </p>
   <p>
   </p>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td height="150" width="100">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="150" src="../images/nflh-5.gif" width="100"/>
     </td>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       <b>
        Beat 25
       </b>
       , kick right foot out in front.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 25
       </b>
       , quickly return right next ot left and put weight on right foot.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 26
       </b>
       , quickly move weight back to left again.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 27
       </b>
       , repeat beat 25.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 27
       </b>
       , repeat beat 25.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 28
       </b>
       , repeat beat 26.
      </p>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <p>
    <span class="bigbold">
     Stand with your both feet still
     <br/>
     during these following beats 29 - 40.
     <br/>
     Move only your hips left - right...
    </span>
   </p>
   <p>
   </p>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       <b>
        Beat 29
       </b>
       , point your right arm straight up.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 29
       </b>
       , start bringing you arm down.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 30
       </b>
       , point right arm down left, while crossing left arm over at 
								the elbow.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 31
       </b>
       , repeat beat 29.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 31
       </b>
       , repeat beat 29.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 32
       </b>
       , repeat beat 30.
      </p>
     </td>
     <td height="70" width="61">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="70" src="../images/nflh-6-7.gif" width="61"/>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <p>
    <span class="bigbold">
     REPEAT beats 29 - 32 Once (beats 33-36) !
    </span>
   </p>
   <p>
   </p>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td height="70" width="61">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="70" src="../images/nflh-6-7.gif" width="61"/>
     </td>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       <b>
        Beat 37
       </b>
       , keep standing in place moving your hips left - right... 
								and roll you arms around each other.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 38
       </b>
       , keep rolling your arms and movin your hips.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 39
       </b>
       , keep rolling your arms and movin your hips.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 40
       </b>
       , keep rolling your arms and movin your hips.
      </p>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       <b>
        Beat 41
       </b>
       , click heels together and do a bird flap with your arms.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 42
       </b>
       , repeat beat 41.
      </p>
     </td>
     <td height="120" width="100">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="120" src="../images/nflh-8.gif" width="100"/>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="25">
    <tr>
     <td height="244" width="256">
      <img align="bottom" border="0" height="244" src="../images/nflh-9.gif" width="256"/>
     </td>
     <td valign="center" width="150">
      <p>
       <b>
        Beat 43
       </b>
       , just touch right toe to the front, remaining weight on left.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 44
       </b>
       , touch right toe back.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 45
       </b>
       , touch right toe to the side and begin turning left by pivoting 
								on your left foor.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Beat 46
       </b>
       , finish your 1/4 turn by kicking right foot out in front.
      </p>
     </td>
    </tr>
   </table>
   <p>
    <span class="bigbold">
     FINISHED - Start all over again 3 more times...
    </span>
   </p>
   <p>
    Some of you might say - 'Hey, what happened to beat 47 - 48?'
    <br/>
    since we finished on beat 46 (2nd beat, in the music)
    <br/>
    but remember you should repeat this sequence 3 more times - total 4 times
    <br/>
    and end up facing front again !
    <br/>
    <br/>
