      <h1 class="pagetitle">
       Police investigating large pizza order
      </h1>
      <div class="postauthor">
       <p>
        Posted by
        <a href="http://dailycollegian.com/author/collegian-news-staff/" rel="author" title="Posts by Collegian News Staff">
         Collegian News Staff
        </a>
        on Monday, November 22, 2010 
        <a href="http://dailycollegian.com/2010/11/22/police-investigating-large-pizza-order/#respond">
         6 Comments
        </a>
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="mailmunch-forms-before-post" style="display: none !important;">
      </div>
      <p>
       The Amherst Police Departments Detective Bureau has opened an investigation into an incident which involved an order for $3,900 worth of pizza from Antonios Pizza.
      </p>
      <p>
       An order was placed late Friday night by phone call to the downtown pizza shop, but was never picked up.
      </p>
      <p>
       At 6:20 a.m. the Amherst PD received a phone call from management at Antonios notifying them of the incident.
      </p>
      <p>
       The order was placed by someone attending the Bob Dylan concert at the University of Massachusetts Mullins Center last Friday.
      </p>
      <p>
       Of the incident, Amherst Police Department Detective David Foster said that the investigation is open and has gone nowhere as of yet.
      </p>
      <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
      </div>
      <p>
       According to the Daily Hampshire Gazette, police are attempting to trace the phone number which placed the pizza order.
      </p>
      <p>
       Antonios Pizza management was not able to be reached by phone before deadline.
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
         Collegian News Staff
       </em>
      </p>
