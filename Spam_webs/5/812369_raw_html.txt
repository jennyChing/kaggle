      <p>
       Netflix has been on a roll of sorts lately. The other day they
       <a href="http://www.lazytechguys.com/news/netflix-inks-deal-with-lionsgate-uk-for-uk-and-ireland-netflix-launch/">
        announced a deal
       </a>
       with Lionsgate Uk to have their content available on the Netflix UK service and now Miramax has made a deal also. Getting Miramax will mean subscribers will get movies like Pulp Fiction.
      </p>
      <p>
       This is only two of many content providers Netflix will need to sign up to fatten up the content, but seeing how quickly these two dealshappened, I have a feeling other studios will look to expand their offerings to Netflix.
      </p>
