       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Cupcakes, Cosmos, and Choos | The Sex and the City Tour
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/author/jdombrowski/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Jennifer Dombrowski
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/sex-and-the-city-tour/#comments">
           34 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <a id="dd_start">
        </a>
        <p>
         Carrie Bradshaw said to Mr. Big,
         <em>
          Youre the
          <em>
           Chrysler Building
          </em>
          !
         </em>
         New York would be naked without it glistening in the skyline, and HBO sure felt naked to me after Sex and the City aired its final episode in 2004. I had devoured the shoes, the fashion, and the locations week after week for six years. I was in love with a city I had never even been to. Sarah Jessica Parker certainly had it right when she said in an interview
         <em>
          Manhattan is the fifth lady
         </em>
         , because of the characters, it was the one I wanted to know most. I began plotting my
         <a href="http://www.shareasale.com/r.cfm?b=584390&amp;u=937443&amp;m=51817&amp;urllink=&amp;afftrack=" target="_blank">
          Sex and the City Hotspots Tour
         </a>
         of New York
         <br/>
         <a href="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Jdombs-Travels-Chrysler-Building-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Chrysler Building " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8119" height="600" src="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Jdombs-Travels-Chrysler-Building-1.jpg" width="450"/>
         </a>
         Little more than a year after Sex and the City ended, three girls set out on their first trip to Manhattan looking for labels and, having each already found love, a love of the city. I didnt need a guide book to plan our visit; instead I watched my DVD box set for inspiration. And when I found On Location Tours also had a
         <a href="http://www.shareasale.com/r.cfm?b=584390&amp;u=937443&amp;m=51817&amp;urllink=&amp;afftrack=" target="_blank">
          Sex and the City Hotspots Tour
         </a>
         , I knew I definitely get to talk a walk in SJPs Choos. Tour booked, all I needed was my very first pair of Jimmy Choos!
        </p>
        <p>
         My mom, our friend Mel, and I boarded the comfortable bus with a group of other Sex and the City fans. Just in case you dont remember the locations youre about to visit, clips from the series and from the Sex and the City movies play on monitors throughout the bus as you traverse Manhattan from end to end.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_8116" style="width: 460px">
         <a href="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Jdombs-Travels-NYC-Sex-and-the-City-Tour-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Carrie Bradshaw's steps" class="size-full wp-image-8116" height="600" src="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Jdombs-Travels-NYC-Sex-and-the-City-Tour-1.jpg" width="450"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Posing on Carrie Bradshaws steps
         </p>
        </div>
        <div>
         I wont spoil your own Sex and the City tour by telling you about all of the locations youll get to visit, but one of my favorite highlights was posing on Carrie Bradshaws famous steps. While in the show, Carrie lived on the Upper East Side, the real-life brownstone is actually located in the West Village between Bleecker and West 4th Streets.
         <em>
          *Note that Carries brownstone is privately owned, and the tour can no longer stop for photo ops on the steps. But there are more than 40 other show and movie locations youll see on your Sex and the City tour.
         </em>
        </div>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_8117" style="width: 610px">
         <a href="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Jdombs-Travels-Magnolia-Bakery-NYC-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Magnolia Bakery" class="size-full wp-image-8117" height="450" src="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Jdombs-Travels-Magnolia-Bakery-NYC-1.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          I devoured the cupcake before I could take a picture
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         While in the West Village, we took a walk to the famous Magnolia Bakery where Carrie and Miranda devoured cupcakes while Carrie dished about her new boyfriend Aiden. Bypassing the line that usually snakes around the corner and down the street, we each got to try a delicious cupcake.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Jdombs-Travels-Bleeker-Street-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Bleecker Street" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8122" height="450" src="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Jdombs-Travels-Bleeker-Street-1.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         On a Magnolia Bakery sugar high, we had time for a little retail therapy in the chic shops along Bleecker Street. We popped in shops like Marc Jacobs, Lulu Guinness, and the surprisingly affordable Patricia Fields (SATC costume designer).
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_8118" style="width: 610px">
         <a href="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Jdombs-Travels-NYC-Sex-and-the-City-Tour-2.jpg">
          <img alt="O'Neal's Speakasy" class="size-full wp-image-8118" height="450" src="http://luxeadventuretraveler.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Jdombs-Travels-NYC-Sex-and-the-City-Tour-2.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Smiling after Cosmos at ONeals Speakasy, aka Scout
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         I was with my mom, so I
         <em>
          may
         </em>
         have blushed a little (or a whole lot) when we visited The Pleasure Chest, the sex toy shop where Charlotte bought The Rabbit. A visit to the Sharper Image, where Samantha went looking to replace her vibrator  er, I mean neck massager  probably wouldnt have been as fun. Though, had we had our fabulous pink cosmos at Onieals Speakeasy, where the scenes for Steve and Aidens bar Scout were filmed, first before hitting The Pleasure Chest, I could have at least blamed by red face on the alcohol!
        </p>
        <p>
         There were plenty of fun stops all around the city and other locations like the gallery that Charlotte worked at, Jimmy Choo, and Tiffany &amp; Co were pointed out from the bus. In all, we saw more than 40 locations from Sex and the City.
        </p>
        <p>
         And in case you were wondering, after the Sex and the City tour we did visit Jimmy Choo and I got my first ever pair of Jimmy Choos. It may have been the start of a life-long addiction, because now at least half a dozen of the lavender boxes dot the shelves of my closet!
        </p>
        <p>
