         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          You Need To Find The Unhappy Franchisee
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          Posted by
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="https://www.thefranchiseking.com/author/the-franchise-king" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             The Franchise King
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <div class="getsocial gs-inline-group">
         </div>
         <br/>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/wrhowell/224894073/" rel="nofollow" title="blood sugar problems by Robbie Howell, on Flickr">
           <img alt="blood sugar problems" height="375" src="http://farm1.staticflickr.com/80/224894073_d44168eef7.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          You need to find one-or more.
         </p>
         <p>
          <i>
           Theyre
          </i>
          the ones you need to talk to.
         </p>
         <p>
          Im going to tell you why.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Lowering Your Risk
         </h2>
         <p>
          Ifyouvebeen following along-reading
          <i>
           and using
          </i>
          my
          <a href="https://www.thefranchiseking.com/category/top-40-franchise-questions" title="best questions to ask franchisees">
           top 40
          </a>
          questions to ask current and former franchise owners, you should be proud of yourself.
         </p>
         <p>
          Youre obviously focused on increasing your chances of
          <a href="https://www.thefranchiseking.com/joel-libava-franchise-consulting-franchise-advisor-services" title="how to be a successful franchise owner">
           success as a franchise owner
          </a>
          , and lowering your risk.
         </p>
         <p>
          Right?
         </p>
         <p>
          The next step in your journey is to locate
          <b>
           unhappy franchisees
          </b>
          that are currently in the franchise system youre investigating.
         </p>
         <p>
          Theyre out there, you know.
         </p>
         <p>
          Here is question
          <b>
           #35
          </b>
          out the 40 questions that I want you to ask franchisees-and its a doozy:
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          
          <b>
           <i>
            Are you aware of any real unhappy franchisees? If so, who are they, and why do you think that theyre unhappy
           </i>
          </b>
          ?
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <script id="mNCC" language="javascript" type="text/javascript">
           // &lt;![CDATA[
  medianet_width='600';  medianet_height= '120';  medianet_crid='814041407';

          </script>
          <script id="mNSC" language="javascript" src="http://contextual.media.net/nmedianet.js?cid=8CUH5HA6K" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h2>
          You
          <em>
           Must
          </em>
          Open The Unhappy Franchisee Can Of Worms
         </h2>
         <p>
          You need to ask question #35.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           <i>
            Even if you dont want to
           </i>
          </b>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          I knowI know.
         </p>
         <p>
          Youre getting
          <i>
           so
          </i>
          close. You may be only weeks away from actually becoming
          <a href="http://money.usnews.com/money/personal-finance/articles/2013/02/27/reasons-you-should-buy-a-franchise-and-reasons-you-shouldnt" target="_blank" title="own a business">
           the owner of your own business
          </a>
          .
          <i>
           You may not have to work for anyone else ever again
          </i>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          How great would
          <i>
           that
          </i>
          be?
         </p>
         <p>
          Answer: It
          <i>
           is
          </i>
          great!
         </p>
         <p>
          But dont blow it now.
         </p>
         <p>
          Dont allow your emotions to get the best of you at this stage in the process
         </p>
         <p>
          Only focus on the facts.
         </p>
         <p>
          You
          <i>
           must
          </i>
          keep your excitement in check for a little while longer.
         </p>
         <p>
          Okay?
         </p>
         <p>
          Its really crucial for you to seek-out
          <b>
           unhappy franchisees
          </b>
          of the franchise business opportunity that youre investigating
          <i>
           before
          </i>
          you commit to becoming an owner.
         </p>
         <p>
          Heres why:
         </p>
         <p>
          You want to know whats
          <i>
           really
          </i>
          going on with the franchise that youre looking into.
         </p>
         <p>
          And, its the
          <i>
           unhappy
          </i>
          ones that are going to tell you
          <i>
           everything
          </i>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Happy Franchisees
         </h2>
         <p>
          Its great to find happy franchise owners; the more you find-the better.
         </p>
         <p>
          But, when you
          <a href="http://www.cnbc.com/id/46115353" target="_blank" title="cnbc story on franchises with joel libava">
           talk to franchisees
          </a>
          that
          <i>
           are
          </i>
          happy-really happy with their businesses, youre going to find that they talk mostly about
          <i>
           their
          </i>
          franchise businesses.
         </p>
         <p>
          And, while its great to hear happy and successful franchisees share their excitement about
          <i>
           their
          </i>
          individual franchise businesses, its not enough for
          <i>
           you
          </i>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Thats because you are a
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/40765798@N00/2395724941/" rel="nofollow" title="Nikko Bulldog by sabianmaggy, on Flickr">
           <img alt="Nikko Bulldog" height="332" src="http://farm3.staticflickr.com/2294/2395724941_2b6908079a.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          And, Bulldogs dont give up until they get what they want.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Be The Bulldog
          </b>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Thats because when it comes to making a huge decision-like buying a franchise,
          <i>
           you need all the information that you can get
          </i>
          . You need more. You need to find out about the franchisoryou need to find out how they roll.
         </p>
         <p>

         <h2>
          Unhappy Franchisees
         </h2>
         <p>
          You need to find out about
          <i>
           The
          </i>
          business, not only
          <i>
           their
          </i>
          businesses.
         </p>
         <p>
          The
          <a href="http://www.unhappyfranchisee.com/mac-tools-distributor/" target="_blank" title="check out unhappyfranchisees.com">
           unhappy franchisees
          </a>
          that you contact
          <i>
           will
          </i>
          tell you all you need to know about
          <i>
           The
          </i>
          business.
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Theyll tell you what the culture at franchise headquarters is like
          </li>
          <li>
           Theyll tell you how responsive the team at headquarters really is
          </li>
          <li>
           Theyll tell you about the mistakes that have been made by management
          </li>
          <li>
           Theyll tell you everything; the good
           <i>
            and
