         Miss Kansas is an Army National Guard soldier (who just re-upped) whos an expert shot with an M-16 and an avid hunter. Interesting that the clip shows the Sunflower State siren shooting an arrow rather than a bullet, but Im sure shes no shrinking violet when it comes to talking about her marksmanship. Score one for the home team. So to speak. And if any of this is sexist its all sexist. Mkay?
