        <h3>
         Some expect the Carlisle Truck Nationals in Pennsylvania to be just another truck show, but those people are way off! This show was a heart pounding display of power, precision and the best in truck engineering &amp; design.
        </h3>
        <p>
         If youve never been to an event hosted at the Carlisle Fairgrounds in Carlisle, PA, you couldnt even begin to fathom the experience youre missing. This year, the Carlisle Truck Nationals 2013 was an especially exciting treat for truck lovers. Thousands entered the event this year and crowds of spectators came out in near record-breaking numbers.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Truck Nationals at Carlisle offers something special for every member of the family. Specialty operations included the Kids @ Carlisle activity center and womens Oasis. People were also able to find nearly any truck part, accessory, or special memorabilia piece imaginable at the huge midway and swap meet. Additionally, there were many celebrities and special guests in attendance and contests were nothing shy of jaw dropping.
        </p>
        <p>
         The real stars of the show were the astonishing trucks that showed up. These incredible vehicles came in every variety truck you could possibly imagine  from the lowest of the low riders to the skyscraper monster trucks and everything in between.
        </p>
        <p>
         A great example of what makes a car show so different is this show of brute strength.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-HDGr]" href="http://rallyways.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/carlisle_truck_nationals_2013_1.jpg">
          <img alt="Carlisle Truck Nationals Challenger" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3647" height="493" src="http://rallyways.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/carlisle_truck_nationals_2013_1-740x493.jpg" width="740"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Where else can you see a pristine Dodge Ram diesel with a flatbed towing a show quality Challenger as an attraction at a car show?
        </p>
        <p>
         For those who wanted a taste of the classic meets old school performance, this Chevy truck was a treat.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-HDGr]" href="http://rallyways.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/carlisle_truck_nationals_2013_2.jpg">
          <img alt="Vintage Truck" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3648" height="493" src="http://rallyways.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/carlisle_truck_nationals_2013_2-740x493.jpg" width="740"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         From a distance, this vintage truck may look like your average car show entry, until you get a little closer and notice its massive supercharger and drag wheels &amp; tires.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats not to say that there wasnt plenty of original condition or restored trucks to be found at Carlisle this year.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-HDGr]" href="http://rallyways.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/carlisle_truck_nationals_2013_3.jpg">
          <img alt="Carlisle Truck Nationals Classic Trucks" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3649" height="493" src="http://rallyways.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/carlisle_truck_nationals_2013_3-740x493.jpg" width="740"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         There was an entry there to represent practically every era, make, and model ever made.
        </p>
        <p>
         For those more interested in modern trucks that display the spectacular leaps and bounds made in engineering and design, they were certainly not disappointed.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-HDGr]" href="http://rallyways.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/carlisle_truck_nationals_2013_4.jpg">
          <img alt="Chevy SSR" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3650" height="493" src="http://rallyways.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/carlisle_truck_nationals_2013_4-740x493.jpg" width="740"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This Chevy SSR showed off the modernization of trucks but also collides with muscle car heritage. Features like the flame graphics and a custom grille are an ode to old school style on this modern truck.
        </p>
        <p>
         Diesel trucks were a huge attraction at the event in 2013.
        </p>

         Monster trucks were out putting on a car crushing show as well as minding their manners as they toted spectators around for a ride. Can you name some of the crushed cars? Hint: only merica! (Except for the Bimmer.)

         It wasnt all about reaching for the sky when it came to highly customized trucks.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-HDGr]" href="http://rallyways.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/carlisle_truck_nationals_2013_10.jpg">

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Slammed mini trucks like this one were testing ground clearance and even competing in a limbo contest.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a data-rel="lightbox[gallery-HDGr]" href="http://rallyways.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/carlisle_truck_nationals_2013_11.jpg">
          <img alt="Airbrushed Dodge Ram" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-3659" height="495" src="http://rallyways.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/carlisle_truck_nationals_2013_11-330x495.jpg" width="330"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         It looked more like an art show when this stunning Dodge Ram rolled into its parking space.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you didnt make it to the Carlisle Truck Nationals 2013, make sure you do yourself a favor and dont miss it again in 2014. People travel from far and wide to attend the event for a reason. It is one of the premier truck events in the United States with amazing support from both fans and sponsors. Whats more, you dont have to necessarily be a truck fan yourself to appreciate some of these fine machines.
        </p>
