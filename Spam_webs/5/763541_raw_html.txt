       is set to plus the shipboard experience once again on its very first cruise ship  the
       <strong>
        <em>
         <a href="http://disneycruise.disney.go.com/disney-magic-enhancements/" target="_blank">
          Disney Magic
         </a>
         
        </em>
       </strong>
       with a dry-dock refitting in September 2013. Taking design cues from her youngest fleet-mates  the
       <em>
        Disney Dream
       </em>
       and
       <em>
        Disney Fantasy 
       </em>
       the 1998-launched
       <em>
        Disney Magic
       </em>
       will inherit many of the lines most recent favorites as well as sport entirely new features (fully illustrated below  click on images to enlarge).
      </p>
      <h3>
       The Opening Scene
      </h3>
      <p>
       Storytelling is at the heart of everything we do, says Joe Lanzisero, senior creative vice president for Walt Disney Imagineering. Walt Disney was a master storyteller and knew how to engage peoples emotions into stories. Our job is to take what we learned from Walt and apply it to the experiences on board our ships. Said storytelling begins onboard at the redesigned art deco atrium.
      </p>
      <p>
       Further opening up the space with the removal of one of the mirrored staircases, the new asymmetrical layout will still spotlight the signature bronze Helmsman Mickey statue, polished with a fresh luster and now offset among the new carpeting below and chandelier above.Lanzisero describes the updated atrium look aselegant art deco, inspired by elements of the sea.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_01_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5584" height="640" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_01_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x640.jpg" title="disney_magic_01_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        The Atrium
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <h3>
       Inspired Favorites
      </h3>
      <p>
       The most dramatic enhancement will be the exterior addition of the
       <strong>
        AquaDunk
       </strong>
       (rendered in the header above and below)  a brand new water slide and thrilling variant of the popular AquaDuck on the
       <em>
        DisneyDream
       </em>
       and
       <em>
        DisneyFantasy
       </em>
       . Contrasting the raft ride that doubles back around those ships more leisurely, the AquaDunk will be a three-deck body slide with a near vertical drop that launches riders into a translucent helix stretching 20-feet over the side of the ship.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_02_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5583" height="426" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_02_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x426.jpg" title="disney_magic_02_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        AquaDunk
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       The poolside
       <strong>
        AquaLab
       </strong>
       , first introduced on the
       <em>
        Disney Fantasy
       </em>
       , will also be coming aboard with the conversion of the existing Mickeys Pool to include new interactivepop jets, geysers and bubblers. Also, the current kiddie slide will be replaced with the higher and longer
       <strong>
        Twist n Spout
       </strong>
       with over 250-feet of run and three helixes.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_03_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5582" height="426" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_03_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x426.jpg" title="disney_magic_03_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        AquaLab and Twist n Spout Water Slide
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       The
       <em>
        Disney Fantasy
       </em>
       will share her once-exclusive
       <strong>
        Animation Magic
       </strong>
       dinner show with the
       <em>
        Disney Magic
       </em>
       at the redesigned
       <strong>
        Animators Palate
       </strong>
       restaurant. Here diners will draw their own character before being treated to dessert and the sights of their creation magically animated alongside legendary Disney characters. The addition of Remy from
       <em>
        Ratatouille
       </em>
       and Rapunzel from
       <em>
        Tangled
       </em>
       , plus new larger high-def displays, will enhance the classic black-and-white to color extravaganza of Drawn to Magic.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_04_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5581" height="361" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_04_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x361.jpg" title="disney_magic_04_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Animators Palate
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       The existing Topsider Buffet will be transformed into
       <strong>
        Cabanas
       </strong>
       with an Australian theme inspired by the film
       <em>
        Finding Nemo
       </em>
       that is sure to feature a flock of seagulls. A welcome improvement here will be the expansion of the indoor dining spacewith an additional 3,400 square feet, bringing the total to 9,460 square feet with 455 seats.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_05_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5580" height="439" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_05_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x439.jpg" title="disney_magic_05_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Cabanas
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Andys Room
       </strong>
       , a kid favorite from the two newest ships, will comprise a new play structure made to look like an oversized bed with a life-sized Mr. Potato Head, Hamm piggy bank and Slinky Dog slide, and
       <strong>
        Pixie Hollow
       </strong>
       will similarly make its way onboard with costumes for dressing up like Tinker Bells fairy friends in a fanciful forest.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_06_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5579" height="320" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_06_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x320.jpg" title="disney_magic_06_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Andys Room
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       The popular Irish pub from the
       <em>
        Disney Fantasy
       </em>
       ,
       <strong>
        OGills
       </strong>
       , will establish itself as a new adults-only offering on the
       <em>
        Disney Magic
       </em>
       as well and will no doubt serve up its famous house Irish cream liqueur. The space will also doubles as a sports bar with a live televised feed of athletic events.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_07_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5578" height="426" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_07_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x426.jpg" title="disney_magic_07_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        OGills
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       Also from the new ships for both kids and adults, Studio Sea will be repurposed into
       <strong>
        D Lounge
       </strong>
       , multi-use entertainment venue and host tofamily-friendly games, shows, dances and activities.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_08_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5585" height="426" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_08_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x426.jpg" title="disney_magic_08_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        D Lounge
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <h3>
       Entirely New Features
      </h3>
      <p>
       Not everything new to the
       <em>
        Disney Magic
       </em>
       will be a carryover from the rest of the fleet. For instance, she will be the first Disney ship to feature famed comic book heroes onboard. Disneys Oceaneer Club will house
       <strong>
        Marvels Avengers Academy
       </strong>
       . Here, young crime-fighters will be transported to a high-tech command post used by The Avengers for special missions and operations training, according to the cruise line. Displays includeIron Mans suit, Captain Americas shield and Thors hammer.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_09_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5571" height="288" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_09_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x288.jpg" title="disney_magic_09_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Marvels Avengers Academy
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">
       <script async="" src="http://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
       </script>
       <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-1298107188482027" data-ad-slot="4900507406" style="display:inline-block;width:468px;height:15px">
       </ins>
       <script>
        (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
       </script>
      </div>
      <p>
       The
       <strong>
        Mickey Mouse Club
       </strong>
       also joins the ship, internationally inspired by a new series of two-dimensional short cartoons that places the worlds most famous mouse in contemporary settings like Paris, New York, Tokyo and Venice, says the cruise line. The new cartoons, which borrow from the style of Mickey Mouses character in the 1930s, will air on The Disney Channel and Disney.com.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_10_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5572" height="294" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_10_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x294.jpg" title="disney_magic_10_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Mickey Mouse Club
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       Tots may be too young to enjoy the new AquaLab and AquaDunk, but they will get their own new
       <strong>
        Nephews Splash Zone
       </strong>
       , formerlyMickeys Splash Zone, described as a splash-a-second space dedicated to interactive fun and starring Donald Ducks mischievous nephews, Huey, Dewey and Louie. The area features all sorts of water play with pop jets, squirting figures of Donald Ducks nephews and plenty of high seas hijinks theyve created for Unca Donald.
      </p>
      <p>
       Parrot Cay will give way to the new
       <strong>
        Cariocas
       </strong>
       , a restaurant inspired by Brazil andnamed after Donald Ducks parrot friend in
       <em>
        The Three Caballeros
       </em>
       . Disney explains, during the day, buffet meals feature a festival theme before night falls and the atmosphere transforms into an after-hours scene of Rio de Janiero, with city-side windows softly illuminated and lanterns flickering overhead. A scenery scrim is drawn to conceal the buffet and kitchen areas for dinner service, providing a more elegant dining locale.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_11_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5574" height="481" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_11_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x481.jpg" title="disney_magic_11_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Cariocas
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       The adults-only area of the ship will be rethemed from Beat Street to
       <strong>
        After Hours
       </strong>
       , with the included Diversions becoming
       <strong>
        OGills
       </strong>
       as described above. Other clubs to see changes are Rockin Bar and Sessions which will become
       <strong>
        Fathoms
       </strong>
       and
       <strong>
        Keys
       </strong>
       respectively.Fathomswill be a celebration of the sea with special effects, lighting and sound that transform the environment throughout the evenings, and Keys will be a piano lounge with a relaxed vibe.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_12_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5573" height="342" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_12_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x342.jpg" title="disney_magic_12_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Fathoms
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <h3>
       Further Enhancements
      </h3>
      <p>
       With the existing pirate theme of Disneys Oceaneer Club being retooled,
       <strong>
        Disneys Oceaneer Lab
       </strong>
       will take over the reigns with a new buccaneer workshop and animators studio for kids includingNavigator Simulators for practicing maritime maneuvers. For the even younger,Flounders Reef Nursery will be rethemed into
       <strong>
        Its a Small World Nursery
       </strong>
       as also found on the
       <em>
        Disney Dream
       </em>
       and
       <em>
        Disney Fantasy
       </em>
       .
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_13_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5575" height="322" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_13_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x322.jpg" title="disney_magic_13_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Disneys Oceaneer Lab
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       As for dining, the cruise line touts that
       <strong>
        Palo
       </strong>
       will be getting a makeover with stylish wood fixtures, glass artwork panels, new stone floors, decorative lighting and hand-tufted carpet. Red and white striped poles reminiscent of those found throughout the canals in Venice are a design feature. A rich interior color palette  inspired by Venetian artwork  complements gourmet dishes that please guests palates.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_14_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5576" height="480" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_14_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x480.jpg" title="disney_magic_14_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Palo
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       Another area onboard to receive improvements is
       <strong>
        Senses Spa &amp; Salon
       </strong>
       . The cruise line expresses, redesigned with a sleek and contemporary style, this haven of personal harmony provides an unmatched ocean-view environment. The size of the spa is being expanded by 725 square feet, to a total size of nearly 11,500 square feet.A new
       <strong>
        Smile Spa
       </strong>
       offers teeth whitening services, a new barbershop offers services for the gentlemen and the new
       <strong>
        Chill Spa
       </strong>
       provides treatments especially for teens 13 to 17 years of age.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center; font-size: 0.9em;">
       <a href="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_15_copyright_disney_cruise_line.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[slides]">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-5577" height="360" src="http://www.popularcruising.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/disney_magic_15_copyright_disney_cruise_line-640x360.jpg" title="disney_magic_15_copyright_disney_cruise_line" width="640"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <em>
        Senses Spa &amp; Salon
        <br/>
        (Copyright Disney Cruise Line)
       </em>
      </p>
      <p>
       Lastly, staterooms onboard are slated to be redesigned with new nautical decor and better use of space with raised beds for improved storage. The updated
       <em>
        Disney Magic
       </em>
       will reemerge with sailings starting on October 20, 2013 from Miami, Florida.
      </p>
      <p>
       To further explore whats in store for the
       <strong>
        <em>
         Disney Magic
        </em>
       </strong>
       , check out the great video below and
       <a href="http://disneycruise.disney.go.com/disney-magic-enhancements/" target="_blank">
        visit
        <strong>
         Disney Cruise Line
        </strong>
        s website here
       </a>
       .
      </p>
