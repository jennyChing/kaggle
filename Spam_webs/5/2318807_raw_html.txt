      <div id="features">
       The AlienBees B1600 is a powerful,
       <span class="bold">
        self-contained studio flash unit
       </span>
       with
       <span class="bold">
        adjustable output
       </span>
       from full power (640 Ws) down to 1/32nd of the total power (20 Ws).  This flashpower is adjusted on the back control panel with a slide fader, capable of adjusting in whole f-stop increments and everywhere in between.  When adjusted from a higher to a lower setting, the unit will automatically dump the excess power for accurate output.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       With precision voltage regulated circuits supplying consistent output, the B1600 boasts true
       <span class="bold">
        what-you-see-is-what-you-get modeling lamp accuracy
       </span>
       . The modeling lamp can be set to full brightness, turned completely off, or set to track the power changes.  Furthermore, the standard 150 Watt modeling lamp may be used as a recycle indicator, turning itself off when the unit is recycling, and coming back on to let you know when the unit is fully recycled and you are ready to shoot again.  Ready for heavy-duty, all-day shooting, the AlienBees have built-in cooling fans to keep up with their fast recycle.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       The B1600 arrives with a
       <span class="bold">
        sync cord
       </span>
       allowing you to connect your camera to the unit to trigger the flash.  The unit additionally has a
       <span class="bold">
        built-in slave tripper
       </span>

       <span class="bold">
        Connecting the flash unit to your camera:
       </span>
       Each AlienBees flash unit arrives with the
       <a href="absc.php">
        ABSC standard 15-foot sync cord
       </a>
       for hardwired connection to your camera. The cord connects to the flash unit on the units back control panel (1/8-inch sync plug), then connects to your cameras PC-sync outlet. This outlet may be labeled PC sync, PC terminal, X-sync socket, external flash connector, or flash sync terminal  the label varies by camera manufacturer. If your specific camera does not have a PC outlet, we offer a
       <a href="hsa.php">
        hot shoe adapter
       </a>
       that fits in the standard ISO hot shoe of most cameras to enable PC-connection.
