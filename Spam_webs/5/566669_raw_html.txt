                </p>
                <ul>
                 <li>
                  <i>
                   <a class="mw-redirect" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mars_Trilogy" rel="nofollow" title="Mars Trilogy">
                    The Mars Trilogy
                   </a>
                  </i>
                  , a series of novels (
                  <i>
                   Red Mars
                  </i>
                  ,
                  <i>
                   Green Mars
                  </i>
                  , and
                  <i>
                   Blue Mars
                  </i>
                  ) by
                  <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kim_Stanley_Robinson" rel="nofollow" title="Kim Stanley Robinson">
                   Kim Stanley Robinson
                  </a>
                  , depicts space elevators on Earth and on Mars, the cables of which are made of
                  <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carbon_nanotube" rel="nofollow" title="Carbon nanotube">
                   carbon nanotubes
                  </a>
                  manufactured on asteroids and lowered into each planet's atmosphere, using the asteroid as a counterweight.
                  <i>
                   Red Mars
                  </i>
                  depicts what happens when a cable is cut at the asteroid anchor point.
                 </li>
                </ul>

                </div>
