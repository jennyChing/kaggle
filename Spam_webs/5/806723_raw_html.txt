         <p>
          A mash-up of Nine Inch Nails Head Like A Hole and Carly Rae Jepsens 
          <a href="http://www.stereogum.com/1784150/carly-rae-jepsen-i-really-like-you/mp3s/">
           I Really Like You
          </a>
           has no right to be any good  especially after someones previous attempt to mash up the same NIN song with Jepsens Call Me Maybe
          <a href="http://www.stereogum.com/1279942/heres-the-call-me-maybe-x-nine-inch-nails-mashup-of-your-nightmares/mp3s/">
           failed so spectacularly
          </a>
          . A combination that turns those two songs into a Trent Reznor/Carly Rae duet should suck completely, absolutely, and without reservation. An amateur remix that turns those two songs into a story about resistant loner Trent Reznor finding love? That should be an abomination unto God. And yet. A random genius named YITT has found a way to turn those two songs into one song
          <em>
           absolutely seamlessly
          </em>
          , his secret weapon being the fact that Head Like A Hole was always a pretty great pop song. Experience the absurdity below.
         </p>
         <div class="mainembed videoembed mainembed-full_width align-center no-image">
          <div class="video-embed-code">

           </iframe>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p>
          Be honest: You now have I Really Like You, and not Head Like A Hole, stuck in your head.
         </p>
