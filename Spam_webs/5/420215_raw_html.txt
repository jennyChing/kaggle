       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/">
        Beautiful DIY Shelving Made Easy
       </a>
      </h1>
     </header>
     <section class="entry entry-single" style="clear: both;">
      <div class="undertitle">
       <div id="sticky">

      <p>
       I hope you all have been enjoying my
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/profiles/erikawest/">
        posts last week inspired by Design Camp
       </a>
       , but hope you didnt miss my DIY adventures too much. In case you did, I decided to start the week on a DIY note, with some beautiful DIY shelves. Lets be honest: most of us have way too much stuff and always seem to be running out of storage space (guilty!). But crafting your own marvelous, unique shelving doesnt have to be difficult. These creators of these 11 DIY shelving projects show us how easy it can be to give your home some functional, yet attractive, storage solutions.
      </p>
      <h3>
       Not Your Average Wood Shelves
      </h3>
      <p>
       Inspired by all of the amazing character and charm of her farmhouse,
       <a href="http://www.firsthomedreams.com/2012/07/building-bathroom-storage.html">
        First Home Dreams
       </a>
       simply used a bit of ingenuity and a cordless drill to create some lovely DIY bathroom shelves. By flipping the way the brackets were hung, she created a whole new vintage vibe for her shelving.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/vintage-inspired-diy-bathroom-shelves/">
        <img alt="Vintage Inspired DIY Bathroom Shelves Beautiful DIY Shelving Made Easy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62780" height="887" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/Vintage-Inspired-DIY-Bathroom-Shelves.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       Worn leather and reclaimed wood are, in my opinion, a match made in heaven! Our next DIY shelf from
       <a href="http://www.designsponge.com/2012/08/diy-project-recycled-leather-wood-shelf.html">
        Design Sponge
       </a>
       used two thrift store belts, two pieces of recycled wood, and a handful of nails to make this masculine, rustic shelving unit.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/diy-leather-belt-shelves/">
        <img alt="DIY Leather Belt Shelves Beautiful DIY Shelving Made Easy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62776" height="683" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DIY-Leather-Belt-Shelves.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       Husband and wife team
       <a href="http://www.subtletakeover.com/blog/2012/6/14/honeycomb-shelves-20.html">
        Subtle Takeover
       </a>
       and
       <a href="http://www.arrowandapple.com/">
        Arrow &amp; Apple
       </a>
       built these breathtaking DIY honeycomb shelves to showcase some of their favorite items. Made out of fence planks and several drywall screws, Im sure the carpenters rule of Measure twice, cut once! certainly applied here with all of those angles!
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/diy-wood-honeycomb-shelves/">
        <img alt="DIY Wood Honeycomb Shelves" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62779" height="900" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DIY-Wood-Honeycomb-Shelves.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h3>
       Coastal Crates and Cubist Boxes
      </h3>
      <p>
       We have all seen the basic crate shelves made from craft store crates (heck, I even
       <a href="http://www.ourcozycubbyhole.com/2012/06/11/flea-market-finds/">
        made some for my house
       </a>
       ). However,
       <a href="http://www.thriftyandchic.com/2012/04/crate-shelves.html">
        Thrifty and Chic
       </a>
       takes this idea to a whole new level by hand crafting her crates to custom sizes using recycled wood to perfectly fit the space. With a coat of white paint and some gorgeous styling, she created an elegant, coastal display wall in her dining room.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/diy-crate-shelves/">
        <img alt="DIY Crate Shelves" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62771" height="754" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DIY-Crate-Shelves.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       Our next DIY shelving project was created by
       <a href="http://livethemma.ikea.se/inspiration/diy-lekfull-hyllning-till-prant">
        Life At Home
       </a>
       (a blog by IKEA) using their Prant storage boxes, some pastel paints, and a few binder clips to attach everything together, resulting in a Cubism-inspired masterpiece. Not only is this fully customizable, but you can change it any time youd like thanks to the binder clips! Unfortunately, it looks like the Prant boxes are no longer available at Ikea, but any basic, thin wood boxes will do. You could even make your own!
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/diy-ikea-box-shelves/">
        <img alt="DIY IKEA Box Shelves" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62772" height="800" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DIY-IKEA-Box-Shelves.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h3>
       Lovely Ladder Shelves
      </h3>
      <p>
       How adorable is this next project!? By creating new planks to rest on the rungs of an old ladder, which was updated with a bright coat of paint,
       <a href="http://www.abeautifulmess.com/2013/02/nesting-ladder-display-makeover.html">
        A Beautiful Mess
       </a>
       created a one-of-a-kind display shelf for her plants.
      </p>
      <div class="yarpp-related">
       <div id="seemore">
        <h3>
         SEE ALSO
        </h3>
        <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2014-05-30/corner-shelves-space-saving-designs/" id="seemoreclick">
         <div id="thumb">
          <img alt="Corner shelves in the bath" class="attachment-180x120 wp-post-image" height="120" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Corner-shelves-in-the-bath-254x180.jpg" width="169"/>
         </div>
         <div id="titlu">
          Want To Maximize Your Space? Try Some Corner Shelving!
         </div>
        </a>
       </div>
      </div>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/diy-ladder-shelves/">
        <img alt="DIY Ladder Shelves" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62773" height="932" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DIY-Ladder-Shelves.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Love the ladder look but dont have an old ladder laying around? Well, not to worry because
       <a href="http://lazylizonless.blogspot.com/2012/05/i-just-cant-stop-building-stuffs-for-my.html">
        Lazy Liz on Less
       </a>
       shows us how to make eye-catching leaning ladder shelves from scratch. Inspired by plan from
       <a href="http://ana-white.com/2010/07/plans/leaning-wall-shelf">
        Ana White
       </a>
       , these shelves add a sleek, simple, (dare I say?) sexy look to any space.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/diy-ladder-shelves2/">
        <img alt="DIY Ladder Shelves2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62774" height="474" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DIY-Ladder-Shelves2.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h3>
       Re-Purposed Objects
      </h3>
      <p>
       I love DIY projects that are super simple. How is this one for you? Step one: saw pallet in half. Step 2: put on wall. BOOM! Done. Okay, so you have to do a bit more jury-rigging to get a third shelf out of a pallet and may want to add some stain, but you get my point. These stunningly rustic pallet shelves by
       <a href="http://www.iloveyoumorethancarrots.com/2013/01/diy-rustic-pallet-shelves.html">
        I Love You More Than Carrots
       </a>
       are an easy way to create a focal point for showing off your favorite items and photos.
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/diy-pallet-shelves/">
        <img alt="DIY Pallet Shelves" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62777" height="399" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DIY-Pallet-Shelves.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       (Insert obligatory branch project here. After all, this is a post by Erika.) True to form, I have featured shelving unit made of birch branches. This amazing shelf, designed by
       <a href="http://www.kokoarch.com">
        Koko Architecture + Design
       </a>
       and featured on
       <a href="http://designawards.wordpress.com/2010/05/27/shiro-shelf/">
        International Design Awards
       </a>
       , isnt a DIY project per se, but I think it can serve as great inspiration for one! Upgrade existing shelves (or use some lumber or reclaimed wood to make some) by adding a few natural birch branches in between for support and woodsy character.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/diy-birch-branch-shelves/">
        <img alt="DIY Birch Branch Shelves" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62770" height="474" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DIY-Birch-Branch-Shelves.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       Our next DIY shelf, created by
       <a href="http://www.byggstudio.com/" target="_blank">
        Byggstudio
       </a>
       and featured on Gardenista, was made with our little sprout friends in mind. Created with several wooden trellises cut to the size of the window opening and hung from cord at different levels, this shelf is a plants dream, as it can soak up all of the sunshine pouring through your windows! It is also a humans dream because it is easy to make (lattice would work and is very inexpensive), looks awesome, and helps bring some green indoors.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/diy-lattice-window-plant-shelf/">
        <img alt="DIY Lattice Window Plant Shelf" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62775" height="900" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DIY-Lattice-Window-Plant-Shelf.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
       <br/>
       The final DIY project for the day is a real looker! Remember when I mentioned above that my favorite DIY projects involve sawing something in half and slapping it on the wall? Well, this one (roughly) follows those rules too and adds a quirky, vintage appeal to your room. This magnificent suitcase shelving wall was created by
       <a href="http://www.kinassauer.com/blogs/blog/7331176-suitcase-shelves-diy">
        Ki Nassauer
       </a>
       for her living room, and I have never seen a unique focal point quite like it! Giving the space a worldly, jet-setting vibe, it is absolutely perfect for displaying your favorite souvenirs from your travels.
      </p>
      <div class="adcontainer" id="adcontainer-15">
      </div>
      <script>
       $(document).ready(function() {lazyLoadAd.addSlot('adcontainer-15'); });
      </script>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-05-13/diy-shelves/diy-suitcase-shelves/">
        <img alt="DIY Suitcase Shelves" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-62778" height="713" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/DIY-Suitcase-Shelves.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Well, that really was easy! Promise me that, after all of this beautiful DIY inspiration, you wont ever have plain, old, boring shelves ever again? Have a wonderful week and happy shelf-making!
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        Which is your favorite DIY shelf? Do you have any unique, easy DIY ideas for shelving? Please share it with us in the comments below!
