         <div class="thecontent" itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           <img alt="whose-line.jpg" height="268" src="http://unrealitymag.bcmediagroup.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/03/whose-line.jpg" width="472"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           I watched about forty of these clips while compiling this list, and now Im quite literally sore from laughing so hard. Im not sure if moment for moment there has ever been a funnier show than
           <em>
            Whose Line
           </em>
           , and it features some of the most talented people ever to appear on television. Improv is hard, but Ryan, Colin and Wayne make it look effortless. Here are the best clips I could find, but believe me, youll spend all day looking for others.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span id="more-3770">
           </span>
           <strong>
            15) Question Only  International Flight
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b-D4NkTnaoo
          </p>
          <p>
           In my opinion this is one of the hardest skits to pull off.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            14) Sound Effects  Jedi
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_YjRPlRqB0Y
          </p>
          <p>
           Most of the best sound effects clips are when its audience members are doing it, but Ryan is just excellent in this one.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            13) Dubbing  Rival Mobsters
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t6yaqgvi2Dk
          </p>
          <p>
           The girl is actually pretty competent for most of it.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            12) Worlds Worst  Things to Say on a Date
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cx4o1gMRGHY
          </p>
          <p>
           Its always funny when they rip on Drew.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            11) Scenes from a Hat  What Not to do in an Earthquake, Bad Bra Names
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GvEX2tUjBrg
          </p>
          <p>
           Im not sure boobs should ever be called Milk Duds.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            10) If You Know What I Mean  Two Office Guys
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u-BfQ7hJ6Po
          </p>
          <p>
           This is before Thats what she said was invented.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            9) Scenes from a Hat  Things You Can Say to Your Dog, but Not Your Girlfriend
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A4UoV2dm_OY
          </p>
          <p>
           You can imagine how this goes.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            8 ) Sound Effects  Tarzan and Jane
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ScAdbXfj2-8
          </p>
          <p>
           Audience members do try their best.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            7) Irish Drinking Song  Wrong Name
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aqg6BNSUG5Q
          </p>
          <p>
           Sometimes its funny when they **** up.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            6) Songstyles  Female Bodybuilder
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jCIak-w0DOc
          </p>
          <p>
           Absolutely terrifying, but awesome.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            5) Green Screen  Attack of the Colin
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9y01_flVpI
          </p>
          <p>
           It all started with a rogue bald joke.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            4) Sound Effects  Defending the Queen
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KwxAcRiTm1w
          </p>
          <p>
           Old people make the best audience participants.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            3) Hollywood Director  Robin Williams
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=11lRFvq8miQ
          </p>
          <p>
           Robin Williams was clearly built for this kind of thing.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            2) Sound Effects  Cops and Ducks
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XSs7NCWp6kA
          </p>
          <p>
           This might literally be dangerous to your health. I dont think laughing this much is healthy.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            1) Living Scenery  Richard Simmons
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tQ_lXJTaT5g
          </p>
          <p>
           And if youre not dead yet, this might do it. Probably one of the videos ever to be put on the internet. No joke.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            BONUS
           </strong>
           : Bloopers
          </p>
          <p>
           httpv://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m2KoSiF4Naw
          </p>

            And now were going to our next game **** me silly
