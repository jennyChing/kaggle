story is sad, because her owner, who was also a rescuer, passed away suddenly,

only overwhelmed by the sudden loss of his beloved wife, but now had to care for
her beloved animals left behind. Luckily, her rescue friends stepped in to
relocate some of the cats and dogs into foster homes, like the one England now
has. However,
she needs a forever home, like AFHs dogs. She is not a dog-lover, but knows
how to keep her distance. She is not fond of her fellow cats, but has agreed to
eat, sleep, and use the litter box, as well as be held and petted by her
foster. Because of her age, young
children would not be recommended.
