      <section class="post entry" id="post-96901">
       <div class="post-inner">
        <header>
         <p class="post-date">
          <span class="post-month">

          </span>

          </span>
         </p>
         <h1 class="post-title">
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/">
           Give Yourself a Glitter Ombre Manicure to Support Your Team on Thanksgiving
          </a>
         </h1>
         <p class="post-author">
          By
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/about/" rel="author">
           Karen
          </a>
         </p>
         <p class="post-categories">
          Published in:
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/category/makeup-tipshow-to/" rel="category tag">
           Makeup Tips/How To
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/category/nails/" rel="category tag">
           Nails
          </a>
         </p>
         <ul class="post-actions">
          <li>
           <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/#comments">
            Leave a comment 
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/#share">
            Share this post 
           </a>
          </li>
         </ul>
        </header>
        <div class="post-content">
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-96904 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/" height="419" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/how-to-do-a-glittery-ombre-manicure-0.jpg" title="how to do a glittery ombre manicure 0" width="540"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          I stopped by a UPS Store to mail a package last week and received an unexpected compliment from the guy working the counter. He pointed at my
          <a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/what-are-you-wearing-on-your-nails-17/">
           glittery red and gold ombre manicure
          </a>
          and said, Atta girl! Youre wearing 49er colors.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Go Niners!" class="alignright size-full wp-image-96953 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/" height="214" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/go-niners-helmet.jpg" title="Go Niners!" width="270"/>
          He said that his wife, a long-time Niners fan, would probably love to wear a mani like that on game day and asked if it was hard to do.
         </p>
         <!--Ad Injection:top-->
         <div style="margin-top:0px;padding-top:10px;padding-bottom:10px;">
          <center>
           <div style="margin:0 0 0 -15px">
            <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
            </script>
            <!-- 300x250 MBB Inside Posts -->
            <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-6078240274198210" data-ad-slot="3843154284" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
            </ins>
            <script>
             (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
            </script>
           </div>
          </center>
         </div>
         <p>
          Not at all, I replied, and I walked him through the process as he prepped my package.
         </p>
         <p>
          FYI: Talking nails with a big, burly UPS guy is weirdly entertaining. About halfway through the process, his eyes took on the same glazed, confused look that El Hub gets when I try to explain why it makes perfect sense for me to own 15 pairs of black shoes
         </p>
         <p>
          Thats when I suggested that the guy have his wife visit MBB.
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          If youre a sports fan, Thanksgiving also means football!  a fun excuse to 1) paint your nails in support of your team and 2) give everyone around you something sparkly to look at while they heap mashed potatoes on their plate (YUM!).
         </p>
         <p>
          To match the 49ers, I used red and gold shades from China Glaze and Lippmann.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-96902 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/" height="417" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/how-to-do-a-glittery-ombre-manicure-bottles.jpg" title="how-to-do-a-glittery-ombre-manicure-bottles" width="540"/>
          <br/>
          <em>
           Polishes from the left: China Glaze Champagne Bubbles, Lippmann Do Ya Think Im Sexy?, and China Glaze Ring in the Red
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          So, Mr. UPS guy, this ones for you and your wife!
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          Kickoff and the start of the first quarter
         </h3>
         <p>
          Lets start by applying a clear base coat to even out the surface of the nail, and then brush on three layers of your background color, in my case golden Champagne Bubbles by China Glaze.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-96903 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/" height="416" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/how-to-do-a-glittery-ombre-manicure-1.jpg" title="how-to-do-a-glittery-ombre-manicure-1" width="540"/>
          <br/>
          <span id="more-96901">
          </span>
         </p>
         <h3>
          End of the first half
         </h3>
         <p>
          Now, to start creating the glittery ombre effect, apply a polish with large pieces of glitter in it. Here Im using Deborah Lippmanns Do Ya Think Im Sexy? (a red), and I begin at the tips and work about halfway down the nail toward the cuticle. That way, most of the glitter gets concentrated near the tips.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-96908 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/" height="407" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/how-to-do-a-glittery-ombre-manicure-2.jpg" title="how to do a glittery ombre manicure 2" width="540"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          The third quarter
         </h3>
         <p>
          Add several layers of the same polish at the tips (just at the tips) to deposit even more glitter and intensify the effect.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-96907 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/" height="389" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/how-to-do-a-glittery-ombre-manicure-3.jpg" title="how to do a glittery ombre manicure 3" width="540"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          The fourth quarter
         </h3>
         <p>
          To fill in the sparse areas and give some depth to the effect, dab a glittery polish that contains very tiny flecks of glitter in the middle of the nail. Here Im using Ring in the Red by China Glaze.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignone size-full wp-image-96906 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/" height="375" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/how-to-do-a-glittery-ombre-manicure-4.jpg" title="how to do a glittery ombre manicure 4" width="540"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          The 2-minute warning!
         </h3>
         <p>
          Finally, apply your favorite shiny topcoat along the entire nail to smooth and protect your work.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-96905 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/" height="431" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/how-to-do-a-glittery-ombre-manicure-5b.jpg" title="how to do a glittery ombre manicure 5b" width="540"/>
         </p>
         <h3>
          Game over
         </h3>
         <p>
          Go team!
          <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/nails/give-yourself-a-glitter-ombre-manicure-to-support-your-team-on-thanksgiving/" src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png"/>
          Now, would someone please pass the pecan pie so that I may stuff myself silly?
         </p>
         <p>
          Your friendly neighborhood beauty addict,
         </p>
         <p>
          Karen
         </p>
