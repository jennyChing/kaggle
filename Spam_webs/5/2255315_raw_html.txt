       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Christmas Ornament Pattern Play
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-11-17T04:13:40+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 17, 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/author/julienixon/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Julie
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/christmas-ornament-pattern-play/#comments">
           2 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         When I saw these adorable mini Christmas Ornaments at Target, I knew theyd turn into a blog post, one way or another. Theyre just so sparkly and UNBREAKABLE! The kiddos couldnt help but touch!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Christmas-Ornament-Pattern-Play.jpg">
          <img alt="Christmas Ornament Pattern Play" class="size-large wp-image-1522 aligncenter" height="1024" src="//www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Christmas-Ornament-Pattern-Play-318x1024.jpg" width="318"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          This post contains affiliate links that help support my family. Read more about that
          <a href="http://www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/disclosure" target="_blank" title="Disclosure">
           here
          </a>
          .
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          If you cant find the ornaments at Target, you can find a
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00P6J547E/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B00P6J547E&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=thfohsho-20&amp;linkId=3QZUR7XWOV24EYUZ">
           similar set here
          </a>
          <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="//ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=thfohsho-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B00P6J547E" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Weve been working on
         <a href="http://www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/2014/11/felt-turkey-pattern-play.html" target="_blank" title="Felt Turkey Pattern Play">
          patterns
         </a>
         in our tot-school. I decided to continue the theme with a Christmas twist. I set out two clear egg cartons and showed Loopy a simple pattern across the top. Then he went about copying it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_3240-e1416185330521.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_3240" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1498" height="1024" src="//www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_3240-e1416185330521-768x1024.jpg" width="768"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I put two egg cartons side by side so the patterns could be more complicate.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_3243-e1416185533104.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_3243" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1501" height="1024" src="//www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_3243-e1416185533104-768x1024.jpg" width="768"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This pattern play can also be simplified so younger kids can sort colors. Eventually, I just let the boys play with them and make their own creations. I was surprised how long it held their interests.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_3261-e1416185669536.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_3261" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1519" height="1024" src="//www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_3261-e1416185669536-768x1024.jpg" width="768"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ive found that toddlers love ANYTHING that they can put in and take out over and over again.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_3247-e1416190172704.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_3247" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1505" height="1024" src="//www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_3247-e1416190172704-768x1024.jpg" width="768"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_3242-e1416184376647.jpg">
          <img alt="IMG_3242" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1500" height="1024" src="//www.mymundaneandmiraculouslife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/IMG_3242-e1416184376647-768x1024.jpg" width="768"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Have a holly jolly Christmas!
         </strong>
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         Cant get enough of the holidays? Follow me on Pinterest. I have a whole board dedicated to it!
        </div>
