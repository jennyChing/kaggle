            Vegan Magic

         <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden field-wrapper body field">
          <p>
           The world of vegan meat substitutions just got a little weirder with
           <a href="http://veganmagic.cc/" target="_blank">
            Magic Vegan Bacon Grease
           </a>
           . Made of coconut oil, soy protein, sea salt, maple syrup, pepper, onion, garlic, torula yeast and smoke flavors, the fat can be used to fry or bake and may even be eaten straight on toast. In fact, its creators have an entire Tumblr dedicated to the greases many uses in recipes ranging from
           <a href="http://vegan-magic.tumblr.com/post/96380144207/magic-vegan-southern-collards-the-first-thing-i" target="_blank">
            collard greens
           </a>
           to
           <a href="http://vegan-magic.tumblr.com/post/105098633282/ppk-chai-latte-snickerdoodles-made-with-a-coconog" target="_blank">
            cookies
           </a>
           , basically any application that would benefit from "smoky, rich, bacony flavor.
          </p>
          <p>
           The product hails from Ontario Canada and can be orderedonlineor picked up in one of the American health food stories found on this
           <a href="http://veganmagic.cc/where.html" target="_blank">
            map
           </a>
           .
          </p>
