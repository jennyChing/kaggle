          <h1 class="entry-title">
           My Top 5 Mothers To Avoid  Or Would You?
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <div class="meta-info-wrap">
           <span class="sep posted-on">
            Posted on
           </span>
           <a href="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/my-top-5-mothers-to-avoid/" rel="bookmark" title="6:20 am">

           </a>

            <span class="sep">
             by
            </span>
            <span class="author vcard">
             <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Mark">

        <div class="entry-content cf">
         <p>
          <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif; font-size: 18pt;">
           Another year and another mothers day celebration but really isnt every day mothers day?
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Right, now that Ive got your attention lets get down to the nitty gritty. On the 2nd Sunday in May each year it is only fitting and right that we celebrate all things which are good and great about our mothers. I would suspect that all of the mothers who somehow fall under the FULLHALFGLASS umbrella will be treated to various combinations of breakfast, lunch and dinner all the while admiring the vast array of chocolates, and flannelette products showered upon them.
         </p>
         <p>
          But spare a thought for some of the kiddies out there whose mother perhaps didnt come from the top shelf. Those mothers who maybe didnt quite grasp the concept of mothering as it is understood in the traditional way. Heres my mothers day list of my top 5 mothers to avoid  or would you? Some of them I suspect would be a real hoot.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
           <strong>
            5. Morticia Addams
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/my-top-5-mothers-to-avoid-2.jpg">
           <img alt="my top 5 mothers to avoid 2" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-7000" height="194" src="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/my-top-5-mothers-to-avoid-2.jpg" width="259"/>
          </a>
          Matriarch of the Addams family she was the glue that held the very fabric of the macarbe and bizzare family together. Mother to Pugley, Wednesday and the often forgotten Pubert she was at her happiest when her children were at there saddest. With her consummate command of French she would often send her poor husband Gomez into a blithering mess. Ruler of her domain she was not a mother to be messed with.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
           <strong>
            4. Peggy Bundy
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/my-top-5-mothers-to-avoid-4.jpg">
           <img alt="my top 5 mothers to avoid 4" class="alignleft wp-image-6998" height="267" src="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/my-top-5-mothers-to-avoid-4.jpg" width="246"/>
          </a>
          Sarcastic, Lazy and I suspect a heavy closet drinker. She is the nemesis of one of the greatest man to have graced our screens ever  Al Bundy. As a role model for motherhood I secretly suspect that there are a few of the fairer sex out there who chant 
          <em>
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <strong>
             You go girlfriend!
            </strong>
           </span>
          </em>
           in response to some of her parenting skills.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
           <strong>
            3. Carol Brady
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/my-top-5-mothers-to-avoid-3.jpg">
           <img alt="my top 5 mothers to avoid 3" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-6999" height="195" src="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/my-top-5-mothers-to-avoid-3.jpg" width="259"/>
          </a>
          Bed fellow of Mike Brady (
          <em>
           although I wonder if there was any under the sheet action
          </em>
          ) her mothering of the blended Brady family took saccharin sweet nausea inducing homespun love and understanding to a diabetic coma type level never before seen and thankfully never again seen after. What other mother could ever hope to rise to her unachievable lofty standards. Fortunately for the kiddies of the world I can think of none.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
           <strong>
            2.
           </strong>
          </span>
          <span style="font-weight: normal; color: #666666;">
           <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
            <strong>
             Monica Liebenow
            </strong>
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/my-top-5-mothers-to-avoid-1.jpg">
           <img alt="my top 5 mothers to avoid 1" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-7001" height="200" src="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/my-top-5-mothers-to-avoid-1.jpg" width="200"/>
          </a>
          I tread carefully here for this mother is both admired and ridiculed in equal proportions. As the creator of
          <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
           The My Family stickers
          </span>
          which have been adorning the rear windows of many of our cars she has coped a fair few bits of choice criticism. I am not a fan of these stick creature but then again  each to their own and in reality I dont have many friends who like them either. Still she must be doing something right given the amazing success of her company.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="font-size: 18pt;">
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/my-top-5-mothers-to-avoid-5.jpg">
             <img alt="my top 5 mothers to avoid 5" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-6997" height="225" src="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/my-top-5-mothers-to-avoid-5.jpg" width="225"/>
            </a>
            1. Janine Smurf Cody
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Who? I hear you ask. What if I said the mother of Pope, Darren and Craig and Grandmother of J. Still struggling? Ok then, think Jacki Weaver in her award winning role as Matriarch of the Melbourne Crime Family in the classic Australian Movie
          <strong>
           Animal Kingdom
          </strong>
          . This was not a warm and fuzzy mum in the true sense or was she. Fiercely loyal to her boys she would do anything to protect them and isnt that what Mums everywhere have done forever?
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           How about you?
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Can you add to my top 5 mothers to avoid list?
          </em>
         </p>
