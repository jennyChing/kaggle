           <h2>
            The New Buzzword: MultiCloud!!
           </h2>
           <p class="meta">
            <i class="icon-time">
            </i>
            Published on 06 Jan 2014  |
            <i class="icon-user">
            </i>
            by
            <a>
             <em>
              Mike Shermon
             </em>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h3>
            <span style="color: #ff6600;">
             Technology takes a new shape everyday. Good old sayingisntit?
            </span>
           </h3>
           <p>
            But it fits so well with
            <strong>
             every day technological scenario
            </strong>
            . As everything has gone onto the cloud-based technology, now the businesses need something that can handle this complex cloud combination and help them manage business well.
           </p>
           <p>
            Well! The
            <strong>
             solution is here!
            </strong>
            This all new technology is named as the MultiCloud or
            <a href="http://consolepark.com/services/cloud-storage/" target="_blank">
             <strong>
              Multiple Cloud Management
             </strong>
            </a>
            . The new concept is more complex than even a hybrid cloud. It makes a mix of two or more clouds that will manage on-demand public as well as private clouds.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="ConsolePark- Cloud Computing" class="size-medium wp-image-799 aligncenter" height="139" src="http://consolepark.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/ConsolePark-Cloud-Computing-300x139.jpg" title="ConsolePark- Cloud Computing" width="300"/>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            The basic key to
            <strong>
             multi-cloud ma
            </strong>
            <strong style="line-height: 1.5em;">
             nagem
            </strong>
            <strong style="line-height: 1.5em;">
             ent
            </strong>
            <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
             is to incorporate the multiple public and private clouds in the cloud computing architecture. This mainly works on three concepts:
            </span>
           </p>
           <ul class="list Arrow Width TextClod" style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
            <li class="Snd" style="width: 100%;">
             Data Security and management is given priority given the distribution and complexity of data over it.
            </li>
            <li class="Snd" style="width: 100%;">
             Data and other technical specifications need to be adjusted owing to its movable and immovable parts.
            </li>
            <li class="Snd" style="width: 100%;">
             Only a proficient service provider can add value to the business as well as fulfill the business requirements.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            Moreover, you need to understand that while
            <strong>
             integrating complicated distributed systems
            </strong>
