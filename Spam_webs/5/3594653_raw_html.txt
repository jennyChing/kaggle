            <div id="artisan_lap_siding">
             <h1>
              <a href="http://www.creoconstruction.com/services/siding/hardie-plank/">
               <img alt="James Hardy Artisan Siding" class="aligncenter wp-image-4514 size-full" height="550" src="http://www.creoconstruction.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/deep-shadow.jpg" width="505"/>
              </a>
             </h1>
             <h1>
              Artisan
              <sup>
               
              </sup>
              Lap Siding
             </h1>
             <p>
              Artisan
              <sup>
               
              </sup>
              lap siding is a premium exterior product that combines elegant aesthetics with the unrivaled durability of
              <a href="http://www.creoconstruction.com/services/siding/hardie-plank/">
               James Hardie
              </a>
              fiber cement siding. This unique product line opens the door to unlimited possibilities giving architects, builders and homeowners alike the opportunity to express themselves through the highest standards in design.
             </p>
             <p>
              Details
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               5/8 thick for distinctively deep shadow lines
              </li>
              <li>
               Resists damage from insects, moisture and extreme weather
              </li>
              <li>
               Ability to miter corners
              </li>
              <li>
               Fire resistant
              </li>
             </ul>
             <h3>
              Introducing Artisan
              <sup>
               
              </sup>
              Accent Trim
             </h3>
             <p>
              Our most innovative trim technology ever, Artisan Accent Trim delivers the finishing touch to your complete Artisan exterior. At 1 1/2 thick, Artisan Accent Trim shares the same performance attributes as all
              <a href="http://www.creoconstruction.com/services/siding/hardie-plank/">
               James Hardie siding
              </a>
              , while maintaining long-lasting beauty.
             </p>
             <p>
              Use architectural-grade Artisan Lap on your commercial buildings and the difference is plain to see. Utilizing the thickest material on the marketArtisan Lap creates
              <a href="http://www.jameshardiecommercial.com/_img/deep-shadow.jpg">
               deeply defined shadow lines
              </a>
              for a premium aesthetic. Additionally, our patented DuraTech technology provides a product with superior structural integrity. Our unique
              <a href="http://www.jameshardiecommercial.com/_img/tongue_groove.jpg">
               tongue &amp; groove
              </a>
              design means tighter tolerances for a precise fit and finish. Artisan lap comes with a 30-year limited warranty.
             </p>
            </div>
