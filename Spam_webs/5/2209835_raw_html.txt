        </a>
       </h2>
      </header>
      <div class="content_wrap">
       <p>
        <a class="colorbox" data-rel="post_gallery" href="http://theworstthingsforsale.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/demeter-crayon.jpg">
         <img alt="demeter-crayon" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2654" height="338" src="http://theworstthingsforsale.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/demeter-crayon.jpg" width="225"/>

       </p>
       <p>
        Demeter perfumes generally smell exactly like whats on the bottle.
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00154ZJGK/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00154ZJGK&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=natdee-20">
         Chocolate Chip Cookie
        </a>
        smells eerily like a freshly-baked cookie.

        </a>

        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000HGYI3W/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000HGYI3W&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=natdee-20">

       </p>
       <p>
        But Ive always been mystified why youd want to put on their
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000C1UE4O/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000C1UE4O&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=natdee-20">
         Dirt
        </a>
        fragrance. It smells like dirt from the ground. Or
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00CCJLMJU/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00CCJLMJU&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=natdee-20">
         Cannabis Flowers.
        </a>
        Or
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001U3TJNK/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B001U3TJNK&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=natdee-20">
         Suntan Lotion.
        </a>
        Or
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00121RZYK/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00121RZYK&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=natdee-20">
         Poison Ivy.
        </a>

        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B005PPROOK/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B005PPROOK&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=natdee-20">
         Play-Doh
        </a>
        scent. And
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B005PPRRRE/ref=as_li_ss_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B005PPRRRE&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=natdee-20">
