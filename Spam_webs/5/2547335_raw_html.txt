      <div class="post-content">
       <p>
        It looks like
        <em>
         Big Brother
        </em>
        is getting some love at the 2015 Reality TV Awards, as they were nominated twice! Now it is your chance to get your favorite show in the winners circle, as the results are up to you! Want to see
        <em>
         Big Brother
        </em>
        win Best Competition Show? Well, get details in my
        <em>
         Big Brother
        </em>
        2015 spoilers below!
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://cdn.bigbigbrother.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Big-Brother-2014-Spoilers-Week-4-Power-of-Veto-Results.jpg">
         <img alt="Big Brother 2014 Spoilers - Week 4 Power of Veto Results" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-14756" height="459" src="http://cdn.bigbigbrother.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Big-Brother-2014-Spoilers-Week-4-Power-of-Veto-Results-480x459.jpg" width="480"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        So, the
        <a href="http://www.realitytelevisionawards.com/" target="_blank">
         Reality TV Awards
        </a>
        were voted on by people in the industry to get it down to the final nominees in each category. From there, the voting is opened up to the public and they get to vote for their favorites to win the awards, which will take place on May 13 at The Avalon in Hollywood.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span id="more-17220">
        </span>
        <br/>
        For
        <em>
         Big Brother
        </em>
        16, it looks like they ended up with two nominees, which were:
       </p>
       <ol>
        <li>
         Best Competition Show
        </li>
        <li>
         Best Creative Challenge Contestants have to roll an oversized dice from within and land the dice on the correct number facing up to answer the trivia question.
        </li>
       </ol>
       <p>
        If you dont remember that competition, it was the PoV early in the season and they were in the big dice and flopping around.
        <strong>
         Jocasta
        </strong>
        was sick and could not compete and
        <strong>
         Donny
        </strong>
        ended up winning it and came into the house and gave it to her, which caused some crazy crying sounds to come out of her!
       </p>
       <p>
        It looks like
        <strong>
         Zach, Devin
        </strong>
        and
        <strong>
         Brittany
        </strong>
        are signed up to be in attendance and more names to come.As far as voting goes, you can vote every day and voting ends on April 30!

         <a href="http://www.realitytelevisionawards.com/vote/" target="_blank">
