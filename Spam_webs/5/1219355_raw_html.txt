         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          How I Learned Everything I Know About Business And Marketing FOR FREE!
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-12-17T20:10:29+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
           December 17, 2014
          </time>
          By
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://embolden.co/author/coreyvf/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             Corey Ferreira
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="entry-comments-link">
           <a href="http://embolden.co/how-i-learned-everything-i-know-about-business-and-marketing-for-free/#comments">
            <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="1460 http://embolden.co/?p=1460">
             2 Comments
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <div id="triberr_endorsement">
         </div>
         <p>
          <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="390" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/YONIrr5rNsI?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="640">
           </iframe>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          When youre just starting out in business or as an entrepreneur there is a lot to learn.
         </p>
         <p>
          How to market yourself and your business, how to format and structure your websites, howto get traffic, how to make money and a ton of other things. Its a lot to learn.
         </p>
         <p>
          The shortcut to learning things that will help you learn all of these things if to pay for course and seminars as opposed to doing years of trial and error and testing.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its a great idea to invest into yourself and your business with your time and your money. Nobody came out of the womb knowing how to create a landing page or create Facebook ads. However, this can get expensive and become very time consuming.
         </p>
         <h2>
          What if I told you nearly everything I learned about business and marketing, I learned for free?
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="what if i told you" class="alignnone wp-image-1454 size-full" height="465" src="http://embolden.co/wp-content/uploads/680a.jpg" width="425"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Im not talking about doing a simple Google search to learn things, either.
         </p>
         <p>
          I learned about business and marketing from the best there are at it and I did it for free.
         </p>
         <p>
          Granted, I still spend money and time investing in courses and seminars to make me a better entrepreneur and help my business. However, a lot of what I do here and apply into my blog and my businesses I learned for free.
         </p>
         <p>
          How?
         </p>
         <p>
          The first thing you need to do is find the best people in your niche and industry.
         </p>
         <p>
          In my case, I found the best internet marketers.
         </p>
         <p>
          The first point is you need to emulate them and see what theyre doing and how they do it. Dont take everything at face-value. You need to reverse engineer it and study it.
         </p>
         <p>
          For example, when I started out trying to make money online,
          <strong>
           I created an email on Gmail just to opt into all these successful internet marketer and entrepreneur mailing lists
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Not because I was interested in what they would offer me or try to sell me, but because I wanted to learn about email marketing.
         </p>
         <p>
          I would check this email everyday and study it. I would look at the subjects they used, the length of the emails, how frequently they would email me, what kinds of emails they would send, the language they used, the images they used, the call to actions, everything. I looked for patterns and similar trends that all the email marketers used. I took this and applied it to my own list.
         </p>
         <p>
          I didnt just look at an email and see it as just an email.
         </p>
         <p>
          One of my professors in college once told me that he loved getting junk mail at his house. He would collect all the pamphlets and flyers he would get every week and study them. Look at the colors, pictures and languages of the ones that caught his eye the most or made him want to buy what they were selling the most. And this is what you should be doing when youre opted into a list.
         </p>
         <p>
          This isnt just for learning about email marketing, either.
         </p>
         <p>
          Take a look at the most successful internet marketers and look at the layout and language of their websites. Is there something consistent about them all? Thats how I learned about pop-ups, sticky bars, squeeze pages, sales page copy, etc.
         </p>
         <p>
          I would take a look at their sales pages for their most successful products and study those as well.
         </p>
         <p>
          You have to understand, what the best in your industry do isnt by accident. Theres a reason why things are the way they are or why things are structured in a certain why. You just need to be analytical and ask why it is done that way and what the benefit would be so you could apply it.
         </p>
         <p>
          So the moral of all this is to opt into as many mailing lists as possible, follow as many internet marketers as possible and everytime you see a Facebook as that catches your eye, analyze that as well. You can learn a lot about business and marketing by just looking at how others or the best in your industry do it.
         </p>
