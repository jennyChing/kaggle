          <span class="st_sharethis_vcount" st_title="How To Make Resin Pendants" st_url="http://www.lilblueboo.com/2012/10/how-to-make-resin-pendants.html">
          </span>
          <span class="st_email_vcount" st_title="How To Make Resin Pendants" st_url="http://www.lilblueboo.com/2012/10/how-to-make-resin-pendants.html">
          </span>
          <span class="st_facebook_vcount" st_title="How To Make Resin Pendants" st_url="http://www.lilblueboo.com/2012/10/how-to-make-resin-pendants.html">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/DIY-RESIN-PENDANTS.jpg">
           <img alt="How to Make DIY Resin Pendants via lilblueboo.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27495" height="1054" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/DIY-RESIN-PENDANTS.jpg" title="How to Make DIY Resin Pendants via lilblueboo.com" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Heres a fun, easy way to make resin jewelry without the scary chemical mixing! First I bought these
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002VMDBJ2/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B002VMDBJ2&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=liblbo-20" target="_blank">
           <em>
            <strong>
             silver plated bezels:
            </strong>
           </em>
          </a>
          <span id="more-27476">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6798.jpg">
           <img alt="Where to Buy Silver DIY Pendants for Resin or Epoxy via lilblueboo.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27477" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6798-512x341.jpg" title="Where to Buy Silver DIY Pendants for Resin or Epoxy via lilblueboo.com" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Heres the
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002VMDBJ2/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B002VMDBJ2&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=liblbo-20" target="_blank">
           <em>
            <strong>
             1/2 inch size:
            </strong>
           </em>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6811.jpg">
           <img alt="Where to Buy Round Silver DIY Pendants for Resin or Epoxy via lilblueboo.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27478" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6811-512x341.jpg" title="Where to Buy Round Silver DIY Pendants for Resin or Epoxy via lilblueboo.com" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Today I filled them with nonpareil sprinkles from the grocery store and glitter:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/things-to-fill-pendant-with.jpg">
           <img alt="Make a Sprinkle and Glitter Filled Resin Pendant Tutorial via lilblueboo.com  #party #partyfavors" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27494" height="695" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/things-to-fill-pendant-with.jpg" title="Make a Sprinkle and Glitter Filled Resin Pendant Tutorial via lilblueboo.com #party #partyfavors" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          I didnt have all the colors of glitter I wanted so I started by painting a base of glitter fabric paint. When that was dry I added a small thin layer of regular craft glue on one side:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6823.jpg">
           <img alt="Make a Rainbow Glitter Resin Pendant via lilblueboo.com #diy #tutorial" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27479" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6823-512x341.jpg" title="Make a Rainbow Glitter Resin Pendant via lilblueboo.com #diy #tutorial" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I sprinkled super fine glitter on top of the glue. When that was dry, I shook off the excess glitter and I repeated with a different color glitter on the other side:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6838.jpg">
           <img alt="Make a Glitter Resin Pendant via lilblueboo.com #diy #tutorial" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27480" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6838-512x341.jpg" title="Make a Glitter Resin Pendant via lilblueboo.com #diy #tutorial" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Instead of using the resin that you have to measure out and mix together I used this
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002U55W92/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B002U55W92&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=liblbo-20" target="_blank">
           <em>
            <strong>
             Gel du Soleil.
            </strong>
           </em>
          </a>
          The .3oz bottle is tiny but its enough for probably 4 or 5 pendants. The fumes arent as toxic and it cures just by setting it in the sunlight or under UV lights:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6840.jpg">
           <img alt="How to make a resin pendant via lilblueboo.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27481" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6840-512x341.jpg" title="How to make a resin pendant via lilblueboo.com" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          You have to squeeze pretty hard, but you can do it in drops.and the gel will even out if you tap the pendant gently on the table. You can do thin layers if you want to make collages. It also sets within an hour and continues to set for another hour so you could have this as a project for a party or youth group!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6886.jpg">
           <img alt="How to make a resin pendant using a bezel via lilblueboo.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27482" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_6886-512x341.jpg" title="How to make a resin pendant using a bezel via lilblueboo.com" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          To make the sprinkle pendant I put a layer of craft glue on the bottom of the pendant and then shook out the sprinkles to cover the bottom. When it was dry I shook off any excess:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7030.jpg">
           <img alt="How to make a sprinkle filled resin pendant via lilblueboo.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27483" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7030-512x341.jpg" title="How to make a sprinkle filled resin pendant via lilblueboo.com" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          If you get any air bubbles in your resin just take a small pin and pop them or pull them over to the side and lift them out:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7042.jpg">
           <img alt="How to remove bubbles from resin jewelry via lilblueboo.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27484" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7042-512x341.jpg" title="How to remove bubbles from resin jewelry via lilblueboo.com" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Heres a side view so that you can see how the resin fills up the entire pendant and has a nice smooth glassy finish:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7080.jpg">
           <img alt="Ideas for making resin jewelry via lilblueboo.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27488" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7080-512x341.jpg" title="Ideas for making resin jewelry via lilblueboo.com" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          So many things you could fill these with to make fun themed jewelry for gifts and party favors:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7063.jpg">
           <img alt="How to make your own resin jewelry via lilblueboo.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27487" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7063-512x341.jpg" title="How to make your own resin jewelry via lilblueboo.com" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Make sure you when you give a gift you let them know that its handmade! I love these
          <a href="http://lilblueboo.com/shop/products/Handmade-for-You-Printable-Cards-%252d-3-Designs-%252d-by-Pen-and-Paint--.html" target="_blank">
           <em>
            <strong>
             handmade with love cards by Pen and Paint
            </strong>
           </em>
          </a>
          . I just printed them out and punched holes as a nice necklace presentation:
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7212.jpg">
           <img alt="Where to buy resin jewelry supplies via lilblueboo.com" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27491" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7212-512x341.jpg" title="Where to buy resin jewelry supplies via lilblueboo.com" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          A
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003S88O7W/ref=as_li_qf_sp_asin_il_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B003S88O7W&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=liblbo-20" target="_blank">
           <em>
            <strong>
             clear plastic cellophane bag
            </strong>
           </em>
          </a>
          makes it look even better!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7216.jpg">
           <img alt="Homemade Gift Ideas: How to make your own resin jewelry necklace via lilblueboo.com #gifts" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-27492" height="341" src="http://www.lilblueboo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/MG_7216-512x341.jpg" title="Homemade Gift Ideas: How to make your own resin jewelry necklace via lilblueboo.com #gifts" width="512"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
