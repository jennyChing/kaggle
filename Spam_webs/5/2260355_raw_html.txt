        <p>
         While fatalities have been around in Mortal Kombat since the very beginning, one of the newer features to show up in Mortal Kombat 9 were X-Ray moves. We still remember the first time we saw the camera zooming in to show the inside of our enemy as his bones were splintered into thousands of tiny pieces. It was glorious. It was hilarious. We wanted more. Mortal Kombat X brings them back once more with each character getting one once again. How many different ways can you watch bones shatter and still find it interesting? We havent found a limit yet! While we love watching each and every characters X-Ray, some bone crushing moves are still made better than others. Weve pulled them all off dozens of times and finally were able to narrow down our top ten favorite Mortal Kombat X X-Ray Moves. Check them all out below in all their amazing hideousness and let us know your favorites in the comments below!
