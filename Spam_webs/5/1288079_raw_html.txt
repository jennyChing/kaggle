           <b>
            Step 6: Hide it out in the open.
           </b>
           For this step, you want it to blend as best as possible with the other items around it. For example, you would not want to store a whipped-cream can amidst a bunch of paint cans. It needs to fit in with its environment.
          </td>
         </tr>
