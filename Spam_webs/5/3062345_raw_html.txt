        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         The Best Chocolate Cake Recipe {Ever}
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-01-25T00:01:18+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 25, 2013
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/author/robyn/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Robyn
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/the-best-chocolate-cake-recipe-ever/#comments">
           2,317 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         The best chocolate cake recipe. Ever? There are plenty of claims for the best
         <strong>
          chocolate cake recipe
         </strong>
         . I get that. But with one bite of this chocolate cake with chocolate frosting, every single person around the table commented that this was the best chocolate cake theyd ever tasted.
        </p>
        <div style="display: none;">
         <img alt="The Best Chocolate Cake Recipe Ever. The Best Chocolate Cake recipe with decadent Chocolate Buttercream Frosting that will quickly become your favorite! // addapinch.com" src="http://addapinch.com/cooking/files/2013/01/chocolate-cake-collage.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/files/2013/01/chocolate-cake-DSC_1768.jpg">
          <img alt="The Best Chocolate Cake Recipe Ever. The Best Chocolate Cake recipe with decadent Chocolate Buttercream Frosting that will quickly become your favorite! // addapinch.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4803" height="1026" src="http://addapinch.com/cooking/files/2013/01/chocolate-cake-DSC_1768.jpg" title="chocolate-cake-DSC_1768" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So as for my house, this chocolate cake recipe now holds that honor.
        </p>
        <p>
         As the compliments continued, I didnt begin to tell them how easy it was to make.
        </p>
        <p>
         In all honesty, it is probably one of the easiest cakes Ive made in a long time, really, but I just smiled and thanked them.
        </p>
        <p>
         As we continued on gobbling up the cake, drinking our coffee or iced cold milk, the compliments just kept coming.
        </p>
        <p>
         Then the requests for the recipe started.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats generally when you know that folks
         <em>
          really
         </em>
         like what youve made if they ask for the recipe and tell you for what event they plan to serve it.
        </p>
        <p>
         As a cook, that really is one of the highest compliments you can receive  well, at least in my book.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-4801">
         </span>
         <br/>
         It was great to hear that they thought the cake was just as good after their last bite as they thought it was with their first.
        </p>
        <p>
         Heres another great thing about this recipe. The cake batter really can be made in a bowl without the use of a heavy duty mixer. Of course, that just makes things easier, but this is easily a recipe my Grandmother would have enjoyed making in her tiny kitchen with a whisk and a bowl. And Im sure it would have turned out perfectly, too.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/files/2013/01/chocolate-cake-DSC_1788.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4805" height="964" src="http://addapinch.com/cooking/files/2013/01/chocolate-cake-DSC_1788.jpg" title="chocolate-cake-DSC_1788" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im not going to kid you, this makes a very rich, moist, and absolutely luxurious chocolate cake recipe. So if you are a faint of heart chocolate cake person, this is not the cake for you.
        </p>
        <p>
         Based on the
         <a href="https://www.hersheys.com/recipes/recipe-details.aspx?id=5273" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Old Fashioned Hersheys recipe
         </a>
         , I sort of went a bit heavy-handed with some changes testing cake after cake until I got it just the way I wanted it, but the end result was oh so worth it.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/files/2013/01/chocolate-cake-DSC_1792.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4807" height="964" src="http://addapinch.com/cooking/files/2013/01/chocolate-cake-DSC_1792.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The few crumbs left on plates gave that away.
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the ingredients that really made this chocolate cake recipe come to life, in my opinion, was the addition of
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008F05W1E?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B008F05W1E&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;tag=httpaddapicom-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          espresso powder
         </a>
         in both the chocolate cake and in the chocolate frosting.
        </p>
        <p>
         If youve not tried a bit of espresso when baking with chocolate, you really should.
        </p>
        <p>
         The espresso works to elevate the flavors in much the same way as vanilla extract and I now think of it as an essential part of my spice cabinet.
        </p>
        <p>
         Espresso powder is easy to find at the grocery store or even online if your grocer doesnt stock it normally.
        </p>
        <p>
         Believe me when I say, it is very much worth the effort to have just for those times you are baking with chocolate!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/files/2013/01/chocolate-cake-DSC_1770.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4804" height="600" src="http://addapinch.com/cooking/files/2013/01/chocolate-cake-DSC_1770-599x600.jpg" title="chocolate-cake-DSC_1770" width="599"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         So my friends, if you have a chocolate lover in your midst, make them this cake. If theyve had a bad day this is certainly something that will brighten it for them. If theyve had a great day, then this chocolate cake is a great way to celebrate with them!
        </p>
        <p>
         It just needs to happen.
        </p>
        <p>
         Promise.
        </p>
        <p>
         Let me just say again, this chocolate cake + this
         <a href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/2014/02/27/perfect-chocolate-buttercream-frosting-recipe/" target="_blank" title="Perfect Chocolate Buttercream Frosting Recipe">
          Chocolate Buttercream Frosting Recipe
         </a>
         = the best chocolate cake Ive ever had!
        </p>
        <p>
         And here it is.
         <br/>
        </p>

         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            <strong>
             Chocolate Cake
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups all-purpose flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup unsweetened cocoa powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoons baking soda
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon
            <a a="" href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008F05W1E?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B008F05W1E&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;tag=httpaddapicom-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             espresso powder
            </a>
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup vegetable oil
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup boiling water
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            <a href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/2014/02/27/perfect-chocolate-buttercream-frosting-recipe/" target="_blank" title="Perfect Chocolate Buttercream Frosting Recipe">
             Chocolate Buttercream Frosting Recipe
            </a>
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 350 F. Prepare two 9-inch cake pans by spraying with baking spray or buttering and lightly flouring.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <strong>
             For the cake:
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, salt and espresso powder to a large bowl or the bowl of a stand mixer. Whisk through to combine or, using your paddle attachment, stir through flour mixture until combined well.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla to flour mixture and mix together on medium speed until well combined. Reduce speed and carefully add boiling water to the cake batter. Beat on high speed for about 1 minute to add air to the batter.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Distribute cake batter evenly between the two prepared cake pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until a toothpick or cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove from the oven and allow to cool for about 10 minutes, remove from the pan and cool completely.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Frost cake with
            <a href="http://addapinch.com/cooking/2014/02/27/perfect-chocolate-buttercream-frosting-recipe/" target="_blank" title="Perfect Chocolate Buttercream Frosting Recipe">
             Chocolate Buttercream Frosting
            </a>
            .
           </li>
          </ol>

           <p>
            To those commenting about the melted butterthe recipe doesnt say to melt it.  You are just supposed to let it soften to room temperature.  If you dont have time for it to soften on its own, you can take it right out of the fridge and microwave it for about 10 seconds tops.  Another good trick for butter in icing is to whip the butter by itself for a loooong time, at least 6-7 minutes before adding the sugar.  That little trick always makes the fluffiest buttercream icing.
           </p>

            1  1/2 sticks of butter does not equal  1  1/2 cups.  Each stick of butter equals 1/2 cup, therefore  1/2 stick equals 1/4 cup,
            <br/>
            so  1  1/2 sticks equals 3/4 cup butter.
           </p>
