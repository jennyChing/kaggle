        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Do you sit in front of a computer a majority of the time you are at work?
         <strong>
          50-70%
         </strong>
         of people spend
         <strong>
          five to seven hours
         </strong>
         a day sitting. Sitting for long periods has health consequences, including increased risk for
         <strong>
          obesity
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          chronic disease
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Even
         <strong>
          one hour
         </strong>
         of daily exercise cannot compensatefor sitting at a desk for a prolong period of time. Try to combat the dangerous effects of sitting by getting up and walking for
         <strong>
          five minutes once an hour
         </strong>
         .Also, stand while on conference calls or eating lunch. Start moving more during the day to help
         <strong>
          fight the dangerous effects of sitting
         </strong>
         .
