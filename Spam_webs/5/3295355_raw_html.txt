            <p>
             Are you thinking about taking a trip to Europe? Would you like to see more cities,sample local food and stay in a good hotels?Would you like a little more then that? Then a package trip is the right option for you.
            </p>
            <p>
             All you have to do is to arrange your flight and buy a package trip. It is the best option for comfortable traveling. Most of the companies include accomodation,city tours,some meals,tour directors and local guides,private motorcoaches and maybe some admissions.
            </p>
            <p>
             How to choose the right tour operator? What is important to look for?
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              -Lenght of your trip 
             </strong>
             decide how long your trip should be and which part of europe you would like to see
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              -Itinerary
             </strong>
              pick a trip and check the detailed itinerary of the trip. This is very important in order to see how is the trip balanced .For example you do not want to spend 6 hours a day on a bus. Some tour companies offer trip with great cities,but most of the time you are driving on the coach. Another important factor is how many nights are you staying in the same hotel. If your trip is lasting 10 days,you do not want to move every night to a different hotel.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              -Hotels
             </strong>
              if you like your comfort, check what hotels youll be staying in.If it is not listed on the website,call and ask.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              -Price of the trip
             </strong>
             - most of the travelers are choosing their trip according to price. That is absolutely fine. But remember to check what is included in the price of your trip and what is still to be covered.Quite often you can find a trip for an excellent price,but later on you will find out that you have to pay for airport transfers(normally $100 pp extra), you are expected to tip your drivers, local guides, tour director, porters and the biggest extra surprise could be Optional Tours. There is nothing more annoying than traveling all day,getting to your hotel and having first group meeting where tour director is trying to sell you optional tours. And either you decide to spend extra few hundreds of dollars and buy the optionals or youll pass and then you have to make your own arrangements.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              -Tour Directors -
             </strong>
             are one of the most important aspect of the trip.They are leading you,showing you places,organizing daily programs,dealling with vendors,restaurants hotels and building the whole experience.If you would like to get know the countries you are visitng, learn about the local culture, listen to stories about the past but as well about current situation in the countries, find out where to buy some special souvenir, help with the local language and feel safe and taking care of the whole time,then look for a company that works exclusively with local tour directors. Who could be a greater asset to your trip then a local professional?
            </p>
            <p align="left">
             Europe Land Travel have taken all of the above mentioned in mind and prepared amazing trips for you in Europe! Look at the itineraries,see immediately what is included and why to travel with us.
            </p>
