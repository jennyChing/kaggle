           <div class="panel entry-content" id="tab-aims-objectives">
            <p>
             When you have completed theCity &amp; Guilds accredited, distance learning qualification, the ITQ Social Media, you will have a practical knowledge of the major Social Media networks including Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook and Google+. You will also have a practical working knowledge of SEO (search engine optimisation), creating video for business, Pinterest, social bookmarking, blogging, Google Analytics and building a WordPress website. You will be able to create a strategy and social media policy for your use of social media and choose the correct channels to carry this out efficiently.
            </p>
            <p>
             Enhanced Learning Credits can be used to pay for this course.
            </p>
           </div>
           <div class="panel entry-content" id="tab-course-content">
            <p>
             On successful completion of the course, you will be awarded the City &amp; Guilds ITQ Level 3 qualification (equivalent to an A Level standard of learning). You are assessed throughout the course and will complete 19tasks including practical creation of profiles and status updates. You will also evidence your use of a range of digital marketing tools.
            </p>
            <p>
             The following
             <b>
              training
             </b>
             courses are included as part of the qualification:

           <div class="panel entry-content" id="tab-assessment">
            <p>
             There are two versions of the assessment tasks  one for those in business and a professional version for those who want to learn about social media but dont currently have a business they can use as a case study.
            </p>
            <p>
             Assessment takes the form of 20 tasks. The tasks comprise of the following:
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Task 1  About you
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 2  Review where you are now
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 3  LinkedIn profiles, discussions and feeds
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 4  Scenarios  LinkedIn Connections
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 5  Twitter  Profile
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 6  Profile Images
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 7  Social Media Management Tools
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 8  Pinterest/Social bookmarking
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 9  Facebook Individual Profile
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 10  Facebook Pages
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 11  Measurement
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 12  Website Design
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 13  WordPress
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 14 Video (YouTube)
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 15 Apps and Widgets
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 16 Connecting and Browsing
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 17 Analytics and Data
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 18  Curation Tools
             </li>
             <li>
              Task 19  Forward Planning
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             To complete the qualification, you are required to work through each task and answer questions or complete activities.
            </p>
            <p>
             For example, for Task 3  LinkedIn profiles discussions and feeds, you are asked to:
            </p>
            <ul>
             <li>
              Complete the LinkedIn training course
             </li>
             <li>
              Complete your LinkedIn personal profile
             </li>
             <li>
              Give a constructive critique of a peers profile
             </li>
             <li>
              Review company profiles
             </li>
             <li>
              Join and contribute to a LinkedIn group
             </li>
             <li>
              Complete a training blog entry reflecting on how you can use LinkedIn going forward based on your aims for Social Media, what you are going to say, how you are going to connect with people and how you intend to promote trust of your LinkedIn profile.
             </li>
             <li>
              Explain features and functions of LinkedIn
             </li>
             <li>
              Plan status updates to share on LinkedIn.
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p>
             You are given detailed feedback on each aspect of the task including your LinkedIn profile and your blog entries to ensure that you have thought about all the aspects of using LinkedIn in your business or for your personal use.
            </p>
