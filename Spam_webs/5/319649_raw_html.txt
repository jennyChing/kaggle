       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Concealer for Fixing Those Imperfections
       </h1>
       <!-- .entry-title -->
       <div class="entry-content clearfix">
        <p style="text-align: right;">
         <a class="btn btn-info medium square" href="http://www.sahmreviews.com/disclosures/non-monetary-compensation" target="_blank">
          Disclosure
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         A year and a half ago, I started having serious skin problems that left my eyelids horribly flaky. I tried a variety of things to make it go away including going make-up free for a week as directed by my doctor. Makeup is something Ive always had to be careful with due to skin sensitivities but over the years I havent had as many issues. This breakout freaked me out and annoyed me enough that I actually went to the doctor to have it checked out. For those that know me, thats a big deal. Im not a fan of going to the doctor.
        </p>
        <p>
         He said it was totally normal  a skin fungus that resides on the skin and sometimes acts up. We dont know what caused the flare-up but a solution was to use prescription-grade hydrocortisone cream. Unfortunately, it didnt work. Not only did it not clear up the problem but it was also very painful. DH researched various products and found a small jar of cream for around $25. Im cheap so I freaked out about the price Until I used it and the skin condition cleared up in only a few days.
        </p>
        <p>
         What this taught me is that there is a difference in the quality of products  whether it be lotions, creams or make-up. Ive tried some higher end products such as
         <a href="http://www.sahmreviews.com/2013/11/forget-your-makeup-its-okay.html" target="_blank">
          Lancome
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://www.sahmreviews.com/2013/11/forget-your-makeup-its-okay.html" target="_blank">
          Ageless Derma
         </a>
         with great success. You truly get what you pay for.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thats one of the reasons I was excited to try out
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B007H2B5GW/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B007H2B5GW&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=lawheecomusedveh&amp;linkId=GP75LXT4NDIOMCDI" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Ageless Derma Camoufleur Concealer
         </a>
         . Im a minimalist when it comes to makeup. Often utilizing concealers causes my skin to react and make the blemish even worse than if I hadnt covered it up at all. That wasnt the case with this. Since Ive been using higher quality products, I havent had near the amount of breakouts as before. Heres a small one that I wanted to camouflage.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Ageless-Derma-Concealer-Before.jpg" rel="prettyphoto[6112]">
          <img alt="We can't all be perfect! See what SahmReviews.com does to hide the imperfections. " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6448" height="404" src="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Ageless-Derma-Concealer-Before.jpg" width="712"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         It isnt really bad but its enough to annoy me. Its been hanging around for a few days and Ive applied a small amount of Ageless Derma Concealer each day to cover it up. To my delight, I havent had a reaction to it. The consistency is rich but not so thick that it cakes on. Ive used it several times and barely made a dent in the surface of the cream. I get a small swipe, not even a dab, and apply it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Ageless-Derma-Concealer-Product.jpg" rel="prettyphoto[6112]">
          <img alt="We can't all be perfect! See what SahmReviews.com does to hide the imperfections. " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6451" height="404" src="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Ageless-Derma-Concealer-Product.jpg" width="712"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Ageless-Derma-Concealer-Applying.jpg" rel="prettyphoto[6112]">
          <img alt="We can't all be perfect! See what SahmReviews.com does to hide the imperfections. " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6449" height="404" src="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Ageless-Derma-Concealer-Applying.jpg" width="712"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         As you can see, a small amount is all it took to conceal the imperfection. If youre saying Well, I can still see it thats because I didnt put any kind of finishing powder on over it. I wanted the example to be clean and realistic. Had I used my foundation powder over the top, you wouldnt have been able to see it at all  in the photo or in person.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Ageless-Derma-Concealer-After.jpg" rel="prettyphoto[6112]">
          <img alt="We can't all be perfect! See what SahmReviews.com does to hide the imperfections. " class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6450" height="404" src="http://www.sahmreviews.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Ageless-Derma-Concealer-After.jpg" width="712"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Its a very natural-feeling product that even this make-up sensitive person can use. Does this sound like a great product for your little imperfections? If so, enter to win one of your own!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="e-widget" href="https://gleam.io/3HAKR/ageless-derma-camoufleur-concealer" rel="nofollow">
          Ageless Derma Camoufleur Concealer
         </a>

         </script>

         Do you have sensitive skin like I do?
        </p>
