        <a class="hasimg" href="http://skytown.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/SKY-2.png">
         <img alt="SKY 2" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-85" height="227" src="http://skytown.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/SKY-2-300x227.png" width="300"/>

       <p>
        13 year old singer and musician Kayla Sky started singing and performing at the early age of seven years old. She started out modeling since she was taller than most kids in her class. She quickly realized that she loves to entertain and started singing harmony with her sister at an early age. They both formed a Gospel group along with their first cousins called The Gospel Angels. They would travel the tri-state area performing for numerous churches. This is where Kayla learned the fundamentals of stage performance.
        <br/>
        As a musician, Kayla studies and plays Guitar and Violin, as well as reading music. She also studies dance. In 2011, Kayla auditioned to sing The National Anthem at the U.S. Open and was pleased and privileged to win with her soulful and spirited rendition! Kayla quadrupled her media exposure when she created her own YouTube channel named HarmonySadeNKayla. It features various artists and diverse genres of songs randomly requested by her loyal and ever-growing fan base. Kayla Sky started arranging harmony and some spoofs of cover songs to the amazement and sheer delight of her adoring followers. One of the cover songs, Lauren Hills Joyful Joyful captured over 6,000 views and likes.
       </p>

        <br/>
        Kayla is a poised and polite teen who inspires her peers by sharing her uplifting thoughts and her voice. She shares a second Facebook page with her best friend called Nerdy But Cool where they give encouraging and uplifting advice to their peers about bullying and peer pressure.
        <br/>
        In pursuit of her dreams as a famous singer and entertainer, Kayla is working with young up in coming producer Kyle Stewart K2 and other producers. Kayla has the support of her management team Alegna McKelvin of Reussite Entertainment and Holly Mitchell.
