     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.butternutsquash.net/2003/04/16/meet-da-punks/2003/12/03/ghettoccino-time/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: ghettoccino time">
       ghettoccino time
      </a>
     </h2>
     <div class="entry">
      <p>
       welcome to a slice of my past, a tasty treat concocted by my dad  coffee and vanilla ice cream -affectionately dubbed the ghettoccino by evan. the recipe is as follows;
      </p>
      <p>
        take freshly brewed columbian coffee and pour into appropriate mug  size can vary.
       <br/>
        then take a dollop of vanilla ice cream (we recommend breyers french vanilla  other flavours can be used for variety, but the original is always the best) and slowly submerge into hot coffee allowing escaping air to froth up.
       <br/>
        sit back, put your feet up and enjoy.
      </p>
