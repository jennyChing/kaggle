         <h1 class="post-title">
          <a href="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/?p=31859" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Obama Proposes Spending Zero Dollars To Defend Against A Russian Nuclear Attack">
           Obama Proposes Spending Zero Dollars To Defend Against A Russian Nuclear Attack
          </a>
         </h1>
         <div class="meta top">
          <span class="meta-author">
           <a href="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/?author=1274" rel="author" title="Posts by Geoffrey Grider">
            Geoffrey Grider
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="meta-date">
           | March 21, 2015
          </span>
         </div>
         <div class="fb-social-plugin fb-like" data-href="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/?p=31859" data-layout="button_count" data-ref="above-post" data-share="true" data-width="450">
         </div>
         <h4>
          The Obama Administration has followed the lead of past Democraticadministrations in viewinghomeland missile defense as (1) too hard, (2) too expensive, and (3) too destabilizing.
         </h4>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The Obama Administration is proposing
          </strong>
          that the United States spend about a billion dollars per week in the fiscal year beginning October 1 to defend Afghanistan, Iraq and other nations against various threats they face. Thats how much money is inthe Pentagonsrequest for overseas contingency operations.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_31860" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/united-states-not-prepared-for-russian-nuclear-strike-obama-putin.jpg" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/downloads/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/united-states-not-prepared-for-russian-nuclear-strike-obama-putin.jpg');">
           <img alt="united-states-not-prepared-for-russian-nuclear-strike-obama-putin" class="wp-image-31860 size-full" height="400" src="http://www.nowtheendbegins.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/united-states-not-prepared-for-russian-nuclear-strike-obama-putin.jpg" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           <strong>
            On the day deterrence fails, Americas highly capable strategic force will be little comfort because it cant do anything to intercept incoming warheads. All it can do is lay waste to Russia.
           </strong>
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          So guess how much money the administration is seeking to defend Americas homeland against an attack from Russiausing nuclear-armed ballistic missiles. Russia has about 1,600 missile warheads capable of reaching U.S. territory, and
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            if even a small fraction were launched, they could wipe out our electric grid, our financial networks, and quite possibly the whole U.S. economy
           </span>
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The answeris that the administration is proposing to spend nothing.
          </strong>
          Even though we know that most of those Russian warheads are pointed at America. Even though we know relations with Russia are deteriorating. Even though we know that
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            Vladimir Putins subordinates haverepeatedly threatened the West with nuclear consequences
           </span>
          </strong>
          if it seeks to block expansionist moves along the Russianperiphery such as last years invasion of Ukraine.
         </p>
         <p>
          Just this week,
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            Putin stated in a documentary
           </span>
          </strong>
          commemorating Moscows annexation of Crimea that he hadconsidered putting the nuclear arsenal on alert to dissuade the West from pushing back, observing
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            that he was ready for the worst possible turn of events.
           </span>
          </strong>
          It isnt so clear what a heightened state of alert would mean, since Russian military officialsinsist that even in peacetime, most ofthe countrysmissiles are ready to launch within minutes.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.forbes.com/sites/lorenthompson/2015/03/20/the-u-s-has-no-defense-against-a-russian-nuclear-attack-really/" onclick="javascript:pageTracker._trackPageview('/outgoing/www.forbes.com/sites/lorenthompson/2015/03/20/the-u-s-has-no-defense-against-a-russian-nuclear-attack-really/');" target="_blank">
           Read the rest of the story
          </a>
          on Forbes
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
