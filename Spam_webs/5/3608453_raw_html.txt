        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Moustache Cupcakes
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2011-07-22T00:50:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          July 22, 2011
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Spoonful of Sugar
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         -
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/2011/07/moustache-cupcakes/#comments">
           16 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-color="red" data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" data-pin-height="32" data-pin-shape="round" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Faspoonfulofsugardesigns.com%2F2011%2F07%2Fmoustache-cupcakes%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2F4.bp.blogspot.com%2F-H1alhWs1Atk%2FTikqoOxzgaI%2FAAAAAAAADc4%2F9lyCNPKI7UU%2Fs1600%2FCupcake%2BStand.jpg&amp;description=Moustache%20Cupcakes" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         We are having a family photo shoot on Sunday (fingers crossed the weather improves!) with the extended family to celebrate my parents recent 50th wedding anniversary. I wanted to make some cupcakes to reflect the occasion and came up with the idea of Moustache (or Mustache for you Americans) Cupcakes as the moustache reminds me of photo booth fun.
        </p>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-ld9yXkpUDfk/Tikp4FSt6TI/AAAAAAAADc0/LUkYCAbZxc4/s1600/Cupcake+Duo.jpg" imageanchor="1">
          <img border="0" height="640" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Cupcake+Duo.jpg" width="470"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I was keen to use my Cricut machine (it was sent away for repairs and just returned after a 5 week absence). A quick internet search and I found a free svg file
         <a href="http://wecanbeaoriginal.com/blog/2011/02/free-svg-file-download-mustache-silhouette/">
          here
         </a>
         . I resized the moustache so that it was 3.39 inches x 1.08 inches, and attached a toothpick with some Japanese washi masking tape to match.
        </p>
        <div>
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-fOO9bI9yyzI/Tiko1AvX2XI/AAAAAAAADcw/N5qveHD5MZg/s1600/Moustache+topper.jpg" imageanchor="1">
          <img border="0" height="283" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Moustache+topper.jpg" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I whipped up a batch of
         <a href="http://www.marthastewart.com/282243/one-bowl-chocolate-cupcakes">
          Marthas one bowl chocolate cupcakes
         </a>
         (our current favourite), added a swirl of cream cheese frosting and the moustache topper.
        </p>
        <div>
         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-XaX-7AFCbNk/TikqxQuNqOI/AAAAAAAADc8/zhJ7YTklVj0/s1600/Trio.jpg" imageanchor="1">
          <img border="0" height="640" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Trio.jpg" width="506"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         Fun. Fun. Fun.
        </p>
        <div>
         <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-H1alhWs1Atk/TikqoOxzgaI/AAAAAAAADc4/9lyCNPKI7UU/s1600/Cupcake+Stand.jpg" imageanchor="1">
          <img border="0" height="640" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Cupcake+Stand.jpg" width="392"/>
         </a>
        </div>
