            <p>
             Music matters. It can connect us to a particular lifestyle, move us to dance, bring us to tears during our favourite films, and trigger powerful memories when we hear a certain melody. According to
             <a href="http://www.livescience.com/5327-music-memory-connection-brain.html" target="_blank">
              <strong>
               Petr Janata
              </strong>
             </a>
             , a cognitive neuroscientist at the University of California, What seems to happen is that a piece of familiar music serves as a soundtrack for a mental movie that starts playing in our head. So just as music can evoke a visual memory, a particular image can also evoke an audial memory. And what better way to celebrate these memories than with a music t-shirt.
            </p>
            <p>
             Personally, I recall the 1990s as a time of remembering live concerts by immortalising the night with a band t-shirt that you could only pick up at the venue. Every time you wore that t-shirt, the night and its sounds would come flooding back. Today music fans have all kinds of t-shirts on offer thanks to many talented indie artists &amp; designers (and the internet!).
            </p>
            <p>
             So without further ado, lets welcome to the Indie Minded stage a round-up of music-inspired t-shirts which can be found and purchased online.

              (
              <strong>
               please note:
              </strong>
              if the title of the t-shirt isnt linked, it is no longer available to purchase. Click through to their site to see whats new on offer.)
