                     <i>
                      <span style="color: #3f4549; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; line-height: 21px;">
                       
                      </span>
                      I'm looking over a sink full of dishes that I overlooked before....
                      <span style="color: #3f4549; font-family: 'Helvetica Neue', arial, sans-serif; font-size: 15px; line-height: 21px;">
                       
                      </span>
                     </i>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     This was the scene when I got home from work today.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     You know I love you all, but I'm pretty sure something in there is going to develop sentience and wage war on
                     <a href="http://chrisbrecheen.blogspot.com/p/cast-and-crew.html">
                      The Hall of Rectitude
                     </a>
                     if I don't do the dishes. Seriously, that's not any more contrived than some Spiderman villains. (Dude turned into sand? Really?) I better get on this.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     The last time I ended up creating another supervillain for Uberdude and The Brain to fight, they were very put out. Of course that guy was crazy good looking and could create copies of himself and used them to seduce women minions who were intrigued with the idea of monogamous group sex.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Good times. Good times.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     But I'm getting off track.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Anyway....there
                     <i>
                      IS
                     </i>
                     this half written post about how to start your own blog, and yes I hoped to get it up right after I got home. But then the screaming started.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     So...I'm going to, with
                     <i>
                      very
                     </i>
                     cautious optimism, say tomorrow, but to be honest it might a double offering on Friday.
