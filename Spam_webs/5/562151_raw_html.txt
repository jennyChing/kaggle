  <p>
   I had the great privilege of attending the McDonalds Flavors of Summer First Taste Event in NYC earlier this week. Its been quite a few years since I have visited NYC, so visiting the city alone was a big deal for me. McDonalds was a gracious host to the 20 bloggers invited to the event. We stayed at the beautiful Empire Hotel, perfectly located between Lincoln Center and Central Park.
  </p>
  <p style="text-align: center;">
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="450" src="http://i1099.photobucket.com/albums/g388/couponinggibsongal/mcd1.jpg" width="600"/>
   My Jr Suite at Empire Hotel
  </p>
  <p>
   My first night in NYC, McDonalds gathered us all together for an amazing dining experience at the Culinary Loft in Soho. The bloggers got to help Chef Dave Martin (Top Chef) prepare an amazing berry infused dinner. We met McDonalds new Chef Jessica Drakulich who also happens to be a Dietitian. I was most happy that we had an opportunity to dine with and talk to many of the McDonalds Executives, such as the McCafe Beverage Line Marketing Director Marta Fearon, about the McDonalds chain and the direction the company is taking with new products like
   <strong>
    Blueberry Banana Nut Oatmeal
   </strong>
   and the
   <strong>
    McCafe Cherry Berry Chiller
   </strong>
   . It was an evening of delicious food and great conversation!
  </p>
  <p style="text-align: center;">
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="450" src="http://i1099.photobucket.com/albums/g388/couponinggibsongal/mcd2.jpg" width="600"/>
   Chef Dave put my saute skills to good use!
  </p>
  <p>
   The
   <strong>
    Flavors of Summer First Taste Event
   </strong>
   featured the tastes and trends of summer, including McDonalds two newest menu items  McCafe Cherry Berry Chiller and Blueberry Banana Nut Oatmeal. It was a day of health and beauty featuring special guests lifestyle expert Bobby Thomas, and Nutrition Experts Tanya Zuckerbrot, MS, RD (F-Factor) and Sylvia Melendez-Klinger, MS, RD, LDN, CPT.
  </p>
  <p style="text-align: center;">
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="600" src="http://i1099.photobucket.com/albums/g388/couponinggibsongal/mcd3.jpg" width="450"/>
   Bobbie Thomas (Today Show)
  </p>
  <p>
   Keeping with the theme of health and beauty, I got a professional manicure by Gina who is manicurist to mom celebrities such as Kelly Ripa. Makeup expert Rommy Najor gave me some great tips. I dont like the heaviness of lipstick, so I use a gloss which doesnt seem to stay on for long. Rommy said to try using a lip stain under my favorite gloss for a more lasting effect. Bobbie Thomas brought a beautiful selection of berry inspired fashions for Summer. Just before heading to the airport, I took advantage of the mini massages being offered, to prepare me for my long flight.
  </p>
  <p style="text-align: center;">
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="450" src="http://i1099.photobucket.com/albums/g388/couponinggibsongal/mcd4.jpg" width="600"/>
   Getting my blueberry inspired manicure!
  </p>
  <p>
   The highlight of the event was getting a first taste of the new McDonalds
   <strong>
    McCafe Cherry Berry Chiller
   </strong>
   and
   <strong>
    Blueberry Banana Nut Oatmeal
   </strong>
   that will be available for a limited time this Summer. I am so impressed by the direction McDonalds is taking by giving us more nutritious menu items such as these two tasty additions.
  </p>
  <p>
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="450" src="http://i1099.photobucket.com/albums/g388/couponinggibsongal/mcd7.jpg" width="600"/>
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    <span style="font-size: x-large;">
     McCafe Cherry Berry Chiller
    </span>
   </strong>
   <br/>
   As much as I enjoyed trying this refreshing drink (and I had 3 of them), I know that the kids will like this even more than I do. The Cherry Berry Chiller is a blended ice beverage made with 100% fruit juice with natural flavors. The natural sweetness of the cherry and raspberry is perfect! Im the person who drinks the cherry juice from a jar of maraschino cherries, so I really appreciate the amount of cherry flavor that this chiller packs. I know well be pulling into McDonalds a lot this Summer for the Cherry Berry Chiller.
  </p>
  <p>
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="600" src="http://i1099.photobucket.com/albums/g388/couponinggibsongal/mcd8.jpg" width="450"/>
  </p>
  <p style="text-align: center;">
   A 12 oz serving of the McCafe Cherry Berry Chiller has only 200 calories.
  </p>
  <p style="text-align: center;">
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="450" src="http://i1099.photobucket.com/albums/g388/couponinggibsongal/mcd5.jpg" width="600"/>
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    <span style="font-size: x-large;">
     Blueberry Banana Nut Oatmeal
    </span>
   </strong>
   <br/>
   Im already a huge fan of the Apple Cinnamon Walnut Oatmeal, so Im thrilled that there is a new oatmeal variety soon to be available! The Blueberry Banana Nut Oatmeal is incredibly satisfying and will keep you full until lunch time. Each serving contains approximately 1/4 cup of fresh blueberries which is no small feat. At the event I had opportunity to speak with Art Galletta, Chairman of the U.S. Highbush Blueberry Council who is making it possible for McDonalds to serve fresh blueberries in all 14,000 U.S. stores this Summer. The Blueberry Banana Nut Oatmeal is infused with crushed, dried banana and comes with a pouch of chopped Walnuts. I seriously love this oatmeal and know you will also. Plus, you can get it any time of the day. Seriously, you gotta try it!
  </p>
  <p>
   <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="450" src="http://i1099.photobucket.com/albums/g388/couponinggibsongal/mcd6.jpg" width="600"/>
  </p>
  <p style="text-align: center;">
   The nutritional values for this hearty dish cant be beat, at only 290 calories per serving and containing 5 grams of fiber with 2 servings of whole grains.
  </p>
  <p>
   Ive been struggling to lose weight for months now, so I was thrilled to learn that McDonalds offers some fantastic meals for less than 600 calories each. I am excited to share these under 600 calories choices with you:
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    Breakfast:
   </strong>
   <br/>
   Blueberry Banana Nut Oatmeal
   <br/>
   1% Low Fat White Milk Jug
   <br/>
   Premium Roast Coffee (Black)
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    Lunch or Dinner:
   </strong>
   <br/>
   Premium Grilled Chicken Classic Sandwich on a bakery-style bun
   <br/>
   Apple Slices
   <br/>
   Bottle of Dasani Water
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    Lunch or Dinner:
   </strong>
   <br/>
   McDonalds Southwest Salad with Grilled Chicken
   <br/>
   1 packet of Newmans Own Creamy Southwest Dressing
   <br/>
   Fruit n Yogurt Parfait (with granola)
   <br/>
   Diet Coke
  </p>
  <p>
   <strong>
    Lunch or Dinner:
   </strong>
   <br/>
   New Happy Meal containing 4-pice Chicken McNuggets, Apple Slices, Kids Fries, and Fat Free Chocolate Milk Jug.
  </p>
  <p>
   Its so easy to eat a healthy and satisfying meal at McDonalds and stick to your diet!
  </p>
  <p>
   A big Thank You to McDonalds for including me in this fabulous event! Disclosure: No compensation was received for this post. About A Mom received an all expense paid trip to attend the event. All opinions are 100% our own.
  </p>

          this.checkboxAndRadioValidation(field);
