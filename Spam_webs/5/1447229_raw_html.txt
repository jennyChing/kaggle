            <h2 id="recipe">
             Tucson Tempeh Salad
            </h2>
           </div>
           <div class="content">
            <p>
             <strong>
              TUCSON TEMPEH PASTA
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             1 block tempeh (~8 oz.)
            </p>
            <p>
             1 yellow onion
            </p>
            <p>
             1 bell pepper
            </p>
            <p>
             2 carrots
            </p>
            <p>
             3-4 cloves of garlic
            </p>
            <p>
             1 teaspoon olive oil
            </p>
            <p>
             2-3 teaspoons soy sauce or tamari
            </p>
            <p>
             2 tablespoons chili powder
            </p>
            <p>
             2 teaspoons dried thyme
            </p>
            <p>
             1 teaspoon dried oregano
            </p>
            <p>
             1  teaspoons ground cumin
            </p>
            <p>
             1 teaspoon liquid smoke (It is near the BBQ sauce at the store I swear. You havent even looked yet so stop fucking complaining.)
            </p>
            <p>
             14 ounce can of tomatoes (make sure you dont buy some brand full of salt)
            </p>
            <p>
             1 pound of pasta (whole wheat, rice, quinoa, whatever the fuck you want)
            </p>
            <p>
             juice of 1 lemon
            </p>
            <p>
             1 cup of green peas or other vegetable
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              Optional
             </em>
