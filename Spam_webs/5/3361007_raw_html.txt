           <p>
            Dubai Marina continues to be a popular location to buy apartments. In spite of it being a well-established community, apartments for sale in Dubai Marina can generally be harder to find however ample options are still available.
            <a href="http://www.bhomes.com/uae/apartments-for-sale-in-dubai-marina.xhtml">
             Apartments for sale in Dubai Marina
            </a>
            can command higher rates than those in newer and less-established communities particularly when buying apartments within the original construction zone because of its restaurants and other shopping facilities. The first apartments for sale in Dubai Marina were the 6 towers: Al Mass, Fairooz, Murjan, Mesk, Anbar, and Al Yass and buying an apartment in Dubai Marina today, especially in these buildings is challenging as they continue to be sought after for their centrality to the community. Those looking for apartments for sale in Dubai Marina can either live in the Phase 1 area just mentioned or the Phase 2 area which consists of primarily high rise buildings built closely together - creating the impression that buying an apartment in Dubai Marina has more of a big city feel than an oceanside, Marina feel.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            The promenade that borders the water runs right through the community and is an attractive selling point for anyone looking to buy an apartment in Dubai Marina; the promenade is popular for riding bikes, visiting cafes and restaurants, jogging, and strolling with infants. In addition to the promenade, those looking for apartments for sale in this community also benefit from its convenient accessibility right off Sheikh Zayed Road however, watch out for traffic congestion at peak times. The Marina Mall offers residents  movies, restaurants and grocery stores with excellent parking; metro stops are easy to reach; the Yacht Club is a key social watering hole for both residents and those looking to buy an apartment in Dubai Marina; and, a broad range of hotels cater to a variety of budget levels, all of which make the community immensely popular. Apartments for sale in Dubai Marina comprise studios, 1, 2, 3 and 4 bedrooms in addition to luxury penthouses.  For those looking to
            <a href="http://www.bhomes.com/uae/apartments-for-sale-in-dubai-marina.xhtml">
             Buy Apartment in Dubai Marina
            </a>
            buildings will generally include a gym, pool and a small grocery downstairs or close by. Most of the properties will be managed by a professional management company and more and more have their own or form a part of a homeowners association group.
           </p>
