         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
          A man defying all the rules of dietary nutritions eats only pizzas to survive and is doing it for the last 25 years
         </h2>
         <p>
          As per the confirmations given to VICE, Dan Janssen, 38, a woodworker from Maryland took to eating pizzas when he was a teenager and turned vegetarian for moral reasons.
          <br/>
          His diet for one single day is two 14-inch pizzas accompanied with coffee. When he was a student of  Harvard University he worked at Domino's to suffice his cravings for
          <a href="http://www.todayoutlook.com/top-fast-food-chains/">
           food
          </a>
          .
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="oneByOne_item">
        <div class="slide_img">
         <img alt="" src="http://www.healthowealth.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/21.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_content">
         <p>
          Viewing 2 of 6
         </p>
         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
         </h2>
         <p>
          He has a family history of
          <a href="http://www.healthowealth.com/home-remedy-for-diabetes/">
           diabetes
          </a>
           barring that Mr. Janssen says he has no other problem, is fit, slim, and healthy.
          <br/>
          'I regularly get myself examined and my blood tests come back clean,' he told The Baltimore Sun.
          <br/>
          'I have no trouble riding a bike 30, 40 miles non-stop.' 'I am in good shape.'
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="oneByOne_item">
        <div class="slide_img">
         <img alt="" src="http://www.healthowealth.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/32.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_content">
         <p>
          Viewing 3 of 6
         </p>
         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
         </h2>
         <p>
          'My message to the men in the world is that you can keep
          <a href="http://www.healthowealth.com/10-top-healthy-drinks-to-get-you-feeling-better/">
           healthy
          </a>
          by eating two 14-inch pizzas a day.'  He found the pizzas at Pontillos one of the tastiest ever; it's a pizza chain based in upstate New York.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="oneByOne_item">
        <div class="slide_img">
         <img alt="" src="http://www.healthowealth.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/12.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <div class="slide_content">
         <p>
          Viewing 4 of 6
         </p>
         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
         </h2>
         <p>
          He thinks that most of the  big-name pizza chains are a 'crap'. Still that doesn't stop him from going there to satisfy his cravings. He says: 'Pizza and
          <a href="http://www.healthowealth.com/sex-education-is-vital-for-all/">
           sex
          </a>
          are identical, both feel good, even when they look bad.'
          <br/>
          As per Mr. Janssen his sex life has never been hampered by his restricted diet and his love life continues to prosper and grow.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="oneByOne_item">
        <div class="slide_img">

         <p>
          Viewing 5 of 6
         </p>
         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
         </h2>
         <p>
          His current fiance is a vegetarian and has no objections about his atypical food preferences. In fact she was happy to go to a pizza parlor on their first date.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>

         </p>
