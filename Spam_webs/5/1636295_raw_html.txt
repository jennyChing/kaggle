       <h2>
        <a href="http://blog.genghisgrill.com/313/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Menu Highlights: Khans Kidz Menu">
         Menu Highlights: Khans Kidz Menu
        </a>
       </h2>
       <h6>
        Written by
        <a href="http://www.genghisgrill.com/" rel="author external" title="Visit Genghis Grills website">
         Genghis Grill
        </a>
        on November 25, 2014
       </h6>
      </hgroup>
     </header>
     <a href="http://i0.wp.com/blog.genghisgrill.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/cheesy-chicken-braccoll-pasta.png">
      <img alt="cheesy-chicken-braccoll-pasta" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-316" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/blog.genghisgrill.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/cheesy-chicken-braccoll-pasta.png?resize=630%2C510"/>
     </a>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      One of the best parts of eating at Genghis Grill is the variety of awesome choices on the Khans Kidz Menu for your little ones! Unlike other restaurants, Genghis Grill offers your children the option to choose from the kids menu or build their own bowl! Here are the delicious options on the Khans Kidz Menu.
     </p>
     <h3>
      Mighty Macaroni and Cheese
     </h3>
     <p>
      Spiral pasta armed with real, melted cheddar cheese
     </p>
     <h3>
      Khans Cheese Quesadillas
     </h3>
     <p>
      Eight unbeatable slices of melted cheddar cheese and fresh tortillas
     </p>
     <h3>
      Superior Spaghetti
     </h3>
     <p>
      Thick-cut udon noodles tamed by roasted tomato sauce
     </p>
     <h3>
      Emperors Pepperoni Pizza
     </h3>
     <p>
      Two tasty tortillas loaded with pepperoni, cheese, and roasted tomato sauce
     </p>
     <h3>
      Hawaiian Fried Rice
     </h3>
     <p>
      Ham, pineapple, and fried rice, crowned with honey soy sauce
     </p>
     <h3>
      Cheesy Chicken and Broccoli Pasta
     </h3>
     <p>
      Spiral pasta teamed up with broccoli, cheese, and chicken
     </p>
     <h3>
      Chicken Tacos
     </h3>
     <p>
      Two tasty tacos loaded with chicken, cheddar cheese, and lettuce
     </p>
     <h3>
      Future Warrior Bowl
     </h3>
     <p>
      If they are ready to take on the grand responsibility of serving their land and building a bowl fit for a true warrior, your children can craft their very own kid-friendly bowls and choose from all of their favorite, healthy, and fresh ingredients!
     </p>
     <p>
      Skip the line and order a meal made especially for your little warrior. Each future ruler will receive his or her own menu, filled with exciting things to keep him or her occupied during the meal. The Khans Kidz Menu gives your kids the freedom theyve always wanted by letting them choose what they want and how they want it.
     </p>
     <p>
      Get the family ready, and spend a night out with Genghis Grill! Your kids will love the choices on the Khans Kidz Menu! Going out to dinner with your family has never been easier than it is at Genghis Grill.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Not all items available at all locations, some items are only available at our select Fresh Casual stores. The only location in DFW is the Lakeworth location.
      </strong>
     </p>
