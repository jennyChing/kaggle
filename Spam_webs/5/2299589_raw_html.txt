                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
                     <a href="http://threefatesdesign.blogspot.com/2014/06/happy-monday-weekend-overview.html">
                      Happy Monday - Weekend Overview
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content">
                     Happy Monday everyone. Today starts my week long stay-cation. I started the day out by running the recycling to the curb because we forgot to take it out last night. It's okay, it got me up and out of bed. I don't really have any plans for this week, I just needed to use vacation time before I lost it at the end of the month (June is the end of the year when you work in academia). I do have a few things I would like to get done. I do plan on painting my canvas for my solar system project.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     And speaking of my solar system project, I got a little bit more beaded on Jupiter. Not much because we had a very interesting, very busy weekend, starting on Thursday. But I'm pretty happy with how it is turning out and I should be able to finish it it this week. So what do you guys think?
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="https://scontent-b-ord.xx.fbcdn.net/hphotos-xpa1/t1.0-9/1525202_10152444392417822_7571020611131052524_n.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="600" src="https://scontent-b-ord.xx.fbcdn.net/hphotos-xpa1/t1.0-9/1525202_10152444392417822_7571020611131052524_n.jpg" width="600"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     The other thing I would like to get done this week is painting my new studio. I picked up two more paint samples, hoping to find the winning one. Here are all four samples. I'm not going to let you know which one I picked until I start painting. It will be a surprise.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-I3wYj6XZVFE/U5Y3ACRvhWI/AAAAAAAAHRY/ycon0dpioK0/s1600/IMG_0013.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-I3wYj6XZVFE/U5Y3ACRvhWI/AAAAAAAAHRY/ycon0dpioK0/s1600/IMG_0013.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     That's all the beady things I did this weekend. We also went to a show on Friday night, which was fun. Then we had the first fire of the year when we got home. It was a lovely night. Saturday we had a friend over to hang out. I made him his first martini. He liked them, he liked them a lot. After some drinks, a Nerf gun fight may or may not have broken out. It was a busy and fun weekend. But now I have to get my butt in gear and get ready for my next art show, which is this coming Saturday.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     What did everyone else do this weekend? Any thing fun and art related? Any martini fueled Nerf gun fights?
