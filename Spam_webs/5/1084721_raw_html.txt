      <div class="row">
       <div class="col-md-8 col-md-offset-2 post-content-page-wrapper">
        <div class="post-content-page single">
         <p>
          I have never been somebody to use drugs, but I needed to share my experience with mushrooms. I have never appreciated anything in life. I am not trying to sound cynical or depressed, but nothing has ever excited me. I was constantly looking for some reason to live, something to be passionate about. My mind was cursed with the thought, "We are going to die anyway, what's the point of sticking it out."
         </p>
         <p>
          Yes, I understand that last statement sounds like the thoughts of someone who is about to willingly remove themselves from this life, but in my mind I was already dead. My actions and life was guided by the decisions of other people. It was no life of my own.
         </p>
         <p>
          Or so it seemed.
         </p>
         <p>
          After reading somebody's experience with mushrooms, I figured I had nothing to lose. Like I said, I had never done drugs before, never even smoked pot. I went in with no expectations, I was planning on just walking around the forest near my family's cabin in Oregon.
         </p>
         <p>
          I ate the mushrooms in the early afternoon, along with a peanut butter and jelly sandwich. They tasted like death. After beginning to feel the effects, I began my journey outside. I had no idea the mental journey I was in for. I immediately came across a pack of deer. I stared in disbelief as these beasts ripped through my presence. This was the first time in my life I can recount feeling beauty. The whole setting had had a presence of undying radiance and intensity. Each ray of light glistened and melted away in front of my eyes, painting a picture of light right before my eyes. It was utterly beautiful.
         </p>
         <p>
          I began to feel a sense of love and joy for everyone and everything. These feelings were so intensely genuine that, I can undeniably swear I have never had such an experience. My face had a smile painted on, nobody was around, but I was in awe at the beauty around me. Part of me anticipated that all these feeling would vanish as soon as the trip ended.
         </p>
         <p>
          I was wrong.
         </p>
         <p>
          These feeling have stuck with me. For some reason, I was not able to previously tap into this part of my mind. After taking mushrooms, It was like these walls broke that were preventing me from experiencing the beauty in life. I now understand why people choose to live. Beauty and passion, two aspects of life that used to be foreign to me, that now drive my life.
         </p>
         <p>
          I am so unbelievably grateful for this experience. Mushrooms did not just save my life, they gave me life. This story is just the gist, I could easily write a 15 page paper on the experience, but I rather not publish it. If anybody has any questions or comments about the experience, I will happily go into greater detail.
         </p>

		}

	//Note, we should refactor below once the messaging system is out.
	//Below is from https://gist.github.com/arbales/1654670
	var ESCAPE_EXPRESSION_FUNCTION, LINK_DETECTION_REGEX, MARKSAFE_FUNCTION, emojify, formatTextForHTML, linkify;

	//below regex is from: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/6927719/url-regex-does-not-work-in-javascript
	LINK_DETECTION_REGEX = /(([a-z]+:\/\/)?(([a-z0-9\-]+\.)+([a-z]{2}|aero|arpa|biz|com|coop|edu|gov|info|int|jobs|mil|museum|name|nato|net|org|pro|travel|local|internal))(:[0-9]{1,5})?(\/[a-z0-9_\-\.~]+)*(\/([a-z0-9_\-\.]*)(\?[a-z0-9+_\-\.%=&amp;]*)?)?(#[a-zA-Z0-9!$&amp;'()*+.=-_~:@/?]*)?)(\s+|$)/gi;

	ESCAPE_EXPRESSION_FUNCTION = Handlebars.Utils.escapeExpression;

	MARKSAFE_FUNCTION = function(str) {
	  return new Handlebars.SafeString(str);
	};
	linkify = function(safeContent) {
	  return safeContent.replace(LINK_DETECTION_REGEX, function(url) {
	  	brEnd = false;
	  	if(url.indexOf("\n")) {
	  		brEnd = true;
	  	}
	  	url = url.trim();
	    url = url.replace(/(?:\r\n|\r|\n)/g, '');
	    var address;
	    address = /[a-z]+:\/\//.test(url) ? url : "http://" + url;    
	    url = url.replace(/^https?:\/\//, '');
	    link = "&lt;a href='" + address + "' target='_blank' rel='nofollow'&gt;" + url + "&lt;/a&gt;";
	    if(brEnd) {
	    	return link + '&lt;br/&gt;';
	    } else {
	    	return link;
	    }
	  });
	};

	formatTextForHTML = function(content) {
	  var safeContent;
	  safeContent = ESCAPE_EXPRESSION_FUNCTION(content);
	  safeContent = linkify(safeContent);
	  safeContent = safeContent.replace(/(?:\r\n|\r|\n)/g, '&lt;br /&gt;');
	  //safeContent = emojify(safeContent);
	  return MARKSAFE_FUNCTION(safeContent);
	};

	Handlebars.registerHelper('formatTextForHTML', formatTextForHTML);
