          <div class="ndn_embed" data-config-distributor-id="90301" id="ndn-video-player-1">
          </div>
          <p>
           MEDFORD -- Police say an 83-year-old man who reportedly left home on Sunday to search for pine cones has been found dead, with his dog still by his side.
          </p>
          <p>
           Lt. Mike Budreau says Jackson County search and rescue personnel first found David Froling's truck in the area where he was thought to be but his keys were locked inside and there was no sign of him. Jackson County searchers then called for help from a group of search and rescue personnel from five southern Oregon counties.
          </p>
          <p>
           Searchers found Froling's body Tuesday afternoon in a heavily wooded area about a mile from his truck. His dog was in good health and was returned to family members.
          </p>
          <p>
           Budreau says the case is not considered suspicious.
          </p>
          <p>
           -- The Associated Press
          </p>
