        <div class="front-view-content excerpt">
         Wristband Inventory Save your colored Tyvek wristbands that you get when entering an event. Dont pull them apart when you get home but cleanly cut them off with scissors. They can be reattached with clear tape later. Ive kept all of my colored bands over the years and when going to an event, I just bring the inventory and wear the colored band that the event is using that day and get in free.
