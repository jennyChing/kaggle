        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Golden Triangle in India, the tourist circuit that includes the Taj city of Agra, capital city of New Delhi and Pink City of Jaipur in Rajasthan.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Golden Triangle tour is one of the prominent trip packages opted by honeymoon couples who cover Uttar Pradesh (Agra) and also the capital city of Rajasthan (Jaipur) at one go.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Trips to these historical cities in India last up to 7 days and involve a minimal budget for accommodation and shopping.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-941 size-large" data-lazy-original="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/279415_187645001297024_130264930368365_477448_4396015_o-500x360.jpg" height="360" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" title="The Golden Triangle Tour " width="500"/>
        </p>
        <noscript>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-941 size-large" height="360" src="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/279415_187645001297024_130264930368365_477448_4396015_o-500x360.jpg" title="The Golden Triangle Tour " width="500"/>
        </noscript>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The Golden triangle is one of the most traveled routes for tourists willing to discover the cultural and architectural gems of the country. The three cities of Agra, Delhi and Jaipur feature the perfect blend of Indo-Islamic architecture in its forts, monuments and palaces. Delhi sultanate to Mughals and the British, the capital city has its won charm with heritage, traditions, shopping and entertainment. Get to know what can make your honeymoon at Golden Triangle a memorable one.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify; font-size: 17px;">
         <strong>
          Fast Facts About Golden Triangle Tours in India :
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          When to Go
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          February  June and September  November
         </strong>
         is the best time for Golden Triangle tour. Summers and winters have extreme temperatures in most north Indian cities.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          What to Visit
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         For the last few years, The Golden Triangle cities in India are doing everything to impress honeymoon couples. Apart from the existing historical structures, these cities are gaining momentum for their shopping outlets, designer boutiques, restaurants, cafeterias and luxury hotels. There is no dearth of places of entertainment as well. Topping over the skyline are also the hotels owned by some international hotel groups that draw couples with their impeccable service, romantic setting and extraordinary amenities. You will stop by the following places during your Golden Triangle honeymoon.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Taj Mahal
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Tajmahal.jpg">
          <img alt="Taj Mahal Tour by Indian Holiday" class="aligncenter wp-image-3203 size-large" data-lazy-original="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Tajmahal-500x312.jpg" height="312" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="500"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Taj Mahal Tour by Indian Holiday" class="aligncenter wp-image-3203 size-large" height="312" src="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Tajmahal-500x312.jpg" width="500"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Monument of love and romance, whatever the architectural history behind the Taj Mahal, for couples, the marble mausoleum is a splendorous example of love. Built in honor of Mumtaz Mahal by her husband and Emperor Shah Jahan, The monument is awe inspiring with its architectural beauty, carvings and the romantic setup. A long pathway, surrounding garden and the fountain, gives it an absolute stunning look at night.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          [ Check out
          <a href="http://www.indianholiday.com/tour-packages-india/taj-mahal-tours.html">
           Taj Mahal Tour Packages
          </a>
          to plan your holiday in India ]
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.indianholiday.com/best-of-india/heritage-historic-sites/agra-fort.html">
          <strong>
           Agra Fort
          </strong>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Agra-Fort.jpg">
          <img alt="Agra Fort - A UNESCO World Heritage site located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India" class="aligncenter wp-image-3205 size-full" data-lazy-original="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Agra-Fort.jpg" height="334" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="500"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Agra Fort - A UNESCO World Heritage site located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India" class="aligncenter wp-image-3205 size-full" height="334" src="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Agra-Fort.jpg" width="500"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Another remarkable structure in Agra city located on the Yamuna banks. Its location and proximity to Taj Mahal makes it an important place of visit.Another World Heritage Site, Agra Fort is also the place where Shah Jahan remained imprisoned by his youngest son, Aurangzeb.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.indianholiday.com/best-of-india/monuments/amer-fort.html">
          <strong>
           Amer Fort
          </strong>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Amer-Fort-in-Jaipur-Rajasthan.jpg">
          <img alt="Amer Fort in Jaipur, Rajasthan,India" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3206" data-lazy-original="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Amer-Fort-in-Jaipur-Rajasthan-500x375.jpg" height="375" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="500"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Amer Fort in Jaipur, Rajasthan,India" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3206" height="375" src="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Amer-Fort-in-Jaipur-Rajasthan-500x375.jpg" width="500"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
         Among the premier forts and palaces of Jaipur, Amer Fort is located in the village of Amer and perched on a hill. The journey on elephants back to the fort can be an experience of a lifetime for you. You can have an insight into the Rajput style of architecture here as the palace overlooks the placid Maota Lake. Sheesh Mahal, Diwan-i-aim, Diwan-e-Khas and the central courtyard are its main attractions. You can unravel a lot of Rajputana history here.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          [Check out
          <a href="http://www.indianholiday.com/packages-by-destinations/jaipur.html">
           Jaipur Holiday Packages
          </a>
          at IndianHoliday.com]
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.indianholiday.com/best-of-india/monuments/red-fort.html">
          <strong>
           Red Fort
          </strong>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Red-Fort-New-Delhi.jpg">
          <img alt="Red Fort, New Delhi - the residence of the Mughal emperors of India" class="aligncenter wp-image-3207 size-large" data-lazy-original="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Red-Fort-New-Delhi-500x332.jpg" height="332" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="500"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Red Fort, New Delhi - the residence of the Mughal emperors of India" class="aligncenter wp-image-3207 size-large" height="332" src="http://blog.indianholiday.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Red-Fort-New-Delhi-500x332.jpg" width="500"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         New Delhis topmost attraction, Red Fort is a 17th century built complex that is listed as a World Heritage Site. Another Mughal monument in the capital city besides Humayuns tomb and Salimgarh Fort, it houses several important structures within such as MotI masjid, Zenana and Hayat Bakhsh Bagh.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          [Check Out the list of
          <a href="http://www.indianholiday.com/packages-by-destinations/new-delhi.html">
           Sightseeing Tours in New Delhi
          </a>
          ]
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Other places that interest tourists are:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          Qutb Minar, Delhi
         </li>
         <li>
          Jama Masjid, Delhi
         </li>
         <li>
          India Gate, Delhi
         </li>
         <li>
          Jantar Mantar, Jaipur
         </li>
         <li>
          City Palace, Jaipur
         </li>
         <li>
          Hawa Mahal, Jaipur
         </li>
         <li>
          Fatehpur Shikri, Agra
         </li>
         <li>
          Akbars Tomb at Sikandra
         </li>
         <li>
          Places to stay
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         There are budget, economy and luxury hotels in all these three cities. Some of the prominent ones in New Delhi are Hotel Imperial Delhi, The Metropolitan Hotel, Taj Mahal Hotel and Hyatt Regency. Popular hotels in Agra are Oberoi Amarvilas, Trident and Radisson Agra. In Jaipur city, you can stay at Oberoi Rajvilas, Taj Rambagh Palace, Marriott Hotel, Shiv Vilas Resort and ITC Rajputana.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          Shopping
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         New Delhi, Jaipur and Agra are paradise for shopaholics and especially if you do not want to miss this integral part of your honeymoon. Connaught Place, Karol Bagh, Palika Bazaar, Sarojini Market, Chandi Chowk and a line of designer boutiques make Delhi a sought after shopping locale. Agra appeals mainly for its stone handicrafts and marble artifacts. Jaipur is irresistible for its variety in bandhni sarees, salwar suits, Rajasthani wear, unique fabric, bags and kundan jewelry.
        </p>
