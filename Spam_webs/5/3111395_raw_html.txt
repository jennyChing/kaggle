        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Bacon Potato and Cheese Waffles
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2014-09-10T15:51:56+00:00">
          September 10, 2014
         </span>
          by
         <span class="author vcard">
          <span class="fn">
           <a class="fn n" href="http://www.momontimeout.com/author/trish/" rel="author" title="Trish - Mom On Timeout">
            Trish - Mom On Timeout
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         
         <span class="post-comments">

          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="16076828" data-link="http://www.momontimeout.com/2014/09/bacon-potato-and-cheese-waffles-recipe/" data-summary="" data-title="Bacon Potato and Cheese Waffles">
         </div>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          These freezer-friendly
          <em>
           <strong>
            Bacon Potato and Cheese Waffles
           </strong>
          </em>
          make school mornings just a little bit easier and alot more yummy!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Im crazy excited to be partnering with
          <a href="http://challengedairy.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           ChallengeDairy
          </a>
          as an ambassador for their Real Challenge Foodies program this year. Ill be bringing you awesome recipes, like this one, made with real Challenge butter!
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="These freezer-friendly Bacon, Potato and Cheese Waffles make school mornings just a little bit easier and a lot more yummy! | MomOnTimeout.com | #breakfast #waffles #Challenge #sponsored" class="aligncenter wp-image-7693" height="825" src="http://www.momontimeout.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/best-bacon-potato-cheese-waffles.jpg" width="550"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Anyone have any trouble at all on school mornings? You know, getting your sleepyhead kids out of bed, getting them dressed, checking for homework, packing lunches, and then getting them out the door and arriving at schoolon time? Shoot! I didnt even mention the most important part, and perhaps the most challenging, breakfast!
         </p>
         <p>
          School days are challengingno doubt. But they can be made easier with a little planning. These Bacon, Potato and Cheese Waffles are exactly what mama ordered. Packed full of flavor, my boys devour these waffles like there arent any more in the freezer
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="These freezer-friendly Bacon, Potato and Cheese Waffles make school mornings just a little bit easier and a lot more yummy! | MomOnTimeout.com | #breakfast #waffles #Challenge #sponsored" class="aligncenter wp-image-7542" height="825" src="http://www.momontimeout.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/bacon-potato-cheese-waffles-recipe.jpg" width="550"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          But there are! Along with our tried and true
          <a href="http://www.momontimeout.com/2011/08/breakfast-burrito-bonanza/" target="_blank">
           <em>
            freezer breakfast burritos
           </em>
          </a>
          , these waffles are a staple in our freezer. I went with bacon for this batch but sausage, ham or anything else you want to throw in there is just fine. You can make them fresh in the morning (they take under 20 minutes) or make ahead and freeze (for up to 3 weeks!). Pop them in the toaster on busy mornings, just as you would store-bought waffles, and breakfast is served in minutes!
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="These freezer-friendly Bacon, Potato and Cheese Waffles make school mornings just a little bit easier and a lot more yummy! | MomOnTimeout.com | #breakfast #waffles #Challenge #sponsored" class="aligncenter wp-image-7543" height="825" src="http://www.momontimeout.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/challenge-bacon-waffles.jpg" width="550"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          These waffles can be eaten on the go, for those
          <em>
           <strong>
            super
           </strong>
          </em>
          crazy mornings, or if youve got the time, topped off with yummy Challenge butter and drizzled with maple syrup. Serve with your favorite fruit and a glass of milk and breakfast is DONE. And youre like, dang, I am gooood!
         </p>
         <p>
          Challenge Butter is made from 100% real cream. For real. The cows that produce this milk are not treated with the growth hormone rbST. Its a butter that I feel good about feeding to my family. Its honestly my favorite butter and I have been using it FOREVER. Kind of awesome that I get to talk about them like, all the time now, because I really do love them SO much!
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="These freezer-friendly Bacon, Potato and Cheese Waffles make school mornings just a little bit easier and a lot more yummy! | MomOnTimeout.com | #breakfast #waffles #Challenge #sponsored" class="aligncenter wp-image-7544" height="770" src="http://www.momontimeout.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/plated-bacon-waffles.jpg" width="550"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Head on over to
          <a href="http://challengedairy.com/" target="_blank">
           Challenge Dairy
          </a>
          to get the full recipe for these
          <a href="http://challengedairy.com/community/bacon-potato-cheese-waffles" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           awesome waffles
          </a>
          !
         </p>
