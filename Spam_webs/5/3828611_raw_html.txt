        <p>
         Fall TV is here and in full swing. So many shows to watch and they are all so good! I was out of town for most of the firstthree weeks when all of the fall shows started. So now Tyler and I are very behind and have a DVR full of shows to watch. We have not even touched The Blacklist yet (gasp) and have watched only one episode of Scandal (SAY WHAT?). Its been hard to want to start the new shows but we have finally started some and man are they good. Today Im sharing
         <strong>
          3 New TV Shows You Should Be Watching
         </strong>
         !
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://lovepastatoolbelt.com/wp-content/uploads/3-New-TV-Shows.jpg">
          <img alt="3 New TV Shows that you should be Watching! If you aren't watching these new fall tv shows then you need to check them out now! " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5455" height="600" src="http://lovepastatoolbelt.com/wp-content/uploads/3-New-TV-Shows-1024x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         1.
         <strong>
          Jane the Virgin, Monday at 9/8c on The CW
         </strong>
          I was very skeptical when I heard the plot line for this show. A virgin girl who gets artificially inseminated, what? Well one day I was bored and caught up on all my shows that I watch without Tyler so I decided to give this one a go and and I was so pleasantly surprised. Its dramatic, funny, endearing and just enough soap opera but not too much. Im already caught up thanks to on demand and anxiously awaiting a new episode! Its already been picked up for a full season so I am excited about that!
        </p>
        <p>
         2.
         <strong>
          Scorpion, Monday at 9/8c on CBS
         </strong>
          When I heard this show was from the writers of Prison Break well there was no way I was saying no. I loved Prison Break, check it out on my shows to binge watch. We started this show and episode one was fast paced, exciting and also endearing. Can you tell I love a good endearing show? I like to feel an emotional attachment to the characters.
        </p>
        <p>
         3.
         <strong>
          Madam Secretary, Sunday 8/7c on CBS
         </strong>
          We finally started this show the other night and it is good! I had to remind myself that not every political drama is Scandal and goes so fast your head is spinning because this show seemed a bit slow at moments. But Tea Leoni is funny and I love Tim Daly so I was excited to see him in a show again too. Its nice to see a show about politics where someone is not a total politician. This show is dramatic, funny and dare I say, endearing?
        </p>
        <p>
         There are a lot of other good new shows out there but I cant list them all. Tell me, whats your favorite new show of the season?
        </p>
