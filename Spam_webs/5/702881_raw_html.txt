        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Iris &amp; June: Serving the people of Victoria
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-meta">
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Amongst the glass and concrete of Victoria, London, lies
         <a href="http://www.irisandjune.com/" target="_blank">
          Iris &amp; June
         </a>
         . This wonderful little artisan cafe provides quality coffee and mouth-watering food with an extra shot of good humour and courtesy.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The chilled out atmosphere is set by the neutral colour palette used throughout the interior of Iris &amp; June. Although it is not noticed straight away the light great walls match the colour of the concrete beams and floor, creating a gentleness to an otherwise stark material.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="IMG_7127RRC2" class="alignnone wp-image-657 size-full" height="877" src="http://marwakhalil.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/IMG_7127RRC2.jpg" width="1500"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         They grey is broken with charcoal and white glossy tiles covering the walls; further dimension is added to the clean interior as the space makes use of complimentary furniture of varying textures. White chairs and high blue stools accompany light plywood tables adding warmth to a primarily cool toned space.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Iris &amp; June makes great use of a neutral colour palette and despite the cafe being surrounded by offices the interior lacks any corporate heartlessness and instead offers a sophisticated warm space to relax in or to get a take away beverage. The varying shades of colours combined with different materials and textures ease you into the space; the concrete juxtaposed with the wood balances the greys and the sharp white components are balanced through the use of different textures and materials.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="IMG_7199RR" class="alignnone wp-image-658 size-full" height="1000" src="http://marwakhalil.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/IMG_7199RR.jpg" width="1500"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The lively music in the background combined with the sounds of the coffee machines at work creates a comfortable yet highly polished environment. The trendy magazines and the bursts of colour from the cups, saucers, salads and more all come together nicely to add to the experience.
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
          Iris &amp; June makes great use of a neutral colour palette and despite the cafe being surrounded by offices the interior lacks any corporate heartlessness and instead offers a sophisticated warm space to relax
         </h3>
        </blockquote>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Iris &amp; June has found the perfect blend of style catering for the artisan coffee shop lovers as well as the suited and booted men and women of Victoria. With the well balanced colours, textures, sounds and smells combined you get an enriching experience whenever you visit Iris &amp; June.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Photographs from
         <a href="http://www.irisandjune.com/" target="_blank">
          Iris &amp; June
         </a>
        </p>
