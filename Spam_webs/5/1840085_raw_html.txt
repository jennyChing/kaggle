           <p>
            Trying to find dates on Tinder can feel like youre trolling for the least rotten apple at the bottom of a very deep barrel. One comedian decided to make her Tinder experience a little less awful by pretending to be a soul sucking ghost on the dating app.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
           </p>
           <div class="ct-item ct-photo">
            <div class="scaling vignette">
             <img alt="" class="" src="http://media1.break.com/dnet/media/552/799/2799552/woman-pretends-to-be-a-horny-ghost-on-tinder-image-1.jpg" style="height:884px; width:498px"/>
            </div>
           </div>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            Admit it, youd still hit that.
           </strong>
           <p>
            Grace Spelman announced on
            <a href="https://twitter.com/gspels/status/544252319089852416">
             her Twitter page
            </a>
            that she re-downloaded the app and created an entirely new profile on Tinder featuring swirled, distorted pictures of her face so she could pretend to be a ghost accessing the app from some other plain of eternal existence. She re-uploaded a bunch of real photos featured her new 
            <em>
             Ring
            </em>
            -ish look and when guys tried to hit on her presumably because they are drunk or extremely farsighted, she spoke to them as if she was a real ghost.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <div class="ct-item ct-photo">
            <div class="scaling vignette">
             <img alt="" class="" src="http://media1.break.com/dnet/media/553/799/2799553/woman-pretends-to-be-a-horny-ghost-on-tinder-image-2.jpg" style="height:882px; width:497px"/>
            </div>
           </div>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <strong>
             Grace Spelman talked to guys on Tinder as if she was a real ghost, which is twice as scary because it implies that ghosts in the afterlife have a better cellphone connection than people in the real world.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            If that wasnt scary enough, she also pretended to be the worlds horniest ghost. We assume shes nothing like the ghost from Ghostbusters that has sex with Ray in one of his dreams. Im basically just imaging that scene but the ghost is only sucking on his soul.
           </p>
