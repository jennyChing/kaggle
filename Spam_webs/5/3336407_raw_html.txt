       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Parenting Quotes: 30 Funny and Inspirational Quotes that Perfectly Capture What Its Like to Be a Parent
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Becoming a parent is something you can never understand unless you experience it. Even then, its almost impossible to put into words the love you feel when you see what youve created. These 30 quotes give us an idea of what parenting feels like: the good, the bad, and the messy.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.corcell.com/wp-content/uploads/Parenting-quote-lasting-bequests.png">
          <img alt="Parenting-quote-lasting-bequests" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5593" height="300" src="https://www.corcell.com/wp-content/uploads/Parenting-quote-lasting-bequests.png" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         No one is ever quite ready; everyone is always caught off guard. Parenthood chooses you. And you open your eyes, look at what youve got, say Oh, my gosh, and recognize that of all the balls there ever were, this is the one you should not drop. Its not a question of choice.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            Marisa de los Santos, Love Walked In
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         When you take the time to actually listen, with humility, to what people have to say, its amazing what you can learn. Especially if the people who are doing the talking also happen to be children.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            Greg Mortenson, Stones Into Schools: Promoting Peace With Books, Not Bombs, in Afghanistan and Pakistan
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         When my kids become wild and unruly, I use a nice safe playpen. When theyre finished, I climb out.
         <br/>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
            Erma Bombeck
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.corcell.com/wp-content/uploads/Parenting-quote-normal-behavior1.png">
          <img alt="Parenting-quote-normal-behavior" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5622" height="300" src="https://www.corcell.com/wp-content/uploads/Parenting-quote-normal-behavior1.png" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Raising kids is part joy and part guerilla warfare.
         <br/>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
           Ed Asner
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Through the blur, I wondered if I was alone or if other parents felt the same way I did  that everything involving our children was painful in some way. The emotions, whether they were joy, sorrow, love or pride, were so deep and sharp that in the end they left you raw, exposed and yes, in pain. The human heart was not designed to beat outside the human body and yet, each child represented just that  a parents heart bared, beating forever outside its chest.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            Debra Ginsberg
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         I dont remember who said this, but there really are places in the heart you dont even know exist until you love a child.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            Anne Lamott, Operating Instructions: A Journal of My Sons First Year
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Parenting-quote-motherhood-is-a-verb2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5594" height="300" src="https://www.corcell.com/wp-content/uploads/Parenting-quote-motherhood-is-a-verb2.png" style="font-size: 13px;" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         You can learn many things from children. How much patience you have, for instance.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
           Franklin P. Adams
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         When a child is locked in the bathroom with water running and he says hes doing nothing but the dog is barking, call 911.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            Erma Bombeck
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Having one child makes you a parent; having two you are a referee.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
           -David Frost
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.corcell.com/wp-content/uploads/Parenting-quote-loving-for-abilities.png">
          <img alt="Parenting-quote-loving-for-abilities" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5597" height="300" src="https://www.corcell.com/wp-content/uploads/Parenting-quote-loving-for-abilities.png" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         I used to believe my father about everything but then I had children myself &amp; now I see how much stuff you make up just to keep yourself from going crazy.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            Brian Andreas, Story People: Selected Stories &amp; Drawings of Brian Andreas
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Everybody knows how to raise children, except the people who have them.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            P.J. ORourke
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you have never been hated by your child, you have never been a parent.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            Bette Davis
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Parenting-quote-unconditional-love" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5551" height="300" src="https://www.corcell.com/wp-content/uploads/Parenting-quote-unconditional-love.png" style="font-size: 13px;" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         While I was drying off Maddie after her bath tonight, she said, I love you to me for the first time. It sounded like All lub boo, but I didnt care. To reciprocate, I showed her what an ex-Marine looks like when he cries.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            Jim Beaver, Lifes That Way: A Memoir
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Before I had kids, I always found it funny how people would talk about their children like they were the cutest things on the planet and how every little thing they did was endlessly fascinating. Now that Ive had kids, I can say with certainty that, my children really are the cutest things on this planet and every little thing they do is endlessly fascinating
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            Jennifer Miller
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         I may not be able to give my kids everything they want but I give them what they need. Love, time, and attention. You cant buy those things.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            Nishan Panwar
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.corcell.com/wp-content/uploads/Parenting-quote-as-kids-grow-up.png">
          <img alt="Parenting-quote-as-kids-grow-up" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5598" height="300" src="https://www.corcell.com/wp-content/uploads/Parenting-quote-as-kids-grow-up.png" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         I believe that what we become depends on what our fathers teach us at odd moments, when they arent trying to teach us. We are formed by little scraps of wisdom.
         <br/>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
           
           <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
            Umberto Eco
           </span>
           ,
           <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
            Foucaults Pendulum
           </span>
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         All of us have moments in our lives that test our courage. Taking children into a house with a white carpet is one of them. 
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
           Erma Bombeck
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Always be nice to your children because they are the ones who will choose your rest home.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
           
           <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
            Phyllis Diller
           </span>
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Parenting-quote-six-theories" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5554" height="300" src="https://www.corcell.com/wp-content/uploads/Parenting-quote-six-theories1.png" style="font-size: 13px;" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The biggest thing I remember is that there was just no transition. You hit the ground diapering.
         <br/>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
           -Paul Reiser
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Were always bluffing, pretending we know best, when most of the time were just praying we wont screw up too badly.
         <br/>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
            Jodi Picoult, House Rules
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Having a child is liking getting a tattooon your face. You better be committed.
         <br/>
         <em>
          <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
            Eat Pray Love screenplay
          </span>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The quickest way for a parent to get a childs attention is to sit down and look comfortable.
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #c0c0c0;">
          <em>
            Lane Olinghouse
          </em>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
