        <p>
         Songs about Sisters  I had so much fun putting this list together. Having a sister myself, I can only imagine how much fun it would be to perform together.The relationship between sisters is special so it is not surprising that dancers and parents enjoy an opportunity for siblings to share the stage together. Whether you are looking for a sister song for a performance or a slideshow, the list of sister songs below offers a variety of theme options that reflect the special bondsisters have with one another.
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          You can kid the world. But not your sister. ~Charlotte Gray
          <!--PIH-->
         </p>
        </blockquote>
