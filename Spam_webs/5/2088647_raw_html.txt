       <p>
        Volkswagen Scirocco GT24 CNG
       </p>
       <a class="enlarge" href="/image/100310423_volkswagen-scirocco-gt24-cng" name="tccwrp_100310423" target="_blank">
        Enlarge Photo
       </a>
      </div>
      <p>
       A couple of months ago, we told you about Volkswagen's
       <a href="http://www.greencarreports.com/blog/1042615_green-racer-2010-volkswagen-jetta-tdi-cup-street-edition" title="Green Racer: 2010 Volkswagen Jetta TDI Cup Street Edition">
        2010 Jetta TDI Cup Street Edition
       </a>
       , a specially trimmed version of its clean-diesel Jetta sedan that commemorates the TDI Cup racing series, now in its third season.
      </p>
      <p>
       But VW has other green racing activities elsewhere too. Our sister site Motor Authority notes that Volkswagen will race a fleet of three natural-gas powered car on the famed Nrburgring circuit in Germany in a 24-hour endurance race that starts on May 12.
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="image_wrapper">
       <img alt="2010 Volkswagen Jetta TDI Cup Street Edition" data-height="563" data-url="http://images.thecarconnection.com/lrg/volkswagen_100307589_l.jpg" data-width="1024" height="352" name="tccimg_100307589_m" src="http://images.thecarconnection.com/med/volkswagen_100307589_m.jpg" title="2010 Volkswagen Jetta TDI Cup Street Edition" width="640"/>
       <p>
        2010 Volkswagen Jetta TDI Cup Street Edition
       </p>
       <a class="enlarge" href="/image/100307589_2010-volkswagen-jetta-tdi-cup-street-edition" name="tccwrp_100307589" target="_blank">
        Enlarge Photo
       </a>
      </div>
      <p>
       The cars are all Scirocco coupes, a model not sold here but based on the same underpinnings as the more familiar Jetta sedan and Golf hatchback. They are fitted with a 2.0-liter engine that puts out 330 horsepower as modified to run on compressed natural gas (CNG).
      </p>
      <p>
       That same engine, running on gasoline, gave VW first and second places in last year's 2.0-liter turbo class.
      </p>
      <p>
       The CNG conversion cuts the cars' carbon dioxide emissions by 80 percent. Other modifications include larger brakes (hardly a surprise) and an active electro-hydraulic differential.
      </p>
      <p>
       As we recently pointed out, the sole natural-gas vehicle sold in the U.S. is the 2010 Honda Civic GX, which
       <a href="http://www.greencarreports.com/blog/1044398_natural-gas-honda-civic-to-oklahoma-as-u-s-lags-in-ngvs" title=" Natural-Gas Honda Civic To Oklahoma As U.S. Lags in NGVs Natural-Gas Honda Civic To Oklahoma As U.S. Lags in NGVs">
        just went on sale in Utah
       </a>
       , only the fourth state in which it's available to retail buyers.
      </p>
      <p>
       That car has won the title of "America's Greenest Car" from the American Council for an Energy-Efficient Economy (ACEEE) every year since 2004. And it's the cleanest vehicle with an internal combustion engine the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency has ever tested.
      </p>
      <p>
       We suspect a 330-hp racing Scirocco might not be quite as green as the 113-hp Civic GX. But it's still going to be very clean indeed.
      </p>
      <p>
       [
       <a href="http://www.motorauthority.com/blog/1044284_volkswagen-returns-to-nurburgring-with-scirocco-gt24-cng-race-car" title="Volkswagen Returns To Nurburgring With Scirocco GT24 CNG Race Car">
        Motor Authority
       </a>
       ]
      </p>
