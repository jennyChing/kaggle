            <h1>
             Renaturation of Recombinant Proteins as Inclusion Bodies
            </h1>
           </div>
           <div class="col-md-3" style="padding-top:8px;">
            <!-- AddThis Button BEGIN -->
            <a class="addthis_button" href="http://www.addthis.com/bookmark.php?v=300&amp;pubid=xa-5184e2be4186e26e">
             <img alt="Bookmark and Share" height="16" src="http://s7.addthis.com/static/btn/v2/lg-share-en.gif" style="border:0" width="125"/>

            <div class="btn-sm btn-success" style="width:120px;margin-top:8px;">
             <a href="/order.aspx?i1=Renaturation of Recombinant Proteins as Inclusion Bodies" style="color:#fff; text-decoration:none;">

         <div class="servicecontent">
          <div class="cents" style="margin: 10px auto; ">
           <p>
            Recombinant proteins expressed in bacteria often form inclusion bodies, especially when they are expressed at high levels. Creative BioMart retrieves expressed GST fusion protein in soluble form after lysis and extraction procedures.
           </p>
           <div class="row">
            <div class="col-md-6">
             <ul style="list-style:disc;">
              <strong>
               Advantages:
               <br/>
              </strong>
              <li style="line-height:22px;padding:3px 0px 3px 35px; background-image:url(images/cGMP%20Cell-based%20Potency%20Assays-3.png); background-repeat:no-repeat;">
               Specifically for solubilizing and renaturing GST inclusion bodies.
              </li>
              <li style="line-height:22px;padding:3px 0px 3px 35px; background-image:url(images/cGMP%20Cell-based%20Potency%20Assays-3.png); background-repeat:no-repeat;">
               Time saving: without lengthy dialysis or dilution step.
              </li>
              <li style="line-height:22px;padding:3px 0px 3px 35px; background-image:url(images/cGMP%20Cell-based%20Potency%20Assays-3.png); background-repeat:no-repeat;">
               No pH variation and Redox Pair involved.
              </li>
             </ul>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6">
             <p>
              <img alt="" class="img-responsive" height="450" src="images/Renaturation of Recombinant Proteins-1.jpg" width="300"/>
             </p>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="clear">
           </div>
           <p>
            Refolding of the solubilised proteins is initiated by the removal of the denaturant. The efficiency of refolding depends on the competition between correct folding and aggregation. To slow down the aggregation process refolding is usually carried out at low protein concentrations, in the range of 10-100 mg/ml. Furthermore, refolding conditions must be optimized for each individual protein.
           </p>
           <div class="row">
            <div class="col-md-6">
             <ul style="list-style:disc;">
              <strong>
               Methods for the refolding of proteins:
              </strong>
              <br/>
              <li style="line-height:22px;padding:3px 0px 3px 35px; background-image:url(images/cGMP%20Cell-based%20Potency%20Assays-3.png); background-repeat:no-repeat;">
               Dialysis.
              </li>
              <li style="line-height:22px;padding:3px 0px 3px 35px; background-image:url(images/cGMP%20Cell-based%20Potency%20Assays-3.png); background-repeat:no-repeat;">
               Slow dilution.
              </li>
              <li style="line-height:22px;padding:3px 0px 3px 35px; background-image:url(images/cGMP%20Cell-based%20Potency%20Assays-3.png); background-repeat:no-repeat;">
               Rapid dilution.
              </li>
              <li style="line-height:22px;padding:3px 0px 3px 35px; background-image:url(images/cGMP%20Cell-based%20Potency%20Assays-3.png); background-repeat:no-repeat;">
               Pulse renaturation.
              </li>
              <li style="line-height:22px;padding:3px 0px 3px 35px; background-image:url(images/cGMP%20Cell-based%20Potency%20Assays-3.png); background-repeat:no-repeat;">
               Chromatography.
              </li>
             </ul>
            </div>
            <div class="col-md-6">
             <img alt="" class="img-responsive" height="300" src="images/Renaturation of Recombinant Proteins-2.jpg" width="380"/>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="clear">
           </div>
           <br/>
           <p>
            Creative BioMart has been successfully refolded many recombinant proteins. We have accumulated a lot of experience in renaturation of recombinant proteins as inclusion bodies. Creative BioMart can provide professional technical service for you.
