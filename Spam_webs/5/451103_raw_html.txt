        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         For The Big Game: SNICKERS Banana Pudding
        </h1>
       </div>
      </header>
      <div class="wrapper">
       <div class="grids">
        <div class="grid-8 column-1">
         <div class="entry-media">
         </div>
         <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
          <div id="triberr_endorsement">
          </div>
          <p>
           This shop has been compensated by Collective Bias, Inc. and its advertiser. All opinions are mine alone. #BigGameTreats #CollectiveBias
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="snickersbananapudding" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-32088" height="1050" src="http://sophistishe.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/snickersbananapudding.jpg" width="700"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           We are true football enthusiasts in that Greg never misses a game and I love drooling over andeating all of the delicious game day inspired finger foods. Over the last few years, Iveshared a few
           <a href="http://sophistishe.com/tag/football/" target="_blank">
            football inspired recipes and spread ideas
           </a>
           and today, I want to share a dessert perfect for the Big Game. Rather than focus on grab-able confections, I decided to do something a little different when challenged by
           <a href="https://www.snickers.com/" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank">
            SNICKERS
           </a>
           to create a recipe perfect for a football viewing party.
          </p>
          <p>
           How many of you love banana pudding? Have you ever wondered how itd taste with the addition of hearty lil nougat and peanut pieces throughout? Well, now is the perfect time to get in your kitchen and find out! Ive run out words to describe desserts.
           <em>
            Fantastic
           </em>
           comes to mind. Just know that when you take your first scoop, youll be satisfied.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="snickersbananapudding3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-32118" height="820" src="http://sophistishe.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/snickersbananapudding3.jpg" width="700"/>
          </p>
          <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
           <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
            SNICKERS Banana Pudding
           </div>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
           <div class="ERSTopRight">
            <img alt="" itemprop="image" src="http://sophistishe.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/snickersbananapudding2-296x197.jpg" title="" width="205"/>
            <div class="ERSSavePrint">
             <span class="ERSSaveBtnSpan">
              <a class="ERSSaveBtn bigoven" href="">
               Save
              </a>
             </span>
             <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
              <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://sophistishe.com/easyrecipe-print/32086-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               Print
              </a>
             </span>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="divERSHeadItems">
            <div class="ERSAuthor">
             Author:
             <span itemprop="author">
              Sheena Tatum
             </span>
            </div>
            <div class="ERSCuisine">
             Cuisine:
             <span itemprop="recipeCuisine">
              dessert
             </span>
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSIngredients">
            <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
             Ingredients
            </div>
            <ul>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 (12 oz) container of whipped topping, thawed
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 cups milk
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 (5 oz) box instant French vanilla pudding mix
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              6 to 8 bananas, sliced
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              14 oz of butter cookies
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
              15 SNICKERS Mini's, chopped in fourths
             </li>
            </ul>
            <div class="ERSClear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSInstructions">
            <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
             Instructions
            </div>
            <ol>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Line the bottom of a 13x9x2-inch dish or bowl with cookies and layer bananas on top.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Using a mixer, combine the milk and pudding. In a separate mixing bowl, mix the cream cheese and condensed milk together until smooth.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Fold the whipped topping into the cream cheese mixture. Add the cream cheese mixture to the pudding mixture and stir until well blended.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Pour the pudding over the cookie and banana and layer with remaining cookies and SNICKERS pieces. Depending on the size of your dish or bowl, you can create layers of cookies, bananas and SNICKERS.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Refrigerate until ready to serve.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <div class="ERSClear">
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSNotesDiv">
            <div class="ERSNotesHeader">
             Notes
            </div>
            <div class="ERSNotes">
             Adapted by Paula Deen
