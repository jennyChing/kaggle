       <h1 class="title">
        Fresh Firebird Mascara
       </h1>
       <div class="post-meta">
        <span class="small">
         By
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://canadianbeauty.com/author/henna/" rel="author" title="Posts by Henna">
           Henna
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="small">
         on
        </span>
        <abbr class="date time published" title="2008-09-18T08:35:32-0600">
         September 18, 2008
        </abbr>
        <span class="small">
         in
        </span>
        <span class="categories">
         <a href="http://canadianbeauty.com/topics/collections/search-for-the-perfect-mascara/">
          Search for the Perfect Mascara
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry">
        <div class="pf-content">
         <p>
          Ive
          <a href="http://canadianbeauty.com/2008/09/most-used-beauty-product/">
           alluded
          </a>
          to the fact that I have a ton of mascara reviews coming up, and this is one of those.
         </p>
         <p>
          While I mostly stick to drugstore mascaras, I will say that the good thing about buying their higher-priced counterparts is that they usually do deliver.
         </p>
         <p>
          Take
          <a href="http://sephora.com/browse/product.jhtml?id=P219415&amp;categoryId=C8330&amp;shouldPaginate=true">
           Freshs Firebird Mascara
          </a>
          . It comes in a lovely blue bottle that does any vanity proud, and though not fantastic on the volumizing side like the copy would have you believe, this mascara does give good separation and coverage. Its a light formula so it wont make your lashes look fake and I found the formula to be light and glossy. I got lush lashes from this and I looked more wide-eyed than I normally do, so I was happy with this mascara. Ill also say that although there are conditioning oils in the formula, this mascara did not smear on me.
         </p>
