          <br/>
          We also adapted the base design too and it came out GREAT! I love working with concrete and creating one of a kind pieces. No perfection here-just handmade garden art and working pieces for the garden.
          <br/>
          This bench cost less than $20 to create and with a frame/mold that we tweak each time, there are no extra costs to create more for friends or to sell.
          <br/>
          I have to make a couple benches soon and we have yet another idea to change up the look! Create and inspire! My hubby is really enjoying helping me create but a cement mixer is now on our "must have" list.
