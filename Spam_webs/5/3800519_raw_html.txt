       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Better Homes and Gardens December Issue Feature and White Chocolate Mudslides
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-11-17T07:00:32+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 17, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/author/jocelyn/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Jocelyn (Grandbaby Cakes)
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/2014/11/white-chocolate-mudslides/#comments">
           92 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <img alt="White Chocolate Mudslides | Grandbaby Cakes" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3295" height="626" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/photo-1-768x1024.jpg" width="470"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Today, Im so excited to finally spread the news about my holiday entertaining cookie swap feature in the 2014 December issue of
         <a href="http://www.bhg.com/" target="_blank" title="Better Homes and Gardens">
          Better Homes and Gardens
         </a>
         which hits the stands today. This feature shows how you can throw a super dainty and fun cookie swap for your girlfriends.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="White Chocolate Mudslides | Grandbaby Cakes" class="aligncenter wp-image-3320 size-medium" height="416" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/1416163274.741954.66-e1416163464515-312x416.jpg" width="312"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         To celebrate, I am sharing my White Chocolate Mudslides recipe that were part of this feature.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="White Chocolate Mudslides | Grandbaby Cakes" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3314" height="705" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/White-Chocolate-Mudslides-1-682x1024.jpg" width="470"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Back in August, we had the photoshoot for the feature in the suburbs of Chicago and had a blast. It is so amazing to see how much goes into making a professional magazine shoot perfection. Prior to the shoot, I worked with the editors for a month on inspiration, ideas, recipes, casting, and so much more. Once we got to the shoot, it was great to see it all come together, starting with some good hair and makeup (yep it takes a lot to get photo ready).
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="White Chocolate Mudslides | Grandbaby Cakes" class="aligncenter wp-image-3305 size-medium" height="416" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/929200_267595483364304_1494022221_n-416x416.jpg" width="416"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         A few of my gal pals like my dear friends
         <a href="http://www.hipfoodiemom.com" target="_blank" title="Hip Foodie Mom">
          Alice
         </a>
         of Hip Foodie Mom blog and
         <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.com/kim-bellware/" target="_blank" title="Kim Bellware">
          Kim
         </a>
         who is an editor for Huffington Post were ready to have some fun!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="1388839_275394522584253_1162931339_n" class="aligncenter wp-image-3303 size-medium" height="416" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/1388839_275394522584253_1162931339_n-416x416.jpg" width="416"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         We shot at the most gorgeous house ever. Thats my mom sitting below on the phone working being the best assistant/stylist ever. She is every bit as important to the Grandbaby Cakes machine (I just made that up) as I am. So grateful for her!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="White Chocolate Mudslides | Grandbaby Cakes" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3306" height="470" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/photo-5-e1416159960302-1024x1024.jpg" width="470"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Here I am ready to get on set and shoot! And yes, I did eat cookies while we shot. It was a cookie swap story afterall. You can also see all the wardrobe choices shattered everywhere.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="White Chocolate Mudslides | Grandbaby Cakes" class="aligncenter wp-image-3309 size-medium" height="416" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/1404403_10154919277240454_2522081310494855293_o-416x416.jpg" width="416"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         After we shot several different scenes, the team wrapped! I have to say that the BHG team was absolutely incredible, so professional and such a joy to work with. This was one of the best experiences of my life! I am so blessed I had the opportunity to work with them.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Whie Chocolate Mudslides | Grandbaby Cakes" class="aligncenter wp-image-3304 size-medium" height="416" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/10598201_539220666224230_1358541338_n-416x416.jpg" width="416"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I am beyond grateful that I had my friends take part in the magic of that day. My pals
         <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.com/kim-bellware/" target="_blank" title="Kim Bellware">
          Kim
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://tv.suntimes.com/show/minority-report" target="_blank" title="Minority Report">
          Micaela
         </a>
         (host of the Chicago Sun-Times show Minority Report),
         <a href="http://www.hipfoodiemom.com" target="_blank" title="Hip Foodie Mom">
          Alice
         </a>
         , Soo (marketing director) and
         <a href="http://www.thekittchen.com" target="_blank" title="The Kittchen">
          Kit (
         </a>
         food blog writer of The Kittchen) were the best group possible to pull this shoot together. We had a blast, and it definitely shows in the story. Make sure you pick up a copy today!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="White Chocolate Mudslides | Grandbaby Cakes" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3302" height="626" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/photo-3-e1416161016232-768x1024.jpg" width="470"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Now back to these mudslides. These are perfection and oh so simple to make. Thats why I think they are the perfect drink for a cookie swap with your best girlfriends. It is dainty when served in martini glasses but down home and cute when served in mugs like the ones I have below.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="White Chocolate Mudslides | Grandbaby Cakes" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3315" height="705" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/White-Chocolate-Mudslides-2-682x1024.jpg" width="470"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I added a fun twist by adding some melted white chocolate to the mixture. These taste like a glorified milkshake, the best one you will ever have. We drank quite a few of these on set. I wish I was exaggerating but Im not. We loved these! And you will too. Check out the recipe below and check out the cookie recipes featured in the swap spread
         <a href="http://www.bhg.com/cookieparty" target="_blank" title="BHG Cookie Party">
          HERE at BHG.com
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="White Chocolate Mudslides | Grandbaby Cakes" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-3316" height="705" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/White-Chocolate-Mudslides-3-682x1024.jpg" width="470"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          White Chocolate Mudslides
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTopRight">
          <img itemprop="image" src="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/White-Chocolate-Mudslides-3.jpg" width="205"/>
          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://www.grandbaby-cakes.com/easyrecipe-print/3298-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Print
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSAuthor">
           Author:
           <span itemprop="author">
            Jocelyn Delk Adams of Grandbaby-Cakes.com
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSServes">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            Makes 4-5 for martini glasses or 2 for mugs
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup white chocolate chips plus 2 tablespoons milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 cups vanilla ice cream
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup Kahlua
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup Irish cream
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 to 5 raspberries for garnish
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add white chocolate chips and milk to a small microwave safe container and microwave for 30 seconds on high.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Stir together melted chips and milk until smooth and set aside to cool.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Next add ice cream, Kahlua, Irish cream and melted chocolate to high power blender.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Blend ingredients until smooth and thick then pour mixture in glasses.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add one raspberry to the rim of each glass and serve.
           </li>
          </ol>
