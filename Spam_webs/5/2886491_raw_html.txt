     <h1>
      3 Creative Ideas For Your Personalised Fridge Magnets
     </h1>
     <h2 style="margin-bottom:3px;">
      Make Personalised Fridge Magnets Even More Fun
     </h2>
     <div class="blog_content_table">
      <div class="blog_content_left">
       <div class="blog_content_precis">
        Personalised fridge magnets can be so much more than a nice way to pin memos to your fridge. Check out these awesome ideas.
       </div>
       <div class="blog_content_content">
        <div>
         What do you do with your photos once youve turned them into personalised fridge magnets? Slap them on your fridge and let them brighten up your day every time you go to make a coffee. Maybe use them to pin notices to the fridge. Andhmmm.
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         Of course the main reason for turning your photos into fridge magnets is to have them preserved beautifully and always on display. But there are some creative ideas for your personalised fridge magnets which will make them much more appealing, especially to kids.
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         <ol>
          <li>
           <span style="font-size: 11pt;">
            Create A Tic Tac Toe Game. You might be wondering how to turn personalised fridge magnets into a tic tac toe game. Its pretty simple, especially since our fridge magnets come printed as a sheet of 9. All you have to do is decide what you want to represent the O and the X. It could be your dog and cat, or you and your significant other. All you need to do is select 5 different pictures of one to represent the X and four for the O. Then find your magnetic surface and let the game begin.
            <br/>
           </span>
           <br/>
          </li>
          <li>
           Turn Your Pictures Into A Puzzle. To make a puzzle out of your personalised fridge magnets youll probably want to use a photo editing suite to divide a single image into several sections. In Photoshop this can be as simple as selecting the Slice tool and then choosing Divide Slice from the options menu and telling the program how many evenly spaced slices you want on the horizontal and vertical axes. For a simple 9 piece puzzle just select 3 horizontal and vertical slices and then save each slice individually. Upload the images and youll have a fun little puzzle to adorn your fridge.
           <br/>
           <br/>
          </li>
          <li>
           <span style="font-size: 11pt;">
            Make A Magnetic Storyboard. Kids love to tell stories and a great idea for your personalised fridge magnets is to turn them into props to make a magnetic story board. You can let their imagination turn wild by selecting exciting pictures that will get their creative juices flowing. Theyll find making up stories involving themselves and their family fascinating and youll be amazed at how absorbed theyll become in the process.
           </span>
           <span style="font-size: 11pt;">
            Whether youre keeping your personalised fridge magnets for yourself or giving them as a gift for friends and family, these ideas make them even more appealing. Better yet theyre easy to achieve. Select your pictures. Upload them to your PicStick account and youre good to go. Try it for yourself today.
           </span>
           <br/>
          </li>
         </ol>
