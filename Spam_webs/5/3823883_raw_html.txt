         <p>
          Im just a little in love with marshmallows  They are good with pretty much everything.
          <a href="http://ashleemarie.com/peanut-butter-cookie-smores/" title="Peanut Butter Cookie Smores">
           Smores
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://ashleemarie.com/the-worlds-best-hot-chocolate/" title="The worlds BEST Hot Chocolate">
           Hot chocolate
          </a>
          , Cake,
          <a href="http://ashleemarie.com/smore-donut-recipe/" title="Smore Donut Recipe">
           Donuts
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://ashleemarie.com/marshmallow-ice-cream-recipe/" title="Marshmallow Ice Cream Recipe">
           ice cream
          </a>
          and of course by themselves. I honestly cant think of much I WOULDNT enjoy with marshmallows. And HOMEMADE marshmallows are EASY and OH so good!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a title="homemade marshmallows are easy to make and taste amazing.  This recipe is super easy to make">
           <img alt="homemade marshmallows are easy to make and taste amazing.  This recipe is super easy to make" class=" aligncenter" height="840" src="//farm9.staticflickr.com/8667/16209478104_55da932cb6_o.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          <span id="more-14459">
          </span>
          <br/>
          Like SERIOUSLY so good! My husband doesnt love sweets (I know, RIGHT???) but even he loved these! I barely had enough left for pictures after he got to them I almost had to make a new batch. darn it!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a title="homemade marshmallow recipe">
           <img alt="homemade marshmallow recipe" class=" aligncenter" height="840" src="//farm8.staticflickr.com/7600/16831819925_c171ae6869_o.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
          <link href="//ashleemarie.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/easy-homemade-marshmallows-500x329.jpg" itemprop="image"/>
          <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
           Homemade Marshmallows
          </div>

          </div>

          <div class="ERSIngredients">
           <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
            Ingredients
           </div>
           <ul>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 Tbsp
             <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=unflavored%20gelatin&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;rh=i%3Aaps%2Ck%3Aunflavored%20gelatin&amp;sprefix=unflavo%2Caps%2C265&amp;tag=imtotu-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Daps" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=unflavored%20gelatin&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;rh=i%3Aaps%2Ck%3Aunflavored%20gelatin&amp;sprefix=unflavo%2Caps%2C265&amp;tag=imtotu-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Daps', 'gelatin');">
              gelatin
             </a>
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/3 C cold water
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 C sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 1/2 C water
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 1/2 tsp vanilla
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/3 C cornstarch
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/3 C powdered sugar
            </li>
           </ul>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSInstructions">
           <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
            Instructions
           </div>
           <ol>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Soak the gelatin in the 1/3 of a cup of cold water in a small bowl and set aside to swell for 10 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In a large saucepan dissolve the sugar in the 1 1/2 C of water over low heat, stirring constantly.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Add the swollen gelatin and dissolve.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Raise the temperature and bring to a boil.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Boil steadily, but not vigorously, for 15 minutes without stirring.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Remove from the heat and allow to cool until lukewarm.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Pour into a bowl, or mixer and add the vanilla and beat the mixture until very thick and white.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Spray a 9x13 pan (or 9x9 inch pan for taller marshmallows) with
             <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;keywords=bakers%20joy&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;qid=1411372021&amp;rh=n%3A16320331%2Ck%3Abakers%20joy&amp;rnid=2941120011&amp;tag=imtotu-20&amp;linkId=ZBJYUWAXVSHWZ3IK" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;keywords=bakers%20joy&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;qid=1411372021&amp;rh=n%3A16320331%2Ck%3Abakers%20joy&amp;rnid=2941120011&amp;tag=imtotu-20&amp;linkId=ZBJYUWAXVSHWZ3IK', 'bakers joy');">
              bakers joy
             </a>
             and let set
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cut into squares and
             <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00NSQF7N8/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00NSQF7N8&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=imtotu-20&amp;linkId=WZQ2YC5B55SN3Q44" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00NSQF7N8/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00NSQF7N8&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=imtotu-20&amp;linkId=WZQ2YC5B55SN3Q44', 'dust ');">
              dust
             </a>
             with a mixture of cornstarch and icing sugar.
            </li>
           </ol>
           <div class="ERSClear">
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSNotesDiv">
           <div class="ERSNotesHeader">
            Notes
           </div>
           <div class="ERSNotes">
            Tip- I use my
            <a href="http://bit.ly/cakepoproller" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://bit.ly/cakepoproller', 'Cake Pop Roller');">
             Cake Pop Roller
            </a>
            to cut the marshmallow's quickly, evenly and easily!
           </div>

           </div>

         <p>
          <a title="no corn syrup homemade marshmallows">
           <img alt="no corn syrup homemade marshmallows" class=" aligncenter" height="840" src="//farm8.staticflickr.com/7636/16209478064_1219f44018_o.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          So I recently bought a
          <a href="http://bit.ly/cakepoproller" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://bit.ly/cakepoproller', 'Cake Pop Roller');">
           Cake Pop Roller
          </a>
          and am in LOVE with it! It seriously rolls everything THEN she told me it it also cuts marshmallows.. WHAT! I love homemade marshmallows, but cutting them is SUCH a pain, spraying the knife, cutting, cleaning, spraying it again, cutting. SUCH a pain! So I HAD to try it out
         </p>
         <style>
          .embed-container { position: relative; padding-bottom: 56.25%; height: 0; overflow: hidden; max-width: 100%; } .embed-container iframe, .embed-container object, .embed-container embed { position: absolute; top: 0; left: 0; width: 100%; height: 100%; }
         </style>
         <div class="embed-container">
          <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="411" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/1Z9LfBasCP0?&amp;rel=0&amp;autohide=1&amp;fs=0&amp;modestbranding=0&amp;showinfo=0&amp;theme=light" width="730">
          </iframe>
         </div>
         <p>
          I actually filmed the very first time I tried it and I wasnt sure if it would really work, ITS AMAZING! Seriously I am SOOOO going to be making marshmallows WAY more often than I used to because of this!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a title="quick, easy and delicious, homemade marshmallow recip">

         <p>
