          <h1>
           NJ Mom Accused Of Taking 5-Year-Old Into Tanning Booth
          </h1>
          <div class="post_meta">
           <span class="author_avatar">
            <a href="http://943thepoint.com/author/lauriecataldo/" title="Laurie Cataldo">
             <img src="http://wac.450f.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/943thepoint.com/files/2012/03/Laurie-Cataldo2.jpg?w=40&amp;h=40&amp;zc=1&amp;s=0&amp;a=t&amp;q=89"/>
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="the_author">
            <span>
             By
            </span>
            <a href="http://943thepoint.com/author/lauriecataldo/" title="Laurie Cataldo">
             Laurie Cataldo
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="the_date">
            May 2, 2012 12:02 PM
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="social-group-top large">
           <div class="social_icons size_large">
            <a class="facebook share_link" href="http://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?u=http%3A%2F%2F943thepoint.com%2Fnj-mom-accused-of-taking-5-year-old-into-tanning-booth%2F%3Ftrackback%3Dfbshare_top&amp;t=NJ+Mom+Accused+Of+Taking+5-Year-Old+Into+Tanning+Booth" target="_blank" title="Share on Facebook">
             <div class="social_icon_large">
             </div>
             <span class="social_icon">
              Share on Facebook
             </span>

            <a class="twitter share_link" href="http://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Check+out+NJ+Mom+Accused+Of+Taking+5-Year-Old+Into+Tanning+Booth+http%3A%2F%2F943thepoint.com%2Fnj-mom-accused-of-taking-5-year-old-into-tanning-booth%2F%3Ftrackback%3Dtwitter_top+%40943thepoint" target="_blank" title="Share on Twitter">
             <div class="social_icon_large">
             </div>
             <span class="social_icon">
              Share on Twitter
             </span>
            </a>
            <span class="social-submenu">
            </span>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="the_content clearfix">
           <figure class="alignleft" id="attachment_76180">
            <a class="fancybox" href="http://wac.450f.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/943thepoint.com/files/2012/05/tanlady.jpg">
             <img alt="Patricia Krentcil (Tanning Booth Mom)" class="size-medium wp-image-76180" height="179" src="http://wac.450f.edgecastcdn.net/80450F/943thepoint.com/files/2012/05/tanlady-300x179.jpg" title="Patricia Krentcil (Tanning Booth Mom)" width="300"/>
            </a>
            <figcaption style="width:300px;">
             WCBS-TV
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            Figures this would happen in Jersey.
           </p>
           <p>
            A 44-year-old woman was arrested and charged with second-degree child endangerment after she allegedly brought her 5-year-old daughter into a tanning booth.
            <span id="more-76178">
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            According to
            <a href="http://abcnews.go.com/US/jersey-mother-accused-letting-daughter-tanning-bed/story?id=16258272#.T6FLRVaTPZ8" target="_blank">
             ABC News
            </a>
            , Patricia Krentcil of Nutley claims her daughters sunburn was a result of doing gardening outside during the recent warm spell.
           </p>
           <p>
            When her daughter Anna went to school and the nurse noticed a rash, Anna said it was from going tanning with her mother.
           </p>
           <p>
            Now, obviously, from looking at her face, this woman has a problem with tanning. Im all for a nice golden glow, but this lady looks ridiculous.
           </p>
           <p>
            As someone who has been in the kind of stand-up tanning booth Krentcil took her daughter into, I feel the need to clarify (though not in any way defend this woman.)
           </p>
           <p>
            Stand-up booths have two (tiny) sections. One where you undress, and the other where actual bulbs are, with a door separating the two. I highly doubt she stood in the bulb section with her daughter because 1) I dont think theyd both fit and 2) NO WAY would this woman allow for THAT kind of tan line.
           </p>
           <p>
            Its more likely she had her daughter stay in the outer section, which Im sure still gets at least a little UV, and definitely still gets very warm.
           </p>
           <p>
            In any case, it seems to me this woman needs some kind of professional help. And a dermatologist. Immediately.
           </p>
