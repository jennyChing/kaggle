         <img alt="Top 10 Sex Scandals Exposed by Social Media" itemprop="image" src="http://www.toptensocialmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/SexScandals-e1383498054864.jpg" title="Top 10 Sex Scandals Exposed by Social Media"/>
         <div class="post-thumb-description">
          <p>
           Performers, socialites and celebrities have all fallen victim to sex scandals
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <!-- END .single-post-thumb -->
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <div>
         <p>
          Celebrities, politicians and socialites have all fallen prey to public relations disasters on the Internet. Their sex scandals incite backlash and intrigue, which is why they are so entertaining to chat about. The advent of social media exposes improprieties and forays on a scale never before seen in human history. Some of the most salacious tweets and posts are reminders of why discretion is so valuable.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Anthony Weiner
         </h2>
         <p>
          U.S. politicians are not known for their tameness, and former Congressman Anthony Wiener is no exception. According to
          <a href="http://www.usatoday.com/story/news/politics/2013/07/25/weiner-scandal-social-media-bonanza/2584221/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           USA Today
          </a>
          , Weiner had the gumption to send pictures of his crotch to multiple women via Twitter. The act created a political firestorm and outrage from the more conservative elements of society. It is difficult to fathom the mans logic.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Kim Kardashian
         </h2>
         <p>
          The ever flashy and provocative Kim Kardashian is no stranger to social media escapades. Shes dated professional athletes, rappers and other high-powered celebrities in her often stormy personal life. Early in Kardashians rise to fame, a sex tape of her and then boyfriend Ray J surfaced online and the rest is history. The video was featured on many adult social media websites.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Paris Hilton
         </h2>
         <p>
          As a socialite and T.V. star focused on aimless wanderings and general unproductivity, it is no surprise Paris Hilton found herself in a sex scandal. The Hilton heiress was featured in an ex-rated sex tape released online via adult social media. Night vision goggles and other oddities created intrigue and disgust all at the same time.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Scarlett Johansson
         </h2>
         <p>
          Considered by many to be the sexiest woman in the world, Scarlett Johansson is someone that maintains a high-profile yet has been plagued by personal setbacks. Unfortunately, her email was hacked and nude photos of her were stolen. The pictures were intended for her then husband Ryan Reynolds, but instead the photos wound up online.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Kanye West
         </h2>
         <p>
          The volatile and controversial rapper Kanye West has at times lacked discretion. For instance, West had the audacity to take pictures of his crotch and send them to multiple women via email. These less than trustworthy gals then shopped the photos around, which ultimately landed the explicit pictures on multiple social media sites.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Miley Cyrus
         </h2>
         <p>
          The Miley Cyrus music video Recking Ball on YouTube feels like a sex scandal. This good girl turned extreme sexual extrovert is making waves with her onstage antics and personal missteps. According to
          <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/03/26/celebrity-sex-scandals-tapes-photos_n_2951655.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           HuffPost
          </a>
          , suggestive photos of Cyrus have circulated on social media showing the singer in compromising positions. Cyruss father must be pulling his hair out.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Rihanna
         </h2>
         <p>
          As one of the most seductive singers in the entire world, it is no wonder Rihanna is a fan favorite and garners the attention of the media. Unfortunately, her taste in men is somewhat less than ideal. Naked photos of Rihanna were leaked online via social media, and it is rumored that Chris Brown was the culprit. The
          <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/03/26/celebrity-sex-scandals-tapes-photos_n_2951655.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           HuffPost
          </a>
          reports Rihanna as saying the experience was humiliating.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Chris Brown
         </h2>
         <p>
          Karma is something that comes around to bite all those perpetrating mischief. Chris Brown was burned by an ex-girlfriend that released full-frontal nude photos of Brown via social media. The incident caused some embarrassment and forced Brown to explain the situation on talk shows. It is difficult to understand why such high-profile people take these photos in the first place.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Jennifer Lopez
         </h2>
         <p>
          As a singer, songwriter and performer, the stunning Jennifer Lopez is an icon. Though her personal life has had turbulence, she has bounced back repeatedly. Unfortunately, a sex tape of her and an ex-husband surfaced on social media. The video was taken without permission and was scandalous in its content.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Snooki
         </h2>
         <p>
          The Jersey Shore matriarch Snooki is famous for her outrageous behavior and outspoken views. Multiple nude photos of Snooki have surfaced online and caused quite a stir. The photos circulated via social media sites and have made their rounds on the Internet. This is yet another example of why it is important to practice caution when one is in the limelight.
         </p>
         <p>
          For those caught up in sex
          <a href="http://www.toptensocialmedia.com">
           scandals exposed by social media
          </a>
          , life is changed forever. It is important to note that the Internet is making life more open and accessible, which makes guarding sensitive information a priority for all people. Be on the lookout for the next sex scandal breaking online.
         </p>
