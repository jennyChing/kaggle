         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="7350330" data-link="http://retronuke.com/bedlam-kickstarter/" data-summary='Get ready to roam the dangerous and harsh wastelands of Bedlam!          </div>
         <p>
          <img alt="Bedlam-logo" class="aligncenter wp-image-2448 size-full" height="300" src="http://retronuke.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Bedlam-logo.jpg" width="700"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Get ready to roamthe dangerous and harsh wastelands of
          <strong>
           Bedlam
          </strong>
          ! Thisnew
          <a href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/skyshinegames/bedlam" target="_blank">
           Kickstarter
          </a>
          project is being developed by Austin indie dev team
          <a href="http://www.gobedlam.com/" target="_blank">
           Skyshine Games
          </a>
          , whose members have worked on many influential gaming and comic projects in the past(Unreal Tournament /Heavy Metal). Using The Banner Saga engine, Skyshine Games is working to create a chaotic, post-apocalyptic RPG in the vein of FTL or XCOM. All of the characters and landscapeshave their own beautifully crafted and unique styles, which is something that sets Bedlam apart from a lot of other video games inthe same genre.In Bedlam, you will take commandof a crew of engineersand soldiersin search of a mysterious utopia called Aztec City.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://retronuke.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/bedlam-5.gif" rel="lightbox[2425]">
           <img alt="bedlam-6" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2434" height="360" src="http://retronuke.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/bedlam-6.gif" width="640"/>
          </a>
          Bedlams style is creativelyinfluenced by gritty 80s / 90s movies and comic books (
          <strong>
           The Road Warrior
          </strong>
          and Frank Millers 
          <strong>
           Hard Boiled
          </strong>
           come to mind). Bedlams game play fuses isometric camera anglesand strategic turn based combat. The game also features the blitz battle system. This turn based system featuresswift play, no turn-order, strategic movement and attacks, crew-buffing Dozer upgrades, and Dozer weapons for maximum destruction!At first glance, the game looks like a motion graphic novel with anaddedlayer of tense, strategic actionthat will keepthings interestingbetween dialogue.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="bedlam-5" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2433" height="360" src="http://retronuke.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/bedlam-5.gif" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          As you travel the wastelands, you will encounter many challenges in the form of radioactive territories, nano plagues, marauders, and mutant infestations. Players will discover ancient technologies, elite crew members and secret artifacts. Collecting resources after completing quests ordefeating enemies is an important part of your journey through Bedlam, andyou will have to make sureyour party is well equipped and fed to survive.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://retronuke.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/skyshine_bedlam_combat_cyborg.gif" rel="lightbox[2425]">
           <img alt="skyshine_bedlam_combat_cyborg" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2444" height="360" src="http://retronuke.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/skyshine_bedlam_combat_cyborg.gif" width="640"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Overall, Bedlam looks bad ass as hell! Its style is refreshing and sets itself apart from other games in itsgenre.Being a huge fan of post-apocalypticfiction, comic books, and RPGs, this game combines everything I have been waiting for and I am really looking forward to the backer updates for this project!
         </p>
         <p>
          Support
          <a href="https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/skyshinegames/bedlam" target="_blank">
           Bedlam
          </a>
          today!

          </iframe>
         </p>
