     <p>
      <a href="/wiki/Frank_Welker" title="Frank Welker">
       Frank Welker
      </a>
      is an American actor who specializes in
      <a href="/wiki/Voice_acting" title="Voice acting">
       voice acting
      </a>
      and has contributed character voices and other vocal effects to American television and motion pictures.
