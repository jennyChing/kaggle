          Glee
         </em>

"She's been talking with Cory's family the entire time and wants to plan the whole funeral," another insider says.         </p>
        <p>

        </p>

         <strong>
          Jonathan Groff
         </strong>

         <em>
          Spring Awakening,
         </em>
         and who played Jesse St. James, romantic rival to Finn (Monteith) for Michele's
         <em>
          Glee
         </em>
         character, Rachel Berry. "He flew to be with her as soon as he heard," another pal says.  "His heart is broken for her -- and he loved Cory too. The three of them would hang out together."
        </p>
        <p>
