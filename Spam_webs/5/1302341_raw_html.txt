        <div id="content_div_38251">
         <p>
          This week Mawson commemorated ANZAC day with a dawn service on a magnificent Antarctic day. Everyone on station attended the service near the flag poles and the statue of Sir Douglas Mawson. It was a moving ceremony and many thanks to Muzz, Grant, Chris and Jeremy for their organisation and participation. We then moved on to an ANZAC day brekky in the Diesos workshop.
         </p>
         <p>
          The day did not end there; Chef, Scotty put a lamb on the spit and we participated in some traditional ANZAC day shenanigans which netted our charity for the year, the Royal Flying Doctor Service, over $300.00.
         </p>
