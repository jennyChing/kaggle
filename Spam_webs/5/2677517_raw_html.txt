      Art Enriches Public Restroom Visits in Asheville

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         One of the things we like best about reviewing Ashevilles public restroom facilities is the art work we encounter, especially hand-painted works by local artists. Asheville, after all, is filled with hard-working artists, so it is little surprise that its lavatories are miniature art galleries.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_350" style="width: 310px">
         <a href="http://stallmansguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-16-11.11.44.jpg">

         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Hand-painted Formula One race care inside the mens room at
          <a href="http://stallmansguide.com/2014/11/creperie-bouchon/">
           Crperie Bouchon
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_355" style="width: 235px">
         <a href="http://stallmansguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-15-14.17.14.jpg">
          <img alt="2014-11-15 14.17.14" class="wp-image-355 size-medium" height="300" src="http://stallmansguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-15-14.17.14-225x300.jpg" width="225"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Hand-painted Raising Arizona tribute on bathroom doors at
          <a href="http://stallmansguide.com/2014/11/asheville-pizza-brewing-co/">
           Asheville Pizza &amp; Brewing Co.
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_359" style="width: 310px">
         <a href="http://stallmansguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-10-18.37.52.jpg">
          <img alt="The hand-painted ice cream cone in the unisex washoom at The Hop in North Asheville." class="size-medium wp-image-359" height="300" src="http://stallmansguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-11-10-18.37.52-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          The hand-painted ice cream cone in the unisex washroom at
