        </h2>
        <div class="entry-category-celebrities entry-category-creative_advocacy entry-category-news_and_updates entry-category-what_activists_are_up_to entry-author-farm_sanctuary entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a010536e26195970b0120a8c3215f970b">
         <div class="entry-inner">
          <h3 class="entry-header">
           Get Natalie Portmans Look
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p>
             <a href="http://farmsanctuary.typepad.com/.a/6a010536e26195970b01310f29f7fc970c-pi" style="DISPLAY: inline">
              <img alt="Natalie Portman" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a010536e26195970b01310f29f7fc970c " src="http://farmsanctuary.typepad.com/.a/6a010536e26195970b01310f29f7fc970c-320wi"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Move over Marc Jacobs, Prada and Chanel  theres a new brand hitting the streets of Hollywood.
            </p>
            <p>
             Yep, you heard right: were talking about our very own Farm Sanctuary merchandise line, which has lately been spotted on certain animal-friendly celebrities.
            </p>
            <p>
             One of these compassionate stars was
             <a href="http://justjared.buzznet.com/photo-gallery/2413420/natalie-portman-black-swan-serious-02/" target="_blank">
              Natalie Portman
             </a>
             , who was recently seen out and about casually sporting a Farm Sanctuary
             <a href="https://secure2.vegsource.com/farmsanc/item.cgi?rm=edit_item&amp;item_id=66958" target="_blank">
              water bottle
             </a>
             .
            </p>
            <p>
             Then there was Hal Sparks, who wore a Farm Sanctuary
             <a href="https://secure2.vegsource.com/farmsanc/item.cgi?rm=edit_item&amp;item_id=66927" target="_blank">
              tweet me, dont eat me t-shirt
             </a>
             on
             <a href="http://www.livestream.com/halsparks/video?clipId=pla_bdd2a7a9-1013-417d-b9ab-dd49d0ec2c9f" target="_blank">
              one of his recent livestream shows
             </a>
             . As if this wasnt cool enough, Hal also did a massive shout out for the work we do and even commented on how much he thinks his viewers would like us. Aw, shucks. We love you too, Hal.
            </p>
            <p>
             Fashion activism is an easy yet effective way of spreading a message of compassion, and we think its awesome that celebrities are jumping on board. If you want to make the world a better place for farm animals too, one t-shirt at a time, be sure to check out
             <a href="https://secure2.vegsource.com/farmsanc/cat_list.htm" target="_blank">
              Farm Sanctuarys online store
             </a>
             . Were pretty sure Natalie would approve.
             <br/>
            </p>
