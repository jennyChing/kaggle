          </strong>
         </p>

          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Studies estimate that it could avoid about 80% of the incidence of type 2 diabetes by changing the pattern of food and replace it with a balanced pattern and promote physical activity and improve the daily life style is generally intended to stimulate the body to fight disease.
          </strong>
