             <h1 style="text-align: center;">
              Yoga Retreat in Bali: Yoga and Culture with Nadine Johnson
             </h1>
             <h2 style="text-align: center;">
              Happiness comes from experiences, not possessions.
             </h2>
             <h2>
              About
             </h2>
             <p>
              In addition to a beautiful private villa, delicious food and gracious hosts, this retreat will help students deepen their own understanding of yoga and find their personal practice. In this intimate retreat, gain a clear awareness of whats sustainable, regenerative and therapeutic in your yoga practice. Theres no right or wrong.
             </p>
             <p>
              Each day includes Vinyasa yoga and meditation in the mornings, restorative yoga in the evenings.We will enjoy a cooking class at the Award Winning Mosaic Restaurant founded by theworld famous Chef Chris Salans.
              <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
               Well take day trips to cultural
              </span>
              sites. There are options for snorkeling or surf lessons, and a day trip to a smaller island to enjoy more of what Bali has to offer. A spa session is included. There is a private pool, and nearby beach for walks and relaxation.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="NadineJohnson-View" class="size-medium wp-image-22568 alignright" height="213" src="http://seekretreat.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/NadineJohnson-View-300x213.jpg" width="300"/>
             </p>
             <p>
              New to yoga? Coming back to the mat? Experienced yogi? All levels are welcome.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
               Patience and inward focus are key elements in Nadine Johnsons teaching and in her own own practice of over 20 years, which is grounded in Ashtanga yoga as taught by the late Sri K. Pattabhi Jois in India. She helps students discover their own inner strength intelligently and with Ahimsa (non-violence). She inspires her students to develop a safe and sustainable lifelong practice, as she has done for herself. Nadine has completed certified trainings with Clayton Horton at Greenpath Yoga, David Swenson, and the Advanced Teacher Training Program at YogaWorks. As an E-RYT 500 certified teacher, she leads public classes in San Francisco at YogaWorks and the Bay Club and retreats around the world.
              </span>
             </p>

              <img alt="4-Bed-Mawa-villa-exterior" class="size-medium wp-image-22556 alignright" height="214" src="http://seekretreat.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/4-Bed-Mawa-villa-exterior-300x214.jpg" width="300"/>

             </p>
             <p>
              The ground floor has a fully stocked kitchen and group area. The private pool offers a wonderful way to relax throughout the day. The onsite staff supports our needs, including all meals, housekeeping and private driver. We recommend unplugging but, if needed, high speed fiber optic Wi-Fi internet is available 24/7. During our stay, everything is secure with registered security PLUS CCTV perimeter surveillance and nightly guards.
