        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Basic Investing Seminar
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         If youre constantly making ends meet, if every after payday you wonder
         <br/>
         where your money went, if you wish to retire early and comfortably, if you
         <br/>
         answered YES to these questions, then this is for you!
        </p>
        <h3>
         Make Your Money Grow (Module 1) Topics:
        </h3>
        <p>
         1. Importance of setting life and financial goals
         <br/>
         2. Practical money saving strategies
         <br/>
         3. Obstacles in saving and investing
         <br/>
         4. Factors in evaluating the right investments
         <br/>
         5. Overview of the major types of investments
         <br/>
         (bank products, stock market, mutual funds, and more!)
         <br/>
         6. Ayas Investment Diagram
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/hWlborjpi6E" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </center>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         TIME IS MONEY  are you using it wisely?
         <br/>
         How you spend your time today will determine the quality of life you will live tomorrow.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://pesosandsense.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/tvm.jpg">
          <img alt="tvm" class="aligncenter wp-image-3377" height="180" src="http://pesosandsense.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/tvm.jpg" width="576"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Time Value of Money (Module 2) Topics:
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         1. Concept of Time Value of Money
         <br/>
         2. Basic investment calculation and tools
         <br/>
         3. Tips to avoid investment scams
         <br/>
         4. Guide in making a financial plan
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Imagine your life 20-30 years from now. Are you still working and making ends meet or are you living comfortably, enjoying your time and money? When the picture you see isnt so good, know that you can change it today!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         In this module, learn the key concepts that will help you take advantage of the time you have now and start growing your money in investments that are best suited for you.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Who is Aya?
        </h3>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/8fKw96OJma0" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </center>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         Aya Laraya, with his 25 years of experience in the financial and investmentindustry, will simplify and help you understand how you can grow yourmoney and learn how to think like a good investor.
