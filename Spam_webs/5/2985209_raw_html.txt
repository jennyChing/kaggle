        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline name">
         Frank Welker will be voicing Soundwave in Transformers Revenge of the Fallen
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2009-04-27T16:06:15+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          Posted on April 27, 2009
         </time>
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2009-04-27T16:06:15+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          at 4:06 pm
         </time>
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          by
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://news.tfw2005.com/author/solfury/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Sol Fury
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-categories">
          under
          <a href="http://news.tfw2005.com/transformers-movie/transformers-movie-just-movie/" rel="category tag">
           Transformers Movie (Just Movie)
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="description articleBody">
        <span itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
         <a class="fancybox" href="http://news.tfw2005.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/10/2009/04/RotFSoundwave_1240865399.jpg" itemprop="contentUrl" rel="fancybox-1" target="_blank" title="RotFSoundwave">
          <img alt="RotFSoundwave" class="alignleft post-image" height="150" itemprop="thumbnailUrl" src="http://news.tfw2005.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/10/2009/04/RotFSoundwave_1240865399-145x150.jpg" width="145"/>
         </a>
         <meta content="RotFSoundwave" itemprop="description"/>
        </span>
        <p>
         In an interview with
         <a href="http://movieblog.ugo.com/movies/roberto-orci-frank-welker-to-officially-voice-soundwave">
          Ugo Movie Blog
         </a>
         , Roberto Orci has officially confirmed that Frank Welker, the voice of many 80s villains including Generation 1 Soundwave, will be reprising his role as Soundwave for Transformers Revenge of the Fallen! Frank Welkers other classic roles span a wide range of 80s cartoons and include Megatron, Dr Claw on Inspector Gadget, and Freddy on Scooby-Doo (and thats not even scratching the surface! Chances are if you have ever watched a cartoon in the 80s, you have enjoyed Mr Welkers talents).
        </p>
        <p>
         Talk about this excellent news on our 2005 Boards!
