          <p>
           <a href="http://www.mlive.com/news/kalamazoo/index.ssf/2015/01/michigans_i-94_eastbound_lanes.html">
            Update: Highway reopens Sunday morning
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            GALESBURG, MI --
           </strong>
           There were
           <a href="http://www.mlive.com/news/kalamazoo/index.ssf/2015/01/it_was_unreal_witness_describe.html#incart_river">
            76 semis and 117 cars
           </a>
           involved in the massive pileup on I-94 as of Friday night, bringing the total to 193 vehicles, according to the latest update from Michigan State Police.
          </p>
          <p>
           The updated numbers were given as of 8 p.m., according to a news release. Police say 26 semis and 34 cars were involved on the eastbound side of the Interstate, while50 semis and 83 cars collided inthe westbound lanes.
          </p>
          <p>
           Police said the fires that started on eastbound I-94 arecontinuing to flare up.
          </p>
          <p>
           Fire crews and hazmat teams are currently on scene extinguishing re-surging flames and monitoring the air, police say.
          </p>
          <p>
           Officials earlier estimated the highway would reopen at midnight Friday, but in the latest update stated that the time now is unknown.
          </p>
