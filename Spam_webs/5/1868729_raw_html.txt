        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Parsley is a pretty little Mediterranean herb with characteristic smell and delicious taste. This herb is not only appreciated because of its taste but also because of its highly nutritious properties.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Parsley has quite small energy value, about 190kJ/100g, which makes it suitable for every profile from diabetics to athletes. It is an excellent source of minerals. 100 grams of dried parsley leafs contains about 1600 mg of minerals, out of which half is potassium and the rest is iron, calcium, manganese, magnesium, copper and smaller amounts of other minerals.
        </p>
