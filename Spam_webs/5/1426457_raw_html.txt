     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_career_beginnings">
       Early life and career beginnings
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Shannen_Doherty&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life and career beginnings">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Doherty was born in
      <a href="/wiki/Memphis,_Tennessee" title="Memphis, Tennessee">
       Memphis, Tennessee
      </a>
      , the daughter of Rosa, a beauty parlor owner, and Tom Doherty, a mortgage consultant.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-filmref_1-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-filmref-1">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        1
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      Doherty has
      <a href="/wiki/Irish_Catholic" title="Irish Catholic">
       Irish Catholic
      </a>
      ancestry on her father's side and English, Scottish, and Scots-Irish ancestry on her mother's side.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        2
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      She was raised in her mother's
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Southern_Baptist" title="Southern Baptist">
       Southern Baptist
      </a>
      denomination.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
       <a href="#cite_note-3">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        3
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <p>
      Doherty had guest spots on TV series including
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Voyagers!" title="Voyagers!">
        Voyagers!
       </a>
      </i>
      and
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Father_Murphy" title="Father Murphy">
        Father Murphy
       </a>
      </i>
      . When a casting notice was released in Hollywood for a regular role on the popular
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Little_House_on_the_Prairie_(TV_series)" title="Little House on the Prairie (TV series)">
        Little House on the Prairie
       </a>
      </i>
      , she jumped at the opportunity and eventually won the role of Jenny Wilder at the age of 11, thanks in part to actor/producer
      <a href="/wiki/Michael_Landon" title="Michael Landon">
       Michael Landon
      </a>
      's seeing her guest spot on
      <i>
       Father Murphy
      </i>
      ,
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Bio_4-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-Bio-4">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        4
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      which he also produced. Doherty stayed in the series until the show's cancellation in 1983.
     </p>
     <p>
      In 1982, Doherty lent her voice to the animated film
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/The_Secret_of_NIMH" title="The Secret of NIMH">
        The Secret of NIMH
       </a>
      </i>
      . In 1985, Doherty starred as Maggie Malene in the teen movie comedy
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Girls_Just_Want_to_Have_Fun_(film)" title="Girls Just Want to Have Fun (film)">
        Girls Just Want to Have Fun
       </a>
      </i>
      alongside actresses
      <a href="/wiki/Helen_Hunt" title="Helen Hunt">
       Helen Hunt
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/wiki/Sarah_Jessica_Parker" title="Sarah Jessica Parker">
       Sarah Jessica Parker
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      Doherty was cast as the oldest Witherspoon sibling, Kris, on the family drama
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Our_House_(1986_TV_series)" title="Our House (1986 TV series)">
        Our House
       </a>
      </i>
      , which ran from 1986 to 1988, a role which garnered her several
      <a href="/wiki/Young_Artist_Award" title="Young Artist Award">
       Young Artist Award
      </a>
      nominations.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Awards_5-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-Awards-5">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        5
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      She then went on to appear in an episode of
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Magnum,_P.I." title="Magnum, P.I.">
        Magnum, P.I.
       </a>
      </i>
      ("A Sense of Debt"), followed by an early episode of
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Airwolf" title="Airwolf">
        Airwolf
       </a>
      </i>
      for which she was nominated as Best Young Actress: Guest in a Series at the
      <a href="/wiki/6th_Youth_in_Film_Awards" title="6th Youth in Film Awards">
       6th Youth in Film Awards
      </a>
      in 1984. Doherty's first major motion picture role was in
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Heathers" title="Heathers">
        Heathers
       </a>
      </i>
      , released in 1989.
     </p>

     <h3>

      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Shannen_Doherty&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Early work: 19902001">

       </span>
      </span>
     </h3>
     <div class="thumb tright">
      <div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;">
       <a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Doherty_and_Priestley.jpg">
        <img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="549" data-file-width="624" height="194" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cc/Doherty_and_Priestley.jpg/220px-Doherty_and_Priestley.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cc/Doherty_and_Priestley.jpg/330px-Doherty_and_Priestley.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cc/Doherty_and_Priestley.jpg/440px-Doherty_and_Priestley.jpg 2x" width="220"/>
       </a>
       <div class="thumbcaption">
        <div class="magnify">
         <a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Doherty_and_Priestley.jpg" title="Enlarge">
         </a>
        </div>
        Doherty with
        <a href="/wiki/Jason_Priestley" title="Jason Priestley">
         Jason Priestley
        </a>
        at the Governor's Ball following the 43rd Annual
        <a href="/wiki/Emmy_Award" title="Emmy Award">
         Emmy Awards
        </a>
        , August 1991
       </div>
      </div>
     </div>
     <p>
      Doherty garnered worldwide attention and fame for her breakout role as
      <a href="/wiki/List_of_Beverly_Hills,_90210_characters#Brenda_Walsh" title="List of Beverly Hills, 90210 characters">
       Brenda Walsh
      </a>
      in the
      <a href="/wiki/Aaron_Spelling" title="Aaron Spelling">
       Aaron Spelling
      </a>
      -produced TV series
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Beverly_Hills,_90210" title="Beverly Hills, 90210">
        Beverly Hills, 90210
       </a>
      </i>
      in 1990. In 1991 and 1992 her portrayal of Brenda earned her a Young Artist Award nomination for Best Young Actress Starring in a Television Series.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Young_Artist_Awards_6-0">
