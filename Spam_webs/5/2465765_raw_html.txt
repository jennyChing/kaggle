         <h4>
          About this course
         </h4>
         <p class="cgm-course-details-long-desc">
          The course contains four lessons: Breath of Yoga, Breath Movement Synchronization, Tadasana Standing Sequence and Seated Forward Bends. For maximum benefit, I highly recommend following the course in that order.

By the end of the course, you will learn how to: develop and maintain slow, smooth ujjayi breath in your practice; learn a sequence of poses that exercises the entire body; develop better awareness of your body; acquire skills to do yoga safely in your personal practice and in group classes.
