        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         The Art Of The Thank You Note
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-08-04T00:00:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          August 4, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">

            robi9832

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <a href="http://robincharmagne.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/birthday-gifts.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest pibfi_float_center">
           <img alt="birthday, gifts, thank you notes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10731" height="600" src="http://robincharmagne.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/birthday-gifts.jpg" width="600"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.robincharmagne.com/blog/?p=10721&amp;media=http://robincharmagne.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/birthday-gifts.jpg&amp;description=The Art Of The Thank You Note')">
           </span>
          </span>

         <br/>
         Hello,
         <br/>
         How was your weekend? Mine was very quiet. I was so tired from birthday festivities that I really needed a couple of days to do nothing. I took some time to really look at the birthday gifts and I was yet again in tears, as my friends and family were so thoughtful in their gift giving. These days with email this and text that, or post this status, I could easily send a digital thank you. But Im old school and love handwritten thank you notes. I appreciate the time taken to select and purchase these gifts so a personal thank you note is in order.
        </p>
        <h2>
         How to Write a Thank You Note
        </h2>
        <p>
         What youll need:
         <br/>
         Note cards/stationary
         <br/>
         Postage stamps
         <br/>
         A good pen
         <br/>
         Address list
         <br/>
         A few heartfelt lines to express your sincere appreciation, mention the gift by name if you like, and a nice closing
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Samples
         </em>
         <br/>
         Dear Friend,
         <br/>
         I am so grateful for the lovely gift.
         <br/>
         Best wishes,
         <br/>
         You
        </p>
        <p>
         Hey Girl,
         <br/>
         Thank you ever so much for the thoughtful present. You know me so well.
         <br/>
         Much love,
         <br/>
         You
        </p>
        <p>
         Dude,
         <br/>
         That gift rocked, cant wait to use it!
         <br/>
         With gratitude,
         <br/>
         You
        </p>
        <p>
         It doesnt have to be a dissertation. Select a note card or stationary that reflects your style and personality. Some cute choices from Etsy,
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/113479463/signature-chevron-thank-you-note-cards?ref=shop_home_active_2" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.etsy.com/listing/113479463/signature-chevron-thank-you-note-cards?ref=shop_home_active_2', 'here']);" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/109319074/printable-thank-you-card-thank-you-note?ref=shop_home_feat_2" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.etsy.com/listing/109319074/printable-thank-you-card-thank-you-note?ref=shop_home_feat_2', 'here']);" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         , and yep
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/161850403/vespa-grazie-thank-you-notes-set-of-10?ref=shop_home_feat_4" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.etsy.com/listing/161850403/vespa-grazie-thank-you-notes-set-of-10?ref=shop_home_feat_4', 'here']);" target="_blank">
          here
         </a>
         . The note should have a warm, friendly or even playful tone.  Show your love and send a hand written thank you note!
        </p>
        <p>
         When it comes to thank you notes, are you old school handwritten or new school electronic?
        </p>
