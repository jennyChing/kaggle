          <a href="http://www.bookofwomensrunning.com/?keycode=142748" target="_blank">
           this plan's for you
          </a>
          . (If not, check out our run/walk program to build you up to 30 minutes). At the end of six weeks you'll be able to do a full hour on the roadand you'll score a killer body that's 10 pounds lighter.
         </p>
         <p>
          Run at a pace that feels challenging but comfortable. Feel free to switch the rest day, but make sure you do one full day of rest each week. And don't forget to
          <a href="/fitness/stretches" target="_blank">
           stretch
          </a>
          after each
          <a href="http://www.bookofwomensrunning.com/?keycode=142748" target="_blank">
