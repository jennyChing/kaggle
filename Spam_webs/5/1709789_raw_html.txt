         <div class="entry-summary">
          <p>
           Often teachers drop garbage off at my office.usually when I am taking a dump.and pretend they are doing me a favor. In actuality, they are just cleaning excess trash out of their classrooms and my office just happens to be closer than the dumpsters. Additionally, anything with a plug is considered to be technology, for example, a toaster. Here is a piece of garbage I found at my door one morning.
          </p>

           I still have no clue what that thing wasnow its trash.

            Why is the Office Closed?
           </a>
          </h1>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <a href="http://smarterthanyourteacher.com/why-is-the-office-closed/" rel="bookmark" title="">
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="" pubdate="">
            </time>
           </a>
          </div>
          <!-- .entry-meta -->
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-summary">
          <p>
           <strong>
            Teacher:
           </strong>
           Why is the office closed?
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            IT Guy:
           </strong>
           They leave at 1:00
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Teacher:
           </strong>
           But its only 1:30
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            IT Guy:
           </strong>
           What?
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Teacher:
           </strong>
           Its only 1:30do they pack right up and leave?
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            IT Guy:
           </strong>
           I would.to avoid having conversations like this.
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Teacher:
           </strong>
           Thats kind of ridiculous
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            IT Guy:
           </strong>
           Just to confirm. It is ridiculous that you, a person who does not work in the summer, sat at home until the office was closed then came to school and expected them to be here waiting for youor that they are not here 30 minutes after closing time?
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Teacher:
           </strong>
           You are a jerk
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            IT Guy:
           </strong>
           I knowand you are making me regret I didnt leave at closing timeor as you call it early.
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>

            Computer Should be Able to Do that #2
           </a>
          </h1>
          <div class="entry-meta">
           <a href="http://smarterthanyourteacher.com/computer-should-be-able-to-do-that-2/" rel="bookmark" title="">
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="" pubdate="">
            </time>
           </a>
          </div>
          <!-- .entry-meta -->
         </header>
         <!-- .entry-header -->
         <div class="entry-summary">
          <p>
           <strong>
            Secretary:
           </strong>
           We have some holds to be placed on student accounts. We need to block some users from viewing online grades till the hold is lifted
           <br/>
           <strong>
            <br/>
            IT Guy:
           </strong>
           Sure. Send me a list
           <br/>
           <strong>
            <br/>
            Secretary:
           </strong>
           Can we just turn off the whole school and I will tell you who to turn back on?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            IT Guy:
           </strong>
           What? You want me to turn off the entire school?
           <br/>
           <strong>
            <br/>
            Secretary:
           </strong>
           Yes.
           <br/>
           <strong>
            <br/>
            IT Guy:
           </strong>
           Why?
           <br/>
           <strong>
            <br/>
            Secretary:
           </strong>
           So I dont have to make a spreadsheet
           <br/>
           <strong>
            <br/>
            IT Guy:
           </strong>
           You want me to block the entire school..then reinstate almost the entire schoolso you dont have to make a spreadsheet?
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Secretary:
           </strong>
           Yeah, the computer should be able to do that.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            IT Guy:
           </strong>
           Well it cant.but people that work in offices SHOULD be able to make spreadsheets. In the mean time I will email support and see if they can build a button that blocks 5% of users and functions via telepathy
          </p>
