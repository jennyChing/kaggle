               New Spiral UFO Sightings - Israel!
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
               <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                New mass spiral UFO sighting over Israel. These images are reminiscent of the 2009 Norway spiral sightings. Are we simply seeing some failed missile launch, or even something intercepting a missile launch?
                <br/>
                If you saw these strange spirals over Israel, we would like to hear from you.
                <br/>
                Please also upload or send your photos of it to us.
                <br/>
                <i>
                 There are two objects. One that looks like an intensely bright and colorful rocket launch, and the other that looks like a comet, that eventually "spirals out."
                </i>
                <br/>
                <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                 <object height="315" width="560">

                  </embed>
                 </object>
                </div>
               </div>
               <i>
                'It was an unusual sight'
The unidentified body was also spotted at the Givataim observatory in central Israel. Dr. Yigal Pat-El, the chairman of the Israeli Astronomical Association and observatory director, confirmed the sighting and said the origin of the object is unclear.
               </i>
               <br/>
               <i>
                "It looked like a small body that suddenly started to grow and make spiral-like movements," he said. "After a while, it turned into a cloud....it looked like an object flying high above ground and then disintegrating."

"It reached a height of some 80 kilometers and flew north of the border with Lebanon," he said. "It wasn't a meteor."
               </i>
               <br/>
               <i>
                "We saw a large trail of light travelling from a distant spot in the sky," Dorit from northern Israel reported. "We could not see the source of the light. The trail was massive. It was an unusual sight. Moments later we saw swirling movement where the light was coming from.
