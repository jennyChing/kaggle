       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Handmade Christmas Gifts When You Dont Have Time
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-12-08T09:12:24+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          December 8, 2014
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.schneiderpeeps.com/author/angi-schneider/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Angi Schneider
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.schneiderpeeps.com/handmade-christmas-gifts-buy/#comments">
           4 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <a href="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/SchneiderPeeps-Giving-handmade.jpg">
          <img alt="SchneiderPeeps-Giving Handmade when you don't have time.  There is no need to feel guilty about not having time (or talent) to make all - or even some- of your gifts.  These wonderful small shops have you covered. " class="aligncenter wp-image-2312" height="714" src="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/SchneiderPeeps-Giving-handmade.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
         While I love tomake Christmas gifts for friends and family I usually have too many ideas for the time I have. So, instead of getting all stressed out about not making everything I wanted to, I just purchase handmade items. This way I can feel good about giving handmade but not have to be stressed out. I also get to support small businesses which I love to do.
        </p>
        <p>
         These are a few of my favorite handmade stores to buy from.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/14156837225931.png">
          <img alt="1415683722593" class="aligncenter wp-image-2302" height="199" src="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/14156837225931.png" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://store.hardlotion.com/gifts_bulk_orders_s/1828.htm&amp;Click=2015">
          Made On Hard Lotion
         </a>
         is one of my favorite companies in the whole world. Renee and her family have worked hard to creat a really great company and wonderful products. Renee is one of the most generous business people I know. Im using her recipe from
         <a href="http://store.hardlotion.com/ebooks_s/1826.htm&amp;Click=2015">
          My Buttered Life ebook
         </a>
         for the hard lotions in our DIY Spa Gift Baksets. I ordered some lip balms to add to them. Yes, I could have made them but shes running such a
         <a href="5store.hardlotion.com/gifts_bulk_orders_s/1828.htm&amp;Click=2015">
          great special on them,
         </a>
         that I decided it was worth it to just buy them. (affiliate links)
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/TCFshop-banner-photo.jpg">
          <img alt="TCFshop-banner-photo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2309" height="300" src="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/TCFshop-banner-photo.jpg" width="566"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://timbercreekfarmer.com/shop/">
          Timber Creek Farm Handmade Store
         </a>
         is a really a really fun store for some pretty unique handmade items. Janet, the owner, takes beautiful pictures of her farm animals and turns them into
         <a href="http://timbercreekfarmer.com/product/postcards-2/">
          postcards
         </a>
         and even
         <a href="http://timbercreekfarmer.com/product/timber-creek-farm-2015-calendar-1/">
          2015 calendars
         </a>
         . She has
         <a href="http://timbercreekfarmer.com/product/100-border-leicester-yarn-natural/">
          beautiful yarn made from her own sheep
         </a>
         , which I think is pretty cool, along with crochetedfingerless gloves.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/iusb_760x100.14642210_52tv.jpg">
          <img alt="iusb_760x100.14642210_52tv" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2301" height="100" src="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/iusb_760x100.14642210_52tv.jpg" width="760"/>
         </a>
         The Homestead Lady Shop is the Etsy store of Tessa The Homestead Lady. The Homestead Lady shops sells really fun kits to help you learn some basic DIY skills like this
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/212669585/upcycled-sweater-mitten-kits-teach-your?ref=shop_home_active_10">
          mittens from a sweater kit
         </a>
         . She also sells
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/212856770/vanilla-honey-8-oz-organic-vanilla?ref=shop_home_active_2">
          vanilla honey
         </a>
         and
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/212863113/non-alcoholic-vanilla-4-0z-vegetable?ref=shop_home_active_1">
          non-alcohol vanilla extract
         </a>
         . Esther really love her jewelry and has informed me she cant wait until she gets her ears pierced.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/as-logo-2-copy-copy.png">
          <img alt="as-logo-2-copy-copy" class="aligncenter wp-image-2305" height="126" src="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/as-logo-2-copy-copy.png" width="600"/>
         </a>
         Aaron is a super talented artist that Ive known (in real life) for many years. He paints on
         <a href="https://squareup.com/market/aaron-simcik/worship-painting">
          canvas
         </a>
         but he also paints on
         <a href="http://aaronsimcikpainting.com/2014/10/20/christmas-package/">
          reclaimed wood
         </a>
         . We have a friend whose son got married a few months ago and Aaron painted a sign with the bride and grooms name on it that matched their wedding colors (and house colors) and we used it to decorate at the shower and reception. It was really simple and elegant. All of that to say, Aaron also does custom pieces so if you dont find something in
         <a href="http://aaronsimcikpainting.com/">
          his shop you like
         </a>
         , email him and ask about a custom piece.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/10858003_1033586403334774_3270770167212663685_n.jpg">
          <img alt="10858003_1033586403334774_3270770167212663685_n" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2310" height="200" src="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/10858003_1033586403334774_3270770167212663685_n.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://joybileefarm.com/">
          Joybilee Farm
         </a>
         is a homestead/farm that sells some really great products made from their farm. They make 100% wool yarn and turn it into things like fingerless gloves. Mr. Joybilee is a woodturner and has made wonderful wood guages and lucets. They also make goats milk soap and even shampoo bars. I really want to try a shampoo bar- it sounds intriguing.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/iusb_760x100.11768595_121f.jpg">
          <img alt="iusb_760x100.11768595_121f" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2306" height="100" src="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/iusb_760x100.11768595_121f.jpg" width="760"/>
         </a>
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/MontanaSolarCreation">
          Montana Solar Creations
         </a>
         is an eco-friendly shop that sell all kinds of fun stuff. I really like their
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/MontanaSolarCreation?section_id=12763805&amp;ref=shopsection_leftnav_5">
          natural wood baby toys
         </a>
         andthe mason jarsolar lanterns. They have many
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/MontanaSolarCreation?section_id=11988832&amp;ref=shopsection_leftnav_1">
          reusable products
         </a>
         to help you stop buying disposable  including dryer balls. But they also have some really unique items such as
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/MontanaSolarCreation?section_id=14741103&amp;ref=shopsection_leftnav_2">
          river rock drawer pulls and wine stoppers
         </a>
         . They even have antler chews for dogs  that would be a huge treat here.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/iusb_760x100.13666168_tt08.jpg">
          <img alt="iusb_760x100.13666168_tt08" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2307" height="100" src="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/iusb_760x100.13666168_tt08.jpg" width="760"/>
         </a>
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/hopeink?ref=l2-shopheader-name">
          Hope Ink
         </a>
         sells original artwork with Scripture on it. I have several Hope Ink pieces, I have the
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/187278568/scripture-art-print-sweeter-than-honey?ref=shop_home_active_1&amp;ga_search_query=honey">
          Sweeter Than Honey
         </a>
         8X10 and some
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/hopeink?section_id=7502560&amp;ref=shopsection_leftnav_2">
          note cards
         </a>
         . I think many of these prints would make a nice gift along with a frame or the notecards and a pretty pen.
         <a href="http://hopeink.com">
          On her blog
         </a>
         , Emily has some free printables including some coloring pages.
         <a href="http://www.hopeink.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/ChristmasHolyNightColoring.pdf">
          I printed this one up for Esther to colo
         </a>
         r. She had so much fun that I printed more to use when I chaparone the cherubs at the Nutcracker performances this week. I am super inspired by Emily and her work, I think you will be, too.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/iusb_760x100.12617709_rf8r.jpg">
          <img alt="iusb_760x100.12617709_rf8r" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2308" height="100" src="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/iusb_760x100.12617709_rf8r.jpg" width="760"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/NinjaChickens">
          Ninja Chickens
         </a>
         has nothing to do with martial arts or with chickens but you will still want to check out this fun store. Maria sells handspun yarn and jewelry made from it. She also has some
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/NinjaChickens?section_id=15269282&amp;ref=shopsection_leftnav_3#">
          body care items
         </a>
         . My favorite thing she has is her
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/NinjaChickens?section_id=15266091&amp;ref=shopsection_leftnav_2">
          tea of the month club
         </a>
         . Last year I traded some homegrown Meyer lemons for some of Marias tea. Ive never had handblended tea before and wellI reluctantlyshared with my family. I know thats not nice to admit, but its the sad truth. The teas were so good and I thought they wouldnt really appreciate them like I did. But they did and Im glad I shared. I noticed yesterday that Maria has a
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/208185421/weather-tree-calendar-poster-get-in?ref=shop_home_feat_1">
          Weather Tree Calendar Poster
         </a>
         in her shop. I bought this to do with my children (mainly Esther) in 2015. It will be fun!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/SchneiderPeeps-cribbage-board.jpg">
          <img alt="SchneiderPeeps - cribbage board" class="aligncenter wp-image-1990" height="334" src="http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/SchneiderPeeps-cribbage-board.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         One last place, and its inventory is really, really sparce. Were opening an Etsy store (yeah, I know its been on the goals list for 3 years) and its still under construction but Gabriel has
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/214445997/cedar-tree-cribbage-board?ref=shop_home_active_1">
          two cribbage boards
         </a>
         that he can sell. Many people have asked about these over the last year and hes ready to sell them. He made them from his a cedar tree he cut down on his very first bee removal.
         <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/214445997/cedar-tree-cribbage-board?ref=shop_home_active_1">
          You can see the listing here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         There is no need to feel bad if you just cannot make all -or any- of your Christmas gifts this year, some years thats just how it is. You can still give handmade and support some really great small businesses while your at it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <strong>
           What are some of your favorite handmade shops? Feel free to leave links in the comments!
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>

   jQuery.noConflict();
jQuery(document).ready(function($) {
$(function() {	
	var process_script_url = 'http://schneiderpeepscom.c.presscdn.com/wp-content/plugins/wp-cart-for-digital-products' + '/ajax_process_cart_requests.php';
	var save_ret_cart_msg = $('div.eStore_save_retrieve_cart_message');
	
	$('.eStore_save_cart_section').on("click", function (){
		save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append("Processing Your Request...");		
		var dataString = 'eStore_cart_action=eStore_save_cart';		
		$.ajax({
			type: "POST",
			url: process_script_url,
			data: dataString,
			dataType: "json",
			success: function(data) {				
				//var dataStr = JSON.stringify(data); console.log(dataStr);
				if(data.status == "success"){
					save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append('Your cart has been saved! Shopping Cart ID : ' + data.ID);
				}
				else{
					save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append('&lt;span class="eStore_warning"&gt;' + data.details + '&lt;/span&gt;');
				}
			}
		});				
		return false;
	});

	$('.eStore_retrieve_cart_section').on("click", function (){
		var cart_id = prompt("Shopping Cart ID", "");	
		if(cart_id == null){
			return false;
		}
		else if(cart_id ==""){
			save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append('&lt;span class="eStore_warning"&gt;The shopping cart ID you entered does not appear to be correct!&lt;/span&gt;');
			return false;
		}
		cart_id = $.trim(cart_id);
		save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append("Processing Your Request...");
		var dataString = 'eStore_cart_action=eStore_retrieve_cart&amp;cart_id=' + cart_id;
		$.ajax({
			type: "POST",
			url: process_script_url,
			data: dataString,
			dataType: "json",
			success: function(data) {
				if(data.status == "success"){	
					save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append('Success');	
					target_url = window.location.href.split("#")[0];
					window.location.href = target_url;					
				}
				else{
					save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append('&lt;span class="eStore_warning"&gt;The shopping cart ID you entered does not appear to be correct!&lt;/span&gt;');	
				}	
			}
		});		
		return false;
	});
});
});
  </script>
