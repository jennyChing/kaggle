         <p>
          Hey there comic book nerds, its new comic book day once again. The middle of the week is where all the comic book shops across the land get their cash registers stuffed full of hot nerdy cash! Lets see what we got.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.thenerdfilter.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Deadpool-Dracula-s-Gauntlet.jpg">
            <img alt="Deadpool-Dracula-s-Gauntlet" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-9915" height="300" src="http://www.thenerdfilter.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Deadpool-Dracula-s-Gauntlet-197x300.jpg" width="197"/>
           </a>
           <a href="http://www.thenerdfilter.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/strain-hardcover.jpg">
            <img alt="strain-hardcover" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-9916" height="300" src="http://www.thenerdfilter.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/strain-hardcover-198x300.jpg" width="198"/>
           </a>
           Deadpool Draculas Gauntlet #1
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          What is this! Another Deadpool mini-series! Last week we got Deadpool Vs X-Force #1 of 4, now we have the first Deadpool Draculas Gauntlet. Its getting hard being a Deadpool fan, #31 of the regular Deadpool series is out this week too! So I know Dazzler was teaming up with Deadpool to fend off Dracula, but now I guess they needed its own incarnation to tell the tale. Draculas Gauntlet is a 7 part series.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Strain Volume 1
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          An eerie series from Dark HorseStrain is now in a hardcover book. Biological warfare, or something demented from another world, nobody knows the origin of the Strain. Volume 1 collects issues 1-11. Also included is an introduction by Guillermo Del Toro.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.thenerdfilter.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/spread-1.jpg">
            <img alt="spread-#1" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-9917" height="300" src="http://www.thenerdfilter.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/spread-1-197x300.jpg" width="197"/>
           </a>
           <a href="http://www.thenerdfilter.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/how-to-survive-sharknado.jpg">
            <img alt="how-to-survive-sharknado" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-9918" height="300" src="http://www.thenerdfilter.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/how-to-survive-sharknado-215x300.jpg" width="215"/>
           </a>
           Spread #1
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Image comics are on fire! The last few years they have done some truly great series. Also their method of not bombarding the market with millions of printed books, proves to add demand to first printings. Spread looks great! About an ancient force that was unearthed a decade ago, the Spread kills anything it touches. Now a man has found a way to stop it, with a baby!
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           How To Survive Sharknado &amp; Other Unnatural Disasters
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Not so much a comic book, but more like a real book. This silly How-To book is the latest from Syfy channel milking the success of Sharknado. This Thursday in select theaters RiffTrax is doing a special live screening of Sharknado! You can check out their
          <a href="http://www.fathomevents.com/event/rifftrax-live-sharknado">
           official site
          </a>
          to see if its playing by a theater by you.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           Bonus Pick:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           A Waste Of Time #1
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.thenerdfilter.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/waste-of-time-1.jpg">
           <img alt="waste-of-time-1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9914" height="265" src="http://www.thenerdfilter.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/waste-of-time-1.jpg" width="400"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          You had me at coke-addicted teddy bear! This sounds really funny. Rick is a rabbit and a cartoonist which is obsessed with getting famous. Waste Of Time is by Rick Worely, so its a fun look at art imitating life! This was published by Northwest Press, a smaller comic distributer, so you might not find it at your local comic shop. Worth the online order.
         </p>
