       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Birthplace of Silicon Valley
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-07-14T05:00:59+00:00">
         July 14, 2014
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://www.courtneyprice.com/author/courtneyprice/" rel="author">
           Courtney Price
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://www.courtneyprice.com/birthplace-of-silicon-valley/#comments">
          12 Comments
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/SiliconValley.jpg">
          <img alt="Silicon Valley on www.CourtneyPrice.com #girlTECH" class="aligncenter wp-image-14166 size-full" data-lazy-src="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/SiliconValley.jpg" height="580" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="580"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Silicon Valley on www.CourtneyPrice.com #girlTECH" class="aligncenter wp-image-14166 size-full" height="580" src="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/SiliconValley.jpg" width="580"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Today I am going to take you on a little field trip to Silicon Valley. The term Silicon Valley, a nickname for the South Bay portion of the San Francisco Bay Area in northern California, was branded in 1970 by Ralph Vaerst, a California entrepreneur. Silicon referred to the silicon chip manufacturers who dominated the region, and Valley referred to the Santa Clara Valley. We are going to head that way to check out one of the landmarks that highlights where the tech craze began
         <span id="more-13952">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarageStreet.jpg">
          <img alt="Palo Alto- HPGarageStreet - Silicon Valley on www.CourtneyPrice.com #girlTECH" class="aligncenter wp-image-13957 size-full" data-lazy-src="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarageStreet.jpg" height="343" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="580"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Palo Alto- HPGarageStreet - Silicon Valley on www.CourtneyPrice.com #girlTECH" class="aligncenter wp-image-13957 size-full" height="343" src="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarageStreet.jpg" width="580"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Palo Alto, about an hour south of San Francisco. Well stop off in a nice, tree-lined, shady neighborhood.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarageHouse.jpg">
          <img alt="HP Garage House,Silicon Valley on www.CourtneyPrice.com #girlTECH" class="aligncenter wp-image-13954 size-full" data-lazy-src="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarageHouse.jpg" height="384" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="580"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="HP Garage House,Silicon Valley on www.CourtneyPrice.com #girlTECH" class="aligncenter wp-image-13954 size-full" height="384" src="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarageHouse.jpg" width="580"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Here we are, on Addison Avenue look special? Well, it is special enough to probably drive the current inhabitants nuts with all of the slow drive-bys and photo stops. This house was chosen by Bill Hewlett and Dave Packard to rent 
         <em>
          because
         </em>
         it had a garage.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarage.jpg">
          <img alt="HPGarage" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13955" data-lazy-src="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarage.jpg" height="387" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="580"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="HPGarage" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13955" height="387" src="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarage.jpg" width="580"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         In 1938, they developed their first product out of this garage, which is now California Historic Landmark # 976. If you say the garage, locals will assume this is the garage you speak of. The back story is cool, see below:
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarageLandmark.jpg">
          <img alt="HP Garage - Landmark #976, Silicon Valley on www.CourtneyPrice.com #girlTECH" class="aligncenter wp-image-13953 size-full" data-lazy-src="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarageLandmark.jpg" height="772" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="580"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="HP Garage - Landmark #976, Silicon Valley on www.CourtneyPrice.com #girlTECH" class="aligncenter wp-image-13953 size-full" height="772" src="http://www.courtneyprice.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/HPGarageLandmark.jpg" width="580"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Bill Hewlett and Dave Packard caught the eye of professor Frederick Terman, who strongly encouraged them (in the height of a depression) to start their own company. The above mentioned low-distortion audio oscillator was made to test sound equipment  it was the subject of Bill Hewletts masters thesis. One of the companys first customers was Walt Disney Productions, who installedsecond generation oscillators for the surround-sound systems in theaters for the movie
         <em>
          Fantasia
         </em>
         . How is THAT for a solid start Dr. Termans instinct was spot on.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">

          <noscript>
