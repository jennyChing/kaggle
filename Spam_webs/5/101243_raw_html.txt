        <h1 class="post-title item fn" itemprop="name">
         Top 10 SciFi Movies
        </h1>
        <a class="comments" href="https://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/top-10-scifi-movies/#comments">
         <i class="fa fa-comments-o">
         </i>
         0

       <div class="post-meta">
        <span class="posted-by">
         By
         <span class="reviewer" itemprop="author">
          <a href="https://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/author/armanadm/" rel="author" title="Posts by TorontoVaporizer">
           TorontoVaporizer
          </a>

        </span>
        <span class="posted-on">
         on
         <span class="dtreviewed">

          </time>

        </span>
        <span class="cats">
         <a href="https://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/fun-stuff/" rel="category tag">

       <div class="post-container cf">
        <div class="post-content-right">
         <div class="post-content description " itemprop="articleBody">
          <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
           <link content="Google+" href="https://plus.google.com/11185199345126485288" rel="author"/>
           <link content="Google+" href="https://plus.google.com/u/0/108229344853687425046" rel="publisher"/>
           <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             Sometimes you just want to get away when vaping. If you cant get away, a good movie that transports you to another time and place will do. Many of us have a good problem in that we have too many awesome science fiction movies to choose from. Last week, we listed the
             <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/lifestyle-fun/top-10-table-top-games/">
              Top 10 Table Top Games
             </a>
             to play while vapin. Today we are going to be looking at some entertaining science fiction films that you can enjoy while vaporizing. Try out these movies while using the
             <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/volcano-vaporizer-digital.html">
              Volcano
             </a>
             <b>
             </b>
             Vaporizer
             <b>
             </b>
             or the
             <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/arizer-extreme-q-vaporizer.html">
              Extreme Q
             </a>
             Vaporizer. Lets get started.
            </div>
            <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Top-10-Vapester-SciFi-Movies.png" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
              <img alt="Top 10 Vapester SciFi Movies " border="0" src="//torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Top-10-Vapester-SciFi-Movies.png" title="Top 10 Vapester SciFi Movies "/>
             </a>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Looper.png" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
              <img alt="Looper" border="0" src="//torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Looper.png" title="Looper"/>
             </a>
            </div>
            <h3 style="text-align: left;">
             10. Looper
            </h3>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             Seeing a man try to kill himself has never been so thrilling. Keep in mind that the man that our man character Joe (Joseph Gordon-Levitt) is trying to kill is an older time travelling version of himself bent on stopping the rise of a super villain in the current timeline. A new and fresh look at time travel and action in science fiction, Looper never stops giving you something to think about.
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
            </div>
            <p>
            </p>
            <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
             <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Star-Trek-2009.png" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
              <img alt="Star Trek (2009)" border="0" src="//torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Star-Trek-2009.png" title="Star Trek (2009)"/>
             </a>
            </div>
            <h3 style="text-align: left;">
             <span>
             </span>
             <span>
             </span>
             9. Star Trek (2009)
            </h3>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             The movie that made it okay to like Star Trek again, this movie used time travel to let us again travel with a revamped and reimagined version of the original series cast. Kirk and Spock must race to save earth from a Romulan threat in the future. Add in high quality effects and a thrilling story, and you have a great way to spend your vape session.
             <span style="white-space: pre;">
             </span>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             <p>
             </p>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/The-Matrix.png" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="The Matrix" border="0" src="//torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/The-Matrix.png" title="The Matrix"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <h3>
              8. The Matrix
             </h3>
             <div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             The Matrix had a huge impact on science fiction in the early 2000s. Combing cyberpunk aesthetics and kung fu, The Matrix brought an exciting and visually mind blowing look to the story of Neo. Following the Heros Journey, Neo must find a way to defeat the evil machines while trying to save humanity from a world that isnt all that it seems. It also spawned all of your friends endless repeating Free your mind.
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             <p>
             </p>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Children-of-Men.png" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="Children of Men" border="0" src="//torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Children-of-Men.png" title="Children of Men"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <h3>
              7. Children of Men
             </h3>
             <div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             A dystopian look at a future where no one can have children anymore, Children of Men offers a chilling look at a future without hope. Clive Owen plays a civil servant who is trying to help a pregnant refugee escape the chaos of a rotting and decrepit United Kingdom. It offers a look at what society can be with hope. Children of Men is a movie that will make you think.
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             <p>
             </p>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Th-Fifth-Element.png" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="The Fifth Element" border="0" src="//torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Th-Fifth-Element.png" title="The Fifth Element"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <h3>
              6. The Fifth Element
             </h3>
             <div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             The Fifth Element is a classic comic book movie that didnt need to be based on a comic. Bruce Willis plays Korben Dallas, a cab driver who gets mixed up in a conspiracy that has the potential to destroy all life in the universe. A unique style and direction from Luc Besson, The Fifth Element combines awesome visual effects and humor to create a film that is unforgettable.
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <p>
            </p>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Rise-of-the-Planet-of-the-Apes.png" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="Rise of the Planet of the Apes  " border="0" src="//torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Rise-of-the-Planet-of-the-Apes.png" title="Rise of the Planet of the Apes  "/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <h3>
              5. Rise of the Planet of the Apes
             </h3>
             <div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             Acting as a new origin story for the franchise, Rise of the Planet of the Apes is an action packed film that looks at the limits and morality issues of science. It has also super intelligent fighting apes. Cool visuals and questions of identity permeate throughout the film. It won a tonne of awards and deserves your time and effort.
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             <p>
             </p>
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Avatar.png" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="Avatar" border="0" src="//torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Avatar.png" title="Avatar"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <h3>
              4. Avatar
             </h3>
             <div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             Avatar is the film that defined 3D movies for the masses. Visually beautiful and striking, Avatar showed us what you can really do with 3d cameras and high quality computer generated imagery. Our protagonist, Jake Sully must operate a living avatar to try to help the indigenous population of Pandora fight back against the enemies trying to colonize it. No other movie has looked this good in three dimensions.
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/inception.png" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="Inception" border="0" src="//torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/inception.png" title="Inception"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <h3>
              3. Inception
             </h3>
             <div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             Christopher Nolan brought this brainbuster out in 2010. It stars a large ensemble cast and features really cool and interesting visuals. Inception follows Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio), who is a thief that performs corporate espionage by infiltrating the subconscious of his victims. He can get his job back if he can do one last impossible job, implanting an idea into another persons mind. The film combines science fiction tropes with a gripping heist style story. It keeps you asking questions even after the credits roll.
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/district-9.png" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="District 9" border="0" src="//torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/district-9.png" title="District 9"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <h3>
              2. District 9
             </h3>
             <div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             Neil Blomkamp brought us this science fiction action thriller in 2009 and there really hasnt been anything like it since. He managed to construct a smart and taut fake documentary about aliens living in the slums of Johannesburg out of surveillance footage, fake interviews and news stories as well as intense action scenes. The film follows a mild mannered manager as he has to help aliens deal with private military and corporate interests while staying alive. The film combines heart racing action with contemporary social issues.
             <p>
             </p>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             <div style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              <a href="http://torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Prometheus.png" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
               <img alt="Prometheus" border="0" src="//torontovaporizer.ca/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Prometheus.png" title="Prometheus"/>
              </a>
             </div>
             <h3>
              1. Prometheus
             </h3>
             <div>
             </div>
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             Ridley Scott returns to the genre that helped define his earlier career. Happening X years prior to Alien, Prometheus finds a different crew searching for answers among the universe. What they didnt expect was that there are some questions that shouldnt be answered. Dark and suspenseful with great visual effects and storytelling, Prometheus is a nonstop thrill ride that keeps you enthralled to the very end. Lets hope that they make a sequel soon to answer even more questions.
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
            </div>
            <div style="text-align: justify;">
             There you have it, some of the best top ten modern science fiction films out there right now. These movies will blow you away with inventive storytelling, explosive visual effects and cool action scenes. If you havent got a vaporizer or need something new, check out our

              vaporizer store
