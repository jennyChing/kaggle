            <p>
             Also known as a White Lady or the Chelsea Sidecar, the Delilah is one of those classic, somewhat forgotten cocktails. Like the
             <a href="/od/atozcocktailrecipes/r/marg_cktl.htm">
              Margarita
             </a>
             , this drink is in the
             <a href="/od/s/r/side_car_cktl.htm">
              Sidecar
             </a>
             family, which uses a base spirit, orange liqueur, and citrus fruit formula. In this instance we use gin, Cointreau, and lemon.
            </p>
            <p>
             This is a fabulous cocktail, particularly
             <a data-inlink="At0cId16WBMQGonVkHtZ6g==" href="/od/ginrecipes/tp/10-Essential-Gin-Cocktails.htm">
              with the right gin
             </a>
             and fresh lemon juice. Experiment with some of the new and artisan gins that are now available because there are some delightful bouquets to be found with this mix.
