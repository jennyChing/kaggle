                <h2>
                 Introduction
                </h2>
                <p>
                 <a href="http://www.phpnuke.org/">
                  phpNuke
                 </a>
                 - is a distributed under
                 <a href="http://www.gnu.org/">
                  GNU/GPL
                 </a>
                 license Web-portals engine, written
  on
                 <a href="http://www.php.net/">
                  PHP
                 </a>
                 . The given definition also is
  applicable to all other engines supported by the given program. But
  originally the program was written for phpNuke therefore it is named
  NukeLang.
                </p>
                <p>
                 One of features of phpNuke system is a possibility of fast switching the
  interface of a portal into different languages. All text information for
  each of languages is stored in the extrenal files.
                </p>
                <p>
                 To edit the language files it is possible in any text editor, however it is 
  rather inconvenient because of its large size. For rise of convenience in
  operation of a translator just this program also was created.
                </p>
                <hr/>

                 </li>
                 <li>
                  less than 1 Megabyte of free space on hard disk.
                 </li>
                </ul>
