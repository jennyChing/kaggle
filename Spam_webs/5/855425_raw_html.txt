             <h3 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://www.saltwater-kids.com/2011/01/bread-making-kit.html">
               bread making kit
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
              </div>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              My best friend Kelly and her husband have been the guinea pigs for my bread making adventure over the past year. They have always given kind feedback, and flattered me by returning their bread bag and asking for more. If they ever tasted a bad loaf, they never said so.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_f5YcRl17q54/TSPxSsKFIII/AAAAAAAAYNs/x18oryFv-8I/s1600/bread+gift+kit+6.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="343" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_f5YcRl17q54/TSPxSsKFIII/AAAAAAAAYNs/x18oryFv-8I/s400/bread+gift+kit+6.jpg" width="400"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              For her belated Christmas gift, I put together a bread making kit which includes
              <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Artisan-Bread-Five-Minutes-Revolutionizes/dp/0312362919/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1294202821&amp;sr=8-1">
               this
              </a>
              book, a baking stone, yeast, handmade bread bags,  and container for storing the dough.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_f5YcRl17q54/TSPxNldmr9I/AAAAAAAAYNc/olfeVYka9Cw/s1600/bread+bag+kit+1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="247" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_f5YcRl17q54/TSPxNldmr9I/AAAAAAAAYNc/olfeVYka9Cw/s400/bread+bag+kit+1.jpg" width="400"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              These bread bags are a little different from the
              <a href="http://www.saltwater-kids.com/2010/04/5-minute-bread-bag-tutorial_9456.html">
               ones I made in the past
              </a>
              . Using flour sack kitchen towels I was able to make 12 bags for under $4 ($0.33/each)!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_f5YcRl17q54/TSPxPJ16VlI/AAAAAAAAYNg/QjPFC53oRFE/s1600/bread+gift+kit+2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="400" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_f5YcRl17q54/TSPxPJ16VlI/AAAAAAAAYNg/QjPFC53oRFE/s400/bread+gift+kit+2.jpg" width="265"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              I love how they turned out. The material is very lightweight, and the wrinkles give the bags such a nice texture.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_f5YcRl17q54/TSPxQI9Yf5I/AAAAAAAAYNk/B4ZojTt2kAc/s1600/bread+gift+kit+3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="400" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_f5YcRl17q54/TSPxQI9Yf5I/AAAAAAAAYNk/B4ZojTt2kAc/s400/bread+gift+kit+3.jpg" width="265"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              I embroidered "fresh" on the bottom of one of the bags, just for fun!
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_f5YcRl17q54/TSPxRtipXwI/AAAAAAAAYNo/trJhoVTaOdY/s1600/bread+gift+kit+5.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="400" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_f5YcRl17q54/TSPxRtipXwI/AAAAAAAAYNo/trJhoVTaOdY/s400/bread+gift+kit+5.jpg" width="362"/>
               </a>
              </div>

          replybox = document.getElementById('comment-editor');

            replyUrlParts = replybox.src.split('#');

              + '#' + replyUrlParts[1];
