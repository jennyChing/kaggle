          <p>
           Id like to make this a weekly feature but who knows if I would be able to find enough really cool alternative uses for alcohol bottles.
           <br/>
           <img alt="private-stock-lamp" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-58836" src="http://www.everyjoe.com/files/2009/04/private-stock-lamp-224x300.jpg" width="150"/>
           <br/>
           Anyway, I found this truly awesome use of a spent Captain Morgan Private Stock bottle.
          </p>
          <p>
           This is what
           <a href="http://www.silverbetty.com" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.silverbetty.com']);" target="_blank">
            Tom
           </a>
           [the creator of this great lamp] has to say about the history behind this cool creation:
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            I had a bottle
            <a href="http://www.rumjumbie.com/rumjumbieliquer.html" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.rumjumbie.com']);" target="_blank">
             Rum Jumbie
            </a>
            a friend got me when he went on a cruise. I had asked him to pick me up one as
            <a href="http://www.sunnyjim.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.sunnyjim.com']);" target="_blank">
             James Sunny Jim White
            </a>
            sings a song about it. I was going to see James Sunny Jim White perform, so I took the bottle. He told me he had seen people make Rum Jumbie bottles into lamps. So I looked into that when I got home. The Rum Jumbie wasnt empty yet, but I had an empty Captain Morgans Private Stock bottle. I used that to create my first liquor bottle lamp.
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           Sure, Tom could have just tossed his bottle but isnt it cool that he re-purposed it and made it into something functional AND cool? Now, its not only a lamp but also a conversation piece.
          </p>
          <p>
           Hell, I found it while randomly searching Flickr and now Bill has his lamp featured on EveryJoe.com.
          </p>
          <p>
           Dont you wish that you had done something as cool?
          </p>
