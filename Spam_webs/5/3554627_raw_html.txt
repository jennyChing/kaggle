          Pearl Ibis
         </h1>
         A Poem by
         <a href="http://www.writerscafe.org/RangerKessel">

         </a>
         <br/>

         <div class="clear">
         </div>

         <div class="writing-item-writing-text" id="writingNotes">
          <span id="docs-internal-guid-de16e49f-465e-c0e4-97a1-ecf6a82d7c61">
           <font face="courier new" size="2">
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              pearl ibis
             </span>
            </p>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              cloistered wings
             </span>
            </p>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              withdrawn
             </span>
            </p>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              in elegant embrace
             </span>
            </p>
            <div style="text-align: left;">
             <br/>
            </div>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              pearl ibis
             </span>
            </p>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              in my heart
             </span>
            </p>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              chiffon
             </span>
            </p>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              refined in lace
             </span>
            </p>
            <div style="text-align: left;">
             <br/>
            </div>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              pearl ibis
             </span>
            </p>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              stately
             </span>
            </p>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              in the dawn
             </span>
            </p>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              elemental grace
             </span>
            </p>
            <div style="text-align: left;">
             <br/>
            </div>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              pearl ibis
             </span>
            </p>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              bristle whiskers
             </span>
            </p>
            <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left; line-height: 1.15; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">
             <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
              painted in wavy sun
             </span>
            </p>
            <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">
             <div style="text-align: left;">
              softly singing swan
             </div>
            </span>
