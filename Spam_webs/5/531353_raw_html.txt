           <h2 class="art-postheader">
            <a href="http://tnsc-blog.co.uk/it-support/top-three-reasons-to-outsource-your-it-support/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Top Three Reasons to Outsource Your IT Support">
             Top Three Reasons to Outsource Your IT Support
            </a>
           </h2>
           <div class="art-postmetadataheader">
            <div class="art-postheadericons art-metadata-icons">
             <img alt="" class="art-metadata-icon" height="18" src="http://tnsc-blog.co.uk/wp-content/themes/tnsc_blog/images/postdateicon.png" width="17"/>
             September 3, 2014
            </div>
           </div>
           <div class="art-postcontent">
            <!-- article-content -->
            <p>
             Have you ever thought about what would happen if your internet went down and you didnt notice for a while? Worse still, once you did notice, you then couldnt identify or fix the problem. Or maybe, your business is evolving and your current solution isnt appropriate for your needs anymore but you dont know what your options are.
            </p>
            <p>
             Whatever your individual circumstances, here are the top three reasons why outsourcing your IT support can mean youll always have someone on hand to answer your questions and make it their job to worry about your internet connectivity, so you can relax and concentrate on running your business.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              1. Proactive monitoring
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Investing in an outsourced IT solution means that you can stop finally stop firefighting. Rather than waiting for a problem to occur with your internet connectivity, an outsourced IT solution will offer your business proactive monitoring. This means someone is employed full time to be watching for issues and foresee problems before they arise rather than after it is too late. In other words, they are on it all of the time, so you can relax and focus on your core business. Downtime for many businesses means a direct loss of sales, so an outsourced solution can be critical to successful business continuity.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              2. Specialist software
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Ensure you have a fully functioning internet connection all of the time. Dont be the last to know that the internet is down and your business has suffered. Many companies that offer IT services provide specialist software which allows you to observe your network data, collate it, scan through it and make reports, so you can find the root of any common problems. This software will also check internet connectivity and wifi connectivity every few seconds, so you dont have to.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              3. The right type of connection for your business
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             The importance of getting the right type of connection for your business is often underestimated. One of the services an outsourced IT service provider can offer you is advice on whether you need a standard ADSL all the way up to an SDSL depending on the size of your business, your speed of growth and the amount of flexibility you need. Making a plan based around your type of business is important. Your business is unique and operationally you might find that, once presented with all options, there is a better solution out there for your existing and future requirements.
            </p>
            <p>
             For more information on outsourcing your IT support, you can visit
             <a href="http://www.tnsc.co.uk/products-01.asp">
              tnsc.co.uk
             </a>
             .
            </p>
