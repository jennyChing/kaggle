      <h1 class="entry-title">
       Portugal, The Man In The Mountain In The Cloud [NEW MUSIC]
      </h1>
      <!-- .entry-title -->
     </header>
     <!-- .entry-header -->
     <div class="entry-content">
      <!-- Start Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic -->
      <!-- End Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic -->
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.portugaltheman.com/" target="_blank">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-15221" height="300" src="http://musicwithoutlabels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/Portugal.-The-Man-In-the-Mountain-in-the-Cloud-2011-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       So a while back I started a new job and since then I have been pretty much flying by the seat of my pants learning and dominating (wait for it) the internet. The internet is my little bitch at the moment. I am king of the internet.
      </p>
      <p>
       Ok so I think that just got a little out of hand, but I have been working so hard recently I have obviously lost touch with reality and sensibility all together. I need something, anything to bring my crazy internet dominating ass back to reality. A few years ago I heard about this band called Portugal, The Man and I gave them a shot. This was back in the day when I thought The String Cheese Incident was the end all be all of musical expertise and perfection. Needless to say Portugal, The Man fell through my gaping, wide open musical cracks. Lucky for me bands like them continue to have success and release new music. The records called In The Mountain In The Cloud and as youll see they aint no String Cheese Incident.
      </p>
      <p>
       Portugal, The Man  So American
      </p>
      <p>
       <object data="http://www.youtube.com/v/NgZb3FQ2uNc" height="350" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="425">
        <param name="movie" value="http://www.youtube.com/v/NgZb3FQ2uNc">

      <p>
       <a href="http://musicwithoutlabels.com/2011/04/29/portugal-the-man-got-it-all-this-cant-be-living-now-music/" target="_blank">
        A little while back my good friend posted the tune Got It All by Portugal the Man on this blog HERE.
       </a>
       Now that the rest of the record has been released I get to share the rest with you. Ill start by saying that Ive listened through this bad boy 2 times now. I cant find a bad note on the whole thing. My bud Ryan whos also a fan said this is the MGMT record we never got after Oracular Spectacular and while this statement holds many truths and I happen to agree Portugal, The Man has delivered a gem all their own. This is the kind of rock and roll people want to hear right now. Bright harmonies, happy rhythms, and fun music. These are the songs you can sing a long to in a seedy club, that is if you can hit the high falsetto vocals that drench this beast of a CD.
      </p>
      <p>
       Portugal, The Man  Head Is A Flame (Cool With It)
      </p>

      <p>
       This music gets me pumped. Its bands like this that I heard about, never gave them the time of day years ago that come back and just slap me in the face and say told ya so, jerk. I am a jerk for not listening to these guys when I should have. Then I could have said oh man Ive been listening to these guys since the beginning and Im cooler than you because of it. Sadly I can not.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.portugaltheman.com/photo/" target="_blank">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-15218" height="200" src="http://musicwithoutlabels.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/ffc776f1-b700-4325-9ad1-94dbf5789339_8-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       So I tip my hat to Portugal, The Man fans everywhere for liking this band before me. I am jealous and I am also proud to now be a new fan. In The Mountain In The Cloud is awesome cover to cover I cant get enough. I even burned it to a CD (what?, I know!) and popped it in my car for my 17 hour commute down the 101 freeway of death here in LA. This is my go to track for sitting in traffic. Soon it will be yours as well.
      </p>
      <p>
       Share With Me The Sun  Portugal, The Man

       They are making stops on the east coast and south before heading to Europe in November, which sucks for me. But I will wait patiently until they come back to LA. You can buy their new release
       <a href="http://store.portugaltheman.com/" target="_blank">
        HERE!

       If you dont buy it Im going to assume you are going through a String Cheese Incident phase and Ill let it slide. Heres the last track from Portugal, The Mans new record In The Mountain In The Cloud.
