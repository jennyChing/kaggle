           <em>

           </em>
          </h3>

        <p>
         The atmosphere and texture that comes from imagery that has rain pouring is definitely unique. It can add that next level of drama or intensity to a scene, and in this video from
         <a href="http://tomantosfilms.com" target="_blank">
          Tom Antos
         </a>
         , he shows us how to do that for less than $20.
        </p>
        <p>
         Tom shows that with a trip to the local hardware store and access to running water, this cool effect can be created with relative ease. Add some backlight to the rain, edit in some storm sound effects in post, and youre in business.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you found this informative, check out some more of Toms tutorials on lighting and film production.
        </p>
