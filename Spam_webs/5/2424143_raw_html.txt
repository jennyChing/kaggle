         <h1>
          Look Up  Be Aware of Your Surroundings and Positively Spin Each Situation
         </h1>
         <div class="post-meta">
          <div class="fleft">
           Posted in:
           <a href="http://heidisiefkas.com/category/blog/" rel="category tag">
            Blog
           </a>
           | December 27, 2014 at 8:30 am
          </div>
          <div class="fright">
           <a class="comments-link" href="http://heidisiefkas.com/look-up-be-aware-of-your-surroundings-and-positively-spin-each-situation/#respond">
            No comments
           </a>
          </div>
         </div>
         <!--.post-meta-->
         <div class="featured-thumbnail">
          <img alt="Look_Up_Maui_Black_Sand_Beach" class="attachment-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" height="150" src="http://heidisiefkas.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/LookUpHeidiSiefkasBalckSandBeachMaui-200x150.jpg" width="200"/>
         </div>
         <div class="post-content">
          <p>
           <strong>
            Happy Look Up Day!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           In 2010, I started Look Up Day to remind me of a powerful lesson I learned along a nine-month recovery from a freak tree accident in New Yorks Hudson River Valley. Celebrated each 27th to coincide with the date of the accident (9/27/09), I share Look Up Day and its two major components with you:
          </p>
          <p>
           1) Be aware of your surroundings, appreciating the beauty as well as the hazards. In essence, be in the moment. Be more mindful.
          </p>
          <p>
           2) Spin each situation positively. Although many situations appears negative at first, as did my tragedy, they are opportunities which may lead you on a needed life detour.
          </p>
          <p>
           Look Up Day was creatively named to pass on the two components in one package. Its double entendre expresses perfectly that which helped me through a full blown life catastrophe. Although today is Look Up Day, the wisdom behind its creation is a powerful tool that you can carry with you daily. It continues to guide me through lifes hiccups. It can do the same for you.
          </p>
          <p>
           I wrote a vignette about the creation and meaning of Look Up Day in my inspirational memoir,
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/When-All-Balls-Drop-Everything/dp/1627871217/ref=cm_cr_pr_product_top">
            <em>
             When All Balls Drop
            </em>
           </a>
           . You can watch a video reading of this vignette filmed in Hanalei Bay, Kauai,
           <a href="http://heidisiefkas.com/happy-look-up-day-reading-from-when-all-balls-drop-in-hanalei-bay-kauai/">
            here
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           If you havent read
           <em>
            When All Balls Drop
           </em>
           , get your copy
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/When-All-Balls-Drop-Everything/dp/1627871217/ref=cm_cr_pr_product_top">
            here
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Happy Look Up Day!
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Heres to looking up!
           </strong>
          </p>
         </div>
