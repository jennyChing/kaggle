             </h1>
            </div>

Wesoowski has already been removed from the clerical state after being charged with possession of child pornography and sexual abuse of minors.

          </span>

         </div>

          </span>
          , to the Bible of
          <span style="font-weight: bold;">

          </span>

         </div>

         </div>

          </span>
         </div>

          <span style="font-style: italic;">

          </span>
         </div>

         </div>

          </span>

         </div>

          <br/>
         </div>

          </span>

          </span>

Next to the Missal of Archbishop Romero is the ferula that belonged to Cardinal Posadas Ocampo, Archbishop of Guadalajara, who was killed by drug dealers in Mexico.
          </span>
         </div>
         <div>
          <br/>
         </div>
         <div>

          <span style="font-weight: bold;">
           this Basilica would honor the lives of martyrs
          </span>
          .
         </div>
         <div>
          <br/>
         </div>

          <span style="font-weight: bold;">
           to collect testimonies of Christian martyrs
          </span>
          who have died because of their faith.
