         <div class="entry-content">
          <p class="first-child ">
           <span class="dropcap" title="H">
            H
           </span>
           ave you heard about the Googles new search engine algorithm? No? Well, then let me introduce you to Googles Penguin search engine algorithm.
          </p>
          <img alt="Google Penguin Algorithm Update" class="featured-image wp-post-image" height="256" src="https://shannonksteffen.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/penguin-sheriff.jpg" width="400"/>
          <p>
           Its not as fluffy as Googles Panda but it still gives quite a punch to those
           <strong>
            search engine optimization (SEO) best practices
           </strong>
           and search engine rankings for
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/9-tips-to-start-getting-your-blog-noticed/" title="9 Tips To Start Getting Your Blog Noticed">
            blogs
           </a>
           as well as personal and business websites. Heres the breakdown on both algorithms so you can see how it may affect you and your online brand.
          </p>
          <h2>
           Google Panda Algorithm Overview:
          </h2>
          <h2>
           <img alt="Google Panda Update" class="alignright size-full wp-image-1633" height="300" src="https://shannonksteffen.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Google-Panda-update.jpeg" title="Google Panda Update" width="263"/>
          </h2>
          <p>
           When Google first launched it search engine algorithm,
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/how-the-seo-panda-works-infographic/" title="How The SEO Panda Works [Infographic]">
            Panda
           </a>
           , it was said by Google that:
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            This update is designed to reduce rankings for low-quality sitessites which are low-value add for users, copy content from other websites or sites that are just not very useful. At the same time, it will provide better rankings for high-quality sitessites with original content and information such as research, in-depth reports, thoughtful analysis and so on.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           In Human SEO
           <sup>
            tm
           </sup>
           terms, the Panda update was to help wean out the low quality websites to bring the higher quality ones to their users. This update hit website owners very hard and many saw their website rankings drop like rock. Low quality websites included those that were
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/never-duplicate-seo-strategies/" title="Why SEO Strategies Should Never Be Duplicated">
            keyword stuffed
           </a>
           , were
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/do-you-have-a-content-farm/" title="Do You Have a Content Farm?">
            content farms
           </a>
           and that added little value to readers. The result was a scurry of webmasters working quickly to increase the quality of their website content and fix any issues that would have the algorithm see their content as being
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/7-tips-for-on-page-seo-success/" title="7 Tips for On-page SEO Success">
            duplicate content
           </a>
           of another website or even on their own.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            In essence, Google Panda looks at human experience and human engagement.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <h2>
           Google Penguin Algorithm Overview:
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="Google Penguin Algorithm Update" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-1631" height="300" src="https://shannonksteffen.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/google-penguin-285x300.jpg" title="Google Penguin Algorithm Update" width="285"/>
           On April 24, 2012, Google launched the newest large algorithm update called Penguin. According to Google, this was an:
           <em>
            important algorithm change targeted at webspam. The change will decrease rankings for sites that we believe are violating Googles existing quality guidelines.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           Unfortunately, that statement leaves a lot to guess about so lets look a little closer.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Penguin search engine algorithm looks for
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/seo-what-color-is-your-lightsaber/" title="SEO: What Color is Your Lightsaber?">
            black hat SEO tactics
           </a>
           that are demonstrated through
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/9-ways-search-engines-value-links/" title="9 Ways Search Engines Value Links">
            irrelevant linking
           </a>
           and webspam. Other black hat SEO tactics can include
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/seo-terminology-glossary/" title="SEO Terminology Glossary">
            cloaking
           </a>
           , deliberate duplicate content creation, keyword stuffing and linking schemes.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            In essence, Google Penguin looks at the technical experience and unethical SEO tactics.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           If youve been seeing a down turn in your SEO rankings,
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/repairing-website-seo-blunders/" title="Repairing Website Ranking After Major SEO Blunders">
            dont panic
           </a>
           , as there can be a number of contributing factors. If you are practicing
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/8-benefits-of-white-hat-seo/" title="The Benefits of White Hat SEO Tactics">
            white hat SEO
           </a>
           tactics and strategies, then it is most likely a simple technological change or that you are lacking in human engagement on your website to allow for
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/grow-business-free-through-social-media/" title="Grow Business Free Through Social Media">
            social sharing
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           There are many simple changes that can help you rank higher with either Google search engine algorithm. Just make sure that you
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/7-tips-for-working-with-a-seo-consultant/" title="7 Helpful Tips for Working with a SEO Company">
            hire an ethical SEO consultant
           </a>
           and that they convey a sense of partnership in regards to your online branding.
          </p>
          <p>
           The more intuitive the search engines become, the more need there is for a
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/4-seo-changes-tips/" title="SEO Changes and 4 Long-Term Success Tips">
            long-term SEO strategy
           </a>
           that works both onsite and well as offsite. Long gone are the days of setting SEO and forgetting about it. Today, it is about Human SEO
           <sup>
            tm
           </sup>
            combining the understanding of human search patterns with technical expertise  to
           <a href="https://shannonksteffen.com/about/human-seo-strategies-that-work/" title="Why Choose Excira for Your SEO Project?">
            work with you as a success partner
           </a>
           .
          </p>
