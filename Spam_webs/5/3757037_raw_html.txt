          <h1 class="asset-name">
           <span class="bigletters">
            X-ray calendar shows off skeleton thin models
           </span>
          </h1>
          <div class="asset-meta">
           <span class="byline">
            June 28, 2010  3:30 PM
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="asset-content">
          <div class="asset-body">
           <span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image">
            <img alt="275x250.jpg" class="mt-image-left" height="300" src="http://newsliteimgs.s3.amazonaws.com/100628_xrayart1.jpg" style="float: left; margin: 0pt 20px 20px 0pt;" width="300"/>
           </span>
           <b>
            A saucy calendar has been produced which shows parts of models you never though you would see - but it's more X-ray than X-rated.
           </b>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The Eizo Pin-Up Calendar shows models in typical 'glamour' poses but has been taken with an X-ray machine rather than a traditional camera.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           All that can be seen in the images is bone structure, the outline of the body and the high-heel stilettos the models are wearing.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The promotional publication was devised by Berlin-based advertising agency BUTTER for their client Eizo, which makes medical display monitors.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           It was initially sent to doctors and other prospective clients, but has since proved a massive hit online and no-one can complain it prompts unhealthy body-types.
           <br/>
           <div>
            <br/>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="asset-more" id="more">
           <span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image">
            <img alt="275x250.jpg" class="mt-image-center" height="350" src="http://newsliteimgs.s3.amazonaws.com/100628_xrayart2.jpg" style="text-align: center; display: block; margin: 0pt auto 20px;" width="620"/>
           </span>
           A spokesperson for Eizo said: "The EIZO Medical pin-up calendar, is designed to show every detail.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           "The calendar  the likes of which this audience had never really seen before and which breaks a few taboos.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <span class="mt-enclosure mt-enclosure-image">
