        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Cheese Sticks
        </h1>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <span style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="416" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_rBrnSm1Xl08/S03Ix_GI4vI/AAAAAAAAF7M/dMSk9VuspDQ/s640/Picture-067.jpg" width="540"/>
         </span>
        </div>
        <p>
         Cheese is one of my favorite things to snack on.Even when I was still working as a call center agent.Back then, I would buy this little pieces of cheese for P10
         <span style="color: #666666;">
          [$0.21]
         </span>
         from a nearby convenience store and I would bring it to my station. You would find me eating little bits of cheese to keep me from being sleepy in between calls.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im trying to influence my daughter into eating cheese because cheese is a very healthy snack but she doesnt like it. But she loves
         <span style="color: #cc0000;">
          
         </span>
         this ooey-gooey cheese inside a really crunchy crustwho wouldnt?
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ingredients:
         </strong>
         <br/>
         Cheddar Cheese/Mozzarella Cheese -cut into -inch thick, 1-inch long strips
         <br/>
         2 cups japanese breadcrumbs
         <br/>
         2 eggs, beaten
         <br/>
         2 cups all-purpose flour
         <br/>
         Canola oil for frying
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="color: #8e7cc3;">
          
         </span>
         for the mayo-mustard sauce:
         <br/>
          cup mayonaise
         <br/>
         1 tbsp mustard
         <br/>
         1 tsp sugar
         <br/>
         a pinch of pepper
        </p>
        <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
         <span style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="390" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_rBrnSm1Xl08/S03lgNzXDJI/AAAAAAAAF7Q/g7eC5GXoCis/s640/Picture-071.jpg" width="472"/>
         </span>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Directions:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          One by one dip cheese strips into beaten egg.
         </li>
         <li>
          Then dredge with flour,dip into the egg again.
         </li>
         <li>
          Then roll into japanese breadcrumbs. Make sure each is fully coated.
         </li>
         <li>
          Deep fry in canola oil until golden brown
         </li>
         <li>
          For the mayo-mustard dip Just combine all the ingredients.Mix well.
         </li>
        </ul>
