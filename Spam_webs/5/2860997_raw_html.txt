            <p>
             To make this bread basket first you will knead bread dough. After that roll it flat in a rectangular shape and cut stripes of it. Then start by placing the stripes on an inverted oven proof bowl covered with aluminum foil in a way as if the stripes are falling out from its top to all the sides. After that with more dough stripes weave between the stripes as shown. Brush the complete dough basket with a beaten egg. Put it in the oven until beautifully golden. Moreover, you can also make a bread braid for the top edge and handle of this bread basket.
            </p>
