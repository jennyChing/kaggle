       <h4>
        The Story Of
       </h4>
       <h1>
        Deborah Pinni
       </h1>
       <div class="tagline">
        1,100 Miles Walked to Help Youth in Need
       </div>
      </div>
      <div class="description">
       <p>
        The spark between Deborah Pinni and her now husband, Kevin was immediate. The two met in in the UK, and quickly connected over a shared passion for similar causes. Although Deborah grew up in Australia and Kevin in Scotland, they had both witnessed the severe impact that deeply ingrained social problems can have on children.
       </p>
      </div>

   <section class="post-wrapper">
    <div class="post-inner clearfix">
     <div class="right-sidebar">
      <div class="story-meta">
       <ul class="list-unstyled">
        <li>
         <img class="meta-icon" src="https://qludn19a1ws1wby8q1l5qshc-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/us/wp-content/themes/edh-portal-2014/assets/img/sidebar-spark.png"/>
         <h5>
          The Spark
         </h5>
         <p>
          Deborah Pinnis desire to help children facing a large set of social obstacles was amplified when she meet her now husband Kevin and the two of them decided to take action.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li>
         <img class="meta-icon" src="https://qludn19a1ws1wby8q1l5qshc-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/us/wp-content/themes/edh-portal-2014/assets/img/sidebar-action.png"/>
         <h5>
          The Action
         </h5>
         <p>
          Deborah and Kevin decided that exploring Britain while fundraising for Youth Off the Streets was going to be their next great adventure.
         </p>
        </li>
        <li>
         <img class="meta-icon" src="https://qludn19a1ws1wby8q1l5qshc-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/us/wp-content/themes/edh-portal-2014/assets/img/sidebar-result.png"/>
         <h5>
          The Result
         </h5>
         <p>
          Walking over 1,100 miles throughout the UK for three months was not the only thing the two accomplished.  They also raised $2,000 for the cause they loved so much.
         </p>
        </li>
       </ul>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="main-col">
      <div class="post-content">
       <p>
        They knew that they wanted to work together to make a difference. Soon, their shared passion for a cause turned into a meaningful, committed relationship.
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         Kevin and I both saw firsthand how family breakdowns and drug and alcohol abuse can negatively impact young people and their families.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        Their willingness to go the distance brought them closer, too. The two had lived in Australia for the past decade, but were in search of a new challenge. They had always wanted to see more of Britain, and decided to combine an adventure with a major fundraising push. Specifically, by walking the length of the U.K. in support of
        <a href="http://www.youthoffthestreets.com.au/" target="_blank">
         Youth Off the Streets
        </a>
        , an organization that provides care for young people who are facing major challenges.
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         Homelessness, violence at home, drug abuse  these are issues that young people around the world are struggling with today.
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p dir="ltr">
        We knew that we had to raise awareness and funds, and to help in any way that we possibly could.
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr">
        At the end of June of 2013, Deborah and Kevin set out to walk 1,100 miles from Lands End (located at the top of the UK) to John OGroats (located at the bottom of the UK).
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: center">
        <a href="http://qludn19a1ws1wby8q1l5qshc-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/us/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/03/Loch-Lomond-Scotland.jpg">
         <img alt="OLYMPUS DIGITAL CAMERA" class="aligncenter wp-image-483" height="450" src="http://qludn19a1ws1wby8q1l5qshc-wpengine.netdna-ssl.com/us/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/03/Loch-Lomond-Scotland.jpg" width="600"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr">
        They set up a
        <a href="https://www.facebook.com/End2End2013?ref=hl" target="_blank">
         Facebook page
        </a>
        for the walk, so their family, friends, and colleagues could follow their journey along the way.
       </p>
       <p dir="ltr">
        Despite the exhaustion, they found the three month journey to be very rewarding. Not only did they see Britain in a unique way, but they raised close to $2,000 on everydayhero for a cause they are very passionate about.
       </p>
       <p>
        The experience was a fulfilling one. The couple hope to do another walk soon, and are currently continuing to speak out and fundraise on behalf of these troubled young people.
       </p>
