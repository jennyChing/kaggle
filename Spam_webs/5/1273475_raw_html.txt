       <h2>
        Las Vegas Distillery  Henderson, NV
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_345932" style="width: 950px">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Las Vegas Distillery" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/las-vegas-distillery1-940x529.jpg" title=""/>
        </noscript>
        <img class="size-large wp-image-345932 lazy" data-original="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/las-vegas-distillery1-940x529.jpg" height="529" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/assets/images/blank1.png" width="940"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo courtesy of

        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_345933" style="width: 950px">
        <noscript>

        </noscript>
        <img class="size-large wp-image-345933 lazy" data-original="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/las-vegas-distillery2-940x529.jpg" height="529" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/assets/images/blank1.png" width="940"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://travelnevada.com/?t=MatadorArticle5WinterDMA1412&amp;utm_source=Matador&amp;utm_campaign=MatadorArticle5WinterDMA1412&amp;utm_medium=Internet">
          TravelNevada
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        After watching millions of Vegas bro trips, bachelorette parties, and ballin high-rollers push mass-market hard liquor through their livers by the bottle, Las Vegas residents have gotten thirsty for something better.
       </p>
       <p>
        All about that hootch, Vegas was right there midwifing the birth of the craft distillery scene in Nevada. When George Racz founded
        <a href="http://www.lasvegasdistillery.com/">
         Las Vegas Distillery
        </a>
        , he bumped up against Nevadas Prohibition-era three-tier alcohol distribution laws, which made it nearly impossible for him to serve samples or sell his products out of his distillery. A huge supporter of all things local, Racz teamed up with a few other Nevada distillers to co-draft the bill that paved the way for up-and-coming craft distilleries in the state. So next time youre leaving a Nevada distillery with a couple bottles of craft booze under your arm and a shot in your belly, you know who to thank.
       </p>
       <p>
        Las Vegas Distillery, in the spirit of friendship and cooperation, values keeping business within their Nevada neighborhood and sources most of their grain from Winnemucca Farms in the north of the state. Check them out for a
        <a href="http://www.lasvegasdistillery.com/#!tours/c52n">
         free tour
        </a>
        (from George himself) or to smell, see, and catch a little buzz off their range of Vegas-born gin, vodka, whiskey, rum, something crazy (or genius) called Rumsky, and the very first Nevada bourbon ever created.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Seven Troughs Distilling Co.  Sparks, NV
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_345937" style="width: 950px">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Seven Troughs Distilling" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/seven-troughs1-940x529.jpg" title=""/>
        </noscript>
        <img class="size-large wp-image-345937 lazy" data-original="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/seven-troughs1-940x529.jpg" height="529" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/assets/images/blank1.png" width="940"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://travelnevada.com/?t=MatadorArticle5WinterDMA1412&amp;utm_source=Matador&amp;utm_campaign=MatadorArticle5WinterDMA1412&amp;utm_medium=Internet">
          TravelNevada
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_345938" style="width: 950px">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Seven Troughs Distilling" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/seven-troughs2-940x529.jpg" title=""/>
        </noscript>
        <img class="size-large wp-image-345938 lazy" data-original="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/seven-troughs2-940x529.jpg" height="529" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/assets/images/blank1.png" width="940"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://travelnevada.com/?t=MatadorArticle5WinterDMA1412&amp;utm_source=Matador&amp;utm_campaign=MatadorArticle5WinterDMA1412&amp;utm_medium=Internet">
          TravelNevada
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <a href="http://7troughsdistilling.com/">
         Seven Troughs
        </a>
        is all about keeping it old school; their process relies on the same methods George Washington used to operate 1799s most popular distillery. The brick-stove and pot-still setup that gives rise to their Recession Proof Moonshine produces super small batches of just 12 gallons at a time  all of which is manually bottled in mason jars and labeled by hand.
       </p>
       <p>
        This kind of historical accuracy is probably not the quickest way to make a fortune, but owner Tom Adams main concern is producing consistently great-tasting booze infused with Nevadas Old West spirit of self-reliance. Their grains are from Winnemucca Farms, their malts are sourced locally, and even the spent grain stays in-state  most of its donated to hog farmers in Yerington, while the gnarliest methanol-rich portions get sent over to a neighbor who repurposes it into additives for tractor and hot rod fuel.
       </p>
       <p>
        In addition to literally hand-crafting their inaugural white lightning, Seven Troughs has also recently added their Reno Rodeo vodka, Black Rock Rum, and Seven Troughs Bourbon to the roster. Pay them a visit in Sparks for a lesson in distilling history, a sample of their spirits, and to personalize a barrel of Nevada Bourbon that you can age under your own supervision.
       </p>
       <h2>
        The Depot Craft Brewery Distillery  Reno, NV
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_345814" style="width: 950px">
        <noscript>

        <p class="wp-caption-text">

       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_345813" style="width: 950px">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="The Depot Craft Brewery Distillery" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/depot_01987-940x624.jpg" title=""/>
        </noscript>
        <img class="size-large wp-image-345813 lazy" data-original="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/depot_01987-940x624.jpg" height="624" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/assets/images/blank1.png" width="940"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo:
         <a href="http://thedepotreno.com/">
          The Depot Craft Brewery Distillery
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        If theres anything that makes train travel even more awesome, its booze.
        <a href="http://thedepotreno.com/">
         The Depot Craft Brewery Distillery
        </a>
        opened in Renos historic Nevada-California-Oregon Railway headquarters building just this year (well, technically their debut was on New Years Eve).
       </p>
       <p>
        Paying homage to their Nevada roots, everything that goes into The Depots whiskey is sourced from neighboring fields and farms to be milled, cooked, fermented, and distilled on-site. Then, out on the floor of their restaurant, you can sample the final product multiple ways in some of the most elaborate and creative cocktails youll find in this part of town  or in any town, really.
       </p>
       <p>
        And because its Reno, theyve brought some of the microbrew scene into the mix. The Depot is the first combined brewery and distillery in the state, which means happy drinking for everyone in your party. Yes, even you, grandma.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Frey Ranch Estate Distillery  Fallon, NV
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_345942" style="width: 950px">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Frey Ranch Estate Distillery" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/frey-ranch-940x529.jpg" title=""/>
        </noscript>
        <img class="size-large wp-image-345942 lazy" data-original="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/frey-ranch-940x529.jpg" height="529" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/assets/images/blank1.png" width="940"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo courtesy of
         <a href="http://travelnevada.com/?t=MatadorArticle5WinterDMA1412&amp;utm_source=Matador&amp;utm_campaign=MatadorArticle5WinterDMA1412&amp;utm_medium=Internet">
          TravelNevada
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_345845" style="width: 950px">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Churchill Vineyards" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/DOW_03791-940x627.jpg" title=""/>
        </noscript>
        <img class="size-large wp-image-345845 lazy" data-original="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/DOW_03791-940x627.jpg" height="627" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/assets/images/blank1.png" width="940"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo:
         <a href="http://www.jeffdow.com/">
          Jeff Dow Photography
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.freyranch.com/">
         Frey Ranch
        </a>
        takes drinking local to a whole other level. Every single ingredient used in their spirits is grown and harvested right there on their own 1,200-acre farm, making it Nevadas only estate distillery (and only the second in the country).
       </p>
       <p>
        The Frey familys been growing grain in Nevada for 150 years, but until they opened their 4,000-square-foot distillery, their farm wasnt producing an actual product to showcase their labor. Now you can sit back and sample some Frey Ranch Vodka with the source in full view right outside the distillery windows.
       </p>
       <p>
        And because Nevadans love helping their neighbors, Frey Ranch provides several other local breweries and distilleries a Nevada-grown grain source. But while you can get your taste of Frey Ranch in bottles distilled elsewhere, theres just something cool about knowing the second you cross the threshold of their property with a bottle of their vodka in hand, thats officially the farthest any ingredient in that bottle has ever traveled.
       </p>
       <h2>
        Verdi Local Distillery  Verdi, NV
       </h2>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_345918" style="width: 862px">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Verdi Local Distillery" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/BmF8BURCQAAxgcf.jpg" title=""/>
        </noscript>
        <img class="size-full wp-image-345918 lazy" data-original="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/BmF8BURCQAAxgcf.jpg" height="1136" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/assets/images/blank1.png" width="852"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo:
         <a href="http://www.verdilocaldistillery.com/">
          Verdi Local Distillery
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_345919" style="width: 950px">
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Verdi Local Distillery" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/bottle4-940x705.jpg" title=""/>
        </noscript>
        <img class="size-large wp-image-345919 lazy" data-original="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/blogs/1/2015/02/bottle4-940x705.jpg" height="705" src="http://cdn1.matadornetwork.com/assets/images/blank1.png" width="940"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Photo:
         <a href="http://www.verdilocaldistillery.com/">
          Verdi Local Distillery
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.verdilocaldistillery.com/">
         Verdi Local Distillery
        </a>
        , at 774 square feet, just might be the smallest distillery in America, if not the world. (Were waiting to hear back from the good people at Guinness World Records.) Its more laboratory than distillery, and taking a tour here is something akin to walking around a friends living room and marveling at their ingenious boozy science projects.
       </p>
       <p>
        Inspired by Fireball whisky, Jeremy and Katey Baumann of Verdi Local set out to create flavored whiskey concoctions  but ones that actually dont taste like burning hot mouthwash. The end result, their Apple Cinnamon Whiskey, hit the mark so hard with its deliciousness that its better known locally as BOJ, or blackout juice. Also in the works are a black licorice whiskey, an experimental hop-filled whiskey, and an orange peel gin.
       </p>
       <p>
        Yes, good things do come in small packages.
       </p>
