           <p>
            Who ever said that dogs and cats could not get along was completely wrong.
           </p>
           <p>
            Although felines and canines dont have a reputation for getting along, these cute pairs prove that interspecies friendship is possible.
           </p>
           <p>
            Put everything you are doing down and take time to enjoy the beautiful friendships of these adorable animals.
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h3>
            A friend will not get into trouble
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Or comfort if the trouble still happened
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats2.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Each one will take an active part
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats3.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            A someone who can take a walk with you
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats4.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Or maybe someone who will always be there to help
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cat5.png" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            And after you get cold or freeze, someone there to get a blanket
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats6.png" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            To share and eat from the same bowl
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats7.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            And there will be always someone to play
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats8.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Even fatigue friendship is not an obstacle
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats9.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            And feel the real love
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats10.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Trusting the most valuable friend
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats11.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            When you feel like the most secure person in the world
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats14.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            One gentle Moew hug and the sadness is gone
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats15.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Muzzle friendly sympathy will not take long when you are sad
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats16.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            That priceless wish to spend the rest of your life with her
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats17.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Movie time while cuddling
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats18.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            The moment when you dont care on which side of the bed you sleep
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats19.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            And the most couzy place on the earth
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="-cats-and-dogs-getting-along" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17724" src="http://www.boreddaddy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/dogs-and-cats20.jpg" width="680"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            Does anyone saw my kitty?. On your head?
