      <article class="entry clr">
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        In the present world, it is possible for any business to reach million numbers of people through digital channels. Also, these digital channels help business evaluate their current and future customer base before embarking on marketing actions. Digital marketing provides a detailed data and analysis of consumer behaviour as well precise results about the effectiveness of marketing methods. Given below are few significant digital marketing strategies that any business owner should implement to help their business grow online.
       </h3>
       <h3>
        <a href="http://www.trootrac.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/power-of-digital-marketing-01.jpg">
         <img alt="power of digital marketing-01" class="aligncenter wp-image-803" height="338" src="http://www.trootrac.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/power-of-digital-marketing-01-300x180.jpg" width="682"/>
        </a>
       </h3>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <strong>
         Setting a Goal:
        </strong>
        Small business might want more customers, more recognition or maybe looking for ways to get ahead of the competition. Starting with a solid goal in mind significantly increase the possibilities of success.
       </h3>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <strong>
         Creating a Marketing Funnel:
        </strong>
        The most successful businesses have an effective marketing funnel that helps to find out a customers expedition to become a lead, and then put certain methodologies in place that will encourage them to move through this funnel. Factors like lead magnets, calls to action, opt-ins and offers are all effective sections of a marketing funnel. A marketing funnel is comprised of four parts that includes: Awareness, Interest, Desire, and Action.
       </h3>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <strong>
         Awareness:
        </strong>
        The potential customer is aware of your product or service.
       </h3>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <strong>
         Interest:
        </strong>
        They are enthusiastically expressing interest in a certain type of your products or services.
       </h3>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <strong>
         Desire:
        </strong>
        They desire to purchase a specific product or service
       </h3>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <strong>
         Action:
        </strong>
        Taking the next step towards purchasing. This is when youre able to turn your potential customer into a lead.
       </h3>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        Having an effective marketing funnel not only just get you more leads, but also helps to turn leads into repeat buyers.
       </h3>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <strong>
         Developing a call-to-action:
        </strong>
        As discussed, a call-to-action is the second step of marketing funnel. It is an image or text that stimulates visitors to take action, such as subscribe to a newsletter, observe a webinar or request a product demo. CTA is beneficial in directing people to landing pages, where its simple to collect visitors contact information in exchange for a remarkable marketing scheme. In this way, an effective CTA results in more number of leads and conversions on the website.
       </h3>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <strong>
         Generating an effective lead magnet:
        </strong>
        The main idea behind a lead magnet is to extract information about the customer. Supply customers with somewhat relevant information about the products or services that they want. Lucrative offers can also be an important way to gather more information about a potential buyer while driving them further into your funnel at the same time. This helps them to become an actual quality lead and will be ready to spend money on your products or services.
       </h3>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <strong>
         Driving traffic to your website:
        </strong>
        In order, to drive people into your marketing funnel, it is essential to have traffic on your website. There a number of ways you can drive traffic to your website.
       </h3>
       <h3>
        <strong>
         Content Marketing:
        </strong>
        Content is the King. Content marketing is not only about creating a lot of content but promoting it through appropriate channels is extremely significant. Creating unique content will not only help you reach out to wider targeted audience, but also urge people to talk about and share your content on social media platforms . This will create a buzz around your content, help you create brand awareness and enhance the presence of brand in a way that will assist in growing business online.
       </h3>
       <h3>
        <strong>
         Social Media Presence:
        </strong>
        In order to have effective internet marketing, it is very important to be active on social media channels like Facebook, Googleplus, Twitter , Pinterest and Tumblr for brand promotion. Social media channels are used to drive traffic on website and generate leads as an indispensable part of marketing. Social Media Marketing has in itself become a wide platform which rationally defines its importance in digital marketing strategy. Every brand is focusing on engaging their audience online through various social media campaigns.
       </h3>
       <h3>
        <strong>
         Mobile Marketing:
        </strong>
        Mobile devices are being used all over the world, therefore it is very important to optimize campaigns for mobile traffic too. The layout of website should be responsive so that it can be easily be accessed from other devices like smart phones, tablets, phablets and ipads.
       </h3>
       <h3>
        <strong>
         Determining Data and Analytics:
        </strong>
        Most distinctive part of your digital marketing strategy is to track all the work done and measure your growth by analysing it. Almost all the digital marketing tools that you use have dashboards that give you a deep dive into the comprehensive analysis of your stats. Its very significant for a digital marketer to preserve these stats in order to examine your performance and understand whats working and whats not.
       </h3>
