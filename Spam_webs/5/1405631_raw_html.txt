         <p>
          <span style="color: #555555;">
           <strong>
            COMPETITION IS CLOSED.
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #555555;">
           The winner of the safari holidaywas randomly chosen by our judges on Wednesday, 20 August 2014. Well done to our winner Pieter Breedt!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          One lucky bush-loving couple stands the chance to win a safari holiday worth R20 000, courtesy of the Shamwari Group. So, who wouldnt want to nip away for a weekend in the bush, for quality time together? Starlit skies, game drives and the Big Five  with magical experiences like these, we rate not a soul would miss out on such an opportunity. Win a two-night escape for you and your partner at the Shamwari Groups Sanbona Wildlife Reserve near Barrydale, or at its Jock Safari Lodge in the Kruger National Park  you choose!
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           The Shamwari Group
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          A pioneering leader inwildlife safaris, conservation andhospitalityfor over 20 years, the
          <a href="http://www.shamwarigroup.com/" target="_blank">
           Shamwari Group
          </a>
          aims to give their guests a superior wilderness experience. Their luxury offering includes 18 lodges,well appointed around Africa, that are designed to complement breath-taking surroundings. Two of these are the Jock Safari Lodge in the Kruger National Parkand the Sanbona Wildlife Reserve near Barrydale.
          <a href="http://www.realestatemagazine.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/RE-July2014-Competitons-Safari-Romance-Shanmwari-Logo-592x394.jpg" rel="prettyphoto[3602]">
           <img alt="shamwari-group-logo-safari-holiday-south-africa-luxury-getaways" class="aligncenter wp-image-3628" height="283" src="http://www.realestatemagazine.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/RE-July2014-Competitons-Safari-Romance-Shanmwari-Logo-592x394.jpg" width="425"/>
          </a>
          Set in the Little Karoo, Sanbona Wildlife Reserve is a magnificently scenic three-hour drive from Cape Town on Route 62  the longest wine route in the world. Sanbona pays homage to the San people with its 8 000 hectare reserve being home to seven San rock-art sites.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3643" style="width: 602px">
          <a href="http://www.realestatemagazine.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/RE-July2014-Competitons-SafariRomance-Sanbona2-592x394.jpg" rel="prettyphoto[3602]">
           <img alt="sanbona-wildlife-reserve-safai-holiday-competition-little-karoo-south-africa-luxury-tents-getaways" class="wp-image-3643 size-full" height="394" src="http://www.realestatemagazine.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/RE-July2014-Competitons-SafariRomance-Sanbona2-592x394.jpg" width="592"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Sanbonas Dwyka Tented Lodge is surrounded by awe-inspiring rock formations which was once the home of ancient Khoisan hunters.
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Inspired by the life of author Sir Percy Fitzpatrick, Jock Safari Lodge was built in the area close to where the famed story of Sir Percy Fitzpatrick and his dog Jock unfolded. A six-hour drive from Johannesburg and two hours from Nelspruit, the lodge enjoys 6 000 hectares of exclusive traversing rights in an area of Kruger National Park perfect for Big Five game viewing.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3642" style="width: 602px">
          <a href="http://www.realestatemagazine.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/RE-July2014-Competitons-SafariRomance-Jock2-592x394.jpg" rel="prettyphoto[3602]">
           <img alt="jock-safari-lodge-game-drive-kruger-national-park-getaways-holidays-south-africa" class="wp-image-3642 size-full" height="394" src="http://www.realestatemagazine.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/RE-July2014-Competitons-SafariRomance-Jock2-592x394.jpg" width="592"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Game drives at Jock Safari Lodge can last up to three hours each and are conducted twice a day in open vehicles.
          </p>
         </div>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           <br/>
           Safari Holiday Up for Grabs
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Looking to get in touch with your wild side? You could win a two-night holiday for you and your partner worth more than R20 000 at one of two Shamwari Group destinations  Sanbona Wildlife Reserve or Jock Safari Lodge. This includes accommodation for one couple sharing, one spa treatment each, and all meals and game drives.
         </p>
         <h4>
          <strong>
           How to Enter
          </strong>
         </h4>
         <p>
          Entering our Safari Holiday competition is easy. Simply
          <a href="http://realestatemagazine.us7.list-manage.com/subscribe?u=823591be16da134d4ad02dd86&amp;id=cfa3293c81" target="_blank">
           subscribe to the Real Estate Magazine newsletter
          </a>
          . For an extra entry, Like our
          <a href="https://www.facebook.com/realestatemag" target="_blank">
           Facebook page
          </a>
          . Your chance to enjoy crackling fires under starry night skies is only a few clicks away
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Closing date:
          </strong>
          15 August 2014
         </p>
         <p>
          For more amazing prizes, visit
          <a href="http://www.realestatemagazine.co.za/blog/category/competitions/" target="_blank">
           REAL ESTATE Competitions
          </a>
          .
         </p>

          </li>
