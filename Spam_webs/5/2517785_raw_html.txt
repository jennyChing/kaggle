        <h2>
         <a href="http://www.runkarlarun.com/2013/12/11/best-gifts-for-runners-2013/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Best Gifts For Runners 2013: Karlas Favorite Things">
          Best Gifts For Runners 2013: Karlas Favorite Things
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="posties clearfix">
         <div class="one">
          <div class="entry">
           <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3008" style="width: 540px">
            <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://i2.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/American_Homestead_Winter_Currier_and_Ives.png" rel="external nofollow" title="">
             <img alt="best gifts for runners" class="size-full wp-image-3008 " data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/American_Homestead_Winter_Currier_and_Ives.png?resize=530%2C353"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             A winter scene by Currier &amp; Ives.
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            It was snowing just yesterday, my Christmas tree is lighting up my living room and Christmas is just two weeks away. Wondering what to get for the runner on your list? These are just a few of my favorite things: the best gifts for runners this year.
           </p>
           <p>
            I get lots of samples from sports companies and buy plenty of gear myself. The products on this list represent my favorite clothes, gadgets and gear currently available from a range of brands. In clothing, Ive tried the womens versions, but many come in mens options too. Ive indicated which items are available for men or in unisex sizinga full 30 of them are. And Ive included the list prices, though youll find many items for less than that.
           </p>
           <p>
            Also, if its been in a past gift guide of mine, I didnt include it here. But many of the products I highlighted in the days of yore are still available. So here are my guides from
            <a href="http://www.runkarlarun.com/2012/12/12/2012-holiday-gift-guide/" target="_blank" title="2012 Run, Karla, Run! Holiday Gift Guide">
             2012
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.runkarlarun.com/2011/12/13/holiday-gift-guide-gifts-for-runners/" target="_blank" title="2011 Holiday Gift Guide: Gifts For Runners">
             2011
            </a>
            , and
            <a href="http://www.runkarlarun.com/2009/12/18/holiday-gift-guide-for-runners/" target="_blank" title="2009 Holiday Gift Guide For Runners">
             2009
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            Without further ado, its time to get shopping and wish-listing. Here are my favorite running jackets, layers, tights, shorts, tanks, socks, sleeves, sports bras, gadgets, books and more this year. There are nearly 50 items in all.
           </p>
           <h2>
            Best Gifts For Runners 2013
           </h2>
           <h3>
            Running Jackets
           </h3>
           <p>
            Three very different options for different needs: visibility, wind and all weather.
            <span id="more-2971">
            </span>
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_3009" style="width: 260px">
            <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://i1.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/New-Balance-Beacon.jpg" rel="external nofollow" title="">
             <img alt="gifts for runners" class="size-medium wp-image-3009 " data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/New-Balance-Beacon.jpg?resize=250%2C183"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             New Balance Beacon Jacket
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <strong>
             New Balance High Visibility Beacon Jacket, $175
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Youre visible three different ways in this ultra-modern running layer with 360-degree reflectivity. But get this: it even glows in the dark. Just 10 minutes of direct light charges the jackets yellow accents. Front pockets with welded seams give the jacket a streamlined finish and flawless fit. My favorite thing about the jacket, though, is that the poly-spandex fabric stretches with youno restriction whatsoever. And its really eye-popping too. Check out a photo of Olympian
            <a href="http://www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/1107ZX_0868MS.jpg" target="_blank">
             Jenny Simpson in the jacket
            </a>
            at the 2013 Wine &amp; Dine Half Marathon MeetUp. Available in a mens cut too.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_2975" style="width: 259px">
            <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://i2.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Asics-Storm-Shelter-Jacket.jpg" rel="external nofollow" title="">
             <img alt="Best gifts for runners" class="size-medium wp-image-2975 " data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Asics-Storm-Shelter-Jacket.jpg?resize=249%2C178"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Asics Storm Shelter Jacket
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Asics Storm Shelter Jacket, $130
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            The elements dont stand a chance against this waterproof jacket with a removable, adjustable hood. Thumbholes in Lycra and Velcro cuffs keep sleeves in place and water out. Fully lined in mesh, the jacket also has zippered vents for breathability, 360-degree reflectivity and an adjustable hem. Three pockets keep your belongings dry, including a port for your MP3 player. All that and the fit is flattering too. Ive been wearing this jacket non-stop on cold running days and just to run errands too. See it in action in an episode of
            <a href="http://www.runkarlarun.com/2013/11/02/nyc-marathon-dash-to-the-finish-line-5k/" target="_blank" title="On The Run at NYC Marathon Dash to the Finish Line 5K">
             On The Run
            </a>
            at the 2013 ING New York City Marathon. Available in a mens cut too.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_2980" style="width: 257px">
            <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://i0.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Zoot-Jacket.png" rel="external nofollow" title="">
             <img alt="best gifts for runners" class="size-medium wp-image-2980 " data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Zoot-Jacket-e1386704527234-350x386.png?resize=247%2C272"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Zoot Ultra Flexwind Jacket
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Zoot Ultra Run Flexwind Jacket, $100
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            Designed to keep runners warm
            <i>
             and
            </i>
            dry, this ultra lightweight windbreaker is shockingly warm. It features MEGAHEAT thermo technology that uses your bodys own perspiration, along with ultraviolet rays, to increase body temps by up to 7 degrees. The vented back lets moisture out and 360-degree reflectivity makes you visible at night. The side zippered pockets have an MP3 port, and a back pocket offers more room to stash your stuff. After one wear, I gave away my old windbreaker and replaced it with this beauty. Available in a mens cut too.
           </p>
           <h3>
            Running Layers
           </h3>
           <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_2981" style="width: 261px">
            <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://i0.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Nike-Aeroloft-Running-Vest.jpg" rel="external nofollow" title="">
             <img alt="best gifts for runners" class="size-medium wp-image-2981 " data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Nike-Aeroloft-Running-Vest.jpg?resize=251%2C186"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Nike Aeroloft Running Vest
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Nike Aeroloft 800 Running Vest, $180
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            This ultra lightweight 800-fill goose down vest packs heavy-duty warmth for cold-weather runs. The down fill is subtle, making the vest sleek and stylish. Best of all, the back pocket turns into a pouch, complete with hand strap, that the vest packs into. I ran a
            <a href="http://www.runkarlarun.com/2013/12/02/race-report-turkey-trot-5k/" target="_blank" title="Race Report: Trot Off Your Turkey 5K Turkey Trot">
             5K personal best
            </a>
            in this vest in 28 degrees. It delivered warmth and breathability as promised. Check it out at the
            <a href="http://www.runkarlarun.com/2013/09/27/nike-just-do-it-nyc-house-innovation/" target="_blank" title="Nike Just Do It NYC House of Innovation Is Running Art">
             Nike House of Innovation
            </a>
            . Available in a mens cut too.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_2983" style="width: 260px">
            <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://i1.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Asics-Thermopolis-LT-Full-Zip.jpg" rel="external nofollow" title="">
             <img alt="best gifts for runners" class="size-medium wp-image-2983" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Asics-Thermopolis-LT-Full-Zip.jpg?resize=250%2C250"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Asics Thermopolis LT Full Zip
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Asics Thermopolis LT Full Zip, $85
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            I love this zip for a few reasons. One major reason? Thumbholes on the sleeves that are actually long enough for me to use. I have long, monkey arms and sleeve length is an issue for me. But this one fits great. The fabric is a super soft, brushed jersey that feels like a warm hug. It makes you want to zip it up and cozy up in front of the fireplace. But its meant for running. The front, outside pockets have a media port, inside pockets give you extra storage, and reflective strips have you covered 360 degrees. Its the perfect extra-layer. See it in action in an episode of
            <a href="http://www.runkarlarun.com/2013/10/31/run-ing-new-york-city-marathon-ep-1/" target="_blank" title="On The Run at the ING New York City Marathon Ep. 1">
             On The Run
            </a>
            at the 2013 ING New York City Marathon and
            <a href="http://www.runkarlarun.com/2013/11/02/nyc-marathon-dash-to-the-finish-line-5k/" target="_blank" title="On The Run at NYC Marathon Dash to the Finish Line 5K">
             On The Run
            </a>
            at the 2013 Dash to the Finish Line 5K. Available in a mens cut too.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_2982" style="width: 260px">
            <a class="ext-link" data-wpel-target="_blank" href="http://i2.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/New-Balance-Impact-Hoodie.jpg" rel="external nofollow" title="">
             <img alt="best gifts for runners" class="size-medium wp-image-2982" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.runkarlarun.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/New-Balance-Impact-Hoodie.jpg?resize=250%2C183"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             New Balance Impact Hoodie
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <strong>
             New Balance Impact Hoodie, $65
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            This is my fave long-sleeve pullover. Somehow, it does the unthinkablekeeps me warm in winter when I need an extra layer and cool enough in summer when I want to cover my skin. Thumbholes hook the sleeves in place and a mesh panel in back makes it breathable. This 100 percent polyester zip is reflective and has a large pocket in the back for a wallet, keys or whatever else you carry when you run. And the fabric is soft as silk. See it in action in an episode of
            <a href="http://www.runkarlarun.com/2013/05/20/video-on-the-run-brooklyn-half-race-report/" target="_blank" title="VIDEO: On The Run at Brooklyn Half Race Report">
             On The Run
            </a>
            at the 2013 Brooklyn Half. I wore it on a running tour of Brooklyn and in a few other segments too.
           </p>

              2009
             </a>
             with an array of products from water bottles and headbands to more books, gadgets and gifts that runners will love.
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             NOTE: Many of the items I receive or buy never make the pages of Run, Karla, Run! I simply dont write about products that I dont love. I have yet to get my hands on one of these, but I included them because Im curiouser and curiouser about them, to borrow a phrase from

              Alice in Wonderland
             </a>
             .
            </em>
           </p>

             Of my Best Gifts For Runners, I purchased the following products myself: Nike Legendary Tight Capris, Lululemon Cool Racerback, Brooks EZ Racerback, Brooks Pulse Lite Glove II, Asics Arm Warmers, Sparkle Athletic Sparkle Sleeves, C9 by Champion Womens Seamless Cami Bra, Fitletic Running Belt, Clif Shot Energy Gels, Clif Shot Blocks, Luna Bar and Luna Protein.
