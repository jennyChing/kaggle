               The Basics Of Food
              </a>
             </h3>
             <div style="clear: both">
             </div>
             <div class="post-header">
              <div class="post-header-line-1">
              </div>

             <div class="post-body entry-content">
              <p>
              </p>
              <div id="summary656710735093155600">
               <br/>
               Food is any substance that provides nutricional value to the body. Normally food is either of plant or animal origin.
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <b>
                Plants
               </b>
               : around 2,000 plant species are cultivated for food. Seed are a good source of food for animals and humans because they contain the nutrients for a plant to grow making them full of healthy fats. Fruits make a significant part of the diets of human beings, they are full of healthy components including fiber, plus they are really tasty! Vegetables are the second type of plant matter that is most commonly eaten as food, they normally consist of low fat and calorie contents and are normally full of potassium and vitamins which are good for you body.
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <img height="170" src="http://preachersinstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Vegetables.jpg" width="320"/>
               <br/>
               <b>
                <br/>
               </b>
               <b>
                Animals
               </b>
               : Food products produced by animals include milk, eggs, honey and more. They also include different types of meat which come from muscle systems or forms of organs. Meat is normally full of proteins and eaten by the majority of humans because of it's unique taste. Even though meat can be so delicious, their are people that opt for not consuming meat or animal products in general by their culture, dietary, ethical or ideological reasons.
               <br/>
               <img height="213" src="http://agriculturewire.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/meat1.jpg" width="320"/>
               <br/>
               <br/>
               <b>
                Cuisine
               </b>
               : Basically the act of mixing and matching different foods to get many distinct flavors. Cooking with various spices, animal products and plant products are the real art of cuisine. The cuisine of a specific country varies a lot in the type of culture in the country. Some cultures prefer the use of certain spices, vegetables, fruits, meats or others.
