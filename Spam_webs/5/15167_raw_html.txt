           <h5 class="heading p-col">
            Ingredients
           </h5>
           <ul class="content ingredients-list">
            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef

            <li itemprop="ingredients">

             <a href="/od/seasoningrecipes/r/bl30420j.htm">
              chili powder

            <li itemprop="ingredients">
             1 teaspoon
             <a data-inlink="RuO81vqH79jYBr89ULjltQ==" href="http://frugalliving.about.com/od/makeyourowningredients/r/Garlic_Powder.htm">
              garlic powder

            <li itemprop="ingredients">

            </li>

            <li itemprop="ingredients">

             1/2 cup chopped onion

          <div class="preparation expert-content-text" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
           <h5 class="heading-style-a p-col">
            Preparation
           </h5>
           <p>
            Heat the oven to 350 F. Lightly grease a 2- to 2 1/2-quart baking dish.
           </p>
           <p>
            In a large skillet,
            <a data-component="link" data-ordinal="1" data-source="inlineLink" data-type="internalLink" href="/od/howtocook/fl/How-to-Brown-Ground-Beef.htm">
             brown the ground beef
            </a>
            . Stir in the chili powder, garlic powder, oregano, and Tabasco. Add the taco sauce and tomato sauce. Taste and add salt, as needed.Simmer the mixture for10 minutes.
           </p>
           <p>
            Put half of the ground beef mixture in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Top with about half of the crumbled corn chips or taco chips.
           </p>
           <div adsense-displaylabel="true" adsense-numlinks="1" class="adsense-slot adsense ads-half" id="adsense1">
           </div>
           <p class="cb-split">
            Add remaining ground beef mixture, a layer of chopped tomato, onion, chopped lettuce, and the shredded cheese. Sprinkle the remaining corn chips over the top.
           </p>
           <p>
            Bake at 350 for 12 to 15 minutes, or until the casserole is hot and cheese is melted.
           </p>
