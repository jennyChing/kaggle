      <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
       <span class="name">
        Deadly Magnitude 6.1 Earthquake Strikes Yunnan
       </span>
      </h1>
      <div class="entry">
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2749" style="width: 628px">
        <a href="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/earthquake-china-buildings-collapsed-yunna-photos-travel-village-luggageo-nline-depressing-scenery.jpg">
         <img alt="longtoushan,china earthquake,china earthquake 2014" class="size-large wp-image-2749" height="408" src="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/earthquake-china-buildings-collapsed-yunna-photos-travel-village-luggageo-nline-depressing-scenery-1024x677.jpg" width="618"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         This photo shows debris from ruined buildings. The photo was taken in the Longtoushan town. This town is in Yunnan China.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Yesterday, China was ravaged by a powerful 6.1 earthquake. There are at least 398 confined dead people. There were also 1800 people injured .These are just the numbers provided. The body count is expected to rise in the next days.
       </p>
       <p>
        China sent 2,500 soldiers to help in the rescue mission. On top of everything, the heavy rains have flooded the nearby roads. The rescue workers have been forced to travel on foot. Some had to walk 3 miles to reach a badly ruined village.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2752" style="width: 628px">
        <a href="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/china-soldier-rescue-operation-walking-baby-luggage-online-depressing-earthquake-yunnan-photos.jpg">
         <img alt="earthquake photos,earthquake chine,earthquake 2014" class="size-large wp-image-2752" height="414" src="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/china-soldier-rescue-operation-walking-baby-luggage-online-depressing-earthquake-yunnan-photos-1024x687.jpg" width="618"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         This photo shows a Chinese soldier helping to carry the injured baby of a woman.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        This was the strongest earthquake that China has experience in the last 14 years. The place looks badly damaged. It is going to take years to fix all the damage.
       </p>
       <p>
        Because of the rain, there is a good chance that there could be a landslide. Should this occur the survivors could be buried alive. These soldiers along with 300 police officers and firefighters have been working nonstop to rescue the victims.
       </p>
       <p>
        The local hospitals are overwhelmed with all the new patients they keep receiving. So far, the firefighters managed to rescue 32 people. They were rescued from collapse buildings in Longtoushan. This was the epicenter of the earthquake.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_2753" style="width: 628px">
        <a href="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/china-earthquake-victims-photos-travel-news-luggage-online-sad-depressing-2014-news-yunnan.jpg">
         <img alt="earthquake in china,china earthquake,earthquakes" class="size-large wp-image-2753" height="430" src="http://www.luggageonline.com/lolnews/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/china-earthquake-victims-photos-travel-news-luggage-online-sad-depressing-2014-news-yunnan-1024x714.jpg" width="618"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         This photo shows soldiers helping to carry an injured man. His family is running behind the soldiers. With the roads ruined, they have to carry the victims to the hospital.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        The survivors had been forced to brace themselves against the elements. They have neither food nor clothing. Those whose house did not tumble over do not have water or electricity. The US promised to send aid to China.
       </p>
       <p>
        This region of China is prone to earthquakes. Most earthquakes are not too strong. The worst earthquake this region experience was in 1970. The Earthquake 7.7 in Yunnan killed 15,000 people.
       </p>
