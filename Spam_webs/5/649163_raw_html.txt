    <h1>
     7 Flight Attendant Uniforms That Make Fashion Statements
    </h1>
    <div class="ssw-content-dek">
     <p>
      Virgin American, Korean Air, Qantasthese airlines' bold sartorial statements reflect the fashion of our time and beyond. Cliff Muskiet, of the popular blog Uniform Freak, tells us what he really thinks about couture at 30,000 feet.
     </p>
    </div>
    <section>
     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.cntraveler.com/daily-traveler/2012/07/virgin-america-banana-republic-new-flight-attendant-uniforms">
       Virgin America
      </a>
     </h2>
     <div class="ssw-content-slide-content">
      <p>
       Banana Republic outdid itself with its recent
       <a href="http://www.cntraveler.com/daily-traveler/2012/07/virgin-america-banana-republic-new-flight-attendant-uniforms">
        uniform redesign for Virgin America
       </a>
       : simple separates that you can wear on the street or in the galley. But
       <a href="http://www.uniformfreak.com">
        Cliff Muskiet, of the popular blog Uniform Freak,
       </a>
       disagrees. "The men's uniform is too casual," he says. "I do like the look for ladies, but the skirts are way too short and look unprofessional. I am not sure if this uniform will look good on a real flight attendant, knowing that a lot of them in the USA don't look as slim and as fresh as the models in this picture."
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="image-wrapper ">
      <img alt="" class="" src="http://photos.cntraveler.com/2014/08/06/53e2d906dddaa35c30f5fe01_virgin-america-uniform-gate-two-people.jpg" title=""/>
     </div>
    </section>
    <section>
     <h2>
      Korean Airlines
     </h2>
     <div class="ssw-content-slide-content">
      <p>
       This
       <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hEvR_DSW4MA">
        molto-feminine collection
       </a>
       , which debuted in 2005, modernized hues from traditional Korean ceramics. Cliff says: "It looks so great, so stylish and special. I love the color combination, and I like the way all flight attendants wear the scarf around their neck. Love the hair accessories too. I would do anything to obtain this uniform."
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="image-wrapper ">
      <img alt="" class="" src="http://photos.cntraveler.com/2014/08/06/53e2d908dddaa35c30f5fe13_KOREAN-AIRLINES-meals.jpg" title=""/>
     </div>
    </section>
    <section>
     <h2>
     </h2>
     <div class="ssw-content-slide-content">
      <p>
       Champagne on Air France
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="image-wrapper ">
      <img alt="" class="" src="http://photos.cntraveler.com/2014/07/31/53d9c484dcd5888e1459b345_coach-amenities-air-france-champagne.jpg" title=""/>
     </div>
    </section>
    <section>
     <h2>
      Singapore Airlines
     </h2>
     <div class="ssw-content-slide-content">
      <p>
       The longevity of Singapore Airlines' signature kebaya uniform should attest to its timelessness. French clothier Pierre Balmain's design has been continuously in service since 1968.
      </p>
      <p>
       Cliff says: "When seeing this uniform you know it is Singapore Airlines right away. But I actually would like for the airline to come with a new look. Time for a change!"
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="image-wrapper portrait">
      <img alt="" class="" src="http://photos.cntraveler.com/2014/08/06/53e2d90bc2d3f39d361050be_Singapore_Airlines_Uniform_Muskiet.jpg" title=""/>
     </div>
    </section>
    <section>
     <h2>
      Bangkok Airways
     </h2>
     <div class="ssw-content-slide-content">
      <p>
       Thai fashion label Asava created relaxed, slinky silhouettes in aerial colors for Bangkok Airways in 2011. Cliff says: "Wow, I love the different colors of blue they have used, and I like the variety. It looks fresh, modern, and feminine at the same time. I thank Bangkok Airways for keeping their uniforms so colorful. I think the blouses are great, and I love the little bow tie at the collar."
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="image-wrapper ">
      <img alt="" class="" src="http://photos.cntraveler.com/2014/08/06/53e2d90ec2d3f39d361050d0_bangkok-airways-uniform.jpg" title=""/>
     </div>
    </section>
    <section>
     <h2>
      Qantas
     </h2>
     <div class="ssw-content-slide-content">
      <p>
       Not all designers have fared well in the sky, however. I can only guess Yves Saint Laurent was intoxicated by excessive Aqua Net fumes when he created this eye-searing Qantas dress in the '80s (pictured). Cliff sees redemption in the newer uniforms: "Love the print of
       <a href="http://lifestyle.ezinemark.com/qantas-uniforms-throughout-history-77367d9769f4.html">
        the current dress
       </a>
       , but I think the material it is made of is a bit too much. In the tropics it way be too warm and uncomfortable." But we're both eager to see what comes next: Designer
       <a href="http://www.sassybella.com/2012/05/martin-grant-to-redesign-qantas-uniforms/">
        Martin Grant
       </a>
       has been tapped to dream up the next incarnation, which should be hitting runways and cabin aisles by 2014.
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="image-wrapper portrait">
      <img alt="" class="" src="http://photos.cntraveler.com/2014/08/06/53e2d912dddaa35c30f5fe25_qantas_uniform_ysl.jpg" title=""/>
     </div>
    </section>
    <section>
     <h2>
      Braniff International Airways
     </h2>
     <div class="ssw-content-slide-content">
      <p>
       Emilio Pucci set some sort of standard with his space bubble in 1965, and no airline uniform designer has been as daring since. We should bring it back for Virgin Galaxy's inaugural flight next year.
      </p>
      <p>
       Cliff says: "The Pucci uniforms are collectors items now. Love the color combinations and the different items the uniforms consisted of. I think today's flight attendants would
       <em>
        not
       </em>
       want to wear such uniforms though."
      </p>
     </div>
     <div class="image-wrapper portrait">
      <img alt="" class="" src="http://photos.cntraveler.com/2014/08/06/53e2d916c2d3f39d361050e2_Braniff_Airlines_Uniform_Muskiet.jpg" title=""/>
     </div>
    </section>
