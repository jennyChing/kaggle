       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-07-19T21:27:09+00:00">
         July 19, 2014
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://wonkywonderful.com/author/nicole-harris/" rel="author" title="Nicole Harris">
           Nicole Harris
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>

          6 Comments
         </a>
        </span>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="16287961" data-link="http://wonkywonderful.com/brown-butter-chocolate-chip-cookies/" data-summary="" data-title="Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies">
        </div>
        <h2>
         If you enjoy soft batch cookies, give these Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies a try! Pure cookie bliss in one little bite!
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/extra-soft-brown-butter-chocolate-chip-cookies-11.jpg">
          <img alt="Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies : Soft Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5373" height="961" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/extra-soft-brown-butter-chocolate-chip-cookies-11.jpg" width="610"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I love a good chocolate chip cookie. Who doesnt!?!?
        </p>
        <p>
         While I love the traditional cookie recipes, Ive been trying out different versions and flavors. Nothing too crazy. Just a hint of something special.
        </p>
        <p>
         These Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies are just that. A traditional soft chocolate chip cookie with a unique hint of flavor, thanks to the brown butter.
        </p>
        <p>
         Have you tried brown butter yet? It gives dishes a more complex, nutty flavor.
        </p>
        <p>
         It does require a bit more effort. And I will not say that it is as easy as cooking butter on the stove top. Brown butter goes from delicious golden brown to burned very quickly. So be aware . . . and dont you dare walk away from that pan while preparing brown butter!
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out this awesome
         <a href="http://www.buzzfeed.com/christinebyrne/how-to-make-perfect-brown-butter?sub=2594894_1614988" target="_blank">
          How To Brown Butter Tutorial
         </a>
         before you get started! It has step by step photos and detailed instructions.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/extra-soft-brown-butter-chocolate-chip-cookies-22.jpg">
          <img alt="Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies : Soft Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5374" height="731" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/extra-soft-brown-butter-chocolate-chip-cookies-22.jpg" width="610"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Once you prepare the brown butter and let it cool to a spreadable consistency, you are ready to get those chocolate chip cookies started!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/extra-soft-brown-butter-chocolate-chip-cookies-33.jpg">
          <img alt="Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies : Soft Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5375" height="610" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/extra-soft-brown-butter-chocolate-chip-cookies-33.jpg" width="610"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Is there anything better than Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies dipped in milk!?!?
        </p>
        <p>
         Nope.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/extra-soft-brown-butter-chocolate-chip-cookies-44.jpg">
          <img alt="Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies : Soft Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5376" height="610" src="http://cf.wonkywonderful.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/extra-soft-brown-butter-chocolate-chip-cookies-44.jpg" width="610"/>
         </a>
        </p>

         <div class="blog-yumprint-recipe-title" itemprop="name">
          Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies
         </div>
         <div class="blog-yumprint-recipe-published" itemprop="datePublished">
          2014-07-19 17:51:49
         </div>
         <div class="blog-yumprint-header">
          <div class="blog-yumprint-save blog-yumprint-action">
           <a href="http://yumprint.com/app/object/ihFX">
            Save Recipe
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-print blog-yumprint-action">
           Print
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="blog-yumprint-spacer">
         </div>
         <div class="blog-yumprint-recipe-contents">
          <div class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-section" yumprintsection="0">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Ingredients
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-ingredients">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="0">
             1/4 Cup Brown Butter - cooled
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="1">
             1/4 Cup Salted Butter - room temperature
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="2">
             1/2 Cup Dark Brown Sugar
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="3">
             1/4 Cup Ultrafine White Sugar
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="4">
             1 Large Egg
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="5">
             1 teaspoon Pure Vanilla Extract
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="6">
             1 Cups All Purpose Flour
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="7">
             1 teaspoon Cornstarch
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="8">
             1/2 teaspoon Baking Soda
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="9">
             1/4 teaspoon Sea Salt
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-ingredient-item" itemprop="ingredients" yumprintitem="10">
             3/4 Cups Milk or Dark Chocolate Chips
            </li>
           </ol>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-method-section" yumprintsection="1">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Brown Butter
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-methods" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="0">
             Cut 1 stick (1/2 cup) salted butter into cubes. Heat in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir continuously the entire time. Remove from heat once the butter has reached a golden brown color. (takes about 4-5 minutes). Cool in the refrigerator until brown butter reaches a spreadable consistency. Measure out a 1/4 cup for the cookie recipe.
            </li>
           </ol>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-method-section" yumprintsection="2">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Cookies
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-methods" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="0">
             Preheat oven to 350F
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="1">
             Position oven rack to the center.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="2">
             Combine dry ingredients: flour, cornstarch, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="3">
             In separate bowl; cream together brown butter, salted butter, brown sugar and white sugar. Mix on medium for 3 minutes, scraping sides of bowl as necessary.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="4">
             Add egg and vanilla. Mix until incorporated.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="5">
             Gradually add the dry ingredients while mixing. Cookie dough will be thick.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="6">
             Mix in the chocolate chips.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="7">
             Refrigerate dough for 1 hour to firm up. (optional)
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="8">
             Drop dough in rounded tablespoons on baking sheet.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="9">
             Bake 9-11 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="blog-yumprint-method-item" yumprintitem="10">
             Transfer to cooling rack.
            </li>
           </ol>
          </div>
          <div class="blog-yumprint-note-section" yumprintsection="3">
           <div class="blog-yumprint-subheader">
            Notes
           </div>
           <ol class="blog-yumprint-notes">
            <li class="blog-yumprint-note-item" yumprintitem="0">
             Makes approximately 34 mini cookies.
            </li>
           </ol>
