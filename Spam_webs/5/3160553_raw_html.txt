        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Italian food  Pumpkin Risotto
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="author vcard">
          <i class="icon-author icon-metas" title="Author ">
          </i>
          <a class="url fn n" href="http://italiantranslation-teaching.com/author/ITT/" title="View all posts by ITT">
           ITT
          </a>
          <span class="bl_sep">
           
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="onDate date updated">
          <i class="icon-time icon-metas" title="Date">
          </i>
          <a href="http://italiantranslation-teaching.com/italian-food/italian-food-pumpkin-risotto/" rel="bookmark">
           December 19, 2014
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="bl_categ">
          <i class="icon-folder-open icon-metas" title="Categories">

        <div class="entry-content">
         <table class="rw-rating-table rw-ltr rw-left rw-no-labels">
          <tr>
           <td>
            <nobr>
            </nobr>
           </td>
           <td>
            <div class="rw-left">
             <div class="rw-ui-container rw-class-blog-post rw-urid-10800" data-img="http://italiantranslation-teaching.com/scarc1p/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/pumpkin-moliugas.png">
             </div>
            </div>
           </td>
          </tr>
         </table>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           One of the reasons tourists love Italy so much is food. Today I want to share with you my familys recipe for
           <strong>
            pumpkin risotto
           </strong>
           , taken from my new book
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/SOS-Italian-Cooking-Chiara-Brambilla-ebook/dp/B00QSJL5CY/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1418989985&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=sos+italian+cooking&amp;pebp=1418989996684" target="_blank">
            Sos Italian cooking
           </a>
           .
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://italiantranslation-teaching.com/scarc1p/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/pumpkin-moliugas.png">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-1082" height="177" src="http://italiantranslation-teaching.com/scarc1p/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/pumpkin-moliugas.png" width="183"/>
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <strong>
            Ingredients
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           300 g (10.58 oz) Arborio rice
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           300 g (10.58 oz) fresh pumpkin
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           3 slices of onion
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           50 g (1.76 oz) unsalted butter
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           8 ladles vegetable stock  1.2 L (5.07 cups), more or less
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           A handful of Parmesan
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Table salt
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           125 mL(0.52 cup) dry white wine
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <strong>
            For the stock
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           1 onion
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           1 stalk of celery
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           1 carrot
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           1 zucchini
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           2.5 L (10.56 cups) water
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <strong>
            What you need
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Pot 22 x 12 cm (8.66 x 4.72 inches)
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Skimmer
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Saucepot 22 x 9 cm (8.66 x 3.54 inches)
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Food processor
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <strong>
            Preparation
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <strong>
            Stock
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <ol>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Wash the zucchini and the celery. Peel the carrot and onion.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            In a pot, add the water and place in whole the zucchini, carrot, celery, and onion. Boil for an hour. Add more water if needed.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Remove vegetables with a skimmer. The stock is now ready to use.
           </span>
          </li>
         </ol>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           <strong>
            Risotto
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <ol>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Peel the pumpkin and dice.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Heat the stock.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            In a saucepot, melt the butter on a low flame. Add the onion and fry gently for one minute.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Add the pumpkin and cook until soft. Add 2 or 3 tbsp of water if needed to prevent the pumpkin from sticking.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Turn off the heat and pure the pumpkin in a blender or food processor until smooth.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Heat the pure in the same saucepot.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Add the rice and stir.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Cook for 3 minutes while stirring.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Add the wine and cook until completely absorbed.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Add two ladles of stock and cook on a medium flame. Continue to cook, adding one or more ladles of stock as soon as the rice absorbs the liquid. Continue stirring.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Cook the risotto according to packet instructions  usually 20 minutes.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Add salt to taste.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            When the risotto is ready, turn off the heat. Sprinkle with Parmesan.
           </span>
          </li>
          <li style="text-align: justify;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            Wait 3 minutes and serve.
           </span>
          </li>
         </ol>
