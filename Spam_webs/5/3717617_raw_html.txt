    <p>
     You can learn how to install free standing shower stall from this article. We provide you the easiest installation instruction that you can follow only in a few minutes. Using only basic tools like shower kit, caulk, caulking gun, Phillips screwdriver, manufacturer installation instructions, paper and level, you can follow this easy instruction.
    </p>
    <p>
     First, the water needs to be shut off to the entire of your house. This is to avoid any possible leakage during the installation process. Then you need to carefully read the instructions to the shower stall kit that you have purchased. You surely dont want to fail in the installation process, dont you think? Read it carefully until you think you have understood every single thing which is important. Reading process is also for ensuring you know what essential things entailing the installation process. You also need to familiarize with each item included in the kit.
    </p>
    <a class="lm" href="http://meredithexpat.com/installing-free-standing-shower-stall/home-shower-stall-kits-mobile/" title="Home Shower Stall Kits Mobile">
     <img alt="Home Shower Stall Kits Mobile Bathroom Installing Free Standing Shower Stall" height="973" src="http://meredithexpat.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Home-Shower-Stall-Kits-Mobile-731x973.gif" width="731"/>

    </a>

    </a>
    <p>
     Second, you should be sure the drain hole is visible in the floor. Your floor area should be level too. After that, the floor can be installed first. This is to ensure the hole in floor piece is lined up with the drain hole. You need to check it all before the installation. Remember that you cannot put any pressure on the floor too until you think the adhesive material used is dry. Using paper or cardboard, you can line the floor to protect the wall during installation.
    </p>
