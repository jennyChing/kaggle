             <em class="title">
              i-FI Lens Coatings
             </em>
             I-Fi lens coatings consist of four layers of coatings to filter transmitted light and protect the lens: an anti-reflective portion cuts glare and distracting extraneous light, hydrophobic component keeps water spots to a minimum, oleophobic coating provides resistance to smudging and fingerprints, and a final hard coat reduces environmental wear and minimizes scratching.
            </span>

           <li>
            <span class="content">
             <img alt="newegg" src="http://c1.neweggimages.com/BizIntell/item/26/665/26-665-043/26-665-043_h2.jpg" style="width: 75px;"/>
             <em class="title">
              fRACTYL Lens Geometry
             </em>
             GUNNAR fRACTYL lens geometry mimics nature using advanced science and engineering to aid the natural focusing power of the eye, allowing the cilliary muscles (the muscles that shape your cornea) to relax. Additionally, the wrapped geometry of the lens sits close to the eye and face, helping maintain the healthy humidity levels in the eye.
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span class="content">
             <img alt="newegg" src="http://c1.neweggimages.com/BizIntell/item/26/665/26-665-043/26-665-043_h3.jpg" style="width: 75px;"/>
             <em class="title">
              iONik Lens Tints
             </em>
             GUNNAR iONik lens tint limits distracting blue light frequencies that are overproduced by phosphors in the bulbs, only allowing light from an optimal spectrum to pass through the lens, resulting in clearer images, less eye strain and increased visual efficiency.
            </span>
