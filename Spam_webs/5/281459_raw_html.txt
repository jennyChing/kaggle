        Lindsay Lohan
       </h2>
       <h4 class="hf2-case">
        Shows Support for Mom
       </h4>
       <h3 class="hf3">
        But None for Her Boobs
       </h3>
      </div>
      <h5 class="article-posted-date">
       9/13/2013 3:45 PM PDT BY TMZ STAFF

      <div class="all-post-body group article-content" itemprop="articleBody">

       <img alt="0913_lindsay_lohan_splash_article" src="http://ll-media.tmz.com/2013/09/13/0913-lindsay-lohan-splash-article-3.jpg"/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        Lindsay Lohan
       </strong>
       just arrived at her mom's house on Long Island -- this after
       <strong>
        Dina Lohan
       </strong>
       's arrest for an off-the-charts DWI -- and she was definitely lacking in the support department.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Lindsay showed up in a black Porsche with her sister, Ali and an unidentified guy -- flashing a ton of side-boob as she walked into the house.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Shortly after Linds showed up ... her old attorney
       <strong>
        Mark Heller
       </strong>
       walked out of Dina's pad, and announced he's representing her in the DWI.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Heller pointed out Dina is a first time DWI offender with no prior record (criminally, anyway) -- and as a result he felt she would be treated with "fairness." Translation: he's hoping for no jail time.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       As we previously reported ... Dina was arrested Thursday night ... and cops say she had a BAC of 0.2 -- nearly 3 times the legal limit.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Dina's still laying low -- the only sign of her all day was in the doorway as she let a friend out. She looked ... hazy.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <img alt="0913_dina_lohan_splash_article" src="http://ll-media.tmz.com/2013/09/13/0913-dina-lohan-splash-article-3.jpg"/>
