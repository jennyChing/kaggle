         One of the biggest challenges that entrepreneurs and start-ups face is finding a way to finance their endeavors and manage their cash flow. You already know that in order for you to start any business venture your brilliant ideas need to be backed up by enough capital. You can use the funding to tackle your expenses even if youre just looking to expand  from recruiting great talent to paying off your student loans and establishing your credibility, extra funds can be a big help.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         If youre established in Quebec, there are a number of ways you can support your business venture. Funds are available from a few different sources, but like you might assume, not all of them are going to be suitable for you. Here are just a few business financing options to consider if you have decided to boost your business ideas:
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 650px">
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/ukincanada/7290949724" rel="nofollow" target="blank" title="Entrepreneurs are GREAT! by British High Commission, Ottawa, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Entrepreneurs - business financing" height="427" src="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8016/7290949724_ff1f70a0c6_z.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/ukincanada/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           UK in Canada
          </a>
          /
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/ukincanada/7290949724/in/photolist-faWWsd-8VJkaw-dBUXwL-9vxtNP-7hrC5a-2dqF9y-c7h2kJ-7qs9mr-bGoAsx-dBPxP4-dBUXvC-a3Pdjy-56tP1m-52gKU-ii15MR-eRDHyG-btu6iG-btu1sU-hqtJHJ-btu1tm-dfarEa-bGoGkR-dB9Ex7-dB4t1R-otU81-a4bnPb-9PVoX6-9Z34nU-nQvhxQ-ny5yzb-9QTrzm-ny5yEb-ecpPMP-9Z33em-9QR6e4-nyHnr5-dPNbsG-gkQHfP-7VX4ii-a6N9pq-9x4G4P-dBUXy3-bfQRh4-4xEsV5-nGxLtc-ny5z3q-nSmuTX-aTP53V-nPaekW-aSN2wR" rel="nofollow" target="blank">

          ) CEO of Hootsuite Ryan Holmes with Sir Richard Branson, Founder of Virgin Group  two of the most widely recognized entrepreneurs.
         </p>
        </div>
        <h2>
         1.Government Grants
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         There are many different grants entrepreneurs like you can use to finance your business venture. But when it comes to government grants of any type  from awards to donations and endowments  bear in mind that the criteria for approvals is extremely particular. Most grants are not only targeting a specific business, but also a specific location or product.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 510px">
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/howardlake/4986288875" rel="nofollow" target="blank" title="Funding by Howard Lake, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Government and NGO Funding - business financing" height="281" src="https://farm5.staticflickr.com/4104/4986288875_12a50c06c7.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/howardlake/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           Howard Lake
          </a>
          /
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/howardlake/4986288875/in/photolist-8AC2XR-8AF7dE-ebuGKN-iU2gF5-9qGejg-533r3u-9mSJ4F-9mVKn3-9mTBXL-jN5dfX-fMxTWR-annm9L-7QPLmA-anju2r-h3G5Ma-h3GsK8-b6MCQF-ir8Wtc-iTZzpx-iU2eaS-avzUrM-9mVKEd-9mSHLv-6DX8M1-aMPgf4-8are3K-4wqmUd-7wNEDi-ap5pDC-8SKvMA-8SKuqG-8SqWtx-8SGnd2-8SG4ov-8SGdUv-8Su3Xb-8SqKqi-8SGokx-8SG7vx-avzRii-mWiA4N-nXb3kJ-bLxXVe-9pfHLg-83gp8g-nXSytj-8SJTE9-8SJWCw-83f9AK-8SKmYG" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           Flickr
          </a>
          / Attribution-ShareAlike 2.0 Generic (
          <a href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/2.0/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           CC BY-SA 2.0
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Available grants for entrepreneurs in Quebec include:
        </p>
        <ul style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.canadabusiness.ca/eng/program/2399/sgc-24/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           Jeunes Promoteurs
          </a>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://www.canadabusiness.ca/eng/program/2399/sgc-24/">
           </a>
          </strong>
           provides financial help for entrepreneurs starting their first business. The age requirements are that you have to be between 18 and 35 years old. There are 3 components to the grant: Preparation of a business plan (75% of expenses covered); Establishment of a first business (50% of expenses covered if you create at least 2 jobs); and, Entrepreneur training (100% of expenses covered).
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.canadabusiness.ca/eng/program/2086/sgc-24/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           Fonds de dveloppement des entreprises dconomie sociale
          </a>
           the capital provided by this grant cannot exceed 80% of eligible expenditures. The grant couldfinance one or more aspects of yoursocial enterprise development project: capital expenditures for expanding or starting your business; acquisition of technology, software or patents; need for operating working capital. In order to be eligible, your business needs to be financially viable, produce goods or services and create long-term goals.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Make sure you check the specific criteria for
         <a href="http://www.canadabusiness.ca/eng/page/2740/sgc-24/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
          each available grant
         </a>
         , before you apply.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         2.
         <a href="http://www.canadabusiness.ca/eng/page/2669/sgc-24/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
          Financing from NGOs
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         In addition to government grants, there is a certain number of non-government funding available to entrepreneurs. Some NGOs may give you a certain amount of money, while others may finance a certain percentage of your start-up. NGO funded grants include the following:
        </p>
        <ul style="text-align: justify;">
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.canadabusiness.ca/eng/program/2425/sgc-24/" rel="nofollow" style="font-style: normal;" target="blank">
           Fondation Montral Incs Grant
          </a>
          provides up to $30,000 to help cover 25% of your start-up costs. Eligible candidates for this grant need to be Canadian citizens between the ages of 18 and 35, willing to start up a business in a borough or a reconstituted city of Montreal.
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.canadabusiness.ca/eng/program/2567/sgc-24/" rel="nofollow" style="font-style: normal;" target="blank">
           Futurpreneur Canada Expansion Program
          </a>
          is another of the many programmes that can support theexpansion of your business. The criteria is similar to that of the Foundation Montreal Incs Grant. The applicants age (must be between 18 and 39) and residency status (they must be Canadian or a permanent resident of Canada) are key eligibility factors to secure the funds.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         3. Crowdfunding
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Crowdfunding is one of the alternative methods of raising the capital you need. For all those unaware of what crowdfunding is  its the collection of financial contributions from people not usually connected to the financial sector.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 510px">
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/analogica/8661000014" rel="nofollow" target="blank" title="Crowdfunding by Roco Lara, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Crowdfunding - business financing" height="348" src="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8124/8661000014_cb0056d718.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/analogica/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           Rocio Lara
          </a>
          /
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/analogica/8661000014/in/photolist-eckTys-fUGLSp-9ygKRY-9ygLr1-extBUY-gTUutQ-9gSuQQ-dNThHa-dJQS6C-fWqosf-ggsKes-ggsKCU-ggsK9h-ggsZZR-ggtqoi-ggsKof-ggt1uj-ggsZjx-ggsKm1-ggsK61-9p7TPh-fQHSxs-m15ZVe-m15u5K-m16UvW-gEvtTU-j5piLT-fuPcKY-afiAby-affMtg-a8jU3P-a8nLk9-a8nLmd-a8nLtL-a8jU9t-a8nLwd-9ygMCb-9ydMBg-9ydLDM-9ygMvm-9ydNJc-9ydMZr-9ygLNL-bquD2Q-bDpxNM-9ygKk7-fQHQZS-gTUukU" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           Flickr
          </a>
          / Attribution-ShareAlike 2.0 Generic (
          <a href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/2.0/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           CC BY-SA 2.0
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         This option is available via crowdfunding platforms on the internet such as
         <a href="http://ecloid.com/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
          Ecloid
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="https://fundo.ca/en/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
          Fundo
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://ncfacanada.org/canadian-crowdfunding-directory/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
          others
         </a>
         . Its not only a great way to involve the public while marketing your start-up and products, but it also allows you to offer rewards and take pre-orders while collecting donations. The cons of crowdfunding, though, include the risk of having your ideas copied by other companies or big corporations. Alternatively, you might consider selling or pitching some of your ideas to possible buyers.The one huge pro of this fundraising method is that you get to test the validity of your start-up or the launch of a new product by getting immediate responses from the public.
        </p>
        <h2>
         4. Private Sector and Personal Assets
        </h2>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 510px">
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/dolmansaxlil/6711650765" rel="nofollow" target="blank" title="Lucky Loonie by Sharon Drummond, on Flickr">
          <img alt="Personal Assets - business financing" height="333" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7157/6711650765_2408c7c122.jpg" width="500"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/dolmansaxlil/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           Sharon Drummond
          </a>
          /
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/dolmansaxlil/6711650765/in/photolist-be5XWD-6KZom-7oevyJ-k6MH-4apjV-4apkJ-8vKaQ1-51GDS9-8KiQN2-gMWR9-8FrNQf-amF5Eo-7r4Knz-91434Z-5Zavff-7oaZa5-6Q9LFA-cFE5n-6wT9YE-5aWR4N-7r8hxv-a6xkKY-aVMjAg-h8yoH-7d8yGv-8sKada-bpRSNd-k6jE7s-7qtwXZ-8m8duq-8h7gB3-7qxuN1-5f74Fx-5aWRC3-gsGFij-7VtjWJ-6HPouy-hi6Tbx-8rEHZ7-eMn8HQ-7mymns-8zwtuJ-izWiHN-5aWQjj-3w4e7-om382-638Fw-5PUc9M-56hWgZ-96FQZp" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           Flickr
          </a>
          / Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 2.0 Generic (
          <a href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/2.0/" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           CC BY-NC-SA 2.0
          </a>
          ) Some lenders require you to invest a certain amount of personal capital
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Most banks will require you to have a starting capital before they lend you any money. In addition, there are a number of documents you need to prepare in order to apply  a cash flow forecast and a credit score statement among others. If you dont have the time, or you dont like waiting, you can instead use personal assets to finance your business. There are plenty of lenders that provide
         <a href="http://www.zillidy.com/business-loans/" target="blank">
          business loans
         </a>
         against personal possessions such as jewelry, watches and other valuables.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Lending against personal possessions is a particularly good option for entrepreneurs who do not have enough or any capital to start their business off. Furthermore, theres no need for countless application forms and filling specific criteria, which makes the process fast and user-friendly. In addition, business loans from private companies, unlike a line of credit, do not impact you personal or business credit.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Which of the financing options described above are you going to try out? Let us know in the comment section below.
        </p>
