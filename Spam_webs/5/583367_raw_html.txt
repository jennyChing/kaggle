          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <em>
            How to Make a Cropped Top
           </em>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Cropped tops are so fun and I would love to wear them so that my flat stomach could peek out, but alas I do not have a flat stomach, never really have, but I still love the idea of cropped tops. I use cropped tops to wear over dresses so that it looks like I am wearing a skirt rather than a dress. The cropped top makes it easier to wear a dress as a skirt because there is less fabric to tuck into the belt you use to cinch your waist. It can be down without a cropped top, but why not crop your top! Its so easy!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://356lgk17nyr2kpv5k1j4iy21.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/How-to-make-a-cropped-top.png">
            <img alt="How-to-make-a-cropped-top" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17452" height="437" src="http://356lgk17nyr2kpv5k1j4iy21.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/How-to-make-a-cropped-top.png" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           First, start with a top that you want to crop. I love the
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1sKCTt3" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Merona Favorite V-Neck Tee
           </a>
           from
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1rnSBIJ" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Target
           </a>
           (support April GoLightly by buying through this link :))
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <a href="http://356lgk17nyr2kpv5k1j4iy21.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Target-Favorite-Tee.png">
            <img alt="Target-Favorite-Tee" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17453" height="788" src="http://356lgk17nyr2kpv5k1j4iy21.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Target-Favorite-Tee.png" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           Next, lay down your favorite cropped top that hits in the perfect spot to use as a guide for your new cropped top. I used my other top from Target 
           <a href="http://bit.ly/1sqY7NA" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Xhilaration Juniors Lace Back Cropped Top
           </a>
           as my guideline.
           <a href="http://356lgk17nyr2kpv5k1j4iy21.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/How-to-make-a-crop-top.jpg">
            <img alt="How-to-make-a-crop-top" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17459" height="678" src="http://356lgk17nyr2kpv5k1j4iy21.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/How-to-make-a-crop-top.jpg" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           I used a
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000B7K9UI/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B000B7K9UI&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=aprilgo-20&amp;linkId=FX5FYCJNVQ3Q5YTP" rel="nofollow">
            Fiskars Rotary Cutting Set
           </a>
           <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=aprilgo-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B000B7K9UI" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
           with rotary cutter, ruler, and self healing mat to cut the perfect straight line across the top. This is the best way I have found to cute a straight line. You can also use a scissors, but I recommend drawing a straight line with a ruler first.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://356lgk17nyr2kpv5k1j4iy21.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DIY-Cropped-Top-6.jpg">
            <img alt="DIY Cropped Top-6" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17445" height="467" src="http://356lgk17nyr2kpv5k1j4iy21.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DIY-Cropped-Top-6.jpg" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://356lgk17nyr2kpv5k1j4iy21.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DIY-Crop-top.jpg">
            <img alt="DIY-Crop-top" class="aligncenter wp-image-17454 size-full" height="662" src="http://356lgk17nyr2kpv5k1j4iy21.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DIY-Crop-top-e1406042634149.jpg" width="700"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Now you have the perfect length cropped top that will tuck nicely into belts without any extra fabric. Here, I am wearing my cropped top over a dress with this stretchy belt to cover where the cropped top ends.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://356lgk17nyr2kpv5k1j4iy21.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/IMG_6694.jpg">

           </a>
          </p>
