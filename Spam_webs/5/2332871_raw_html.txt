        <div class="article-content rich-text">
         <p>
          Taylor Swift has debuted the video for
          <em>
           Style
          </em>
          , the third single from her colossal pop album
          <em>
           1989.
          </em>
          Set partly on the beach and partly in the woods, it's a cute, arty promo clip that often looks as if it's been put through a variety of bleary Instagram filters. Taylor looks great working the "red lip classic thing" from the song's lyrics, and her English co-star Dominic Sherwood is a suitably beautiful, brooding love interest, but there's nothing to rival the best bits from her last video,
          <em>
           Blank Space
          </em>
          - remember Taylor standing on top of a horse? That was amazing. This video delivers the style of the song's title, but fewer GIF-making opportunities.
         </p>
         <p>
          The most interesting moment arrives at the 3.23 mark with a blink-and-you'll-miss-it glimpse of Taylor holding a paper airplane necklace. According to Swiftlore, this necklace is a gift Harry Styles gave the singer back when they were dating. Is this Taylor's way of sneakily confirming that she wrote
          <em>
           Style
          </em>
          about the One Direction star, or is she just teasing us? Can you imagine Harry with a "James Dean daydream look" in his eye like Taylor sings on the chorus? Let the Twitter debate commence...
         </p>
