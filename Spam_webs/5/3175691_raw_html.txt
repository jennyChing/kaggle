      <div class="p402_premium ">
       <p>
        Chris Pratt may star in the upcoming dino flick "Jurassic World," but when it comes to T-Rex trivia, it looks like his skills are a little, er, extinct.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="line-height: 1.6em;">
         In an appearance on "The Ellen Show," Pratt faced off in a dinosaur trivia game against 6-year-old Noah Ritter, better known as the "apparently kid" whose video interview repeating the word went viral back in August.
        </span>
       </p>

        </span>
       </p>
