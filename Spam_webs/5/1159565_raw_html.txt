        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Get Road Trip Ready in 10 Easy Steps  A Printable Checklist
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-07-27T09:30:07+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          July 27, 2014
         </time>
         By:
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
           Mel Lockcuff
          </span>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://adventuresofmel.com/2014/07/get-road-trip-ready-in-10-easy-steps-a-printable-checklist.html#comments">
           6 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <img alt="Walmart Auto Disclosure #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23603" height="45" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Walmart-Auto-Disclosure.png" width="700"/>
         Tis the season for a good road trip! Some day, my family will be climbing and maneuvering the switchbacks of Mount Evans in Colorado. We can hardly wait to climb above the clouds again and watch the sun set from above 14,000 feet. Thats not all. The place were staying says the only way in during winter is to have 4wd; thank goodness for warm, dry weather, cause Im not sure what weve gotten ourselves into. Of course, a road trip means getting our faithful Honda ready for said trip. Below youll find a printable checklist for car care before a big road trip. just a few things we try to do before every trip to make sure the car is completely ready.
         <img alt="Get Road Trip Ready in 10 Easy Steps - Tips and Printable Checklist from MamaBuzz #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23595" height="850" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Get-Road-Trip-Ready-in-10-Easy-Steps-Tips-and-Printable-Checklist-from-MamaBuzz-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Weve been on so many adventures over the last few years. There was the time we hit the road for Austin, broke down, and limped our way to a car dealer where we traded our old Honda in for a new to us Honda. Boy, thats a story in and of itself; never in our lives would we ever have imagined ourselves doing something like that. But I think that experience spurred in us even more of a desire to really take care of our vehicles.
        </p>
        <p>
         Prepping for a road trip is pretty simple; it just takes time and energy.
        </p>
        <h4>
         <strong>
          Road Trip Ready in 10 Easy Steps
         </strong>
        </h4>
        <p>
         1.
         <strong>
          Clean your cars interior.
         </strong>
         Trust me, you dont want to be going down the road, trying to figure out what that funky smell is. Clean out that car, and give it a little TLC. Take out all the chairs and blankets from your last stint at the drive in; you know, when you hurriedly threw everything in the car so you wouldnt have to wait in line to get on the road again.
         <img alt="Chair and blanket mess from the drive in #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23593" height="511" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Chair-and-blanket-mess-from-the-drive-in-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Let me tell you,
         <a href="http://bit.ly/U9J8Ib" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Walmart Auto
         </a>
         has made this step so easy, its not even funny. In fact, its kinda fun.
         <img alt="Car Care Products including Armor All #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23592" height="506" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Car-Care-Products-including-Armor-All-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
         Just grab a big trash bag, and set the kids to work cleaning out all the trash and gunk that builds up over time. dont act like you dont know what Im talking about; just move your car seat, and youll see exactly what Im talking about. After all the trash and other strange things are removed, grab the vacuum and give it a good cleaning, seats, rugs, floors, and all. Next use Armor All Disinfecting Wipes to clean and disinfect surfaces in your car. places like the steering wheel, door handles, buttons and knobs, cup holders, areas on your car seat or booster seat, and more. These wipes are great for nonporous surfaces like plastic, vinyl upholstery, metal, glass, and car seats. They dry quickly too, and they dont leave a film or residue.
         <img alt="Armor All Disinfectant Wipes #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23588" height="519" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Armor-All-Disinfectant-Wipes-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
         Next take Armor All Linen Protectant Spray, and wipe down that dash. What a fresh scent it leaves behind. And it leaves everything so shiny and clean, almost as if you have a brand new car all over again.
         <img alt="Wiping down the interior with Armor All Protectant #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23601" height="900" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Wiping-down-the-interior-with-Armor-All-Protectant-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
         (Hint: Just be sure to spray this directly on your rag when cleaning the dash, because otherwise, it may get on your window.) When all done with the inside, take a step back and admire your work cause it probably wont last long, especially if you have kids.
         <img alt="Clean car interior, ready for a road trip #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23594" height="509" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Clean-car-interior-ready-for-a-road-trip-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         2.
         <strong>
          Wash and wax your cars exterior.
         </strong>
         Oh, this step was the absolute best; we got the whole family involved. Jaden had been looking forward to this all afternoon. Armor All Premium Wash &amp; Wax + Protect really makes this so easy. It mixes with water, so be sure to have a bucket on hand.
         <img alt="Armor All Wash &amp; Wax + Protect #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23590" height="521" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Armor-All-Wash-Wax-+-Protect-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
         Take a car brush or rags to your car, and wash all the dirt and grime away.
         <img alt="Washing the car #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23599" height="501" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Washing-the-car-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
         This lends to some pretty amazing family memories. so much better than sitting in an automatic car wash. Sure, you might get soaked. ok, so you will get soaked. Just go with it!
         <img alt="Make washing the car a family affair #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23596" height="522" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Make-washing-the-car-a-family-affair-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         When done washing and waxing, Armor All Trim &amp; Plastic Restorer helps to shine up plastic, vinyl, and rubber trim. Armor All Tire Glaze shines up those tires.
         <img alt="Armor All Tire Glaze #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23589" height="530" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Armor-All-Tire-Glaze-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
         Our car looked like a brand new car when we were done. Wowsers! I think this could become a regular family activity. We were having such a good time, we even washed up Daddys car. Hey, might as well while we have everything out, right?
         <img alt="Both cars spic and span, oh so clean! #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23591" height="503" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Both-cars-spic-and-span-oh-so-clean-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         3.
         <strong>
          Check all fluids.
         </strong>
         Check the oil, transmission fluid, and windshield wiper fluid. If the oil needs changed, make sure thats done before the big trip.
        </p>
        <p>
         4.
         <strong>
          Tire check!
         </strong>
         Are the tires good? Do they still have good tread? Do they have enough air? Are there any leaks? Definitely important to check before heading out.
        </p>
        <p>
         5.
         <b>
          Check your spare.
         </b>
         First of all, make sure you have a good spare. And make sure you have everything you need if you were to have a flat.
        </p>
        <p>
         6.
         <strong>
          Grab a few tools.
         </strong>
         Dan always grabs a few tools he might need if we were stranded, whether it be a flat tire or something else that were to go wrong. It never hurts to have a small bag of tools ready to go should problems arise.
         <strong>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         7.
         <strong>
          Pack emergency supplies.
         </strong>
         This is something my parents ingrained in me since I was a young college student driving back and forth cross country on my own, even getting lost in a blizzard in New York one time (you think Im kidding.). This is especially important in winter or if traveling in inclement weather. Pack things like blankets, water, flashlights, extra food. it never hurts to be prepared because things happen. Life happens.
        </p>
        <p>
         8.
         <strong>
          Pack a First Aid Kit.
         </strong>
         With my boys, this has just become a given on every single trip. And it looks like I need to replenish a few things in our kit before our big trip. Make sure creams and topicals arent expired. Pack things like Band-Aids, wraps, a cold pack, pain reliever, topical creams, etc.
         <img alt="Prepare a First Aid Kit for road trips #WalmartAuto" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-23597" height="526" src="http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/Prepare-a-First-Aid-Kit-for-road-trips-WalmartAuto.jpg" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         9.
         <strong>
          Prep cables, plugs, GPS/atlas, etc.
         </strong>
         We actually have a wireless router in our car, thanks to my techie husband. Make sure you have all your cables and everything you need to keep electronics running smoothly.
        </p>
        <p>
         10.
         <strong>
          Pack all your gear and have a blast!
         </strong>
         This is probably the most important step. Just have a blast! And create a ton of amazing memories your whole family will never forget.
        </p>
        <p>
         You can find all of the
         <a href="http://bit.ly/U9J8Ib" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Walmart Auto
         </a>
         cleaning and washing products mentioned above at a Walmart near you or on Walmart.com.
        </p>

jQuery(document).ready(function($) {
$(function() {	
	var process_script_url = 'http://adventuresofmel.com/wp-content/plugins/wp-cart-for-digital-products' + '/ajax_process_cart_requests.php';
	var save_ret_cart_msg = $('div.eStore_save_retrieve_cart_message');
	
	$('.eStore_save_cart_section').on("click", function (){
		save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append("Processing Your Request...");		
		var dataString = 'eStore_cart_action=eStore_save_cart';		
		$.ajax({
			type: "POST",
			url: process_script_url,
			data: dataString,
			dataType: "json",
			success: function(data) {				
				//var dataStr = JSON.stringify(data); console.log(dataStr);
				if(data.status == "success"){
					save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append('Your cart has been saved! Shopping Cart ID : ' + data.ID);
				}
				else{
					save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append('&lt;span class="eStore_warning"&gt;' + data.details + '&lt;/span&gt;');
				}
			}
		});				
		return false;
	});

	$('.eStore_retrieve_cart_section').on("click", function (){
		var cart_id = prompt("Shopping Cart ID", "");	
		if(cart_id == null){
			return false;
		}
		else if(cart_id ==""){
			save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append('&lt;span class="eStore_warning"&gt;The shopping cart ID you entered does not appear to be correct!&lt;/span&gt;');
			return false;
		}
		cart_id = $.trim(cart_id);
		save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append("Processing Your Request...");
		var dataString = 'eStore_cart_action=eStore_retrieve_cart&amp;cart_id=' + cart_id;
		$.ajax({
			type: "POST",
			url: process_script_url,
			data: dataString,
			dataType: "json",
			success: function(data) {
				if(data.status == "success"){	
					save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append('Success');	
					target_url = window.location.href.split("#")[0];
					window.location.href = target_url;					
				}
				else{
					save_ret_cart_msg.html("").append('&lt;span class="eStore_warning"&gt;The shopping cart ID you entered does not appear to be correct!&lt;/span&gt;');	
				}	
			}
		});		
		return false;
	});
});
});
