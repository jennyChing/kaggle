     </h4>
    </div>

    <h2>
     Concealable body armor
    </h2>
    To build EnGardes concealable body armor we start with  Dyneema, the strongest, lightest PE fiber on the market. We combine those space age fabrics with a flexible but tough exterior that makes our concealable body armor resistant to abrasions but still flexible enough to wear under street clothes.
    <p>
     Exterior pockets that can accommodate hard armor inserts means our concealable body armor can be upgraded to meet the most serious threats from assault rifles and machine guns. Stealth combined with a flexible response means our concealable body armor is useful in a wide range of military, law enforcement and private security applications.
