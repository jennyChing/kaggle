                When people think about remodeling, they think it has to cost a lot of money. That is because they think of a huge overhaul.                <p class="MsoBodyText">
                <b>
                 Budgets Under $50
                </b>
               </p>
               <p class="MsoBodyText">
                Now I know what you are thinking, What can I do for $50?! Well for starters, you can paint your kitchen. If you have never repainted your kitchen, chances are, it needs it. When you go to buy paint for your kitchen, be sure to use a semigloss latex paint. This way, you will be able to easily use a sponge to wipe off food that may spatter onto the walls.

               </p>

                 Budgets Under $100
                </b>
               </p>
               <p class="MsoBodyText">
                Chances are, your kitchen has more space than what you might think. However, you just have to organize your stuff in order to see that. No matter how much money you spend on your cabinets or counter tops, if you are not organized, your kitchen is going to look junkie. Buy some kitchen organization items, including lid holders, pot racks, drawer organizers and even pullout trays. These items can really make your kitchen look great, and its only going to cost you the organization items you buy.
               </p>

               </p>

                 Budgets Under $200
                </b>

                <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">
                </span>

               </p>

               </p>

                <b>
                 Budgets Over $350
                </b>
               </p>
               <p class="MsoBodyText">
                Refurbish those cabinets! Usually cabinets are the most expensive part to remodeling your kitchen. So if you can save your old ones, go for it! Replacing worn out parts, repainting and adding some new hardware to your cabinets can make them look like new again without actually having to spend a bundle of money.
               </p>
               <p class="MsoBodyText">
               </p>

                <b>
                 Budgets Over $500
                </b>
               </p>
               <p class="MsoBodyText">
                This topic is going to range from the $500 minimum to the top of our budget at $1000. That is because we are going to talk about redoing your floors. Depending on the type of material you choose for your floors is going to determine just how much its going to cost. For example, vinyl floor tiles are not only comfortable to walk on, but they are very affordable. Going with this kind of flooring, over title is going to save you a ton of money, and you may be able to install it yourself.
