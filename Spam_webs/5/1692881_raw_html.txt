Each individual Chandelier has been lovingly restored and customised to create original one off pieces, thus producing a unique and bespoke Chandelier. Every chandelier has been completely stripped, rewired and earthed with new ttings to conform with British standards thus eliminating the cost of electricians re-wiring fees. The Vintage Chandelier Company offer a unique and individual product and can supply Chandeliers in various sizes, shapes and colours that will suit any home, both traditional or contemporary.
