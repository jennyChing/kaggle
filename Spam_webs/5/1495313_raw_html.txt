        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Water Around the World
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <em>
          Flow like a Real River
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Pihtsuskngsgo_ur_jauzia" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4680" height="480" src="http://images.realtaiji.com/Pihtsuskngsgo_ur_jauzia.jpg" width="640"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Whether you're
         <strong>
          flowing like a Great River
         </strong>
         or imagining a cool mountain stream, Water is a great concern among internal artists. In Taijiquan, we seek to emulate the river:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          its whirls and eddies...
         </li>
         <li>
          its slow churning
          <em>
           and
          </em>
          its abrupt energetic power...
         </li>
         <li>
          its secret calm carrying vibrant life-forces from fresh sources to salty seas...
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         We don't often imagine a dirty, chemically stinky river. It's not likely that you would enjoy becoming poisoned water that compromises the health of young children and sensitive life-forms.
        </p>
        <p>
         Water is a major concern for any practitioner and every person. Perhaps today, in the park, or wherever you practice your motions:
         <strong>
          imagine you're one of the following modern forms of water.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Watch two brief videos, both about water, in honor of
         <a href="http://blogactionday.change.org/" target="_blank" title="Open a new window to Blog Action Day...">
          Blog Action Day
         </a>
         ...
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-4087">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
         L'Desh Fresh - The World's Most Authentic Drinking Water
        </h3>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="371" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/cd1CNPfx3Bc?feature=oembed" width="660">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Fluoride In Drinking Water
        </h3>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="495" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/7Ar5hbsKPIs?feature=oembed" width="660">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         How's that for a kick in the pants?
        </p>
