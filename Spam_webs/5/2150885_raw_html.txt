&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5764" src="http://www.livinglavidaholoka.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/DSCN4527.jpg" alt="cherrybombcocktail" height="461" width="650"&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Making the drink stirrers is super easy! &nbsp;Glitter foam is cut into heart shapes using cookie cutters for tracing. &nbsp;I used a hot glue gun to attach the hearts to a bamboo skewer. &nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5768" src="http://www.livinglavidaholoka.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/DSCN4523.jpg" alt="DSCN4523" height="488" width="650"&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;&lt;b&gt;GLITTER HEART DRINK STIRRER&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;SUPPLIES&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&#8211; &lt;a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0026HXZHA/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0026HXZHA&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=livlavidhol00-20&amp;linkId=2OROVZOAPSCDZ3S7"&gt;red glitter foam sheet&lt;/a&gt;&lt;img style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=livlavidhol00-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B0026HXZHA" alt="" height="1" width="1" border="0"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; bamboo skewers&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; &lt;a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0000CF4KC/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0000CF4KC&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=livlavidhol00-20&amp;linkId=7R2ILSXEC4ISAZFN"&gt;heart cookie cutters, for tracing&lt;/a&gt;&lt;img style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=livlavidhol00-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B0000CF4KC" alt="" height="1" width="1" border="0"&gt;&lt;br /&gt;
&#8211; glue&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;STEPS&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;1. Using heart-shaped cookie cutters, or drawing freehand, draw three different sized hearts on the backside of the foam sheet.&lt;br /&gt;
2. Cut the hearts out and using hot glue, or glue of choice, attach hearts onto the bamboo skewer. Layer the hearts with the biggest on top and smallest on the bottom.&lt;br /&gt;
3. Allow glue to dry and add to a Cherry Cola Cocktail or your favorite Valentine&#8217;s Day drink.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5772" src="http://www.livinglavidaholoka.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/DSCN4525.jpg" alt="DSCN4525" height="523" width="650"&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Now that you&#8217;ve made your glitter stirrers, you need a cocktail to use them in! &nbsp;The classic flavors of cherry cola get amped up with cherry flavored vodka and maraschino cherries.&lt;/p&gt;
