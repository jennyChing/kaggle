           <h1 class="title">
            The 13 Worst, Weirdest and Tackiest Christmas Presents To Spoil The Season
           </h1>
           <!-- google_ad_section_end -->
           <div itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Article">
            <meta content="The 13 Worst, Weirdest and Tackiest Christmas Presents To Spoil The Season" itemprop="headline"/>

              <div class="KonaBody">
               <span itemprop="articleBody">
                <!--paging_filter-->
                <p>
                 Christmas is here again, which means its time get gifts for
your selfish friends and the family members you care so much about that you
only see them annually. While time is running out to get gifts, this guide can
help you find the right present for all the wrong people in your life, including:
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 The Girl You're Stalking:
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B000FPH3X2?tag=ispotim-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  <span class="inline inline-middle">
                   <img alt="" class="image image-img_assist_custom" height="400" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/il_430xN.102322588.img_assist_custom.jpg" title="" width="400"/>
                  </span>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p>
                 As you may have noticed in last year's
                 <a href="/articles/15_strange_funny_weird_bizarre_sick_gifts_20878" target="_blank">
                  Christmas gift list
                 </a>
                 and the
                 <a href="/articles/15_best_worst_valentines_day_gifts_22512?single=1" target="_blank">
                  Valentine's gift list
                 </a>
                 , nothing says "I love you" like a
                 <a href="http://www.etsy.com/view_listing.php?listing_id=34459069his" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  dead animal
turned into jewelry
                 </a>
                 . Diamonds may be forever, but so is death when you think
about it --and hey, girls love animals, right?
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 Your Twilight-Loving Girlfriend:
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-img_assist_custom" height="285" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/necklacebite.img_assist_custom.jpg" title="" width="449"/>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 We all know that the most romantic thing a guy can do is
suck out your blood to make you immortal. Unfortunately, most of us don't
actually know any real vampires (and no, the loser goths with dental-implant
fangs don't count), so if you want to show someone how much you want them to be
with you forever, get them a necklace that symbolizes your lust for their
blood. (
                 <u>
                  Update
                 </u>
                 : This necklace is no longer available. Perhaps this
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004XU88PM?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B004XU88PM&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  blood chocker
                 </a>
                 would be a good alternative.)
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 The Philanthropic Idiot You Secretly Can't Stand:
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-_original" height="160" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/m1.jpg" title="" width="201"/>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 You know the jerk who makes donations in everyone's name for
a cause he cares about when the gift receiver would rather have presents? What
better way to get back at him then donating in his name to a charity of your
choice --one that doesn't really exist and costs you nothing!
                 <a href="http://care4less.org/" target="_blank">
                  Care4Less
                 </a>
                 allows
you to choose one of eight fake charities, like Leprechaun Leprosy,
and then your non-loved one will receive a personalized email with the amount
you didn't really donate in his or her name.
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 The Girl Who's Desperate to Snag a Husband at Any Cost:
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-_original" height="460" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/fakepregnancytest.jpg" title="" width="270"/>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Sure you'll be doing a disservice to all mankind and
creating an incredibly Glee-like scenario with a girl faking her pregnancy with this always
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003QJV2FE?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B003QJV2FE&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  positive pregnancy test
                 </a>
                 , but
at least your psychotically desperate friend will have a husband --at least,
for a few months until he finds out about the deception. (
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003QJV2FE?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B003QJV2FE&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  Buy here
                 </a>
                 )
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 The Office Workers Who Always Get Their Lunches Stolen:
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-img_assist_custom" height="291" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/abc-cookies-l.img_assist_custom.jpg" title="" width="450"/>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 At most offices, the majority of employees are respectful
enough to avoid eating one another's lunches. Unfortunately, not all workers
share this same level of courtesy (like the disgusting girl at my office who
also pees all over the toilet seat whenever she goes to the bathroom despite
constant warnings...but enough about my pathetic life). If you know some poor
office schlub who always finds their lunch stolen, get them these
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000FPH3X2?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B000FPH3X2" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  great cookie
cutters
                 </a>
                 so at least their dessert will stay safe. When coupled with a moldy
lunch bag, your friend may actually start to enjoy lunch at the office again. (
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000FPH3X2?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B000FPH3X2" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  Buy here
                 </a>
                 )
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 The Teens Who Try Way Too Hard To Fit In:
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0099X2BOM?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B0099X2BOM&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  <span class="inline inline-middle">
                   <img alt="" class="image image-_original" height="450" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/iphone-3g-dummy-black-back.jpg" title="" width="450"/>
                  </span>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Whether its for your own child or one of your obnoxious
cousins, nothing says "I care about your reputation but don't want to spend
too much on you" more than a $20
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0099X2BOM?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B0099X2BOM&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                 </a>
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0099X2BOM?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B0099X2BOM&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  fake iPhone
                 </a>
                 . Now teens can go to class and pretend to
play with their smart phone just like everyone else --and at least if it gets
confiscated by the teacher it's no big deal.
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 The Pimped Out Geek (Or Geekette):
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-img_assist_custom" height="301" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/Falcon-Bed.img_assist_custom.jpg" title="" width="450"/>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 These days, it's not
uncommon for there to be a larger percentage of
                 <a href="http://www.ruethedayblog.com/2008/08/25-hot-comic-con-girls/" target="_blank">
                  hot girls at a comic book
convention
                 </a>
                 than at an MMA fight. That's why
                 <a href="http://www.facebook.com/album.php?aid=127502&amp;id=104545533252" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  this bed
                 </a>
                 , made by the same brilliant woman who created the
                 <a href="/articles/food_trends_2009_hop_burger_bandwagon_23726" target="_blank">
                  cheeseburger bed
                 </a>
                 , might actually help your
geeky friends impress possible romantic conquests, rather than just chasing them away.
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 The Woman Who Sounds Like Fran Drescher:
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-img_assist_custom" height="221" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/Beauty_Voice_Trainer.img_assist_custom.jpg" title="" width="450"/>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 We all know someone with a grating voice. Sure, they may be
a great person, but to actually listen to them talk can be like hours spent in
purgatory. Fortunately, this great
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00C1Y5K44?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B00C1Y5K44&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  Japanese invention
                 </a>
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00C1Y5K44?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B00C1Y5K44&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                 </a>
                 can help them trade in
their Frannie voice and start sounding like a Marilyn in no time at all.
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 The "Ironic" Tee Shirt Wearer:
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-_original" height="480" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/il_430xN.87840957.jpg" title="" width="380"/>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 You know the guy who started listening to Def Leopard
because he thought it was funny? Yeah, this Jewpac shirt is just
what every non-Jewish, rap-hater needs to add to their collection of humorous
(I use the word loosely) shirts. (
                 <u>
                  Update
                 </u>
                 : This shirt is no longer available.)
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 The Guy Who Has to Wear a Chain to Know Where to Stop Shaving:
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-_original" height="155" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/razorba_classic_small.jpg" title="" width="403"/>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Let's face it, back hair, particularly sweater-thick back
hair, is pretty gross. Still, it's not exactly easy to take care of the problem
yourself --until now. Finally someone has mixed the ever-useful back scratcher
with a
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002MUAP2E?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B002MUAP2E" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  hair-removal tool
                 </a>
                 so anyone can have a stubbly, but nude-sweater-free
back. (
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002MUAP2E?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B002MUAP2E" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  Buy here)
                 </a>
                 <b>
                  <br/>
                 </b>
                </p>
                <h3>
                </h3>
                <h3>
                </h3>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                </h3>
                <h3>
                 For Your Creepy Alcoholic Uncle:
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-_original" height="427" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/tacky.JPG" title="" width="290"/>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 You know he's going to drink too much and hit on everyone
not directly related to him this year, but at least you can help him do it in a
festive manner with this
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B006CUJXMI?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B006CUJXMI&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  delightfully tacky treat
                 </a>
                 . Better still, it will help
make everyone a little less uncomfortable, after all, if Santa pees in the
corner, we know it's only because he's so tired after staying up all night
delivering presents.
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 The World's Most Pampered Pooches:
                </h3>
                <h3>
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <span class="inline inline-middle">
                  <img alt="" class="image image-_original" height="315" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/limited-edition_0.jpg" title="" width="300"/>
                 </span>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Remember, just because
your pup smells other dog's butts doesn't mean she wants them to smell her
unscented derriere. Of course, if your dog is this snooty, don't cheap out on
the $50 bottle of
                 <a href="http://www.sexybeaststyle.com/fragrance-le.html" target="_blank">
                  Sexy Beast
                 </a>
                 , you'd better go all the way and buy the $850
limited edition bottle adorned with Swarovski crystals.
                </p>
                <h3>
                 The Perfect Gift For
                 <br/>
                 The Guy Who Thinks He's An Orc:
                </h3>
                <p>
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002ZNPE8S?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B002ZNPE8S&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  <span class="inline inline-middle">
                   <img alt="" class="image image-img_assist_custom" height="338" src="http://f00.inventorspot.com/images/THRP10001.img_assist_custom.jpg" title="" width="450"/>
                  </span>
                 </a>
                </p>
                <p>
                 Whether he loves Lord of the Rings a bit too much or has
actually started believing he is his World of Warcraft avatar, any orc-obsessed
fan boy would be honored to drink out of this
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002ZNPE8S?tag=americaninven-20&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B002ZNPE8S&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  manly stein
                 </a>
                 . Best of all, if he
fills the two-liter drinking vessel with Monster instead of beer, it will give him
just enough energy to get in another 24 hours of gaming.
                </p>
                <h3>
                </h3>
                <p>
                 So what do you think about these gift ideas? Any folks you are having difficulty finding the perfect gift for?
                </p>
