      <h1 class="post-title">

      <p class="post-meta">
       <a href="http://markavery.info/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by Mark">
        Mark
       </a>
       
       <a href="http://markavery.info/" rel="bookmark" title="HOME">
        February 5, 2011
       </a>
       
       <a href="http://markavery.info/#respond">

      </p>
      <p>
       Hi! This is the place to get in touch with me, find out what I am writing about, hire me for speaking engagements or ask me to work for you.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://markavery.info/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Mark-Avery-110409-10.jpg">
        <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-63" height="300" src="http://markavery.info/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Mark-Avery-110409-10.jpg" title="Mark Avery 110409 10" width="241"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Im open to any ideas for work provided it means standing up for nature. I enjoy working with like-minded people who are trying to make the world a better place and against those whose aims will diminish the natural beauty around us.
      </p>
      <p>
       My favourite quotation is that attributed to Edmund Burke  the only thing necessary for the triumph of evil is that good people do nothing.
      </p>
      <p>
       What might I do for you?
      </p>
      <p>
       
       <strong>
        writing
       </strong>
       (opinion pieces, guest blogs, articles on the natural world, articles on the politics that influence nature, book reviews)
      </p>
      <p>
       
       <strong>
        speaking
       </strong>
       (Ill come and talk to your conference, meeting or group)
      </p>
      <p>
       
       <strong>
        hire me as a Reverse Intern
       </strong>
       . Interns usually dont know too much about your business and really want a permanent job  but they are cheap. I know a lot about the environment, the organisations and individuals who influence it, I dont want a permanent job at the moment and I am good value for money. I could help your organisation with its strategy development, management, lobbying, social media work, publicity and a range of other issues.
      </p>
      <p>
       To suggest ideas or find out more then get in touch  email me at
       <strong>
        mark@markavery.info
       </strong>
       .
      </p>
