           <img border="0" height="320" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-z1Rxu2h8B9s/Uaybr4bAo8I/AAAAAAAAdmo/tEIIFwMQMmE/s320/tumblr_m5taquzyKe1rqgmii.jpg" width="236"/>

          Buff Diss threw away the paints when he decided to start decorating the world. Instead he grabbed rolls of multi-color masking tape and his incredible vision.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          His artistic works are created by using narrow strips of tape, a steady hand, and his mental vision. His powerful imagery capture our imagination and leaves us completely in awe. Buff has adorned walls, streets, sidewalks, trains, and barns with his masking tape artwork. His ability to create the feeling of motion and depth with tape is incredible.
