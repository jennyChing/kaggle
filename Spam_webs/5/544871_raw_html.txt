        <p>
         <span id="inserted504" style="font-size: 11px;">
          <em>
           <span id="inserted9628" style="font-size: 12px;">

         <a href="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Daisy-bb-gun-hero-web.png">
          <img alt="Daisy-bb-gun-hero-web" class="aligncenter wp-image-1209 size-full" height="997" src="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Daisy-bb-gun-hero-web.png" width="1000"/>
         </a>
         If youre looking for
         <a href="http://www.daisy.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Cool Christmas Gifts">
          cool Christmas gifts
         </a>
         this holiday season be sure to consider a Daisy BB gun. Daisy makes excellent gifts for boys and girls. They have a large selection of pistol and rifle models that are sure to bring a huge smile to your loved ones face.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Daisy-bb-gun-rifle-gift-christmas-tree-web.png">
          <img alt="Daisy-bb-gun-rifle-gift-christmas-tree-web" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1215" height="664" src="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Daisy-bb-gun-rifle-gift-christmas-tree-web.png" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Growing up I used to enjoy shooting BB guns. My uncle who is an Army veteran introduced me to shooting sports from a young age. He bought me my first Daisy BB gun and taught mehow to safely handle and shoot them. Since then I have continued to enjoy shooting sports and have begun the tradition with my son and nephews.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Daisy-bb-gun-rifle-gift-present-christmas-tree-web.png">
          <img alt="Daisy-bb-gun-rifle-gift-present-christmas-tree-web" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1217" height="1000" src="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Daisy-bb-gun-rifle-gift-present-christmas-tree-web.png" width="664"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         A BB gun is an excellent choice of gift that is sure to provide plenty of bonding time between parents and children. Not only does it encourage children to participate in outdoor activities but it also educates them in safety and discipline.Daisys educational curriculum has been around since 1948 helping to educate shooters of all ages. Daisy educates roughly one million of Americas youth a year through their Ten Lesson Shooting Curriculum about BB gun use and safety (
         <a data-original-title="" href="http://www.daisy.com/forms/Ten_Lesson_Curriculum_for_Shooting_Education_2013.pdf" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Daisy Curriculum">
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <span style="color: #0066cc;">
            found here
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         ).
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Daisy-880-bb-gun-rifle-web.png">
          <img alt="Daisy-880-bb-gun-rifle-web" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1221" height="664" src="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Daisy-880-bb-gun-rifle-web.png" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         My best advice for all shooting sports with younger children and teens is safety first! Going over all safety procedures before the fun begins is key. It is imperative to teach them how to operate it as well as make safe for storing. Safety glasses should always be worn even by spectators. I personally sit closely while the kids are shooting and observe everything they do. I Take frequent pauses to go over how theyre shooting as well as teach them techniques for better accuracy. There is no reason why children shouldnt learn how to safely operate a BB gun. I think it is great sport that teaches children responsibility and respect of property.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Daisy-bb-gun-collage-web.png">
          <img alt="Daisy-bb-gun-collage-web" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1244" height="698" src="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Daisy-bb-gun-collage-web.png" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         As with most of my outdoor purchases, Daisy products are conveniently found at your local
         <a href="http://www.basspro.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Bass Pro Shops">
          Bass Pro Shops
         </a>
         . They carry a large selection of models and safetyaccessories and pellets/BBs. If youre shopping with your child be sure to drop by Santas Wonderland.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Bass-pro-shop-daisy-bb-gun-aisle-web.png">
          <img alt="Bass-pro-shops-daisy-bb-gun-aisle-web" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1212" height="750" src="http://www.theoutdoorboys.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Bass-pro-shop-daisy-bb-gun-aisle-web.png" width="1000"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>

        </p>
