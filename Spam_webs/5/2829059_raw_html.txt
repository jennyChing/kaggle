         <h1 class="blog-h1">
          Five Tips For Using Mirrors In Interior Design
         </h1>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="grid_8">
        <img src="http://www.sarahwardassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Screenshot-2014-01-15-20.33.42-455x296.png"/>
       </div>
       <div class="grid_12">
        <p>
         I often use mirrors to add
         <em>
          an extra dimension
         </em>
         to a property Im designing.Properly chosen and positioned, they can create light, an illusion of space, evoke a particular atmosphere and reinforce a style.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are
         <strong>
          five different ways for using mirrors in interior design
         </strong>
          if youve any to add please leave a comment below or tweet me @SarahWardAssoc
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 630px">
         <a href="http://li.zoocdn.com/0442c0ab16ce8924280dfc6fe431059135ac570b_645_430.jpg">
          <img alt="mirrors used in hallway for interior design " height="430" src="http://li.zoocdn.com/0442c0ab16ce8924280dfc6fe431059135ac570b_645_430.jpg" width="620"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Mirrors are an effective way of adding light and depth to an area
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          If you want to double the light you have in a room (either natural or artificial), put a mirror next to a light so that it reflects out. I particularly like doing this on each side of a bed  look out for mirrored wall sconces for bedsides which remove the need for a bedside lamp and so give you more room available on a night stand.
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_971" style="width: 554px">
           <a href="http://www.sarahwardassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/v.jpg">
            <img alt="mirror used in hallway " class=" wp-image-971 " height="819" src="http://www.sarahwardassociates.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/v-680x1024.jpg" width="544"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Use a mirror to add light to a dark hallway
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          Remember that a mirror is going to reflect whatever is in front of it so make sure that its something attractive. Avoid putting a mirror by an uninspiring view for just that reason  think about how you can dress up the reflected area if needs be.
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 490px">
           <a href="http://www.homeworkshop.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Antique-Window-Dresses-Up-Plain-Brick-Wall.jpg">
            <img alt="mirror used in garden " class=" " height="360" src="http://www.homeworkshop.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/Antique-Window-Dresses-Up-Plain-Brick-Wall.jpg" width="480"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Actually its a mirror 
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Mirrors can be used to good effect in a garden to add depth. Frame with some trellis to add an illusion of walking into another space  a bit like Alice in Wonderland.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 630px">
           <a href="http://li.zoocdn.com/a089a44e2791085b72f081f850259c82a15c902d_645_430.jpg">
            <img alt="mirror used in sitting room for interior design " height="410" src="http://li.zoocdn.com/a089a44e2791085b72f081f850259c82a15c902d_645_430.jpg" width="620"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            A mirror is going to reflect whatever is in front of it  so make sure its something attractive
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          If youre planning a mirrored feature wall and want to add art, paintings or shelves to it then youll need to make a template for the mirror cutter. He will then predrill the holes so that you can put the extra fixings in place.
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px">
           <a href="http://www.sellingantiques.co.uk/photosnew/dealer_jasperjacks/antique-french-mirror-162690.jpg">
            <img alt="antique French mirror " height="763" src="http://www.sellingantiques.co.uk/photosnew/dealer_jasperjacks/antique-french-mirror-162690.jpg" width="500"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            A beautiful mirror will become a work of art in its own right
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
         </li>
         <li>
          A framed mirror will become a piece of art  there are some amazingly decorative surrounds to choose from. By contrast an unframed mirror adds a certain dynamic to a space. If youre looking for something with a frame youll find some stunning antique mirrors in the Fulham Road, on eBay and in markets. Or anywhere from Ikea to the
          <a href="http://chelseadesignquarter.co.uk/" target="_blank">
           Chelsea Design Quarter
          </a>
          , depending on your budget and what youre searching for.
