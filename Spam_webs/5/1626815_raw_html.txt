                <p>
                 In efforts to drum up some interest for a new reality dating show, they sent out a Danish model with a Chanel shirt so tight it looks like it was painted on. Oh waitit is painted on. Literally.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The tall, blond bombshell hit the one of the busiest streets in Frederiksberg which would be equivalent to walking down Times Square in New York.
                </p>
                <p>
                 The Danish TV company who produced the commercial said that they wanted to make some noise during a summer of new shows. Their producer Trine Frovin said,
                </p>
                <blockquote>
                 <p>
                  We decided to think less as TV people and more like advertising and do something different.My producer Stine Stahr came up with this great idea.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  The exact content of the show has yet to be revealed, but I can say that we want people to date in a new and fair way where the focus is on the personality  not looks, appearance or personal branding.
                 </p>
                 <p>
                  The girl in the video is a model because, although we are casting for ordinary people, we needed someone bold enough to take a stroll one of the busiest streets of Copenhagen.
                 </p>
                 <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
                 </div>
                </blockquote>
                <p>
                 With 321K views in just one day I would say, mission accomplished.
