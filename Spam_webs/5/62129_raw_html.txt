      <p>
       Peanuts and almonds can pose the possibility of dangerous anaphylactic allergic reactions in Meghan Weingarth.
      </p>
      <p>
       Food labels do not always tell the whole story, and if equipment used to prepare nuts is also used for other food, these traces can be deadly to little girls like Meghan. This is just where LilyBelle comes in.
      </p>

       This cute Goldendoodle is able to sniff out the tiniest traces of almonds or peanuts in the food Meghan is going to eat. The training LilyBelle received helps keep Meghan safe.
