          <h1 class="entry-title">
           Excuses to get out of mowing the lawn
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          <div class="meta-info-wrap">
           <span class="sep posted-on">
            Posted on
           </span>
           <a href="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/excuses-get-mowing-the-lawn/" rel="bookmark" title="7:08 am">
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2013-11-21T07:08:16+00:00">
             November 21, 2013
            </time>
           </a>
           <span class="by-author">
            <span class="sep">
             by
            </span>
            <span class="author vcard">
             <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Mark">

        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content cf">
         <p>
          Theres been a bit of rain around old Brissy town of late. Not a lot mind you. The odd storm here and there, a bit of thunder and lightning and wet stuff falling from the sky. Its a disaster I tell you, for the grass is growing again and soon Ill need to be out mowing the lawn and Im not a happy chappy.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/lawn-mowing-2.jpg">
           <img alt="mowing the lawn 1" class="wp-image-5729 aligncenter" height="270" src="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/lawn-mowing-2.jpg" width="375"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Ive done everything that the climate scientists told me to do. Ive gone out and left on lights, air conditioners keeping an empty house clean and TVs happily entertaining themselves. I drive even the shortest of distances to increase the CO2 in the atmosphere. I eat meat to encourage the further breeding of cattle so that even more greenhouse methane will be bottom burped. I do all these things because they tell me that it will lead to drought and still it rains and the grass grows. Whats a man to do?
         </p>
         <p>
          Fortunately Ive married well, as The Receptionist and I both have a healthy disdain for gardening. But as much as we despise it we hate snakes more,so occasionally the grass has to be cut. The various tax deductions weve produced over the years are no help.
          <em>
           God Bless em.
          </em>
          Our genetic dislike of working in the yard has been passed onto them so they are no help.
         </p>
         <p>
          When it all boils down, the buck stops with me and reluctantly Ill fire up the old Victa Silver Streak 4 Stroke when absolutely there are no other options. But everyone knows that there are times when you just cant face mowing the lawn, even when you know you should. For these moments you can borrow any of my ready made excuses **
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
            The mower just wont start  I think it might be the spark plug
           </span>
           Of course its the spark plug! Youve already taken it out. This ploy works best on a Sunday when mower shops are closed.
          </li>
          <li>
           
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
            Looks like a bad storms on the way
           </span>
            For this to work make sure youve got a screen save of a really black radar image from the BOM site. Works best if cloudy but if desperate can be tried with blue skies above
          </li>
          <li>
           
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
            Cmon, Ill shout you lunch
           </span>
            Yes, it might cost a few dollars but can you really put a price on getting out of mowing the lawn
          </li>
          <li>
           
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
            I cant find the sunscreen
           </span>
            No one will expect you to mow if theres a risk of developing skin cancer. Of course you cant find out, you threw it out three days ago.
          </li>
          <li>
           
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
            My tummy hurts
           </span>
            You might be labelled a sook and a knob but at least you wont be mowing the lawn
          </li>
          <li>
           
           <span style="font-family: 'comic sans ms', sans-serif;">
            The dog ate the mower
           </span>
            Granted this is a long shot and at least half the time wont work. To maximise chances of success, rub oil around your dogs mouth.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/lawn-mowing-1.jpg">
           <img alt="mowing the lawn 1" class="wp-image-5730 aligncenter" height="270" src="http://www.fullhalfglass.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/lawn-mowing-1.jpg" width="390"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          ** Excuses come with no guarantee of success.
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           If you have your own excuses please add to this list
          </em>
         </p>
