   <h1>
    What Is Nature Radio?
   </h1>
   <p>
    Nature Radio is an internet radio station that specializes in playing ambient, new age and enviromental genres. More specifically, nature sounds such as ocean waves, birdsong, rain, thunderstorms and more; as well as relaxation and meditation music with nature sounds mixed in. Nature Radio is 100% commercial free. That means, you will never hear any commercials in our stream.
   </p>
   <h1>
    How Can Listening To Nature Radio Help Me?
   </h1>
   <p>
    Listening to Nature Radio can benefit you in many different ways. Here are just some of the amazing things listening to Nature Radio can help you with:
   </p>
   <ol>
    <li>
     <strong>
      Reducing Stress:
     </strong>
     - Listening to soothing sounds of nature and music that relaxes your body and elevates your mind can significantly reduce how much stress you feel. Relaxation music helps you connect to your inner peace. When you quiet your thoughts and feel your source of peace within, it's like having a stress-proof field around you. Reducing stress not only makes you feel much better, it can also bring improved health.
    </li>
    <li>
     <strong>
      Relax Your Body:
     </strong>
     - Whether you're enjoying a soothing hot bath at home, a visit to a spa, an acupuncture treatment, a deep massage, a Reiki session or sitting quietly in a garden or in nature, you will benefit more when you are deeply relaxed. Listening to nature sounds and healing music can help you let go and surrender to relaxation, it can inspire you as well, resulting in a deeper experience.
    </li>
    <li>
     <strong>
      Healing, Renewal:
     </strong>
     - It has been proven that when the body and mind are relaxed, the body's natural ability to heal itself is greatly enhanced. By listening to deeply relaxing sounds of nature and music, you can assist your body to release its healing powers. Studies have shown positive benefits when patients listen to relaxing sounds before and after surgery and in hospice settings. in. Studies have also shown that people who listen to soothing music have requested less pain medication after surgery and that relaxation music can also help them manage chronic pain.
    </li>
    <li>
     <strong>
      Balance Your Life:
     </strong>
     - In these busy times, it can be difficult to create a sense of balance in our lives. By listening to natural sounds and relaxation music, you can create a zone of peace that allows you to experience each day from a place of alignment with your true nature.
    </li>
    <li>
     <strong>
      Sleep Better:
     </strong>
     - Many of our listeners find that listening to Nature Radio before retiring or as they drift off to sleep allows them to let go of the day's worries, quiet their thoughts and fall asleep easier. The soothing sleep music helps to re-establish a connection to your sense of inner peace and allows you to feel completely secure and enjoy deeper, more restful sleep.
    </li>
    <li>
     <strong>
      Soothing Babies and Young Children:
     </strong>
     - Nature sounds and music can be a big help for parents at naptimes and in the evenings before bed, with its soothing effect on infants and children. Having children ourselves, we have first hand experience with and have seen the positive effects for children. It is a well known proven fact that ocean sounds and white noise can help calm young child or baby, this is why many toys have these kind of sounds built in.
    </li>
    <li>
     <strong>
      Work, Reading and Studying:
     </strong>
    </li>
    <li>
     <strong>
      Inspiration and Thought:
     </strong>
     - When you start the day filled with gratitude and inner peace, the rest of your day unfolds in a more harmonious way. Listening to Nature Radio in the morning can help put you in a peaceful mood that will stay with you throughout the day. It is also proven that thought processes are much more fluid and more powerful when you are relaxed and in a state of calm.
    </li>
   </ol>
