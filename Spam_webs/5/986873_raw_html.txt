        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/candy-bar-cookies.png">
          <img alt="Candy Bar Cookies - OH so good!" class="aligncenter wp-image-14651" height="560" src="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/candy-bar-cookies.png" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I love to bake from scratch, but sometimes I dont have the time or just simply dont want to. Thats when these Candy Bar Cookies come in handy. Guaranteed to please  Candy Bar Cookies will surely become one or your favorites!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Made famous by my sister-in-law at family gatherings, this simple and fun to make bar cookie can be made by using whatever candy
         <em>
          bar
         </em>
         or
         <em>
          bars
         </em>
         you have on hand.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6265.jpg">
          <img alt="candy bar cookie ingredients" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-7734" height="368" src="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6265-1024x682.jpg" title="bar cookies" width="553"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Today, I decided to make a very kid (and adult) friendly version of this rich cookie bar  M&amp;Ms and Reeses Cups! But, you could use whatever candy bar you like the best.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6268.jpg">
          <img alt="cookie" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-7736" height="368" src="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6268-1024x682.jpg" title="cookie" width="553"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Start with pre-packaged cookie dough and press it into a cookie sheet or 139 pan. Bake for 10 minutes or until brown.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6272.jpg">
          <img alt="candy bar cookie" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-7737" height="368" src="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6272-1024x682.jpg" title="candy bar cookie" width="553"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         While it cools, add a few dollops of peanut butter on top.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6273.jpg">
          <img alt="candy bar cookie" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-7738" height="368" src="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6273-1024x682.jpg" title="candy bar cooki" width="553"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Allow to soften before smoothing all over the top.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6279.jpg">
          <img alt="candy bar cookie" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-7739" height="368" src="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6279-1024x682.jpg" title="candy bar cookie" width="553"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Add crushed up candy bars or M&amp;Ms. Mmmm.. The kids were over the moon with this combination!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6275.jpg">
          <img alt="candy cookie bar" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-7740" height="368" src="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_6275-1024x682.jpg" title="candy cookie bar" width="553"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Ella even gave a little peace sign and a pose when I snapped a picture of her eyeballing the dessert while waiting on it to cool.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_62922.jpg">
          <img alt="candy cookie bar" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-7741" height="368" src="http://allthingsmamma.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_62922-1024x682.jpg" title="candy cookie bar" width="553"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Candy Bar Cookies  oh so good! You will NEED to go back for secondsand thirdsand fourths.!!!!
        </p>
