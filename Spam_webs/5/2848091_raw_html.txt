        <h2>
         Denver Comic Con: May 23, 2015:
        </h2>
        <p>
         The wife and I had a fun time at Denver Comic Con. We went as Mr. and Mrs. Shepard. However, if the truth be told, I often wear Mass Effect gear as my standard issue street clothes! The wife looked just marvelous, and all had fun. My only complaint was that there just seemed to be way too many people! It was hard to concentrate on any one thing due to massive sensory overload. I wanted to assume the memory elimination posture towards the evening.
