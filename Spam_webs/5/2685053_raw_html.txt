            <h2 class="blog-title">
             <a class="blog-title-link blog-link" href="http://www.meyersautoparts.com/blog/-a-womans-guide-in-choosing-the-right-car">
              A Womans Guide in Choosing the Right Car
             </a>
            </h2>
            <p class="blog-date">
             <span class="date-text">
              1/14/2015 12:24:35
             </span>
            </p>
            <p class="blog-comments">
             <a class="blog-link" href="http://www.meyersautoparts.com/blog/-a-womans-guide-in-choosing-the-right-car#comments">
              3 Comments
             </a>
            </p>
           </div>
           <div class="blog-separator">
           </div>
           <div class="blog-content">
            <span class="imgPusher" style="float:left;height:0px">
            </span>
            <span style="display: table;z-index:10;width:auto;position:relative;float:left;max-width:100%;;clear:left;margin-top:0px;*margin-top:0px">
             <a href="http://www.meyersautoparts.com/blog/-a-womans-guide-in-choosing-the-right-car">
              <img alt="Picture" class="galleryImageBorder wsite-image" src="/uploads/1/6/0/1/16016966/8731562.png?254" style="margin-top: 5px; margin-bottom: 10px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 10px; border-width:1px;padding:3px; max-width:100%"/>
             </a>
             <span class="wsite-caption" style="display: table-caption; caption-side: bottom; font-size: 90%; margin-top: -10px; margin-bottom: 10px; text-align: center;">
             </span>
            </span>
            <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:justify;display:block;">
             <span style="">
              When buying cars, it is normal for anyone to consider the price, financing scheme available, fuel efficiency, aesthetics, car maintenance and brand loyalty. Car buying tips have been available everywhere and it seems like they mostly focus on how you can get the best discount on the sales price and how to get more freebies.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <span style="">
              As a woman, we often wonder if salesmen would treat us any differently than if it is a man who is considering buying a car. The reality now is, yes, there are still salesmen out there who would probably take advantage and assume that a woman does not know the first thing about buying cars.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
            </div>
            <hr style="width:100%;clear:both;visibility:hidden;"/>
            <div>
             <!--BLOG_SUMMARY_END-->
            </div>
            <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
             <span style="">
              But are there really things that women should know before buying a car? Are there exclusive car buying tips for women? The answer to these questions is NOT REALLY. There should be literally not much difference whether a man or a woman is trying to purchase a vehicle. But to avoid getting ripped off on sales negotiation, you better study and make your research first.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <strong>
              <span style="">
               What should you prepare for, before visiting your nearest dealership?
              </span>
              <br/>
             </strong>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <span mso-fareast-font-family:symbol="" style="">
              
             </span>
             <span line-height:115="" style="">
              Your preferred car type. If it is for family use, then you might consider an SUV, if for personal use, then a compact car or sedan is a good choice. But if you want a car that will grow with you and your future needs, then you might consider a model that will suit every need.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <span mso-fareast-font-family:symbol="" style="">
              
             </span>
             <span line-height:115="" style="">
              Decide if you want function over form or vice versa. Some cars do not look very sleek, but offer great overall performance.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <span mso-fareast-font-family:symbol="" style="">
              
             </span>
             <span line-height:115="" style="">
              Check out promos via the internet, there are usually specific months of the year, where dealerships offer a lot of freebies and discount so you can choose the perfect timing.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <span mso-fareast-font-family:symbol="" style="">
              
             </span>
             <span line-height:115="" style="">
              Compare prices and offers. This will help you in haggling with your sales agent once you are decided already.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <strong>
              <span style="">
               What are the other things that women should know about cars?
              </span>
              <br/>
             </strong>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <span mso-fareast-font-family:verdana="" style="">
              1.
              <span style="">
              </span>
             </span>
             <span line-height:115="" style="">
              Maintenance Cost
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span line-height:115="" style="">
              A lot of times, when we are getting our first car, we fail to ask about how much it would cost for the regular car maintenance. The bigger your car gets, the higher your expenses. So include that in your yearly budget.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <span line-height:115="" style="">
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <span mso-fareast-font-family:verdana="" style="">
              2.
              <span style="">
              </span>
             </span>
             <span line-height:115="" style="">
              After Sales Service
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <span line-height:115="" style="">
              When you are buying a car, your sales agent would probably be always around to answer all your questions and take care of your needs. But once the deal is sealed, we are now confronted with after sales service. If you dealerships customer support is poor, then you will have difficulties in getting the most out your vehicle and dealing with problems when there are technical issues.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <span mso-fareast-font-family:verdana="" style="">
              3.
              <span style="">
              </span>
             </span>
             <span line-height:115="" style="">
              Car Insurance
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <span line-height:115="" style="">
              Getting a car also means yearly insurance cost. This means additional add-on expenses for you. This is actually considered as car maintenance cost as well.
             </span>
             <br/>
             <br/>
             <span style="">
             </span>
             <br/>
