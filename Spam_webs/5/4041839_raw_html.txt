       <h1 class="the-title">
        8 Weeks to 10k Challenge  Write the Words That Sell and Solo Ads
       </h1>
       <div class="single-post-content cf">
        <p>
         <a href="http://simplifiedmlm.com/wp-content/uploads/MR900422730.jpg">
          <img alt="Social Networking" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-590" height="96" src="http://simplifiedmlm.com/wp-content/uploads/MR900422730.jpg" width="96"/>
         </a>
         <span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: medium;">
          Follow our weekly summary of Jose Copes myMLMFormula8 Weeks to 10k Challenge training sessions to learn the techniques that leverage the raw power of the Internet in an active and effective manner that is growing our prospects list for our home based business.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          <strong>
           Session 4:
          </strong>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: medium; color: #0000ff;">
          How To Write The Words That Will Tell, Sell, And Even Sponsor New Reps For You Automatically
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          The session presented the 4 rules for writing ads that will sell your products and services.
         </span>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Know your target market intimately and give them what they want by highlighting theirs pains, problems and desires
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Be different  check the competition to see what they left out of their ad and lead with value to interrupt the normal pattern
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Features Tell, Benefits Sell  talk about the feel of raw power and not the 500 HP Fuel injected engine
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Tell exactly what action to take
          </span>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          <strong>
           Session 5:
          </strong>
         </span>
         <span style="font-size: medium; color: #0000ff;">
          Special Review Session
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          This was a review of the previous 4 sessions and then went into some depth on the myMLMFormulablog: specifically setting up About and Contact pages and how to publish pages and posts.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          <strong>
           Session 6:
          </strong>
         </span>
         <span style="color: #0000ff; font-size: medium;">
          How To Use Solo Ads To Get A Flood Of Targeted Leads, Sales, And New Reps For Your Business
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          A Solo Ad is an email ad sent to people subscribed to specific email lists of specific interest. A solo Ad is the fastest way to build a prospects list but is not a guarantee of generating leads.
          <br/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          Solo Ad checklist:
         </span>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Send Solo Ads to a proven email list
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Subject must be relevant
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Subject must offer huge and relevant benefit or invoke the readers curiosity to entice the reader to open the email
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Open with a strong call to action and a link
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Make the email short and to the point (200 to 300 words)
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Use a friendly, 1st person, writing style
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Continue call to action in body of email 2 or 3 times
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Imply scarcity when possible
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Ensure congruity of the subject, body, and call to action
          </span>
         </li>
         <li>
          <span style="font-size: medium;">
           Keep spam words out of the ad
          </span>
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          All training participants were asked to select a Solo Ads package and the Solo Ads will be going out early next week.
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: medium;">
          We cant wait to see the results!
