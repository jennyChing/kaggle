          You may choose from pallet of engine choices: two V6 engines and the optional Eco Boost four-cylinder. The Edge provides a sportier handling than some of the rivals from the class.
         </p>
         <p>
          The steering-wheel is a little too heavy but very accurate, at the same time.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/carwikipedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/060214_1637_Qualitycomf1.jpg?w=680"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          2014 Ford Edge has an upscale interior, made with high-quality materials, with plenty of space for the front seats.
         </p>
         <p>
          For your safety the car has been equipped with stability control systems, side airbags, front airbags and full-length side airbags and tire pressure monitoring system.
         </p>
         <p>
          The passenger space is large and the cargo space allows 32.2 cubic feet with the rear seats up and 68.9 cubes when folding the down.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/carwikipedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/060214_1637_Qualitycomf2.jpg?w=680"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          The Standard Ford Edge has MyFord Touch system, auxiliary jack input and six-speaker stereo. If you want more than you can also choose for
          <span style="color: black;">
           leather
          </span>
          interior,
          <span style="color: black;">
           10-way power adjustable passenger seat, heated front seats
          </span>
          , rear-view
          <span style="color: black;">
           camera. For more technology you may also choose for
          </span>
          Fords SYNC system (which provides Blue-tooth, USB port, voice commands for compatible music devices and mobile devices), 12-speaker Sony stereo system, dual-zone automatic climate control, optional safety option which includes forward collision warning and adaptive cruise control, which are available with the up-scaled versions.
         </p>
         <p>
          There are also included in the optional list a power lift-gate, a blind sport warning system with cross traffic alert, a navigation system and a panoramic vista sunroof.
         </p>
