      <h2>
       Taking a Closer Look at Search Engine Optimisation
      </h2>
      <div class="post-6 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-search-engine-optimisation" id="post-6">
       <p>
        In the internet world, Search Engine Optimisation or also known as
        <a href="http://perthseocompany.net/" target="_blank">
         SEO is the process of enhancing the visibility of any chosen website in a search engine
        </a>
        . SEO is different from the search ads we see along with our results; SEO is free. It is the process of inviting traffic to a website or page with the help of unpaid, organic search results from search engines.
       </p>
       <p>
        Whenever we search for something on internet, we type words into the search box of a search engine and then we get a number of results. These results are not just a mere set of random content available; they are ranked based on which the search engine consider as the most appropriate, helpful and reliable. So, the goal of SEO professionals is to manipulate these rankings, results, and traffic according to their needs or their clients needs.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://perthseocompany.net/">
         <img alt="Search Engine Optimisation" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-7 alignleft" height="200" src="http://getlinksindir.info/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/aa-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        The process of SEO is not as simple as it sounds as this job requires professional skills. This job is usually managed by individuals or set of individuals who use SEO tactics and methodologies effective to manipulating the search results. There are two main priorities of SEO professionals  production of good content and using credible links.
       </p>
       <p>
        SEO gurus want to produce quality, relevant and engaging content which doesnt only grab the readers attention but also motivates them to refer it to their friends and family. They produce content that is user-friendly and easy to read with precise information that fulfils all the readers needs.
       </p>
       <p>
        On the other hand, using credible links is also one of the main strategies of SEO. These experts ensure the relevance and activity of all the links they used in their site before launching it on the internet.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
