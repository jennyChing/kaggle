  <h2 style="margin-top:113px;position:absolute;left:260px;color:#333399;font-size:20px;">
   Euratlas World Atlas
  </h2>
  <p style="background:white;color:black;padding:6px;position:absolute;left:756px;top:170px; width:260px;line-height:18px;">
   Euratlas is a website mainly dedicated to the history and geography of Europe but it offers also a world atlas in order to offer a comprehensive view of geography.
   <br/>
   <br/>
   <br/>
   Through the thumbnails, here on the left, you will get access to clickable lists of the mountains, rivers and countries of the world. Moreover, if you subscribe to the
   <a href="http://info.euratlas.net/index.html">
    Euratlas-Info
   </a>
   newsletter, you will be entitled to download several free printable world maps. These maps are provided in color or black/white, labelled or blank.
   <br/>
   <br/>
   <br/>
   <br/>
   <br/>
   <br/>
   <br/>
   <br/>
   <br/>
   <br/>
   <i>
    If you want to view other world atlases, you have also access to third party websites offering good and reliable maps. Click on the thumbnails, here on the left.
   </i>
