       <h1 class="postTitle">
        What Should You Do with Your Loved Ones Ashes?
       </h1>
       <div class="blogMeta">
        November 12th, 2014 | By  | Posted in
        <a href="http://www.losangelescremation.net/category/uncategorized/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Uncategorized">
         Uncategorized
        </a>
        |
       </div>
       <div class="theContent">
        <p>
         <img align="right" alt="" src="http://www.losangelescremation.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/funeral-3.jpg" width="300px"/>
         <br/>
         Are you trying to figure out what to do with the ashes of a loved one who was recently cremated? With traditional burial, there isnt much choice in what you do with a loved ones remains. But with cremation, you have a number of great choices. Here are a few things to consider before finding a permanent home for a loved ones ashes.
        </p>
        <h2>
         What to Do with the Ashes
        </h2>
        <p>
         1. Was your family member an integral part of your household and beloved by all? If so, leaving your loved ones remains in an urn or box in your home is a great way to pay respect and show your love for the deceased. Being in such close proximity to your loved one will help you feel their presence even after their passing.
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Did your loved one have a favorite spot or activity? If so, spreading their ashes in a favorite location can be the ultimate act of consideration and care. Whether you spread their ashes in a favorite lake or you take them to the top of a favorite mountain and spread them in the wind, allowing them to become part of their favorite place is a great choice.
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Did you know that you can have your loved ones ashes turned into jewelry? There are a few companies who take cremated remains and turn them into diamonds for rings, necklaces, and other pieces of jewelry.
        </p>
