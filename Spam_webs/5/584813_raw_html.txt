     </div>
     <div class="span-16 last">
      <div class="column span-7 ">
       <p>
        The Delaunay Raster is an original graphical process I invented in 2008, to create abstracted versions of images using the Delaunay Triangulation, color averaging and hand assisted placement of points.
       </p>
       Delaunay Raster was developed using
       <a href="http://scriptographer.com" target="_blank">
        Scriptographer
       </a>
       and color averaging by
       <a href="http://lehni.org" target="_blank">
        Jurg Lehni
       </a>
       .
      </div>
      <div class="column span-7 ">
       <p>
        Please note that the tool is not currently for sale to the general public and any other implementation of the technique has been developed without my approval unless otherwise noted.
       </p>
       <p>
        I have chosen not to make the Delaunay Raster open-source, because I am able to use it to fund my studio and open source work for projects like
        <a href="http://scriptographer.com" target="_blank">
         Scriptographer
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://paperjs.org" target="_blank">
         Paper.js
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Therefore I would also hope that developers and illustrators have the common decency not to implement / copy this technology / technique using the technology du-jour on the gadget du-jour without getting my permission.
       </p>
       Why not make something new instead? Innovate!
      </div>
     </div>
