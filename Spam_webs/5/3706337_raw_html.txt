      <h2>
       Evidence-based wellness
      </h2>
      <p>
       The TM technique has been learned by over 6 million people, and over 380
       <a class="research fancybox.iframe" href="/learn/popups/research.html">
        published studies
       </a>
       have found it highly effective for stress, anxiety, insomnia, hypertension, and more.
      </p>

      <div class="accordion">
       <a class="toggle">
        <span>
         Can I learn TM from a book or video?
        </span>
       </a>
       <div>
        <p>
         Imagine trying to learn a natural golf swing or the violin. You know how valuable it is for a good teacher to show you the proper technique.
        </p>
        <p>
         The TM technique is easy to learn, but requires personalized interactive guidance. For this reason, its taught only through
         <a href="/learn-tm">
          one-on-one instruction
         </a>
         by a certified TM teacher.
        </p>
        <p>
         There is no other way to learn the authentic TM technique  and there is no evidence that anything else provides the full range of benefits documented in the
         <a class="research fancybox.iframe" href="/learn/popups/research.html">
          published research
         </a>
         on the TM technique.
        </p>
       </div>
       <a class="toggle">
        <span>
         Local cities we serve
        </span>
       </a>
       <div>
        <p>
         the Indianapolis area.
        </p>
       </div>
       <a class="toggle">
        <span>
         What is the TM technique?
        </span>
       </a>
       <div>
        <p>
         Its a simple, natural technique practiced 20 minutes twice each day while sitting comfortably with the eyes closed.
        </p>
        <p>
         The TM technique is easy to learn and enjoyable to practice, and is not a religion, philosophy, or lifestyle. Over six million people have learned it  people of all ages, cultures, and religions.
        </p>
       </div>
       <a class="toggle">
        <span>
         What happens during TM?
        </span>
       </a>
       <div>
        <p>
         The TM technique allows your mind to
         <em>
          easily
         </em>
         settle inward, through quieter levels of thought, until you experience the most silent and peaceful level of your own awareness  pure consciousness.
        </p>
        <p>
         This
         <a class="video fancybox.iframe" href="/popups-new/roth-intro.html">
          introductory video
         </a>
         provides a clear description of what happens during TM practice.
        </p>
       </div>
       <a class="toggle">
        <span>
         What if Im not good at controlling my mind?
        </span>
       </a>
       <div>
        <p>
         No problem. Unlike other forms of meditation, TM practice involves no concentration, no control of the mind, no contemplation, no monitoring of thoughts.
        </p>
        <p>
         Anyone can do it  even
         <a class="text fancybox.iframe" href="/popups-new/adhd.html">
          children with ADHD
         </a>
         and
         <a class="text fancybox.iframe" href="/popups-new/ptsd.html">
          soldiers with PTSD
         </a>
         .
        </p>
       </div>
       <a class="toggle">
        <span>
         What if Im skeptical?
        </span>
       </a>
       <div>
        <p>
         The TM techniques effectiveness is the same whether you believe it will work or are completely skeptical. Thats because it automatically and effortlessly allows your active thinking mind to settle down to a state of deep inner calm.
        </p>
