       <div class="post-title">
        <h2>
         <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/48-cloud-computing-events-conferences-attend/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to 48 Big Data &amp; Cloud Computing Events and Conferences You Should Attend in 2014 &amp; Beyond">
          48 Big Data &amp; Cloud Computing Events and Conferences You Should Attend in 2014 &amp; Beyond
         </a>
        </h2>
       </div>
       <!--/post-title -->
       <div class="post-date">
        <em>
         On 04.07.14, In
         <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/cloud-computing-news/" rel="category tag">
          Cloud Computing
         </a>
         , by
         <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/author/william/" rel="author" title="Posts by William Toll">
          William Toll
         </a>
        </em>
       </div>
       <p>
        In the technology landscape, attending conferences and events isnt a luxury  its a must. These events are typically held in major cities with technology and innovation hubs like Silicon Valley or Boston or a neutral city with great convention center spaces. These events are packed with some of the worlds brightest minds in the hottest industries, including cloud computing, Big Data, analytics, hosting, and similar fields.
       </p>
       <p>
        Whether youre a developer, data scientist, CIO/CMO or another member of the C-suite, data architect, networking or systems specialist, or a professional in any related IT sector, theres a conference happening this year or next thats packed with valuable resources and information to keep your business ahead of the competition. The following 48 events arent ranked or rated in terms of importance, relevance, or attendees, but represent 48 of the most innovative, opportunity-rich conferences and events happening in the cloud and Big Data space in 2014 and beyond.
       </p>
       <p>
        Big Data and cloud computing are here to stay; its up to you to figure out how to best optimize these technologies and capitalize on them to catapult your company to success. ProfitBricks will be attending or sponsoring or speaking or exhibiting at many events in 2014. What events will you be attending? What events did we not include? Leave a comment if we missed your favorite Cloud Computing or Big Data event.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         1.
         <a href="http://strataconf.com/" target="_blank">
          OReilly Strata Conference
          <br/>
         </a>
        </b>
        February 18-20, 2015
        <br/>
        <b>
         San Jose, CA
         <br/>
        </b>
        <b>
         Big Data
         <br/>
         <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/strata.png">
          <img alt="strata" class="alignnone wp-image-3073" height="226" src="https://blog.profitbricks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/strata.png" width="487"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
        </b>
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
        </b>
        Strata hosts a number of conferences throughout the year, including the OReilly Strata Conference, typically held in February. The essential training and information source for data science and Big Data, Strata provides an abundance of resources for cloud and IT professionals, as well as business leaders, both via in-person conferences and events and webcasts. OReilly also provides a
        <a href="http://oreilly.com/conferences/" target="_blank">
         number of other conferences
        </a>
        for cloud and data technology professionals.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         2.
         <a href="http://www.gigaom.com" target="_blank">
          Gigaom Structure Data
          <br/>
         </a>
        </b>
        March 19-20, 2014
        <br/>
        <b>
         New York, NY
         <br/>
        </b>
        <b>
         Big Data
         <br/>
         <a href="https://blog.profitbricks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/gigaom.png">
          <img alt="gigaom" class="alignnone wp-image-3075" height="226" src="https://blog.profitbricks.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/gigaom.png" width="487"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
        </b>
       </p>

        Boston loves its data conferences, adding the INFORMS Conference to its lineup in 2014. The INFORMS Conference emphasizes business analytics and operations research, two key areas of focus for enterprises entering the Big Data landscape. This years event features keynote speakerThomas H. Davenport, PhD, co-author of
        <em>
         Competing on Analytics
        </em>
        andFounder of the International Institute of Analytics, who talks about the emergence and importance of data products in the new data-driven business model. Davenport pioneered the concept of Competing on Analytics.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         48.
         <a href="http://www.bigdataexchangeusa.com/" target="_blank">
          The Big Data Exchange
         </a>
        </strong>

        <br/>
        <strong>

        <strong>

        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Another event that just commenced, The Big Data Exchange is a forum for thought leaders in information management and technology to exchange ideas and drive innovation in Big Data. This years event emphasized Leveraging Big Data to Improve Company Performance and Increase R.O.I. through Efficient Enterprise Analytics, easily the most sought-after content among data conferences in 2014. If you missed it this year,
        <a href="http://www.bigdataexchangeusa.com/redForms.aspx?eventid=8347&amp;id=389080&amp;FormID=10&amp;frmType=1&amp;m=10610&amp;FrmBypass=False&amp;mLoc=U&amp;SponsorOpt=False&amp;isForm=true" target="_blank">

        Whatever drives you, whether its Big Data, analytics, hosting and networking, or cloud computing, the conference circuit in 2014 is rife with opportunities to advance your professional knowledge, connect with industry-leading vendors providing cutting-edge technologies to advance business goals, and absorb the innovative ideas of the worlds most recognized data scientists and thought leaders.
