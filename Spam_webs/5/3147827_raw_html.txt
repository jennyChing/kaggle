       <h1 class="post-title" itemprop="name">
        <a href="http://www.hersuiteescape.com/guide-to-brussels/" rel="bookmark" title="Guide to Brussels">
         Guide to Brussels
        </a>
       </h1>
      </header>
      <article class="post-content">
       <p>
        So Ive been given this wonderful opportunity to do my exchange in the City of Love  Paris. Before I go on and talk about how amazing Paris is, this post is not about Paris. Ill have a separate post dedicated to Paris. This post is about a city that may be not be as hyped up as other cities like Paris and Rome, but has just as much to offer. Besides, it is just a stone throw away from Paris. So heres a guide to help you start with the planning of your trip to Brussels and it answers the question of what comes to mind when people talk about this lovely city.
       </p>
       <h1>
        Trip Information
       </h1>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Destination
        </strong>
        : From Paris to
        <em>
         Brussels, Belgium
         <span style="color: #ffffff;">
          Guide to Brussels
         </span>
        </em>
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Duration
        </strong>
        : 4 Days
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Date
        </strong>
        : 17th Jan 2014  20th Jan 2014
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Transport
        </strong>
        : iDBUS
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Accommodation
        </strong>
        : airbnb
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Amount Spent
        </strong>
        : Approximately 150 Euros
       </p>
       <p>
        <span id="more-275">
        </span>
       </p>
       <h1>
        Transportation: iDBUS
       </h1>
       <p>
        Amount Spent on Transportation: 18 Euros Nett for return tickets
        <br/>
        Price is inclusive of all taxes and passengers are allowed to bring one piece of luggage.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_278" style="width: 403px">
        <a href="http://www.hersuiteescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Screen-Shot-2014-02-15-at-1.27.44-am.png">
         <img alt="Details" class="size-full wp-image-278" height="298" src="http://www.hersuiteescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/Screen-Shot-2014-02-15-at-1.27.44-am.png" width="393"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Details
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Reason for choice:
        </strong>
        The main reason for choosing this mode of transport is because of how economical it is. There was a promotion going on and thus, we were able to get such low rates for our 5 hour journey to Brussels. Overall, the iDBUS experience was quite good. Although the connection is not very stable, there is wifi provided on the bus. Also, the seats are rather spacious and comfortable. No complaints from me
        <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.hersuiteescape.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
        <span style="color: #ffffff;">
         Guide to Brussels
        </span>
       </p>
       <h1>
        Accommodation: airbnb
       </h1>
       <p>
        Amount spent on accommodation: 56 Euros per night. (The total of 168 Euros for 3 nights is split amongst 3 people)
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_280" style="width: 395px">
        <a href="http://www.hersuiteescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/photo-2-e1392470694303.jpg">
         <img alt="our room - Kitchenette &amp; Bed" class=" wp-image-280 " height="514" src="http://www.hersuiteescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/photo-2-e1392470694303-768x1024.jpg" width="385"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         our room  Kitchenette &amp; Bed
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_281" style="width: 395px">
        <a href="http://www.hersuiteescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/photo-1-e1392470801889.jpg">
         <img alt="our room - living area" class=" wp-image-281 " height="514" src="http://www.hersuiteescape.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/photo-1-e1392470801889-768x1024.jpg" width="385"/>
        </a>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         our room  living area
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Reason for choice:
        </strong>
        I always prefer to use airbnb instead of hostel when I travel. For a start, it is definitely more value for money for me. Most of the time, I am able to find accommodations on airbnb that is about the same price as that of the hostel. Only difference is that it is better furnished and you dont have to share room with 14 or 19 other people.
       </p>
