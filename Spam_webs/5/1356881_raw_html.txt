       <h1 class="entry-title single-title">
        <span>
         Celebrate Valentines with Color of the Year
        </span>
       </h1>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-media">
      </div>
      <div class="wrapper">
       <div class="grids">
        <div class="grid-8">
         <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content">
          <div class="at-above-post addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide" data-title="Celebrate Valentines with Color of the Year" data-url="http://www.aboutrubyjewelry.com/valentines-day/celebrate-valentines-with-color-of-the-year">
          </div>
          <div class="at-above-post-recommended addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide">
          </div>
          <p align="justify">
           <strong>
            Valentines
           </strong>
           month is here with new possibilities and hopes. Not only the day, but the whole month before and after
           <strong>
            February 14
           </strong>
           unwinds an air of love and romance among the young and old. The coming of Valentines festivities and the announcement of Pantone Color of the Year as Marsala have brought out excellent opportunities of styling and accessorizing, especially for female valentines.
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <img alt="Celebrate Valentines with Color of the Year" border="0" height="532" src="http://www.aboutrubyjewelry.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Celebrate-Valentines-with-Color-of-the-Year.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; border-top: 0px; margin-right: auto; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="Celebrate Valentines with Color of the Year" width="690"/>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           Usually, women are quite puzzled about how to dress up for their valentines evening. Wishing to look the most gorgeous in the company of our soul mate, we end up searching the whole web for a lovely color, a lovable outfit, and classy accessories. This year, the muted red Marsala has laid out many fabulous options in front of all young and pretty girls. You can enjoy the best-ever date, with loads of compliments coming your way.
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           Here are few tips to include this tasteful and tender red to your
           <strong>
            Valentines Day
           </strong>
           attire. You will be the wine for him 
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <strong>
            Clothing:
           </strong>
           Marsala is an extremely variegated shade that lets you pick up bright, pastel, textured, metallic, and leathered colors of red. Look for rich and royal textures and fabrics; you may have one in your wardrobe. Brown and burgundy dresses can also be a great selection. They can be chic contrasts with vivid tones.
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <img alt="Marsala Colors Clothing Fashion Style" border="0" height="693" src="http://www.aboutrubyjewelry.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Marsala-Colors-Clothing-Fashion-Style.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; border-top: 0px; margin-right: auto; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="Marsala Colors Clothing Fashion Style" width="690"/>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <strong>
            Nail Enamel and Makeup:
           </strong>
           Glamorous and
           <b>
           </b>
           classy, brick red or reddish-brown nail colors are way too inviting with any colored outfit. Make a flattering statement with a scarlet matte lipstick
           <b>
           </b>
           and pair it up with dusky black eye makeup. You are certainly going to turn heads in the party (please dont make your man enter into a wrangle with others present in the party).
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <img alt="Nail Enamel and Makeup" border="0" height="693" src="http://www.aboutrubyjewelry.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Nail-Enamel-and-Makeup.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; border-top: 0px; margin-right: auto; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="Nail Enamel and Makeup" width="690"/>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <strong>
            Hair Color:
           </strong>
           Marsala can bring in luxury and urbanity in your look. Color your hair streaks into a rich burgundy, cherry or auburn hair dye, adding a funky glow to your appearance.
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <img alt="Marsala Hair Colors" border="0" height="695" src="http://www.aboutrubyjewelry.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Marsala-Hair-Colors.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; border-top: 0px; margin-right: auto; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="Marsala Hair Colors" width="690"/>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <strong>
            Jewelry:
           </strong>
           Women are really fortunate in jewelry section as they can cherish the sparkle of rubies, ruddiness of garnet, and the brilliance of red diamonds, connecting with the latest fashion of the year.
           <a href="http://www.angara.com/">
            Click on this jewelry shopping link
           </a>
           to get diverse and contemporary collections of
           <strong>
            ruby
           </strong>
           and
           <strong>
            garnet
           </strong>
           blings.
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <img alt="Marsala Colors Jewelry Fashion Style" border="0" height="431" src="http://www.aboutrubyjewelry.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Marsala-Colors-Jewelry-Fashion-Style.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; border-left: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; margin-left: auto; border-top: 0px; margin-right: auto; border-right: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="Marsala Colors Jewelry Fashion Style" width="690"/>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           <a href="http://www.aboutrubyjewelry.com/celebrity-fashion/spring-2015-pantone-color-of-the-year" title="Spring 2015: Pantone Color of the Year">
            Celebrate a Marsala-filled Valentines
           </a>
           with your partner and turn up the romance between. Share pictures of your Marsala-love day. Looks like a delicious season of love is about to come!
          </p>
