                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       After the great first day tour, it's now time to move on to our 2nd day of the tour with the same boatman and Bong our tour guide.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                        FIRST STOP: KAYANGAN LAKE
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       This place probably is the most famous attraction in Coron among the other destinations around the Island. We stayed here the whole morning as we enjoyed the clear waters of Kayangan Lake.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-PSlabS5S9do/UYWvNUdrB9I/AAAAAAAADtg/ggMzZJt-iiA/s1600/P4071481.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="300" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-PSlabS5S9do/UYWvNUdrB9I/AAAAAAAADtg/ggMzZJt-iiA/s400/P4071481.JPG" width="400"/>
                       </a>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       Halfway to your destination after climbing a few steps you will be taken to a place just on your right to get this magnificent view of the drop-off area to get to the lake.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-2tiogZNhoN0/UYWwNcj8m5I/AAAAAAAADuA/gauvGg9mdAc/s1600/P4071486.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                        <img border="0" height="300" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-2tiogZNhoN0/UYWwNcj8m5I/AAAAAAAADuA/gauvGg9mdAc/s400/P4071486.JPG" width="400"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       There is also a cave right where the viewing area is located. I went inside and it was creepy. I would really like to explore inside a little more. That I will save for next time.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-j-QDYWWE27E/UYWvJG_waxI/AAAAAAAADtY/Igdh7stl4Lw/s1600/P4071492.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                        <img border="0" height="300" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-j-QDYWWE27E/UYWvJG_waxI/AAAAAAAADtY/Igdh7stl4Lw/s400/P4071492.JPG" width="400"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       The water in Kayangan Lake is not very salty. The salinity of water is not that strong. It is also considered to be one of the cleanest lake in the Philippines.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-pj2wIYE-7pM/UYWyGrG0tWI/AAAAAAAADuk/tK80ycxztVQ/s1600/P4071692.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="300" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-pj2wIYE-7pM/UYWyGrG0tWI/AAAAAAAADuk/tK80ycxztVQ/s400/P4071692.JPG" width="400"/>
                       </a>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       I love swimming and wanted to go underwater as much as possible since I was using an underwater camera.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-KbLLZH89F8A/UYWwK4CXiNI/AAAAAAAADt4/8zNjbdjohik/s1600/P4071500.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                        <img border="0" height="300" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-KbLLZH89F8A/UYWwK4CXiNI/AAAAAAAADt4/8zNjbdjohik/s400/P4071500.JPG" width="400"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-WuDXMLby_wQ/UYWv1LtJKLI/AAAAAAAADto/OC19FELBt0A/s1600/P4071592.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <span style="font-family: Helvetica Neue, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;">
                        <img border="0" height="400" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-WuDXMLby_wQ/UYWv1LtJKLI/AAAAAAAADto/OC19FELBt0A/s400/P4071592.JPG" width="300"/>
                       </span>
                      </a>
