      <h1 class="xxlarge">
       25 Amazing DIY Hanging Table Ideas
      </h1>
      <p class="postmetadata">
       <a href="http://www.architectureartdesigns.com/25-amazing-diy-hanging-table-ideas/#respond">
        <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="21874 http://www.architectureartdesigns.com/?p=21874">
         0 comments
        </span>
       </a>
      </p>
      <div class="post-content">
       <p>
        Make creative piece of furniture for your home. Hanging table for the dining room or garden. Swing tables are a quick and simple DIY tables that can be attached to trees, overhangs, gazebos, under porches or freely hang anywhere. Also you can make hanging table for your dining room. Hanging tables are great way to save space and its the perfect conversation piece for any indoor or outdoor party. All you need is plywood, drill and rope or wire. You can decorate the table with flowers or candles. We present you 25 beautiful and romantic hanging tables. Get inspired!
