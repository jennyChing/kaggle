        <p>
         Occupying the top three floors of the west tower of a famous London landmark, St Pancras Penthouse Apartment by
         <a href="http://www.tg-studio.co.uk" target="_blank">
          TG Studio
         </a>
         is a charming eclectic home. Initially discovered by Freshome on
         <a href="http://interiorzine.com/2013/04/23/personal-and-luxurious-home/">
          Interiorzine
         </a>
         , the project consisted of a massive redesign and as expected, the creative team faced quite a few challenges: 
         <em>
          As the property is of highest public interest and the interiors listed by English Heritage as very significant, the floor plan had to be broadly maintained but all staircases, balustrading and partitioning, where possible, were replaced
         </em>
         . The bathrooms, floor finishes, wardrobes and the kitchen were removed and a new staircase was added.
        </p>
