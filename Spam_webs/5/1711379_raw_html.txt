           <h1 class="post-title">
            Camping Retreat: Drew House by Simon Laws / Anthill Constructions
           </h1>
           <p class="post-meta">
            Posted by
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/author/caroline/" rel="author" title="Posts by Caroline Williamson">
             Caroline Williamson
            </a>
            on 03.14.13 in
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/category/architecture/" rel="category tag">
             Architecture
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/category/main/" rel="category tag">
             Main
            </a>
           </p>
          </header>
          <div class="post-content">
           <div class="row featured-image">
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/drew-house-by-simon-laws-anthill-constructions/anthill-drew-house-1/" target="_self">
             <img alt="Camping Retreat: Drew House by Simon Laws / Anthill Constructions" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-1.jpg"/>
            </a>
            <a class="contrast-bar gallery-button" href="http://design-milk.com/drew-house-by-simon-laws-anthill-constructions/anthill-drew-house-1/" target="_self">
             <i class="icon ss-icon">
              forward
             </i>
             View The Photo Gallery
            </a>
           </div>
           <p>
            <a href="http://anthillconstructions.com.au/3960_Drew_House.html" target="_blank">
             Drew House
            </a>
            is a weekend retreat meant to reflect the times the homeowners spent camping in their youth, so Simon Laws of
            <a href="http://anthillconstructions.com.au" target="_blank">
             Anthill Constructions
            </a>
            broke the residence up into separate pods giving it somewhat of a luxury campsite feel.
           </p>
           <p>
            <span id="more-133925">
            </span>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133928" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-2.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Located near Gladstone, on the east Australian coast, the home is situated amongst native trees, allowing the family to enjoy the natural environment.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133929" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-3.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            There are two sleeping pods, a bath house, and a long tube-like structure that houses the living room and kitchen. All were built off-site, as not to disturb the surrounding landscape.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133930" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-4.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            The roof structure and decks were prefabricated but constructed once onsite, all of which connect the pods.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133931" height="749" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-5.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            The triangular wooden panels open up in the living room helping to bring the outdoors in.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133932" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-6.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133933" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-7.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133934" height="749" src="http://2.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-8.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133935" height="334" src="http://2.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-9.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133936" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-10.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133937" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-11.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133938" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-12.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            The homeowner happens to be a photographer and the shower door features her work.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133939" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-13.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            I love how the wooden doors open right up letting you take a bath or shower while you enjoy the view.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133940" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-14.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133941" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-15.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133942" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-16.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-133943" height="334" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2013/03/Anthill-Drew-House-17.jpg" width="500"/>
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
