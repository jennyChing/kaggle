       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Mastering the Art of Creating Your Own Reality
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_2476" style="width: 170px">
         <a href="http://apod.nasa.gov/apod/image/0305/helix03_hst.jpg" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-2476 pib-hover-img" height="140" src="http://timberwolfhq.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/Helix_Eye-of-God-300x225.jpg" width="160"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Eye of God
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <em>
          Every story of
          <strong>
           conscious evolution
          </strong>
          is interesting, different,
          <strong>
           unique
          </strong>
          and only elaborates on the fact that there are
          <strong>
           so
          </strong>
          many ways to reach truth, to reinstate so to speak
          <strong>
           ones true self
          </strong>
          <sup class="footnote">
           <a href="#fn-2450-1" id="fnref-2450-1">
            1
           </a>
          </sup>
          . Mine began in earnest twenty years ago when I walked into my office at work, looked around and thought. I dont belong here anymore. It was a frightening feeling, a thought, a feeling that I wasnt sure how to navigate around but forward I pressed. Had I known what lay ahead I may have been more reticent than
          <strong>
           my intuitive hunch gave clues
          </strong>
          to!!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         Leading up to this moment I had been instrumental in my professional life in revamping the entire training system for a whole industry. Previously all serious education lay in the hands of the ivory tower educators of university status, a system too unwieldy, arrogant and not at all meeting the actual needs of employers/employees. I developed
         <strong>
          an incremental training system
         </strong>
         that introduced people to an industry with only the qualifications they needed and no excess baggage. The ability to, accredit each mini-qualification and build up to a stronger and appropriate preparation for dealing with what the industry required. So even then I was
         <strong>
          looking for hands on
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          immediate
         </strong>
         and more
         <strong>
          real
         </strong>
         , appropriate
         <strong>
          means to
         </strong>
         be versed in
         <strong>
          lifes requirements
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_2478" style="width: 164px">
         <a href="http://www.pc-wallpapers.co.uk/wallpapers/nature/forces-of-nature/eye-of-the-storm-hurricane-elena-september-1-1985_1600x1200.jpg" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Eye of the Storm" class="size-medium wp-image-2478 pib-hover-img" height="116" src="http://timberwolfhq.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/eye_of_the_storm-300x225.jpg" width="154"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Transforming Storm
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         A song that always expressed the truth of my life was the old number
         <em>
          Is that all there is?
         </em>
         and perhaps my ever present question to myselfIs this it? Is this all there is? Mind you I had a comparatively great life and yet it needed more depth, more meaning, more of something that I could really believe in, really connect with and so
         <strong>
          my personal life became transformed
         </strong>
         by the words of
         <strong>
          a Buddhist monk
         </strong>
         with whom I studied meditationhe
         <strong>
          said
         </strong>
         ,
         <em>
          <strong>
           You create your own realitymind precedes matter, mind precedes phenomena
          </strong>
         </em>
         and so began my passionate, ardent and a totally tenacious fixation with any
         <strong>
          personal development
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          enlightening material
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          books
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          courses
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          gurus
         </strong>
         and whatever I could get my hands on that served to inspire me and
         <strong>
          enlighten
         </strong>
         my
         <strong>
          spiritual life
         </strong>
         , open my mind further to the notion that my
         <em>
          <strong>
           fate was in my hands and my choices created my life
          </strong>
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         And, at this point my
         <strong>
          spirituality
         </strong>
         was about to move from vicarious engagement via the delving into others experiences and insights tovisceral commitment to a deeply personal journey comprised of
         <strong>
          my
         </strong>
         own prodigal
         <strong>
          trek away
         </strong>
         from
         <strong>
          soul connection
         </strong>
         <strong>
          via
         </strong>
         the route of
         <strong>
          socio-cultural programming
         </strong>
         into the
         <strong>
          indoctrination
         </strong>
         of a worldview, a reality that bites deep
         <strong>
          into
         </strong>
         your being and sets in place
         <strong>
          a blueprint for life
         </strong>
         and then, hopefully back home
         <strong>
          to
         </strong>
         the source of
         <strong>
          me and cosmic oneness
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Away from and back home to happiness as
         <strong>
          the
         </strong>
         getting of
         <strong>
          wisdom process
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_2479" style="width: 147px">
         <a href="http://c2.ac-images.myspacecdn.com/images01/58/m_67a82360bdc9b7c510bd373c7b984875.jpg" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Cosmic Consciousness" class="size-full wp-image-2479 pib-hover-img" height="187" src="http://timberwolfhq.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/Cosmic-Consciousness.jpg" width="137"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Cosmic Consciousness
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Led by the
         <em>
          <strong>
           voice within
          </strong>
         </em>
         I began to turn all of my attention to the
         <strong>
          sensations
         </strong>
         ,
         <strong>
          feelings
         </strong>
         and the
         <strong>
          emotions
         </strong>
         that were evoked within my body, triggered by I suppose the difference between how my life ACTUALLY was, to what my
         <strong>
          intuition
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          insights
         </strong>
         gained from
         <strong>
          exposure to spiritual ideas
         </strong>
         told me it could be. I noticed that when I aspired to a higher way of living, contrary to what I had lived, up till now in my world a situation my body would respond
         <em>
          a behaviour or incident could ignite or
         </em>
         shall we say
         <em>
          trigger a negative or contrary emotional response
         </em>
         in my physiology. I learnt to not be run by it, medicate or eradicate it, but rather watch it, wonder about it, talk to it, ask it, what are you? Where are you from? I could track it, like a detective might find a clue and trace it back to its origin and find the culprit,
         <em>
          I could find the original moment in time when the enculturation event occurred which literally derailed my own natural spiritual a-tune-ment to oneness and the comfort/happiness
         </em>
         . That means, from a sense of wholenessdown the track towards the more fear based fight or flight notions of a more
         <strong>
          animalistic perspective of life
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         I was always fascinated by the saying,
         <em>
          If I only knew then what I know now!
         </em>
         Well,
         <strong>
          now I could do that
         </strong>
         . I could literally take my now wisdom back into that past event and
         <em>
          delete old fear based files in my consciousness and reinstate values like love, hope, joy, faith, knowing
         </em>
         and then return to the present and be more whole than I was a moment ago, and
         <strong>
          I would immediately notice the changes in how my life unfolded, now I am creating my reality
         </strong>
         differently, now I am creating a reality
         <strong>
          more attuned to who I know myself to truly be
         </strong>
         , rather than what my historical cultural teachers created for me as a version of reality that tasted yuk even at the time but what choice did I have then, if I was to survive and be loved and approved of.
        </p>
        <p>
         For twenty years I have devoted my life to
         <em>
          a conscious process of mining the truth behind each unpleasant, uncomfortable or painful emotion
         </em>
         in my body. 24/7 I have shifted my attention into the
         <strong>
          vibration
         </strong>
         of each sensation as my body would deliver it to me. I would sit with it, move into it and literally discover its story, mine the gold and come up renewed and wiser for the discoveries. Much wisdom and information has been gained in this process and all of it visceral, all of it firsthand.
        </p>
        <p>
         Its MY STORY and tailor made to my unfolding journey.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_2486" style="width: 156px">
         <a href="http://i115.photobucket.com/albums/n297/kgbwell/my%20pics%202/untitled.jpg" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Spiritual Beings" class="size-medium wp-image-2486 pib-hover-img" height="203" src="http://timberwolfhq.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/Spiritual-Beings-216x300.jpg" width="146"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Spiritual Beings
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         Ive learnt in those 20 years in a way no-one could have told me that would have stuck within me, that
         <em>
          <strong>
           we truly are spiritual beings
          </strong>
         </em>
         in a human experience and as such live like schizophrenics
         <strong>
          in our experience of duality and paradox
         </strong>
         . Our human self has an
         <strong>
          animalistic
         </strong>
         part which defers to the
         <strong>
          flight or flight
         </strong>
         instinct and is motivated largely by fear. Our human animal aspect fears death, fears fate, fears life as being unsupportive, harsh and competitive, it feels ignorant and senses powers greater than it that define and decide its fate. It can be sucked into venerating authorities greater than itit will take someone elses word for it more so than its own senses as it doesnt trust what it sees.
        </p>
        <p>
         Meanwhile we also live with
         <strong>
          our spiritual self
         </strong>
         which is
         <strong>
          motivated and driven by love
         </strong>
         , it
         <strong>
          knows its own immortality
         </strong>
         to be how it is and senses the one truth of all life, that
         <strong>
          we are all cosmically connected in one unified field of energy
         </strong>
         . It experiences its soul as
         <strong>
          the god force
         </strong>
         and knows
         <em>
          it creates its own reality in a friendly universe that is ultimately supportive
         </em>
         . It knows and has
         <strong>
          cellular memory
         </strong>
         which it can access and be one with so all of the knowledge and information it needs it already has access to.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_2480" style="width: 190px">
         <a href="http://rlv.zcache.com/humanimal_poster-p228193693706922894trma_400.jpg" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-2480 pib-hover-img" height="180" src="http://timberwolfhq.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/03/Human-Animal-Nature-300x300.jpg" width="180"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Human Animal Nature
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <em>
          We
         </em>
         humans can
         <em>
          interpret our reality
         </em>
         and draw conclusions
         <em>
          based on our observations from an animalistic or spiritual perspective
         </em>
         . When
         <strong>
          Darwin
         </strong>
         observed animal behaviour and developed the ethos of
         <em>
          survival of the fittest
         </em>
         , or
         <strong>
          Newton
         </strong>
         described
         <em>
          cause and effect
         </em>
         each was confining their observations to
         <em>
          superficial behaviour
         </em>
         that just saw what happened on the surface of concrete reality and did
         <strong>
          not
         </strong>
         look deeper into
         <strong>
          the soul of the situation
         </strong>
         . Neither asked what caused the
         <strong>
          phenomena
         </strong>
         and how it related to mans soul. When
         <strong>
          Einstein
         </strong>
         was given
         <em>
          a compass at the age of eight
         </em>
         , he automatically
         <em>
          observed it from a spiritual point of view
         </em>
         , he asked
         <strong>
          what force was moving the compass needle
         </strong>
         and thus the
         <em>
          theory of relativity was born
         </em>
         . The animalistic and perhaps normal everyday use of the compass is to look at where it is pointing, not how its doing what its doing. Our spiritual selves always look deeper while our animal selves, more attuned to survival merely cope with what appears to be happening.
        </p>
        <p>
         We live with this contradiction within us always and our journey is always about mining the deeper truths and animating our human selves with our deeper spiritual knowing.
         <strong>
          Were here to blend two opposite points of view
         </strong>
         so that the less aware point of view gives way to the more aware and both make the very most of what it is to be human, a soul inhabiting a physical circumstance.
        </p>
        <p>
         I sense the spiritual metaphor in the legendary garden of Eden story is about our spiritual selves floating unknowing in the bliss of cosmic consciousness enjoying the ocean of wonder without a clue about whats going onnot the wherewithal for intimate and intense connection to what could be the physical manifestation of this spiritual paradise. We were told, we were warned how it would beIF we were willing to endure the journey of conscious awakeningthis journey of evolving minds capable of consciously knowing paradise would take us to all the opposite places first, but we had to do it didnt we, and here we are. We are where we areso far!
        </p>
        <p>
         And theres always more.
        </p>
