           <div class="blog-post" id="blog-post-280435538886479758">
            <div class="blog-header">
             <h2 class="blog-title">
              <a class="blog-title-link blog-link" href="http://www.eatsimplynow.com/tonights-menu/delightful-lemon-chicken">
               Delightful Lemon Chicken
              </a>
             </h2>
             <p class="blog-date">
              <span class="date-text">
               5/5/2014
              </span>
             </p>
             <p class="blog-comments">
              <a class="blog-link" href="http://www.eatsimplynow.com/tonights-menu/delightful-lemon-chicken#comments">
               0 Comments
              </a>
             </p>
            </div>
            <div class="blog-separator">
            </div>
            <div class="blog-content">
             <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
              Delightful Lemon Chicken. Yummy. The family love this one too. I recently served this up for some dinner guests and they left with the recipe. Be Creative. ~Kaz
              <br/>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div class="wsite-image wsite-image-border-thin " style="padding-top:10px;padding-bottom:10px;margin-left:0;margin-right:0;text-align:center">
               <a>
                <img alt="Picture" src="/uploads/2/7/0/2/27022259/235856362.jpg?437" style="width:auto;max-width:100%"/>
               </a>
               <div style="display:block;font-size:90%">
               </div>
              </div>
             </div>
             <h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;">
              Ingredients:
              <br/>
             </h2>
             <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
              <strong>
              </strong>
              <u>
               <strong>
                For the sauce:
               </strong>
              </u>
              <br/>
              <ul style="">
               <li style="">
                1 1/4 cups chicken stock
               </li>
               <li style="">
                1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
               </li>
               <li style="">
                2 teaspoons lemon zest
               </li>
               <li style="">
                1 tablespoon olive oil
               </li>
               <br/>
               <u>
                <strong>
                 For the lemon rub
                </strong>
               </u>
               <li style="">
                1 tablespoon olive oil
               </li>
               <li style="">
                1 tablespoon whole grain mustard
               </li>
               <li style="">
                1 teaspoon lemon zest
               </li>
               <li style="">
                1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
               </li>
               <li style="">
                1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
               </li>
               <li style="">
                1/4 teaspoon salt
               </li>
               <li style="">
                8 skin-on chicken thighs, drumsticks, Maryland, breast (the choice is yours), skin on is better for this dish, but you can use chicken without skin.
                <br/>
               </li>
              </ul>
             </div>
             <h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;">
              Ingredients:
              <br/>
             </h2>
             <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
              <ol>
               <li style="">
                Preheat oven to 200 degree Celsius
                <br/>
               </li>
               <li style="">
                To  make the lemon rub, combine olive oil, mustard, lemon zest, oregano,  thyme and salt in a small bowl. Using your fingers, work the  lemon rub onto both sides of the chicken.
               </li>
               <li style="">
                In a large bowl, whisk together chicken stock, lemon juice and lemon zest; set aside.
               </li>
               <li style="">
                Heat  olive oil in a large fry pan over medium high heat. Add  chicken, skin-side down, and sear both sides until golden brown, about  2-3 minutes per side.
                <br/>
               </li>
               <li style="">
