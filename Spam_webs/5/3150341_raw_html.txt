           <h1 class="post-title">
            Best areas close to Queenstown
           </h1>
           <p class="post-byline">
            by
            <a href="http://www.flightsinternationaluk.co.uk/blog/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by admin">
             admin
            </a>
             July 16, 2014
           </p>
           <div class="clear">
           </div>
           <div class="entry">
            <div class="entry-inner">
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              <a href="http://www.flightsinternationaluk.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/queenstown.jpg">
               <img alt="queenstown" class="size-full wp-image-4333 alignleft" height="252" src="http://www.flightsinternationaluk.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/queenstown.jpg" width="378"/>
              </a>
              The stunning Queenstown is placed on the coast of a translucent Lake Wakatipu in New Zealand and is surrounded by tremendous rivers and mountains. Queenstown has become very popular because of its beautiful sceneries, sociable people, and wineries. It is also very famous as a best destination for cycling purpose. It is considered to be an excellent holiday place and is visited by two million people every year.If you are planning to visit Queenstown, it means you are going to live best time ever in your life. There are many fabulous places on short drive from Queenstown. You can also visit those places easily. Here are the destinations which are located near to Queenstown.
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              <b>
               <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                Gibbston Valley Wines
               </span>
              </b>
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              Gibbston Valley Wines is situated very close to Queenstown and it takes only 20 minutes to reach at this place from Queenstown. It is one of the New Zealands most popular and award winning wines. Wine Cave of Gibbston Valley Wine is the largest in New Zealand. You can also visit this wines cave and can learn the whole procedure of making wine here. Having a visit to Gibbston Valley Wines would be a great experience of your life.
              <span id="more-4332">
              </span>
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              <b>
               <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                Milford Sound
               </span>
              </b>
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              Elegant Milford Sound is enriched with a worlds famous inlet, which is located very close to Queenstown. It is a very attractive destination and one day tour to this place will make your trip more memorable and exciting. Rental cars are easily available in Queenstown and you can easily and more comfortably travel towards Milford Sound through these cars. When you are on the way to Milford Sound, you will see many other eye-catching places throughout the route like Fiordland National Park, Mirror Lakes, Eglinton Valley and Hollyford Valley. After reaching at Milford Sound, you can go to the gorgeous ford through boats and can enjoy the unbelievable birds and aquatic life there.
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              <b>
               <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                Mount Cook
               </span>
              </b>
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              Mount Cook is a very special place to watch, situated Head North out of Queenstown and there you can see the largest mountain of New Zealand which is 3,754 meters high. This mountain is fantastic, and visiting this mountain through helicopter would be much better and exciting. Another way to enjoy all the sceneries of this place is to walk next to a mountain track. You can reach at Mount Cook by passing through Kawarau River Gorge, home to the old gold mines and then Mackenzie Basin. It would definitely be an enjoyable track for you.
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              <b>
               <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                Lakes District Museum
               </span>
              </b>
              <b>
              </b>
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              Lakes District Museum is placed in a small town of Wakatipu District named Arrow town. Wakatipu District is very easily reachable from Queenstown. Many exhibitions are held in this museum which represents the gold rush of 19
              <sup>
               th
              </sup>
              century and Europeans entrance in this particular area.
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              <b>
               <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                Skyline Gondola
               </span>
              </b>
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              Having a ride of striking Gondola is a best way to have a look of all beautiful sights, lakes and mountains around the Queenstown. There is a cafe at the top through which you can enjoy a lot.
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              <a href="http://www.flightsinternationaluk.co.uk/cheap-flights-to/queenstown.htm">
               <b>
                <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
                 Cheap flights to Queenstown
                </span>
               </b>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              You can visit this place by travelling through china airline, air new Zealand and virgin Australia.
             </p>
