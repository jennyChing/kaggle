        Ingredients:

       <h2>
        Preparation:
       </h2>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        In a bowl add the flour, salt, sugar and dry yeast. In a pot
        <a href="http://www.everydayinbox.com/gluten-free-cherry-cake/" title="Gluten Free Cherry Cake!">
         milt the butter
        </a>
        and then add the milk, egg and mix it all together and on the end add the yeast. Make dough and live it to grow. When the dough is ready add the dry fruit and almonds, mix it and live them to rest. Then drag the dough on
        <strong>
         30 x 20 cm
        </strong>
        and in the middle add the marzipan, fold from one side then from another side and make roll. Leave it on hot and when is done bake it in a preheated oven on
        <strong>
         180C
        </strong>
        for
        <em>
         <strong>
          30
         </strong>
         minutes
        </em>
        . When the bred is little bit cold
        <a href="http://www.everydayinbox.com/delicious-christmas-cookies-sweet-stars/" title="Delicious Christmas Cookies: Sweet Stars!">
         spray it with dust sugar
        </a>
        . Advice if you are intolerant to gluten you can use corn flour.
       </p>
