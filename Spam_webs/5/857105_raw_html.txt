        <p>
         Stipple,
         <span class="J-JK9eJ-PJVNOc" id=":vy.1" tabindex="-1">
          spoolie
         </span>
         , and fan, what does it all mean? There are a lot of makeup brushes out there and its easy to confuse and misuse them. Our
         <span class="J-JK9eJ-PJVNOc" id=":vy.2" tabindex="-1">
          infographic
         </span>
         , 
         <span class="J-JK9eJ-PJVNOc" id=":vy.3" tabindex="-1">
          Brushology
         </span>
         , is your straight forward guide to makeup brushes. Having a proper understanding of what each brush was made for can help you get the most out of your makeup products.
        </p>
        <p>
         Did you know fan brushes are for highlighting and brushing off loose eye shadow?Getting a handle on your makeup brushes is easier than you think. From the eye shadow brush to the more complex stippling brush, weve got their uses covered. Never again will you mistake your blush brush for your foundation brush with this handy guide.
        </p>
