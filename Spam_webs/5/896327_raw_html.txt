     <p>
      Proven Oil Reserves is valued through geological and engineering processes to produce an estimated value of oil stocks in the ground. Proven Oil Reserves showcases the commercially available (and recoverable) oil reserves of a given country based on its recognized reservoirs. For the purposes of the GFP ranking, oil is a highly valued factor when considering  a prolonged war and is used in conjunction with
      <a href="/oil-consumption-by-country.asp">
       oil consumption
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/oil-production-by-country.asp">
       oil production
      </a>
      .
     </p>

       Barrels per Day (bbl)
