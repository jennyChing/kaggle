          <h2 class="subtitle">
           Taking that Dental Phobia Away from Your Child
          </h2>
          <div class="txtd" id="txtd_16585123" style="">
           <p>
            Do you have problems with bringing your kids to see the dentist? It is undeniable that most kids are scared to go to any dental institution primarily because they dont want to get hurt. Kids dental phobia is a very powerful motivator for them to act out prior to a dental appointment. One belief is that once you go to the dentist, their tooth will be taken out and it is going to hurt. They may hear about dentists that use needles from a brother, sister or friend and that freaks them out. As parents, your task is to calm these fears and dispel any myths before the initial visit. We all know that their dental health is also important and hope that the first experience is a reassuring event
           </p>
           <p>
            As a kids dentist in Central Bucks County , we get a chance to see and hear a wide range of reactions from both toddlers and their parents. Having a warm and welcoming environment to great children when they first arrive is of utmost importance to begin introducing a toddler to their new pediatric dental offices. Our hope is that the primary care giver at home joins the toddler during these critical first visits since dental hygiene lessons taught in the office are continued at home.
           </p>
           <p>
            Why is dental health important to kids?
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Almost every kid has a sweet tooth and processed sugar left in between teeth is the most common cause of cavities in children.
            </li>
            <li>
             Toddlers like to exhibit independence. Parents must be relentless in teaching good dental hygiene habits at an early age. The alternative is a low self esteem when they are teens with yellowing teeth.
            </li>
            <li>
             While the teeth are still young, poor dental health may affect facial bone structure and could alter the formation of the permanent teeth.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            Painless dentistry for kids is what any parent would want for their child. Pediatric Dentists in particular should do their best to minimize or remove any pain associated with the more common dental treatments such as cleanings, fillings and routine x-rays. Quality Pediatric Dental Care focuses on:
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Developing routine oral care habits from the 1st visit.
            </li>
            <li>
             Delivering an exceptional Chair Side Manner forging a great Dentist to patient relationship.
            </li>
            <li>
             Providing safe and effective preventative dental care to children.
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p>
            <a href="http://growingsmilesinfloralvale.com/children-dental-services/">
             Fixing a broken tooth for kids
            </a>
            is one of the most challenging parts for any pediatric dentist near New Hope . Repairing a broken tooth takes a careful hand to restore and often more skill than actually removing a tooth. An impatient child coupled with a panicked mother can be an explosive combination. A calming reassuring tone and an air confidence is what a gentle dentist in Bucks County requires to keep both patients calm.
           </p>
           <p>
            If painless and professional dental care for children is not practiced 100% of the time at your
            <a href="http://growingsmilespa.com/">
             pediatric dental offices in Council Rock
            </a>
            , then find one that focuses on painless dentistry for kids in Bucks County . Remember that those first few visits are critical to establish a good rapport with your pediatric dentist offices.
           </p>
