     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.100bestdatingsites.com/blog/2008/wedding-planning-on-the-web-100-tools-and-resources-for-brides-to-be/">
       Wedding Planning on the Web: 100 Tools and Resources for Brides to Be
      </a>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Wedding planning is an exciting but incredibly involved undertaking, so its important that you take advantage of all of the tools you have at your disposal. One of the most useful of these tools is the Internet, which is full of advice, planners, and other great resources for pulling everything together. Here, weve shared 100 of the best of what the Internet has to offer for brides.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       Motherlode
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      These sites are the ones you hear about often. They generally have full-service tools, vendor listings, inspiration and more, creating a one-stop shop for brides.
     </p>
     <ol>
      <li>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.theknot.com/default.shtml">
         The Knot
        </a>
       </strong>
       : This perennial favorite has it all-dresses, fashion, planning tools, and loads of inspiration. Just take it slowly, and dont get overwhelmed.
      </li>
      <li>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://tools.marthastewart.com/wedding/UserHome">
         Martha Stewart Weddings
        </a>
       </strong>
       : Martha has awesome planning tools, crafty advice, etiquette, and so much more.
      </li>
      <li>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.onewed.com/">
         OneWed
        </a>
       </strong>
       : OneWed offers planning resources, an active community of brides, plus free wedding websites.
      </li>
      <li>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.brides.com/">
         Brides.com
        </a>
       </strong>
       : This popular bridal magazines website is alive and well offering wedding style, planning, and much more.
      </li>
      <li>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.weddingwire.com/">
         WeddingWire
        </a>
       </strong>
       : Wedding Wire is an awesome resource for brides. You can find vendors and ask them to bid their services, build a site, talk to other brides, and a whole lot more.

      <li>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://www.getmarried.com/">
         Get Married
        </a>
