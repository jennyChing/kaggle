        <p style="text-align: center;">
         This
         <strong>
          Fresh Blueberry Muffin Cake
         </strong>
         is a fruity and moist cake with a
         <br/>
         luxcious, muffin-like texture.Its studded with juicy, sweet
         <br/>
         blueberries thatpopa flavor explosionin each andevery bite!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://wp.me/p3iHoj-aS1" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Blueberry Muffin Cake " class="aligncenter wp-image-41981" height="750" src="http://cf.callmepmc.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Blueberry-Muffin-Cake-label-466x700.jpg" title="Blueberry Muffin Cake" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         The thing about this cake is that its more like a muffin. Its big, bold and beautiful!But unlike muffins, you dont have to scrub the muffin pan after. Thats always a bonus, right?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         So what makes this a muffin cake and not just a cake? Its pretty simple, I wanted muffins.But, dang, I hate to wash a muffin pan. And, I just didnt want to stand andscoop muffins into each. little. pan.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Unlike a real cake, this muffin cake is more dense and very moist. The batter comes together really quickly, exactly what you need on hectic mornings!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         And those
         <strong>
          blueberries!
         </strong>
         Oh, the blueberries! the juicy blueberries give every bite a pop of flavor!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://wp.me/p3iHoj-aS1" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Blueberry Muffin Cake " class="aligncenter wp-image-41982" height="750" src="http://cf.callmepmc.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Blueberry-Muffin-Cake-3-wm-466x700.jpg" title="Blueberry Muffin Cake" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          How to choose the best blueberries.
         </strong>
         Whether youre hand picking berries are purchasing at a farmers market, here are guidelines for picking and storing fresh produce.
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          Look for bright colors. The skins should be taut and shiny. If the skins are dull, wrinkled and oozing juice, they arent good. Avoidsoft, watery or moldy blueberries (obviously).
         </li>
         <li>
          Look at the bottom of the basket for bad berries and leaking juice.
         </li>
         <li>
          Store blueberries in air-tight container in the refrigerator and use as quickly as possible.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         The method for this Fresh Blueberry Muffin Cake is simple. I place all the dry ingredients in a bowl and stirred to combine. Then I put all the wet ingredients in another bowl and stir to combine. Whisk this mixture until the egg is well beaten and evenly distributed. Add the wet into the dry and
         <em>
          lightly
         </em>
         stir until just combine.Theyll be lumps and thats okay. Next, youll add your blueberries and fold to combine being careful not to break the berries.
        </p>
        <p>
         I use fresh or frozen blueberries in my recipes. Lucky for me my mom has blueberry bushes that usually produce tons of blueberries. When theyre not in season, I use frozen. If youre using frozen, I suggest put them in the batter frozen, not thawed. Thawed berries tend to bleed more into the cake.
        </p>
        <p>
         Another note, blueberries will stain your hands. Theyll also stain clothing. Keep that in mind, especially if you have little ones helping. Why is it that all kids wipe their hands down their shirts?
        </p>

         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          Moist muffin-like cake is studded with fresh blueberries in this easy-to-prepare breakfast or dessert recipe.
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup self-rising flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup granulated sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon cinnamon
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            pinch of salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 egg
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup sweet milk, I used 2% milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 tablespoon sour cream (or Greek Yogurt)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 tablespoon canola oil
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 and  cup fresh or frozen blueberries
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahreinheit and spray a 8x8 or a 9x9 inch can pan or casserole dish with non-stick spray.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a medium size bowl, add flour, sugar, salt, and cinnamon .
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In another small bowl, beat egg then add milk, sour cream, oil and vanilla. Whisk to combine.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Add wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until combined. You'll still have some lumps, this is ok.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Fold in blueberries.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bake 22 to 27 minutes, depending on your oven, watch cake carefully. Cake is done with a wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove from oven and allow to cool 5 to 10 minutes before inverting onto a wire rack. Serve warm or room temperature.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cake is good served warm with butter. You can also dust with confectioners sugar or drizzle with a glaze of 1 cup confectioners and 3 tablespoon milk (combine and stir until smooth)
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
