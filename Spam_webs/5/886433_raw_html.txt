   <div class="hero">
    <img alt="" class="hero-img" src="http://www.proflowers.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/BlogHero-JumpStartSpring-1b.jpg"/>

     <h1 class="entry-title">
      Jumpstart Spring
     </h1>
     <div class="meta right">
      <time class="published" datetime="2014-03-07T09:04:17+00:00">
       March 7, 2014
      </time>
     </div>
    </header>
   </div>
   <div class="wrap">
    <div class="container" role="document">
     <div class="content row">
      <main class="main " role="main">
       <div class="entry-content col-md-offset-1 col-md-10 col-lg-offset-2 col-lg-8">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p class="Normal1" style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Jumpstart Spring Header" class="aligncenter" height="275" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/SpringHeader_blog140307" width="610"/>
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         Its been a long and cold winter for much of the U.S., but spring has finally arrived and we cant wait to embrace the new season and new fashions. Whats more appropriate for spring than floral fashions? Theyre feminine, flirty, pretty, colorful and timeless.
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         When it comes to fashion, a single flower can inspire an entire look. However, that doesnt mean that the whole ensemble must be floral. As you can see below, fashion blogger Ellen from theuniverseandyou.com has captured
         <a href="http://www.proflowers.com/roses-ros" target="new">
          the essence of roses
         </a>
         with the single detail of her floral handbag. Its simple, yet feminine, adds a coordinating color, and she carries it with style.
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         <img alt="Simple but elegant" height="1200" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/girl-in-red_blog140307?wid=800" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         <a href="http://www.universeandyou.com.br/the-look-of-love/" target="new">
          http://www.universeandyou.com.br/the-look-of-love/
         </a>
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         Of course, even if youre not a pattern person, there are still ways to incorporate a floral theme into your spring wardrobe. Its all about color! This collage by Kati at
         <a href="http://www.glitzandgold.com/" target="new">
          glitzandgold.com
         </a>
         below is all about simplicity, and there are no floral patterns. Rather, they use reds and pinks that evoke a feel of the garden on a sunny day, without including actual flowers. You can pair a bright accessory with neutral separates or shoes to make a basic outfit an eye-catching original.
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         <img alt="Floral-Inspired Fashion" height="763" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/fashion_blog140307" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         If your look is more on the fun and frivolous side, add a touch of whimsy with some brightly colored accessories that will add spring in your step, and adding a spring feel to your wardrobe. Below, check out the luscious pink-petaled umbrella and hot pink rain boots
         <a href="http://www.proflowers.com/tulips-tul" target="new">
          inspired by spring tulips
         </a>
         . After all, we always say that April showers bring May flowers, right? So be prepared  with style. Rain boots are a hot trend for 2014, and hot pink ones are even hotter. Any of these accessories will have you turning heads and exuding optimism even on rainy days.
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         <img alt="Collage by Julia" height="902" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/pink_blog140307?wid=800" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         Collage by Jullia at
         <a href="http://www.glitterandjuls.com/" target="new">
          www.glitterandjuls.com
         </a>
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         If youre not sloshing around in puddles, consider a chic floral pattern for your next event. What happens on glitzy red carpets tends to influence the years fashion trends.
         <a href="http://www.vanityfair.com/vf-hollywood/baftas-best-dressed" target="new">
          Vanity Fair
         </a>
         dubbed Cate Blanchett one of the best-dressed at the 2014 British Academy of Film and Television Arts awards, and Cate turned heads in a sleek black Alexander McQueen dress with a silver floral pattern. The dress featured below is a more casual version of Cates sleek and sexy look. This one is shorter and exudes a fun-loving elegance, especially when paired with the leather jacket and bright red handbag.
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         If youre not heading to any red carpet galas or dressy events in the near future but youre looking to spice up your wardrobe with stylish florals, there are plenty of options.
         <a href="http://www.vogue.com/" target="new">
          Vogue
         </a>
         s
         <a href="http://www.vogue.com/guides/vogues-guide-to-spring-2014-fashion/#/guide/10667/32/" target="new">
          Fashion Guide for Spring 2014: Power of Flowers
         </a>
         says that this year is about transformative clothes. Specifically, it refers to the power of flowers. Theyre artful and a great way to express a strong personality. In the guide, Vogue shows off beaded, Victorian Hawaiian-looking varietals at Marc Jacobs and the dangerous-looking vines at Christian Dior, among others.
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         <img alt="Woman with purse" height="830" src="http://a1128.g.akamai.net/7/1128/497/0001/origin-d5.scene7.com/is/image/ProvideCommerce/woman-with-purse_blog140307?wid=800" width="800"/>
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         Personality is everything when it comes to fashion. You can create a fun, super-casual look like Kristin at
         <a href="http://thefancythings.com/" target="new">
          thefancythings.com
         </a>
         by tossing a pretty floral top over distressed jeans and to-die-for heels and you will certainly be someone to watch.
        </p>
        <p class="Normal1">
         All told, 2014 may just be the year of the floral ensemble. And, why not? If youve had a gloomy winter, a new floral print will add some sunshine to your wardrobe, whether the actual sun is shining or not. And, while youre at it, dont wait for the flowers to bloom outside. If youre not a fashionista, you can still love the happiness that flowers bring to a room. A splash of color in the form of a luscious flower arrangement might be just the thing to get you through until the warm temperatures (and colors) of spring.
        </p>
       </div>
