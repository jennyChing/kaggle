     <br/>
     <font face="arial" size="2">
      Before even finishing junior high school, Jimmy Castor had written his first million seller for Frankie Lymon and the Teenagers called, I Promise To Remember.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      While furthering his education at New York Citys prestigious High School of Music &amp; Art and later attending City College, Jimmy pursued his musical career by assembling a band of his own playing an assortment of major New York nightspots.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      He then wrote his second million seller, Hey Leroy Your Mamas Calling You, on Mercury Records, through which a new flashy and spirited performer was introduced to the public  Jimmy Castor. Still early into his career, Castor befriended such music greats as Jimi Hendrix, Sly Stone, Tito Puente and King Curtis to name only a handful.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Moving on to RCA Records, The Jimmy Castor Bunch continued to show off Castors witty, pop/r&amp;b writing ability through his amusing characterizations of...
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Troglodyte and Luther the Anthropoid, hit singles from their first and second RCA albums, Its Just Begun and Phase Two  the title cut from the Its Just Begun album was featured in the movie Flashdance.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      On the third RCA LP, Jimmy further established his versatility as an artist/producer by expanding the groups more serious instrumental abilities as first recognized through Castors haunting rendition of The First Time Ever I Saw Your Face and A Whiter Shade of Pale.
