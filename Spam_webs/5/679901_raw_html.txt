       <h1>
        DIY: Crossed Bun
       </h1>
       <div class="left">
        <p>
         Who wouldnt want this playful crossed bun? First, curl hair. Secondly, bind the hair on the back of
your head together, leaving all the hair in front of your ears loose. Tie the back hair into a knot. Now, braid 
the loose hair on the front into two separate braids and pin them on top of the knot. Try it!
        </p>
