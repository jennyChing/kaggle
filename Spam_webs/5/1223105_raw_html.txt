                        &lt;p&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Online marketing has been around since the appearance and spread of the internet. But online marketing has been evolving and adjusting itself to the internet users and their socializing trends. The most noticeable change it has shown is the use of the internet social media as an online marketing resource. This has emerged as a direct effect of the birth and popularization of the social media networks. They ways of socially interacting among the population have been changing and the social media networks have shown an amazing growth spurtmarketing needed to update to peoples habits and use the marketing tools that were becoming available.this need is what made&lt;a href="http://www.guorillamedia.com" target="_blank"&gt; social media marketing&lt;/a&gt; one of the most popular and effective promoting tools.&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The evolution of the social media marketing allows social media to be a most suitable mean to not only spread a name or brand, but also establish commercial contacts, build an online reputation, create an online community referred on the subject or field we want to promote, give customers support, get consumers feedback, keep the users informed, to mentions just a few. We are not leaving aside from online marketing the use of the company/person/product web site, on the contrary, the idea is to take advantage of the social networks to also lead traffic to website whose products we want to promote.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;We could say that almost any social network user can use these socialmedia marketing instruments; the ideal to take most advantage of thesesocial media marketing tools would be to have a marketing&lt;strong&gt; &lt;/strong&gt;professional setting up different online media marketing strategies to obtain maximal benefits of them. Many companies have a marketing professional within their staff of employees in charge of this task, while others directly hire a specialized marketing&lt;strong&gt; &lt;/strong&gt;agency to do it for them.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Social media marketing exploits with promotional purposes the different social services and networks available, such as Youtube, Facebook, Twitter, and Myspace. &lt;a href="http://www.guorillamedia.com/packages/facebook-marketing" target="_blank"&gt;Facebook marketing &lt;/a&gt;or &lt;a href="http://www.guorillamedia.com/packages/twitter-marketing" target="_blank"&gt;Twitter marketing&lt;/a&gt; for instance, always aim to redirect the user to the webpage of the company or product that is being promoted. Through the social networks they encourage users to visit their sites, something they even get to track with a visitors counter, and in some cases you can even find a mini poll asking the visitors how they got to land in their site.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;A most interesting fact behind social media marketing is that it is the user and potential customer that is browsing within their areas of interest and seek the information about a topic or product of their interest. So, instead of being annoying, this promotion, if properly inserted in the social networks, captures the interest of the web surfers into it, who even may get to lead their online contacts, friends or followers to also view the promotional information or sites.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Besides, though most effective &lt;a href="http://www.guorillamedia.com" target="_blank"&gt;social media marketing&lt;/a&gt; is the one properly inserted in the different online communities, the worldwide potential of growth is unlimited.&lt;/p&gt;
