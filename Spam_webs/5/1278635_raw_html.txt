                     Ashton Kutcher's Brilliant Quotes

                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-657328773511257411" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div style="text-align: justify;">
                      Ashton Kutcher is one of the actors these days that has won my respect while watching the Teen Choice Awards 2013. I thought all along that this heartthrob sexy Ashton is just one of those actors who focuses on women, fortune, fame, money and drugs. When I heard his speech educating the teens, I was impressed by his personality as a person.
                      <br/>
                      <img border="0" height="507" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-QnMko-sbJfY/UhaezGXQR6I/AAAAAAAATf8/_xHoJ6CcGe0/s640/Ashton+Kutcher+Awards+Night.jpg" width="640"/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        "I believe that opportunity looks a lot like hard work.
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        When I was 13 I had my first job with my dad
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        carrying shingles up to the roof.
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        And then I got a job washing dishes at a restaurant.
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        And then I got a job in a grocery store deli.
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        And then I got a job in a factory
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        sweeping cheerio dust off the ground.
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        And I've never had a job in my life that I was better than.
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        I was always just lucky to have a job.
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        And every job I had was a steppingstone to my next job,
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        and I never quit my job until I had my next job.
                       </i>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <i>
                        And so opportunities look a lot like work."
                       </i>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     Isn't that inspiring? What do you think?
