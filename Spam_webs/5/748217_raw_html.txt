         </p>
         <p>
          "A few people in scrubs are there now. Still no baby. Still no #Obama." Clifford tweeted with a picture.
         </p>
         <p>
          The hashtag#ObamaBabyWatchstarted being used by onlookers.
         </p>
         <p>
          According to KNBC, as of early Thursday, there was no word on whether the baby had arrived.
