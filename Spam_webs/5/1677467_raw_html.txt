         2nd Annual Fabulous Flea Market Held At Town Hall Theatre 9/26
        </h1>

         <p>
          The second annual Fabulous Flea Market of antiques collectibles, and upscale treasures will take place at Town Hall Theater on Saturday, September 26 from 9am until 2pm.
         </p>
         <p>
          Very well attended last year, the event promises to be even bigger this year with over 25 vendors selling their wares. Among the treasures are country furniture, brass candlesticks, crocks, footstools, china, artwork, Chinese antiques, Himalayan jewelry, furniture embellished with trompe l'oeil painting, jewelry of all sorts, hooked rugs, vintage aprons, pottery, artwork, folk art, Nancie Dunn's fabulous cards, vintage collectibles and more. Prices start as low as one dollar.
         </p>
         <p>
          Robin Huestis of Round Robin and Jean Montross are storing away all kinds of wonderful things for the flea market. Vendors returning from last year are Round Robin, Sweet Charity, Mike Wilson, John Banach, Michael Forte,
          <a href="/people/Jane-Miller/">
           Jane Miller
          </a>
          , Joy MacIntyre, Kim Ehritt, Barbara Hemingway, Nancie Dunn, and Robin Falta. New this year are Greg Hamilton, Jackie English, HOPE, Deb Hillman, Kathy Mitchell, Rita Masso, Yinglei Zhang, Jane and Marshall Eddy, Carrie Wulfman, Joan Korda, and Susan Nelson.
         </p>
         <p>
          Food will be available for purchase, provided by Karen Goettelman.
         </p>
         <p>
          In addition to private and community vendors, Town Hall Theater will have its own tables selling antiques and other donated items. Proceeds from these tables benefit THT. For information about the Fabulous Flea Market, or to donate some of your unused treasures to the Town Hall Theater tables, call Barbara at 388-8268.
         </p>
