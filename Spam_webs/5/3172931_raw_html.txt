        <h1>
         Spread the Net against malaria
        </h1>
        <div class="captionrightwide" style="background-color: #fff;">
         <img alt="Spread the Net against malaria" src="/image/_new2015/logos/SPREADTHENET_EN_wTagline_Online.jpg" style="padding-left: 10px;"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         Each year, close to 600,000 lives are lost to malaria, the majority of which are pregnant women and children under 5. 90% of these malaria-related deaths occur in Africa.
        </p>
        <p>
         Since 2007, Spread the Net has protected the lives of more than 7.2 million people including children and pregnant women and delivering over 2.5 million malaria-preventing bed nets to families in Africa.
        </p>

        <h5>
         Bed nets: The solution
        </h5>
        <p>
         Insecticide-treated bed nets are one of the
         <strong>
          most effective and inexpensive ways
         </strong>
         to prevent the transmission of malaria.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Since 2000, an estimated 4.2 million lives have been saved as a result of malaria interventions, which includes the distribution of bed nets.
         </li>
         <li>
          One bed net can protect 2 people for up to 3 years.
         </li>
         <li>
          Thousands of lives could be saved every year if all children under the age of 5 in Africa slept under a bed net.
         </li>
        </ul>
