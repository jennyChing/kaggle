          <img alt="IMG_20141009_095818857[1]" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-367763" height="337" src="http://cdn.bleedingcool.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/IMG_20141009_0958188571-600x337.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Hannah Means-Shannon
          </strong>
          is currently
          <a href="http://www.bleedingcool.com/2014/10/09/live-from-the-diamond-retailer-appreciation-breakfast-at-nycc-2014/">
           liveblogging
          </a>
          the Diamond Retailer Breakfast at New York Comic Con.
         </p>
         <p>
          Which has just just announced in March, a fourth
          <strong>
           Doctor Who
          </strong>
          comic book series from Titan Comics, featuring the Ninth Doctor, with the likeness of
          <strong>
           Christopher Eccleston
          </strong>
          and also starring Rose and Captain Jack. It will be out for the tenth anniversary of the return of the TV series.
         </p>
         <p>
          And a little fuzzily shot snaps of art from The Tenth and The Eleventh series
