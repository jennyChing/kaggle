     <div id="description">
      <span itemprop="articleBody">
       Skeet shooting is a lot of fun outdoors, but not so much indoors. Well, now you can practice your targeting and timing skills all year long, indoors or out, with the cool new
       <a href="http://go.thegreenhead.com?id=13163x703919&amp;xs=1&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.hammacher.com%2FProduct%2F78806&amp;sref=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.thegreenhead.com%2F2010%2F08%2Fskeet-shooter-game.php" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Hammacher.com">
        Skeet Shooter Game
       </a>
       . Just drop the skeets into the slots on the device, grab your electronic, infrared blaster and fire a shot at the Skeet Shooter sensor to begin playing. It launches three timed skeets up into the air that you shoot at with a safe, narrow infrared beam from your blaster. When the skeet is hit by the beam, it breaks apart just like in real life!
