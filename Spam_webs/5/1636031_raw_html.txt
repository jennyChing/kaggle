         </p>

         <h2>
          <span id="What_on_earth_are_Rainbow_Loom_rubber_bands">
           What on earth are Rainbow Loom rubber bands?
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.supermommyclub.com/disclosure-policy/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <img alt="Read our disclosure policy" class="alignright" height="259" src="http://www.supermommyclub.com/wp-content/uploads/disclosure_momsign_affiliate.png" width="132"/>
          </a>
          Shall we start at the beginning? Just in case the craze has passed you by. Rainbow Loom rubber bands are exactly what the name suggests.
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B00K18YIOU/?tag=aretheytwins-20" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           Little rubber bands
          </a>
          in a whole rainbow of colours that can be turned into charms or bracelets or other crafty things with a hook (like a crochet hook) and a
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B00KOFM2MU/?tag=aretheytwins-20" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           loom
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Now the great thing about Rainbow Loom is that your kids think theyre just having fun, but really theyre developing a wide number of skills:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Fine motor skills  in order to weave the bands together on the loom they need to be able to master that hook, which is held in the same way as a pencil. So while theyre crafting their charms and bracelets theyre working on the muscles and the coordination skills they need to be able to write.
          </li>
          <li>
           Pattern skills  the bracelets that kids make with these little rubber bands are very intricate and often have a repeating pattern. Little do they realise it but theyre developing spacial skills that will be so important in later life, to help them follow instructions, read a map and understand maths and science.
          </li>
          <li>
           Perseverance  if your child gets easily frustrated you may need to offer them a little more help when theyre just starting out with their loom, but once they master this skill it will help them build perseverance.
          </li>
          <li>
           Increases attention span  youll be surprised at how long your kids will happily sit just focusing on their loom and the bracelet or charm they are creating. This is a wonderful way to increase their attention spans.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          Can you see why these little bands are the perfect Christmasor birthday gift for kids who are struggling in school?
         </p>
         <p>

           Where to buy Rainbow Loom bands

          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B00K18YIOU/?tag=aretheytwins-20" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           Rainbow Loom bands
          </a>
          then the best place is Amazon.
         </p>
         <p>
          If youre out and about you might see them in the grocery store, or even the Dollar Tree, but make sure that they are actual Rainbow Loom bands.
         </p>
         <p>
          These are very popular toys and as Im sure you can imagine there are numerous companies popping up making their own versions of the bands, but they may not meet the current Safety Standards and you dont want to be messing with your kids safety to save a few bucks.

         <h2>
          <span id="Rubber_Band_Loom">
           Rubber Band Loom
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p>
          The rubber band loom itself is a series of plastic pegs. You place it down on a flat surface and then use the hook to weave the rubber bands around the pegs, and loop them through each other to form a pattern. As you place each band on the loom you are weaving them together into a bracelet, a charm, or any other item you might decide to make.
         </p>
         <p>
          The rubber band loom is included in
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B00KOFM2MU/?tag=aretheytwins-20" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           this starter set
          </a>
          , and its also possible to buy a smaller
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/dp/B00J7PI4MU/?tag=aretheytwins-20" rel="nofollow" target="blank">
           travel loom
          </a>
          , which is perfect for car, plane and train journeys, and even for keeping kids occupied in restaurants.
         </p>
