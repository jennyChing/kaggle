                        </span>
                       </div>

                        <b>
                         60%
                        </b>
                        more easily than that discussed in written form. Audience are therefore browsing for more helpful videos and webinars. So, in order to drive new audience and traffic to your blog, post some videos in you tube and don't forget to put your website or blog's link at the end of the video discussion.
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <b>
                         <br/>
                        </b>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <b>
                         Free giveaways
                        </b>
                        in the form of videos, eBooks, PDF's are essential and I can say an important tool especially for the beginners to expand their presence in the web market. Just convert your blog posts in video formats and ensure it's simplicity in terms of
                        <b>
                         easy explanations and understand-ability

                        .

                       </div>

                        </span>
                       </h3>
                       <div>
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        Guest blogging is one of the important concern for SEO during late 2010's but slowly it lost it's importance in the recent years. Google webmaster guru '
                        <b>
                         Matt Cutt
                        </b>
                        ' said that Google no longer considers guest blogging as an essential tool for
                        <b>
                         PageRanks
                        </b>
                        and
                        <b>
                         SEO
                        </b>
                        , due to the growth of spammers. But to be precise, it does help you build traffic to your blog ! Even though we can't expect much back links or SEO, the reason why we should still care for guest blogging is that we get an all new audience from others website or blog.
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        If you give very good explanations with appreciable
                        <b>
                         facts
                        </b>
                        about some topics in others blog as a guest (topic that is related to your blog's content), then readers of that blog comes to know about your
                        <b>
                         expertise
                        </b>
                        in that topic and so they will look for your similar posts. In this way you can attract them. While guest posting, ask the blog owners to include
                        <b>
                         attributes
                        </b>
                        to you and also preferably
                        <b>
                         with a link
                        </b>
                        to your blog. Also post only in those blogs, the content of which are more or less related to your's.

                       </div>

                         PageRanks

                        . Having numerous inbound links are an indication of your blog or website popularity as well as importance in the web world and so Google gives higher importance to such sites. Incorporate good practice of leaving backlinks. The best way being by
                        <b>
                         leaving comments
                        </b>
                        in others blog or trading for links. (
                        <u>
                         <span style="color: blue;">
                          <a href="http://easybloggerstuff.blogspot.com/2014/03/improve-pageranks-with-backlinks-and-comments.html">
                           Learn more about backlinks and good comment practice here
                          </a>
                         </span>
                        </u>
                        )
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         6. Use Analytics and other stat tools
                        </span>
                       </h3>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        It's very important to understand the behavior of your audience.
                        <b>
                         Analyzing
                        </b>
                        and understanding your audience helps to decide the following
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                       </div>
                       <ul>
                        <li>
                         what to write or post
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         when to post
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         where to post
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Whom are the target audience (demographics)
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Whether to elaborate any specific topics
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Which topic is getting popular
                        </li>
                        <li>
                         Whats the audience needs
                        </li>
                       </ul>
                       <br/>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        Delivering the needs of audience might help you grow your network. Audience loves to get their requirements fulfilled, and if you understand their's, then it's very easy to build traffic. Use
                        <b>
                         Google Analytics tool
                        </b>
                        to analyze their behavior. Also post only when your audience are active and not when you find time ! Remember we are writing for audience only and not for robots or self.
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        (
                        <span style="color: blue;">
                         <u>
                          <a href="http://easybloggerstuff.blogspot.com/2014/03/benefits-of-upgrading-to-universal-Aanalytics.html">
                           check benefits of upgrading to Universal Analytics here
                          </a>
                         </u>
                        </span>
                        )
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         7. Re share your old content with people
                        </span>
                       </h3>
                       <div>
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        Like I said above, you may
                        <b>
                         re-share
                        </b>
                        your content with some social networks like twitter, pinterest and even G+. Everyday some hundreds will be joining and so when you give the same topic, the newbies may find it useful, thus leading to greater exposure. So, it's no harm in re-sharing a content, but better maintain reasonable gap.
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         8. Use friends to promote
                        </span>
                       </h3>
                       <div>
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        This is also a best way of getting traffic.
                        <b>
                         Ask
                        </b>
                        your friends and family members to
                        <b>
                         share
                        </b>
                        your content with their friends and known people. People give more value to content that has been shared by their friends than a content shared by someone unknown.
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         9. Submit blogs to search engines, pinglers and directories
                        </span>
                       </h3>
                       <div>
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <b>
                         Directories
                        </b>
                        are great places for categorizing blogs under different headings. They might help in directing traffic to your blog (through backlinks) and hence got more credibility. Also
                        <b>
                         submit your site
                        </b>
                        or blog to free services like Google webmaster, Yahoo, Bing, MSN and other search engines as well as to pinglers.
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <b>
                         (
                        </b>
                        <span style="color: blue;">
                         <u>
                          <a href="http://easybloggerstuff.blogspot.com/2014/02/where-to-submit-blog-for-traffic.html">
                           Learn here where to submit your blog to drive traffic
                          </a>
                         </u>
                        </span>
                        <b>
                         )
                        </b>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         10. Social book marking sites
                        </span>
                       </h3>
                       <div>
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        Use social bookmarking sites like Reddit, Delicious, Stumbleupon and more to share your content as well as get good traffic.
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         11. Include visual media (image and videos)
                        </span>
                       </h3>
                       <div>
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        Well, here comes today's trend - visual media through images or info graphics. Visual media content is
                        <b>
                         productive
                        </b>
                        and
                        <b>
                         attracts
                        </b>
                        audience well (like videos). Images can communicate content in an
                        <b>
                         interesting, easy
                        </b>
                        and yet in
                        <b>
                         entertaining
                        </b>
                        manner. Remember, images will also appear in image search results and therefore you will get visitors this way too.
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         12. Keep posting frequently
                        </span>
                       </h3>
                       <div>
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        Audience will only remember you, when you are active and post content frequently. Set a specific
                        <b>
                         time
                        </b>
                        frame say daily, once in two days and in worst case once in a week. This is to ensure that you are active. Audience will be waiting to see your articles !
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         13. Maintain a great blog layout, easily accessible
                        </span>
                       </h3>
                       <div>
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        A very good and
                        <b>
                         visual appealing
                        </b>
                        blog layout attracts the visitors as well as search engines. Preferably don't include pop up ads, that will annoy users. Include HTML/user sitemap to give your audience an easy and good navigation experience.
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        (
                        <span style="color: blue;">
                         <u>
                          <a href="http://easybloggerstuff.blogspot.com/2014/02/how-important-is-html-sitemap-to-seo.html">
                           Lean how importance is HTML sitemap to SEO
                          </a>
                         </u>
                        </span>
                        )
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         14. Content
                        </span>
                       </h3>
                       <div>
                        <span style="color: red;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: blue;">
                         a. Offer original content
                        </span>
                       </h4>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        Content that is very rare to find in internet (not always possible) or at least; content that stands out of the crowd from others (regarding the same topic) are mostly loved by people. Show your
                        <b>
                         passion and expertise
                        </b>
                        in your content. I had also given a very detailed guide about Content marketing, you may check that for better understanding.
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: blue;">
                         b. Do keyword research before posting
                        </span>
                       </h4>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        For better search results, select page titles and include most searched keywords in page titles, URL as well as in search description. (
                        <u>
                         <span style="color: blue;">
                          <a href="http://easybloggerstuff.blogspot.com/2014/02/add-separate-search-description-for-all-blog-posts.html">
                           Learn how here
                          </a>
                         </span>
                        </u>
                        )
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: blue;">
                         c. Organize your content
                        </span>
                       </h4>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <br/>
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        Include appropriate page breaks, phrases or paragraphs. Write in a friendly tone and give facts.
                        <b>
                         Don't stuff
                        </b>
                        keywords or unnecessary words in your articles to make it lengthy. Be
                        <b>
                         natural
                        </b>
                        in writing, let the article be short.
                       </div>
                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        <span style="color: blue;">

                        <span style="color: red;">
                         15. Don'tgive up- be consistent
                        </span>
                       </h3>

                       <div style="text-align: justify;">
                        Finally, driving traffic is a
                        <b>
                         long process
                        </b>
                        and there's nothing like 'shortcuts', you need to do a lot of research in the selected niche and keep writing/posting even if you don't enjoy much traffic in the beginning. It's a slow process but steadily your site receives good traffic as well as trust in people. This is a web world and there are thousands competing with you about the same topic and it's really hard to get noted soon. Be patient and maintain consistency. This way you grow soon and enjoy.
