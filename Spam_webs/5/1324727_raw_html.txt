       <h3>
        Buffalo ups flash capacity
       </h3>
       <p class="body_description">
        Japanese memory and storage device maker Buffalo has doubled the maximum capacity of its tiny
        <a href="http://macnn.com/rd/134242==http://buffalo.jp/products/catalog/flash/usbmemory_design.html" rel="nofollow">
         RUF2-PS series
        </a>
        thumbkey USB flash drives from 8GB to 16GB. The low-profile USB drives stick out of the USB connections by less than a fifth of an inch (5mm) and are also available in 2GB, 4GB and 8GB capacities.  The design lets them stay connected to notebooks even when they're packed away in in a carrying case.
       </p>
       <p style="margin-top:20px;">
        The 16GB ThumbKey drives are expected to be available via
        <a href="http://macnn.com/rd/134205==http://www.geekstuff4u.com/buffalo-16gb-5mm-thumbkey.html" rel="nofollow">
         Geek Stuff 4 U
        </a>
        near the end of the month in black, red or white, priced at about $136. [
        <a href="http://macnn.com/rd/134206==http://www.akihabaranews.com/en/news_details.php?id=18357" rel="nofollow">
         via
