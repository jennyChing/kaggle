        <h2>
         Help me support DArcys A.R.C. as a great charity
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="http://bit.ly/13Wcvjp" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Vote for D'Arcy's Animal Rescue Centre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3644" height="113" src="http://blog.winnipeghomefinder.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Screen-Shot-2013-08-15-at-9.04.11-AM.png" width="619"/>
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <figure class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_3597" style="max-width: 300px">
         <a href="http://blog.winnipeghomefinder.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Alice.jpg">
          <img alt='...she deserves our help"' class="size-medium wp-image-3597" height="300" src="http://blog.winnipeghomefinder.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Alice-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
          she deserves our help
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <h3>
         (NOT asking for money today! Simply your vote of support is enough!!)
        </h3>
        <p>
         Hi. Im Bo Kauffmann of Remax Performance Realty. I recently signed up for a new website called
         <a href="http://propertydealtoday.com/Winnipeg/Property/Show/DArcys+Animal+Rescue+Centre/251" target="_blank">
          Property Deal Today
         </a>
         , which matches real estate agents with charities of their choice. The site brings together real estate agents with handymen, roofers, movers, lawyers, heating and a/c companies, basically anything real estate and home ownership related.
        </p>
        <p>
         The site also donates
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          <strong>
           10% of our membership fees towards charities
          </strong>
         </span>
         , thereby raising thousands of dollars per year. This money is
         <strong>
          divided among the charities
         </strong>
         according to
         <strong>
          how much public support each one
         </strong>
         of them receives. With so many great causes, choosing just one was not easy. But our love for animals has led me to support
        </p>
        <h3>
         <em>
          <strong>
           DArcys Animal Rescue Centre in Winnipeg
          </strong>
         </em>
         .
        </h3>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          <em>
           DArcys helps
           <strong>
            stray and homeless animals
           </strong>
           , as well as cares for
           <strong>
            neglected and abused cats and dogs
           </strong>
           . They are largely volunteer based, and operate on donations, and the more I learned about them, the more I realized that this is the charity I wanted to support.
          </em>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.winnipeghomefinder.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/bo-week-3.jpg">
          <img alt="bo-week-3" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-4519" height="135" src="http://blog.winnipeghomefinder.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/bo-week-3-300x135.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         Here is the unique part of this: YOU can help to make sure that DArcy receives our financial support,
         <strong>
          WITHOUT ever donating a single penny
         </strong>
         . All it would take is about 30 seconds of your time. Here is how:
        </p>
        <p>
         Click on the link below, which will take you to the voting page on propertydealtoday.com. A popup will give you the option to vote for DArcys Animal Rescue Centre in any number of ways: via facebook, twitter, linkedin or by simply e-mailing that page to a friend. Taking each of these actions will result in a vote, so, (just like in a russian federal election), you can vote up to 4 times.
         <strong>
          Please vote as often as possible, and make sure to email the page
         </strong>
         to a friend and ask them to do the same.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         .
         <a href="http://bit.ly/13Wcvjp">
          <img alt="Vote for D'Arcy's Animal Rescue Centre" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3644" height="113" src="http://blog.winnipeghomefinder.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Screen-Shot-2013-08-15-at-9.04.11-AM.png" width="619"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
