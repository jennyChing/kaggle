                <a data-lightboxplus="lightbox[5311]" href="http://beta.gadgetzz.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/myo-armband-xl-740x493.jpg">
                 <img alt="myo-armband-xl-740x493" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-5312" height="199" src="http://beta.gadgetzz.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/myo-armband-xl-740x493-300x199.jpg" width="300"/>

                <br/>
                This is the MYO, a new motion controller mouse, but  this one is special.
                <br/>
                MYO has a sensor to detect the electrical impulses in the muscles of your forearm. This, alongside a motion detector, allows the armband to resolve your hand and arm movements with extreme precision, down to the individual fingers. It may even seem at times that your finger twitch is detected even before youve actually done so: this is because the muscles are activated slightly before your fingers actually start moving, and the MYO picks the signal up first.
               </p>
               <p>
                So what can you do with it? Thats still in development. Theres going to be an API, and it will work with PCs and Macs, but the specific kinds of things you can do is up in the air. Were thinking mouse replacement, but of course its much more than that. 3D model manipulation, gaming If done right, the possibilities are, as they always say, endless. The best part is the price: $149, to be released late this year. And yes, you can
                <a href="https://getmyo.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                 pre-order now.
