           The Most Counterintuitive Ways That Melt A Mans Heart
          </a>
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-meta">
         </div>
         <!-- .entry-meta -->
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="6861547" data-link="http://katarinaphang.com/the-most-counterintuitive-ways-that-melt-a-mans-heart/" data-summary="" data-title="The Most Counterintuitive Ways That Melt A Man's Heart">
         </div>
         <h2>
          <img alt="how to be an irresistible woman" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-107264" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/katarinaphang.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/attract-love.jpg?resize=500%2C304"/>
         </h2>
         <h2>
          How to be an Irresistible Woman using my Counter-intuitive Ways
         </h2>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Soanother goddess has been born. Another Woman has discovered how to be an irresistible woman. Gina signed up for both my previous cycles of
          <a href="http://katarinaphang.com/coaching/leaning-back-and-cultivating-feminine-mystique-workshop/" title="Leaning Back And Cultivating Feminine Mystique Workshop">
           Leaning Back workshop
          </a>
          (well start Cycle 3 February 9). She is now among my star students who just get it (read her Holiday Blues story
          <a href="http://katarinaphang.com/how-to-deal-with-holiday-blues-let-go-of-your-holiday-expectations/" title="How To Deal With Holiday Blues: Let Go Of Your Holiday Expectations">
           here
          </a>
          ). Her Emotionally Unavailable Man just claimed her a few weeks ago. Attracting men and keeping them attracted are just now so effortless thanks to her newly gained insight.
         </p>
         <p>
          Her post in the group touches two issues I want to address here: 1) why softening boundaries is the most counterintuitive thing that I teach and how to be an irresistibits responsible in creating the aura of irresistibly in my clients, and 2) why men need to give in relationship to feel like a man.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <blockquote>
          <h4>
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #993300;">
             <em>
              Ive really loved how Katarina always urges us to soften our standards. I used to only see my guy once a week or so, and I couldnt get him to invite me on one of his out-of-town trips to save my life. Communicating my desire for both didnt help the situation. Once I let go of the desire for both and shifted my energy toward other people and activities that made me happy, he really stepped up. Now our dates last three days and he plans romantic get-away weekends down to every little detail. Try letting go of your expectations and the universe will bring you unexpected gifts.
             </em>
            </span>
           </strong>
          </h4>
          <h4>
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #993300;">
             <em>
              I think its miraculous. His complete turn around is why I call Katarina a genius. Do less and receive more, its truly amazing. I have also made it a point to compliment him on his masculinity: strength, handsomeness, sexiness. Can you please lift my 5-gallon water container onto the dispenseryou are so strong! Thank you, you are the best. Im a triathlete, I could lift my water myself, but why miss the opportunity to make him feel great for making my life easier?! ~Gina, Los Angeles
             </em>
            </span>
           </strong>
          </h4>
         </blockquote>
         <p>
          <span id="more-4094">
          </span>
          First, what is the softening of boundaries?
         </p>
         <p>
          I talk about it at length in my
          <a href="http://katarinaphang.com/coaching/the-journey-inward/" title="The Journey Inward: The First Defining Steps Toward Feminine Magnetism Mastery">
           Journey Inward program
          </a>
          but as a quick summary softening of boundaries pertains to the sense of oneness with everything else in the universe. The more spiritually evolved we are, the more we feel connected to everyone else. Boundaries, by definition, separate and with those walls we build around ourselves we often recoil in this constricted sense of self that traps us in our suffering. Boundaries dont invite love to get in and out of your life freely so it grows by leaps and bounds nourishing our soul. The more boundaries we erect the more readily we perceive slights to the integrity of our self.
         </p>
         <p>
          Those with very rigid boundaries come across as jaded and hardly feminine. By definition, femininity is about softness. When you are hardened, you are blocking your feminine radiance from shining through. If you are
          <a href="http://katarinaphang.com/the-reasons-why-you-are-perpetually-single/" target="_blank">
           perpetually single
          </a>
          -and not happy-, this may be one of the main reasons.
         </p>
         <p>
          A lot of people falsely believe that by erecting boundaries they protect themselves from hurt. The opposite is what actually happens. Boundaries which are motivated by fear motivate an anticipation of problems and energy flows where attention goes. You see problems everywhere, so problems arise. Thats how powerful our mind is.
         </p>
         <p>
          Boundaries also spring from expectations, more often than not. And the reinforcement of them springs from reactiveness. This kind of boundaries dont get you closer to what you want. Its throwing the baby with the bathwater. This is not how to be irresistible to men.
         </p>
         <p>
          Having said that, of course each one of us must have healthy boundaries. What are healthy boundaries? Those are your deal-breakers, the boundaries that have been tested and are implemented after rigorous inner work so you know for sure what you will put up with and what you wont.
         </p>
         <p>
          Second, the nature of the masculine is giving and doing. For a man, when hes in the position of giving and accomplishing something he feels his male power. And the opposite is true too; i.e. when a man is conditioned to receive in relationship, it is slowly and surely emasculating to him whether or not either realizes it. It is very damaging (and Ive come to the conclusion since I was in this role for over 16 years).
         </p>
         <p>
          A man who is in the receiving role in relationship will feel overpowered and feminized by what his limbic system perceives every day. It depolarizes the relationship and it makes him lose all the motivations to be a man in relationship and thats when he starts taking his woman for granted. Its all unconscious, albeit.
          <br data-reactid=".1c0.1:3:1:$comment10152921898551686_30636981:0.0.$right.0.$left.0.0.0:3.0.$end:0:$1:0"/>
          <br data-reactid=".1c0.1:3:1:$comment10152921898551686_30636981:0.0.$right.0.$left.0.0.0:3.0.$end:0:$3:0"/>
          Now I am taking the conscious decision to be in the feminine role of receiving in relationship and this is the core of my teaching and the results in my own and my clients lives have been AMAZING.
         </p>
         <p>
          A man has this incessant thirst for making his woman happy and he thrives on the rewards (gratitude and appreciation) she showers him with. He feels like a man and thrives on it and the more he will shine in his masculine while at the same time the deeper you bask in your feminine. Being in your feminine is how to be an irresistible woman.
         </p>
         <p>
          You see how this works in a feedback loop?
         </p>
         <p>
          So when you want your guy to shower you with love and affection, the most effective of doing it by being a joyful and grateful feminine woman. Being reactive, jaded, and expressive of your upset feelings (in the name of boundaries as per above) because you focus on his shortcomings unfortunately only create resistance in him.
         </p>
         <p>
          So does it mean you shouldnt give at all? Of course not. The best way is to give without expecting anything in return and it makes you happy in itself. But always beware when you over give (more than he does)because the next minute that can be the reason of your relationships demise.
         </p>
         <p>
          When a man feels understood and accepted, he will be more than happy giving you everything you want. Thats the essence of luring with honey.
         </p>
         <p>
          Whatever your situation is my programs can make it better as long as you are committed to changing yourself to be comfortable in your feminine role.
         </p>
         <p>
          When you change to become more feminine, your man cant NOT notice and change himself accordingly. I promise you, I have seen this play out time and again in my and my clients lives. This is why my method works so fast.
         </p>
         <p>
          This is sexual polarity in action.
         </p>
         <p>
          In light of that, you dont want to miss
          <a href="http://katarinaphang.com/coaching/leaning-back-and-cultivating-feminine-mystique-workshop/" title="Leaning Back And Cultivating Feminine Mystique Workshop">
           this one-of-a-kind workshop.
          </a>
          Three weeks of very affordable coaching that will change your perspective on men and relationship forever the way it changed Ginas.
         </p>
         <p>
          And the new program
          <a href="http://katarinaphang.com/coaching/four-components-of-melting-his-heart-group-coaching/" target="_blank" title="Four Components Of Melting His Heart Group Coaching">
           Four Components Of Melting His Heart
          </a>
          will take you on the path toward lifelong bonding with your man.
         </p>
