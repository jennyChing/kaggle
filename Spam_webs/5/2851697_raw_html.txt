         <div class="section warnings sticky ">
          <h2>
           <span class="mw-headline" id="Warnings">
            Warnings
           </span>
          </h2>
          <div class="section_text" id="warnings">
           <ul>
            <li>
             In Arabic, gender related words are very common. For example,
             <i>
              Anta
             </i>
             (you) for a male and
             <i>
              Anti
             </i>
             (you) for a female.
            </li>
            <li>
             Some people in the Middle East (especially kids) can't understand foreign pronounced Arabic word so try to make your pronunciation as accurate as you can.
            </li>
           </ul>
