       <h1>
        Conceptis Slitherlink: Loop the Loop on Your iPad and iPhone!
       </h1>
       <span class="date">
        Thursday, May 2, 2013
       </span>
       <img alt="Conceptis Slitherlink: Loop the Loop on Your iPad and iPhone!" class="no-caption fw492 wrappedimage" src="picture/12/4059.jpg" title="Conceptis Slitherlink: Loop the Loop on Your iPad and iPhone!"/>
       <p>
        Conceptis Slitherlink for iPad and iPhone, a no-frills puzzle game that requires creating a loop passing around clues in a grid using logic deduction, is now available free on App Store. With 120 free puzzles starting with very easy 6x8 grids for beginners and increasing in size to extremely hard 16x22 grids for the experts, Conceptis Slitherlink will get every puzzle fan hooked before the first loop is looped!
       </p>
       <div class="sidestory">
        <h1>
         Enjoy Slitherlink on your mobile device?
        </h1>
        <p>
         Tap: Help  More  Rate and Review to support Conceptis by rating and reviewing the game on the App Store!
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Slitherlink, also known in the west as Loop the Loop, Loopy and Fences, is an addictive loop forming puzzle which was invented in Japan. Conceptis Slitherlink is designed to provide the ultimate experience for playing these puzzles on both iPad and iPhone. The game features 2-finger tapping for quick zoom, auto complete clues setting and a link segment highlighting option to help to avoid creating separate loops.
       </p>
       <p>
        Can't wait to put your looping skills to the test? Download
        <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/conceptis-slitherlink/id605918324?ls=1&amp;mt=8" shape="rect" target="_blank">
         Conceptis Slitherlink
        </a>
        [iTunes link] and start solving right now!
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/conceptis-slitherlink/id605918324?ls=1&amp;mt=8" shape="rect" target="_blank">
         <img alt="Conceptis Slitherlink" src="picture/21/3847.png"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Features
       </h2>
       <ul class="circle">
        <li>
         120 free Classic Slitherlink puzzle samples
        </li>
        <li>
         Concurrently playing and saving multiple puzzles
        </li>
        <li>
         Puzzle library sorting options
        </li>
        <li>
         Unlimited Undo and Redo
        </li>
        <li>
         Auto complete clues
        </li>
        <li>
         Highlight link segment
        </li>
        <li>
         Quick zoom using 2-finger tap
        </li>
        <li>
         Check puzzle
        </li>
        <li>
         Track puzzle solving times
        </li>
       </ul>
