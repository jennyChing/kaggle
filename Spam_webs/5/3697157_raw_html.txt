             The best way to notify users about upcoming migration is to place a banner on a front page or an email notification.
            </li>
            <li>
             Customize your new storefront after migration is done.
            </li>
            <li>
             Make a test purchase on Shopify to ensure everything is running fine.
            </li>
           </ul>
