          <h1>
           Top Farewell Gift Ideas
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="post-info">
          <span>
           Posted by
           <i class="icon icon-tags">
           </i>
           <span itemprop="articleSection">
            <a href="https://www.redwrappings.com.au/blogs/category/1_gift-suggestions.html">

          </div>
          <div class="sdsarticle-des">
           <p>
            Can't think of a good farewell gift? Here are some top farewell gift ideas when you have to say goodbye. Find the best hand-picked farewell gift ideas for men, women, colleagues, friends and family when you want to say 'bon voyage'.If they are visitors leaving Australia after their visit, the Aboriginal Collectables will make a great farewell gift to remember Australia by. If they are moving to somewhere cold, a Gold Coast Jacket would be a thoughtful farewell gift idea. If they are taking time out to bum around and see the world, a Transit Duffle Bag will be useful. If they are off to London, how about a set of London Bus Cufflinks for a bit of humour to take away the sadness of having to say goodbye? Top Farewell Gift Ideas
           </p>
           <p>
            ---
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Journey Cuff Bangle with Inspirational Quote
             </strong>
             - This cuff bangle with its inspirational quote is a great way to say goodbye and wish them a safe journey.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Caricature - Personalised Single Watercolor and Ink
             </strong>
             - Having a caricature of the person going away would be a lovely farewell gift idea, as a precious keepsake.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Seasonal Sensation
             </strong>
             - Leave a long lasting memory with this bright, beautiful selection of seasonal flowers - a nice gesture when saying good bye.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Guys Work Day Survival Kit
             </strong>
             - Say 'good luck on your new job' to your coworker with this funny farewell gift - a workday survival kit for him. Workday survival kit for her is also available.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Aboriginal Collectables - Didgeridoo Long
             </strong>
             - Bid farewell to a visitor with this Aboriginal Collectable  a great farewell gift.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Traveller Gift Hamper for Men
             </strong>
             - He will appreciate the comfort provided by the squidgy air neck pillow when he travels, not to mention the travel essentials.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Gold Coast Jacket
             </strong>
             - A warm jacket is definitely a thoughtful farewell gift idea for someone moving to a cold country.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Globes - 1921 USA Globe Weber Costello
             </strong>
             - A friend going overseas for good? Say farewell with this globe which will be a constant reminder of where home is each time they look at it.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Citizen Gents Eco Driver Super Chrono Watch
             </strong>
             - When it's a retirement farewell gift, you want it to be special. Say goodbye with this Super Chrono Watch.
            </li>
            <li>
             <strong>
              Gift Box of Truffles &amp; Petit Fours
             </strong>
             - Having a farewell party in the office? Enjoy this Gift Box of Truffles &amp; Petit Fours together with all your coworkers!
            </li>
           </ul>
