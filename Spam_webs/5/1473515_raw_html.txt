      <section class="artist-wiki">
       <header>
        <hgroup>
         <h2 class="rm">
          Biography
         </h2>
         <h3 class="remove-top-margin">
          Bangor and Donaghadee, Northern Ireland, UK    
    (
          <span itemprop="foundingDate">
           2007
          </span>
           present)
         </h3>
        </hgroup>
       </header>
       <div class="wiki">
        <div class="wiki-abstract" data-require="music/wiki" id="wikiAbstract">
         <div class="wiki-text">
          Two Door Cinema Club is a Bangor and Donaghadee, Northern Ireland, UK,
          <a class="bbcode_tag" href="http://www.last.fm/tag/electropop" rel="tag">
           electropop
          </a>
          / alternative band, formed 2007, consisting of
          <a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Alex+Trimble">
           Alex Trimble
          </a>
          (vocals, guitar),
          <a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Kevin+Baird">
           Kevin Baird
          </a>
          (bass, vocals),
          <a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Sam+Halliday">
           Sam Halliday
          </a>
          (guitar, vocals) and, live only,
          <a class="bbcode_artist" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Benjamin+Thompson">
           Benjamin Thompson
          </a>
          (drums). Their dbut EP was
          <a class="bbcode_album" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Two+Door+Cinema+Club/Four+Words+to+Stand+On" title="Two Door Cinema Club - Four Words to Stand On">
           Four Words to Stand On
          </a>
          (Mar 2008, self-release) and album
          <a class="bbcode_album" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Two+Door+Cinema+Club/Tourist+History" title="Two Door Cinema Club - Tourist History">
           Tourist History
          </a>
          (Mar 2010,
          <a class="bbcode_label" href="http://www.last.fm/label/Kitsun%C3%A9">
           Kitsun
          </a>
          ). Their latest is single 
          <a class="bbcode_album" href="http://www.last.fm/music/Two+Door+Cinema+Club/Handshake" title="Two Door Cinema Club - Handshake">
           Handshake
          </a>
           (May 2013, Kitsun).
          <span class="hide">
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The bands name resulted from Hallidays mispronunciation of the local cinemas name, Tudor Cinema.
           <br/>
           <br/>

           <br/>
