&lt;div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;I interviewed Angela this week about&lt;a href="http://www.angengland.com/idevaffiliate/idevaffiliate.php?id=107"&gt; her course&lt;/a&gt; so that she could share a little bit more about it and help you decide if it is the right thing for you! One thing that has been so impressive and unique about Angela, her products, books and courses, is the personalized help that she offers. I was in crisis mode unable to figure out a formatting glitch in my own book, so I emailed her about it one Saturday night. She went back and forth with me for over an hour until we figured it out, encouraging me all the way. She treats every client like they are the most important person, working on the most important book in the world. She is fantastic to work with, which is why I can whole-heartedly recommend her course to each of you, that are hoping to &lt;a href="http://www.angengland.com/idevaffiliate/idevaffiliate.php?id=107"&gt;self publish&lt;/a&gt;!&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div&gt;But enough from me, on to the interview with Angela!&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.angengland.com/idevaffiliate/idevaffiliate.php?id=107_2_1_9" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter" style="border: 0px;" alt="Ready? &lt;div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;&lt;strong&gt;I&#8217;ve read a lot about &lt;a href="http://www.angengland.com/idevaffiliate/idevaffiliate.php?id=107"&gt;your program&lt;/a&gt;, but I know many of the readers here have not, can you introduce us to the course in a few sentences?&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;Sure! I&#8217;m so excited about this course because writing a book can be a transforming experience for people when it&#8217;s done right, but there are plenty of authors that never see the success they want. In fact, the average self-published author only sells 250 copies of their book. My goal with the course was to walk people through the system I used to sell thousands of copies of my books and usually charge 4 or 5 figures to employ on behalf of clients one-on-one. The course gives people access to everything, including personalized help.&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;&lt;strong&gt;What makes&lt;a href="http://www.angengland.com/idevaffiliate/idevaffiliate.php?id=107"&gt; your course&lt;/a&gt; unique from the other classes on publishing?&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;That&#8217;s such a good question &#8211; there are some amazing courses out there already. What I find is that they tend to focus either on how to be a writer (Like Jeff Goins awesome Tribe Writers), or how to make a million bucks on Kindle. With Achieve Your Publishing Dreams people are equipped to not only select a savvy topic, but how to develop their idea into a real book, produce it professionally, and then share it with the world in a powerful and effective way. Plus they get a full toolkit of handouts, checklists, and other resources to aid them during the entire process.&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;

&lt;div&gt;&lt;strong&gt;At what point in their writing career should some one purchase your course? Do potential clients need to have all their book-plans already in order? Or will the course help them figure out exactly what they want to write and how to do it?&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
