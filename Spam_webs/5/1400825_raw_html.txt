        <p style="text-align: center">
         This is one of the most weird skin conditions to have ever been discovered. An Indonesian teenager Ari Wibowo sheds his skin just like the snakes do. He shades his skin after every 41 days and he must always smear his body more often with moisturizer just to prevent his skin from hardening and seizing up.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <a href="http://www.weirdlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/1411525750912_wps_6_Ari_Wibowo_a_16_year_old_.jpg" rel="prettyphoto[3637]">
          <img alt="1411525750912_wps_6_Ari_Wibowo_a_16_year_old_" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3638" height="400" src="//www.weirdlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/1411525750912_wps_6_Ari_Wibowo_a_16_year_old_.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div class="ad-inserter ad-inserter-2" style="text-align:center;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;margin-top:8px;margin-bottom:8px;">

        <p style="text-align: center">
         The Indonesian doctors have confessed to be unwilling and unable to treat the disease of this weird and rare skin condition which has now developed and left the teenager with a very extraordinary appearance.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         It is said that Ari Wibowo sufferers from Erythroderma, which is an inflammatory skin disease also known as red man syndrome, that causes the skin over almost the entire body to become scaly and flake off.
         <br/>
         It is being claimed that Ari has already shed his skin for every 41 days since he was born 16 years ago. The teenager must always soak his body in water every hour  night and day  and smear himself with lotion every three hours to prevent drying out.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         This boys weird skin condition was documented by photographer Nurcholis Anhari Lubis, 35 as part of his essay project. Nurcholis describes the boys skin condition as scaly like a snake about to shed his skin.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         The teenagers body would shrivel and harden up like a sculpture and he wont be able to move if he is not moisturize or soak his skin in water.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center">
         <img alt="index" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3641" height="333" src="//www.weirdlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/index4.jpg" width="500"/>
         <br/>
         In case he is left unattended for long, he will not be able to speak since the wrinkles inside his mouth would go hard and it would dry out all his blood in his body. Nurcholis Anhari Lubis revealed.
         <br/>
         <img alt="images" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3640" height="403" src="//www.weirdlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/images.jpg" width="600"/>
         <br/>
         Mr Lubis further claimed that Aris family were told by doctors at the hospital where he was born that it did not have the resources to treat the condition, and asked them to take the infant away.
         <br/>
         Ari eats normal food like everyone else and his favorite snacks are instant noodles and crackers.
        </p>
