           <h2 class="posts_title entry-title">
            <a href="http://www.quickneasyrecipes.net/apple-pie-baked-in-the-apples/">
             Apple Pie Baked in the Apples
            </a>
           </h2>
           <div class="ribbon-shadow">
           </div>
           <!-- ribbon shadow -->
          </div>
          <!-- ribbon top -->
          <article class="post_container">
           <!--Call @response Meta hook-->
           <div class="meta">
            in
            <a href="http://www.quickneasyrecipes.net/category/desserts/apple/" rel="category tag">
             Apple
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://www.quickneasyrecipes.net/category/desserts/" rel="category tag">
             Desserts
            </a>
            .
           </div>
           <div class="entry entry-content">
            <div class="wpInsert wpInsertInPostAd wpInsertAbove" style="margin: 5px; padding: 0px; align: center;">
             <script src="http://ap.lijit.com///www/delivery/fpi.js?z=202875&amp;u=easykitchenrecipes&amp;width=300&amp;height=250" type="text/javascript">
             </script>
            </div>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.quickneasyrecipes.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/Apple-Pie-4.png">
              <img alt="" class="size-full wp-image-388 aligncenter" height="231" src="http://www.quickneasyrecipes.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/Apple-Pie-4.png" title="Apple-Pie-4" width="466"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             <strong>
              Step 1:
             </strong>
             Cut off the top of 4 apples off and discard. Remove the inside of each apple with a spoon or melon baller very carefully, as to not puncture the peel.
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             If youre a skilled interior apple excavator, salvage as much as you can so you can use it for Step 2. I, on the other hand, am not skilled so I just had to throw my interior apples away and chop up additional apples for filling.
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             <a href="http://domesticdilettante.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Apple-Pie-3.png">
              <img alt="" height="204" src="http://domesticdilettante.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Apple-Pie-3.png" title="Apple " width="189"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             This recipe will make 4 baked apple pies (in the apple).
             <span id="more-387">
             </span>
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             <strong>
              Step 2:
             </strong>
             Remove skin from remaining apple(s) and slice very thinly. These apple pieces will give you the additional filling needed to fill the four apples you are baking.
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             Mix sliced apples with sugars and cinnamon in a bowl. If you prefer more or less cinnamon make adjustments as desired. Same goes for the sugar.
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             Scoop sliced apples into hollow apples.
            </p>
            <div class="wpInsert wpInsertInPostAd wpInsertMiddle" style="margin: 5px; padding: 0px;">
             <script id="mNCC" language="javascript">
              medianet_width='468';  medianet_height= '60';  medianet_crid='178417384';
             </script>
             <script id="mNSC" language="javascript" src="http://contextual.media.net/nmedianet.js?cid=8CUTE77EL">
             </script>
            </div>
            <p align="center">
             <strong>
              Step 3:
             </strong>
             Roll out pie crust and slice into 1/4 inch strips. You can also add a strip of pastry inside the top of the apple almost like a liner to add a little more texture/sweetness to the pie.
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             Cover the top of the apple in a lattice pattern with pie crust strips.
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             <a href="http://domesticdilettante.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Apple-Pie-2.jpeg">
              <img alt="" height="195" src="http://domesticdilettante.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Apple-Pie-2.jpeg" title="Apple Pie" width="270"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             <strong>
              Step 4:
             </strong>
             Place apples in an 88 pan. Add just enough water to the cover the bottom of the pan.
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             Cover with foil and bake for 20-25 minutes.
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             Remove foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes or until crust is golden brown and sliced apples are soft.
            </p>
            <p align="center">
             <a href="http://domesticdilettante.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Apple-Pie-1.jpeg">
              <img alt="" height="202" src="http://domesticdilettante.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Apple-Pie-1.jpeg" title="Apple Pie" width="270"/>
             </a>
            </p>
