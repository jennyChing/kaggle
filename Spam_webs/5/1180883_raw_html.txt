         <p>
          What could be possibly be more fun than making your own slime? Flubber is fun to play with and fun to make. You can stretch it, bounce it, roll it, and you can even stick straws into it and blow it! Making your own Flubber is a great science experiment.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Flubber recipe
         </h2>
         <p>
          2 cups white glue
          <br/>
          1 1/2 cups warm water
          <br/>
          1 cup hot water
          <br/>
          3 teaspoons Borax
          <br/>
          food coloring
         </p>
         <p>
          Mix glue, warm water and several drops of green food coloring. Mix and dissolve Borax in hot water. Pour into glue mixture, 1/3 at a time, mixing thoroughly with hands after each addition.
         </p>
         <p>
          Flubber is non-edible. Store Flubber in an air-tight container for up to 3 weeks.When you are done with it, do not wash down the drain.
