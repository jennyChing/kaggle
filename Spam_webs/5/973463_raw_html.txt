       <h1>
        SEO in 2015: Revamp &amp; Adapt Your SEO Strategy
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="attribution">
         Posted by admin2 on
         <span class="date">
          01/05/15
         </span>
        </span>
        |
        <a class="comments" href="http://www.increaseyourrank.com/seo-in-205-strategy.html#exp-comments">
         0 Comments

        <span class="tags">
         Tags:

          seo
         </a>

         <a href="http://www.increaseyourrank.com/tag/social-media">

         </a>

        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="bodycopy">
        <p>
         <img alt="SEO in 2015" src="/files/SEO-in-2015.jpg" style="width: 250px; height: 166px; float: right; margin: 7px;"/>
         In 2015, the SEO climate is expecting rapid changes. And as a result, businesses and website owners need to revamp and adapt their SEO strategies to remain both competitive and free of any penalty risks.
        </p>
        <p>
         To help you plan for a solid approach to
         <strong>
          SEO in 2015
         </strong>
         , below we share some insights, elements, and considerations to embrace for this new year. Let's dive right in.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Social Media
        </h2>
        <p>
         Search engines will be putting a greater emphasis on the content that is generated by way of social media. Therefore companies that are increasing their presence on the major social media platforms (Twitter, FaceBook, Pinterest, etc.) stand to gain considerable benefits early.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Website Content
        </h2>
        <p>
         Content that is does not serve the latest algorithmic updates should be revamped. This includes content via websites, blogs, static articles, and product descriptions.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Semantic Search
        </h2>
        <p>
         Since search engines are learning to determine the context of a search, this new approach is quickly making a dinosaur of the traditional keyword search. A semantic search is based upon multiple variables that range from previous searches to geographical location. For 2015, business must strive to target their audience while determining what motivates them.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Analytics in Real-time
        </h2>
        <p>
         Google Analytics and more SEO specialists have tools that allow them to discover immediately how well campaigns are faring by way of search engines. This will assist specialists will faster planning and improved implementations.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Increased Ratings Competition
        </h2>
        <p>
         In 2015, there are an increased number of companies that are learning that SEO is an important marketing tool. With that being said, the competition regarding top rankings will rival Olympic trials in the New Year.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Mobile Web Takes the Lead
        </h2>
        <p>
         Now that the vast majority of the world has a smartphone and/or tablet, most of their web browsing is taking place there as well. Therefore businesses must take note that mobile search engines differ from traditional search engines. Having a
         <a href="http://www.oicgroup.net/">
          mobile-friendly web design in Peoria
         </a>
         will be essential to survive, along with SEO.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Quality over Quantity
        </h2>
        <p>
         The days were well placed keys words would guarantee your company impeccable ratings are swiftly dwindling away. Due to the change in searches as well as consumers desiring actual information, content needs to be filled with quality content. Key words can still be used but not as a dominate feature.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Paid Amplifications
        </h2>
        <p>
         With the increase of companies using every form of web content available (static articles, blogs, etc.) companies will be forced to pay for advertising in order to achieve an edge above their competition. This method will allow your brand to be placed before millions of people as your competitors continued to crank out a multitude of web content.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Augmented Reality
        </h2>
        <p>
         With the introduction of products such as the Apple Watch, augmented reality will pose quite a few issues for
         <a href="http://www.clickcentricseo.com/">
          click centric SEO specialists
         </a>
         for the majority of 2015. Content that is expressly immersive (videos, pictures) will continue to be valuable as a method of attracting new customers.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Softening of the Sales Technique
        </h2>
        <p>
         <img alt="SEO Social Media" src="/files/SEO-Social-Media.jpg" style="width: 250px; height: 256px; float: left; margin: 7px;"/>
         In 2015 you will begin to notice that consumers have far less tolerance for aggressive sales techniques. Therefore sales tactics must be altered in order to avoid missing out on potential revenue. Be prepared for the consumer to be in more in control than ever before as it pertains to how they are sold products and services.
        </p>
        <p>
         Moreover,
         <a href="http://www.tctopofmind.com/traverse-city-seo-guide">
          SEO strategies for 2015
         </a>
         will be ushering in a few changes that will need to be adhered to in order for companies to remain in the eye of the public. The realm of SEO is forever changing and it is imperative to never become comfortable with current trends to the point that you become outdated more swiftly than the wind blows in Chicago.
        </p>
       </div>
