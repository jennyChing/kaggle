        <h2 class="theme-p-title theme-color3" data-clicktarget="#" data-target="title">
         Personality Assessment: Color Personality Test!
        </h2>
        <div class="theme-p theme-color-4" data-target="content">
         <p>
          Personality assessment of a person is an attempt to objectively measure different aspects of your personality. Nowadays, you are most likely to encounter psychometric testing as a part of the recruitment or selection process. Moreover, occupational psychometric tests are also specifically designed to provide employers with a reliable method of selecting the most suitable job applications or candidates for promotion.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Color personality test:
           <br/>
           <br/>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Have you ever wondered why there are so many color choices available for almost everything we purchase? It is because your preference for a particular color is a very personal one and psychological. It tells about your personal attitude towards life, as well as your emotional states.
          <br/>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          You can find various online color personality tests. These tests have a history of over 20 years as it was first invented by Dr.Max Luscher, a psychologist from Switzerland. He believed that sensory perception of color is objective and universally shared by all, but the color preferences are subjective, and that this distinction allows subjective states to be objectively measured by using test colors.
          <br/>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          According to him, a persons choice of color could reveal their personality traits. Therefore, people who select identical combination of colors have similar personalities.
          <br/>
          <br/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Significance
           <br/>
           <br/>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Color preferences and their relationship with personality and psychological traits have been utilized in advertising, automotive and the fashion industry for years. It has become easy to gain some insight into your inner struggles and problems with this highly effective color test.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Other than
          <a data-id="l-8454548044" href="http://www.mysecretpotential.com/personality-test" rel="nofollow">
           personality
          </a>
          <a data-id="l-3273907350" href="http://www.mysecretpotential.com/personality-test" rel="nofollow">
           tests
          </a>
          there are various other performance tests, such as aptitude test, psychology test, stress test and many more. These tests are particularly aimed at measuring attributes like intelligence, aptitude and personality, providing employer an insight into how well you work with other people, how well you handle stress, and whether you will be able to cope with the intellectual demand of the job. There is no specific preparation required for such tests. Nevertheless, you can find a number of aptitude tests online. Most of these are free of cost and easy to understand.
         </p>

                this.pingTimerId = window.setTimeout(function () {

                }, this.pingTimeInterval);

                if (this.timeLimitReached()) {
                    this.stop();
                } else {
                    this.incrementPingTimeInterval();

             * 2X time : from 5 sec to 10 min (5, 10, 20, 40, 80, 160, ...)

                    this.pingTimeInterval += 15 * MINUTE;
                } else {
                    this.pingTimeInterval *= 2;
                }

                beaconPixel.src = PING_URL + '?timestamp=' + timestamp;
