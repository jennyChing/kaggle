        <p>
         Anybody whos ever
         <strong>
          worked in the food services industry
         </strong>
         , or any industry for that matter, has probably come across some odd complaints. However, the one Sarah from a Whole Foods in Boston received might just take the cake for weirdest complaint hidden in a compliment ever.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="myonlycomplaintisthecarrots" class="size-full wp-image-2394 aligncenter" src="http://www.topcultured.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/myonlycomplaintisthecarrots1.jpg" style="border: 1px solid black; padding: 3px;" title="myonlycomplaintisthecarrots" width="430"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <span id="more-2392">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         And for those having a hard time reading the handwritten complaint:
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Love this Whole Foods. Best in Boston area. However, the carrots are too pointy and long. Its intimidating. Can you shorten them and make them less scary?
          </strong>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
