        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         World Map Wall Art DIY
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-01-23T09:07:49+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          23 January 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/author/bumblev/" itemprop="url" rel="author">

            Vanessa Brady

          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">

          </a>

        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-11-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="World Map Wall Art DIY - Make this large art piece with just a few supplies! | triedandtrueblog.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8643" height="1020" src="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-11-2.jpg" width="680"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/world-map-wall-art-diy/&amp;media=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-11-2.jpg&amp;description=World Map Wall Art DIY')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <em>
          Ive got a super special treat for you all, my sister is back with another awesome DIY home tutorial! Shes got an amazing eye for home decor and is crazy crafty. The perfect combination for awesome DIY projects. I know you guys are going to love this World Map Wall Art tutorial!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Hi everyone, Im Vanessas sister, Adriana, and itstime to fill up another wall in the house. Last time I was here, I shared the
         <a href="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/minimal-modern-indoor-herb-garden/" target="_blank" title="Minimal Indoor Herb Garden">
          Minimal Indoor Herb Garden
         </a>
         that I made forour dining room. Now its time to focus on our living room! WhenI started designing my room I totally fell in love with all the world map art that is prevalent on Pinterest. Knowing that I needed a specific size and wood stain, I consulted with my sisterto see if DIY-ing this was a possibilityand it was!
         <span id="more-8635">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-12-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="World Map Wall Art DIY - Blank Wall" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8645" height="453" src="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-12-2.jpg" width="680"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/world-map-wall-art-diy/&amp;media=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-12-2.jpg&amp;description=World Map Wall Art DIY')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         <em>
          This post may contain affiliate links for your convenience and to assist in the maintenance of this site.
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         It first started with purchasing a -inch 8x4 piece of plywood and having it cut to four 2x1.5 panels by the kind folks at our local
         <a href="http://www.homedepot.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.homedepot.com', 'Home Depot']);" target="_blank" title="Home Depot">
          Home Depot
         </a>
         . We gave each panel a light sanding and then stained each piecetwice to achieve our desired color.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="World Map Wall Art DIY - Silhouette" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8636" height="453" src="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-3-2.jpg" width="680"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/world-map-wall-art-diy/&amp;media=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-3-2.jpg&amp;description=World Map Wall Art DIY')">
          </span>
         </span>
         Next, we used a
         <a href="http://amzn.to/1CZ6GjS" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://amzn.to/1CZ6GjS', 'Silhouette cutting machine']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Amazon: Silhouette">
          Silhouette cutting machine
         </a>
         to cut the world image on contact paper. This was a little tricky as the width of the panels was larger than the width of the Silhouette machineso a few hairs were pulled out trying to remember which sections had already been cut (heres the
         <a href="http://www.silhouettedesignstore.com/?page=view-shape&amp;id=17996" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.silhouettedesignstore.com/?page=view-shape&amp;id=17996', 'Silhouette World Map file ']);" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Silhouette World Map File">
          Silhouette World Map file
         </a>
         we used.)
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-4-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="World Map Wall Art DIY - Contact Paper" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8637" height="1020" src="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-4-2.jpg" width="680"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/world-map-wall-art-diy/&amp;media=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-4-2.jpg&amp;description=World Map Wall Art DIY')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         It was time to place the contact paper on each panel! I tried to recruit my husband to help but hes a smart man and opted to save our marriage instead of work on this together. This wasa slow process and required a lot of patience. Go slow and take your time!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-5-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="World Map Wall Art DIY - Spray Paint" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8638" height="1020" src="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-5-2.jpg" width="680"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/world-map-wall-art-diy/&amp;media=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-5-2.jpg&amp;description=World Map Wall Art DIY')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         Yay, time for spray paint! I ended up using a black chalkboard spray paint mainly because I liked how matte it is and thought we could eventually use the map for educational purposes. Love the idea of using chalk to write in all the places we visit!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-6-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="World Map Wall Art DIY - Remove" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8639" height="1020" src="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-6-2.jpg" width="680"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/world-map-wall-art-diy/&amp;media=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-6-2.jpg&amp;description=World Map Wall Art DIY')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         After a couple hours it was time for some funslowly peeling back the contact paper and seeing all land masses intact. I found some post adapters at Home Depot to use as the hangers. Because the panels were so thin, I had to use Gorilla Glue to attach them to the backs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-8-2.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="World Map Wall Art DIY - Brackets" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8640" height="1020" src="http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-8-2.jpg" width="680"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/world-map-wall-art-diy/&amp;media=http://www.triedandtrueblog.com/triedandtrue/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/World-Map-Art-Panels-8-2.jpg&amp;description=World Map Wall Art DIY')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         Im super happy with how my World Map Wall Art DIY turned out! It cost about40 bucks in supplies, so much cheaper than most of the other map options Ive seen. This project is perfect for any large wall section you need to cover. Hope you all enjoyed this tutorial and decide to give it a try!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
