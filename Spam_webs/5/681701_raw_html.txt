     <h2>
      Nutella Milkshakes
     </h2>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6087" height="600" src="http://www.thelittlekitchen.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/nutella-milkshake-125.jpg" title="Nutella Milkshakes from The Little Kitchen" width="401"/>
      <br/>
      Last month I was in Boston for my sister Connies graduation from Northeastern Law. Im so incredibly proud of my sister. She has worked so hard and has overcome so much and shes such an amazing person, sister and student. Shes the first attorney in the family!! Were so proud of her and I hope she knows it, for real (I do tell her all the time). I know shes going to do great things with her law degree. She cares about others and wants to do a lot of good. It was hard for me not to get emotional during her graduation ceremony, shes my baby sister. Go Connie!
     </p>
     <p>
      Thats me and her pictured below.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: center;">
      <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6095" height="500" src="http://www.thelittlekitchen.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/julie-and-connie.jpg" title="Me and my sister, Connie" width="500"/>
      <em>
       Dont ask me why Im holding her stomach.
      </em>
     </p>
     <p>
      <span id="more-6081">
      </span>
      I always love
      <a href="http://www.thelittlekitchen.net/2011/08/09/boston-americas-test-kitchen-the-food-my-sister-techmunch/" title="Boston: Americas Test Kitchen, the food, my sister &amp; #TECHmunch">
       visiting Boston
      </a>
      , its always a fun time with my sister and we always have good food. One of the places we stopped at was Boloco and I ordered a nutella milkshake. It was so good and made with skim milk and low fat frozen yogurt, I knew I wanted to come up with a recipe for a nutella milkshake. And I felt a little less guilty about umm drinking both milkshakes.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6090" height="600" src="http://www.thelittlekitchen.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/nutella-milkshake-141.jpg" title="Nutella Milkshakes from The Little Kitchen" width="401"/>
      <br/>
      Its so easy, I dont know why I dont make milkshakes more often.
     </p>

       <span class="item">
        <h2 class="fn">
         Nutella Milkshakes
        </h2>
       </span>
       <div class="time">
        <p>
         <strong>
          Yield:
         </strong>
         <span class="yield">
          2 shakes
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Prep Time:
         </strong>
         <span class="preptime">
          <span class="value-title" title="PT5M">
          </span>
          5 min
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Total Time:
         </strong>
         <span class="duration">
          <span class="value-title" title="PT5M">
          </span>
          5 min
         </span>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="ingredients">
        <h3 style="clear:left">
         Ingredients:
        </h3>
        <p>
         <span class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           1/3 cup
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           nutella
          </span>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           1 cup
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           skim milk
          </span>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span class="ingredient">
          <span class="amount">
           3 big scoops of
          </span>
          <span class="name">
           low fat vanilla frozen yogurt
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="directions">
        <h3>
         Directions:
        </h3>
        <div class="instructions">
         <p>
          Add all ingredients into a blender and blend.
         </p>
         <p>
          Serve immediately. Makes 2 10 ounce milkshakes.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
