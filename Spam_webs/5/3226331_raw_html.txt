          <span class="post-featured-img">
           <img alt="responsive web design marketing" class="attachment-full wp-post-image" height="350" src="http://3s8xvf1ayx7h3se9z31q4jsr.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/responsive-web-design-marketing.png" title="" width="800"/>

          <div class="wps-seo-booster-headline" itemprop="headline">
           10 Game Changing Responsive Website Designs
          </div>
          <div itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            When it comes to
            <a href="http://socialgarden.com.au/website-design-melbourne/" target="_blank">
             designing the perfect website for your company
            </a>
            , you want the very best design possible, something that will easily translate across multiple platforms of technology.
           </p>
           <p>
            You might have heard about responsive website design, but not have known much about it or why its so popular. Responsive websites use the latest in HTML and CSS coding to create smooth, user-friendly web design. Your designer will then be able to create apps that can be used on any mobile device.
           </p>
           <p>
            Why is this a big deal? Because Google prefers it. If you build a
            <a href="http://socialgarden.com.au/website-design/why-responsive-web-design-is-the-cornerstone-to-any-mobile-strategy/" target="_blank">
             responsive website that is the same on all devices
            </a>
            , Google will almost always push it higher up in its search results over other sites that use two sites, one that works on computers and one on tablets and phones, for the same company site. Thats a huge automatic benefit for you over your competitors.
           </p>
           <p>
            Thinking about your design? Here are 10 great responsive website designs from real companies that are leading the way in terms of design:
           </p>
           <h2 itemprop="alternativeHeadline">
            1. Design a Watch
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://designawatch.mijlo.com/">
             http://designawatch.mijlo.com/
            </a>
            <br/>
            Timekeeping need not be a hassle any more. Design a Watch lets you customise your very own watch any way you like. Its playful design walks you through each and every step of the designing process.
            <br/>
            <img alt="design-a-watch" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3357" height="338" src="http://3s8xvf1ayx7h3se9z31q4jsr.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/design-a-watch1.jpg" width="800"/>
           </p>
           <h2>
            2. AC Laschet
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.aclaschet.be/">
             http://www.aclaschet.be/
            </a>
            <br/>
            This portfolio, created by the designers at
            <a href="http://sodelicious.be/en">
             Delicious
            </a>
            , likens to the architectural designs of the architects it represents. Potential clients can easily scroll through and see what AC Laschet has to offer.
            <br/>
            <img alt="ac-laschet" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3340" height="338" src="http://3s8xvf1ayx7h3se9z31q4jsr.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/ac-laschet.jpg" width="800"/>
           </p>
           <h2>
            3. Adopt Wales
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.adopt-wales.co.uk/">
             http://www.adopt-wales.co.uk/
            </a>
            <br/>
            This agency is dedicated to helping families in the adoption process. It is beautifully designed, complete with stunning visuals and videos. Nearly all of this design is also available on their mobile site.
            <br/>
            <img alt="adopt-wales" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3341" height="338" src="http://3s8xvf1ayx7h3se9z31q4jsr.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/adopt-wales.jpg" width="800"/>
           </p>
           <h2>
            4. VeloSport Online
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://velosportonline.com/">
             http://velosportonline.com/
            </a>
            <br/>
            This online store hired the team at
            <a href="http://hoohaadesign.co.uk/">
             HoohaaDesign
            </a>
            to take care of its brand. The store expertly showcases the product, and nothing gets lost between the desktop and the smartphone.
            <br/>
            <img alt="velosport" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3349" height="338" src="http://3s8xvf1ayx7h3se9z31q4jsr.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/velosport.jpg" width="800"/>
           </p>
           <h2>
            5. Manual
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.manual.is/">
             http://www.manual.is/
            </a>
            <br/>
            Manual showcases a wide variety of sleek kitchen utensils that make cooking look so effortless and elegant. This simple website lets buyers easily browse and purchase exactly what they need.
            <br/>
            <img alt="manual" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3346" height="338" src="http://3s8xvf1ayx7h3se9z31q4jsr.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/manual.jpg" width="800"/>
           </p>
           <h2>
            6. Oscar Health Insurance
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="https://www.hioscar.com/">
             https://www.hioscar.com/
            </a>
            <br/>
            If theres one thing you want access to no matter where you go, its your health insurance. Oscar Healths site easily translates over to a mobile device and makes it exceedingly simple for users to access their information quickly.
            <br/>
            <img alt="hi-oscar" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3345" height="338" src="http://3s8xvf1ayx7h3se9z31q4jsr.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/hi-oscar.jpg" width="800"/>
           </p>
           <h2>
            7. Pepper.ph
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.pepper.ph/">
             http://www.pepper.ph/
            </a>
            <br/>
            This unique food blog makes sure that mobile users with their tiny screens come first. Visually stimulating and perfect for any foodie, this site boasts recipes, reviews and fun trivia games for any user anywhere.
            <br/>
            <img alt="pepper-ph" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3351" height="338" src="http://3s8xvf1ayx7h3se9z31q4jsr.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/pepper-ph1.jpg" width="800"/>
           </p>
           <h2>
            8. Ben the Bodyguard
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://benthebodyguard.com/index.php">
             http://benthebodyguard.com/
            </a>
            <br/>
            This awesome site protects your contacts, photos, notes and other important information stored in your phone. This site itself asks you to scroll down a busy street as you walk with Ben, who explains the need for his services. Though the app is no longer available, Ben the Bodyguard remains a good example of responsive website design
            <br/>
            <img alt="ben-the-bodyguard" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3350" height="338" src="http://3s8xvf1ayx7h3se9z31q4jsr.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/ben-the-bodyguard1.jpg" width="800"/>
           </p>
           <h2>
            9. New Zealand
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://story.newzealand.com/story">
             http://story.newzealand.com/story
            </a>
            <br/>
            New Zealands story is told in a similar fashion as Ben the Bodyguards. Users can scroll down to learn more about the people and culture of New Zealand with the sites interactive features. This cool experience transfers to mobile devices so you can explore all the cool pages of the site wherever you go.
            <br/>
            <img alt="newzealand-story" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3347" height="338" src="http://3s8xvf1ayx7h3se9z31q4jsr.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/newzealand-story.jpg" width="800"/>
           </p>
           <h2>
            10. Creative Knight
           </h2>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.creative-knight.com/">
             http://www.creative-knight.com/
            </a>
            <br/>
            Creative Knight turns the home page into an interactive menu, which is a great feature for anyone using a mobile device. Readers can easily click through all the different aspects of the site and learn about the work the designer has done.
            <br/>
            <img alt="creative-knight" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3343" height="338" src="http://3s8xvf1ayx7h3se9z31q4jsr.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/creative-knight.jpg" width="800"/>
           </p>
          </div>
