         <div id="description-text">
          They're back!! I've reworked them a bit from last time to make them look more like stones and used a smaller loop to attach the chain.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          If you play World of Warcraft, you know what this is. Now you can have a RL version in your pocket. These stones are kiln-fired ceramic with a center of Glow-In-The-Dark polymer clay! They are very durable and include a ball-chain style keychain. The perfect gift for the gamer\/geek who has everything. Each is handmade so no two are exactly alike.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Note: the price listed is for one hearthstone.
