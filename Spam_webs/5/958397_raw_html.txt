              <p>
               Grants Pass is a small Southern Oregon town that sits along the RougeRiver. The downtown area is quaint and historic and home to a
               <em>
                real
               </em>
               soda fountain. The concept was foreign to my kids so they opted for ice cream. The cultural part of the trip didn't end there, my kids were also exposed to their first AK47 and allowed to steer the Mule (a little golf cart thing one may see on a ranch but not in the city). It was also noted that the mailman drives a Jeep Cherokee.
