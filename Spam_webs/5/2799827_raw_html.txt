        <div id="nrelate_flyout_placeholder">
         <a href="http://artboom.info/sculpting/sayaka-ganz.html/attachment/sayaka-ganz-convcomp_s1s" rel="attachment wp-att-1328">
          <img alt="Sayaka Ganz ConvComp S1s 498x664 Sayaka Ganz" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1328" height="664" src="http://cdn3.artboom.info/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/Sayaka-Ganz-ConvComp_S1s-498x664.jpg" title="Sayaka Ganz - ConvComp_S1s" width="498"/>
         </a>
         <span class="post-first-letter">
         </span>
         <p>
          My working process is reminiscent of my experiences growing  up in several different countries, of being disconnected from the place  I was born. Then, I began searching for a new community where I truly  belong. I find discarded objects from peoples houses and give them a  second life, a new home.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://artboom.info/sculpting/sayaka-ganz.html/attachment/sayaka-ganz-dsc05151s" rel="attachment wp-att-1329">
           <img alt="Sayaka Ganz DSC05151s 498x417 Sayaka Ganz" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1329" height="417" src="http://cdn.artboom.info/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/Sayaka-Ganz-DSC05151s-498x417.jpg" title="Sayaka Ganz - DSC05151s" width="498"/>
          </a>
          <span id="more-1324">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          For my sculptures I use plastic utensils, toys  and metal pieces among other things. I only select objects that have  been used and discarded. The human history behind these objects gives  them life in my eyes. My goal is for each object to transcend its  origins by being integrated into an animal form that seems alive. This  process of reclamation and regeneration is liberating to me as an  artist.
         </p>
         <a href="http://artboom.info/sculpting/sayaka-ganz.html/attachment/sayaka-ganz-emergenceconv1s" rel="attachment wp-att-1331">
          <img alt="Sayaka Ganz EmergenceConv1s 498x404 Sayaka Ganz" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1331" height="404" src="http://cdn3.artboom.info/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/Sayaka-Ganz-EmergenceConv1s-498x404.jpg" title="Sayaka Ganz - EmergenceConv1s" width="498"/>
         </a>
         <p>
          By building these sculptures I try  to understand the human relationships that surround me. It is a way for  me to contemplate and remind myself that even if there is conflict right  now, there is a way for all the pieces to fit together. That even if  some people dont feel at home here and now, there is a place where they  belong and that they will eventually find it.
         </p>
         <a href="http://artboom.info/sculpting/sayaka-ganz.html/attachment/sayaka-ganz-flare04s" rel="attachment wp-att-1335">
          <img alt="Sayaka Ganz Flare04s 498x363 Sayaka Ganz" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1335" height="363" src="http://cdn.artboom.info/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/Sayaka-Ganz-Flare04s-498x363.jpg" title="Sayaka Ganz - Flare04s" width="498"/>
