         <div class="inline-content inline-video">
          <div class="posttv-video-embed" data-category-id="wirereuters" data-duration="65680" data-headline="After 53-year run, Univision to end 'Sbado Gigante'" data-is-truth-teller="0" data-max-height="-1" data-max-width="-1" data-object-id="55319bf7e4b0626caaa83bf8" data-show-endscreen="1" data-show-promo="https://img.washingtonpost.com/rf/image_1024w/2010-2019/WashingtonPost/2015/04/17/Production/WashingtonPost/Images/Sabado_Gigante_Closing-0f751-3579.jpg" data-uuid="964e3a72-e557-11e4-ae0f-f8c46aa8c3a4" data-youtube-id="">

          <div class="inline-video-caption">
           <span class="pb-caption">
            The longest running TV variety show in the world, according to Guinness World Records, is coming to an end after 53 years on the air. Spanish language network Univision has announced the end of its top-rated show "Sbado Gigante." (Reuters)
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         Its the end of another TV era: Univision announced on Friday morning that Sbado Gigante, the hugely successful show that was a mainstay on Spanish TV for 53 years, will end after this current season.
        </p>
        <p>
         Saturday, Sept. 19 is the date set for the final show, which holds the record for the longest-running variety show in television history.
        </p>
        <p>
         Sbado Gigante, which had everything from singing competitions to skits to games to interviews with Latin music stars and first lady Michelle Obama and other high-profile guests, launched in 1962. Mario Kreutzberger, the host better known as Don Francisco, also created the program that started on Chiles Channel 13. The show started airing in the United States in 1986.
        </p>
        <p>
         Marios uncanny ability to connect with audiences has transformed how we look at variety shows and allowed Sbado Gigante to endure the test of time, Univision programming president Alberto Ciurana said in a statement. For so many in the Spanish-speaking community, Don Franciscos weekly three-hour show defines Saturday evening entertainment, and I want to thank him and the incredible team for their outstanding work.
        </p>
        <p>
         The network said that Don Francisco will continue to be a regular presence and host various entertainment specials; he will also helm TeletnUSA, a campaign where the proceeds benefit disabled children. In addition, the network noted, hell help as Univision looks to develop new on-air talent. This season, Sbado Gigante, was averaging about 2 million total viewers each week, the same as the previous year. The show averages about 828,000 viewers in the coveted 18-to-49-year-old demographic, which is up 8 percent from the previous season.
        </p>
        <p>
         When we began in the United States in 1986, we told them that we were separated by distance and united by the same language. Today I can say with great pride and satisfaction that that distance turned into closeness and affection, Kreutzberger said in a statement. To all those who joined us by tuning in to Sbado Gigante with their constructive criticism, work and loyalty, I would like to say from the bottom of my heart and in capital letters, THANK YOU VERY MUCH.
        </p>
