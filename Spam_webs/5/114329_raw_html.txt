       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Low Carb Cocoa Coconut Shake. Fuel up with Good Fats!
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-04-01T05:00:06+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          April 1, 2015
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.cottercrunch.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Cotter Crunch
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-categories">
          Filed Under:
          <a href="http://www.cottercrunch.com/category/gut-health-and-sports-nutrition-recipes/" rel="category tag">
           Gut Health and Sports Nutrition Recipes
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.cottercrunch.com/category/smoothies/" rel="category tag">
           smoothies
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.cottercrunch.com/low-carb-cocoa-coconut-shake-fuel-up-with-good-fats/#comments">
           58 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="6587649" data-link="http://www.cottercrunch.com/low-carb-cocoa-coconut-shake-fuel-up-with-good-fats/" data-summary="" data-title="Low Carb Cocoa Coconut Shake. Fuel up with Good Fats!">
        </div>
        <a id="dd_start">
        </a>
        <p>
         <em>
          Fuel up with this Low Carb Cocoa Coconut Shake. Its a great way to start the day! The health benefits of full fat coconut cream plus cocoa keep you energized, fueled, and nourished. Paleo and Vegan friendly.
         </em>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <a href="http://www.cottercrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Cocoa-Coconut-shake-low-carb-copy.jpg">
          <img alt="Fuel up with this Low Carb Cocoa Coconut Shake. It's a great way to start the day! The health benefits of full fat coconut cream plus cocoa keep you energized, fueled, and nourished. Paleo and Vegan friendly. " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17496 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://www.cottercrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Cocoa-Coconut-shake-low-carb-copy.jpg" data-pin-url="http://www.cottercrunch.com/low-carb-cocoa-coconut-shake-fuel-up-with-good-fats/" height="1656" src="http://www.cottercrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/Cocoa-Coconut-shake-low-carb-copy.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </h3>
        <p>
         I spent a good 20 minutes trying figure out whether to call this recipe a shake, drink, or smoothie. I gave up, went with shake, because weneed to
         <em>
          <strong>
           SHAKE
          </strong>
         </em>
         things up. Then there was the the dilemma of calling it a fat shake. Glad I didnt go with that title. Note to self, dont ever google images of fat shake.. just sayin.
        </p>
        <p>
         But I digress. Why I really wanted to shake things up a bit was becausethats what we are doing around here. Shaking up our training routine, our
         <a href="http://www.cottercrunch.com/2015/03/apple-leek-and-cabbage-salad-with-beef-tips/" title="Power Food Pairings  Apple, Leek, and Cabbage Salad with Beef Tips">
          eats
         </a>
         , our
         <a href="http://www.cottercrunch.com/2015/03/what-to-do-when-youve-given-it-all-you-got-but-havent-reached-your-goals/" title="What I learned After Realizing I Wasnt Going to Meet My Goals">
          goals
         </a>
         , our focus.
        </p>
        <p>
         Lets start with breakfast. Morningsare still early, but we are not necessarily up to workout or train. More to work and/or coach, basically straight through until lunch. That being said, fueling with
         <strong>
          HEALTHY
         </strong>
         fat is a good way to keep energy up.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Fuel up with this Low Carb Cocoa Coconut Shake. It's a great way to start the day! The health benefits of full fat coconut cream plus cocoa keep you energized, fueled, and nourished. Paleo and Vegan friendly. " class="aligncenter wp-image-17471 size-full pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.cottercrunch.com/low-carb-cocoa-coconut-shake-fuel-up-with-good-fats/" height="886" src="http://www.cottercrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/choc-fat-shake21.jpg" width="735"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Let me explain.
        </p>
        <p>
         Remember our love for
         <a href="http://www.cottercrunch.com/2014/11/3-ways-eat-drink-mct-oil/" title="3 Ways to Eat (or Drink) MCT Oil">
          MCT oil?
         </a>
         And how its great for quick energy? Lets recap.
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          Medium chain triglycerides (MCTs). Medium chain triglycerides are a special kind of lipid (fat). These triglycerides are quite unique in their chemical formal (structure) in that they bypass the regular digestion:intestines, to liver to, blood.
         </p>
         <p>
          Instead, these fats are digested fastandeasily. MCTs are absorbed right awayand taken to the liver, where they are used directly for glucose. Similar to the processof simple sugar/carbohydrates absorption. Its fast and can be converted to ENERGY STAT! This is why its great for endurance athletes. Instead of a spoonful of sugar, trya spoonful of MCT oil for quick energy! This is also why some
          <strong>
           endurance
          </strong>
          athletes carry MCT oil on their long rides, runs, etc.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         Yes, this fat is ourfriend and cankeep you feeling full for longer and provideenough fuel to keep your energy up for a longer period of time, whether you are training or not. One of the riches forms of MCT fat is found in coconut oil/butter, which is why we love coconut even more. More facts on MCT oil
         <a href="http://paleoleap.com/mct-oil-need-know/">
          here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.cottercrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/cocoa-coconut-fat-shake.jpg">
          <img alt="cocoa coconut fat shake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-17523 pib-hover-img" data-pin-media="http://www.cottercrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/cocoa-coconut-fat-shake.jpg" data-pin-url="http://www.cottercrunch.com/low-carb-cocoa-coconut-shake-fuel-up-with-good-fats/" height="1003" src="http://www.cottercrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/cocoa-coconut-fat-shake.jpg" width="735"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         And ifyou combine this beloved fatwithcocoa powder, youll get one creamy chocolate taste plus a boostin antioxidants. WINNER!
        </p>
        <p>
         There are several ways to make this so called shake. Warm or cold, but this is our favorite combo. I usually add in an extra tbsp ofcream or oil for the Kiwi. You definitely want to adjust the fat content with your bodys needs/activity and sensitivity to lipids.
        </p>
        <p>
         Make sense?
        </p>
        <p>
         Now, about that FAT. Let me get to the recipe.
         <br/>
        </p>
