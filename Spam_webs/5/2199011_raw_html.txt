      <h2 class="post-title">
       <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/" rel="bookmark" title="DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard">
        DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard
       </a>
      </h2>
      <div class="post-category">
       categories:
       <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/category/before-and-after/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Before and After">
        Before and After
       </a>

       <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/category/diy-2/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in DIY">

     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_63721.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
         <img alt="IMG_6372" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1742" height="453" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_63721.jpg" width="680"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_63721.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_63721.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Yay! Im excited to be typing out this post today. I have been thinking about and dreaming about making this headboard for months, but it kept getting put on the backburner until finally we made it happen last week!
     </p>
     <p>
      Check out the before
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6287.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <img alt="IMG_6287" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1686" height="453" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6287.jpg" width="680"/>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6287.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      And after!
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6368.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
         <img alt="IMG_6368" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1734" height="453" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6368.jpg" width="680"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6368.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6368.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      The fresh gray stripes make the bed look more sophisticated and elegant but still fun (which is very important). I had to take off the floral print pillow shams though, as they were competing a little too much visually with the stripes. It was just a little too much to take in! So Im planning to switch out the bedding for neutral colored bedding, but maybe with a little more texture to contrast with the stripes.
     </p>
     <p>
      And the headboard was actually pretty easy to do. I love that this really trendy look is so easy to DIY, making it approachable for most of us.
     </p>
     <p>
      First you cut a sheet of plywood the size of your headboard. We wanted a thicker, more substantial headboard, so we framed the back of the plywood out with 23s.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6334.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <img alt="IMG_6334" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1736" height="453" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6334.jpg" width="680"/>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6334.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Then we cut out our foam to fit (not pictured) and secured it with hot glue. We actually cannibalized an old foam mattress for our foam. However, because the foam was thicker (about 1.75), it was quite heavy and didnt stick to the plywood that well. We didnt want to use spray adhesive because it is quite high in VOCs, and we didnt want to be breathing it in. We later solved the problem of the slightly loose foam by doing some simple button tufting.
     </p>
     <p>
      After sticking the foam on, we wrapped the whole thing with polyester batting and then the fabric.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6344.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <img alt="IMG_6344" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1737" height="426" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6344.jpg" width="640"/>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6344.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      I used 3 yards of Premier Prints Canopy Stripe
      <a href="https://www.fabric.com/buy/uo-841/premier-prints-canopy-stripe-twill-storm" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.fabric.com']);">
       Twill Storm
      </a>
      . I wanted a vertical stripe though, instead of horizontal, so I cut the fabric in half and sewed it together right in the middle with a seam along one of the stripes. You cant even tell its there =).
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6345.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <img alt="IMG_6345" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1738" height="426" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6345.jpg" width="640"/>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6345.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      We used a staple gun (and when our staple gun gave out, a hammer stapler), and tapped in any loose staples with a hammer.
     </p>
     <p>
      This is how I folded the corners:
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6349.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <img alt="IMG_6349" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1739" height="426" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6349.jpg" width="640"/>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6349.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      I wrapped the extra folded fabric on the inside first and stapled that down
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6352.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <img alt="IMG_6352" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1740" height="426" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6352.jpg" width="640"/>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6352.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Then I folded the fabric right along the corner for a nice square corner.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6355.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <img alt="IMG_6355" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-1741" height="426" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6355.jpg" width="640"/>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6355.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      At this point we added some simple button tufting using a button kit from Joanns, an upholstery needle, and waxed button thread (or upholstery thread). I didnt take pictures of this step but heres a
      <a href="http://littlegreennotebook.blogspot.com/2012/02/diy-tufted-headboard.html" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://littlegreennotebook.blogspot.com']);">
       great tutorial
      </a>
      . We put in just 10 buttons, and they worked great for keeping the foam tight and secure. The extra tightness also helped smooth out some of the wrinkles that I couldnt iron out.
     </p>
     <p>
      Im really happy with the finished product! It makes me feel like Im waking up in a hotel every day.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6366.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <img alt="IMG_6366" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1733" height="453" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6366.jpg" width="680"/>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6366.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      I also feel like it is true to my personal style, which also makes me happy. Its fresh, contemporary, and unfussy, but still elegant Or at least I like to think so, ha!
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6368.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
         <img alt="IMG_6368" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1734" height="453" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6368.jpg" width="680"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6368.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_6368.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
        </span>
       </span>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      As for how its attached, it is hung on the wall with brackets but is also sitting on the bed frame behind the mattress.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_63721.jpg">
       <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
         <img alt="IMG_6372" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1742" height="453" src="http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_63721.jpg" width="680"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://somethingisdone.com/diy-striped-upholstered-headboard/&amp;media=http://somethingisdone.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/IMG_63721.jpg&amp;description=DIY Striped Upholstered Headboard')">
         </span>
        </span>
