         <div class="text_article_extra">
          The Japanese supercar has made its way to Europe at long last.
         </div>
         <div class="article_body">
          <div class="article_body_text ">
           The
           <a class="link_article_model" href="http://www.carbuzz.com/Lexus/2012_Lexus_LFA/">
            Lexus LFA
           </a>
           <a class="link_article_model" href="http://www.carbuzz.com/tag/Nurburgring/">
            Nurburgring
           </a>
           Edition has indeed been delivered to a very lucky customer in the UK. The Japanese supercar is already in the hands of a select few in the U.S. and this particular orange model is the second we have seen in this color of the special editions. A black variant and white variant have also been spotted previously. A limited run of 50 Nurburgring Edition units will be built (side note: only 500 standard LFAs will be produced), each packing the stock's 4.8-liter V10 tuned for an extra 11hp.
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="article_body">
          <div class="lazyimage share-action">
           <span class="fb-btn" data-link="http://www.carbuzz.com/news/2012/4/16/Lexus-LFA-Nurburgring-Edition-Makes-Premier-Delivery-in-Europe-7708355/">
            Share
           </span>
           <img alt="Lexus LFA Nurburgring Edition Makes Premier Delivery in Europe" src="http://db.carbuzz.com/images2/170000/2000/600/172627.jpg"/>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="article_body">
          <div class="article_body_text2">
           <div align="right" class="text_article_also">
            <!--b&gt;more at CarBuzz:&lt;/b&gt;&lt;br-->
            <a href="http://www.carbuzz.com/news/2015/7/14/A-1988-E28-BMW-M5-For-Less-Than-20k-We-Found-One-But-There-s-A-Catch-7728047/">
             A 1988 E28 BMW M5 For Less Than $20k? We F...
            </a>
            <a href="http://www.carbuzz.com/news/2015/7/14/Have-You-Seen-An-Uglier-Car-Than-The-2016-Toyota-Prius-7728026/">
             Have You Seen An Uglier Car Than The 2016 ...
            </a>
            <a href="http://www.carbuzz.com/news/2015/7/14/Could-This-SUV-Be-Alfa-s-Follow-Up-To-The-Ravishing-Giulia-7727966/">
             Could This SUV Be Alfa's Follow-Up To The ...
            </a>
           </div>
           That brings total output to 562hp. The extra power comes in addition to aerodynamic styling changes, new tires and a modified tranny and suspension set-up for maximum track performance. Performance numbers are quite impressive, with 0-62mph timed at a rapid 3.7 seconds and top speed quoted as 202mph. The changes have allowed the LFA Nurburgring to lap the famed German track in an impressive time of only 7:14.64. That is good for a fifth all-time on the Nordschleife, beating such impressive models as the
           <a class="link_article_model" href="http://www.carbuzz.com/Porsche/">
            Porsche
           </a>
           911 GT2 RS and the Corvette C6 ZR1, amongst others.
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="article_body">
          <div class="article_body_text ">
           <a class="link_article_model" href="http://www.carbuzz.com/Lexus/">
            Lexus
           </a>
           is throwing in a year-long subscription to the Nurburgring race track in Germany along with a free driving course to those who purchase the LFA Nurburgring Edition, not too shabby for those with a cool $445,000 lying around.
          </div>
