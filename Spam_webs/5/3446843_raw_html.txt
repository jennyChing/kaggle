         <div id="single-title">
          <h1 class="title">
           Technical Yacht Management Services  Bond TM
          </h1>
         </div>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.luxisto.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/boats-187141_1280.jpg">
           <img alt="boats-187141_1280" class="aligncenter wp-image-122 size-large" height="450" src="http://www.luxisto.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/boats-187141_1280-1024x768.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Founded in 2007, Bond TM has now established itself as the leading
          </span>
          <a href="http://www.bondtm.com/" target="_blank">
           technical yacht management
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           firm, integratingthe finest on-board audio visual, IT, communications, security and navigation systems with some of the worlds most advanced new build and refit superyachts.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Bond TM originally began as a consulting firm. Company director
          </span>
          <a href="http://www.bondtm.com/offices-and-team/monaco" target="_blank">
           Will Faimatea
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           (pictured below centre) worked on board yachts as an ETO, and it was during this time that he decided there was a need for a client focused yacht technology management service in the market.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <img alt="cyprus1" class="aligncenter wp-image-1477" height="536" src="http://www.luxisto.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/cyprus1.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Will drew inspiration from his 14 years of experienceworking on yachts and as an owners representative, andcollaborated with several skilledengineers to found Bond TM. The company has gone on to revolutionise the yacht technology consulting industry. Bond TM have commissioned the worlds largest vessels and have beeninvolved in increasingly significant projects.Bond TM is also the first in the superyacht industry to offer fixed fee support services and is distinguishedfrom other yacht technology management firms byits unique relationships with integrators which allow Bond TM torecommend and work with the best solution providers in the yacht industry.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Bond TM provide a wide range of yacht technology management services from
          </span>
          <a href="http://www.bondtm.com/our-services/technology-support-at-sea" target="_blank">
           yacht vsats
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           to graphic user interface design systems. Watch the following official Bond TM video for more information on all of the companys operations.
          </span>
