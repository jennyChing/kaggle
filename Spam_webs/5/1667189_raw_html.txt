            <h2 class="blog-title">
             <a class="blog-title-link blog-link" href="http://www.solutionsforagents.com/blog/social-media-best-practices-for-real-estate-professionals">
              social media best practices for real estate professionals
             </a>
            </h2>
            <p class="blog-date">
             <span class="date-text">
              6/27/2014
             </span>
            </p>
            <p class="blog-comments">

            <div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;">
             <span style="line-height: 0; display: none;">
              
             </span>
             <font size="1">
              <font color="#515151">
               LocationLocationLocation, you hear it all the time from your buyers.
              </font>
             </font>
             <span style="line-height: 0; display: none;">
              
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <font color="#3f3f3f">
              LocationLocationLocation, you hear it all the time from Real Estate Agents
             </font>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <font size="3">
              <font color="#2a2a2a">
               LocationLocationLocation, how YOU sold your listing faster
              </font>
             </font>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <font color="#2a2a2a">
              A couple best practices for Real Estate Professional on how to locate,
              <em>
               and
              </em>
              relocate social media content and advertising
             </font>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <em>
              <u>
               <font color="#8d2424">
                Post
               </font>
              </u>
             </em>
             <font color="#2a2a2a">
               You cannot sell what they cannot see. If you are listing a property using social media, it should be posted where people are located online. Currently, this is Facebook. A secondary method would include listing across multiple platforms to reach the greatest possible number of people. (Provided that you have an existing usable audience / example - Twitter, G+, Pinterest).
             </font>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <em>
              <u>
               <font color="#8d2424">
                Like
               </font>
              </u>
             </em>
             <font color="#2a2a2a">
               It is the simplest way to show that a post has interest. This activity usually activates triggers in algorithms that are utilized by social media sites. In turn, the content that has been liked multiple times is more likely to rank higher in searches and/or feeds.
             </font>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <em>
              <u>
               <font color="#8d2424">
                Comment
               </font>
              </u>
             </em>
             <font color="#2a2a2a">
               Is the most comprehensive, and helpful best practice when using social media. Monitoring comments made by others, and commenting yourself can help clarify content, answer questions, and encourage discussion(s) about your listing.
             </font>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <br/>
             <span>
             </span>
             <em>
              <u>
               <font color="#8d2424">
                Share
               </font>
              </u>
             </em>
             <font color="#2a2a2a">
               Utilizes the ability to exponentially expand the reach of your listing organically on social media. You have friends, your friends have different friends, and so on Encourage your friends (and their friends) to share the post/tweet/video/pin about a listing on their social media platforms. This will naturally advertise the listing to a like-minded group within a general area.
              <br/>
              <span>
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span>
              </span>
              Implementing these social media techniques into the marketing of your listing will contribute to your success as a real estate agent.
              <br/>
             </font>
