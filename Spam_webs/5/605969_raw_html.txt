         Chocolate Cake with Peanut Butter Cheesecake Filling!
        </h2>
        <p>
         I have not baked anything in a while. Mostly because it has been soooo darn hot lately! If I could not throw it on the grill, I refused to make it this week. But, sometimes you just need chocolate, and I had a crazy craving for chocolate cake and peanut butter cheesecake. Mt sweet tooth was going through major withdrawal, so I knew it was time to get something in the oven. I could not make up my mind on what to make so I decided to combine my cravings into aChocolate Cake with Peanut Butter Cheesecake Filling!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://someofthisandthat.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_8162.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Chocolate Cake with Peanut Butter Cheesecake Filling!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1194" height="1500" src="http://someofthisandthat.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_8162.jpg" width="1000"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://someofthisandthat.com/chocolate-cake-peanut-butter-cheesecake-filling/&amp;media=http://someofthisandthat.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_8162.jpg&amp;description=Chocolate Cake with Peanut Butter Cheesecake Filling!')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
         The rich chocolate cake and the creamy peanut butter filling was just what I needed. If you are new here, I have a total chocolate and peanut butter addiction, to the extent that I turn a normal breakfast into a
         <a href="http://someofthisandthat.com/triple-chocolate-cinnamon-rolls/" target="_blank">
          chocolate lovers dream
         </a>
         . The best part about this recipe is that the cheesecake filling is no bake, so it may look intimidating, but this recipe is super easy to make!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://someofthisandthat.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_8167.jpg">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Chocolate Cake with Peanut Butter Cheesecake Filling!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1191" height="1500" src="http://someofthisandthat.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_8167.jpg" width="1000"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://someofthisandthat.com/chocolate-cake-peanut-butter-cheesecake-filling/&amp;media=http://someofthisandthat.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/IMG_8167.jpg&amp;description=Chocolate Cake with Peanut Butter Cheesecake Filling!')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Oh and to give it just a little bit more decadence, I frosted it in peanut butter frosting and covered it in a dark chocolate ganache! YUM!
         <br/>
        </p>

         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Chocolate Cake with Peanut Butter Cheesecake Filling!
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSDetails">
          <div class="ERSAuthor">
           Author:
           <span itemprop="author">
            Jamie
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSHead">
           Recipe type:
           <span itemprop="recipeCategory">
            Dessert
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSTimes">
           <div class="ERSHead">
            Prep time:
            <time datetime="PT1H30M" itemprop="prepTime">
             1 hour 30 mins
            </time>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSHead">
            Cook time:
            <time datetime="PT35M" itemprop="cookTime">
             35 mins
            </time>
           </div>
           <div class="ERSHead">
            Total time:
            <time datetime="PT2H5M" itemprop="totalTime">
             2 hours 5 mins
            </time>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
          <div class="ERSHead">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            8 Slices
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Chocolate Cake:
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 cups white sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cups all-purpose flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup unsweetened cocoa powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoons baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoons baking soda
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup vegetable oil
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup boiling water
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Filling:
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cups frozen whipped topping, thawed
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup white sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup creamy peanut butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Frosting:
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 cups powdered sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup peanut butter
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoons vanilla extract
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             to  cup milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Topping:
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            9 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup heavy cream
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cake:
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease two 9' inch cake pans and set aside.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a large bowl combine sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Beat in the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla until well combined. Slowly stir in boiling water.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Transfer batter to baking sheets and bake for 30 to 35 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Allow to cool completely.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            While the cake is cooling time for the filling!
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Filling:
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a large mixing bowl, beat together cream cheese, sugar and peanut butter. Fold in whipped topping.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            To make this process easier, I like to use a 9 inch spring form. Place one cake into the bottom of the spring form pan and pour filling over top smoothing it out as you go. Place the second cake on top and gently press down so everything is nice and tight.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Refrigerate for 1 hour before removing and frosting.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Frosting:
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a small mixing bowl, beat together powdered sugar and peanut butter. Continue beating on low and add in vanilla and milk. Beat until smooth can creamy.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            After cake has chilled, remove from spring form and frost!
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Topping:
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Bring heavy cream to a boil and remove from heat. Pour over chocolate and whisk until smooth.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Allow chocolate to set and cool for 10 minutes before pouring over cake.
           </li>
          </ol>
