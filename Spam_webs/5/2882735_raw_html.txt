       <h4 class="text-center" style="text-align: left;">
        Treated With The Trivedi Effect
        <sup>
         
        </sup>
        Bottle Gourd
       </h4>
       <p style="text-align: justify!important;">
        The scientific name of bottle gourd is
        <em>
         Lageneria siceraria
        </em>
        .
        <strong>
         Bottle Gourd plants are climbers, but, after receiving Mr. Trivedis Treatment, for the first time in observation they germinated and grew like other plants, standing erect for 45 days
        </strong>
        , and then converted to climbers. The stem of the plant was strong so it could hold the plant erect. The shapes and sizes of leaves were completely altered. The leaves were thinner and all of the leaves were pointing towards the sun. The green coloring was bright, uniform and completely different from normal bottle gourd plants. As compared to the control group, there was more hair on the leaves and the rest of the plant, which this is a good defense mechanism against insect attack. The crop of treated plants was absolutely disease free.
       </p>
