             </a>
            </h2>
            <p class="has-media media-470 has-image" data-textannotation-id="862772e9e08b4a2c24936c76dffc4591">
             <span class="lightBoxWrapper">
              <span class="img-border">
               <img alt="Most Popular Repurposing Tricks of 2010" data-asset-url="http://i.kinja-img.com/gawker-media/image/upload/s--FvjDEX3N--/c_fit,fl_progressive,q_80,w_320/1866j8ppfmblopng.png" data-chomp-id="1866j8ppfmblopng" data-format="png" height="311" src="http://i.kinja-img.com/gawker-media/image/upload/s--FvjDEX3N--/c_fit,fl_progressive,q_80,w_320/1866j8ppfmblopng.png" width="320"/>
