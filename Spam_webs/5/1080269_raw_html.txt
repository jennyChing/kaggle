        <p>
         Recently I found myself in a disappointing situation. The ice cream selection in my freezer was boring the heck out of me.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.keepitsweetdesserts.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/ThinMintIceCream10.jpg">
          <img alt="Thin Mint Ice Cream - loaded with thin mints but not TOO minty!" border="0" height="600" src="http://www.keepitsweetdesserts.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/ThinMintIceCream10_thumb.jpg" style="display: inline; border: 0px;" title="Thin Mint Ice Cream - loaded with thin mints but not TOO minty!" width="400"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         To remedy the situation I decided to take matters into my own hands and make some no churn ice cream. You all know
         <a href="http://www.keepitsweetdesserts.com/tag/no-churn/" target="_blank">
          Im obsessed
         </a>
         , its kind of
         <span style="text-decoration: line-through;">
          becoming a problem
         </span>
         the best thing ever. You probably should make it too.
         <a href="http://meyersstyles.com/thin-mint-ice-cream/" target="_blank">
          Head over to Meyers Styles for all the details!
         </a>
