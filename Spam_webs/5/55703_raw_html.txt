       <h2 class="posttitle">
        Choosing Cabinetry For Your Home
       </h2>
       <div class="postmeta">
        <em>
         posted by
        </em>
        Sommer 
        <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/choosing-cabinetry-for-your-home/#comments">
         2 comments 
        </a>
       </div>
      </header>
      <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="17464635" data-link="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/choosing-cabinetry-for-your-home/" data-summary="" data-title="Choosing Cabinetry For Your Home">
      </div>
      <p>
       Were sharing our experience in
       <strong>
        Choosing Cabinetry For Your Home
       </strong>
       today, as it pertains to our new office and photography studio.
       <span id="more-31667">
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2014/10/office-remodel-reveal.html/office-remodel-21" rel="attachment wp-att-31786">
        <img alt="Office Remodel Reveal! #DIY #homeimprovement" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-31786" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/office-remodel-21.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       On Tuesday we shared the
       <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2014/10/office-remodel-reveal.html" target="_blank" title="Office Remodel Reveal!">
        <em>
         big reveal
        </em>
       </a>
       of our latest home project, remodeling our detached garage into the new ASP Headquarters.
      </p>
      <p>
       Prior to the project, we would stand in the empty garageanddream about what it could look like and how it might meet our needsasa new work space.
      </p>
      <p>
       The garage had good bones a vaulted ceiling, skylights, and old-style french doors instead of regular garage doors. But what we really needed was storage, and lots of it.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2014/10/office-remodel-reveal.html/office-remodel-11" rel="attachment wp-att-31771">
        <img alt="Office Remodel Reveal! #DIY #homeimprovement " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-31771" height="875" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/office-remodel-11.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       We wanted the new office to have a clean modern feel, yet not clash with our 80+ year old house.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2014/08/garage-remodel.html" title="Garage Remodel  HELP!">
        With your help
       </a>
       we selected a timeless cabinet style from
       <a href="http://shenandoahcabinetry.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        Shenandoah Cabinetry
       </a>
       called Solana, in the Mocha Maple finish. Then we paired it with clean modern draw pulls.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2014/10/office-remodel-reveal.html/office-remodel-23" rel="attachment wp-att-31773">
        <img alt="Office Remodel Reveal! #DIY #homeimprovement " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-31773" height="400" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/office-remodel-23.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Shenandoah is sold exclusively at Lowes, where the design team does a phenomenal job digitally building the plans for your new office (or kitchen or bathroom). The design process is painless actually, its fun! We talked with a designer about our specific needs and she came up with several options for us to consider.
      </p>
      <p>
       We chose to go with tall cabinetry against the two side walls, and a large double-sided desk down the middle of the room.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2014/10/office-remodel-reveal.html/office-remodel-10" rel="attachment wp-att-31805">
        <img alt="Office Remodel Reveal! #DIY #homeimprovement " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-31805" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/office-remodel-10.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       The cabinets are expansive enough to house all my dishes and props. You can see, the pretty pieces are displayed on open shelves, but I have tons of closed cabinets and drawers for photography equipment, and office supplies.
      </p>
      <p>
       Then we left open space in the front, by the garage doors, for me to roll my photography table around to shoot in the best light.
      </p>
      <p>
       Doesnt it look amazing?
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2014/10/office-remodel-reveal.html/office-remodel-29" rel="attachment wp-att-31789">
        <img alt="Office Remodel Reveal! #DIY #homeimprovement " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-31789" height="400" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/office-remodel-29.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2014/10/office-remodel-reveal.html/office-remodel-6" rel="attachment wp-att-31802">
        <img alt="Office Remodel Reveal! #DIY #homeimprovement" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-31802" height="400" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/office-remodel-6.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Shenandoah Cabinetry is so sturdy, we know it will last for years to come. It may even outlive us!
      </p>
      <p>
       So we want to share a few tips weve learned about Choosing Cabinetry For Your Home. Now that weve remodeled
       <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2013/04/kitchen-makeover-day-2-my-cabinetry.html" title="Kitchen Makeover Day 2: My Cabinetry">
        our kitchen
       </a>
       and office, were practically experts on the matter. *wink*
      </p>
      <h4>
       Choosing Cabinetry For Your Home
      </h4>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Consider the lighting in the room when selecting a cabinetry finish. Dark cabinetry offers richness to a space, but darken it down as well. Light cabinets reflect light and give a space an open airy feel.
       </li>
       <li>
        Dont choose a cabinetry style for your home based on popular opinion. When trends change youll be stuck with last seasons style for years. Select a cabinetry style and finish you
        <em>
         know
        </em>
        you will not get tired of over time. Purchasing new cabinetry is like getting married. You need to takeyour decision seriously.
       </li>
       <li>
        On the flip-side, draw pulls
        <em>
         are
        </em>
        something that can be easily swapped out. Think of it as accessorizing, and pick handles that express your personal style. If you want to change them out a few years down the road, you can give your cabinetry
        <a href="http://shenandoahcabinetry.com/product-browser" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         a whole new look
        </a>
        for minimal investment.
       </li>
       <li>
        Plan with purpose. Before you meet with the designer at Lowes, think about how you would liketo use your space, what you needto store, and how you want to feel in your new room. The designer can implement storage options andcustomized features that will helpyou love your new space for years to come.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       You can see all of Shenandoahs door styles, finishes, and handle options
       <a href="http://shenandoahcabinetry.com/product-browser" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
        on their website
       </a>
       , or at Lowes!
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2014/10/office-remodel-reveal.html/office-remodel-9" rel="attachment wp-att-31809">
        <img alt="Office Remodel Reveal! #DIY #homeimprovement " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-31809" height="900" src="http://xnjm5arbt0-flywheel.netdna-ssl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/office-remodel-9.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="color: #808080;">
        <em>
         Disclosure: Shenandoah Cabinetry partnered with us on our office remodel. All opinions are our own.
        </em>
       </span>
      </p>
