             <p>
              Cancer is referred as an uncontrollable growth of body cells in any part, which harms its surrounding tissues. Several types of cancers are there and
              <strong>
               Skin Cancer
              </strong>
              is one of them. This type of cancer arises from skin due to development of unusual cells and these cells have the ability to spread in other parts of body.
             </p>
             <p>
              According to the Signs of Skin Cancer, it is divided into three types: Basal Cell Cancer (BCC), Squamous Cell Cancer (SCC) and
              <strong>
               Melanoma
              </strong>
              . Basal and Squamous Cell are very common and their treatment is very effective, but the third one; Melanoma could be difficult to treat.
             </p>
             <h3>
              <strong>
               Scrutinize All the Causes of Melanoma Symptoms in Human
              </strong>
             </h3>
             <p>
              Almost greater than 90% of skin cancers are caused due to the UV (Ultra Violet) rays comes from the Sun; this exposure increases the risk of all the three types of skin cancers.

             <h5>
              <strong>
               Melanoma
              </strong>
             </h5>
             <p>
              This type of
              <strong>
               <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skin_cancer" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="skin cancer">
                skin cancer
               </a>
              </strong>
              develops in the pigment cells called as Melanocytes, they could develop anywhere in the body and especially in those body parts who had more exposure to the sun.
             </p>

             <p>
              It can also develop in such areas of your body which had little or no exposure to the sun like spaces between your toes and palms, scalp, under your nail, in your mouth, digestive tract, urinary tract, inside the eye, etc. Such Melanomas are likely to diagnosed by the Doctors only.
             </p>
             <h5>
              <strong>
               Melanoma Symptoms
              </strong>
             </h5>
             <p>
              Melanoma Symptoms are first detected by the patients themselves; if it caught early then it could be cured with minor surgery. The symptoms include:
             </p>
             <p>
              Moles are very common on human skin; almost everyone has at least one mole over their body. Normally it is an evenly black or brown colored spot, which is either flat or raised. But if you recognized any changes in your mole like change in size, shape or color then it could be the indication of Melanoma.
             </p>
             <p>
              If you found any new spot over your skin, which is changing its shape, size or color and affecting its surrounding skin.
             </p>
             <p>
              If touching to the spot gives you itching, tenderness or pain.
             </p>
             <p>
              If the edges or boarders of your mole are different, irregular, blurred or ragged.
             </p>
             <p>
              If you found any of these symptoms then stop just looking them and show it your Doctor without any hesitation for its treatment.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Causes of Skin Cancer
              </strong>
             </p>
             <ul>
              <li>
               Ultra Violet Radiation
              </li>
              <li>
               Smoking Tobacco
              </li>
              <li>
               Some Genetic Syndromes
              </li>
              <li>
               Chronic Non Healing Wounds
              </li>
              <li>
               Certain Medication like, Immunosuppressive
              </li>
             </ul>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Prevention
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              Applying a good sunscreen lotion is doctor recommended prevention of Melanoma and Squamous Cell
              <em>
               cancer
              </em>
              . Also try to avoid sun burning by wearing protective cloths, sunglasses and hat.
             </p>
             <p>
              <strong>
               Treatment
              </strong>
             </p>
             <p>
              The treatment of skin cancer is completely depends on the stage, type, location of the cancer and age of the person. If the cancer is diagnosed in its early stage then its little easy or effective to cure completely with a minor surgery and medications.
             </p>

              I wish you never come across with such type of disease but, if you recognized or suspect about any Signs of
              <strong>
               Skin Cancer

              then without vesting more time take an immediate appointment with your doctor and consult with him. Take the necessary and suggested precautions to recover it as early as possible.
