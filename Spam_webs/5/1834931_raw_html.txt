           <br/>
           When it comes to making the biggest jewelry purchase of your entire life, you will need to do a little work before simply handing over your debit or credit card! You must do a little bit of the legwork to make sure you get a great deal on her diamond engagement ring. This is the ring she will wear forever, so you better have a clear idea of what she would want on that ring finger of hers. Do not worry, though, because even if you have no idea what kind of ring she would like, you can enlist the help of her friends. One of them is bound to remember her saying what style of diamond engagement ring she likes. It could have been a solitaire engagement ring or a vintage one, but there are methods of extracting this information so you can make an informed purchase.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           You will also maybe want to keep an eye on the sales that are going on. Of course, around every major holiday, there will be some sort of sale. This is the time for you to try and do some wheeling and dealing since you know a little about what you want at this point in the process.
