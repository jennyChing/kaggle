       <div class="preparation_method">
        <div class="preparation_list">
         <h2>
          Preparation Method :
         </h2>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Heat the oil in a heavy-based frying pan. Add the curry leaves
to the pan. Fry them gently for a minute.
          </li>
          <li>
           Add the ground paste to the fried curry leaves and cook gently
for about 3-5 minutes.
          </li>
          <li>
           Add the curry powder, garam masala, cumin, ground coriander and
chilli powder to the pan, cook, stirring, for about 2-3 minutes.
Adjust seasoning with salt.
          </li>
          <li>
           Add the chicken with water if needed. Stir to coat the chicken
evenly in the spice mixture, then cover the pan and cook for about
20-25 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
          </li>
          <li>
           Remove the lid, increase the heat and cook the curry for a
further 2-3 minutes or until thick and dry. Serve the curry
immediately.
          </li>
         </ul>
        </div>
        <div class="ingridients">
         <h3>
          INGREDIENTS
         </h3>
         <div>
          <ul>
           <li>
            750 grams boneless chicken breasts, cut into small cubes
           </li>
           <li>
            50 ml sesame oil
           </li>
           <li>
            150 ml water
           </li>
           <li>
            3 Teaspoon ground cumin
           </li>
           <li>
            3 Teaspoonground coriander
           </li>
           <li>
            1 tablespoon curry powder
           </li>
           <li>
            1 teaspoon garam masala
           </li>
           <li>
            Chilli powder and salt, to taste
           </li>
           <li>
            10 dried curry leaves
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Ground to Coarse Paste
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            4-6 garlic cloves, chopped
           </li>
           <li>
            1 large onion, chopped
           </li>
           <li>
            1 inch piece fresh root ginger, chopped
           </li>
          </ul>
