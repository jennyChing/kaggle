       Oh no, is the summer almost over already?! That means it's time to dust off the kids' lunch boxes! While there are certainly ways to
       <a href="http://www.kitchendaily.com/read/new-ways-liven-pbj" target="_blank">
        liven up that oldPB&amp;J sandwich
       </a>
       , if you want to go another route, it's time to get inspired for the best
       <a href="http://www.kitchendaily.com/read/12-recipes-back-school-lunches" target="_blank">
        back to school ideas
       </a>
       and
       <a href="http://www.kitchendaily.com/read/10-healthy-and-delicious-snacks-after-school-sports" target="_blank">
        snacks
       </a>
       . Luckily, our contributors have you covered. They have some super easy and delicious ideas that will make heading back to school much easier. The kids will never bring home an uneaten brown bag of food when they're filed with
       <a href="http://www.organizeyourselfskinny.com/2011/10/18/homemade-chicken-nuggets" target="_blank">
        homemade chicken nuggets
       </a>
       or
       <a href="http://cookingwithelise.com/fruit-and-nut-breakfast-bars/" target="_blank">
        granola bars
       </a>
       !
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.kitchendaily.com/read/best-back-school-recipes#show-gallery" target="_parent">
        Check out the slideshow above
       </a>
       for the 10 best back to school recipes from our contributors.
