         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           Business Management Daily Compiles List of Its Top 10 Business Etiquette Articles
           <br/>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Falls Church, Va.  For organizations and employees alike, recognizing the critical link between business etiquette and profit is key to success. Its essential for business professionals to learn how to confidently interact with colleagues in ways that make them and their entire organizations shine.
         </p>
         <p>
          The truth is, multimillion-dollar deals and peoples jobs can be lost as a result of poor etiquette, says Business Management Dailys Senior Web Editor Elizabeth Hall. In todays competitive climate, knowing how to act  and how not to  can spell the difference between success and failure.
         </p>
         <p>
          Its important for professionals to learn best practices on everything from meeting and cubicle etiquette to business letter and email protocol. Business Management Daily provides a variety of resources to help individuals improve their business etiquette skills and increase their success in the workplace.
         </p>
         <p>
          To emphasize the importance of good etiquette at work, Business Management Daily has compiled a list of its top 10 articles on business etiquette:
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/14294/etiquette-rules-for-the-modern-age?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          1.
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/14294/etiquette-rules-for-the-modern-age?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
           Etiquette rules for the modern age
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          2.
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/30972/add-etiquette-to-employee-expectations-please?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
           Add etiquette to employee expectations, please
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          3.
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/10373/executive-meeting-etiquette-7-tips-to-help-you-shine?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
           Executive meeting etiquette: 7 tips to help you shine
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          4.
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/20336/email-faux-pas-can-do-serious-damage-to-your-career?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
           Email faux pas can do serious damage to your career
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          5.
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/6522/good-call-getting-phone-etiquette-right?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
           Good call! Getting phone etiquette right
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          6.
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/14299/dos-and-donts-of-business-meals?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
           Dos and donts of business meals
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          7.
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/21060/nosy-rude-co-workers-10-etiquette-moves-to-stay-on-top?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
           Nosy, rude co-workers? 10 etiquette moves to stay on top
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          8.
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/10894/5-ways-to-create-a-grateful-workplace?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
           5 ways to create a grateful workplace
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          9.
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/2239/have-wardrobe-challenged-staff-try-on-image-consultants?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
           Have wardrobe-challenged staff? Try on image consultants
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          10.
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/5857/office-decorations-balance-personal-and-professional?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
           Office decorations: Balance personal and professional
          </a>
         </p>
         <ol>
         </ol>
         <p>
          Its a snowball effect: The more poised and confident you are, the more people will like and admire you, which in turn will boost your confidence even more, and so on. The strategies in these articles will teach business professionals to handle a variety of workplace situations with ease and grace.
         </p>
         <p>
          For more best practices on business etiquette, download Business Management Dailys
          <a href="http://www.businessmanagementdaily.com/glp/41100/Business-Etiquette.html?src=PR-Art-OC-BusinessEtiquette">
           <i>
            14 Tips on Business Etiquette: Setting a professional tone with co-workers, clients and customers
           </i>
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          Your organizations success depends on the skillful use of business etiquette and protocol, Hall says. Not to mention professional interactions are great opportunities to shine while advancing your own career.
