          <p>
           <a href="http://fullfrontal.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Cindy-Crawford-Gallery-8.jpg">
            <img alt="Throwback Thursday Cindy Crawford" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-779" height="425" src="http://fullfrontal.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Cindy-Crawford-Gallery-8.jpg" width="692"/>

          <p>
           Every generation of teenage boys has their ultimate pinup girl well they used to at least not sure if that is still true with smartphones and the like. Regardless, for me growing up, Cindy Crawford was the ultimate pinup girl. In an age in which the use of Photoshop was virtually nonexistent, her birthmark was seen as making her even more attractive.
