     <p>
      <b>
       WWE Studios, Inc.
      </b>
      ; commonly known as
      <b>
       WWE Studios
      </b>
      (formerly known as
      <b>
       WWE Films
      </b>
      ) is a
      <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">
       Los Angeles
      </a>
      -based subsidiary of
      <a href="/wiki/WWE" title="WWE">
       WWE
      </a>
      which was created in 2002 as to develop and produce
      <a href="/wiki/Feature_film" title="Feature film">
       feature film
      </a>
      properties for its wrestlers and personnel. WWE Studios movies frequently mix well-known actors and actresses in lead roles with WWE wrestlers, and teams with existing production and distribution companies to deliver the product at a cheaper cost for both parties.
     </p>

     <p>
      Before the formation of WWE Studios, the WWF had produced the 1989 film
      <i>

        No Holds Barred
       </a>

       Hulk Hogan
      </a>
      under the Shane Distribution Company banner, a precursor to today's company. The film was given a DVD release in 2012.

        </span>
