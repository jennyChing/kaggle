        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Getting healthy- Are you waiting until the New Year?
        </h1>
        <a class="YUMMLY-YUM-BUTTON" href="//yummly.com" rel="nofollow">
         Yum
        </a>
        <script src="https://www.yummly.com/js/widget.js?wordpress">

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="pw-widget pw-counter-vertical pw-horizontal" pw:image="" pw:title="Getting healthy- Are you waiting until the New Year?" pw:url="http://thespringmount6pack.com/2012/12/getting-healthy-new-year/">

        <p>
         So many people are talking about losing weight , after the New Year. Are you one of them? Why are you waiting? I have been working on losing weight for almost a year now, and I have lost 30 lbs. But even when I was losing weight, I was doing a lot to get healthy. I was on Nutrisystem, but I wasnt really dieting the way I should. Or maybe I was dieting, and not changing my life. Like I have said lots and lots of time, NS has taught me a lot. And I would not have lost the weight I have without the program. I am even getting ready to host a giveaway for a week trial, be sure to keep watching.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="lose weight now" class="aligncenter wp-image-11293" height="287" src="http://thespringmount6pack.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/eatSmart-Scale.png" width="314"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         But I didnt change my life in the sense of adding in exercise like I should and not eating junk. If I had done both, on a better basis, I would have lost more. I am turning 36 in less than a month. Every winter my asthma gets out of control with the weather. I realized I really do not want to be the fat girl, the unhealthy girl, the one who can not keep up. I have watched my brother, who is turning 50, change his eating, his activity level, basically his whole life. I love how much energy he has now, and how much he can do. I want that!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="lose weight now" class="aligncenter wp-image-11594" height="353" src="http://thespringmount6pack.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/weights_3.gif" title="lose weight now" width="280"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         So many people are saying that they are going to lose weight in the New Year. They are going to get healthy after New Years. Why not start now. Why not lose weight now? Who wants to join me and hold each other responsible? Just adding in a few exercises, or an extra walk, maybe take the stairs instead of the elevator, race your kids, play keep away and catch me if you can at the park. Just these simple things.
        </p>
        <p>
         What are you goals right now?
         <br/>
         <!--more-->
         <!-- BlogGlue Cache: No -->
