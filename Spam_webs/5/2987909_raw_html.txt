          <h1 class="post-title single">
           <a href="http://gather.com/kim-kardashian-kris-humphries-divorce-leads-to-one-of-2011s-hottest-revenge-romances/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Kim Kardashian, Kris Humphries Divorce Leads to One of 2011s Hottest Revenge Romances">
            Kim Kardashian, Kris Humphries Divorce Leads to One of 2011s Hottest Revenge Romances
           </a>
          </h1>
          <div class="meta">
           <span class="meta-cats">
            Filed in
            <a href="http://gather.com/category/celebs/" rel="category tag">
             Gather Celeb News Channel
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-author">
            by
            <a href="http://gather.com/author/erinanthony/" rel="author" title="Posts by Erin Anthony">
             Erin Anthony
            </a>
           </span>
           <span class="meta-date">
            on January 2, 2012
           </span>
           <span class="meta-comments">
            
            <a href="http://gather.com/kim-kardashian-kris-humphries-divorce-leads-to-one-of-2011s-hottest-revenge-romances/#comments" rel="bookmark" title="Comments for Kim Kardashian, Kris Humphries Divorce Leads to One of 2011s Hottest Revenge Romances">
             0 Comments
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
          <p>
           Kim Kardashian and Kris Humphries divorce along with Jennifer Lopez and Marc Anthonys divorce have lead to some of the hottest revenge romances for 2011. According to
           <a href="http://www.hollywoodlife.com/2012/01/01/2011-hot-revenge-romances-jennifer-lopez-demi-moore-pics/#542903-0-010112_revenge_romances" rel="nofollow">
            HollywoodLife.com
           </a>
           , Lopez really showed Marc shes still got the good to tame a boy toy by dating one of her backup dancers, Casper Smart. Not only is she showing those special moves to Smart but to the world, because J.Lo and Smart were spotted
           <a href="http://celebs.gather.com/viewArticle.action?articleId=281474980974456">
            publicly grinding each other
           </a>
           at a night club. Ouch, that must break Marcs
           <em>
            old-man
           </em>
           heart.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://goo.gl/rtLFa" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            <img alt="Kim Kardashian HD 11x17 Photo Poster Busty Naked #12 HDQ" border="0" height="167" id="prodImage" src="http://ecx.images-amazon.com/images/I/41oqPr1V%2BnL._SL500_AA300_.jpg" style="float: left" width="167"/>
           </a>
           Kardashians
           <a href="http://celebs.gather.com/viewArticle.action?articleId=281474980974500">
            filing for divorce blindsided
           </a>
           Humphries, but he got her back by dating one of her rivals, Dollicia Bryan (but that could just be an unsubstantiated rumor like so many other rumors about the Kardashians). Meanwhile, fellow cougar
           <a href="http://celebs.gather.com/viewArticle.action?articleId=281474980946448">
            Demi Moore
           </a>
           was devastated when her young cub, Ashton Kutcher, decided to prowl for sex kittens on their six-year anniversary. Moores supposed revenge romance was with Scott Vincent-Borba. Next up is another reality TV starlet, Leah Messer, of
           <em>
            Teen Mom 2
           </em>
           who was shocked when her ex, Corey Simms, paraded his new girlfriend, Summer Tavarez, around. And, lastly, when
           <em>
            High School Musical
           </em>
           mega couple Zac Efron and Vanessa Hudgens split, she sought comfort in the arms of another Disney hottie, Austin Butler.
          </p>
          <p>
           Well, there you have it. HollywoodLife.coms compilation of 2011s hottest revenge romances. Frankly, they missed a major one, because Ashton Kutchers PDA with Lea Michele cut like daggers into Demi Moores heart. That was a hot revenge that went the wrong way!
          </p>
          <p>
            Erin Anthony 2011
          </p>
