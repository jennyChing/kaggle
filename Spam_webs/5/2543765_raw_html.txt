Make friends and "maybe more" as you watch inspiring or trashy videos; nobody (including us) will know unless they are watching the same thing.  Dipify is the most natural way to meet somebody like-minded.  No more laboring through online dating profiles.  Meet only the right people at the right time.  Just be yourself.
