         <header class="entry-header">
          <h1 class="entry-title">
           Live an adventure in the Citroen C4 Aircross or Cactus
          </h1>
          <p class="meta-line">
           <a class="meta-label meta-date" href="http://blog.automart.co.za/2015/05/29/" rel="bookmark" title="2:43 pm">
            <i class="icon-left fa fa-calendar-o">
            </i>
            <time class="entry-date" datetime="2015-05-29T14:43:49+00:00" itemprop="startDate">
             May 29, 2015
            </time>
           </a>
           <span class="meta-label comment-label">
            <i class="fa fa-comment">
            </i>
            <a href="http://blog.automart.co.za/2015/05/29/live-an-adventure-in-the-citroen-c4-aircross-or-cactus/#respond">
             Comment
             <i class="">
             </i>
            </a>
           </span>
          </p>
          <span class="smartlib-category-line">
           <a href="http://blog.automart.co.za/category/car-reviews/" rel="category tag">
            Car reviews
           </a>
          </span>
         </header>
         <div class="smartlib-single-image-container">
          <img alt="citroen_c4_aircross" class="attachment-wide-image wp-post-image" height="600" src="http://blog.automart.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/citroen_c4_aircross.png" width="800"/>
         </div>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           With its elegant lines and high quality design, the
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.automart.co.za/cars/citroen/?utm_source=social&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=BlogAutoMartSEO201505">
             Citroen C4
            </a>
           </strong>
           turns heads. Innovative features and cutting-edge technology make the C4 stand out from the crowd. With its stylish exterior and great performance, it is easy to see why finding a
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.automart.co.za/cars/citroen/?utm_source=social&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=BlogAutoMartSEO201505">
             Citroen C4 for sale
            </a>
           </strong>
           is a popular choice. Whether you are looking for a
           <strong>
            Citroen C4 Aircross
           </strong>
           or a
           <strong>
            Citroen C4 Cactus
           </strong>
           , it is worth taking a closer look at both models.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="383" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/rlUYx1ScdEg" width="680">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           Robust and sophisticated, the exterior of the C4 has an aggressive stance on the road. Its strong frame and excellent features offer the driver outstanding stability. The chrome twin chevrons enhance the C4s attractive appearance. Its stretched headlamps are both stylish and functional.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Citroen-C4_Aircross" class="aligncenter wp-image-7736" height="404" src="http://blog.automart.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Citroen-C4_Aircross.png" width="680"/>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           The luxurious design of the interior reflects great attention-to-detail. Top quality materials and a relaxing ambiance are used to create an inviting interior. The interior has a compact design which allows plenty of space for passengers and luggage.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="CitroenC4Cactus" class="aligncenter wp-image-7737" height="453" src="http://blog.automart.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/CitroenC4Cactus.jpg" width="680"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           The C4 is a responsive ride with excellent handling capabilities. The engines intelligent design offers both power and efficiency. The Citroen C4 Cactus has a turbocharged PureTech e-THP engine which delivers a power of 81 kW. It has an impressive fuel consumption of 4.6 L/100 km with 107g/km of CO2 emissions. It also boasts Airbump technology, a world exclusive, protecting your car from minor bumps and scrapes. The C4 Cactus has a boot capacity of up to 358 litres.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 60px;">
           <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="383" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/EEdNDdz-KXQ" width="680">
           </iframe>
          </p>
          <p>
           The
           <strong>
            Citroen C4 Aircross
           </strong>
           , on the other hand, offers a la carte transmission of 2WD and 4WD giving a smooth drive and comfortable suspension. CO2 emissions of this model are 119g/km on the 2WD and 129g/km on the 4WD and the boot capacity is 384 litres. Another great feature of the Aircross is its hands-free access and starter for greater ease of use.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="Aircross" class="aligncenter wp-image-7739" height="413" src="http://blog.automart.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/Aircross.jpg" width="680"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           A crucial difference between the
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.automart.co.za/cars/citroen/?utm_source=social&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=BlogAutoMartSEO201505">
             Citroen C4 Aircross
            </a>
           </strong>
           and the
           <a href="http://www.automart.co.za/cars/citroen/?utm_source=social&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=BlogAutoMartSEO201505">
            <strong>
             Citroen C4 Cactus
            </strong>
           </a>
           is the price. The new C4 Cactus starts at R224,900 while the Aircross falls under a higher price bracket starting at R313,900.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img alt="C4Cactus" class="aligncenter wp-image-7738" height="453" src="http://blog.automart.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/C4Cactus.png" width="680"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are looking for a capable and trustworthy vehicle, then the C4 is an awesome choice. This model will give you the confidence to tackle any situation. Ready to find a
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.automart.co.za/cars/citroen/?utm_source=social&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=BlogAutoMartSEO201505">
             Citroen C4 for sale
            </a>
           </strong>
           ? Auto Mart is the perfect place to start, allowing you to find and compare prices of
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.automart.co.za/cars/citroen/?utm_source=social&amp;utm_medium=blog&amp;utm_campaign=BlogAutoMartSEO201505">
             Citroens for sale
            </a>
           </strong>
           in your area.
          </p>
          <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="11810419" data-link="http://blog.automart.co.za/2015/05/29/live-an-adventure-in-the-citroen-c4-aircross-or-cactus/" data-summary="" data-title="Live an adventure in the Citroen C4 Aircross or Cactus">
          </div>
          <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="recommendations" data-app-id="11810427" data-link="http://blog.automart.co.za/2015/05/29/live-an-adventure-in-the-citroen-c4-aircross-or-cactus/" data-summary="" data-title="Live an adventure in the Citroen C4 Aircross or Cactus">
