         <em>
          Re-live your 'Bible Studies Category' (my soul). Know that the '
         </em>

        <br/>
        <div class="blogContent" id="blogContent">
         Re-live your 'Bible Studies Category' (my soul). Know that the 'Full Entry' of each blog is published and easy to find. Note your spiritual progress through time by God's grace - two steps forward and one step back. Be ever mindful of His mercy to you.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The Mighty Gospel Of Romans.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Soul instruction on the good news. The forty fifth book of the Bible, in chapter One reveals the gentiles' need for salvation. You see there the holy God giving long-standing rebels over to debased minds and further unrighteousness. This is simply how He expresses His wrath. Lesbianism and homosexuality is a result of this process and downward spiral. These particular sins are an indication that God's wrath is in full force. Unchecked man-kind will descend into all manner of disgusting uncleanness. The gentiles, which includes you as well, are in need of salvation. The wickedness of man brings dishonor to God his creator. Man usually worships lesser gods that often result in idolatry. Other groups deny God, in effect worshiping humanity or their own-selves producing an attitude of ungratefulness and foolishness. They become genuinely blind to the clear testimony of nature and seek perverse explanations. They lose the ability to discern His eternal power and Godhead. Man continues on toward destruction stifling the Truth that God has revealed. This same chapter also reveals the gospel in a nut-shell - a drink of living water. The gospel is the revelation of the righteousness of God through faith. It is God doing... (11/20/2008).
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The Blood, Forbearance, And Reconciliation - Part Of Being Saved.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         1. THE BLOOD. You (my soul) must have a change in life, by renewing your mind in the depths of your heart, until you have a new life from God - a type of blood transfusion .(Lev. 17:11; Mt. 26:28; Rom. 5:1-11; 6:1-23; 8:1-13; Eph. 1:7; 1 Jn. 1:7; Rev. 1:5; 5:8-10). For the life of the flesh is in the blood: and I, the Lord, have given it to you upon the altar to make an atonement for your souls: for it is the blood that makes an atonement for the soul. And Jesus continues with; "For this is my blood of the new testament, which is shed for many for the remission of sins". And Paul drives home the application to you (my soul); 'But God commends his love toward you, in that, while you were yet a sinner, Christ died for you. Much more then, being now justified by his blood, you shall be saved from wrath through him'. Don't you know, that when you were baptized into Jesus Christ you were baptized into his death? Therefore you are buried with him by baptism into death: that like as Christ was raised up from the dead by... (03/16/2009).
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Humble Yourself In The Sight Of The Lord.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Soul training on humility. Dear soul, do not seek happiness but a place where you can rest, repose, reside - with someone you can entrust yourself to and confide in. The Lord said Learn from me for I am meek and humble in heart and you will find repose for your souls (Matt. 11:29). Can you expect to learn from Jesus if you are filled with arrogance - that very thing that you have renamed as self-esteem, assertiveness, positiveness, or confidence; but which He consistently identifies as pride and therefore resists? Those who have experience will tell you that quietness and stillness are in themselves an excellent way and teacher. The fastest way to know God is to be still (Psalms 4:4; Psalms 46:10). Observe how a child settles down when placed in its mothers arms. Notice the manner in which John was able to take in the love that Jesus had and expressed toward him (John 13:23). This quietness and stillness - rest and repose - also includes the idea of meditation and contemplative knowing.[1]. Jesus is consistent on this theme, the Lord said Whoever will humble himself as this child shall be exalted; he, then, who exalts himself... (08/03/2009).
