                love

               this photoshopped idea by Roberto Vergati Santos. Imagine when big brands start sponsoring SuperHeroes Iron Man/McDonalds, Batman/Nike, Avengers/Coke, Flash/Red Bull, Silver Surfer/

                Imagine if one day capitalism reaches the point, where the big brands

                 starts to sponsor the superheroes
                </a>
                . How would this influence their images? Based on this hypothesis, I decided to experiment with some characters, and see what would be the results of such idea.
