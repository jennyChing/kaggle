        <h2>
         <a href="http://www.pyxle.net/media-center/tell-me-about-yourself-pyxle-icebreaker-of-the-week-2/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Tell me about yourself?  Pyxle Icebreaker of the Week">
          Tell me about yourself?  Pyxle Icebreaker of the Week
         </a>
        </h2>
        <p>
         by Seo Pyxle on March 6, 2015
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.pyxle.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Gethmini-Post1.png">
         <img alt="Social Media" class="alignnone wp-image-1540" height="388" src="http://www.pyxle.net/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Gethmini-Post1.png" width="725"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Gethmini Gooneratne  Social Media Executive
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         AKA: G/ Gethminion/Mini/
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        <strong>
         Why did you join Pyxle and how long have you been with the Pyxle Family?
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        I joined the Pyxle family to expand my career in
        <a href="http://www.pyxle.net/social-media-management/" target="_blank" title="Social Media Marketing">
         social media marketing
        </a>
        . It has been a wonderful 1.5 years, but feels like yesterday 
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        <strong>
         What motivates you to come to work every day?
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        Salary!! Kidding (LOL). Actually its the type of work I do, everyday there is something new we learn
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        <strong>
         What do you think about the Pyxle culture?
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        It is very flexible
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        <strong>
         Most interesting person in Pyxle and why?
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        I would say its everyone in the Pyxle Family, but mainly its Rameesh and Chrishan who leaves me clueless
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        <strong>
         Most unforgettable moment at Pyxle?
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        I have two remarkable events, one is the Pyxle Aurudu festival and the other one is the
        <a href="http://www.pyxle.net/media-center/win-a-christmas-tree-campaign-2014-feel-the-splendor-of-christmas/" target="_blank">
         Pyxle Christmas tree competition
        </a>
        (Facebook likes battle)
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        <strong>
         What do you think about the Pyxle working environment and why do you think a fun working environment is important?
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify">
        Pyxle environment is fun and flexible. A fun working environment makes you feel that the time is too short and it doesnt make you feel that you have been working for 8 hours 
       </p>
