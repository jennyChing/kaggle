       <p class="nd_single">
        <a href="http://beforeitsnews.com/prophecy/2014/06/life-altering-physicist-discovery-boggles-the-minds-of-millions-mind-blowing-video-2462416.html">
         (Before It's News)

       </p>
       <p style="text-align:justify">
        <img alt="" src="http://foreverconscious.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/holographic-universe-e1387413156355.jpg" style="height:354px;width:629px"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align:justify">
       </p>
       <p style="text-align:justify">
        Could everything you have ever seen and touched all be part of an elaborate hologram? Is the world really as real as we believe it to be, or is the spirit world (the afterlife) more real than life itself? These questions and more were addressed by a team of physicists who have delivered some of the firmest evidence that the earth is one big holographic projection.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        The
        <a href="/r2/?url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/12/11/universe-hologram-physicists_n_4428359.html" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         article in Huffington Post Reads:
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p style="margin-left:40px;text-align:justify">
        In 1997, theoretical physicist Juan Maldacena proposed that an audacious model of the Universe in which gravity arises from infinitesimally thin, vibrating strings could be reinterpreted in terms of well-established physics. The mathematically intricate world of strings, which exist in nine dimensions of space plus one of time, would be merely a hologram: the real action would play out in a simpler, flatter cosmos where there is no gravity.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p style="margin-left:40px;text-align:justify">
        Maldacenas idea thrilled physicists because it offered a way to put the popular but still unproven theory of strings on solid footing  and because it solved apparent inconsistencies between quantum physics and Einsteins theory of gravity. It provided physicists with a mathematical Rosetta stone, a duality, that allowed them to translate back and forth between the two languages, and solve problems in one model that seemed intractable in the other and vice versa. But although the validity of Maldacenas ideas has pretty much been taken for granted ever since, a rigorous proof has been elusive.
       </p>
       <p style="margin-left:40px;text-align:justify">
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <iframe frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/VJg9rsu2mFg?feature=player_detailpage" width="640">
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align:justify">
        We need to remember that earth is a testing ground for how we will spend eternity. We are here for just a second of time when compared to eternity. Our character will be tried, tested, and proven. The choices we make in this life will ultimately determine our eternal destiny.
       </p>
