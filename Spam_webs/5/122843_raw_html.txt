      <h2>
       <a href="http://www.thetroubadoursroad.com/featured-artist-ellie-goulding-lights/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: Featured Artist, Ellie Goulding, Lights">
        Featured Artist, Ellie Goulding, Lights
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p class="small">
       February 22nd, 2012				| 
 by Nels Thoreson				 | Published in
       <a href="http://www.thetroubadoursroad.com/category/song-of-the-day/" rel="category tag">
        Featured Songs
       </a>
      </p>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.thetroubadoursroad.com/featured-artist-ellie-goulding-lights/ellie-goulding/" rel="attachment wp-att-1551">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1551" height="386" src="http://www.thetroubadoursroad.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Ellie-Goulding-590x386.png" title="Ellie Goulding" width="590"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Todays Featured Artist and Song of the Day showcases Hereford, Englands own, Ellie Goulding, with her song, 
        <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/ellie-goulding/id338264227" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/ellie-goulding/id338264227', 'Lights');" target="_blank">
         Lights
        </a>
        , off her album,
        <em>
         Lights
        </em>
        , which was released through Polydor Records. The album hasreceivedmuch deserved critical acclaim, and topped the UK Album Charts. Gouldings work spans many genres and she plays a number of instruments, including guitar, piano, drums, clarinet andtambourine. The featured song has been remixed by many artists (Bassnectar, MK Charlee Dub, Max Gordon, Shook, and others), and has been played in many venues throughout the world.
       </p>
       <p>
        Goulding also has an incredible cover of Elton Johns, Your Song, which shows her diversity as an artist. That will be featured soon in an upcoming article of The Troubadours Road. Goulding has been on a whirlwind of tour dates recently, and currently, has no upcoming tour dates, but when they become available, theyll be published here.
       </p>
       <p>
        These words find their place in the song, Lights,
       </p>
       <div style="text-align: center;">
        <em>
         <em>
          I had a way then losing it all on my own
          <br/>
          I had a heart then but the queen has been overthrown
          <br/>
          And Im not sleeping now the dark is too hard to beat
          <br/>
          And Im not keeping up the strength I need to push me
          <p>
           You show the lights that stop me turn to stone
           <br/>
           You shine them when Im alone
           <br/>
           And so I tell myself that Ill be strong
           <br/>
           And dreaming when theyre gone
          </p>
          <p>
           Cause theyre calling, calling, calling me home
           <br/>
           Calling, calling, calling home
           <br/>
           You show the lights that stop me turn to stone
           <br/>
           You shine them when Im alone
          </p>
         </em>
        </em>
        <em>
         <em>
          
         </em>
        </em>
       </div>
       <p>
        For updates, tour dates, and other news click on her
        <a href="http://us.elliegoulding.com/Default.aspx" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://us.elliegoulding.com/Default.aspx', 'website');" target="_blank">
         website
        </a>
        , you can buy her music on
        <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/ellie-goulding/id338264227" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://itunes.apple.com/us/artist/ellie-goulding/id338264227', 'iTunes');" target="_blank">
         iTunes
        </a>
        , and follow her on her
        <a href="https://www.facebook.com/elliegoulding" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.facebook.com/elliegoulding', 'Facebook Page');" target="_blank">
         Facebook Page
        </a>
        . Until the next time youll be able to see a show or listen to her, here is Ellie Goulding, with her song, Lights, through Live Rising, enjoy,
       </p>
