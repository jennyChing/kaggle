         [RECIPE] Lemon Drop  Best way to get a girl drunkt!
        </h1>

         <div>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.drunkt.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/SDC12052.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="size-medium wp-image-116 aligncenter" height="225" src="http://www.drunkt.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/SDC12052-300x225.jpg" title="Lemon Drops" width="300"/>
           </a>
           <em>
            Drop em Panties!
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span style="font-size: small;">
            Cant find a drink that your girl / wife/ date is willing to budge on? Make this your goto drink, it works every time.
           </span>
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          It may take some work to make this drink but it will be worth seeing your date drunkter and a lot more laid back!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Name
          </strong>
           Lemon Drop
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           What you need -
          </strong>
          Ice, vodka, Lemon, sugar, and lemon drop mix
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Type of cup -
          </strong>
          Martini Glass
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Directions -
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          1. Fill up the drink shaker with ice in it.
         </p>
         <p>
          2. Pour a shot of vodka (lemon flavored vodka works well)
         </p>
         <p>
          3. Add two shot of lemon drop mix then shake
         </p>
        </div>
        <div>
         <p>
          4. wet the edges of the martini glass and put sugar around the rim.
         </p>
         <p>
          5. pour drink into the glass and watch your date get drunkt!!
         </p>
