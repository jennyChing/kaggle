       <div class="home_about">
        <h2 class="home_ab">
         About this collection
        </h2>
        <div class="hb">
         <div>
          <p>
           In this collection of television and radio programmes we celebrate the craftsmanship inherent in steam trains and the beauty of the British countryside through which these locomotives have passed.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           For   additional archive television programmes visit the
           <a href="http://www.bbc.co.uk/bbcfour/collections/p01277qd/steam-railways" title="http://www.bbc.co.uk/bbcfour/collections/p01277qd/steam-railways">
            BBC Four   Steam Railways
           </a>
           collection.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
