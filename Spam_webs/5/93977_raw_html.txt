         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Developer apps cydia launch release Everest, This tweak works with iOS 8.Everest jailbreak tweak lets you display a notification like banner on the top when activated, therefore allowing you to play/pause and change the music all from the banner. The song can be played or paused by tapping on the play/pause and button. Additionally, by swiping left or right on the banner changes the previous or next song.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          To bring the Everest notification banner you have to perform an
          <a href="http://idroidnews.net/popular-jailbreak-tweak-activator-updated-with-various-improvement-and-full-support-ios-8.html" target="_blank" title="Popular Jailbreak Tweak Activator Updated With Various Improvement and Full Support iOS 8">
           Activator
          </a>
          action or gesture that you can select from tweaks settings page. Apart from that Everest is also shown when a new song starts playing from a playlist. After installation you can configure options from the Settings.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <img alt="everest-cydia-tweak" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5847" height="424" src="http://idroidnews.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/everest-cydia-tweak.jpg" width="480"/>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Whats new in Everest v1.0-64
         </h2>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Initial release
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          If youre
          <a href="http://idroidnews.net/how-to-untethered-jailbreak-ios-8-ios-8-1-using-pangu.html" target="_blank" title="How To Untethered Jailbreak iOS 8  iOS 8.1 Using Pangu">
           jailbroken
          </a>
          , you can downloadEverest deb available in
          <a href="http://idroidnews.net/category/apple-idevice/cydia" target="_blank">
           cydia
          </a>
          via the BigBoss
          <a href="http://idroidnews.net/best-cydia-sources-2013.html" target="_blank" title="Best Cydia Sources 2013">
           repo
          </a>
          for $1 and supports iOS 8.
         </p>
