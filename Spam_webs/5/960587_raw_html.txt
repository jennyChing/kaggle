          <p>
           05.1.2012 | By
           <a href="http://showbizcafe.com/author/theboss/" rel="author" title="Posts by Jack Rico">
            Jack Rico
           </a>
           |
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="content">
          <p>
           Could one of the greatest franchises in cinema history,
           <strong>
            The Godfather
           </strong>
           , get a new sequel with
           <strong>
            Andy Garcia
           </strong>
           ?
           <strong>
            Jack Rico
           </strong>
           spoke to the Cuban actor, Garcia himself about the status of
           <strong>
            The Godfather Part 4
           </strong>
           , how he didnt speak to Luis Guzman about project, and how he would only do it if
           <strong>
            Francis Ford Coppola
           </strong>
           would direct it.
           <span id="more-11219">
           </span>
           <span style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
            If a movie like this is going to get made, Coppola himself would have to give it his blessing and hed have to direct it and write it. Andy Garcia would be the central piece to it moving forward in the role of
           </span>
           <strong style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
            Vincent Mancini
           </strong>
           <span style="line-height: 1.714285714; font-size: 1rem;">
            .
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Godfather Part III
           </strong>
           ended with Garcia taking over the reigns of the Corleone family from Al Pacinos Michael Corleone, as its undisputed leader.
          </p>
          <p>
           The role would mean a comeback to an A-list level for Garcia who was nominated for his role in
           <em>
            The Godfather Part III
           </em>
           , but for the past few years he has been concentrating on personal independent films such as 
           <strong>
            City Island
           </strong>
            and now 
           <strong>
            For Greater Glory
           </strong>
           .
          </p>
          <p>
           There were reports that ubiquitous Puerto Rican actor
           <strong>
            Luis Guzman
           </strong>
           was asked by Garcia to join him in the film if it was greenlit, but Garcia quickly shot it down saying, I never spoke to Luis Guzman.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Leonardo Dicaprio and Ray Liotta
           </strong>
           would be in the mix, but who else would make sense? A specter of Al Pacino, De Niro, Duvall?
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Would you want to see a The Godfather 4 sequel?
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>

  								previmage = jQuery("div.fullwidthbanner-container ul li:last-child").find('.tinyfeatured img').attr('src');
  							}
  							
  							jQuery('div.fullwidthbanner-container').find('.preview.right .slide_title').text(nexttext);
  							jQuery('div.fullwidthbanner-container').find('.preview.left .slide_title').text(prevtext);
  							
  							jQuery('div.fullwidthbanner-container').find('.preview.right .slide_thumb').html('&lt;img class="thumbnail" src="'+nextimage+'" /&gt;');
  							jQuery('div.fullwidthbanner-container').find('.preview.left .slide_thumb').html('&lt;img class="thumbnail" src="'+previmage+'" /&gt;');
  						}
  					});
  				},
  				function () {
  				}
			);
			
			function isNear( element, distance, event ) {
			
			    var left = element.offset().left - distance,
			        top = element.offset().top - distance,
			        right = left + element.width() + 2*distance,
			        bottom = top + element.height() + 2*distance,
			        x = event.pageX,
			        y = event.pageY;
			
			    return ( x &gt; left &amp;&amp; x &lt; right &amp;&amp; y &gt; top &amp;&amp; y &lt; bottom );
			
			};
		})
