        <div class="format_text entry-content">
         <p>
          After spending the better part of a day at the beautiful seaside village and
          <a href="http://cherylhoward.com/2012/10/24/instagramming-porto-venere-italy/" title="http://cherylhoward.com/2012/10/24/instagramming-porto-venere-italy/">
           UNESCO world heritage site of Porto Venere
          </a>
          , our group settled in nearby
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sarzana" target="_blank" title="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sarzana">
           Sarzana
          </a>
          for the night indulging in (believe it or not) even more
          <strong>
           Ligurian food and wine
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          I stopped to wonder if Italian people really ate like this every day and further ponder as to why they arent all overweight. I vowed to go on a diet and start a new exercise routine immediately upon my return
          <a href="http://cherylhoward.com/2012/03/18/so-i-moved-to-berlin-the-back-story-and-why/" title="http://cherylhoward.com/2012/03/18/so-i-moved-to-berlin-the-back-story-and-why/">
           home to Berlin
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          The next morning, we set out to conquer yet another
          <strong>
           small Italian village in Liguria
          </strong>
          . We headed to
          <a href="http://www.comune.vareseligure.sp.it/cm/pages/ServeBLOB.php/L/IT/IDPagina/1" target="_blank" title="http://www.comune.vareseligure.sp.it/cm/pages/ServeBLOB.php/L/IT/IDPagina/1">
           Varese Ligure
          </a>
          , one of the best preservedmedieval communitiesin all of Italy filled with stone houses and various ancient sites. While Porto Venereboasts a dramatic and majestic coastal landscape and is typically filled with tourists, Varese Ligureis nestled further inland amidst the regions gentle rolling hills. In comparison, its quiet, understated and completely charming.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Varese Ligure, Italy. " class="aligncenter" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy8.jpg" title="Varese Ligure, Italy. " width="612"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy8.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          We were ledon a tour through Varese Ligureby local, Karen Nilson who happens to bethe wife of a chef whod lead us in cooking lessons later that day.Karen explained that more and more people are coming to spend their holidays here, especially artists who thrive on the pace of small-town life and the serenity of the surroundings.
         </p>
         <p>
          We began our explorations and encountered the beautiful
          <strong>
           16th century Grexino bridge
          </strong>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Varese Ligure, Italy. " class="aligncenter" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy3.jpg" title="Varese Ligure, Italy. " width="612"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy3.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Varese Ligure, Italy. " class="aligncenter" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy10.jpg" title="Varese Ligure, Italy. " width="612"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy10.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          We were then introduced to some other locals. While we came upon an adorable dog in Porto Venere, we came upon Puss N Boots himself in Varese Ligure!
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Varese Ligure, Italy. " class="aligncenter" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy5.jpg" title="Varese Ligure, Italy. " width="612"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy5.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Varese Ligure, Italy. " class="aligncenter" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy6.jpg" title="Varese Ligure, Italy. " width="612"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy6.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Humming the tune 
          <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP4NMoJcFd4" target="_blank" title="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sP4NMoJcFd4">
           I Love Cats
          </a>
           (ok, it was only me humming), we continued along to the center of town to view some other notable siteslike this castle complete with a tower dating from 1435. Unfortunately, the castle is not currently open to the public but its stillworth checking out. Who doesnt love castles?
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy7.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="Varese Ligure, Italy. " class="aligncenter" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy2.jpg" title="Varese Ligure, Italy. " width="612"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy2.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          As the town is small, it can beexplored within2-3 hoursonfoot and youll soon realize that the layout of Varese Ligure is round for former defence purposes. Also rather fortunately, most ofthe
          <a href="http://www.lifeinitaly.com/tourism/liguria/varese-ligure" target="_blank" title="http://www.lifeinitaly.com/tourism/liguria/varese-ligure">
           major points of interest
          </a>
          are located close to the center so its easy to walk around and get a feel for the place.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Varese Ligure, Italy. " class="aligncenter" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy7.jpg" title="Varese Ligure, Italy. " width="612"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy7.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy1.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="Varese Ligure, Italy. " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8552" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy1.jpg" title="Varese Ligure, Italy. " width="612"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy1.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Varese Ligure, Italy. " class="aligncenter" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy4.jpg" title="Varese Ligure, Italy. " width="612"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy4.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          We also made a stop to the workshop of Pietro Picetti, an artisanwho makes wooden stamps used to mark pasta. Continuing an age oldtradition to personally stamp pasta with family crests or other symbols, he now sells themtopeople from all around the world.You canvisit the workshop yourself to purchaseone or even place a custom order.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           Address
          </span>
           Via Pieve, 19028 Varese Ligure. +39 0187 842195
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Varese Ligure, Italy." class="aligncenter" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy9.jpg" title="Varese Ligure, Italy. " width="612"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/varese_ligure_italy9.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Then it time for our cooking lessons to begin! Led by Chef Marco Marcone from the Michelin starred
           <a href="http://www.albergoamici.com/" target="_blank" title="http://www.albergoamici.com/">
            AlbergoAmici Ristorante
           </a>
           , we made pasta from scratch using different stamps.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #888888;">
           <em>
            <a href="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/pasta_stamping_italy.jpg">
             <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
              <img alt="Stamping or Branding Ligurian Pasta. " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8562" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/pasta_stamping_italy.jpg" title="Stamping or Branding Ligurian Pasta. " width="612"/>
              <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/pasta_stamping_italy.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
              </span>
             </span>
            </a>
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #888888;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            This was the 
            <em>
             fruit of our labors
            </em>
              two kinds of pasta withcheese and pesto. And not surprisingly this was only one of many courses! The food was of course delicious (not just because we had a hand in making it) and as such, Id recommend stopping by Albergo Amici for a tasty lunch of dinner.
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #888888;">
           <span style="color: #000000;">
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             Address:
            </span>
            Via Garibaldi 80 Varese Ligure 19028. +39 (0) 187 842139
           </span>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #888888;">
           <em>
            <a href="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/pasta_ligurian_italy.jpg">
             <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
              <img alt="Famous Pesto Pasta of Liguria." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8561" height="612" src="http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/pasta_ligurian_italy.jpg" title="Famous Pesto Pasta of Liguria." width="612"/>
              <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://cherylhoward.com/2012/11/01/instragramming-varese-ligure-italy/&amp;media=http://cherylhoward.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/pasta_ligurian_italy.jpg&amp;description=Instragramming  Varese Ligure, Italy.')">
              </span>
             </span>
            </a>
           </em>
          </span>

         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Before we knew it,
           <strong>
            our time in Varese Ligure
           </strong>
           was over and we were off to explore yet another beautiful coastal town. Stay tuned until next week when I showcase Chiavari, Italy.
