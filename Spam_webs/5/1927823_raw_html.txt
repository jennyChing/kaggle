                     <a href="http://roomtoinspire.blogspot.com/2010/03/easter-egg-project.html">
                      Easter Egg Project ~
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXgsIm1mI/AAAAAAAAC4Q/URWS58vqzM4/s1600-h/egg2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="213" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXgsIm1mI/AAAAAAAAC4Q/URWS58vqzM4/s320/egg2.jpg" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      I recently picked up this egg at Micheals and my husband kept asking "What ARE you going to do with this?" Well I decided to transform my blue foam egg!
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXeR9KTII/AAAAAAAAC4A/bbiMt9tFez4/s1600-h/blue+egg.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="260" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXeR9KTII/AAAAAAAAC4A/bbiMt9tFez4/s400/blue+egg.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      I started by spray painting it Heirloom White -
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXnDy0v7I/AAAAAAAAC5I/wrsCKQ9Qpms/s1600-h/white+egg.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="280" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXnDy0v7I/AAAAAAAAC5I/wrsCKQ9Qpms/s400/white+egg.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      I then mixed together silver and gold paint and sort of "fauxed" the egg to give it a little texture -
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXlIewqfI/AAAAAAAAC44/bHbD0JbOnBs/s1600-h/eggpainted.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="267" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXlIewqfI/AAAAAAAAC44/bHbD0JbOnBs/s400/eggpainted.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      And what egg is complete without a few dots -
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXoE-AHpI/AAAAAAAAC5Q/yINi_Vw40f8/s1600-h/with+dots.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="267" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXoE-AHpI/AAAAAAAAC5Q/yINi_Vw40f8/s400/with+dots.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Added a ribbon -
                     </div>
                     <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXpJatSgI/AAAAAAAAC5Y/DNKaEUJWjAA/s1600-h/withribbon.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                      <img border="0" height="267" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXpJatSgI/AAAAAAAAC5Y/DNKaEUJWjAA/s400/withribbon.jpg" width="400"/>
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      And I wanted to make a tag so I grabbed one of these frames I picked up at Micheals for $1 -
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/_-3WBViHssK8/S5SXmNYbCWI/AAAAAAAAC5A/19De3PyBVj4/s1600-h/frame.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">

                      </a>

                     </div>
