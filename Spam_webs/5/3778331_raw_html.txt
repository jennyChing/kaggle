      <h3 class="heading">
       The Founding Story
      </h3>
      <p>
       Brilliant Earth was co-founded by Beth Gerstein and Eric Grossberg, who met at Stanford University. When Beth was about to be engaged, she experienced firsthand, with her fianc Alex, the challenge of finding a conflict free engagement ring that represented her values. Eric, through his research at Stanford, became impassioned with the idea that responsibly-sourced jewelry could be an effective tool for social change in developing countries. Together, Beth and Eric founded Brilliant Earth in 2005.
      </p>
      <div class="her-values">
       <h4 class="ls-p5 text-success">
        <span class="max-w640 center-block">
         When Beth was about to be engaged, she experienced the challenge of finding an engagement ring that represented her values
        </span>
       </h4>
      </div>
      <p>
       What began as a partnership between two entrepreneurs has grown into a community of people with a passion for fine jewelry and a commitment to change. The Brilliant Earth team is a group of dedicated individuals  jewelry designers, sales associates, gemologists, and more  all working to provide exceptional jewelry that our customers can feel good about wearing. Our customers, too, are part of our community. It is with our customers' help that we are cultivating a more humane jewelry industry, one stunning piece of jewelry at a time.
      </p>
