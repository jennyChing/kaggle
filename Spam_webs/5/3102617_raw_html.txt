             <h1 style="text-align: center;">
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               SUITE DREAMS
              </span>
             </h1>
             <h3 style="text-align: center;">
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               Le Mridien Beach Plaza Monte Carlo
              </span>
             </h3>
             <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
              <span class="su-dropcap su-dropcap-style-flat" style="font-size:2em">
               L
              </span>
              ive the high life at
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               <strong>
                <a href="http://www.lemeridienmontecarlo.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.lemeridienmontecarlo.com/', 'Le Mridien Beach Plaza hotel');" style="color: #000000;" target="_blank" title="le Meridien Monte Carlo">
                 Le Mridien Beach Plaza hotel
                </a>
               </strong>
               and love every minute of your stayin
               <strong>
                <a href="http://www.fathomaway.com/guides/europe/monaco/cheatsheet/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.fathomaway.com/guides/europe/monaco/cheatsheet/', 'Monaco');" style="color: #000000;" title="Fathom">
                 Monaco
                </a>
               </strong>
               .
              </span>
              Glitz and glamour arent hard to come by in this part of the world but if you want to make the most of the Mediterranean, Le Mridien Beach Plaza Hotel is the only hotel in Monte Carlo with its own private beach
             </p>
             <p>
              <span id="more-1279">
              </span>
             </p>
             <h3 style="text-align: center;">
              GLAMOUR &amp;LUXURY
             </h3>
             <p style="text-align: left; padding-left: 30px;">
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               <div class="row-fluid ">
                <div class="span4">
                 <strong>
                  <p class="dropcap normal">
                   T
                  </p>
                  heglitzy sibling of theCte dAzur,
                 </strong>
                 the municipality of Monaco has squeezed into an area no bigger than Londons Hyde Park. Directly between the flashy French and Italian Rivieras, Monte Carlo is dominated by anunmistakable cluster of towers. What itlacks in size, it more than makes up for in extravagance. Michelin stars, magnificent buildings, luxury stores, mega yachts and uber expensive car dealerships saturate thiswell establishedMediterranean playground.Ifcars are your thing, youll want to keep your mouth closed to contain your drooling. You wont see many regularrust buckets round here, the roads are royally clogged withAston Martins, Ferraris and Bentleys, all out forajoy ride alongtheroute made famous by Monte Carlos annual transformation into a
                 <strong>
                  <a href="http://french-riviera-blog.com/2014/03/23/enjoying-a-hassle-free-monaco-f1-grand-prix-experience-a-full-practical-guide-for-first-time-visitors/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://french-riviera-blog.com/2014/03/23/enjoying-a-hassle-free-monaco-f1-grand-prix-experience-a-full-practical-guide-for-first-time-visitors/', 'Formula One Grand Prix');" style="color: #000000;" title="Formula One Grand Prix Monaco">
                   Formula One Grand Prix
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 race track.
                </div>
                <div class="span4">
                 <strong>
                  <p class="dropcap normal">
                   Y
                  </p>
                  ou certainly wouldnt ever be the only one
                 </strong>
                 inside the spectacular Casino de Monte Carlo enjoying a martini in an ode to James Bond- shaken, not stirred, of course. One of the worlds first major casinos and probably its most lavish, the Casino de Monte Carlo casts an extravagant spell over Place du Casino that sets the town abuzz. But when youre done with the martinis and youve finished marinating in the madness of the worlds most impressive car park,Le Mridien Beach Plaza is the perfect place to relax. Nestled beside the huge outdoor pool complex, youll find your very own beachparadise.Decked out with sun lounges and serviced by both a bar and a gelato shop,the only running around youll really needto do, will be to cool off in the clear, blue shallows of the MediterraneanThehotels Deluxe rooms were a brightblend of contemporary and classic, all of them with sizeable private balconies, but it was the incredible resort facilities that really took the cake. Thefitness facilities were top notch.The two level gym was one of the better hotel set upsIve encountered.
                </div>
                <div class="span4">
                 <strong>
                  <p class="dropcap normal">
                   G
                  </p>
                  uests are spoilt for choice in the relax &amp; unwind departmentas well.
                 </strong>
                 Complimenting the private beach andimmaculate outdoor pool complex there is an indoor pool right beside the gym. Aqua aerobics classes took place early each morning; lead by a young twinkle toed French man from the side of the pool, it looked like hilarious fun. Huge buffet breakfasts, seafood lunches and outdoor barbecues were served on the terrace overlooking the beach. For the late night sweet tooth, some seriously delicious desserts were available on the room service menu. The deconstructed lemon Meringue number was as exquisite as the chocolate mousse was life-changing. Definitely the perfect place to stayon your next
                 <strong>
                  <a href="http://www.aluxurytravelblog.com/2014/04/03/5-of-europes-best-luxury-destinations-for-the-summer/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.aluxurytravelblog.com/2014/04/03/5-of-europes-best-luxury-destinations-for-the-summer/', 'visit to Monaco');" style="color: #000000;" target="_blank" title="A Luxury Travel Blog">
                   visit to Monaco
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 . If you fancy venturing just a few hundred metres down the road, you should try the exquisite Michelin star restaurant Elsa while youre in the area. See myraving review of the experience
                 <strong>
                  <a href="http://www.backtobuckley.com/elsa-monaco/" style="color: #000000;" target="_blank" title="Dine at Elsa Monaco">
                   here
                  </a>
                 </strong>
                 .
                </div>
               </div>
               <!-- .row-fluid (end) -->
              </span>
             </p>
