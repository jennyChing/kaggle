        <p>
         Is it SERIOUSLY May 1st? And are we SERIOUSLY getting a major cold front through most of the country tomorrow?
        </p>
        <p>
         Ugh.
        </p>
        <p>
         Lets forget about the cold winds, rain and snow and sweaters well need in a few hours. Lets plan for a warm-weather day with some
         <strong>
          Cinco de Mayo recipes
         </strong>
         !
        </p>
        <p>
         We love Tex-Mex and true Mexican flavors in our family; Taco Tuesday is a regular feature in my
         <a href="http://followinginmyshoes.com/category/healthy-living/meal-planning/" target="_blank">
          menu planning
         </a>
         and the Kidlets look forward to that meal more than any other each week. In fact, Tex-Mex inspired recipes make up the largest flavor sub-group in my list of
         <a href="http://followinginmyshoes.com/recipe-index/">
          easy family-friendly dinner ideas
         </a>
         . So, yeah, it wasnt hard to find inspiration within my own archives for this recipe round-up , and a quick search of some of my favorite food bloggers revealed other yummy looking recipes  all perfect for a
         <strong>
          Cinco de Mayo Fiesta
         </strong>
         !
        </p>
