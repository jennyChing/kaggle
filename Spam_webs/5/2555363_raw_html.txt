         <h1 class="title">
          How did Rory get to East Lake? Practice
         </h1>
         <div class="column" id="content">
          <div class="section">
           <div id="content-area">
            <div class="node node-type-blog clearfix" id="node-336789">
             <div class="content">
              <div class="blog-image">
               <div class="field field-type-filefield field-field-blog-image">
                <img alt="Rory McIlroy" class="imagecache imagecache-blog_large imagecache-default imagecache-blog_large_default" height="360" src="http://www.pga.com/sites/default/files/imagecache/blog_large/blogs/Rory-McIlroy-55-putts.jpg" title="Rory McIlroy" width="640"/>

              <div class="field field-type-text field-field-blog-body">
               <p>
                Well, I've run out of fingers and toes on which to count.
               </p>
               <p>
                While practicing before the final round of the Tour Championship at East Lake, Rory McIlroy made 55 consecutive putts from 10 feet. That's right, 55 in a row.
               </p>
               <p>
                That alone is an impressive accomplishment. When you see all 55 made in 44 seconds, it looks even more amazing.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <div class="media_embed" height="360" width="640">
                <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/2mlUKN8QfE8" width="640">
                </iframe>
               </div>
               <p>
                That video is just further proof of why Rory is the No. 1-ranked golfer in the world right now.
               </p>
               <p>
                <span style="font-size: 12px; line-height: 20.3999996185303px;">
                 In case you were wondering, Guinness World Records does not list an entry for most consecutive putts made. Maybe Rory should submit this video.
                </span>
               </p>
               <p>
                Related:
                <a href="http://www.pga.com/news/golf-buzz/guinness-world-records-2015-longest-usable-golf-club">
                 Man creates longest "usable" golf club
                </a>
               </p>
               <p style="font-size: 12.222222328186px; line-height: 20.3999996185303px;">
                McIlroy entered the final round tied with Billy Horschel for the lead at 9 under. They were two shots clear of Jim Furyk.
               </p>
               <p>
                Want to be able to practice like Rory? We can't guarantee that you'll make 55 putts in a row, but this video should help you out.
               </p>
               <p>
               </p>
               <div class="media_embed" height="500" width="640">
                <iframe frameborder="0" height="500" scrolling="no" src="http://www.pga.com/video-embed/video/instruction/2014/05/08/pga_ins_putting_practicegreen_routine.pga" width="640">
                </iframe>
               </div>
               <p>
