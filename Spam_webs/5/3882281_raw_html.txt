       <h1 class="entry-title">
        The Feminist Breeder Banned From Facebook Again
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">
         Posted on
        </span>
        <span class="entry-date">
         December 7, 2013 6:31 pm

       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-639030" height="427" src="http://getoffmyinternets.net/gomipress/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/snore.png" title="snore" width="537"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Feminist Breeder, a
         <a href="http://getoffmyinternets.net/the-feminist-breeder-is-a-strong-black-woman/" target="_blank">
          strong black woman
         </a>
         , is having yet another emotional uproar. It seems she posted a picture of her topless daughter to Facebook, and the image was removed. In typical mature Gina fashion, she responded by reposting the image, along with a caption calling employees at Facebook a bunch of perverts. Not so shockingly, Facebook responded by once again removing the image, and then
         <a href="http://thefeministbreeder.com/why-subscribe/tfb-banned-facebook/" target="_blank">
          blocked her for 30 days
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          I just ugh. [****] facebook so hard. And [****] every pervert who reported that picture. I hope you rot in hell.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         Yes, instead of just posting about this on her own blog in the first place, Gina poked the bear and then proceeded to whine when she got a claw across her face. Meanwhile, the Feminst Breeder Facebook page is
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/thefeministbreeder/posts/10151795857952727" target="_blank">
          making hay
         </a>
         while the sun shines and dialing up the outrage to 9000000 decibels. Fangirls are planning revenge campaigns  everything from flooding Facebook with topless toddler pics to boycotting Facebook for a month. People are even
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/thefeministbreeder/posts/10151797584067727" target="_blank">
          tweeting at Randi Zuckerberg
         </a>
         - who no longer even works at Facebook -asking her to talk to her brother about the situation.
        </p>
        <p>
         Considering how draconian Gina is about
         <a href="http://getoffmyinternets.net/the-feminst-breeder-deleted-your-account/" target="_blank">
          deleting users
         </a>
         and comments from her own outlets, youd think shed have a little more respect for Facebook running their company the way they want.
        </p>
