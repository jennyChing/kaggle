        Contemporary Brazilian Home Promises Spectacular View And A Stunning Art Gallery
       </a>
      </h1>
     </header>

      <p>
       Brazil is renowned for some exotic vacations and holidays that rekindle your spirit. Located in Nova Lima and surrounded by a stunning landscape, this gorgeous modern home is all about a relaxed lifestyle. Designed in an extravagant and exquisite fashion by
       <a href="http://www.facebook.com/dmacedoarquitetura" target="_blank">
        Denise Macedo Arquitetos Associados
       </a>
       , the residence offers unabated views of valleys and mountains of Minas Gerais. The view on the outside is matched by an equally appealing collection of modern artwork on the inside.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-04-19/contemporary-brazilian-home-promises-spectacular-view-and-a-stunning-art-gallery/nova-lima-house-in-brazil/">
        <img alt="Nova Lima House in Brazil Contemporary Brazilian Home Promises Spectacular View And A Stunning Art Gallery" class="size-full wp-image-60770 aligncenter" height="400" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Nova-Lima-House-in-Brazil.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Designed to combine the large art collection of the owners of the house with the beautiful scenery outside, the Nova Lime House takes the shape of a modern museum encased in glass. The interiors of the home are largely neutral and spacious and sport a calm and tranquil atmosphere. Custom designed gallery and corridors ensure that the wide variety of art pieces and paintings are displayed perfectly.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-04-19/contemporary-brazilian-home-promises-spectacular-view-and-a-stunning-art-gallery/beautifully-lit-house-in-the-evening/">

       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-04-19/contemporary-brazilian-home-promises-spectacular-view-and-a-stunning-art-gallery/enjoy-some-lovely-evenings-on-the-patio/">
        <img alt="Enjoy some lovely evenings on the patio" class="size-full wp-image-60754 aligncenter" height="400" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Enjoy-some-lovely-evenings-on-the-patio.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-04-19/contemporary-brazilian-home-promises-spectacular-view-and-a-stunning-art-gallery/spacious-interiors-of-nova-lima/">
        <img alt="Spacious interiors of Nova Lima" class="size-full wp-image-60755 aligncenter" height="400" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Spacious-interiors-of-Nova-Lima.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-04-19/contemporary-brazilian-home-promises-spectacular-view-and-a-stunning-art-gallery/plush-decor-in-muted-tones/">
        <img alt="Plush decor in muted tones" class="size-full wp-image-60756 aligncenter" height="400" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Plush-decor-in-muted-tones.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2013-04-19/contemporary-brazilian-home-promises-spectacular-view-and-a-stunning-art-gallery/flowing-interiors-that-are-visually-connected-throughout/">
        <img alt="Flowing interiors that are visually connected throughout" class="size-full wp-image-60757 aligncenter" height="400" src="http://cdn.decoist.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/04/Flowing-interiors-that-are-visually-connected-throughout.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Large floor-to-ceiling glass windows ensure that the lush green natural backdrop outside always stays visually connected with the interiors. Burnt cement floors, white walls and sleek modern dcor grace are found throughout the residence. A large dining area, ergonomic kitchen and a lavish lounge add to the relaxed ambiance inside. A dash of natural green is also added to the interiors while
       <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2012-08-23/23-modern-children-bedroom-ideas-for-the-contemporary-home/" target="_blank">
        plush bedrooms
       </a>
       and large patio space provide an opportunity to relax and take in the many sights and sounds just outside.
      </p>
