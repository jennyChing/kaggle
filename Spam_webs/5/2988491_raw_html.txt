                          <img border="0" height="266" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-XerQy2uJ5EY/U-JdhfCOd0I/AAAAAAAAGVA/T5oudHzTEgQ/s1600/Photo+on+8-6-14+at+9.48+AM+%232.jpg" width="400"/>
                         </a>
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">
                         That's
                         <a href="http://chrisbrecheen.blogspot.com/2013/12/enter-contrarian.html">
                          The Contrarian
                         </a>
                         taking his morning nap in the carrier.
                         <br/>
                         He assured me he wasn't tired ("I am not!") right up until he passed out.
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                      </tbody>
                     </table>
                     <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                      If you want to love
                      <i>
                       Writing About Writing
                      </i>
                      and it's short-haired but adorable writer, I'm here to tell you a few tips on how to make them smile.
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Hi everybody!!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Chris here. I hope you're having a wonderful hump day filled with humping the hell out of someone, even if it's just yourself. (If you're into that sort of thing, of course. I wouldn't want you to hump yourself without your consent just because it's Wednesday. That's just not cool.)
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     We have a lot of great posts in the works right now. The latest part of
                     <a href="http://chrisbrecheen.blogspot.com/2013/05/a-demons-rubicon-by-chris-brecheen.html">
                      A Demon's Rubicon
                     </a>
                     will likely be up this week or early next, and this weekend you can expect the first part of a short series about starting your own blog. Friday's hate mail is going to be a doozy.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Today, however, I'm taking a quick moment out to tell you how you can help. Because, frankly,
                     <a href="http://chrisbrecheen.blogspot.com/2014/08/blogust-is-here.html">
                      Blogust
                     </a>
                     isn't going so well.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                      <tbody>
                       <tr>
                        <td style="text-align: center;">
                         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-B3bnluO0L_k/U-J0NbxBNVI/AAAAAAAAGVY/BMQKN3BuePE/s1600/Photo+on+8-6-14+at+11.28+AM.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                          <img border="0" height="133" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-B3bnluO0L_k/U-J0NbxBNVI/AAAAAAAAGVY/BMQKN3BuePE/s1600/Photo+on+8-6-14+at+11.28+AM.jpg" width="200"/>
                         </a>
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                       <tr>
                        <td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">

                         <br/>
                         Do not even attempt to contain your tears.
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                      </tbody>
                     </table>
                     Okay, that's not exactly true. Blogust is actually going quite well.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                      <tbody>
                       <tr>
                        <td style="text-align: center;">
                         <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-PBv7OY-5p3w/U-J4NJve7oI/AAAAAAAAGV4/sGyi8_aKxf0/s1600/Photo+on+8-6-14+at+11.46+AM.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                          <img border="0" height="133" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-PBv7OY-5p3w/U-J4NJve7oI/AAAAAAAAGV4/sGyi8_aKxf0/s1600/Photo+on+8-6-14+at+11.46+AM.jpg" width="200"/>
                         </a>
                        </td>

                         So...we're okay then?
                        </td>
                       </tr>
                      </tbody>
                     </table>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      The numbers are absolutely fantastic, but they're nowhere near what we would need to reach 50,000
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;">
                      <tbody>
                       <tr>
                        <td style="text-align: center;">
                         <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-D8iobF9xcjM/U-J4py793wI/AAAAAAAAGWA/mQU2NYqQJz0/s1600/Photo+on+8-6-14+at+11.48+AM.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">
                          <img border="0" height="133" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-D8iobF9xcjM/U-J4py793wI/AAAAAAAAGWA/mQU2NYqQJz0/s1600/Photo+on+8-6-14+at+11.48+AM.jpg" width="200"/>
                         </a>
                        </td>

                     So what can you do to help
                     <i>
                      Writing About Writing
                     </i>
                     hit it's (admittedly insanely overambitious) goals and make this:
                     <br/>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-B3bnluO0L_k/U-J0NbxBNVI/AAAAAAAAGVY/BMQKN3BuePE/s1600/Photo+on+8-6-14+at+11.28+AM.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="133" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-B3bnluO0L_k/U-J0NbxBNVI/AAAAAAAAGVY/BMQKN3BuePE/s1600/Photo+on+8-6-14+at+11.28+AM.jpg" width="200"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      Turn into this:
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-mikB6OubEd4/U-KVdvCeDQI/AAAAAAAAGWY/D6z-YgfwTo0/s1600/Photo+on+8-6-14+at+1.51+PM.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="133" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-mikB6OubEd4/U-KVdvCeDQI/AAAAAAAAGWY/D6z-YgfwTo0/s1600/Photo+on+8-6-14+at+1.51+PM.jpg" width="200"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      Seeming non sequitur that isn't: I got an e-mail this week from my boss. She told me that they have another class on Monday nights that I could teach. I'm already teaching one on Wednesday. Normally I would have to take at least two classes to keep up with the sundry things that aren't covered by cleaning The Hall of Rectitude. This semester I was able to tell her that I was okay. That is because of all of you. I have been able to drop to part time and put up more articles each week. It really does make a difference.
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      I always feel a little weird and dirty writing these posts. And most of the time I'm pointing towards the Paypal donation button and clearing my throat, not just hoping you'll help me with some promotion. But self-promotion has to be shameless. The myth of the writer who doesn't need money and is too good to ever have to ask for it not only isn't true, but it never really was. I'm very serious when I say I can't do it without you. I don't have the social media reach. I'm using every trick I know, and still coming up about 400 short a day.
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      Blogust's success depends on all of you.
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      I will draw the line at simply telling you how you can help. I'm not begging. Not demanding. Not guilt tripping. Not posting pictures of starving kids from developing nation and telling you that only you can help them beat the dysentery. I don't want to turn this into the last ten minutes of a 700 Club episode (though if you listen to NPR, you know that pledge week is ten times worse.) I will simply tell you how you can help if you are so inclined.
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                       <b>
                        Share an older article you liked.
                       </b>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                       <i>
                        One article.
                       </i>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      Not
                      <i>
                       this
                      </i>
                      article. I mean one of my good ones from the past. Something that you really enjoyed. Maybe something I haven't reshared in a while. You all have friends I don't have. You all know people who might like stuff that I simply can't reach. Every time one of you has shared an article it has gotten lots of new traffic.
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      If you look over to the right side you can see my top ten articles of all time. If the article you want isn't there, you can use the text box at the top right to search (just
                      <i>
                       Writing About Writing
                      </i>
                      ) for the article you want. You can also go to
                      <a href="http://chrisbrecheen.blogspot.com/p/the-reliquary.html">
                       The Reliquary
                      </a>
                      if you have an idea of the category of article you'd like to share but aren't sure about the rest.
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      An old
                      <a href="http://chrisbrecheen.blogspot.com/2012/09/the-best-of-mailbox.html">
                       mailbox
                      </a>
                      (even a
                      <a href="http://chrisbrecheen.blogspot.com/2014/04/a-tall-cool-frothing-glass-of-haterade.html">
                       hate mail
                      </a>
                      ) you really liked? One of my
                      <a href="http://chrisbrecheen.blogspot.com/2012/02/writing-prompts.html">
                       prompts
                      </a>
                      that you loved? Do you have an old favorite
                      <a href="http://chrisbrecheen.blogspot.com/2012/04/leela-bruce-kung-fu-fights-bad-advice.html">
                       Leela Bruce
                      </a>
                      or
                      <a href="http://chrisbrecheen.blogspot.com/2012/02/goodman-st-white-hates-speculative.html">
                       Guy Goodman
                      </a>
                      guest blog? Or maybe you prefer when I write about "
                      <a href="http://chrisbrecheen.blogspot.com/search/label/That%20%22Feminist%20Crap%22">
                       that feminist crap
                      </a>
                      ."
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      Even one article will help. The share buttons are down at the bottom of every single article. All you have to do is click once. Easy peasy.
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      Thank you. I'm sorry that modern self-promotion is a little skeevy and pimptacular. You are all wonderful.
