       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Simple Fall Centerpiece Ideas
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-10-29T06:00:06+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          October 29, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.alifeinbalance.net/author/the-farm-girl-gabs/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Melissa Russo
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.alifeinbalance.net/simple-fall-centerpiece-ideas/#comments">
           25 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <center>
         <iframe border="0" frameborder="0" height="250" marginwidth="0" scrolling="no" src="http://rcm-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/cm?t=mydaro-20&amp;o=1&amp;p=12&amp;l=ur1&amp;category=primeday&amp;banner=0PP07G32K0XD5NP65HG2&amp;f=ifr&amp;lc=pf4&amp;linkID=QHON7X225QRU5LVG" style="border:none;" width="300">
         </iframe>
        </center>
        <p>
         <em>
          Please welcome Melissa of
          <a href="http://thefarmgirlgabs.com/" target="_blank">
           Farm Girl Gabs
          </a>
          who lives and works on a 500-acre farm in New Jersey with her beautiful family. Melissas amazing recipes and diy decor caught my eye this fall inspiring me to do more
          <a href="http://www.alifeinbalance.net/fall-crafts/" title="Fall Crafts and Fall Decor for the Home">
           seasonal decorating
          </a>
          . I am so excited to share her tips for creating simple fall centerpieces, especially with
          <a href="http://www.alifeinbalance.net/thanksgiving-recipes-crafts-decorations/" title="Thanksgiving: Recipes, Crafts Decorations">
           Thanksgiving
          </a>
          coming so soon!
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          When it comes to creating seasonal centerpieces, I love to scan the popular home dcor magazines and catalogs for ideas and inspiration.
         </strong>
         Once I see an idea I like, I use my creative resources to recreate the look for a lot less money. With Thanksgiving just around the corner, I thought I would put together some simple fall centerpieces that you can recreate at home. They would look beautiful not only on your Thanksgiving table but throughout the entire fall season.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.alifeinbalance.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Simple-Fall-Centerpieces-Pinterest.jpg">
          <img alt="Simple and easy fall centerpieces to make. Perfect for your Thanksgiving table or to enjoy the entire fall season." class="aligncenter wp-image-29504" height="739" src="http://www.alifeinbalance.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/Simple-Fall-Centerpieces-Pinterest.jpg" title="Simple Fall Centerpiece Ideas" width="522"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div class="insert-post-ads" style="clear:both;float:left;width:100%;margin:0 0 20px 0;">
         <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
         </script>
         <!-- Text only Ad with links within post -->
         <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-0109358242030278" data-ad-format="auto" data-ad-slot="2492221166" style="display:block">
         </ins>
         <script>
          (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
         </script>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          When creating a centerpiece for any season, I like to repurpose old seasonal dcor.
         </strong>
         They are of no use if they are tucked away in a box stored in the basement. I love to be thrifty. Beautiful doesnt mean expensive. Hit up the end of season clearance sales at the local craft stores and think ahead for the following year. Fall garlands, wreaths, picks and dcor are much cheaper if purchased on sale.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Luckily for me, I live on a 500-acre fruit and vegetable farm.
         </strong>
         We grow a lot of pumpkins, gourds and fall items for both our retail store and wholesale operation, so I have access to everything fall. You can too! Shop at your local farmers market. You can usually buy these seasonal items at a really good price. I love adding these natural accents to my fall centerpieces. Why not bring the outdoors in? Gourds, mini-pumpkins and Indian corn are all wonderful to decorate with.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Here are some simple fall centerpieces for you to try. Celebrate the colors of fall. The red, yellow, orange and green hues are so beautiful together. Have fun and let those creative juices flow!
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.alifeinbalance.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/wreath-on-cake-stand.jpg">
          <img alt="Simple Fall Centerpiece Ideas" class="aligncenter wp-image-29495 size-full" height="450" src="http://www.alifeinbalance.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/wreath-on-cake-stand.jpg" title="Simple Fall Centerpiece Ideas" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Make use of that
         <strong>
          cake stand
         </strong>
         that is stored in your cabinet. For this centerpiece, I used an old fall wreath andadded a grapevine pumpkin to the center. I filled in the bottom with some small pumpkins and fall garland.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.alifeinbalance.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/CakeStand-with-Candles.jpg">
          <img alt="Simple Fall Centerpiece Ideas" class="alignnone wp-image-29497 size-full" height="450" src="http://www.alifeinbalance.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/CakeStand-with-Candles.jpg" title="Simple Fall Centerpiece Ideas" width="680"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         To create a more clean and simplistic look, line the perimeter of the
         <strong>
          cake stand
         </strong>
         with munchkins and placesome candles of various sizes in the center. You can add some garland around the base or just leave plain.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">

         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Dont pack away those
         <strong>
          outdoor lanterns
         </strong>
         just yet. Use it to create a beautiful fall tablescape.Fill the lantern with a candle and then add gourds and garland sprays to each side.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://www.alifeinbalance.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/long-tray-with-pumpkins.jpg">

         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Take a
         <strong>
          wooden tray
         </strong>
         and fill it with sugar pumpkins, gourds, candles and some berry garland.Choose a variety of different shapes and colors so there is a beautiful contrast.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          As you can see, creating your own fall centerpiece is reallysimple! It is definitely not as hard as it seems. Using your creative resources, along with some repurposed decorations and seasonal items, you can create your own fall centerpiece in no time. Hope these ideas spark yourimagination and creativity. I would love to see what you create.
