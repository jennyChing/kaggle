        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="posted-on">

        <blockquote>
         <p>
          Did I offer peace today? Did I bring a smile to someones face? Did I say words of healing? Did I let go of my anger and resentment? Did I forgive? Did I love? These are the real questions. I must trust that the little bit of love that I sow now will bear many fruits, here in this world and the life to come.  Henri Nouwen
         </p>
        </blockquote>
