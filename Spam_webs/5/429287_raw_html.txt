        <section class="recipe-ingredients">
         <h3 class="recipe-section-header">
          Ingredients
         </h3>
         <div class="recipe-ingredients-group">
          <ul class="recipe-list recipe-ingredients-list">
           <li class="recipe-ingredients-item" itemprop="ingredients">
            0.25 c. extra-virgin olive oil
           </li>
           <li class="recipe-ingredients-item" itemprop="ingredients">
            0.50  onion
           </li>
           <li class="recipe-ingredients-item" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 clove garlic
           </li>
           <li class="recipe-ingredients-item" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 sprig Dill
           </li>
           <li class="recipe-ingredients-item" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
           </li>
           <li class="recipe-ingredients-item" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tsp. finely grated lemon zest
           </li>
           <li class="recipe-ingredients-item" itemprop="ingredients">
            0.50 tsp. dried oregano
           </li>
           <li class="recipe-ingredients-item" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 lb. boneless leg of lamb
           </li>
           <li class="recipe-ingredients-item" itemprop="ingredients">
            salt
           </li>
           <li class="recipe-ingredients-item" itemprop="ingredients">
            Freshly ground pepper
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
        </section>
        <section class="recipe-directions">
         <h3 class="recipe-section-header">
          Directions
         </h3>
         <ol class="recipe-list recipe-directions-list" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
          <li class="recipe-directions-item">
           In a blender, puree the oil, onion, garlic, dill, lemon juice, zest, and oregano. In a baking dish, pour the marinade over the lamb and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours. Remove from the refrigerator 1 hour before cooking. Light a grill. Scrape off the marinade and season the lamb with salt and pepper. Grill over moderate heat for 28 minutes, turning once, until medium-rare. Transfer to a surface and let rest for 10 minutes. Slice the lamb against the grain and serve.
          </li>
         </ol>
