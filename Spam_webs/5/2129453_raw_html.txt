          <h2 class="post-title">
           How Derren Brown Used A Fake Game Show to Ultimately Prove Humanity is Broken
          </h2>
          <div class="post-calendar-date">
           <div class="calendar-date">
            <span>
             Feb

          <p>
           <a href="http://i1.wp.com/remycarreiro.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/derreb-bro.jpg">
            <img alt="derreb bro" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8500" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/remycarreiro.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/derreb-bro.jpg?resize=520%2C346"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           If you are not familiar with the work of
           <a href="http://derrenbrown.co.uk/">
            Derren Brown,
           </a>
           you have done your brain and yourself a great injustice. Derren Brown is, via his
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Derren_Brown">
            Wikipedia page
           </a>
           , an illusionist, mentalist, trickster, hypnotist, painter, writer, and skeptic. Whereas American magicians (using term in quotes because that is not what Brown is, but what he can best be compared to) use editing tools and simple street magic to impress us, the London-born Brown clearly states time and again that what he does is NOT magic. He uses misdirection and basic psychology along with a slew of other perfect storm elements to create his illusions, though they are hardly illusions. He is a man who understands the mind and the
           <a href="http://remycarreiro.com/the-art-of-gaslighting-or-how-i-learned-to-love-driving-someone-insane/">
            art of mentalism
           </a>
           , and doesnt need a cheesy Las Vegas show to validate any of this. Though he has an impressive array of specials (
           <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oC9J6O6soHA">
            like this one
           </a>
           ), I want to take a moment to talk about one particular special he did where he essentially held a mirror up to mankind to show us what weve become. It was an episode of his amazing
           <em>
            The Experiments
           </em>
           show, and in this case, he used a fake game show as his backdrop to lure in some people. What happens next may show you a side of yourself you are not quite ready to admit exists, yet clearly does.
           <span id="more-8499">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/remycarreiro.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/brown.jpeg">
            <img alt="brown" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8505" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/remycarreiro.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/brown.jpeg?resize=520%2C293"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <em>
            Think what you want, but dude is David Bowie levels of cool.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           First off, let me address the doubters and haters. No, magic isnt real. That is 90% of what Derren Brown goes to say and support in many of his specials, live shows, and gaffs. There is a reason behind everything, but Brown just knows the human mind and knows how to work it and manipulate it. Truth is, I have spent years studying all his books and shows and specials, to the point where I can get people to give me their ATM numbers without even being aware. I do not abuse it, have never robbed anyone, but it is REALLY nice having this sort of mental advantage over most you encounter. You would be surprised the amount of things you can learn to do just from simple misdirection and social cues, but I digress.
          </p>
          <p>
           So regarding the hater, people have tried time and time again to prove him to be bullshit, and guess what?
           <a href="http://www.reddit.com/r/IAmA/comments/1tho9x/iama_guy_who_participated_and_was_hypnotised_in/">
            This happens.
           </a>
           And
           <a href="http://www.reddit.com/r/skeptic/comments/1r884y/can_we_have_a_discussion_about_derren_brown/">
            this happens
           </a>
           . He has no stooges, nothing is faked. In some cases, he has done some trickery on the public (lottery bit), but for the most part, he knows the mind and knows how to manipulate it. 
           <em>
            Remote Control
           </em>
            was a perfect example of this. In this case, all he needed to do was give an audience masks and some power, and the monster that lives in all of us reveals itself instantly.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i1.wp.com/remycarreiro.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/remote.jpg">
            <img alt="remote" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8501" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/remycarreiro.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/remote.jpg?resize=520%2C346"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <em>
            Its scary what we will do and say when we think we are part of a group and are anonymous.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           This brings us up to his
           <em>
            Remote Control
           </em>
           episode of his
           <em>
            Experiments
           </em>
           show. Do you see the masked audience above? I have already talked about the show
           <em>
            <a href="http://remycarreiro.com/black-mirror-best-tv-show-not-enough-watching/">
             Black Mirror,
            </a>
           </em>
           but this was very much like an episode of that show, but in REAL LIFE. Derren essentially brings in an audience, masks them to make them anonymous and prove mob mentality is a real thing, and then hands them the power of God over one man. The man is being filmed and doesnt know it, and the audience is tasked with what should or shouldnt happen to him throughout the night. While I wont ruin for you, I want you to watch it, aware that under those masks is us. Each and every one of us. Suffice it to say, you have any doubts about the legitimacy, wait to see the audience and their reaction in the final moments of this staggering social experiment. Tears, gasps, and deafening silence that speaks volumes. Was it a tad bit manipulative on Browns part? Yes, but the rest was up to us, and youll see how we choose. Mob mentality is a very real and VERY scary thing, and it lives inside each of us.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
            <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="323" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/scOJqyiYVtk?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="520">
            </iframe>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            I also wish to express no disrespect to Derren Brown or 4OD for me sharing this (without permission). I believe its need to be seen by all supersedes any stupid laws or rules that may surround my right to show it. If at any point, you wish it taken down,
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/RemyCarreiroIsBetterThanBacon?ref=bookmarks">
             contact my people
            </a>
            and it will be taken down. I am a huge fan of Brown and am only hoping to help spread awareness of the man whilst also making us aware of our social condition as well.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            But back to the matter at hand.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           We have collectively become an awful species, and though revealing that to us that may not have been Derren Browns goal with this special (his goal being to show that anonymity and mob rules affect social behavior), I do think he proved we are a bit broken. He proved if we were given a face mask and absolute control, we would become monsters (at least a large majority of us would). Sorry, but thats not okay of us. All traces of humanity are slowly being stripped away from as humans, and we are doing it. My goal is sharing this is that maybe, in seeing this we can be more aware of that, and keep ourselves from fully becoming it.
          </p>
          <p>
           Or maybe we already have and its too late.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/remycarreiro.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/rem-of-the-opera.jpg">
            <img alt="rem of the opera" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8504" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/remycarreiro.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/rem-of-the-opera.jpg?resize=404%2C539"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <em>
            I have my mask I can hide behind. Whats yours?
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            If you enjoy using your brain and actually have a soul, I think you should
            <a href="https://www.facebook.com/RemyCarreiroIsBetterThanBacon?ref=bookmarks">
             come over here and become a REMlin.
            </a>
            We are a tight-knit group who need more like us. And we promise, we are mask-free (for the most part).
           </strong>
          </p>
