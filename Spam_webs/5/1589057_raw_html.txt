          <div class="post-inner group">
           <h1 class="post-title">
            DayZ tips, hints and tricks vital for your survival
           </h1>
           <p class="post-byline">
            by
            <a href="http://thenoob.net/post/author/voidproxy/" rel="author" title="Posts by VOiDProXy">
             VOiDProXy
            </a>
             February 11, 2014
           </p>
           <div class="clear">
           </div>
           <div class="entry">
            <div class="entry-inner">
             <div class="getsocial gs-inline-group">
             </div>
             <br/>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              <a href="http://thenoob.net/gaming/game-guides/46-vital-hints-tips-tricks-playing-dayz/attachment/dayz1/">
               <img alt="DayZ Screenshot Pond Nature" class="aligncenter wp-image-2692 size-medium" height="450" src="//thenoob.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/dayz1-800x450.jpg" width="800"/>
              </a>
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify;">
              I hope to play DayZ as soon as I am able to get my hands on a computer that will run it, Ive written a list (because everyone loves lists) of
              <del>
               33
              </del>
              46 useful DayZ tips Ive found across the web and from watching people like Frankie play DayZ on YouTube:
             </p>
             <ol>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               If you cant find any servers, you might have to click on the tab that says Internet, as the game defaults to LAN sometimes.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               If your game is lagging, or you have poor framerate:
               <ol>
                <li style="text-align: justify;">
                 Go to your documents.
                </li>
                <li style="text-align: justify;">
                 Locate the DayZ folder.
                </li>
                <li style="text-align: justify;">
                 Open DayZ.cfg in Notepad.
                </li>
                <li style="text-align: justify;">
                 Change GPU_MaxFramesAhead to 1
                </li>
                <li style="text-align: justify;">
                 Change GPU_DetectedFramesAhead to = 1
                </li>
               </ol>
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               If you still have bad frame rate, try turning off Ambient Occlusion, this can dramatically boosts frame rate and personally I find it annoying.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Avoid the coastline unless youre looking for trouble.
               </strong>
               Head in-land directly after spawning into DayZ.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               Its important to
               <strong>
                keep hydrated
               </strong>
               , if you
               <em>
                or
               </em>
               your DayZ character get a headache, drink water!
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               You spawn with a torch (depending on where in the world you are, this is also pronounced flashlight) and a 9-volt battery, to get it working just follow these steps:
               <ol>
                <li style="text-align: justify;">
                 Open up your inventory with the Tab key, its located above your caps lock key to the left.
                </li>
                <li style="text-align: justify;">
                 Drag the battery onto the torch/flashlight.
                </li>
                <li style="text-align: justify;">
                 Drag the torch/flashlight onto your hand to put it in your hand.
                </li>
                <li style="text-align: justify;">
                 If you press space, you point the torch ahead of you at the cost of movement speed.
                </li>
               </ol>
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               To
               <strong>
                fix a broken leg
               </strong>
               , use a splint or a Morphine Injector.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               Just because two humans have blood, doesnt mean it works in another human. Injecting the
               <strong>
                incorrect blood type into your own blood will kill you
               </strong>
               , this applies to real life as well.
               <strong>
                Scroll down the page for a table on which blood type goes with which!
               </strong>
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Hold alt (on your keyboardby default)to free-look
               </strong>
               , or double-tap alt to permanently free-look.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               Double tap Caps Lock (on your keyboardby default)to permanently talk over mic.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Go Prone is faster
               </strong>
               to get down to the floor than just Prone, check your keybind lists and switch out the Z-key!
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               If youre
               <strong>
                stuck inside a wall
               </strong>
               , you can go prone to get out.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               Use the number keys to
               <strong>
                quickly cycle through your weapons
               </strong>
               .
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               You can
               <strong>
                mute players
               </strong>
               by pressing P(on your keyboardby default) and then selecting the player in the list and pressing the Mute / Unmute button.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               The lower your Field of View (FOV) is in settings, the further you can zoom in with or without a scope.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Avoid running across open fields if possible
               </strong>
               , as it will get yourself exposed and 90% probably get you killed. Stick to the treeline if you can!
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               An old quote from Operation Flashpoint (The first Bohemia Interactive game I played) was Get off the road if you want to grow old (Pink Floyd)
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               You can
               <strong>
                change your characters name
               </strong>
               in the title screen by clicking on it.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <a href="http://thenoob.net/wp-content/uploads/dayz-ont-he-horizon-wallpaper.jpg" rel="lightbox-0">
                <img alt="DayZ Horizon Man" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-2181" height="168" src="//thenoob.net/wp-content/uploads/dayz-ont-he-horizon-wallpaper-300x168.jpg" width="300"/>
               </a>
               You can carry two of any two-handed weapons one on your back while the other is in your hands, but you will drop whichever weapon is in your hands to use your hands for anything or picking something up.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Lower/holster your primary weapon
               </strong>
               by tapping ctrl twice, sort of at ease, for that relaxed survivor look.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               If you are running and change weapon while its raised, you will stop running. If you change your weapon while it is lowered, you continue keep running.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               If you vault (press V on your keyboardby default) with your fists up, youll slow down to step over something, but if you vault with your fists down youll actually make a running jump. This is very useful to outrun brain-hungry Zombies.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               Double click items that youre looting to quickly loot them, if you have the inventory space.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               You can hide bodies to make them disappear and becomeunlootable by other players.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               To switch firing from full automatic to semi-automatic fire press T(on your keyboardby default), this also affects whether you are shooting with one or two barrels with the shotgun.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               When your weapon has a bipod its extra accurate when you lay prone, but less accurate when you stand up or crouch.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               The
               <strong>
                less colorful or saturated your screen is, the lower your blood level is
               </strong>
               .
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               The more blurry your screen is, the lower your health is.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               You run slower
               <strong>
                when youre thirsty
               </strong>
               .
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               The weather affects you, if youre freezing you will start to shake uncontrollably and you might get sick.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               Double tap shift to get your character permanently running or walking.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               If you are
               <strong>
                bleeding but have no bandages
               </strong>
               , remove your shirt and right click it to tear off a rag  this can be used to stop the bleeding. Note: This does
               <em>
                not
               </em>
               work if your clothes are damp.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                You dont have to drop your shirt
               </strong>
               to the ground to rip it into bandages.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Keep a spare pair of shoes
               </strong>
               in case they get wet or damaged, not being able to run hinders your chance of survival.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               An EpiPen or a defibrillator (The defibrillator requires a 9-volt battery to work, and can take several attempts to successfully revive a player) can be used to revive an incapacitated player, if they are incapacitated due to blood loss, first bandage them (or yourself if youre the one bleeding) and fix them using a blood bag or saline bag (use an IV Start Kit to create a Saline Drip).
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                If you get sick
               </strong>
               , eatantibiotics and be idle until youre cured.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               Eating rice or cereal makes you thirsty quicker (These cereals are making me thirsty!)
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               As ammunition sometimes is sparse, if
               <strong>
                somebody is holding you at gunpoint
               </strong>
               , you can usually see whether they have a mag in it or not.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               Some
               <strong>
                bushes have berries for nutrition
               </strong>
               , some
               <strong>
                bushes have berries that are poisonous
               </strong>
               , if you feel lucky, try them by pressing F (on your keyboardby default) and find out for yourself!
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               The more youre active running or working, the hungrier and thirstier you get.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               The
               <strong>
                clouds always move in one general direction
               </strong>
               , just almost to the east (thats to the right of the map for those who dont know their North, East, South and West), this makes navigating easier if you dont have a compass available.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               The sun rises in the South East, and the sun sets in the South West. Pointing your compass towards the sun will get you an idea of what time it is.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               You dont have to equip the compass to use it, you can just right-click and inspect it.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                If you log in and have no gear
               </strong>
               , you can fix it by logging out, then logging in to the previous server you were on, log back out, and then log back into the new server.
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               <strong>
                Logging out while unconscious or handcuffed will kill you.
               </strong>
              </li>
              <li style="text-align: justify;">
               Being friendly to other players is the easiest way to stay alive, dont kill a player on sight if youre
               <em>
                unsure
               </em>
               whether they are going to be friendly or not but do not let your guard down, just in case!
              </li>
             </ol>
