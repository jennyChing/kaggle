          <h1 class="post-title">
           Sultans of Style: Robert Brunner
          </h1>
          <div class="post-preamble">
          </div>
          <div class="post-content">
           <p>
            <em>
             In Styletures Sultans of Style series, we profile game-changing artists who have pioneered their way into the history books of design.
            </em>
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_3850" style="width: 164px">
            <a href="http://www.styleture.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/Robert-Brunner1.jpg" rel="lightbox[3845]">
             <img alt="Robert-Brunner" class="size-full wp-image-3850" height="154" src="http://www.styleture.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/Robert-Brunner1.jpg" title="Robert-Brunner" width="154"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Robert Brunner
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.styleture.com/2011/02/17/robert-brunner/" target="_blank" title="Robert Brunner">
             Robert Brunner
            </a>
            , born in 1948 in the U.S., is an accomplished industrial designer who has produced groundbreaking works for high-profile clients. When hes not hanging out with Lady Gaga or Dr. Dre, Brunner is off in his own world creating leading-edge products; most notably, he provided design and direction for Apple Computer in the early 1990s, coming up with the look for the PowerBook. Today, Brunner leads the design team at
            <a href="http://www.ammunitiongroup.com/" target="_blank">
             Ammunition LLC
            </a>
            , responsible for a plethora of notable designer products: Beats by Dr. Dre is a new line of high-performance headphones developed in partnership with hip-hop artist and producer Dr. Dre; the Element Grill by Fuego is an affordable, gas-powered grill that extends the design language of the Fuego product line into a more mainstream product family; and the Arc Collection by Zephyr is a line of high-performance, design-forward
            <a href="http://www.zephyronline.com/" target="_blank" title="range hoods">
             range hoods
            </a>
            that deliver compact power and sophistication to the kitchen.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3846" style="width: 535px">
            <a href="http://www.styleture.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/apple-powerbook1.jpg" rel="lightbox[3845]">
             <img alt="apple-powerbook" class="size-full wp-image-3846" height="394" src="http://www.styleture.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/apple-powerbook1.jpg" title="apple-powerbook" width="525"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Apple PowerBook
            </p>
           </div>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3847" style="width: 403px">
            <a href="http://www.styleture.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/Beats-By-Dre1.jpg" rel="lightbox[3845]">
             <img alt="Beats-By-Dre" class="size-full wp-image-3847" height="450" src="http://www.styleture.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/Beats-By-Dre1.jpg" title="Beats-By-Dre" width="393"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Beats by Dr. Dre headphones
            </p>
           </div>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3849" style="width: 540px">
            <a href="http://www.styleture.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/rober-brunner-range-hood1.jpg" rel="lightbox[3845]">
             <img alt="rober-brunner-range-hood" class="size-full wp-image-3849" height="410" src="http://www.styleture.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/rober-brunner-range-hood1.jpg" title="rober-brunner-range-hood" width="530"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Zephyr Arc Collection range hood
            </p>
           </div>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_3848" style="width: 535px">
            <a href="http://www.styleture.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/fuego-element-grill1.jpg" rel="lightbox[3845]">
             <img alt="fuego-element-grill" class="size-full wp-image-3848" height="525" src="http://www.styleture.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/fuego-element-grill1.jpg" title="fuego-element-grill" width="525"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Element Grill by Fuego
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            Such products are proof that Brunner is not only a gifted designer, but an icon of modern style. His eye-catching designs are welcome on the pages of Styleture any time.
           </p>
