       <h1 class="title">
        Ben Ry, a wonderful Passito di Pantelleria from Donnafugata.
       </h1>
       <div class="post-meta">
        <span class="small">
         By
        </span>
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://thewinehub.com/home/author/luiz/" rel="author" title="Posts by Luiz">
           Luiz
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="small">
         on
        </span>
        <abbr class="date time published" title="2013-12-18T19:14:29-0400">
         December 18, 2013
        </abbr>
        <span class="small">
         in
        </span>
        <span class="categories">
         <a href="http://thewinehub.com/home/category/westsicilywine/">
          #westsicilywine
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://thewinehub.com/home/category/winelover/">
          #winelover
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="380379" data-link="http://thewinehub.com/home/2013/12/18/ben-rye-a-wonderful-passito-di-pantelleria-from-donnafugata/" data-summary="" data-title="Ben Ry, a wonderful Passito di Pantelleria from Donnafugata. ">
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://thewinehub.com/home/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/news_ingrandimento_1_9653274801.jpg">
          <img alt="news_ingrandimento_1_9653274801" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8149" height="211" src="http://thewinehub.com/home/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/news_ingrandimento_1_9653274801.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <em>
          Ben
         </em>
         Ry, Passito di Pantelleria.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Tonight (12/18) we will be talking about the
         <em>
          Ben
         </em>
         Ry 2011 during our twitter tasting at 8pm EST. Learn a thing or two about this great wine before you join us!
        </p>
        <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="155.96464464810845" data-font-name="g_font_9_0" dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          Ben Ry 2011
         </strong>
        </div>
        <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="653.6000194787978" data-font-name="g_font_11_0" dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
         2011 was a warm and sunny year. This Ben Ry is characterized by an ample and round bouquet.
        </div>
        <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="653.6000194787978" data-font-name="g_font_11_0" dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
         On the palate caressing sweetness is well balanced by crisp acidity.
        </div>
        <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="653.6000194787978" data-font-name="g_font_11_0" dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
        </div>
        <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="653.6000194787978" data-font-name="g_font_11_0" dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="157.23904468608856" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          Type: natural sweet white  Passito di Pantelleria DOP.
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="52.4915215643692" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          Grapes:
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muscat_of_Alexandria" target="_blank">
           Zibibbo (Muscat of Alexandria)
          </a>
          .
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          Production area: Island of Pantelleria (an island of
          <strong>
           volcanic origin
          </strong>
          , lying between Sicily and Africa)
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          ___
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          <a href="http://thewinehub.com/home/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/WLU_9430.jpg">
           <img alt="WLU_9430" class="aligncenter wp-image-8164" height="411" src="http://thewinehub.com/home/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/WLU_9430.jpg" width="621"/>
          </a>
          The grapes that are left to dry naturally and Antonio Rallo is showing us how delicious they are!
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          ____________________________________________________________________________
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          Q&amp;A
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          <strong>
           How much alcohol?
          </strong>
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          14.24% ABV.
         </div>
         <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="479.0182542758559" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          </div>
          <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="479.0182542758559" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          </div>
          <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="479.0182542758559" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            When I was the first vintage of this wine?
           </strong>
          </div>
          <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="479.0182542758559" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
           1989
          </div>
          <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="479.0182542758559" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          </div>
          <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="479.0182542758559" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          </div>
          <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="479.0182542758559" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
           <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="34.0473610146904" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
            <strong>
             What is the aging potential?
            </strong>
           </div>
           <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="34.0473610146904" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
            It will age well for over 15 years.
           </div>
           <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="34.0473610146904" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
           </div>
           <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="34.0473610146904" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
           </div>
           <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="34.0473610146904" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
            <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="334.27648996221535" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
             <strong>
              When should I drink it?
             </strong>
            </div>
            <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="585.73825745636" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
             <em>
              Ben
             </em>
             Ry is an extraordinary meditation wine. Its the ideal companion while reading or listening to music.
            </div>
            <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="479.0182542758559" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
             And it is as sweet ending of a dinner with friends as well as a romantic date.
            </div>
            <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="479.0182542758559" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
             With or without cheeses or desserts!
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
          <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="479.0182542758559" data-font-name="g_font_5_0" dir="ltr">
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="653.6000194787978" data-font-name="g_font_11_0" dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
        </div>
        <div data-angle="0" data-canvas-width="653.6000194787978" data-font-name="g_font_11_0" dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         Cheers,
        </div>
