        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Valentine Gifts for Gardeners
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-01-29T02:35:42+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 29, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://gardenchick.com/author/gardenchick/" itemprop="url" rel="author">

            Karen Creel

          </a>

        </p>
       </header>

        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Chocolate and flowers are the traditional gifts for Valentines day, and most anyone would love to get either or both of these. But, if you want to do something a little different for the gardener in your life, stretch your imagination, and think of some gifts that can be used and enjoyed way past February 14th.
        </p>
        <p>
         I love the names of flowers, so I started looking at flowers that were associated with Valentines or love. I found a flower called Cupids Dart and ordered them from Amazon.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/pic-monkey-cupids-dart.jpg">
          <img alt="pic monkey cupids dart" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1205" height="900" src="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/pic-monkey-cupids-dart.jpg" width="718"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Cupids Dart is a perennial and self seeds easily. It opens during the day and closes at night, requires full sun, and can be grown in zones 4 through 8.
        </p>
        <p>
         The other seed I chose to place in the basket was Kiss Me Over the Garden Gate
         <a href='0px !important;" /&gt;' target="_blank">
          .
         </a>
         Dont you just love the name of this flower? I knew I had to include it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Kiss-Me-Over-The-Garden-Gate-flower-web-fl360.jpg">
          <img alt="Kiss-Me-Over-The-Garden-Gate-flower-web-fl360" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1210" height="450" src="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Kiss-Me-Over-The-Garden-Gate-flower-web-fl360.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Kiss me over the garden gate is an old fashionedannual that you dont see very often.It self seeds easilyin the garden.Growing between 7 and 9 foot tall with drooping pink flowers, it makes quite a dramatic picture inthe garden.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Seed-Starting-Basket.jpg">
          <img alt="Seed Starting Basket" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1221" height="600" src="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Seed-Starting-Basket.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/seed-basket-close-up.jpg">
          <img alt="seed basket close up" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1222" height="768" src="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/seed-basket-close-up-1024x768.jpg" width="1024"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Birds and gardens go hand in hand, so creating a bird lover basket would be a valentine gift anyone would love to receive. You can add bird seed, suet, bird magazine (such as bird and blooms) birdfeeders, and bird houses.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/blue-birds-and-seeds.jpg">
          <img alt="blue birds and seeds" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1213" height="600" src="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/blue-birds-and-seeds.jpg" width="470"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Valentine-Bird-Basket.jpg">
          <img alt="Valentine Bird Basket" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1219" height="600" src="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Valentine-Bird-Basket.jpg" width="800"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Looking for more ideas?
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/bee-valentine.jpg">
          <img alt="bee valentine" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1214" height="345" src="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/bee-valentine.jpg" width="320"/>
         </a>
         Bee basket with honey, honey jar, and honey dippers.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/tea-valentine.png">
          <img alt="tea valentine" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1215" height="469" src="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/tea-valentine.png" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         A tea basket with herbal teas, honey, a vintage teacup and saucer, and a magazine.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/popcorn-valentine.jpg">
          <img alt="popcorn valentine" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1216" height="480" src="http://gardenchick.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/popcorn-valentine.jpg" width="365"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         And lastly, for your popcorn loving friends, a popcorn basket filled with gourmet popcorn, different popcorn salts, and brightly colored popcorn bowls.
        </p>
        <p>
         Visit
         <a href="http://www.%20vintage holiday crafts.com/free-vintage-kids-valentine-cards" target="_blank">
          www.vintage holiday crafts.com/free-vintage-kids-valentine-cards
         </a>
         for loads of ideas to create your one of a kind valentine gift.
        </p>
        <p>
         Happy Valentines Day
        </p>
        <p>
         Karen
