Graffiti has existed since the Classical era of the Greek and Roman Empires, but in modern times, is largely created using spray paint to create unique and recognizable artworks.  By tagging various locations with their graffiti, artists can gain consider recognition for their graffiti prowess.
         </p>
         <p>
          For designers, graffiti fonts can provide a unique and edgy feel to many forms of design, so in this post, Peter Olexa has rounded up 20 awesome urban and graffiti fonts for designers.  Best of all, theyre free.
