       <h1>
        <a href="http://guitarnoize.com/esp-superman-man-steel-guitar/" rel="bookmark" title="ESP Superman Man Of Steel Guitar">
         ESP Superman Man Of Steel Guitar
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="post-meta">
        <p class="posted_date">
         December 10, 2013 at 9:00 am | Posted by
         <strong>
          Jon
         </strong>
         in
         <a href="http://guitarnoize.com/category/crazy-guitar-designs/" rel="category tag">
          Crazy Guitar Designs
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Tags:
         <a href="http://guitarnoize.com/tag/esp/" rel="tag">
          ESP
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
       <ul class="bxslider">
        <li>
         <img alt="Superman man of steel guitar " src="http://guitarnoize.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/man-of-steel-1.jpg" title="Superman man of steel guitar "/>
        </li>
        <li>
         <img alt="Superman man of steel guitar front and back" src="http://guitarnoize.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/man-of-steel-2.jpg" title="Superman man of steel guitar front and back"/>
        </li>
        <li>
         <img alt="Superman man of steel guitar headstock" src="http://guitarnoize.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/man-of-steel-3.jpg" title="Superman man of steel guitar headstock"/>
        </li>
       </ul>
       <div class="post-content">
        <p>
         Now here is a
         <a href="http://guitarnoize.com/category/crazy-guitar-designs/">
          Crazy Guitar Design
         </a>
         for you, I think my son would love this one although it is a little out of his price range (he is 2). I first noticed this guitar on
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/bmusicaustralia">
          BMusic Australias Facebook page
         </a>
         , they are the main distributor of ESP Guitars here in Australia. I then did a little bit of research and found an article on
         <a href="http://geekmodeonline.com/man-of-steel-guitar/">
          Geek Mode
         </a>
         confirming that this guitar is to be built by
         <a href="http://www.espguitars.com/">
          ESP Guitars
         </a>
         . It appears in the
         <a href="https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=627810827275917&amp;set=a.575713062485694.1073741866.241673545889649&amp;type=1&amp;theater">
          photo that BMusic posted
         </a>
         that production will be limited to 100, I could be wrong however as I dont speak Japanese Im just guessing what a sticker says. There is also a figure of 241,500 which may be the price in Yen? Sounds about right as this would be around the USD $2300 mark.
        </p>
        <p>
         As you can see the pickguard on the guitar is shaped like the iconic Superman logo in a shiny metallic finish, not sure if it is actually metal or just plastic. The body is then shaped to look like Supermans cape draped below, clever. The guitar has a sparkle finish too, even on the headstock which just has the words Man Of Steel and the Superman logo. A single humbucker ensures that the logo is not covered up and Im guessing Superman would definitely be happy with this Metal setup. The guitar also has a hard tail bridge, maybe thats because Superman would pick so hard it would put the strings out of tune if he had a trem?
        </p>
        <p>
         So what do you think? Super-cool or Super-lame?
        </p>
