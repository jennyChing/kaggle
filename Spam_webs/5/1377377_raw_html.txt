        <p>
         <a href="http://www.cheesesteakshop.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.cheesesteakshop.com/', '24 Bay Area locations');" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          24 Bay Area locations
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.cheesesteakshop.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.cheesesteakshop.com/', '');" rel="nofollow">
          <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-9547" height="125" src="http://www.cookiesandclogs.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/phillycheesesteak0.jpg" title="phillycheesesteak0" width="125"/>
         </a>
         Good for: Everyone
         <br/>
         Price Range: Reasonable
         <br/>
         Features: Tasty basics
        </p>
        <p>
         For over 15 years I have eaten Philly Cheese Steaks from the
         <a href="http://www.cheesesteakshop.com/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.cheesesteakshop.com/', 'Cheese Steak Shop');" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Cheese Steak Shop
         </a>
         (used to go to the Oakland, CA location). Now franchised stores are popping up all around the Bay Area much to my delight.
        </p>
        <p>
         The cheese steak sandwiches from here are the best. Ive tried elsewhere but those have all been dry and tasteless. At the Cheese Steak Shop, they use very finely sliced meat, fresh Provolone cheese, sweet peppers,etc. The bread is the perfectconsistencyand compliments the other ingredients well. I usually order the mushroom one but I am slowly leaning toward the spinach &amp; mushroom sandwich.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9548" height="299" src="http://www.cookiesandclogs.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/philly2.jpg" title="philly2" width="400"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Do you see all that cheese and yummy juices? Even though the sandwiches do not taste greasy at all, I do have this mental image of my arteries clogging up as the food goes down. There are chicken and vegetarian menu items but I just cant imagine those tasting half as heavenly as the philly subs. If you really feel guilty, the curly fries would probably not be a good idea. For me, a trip to a Cheese Steak Shop is a nice treat 1-3 times a year.
        </p>
