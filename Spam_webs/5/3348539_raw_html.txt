                 <div class="date-posts">
                  <div class="post-outer">
                   <div class="post hentry" itemprop="blogPost" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
                    <meta content="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-U9lIIM__YqI/Ukjm2ky-EiI/AAAAAAAAC_M/bib35LQLRII/s640/leekandyamcover.jpg" itemprop="image_url"/>

                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Leek and Yam Chowder #recipe
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-3704605077791185150" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      A must try - Leek and Yam Chowder Recipe! Fast, easy and perfect for a cool fall evening!
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      This is another fall family favorite in our home and it is something I would have never thought I would crave.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      After going on Jude's field trip (more to come on that trip) we ended up with a ton of fresh produce on our counter tops. Yum! So I had to figure out what we were going to do with it all.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      First up...use our leeks and SOME of the potatoes that we harvested at a local farm by making this great chowder!
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-U9lIIM__YqI/Ukjm2ky-EiI/AAAAAAAAC_M/bib35LQLRII/s1600/leekandyamcover.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Leek and Yam Chowder Recipe" border="0" height="426" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-U9lIIM__YqI/Ukjm2ky-EiI/AAAAAAAAC_M/bib35LQLRII/s640/leekandyamcover.jpg" title="" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       <span style="font-size: x-large;">
                        Leek and Yam Chowder
                       </span>
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;">
                      <i>
                       Free
                       <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/recipesfrmbuildingourstory/leek-and-yam-chowder">
                        Printable Recipe
                       </a>
                       Link
                      </i>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       Ingredients:
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      1/4 stick of butter
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      2 cans of Italian Stewed Tomatoes
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      2 regular potatoes (Peeled and cubed)
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      1 large yam (peeled and cubed)
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      3 medium leeks (sliced and cleaned)
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      4 cups boiling water
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      2 vegetable bouillon cubes
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      small carton heavy cream
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;">
                      Sweet Basil and salt and pepper
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_hk5xoWaR2k/Ukjm3gz2ciI/AAAAAAAAC_g/HyyoEJMmLgg/s1600/leekandyam1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Leek and Yam Chowder Recipe" border="0" height="426" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_hk5xoWaR2k/Ukjm3gz2ciI/AAAAAAAAC_g/HyyoEJMmLgg/s640/leekandyam1.jpg" title="" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal">
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <b>
                       Preparation:
                      </b>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      1. Melt 1/4 stick of butter in a large
pot.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Ba99oHl-Els/Ukjm4bltYJI/AAAAAAAAC_c/lfcmM2_C4Wc/s1600/leekandyam2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Leek and Yam Chowder Recipe" border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Ba99oHl-Els/Ukjm4bltYJI/AAAAAAAAC_c/lfcmM2_C4Wc/s640/leekandyam2.jpg" title="" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      2. Add 3 medium leeks (sliced and
cleaned). Stir and cook until
tender.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      3. Add 2 cans of Italian Stewed
tomatoes, 1 large yam (peeled and cubed), 2 regular potatoes (peeled and
cubed), pepper to taste to the large pot.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-L6iNPXOp6UI/Ukjm5ixRhAI/AAAAAAAAC_s/QNmu8VTKLGI/s1600/leekandyam3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Leek and Yam Chowder Recipe" border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-L6iNPXOp6UI/Ukjm5ixRhAI/AAAAAAAAC_s/QNmu8VTKLGI/s640/leekandyam3.jpg" title="" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      4. Dissolve 2 vegetable bouillon cubes
in a 4 cups of very hot boiling water.
Add to large pot. Stir.
                     </div>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">

                       Add 1 small
