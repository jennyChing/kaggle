        <h1>
         Am I Gay: 1 Wonder of the World
        </h1>
        <div class="post-desc-full">
         <ul class="post-meta-special">

           <span class="ssbp-toggle-switch ssbp-toggle-close" data-ssbp-toggle-class="ssbp-container">

           <div class="ssbp-container" data-ssbp-lazy-direction="left" data-ssbp-lazy-effect="fade" data-ssbp-mobile-direction="down" data-ssbp-post-id="8687" data-ssbp-share-text="Please share this with your friends..." data-ssbp-short-url="" data-ssbp-title="Am I Gay: 1 Wonder of the World" data-ssbp-url="http://nobullying.com/am-i-gay/">
           </div>

          <p>
           <em>
            <strong>
             Am I gay? Thousands of people find themselves asking this question. Some people do not view homosexuality as a problem or threat, but there are those who do. Many people who are gay wish they werent, and they seek advice, guidance, and answers to the questions they have about the events that transpire in their life.
            </strong>
           </em>
          </p>
          <h3>
           Am I Gay?
          </h3>
          <p>
           If a friend approaches you and says Im gay, how would you react? Many homosexuals are afraid to tell their friends and family members theyre gay because of the reaction they may get. Some friends and family members accept a gay person for who they are without hesitation, and there are those who will not, so many homosexuals do not tell anyone they are gay to eliminate the chance of being and feeling rejected. People do not want to be because they do not want to be shunned or outcast.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://nobullying.com/gay-rights/" target="_blank">
            There are many different online tests that claim to tell people if they are gay or not
           </a>
           . The results of these tests do not provide beneficial information. The place to begin is with yourself. You need to know and get to understand yourself before you ask any in-depth questions. There is no right or wrong way to tell if you are gay or not, but there are many reasons you may question your sexual being or sexual orientation.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Questioning Your Sexual Orientation
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://nobullying.com/sexual-orientation-bullying/" target="_blank">
            Your sexual orientation is a strange mystery until you begin to notice certain things.
           </a>
           There are many factors that may cause you to question your sexual orientation.
          </p>
          <p>
           1. Having friends who are gay or
           <a href="http://nobullying.com/am-i-bisexual/" target="_blank">
            bisexual
           </a>
           may cause you to wonder if you are gay because your friends are, and you enjoy hanging around them.
          </p>
          <p>
           2. Maybe your best friend is gay, and they have always secretly had a crush on you, and they may express how they feel by romantic gestures or stating sexual attraction.
          </p>
          <p>
           3. Engaging in same-sex behavior may be the primary reason you question your sexual orientation. Something is as simple as kissing someone of the same sex can peak your curiosity whether you are under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
          </p>
          <p>
           4. Another reason you may question your sexual orientation is because you are not attracted to the opposite sex. Whatever the reason, the opposite sex does not amuse you or stimulate you in a way that it does for other people.
          </p>
          <p>
           5. Who do you find attractive? You may find people of the opposite sex physically attractive. Having these feelings do not make you gay, but it can make you wonder about your sexuality and your sexual preference.
          </p>
          <p>
           6. Experiencing a deep emotional connection with a person of the same-sex does not make you a homosexual, but
           <a href="http://nobullying.com/gay-marriage/" target="_blank">
            other feelings may develop which may cause you to question your sexual orientation
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <h3>
           What Does Gay Mean?
          </h3>
          <p>
           Gay in this sense refers to a homosexual.

Coming out is the process of confirming you are gay within yourself and telling your friends and family.
          </p>

You need to ask yourself a few questions to help you on a journey to finding out who you are. Are you physically attracted to the opposite sex? Do you find it easier to bond emotionally with people of the same sex? Have you thought about having a relationship with someone of the same sex? You may want to dig a little deeper within yourself and ask more in-depth questions. These questions are basic questions that you need to consider answering. The most important thing to remember when you are answering these questions is to answer honestly.

For years, and some people today believe one of two things: its the childs surroundings that cause them to be gay or their familys genes are to blame. Recent studies reveal that it may be neither of these theories. A lot of people are accepting homosexuals in todays society, but there are still a lot of people who frown and cringe at the thought of homosexuality. Researchers are still trying to pinpoint where homosexuality comes from and what causes a person to be homosexual. Many homosexuals keep their thoughts and feelings to themselves due to the way many people in society view homosexuals. For as much as homosexuals try to change, homosexuality is not something you can wash off or take a pill to get remove. Homosexuality is not a disease, and this is the most difficult part for many people to understand.

Although its different from others or some people call it out of the ordinary, you should not let the thoughts of others control you and your decisions. Once you are happy with your life and sexuality, there will not be too much anything can say or do to discourage you and try to talk you into being someone you are not.

Be proud of who you are and live your life as the next person would. If you are not proud of yourself and content with who you are, you cannot expect other people to do what you are not willing to do for yourself.
