      <h3>
       Knoxville Home Builders
      </h3>
      <p>
       What does it mean to build smart? Building smart means creating a home from the ground up that is more efficient, uses less energy, and meets your needs more efficiently. Most of all, it means choosing a Knoxville home builder with a reputation for building high quality and ENERGY STAR-qualified homes. Brandau Construction, LLC, does renovations, remodeling, and new construction to provide you with the home you are dreaming of.
      </p>
      <h5>
       Your Dream Home
      </h5>
      <p>
       Your home is so much more than a roof over your head. Mike Brandau of Brandau Construction, LLC, knows that your home is a place to eat, sleep, play, work, and stay comfortable. We can build your new home with the features you really need.
      </p>
      <h5>
       Energy Efficiency
      </h5>
      <p>
       Brandau Construction LLC in Knoxville, TN, know what it takes to build a home that is ENERGY STAR-qualified. Everything we do makes your home more energy efficient and cost effective. From your crawl space to your roofing, we provide superior quality. Your house will have better air quality, lower risk of mold and mildew, and reduced energy costs by up to 25% to 50%. Not only will you save money, but your indoor air will also be healthy and comfortable all year round.
      </p>
      <h5>
       Experience Matters
      </h5>
      <p>
       Brandau Construction, LLC, provides quality you can trust. Mike Brandau has over 30 years of experience construction and 15 years of custom home building . We are a recognized ENERGY STAR builder, registered with the Better Business Bureau, and we are fully licensed and insured. This Knoxville home builder can tackle any project, including repairs after storm damage, renovations, and ENERGY STAR improvements to your home. Contact us today for certified construction techniques and the highest standards of service and workmanship.
      </p>
