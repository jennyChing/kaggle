         <div id="description-text">
          Vintage Czech Glass Pin Brooch, Brass Setting, Signed, Small, 1920s:
          <br/>
          <br/>
          A lovely small rectangle of clear faceted glass mounted on brass.  The glass stone is prong set.  The catch is a simple "C" type.  Small and charming.  It would be lovely tucked into a bridal bouquet.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Measurements: 1 1/10" by 3/4".
          <br/>
          <br/>
          A gentle reminder; with all things vintage there is normally some wear attributed to being handled or even stored over the course of decades. Sometimes not!
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Please visit my shop
          <a href="http://www.etsy.com/GracesVintageGarden" target="_blank">
           www.etsy.com/GracesVintageGarden
          </a>
          to see more lovely vintage jewelry. I add to my inventory all the time, so please check back. Feel free to contact me with any questions regarding a piece you may have an interest in.
