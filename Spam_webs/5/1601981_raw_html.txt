        <strong>
         <em>
          The US Air Force just test-fired a Minuteman III intercontinental ballistic missile from California, providing a visual to the world.
         </em>
        </strong>
       </div>
       <div style="background-color: white;lucida grande', verdana, sans-serif; line-height: 17.3333320617676px; margin-bottom: 0.75em; margin-top: 0.25em;">
        <iframe frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/EZFnQEwxkeM" width="560">

         <em>
          <br/>
         </em>
        </strong>
       </div>
       <div style="background-color: white;lucida grande', verdana, sans-serif; line-height: 17.3333320617676px; margin-bottom: 0.75em; margin-top: 0.25em;">
        <a href="/r2/?url=http://rt.com/usa/243353-usaf-nuclear-icbm-test/" rel="nofollow" style=" line-height: 17.3333320617676px; text-decoration: none;" target="_blank">
         As RT reports,
        </a>
        <span style=" line-height: 17.3333320617676px;">
        </span>
        <span style=" line-height: 17.3333320617676px;">
         an
        </span>
        <span style=" line-height: 17.3333320617676px;">
        </span>
        <strong style=" line-height: 17.3333320617676px;">
         intercontinental ballistic missile was fired early Monday from a facility in California, the US Air Force announced, saying the tests were a message to the world about Washington's nuclear capabilities.
        </strong>
       </div>
       <blockquote style="background-color: white; font-family: 'Lucida Grande', Verdana, sans-serif; font-size: 13.3333330154419px; font-style: italic; line-height: 17.3333320617676px; margin: 1em 2em !important; padding: 1em !important; position: relative;">
        <div style="border-left-color: rgb(233, 239, 243); border-left-style: solid; border-left-width: 1px; border-top-color: rgb(233, 239, 243); border-top-style: solid; border-top-width: 1px; bottom: 3em; left: 0px; min-height: 2em; position: absolute; top: 0px; width: 86.921875px;">
        </div>
        <div style="border-bottom-color: rgb(233, 239, 243); border-bottom-style: solid; border-bottom-width: 1px; border-right-color: rgb(233, 239, 243); border-right-style: solid; border-right-width: 1px; bottom: 0px; position: absolute; right: 0px; top: 2.3em; width: 86.921875px;">
        </div>
        <div style="width: inherit !important;">
         The Minuteman III missile was fitted with a test re-entry vehicle instead of a live thermonuclear warhead, and was
         <strong>
          test-launched from Vandenberg Air Force Base (AFB) in California at 03:36 local time (10:36 GMT)
         </strong>
         , according to the Air Force statement.
        </div>
        <div style="width: inherit !important;">
         <strong>
          With these launches, we not only verify our processes and the ICBM (intercontinental ballistic missile) weapon system, we provide a visual to the world that the Minuteman III is capable of striking pretty much anywhere with extreme precision,
         </strong>
         said Lt. Col Tytonia Moore of the 90th Missile Wing, based out of the Francis E. Warren AFB in Wyoming, according to the statement.
        </div>
        <div style="width: inherit !important;">
         The Mighty Ninety is one of the USAFs three missile wing commands  the other two being the 91st at Minot AFB in North Dakota and the 341st at Malstrom AFB in Montana.
         <strong>
          There are roughly 450 ICBMs between the three.
         </strong>
        </div>
       </blockquote>
