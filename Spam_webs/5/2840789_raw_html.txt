        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Do you ever struggle when speaking a foreign language? Well, you are not alone. Take a look at our favorite signs sure to have you in stitches.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Call me politically incorrect or just immature but I find flawed signage hilarious. Here are a few signs that have me laughing out loud.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         1.
         <strong>
          So, can I or cant I?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="feman" class="size-full wp-image-2201 aligncenter" height="500" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/feman.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="feman" width="407"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         2.
         <strong>
          Their service stinks.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="nokia-connocting-poopie (440 x 330)" class="size-full wp-image-2202 aligncenter" height="330" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/nokia-connocting-poopie-440-x-330.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="nokia-connocting-poopie (440 x 330)" width="440"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         3.
         <strong>
          For a fresh mayonnaise scent that lasts.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="coleslaw-wash (412 x 440)" class="size-full wp-image-2203 aligncenter" height="440" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/coleslaw-wash-412-x-440.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="coleslaw-wash (412 x 440)" width="412"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         4.
         <strong>
          I should have paid more attention.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="freezer-out-of-control (440 x 347)" class="size-full wp-image-2204 aligncenter" height="347" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/freezer-out-of-control-440-x-347.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="freezer-out-of-control (440 x 347)" width="440"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         5.
         <strong>
          There must be a teenager inside.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="listen-to-mee (440 x 330)" class="size-full wp-image-2205 aligncenter" height="330" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/listen-to-mee-440-x-330.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="listen-to-mee (440 x 330)" width="440"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         6.
         <strong>
          Is provoking ok?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="please-do-not-annoy (440 x 344)" class="size-full wp-image-2206 aligncenter" height="344" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/please-do-not-annoy-440-x-344.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="please-do-not-annoy (440 x 344)" width="440"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         7.
         <strong>
          Abstract reasoning, introspection, and t-shirts.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <img alt="do-not-be-a-monkey (457 x 306)" class="size-full wp-image-2141 aligncenter" height="310" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/do-not-be-a-monkey-457-x-306.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="do-not-be-a-monkey (457 x 306)" width="461"/>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         8.
         <strong>
          Ok, but Im much better at carefree new yearsing.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <img alt="lets-happy-christmas (448 x 336)" class="size-full wp-image-2143 aligncenter" height="336" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/lets-happy-christmas-448-x-3361.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="lets-happy-christmas (448 x 336)" width="448"/>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         9.
         <strong>
          I thought you could use one.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <img alt="thank-you-for-your-mother" class="size-full wp-image-2145 aligncenter" height="319" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/thank-you-for-your-mother.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="thank-you-for-your-mother" width="446"/>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         10.
         <strong>
          Tellin it like it is?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <img alt="zero-care" class="size-full wp-image-2147 aligncenter" height="295" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/zero-care1.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="zero-care" width="450"/>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         11.
         <strong>
          And another thing, Sparky. I do not appreciate your insolence when I request that you be seated.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="refrain-from-barking" class="size-full wp-image-2151 aligncenter" height="330" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/refrain-from-barking.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="refrain-from-barking" width="450"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         12.
         <strong>
          Discount on obsessive texters.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <img alt="creep-shop (458 x 307)" class="size-full wp-image-2152 aligncenter" height="307" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/creep-shop-458-x-307.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="creep-shop (458 x 307)" width="458"/>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         13.
         <strong>
          How about a whiskey on the socks?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <img alt="foot-saloon" class="size-full wp-image-2153 aligncenter" height="341" src="http://www.tripbase.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/04/foot-saloon.jpg" style="border: 2px solid black; margin-top: 3px; margin-bottom: 3px;" title="foot-saloon" width="442"/>
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          Have you noticed any odd signs lately? Dont keep them to yourself! Send your silly signs to blog@tripbase.com and well feature our favorites on the blog.
          <br/>
         </strong>
        </p>
