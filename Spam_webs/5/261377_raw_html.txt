          <p>
           Have you all seen Senator Rand Pauls speech on Friday, from the Values Voter summit? It was an amazing, brutally honest rundown of the attacks by Islamist groups against Christians around the world. It included President Obamas arming of the rebels in Syria, a group composed of Islamist militants, many of which are involved in these horrific scenes of violence, as well as a list of church leaders who have been killed for refusing to convert. It truly was an amazing speech, simply because no one in government has addressed the atrocities carried out against Christians. And it was because Paul finally did so, that I sat glued to my computer screen waiting. When the clip came to an end, I was stunned, really. Stunned because throughout his speech, Congressman Paul pointed time and again to the Muslim Brotherhood.
          </p>
          <p>
           Recently named a terrorist organization, the Muslim Brotherhood has been and continues to be the head of the terrorist octopus, financing Hamas, Al Qaida the Taliban and Al Shabob, among others. CAIR, an unindicted co-conspirator in the Holyland Foundation trial, is another arm of the same octopus, funneling money to these groups from the United States as they pose as a Muslim community/religious unity group. Often invited to use Christian churches for meetings, and providing fraudulent legal representation to local Muslims, they make a point of pressuring local governments to open mosques in residential neighborhoods, often overruling communities with threats of islamphobia. People like Pamela Geller have done an incredible job of informing the public of this dangerously disarming group, but unbelievably they are still allowed to function freely here.
          </p>
          <p>
           This wasnt mentioned by Paul, nor would I expect it to be, as he had a limited time to speak and touched on many issues related to the Christian genocide. However, he did attack the President on many levels and I was certain he would bring up the biggest concern many of us have. Why is congress okay with a Cabinet, filled to capacity, with members of the Muslim Brotherhood? Why is Valerie Jarett, Obamas closest advisor, herself a person with strong alleged ties to the Brotherhood, not escorted from the White House? Why are military handbooks still considered acceptable when they were written by a Brotherhood member, as is a high ranking DHS supervisor, whose job it is to protect us? In every case, these individuals are privy to classified documents, many of which even congress is blocked from reading. Is this a secret? I dont think so. Promotions and assignments are frequently published, yet no one seems concerned by these facts.
          </p>
          <p>
           Even if we dont accept the Egyptian peoples version of life under Morsi, or the new Egyptian Presidents evidence of the Brotherhoods tyranny, we must still condemn the group for atrocities carried out against non-Muslims. Membership to any group is in itself an indication of a belief system. So heres my question: why is this particular group inserted into our government? And, more to the point, why is no one talking about it?
          </p>
