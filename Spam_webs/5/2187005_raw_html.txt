        <a href="http://www.onewed.com/wedding-dresses/style/glamorous/" rel="follow">

        <a href="http://www.onewed.com/reviews/wedding-photographers/" rel="follow">
         wedding photographer
        </a>
        gets loads of killer shoe shots!  And bridal heels in a glittery metallic gold, silver or bronze... on-trend and beyond chic!
