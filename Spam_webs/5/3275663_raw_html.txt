      <h1 class="post-title">
       10 Summertime Truths
      </h1>
      <div class="post-meta">
       Posted on July 4, 2013
      </div>
      <div class="post-content">
       <h4 id="pressboard-ad-sponsorship-tag" style="margin-bottom: 35px;">
       </h4>
       <p>
        BREAKING: it is summertime. Proper sunny, sweaty, give-up-on-your-bangs-thats-how-your-hair-looks-now summertime, and with it comes a collective abandoning of responsibility and embracing of the NOW/nude beach. It is also time to acknowledge some truths. These are them.
       </p>
       <p>
        1) A tan is just a sunburn that looks good. It is just as harmful to your skin and will make you age quicker. Spray it on or bid it farewell. RIP, tans.
       </p>
       <p>
        2) A bikini body is any body in a bikini, and it is your goddamn prerogative to wear one no matter what you look like. Who wants to have to get full naked to pee?
       </p>
       <p>
        3) Just because the city of Toronto looks like a beautiful movie set in the hazy nights of summer does not mean you can go overboard with things and start talking like you are actually in a movie. I think Im just figuring out my journey. I guess, in a way, we all are is not a real thing a human would say.
       </p>
       <p>
        4) There are parks in this city that are not Trinity Bellwoods and they have just as much nice grass and maybe 400x more breathing space. Consider them! We are not lemmings in crop tops! Park for yourseeeeeeelf!
       </p>
       <p>
        5) You cannot day drink and expect to be on time/presentable for your evenings social engagements.
       </p>
       <p>
        6) Summertime make up is for chumps. Pop some pressed powder and waterproof mascara on that face and forget about it. Or just slather on the sunscreen, natures make up.
       </p>
       <p>
        7) Short shorts are for everyone! The city of Toronto is making me SO happy with all the chunky thighs in short shorts Ive seen in the past few weeks. GIT THEM THIGHS OUT, CHUBBY PALS OF TORONTO. I AM WITH YOU.
       </p>
       <p>
        8)
        <a href="https://www.change.org/petitions/the-city-of-toronto-formally-allow-the-consumption-of-alcoholic-beverages-in-city-parks-2" target="_blank">
         We should all be allowed to enjoy a casual bevvie on a picnic blanket under a nice shady tree.
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        9) Any pop drink in a wine is totally okay as a drink and has a fancy name. Uh, sorry, do you take issue with my diet coke/red wine combo? Do you take issue with SPAIN? (They do it like that in Spain.) (Sometimes, in some places.) (OL!) Not into spritzers? Do you hate all moms?!
       </p>
       <p>
        10) If a boy asks you to do an outdoor sport with him in the heat it is because he wants to touch you, probably.
       </p>
       <p>
        So, turns out I think that the summertime is almost exclusively about drinking/flirting in parks while wearing very little clothing. Turns out Im RIGHT! Happy summer!
       </p>
