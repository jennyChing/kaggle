It is a forward looking instrument for scientific applications and is used for viewing small things through a single lens. A monocular microscope comes with interchangeable eye pieces. The user can observe the slide only through one eye. You may accomplish this by closing the eye that is not on the microscope while observing the slide. A monocular microscope is often used in scientific research to closely observe small objects invisible to the naked eye.
