      <div>
       <h2 class="TxtBlue HLineC">
        Introduction:
       </h2>
       <div class="Clear">
       </div>
       <p>
        Getting rich is the American dream!  But few people are willing to actually take steps to get rich and stick with it.  You can get rich by taking some of these steps.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="FL W100 MT20" id="DivListing">
       <span class="StepClr1">
       </span>
       <h2 class="TxtBlue HLineB">
        Live on Half of Your Paycheck
       </h2>
       <p class="W356 FR">
        Learn to live on half of whatever you are paid and bank the rest.  Sound crazy?  It is possible, our perception of what we need to live is off balance.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="FL W100 MT20" id="DivListing">
       <span class="StepClr2">
       </span>
       <h2 class="TxtBlue HLineB">
        Start Paying Cash For Everything
       </h2>
       <p class="W356 FR">
        I know this idea is contrary to popular opinion, but this helps you understand how you are spending money on unnecessary things. Plus, you will never overspend.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="FL W100 MT20" id="DivListing">
       <span class="StepClr3">
       </span>
       <h2 class="TxtBlue HLineB">
        Do Things For Yourself
       </h2>
       <p class="W356 FR">
        Learn to fix items, mow your own yard, learn how to fix plumbing. You will gain valuable skills, save a lot of money, and gain confidence in yourself.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="FL W100 MT20" id="DivListing">
       <span class="StepClr4">
       </span>
       <h2 class="TxtBlue HLineB">
        Become Debt Free
       </h2>
       <p class="W356 FR">
        This one step can insure you will become rich.  Only having basic bills for utilities etc.can change your life.  You would be free from worry and stress!
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="FL W100 MT20" id="DivListing">
       <span class="StepClr5">
       </span>
       <h2 class="TxtBlue HLineB">
        Learn from People Who Have Become Rich
       </h2>
       <p class="W356 FR">
        The internet is full of blogs of people who are saving money and being frugal and giving you ideas of how to be radical and enjoy life!  Learn from people who have been there.
       </p>
      </div>
