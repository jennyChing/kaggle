       <p>
        Amanda is the founder of Writers Write. She creates business and creative writing courses.
        <br/>
       </p>
       Her signature creative course,
       <a href="http://writerswrite.co.za/pages/course-information" target="_blank">
        <b>
         Writers Write
        </b>
       </a>
       , specializes in the teaching of fiction writing. More than 150 Writers Write students have been published.
       <br/>
       Her signature business writing course is
       <a href="http://writerswrite.co.za/pages/about-writers-write" target="_blank">
        <b>
         The Plain Language Programme
        </b>
       </a>
       . This course teaches government structures and big business to write in plain language.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <b>
        Nomination
       </b>
       s: Woman of the Year, Most Influential Woman in Business &amp; Government, Mondi Awards, Feather Awards.
       <br/>
       <b>
        Her charity
       </b>
       , The Write Foundation, is dedicated to empowering South Africans through learning to write and to read.
       <br/>
       She has written two romance novels, a crime novel, a memoir and a writers diary.
