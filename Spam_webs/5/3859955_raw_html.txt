        <p>
         Losing weight can be difficult, but having the right motivation can make it easier for you. Some things that can give you motivation include your family, money, and clothing. Once you start to lose weight and feel like giving up, think of these things, and you will want to continue.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Take Care of Your Family
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         The main motivation of eating healthier and losing weight should be your family, such as your husband and children. You want to stay healthy as long as you can so you can take care of them. Each time you want to eat something that is unhealthy, look at your family, and remember why you want to lose weight.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Play With Your Children
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you are overweight, you may have a hard time being active. This is because you may become short of breath very easily. Carrying around extra weight causes your body to work much harder than someone that is of normal weight. Losing weight will allow you to go to the park with your children and play with them instead of sitting on the bench. It will also allow you to do many more activities that you enjoy, such as jogging, walking, swimming, and other activities.
        </p>
        <p>
         One great motivation is showing your children that you are able to reach your goals. This shows them that working hard at something really does work. This teaches them a great lesson they will never forget. If they have a weight loss problem in the future, they will think back to you, and this will give them the motivation they need to lose weight.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Buy Better Clothing
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Being able to purchase the
         <a href="http://fashionista.com/2013/08/why-is-it-so-difficult-for-the-average-american-woman-to-shop-for-clothes" rel="nofollow">
          clothing
         </a>
         that you want and being more comfortable in it is another motivation. You may not be able to find as many plus clothes sizes as you can regular sizes. Losing weight will allow you to be able to purchase a wide variety of clothes that you never thought you could. Swimsuit season will also not be stressful for you, because you can proudly display your body to the world in a beautiful swimsuit.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Save Money
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Part of losing weight means not eating fast food, and this will help you save money. Insurance companies may offer you a lower premium if you are at your optimum weight. In many cases, overweight people have more medical issues, such as high blood pressure, diabetes, and heart disease. If you have these problems, you will have more medical bills, which can get very expensive.
        </p>
        <p>
         In the end, you have to lose weight for yourself first, and you have to want to do it or you will not. Losing weight is difficult to do, and it does take very hard work and motivation. Dont worry if you need a little help. There are products out there that can make your weight loss go a little smoother, including
         <a href="http://officialhcgdietstore.com/hcg-diet-products/" target="_blank">
          hcg drops
         </a>
         .
        </p>
