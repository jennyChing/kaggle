                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Painted Dishes
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4698801741221433465" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-8LZwiiNh2JM/U4JPvyIfn6I/AAAAAAAAGBI/mMZl0n3vkoI/s1600/100_3299+painted+and+stenciled+dishes.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Vintage, Paint and more... stenciled dollar store plate and painted thrift store finds" border="0" height="320" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-8LZwiiNh2JM/U4JPvyIfn6I/AAAAAAAAGBI/mMZl0n3vkoI/s1600/100_3299+painted+and+stenciled+dishes.jpg" title="Vintage, Paint and more... Painted Dishes" width="240"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      I have an open space area above my kitchen cabinets
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      that a lot of houses have these days.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      It's an area that I have seen merchandised so nicely
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      in a lot of magazine pictures
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      and
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      I wanted to do the same thing.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                     </div>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-YUyagLYKMzo/U4JTlxt62VI/AAAAAAAAGCA/gv05tXwtO9Y/s1600/100_3311+space+above+kitchen+cabinets.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Vintage, Paint and more... above cabinet vignettes" border="0" height="480" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-YUyagLYKMzo/U4JTlxt62VI/AAAAAAAAGCA/gv05tXwtO9Y/s1600/100_3311+space+above+kitchen+cabinets.jpg" title="Vintage, Paint and more... Painted Dishes" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      This is the only picture I can get of this space.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      My kitchen is a hallway kitchen - very long and narrow,
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      with no windows and a center light.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      We rent - we deal with it.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Anyway, this shows the space I am referring to.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      When you come into the kitchen
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      all you see is a very long blank space.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      I wanted to fill this void with something pretty, cottageand vintage....
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      However, I really didn't have anything I thought would add
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      interest to the space
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      and
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      being on a budget was limiting what I could use.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      I had to improvise.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      You know me and how I love paint by now.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      If it doesn't move - it gets painted.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      I headed to Dollar Tree - my favorite place
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      and
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      bought some clear glass plates, a plain white plate, a couple of candle sticks,
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      and
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      a silver platter.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      Then to the thrift stores - my second favorite place
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      and
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      picked up a sugar bowl and creamer - in a not so pretty color, a white pitcher,
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      and
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      a wooden tray.....
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-qdUrzoWxOKU/U4JVAYW2fjI/AAAAAAAAGCU/wYOu6z09pgA/s1600/10-13-2013+003+thrifting+3+thrifted+sugar+and+creamer%252C+candlestick%252C+frame.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Vintage, Paint and more... thrifted dishes" border="0" height="400" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-qdUrzoWxOKU/U4JVAYW2fjI/AAAAAAAAGCU/wYOu6z09pgA/s1600/10-13-2013+003+thrifting+3+thrifted+sugar+and+creamer%252C+candlestick%252C+frame.jpg" title="Vintage, Paint and more... Painted Dishes" width="366"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      along with a few other interesting things I intend to use for the other side.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
