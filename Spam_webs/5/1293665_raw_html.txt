             Kris Humphries is alive and seemingly well.  No Kardashian gonna hold him down!  Instead hes enjoying himself at the Kentucky Derby parties.
            </p>
            <p>
             Friday night was the annual Derby Fillies &amp; Stallions party. Celebrities and athletes, including Aaron Rodgers, Clay Matthews, Dallass Josh Henderson, and Kris Humphries came together in Louisville to celebrate before the big race. Additionally, Top Chef judge Hugh Acheson, who created the partys featured and appropriately named Mint Mutiny cocktail, was in attendance.
