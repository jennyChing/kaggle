           <article class="et_pb_post et_pb_no_thumb post-2732 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-newsletter" id="post-2732">
            <h2>
             <a href="http://craigtracy.com/questions">
              Skin Wars Season 2 is here!, Returning from Los Angeles and Traveling to Austria
             </a>
            </h2>
            <p class="post-meta">
             Jun 1, 2015
            </p>
            <div class="one_half">
             <strong>
              The Wait Is Over!!
             </strong>
             Skin Wars Season 2 premieres Wednesday June 10th at 9/8c on GSN. Craig is very excited to be back as judge alongside his fantastic co-judges Rupaul and Robin Slonina. The beautiful and talented Rebecca Romijn makes her return as host of the show. Craig promises that Skin Wars season 2 will be even more exciting than season 1, with more interesting challenges, talented artists and surprises. Be sure to tune in OR set your DVRs and Tivos to GSN Wednesday Nights at 9/8c. Heres a link to help you find GSN in your area http://gsn.viewerlink.tv/
             <p>
              On May 18th, Craig returned from Los Angeles where he was a judge at the inaugural Body Fine Art Competition held May 16th at The Springs in Los Angeles, California.  The event was the first of its kind for Los Angeles and a $5,000 first place prize was awarded. The event was a huge success and the producers hope to make it an annual competition.
             </p>
             <p>
              <img alt="SWx2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2735" height="203" src="http://craigtracy.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/SWx2.png" width="250"/>
             </p>
            </div>
            <div class="one_half last">
             Craig will be traveling to Austria on June 28th-July 8th for The World Bodypainting Festival. Craig will be participating in his 12th consecutive championships, and once again he will be a member of the jury and a featured speaker. Craig absolutely loves the festival and looks forward to seeing all of his friends from across the globe at this years event.
