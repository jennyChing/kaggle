          <p>
           This is called Veritas RS 111, and the production version of this fantastic looking sports car was shown to the world for the first time last week at Londons exclusive Salon Priv.
           <br/>
           Driven by a modified version of the E60 M5s V10, the topless RS III has about 600hp to push its lightweight 1070kg frame. Veritas RS III expects to sprint to 100 from a standstill in just 3.2 seconds while top speed is expected to be 347kph.
           <br/>
           Exclusivity is a big selling point when it comes to pitching cars on the mega-rich, then Veritas will only build 50 of these a year. Deliveries are expected to begin by the end of this year, but yet there has been no notice of how many euros will get you into one.
          </p>
