         <p>
          World class holistic Oriental therapies and stunning spa design combineto offer a unique
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/spaguide/london" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           London spa day.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-oriental-ext.jpg" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           <img alt="mandarin oriental ext A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6123" height="382" src="http://bloguk.sfw-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-oriental-ext.jpg" title="A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          On a grey evening in July, the heavy clouds were threatening to burst into the type of impromptu summer downpour which Londoners have come to expect, and for which optimistic tourists often fail to account for. I was navigating my way through Knightsbridge station, silently dividing the crowds into two groups: those with umbrellas at the ready(Londoners), and those of the cheery-faced, t-shirt-wearing variety (definitely not Londoners).
         </p>
         <p>
          With the weather being so bleak, I was very excited about how I would be spending my evening: immersed in the luxurious spa at the
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/Spa/12824-Mandarin-Oriental-Hyde-Park" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The Package: Luxury Wellness Escape, 260
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/Spa/12824-Mandarin-Oriental-Hyde-Park" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           Mandarin Oriental
          </a>
          have a fantastic offer on this summer: the
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/Spa/12824-Mandarin-Oriental-Hyde-Park#deals" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           Luxury Wellness Escape
          </a>
          is billed as a heavenly indulgent day of spa pampering and it is exactly that. Guests enjoy a 1 hour 50 minute Time Ritual Treatment, which isnt a specific treatment but rather a booking of time  you and your therapist discuss what you need and they will tailor a bespoke treatment especially for you. The package also includes full use of the luxurious Heat and Water experiences, a light lunch and smoothie and use of the fitness facilities.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-pool.jpg" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           <img alt="mandarin pool A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6124" height="355" src="http://bloguk.sfw-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-pool.jpg" title="A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The Spa: Refined Elegance
          </strong>
          <br/>
          Simply walking through the entrance of the 5-star
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/Spa/12824-Mandarin-Oriental-Hyde-Park" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           Mandarin Oriental
          </a>
          is an experience in itself. The legendary elegance, style and impeccable service are evident immediately  the hotel is widely regarded as one of Londons most luxurious hotels, and its not hard to see why.
         </p>
         <p>
          At the lobby, a friendly member of staff led me down to the 2-floor subterranean spa. I was given a guided tour of the impressive spa facilities and was eager to try out everything on offer. The low lighting, sense of space and the fact that only a few guests are permitted in the spa at any time makes for a supremely relaxing experience  I felt like I had the entire spa to myself. The spa is divided into male and female areas, which I personally prefer to unisex facilities.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-therapy-room.jpg" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           <img alt="mandarin therapy room A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6127" height="336" src="http://bloguk.sfw-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-therapy-room.jpg" title="A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The spa recommends that guests arrive at least 45 minutes before their treatment, so I had plenty of time to relax in the sauna (there are head rests so you can comfortably recline), amethyst crystal steam room (dark and peaceful, like being inside a luminous cave), and Vitality Pool. Ten minutes before my treatment, I made my way to the Colour Therapy Room for a few moments of meditation. The colours on the walls blend from greens to blues to oranges and back again  it sounds simple, but its so relaxing to just sit and watch this subtle light show.
         </p>
         <p>
          The design is minimalist and exudes a soothing zen vibe: Buddha statues and candles pepper the stairways, curvaceous Stephen Cox sculptures border the Vitality Pool and the expert use of tranquil lighting heightens the calmness of the space. Designed by celebrated architect Eric Parry, the
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/Spa/12824-Mandarin-Oriental-Hyde-Park" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           Mandarin Orienta
           <span style="text-decoration:underline">
            l Hyde Park
           </span>
          </a>
          is a world-class example of urban spa design.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-treatment.jpg" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           <img alt="mandarin treatment A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6125" height="354" src="http://bloguk.sfw-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-treatment.jpg" title="A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The Treatment: Aromatherapy Associates Massage + Customised Facial
          </strong>
          <br/>
          My therapist Silvia met me in the Colour Therapy Room and led me upstairs to the treatment rooms. She was very friendly and knowledgeable: when inspecting my skin before the facial, I pointed out that my forehead breaks out in spots a few times each month. Silvia pointed out that in Chinese Medicine, the forehead is linked to the large intestine  so any, shall we say, culinary and red wine-related overindulgences will likely show on the forehead. It was something I had never considered and was a great bit of knowledge to take away.
         </p>
         <p>
          The treatment began with a 1 hour
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/aromatherapy.htm" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           Aromatherapy
          </a>
          Associates massage. Silvia asked me how I wanted to feel and which scents I prefer. My head had been in the clouds recently so I wanted to feel more grounded. She dabbed two different essential oils blends on my hands, both custom made for Mandarin Oriental. I chose the Reflect oil, an earthy mix of jasmine, black pepper, juniper and ginger. The
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/massage-types.htm" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           massage
          </a>
          was exceptional and Silvia found tight areas on my back immediately, expertly kneading the kinks and knots. To complete the massage, she gave me one of the best head massages Ive ever experienced. Indian Head
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/massage-types.htm" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           Massage
          </a>
          is my all-time favourite spa treatment  instantly relaxing and stress-relieving.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Aromatherapy Associates Customised
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/facials.htm" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           Facial
          </a>
          followed the massage. My skin has been very dry lately, so Silvia chose a series of Aromatherapy Associates facial products with moisturising rose. My skin felt nourished and pampered after the 50 minute facial, and Silvia encouraged me to take better care of my complexion.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-fitness.jpg" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           <img alt="mandarin fitness A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6128" height="322" src="http://bloguk.sfw-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-fitness.jpg" title="A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          After the treatments, she brought through a delicious tea as I came back to reality. I then got changed and headed to a private room in the fitness area to enjoy a tasty vegetarian club sandwich and green juice. Watching The Simpsons in the Mandarin Oriental while sipping on green juice was definitely a first!
         </p>
         <p>
          The only off-note of the evening was that after my meal, there was no one at the fitness reception. I wasnt sure if I needed a key to access the elevator and exit the hotel, so I waited for five minutes. I would have liked to have said thank you for such a relaxing experience.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-spa-massage.jpg" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           <img alt="mandarin spa massage A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6129" height="266" src="http://bloguk.sfw-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/mandarin-spa-massage.jpg" title="A Zen Wellness Journey: Review of Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park" width="620"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           The Conclusion
          </strong>
          <br/>
          If youre feeling the stress of city living, I cant recommend the
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/Spa/12824-Mandarin-Oriental-Hyde-Park" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park
          </a>
          enough. I left the spa feeling positive and relaxed, and my skin was glowing from the
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/facials.htm" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           facial
          </a>
          . Silvias knowledge of Chinese Medicine has also inspired me to read up about this fascinating subject  and take care of my skin a bit better! The Mandarin Oriental is the perfect
          <a href="http://www.spafinder.co.uk/spaguide/london" target="_blank" value="a-zen-wellness-journey-review-of-mandarin-oriental-hyde-park">
           London spa
          </a>
          to visit if youre craving a bit of peace and solitude in a truly luxurious environment.
         </p>
