             <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
              The 5 Best New Windows Phone Operating System 10 Features | All Tech Affairs
             </h1>
             <span>
              <div class="post-header">
               <div class="post-header-line-1">
               </div>
               <font style="font-size:13px; font-family:verdana; word-spacing:1px; color:#555555;">
                Posted by:
                <a href="https://plus.google.com/u/0/+AishwarBabber/" rel="nofollow">
                 Kathula Santhosh
                </a>

             </span>

               <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                  Hii Friends, I think this is really good news for you all. Microsoft will give away its upcoming Windows 10 Operating System as a free upgrade to its Windows 7, 8, 8.1 users, as the world's largest software company tries to hold onto customers in the new mobile era that has been widely by-passed it. The announcement by Terry Myerson, who runs Microsoft's operating systems groups is a marked change of Microsoft, which has charged for new versions of Windows, one of its main profit drivers.Windows 10,expected on the market this month or next month, will beavailablefor one year as a free upgrade to users ofWindows 7, Windows 8.1 and Windows Phone 8.1, Myerson said.
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-pL9bqDViKkE/VMCXvaGx5GI/AAAAAAAAC5U/dlymqACUS2I/s1600/microsoft-windows10.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                  <img border="0" height="190" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-pL9bqDViKkE/VMCXvaGx5GI/AAAAAAAAC5U/dlymqACUS2I/s1600/microsoft-windows10.jpg" width="320"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif; font-size: large;">
                  Also Read :
                  <a href="http://www.alltechaffairs.com/2015/01/redmi-note-4g-smartphone-price-features.html" target="_blank">
                   Redmi Note 4G Smartphone Released In India.
                  </a>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                  The shift shows Microsoft is moving toward a frequently updated, subscription model for its flagship operating system, rather than major, paid for upgrades every few years.
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                  It is also recognition that in the last decade Windows, featured on roughly 15% of computing devices including phones and tablets, has become largely irrelevant for many consumers. Microsoft hopes that by making the software free, it will attract the more than One Billion personal computer users of Windows to run Windows on other devices.
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                  The new approach, which Myerson called " Windows as a service" takes a similar approach to Apple Inc and Google Inc, which regularly update their software systems free for customers.
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                  At an event to show off some upcoming features in Windows 10, Microsoft also unveiled its Cortana voice activated search function for the PC Desktop, which has only been available on mobile devices.
                 </span>
                </div>
                <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif; font-size: x-large; font-weight: normal;">
                  Before Windows 10 download :
                 </span>
                </h2>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                 <span style="font-family: Trebuchet MS, sans-serif;">
                  There are some warnings you should be aware of before you download windows 10.
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div style="text-align: justify;">
                </div>
                <ol>
                 <li>
                  <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                   First back up your data. Not that you had any execuse not to anyway, but be sure to perform a backup on your upgrade if you have anything you can't recover. Or even if you don't. Just do a backup.
                  </span>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                   You won't be able to use your recovery partition to downgrade. If you have a recovery partition on your system, it will no longer be ablr to restore your computer to the version of Windows you had before.
                  </span>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <span style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                   You will need external recovery media to undo the upgrade. As you might expect since you don't have a recovery partition, you will need a disc or USB drive don't like or can't use Windows 10.
                  </span>
                 </li>
                </ol>
