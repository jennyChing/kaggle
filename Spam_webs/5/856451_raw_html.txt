        <p>
         Searching Flickr groups for Lego returns
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/search/groups/?q=lego">
          over four-thousand hits
         </a>
         .  Among the interesting oddities Ive discovered so far is the
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/groups/legoweps/">
          LEGO Life-Size weapons group
         </a>
         , which has 160 items.  Shown here is
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/dawgsguns/4594039008/in/pool-legowepsphotos/dawgsguns/4594039008/in/pool-568528@N24/">
          M4 v5 by The Dawgs Guns
         </a>
         .   There are also
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/bluestarmedia/4518769279/in/pool-legoweps#/photos/bluestarmedia/4518769279/in/pool-568528@N24/">
          Lego knives
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/monsterbrick/4480093604/in/pool-568528@N24/">
          Lego bombs
         </a>
         even a
         <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/simafol/3079167646/in/pool-568528@N24/">
          wearable Lego Boba Fett get-up
         </a>
         .
