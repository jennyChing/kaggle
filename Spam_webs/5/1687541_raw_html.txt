        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          A Few Highlights:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <div class="iframe-wrap">
         <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" height="380" src="https://embed.spotify.com/?uri=spotify:track:0SwadQnQE9boiTnSecjMQM" width="300">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          "Child Stars" -- Science Partner
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Why it stands out:
         </strong>
         This song just cuts so hard, especially live. It's an epic, edgy tune with Science Partner's characteristic vocal harmonies. Go see the band play it at 12:45 PM at Krewe during the actual Showcase.
        </p>
        <div class="iframe-wrap">
         <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" height="380" src="https://embed.spotify.com/?uri=spotify:track:5B95fhp9gXTjXtqWFcJcaH" width="300">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        <div class="ads middle placeholder" data-vmg-ad="Middle" data-vmg-format="lazy">
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          "Weatherman (feat. Mathenee)" -- Foxfield Four
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Why it stands out:
         </strong>
         So happily pop-driven, this song is among the catchiest on the playlist.
        </p>
        <div class="iframe-wrap">
         <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" height="380" src="https://embed.spotify.com/?uri=spotify:track:7vf9ytYKkLgO2hSJUu3Qk7" width="300">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          "Folklore" -- Josh Quinlan Quartet
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Why it stands out:
         </strong>
         Josh Quinlan shows off his chops in this smooth, Charlie Parker-esque groove, embodying the "sheets of sound" mindset.
        </p>
        <p class="anchor" id="page-2">
         <a name="page-2">
         </a>
        </p>
        <div class="iframe-wrap">
         <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" height="380" src="https://embed.spotify.com/?uri=spotify:track:4p0bupSt8kTAtHD4knQ64a" width="300">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        <div class="iframe-wrap">
         <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" height="380" src="https://embed.spotify.com/?uri=spotify:track:3sdG2fcQLaS18kn9jyfEeg" width="300">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        <div class="ads middle placeholder" data-vmg-ad="Middle" data-vmg-format="lazy">
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          "Feeling Good Again" -- Rob Drabkin
    "Golden Some Day" -- Jeremy Dion
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Why these stand out:
         </strong>
         The melodies, energy and delivery of both Rob Drabkin and Jeremy Dion rival most performers at any level. Get lost in the sweet, sweet crooning of these artists.
        </p>
        <div class="iframe-wrap">
         <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" height="380" src="https://embed.spotify.com/?uri=spotify:track:7GT0Ox6X1D0WlqfeSmYPCQ" width="300">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          "Should've Been Me" -- Devan Blake Jones
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Why it stands out:
         </strong>
         Devan Blake Jones' voice is in the apex of smooth. Jones is a blossoming artist -- he was on
         <em>
          American Idol
         </em>
         during seasons eleven and twelve and released a mix tape this year.
        </p>
        <div class="iframe-wrap">
         <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" height="380" src="https://embed.spotify.com/?uri=spotify:track:5LZjTPODnDoIkwOTUiD4SA" width="300">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          "Riot" -- MTHDS
         </strong>
        </p>
        <div class="ads middle placeholder" data-vmg-ad="Middle" data-vmg-format="lazy">
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Why it stands out:
         </strong>
         MTHDS is weirdly addicting to listen to. MTHDS has an unbelievably unique sound, drawing inspiration from unlikely sources such as Cake, Lupe Fiasco and Black Sabbath. MTHDS, in its own words, "combines the fury of punk, the street-wise soul of hip-hop and funk with an over-the-top bombastic roots party groove."
        </p>
        <div class="iframe-wrap">
         <iframe allowtransparency="true" frameborder="0" height="380" src="https://embed.spotify.com/?uri=spotify:track:2t57e1Gk7mqAzW41oYKFXj" width="300">
         </iframe>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          "Sweetest Girl" -- Vices I Admire
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Vices I Admire harnesses an unbelievable amount of energy in their studio recordings. Lead singer Dave Curtis can really belt a song.
        </p>
