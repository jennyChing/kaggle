       <h2 id="post-949">
        <a href="http://huggingthecoast.com/2010/07/13/rum-watermelon-for-the-adults-and-wiggly-watermelon-for-the-kids-fruit-dessert-recipes/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Rum Watermelon For the Adults and Wiggly Watermelon For the Kids (Fruit Dessert Recipes)">
         Rum Watermelon For the Adults and Wiggly Watermelon For the Kids (Fruit Dessert Recipes)
        </a>
       </h2>
       <p class="postmetadata">
        Posted by DuCap in
        <a href="http://huggingthecoast.com/category/food/recipes/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Recipes">
         Recipes
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://huggingthecoast.com/category/food/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Food">
         Food

        ,
        <a href="http://huggingthecoast.com/category/hugging-the-coast-features/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Special Features">

        <a href="http://huggingthecoast.com/category/hugging-the-coast-features/fruit-dessert-of-the-week/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Fruit Dessert Ideas">
         Fruit Dessert Ideas
        </a>
       </p>
      </div>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <a href="http://www.gourmetmomonthego.com/2009/05/w-is-for-wiggly-watermelon.html">
         <img alt="Rum Spiked Watermelon Recipe For the Adults and a Wiggly Jiggly Jello Watermelon Recipe For the Kids (Fruit Dessert Recipes)" border="0" height='302""' src="http://www.huggingthecoast.com/flickr-wiggly.jpg" width="482"/>
        </a>
       </center>
       <p>
        <a href="http://huggingthecoast.com/about/fruit-dessert-recipes-bananas-apples-oranges-strawberries-and-more/">
         <img align="right" alt="See More Fruit Dessert Recipes Organized by Type" border="0" height="181" right="" src="http://www.huggingthecoast.com/fruitdesserts.jpg" width="229"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        If youre looking for fun watermelon dessert recipes that you can make with 1 large watermelon, youll love these 2 easy make ahead recipes for
        <strong>
         Rum Spiked Watermelon for Adults
        </strong>
        and
        <strong>
         Wiggly Jiggly Jello Watermelon for Kids
        </strong>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        One of the great things about these 2 watermelon recipes is how perfectly they compliment each other. The Rum Spiked Watermelon for Adults uses only the sweet center of the watermelon itself while the Wiggly Jiggly Jello Watermelon uses only the rind; allowing you to serve 1 watermelon 2 different ways.
       </p>
       <p>
        Both of these fruit recipes are very easy to make and would work well for any casual party or get together.
       </p>
       <p>
        Enjoy!
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Ingredients for the
         <strong>
          Rum Spiked Watermelon for Adults
         </strong>
         from the folks at
         <a href="http://kitchen-goddess.yumsugar.com/">
          Yumsugar Kitchen Goddess
         </a>
         :
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        1 large watermelon
        <br/>
        1 750-ml bottle of white rum, chilled
        <br/>
        2-3 large Ziploc bags
       </p>
       <p>
        How to Make
        <a href="http://kitchen-goddess.yumsugar.com/Rum-Spiked-Watermelon-1914354">
         <strong>
          Rum Spiked Watermelon for Adults
         </strong>
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Ingredients for
         <strong>
          Wiggly Jiggly Jello Watermelon for the Kids
         </strong>
         from the folks at
         <a href="http://www.gourmetmomonthego.com">
          Gourmet Mom on the Go
         </a>
         :
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        (This recipe makes enough to fill 1/2 of a watermelon. To make enough for an entire watermelon, just double the ingredients.)
       </p>
       <p>
        Watermelon rind
        <br/>
        4 large boxes red jell-o (6 oz., we used cherry)
        <br/>
        5 cups water (DO NOT follow the water instructions on the jell-o box, your watermelon will not hold its shape)
        <br/>
        1/4 c. mini chocolate chips
       </p>
       <p>
        How to Make
        <a href="http://www.gourmetmomonthego.com/2009/05/w-is-for-wiggly-watermelon.html">
         <strong>
          Wiggly Jiggly Jello Watermelon for the Kids
         </strong>
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Have fun and stay cool!
       </p>
       <p>
        You can also see our
