        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         The Divatude Challenge 2013: Welcome Whitney
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-02-12T06:00:29+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          February 12, 2013
         </time>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div id="greet_block">
         <noscript>
          <div class="greet_block wpgb_cornered">
           <div class="greet_text">
            <div class="greet_image">
             <a href="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/feed/rss/" rel="nofollow">
              <img alt="WP Greet Box icon" src="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/wp-content/plugins/wp-greet-box/images/rss_icon.png"/>
             </a>
            </div>
            Hello there! If you are new here, you might want to
            <a href="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/feed/rss/" rel="nofollow">
             <strong>
              subscribe to the RSS feed
             </strong>
            </a>
            for updates on this topic.
            <div style="clear:both">
            </div>
           </div>
          </div>
         </noscript>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align:left;">
         Im excited to introduce you all two the 2nd participant in The Divatude Challenge 2013! Whitney blogs over at
         <a href="http://cafemochareflections.com" target="_blank" title="Cafe Mocha Reflections">
          Cafe Mocha Reflections
         </a>
         and has become a great blogger friend and inspiration over the past year. Whitneys professional goals this year are focused on building her blogs brand and building her
         <a class="aal" href="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/goto/1/direct-sales" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="direct sales">
          direct sales
         </a>
         businesses.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im a huge proponent of direct sales companies as many of them are women owned and pave the way for entrepreneurship by giving you an affordable way to build capital, learn marketing skills and network with others. Most of my favorite beauty and household products are gotten through my favorite independent consultants!
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <em>
           <a href="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/whitney.jpg">
            <img alt="Cafe Mocha Reflections" class="aligncenter" height="300" src="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/whitney.jpg?w=231" width="231"/>
           </a>
          </em>
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         My name is Whitney Wesley. Im a 50-year-old diva who lives in St Louis, MO with my new husband and two daughters  ages 17 &amp; 12.5.
        </p>
        <p>
         I work from home and for the past year I started blogging (which I absolutely love!) and Ive started a business as a Lifestyle Consultant for Soul Purpose, a personal care and wellness company I didnt get that business off like I should have due to fear and lack of motivation on my part. We had a lot going on last year with two moves within 5 months and I put my business on the back burner. I blamed lack of knowing people in St Louis as my excuse, which is crazy! I, also, plan on becoming an Independent Representative for Silpada Designs, a sterling silver jewelry company.
        </p>
        <h3>
         My professional goals for 2013
        </h3>
        <p>
         I will focus on building my brand at my blog Cafe Mocha Reflections as a middle-aged woman with a love of life and the push and determination to achieve her goals. I want to show women in my age group that are (or soon will be) going through menopause like myself to keep pushing and learning.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Start using ads on blog to generate revenue
         </li>
         <li>
          Promote my blog 4 general social media sites: Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn and Pinterest
         </li>
         <li>
          Invite guest bloggers that have the same interest as I do
         </li>
         <li>
          Continue my partnership with other bloggers and blogging communities where I am writing and sharing my goal and brand.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         These will be on-going goals throughout the year. Id like to consistently do them all and have check-ins with myself and others to see how well Im doing, what needs to be tweaked and/or revamped.
        </p>
        <p>
         My target date for my direct sales goals for my direct sales businesses will be six months from now  August 2013. Its my plan to have at least 1 party a week with Silpada and 2 sales a week with Soul Purpose.
        </p>
        <p>
         Networking is a must for any business  I plan on stepping up my game by going to the local businesses to establish relationships and make myself a presence for my business ventures.
        </p>
        <p>
         My goal for
         <a href="http://www.soulpurpose.net/esuite/home/whitneywesley/" target="_blank" title="Whitney's Soul Purpose">
          Soul Purpose
         </a>
         is to generate repeat customers since this is a tangible product that people will have a need for because they love the
        </p>
        <p>
         I will:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Approach people and introduce them to the company by handing out product sample packages. This includes people from church, girls school, my neighbors, etc.
         </li>
         <li>
          Set up one-on- one appointments with people to familiarize them with the products
         </li>
         <li>
          Sell product to local people and on the internet using social media.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         My goals for Silpada Designs will be to:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Wear the jewelry, show the jewelry, and have parties to promote the jewelry and bring women together to have fun and promote socializing and networking. This, also, includes church members, school, neighbors, etc.
         </li>
         <li>
          Set up shows and build up a client and hostess list.
         </li>
         <li>
          Sell jewelry to local people and on the internet using social media.
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         I want my business ventures to replace my income as an independent contractor. I need to free up my time so I can be creative with my goals.
        </p>
        <p>
         Scheduling is going to be very important to be successful. I will set aside time for each one and dedicate myself to that particular business and work on it for the designated time.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align:center;">
         <a href="http://divaswithpurpose.wordpress.com/category/divatude-challenge/">
          <img alt="The Divatude Challenge 2013" class="aligncenter wp-image-1267" height="203" src="http://www.divaswithapurpose.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/divatudechallenge1.jpg" width="193"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Its going to be exciting to watch Whitney build her brands over the next year and step out of her comfort zone with networking and marketing. Many thanks to her for participating in The Divatude Challenge 2013!
         </em>
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align:center;">
         What advice would you give Whitney to help build her brands?
