           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        <span id="more-196712">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        1. Choose the perfect place for your kitchen work center.A dead space could be a good spot for an office. An empty wall, a table and a comfortable chair can do wonders.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/kitchen-los-angeles-office-desk/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196719" height="884" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/kitchen-los-angeles-office-desk.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/grey-kitchen-walls-office-desk/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196721" height="399" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/grey-kitchen-walls-office-desk.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        2. Use your walls as much as you can! If youre always in a hurry and you tend to forget things, try a cork backsplash. This is perfect for hanging calendars, memo notes, recipes, or even pictures with your family.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/bamboo-dining-chair-caribbean-green-kitchen-desk/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196723" height="711" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/bamboo-dining-chair-caribbean-green-kitchen-desk.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        3.If you dont have a corkboard, you can always use a chalkboard (or paint your wall with chalkboard painting). This is a good way to remember important things like calling your boss or paying your bills.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/chalkboard-for-desk-instead-corkboard/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196724" height="399" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/chalkboard-for-desk-instead-corkboard.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        4.Repurpose some of your kitchen shelves or drawers.Try to use the storage space above the office to organize your paperwork and books.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/white-kitchen-space-turned-into-desk/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196725" height="469" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/white-kitchen-space-turned-into-desk.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        5.Save some workspace surfaces by using pullout shelves to store printers and fax machines.This is also a way to protect them from dustand, of course, to keep them away from children.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/pull-put-desk-drawers1/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196726" height="801" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/pull-put-desk-drawers1.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/pull-put-desk-drawers/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196727" height="800" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/pull-put-desk-drawers.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/drawer-pull-out-for-printing/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196728" height="881" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/drawer-pull-out-for-printing.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        6.Kitchen islands are great for extra storage. This way you will create a boundary between your actual cooking area and the office. Add versatility by using a movable kitchen island. You will have more shelves and less worry regarding storage options.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/round-kitchen-island-for-big-areas/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196729" height="397" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/round-kitchen-island-for-big-areas.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/glass-countertop-for-kitchen-island/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196730" height="480" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/glass-countertop-for-kitchen-island.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        7.Think about other space saving options. For example, create an under desk storage area. Use empty boxes, magazine racks, or whatever else comes into your mind, and hide them using a curtain.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/underdesk-storage-for-desk-from-kitchen/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196731" height="800" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/underdesk-storage-for-desk-from-kitchen.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        8.Make sure you have enough power sources for all your equipment (computer, fax, charges, print and so on). If you dont have enough plugs, use a power strip. Trust us, you will need it!
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/power-sources-for-all-gadgets/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196732" height="396" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/power-sources-for-all-gadgets.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        9.Avoid having all sorts of wires and cords around the countertop. It doesnt look good and can be dangerous. Think about it: knifes, wires and maybe few drops of water definitely not a good idea!
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/wire-hide-under-desk/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196733" height="388" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/wire-hide-under-desk.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        10.Lighting is another detail that must be considered. In order to have access to a natural light source, you should place the desk close to the window. In terms of artificial lighting, if you dont have enough space for a desk lamp, try under cabinet lighting.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/the-importance-of-light-for-your-desk/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196734" height="400" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/the-importance-of-light-for-your-desk.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        11.Fix your computer monitor on the wall, and gain more space for the keyboard.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/computer-monitor-fixed-on-the-wall/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196735" height="821" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/computer-monitor-fixed-on-the-wall.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        12.Use a comfortable chair, especially if you spend a lot of time at the computer. You can use one of the chairs in the kitchen, or you can choose another one.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/comfortable-chair-for-desk-in-the-kitchen/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196736" height="739" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/comfortable-chair-for-desk-in-the-kitchen.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        13.If the space allows you, create a window seat in your office kitchen. Dont you think this would be a great place to spend a late summer evening?
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/corner-desk-in-the-kitchen-window-seat/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196737" height="393" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/corner-desk-in-the-kitchen-window-seat.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        14.Use boxes or wood baskets to store office junk.Forget about the regular and boring trash can! Be inventive if you want your kitchen office to be different from any other offices. Boxes are simple, cute and can be customized. The wood basket reminds me of grandmas goodies.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/boxes-to-keep-all-files-organized/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196738" height="394" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/boxes-to-keep-all-files-organized.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        15.Hide your office in the closet.I bet you didnt see that coming! We love this idea and we believe its the fastest way to hide clutter when you have unexpected guests. Just close the doors and thats it,no one will notice anything!
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/hide-your-office-against-closet-doors/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196739" height="909" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/hide-your-office-against-closet-doors.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        16.Use some of your kitchen tools to organize your office. For example, you can store paper clips in your old ice cubed trays or muffin tins. I think its a good idea, dont you think?
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/kitchen-tools-to-keep-all-organized1/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196740" height="800" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/kitchen-tools-to-keep-all-organized1.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/kitchen-tools-to-keep-all-organized/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196741" height="400" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/kitchen-tools-to-keep-all-organized.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
         17.If you are creative enough, youve probably used a plastic lunch tray for various other purposes. If not, we have an idea for you: use it as an in-drawer organizer!
        </span>
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/organized-drawers/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196742" height="513" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/organized-drawers.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        18.Remember those old graters your grandma used to have in her kitchen? Dont you think it would be a shame to throw them away when you can turn them into beautiful pencil holders?
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/old-graters-office-organizer/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196743" height="800" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/old-graters-office-organizer.jpg" width="600"/>
          <span class="goto-gallery">
           View in gallery
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
       </figure>
       <p>
        19.Use cup holders and wall mounted cups/buckets to store pens or pencils. Manson jars will also do the trick.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.homedit.com/design-a-functional-office-in-your-kitchen/masom-jars/">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-196744" height="450" src="http://cdn.homedit.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/masom-jars.jpg" width="600"/>

           View in gallery

        20.Use a wine rack to store rolled up magazines or documents.
       </p>
       <figure class="post-image">
