      <h2 class="postTitle" title="Sony Ericsson TM506">
       Sony Ericsson TM506
      </h2>
      <p class="postInfo">
       Posted by: Sierra in

        Sony Ericsson

       <a href="http://www.cameraphonesplaza.com/category/t-mobile/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in T-Mobile">
        T-Mobile
       </a>
      </p>

       </p>

       <br/>
       The
       <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Advanced_Wireless_Services">
        Advanced Wireless Services
       </a>
       spectrum offers support to cell phones for the 1700/2100 frequency bands, enabling voice, data, video, and messaging services. In the United States T-Mobile is the one who offers this feature for the 3G network.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <a href="http://www.cameraphonesplaza.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/sony-ericsson-tm506-2.jpg">
        <center>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2677" height="345" src="http://www.cameraphonesplaza.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/sony-ericsson-tm506-2.jpg" title="sony-ericsson-tm506-2" width="370"/>
        </center>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       With support for the AWS, the telecommunication company has released a new cell phone model designed by
       <a href="http://www.sonyericsson.com">
        Sony Ericsson
       </a>
       . TM506 is the first model from Sony Ericsson with such feature and will be available next month on the market in a nice clamshell form factor with interesting Chrome with Amber or Emerald accents and an ebony gloss.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <center>
        <script type="text/javascript">
         var vclk_options = {sid:53720,media_id:6,media_type:8,version:"1.4"};
        </script>
        <script class="vclk_pub_code" src="http://cdn.fastclick.net/js/adcodes/pubcode.min.js?sid=53720&amp;media_id=6&amp;media_type=8&amp;version=1.4&amp;exc=1" type="text/javascript">
        </script>
        <noscript>
         <a href="http://media.fastclick.net/w/click.here?sid=53720&amp;m=6&amp;c=1" target="_blank">
          <img border="1" height="250" src="http://media.fastclick.net/w/get.media?sid=53720&amp;m=6&amp;tp=8&amp;d=s&amp;c=1&amp;vcm_acv=1.4" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </noscript>
       </center>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       "The TM506 not only signifies Sony Ericsson's expanding U.S. portfolio of handsets, but more importantly, that we have forged a strong and promising relationship with T-Mobile," said Najmi Jarwala, president of Sony Ericsson North America. "With the launch of the TM506 with T-Mobile, Sony Ericsson is positioned to offer more choice of mobile handsets for a broader range of U.S. consumer segments at a variety of price points."
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <a href="http://www.cameraphonesplaza.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/sony-ericsson-tm506-3.jpg">
        <center>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2678" height="345" src="http://www.cameraphonesplaza.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/sony-ericsson-tm506-3.jpg" title="sony-ericsson-tm506-3" width="370"/>
        </center>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       Features include a long lasting battery of up to 9.5 hours talk time with 250 hours of standby, a built-in 2MP camera with video recording,
       <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AGPS">
        Assisted GPS
       </a>
       for location tags on pictures, the TeleNav Navigator, RSS feeds, myFaves, Audio Postcard, Mobile Backup of the address book, Bluetooth 2.0 with
       <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A2DP#Advanced_Audio_Distribution_Profile_.28A2DP.29">
        A2DP
       </a>
       for stereo streaming, games, M2 Memory Stick slot, email, web browsing, and Instant Messaging.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <a href="http://www.cameraphonesplaza.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/sony-ericsson-tm506.jpg">
        <center>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2679" height="345" src="http://www.cameraphonesplaza.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/08/sony-ericsson-tm506.jpg" title="sony-ericsson-tm506" width="370"/>
        </center>
       </a>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       "
       <a href="http://www.t-mobile.com">
        T-Mobile
       </a>
       continues its 3G network deployment across the country, and we're pleased to leverage Sony Ericsson's 3G handset experience to help deliver the benefits of our next-generation network to our customers," said Cole Brodman, chief technology and innovation officer, T-Mobile USA. "The
       <a href="http://www.t-mobile.com/shop/phones/Cell-Phone-Detail.aspx?tp=tb1&amp;cell-phone=Sony-Ericsson-TM506-Amber">
        Sony Ericsson TM506
       </a>
       is a compelling, feature-rich phone with a vibrant display that our customers will enjoy using to connect with the important people in their lives."
