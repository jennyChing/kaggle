        <h3>
         <strong>
          10. Jaguar XJ220
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <a href="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Jaguar-XJ220.jpg">
          <img alt="Jaguar-XJ220" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2480" height="332" src="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Jaguar-XJ220.jpg" title="Jaguar-XJ220" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </h3>
        <p>
         At number 10 spot this year is the Jaguar XJ200 which is a superb and a fast car. This car is a mixture of design and speed. The top speed of this car is more than 220 mph and covers a great distance within no time. The price of this car is around $650,000. This car was made in the early 90,s but it is still in the top 10 list even in 2012.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          9. Ferrari Enzo
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/ferrari-enzo.jpg">
          <img alt="ferrari enzo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2481" height="390" src="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/ferrari-enzo.jpg" title="ferrari enzo" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Nearly 400 cars of this model have been manufactured till now which means there are only a few hundred individuals in the whole world who own this car. The top speed of the car is around 220 mph and goes to a speed of 60 miles in 4 seconds. The body and the engine are the parts which are the most noticeable in this car. The price is around $ 670,000.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          8. Ascari A10
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Ascari-A10.jpg">
          <img alt="Ascari-A10" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2482" height="400" src="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Ascari-A10.jpg" title="Ascari-A10" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This car goes to a speed of 60 miles in less than 3 seconds. The company has only produced a few of these super fast cars. It has a 625 horse power engine and the price of the car is around $ 650,000 which is the base price. The design of the car makes it worth desiring. Top speed: 220 mph.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          7. Aston Martin One-77
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/aston-martin-one-77.jpg">
          <img alt="aston-martin-one-77" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2483" height="375" src="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/aston-martin-one-77.jpg" title="aston-martin-one-77" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This car is another luxurious and a high priced car with a spectacular and powerful engine. The engine has a horse power of about 750 and the car goes to a speed of 60 miles in 3.43 seconds. The price of the car is $1,850,000. The car is the best symbol of beauty, style and power packed into one body. Top speed: 220mph.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          6. Noble M600
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Noble-M600.jpg">
          <img alt="Noble M600" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2484" height="375" src="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Noble-M600.jpg" title="Noble M600" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This car has an engine which has a number of features that are rare in many cars. The engine has a horse power of about 650 and a twin turbo charging system. The car is indeed very cool with a base price of about $330,000. The design is indeed very unique and slender. The top speed of the car is around 225 mph.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          5. Zenvo ST1
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Zenvo-ST1.jpg">
          <img alt="Zenvo-ST1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2485" height="500" src="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Zenvo-ST1.jpg" title="Zenvo-ST1" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The car goes to a speed of 60 mph in 2.9 seconds and has a top speed of about 233 mph. The engine is twin charge v8 with a horse power of 1,104. The price of this car is $ 1,125,000. It is the first super car which was made by a sports company of Denmark.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          4. Mclaren F1
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Mclaren-f1.jpg">
          <img alt="Mclaren f1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2486" height="368" src="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Mclaren-f1.jpg" title="Mclaren f1" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The price of this super car is about $ 970,000 and goes to a top speed of 240mph. it goes from 0-60 in 3.22 seconds and has a very unique and heavy engine. The doors of this car resemble the wings of a bat which makes it a flyer. It has been used in super hero movies many a times.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          3. Saleen S7 Twin Turbo
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Saleen-S7-Twin-Turbo.jpg">
          <img alt="Saleen S7 Twin Turbo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2487" height="321" src="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Saleen-S7-Twin-Turbo.jpg" title="Saleen S7 Twin Turbo" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This car has a top speed of 248 mph and a super cool engine. The car speeds to 60 miles in 2.7 seconds. It has a twin turbo engine made of aluminum with a horse power of 750. The price of this car is around $ 560,000. The entire body is smooth lined and a symbol for glamour. The performance and the user feedback of this car has been positive which made it qualify for the 3
         <sup>
          rd
         </sup>
         spot.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          2. SSC Ultimate Aero
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/SSC-Ultimate-Aero.jpg">
          <img alt="SSC Ultimate Aero" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2488" height="374" src="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/SSC-Ultimate-Aero.jpg" title="SSC Ultimate Aero" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         This car is indeed a very fast car which a top speed of 257 mph and a price of about $ 654,000. It goes from 0-60 in 2.5 seconds and has a twin turbo charging engine. It used to be the worlds fastest car till 2007 when it was dethroned by another car.
        </p>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          1.
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Bugatti Veyron Super Sport
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Bugatti-Veyron-Super-Sport.jpg">
          <img alt="Bugatti Veyron Super Sport" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2489" height="281" src="http://www.toptensthings.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/04/Bugatti-Veyron-Super-Sport.jpg" title="Bugatti Veyron Super Sport" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         At number one spot is the worlds fastest and most expensive car which is desired by the name of Bugatti. The top speed of the car is 267 mph with a 1200 hp prototype engine. The price of the car is $ 2,400,000. This car has all the best features of all other cars and is truly a master piece.
        </p>
