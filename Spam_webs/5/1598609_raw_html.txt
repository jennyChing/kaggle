              </a>
              .
             </span>
            </div>
            <div>
             <span>
              <br/>
             </span>
             The Hot Cocoa Ice Cream Cupcake is a chocolate cupcake with chocolate ice cream and marshmallow buttercream frosting. Simple but delicious. Its based on a chocolate cupcake I make a lot. A friend of mine says it tastes like hot cocoa so I thought it would make for a cute ice cream idea, going for the irony.
             <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.cupcakeproject.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
             I just started baking cupcakes two months ago so this is my first contest, Im excited. Thanks for the inspiration!
            </div>
            <div>
            </div>
            <div>
             The recipes for the cake, ice cream and frosting are below- all are homemade.
            </div>
            <div>
             <div class="zlrecipe-container-border" id="zlrecipe-container-386" style="border: 1px dotted;">
              <div class="serif zlrecipe" id="zlrecipe-container" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
               <div id="zlrecipe-innerdiv">
                <div class="item b-b">
                 <div class="zlrecipe-print-link fl-r">
                  <a class="butn-link" href="javascript:void(0);" onclick="zlrPrint('zlrecipe-container-386', 'http://www.cupcakeproject.com/wp-content/plugins/ziplist-recipe-plugin/'); return false" title="Print this recipe">
                   Print

                 <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
                  Hot Cocoa Ice Cream Cupcakes by Rachel Schill
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
                 <div class="fl-l width-50">
                 </div>
                 <div class="fl-l width-50">
                  <p id="zlrecipe-yield">
                   Yield:
                   <span itemprop="recipeYield">
                    16 regular cupcakes; 5 cups ice cream
                   </span>
                  </p>
                 </div>
                 <div class="zlclear">
                 </div>
                </div>
                <div class="img-desc-wrap">
                 <p class="t-a-c hide-card hide-print">
                  <img alt="Hot Cocoa Ice Cream Cupcakes by Rachel Schill" class="photo" itemprop="image" src="http://www.cupcakeproject.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/Hot+Cocoa+Ice+Cream+Cupcake.jpg" style="width: 250px;" title="Hot Cocoa Ice Cream Cupcakes by Rachel Schill"/>
                 </p>

                <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
                 Ingredients
                </p>
                <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
                 <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0">
                  Chocolate Cupcake Ingredients
                 </div>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1/2 cup butter
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1 1/4 cup sugar
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
                  2 large eggs
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
                  3/4 cup flour
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1/2 teaspoon baking powder
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1/4 teaspoon baking soda
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1/4 teaspoon salt
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1/2 cup milk
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1 teaspoon vanilla extract
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1 teaspoon coconut extract
                 </li>
                 <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-12">
                  Chocolate Ice Cream Ingredients
                 </div>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-13" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1 cup whole milk
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-14" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1/2 cup granulated sugar
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-15" itemprop="ingredients">
                  8 ounces semi-sweet chocolate (can also use bittersweet)
                 </li>
                 <div class="ingredient-label" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-16">
                  Marshmallow Buttercream Frosting Ingredients
                 </div>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-17" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1 1/2 cup frosting
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-18" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1/2 cup butter
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-19" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1 7oz jar marshmallow fluff
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-20" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1 cup confectioners sugar
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-21" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1/2 teaspoon vanilla bean paste
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-22" itemprop="ingredients">
                  2 cups heavy cream, well chilled
                 </li>
                 <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-23" itemprop="ingredients">
                  1 teaspoon pure vanilla bean paste
                 </li>
                </ul>
                <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
                 Instructions
                </p>
                <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
                 <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0">
                  Chocolate Cupcake Instructions
                 </div>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Beat butter until softened. Add sugar and beat until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
                 </li>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Add egg and beat until well combined.
                 </li>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Measure the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder into a small sized bowl and whisk to combine.
                 </li>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Measure out the milk, vanilla extract and coconut extract and stir to combine
                 </li>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Add about a third of the dry ingredients to the butter/sugar and beat to combine. Add about a half of the milk/vanilla/coconut and beat to combine. Continue adding, alternating between dry and wet and finishing with the dry.
                 </li>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Scoop batter into cupcake cups about 1/2 full. Bake cupcakes at 350 degrees for about 17-20 minutes or until a cake tester comes out clean.
                 </li>
                 <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7">
                  Chocolate Ice Cream Instructions
                 </div>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-8" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Heat the whole milk until it is just bubbling around the edges (this may be done on the stovetop or in a microwave).
                 </li>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-9" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  In a blender or food processor fitted with the metal blade, pulse to process the sugar with the chocolate until the chocolate is very finely chopped.
                 </li>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-10" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Add the hot milk, process until well blended and smooth. Transfer to a medium bowl and let the chocolate mixture cool completely.
                 </li>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-11" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Stir in the heavy cream and vanilla to taste. Chill for 30 minutes or longer.
                 </li>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-12" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Pour chilled mixture into ice cream machine and let mix until thickened, about 25  30 minutes.
                 </li>
                 <div class="instruction-label" id="zlrecipe-instruction-13">
                  Marshmallow Buttercream Frosting Instructions
                 </div>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-14" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Beat butter in a medium bowl with mixer on medium speed until creamy.
                 </li>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-15" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  Beat in marshmallow cream.
                 </li>
                 <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-16" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
                  When well blended, beat in confectioners' sugar and vanilla. Increase speed to high and beat 3-4 minutes until fluffy.
                 </li>
                </ol>
