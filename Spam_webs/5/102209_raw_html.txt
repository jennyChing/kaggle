          <img alt="Tuesday Ten: Coachella Essentials Mix" class="aligncenter" height="376" src="http://farm8.staticflickr.com/7052/7027886249_96a617eb6f.jpg" title="Tuesday Ten: Coachella Essentials Mix" width="500"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          At the end of this week, I will be putting on my finest hipster attire and heading out to the desert for three fun-filled days of music, dancing and other shenanigans in honor of Coachella.
         </p>
         <p>
          If you havent heard of it before, Coachella is a three-day music and arts festival that takes place just outside of Palm Springs. Everyone from The Black Keys and Miike Snow to Florence + the Machine, Avicci and Girl Talk will be performing. And I cannot wait! I havent been to Coachella yet but Ive heard nothing but good things so I am really excited to finally go. A bunch of my friends have been faithful
          <em>
           Coachellers
          </em>
          for years so I feel fortunate to be going with a group of seasoned attendees.
         </p>
         <p>
          While
          <a href="http://bit.ly/LCfestivalfashion44" target="_blank">
           packing
          </a>
          is definitely one of my #1 priorities, putting together a killer Coachella playlist is just as important if you ask my friend
          <a href="http://jillyhendrix.com/" target="_blank">
           Jilly
          </a>
          . And shes got a point. Its a
          <em>
           music
          </em>
          festival! Thankfully, Jilly has us covered with a great Coachella playlist. Whether youre heading to Coachella or not, this playlist is the perfect upbeat mix to take your through spring and summer
         </p>
         <p>
          But first, a little note from Jilly:
         </p>

          <p>

            My favorite time of the year is quickly approaching. It doesnt involve cakes and white dresses; only flowers, love, and music. Also known as Coachella. I put together a list of 10 artists Im most excited to see. I hope you enjoy!
