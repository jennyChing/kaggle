       <p>
        <img alt="The Smart Passive Income Podcast Logo" class="alignright wp-image-7433" height="180" src="http://cdn.smartpassiveincome.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/spi-artwork-rounded-200.png" title="The Smart Passive Income Podcast Logo" width="180"/>

       <p>
        In this session of The Smart Passive Income Podcast (actually, you could call it more like a course!) Im happy to welcome my good friend Clay Collins to the show!
       </p>
       <p>
        Unlike other podcast sessions where I interview others about their story and how theyve become successful, Clay and I get deep (and immediately) into tactics and strategies that will help you optimize conversions into your email list.
       </p>
       <p>
        Why is this so important?
       </p>
       <p>
        Because its much much easier to do things like double your opt-in rates than double your traffic, and over time youll find that the more people you put onto your email list, the more traffic youll eventually get too.
       </p>
       <p>
        Its an
        <strong>
         email list -&gt; traffic -&gt; email list
        </strong>
        cycle, and its the fastest way to perpetually grow your audience.
       </p>
       <p>
        This session really does feel like a course, and along with everything that Clay shares on the show, you get a ton of free downloads/giveaways from Clay to help you out, which are linked to near the bottom of this post.
       </p>
       <p>
        Clay and his team are the masterminds behind
        <a href="http://www.leadpages.net/pat" target="_blank">
         LeadPages
        </a>
        , which is my favorite marketing tool that Im using at the moment. Im currently using it to create my coming soon landing page and collect leads for my Food Truck website for Niche Site Duel 2.0, and Im also using it to quickly setup great looking webinar registration pages and thank you pages here on SPI.
       </p>
       <p>
        All of the tips and strategies we talk about implementing in this session can be done without the use of LeadPages, and in fact Clay gives away the exact HTML code for the types of pages we mention so you can start implementing right away, but Im extremely impressed with LeadPages, so much in fact that I agreed to come on as an advisor for Clays company, which Im super stoked about!
       </p>
       <p>
        I re-listened to this session after our conversation together, and I have to say that this podcast episode definitely gives all others a run for its money as far as the best and most useful podcast session to date! Ill let you be the judge of that.
        <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://cdn.smartpassiveincome.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         More specifically, in this session youll find out about:
        </strong>
        <br/>

        </span>
       </p>

        <li>
         <span style="line-height: 13px;">
          The highest leverage thing you can do today that will have the biggest impact on your business over anything else you do this month.
          <br/>
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>
         <span style="line-height: 13px;">
          Why you need to create a
          <i>
           Resource Guide Opt-in Page.
          </i>
          <br/>
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>
         <span style="line-height: 13px;">
          The highest converting landing page in Clays business and the exact headline he uses.
          <br/>
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>
         <span style="line-height: 13px;">
          Why people prefer getting tools instead of acquiring a new skill.
          <br/>
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>
         <span style="line-height: 13px;">
          Example of counter-intuitive headlines and why they work the best.
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>
         <span style="line-height: 13px;">
          How to say the right words to get people to download your opt-in Lead Magnet
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>

          Why its a huge loss when people get to a regular 404 page.
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>
         <span style="line-height: 13px;">
          Why you should give something new away with every blog post, and what kinds of things people can opt-in for.
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>
         <span style="line-height: 13px;">
          How individual posts can help you test different types of lead magnets.
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>

          Why putting on a webinar is good  even if no one shows up or generate any business for you.
          <br/>
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>
         <span style="line-height: 13px;">
          What to do on a webinar when you dont know what to do.
          <br/>
         </span>
        </li>
        <li>
         <span style="line-height: 13px;">
          How webinars are the cure-all for writers or content block.
          <br/>

          How adding more steps actually increases opt-in rates.
