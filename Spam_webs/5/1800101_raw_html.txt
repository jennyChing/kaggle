          Shopping used to be one of my hobbies. No joke, I loved wandering around cute boutiques on the weekends and trying on different pieces. Finding great seasonal separates and planning for new wardrobe investments was fun! Enter, adorable baby Lydia. She is definitely cute, but not really the best shopping companion. Obviously, my leisurely shopping trips have been shortened (or cancelled) since her arrival. While I may be spending weekends at playdates and the park instead of the mall, that doesnt mean I want to sacrifice my sense of style. I love putting together outfits, and trying on new piecesbut dont have the time to search them out.
         </p>
         <p>
          Enter my latest obsession,
          <a href="http://affiliate.trk4.com/rd/r.php?sid=7123&amp;pub=105177&amp;c1=9814">
           Golden Tote
          </a>
          .

           Golden Tote
          </a>
          is your own (affordable) personal shopping service.
          <a href="http://affiliate.trk4.com/rd/r.php?sid=7123&amp;pub=105177&amp;c1=9814">
           Golden Tote
          </a>
          s fashion fairies asked me a series of questions complete with inspiration boards about my personal style and fashion preferences. Then, the magic began. My tote arrived..and I was blown away! Ive done personal styling services before..and usually end up with one or two pieces I like. My tote was filled with 6 items..and I was in love with ALL of them! I kept squealing to my husband that I would have bought each of these items on my own.
