										&lt;p&gt;
																						&lt;p&gt;Have you ever wondered why some people seem to have all the success that they ever wanted, while others are always &#8220;wavering&#8221;, never really seeing any success at all in anything?.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;span style="color: #000000"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Why do you think that is?&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;style&gt;.video-container{text-align:center;}
  </style>
 </head>
 <body>
  <div class="video-container">
   <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" class="wistia_embed" frameborder="0" height="400" src="//fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/u06hj4rz90" width="640">
   </iframe>
  </div>
  <p>
   We see it everyday. People making massive amounts of money while others appear to be drifting, making very little or nothing at all.
  </p>
  <p>
   <span style="color: #000000">
    <strong>
     Its really down to something that most people pay very little attention to.
    </strong>
   </span>
   Its down to the way we think. Very little of what a very successful online marketer does online is down to actual skills. All skills needed to perform certain tasks online are readily available, often or not for free.
  </p>
  <p>
   Its not that people cant have, or dont have the Skillset to be successful, very few people actually have the Mindset. This is where most people miss out. They are not working on their mind. DAILY..
  </p>
  <p>
   Its not just about reading books, watching videos or attending webinars. Dont you think most unsuccessful people do this too?. Its actually about putting what you learn into action.
  </p>
  <p>
   You can have great knowledge about anything but if you keep it locked up in your head all your life, very little will ever come of it. Its not the person who has the most knowledge who wins, its the people who learn, so and share that make all the money.
  </p>
  <p>
   In the video above you will hear me talk about 3 vital elements to your success which you need to have going on for you on a daily basis. Success in anything is often a few steps repeated daily, and success online is no exception.
  </p>
  <p>
   98% of people online fail to make money. Why is this?. Its certainly NOT that they cant do it, because they can. Its because they sont stick to the three things Im talking about in the video. Belief, in themselves and their company. Taking action on what they learn and sticking at it until they are successful.
  </p>
  <p>
   <span style="color: #000000">
    <strong>
     Most people quit before they get to the Gold.
    </strong>
   </span>
   This is a common problem online, people give up three feet away from success.
  </p>
  <p>
   Stick with people who are going places. Learn from people who are already successful, and are willing to share that knowledge with you.
  </p>
  <p>
   And best of all, become that person that you admire online. People who give the most, tend to make the most. They learn, they go out and do, then they teach others.
  </p>

  </p>
