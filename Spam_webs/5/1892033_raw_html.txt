         </p>
         <div>
          <blockquote>
           <h5>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             CHARGE(S)
            </span>
           </h5>
          </blockquote>
         </div>
         <div>
          <blockquote>
           <div>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #000000;">
              <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
               Count:
              </span>
              <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
               1
              </span>
              Charge Code:
              <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
               28-1381A1
               <br/>
              </span>
              Charge Description:DUI LIQUOR/DRUGS/VAPORS/COMBO
             </span>
            </p>
           </div>
           <hr/>
           <div>
            <div>
             <span style="color: #000000;">
              Count:
              <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
               2
              </span>
              Charge Code:
              <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
               28-1381A2
              </span>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div>
             <span style="color: #000000;">
              Charge Description:DUI W/BAC .08 OR MORE
             </span>
            </div>
           </div>
           <hr/>
           <div>
            <div>
             <span style="color: #000000;">
              Count:
              <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
               3
              </span>
              Charge Code:
              <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
               28-1382A1
              </span>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div>
             <span style="color: #000000;">
              Charge Description:EXTREME DUI BAC .15 -.19 1ST
             </span>
            </div>
           </div>
           <hr/>
           <div>
            <div>
             <span style="color: #000000;">
              Count:
              <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
               4
              </span>
              Charge Code:
              <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
               28-1382A2
              </span>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div>
             <span style="color: #000000;">
              Charge Description:EXTREME DUI BAC &gt;= .20 1ST
             </span>
            </div>
           </div>
           <hr/>
          </blockquote>
          <div>
           <blockquote>
            <div>
             <span style="color: #000000;">
              Count:
              <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
               5
              </span>
              Charge Code:
              <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
               28-4135C
              </span>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div>
             <span style="color: #000000;">
              Charge Description:FAIL TO PROD EVID FIN RESP (1ST)
             </span>
            </div>
           </blockquote>
           <div>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             This is the translation:
            </span>
           </div>
           <div>
            <ol>
             <li>
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               you drove drunk
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               You must have been drunk because of some magic arbitrary number equal to that second beer.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               You were drunk under the old measurements, before the insurance companies rewrote DUI laws to inflate their profits.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               Ok, yes, you were numerically all fucked up.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               And fuck you by the way, for being too drunk to find your proof of insurance, probably because it was too blurry to read.
              </span>
             </li>
            </ol>
           </div>
           <div>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
              Ive s
             </span>
             <span style="letter-spacing: 0.05em; line-height: 1.6875;">
              at on a Colorado DUI jury. There, it was not which charge applies, it was how many. Evidently, if the jury believes the BAC accurately measured &gt; 0.2, thats 3 DUI guilties without factually determining the guy was drunk driving. No discretion to vote otherwise (unless youve heard of juror nullification) And, these charges could all have dropped on a guy simply sleeping it off in an alley in his car.
             </span>
            </span>
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div>
            <span style="color: #000000;">
             [
             <em>
              Ed.: Obviously not the case because theres no gratuitous parking ticket thrown on there.
             </em>
             ]
            </span>
           </div>
           <div>
           </div>
           <div>
            <div>
             <span style="color: #000000;">
              Make no mistake, drunk driving is bad, and should not be
              <a href="http://www.messengerpuppet.com/2004/04/20/stupid-drunken-things-not-to-do-parts-17-22/" title="Rockin Robin">
               <span style="color: #000000;">
                treated like a sport
               </span>
              </a>
              . Setting aside all our moralizing about how its just a species of reckless driving, unless someone gets hurt, lets get real. Four DUI charges?
              <strong>
               Go fuck yourselves, cops
              </strong>
              . This is just a money grab. The only surprise is that impaired driving was not thrown in there too. Maybe the cop ran out of room on the ticket book.
             </span>
            </div>
