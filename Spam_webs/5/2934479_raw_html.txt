         <h4>
          Check out The Cork For Covington Georgias Specialty Wine and Beer!
         </h4>
         <a id="dd_start">
         </a>
         <p>
          <a href="http://gocovington.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/thecork.png">
           <img alt="The Corks Specialty Wine" class="alignleft wp-image-5878" height="156" src="http://gocovington.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/thecork-300x198.png" width="236"/>
          </a>
          December is the perfect time of year to enjoy a relaxing glass of wine. If you are in the market for just that, check out The Cork in downtown Covington Georgia. It is conveniently located on Church St. in Covington. It is home to Georgias specialty wine, craft beer, cigars and gourmet food. It is family-owned and operated and opened in October of 2013.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Cork is a unique specialty store in Covington where you will always be greeted with a welcoming smile as soon as you enter. The owners are always open to questions and will likely have the answers you are looking for. Not only do they offer specialty wines and craft beers, but local wines and craft beers from Covington, too.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://gocovington.com/vampire-diaries-series-cork-boutique-gifts/" title="The Vampire Diaries Series- The Cork Boutique and Gifts">
           The Cork is home to a variety of wine, craft beer, cigars and gourmet food
          </a>
          . Their selection could keep you busy all day, and the prices vary from under $10 to more expensive, however, the quality of the wine will be excellent no matter what the price. The same is true for The Corks craft beer. They hold tastings and events quite often in Covington; while also serving samples to customers who are unsure about what they should take home.
         </p>
         <p>
          The Cork is the perfect way to spend a day experimenting new wines and craft beers. You never know what your favorite is until you try, so stop by and try The Corks specialties! Overall, The Cork is a fun, exciting experience to take your time and pick out a quality bottle of wine or a fine craft beer; and dont forget  before you leave, be sure to check out their gourmet food! It will go perfectly with your new bottle of wine or craft beer!
         </p>
