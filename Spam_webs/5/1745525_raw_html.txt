Last year we forgot to exchange them altogether though so Im hoping I can reuse? The year before, we gave each other the same funny card. At least we are on the same page and we get each other. That matters, right? Sense of humor is everything.

        <div style="text-align: justify;">
         Who cares. It tastes phenomenal. Thats what counts! These sweet vanilla crepes are spiked with a little champagne. The flavor is subtle, but definitely adds extra dimension. The white chocolate mousse filling is super simple, and something youll want to make all on its own to eat spoonful by massive spoonful. Its light and fluffy, but decadent with lots of white chocolate deliciousness. And the sauce fresh strawberries cooked with sugar and champagne until they break down and release their perfectly fruity juices!
        </div>

         This dish is easier than it looks, but your Valentine doesnt need to know that. These crepes are perfect for a Valentines Day breakfast or dessert!

         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          Fresh Strawberries &amp; Champagne sauce spooned over crepes filled with an easy White Chocolate Mousse!
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSAuthor">
           Author:
           <span itemprop="author">
            Sarah @ The Gold Lining Girl
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSCategory">
           Recipe type:
           <span itemprop="recipeCategory">
            Dessert, Breakfast
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSServes">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            8-10 crepes (4 servings)
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Strawberries &amp; Champagne Sauce:
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 - 16 oz. container fresh strawberries, washed and sliced
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 tbsp. sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             c. champagne
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             tsp. almond extract
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Crepes:
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 eggs
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 c. milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             c. water
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             c. champagne
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tsp. vanilla
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 c. flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            6 tbsp. melted butter, slightly cooled
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             c. sugar
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           White Chocolate Mousse Filling:
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             c. white chocolate chips
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 oz. cream cheese, softened
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 c. whipped topping
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Directions
          </div>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           For Strawberries &amp; Champagne Sauce:
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a large pot, combine all ingredients and cook on medium-high heat, bringing to a boil.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Reduce heat to low, and simmer for 15 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove from heat, and allow to cool until ready to serve it over the crepes.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           For Crepes:
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and whisk until smooth.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a large saucepan over medium heat, pour about  -  c. batter, covering the entire bottom of the pan in a thin layer.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until the top looks mostly cooked too, even before flipping. Carefully (it will be thin and delicate) flip the crepe over, and cook for about 30 seconds.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove crepe from pan, and continue making crepes with remaining batter.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           For White Chocolate Mousse Filling:
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a small saucepan over low-medium heat, melt the chocolate chips until smooth. Cool to tepid.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Meanwhile, in a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Gradually add the cooled chocolate to the mixing bowl, and beat until thoroughly combined and smooth.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Fold in whipped topping.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            To assemble crepes, spoon or pipe 3-4 tbsp. of white chocolate mousse across the diameter of each crepe, and roll them into 1-inch tubes. Serve 2-3 crepes per person with about  c. sauce spooned over each serving.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSNotesDiv">
          <div class="ERSNotesHeader">
           Notes
          </div>
          <div class="ERSNotes">
           If not serving immediately, the crepes keep well in the refrigerator for several days, stacked between plastic wrap lightly greased with non-stick spray.
           <br/>
           The strawberry sauce also keeps for 3-4 days. All the components can be prepared ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator until ready to assemble and serve!
          </div>
