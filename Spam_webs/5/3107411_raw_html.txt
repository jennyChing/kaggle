            <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
             Make a Statement with Branded Stationery
            </h1>
            <div class="entry-meta entry-meta-above">
             Posted on
             <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2014-10-01T10:30:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
              October 1, 2014
             </time>
             by
             <span class="entry-author vcard" itemprop="author" itemptype="http://schema.org/Person" itemscope="">
              <a class="author-link fn nickname url" href="http://rabidofficemonkey.com/author/guest-author/" itemprop="url" title="View all posts by Contributor">
               <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
                Contributor
               </span>
              </a>
             </span>
             
             <a class="entry-comments" href="http://rabidofficemonkey.com/make-a-statement-with-branded-stationery/#comments" title="Make a Statement with Branded Stationery  Comments">
              0 Comments
             </a>
            </div>
            <!-- .entry-meta -->
           </header>
           <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
            <div id="triberr_endorsement">
            </div>
            <p>
             There are hundreds, no thousands of little details that go into running your business, so it is fairly easy to overlook something as trivial as branded stationery. However, is branded stationery really as unimportant as it seems? Putting your brand on stationery can actually be a worthwhile investment for your business. From notepads to pens, the right design can leave a positive impression with clients and vendors. So, why should you start plastering your brand across sticky notes, calendars and erasers?
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Strengthen Your Company Identity
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Potential clients, current clients and vendors are all working with limited amounts of time and money, much the same you, so these people dont want to waste resources on an unprofessional organization. You might be an expert in your field, offering only the best service and products, but promoting your expertise can be difficult if you do not have a strong company identity.
            </p>
            <p>
             Branding stationery can project a public identity that is professional and competent, and your clients will automatically connect that identity to your outstanding products and services. A clever, professional stationery design can provide a unifying image that will connect all aspects of your companys identity.
            </p>
            <p>
             Dont forget your employees either. A consistent and professional stationery design is great  but taking the next step and utilising high quality stationery means that your employees will want to actually use it. Having your employees endorse your business identity can lead to a unified, healthy, and happy workplace, not to mention walking advertisements for your brand. Think of all those client meetings, commuting hours and phone call doodles that could be used as slight, but effective marketing for your brand.
            </p>
            <p>
             Attractive and functional stationery can forge a strong sense of company identity. Unfortunately, the opposite is true for stationery designs that look cheap and unattractive. Having a bad brand can be just as debilitating as having no brand at all, so it is important to ensure professional and attractive branded stationery targets your audience.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Almost Effortless Networking
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             We all know that building your network of clients and vendors can require time, money, and creativity. However, making small inroads can be as simple as dispersing branded stationery; it requires very little input from you.
            </p>
            <p>
             Have a little think about popular hang-outs around your office  leave stationery in popular common areas, such as the local caf, book store and grocery shop or you can actively give away branded pens, notepads, notebooks, and more to friends, family and colleagues.
            </p>
            <p>
             After the stationery reaches your clients and vendors, your company image will be viewed by friends, friends of friends, family members and business associates. The humble pen might not seem like a major marketing tool, but placing your professional brand on a pen that writes flawlessly or a notepad thats used daily can actually leave a positive and lasting impression.
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              First Impressions
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             We all know that first impressions last, so its obvious that the first impression is the most important impression. If youve already gone down the branded stationery route, remember you cannot always undo the damage that is caused by cheap stationery, bad designs, and an underdeveloped company identity. However, you can begin to make amends.
            </p>
            <p>
             Simple follow these golden rules for branding stationery:
            </p>
            <ol>
             <li>
              Always use only the highest quality base products  this means pens that right well, high quality paper and notepads that wont fall apart
             </li>
             <li>
              Never (well almost never) use novelty stationery  if you want your company to look professional and convey a sense of trustworthiness then utilise stationery that can be used during day to day business. Additionally, novelty stationery rarely works well
             </li>
             <li>
              Provide a unique URL  Put a distinct URL on the stationery so you can track visits to your website off the back of the stationery distribution
             </li>
             <li>
              Brand an entire suite of stationery  including pens, notepads, A4 paper, erasers, sticky notes and desktop calendars. The more products with your brand on them, the more exposure youll get
             </li>
            </ol>
            <p>
             Branding stationery is a simple and effective method of marketing your business and staying front of mind with clients.
            </p>
            <p>
             The benefits discussed above are just a summary of the major overall benefits of branding stationery, but your business is sure to benefit in a wide variety of ways. Adding a professional touch should never be overlooked, and branding stationery is definitely worth the time and money.
            </p>
