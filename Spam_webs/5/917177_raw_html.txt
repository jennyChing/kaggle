      <h1>
       Los Angeles Car Accident Lawyer
      </h1>
      <h2>
       Injured in a Los Angeles Car Accident?
      </h2>
      <p>
       Because California is a densely populated state with millions of cars, there are many personal injury law firms that focus on motor vehicle crashes and
       <a href="http://www.lntlb.com/PracticeAreas/Pedestrian-Accidents.htm" title="pedestrian accidents">
        pedestrian accidents
       </a>
       . However, few law firms have the background that we do.
      </p>
      <img alt="Los Angeles Motor Vehicle Accident Lawyers" class="left" src="/images/auto-banner.jpg"/>
      <p>
       Our two lead attorneys were once insurance company lawyers. Now they are on your side. After many years of defending insurance companies in disputes, they now focus all of their attention on helping you get justice and compensation for your injuries. Our law firm has a long history of success and has helped people like you throughout California recover millions of dollars in compensation. If you have been injured anywhere in Southern California as a result of a drivers negligence or reckless behavior,call310-312-1860 for a free consultation.
      </p>
      <p>
       We handle cases of the greatest complexity that involve catastrophic injuries: burns, paralysis, and other serious injuries. But we also have a long history of getting our clients maximum compensation in cases where injuries may be less severe but still challenging. No matter the severity of your vehicle collision, we can help you determine if you have a case. We accept cases on a contingent fee basis which means we collect nothing unless we win. Whether through settlement or judgments, we have helped people throughout California recover millions of dollars in compensation after a vehicle crash.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Don't Let an Insurance Company Take Advantage of you
      </h2>
      <p>
       David Lederer and John Nojima will defend your rights against the insurance company. Our attorneys used to be on the side of such companies in auto accident cases. Both Lederer and Nojima worked for massive insurance corporations before they founded their own firm. Defending the insurance company, trying to avoid paying claims, questioning valid requests for benefits:Lederer &amp; Nojimaused to do this. Not anymore.
      </p>
      <p>
       Now they fight for people involved in car accidents. Because of their background in the insurance industry, they know how the insurance companies work. They know how the insurance company will try to avoid paying claims. If you have been involved in any kind of motor vehicle accident and need a lawyer who knows insurance company tactics and how to fight them, reach out to our law firm.
      </p>
      <p>
       From the time of your free initial consultation, we will gather information about your car accident and begin determining the cause: driver error, auto manufacturer negligence, inattention by the other driver, texting, excessive speed, poor road conditions,poorly marked construction zones, or a wide-variety of other causes that may have led to your injury or loved one's
       <a href="/PracticeAreas/Wrongful-Death.htm">
        wrongful death
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       If you have been injured as the result of a car accident,contact Los Angeles car accident attorney now. You deserve compensation. And remember: if we dont win, you dont pay.
       <a href="http://www.lntlb.com/CM/Espanol/Bienvenidos.htm" title="Se habla espaol">
        Se habla espaol
