                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ekoxBNgbDbk/U5dkWmYU_nI/AAAAAAAAE5Y/ugLWkto7yAw/s1600/1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="640" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ekoxBNgbDbk/U5dkWmYU_nI/AAAAAAAAE5Y/ugLWkto7yAw/s1600/1.jpg" width="426"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div align="justify">
                     </div>
                     <div align="justify">
                      <br/>
                      This recipe comes from my Grandmother Diane. She gave it to me at one of my bridal showers oh so many years ago and told me that she had spent quite a lot of time getting it just right. When a Grandma gives you their special "just right" recipe, you make sure you use it and don't lose it!
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-l76TYAyQ5NE/U5hxJ-HG6kI/AAAAAAAAE7c/qVivj6uyDo0/s1600/4.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="640" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-l76TYAyQ5NE/U5hxJ-HG6kI/AAAAAAAAE7c/qVivj6uyDo0/s1600/4.jpg" width="426"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div style="text-align: center;">
                       Grandma Diane
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      When my Grandma was a young mom like me, she and my Grandfather moved their family of five to Australia to try their hand at farming in the Outback. It was during this time in her life that she made a lot of her special No Bake Cookies. Here are a few pictures from their time there.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-NhwYTO1ARfU/U5hxGsUeawI/AAAAAAAAE7M/G4ABH7gsDgY/s1600/pic3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-NhwYTO1ARfU/U5hxGsUeawI/AAAAAAAAE7M/G4ABH7gsDgY/s1600/pic3.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       Grandpa on the farm.
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ua7MxSj7fII/U5hxLHHL2zI/AAAAAAAAE7g/dDNobCD4bt4/s1600/pic4.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       </a>
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ua7MxSj7fII/U5hxLHHL2zI/AAAAAAAAE7g/dDNobCD4bt4/s1600/pic4.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-ua7MxSj7fII/U5hxLHHL2zI/AAAAAAAAE7g/dDNobCD4bt4/s1600/pic4.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       My Dad ready to go for a swim.
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ihiAQGrmoao/U5hxOo375jI/AAAAAAAAE7s/Vd7LeuirTEg/s1600/pic5.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ihiAQGrmoao/U5hxOo375jI/AAAAAAAAE7s/Vd7LeuirTEg/s1600/pic5.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       Their Aussie home.
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-NXg0ucgcDVs/U5hxI790eCI/AAAAAAAAE7U/zoMjYttK0wE/s1600/pic2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-NXg0ucgcDVs/U5hxI790eCI/AAAAAAAAE7U/zoMjYttK0wE/s1600/pic2.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <div align="center">
                       My uncle Kevin (little guy crouched down)with some friends and a wallaby.
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      My Grandma told me that while they were living there she avoided using the oven as much as possible since it would get so warm in the house. Because of this, she made No Bake Cookies quite often and had lots of opportunities to perfect her recipe. As an Arizonian, I love having this recipe on hand! It is quick and easy and also keeps
                      <i>
                       my
                      </i>
                      desert kitchen cool in the summer. This recipe is not all that different from many No Bake Cookie recipes you'll find on the internet, but I have made it many times and I knowit's a winner! I think Grandma got the ingredient amounts just right.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-0bGPu3mI-xE/U5dkX1b9PgI/AAAAAAAAE5g/KZ_dtu9kPkA/s1600/2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-0bGPu3mI-xE/U5dkX1b9PgI/AAAAAAAAE5g/KZ_dtu9kPkA/s1600/2.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <b>
                       <u>
                        Ingredients:
                       </u>
                      </b>
                      <br/>
                      2 c. sugar
                      <br/>
                      2 T. cocoa
                      <br/>
                      1/4 c. butter
                      <br/>
                      1/2 c. milk
                      <br/>
                      1/2 c. peanut butter
                      <br/>
                      1 t. vanilla
                      <br/>
                      3 c. quick cooking oats
                     </div>
                     <div align="justify">
                      <br/>
                      Step 1 - Put your oats, peanut butter, and vanilla together in a large bowl and set aside.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-NaoAukldzTc/U5dlPuafrKI/AAAAAAAAE54/xj76hIZ_R7E/s1600/IMG_5044.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-NaoAukldzTc/U5dlPuafrKI/AAAAAAAAE54/xj76hIZ_R7E/s1600/IMG_5044.JPG" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      Step 2 - Combine sugar, cocoa, butter, and milk in a saucepan and heat until boiling on the stove. Keep stirring to prevent burning while the mixture heats up.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-rnU0sHsu_j8/U5dlJSe7eeI/AAAAAAAAE5w/2CCksRUl-Qg/s1600/IMG_5048.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-rnU0sHsu_j8/U5dlJSe7eeI/AAAAAAAAE5w/2CCksRUl-Qg/s1600/IMG_5048.JPG" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-E0RXK0bTN-4/U5dlYsskF_I/AAAAAAAAE6A/yZCf1JK1tt0/s1600/IMG_5051.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-E0RXK0bTN-4/U5dlYsskF_I/AAAAAAAAE6A/yZCf1JK1tt0/s1600/IMG_5051.JPG" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      Step 3 - Pour the heated mixture into the large bowl containing the rest of the ingredients and mix well.
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-f3bdIACV-wQ/U5dkZZ2jevI/AAAAAAAAE5o/C-nz_rWwaxw/s1600/3.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-f3bdIACV-wQ/U5dkZZ2jevI/AAAAAAAAE5o/C-nz_rWwaxw/s1600/3.jpg" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-lQUHor89auQ/U5dmF4jzJqI/AAAAAAAAE6I/XwkU4QORKTc/s1600/IMG_5060.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-lQUHor89auQ/U5dmF4jzJqI/AAAAAAAAE6I/XwkU4QORKTc/s1600/IMG_5060.JPG" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-HjC1ldnh65U/U5dmIsPEaJI/AAAAAAAAE6Y/GBTjSZI_RdE/s1600/IMG_5061.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-HjC1ldnh65U/U5dmIsPEaJI/AAAAAAAAE6Y/GBTjSZI_RdE/s1600/IMG_5061.JPG" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      Step 4 - Drop the cookie mixture by the spoonful onto waxed paper lined cookie sheetsand shape to your desired size. At this point you can simply let your cookies cool on the counterand then eat, but we like to put ours right into the freezer and keep them frozen until we are ready to enjoy them. Yum!
                      <br/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-kNVLWMWPeaE/U5dmG_SKvDI/AAAAAAAAE6Q/6B8rKdEZpqM/s1600/IMG_5064.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" height="426" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-kNVLWMWPeaE/U5dmG_SKvDI/AAAAAAAAE6Q/6B8rKdEZpqM/s1600/IMG_5064.JPG" width="640"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      <br/>
                      I hope you enjoy this classic summertime treat! Thanks for stopping by!
