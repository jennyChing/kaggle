          <img alt="Rory Stewart MP features in BBC Radio 4s Book of the Week" class="aligncenter full-media nomargin" src="http://www.cumbriacrack.com/wp-content/themes/stylebook/timthumb.php?src=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.cumbriacrack.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2011%2F05%2FOxtravels-Cover-300x300.jpg&amp;q=90&amp;w=795&amp;zc=1" title="Rory Stewart MP features in BBC Radio 4s Book of the Week"/>
          <p>
           <span style="color: #800000;">
            <strong>
             Rory Stewarts short travel story, The Wrestler  part of the recently-published Oxfam travel anthology Ox Travels  is to feature in next weeks Book of the Week slot on BBC Radio 4. Rorys story will be read on Friday June 3rd.
             <a href="http://www.cumbriacrack.com/2011/05/26/rory-stewart-mp-features-in-bbc-radio-4s-book-of-the-week/oxtravels-cover/" rel="attachment wp-att-4605">
              <img alt="" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-4605" height="300" src="http://www.cumbriacrack.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Oxtravels-Cover-300x300.jpg" title="Oxtravels-Cover" width="300"/>
             </a>
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Ox Travels is a paperback published to support the charitys work, bringing together 36 contemporary travel writers in less than 500 pages. Rorys story features alongside such celebrated travel writers as Colin Thubron, William Dalrymple, Paul Theroux and Patrick Leigh Fermor.
          </p>
          <p>
           The book can be bought at Penriths Oxfam, and is on sale for 8.99.
          </p>
          <p>
           All proceeds will go to Oxfams work fighting global poverty.
          </p>
