       <p>
        <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva;">
         <strong>
          Old Trafford
         </strong>
         <strong>
          Stadium
         </strong>
         is a football ground which is one of the biggest stadiums of football. It is the home ground of English Football club
         <strong>
          Manchester United
         </strong>
         . This is not only used for football matches but also for Concerts, stage performance etc. It is also called as the
         <b>
          Theatre of Dreams.

       <h2>
        <span style="color: #3366ff; font-size: large; font-family: verdana,geneva;">
         Old Trafford history
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva;">
         <a href="http://www.footballwood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/oldt1980.jpg">
          <img alt="oldt1980" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2627" height="326" src="http://www.footballwood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/oldt1980.jpg" width="581"/>
         </a>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva;">
         A Scottish architect named
         <strong>
          Archibald Leitch
         </strong>
         designed this &amp; also designed more grounds. When he started its design then total cost was expected to be
         <strong>
          60,000 pounds
         </strong>
         . But when the construction proceeds, cost also raise &amp; and extra cost of 30,000 pounds spent again. Initially stadium was constructed for 1Lakh capacity unfortunately capacity dropped down to approximately
         <strong>
          80,000
         </strong>
         .
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva;">
         First match between
         <strong>
          Red Devils
         </strong>
         and
         <strong>
          Liverpool
         </strong>
         was played on
         <strong>
          19-02-1910
         </strong>
         . It was not a good opening for the home team &amp; they lost by 3-4 against Liverpool. Trafford was so eye catching that a journalist said The ground is most handsome most remarkable &amp; spacious arena I have ever visited.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva;">
         When Englands biggest stadium
         <strong>
          Wembley
         </strong>
         was under construction, it got first chance to organize the final matches of
         <strong>
          FA Cup of 1923.
         </strong>
         During Second World War, it was requisitioned by forces of military to be used as depot. On
         <strong>
          22
          <sup>
           nd
          </sup>
          December 1940
         </strong>
         , German bombard on Trafford &amp; damaged the stadium. Thereafter football continued to play on 8-03-1941 but one more raid of German on 11-03-1941 &amp; was destroyed badly. The Arena went for reconstruction during year of 1990, 2000. During the period of reconstruction, home club played its match at Maine Road.
        </span>
       </p>
       <h2>
        <span style="font-size: large; color: #3366ff; font-family: verdana,geneva;">
         Records
        </span>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <span style="font-family: verdana,geneva;">
         <a href="http://www.footballwood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Records.jpg">

        </span>
       </p>

        </span>
       </p>
