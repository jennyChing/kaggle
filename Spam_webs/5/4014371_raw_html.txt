        <p>
         <img align="bottom" alt="A week away from TV" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.marcandangel.com/images/tv-free-week.jpg?resize=464%2C190"/>

        <p>
         What could you accomplish if you stopped watching TV? What would you do with all the extra time? My wife asked me these questions two weeks ago. After a short discussion, we decided to find out for ourselves. We completely eliminated television from our lives last week. It turned out to be an incredibly rewarding experience. Wenow have a plan to drastically reduce our future viewing habits.
        </p>
        <p>
         Here are 40 positive effects from our week without television.
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Wrote4 blog posts.
          </strong>
           I usually only have timeto write2 articles a week.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Made it to the gym 5 times.
          </strong>
           I went Monday through Friday for about 45 minutes, hitting every major muscle group.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Increased household communication.
          </strong>
           TV kills the flow of household communication. We both noticed that we had a lot more time to talk.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Read a novel cover to cover.
          </strong>
           I read the short novel 
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/redirect.html?ie=UTF8&amp;location=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.amazon.com%2FSiddhartha-Hermann-Hesse%2Fdp%2F0553208845%3Fie%3DUTF8%26s%3Dbooks%26qid%3D1217212693%26sr%3D8-2&amp;tag=marandang-20&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325">
           Siddhartha
          </a>
          <img border="0" height="1" src="http://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=marandang-20&amp;l=ur2&amp;o=1" style="margin: 0px; border: medium none" width="1"/>
           by Herman Hesse. Its a powerful story about the importance of life experiences as they relate to approaching an understanding of reality and attaining enlightenment. I highly recommend it.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Caught up on current events twice as fast.
          </strong>
           I typically waste a good hour everyday watching CNN. This week I grabbed my news off the web. It took me significantly less time to scan CNN.com.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Enhanced focus on everything.
          </strong>
           TV is a huge distraction, even when you hear it from the other room. Its so much easier to concentrate when you dont have a TV yapping at you. I had no idea how much if affected my ability to concentrate until it was gone.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Learned a few new skills.
          </strong>
           I took
          <a href="http://www.marcandangel.com/2008/06/02/50-things-everyone-should-know-how-to-do/" title="50 Things Everyone Should Know How To Do">
           my own advice
          </a>
          and learned how to change the oil in my truck pretty darn easy.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Spent more time with friends and family.
          </strong>
           We invited Angels sister and some friends over for a mid-week dinner party.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Cleaned the house.
          </strong>
           Something Angel and I habitually evade by watching TV instead.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Learned new recipes and prepared 5 home cooked meals.
          </strong>
           We cooked large portions so we could have leftovers for lunch every day.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Spent more time enjoying simple pleasures.
          </strong>
           Deep conversations, long walks, telling jokes, etc.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Finally trimmed the hedges in our front yard.
          </strong>
           I always put this off because I am tired after mowing the lawn. This time, however, I did it on a totally separate evening. It only took me 30 minutes.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Cleaned out the garage and sold 2 old dehumidifiers on eBay.
          </strong>
           We setup a 5-day eBay auction and got $65 for each one of them!
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Took 2 evening strolls around a local park.
          </strong>
           We hadnt been to this park in years theres only 1 reason why.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Saved money on our electric bill.
          </strong>
           Our power companys website allows you to check your daily usage. Sure enough, less TV equals less electricity usage.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Burned more calories.
          </strong>
           We were off the couch moving around.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Backed-up critical files on both of our laptops.
          </strong>
           Transferred them all over to our
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/redirect.html?ie=UTF8&amp;location=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.amazon.com%2FIomega-Desktop-Drive-Hi-Speed-External%2Fdp%2FB000HI9O5W%3Fie%3DUTF8%26s%3Delectronics%26qid%3D1217012890%26sr%3D8-1&amp;tag=marandang-20&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325">
           Iomega 500 GB external drive
          </a>
          <img border="0" height="1" src="http://www.assoc-amazon.com/e/ir?t=marandang-20&amp;l=ur2&amp;o=1" style="margin: 0px; border: medium none" width="1"/>
          .
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           I played my guitar.
          </strong>
           for the first time in a year. Im hooked again!
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Found 2 new bands I like.
          </strong>
           I used online
          <a href="http://www.marcandangel.com/2007/05/08/find-similar-artists-or-bands-based-on-music-you-like/" title="Find Similar Artists or Bands Based on Music You Like">
           music comparisonservices
          </a>
          to find 2 awesome bands with similar music styles to my favorite band, Counting Crows.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Listened to more music.
          </strong>
           In addition to finding new bands, I had more time to listen to my favorite music.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           We stargazed in our backyard.
          </strong>
           for the first time since Angel and I first started dating. It was blissful.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Caught up on times with an old friend.
          </strong>
           I hadnt spoken to my buddy Jon since my wedding a year and a half ago. We had a pleasant 30 minute conversation on the phone.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Handled basic household maintenance.
          </strong>
           Replaced the AC filter, applied pest control spray, replaced fire alarm batteries, etc.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Took Angel out on a romantic date.
          </strong>
           I took her to one of our favorite Italian restaurants and then out to a local pub where we slow danced to a live music.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Finished up a little DIY project.
          </strong>
           Ive been stalling on staining, sanding and hanging a certain wooden shelf for nearly 6 months.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Updated the photo frames throughout the house.
          </strong>
           Sometimes its nice to mix things up. We spent some time sorting through our digital photos and replaced a few photos in various photo frames throughout the house.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Played racquetball.
          </strong>
           Great 2-person sport!
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Organized my monthly planner.
          </strong>
           Entered all my known commitments into my planner and did a little time planning each night.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Reviewed our long-term goals.
          </strong>
           Angel and I had an open discussion last Tuesday night about our goals for the next 3  5 years.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Setup recurring automatic payments for most of our bills.
          </strong>
           Now our electricity, phone, insurance and cable bills are automatically withdrawn from our account each month. This basically buys me 30 additional minutes of free time every month.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Updated my resume.
          </strong>
           It only took me 45 minutes.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Quiet relaxation.
          </strong>
           Instead of clicking on the TV, I spent some quiet time gathering my thoughts each day when I returned home from work.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Intelligent conversations at suppertime.
          </strong>
           Quite frequently we eat supper in front of the TV. Since there was no TV watching this week, Angel and I had some really interesting conversations instead.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Hit it off with our new neighbors.
          </strong>
           We spent more time outside and ended up running into our new neighbors. They seem like pretty cool people. Theres certainly the potential for friendship here.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Exchanged back massages.
          </strong>
           It sure beats canned laughter.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           There was more time for quality time.
          </strong>
           You know just me and her and some classical music in the background.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           More real life experiences.
          </strong>
           Because we were off of the couch, actually doing something.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Watched 1 quality movie.
          </strong>
           Instead of watching whatever was on, we went to Blockbuster and selected 1 quality movie to rent.
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           Ironed my clothes each weeknight before bedtime.
          </strong>
           since I had more free time in the evenings. It made my
          <a href="http://www.marcandangel.com/2008/03/26/reduce-morning-stress-in-11-simple-steps/" title="Reduce Morning Stress in 11 Simple Steps">
           mornings less stressful
          </a>
          .
         </li>
         <li>
          <strong>
           We got more done.
          </strong>
           Because we had so much more time to do it all.
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         I strongly recommend a week without television to everyone. Youll be amazed
        </p>
