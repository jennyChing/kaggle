           <h1 class="entry-title margin-bottom-30">
            6 geographical facts youre not going to believe
           </h1>
           <div class="entry-meta margin-bottom-30 hidden-print" style="display:none">
            <span class="posted-on">
             <time class="entry-date published pull-left" datetime="2010-02-22T15:21:55+00:00">
              February 22, 2010
             </time>
             <time class="updated hidden" datetime="2015-02-18T10:20:19+00:00">
              February 18, 2015
             </time>
            </span>
            <span class="byline">
             <span class="author vcard">
              <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.zmescience.com/author/osamik/" rel="author">
               Mihai Andrei
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>

          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            Our world is a strange and awesome place  but some things are just hard to believe! Here are 6 of them:
           </p>
           <h3>
            Canada has more lakes than the rest of the world combined
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="canada-lake" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2930" height="466" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/canada-lake.jpg" title="canada-lake" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Canada is the second largest country on the face of the Earth, but USAs northern neighbor has a lot to brag about.Out of all the natural lakes in the world, more than 50 percent are situated in Canada.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <img alt="canada-lake-2" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-2931" height="369" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/canada-lake-2-1024x768.jpg" title="canada-lake-2" width="491"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            According to Wikipedia,
            <em>
             More than 60 percent of the worlds lakes are in Canada.
            </em>
            I wasnt able to find the total number, but considering that only Finland has187 888 lakes (thanks Arttu), the number must be huge.
           </p>
           <p>
            Update: Found it!The number of lakes larger than three square kilometres is estimated at close to 31,752 by the
            <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlas_of_Canada" title="Atlas of Canada">
             Atlas of Canada
            </a>
            , with 561 lakes with a surface area larger than 100km
            <sup>
             2
            </sup>
            ,including four of the Great Lakes. All in all, a whopping 9% of Canadas surface is covered in fresh water.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="canada-lake-3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2932" height="332" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/canada-lake-3.jpg" title="canada-lake-3" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            After Warsaw, Chicago has the largest Polish population in the world
           </h3>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2933" style="width: 510px">
            <figure class="hammy-responsive size-full wp-image-2933" data-media="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2010/02/warsaw/810333343.jpg" data-media300="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2010/02/warsaw/1524464475.jpg" title="warsaw">
             <noscript>
              <img alt="warsaw" height="384" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/warsaw.jpg" title="warsaw" width="500"/>
             </noscript>
            </figure>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Warsaw
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            Warsaw is Polands capital, having under 3 million inhabitants, out of which 95% are have Polish origins. However, out of the almost 40 million inhabitants Poland has, there isnt a single city with a larger population than the one in Chicago.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2934" style="width: 394px">
            <figure class="hammy-responsive size-full wp-image-2934 " data-media="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2010/02/chicago-1/272060596.jpeg" title="chicago-1">
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Polish market" height="288" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/chicago-1.jpeg" title="chicago-1" width="384"/>
             </noscript>
            </figure>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Polish market in Chicago
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            Chicago is the third largest city in the US, with 7.5 million people living in the metropolitan area. Due to the huge Polish population, the architecture and culture of the city greatly resembles that of Poland, and you can find Polish theaters, markets, even a newspaper.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="chicago2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2935" height="375" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/chicago2.jpg" title="chicago2" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Its pretty difficult to say just how much of Chicagos popullation is Polish, with estimates ranging from 150,000 inhabitants to 10 times more  theres no exact number.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="chicago3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2936" height="333" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/chicago3.jpg" title="chicago3" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            The largest city in the world is Hulunbuir, at 263,953 km2
           </h3>
           <p>
            Where would you expect to see the worlds largest city? China, India, Russia? Just so you can make an idea, this city in inner Mongolia (China) is about as half as big as France, and its just a city !
           </p>
           <p class="entry-stuff clear margin-bottom-15 hidden-print single-post-ads-between center-block text-center">
            <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
            </script>
            <!-- ZME Science -->
            <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-2424323874644721" data-ad-format="auto" data-ad-slot="5307795920" style="display:block">
            </ins>
            <script>
             (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
            </script>
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="city1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2937" height="281" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/city1.jpg" title="city1" width="500"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            However, the urban agglomeration is just a small fraction of the city, with the area population density being otherwise really small. All accross the megacity you can see large landscapes of grassland, and industrialization is only existant in the center. For all its surface, the city has only 2.5 million inhabitants.
           </p>
           <p>
            <img alt="82_avjmx" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2938" height="300" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/82_avjmx.jpg" title="82_avjmx" width="450"/>
           </p>
           <h3>
            The driest place on Earth is near Ross Island; it hasnt rained there for millions of years
           </h3>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2939" style="width: 485px">
            <figure class="hammy-responsive size-full wp-image-2939" data-media="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2010/02/atacama/3787615602.jpg" data-media300="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2010/02/atacama/2107767316.jpg" title="atacama">
             <noscript>
              <img alt="In some parts of the Atacama desert there hasn't been any rain for 400 years, but this is still not the driest place on Eart" height="326" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/atacama.jpg" title="atacama" width="475"/>
             </noscript>
            </figure>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             In some parts of the Atacama desert there hasnt been any rain for 400 years, but this is still not the driest place on Eart
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            Yes, Antarctica is the driest place on the Earth, with the Atacama desert being 2nd, and Sahara third.
           </p>
           <h3>
            The deepest hole dug by man is over 12 km deep
           </h3>
           <p>
            <img alt="geolsection" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2987" height="521" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/geolsection.gif" title="geolsection" width="424"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            The Kola Superdeep Borehole has a depth of 12,261 meters, thats once and a half the size of Everest, or deeper than the Mariana trench.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2988" style="width: 410px">
            <figure class="hammy-responsive size-full wp-image-2988" data-media="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2010/02/kola_superdeep_borehole_site/3519803260.png" title="kola_superdeep_borehole_site">
             <noscript>
              <img alt="Edited* Previous picture was indeed, not of the Superdeep Borehole" height="260" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/kola_superdeep_borehole_site.png" title="kola_superdeep_borehole_site" width="400"/>
             </noscript>
            </figure>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Edited* Previous picture was indeed, not of the Superdeep Borehole
            </p>
           </div>
           <p>
            The borehole also led to some interesting discoveries, including a massive amount of hydrogen, so massive that the mud was actually boiling with it.
           </p>
           <h3>
            In New York, there are more Italians than in Rome, more Irish than in Dublin and more Jews than in Tel Aviv
           </h3>
           <p>
            The big apple stands out anytime, no matter who you are or what youre interested in. But still, I was really shocked to see this. I mean, with Rome having more than 3.5 million people, Dublin at 1 million and Tel Aviv at more than 3, thats almost 8 million people !
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <img alt="new_york_skyline3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2942" height="300" src="http://cdn.zmescience.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/02/new_york_skyline3.jpg" title="new_york_skyline3" width="480"/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Not to mention the other nationalities (which are quite abundent), one can only wonder how many Americans are living in New York.
           </p>
           <p>
            Now cmon people, hit me with your best geographical fact !
