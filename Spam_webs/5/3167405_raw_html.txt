       <h2 class="posttitle">
        Im Lovin It  Watermelon
       </h2>
      </div>
      <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
       <a data-pin-color="gray" data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Ftidymom.net%2F2011%2Fwatermelon%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Ftidymom.net%2Fblog%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2011%2F07%2Fwatermelon-summer_500.png&amp;description=I%27m%20Lovin%27%20It%20-%20Watermelon" rel="nobox">
        <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
       </a>
      </div>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <em>
         Sit back and enjoy summer with watermelon on a stick, recipes, coolers, cookies, popsicles, salsa and salad and more.
        </em>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <img alt="watermelon on a stick" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12376" height="368" src="http://tidymom.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/watermelon-summer_500.png" title="watermelon on a stick at TidyMom.net" width="500"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <a data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonPin" href="//pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Ftidymom.net%2F2011%2Fwatermelon%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Ftidymom.net%2Fblog%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2011%2F07%2Fwatermelon-sticks_400.png&amp;description=This%20screams%20SUMMER!!%20Watermelon%20on%20a%20stick%20%2B%20lots%20of%20watermelon%20recipes%20at%20TidyMom.net">
        <img alt="" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       I think watermelon is one of natures most perfect foods! The luscious lime green and white rind with a juicy deep red inside.even the seeds are cute. Beautiful, perfectly sweet and juicy, thats hard to beat!
      </p>
      <p>
       This heavenly fruit is a summer icon and can be found in our fridge most of the season. I love to save time, by just cutting wedges and putting them on a stick for easy eating!
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12377" height="602" src="http://tidymom.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/watermelon-sticks_400.png" title="This screams SUMMER!! Watermelon on a stick + lots of watermelon recipes at TidyMom.net" width="400"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       I think the day I first saw seedless watermelon at the store, my heart skipped a beat! As cute as those little black seeds are, theyre also so annoying! Spitting seed out is not lady like, and picking them out, well that just takes time. Either way, seeded or seedless, Ill take it, as long as its sweet, ripe and cold.
      </p>
      <p>
