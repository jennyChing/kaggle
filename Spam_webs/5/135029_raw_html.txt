           <h1 class="post-title">
            Aspect Solar EnergyBar 250 Review: A Great Power Bank with Solar Charging Compatibility
           </h1>
           <p class="post-byline">
            by
            <a href="http://bestportablesolargenerators.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by Amanda Swan">
             Amanda Swan
            </a>
             January 8, 2015
           </p>
           <div class="clear">
           </div>
           <div class="entry">
            <div class="entry-inner">
             <!-- Easy Plugin for AdSense V7.60 -->

             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               We have already coveredAspect Solars flagship portable solar generator called,
               <a href="http://bestportablesolargenerators.com/portable-power/sunsocket-sun-tracking-solar-generator-review-automatically-tracking-sun-better-output/">
                SunSocket Sun-Tracking Solar Generator
               </a>
               . It is a complete, medium-powered solar generator with all its parts and components including the automatically sun-tracking solar panels built into a single case, which is solid and very much portable.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               What about a solar-compatible, portable lightweight battery pack from Aspect Solar now? Here we are going to introduce you to the Aspect Solars much-frenzied EnergyBar 250 that can be charged via both wall AC outlet and solarenergy, making it a really worthy pick for your survival gear.
              </span>
             </p>
             <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_2744" style="width: 410px">
              <a href="http://bestportablesolargenerators.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Aspect-Solar-EnergyBar-250-.jpg" rel="lightbox-0">
               <img alt="Aspect Solar EnergyBar 250" class="wp-image-2744" height="309" src="http://bestportablesolargenerators.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Aspect-Solar-EnergyBar-250-.jpg" width="400"/>
              </a>
              <p class="wp-caption-text">
               aspectsolar.com
              </p>
             </div>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               In this
               <strong>
                Aspect Solar EnergyBar 250 review
               </strong>
               , I would love to talk about this cool product with no limit. I feel much interest in this portable power product that comes in form of a briefcase. With a lightweight and easily-portable build, it is really an awesome emergency and outdoor power pack, which is capable to store 25 hours of power for your smartphones and as many as 3 to 5 hours of power for your laptop.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               To have a DC wall input option means you can use the device any time, regardless ofweather conditions. Even if the sky is overcast, you can make your way into the wild with no worry. This edge also gives an extra score for the product among the outdoor enthusiasts, who hail from rainy places. In my
               <strong>
                Aspect Solar EnergyBar 250 review
               </strong>
               below, you can learn more about the product.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: verdana, geneva;">
               <strong>
                <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00M45PI56/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00M45PI56&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=rgon-20&amp;linkId=QEZ2NP2CDJ65OTIS" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                 Buy AspectSolar EnergyBar 225 SunSocket Here
                </a>
               </strong>
              </span>
             </p>
             <h3>
              <strong>
               Aspect Solar EnergyBar 250 Tech Specs
              </strong>
             </h3>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               Before moving to the points that make the device a unique one, I want you to run your eyes over its basic specs. Unlike the SunSocket Solar Generator, it doesnt bundle solar panels, and that is why you can take it home for a smaller price. But if you are an avid outdoor enthusiast and want to make use of sun energy to charge it up, you should spend extra on solar cells.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               The EnergyBar 250 sports a 12.8V 20AH, 250Wh battery, which is Aspect Solars exclusive LiFePO4 Lithium Iron Phosphate unit. This battery is known for somemuch-appreciated reasons  less weight, long cycles and high efficiency.
              </span>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt; line-height: 1.5;">
               To distribute the gathered energy, the power pack has four 5V VA Max USB sockets, a 12V DC socket and a universal AC outlet, which offers 100W modified Sine Wave power for your laptop and other devices. The backpack-friendly gadgetsmeasures at 2.0 x 16.5 x 6.3 inches and scales up just 8 lbs.
              </span>
             </p>
             <h3>
              <strong>
               Aspect Solar EnergyBar 250  Why Should you Buy?
              </strong>
             </h3>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               Below are the reasons I found that should inspire you to buy this product.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               <strong>
                Multiple Charging Options:
               </strong>
               If you are heavily obsessed to green energy, this point wont make you feel that excited. The EnergyBar 250, like the Goal Zero portable power products, features both solar and wall options for collecting charge.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               Being an outdoorsman, you will be having much resource to charge the device from sun itself, unless the climate is not shadowy. Even thoughyou live in an area, where sunshine is not available all the time, this gadget is a nice option to go with, because you can go out on an outing by storing enough power for your gadgets from the wall outlet.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               <strong>
                Travel-Friendly:
               </strong>
               As a power bank, EnergyBar 250 is tremendously travel-friendly. It is shaped up in form of a briefcase so that it will be a wonderful experience to carry it along on your shoulders. You can store it anywhere, under the car seats, office table or cupboard. However, along with an external solar pack, it may be adding some burden to your travel belongings.
              </span>
             </p>
             <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_2745" style="width: 410px">
              <a href="http://bestportablesolargenerators.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Aspect-Solar-EnergyBar-250-2.jpg" rel="lightbox-1">
               <img alt="Aspect Solar EnergyBar 250  2" class="wp-image-2745" height="303" src="http://bestportablesolargenerators.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Aspect-Solar-EnergyBar-250-2.jpg" width="400"/>
              </a>
              <p class="wp-caption-text">
               aspectsolar.com
              </p>
             </div>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               <strong>
                Long-lasting Battery:
               </strong>
               Aspect Solars ground-breaking LiFePO4 battery is a great advantage of the system. It is amazingly lightweight and efficient in storing power. Its self-discharge rate is as low as 0-2% per month, meaning that you can keep it for years without using. In terms of lifespan, it promises1000 power cycles, coming out to be a great benefit for serious users.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               <strong>
                Ultra-slim, Weatherproof Design:
               </strong>
               EnergyBar 250 is weather-resistant, thanks to its high quality protective neoprene carrying case. As you zip it up, you can take it anywhere irrespective of weather conditions. Rather it is known for its ultra slim body, a major reason why many people go for it without a second thought.
              </span>
             </p>
             <h3>
              <strong>
               Price and Availability
              </strong>
             </h3>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               Talking about price, EnergyBar 250 is available for $260.42 on Amazon as of writing this piece. Remember, it is only the value of the power bank, excluding solar panels that you should buy separately.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               By the way, EnergyBar 250pairs perfectly with Aspect Solar EP-60 solar panels, but customers on Amazon have frequently bought EP-55 Solar Panel SunSocket, priced at $283.16.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               That means to set up a full solar generator you should spend twice the cost of EnergyBar 250. Below I have Amazon links to buy the power bankand the solar socket.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
               <span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: verdana, geneva;">
                <strong>
                 <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00M45PI56/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00M45PI56&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=rgon-20&amp;linkId=QEZ2NP2CDJ65OTIS" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                  Buy AspectSolar EnergyBar 225 SunSocket
                 </a>
                </strong>
               </span>
              </span>
             </p>
             <h3>
              <strong>
               Summing Up
              </strong>
             </h3>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               While concluding this
               <strong>
                Aspect Solar EnergyBar 250 review
               </strong>
               , I would like to remember you its major qualities. First of all, it is a simple power pack that you can carry anywhere with less burden to your backpack. It is quite enough to power up your handhelds as well as laptop while you are spending time outdoors.
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
               If you are kind ofconcerned about the scarcity of wall electricity, buy an extra solar panel pack and get it charged directly from sun, which, though costing you some extra bucks, will give you free, portable energy anywhere. Overall, Solar EnergyBar 250 is a lightweight, greatlyportable power backup for your camping, emergencies and many otherconditions.
              </span>
             </p>
