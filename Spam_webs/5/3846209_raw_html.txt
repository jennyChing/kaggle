      <p>
       Thank you for choosing Hills &amp; Dales AutoCare Inc. We are a total car care facility, offering maintenance and repair on all makes and models. Our aim is to provide the best possible service using the highest quality parts, backed with a comprehensive warranty program and a dedication to excellent customer service.
      </p>
      <p>
       Foreign, domestic, old, or new, automotive repair is what we do! Since opening our doors in 2001, the automobile service shop of Hills &amp; Dales Auto Care has been meeting the automotive maintenance and repair needs of Canton, Ohio. Known in the Canton area for our professional service, respectful attitudes, and high quality work, Hills &amp; Dales Auto Care strives for customer satisfaction. We have even won a Peoples Choice Award from the Canton Repository. Our staff of expert mechanics are wise beyond their years when it comes to auto repair, despite the fact that Hills &amp; Dales is only a little over a decade old.
      </p>
      <p>
       With so many automotive repair shops in the area, its hard to tell the difference between the trusted professionals and the inexperienced mechanics. Thankfully, when a driver entrusts the care of their car to Hills &amp; Dales Auto, they know theyre in good hands. Our loyal customers come to us from all over Stark County to have their vehicles worked on by our top of the line ASE certified mechanics. We are proud to continue living up to our trademark promises of Same Day Service, No Appointments Needed, and Available Shuttles. Hills &amp; Dales commitment to customer satisfaction is second to none, providing Canton, Ohio residents with the best possible combination of pricing, convenience, and service resulting in superior automotive care.
      </p>
