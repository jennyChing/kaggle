             <p>
              <br/>
              Vermont Senator Bernie Sanders made an inspiring plea to his colleagues on Monday to vote to start the process for a constitutional amendment to overturn Citizens United and get money out of politics by giving the peoples representatives the power to regulate campaign finance and keep billionaires from purchasing our political system.
             </p>

             <p>
              Some of the most notorious offenders of corrupting our political system are the billionaire Koch brothers, who have already funded almost 44,000 campaign ads and have an extremist corporate libertarian agenda to dismantle the United States government, privatizing it piece by piece, and eliminate government programs that help working class families and the elderly survive.
             </p>
             <p>
              People do not spend hundreds of millions of dollars on campaigns for fun, for the hell of it, Bernie Sanders declared. They are spending money because they have an agenda, and the billionaire agenda is not the agenda of the American people.
             </p>
