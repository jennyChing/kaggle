          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           The good folks over at Toyota are not strangers to crafting iconic vehicles, their classic Land Cruiser 70 is one of the most sought after vehicles on the planet. Well today Toyota is back in the news, this time with word that the Land Cruiser 70 will be back, for one year only, and only in Japan.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           The legendary ride gets some upgrades, mainly a new look to the front end, which makes for an interesting and still iconic new look. Check out the impressive machine below and speak your mind on it after the jump.
