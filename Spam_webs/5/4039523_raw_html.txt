         Towards the end of the 19th century, Luis Salvador of Austria, settled in the area of Valldemossa and in subsequent years he constructed a nice path in the nearby mountains in the Sierra Tramuntana. Nowadays this area is a splendid place for hikers as there are numerous trails to be discovered and the route from Valldemossa to Dea or to the Teix are very nice, offering splendid views over Mallorca and down on the Mediterranean towards the Northwest. There are numerous viewpoints nearby as well and you can see the Sa Foradada rocks with the emblematic hole in them.
