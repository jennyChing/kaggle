         <h2>
          Dreaming of a White Christmas Tree (or Two)
         </h2>
         <p style="text-transform: uppercase; font-size: 12px;">
          December 9, 2010
         </p>
         <div class="entry">
          <p>
           When I was little, I remember my grandparents always had a flocked Christmas tree. As a girl, I always loved sitting under my Memah and Papas white tree, it was so enchanting, like something out of a storybook. The tree had a crispy texture and funny smell, but I still loved it. Every single time I see a flocked tree in reminds me of my grandparents. (And that mothball scene when Lucy hides in the wardrobe before she discovers Narnia, strange but true.)
          </p>
          <p>
           Growing up, my parents always kept it green and natural, so after I got married, I followed their lead. Never ever have I even considered a white Christmas tree in my house. At the lot the other day, while walking passed the flocked trees, I told my hub about my affection for my grandparents tree. Funny, weve spent fifteen Christmases together and he never knew!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/cg-white-tree-left-side.jpg">
            <img alt="cg white tree left side" border="0" height="508" src="//www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/cg-white-tree-left-side_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; border-left-width: 0px; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px" title="cg white tree left side" width="504"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           So this year, we decided we wanted a flocked tree too, as a throwback to their generation. The decorations are a fun but unexpected mix of gold berries, mini disco balls, teal plastic garland, crystals, brown ribbons and mercury glass, and of course, new and old treasures.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/cg-flocked-tree-fur-skirt-gold-cross.jpg">
            <img alt="cg flocked tree fur skirt gold cross" border="0" height="543" src="//www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/cg-flocked-tree-fur-skirt-gold-cross_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; border-left-width: 0px; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px" title="cg flocked tree fur skirt gold cross" width="491"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <span id="more-13634">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           There is no tree smell but I have greenery other places, and since we dont get any snow around here, faking it has its appeal. I so love how the white backdrop shows off the ornaments on display !
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/frame-ornaments.jpg">
            <img alt="frame ornaments" border="0" height="434" src="//www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/frame-ornaments_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; border-left-width: 0px; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px" title="frame ornaments" width="469"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/celebrate.jpg">
            <img alt="celebrate" border="0" height="416" src="//www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/celebrate_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; border-left-width: 0px; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px" title="celebrate" width="470"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/blue-gold-ornaments.jpg">
            <img alt="blue gold ornaments" border="0" height="438" src="//www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/blue-gold-ornaments_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; border-left-width: 0px; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px" title="blue gold ornaments" width="483"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p align="center">
          </p>
          <p align="left">
           I spy with my little eye last years DIY
           <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/2009/12/fur-trimmed-tree-skirt/" target="_blank">
            <font color="#408080">
             fur trimmed tree skirt
            </font>
           </a>
           . LOVE it. That skirt is stuffed like a Santa suit to give it extra fluff. Just a few summer throw pillows and some leftover bubble wrap, fo reals.
          </p>
          <p align="left">
           <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/cg-white-flocked-christmas-tree1.jpg">
            <img alt="cg white flocked christmas tree" border="0" height="508" src="//www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/cg-white-flocked-christmas-tree_thumb1.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; border-left-width: 0px; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px" title="cg white flocked christmas tree" width="526"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           This flocking idea is new to me, and as I understand, it acts as a tree preservative. Im the kind of gal who keeps preservatives at arms length, but when I heard a properly stored flocked tree could be reused for at least another year, maybe two, I thought, well thats pretty darn economical. There is one more drawback: disposal. Flocked trees can be reused, not recycled. In my excitement, I forgot about that, so well have to figure out proper disposal when the time comes.
          </p>
          <p align="center">
           Meanwhile, Ill thoroughly enjoy its beauty throughout the season!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/noel-on-table.jpg">
            <img alt="noel on table" border="0" height="496" src="//www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/noel-on-table_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; border-left-width: 0px; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px" title="noel on table" width="436"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           The kids got an extra treat too this year in the form of their own little tree in their play space. Last year, I hit up the Goodwill the first week of January. Tell me you do this too.
           <em>
            Tell me you visit the Goodwill in January for holiday decorations
           </em>
           !
          </p>
          <p>
           OK, truth be told, I usually drop off all the stuff I dont want in the back, then go right around the front to see if theres anything new I really want. Tragic but true. But talk about a goldmine! I found this white tree for a few bucks and I specifically recall paying 25 for this sweet candy garland!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/white-tree-red-bucket.jpg">
            <img alt="white tree red bucket" border="0" height="552" src="//www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/white-tree-red-bucket_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; border-left-width: 0px; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px" title="white tree red bucket" width="386"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/mickey-garland.jpg">
            <img alt="mickey garland" border="0" height="318" src="//www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/mickey-garland_thumb.jpg" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; border-left-width: 0px; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px" title="mickey garland" width="387"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p align="center">
          </p>
          <p align="center">
           Santa and his friend keep all their favorite books and films ready to go!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.centsationalgirl.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/fav-books-and-films.jpg">
