       </div>
       Logic would have us believe that grooming is a morning activity, a daily ritual designed to make men more presentable  attractive, even  when viewed in broad daylight. But what if the real payoff to grooming was gained overnight, when nobody was looking? An evening routine is a critical part of preserving your looks at a cellular level.
       <p>
        We understand. The last thing you want to do when you roll in at the end of a long day is spend time immersing yourself in an evening skincare ritual. But hear us out. Sleep isnt just an opportunity to switch your mind off; it offers every last cell, tissue and muscle an opportunity to detoxify and regenerate in a way that is near impossible during waking hours. In short, beauty sleep is a real, quantifiable thing.
       </p>
       <p>
        It would be wise, therefore, to capitalize on the potential of cell regeneration during the night by taking five minutes to use a decent overnight treatment before you hit the sack. In fact, your skin will be far more receptive to an active skin care product during the wee hours since it is relieved of the pressure of having to fight off environmental aggressors like pollution, stress and UV damage.
       </p>
       <p>
        This is doubly important if youre scrimping on shuteye to make it through a hectic work / family / social schedule and have to wake up at the crack of dawn (though wed always recommend cramming in more time in bed  research shows that a good snooze wards off everything from heart disease, type 2 diabetes and depression).
       </p>
       <p>
        And yes, there are dedicated products for nighttime use  they tend to be richer in texture, higher in active ingredients and lower in protective ingredients (that built-in sunscreen is not much use if you sleep in the dark). Many of them will be marketed as anti-ageing products since it is poor cell turnover, among other things, that contributes to visible signs of aging.
       </p>
       <p>
        A good product should support the natural regeneration and detoxification process while replenishing vitamins and minerals that have been lost during the day. And as an added bonus, you get to wake up looking hydrated and refreshed  even if you feel like a train wreck on the inside.
       </p>
