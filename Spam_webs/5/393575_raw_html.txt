         <h1 id="mr_article_header">
          MICRODIA Unleashes Worlds First Micro USB Type-C OTG Flash Drive &amp; Reader
         </h1>
         <div id="mr_article_sms">
          World's First Micro USB Type-C OTG Flash Drive &amp; Reader
         </div>
         <div class="art">
          <p class="storybody">
           USA, January 8, 2015  MICRODIA Semiconductor Ltd. (MICRODIA) today announced the immediate availability of its new Micro USB Type-C OTG Flash Drive &amp; Reader (FDR). It is a combination card reader and USB flash drive incorporating the new USB standard for its micro USB connector. Reversible connectors eliminate the need to verify if the user is plugging in the micro USB connector the right-way up. Consequently, the OTG FDR is truly plug n play.
          </p>
          <p class="storybody">
           The USB Type-C standard is from the USB-IF and was finalised during the latter half of 2014. It will be implemented as the new standard in Europe in mid-2015 for all mobile devices, including next generation Android smart phones.
          </p>
          <p class="storybody">
           The OTG FDR can read microSDXC cards up to 512GB and is able to transfer data at up to 10Gbps. For users, this means uploading and downloading huge data is possible in seconds rather than minutes*.
          </p>
          <p class="storybody">
           This is a new standard from the USB-IF and a valuable investment that will pay dividends, said Louis Leung, CEO of MICRODIA Ltd. USB Type-C will unify all USB connectors and ports and render many devices compatible, therefore reducing the number of extra leads and adapters. As such, we expect market demand for USB Type-C-enabled OTG devices to explode.
          </p>
          <p class="storybody">
           Specifications
          </p>
          <p class="storybody">
           Interface: Micro USB Type-C
Standard USB3.0
Colours: Piano black with semi-transparent charcoal black lid.
Dimensions: Piano black (approximately): 30mm (L) x 15mm (W) x 10mm (H). 
Input Media: microSD, microSDHC, microSDXC, capacities up to 512GB.
          </p>
          <p class="storybody">
           * Speed of download and upload depends on configuration used.
          </p>
          <p class="storybody">
           About MICRODIA LTD
          </p>
          <p class="storybody">
           MICRODIA is a global leader in ash memory technologies and the worlds 2nd largest (in terms of production global capacity) manufacturer of flash memory cards. With its heavy emphasis on R&amp;D, MICRODIA leads the way in bringing the latest and most advanced ash memory technology to market ahead of the competition. Since its inception in 1991 as a magnetic data storage media manufacturer, MICRODIA has earned a reputation for quality, reliability and technical excellence. Today, the company provides a full range of Flash Memory Data Storage Solutions, Portable Multimedia Devices and Wireless Connectivity Solutions. MICRODIAs global coverage extends to many corners of the world and continues to penetrate existing markets and expand into new territories. For additional product information, please visit MICRODIAs website at www.microdia.com.
          </p>
