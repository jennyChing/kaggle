			&lt;div class="clear-article-share"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;p&gt;As Housing forays into 2 new cities of Noida and Ghaziabad, we have also added back the highly popular Shortlist feature. Now, you can locally save the flats that made you go &#8216;Perfect. This flat might be my next!&#8217; through the shortlist feature. The best part, there are no pesky sign-ups required to do that. The information is stored locally on your browser.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;span style="color: #008000;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Shortlist feature: How to use&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;1. Select any flat on map or from the list view to and click the star shaped Shortlist button.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div id="attachment_933" style="width: 409px" class="wp-caption alignnone"&gt;&lt;a href="https://housing.co.in/blog/2013/07/22/housing-adds-a-sexy-shortlist-feature-and-launches-in-2-new-cities/shortlist/" rel="attachment wp-att-933"&gt;&lt;img class="size-full wp-image-933" title="Shortlist" src="https://housing.co.in/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Shortlist.jpg" alt="" width="399" height="440" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;p class="wp-caption-text"&gt;Shortlist feature&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;span id="more-932"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;2. Your flat will be added to the &#8216;Shortlist&#8217; panel at the top left portion of the screen.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div id="attachment_934" style="width: 460px" class="wp-caption alignnone"&gt;&lt;a href="https://housing.co.in/blog/2013/07/22/housing-adds-a-sexy-shortlist-feature-and-launches-in-2-new-cities/s1/" rel="attachment wp-att-934"&gt;&lt;img class="size-full wp-image-934" title="s1" src="https://housing.co.in/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/s1.jpg" alt="" width="450" height="331" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;p class="wp-caption-text"&gt;All your shortlisted flats will show up in the Shortlist panel&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;3. Clicking on the flat entry in shortlist panel will show you the details of the flat.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div id="attachment_939" style="width: 1375px" class="wp-caption alignnone"&gt;&lt;a href="https://housing.co.in/blog/2013/07/22/housing-adds-a-sexy-shortlist-feature-and-launches-in-2-new-cities/s2-4/" rel="attachment wp-att-939"&gt;&lt;img class="size-full wp-image-939" title="s2" src="https://housing.co.in/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/s23.jpg" alt="" width="1365" height="567" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;p class="wp-caption-text"&gt;Details of the shortlisted flat&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;4. The flat you have shortlisted will now be tagged with an orange marker like the one shown below. You can continue to browse more flats on the map uninterrupted.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div id="attachment_937" style="width: 739px" class="wp-caption alignnone"&gt;&lt;a href="https://housing.co.in/blog/2013/07/22/housing-adds-a-sexy-shortlist-feature-and-launches-in-2-new-cities/s2-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-937"&gt;&lt;img class="size-full wp-image-937" title="s2" src="https://housing.co.in/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/s21.jpg" alt="" width="729" height="440" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;p class="wp-caption-text"&gt;Shortlisted and Visited flats marked on map&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;5. You can remove shortlisted flats from the list by any of the 2 methods. Hovering above a flat in the Shortlist panel will show you an option to cross it out of the list. You can also clear the shortlist by clicking on &#8216;Clear Shortlist&#8217; button located above zoom buttons on the map.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div id="attachment_935" style="width: 466px" class="wp-caption alignnone"&gt;&lt;a href="https://housing.co.in/blog/2013/07/22/housing-adds-a-sexy-shortlist-feature-and-launches-in-2-new-cities/remove/" rel="attachment wp-att-935"&gt;&lt;img class="size-full wp-image-935" title="remove" src="https://housing.co.in/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/remove.jpg" alt="" width="456" height="256" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;p class="wp-caption-text"&gt;Remove flats from your Shortlist&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;span style="color: #008000;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Launch in Noida and Ghaziabad&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;House hunting is going to change in 2 more cities! Noida, a major industry hub &amp; home to multiple multinational firms and Ghaziabad, the second fastest growing city in the world owing to an intense construction boom are 2 more cities on Housing. With these two additions, we are now active in 7 cities in India. This is not it though. We are coming to more cities. Keep watching this space for more!&lt;/p&gt;
