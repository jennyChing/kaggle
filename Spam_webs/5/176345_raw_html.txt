        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Happy Birthday M&amp;M Pretzel Buttons!
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2015-01-16T00:00:33+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 16, 2015
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/author/yourorganizedfamily/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Becky (Your Modern Family)
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/#comments">
           6 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="8033237" data-link="http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/" data-summary="" data-title="Happy Birthday M&amp;M Pretzel Buttons!">
        </div>
        <p>
         These Happy Birthday
         <strong>
          M&amp;M Pretzel Buttons
         </strong>
         are really good!  They also look great on the table at a
         <a href="http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/girls-engineer-party/" target="_blank" title="Girls can be engineers, too Birthday Party Theme   #LookatGoldie">
          birthday party!
         </a>
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="birthday buttons 2" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-16279" height="1000" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/birthday-buttons-2-667x1000.jpg" width="667"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/birthday-buttons-2-667x1000.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">
          </span>
         </span>
         <br/>
         I am all for having
         <a href="http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/easy-cheap-birthday-party-ideas/" title="5 easy cheap birthday party ideas">
          easy birthday parties
         </a>
         . I dont go to the extreme, because kids just want to play with their friends. They dont care about the details like we do, so Idont do much for the parties (Honestly, I do it this way because I do not want to be stressed at a birthday party, so keeping it easy will minimize my stress!)
        </p>
        <p>
         To make these M&amp;M Pretzel Buttons you will just need three ingredients:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004XRIV6Q/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B004XRIV6Q&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=youmodfam-20&amp;linkId=AQS2GF37WFBQAVFV" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004XRIV6Q/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B004XRIV6Q&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=youmodfam-20&amp;linkId=AQS2GF37WFBQAVFV', 'M&amp;Ms']);" target="_blank" title="Red M&amp;Ms">
           M&amp;Ms
          </a>
          (you can buy the colored ones or you can pick one color if you have a theme)
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004OT1TA8/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B004OT1TA8&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=youmodfam-20&amp;linkId=Y64R3XC4TSUG45IQ" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004OT1TA8/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B004OT1TA8&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=youmodfam-20&amp;linkId=Y64R3XC4TSUG45IQ', 'Square Pretzels']);" target="_blank" title="Square Pretzels">
           Square Pretzels
          </a>
          (we get
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004OT1TA8/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B004OT1TA8&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=youmodfam-20&amp;linkId=Y64R3XC4TSUG45IQ" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004OT1TA8/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B004OT1TA8&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=youmodfam-20&amp;linkId=Y64R3XC4TSUG45IQ', 'this giant binof pretzels']);" target="_blank" title="this giant bin&nbsp;of pretzels">
           this giant binof pretzels
          </a>
          because the kids eat them &amp; then I use the jar to hold our kids blocks or legos!)
         </li>
         <li>
          Hershey Kisses (any kind) I love the
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0063J2FDM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0063J2FDM&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=youmodfam-20&amp;linkId=T33UV2KBACZIOR75" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0063J2FDM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B0063J2FDM&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=youmodfam-20&amp;linkId=T33UV2KBACZIOR75', 'peppermint candy cane flavored ones,']);" target="_blank" title=" peppermint candy cane flavored ones,">
           peppermint candy cane flavored ones,
          </a>
          but any will work!
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="birthday buttons 3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16278" height="467" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/birthday-buttons-3.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/birthday-buttons-3.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">
           </span>
          </span>
          <br/>
          I tooka baking sheet &amp; lined it with foil. I then took pretzel squares and put them on the cookie sheet, side-by-side.
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="valentine buttons 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16211" height="467" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-2.jpg" width="700"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-2.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
          <p>
           Next, put the hershey kisses on top
           <br/>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="valentine buttons 3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16210" height="467" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-3.jpg" width="700"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-3.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <br/>
           Now put them into the oven at 200 degrees for 4 minutes.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="valentine buttons 4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16209" height="456" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-4.jpg" width="684"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-4.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Take them out and let them sit for about 1-2 minutes.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="valentine buttons 5" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16208" height="467" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-5.jpg" width="700"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-5.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Carefully place one M&amp;M on each one:
           <br/>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="valentine buttons 6" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16207" height="467" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-6.jpg" width="700"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-6.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
           <br/>
           Press themdown, just a little bit:
           <br/>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="valentine buttons 7" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16206" height="491" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-7.jpg" width="700"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/valentine-buttons-7.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Thats all there is to it!  They look adorable and they taste just as good!
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="birthday buttons 4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16277" height="467" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/birthday-buttons-4.jpg" width="700"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/birthday-buttons-4.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">
            </span>
           </span>
           <br/>
           Enjoy your
           <strong>
            Happy Birthday M&amp;M Pretzel Buttons!
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="happy birthday buttons" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16280" height="595" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/happy-birthday-buttons.jpg" width="700"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/happy-birthday-buttons.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>

           </a>
           <br/>
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="happy birthday baby leggings - FREE!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12338" height="444" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/happy-birthday-baby-leggings-FREE-.jpg" width="500"/>

           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Do you want more Birthday Ideas? Check out this
           <a href="http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/bee-day-party/" target="_blank" title="Bee-day party  birthday party theme that you will love!">
            Bee-Day Party!
           </a>
           <br/>
           <a href="http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/bee-day-party/" target="_blank" title="Bee-day party  birthday party theme that you will love!">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="bee-day party" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14060" height="929" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bee-day-party.jpg" width="650"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bee-day-party.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           This is also one of my
           <a href="http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/no-gift-birthday-party/" target="_blank" title="Best no gift birthday party">
            very favorite birthday ideas.
           </a>
           You can do this for any age, but we chose to do this for our daughter when she turned one.
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="no present birthday party" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5126" height="700" src="//www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/1st-bday-493x700.jpg" width="493"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/happy-birthday-mm-pretzel-buttons/&amp;media=http://www.yourmodernfamily.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/1st-bday-493x700.jpg&amp;description=Happy Birthday MandM Pretzel Buttons!')">

          <p>
           Another idea that we do for our birthday parties is to have each guest bring a re-gift.
