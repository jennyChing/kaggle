          <p>
           Many times we would have faced alignment issues when the website/blog is opened in different browsers. I too had similar problems with Firefox and Opera. While reading
           <a href="http://www.zigire.net/" title="Zigire">
            Zigire
           </a>
           , I came across this site
           <a href="http://browsershots.org/" title="Browsershots">
            Browsershots.org
           </a>
           ,  which tests the design of websites in different browsers.
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            Browsershots makes screenshots of your web design in different browsers. It is a free open-source online service. When you submit your web address, it will be added to the job queue. A number of distributed computers will open your website in their browser. Then they will make screenshots and upload them to the central server here.
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           Supported browsers/operating systems:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Linux
            </strong>
            : Dillo 0.8, Epiphany 2.14, Epiphany 2.16, Firefox 1.5, Galeon 2.0, Iceweasel 2.0, Konqueror 3.5, Mozilla 1.7, Opera 9.2, Opera 9.10
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Windows
            </strong>
            : Firefox 1.5, 2.0, IE5, IE5.5, IE6, IE7
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Mac
            </strong>
            : Firefox 2.0, Safari 2.0
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           The maximum wait time is 4 hours and minimum is 15 min.  Visit
           <a href="http://www.browsershots.org/">
            browsershots.org
           </a>
           to know more.
