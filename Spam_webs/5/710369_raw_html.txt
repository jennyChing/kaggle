I am also planning on re-finishing and recovering the chair sitting under the mirror. It will be covered in the same fabric as the drapes and painted the same color as the table legs. Oh yeah, did you notice I painted and distressed the table legs to match
         <a href="http://www.scissorsandspatulas.com/2011/06/grannys-old-dresser.html">
          grannys dresser
         </a>
         {see below}. My sister-in-law bought me a wooden highchair from a garage sale that Im planning on finishing in the same paint and putting to the right of the cabinet.
