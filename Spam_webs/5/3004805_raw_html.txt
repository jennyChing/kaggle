         <h2 class="posttitle">
          <a href="http://filmforecaster.com/funny-celebrity-middle-names/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Funny &amp; Bizarre Celebrity Middle Names">
           Funny &amp; Bizarre Celebrity Middle Names
          </a>
         </h2>
         <p class="date">
          <span class="byline">
           Aug 14 2014 in
           <a href="http://filmforecaster.com/category/viral/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Viral">
            Viral
           </a>
           <em>
            by Jacqueline Till
           </em>
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="entry">
          <p>
           You may think you know your favorite actors and actresses in and out, but do you know whats in-between their famous first and last names? Check out these ten funny and surprising middle names.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            Hugh John Mungo Grant
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           British actor Hugh Grant thankfully has two middle names. Without John, his name would sound like a humongous joke.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 650px">
           <img alt="" height="360" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-D0Z3EjrGjX8/Ut6mhdNYDLI/AAAAAAAAAJ0/Sz-1dbNPux4/s1600/tumblr_lr4h04HdIz1qzxxd7o1_500.gif" width="640"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Hugh John Mungo Grant in Four Weddings and a Funeral (1994)
           </p>
          </div>
          <p dir="ltr">
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            Kate Garry Hudson
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           The actress who plays the female lead in romantic comedies doesnt share the same femininity in her roles as she does with her middle name.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 508px">
           <img alt="" height="280" src="http://31.media.tumblr.com/f3f910511a7af66b488a24145e23fe76/tumblr_n8gl79H4vs1tcb6yuo1_500.gif" width="498"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Kate Garry Hudson in How To Lose A Guy In 10 Days (2003)
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            Zach Knight Galifianakis
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           Perhaps not the knight in shining armor you imagined, but at least its easier to pronounce than his last name.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 510px">
           <img alt="" height="418" src="http://38.media.tumblr.com/tumblr_ltpbygYDNW1qlvb3fo1_500.gif" title="Zach Knight Galifianakis in The Hangover (2009)" width="500"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Zach Knight Galifianakis in The Hangover (2009)
           </p>
          </div>
          <p dir="ltr">
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            Richard Tiffany Gere
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           In Pretty Woman, it wasnt a Tiffany box that Richard Gere closed on Julia Roberts fingers- but that does happen to be his middle name.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 255px">
           <img alt="" height="166" src="http://25.media.tumblr.com/440ac489049fe1ed8b3f1939def41b00/tumblr_n03hdy77nI1s00ervo3_250.gif" width="245"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Richard Tiffany Gere in Chicago (2002)
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            Mel ColmCille Gibson
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           Known for violent movies as well as The Passion of Christ, Mel Gibsons middle name is from his mothers parish in Ireland.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 360px">
           <img alt="" height="149" src="http://media1.giphy.com/media/mq1yb0iDLvTzi/giphy.gif" width="350"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Mel ColmCille Gibson in Braveheart (1995)
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Blake Ellender Lively
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           Adorned for her sexiness and youthfulness, Blake Livelys middle name doesnt exactly fit in with the rest of the actress portfolio.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 510px">
           <img alt="" height="229" src="http://25.media.tumblr.com/9aa5fe8d92944c1e625b7a7548fd8ae9/tumblr_mwfipsxhzD1rnlz23o1_500.gif" width="500"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Blake Ellender Lively in Gossip Girl (2007)
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            Nicolas Kim Cage
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           Born Nicolas Kim Coppola, Nicolas later changed surname to Cage and nicked the girly middle name while he was at it.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 255px">
           <img alt="" height="194" src="http://cdn0.dailydot.com/uploaded/images/original/2013/1/7/cage32.gif" width="245"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Nicolas Kim Cage in Vampires Kiss (1989)
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            Leonardo Wilhelm DiCaprio
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           Requiring a middle name as elaborate as his first and last, the odd name is actually the German form of William.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="" height="265" src="http://24.media.tumblr.com/6c2d6ce1418648170c277d2ae5562266/tumblr_n06j265WVR1r7572oo6_r2_400.gif" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Leonardo Wilhelm DiCaprio in The Wolf on Wall Street (2013)
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            Beyonce Giselle Knowles
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           Giselle? Well, I guess we should bow down considering its not like anyone questions her first name.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 410px">
           <img alt="" height="188" src="http://31.media.tumblr.com/tumblr_lrhklpNLMx1qi0thko1_400.gif" width="400"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Beyonce Giselle Knowles in Obsessed (2009)
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           <strong>
            Matt Paige Damon
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p dir="ltr">
           Although the name Paige is honoring his mothers maiden name, its fun to see a macho man with a popular girls first name.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 434px">
           <img alt="" height="233" src="http://31.media.tumblr.com/407d3cd874a7f6d806d1c5a2da97caba/tumblr_mrtds9EB3g1s6qxzpo1_500.gif" width="424"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Matt Paige Damon in Good Will Hunting (1997)
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Richard Morris Dawson
           </strong>
           <strong>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           The late Family Feud host, infamous for his love for female contestants
           <strong>
           </strong>
           (one who he later married) has a simple, astute name so that his wisecracking sense of humor can truly shine through. Who would have known a kissing fanatic was behind this name?
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption alignleft" style="width: 330px">
           <a href="http://guycodeblog.mtv.com/wp-content/uploads/clutch/2012/06/dawson4.gif">
            <img alt="" height="240" src="http://guycodeblog.mtv.com/wp-content/uploads/clutch/2012/06/dawson4.gif" width="320"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Richard Dawson kissed about 20,000 women during his time as the host of Family Feud
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            What name did you find most surprising? Were you given a bizarre middle name?
           </strong>
          </p>
