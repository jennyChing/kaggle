              <h1 class="story-title entry-title" itemprop="name headline">
               He Found Her Sleeping on the Ground Outside His HomeAnd Gave Her an Awesome Surprise
              </h1>
              <div class="relative" id="right-content">

                 <span class="entry-title" itemprop="name headline">

                <!--hide-title-->

                <p>
                 <!-- BEGIN THEIA POST SLIDER -->
                </p>
                <p>
                 What would you do if you find people making the street outside you home as their own home, sleeping on the ground and just hanging out there?
                </p>
                <p>
                 Well, that is a tricky thing to consider and the answer would largely depend on the situation, but for one man named Elvis Summers, the answer was simple: build that homeless person a home!
                </p>
                <p>
                 After all, he had plenty of materials to spare and had the knack for building things. So, he created a tiny home that was just right for this homeless person he does not even know! People who drove by asked whether he was renting the tiny home but Elvis was sure he would give it to the woman as a gift.
                </p>
                <p>
                 It took him days to finish the DIY project but the finished product was awesome! It even had double locks to make sure she was safe and could also go out at will without worrying someone would steal her house. It also had wheels for mobility, in case she needed to move to another spot.
                </p>
                <p>
                 Thanks to Elvis, one homeless lady has finally found a home to call her owneven if it was small and wasnt conventional likes most homes.
                </p>
                <p>
                 When Elvis finally gave her the keys, she was so overwhelmed with emotion. Watch the touching video here:
                </p>
