      <h1 class="title">
       I Will Trash Their Pool
      </h1>
      <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
      <p>
       I walk down a street called Hillside filled with two story homes and nice lawns. Things look way too comfortable here. Even the sidewalk feels too level and smooth. I sense my objective and slow my walk to half speed. I glance to my right. Im getting a weird look from a man on the other side of the street washing his car. Why the hell do people stare at you when you walk half speed?
      </p>
      <p>
       I stop. Im here, I know it.What is it? I turn to my left and face a two story white brick house with a large porch. Behind the house I see a tall pine tree. I stare at the branches and needles for 45 seconds. Im supposed to climb it. I dont know why and I wont find out until I make it to the top.
      </p>
      <p>
       I walk across the manicured lawn to the 5 foot tall wrought iron fence separating the front from the back. I scrape my stomach a little but I scale it easily. I lift my shirt and only see a few small drops of blood. The tree is straight ahead and right next to a large swimming pool. I take a quick look around the patio area and see no one. Good. I manage to pull myself up on the lowest branch and start climbing.
      </p>
      <p>
       Ive had to climb much more difficult trees in the past. This one has nice thick limbs and lots of them. In less than five minutes, Im easily 20 feet high. I look down. Now I know. Someone is going to drown in this pool. Someone who isnt supposed to. Soon. I take out my knife and start cutting off small branches. I throw pine cones, pine needless, sticks, and toss them all into the pool. In less than 10 minutes the once pristine swimming pool looks like crap.
      </p>
      <p>
       I hear a sliding glass door open. Out walk two boys. They look a little older, maybe 12 or 13 years old. They are running toward the tree. Its a brown hair and a red hair. Red hairs are usually trouble.
      </p>
      <p>
       What do you think youre doing in my backyard!? shouts Red Hair.
       <br/>
       I keep working. Maybe they will just go away. I snap a six foot long branch and toss it in. Splash.
       <br/>
       Dude, look at your pool, says Brown Hair.
       <br/>
       Red Hair is unglued. You better get your little butt outta that tree right now and clean this up. Right Now!
       <br/>
       He spits when he talks. I ignore him.
      </p>
      <p>
       A rock just hit me.Now I cant ignore him.
       <br/>
       I look down at them, You really want to start throwing things? Why dont I just drop a branch on your heads? Im almost finished. I dont want any trouble, so get your soft asses back inside.
       <br/>
       They look shocked. I reach for another pine cone and throw it into the pool.
      </p>
      <p>
       Soft asses!? Im getting my BB gun. Wait here, says Red Hair as he runs back inside.
       <br/>
       I pause and think for a moment.
       <br/>
       Hey Brown Hair. You ever been hit with a BB?
       <br/>
       No.
       <br/>
       I have. Lots of times. You might not know this but it stings like crazy.
       <br/>
       Oh well, says Brown Hair.
       <br/>
       I pull my knife out of my backpack so he can see the blade.
       <br/>
       If I get hit with one BB, fine. If I get hit with multiple BBs. Im coming down there and shits gonna get real.
       <br/>
       I throw in a few more bunches of pine needless. I survey the pool. The skimmers are clogged, there are pine cones all over the bottom and pine needless are everywhere. This will take hours to clean. I nod and start my decent.
      </p>
      <p>
       I reach the bottom. Brown hair takes a few steps back. Red Hair comes walking toward us, biting his lip, poking his chest out, and carrying a Red Ryder?
      </p>
      <p>
       Hes stops five or six feet from me and shows his teeth. He looks like hes about to implode. I stare in silence.
       <br/>
       Get ready kid Im gonna pump this thing twenty times!
       <br/>
       Me and brown hair watch him while he pumps the Red Ryder. 1 2 3 He fumbles with it for a second. 4
      </p>
      <p>
       Okay, then. I yawn. I turn around and walk toward the back fence. I wonder who I saved. Brown Hair maybe? He might not be able to swim. Maybe Red Hair has a little brother or sister. Who knows. I start climbing the fence. Freeze!
       <br/>
       I look back. You got all the way to twenty?
       <br/>
       Yep! says Red Hair pointing the little BB gun at me.
       <br/>
       Well, Im pretty sure you arent supposed to pump those more than five times.
       <br/>
       You trying to be funny?
       <br/>
       Im confused. What did I say that was funny? It doesnt matter. Nevermind. I climb to the top and straddle the fence.
       <br/>
       Youre about to get shot if you dont come down and clean this up. Right now!
       <br/>
       I look at his fuming, watery eyes. If I get shot it will be because your finger slipped.
       <br/>
       You have three seconds. 1.
       <br/>
       I nod and hop over the fence.
       <br/>
       I walk the fence line while he frantically paces on the other side.
       <br/>
       You come back here right now or youre dead! Dead, you hear me! Dead!
      </p>
      <p>
       I hope I didnt save him.Im pretty sure death by Red Rider is impossible so why say it? Especially so many times. I stop listening and start whistling. I walk across the neighbors backyard and smell something being cooked outside. I take a quick look and dont see anyone. I walk over and open the grill. Ribs! I grab them with a dish towel lying on the ground. These will be awesome. I walk to the next street carrying a beautiful full rack of ribs for supper. This is a good day, even if I did save Red Hair. As I walk I notice a few people staring at me. Thats odd, Im not walking half speed. I dont understand people.
      </p>
