        <h1 class="entry-title">
         DIY: A Cupcake Topping Bar
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">
         <span class="date published time" title="2010-11-17T06:15:26+00:00">
          Wednesday, November, 17, 2010
         </span>
         at
         <span class="date published time" title="2010-11-17T06:15:26+00:00">
          6:15 am
         </span>
         //
         <span class="post-comments">
          <a href="http://greenweddingshoes.com/diy-a-cupcake-topping-bar/#comments">
           <span>
            Comments:
           </span>
           52
          </a>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="diy cupcake topping bar" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9846" height="840" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/diy-cupcake-topping-bar-01.jpg" title="diy-cupcake-topping-bar-01" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          I love any kind of topping bar, so when Amber of
          <a href="http://enjoycupcakes.com/" target="_blank">
           Enjoy Cupcakes
          </a>
          asked if Id be interested in sharing a DIY for a cupcake topping bar, I said  of course!! :)
         </p>
         <p>
          From Amber,
          <em>
           Kevin + I have had a cupcake topping bar idea in mind for several months. So we teamed up with
           <a href="http://www.jenniferyoungblog.com" target="_blank">
            Jennifer Young
           </a>
          </em>
          (GWS featured
          <a href="http://greenweddingshoes.com/real-wedding-jenn-joshs-new-years-day-smog-shoppe-wedding/">
           Jenns wedding
          </a>
          way back in the early days of GWS  it was gorgeous if you missed it!)
          <em>
           to have her capture whats been swirling around in our minds. We created a rustic, fall themed cupcake topping bar to fit the current season, however the overall concept easily translates to any wedding theme or style. Enjoy Cupcakes offers the BUILD IT cupcake topping bar for weddings, but you can also achieve the concept on your own for parties or special occasions. Here are some tips
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
          </em>
          <img alt="cupcakes with frosting" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9847" height="428" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/diy-cupcake-topping-bar-02.jpg" title="diy-cupcake-topping-bar-02" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          We frosted cupcakes with a just a ring of frosting so the toppings could fill the holes in the middles.
         </p>
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="cupcakes with toppings" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9848" height="840" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/diy-cupcake-topping-bar-03.jpg" title="diy-cupcake-topping-bar-03" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          We filled vintage syrup dispensers with our house-made sauces and syrups.
         </p>
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="cupcake topping bar" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9855" height="484" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/diy-cupcake-topping-bar-10.jpg" title="diy-cupcake-topping-bar-10" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="cupcake topping bar enjoy cupcakes" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9849" height="840" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/diy-cupcake-topping-bar-04.jpg" title="diy-cupcake-topping-bar-04" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          Mini hallowed out pumpkins served as bowls that we filled with a variety of cupcake toppings.
         </p>
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="cupcake topping bar" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9850" height="840" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/diy-cupcake-topping-bar-05.jpg" title="diy-cupcake-topping-bar-05" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          To further carry out the fall theme, we cut slits in mini pumpkins and used them as flavor card holders for the sauces, and hallowed regular size pumpkins and used them as flower vases. When creating a banner for your topping bar, burlap, stencils and left over house paint can go a long way!
         </p>
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="cupcake topping diy tutorial" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9851" height="430" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/diy-cupcake-topping-bar-06.jpg" title="diy-cupcake-topping-bar-06" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         The Build It cupcake bar allows your guests the opportunity to create their own personalized cupcake! Easy + fun  I love it!
         <p>
          In addition, Amber also made some mini cutting cakes for this photo shoot because she (like us here at GWS) is kind of obsessed with tiny cakes:)
         </p>
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="mini cakes" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9852" height="840" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/diy-cupcake-topping-bar-07.jpg" title="diy-cupcake-topping-bar-07" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="mini cakes for wedding" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9853" height="840" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/diy-cupcake-topping-bar-08.jpg" title="diy-cupcake-topping-bar-08" width="650"/>
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <p>
          The lovely Amber (photo sent to us by her adoring husband who is so proud of her and wanted to show Amber off  seriously one of the sweetest emails Ive received!). Thanks so much to Amber + Kevin of
          <a href="http://enjoycupcakes.com/" target="_blank">
           Enjoy Cupcakes
          </a>
           and now Im seriously craving some cupcakes! :)
         </p>
         <p class="only-images">
          <img alt="cupcake topping bar" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-9854" height="432" src="http://greenweddingshoes.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/diy-cupcake-topping-bar-09.jpg" title="diy-cupcake-topping-bar-09" width="650"/>
         </p>
