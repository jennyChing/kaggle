      </a>
     </h1>
     <div class="date-comments">

      <p class="fr">

       <img alt="Would you ditch Cingular?" class="noborder" height="144" src="http://www.hiptechblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2006/12/cingular_01.jpg" title="Would you ditch Cingular?" width="171"/>
      </div>
      <p>
       If for some reason you always wanted to break your contract with Cingular, nows your chance to do so without paying a termination fee.
      </p>
      <p>
       Effective from Jan 21, 2007, Cingulars text message rates will go up from $0.10 to $0.15.
      </p>
      <p>
       Heres the good stuff, from Cingulars Terms of Service:
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        IF WE INCREASE THE PRICE OF ANY OF THE SERVICES TO WHICH YOU SUBSCRIBE YOU MAY TERMINATE THIS AGREEMENT WITHOUT PAYING AN EARLY TERMINATION FEE PROVIDED YOUR NOTICE OF TERMINATION IS DELIVERED TO US WITHIN THIRTY (30) DAYS AFTER THE FIRST BILL REFLECTING THE CHANGE.
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       And here:
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        7.
        <strong>
         Provide customers the right to terminate service for changes to contract terms.
        </strong>
        Carriers will not modify the material terms of their subscribers contracts in a manner that is materially adverse to subscribers without providing a reasonable advance notice of a proposed modification and allowing subscribers a time period of not less than 14 days to cancel their contracts with no early termination fee (ETF).
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       Well, theyll probably give you a difficult time. But stay persistent, and your unwillingness to give in will pay off.
      </p>
      <p>
       Good luck!
      </p>
