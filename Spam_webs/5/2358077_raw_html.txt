                <span>
                 How to change the world with a burrito Or the one post I hope everyone reads
                </span>
               </h1>
               <!-- content region -->
               <div class="region region-content content nested grid16-12" id="content">
                <div class="content-inner inner" id="content-inner">
                 <div class="block block-system first odd" id="block-system-main">
                  <div class="gutter inner clearfix">
                   <div class="content clearfix">
                    <div about="/blogs/sammy-adebiyi/how-change-world-with-a-burrito-or-one-post-i-hope-everyone-reads" class="node node-blog-entry node-promoted fusion-float-imagefield-left odd full-node clearfix" id="node-515" typeof="sioc:Item foaf:Document">
                     <span class="rdf-meta element-hidden" content="How to change the world with a burrito Or the one post I hope everyone reads" property="dc:title">
                     </span>
                     <div class="meta">
                     </div>
                     <div class="content">
                      <div class="content-inner">
                       <div class="field field-name-field-blog-image field-type-image field-label-hidden">
                        <div class="field-items">
                         <div class="field-item even">
                          <img alt="" height="158" src="http://www.sammyadebiyi.com/sites/sammyadebiyi.com/files/styles/blog-image/public/images/blog/starvation_0.jpg?itok=N0pEdpT_" typeof="foaf:Image" width="240"/>
                         </div>
                        </div>
                       </div>
                       <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
                        <div class="field-items">
                         <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
                          <p>
                           Dear Zakuskans/Friends,
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Last night was incredible.
                           <u>
                            Ive never been more tired from speaking
                           </u>
                           . I got home and just fell on my living room floor light-headed and exhausted. I wanted to just crawl up in my wifes embrace and ball my eyes out.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <em>
                            I was whooped!
                           </em>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <strong>
                            But then I had a thought that slightly saddened but re-energized me...
                           </strong>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <u>
                            I didnt even lose my voice
                           </u>
                           . So as tired as I was, I felt like I could have given a little more. I could have spoken a little louder for the voiceless.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           I think that's the only logical posture to have If what I shared last night is true. Weve got to try, try and die trying.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           I wont stat you to death but Ill repeat the most compelling stat to me.
                           <strong>
                            <span style="color: rgb(255, 0, 0);">
                             <u>
                              Every 5 seconds a child on earth starves to death
                             </u>
                             .
                            </span>
                           </strong>
                           Every 5 seconds.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <strong>
                            <span style="font-size: 9px;">
                             1001
                            </span>
                            <br/>
                            <span style="font-size: 10px;">
                             1002
                            </span>
                            <br/>
                            <span style="font-size: 11px;">
                             1003
                            </span>
                            <br/>
                            <span>
                             1004
                            </span>
                            <br/>
                            <span style="font-size: 14px;">
                             1005
                            </span>
                           </strong>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <strong>
                            A
                            <strike>
                             child
                            </strike>
                            just literally took their last breathe from hunger.
                           </strong>
                           If that doesnt make you sad/angry/furious/jacked up, I dont know what will.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Heres the 2nd most compelling stat to me:
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <strong>
                            $7 will provide a month's supply of food for 1 person.
                           </strong>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           It blows my mind how much difference we can make if we choose to act with
                           <span style="font-size: 10px;">
                            little
                           </span>
                           sacrifice. I dont care who you are, if youre reading this, you can spare $7 a month [skip a chipotle burrito, a movie or 2 mocha's a month] to save a life.
                           <u>
                            Im still waiting to meet that American who will convince me that's too steep a sacrifice.
                           </u>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           For those of you who chose to fight last night, thank you. For those of you who didnt get to, its not too late. Click link below and join the team.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <strong>
                            Everyone, please be a voice.
                           </strong>
                           Spread the link. Don't just read. Please share this on your face-book. Email your family and friends. There is power in numbers. Lets build this team.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           As I laid on the floor praying pre-sermon last night, I felt like Jesus kept saying..
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <em>
                            <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 255);">
                             'Sammy
                             <u>
                              it matters
                             </u>
                             so much to me'.
                            </span>
                           </em>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           I said 'Jesus I know' but he kept saying
                           <em>
                            <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 255);">
                             You have
                             <u>
                              NO idea
                             </u>
                             .
                            </span>
                           </em>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <em>
                            <span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 255);">
                             This matters to me Sammy.
                             <u>
                              It matters
                             </u>
                             so much, even if you only save 1.
                            </span>
                           </em>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           It matters.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Heres the link. Sign up. Invite your friends.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <span style="color: rgb(128, 0, 0);">
                            <a href="https://www.themochaclub.org/signup/93">
                             I will skip a burrito to save an orphan
                            </a>
                           </span>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Question:
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Last night I shared how best case scenario, Ive only seen about 30% of Christians [exception of those already doing something else] choose to act when I share above stats. This baffles me. I just dont get it. Help me understand.
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <strong>
                            <span style="color: rgb(255, 0, 0);">
                             Why do you think this is?
                            </span>
                           </strong>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Also, so many of you stood up last night during the challenge... I wanna know...
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <span style="color: rgb(255, 0, 0);">
                            <strong>
                             What was God stirring in your heart? What are your dreams? How do you want to fight injustice? what do you see as the potential biggest obstacles in the way of you going to the least of the least? What freaks you out? What made you mad/sad last night?
                            </strong>
                           </span>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           If you weren't there last night, still wanna hear from you too. Please answer above questions or just tell us...
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <strong>
                            <span style="color: rgb(255, 0, 0);">
                             What comes to mind when you read those stats? Why do you think most people don't care?
                            </span>
                           </strong>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           Raw link address:
                           <a href="https://www.themochaclub.org/signup/93" title="https://www.themochaclub.org/signup/93">
                            https://www.themochaclub.org/signup/93
                           </a>
                          </p>
                          <p>
                           <span style="font-size: 10px;">
                            [Regulars, sorry if this was out of sorts. Im working on a project so we can do something soon on here together but for now, please jump in somewhere. Join the dialogue. Check out the link. Join our team and spread the word]
                           </span>
                          </p>
