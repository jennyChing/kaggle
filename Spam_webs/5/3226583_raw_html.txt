      <h2 class="title">
       Shipping Container Retreat
      </h2>
      <div class="entry">
       <p style="margin-bottom:15px;">
        Port Container Services featured a Mod of the Month a few newsletters ago which was a container with a hydraulic deck that lowered from one of the side walls. This unit with a few other modified containers built by Port Container Services has had it's final fit out applied by architect Drew Heath. The 'Container Retreat' was designed as a place for mountain bikers to stay and enjoy the tracks around the complex. Featuring a kitchen, dining area, bedroom and bathroom set under a large roof with rolling walls to enclose the open areas between the containers. Water tanks and solar panels were also installed collect water from the roof and power the site.
        <br/>
        Images - photographed by Brett Boardman
       </p>
       <p style="margin-bottom:15px;">
        If you're interested in a container home like the above, we can design and construct a solution to your specifications. For more information call 1300 793 668 or visit our website for more information and get an
