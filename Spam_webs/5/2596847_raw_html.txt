        <h1 style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          The power of visualisation
         </span>
        </h1>
        <p>
         Visualisation is a tool that I use to create the life I desire, I visualise in the morning and before bed. I also visualise while mediating and even relaxing in the bath. I try and visualise as much as I can. When you do this you are creating a neural pathway in your sub conscious which contains the winning moves to create the life you visualise. You become what you think, so dream,visualise and think big. All you have to do is trust yourself and jump on that neural pathway and go for the ride of your life.
        </p>
        <p>
         I see visualisation as reprogramming the brain. As we have been programmed with so much stories from school,parents,media,friends and tutors all our lives. All these stories have had a negative impact on our lives. Think of all the times you were told that you could not do something or you were not good enough. All these negative impacts on your life create neural pathways and when it is repeated over and over it becomes programmed into your brain. It leaves you limited only to negative thoughts.
         <span id="more-67">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         The power of visualisation is a reboot or reprogramming the mind, making and creating new neural pathways and making them positive pathways. It breaks the limitations you have created all your life. It is like a habit you have to break the habit and habits are hard to break, but it is possible. It is your choice to create the life you desire by reprogramming and using the power of visualisation.
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: center;">
         The power of visualisation
        </h2>
        <p>
         You can use visualisation for anything you want there are no limits. It can be for better health, a fit body, being confident, speaking in public or at work meeting. It can be used for overcoming fear, having more money in your bank account. In order for you to make your visualisation as powerful as it can be you must also feel the what your visualising. Your subconscious does not register your thought as made up or true. It just creates the path. You can not create a path just thinking it but feeling down or in the back of your mind is doubt. It needs to be all or nothing to make it work. This is where people go wrong and their visualising does not manifest because they are not using the power of visualisation to the maximum.
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: center;">
         The power of visualisation
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         How to visualise?
        </p>
        <p>
         You really need to get comfortable and relax and first of all do some meditation before you begin to visualise. This slows the brain waves down and connects you better and closer to your subconscious. So meditate until you feel calm. Then actually visualise what you want to achieve . I will give you a example say you have been struggling to go on a family holiday. You visualise the whole journey getting ready packing, finding the passports. Visualise the flight and feel it and believe it. Visualise reading a book on the beach sipping a cocktail, feel the coldness of the cocktail and the glass, Visualise the cold drink going down your throat and how refreshing it tastes. Visualise playing in the pool with the kids splashing and swimming and having fun. You keep repeating the visualisation and it will manifest. You have to make it creative and imagine it has actually happened. Play the scene through your head like a movie. The more you play it through your head the stronger the neural pathway will be
        </p>
