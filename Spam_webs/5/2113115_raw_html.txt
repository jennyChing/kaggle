              <h2 style="padding:0.2em 0.4em; margin:10px 0; border:1px solid #ddd; background:#eee; color:black; font-size:150%; font-weight:bold; text-align:center; -border-radius:0.2em; font-family:Courier New, Courier, Monospace;">
               <span class="mw-headline" id="Featured_Article">
                Featured Article
               </span>
              </h2>
              <div style="float:left; margin-right:15px">
               <p>
                <a class="image image-thumbnail" href="http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/mspaintadventures/images/e/e1/PS_title_screen.png/revision/latest?cb=20150324134430" title="The Problem Sleuth title screen">
                 <img alt="PS title screen" class="lzy lzyPlcHld " data-image-key="PS_title_screen.png" data-image-name="PS title screen.png" data-src="http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/mspaintadventures/images/e/e1/PS_title_screen.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20150324134430" height="104" onload="if(typeof ImgLzy==='object'){ImgLzy.load(this)}" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" width="150"/>
                 <noscript>
                  <img alt="PS title screen" class="" data-image-key="PS_title_screen.png" data-image-name="PS title screen.png" height="104" src="http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/mspaintadventures/images/e/e1/PS_title_screen.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20150324134430" width="150"/>
                 </noscript>
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <p>
               <i>
                <b>
                 Problem Sleuth
                </b>
               </i>
               is the third
               <i>
                <a href="/wiki/MS_Paint_Adventures" title="MS Paint Adventures">
                 MS Paint Adventure
                </a>
               </i>
               . Initially a parody of detective noir stories, though quickly abandoning that premise in favor of stranger and wackier plot directions, the adventure tells the story of
               <a href="/wiki/Problem_Sleuth_(character)" title="Problem Sleuth (character)">
                Problem Sleuth
               </a>
               ,
               <a href="/wiki/Ace_Dick" title="Ace Dick">
                Ace Dick
               </a>
               and
               <a href="/wiki/Pickle_Inspector" title="Pickle Inspector">
                Pickle Inspector
               </a>
               , three competing private detectives who operate out of neighboring offices in the same building. When all three become trapped inside their respective offices, they join forces to find a way out of their predicament and defeat the evil
               <a href="/wiki/Mobster_Kingpin" title="Mobster Kingpin">
                Mobster Kingpin
               </a>
               , who possesses the key they need to leave their building.
              </p>
              <p>
               <i>
                Problem Sleuth
               </i>
               debuted on March 10, 2008, relaunching
               <i>
                MS Paint Adventures
               </i>
               after the abandonment of
               <i>
                <a href="/wiki/Bard_Quest" title="Bard Quest">
                 Bard Quest
                </a>
               </i>
               eight months earlier caused the site to be shut down indefinitely. Exactly one year later, on March 10, 2009, the central storyline reached its conclusion, and, after the release of an additional epilogue chapter,
               <i>
                Problem Sleuth
               </i>
               ended on April 7 with a total of 1,673 pages.
              </p>
              <p>

                <a href="/wiki/Problem_Sleuth" title="Problem Sleuth">
                 here
                </a>
                .
               </i>

              </p>
