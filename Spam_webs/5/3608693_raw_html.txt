        <h3>
         Welcome to Math Insight
        </h3>
        <p>
         The Math Insight web site is a collection of pages and applets designed to shed light
on concepts underlying a few topics in mathematics.  The focus is on qualitative description 
rather than getting all technical details precise.  
Many of the pages were designed to be read even before students attend lecture on the topic, so they are intended to be somewhat readable introductions to the basic ideas.
        </p>
        <p>
         You can browse the pages organized into
         <a href="/thread/list">
          threads
         </a>
         , which are sequences through a subset of pages organized by particular topics.  An
         <a href="/index/general">
          index
         </a>
         can help you find pages discussing a particular term.  You can also
         <a href="/search/">
          search
         </a>
         through the pages, applets, and image captions.  A few pages allow you to change the
         <a href="/about/notationsystems">
          notation system
         </a>
         used to render the mathematics.
        </p>
        <p>
         We hope Math Insight can help you understand key mathematical concepts.  We
         <a href="/about/contact">
          welcome comments
         </a>
         on how we can improve it.
        </p>

	}
