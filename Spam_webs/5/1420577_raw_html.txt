       <h2>
        DIY Paper Pumpkins To Become Cool Autumn Decorations
       </h2>
       <h3>
       </h3>
       <div class="ad_posttop">
        <div class="ad_posttop_inside">
         <div class="adPostTop">
          <script type="text/javascript">
           google_ad_client="pub-3263016571473863";google_ad_slot="4549715071";google_ad_width=336;google_ad_height=280;
          </script>
          <script src="http://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/show_ads.js" type="text/javascript">
          </script>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="entry">
        <div class="text">
         <div class="firstimg">
          <a href="http://www.shelterness.com/diy-paper-pumpkins-to-become-cool-autumn-decorations/pictures/12214/" title="">
           <img height="318" src="http://www.shelterness.com/pictures/diy-paper-pumpkins-1-500x318.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <p>
          What can be better and cheaper than paper pumpkins when it comes to Autumn decorations? They are easy to make but looks really cool. Besides they can be easily stored between seasons. To make such pumpkins youll need 2 sheets of matching scrapbook paper, ribbon, a hole punch and a paper cutter.
         </p>
         <p>
          First of all cut 1 strips of paper. If you want pumpkins of different sizes then cut your sheets in different lengths. Punch a hole in the ends of each strip. Tie a knot in one end of your ribbon and string it through all the holes on one end of your strips. Next, use lighter to seal the edge and put the ribbon through the other set of holes. Tie a knot and youre done. For more details visit
          <a href="http://www.craftinessisnotoptional.com/2010/10/paper-pumpkin-tutorial.html" rel="nofollow">
           craftinessisnotoptional
          </a>
          .
         </p>
