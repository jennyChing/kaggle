        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Panasonic Eluga S an Auto Selfie Smartphone Unveiled at Rs.11.190
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <a class="author" href="http://blog.khojle.in/author/khojleadmin/" rel="author">
          khojleadmin
         </a>
         /
         <a class="entry-date" href="http://blog.khojle.in/panasonic-eluga-s-an-auto-selfie-smartphone-unveiled-at-rs-11-190/">
          November 13, 2014
         </a>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <h3>
         <strong>
          After getting huge success in its Eluga range of Smartphones, Panasonic India today announced the launch of its new variant 
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Eluga S
          </span>
           for the Indian market, priced at Rs 11,190.
          <a href="http://blog.khojle.in/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Panasonic-Eluga-S-khojle.jpeg">
           <img alt="Panasonic Eluga S-khojle" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2096" height="476" src="http://blog.khojle.in/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Panasonic-Eluga-S-khojle.jpeg" width="635"/>
          </a>
          It is the selfie-centered Smartphone and will be up for grabs towards the end of this month.
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          In terms of specifications, the handset comes preloaded with an Octa-core processor clocked at
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           1.4GHz alongside 1GB RAM
          </span>
          and 8GB internal memory which can be expanded up to 32GB.
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          The newly launched 5 inch Eluga S sports a HD IPS display features the eye-definition Blink Play that lets one click flawless selfies or groupies with a
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           5MP front snapper
          </span>
          .
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           Read more at :
          </span>
          <a href="http://blog.khojle.in/lenovo-vibe-x2-gold-first-smartphone-launched-with-a-tri-layered-design/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Lenovo Vibe X2 (Gold) First Smartphone Launched With a Tri-layered Design">
           Lenovo Vibe X2 (Gold) First Smartphone Launched With a Tri-layered Design
          </a>
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          When you are ready to shoot, simply blink your eyes &amp; the phone will recognize the eye gesture to initiate the automatic three-second countdown. That lets one catch every moment in the blink of an eye. The Eluga S Smartphone also integrates an
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           8 MP Auto Focus rear camera
          </span>
          and is powered by a 2100 mAh battery.
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Specification Summary of Panasonic Eluga S Selfie Phone
          </span>
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <em>
           <strong>
             5-inch HD IPS display
           </strong>
          </em>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <em>
           <strong>
             Android 4.4.2 KitKat operating system
           </strong>
          </em>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <em>
           <strong>
             1.4 GHz true octa-core processor
           </strong>
          </em>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <em>
           <strong>
             1 GB RAM
           </strong>
          </em>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <em>
           <strong>
             8 GB internal memory expandable up to 32 GB
           </strong>
          </em>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <em>
           <strong>
             8 MP Auto Focus rear camera
           </strong>
          </em>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <em>
           <strong>
             5 MP front camera
           </strong>
          </em>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <em>
           <strong>
             2100 mAh battery
           </strong>
          </em>
         </span>
        </h3>
