         <div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           <a href="http://potholesinmyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Lusine-Waiting-Room.jpg">
            <img alt="Lusine Waiting Room Stream Lusines The Waiting Room In Its Entirety" class="alignnone wp-image-54550" height="554" src="http://potholesinmyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Lusine-Waiting-Room.jpg" title="Stream Lusines The Waiting Room In Its Entirety" width="554"/>
           </a>
           SeattlesJeff McIlwain seems to have a found his comfort zone between pop and experimentation. Operating under the moniker Lusine, this eclectic producer intertwines catchy rhythms with elements of more distant genres. On his third full-length
           <em>
            The Waiting Room
           </em>
           , he pulls everything together, expanding on prior sounds with newfound insight. Many of the songs on the latest offering feature vocalists, which Lusine is able to handle with finesse and care.
          </p>
          <p>
           Stream Lusines
           <em>
            The Waiting Room
           </em>
           below, which is available now via
           <a href="http://www.theghostlystore.com/collections/music/products/lusine-the-waiting-room" target="_blank">
            Ghostly
           </a>
           .
          </p>
