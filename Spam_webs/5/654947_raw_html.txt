        <p class="intro">
         Developers of Right or Wrong, BachTuocSo, have come up with an intriguing demo video for a mind-boggling and entertaining brain puzzle app.
        </p>
        <!--ADSOFF-->
        <p>
         Viet Nam- December 22, 2014 - Right Or Wrong, an amazing brain puzzle app that tests and trains the brain reflexes through its intriguing gameplay, has recently released a compelling demo video for the app. The
         <a class="trackedLink" data-orig-url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iFncc3rwblU" href="http://whate.ch/1CE0TjI" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <strong>
           apps demo video
          </strong>
         </a>
         is meant to communicate the purpose and how-to of the app in a fun manner.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Not only does the video manage to quickly communicate all the game perks to the viewers in less than a minute, but it is compelling enough to drive the viewers to check out the app and engage themselves with an exciting gameplay.
        </p>
        <p>
         Right Or Wrong, as the name suggests, offers a gameplay wherein players will encounter two play modes.
         <!--ADSOFF-->
         Within Play mode, players will get to play and test their brain reflexes by interacting with different game elements, from similarity, memory, math, count to roshambo, puzzle and difference.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Players will have to reply with either right or wrong as an answer to a specific question as quickly as possible to cash in maximum score. Whereas, within the Train mode, players can simply go through fun training sessions wherein they can practice and train their brain reflexes based on their chosen game elements.
        </p>
        <p>
         Right or Wrong, being developed after conducting various researches on human brain learning process, is successful in churning out the most interesting questions based on different mechanics that are sure to captivate players interest. said Phan Minh Chien, the Manager of BachTuocSo JSC. He further stated, Our precisely build mechanics are this puzzle apps perks that we thought will help us reach our maximum potential users around the world, hence dedicating a game trailer was evident that speaks volumes about our special game elements.
        </p>
        <p>
         While the app has already acquired large number of dedicated players who are engaging on daily basis to improve their reflexes in their day to day chores, potential players can download the Right Or Wrong app for free on Google Play, iTunes as well as on Windows Phone Store.
        </p>
