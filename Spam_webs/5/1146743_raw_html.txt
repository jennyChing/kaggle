Has you all may be familiar with the next big thing in the Google Play Store and if Game of War doesnt reach huge popularity then I dont know what is wrong with the world.

         <strong>
          How will these infect Kate Upton career?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Starting form the point where she is going to be on every pop up Game of War commercial thats going to appear on our phones and her face will be memorized by 1 billion android users.
        </p>
