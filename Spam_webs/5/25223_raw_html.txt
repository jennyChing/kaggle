           <h1 style="margin-top: 5px">
            <div style="font-size:12px">
             Gold Ends at a 0.5 Percent Loss on Friday July 25th
            </div>
           </h1>
           <div style="text-indent: 0px">
            2014-08-25
           </div>
           <p style="text-indent: 0px; position: relative; z-index: 5">
            Posted by:
            <a href="https://plus.google.com/105228133041447542001?rel=author">
             George Hornberger
            </a>
           </p>
           <div style="margin-top:40px;margin-bottom:40px;">
            <p style="text-indent: 0px">
             Prices for gold dropped for three sessions and recovered on Friday the 25th of July, slightly affecting the price of some rare coins in Florida. The price on Friday ended at over $1,300 per ounce, and gold deliveries for August picked up 1% the same day.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Since the end of 2013, gold prices have increased 8.4% percent. Gold ended at $1202.30 per ounce and its increase in price has resulted in higher bullion prices at rare coin stores in Florida. The political instability in the Ukraine among other geopolitical problems have affected the price of gold lately.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Although silver prices declined 1.2% for the week on Friday the 25th of July, the overall prices for silver are up 6.5% compared to 2013. Palladium was also up a substantial 22.5% for the year while platinum was up 7.6% on Friday. The increase in precious metal prices suggests that bullion continues to be in high demand at rare coin stores in FL.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             Investors speculate that the price for precious metals including gold may drop over the next few weeks, which may reduce prices for
             <a href="http://www.southfloridacoins.com/rare_coins.php">
              bullion and rare coins in Florida
             </a>
             . Regardless of the price fluctuations, it is likely that precious metals will continue to remain at a higher price compared to their ending price in 2013 as the upcoming months of August to December are usually the strongest period for gold prices, and August in particular almost always has increases in precious metal prices.
             <br/>
             <br/>
             For more information about how to buy or sell bullion at rare coin stores in Florida,
             <a href="http://www.southfloridacoins.com/contact_us.php">
              contact South Florida Coins today
             </a>
             at (561)-600-5611.
             <br/>
