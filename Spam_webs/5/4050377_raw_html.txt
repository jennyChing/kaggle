     <p>
      Another new Mobile Phone from Nokia is the X2-05, an affordable candy-bar phone that will be offered in emerging markets. Specifications of the Nokia X2-05 includes a 2.2-inch QVGA display with a 240?320 pixel resolution, it comes with a VGA camera and its external speaker is rated at 106phons which is the unit for measuring loudness and according to Nokia it is loud enough to fill an entire room. It measures 113mm x 50mm x 15mm (4.45in x 1.97in x 0.59in) and weighs 87.8g (3.1oz), and comes with some features as found on the C2-05 such as the ability to play MP3 and MP4 files directly from its microSD card and it will support cards up to 32GB in capacity. The Nokia X2-05 is expected to be released in the colors of black, silver, white, and bright red color options in Q4 of this year, it comes with a battery that promises up to 4 hours of talk time and up to 25 days of standby time.
