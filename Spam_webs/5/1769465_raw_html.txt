          <h1 class="h1__text" itemprop="headline">
           Porsche owner turns long driveway into racetrack
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="img-lead">
          <img class="img-responsive" src="http://o.aolcdn.com/dims-shared/dims3/GLOB/legacy_thumbnail/800x450/format/jpg/quality/85/http://www.blogcdn.com/www.autoblog.com/media/2011/06/porsche-driveway.jpg"/>
         </div>
         <div class="copy clearfix">
          <p class="byline pull-left">
           <span class="author hcard">
            <span class="author-headshot">
             <a href="/bloggers/zach-bowman/">
              <img class="img-responsive" src="http://o.aolcdn.com/os/wheels/img/profile_placeholder.png"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="fn" itemprop="author">
             <a href="/bloggers/zach-bowman/">
              Zach Bowman
             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <div itemprop="articleBody">
           <div class="iphone_hide" style="text-align: center; font-weight: bold; font-size: 10px; font-style: italic;">
            <a class="injectedLinkmain" href="http://www.autoblog.com/porsche/911/">
             Porsche 911
            </a>
            Turbo on a driveway race track - Click above to watch video
            <a href="/2011/06/21/porsche-owner-turns-long-driveway-into-racetrack/#continued">
             after the jump
            </a>
           </div>
           <br/>
           We don't want much in this world, but a tract of land with 400 acres or so in its borders wouldn't hurt our feelings. Aside from giving us a comfortable buffer between ourselves and any irritating neighbors, a slice of earth that large would allow for one very entertaining driveway  a driveway like the one in the video
           <a href="/2011/06/21/porsche-owner-turns-long-driveway-into-racetrack/#continued">
            after the jump
           </a>
           . We can't attest to just how much property is attached to the sprawling manse in the clip or whether or not the pilot of the
           <a href="http://www.autoblog.com/porsche/911">
            Porsche 911 Turbo
           </a>
           can actually lay claim to the kingdom, but we do know the guy has a blast getting around the estate.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           The short clip covers a quick loop around the house's exquisite concrete driveway, complete with a small jump, plenty of elevation changes and at least one hairpin. Sounds like a mighty fine way to make the drive to work a little more appetizing each morning.
           <a href="/2011/06/21/porsche-owner-turns-long-driveway-into-racetrack/#continued">
            Hit the jump
           </a>
           to check it out for yourself.
