       Blazing Minds Will Be At This Years Sci-Fi Weekender
      </h2>
      <div class="postmeta-primary">
       <span class="meta_date">
        February 20, 2013
       </span>

      <div class="entry clearfix">
       <img alt="Sci-Fi Weekender" class="alignleft featured_image wp-post-image" height="217" src="http://blazingminds.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Sci-Fi-Weekender-300x217.jpeg" width="300"/>
       <p>
        <img alt="Sci-Fi Weekender" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14426" height="363" src="http://blazingminds.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/Sci-Fi-Weekender.jpeg" width="500"/>
        Great news folks, Ive mentioned briefly in the last six months about this years Sci-Fi Weekender which is happening here in lovely Wales on the 1-3 March, so I thought Id bring you a little more information and what Ill be doing while Im there.
       </p>
       <p>
        This is the 4th Sci-Fi Weekender, formally known as the SFX Weekender, this year the event ishappeningathavens
        <a class="external" href="http://www.haven.com/parks/north-wales/hafan-y-mor/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Hafan y Mor Holiday Park
        </a>
        , the event will be kicking off early on the 28th February with a pre-party show for the early birds with
        <a class="zem_slink external" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Craig_Charles" rel="wikipedia nofollow" target="_blank" title="Craig Charles">
         Craig Charles
        </a>
        (Red Dwarf) DJing.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Blazing Minds Will Be At The Sci-Fi Weekender
       </h3>
       <p>
        The Sci-Fi Weekender is going to be pretty awesome this year with a great collection of guests including Brain Blessed (Flash Gordon, Star Wars, Doctor Who),
        <a class="zem_slink external" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Madden" rel="wikipedia nofollow" target="_blank" title="Richard Madden">
         Richard Madden
        </a>
        (Game of Thrones), Peter Davidson (Doctor Who) and many more withinterviews, Q&amp;A sessions, movie screenings, comic workshops, videogaming, music, book readings and loads more. For more of the line up check out the
        <a class="external" href="http://www.scifiweekender.com/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         Sci-Fi Weekender line up
        </a>
        page.
       </p>
       <blockquote>
        <p>
         The Sci-fi Weekender attendees will get the chance to fully immerse themselves in sci-fi culture. Fans can discover how comics are made, discuss burning issues with their favourite author, or be among the first to see exclusive screenings of films and TV shows. There are also many autograph opportunities: the full line-up will be revealed over the coming months. Above all, though, its a chance to hang out with like-minded people and enjoy a few drinks in a safe environment with other fans.
        </p>
        <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
        <div style="float:none;margin:10px 0 10px 0;text-align:center;">

          (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});

       </blockquote>
       <p>
        So as I mentioned earlier, Ill be at the Sci-Fi Weekender for the three days and hopefully Ill be tweeting out along withthousandsof other tweeters with the
        <strong>
         #sfw4
        </strong>
        hash tag with some pictures and loads more.
       </p>
       <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" width="500">
        <p>
         I'm so looking forward to the
         <a class="external" href="https://twitter.com/scifiweekender" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          @scifiweekender
         </a>
         not long now, I must think of a hash tag to tweet while I'm there 
        </p>
        <p>
          Karen Woodham (@BlazingMinds)
         <a class="external" href="https://twitter.com/BlazingMinds/statuses/304184546797158400" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          February 20, 2013
         </a>
        </p>
       </blockquote>
       <p>
        <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
        </script>
       </p>
       <p>
        Ill also have my great mate Lee, from CelebExposed, with me for some exclusive photos that well be posting up here on the blog during the weekend or just after the show, depends if we can get some tasty WiFi while we are there 
       </p>
       <p>
        Hopefully Ill be able to get some great interviews from some of the stars at the Sci-Fi Weekender and also some from the people at the event giving us their thoughts for the show, which will bring a great insight in to this amazing event.
       </p>
       <p>
        So remember to follow me @BlazingMinds,@PointAndView and @SciFiWeekender to keep up with whats happening and remember to save the
        <strong>
         #sfw4
        </strong>
        hash tag in your Twittersearches for everything that is going on.
       </p>
       <p>
        If you are going to this years Sci-Fi Weekender then let me know here in the comments section and perhaps we can all have a Tweet up over the weekend 
       </p>
       <p>
        See you there..
       </p>
