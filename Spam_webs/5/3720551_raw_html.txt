         <p>
          Whatever your pleasure, you'lllove London's top markets.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="hero" height="360" itemprop="image" src="http://cdn.londonandpartners.com/visit/london-organisations/borough-market/76421-640x360-borough-market-fruit-stall-640.jpg" title="Borough Market. Image: Michael Heffernan" width="640"/>
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="cf article-body" itemprop="articleBody">
        London markets sell everything from food to flowers, modern art to antiques, clothes to curios. Large, sprawling markets such as Spitalfields, Camden Market or Portobello Road are ideal if you want to spend a day wandering about, hopefully finding a few bargains along the way. Greenwich is great for gifts and local designers, and you can't beat Borough for a quick bite.

           <h2>
            <a href="/things-to-do/place/267031-alfies-antique-market">
             Alfies Antique Market
            </a>
           </h2>
           <a href="/things-to-do/place/267031-alfies-antique-market">
            <img alt="" class="hero" height="360" itemprop="image" src="http://cdn.londonandpartners.com/assets/attractions/shopping/35360-640x360-alfies_640.jpg" width="640"/>
           </a>
           <p>
            This is the place to come for antique and vintage clothes, collectables and 20th-century design pieces. It's the largest indoor market of its kind in London. Vintage lovers will spend hours browsing through the market's 600 stalls crammed with everything from rare vintage finds to colourful baubles.
            <b>
             Alfies Antique Market
            </b>
            also has a rooftop cafe boasting panoramic views over London.
           </p>
           <a class="readmore" href="/things-to-do/place/267031-alfies-antique-market" title="Read more about Alfies Antique Market ">
            Read More
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="vlSnCallout640 snippet cf">
           <h2>
            <a href="/things-to-do/place/285604-borough-market">
             Borough Market
            </a>
           </h2>
           <a href="/things-to-do/place/285604-borough-market">
            <img alt="" class="hero" height="360" itemprop="image" src="http://cdn.londonandpartners.com/visit/london-organisations/borough-market/76688-640x360-borough-market-couple-640.jpg" width="640"/>
           </a>
           Discover this gourmet's paradise in London. With a range of fresh produce including fruit, veg and all kinds of meat, as well as condiments, bakery treats and delicious street food,
           <b>
            Borough Market
           </b>
           is great for lunch or grocery shopping. There's also a wide selection of specialist ingredients and indulgent treats.
           <a class="readmore" href="/things-to-do/place/285604-borough-market" title="Read more about Borough Market">
            Read More
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="vlSnCallout640 snippet cf">
           <h2>
            <a href="/things-to-do/place/279336-brick-lane-market">
             Brick Lane Market
            </a>
           </h2>
           <a href="/things-to-do/place/279336-brick-lane-market">
            <span class="delayed-image" data-alt="" data-class="hero" data-height="360" data-imageprop="image" data-src="http://cdn.londonandpartners.com/visit/files/things-to-do/shopping/brick-lane-market/69043-640x360-brick-lane-640.jpg" data-title="" data-width="640">
            </span>
           </a>
           <p>
            Spreading across several streets in the
            <b>
             Brick Lane
            </b>
            area, you'll find both junk and gems in this market! There's bric-a-brac, second-hand furniture, cheap leather clothes, shoes and lots more. There are also some fantastic stalls selling exotic spices and colourful saris. Sunday is the best day to visit the market.
           </p>
           <a class="readmore" href="/things-to-do/place/279336-brick-lane-market" title="Read more about Brick Lane Market">
            Read More
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="vlSnCallout640 snippet cf">
           <h2>
            <a href="/things-to-do/place/14261078-broadway-market-hackney">
             Broadway Market
            </a>
           </h2>
           <a href="/things-to-do/place/14261078-broadway-market-hackney">
            <span class="delayed-image" data-alt="" data-class="hero" data-height="360" data-imageprop="image" data-src="http://cdn.londonandpartners.com/assets/attractions/shopping/59916-640x360-broadway_market_640.jpg" data-title="Broadway Market  Bex Walton" data-width="640">
            </span>
           </a>
           <p>
            <b>
             Broadway Market
            </b>
            in Hackney sells an eclectic mix of goods from local farmers, artists and craftspeople in order to temp the customer to sample and buy. Shop here for delicious ingredients, tasty ready-made food, exciting and unusual clothes and gifts as well as furniture, lighting and more!
           </p>
           <a class="readmore" href="/things-to-do/place/14261078-broadway-market-hackney" title="Read more about Broadway Market">
            Read More
           </a>
          </div>
          <div class="vlSnCallout640 snippet cf">
           <h2>
            <a href="/things-to-do/place/281407-camden-lock">
             Camden Lock Market
            </a>
           </h2>
           <a href="/things-to-do/place/281407-camden-lock">
            <span class="delayed-image" data-alt="" data-class="hero" data-height="360" data-imageprop="image" data-src="http://cdn.londonandpartners.com/visit/general-london/areas/camden/78054-640x360-camden-lock-market-wraps-640.jpg" data-title="Camden Lock Market. Image: Michael Heffernan" data-width="640">
            </span>
           </a>
           <p>
            <b>
             Camden Lock
            </b>
            is the central hub of Camden's huge market, which is home to more than 100 shops and stalls. Here you'll find an extensive range of goods from designer clothes and accessories, to one-off pieces of art, food and furnishings. There's also designer workshops, studios, stalls, shops, cafes, restaurants and bars.
           </p>

           <h2>
            <a href="/things-to-do/place/285623-columbia-road-flower-market">
