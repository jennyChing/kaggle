        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Seasonal Skinny Cocktail: Cranberry + Vanilla
        </h1>
       </div>
       <div class="format_text entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3385" height="700" src="http://thefromagette.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/5.jpg" title="5" width="433"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Oh the weather outside is frightful, but this cocktail is so delightful!
         <br/>
         And since weve no place to go, Let it snow! Let it snow! Let it snow!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3386" height="433" src="http://thefromagette.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/41.jpg" title="4" width="700"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Now this is a cocktail I can really get behind: it contains more than a days worth of vitamin C and is sugar free! I dont know about you, but Id like to see it replace the cabbage-soup diet. These tangy, ruby-red berries are an integral part of the holiday tradition, and as a potent vehicle of important antioxidants, we should enjoy them in abundance while the season allows
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Consider this my Rx for making spirits bright.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://thefromagette.com/blog/2011/11/25/seasonal-skinny-cocktail-cranberry-vanilla/print/" rel="nofollow" title="Print This Recipe">
          <img alt="Print This Recipe" class="WP-PrintIcon" src="http://thefromagette.com/blog/wp-content/plugins/wp-print/images/printer_famfamfam.gif" style="border: 0px;" title="Print This Recipe"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://thefromagette.com/blog/2011/11/25/seasonal-skinny-cocktail-cranberry-vanilla/print/" rel="nofollow" title="Print This Recipe">
          Print This Recipe
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         juice + zest of 3 large navel oranges
         <br/>
         2 12oz bags fresh cranberries, rinsed and picked over
         <br/>
         1/2 cup Truvia
         <br/>
         2 cups water
         <br/>
         3 cups vanilla vodka [I like Absolut]
         <br/>
         1 large bottle sparkling water
        </p>
        <p>
         Puree the juice, zest, whole cranberries and 2 cups water in blender, then push through a sieve to remove solids. Transfer into a pitcher, add the Truvia and vodka and mix. To serve: fill each glass with ice, add vodka mixture to the 3/4 point [or to taste] and top off with sparkling water. Give it a gentle stir and garnish with orange zest and cranberries.
        </p>
        <p>
