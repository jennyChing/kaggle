         13 Best Airshows In The World
        </h1>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="fontsize13">
         Posted on
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/blog/13-best-airshows-in-the-world/" rel="bookmark" title="5:00 am">

        <p>

        <h3>
         <strong>
          The Paris Air Show (France, June  Biennial)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img align="left" alt="clip_image001" border="0" height="176" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image001_thumb.jpg" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image001" width="244"/>
         The Paris Air Show (
         <i>
          Le Bourget Air Show
         </i>
         ) in France, is the worlds oldest and largest air show. Started in 1909, its held in June on odd-numbered years at Le Bourget Airport and brings in approximately 400,000 visitors.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Air Show typically starts with 4 days closed to the public for professionals followed by Friday-Sunday that is open to the public showcasing static and aerobatic displays.The Paris Air Show is mostly a large commercial event demonstrating military and civilian aircrafts to potential customers.
         <br/>
         <span id="more-1541">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Royal International Air Tattoo (RIAT) (Gloucestershire, UK, July  Annually)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image003.jpg">
          <img align="left" alt="clip_image003" border="0" height="164" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image003_thumb.jpg" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image003" width="244"/>
         </a>
         The Royal International Air TattooatRAF Fairford,United Kingdom is theworlds largest military air show. The RIAT gathers military aircraft and military display teams from all over the world. The original air tattoo was held at North Weald Airfield in Essex in 1971 with only 100 aircrafts participating. Today, RIAT typically attracts an average 160,000+ spectators. The show is held annually over the 3
         <sup>
          rd
         </sup>
         weekend in July.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          MAKS (Moscow, Russia, Biennial)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image005.jpg">
          <img align="left" alt="clip_image005" border="0" height="184" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image005_thumb.jpg" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image005" width="244"/>
         </a>
         MAKS is an International Air Show held near Moscow, Russia on Zhukovskiy LII airfield. MAKS is the biggest air show and aerospace trade show in Russia and has become an important event in Russian business.The first show, Mosaeroshow-92, was held in 1992. Since 1993, it was renamed to its current name and the air show is held biennially typically in the last week of August.
        </p>

        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          RAF Waddington International Air Show (Waddington, UK, July  Annually)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image007.png">
          <img align="left" alt="clip_image007" border="0" height="158" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image007_thumb.png" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image007" width="244"/>
         </a>
         The Royal Air Forces largest Air Show is held Annually at RAF Waddington and is known as the RAF Waddington International Air Show. The RAF Waddington International Air Show was first established in 1995 and takes place annually in the first week of July.The air show features participants from around the World and highlights the work of the RAF (Royal Air Force) and its allies. It attracts over 140,000 visitors and has become the largest of all Royal Air Force shows.
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Bournemouth Air Festival (Bournemouth, UK, August  Annually)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image009.jpg">
          <img align="left" alt="clip_image009" border="0" height="164" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image009_thumb.jpg" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image009" width="244"/>
         </a>
         Europes largest free annual air show is the Bournemouth Air Festival held on the Bournemouth seafront. The air show features aircraft from the Royal Air Force and Royal Navy as well as civil aviation displays.It takes place over the course of four days in August and has attracted over 1,300,000 spectators in previous years.
        </p>

        <h3>
         <strong>
          Eastbourne International Airshow (Eastbourne, UK, August  Annually)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image011.jpg">
          <img align="left" alt="clip_image011" border="0" height="159" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image011_thumb.jpg" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image011" width="314"/>
         </a>
         Eastbourne International Air Show is the worlds biggest free seafront air show. The event shows aerobatic displays as well as static. Some of the most prominent displays are the Red Arrows display team, the Battle of Britain memorial flight and aircrafts from the RAF, USAF and many other.It is a 4-day international event held annually in August.
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          E
         </strong>
         <strong>
          AA AirVenture Oshkosh (Oshkosh, USA, August  Annually)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image013.jpg">
          <img align="left" alt="clip_image013" border="0" height="168" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image013_thumb.jpg" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image013" width="257"/>
         </a>
         The annual EAA AirVenture Oshkosh convention is organized by the Experimental Aircraft Association and is known for its crowded fly-in of visitors. The air show includes multiple displays as well as talks by influential people in aviation.During the show, which lasts for weeks, the aircraft control tower is the busiest control tower in the world (WOW!). Its estimated that 10,000  15,000 aircrafts visit the airfield during the fly-in each year and approximately 500,000 people attend.
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          The Reno Air Races (Reno, USA, September  Annually)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image015.jpg">
          <img align="left" alt="clip_image015" border="0" height="171" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image015_thumb.jpg" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image015" width="254"/>
         </a>
         The Reno Air Races, also known as the National Championship Air Races, take place annually in September at the Reno Stead Airport a few miles north of Reno, Nevada. The event includes many days of qualifying followed by four and a half days of multi-aircraft heat racing ending with the Unlimited Class Gold Race. The event also features civil air show acts and military flight demonstrations between races, plus vendor areas and a large civil and military static aircraft display.
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Aero India (Bangalore, India, February  Biennial)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <img align="left" alt="clip_image017" border="0" height="164" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image017_thumb.jpg" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image017" width="244"/>
         Aero India is a biennial air show held in Bangalore, India at the Yelahanka Air Force Station. It was first held in 1996 and since then has become one of the largest air shows in the world. The show features static and aerial displays from major aircraft manufacturing companies.In past years it drew 607 exhibitors from over 25 countries making it the largest air show in Asia. Some of the main attractions include the Surya Kiran &amp; Russian Knights aerobatic teams as well as the HAL light combat helicopter and many more.
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Abbotsford International Airshow (British Columbia, Canada, August  Annually)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image019.jpg">
          <img align="left" alt="clip_image019" border="0" height="179" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image019_thumb.jpg" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image019" width="237"/>
         </a>
         The Abbotsford Airshow is Canadas largest air show. It was started in 1962 by the Abbotsford Flying Club. The show features both military and civilian aircraft from Canada and the United States. The most notable performances at the air show are the Snowbirds, Thunderbirds &amp; Blue Angel demonstration teams as well as the Sky Hawks parachute team.The annual attendance of the air show is on average 125,000+ people.
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Australian International Airshow (Melbourne, Australia, March  Biennial)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image021.jpg">
          <img align="left" alt="clip_image021" border="0" height="164" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image021_thumb.jpg" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image021" width="244"/>
         </a>
         The Australian International Airshow is held every two years at Avalon Airport. It is known as the largest air show in the Southern Hemisphere. The show regularly shows planes from the Royal Australian Air Force, United States Navy and the United States Air Force. The air shows both static and aerobatic displays as well as a special Friday Night Alight show where military jets and gliders performed aerobatic displays with a laser show and flare drops.
        </p>

        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          CAF AIRSHO (Midland, Texas, October  Annually)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image023.jpg">
          <img align="left" alt="clip_image023" border="0" height="184" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image023_thumb.jpg" style="margin-left: 0px; display: inline; margin-right: 0px; border: 0px;" title="clip_image023" width="244"/>
         </a>
         The CAF AIRSHO is the largest Warbird air show in the world with over 80 warbirds flying per show. The show is held by the Commemorative Air Force annually in Midland, TX each October. AIRSHO gives many the excuse to get together annually as the aircrafts tend to be spread out over large geographic distances and most ghost squadron aircrafts rarely fly more than a few hours from their home base.
        </p>

        </h3>
        <h3>
         <strong>
          Singapore Airshow (Changi, Singapore, February  Biennial)
         </strong>
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image025.jpg">
          <img align="left" alt="clip_image025" border="0" height="151" hspace="12" src="http://www.torontoairways.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/clip_image025_thumb.jpg" title="clip_image025" width="244"/>
         </a>
         TheSingapore Airshow, formerly known as
         <i>
          Changi International Airshow
         </i>
         , is held biennially since2008. Asias largest and one of the most important aerospace and defence exhibitions in the world.The event was launched as a partnership between the Singaporean agenciesCivil Aviation Authority of Singaporeand the Defence Science and Technology Agency after the relocation ofAsian Aerospacefrom Singapore.
        </p>
        <p>

         <i>
          Toronto Airways Ltd., Canadas largest flight training school, has trained over 10,000 pilots since 1963. We have five flight simulators, 50 flight instructors, and 45 planes. Flight training courses at Toronto Buttonville Municipal Airport begin each semester and run for 14 weeks. Book an Introductory Flight or call 905-477-8110 and get your dreams of flying off the ground today.
         </i>
