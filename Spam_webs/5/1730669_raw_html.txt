                        &lt;h1 class="post-title"&gt;
                            &lt;a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/hair/do-you-do-beachy-waves/"&gt;Do You Do Beachy Waves? &lt;/a&gt;
                        &lt;/h1&gt;
                        &lt;p class="post-author"&gt;By &lt;a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/about/" rel="author"&gt;Karen&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
                        &lt;p class="post-categories"&gt;Published in: &lt;a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/category/hair/" rel="category tag"&gt;Hair&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/category/just-for-fun/" rel="category tag"&gt;Just For Fun&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
                        &lt;ul class="post-actions"&gt;
                            &lt;li&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/hair/do-you-do-beachy-waves/#comments"&gt;Leave a comment &raquo;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
                            &lt;li&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/hair/do-you-do-beachy-waves/#share"&gt;Share this post &raquo;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
                        &lt;/ul&gt;
                    &lt;/header&gt;
                
                
                
                &lt;div class="post-content"&gt;

&lt;p&gt;&lt;img src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/07/me-beachy-waves.jpg" alt="Do you do beachy waves?" title="Do you do beachy waves?" width="450" height="450" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-26646 pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/hair/do-you-do-beachy-waves/" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Are any of you gals going to the beach this weekend? I wish it were warmer here right now because I sure could use a beachy break. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The bay out here is always pins-and-needles cold &#8212; not exactly bikini friendly &#8212; even when it&#8217;s hot outside, and most swimmers will wear a wet suit in the water. &lt;/p&gt;&lt;!--Ad Injection:top--&gt;
&lt;div style='margin-top:0px;padding-top:10px;padding-bottom:10px;'&gt;&lt;center&gt;&lt;div style="margin:0 0 0 -15px"&gt;
&lt;script async src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js"&gt;&lt;/script&gt;
&lt;!-- 300x250 MBB Inside Posts --&gt;
&lt;ins class="adsbygoogle"
     style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px"
     data-ad-client="ca-pub-6078240274198210"
     data-ad-slot="3843154284"&gt;&lt;/ins&gt;
&lt;script&gt;
(adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
&lt;/script&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/center&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;I would, too, if they didn&#8217;t make me look and feel like a sausage, bluh!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Even when it&#8217;s too cold for the beach, I still like to dress the part, which is why I&#8217;ve been all about sundresses, flip flops, sunglasses and hair with beachy waves for the past few weeks. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;There are different ways to do &#8217;em, but here&#8217;s an approach that I use. &lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;How I do my beachy waves&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;ol&gt;
&lt;li&gt;For me it all starts the night before. I get better results in the morning when I&#8217;ve washed and conditioned my hair before bed. Lately, I&#8217;ve been using and loving &lt;a href="http://livingproof.com/product/nofrizz/from_the_lab.php"&gt;Living Proof No Frizz Shampoo and Conditioner ($48)&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;After my shower, I wrap my hair with a highly-absorbent microfiber towel like the &lt;a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/product-reviews/aquis-hair-towel-a-hair-do/"&gt;Aquis ($18; hello, awesomeness)&lt;/a&gt; for 15-20 minutes. Then, I remove the Aquis, which has sucked out most of the excess water, and air dry until bed. &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;Right before bed I apply and comb through a leave-in conditioner like &lt;a href="http://www.sephora.com/browse/product.jhtml?id=P244511&#038;shouldPaginate=true&#038;categoryId=5936"&gt;Living Proof Leave-In Conditioner ($24)&lt;/a&gt;, concentrating on the mid-shaft to the ends. &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;With my hair conditioned, I put it in a loose French braid; the thicker the pieces in the braid, the bigger the waves the next morning. I secure the end of the braid with a ponytail elastic band (I like these summery, bright bands from &lt;a href="http://www.bumbleandbumble.com/product/spp.tmpl?CATEGORY_ID=CAT957&#038;PRODUCT_ID=4748"&gt;Bumble &#038; Bumble&#8217;s Bandball, $20&lt;/a&gt;), and then I hit the haaaaay. &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;The next morning, I remove the braid and gently run my fingers through the waves, misting with &lt;a href="http://www.bumbleandbumble.com/product/spp.tmpl?CATEGORY_ID=CAT907&#038;PRODUCT_ID=3964"&gt;Bumble &#038; Bumble Curl Conscious Reactivating Mist ($22)&lt;/a&gt;. I think it gives the waves some extra spring. &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;For extra definition and hold, I spray on a light hold styling product like &lt;a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/product-reviews/redken-layer-lift-big-hair-dreams-do-come-true/"&gt;Redken Layer Lift ($15)&lt;/a&gt; on the upper and middle layers; when I want a softer, slightly messier look, I&#8217;ll use &lt;a href="http://www.bumbleandbumble.com/product/spp.tmpl?CATEGORY_ID=CAT72&#038;PRODUCT_ID=221"&gt;Bumble &#038; Bumble Surf Spray ($22)&lt;/a&gt; instead. &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;To finish, I scrunch the ends and let the products dry.  &lt;img src="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" alt=":)" class="wp-smiley pib-hover-img" data-pin-url="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/hair/do-you-do-beachy-waves/" /&gt; &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ol&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;Drugstore alternatives&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;You can also substitute any of the above products with the following drugstore goodies&#8230;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Shampoo and conditioner:&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/product-reviews/suave-professionals-vibrant-shine-shampoo-and-conditioner-a-winning-7-drugstore-combo/"&gt;Suave Professionals Vibrant Shine Shampoo and Conditioner ($3.50 each, $7 together)&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Leave-in conditioner:&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;a href="http://www.makeupandbeautyblog.com/product-reviews/aveeno-nourish-hair-care-collection-knot-your-average-hair-care/"&gt;Aveeno Nourish+ Condition Leave-In Treatment ($7)&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Ponytail elastics:&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;a href="http://goody.com/Products/Accessories/Ponytail_Holders/Elastics/Elastics.aspx"&gt;Goody StayPut Ellastics ($4)&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Curl re-activating mist:&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;a href="http://www.drugstore.com:80/products/prod.asp?pid=138788&#038;catid=73339"&gt;CurlFriends Texturizing Mist ($11)&lt;/a&gt; or &lt;a href="http://www.walgreens.com/store/product.jsp?CATID=100322&#038;navAction=jump&#038;navCount=2&#038;nug=VPD&#038;skuid=sku5436754&#038;id=prod5437887"&gt;Garnier Fructis Wave Enhancing Spray ($3.50)&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
&lt;li&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Hair styling product:&lt;/strong&gt; &lt;a href="http://www.walgreens.com/store/product.jsp?CATID=100322&#038;navAction=jump&#038;navCount=0&#038;nug=VPD&#038;skuid=sku3120376&#038;id=prod3121543"&gt;Aussie Sprunch Spray ($3.50)&lt;/a&gt; or &lt;a href="http://www.drugstore.com:80/products/prod.asp?pid=182172&#038;catid=32548"&gt;Sunsilk Waves of Envy Sea Mist ($4)&lt;/a&gt; &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;font style="font-size:1.4em;"&gt;Do you do beachy waves? What products/techniques do you use to get the look?&lt;/font&gt;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/blockquote&gt;
