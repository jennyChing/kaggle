         <h1>
          Wedding Photo Ideas: 10 Creative Ways To Pose
         </h1>
         <h4>
          May 21, 2014, 10:55 a.m.
         </h4>
        </header>
        <a href="http://www.crazyforus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/wedding-photo-idea-heart.jpg">
         <img alt="wedding-photo-idea-heart" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14433" height="550" src="http://www.crazyforus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/wedding-photo-idea-heart.jpg" width="550"/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Many times wedding photos can be cheesy and unoriginal. But if you want wedding photos that look and feel authentic and full of love, you can find
        <em>
         <a href="http://www.crazyforus.com/?p=10439" target="_blank">
          amazing wedding photo ideas and poses to show your wedding photographer
         </a>
        </em>
        as a guide.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Since you want to make sure that you and your wedding photographer are on the same page about your wedding photos, it is always a good idea
        <em>
         <a href="http://www.crazyforus.com/?p=11513" target="_blank">
          to meet with your wedding photographer well in advance,
         </a>
        </em>
        to discuss your wedding photo ideas.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        There are some
        <em>
         <a href="http://www.crazyforus.com/?p=13870" target="_blank">
          great photo ideas and poses for your bridesmaid
         </a>
        </em>
        as well as for
        <em>
         <a href="http://www.crazyforus.com/?p=14023" target="_blank">
          your groomsmen.
         </a>
        </em>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Here are more wedding photo ideas: 10 creative ways to pose,
        <a href="http://www.weddingbells.ca/planning/photo-video/wedding-photo-ideas/" target="_blank">
         <em>
          from weddingbells:
         </em>
        </a>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        While a traditional wedding photo taken with the bridal party and family members makes a nice keepsake, we love when couples go above and beyond with their wedding shots. Striking a unique bridal pose with your posse (or your mister!) is one way to instill your fun personalities into any wedding photo. Not to mention, they would be great printouts for you to hang around the houseor would even make nice thank-you photo cards.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Think that taking a creative wedding photo just isnt your style? There are all sorts of way to get inventive with your guests, while still keeping things classy and romantic. For example, having an intimate celebration with less than 50 people? Have your photographer gather everyone together to create a heart-shaped pose (as pictured above). Or, if youre having wedding sparklers at the end of the evening, twirl them around to create words, your wedding date, or letters (your initials) for picture-perfect wedding photos. For couples that love the idea of including props, try including a frame (big enough to fit you and your groom).
        <br/>
        <br/>
        To get you inspired for your own shots, heres a round-up of 10 fun wedding photo ideas to try.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.crazyforus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/wedding-photo-idea-love.jpg">
         <img alt="wedding-photo-idea-love" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-14434" height="750" src="http://www.crazyforus.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/wedding-photo-idea-love.jpg" width="550"/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Add "l-o-v-e" by creating the letters with your fingers for a picture-perfect shot.
