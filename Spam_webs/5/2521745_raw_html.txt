  <h3>
   <a href="../">
    Video Productions
   </a>
   :Not Knot
  </h3>
  <i>
   Not Knot
  </i>
  is a guided tour into computer-animated hyperbolic space.  It proceeds from the world of knots to their complementary spaces -- what's not a knot.  Profound theorems of recent mathematics show that most knot complements carry the structure of hyperbolic geometry, a geometry in which the sum of the three angles of a triangle is always less than 180 degrees.
