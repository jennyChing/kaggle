        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Spice Cakes
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-01-04T16:05:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 4, 2013
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Spoonful of Sugar
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         -
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/2013/01/spice-cakes/#comments">
           6 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-color="red" data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" data-pin-height="32" data-pin-shape="round" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Faspoonfulofsugardesigns.com%2F2013%2F01%2Fspice-cakes%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2F1.bp.blogspot.com%2F-_xc6_7pp3mo%2FUN7fHBZXr8I%2FAAAAAAAANlQ%2FC7kcfanCCiw%2Fs1600%2FSpice%2BCakes%2B%2B%2B%2B1.jpg&amp;description=Spice%20Cakes" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div>
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-_xc6_7pp3mo/UN7fHBZXr8I/AAAAAAAANlQ/C7kcfanCCiw/s1600/Spice+Cakes++++1.jpg">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="418" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Spice+Cakes++++1.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p>
         I am sure it is no surprise that Sarah loves to receive baking equipment, supplies and cook books for Christmas. In fact we encourage it as she then in turn bakes us delicious treats. She received a
         <a href="https://www.house.com.au/product/soffritto-12-cup-heart-pan-" target="_blank">
          12 cup Heart baking pan
         </a>
         as a gift and found the perfect recipe to test it out! Sarah baked a batch of Spice Cakes. They are a lovelymoist, spicy cake that are rolled in sugar and spice mix. They were especially delicious warm from the oven.
        </p>
        <div>
         <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-lsBtXL4FT8k/UN7h-YA4qQI/AAAAAAAANnU/rSx1uinlO5I/s1600/Spice+Cakes+++++6.jpg">
          <img alt="" border="0" height="448" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Spice+Cakes+++++6.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Spice Cakes
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTopRight">
          <img itemprop="image" src="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Spice+Cakes++++1-300x195.jpg" width="205"/>
          <div class="ERSSavePrint">
           <span class="ERSPrintBtnSpan">
            <a class="ERSPrintBtn" href="http://aspoonfulofsugardesigns.com/easyrecipe-print/2890-0/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             Print
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTimes">
          <div class="ERSTime">
           <div class="ERSTimeHeading">
            Prep time
           </div>
           <div class="ERSTimeItem">
            <time datetime="PT20M" itemprop="prepTime">
             20 mins
            </time>
           </div>

          <div class="ERSTime ERSTimeRight">

          <div class="ERSTime ERSTimeRight">
           <div class="ERSTimeHeading">

          <div class="ERSClearLeft">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSSummary" itemprop="description">
          Moist spiced cakes that are rolled in sugar and spices.
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSAuthor">
           Author:
           <span itemprop="author">
            Sarah
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSCategory">
           Recipe type:
           <span itemprop="recipeCategory">
            Baling
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSServes">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            12
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            For the spice mix:
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons cinnamon
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon nutmeg
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon mixed spice
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            For the cakes:
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup honey
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup golden syrup
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup packed dark brown sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup milk
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup plain (all purpose) flour
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoons baking powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon spice mix
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            4 tablespoons unsalted butter, kept cold
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 large egg
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            Sugar coating:
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            equal parts sugar and spice mix, combined.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat the oven to 175 C (350 F). Grease a 12 mini bundt cake pan or similar.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a small saucepan set over medium heat, stir together the honey, golden syrup, dark brown sugar and milk until the sugar is dissolved. Remove from the heat and reserve.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In a food processor or with a pastry blender, combine the flour, baking powder, spice mix, and unsalted butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Whisk the egg into the cooled milk mixture and add it to the flour mix. Pulse a couple of times until the mixture is smooth.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Divide the batter evenly among the prepared pan and cook for 20 to 25 minutes or until a knife inserted in the middle comes out free of crumbs. Let the cakes cool completely before rolling them in the sugar coating.
           </li>
          </ol>
