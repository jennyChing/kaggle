       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        BEHOLD!!!!
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       When my pal Ryan asked if Id like to join him on a trip to Kailua, Hawaii for a friends wedding, I couldnt say no! Id never been to Hawaii, and I needed to get away for a bit. :) What am I sayingI always say I need to get away for a bit. The truth is, I love to travel more than just about anything else in the world and will make up just about any excuse to do it! :)
      </p>
      <p>
       Of course, I wouldnt be
       <em>
        me
       </em>
       if I didnt drag my pal to the nearest thrift shop right away! ;) I was surprised at how typical it was. You cant even tell this is in Hawaii:
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_13489" style="width: 473px">
       <a class="colorbox" href="http://i2.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4820.jpg" rel="colorbox">
        <img alt="Huh." class=" wp-image-13489" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4820.jpg?resize=463%2C311"/>

       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Huh.

      </p>

       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Thats better!

       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        But look closer!

      </p>
      <p>
       I found a few things to play with. :) One of these things was this Mens 3x shirt!
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_13496" style="width: 373px">
       <a class="colorbox" href="http://i2.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4832.jpg" rel="colorbox">
        <img alt="It's actually quite comfy!" class=" wp-image-13496" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4832.jpg?resize=363%2C534"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Its actually quite comfy!
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       Okay, I failed to take any during pics, so Im just gonna have to explain this one. I made the neck hole the top of my new dress by tucking the collar in and buttoning up the shirt as far as I could under my arms. I added a safety pin inside to make sure it was nice n snug. THEN, I tucked the sleeves in to make funky faux pockets. :)
      </p>
      <p>
       And here it is!
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_13498" style="width: 404px">
       <a class="colorbox" href="http://i1.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4864.jpg" rel="colorbox">
        <img alt="Aloha!" class=" wp-image-13498" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4864.jpg?resize=394%2C548"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Aloha!
       </p>

      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       You can see how Ive cinched the waist of my new frock with one of my favorite thrifted belts. I even had a cute pineapple necklace I scored the week before at a local theatres costume sale!
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_13500" style="width: 345px">
       <a class="colorbox" href="http://i1.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4869.jpg" rel="colorbox">
        <img alt="It's pretty great, isn't it?  :)" class=" wp-image-13500" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4869.jpg?resize=335%2C523"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Its pretty great, isnt it? :)
       </p>

      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       My new dress was comfortable as could be and got tons of compliments from people who had no idea that it was actually a shirtuntil I told them of course! I keep no refashion secrets!
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_13497" style="width: 385px">
       <a class="colorbox" href="http://i0.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4862.jpg" rel="colorbox">
        <img alt="Sort of has a 60's feel to it, doesn't it?" class=" wp-image-13497" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4862.jpg?resize=375%2C490"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Sort of has a 60s feel to it, doesnt it?

      </p>
      <p>
       Ryan and I had an amazing time checking out Oahu!
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_13499" style="width: 482px">
       <a class="colorbox" href="http://i2.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4867.jpg" rel="colorbox">
        <img alt="Hello from Paradise!" class=" wp-image-13499" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/refashionista.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/DSC_4867.jpg?resize=472%2C325"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Hello from Paradise!

       We ate some amazing local grub!

        Fire Spinners!

       And the view from our house just couldnt be beat.

       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        *sigh*

      </p>
      <p>
       New-Sew refashions can be great for traveling. I didnt even end up keeping this shirt, as I always pack
       <em>
        extremely
       </em>
       light and didnt have any room in my luggage. So, I just laundered it and re-donated it. More money for charity, AND I had a cute new dress for the day! What a win/win!
      </p>
      <p>
       Cheers!
