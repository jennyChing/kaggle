        Pit Bull Hero Saves Little Girl from Dog Attack
       </h1>
       <p class="post-meta">
        <img alt="" src="http://www.aplaceforpitbulls.com/wp-content/themes/headlines/images/ico-time.png"/>
        April 7, 2015
        <span class="comments">

        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         A Family in West Palm Beach, Florida is now calling their pit bull a hero after he put a stop to a vicious dog attacking their five year old daughter. WPTV News states that Remayah, a five-year-old girl, was riding her bike down the street when a Labrador retriever dog mix jumped out from her neighbors yards and attacked her. The Labrador retriever latched onto the girls face and before Remayahs mother Lucila Hernandez, could get to her daughter, the family Pit bull jumped into action.
        </p>
        <p>
         Trigger, the familys pit bull dog, jumped over the fence and defended his owner by attacking the Labrador dog.
        </p>
        <p style="color: #1a1a1a;">
         Hes like a hero. Hes like a protective dog. We taught him to be protective and caring for the family, Hernandez said. Hes kind of a super hero, said Remayah.
        </p>
        <p>
         After Triggers heroic actions, Remayah received less injuries than what she would have received if the Pit bull did not interfere. Remayah is now seeking treatment to repair the severe facial injuries received for the Labrador attack with a full recovery prognosis. According to examiner.com, the Labrador retriever mix was seriously injured after Triggers interference.
        </p>
