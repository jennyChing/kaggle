        <article class="post-7215 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-charity category-music tag-belinda-carlisle" id="post-7215">
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           <a href="http://celebritiesdogood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/belinda-carlisle.jpg" rel="dofollow">
            <img alt="belinda-carlisle" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-7217" height="360" src="http://celebritiesdogood.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/belinda-carlisle.jpg" width="640"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Belinda Carlisle, one of my favorite pop stars from back in the late 80s, is on a mission to raise money on a motorcycle.  And get this, she JUST got her motorcycle license to accomplish this goal!
          </p>
          <p>
           According to Digital Spy,
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            The Go-Gos star Belinda Carlisle has revealed plans to drive an auto-rickshaw across India to raise money for charity.  The Heaven Is a Place on Earth singer has just got her motorcycle license in Los Angeles in preparation for the 1,135 mile journey.
           </p>
           <p>
            Im doing a rickshaw ride in India in March from Kolkata to Udaipur, Belinda shared in a video shot in her LA living room with her pug Bam Bam.
           </p>
           <p>
            So why not make a donation in somebodys name this holiday season  that person who is impossible to buy for.
           </p>
           <p>
            Carlisle has a fundraising goal of $25,000 (16,000) to help the Animal People Alliance (APA), which works to improve the lives of slavery survivors with economic empowerment and education strategies.
           </p>
           <p>
            The charity helps to train and employ vulnerable youth and women as veterinary technicians, dog handlers and animal caregivers in India.
           </p>
           <p>
            Fans of the singer can donate to get signed copies of Carlisles studio albums, a Skype call from the star and a chance to ride the rickshaw with the singer on the last day of the journey.
           </p>
           <p>
            The US singer heads to Rishikesh in the new year to undertake a Kundalini Yoga teacher-training course, before setting off on the West Bengal-to-Rajasthan trip.
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           You can donate to Carlisles fundraising rickshaw trip across India
           <a href="https://www.crowdrise.com/animalpeoplerickshawride/fundraiser/belindacarlisle" rel="nofollow">
            here
           </a>
           .
          </p>
