         Its done. Ive hopped on the train for this trend. My ghost chair is on its way in the mail and I cant WAIT. In my excitement and anticipation, Ive been looking around at tons of ghost chair styling inspiration. Its such a great small space solution since it looks like it takes up no room and it also works with so many different styles, which I wouldnt have suspected on my own. It can be chic, eclectic, modern, whimsical, elegant, anything!
