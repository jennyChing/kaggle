              <h1 class="title">
               5 Steps to Creating a Mobile Command Center for Your Real Estate Business
              </h1>
              <div class="post-meta">
               <p class="meta-info">
                Posted  by
                <a href="http://realestatecommunities.com/author/justin/" rel="author" title="Posts by Justin">
                 Justin
                </a>
                on Sep 15, 2013 in
                <a href="http://realestatecommunities.com/category/real-estate-agents/real-estate-marketing-strategies/" rel="category tag">
                 Real Estate Marketing Strategies
                </a>
                |
                <a href="http://realestatecommunities.com/5-steps-to-creating-a-mobile-command-center-for-your-real-estate-business/#respond" title="Comment on 5 Steps to Creating a Mobile Command Center for Your Real Estate Business">
                 0 comments
                </a>
               </p>
              </div>
              <!-- end .post-meta -->
              <p>
               In the real estate business, its essential to have access to everything, no matter where you are. There are many benefits to taking your business mobile, but its not something that you can do overnight. Here are the steps that you need to take to turn your business mobile.
              </p>
              <p>
               <b>
                Determine What You Need
               </b>
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="http://realestatecommunities.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Cell-Phones.jpg">
                <img alt="Cell Phones" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3206" height="480" src="http://realestatecommunities.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Cell-Phones.jpg" width="640"/>
               </a>
              </p>
              <p style="text-align: center;">
               Image via
               <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/honou/3792130154/">
                Flickr
               </a>
               by Honou
              </p>
              <p>
               The very first thing that you should do once you decide to take your real estate business mobile is determine how youre going to take it mobile. Youre obviously going to need some sort of laptop or tablet so you can show off other property to interested people, but what about mobile devices? Youll want to keep all of your employees connected to each other, so you can discuss any topic that may come up to ensure that you close the deal.
              </p>
              <p>
               The best option for this is the new
               <a href="http://us.blackberry.com/software/smartphones/blackberry-10-os.html">
                BlackBerry 10
               </a>
               line of phones. With the new BlackBerry 10 operating system, youll be able to share your screen with other employees. This helps you share documents visually, regardless of where you are. With BlackBerry Balance, your employees will be able to separate their all of their contracts and leases from from their personal items, ensuring things will never get mixed up.
              </p>
              <p>
               <b>
                Register With a Cloud Service
               </b>
              </p>
              <p>
               Another problem with having a mobile office is that it can be difficult to keep documents up to date. E-mail is a great way to stay in touch and share small files, but what about presentations and larger files? Cloud storage is a great solution to those looking to make the jump and make their business mobile.
              </p>
              <p>
               With cloud storage, you can store all of the files you want. If you need more space, you can get more for a small fee. If an employee needs an updated contract, they can just log in and download it. Multiple employees can work on the same file regardless of where theyre at, so changes can be made immediately. As long as theres an Internet connection, theyre good to go.
              </p>
              <p>
               You can also move your entire business to the cloud if you need to. By moving your business to the cloud, youre saving a ton of money on electric bills. If you need to upgrade to add-on to your hardware, a simple phone call can take care of all of your issues. If you want, you can set up a portal for customers and interested parties to look at estate and property maps.
              </p>
              <p>
               <b>
                Keep Everyone Up-To-Date
               </b>
              </p>
              <p>
               One of the biggest problems about going mobile is that people arent on the same page. With Enterprise Mobility Management software on the BlackBerry 10 OS, youll be able to push every update to your employees, regardless of what operating system theyre using.
              </p>
              <p>
               Enterprise Mobility Management also offers a full range of security needs, and youll be able to restrict access to certain documents if you feel the need to do so. Youll always be behind a firewall, so no matter what device youre connecting to, you can feel confident that any information that youre sharing is being shared through a secure connection.
              </p>
              <p>
               <b>
                Accessorize
               </b>
              </p>
              <p>
               If youre going to have a mobile business, youll have to prepare your employees to be mobile. The first thing youll need is a carrying case for the device you wouldnt want the device to shatter if it accidentally falls!
              </p>
              <p>
               The next things youll need are the basic accessories a bluetooth headset so your employees can talk and use their hands while showing a property, a wireless keyboard for when typing on a mobile phone or touchscreen becomes a little cumbersome, and a backup battery pack for when you need a little bit more juice. It would also be wise to invest in a power strip so you can avoid power surges, a car charger so you can charge on the go, and a card reader, so your employees can receive a deposit on a property.
              </p>
              <p>
               <b>
                Maintenance
               </b>
              </p>
              <p>
               Dedicate a time during the week to doing maintenance on all systems and devices. This shouldnt be done during business, and since it can be an automated process, youre able to do itovernight. Use reliable anti-virus software and optimization apps to keep all of your devices in top shape. Thanks to mobile device management, you can push updates to everyone simultaneously, so you can do this on a certain night each week or every night, if you wish.
              </p>
              <p>
               If youre using a cloud service, make sure to set the phone to back up daily, if not hourly. If the worst happens and they lose their phone, youll be able to lock and remotely wipe their device, but in doing so, youll lose every contract that you have.
              </p>
              <p>
               Even a fear years ago, mobilizing your business was just a pipe dream. Thanks to mobile management software and cloud services, that pipe dream is now a reality. More people than ever are telecommuting and working in the field and because of that, more companies are flourishing thanks to being able to hire better workers regardless of their physical location. With proper planning, youll be able to successfully mobilize your real estate business and see a positive impact.
