        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Baked BBQ Zucchini Chips
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-01-27T19:45:39+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          January 27, 2014
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://afewshortcuts.com/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Amanda @ A Few Short Cuts
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://afewshortcuts.com/2014/01/baked-bbq-zucchini-chips/#comments">
           2 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Baked-BBQ-Zucchini-Chips" class="wp-image-95117 aligncenter" height="480" src="http://afewshortcuts.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Baked-BBQ-Zucchini-Chips.png" width="320"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I am going to be honest. As hard as I try, I have a child who wouldnt touch a green vegetable to save his life. I cant get mad at him. I was the same way when I was little.although I would eat lettuce and cucumbers. I cant even get him to do that. Oh, but this sweet child would eat a bag of BBQ Chips beginning to end without batting an eye.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         He is a snacker. He cant help it. I am guessing it is genetic because I know certain people in my family who are the same way. We could go without meals but just snack all day long. Some say it is healthier to eat small bits here and there, but it is frustrating when you are trying to get ALL 3 of your kids to eat healthy and 1 simply refuses.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Baked BBQ Zucchini Chips-1-6" class=" wp-image-95121 aligncenter" height="240" src="http://afewshortcuts.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Baked-BBQ-Zucchini-Chips-1-6.jpg" width="360"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         SABOTAGE! YEP, I am not above trickery. Sometimes you have to pull a trick out of your sleeve to get people to realize they are just plain stubborn. LOL So I made a batch of BBQ Chips.Zucchini BBQ Chips. He had no idea!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Baked BBQ Zucchini Chips-1-5" class="wp-image-95120 aligncenter" height="240" src="http://afewshortcuts.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Baked-BBQ-Zucchini-Chips-1-5.jpg" width="360"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         The BBQ Seasoning itself is easy to mix together and makes plenty for a couple batches. If you like them less spicy, cut back on the chili powder a bit.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Baked BBQ Zucchini Chips-1-4" class=" wp-image-95119 aligncenter" height="360" src="http://afewshortcuts.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Baked-BBQ-Zucchini-Chips-1-4.jpg" width="240"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Slice the zucchini thin, coat it with a little olive oil, sprinkle some seasoning and bake. It is that simple. The verdict. I put the plate on the table, and by the time they were half gone I think my grin gave it away. MOM.what are these? ROFL YEP.Dude, you just ate Zucchini.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <strong>
          SCORE
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         Mom=1
         <br/>
         Kids=0

           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2-3 zucchini sliced thin (1/8 inch)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            olive oil
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           BBQ Seasoning
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 teaspoons chili powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tablespoon sugar
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 tablespoon salt
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon garlic powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 teaspoon paprika
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon cumin
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon mustard powder
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             teaspoon pepper
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Preheat the oven to 300 Degrees F. Cover 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Slice the zucchinis very thin, about  inch thick. Place in a bowl and toss to coat with a couple tablespoons of olive oil.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            In another small bowl mix all of the spices together.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Place the zucchini slices in a single layer on the baking sheets. Sprinkle with some of the bbq seasoning and bake for 40 minutes.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Remove from the oven and flip the zucchinis over. Sprinkle the other side with some of the seasoning and return to the oven for 20 minutes. Keep an eye on them so they do not over bake.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Cool and enjoy. Store uneaten chips in an airtight container.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="endeasyrecipe" style="display: none" title="style002">
          3.2.2265
         </div>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         These chips are also a super healthy alternative for anyone. They are gluten free, paleo, vegan, super simple, and very tasty.
