    <div class="tg-cont-left">
     <div class="tg-info-cont">
      <span class="tg-article-name">
       <strong>
        Sirocco at Lebua State Tower
       </strong>
      </span>
     </div>
     <div class="tg-banner">
      <div id="container">
       <img height="360px" src="/services/ImageData/Travel_guide/ma131000091.jpg" width="675px"/>
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="clear">
     </div>
     <span class="tg-article-desc">
      <br/>
      <div>
       <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 10pt;">
        <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: small;">
         Undoubtedly Bangkoks most romantic spot, the
         <a alt="Sirocco Rooftop Bar and Rest." href="http://www.hoteltrip.com/TravelGuide/Thailand/Bangkok/Sirocco_Rooftop_Restaurant_Bar.html" target="_self" title="Sirocco Rooftop Bar and Rest.">
          Sirocco
         </a>
         Restaurant offers delectable cuisine, flavorful drinks, and the experience of a lifetime. Usually worth a stopover during a special occasion due to his reputation for exorbitant menus, I would personally pick this place for the perfect proposal (provided we stay married forever).
        </span>
       </p>
       <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 10pt;">
        <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: small;">
         If you happen to be in Bangkok city with your wife/husband/boyfriend/girlfriend- this setting would completely sweep them off their feet. Be that as it may- your anniversary, an extravagant proposal, birthday, or a simple night out; Sirocco Restaurant is worth every penny. You arent paying for the food or drinks; youre paying for the view. Overlooking two sides of Bangkok, the views are completely juxtaposed. One facing Thailands cultural symbol being the breathtaking Chao Phraya River, followed by a view of Bangkoks commercial side- Sathorn and Silom. Depending on your partiality, you are free to choose which side to be seated on; or stand when chilling by the bar.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 10pt;">
        <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: small;">
         As you enter the colossal sized lobby of the State Tower Bangkok, you are initially inquired by the respectful staff in regards to your whereabouts. Simply quote Sirocco. Consequently, youre directed to the elevator shaft taking you up 52 floors. Followed by another elevator field trip to the 63
         <sup>
          rd
         </sup>
         floor. Being the second tallest building amongst Bangkoks skyline, you are simply on cloud nine.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 10pt;">
        <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: small;">
         Once you disembark, you are faced with a school of elegant instrument entertainers. Just like the scene in the Titanic, you inevitably feel affluent. Faced with the towering height of the building, the zesty breeze creeps up on you so dont forget to wear a sweater. As you are drawn near to the enormous seating/standing area, you are once again inquired upon your intentions. Dinner or drinks sir?.
         <span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 10pt;">
        <span style="font-family: helvetica; font-size: small;">
         Depending on your choice of preference, you can succumb to either dining upon a lavish ten course meal, or simply just stand by the bar and experience Bangkoks vista.
         <span>
         </span>
         Ten course meals consist of a wide array of continental foods which are braised and cooked to perfection. Stay as long as you please, breathe in Bangkoks unpolluted air for a change, and unwind.
        </span>
       </p>
