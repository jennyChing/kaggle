          is delish! And you can often find it for a good price! Its crisp, earthy, zesty, grassy, and just yummy. Hey. O. to this guy. Enjoy this with asparagus, scallops, zesty dishes, or with me on my back porch!
          <br/>
          Price: $13-18
          <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://abeautifulmess.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8358081ff69e201a3fcc465f6970b-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
           <span style="background-color: #ffffff;">
            <img alt="Simi" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8358081ff69e201a3fcc465f6970b image-full img-responsive" src="http://abeautifulmess.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8358081ff69e201a3fcc465f6970b-800wi" title="Simi"/>
           </span>
          </a>
          This next wine is a special wine to me. I used to sing jazz music at fine dining restaurants in college (Kocktails with Kat). That was a really fun season for me. With an amazing drummer and fabulous piano player next to me, I got to sit on a tall bar stool, sing my favorite jazz songs, and drink a glass of
          <a href="http://www.simiwinery.com/product/2012-SIMI-Sonoma-County-Chardonnay?pageID=47936F35-EDB3-49DF-4595-2DC3828DFB3B&amp;sortBy=DisplayOrder&amp;maxRows=12&amp;" target="_blank">
           <span style="background-color: #ffffff;">
            Simi Chardonnay
           </span>
          </a>
          . Simi is a classic chardonnay that offers a great mix of acidic and buttery textures. If youre looking for a nice crowd-pleasing chardonnay, this one is a winner.
          <br/>
          Price: $12-17
          <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://abeautifulmess.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8358081ff69e201a73d7f783e970d-popup" onclick="window.open( this.href, '_blank', 'width=640,height=480,scrollbars=no,resizable=no,toolbar=no,directories=no,location=no,menubar=no,status=no,left=0,top=0' ); return false" style="display: inline;">
           <span style="background-color: #ffffff;">
            <img alt="Souvenrain" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8358081ff69e201a73d7f783e970d image-full img-responsive" src="http://abeautifulmess.typepad.com/.a/6a00d8358081ff69e201a73d7f783e970d-800wi" title="Souvenrain"/>
           </span>
          </a>
          Last, and definitely not least, is a nice creamy chardonnay. For the price, the 2011
          <a href="http://www.totalwine.com/eng/product/souverain-chardonnay/1083750" target="_blank">
           <span style="background-color: #ffffff;">
            Souverain Chardonnay
           </span>
          </a>
          offers a buttery and smooth Chardonnay. This will go well with a risotto, scallops seared in butter, or a creamy butternut squash soup. (Most of the really buttery chards are over $30, but if you like really creamy chardonnays, these are worth the price for a special occasion.)
          <br/>
          Price: $10-15
