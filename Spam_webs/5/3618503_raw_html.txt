                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     MMMmonday - Lemon-Curry Soup w/ a linky
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5705113029799429782" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      The weather here in SC is still colder than usual... and while I can't complain (my family in Canada won't let me ;) it has made me long for more yummy soups... and this one is fabulous and lighter than some of the others out there, it's perfect for lunch with a small side salad! I got this recipe from one of my favorite sources - the chef at
                      <a href="http://wwrecipes.com/">
                       wwrecipes.com
                      </a>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       Lemon-Curry Soup
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       1/2 cup (125 ml) rice
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       6 cups (1.5 - 2 L) beef, chicken, or vegetable stock
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       3 eggs
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       3 Tbs (45 ml) lemon juice
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       1/2 tsp (2 ml) curry powder
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       Chopped fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) or parsley
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       for garnish
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       Combine the rice and stock in a pot and bring to a boil over
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       moderate heat. Reduce the heat and simmer covered until
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       the rice is tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       In a bowl, beat together
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       the eggs, lemon juice, and curry powder. Add about 1/4 cup
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       (60 ml) of the broth and stir. Add the egg mixture to the pot
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       and simmer, stirring constantly, for 2 to 3 minutes, until the
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       soup has thickened slightly. Do not boil.
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       Season with salt
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       and pepper and garnish with chopped cilantro or parsley.
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       Serves 4 to 6.
                      </span>
                      <span class="Apple-style-span">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
                     <span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: 'Trebuchet MS', sans-serif;">
                      <br/>
                     </span>
                     <br/>
