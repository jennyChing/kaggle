        <h1 itemprop="name">
         Hugh Jackman discusses 'The Wolverine' details
        </h1>
        <p class="article_sell">
         Actor hints that 'X-Men' character Jean Grey will figure heavily in film - despite being dead...
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="article_social">
        <div class="socialbuttons pw-widget pw-counter-none pw-size-medium" pw:layout="horizontal">
         <a class="pw-button-facebook pw-look-native pw-size-xlarge">
         </a>
         <a class="pw-button-twitter pw-look-native pw-size-xlarge">
         </a>
         <a class="pw-button-googleplus pw-look-native pw-size-xlarge">
         </a>
         <a class="pw-button-post-share pw-look-native pw-size-xlarge">
         </a>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="article_image">
        <img imageprop="image" src="http://nme.assets.ipccdn.co.uk/images/2013TheWolverineMarvelPress210213.article_x4.jpg" width="655"/>
        <span class="credit">
         Marvel
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="caption">
        Photo: Marvel
       </div>
       <div class="article_text">
        <span itemprop="articleBody">
         <strong>
          Hugh Jackman
         </strong>
         has spoken about the plot details for his
         <i>
          X-Men
         </i>
         spin-off
         <i>
          The Wolverine
         </i>
         by hinting that former character Jean Grey will be a pivotal figure in the flick - despite being dead.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The film is set after the events of
         <i>
          X-Men: The Last Stand
         </i>
         , in which Grey was killed, but Jackman says that her death and the impact on his character will be an important part of the movie.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <img align="right" src="http://static.nme.com/images/article/wolverine.jpg"/>
         According to
         <a href="http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1704484/the-wolverine-trailer-hugh-jackman.jhtml" target="_blank_">
          MTV
         </a>
         , he said:"There's no doubt that the most important relationship in his life, we've seen through the movies, is his relationship with Jean Grey."
         <br/>
         <br/>
         He added:
         <blockquote>
          Yes, we saw her die at the end of
          <i>
           X-Men: The Last Stand
          </i>
          . but in this movie, she has a presence which I think is vital to the movie, particularly for him confronting the most difficult thing within himself.
         </blockquote>
         <br/>
         The first trailer for
         <i>
          The Wolverine
         </i>
         was released earlier this week. shows Jackman's claw-sporting mutant jetting off to Japan, where a rich businessman offers him the chance to be mortal again and appropriately, it also features a glimpse of Famke Janssen's Jean Grey in a flashback sequence.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         A standalone Wolverine movie has been in the works since 2009, when Darren Aronofsky (
         <i>
          Black Swan
         </i>
         ,
         <i>
          The Wrestler
         </i>
         ) was set to direct. However, Aronofsky later dropped out and was replaced by
         <i>
          Walk The Line
         </i>
         director James Mangold, who shot the film in Sydney and Japan last year.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Alongside Jackman, the film stars Svetlana Khodchenkova from
         <i>
          Tinker, Tailor, Soldier, Spy
         </i>
         and model-turned-actress Tao Okamoto, both of whom appear in the trailer.
         <i>
          The Wolverine
         </i>
         is scheduled to arrive in UK cinemas on July 25.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Jackman has already made five appearances as Wolverine in
         <i>
          X-Men
         </i>
         movies, including an uncredited cameo in 2011's
         <i>
          X Men: First Class
         </i>
         . Director Bryan Singer has recently confirmed that Jackman's Wolverine will also appear in the next film,
         <i>
          X-Men: Days Of Future Past
         </i>
         , which begins filming next month (April).
         <br/>
         <br/>

         </iframe>
         <br/>

        </span>
