         <div id="description-text">
          This lovely bracelet was put together bead weaving seed beads into a Japanese braid known as Kumihimo, it features czech 10/0 turquoise blue luster glass seed beads and silver seed beads the combination of the two creates a spiral design making it very elegant and can be worn to dress up an everyday outfit and it's perfect for a wedding.  It is finished off with scrolled pewter end caps and an S clasp.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Fits wrists up to 7 1/2" as per bracelet sizer.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Measuring 8" inches or 203mm in total length.
          <br/>
          <br/>
