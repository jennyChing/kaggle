				});

				// if loading error remove div

        <h2 class="blog_header">
         <a href="http://drrudderman.com/blog/bringing-back-those-10-years-with-breast-surgery/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: Bringing Back Those 10 Years with Breast Surgery">
          Bringing Back Those 10 Years with Breast Surgery
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="top_metadata">
         Posted
         <a href="http://drrudderman.com/blog/2014/01/">
          Jan 15 2014
         </a>
         by
         <a href="http://drrudderman.com/blog/author/seo/" rel="author" title="Posts by Dr. Rudderman">
          Dr. Rudderman
         </a>
         in
         <a href="http://drrudderman.com/blog/category/procedures/" rel="category tag" title="View all posts in Procedures">
          Procedures
         </a>
         with
         <span>
          Comments Off
         </span>
        </div>
        <div class="entry">
         <p dir="ltr">
          <a href="http://drrudderman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/shutterstock_97290875.jpg">
           <img alt="Woman shopping for clothes looking in mirror" class="size-medium wp-image-3461 alignright" height="300" src="http://drrudderman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/shutterstock_97290875-200x300.jpg" style="margin: 4px 8px;" width="200"/>
          </a>
          Have you ever paused to think about how different you are now compared to how you were ten years ago? Ten years ago, you could probably head out the door with no makeup, no Spanx, and no push-up bra to hold everything in and together. You could get up and just go with little thought to your appearance.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          These days, getting ready for social events may seem a little more intimidating than it did ten years ago. Certain body parts have become soft, wrinkled, or simply pointed south. It now takes at least an hour of primping to get everything just right, and suddenly, the words pool party sound more dreadful than desirable. The confidence you once had seems to have gotten lost somewhere in the sands of time. Board-certified plastic surgeon Dr. Randy Rudderman is here to tell you that you can get those last ten years back with some of the safest, most effective cosmetic breast procedures in Alpharetta, Georgia and the surrounding areas of Atlanta.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          Dr. Rudderman believes that the first step to retrieving those last ten years is to restore a sense of confidence. You must identify the area that is holding you back from doing all the things you used to do. If you dont know where to begin, one of the best procedures to boost a womans confidence and restore a sense of femininity is
          <a href="http://drrudderman.com/breast/breast-augmentation-2/">
           breast augmentation
          </a>
          . This procedure is so popular and well-studied that there are a myriad of options available to meet your needs and cater to your concerns. You can choose the incision location and the type, size, and shape of the implant that will best meet the results you desire. Your breast size and shape will be increased and enhanced with this procedure.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          If you have poor skin elasticity or sagging breasts, a
          <a href="http://drrudderman.com/breast/breast-lift-2/">
           breast lift
          </a>
          will restore your breasts to a more youthful appearance. With this procedure, you will look better in clothes, swimsuits, underwear and no clothes at all! For more complete body rejuvenation, Dr. Rudderman offers the Mommy Makeover, which targets the areas most mothers are concerned about: the breasts, abdomen, and even the genital area.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Woman relaxing in pool" class="wp-image-3464 alignleft" height="162" src="http://drrudderman.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/shutterstock_71080144-300x200.jpg" style="margin: 4px;" width="243"/>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          Some women who may have had larger breasts in their youth still have large breasts, but now they are sagging, pendulous, and burdensome.
          <a href="http://drrudderman.com/breast/breast-reduction/">
           Breast reduction
          </a>
          is one of the most rewarding procedures because it can eliminate back and shoulder pain caused by heavy breasts. Like the breast lift procedure, a breast reduction can restore the breasts to a more youthful position while also removing excess weight in the area.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          There is no doubt that when the body is out of proportion, it can weigh on a womans confidence level. Dr. Rudderman can correct a variety of concerns, such as
          <a href="http://www.drrudderman.com/breast/tubular-breast/">
           tubular breasts
          </a>
          or
          <a href="http://www.drrudderman.com/breast/breast-asymmetry/">
           asymmetry
          </a>
          . He also performs
          <a href="http://drrudderman.com/breast/breast-revision-surgery/">
           breast revision surgery
          </a>
          to replace breast implants or exchange them for a larger size. Certain complications like
          <a href="http://www.drrudderman.com/breast/capsular-contracture/">
           capsular contracture
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.drrudderman.com/breast/symmastia/">
           symmastia
          </a>
          can also be corrected at his Alpharetta, GA practice.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          <em>
           Restoring your breast appearance can give you the confidence necessary to feel like you once did ten years ago. If you are considering breast surgery in the Atlanta, GA area, please contact Dr. Randy Rudderman at his Alpharetta practice by calling (678) 566-7200. For your convenience, you may also fill out our
           <a href="http://www.drrudderman.com/our-office/contact-2/">
            online contact form
           </a>
           .
          </em>
         </p>
