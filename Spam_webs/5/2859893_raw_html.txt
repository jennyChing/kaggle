           <h1 class="entry-title">
            The Color of Health: Five Quercetin Benefits
           </h1>
           <div class="entry-meta">
            <div>
             <span class="posted-on">
              Posted on
              <a href="http://thewellandcompany.com/well-now/healthy-energy/the-color-of-health-five-quercetin-benefits/" rel="bookmark">
               <time class="entry-date published" datetime="2015-03-03T21:52:02+00:00">
                March 3, 2015
               </time>
              </a>
             </span>
             <span class="byline">
              by
              <span class="author vcard">
               <a class="url fn n" href="http://thewellandcompany.com/well-now/author/kimberly-b/">
                Well and Company
               </a>
              </span>
             </span>
            </div>
           </div>
          </header>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_7105" style="width: 640px;">
            <a href="http://thewellandcompany.com/well-now/healthy-energy/the-color-of-health-five-quercetin-benefits">
             <img alt="The skin of red apples provide Quercetin benefits -Image Source: Joyosity Flickr Creative Commons " class="wp-image-7105 size-full" height="480" src="http://thewellandcompany.com/well-now/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/2913165421_846b4317b4_z.jpg" width="640"/>
            </a>
            <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
             <strong>
              The skin of red apples provides Quercetin benefits -Image Source: Joyosity Flickr Creative Commons
             </strong>
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            Forget about the color of 
            <a href="http://www.wired.com/2015/02/science-one-agrees-color-dress/%20">
             that dress
            </a>
             thats been all over social media recently, and lets focus on a more energetic huered. Red is vibrant, and carries many connotations: passion, anger, danger, power, lovebut it can also be the color of wellness.
            <a href="http://thewellandcompany.com/well-now/healthy-energy/ten-delicious-high-energy-foods-fuel-day/">
             Fruits and vegetables
            </a>
            that hold red pigment in their flesh and skin contain a flavonoid thats super healthy, known as Quercetin. Lets talk about Quercetin benefits thatll fit your body better than any outfit.
            <span id="more-7102">
            </span>
           </p>
           <p>
            Quercetin is found in the skin of red apples, red onions, grapes, cherries, cranberries, and in red wine, green tea, and other fruits and vegetables. Not all foods containing Quercetin are red; it is also found in blueberries, citrus fruit, kale and many other
            <a href="http://www.quercetin.com/overview/food-chart">
             delicious and healthy food choices
            </a>
            .
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             What are the Benefits of Quercetin?
            </strong>
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             The Stress Fighter:
            </strong>
            This flavonoid is
            <a href="http://www.globalhealingcenter.com/natural-health/health-benefits-of-quercetin/">
             loaded with antioxidants
            </a>
            , which fight free radicals that cause damage to cells, even at the DNA level. When we are stressed, our bodies produce cortisol. This is a hormone that is responsible for the fight or flight response. Some cortisol is a good thing, but when you are under constant stress, the effects of cortisol can deplete you of energy and cause damage to your body. When combined with relaxation therapy, exercise, and embracing a positive and happy life, Quercetin can help you reduce one of the biggest threats to your health, and help you live longer.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             A Happy Heart:
            </strong>
            If we dont eat the right foods and get enough exercise, we arent maintaining our cardiovascular health. Quercetin helps protect your heart by improving blood flow. The American Heart Association provides extensive information on ways to lower your
            <a href="http://www.heart.org/HEARTORG/Conditions/Cholesterol/AboutCholesterol/Good-vs-Bad-Cholesterol_UCM_305561_Article.jsp">
             LDL (the bad cholesterol)
            </a>
            , which is known to cause plaque that clogs arteries and can lead to heart attack and stroke. Scientific studies have discovered that Quercetin has demonstrated
            <a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3445532/">
             favorable results
            </a>
            in managing cholesterol levels.
           </p>
           <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_7104" style="width: 640px;">
            <a href="http://thewellandcompany.com/well-now/healthy-energy/five-amazing-quercetin-benefits-the-plant-pigment-that-is-the-vibrant-color-of-health">

            </a>
            <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
             <strong>
              Red Onions are a great source of Quercetin  Image Source: swong95765 Flickr Creative Commons
             </strong>
            </figcaption>
           </figure>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Breathe Better!
            </strong>
            Another great benefit of Quercetin is that it has
            <a href="http://www.health-prism.com/experts-columns_the-end-of-allergies.html">
             natural antihistamine
            </a>
            properties. Its a much gentler solution to treat breathing problems caused by a cold, asthma, and even seasonal allergies.Studies have shown that this flavonoid helps dilate the bronchial passages, allowing you to breathe easier.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Healthy Bones
            </strong>
            : Weve already talked about Quercetins ability to reduce cortisol levels. Too much cortisol production has a negative effect on many parts of our body, including our bones. Cortisol can affect bone formation and density, which is a real concern as we ageespecially for women, who are more at risk for conditions such as osteoporosis. Quercetin helps stop the
            <a href="http://saveourbones.com/the-antioxidant-that-builds-your-bones-and-so-much-more/">
             damage to your bones
            </a>
            caused by high cortisol levels.
           </p>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Embrace Your Age, and Feel Good
            </strong>
            : As we age, we really need to fill our bodies with antioxidant-rich foods and supplements to counteract the attack on our health from toxins and stress. Quercetin provides support for all parts of our body. Its also a natural anti-inflammatory. Scientists are studying
            <a href="http://umm.edu/health/medical/altmed/supplement/quercetin">
             the effect of Quercetin
            </a>
            as part of a cancer-prevention diet, and as a supplement that aids those suffering from various cancers. There is no definitive conclusion at this time, but antioxidants in general have been shown to have a positive impact on cancers. We must stress that it is not a cure, but Quercetin is one of the flavonoids that has shown promising results for overall health and healing.
           </p>
           <p>
            Make Quercetin part of your wellness program. Flavonoid-rich fruits and vegetables are also delicious, and add color to your plate.
            <a href="http://thewellandcompany.com/well-now/healthy-energy/how-practicing-mindfulness-can-improve-your-workout/">
             Mindful exercise
            </a>
            , relaxation, and restful sleep will also helpkeep your body and mind strong.
           </p>
           <p>
            Do you get enough Quercetin-rich foods? Wed all like to eat more nutritious meals, but sometimes we still dont get enough of the good things. Taking supplements like
            <a href="http://thewellandcompany.com/shop-products/">
             Wake Well, Revive Well, and Rest Well
            </a>
            can help you get the health benefits of Quercetin in an easy-to-take powdered drink formula or chew. Adding more Quercetin into your diet, regardless of its form, is a great way to add vitality, longevity, and color to your life.
           </p>
