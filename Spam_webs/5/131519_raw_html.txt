           <p>
            <b>
             LEmed, the specialist stem cell, laser therapy, research and distribution group, announced today that it is offering treatment for Muscular Dystrophy.
            </b>
           </p>
           <p>
            <br/>
            <strong>
             Wan Chai, Hong Kong, December 01, 2014 -(PressReleasePoint)-
            </strong>
            LEmed (Learning Edge Medical Ltd.), the specialist stem cell, laser therapy, research and distribution group, announced today that it is offering treatment for Muscular Dystrophy.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The muscular dystrophies are a group of genetic diseases that affect skeletal muscles and often also heart muscle. The symptoms include muscle weakness and progressive muscle wasting. It is caused by a genetic fault which prevents the production of a protein called dystrophin. Without dystrophin, muscles are fragile and are easily damaged. Over time so much damage builds up that the body cant repair it and muscles waste away, causing progressive disability in patients. About 1 in every 3,500 boys worldwide are born with this most common form of the disease.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            The most common forms of muscular dystrophy are: Myotonic, Duchenne, Becker, Limb-girdle, Facioscapulo-humeral, Congenital Oculo-pharangeal Distal and Emery-Dreifuss
            <br/>
            <br/>
            For patients our treatment is a very attractive option because injection with a specific type of stem cell that can make muscle and express the missing protein has the potential to repair abnormal muscle function
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Producing healthy muscle fibres with the help of: Myoblasts  a type of cell formed from satellite cells , Mesoangioblasts (MABs)  a type of stem cell or progenitor cell and Induced pluripotent stem cells (iPS cells)  made from skin cells in the lab and are pluripotent.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Reducing inflammation with the help of Mesenchymal stem cells (MSCs)  found in the bone marrow. In muscular dystrophy damaged muscles become very inflamed. This inflammation speeds up muscle degeneration. Certain types of stem cells release chemicals that reduce inflammation, slowing the progress of the disease.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            Beside stem cells, other therapeutic strategies such as gene therapy or small-molecule drugs for repairing the damaged gene are also used. Therapies use a combination of more than one of these approaches.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            <strong>
             About Leading Edge Medical Ltd:
            </strong>
            <br/>
            Cellular medicine is revolutionizing medical treatment in the 21st century and Leading Edge Medical's treatments are at the forefront of the field. Thousands of patients around the world have already benefited from bio-technologies using stem cells and intravenous laser therapy. Diseases once considered incurable are responding well to stem cell therapies and are restoring a quality of life to patients they thought they had lost forever.
            <br/>
            <br/>
            LEmed is committed to applying modern day best practices to the growing field of regenerative medicine. At its advanced medical treatment facilities, LEmed offers personalized, ongoing care plans and the highest quality of customer service as part of its dedication to providing a better life for chronic disease patients.
