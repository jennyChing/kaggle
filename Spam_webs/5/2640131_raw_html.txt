          Winter Vintage: Layer it Up Baby!
         </h1>
         <aside class="entry-meta" id="meta-63155">
          <span class="posted-on">
           Posted on
          </span>

           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">

            vilvintage
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="posted-in">
           in

        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          I was asked by
          <a href="http://www.shareasale.com/r.cfm?u=1014371&amp;b=417942&amp;m=43745&amp;afftrack=&amp;urllink=www%2Emodcloth%2Ecom" target="_blank">
           Modcloth
          </a>
          to create a vintage-inspired layered outfit perfect for this Autumn and, well, you know I cant resist a challenge! We all know that dressing vintage couldnt be simpler in the summer  all you need is a cute sundress, some great sunglasses and youre pretty much there!
         </p>
         <p>
          As the nights draw in and the weather turns though its oh-so-easy to slip back into bad habits: throwing on jeans and
          <a href="http://www.modcloth.com/shop/sweaters/" target="_blank">
           sweaters
          </a>
          , lounging around in soft comfy PJs and really not making much of an effort. Thats exactly what Ive done in response to the driving rain today! I need to give myself a kick and remember its easy to turn your summer wardrobe into a winter one once you grab yourself the right layers and a stylish coat!
         </p>

            </a>
