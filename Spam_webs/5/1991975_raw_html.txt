        <p>
         <a href="http://arrestedmotion.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/dk_am_8.jpg" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://arrestedmotion.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/dk_am_8.jpg', '']);">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-73819" height="379" src="http://arrestedmotion.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/dk_am_8-450x379.jpg" title="dk_am_8" width="450"/>

        </p>
        <p>
         Sometimes, you just need to get away from it all. Thats exactly what
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.davekinsey.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.davekinsey.com/', 'Dave Kinsey']);">
           Dave Kinsey
          </a>
         </strong>
         did for his upcoming show at New Yorks
         <strong>
          <a href="http://joshualinergallery.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://joshualinergallery.com/', 'Joshua Liner Gallery']);">
           Joshua Liner Gallery
          </a>
         </strong>
         by moving up to Central Californias Three Rivers and focusing his energy into this fresh body of work. The head of
         <strong>
          <a href="http://www.blkmrktgallery.com/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.blkmrktgallery.com/', 'Blk/Mrkt']);">
           Blk/Mrkt
          </a>
         </strong>
         took his game to natures back yard, retreating from his urban studio in Los Angeles and from what we see, its given this new collection of paintings a whole new dimension.
         <strong>
          <a href="http://arrestedmotion.com/page/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article-int', 'http://arrestedmotion.com/page/', 'AM']);">
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            AM
           </span>
          </a>
         </strong>
         brings you an inside look into Mr. Kinseys elusive mountain studio and a preview of this captivating show.
        </p>
