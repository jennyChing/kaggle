    <h2>
     About us
    </h2>
    <p>
     <strong>
      XERO
     </strong>
     are an rock band  formed in the North East of England. After meeting at college and finding  themselves spending more time making music than actually attending lessons,  Steff, Jamie, Jordan &amp; Connor created
     <strong>
      XERO
     </strong>
     . Their music is  fuelled by a mutual love for groove-based hard rock and the intent to make this  decade the one that brings rock back from the grave.
    </p>
    <p>
     After  over a year of gigging consistently in the North of England, in 2014, they  decided to up-sticks and get a squat together on the outskirts of London.
    </p>
    <p>
     A  love of playing live saw them touring venues and festivals around the north of  England, gaining them heavy rotation radio play on BBC Introducing NE. To  date, they have released an EP which saw them gain interest and sales on sites  such as Bandcamp. They are currently writing and recording for their next  release, which sees them further develop their defined and dynamic songwriting  technique. Having recently signed a digital distribution agreement the  band will step up a gear with the release of their next single, which will be  available on all digital download and streaming sites.
    </p>
    <p>
     They  are influenced by the sludgy riffs of Sabbath, the quirkiness of Queens of the  Stone Age and the tight grooves of Primus, amongst others, aiming to create a  distinctive sound of their own.
    </p>
