             Badlapur Preview :-
            </strong>
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            <i>
             Badlapur
            </i>
           </b>
           (stylised as

           </b>
           <b>
            PUR

           ): It is an upcomingIndianneo-noiractioncrimedramafilm starring critically acclaimed actors and actress Nawazuddin Siddique, Huma Qureshi, Yami Gautam, Radhika Apte. Varun Dhawan plays the lead role. Sriram Raghavan who is known for his unusual crime thriller dramas is directing this film. There are quite a few reasons why it is one of the awaited films in 2015.
          </p>

          <ul style="text-align: justify;">
           <li>
            <strong>
             Varun Dhawan:
            </strong>
            Varun Dhawan rose to fame with his Romantic roles in Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania, Main Tera Hero in 2014. He alrady tatsted the success with his first film itself Student of the year. But he is often criticized for his lesser acting skills and more of Salman-kinda-attitude. We have to look forward how he managed to do a Sriram Raghavan film, or alongside Nawazuddi Siddique.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <ol style="text-align: justify;">
           <li>
            <b>
             Nawazuddin Siddique, Huma Quershi:
            </b>
            They are brilliant actors, whatever the roles they have done in the past received great name fame, esp Gangs of Wasseypur. Hope they made a great choice for the scripts again.
           </li>
           <li>
            <b>
             Hot pairs
            </b>
            : Varun will also be seen kissing Divya Dutta in Badlapur. Talking about the same, he said in a press conference, I was very comfortable kissing Divya. Why not? Look at her. She is so sexy. While Divya, Nawazuddin and Vinay Pathak are seasoned actors known for their movies, Varun and Yami are relatively new and this interesting mix of actors will be a treat to watch.
           </li>
           <li>
            <b>
             Srira Raghavan and his Thriller films
            </b>
            : Undoubtedly Johnny Gaddar is the main reason why everyone like Sriram Raghavan. All his fims are so gripping and thrilling. Even the trailer is too crime driven, violent and unpreditable. Surely this film is for everyone who is expecting a freshness in the themes. Says Sriram, These aspects are part of our story and we felt the title is apt. It was just a working title, but then a lot of people reacted positively to it. So, we retained it.
           </li>
           <li>
            <b>
             Music
            </b>
            : The duo of Sachin-Jigar have had several hits to their credit including Char Baj Gaye, Thank God Its Friday, D Se Dance, Lucky Tu Lucky Me and Daingad Daingad to name a few. Srirams film music will suit the thriller genre, be it Johnny Gaddar, Ek Hasina Thi or Agent Vinod. And Badlapur should be no exception. In fact, actress Huma Qureshi has a special song in the film. As per a report on bollywoodtrade.com, the song will be reminiscent of the 90s and Huma is said to be watching Urmila Matondkar and Raveena Tandon songs for the same. In fact, this special number has reportedly been inspired by songs like Rangeela Re and Ek Do Teen. How Sachin and Jigar adapt to Badlapurs theme will be worth a listen!
           </li>
           <li>
            <b>
             You and Me Face
            </b>
            
            <b>
             Off of leads:
            </b>
            Highlighting their face-off, Nawaz says, Theres a line in the film 
            <i>
             Kaunhai tu dekh main tera kya haal karta hoon.
            </i>
             It is apt for this. Varun adds, My counter dialogue to his line is 
            <i>
             Main isey tadpa tadpa ke marunga
            </i>
            .
           </li>
          </ol>

          <p style="text-align: justify;">
           <b>
            Whats the meaning of the title:
           </b>
           For starters, the subject of the film is largely based on revenge  badla. Not just that, Varuns character Raghu undergoes a transition during the course of the story, leading to badlav (change). In Mumbai, there is a station named
           <i>
            Badlapur
           </i>
           on the Central Railway.
           <i>
            Badlapur
           </i>
           used to be one of the stations where the trains would change tracks. This aspect is signified in the film as the station features in the backdrop when the plot undergoes a change.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: justify;">

            </strong>

            Plot synopsis
           </b>
           : Raghav alias Raghu is a man with a past. He witnessed his wife and son being killed during a robbery. 15 years later, Raghu finds out who pulled the trigger to kill his family and the journey begins.
