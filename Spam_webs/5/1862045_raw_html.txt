        <p>
         Need a quick and
         <strong>
          easy dinner
         </strong>
         for those busy after school nights?  This
         <strong>
          Veggie Supreme Pasta Bake
         </strong>
         will have you sitting down to dinner with your family in no time.
        </p>
        <p>
         Today I am partnering with the
         <a href="http://www.drink-milk.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          American Dairy Association, Mideas
         </a>
         t again to show you how to add more healthy dairy to your life.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-title.jpg">
          <img alt="Veggies and melty cheese will make this pasta dinner a family favorite. #cheese #dinner #recipe #dairyfuel" class="aligncenter wp-image-11499 size-full" data-lazy-src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-title.jpg" height="750" src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" title="Veggie Supreme Pasta Bake picture" width="498"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Veggies and melty cheese will make this pasta dinner a family favorite. #cheese #dinner #recipe #dairyfuel" class="aligncenter wp-image-11499 size-full" height="750" src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-title.jpg" title="Veggie Supreme Pasta Bake picture" width="498"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I dont know about your school year, but ours is extremely busy the first month and a half. Our two boys are playing soccer, and our daughter is playing volleyball and learning tap dance. Talk about a crazy schedule trying to juggle all those practices and games with our work and life schedule. Im still trying to figure out where homework is going to fit in.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Even with the craziness of the sports schedule, we still try to sit down for dinner as a family as much as possible.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Obviously there are going to be some nights where we just grab something quick as we drop off the school bags and go right back out the door for a game.  Those days might even be easier if we hit the drive thru. What? There are some healthy options there too.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-1.jpg">
          <img alt="Veggies and melty cheese will make this pasta dinner a family favorite. #cheese #dinner #recipe #dairyfuel" class="aligncenter wp-image-11495 size-full" data-lazy-src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-1.jpg" height="750" src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" title="Veggie Supreme Pasta Bake photo" width="497"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Veggies and melty cheese will make this pasta dinner a family favorite. #cheese #dinner #recipe #dairyfuel" class="aligncenter wp-image-11495 size-full" height="750" src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-1.jpg" title="Veggie Supreme Pasta Bake photo" width="497"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Pasta and cheese gets thumbs up from everyone here just about every single time we put it on the menu.  Doing all the prep work the night before definitely gets a thumbs up from me on nights when the hungry monsters want to devour everything in sight as soon as they walk in the door.
        </p>
        <p>
         I like that I can add so many different dairy products into this cheesy dish.
         <strong>
          Milk, yogurt, and 2 kinds of cheese packs a big protein punch.
         </strong>
         That protein power will keep everyone full and energized as we moo-ve on to the next thing on our schedule.
        </p>
        <p>
         Experts recommend that everyone should be consuming 3 servings of dairy per day, but did you know that most people only eat 1? By getting more dairy into your diet, you are paving the way for a healthier lifestyle later in life. Who doesnt want strong bones and healthy teeth as they get older.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-2.jpg">
          <img alt="Veggies and melty cheese will make this pasta dinner a family favorite. #cheese #dinner #recipe #dairyfuel" class="aligncenter wp-image-11496 size-full" data-lazy-src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-2.jpg" height="750" src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" title="Veggie Supreme image" width="496"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Veggies and melty cheese will make this pasta dinner a family favorite. #cheese #dinner #recipe #dairyfuel" class="aligncenter wp-image-11496 size-full" height="750" src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-2.jpg" title="Veggie Supreme image" width="496"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Prepping your vegetables and pasta the night before will help you have dinner ready faster.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         I like to cut all the veggies and place them in containers or ziplock bags while my kids are getting ready for bed. You can also cook your pasta and stir some olive oil into it after it drains to keep it from sticking together.
        </p>
        <p>
         Another great thing about this dish is that you can customize your veggies to suit your taste. I happen to like hiding mushrooms in minemy kids never know until after the fact. Although they are getting wise to my tactics.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Veggies and melty cheese will make this pasta dinner a family favorite. #cheese #dinner #recipe #dairyfuel" class="aligncenter wp-image-11498 size-full" data-lazy-src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-collage-2.jpg" height="550" src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" title="Veggie Supreme Pasta Bake collage picture" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <noscript>
         <img alt="Veggies and melty cheese will make this pasta dinner a family favorite. #cheese #dinner #recipe #dairyfuel" class="aligncenter wp-image-11498 size-full" height="550" src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-collage-2.jpg" title="Veggie Supreme Pasta Bake collage picture" width="550"/>
        </noscript>
        <p>
         Start by sauting the butter and onion, then add more of the veggies and some flour.
         <strong>
          Make sure you cook that for another 1-2 minutes to get that flour cooked down.
         </strong>
         Whisk in the milk and cook until it thickens slightly. I use skim milk, so it never really gets too thick. Let it cook about 2 minutes and then add the cheese and yogurt. Its an moo-velous sight to see all that thick and creamy sauce.
        </p>
        <p>
         Stir in those noodles and the cans of tomatoes and green chilies.
         <strong>
          Now if you are in a hurry just add the extra cheese on top and cover the skillet for a few minutes to melt the cheese on top.
         </strong>
         BUTif you can stretch dinner time for another 20 minutes, I recommend baking it in the oven to melt the cheese.
        </p>
        <p>
         Then set it on the table and watch everyones eyes light up as they see all that melty cheese goodness!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-3.jpg">
          <img alt="Veggies and melty cheese will make this pasta dinner a family favorite. #cheese #dinner #recipe #dairyfuel" class="aligncenter wp-image-11497 size-full" data-lazy-src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-3.jpg" height="750" src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/plugins/speed-booster-pack/inc/images/1x1.trans.gif" title="Veggie Supreme Pasta Bake pic" width="497"/>
          <noscript>
           <img alt="Veggies and melty cheese will make this pasta dinner a family favorite. #cheese #dinner #recipe #dairyfuel" class="aligncenter wp-image-11497 size-full" height="750" src="http://insidebrucrewlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Veggie-Supreme-Pasta-Bake-3.jpg" title="Veggie Supreme Pasta Bake pic" width="497"/>
          </noscript>
         </a>
        </p>

           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <span id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             16 ounce box pasta (I used cavatappi)
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
             1Tablespoon olive oil
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 Tablespoon butter
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup chopped onion
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 Tablespoon minced garlic
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup chopped green peppers
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-10" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 cup chopped fresh mushrooms
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-11" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 - 2.25 ounce can sliced black olives, drained
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-12" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup chopped spinach
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-13" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 Tablespoons flour
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-14" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 cups milk
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-15" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-16" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup plain greek yogurt
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-17" itemprop="ingredients">
             3 cups shredded Italian blend cheese, divided
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-18" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 - 4 ounce can diced green chilies
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-19" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 - 14.5 ounce can diced tomatoes, drained
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-20" itemprop="ingredients">
             salt and pepper
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-21" itemprop="ingredients">
             1-2 teaspoons oregano
            </div>
            <div class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-22" itemprop="ingredients">
            </div>
           </span>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cook the pasta to al dente following the package directions. Drain. Spoon back into hot pan and stir in olive oil. Cover and set aside.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Melt the butter in a large skillet. Add the onions and saut for 2 minutes. Add the garlic, green pepper, mushrooms, olives, spinach, and flour. Stir and cook for 1-2 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Whisk in the milk and cook until it thickens slightly. Stir in the parmesan cheese, yogurt, and 2 cups cheese. Stir until melted.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Stir in the tomatoes, green chilies, and cooked pasta. Season to taste with the salt and pepper.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Spoon into a greased 9x13 baking dish. Top with the remaining cheese and sprinkle with oregano.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Broil for 2 minutes to brown the cheese. Serve immediately. Serves 8-10.
            </li>
           </ol>
