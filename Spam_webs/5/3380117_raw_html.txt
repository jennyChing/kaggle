          <div class="entry-content">
           <p>
            A video posted on YouTube showing a trick to fly the paper airplanes for a long time continuously.
           </p>
           <div align="center" style="padding: 6px 6px 10px 0;">
            <h3 style="color:#CCC;float:left;font:400 8px/8px sans-serif;text-transform:uppercase;width:100%;margin-bottom:5px">
             Advertisement
            </h3>
            <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">

            <!-- lidtime 300x250 -->

            </ins>

             (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
            </script>
           </div>
           <p>
            In video, the performer used three things to make the paper planes fly for a long time, first thing is the paper plane itself, second a dryer and third a chair.
           </p>
           <p>
            The performer explained that the trick is based on the principle of Dynamic Soaring.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://www.lidtime.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/How-to-make-a-paper-airplane-fly-for-a-long-time-1.jpg">
             <img alt="How to make a paper airplane fly for a long time (1)" class="aligncenter wp-image-5457" height="399" src="http://www.lidtime.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/How-to-make-a-paper-airplane-fly-for-a-long-time-1.jpg" width="710"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Actually, Dynamic Soaring is a method or technique that gain energy by repeatedly crossing the boundary between air masses of significantly different velocity.
           </p>
           <p>
            Here, in this trick, the chair act as the slope and the dryer produces wind.
           </p>
           <p>
            The video received thousands of hits and comments from the users.
           </p>
