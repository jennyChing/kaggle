						&lt;p&gt;&lt;div id="attachment_465096" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignright first_image"&gt;&lt;img src="http://s1.gossipcop.com/up/2015/01/Dax-Shepard-Ellen-300x241.png" alt="Dax Shepard Ellen" width="300" height="241" class="size-medium wp-image-465096"&gt;&lt;p class="wp-caption-text"&gt;(Warner Bros.)&lt;/p&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://gossipcop.com/tag/dax-shepard"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Dax Shepard&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/a&gt; explained how he and &lt;a href="http://gossipcop.com/tag/kristen-bell"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Kristen Bell&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/a&gt; decided to name their baby daughter &lt;strong&gt;Delta&lt;/strong&gt; because of a joke, and described in alternately horrifying and hilarious detail Bell&rsquo;s 33-hour labor and C-section, during a Thursday appearance on &ldquo;The &lt;strong&gt;Ellen DeGeneres&lt;/strong&gt; Show.&rdquo; Watch the video below.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;The actor told DeGeneres that Bell&rsquo;s delivery was &ldquo;more difficult&rdquo; than with the couple&rsquo;s first daughter Lincoln, saying the actress was &ldquo;in labor for 33 hours.&rdquo; Shepard thinks he should have gotten medication, too.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Shepard explained, &ldquo;I deserve something because, I was along for the ride. It&rsquo;s still a car crash, and I&rsquo;m in the passenger seat. I&rsquo;m playing Katy Perry and I&rsquo;m breathing and I&rsquo;m rubbing her back and I have fatigue and I think, &lsquo;I need something for this, help me.'&rdquo;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Nothing prepared him for seeing the results of Bell&rsquo;s C-section. &ldquo;So there&rsquo;s a sheet and then they go, &lsquo;The baby&rsquo;s here!&rsquo; Then you peek around the sheet and they&rsquo;re lifting out the baby, but then you notice your wife is completely disassembled,&rdquo; said Shepard. &ldquo;I can see inside of her.&rdquo;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;He continued, &ldquo;I was like, &lsquo;It&rsquo;s a girl!&hellip; Your liver&rsquo;s out, I think. And those are definitely your intestines. And she has &hellip; are you all right! Oh my God, put her back together correctly!'&rdquo; Shepard joked, &ldquo;After seeing this autopsy, I would rather see a school bus drive out of her vagina.&rdquo;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;As for the couple&rsquo;s choice of the name Delta, Shepard explained that it actually began as a joke. He told DeGeneres, &ldquo;It was a joke because our first daughter&rsquo;s name is Lincoln, which is very masculine, and a friend of mine teasingly texted me, &lsquo;Oh great, what&rsquo;s this one gonna be? Navy Seal? Delta Force? Green Beret?&rsquo; I was reading this text out loud to Kristen, and I said, &lsquo;Oh, Steve said what if we named her Delta?&hellip; &lsquo;Delta! Delta Bell Shepard! That&rsquo;s it!&rsquo; And that&rsquo;s it.&rdquo; Watch the video below!&lt;/p&gt;
