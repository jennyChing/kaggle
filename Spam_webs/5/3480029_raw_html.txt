                       <span style="color: #141823; font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                        <span style="line-height: 18px;">
                         <i>

                           The Tree That Time Built
                          </a>
                         </i>
                         , selected by children's poet laureate Mary Ann Hoberman
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </li>
                     </ul>
                     <br/>
                     <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      <br/>

                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Titles, clockwise from top left:
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                     </div>

                        about this title)

                       </span>
                      </li>
                      <li>
                       <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">

                         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000NW9W4U/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B000NW9W4U&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=postapocaly06-20&amp;linkId=C3EV44S3PGF74AKV" target="_blank">

                         </a>

                        , selected by David Ross

                       </span>

                      <li>
                       <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                        <i>

                         </a>

                        , illustrated by Cyndy Szekeres

                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       And here are two very special contemporary poems I first met in the Kennedy-Muth
                       <i>
                        Family
                       </i>
                       book. I think both them might resonate with the readership of this blog.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       To P.J. (2 yrs. old. who sed write a poem for me in Portland, Oregon)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       by Sonia Sanchez
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       if i cud ever write a
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       poem as beautiful as u
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       little 2/yr/old/brotha,
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       i wud laugh, jump, leap
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       up and touch the stars
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       cuz u be the poem i try for
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       each time i pick up a pen and paper
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       u. and Morani and Mungu
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       be our blue/blk/stars that
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       will shine on our lives and
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       makes us finally BE.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       if i cud ever write a poem as beautiful
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       as u, little 2/yr/old/ brotha,
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       poetry wud go out of bizness.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Scaffolding
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       by Seamus Heaney
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Masons, when they start upon a building,
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Are careful to test out the scaffolding;
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Make sure that planks won't slip at busy points,
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Secure all ladders, tighten bolted joints.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       And yet all this comes down when the job's done,
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Showing off walls of sure and solid stone.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       So if, my dear, there sometimes seems to be
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Old bridges breaking between you and me,
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Never fear. We may let the scaffolds fall,
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       Confident that we have built our wall.
                      </span>
                     </div>

                     <a href="http://postapocalyptichomeschool.blogspot.com.au/2015/04/why-i-love-having-large-home-library.html">
                      Why I love having a large home library
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-1349085387220680797" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <br/>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);">
                       Why I love having a large home library: At 830 on a Saturday night, when kiddo looks up at the stars and wants to know about that killer in the sky, we can pull a variety of works from our shelves and do a mini-unit study on the constellation Orion.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       "The Hunter"
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       A night long
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       Across the sky
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       Orion the hunter
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       Goes striding by
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       What is the quarry
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       He pursues
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       Through stars as bright
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       And thick as dews
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       And who has heard
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       His great dog bark
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       Sirius baying
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       Through the dark?
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto;">
                       <br/>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div>
                      <div>
                       <span style="-webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; background-color: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0);">
                        <br/>
                       </span>
                      </div>

                     <h2>
                      <b>
                       There Was a Child Went Forth
                      </b>
                     </h2>
                     <h3>
                      <b>
                       by Walt Whitman
                      </b>
                     </h3>
                     <br/>
                     There was a child went forth every day;
                     <br/>
                     And the first object he looked upon, that object he became.
                     <br/>
                     And that object became part of him for the day, or a certain
                     <br/>
                     part of the day, or for many years, or stretching cycles of years:
                     <br/>
                     The early lilacs became part of this child....
                     <br/>
                     And the apple-trees covered with blossoms, and the fruit
                     <br/>
                     afterward, and wood-berries, and the commonest weeds by the road;
                     <br/>
                     And the schoolmistress that passed on her way to the school....
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     The blow, the quick loud word, the tight bargain, the crafty lure,
                     <br/>
                     The family usages, the language, the company, the furniture
                     <br/>
                     --the yearning and swelling heart....
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     The doubts of day-time and the doubts of night-time--the
                     <br/>
                     curious whether and how.
                     <br/>
                     Whether that which appears is so, or is it all flashes and specks?
                     <br/>
                     Men and women crowding fast in the streets--if they are
                     <br/>
                     not flashes and specks, what are they?
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     These became part of that child who went forth every day,
                     <br/>
                     and who now goes, and will always go forth every day.
                     <div style="clear: both;">
                     </div>
