          GAF HD Shingles More Than Meets The Eye
         </h1>
        </header>

        <section class="entry">
         <h3>
          <a href="http://www.creoconstruction.com/contact-us/">
           Creo Construction is a Factory Certified GAF Master Elite Roofer
          </a>
         </h3>
         <h3>
          The many layers of a
          <br/>
          Timberline HD
          <sup>
           
          </sup>
          Shingle
         </h3>
         <p>
          <img alt="Timberline HD Shingle Layers" class="imgResponsiveFixGAF" src="http://www.gaf.com/Roofing/Residential/Products/Shingles/Timberline/_Images/Exploded-timberline.gif"/>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           <h4>
            Diamond Cut Granules
           </h4>
           <p>
            Multi-faceted design and light-reflective construction add dimension and depth to the shingle
           </p>
          </li>
          <li>
           <h4>
            UV Blocker (Granules)
           </h4>
           <p>
            Protects against damaging sunlight, which improves the durability and extends the life of the shingle
           </p>
          </li>
          <li>
           <h4>
            Color Lock Ceramic Firing (Granules)
           </h4>
           <p>
            Maintains the true color of the shingle longer
           </p>
          </li>
          <li>
           <h4>
            Dura Grip
            <sup>
             
            </sup>
            Adhesive
           </h4>
           <p>
            Locks the shingles in place on the roof, gripping tight in even strong gale-force winds
           </p>
          </li>
          <li>
           <h4>
            SpecSelect Grading System (Asphalt)
           </h4>
           <p>
            Use of finest-quality asphalt improves weathering in harsh conditions
           </p>
          </li>
          <li>
           <h4>
            Micro Weave
            <sup>
             
            </sup>
            Core
           </h4>
           <p>
            Offers a superior-strength foundation that resists cracking and splitting
           </p>
          </li>
          <li>
           <h4>
            FiberTech
            <sup>
             
            </sup>
            Components (Core)
           </h4>
           <p>
            Incorporates fibers that are non-combustible, providing a UL Class A fire rating
           </p>
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <img alt="Timberline_HD_Weathered_Wood_Photo_2" class="aligncenter wp-image-4293 " height="354" src="http://www.creoconstruction.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Timberline_HD_Weathered_Wood_Photo_2-1024x663.jpg" width="547"/>
         </p>
        </section>
