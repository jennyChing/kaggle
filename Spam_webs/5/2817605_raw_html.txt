      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/curiousoctopus" target="_blank" title="Follow Paola Antonelli On Twitter">
         Paola Antonelli
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        38. A common mistake that people make when trying to design something completely foolproof is to underestimate the ingenuity of complete fools.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Douglas_Adams#Death_and_legacy" target="_blank">
         Douglas Adams
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        39.Sometimes when you innovate, you make mistakes. It is best to admit them quickly, and get on with improving your other innovations.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steve_Jobs" target="_blank">
         Steve Jobs
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        40. Your customers are not you. They dont look like you, they dont think like you, they dont do the things that you do and they dont have your expectations or assumptions. If they did, they wouldnt be your customers; theyd be your competitors.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/mikekuniavsky" target="_blank" title="Follow Mike Kuniavsky On Twitter">
         Mike Kuniavsky
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h2 style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="font-size: 40px; line-height: 1.5em;">
        <i class="icon-thumbs-up">
        </i>
        10 Top Social Media Quotes
       </span>
      </h2>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        41. Social Media is about sociology and psychology more than technology.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/briansolis" target="_blank" title="Follow Brian Solis on Twitter">
         Brian Solis
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        42. Build it, and the will come only works in the movies. Social Media is a built it, nurture it, engage them, and them may come &amp; stay.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/ThisIsSethsBlog" target="_blank" title="Follow Seth Godin on Twitter">
         Seth Godin
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        43. Focus on how to be social, not how to do social.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/jaybaer" target="_blank" title="Follow Jay Baer on Twitter">
         Jay Baer
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        44. 90% trust peers on social networks (even strangers); only 1518% trust brands.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/DannyBrown" target="_blank" title="Follow Danny Brown on Twitter">
         Danny Brown
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        45. People dont want to be marketed at in the social channel. This is where they want to talk to each other, and brands are there by invitation. They have to be a great guest at the table.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/VFielding" target="_blank" title="Follow Veronica Fielding on Twitter">
         Veronica Fielding
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        46. Sell-sell-sell sales methods simply do not work on social media.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/kimgarst" target="_blank" title="Follow Kim Garst on Twitter">
         Kim Garst
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        47.We embed social media inside our processes. Lets look at our processes and see how we can enhance them with social.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/sandy_carter" target="_blank">
         Sandy Carter
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        48. 
        <span style="color: #262626;">
         Think like a publisher, not a marketer.
        </span>
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/dmscott" target="_blank" title="Follow David Meerman Scott On Twitter">
         David Meerman Scott
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>
      <h3>
       <strong>
        49.The overarching problem is that everyone sees and uses social media from a different perspective.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/NealSchaffer" target="_blank" title="Follow Neil Shaffer On Twitter">
         Neal Schaffer
        </a>
       </strong>
      </p>

       <strong>
        50. Privacy is dead, and social media holds the smoking gun.
       </strong>
      </h3>
      <p>
       <strong>
        <a href="https://twitter.com/petecashmore" target="_blank" title="Follow Pete Cashmore On Twitter">
