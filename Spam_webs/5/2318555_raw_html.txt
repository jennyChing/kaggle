          <em>
           Goon
          </em>
         </strong>
          The cult 2011 hockey comedy is finally moving forward on a long-promised follow-up.
         <a href="http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/news/jay-baruchel-direct-goon-sequel-795932" target="_blank">
          According to The Hollywood Reporter
         </a>
         ,
         <strong>
          Jay Baruchel
         </strong>
         , whoco-starred, co-wrote and produced the original is now this time is co-starring, co-writing (with
         <strong>
          Jesse Chabot
         </strong>
         ) and making his directorial debut.
         <strong>
          Sean William Scott
         </strong>
         will be back in the lead of the sequel, which will be titled
         <strong>
          <em>
           Goon: Last of the Enforcers
          </em>
         </strong>
         and deal with a pro lockout.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_259978" style="width: 690px">
         <img alt="Sony Pictures Entertainment" class="size-medium wp-image-259978" height="371" src="http://filmschoolrejects.com/images/1RXK4Dvp1zUEZ3ybZSSSfPJ8ZkL-680x371.jpg" width="680"/>

          Sony Pictures Entertainment
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           Underworld
          </em>
         </strong>
         
         <a href="http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/kate-beckinsale-returning-underworld-5-795737" target="_blank">
          The Hollywood Reporter also confirmed
         </a>
         that
         <strong>
          Kate Beckinsale
         </strong>
         will return for the fifth
         <em>
          Underworld
         </em>
         movie. This installment of the vampires and werewolves franchise, titled
         <em>
          <strong>
           Underworld: The Next Generation
          </strong>
         </em>
         on IMDb,will be directed by
         <strong>
          Anna Foerster
         </strong>
         , who has done second unit work and cinematography for Roland Emmerich movies and directed some TV.
         <strong>
          Theo James
         </strong>
         will also be back to reprisehis brief role in
         <em>
          Underworld: Awakening
         </em>
         .
        </p>

         </strong>
         will re-team for
         <strong>
          <em>
           Shanghai Dawn
          </em>
         </strong>
         ,
         <a href="http://www.tracking-board.com/tb-exclusive-mgm-moving-forward-with-jackie-chan-and-owen-wilson-sequel-shanghai-dawn/" target="_blank">
          according to The Tracking Board
         </a>
         . This will be thesecond sequel to the 2000 Western comedy. Thelast installment was
         <em>
          Shanghai Knights
         </em>
         in 2003. It could be a while still before this one arrives, as there doesnt seem to be a script yet.
        </p>

        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           Jurassic Park
          </em>
         </strong>
          With the third sequel,
         <strong>
          <em>
           Jurassic World
          </em>
         </strong>
         , arriving in theaters soon, theres a new viral video showing why the new park is much safer than the attempt depicted in
         <em>
          Jurassic Park
         </em>
         more than 20 years ago. Watch BD Wong, the only returning actor from the original, explain via an InGen Technologies infomercial:
        </p>

         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="383" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/FYVUnaoyfkk?rel=0" width="680">

          Lions Gate Films Home Entertainment
         </p>

        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles
          </em>
         </strong>
          The sequel to last years reboot
         <a href="http://deadline.com/2015/05/gary-anthony-williams-teenage-mutant-ninja-turtles-2-1201427329/" target="_blank">
          has cast
         </a>
         <strong>
          Gary Anthony Williams
         </strong>
         in the part ofBebop, the punk who is turned into a mutant pig. Williams is best known for his voice work, including the role of Uncle Ruckus on
         <em>
          The Boondocks
         </em>
         . Now all he needs is an actor to play his partner in crime, Rocksteady.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_250577" style="width: 690px">

        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           Tremors
          </em>
         </strong>
          While not exactly news of any official development,
         <strong>
          Kevin Bacon
         </strong>
         <a href="http://www.movies.com/movie-news/new-tremors-movie-kevin-bacon/18491" target="_blank">
          told a UK paper
         </a>
         that he wants to return to this horror comedy franchise now that its 25 years later. There have already been three sequels that he skipped out on, and another hes not in arrives this fall.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Fight Club" class="alignnone wp-image-112615" height="328" src="http://filmschoolrejects.com/images/YouDoNotTalkAboutFightClub-e1306421444263.jpg" width="680"/>

        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           Fight Club
          </em>
         </strong>
          Both a prequel and a sequel to
         <strong>
          Chuck Palahniuk
         </strong>
         s novel are arriving in stores on May 26th. The latter is in comic book form written by Palahniuk and involves a reawakened Tyler Durden kidnapping the son of Sebastian (Edward Nortons character in the movie, renamed) and Marla. The former is a short story titled Expedition in Palahniuks new collectionMake Something Up: Stories You Cant Unread and features Durden in a small but significant role. You can actually read the whole story here,
