             <p>
              Classic Gin Martini

INGREDIENTS
2  ozGin
 oz Dry vermouth

Garnish with a  Lemon twist or an Olive

Method:
Add all the ingredients to a mixing Beaker filled with ice. Stir until very cold and strain into a chilled Martini glass. Garnish with a lemon twist or an Olive
