            <p>
             <em>
              <strong>
               2016 Toyota Avalon Redesign,
              </strong>
             </em>
             Fourth generation of Toyotas luxury sedan is coming soon. New 2016 Toyota Avalon is almost ready to show off its great features and win some new admirers.
            </p>
            <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_103" style="width: 310px">
             <a href="http://www.carstim.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/2016-Toyota-Avalon-Release-Date.jpg">
              <img alt="2016 Toyota Avalon Release Date" class="size-medium wp-image-103" height="300" src="http://www.carstim.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/2016-Toyota-Avalon-Release-Date-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>

            <p>
             <em>
              <strong>
               2016 Toyota Avalon Changes and Improvements
              </strong>
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
             New Avalon will undergo some substantial changes. Both the exterior and the interior of this great sedan will be improved and updated which will even more contribute to the luxurious aspect of the new model. Most of the exterior changes will be noticeable on the front side. This will include larger grille as well as updated headlights and fog lights. The back side will have a powerful appearance and will feature chrome detailed bumper and renewed taillights. Rear windows will be slightly smaller than on the previous model. Attractive design will be continued to the interior of the new 2016 Toyota Avalon. The cabin will be able to comfortably accommodate up to five passengers. Seats will be wrapped in leather and will come with heating option for the front seats. A lot of high-quality materials such as aluminum and chrome will be seen throughout the cabin. Dashboard will be redesigned and it will include a 6.1-inch touch screen. Toyota will incorporate a plenty of high-tech and safety features that will put their new model at the top of its class.
            </p>
            <!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->

            <p>
             <em>
              <strong>
               2016 Toyota Avalon Engine
              </strong>
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
             There will be two engine options offered with the new 2016 Toyota Avalon. First one will be a 3.5 liter V6 engine that will be capable of delivering around 286 horsepower. Another option for the new Avalon will be a hybrid engine. This will be a 2.5 liter 4-cylinder engine with an electric motor. It will be able to produce up to 200 horsepower and accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just around 8.2 seconds. The hybrid version will expectedly provide great fuel economy. When it comes to the transmission system, both of these engines will come with 6-speed automatic transmission and will be offered in front-wheel drive.
            </p>
            <p>
             <em>
              <strong>
               2016 Toyota Avalon Release date and Price
              </strong>
             </em>
            </p>
            <p>
             This great sedan is expected to hit the market at the end of 2015. When it comes to the price of the new 2016 Toyota Avalon, it will most likely start at $32,000 and will go up to around $40,000.
