     Ireland, May 08,2015/Free-Press-Release.com/ -- If you are looking to do the FETAC occupational first aid courses, just check out for the best training institute that is ISO certified and has experience in offering healthcare and safety programs suitable for the workplace organizations. This occupational first aid course is very much useful to handle and first aid cases in the workplaces and it is important that at least a few employees of the organizations are qualified in this course to respond in emergency conditions at the work places. By doing this course the candidates become aware about the different procedures that needs to be taken care of during an accident and administer the appropriate first aid to the patients.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     The aim of the FETAC occupational first aid courses is that the delegates who have done the course must be able to increase awareness about possible work place accidents, identify an accident that needs first aid treatment, use automated external defibrillator and apply first aid techniques to enhance performance and health in the workplace. The course covers first aid in the workplace, how to assess patients, how to react in respiratory emergencies, cardiac first response, attending wounds and bleeding, checking altered levels of consciousness of the patients and also treating musculoskeletal injuries, electric shocks, poison, burns and chemicals etc. as each has a different first aid procedure.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     On completion of the course the attendees need to appear for an examination to test their knowledge and skills and on successfully passing out they shall be awarded the FETAC occupational first aid certificate. The institute also offers many other workplace safety programs and depending on your field you can look out for a course suitable to your interests. There is also pesticide training offered to those who use pesticides on how to safely handle them through the handheld sprayers.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     The pesticide training course covers contents like why we have to use pesticide, different pest control methods, selection and storage of pesticide, precautions while applying pesticides, handling and storing of sprays, PPE &amp; equipment etc. which is generally a 2 day course. By doing this course one can become proficient in handling pesticides rationally and safely without any hazards. All the classes are taken by well experienced and qualified tutors with the programs being offered either in the classrooms or online. You can check out the schedules online to book your slot to attend the training programs that are very much useful for the organizations to maintain health and safety standards in the workplace.
     <br/>

     Qualtec is the best safety training and consulting company in Ireland. It offers high quality health and safety training programs such as Safe Pass Course ,Safe Pass Courses,Safe Pass Courses Dublin,manual handling courses,patient handling courses,FETAC Occupational First Aid Course and more.
