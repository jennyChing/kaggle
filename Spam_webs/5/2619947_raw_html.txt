       <h1>
        Home Tour: Design Updates In My Dining Room
       </h1>
      </div>
      <div class="post-entry">
       <div style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-1.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-1" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5753" height="780" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-1.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        As we had friends coming over for dinner recently it offered the perfect opportunity to dress the table and shoot the dining room for you all to see. Over the last few months Ive been slowly adding a few design updates to make the space feel more like home. Join me as I show you around the space and explain why I chose the pieces I did
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-2.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-2" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5754" height="355" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-2.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        Arent these Marimekko
        <a href="https://www.amara.com/products/oiva-white-blue-plate-25cm-dia" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.amara.com/products/oiva-white-blue-plate-25cm-dia', 'dining plates');" target="_blank">
         dining plates
        </a>
        simply gorgeous? I find myself just staring at the watercolour beauty of them. Yes, my husband thinks I have gone insane. And he would be right. Still, they are *so* good, right?! I love how the blues pair with the copper
        <a href="http://www.grahamandgreen.co.uk/isaac-iron-chair-in-copper" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.grahamandgreen.co.uk/isaac-iron-chair-in-copper', 'dining chairs');" target="_blank">
         dining chairs
        </a>
         its a chic yet unpretentious combination; a gentle air of luxury but not in an OTT way.
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-3.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-3" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5755" height="390" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-3.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-4.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-4" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5756" height="792" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-4.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        The linen
        <a href="http://www.hm.com/gb/product/12109?article=12109-F#article=12109-G" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.hm.com/gb/product/12109?article=12109-F#article=12109-G', 'table runner');" target="_blank">
         table runner
        </a>
        was a direct result of the dining plates and a total steal from H&amp;M Home. Regular readers will know how much I love the Danish brand Khler, so its no surprise that I fell for their jubilee
        <a href="http://royaldesign.co.uk/viewitem.aspx?ID=121237" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://royaldesign.co.uk/viewitem.aspx?ID=121237', 'vase');" target="_blank">
         vase
        </a>
        that celebrates 175 years since the company made their first piece. It looks so striking against the navy backdrop, as well as pairing perfectly with the dining chairs. Im thrilled with it!
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-5.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-5" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5757" height="521" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-5.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        Another of my favourite elements in this space is the
        <a href="http://www.skinflintdesign.co.uk/store/ceiling_lights/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.skinflintdesign.co.uk/store/ceiling_lights/', 'vintage factory pendant light');" target="_blank">
         vintage factory pendant light
        </a>
         the turquoise-meets-mint colourway sold it to me from the off. It really helps to anchor the dining table and draw the eye down to the table. A little dash of colourful industrial chic is always a good thing, I think. Plus, it goes so well with my
        <a href="http://soniceditions.com/gallery/bright-bazaar" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://soniceditions.com/gallery/bright-bazaar', 'Bright.Bazaar art prints!');" target="_blank">
         Bright.Bazaar art prints!
        </a>
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-6.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-6" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5758" height="346" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-6.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-7.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-7" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5759" height="390" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-7.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        Thanks to their chevron detail, the
        <a href="http://www.westelm.co.uk/cotton-canvas-bold-stripe-window-panel-blue-lagoon-t583" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.westelm.co.uk/cotton-canvas-bold-stripe-window-panel-blue-lagoon-t583', 'curtains');" target="_blank">
         curtains
        </a>
        inject a splash of pattern and graphic vibrancy into the space. I pulled out the navy colour when choosing the plates and linens for the tablescape.
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-8.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-8" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5760" height="347" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-8.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-9.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-9" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5761" height="735" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-9.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        I laid
        <a href="http://www.quick-step.co.uk/laminate/classic/bleached-white-teak-planks?id=Flooring_FloorAggregate/005_408100" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.quick-step.co.uk/laminate/classic/bleached-white-teak-planks?id=Flooring_FloorAggregate/005_408100', 'Quick Steps Bleached White Teak flooring');" target="_blank">
         Quick Steps Bleached White Teak flooring
        </a>
        as a cooling base to ground all of the other elements of the space. It was very straight forward to lay and really made a difference to the space by maximising the benefits of the natural light in the space  I love it!
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-10.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-10" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5762" height="353" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-10.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-11.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-11" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5763" height="390" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-11.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-12.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-12" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-5764" height="373" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-12.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
        Im so pleased with how this space has come together  it feels perfect for sharing cosy autumnal dinners with my heartmate and friends. Which elements of the space do you like? Id love to hear how you would decorate with these designs in your home?
        <a href="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-13.jpg">
         <img alt="bright-bazaar-dining-room-13" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5765" height="836" src="http://brightbazaaar.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/bright-bazaar-dining-room-13.jpg" width="520"/>
        </a>
