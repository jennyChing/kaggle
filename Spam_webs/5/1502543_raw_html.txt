       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Another Female Interviewer on a Muslim News Show Dares Defy a Male Guest
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2015-03-07T09:00:59+00:00">
         March 7, 2015
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://www.patheos.com/blogs/friendlyatheist/author/terry/" rel="author" title="Terry Firma">
           Terry Firma
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://www.patheos.com/blogs/friendlyatheist/2015/03/07/another-female-interviewer-on-a-muslim-news-show-dares-defy-a-male-guest/#respond">
          <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="128361 http://admin.patheos.com/blogs/friendlyatheist/?p=128361">
           Leave a Comment
          </span>
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Female Muslim TV hostsare beginning to make a delicious habit of their uppity insolence.
        </p>
        <p>
         Last month we enjoyed the spectacle of a Saudi TV host losing her composure and
         <a href="http://www.patheos.com/blogs/friendlyatheist/2015/02/10/female-saudi-tv-host-laughs-in-male-guests-face-when-he-proposes-a-bizarre-solution-for-the-driving-ban/">
          laughing in amale guests face
         </a>
         when he proposed something absurdly sexistand offensive.
        </p>
        <p>
         Now its the turn of interviewer
         <strong>
          Rima Karaki
         </strong>
         of LebanonsAl-Jadeed TV. When she attempts to rein in her loquacious, time-wasting guest,
         <a class="ext-link" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hani_al-Sibai" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank" title="">
          London-based Islamist
          <strong>
           Hani Al-Sibai
          </strong>
         </a>
         ,the manquickly goes
         <a class="ext-link" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bOlpK6Yj5a4" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank" title="">
          from irritatedto upset to nasty
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <a class="ext-link" href="http://i.imgur.com/q0Bw0yV.jpg" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank" title="">
          <img class="alignnone" height="317" src="http://i.imgur.com/q0Bw0yV.jpg" width="418"/>
         </a>
        </center>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/bOlpK6Yj5a4" width="420">
         </iframe>
        </center>
        <p>
         <a class="ext-link" href="http://www.reddit.com/r/TwoXChromosomes/comments/2y172h/lebanese_tv_host_rima_karaki_turns_off_the_mic_of/" rel="external nofollow" target="_blank" title="">
          This version of events
         </a>
         says that in the end, she had his mic turned off, presumably terminating his appearance, but the video doesnt exactly show that. Still, kudos to Karaki for not taking guff from this disagreeable cretin.
        </p>
