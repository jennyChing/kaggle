         <p>
          Carbon App Sync and Backup for Android is an app backup tool for root users, its still in testing but this is a sneak peak at how it works used our trusty Samsung Galaxy Note 2 for this one.
         </p>
         <p>
          Carbon App Sync and Backup allows you to backup any app on your device to a cloud services like Google Drive or DropBox you can then restore the apps to any device later on.
         </p>
         <p>
          The great thing about this app is its easy to use so ideal for all Android users not just those that figure out Titanium Backup.
         </p>

          Carbon App Sync and Backup is in the play store now and its free!
