      <h1>
       Oval Face Shape
      </h1>
      <h3>
       <i>
        "Styling Tricks that Flatter Your Oval Face Shape"
       </i>
      </h3>
      <br/>
      <center>
       <img alt="Celebrities with Oval Face Shape, Halle Berry, Charlize Theron, Megan Fox" height="200" src="http://www.thechicfashionista.com/images/365x200xovalface-celebs.jpg.pagespeed.ic.3O_YbZsSNL.jpg" width="365"/>
       <br/>
       <img alt="5 star" height="14" src="http://www.thechicfashionista.com/images/starmini.gif.pagespeed.ce.9iuAO7Ib51.gif" width="14"/>
       <font size="1">
        <b>
         Famous Oval face shapes:
        </b>
        From left to right: From left to right:
        <i>
         Halle Berry, Megan Fox, Charlize Theron
        </i>
        ...  and Julia Roberts, Britney Spears,  Sharon Stone, Heather Locklear, Hilary Duff, Eva Longoria, Rihanna
       </font>
      </center>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      An
      <b>
       Oval Face Shape
      </b>
      is considered to be the ideal facial shape and a fashion stylist's dream because it's easier to style than the other face shapes. An oval can get away with almost any haircut and jewelry because of its balanced proportions.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <center>
       <a href="#Face shape styling">
        <img alt="Shortcut banner to styling tips for your face shape" height="132" src="http://www.thechicfashionista.com/images/400x132xfaceshape-shortcut.png.pagespeed.ic.5AjTRNLYGB.png" width="400"/>
       </a>
      </center>
      <br/>
      <h2>
       How Do You Know If Your Face Shape is Oval?
      </h2>
      The key sign is that it appears well balanced and proportional.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <h3>
       Here are the typical characteristics of an Oval face shape:
      </h3>
      <ul>
       <li class="check">
        Slightly wider at the mid-points of the sides (cheekbone area, right below eye level)
       </li>
       <li class="check">
        Your jawline is softly rounded, not too narrow yet not too wide
       </li>
       <li class="check">
        You have a slightly narrower jawline than at your temples
       </li>
       <li class="check">
        Your hairline may be slightly wider than your lower face and might resemble an inverted egg shape. This means your face shape is a mix of oval and heart.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <br/>
      <blockquote>
       <img src="http://www.thechicfashionista.com/images/mininote.gif.pagespeed.ce.xUiA42_H8h.gif"/>
       <b>
        Note:
       </b>
       If you have all of the characteristics above but your forehead and jawline are narrow tapered, then you may have a
       <a href="http://www.thechicfashionista.com/diamond-face-shape.html" onclick="return FIX.track(this);">
        Diamond Face Shape
       </a>
       .
      </blockquote>
      <br/>
      <h4>
       Be Open to a Secondary (or even a third) Face Shape
      </h4>
      <p>
       You may not be an exact Oval so you probably have a secondary facial shape. It depends on your features. If you pay attention to the proportions and bone structure of your face, you'll know what needs to be visually widened and trimmed down.
      </p>
      <p>
       If you have along vertical line but still maintain the oval shape of your face then I suggest that you follow the guidelines for
       <a href="http://www.thechicfashionista.com/oblong-face-shape.html" onclick="return FIX.track(this);">
        Oblong face shape
       </a>
       . Or if you have a slightly full jawline or cheeks you might want to check out my tips for
       <a href="http://www.thechicfashionista.com/round-face-shape.html" onclick="return FIX.track(this);">
        Rounds
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       If you find yourself in this category then you need to work with the other styling strategies as well. Review them and decide what will help you keep your proportional, oval facial shape.
       <br/>
       <br/>
      </p>
      <div class="hr">
       <hr/>
      </div>
      <a name="Face shape styling">
      </a>
      <h2>
       How to Style Your Oval Face Shape
      </h2>
      Since your face is well-proportioned you can pull off all kinds of hairstyles and can experiment with various shapes - from different necklines to jewelry styles.
      <p>
       However, even though you have the perfect facial outline, there are other factors to take into consideration when styling according to your face shape. One of them being your facial features, such as your nose, chin, eyes and lips. You may have strong or delicate features.
      </p>
      <p>
       Let's say you have an oval face with a very prominent nose. If you get a haircut with bangs you'll notice that your nose appears bigger. That's because the bangs draw the attention to your forehead and nose area.
      </p>
      <p>
       Your neck length will also come into play when selecting hairstyles, jewelry and other accessories placed close to your face.
       <a href="http://www.thechicfashionista.com/common-face-shapes.html" onclick="return FIX.track(this);">
        Click here to learn more about how to flatter your face shape
       </a>
       .
       <br/>
       <br/>
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <img src="http://www.thechicfashionista.com/images/mininote.gif.pagespeed.ce.xUiA42_H8h.gif"/>
       <b>
        Note:
       </b>
       Depending on the scale of your face - bangs, horizontal shaped earrings and necklace pendants can shorten your face shape and make it look wide and round.
      </blockquote>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      <font size="1">
       <a href="#Top">
        Back to Top
       </a>
      </font>
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Let's take a look at the different strategies to flatter your Oval face shape:
      <h2 class="hbg">
       Your Best Hairstyles
      </h2>
      Some of your ideal hairstyles:
      <ul>
       <li class="check">
        <b>
         Tousled shoulder-length
        </b>
        brings attention to your perfect proportions while maintaining length
       </li>
       <li class="check">
        <b>
         Side-part bob
        </b>
        gives you a trendy and flirty look
       </li>
       <br/>
       <center>
        <img alt="Best hairstyles for Oval Face Shape" height="263" src="http://www.thechicfashionista.com/images/500x263xovalface-hair.jpg.pagespeed.ic.gzKlOOWw4m.jpg" width="500"/>
       </center>
       <br/>
       <li class="check">
        <b>
         Pixie cut
        </b>
        shows off your Oval face shape.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <br/>
      Bangs and dangling hair in front of your face hide your beautiful face shape. You should work on showing it off. However if you have bangs you can still look good by sweeping it to one side, or part it in the middle and sweep it to both sides. This helps "open" up your face.
      <br/>
      <br/>

      <h2 class="hbg">
       Your Best Jewelry
      </h2>
      Again, you'll look good in any jewelry style or length - but
      <b>
       angular shapes but with slight rounded corners
      </b>
      are your best pieces. Triangular earrings for example will give definition to your face and bring out the natural beauty of your oval face shape. Opt for medium to short length earrings  too long ones will drag down your face and make you look more like an Oblong.
