     <h2>
      <a href="http://www.improbable.com/2009/04/28/tastes-like-chicken-recalled/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: Tastes Like Chicken, recalled">
       Tastes Like Chicken, recalled
      </a>
     </h2>
     <div class="entrytext">
      <p>
       <a href="http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Gallus_gallus_male_big.jpg">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5222 alignright" height="237" src="http://improbable.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/02/gallus_gallus_male_250w.jpg" style="float: right;" title="gallus_gallus_male_250w" width="250"/>
       </a>
       Investigator Sally Shelton sends this observation that appeared on a paleontologists discussion listserv:
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        Im often amazed that people on this list seem unaware of some of the classic literature Culinary phylogenetics was addressed in an issue of
        <em>
         AIR
        </em>
        way back in 1998.
        <a href="http://decapoda.nhm.org/pdfs/2331/2331.pdf">
         You can find a pdf here
        </a>
        .
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       The discussant refers to
       <a href="http://www.biol.sc.edu/~coull_lab/staton/joebio.html">
        Joe Staton
       </a>
       s study Tastes Like Chicken, published in
       <a href="/airchives/paperair/volume4/v4i4/v4i4-toc.html">
        volume 4, number 4 of the
        <em>
         Annals of Improbable Research
        </em>
       </a>
       , and which begins with the statement
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        The field of culinary evolution faces one great dilemma: why do most cooked, exotic meats taste like cooked
        <a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/projects/genome/guide/chicken/">
         Gallus gallus, the domestic chicken
        </a>
        ?
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       A chart from the study is reproduced below.
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <a href="http://improbable.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/02/tasteslikechicken-air1998.gif">
        <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-5221 aligncenter" height="767" src="http://improbable.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/02/tasteslikechicken-air1998.gif" title="tasteslikechicken-air1998" width="500"/>
       </a>
      </p>
