         <p>
          When we want a quick and easy snack that the kiddo can help with, we usually go for Rice Krispie treats! However, why not kick them up a notch by adding a yummy chocolate and salted caramel topping.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           You Will Need:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          3 tablespoons of butter
          <br/>
          4 cups of mini marshmallows
          <br/>
          6 cups of Rice Krispies
          <br/>
          10 oz bag of dark chocolate chips
          <br/>
           bag of caramel squares
          <br/>
          seasalt
         </p>
         <p>
          In a large pan, melt the butter, and then add in the marshmallows. Stir the marshmallows until melted as well.
         </p>
         <p>
          Add in the Rice Krispies and carefully stir. Press into a 913 pan coated with non-stick spray or lined with parchment paper.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.theneighborhoodmoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/sckrispies1.jpg">
           <img alt="Salted Caramel Rice Krispies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5062" height="479" src="http://www.theneighborhoodmoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/sckrispies1.jpg" width="718"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
          Melt the chocolate in a bowl in the microwave with a tablespoon of butter. Stir well and spread on top of the rice krispies. Immediately sprinkle sea salt on top of the chocolate while it is still soft.
          <br/>
          <a href="http://www.theneighborhoodmoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/sckrispies2.jpg">
           <img alt="Salted Caramel Rice Krispie Treats" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5064" height="479" src="http://www.theneighborhoodmoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/sckrispies2.jpg" width="718"/>
           <br/>
          </a>
          Put the caramels in a bowl and melt in the microwave. Drizzle all over the Rice Krispies.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.theneighborhoodmoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/sckrispies3.jpg">
           <img alt="Salted Caramel Rice Krispie Treats" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5065" height="479" src="http://www.theneighborhoodmoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/sckrispies3.jpg" width="718"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Let everything cool and harden. If you used parchment paper, lift it out of the pan and cut into squares.
         </p>
         <p>
          Enjoy!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.theneighborhoodmoms.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/saltedcaramelrkt.jpg">

          </a>
         </p>
