           <h1 class="entry-title">
            The SegSeat, A Seat For Your Segway
           </h1>
           <div class="entry-meta entry-meta-above">
            By
            <a class="author-link fn nickname url" href="http://www.ohgizmo.com/author/admin1213/" title="View all posts by David Ponce">
             David Ponce
            </a>
            on
            <span class="entry-date published" title="2006-06-14T00:55:12+00:00">
             06/14/06
            </span>
            
            <a class="entry-comments" href="http://www.ohgizmo.com/2006/06/14/the-segseat-a-seat-for-your-segway/#comments" title="The SegSeat, A Seat For Your Segway  Comments">
             3 Comments
            </a>
           </div>
           <div class="entry-content">
            <p>
             <img align="right" alt="segseat" id="image2274" src="//www.ohgizmo.com/wp-content/uploads/2006/06/segseat.jpg"/>
             By David Ponce
            </p>
            <p>
             When the Segway came out, I was frothy at the mouth with excitement. I dont really know why. I think it was the combination of stupid marketing tactics (remember Ginger?) with the realization that this really was one cool product. Of course, then I saw the price tag and Ive been crying ever since.
            </p>
            <p>
             Still, great as the Segway is, there are those among us who live in a great big pit of lazy, and for whom
             <em>
              standing
             </em>
             to get around is a little too much effort. Enter the
             <a href="htp://www.segseat.com/">
              SegSeat
             </a>
             , an accessory that allows you to, well, sit on the Segway while retaining its unique driving dynamics.
             <br/>
            </p>
            <blockquote>
             The SegSeat glides forward and back upon a braced rail, allowing sitting riders to emulate a standing riders typical leaning movements that control the Segway. SegSeats are also collapsible, allowing standing riders to use modified vehicles without interference.
            </blockquote>
            <p>
             Being this lazy is going to cost you though: the device retails for $750 either from
             <a href="http://segway.com/shop/locator%5C">
              Segway dealers
             </a>
             or
             <a href="http://www.segseat.com/">
              SegSeat
             </a>
             .
            </p>
