        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         How To: Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-03-11T23:02:19+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          March 11, 2013
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.heatherlikesfood.com/author/cheneycat/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Heather Cheney
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">

       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <input data-frizzlyhovercontainer="" data-frizzlypostcontainer="" data-frizzlyposttitle="How To: Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies" data-frizzlyposturl="http://www.heatherlikesfood.com/homemade-ice-cream-cake-crunchies/" type="hidden" value=""/>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         You know you love it that layer of crisp, creamy, cookie crunch that you find nestled between layers of ice cream cake at places like Dairy Queen. And if youre like me, you strategically map your bites so that you get the perfect crunch-to-ice-cream ratio with every forkful. But no matter how hard you try, theres never enough.
         <span id="more-4357">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         NEVER ENOUGH!
        </p>
        <p>
         Well todays the day that there will be enough. Enough for every bite of ice cream. Enough to spoon over the tops of your sundaes. Enough to eat with a spoon if you so desire. Its a revolution, I tell you!
        </p>
        <p>
         These crunchies are super simple to make and have the potential to be very detrimental to my well-being. Things this good should not be so easy to make. But alas, I love you guys so I had to take one for the team and make up a batch just to show you how easy they are.
        </p>
        <p>
         Youll start with some Oreos and a bottle of Magic Shell. In a standard size package of Oreos youll need 2 rows.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4358" height="402" src="http://www.heatherlikesfood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/DSC04051_1.jpg" title="Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies | heatherlikesfood.com" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Plop those cookies into a food processor or get out some aggression with the rolling pin. However you swing it you want to end up with a bowlful of finely crushed cookies. If youre in need of a flippin sweet food processor, dont forget to enter my
         <a href="http://www.heatherlikesfood.com/homemade-chicken-nuggets/">
          Magimix giveaway
         </a>
         !
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4359" height="402" src="http://www.heatherlikesfood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/DSC04053_1.jpg" title="Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies | heatherlikesfood.com" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Pour an entire 7.5 oz bottle of Magic Shell over the Oreo crumbs
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4360" height="900" src="http://www.heatherlikesfood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/DSC04061_1.jpg" title="Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies | heatherlikesfood.com" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         And mix until its well combined. At this point you can spread it between layers of ice cream and brownies, swirl it into a milkshake, or use it as ice cream topping.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4361" height="402" src="http://www.heatherlikesfood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/DSC04068_1.jpg" title="Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies | heatherlikesfood.com" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         These mint crunch parfaits were a huge hit at my house! I just layered mint brownie ice cream with the crunchies and topped it off with a little whipped cream and some Andes mints. So, so good!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4362" height="900" src="http://www.heatherlikesfood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/DSC04082_1.jpg" title="Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies | heatherlikesfood.com" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         What would you add these ice cream cake crunchies to?
        </p>
        <div class="easyrecipe" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
         <div class="ERSName" itemprop="name">
          Homemade Ice Cream Cake Crunchies
         </div>
         <div class="ERSClear">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSTopRight">
          <img itemprop="image" src="http://www.heatherlikesfood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/DSC04082_1.jpg" width="205"/>

         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             of a standard packaged of Oreos (2 C Oreo cookie crumbs)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 (7.25 oz) Magic Shell Ice Cream topping
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Crush the Oreos and transfer into a bowl.. Pour the Magic Shell topping over the cookie crumbs and mix well to combine. Use as an ice cream topping or filling for frozen cakes or pies.
           </li>
          </ol>
