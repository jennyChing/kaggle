          The PROCESS:
          <br/>
          <br/>
          It's all about the yarns!
          <br/>
          <br/>
          To begin with, I select only the softest, most unique and luxurious yarns that look and feel great to me.  Most of these come from high end specialty yarn shops, not the local craft mart with your regular old scratchy 4-ply yarn.  Grandma may have taught me how to knit, but that doesn't mean I need to stick with the kind of yarn available back in her day.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Next, I let the yarn dictate whether it wants to stand alone, or be carefully mixed with others to make it even more fabulous.  Then, I decide on a creation, either fashionable, functional, or both, that best suits the feel and drape of the yarn.  Finally, a gorgeous handmade item results for you to enjoy!
