        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Geek Girls Holiday Gift Guide
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-11-29T19:58:21+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 29, 2013
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://geekgirltravel.com/author/fouts/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            fouts
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div addthis:title="Geek Girls Holiday Gift Guide " addthis:url="http://geekgirltravel.com/geek-girls-holiday-gift-guide/" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style ">
         <a class="addthis_button_facebook_like" fb:like:layout="button_count">
         </a>
         <a class="addthis_button_tweet">
         </a>
         <a class="addthis_button_pinterest_pinit">

         <a class="addthis_counter addthis_pill_style">

        <div id="triberr_endorsement">
        </div>
        <p>
         Who isnt looking for something unusual to gift this year? And for those of us travel geeks there are some pretty cool finds that will make the geek in us swoon. Heres our short list for this year.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://geekgirltravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Screen-Shot-2013-11-29-at-11.01.19-AM.png">
          <img alt="http://www.amazon.com/RAVPower-Flashlight-Lightning-Sensation-Thunderbolt/dp/B0084Y47SK/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1385751533&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=RavPower+pb08" class="alignleft wp-image-113" height="163" src="http://geekgirltravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Screen-Shot-2013-11-29-at-11.01.19-AM-300x204.png" width="200"/>
         </a>
         <strong>
          RavPower Dynamo-on-the-Go Power Bank
         </strong>
         <br/>
         We took this little baby to Mexico with us last month and it performed beautifully. Compact so it slips in your bag yet powerful with a capacity of 2600Ah and 1Amp output, perfect for your camera or iPhone. It has built in over current protection so you dont jolt your equipment and the Lithium Ion battery is the most reliable power source going.
        </p>
        <p>
         The USB port allows you to plug in your devices with their own cords so you dont have to worry about whether the plugs change and youre eft with no charger.Its even got an LED flashlight!
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/RAVPower-Flashlight-Lightning-Sensation-Thunderbolt/dp/B0084Y47SK/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1385751533&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=RavPower+pb08" target="_blank">
          $18.99 on Amazon
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://geekgirltravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Screen-Shot-2013-11-29-at-11.14.23-AM.png">
          <img alt="mcam lite" class="wp-image-114 alignright" height="133" src="http://geekgirltravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Screen-Shot-2013-11-29-at-11.14.23-AM-300x190.png" width="210"/>
         </a>
         <strong>
          mCAM Camera body for iPhone
         </strong>
         <br/>
         For those of us who take tons of photos with the iPhone but wish it had a tripod mount an external mic or better lenses, this is a geek girls dream.
        </p>
        <p>
         The mCam is an aluminum case that holds your iPhone and allows you to attach special lenses like fisheye, macro and wide-angle lenses. There is an external 180 microphone and 4 tripod mounts so you can add on accessories. The iphone 5, C or S (Andriod is available too) all fit into the silicone case, then you slip that into the aircraft-grade aluminum case. We like the silicone case so much we left it on the phone for a little extra no-slip protection.
        </p>
        <p>
         I dont know about you but one of the problems I have with iPhone photo is shakiness. The weight of the case isnt huge but it really does help stabilize for hand held shots.
         <a href="http://www.actionlifemedia.com/mcamlite" target="_blank">
          $129 with both wide angle and macro lenses and a mic
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          The Secret Sweater
         </strong>
         <br/>
         For those of us who travel for business we often find ourselves in business attire and freezing our butts off in over-cooled conference rooms. Bundling up means packing bulky sweaters and there goes your carry on! Enter the secret sweater a brilliant idea to keep warm and still look great in your business suit. Ill let the video fill you in.
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/0O8jh43fH9I?rel=0" width="640">
         </iframe>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.thegrommet.com/secret-sweater-layering-cardigan" target="_blank" title="secret sweater">
          $29.95 on The Grommet
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://geekgirltravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Screen-Shot-2013-11-29-at-11.28.36-AM.png">
          <img alt="womens QZip" class="alignleft wp-image-115" height="210" src="http://geekgirltravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Screen-Shot-2013-11-29-at-11.28.36-AM-264x300.png" width="185"/>
         </a>
         <strong>
          Q-Zip from ScotteVest
         </strong>
         <br/>
         You know I already love my
         <a href="http://geekgirltravel.com/scottevest-lola-jacket/" target="_blank" title="Scottevest Lola Jacket">
          down Lola jacket
         </a>
         from ScotteVest but if you dont need the warmth of down but still need a place to stash your goodies, the Q-zip could be your answer. Personally I hate carrying a purse or a bag for just a couple of things, especially when Im hiking or riding. The Q-zip is lightweight with a side seam zipper that gives you access to 2 hidden pockets for your phone, a camera or ipod and includes the tabs to hold your headphones neatly in place inside the jacket. No more snagging the cord!
        </p>
        <p>
         Made of silky 100% Dri-RELEASE wool its cool in the summer and snug in the winter, but you can throw it in the washer.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.scottevest.com/v3_store/Womens-Q-Zip.shtml" target="_blank" title="scottevest">
          $55 from Scottevest
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://geekgirltravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Screen-Shot-2013-11-29-at-11.47.10-AM.png">
           <img alt="usb multi-tool" class="wp-image-116 alignright" height="189" src="http://geekgirltravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Screen-Shot-2013-11-29-at-11.47.10-AM-248x300.png" width="157"/>
          </a>
          Utility Charge Tool
         </strong>
         <br/>
         The Swiss army knife of chargers, this baby has fold out connectors for all of your favorite gadgets in one place (.USB, mini-USB, microUSB and 30-pin connector).
        </p>
        <p>
         Combine it with the Rav Power charger above and youre good to go in a very compact-no-nest-of-cords nirvana.Sorry, it doesnt have a lightening connector for that iPhone.
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.coolthings.com/usb-utility-charge-tool/" target="_blank">
          $20 from Coolthings.com
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <a href="http://geekgirltravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Screen-Shot-2013-11-29-at-11.50.02-AM.png">
           <img alt="Tom's ballet flats" class="alignleft wp-image-117" height="123" src="http://geekgirltravel.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Screen-Shot-2013-11-29-at-11.50.02-AM-300x176.png" width="210"/>
          </a>
          Toms Ballet Flats
         </strong>
         <br/>
         They slip off at TSA and feel great on the plane, dress up a casual outfit, pack in basically NO space and for every pair you help to change lives. Whats NOT to love about Toms shoes? I like the ballet flats for their packability and comfort as well as adding a little flair to my wardrobe. Heck, even their
         <a href="http://www.toms.com/catalog/product/view/id/62384/s/gift-wrap-kit/x/category/631/x" target="_blank">
          wrapping paper
         </a>
         has a cause. Get Toms Red Lina Ballet Flats $79 and a pair goes to a person in need.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Note: Some of the products above were received for review or as gifts. All opinions are my own.
        </p>
