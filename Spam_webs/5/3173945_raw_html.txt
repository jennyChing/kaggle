        <p style="text-align: left;">
         A fresh batch of this Mango Lemonade would be perfect for a hot day working in the garden. It is an easy beverage recipe, made from fresh ingredients, that your whole family will enjoy. It is less tart than standard lemonade, so fussier kids may like it over a regular lemonade recipe.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/mango-lemonade-ingredients.jpg">
          <img alt="mango lemonade ingredients" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-45113" data-wp-pid="45113" height="470" src="http://blommi.com/wp-content/uploads/mango-lemonade-ingredients.jpg" width="720"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         It wont cost you much to make a batch of this mango lemonade either. Neither lemons nor mangoes are particularly expensive fruits, and the recipe only requires a few. It would also be a great one to enlist the kids help one.
         <br/>
        </p>

          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients

          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 large mangoes
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 4 lemons)
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             cup sugar
           </li>

            3 cups Water

          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Dissolve the sugar in 1 cup of the water in saucepan over medium heat. Allow to cool.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Peel and puree the mango.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Mix all the ingredients together well, in a large pitcher.
           </li>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Serve over ice.
           </li>
