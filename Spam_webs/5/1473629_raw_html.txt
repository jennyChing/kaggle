      <h1 class="title">
       <a href="http://www.4tunate.net/2013/11/10-must-watch-christmas-movies-to-enjoy-with-your-kids/" rel="bookmark" title="10 Must-Watch Christmas Movies to Enjoy with Your Kids">
        10 Must-Watch Christmas Movies to Enjoy with Your Kids
       </a>
      </h1>
      <div class="entry">
       <div id="fcbk_share">
        <div class="fcbk_like">
         <div id="fb-root">
         </div>
         <script src="http://connect.facebook.net/en_US/all.js#appId=224313110927811&amp;xfbml=1">
         </script>
         <fb:like font="" href="http://www.4tunate.net/2013/11/10-must-watch-christmas-movies-to-enjoy-with-your-kids/" layout="button_count" send="false" show_faces="false" width="450">
         </fb:like>
        </div>

       <p>
        We hold back on most things Christmas until AFTER Thanksgiving.
        <em>
         Shocking, I know.
        </em>
        In fact, Ive been a little bah-humbug about it all, until I remembered
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0002F6BRE?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B0002F6BRE&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;qid=1384481879&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=elf" target="_blank">
         ELF
        </a>
        ! I LOVE watching Elf every year!
        <em>
         Not exactly meaningful, but fun and light-hearted nonetheless!
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         So I just might make an exception to holding off on ALL things Christmas for moviesand maybe even music as we get closer to December.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        I love the traditions of family Christmas movies. Last year, we surprised the kids as we tucked them in bed for the night with a
        <a href="http://www.4tunate.net/tag/polar-express/">
         trip downtown to watch Polar Express in 3D in their pajamas
        </a>
        .
        <em>
         One of the best memories ever!
        </em>
        Heres 10 of our favorites that we watch most every year.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.4tunate.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/10-Must-Watch-Christmas-Movies-to-Enjoy-with-Your-Kids.jpg">
         <img alt="10 Must-Watch Christmas Movies to Enjoy with Your Kids" class="alignnone wp-image-9721" height="500" src="http://www.4tunate.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/10-Must-Watch-Christmas-Movies-to-Enjoy-with-Your-Kids-1024x1024.jpg" width="500"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <h3>
        10 Must-Watch Christmas Movies to Enjoy with Your Kids This Season:
       </h3>
       <p>
        1.
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001CO42J8?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B001CO42J8&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;qid=1384456940&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=charlie+brown+christmas" target="_blank">
          Charlie Brown Christmas
         </a>
        </strong>
         1965 gave us a true classic in this Christmas gem. Linus taking the stage to tell us about the true meaning of Christmas from Luke 2 gets me every.single.time. Its the first Christmas movie the boys ever watched, and of course I have it in the
        <a href="http://www.4tunate.net/2007/12/its-beginning-to-look-lot-like-1st/" target="_blank">
         blogging vault
        </a>
        .
        <em>
         (The entire
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001CO32FI?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B001CO32FI&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20" target="_blank">
          Peanuts Holiday Collection
         </a>
         is on sale for $23.99 on Amazon!)
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        2.
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00008N6NQ?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B00008N6NQ&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;=movies-tv&amp;qid=1384457181&amp;sr=1-3&amp;keywords=home+alone" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          Home Alone
         </strong>
        </a>
         Last year was the first year my boys really got the humor and story line in this movie, and I think this years viewing will be even better!
       </p>
       <p>
        3.
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000VDDDVO?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B000VDDDVO&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;=movies-tv&amp;qid=1384457322&amp;sr=1-2&amp;keywords=it%27s+a+wonderful+life" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          Its a Wonderful Life
         </strong>
        </a>
         Another Christmas classic that I cant watch too many times.
        <em>
         Jimmy Stewart, you outdid yourself.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        4.
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000R7G6KE?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B000R7G6KE&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;=movies-tv&amp;qid=1384457584&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=rudolph+the+red+nosed+reindeer" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          Rudolf the Red-Nosed Reindeer
         </strong>
        </a>
        
        <em>
         Can I just confess to you that this isnt my favorite?
        </em>
        It creeps me out.
        <em>
         {Ducks.}
        </em>
        However, its a classic and the boys favorite, so Ill do my duty to carry on the tradition.
        <em>
         (
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0057FGCJQ?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B0057FGCJQ&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;=movies-tv&amp;qid=1384457856&amp;sr=1-1&amp;keywords=classic+christmas+movies " target="_blank">
          TheOriginalChristmas Classics Gift Set
         </a>
         with Rudolf, Frosty, Santa Claus is Coming to Town, and more is on sale for only $11.99!)
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        5.
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000AGTPUU?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B000AGTPUU&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;=movies-tv&amp;qid=1384457748&amp;sr=1-2&amp;keywords=polar+express" target="_blank">
         <strong>
          The Polar Express
         </strong>
        </a>
         This is just a visually beautiful film. Even better on the big screen, if you can catch a theater in your area showing it this season.
       </p>
       <p>
        6.
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002MU4NN6?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B002MU4NN6&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;=movies-tv&amp;qid=1384458071&amp;sr=1-2&amp;keywords=white+christmas" target="_blank">
          White Christmas
         </a>
        </strong>
         I havent quite convinced Brad that this is a must-watch just yet. Men and their unappreciation for musicals
        <em>
         maybe this year
        </em>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        7.
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0002F6BRE?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B0002F6BRE&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;qid=1384481879&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=elf" target="_blank">
          Elf
         </a>
        </strong>
        - I laugh at the same jokes all season long.
        <em>
         Modern Classic.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        8.
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B003P3PQMQ?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B003P3PQMQ&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;qid=1384482457&amp;sr=8-4&amp;keywords=frosty+the+snowman" target="_blank">
          Frosty the Snowman
         </a>
        </strong>
        - This was my favorite to watch every year as a TV Special.
        <em>
         (I just noticed if you use Amazon Instant Video, you can buy this classic for $4.99!)
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        9.
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000VBIGCW?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B000VBIGCW&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;qid=1384483561&amp;sr=8-2&amp;keywords=a+christmas+story" target="_blank">
          A Christmas Story
         </a>
        </strong>
        - This is for my neighbor Scot, proud owner of the
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0000C6ECF?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393177&amp;creativeASIN=B0000C6ECF&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;qid=1384483865&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=leg+lamp" target="_blank">
         leg lamp
        </a>
        , and has visited the infamous house. Besides,everyone needs to get
        <em>
         youll shoot your eye out
        </em>
        drilled into them this time of year.
       </p>
       <p>
        10.
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002JUFPUE?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393185&amp;creativeASIN=B002JUFPUE&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;qid=1384483974&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=dr+seuss+how+the+grinch+stole+christmas" target="_blank">
          Dr. Seuess How the Grinch Stole Christmas
         </a>
        </strong>
        - Personally, I love the classic cartoon version best, but I love watching the Jim Carrey one just to hear Faith Hill sing,
        <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001NB1FUY?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393177&amp;creativeASIN=B001NB1FUY&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;qid=1384484230&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=where+are+you+christmas" target="_blank">
         <em>
          Where Are You Christmas
         </em>
        </a>
        .
        <em>
         (Just
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001NB1FUY?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=213733&amp;creative=393177&amp;creativeASIN=B001NB1FUY&amp;linkCode=shr&amp;tag=4tunate-20&amp;qid=1384484230&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=where+are+you+christmas" target="_blank">
          downloaded it for 69 cents
         </a>
         , but Im going to try and hold off playing it until late November.)
         <img alt=";)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.4tunate.net/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_wink.gif"/>
        </em>
       </p>
