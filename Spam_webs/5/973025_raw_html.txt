       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Easy Dessert: No Bake Caramel Pie
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com/5825/easy-no-bake-caramel-pie/#comments">
           28 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="14151811" data-link="http://www.itisakeeper.com/5825/easy-no-bake-caramel-pie/" data-summary="" data-title="Easy Dessert: No Bake Caramel Pie">
        </div>
        <p>
         I am a member of the Collective Bias Social Fabric Community. This shop has been compensated as part of a social shopper amplification for Collective Bias and its advertiser. This No Bake
         <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com/5507/easy-dessert-recipes-pies/" title="Easy Dessert Recipes: Pies">
          <strong>
           Caramel Pie
          </strong>
         </a>
         is an easy
         <a href="http://coupons.walmart.com/?cid=18153148" target="_blank rel=">
          dessert
         </a>
         that the whole family will love.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com">
          <img alt="Easy Dessert No Bake Caramel Pie from It's a Keeper" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5879" src="http://www.itisakeeper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easy-Dessert-No-Bake-Caramel-Pie-from-Its-a-Keeper-3-of-4-682x1024.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the most popular recipes on Its a Keeper is my
         <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com/4535/butterfinger-pie-easy-dessert-recipes/" target="_blank" title="Impossibly Easy Butterfinger Pie">
          Impossibly Easy Butterfinger Pie
         </a>
         . And, for good reason. Its CRAZY GOOD! And, super easy to make.
        </p>
        <p>
         As soon as I made this pie, I knew I had to try other combinations and flavors. First up, my favorite candy barMilky Way!
        </p>
        <p>
         This recipe is a bit of a hybrid. To add a little sweetness and richness to the pie, in added a can of Dulce de Leche which is a rich and decadent sauce thats a lot like caramel. Its very popular in South American cuisines.
        </p>
        <p>
         For the Milky Way component, I used these new Milky Way Simply Caramel Bites that I found at Wal-Mart.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com">
          <img alt="Easy Dessert No Bake Caramel Pie from It's a Keeper" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5880" src="http://www.itisakeeper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easy-Dessert-No-Bake-Caramel-Pie-on-Its-Keeper-1-of-4.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com">
          <img alt="Easy Dessert No Bake Caramel Pie from It's a Keeper" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5881" src="http://www.itisakeeper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easy-Dessert-No-Bake-Caramel-Pie-from-Its-a-Keeper-2-of-4.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr">
         The great thing is, this time of the year, they are very easy to find. A quick stop in the seasonal aisle and I was able to spot them right away!And, heres a coupon for
         <a href="http://coupons.walmart.com/?cid=18153149" target="_blank rel=">
          $0.75 off when you buy (2) 2.83 oz. bags
         </a>
         or
         <a href="http://coupons.walmart.com/?cid=18153148" target="_blank rel=">
          $3.00 off when you buy (2) 6 oz. bags or larger
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://coupons.walmart.com/?cid=18153148 rel=">
          <img alt="MARS_Bites_Coupon (2)" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5882" height="473" src="http://www.itisakeeper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/MARS_Bites_Coupon-2.jpg" width="901"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         Now, let me show you how easy it is to make this pie.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         Start by chopping an entire 6 oz bag of Milky Way Bites.  Try not to eat them as youre chopping them. Its hard. I know.  Reserve 1/4 cup for the topping. Again, try not to sneak too many.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com">
          <img alt="Easy Dessert Recipe No Bake Caramel Pie from It's a Keeper" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5883" src="http://www.itisakeeper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easy-Dessert-Recipe-No-Bake-Caramel-Pie-1-of-10-1024x759.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         Prepare a batch of Dulce de Leche if your local supermarket doesnt carry it (mine didnt). Here are
         <a href="http://www.bakersroyale.com/basic-baking-series/baking-basics-how-to-make-homemade-dulce-de-leche/" target="_blank">
          directions
         </a>
         to easily make your own. I used the microwave version. Place 16 oz of whipped topping into a large mixing bowl.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com">
          <img alt="Easy Dessert Recipe No Bake Caramel Pie from It's a Keeper " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5884" src="http://www.itisakeeper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easy-Dessert-Recipe-No-Bake-Caramel-Pie-from-Its-a-Keeper-5-of-10-1024x682.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         Carefully fold the Dulce de Leche into the whipped topping.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com">
          <img alt="Easy Dessert Recipe No Bake Caramel Pie from It's a Keeper " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5886" src="http://www.itisakeeper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easy-Dessert-Recipe-No-Bake-Caramel-Pie-from-Its-a-Keeper-6-of-10-1024x697.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         Add the chopped Milky Way Bites and fold into Dulce de Leche mixture. Make sure you reserve 1/4 cup for the topping.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         Now, you might be tempted to lick the spatula at this point. But dont. You still need it!
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com">
          <img alt="Easy Dessert Recipe No Bake Caramel Pie from It's a Keeper " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5887" src="http://www.itisakeeper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easy-Dessert-Recipe-No-Bake-Caramel-Pie-from-Its-a-Keeper-7-of-10-1024x723.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         Pour filling into a store-bought chocolate cookie crust. Hang on. Its almost spatula licking time!
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easy-Dessert-Recipe-No-Bake-Caramel-Pie-from-Its-a-Keeper-8-of-10.jpg">
          <img alt="Easy Dessert Recipe No Bake Caramel Pie from It's a Keeper 8 of 10" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5889" src="http://www.itisakeeper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easy-Dessert-Recipe-No-Bake-Caramel-Pie-from-Its-a-Keeper-8-of-10-1024x687.jpg"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         Top the pie with an additional 8 oz of whipped topping and then sprinkle the reserved chopped Milky Way Bites on top. And, viola! Youre done! Let the spatula licking commence.
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         While youre enjoying that, make sure you refrigerate the pie for about 4 hours before serving. If you can wait that long!
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.itisakeeper.com">
          <img alt="Easy Dessert Recipe No Bake Caramel Pie from It's a Keeper " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5890" src="http://www.itisakeeper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/Easy-Dessert-Recipe-No-Bake-Caramel-Pie-from-Its-a-Keeper-9-of-10-1024x727.jpg"/>
         </a>
         I told you this pie was super easy! And, wait until you try it! It is sooooo good! If I were you, I would pin this now! Just click the little Pin It button below!
        </p>
        <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">

        </p>

         <h2 dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
          No Bake Caramel Pie
         </h2>
         <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
          <em>
           Compliments of Its a Keeper
          </em>
          <br/>
          <em>
           www.itisakeeper.com
          </em>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
          Ingredients:
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;">
          6 oz bag Milky Way Bites
          <br/>
          16 oz + 8 oz frozen whipped topping, thawed
          <br/>
          14 oz can Dulce de Leche (if you cant find Dulce de Leche you can
          <a href="http://www.bakersroyale.com/basic-baking-series/baking-basics-how-to-make-homemade-dulce-de-leche/" target="_blank">
           make your own
          </a>
          from a 14 oz can of sweetened condensed milk)
          <br/>
          1 store-bought chocolate cookie pie shell
         </p>
         <p>
          Instructions:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Chop Milky Way Bites into small pieces. Reserve 1/4 cup.
          </li>
          <li>
           Place 16 oz of whipped topping in a large mixing bowl.
          </li>
          <li>
           Fold in Dulce de Leche.
          </li>
          <li>
           Fold in chopped Milky Way Bites.
          </li>
          <li>
           Pour mixture into pie shell.
          </li>
          <li>
           Top with 8 oz whipped topping.
          </li>
          <li>
           Sprinkle reserved chopped Milky Way Bites on top.
          </li>
          <li>
           Refrigerate for 4 hours before serving.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
         </p>
