          Essential DIY Home Improvement that even a Geek could do!
         </a>
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <p class="postedon">
          <span class="posted-on">
           <time class="entry-date published" datetime="2014-08-18T22:30:58-05:00">
            August 18, 2014

           <span class="author vcard">

            </a>
           </span>
          </span>
          <span class="sep">
           |
          </span>
          <span class="comments-link">
           <a href="http://muine.gooeylinux.org/essential-diy-home-improvement-that-even-a-geek-could-do/#respond">

           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </div>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/wgdg8Ypcmqs?rel=0" width="420">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         Everyone loves
         <a href="http://lifehacker.com/">
          simplification and refinement of lifestyle
         </a>
         and all human efforts are channelized towards this end these days. One of the most tedious tasks in the bygone era of manual garage doors was getting in and out of the garages. Car owners had to sweat it out to park their cars into the garage and get their cars out of it which consumed a lot of time and energy. However, car owners now have the luxury of getting a remote controlled garage door installed in their garages which eases out the entire entry and exit process.
        </p>
        <p>
         Before getting any remote controlled garage door installed, it is very important to have a thorough understanding of the devices used in automated garage doors. The type of automating device depends on the type of garage door that you wish to have installed. It can be a large door made of heavy material where the garage door opener may have to endure more power; say more that 1HP or it may be a lighter one that requires less power to operate.
        </p>
        <p>
         The basic criteria for selecting drives for automation of garage doors and gates are:
        </p>
        <p>
         1. Weight of the door.
        </p>
        <p>
         2. Height of the door.
        </p>
        <p>
         3. The intensity of use.
        </p>
        <p>
         4. Installation conditions.
        </p>
        <p>
         Commercially available drives (actuators) for garage doors are classified according to the method of opening which can be either swing or slide. High quality remote controlled garage doors typically have electronic speed control on their equipment which ensures quiet operation and a gentle closure of the gate.
        </p>
        <p>
         In case of a loss, garage remotes are easy to replace. You can either contact the manufacturer of the remote or even go online and shop for one. All that you need is the model number of the remote that you have been using.
        </p>
        <p>
         One of the latest entries into garage remote market is the universal garage remote. These remotes work for most of the automated garage doors with exception of a few. There are many companies out there in the markets producing such universal RF remotes however, such a remote is also seen as a security threat.
        </p>
        <p>
         It is worth mentioning that kids love to play with anything that gets into action with the click of a button and garage remotes fit this category the most. It is wise to check out the advice of
         <a href="http://www.doors.org/">
          International Door Association
         </a>
         contained in their D.O.O.R safety program for safety of children.
        </p>
        <p>
         While installing an automatic garage door is not as difficult as
         <a href="http://multnomahheating.net/service/">
          installing a new furnace
         </a>
         , you should still consider having a professional help with the task if youre not comfortable doing it yourself.
        </p>
        <p>
         Remote controlled garage doors have proved themselves to be nothing less than a boon for car owners. They are particularly useful for old people, women or even young men who do not want to drench their outfit with sweat and soil it with dirt by opening the garage door manually. If you still do not have a remote controlled garage door installed, it is time to get one installed to enter and exit your garage with ease and style!
        </p>
       </div>

          Muine Music Player for GNOME
         </a>
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <p class="postedon">
          <span class="posted-on">
           <time class="entry-date published updated" datetime="2014-02-20T05:49:00-06:00">
            February 20, 2014
           </time>
          </span>
          <span class="byline">

       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <b>
          Muine
         </b>
         is a music player for
         <a href="http://www.gnome.org/">
          GNOME
         </a>
         . It features:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          A simple, intuitive user interface
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.vorbis.com/">
           Ogg Vorbis
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://flac.sourceforge.net/">
           FLAC
          </a>
          , AAC and MP3 music playback support
         </li>
         <li>
          Automatic album-cover fetching
         </li>
         <li>
          Support for embedded ID3v2 album images
         </li>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.replaygain.org/">
           ReplayGain
          </a>
          support
         </li>
         <li>
          Support for multiple artist and performer tags per song
         </li>
         <li>
          Plug-insupport
         </li>
         <li>
          Translations into many languages
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <b>
          Muine
         </b>
         is maintained by Jorn Baayen.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          0.8.3 has been released, grab ithere.
         </b>
        </p>
