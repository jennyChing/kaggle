     <h1>
      Wadih Pazos Named Partner at MBAF: At 32 years of age, the co-inventor of PaperSave is the first non-Certified Public Accountant partner in the firm.
     </h1>
     <p class="date">
      Monday, July 06, 2009
     </p>
     <!-- begin body -->
     <p>
      <strong>
       MIAMI
      </strong>
       Morrison, Brown, Argiz &amp; Farra, LLP (MBAF), Florida's largest independent public accounting firm, proudly announces that
      <a href="/en/about/partners-directors/wadih-pazos.aspx">
       Wadih Pazos, Sr.
      </a>
      has been promoted to partner. Pazos was the director of the Technology Services Department of MBAF, a vice-president of
      <a href="http://www.satmbaf.com" rel="external">
       Solutions@MBAF, LLC
      </a>
      , the technology affiliate of MBAF, and in charge of the Business Process Automation Group. In his new role, Pazos will continue expanding MBAF's technology offerings into other areas and increasing the reach of PaperSave.
     </p>
     <p>
      Pazos is a technology expert who co-envisioned and co-invented PaperSave, a software product that automates the process of imaging and storing source documents into information systems such as Microsoft's Great Plains, BlackBaud's Financial Edge and BlackBaud's Raiser's Edge. He specializes in streamlining client operations by reducing manual processing, increasing efficiency and minimizing defects. He is the leader of Solutions@MBAF's Business Process Automation team, for which he built a team of developers in the United States and India.
     </p>
     <p>
      "Wadih is a very valuable asset to the firm. He has diversified and strengthened MBAF's client offerings through innovative solutions such as PaperSave," said Tony Argiz, CEO and managing partner at MBAF. "His contributions to MBAF are well-known and deserving of this recognition"
     </p>
     <p>
      Pazos joined MBAF directly after finishing college and has been with the company for 10 years. During his tenure at MBAF, he designed, engineered and managed numerous software and information technology projects. He has won several awards while at the firm including the prestigious Most Creative Technology Solution Award from General Electric in 2004.
     </p>
     <p>
      At 32 years of age, he is the first non-Certified Public Accountant partner in the firm. Pazos earned his Bachelor of Science degree in computer science from
      <a href="http://www.fiu.edu" rel="external">
       Florida International University
      </a>
      . He is a member of the
      <a href="http://www.unitedwaymiami.org/" rel="external">
       United Way of Miami-Dade
      </a>
      , the
      <a href="http://www.redcross.org/fl/miami" rel="external">
       American Red Cross of Greater Miami &amp; the Keys
      </a>
      , the
      <a href="http://www.fiualumni.com/" rel="external">
       FIU Alumni Association
      </a>
      and the
      <a href="http://www.iasa.org/" rel="external">
       International Association of Software Architects South Florida Chapter
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <h2>
      About MBAF
     </h2>
     <p>
      MBAF is ranked in the top 50 accounting firms in the nation and is the largest independent Florida-based certified public accountant firm in the state. The firm has been ranked as
      <a href="http://www.plattgroupllc.com/ipa.html" rel="external">
       "Best of the Best,"
      </a>
      the list of the top 25 performing firms in the country, for 14 years. MBAF provides tax and accounting, audit, technology consulting and litigation support and business valuation services to entrepreneurs, wealthy individuals, private and public corporations and legal practitioners across a broad range of industries.
     </p>
