        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Samsung Galaxy Alpha now Available in the UK
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         Samsung Galaxy Alpha becomes available to purchase in the UK. Carphone Warehouse has today confirmed the actual Samsung Galaxy Alpha is currently available both in-store and online from 33 per month with supporting networks (without upfront fee). The 4.7-inch smartphone from Samsung is an interesting departure from its usual design, utilizing metals and other components.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.galaxyalphamanual.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/galaxy-alpha-uk.jpg">
          <img alt="galaxy alpha uk" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-100" height="338" src="http://www.galaxyalphamanual.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/galaxy-alpha-uk.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Carphone Warehouse are going to stock Charcoal Black, Sleek Silver, Dazzling White and exclusive Scuba Blue. Black and white variants are available from today, while those who order silver and exclusive blue colors will have to endure a short wait until October 3.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Carphone Warehouse Press Release about Samsung Galaxy Alpha
        </h2>
        <div class="pressrelease">
         <blockquote>
          <p>
           LONDON, 12th September: Samsungs newest smartphone, the Samsung Galaxy Alpha is now available from Carphone Warehouse both online and in store from 33 per month* with no upfront cost. The UKs largest mobile retailer will be stocking the brand new handset in Charcoal Black, Sleek Silver, Dazzling White and exclusive Scuba Blue.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Samsung Galaxy Alpha is the first Samsung device to feature a beautifully designed metal frame. Its metal body is incredibly thin and lightweight, at 4.7 the Alphas fully HD screen is slightly smaller than the Samsung Galaxy S5, meaning that its compact enough to use one handed and but big enough to watch movies and videos or play games.
          </p>
          <p>
           The handset also comes with the high tech features Samsung has become known for as standard, such as a fingerprint scanner to unlock your phone. It also supports PayPal allowing you to authorise payments securely with only your fingerprint. The device also comes with the built in S-Health app to help you keep track of your fitness, calories burned, your weight and heart rate.
          </p>
          <p>
           Customers can get their hands on the Samsung Galaxy Alpha in- store and online in Charcoal Black and Dazzling White from today. Customers who purchase the brand new smartphone online in Sleek Silver and exclusive Scuba Blue will have their handsets delivered by 3rd October.
          </p>
         </blockquote>
         <p>
          Carphone Warehouse customer can buy at their online store by going
          <a href="http://www.carphonewarehouse.com/phones/samsung-galaxy-alpha" target="_blank">
           here
          </a>
          .
         </p>
        </div>
