     <p>
      <b>
       Shannon Reed
      </b>
      is a
      <a href="/wiki/Character_(arts)" title="Character (arts)">
       fictional character
      </a>
      from the Australian soap opera
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Home_and_Away" title="Home and Away">
        Home and Away
       </a>
      </i>
      , played by
      <a href="/wiki/Isla_Fisher" title="Isla Fisher">
       Isla Fisher
      </a>
      . She made her first on screen appearance on 16 September 1994. She departed on 6 August 1997. Ashley Murray played a young Shannon in flashbacks.
     </p>

     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Casting">
       Casting
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Shannon_Reed&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Casting">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Fisher joined the cast of Home and Away in 1994, shortly after completing her role on
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Paradise_Beach" title="Paradise Beach">
        Paradise Beach
       </a>
      </i>
      , along with co-star
      <a href="/wiki/Shane_Ammann" title="Shane Ammann">
       Shane Ammann
      </a>
      to play the respective roles of Shannon and
      <a href="/wiki/Curtis_Reed_(Home_and_Away)" title="Curtis Reed (Home and Away)">
       Curtis Reed
      </a>
      .
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        1
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <p>
      In an interview with Rachel Browne of
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/The_Sun-Herald" title="The Sun-Herald">
        The Sun-Herald
       </a>
      </i>
      , Fisher spoke of her success and experiences on the show. "I would be stupid to let it go to my head because it could all end tomorrow and I would just fade back into obscurity." She added: "I like working on
      <i>
       Home And Away
      </i>
      but it's a heavy workload so I get stressed out a lot, We work about 15 hours a day, including the time it takes to learn lines. "I know a lot of people work those sort of hours but I think we really feel it because most of us are young and fairly inexperienced. I don't have a life, really. What you see on the screen is what I do. But I am very grateful because it is good experience. It's like an apprenticeship but we do it in front of 20 million people so all our mistakes are up for the world to see."
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        2
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <p>
      In 1997, Fisher announced she had left the show.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
       <a href="#cite_note-3">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        3
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Character_development">
       Character development
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Shannon_Reed&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Character development">

        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Shannon was bisexual. In her final storyline it was implied that she had moved away with a lesbian lover, but never directly stated.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lo_4-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-lo-4">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        4
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Storylines">
       Storylines
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Shannon_Reed&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Storylines">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Shannon and Curtis arrive in Summer Bay to be fostered by
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Michael_Ross_(Home_and_Away)" title="Michael Ross (Home and Away)">
       Michael
      </a>
      (Dennis Coard) and
      <a href="/wiki/Pippa_Ross" title="Pippa Ross">
       Pippa Ross
      </a>
      (
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Debra_Lawrence" title="Debra Lawrence">
       Debra Lawrence
      </a>
      ). The Rosses have room for only one of the pair, so
      <a href="/wiki/Alf_Stewart" title="Alf Stewart">
       Alf
      </a>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Ray_Meagher" title="Ray Meagher">
       Ray Meagher
      </a>
      ) and
      <a href="/wiki/Ailsa_Stewart" title="Ailsa Stewart">
       Ailsa Stewart
      </a>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Judy_Nunn" title="Judy Nunn">
       Judy Nunn
      </a>
      ) foster Curtis. When Shannon and Curtis are caught kissing by Alf, it causes controversy but the fuss dies down when they reveal they are foster siblings. Shannon clashes with
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Selina_Cook" title="Selina Cook">
       Selina Cook
      </a>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Tempany_Deckert" title="Tempany Deckert">
       Tempany Deckert
      </a>
      ) who takes an interest in Curtis but they soon become best friends. Shannon and Curtis split up and she dates
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Eric_Phillips" title="Eric Phillips">
       Eric Phillips
      </a>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Goddard_(actor)" title="Daniel Goddard (actor)">
       Daniel Goddard
      </a>
      ), who is revealed to be a drug dealer.
      <a href="/wiki/Damian_Roberts" title="Damian Roberts">
       Damian Roberts
      </a>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Matt_Doran" title="Matt Doran">
       Matt Doran
      </a>
      ) and
      <a href="/wiki/Jack_Wilson_(Home_and_Away)" title="Jack Wilson (Home and Away)">
       Jack Wilson
      </a>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Amalm" title="Daniel Amalm">
       Daniel Amalm
      </a>
      ) warn Shannon but she will not listen and tells them to keep out of her business. Shannon later breaks up with Eric after he gets heavy with her. She then experiences recurring nightmares of sexual abuse at the hands of her Uncle Tim and confides in teacher and counsellor
      <a href="/wiki/Donna_Bishop" title="Donna Bishop">
       Donna Bishop
      </a>
      (Nicola Quilter).
     </p>
     <p>
      Shannon begins babysitting
      <a href="/wiki/Angel_Parrish" title="Angel Parrish">
       Angel Parrish
      </a>
      's (
      <a href="/wiki/Melissa_George" title="Melissa George">
       Melissa George
      </a>
      ) son, Dylan (Corey Glaister) and forms an attraction to Angel's husband
      <a href="/wiki/Shane_Parrish" title="Shane Parrish">
       Shane
      </a>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Dieter_Brummer" title="Dieter Brummer">
       Dieter Brummer
      </a>
      ). She tries to seduce Shane but he rejects her. When Angel confronts Shannon, she lies and tells Angel she and Shane have been sleeping together, which Angel believes and promptly leaves Shane, taking Dylan with her. Angel and Dylan are involved in seaplane crash which leaves them stranded in the bush for several days but they are found a reunited with Shane. Angel refuses to forgive Shannon and her guilt spirals into anorexia, causing her to nearly drown when she passes out during a swim one day. Angel dives in to save her but is still frosty towards her. At Pippa and Michael's insistence, Shannon seeks help for her condition. After recovering, Shannon takes an interest in writing but things are still no better with Angel, who accuses her of stalking her. The culprit is revealed to be Angel's ex-boyfriend, Paul Harris (Ramsay Everingham), Dylan's biological father. Shannon saves Angel and Dylan when Paul attacks and Angel finally forgives her.
     </p>
     <p>
      Shannon invites author
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mandy_Thomas" title="Mandy Thomas">
       Mandy Thomas
      </a>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Rachael_Blake" title="Rachael Blake">
       Rachael Blake
      </a>
      ) to stay in Summer Bay. Shannon admires Mandy and they begin spending a lot of time together. Shannon's crush on Mandy becomes apparent and when Mandy's editor and former lover
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Toni_Jarvis" title="Toni Jarvis">
       Toni Jarvis
      </a>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Bridie_Carter" title="Bridie Carter">
       Bridie Carter
      </a>
      ) arrives she is extremely put out and begins avoiding Mandy altogether. However, Shannon and Mandy reconcile at a Benefit cruise to raise money for victims of the recent bush fire. Pippa and Selina become concerned when Shannon and Mandy openly dance with each other and Selina's comments do little to impress Shannon. Mandy leaves after reading a love story Shannon wrote with the characters based on her and Shannon.
     </p>
     <p>
      Shannon is devastated by Mandy's sudden departure but enters a relationship with local artist
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Alex_Bennett_(Home_and_Away)" title="Alex Bennett (Home and Away)">
       Alex Bennett
      </a>
      (Nick Freedman) who saves her, Selina and Damian from a gang of youths. Their relationship is met with disapproval from Michael once he learns Shannon plans to pose nude for one of Alex's paintings. When Alex rescues Selina from his father,
      <a href="/wiki/Saul_Bennett" title="Saul Bennett">
       Saul Bennett
      </a>
      's (David Ritchie) commune, Shannon becomes momentarily worried Selina may have a crush on Alex. Mandy returns telling her that she wants to dedicate her next book to her and Alex is jealous of their closeness, however he develops a friendship with Mandy. After
      <a href="/wiki/Donald_Fisher_(Home_and_Away)" title="Donald Fisher (Home and Away)">
       Donald Fisher
      </a>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Norman_Coburn" title="Norman Coburn">
       Norman Coburn
      </a>
      ) discovers that Shannon and Mandy have unconsciously plagiarised each other, Shannon forfeits an award in order to save Mandy's career.
     </p>
     <p>
      Shannon discovers that her real mother, Katherine Walker (
      <a href="/wiki/Linden_Wilkinson" title="Linden Wilkinson">
       Linden Wilkinson
      </a>
      ) is a ruthless politician and their relationship is very antagonistic. Alex soon leaves for
      <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">
       Paris
      </a>
      and Shannon begins dating Mandy's brother, Geoff (
      <a href="/wiki/Martin_Henderson" title="Martin Henderson">
       Martin Henderson
      </a>
      ), a sailor on shore leave. She and Geoff know they only have a short time so they put on a play showing how they would live their life in a day. After completing her HSC, Shannon enrolls at University and decides on an image change by dyeing her hair black and dating Kye Lyons (
      <a href="/wiki/Ian_Bliss" title="Ian Bliss">
       Ian Bliss
      </a>
      ) and moves in with him. She soon realises Kye is violent and dumps him. Kye then follows her to Summer Bay house and gets violent and he hits Pippa, leaving her comatose. Shannon then blames herself, Pippa recovers but is left with temporary memory loss.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="/wiki/Lachlan_Fraser" title="Lachlan Fraser">
       Lachlan Fraser
      </a>
      (
      <a href="/wiki/Richard_Grieve" title="Richard Grieve">
       Richard Grieve
      </a>
      ), the new local doctor takes an interest in Shannon and they become a couple. He takes her to have lunch with his dysfunctional family, most of whom are doctors and they witness his parents Peter (
      <a href="/wiki/Helmut_Bakaitis" title="Helmut Bakaitis">
       Helmut Bakaitis
      </a>
      ) and Diana (Kerry McGuire) arguing. After a brief spell of living together, Shannon and Lachie cool things down but Lachie soon proposes. Mandy returns once more and asks Shannon to join her in Paris. After much soul searching, Shannon decides her future is with Mandy and shares emotional goodbyes with Pippa, Lachie and Selina.
     </p>

      </sup>

       <a href="#cite_note-6">

         [

        <span>
