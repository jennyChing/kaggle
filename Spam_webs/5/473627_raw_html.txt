      <h1>
       Platform Lifts in Schools, Colleges and Universities
      </h1>
      <div class="homeHeaderImageMob">
       <picture alt="Platform Lifts in Schools, Colleges and Universities" class="none" title="Platform Lifts in Schools, Colleges and Universities">
        <!--[if IE 9]&gt;&lt;video style="display: none;"&gt;&lt;![endif]-->
        <source media="(max-width: 642px)" srcset="http://www.gartec.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2015/02/gartec-aritco-6000-kids-exiting-lift-603x469.jpg ">
         <source media="(max-width: 1025px)" srcset="http://www.gartec.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2015/02/gartec-aritco-6000-kids-exiting-lift-603x469.jpg ">
          <source media="(max-width: 1440px)" srcset="http://www.gartec.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2015/02/gartec-aritco-6000-kids-exiting-lift-313x243.jpg ">
           <source media="(max-width: 1860px)" srcset="http://www.gartec.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2015/02/gartec-aritco-6000-kids-exiting-lift-413x321.jpg ">
            <source media="(max-width: 9999px)" srcset="http://www.gartec.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2015/02/gartec-aritco-6000-kids-exiting-lift-578x450.jpg ">
             <!--[if IE 9]&gt;&lt;/video&gt;&lt;![endif]-->
             <img class="retina-false" srcset="http://www.gartec.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2015/02/gartec-aritco-6000-kids-exiting-lift-578x450.jpg "/>
             <noscript>
              <img src="http://www.gartec.com/wp-content/uploads/cache/2015/02/gartec-aritco-6000-kids-exiting-lift-578x450.jpg"/>
             </noscript>
            </source>
           </source>
          </source>
         </source>
        </source>
       </picture>
      </div>
      <!-- /.homeHeaderImage -->
      <p>
       The learning environment is unique and challenging when it comes to choosing the right access solutions to move staff, pupils or visitors smoothly around the building all day. Educational buildings are also likely to change regularly, for example an annexe or an additional level being added. Therefore existing systems must be able to interconnect easily, enabling everyone access to education whether able bodied or disabled.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Choosing The Right Commercial Lift
      </h2>
      <p>
       When it comes to choosing
       <a href="http://www.gartec.com/platform-lifts">
        platform lifts
       </a>
       for an educational building, there are three main considerations:
      </p>
      <ol>
       <li>
        <strong>
         <em>
          Durability
         </em>
        </strong>
         the
        <a href="http://www.gartec.com/aritco-7000">
         platform lift
        </a>
        must be tough enough for the job. Consider features that are vandal resistant such as flush buttons, and the option to restrict access via a locking facility.
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         <em>
          Safety
         </em>
        </strong>
         this is essential. Features should be included such as safety sensors around the lift platform, non-slip flooring and overload indication as standard. For more boisterous behaviour a full shaft enclosure is also advisable.
       </li>
       <li>
        <strong>
         <em>
          Building Regulations
         </em>
        </strong>
         a functional lift should also meet the requirements of Part M of the Building Regulations, EN81-41, as well as the Equality Act.
       </li>
      </ol>
      <p>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Considerations For Commercial Lift Installations In Educational Buildings
      </h2>
      <p>
       Platform size is key, typically 1100 x 1400 to meet Part M. However the size must be suitable for the unique needs of the educational building itself as regulatory requirements can vary depending on usage and the type of building the lift will be used in.
      </p>
      <p>
       Good practice is to control the access to the lift in a school environment to avoid potential misuse by students. School Locking Systems, whereby a key is supplied to authorised students and staff, ensures control of access is maintained
      </p>
      <p>
       For existing learning facilities inevitably there will be disruption. Keep this to a minimum by ensuring the lift provider is on site for the minimum time. Provision of a platform lift ensures quick installation can be virtually guaranteed, with no or very little requirement for building works  do ensure you choose a reputable company to carry out lift works.
      </p>
