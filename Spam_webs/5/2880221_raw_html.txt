        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Chicken
        </h1>
        <div class="post-info">

         </span>

         <p>
          <a href="http://i2.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Slow-Cooker-Lemon-Garlic-Chicken.jpg">
           <img alt="Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Chicken" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-11658" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Slow-Cooker-Lemon-Garlic-Chicken.jpg?resize=1024%2C679"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Families on the go need all sorts of
          <em>
           go-to
          </em>
          recipes that can be made in a snap! This is one of them, easy and delicious, just throw everything into your slow cooker or prep it as part of our
          <strong>
           <a href="http://whoneedsacape.com/2013/09/20-meals-in-2-hours-slow-cooker-freezer-meals/">
            20 Meals/2 Hours Slow Cooker Freezer Meals
           </a>
          </strong>
          post.  Youll be making something that the whole family will enjoy! Dont you find that sometimes the simple dishes wind up being the ones that get requested again &amp; again? They do at my house!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Pair up this dish with our flavorful
          <a href="http://whoneedsacape.com/2013/06/greek-pasta-salad/" target="_blank" title="Greek Pasta Salad">
           Greek Pasta Salad
          </a>
          or our
          <a href="http://whoneedsacape.com/2013/03/mashed-potatoes-with-wilted-spinach/" target="_blank" title="Mashed Potatoes with Wilted Spinach">
           Mashed Potatoes with Wilted Spinach
          </a>
          , so versatile! Gotta love the ease of your slow cooker!
          <br/>
          <a href="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/20-Freezer-Meals-in-2-Hours1.jpg">
           <img alt="20 Meals in 2 Hours Slow Cooker Freezer Meals | Who Needs A Cape?" class="aligncenter wp-image-11757" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/20-Freezer-Meals-in-2-Hours1.jpg?resize=800%2C800"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <div class="zlrecipe-container-border" id="zlrecipe-container-404" style="border: 1px dashed;">
          <div class="serif zlrecipe" id="zlrecipe-container" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Recipe">
           <div id="zlrecipe-innerdiv">
            <div class="item b-b">
             <div class="zlrecipe-print-link fl-r">
              <a class="butn-link" href="javascript:void(0);" onclick="zlrPrint('zlrecipe-container-404', 'http://whoneedsacape.com/wp-content/plugins/ziplist-recipe-plugin/'); return false" title="Print this recipe">
               Print

             </div>
             <div class="zl-recipe-link fl-r zl-rmvd" id="zl-recipe-link-404">
             </div>
             <div class="b-b h-1 strong" id="zlrecipe-title" itemprop="name">
              Slow Cooker Lemon Garlic Chicken
             </div>
            </div>
            <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
              <p id="zlrecipe-cook-time">
               Cook Time:
               <span content="PT6H" itemprop="cookTime">
                6 hours
               </span>
              </p>
             </div>
             <div class="fl-l width-50">
              <div id="zlrecipe-nutrition" itemprop="nutrition" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/NutritionInformation">
               <p id="zlrecipe-serving-size">
                Serving Size:
                <span itemprop="servingSize">
                 8 servings
                </span>
               </p>
              </div>
             </div>
             <div class="zlclear">
             </div>
            </div>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
             Ingredients
            </p>
            <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 lbs boneless chicken breasts
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 t dried oregano
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 t Lawry's Seasoning Salt
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/4 t ground pepper
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 cloves garlic, minced
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 t dried parsley
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
              3 T lemon juice
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 cup chicken broth
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
             Instructions
            </p>
            <ol class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Spray slow cooker with non-stick cooking spray.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Place chicken breasts into slow cooker.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Add all remaining ingredients and cover.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Cook 6-8 hours on low depending on your slow cooker.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Serve over rice or buttered egg noodles.
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              ***If freezing, place all ingredients in freezer bag***
             </li>
            </ol>
