        <h1>
         Where can I buy insulation?
        </h1>
        <p>
         Knauf Insulation's range of products are available across the UK through both trade and retail outlets.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Trade Insulation
        </h2>
        <p>
         Most Knauf Insulation products are available from your local builders merchant or distributor. If any product is not in stock, your merchant or distributorshould be able toorder it directly from us very quickly.
        </p>
        <p>
         Specialist blown insulation products, such as Supafil Cavity Wall Insulation and
         <a href="http://www.knaufinsulation.co.uk/en-gb/products/blown-mineral-wool/supafil-frame.aspx" title="Supafil Frame">
          Supafil Frame
         </a>
         are only available to Knauf Insulation approved cavity wall insulation installers directly from us.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Retail/DIY Insulation
        </h2>
        <p>
         Knauf Insulation produce the
         <a href="http://www.knaufinsulation.co.uk/en-gb/products/space-insulation-diy-loft-insulation.aspx">
          Space Insulation
         </a>
         range of products specifically for DIY'ers to use in loft insulation, and is available from:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          B&amp;Q
         </li>
         <li>
          Homebase
         </li>
         <li>
          Wickes
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         The availability of specific Space Insulation products can be foundat
         <a href="http://www.space-insulation.com/" onclick="window.open(this.href, '_blank'); return false;" onkeypress="if (event.keyCode==13) {window.open(this.href, '_blank'); return false;}" title="Where can I buy Space Insulation?">
          www.space-insulation.com
         </a>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Cavity Wall Insulation
        </h2>
        <p>
         <a href="#">
          Supafil Cavity Wall Insulation
         </a>
         is blown into cavity walls but this can only be done by an approved cavity wall installer.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.thinkinsulation.co.uk/contact.aspx">
          To request a obligation free survey and quote for insulating your cavity walls (and/or professionally insulating your loft), please visit www.thinkinsulation.co.uk
         </a>
        </p>
