For some perspective on the current state of the stock market, today's chart presents the prevailing trend of the Nasdaq.  Today's chart illustrates that the Nasdaq trades with in the confines of its one-year downtrend and is currently testing support (green line).  It is worth noting that the Nasdaq is currently trading lower than where he did at the beginning of 2004.
