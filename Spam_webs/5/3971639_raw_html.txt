         Saab 9-3 Aero Convertible Special Edition
        </a>
       </h1>
       <div class="post-body">
        <p>
        </p>
        <div style="clear:both;">
        </div>
        <span style="font-size:180%;">
         <span style="font-weight: bold;">
          2009 Saab 9-3 Aero Convertible Special Edition -
          <span style="font-style: italic;">
           206 kW V6 Turbo
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
        <br/>
        <span style="font-style: italic;font-size:100%;">
         GM Media Release
        </span>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.autoclub.com.au/uploaded_images/saab-9-3-aero-convertible-special-edition-2009-712306.jpg" target="_blank">
         <img alt="Saab 9-3 Aero Convertible Special Edition 2009" border="0" src="http://www.autoclub.com.au/uploaded_images/saab-9-3-aero-convertible-special-edition-2009-712297.jpg" style="margin: 5px 0pt 5px 10px; float: right; cursor: pointer; width: 250px; height: 166px;"/>
        </a>
        To celebrate the arrival of spring and the season of racing, the Saab Special Edition 9-3 Aero Convertible will make its international debut at the Melbourne Cup Carnival.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        In keeping with the racing theme, the new convertible will be showcased outside Saab's Flemington marquee in a unique champagne colour.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        For the first time in Australia, the Aero convertible will feature the 1.9-litre, 132 kW TTiD diesel engine in addition to the 2.8-litre, 206 kW V6 Turbo and both engine variants can be matched to six-speed manual or six-speed automatic transmissions.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        The 16-valve two-stage turbo engine meets a growing demand from drivers unwilling to sacrifice performance in the interests of fuel economy. Among the best in its class, the TTiD Aero convertible is fuel efficient with 5.9/100 km for the manual and 6.9/100 km for the automatic.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.autoclub.com.au/uploaded_images/saab-9-3-aero-convertible-special-edition-2009-738814.jpg" target="_blank">
         <img alt="Saab 9-3 Aero Convertible Special Edition 2009" border="0" src="http://www.autoclub.com.au/uploaded_images/saab-9-3-aero-convertible-special-edition-2009-738798.jpg" style="margin: 5px 10px 5px 0pt; float: left; cursor: pointer; width: 250px; height: 166px;"/>
        </a>
        The Special Edition also features many of the outstanding exterior characteristics which premiered on its stablemate the Turbo X to include 18" wheels in silver, bold front bumper treatments and sports steering wheel.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        The interior highlights a modern carbon fibre decor and 60th anniversary floor coverings.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        Saab surprised the automotive world when it pioneered the concept of a 'four season, four-seater' soft top Convertible more than 25 years ago. Designed to be practical and stylish, the Scandinavian-styled convertible changed the perception of open top vehicles to be used all the year round.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        "Saab has sold more than a quarter of a million convertibles worldwide and this latest edition highlights the fantastic design and performance that Saab convertibles have always represented" said Parveen Batish, Director of GM Premium Brands.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://www.autoclub.com.au/uploaded_images/saab-9-3-aero-convertible-special-edition-2009-763323.jpg" target="_blank">
         <img alt="Saab 9-3 Aero Convertible Special Edition 2009" border="0" src="http://www.autoclub.com.au/uploaded_images/saab-9-3-aero-convertible-special-edition-2009-763316.jpg" style="margin: 5px 0pt 5px 10px; float: right; cursor: pointer; width: 250px; height: 166px;"/>
        </a>
        The convertible will be available in three metallic colours to include Jet Black, Carbon Grey and the new Bright Champagne.
        <br/>
        <br/>
        On sale in November, the Saab Special Edition 9-3 Convertible will be priced starting at AUD$95,000, plus dealer delivery and government charges.
