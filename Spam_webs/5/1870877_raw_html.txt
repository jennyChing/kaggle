            <div class="title-secondary">

             <a>
              Usha Sharma

             <p>
             </p>
             <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
              <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
               Bollywood actress
               <b>
                Shraddha Kapoor
               </b>
               's first item song titled, `
               <b>
                Dance Basanti
               </b>
               ` for `
               <b>
                Ungli
               </b>
               ` is finally out. The much-awaited song from the film had been doing the rounds for quite sometime now, as the talented diva will be seen in a glamorous item girl avatar.
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
              </span>
              <br/>
              <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
               <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-N7lEWH5hK0k/VFDjZ37jxUI/AAAAAAAAAuU/ZG6YulH6EiU/s1600/Dance-Basanti-Ungli-Emraan-Hashmi-Shraddha-Kapoor.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                <img border="0" height="230" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-N7lEWH5hK0k/VFDjZ37jxUI/AAAAAAAAAuU/ZG6YulH6EiU/s1600/Dance-Basanti-Ungli-Emraan-Hashmi-Shraddha-Kapoor.JPG" width="400"/>
               </a>
              </div>
              <br/>
              <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
               Touted to be as the party anthem of the year, Shraddha has revived her look in Karan Johar's production venture `Ungli`. From being the girl-next-door, she has taken the dance floor by her oomph and style with serial kisser Emraan Hashmi.
               <br/>
               <br/>
               This is the first time the duo will be seen together on-screen
               <br/>
               <br/>
               'Dance Basanti' surely will top the chartbusters list very soon. Here take a look at the peppy number:
              </span>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <span style="font-family: Verdana,sans-serif;">
               <b>
                Dance Basanti - Official Song - Ungli - Emraan Hashmi, Shraddha Kapoor
               </b>
              </span>
