          <h1 class="headline">
           <a href="http://www.iviewus.com/blog/tips-for-windows-8-1-users/" rel="bookmark">
            Tips for Windows 8.1 Users
           </a>
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="posts-date">
          <span class="post_date" title="2014-10-27">
           October 27, 2014
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="posts-body">
        <div class="post_content .post-proper">
         <p>
          Windows 8.1 just released lots of great features for tablet users and PC users alike. There are tons of useful tips or tricks that you must know before using them. Weve compiled some handy and useful tips for Windows 8.1 users using the new
          <a href="http://www.iviewus.com/index.php/iview-tablet/iviewwindowstablet.html" title="Iview Windows Tablets">
           IView Windows tablets
          </a>
          on Windows 8.1 O/S.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.iviewus.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/i800qw_2_.jpg">
           <img alt="i800qw_2_" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-759" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.iviewus.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/i800qw_2_.jpg?resize=600%2C600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           For The Start Screen
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          1. Customize your home screen
         </p>
         <p>
          You can customize your home screen by long press any tile on the Start screen and there will be four different options. You can move any tile into any different group you wish to put in and rename it, type Name group to do so.
         </p>
         <p>
          2. Change default apps
         </p>
         <p>
          If you wish to change your default app to Pandora, iHeartRadio etc. instead of Xbox music app, this is what you going to do. You can change your default app by swiping to the left from Start screen and tap Settings, then select Change PC settings. Press defaults at the bottom and choose a new app you wish to do so.
         </p>
         <p>
          3. Change your wallpaper or color
         </p>
         <p>
          If you wish to change your wallpaper, just swipe to the left from the home screen and select Settings &gt; Personalize and change the colors or wallpapers you wish to be changed.
         </p>
         <p>
          4. Change your password
         </p>
         <p>
          If you wish to change your password, in settings click PC settings &gt; Users &gt; Sign-in options &gt; create picture password. You can choose any photo from your camera roll.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           For productivity
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
          </strong>
          5. Taking a picture from the lock screen.
         </p>
         <p>
          From the lock screen, if you dont want to enter your password. Simply swipe down from the top to take a picture.
         </p>
         <p>
          6. Hot corners
         </p>
         <p>
          The hot corners can be able or disabled by going to Settings &gt; PC Settings &gt; PC and devices &gt; Corner and Edges &gt; Turn Corner Navigation Off. The hot corners are the zones that activate various features when you are using Windows 8.1. By swiping to the left from the right side of the screen, you will see the Charms menu, and you will see the settings, share button, search and a shortcut to the Start Screen.
         </p>
         <p>
          7. Update automatically
         </p>
         <p>
          Press Store app &gt; swipe to the left &gt; settings &gt; app updates and turns Automatically update my apps to On.
         </p>
         <p>
          8. Refine Search
         </p>
         <p>
          From the Start Screen, if you wish to search something, you do not need to go on browse. Just simply type whatever you wish to search on your Start Screen and it will shows up.
         </p>
         <p>
          Windows 8.1 is a reliable, versatile operating system that is ideal for entertainment seeking tablet users and educational purposes alike. With a little customization on the homescreen and app selection you will be on your way to a customized tablet that caters to your unique, individual needs and features.
         </p>
