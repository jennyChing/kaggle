      <p>
       Nepal is one of the natural wonders on Earth.Having been listed as '1000 places to go before you die' or
       <br/>
       'Best trekking routes in the world' and many more by World's top most travel magazines and websites,
       <br/>
       Nepal has lot more to offer than tourists of varied interest ever desire.
       <br/>
       Here's a collection of some of the amazing videos that will virtually show you the natural magnificience
       <br/>
       and cultural grandeur that lies in this piece of heaven.
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-size: small;">
        <strong>
         Here are the answers to Why Nepal? on your travel destination
        </strong>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/oc1HDF2AyNE" width="560">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p>
       <br/>
       1. Lets Start with Video "
       <strong>
        Happy Kathmandu
       </strong>
       " video. This Video Captured almost most happening Places of Kathmandu, like Durbar Marg ( Kings way), Basantapur, Swyambhunath, PashupatiNath etc. You will get an idea about the People, Places and surrounding.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <iframe height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/VYZvgd71XLY" width="560">
       </iframe>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       2.The video, with the background OST of
       <strong>
        Game of Thrones
       </strong>
       takes us right into the scenic and landscape diversity of Nepal through the Gates of Muktinath(from a drone view). One of the most important sacred places ofNepal, and also the most travelled trekking destination which falls under the ACAP circuit. The video also shows, Bhakhatpur Durbar Square, Shoyambhunath Temple, two of the World Heritage site and Chitwan National Park which is a natural heritage site. Other famous places include Poon hill. With occasional view of Dhaulagiri, Machchhaapuchhre.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <iframe height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/fb9_U8pIFsM" width="560">
       </iframe>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        <br/>
        <br/>
        3. Bungy at Bhotekoshi
       </strong>
       : In the recent years,
       <a href="../../category-Bungy/bungee-in-bhotekoshi-nepal/" target="_blank">
        Bungee in the Last Resort
       </a>
       has been famous among international communities as well as Nepali citizen. The drop is 160 meters, in to a George that strikes fear in everyone, falling right into and above one of the deadliest rivers in Nepal.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <iframe height="281" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/43968945?portrait=0" width="500">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p>
       4:
       <strong>
        Poonhill,Pokhara and Lumbini:
       </strong>
       Well Captured tour during
       <a href="../../category-Trekking/ghorepani-poon-hill-ghandruk-trek/" target="_blank" title="Ghorepani, Poonhill, Ghandruk Trek">
        Poonhill
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="../../category-Travel-Tips/3-reasons-to-love-pokhara-1/" target="_blank">
        Pokhara
       </a>
       and
       <a href="../../category-Tour/Lumbini-the-world-heritage/" title="Lumbini - The World Heritage Site">
        Lumbini
       </a>
       . This video almost give you the glimplse of the Poonhill trek and Lumbini.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <iframe height="281" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/95937973" width="500">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        5. Langtang Cycling:
       </strong>
       <br/>
       This video shows, one of the most trekked regions of Nepal. The shortest place from Kathmandu to have a good trekking experience.The video shows
       <a href="../../category-Trekking/langtang-kyanjin-nepal-trek/" target="_blank" title="Langtang Valley Trek">
        Langtang valley and KyanjinRi
       </a>
       , from where the cyclist rides down the rough terrain of Langtang.He rides down to Langtang valley from Kyanjin and then down towards Syafru, riding through the terrains of Ghodaatabela, Riverside, Rimche Bamboo. This trail is one of the best trekking trails and not only for hikers but also mountain biking.
      </p>
      <p>
       <iframe height="281" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/42510604" width="500">
       </iframe>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        6. Gosaikunda Lake:
       </strong>
       The video, 3 Sacred lakes shows the
       <a href="../../category-Trekking/gosaikunda-trek/" target="_blank" title="See photos and details of Gosaikunda Trek">
        Gosaikunda lake
       </a>
       and its surrounding lakes ,BhairavKunda, Naagkunda! The trek falls under Langtang  Helambu trek and is one of the most beautiful places in Nepal. Not only in scenic beauty but it holds natural as well as religious importance. Pilgrims from all over Nepal as well as from India travel to Gosaikunda during a festival around May-June. Situated at an altitude of 4380 meters, Gosaikunda lake is one of the most visited trekking destination.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <iframe height="375" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/58928987" width="500">
       </iframe>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        7. Mustang:
       </strong>
       The video titled
       <strong>
        something good
       </strong>
       shows the dry desert of Nepal,
       <a href="../../category-Trekking/Discover-Mustang/" target="_blank" title="Discover Mustang">
        Mustang
       </a>
       . Somewhere along, Mustang the rider is on his journey in a bicycle and it shows that the terrain of Mustang is very good for Cycling purposes.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <iframe height="281" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/83867687" width="500">
       </iframe>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        8: Rara Lake:
       </strong>
       The Rara Lake is situated in Mugu. It is the Largest Lake in Nepal. It is considered a very rough trekking route, yet one of the most beautiful trekking places in Nepal. It falls inside Rara National park which is rich in diversity, more than 200 species of Birds are found in Rara, along with few species of Endemic fishes.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <iframe height="281" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/61328584" width="500">
       </iframe>
       <br/>
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        9: Documentry on 1000 Places to see Before you die "NEPAL"
        <br/>
        <br/>
       </strong>
       <iframe height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/K2jw6ktOi20?list=PLD0FD8754B5586108" width="560">
       </iframe>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        10: Annapunra Trek:
       </strong>
       <a href="../../category-Trekking/around-annapurna-circuit/" target="_blank" title="Around Annapurna Circuit">
        Annapurna trek
       </a>
       is one of the most famous trekking destinations in Nepal. Annapurna Conservation Area Project (ACAP) circuit covers wide range of various short trekking destinations with in it. Ghorepani-Ghandurk, ABC trek is very famous as well as
       <a href="../../category-Tour/Jomsom-Muktinath-Tour-From-Kathmandu-to-Beni-to-Jomsom/" target="_blank">
        Jomsom
       </a>
       Mustang. Tilicho lake and Manang and as a whole circuit starting from BesiSahar is attraction of the circuit, with multicultural as well as mountain and wildlife diversity.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <iframe height="281" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/28254943" width="500">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        11: Everest Base Camp:
       </strong>
       <a href="../../category-Trekking/Trek-to-Everest-Base-camp/" target="_blank">
        Everest Base camp
       </a>
       has to be the ultimate travel destination in everyone list. Mount Everest, the highest mountain peak in the earth lies in Nepal. One of the most travelled destination in Nepal, received thousands of tourists every year.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <iframe height="281" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/46559181?title=0&amp;byline=0&amp;portrait=0&amp;color=ffffff" width="500">
       </iframe>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        12: Pokhara City:
       </strong>
       Pokhara city, the most travelled place in Nepal. Not only is it rich in scenic beauty and lakes, it also has one of the most lively places in Nepal. Lakeside is as lively as ever! Its also a hub for various trekking destination around Annapurna Circuit. There are lots of places to visit in Pokhara, as shown in the video. Davis fall, Bat Cave, Mahendra cave are the attraction. Fewataal, Begnastaal are other major attraction.
       <br/>
       <br/>
      </p>
      <p class="MsoNormal">
       <iframe height="281" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/39764008" width="500">
       </iframe>
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        13: Pictorial Journey:
       </strong>
       Caption from the video:"I've spent almost 3 weeks in Nepal during March-April 2011. This slideshow includes some of photos that were captured in that amazing journey. Places and events: 1. Holi celebrations, Kathmandu 2. Swayambhunath 3. Nagarkot 4. Boudhanath 5. Pokhara 6. Phewa Lake 7. Part of Annapurna Trek 8. Chitwan National Park"
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <iframe height="281" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/25409179" width="500">
       </iframe>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        14: Kathmandu City:
       </strong>
       Kathmandu City, the city of Temples, the same goes for the whole valley. Kathmandu valley is rich culturally, traditionally as well as architectural heritage. 7 monuments within the valley fall under World Heritage list. The three famous Durbar Squares, Kathmandu, Bhaktapur, Lalitpur, Pashupatinath temple, Soyambhunath, Bouddhanath and Changunarayan
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <iframe height="281" src="//player.vimeo.com/video/86390452" width="500">
       </iframe>
      </p>
