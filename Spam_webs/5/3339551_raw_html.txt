Neons are sadly lacking in my nail polish stash, which now holds &lt;u&gt;only&lt;/u&gt; some 300 bottles. Apart from China Glaze Four Leaf Clover, which my camera miraculously turns into a turquoise blue instead of the neon green it is, I don't own any real neon polishes. I received my first Color Club nail polish in my &lt;a href="http://www.mybeautyjunction.com/2014/04/lip-factory-beauty-box-april-2014-unboxing-review.html" target="_blank"&gt;April Lip Factory Box&lt;/a&gt; - it was Peace Out Purple, a bright purply-pink, and decided to rope it in as my base colour. I then picked some fluorescent green nail art stickers I had picked up back in 2012 from a roadside shop. Believe it or not, the glue still adheres! &lt;br /&gt;
&lt;a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008BCSA6U?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creativeASIN=B008BCSA6U&amp;linkCode=xm2&amp;tag=mybeajun-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"&gt;Color Club Peace Out Purple&lt;/a&gt; belongs to the Poptastic Neons Collection, which includes "psychedelic" shades inspired by the 1960s. It certainly looks Pink Floyd-ish! Here is how the nail polish looks without the stickers, in natural light:&lt;br /&gt;
&lt;a name='more'&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Fln2Dp-N3qc/U2ppeh7R5-I/AAAAAAAAPWc/RmRyTLTp5-A/s1600/Neon-green-nail-art-stickers-on-color-club-peace-out-purple-polish+(14).JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"&gt;&lt;img alt="Neon manicure with Color Club Peace Out Purple and nail art stickers" border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Fln2Dp-N3qc/U2ppeh7R5-I/AAAAAAAAPWc/RmRyTLTp5-A/s1600/Neon-green-nail-art-stickers-on-color-club-peace-out-purple-polish+(14).JPG" height="276" title="Neon manicure with Color Club Peace Out Purple and nail art stickers" width="400" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
The formula is more crelly than creme. There was some streaking even with two coats and it took me three coats to achieve opacity - even so, you can see faint outlines of the nail tips. Drying time was longer than I had expected and I got a few dings even with Sephora drying oil, which is why my ring finger already has the nail art stickers affixed. &lt;br /&gt;
When you peel off this polish (as I do when I'm bored), it "squishes" off instead of coming off in peels! The polish is three-free (no formaldehyde, toluene or dibutyl-phthalate), cruelty-free and vegan. I absolutely love the finish; it is so glazed that you won't need topcoat. &lt;br /&gt;
&lt;div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"&gt;
&lt;a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-xNU7zLB7l-E/U2potZp98cI/AAAAAAAAPWU/NhNMMpnz7bE/s1600/Neon-green-nail-art-stickers-on-color-club-peace-out-purple-polish+(6).JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"&gt;&lt;img alt="Neon manicure with Color Club Peace Out Purple and nail art stickers" border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-xNU7zLB7l-E/U2potZp98cI/AAAAAAAAPWU/NhNMMpnz7bE/s1600/Neon-green-nail-art-stickers-on-color-club-peace-out-purple-polish+(6).JPG" height="277" title="Neon manicure with Color Club Peace Out Purple and nail art stickers" width="400" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
I did, however, apply topcoat after affixing the nail art stickers, to keep them in place. I have a few boxes of assorted stickers and have just found out how convenient and quick they are - and pretty - so you will see use of stickers in my manicures more frequently. The first photograph was with flash; the smaller pictures are in natural light. Does neon suit me? Do share your thoughts on this mani!&lt;br /&gt;

            replybox.height = '250px';

              + '#' + replyUrlParts[1];

        }
