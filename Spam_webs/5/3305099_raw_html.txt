                     <b>
                      <span style="color: orange;">
                       exfoliate
                      </span>
                     </b>

                     <b>
                      <span style="color: orange;">
                       Moisturizing is key
                      </span>
                     </b>
                     to not only keeping your skin from getting dry, but helps to prevent the onset of premature wrinkles and fine lines.                      <b>
