         <h1>
          Man charges a LEAF electric car using an Hawaiian volcano! (video)
         </h1>
         <hr/>
         <div class="mceContentBody">
          <a class="main-image-link fancybox" href="/media/blog-images/nissan-maui-volcano-recharge-1.jpg.662x0_q100_crop-scale_jpg_610x2000_q85.png">
           <img alt="Man charges a LEAF electric car using an Hawaiian volcano! (video)" height="142" src="/media/blog-images/nissan-maui-volcano-recharge-1.jpg.662x0_q100_crop-scale_jpg_280x2000_q85.png" width="280"/>
          </a>
          <p>
           Green car geeks have known everything there is to know about regenerative braking ever since the first versions of the Honda Insight and Toyota Prius came out over a decade ago, but for most of the general public, it is still a mystery. That's why I think this video by Nissan does a good job; it's not about how things work on the technical level, but rather on the practical level.

           <br/>
           The video tells the story of Neil Wagner, a man who just wants to see the sun rise from the top of a volcano (who wouldn't, right?). He happens to live on...
