       <p>
        PornMD has built
        <a href="//www.pornmd.com/sex-search">
         an interactive map
        </a>
        showing the top 10 porn search terms by geographic region. It's an interesting look at what unites and divides the people of the world, when it comes to watching the people of the world have sex. Below you'll find the map itself, as well as the 13 most interesting things we found while poking around.
        <br/>
        <iframe height="700" scrolling="no" src="//www.pornmd.com/resources/infograph/index.php" style="border: none;" width="100%">
         <a href="//www.pornmd.com/sex-search">
          Global Sex Search Habits
         </a>
        </iframe>
       </p>
       <h3>
        1. Indian porn is popular in India, Nigeria, and New Jersey. New Jersey is the only state in which "Indian" cracks the top 10.
        <br/>
        2. Kentucky is the only state to specify that they want their porn free. Kentuckians are either great at getting a bargain or unaware that most porn online is free.
        <br/>
        3. Both California and Hawaii have "asian" as their top search, but Californians seem more interested in Koreans, while Hawaii searches more for "Japanese." This rivalry continues on the world stage where China's top search is "Japanese," but Cambodia's is "korean sex (gay)."
        <br/>
        4. "College" appears in every state's top ten searches except Wyoming, who seems to be undervaluing the American education system.
        <br/>
        5. The fourth most popular search term in Peru is "just bieber (gay)." This is also the fourth most popular YouTube comment in America.
        <br/>
        6. A search for "Native American" does appear, but in Iceland for some reason.
        <br/>
        7. In Romania, "mom and son" is so popular it appears in both the first and fifth position.
        <br/>
        8. In Korea the search term "mother" is more popular than "toilet" and "shitting," but still less popular than "shit." Better luck next year, Mom.
        <br/>
        9. America really, really, really likes teens.
        <br/>
        10. In Kazakhstan the second most popular search is "squirt machine" which is hopefully a person and not some kind of terrifying device built in a basement.
        <br/>
        11. In Morocco, the #3 and #4 search terms are "anorexic" and "big ass" because Morocco hasn't learned that you can't have it both ways.
        <br/>
        12. Nigeria wins the award for Least Creative Porn Search with the third most popular item: "things I jack off to," presumably beating out "Videos and/or photos of people procreating."
        <br/>
        13. In Libya the best milfs seem to come from Japan, but the best anal sluts come from America. See, Wyoming? Libya knows what's up.
       </h3>
