        <div style="float: right; margin-left: 10px;">
         <a class="twitter-share-button" data-count="vertical" data-hashtags="Badgley+Mischka,Decorated+Bridal+Shoes,Decorated+Shoes,Designer+Bridal+Shoes,Designer+Wedding+Shoes,Dyeable+Wedding+Shoes,Dyeables,Something+Bleu+Bridal+Shoes,Something+Bleu+Wedding+Shoes,Something+Blue,Wedding+Shoes,Wedding+Trends" data-url="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/blog/how-to-choose-the-perfect-blue-wedding-shoes/" data-via="MyGlassSlipper" href="https://twitter.com/share">

        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/blue-wedding-shoes.jpg">
          <img alt="blue-wedding-shoes" class=" wp-image-6923 aligncenter" height="205" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/blue-wedding-shoes-300x187.jpg" width="315"/>
         </a>
         While white wedding shoes have not lost their popularity, more and more brides are looking to expand their color palettes. Blue wedding shoes are becoming quite popular with brides who crave creativity and want to create a unique look on the big day.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/blue3.jpg">
          <img alt="blue3" class="300 pix 200 pix" height="200" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/blue3.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/blue4.jpg">
          <img alt="blue4" class="alignnone wp-image-6925" height="200" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/blue4-300x196.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Wedding Colors
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Blue is one of the most popular wedding colors, and for good reasons. A large percentage of people say that blue is their favorite color, so it is only natural that brides would want to incorporate it into their ceremonies. Blue also coordinates well with other colors, so its inclusion in the wedding can be a smart move.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_6737" style="width: 300px">
         <a href="http://www.myglassslipper.com/DANCE#.U4T8AnknJXI">
          <img alt="DANCE01.700.1" class=" wp-image-6737 " height="300" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/DANCE01.700.11-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          The Dance by Betsy Johnson is a beautifully designed shoe shows off with its simple round toe and low heel made in a sparkly fabric. The gentle scallops on the edges add subtle detail to an elegant shoe. The blue sole can be your something blue for your walk down the aisle.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         No matter what the reason, blue wedding shoes are becoming increasingly popular. That gives brides a real opportunity to break from traditional and choose the colors they love. If you have chosen blue as one of your wedding colors, you need to decide how to use that color for your wedding shoes. Some brides will wear traditional white shoes adorned with blue accents that match the wedding theme. Others will prefer
         <a href="http://www.myglassslipper.com/blue-wedding-shoes">
          blue wedding shoes
         </a>
         in their chosen shade. As with everything else wedding related, the choice of shoes is a highly personal one.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Color Swatches
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         A big part of choosing the perfect blue wedding shoes is getting the color just right. That will be impossible without a good set of color swatches from your wedding planner or dress designer. No matter who is planning your wedding, it is important to have the color information you need before you head out in search of the perfect shoes.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Egg-Blue.jpg">
          <img alt="Egg Blue" class="alignnone wp-image-6927" height="100" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Egg-Blue-300x300.jpg" width="100"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Light-Blue.jpg">
          <img alt="Light Blue" class="alignnone wp-image-6928" height="100" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Light-Blue-300x300.jpg" width="100"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Peacock.jpg">
          <img alt="Peacock" class="alignnone wp-image-6929" height="100" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Peacock-300x300.jpg" width="100"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Royal-Blue.jpg">
          <img alt="Royal Blue" class="alignnone wp-image-6930" height="100" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Royal-Blue-300x300.jpg" width="100"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Seafoam.jpg">

        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Looking for the perfect hue of blue to dye your wedding shoes? You can order a
         <a href="http://www.myglassslipper.com/order-a-swatch?pageNo=2">
          colorswatch
         </a>
         fromMy Glass Slipper.com.
        </p>
        <p>
         Once you have the color swatches in hand, you can start seeking out the perfect shoes. If you have already ordered your gown, you may be able to get your blue wedding shoes from the same source. If not, it is important to start shopping early, since it may take some time to find blue wedding shoes that match both your color needs and personal taste and style.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/blue.png">
          <img alt="blue" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6921" height="183" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/blue.png" width="275"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Dye Your Own
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">

Even if the bridal shop does not have the perfect shade in stock, the owner may be able to order a pair of shoes that will perfectly match your wedding colors and look perfect on the big day.

        <p style="text-align: center;">
         While many brides still choose traditional high heels for the big day, a growing number of people are looking beyond those uncomfortable shoes and seeking out more practical alternatives.
         <a href="http://www.myglassslipper.com/flat-bridal-shoes">
          Flat wedding shoes
         </a>
         are becoming increasingly popular with modern brides, and they are turning up in some very unexpected places.
        </p>

         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Flat wedding shoes have a number of important advantages for busy brides. They are more comfortable, an important consideration for brides who will be on their feet from morning to night. Flat shoes are also versatile and easy to customize. Brides can use adornments and accessories to match their shoes to their wedding gowns and incorporate their wedding colors into their footwear. Whether you are planning a fun wedding ceremony at the beach or a formal party at the local country club, flat wedding shoes can be part of the celebration.

         </span>

        </p>

        <p>
         Anyone whos familiar and appreciative of fashion has likely heard of Badgley Mischka. The American designer house launched in 1988 after the two founders met in college. The bridal department, however,didnttake center stageuntil 1993.
        </p>
        <p>

         </a>
         Often selling side by side major designers like Manolo Blahnik, Betsy Johnson, and Jimmy Choo; Badgley Mischka offers stunning designs at a reasonable price point when compared to several competitors.
        </p>

        </p>

          Types
         </b>
        </p>

         Badgley Mischka is primarily a dress shoe. As such, the most popular styles revolve around high heeled pumps, sandals, platforms, and wedges. The standard 2.5  5 inch heels are thick enough to feel secure while walking and come in closed toe, open toe, dOrsay, and traditional pointed or rounded toe pump designs.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/SWAN01.700.31.jpg">
          <img alt="SWAN01.700.3[1]" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-6870" height="300" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/SWAN01.700.31-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>

        </p>
        <p>
         Many of the designs emulate 1940s glam and include adornments of lush fabric and sparkling brooch embellishments. 1/2 inch platform leather soles ease novices to designer heels into the heel height challenge. While sumptuous leather, satin and rich silks dress the shoes themselves.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/GOODIE01.700.11.jpg">
          <img alt="GOODIE01.700.1[1]" class=" wp-image-6866 alignleft" height="300" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/GOODIE01.700.11-300x300.jpg" style="width: 404px; height: 422px;" width="300"/>
         </a>
         The dress shoe styles are labeled with adorable modern female names and range in design from sophisticated to sassy to sexy. While the shoes add a dazzling touch to any special event, weddings are where they truly shine. Several stunning designs are available for a brides special day, but the
         <a href="http://www.myglassslipper.com/GOODIE-ES">
          Goodie-ES
         </a>
         is one of the top sellers from year to year. Its a classic pump fashioned after true Hollywood glamour with a sparkling center gem above the open platform toe. The sturdy heel gives the star of the day 4 1/4 extra inches of height. Many bridal styles come in ivory, but can be dyed to your chosen wedding colors. But are they comfortable?
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.myglassslipper.com/designer-wedding-shoes/badgley-mischka">
          Badgely Mischka shoes
         </a>
         tend to run small, so its important to try them on if possible to ensure the proper fit. Most of the time, ordering a full size larger than your normal size will do the trick. In the event of a wedding, youll be on your feet more than youll be off of them. Comfort is an issue. If heels are a requirement, an open toe design may help in that event. Once the proper fit is decided, these shoes often provide all day comfort. Shoe cushions and sole grips can help ensure the proper comfort levels for your event.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/TIP-TOES-TO-GO01.700.11.jpg">
          <img alt="TIP-TOES-TO-GO01.700.1[1]" class="size-medium wp-image-6871 alignleft" height="300" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/TIP-TOES-TO-GO01.700.11-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>

         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/blog/explore-the-advantages-of-dyeable-wedding-shoes/" rel="bookmark">
          Explore the Advantages of Dyeable Wedding Shoes
         </a>
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="byline">
          by
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="fn" href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/blog/author/tylar/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Tylar Polson">

         <span class="posted-on">

            April 28, 2014
           </time>
          </a>
         </span>
         //
         <span class="comments">
          <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/blog/explore-the-advantages-of-dyeable-wedding-shoes/#comments">

           </i>

        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div style="float: right; margin-left: 10px;">
         <a class="twitter-share-button" data-count="vertical" data-hashtags="Badgley+Mischka+Wedding+Shoes,bridal+shoes,BridalShoes.com,Colored+Wedding+Shoes,Decorated+Bridal+Shoes,Designer+Bridal+Shoes,Designer+Wedding+Shoes,Dyeable+Shoes,Dyeable+Wedding+Shoes,Dyeables,Dyed+Shoes,My+Glass+Slipper,My+Glass+Slipper+History,Real+Weddings,Twitter" data-url="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/blog/explore-the-advantages-of-dyeable-wedding-shoes/" data-via="MyGlassSlipper" href="https://twitter.com/share">
          Tweet
         </a>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         When brides plan their weddings, the shoes they and their bridal party will be wearing often get short shrift compared to the wedding gown and bridesmaid dresses. That is a shame, since the shoes play a huge role in any wedding ceremony.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Pink_bridesmaid_shoes-e1308960500183.jpg">
          <img alt="Pink_bridesmaid_shoes-e1308960500183" class="alignnone wp-image-6846" height="201" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/Pink_bridesmaid_shoes-e1308960500183-300x201.jpg" style="width: 427px; height: 326px;" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         If the bride spends her big day wearing uncomfortable shoes and battling sore feet, she will not exactly be in the mood to dance the night away and greet her wedding guests. If the bridesmaids are stuck with unflattering shoes in hideous colors, they may not be so happy either. Spending some time picking out the perfect shoes can help you avoid those uncomfortable situations and have a wonderful wedding.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Dyeable Wedding Shoes for Every Occasion
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Dyeable wedding shoes are beginning to make real inroads in the wedding industry. More and more brides are discovering just how beautiful and versatile dyeable wedding shoes really are, and they are including them in their wedding plans.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/JEWEL01.700.11.jpg">
          <img alt="JEWEL01.700.1[1]" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-6857" height="300" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/JEWEL01.700.11-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/JEWEL01.RASPBERRY.700.11.jpg">
          <img alt="JEWEL01.RASPBERRY.700.1[1]" class="alignnone wp-image-6859" height="300" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/JEWEL01.RASPBERRY.700.11-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         The
         <a href="http://www.myglassslipper.com/JEWEL?defaultColor=CORAL&amp;#.U1qs6XknJXI">
          Jewel
         </a>
         by David Tuterafeatured in ivory and raspberrycan be dyed any of our vibrant hues!
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Bridesmaids love
         <a href="http://www.myglassslipper.com/dyeable-wedding-shoes">
          dyeable wedding shoes
         </a>
         because they give new life to what had been a one-time purchase. Instead of throwing their bridesmaid shoes in the back of the closet and forgetting about them, your bridesmaids can simply grab a bottle of dye and gain a great new pair of pumps.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/bride-shoe.jpg">
          <img alt="bride shoe" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-6841" height="300" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/bride-shoe-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         Brides also love dyeable wedding shoes for their versatility and style. Many brides are surprised to find that dyeable wedding shoes come in every type of style, from stylish high heels to casual flats and demure pumps. No matter what your style, you will be able to find the perfect pair of dyeable wedding shoes.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          The Perfect Venue  The Perfect Shoes
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/nestldown-wedding-venue-outdoor.jpg">
          <img alt="nestldown-wedding-venue-outdoor" class="size-medium wp-image-6860 alignleft" height="199" src="http://blog.myglassslipper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/nestldown-wedding-venue-outdoor-300x199.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         No matter what type of wedding you have planned, you need to choose your dyeable wedding shoes accordingly. The venue you have chosen for your wedding and reception will give you some important clues about the most appropriate type of footwear.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         A pair of stiletto heels dyed to match your wedding dress may be perfectly appropriate for a formal country club wedding, but they would be totally out of place for a wedding at the beach or LocalPark. If you are planning a casual country wedding or beachside ceremony, you can find any number of wonderful dyeable wedding flats that will be right at home on the grass or in the sand.
        </p>
       </div>
