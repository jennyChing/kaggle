      <h1 class="post-title">
       3 Good Reasons to Love GelinAbaci
      </h1>
      <div class="post-meta">
       Posted on April 21, 2015 in:
       <a href="http://www.engagediamonds.com/engaged/" rel="category tag">
        Engaged
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="http://www.engagediamonds.com/featured/" rel="category tag">
        Featured
       </a>
       <span class="sep">
        |
       </span>
       <a href="#comments">
        Jump To Comments
       </a>
      </div>
      <div class="post-content">
       <p>
        At Engage Diamond Studio, we have the opportunity to work with an endless number of pieces from designers and manufacturers known for their artwork and craftsmanship in jewellery. Among our favourites is
        <a href="http://www.engagediamonds.com/engagement-rings/tension-set/">
         GelinAbaci
        </a>
        , the tension ring specialist who has set the standard for this class of diamond engagement and wedding rings. Not only do we enjoy working with the GelinAbaci collection, but we also enjoy being able to experience the happiness of our clients as we present them with exquisite pieces.
       </p>
       <p>
        We welcome couples from all over Canada to our Toronto showroom, from which they can see and experience the finest GelinAbaci tension rings in the world. It is not long before they realize what exquisite jewellery this is. If you have not seen it for yourself, here are three good reasons to love GelinAbaci:
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="GelinAbaci" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11343" height="550" src="http://www.engagediamonds.com/wp-content/uploads/GelinAbaci.jpg" width="550"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         1. Stunning Brilliance
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        The tension setting preferred by GelinAbaci has been created for one primary purpose: to allow as much light as possible to pass through the diamond. As you probably know, a well-cut diamond with plenty of light passing through it is brilliant to look at. A GelinAbaci tension setting is the perfect host for a skillfully cut diamond with dozens of facets.
       </p>
       <p>
        Stunning brilliance is the best way to describe a GelinAbaci diamond in a tension setting. Seeing one under the lights of our studio is a breathtaking experience, but even more so when our clients leave the showroom and step out into the bright, natural sunshine. There is nothing quite so brilliant anywhere else.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         2. Beautiful Elegance
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        As a premiere seller of diamond and custom jewellery in Toronto, we know that there is a fine line between taste and ostentation. GelinAbaci seems to walk that line effortlessly. Their diamond engagement rings and wedding bands are too beautiful to ignore, yet they offer a sophisticated elegance that leaves you inspired. Not every jeweller can pull this off as GelinAbaci does.
       </p>
       <p>
        One of the signs of beautiful elegance is a diamond ring that looks great regardless of what you are wearing. This can be tricky, depending on the metals and stones used to create a beautiful ring. Once again, though, GelinAbaci pulls it off effortlessly. Their pieces are suitable for every occasion and ensemble.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         3. Exquisite Quality
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        A couple investing in diamond engagement and wedding rings deserves the best quality money can buy. Engage Diamond Studio would not have it any other way. We are not much for selling poor quality pieces that will not withstand the test of time; rather, we want our clients to possess the kind of jewellery that will last as long as their love.
       </p>
       <p>
        GelinAbaci has made a name for themselves not only for the beauty of their diamond rings, but also for the quality. A GelinAbaci purchase is an investment our clients know they can trust for the long-term. If quality were not an integral part of their reputation, we would not carry GelinAbaci jewellery.
       </p>
       <p>
        There are other reasons to love GelinAbaci diamond rings and wedding bands. If you have yet to experience GelinAbaci for yourself, we invite you to visit our Toronto studio to learn more. We would be happy to welcome you and show you our entire collection of diamond engagement rings, wedding rings, anniversary jewellery, and custom designs.
       </p>
       <p>
        At Engage Diamond Studio, our number one priority is to make sure each client is both happy and satisfied with their purchases. We go the extra mile by providing attentive customer service, attention to detail and high-quality diamonds and settings. We hope you will consider a visit to our studio for your next diamond purchase.
       </p>
