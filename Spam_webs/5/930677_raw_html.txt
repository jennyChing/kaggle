          4 Atlanta Sweet Spots That Are Perfect For Valentines Day
         </h1>

        <div class="post-text-area text-area">
         <p>
          Whether you plan to spend your day with your significant other or in bed watching romantic comedies and pouting, sweets are an essential part of Valentines Day. Nothing says I love you like a gift of a red and pink dessert. And nothing says Im single like eating the same red and pink dessert at home with a box of tissues.
         </p>
         <p>
          With the limited time and energy we have as college students, picking up a treat is much easierthan baking one ourselves. Check out these four Atlanta dessert spots that all offer a special way for you to say I love you (or drown your single sorrows) this February 14
          <sup>
           th
          </sup>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cacaoatlanta.com" target="_blank" title="cacao">
           <strong>
            Cacao Atlanta Chocolate Company
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1316" style="width: 680px">
          <a href="http://emory.spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/26/2015/02/DSC_0170.jpg">
           <img alt="Photo by Madison Mounty" class="lazy" data-original="http://emory.spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/26/2015/02/DSC_0170.jpg" height="445" src="http://spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/themes/spoon/images/loading.svg" width="670"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Photo by Madison Mounty
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Head over to the Virginia Highlands to pick up special Champagne ganache chocolates covered in shaved raspberries. Even though it is not baked only for Valentines Day, try out the chocolate cake, too. I promise it is worth the calories.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1312" style="width: 680px">
          <a href="http://emory.spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/26/2015/01/DSC_0177.jpg">
           <img alt="Photo by Madison Mounty" class="lazy" data-original="http://emory.spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/26/2015/01/DSC_0177.jpg" height="445" src="http://spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/themes/spoon/images/loading.svg" width="670"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Photo by Madison Mounty
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.godiva.com" target="_blank" title="godiva">
           <strong>
            Godiva
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          You cant go wrong gifting Belgian chocolate. So head to Buckhead and pick up a heart-shaped box filled with truffles. If truffles arent your thing, still try out Godiva because there is bound to be something for you amongall of their delicious offerings.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.shipacookie.com" target="_blank" title="alis cookies">
           <strong>
            Alis Cookies
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          If you dont have a car, or just dont want to travel far, this is the perfect spot for you to pick up a Valentines Day dessert. Order a Valentines Day-themed cookie cake and have it personalized for your special someone.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.sprinkles.com" target="_blank" title="sprinkles">
           <strong>
            Sprinkles
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1315" style="width: 680px">
          <a href="http://emory.spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/26/2015/02/DSC_0149.jpg">
           <img alt="Photo by Madison Mounty" class="lazy" data-original="http://emory.spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/26/2015/02/DSC_0149.jpg" height="446" src="http://spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/themes/spoon/images/loading.svg" width="670"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Photo by Madison Mounty
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          For those of you who love cupcakes as much as I do, order in advance and head over to Sprinkles in Buckhead to pick up Valentines Day-themed cupcakes. With cute decorations like Be Mine and XOXO, these are sure to be a hit. Because they havevegan and gluten-free cupcake offerings, there will definitely be something that will workfor you.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_1307" style="width: 680px">
          <a href="http://emory.spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/26/2015/01/DSC_0140.jpg">
           <img alt="Photo by Madison Mounty" class="lazy" data-original="http://emory.spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/26/2015/01/DSC_0140.jpg" height="445" src="http://spoonuniversity.com/wp-content/themes/spoon/images/loading.svg" width="670"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Photo by Madison Mounty
          </p>
