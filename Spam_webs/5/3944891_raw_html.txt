          <h2>
           A winters tale in the night garden.
          </h2>
          <p>
           This is for all children everywhere, no matter how young or old.
          </p>
          <p>
           It is a winters evening, clear blue skies covered with stars, and our adventure takes place in the Lacinatob Park of Snedrag hidden deep in the ancient city of Eidynburgo; many, many years ago this was the land of the Gododdins sometimes called by ancient Romans, the Votadini.
          </p>
          <p>
           Over time, and many blue moons, Eidynburgo became overrun with modern ways and modopeeps, as the Gododdins call the modern people. And so in the fullness of time the Gododdins sought refuge with the trees and bushes, the plants and flowers in the Lacintabob park. The only time they are ever seen now, if you are lucky, is in late autumn, after dark, when the park, in modern parlance The Royal Edinburgh Botanical Gardens, is lit with a vast array of modern lighting. At this time, as an early winter sharpness cuts the evening air, and the fullest of moons kisses the horizon, the modopeeps come out in crowds to marvel at the sights and
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk" target="_blank" title="Quality Designer Lighting">
            shapes and colours created by the lighting
           </a>
           and let their imaginations and dreams be brought to life. It is a time for their eyes to sparkle and their senses to be awakened.
          </p>
          <p>
           However, what the adult modopeeps sometimes dont realise is that in this modern world, awash with information and technology, and multi-media designer lighting, there is a growing scarcity of creativity, imagination and wisdom. Some believe that having access to more information and gadgets produces more knowledge, which should then fire their imagination, and result in more wisdom. Knowledge is power they have been told! Many scoff at the idea that we can also be inspired by the power of shades of light and dark, the shapes and colours of the natural world, even if highlighted by modern lighting features. Just because they cannot put this most enriching of experiences into words, some tend to dismiss this as for young people only.
          </p>
          <p>
           But modochilders know otherwise; for theirs is the world of imaginarium, of discovering lifes deeper mysteries. They know that when you are really quiet, you can hear the Gododdins and the trees talk to you and equally, if you suspend disbelief, and are prepared maybe to feel a little bit silly, you can talk to them.
          </p>
          <p>
           On this spellbinding night, full of wonder and expectation, a night of lighting spectacular, the young Nairod, for that is the name he knew was given by his Gododdin ancestors, came to Snedrag with his parents Ilime and Zsuirad. And eager to witness the magic of the night garden they set to; to follow the path of Lacinatob park. As he entered the gateway, there before him, stood a tall hedge of leylandii trees. Lit as if by lime lights on the closed curtains of an old musical hall theatre they seemed to say, Prepare, the show is about to begin. Can you even begin to imagine what lies behind this curtain of green? Welcome Master Nairod.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-001.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Modelight Blog Night In The Garden Botanic Lights Edinburgh 2014 Photo 1" class="alignleft wp-image-1673" height="407" src="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-001.jpg" width="610"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/a-winters-tale-in-the-night-garden/&amp;media=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-001.jpg&amp;description=Botanic Lights  Night In The Garden  Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Nairod was captivated; he had heard no human voices, and yet the trees seemed to speak. Ilime and Zsuirad seemed not to have heard, nor did their friend Werdna. They instead seemed to admire the beauty of these tall, stately trees and were keen to see what awaited them. But Noraid felt sure, and he knew not why, that if he lingered a moment, was calm and patient, quietened his mind, he would find the secret of the trees.
          </p>
          <p>
           They wandered for a few moments through different groups of trees, gently lit from below, sometimes from above, and other times from within as if revealing the inner heart and truth of each tree. Some trees seemed more awake than others; some felt warm and friendly, others remained aloof and on duty. Perhaps, thought Nairod, Those are the protectors of the Gododdins. We must save the gododdins. Whatever, in that moment he knew, just knew, that each tree, each plantand every living thinghas a vibration, and as such, a personality. He knew that to communicate with this he had to do so with his whole body, not just his mind. And in this moment too he realised that when adult modopeeps live only live by their minds, they lose so much.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Suddenly it was as if the green tree curtain had been raised, and there before all the modopeeps the scene was set, with even more tall trees, either side of the stage, and a huge driveway leading up to the most enchanted backdrop of a magical glass hall of ever changing colours Noraid had ever seen. How did it do that? he wondered.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-002.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Modelight Blog Night In The Garden Botanic Lights Edinburgh 2014 Photo 2" class="alignleft wp-image-1675" height="407" src="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-002.jpg" width="610"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/a-winters-tale-in-the-night-garden/&amp;media=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-002.jpg&amp;description=Botanic Lights  Night In The Garden  Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            It felt as if any moment, the doors would open and the Gododdins would appear in splendid community dance.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-003.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Modelight Blog Night In The Garden Botanic Lights Edinburgh 2014 Photo 3" class="alignleft wp-image-1693" height="407" src="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-003.jpg" width="610"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/a-winters-tale-in-the-night-garden/&amp;media=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-003.jpg&amp;description=Botanic Lights  Night In The Garden  Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           The young Nairod, like many others around, gasped in awe and he felt moved to rub some fresh needles of a small pine tree between his fingers. He momentarily held a sharp intake of breath, and slowly inhaled the precious fragrance. The scent felt cleansing but more than that, the cluster of pine trees seemed to offer peace and protection. This was a good place to be.
          </p>
          <p>
           As he looked ahead, new kinds of trees appeared, lit by ever changing lighting features.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-004.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Modelight Blog Night In The Garden Botanic Lights Edinburgh 2014 Photo 4" class="alignleft wp-image-1681" height="407" src="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-004.jpg" width="610"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/a-winters-tale-in-the-night-garden/&amp;media=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-004.jpg&amp;description=Botanic Lights  Night In The Garden  Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           But what was that? Were those Gododdins Nairod was witnessing?
          </p>
          <p>
           I want to run ahead papa, he said. please may I?
          </p>
          <p>
           But before Zsuirad or Llime could answer, Noraid was heeding an inner call from the trees, a blue one in particular. Mysteriously he let himself be drawn toward the tree and, slowly, moving ever respectfully closer toward it, for Noraid had a profound sensitivity, as all adults do when they are his age, he took in every part of it, from its majestic grand roots to its fanning branches and leaves, to its top.
          </p>
          <p>
           Feeling still the protection of the pines, Nairod was inspired to move closer, then draw back a little, it felt like a good game, and then move even closer. Over and over again.
          </p>
          <p>
           What are you doing? cried Ilime.
          </p>
          <p>
           Im talking with the trees mama!
          </p>
          <p>
           And what are they saying to you? she laughed slightly.
          </p>
          <p>
           I really dont know mama. I just know I feel something going on, some trees here make me feel bigger, stronger, softer, sweeter  I dont know; they are not using words. We are playing games. They are making me feel, feel so many things. Its good Mama.
          </p>
          <p>
           He held up his hands, palms towards the trees, and as he moved them inwards and outwards, he could feel the trees make him even stronger then gentler, then stronger again.
          </p>
          <p>
           In the way that young people do, Nairod was understanding so much, but somehow without words. He had an inner knowing that trees, like every other being on the planet, like he did, liked to be loved and noticed. He knew the modopeeps, just like trees, get nourishment too from being noticed, respected, and loved.
          </p>
          <p>
           And such a feeling without words took Noraid into his imagination, just at the point where he rounded a corner on the path and came face to face with a large lake.
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk/led-lights,001.html" target="_blank" title="Designer LED Lights">
            A whole other world of light
           </a>
           , floor level lighting, wall lights (albeit the trees were the walls) the hanging lights of the stars. Was he seeing things? Not real things but in his minds eye, things of another world? As he gazed upon the scene in front, was that really a crystal cave he saw? And was that an old Gododdin seated at the opposite end of the lake, cross-legged round a burning fire? A swan? A spirit?
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-005.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Modelight Blog Night In The Garden Botanic Lights Edinburgh 2014 Photo 5" class="alignleft wp-image-1682" height="407" src="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-005.jpg" width="610"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/a-winters-tale-in-the-night-garden/&amp;media=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-005.jpg&amp;description=Botanic Lights  Night In The Garden  Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           As quickly as the magical scene had appeared, it changed again, right before Nairods eyes and with it, his imagination brought other creations to his mind.
          </p>
          <p>
           The trees round the lake were friendly and protective but nonetheless Noraid turned round, to ensure that Ilime and Zsuirad were not too far behind. They too were lost in their own world.
          </p>
          <p>
           They were all now deep in the tree zone and something was nudging them to use the occasion to really disconnect from the world of technology and multi-media, and to tap into natures wisdom.
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            The Gododdins were nowhere to be seen but were definitely now here, their presence being felt, being of light. And through their own unique designer form of lighting, they were transmitting lighting beams of information, wisdom questions fundamental to life  What is life about? Why are you here? What is your purpose?
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            The same questions were received by each person in different ways and answers found also in a myriad ways  some as words, a whisper on the breeze, a gentle but certain knowingness. Adultpeeps had to re-learn their gift of being receptive and lacking expectation The kinderpeeps just knew it was enough to enjoy and feel the moment; even if nothing comes, they were still outside in nature, in the embrace of a tree, drawn to the light, witnessing in their own way the natural world of the Gododdins.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-006.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Modelight Blog Night In The Garden Botanic Lights Edinburgh 2014 Photo 6" class="alignleft wp-image-1684" height="407" src="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-006.jpg" width="610"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/a-winters-tale-in-the-night-garden/&amp;media=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-006.jpg&amp;description=Botanic Lights  Night In The Garden  Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           The lights changed again, from deep mysterious blues and azure tints, to autumnal reds and russets the trees expressing their full grandeur and beauty and striking poses of the wisdom of their years.
          </p>
          <p>
           Beams of white light danced on the lake, bouncing off it and upwards to the skies. Then, when least expected, every now and then the spotlights would settle for a few moments in one place and if you focussed and waited intently, little jets of water would splash up and ripple out. Nairod was more intrigued by now. What were they? Were they mysterious creatures of the deep, simple friendly fish, jets of water, or even kelpies, the water spirits in the form of a horse, come to forewarn of danger of drowning if he went too close to the lake. Perhaps it could be all of these things. This enchanted world is full of possibilities; full of what you imagine it to be, a canvass waiting for you to paint.
          </p>
          <p>
           The family climbed a small hill together; Nairod kept close to the adultpeeps when the lighting seemed lacking.
          </p>
          <p>
           But it was like the darkness before dawn for after a short climb, they emerged into a vast open space, a chalice to hold the awesomeness, and breathe in freedom.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-007.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Modelight Blog Night In The Garden Botanic Lights Edinburgh 2014 Photo 7" class="alignleft wp-image-1685" height="407" src="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-007.jpg" width="610"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/a-winters-tale-in-the-night-garden/&amp;media=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-007.jpg&amp;description=Botanic Lights  Night In The Garden  Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           And before them, an enchanting view of the castle of Eidyn.
          </p>
          <p>
           Further on from this amazing scene, was an equally amazing and intriguing view of some special trees. Nairod was curious. What were these trees saying to him?
          </p>
          <p>
           He was enthralled, lost in his own little world when an elderly lady with a haggard but wise countenance, tapped him lightly on the shoulder.
          </p>
          <p>
           I see you are drawn to this tree. Why not send it warm loving energy from your heart and ask it to allow you to draw closer and spend time with it.
          </p>
          <p>
           Nairod was hesitant. Suddenly he had been brought back to the world of language and adults. He was also unsure if she were real. Perhaps she was an elder Gododdin.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-008.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Modelight Blog Night In The Garden Botanic Lights Edinburgh 2014 Photo 8" class="alignleft wp-image-1686" height="407" src="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-008.jpg" width="610"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/a-winters-tale-in-the-night-garden/&amp;media=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-008.jpg&amp;description=Botanic Lights  Night In The Garden  Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Go on. If your request is granted, walk closer to the tree and circle it slowly in a sunwise direction.
          </p>
          <p>
           What is sunwise? asked Nairod, looking more than a little afraid but equally still sensing the protection of the trees.
          </p>
          <p>
           Sunwise? In the folklore of my people, the Votadini, Sunwise or Sunward means clockwise and is considered the most prosperous direction turning from east to west in the direction of the sun. So put both your hands and your body against the trunk. Notice how the tree looks now you are close up, how it smells and how it feels against your skin.
          </p>
          <p>
           Noraid moved toward the tree and surrendered to its embrace.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-009.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Modelight Blog Night In The Garden Botanic Lights Edinburgh 2014 Photo 9" class="alignleft wp-image-1687" height="407" src="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-009.jpg" width="610"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/a-winters-tale-in-the-night-garden/&amp;media=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-009.jpg&amp;description=Botanic Lights  Night In The Garden  Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Open yourself to the power of the tree, and let it take you into a deep state of peace. All you must do is stay relaxed and present. Let the tree calm your thoughts and gently cleanse your mind of all the agitation you know you feel in this modern world. Anytime you are upset, find yourself in nature, find a tree and talk with it. Tell it whats on your mind; ask questions about it, about yourself, and also how you can resolve any of lifes challenges. Stay with the tree, sit in its silence and wait for a response. After tonight, you know how the responses come.
          </p>
          <p>
           Nairod now sat with his tree and when he came back from imagination into the world of people he noticed that the wise old crone had disappeared. He slowly got up, thanked the tree, and moved on. Mama and Papa were still behind. For them, time had not stood still.
          </p>
          <p>
           Round the next corner there appeared the most amazing site, a field of glittering, tall blue poppies made of CDs, and far off in the distance the now risen full moon.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-010.jpg">
            <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
             <img alt="Modelight Blog Night In The Garden Botanic Lights Edinburgh 2014 Photo 10" class="alignleft wp-image-1688" height="407" src="http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-010.jpg" width="610"/>
             <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/a-winters-tale-in-the-night-garden/&amp;media=http://www.modelight.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/night-in-the-garden-botanic-lights-edinburgh-modelight-010.jpg&amp;description=Botanic Lights  Night In The Garden  Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh')">
             </span>
            </span>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Nairod was filled with gratitude for a world of once upon a time, a world of now, a world of possibility. He knew it was time, just knew.
          </p>
          <p>
           He readied himself and placed his hands on the nearest tree.
          </p>
          <p>
           From my heart to yours, to all the people of the dark who are really of the Light, to all trees everywhere, to the light that shows them at their best, I give thanks.
           <em>
            May you always thrive and be blessed so that you may be healthy and happy and inspire all beings to be healthy and at peace.
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           You love to be thanked. Especially when youve shared your heart and soul with someone in deep connection.
          </p>
          <p>
           We hope this has inspired you to begin on the journey to discovering some of lifes deeper mysteries. It is quite the adventure, as you will soon find out and the inspiration can start at the Royal Botanical Gardens in Edinburgh during their Botanic Lights  Night In The Garden event from October 30th to November 23rd 2014.
          </p>
          <p>
           http://www.rbge.org.uk
          </p>
