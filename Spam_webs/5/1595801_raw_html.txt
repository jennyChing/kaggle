  <p>
   <b>
    Welcome to my site!
   </b>
   <br/>
   I'm Scorch, and I use pyrography and other craft techiques to decorate various wooden, leather and other items as a hobby, creating unique hand-crafted items, ideal as personalised gifts. I am happy to take commissions to make items just as you'd like them, with a design of your choice - it keeps me in blanks for my own amusement!
   <br/>
   Pyrography is the traditional art of burning a design onto wood. A picture in subtle sepia tones can be created, or a bold, dramatic design.. all by varying the heat of the iron, the time the iron is in contact with the wood, and the method of applying the tip.
Personalised, hand-pyrographed items like these make unique and lasting gifts for birthdays, weddings, christenings, Easter, Christmas, etc.
   <br/>
   All my recent work is in the
   <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/tannison/sets/72157606138937826/" target="_blank">
    Crafts
   </a>
   section of my
   <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/tannison" target="_blank">
    Flickr page
   </a>
   , though - it's much more up to date than this site is - you'll find all the new items there.
   <br/>
  </p>

   <b>
    Like what you see?
   </b>
   <br/>
   If you would like to purchase an item shown here, or to request a similar item, or to commission something from me, just
   <a href="mailto:TAnnison@scorchpyro.co.uk?subject=Pyrography%20Commission%20enquiry">
    contact me
   </a>
   with details of what you'd like - it couldn't be easier!
   <br/>
   I will happily make whatever you like to your specifications, as long as you have an idea of what you want (so I can make/find a design I can execute), it's within the bounds of reason, and I have or can obtain the materials required! Don't worry if you don't know quite what you want yet - just contact me, and we'll come up with something together. After all, I'm not running a business here - I just do this for the fun of it, as a hobby in my spare time, so it's all good by me!
