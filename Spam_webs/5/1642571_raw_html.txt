       <h1 class="a-blog-post-title">
        <a href="http://www.modenus.com/blog/blogtour/blogtourlondon/tent-london-mineheart" rel="bookmark">
         Tent London: Boundless creativity from Mineheart
        </a>
       </h1>
       <h5 class="a-blog-post-byline">
        By
        <a href="http://www.modenus.com/blog/author/florence-von-pelet" rel="author" title="View all posts by Florence von Pelet">
         Florence von Pelet
        </a>
       </h5>
      </header>
      <div class="r-blog-text">
       <p>
        Exposed light bulbs are undoubtedly the new craze, however, Mineheart takes the lighting trend to a whole new level with exposed chandelier lighting, a must see for upcoming Blogtour at Tent London.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://www.modenus.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Mineheart-King-Edison-Pendant-purple.jpg">
         <img alt="Mineheart King Edison Pendant" class="aligncenter wp-image-175776" height="850" src="http://www.modenus.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Mineheart-King-Edison-Pendant-purple.jpg" title="Mineheart King Edison Pendant purple" width="600"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        The King Edison Pendant lamp designed by Young &amp; Battaglia blends the designs and simplicity of the Edison light bulb and an extravagant chandelier. The collection is intended to hang in clusters for the ultimate effect. The miniature brass chandelier hangs inside of a clear hand blown glass shade fitted with satin chrome. Seriously fun design.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <a href="http://www.modenus.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Mineheart-Lady-Peacock-Cushions.jpg">
         <img alt="Mineheart Lady Peacock Cushions" class="aligncenter wp-image-175777" height="426" src="http://www.modenus.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Mineheart-Lady-Peacock-Cushions-1024x682.jpg" title="Mineheart Lady Peacock Cushions" width="600"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
        Boundless in their creativity, Mineheart also design accessories including fabulous pillows. We love the versatility of pillows and their ability to spruce up a couch or chair like the one above. The Classics revisited collection by American artist Chad Wys is creative and classic with a modern twist. The portrait images are printed on cotton satin and finished with black cotton and feather filled pads. Mineheart describes the collection as, strangely reminiscent of Great Expectations, Dorian Grey, Cluedo and Wuthering Heights. What a unique way to depict artful masterpieces.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: left;">
       </p>
