          <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
           Christmas Crafts with Yellow Moon 2014
          </h1>
          <p class="entry-meta">
           <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-12-18T12:11:02+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
            December 18, 2014
           </time>
          </p>
         </header>
         <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
          <div style="width: 600px; margin: 0 auto;">
           <div style="position: relative;">
            <a href="http://www.polyvore.com/christmas_craft_ideas_with_yellow/set?.embedder=12487272&amp;.svc=copypaste&amp;id=143517055" target="_blank">
             <img alt="Christmas Craft Ideas with Yellow Moon 2014" border="0" height="498" src="http://cfc.polyvoreimg.com/cgi/img-set/.sig/P87WlLQ5EDiyG0irQTgDxQ/cid/143517055/id/ODhQdKmG5BGsLhy4myklpg/size/c600x498.jpg" title="Christmas Craft Ideas with Yellow Moon 2014" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </div>
           <div style="position: relative;">
           </div>
          </div>
          <p>
           I think I may have mentioned a few times now on this blog how much I love
           <a href="http://www.yellowmoon.org.uk" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Yellow Moon">
            Yellow Moon
           </a>
           . Whatever craft activity we want to do, whatever we want to make, Yellow Moon have something that we can use and always at great value too. I had already ordered a few bits and pieces from them for some craft projects that I had in mind and then we received a box of festive goodies to try out.
          </p>
          <p>
           Due to the ridiculous amount of birthdays in December, we have been rather busy and so we havent yet had time to try allof the products out but when the children break up on Friday, they will be ideal last minute craft activities to keep them all occupied withnext week.
          </p>
          <p>
           My favourite from the box was the Santas Workshop Kits, made up of foam house templates with adhesive stickers to decorate, these kits are fun and easy to make and look really effective. A and I sat and built one to put on the table over the festive season and I think it looks great. It is also quite good for hiding special treats inside too.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://stressymummy.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Santas-Workshop-Kits.jpg">
            <img alt="Santa's Workshop Kits" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4685" height="439" src="http://stressymummy.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Santas-Workshop-Kits.jpg" width="648"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           The Wooden House boxes are really clever and with a lid that slides open, you can decorate them however you want and these would make perfect presents to send to friends or family.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://stressymummy.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/wooden-house-boxes-.jpg">
            <img alt="wooden house boxes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4688" height="493" src="http://stressymummy.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/wooden-house-boxes-.jpg" width="648"/>
           </a>
           Not only are there lots of craft materials, kits and ideas, but Yellow Moon is also a brilliant site for stocking fillers, small gifts and party bag fillers. All of the children loved the finger lights which are four different coloured lights that you strap to your fingers.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://stressymummy.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Finger-lights-.jpg">
            <img alt="Finger lights" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4689" height="648" src="http://stressymummy.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Finger-lights-.jpg" width="493"/>
           </a>
           The first thing that A noticed when she opened the box were the snow pal mugs which are three small plastic mugs, each with a snowy animal on the front. No prizes for guessing which colour mug A has picked to use
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://stressymummy.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/christmas-cups-.jpg">
            <img alt="christmas cups" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-4690" height="493" src="http://stressymummy.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/christmas-cups-.jpg" width="648"/>
           </a>
           For more information or ideas, take a look at the Yellow Moon
           <a href="http://www.yellowmoon.org.uk" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="Yellow Moon">
            website
           </a>
           .
          </p>
