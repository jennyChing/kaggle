        <p>
         I love cooking. I think Im the only one in my family who enjoys cooking a nice meal whether that will be for breakfast, lunch or dinner. Im always ready to do some experiments with the food.
        </p>
        <p>
         I know there are many people out there who share the same passion about cooking and food with me and thats why Im ready to share few simple cooking hacks that will make your cooking easier and tastier.
        </p>
        <p>
         Your family and friends will be surprised of how fast you made that diner or of the taste of the lunch.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im not going to share my magic that I use for cooking, but these simple hacks will do the trick. Just kidding, there is no magic only love for what you do.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you love to cook, than these simple hacks are just for you.
        </p>
        <p>
         We are looking forward hearing from you if you have some hacks that will help us cook faster and tastier. Share those tricks in the comments section below.
        </p>
        <p>
         Before you do that, here is what I found:
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         1.Ice holds down the fruit perfectly
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="imageedit_1_6257278151" class="aligncenter wp-image-75778" data-wp-pid="75778" height="356" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_1_6257278151.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         2.Tin foil hat will protect your holiday turkey from overcooking
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75779" data-wp-pid="75779" height="327" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_4_2479008182.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         3.Steak drying has never been easier using pan tins, paper towel and skewers
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75780" data-wp-pid="75780" height="371" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_7_5471808802.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         4.Taco seasonings
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="Taco seasonings" class="aligncenter wp-image-75781" data-wp-pid="75781" height="366" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_9_7102731861.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         5.Cut a squash like a pro tapping butchers knife with a mallet
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75782" data-wp-pid="75782" height="367" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_11_7166577257.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         6.Use your coffee grinder for blending spices
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75783" data-wp-pid="75783" height="370" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_16_4175373040.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         7.This tin foil pan will protect the asparagus from burning
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75784" data-wp-pid="75784" height="367" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_18_9764117445.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         8.Pitting olives for tapenade has never been easier
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75785" data-wp-pid="75785" height="367" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_20_6044208899.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         9.Make a shift terrine pan using 2 bread tins
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75786" data-wp-pid="75786" height="366" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_22_3931631427.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         10.Make a bamboo steamer using pie tins
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75787" data-wp-pid="75787" height="366" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_24_7934612195.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         11.steamer is perfect for roasting peppers
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75788" data-wp-pid="75788" height="366" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_26_8981446593.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         12.Panini press for homemade flat bread
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75789" data-wp-pid="75789" height="366" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_28_7654010757.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         13.Or roasted chicken
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75791" data-wp-pid="75791" height="366" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_32_3814618375.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         14.The waffle iron will become your perfect bacon maker
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75790" data-wp-pid="75790" height="366" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_30_7334165252.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         15.Use a potato masher for dicing the avocados if you want to make a guacamole
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75792" data-wp-pid="75792" height="366" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_1_3875952541.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <h4 style="text-align: center;">
         16.Turn the grill into smoker by filling up a tin foil with wood-chips
        </h4>
        <p>
         <img alt="imageedit_10_3151333730" class="aligncenter wp-image-75794" data-wp-pid="75794" height="365" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_10_3151333730.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-75793" data-wp-pid="75793" height="367" src="//www.explosion.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/imageedit_6_3581806413.gif" width="550"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         See?  It wasnt that hard.
