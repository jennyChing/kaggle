       8 Craft Beers to Celebrate the Super Bowl
      </a>
     </h1>
     <div class="post-meta">
      January 29, 2014
      <span class="separator">
       
      </span>
      By
      <a href="http://www.celebritychef.tv/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by admin">

      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        <!-- BEGIN THEIA POST SLIDER -->
       </p>
       <p>
        There are a few fool-proof dynamic duos in this world. A nice glass of wine with dinner, a bag of popcorn for a movie and an ice-cold beer for the Super Bowl. This weekend, we recommend you go all-out on that last one. It is the Super Bowl, after all. Dont know where to start? We have compiled quite the list of quality craft beers available in your own hometown. Weve got the best from the Super Bowl cities, Denver and Seattle, as well as a variety of Ales, Stouts and Lagers.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Super Bowl Bruery Humulus Lager" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6240" height="360" src="http://www.celebritychef.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Super-Bowl-Bruery-Humulus-Lager.jpg" width="540"/>
       </p>
       <div class="cunlock_main_content">
       </div>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         The Humulus Lager
        </strong>
        is an aromatic hops beer with 7.4 percent ABV from
        <strong>
         The Bruery
        </strong>
        in Placentia, CA.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="Super Bowl-Left hand milk stout" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6242" height="360" src="http://www.celebritychef.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Super-Bowl-Left-hand-milk-stout.jpg" width="540"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        From the
        <strong>
         Left Hand Brewing Company
        </strong>
        in Denver, CO comes the
        <strong>
         Left Hand Milk Stout
        </strong>
        . A sweet but dark beer with an ABV of 6 percent.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="Super Bowl Elysian Immortal Ipa" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6243" height="360" src="http://www.celebritychef.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Super-Bowl-Elysian-Immortal-Ipa.jpg" width="540"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         The Immortal IPA
        </strong>
        is a sort of thirst quencher, with a citrusy spin on the traditional hops taste. Coming from the
        <strong>
         Elysian Brewing
        </strong>
        in Seattle, WA, this beer rings in at 6.21 percent ABV.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="Super Bowl Expresso Oak-aged Yeti" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6247" height="360" src="http://www.celebritychef.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Super-Bowl-Expresso-Oak-aged-Yeti.jpg" width="540"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        Enjoy the savory coffee and chocolate taste of the
        <strong>
         Espresso Oak Aged Yeti
        </strong>
        from the
        <strong>
         Great Divide Brewing Company
        </strong>
        in Denver, CO. With an ABV count of 9.5 percent, this delicious dark beer is sure to satisfy your taste buds.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="Super Bowl Redhook Audible Ale" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6248" height="400" src="http://www.celebritychef.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Super-Bowl-Redhook-Audible-Ale.jpg" width="400"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         The Redhook Brewery
        </strong>
        in Seattle, WA paves the way to the big game with
        <strong>
         Audible Ale
        </strong>
        ; 4.7 percent ABV.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="sb breckinridge vanilla porter" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6250" height="602" src="http://www.celebritychef.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/sb-breckinridge-vanilla-porter.jpg" width="400"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        The
        <strong>
         Remarkable Vanilla Porter
        </strong>
        from
        <strong>
         Breckenridge Brewery
        </strong>
        will warm you up with flavors of vanilla and cocoa; 4.7 percent ABV.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="Flying Fish Brewery" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6283" height="360" src="http://www.celebritychef.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Flying-Fish-Brewery.jpg" width="540"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         The Flying Fish Brewery
        </strong>
        in New Jersey brings us the
        <strong>
         HopFish IPA
        </strong>
        with an ABV count of 6.5 percent.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        <img alt="Super Bowl Fremont Universale Pale Ale 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6284" height="360" src="http://www.celebritychef.tv/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Super-Bowl-Fremont-Universale-Pale-Ale-2.jpg" width="540"/>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: center;">
        The
        <strong>
         Universal Pale Ale
        </strong>
        from
        <strong>
         Fremont Brewery
        </strong>
        is definitely a delight. With an ABV of 5.6 percent and savory toasted malts, this will be the first beer to run out at your party.
       </p>
       <p>
