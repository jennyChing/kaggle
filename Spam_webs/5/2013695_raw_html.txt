    <h3>
     Featured Lessons
    </h3>
    <hr/>
    <p>
     Learning how to draw what you see is very difficult to do especially for beginners. The learning process requires a lot of patience. Even the most advanced artists will tell you that it took years of practice before they were able to draw photos or subjects the way they actually appear in real life. That is why I teach my students several techniques instead of just the ones that I prefer.
    </p>
    <p>
     I divided the lessons into three separate groups: beginner, intermediate, and advanced. Under each group the lessons have been divided again according to the technique that is used.
    </p>
