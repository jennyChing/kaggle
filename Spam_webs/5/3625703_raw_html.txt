                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  Write quickly. Give yourself less than 60 seconds a
question. Preferably less than 30 seconds.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  Be honest. Nobody will read it. Its important to write
without editing.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  Enjoy the moment and smile as you write.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <b>
                  <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                   <br/>
                  </span>
                 </b>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <b>
                  <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                   15
Questions:
                   <o:p>
                   </o:p>
                  </span>
                 </b>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  1. What makes you smile? (Activities, people, events,
hobbies, projects, etc.)
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  2. What are your favorite things to do in the past? What
about now?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  3. What activities make you lose track of time?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  4. What makes you feel great about yourself?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  5. Who inspires you most? (Anyone you know or do not know.
Family, friends, authors, artists, leaders, etc.) Which qualities inspire you,
in each person?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  6. What are you naturally good at? (Skills, abilities, gifts
etc.)
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  7. What do people typically ask you for help in?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  8. If you had to teach something, what would you teach?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  9. What would you regret not fully doing, being or having in
your life?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  10. You are now 90 years old, sitting on a rocking chair
outside your porch; you can feel the spring breeze gently brushing against your
face. You are blissful and happy, and are pleased with the wonderful life
youve been blessed with. Looking back at your life and all that youve
achieved and acquired, all the relationships youve developed; what matters to
you most? List them out.
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                  11. What are your deepest values?
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  Select 3 to 6(
                 </span>
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%; mso-bidi-font-family: Arial;'>
                  Achievement,
Adventure, Beauty, Being the best, Challenge, Comfort, Courage, Creativity, Curiosity,
Education, Health, Honesty, Independence, Inner peace, Performance, Personal
growth, Play, Productivity, Relationship)
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  and prioritize the
words in order of importance to you.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  12. What were some challenges, difficulties and hardships
youve overcome or are in the process of overcoming? How did you do it?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  13. What causes do you strongly believe in? Connect with?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  14. If you could get a message across to a large group of
people. Who would those people be? What would your message be?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  15. Given your talents, passions and values. How could you
use these resources to serve, to help, to contribute? ( to people, beings,
causes, organization, environment, planet, etc.)
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <b>
                  <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                   <br/>
                  </span>
                 </b>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <b>
                  <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                   Your
Personal Mission Statement
                   <o:p>
                   </o:p>
                  </span>
                 </b>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  Writing or reviewing a mission statement changes you
because it forces you to think through your priorities deeply, carefully, and
to align your behaviour with your beliefs
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <b>
                  <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                   ~Stephen
Covey
                   <o:p>
                   </o:p>
                  </span>
                 </b>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  A personal mission consists of 3 parts:
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                </div>
                <ul style="text-align: left;">
                 <li>
                  <span style="font-family: Georgia, serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                   What do I want to do?
                  </span>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <span style="font-family: Georgia, serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                   Who do I want to help?
                  </span>
                 </li>
                 <li>
                  <span style="font-family: Georgia, serif; font-size: 12pt; line-height: 115%;">
                   What is the result? What value will I create?
                  </span>
                 </li>
                </ul>
                <br/>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  Steps to Creating Your Personal Mission Statement:
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  1. Do the exercise with the 15 questions above as quickly as
you can.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  2. List out actions words you connect with.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <b>
                  <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                   a.
Example:
                  </span>
                 </b>
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  educate, accomplish, empower, encourage, improve, help,
give, guide, inspire, integrate, master, motivate, nurture, organize, produce,
promote, travel, spread, share, satisfy, understand, teach, write, etc.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  3. Based on your answers to the 15 questions. List
everything and everyone that you believe you can help.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <b>
                  <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                   a.
Example:
                  </span>
                 </b>
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  People, creatures, organizations, causes, groups,
environment, etc.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  4. Identify your end goal. How will the who from your
above answer benefit from what you do?
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  5. Combine steps 2-4 into a sentence, or 2-3 sentences.
                  <o:p>
                  </o:p>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <br/>
                <div class="MsoNormal">
                 <b>
                  <span style='font-family: "Georgia","serif"; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 115%;'>
                   What is
your purpose? What is your mission? Wed love to hear about your goals and
aspirations. Please share with a comment!
                   <o:p>
                   </o:p>
                  </span>
                 </b>
                </div>
               </div>
