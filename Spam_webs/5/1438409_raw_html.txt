         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.pandpkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/IMG_8408.jpg">
           <img alt="Macadamia Nut Caramel Bars Easy and to Die For Bars! www.pandpkitchen.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1954 img-responsive" height="900" src="http://www.pandpkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/IMG_8408.jpg" title="Macadamia Nut Caramel Bars Easy and to Die For Bars! www.pandpkitchen.com" width="600"/>

         <p>
          Words cannot describe how wonderful these Macadamia Nut Caramel Bars are. These just jumped off the plate and into my mouth! I had no control over it. It was like I was possessed and I just could not stop eating these! These whip together lickty split and are about as easy as 1-2-3!  All you need is a box mix of yellow cake, caramel ice cream topping and refrigerated White Chocolate Macadamia Nut ready to bake cookies and presto you have decadence on a plate!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.pandpkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/IMG_8412.jpg">
           <img alt="Macadamia Nut Caramel Bars Easy and to Die For Bars! www.pandpkitchen.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1955 img-responsive" height="900" src="http://www.pandpkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/IMG_8412.jpg" title="Macadamia Nut Caramel Bars Easy and to Die For Bars! www.pandpkitchen.com" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Macadamia nuts really are a great summer treat. Thats not to say that you cannot enjoy them year round, but to me they really say summer. They arent Hawaiis largest import for nothing! I love the texture they almost have a buttery like feel. Bright white and full of flavor. You just cant pass these up! I just cant get over the sweet sticky caramel middle of these bars. They are so moist and chewy and really I cant say it enough.. To Die For Good!
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://www.pandpkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/IMG_8413.jpg">
           <img alt="Macadamia Nut Caramel Bars Easy and to Die For Bars! www.pandpkitchen.com" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1956 img-responsive" height="900" src="http://www.pandpkitchen.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/IMG_8413.jpg" title="Macadamia Nut Caramel Bars Easy and to Die For Bars! www.pandpkitchen.com" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          As you can see I could not contain myself as I was taking the photos. That big Ole Bite in the middle. yep you can take dental prints. Its Me! Look at that sweet oozing caramel. Yum-O! The only thing better about these bars and how they taste is how easy they are to make. Make a batch and everyone will love you to pieces! Not that they dont already, this just ensures it!
         </p>

            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
             Ingredients
            </p>
            <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 box of yellow cake mix
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 cup vegetable oil
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/4 cup water
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
              2 eggs
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
              1/2 cup caramel ice cream topping
             </li>
             <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
              1 package of ready to bake refrigerated white chocolate macadamia cookies
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
             Instructions
            </p>
            <ul class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              preheat oven to 350
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              in a mixing bowl combine the cake mix
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              the oil
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              the water
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              the eggs and mix till batter is smooth
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              pour into an ungreased 9x13 baking dish and bake 18 min
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              remove form oven
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              melt the  caramel sauce in the microwave for 15-25 seconds
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-8" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              drizzle over the cake
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-9" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              break up the cookies into little chunks and spread over the cake and caramel
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-10" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              bake an additional 20 min
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-11" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              let cool completely before cutting into bars
             </li>
             <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-12" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
              Enjoy
             </li>
            </ul>
