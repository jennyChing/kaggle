         <div class="text-holder">
          <h3>
           SELF ACTIVATING
          </h3>
          <p>
           Introducing the world's first self-activating SIM card. Just pop a Ready SIM in your device, text your ZIP code to
           <a href="#">
            7850
           </a>
           , and that's it. You'll receive a text message confirmation with your local phone number and expiry date of your plan.
          </p>
         </div>

        <div class="text-row" id="line-private">
         <img alt="" src="http://www.readysim.com/media/wysiwyg/img8.png"/>
         <div class="text-holder">
          <h3>
           Instant, Easy, Private
          </h3>
          <p>
           It's easy to get started with Ready SIM. All you need to get started is to buy a SIM and activate - that's it! No registration also means Ready SIM is private. Ideal if you are wary of sharing your primary phone number when posting to Craigslist, social media, dating sites, or conducting job searches.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="text-row odd" id="line-talk">
         <img alt="Unlimited Nationwide Talk" src="http://www.readysim.com/media/wysiwyg/img9.png"/>
         <div class="text-holder">
          <h3>
           Unlimited Nationwide Talk
          </h3>
          <p>
           Every Ready SIM includes unlimited nationwide USA calling, including Hawaii and Puerto Rico. No limits on inbound or outbound calls. Receive unlimited incoming calls from international numbers. No evening or weekend restrictions - just straight forward unlimited calling. How refreshing.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="text-row" id="line-sms">
         <img alt="Unlimited International SMS" src="http://www.readysim.com/media/wysiwyg/img10.png"/>
         <div class="text-holder">
          <h3>
           Unlimited International SMS
          </h3>
          <p>
           Ready SIM offers free unlimited international SMS messaging to any country around the world, while you're in the USA. That includes both incoming and outgoing messages.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="text-row odd" id="line-data">
         <div class="text-holder">
          <h3>
           Blazing Fast 4G LTE Data
          </h3>
          <p>
           4G LTE is a faster data network with greater capacity and significantly improved speeds. This allows for faster downloading, smoother streaming and a better overall experience. Our 4G LTE Network covers all major metro areas in the USA including New York, Los Angeles, Seattle, Las Vegas, Dallas, Houston, Miami, Detroit, and more.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="text-row" id="line-coverage">

          <h3>
           Nationwide Coverage
          </h3>
          <p>
           Ready SIM connects you to our nationwide 4G LTE network, which means that you'll enjoy blazing-fast Internet access and reliable coverage across the US, including Hawaii and Puerto Rico.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="#">
            Check our coverage map for more details.
           </a>
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="text-row odd" id="line-up">
         <div class="text-holder">
          <h3>
           Top Up
          </h3>
          <p>
           Ready SIM cards can be topped up with any one of our plans. You have 72 hours after your active plan expires to complete the top up before your Ready SIM is deactivated. To top up, locate a participating wireless top up terminal, enter your Ready SIM phone number and the plan you want to purchase. Your new plan will be active within 10 minutes.
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>

          </p>

           Data Tethering

          <p>

          </p>
