        <div class="markdown description col7" id="description">
         <div id="description-text">
          These tapas plates are individually handmade by me from textured terracotta clay.  Each plate starts off by rolling out a slab of clay, cutting the base shape and then attaching a coil of clay to build up the rim.  I then decorate with slip and apply a gorgeous floral or geometric pattern and fire in the kiln.  After two firings, I give each plate a coat of glaze and then fire again.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          In many of my ceramic pieces you will find slight imperfections and marks left by the handmade process. These contribute to the uniqueness and beauty of the forms and are simply part of the character of the individual piece.  Bases are unglazed.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Plates are irregular shapes and as they are completely hand made, may differ slightly from the image shown. All approx 2cm deep and measure approx at the widest point :
          <br/>
          Large plate 19cm  Medium plate 17cm  Small plate 12cm
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Please note that this item is made-to-order and delivery is currently 4-6 weeks.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          * Lead free glaze
          <br/>
          * Hand wash only
          <br/>
          * Not suitable for microwave
