         <p>
          <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="http://www.lazerhorse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/North-Korea-Futuristic-Architecture-Mount-Kumgang-region.jpg">
           <img alt="North Korea Futuristic Architecture - Mount Kumgang region" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12911" height="360" src="//www.lazerhorse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/North-Korea-Futuristic-Architecture-Mount-Kumgang-region.jpg" width="640"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          A company called Koryo Tours that organise trips to
          <a href="http://www.lazerhorse.org/2014/04/12/kijong-dong-north-koreas-deserted-propaganda-village/" target="_blank" title="Kijong-dong: North Koreas Deserted Propaganda Village">
           North Korea
          </a>
          have come up with a neat little viral ad campaign. They gave a project toan architect workinginthe
          <a href="http://www.lazerhorse.org/2014/05/18/forbidden-photos-north-korea/" target="_blank" title="Forbidden Photos From North Korea">
           Pyongyang
          </a>
          based atPaektusan Architectural Institute. They asked this fella to draw up some sketchesof the types of buildingshe would designif he had no restrictions on time, money or cost.
         </p>
         <p>
          The following rather kitsch, Jetson style drawings arewhat hes come up with over the last few years. I dont know how skilled North Korean architects are, but from a previous article I wrote, we know that their
          <a href="http://www.lazerhorse.org/2014/05/28/north-korean-construction-industry-screwed/" target="_blank" title="North Korean Construction Industry Is Screwed">
           building trade is screwed
          </a>
          . So Im assuming, like with most things in the DPRK, they are slightly behind the rest of the world.
         </p>
         <p>
          These drawings are of course fantastical, that was partly the point. The architect (who has remained anonymous) was asked to design buildings to attract trade and tourism and promote an eco-friendly vibe. The style sort of gives you the impression of a country frozen in a Soviet bubble from the 1950s. Their idea of the future appears distinctly retro to us.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="http://www.lazerhorse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/North-Korea-Futuristic-Architecture-tree-house.jpg">
           <img alt="North Korea Futuristic Architecture - tree house" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12914" height="360" src="//www.lazerhorse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/North-Korea-Futuristic-Architecture-tree-house.jpg" width="640"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The thing is,
          <a href="http://www.lazerhorse.org/2013/08/14/mobile-phone-embarrassment-north-korea-south-korea/" target="_blank" title="Mobile Phone Embarrassment In North Korea AND South Korea">
           no one inside North Korea has
           <em>
            any
           </em>
           exposure to
           <em>
            anything
           </em>
           modern
          </a>
          . Not even the elite live a life of luxury. So these designs were made by a man who probablystudied architecture for years, then practiced architecture for years all within this opaque bubble. Its quite an interesting experiment really.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="http://www.lazerhorse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/North-Korea-Futuristic-Architecture-roadway.jpg">
           <img alt="North Korea Futuristic Architecture - roadway" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12913" height="360" src="//www.lazerhorse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/North-Korea-Futuristic-Architecture-roadway.jpg" width="640"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          The designs are a heady mix of Japanese style landscape artand retro
          <a href="http://www.lazerhorse.org/2013/08/14/russian-propagandist-tourism-posters-1920s-1930s/" target="_blank" title="Russian Propagandist Tourism Posters From the 1920s and 1930s">
           Russian propaganda posters
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="http://www.lazerhorse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/North-Korea-Futuristic-Architecture-retro-eco.jpg">
           <img alt="North Korea Futuristic Architecture - retro eco" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12912" height="470" src="//www.lazerhorse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/North-Korea-Futuristic-Architecture-retro-eco.jpg" width="630"/>
          </a>
          <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="http://www.lazerhorse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/North-Korea-Futuristic-Architecture-hotel.jpg">
           <img alt="North Korea Futuristic Architecture - hotel" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12910" height="508" src="//www.lazerhorse.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/North-Korea-Futuristic-Architecture-hotel.jpg" width="680"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a class=" wwm_socialshare_imagewrapper " href="http://www.lazerhorse.org/category/asia-2/" target="_blank">
