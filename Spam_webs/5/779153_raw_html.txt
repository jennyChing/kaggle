       500,000 businesses launch every year
      </strong>

     </p>
     <p>
      are bucking the traditional, rigid, product or service business
     </p>
     <p>
      model. We are now seeing once unique business models -
     </p>
     <p>
      such as high-end subscriptions - becoming more
     </p>
     <p>
      mainstream.
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       These are the business models
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p>
      <strong>
       challenging the status quo.

     </p>

   <div class="section-2">

    <ul class="text cf">
     <li>
      This business model appeals to consumers with a heart of gold. The belief is that shoppers are more likely to buy from a brand if they know that their actions have positive consequences elsewhere.
     </li>
     <li>
      Since 2006, Toms has given more than 10 million shoes to children in more than 60 countries.
     </li>
    </ul>
    <img class="brands" src="images/section-2-brands.png"/>
   </div>
   <div class="begin-blocks">
    <div class="section-3">
     <img class="title-image" src="images/animation-3.gif"/>
     <p>
      This business model relies on the belief that happy customers are repeat customers, and repeat customers lead to profits.
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Zappos service is legendary. There are lores of customers calling for pizza delivery, and a customer service rep providing info for the best pizza place nearby.
      </li>
      <li>
       75% of Zappos customers are repeat buyers. CEO Tony Hsieh stated, I wanted us to have a whole company built around customer service.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <img class="brands-image" src="images/section-3-brands.png"/>
    </div>
    <div class="section-4">
     <img src="images/animation-4.gif"/>
     <p>
      This business model is built on the assumption that people are more willing to make a purchase if they have the flexibility to test items out first. In a store, thats easy, but it is much more complex for e-commerce.
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Warby Parker raised more than $60 million in 2013 for their unique business model.  The company will send you 5 pairs of glasses at a time to ensure you love your purchase.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <img src="images/section-4-brands.png"/>
    </div>
    <div class="section-5">
     <img src="images/animation-5.gif"/>
     <p>
      When you dont want to pay full price for something, renting can be a great alternative. The consumer pays less and simply rents - rather than full-out owns.
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       The rental business is a BIG deal.  Rent the Runway has raised $54.4M in funding from some of the biggest venture firms in the US.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <img src="images/section-5-brands.jpg"/>
    </div>
    <div class="section-6">
     <img class="animation" src="images/animation-6.gif"/>
     <p>
      In response to crummy public transit or unreliable cabs, several companies have come forth with transportation apps that are transforming city living.
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       A lead investor in Uber, who also invested early in eBay stated, Uber is growing faster than eBay did.  Considering his $6.7M investment was worth more than $5B in 2 years, that is not a statement to be taken lightly.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <img src="images/section-6-brands.png"/>
    </div>
    <div class="section-7">
     <img src="images/animation-7.gif"/>
     <p>
      Coworking is one of the more common workplace trends. This model allows freelancers, entrepreneurs, and small businesses to work alongside each other in shared office space. You pay for your space for as long as you need it.
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       A 2013 study revealed that more than 110,000 people currently work in one of the nearly 2,500 coworking spaces available worldwide. That is 83% more co-working space than in 2012 and the numbers continue to rise sharply.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <img src="images/section-7-brands.png"/>
    </div>
   </div>
   <div class="boxes-wrap" id="parallax">
    <div class="section-8">
     <img src="images/animation-8.gif"/>
     <p>
      Monthly deliveries to members add an allure of exclusivity, while also promoting loyal, returning customers.
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       BirchBox has over 400,000 monthly subscribers, and increased their revenue stream further by allowing customers to purchase their favorite full-sized products from their boxes.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <img class="brands" src="images/section-8-brands.png"/>

      Marketplaces that smoothly facilitate purchasing products and services from sole proprietors are now flourishing business models in their own right.
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       Etsy alone boasts more than 800,000 online shops and 15 million items in the marketplace at a given time.
      </li>
     </ul>
