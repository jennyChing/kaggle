        How to Install LED Lighting
       </h1>

        <p>
         <em>
          <span class="Apple-style-span" style="color: #666666;">
           This is a sponsored post on behalf of

         <a href="http://sewwoodsy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/How-to-Install-LED-Lighting.jpg">

             Homes.com
            </a>
            .
           </span>
          </em>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://sewwoodsy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/How-to-Install-LED-Lighting.jpg">
          <img alt="How to Install LED Lighting" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9722" height="966" src="http://sewwoodsy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/How-to-Install-LED-Lighting-678x1024.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Before Ryder was born, Jon filmed a video for our friends over at Homes.com to share how to install LED lighting. We did this underneath and above the kitchen cabinets. We decided to recess the crown moulding and drop some LED rope lighting into the
         <a href="http://sewwoodsy.com/2013/06/video-how-to-install-crown-moulding.html" target="_blank" title="{Video} How-to Install Crown Moulding">
          crown moulding
         </a>
         for the nursery. It gives the perfect glow at 2am in the morning when you really dont want all the lights are on.
        </p>
        <p>
         Watch the video to learn just how Jon
         <a href="http://blog.homes.com/2014/08/homes-com-diy-experts-how-to-install-led-lighting/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          installs LED lighting
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/GQqOoI4cF2Q" width="560">
         </iframe>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://sewwoodsy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/How-to-Install-LED-Lighting-6.jpg">
          <img alt="How to Install LED Lighting 6" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9721" height="966" src="http://sewwoodsy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/How-to-Install-LED-Lighting-6-678x1024.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Pretty amazing I know and even better we have 30 different colors the room can change color to.
         <span id="more-9833">
         </span>
         <a href="http://sewwoodsy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/How-to-Install-LED-Lighting-5.jpg">
          <img alt="How to Install LED Lighting 5" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9720" height="464" src="http://sewwoodsy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/How-to-Install-LED-Lighting-5.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         You would never know that the entire ceiling didnt have lighting.
         <a href="http://sewwoodsy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/How-to-Install-LED-Lighting-4.jpg">
          <img alt="How to Install LED Lighting 4" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9719" height="966" src="http://sewwoodsy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/How-to-Install-LED-Lighting-4-678x1024.jpg" width="640"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I think Ryder is going to love that his room can change so many colors. When I put him in his crib and I need to do something in his room I put on the multi-color option and it flashes through different colors!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://sewwoodsy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/How-to-Install-LED-Lighting-3.jpg">
          <img alt="How to Install LED Lighting 3" class="aligncenter wp-image-9718 size-full" height="464" src="http://sewwoodsy.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/How-to-Install-LED-Lighting-3.jpg" width="700"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So, for $25 what do you think? Not a bad idea and way to get lighting in any room of your house!
        </p>
