         <p>
          Every year since 1970, we have celebrated Earth Day on April 22. My first memory of Earth Day was back in 1990 when I was in grade 6. We watched videos and did activities in science class to learn more about it. I remember back then feeling fear at what our Earth was becoming. Improvements have been made, but I think we still have a long way to go.
         </p>
         <p>
          In honour of Earth Day this year, Ive made these Earth Day quotes with words of wisdom that resonated with me. How do you celebrate Earth Day?
