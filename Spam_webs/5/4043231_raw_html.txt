        </a>
        during your next visit? If so, dont worry because there are a lot of things to do in Cape Town that do not cost any money at all and will surely leave you exhilarated.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2051" style="width: 660px">
        <a href="https://hostelrocket.com/">
         <img alt="Cape Town, South Africa" class="lazy lazy-hidden size-large wp-image-2051" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://lpmfc375762dcvmc48alm81c.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/CapeTown1-1024x628.jpg" height="398" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="650"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Cape Town, South Africa" class="size-large wp-image-2051" height="398" src="http://lpmfc375762dcvmc48alm81c.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/CapeTown1-1024x628.jpg" width="650"/>
         </noscript>
        </a>
        Enjoy the view of Cape Town from Table Mountain
       </div>
       <ul>
        <li>
         If you are a nature lover,
         <a href="http://hostelrocket.flywheelsites.com/stay-fit-hostels/" title="HOW TO STAY FIT IN HOSTELS">
          you can hike
         </a>
         up Lions Head. It is between Table Mountain and Signal Hill. The place is breathtakingly beautiful and even more spectacular in the glow of a full moon. Talk some
         <a href="http://hostelrocket.flywheelsites.com/6-ways-to-make-your-hostel-dorm-mates-love-you/" title="6 WAYS TO MAKE YOUR HOSTEL DORM MATES LOVE YOU">
          friends from the hostel
         </a>
         into joining you on this adventure. As with any hike, let someone (maybe even the hostel staff) know where you are going and when you plan to return.
        </li>
        <li>
         You can also spend some time in the Green Point Park to get your nature fix. It is an immaculately landscaped urban garden.
        </li>
        <li>
         Listen to free music. African and other local music is performed regularly at restaurants and pubs throughout the city.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2052" style="width: 660px">
        <a href="https://hostelrocket.com/">
         <img alt="Cape Town Beach South Africa" class="lazy lazy-hidden size-large wp-image-2052" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://lpmfc375762dcvmc48alm81c.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/CapeTown2-1024x682.jpg" height="432" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="650"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Cape Town Beach South Africa" class="size-large wp-image-2052" height="432" src="http://lpmfc375762dcvmc48alm81c.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/CapeTown2-1024x682.jpg" width="650"/>
         </noscript>
        </a>
        Soak up some rays on Cape Town Beach
       </div>
       <ul>
        <li>
         There are a lot of markets in Cape Town that are all about shopping (which can mean spending lots of money), but feel free to wander around and explore. You can try the Greenmarket Square and Neighbourgoods Market that hand out liberal samples of beverages and gourmet food.
        </li>
        <li>
         You can also have a tour at The Diamond Works and watch diamonds being cut. This is a very interesting and unique opportunity to take advantage of while in Cape Town.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2053" style="width: 660px">
        <a href="https://hostelrocket.com/">
         <img alt="Enjoy thr beautiful Bo Kaap District Cape Town" class="lazy lazy-hidden size-large wp-image-2053" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://lpmfc375762dcvmc48alm81c.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/CapeTown3-867x1024.jpg" height="767" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="650"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Enjoy thr beautiful Bo Kaap District Cape Town" class="size-large wp-image-2053" height="767" src="http://lpmfc375762dcvmc48alm81c.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/CapeTown3-867x1024.jpg" width="650"/>
         </noscript>
        </a>
        Enjoy the beautiful Bo Kaap District
       </div>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Explore the beautiful Bo Kaap District of Cape Town and be sure to do so at noon sharp. I will not spoil the surprise but you cant miss it!
        </li>
        <li>
         You can spend your day at Cape Town beach and enjoy the wind-sheltered sandy expanses of Camp Bays and Llandudno.
        </li>
        <li>
         Visit and explore the V&amp;A Waterfront.
        </li>
        <li>
         Seek out TEARS and give them a hand with their dog walking program. The nonprofit cares for abandoned dogs and needs all the help they can get walking their furry little friends.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2054" style="width: 660px">
        <a href="https://hostelrocket.com/">
         <img alt="Aerial view of Cape Town South Africa" class="lazy lazy-hidden size-large wp-image-2054" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://lpmfc375762dcvmc48alm81c.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/CapeTown4-1024x682.jpg" height="432" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="650"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Aerial view of Cape Town South Africa" class="size-large wp-image-2054" height="432" src="http://lpmfc375762dcvmc48alm81c.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/CapeTown4-1024x682.jpg" width="650"/>
         </noscript>
        </a>
        Aerial view of Cape Town and Table Top Mountain
       </div>
       <ul>
        <li>
         Take a walk or jog along at the Sea Point Promenade. It is also suitable for picnics or just people-watching. You can even flex your muscles at the outdoor gym.
        </li>
        <li>
         Take a free tour of the Parliament building and get a taste of the South African political scene.
        </li>
        <li>
         Go on a free wine tasting tour at Wine Concepts at Lifestyle on Kloof. Taste some great wine and catch a little buzz for free!
        </li>
        <li>
         See if you can borrow a bike and meet up for the Moonlight Mass near the Green Point roundabout around 9pm on the night of a full moon. You will enjoy the ride that usually ends with some social drinking. Just dont drink too much and forget to return the bike you borrowed.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        Like any great city, there are tons of free things to do in Cape Town. Enjoy your visit and have a great time in Cape Town.
       </p>
