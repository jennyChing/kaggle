         <p>
          Toy Story, not really
          <br/>
          <span id="more-9641">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="DIY-3D-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Toy-Blocks" class="aligncenter wp-image-9687 size-large" height="800" src="http://monsterscircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-3D-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Toy-Blocks-768x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Budgetvenlig makeover til sofabordet  dette er en super nem og enkel mde, at f et enestende mbel ved brug af legetj.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           To say that adding a personal touch to your home is a mega trendwould be a massive understatement  and a big YAY to that.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9649" height="800" src="http://monsterscircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-details-768x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          I et af mine allerfrste
          <strong>
           <a href="http://monsterscircus.com/2012/06/15/diy-your-own-wall-art/" target="_blank">
            blog indlg
           </a>
          </strong>
          forsnart 3 r siden (gisp!) lavede jeg denne 3D vgdekoration med brnebyggeklodser i tr  iden er stadig en af mine favoritter, men idag skal klodserne bruges til ngt. helt andet.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Transform your coffee table on the cheap requires some kids and their toys  Im not gifted with any of thoseso Ive bought some toy blocks in the local toy store.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Ive used these wood blocks in one of my very first blog posts
           <a href="http://monsterscircus.com/2012/06/15/diy-your-own-wall-art/" target="_blank">
            <strong>
             here
            </strong>
           </a>
           , where I made an artistic wall decoration, it looked like this.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="MAKE-3D-Art-Wall-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9685" height="800" src="http://monsterscircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/MAKE-3D-Art-Wall-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-768x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt=" DIY-3D-Wall-Art-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-BuildingBlocks" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9674" height="800" src="http://monsterscircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-3D-Wall-Art-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-BuildingBlocks-768x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Hvis du, som jeg, stod i k sidste fredag da
          <a href="http://www.grenes.dk/" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            Sstrene Grene
           </strong>
          </a>
          lancerede deres
          <a href="http://monsterscircus.com/2015/03/05/soestrene-grene-interior-spring-news-2015/" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            nye kollektion
           </strong>
          </a>
          har du mske allerede erhvervet dig det smukke bord, som jeg har brugt til dagens DIY.
         </p>
         <p>
          Det er jo en sknhed i sig selv, med en feature som sammeklappelige ben, perfekt hvis pladsen er trang, men jeg har tilfjet det lidt ekstra kant.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Materialer:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.grenes.dk/" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            Sofa Bord
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Legeklodser i Tr
         </p>
         <p>
          Spray Maling
         </p>
         <p>
          Limpistol
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Trin 1
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Bestem dig for et design med trklodserne og lim klodserne fast p bordpladen.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Today Im going to show how you can transform your coffee table
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Ive used my new side table from the new
           <a href="http://monsterscircus.com/2015/03/05/soestrene-grene-interior-spring-news-2015/" target="_blank">
            <strong>
             Spring Interior Collection
            </strong>
           </a>
           from
           <a href="http://www.grenes.dk/en/home" target="_blank">
            <strong>
             Sstrene Grene
            </strong>
           </a>
           . Its avery versatile little piece that easily can be tucked into any small roomand utilized in multiple ways.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           <strong>
            Supplies:
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.grenes.dk/en/home" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            <span style="color: #999999;">
             Side Table
            </span>
           </strong>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Wooden Toy Blocks
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Spray Paint
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Glue Gun
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           <strong>
            Step 1
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Play around with the blocks and decide for a design  glue each block directly on the table top
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-Supplies" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9660" height="600" src="http://monsterscircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-Supplies-1024x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Trin 2
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Tag bordet med udenfor, spraymal hele herligheden og lad malingen trre  for et smukkere resultat, pfr et jvnt men tyndt lag maling af mindst 2 omgange
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           <strong>
            Step 2
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Take the table outside and spray paint the baby its advisable to apply several thin layers rather than one thick coating
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-Tutorial" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9661" height="387" src="http://monsterscircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-Tutorial-1024x660.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Sdan! Et styks artistisk og ekletisk sofabord med bde kant og personlighed.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Et voil, youve just created asuper simple and stylishpiece offunctional art.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9659" height="800" src="http://monsterscircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-768x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Alt efter hvor meget tid duvlger at investere i projektet, kaniden varieres i det uendelige  giv helhedslooket en ekstra dimension ved at nuancere klodsernes sider med netop dine yndlingsfarver.
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Want to advance and invest more time in this project? Add different paint colours on the wood blocks  pops of colour in saturated hues wouldnt make this less interesting.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="DIY-3D-Art-on-Table-Top-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9670" height="800" src="http://monsterscircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-3D-Art-on-Table-Top-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-768x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Er s spndt p at hre hvad I siger til iden?
         </p>
         <p>
          Hvis du har brn, er jeg sikker p, at de vil elske at vre klods majoren i dette projekt:-)
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Im very excited to hear if you fancy the result?
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Got kidsand the toy blocks? Children labour wouldnt be such a bad idea;-) Think theyll love to be decision maker in theDIY design process.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-Makeover" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9653" height="800" src="http://monsterscircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-Makeover-768x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          Hber I har lyst til at forsge Jer ad med klodserne og del endelig i kommentarfeltet, hvis I har andre ider til brugenaf klodserne.
         </p>
         <p>
          Ha en super smuk weekend i forrets tegn, vi ses!
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           I really like the final look even though Ive painted the table top and wood blocks all white, I think the table hassuch abeautiful visual tension.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #999999;">
           Only thing left to say: Build them Blocks and happy weekend!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-SstreneGrene" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-9677" height="800" src="http://monsterscircus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Statement-Coffee-Table-with-Wood-Toy-Blocks-Sstrene-Grene-768x1024.jpg" width="600"/>
         </p>
