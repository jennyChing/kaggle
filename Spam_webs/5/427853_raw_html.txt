         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="312528" data-link="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/why-vacation-rentals-are-best-for-luxury-family-travel/" data-summary="" data-title="Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel ">

         <h1 class="fontplugin_fontid_379_BUDHAND2" style="text-align: center;">
          Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel
         </h1>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Vacation-Rentals-Luxury-Family-Travel.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
            <img alt="Vacation Rentals Luxury Family Travel 1024x768 Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4274" height="768" src="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Vacation-Rentals-Luxury-Family-Travel-1024x768.jpg" title="Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel " width="1024"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/why-vacation-rentals-are-best-for-luxury-family-travel/&amp;media=http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Vacation-Rentals-Luxury-Family-Travel-1024x768.jpg&amp;description=Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
          Family Travel can be difficult, and
          <strong>
           luxury family travel
          </strong>
          can be near impossible for those of us who have not won the inheritance lottery.
         </p>
         <p>
          A hotel room was sufficient. until the 3rd kid came along. Its that magic number of 5 that kicked us out of the standard hotel room and into two rooms or a suite.
         </p>
         <p>
          Im not going to lie. We got away with a single hotel room for a while. We told the hotel that the 3rd child was just a baby, and slept in a crib. It started to get a little awkward when my baby introduced himself to the hotel manager.
         </p>
         <p>
          Hence, we are officially in the most expensive rooms available category when staying at a luxury hotel or resort. You can literally spend thousands of dollars a night at a Ritz Carlton or Four Seasons for your kid to sleep on a sofabed in the living area.
          <strong>
           V
          </strong>
          <strong>
           acation Rentals
          </strong>
          are honestly the best option for
          <strong>
           luxury family travel
          </strong>
          if you have not won the inheritance lottery either.
         </p>
         <p>
          We are spending our summer in
          <strong>
           Orange Beach Alabama
          </strong>
          , and there are some amazing
          <strong>
           vacation rental
          </strong>
          options for
          <strong>
           luxury family travel
          </strong>
          .
          <a href="http://www.TurquoisePlaceRental.com" target="_blank" title="Turquoise Place Orange Beach">
           <strong>
            Turquoise Place
           </strong>
          </a>
          in Orange Beach Alabama is one of the most luxurious accommodations on the beach that I know of, and I travel a lot!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/turquoise-place-rental-orangebeach-603c-hottub.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
            <img alt="turquoise place rental orangebeach 603c hottub 1024x681 Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4278" height="681" src="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/turquoise-place-rental-orangebeach-603c-hottub-1024x681.jpg" title="Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel " width="1024"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/why-vacation-rentals-are-best-for-luxury-family-travel/&amp;media=http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/turquoise-place-rental-orangebeach-603c-hottub-1024x681.jpg&amp;description=Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          In the peak season, a 3 bedroom at
          <a href="http://www.prickettproperties.com/rental_listing.php?loc=&amp;beds=&amp;complex=Turquoise+Place" target="_blank">
           <strong>
            Turquoise Place
           </strong>
          </a>
          will cost around $650.00 a night. In the off-peak season, Turquoise Place is as little as $275.00 a night. Try finding those prices at a Ritz Carlton! Even though Turquoise Place is not technically a hotel, it does not lack for amenities.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Turquoise-Place-Alabama.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
            <img alt="Turquoise Place Alabama 1024x768 Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4275" height="768" src="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Turquoise-Place-Alabama-1024x768.jpg" title="Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel " width="1024"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/why-vacation-rentals-are-best-for-luxury-family-travel/&amp;media=http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Turquoise-Place-Alabama-1024x768.jpg&amp;description=Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.ChicFamilyTravels.com" target="_blank" title="Luxury Family Travel ">
           Chic Family Travels
          </a>
          is spending most of our summer vacation at another
          <a href="http://www.prickettproperties.com/orange-beach/condo/rentals/" target="_blank" title="Vacation Rental in Orange Beach">
           <strong>
            vacation rental in Orange Beach
           </strong>
          </a>
          . The
          <strong>
           Bella Luna
          </strong>
          is a vacation rental located on the bay-side of Orange Beach. The views are not so bad on the bay either!
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_2049" style="width: 1034px">
          <a href="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Bella-Luna-Rental-902-Views.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
            <img alt="Bella Luna Rental 902 Views 1024x681 Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel " class="size-large wp-image-2049" height="681" src="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Bella-Luna-Rental-902-Views-1024x681.jpg" title="Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel " width="1024"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/why-vacation-rentals-are-best-for-luxury-family-travel/&amp;media=http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Bella-Luna-Rental-902-Views-1024x681.jpg&amp;description=Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Bella Luna in Orange Beach Alabama
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Our 3 bedroom condo at the
          <a href="http://www.prickettproperties.com/145/orange-beach/condos/bella-luna/902/" target="_blank" title="Bella Luna Condo">
           <strong>
            Bella Luna
           </strong>
          </a>
          costs around $350 a night in the peak season! Our kids are in heaven with 4 pools, hot tubs, marina &amp; the beach across the street. Our days are full of boating, hanging out at islands, and taking long walks on the beach. Our evenings include taking sunset cruises, grilling out and occasionally taking the kids wake-boarding. If you dont own a boat, there are plenty for rent in Orange Beach!
         </p>
         <p>
          Honestly, I dont know a lot of places that you stay as nice as the Bella Luna with 3 bedrooms for only $350 a night and no one has to sleep on the sofa!
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Bella-Luna-Condo.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest">
            <img alt="Bella Luna Condo 1024x681 Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel " class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4277" height="681" src="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Bella-Luna-Condo-1024x681.jpg" title="Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel " width="1024"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/why-vacation-rentals-are-best-for-luxury-family-travel/&amp;media=http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Bella-Luna-Condo-1024x681.jpg&amp;description=Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Disclaimer: I own an
          <a href="http://www.PrickettProperties.com" target="_blank" title="Orange Beach Vacation Rental Company">
           <strong>
            Orange Beach vacation rental company
           </strong>
          </a>
          , but my opinions are my own. I also firmly believe that vacation rentals are best for luxury family travel whether it be in Orange Beach or anywhere else in the world! Nonetheless, I still love room service. Dont expect me to quit over paying for a suite at a luxury hotel anytime soon
          <img alt="icon smile Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel " class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.chicfamilytravels.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif" title="Why Vacation Rentals Are Best For Luxury Family Travel "/>
         </p>

           top 25 family travel bloggers
          </a>
          at
          <strong>
           Flip Key

          where you can find a vacation rentals all over the world.
