     <p>
      <b>
       Sofia Boutella
      </b>
      (born April 3, 1982) is an
      <a href="/wiki/Algeria" title="Algeria">
       Algerian
      </a>
      -
      <a href="/wiki/French_people" title="French people">
       French
      </a>
      dancer, actress and musician, known mainly for hip-hop and street dance, and as the Nike Girl from
      <a href="/wiki/Nike,_Inc." title="Nike, Inc.">
       Nike
      </a>
      Women advertising campaigns. She has starred in films such as
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/StreetDance_2" title="StreetDance 2">
        StreetDance 2
       </a>
      </i>
      ,
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Monsters:_Dark_Continent" title="Monsters: Dark Continent">
        Monsters: Dark Continent
       </a>
      </i>
      and
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Kingsman:_The_Secret_Service" title="Kingsman: The Secret Service">
        Kingsman: The Secret Service
       </a>
      </i>
      .
     </p>

     <h2>
      <span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">
       Life and career
      </span>
      <span class="mw-editsection">
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        [
       </span>
       <a href="/w/index.php?title=Sofia_Boutella&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">
        edit
       </a>
       <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
        ]
       </span>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      Boutella was born in the
      <a href="/wiki/Bab_El_Oued" title="Bab El Oued">
       Bab El Oued
      </a>
      district of
      <a href="/wiki/Algiers" title="Algiers">
       Algiers
      </a>
      , the daughter of jazz musician
      <a href="/wiki/Safy_Boutella" title="Safy Boutella">
       Safy Boutella
      </a>
      and an architect mother.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
       <a href="#cite_note-1">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        1
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      She started classical dance education at the age of 5 and at the age of 10 in 1992 she left
      <a href="/wiki/Algeria" title="Algeria">
       Algeria
      </a>
      with her family, and moved to France, where she started rhythmic gymnastics, and joined the French national team at the age of 18.
     </p>
     <p>
      Meanwhile, she had taken up
      <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop" title="Hip hop">
       hip hop
      </a>
      and street dance, and was part of a group called the Vagabond Crew, which won the
      <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Year" title="Battle of the Year">
       Battle of the Year
      </a>
      in 2006. She also participated in a group called "Chienne de Vie and Aphrodites" created by Momo from the Vagabond Crew. Sofia is a graduate of
      <a href="/wiki/Berklee_College_of_Music" title="Berklee College of Music">
       Berklee College of Music
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      At the same time, she had been rehearsing since the age of 17 with choreographer
      <a href="/wiki/Blanca_Li" title="Blanca Li">
       Blanca Li
      </a>
      . She has danced in several film and TV appearances, as well as commercials and concert tours.
     </p>
     <p>
      Her real breakthrough arrived in 2007, when she was picked for the
      <a href="/wiki/Jamie_King" title="Jamie King">
       Jamie King
      </a>
      choreography for Nike, as a role model of femininity and hip hop.
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
       <a href="#cite_note-2">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        2
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      This was a major boost in her career and led to more work alongside stars like
      <a href="/wiki/Madonna_(entertainer)" title="Madonna (entertainer)">
       Madonna
      </a>
      , in her
      <a href="/wiki/Confessions_Tour" title="Confessions Tour">
       Confessions Tour
      </a>
      , and
      <a href="/wiki/Rihanna" title="Rihanna">
       Rihanna
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      She auditioned for the Michael Jackson
      <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/This_Is_It_(Michael_Jackson_concerts)" title="This Is It (Michael Jackson concerts)">
       This Is It concerts
      </a>
      but, although successful, could not attend due to the extension of the Madonna tour whose dates coincided with the Jackson residency. In February 2011, she was the main character in the music video of "
      <a href="/wiki/Hollywood_Tonight" title="Hollywood Tonight">
       Hollywood Tonight
      </a>
      ".
     </p>
     <p>
      Boutella played lead character Eva in the film
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/StreetDance_2" title="StreetDance 2">
        StreetDance 2
       </a>
      </i>
      ,
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
       <a href="#cite_note-3">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        3
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      the sequel to
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/StreetDance_3D" title="StreetDance 3D">
        StreetDance 3D
       </a>
      </i>
      , and co-starred in
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Kingsman:_The_Secret_Service" title="Kingsman: The Secret Service">
        Kingsman: The Secret Service
       </a>
      </i>
      .
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">
       <a href="#cite_note-4">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        4
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
      She will appear in
      <i>
       <a href="/wiki/Star_Trek_Beyond" title="Star Trek Beyond">
        Star Trek Beyond
       </a>
      </i>
      , scheduled for release in 2016.
     </p>
     <p>
      Her influences include
      <a href="/wiki/Fred_Astaire" title="Fred Astaire">
       Fred Astaire
      </a>
      , Karim Barouche,
      <a href="/wiki/Jean-Michel_Basquiat" title="Jean-Michel Basquiat">
       Jean-Michel Basquiat
      </a>
      ,
      <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Day-Lewis" title="Daniel Day-Lewis">
       Daniel Day-Lewis
      </a>
      , and
      <a href="/wiki/Bob_Fosse" title="Bob Fosse">
       Bob Fosse
      </a>
      .
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-JusteDebout_5-0">
       <a href="#cite_note-JusteDebout-5">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        5
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
     <p>
      Boutella is currently dating her Jet Trash co-star
      <a href="/wiki/Robert_Sheehan" title="Robert Sheehan">
       Robert Sheehan
      </a>
      .
      <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6">
       <a href="#cite_note-6">
        <span>
         [
        </span>
        6
        <span>
         ]
        </span>
       </a>
      </sup>
     </p>
