          <div class="row">
           <div class="col col-8">
            <div class="mb6">
             <h3 class="normal mb4 mobile-hide">
              About this project
             </h3>
            </div>
            <div class="full-description js-full-description responsive-media formatted-lists">
             <h1>
              About The Calendar
             </h1>
             <p>
              One day, we jokingly said we should do a wine of the month calendar. It sort of snowballed from there.
             </p>
             <p>
              Each month in
              <i>
               Wine Is Rad: The Calendar. Brought To You By Tyler Hauptman and Rob Baird. The 2014 Grape Expectations Edition
              </i>
              has an awesome seasonal image and an even more awesome wine to go along with it!
             </p>
             <div class="template asset" contenteditable="false" data-id="999206">
              <figure>
               <img alt="Our Freedom Inspired July Image" class="fit" src="https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/000/999/206/8ed5e7b161592439d25020bee0a4b748_original.jpg?v=1381245362&amp;w=680&amp;fit=max&amp;auto=format&amp;q=92&amp;s=67f76ab5deb89a9c01c2db2e5a524da2"/>
               <figcaption>
                Our Freedom Inspired July Image
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <p>
              Not only do you get beautiful pictures, but you also get Tylers thoughts on wine, and fun wine facts. We even made sure you could write on the calendar and put down all of your important dates, or draw funny mustaches on Tylers face.
             </p>
             <h1>
              Making Wine Accessible
             </h1>
             <p>
              Lots of people love wine, but it can still seem stuffy and overwhelming. We wholeheartedly believe that wine is fun and great to share. There are a lot of great wines out there to explore, and we wanted to use this calendar to make wine a bigger part of your everyday life.
             </p>
             <h1>
              Why a Calendar?
             </h1>
             <p>
              Because we thought it was the best way to grab people's attention and show a series of images. Unlike a web gallery that people click through quickly, or a book that collects dust on the shelf, a calendar stays on your wall for the entire year. As you flip to a new month, the image and information displayed change, keeping it fresh and exciting, as well as giving you something to look forward to as the year goes on.
             </p>
             <div class="template asset" contenteditable="false" data-id="1068195">
              <figure>
               <img alt="Behind the scenes on our tiki-inspired August shoot" class="fit" src="https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/001/068/195/d8b7229254039a5f73836c9ad934598b_original.jpg?v=1381506772&amp;w=680&amp;fit=max&amp;auto=format&amp;q=92&amp;s=5b7b5b6e59fb882f3859b481c0497b8f"/>
               <figcaption>
                Behind the scenes on our tiki-inspired August shoot
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <h1>
              The Shoots
             </h1>
             <p>
              We've tried to do interesting shoots that captured the spirit of each month. We worked really hard to try and find the best wine to go with each shoot, whether a lovely ros to enjoy at a July BBQ, or a sweet port to drink with your Halloween candy in October.We want to make sure that each shot tells its own story, so we are utilizing twelve different locations, with a variety of props and styles.
             </p>
             <p>
              We had a lot of fun working on the shoots!
             </p>
             <div class="template asset" contenteditable="false" data-id="999772">
              <figure>
               <img alt="Behind The Scenes On Our February Shoot" class="fit" src="https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/000/999/772/eb8cc87f7077bf42354c34d48a5df300_original.JPG?v=1381252482&amp;w=680&amp;fit=max&amp;auto=format&amp;q=92&amp;s=fb7bf5bc88f7c1cf04b6168a0e0ec2ff"/>
               <figcaption>
                Behind The Scenes On Our February Shoot
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <h1>
              About Us
             </h1>
             <p>
              Tyler being a sommelier and Rob a photographer, we have really cherished the opportunity to channel our skills and motivation into something so fun! This project is something we are very passionate about, and have spent a lot of time trying to make it as great as it can possibly be.
             </p>
             <div class="template asset" contenteditable="false" data-id="999299">
              <figure>
               <img alt="Rob &amp; Tyler Working In The Office" class="fit" src="https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/000/999/299/e23fd30f34a5441ed6a85150da4e7c30_original.jpg?v=1381246643&amp;w=680&amp;fit=max&amp;auto=format&amp;q=92&amp;s=69344c7fb743095b7331a64ba98dcd43"/>
               <figcaption>
                Rob &amp; Tyler Working In The Office
               </figcaption>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <h1>
              What Can You Get?
             </h1>
             <p>
              <b>
               The Calendar!
              </b>
             </p>
             <p>
              You'll get the wonderful calendar that you've been hearing so much about.
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               Sweet Stickers!
              </b>
             </p>
             <div class="template asset" contenteditable="false" data-id="999577">
              <figure>
               <img alt="" class="fit" src="https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/000/999/577/8050a4d3d81079ffa2839b4cfa8df037_original.png?v=1381250086&amp;w=680&amp;fit=max&amp;auto=format&amp;lossless=true&amp;s=925838e461613e4fb17a08c6956e75ff"/>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <p>
              <b>
               Awesome Poster!
              </b>
             </p>
             <p>
              Our poster is going to be 18"x24" and will be a lovely addition to any home.
             </p>
             <div class="template asset" contenteditable="false" data-id="1000350">
              <figure>
               <img alt="" class="fit" src="https://ksr-ugc.imgix.net/assets/001/000/350/3de31f887a534ae9c1e73a59264028b0_original.png?v=1381261610&amp;w=680&amp;fit=max&amp;auto=format&amp;lossless=true&amp;s=31761a933af0d36e01cf66710f50950c"/>
              </figure>
             </div>
             <p>
              <b>
               Personal Delivery!
              </b>
             </p>
             <p>
              If you live in Portland, you can get Tyler to deliver your rewards in person! You'll also be one of the first people to get the calendar, right after it is back from the printer.
             </p>
             <p>
              <b>
               Tyler's WineByText!
              </b>
             </p>
             <p>
              Don't know what type of wine to pair with your meal? Better text Tyler! For one week, Tyler will be your personal wine guru to help you get the most out of your food, by pairing it with the perfect wine.
             </p>
             <h1>
              What You're Supporting
             </h1>
             <p>
              Your backing is primarily going to printing costs. We have found a local printer that we are working with. The finished size of the calendar will be 12x12, and it will be saddle-stitched with a hole drilled to hang it.
             </p>
             <p>
              If we overfund, we will be able to improve our printing even more. While we love the paper stock we have selected, we will invest in a slightly heavier stock, protecting it from dings when you flip back and forth to admire the pages.
             </p>
            </div>
            <div class="mb6 mb2">
             <div class="mobile-show py2">
             </div>
             <h3 class="normal mb4">
              Risks and challenges
             </h3>
             <p>
              We understand that it's important to have the calendar in time for the holiday season, and the new year. We are working closely with a local printer. As soon as the Kickstarter is finished, the printer will start work on the calendars, which we will immediately begin sending out.
