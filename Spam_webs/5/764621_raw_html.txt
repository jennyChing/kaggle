        <p>
         <em>
          Sesame GingerSautedCabbage and Carrots is a quick and easy side dish bursting with Asian-inspired flavors such as sesame, ginger and scallion.
         </em>
         <br/>
         <img alt="Sesame Ginger Sauteed Cabbage and Carrots - The Lemon Bowl" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-9005" height="837" src="http://cdn2.thelemonbowl.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Sesame-Ginger-Sauteed-Cabbage-and-Carrots-The-Lemon-Bowl.jpg" width="575"/>
         Admittedly, our family eats a lot of vegetables. Between our weekly Door to Door Organics box, frequent trips to the farmers market and my inability to cook small portions, we cant get enough of them.
        </p>
        <p>
         Of course, vegetables can get really boring really quickly if you dont season them appropriately. Especially the humble cabbage which gets a bad rep thanks to memories of over-boiled cabbage on St. Patricks Day.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Greek Marinated Grilled Eggplant and Summer Squash - The Lemon Bowl" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6719" height="600" src="http://cdn2.thelemonbowl.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Greek-Marinated-Grilled-Eggplant-and-Summer-Squash-The-Lemon-Bowl.jpg" width="600"/>
         While I love the simplicity of
         <a href="http://thelemonbowl.com/2013/08/greek-marinated-grilled-eggplant-and-summer-squash.html" target="_blank">
          grilling
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://thelemonbowl.com/2014/04/zaatar-roasted-carrots-and-green-beans.html" target="_blank">
          roasting vegetables
         </a>
         in a little olive oil and salt, I also enjoyputting an Asian spin on things.Low calorie, flavor-packed ingredients like fresh ginger, soy sauce and scallions can transport a humble vegetable like cabbage into a simple yet exotic side dish your family will devour in minutes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Head on over to The Law Students Wife to get this delicious
         <a href="http://www.thelawstudentswife.com/2014/06/sesame-ginger-sauteed-cabbage-carrots/" target="_blank">
          Sesame Ginger Sauteed Cabbage and Carrots recipe
         </a>
         ! I am guest posting for my friend Erin who is currently traveling around France. We met last month in Miami and she is an absolute doll!
        </p>
        <p>
         Your fork is waiting.
        </p>
