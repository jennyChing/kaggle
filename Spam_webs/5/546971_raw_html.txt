          <p>
           Whilst most of the country sees New Year, New You as an inspiration to cut out the takeaways and join a gym  at FADS, we think its the perfect time to freshen up the interiors and add a little colour to the home as the days get longer and the weather (hopefully) starts to improve.
           <span id="more-2337">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           With this in mind, we have decided to run another of our popular Pinterest competitions  asking you to design the perfect room for your home this month. Our favourite design will receive a 250 FADS voucher to use on any product(s) on the
           <a href="http://www.fads.co.uk/" title="FADS">
            FADS.co.uk
           </a>
           website  giving you the chance to make your dream interior design a reality.
          </p>
          <p>
           You can choose any room in the home and collect images from FADS, Pinterest and around the internet to create a beautiful Spring-inspired interior. Whilst you do have complete freedom over the colour and style of the room, we do ask you include at least one piece from any of the ranges on FADS.co.uk.
          </p>
          <p>
           Take a look at
           <a href="https://www.pinterest.com/fadsuk/fadsinspiredspringinteriors/" target="_blank" title="FADS Pinterest Spring Interiors board">
            our sample living room board here
           </a>
           .
          </p>

           <strong>
            How to Enter
           </strong>
          </h3>
          <p>

          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>

            <a href="https://uk.pinterest.com/" target="_blank">
             up here
            </a>
            .
           </li>
           <li>
            Create a Pinterest board entitled #FADSInspiredSpringInteriors and add features and dcor youd love in your dream Spring room.
           </li>
           <li>
            Add
            <strong>
             at least
            </strong>
            one product from the FADS online store.
           </li>
           <li>
            Follow FADS on Pinterest at
            <strong>
             <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/fadsuk" target="_blank">
              www.pinterest.com/fadsuk
             </a>
            </strong>
           </li>
           <li>
            Share the URL of your board with us by commenting at the bottom of this blog post including your boards URL before 16 March 2015.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           Entrants can also share this competition  and your #FADSInspiredSpringInteriors Pinterest Board of course!  on your personal/interior design blog to help inspire others to get involved.
