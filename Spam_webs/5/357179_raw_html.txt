      <p>
       Decorating your front door when the seasons change doesnt have to be difficult, or expensive.
      </p>
      <p>
       Grapevine wreaths come in many sizes, and are very easy to decorate with flowers to create a simple and stylish look. This entire project cost me around $5.
      </p>
      <p>
       Keep reading if youve ever wanted to know how to make a beautiful fall grapevine wreath!
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://parentpretty.com/how-to-make-a-fall-grapevine-wreath/fallwreath/" rel="attachment wp-att-199">
        <img alt="Fall Wreath" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-199" height="430" src="http://parentpretty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/FallWreath.jpg" width="660"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h3>
       Grapevine wreath making supply list:
      </h3>
      <ul>
       <li>
        1 Small Grapevine Wreath
       </li>
       <li>
        1 Small Fall Floral Pick (or individual stems of fall flowers)
       </li>
       <li>
        Wire cutters
       </li>
       <li>
        Scissors
       </li>
       <li>
        Hot Glue Gun and Hot Glue Stick
       </li>
       <li>
        Brown Ribbon
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       <a href="http://parentpretty.com/how-to-make-a-fall-grapevine-wreath/fallwreathsupplies/" rel="attachment wp-att-200">
        <img alt="Fall Wreath Supplies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-200" height="430" src="http://parentpretty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/fallwreathsupplies.jpg" width="660"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <h3>
       Grapevine wreath making directions:
      </h3>
      <p>
       Using your wire cutters, carefully remove the flowers individually from the floral pick (or stems, depending on what you decide to use).
      </p>
      <p>
       On the pieces that you want to use for the arrangement, make sure that they have a stem that it about an inch to an inch and a half in length so that you can insert it far enough into the wreath.
      </p>
      <p>
       I didnt use some of the ones in the photo below on my grapevine wreath, because they were too short.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://parentpretty.com/how-to-make-a-fall-grapevine-wreath/fallwreathassembly/" rel="attachment wp-att-201">
        <img alt="Fall Wreath Assembly" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-201" height="430" src="http://parentpretty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/FallWreathAssembly.jpg" width="660"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Decide where you want your leaves to lay. Place a small dab of hot glue on the stem and insert it into the wreath.
      </p>
      <p>
       Always lay your arrangement out first before gluing to your grapevine wreath. Its hard to undo something you have hot glued without tearing a bit of the wreath.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://parentpretty.com/how-to-make-a-fall-grapevine-wreath/fallwreathleaves/" rel="attachment wp-att-204">
        <img alt="Fall Wreath Leaves" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-204" height="421" src="http://parentpretty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/FallWreathLeaves.jpg" width="660"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Place a dab of hot glue on the stem of your main flower and insert it into the grapevine wreath. Now is the time to use your hot glue gun to secure anything that may be out of place. It helps to reinforce it a bit so that if you hang the wreath outdoors, the wind doesnt blow your flowers away.
      </p>
      <p>
       Cut a piece of the brown ribbon to tie around your wreath. The length should be determined by personal preference, as well as what you will be hanging it from. Tie the ribbon around the wreath and make a small bow at the top.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://parentpretty.com/how-to-make-a-fall-grapevine-wreath/fall-wreath/" rel="attachment wp-att-205">
        <img alt="Fall Wreath" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-205" height="430" src="http://parentpretty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Fall-Wreath.jpg" width="660"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       Hang your grapevine wreath up and enjoy it!
      </p>
      <p>
       If you want to save your grapevine wreath for many years, wrap it in tissue paper and store in a cool, dry place.
      </p>
