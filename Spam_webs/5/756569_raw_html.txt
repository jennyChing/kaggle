     <h1 itemprop="headline">
      Greenhouse Lunching &amp; Offbeat Hunting in East London
     </h1>
    </header>
    <div class="meta left">
     <!-- Author avatar -->
     <p class="avatar">
     </p>
     <!-- Author name -->
     <p class="author">
      By
      <a href="http://www.messynessychic.com/author/vanessa/" rel="author">
       MessyNessy
      </a>
     </p>
     <!-- Date published -->
     <p class="date">
      27th Apr, 2015
     </p>
     <!-- Comment count -->
     <a class="comments" href="#comments">
      <span class="fa fa-comment">
      </span>
      <fb:comments-count href="http://www.messynessychic.com/2015/04/27/greenhouse-lunching-offbeat-hunting-in-east-london/">
      </fb:comments-count>
     </a>
    </div>
    <div class="content left" itemprop="articleBody">
     <p>
      <img alt="exmouthmarket" class="size-full wp-image-64633" height="518" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/exmouthmarket.jpg" width="735"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <em>
       <strong>
        T
       </strong>
      </em>
      ucked away in an overlooked neighbourhoodof East London, quieter and smallerthan Shoreditch but with the potential to outshine Brick Lane, Exmouth Market is an unsung gem of the city, a failsafe foodie stomping ground, safeguarded by locals and creative start-ups who set up shop in the hood over the last decade.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64640" height="1277" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth-930x1277.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      I had made a reservation for lunch just behind the market but the street food stands set up just early enough to make meseriously consider whether having two lunches was an acceptable thing to do.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64634" height="698" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/meathead-930x698.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with e3 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64641" height="930" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth3-930x930.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      The German BBQ almost had me.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with m5 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64642" height="929" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth1-930x929.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      This seafood restaurant, with its charming terrace had me asking, fish wouldnt count as an extra meal, would it?
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with e3 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64643" height="698" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth2-930x698.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Luckily there wasnt
      <em>
       just
      </em>
      food to distract me while I waited for midday to strike
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64644" height="1240" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth4-930x1240.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      But then I finally caved for this Tiffany blue Provenale caf and ordereda late breakfast. But breakfast hour quickly turned into lunch and we scurried off behind Exmouth Market, past the pretty neighbourhoodgreen, arriving at the destination we had so faithfully denied ourselves all that street food for
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64645" height="1240" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth7-930x1240.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      I think youknow how fond I am of greenhouses,so when I heard there was one I could have lunch in, I made sure to be the first oneto arrive on my first Spring day in London at
      <a href="http://bandhbuildings.com/" target="_blank">
       <strong>
        Bourne &amp; Hollingsworth Buildings
       </strong>
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="IMG_6747" class="size-large wp-image-64658" height="1240" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_6747-930x1240.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      I dont know how much I really need to say here to convince you that thisBritish brasserie is just an absolute dreamboat, from the Beverly Hills Hotel upholstered chairs to the transparent fireplaces and the white-washed walls of an old converted warehouse.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64647" height="698" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth6-930x698.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      (Always make early midday-12.15pm reservations if you want to make the most of your lunch and its surroundings without the crowds).
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64648" height="698" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth14-930x698.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      We sunk into our armchairs and knew wed come to the right place.
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64649" height="1240" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth13-930x1240.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with e6 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64650" height="698" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth10-930x698.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      He got the fancy fish &amp; chips, battered tempura cod served on a hash brown and mushy garden peas
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64651" height="698" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth11-930x698.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      I got the pumpkin chicken curry with crunchy rice
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64652" height="1240" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth12-930x1240.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      Of course I had to show him howto do the perfect fish &amp; chips forkful!
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64653" height="698" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth8-930x698.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      I could have stayed here all day, sampling everythingon the menu in a different corner of the restaurant. Its the Goldilocks in me.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with m5 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64654" height="698" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth9-930x698.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64655" height="698" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/xmouth15-930x698.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      But eventually, we left our little Exmouth market haven and headed off for Shoreditch
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" class="size-full wp-image-64659" height="1280" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/princlet.jpg" width="922"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      And headed straight to Princelet Street to check inon my favourite historic buildings in the city
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with e3 preset" class="size-large wp-image-64660" height="698" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_6745-930x698.jpg" width="930"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Im back in Paris now but Ill have more London tips for you this week
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <img alt="Processed with VSCOcam with e8 preset" class="size-full wp-image-64662" height="1280" src="http://static.messynessychic.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/IMG_6746_Fotor.jpg" width="910"/>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      In the meantime, you can read more about these wonderful old East London buildings
      <a href="http://www.messynessychic.com/2015/02/10/borrow-this-east-london-time-capsule-house/" target="_blank">
       <strong>
        here
       </strong>
      </a>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>

     </p>
