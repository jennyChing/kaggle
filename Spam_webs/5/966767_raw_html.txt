     <h2 class="title">
      Obamas hunger knows no bounds
     </h2>
     <div class="entry clearfix">
      <img alt="LutJAoS" class="alignleft featured_image wp-post-image" height="225" src="http://todaysinquirer.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/LutJAoS-300x264.jpg" width="256"/>
      <p>
       The Great Devourer makes headlines once again. He has been quoted as statingIm going to get something to eat. National and international news have exploded with this earth shattering news story, warning thepublic to lock their doors and hide their children and small pets. Fears arise that this may be a repeat of the dark feast of 2013 in which Obama was accused of devouring over 200 small children. The evidence was too flimsy though and he was never properly linked to the tragedy. Possibly he never will be since he is protected in his seat of power by most of the government.
      </p>
      <p>
       Speculation has arisen that Obama is in fact aRakshasa, a type of man eating demon capable of transforming to human form. The Rakshasa originated in Hindu myth and later in Buddhism. Their origins can be traced back to the
       <span style="color: #252525;">
        Rig Veda where they are classified asYatudhanas, or demons that consume men. Gathering evidence to support this theory has proven difficult due to the high level of security surrounding the president.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       Obamas staff have stated categorically that he is a normal human being. A spokesperson contacted us to say Obama is 100% normal. A true red blooded American., but the fear in his eyes spoke a different truth. Is it possible that any that speak out against him are summarily dismissed, even, devoured themselves? Here at Todays Inquirer we are not afraid and we pledge to get to the bottom of this.
      </p>
      <p>
       Obama supporters have spoke out against what they are calling a witch hunt. A small group of very vocal supporters claim that the idea of Obama eating children is ridiculous and even the act of reporting that he is going to get something to eat is notnewsworthy. Our own investigative reporters claim that these naysayers are simply part of the elaborate conspiracy used to protect the president and his nightly hunts.
      </p>
