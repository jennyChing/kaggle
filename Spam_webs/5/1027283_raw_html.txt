       Blush Response 021  Tropical Nebula
      </h1>
      <div class="meta">

        </time>

      <article class="article wi-single post-6116 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-biorequiem" id="post-6116">
       <div class="post-content">
        <!--	//////////////////	CONTENT		///////////////////		-->
        <p>
         <img alt="Blush Response" src="http://www.biorequiem.com/wp-content/titles/blushresponse.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Todays eye makeup is inspired by nebulae and the tropics; a scorching mix of hot pink,   orange and bright yellow, which Ive been wanting to showcase for a while now.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6201" height="130" src="http://www.biorequiem.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Orange_pink_yellow1.jpg" title="OLYMPUS DIGITAL CAMERA" width="520"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Shooting Haute Macabres
         <a href="http://www.rocklove.com/haute-macabre/haute-macabre-inverted-cross-chain-earrings" target="_blank" title="HM+RL Earrings">
          new
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.rocklove.com/haute-macabre/haute-macabre-lariat" target="_blank" title="HM + RL Lariat Necklace">
          collaboration
         </a>
         with   RockLove Jewelry made for a good enough reason to do this. Besides, its summer, and a section of my hair is a complementary purple at the moment  all stars aligned, so to speak. This dramatic look is easy and fast, because well be using a large eyeshadow brush for max coverage and blending power.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6118" height="780" src="http://www.biorequiem.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Phoenix_EYE_small2.jpg" title="Phoenix_EYE_small2" width="520"/>
         <br/>
         <small>
          Makeup, styling and photos by me
         </small>
        </p>
        <p>
         Yes, this
         <em>
          is
         </em>
         the
         <a href="../blush-response/blush-response-004-strung-out-on-sugarpill/" target="_blank">
          third
         </a>
         <a href="../blush-response/blush-response-016-blazing-eyes/" target="_blank">
          tutorial
         </a>
         featuring warm eye shadow colors. Not in a row, but still. I vow to do a series of cool palette looks, starting with sea foam greens, next. Onward!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6120" height="341" src="http://www.biorequiem.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Phoenix_EYE_small4.jpg" title="Phoenix_EYE_small4" width="520"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          TOOLS
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <a href="http://www.sugarpillshop.com/" target="_blank">
           Sugarpill
          </a>
          eyeshadows: Dollipop, Flamepoint, Buttercupcake, Goldilux
         </li>
         <li>
          Mac eyeshadow in Contrast for the brows. Not pictured: an angled brush for eyebrows
         </li>
         <li>
          Cover Girl Lash Blast Fusion Mascara
         </li>
         <li>
          A large eyeshadow brush
         </li>
         <li>
          Eyeshadow sponge applicator
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6117" height="353" src="http://www.biorequiem.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Phoenix_EYE_small.jpg" title="Phoenix_EYE_small" width="520"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          METHOD
         </strong>
        </p>
        <ol>
         <li>
          After your base and brows are on, begin by applying Dollipop to the outer edges of the crease and eye with the large brush, blending outward, and contouring the crease.
         </li>
         <li>
          Shake off excess eyeshadow, and with the same brush apply Flamepoint to the middle of the eyelid, blending upward toward the brow and a bit over Dollipop, too
         </li>
         <li>
          Apply Buttercupcake to the inner corner of the eye, blending a little with Flamepoint and pushing the color up to the inner edge of brow and down around the inner eye corner, about 2/3rds of the way around the bottom of the eye
         </li>
         <li>
          With a damp sponge applicator, dab a small amount of Goldilux under, and in the inner corners of the eye. Pull a bit of this color along the upper lash line.
         </li>
         <li>
          Mascara, 3 coats
         </li>
        </ol>
        <p>
         I topped the look off with my trusty clear
         <a href="http://wnwbeauty.com/Product.php?Pid=54" target="_blank">
          Glassy Gloss
         </a>
         on the lips. Done!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6119" height="278" src="http://www.biorequiem.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Phoenix_EYE_small3.jpg" title="Phoenix_EYE_small3" width="520"/>
        </p>
