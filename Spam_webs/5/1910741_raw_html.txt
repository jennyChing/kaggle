        <h2>
         Empty rooms transformed into dancing displays of light. Stop and stare for a minute and feel a sense of calm washing over you.
        </h2>
        <p>
         <span class="embed-youtube" style="text-align:center; display: block;">
          <iframe allowfullscreen="true" class="youtube-player" frameborder="0" height="390" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/RfUCouLX8-Y?version=3&amp;rel=1&amp;fs=1&amp;showsearch=0&amp;showinfo=1&amp;iv_load_policy=1&amp;wmode=transparent" type="text/html" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         Julio Le Parcs use of light and reflections to create wall sized interactive pieces of art is something everyone can enjoy viewing no matter what age you are.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Argentines
         <a href="http://www.serpentinegalleries.org/exhibitions-events/julio-le-parc" target="_blank" title="Julio le parc exhibition">
          first UK solo retrospective
         </a>
         is both simple and clever in equal measure.
        </p>
        <p>
         Le Parcs skill is two fold. Firstly, the arrangement of materials to create these pieces of work that play on infinity. The second being the play of light itself as the outcome, art in two dimensions.
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_1994" style="width: 650px">
         <img alt="Julio Le Parc exhibition, Serpentine Sackler Gallery" class="size-medium wp-image-1994" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.imwanderingandpositive.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Julio-Le-Parc-exhibition-Serpentine-Sackler-Gallery.jpg?resize=640%2C360"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Julio Le Parc exhibition, Serpentine Sackler Gallery,
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         The exhibition is on display until 15th February at Serpentine Sackler Gallery.
        </p>
