     <h2>
      How to get your Irish Tax Back 2014/2013/2012
     </h2>
     <p>
      Trying to obtain a your taxback for your earnings from Ireland in
      <b>
       2014
      </b>
      or
      <b>
       2013
      </b>
      ? Take a look at each of the following taxation centers to do with those P45's and P60's, so you can claim your taxback back now!
      <br/>
      <br/>
      Remember to include as much information when you are applying for your tax returns, and make copies of the forms you fill out, before you submit them to the
      <a href="http://www.revenue.ie/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="The Irish Revenue Commissioners">
       Revenue Commissioners
      </a>
      who are based in Dublin, but also have offices in Cork, Galway and Limerick.
     </p>
     <h2>
      Taxback Online
     </h2>
     <p>
      You can apply for your
      <b>
       taxback online
      </b>
      via our partner
      <b>
       <a href="http://www.eirjobs.com/taxback/" rel="nofollow" title="Taxback.com online tax returns">
        TaxBack.com
       </a>
      </b>
      .
     </p>
     <p style="margin: 4px; float: right">
      <a href="http://www.eirjobs.com/taxback/" rel="nofollow" title="Taxback.com online tax returns">
       <img alt="the tax process" src="http://www.eirjobs.com/images/irish-tax-back.jpg"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Their service staff will help you fill out the forms and claim your the tax that you are owed, all in a manner that is really friendly and helpful.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      First start off with selecting your nationality, and then the country you want to apply for taxback. You can claim a tax return if you worked in the
      <b>
       United States between 2011 - 2015
      </b>
      .
     </p>
     <p style="margin: 4px; float: left">
      <a href="http://www.eirjobs.com/taxback/" rel="nofollow" title="Taxback.com online tax returns">
       <img alt="the tax process" src="http://www.eirjobs.com/images/irish-tax-back-claim.jpg"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      Then you will be required to fill out some basic details and they will apply on your behalf.
      <br/>
      <br/>
      So there isn't a need to fill out any complex paper work.
      <b>
       <a href="http://www.eirjobs.com/taxback/" rel="nofollow" title="Taxback.com online tax returns">
        TaxBack.com
       </a>
      </b>
      will then track your tax return and will deal with the necessary agencies. There is a $60 charge for Federal returns &amp; $30 for State returns. The average return in the region of
      <strong>
       700/USD$800
      </strong>
      , and for the time and expertise that
      <a href="http://www.eirjobs.com/taxback/" rel="nofollow" title="Taxback.com online tax returns">
       TaxBack.com
      </a>
      performs with Irish Tax Return, we think it is well worth the price
     </p>
     <p>
      <b>
       <a href="http://www.eirjobs.com/taxback/" title="Online tax returns">
        Apply for your tax return NOW !
       </a>
      </b>
     </p>
     <br/>
     <h2>
      Tax from previous years
     </h2>
     <p>
      As
      <a href="http://forums.eirjobs.com/viewtopic.php?t=30" title="tax recalculated">
       posted here
      </a>
      , you can e-mail your local PAYE/tax office and ask them to check your taxes back as far as 2002 (P21 balancing statement) then they will send you out a balancing statement and if they owe you money a check will come before the statement.
     </p>
     <p>
      Use
      <a href="http://www.revenue.ie/index.htm?/locator2.htm" title="locator">
       this link
      </a>
      to find the e-mail address of your loacal tax office, scroll to the bottom of the page where it says "Open Contact Locator and just type in your pps number.
     </p>
