    <h2>
     Recently Answered Questions
    </h2>
    <p class="q">
     <strong>
      Q. How to get missing taskbar icons 

in my Windows XP ?
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p class="a">
     <strong>
      A.
     </strong>
     To get back the icons 

firstly press Windows + {R} key, type "regedit" and press enter. After that 

browse to HKEY_CURRENT_USER\ Software\Classes\Local Settings\Software

\Microsoft\Windows\ CurrentVersion\TrayNotify, and delete both IconStreams 

and PastIconsStream values.Then reboot and login again.
    </p>
    <p class="q">
     <strong>
      Q.
     </strong>
     Why my PC does not 

sleep ?
    </p>
    <p class="a">
     <strong>
      A.
     </strong>
     A PC does not sleep 

becuase a device is configured to bring it out of standby. To identify the 

device. To identify it press Windows + {R} key, type "cmd" and press enter. 

Theny type : powercfg devicequery wake_armed, Now again press Windows + {R} 

key, type "devmgmt.msc" and press enter. Find any devices listed earlier, 

then double-click them and look for an Advanced or Power Management tab.
Check if the device is allowed to bring the PC out of standby  if it is, 

untick all the boxes that wake it. Click OK, close Device Manager and test 

it worked; repeat the process if necessary.
    </p>
