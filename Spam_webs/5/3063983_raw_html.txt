              </a>
             </h1>
             <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-85524687047509212" itemprop="description articleBody">

               <a href="http://www.artpromotivate.com/2012/01/site-tour-artists-promotion-motivate.html" target="_blank" title="art promotion tips">
                art promotion tips
               </a>
               to something much larger. With 8252 plus followers from combined Facebook, Twitter, Google+, Blogger, and RSS subscribers, APM has grown faster than I would ever have imagined.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                I have all of you to thank for the growth of this website, which is why I have decided to dedicate this post to you.
               </strong>
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>
              <p>
               Without readers, there is no purpose in having a blog at all.
               <strong>
                All of you give me the incentive to continually write at Artpromotivate, something which I enjoy doing very much
               </strong>
               . At APM, I post portfolio site reviews,
               <a href="http://www.artpromotivate.com/2012/05/how-to-get-painting-onto-wall-in-room.html" target="_blank" title="Photoshop tutorials">
                Photoshop tutorials
               </a>
               , art promotion tips, and much, much more. As you can see from the archive, the article count is up to 181. The majority of these I have spent countless hours and late nights writing. 40 artists have been interviewed in the
               <a href="http://www.artpromotivate.com/2011/12/submit-to-our-artist-spotlite.html" target="_blank" title="artist spotlight">
                artist spotlight
               </a>
               so far, but there are still lots more to be featured! For those who are wondering when you will be featured, please be patient. I will personally let you know when your spotlight is posted.
              </p>
              <a name="more">
              </a>
              <p>
               Lately, I have been sharing all the tips I can think of to find visitors to your own websites and
               <a href="http://www.artpromotivate.com/2012/05/103-topics-to-write-about-for-art-blogs.html" target="_blank" title="art blogs">
                art blogs
               </a>
               . There is still much to share with you here regarding this topic and others, so I will let you all know the various ways you can connect with Artpromotivate - so certain helpful posts will not be missed.
              </p>
              <p>
               <strong>
                I am always interested in hearing what you have to say
               </strong>
               . So, if you have anything to share, or any suggestions at all, please leave a comment below this post.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

               Ways to Stay Connected with Artpromotivate Website Updates
              </h3>

                 </strong>
                </a>

                 When signing up, wait for an email with a confirmation link
                </strong>
                . Click the link in this email to complete the signup process.
                <br/>
                <br/>

                <br/>
               </li>

                <h4>
                 Connect by RSS
                </h4>
                <img align="right" alt="rss subscribe" border="0" height="128" src="http://lh5.ggpht.com/-9x3AKsEtr88/T7nCOVGO1ZI/AAAAAAAAFcg/kfbiUNBIIq0/rss-subscribe%25255B4%25255D.png?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; float: right; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="rss subscribe" width="128"/>

                 RSS feed
                </a>
