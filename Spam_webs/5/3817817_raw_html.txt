Usually we are pretty good at not losing the pieces, but there have been a few that have disappeared (with the dog&#8217;s help). Like the crafting hoarder that I am, I&#8217;ve saved them, waiting for the perfect craft. This week we recycled the puzzle piecesinto this pretty &lt;strong&gt;puzzle piece wreath ornament&lt;/strong&gt;!&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;a

(After all, glitter makes it all better!)&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;Then we glued on small red beads.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;Once it was dry, we tied on a piece of red yarn to hang it with and glued on a small red bow.&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;a
