      <h1 class="single-title">
       Redheads Are Monsters! (5 Things To Know)
      </h1>
     </header>
     <!-- .entry-header -->
     <div id="list">
      <article class="post-42252 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-entertainment-lists category-lists tag-crime tag-ginger tag-red-hair tag-red-head tag-roofie tag-witch tag-zombie" id="post-42252">
       <div class="entry-meta">
        <span class="category">
         <ul class="post-categories">
          <li>
           <a href="http://regretfulmorning.com/category/lists/entertainment-lists/" rel="category tag">
            Entertainment
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://regretfulmorning.com/category/lists/" rel="category tag">
            Lists
           </a>
          </li>
         </ul>
        </span>
        <span class="author">
         By
         <a href="http://regretfulmorning.com/author/matt-pass/" rel="author" title="Posts by Matt Pass">
          Matt Pass
         </a>
        </span>
        <span class="date">
         November 22, 2013
        </span>
        <span class="post-views">
         1892 Views
        </span>
       </div>
       <!-- .entry-meta -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_42288" style="width: 611px">
         <img alt="Shit hit the fan and she was standing right next to the fan." class="size-full wp-image-42288 " height="381" src="http://regretfulmorning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/freckled-e1385074271299.jpg" width="601"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Shit hit the fan and she was standing right next to the fan.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         We live in a touchy world that at least
         <em>
          tries
         </em>
         to be politically correct. We know that its in poor taste to mock or discriminate against somebody for their gender, color, sexual preference, religion, etc. The list goes on and on. One thing
         <em>
          not
         </em>
         on the list, however, is hair color. If you see some asshole with bright green hair, you have every right to tell him that he is an asshole with bright green hair (though he likely already knows one or both of those things).
        </p>
        <p>
         Which brings us to redheads, or, gingers. Theyre monsters. Straight up evil monsters. And Im not just saying that because one broke my heart (
         <em>
          OKAY
         </em>
         , Elaina!?).
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          <strong>
           Theyre Witches, According To Ancient Text
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          <strong>
           <img alt="witch" class="size-full wp-image-42255 aligncenter" height="200" src="http://regretfulmorning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/witch-e1385078014455.jpg" width="500"/>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         The
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.bibliotecapleyades.net/cienciareal/cienciareal12.htm">
           Malleus Maleficarium
          </a>
         </em>
         is likely the most famous and influential book youve never heard of. It was published in 1486, and it was considered to be just as true as the bible. So what does the name mean? Witch Hammer. It was a guidebook for identifying and
         <del>
          burning
         </del>
         politely dealing with witches (so actually a little truer than the Bible).
        </p>
        <p>
         The main way to determine a witch was by her red hair as well as green eyes and often left-handednessuncommon traits essentially. No word on if being gay or colorblind makes you a witch.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_42254" style="width: 358px">
         <img alt="Pictured: Something we should definitely kill." class="size-full wp-image-42254" height="217" src="http://regretfulmorning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/red-hair-green-eyes.jpg" width="348"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Pictured: Something we should definitely kill with fire.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          <strong>
           Half Of All Female Criminals Have Red Hair
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_42259" style="width: 510px">
         <img alt="And dyeing it won't help, either." class="size-full wp-image-42259 " height="497" src="http://regretfulmorning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/lohan-mug-shot-e1384986945840.jpg" width="500"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          And dyeing it wont help either, Lindsay.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         In their book
         <em>
          Criminal Woman, the Prostitute, and the Normal Woman
         </em>
         , Cesare Lombroso and Guglielmo Ferrero confirm that
         <a href="http://books.google.com/books?id=S3g29DlTxNsC&amp;printsec=frontcover&amp;dq=criminal+woman,+the+prostitute,+and+the+normal+woman&amp;hl=en&amp;sa=X&amp;ei=Arh-Uu7GDbas4AOMhIH4BQ&amp;ved=0CC8Q6AEwAA#v=onepage&amp;q=criminal%20woman%2C%20the%20prostitute%2C%20and%20the%20normal%20woman&amp;f=false">
          48% of female criminals have red hair
         </a>
         . Also that one guys name has to be pronounced as googly Elmo, but were here to make fun of hair color, not country of origin.
        </p>
        <p>
         While this statistic refers specifically to women, we must keep in mind that red headed males often transform into a leprechaun to evade police, as seen in the 1993 documentary
         <em>
          Leprechaun
         </em>
         (
         <a href="http://regretfulmorning.com/2013/10/famous-actors-who-started-in-horror/">
          <em>
           the
          </em>
          Jennifer Anistons acting debut
         </a>
         ).
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_42264" style="width: 310px">
         <img alt="Of course, we can't forget the Gingerdead Man." class="size-full wp-image-42264" height="267" src="http://regretfulmorning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/gingerdead-man-e1384990747637.jpg" width="300"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          They can also turn into cookies or whatever.
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: large;">
          <strong>
           Their Weaknesses
          </strong>
         </span>
         <br/>
         <img alt="skull" class="size-full wp-image-42294 aligncenter" height="188" src="http://regretfulmorning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/skull-e1385077626252.png" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Despite clearly not being made for sunny weather, red heads more sensitive to cold than others. This is apparent from the bright red nose they sport in the winter time, making them look like well, I cant actually think of any famous characters that are red-nosed.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_42253" style="width: 449px">
         <img alt="Petey the Pink-nosed Pig?" class="size-full wp-image-42253" height="222" src="http://regretfulmorning.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/cold-pig-nose-e1384986653617.jpg" width="439"/>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Petey the Pink-nosed Pig?
         </p>
        </div>
        <p>
         They also bruise easily, which is common knowledge to anyone who has ever had a redheaded step-son.
         <br/>
         Additionally, they are especially prone to
         <a href="http://sciencenordic.com/redheads-feel-different-kind-pain">
          fear going to the dentist
         </a>
         . Wait, what? Cmon. Dentists arent that badthey do give you anesthesia after all. What are you red heads, immune to anesthetics?
        </p>
        <p>
