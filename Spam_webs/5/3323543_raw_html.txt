                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     <a href="http://cardiokickboxingatlanta.blogspot.com/2014/05/workout-partner.html">
                      Workout Partner
                     </a>
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5620955931594090" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <br/>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
                      <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
                       <span style="mso-tab-count: 1;">
                       </span>
                       <span style="mso-tab-count: 2;">
                       </span>
                       <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
                        <span style="font-size: 18pt; line-height: 115%;">
                         <span style="mso-tab-count: 1;">
                         </span>
                         <span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">
                         </span>
                         Workout Partner
                         <o:p>
                         </o:p>
                        </span>
                       </b>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
                      <o:p>
                       <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
                       </span>
                      </o:p>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
                      <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
                       <span style="mso-tab-count: 1;">
                       </span>
                       A great
way to stick to working out and having a healthy lifestyle is to have a partner
in crime! Have you ever caught yourself just having an off day and you just dont
feel like going to the gym? Well thats where your workout partner comes into
play. They will make you go because you have committed to them as well to go and
they know it benefits the both of you. A great place to be able to come and
have a lot of workout partners is ILoveKickboxing Atlanta. Its a great place
to form friendships and be somewhere that people all have goals they are trying
to accomplish.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-yJgd__vO9dg/U3D8GstOuDI/AAAAAAAAACE/vk_ycxnHn74/s1600/ilkb+people.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="257" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-yJgd__vO9dg/U3D8GstOuDI/AAAAAAAAACE/vk_ycxnHn74/s1600/ilkb+people.JPG" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
                      <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
                       <o:p>
                       </o:p>
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0in 0in 10pt;">
                      <span style="font-family: Calibri;">
                       <span style="mso-tab-count: 1;">
                       </span>
                       Goals
are obviously very important to you as well as your partner. So, you do want to
share each others goals that way you are held accountable to them. Its good
to have each other involved as much as possible. You can even take it a step
further than just workout partners, make them your go to person for anything
that you need to help in reaching your goals. This means if youre walking past
an ice cream shop and you see a really delicious chocolate fudge sunday and you
are just so tempted to go in and get it, call your other half so they can help
talk you out of it. ILoveKickboxing Atlantas instructors are here for you as
well to help you get over whatever struggles you have. Stick to what youre
doing and believe in it with your awesome workout friend. The greatest point
that you will get to is when you can share the accomplishments you have made
and thank each other for sticking it through with one another. Also, having
that awesome workout that you love coming to always helps! Come check out
ILoveKickboxing Atlanta.
                      </span>
