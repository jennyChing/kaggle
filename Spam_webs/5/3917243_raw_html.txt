                  <span style="font-size: large;">
                   <span style="color: red;">
                    Method 1 (Safe Mode)
                   </span>
                  </span>
                 </b>

                 <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                  <img border="0" height="320" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-rlTkpbKr-bs/VHaqZZ5TJII/AAAAAAAADP8/_ddSqs3GzKc/s1600/safe-mode-zenfone.jpg" width="192"/>

                  <li>
                   Fix the Problem by deleting the cause of Bootloop
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Restart your Zenfone, and see your bootloop is fixed or not.
                  </li>
                 </ol>

                 <b>
