  <meta content="I have a confession  I didnt go to either one of my graduations
(bachelors or masters degrees). You know what? I regret it! At the
time I didnt realize what a big deal it is to graduate, but lets face
it: its a lot of hard work. And that goes for everything from preschool
on up. Celebrate the special moments the way that they deserve. To pair
with Chelseys graduation fortune cookie project, this DIY graduation
frame is one of those creative ways to celebrate. You can make this as a
centerpiece for the special grads gift table, and then display it on
your mantel afterwards. And lets face it, a frame is so versatile! You
can customize for nearly every occasion using papers from a Snapfish
party kit, which is what I did. Learn how to make this cute frame below.
Supplies HP Officejet Pro 8600 AiO Printer + Original HP Ink Tip: You
need both Original HP Ink and photo paper for this project. Our Ink and
Paper Value Packs supply both ink and photo paper! HP Premium Inkjet
Matte Presentation Paper Snapfish Party Kits  I used the Reach for the
Stars collection 12 x 12 MDF wood frame Mod Podge Matte Mod Podge
Rocks adhesive stencil  Starlite Acrylic paint  metallic peach and
white Spray primer  white Embroidery floss  1 skein of gold and 2
skeins of whatever color you would like your tassel Gold ribbon  2
long Piece of cardboard  10 long Foam spouncer Craft knife or scissors
Paintbrush Hole punch Hot glue gun TIP #1: If you intend to print out
your childs graduation photo and put in your handmade frame, check out
the available HP combo packs  you can get color inks, photo paper for
printing, envelopes and a project idea book! Step 1 I always prime MDF
wood before I paint it  the primer soaks into the surface and prevents
you from having to use too much acrylic paint. Prime all sides of your
frame, both the front and the back and let it dry. Then paint the entire
thing with a few coats of white acrylic paint. Let dry. Step 2 Remove
your adhesive stencil from the backer and center on the left (wider)
portion of the frame. Smooth down. Step 3 Use your spouncer to apply the
metallic paint. Once you are finished, peel up the stencil immediately
and rinse. Return it to the backer. Let the paint on the frame dry, and
let the stencil dry too. When both are completely dry, stencil the frame
again in the same manner to finish the pattern. Step 4 While your frame
is drying, make your tassel. Get a strip of cardboard about 10 long and
wind both skeins of your main color around it. TIP #2: A cereal box
works well to create a tassel. Step 5 Take your gold embroidery floss
and tie it into a knot at the top of the tassel. Then use the scissors
to cut the embroidery floss in the same place at the bottom. Step 6 Use
either a piece of gold ribbon or some of your gold embroidery floss to
secure the tassel at the top. If you use ribbon, hot glue to secure. If
you use embroidery floss, tie around several times and knot at the back.
Step 7 Lay your tassel down over the stenciled part of the frame and
trim the bottom to size with scissors. I cant believe how much it looks
like a real tassel! Step 8 Print out the party tags, napkin rings, and
water bottle labels onto your HP Premium Inkjet Matte Presentation
Paper. Cut out your favorite color tag and Congrats Grad saying with
scissors. Also choose a napkin ring to go below the frame opening. Step
9 Punch a hole in the top of the tag and string onto the gold embroidery
floss at the top of the tassel. Step 10 Use Mod Podge to adhere
Congrats Grad to the top of the frame, and the napkin ring of your
choice to the bottom. Let dry. I chose the You Made It napkin ring for
the bottom of the frame. Step 11 Coat the entire front of the frame with
Mod Podge. Let dry. Step 12 Hot glue the gold embroidery thread that
holds the tassel to the back top of the frame. TIP #3: If you want to
attach your (real) graduation tassel to the frame, you can tie it to
gold embroidery floss and attach to the back in a similar fashion.
Insert an image of your favorite graduate. If you print your image, do
it on HPs Glossy Photo Card Paper  this premium paper ensures vivid
colors, and your graduation photo will look its best! Id love for you
to make this project too and share it in our Idea Exchange. Share a
picture of it at the party table!" property="og:description"/>

                                 <div class="lia-message-body-content">
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10366i7D1EB293D9304A86/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                    <br/>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p align="center">
                                   <strong>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   I have a confession  I didnt go to either one of my graduations (bachelors or masters degrees). You know what? I regret it! At the time I didnt realize what a big deal it is to graduate, but lets face it: its a lot of hard work. And that goes for everything from preschool on up. Celebrate the special moments the way that they deserve. To pair with Chelseys graduation fortune cookie project, this DIY graduation frame is one of those creative ways to celebrate. You can make this as a centerpiece for the special grads gift table, and then display it on your mantel afterwards. And lets face it, a frame is so versatile! You can customize for nearly every occasion using papers from a
                                   <a href="http://owl.li/tL0ca" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                                    Snapfish party kit
                                   </a>
                                   , which is what I did. Learn how to make this cute frame below.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10368i15BB0A08CC63A63B/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Supplies
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <ul>
                                   <li>
                                    <a href="http://www.shopping.hp.com/en_US/home-office/-/products/Printers/HP-Officejet-Pro/CM749A?jumpid=SC_R11513_Li_HPCreate_officejet-rpo" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                                     HP Officejet Pro 8600 AiO Printer
                                    </a>
                                    +
                                    <a href="http://shopping.hp.com/en_US/home-office/-/products/Ink_Toner_Paper/Ink_Toner_Paper?jumpid=sc_R11513_Li_HPCreate_Ink_Toner_Paper" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                                     Original HP Ink
                                    </a>
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Tip: You need both Original HP Ink and photo paper for this project. Our
                                    <a href="http://www.shopping.hp.com/en_US/home-office/-/products/Ink_Toner_Paper/Ink_Toner_Paper?SearchParameter=%26%40QueryTerm%3D*%26CategoryUUIDLevelX%3DcsYQxXjbOdEAAAE0GydcZzPI%26inktype_dte2%3DInk%2Band%2BPaper%2BValue%2BPacks%26%40Sort.prodname%3D0&amp;PageSize=15&amp;jumpid=cp_r163_us/en/ips/print_relevance/index/buynow&amp;jumpid=sc_R11513_Li_HPCreate_Ink_Paper_Value_Pack" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
                                     Ink and Paper Value Packs
                                    </a>
                                    supply both ink and photo paper!
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    <a href="http://www.shopping.hp.com/en_US/home-office/-/products/HP-Paper/Brochure-and-Presentation-paper/D0Z55A?HP-Premium-Inkjet-Matte-Presentation-Paper-120-gsm-100-sht-Letter-8.5-x-11-in&amp;jumpid=sc_R11513_Li_HPCreate_Premium_Inkjet_Matte_Presentation_Paper" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                                     HP Premium Inkjet Matte Presentation Paper
                                    </a>
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    <a href="http://owl.li/tL0ca" rel="nofollow" target="_self">
                                     Snapfish Party Kits
                                    </a>
                                     I used the Reach for the Stars collection
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    12 x 12 MDF wood frame
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Mod Podge Matte
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Mod Podge Rocks adhesive stencil  Starlite
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Acrylic paint  metallic peach and white
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Spray primer  white
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Embroidery floss  1 skein of gold and 2 skeins of whatever color you would like your tassel
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Gold ribbon  2 long
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Piece of cardboard  10 long
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Foam spouncer
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Craft knife or scissors
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Paintbrush
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Hole punch
                                   </li>
                                   <li>
                                    Hot glue gun
                                   </li>
                                  </ul>
                                  <p>
                                   <em>
                                    TIP #1: If you intend to print out your childs graduation photo and put in your handmade frame, check out the available
                                   </em>
                                   <a href="http://www.shopping.hp.com/en_US/home-office/-/products/Ink_Toner_Paper/Ink_Toner_Paper?SearchParameter=%26%40QueryTerm%3D*%26CategoryUUIDLevelX%3DcsYQxXjbOdEAAAE0GydcZzPI%26inktype_dte2%3DInk%2Band%2BPaper%2BValue%2BPacks%26%40Sort.prodname%3D0&amp;PageSize=15&amp;jumpid=cp_r163_us/en/ips/print_relevance/index/buynow&amp;jumpid=sc_R11513_Li_HPCreate_Ink_Paper_Value_Pack" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                                    <em>
                                     HP combo packs
                                    </em>
                                   </a>
                                   <em>
                                     you can get color inks, photo paper for printing, envelopes and a project idea book!
                                   </em>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <em>
                                   </em>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10370iE789FA7DE82882F3/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 1
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   I always prime MDF wood before I paint it  the primer soaks into the surface and prevents you from having to use too much acrylic paint. Prime all sides of your frame, both the front and the back and let it dry. Then paint the entire thing with a few coats of white acrylic paint. Let dry.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10372iB7E2C7E157B761C2/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 2
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Remove your adhesive stencil from the backer and center on the left (wider) portion of the frame. Smooth down.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10374i7DA8C90004C2E6BE/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 3
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Use your spouncer to apply the metallic paint. Once you are finished, peel up the stencil immediately and rinse. Return it to the backer. Let the paint on the frame dry, and let the stencil dry too. When both are completely dry, stencil the frame again in the same manner to finish the pattern.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10376i14A8383909F646EF/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 4
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   While your frame is drying, make your tassel. Get a strip of cardboard about 10 long and wind
                                   <u>
                                    both
                                   </u>
                                   skeins of your main color around it.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <em>
                                    TIP #2: A cereal box works well to create a tassel.
                                   </em>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <em>
                                   </em>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10378i419135EDB29FE2FC/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 5
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Take your gold embroidery floss and tie it into a knot at the top of the tassel. Then use the scissors to cut the embroidery floss in the same place at the bottom.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10380i579262268B139ACC/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 6
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Use either a piece of gold ribbon or some of your gold embroidery floss to secure the tassel at the top. If you use ribbon, hot glue to secure. If you use embroidery floss, tie around several times and knot at the back.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10382i3C3F0A8A69AF0139/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 7
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Lay your tassel down over the stenciled part of the frame and trim the bottom to size with scissors. I cant believe how much it looks like a real tassel!
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10384i63F78BB02135E496/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 8
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Print out the party tags, napkin rings, and water bottle labels onto your
                                   <a href="http://www.shopping.hp.com/en_US/home-office/-/products/HP-Paper/Brochure-and-Presentation-paper/D0Z55A?HP-Premium-Inkjet-Matte-Presentation-Paper-120-gsm-100-sht-Letter-8.5-x-11-in&amp;jumpid=sc_R11513_Li_HPCreate_Premium_Inkjet_Matte_Presentation_Paper" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                                    HP Premium Inkjet Matte Presentation Paper
                                   </a>
                                   . Cut out your favorite color tag and Congrats Grad saying with scissors. Also choose a napkin ring to go below the frame opening.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10386i50D2EE476E1CEC25/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 9
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Punch a hole in the top of the tag and string onto the gold embroidery floss at the top of the tassel.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10388i3DF047B1FEAF0732/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 10
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Use Mod Podge to adhere Congrats Grad to the top of the frame, and the napkin ring of your choice to the bottom. Let dry.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   I chose the You Made It napkin ring for the bottom of the frame.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10390i6250430BB5C51166/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 11
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Coat the entire front of the frame with Mod Podge. Let dry.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10392i59D055A2C1EF4F93/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <strong>
                                    Step 12
                                   </strong>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Hot glue the gold embroidery thread that holds the tassel to the back top of the frame.
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <em>
                                    TIP #3: If you want to attach your (real) graduation tassel to the frame, you can tie it to gold embroidery floss and attach to the back in a similar fashion.
                                   </em>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   <img align="center" alt="HP Create Graduation Frame " border="0" src="/t5/image/serverpage/image-id/10394iEA984BC0EB74E86A/image-size/original?v=mpbl-1&amp;px=-1" title="HP Create Graduation Frame "/>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                   Insert an image of your favorite graduate. If you print your image, do it on
                                   <a href="http://shopping.hp.com/en_US/home-office/-/products/HP-Paper/Premium-photo-paper/D2W45A?HP-Glossy-Photo-Cards-10-sht-5-x-7-in-with-envelopes&amp;jumpid=sc_R11513_Li_HPCreate_Glossy_Photo_Cards_10_sht" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                                    HPs Glossy Photo Card Paper
                                   </a>
                                    this premium paper ensures vivid colors, and your graduation photo will look its best
                                   <span>
                                    ! Id love for you to make this project too and share it in our
                                   </span>
                                   <a href="http://owl.li/t7Sil" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
                                    Idea Exchange
                                   </a>
                                   <span>
                                    . Share a picture of it at the party table!
                                   </span>
                                  </p>
                                  <p>
                                  </p>
