          <p>
           <a href="http://pastorplanner">

          </p>
          <p>
           You see, I have yet to find a planner that is organized in a way that helps me, a visual person. I have to see the year, the month, and week. I have had planners that highlighted the month, but not weeks. I have had planners that highlighted weeks, but not the months.
          </p>
          <p>
           At the same time, each planner was blank, leaving me the responsibility of customizing it. To top that off, the last planner I bought had stickers for weddings, and those stickers included gay marriage.
          </p>
          <p>
           I had it!
          </p>

           </li>
