             I am now going to start walking my 'process'. This allows me to show for myself who I really am and who I have become as life. I think that for the most part I am here because of my bad choices in the past. Now that I have accepted and allowed myself to make bad choices, I forgive myself for this. I think that it is important for me to stay on my 'program', because it gives me a sense of purpose and allows me to actually live as all as One as Equal. I find that very facisinating because in this I have shown myself to myself who I actually am, and have the compassion and ability to live as this. I think it is the best for me to suggest, it's done. I know lots of groups are living this task but I would like to start by saying thanks.
