     <h2>
      Banana Nutella Swirl Cake with Nutella Frosting
     </h2>
     <div class="postmeta">
      <em>
       by
      </em>
      Two Peas
      <em>
       on
      </em>
      September 17, 2012
     </div>
     <p>
      <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13148" height="640" src="http://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/banana-nutella-cake4.jpg" title="Banana Nutella Swirl Cake with Nutella Frosting from www.twopeasandtheirpod.com #recipe #Nutella" width="450"/>
       <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com/banana-nutella-swirl-cake-with-nutella-frosting/&amp;media=http://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/banana-nutella-cake4.jpg&amp;description=Banana Nutella Swirl Cake with Nutella Frosting')">
       </span>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      The brown bananas on the counter got me again. They always sneak up on me! I made a loaf of
      <a href="http://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com/buttermilk-banana-bread/" target="_blank">
       Buttermilk Banana Bread
      </a>
      , but still had three bananas on the counter staring me down. They were begging to be used. I decided to make a Banana Nutella Swirl Cake with Nutella Frosting. Bananas and Nutella are a match made in heaven and this cake certainly is heavenly!
     </p>
     <p>
      <span id="more-13147">
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="javascript:void((function(){var%20e=document.createElement('script');e.setAttribute('type','text/javascript');e.setAttribute('charset','UTF-8');e.setAttribute('src','http://assets.pinterest.com/js/pinmarklet.js?r='+Math.random()*99999999);document.body.appendChild(e)})());">
       <img alt="" src="http://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/pinit.png" style="border: none;"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
       <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13149" height="427" src="http://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/banana-nutella-cake1.jpg" title="Banana Nutella Swirl Cake with Nutella Frosting | Two Peas and Their Pod (www.twopeasandtheirpod.com) #recipe #cake" width="640"/>
       <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com/banana-nutella-swirl-cake-with-nutella-frosting/&amp;media=http://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/banana-nutella-cake1.jpg&amp;description=Banana Nutella Swirl Cake with Nutella Frosting')">
       </span>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      The banana cake is as easy as they come. You probably have all of the ingredients on hand. The bananas and Greek yogurt make the cake super duper moist. I swirled Nutella into the cake.       <span class="item">
       <h2 class="fn">
        Banana Nutella Swirl Cake with Nutella Frosting
       </h2>
      </span>
      <div class="time">
       <p>
        <strong>
         Yield:
        </strong>
        <span class="yield">
         Serves 16
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Prep Time:
        </strong>
        <span class="preptime">
         15 minutes
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Cook Time:
        </strong>
        <span class="cooktime">
         25 minutes
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Total Time:
        </strong>
        <span class="duration">
         40 minutes
        </span>
       </p>
      </div>
      <div style="clear:left">
      </div>
      <div class="summary">
       <p>
        Banana cake with a Nutella swirl and Nutella Buttercream frosting. This cake is easy to make and a great way to use up brown bananas.
       </p>
      </div>
      <h3>
       Ingredients:
      </h3>
      <div class="ingredient">
       <p>
        For the cake:
       </p>
       <p>
        2 cups all-purpose Gold Medal flour
        <br/>
        1 teaspoon baking soda
        <br/>
        1/4 teaspoon salt
        <br/>
        1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
        <br/>
        1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
        <br/>
        2 large eggs
        <br/>
        1 cup plain Greek yogurt (I used Chobani 0%)
        <br/>
        1 teaspoon vanilla extract
        <br/>
        3 ripe bananas, peeled and mashed (to equal 1 cup)
        <br/>
        1/2 cup Nutella
       </p>
       <p>
        For the Nutella Frosting:
       </p>
       <p>
        1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
        <br/>
        1 cup Nutella
        <br/>
        1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
        <br/>
        2-3 tablespoons milk
        <br/>
        1/2 teaspoon hazelnut extract, optional
       </p>
      </div>
      <h3>
       Directions:
      </h3>
      <div class="instructions">
       <p>
        1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9x13 pan and set aside.
       </p>
       <p>
        2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
       </p>
       <p>
        3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until creamy and smooth, about 3 minutes. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the yogurt and vanilla extract. Mix in the mashed bananas.
       </p>
       <p>
        4. Gradually add the dry ingredients and mix until flour disappears. Spread cake batter evenly into the prepared pan. Swirl the Nutella into the cake using a knife of toothpick.
       </p>
       <p>
        5. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow cake to cool completely.
       </p>
       <p>
        6. While the cake is cooling, make the Nutella frosting. In the bowl of a stand mixer, beat the butter and Nutella on high until smooth. Slowly add the powdered sugar, 1/4 cup at a time. Beat on high until powdered sugar is incorporated. Add the milk and hazelnut extract, if using. Beat until frosting is smooth and desired consistency. If you need to add more milk, add a little bit at a time.
       </p>
       <p>
        7. Frost the cake with Nutella frosting. Cut into squares and serve. I added sprinkles for fun!
       </p>
      </div>
