           Zurich, Auckland and Munich were ranked in the second, third and fourth positions respectively. Vancouver came in fifth place to be at the top ranking on the list of cities in North America, while Singapore came in sixth and was ranked twenty-six (26) to be among the top Asian cities.
          </p>

           Mercer Index -for the quality of living -takes into account the political and social environment of the city, in addition to health care, medical considerations, public services, recreation facilities and natural environment, and other factors.
