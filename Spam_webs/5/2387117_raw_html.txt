            <h1 class="arw-postheader">
             <span class="arw-postheadericon">
              Wedding Planners are Party Planners
             </span>
            </h1>
            <div class="arw-postheadericons arw-metadata-icons">
             <span class="arw-postdateicon">
              <span class="date">
               Published
              </span>
              <span class="entry-date" title="8:28 pm">
               May 20, 2012
              </span>
             </span>
             |
             <span class="arw-postauthoricon">
              <span class="author">
               By
              </span>
              <span class="author vcard">
               <a class="url fn n" href="http://blog.stepandrepeatla.com/author/jewel/" title="View all posts by Jewel">
                Jewel
               </a>
              </span>
             </span>
            </div>
            <div class="arw-postcontent">
             <!-- article-content -->
             <div>
              <strong>
               Did you know that when wedding planners take on new clients they are actually planning not one event (the wedding) but two complete events. They not only are wedding planners, but also party planners.
              </strong>
             </div>
             <div>
              <div>
               <p>
                Step and Repeat LA has worked with many wedding planners and coordinators and has noticed that the successful ones are incredible planners and organizers. They keep track of meticulous details for each step from start to finish for the wedding and wedding reception. Just to give you an idea of the number of activities that will take place in the set-up and running of these events here is a list of areas of importance that a wedding planner has to keep track of:
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 Deciding on the date
                </li>
                <li>
                 Locations
                 <ol>
                  <li>
                   Wedding
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Reception
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Indoor or outdoor
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Theme
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Time of year
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Type of venue, ie. church, hall, park, etc.
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Availability of Venues
                  </li>
                 </ol>
                </li>
                <li>
                 Wedding theme and colors
                </li>
                <li>
                 Wedding Flowers and decor for wedding and reception
                </li>
                <li>
                 Set-up of floor plan and seating for wedding reception
                </li>
                <li>
                 Buffet or sit-down dinner
                </li>
                <li>
                 Music
                 <ol>
                  <li>
                   DJ or live band
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Wedding songs
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Songs for dances
                  </li>
                 </ol>
                </li>
                <li>
                 Wedding cake location
                </li>
                <li>
                 Lighting
                </li>
                <li>
                 Banner and red-carpet location
                </li>
                <li>
                 Timing of the mini-events such as
                 <ol>
                  <li>
                   Cake cutting
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Garter removal
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Entrance of wedding party
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   First dances
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Toasts
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Throwing of bouquet
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Wedding photos, bridal party photos
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Bride and grooms exit
                  </li>
                 </ol>
                </li>
                <li>
                 Selection of Photographers/Videographers for wedding and wedding reception
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 <br/>
                 AND the myriad of tiny details preparatory to the events happening such as:
                 <br/>
                </strong>
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 Picking out wedding clothes
                 <ol>
                  <li>
                   Dress
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Tuxedos
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Bridesmaids gowns
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Flower girls
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Groomsmen
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Bridal party
                  </li>
                 </ol>
                </li>
                <li>
                 Dining
                 <ol>
                  <li>
                   Table settings
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Silver
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Glasses
                  </li>
                  <li>
                   Etc
                  </li>
                 </ol>
                </li>
                <li>
                 Guest list
                </li>
                <li>
                 Invitations
                </li>
                <li>
                 Dance lessons
                </li>
                <li>
                 etc. etc. etc.
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 <br/>
                 Your wedding planner and you will determine who is responsible for getting which tasks done and what you need to work on together in order to create the most memorable day of a couples life together.
                </strong>
               </p>
               <p>
                Step and Repeat LA is very happy to take part in this wonderfully orchestrated event. We love to receive pictures to share with othersof your successful adventure into matrimony.
               </p>
               <p style="text-align: center;">
                <img align="middle" alt="Wedding picture in front of step and repeat banner" class="aligncenter" height="466" src="http://www.stepandrepeatla.com/images/SR-Wedding-Codi-small.jpg" width="444"/>
               </p>
              </div>
