            <div class="textilized" id="project-content">
             <p>
              Made from barn siding and dimensional pine lumber.  I drew this out on a note pad to show my wife and she nodded her approval.  Its 73 tall x 40 wide and 18 deep.  This is a barn door style, but as you look at the pictures, you can see the multitude of choices you have to change this to fit any need.  For the top: glass, stain-glassed, mirrors, etc.  For the bench: Open as I built this, but can be three, two or single drawers or bench box for quilts, toys, etc.  For paint/stain: Seriously this is wide open.  I figure just from this one simple design, I could come up with over 12 possible builds.
             </p>
             <p>
              Pic 1:  Finished Hall Tree
              <br/>
              Pic 2:  The bench by itself (is attached to door with 12  3 1/2 long screws)
              <br/>
              Pic 3:  Door is finished. Not a good picture of it as its laying down.
              <br/>
              Pic 4:  The start, a wood pile!
              <br/>
              Pic 5:  My boss, who approves each and every project and helps on them all.  I couldnt do half of what I accomplish without her! You know the saying, behind every good man is a better woman!
              <br/>
              Pic 6:  Customer after she finished it to her liking!  She called it antiquing!
             </p>
