        <p>
         Welcome! I am now in possession of a
         <a href="https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/68448/7-wonders">
          7 Wonders
         </a>
         bundle which consists of the 7 Wonders base game, Leaders Expansion,
         <strong>
          1
         </strong>
         7 Wonders Play Mat and 1 package of 7 Wonders Card Sleeves that will go to one lucky person.
        </p>
        <p>
         All you have to do is enter your name and email address aboveto enter the contest. Please read the
         <a href="http://www.boardgamegiveaway.com/official-rules/">
          Official Rules
         </a>
         before entering the contest. Please head on over and congratulate the winner of my last contest for
         <a href="http://www.boardgamegiveaway.com/congrats-are-in-order/">
          Star Wars: Imperial Assault
         </a>
         and check out the
         <a href="http://www.boardgamegiveaway.com/albums/wall-of-winners/">
          wall of winners
         </a>
         for photos of past contest winners with their prizes.
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out
         <a href="http://alkony.enerla.net/en/">
          alkony.enerla.net
         </a>
         , The Alkony is a dark fantasy roleplaying setting, developed by the Alkony Workshop. On this website you will find the works of the Alkony Workshop  games, graphic art, and literature.
        </p>
        <p>
         Make sure you check out this months board game review for
         <a href="http://www.questsofquirkiness.com/2015/04/02/board-game-review-tragedy-looper/">
          Tragedy Looper
         </a>
         by game designer BakaFire, produced by Z-man Games, over at
         <a href="http://www.questsofquirkiness.com/">
          Questsofquirkiness.com
         </a>
         . Be sure to cast your vote in the poll to the right of this post, for the game with the most votes by April 20th will be featured as the next board game give away.
        </p>
        <p>
         Dont forget, after you sign up, to share this post via the facebook/twitter links at the bottom of the contest boxor copy and paste the referral link; for every referral you bring to the contest you will receive an additional entry.
        </p>
        <p>
         The winner will be contacted via email upon which the individual will be required to send me their mailing address so I can get the game to them ASAP.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thank you and have fun!
        </p>
