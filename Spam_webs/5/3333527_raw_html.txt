            <img alt="Picture of mushroom chef by the mushroom council" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-307" height="534" src="http://itsgoodthis.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/mushroom-chef.png" width="717"/>

          <p>
           Its the eternal battle of meat lovers vs. veggie lovers: while the veggie lovers insist vegetables are just as delicious, or perhaps even better tasting, than meat, the meat lovers refuse to change their ways. That is, until The Mushroom Council released the results of a taste test it ran on tacos. After serving participants a variety of all-beef and mushroom-beef mixes, the study found that many participants agreed the best-tasting specimen was the 50/50 mix of mushrooms and ground beef. Considering the
           <a href="http://dashburst.com/sustainable-foods-prevent-global-crisis/" target="_blank">
            global food crisis
           </a>
           and humanitys desperate need to consume less meat, this is quite a positive outcome for the food industry.
          </p>
