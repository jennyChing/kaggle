        About Me
       </h1>
      </header>

       <p>
        Hi! My name is Laura and I hope that you will join me in my kitchen and share a cup of coffee while we take some time to get to know each other, so that soon we are old friends. I hope that we can share, not only recipes and tips, but also secrets, hopes, dreams, struggles, wisdom (or lack thereof), and laughs, and that we can learn from each other along the way.
       </p>
       <p>
        I believe in eating as naturally as possible using fresh, quality ingredients, and making things the old-fashioned way by keeping food close to its original source, made from scratch, so that it tastes homemade. Its not always easy or even possible, but its a journey and a process.
       </p>
       <p>
        My husband and I have been together for more than half of my life. We have three children and we reside in the beautiful and picturesque Canadian prairies where the sun shines bright and the Aurora Borealis lights up the night sky.
       </p>

        </a>
       </p>
