        <h1>
         Your Neopet Dailies
        </h1>
        <p>
         There are lots of special activities on Neopets that you can visit that are called "dailies", usually since you can visit them once a day. Since there are so many to earn Neopoints from, we've created a useful list below of all the places you could be visiting. :) If you login to your
         <a href="?go=login">
          jnAccount
         </a>
         and add the "Your Dailies" service, you can customize this list to pick which dailies to include, and even add in your own custom dailies!
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Tip:
         </b>
         Bookmark this page by pressing CTRL + D or CMD + D on your keyboard! You can come back everyday to complete these dailies again.
        </p>
