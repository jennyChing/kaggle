        <p class="post-meta">
         Posted By
         <a href="http://romanianmum.com/author/otilia" rel="author" title="Posts by Otilia">
          Otilia
         </a>
         on Oct 1, 2014 |

        </p>
        <p>
         Autumn  lots of rain and chilli its a time when we are all preparing to spend more time indoors with our family. This is when I start checking up the web for things that can make my home look more nice and warm.
        </p>
        <p>
         I must confess I do spend a lot of my evenings online browsing and browsing for things to buy. Here are the things I think will make you home more autumn and warm looking this autumn:
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           BLANKETS 
          </strong>
          I simply can sit or lay on the couch without covering my knees. It might be the old age but as soon as the autumn is coming I am loving blankets even more! I am loving the
          <a href="http://www.ikea.com/us/en/catalog/products/30200696/#/80200694" target="_blank">
           URSULA throw
          </a>
          from Ikea and really need to get hubby to buy it for me:
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.ikea.com/gb/en/catalog/products/80200694/#/80200694" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-3936 size-full" height="453" src="http://romanianmum.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Screen-Shot-2014-09-30-at-20.06.51.png" width="551"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          <strong>
           CUSHIONS 
          </strong>
          Unfortunately my couch is not big enough to have as many
          <strong>
          </strong>
          cushionsas I wish I had but that doesnt mean that I cant make covers to the ones I already have.I am liking some brown shades of
          <a href="http://www.yorkshirelinen.com/cushions-cushion-covers/" target="_blank">
           Yorkshire Linens cushions
          </a>
          which will go really well on my light cream couch:
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.yorkshirelinen.com/cushions-cushion-covers/chenille-spot-choc-cushion-cover-55cm.html">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3937" height="298" src="http://romanianmum.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Screen-Shot-2014-09-30-at-20.13.08-300x298.png" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you like fusions as much as I do why not have a look at my cushions pinterest board  some really interesting designs there for you to be inspired. Not autumn looking but really nice looking:
         <br/>
         <a data-pin-board-width="400" data-pin-do="embedBoard" data-pin-scale-height="200" data-pin-scale-width="80" href="http://www.pinterest.com/romanianmum/pretty-fantastic-pillows/">
          Follow Otilia Stockss board Pretty fantastic pillows on Pinterest.
         </a>
         <!--[wpfcNOT]-->
         <script async="" src="//assets.pinterest.com/js/pinit.js" type="text/javascript">
         </script>
        </p>
        <ul>
         <ul>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <strong>
             CANDLES
            </strong>
             Lots of candles. As many as possible if you canIn the evenings I prefer to have my candles lighten up rather than the lights. It reminds me of the log fire we use to have at my grandmother house when I was a child. I think right now candles that are pretty and in colours like green, red, orange or brown are at the top. Scents like
            <a href="http://www.debenhams.com/webapp/wcs/stores/servlet/prod_10701_10001_58343+1055974E_-1" target="_blank">
             cinnamon
            </a>
            and vanilla are my favourites.
           </li>
          </ul>
         </ul>
        </ul>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.debenhams.com/webapp/wcs/stores/servlet/prod_10701_10001_304029737942_-1" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Screen Shot 2014-09-30 at 20.22.02" class="alignleft wp-image-3938 size-medium" height="300" src="http://romanianmum.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Screen-Shot-2014-09-30-at-20.22.02-244x300.png" width="244"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.next.co.uk/g402662s3#688935g40" target="_blank">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-3939 size-medium" height="300" src="http://romanianmum.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Screen-Shot-2014-09-30-at-20.15.15-196x300.png" width="196"/>
         </a>
         You can also make your own candles and decorate them like the ones below:
         <a href="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/cinnamon-stick-candles.jpg" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Cinnamon Candles" class="aligncenter" height="364" src="http://theinspiredroom.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/cinnamon-stick-candles.jpg" width="549"/>
         </a>
         Tip: Dont light up too many scents at the same time as it might give you headaches.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <ul>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <strong>
             FLOWERS 
            </strong>
            Its been a while now since I started to buy my own flowers in order to make my home look more pretty. This autumn I am buying chrysanthemum.
            <pre class="tw-data-text vk_txt tw-ta tw-text-medium" data-fulltext="" data-placeholder="Traducere" dir="ltr" id="tw-target-text">
             <a href="http://romanianmum.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_0684.jpg">
              <img alt="chrysanthemums" class="aligncenter wp-image-3944" height="297" src="http://romanianmum.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/IMG_0684-300x200.jpg" width="446"/>
             </a>
            </pre>
            <p>
            </p>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </ul>
        </ul>
        <ul>
         <ul>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <strong>
             PINE CONES, LEAVES WREATHS AND ACORNS 
            </strong>
            Pick them up with your kids , craft them up in something nice and decorate your walls and other places where you donut happen to have toys. Here are a couple of ideas from my own Pinterest board 
            <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
             <strong>
              <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/romanianmum/autumn-holidays-decorations-food-crafts/" target="_blank">
               Autumn Holidays  Decorations, Food &amp; Crafts
              </a>
              :
             </strong>
            </span>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </ul>
        </ul>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span style="font-size: 14pt;">
          <strong>
          </strong>
         </span>
         <!--[wpfcNOT]-->
         <script async="" src="//assets.pinterest.com/js/pinit.js" type="text/javascript">
         </script>
         <br/>
         <!--[wpfcNOT]-->
         <br/>
         <script async="" src="//assets.pinterest.com/js/pinit.js" type="text/javascript">
         </script>
         <!--[wpfcNOT]-->
         I love autumn more than any other season in the whole world so would be lovely to hear how you decorate your home this autumn.
        </p>
