         <h2 class="posttitle">
          How Hashtags Can Benefit Brands
         </h2>
         <div class="entry">
          <img alt="Hashtags-Useful-To-Follow-Interests-Brands" class="display_none wp-post-image" height="256" src="http://www.mdgadvertising.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/blog_Hashtags-Useful-To-Follow-Interests-Brands.jpg" title="Hashtags-Useful-To-Follow-Interests-Brands" width="475"/>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.mdgadvertising.com/blog/?p=26711">
            <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-26712" height="256" src="http://www.mdgadvertising.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/blog_Hashtags-Useful-To-Follow-Interests-Brands.jpg" title="Hashtags-Useful-To-Follow-Interests-Brands" width="475"/>
           </a>
           <br/>
           The hashtag has been steadily gaining popularity over the last few years and seemed to reach its peak during this years Super Bowl. Not only did hashtags punctuate most ads, but they were a stroke of real-time marketing genius during the sudden blackout. The
           <a href="http://www.forbes.com/sites/thesba/2013/06/19/4-reasons-businesses-should-take-hashtags-seriously/" target="_blank" title="prevalence of hashtags in Twitter group conversations">
            prevalence of hashtags in Twitter group conversations
           </a>
           has led to their ubiquity in general social media conversations, as well as their mainstream use by major brands like Oreo and Volkswagen. While some may have wondered about their value to brands,
           <a href="http://marketingland.com/mobile-hashtag-survey-finds-users-more-likely-to-explore-content-using-hashtags-if-offered-discounts-37778" target="_blank" title="a recent study by RadiumOne">
            a recent study by RadiumOne
           </a>
           has helped validate their worth by revealing that one-third of consumers have used hashtags to follow brands and topics of interest, while 17% have actually discovered a brand through its hashtag. In addition, more than half of people would share hashtags more often in return for an incentive.
           <em>
            MediaPost.com
           </em>
           explained
           <a href="http://www.mediapost.com/publications/article/196645/hashtags-useful-to-follow-interests-brands.html#axzz2OrwMA62N" target="_blank" title=" how the hashtag has become the darling of digital marketing">
            how the hashtag has become the darling of digital marketing
           </a>
           and ways brands are leveraging its promotional power.
          </p>
          <p>
           Hashtags are a new discovery to many marketers, so RadiumOne recently launched tools to help them make the most of this marketing activity. One of their services offers the ability to target ads to audiences by the hashtags they tweet and the content within their hashtags. For maximum market coverage, these ads targeted to hashtag users can be viewed on both desktop and mobile devices.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span id="more-26711">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Hyundai used this service during the Super Bowl for a campaign that targeted viewers that used hashtags related to their commercials and the big game. The hashtags were used to drive online video views of the Hyundai ads both during and after their airing. The results showed that a whopping
           <a href="http://www.mobilemarketer.com/cms/news/research/15049.html" target="_blank" title="70% of consumers who clicked on Hyundais TV ads through hashtags wound up watching the entire commercial">
            70% of consumers who clicked on Hyundais TV ads through hashtags wound up watching the entire commercial
           </a>
           .
          </p>
