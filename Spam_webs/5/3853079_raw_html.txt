        <h2 class="title">
         <a style="font-size: 24px;">
          VIDEO: The 7 most astounding moments from Kathleen Sebeliuss testimony before Congress
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="body">
         <p>
          <img src="http://38.media.tumblr.com/16f2476e4978b13f959fab20836407b2/tumblr_inline_mvifqqb7xQ1qij8k6.png"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          The sparks were flying today on Capitol Hill as the House Energy and Commerce Committee grilled Kathleen Sebeliuss over Obamacare and the horrific rollout of the exchange website.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           1. Rep. Mike Rogers lights Sebelius up over the glaring security weaknesses in the Obamacare exchange website.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe frameborder="0" height="390" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/Thr-dMrkWVc" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           2. When Rep. Greg Harper asks Sebelius if President Obama is ultimately responsible for the failure of the Obamacare rollout, she replies Whatever!"
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/z8dkGXktWl8?list=UU0NDAIG6R5qbWOm0-NNo7Ag" width="560">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           3. Sebelius claims the website has never crashed.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe frameborder="0" height="390" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/t-1K50NBmGo" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           4. Sebelius lies about the law when Rep. Bill Long asks her why she isnt enrolled through the exchanges.
          </strong>
          Sebelius claims that the law prevents her from doing so, which
          <a href="https://www.healthcare.gov/what-if-i-have-job-based-health-insurance/" target="_blank">
           even the Obamacare website says isnt true.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/RIx_VuUHoZ8?feature=player_embedded" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           5. Hot mic catches Sebelius begging "Dont do this to me! under her breath when asked a question.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/so8l0vsMprM?feature=player_embedded" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           6. Rep Marsha Blackburn grills Sebelius over the millions of Americans who have received cancellation notices from the insurance companies because of Obamacare.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe frameborder="0" height="315" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/-NCapBT_bow" width="420">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          7.
          <strong>
           Rep. Rene Ellmers asks why Obamacare forces males to have maternity coverage on their insurance plans.
          </strong>
          <span>
           A single male, aged 32, does need maternity coverage. To the best of your knowledge, has a man ever delivered a baby?
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <iframe frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/l2CzYm7MTaE?feature=player_embedded" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          Needless to say, it was a very bad, horrible, no good day for Kathleen Sebelius.
         </p>
