         <h3 class="posttitle">
          <a href="http://www.culturesouthwest.org.uk/guides/how-to-work-the-2015-trend-of-glossy-blue-walls/" rel="bookmark">
           How To Work The 2015 Trend Of Glossy Blue
          </a>
         </h3>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/CLASSIC-1.png" rel="lightbox4517" title="How To Work The 2015 Trend Of Glossy Blue Walls">
            <img alt="How To Work The 2015 Trend Of Glossy Blue Walls" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4522" data-lazy-src="http://cdn2.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/CLASSIC-1-480x350.png" height="350" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="480"/>
            <noscript>
             <img alt="How To Work The 2015 Trend Of Glossy Blue Walls" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4522" height="350" src="http://cdn2.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/CLASSIC-1-480x350.png" width="480"/>
            </noscript>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Dont be afraid of using dark colours on your walls, they can work even in the smallest of rooms.
          </p>
          <p>
           <span id="more-4517">
           </span>
          </p>
          <p>
           Its all about how much light you can add to that room. This can be achieved by using lacquered/glossy paint and white or light accessories, plus mirrors. These pics give you a few examples of what you could achieve.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/how-to-design-a-small-space-3.jpg" rel="lightbox4517" title="Designer Todd Alexander Romano's Blue Lacquered Manhattan Apartment">
            <img alt="Designer Todd Alexander Romano's Blue Lacquered Manhattan Apartment" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4526" data-lazy-src="http://cdn3.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/how-to-design-a-small-space-3-480x574.jpg" height="574" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="480"/>
            <noscript>
             <img alt="Designer Todd Alexander Romano's Blue Lacquered Manhattan Apartment" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4526" height="574" src="http://cdn3.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/how-to-design-a-small-space-3-480x574.jpg" width="480"/>
            </noscript>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Image
            <a href="http://www.decoist.com/2011-05-11/rich-coloured-small-apartment-in-manhattan-small-wonder-by-todd-alexander-romano/" target="_blank">
             Source
            </a>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           Designer Todd Alexander Romanos blue lacquered Manhattan apartment shows that even if you have a small space, you can paint with bold colours such as blue. The gloss of the blue lacquer helps reflect the light, the white ceiling and predominantly white artwork helps break up the blue walls, and the wooden flooring adds a warm depth to the room.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/PatrickCline_Lonny_CK009.jpg" rel="lightbox4517" title="Lacquered Blue Walls">
            <img alt="Lacquered Blue Walls" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4524" data-lazy-src="http://cdn2.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/PatrickCline_Lonny_CK009-480x617.jpg" height="617" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="480"/>
            <noscript>
             <img alt="Lacquered Blue Walls" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4524" height="617" src="http://cdn2.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/PatrickCline_Lonny_CK009-480x617.jpg" width="480"/>
            </noscript>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Image
            <a href="http://www.kembleinteriors.com/residential/" target="_blank">
             Source
            </a>
           </em>
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/77a61536842e22f850bc1a8fba6c78c4.jpg" rel="lightbox4517" title="Blue Bedroom With Blue Gloss Walls">
            <img alt="Blue Bedroom With Blue Gloss Walls" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4525" data-lazy-src="http://cdn2.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/77a61536842e22f850bc1a8fba6c78c4-480x552.jpg" height="552" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="480"/>
            <noscript>
             <img alt="Blue Bedroom With Blue Gloss Walls" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4525" height="552" src="http://cdn2.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/77a61536842e22f850bc1a8fba6c78c4-480x552.jpg" width="480"/>
            </noscript>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Image
            <a href="http://www.housebeautiful.com/design-inspiration/house-tours/g1533/apartment-with-stylish-details-1113/?slide=14" target="_blank">
             Source
            </a>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           The style and colour of this room pictured by HouseBeautiful gives it a modern and masculine feel.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/2908662-14897937-thumbnail.jpg" rel="lightbox4517" title="Feminine Bedroom With Gloss Blue Painted Wall">
            <img alt="Feminine Bedroom With Gloss Blue Painted Wall" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4532" data-lazy-src="http://cdn4.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/2908662-14897937-thumbnail-480x746.jpg" height="746" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="480"/>
            <noscript>
             <img alt="Feminine Bedroom With Gloss Blue Painted Wall" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4532" height="746" src="http://cdn4.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/2908662-14897937-thumbnail-480x746.jpg" width="480"/>
            </noscript>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Image
            <a href="http://thedecorista.tumblr.com/page/18" target="_blank">
             Source
            </a>
           </em>
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/d0a76b57b445511e4889c2f0eaa27ab3.jpg" rel="lightbox4517" title="Gloss &amp; Matt Blue Painted Walls With White Sofa">
            <img alt="Gloss &amp; Matt Blue Painted Walls With White Sofa" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4529" data-lazy-src="http://cdn4.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/d0a76b57b445511e4889c2f0eaa27ab3-480x610.jpg" height="610" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="480"/>
            <noscript>
             <img alt="Gloss &amp; Matt Blue Painted Walls With White Sofa" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4529" height="610" src="http://cdn4.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/d0a76b57b445511e4889c2f0eaa27ab3-480x610.jpg" width="480"/>
            </noscript>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Image
            <a href="http://clovermag.com/2011/best-in-show/unbridled-style-at-kips-bay-decorator-show-house/" target="_blank">
             Source
            </a>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           A bold and stylish room designed by Sherrill Canet at sherrillcanet.com. I love the way the blue gloss strips on the wall are paired with blue matt strips; Really adds towards the different depths within the room. As noted earlier, the mirror is also a designers go-to choice when wanting to add more light to a room, especially beneficial in a room painted with a darker colour.
          </p>
          <hr/>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/fc98c4323e5a5067a3b84ac377dd3146.jpg" rel="lightbox4517" title="Lacquered Blue Gloss Door">
            <img alt="Lacquered Blue Gloss Door" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4530" data-lazy-src="http://cdn2.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/fc98c4323e5a5067a3b84ac377dd3146-480x621.jpg" height="621" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODdhAQABAPAAAP///wAAACwAAAAAAQABAEACAkQBADs=" width="480"/>
            <noscript>
             <img alt="Lacquered Blue Gloss Door" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4530" height="621" src="http://cdn2.culturesouthwest.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/fc98c4323e5a5067a3b84ac377dd3146-480x621.jpg" width="480"/>
            </noscript>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Image
            <a href="http://blog.effortless-style.com/2012/10/navy-sunshine-entryway-style-plan/" target="_blank">
             Source
            </a>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           If you want to add that touch of blue gloss into your home, but painting the walls is not quite for you, then painting furniture or doors could be the answer.
          </p>
