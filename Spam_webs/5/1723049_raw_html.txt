          <p>
           The Voice is returning to our screens for the first episode  of the Battle Rounds and to drum up more excitement for the anticipated return of the show, Usher, Jill Scott and Aloe Blacc have given interviews.
          </p>
          <p>
           Jill Scott who is supporting judge Usher as an adviser talks about why The Voice is one of her favorite shows and how she will contribute in helping Usher to decide which contestants to choose.
          </p>
          <p>
           The Philly R&amp;B soul singer advises to sing with all your heart even though there will naturally be nerves.
          </p>
          <p>
           Aloe Blacc who will support judge Adam Levine talks about being yourself and letting the audience accept you as an artist and individual.
          </p>
          <p>
           Finally, Usher himself also speaks on what he is looking for. The Climax singer wants to see more soul in the contestants performances.
          </p>
          <p>
           Check out the interviews after the jump and tune in on NBC for the Voice which airs on Mondays and Tuesdays.
