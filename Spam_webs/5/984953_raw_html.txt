             <span class="title">
              Insanity literally heals me from depression
             </span>
             <span class="entrytext">
              <p>
               <img height="600" src="http://i64.photobucket.com/albums/h180/viva_five/TRANS1_zps20de77fe.jpg" width="602"/>
              </p>
              <p class="p1">
               Another transformation pic from me :)
              </p>
              <p class="p1">
               Before doing
               <a href="http://www.home-workouts-for-men.com/">
                <span class="s1">
                 <strong>
                  <em>
                   INSANITY by Beachbody
                  </em>
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </a>
               and during INSANITY (lost 12 centimeters from my belly and 3,5 kilograms in a month). I started Insanity since early February. Clean eating and workout everyday. And I gotta tell you, clean eating is really important. I dont count calories, its too burdensome for me. The important thing is that you need to be conscious of whatever youre eating. I took photos of every meals I had and keep them in my phone, it helps me to decide my next meals by looking at the food Ive been eating.
              </p>
              <p class="p1">
               If youre an iPhone user, you can also use
               <a href="https://eatery.massivehealth.com/">
                <span class="s1">
                 <strong>
                  <em>
                   #Eatery app
                  </em>
                 </strong>
                </span>
               </a>
               . An old app, but it works for me.
              </p>
              <p class="p1">
               Oh, and water is a must.
              </p>
              <p class="p1">
               INSANITY is a really tough workout program for me, I guess it mainly because I havent been doing a real workout for about 3-4 years. During Insanity, I was tired as fuck and slacking a lot. But I just didnt stop. So yeah, you can see that eventho I was slacking quite a lot during the workout, the result will always there because I dont give up and keep on going. Always think of the result and pushing your mind into it, you can.
              </p>
              <p class="p1">
               My followers maybe know that I used to be depressed. I used to hate the world and I really hated my self. I used to be awake the whole night, and refused to come out from my room during the day. But those days are gone, now. I decided to recover, I committed to live the healthy life Im supposed to do, and I committed to be a better me, not bitter anymore. I keep on going, and I feel much more happier.
              </p>
              <p class="p1">
               <strong>
                <em>
                 So yeah, this is not a result yet :) (Height 173 cm)
                </em>
