             <h2>
              Air Actuated Stainless Steel Ball Valves for the Oil and Gas Industry
             </h2>
             <dl class="article-info">
              <dd class="create">
               Thursday, 12 December 2013
              </dd>
             </dl>
             <p>
              <a href="http://waverleybrownall.co.uk/~waverley/products/ball-valves/air-actuated-ball-valves" target="_blank">
               <img alt="air actuated ball valves" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-165" height="174" src="http://www.waverleybrownall.co.uk/~waverley/blog/wp-content/uploads/air_actuated.png" width="250"/>
              </a>
              Waverley brownall is a leading manufacturer and global distributor of stainless steel and exotic material compression tube fittings, valves and bespoke instrumentation products.
             </p>
             <p>
              Established in 1929 our philosophy has always been dominated by the quality of both the products we design and manufacture, to the customer services we provide.
             </p>
             <p>
              Our range includes
              <a href="http://waverleybrownall.co.uk/~waverley/products/ball-valves/air-actuated-ball-valves" target="_blank" title="stainless steel air actuated valves">
               Air Actuated Stainless Steel Ball Valves
              </a>
              . The Waverley brownall range of air actuated ball valves are entirely manufactured in the UK and offer a unique compact design. The actuators are the ideal complement to Waverleys range of 1000lb, 3000lb and 6000lb class valves on previous pages.
              <span id="more-164">
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Waverley brownalls actuated ball valves are sold as complete factory mounted units and offer space saving, corrosion resistance and long maintenance free operation.
             </p>
