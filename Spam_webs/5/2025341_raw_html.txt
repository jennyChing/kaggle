      <h1 class="entry-title">
       Pom Pom Wreath
      </h1>
      <div class="entry-meta">
       <em>
        by
       </em>
       Karen
       <em>
        on
       </em>
       March 30, 2011
      </div>
      <!-- .entry-meta -->
      <div class="entry-content">
       <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="5342567" data-link="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/pom-pom-wreath/" data-summary="" data-title="Pom Pom Wreath">
       </div>
       <p>
        Remember those
        <a href="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/tissue-paper-pom-poms">
         tissue paper pom poms
        </a>
        I showed you how to make last week? Did you practice? Did you get good at them? Cause NOWS THE TIME TO PUT THAT TALENT TO USE!!!
       </p>
       <p>
        Make a bunch of them. Bunch is a technical term most often used in the grape industry.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-14298" height="415" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_00832-625x415.jpg" title="pom poms" width="625"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Cut a circle out of some cardboard.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-14299" height="415" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_00882-625x415.jpg" title="cutting cardboard" width="625"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Playfully frame a bunch of flowers with it.  Cause youre cute, and playful!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14300" height="478" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_00922-e1301532963341.jpg" title="framed roses" width="625"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Grab a glue gun.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-14301" height="415" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0086-625x415.jpg" title="glue gun" width="625"/>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        And start gluing the pom poms to the cardboard. Ya know  in a pleasing manner.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14302" height="729" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0095-e1301533191215.jpg" title="pom pom wreath on door" width="485"/>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        The best part about this spring wreath is its weather proof. No its not. Lemme start again 
       </p>
       <p>
        The best part about this spring wreath is its so light you can hang it anywhere with just a piece of 2 way tape.
       </p>
       <p>
        Of course this next picture has the wreath propped on a picture stand. Lets ignore that.
       </p>
       <p>
        When displayed this way it looks like a funeral alter for a circus clown. Lets ignore that.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14303" height="729" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0122-e1301533265310.jpg" title="Pom pom Wreath" width="485"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Oh. O.K., and here I used a tiny piece of wire to hang the wreath because the door was too wonky for the tape to work.  The louvers made it difficult.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14304" height="729" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_01242-e1301533334948.jpg" title="pom pom wreath on door" width="485"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        See there though! In the background! I used the two way tape to stick the wreath to a mirror. Honestly I did!
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14305" height="729" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0146-e1301533410167.jpg" title="pom pom" width="485"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        See? Heres proof. Its what we in the biz refer to as an action shot.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14307" height="729" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0172-e1301533468681.jpg" title="hanging wreath" width="485"/>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14306" height="729" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_0158-e1301533623803.jpg" title="pom pom wreath over buffet" width="485"/>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Again  stuck it with some two way tape.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-14309" height="415" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/DSC_00901-625x415.jpg" title="Easter wreath on window" width="625"/>
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        And later that evening  the wreath magically appears in yet ANOTHER spot! Honestly, a hunk of cardboard and a vomit of tissue paper is incredibly versatile.
       </p>
       <p>
        Im even thinking it would work as a belt for a fancy dress party.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14318" height="729" src="http://www.theartofdoingstuff.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/curio2-e1301542558975.jpg" title="curio2" width="485"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Do NOT however, stick your Pom Pom wreath to the wall above your cat food dishes. Because, if the wreath were to fall right ON the formerly homeless, already skittish cat while it was eating  it might never recover.
       </p>
       <p>
        Hypothetically speaking of course.
       </p>
       <p>
        By the way, I was reallyyyy rushed when I did this wreath.  If I were to do it again Id do a somewhat subtler combination of colours.  Not quite such a RIOT of colour. Then again perhaps its appropriate  I mean  it
        <em>
         is
        </em>
        Spring.
       </p>
       <p>
        Hypothetically speaking of course.
       </p>
