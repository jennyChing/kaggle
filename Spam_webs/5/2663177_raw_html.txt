        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Chicken Breast Stuffed with Pancetta, Onions and Nuts
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-06-25T08:43:42+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          June 25, 2013
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">

       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-color="gray" data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.spiciefoodie.com%2F2013%2F06%2F25%2Fchicken-breast-stuffed-with-pancetta-onions-and-nuts%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.spiciefoodie.com%2Fblogimages%2F2013%2F06%2FStuffedChicken_Pancetta_Nuts01.jpg&amp;description=Chicken%20Breast%20Stuffed%20with%20Pancetta%2C%20Onions%20and%20Nuts" rel="nobox">

        <p>
         <img alt="Chicken Breast Stuffed with Pancetta, Onions and Nuts Recipe by @SpicieFoodie | #chicken #pancetta #nuts #dinner" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.spiciefoodie.com/blogimages/2013/06/StuffedChicken_Pancetta_Nuts01.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         From Chicken Kiev to Chicken Cordon Blue, and everything in between, stuffed chicken breasts are a delicious and hassle free meal. Though Chicken Kiev and Cordon blue are among my favorite stuffed chicken breasts, the calories and frying are not something I care to indulge in on a regular basis. Thats why lighter stuffed chicken breasts, like the one Im sharing today, make a fantastic alternative.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Chicken Breast Stuffed with Pancetta, Onions and Nuts Recipe by @SpicieFoodie | #chicken #pancetta #nuts #dinner" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.spiciefoodie.com/blogimages/2013/06/StuffedChicken_Pancetta_Nuts02.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Todays recipe, Chicken Breast Stuffed with Pancetta, Onions and Nuts, comes together so fast and I bet you have all the ingredients in your refrigerator. The stuffing brings together salty
         <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pancetta" target="_blank" title="What is pancetta?">
          pancetta
         </a>
         pieces with sweet caramelized onions and crunchy nuts. I used walnuts this time but pecans, almonds, or even pumpkin seeds can be used. A little more flavour boost is added with some minced garlic, salt and ground black pepper. Really a very simple meal that I know you and your family will love  we do!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Chicken Breast Stuffed with Pancetta, Onions and Nuts Recipe by @SpicieFoodie | #chicken #pancetta #nuts #dinner" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.spiciefoodie.com/blogimages/2013/06/StuffedChicken_Pancetta_Nuts03.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         On the side I served potato wedges that I spiced with turmeric, granulated garlic and salt. The potatoes were actually left over from the previous night so it was really a matter of only preparing the chicken breasts for this days dinner. You of course can serve the chicken with another vegetable of choice. But if youd like to know how I make my roasted potatoes please read my
         <a href="http://www.spiciefoodie.com/2012/05/16/spicie-roasted-potatoes-vegan/" title="Spicie Roasted Potatoes, Vegan">
          Spicie Roasted Potatoes recipe.
         </a>
         <br/>
        </p>

          Chicken Breast Stuffed with Pancetta, Onions and Nuts, a delicious and hassle free dinner your family will love. Serve them with turmeric spiced roasted potatoes for an extra healthy boost!
         </div>
         <div class="divERSHeadItems">
          <div class="ERSAuthor">
           Author:
           <span itemprop="author">
            SpicieFoodie.com &amp;Nancy Lopez-McHugh
           </span>
          </div>
          <div class="ERSCategory">
           Recipe type:
           <span itemprop="recipeCategory">
            Dinner

           Cuisine:
           <span itemprop="recipeCuisine">
            World Cuisine
           </span>

          <div class="ERSServes">
           Serves:
           <span itemprop="recipeYield">
            2
           </span>

         </div>
         <div class="ERSIngredients">
          <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">
           Ingredients
          </div>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Stuffing:
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            100 gms pancetta*, finely sliced or chopped
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            1 small yellow (brown) onion, finely chopped
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 tbsp finely crushed nuts of choice**
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            3 garlic cloves, minced
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            pinch of black pepper
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            extra virgin olive oil
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Stuffed Chicken Breasts:
          </div>
          <ul>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            2 boneless and skinless chicken breasts
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            pinch of salt and black pepper
           </li>
           <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
            extra virgin olive oil
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="ERSInstructions">
          <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
           Instructions
          </div>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Stuffing:
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Heat a little bit of olive oil and saute the onions until soft, browned and caramelized. Add the garlic, pancetta and pepper and cook until pancetta is crispy. Drain off any extra oil, add the crushed nuts and cook another 2 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSSectionHead">
           Stuffed Chicken Breasts:
          </div>
          <ol>
           <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            Pound the chicken breasts into an even sized thickness. Rub a bit of salt and pepper on the meat. Add even amounts of the stuffing into the center of each breast, gently tuck and roll the chicken. Heat the oil and once hot add the chicken breasts. Cook on both sides until the juices run clear and the meat feel firm. You may cut into the meat to check for doneness. Serve with your favorite veggie side dishes.
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div class="ERSClear">
          </div>

          <div class="ERSNotesHeader">
           Notes
          </div>
          <div class="ERSNotes">
           *If pancetta is not available, use bacon.
