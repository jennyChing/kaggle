        Visit Euroluce 2015 and have dinner at one of this TOP 10 Restaurants in Milan
       </div>
       <div class="post-6522 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-contemporary category-uncategorized tag-armani-nobu tag-euroluce-2015 tag-fiera-milano tag-giacomo tag-international-lighting-exhibition tag-michelin-stars tag-milan tag-top-10-restaurants-in-milan">
        <div class="content post-inner-single clearfix">
         <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="8140850" data-link="http://www.homedesignideas.eu/visit-euroluce-2015-and-have-dinner-at-one-of-this-top-10-restaurants-in-milan/" data-summary="" data-title="Visit Euroluce 2015 and have dinner at one of this TOP 10 Restaurants in Milan ">
         </div>
         <p>
          <em>
           Ciao
          </em>
          Interior Design
          <em>
           innamorati
          </em>
          ,
         </p>
         <p>
          Euroluce 2015is just one step away and we want to help you having the best experience in Milan. Today Home Design Ideas brings you the
          <span style="color: #333399;">
           TOP 10 Restaurants in Milan
           <span style="color: #333333;">
            to have dinner
           </span>
          </span>
          , because if youre visiting this
          <span id="ID0EGC">
           well-knownInternational Lighting Exhibition that is
           <span style="color: #333399;">
            <a href="http://www.fieramilano.it/" style="color: #333399;" target="_blank">
             Fiera Milano
            </a>
           </span>
           , you will want to seat down at night and have a nice dinner! And you can always expect to eat well in a country that has one of the finest cuisines worldwide!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           ARMANI / NOBU
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          The cuisine at this luxury restaurant is Japanese with Latin Amerian influence.Its like a heavenscombination of high Italian fashion with world-renowned sushi.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Visit Euroluce 2015 and have dinner at one of this TOP 10 Restaurants in Milan  armani" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6531" height="410" src="http://www.homedesignideas.eu/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Visit-Euroluce-2015-and-have-dinner-at-one-of-this-TOP-10-Restaurants-in-Milan-armani.jpg" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           IL LUOGO DI AIMO E NADIA
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Awarded with two Michelin stars, this family restaurant has become a big hit in Milan with its Tuscan cuisine.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="IL LUOGO DI AIMO E NADIA il luogo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6529" height="410" src="http://www.homedesignideas.eu/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/IL-LUOGO-DI-AIMO-E-NADIA-il-luogo.jpg" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           GIACOMO
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Located 15 minutes away from Milanshistorical center, Giacomo is know for its seafood cuisine.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Visit Euroluce 2015 and have dinner at one of this TOP 10 Restaurants in Milan  armani GIACOMO" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6530" height="410" src="http://www.homedesignideas.eu/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Visit-Euroluce-2015-and-have-dinner-at-one-of-this-TOP-10-Restaurants-in-Milan-armani-GIACOMO.jpg" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           CRACCO
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          With an excellent Italian Nouveau cuisine, this is the most awarded restaurant in the city.
          <em>
           Per favore
          </em>
          , give Chef Carlo Cracco a chance.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Visit Euroluce 2015 and have dinner at one of this TOP 10 Restaurants in Milan  craco" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6532" height="410" src="http://www.homedesignideas.eu/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Visit-Euroluce-2015-and-have-dinner-at-one-of-this-TOP-10-Restaurants-in-Milan-craco.jpg" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           IL TEATRO RESTAURANT
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          This famous Italian Gourmet restaurant is placed in the Four Seasons Hotel. The big dealabout this restaurant is that youcan choosethe Chefs Table, a private room where you can dine with the chef and learn the secrets of his recipes.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Visit Euroluce 2015 and have dinner at one of this TOP 10 Restaurants in Milan  IL TEATRO RESTAURANT" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6533" height="410" src="http://www.homedesignideas.eu/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Visit-Euroluce-2015-and-have-dinner-at-one-of-this-TOP-10-Restaurants-in-Milan-IL-TEATRO-RESTAURANT.jpg" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           SADLER
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p class="first-sentence-highlighted">
          Claudio Sadler has dedicated his creativity to Modern Italian cuisine and hesreinventing traditional dishes in bold ways using primary ingredients. It may be the reason for the two Michelin stars.
         </p>
         <p class="first-sentence-highlighted">
          <img alt="Visit Euroluce 2015 and have dinner at one of this TOP 10 Restaurants in Milan sadler" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6538" height="410" src="http://www.homedesignideas.eu/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Visit-Euroluce-2015-and-have-dinner-at-one-of-this-TOP-10-Restaurants-in-Milan-sadler1.jpg" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p class="first-sentence-highlighted">
          <strong>
           ALLA CUCINA DELLE LANGHE
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p class="first-sentence-highlighted">
          I just need to say a thing about this place: Tom Ford isthis restaurants most famous customer!
         </p>
         <p class="first-sentence-highlighted">
          <img alt="Visit Euroluce 2015 and have dinner at one of this TOP 10 Restaurants in Milan alla cucina" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-6534" height="410" src="http://www.homedesignideas.eu/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/Visit-Euroluce-2015-and-have-dinner-at-one-of-this-TOP-10-Restaurants-in-Milan-alla-cucina1.jpg" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p class="first-sentence-highlighted">
