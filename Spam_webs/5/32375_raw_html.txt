         <p>
          <em>
           Face Time is a new monthly column on Glam in which we ask Canadian beauty experts, social influencers, and all-around tastemakers to give us a sneak peek into their beauty stash. For this edition, we put the spotlight on Aldos PR darling
           <strong>
            Kathy Acimovic
           </strong>
           .
          </em>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://ca.glam.com/files/2013/08/Facetime_KathyAcimovic.jpg">
           <img alt="Facetime" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-301434" src="http://ca.glam.com/files/2013/08/Facetime_KathyAcimovic.jpg"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Aldo Senior Strategist,
          <strong>
           Kathy Acimovic
          </strong>
          , is the Montreal-based, shoe-obsessed gal better known to all of her digital followers as Aldo PR Girl (
          <a href="https://twitter.com/shoesCherie" target="_blank">
           @ShoesCherie
          </a>
          ). Here, the public relations wiz proves that shes not just a master when it comes to her shoe closet, but she also knows a thing or two about how to how to keep up a well-stocked vanity.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: In 140 characters or less, how would you describe your personal beauty philosophy?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          Face that says Im not trying, hair thats the envy of frenemies, and nails that scratch somewhere between femme fatale and its my party.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: What are your five must-have beauty products?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          
          <a href="http://www.sephora.com/yves-saint-laurent#!&amp;brandName=yves-saint-laurent&amp;pageSize=60&amp;defaultPageSize=60&amp;sortBy=-1&amp;defaultSortBy=-1&amp;buttonMediaId=13700134" target="_blank">
           YSL Touche clat
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.en.laroche-posay.ca/International/beauty/home-h.aspx" target="_blank">
           La Roche-Posay Effaclar Duo
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.sephora.com/guerlain" target="_blank">
           Guerlain bronzer
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://www.chanel.com/en_US/fragrance-beauty/Makeup-88484" target="_blank">
           Chanel Sublimage La Crme Yeux
          </a>
          , and
          <a href="http://www.maccosmetics.com/index.tmpl?LOCALE=en_CA" target="_blank">
           MAC Lipstick
          </a>
          in Myth mixed with lipbalm for a natural, nude lip.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: What is your usual skincare routine?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          I have skin prone to blemishes, so I cant be too indulgent with creams. I cleanse, tone and moisturize my skin morning and night with the La Roche-Posay Effaclar lineif you have problem skin, theres nothing better! And, I try to use my
          <a href="http://www.clarisonic.ca/en/Home.html" target="_blank">
           Clarisonic
          </a>
          at least once a day, usually in the evening.
          <br/>
          <strong>
           <br/>
           Glam: Which beauty products do you like to splurge on and which are usually drugstore buys?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          Eye creams are my splurge of choice and I like any product or brand that begins with La, i.e.
          <a href="http://shop.nordstrom.com/s/la-mer-moisturizing-cream-ultrarich-cream/3057002" target="_blank">
           La Mer
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.shoplaprairie.com/default.aspx" target="_blank">
           La Prairie
          </a>
          . Im a huge fan of both La Roche-Posay and
          <a href="http://www.en.vichy.ca/Canada/Laboratoires-Vichy/home-h.aspx" target="_blank">
           Vichy
          </a>
          ; its always a bit of a wonder to me that theyre drugstore brands. Every once and a while I also like to add a
          <a href="http://www.maybelline.ca/" target="_blank">
           Maybelline
          </a>
          product to my cart. Maybe its nostalgia, but its also never failed me.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: Do you have a signature fragrance?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          Silver Mountain Water by
          <a href="http://www.creedboutique.com/" target="_blank">
           Creed
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: Are there any products youd love to try but havent yet?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          La Mer Body Crme.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: What is your usual day-to-day beauty routine?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          I cover up any imperfections with
          <a href="http://www.lisewatier.com/ca_en/portfolio-professional-correctors/" target="_blank">
           Lise Watiers Portfolio Corrector
          </a>
          , then apply some YSL Touche clat to freshen up my under eye area. I give myself a tan with my Guerlain bronzer, and finish off with mascara.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: What new beauty trends are you excited to try for fall?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          Im most trend-adventurous with my nails. Each fall I dabble back into a smoky eye. That said, I do love experimenting with colored mascara.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: Whats your favourite special occasion beauty trick?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          
          <a href="http://shop.nordstrom.com/c/giorgio-armani" target="_blank">
           Giorgio Armani
          </a>
          has these black stick-on crystals that you can apply along your eyelid. They look especially great when you do a smoky eye. Its unexpected, and when they catch the light your eyes literally sparkle. I think they may have been discontinued, and in fear of this I stocked up a while back! Oh, and
          <a href="http://www.shuuemura.ca/" target="_blank">
           Shu Uemura fake eyelashes
          </a>
          never disappointsometimes they turn out to be more special than the occasion itself.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: Whos in your Glam squad?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          My hairdresser is the longest relationship Ive had to date. I swear by
          <a href="http://www.orbite.com" target="_blank">
           Orbite
          </a>
          and, for the record, Louis Hechter is the only person allowed near me with a pair of scissors. My colourist is Susan Carter and she is actually a miracle worker. Its rare to find a colourist who can master both brunettes and blondes. As for my nails, I have a weekly appointment with Jenny at
          <a href="http://www.tamibeautedesongles.ca" target="_blank">
           Tamis
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: Whats your usual form of exercise?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          A weekly mix of pilates, soul cycle-style spinning and Bikram yoga. When you hit your thirties theres no messing around, and with no muscle memory from my twenties I have to overcompensate
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: Have you had any past beauty blunders that youd rather forget?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          Um yeah, that time I thought Id colour my own hair red. I blame Claire Danes and My So-Called Life.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Glam: Who is your beauty inspiration?
          </strong>
          <br/>
          <strong>
           Kathy:
          </strong>
          Is it wrong to say Gwyneth? Mostly because I feel beauty is more than just what products you use; its also what you eat and how you live.
         </p>

           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
