       <p>
        Sure, this New Zealand home is heavy on the industrial, nuclear reactor look, but it has a certain draw to it, don't you think? I was pointed to these images in a
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/petraalsbach/sets/72157594572387467/">
         flickr photoset
        </a>
        owned by
        <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/petraalsbach/sets/">
         petraalsbach
        </a>
        and was struck by the interesting use of containers  as you can tell, the home was built right up to, and possibly into, the hill. Containers are strong and stackable,and it seems like lots of people are using them right now in home design. Container homes may just become more popular than modern prefab
