        <p>
         You guys may know by now that I work in the tech industry. I am a self-professed tech geek, and I love my iPhone more than life itself. (Okay, maybe thats a slight exaggeration, but you get what Im saying.) Something I love about iPhones are the amazing amount of helpful apps in the App Store.
        </p>
        <p>
         I challenge myself to download 5 news ones each day for my job, you know, research and all. Comes with the designer territory. But since I do so much work related research, I figured Id share some of the best ones Ive downloaded recently that I think are helpful for the holiday season! So read on for
         <strong>
          10 helpful Christmasapps for iPhone
         </strong>
         , that should make your busy holiday season a little easier.
        </p>

         10 Helpful Christmas Apps for iPhone
        </h2>
        <p>
         <span id="more-3015">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3>
         Shopping
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/retailmenot-santas-bag-christmas-gift-list-iphone-apps.jpg" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="RetailMeNot &amp; Santa's Bag Christmas Gift List iPhone Apps / hellorigby!" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3111" height="617" src="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/retailmenot-santas-bag-christmas-gift-list-iphone-apps.jpg" width="700"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://hellorigby.com/10-helpful-christmas-apps-for-iphone/&amp;media=http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/retailmenot-santas-bag-christmas-gift-list-iphone-apps.jpg&amp;description=10 Helpful Christmas Apps for iPhone')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         1.
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/retailmenot-coupons/id521207075?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/retailmenot-coupons/id521207075?mt=8', 'RetailMeNot']);" target="_blank" title="RetailMeNot">
          <strong>
           RetailMeNot
          </strong>
         </a>
         (Free) This is basically the same as their website, but what I like about the app is that theyll use your location to send you sale alerts. Make sure you favorite stores you like to shop, and save your favorite deals.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Bonus:
         </strong>

          Cartwheel App
         </a>
         for extracoupon savings. Another great companion is
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/shopkick-deals-free-gift-cards/id383298204?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/shopkick-deals-free-gift-cards/id383298204?mt=8', 'ShopKick']);" target="_blank" title="Shopkick App">

          my referral link here
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         2.
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/santas-bag-christmas-gift/id397698040?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/santas-bag-christmas-gift/id397698040?mt=8', 'Santas Bag Christmas Gift List']);" target="_blank" title="Santa's Bag Christmas Gift List App">
          <strong>
           Santas Bag Christmas Gift List
          </strong>
         </a>
         (Free) This is a neat little tool that helps you track who youve bought for, how much of your budget youve spent per person, and what youve purchased.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Travel
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/uber-lyft-car-rideshare-apps-iphone.jpg" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Uber &amp; Lyft Car Rideshare Travel Apps for iPhone / hellorigby!" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3113" height="732" src="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/uber-lyft-car-rideshare-apps-iphone.jpg" width="830"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://hellorigby.com/10-helpful-christmas-apps-for-iphone/&amp;media=http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/uber-lyft-car-rideshare-apps-iphone.jpg&amp;description=10 Helpful Christmas Apps for iPhone')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         3.
         <strong>
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/uber/id368677368?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/uber/id368677368?mt=8', 'Uber']);" target="_blank" title="Uber App">
           Uber
          </a>
          &amp;
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/lyft/id529379082?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/lyft/id529379082?mt=8', 'Lyft']);" target="_blank" title="Lyft App">
           Lyft
          </a>
         </strong>
         (Free) If youre going to be traveling to a city this holiday season, I highly recommend using either Uber or Lyft. Both are ride share services, and are awesome for last-minute rides. With Lyft, you can sit in the front seat with your driver, because theyre typically just normal people who drive part-time. Uber tends to be off-duty cab drivers or town car services, so sit in the back in their vehicles. ;)We use UberX all the time in Seattle. (Get a free ride with

          Lyft
         </a>
         )
        </p>
        <h3>
         Tunes
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/spotify-music-app-iphone.jpg" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Spotify Music App for iPhone / hellorigby!" class="alignnone wp-image-3114" height="622" src="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/spotify-music-app-iphone.jpg" width="350"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://hellorigby.com/10-helpful-christmas-apps-for-iphone/&amp;media=http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/spotify-music-app-iphone.jpg&amp;description=10 Helpful Christmas Apps for iPhone')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         4.
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/spotify-music/id324684580?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/spotify-music/id324684580?mt=8', 'Spotify']);" target="_blank" title="Spotify App">
          <strong>
           Spotify
          </strong>
         </a>
         (Free) Create your holiday playlist! What I dig about Spotify is that you can create a collaborative playlist and add/remove songs as you please with anyone who also is on Spotify. Were doing this at work right now for our office playlist, and its so fun to hear everyones favorites. Or if you dont have time to create a playlist of your own, try
         <strong>
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/christmas-radio/id296382774?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/christmas-radio/id296382774?mt=8', 'Christmas Radio']);" target="_blank" title="Christmas Radio">
           Christmas Radio
          </a>
         </strong>
         .
        </p>
        <h3>
         Gift Giving
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/elfster-secret-santa-wishlist-iphone-app.jpg" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Elfster Secret Santa Wishlist iPhone App / hellorigby!" class="alignnone wp-image-3121" height="622" src="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/elfster-secret-santa-wishlist-iphone-app.jpg" width="350"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://hellorigby.com/10-helpful-christmas-apps-for-iphone/&amp;media=http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/elfster-secret-santa-wishlist-iphone-app.jpg&amp;description=10 Helpful Christmas Apps for iPhone')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         5.
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/US/app/id751594890" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/US/app/id751594890', 'Elfster']);" target="_blank" title="Elfster 2.0 App">
          <strong>
           Elfster
          </strong>
         </a>
         (Free) In charge of organizing your offices Secret Santa? No sweat, just have everyone sign up for Elfster! Itll do the dirty work like drawing names, and you can secretly ask questions of your draw too.
        </p>
        <h3>
         Food
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/yummly-butterball-turkey-app-iphone-food-cooking.jpg" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Yummly &amp; Butterball Turkey iPhone App for Food and Cooking / hellorigby!" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3115" height="732" src="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/yummly-butterball-turkey-app-iphone-food-cooking.jpg" width="830"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://hellorigby.com/10-helpful-christmas-apps-for-iphone/&amp;media=http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/yummly-butterball-turkey-app-iphone-food-cooking.jpg&amp;description=10 Helpful Christmas Apps for iPhone')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         6.
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/yummly-recipes-grocery-shopping/id589625334?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/yummly-recipes-grocery-shopping/id589625334?mt=8', 'Yummly']);" target="_blank" title="Yummly">
          <strong>
           Yummly
          </strong>
         </a>
         (Free) Save recipes and get a grocery list, all in the same app. What I like about their recipe selection is that they grab the best of the best  from places like Food52, Steamy Kitchen, and AllRecipes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Bonus:
         </strong>
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/butterball-cookbook-plus-turkey/id549904778?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/butterball-cookbook-plus-turkey/id549904778?mt=8', 'Butterball Cookbook Plus']);" target="_blank" title="Butterball Cookbook Plus">
          <strong>
           Butterball Cookbook Plus
          </strong>
         </a>
         (Free) Do you know how to cook a turkey? I sure dont, but I would trust the people at Butterball to help me through cooking the bird!
        </p>
        <h3>
         Planning
        </h3>
        <p>
         <a href="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/flashplan-event-planning-app-dreamdays-countdown-iphone-app.jpg" target="_blank">
          <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
           <img alt="Flashplan Event Planning App and Dreamdays Countdown iPhone App / hellorigby!" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3119" height="732" src="http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/flashplan-event-planning-app-dreamdays-countdown-iphone-app.jpg" width="830"/>
           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://hellorigby.com/10-helpful-christmas-apps-for-iphone/&amp;media=http://hellorigby.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/flashplan-event-planning-app-dreamdays-countdown-iphone-app.jpg&amp;description=10 Helpful Christmas Apps for iPhone')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         7.
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/flashplan/id849449789?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/flashplan/id849449789?mt=8', 'Flashplan']);" target="_blank" title="Flashplan App">
          <strong>
           Flashplan
          </strong>
         </a>
         (Free) Too many event sites to choose from? Get everyone on the same mobile page with Flashplan. Instead of requiring Facebook, Twitter, or another account, Flashplan just uses your phone number to create an account.
        </p>
        <p>
         8.
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/lite-all-new-dreamdays-countdown/id827626206?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/lite-all-new-dreamdays-countdown/id827626206?mt=8', 'Dreamdays ']);" target="_blank" title="All New Dreamdays Lite App ">
          <strong>
           Dreamdays
          </strong>
         </a>
         (Free)Use Dreamdays to countdown to the days that matter  Christmas, New Years, your birthday. Whatever it is, add your date and youre set!
        </p>
        <h3>
         For Fun
        </h3>
        <p>
         9.
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/reindeercam/id568939466?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/reindeercam/id568939466?mt=8', 'ReindeerCam']);" target="_blank" title="ReindeerCam">
          <strong>
           ReindeerCam
          </strong>
         </a>
         ($1.99) Basically the Puppy Cam for Reindeer, this cute little app would probably be fun to share with the little ones in your life or the co-workers who still act like children. ;)
        </p>
        <p>
         10.
         <strong>
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/a-charlie-brown-thanksgiving/id703585226?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/a-charlie-brown-thanksgiving/id703585226?mt=8', 'A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving']);" target="_blank" title="Charlie Brown Thanksgiving App">
           A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving
          </a>
          or
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/a-charlie-brown-christmas/id476508724?mt=8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/a-charlie-brown-christmas/id476508724?mt=8', 'Christmas']);" target="_blank" title="Charlie Brown Christmas App">
           Christmas
          </a>
         </strong>
         ($5.99 each) If you love the classic Charlie Brown Holiday Specials, these are for you! Experience the holidays with Charlie and gang in a new and interactive way on your iPhone or iPad.
        </p>
        <p>
         What apps do you download and use for the holidays? Id love to hear your suggestions!
        </p>
