        <h2>
         The Sun Has Got His Hat On Dont Forget Your Camera!
        </h2>
        <small>
         9th June 2014 14:54 | Tags: Photography, Countryside, Architecture, Cotswolds, England  | comments(0)
        </small>
        <p>
         The sun has got his hat on and were looking forward to weekends of glorious sunshine. There are lots of places to visit across the country, both indoors and out. Whatever you are doing this weekend  Dont forget your camera!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="The Sun Has Got His Hat On Dont Forget Your Camera!" height="422" src="../media/The-Old-Mill.jpg" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="The Old Mill." width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         When travelling around, you never know when you'll find yourself a perfect photo opportunity moment. So have that camera ready, and be looking for unusual, quirky, or just something you like.
        </p>
        <p>
         Theres something enjoyable about looking back through your weekends snaps when you get home.
        </p>
        <p>
         Make the most of your pics and print and frame them. You will need good quality
         <a href="../printer-ink-cartridges">
          printer ink
         </a>
         or
         <a href="../toner-cartridges">
          toner cartridges
         </a>
         and high quality
         <a href="../paper">
          photo paper
         </a>
         .
        </p>
