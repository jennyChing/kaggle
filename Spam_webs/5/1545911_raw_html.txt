        <h2>
         Exchange Ideas and Insights
        </h2>
        <p>
         As an online student, you have the opportunity to connect with students and faculty from a variety of locations and backgrounds. The result is an engaging and vibrant exchange of ideas, opinions, perspectives, and experience. Our online learning experience also encourages a deeper level of student discussion and more frequent conversations that involve everyone in your online classroom.
        </p>
        <p>
         Through these interactions with professionals from different fields and cultural experiences, you can gain a broad range of perspectives and best practices to apply in your own work and
         <a href="/experience/walden/make-an-impact">
          community
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Learn more about the network youll form at our online university.
         <a href="/request-information-us">
          Request information
         </a>
         or call 1-866-492-5336 in the United States or see a list of
         <a href="/contact">
          international phone numbers
         </a>
         .
