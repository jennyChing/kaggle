        </h1>
        <aside class="entry-meta">

        <p style="text-align: center">
         <img alt="coffee-bar.jpg" height="118" src="http://www.caffination.com/wp-content/uploads//2008/03/coffee-bar.jpg" width="381"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Today is a special day.
        </p>
        <p>
         Everyday is special but today is the first full day that the silliness over at Twitter has truly embraced @
         <a href="http://wwww.twitter.com/teamcoffee" target="_blank">
          TeamCoffee
         </a>
         . So in honor of our first full day on the planet I figured a Form over Function Friday dedicated to the team as an inspiration as well. Form Over Function Friday is a recent tradition here at the CaffiNation Podcast. Basically every friday I take some element of Podsafe, independant music, and work it into a design which ends up as a wallpaper, available to you for your download enjoyment.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-588">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         For the podsafe music in my head while I worked on this? Well last night I was kicking around a little
         <a href="http://www.shupe.net" target="_blank">
          Ryan Shupe and the rubberband
         </a>
         . They are a fun band to listen to so it made embracing the nonsense even that much easier. They have a light hearted style with a unique sound, which is perfect for our little cadre of well caffeinated people.
        </p>
        <p>
         For the CaffiNation Regulars who may not know what this nonsense is about i direct you not only to the brand new team page at the top of the site, but
         <a href="http://www.caffination.com/minutia/go-team-coffee-581/" target="_blank">
          my original posting on the matter
