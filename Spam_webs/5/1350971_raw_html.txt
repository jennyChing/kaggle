          <p>
           <strong>
            Lincoln never was famous for making sports car, but that doesnt mean that they dont try to do that.
           </strong>
           <br/>
           Back in 1955 Fords luxury marque made model Indianapolis in collaboration with Carrozzeria Boano, an Italian coachbuilder that build Ghia the year before.
          </p>
          <p>
           They made orange coupe with V8 engine that produce 200HP and 4-speed automatic transmission.
          </p>
          <p>
           The promotion of this model was at the Turin Motor Show.
           <br/>
           1955 Lincoln Indianapolis concept is now going up for auction organized by RM Auctions and Sothebys for upcoming Art of the Automobile event in Manhattan n November 21.
