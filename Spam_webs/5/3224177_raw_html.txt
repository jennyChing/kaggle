              It is all fun and laughters until you realize how efficient some individuals have become in the art of seduction. Two of my best friends are literally dating supermodels from Europe and America without having the need to drive a Ferrari or Lamborghini from wealth accumulation. One of their dates had an ex-boyfriend who plays in the English Premier League and earns a quarter of a million pounds a week. The other of my friend dates a supermodel from America who earns in the 5 figures every month through cat walks and photo-shoots. So what makes two of my best friends so attractive despite the conceived notion that only the ultra rich can date the super beautiful?
             </p>
             <p>
              In fact there exists even more efficient seducers in modern time. One of these seducers, Richard La Ruina, a dating coach and an entrepreneur, was able to travel to a new location with only the flight ticket to that city. Upon alighting from the flight, he would begin approaching women on the streets. He is so efficient at it that they would lend him their place to stay over. He was able to successfully perform this so much so that he travelled the entire Europe spending only on flights. Today, I shall share with you how a masculine man should behave in order to flip primitive hard-wired attraction switches in women.
             </p>
             <h1>
              <strong>
               1) Dominance
              </strong>
             </h1>
             <p>
              In general, very successful men are so domineering that it scares off the weak. I do not refer to men who use violence and physical assaults to attain authority so as to scare off others, but rather men who are so emotionally stable, assertive and decisive that others can witness their strength from within.
             </p>
             <p>
              I was out with one of my friends to Clarke Quay and we saw 2 ladies with 10 other guys in a group. My friend went up alone and approached the girl he likes anyway and was not afraid of confrontation whatsoever because he is courageous in his pursue. After a few minutes, the other guys became affected by our presence, but my friend was willing to put some fists into their face for being rude to us. But most of the time if a girl is surrounded by plenty of other men, yet you are fearless in your approach, can demonstrate extreme level of confidence only found in very successful and powerful men. So go out there, and approach anyway regardless of who she might be with.
             </p>
             <h1>
              <strong>
               2) Clarity Of Purpose
              </strong>
             </h1>
             <p>
              Knowing what you want and going after it with pure determination and conviction because it is a mans role to be clear of his lifes objective. Beating around the bush and becoming passive is simply not a mans way of dealing with obstacles. Becoming a man is about knowing what you want to achieve in your life and going after it regardless of the pain and sacrifices you have to make.
             </p>
             <p>
              Our ancestors have to be clear in their mind when they are out hunting large and dangerous preys as confusion in strategic execution will meant death. Therefore, as an alpha male of the tribe, you must be able to direct and assert with overwhelming power so as to take down the ferocious beast.
             </p>
             <h1>
              <strong>
               3) Responsibility
              </strong>
             </h1>
             <p>
              To take responsibility in your life is to accept all that has happened to you is a result of your action and your inaction. If you begin pushing blames and responsibilities to other events or people, you will never be able to change the fate of your life. Whatever has happened to the one who you are, is a result of your habits and your attitude towards life.
             </p>
             <p>
              When I first began my journey in pick up artistry, I was willing to accept the fact that I have so much more to learn and I put all my pride and ego aside so that I may become a stronger version of myself. I accepted the fact that I am not an attractive man yet, and I was willing to get rejected over and over again so that I may learn from my mistakes and think in creative ways to better my social skills. In fact, I got rejected approximately 40 times before I got my first number because I was that bad at cold approach pick up.
             </p>
             <p>
              These 3 simple pointers shared today can take weeks and months or even years as changing yourself from within is much harder to accomplish than the alteration of your exterior shell. But if you can push your mind consistently, you will be much able to withstand harsh conditions and circumstances because your mind has already been sharpened through the endeavours you have undertook. Now get out there and chase after your dreams relentlessly making sure that you have truly given your gift to the world as much as you can.
