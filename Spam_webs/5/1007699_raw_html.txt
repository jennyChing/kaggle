     <h1 title="Plastic Surgery New York City">
      Plastic Surgery in New York City
     </h1>
     <p>
      Plastic Surgery is a very important decision. You need surgeons who understand your need and can connect with you on a professional and personal level. Surgeons who are attentive and work towards making sure that you look and feel your best. An AAAASF-accredited plastic surgery practice in Manhattan, New York City, bodySCULPT
      <sup>
       
      </sup>
      offers a wide range of surgical and non-invasive procedures to help you achieve your aesthetic goals. Our plastic surgeons - Spero J Theodorou and Christopher T Chia are experienced in a variety of procedures that will be of help for all those who want to achieve a beautiful, balanced, healthy and well-defined physique.
     </p>
     <p class="heading">
      State-of-the Art Technologies
     </p>
     <p>
      All our treatments are performed using the latest technologies and techniques for safe and effective results. The advanced technologies we use at our NYC practice include.
     </p>
     <ul>
      <li>
       <a href="smartlipo-triplex.html" title="SmartLipo Triplex">
        <strong class="orange">
         SmartLipo Triplex
        </strong>
       </a>
       : The laser-enabled multi-wavelength body sculpting device works to disrupt localized fat deposits and tighten loose skin.
      </li>
      <li>
       <a href="bodytite-liposuction-women.html" title="BodyTite">
        <strong class="orange">
         BodyTite
        </strong>
       </a>
       : This radiofrequency assisted liposuction (RFAL) technology removes stubborn fat and achieves significant body tightening with its gentle, deep heating capability.
      </li>
      <li>
       <a class="orange" href="vaserlipo.html" title="VASERlipo">
        <strong>
         VASERlipo
        </strong>
       </a>
       : Ultrasonic waves in this device break up and separate fat cells for removal, leaving the surrounding tissues unharmed.
      </li>
      <li>
       <a class="orange" href="cellulaze-cellulite-treatment.html" title="Cellulaze">
        <strong>
         Cellulaze
        </strong>
       </a>
       :  Cynosure's new technology Cellulaze is FDA approved and is the first one time treatment for cellulite.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p>
      Ask for a virtual consultation with one of our non-physician staff.
     </p>
