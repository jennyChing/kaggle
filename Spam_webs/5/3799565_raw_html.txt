            <p>
             <strong>
              Food, music and YOU
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Being involved in the entertainment industry, we regularly observe the existence of an abundance of great artists searching for opportunities to get on stage and perform. There is also a huge party-hungry audience who is very much open to the idea of attending various kinds of live acts as much it is to the idea of going out and enjoying. The only thing missing from this is the fact there exists a lack of a connection between the venues andthe artists. What can
             <a href="http://www.gigstart.com">
              Gigstart
             </a>
             do? Where can we help?
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://blog.gigstart.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/cropped-concert-audience-786x305.jpg">

            <p>
             The ideology and the concept behind Gigstart is that we want to get artists hired, get them appreciative audiences. Thus, it only seemed logical for Gigstart to expand and come up with something that allows restaurants and pubs to pitch in and help us accomplish that. This idea became the proud mother of
             <strong>
              Gigstart Live n Local
             </strong>
             . A destination where one can listen to various genres of music played live by independent artists belonging to that locality. Gigstart Live n Local thus aims to be an opportunity for various new and independent artists to get regular and paid gigs all around Delhi, as of now. The event itself would be a package of good music at a great venue with delicious food.
            </p>

             <strong>
              More venues, more artists and a bigger audience
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Gigstart has already collaborated with some of the best eateries, including fine dining and cafes, on a shared-cost basis where Gigstart will be paying a certain amount of the cost that the gig incurs so that even those venues which are hesitant to book artists for live performances start taking a chance on new and independent artists. We are currently in talks with multiplevenues. The response from these venues has been spectacular and the openness that they have shown to the idea of Gigstart Live n Local is inspiring. If youre a venue which would like to collaborate with us, please find contact details at the end of this article.
            </p>
            <p>
             Though, the first couple of gigs may only involve bands and musicians, we have already on-boarded various magicians and stand-ups for the second phase of Gigstart Live n Local.

            <p>
             As for the artists, we have a full roster of bands, solo artists and duos/trios, which are ready to go on stage and perform. These musicians would be covering various genres like rock, jazz, fusion, blues, acoustic etc. We are still looking to expand our roster of artists, so we welcome any musician who would like to collaborate with us (contact details at the end of this article).
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              Expect us soon, very soon!
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             Though we are beginning with only Delhi, we wish to be present all over India someday. The first gig which would be part of Gigstart Live n Local will be held on the 31st of January with Sould Out performingat the Quote Bar, CP. The bands will be announced soon as well. Stay tuned in to our
