          <p style="text-align: left;">
           <a href="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/POSTAGE.png">
            <img alt="what to get students study abroad" class="aligncenter wp-image-2847" height="440" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/POSTAGE.png" width="587"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           <em>
            With the holiday season not too far away, youre bound to be looking for unique and useful gift ideas for friends and family. Our Traveler Gift Guide series will help you find the perfect gift,
            <i style="color: #4d4d4d;">
             whether its for
             <a href="http://theabroadguide.com/gift-idea-study-abroad-students/" style="color: #56a5ec;" title="11 Gift Ideas for Students Studying Abroad">
              a student who is about to study abroad
             </a>
             , a family member whos
             <a href="http://theabroadguide.com/gift-ideas-students-suffering-reverse-culture-shock/" style="color: #56a5ec;" title="The Perfect Gifts for People Suffering From Reverse Culture Shock">
              just returned from abroad
             </a>
             , someone who
             <a href="http://theabroadguide.com/gift-guide-constant-traveler/" style="color: #56a5ec;" title="Gift Ideas for the Constant Traveler in Your Life">
              always seems to be traveling
             </a>
             , or
             <a href="http://theabroadguide.com/gift-guide-traveling-shutterbug/" title="Gift Ideas for Your Favorite Traveling Shutterbug">
              your favorite shutterbug
             </a>
             .
            </i>
           </em>
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Pick up one of these gifts for a future study abroad-er and youll be getting them something that they need and will definitely use!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00LHI4Y24/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00LHI4Y24&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=sweatpandsung-20&amp;linkId=747BAWKRGQFCSL6U">
            <img alt="Gifts for study abroad students, what to give someone going abroad" class="aligncenter wp-image-2826" height="377" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/LuggageOwl-199x300.jpg" width="250"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            1. Carry-on sized luggage
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Study abroaders travel a ton during the semester so a good piece of
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00LHI4Y24/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00LHI4Y24&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=sweatpandsung-20&amp;linkId=747BAWKRGQFCSL6U" target="_blank">
            luggage
           </a>
           that is small enough to carry onto budget airline flights such as RyanAir will definitely be appreciated. Try the colorful option above or check out our post on choosing between suitcases and backpacks for more options.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002VS8H3G/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B002VS8H3G&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=sweatpandsung-20&amp;linkId=OLPT3OXRT2KWZT6S">
            <img alt="gifts for study abroad students, presents for someone studying abroad" class="aligncenter wp-image-2825 size-medium" height="300" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/go_toob_3_pack-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            2. Travel-sized refillable bottles
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           It can often be tough to find travel sizes of toiletries in foreign countries, so by having their own refillable travel-sized bottles, your traveler will be able to just fill them up as needed without getting their much-needed shampoo taken away at airport security check.
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002VS8H3G/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B002VS8H3G&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=sweatpandsung-20&amp;linkId=OLPT3OXRT2KWZT6S" target="_blank">
            Go Toobs
           </a>
           are a new favorite of ours as theyre easier to refill than other options.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0009VCB6Q/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0009VCB6Q&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=MNMJTLOGEVMMMGHV">
            <img alt="gift ideas for study abroad students, gift ideas for someone going abroad" class="aligncenter wp-image-2818 size-medium" height="300" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/51D7aiaLv0L._SY450_-138x300.jpg" width="138"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            3. Space Bags
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Especially good for overpackers,
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0009VCB6Q/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B0009VCB6Q&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=MNMJTLOGEVMMMGHV" target="_blank">
            Space Bags
           </a>
           will help your future study abroader pack more in less space. This pack has an array of different sizes and doesnt need a vacuum to use.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B005X1Y7I2/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B005X1Y7I2&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=JSESHRECQDGJHIVC">
            <img alt="gift guide for students going abroad, what to get study abroad student" class="aligncenter wp-image-2821 size-medium" height="300" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/PhoneCharger-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            4. Portable phone charger
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Perfect for those long travel days where listening to their favorite music brings their phone battery to zero percent. Also great for study abroaders who love to take videos with a portable charger, they can do so without worrying about being phone-less. This
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B005X1Y7I2/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B005X1Y7I2&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=JSESHRECQDGJHIVC" target="_blank">
            charger
           </a>
           option on Amazon is highly rated and a great value.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00JG8GOWU/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00JG8GOWU&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=4XDODFA2T6RXV45K">
            <img alt="gift ideas for travelers, what to get someone going abroad" class="aligncenter wp-image-2827" height="292" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/popup-paperwhite-diff-2-300x266.jpg" width="329"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            5. Kindle
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           If your traveler is a bookworm, then a
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00JG8GOWU/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00JG8GOWU&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=4XDODFA2T6RXV45K" target="_blank">
            Kindle
           </a>
           is a must. It saves a ton of space in travel bags and is much lighter than carrying a book or two. An
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gift-cards/b/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;node=2238192011&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=HO55DZMBATLCDUCR" target="_blank">
            Amazon Gift Card
           </a>
           would go well with this so they can download some books before they go.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.uncommongoods.com/product/scratch-travel-journal">
            <img alt="cool gift ideas for study abroad students, what to get someone going to study abroad" class="aligncenter wp-image-2816" height="340" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Travelogue-300x300.jpg" width="355"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            6. Journal
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Even if your study abroader is planning on blogging while abroad, a journal will come in handy for remembering those little bits of pieces of their weekend away while they dont have a computer with them. Journaling can also help pass the time something that every traveler is thankful for! Go for a traditional one or take it up a notch and give them this awesome
           <a href="http://www.uncommongoods.com/product/scratch-travel-journal" target="_blank">
            scratch travel
           </a>
           .
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00E0FZSQC/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00E0FZSQC&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=G6VVV6OCTR3NMPDP">
            <img alt="christmas ideas for someone going abroad, christmas gift ideas for someone studying abroad" class="aligncenter wp-image-2815 size-medium" height="300" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Adaptor-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            7. Power Adapter
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           A must-have for anyone traveling. Pick up this universal one and theyll never be stuck without power.
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00E0FZSQC/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00E0FZSQC&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=G6VVV6OCTR3NMPDP" target="_blank">
            Adapters
           </a>
           come in many different ways for specific countries, but more and more, they are compact and have all adaptors so you dont have to worry. If you do want to just get one for where theyre going, make sure to research where theyll be traveling as well so you know their adapter will be easy to use everywhere they go!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004UB056E/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B004UB056E&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=6ZFXPWW76KXR5XWO">
            <img alt="what to get a student going abroad, what to get study abroad student" class="aligncenter wp-image-2820 size-medium" height="300" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/TravelPillow-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            8. Travel neck pillow
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Give them a
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B004UB056E/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B004UB056E&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=6ZFXPWW76KXR5XWO" target="_blank">
            travel pillow
           </a>
           . You can thank us later.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00I58LPYO/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00I58LPYO&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=HL3SJ2ICHEU6Z6WW">
            <img alt="what to get your friend going abroad, what to get for your child studying abroad" class="aligncenter wp-image-2819" height="241" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/71sZtnMTUKL._SX522_-300x200.jpg" width="362"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3>
           <b>
            9. Camera
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Every student going abroad wants and needs a camera. Memories fade, but pictures dont. A great camera for study abroad that is of good value is
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00I58LPYO/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00I58LPYO&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=HL3SJ2ICHEU6Z6WW" target="_blank">
            Canon PowerShot ELPH135
           </a>
           ,but weve got more suggestions for
           <a href="http://theabroadguide.com/the-3-best-cameras-for-study-abroad/" target="_blank" title="3 Awesome Digital Cameras for Study Abroad">
            great study abroad cameras
           </a>
           if youre looking to give your traveler something a bit more advanced.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Lonely-Planet/e/B00M9Y0CHQ/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;qid=1416146468&amp;sr=1-2-ent&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=NJNB2ZGLLIKEGCHO">
            <img alt="Gifts for Study aBroad, what to get for study abroad" class="aligncenter wp-image-2845 size-full" height="346" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Gifts-for-Study-aBroad.jpg" width="225"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            10. Travel guide for their study abroad destination
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p>
           Student travelers spend the most time in their study abroad destination, but they can sometimes find it hard to discover bits and pieces due to trying to balance schoolwork, traveling and a social life. Gift a travel guide for their city and it will help them take the guesswork out of exploring their new home.
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/s/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;field-keywords=lonely%20planet&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;url=search-alias%3Daps&amp;linkId=OHYWZXRO7WQI73AD" target="_blank">
            Lonely
           </a>
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Lonely-Planet/e/B00M9Y0CHQ/?_encoding=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;linkCode=ur2&amp;qid=1416146468&amp;sr=1-2-ent&amp;tag=theabroadguide-20&amp;linkId=NJNB2ZGLLIKEGCHO" target="_blank">
            Planet
           </a>
           books or e-books are some of our favorites, or check out thispostfor
           <a href="http://theabroadguide.com/use-guide-books-traveling/" target="_blank" title="Ditch the Digital: Why You Should Use a Print Guidebook When Traveling">
            more travel guides
           </a>
           that we approve of.
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
           <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/151322971/kansas-city-missouri-map-pillow-green?ref=shop_home_active_22">
            <img alt="fun gifts for someone studying abroad, gift guide for studying abroad" class="aligncenter wp-image-2828" height="298" src="http://theabroadguide.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/pillowhome-300x225.jpg" width="397"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3 style="text-align: left;">
           <b>
            11. Something sentimental
           </b>
          </h3>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           Dont forget that sentimental gifts can go a long way. Give your traveler a photo collage of you together, or something that
           <a href="https://www.etsy.com/listing/151322971/kansas-city-missouri-map-pillow-green?ref=shop_home_active_22" target="_blank">
            will remind them of home
           </a>
           . Just remember to keep it small and light as theres only so much room in a suitcase!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: left;">
           <strong>
            What gifts do you think are great to give someone who will be studying abroad?
           </strong>
           Let us know in the comments.
          </p>
