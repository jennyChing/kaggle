                        <div class="hs-migrated-cms-post">
                         <p>
                          <img alt="technology sales leads generation" border="0" class="alignRight" id="img-1334077955057" src="//cdn2.hubspot.net/hub/28329/file-13653602-jpg/images/generating_leads.jpg?t=1435739964486&amp;width=332" style="float: right; width: 332px;" title="technology sales leads generation" width="332"/>
                         </p>
                         <p>
                          <span>
                           When running a sales leads generation program you want to provide your team with the best chance of creating sales. With a little bit of planning and these six steps, you can be well on your way to new sales.
                          </span>
                         </p>
                         <ol>
                          <li>
                           <span>
                            <span>
                            </span>
                           </span>
                           <strong>
                            <span>
                             Define lead
                            </span>
                           </strong>
                           <span>
                            : In a true sense a qualified lead is someone that has (a) budget, (b) timeline, (c) need and (d) authority. Building a list of qualified contacts and companies that fit your definition of a lead is the 1
                           </span>
                           <sup>
                            <span>
                             st
                            </span>
                           </sup>
                           <span>
                            step in your technology sales leads generation program. Be clear about what you are going after.
                           </span>
                          </li>
                          <li>
                           <span>
                            <span>
                            </span>
                           </span>
                           <strong>
                            <span>
                             Define goals for each project
                            </span>
                           </strong>
                           <span>
                            : Like generate a list of 500 contacts that are potential decision makers of enterprise storage technologies in global 1000 companies or  Identify companies that have deployed a marketing automation system and have an email volume of 10,000 a month.
                           </span>
                          </li>
                          <li>
                           <span>
                            <span>
                            </span>
                           </span>
                           <strong>
                            <span>
                             Define process and techniques
                            </span>
                           </strong>
                           <span>
                            : Choose what is likely to yield best results. Telemarketing, Webinars, Internet based research, Surveys etc.
                           </span>
                          </li>
                          <li>
                           <span>
                            <span>
                            </span>
                           </span>
                           <strong>
                            <span>
                             Estimate
                            </span>
                           </strong>
                           <span>
                            : Make a ballpark estimate of number of resources required, timeline and budget.
                           </span>
                          </li>
                          <li>
                           <span>
                            <span>
                            </span>
                           </span>
                           <strong>
                            <span>
                             In-house Vs Outsource
                            </span>
                           </strong>
                           <span>
                            : You may have the most knowledge of your products, services and industry but do you have time and resources to accomplish the goal. There are specialized technology sales leads generation companies that can do the job.
                           </span>
                          </li>
                          <li>
                           <span>
                            <span>
                            </span>
                           </span>
                           <strong>
                            <span>
                             Measure
                            </span>
                           </strong>
                           <span>
                            : Set a clear metrics to measure the performance of your own or the outsourced team on the deliverables of your technology leads sales generation program.
                           </span>
                           <span>
                           </span>
                          </li>
                         </ol>
                         <p>
                          <span>
                           <!--more-->
                          </span>
                         </p>

                          <strong>
                           <span>
                            <span>
                             How to Get Customized Install Base Lists
                            </span>
                           </span>
                          </strong>

                          <span>
                           are the best way to quickly and accurately find your target prospects. We will work with you to customize the leads list so that your marketing efforts results in the maximum return and that you meet your sales goals.
