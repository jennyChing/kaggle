         <h1>
          Tumblr Install Guide
         </h1>
         <p>
          StatCounter provides free customisable
          <a href="/free-hit-counter/">
           hit counters
          </a>
          <a href="/free-hit-counter/">
           ,
          </a>
          <a href="/free-invisible-web-tracker/">
           visitor tracking
          </a>
          , web analytics and
          <a href="/free-web-stats/">
           website stats
          </a>
          for Tumblr.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Tumblr" src="http://www.statcounter.com/images/installguide/tumblr_logo.jpg" title="Tumblr"/>
         </p>
         <div id="install">
          <ol>
           <li>
            <iframe border="0" src="/sign-up-step/?htmlname=tumblr" style="border:0;width:470px;height:124px;vertical-align:top;">
            </iframe>
           </li>
           <li>
            Copy the StatCounter code.
           </li>
           <li>
            Log into your
            <a href="http://www.tumblr.com/" target="_blank">
             Tumblr account
             <span class="new-window-msg">
              (opens in a new window)
             </span>
            </a>
            with your username and password.
           </li>
           <li>
            Select "Customize" near the top right of your screen.
            <img height="196" src="http://www.statcounter.com/images/installguide/tumblr1_2014_updated.jpg" style="border:5px solid #2a578e; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 10px;" width="481"/>
           </li>
           <li>
            Select edit HTML just below the name of your theme.
            <img height="405" src="http://www.statcounter.com/images/installguide/tumblr2_2014_updated.jpg" style="border:5px solid #2a578e; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 10px;" width="295"/>
           </li>
           <li>
            Insert your StatCounter code at the bottom of HTML, before the closing tags. Select Update Preview.
            <img height="324" src="http://www.statcounter.com/images/installguide/tumblr3_2014_updated.jpg" style="border:5px solid #2a578e; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 10px;" width="557"/>
           </li>
           <li>
            Select Save.
            <img height="48" src="http://www.statcounter.com/images/installguide/tumblr4_2014_updated.jpg" style="border:5px solid #2a578e; margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 10px;" width="536"/>
           </li>
          </ol>
          <div>
           StatCounter is now active on your Tumblr site and will immediately begin recording visitor information.
           <p>
            <i>
             These instructions have been compiled and verified: November 2014
            </i>
           </p>
          </div>
