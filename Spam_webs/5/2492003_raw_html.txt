         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <strong>
           <em>
            Creamy New York-Style Blueberry Cheesecake recipe with a buttery, thick graham cracker crust and sweet fruit topping. Guests will be begging for seconds!
           </em>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://wishesndishes.com/images/2015/05/New-York-Style-Blueberry-Cheesecake-Recipe-2.jpg">
           <img alt="Creamy New York-Style Blueberry Cheesecake recipe with a buttery, thick graham cracker crust and sweet fruit topping. Guests will be begging for seconds!" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-11453" height="720" src="http://wishesndishes.com/images/2015/05/New-York-Style-Blueberry-Cheesecake-Recipe-2.jpg" width="478"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          I have been so busy this week and caught up in my little world that I almost forgot about Mothers day! How could I? Im so sorry to all of you mothers out there (and my own mother, haha). I hope I can make it up to all of you with this New York-Style Blueberry Cheesecake. I know brunch get-togethers are super popular for Mothers day, and although a cheesecake is not officially a brunch item, it really will fit the bill for whatever gatherings you are planning. Thats the beauty of dessert recipes  they are good for breakfast, lunch OR dinner (or brunchor brinneror just anytime of the day.)
         </p>
         <div style="text-align: center;">
          This New York-Style Blueberry Cheesecake was cooked perfectly, creamy and fairly dense. Im representing New York today! The cheesecake filling is luxurious and rich with the perfect fruit  REAL fruit  topping. The topping takes hardly any extra time at all and its very much worth it to make your own as opposed to buying the stuff from the can. The taste is just much more authentic when you make it yourself. Some people dont notice a difference so thats OK if you prefer to use the canned pie filling! It will still taste great.
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: center;">
         </div>
         <div>
          <a href="http://wishesndishes.com/images/2015/05/New-York-Style-Blueberry-Cheesecake-Recipe-1.jpg">
           <img alt="Creamy New York-Style Blueberry Cheesecake recipe with a buttery, thick graham cracker crust and topped with fresh blueberries.  Great for any holiday." class="aligncenter wp-image-11454" height="600" src="http://wishesndishes.com/images/2015/05/New-York-Style-Blueberry-Cheesecake-Recipe-1.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: center;">
          What is the best part about this New York-Style Blueberry Cheesecake you might ask? Well, the best part is that even if your cheesecake cracks on top and you end up with a crack the size of the grand canyon, you are covering it with the blueberry topping anyways. I know that is everyones biggest fear with cheesecakes is the cracking on top during the baking or cooling process when the crust starts to pull away from the edges of the pan. No worries with this one, my friends.
          <strong>
           Note:
          </strong>
          mine did
          <em>
           not
          </em>
          crack, but Im just saying to you that it doesnt have to look all pretty on top because the blueberries do that job for you. Fruit topping covers a multitude of sins. Enjoy!

            Ingredients
           </div>
           <ul>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup graham cracker crumbs
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 tablespoons unsalted butter
            </li>

            </li>

          <div class="ERSInstructions">
           <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">
            Instructions
           </div>
           <ol>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Butter the bottom of a
             <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000237FSA/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B000237FSA&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wishanddish-20&amp;linkId=2KISDJVOQLJAAZ5K" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000237FSA/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B000237FSA&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wishanddish-20&amp;linkId=2KISDJVOQLJAAZ5K', '9-inch springform pan']);" target="_blank" title="9-inch springform pan">
              9-inch springform pan
             </a>
             . In a
             <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00004SZ7H/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B00004SZ7H&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wishanddish-20&amp;linkId=FE2Z22VR55HQHWT3" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00004SZ7H/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B00004SZ7H&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wishanddish-20&amp;linkId=FE2Z22VR55HQHWT3', 'mixing bowl']);" target="_blank" title="mixing bowl">
              mixing bowl
             </a>
             , combine graham cracker crumbs, butter and sugar and mix well.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Press onto bottom of
             <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000237FSA/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B000237FSA&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wishanddish-20&amp;linkId=2KISDJVOQLJAAZ5K" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000237FSA/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B000237FSA&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wishanddish-20&amp;linkId=2KISDJVOQLJAAZ5K', 'springform pan']);" target="_blank" title="springform pan">
              springform pan
             </a>
             and bake until golden, about 8 minutes. Set aside to cool. When completely cooled, butter the sides of the pan.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Increase oven temperature to 500 degrees F.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In a large mixing bowl with an
             <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00005UP2P/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B00005UP2P&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wishanddish-20&amp;linkId=NEB3WGQQNAFHCQHR" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00005UP2P/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=B00005UP2P&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=wishanddish-20&amp;linkId=NEB3WGQQNAFHCQHR', 'electric mixer']);" target="_blank" title="electric mixer">
              electric mixer
             </a>
             , combine cream cheese, sugar, zests and vanilla and beat until light and creamy.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Add the flour, then the eggs and yolks 1 at a time, mixing well after each addition. Add the sour cream and mix until smooth.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Pour mixture into prepared pan (use water bath if desired, I did not for this cheesecake and it didn't crack) and bake for 12 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Reduce oven temperature to 200 degrees F and bake for 1 hour more. Transfer cake to a cooling rack and let cool completely.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Refrigerate cake overnight before serving.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             To serve, top with blueberry sauce. Slice with a knife dipped in hot water and wiped with a clean cloth, and serve
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             <strong>
              Blueberry sauce:
             </strong>
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             In a small saucepan over medium-high heat stir together the sugar, cornstarch, water, orange juice, and orange zest.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 5 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Stir in the blueberries and simmer the mixture, stirring occasionally, until the berries have burst, about 5 minutes.
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             Add the butter and stir until melted. Remove from heat and let cool before pouring over the cheesecake. (Yield: 3 cups)
            </li>
           </ol>
