     <h2>
      Feeding hack (and cute party food!): Caprese Salad Skewers
     </h2>
     <p>
     </p>
     <p>
      <img alt="OneHungryMama Caprese Skewers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-13286" height="512" src="http://onehungrymama.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/OneHungryMama-Caprese-Skewers.jpg" title="OneHungryMama Caprese Skewers" width="530"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Im not normally into cutesy food presentation, but apparently theres something about caprese salad that inspires playfulness in me. (Remember this
      <a href="http://onehungrymama.com/2010/09/recipe-lunchbox-caprese-salad-even-in-winter/" target="_blank">
       lunchbox caprese salad
      </a>
      ?) Go figure.
     </p>
     <p>
      These Caprese Salad Skewers came to me in an effort to
      <del datetime="2013-06-14T14:11:37+00:00">
       stick it to
      </del>
      encourage my little one to revisit his thoughts on cheese. Once a cheese lover, my little one has declared a distaste for any fromage that doesnt come flat. Thats right, people: the boy wants only deli sliced cheese. I figured that a new, fun presentation might help cure his affliction.
     </p>
     <p>
      <span id="more-13285">
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      The good news is that it did! (Im still amazed at what a big difference skewers and dip make to little ones.)
     </p>
     <p>
      The bad news is that were dairy-free now, at least for a while.
     </p>
     <p>
      Since I cant use this lovely idea right now, you can. Just get cute toothpicks and thread on half of a grape tomato, a little basil leaf, one bocconcino (the small balls of fresh mozzarella), another small leaf of basil, and the other tomato half. Sprinkle with good sea salt and a drizzle of olive oil, and youre good to go.
     </p>
     <p>
      Besides being a fun kids food, these make a great, easy cocktail party snack or maybe even fathers day appetizer!
     </p>
     <p>
      Just make sure to keep your kids from using leftover toothpicks as weapons. (Or is that just my kids?!?)
     </p>
