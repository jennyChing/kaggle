            <h2 style="text-align: left;">
             <span style="font-size: x-large;">
              Steps to upgrade Paytm Wallet
             </span>
            </h2>
            <ol style="text-align: left;">
             <li>
              <span style="font-size: large;">
               Click Paytm Wallet on the top right corner screen,
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="font-size: large;">
               Click Profile on left of the screen
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="font-size: large;">
               Click on Address. Add a pickup address from where Paytm customer care team can visit you.
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="font-size: large;">
               Click on Upgrade Your Account, select or enter your pickup address and give a time slot which suits you, click on SUBMIT,
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="font-size: large;">
               You will receive a call back from Paytm customer care team to schedule a KYC visit,
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="font-size: large;">
               Our executive will visit your address for collection of document copies &amp; verifying your originals,
              </span>
             </li>
             <li>
              <span style="font-size: large;">
               The submitted documents will be reviewed by Paytm
              </span>
