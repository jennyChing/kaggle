     <h1 class="entry-header">
      Football: Younger brother of Taylor Martinez has quite a game
     </h1>
     <div class="time" style="margin-bottom:8px;">
      September 20, 2011|
      <span style="font-size:130%">
       8:26
      </span>
      am
     </div>
     <div class="entry-content">
      <div class="entry-body">
       <!-- sphereit start -->
       <p>
        Drake Martinez, a junior running back-defensive back at Laguna Beach who's the younger brother of Nebraska quarterback Taylor Martinez, had an
        <a href="http://www.ocvarsity.com/articles/martinez-27976-beach-laguna.html" target="_self">
         MVP-like performance
        </a>
        against Huntington Beach Ocean View.
       </p>
       <p>
        He rushed for 334 yards in 17 carries, scoring four touchdowns, with each TD run beyond 50 yards. He made two interceptions, returning one for a TD. He blocked a kick and also caught a two-point conversion.
       </p>
       <p>
        Even during his days at Corona Centennial, Taylor Martinez would have had a tough time duplicating his brother's performance.
       </p>
       <p>
        -- Eric Sondheimer
       </p>
