                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Sheriff Arpaio Must Report To A Monitor
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-544606249722869087" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      </div>
                      <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                       <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-uVCz6ikHD7g/UiFffKgk9tI/AAAAAAAAKqU/TARvtEEWPuM/s1600/spa.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                        <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-uVCz6ikHD7g/UiFffKgk9tI/AAAAAAAAKqU/TARvtEEWPuM/s1600/spa.jpg"/>
                       </a>
                      </div>
                      This is too funny! Sheriff Arpaio, from Arizona, who makes prisoners wear pink underwear and hates people who demand that he respect their civil rights, has been found guilty of
                      <a href="http://news.yahoo.com/judge-plans-appoint-monitor-controversial-arizona-sheriff-012058393.html">
                       unlawfully profiling people of Latin descent
                      </a>
                      . So Judge Murry Snow is going to make Arpaio report to a monitor every month or so, in order to ensure that he is not violating the civil rights of Arizona citizens, or anyone else passing through the state. We have probation officers, DWI monitors, and hall monitors, and now Sheriff Arpaion will have his own monitor. According to his attorney, he is not happy about this either, but since he has been ordered by a judge to report to a monitor, and since he claims to be all about law and order, he should really just shut up and cooperate. The judge apparently does not trust the sheriff to remember that this is America, and that the Constitution was not written for only a select few, but for all Americans. This is actually a great idea; possibly the best law enforcement idea since making prisoners wear pink! I'll bet the sheriff never thought that he could be profiled lawfully by Karma!
