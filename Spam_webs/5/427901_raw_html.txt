           <h3>
            Dialog:
           </h3>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Port:
             <ul>
              <li>
               In the relatively recent past scientists believed that there were canals on mars.
              </li>
              <li>
               A discipline that gives us such falsities can not be trusted.
              </li>
             </ul>
            </li>
            <li>
             Starboard:
             <ul>
              <li>
               But it was through science that we have discovered many valuable things, including the lack of canals on mars.
              </li>
             </ul>
            </li>
            <li>
             Port:
             <ul>
              <li>
               Oh, so they contradict themselves also?
              </li>
              <li>
               The case for science is getting weaker all the time.
              </li>
             </ul>
            </li>
           </ul>
