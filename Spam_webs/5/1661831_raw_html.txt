Though these healthy foods are beneficial to the whole body, the list below is a fun reminder of what to eat to target specific areas.

        <p>
         Slice a carrot in half crosswise and it's easy to see that the veggie resembles an eyelook closely and you'll even notice a pattern of radiating lines that mimic the pupil and iris. And the old wives' tale is true: Munching on carrots will actually promote healthy eyes. "Carrots are filled with vitamins and antioxidants, like beta-carotene, that decrease the chance of macular degeneration, the leading cause of vision loss in older people," says Sasson Moulavi, MD, medical director of Smart for Life Weight Management Centers in Boca Raton, Florida.
