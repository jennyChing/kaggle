         <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
          <span itemprop="name">
           10 Best Equipped Small Car Models 2015
          </span>
         </h1>
         <div class="entry">
          <p>
           <img alt="940x347-header-stylish-500gq_v26" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8468" height="345" src="http://bericebaby.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/940x347-header-stylish-500gq_v26-e1422354808350.jpg" width="775"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           It is 2015 and that means just one thing; time to have a look at the very best equipped small car models available this year. There are a huge range of new vehicles on the market, differing in size, engine, cost, style and design. However, the latest statement to be made is a silent engine with improved practicality; something all car manufacturers have really taken seriously this year.
          </p>
          <p>
           British buyers are well-known for their love of smaller cars  in fact we are the only country to prefer smaller cars over larger ones!I currently own a Peugeot 107 and have done for 5 years now  very tempted to get a 108 next its so darn cute.
          </p>
          <p>
           Whether you want the
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.fiat.co.uk/fiat-500C/500-gq" target="_blank">
             best equipped convertible yet compact car
            </a>
           </strong>
           or simply the cheapest  here are the top 10 best equipped small car models 2015 on the market:
          </p>
          <h4>
           VW Polo Hatchback
          </h4>
          <p>
           Whilst this car has been around for around four years now, its sleek interior and high quality engine is one of the best. It is predicted to hold its value longer than any other car on the market at the moment.
          </p>
          <h4>
           <img alt="FiestaSTRaceRedRHD_Rear_00002" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8469" height="433" src="http://bericebaby.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/FiestaSTRaceRedRHD_Rear_00002-e1422354647541.jpg" width="877"/>
          </h4>
          <h4>
           Ford Fiesta Hatchback
          </h4>
          <p>
           This is everything a small car should be, with its stylish good looks and superior engine  it is easy and comfortable to drive. This is also recommended as a perfect first car too.
          </p>
          <h4>
           Kia Rio Hatchback
          </h4>
          <p>
           This is thought to be one of the best value for money cars out there. It is cheaper than most and has the most amount of equipment. It is both reliable and economical  no wonder it has made this list.
          </p>
          <h4>
          </h4>
          <h4>
           Fiat 500 GQ
          </h4>
          <p>
           The
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.fiat.co.uk/fiat-500C/500-gq" target="_blank">
             style conscious Fiat 500 GQ
            </a>
           </strong>
           features two-tone paintwork and comes equipped with a leather sports wheel. With Bluetooth, voice recognition and
           <strong>
            <a href="http://www.autoexpress.co.uk/fiat/500/65595/fiat-500-gq-special-edition" target="_blank">
             black leather seats with orange stitching
            </a>
           </strong>
            this car isnt for the faint hearted.
          </p>
          <h4>
           Mini Hatchback
          </h4>
          <p>
           Whilst this is now owned by BMW, the stylish interior and high-tech user interface makes this car a no brainer when it comes to both style and function.
          </p>
          <h4>
           <img alt="Peugeot-108-2014-Genfer-Autosalon-Kleinwagen-05" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-8470" height="704" src="http://bericebaby.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Peugeot-108-2014-Genfer-Autosalon-Kleinwagen-05.jpg" width="938"/>
          </h4>
          <h4>
           Peugeot 208 Hatchback
          </h4>
          <p>
           This comes with economical usage of petrol and diesel and is fantastic when it comes to driving fun.
          </p>
