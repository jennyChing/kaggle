         Funky Finds

        <a href="http://craftshowexplosion.com/2010/07/25/selling-crafts-on-the-surface-of-the-sun/">

        </a>

        <a href="http://www.etsy.com/storque/seller-handbook/craft-show-display-tips-must-haves-7931/">

        </a>

        <a href="http://www.etsy.com/storque/seller-handbook/seller-how-to-craft-fair-tips-2021/">

        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        From
        <a href="http://meylah.com/">
         Meylah
        </a>
        , a great article on
        <a href="http://meylah.com/meylah/how-to-make-a-memorable-product-tag">
         how to make memorable product tags
        </a>
        (because  every single item that leaves your booth should be tagged) and because  you cant have too many ideas for setting up your craft booth, here are  some additional
        <a href="http://meylah.com/meylah/how-to-set-up-a-craft-booth-that-customers-cant-resist">

        </a>

        <a href="http://www.ohmyhandmade.com/">

Good to have in stock: push pins, pens, more business cards, extra post cards, post-its, receipt booklets, tape, scissors, tea lights, matches, calculator, extra paper roll, shims, batteries, and anything else I have found that I have needed in the past.
