           <h2>
            Mix and Match the foundation
           </h2>
           <p>
            Because the price is very reasonable you could eventually mix some shades to get the perfect foundation matching the color of your skin. To find the right color, you put a dot on your jaw line. Never in your hand, because the complexion of your hands is not 100% identical to the color of your face. There are 12 shades, from very light (porcelain ivory) to dark (coconut). Is your ideal shade missing? You can also easily mix two shades mix since the texture is so fluid and is as an invisible layer on your skin. It is noticeable that your skin feels smooth and fresh.
