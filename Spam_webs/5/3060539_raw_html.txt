                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Summer Skin Routine
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-8430774016076759212" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Vwa2XnKVyx0/U8qLdwtwQ6I/AAAAAAAABC0/3t9MC79A6Bk/s1600/summer+skin.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Vwa2XnKVyx0/U8qLdwtwQ6I/AAAAAAAABC0/3t9MC79A6Bk/s1600/summer+skin.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-zaKGnGoKwHY/U8qLcNxlilI/AAAAAAAABCk/nhLnDcJIYfo/s1600/summer+skin2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-zaKGnGoKwHY/U8qLcNxlilI/AAAAAAAABCk/nhLnDcJIYfo/s1600/summer+skin2.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-zPV453tCCLw/U8qLcAFUx2I/AAAAAAAABCo/JKdZ3Mfw-78/s1600/summer+skin1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-zPV453tCCLw/U8qLcAFUx2I/AAAAAAAABCo/JKdZ3Mfw-78/s1600/summer+skin1.jpg"/>
                      </a>

                     <br/>
                     We all want glowing skin for Summer. Today I share with you my summer skin routine. These are some of my favorite products to use to really get rid of dead skin and get healthy, moisturized, and glowing skin.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://www.walmart.com/ip/Body-Benefits-by-Body-Image-Natural-Bristle-Bath-Brush/22728311" target="_blank">
                      <b>
                       <u>
                        Dry Brushing
                       </u>
                      </b>
                     </a>
                     // I like to dry brush before a shower to get all of the dead skin off. It also helps in reducing cellulite, improving skin tone, promotes tighter skin etc There are many benefits to dry brushing. The trick is to getting into a habit, at least 3 times a week, to really get results.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=261474248&amp;pid=uid2609-24452103-34" target="_blank">
                      <b>
                       <u>
                        Exfoliating Gloves
                       </u>
                      </b>
                     </a>
                     // These are really inexpensive and you can easily find them in many stores. I use any body wash I have and gently rub on my skin. They can be a little rough so you have to be careful not to rub too hard. You would be amazed at how smooth your skin feels after using them!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     I alternate between dry brushing and using the exfoliating gloves but sometimes I will use both if I really need it!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <b>
                      <u>
                       <a href="http://www.lushusa.com/Ro%27s-Argan-Body-Conditioner/03590,en_US,pd.html#q=jos%2520argan&amp;start=1" target="_blank">
                        Jo's Argan Body Conditioner
                       </a>
                      </u>
                     </b>
                     // This body conditioner is really great if you're feeling a bit lazy and don't want to apply lotion after a shower. It smells great, really moisturizers your skin, and gives you smooth skin for hours!
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <b>
                      <u>
                       <a href="http://www.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=445204516&amp;pid=uid2609-24452103-34" target="_blank">
                        Jergens BB Body Perfecting Skin Cream
                       </a>
                      </u>
                     </b>
                     // At first I thought that it was such a silly product to buy but one day I decided to give it a try. It basically acts like any BB cream you would purchase for the face, it's tinted, covers any imperfections, and moisturizes. If I am going out I will apply this lotion to cover any imperfections but to also get a glow. It gives a bit of tint to the skin but its not too obvious, it doesn't work like a gradual tanning lotion would.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     What is
                     <i>
                      your
                     </i>
                     summer skin routine?
