           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B007A2LQRM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B007A2LQRM&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=whnemo-20&amp;linkId=T75GFY53XFXGBAYL" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B007A2LQRM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B007A2LQRM&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=whnemo-20&amp;linkId=T75GFY53XFXGBAYL', 'unsweetened dried cranberries');" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            unsweetened dried cranberries
           </a>
           , etc.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <strong>
            Method
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           1. Preheat oven to 350.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           2. In large bowl, whisk together flax and water. Let set until gelled (about 5 minutes).
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           3. Stir in remaining ingredients.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           4. Bake for 12 minutes, or til golden brown
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           <strong>
            NOTES
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           1. Use this
           <a href="http://wholenewmom.com/recipes/homemade-nut-and-seed-butters/" target="_blank" title="Homemade Nut and Seed Butter">
            Homemade Nut or Seed Butter
           </a>
           to make this recipe totally special diet-friendly.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           2. See this post on
           <a href="http://wholenewmom.com/kitchen-tips/stevia-what-it-is-and-how-to-use-it/" target="_blank" title="Stevia  What it Is and How to Use It">
            What Stevia Is and How to Use It
           </a>
           for tips on this low carb sweetener.
          </p>
          <p style="padding-left: 30px;">
           3. If you wish to skip the mix ins you could simply add more coconut or even chopped nuts or seeds.
          </p>
