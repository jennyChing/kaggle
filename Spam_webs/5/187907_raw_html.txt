          <p>
           <a href="http://myhoneysplace.com/creamy-kool-aid-pie/043fix/" rel="attachment wp-att-2839">
            <img alt="creamy kool aid pie" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-2839" height="300" src="http://myhoneysplace.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/043fix.jpg" style="margin: 5px 20px;" title="043fix" width="400"/>

          <p>
           <strong>
            Super easy Creamy Kool Aid Pie
           </strong>
          </p>
          <div>
           <strong>
            Ingredients:
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            1  8 ounce tub of Cool Whip Whipped Topping
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            1  14 ounce can of Sweetened Condensed Milk
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            1  pack of unsweetened Strawberry or Lemonade Kool -Aid
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            1  9 inch Graham Cracker or Nilla Wafer Pie Crust
           </strong>
          </div>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Directions:
           </strong>
           <br/>
           <strong>
            Add all the ingredients together and pour into a prepared graham cracker crust.
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Chill about 2 hours then serve.
           </strong>
          </p>
