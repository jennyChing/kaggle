Throughout history the hotel has undergone many changes but always keeping in line its impeccable interiors of the latest design and highest craftsmanship. So when it needed its last reform who better than to ask than renowned architect and interior designer

          <span style="font-size: small;">

Pass from room to room, with an open imagination and be prepared to be impressed, as only someone with this talent and know- how could possible achieve this outcome. If you pass through Paris this hotel is another (not to be missed) on your agenda
