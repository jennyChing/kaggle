        <div class="content clearfix">
         <h3>
          Want to see and experience what is it all about?
         </h3>
         <p>
          Already a snorkeler or avid swimmer interested in scuba diving or you would just like to experience what is the feeling of breathing underwater. This program was created to be accessible and safe, while permitting a true experience of scuba diving. With the general public in mind it allows instructors to take you on a full fledge dive on some of the most sought after dive sites around.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="First time diving? Never dive before?" class="size-medium wp-image-1078 aligncenter" height="360" src="http://www.lotusdiving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/discover-scuba-diving-lotus-diving-koh-phangan1-480x360.jpg" width="480"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Discover Scuba Diving
          </strong>
          comes in different setup ranging from a pool only session to a full day of diving. It can be done in one or two days. For one day program, skills and briefing will be done directly on the boat and at the dive site.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           What you learn:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Basic knowledge about scuba diving equipment, diving techniques and relation with water.
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Requirements:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           10 years old
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <strong>
           What else is included:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Transit service from and to you resort or hotel*
          </li>
          <li>
           Instructor
          </li>
          <li>
           Equipment rental
          </li>
          <li>
           Full tanks rental
          </li>
          <li>
           2 dives
          </li>
         </ul>
