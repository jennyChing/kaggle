             <p>
              So often we get stuck striving for perfectionism.
             </p>
             <p>
              We want things to be perfect, to look perfect, to have everything perfect. Are youmaking this critical striving for perfection mistake?
             </p>
             <p>
             </p>
             <p>
              I know that I was..
              <br/>
              For the longest time I would strive to make everything perfect.
             </p>
             <p>
              In my business.. my videos had to be perfect..
              <br/>
              My blog had to be perfect.
              <br/>
              My conversations had to be perfect.
              <br/>
              Nothing less than perfect actually.
             </p>
             <h1>
              I was constantly striving for perfection
             </h1>
             <p>
              Guess what happened?
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="text-decoration: underline">
               <strong>
                Two things actually..
               </strong>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              1. I ended up failing because I could never measure up to myself.
             </p>
             <p>
              2. I actually repelled customers and team members because they felt they could never measure up and do what I do.
             </p>
             <p>
              <em>
               Has this happened to you?
              </em>
             </p>
             <p>
              Ok, ill be honest.. I didnt even know how much of a perfection problem I had.  Because when it came to like making the bed, and the house.. stuff like that, Im far far from perfect.
             </p>
             <p>
              But when it came to my online business, ooo you better believe I had some serious perfectionistic qualities going on. You know.. my pictures werent perfect, the spacing in my blog wasnt the perfect size. I had to reshoot my videos over and over.. edit them and cut things out so they were perfect.
             </p>
             <p>
