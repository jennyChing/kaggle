        <p style="text-align: justify;">

        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
         What is Taco Bell?
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Taco Bell serves a variety of Tex-Mex foods: Tacos, burritos, nachos and many value menu items. Taco Bell now serves more than 2 billion customers each year in the U.S., through more than 6500 Taco Bell outlets.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Taco Bell was started by Glen Bell in San Bernardino, California in 1946, and is headquartered in Irvine, California.In 1991 the first Taco Bell Express opened in San Francisco.. Taco Bell Express has a limited menu (mostly items priced under $1).
        </p>
        <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
         The Taco Bell Menus.
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Taco Bell specializes in low-priced Tacos, Burritos, Nachos and other Tex-Mex favourites.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         The full menu can also be seen atwww.tacobell.com/m/menu , but unfortunately this menu is spread out over 12 different pages and does not include prices.
