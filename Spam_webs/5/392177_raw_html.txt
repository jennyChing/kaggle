        <h1 class="category-title">
         NFL Drops Ball on Ray Rice &amp; Domestic Violence  Will it Cost Goodell His Job?
        </h1>
        <p class="description">
         Posted  by
         <a href="http://mybookie.ag/author/tony-newborn/" rel="author" title="Posts by Tony Newborn">
          Tony Newborn
         </a>
         on Sep 11, 2014 in
         <a href="http://mybookie.ag/sports-betting/nfl-football/" rel="category tag">
          NFL
         </a>
         |
         <a href="http://mybookie.ag/sports-betting/nfl-football/ray-rice-domestic-violence-cost-goodell-his-job/#respond">
          0 comments
         </a>
        </p>
       </div>
       <!-- end #category-inner -->
      </div>
      <!-- end #category-name -->
      <div class="col-md-9">
       <div class="entry post clearfix">
        <div class="post-thumbnail">
         <img alt="NFL Drops Ball on Ray Rice &amp; Domestic Violence  Will it Cost Goodell His Job?" class="post-thumb" height="338" src="http://mybookie.ag/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/goodrice-600x400.jpg" width="700"/>
         <span class="post-overlay">
         </span>
         <p style="color:#C0C0C0; font-size:12px; font-style:italic;'">
          Will NFL Commish be fired by the league or will he resign due to the Ray Rice fallout?
         </p>
        </div>
        <!-- end .post-thumbnail -->
        <p>
         In the wake of the widespread fallout from the Ray Rice scandal, NFL commissioner Roger Goodell is now facing his own slippery slope as organizations and individuals across the nation call for his firing.
        </p>
        <p>
         Of course, in todays technologically-advanced times where sports gaming enthusiasts everywhere can almost literally place a wager on every sporting event across the globe, Rice and Goodell have now paved the way for you to cash in on the odds surrounding the possible firing or resignation of Goodell.
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/yzcSZvzowCc" width="640">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         Having said that, mybookie.ag has both, denounced domestic violence everywhere and come up with some value-packed
         <a href="http://www.mybookie.ag/lines.php?selected=25&amp;league=852">
          odds on whether or not Goodell, the leagues commissioner since 2006, will be fired or resign.
         </a>
         A portion of the proceeds will be donated to womens shelters across America.
        </p>
        <p>
         The Baltimore Ravens will host the Pittsburgh Steelers tonight on
         <a href="http://nflcommunications.com/2014/09/02/cbs-nfl-network-run-this-town-for-thursday-night-football-open-with-music-featuring-rihanna-narration-by-don-cheadle/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://nflcommunications.com/2014/09/02/cbs-nfl-network-run-this-town-for-thursday-night-football-open-with-music-featuring-rihanna-narration-by-don-cheadle/', 'Thursday Night Football with Rihanna');">
          Thursday Night Football with Rihanna
         </a>
         (who was also the subject of domestic violence) singing the opening theme.
         <a href="http://www.mybookie.ag/sports-betting/nfl-football/insights-pittsburgh-steelers-vs-baltimore-ravens/">
          Click here
         </a>
         for a full game preview.
        </p>
        <p>
         Lets take a look at these odds and my comments on the likelihood of each scenario taking place.
        </p>
        <h2>
         <strong>
          NFL Betting Prop  What Will Happen To Roger Goodell After The Ray Rice Fallout
         </strong>
        </h2>
        <p align="center">
         <strong>
          Will Roger Goodell Be Fired In 2014 Yes -500
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Well, that depends on which side of the fence youre standing on.
        </p>
        <p>
         Goodell himself spoke about the massive  and apparently increasing  wave of criticism directed toward him.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im used to criticism. Im used to that. Every day, I have to earn my stripes, Goodell said in a portion of Tuesdays nationally-televised interview that aired on CBS This Morning on Wednesday. Every day, I have to, to do a better job. And thats my responsibility to the game, to the NFL and to what I see as society.
        </p>
        <p>
         I think the only two ways that Roger Goodell gets fired is if the national media spotlight on this case continues to shine bright long after it should have subdued  or if big-name sponsors start threatening to pull out of the league. Otherwise, I just dont see the leagues owners getting together to force Goodell out.
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <strong>
          Will Roger Goodell Be Fired In 2014 No -1000
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Goodell told CBS News earlier this week thathe doesnt believe his job as commissioner is in jeopardyas a result of his handling of the Rice situation. He did however; admit that the league has a domestic violence problem.
        </p>
        <p>
         Asked if he felt his job was on the line, Goodell answered, No.
        </p>
        <p>
         However, many others feel differently.
        </p>
        <p>
         Terry ONeill, president of the National Organization for Women (NOW), has called for Goodell to resign, using Rices assault on his then-fiance as one of numerous examples of where the league has failed with its policy on domestic violence.
        </p>
        <p>
         The NFL has lost its way. It doesnt have a Ray Rice problem; it has a violence against women problem, ONeill said in a statement. The only workable solution is for Roger Goodell to resign and for his successor to appoint an independent investigator with full authority to gather factual data about domestic violence, dating violence, sexual assault and stalking within the NFL community and to recommend real and lasting reforms.
        </p>
        <p>
         Personally, I dont think Goodell will be fired  and Im not particularly that he should be  especially after coming out and admitting that he made a mistake in handling this case.
        </p>
        <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en">
         <p>
          Former FBI Director Robert S. Mueller III will conduct an independent investigation Ray Rice incident, Commissioner Roger Goodell announced.
         </p>
         <p>
           Adam Schefter (@AdamSchefter)
          <a href="https://twitter.com/AdamSchefter/status/509897311980642304" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'https://twitter.com/AdamSchefter/status/509897311980642304', 'September 11, 2014');">
           September 11, 2014
          </a>
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
         </script>
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <strong>
          Will Roger Goodell Resign In 2014 Yes -500
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         I think the likelihood of Goodell resigning is almost nil.
        </p>
        <p>
         Goodell is a charismatic speaker at knows what to say when he steps in front of a microphonelike the following.
        </p>
        <p>
         People expect a lot from the NFL. We accept that. We embrace that. Thats our opportunity to make a difference, not just in the NFL but in society in general. We have that ability. We have that influence. And we have to do that. And every day, thats what were going to strive to do.
        </p>
        <p align="center">
         <strong>
          Will Roger Goodell Resign In 2014 No -1000
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Personally, I dont see Goodell resigning, so this wager looks very attractive from where Im sitting. The guys got an ego the size of Mount Rushmore and loves wielding his broad powers to whatever degree he pleases.
        </p>
