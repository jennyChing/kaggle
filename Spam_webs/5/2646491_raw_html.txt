       <h2>
        Texturing Update
       </h2>
       <div class="entry">
        <p>
         No new assets to show off this week, but we do have a couple of updates to show you guys. Specifically, a few of our older models are now sporting some beautiful textures!
        </p>
        <p>
         First up, the
         <a href="http://www.starryexpanse.com/2014/05/07/down-the-hatch/">
          submarine
         </a>
         exterior has been kitted out with a beautiful battered copper material. Were still working on perfecting the details, but it is already up and running in Unreal 4. Unreals new physically-based material engine is absolutely perfect for surfaces like this, and were taking full advantage of it.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="frame-outer aligncenter size-medium wp-image-721">
          <span>
           <span>
            <span>
             <span>
              <a href="http://www.starryexpanse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/photo.jpg" rel="lightbox[720]">
               <img alt="Shiny Submarine" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-721" height="328" src="http://www.starryexpanse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/photo-425x336.jpg" width="415"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Secondly, the awesome
         <a href="http://www.starryexpanse.com/2014/06/05/gehns-gallows/">
          wahrk skulls
         </a>
         that we posted last time are now made of bone, instead of theshiny gray default material. We think they look much better like this.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="frame-outer aligncenter size-medium wp-image-722">
          <span>
           <span>
            <span>
             <span>
              <a href="http://www.starryexpanse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/g2.jpg" rel="lightbox[720]">
               <img alt="Skulls of Bone" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-722" height="311" src="http://www.starryexpanse.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/g2-425x318.jpg" width="415"/>
              </a>
             </span>
            </span>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         As we get closer to
         <a href="http://www.mysterium.net">
          Mysterium
         </a>
         , well be working hard on fine-tuning our demo. Expect more updates like this in the next few weeks!
        </p>
