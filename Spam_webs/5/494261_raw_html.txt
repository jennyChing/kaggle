       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Living Locurto Studio Tour
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2014-06-02T10:13:56+00:00">
         June 2, 2014
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/author/amy-from-texas/" rel="author">
           Amy Locurto
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonBookmark" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.livinglocurto.com%2F2014%2F06%2Fliving-locurto-studio-office%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.livinglocurto.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2014%2F06%2FLocurto-Studio-Tour.jpg&amp;description=Take%20the%20Living%20Locurto%20Studio%20Tour%21%20A%20beautiful%20colorful%2C%20modern%20work%20space.%20LivingLocurto.com" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <div class="mailmunch-forms-before-post" style="display: none !important;">
        </div>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2014/06/living-locurto-studio-office/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" class="alignnone wp-image-27664 size-full" height="706" src="http://cf.livinglocurto.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Locurto-Studio-Tour.jpg" title="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Im so excited to take you on a tour through thenew
         <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2014/06/living-locurto-studio-office/" target="_blank">
          Living Locurto Studio
         </a>
         ! After working from home for nearly 15 years, I finally decided to make the leap to move into an office space of my own just for work.
        </p>
        <p>
         Having my own studio space is something that I have dreamed about for years. After starting to do more
         <a href="http://bit.ly/14YTgVQ" target="_blank">
          videos for my blog
         </a>
         , and filling my house with more and more work supplies, it was a must move. Now, itssuch a relief to
         <em>
          finally
         </em>
         be able to leave equipment like tripods, props, cameras and lights set up without having to worry about kids knocking them over.Whew!
        </p>
        <p>
         I know my family is happy to have most of my junkout of the house too. Notice I said most, yep there is still a ton to move. Remember, its been 15 years. Im actually really scared to go into my home office closet to organize that beast! If you work from home, you probably no doubt have a scary closet yourself. ha!
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2014/06/living-locurto-studio-office/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" class="alignnone wp-image-27667 size-full" height="736" src="http://cf.livinglocurto.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Living-Locurto-Studio-Tour.jpg" title="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The studio decor hasbeen a work in progress for several months, but Im happy to say the main work and lounge areas are finished. Yay! The craft room/office will take a lot longer to organize as I have so many supplies and need to find it all stashed around my house. ha!
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          <em>
           Im super excited to show youall of the details! I hope you enjoy the tour
          </em>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-27662">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         My favorite parts are the bigwindows and natural light. If you follow me on
         <a href="http://Instagram.com/livinglocurto" target="_blank">
          Instagram
         </a>
         ,then you have seen some ofmy progress through photos. It sure looks different from the day I moved in!
        </p>
        <p>
         <iframe frameborder="0" height="598" scrolling="no" src="//instagram.com/p/lQdUg5POi-/embed/" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2014/06/living-locurto-studio-office/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" class="alignnone wp-image-27675 size-full" height="333" src="http://cf.livinglocurto.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Living-Locurto-Studio.jpg" title="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" width="500"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I was inspired for the studios colors by theWayfairs hot pink roomat
         <a href="http://www.livinglocurto.com/2014/02/bloggers-alt-summit/" target="_blank">
          Alt Summit
         </a>
         . After walking in their gorgeous room, I knew that was thestyle I wanted!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Wayfair Room Alt Summit" class="size-full wp-image-27678 alignright" height="200" src="http://cf.livinglocurto.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Wayfair-Room-Alt.jpeg" width="252"/>
         So I began planning the look with a
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/livinglocurto/studio/" target="_blank">
          Pinterest Board
         </a>
         for inspiration and ideas. I decided on doing mostly whitewith pops of bright colors and fun bold patterns.
        </p>
        <p>
         When it was time to move into my new space, I was so happy that
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1omx3ZH" rel="nofollow" target="_blank ">
          Wayfair
         </a>
         partnered with me on some of the decor. Its so fun decorating with something for JUST for me in mind. Plus, I had been eyeing Wayfairs
         <a href="http://bit.ly/T6wbyG" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          acrylic coffee tables
         </a>
         for months! Now I finally had a place to put one:-)
        </p>
        <p>
         The coffee table isvery light and easy for me to pick up and move when I need to take photosnear the window. I love how modern it makes the space! Very different from my home.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" class="alignnone wp-image-27683 size-full" height="339" src="http://cf.livinglocurto.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Living-Locurto-Studio-Acrylic-Table.jpg" title="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I also needed a table to use for photos and entertaining. I wanted a large table, but the studio is not thatbig.When I found afolding table at Ikea, it was the perfect fit!
        </p>
        <p>
         I have this tablenear the windows so when I need to do photos or videos, I can pull out one side of it and work. The natural light from the windows isperfect for photos. I plan on getting another one of these tables for my desk and the kids rooms because I love it so much.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" class="alignnone wp-image-27668 size-full" height="750" src="http://cf.livinglocurto.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Living-Locurto-Studio-Table.jpg" title="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         I had some antique yellow metal chairs from a flea market find years ago and knew I wanted to use them in the studio. Istill needed more seating for parties and guests, but I didnt want to spend a lot on seating.
        </p>
        <p>
         Thesefun
         <a href="http://bit.ly/1pLzhCQ" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          yellow ottomans
         </a>
         ended up being so perfect! I didnt realize that they were indoor/outdoor material until they were delivered, but was pleasantly surprised! Now I dont have to worry about spills. I plan on getting more of these ottomans for my home patio for sure.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" class="alignnone wp-image-27680 size-full" height="720" src="http://cf.livinglocurto.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Living-Locurto-Studio-Ottoman.jpg" title="Living Locurto Studio Tour! Beautiful modern work space. LivingLocurto.com" width="500"/>
        </p>
        <div class="mailmunch-forms-in-post-middle" style="display: none !important;">
