         <p>
          <a href="http://lisajobaker.com/five-minute-friday/" title="Five Minute Friday">
           <img alt="Five Minute Friday - crowd" class="alignleft" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/lisajobaker.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/5minutefriday.jpg?resize=144%2C144" title="Five Minute Friday"/>
          </a>
          So the saying goes
          <em>
           you can be standing in a room with a hundred men and be completely alone
          </em>
          and isnt that the truth? Oh so many times I have been in a house thats absolutely full, like Christmas has had 30-40 people depending on the year and who comes home but I feel alone with all those people. We may share blood but thats about it. I rarely drink. I dont party or go out. I was a teen mom. I have PTSD. So what does this crowd of seemingly happy people do for me? Honestly?
         </p>
         <p>
          Nothing.
         </p>
         <p>
          The crowd that is my family, my adoptive family if you will, are those people who have hurt and been hurt, who understand without trying to compare experiences because they know that each experience is different and that their pain is just as valid as mine regardless of the cause. The pain receptors in our brains dont know any difference.
         </p>
         <div class="tm-tweet-clear">
         </div>
         <div class="tm-click-to-tweet">
          <div class="tm-ctt-text">

            I grasp onto the zip line and soar through the sky above the trees
           </a>
          </div>
          <p>
           <a class="tm-ctt-btn" href="https://twitter.com/share?text=I+grasp+onto+the+zip+line+and+soar+through+the+sky+above+the+trees&amp;via=trendingmama&amp;related=trendingmama&amp;url=http://trendingmama.com/crowd-five-minute-friday-post/" target="_blank">
            Click To Tweet
           </a>
          </p>
          <div class="tm-ctt-tip">
          </div>
         </div>
         <p>
          So this crowd may be thin, it may exist mostly online but at least this crowd is full of people whom I love, who share similar values, who dont judge me or my past. A crowd of people who love God and practice unconditional love in ways that I had never experienced before.
         </p>
         <p>
          This hollow part of my heart that has been there for as long as I can remember is finally feeling crowded by all of the positive lovelies that I otherwise never would have had.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="crowd" class="aligncenter" height="180" src="https://encrypted-tbn1.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcQWEIGdkHNqQeJ039_YaO27xW1UkbjkMAsxOOMuVb1N-SikbKWn" width="279"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Sometimes, I am my own crowd and I push myself out but this always means I grasp onto the zip line and soar through the sky above the trees right back into the safety of Gods loving arms.
         </p>
