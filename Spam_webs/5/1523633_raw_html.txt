       <p>
        Pocket-lint has teamed up with
        <a href="http://goo.gl/tCcK82">
         Asus
        </a>
        (#LoveFromAsus) to give you the chance to win an Asus
        <span>
         Transformer
        </span>
        AiO P1801 A Windows 8 PC &amp; Android Tablet All-in-One computer.
       </p>
       <p>
        With a unique form factor the
        <a href="http://www.pocket-lint.com/asus">
         Asus
        </a>
        Transformer AiO creates brand new possibilities to enjoy life, bringing people closer together this Christmas period. The Windows 8 PC Station and Android tablet can operate independently at the same time in different places or they can be joined together to provide the perfect desktop experience.
       </p>

        <div data-sw-args='{"z":5338,"source":"Laptops","width":300,"targetId":"_sph_5338_x2h0e9","domain":"delivery.platform.switchads.com"}' id="_sph_5338_x2h0e9" style="width:300px;min-height:250px">
        </div>
       </div>

        <iframe class="video" height="480" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Wg_8XnebB3I?rel=0" width="640">
        </iframe>
       </div>
       <p>
        To win the Asus Transformer AiO P1801 A Windows 8 PC &amp; Android Tablet All-in-One all you have to do is share with us why someone in your life deserves a special gift and then nominate a loved one to win this incredible gift.
       </p>
       <p>
        To find out more about theAsus
        <span>
         Transformer
        </span>
        AiO P1801 A Windows 8 PC &amp; Android Tablet All-in-One computer why not visit the Asus dedicated microsite for the
        <a href="http://goo.gl/tCcK82">
         Asus Transformer AiO
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        The winner will be announced on ASUS Facebook page (
        <a href="http://facebook.com/asusuk">
         facebook.com/asusuk
        </a>
        ) on 3 January.
       </p>
