       <a href="http://www.studiodiy.com/2015/03/16/diy-graphic-tees/" rel="bookmark" title="DIY Graphic Tees">
        DIY Graphic Tees
       </a>
      </h1>
      <p class="byline">
       <span class="byline-prep byline-prep-author">
        By
       </span>
       <span class="author vcard">
        <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.studiodiy.com/author/admin/" title="Kelly">

       <p>
        <a href="http://www.studiodiy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Graphic-Tees14.jpg">
         <img alt="Make Your Own Graphic Tees!" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-20335" height="900" src="http://www.studiodiy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Graphic-Tees14-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        YOU GUYS. In case you havent noticed, Ive been
        <a href="http://www.studiodiy.com/shop/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         obsessed
        </a>
        with graphic tees lately. When I was in middle school, graphic tees with things like Princess and Angel were all the rage and Im kind of STOKED that these are back in action right now, but with far better sayings. I decided I needed to make some of my own, with some of MY favorite phrases, quotes and lyrics. CAUSE WHY THE HECK NOT!? It is SO EASY, you only need two supplies and an iron. Have a t-shirt making party with all your friends and laugh your faces off as you reminiscence about the best chick flicks and rap lyrics! Bonus points if you can name the sources of most of
        <em>
         my
        </em>
        references.
       </p>

        </a>
       </p>
       <p>

       <p>
        <a href="http://www.studiodiy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Graphic-Tees12.jpg">

         <img alt="DIY Graphic Tees" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-20342" height="900" src="http://www.studiodiy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Graphic-Tees7-600x900.jpg" width="600"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <div class="post_graphic supplies">
       </div>
       <p>
        T-Shirts (I went to the American Apparel Flea Market in downtown LA for cheap shirts! But heres some good options:
        <a href="http://bit.ly/1EiIQi3" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         t-shirts
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://bit.ly/1BKzP44" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         sweatshirts
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://store.americanapparel.net/unisex-power-washed-tank_2411w" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         tanks
        </a>
        )
        <br/>
        Iron-on Letters (I used
        <a href="http://bit.ly/1MEDkLj" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         these black ones
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://bit.ly/1DquQrx" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         these white ones
        </a>
        . For kids/baby sized graphic tees, you can use
        <a href="http://bit.ly/1BKztdP" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         these smaller ones
        </a>
        too!)
        <br/>
        Iron
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.studiodiy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Graphic-Tees9.jpg">
         <img alt="DIY Graphic Tees" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-20340" height="446" src="http://www.studiodiy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Graphic-Tees9-297x446.jpg" width="297"/>
        </a>
        <a href="http://www.studiodiy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Graphic-Tees8.jpg">
         <img alt="DIY Graphic Tees" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-20341" height="446" src="http://www.studiodiy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Graphic-Tees8-297x446.jpg" width="297"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <div class="post_graphic make">
       </div>
       <p>
        1. Choose your phrase and peel those letters (carefully!) off the sheet. Line them up and space them out as you want them.
        <br/>
        2. Iron according to package directions. (Thats really it!!!)
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.studiodiy.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/DIY-Graphic-Tees11.jpg">
