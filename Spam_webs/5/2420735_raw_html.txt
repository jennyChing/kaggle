         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          Old-School Japanese Watch Advertisements
         </h1>
         <p class="entry-meta">
          <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
           Written by
           <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.unfinishedman.com/author/chad/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
            <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
             Chad
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
          <span class="entry-categories">
           in
           <a href="http://www.unfinishedman.com/category/art-photography/" rel="category tag">
            Art &amp; Photography
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </header>
        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
         <img alt="vintage-japanese-watches" class="post-image entry-image attachment-content-wide wp-post-image" height="473" itemprop="image" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/vintage-japanese-watches.jpg" width="560"/>
         <p>
          Im not usually the one who writes about
          <a href="http://www.unfinishedman.com/category/fashion/watches/" target="_blank">
           watches
          </a>
          , because frankly I think theyre mostly useless these days. I have my cell phone, thank you very much. Having said that, I absolutely love seeing old advertising, especially if its created by the Japanese.
         </p>
         <p>
          Im sure we can all agree that Japan is a place full of wacky antics, and even crazier gadgets, and this set of
          <span id="more-35266">
          </span>
          old-school Japanese watch advertisements pretty well covers both those areas. I admit, that the Seiko and Citizen watches are pretty typical, but what about the robots and women with bowl-cuts that are wearing them?
         </p>
         <h2>
          Japan would like to sell you a watch
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a name="imageclose-35267">
           <div class="lb-album">
            <a href="#image-35267">
             <img alt="old seiko watch" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35272" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts06-e1343746507872.jpg"/>
             <span>
             </span>
            </a>
           </div>
          </a>
          <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-35267">
           <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-35267">
            <img alt="image-35267" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts06.jpg"/>
           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          We start out pretty simple here, with a very yellow woman (Im not being racist!) sitting on a giant J a hideous slender watch floating idly above her.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a name="imageclose-35268">
           <div class="lb-album">
            <a href="#image-35268">
             <img alt="vintage japanese watch" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35271" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts05-e1343746529488.jpg"/>
             <span>
             </span>
            </a>
           </div>
          </a>
          <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-35268">
           <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-35268">
            <img alt="image-35268" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts05.jpg"/>
           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Then we have oh my god, what is wrong with their faces? They look likepossessedmannequins mannequins promoting unbelievably ugly gem-stone colored watches. Can you believe it that people actually wore these monstrosities?
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a name="imageclose-35269">
           <div class="lb-album">
            <a href="#image-35269">
             <img alt="seiko watch singers" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35270" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts04-e1343746544122.jpg"/>
             <span>
             </span>
            </a>
           </div>
          </a>
          <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-35269">
           <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-35269">
            <img alt="image-35269" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts04.jpg"/>
           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Nooooo, dont buy it!
          </em>
          they scream out, and I would have to agree. Unless I want my wrist to look like a piece of a boiler, or the hatch of a submarine, I think Ill have to take a pass on this one.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a name="imageclose-35270">
           <div class="lb-album">
            <a href="#image-35270">
             <img alt="vintage watch advert" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35269" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts03-e1343746561168.jpg"/>
             <span>
             </span>
            </a>
           </div>
          </a>
          <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-35270">
           <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-35270">
            <img alt="image-35270" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts03.jpg"/>
           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          These Citizen watches actually look relatively normal, though the person levitating them in front of her does not. Is the giant pink star on her forehead a part of the company logo, or was that simply an act of desperation to make this advertisement more interesting?
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a name="imageclose-35271">
           <div class="lb-album">
            <a href="#image-35271">
             <img alt="robot wearing citizen watch" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35268" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts02-e1343746577148.jpg"/>
             <span>
             </span>
            </a>
           </div>
          </a>
          <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-35271">
           <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-35271">
            <img alt="image-35271" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts02.jpg"/>
           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Okay, now this is awesome. Its a fuckin robot wearing a watch, that looks like his face! This just screams meta, and if you look not so carefully, youll see theres yet
          <em>
           another
          </em>
          shot of the robot
          <em>
           behind
          </em>
          the robot in front! Thats some real Lost shit right there.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a name="imageclose-35272">
           <div class="lb-album">
            <a href="#image-35272">
             <img alt="seiko 1975" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-35267" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts01-e1343746593926.jpg"/>
             <span>
             </span>
            </a>
           </div>
          </a>
          <a class="css3lightbox-close" href="#imageclose-35272">
           <div class="lb-overlay" id="image-35272">
            <img alt="image-35272" src="http://www.unfinishedman.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/old-japanese-watch-adverts01.jpg"/>
           </div>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Tiny people, making very tiny little ugly watches. At the very least, they all seem happy to be doing whatever it is their doing some sort of watch related festival and dance routine from the looks of things. Im sure the laughing gas helped with that.
         </p>
         <h3>
          Closing Thoughts
         </h3>
         <p>
          Though these adverts are a little bit hokey, and perhaps even a bit crazy, theyve got nothing on the products coming out of modern day Japan. Though Japanese manufacturers still produce a ton of amazing goods (such as jeans), they all create and manufacture a ton of really insane stuff, especially when it comes to robots. The distinct lack of tentacles and eels in these adverts is really kind of refreshing.
         </p>
         <p>
          If youre interested in seeing more vintage Japanese advertising, well let me save you some trouble.
          <a href="http://www.crackajack.de" target="_blank">
           Nerdcore
          </a>
          has posted a whole bunch of them up
          <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/nerdcoreblog/sets/72157630811641692/" target="_blank">
           on Flickr
          </a>
          , and if you liked the small selection I shared with you here, then youll be blown away by what youll see in a moment. Hint: That person who looks like a man in the thumbnail yeah, theyre actually not.
         </p>

           Published on August 2, 2012.
          </time>
         </p>
