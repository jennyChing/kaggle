                                    &lt;p&gt;&lt;p&gt;&lt;a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fashion_design" target="_blank"&gt;Fashion&lt;/a&gt; has never been as expensive as now. Luxury retailers say prices have risen &lt;a href="http://nymag.com/fashion/06/spring/15735/" target="_blank"&gt;25 to 50 per cent&lt;/a&gt; over the last five years. Exclusive&nbsp;products and accessories often reach extremely high prices. However at the same time fashion has never been as democratic as today.&nbsp;The number of&nbsp;people in the world that can afford fashion products has also grown.&nbsp;Innovation, originality, new materials and variety of choice are some of the arguments used to justify the high prices.&nbsp;Larger margins, promotion costs&nbsp;and the high fees of star designers may be other reasons for such increases.&lt;/p&gt;

&lt;p&gt;What do you think? Is fashion worth the price? Vote here and share your views in the comment section below.&lt;/p&gt;
