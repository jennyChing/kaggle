         <h1 class="g20-article-title">
          11 Quotes for Your 20s
         </h1>
         <div class="post-meta">
          <span class="post-author">

            GenTwenty

        <div class="row post-content">
         <div>
          <img alt="Quotes" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2079" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/gentwenty.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Quotes.jpg?resize=610%2C458"/>
         </div>
         <p>
          <em>
           Your twenties are a tumultuous new chapter of your life. And every now and then, you need a little inspirational mantra to get you through the challenging times. Here are the quotes we remember when the going gets tough
          </em>
          :
         </p>
         <p>
          1. Make the most of yourself, for that is all there is of you.  Ralph Waldo Emerson
         </p>
         <p>
          2. To me, fearless is not the absence of fear. Its not being completely unafraid. To me, Fearless is having fears. Fearless is having doubts. Lots of them. To me, fearless is living in spite of those things that scare you to death.  Taylor Swift
         </p>
         <p>
          3. I think we dream so we dont have to be apart for so long. If were in each others dreams, we can be together all the time. A.A. Milne,
          <i>
           Winnie-the-Pooh
          </i>
         </p>
         <p>
          4. Its never too late to be who you might have been.  George Eliot
         </p>
         <p>
          5. Some women choose to follow men, and some women choose to follow their dreams. If youre wondering which way to go, remember that your career will never wake up and tell you that it doesnt love you anymore.  Lady Gaga
         </p>
         <p>
          6. A self is not something static, tied up in a pretty parcel and handed to a child, finished and complete. A self is always becoming.  Madeleine LEngle
         </p>
         <p>
          7. Nobody puts baby in a corner.  Johnny (Dirty Dancing)
         </p>
         <p>
          8. Monsters are real, and ghosts are real too. They live inside us, and sometimes, they win.  Stephen King
         </p>
         <p>
          9. Remember no one can make you feel inferior without your consent.  Eleanor Roosevelt
         </p>
         <p>
          10. The beautiful journey of today can only begin when we learn to let go of yesterday.  Steve Maraboli
         </p>
         <div>
          <p>
           11. The best years of your life are the ones in which you decide your problems are your own. You dont blame them on your mother, the ecology, or the President. You realize that you control your own destiny.  Albert Ellis
          </p>
