            <strong>

           , the former Olympic athlete and reality television star, has revealed he has struggled with gender identity since he was a child and that he has "lived a lie his whole life".
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="secondPar">
          <p>
           Ending months of speculation that he was transitioning into a woman, Jenner said "for all intents and purposes" he is female - becoming the most high-profile American to come out as transgender.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="thirdPar">

         <div class="fourthPar">
          <p>
           Breaking his silence in his first interview on the subject, the 65-year-old decathlon gold medalist told ABCs Diane Sawyer that he explained it to his six children by describing the feeling he was born with a male body and a female soul.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="fifthPar">
          <p>
           Known to older generations as one of the greatest Olympians in US history, and to younger generations as the patriarch of reality TV's Kardashian clan, Jenner declared it would be his last interview as Bruce, and he would re-emerge as a woman.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="body">
          <p>
          </p>
          <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en">
           <p>

          <p>
           "I have always been very confused with my gender identity since I was this big," admitted Jenner, lowering his hand to several feet above the ground.
          </p>
          <p>
           While he asked to be addressed in the interview with male pronouns, speaking in front of millions of viewers, Jenner said: My whole life has been getting me ready for this.
          </p>
          <p>
           Id have been so mad at myself if I never explored that side of me, he said, pulling down his long hair from its ponytail.
          </p>
          <p>
           He revealed that at the age of eight he started dressing up in his mother and sisters clothes: At that time I didnt know why I was just doing it, just that it felt good, he said.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img caption="Bruce Jenner with stepdaughter Kim Kardashian" credit="Broadimage/REX Shutterstock" declared-caption="" height="387" name="61006906_Mandatory Credit Photo by Broadimage-REX Shutterstock 4216093f  Bruce Jenner and Kim Ka" photographer="Broadimage/REX Shutterstock" refid="3277363" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03277/61006906_Mandatory_3277363b.jpg" version="b" width="620"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           I hate the term boy stuck in a girls body. Im not stuck in anybodys body... Im me. My brain is much more female than it is male. That's what my soul is. Bruce lives a lie. She is not a lie."
          </p>
          <p>
           Born outside New York in October 1949, thrice-married Jenner became an American sports hero when he set a world record with his Montreal decathlon victory.
          </p>
          <p>
           Leveraging his fame, he appeared on boxes of Wheaties, a popular American cereal known as "the breakfast of champions," then tried his hand as a movie actor, with less than stellar results.
          </p>
          <p>
           But it was through his marriage to Kris Kardashian that he once again became a household name in Keeping Up with the Kardashians in which he appeared as the sometimes bemused stepfather of her flamboyant daughters Kourtney, Kim and Khloe.
          </p>
          <p>
           Shown a photograph of himself by Sawyer taken after his win at the 1976 Montreal Summer Olympics, he described it as a time he was running away from who he was.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img caption="Bruce Jenner celebrates as he wins the gold medal in the Decathlon at the 1976 Olympics" credit="Colorsport/REX Shutterstock" declared-caption="" height="387" name="bruce medal" photographer="Colorsport/REX Shutterstock" refid="3280609" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03280/bruce_medal_3280609b.jpg" version="b" width="620"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           I was scared to death, big-time fear - I dint know what my future held, he told Sawyer.
          </p>
          <p>
           When asked to describe what he saw when he looked at the picture, he said: That is me, that is her.
          </p>
          <p>
           I was a lonely boy, Im still a lonely big boy, he told ABC. I dont socialise a lot, when you deal with this issue you never fit in. I look at women and Im so envious they can be so comfortable with themselves.
          </p>
          <p>
           He said he had been too afraid to go public as he did not want to disappoint anyone.
          </p>
          <p>
           The first person he told was first wife Chrystie Crownover, saying he liked to cross-dress without discussing the extent of his gender confusion.
          </p>
          <p>
           It was not until 1984 that he admitted for the first time, to his second wife Linda Thompson, that he thought of himself as a woman.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            
            <a href="/news/worldnews/northamerica/usa/11562597/Bruce-Jenner-started-transgender-journey-in-1980s.html" id="11562597">
             Bruce Jenner 'started transgender journey in 1980s'
            </a>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           He started taking the female hormone estrogen in the late Eighties for five years, at the same time having surgery done on his nose and undergoing electrolysis to remove his facial hair.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img caption="Bruce Jenner gave a much-anticipated interview ABC's Diane Sawyer " declared-caption="" height="387" name="59289670_FILE - APRIL 02 Bruce Jenner is facing a wrongful death lawsuit over a car accident he" photographer="Janette Pellegrini" refid="3277355" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03277/59289670_FILE_-_AP_3277355b.jpg" version="b" width="620"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           He would later confided in his sister, who kept his secret for 30 years.
          </p>
          <p>
           Sawyer then turned to the issue of his sexuality, asking which gender he was attracted to.
          </p>
          <p>
           Im not gay, Ive never been with a guy, he said. I was never attracted to guys, the sexuality was a different issue.
          </p>
          <p>
           Identifying himself as assexual, he said: I felt heterosexual. Im stuck here in the middle - which way do I go?
          </p>
          <p>
           <img caption="Bruce Jenner and Kris Kardashian in 2004" credit="Startraks Photo/REX Shutterstock" declared-caption="" height="387" name="bruce kris" photographer="Startraks Photo/REX Shutterstock" refid="3280612" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03280/bruce_kris_3280612b.jpg" version="b" width="620"/>
           <sub>
            Bruce Jenner and Kris Kardashian in 2004
           </sub>
          </p>
          <p>
           Jenner also revealed he had over the last few years gone out in public dressed as a woman, but no one noticed.
          </p>
          <p>
           Clutching a tissue, he said he had on occasion contemplated suicide, but ultimately decided against it, wanting to know how the story ends".
          </p>
          <p>
           He told his last wife, Kris Jenner, in the early days of their relationship that he was taking hormone pills and said she often saw him dressing up as a woman, but she shrugged it off.
          </p>
          <p>
           For almost a decade, the two have starred in the hit reality show Keeping up with the Kardashians: Kris as the domineering "momager" and head of the household, and Bruce the dutiful, put-upon husband and father.
          </p>
          <p>
           The couple, who have two daughters Kylie and Kendall together, split in 2013 after nearly 25 years of marriage, with Kris citing irreconcilable differences. If she had been really good with it, wed still be together," he admitted tearfully. It was tough on her.
          </p>
          <p>
           <img caption="Jenner with ex-wife Kris, and stepdaughters Khloe, Kourtney and Kim in 2008" credit="Picture Perfect/REX Shutterstock" declared-caption="" height="387" name="61006903_Mandatory Credit Photo by Picture Perfect-REX Shutterstock 743162e  Bruce Jenner Kris J" photographer="Picture Perfect/REX Shutterstock" refid="3277364" src="http://i.telegraph.co.uk/multimedia/archive/03277/61006903_Mandatory_3277364b.jpg" version="b" width="620"/>
           <sub>
            Jenner with ex-wife Kris, and stepdaughters Khloe, Kourtney and Kim in 2008
           </sub>
          </p>
          <p>
           He said telling his children was the most difficult part. Step-daughter Khloe, he said, was taking it the hardest, while Kim was "by far the most accepting" to his decision to transform into a woman.
          </p>
          <p>
           Weve done 445 episodes (of Keeping up with the Kardashians), and during the entire run I kept thinking to myself, the one real true story was the one I was hiding and no one knew about it. I could not tell that story.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>

          </p>
          <p>

        /*
         Read Next Widget
         */
        var readNextWidget = (function(){

            var settings = {
                articleImage: "",
                articleTitle: "",
                articleUrl: "#",
                articleId: "",
                currentArticleId: "",
                articleList: [],
                articleListToSkip: [],
                readNextCookieName: "tmg_read_next",
                openButton: "#readNextOpen a",
                closeButton: "#closeBtn a",
                articleContainer: "#readNextArticleContainer",
                widgetContainer: "#readNextWidget",
                displayThumbnail: false,
                panelOpen: false,
                autoOpen: true
            };

            var renderTemplate = function() {
                var template = '&lt;div id="readNextWidget" style="display:none"&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="readNextOpen"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;div id="readNextOpenInner"&gt;&lt;a href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;span&gt;Next Story&lt;/span&gt;&lt;span class="icn"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="readNextArticleContainer" style="display:none"&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticle"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticleWrapper"&gt;'+
                        '&lt;div id="nextArticleInnerWrapper"&gt;'+
                        '&lt;p id="readNextLabel"&gt;MOST VIEWED IN NEWS&lt;/p&gt;';
                        
				if(settings.articleImage &amp;&amp; settings.articleImage!="null" &amp;&amp; settings.displayThumbnail){
					template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageThumbnail" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;img src="'+ settings.articleImage +'"/&gt;&lt;/a&gt;';
				}
                        
				template = template + '&lt;a id="nextPageTitle" href="' + settings.articleUrl + '"&gt;&lt;span&gt;' + settings.articleTitle + '&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;div id="closeBtn"&gt;&lt;div class="topBar"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;&lt;a href="#"&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;/div&gt;' +
                        '&lt;iframe style="border:none;position:absolute;top:0;left:0; height:100%;width:100%;z-index:-1;" id="iefix" src="about:blank"&gt;&lt;/iframe&gt;' + //fix for IE bug
                        '&lt;/div&gt;';
                return template;
            };


            var init = function(currentArticleId, articleList, displayThumbnail) {

                settings.articleList = articleList;
                settings.currentArticleId = currentArticleId;
                settings.displayThumbnail = displayThumbnail;

                settings.articleListToSkip = unescape($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName)).split(',');

                if ($.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName) != null) {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    updateCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                } else {
                    chooseNextArticle(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    createCookieBasedOnArticleList(settings.currentArticleId, settings.articleList, settings.articleListToSkip);
                }


                if (settings.articleId != "") {

                    $(renderTemplate()).appendTo("body");

                    $("#nextArticleWrapper a, #readNextOpenInner a").click(function () {
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_headline', 'version_1']);

                        getNavPos($(this).attr("href"), "wayfarerReadNext");
                    });

                    bindUIActions();

                    var displayed = false;

                    setTimeout(function () {
                        if (!displayed) {
                            displayed == true;
                            readNextWidget.display();
                        }

                    }, 10000);

                    $(window).scroll(function () {
                        if (isScrolledIntoView("#tmg-related-links")) {
                            if (!displayed) {
                                displayed == true;
                                readNextWidget.display();
                            }
                        }

                        if(isScrolledIntoView("#shareBottom") || isScrolledIntoView("#newGigyaShareBottom")) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }

                        if($(window).scrollTop() + $(window).height() == $(document).height()) {
                            if(settings.autoOpen) {
                                openArticleContainer();
                            }
                        }
                    });
                }
            };

            var display = function () {
                $(settings.widgetContainer).fadeIn(2000, function () {
                    //animation complete
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_closed', 'version_1']);
                });
            };

            var bindUIActions = function () {
                $(settings.openButton).mouseenter(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'hover_closed', 'version_1']);
                    openArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.widgetContainer).mouseleave(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    closeArticleContainer();
                });
                $(settings.closeButton).click(function (event) {
                    event.preventDefault();
                    _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'click_close', 'version_1']);
                    updateCookie(settings.articleId, settings.articleListToSkip);
                    closeArticleContainer();
                    settings.autoOpen = false;
                });
            };

            var openArticleContainer = function () {
                if (!settings.panelOpen) {
                	$("#readNextArticleContainer").show();//show/hide because height is calculated incorrectly for elements with display:none
                    $("#readNextArticleContainer").hide();
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                        _gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'wayfarer_read_next', 'display_open', 'version_1']);
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                    settings.autoOpen = false; //set auto open to false once user has viewed this once
                }
                return false;
            };

            var closeArticleContainer = function () {
                if (settings.panelOpen) {
                    $(settings.articleContainer).toggle('slide', {direction: 'left'}, function () {
                        //animation complete
                    });
                    settings.panelOpen = !settings.panelOpen;
                }
                return false;
            };


            var isScrolledIntoView = function(elem) {
                if($(elem).length) { //check if element exists
                    var docViewTop = $(window).scrollTop();
                    var docViewBottom = docViewTop + $(window).height();

                    var elemTop = $(elem).offset().top;
                    var elemBottom = elemTop + $(elem).height();

                    return ((elemBottom &lt;= docViewBottom) &amp;&amp; (elemTop &gt;= docViewTop));
                } else {
                    return false;
                }
            };

            var unique = function(array) {
                return array.filter(function (el, index, arr) {
                    return index == arr.indexOf(el);
                });
            }

            var createCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        articleListToSkip[0] = currentArticleId;
                        $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookieBasedOnArticleList = function(currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if (article.id == currentArticleId) {
                        updateCookie(currentArticleId, articleListToSkip);
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            var updateCookie = function(articleIdToSkip, articleListToSkip) {
                articleListToSkip.push(articleIdToSkip);
                articleListToSkip = unique(articleListToSkip);
                $.cookie(settings.readNextCookieName, escape(articleListToSkip.join(',')), {path: '/', expires: 1, domain: 'telegraph.co.uk'});
            }

            var chooseNextArticle = function (currentArticleId, articleList, articleListToSkip) {
                articleList.some(function (article) {
                    if ((article.id != currentArticleId) &amp;&amp; ($.inArray(article.id, articleListToSkip) === -1)) {

                        //set article
                        settings.articleTitle = article.title;
                        settings.articleUrl = article.url;
                        settings.articleId = article.id;
						settings.articleImage = article.imgUrl;
                        return true;
                    }
                });
            }

            //public API
            return {
                settings: settings,
                init: init,
                display: display,
                openArticleContainer: openArticleContainer,
                closeArticleContainer: closeArticleContainer
            }
        })();

    };
