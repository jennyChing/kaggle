     </div>
     <section>
      <div class="label">
       <p>
        The Kripke Center is dedicated to facilitating scholarly activity in the areas of religion and society. Special attention is given to promoting understanding between and among faith communities, including especially Judaism, Christianity, and Islam. The Kripke Centers primary audience is the academic community, but its scholarship and services are available to all who seek them.
       </p>
       <p>
        The Center is named in honor of Rabbi Myer and Dorothy Kripke.
       </p>
