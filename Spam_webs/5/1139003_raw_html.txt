          <strong>
           Kids Eat Free Meal Deals
          </strong>
           to help stretch our dollars.
         </p>
         <p>
          You may want to PIN this list or bookmark it. Be sure and take it with you when you travel this summer. If your kids are picky eaters, wouldnt you feel better if their meal was free? A big thank you to
          <a href="http://moms.popsugar.com/Free-Dining-Kids-34814278?utm_source=com_newsletter&amp;utm_medium=email&amp;utm_campaign=com_newsletter_v1_06032014&amp;em_recid=&amp;utm_content=placement_1_desc" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">

         <p>

         </p>
         <p>
          If you like restaurant freebies, {who doesnt?} be sure and sign up the whole family for the free meals you can get during your birthday month. Use a separate free email from Gmail or Yahoo for your freebie sign-ups. You dont want your email inbox to look like mine does!
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Applebees:
          </b>
          When an adult orders a meal, a child can dine for $1 at select locations.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Baja Fresh Mexican Grill:
          </b>
          Kids meals are free with each adult meal purchased.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Bob Evans
          </strong>
           Kids eat free on Tuesdays with an adult entree purchase.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Buffalo Wings &amp; Rings:
          </b>
          On Mondays, kids can eat all the chicken wings they want without spending a dime.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Chevys Fresh Mex
          </strong>
           Kids eat free with an adult meal purchase.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Chick- fil-A
          </strong>
          -Kids get a free kids meal with each adult meal purchase every Tuesday.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Chilis:
          </b>
          Depending on the location, kids under the age of 12 can eat for free with the purchase of an adult meal.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Cicis Pizza:
          </b>
          Every day, kids 3 and under can enjoy the Cicis buffet for free. Participating locations also offer free dining to kids under 12 years old on certain weeknights.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Cocos
          </strong>
           Kids 10 and under can eat for free after 4 PM every Tuesday.
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-71397">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           Dennys:
          </b>
          On Tuesdays and Thursdays from 4 to 10 p.m., kids 10 and under can eat for free. The deal, however, is limited to two free entrees for each adult entree purchased.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Earth Fare
          </strong>
           Kids eat free on Thursdays from 4  8 pm with a $5 adult purchase.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Fuddruckers
          </strong>
           Kids 12 and under can choose anything on the kids menu for only $0.99 on Monday and Tuesday, includes a drink.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Golden Corral
          </strong>
           Monday through Wednesday, from 5 PM  9PM, kids under age 10 can eat for free with an adult purchase.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           IHOP
          </strong>
           Kids under age 12 can eat for free with an adult purchase from 5PM  9PM.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Ikea
          </strong>
           Kids eat free on Tuesdays.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Islands
          </strong>
           Kids can eat free on Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Firehouse Subs
          </strong>
           Kids 12 and under eat free from 4PM  9PM with each adult purchase.
         </p>
