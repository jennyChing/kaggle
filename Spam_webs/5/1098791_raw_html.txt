         <div id="description-text">
          North Dakota naturally shed whitetail deer antler. Each antler listed for sale or used in my pieces has been collected by myself or family right here on our farm land in North Dakota.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Whitetail antlers are an amazing example of natures wonderful and beautiful handiwork. They range from tiny sharp spikes to amazing typical and non-typical racks. Antlers develop into every size and shape. An antler is comprised of solid bone tissue with a honeycombed structure. Whitetail deer shed their antlers every year prior to the re-growth of new ones.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          All of the antlers I have listed are tagged with a reference number; this ensures that you will have specific photos, information and details pertaining to the actual antler that you will receive. Deer antlers make amazing decorative pieces just as they are or can be turned into your very own personalized work of art.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          This listing is for a single whitetail deer antler, reference # H8. This is a left side 3 point antler measuring 17" along the main beam and weighs 13 ounces. This is an unusual antler in the fact that there is very little curve to the main beam, as well as the fact that although it is technically considered a 3 point antler there are 2 additional points that are just not quite long enough to count.  This antler has turned a porcelain white color with just a small amount of brown color visible.  There are no chips, cracks or nibbles.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          *I am happy to combine shipping on multiple antler orders!  The highest shipping rate is used first, each additional antler is $5.  If you would like to make a multiple antler purchase message me with the reference #'s and I will adjust the shipping rate for you.  Or, you can make the purchase and I will refund the difference.
