        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          birthdays require a special breakfast treat
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          and nothing says happy birthday better than
         </em>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <strong>
          <em>
           funfetti pancakes
          </em>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <img alt="funfetti pancakes" class="alignnone" height="900" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7484/16046669219_82edeaa686_b.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         This boy
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.heathersfrenchpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Charlie-Collage.jpg">
          <img alt="Charlie Collage" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-4463" height="600" src="http://www.heathersfrenchpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Charlie-Collage-600x600.jpg" width="600"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         taught me the
         <em>
          true
         </em>
         meaning of love at first sight, and that things dont always work out as planned, usually for the best
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         gave patience an entirely new meaning
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         has the biggest heart, and is not afraid to show it
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         has not stopped moving, ever
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         and 15 years ago this boy made me a mother
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         I think that deserves funfetti birthday cake pancakes
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <img alt="funfetti pancakes" class=" aligncenter" height="900" src="https://farm8.staticflickr.com/7479/16230918631_8b22f2a779_b.jpg" width="600"/>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
        </p>

             funfetti pancakes
            </div>

           <div class="zlmeta zlclear">
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
            </div>
            <div class="fl-l width-50">
            </div>

            </div>

           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-ingredients">
            Ingredients
           </p>
           <ul id="zlrecipe-ingredients-list">
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-0" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup flour
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-1" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 Tbsp sugar
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-2" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tsp baking powder
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-3" itemprop="ingredients">
             1/2 tsp salt
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-4" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 cup milk
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-5" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 egg
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-6" itemprop="ingredients">
             2 Tbsp butter
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-7" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tsp vanilla
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-8" itemprop="ingredients">
             1 tsp butter extract
            </li>
            <li class="ingredient" id="zlrecipe-ingredient-9" itemprop="ingredients">
             sprinkles
            </li>
           </ul>
           <p class="h-4 strong" id="zlrecipe-instructions">
            Instructions
           </p>
           <ul class="instructions" id="zlrecipe-instructions-list">
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-0" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             pre-heat skillet or griddle - medium heat (325 - 350 degrees)
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-1" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             combine the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-2" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             whisk together the milk, egg, butter, vanilla, and butter extract
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-3" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             pour the wet ingredients over the dry, and stir just until combined, add sprinkles
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-4" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             pour batter (use 1/4 - 1/3 cup per pancake)
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-5" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             cook until bubbles appear on the surface, and then flip
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-6" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             cook until  the bottoms are light golden, about another minute
            </li>
            <li class="instruction" id="zlrecipe-instruction-7" itemprop="recipeInstructions">
             serve with butter and syrup
            </li>
           </ul>
