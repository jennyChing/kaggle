         5 Foods To Boost Your Productivity At Work
        </h1>

        <p>
         <span style="color: #000000;">
          <span style="color: #000000;">
           Ever find it difficult to stay motivated at work? Try eating these foods to boost your productivity and improve your concentration.
          </span>
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.edensprings.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/foods-productivity1.png">
          <img alt="foods-productivity" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-2279" height="447" src="http://www.edensprings.co.uk/blog/wp-content/uploads/foods-productivity1-1024x733.png" title="click to enlarge" width="625"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #1e2567;">
          Blueberries
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Blueberries contain antioxidants which help boost your concentration and keepyour mind fresh by activating the flow of blood to your brain, aiding learning.
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #51af31;">
          Avocado
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Avocados are high in fibre and healthy fats which increase your ability toconcentrate as it keeps your hunger at bay.
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #ec671d;">
          Fish
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Fish is high in Omega 3 helping to aid brain function and memory, leading toimprovements in your concentration and mood in general.
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #f6a21d;">
          Green Tea
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Green tea gives you a natural caffeine boost and releases it into your body ata slower rate which allows you to be productive for longer.
        </p>
        <h3>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         <span style="color: #e61f28;">
          Dark Chocolate
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p>
         Dark chocolate contains a small amount of caffeine which helps to improvemental alertness whilst also satisfying your sugar cravings.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
