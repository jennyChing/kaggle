         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
          French Fries
         </h2>
         <p>
          Anything fried is already harmful for the body it contains polyunsaturated fats that release cancer causing free radicals in the body. And when potato products are fried it becomes more harmful as they release acrylamide a cancer causing chemical. Therefore not only do they provide fats but French fries are harsh enough to cause cancer in the future.
         </p>
        </div>

          Viewing 2 of 10
         </p>
         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
          Hot Dogs
         </h2>
         <p>
          Hot dogs seem delightful when brought from the neighboring street cart or when made at the backyard barbeque. But these meat sticks are processed and are harmful for the body because they increase the risk f diseases like heart disease, cancer, and diabetes. Not only the meat but the huge quantity of salt and preservatives also adds up to the negatives of this delightful food item.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>

         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
          Doughnuts
         </h2>
         <p>
          These round frosted sweets are always loved by people. But these doughnuts even without frosting are full of fat but are not filling for the stomach. They contain almost no nutrients therefore dont satisfy the hunger for a long time though 500 calories are dumped in as we eat them.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>

         </p>
         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
          Bacon
         </h2>
         <p>
          This tasty meat snack purely contains fat with apportion of it being polyunsaturated which becomes unhealthy once cooked.  Along with this the high content of sodium in bacon makes it more harmful as we consume it. It has one of the biggest sodium per gram content.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>

         </p>
         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
          Potato Chips
         </h2>
         <p>
          They cause the same harm which French fries cause as both are fried potato products. In addition French fries are quite filling for the stomach unlike these chips which are eaten in large quantities by people. Also the presence of sodium and  preservatives in large amount again becomes hazardous for the body.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>

          Viewing 6 of 10

         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
          Ice-cream
         </h2>
         <p>
          The most loved desert like by everyone is not that much sweet now. Ice creams have a very low amount of sugar but they are full of saturated fats. In solid form it is still less harmful but combining it with milkshake makes it more harmful as the number of calories rises up to 1000
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>

         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
          Mozzarella sticks
         </h2>
         <p>
          This snack is a delight to the cheese lovers. Though mozzarella is a healthy cheese yet it becomes unhealthy once it is breaded and fried. It also has  of the sodium content that is required for our body everyday making it more harmful for the body.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>

         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
          Hamburgers
         </h2>
         <p>
          Having a large quantity of fats and calories these meat patties are low in redeeming nutritive value. They have a direct connection to diseases like obesity and heart ailments.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>

         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
          Fish sticks
         </h2>
         <p>
          Fish is always said to be beneficial and healthy. But when it comes to fish sticks the statement gets wayward because the healthy fish is processed and then fried. Also the presence of salt and preservatives in a good quantity further makes it unhealthy for the body.
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>

         <h2 class="Heading4 title">
          Deep dish pizza
         </h2>
         <p>
          Pizza is one of the unhealthiest foods. Pizza has redeeming antioxidant properties but a deep dish pizza is loaded with cheese and has a thicker crust. Even a deep dish pizza  without meat toppings has streaming quantities of saturated fat and sodium.
