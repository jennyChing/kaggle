          <h2 style="text-align: center;">
           <em>
            <strong>
             Or Am I Not Trusting God
             <br/>
            </strong>
           </em>
          </h2>
          <p>
           After 3 months of looking for a job, I finally went on my first interview and got the job.
          </p>
          <p>
           But there seems to be a problem.
          </p>
          <p>
           During the interview he asks, What is your availability like?
          </p>
          <p>
           I proceed to tell him, Im fairly open except for Sundays.
          </p>
          <p>
           His response to me was something I actually expected, but wasnt hoping for. Then I dont have a job for you. During the busy season we need open availability here. Theres tons of money to be made here, so there are plenty of people who want to work.
          </p>
          <p>
           Okay, Im available on Sunday. I said this reluctantly, but what was I supposed to say? I havent worked in over 3 months and this is my first callback and interview within that time frame.
          </p>
          <p>
           That response started weighing on my heart as I walked out of the restaurant. This means Ill miss church. I didnt even fight for God.
          </p>
          <p>
           I barely have any excitement about finally having a job. I thought I was going to celebrate with the kids with pure excitement and joy. But instead all the thoughts for the exact reason I stopped working outside of the house started flooding my mind.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://ispeakhisname.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/working_at_restaurant.jpg">
            <div class="auto-pin-it">
             <img alt="Takine restaurant job not trusting god" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-615" height="200" src="http://ispeakhisname.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/working_at_restaurant-300x200.jpg" width="300"/>
             <div class="auto-pin-it-button">
              <a data-pin-config="none" data-pin-do="buttonPin" href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fispeakhisname.com%2Fis-god-testing-me-or-blessing-me%2F&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Fispeakhisname.com%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2015%2F03%2Fworking_at_restaurant-300x200.jpg&amp;description=Is%20God%20Testing%20Me%20or%20Blessing%20Me%3F" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
               <img src="http://passets-cdn.pinterest.com/images/pinit_preview_none.png"/>
              </a>
             </div>
            </div>
           </a>
           Im a single mother of 4 children. For the past two years I have worked from home and it has been amazing and difficult at the same time. But for many years before that I worked as a bartender and server. I was gone every weekend working and many nights during the week. I wasnt there for my kids. I missed important events in their schools and they spent alot of time home without me.
          </p>
          <p>
           Now Im heading back to that place. A place that I thought God pulled me out of. Funny thing is I think its my fault that Im heading back there.
          </p>
          <p>
           Let me explain,
          </p>
          <p>
           While on vacation in July I found out that my hours were going to be cut down. I was working from home doing the thing I loved to do marketing. I was working 40 hours a week down and it was going to be cut down to 20 hours. I was told that it would be this way for a few months. Then in August, the 20 hours went to 10 hours and then down to nothing. My boss had suffered a heart attack and wasnt able to work.
          </p>
          <p>
           So I picked up a few temporary jobs and stayed somewhat busy for a few months after that. All the time trying to work on this blog hoping for it to be successful enough so that I wouldnt have to worry about a job. But I only worked on my blog when I didnt have work. I wasnt doing both.
          </p>
          <p>
           After praying and asking for Gods guidance on what Im supposed to be doing. I couldnt keep a job for too long. He answered me. You arent trusting me. Dont you know who I am.
          </p>
          <p>
           Wow. I had to sit and reflect on this for a while. I wasnt trusting God, was I?
          </p>
          <p>
           This blog is for him. Its a way of my faith growing, helping others and worshiping Him. Ive learned so much from writing. Ive worked through thoughts, figured things out about myself and got closer to God. Ive been told by a few people that Ive even helped them.
           <em>
            Shouldnt this be enough of a reason to continue?
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           But I was still worried, still holding on to my fear about not having money to pay bills. After all its not logical to work on something that doesnt provide you with money. You need money and there is no way around this fact.
          </p>
          <p>
           But then Im not trusting God when I think that way, right?
          </p>
          <p>
           Ugh, Im so confused.
          </p>
          <p>
           So since yesterday at 3:57pm Ive been struggling with this
           <em>
            lack of trusting God
           </em>
           thing again. I thought I defeated it. Ive been working diligently on this blog trusting that God will provide for the bill, and He has been. Ive been blessed.
          </p>
          <p>
           I started praying that God only open the doors that he wants me to walk through while I searched for a job. Because I didnt want to sit still if I was making the wrong move just focusing on my blog.
           <em>
            (Again this isnt trusting is it?)
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           I figured I would work for a job so Id have money, and then work on my blog at night until it started providing the necessary income to support my family.
          </p>
          <p>
           Logical right?
          </p>
          <p>
           Then why do I feel guilty about taking a job before I even started working.
          </p>
          <p>
           Ill be missing out on my daughters graduation the first day I start working. Shes graduating from a club at school. This club is to develop her self-esteem. Ill miss it.
          </p>
          <p>
           I could potentially be missing out on church every Sunday for the next 6 months or more. Ill miss it.
          </p>
          <p>
           Will I be too tired from working 6 days a week to work on my blog?
          </p>
          <p>
           All because of money. Money that I need to provide the right life for my children. Do I risk not taking it and hold out for another job. Do I stick with just this blog and work on it full time as Ive been doing trusting God will provide for us.
          </p>
          <p>
           Im lost and confused.
          </p>
          <p>
           I believe I know the answer, but fear the journey. I would love to hear your Godly advice for me. Maybe he will speak to me through you.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
