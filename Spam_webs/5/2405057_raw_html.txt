        <p class="post-meta">
         Posted By

          <a href="http://resourcemagonline.com/2014/07/16-interesting-photography-podcasts/41130/#respond">

        </p>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          Whether you are a novice or a professional, photography podcasts are a great way to receive advice when youre on-the-go, which is an obvious universal trait amongst all photographers. As a result, Resource
          <em>
           Magazine
          </em>
          created a diverse list of some of the most inspirational photo podcasts around. Oh, and it doesnt hurt that most of the podcasts are free. In no particular order, here are the sixteen most interesting photography podcasts.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h2 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          16 Most Interesting Photography Podcasts
         </span>
        </h2>
        <h3 style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          1.
         </span>
         <span class="s1">
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/photography-tips-from-top/id73329429?mt=2" target="_blank">
           Tips from the Top Floor
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h3 style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/photography-tips-from-top/id73329429?mt=2" style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Tips-from-the-Top-Floor, Photography, Podcasts, Chris-Marquardt" height="261" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/tips_from_the_top_floor_byckc.jpg" width="261"/>
         </a>
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/cbolivar/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Tips-from-the-Top-Floor, Photography, Podcasts, Chris-Marquardt" height="400" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/chris_marquardt_mtsot.jpg" style="float: undefined;" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Photo 
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/cbolivar/" target="_blank">
           Carlos Bolivar
          </a>
          via
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/creativecommons/" target="_blank">
           Flickr Commons
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          Photographer and instructor
          <a href="http://chrismarquardt.com/" target="_blank">
           Chris Marquardt
          </a>
          s award-winning show, Tips from the Top Floor, is the longest running photography podcast to date. In his latest episode entitled, Toys, The Key To Photographic Success, Marquardt openly admits that hes recording from his home in Germany. His unequivocal honesty and humor is what attracts many to this podcast series.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          2.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/history-photography-podcasts/id94009100?mt=2" target="_blank">
           History of Photography
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/history-photography-podcasts/id94009100?mt=2" target="_blank">
          <img alt="Jeff-Curto, History-of-Photography, Podcasts, Photography, interesting-photography-podcasts" height="236" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/history_of_photography_4otfz.png" width="240"/>
         </a>
        </h3>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          <a href="http://www.jeffcurto.com/index.htm" target="_blank">
           Jeff Curto
          </a>
          , a photography professor at the College of DuPage near Chicago, records his class lectures about the history of photography. He not only teaches different time eras in photography history, but dedicates entire class sessions to prominent photographers like
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eadweard_Muybridge" target="_blank">
           Eadweard Muybridge
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Szarkowski" target="_blank">
           John Szarkowski
          </a>
          .
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          3.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-art-of-photography/id294540619?mt=2" target="_blank">
           The Art of Photography
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-art-of-photography/id294540619?mt=2" target="_blank">
           <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Ted-Forbes, Dallas-Museum-of-Art, Podcast, Photography, the-art-of-photography" height="244" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/the_art_of_taking_photography_qqgjy.png" style="float: undefined;" width="240"/>
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-art-of-photography/id294540619?mt=2" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Ted-Forbes, Dallas-Museum-of-Art, Podcast, Photography, the-art-of-photography" height="438" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/ted_forbes_1z6tq.jpg" style="float: undefined;" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Photo  MrDAT via Flickr Commons
         </p>
        </div>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          Every week, manager of multimedia for the Dallas Museum of Art,
          <a href="http://tedforbes.com/" target="_blank">
           Ted Forbes
          </a>
          , discusses an array of topics from composition to technique. He also provides intimate profiles of renowned photographers such as Andr Kertszs polaroids, Bruno Barbeys conflict photography and Julius Shulmans architectural photography.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          4.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/on-taking-pictures/id526466395?mt=2">
           On Taking Pictures
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/on-taking-pictures/id526466395?mt=2" style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, On-Taking-Pictures, Jeffery-Saddoris, Bill-Wadman, Podcasts" height="236" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/on_taking_pics_iwtsd.png" width="240"/>
         </a>
        </h3>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          What separates
          <a href="http://jefferysaddoris.com/" target="_blank">
           Jeffery Saddoris
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.billwadman.com/" target="_blank">
           Bill Wadman
          </a>
          s show, On Taking Pictures, from other podcasts is the in-depth repartee between the two hosts. New media artist, Saddoris, previously worked for Universal Pictures and founded
          <a href="http://fadedandblurred.com/" target="_blank">
           Faded + Blurred
          </a>
          . Wadman, a portrait photographer based out of Brooklyn, N.Y., had his work featured in
          <em>
           Time Magazine
          </em>
          and
          <em>
           The New Yorker
          </em>
          , just to name a few. They give personal and detailed advice for newcomers and experts.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          5.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/ask-tim-grey-digital-photography/id697117283?mt=2" target="_blank">
           Ask Tim Grey
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/ask-tim-grey-digital-photography/id697117283?mt=2" target="_blank">
          <span class="s1">
           <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Ask-Tim-Grey, Tim-Grey, podcasts, photography" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/tim_grey_inbp4.png" style="float: undefined;" title=""/>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          Digital photographer and author,
          <a href="http://www.timgrey.com" target="_blank">
           Tim Grey,
          </a>
          offers exclusive insight to his audience by allowing them to interact with him during the show. The basis of Greys show revolves around questions submitted by audience members.
          <strong>
          </strong>
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          6.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/martin-bailey-photography/id79677184?mt=2" target="_blank">
           Martin Bailey Photography Podcast
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <a href="http://www.martinbaileyphotography.com/">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Martin-Bailey-Photography-Podcast, Podcast, Pohotography, Martin-Bailey" class=" " height="400" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/martin_bailey_q5blf.jpg" style="float: undefined;" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Photo
          <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/robots-dreams/" target="_blank">
           @ Lem Fugitt
          </a>
          via
          <a href="http://www.flickr.com/creativecommons/" target="_blank">
           Flickr Creative Commons
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://www.martinbaileyphotography.com" style="line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          Martin Bailey
         </a>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          has one of the longest running photography podcasts devoted to a mixture of interviews, gear reviews, technique and critiques.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          7.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/shutters-inc/id79639195?mt=2" target="_blank">
           Shutters Inc.
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          Australian photographers
          <a href="http://www.shuttersincpodcast.com" target="_blank">
           Bruce Williams and Glynn Lavenders
          </a>
          podcast ranges from topics like why it sucks to be a mid-level wedding photographer to critiques on D-day veteran portraits.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          8.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/serge-ramelli/id543368680?mt=2" target="_blank">
           Photoshop, Lightroom Tips by Serge Ramelli
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <a href="http://photoserge.com/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Photoshop-Lightroom-Tips-by-Serge-Ramelli, photography, podcasts, Serge-Ramelli" class=" " height="400" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/serge_2_wrtdn.jpg" style="float: undefined;" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Photo
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/22375156@N02/" target="_blank">
            photoserge.com
          </a>
          via
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/creativecommons/" target="_blank">
           Flickr Creative Commons
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          Dramatic landscape photographer,
          <a href="http://photoserge.com" target="_blank">
           Serge Ramelli,
          </a>
          keeps his tutorials simple without using heavy tech terms. He abides by the visual example only principle when teaching new techniques.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          9.
          <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/photo-couch/id325202718?mt=2" target="_blank">
           Photo Couch
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <a href="http://seimstudios.com/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Photo-Couch, Gavin-Seim, Podcasts, photography" class=" " height="464" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/gavin_seim_r4ugm.jpg" style="float: undefined;" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          <br/>
          <span class="s1">
           Photo
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/seim/" target="_blank">
             Gavin Seim
           </a>
           via
           <a href="https://www.flickr.com/creativecommons/" target="_blank">
            Flickr Creative Commons
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          <a href="http://seimstudios.com" target="_blank">
           Gavin Seim
          </a>
          gives fun informal banter about landscape and portrait photography. Although
          <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/photo-couch/id325202718?mt=2" target="_blank">
           Photo Couch
          </a>
          hasnt been updated since 2012, it is still worth a listen.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
        </h3>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          10.
          <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/this-week-in-photo/id272987532?mt=2" target="_blank">
           This Week in Photo
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/this-week-in-photo/id272987532?mt=2" style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, This-Week-in-Photo, Frederick-Van-Johnson, Podcast, photography" height="242" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/twip_tzcy6.png" width="240"/>
         </a>
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <a href="http://frederickvan.com/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, This-Week-in-Photo, Frederick-Van-Johnson, Podcast, photography" height="400" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/frederick_ilrts.jpg" style="float: undefined;" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Photo
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/fredjohnson/" target="_blank">
            Frederick Van Johnson
          </a>
          via
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/creativecommons/" target="_blank">
           Flickr Creative Commons
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          What sets apart photographer
         </span>
         <a href="http://frederickvan.com" style="line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          Frederick Van Johnsons
         </a>
         <a href="http://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/this-week-in-photo/id272987532?mt=2" style="line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          This Week in Photo
         </a>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          from others is the social commentary. Issues like plagiarism and the future of photography are discussed in his forty minute to two hour show.
         </span>
         <a href="http://twitter.com/ThisWeekinPhoto" style="line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          TWiP
         </a>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          also offers a variety of tips ranging from portrait photography to tech tutorials.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          11.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/candid-frame-photography-podcast/id127842171?mt=2" target="_blank">
           The Candid Frame
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/candid-frame-photography-podcast/id127842171?mt=2" target="_blank">
          <span class="s1">
           <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Ibarionex-Perello, Candid-Frame-Photography-Podcast, Photography, Podcast" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/the_candid_frame_ijzsr.png" style="float: undefined;" title=""/>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <a href="http://www.perellophoto.com/-/perellophoto/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Ibarionex-Perello, Candid-Frame-Photography-Podcast, Photography, Podcast" height="400" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/ibarionex_nhruh.jpg" style="float: undefined;" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Photo
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/tedwaitt/" target="_blank">
            Ted Waitt
          </a>
          via
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/creativecommons/" target="_blank">
           Flickr Creative Commons
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="http://ibarionex.net" style="line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          <span class="s1">
           Ibarionex Perello
          </span>
         </a>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          
         </span>
         <span class="s1" style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          photographer, former technical engineer for Nikon and an associate editor for an array of photography magazineshosts a weekly hour long show dedicated to conversing with world renowned photographers.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          12.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/lenswork-photography-creative/id73802605?mt=2" target="_blank">
           LensWork
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <div class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 610px">
         <a href="http://www.lenswork.com/" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Brooks-Jensen, Lenswork, Photography, Podcast" height="443" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/lenswork_z9qhz.jpg" style="float: undefined;" width="600"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Photo
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/scotthendershot/" target="_blank">
            Scott (Jones) Hendershot
          </a>
          via
          <a href="https://www.flickr.com/creativecommons/" target="_blank">
           Flickr Creative Commons
          </a>
         </p>
        </div>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          Stemming from the twenty-year-old photography publication,
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.lenswork.com/index.html" style="line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          <em>
           LensWork
          </em>
          ,
         </a>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          this podcast has been around since 2004.
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.brooksjensenarts.com" style="line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          Brooks Jensen
         </a>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          the editor of
         </span>
         <em style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          LensWork
         </em>
         <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
          shares his experiences in the six minute long videos.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          13.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/photofocus/id512223214?mt=2" target="_blank">
           Photofocus
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/photofocus/id512223214?mt=2" style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Scott-Bourne, Richard-Harrington, Photofocus, Podcast, photography" height="242" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/photo_focus_innvk.png" width="240"/>
         </a>
        </h3>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          <a href="http://scottbourne.com" target="_blank">
           Scott Bourne
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.richardharrington.com" target="_blank">
           Richard Harringtons
          </a>
          podcast features pro photographers and examine the latest in the digital photography world. Listeners have the ability to ask questions on the show, which is recorded three times a month.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          14.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-freelance-radio-show/id888195252?mt=2" target="_blank">
           Freelance Radio
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          Although
          <a href="http://freelanceradio.com" target="_blank">
           Freelance Radio
          </a>
          is not a photo podcast, it offers advice for any freelancer trying to make it in the art world.
          <a href="http://about.me/dickieadams" target="_blank">
           Dickie Adams
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://kristenfischer.com" target="_blank">
           Kristen Fischer
          </a>
          , John Brougher and
          <a href="http://www.vonglitschka.com" target="_blank">
           Von Glitschka
          </a>
          discuss issues like running businesses, branding yourself as a freelancer and financial inquiries.
          <br/>
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          15.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-digital-story/id81837345?mt=2" target="_blank">
           The Digital Story
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-digital-story/id81837345?mt=2" style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Derrick-Story, The-Digital-Story, Podcasts, Photography " src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/digital_story_2_qmpn4.png" title=""/>
         </a>
        </h3>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          <a href="http://www.oreilly.com/pub/au/54" target="_blank">
           Derrick Story
          </a>
          provides a tech-heavy show for gadget fanatics, which includes equipment tips and reviews. His genial and easygoing demeanor fits nicely with the informative aspect of the show.
         </span>
        </p>
        <h3 class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          16.
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-grid/id424914578?mt=2" target="_blank">
           The Grid
          </a>
         </span>
        </h3>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-grid/id424914578?mt=2" target="_blank">
          <img alt="interesting-photography-podcasts, Scott-Kelby, Matt-Kloskowski, The-Grid, Photography, podcast" src="http://www.betaoutcdn.com/354/2014/07/the_grid_7ei7o.png" style="float: undefined;" title=""/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p class="p1" style="text-align: left;">
         <span class="s1">
          This live, interactive talk-show is hosted by photographers
          <a href="http://scottkelby.com" target="_blank">
           Scott Kelby
          </a>
          and
          <a href="http://www.mattk.com" target="_blank">
           Matt Kloskowsk
          </a>
          i and each show highlights a different guest. This podcast covers everything from how to use Snapseed to providing commentary on viewers photos. Viewers have the chance to interact with Scott and Matt through the shows blog on
          <a href="http://kelbytv.com" target="_blank">
           KelbyTV.com
          </a>
          or on
          <a href="https://twitter.com/TheGridLive" target="_blank">
           Twitter
          </a>
          . Just make sure to add
          <a href="https://twitter.com/search?q=%23thegridlive&amp;src=typd" target="_blank">
           #TheGridLive
          </a>
          at the end of your Tweet.
         </span>
