      <h1 class="name post-title entry-title" itemprop="itemReviewed" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Thing">
       <span itemprop="name">
        Former The Last Guardian Team Members Form Indie Game Studio
       </span>
      </h1>
      <p class="post-meta">
       <span>
        Posted by:
        <a href="http://techraptor.net/content/author/bryan" title="">
         Bryan Heraghty
        </a>
       </span>
       <span>
        June 23, 2014
       </span>
       <span>
        in
        <a href="http://techraptor.net/content/category/gaming" rel="category tag">
         Gaming
        </a>
        ,
        <a href="http://techraptor.net/content/category/gaming/news-gaming" rel="category tag">
         News
        </a>
       </span>
       Note: TR makes money off some product links
       <a href="http://techraptor.net/ethics-and-standards#affiliate">
        (info)
       </a>
      </p>
      <div class="clear">
      </div>
      <div class="addthis_sharing_toolbox">
      </div>
      <div class="entry">
       <p>
        The Last Guardian was officially announced five years ago, and since then weve heard all sorts of rumors about the team, its development, release date, etc. After news that some Sony staff members were leaving the company, many fans were, and still are, uneasy about the future of the game. But a few of those ex-members have come together to forma new studio under the name
        <a class="external" href="http://www.friendandfoegames.com/" target="_blank">
         Friend and Foe
        </a>
        . Based in Tokyo, Japan, the game believes in self-funding and self-publishing their games as far as possible.Ambitiously, the 5 person team is already working on two games simultaneously.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://techraptor.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Team_01.jpeg">
         <img alt="Team_01" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-10940" height="225" src="http://techraptor.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Team_01-1024x373.jpeg" width="618"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a class="external" href="http://www.friendandfoegames.com/dmen/" target="_blank">
         Dangerous Men
        </a>
        is a pulpy action arcade title based on the buddy cop movies of the 80s. Two cops, one city, and tons of explosions! The game stars officers Dan Mendoza and Don InsertNameHere (that wasnt a mistake, thats the characters actual name). The game has the characters literally jumping from explosion to explosion in an effort to rid the city of the menace known as Punk Gang. As of now, the Friend &amp; Foe team has yet to announce a platform or release date for this game.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://techraptor.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Dangerous-Men.jpg">
         <img alt="Dangerous Men" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10945" height="350" src="http://techraptor.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Dangerous-Men.jpg" width="650"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Secondly we have
        <a class="external" href="http://www.friendandfoegames.com/vane/" target="_blank">
         Vane
        </a>
        , an open world journeystarring a young boy with a mysterious ability. You unravel the mysteries of the land through solving puzzles, exploring, and other features yet to be revealed (though likely the mysterious ability mentioned). Vane is set to release for the PC, with other platforms to be announced. The release date was shrewdly left When its done.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://techraptor.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Vane-Storm.gif">
         <img alt="Vane Storm" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10943" height="261" src="http://techraptor.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Vane-Storm.gif" width="500"/>
        </a>
        The collective resumes of the team members includes such AAA titles The Last Guardian, Bionic Commando and Killzone! So this isnt exactly a team of underdogs, more a collection ofAAA veterans ready to try their hand in the indie market! For more developments of these games, this team, and whatever may happen to theelusive Team ICO game The Last Guardian, keep your sights on Tech Raptor!
       </p>
