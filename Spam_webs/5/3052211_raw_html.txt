         <h2 class="post-title">
          Have dinner with best-selling author Tim Ferriss
         </h2>
        </div>
        <div class="clear">
        </div>
        <div class="post-content-content">
         <span class="wpsr_floatbts_anchor" data-offset="25">
         </span>
         <p>
          <a href="http://edit911.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/img_7091.jpg?b09992">
           <img alt="tim ferriss best selling author podcast arnold schwarzenegger" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-4283" height="375" src="http://edit911.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/img_7091.jpg?b09992" width="500"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Tim Ferriss is an American author, entrepreneur, angel investor, and public speaker. Author of
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_4-Hour_Workweek" title="The 4-Hour Workweek">
           The 4-Hour Workweek
          </a>
          , which was a No. 1 New York Times bestseller, a No. 1
          <i>
           Wall Street Journal
          </i>
          bestseller, and a
          <i>
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USA_Today" title="USA Today">
            USA Today
           </a>
          </i>
          bestseller.
          <span style="font-size: 13.3333330154419px; line-height: 20px;">
          </span>
          In 2010, he followed up with
          <i>
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_4-Hour_Body" title="The 4-Hour Body">
            The 4-Hour Body
           </a>
          </i>
          , which was another No. 1
          <i>
           New York Times
          </i>
          bestseller.
          <span style="font-size: 13.3333330154419px; line-height: 20px;">
          </span>
          Ferriss is an angel investor or an advisor to Facebook, Twitter,
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/StumbleUpon" title="StumbleUpon">
           StumbleUpon
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evernote" title="Evernote">
           Evernote
          </a>
          , and
          <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uber_(company)" title="Uber (company)">
           Uber
          </a>
          , among other companies.
          <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-forbesfacebook_14-0">
          </sup>
         </p>
         <p>
          For a chance to have dinner with him, just promotehis latest podcast (an incredible interview with Arnold Schwarzenegger) via any means you choose.
         </p>
         <p>
          In the interview, Arnold tells thestory abouthis poor upbringing in Austria, making money in home construction, his body building and acting,lessons learned, routines, favorite books, meditation, and much more.
         </p>
         <p>
          Check out the podcast on
          <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-tim-ferriss-show/id863897795?mt=2">
           iTunes here
          </a>
          , or his blog
          <a href="http://fourhourworkweek.com/arnold">
           here
          </a>
          .
         </p>
