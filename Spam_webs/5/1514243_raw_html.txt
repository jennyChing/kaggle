               <p>
                The video appears to have been taken at Brush High School in northeast Ohio, when Good Games Athletics' "Camp Haden" was taking place.
               </p>
               <p>
                Also take note of the kid in the Haden No. 23 shirt who gets his signature with no issue.
               </p>
               <p>
                Haden takes the game seriously. Look no further than his
                <a adhocenable="false" href="http://www.newsnet5.com/sports/browns/browns-joe-haden-pours-heart-out-in-response-to-cecil-shorts-game-winning-touchdown-for-jaguars" target="_blank">
                 emotional response after a loss to the Jaguars
                </a>
                during the Browns' losing streak last season. His reaction to the kid is all in good fun, but the Browns-Steelers rivalry is serious business for the players and fans in Cleveland.
               </p>
               <p>
                (h/t
                <a adhocenable="false" href="http://www.waitingfornextyear.com/2014/07/joe-haden-shoos-away-kid-in-a-hines-ward-jersey/" target="_blank">
                 Waiting For Next Year

                )
