        <p class="mol-para-with-font">
         <font style="font-size:1.2em">
          On Friday night Ill be wearing Baths No 6 shirt for the second time in a Premiership game. The switch from midfield has worked for myself and the team, so Im hoping I can carry on playing in the back row for the rest of this season.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p class="mol-para-with-font">
         <font style="font-size:1.2em">
          The change of position was a decision my head coach, Mike Ford, made after the Champions Cup quarter-final against Leinster.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p class="mol-para-with-font">
         <font style="font-size:1.2em">
          He phoned me a day after that game to discuss playing me in the back row to maximize my on-field minutes in our run home, to what I call the big dance: thats the finals. He thought No 6 would suit the way we are trying to play as a team and also suit my personal strengths and I agreed.
         </font>
        </p>
