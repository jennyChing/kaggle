       <h3>
        Instructions
       </h3>
       <ul class="starlist">
        <li>
         Do not hyperextend your neck
        </li>
        <li>
         Lie flat on your front
        </li>
        <li>
         Place your hands on your lower back
        </li>
        <li>
         Extend your back
        </li>
        <li>
         Only extend until you feel a slight squeeze in your lower back muscle
        </li>
        <li>
         Lower yourself so your chest touches the floor
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Note
         </strong>
         - If you feel any pain in your lower back while performing the exercise other than the muscle contraction, stop immediately
        </li>
       </ul>
