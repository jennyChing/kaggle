              <section class="field-body">
               <table align="center" border="0" cellpadding="5" cellspacing="1" style="width: 100%">
                <tbody>
                 <tr>
                  <td>
                   <ul>
                    <li>
                     16 pounds tomatoes, peeled and cut into chunks (about 24 cups)
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     10 to 12 large onions, chopped (about 18 cups)
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     1 cup hot peppers, chopped
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     5 (or more to taste) cloves garlic, finely chopped
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     4 cups green bell peppers, chopped
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     2 tablespoons honey or sugar
                    </li>
                    <li>
                     2 cups cider vinegar
                    </li>
                   </ul>
                  </td>
                  <td>
                   <div class="media media-element-container media-full">
                    <div class="main-image">
                     <div class="image-inner">
                      <img alt="" data-src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/rodalesorganiclife.com/files/images/channel_images/cook/Salsa_1a.jpg" src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/all/modules/custom/lazyloader/image_placeholder.gif" style=" width: 300px" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                      <noscript>
                       <img alt="" src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/rodalesorganiclife.com/files/images/channel_images/cook/Salsa_1a.jpg" style=" width: 300px" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                      </noscript>
                     </div>
                    </div>
                   </div>
                  </td>
                 </tr>
                 <tr>
                  <td>
                   <p>
                    <strong>
                     1.
                    </strong>
                    Combine the ingredients in a large kettle (16 to 24 quarts), bring to a boil, reduce the heat, cover and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring at least once.
                   </p>
                  </td>
                  <td>
                   <div class="media media-element-container media-full">
                    <div class="main-image">
                     <div class="image-inner">
                      <img alt="" data-src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/rodalesorganiclife.com/files/images/channel_images/cook/Salsa_2a.jpg" src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/all/modules/custom/lazyloader/image_placeholder.gif" style=" width: 300px" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                      <noscript>
                       <img alt="" src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/rodalesorganiclife.com/files/images/channel_images/cook/Salsa_2a.jpg" style=" width: 300px" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                      </noscript>
                     </div>
                    </div>
                   </div>
                  </td>
                 </tr>
                 <tr>
                  <td>
                   <strong>
                    2.
                   </strong>
                   Using a slotted spoon, scoop the salsa into sterilized pint or quart jars, pack down and run a rubber spatula (or chopstick) around the inside edge to eliminate any air bubbles.
                  </td>
                  <td>
                   <div class="media media-element-container media-full">
                    <div class="main-image">
                     <div class="image-inner">
                      <img alt="" data-src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/rodalesorganiclife.com/files/images/channel_images/cook/Salsa_3a.jpg" src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/all/modules/custom/lazyloader/image_placeholder.gif" style=" width: 300px" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                      <noscript>
                       <img alt="" src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/rodalesorganiclife.com/files/images/channel_images/cook/Salsa_3a.jpg" style=" width: 300px" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                      </noscript>
                     </div>
                    </div>
                   </div>
                  </td>
                 </tr>
                 <tr>
                  <td>
                   <strong>
                    3.
                   </strong>
                   Seal and process in a hot-water bath: 35 minutes for pints, 45 minutes for quarts.
                  </td>
                  <td>
                   <div class="media media-element-container media-full">
                    <div class="main-image">
                     <div class="image-inner">
                      <img alt="" data-src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/rodalesorganiclife.com/files/images/channel_images/cook/Salsa_4a.jpg" src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/all/modules/custom/lazyloader/image_placeholder.gif" style=" width: 300px" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                      <noscript>
                       <img alt="" src="http://www.rodalesorganiclife.com/sites/rodalesorganiclife.com/files/images/channel_images/cook/Salsa_4a.jpg" style=" width: 300px" typeof="foaf:Image"/>
                      </noscript>
                     </div>
                    </div>
                   </div>
                  </td>
                 </tr>
                </tbody>
               </table>
               <p>
                <em>
                 YIELD: 10 quarts of salsa (2 quarts of liquid for hot sauce)
                </em>
               </p>
