     <div id="accessoriesContainer">
      <div id="accessoriesHeader">
       <h2>
        Signs of Use
       </h2>
       <p style="margin-top:0px">
        Scabs. Rotting teeth. Acne. Meth can do some nasty things to your appearance. Check out some of the most common.
       </p>
      </div>
      <div id="accessoriesStart">
       Tap the items below
      </div>
      <div class="accessoriesCopy verticalAlignIt" id="makeOverSpot1-Copy" style="padding-top:40px;">
       <span class="ROHeadline">
        Hair Loss
       </span>
       <span class="ROCopy">
        Meth users bodies are typically starved of nutrients needed to maintain healthy hair. Without these nutrients, hair becomes brittle and can fall out.
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="accessoriesCopy verticalAlignIt " id="makeOverSpot2-Copy" style="padding-top:25px;">
       <span class="ROHeadline">
        Decaying Teeth
       </span>
       <span class="ROCopy">
        Rotting teeth are so common to Meth users that the effect has a nameMeth Mouth. A combination of factors contributes to this dental decay, from tooth grinding to users disregard for hygiene or brushing.
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="accessoriesCopy verticalAlignIt" id="makeOverSpot3-Copy" style="padding-top:25px;">
       <span class="ROHeadline">
        Obsessive Plucking
       </span>
       <span class="ROCopy">
        Addicts use tweezers or other sharp objects to pick at imaginary bugs under their skin and some will pluck out their eyebrows in a tweaked-out state. They wind up with scabs and sores on their face and body.
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="accessoriesCopy verticalAlignIt" id="makeOverSpot4-Copy" style="padding-top:25px;">
       <span class="ROHeadline">
        Sickly Skin
       </span>
       <span class="ROCopy">
        Methamphetamine is a toxin so the body gets rid of the drug through the pores, causing severe acne and a diseased appearance. The drug also impedes the bodys ability to repair itself so sores and skin infections take longer to heal.
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="accessoriesCopy verticalAlignIt" id="makeOverSpot5-Copy" style="padding-top:25px;">
       <span class="ROHeadline">
        Foul Body Odor
       </span>
       <span class="ROCopy">
        Meth addicts can have a seriously unpleasant smell. The drug increases sweating and users often go for days without changing their clothes or bathing. In addition, the ammonia-like smell of the drug, similar to cat urine, stays with them.
       </span>
      </div>
      <div class="accessoriesCopy verticalAlignIt" id="makeOverSpot6-Copy" style="padding-top:10px;">
       <span class="ROHeadline">
        Weight Loss
       </span>
       <span class="ROCopy">
        Meth suppresses the appetite and users who go on bingesdoing Meth for days or even weekswill eat very little. Theyll experience an unhealthy loss of body mass, muscle atrophy, and weakness as the body goes into a dangerous state of starvation and starts eating away at muscle and fat.
       </span>
      </div>
