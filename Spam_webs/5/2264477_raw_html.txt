        <h1 class="sPost-hl">
         2 nights in Lapland, Kiruna, Sweden for just 297.10 each including flights, hotel, breakfast and airport bus!!
        </h1>
        <small class="sPost-mt">
         <span class="sPost-soc">
          <iframe allowtransparency="true" class="sPost-soc-it" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" src="https://www.facebook.com/plugins/like.php?href=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.holidaypirates.com%2Fholidaypackages%2F2-nights-lapland-sweden-gbp-297-flights-hotel-breakfast-bus&amp;send=false&amp;layout=button_count&amp;width=160&amp;show_faces=false&amp;font&amp;colorscheme=light&amp;action=like&amp;height=20" style="border:none; overflow:hidden; width:170px;">
          </iframe>
          <iframe allowtransparency="true" class="sPost-soc-it" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" src="https://platform.twitter.com/widgets/tweet_button.html?count=horizontal&amp;lang=en&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.holidaypirates.com%2F%3Fp%3D14540" style="width:100px;">
          </iframe>
         </span>
         <time class="sPost-dt" datetime="2014-11-25">
          25. Nov. 2014,  4:39 pm
         </time>
        </small>
       </div>
       <div class="sPost-ct editor">
        <p>
         <strong>
          2 nights in Northern Sweden (Lapland/Arctic Circle), Kiruna for just 297.10 including flights, hotel and airport bus.
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         This deal is based on 2 people travelling from London Gatwick on 12th January returning 14th January and staying in the Hotel Samegarden.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you would like to extend your trip by a day or two the prices are the same for the flight for the majority of the month as is the hotel we have chosen, use
         <a href="http://www.holidaypirates.com/recommends/Skyscanner/4472/post_content/14540" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Skyscanner
         </a>
         to search for other dates.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img src="http://www.holidaypirates.com/media/images/2014/11/13187037684-c70be52208-z-1416929732-Jkla-medium.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         Kiruna is in the Swedish Lapland (Arctic Circle), if we can still call it Lapland in January that is!! It is a National Park in the North of Sweden, far away from all the hustle and bustle of day to day life! It is super cold here all year round but especially in winter months with temperatures averaging -12oC in January so make sure you wrap up warm!!!
        </p>
        <p>
         The Aurora Borealis (Northern lights) are one of the main reasons people come to this gorgeous part of Sweden and as we have sent you here in January this is one of the best times to see the lights as the country is in darkness for the majority of the day!! Take one of the many tours to catch the best spots to see these magical lights and make it a holiday to remember!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img src="http://www.holidaypirates.com/media/images/2014/11/4612869968-8de8bbd755-z-1416929722-Qbik-medium.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         There is so much to see and do here including many Husky sledding tours, if its just taking a ride on a sled or taking a tour of Kiruna, doing one of these tours is definitely a must when here! There are many tour operators who offer this tour so make sure you shop around before you book the first one!
        </p>
        <p>
         Other activities include snowboarding, skiing, riding a snowmobile and mountain climbing!! There are plenty of things to do for everyone, check out TripAdvisor for more things to see and do in this beautiful area.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img src="http://www.holidaypirates.com/media/images/2014/11/kirunahtoelpic-1416929407-8JWq-medium.png"/>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         We have based you in the
         <a class="property_title" dir="ltr" href="http://www.holidaypirates.com/recommends/Hotel_Samegarden/10853/post_content/14540" id="property_1017532" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Hotel Samegarden
         </a>
         which has some really good reviews on Tripadvisor with a rating of 4/5. The hotel is located in the heart of Kiruna and is only a 5 minute walk to the bus station and an 8 minute walk from the train station!! The hotel has free Wifi, a sauna, lovely views, a flat screen TV and free parking if you would like to hire a car!!
        </p>
        <p>
         If you don't like the hotel we have chosen check out
         <a href="http://www.holidaypirates.com/recommends/Trivago/9092/post_content/14540" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Trivago
         </a>
         who compare hotels for some great prices.
        </p>
        <p>
         We suggest using
         <a href="http://www.holidaypirates.com/recommends/Carrentals/5389/post_content/14540" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Carrentals
         </a>
         .co.uk if you would like to hire a car.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you would like to get a taxi from the airport to the hotel it will cost around 30 each
        </p>
        <p>
         Flights bookable through
         <a href="http://www.holidaypirates.com/recommends/Norwegian/10854/post_content/14540" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Norwegian
         </a>
         - 406.20 - 203.10 each
        </p>
        <p>
         Hotel bookable through
         <a href="http://www.holidaypirates.com/recommends/Booking.com/10855/post_content/14540" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          Booking.com
         </a>
         - 154 - 77 each
        </p>
        <p>
         Bus tickets available on board 34 return  - 17 each
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Total price for 2 people - 594.20
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Total price each - 297.10
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
