       <h1>
        <span class="post-heading">
         Online Games In Windows Phone
        </span>
       </h1>
      </header>
      <span class="post-info">
       Posted by knowledgegame on May 13, 2014
      </span>
      <div class="tags">
      </div>
      <div class="single-news-page">
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Playing online games have become a fashion now a days. Where ever we go, who we meet specially the young generation, everywhere, everybody is just crazy about playing games online. Specially, in the mobile phones, it hardly matters where the place or what the time or situation is, we just log on to any site and start playing. It is the best mode of entertainment invented ever.
       </p>
       <p>
        The term Online Game represents the meaning as it requires internet connection and almost all of us are always connected to the internet either via computer or via mobile phones. Most of the branded mobile phones provide the facility of being connected to the internet in the platform of the operating system installed in the phones.
       </p>
       <p>
        There are two popular operating systems being installed in any branded phones out of which the most popular one is Windows Operating system for mobile phones and the other one is Android Operating System.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span id="more-14">
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        We all know that the Windows Operating is developed by Microsoft and the Windows Phone was first collaborated with NOKIA and launched in October 2010 with Windows Phone 7 and on October 29, 2012, Microsoft released Windows Phone 8. There are lots of features provided like User Interface, on screen text input, Messaging, Web Browser, Contacts, E-mail, Multimedia, Media Support, Games, Search Field, Office Suite, Multi-tasking and many more.
       </p>
       <p>
        As we all can see that the user of
        <strong>
         <a href="http://www.foreseegame.com/" target="_blank">
          online games
         </a>
        </strong>
        are increasing in a terrific high motion, it is very simple that the use of mobile phone is also increasing as it is very much convenient and popular to use internet in mobile phones so as to play online games in mobile phones. There are lots of high definition games being published every moment which are readily available to play online. We just need to have a compatible device or mobile phone and Windows phone is the best for it. The operating system installed in it is known as Mango which provides many user friendly applications to be played with.
       </p>
       <p>
        The game developers are inventing various types of games every moment and these games are either being installed and played offline or played directly online. Windows phone supports almost all of them and due to the high definition features of this phone, it has become very popular.
       </p>
