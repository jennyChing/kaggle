           <p>
            Rogue Brutal Bitter Beer Kit
Another great beer kit from Brewcraft USA and Rogue Ales. Brutal Bitter is similar to a classic English Bitter, only much, much bigger. 63 IBU and 6.1% alcohol... Not for the faint of heart!
            <br/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Kit makes 5 gallons. Includes instructions and all ingredients except yeast and a grain bag. Rogue's proprietary Pacman Ale yeast packaged by Wyeast is usually available to be purchased separately or as an option with this kit although the American ale yeast strains will also work well.
            <br/>
           </p>
           <p>
            Includes:
           </p>
           <ul>
            <li>
             Dry malt and wheat extract
            </li>
            <li>
             Specialty grains
            </li>
            <li>
             5.5 ounces of Crystal hops
            </li>
            <li>
             Yeast, bags, caps and priming sugar are NOT included
            </li>
            <li>
             Makes 5 gallons
            </li>
           </ul>
