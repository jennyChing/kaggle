              <p>
               Your "Simpsons" Lego minifigures will never go hungry again thanks to the well-stocked shelves of this Kwik-E-Mart set, complete with store manager Apu Nahasapeemapetilon.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

              <p>
               The Kwik-E-Mart has opening rear walls, a removable roof with Apu's secret vegetable garden, signage, light-blue walls, dusty blue floors, turquoise welcome mat, shelves, refrigerated cases, front counter, Buzz Cola soda fountain, juice dispensers, coffee machine, two arcade games, an ATM, crates of Powersauce bars, surveillance cameras, rear storage closet with a rat, cheese, rat hole and an exit door.
              </p>
              <p>
               The shelves are stocked with beauty products, diapers, dog food, pastries, fruits, vegetables and more -- including Krusty-O's and Chef Lonelyheart's Soup for One. The refrigerator cabinets are stocked with beverages including cans of Buzz Cola and even frozen Jasper.
              </p>
              <p>
               The front counter has a cash register, magazine and card display, lottery machine, hot dog oven, donut display and a Squishee dispenser with two Squishees.
              </p>
              <div class="ad-inpage-video-top" data-ad="inpage-video-top" id="inpage-video-top-55a4b087eb23a">

              <p>
               In addition to Apu, the set includes minifigures of Homer Simpson, Bart Simpson, Marge Simpson, Chief Wiggum and Snake the Jailbird (exclusive to the set).  Accessories for this set include Bart's spray can, Marge's shopping basket, Apu's broom and Chief Wiggum's cuffs and truncheon.
              </p>
