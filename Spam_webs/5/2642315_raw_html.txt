        <h2>
         Don't Leave Hoi An Without Doing a Little Tailor-made Shopping
        </h2>
        <p>
         Hoi An in Vietnam is renowned as being the best and cheapest place to pick yourself up a tailor-made suit. You can simply go into one of the many stores and they'll measure you up right there and then for a suit in any material you like. Saunders and Ollie's recent Dong Stray Bus tour included the opportunity to visit one of these shops in Hoi An.
        </p>
        <p>
         Saunders decided to get himself a very dapper get up, great for backpacking through Laos I'm sure.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you're not really the suit type you can also get dresses, jackets, trousers, handbags and shoes tailor made in Hoi An, just let your imagination run free!
        </p>
