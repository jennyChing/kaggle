         The Austin Chapter of the
         <a class="external" href="http://www.isesaustin.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.isesaustin.com', 'International Special Events Society (ISES)']);" target="_blank" title="International Special Events Society (ISES) Austin">

         <a class="external" href="http://avtechnicalsupport.com" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://avtechnicalsupport.com', 'AV Technical Support (AVTS)']);" target="_blank" title="AV Technical Support (AVTS)">

          Kelly Shores

        <p>
         Kelly discussed 10 different trends in video technology that can help build a more memorable experiencefor audiences at your event. Whether you are planning a social event, trade show, conference, or evening gala, these ideas canbe applied to help set your event apart from the crowd. Take a look at the video below to learn about these impactful tips.
