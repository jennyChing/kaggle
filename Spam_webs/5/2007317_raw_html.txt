         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          I Have Excuses
         </h1>
         <div class="entry-meta">
          Posted 	on
          <time class="entry-time" datetime="2014-11-11T18:27:38+00:00" itemprop="datePublished" title="Tuesday, November 11, 2014, 6:27 pm">
           November 11, 2014
          </time>
          <span class="entry-comments-link">
           <a href="http://kimberlychapman.com/monkeyloo/2014/11/11/i-have-excuses/#comments">
            Leave a Comment
           </a>
          </span>

        <div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
         <g:plusone href="http://kimberlychapman.com/monkeyloo/2014/11/11/i-have-excuses/" size="standard">
         </g:plusone>
         <p>
          I have been remiss in updating because Halloween and the Birmingham cake show were kicking my ass.  But those are over with now, so I hope to soon post lots of stuff thats been piling up.  I also need to post my cake show results over at
          <a href="http://eat-the-evidence.com">
           Eat the Evidence
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          But generally speaking, I get a lot less done than I intend each day because I am homeschooling Peo and Robin likes to climb up beside me whenever Im on the computer and grin into my peripheral vision until I acknowledge how cute she is.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_153" style="width: 510px">
          <img alt="Robin grinning at me" class="size-full wp-image-153" height="333" src="http://kimberlychapman.com/monkeyloo/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/11-4-14-robinpeeking1.jpg" width="500"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Robin uses her CUTE GRIN.  It is SUPER EFFECTIVE.
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Or if that doesnt get her enough attention, she just sticks her finger in my ear.
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_157" style="width: 510px">
          <img alt="She's shoving her baby wisdom directly into my brain.  Lucky me.  Because nothing helps me write my next romance novel like a Wet Willie." class="size-full wp-image-157" height="333" src="http://kimberlychapman.com/monkeyloo/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/2014-nov11-robinfingerear.gif" width="500"/>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Shes shoving her baby wisdom directly into my brain.  Lucky me.  Because nothing helps me write my next romance novel like a Wet Willie.
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Soyeah.  Be patient.  More monkeyloo goodness coming soon.
         </p>
         <p>
          And someone pass the cotton swabs, please.
         </p>
