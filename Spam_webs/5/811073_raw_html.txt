                        <br/>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; text-align: justify;">
                       <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                        Since it was a temporary
project, Abin Design Studio planned for the poles to be reused after the
festival and they were thus carefully re-catalogued and stored for the future.
                        <o:p>
                        </o:p>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt; text-align: justify;">
                       <br/>
                      </div>
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; text-align: justify;">
                       Today, they have found use
as decorative fencing for a football field of the same community  giving the
