        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         Email marketing is a cost-effective online marketing solution for businesses and you can cutback the cost of other forms of more costly advertising via TV, print or radio. But theres such an abundance of companies that offer email marketing solutions. How can you determine which one is the best for you?
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         When searching for the best email marketing partner for your business, it mainly depends on what you need. For example, are you looking for elegant email templates, social media integration, contact management systems, ease of use with drag and drop functionality, or maybe just something simple for low to no cost?
        </p>
        <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
         <span style="color: #e62f17;">
          List of4 Best Email Marketing Services
         </span>
        </h2>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         There are plenty of email marketing tools/services available, whatever your business needs may be. Here is a list of the top four email marketing services that money can buy.
         <span id="more-85">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <strong>
          <div class="su-note" style="border-color:#b8cadc;border-radius:3px;-moz-border-radius:3px;-webkit-border-radius:3px;">

            01. AWeber
           </div>
          </div>
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <img alt="aweber" class="alignleft wp-image-87 size-thumbnail" data-recalc-dims="1" src="//i0.wp.com/www.woblogger.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/aweber.jpg?resize=150%2C150"/>
         Aweber, an Email marketing service is best known for its versatile email marketing for bloggers. Top bloggers like John Chow, Shoemoney, Yaro Starak are tremendously making use of Email Marketing to promote their affiliate product and services using Aweber. Aweber was started specially for email marketing and to provide email newsletter.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         AWber offers very useful features for just $1 for your first month. For that you receive an autoresponder, so you can send emails to different groups of your subscribers for different reasons (i.e., welcome and thank you emails, etc.). And after a month, your monthly fee increases to $19 (cost $19/monthly to send newsletter to 0  500 subscribers), which is a decent price for the services they offer.
