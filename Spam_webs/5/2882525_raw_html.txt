             <p style="text-align: justify; width: 640px;">
              <img alt="A-Cero_Vivienda-06" height="425" src="/images/stories/10_q2_projects/A-Cero_Vivienda/A-Cero_Vivienda-06.jpg" style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; float: left;" width="640"/>
              A-cero Architecture describes the design of this house as The maximum expression of minimalism. The house is situated in the upscale neighbourhood of La Finca in Pozuelo de Alarcn (Madrid), within an exclusive housing development, an area with wide green spaces, lakes and spectaculars houses, and most if them actually designed by A-cero.
             </p>
             <p style="text-align: justify; width: 640px;">
              <img alt="A-Cero_Vivienda-01" height="475" src="/images/stories/10_q2_projects/A-Cero_Vivienda/A-Cero_Vivienda-01.jpg" style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; margin-right: 10px; float: left;" width="315"/>
              <img alt="A-Cero_Vivienda-02" height="475" src="/images/stories/10_q2_projects/A-Cero_Vivienda/A-Cero_Vivienda-02.jpg" style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; float: left;" width="315"/>
              <img alt="A-Cero_Vivienda-05" height="425" src="/images/stories/10_q2_projects/A-Cero_Vivienda/A-Cero_Vivienda-05.jpg" style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; float: left;" width="640"/>
              The luxurious residence is composed of rectilinear volumes, straight lines and simple shapes. The exterior is cladded with Travertine marble interjected with a generous amount of fenestration that helps in bringing a healthy dose of natural sunlight indoor.
              <br/>
              <img alt="A-Cero_Vivienda-03" height="425" src="/images/stories/10_q2_projects/A-Cero_Vivienda/A-Cero_Vivienda-03.jpg" style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; float: left;" width="640"/>
              <br/>
              <img alt="A-Cero_Vivienda-10" height="425" src="/images/stories/10_q2_projects/A-Cero_Vivienda/A-Cero_Vivienda-10.jpg" style="margin-top: 15px; margin-bottom: 15px; float: left;" width="640"/>
              While adapting the house to its sloping of the site, A-cero distributes the functions of this 1,600 m2 (17,000 sq ft) house on three floors including basement within a typical pattern; The garage and service area are in the basement, the public spaces (lounge, dining room, living room) on the first floor, while bedrooms and other private rooms are in the high floor.
              <br/>
