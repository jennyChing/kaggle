       <img alt="tomek plonka illustration colour surreal yellow mickey mouse disney" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-66614" height="1235" src="http://illusion.scene360.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/tomek-11.jpg" width="1700"/>
       <div class="adv-single-top-leaderboard-box">
        <div class="adv-single-top-leaderboard">

       <p>
        Taking pop culture icons like Mickey Mouse and Pluto and reconstructing them with a zany colour palette and a surreal twist, is what Warsaw-based illustrator
        <a href="http://infernalcollection.com" target="_blank">
         Tomek Ponka
        </a>
        does so well. The contrast he achieves in his art between colour and black and white is beautiful. His charactersare nightmarish visions of traditional subject matters, as we see human portraits distorted with tree trunks and branches knotted into fantastical shapes.
       </p>
       <img alt="tomek plonka illustration colour surreal pluto disney" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-66601" height="1304" src="http://illusion.scene360.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/tomek-02.jpg" width="1500"/>

       <img alt="tomek plonka illustration surreal black white portrait" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-66602" height="1866" src="http://illusion.scene360.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/tomek-03.jpg" width="1500"/>
