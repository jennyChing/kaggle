         <p>
          Stuntman Gary Connery has sensationally completed an audacious skydive that saw him plummet 2400 ft towards the ground WITHOUT a parachute.
         </p>
         <p>
          The 42-year-old basejumper became the first person to achieve the feat from such a height after he crash-landed into a landing strip of cardboard boxes after making the leap of faith out of a helicopter over Ridge Wood in Buckinghamshire.
         </p>

         </p>

          "I'm 100 per cent confident I can achieve this jump, Gary said beforehand.
         </p>
         <p>
          I know I can fly, I know I can hit the target, I feel I've assessed the risk that I'm presented with and I've put everything in place to minimise that risk.
         </p>
         <p>
