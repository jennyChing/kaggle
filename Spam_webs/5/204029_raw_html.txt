             You have probably found this page, because you are interested in bringing home a little ball of fur home.
            </p>
            <p>
             I am here to let you in on a little secret. And maybe even bust your bubble about buying a pet rabbit.
            </p>
            <p>
             Ihave a pet rabbit name Mrs. Puff Of Fur, she is a Lion Head Rabbit Mix.
            </p>

            <h2>
             Think Hard About Buying A Bunny Rabbit As A Pet
            </h2>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <!--more-->
             <a href="http://leadsystemnetwork.com/wp/this4allen/wp-content/uploads/sites/952/2014/09/RABBIT.png">
              <img alt="Rabbits,how to care for a rabbit, what to consider before buying a rabbits,dwarf rabbits,loopy ear rabbit,lion-head rabbits" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-427" height="960" src="http://leadsystemnetwork.com/wp/this4allen/wp-content/uploads/sites/952/2014/09/RABBIT-682x1024.png" width="640"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             My husband brought her home, well he called me first.
            </p>
            <p>
             He asks me if wanted a pet rabbit. I first told him no. Allen went on and explain the situation to me.
            </p>
            <p>
             The ownerwas a client of my husband. She was moving and couldnt take Bambi with her.
            </p>
            <p>
             (FYI) rabbit name before I change it. The woman had 6 huskys andBambi was kept in the cage all the time.
            </p>
            <p>
             The woman explained, to Allen she brought Bambi for her daughter. The woman forgets one thingabout her daughter, she is a teenager.
            </p>
            <p>
             The girl had no interest in Bambi the only thingshe did, do was throw a bunch of food in her cage.
            </p>
            <p>
             Finally, I agree after the woman sent me a picture of her, I fell in love instantly.
            </p>
            <p>
             My husband brought her home, I went out to the car and the poor thing was shaking. Clearly by looking at her, she was not groomedor use to being around people.
            </p>
            <p>
             I ask many questions about the rabbithistory, Allen told methe women did her best to take care ofBambi. She knew it wasnt good enough that is why she wanted to find Bambi a good home where she will be loved.
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <span style="text-decoration: underline">
              <strong>
               Pet Rabbit Breeds
              </strong>
             </span>
            </p>
            <p>
             You have to investigate which breed is best for you or your family
            </p>
            <p>
             They are many kinds of rabbit breeds.
             <br/>
             For, Example: angora rabbits, dwarf rabbits, lion-head rabbit. Just to name a few.
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              <span style="text-decoration: underline">
               Rabbits As Pets
              </span>
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             You need to know rabbits can live a long time are you willing to make a commit?
             <br/>
             Did you know that rabbits can live up to 9-12 years Or longer.
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              <span style="text-decoration: underline">
               Pet Rabbit Care Information
              </span>
             </strong>
            </p>
            <p>
             A rabbit needs food and water and plenty of exercise.
            </p>
            <p>
             Also, there are very sociableanimals, if you have one already you need to get another.Just make sure its spayed, You cant keep a rabbit in a cage all day or night.
            </p>
            <p>
             And they need interaction.
            </p>
            <p>
             You need to bunny proof your home, To protect you and the rabbit, You dont want to put the yourrabbit in a situation.
            </p>
            <p>
             Because the Rabbit chewed on your walls or furniture and wires.
            </p>
            <p>
             When this happens, people want to get rid of them.
            </p>
            <p>
             Many people like to get baby rabbits for Easter holiday as gifts, not a good ideal.
            </p>
            <p>
             Your finances need to be stable not to say anything will happen. You need to take the rabbit to the vetmake sure the rabbit stays healthy and to get be sprayed.Because rabbits can reproduce quicker than humans.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://leadsystemnetwork.com/wp/this4allen/wp-content/uploads/sites/952/2014/09/rabbit-426131_640.jpg">
              <img alt="rabbit-426131_640" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-353" height="426" src="http://leadsystemnetwork.com/wp/this4allen/wp-content/uploads/sites/952/2014/09/rabbit-426131_640.jpg" width="640"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             Many people dont know how to tell the sex of their rabbit and think they have purchased 2 females instead one of them is a male.
            </p>
            <p>
             A rabbit can become pregnant as young as 12 weeks.
            </p>
            <p>
             Rabbits gestate for only 30 days, and usually have litters of between 4 and 12 babies (kits), depending on the breeds.
            </p>
            <p>
             Once the babies are born, the doe can mate and get pregnant again as soon as the following day.
            </p>
            <p>
             If they maintain a pace like that and all the kits survive, the large-litter breeds are looking at about 100 babies per season.
            </p>
            <p>
             Stretch that out over a lifetime, and youve got over 1,000 babies per rabbit.
            </p>
            <p>
             You do not want that nightmare, and thats is why many rabbits end in shelters put down or set free. Because their owners didnt do their research and couldnt take care of them.
            </p>
            <p>
             Some even say rabbits are mean. Young rabbit tends to more aggressive, more energy sometime their bite or stomp their paws. Just trying toestablish territory, You need to watch your rabbit body language and they do communicate. Remember to give them space.
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://leadsystemnetwork.com/wp/this4allen/wp-content/uploads/sites/952/2014/09/rabbit-361666_640.jpg">
              <img alt="angora rabbits, animal, animals, baby rabbit, baby rabbits, cage for rabbit, cute rabbit, dwarf rabbits, lionhead rabbit, pet, pet rabbit, pets, rabbit, rabbit cage, rabbit cages, rabbit food, rabbit hutches, rabbits, rabbits breeds, rabbits for sale" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-345" height="480" src="http://leadsystemnetwork.com/wp/this4allen/wp-content/uploads/sites/952/2014/09/rabbit-361666_640.jpg" width="640"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              <span style="text-decoration: underline">
               Rabbit Care Basics
              </span>
             </strong>
             <br/>
             <strong>
              <span style="text-decoration: underline">
               A Bunny Need To Have Supplies
              </span>
             </strong>
             <br/>
             Rabbit Proofing Your House &amp; Valuables
            </p>
            <p>
             Rabbit Food
            </p>
            <p>
             Vegetables &amp; Water
            </p>
            <p>
             (Carrots Limited Amount) Dont want to give them too much sugar)
            </p>
            <p>
             Pet rabbit cage or Hutch
            </p>
            <p>
             Pet bunny toys
            </p>
            <p>
             Friendship
            </p>
            <p>
             Fun
            </p>
            <p>
             Clean environment
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             <strong>
              <span style="text-decoration: underline">
               All things Consider Before Buying A Pet Rabbit

             </strong>

            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
            </p>
            <p>
             I hope this information was helpful, able to givethe whole pictureto help you makea good decision to bringhome a Pet rabbit.
            </p>
            <p>
             You need to know rabbits can live a long time not a disposablepet.
            </p>
            <p>
             And lastly, you have to love them like you would your own children.
