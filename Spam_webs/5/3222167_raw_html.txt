        <p class="caps">
        </p>
        <h5>
         The DJ was very professional, personable, and was open to any suggestions that I had. During the reception, he seamlessly transitioned from one song to the next which kept everyone on the dance floor. It was exactly what Id hoped for!  Joslin Latz
        </h5>
        <p class="intro">
         This weekend I was honored to be a part of a company friends special day. Maureen Davis hired us last year for her son Kyles
         <strong>
          Wedding Reception
         </strong>
         , which was held this past Sunday in
         <strong>
          <em>
           Seymour, Ct
          </em>
         </strong>
         at the
         <a href="http://www.villabianca.com" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.villabianca.com', 'Inn at Villa Bianca');" target="_blank">
          Inn at Villa Bianca
         </a>
         . We setup a small
         <em>
          PA system
         </em>
         at the lower pavilion for an outside ceremony. A
         <em>
          wireless microphone
         </em>
         was also provided for the officiant and a reader. The bridal party walked down the aisle to
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CbMeAOTPJzM" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CbMeAOTPJzM', ' Cat Powers Sea of Love');" target="_blank">
          Cat Powers Sea of Love
         </a>
         . The Bride entered to the classical
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hOA-2hl1Vbc" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hOA-2hl1Vbc', 'Canon in D');" target="_blank">
          Canon in D
         </a>
         . The
         <strong>
          Ceremony
         </strong>
         was performed by a Justice of the Peace, and a reading of What Love Is by a family member. After the couple was married, they walked back down the aisle to
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cl88QEll-Xc" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cl88QEll-Xc', 'Jason Mraz Im Yours');" target="_blank">
          Jason Mraz Im Yours
         </a>
         .
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4088.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCN4088" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-777" height="225" src="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4088-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         The couple went for pictures while the guests made their way to the main pavilion the cocktail hour. When it was time to introduce the
         <strong>
          <em>
           Bridal Party
          </em>
         </strong>
         , parents and couple int o the main recption hall, each couple was introduced to their own song which they chose or was selected by the couple for a special meaning.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4118-e1346809023470.jpg">
          <img alt="Bride &amp; Groom First Dance with Purple Hearts Monogram" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-782" height="300" src="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4118-e1346809023470-225x300.jpg" width="225"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Directly after introductions Kyle and Joslin danced for the first time as Husband and Wife together to
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aDm2AIw7Myo" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aDm2AIw7Myo', 'Tim Mcgraws My Best Friend');" target="_blank">
          Tim Mcgraws My Best Friend
         </a>
         . As Dinner was served, the staff at the Villa Bianca had a complete table setting for the photographer and I, and served us all 3 courses. I mention this because this was just one facet of this venue that testified to their professionalism and committment to a successful event. The Maitre D Tiffany was on top of things all night, and coordinated the timeline and all events with us to perfection. Our
         <a href="http://ctpartystudios.com/parties/lighting/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://ctpartystudios.com/parties/lighting/', 'lighting');" title="Lighting">
          lighting
         </a>
         for this elegant evening included LED color Wash lights for the dance floor, purple/blue uplighting on the pavilion pillars and a
         <a href="http://ctpartystudios.com/led-party-lighting-explained/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://ctpartystudios.com/led-party-lighting-explained/', 'dance floor monogram');" title="LED Party Lighting Explained">
          dance floor monogram
         </a>
         . The monogram was a heart pattern, set with a purple color.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4105.jpg">
          <br/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4100.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCN4100" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-779" height="225" src="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4100-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4093-e1346809085232.jpg">
          <img alt="LED Uplighting Outside Patio Pillars" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-778" height="300" src="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4093-e1346809085232-225x300.jpg" width="225"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Dinner included a salad, pasta, and choice of Beef, Chicken or Fish. After Dinner the couple cut a 4tier Chocolate Cake, decorated in a beach sand theme. During Dinner, I got the chance to get acquainted with the
         <strong>
          photographer
         </strong>
         of the evening. I was thrilled to discover that she was based out of Quaker Hill, just minutes from our office. her name is Karen Schlink, and her website
         <a href="http://www.karenschlink.com" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.karenschlink.com', 'www.karenschlink.com');" target="_blank">
          www.karenschlink.com
         </a>
         should be updated soon with high quality professional shots of this Wedding. We will keep you updated and make an attempt to post some of Karens work here, with her permission.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4138.jpg">
          <img alt="DSCN4138" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-784" height="225" src="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4138-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
         <a href="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4105.jpg">
          <img alt="PurpleHeart Lighted Dance Floor Monogram" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-780" height="225" src="http://djmikeortiz.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN4105-300x225.jpg" width="300"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         We kept the music flowing smoothly with mixes that transitioned seamlessly from the 70s to 80s and all the way to Pitbull. Below is a video mix of the dance floor in action, many with the Bride out in front having a blast! We always tell our clients that as long as the Bride is having fun, the rest of the party will follow. http://youtu.be/yrIgGed_BcY  I must add the following to this post because I think some couples will be able to use this idea. Below is a picture of a koozie the couple had made with their name and Wedding date on it. There was a table with these laid out, and enough for each guest to have more than 1. I was given one of these myself. I must say this is more than just a great Wedding favor idea, it is so practical, modern, and useful. I have been using mine all Labor Day weekend since!
        </p>
