          <h3 class="entry-title">
           In the Fast Lane: Internet Connection Speeds
          </h3>
          <p>
           Do you get fed up waiting for a website to load? Do videos freeze when you try to watch them online? In terms of Internet connection speeds, the United States falls behind many other countries in the world.
           <span id="more-5844">
           </span>
           Currently, the global average peak connection speed is 18.9 Mbps, while the global average connection speed is 3.3 Mbps. The United States is well above the global average for both speeds, but falls way short to countries like South Korea, Switzerland and Hong Kong.
           <a href="http://thinkpyxl.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Internet_InThe_FastLane_Pyxl_v2-1.jpg">
            <img alt="internet connection speeds" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-5845" height="1444" src="http://thinkpyxl.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Internet_InThe_FastLane_Pyxl_v2-1-450x1444.jpg" width="450"/>
           </a>
           We hope the United States will follow suit and jump on the fast-Internet bandwagon soon! Wouldnt it be nice if you didnt have to sit in front of a video buffering for five minutes?
          </p>
