          <h2 class="style3New">
           Huda Residential Plots in Gurgaon
          </h2>
          Haryana Urban Development Plots has been raining plots lately with various plots being made available across the region. All plots are for the residential as well as commercial purposes that are allotted by the modus of inviting applications from the prospective buyers. These applications are readily made available in leading National newspapers. All applications received against the advertisements are pooled into a draw and allotments are done to successful applicants. Each successful applicant receives complete details of terms and conditions along with mode and schedule of payments to be done.
          <br/>
          <h3 class="style3New">
           Payment Mode and Schedule
          </h3>
          The complete procedure of allotment and payment is as per the HUDA Act of 1977. 10 percent of the probable cost of the plot is to be deposited as earnest money at the time of submission of the application. Once the letter of allotment is issued 15 percent of the money is to be deposited within 30 days of date of issuance of the letter.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Allotted applicants have to pay the balance amount in 60 days of date of issuance of letter without any interest. Alternatively, they have an option to pay the balance money in six annual installments at the rate of 9 percent interest. The first installment is payable after the expiry of one year of date of issuance of the letter. The interest on the balance amounts counts from the date of offer of possession.
          <br/>
          <h3 class="style3New">
           Huda Plots Investment
          </h3>
          Mostly these residential and commercial HUDA plots has been expected to be lower than the market price. They have also been expected to give huge return on investments. In most of the cases, these plots have been taken with a foresight of investment purposes. In general HUDA plots have gradually seen an uptrend in prices. Sector 31, 39, 40, 45, 46 and 47 are some of the well-located and developing sectors that have better chances of giving return on investments. These plots are expected to give better return on investments in next five years especially the ones being developed by private colonies.
          <br/>
          <h4 class="style3New">
           HUDA Plot Regions
           <h4>
            HUDA plots have been offered in five major urban regions of Haryana including Panipat, Sonepat, Panchkula, Bahadurgarh and Faridabad. The largest numbers of plots are available around Faridabad which also happens to be the largest city of the state. HUDA plots are being regularly offered for residential or commercial purposes by the government for those who need to invest for a longer period of time and good return on investments. HUDA is now planning to offer new plots in New Gurgaon near Manesar region. Plots sizes would be available in 60 sq yards, 160 sq yards, 263 sq yards, 360 sq yards, and 500 sq. Yards.
