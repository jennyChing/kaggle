        The Green and White Rug Pattern
       </h2>

          <div class="field field-type-filefield field-field-pattern-image">
           <div class="field-items">
            <div class="field-item odd">
             <img alt="The Green and White Rug Pattern" class="imagefield imagefield-field_pattern_image" height="300" src="http://freevintagecrochet.com/sites/default/files/images/star93-p5.jpg?1414945401" title="The Green and White Rug Pattern" width="259"/>
            </div>

          <br/>
          <p>
           <b>
            23 Inches x 37  Inches
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Materials Required: AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY
            <br/>
            "STAR" BLEND YARN, Article 75
           </b>
           <br/>
           15 skeins White.
           <br/>
           14 skeins Dk. Green.
          </p>
          <p>
           Each square measures 7  inches x 7  inches.
          </p>
          <p>
           Steel crochet hook No. 0.
          </p>
          <p>
           With White ch 50, d c in 8th st from hook, * ch 2, skip 2 sts of ch, d c in next st, repeat from * across ch, ch 5, turn (15 meshes).
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            2nd Row.
           </b>
           D c in next d c, * ch 2, d c in next d c, repeat from * across row, ending row with ch 2, d c in 3rd st of end ch, ch 5, turn. Repeat the last row 13 times, cut yarn.
          </p>
          <p>
           Attach Green in upper right hand corner mesh of outer row of meshes, ch 3, 5 d c in same mesh, * working downward, 3 d c over next post, working across, work 3 d c over top of next mesh of previous row, working upward, 3 d c over next post, working across, work 3 d c over top of next mesh, repeat from * to 2nd mesh before corner, working downward, 3 d c over next post, working across, 3 d c over top of next mesh, working upward, 3 d c over next post, work 6 d c in corner mesh, repeat from 1st * all around ending round to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch, cut yarn.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Next Round.
           </b>
           Attach Green in post on left hand side of corner mesh of next free round, ch 3, working upward, 2 d c over post, * working across, 3 d c over next free post of same round, working downward, 3 d c over next post, working across, 3 d c over next post, working upward, 3 d c over next post, repeat from * to mesh before corner mesh, working across, 3 d c over next free post, working downward, 3 d c over next post of corner mesh, working upward, 3 d c over next post of same mesh, repeat from 1st * all around ending round to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch, cut yarn.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Next Round.
           </b>
           Attach White in post on right hand side of any corner mesh of next free row, ch 3, working upward, 2 d c over post, ** working across, 3 d c over next free post of same mesh, * working downward, 3 d c over next post, working across, 3 d c over next free post, working upward, 3 d c over next post, working across, 3 d c over next free post, repeat from * to corner mesh, repeat from ** all around ending round to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch, cut yarn. Repeat the last 2 rounds working in same manner until all meshes are worked, working 1 more row White, 2 rows Green, 1 row White. Work 14 more squares in same manner. Sew squares together as illustrated.
          </p>
