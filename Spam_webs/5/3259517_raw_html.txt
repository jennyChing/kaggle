            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/tag/jason-bateman/" target="_blank" title="Jason Bateman">
             Jason Bateman
            </a>
            was photographed enjoying the day out on Sunday with his wife
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/tag/amanda-anka/" target="_blank" title="Amanda Anka">
             Amanda
            </a>
            and their two daughters, Francesca and Maple.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/09/22/jason-bateman-and-amanda-anka-stroll-in-la-with-their-girls/jason-bateman-and-wife-amanda-anka-out-in-la-with-their-daughters-frances-and-maple-2/" rel="attachment wp-att-92439">

            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The girls ate oranges from the market and ran ahead while their parents carried their produce and fresh flowers.
           </p>
           <p>
            Jason was weekend casual in jeans, sneakers, a hoodie and a baseball cap. He kept his girls close as he picked out some fresh fruit.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/2014/09/22/jason-bateman-and-amanda-anka-stroll-in-la-with-their-girls/jason-bateman-out-in-la-with-his-daughter-frances/" rel="attachment wp-att-92440">
             <img alt="Jason Bateman out in LA with his daughter Frances" class="aligncenter wp-image-92440" height="687" src="http://www.growingyourbaby.com/wp-content/uploads/Jason-Bateman-out-in-LA-with-his-daughter-Frances-491x750.jpg" width="450"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Jason has been busy filming three back to back films. This Summer he directed and starred opposite Nicole Kidman and Christopher Walken in The Family Fang. Over the weekend his new movie, This Is Where I Leave You opened in theaters, and Horrible Bosses 2 will follow on November 26!
           </p>
