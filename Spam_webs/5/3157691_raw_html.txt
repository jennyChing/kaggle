     <h1 class="article-headline wordy no-hero">
      Stargate Set to be Rebooted as a Movie Trilogy
     </h1>
     <div class="article-content" id="article-content">
      <div class="article-subhead">
       <div class="article-share-count-container">
        <span class="icon icon-sharrow">

       The sci-fi franchise may get a new beginning in three parts.
      </div>
      <span class="article-byline icon-pointy">
       By
       <a href="http://people.ign.com/Luce_IGN_AU">
        Lucy O'Brien

      <p>
       Independence Day and White House Down director Roland Emmerich has plans to reboot his
       <a class="autolink" data-legacy-id="41016" href="http://www.ign.com/movies/stargate" title="Stargate">
        Stargate
       </a>
       franchise as a movie trilogy.
      </p>
      <p>
       Emmerech, one of the co-creators of the original Stargate movie back in 1994, has pitched the reboot of his series to current rights-holder MGM.
      </p>
      <p>
       "We went to MGM...and proposed to them to do a sequel, but as a reboot" Emmerich told
       <a href="http://www.digitalspy.com.au/movies/news/a513056/stargate-to-receive-movie-reboot-trilogy-planned.html" rel="nofollow">
        Digital Spy
       </a>
       . "And reboot it as a movie and then do three parts. Pretty soon we'll have to look for a writer and start."
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center">
       <a href="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/wordpress/stg.ign.com/2013/09/Stargate.jpg" rel="nofollow">
        <div class="img-div img-div-center">
         <img alt="Stargate" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-808551" height="342" src="http://oyster.ignimgs.com/wordpress/stg.ign.com/2013/09/Stargate-610x342.jpg" width="610"/>
        </div>
        <p>
        </p>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       When asked by the website if Emmerich has plans to reunite original Stargate cast members Kurt Russell and James Spader, Emmerich was...well,
       <em>
        realistic
       </em>
       with his answer."The actors look totally different... it would not work," he said.
      </p>
      <p>
       Emmerich is currently working on another sequel to one of his big-name properties, Independence Day 2, which
       <a href="http://au.ign.com/articles/2013/09/06/will-smith-may-still-return-for-id4-sequel">
        might star Will Smith after all.
       </a>
      </p>
