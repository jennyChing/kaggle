     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <a href="http://www.jobsmaker.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/OPGC-Logo.jpg">
       <img alt="OPGC Executive Trainee Recruitment 2015 Notification" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-214" height="79" src="http://www.jobsmaker.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/OPGC-Logo.jpg" width="127"/>

      Dear Candidates,
      <a href="http://www.jobsmaker.in/">
       <strong>
        Jobsmaker.in
       </strong>
      </a>
      informs to all eligible person, who are searching for OPGC Executive Trainee Recruitment 2015 Notification for 31 Posts. So, all candidates, who are interested for government jobs, please apply to OPGC Opening Vacancies for Engineer through company official website
      <strong>
       www.opgc.co.in
      </strong>
      . Its a big chance for those people who are work with Odisha Power Generation Corporation and settle their life in government sector. OPGC recruitment through Gate 2015 Opening Total 31 vacancies for Executive Trainee jobs and applicants should have minimum educational qualification of B.E/ B.Tech in Civil, Industrial, Mechanical Engineering, ICE and other subjects from any authorized University/ Institute. For this post, applicants upper age should be 25 years as on 01-January-2015. OPGC informs to all applicants should be selected only those person are qualify of Gate 2015, skill test and last round personal interview.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      If you want to apply for OPGC jobs 2015, please click the below the given link and download application forms and fill up all mandatory details, before apply for Executive Trainee in opgc recruitment 2015, all details are mentioned below, please read carefully for OPGC recruitment notification 2015 in Odisha.
     </p>
     <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
      <span style="color: #800000;">
       <strong>
        OPGC Executive Trainee Recruitment 2015 Notification for 31 Posts Details
       </strong>
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Organization Name:
      </strong>
      Odisha Power Generation, Corporation Ltd.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Official Website:
      </strong>
      www.opgc.co.in
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Post Name:
      </strong>
      Executive Trainee
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Total Vacancies:
      </strong>
      31 Posts
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Job Location:
      </strong>
      Odisha, India
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Qualification:
      </strong>
      B.E/ B.Tech
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Age:
      </strong>
      25 years
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Salary:
      </strong>
      Not Declared
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Job Category:
      </strong>
      Government Jobs
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Educational Qualification Details:
      </strong>
      The Person should have completed B.E/ B.Tech in Civil, Industrial, ICE, Mechanical Engineering and other subjects from authorized Institute/ University.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Age Details:
      </strong>
      The Person age should have upper age limit as on 01-January-2015 is 25 years for all posts.
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Selection Process:
      </strong>
     </p>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       GATE 2015
      </li>
      <li>
       Skill Test
      </li>
      <li>
       Personal Interview
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       How To Apply:
      </strong>
      Jobsmaker.in inform to all eligible and interested person to apply for OPGC Jobs Notification for Executive Trainee through online mode before 31-Jan-2015. If you want to get more information about OPGC Opening Vacancies for engineers, please click below the given link.
     </p>
     <ul style="text-align: justify;">
      <li>
       Applicants first go to OPGC company official website opgc.co.in
      </li>
      <li>
       Then search online application form
      </li>
      <li>
       After that, fill-up all mandatory details and upload all necessary documents.
      </li>
      <li>
       Then after, click on submit button.
      </li>
      <li>
       Then save application registration number for future use.
      </li>
     </ul>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <span style="color: #ff0000;">
       <strong>
        Important Dates:
       </strong>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Starting Date of Application Forms:
      </strong>
      01-January-2015
     </p>
     <p style="text-align: justify;">
      <strong>
       Last Date of Application Forms:
      </strong>
      31-January-2015
