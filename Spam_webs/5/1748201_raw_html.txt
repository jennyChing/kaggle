         <strong>
          Big K.R.I.T.

        </a>
        is known to be humble. His Southern fried flavor bangs from coast to coast, but hekeeps it lowkey in Mississippi without lapping up any of the luxuries of a star.
       </p>
       <p>
        In the latest edition of A Day In The Hustle, we flew down to Atlanta kick it withK.R.I.T.as he got a hair cut, ate some lunch, and then hit the studio to finish off his upcoming album,
        <em>
         Cadillactica.
        </em>
        Watch the clip above.
       </p>
