        <div class="article-content clearfix">
         <p>
          In Brandons
          <a href="http://stereogum.com/299521/the-national-bell-house-brooklyn-31210/news/">
           beautiful review
          </a>
          of the Nationals excellent show at Bell House last month, he mentioned Runaway, a song that will be the slow jam of 2010. Were still waiting on its studio form, but until then there is Afraid Of Everyone, which is another
          <a href="http://stereogum.com/329242/the-national-afraid-of-everyone-conversation-16-live-video/news/">
           previously previewed in concert
          </a>
          track and follows 
          <a href="http://stereogum.com/314001/the-national-bloodbuzz-ohio/mp3s/">
           Bloodbuzz Ohio
          </a>
           as an official blog-baiting MP3 for cheapskates. Its ghostly and brassy, features Sufjan Stevens on background vocals, and builds patiently because it is the National, and its key refrain is evocative of the midlife issues Matt Berningers baritone chronicles so aptly: With my kids on my shoulders I try not to hurt anybody out loud / But I dont have the drugs to sort it out. Its more of a cultural crisis than a midlife one; were talking social disappointment, not agoraphobia. Download it.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://static.stereogum.com/uploads/mp3/The National - Afraid Of Everyone.mp3">
           The National  Afraid Of Everyone
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           <a href="http://highviolet.com/">
            High Violet
           </a>
          </em>
          is out 5/11 via 4AD.
         </p>
