         <p>
          Last night we saw the premiereof a brand new spin off series,
          <em>
           <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/better-call-saul" target="_blank" title="Better Call Saul">
            Better Call Saul
           </a>
           ,
          </em>
          which may sound familiar to all you
          <em>
           Breaking Bad
          </em>
          fans out there. As a big
          <em>
           Breaking Bad
          </em>
          fan myself, I was super excited to find out that they were going to attempt a spin off. Well, spin-off might not be the right word for it, considering
          <em>
           Better Call Saul
          </em>
          may end up being just as successful as
          <em>
           Breaking Bad
          </em>
          once was. Based on the numbers it pulled last night, I would think so!
          <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Better-Call-Saul-Premieres-With-Record-Breaking-Numbers.jpg">
           <img alt="Better Call Saul Premieres With Record Breaking Numbers" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-63913" height="333" src="http://gossipandgab.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Better-Call-Saul-Premieres-With-Record-Breaking-Numbers-500x333.jpg" width="500"/>
          </a>
          Last nights premiere of
          <i>
           Better Call Saul
          </i>
          brought in a record breaking debut rating in cable history with 6.9 million viewers! I think it is safe to say that many
          <em>
           Breaking Bad
          </em>
          fans were looking forward to the premiere of
          <em>
           Better Call Saul
          </em>
          and the return of everyones favorite attorney
          <strong>
           Saul Goodman,
          </strong>
          or should I say
          <strong>
           James McGill
          </strong>
          or even
          <strong>
           Gene,
          </strong>
          which is what his name tag in the beginning of the show said.
         </p>
         <p>
          Although it has been said that
          <strong>
           BryanCranston
          </strong>
          and
          <strong>
           Aaron Paul
          </strong>
          will not be joining the cast for the first season,
          <a href="http://gossipandgab.com/61153/sorry-breaking-bad-fans-better-call-saul-will-not-feature-walt-and-jesse-video" title="Sorry Breaking Bad Fans, Better Call Saul Will Not Feature Walt And Jesse (VIDEO)">
           as previously posted
          </a>
          , it has been said that it could happen in a future season.The writers wanted to make sure that the show could stand on its own without the help of Cranston and Paul, but they arent closing the door on the idea, not yet.
         </p>
         <p>
          I would personally like to see Cranston and Paul join the cast, even if it is just an episode here and there, but we will all just have to wait and see what the writers decide. Did you watch the
          <em>
           Better Call Saul
          </em>
          series premiere? I did and in the future if you happen to miss any episodes, you can always come back here and find a recap!
         </p>
