          Doug Eckert Selected to Join Board of Chess Club and Scholastic Center of Saint Louis
         </div>
         <div itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           <a href="http://bswllc.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Eckert_Doug.jpg">
            <img alt="Doug Eckert, CPA" class="alignright size-full wp-image-2049" height="181" src="http://bswllc.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/Eckert_Doug.jpg" width="129"/>
           </a>
           <a href="http://bswllc.com/about-us/our-team-v3/doug-eckert-cpa/">
            Doug Eckert, Member, Tax Services
           </a>
           ,has been selected to join the board of directors for the Chess Club and Scholastic Center of Saint Louis.
          </p>
          <p>
           Eckert is a former two-time U.S. Junior Open chess champion, as well as the author of
           <i>
            Sicilian Scheveningen: Keres Attack
           </i>
           .As the practice leader for the International Tax Services practice at Brown Smith Wallace, Eckert travels the world extensively and says he enjoys the analytical parallel that exists between his favorite sport and his career.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.saintlouischessclub.org/news/2014-02-20/eckert-joins-ccscsl-board-directors" target="_blank">
            Click to read more.
           </a>
          </p>
