        <p>
         Here you can see the Red Box on the dock at Pussers Marina Cay, this idyllic little island in the beautiful British Virgin Islands.
        </p>
        <p>
         A high-resolution video camera is installed on the wall of the fuel dock facing the Red Box, a British antique with an operating telephone.
        </p>
        <p>
         To have your picture taken, just stand in front of the door of the Red Box that faces the Fuel Hut. The camera takes a new photo every 15 seconds that is posted to the Red Box in the Find Your Picture section.
        </p>
        <p>
         Its fun to make a call from the Red Box and tell a friend to view you on their computer at the Red Box  Current View, where they can take a photo of you by clicking the button shown on the side on the page.
        </p>
