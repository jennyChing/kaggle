           <h1>
            Toolkit for Activists
           </h1>
           <p>
            You can choose your level of activity: from sending a
            <a href="http://capwiz.com/fclca/issues/?style=D">
             pre-written message to your legislator
            </a>
            through our online Action Center to
            <a href="/images/stories/pdfs/lobbyingbeginsathome.pdf" target="_blank">
             organizing a delegation
            </a>
            to meet with your legislator in your district or at the Capitol.
           </p>
           <h2>
            Step One:Connect.
           </h2>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <a href="http://capwiz.com/fclca/mlm/signup">
              Join        the Action Network
             </a>
            </li>
            <li>
             
             <a href="http://www.facebook.com/fclca.org" target="_blank">
              Like        Us on Facebook.
             </a>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <h2>
            Step Two: Learn more about your legislators.
           </h2>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <a href="http://www.capwiz.com/fclca/directory/statedir.tt?state=CA&amp;lvl=state">
              Look them up here.
             </a>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <h2>
            Step Three:
            <a href="http://www.capwiz.com/fclca/home/">
             See       the issues were working on and take action
            </a>
            .
           </h2>
           <h2>
            Step Four: Write a letter to the editor
           </h2>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <a href="/images/stories/pdfs/websitetipsletterstotheeditor.pdf" target="_blank">
              Get tips on what        gets published.
             </a>
            </li>
            <li>
             <a href="http://www.capwiz.com/fclca/dbq/media/?command=state_search&amp;state=ca">
              Find a publication and send your letter
             </a>
             .
            </li>
           </ul>
           <h2>
            Step Five: Organize a visit to your legislators       office.
           </h2>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <a href="/images/stories/pdfs/lobbyingbeginsathome.pdf" target="_blank">
              Read tips on        organizing a delegation to lobby.
             </a>
            </li>
            <li>
             <a href="mailto:fcladmin@fclca.org">
              Contact us        to find out more.
             </a>
            </li>
           </ul>
           <h2>
            Step Six :
            <a href="/component/content/?id=76&amp;Itemid=166">
             Learn       more about our coalitions and allies.
            </a>
           </h2>
           <ul>
            <li>
             <a href="/component/content/?id=85&amp;Itemid=175">
              Learn more about       Californias government, budget and issues.
             </a>
            </li>
           </ul>
