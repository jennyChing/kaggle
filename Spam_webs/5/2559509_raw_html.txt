            <span class="print-link">
            </span>
            <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden view-mode-full">
             <div class="field-items">
              <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
               <p>
                <strong>
                 1.
                </strong>
                From the very moment she announces her pregnancy, shell be the center of attention  not you. Get used to it.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 2.
                </strong>
                When the baby comes, theyll both be the center of attention  not you. Arent you glad you had nine months to practice going unnoticed?
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 3.
                </strong>
                Your house is too small, it was always too small, and to suggest otherwise simply proves that your brain is too small.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 4.
                </strong>
                Are you about to make your mom and dad grandparents for the first time? Get ready for some ambivalence. Theres no such thing as a young grandparent; give them some time to deal with the shock.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 5.
                </strong>
                She will want to use a birthing center. She will want a midwife. She will want a doctor. She will not want an epidural. She will scream for an epidural. Cesareans will sound great; they will sound awful. Agree with her always.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 6.
                </strong>
                Lamaze is to childbirth what yoga is to football. Sort of. Just do it.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 7.
                </strong>
                Her sense of smell will be so acute, youll be tempted to airlift her to join a search-and-rescue team.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 8.
                </strong>
                Youre not really the coach. Theyll tell you that you are, but there will come a time when its time to shut your mouth and let her finish out the last two minutes of the game. Then youll step in and cut the net.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 9.
                </strong>
                You will be short on cash. You will not buy clothes for yourself for a year. You will consider canceling cable. You will never own a flat-screen TV. But there will always be money for a crib, three car seats, two strollers and more plastic things in Day-Glo colors than you can throw a rattle at.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 10.
                </strong>
                Buy new tires now.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 11.
                </strong>
                During the first week home from the hospital, you will learn to love lasagna.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 12.
                </strong>
                Yes, youre holding the baby wrong. Do it her way.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 13.
                </strong>
                By the time you change your third diaper, it will seem like the most normal thing in the world.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 14.
                </strong>
                You wont faint. No one does.
               </p>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 15.
                </strong>
                Be careful about the word we. For instance, never say, We didnt mind amniocentesis at all.
               </p>
