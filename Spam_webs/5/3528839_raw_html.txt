           <h1 class="post-title">
            Designing a Modern Fast Food Restaurant
           </h1>
           <p class="post-meta">
            Posted by
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/author/nanettew/" rel="author" title="Posts by Nanette Wong">
             Nanette Wong
            </a>
            on 02.23.15 in
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/category/interior-design/" rel="category tag">
             Interior Design
            </a>
            ,
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/category/main/" rel="category tag">
             Main
            </a>
           </p>
          </header>
          <div class="post-content">
           <div class="row featured-image">
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/designing-modern-fast-food-restaurant/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-7/" target="_self">
             <img alt="Designing a Modern Fast Food Restaurant" src="http://0.design-milk.com/images/2015/02/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-7.jpg"/>
            </a>
            <a class="contrast-bar gallery-button" href="http://design-milk.com/designing-modern-fast-food-restaurant/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-7/" target="_self">
             <i class="icon ss-icon">
              forward
             </i>
             View The Photo Gallery
            </a>
           </div>
           <p>
            <a href="http://brandon.su/" target="_blank">
             Brandon Agency
            </a>
            of Ukraine was posed with an interesting challenge: Design an innovative fast food restaurant,complete from the logo to its corporate identity to itsinterior design.
           </p>
           <p>
            Focused onsimple and original food from local, seasonal ingredients,the name 
            <a href="https://www.behance.net/gallery/22043577/Simple-" target="_blank">
             simple
            </a>
            . was chosen for the restaurant. Located in central Kiev, they kept to using organic materials (wood, kraft paper, plywood) and natural colors to exemplify the theme.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/designing-fast-food-restaurant-future/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-4/" rel="attachment wp-att-216089">
             <img alt="brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-4" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-216089" height="400" src="http://2.design-milk.com/images/2015/02/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-4-600x400.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/designing-fast-food-restaurant-future/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-6/" rel="attachment wp-att-216091">
             <img alt="brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-6" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-216091" height="400" src="http://1.design-milk.com/images/2015/02/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-6-600x400.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/designing-fast-food-restaurant-future/simple_md_15/" rel="attachment wp-att-216102">
             <img alt="simple_MD_15" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-216102" height="944" src="http://0.design-milk.com/images/2015/02/simple_MD_15-600x944.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            It extends to the small details as well, like using rolling pins as menus and rakes as coat racks.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/designing-fast-food-restaurant-future/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-11/" rel="attachment wp-att-216096">
             <img alt="brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-11" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-216096" height="391" src="http://2.design-milk.com/images/2015/02/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-11-600x391.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            The simple continues in the strict design standards for the icons, illustrations, and overall posters of the space. They even developed game sets, stickers, and multiple menus specifically for simple.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/designing-fast-food-restaurant-future/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-8/" rel="attachment wp-att-216093">
             <img alt="brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-8" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-216093" height="400" src="http://1.design-milk.com/images/2015/02/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-8-600x400.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/designing-fast-food-restaurant-future/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-9/" rel="attachment wp-att-216094">
             <img alt="brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-9" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-216094" height="413" src="http://1.design-milk.com/images/2015/02/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-9-600x413.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/designing-fast-food-restaurant-future/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-12/" rel="attachment wp-att-216097">
             <img alt="brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-12" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-216097" height="463" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2015/02/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-12-600x463.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/designing-fast-food-restaurant-future/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-10/" rel="attachment wp-att-216095">
             <img alt="brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-10" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-216095" height="400" src="http://1.design-milk.com/images/2015/02/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-10-600x400.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://design-milk.com/designing-fast-food-restaurant-future/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-16/" rel="attachment wp-att-216101">
             <img alt="brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-16" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-216101" height="425" src="http://3.design-milk.com/images/2015/02/brandon-agency-simple-restaurant-16-600x425.jpg" width="600"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Photos by Andrey Shalimov and Yana Korobenko.
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>
             <strong>
              Editors Note:
             </strong>
             Some of the photos from this post were removed because it had been discovered that a copy of
             <a href="http://www.mattiazzi.eu/products/radice/" target="_blank">
              Mattiazzis Radice chair
             </a>
             was used in the interior design of this space. As media partners and members of
             <a href="http://www.beoriginalamericas.com/" target="_blank">
              Be Original Americas
             </a>
             , we do not condone the use of reproduction or copies and always encourage designers to use original and authentic pieces in their work.
            </em>
           </p>
