&lt;h2&gt;Booties 3 ways&lt;/h2&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Surely by now there&#8217;s a hot pair of booties you&#8217;re coveting; I can&#8217;t tell you how many booties are on my wish list this Fall, it&#8217;s a little embarrassing. And the everlasting question with trends of any kind, like ankle booties, is how on earth to wear them without looking ridiculous, so I present to you &lt;em&gt;3 ways to wear booties&lt;/em&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h1&gt;&lt;/h1&gt;
&lt;h1&gt;1 // skinnies&lt;/h1&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Wear a pair of stunning black bootie heels with some gorgeous leather pants, a slightly oversize long blouse and a tank, a gorgeous pendant or statement necklace, a big watch, and a stack of bangle bracelets. Perfect for the chilly days to come this Fall.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;a href="http://www.polyvore.com/pencil_skirt/set?.embedder=1388469&amp;.svc=copypaste&amp;id=62144087" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img class="aligncenter pin-it" title="pencil skirt" src="http://cfc.polyvoreimg.com/cgi/img-set/.sig/yqZpuorp5vXmjmv7O5sDA/cid/62144087/id/DSfB4RBIRW6tX8vqLhgV7g/size/c700x531.jpg" alt="pencil skirt" width="700" height="531" border="0" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h1&gt;2 // pencil skirt&lt;/h1&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Dress up a little for the office in some equestrian themed awesome, unless your office is actually boots on the ground with horses, in which case maybe not so much. Otherwise grab a fun patterned pencil skirt, a patterned blouse (but it MUST be in the same color scheme), some platform booties, a horse ring (a horse is a horse, of course!), a black leather envelope clutch, and another classic bold statement ring.&lt;em&gt; Boom.&lt;/em&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div style="width: 700px; margin: 0 auto;"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="width: 700px; margin: 0 auto;"&gt;
&lt;div style="position: relative;"&gt;&lt;a href="http://www.polyvore.com/mini_dress/set?.embedder=1388469&amp;.svc=copypaste&amp;id=62137832" target="_blank"&gt;&lt;img title="mini dress" src="http://cfc.polyvoreimg.com/cgi/img-set/.sig/bm0UWCYJ6oy7sGoTksgg/cid/62137832/id/L9bB3VvDR9S7E5jdQzt_Kg/size/c700x530.jpg" alt="mini dress" width="700" height="530" border="0" class=" pin-it" /&gt;&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h1&gt;3 // mini dress&lt;/h1&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;I can&#8217;t think of a single reason not to wear a mini dress with tights, can you? The outfit is all put together, all you need to add is a handful of gorgeous accessories (no necklace on this, too many competing patterns), some yummy opaque tights, and a pair of to-die-for booties (ones with tassels won&#8217;t hurt anyone). I wish I were wearing this entire outfit right now, head to toe. Wear it out to lunch with your girls, date night, or for that giant presentation in front of the boss/whole class next week.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align: left;"&gt;&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align: left;"&gt;What do you think of booties, are you all in? I can&#8217;t think of a better shoe to keep your closet current and your brain happy. So there you have it, 3 ways to wear booties this Fall.&lt;/div&gt;
