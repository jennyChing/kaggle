            In honor of Geek Appreciation Month, we here at Geeks of Doom would like to direct your attention to a really cool video compiled by the good folks over at
            <a href="http://elder-geek.com/2010/08/the-insane-console-history-video-20/" target="_blank">
             Elder Geek
            </a>
            which shows every video game console ever made!
           </p>
           <p>
            You can watch it below.
           </p>
           <p>
            This video is a great piece that has not only pictures of every console, but video game music as well from music from 8-bit games all the way up to todays video game music.
           </p>
           <p>
            I have to tell you I fell in love with this video, because it is such a great tribute to all the great and not so great consoles of the past and current day. I hope you enjoy this video, which runs about 23 minutes.  I saw the first console I ever owned in this video, it was the SEGA Genesis.
