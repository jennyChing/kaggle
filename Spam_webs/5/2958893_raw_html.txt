                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Rainbow Discovery Bottles
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-4621390020324745061" itemprop="articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-iST5e5rWiPo/VVOgjQxzIzI/AAAAAAAAIaE/Hn7re2S5k88/s1600/0%2BWater%2BBead%2BPin.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="">
                       <img alt="Play to Learn Preschool" border="0" height="278" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-iST5e5rWiPo/VVOgjQxzIzI/AAAAAAAAIaE/Hn7re2S5k88/s280/0%2BWater%2BBead%2BPin.jpg" title="Rainbow Discovery Bottles - Light Table Play" width="280"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     Last week we made
                     <a href="http://www.playtolearnpreschool.us/2015/04/weighted-sensory-bottles.html" target="_blank">
                      weighted discovery bottles
                     </a>
                     which are great "heavy work" for our very active kids. They loved them. So this week, we decided to try another
                     <a href="http://www.preschoolinspirations.com/2015/04/27/magic-two-ingredient-sensory-bottle/" target="_blank">
                      idea from Preschool Inspirations
                     </a>
                     .
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-sst-oYpWnZM/VVOgs9LDN8I/AAAAAAAAIbM/uLGhw8X61r4/s1600/IMG_5358.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="">
                       <img border="0" height="186" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-sst-oYpWnZM/VVOgs9LDN8I/AAAAAAAAIbM/uLGhw8X61r4/s280/IMG_5358.JPG" width="280"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     We made "
                     <a href="http://www.preschoolinspirations.com/2015/04/27/magic-two-ingredient-sensory-bottle/" target="_blank">
                      <b>
                       Magic Two Ingredient
                      </b>
                     </a>
                     " bottles with items from the Dollar Store.
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-yrSi70x8vTE/VVOgkuqB0jI/AAAAAAAAIaM/MZhsOlqXuB8/s1600/IMG_0221.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="">
                       <img border="0" height="186" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-yrSi70x8vTE/VVOgkuqB0jI/AAAAAAAAIaM/MZhsOlqXuB8/s280/IMG_0221.JPG" width="280"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     We followed
                     <a href="http://www.preschoolinspirations.com/2015/04/27/magic-two-ingredient-sensory-bottle/" target="_blank">
                      <b>
                       these directions
                      </b>
                      from Preschool Inspirations
                     </a>
                     and it was so, so, super easy!
                     <br/>
                     <div style="text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-fRQgWmUuX64/VVOgis_4L2I/AAAAAAAAIZ4/abPu7GWQyyM/s1600/IMG_0211.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="">
                       <img border="0" height="400" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-fRQgWmUuX64/VVOgis_4L2I/AAAAAAAAIZ4/abPu7GWQyyM/s400/IMG_0211.JPG" width="266"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     At first, they were in the science center, but one of our kiddos wanted to play with them by the window. Of course, as soon as he did that, a bunch of kids exclaimed, "Go put them on the light table!"
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-9Vv9tWRPTeg/VVOgj4DEXWI/AAAAAAAAIaI/LEM6nZAeJ_E/s1600/IMG_5280.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="">
                       <img border="0" height="201" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-9Vv9tWRPTeg/VVOgj4DEXWI/AAAAAAAAIaI/LEM6nZAeJ_E/s280/IMG_5280.JPG" width="280"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      Aren't they gorgeous? The consistency appears to change when we tip them right side up and sideways.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-6C9npmX9Jak/VVOgo--TK7I/AAAAAAAAIbA/nyu7OV5CTvQ/s1600/IMG_5289.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="">
                       <img border="0" height="186" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-6C9npmX9Jak/VVOgo--TK7I/AAAAAAAAIbA/nyu7OV5CTvQ/s280/IMG_5289.JPG" width="280"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      We rolled them around and shook them up.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-S6O-SI38Mmo/VVOglYEwCyI/AAAAAAAAIac/0IBJ2iFIhfk/s1600/IMG_5301.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="">
                       <img border="0" height="185" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-S6O-SI38Mmo/VVOglYEwCyI/AAAAAAAAIac/0IBJ2iFIhfk/s280/IMG_5301.JPG" width="280"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     ... and clanked them together.
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-PM0GSm5IyJs/VVOglIIhTeI/AAAAAAAAIaY/CUUlbNf87Jg/s1600/IMG_5295.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="">
                       <img border="0" height="386" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-PM0GSm5IyJs/VVOglIIhTeI/AAAAAAAAIaY/CUUlbNf87Jg/s280/IMG_5295.JPG" width="280"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     ...and lined them all up in rainbow colors. Can you tell that I've had these amazing kiddos for 3 years? :)
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/--pDZwpqah68/VVOgm-pTsOI/AAAAAAAAIa0/v3X4UjV4in0/s1600/IMG_5333.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="">
                       <img border="0" height="533" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/--pDZwpqah68/VVOgm-pTsOI/AAAAAAAAIa0/v3X4UjV4in0/s280/IMG_5333.JPG" width="280"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     We even tried to build a tower with them.
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-mfozE_-fv34/VVOmAJShxkI/AAAAAAAAIbk/fwv6HQPdzZk/s1600/IMG_5352.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="">
                       <img border="0" height="420" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-mfozE_-fv34/VVOmAJShxkI/AAAAAAAAIbk/fwv6HQPdzZk/s280/IMG_5352.JPG" width="280"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both;">
                      The bottles are transparent enough that when two are placed together, they make a new color.
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-kPRiUnL4n0E/VVOg2DW5wnI/AAAAAAAAIbU/9m0mXTLpI-A/s1600/IMG_5351.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="">
                       <img border="0" height="186" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-kPRiUnL4n0E/VVOg2DW5wnI/AAAAAAAAIbU/9m0mXTLpI-A/s280/IMG_5351.JPG" width="280"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     Discovery bottles are so visually appealing. They are perfect for kids who need a calming sensory experience.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     Have fun playing and learning with your children today!
