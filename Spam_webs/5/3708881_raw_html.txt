       <h1 class="section_heading">
        How to Grow Hair Longer and Thicker at Home
        <span class="title_bold">
        </span>
       </h1>
      </div>
      <div class="clear">
      </div>
      <div class="col-sm-4">

     <img alt="black hair woman" class="alignleft img-responsive wp-post-image" height="150" src="http://www.hairtransplantdubai.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/black-hair-woman11-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
     <p>
      Have you seen Rapunzel and how she has long and strong hair? At times hair take longer to grow. There are quite a lot of things that you can do in order to make your hair grow faster. You can use different oils and solutions for better results. It takes time and dedication to grow your hair longer and thicker, but sky is the limit.
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <h2>
      Braid Your Hair
     </h2>
     <p>
      If you have hair that at least fall up to your shoulders, braid them on a daily basis. It is considered to be the best way to make your hair longer. You can prevent split ends and breakages as well. It directly affects the length of your hair.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://hairtransplantdubai.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Braid-Your-Hair11.jpg">
       <img alt="Braid Your Hair" class="aligncenter wp-image-1321" height="635" src="http://hairtransplantdubai.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Braid-Your-Hair11.jpg" width="500"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <h2>
      <span style="color: #000000;">
       Keep a Keen Eye on the Ends
      </span>
     </h2>
     <p>
      You need to keep a close eye on the ends of your hair. If they are experiencing problems, cut them or get a trim. This will help in hair growth. Hair with split ends will always look shorter than usual.
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://hairtransplantdubai.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Hair-Ends11.jpg">
       <img alt="Hair Ends" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1324" height="224" src="http://hairtransplantdubai.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Hair-Ends11-300x224.jpg" width="300"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <h2>
      Massaging Your Scalp
     </h2>
     <p>
      The myths about hair massages helping in hair growth are actually true. You need to know the right type of oil that should be used on your scalp. Massaging improves the blood flow on your scalp and that further helps in hair growth and other important things that happens on your hair follicles. With hair follicles receiving more nutrients from the blood stream, there is a high possibility that you will see stronger hair.
     </p>
