       <h1 class="entry-title">
        12 Insane Animal Stories That Are Completely True
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2011-03-14T20:31:44+00:00">
         March 14, 2011
        </span>
        by
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://www.oddballdaily.com/author/esteban/" rel="author">
           Esteban
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <style type="text/css">
         &lt;!-- .list { background:url("images/navbar.gif") repeat scroll 0 0 #0f0f0e; color:#ff8902; text-shadow: #000000 1px 1px; display:block; font-size:1.3em; font-weight:bold; margin:20px 0 10px; padding:5px; width:600px; } --&gt;
        </style>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.oddballdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/elephant-dog1.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-608" height="413" src="http://www.oddballdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/elephant-dog1.jpg" title="elephant-dog" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Nature: when its not trying to kill us with tornadoes, fires, mudslides, hurricanes, floods, and earthquakes, it can really captivate us with its crazy animals. Below are 12 insane stories about natures creatures that are totally, 100% true. So sit back and enjoyjust dont take your guard down, because thats precisely when nature likes to strike.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          12. Adventures of the Honey Badger
         </span>
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="405" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/c81bcjyfn6U" title="YouTube video player" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         The supreme authority on awesomeness,
         <i>
          The Guinness Book of World Records
         </i>
         , has declared the
         <i>
          Mellivora capensis
         </i>
         , or Honey Badger, the most fearless animal in the world. These little creatures eat birds, bugs, rodents, and even large venomous snakes. Oh, and in India there have been reports of Honey Badgersthey sound so cute and cuddlydigging up and feeding on human corpses. Amazing, no?
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          11. Duck Gives Doberman Piece of Its Mind
         </span>
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="405" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/BHRUvr6K2Rs" title="YouTube video player" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         Check out this pissed-off duck. It may challenge that Honey Badger for bravery (or stupidity) in the next edition of the
         <i>
          Guinness Book of World Records
         </i>
         . Its lucky the dog didnt just lay into it like a dollar-store squeak toy.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          10. Bear Locks Itself In Car, Destroys It
         </span>
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="443" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/VcqQ1Eo2RaI?rel=0" title="YouTube video player" width="550">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         Sometimes a bear just needs a sandwich. This one followed his nose into an unlocked car and, unable to escape, tore the car to bits. In the process, he (or she) shifted the car into neutral and rolled it into a ditch. Good luck with the insurance claim, folks.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          9. Kangaroo Dogs
         </span>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.oddballdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/kangaroo_dogs_philippines.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-600" height="403" src="http://www.oddballdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/kangaroo_dogs_philippines-e1300068037876.jpg" title="kangaroo_dogs_philippines" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         These pooches from Quezon City, Philippines are named Cute and Bambi, and both were born without their front pair of legs. Not knowing any better, they just went about their lives, hopping around like kangaroos. People have offered to buy them off their owner, a local street vendor, but he insists theyre not for sale.
        </p>
        <p>
         Source: http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/unleashed/2009/06/dogkangaroos-turn-heads-in-the-philippines.html
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          8. Whale Jumps Up and Smashed Boat
         </span>
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="339" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/pkTicMky9ho?rel=0" title="YouTube video player" width="550">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         Honestly, its hard to feel too sorry for middle-aged people who have the time and money to take sailing lessons. But, still, this had to be pretty traumatic.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          7. Horse Allergic to Grass
         </span>
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="405" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/BFH8iYYZcOQ" title="YouTube video player" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         If horses had Facebook accounts, every single one of them would list eating grass as their favorite hobby. So, if you think you have crap luck, just be glad youre not this horse.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          6. Karate Chimp
         </span>
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="405" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/bMCsZdIq3Uo" title="YouTube video player" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         I have nothing against Jaden Smith. Hes a cute kid. But wouldnt you have rather seen The Karate Kid remade starring Jackie Chan and this Chimpanzee?
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          5. Monster Bunnies to Feed North Korea
         </span>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.oddballdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/giant_bunny_food_for_north_korea.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-599" height="420" src="http://www.oddballdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/giant_bunny_food_for_north_korea-e1300067878505.jpg" title="giant_bunny_food_for_north_korea" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         So heres the story: some important North Korean official read about how this guy in Germany was breeding giant rabbits, and he thought, this would be a perfect way to solve our perpetual food shortage! Praise to the Leader!
        </p>
        <p>
         Now, okay, look. Im not against eating fluffy bunny rabbits. Im not crazy about it, but Im not against it. However, it is a scientific fact that bunny cuteness increases exponentially with size (C = M3/). So, really, is there
         <i>
          no other animal
         </i>
         North Korea could gigantify and mass-produce to feed its people? This is why no one likes you, Kim Jung Il.
        </p>
        <p>
         Source: http://www.spiegel.de/international/0,1518,458863,00.html
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          4. Woman Smuggles Monkey Under Shirt
         </span>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.oddballdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/Rhesus_Macaques_monkey_smuggled_from_thailand-e1300067728763.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-598" height="412" src="http://www.oddballdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/Rhesus_Macaques_monkey_smuggled_from_thailand-e1300067728763.jpg" title="Rhesus_Macaques_monkey_smuggled_from_thailand" width="550"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Gypsy Lawson and her mom, Fran Ogren, decided they wanted a kick-ass souvenir from their Thai vacation. So they drugged a rhesus macaque monkey, and Gypsy put it under her shirt pretending to be pregnant. They would have gotten away with it if they, you know, were not TRYING TO SMUGGLE A MONKEY FROM THAILAND UNDER A SHIRT.
        </p>
        <p>
         Source: http://edition.cnn.com/2008/CRIME/12/09/monkey.smuggling/
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          3. Elephant + Dog = BFF
         </span>
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="405" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/cBtFTF2ii7U" title="YouTube video player" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         Elephants are biologically driven to find life-long partners and, apparently, are not too choosy when pickings are slim. These two met at an elephant retirement community, hit it off and became inseparable. Enjoy the hilarious puns in the newsmagazine piece from CBS.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          2. Cat Grows a Pair of Wings
         </span>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.oddballdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/cat_with_wings-e1300067548693.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-597" height="349" src="http://www.oddballdaily.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/cat_with_wings-e1300067548693.jpg" title="cat_with_wings" width="549"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         What are they feeding cats in China? This one from Chongquing was fine until it was about one year old. Then is grew a pair of wings. Scientists are divided regarding the cause: some say genetic mutation, others say Siamese twin growing inside the little kitty. Cute!
        </p>
        <p>
         Source: http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/worldnews/article-1188875/Dont-flap-just-cat-furry-wings.html
        </p>
        <p>
         <span class="list">
          1. Alcoholic Monkeys
         </span>
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="311" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/pSm7BcQHWXk" title="YouTube video player" width="500">
         </iframe>
        </p>
        <p>
         If you take a vacation on the Caribbean island of St. Kitts, keep an eye on your drink or and alcoholic vervet monkey might steal it. Really.
        </p>
