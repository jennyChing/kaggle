        <div class="notes markdown core_item_content__text_block">
         <p>
          Inspired by and adapted from the
          <a href="http://www.ravelry.com/patterns/library/bobbling-along-aran-tote">
           Bobbling Along Aran Tote
          </a>
          by Celeste Young, with kind permission of the author.
         </p>
         <p>
          This pattern creates a very thick, cushiony fabric to cover your laptop; the bobbles, popcorns, and double-thick cables act just like bubble wrap to absorb minor bumps and shocks.
         </p>
         <p>
          I chose an acrylic yarn because its durable and washable; a cotton or wool-acrylic blend yarn also would work well.
         </p>
         <p>
          While I chose not to, you could add a synthetic oilcloth liner to make the sleeve water-resistant.
         </p>
         <p>
          The fantastic buttons are handmade from oak branches by Jay Beesmer at
          <a href="http://www.etsy.com/shop/WoodenTreasures">
           Wooden Treasures
          </a>
          .
         </p>
