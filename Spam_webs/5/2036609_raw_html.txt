       <h1 class="entry-title">
        Burn Fat in a Faster and Efficient Manner with Garcinia Camboginia
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p>
         <b>
          <a href="https://nexgenbiolabs.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Garcinia-Cambogia-weight-loss-1.jpg">
           <img alt="Garcinia-Cambogia-weight-loss-1" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-260 colorbox-259" height="190" src="https://nexgenbiolabs.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Garcinia-Cambogia-weight-loss-1.jpg" width="266"/>
          </a>
          Garcinia Cambogia Extract
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         The pumpkin-shaped, small fruit,
         <a href="https://nexgenbiolabs.com/product_info.php/slenderfruit-p-34" title="Garcinia Cambogia">
          Garcinia Cambogia
         </a>
         is also known by the name of tamarind. Even though, it is native to Indonesia but, the fruit is also found in Southeast Asia, India as well as Central and West Africa. It has been part of South Asian dishes like chutneys and curries for quite a long period of time. Meals tend to be more filling when this ingredient is added. Few Malaysian villages use this fruit for making soup which is consumed before meals, as it helps with weight loss through its appetite blocking abilities. The extract from the fruit is known as hydroxycitric acid and considered to be amongst the least priced herbal supplements available in the market. Emotional eaters can also benefit from this fruit as it helps to surge the serotonin levels within the body that assists with sleep patterns and improved mood.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          How It Works?
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         HCA extract available from
         <a href="http://www.doctoroz.com/videos/garcinia-cambogia-hca" rel="external nofollow">
          Gracinia Camboginia
         </a>
         supports weight loss in two ways: by
         <a href="https://nexgenbiolabs.com/product_info.php/phenrx-p-35" title="Appetite suppressant ">
          suppressing the appetite
         </a>
         and blocking fat accumulation. The primary purpose of HCA is to block the citrate lyase enzyme which assists with the production of fat from carbohydrates within the body. The blocking of the enzyme by HCA leads to a halt in the fat-making process which leads to decreased production of triglycerides and bad cholesterol. The appetite levels are also suppressed by HCA from Garcinia Camboginia through increased production of serotonin. When serotonin levels decease, people feel anxious and depressed which can lead to reactive or emotional eating. When mood is improved, reactive or emotional eating reduce and the body releases more fat from the fat cells.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          How safe is Garcinia Camboginia?
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         Consumption of Garcinia Camboginia has been continuing for years without any evidence of side-effects. In normal amounts, the HCA extract is absolutely safe and do not cause any type of adverse effects. Women who are breastfeeding or pregnant are always advised to take consultation from the doctor before consuming the extract. Apart from that, diabetic people should also take advice from the doctor regarding consumption of the extract as it can drop blood sugar levels of the body below normal. People on medications like statin need to use GCA with extreme caution as it can cause muscle degradation or rhabdomyolysis. The extract can worsen the risks of dementia which is why people affected with such disorders need to consult with their doctors first.
        </p>
        <p>
         <b>
          Dosage of Garcinia Camboginia
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Garcinia Camboginia extract is available online as well as different health food stores. It is always recommended to purchase GCA supplements that have a minimum of fifty percent HCA concentration. A recommended dose of 500mg to 1000mg is advised before every meal and the total consumption for the day should not exceed 3000mg. While buying the supplement, it is important to make sure that it is free from artificial ingredients or fillers. In order to achieve fast results, users should combine the supplement with a balanced diet and proper workouts throughout the work. It is possible to lose around two to four pounds within a month by following a healthy weight loss routine along with proper doses of the extract.
        </p>
