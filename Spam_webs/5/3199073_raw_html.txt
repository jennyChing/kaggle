         Methi Pulao is good energy and salty taste able recipe. Everyone like this white dish and mostly use in iftar time. People can also try in home with simple method
        <strong>
         Iftar | Methi Pulao Urdu Recipes

       </p>
       <h2 style="text-align: center;">
        Iftar | Methi Pulao Urdu Recipes
       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.urdu-recipes.com/iftar-methi-pulao-urdu-recipes.html/iftar-methi-pulao-urdu-recipes-3">
         <img alt="Iftar | Methi Pulao Urdu Recipes " class="aligncenter wp-image-791 size-large" height="587" src="http://www.urdu-recipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/iftar-methi-pulao-urdu-recipes-440x587.jpg" title="iftar-methi-pulao-urdu-recipes" width="440"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <h3 style="text-align: center;">
        Iftar | Methi Pulao English Recipes
       </h3>
       <p>
        Methi pulao is a healthy and delicious rice dish that is very easy to make. I often make this versatile recipe for my kids as it fenugreek provides a number of healthy nutrients. If you also want to have delicious taste accompanied by vital nutrients, then must try this healthy recipe.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Prep time:
        </strong>
        15 min
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Cook time:
        </strong>
        45 min
        <br/>
        <strong>
         Serves:
        </strong>
        4
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Methi Pulao Recipe: Required ingredients
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        1/2 kg rice
        <br/>
        2 onions
        <br/>
        1 tsp red chili powder
        <br/>
        1/2 tsp turmeric powder
        <br/>
        Salt to taste
        <br/>
        2 tomatoes (thinly chopped)
        <br/>
        8 green chilies (cut into thin and long slices)
        <br/>
        2 cups methi (fenugreek)
        <br/>
        1 tbsp. thinly chopped coriander leaves
        <br/>
        1 tbsp. mustard seeds
        <br/>
        Water (as needed)
        <br/>
        2 tbsp. butter
        <br/>
        2 tbsp. cooking oil
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Methi Pulao Recipe: Cooking instructions
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Clean and wash rice thoroughly and submerge in fresh water for about 15 minutes.
        <br/>
        Take cooking oil in a pan and heat it over medium heat.
        <br/>
        Add mustard seeds in hot oil and fry for one minute. Then fry chopped onion till it turns golden brown.
        <br/>
        Add green chilies, turmeric powder and red chili powder and fry for two minutes.
        <br/>
        After that, add chopped tomatoes and cook well.
        <br/>
        Remove the water from rice and add it into the spice. Fry for one minute and then add methi, salt and sufficient amount of water.
        <br/>
        Cover the pan with its lid and let the rice cook over high flame till all the water dries completely.
        <br/>
        Steam the methi pulao for few minutes and then remove it from stove.
        <br/>
        Delicious methi pulao is ready now. Transfer it in serving dish and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
        <br/>
        Serve hot with pickle and yogurt chatni.
       </p>
