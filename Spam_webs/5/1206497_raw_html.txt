         The Diamond Tools which is used for cutting and grinding process on different hard materials and metals. Diamond tool is a tool in which the diamond crystals or particles or powers are held on the front part of the tools through which the action will be done.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         As knowing, Diamond is the hardest metal in the world. That is why it is very easier and efficient to cut or grind any other harder metal with the help of it. Diamond tools can also be used to give the proper shape to any metal like glass.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Diamond tools can work with the high grinding efficiency but low forces. Because of this it can also reduce the time and effort of the human efforts. It can also develop the employment environment.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         As discuss above, in diamond tools the diamond is available in the form of crystals, particles, blades or powders. So the manufacturing method is differing as per its application.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Diamond tools are categorized by its manufacturing method and its application like metal bonded, resin bonded, polycrystalline, ceramic bonded etc.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Application of Diamond Tools:
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Construction, woodworking, stone processing, auto spare parts processing, engineering ceramic processing, mechanical tools processing etc.
