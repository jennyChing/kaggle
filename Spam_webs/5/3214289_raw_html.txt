            <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
             Eating tips for a healthier lifestyle
            </h1>
           </header>
           <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
            <!--Ad Injection:top-->
            <div style="float:left;margin-right:5px;">
             <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
             </script>
             <!-- fitness top -->
             <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-1734555513950336" data-ad-slot="1467728159" style="display:inline-block;width:300px;height:250px">
             </ins>
             <script>
              (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
             </script>
            </div>
            <p>
             Here is a list of great healthy lifestyle tips. 25 eating tips to a healthier lifestyle
             <a href="http://www.reneegetsfit.com/">
              <br/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <h2>
             Healthy Lifestyle Tips
            </h2>
            <div class="wp-caption alignright" id="attachment_3290" style="width: 260px">
             <img alt="Healthy lifestyle Tips" class="size-full wp-image-3290 " height="232" src="http://www.fitnesstipsforlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/healthy.jpg" title="Healthy lifestyle Tips" width="250"/>
             <p class="wp-caption-text">
              Healthy lifestyle Tips
             </p>
            </div>
            <p>
            </p>
            <!--Ad Injection:random-->
            <div style="">
             <center>
              <script async="" src="//pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js">
              </script>
              <!-- fitness middle -->
              <ins class="adsbygoogle" data-ad-client="ca-pub-1734555513950336" data-ad-slot="8851394150" style="display:inline-block;width:336px;height:280px">
              </ins>
              <script>
               (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
              </script>
             </center>
            </div>
            <ol>
             <li>
              Add just one fruit or veggie serving daily. Get comfortable with that, then add an extra serving until you reach 8 to 10 a day.
             </li>
             <li>
              Eat at least two servings of a fruit or veggie at every meal.
             </li>
             <li>
              Resolve never to supersize your food portionsunless you want to superize your clothes.
             </li>
             <li>
              Make eating purposeful, not mindless. Whenever you put food in your mouth, peel it, unwrap it, plate it, and sit. Engage all of the senses in the pleasure of nourishing your body.
             </li>
             <li>
              Start eating a big breakfast. It helps you eat fewer total calories throughout the day.
             </li>
             <li>
              Make sure your plate is half veggies and/or fruit at both lunch and dinner.
             </li>
             <li>
              Eating out? Halve it, and bag the rest. A typical restaurant entree has 1,000 to 2,000 calories, not even counting the bread, appetizer, beverage, and dessert.
             </li>
             <li>
              When dining out, make it automatic: Order one dessert to share.
             </li>
             <li>
              Use a salad plate instead of a dinner plate.
             </li>
             <li>
              See what you eat. Plate your food instead of eating out of the jar or bag.
             </li>
             <li>
              Eat the low-cal items on your plate first, then graduate. Start with salads, veggies, and broth soups, and eat meats and starches last. By the time you get to them, youll be full enough to be content with smaller portions of the high-calorie choices.
             </li>
             <li>
              Instead of whole milk, switch to 1 percent. If you drink one 8-oz glass a day, youll lose 5 lb in a year.
             </li>
             <li>
              Juice has as many calories, ounce for ounce, as soda. Set a limit of one 8-oz glass of fruit juice a day.
             </li>
             <li>
              Get calories from foods you chew, not beverages. Have fresh fruit instead of fruit juice.
             </li>
             <li>
              Keep a food journal. It really works wonders  this is a great healthy lifestyle tips
             </li>
             <li>
              Follow the Chinese saying: Eat until you are eight-tenths full.
             </li>
             <li>
              Use mustard instead of mayo.
             </li>
             <li>
              Eat more soup. The noncreamy ones are filling but low-cal.
             </li>
             <li>
              Cut back on or cut out caloric drinks such as soda, sweet tea, lemonade, etc. People have lost  by making just this one change. If you have a 20-oz bottle of Coca-Cola every day, switch to Diet Coke. You should lose 25 lb in a year.
             </li>
             <li>
              Take your lunch to work.
             </li>
             <li>
              Sit when you eat.
             </li>
             <li>
              Dilute juice with water.
             </li>
             <li>
              Have mostly veggies for lunch.
             </li>
             <li>
              Eat at home.
             </li>
             <li>
              Limit alcohol to weekends.
             </li>
            </ol>
            <p>
             This is a great set of Healthy lifestyle Tips but of course there are a lot more. When you are looking to implement some Healthy lifestyle tips try to just add one per week so you dont get overwhelmed.
            </p>
