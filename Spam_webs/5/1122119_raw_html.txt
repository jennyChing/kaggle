         <p>
          Hello Kitty is almost 40 years old. In that time she went from being beloved by young girls to being loved by many adults also. And the number and types of Hello Kitty products has grown and grown. Sparklebee is now creating Hello Kitty tech gadgets that would make gifts any girl (and some women) would love.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center;">
          <a href="http://connectwithyourteens.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Hello-Killy-phone-case.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
            <img alt="Hello Kitty tech gadgets" class="aligncenter size-thumbnail wp-image-3221" height="150" src="http://connectwithyourteens.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Hello-Killy-phone-case-150x150.jpg" width="150"/>
            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://connectwithyourteens.net/hello-kitty-tech-gadgets/&amp;media=http://connectwithyourteens.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/Hello-Killy-phone-case-150x150.jpg&amp;description=Hello Kitty Tech Gadgets Make Great Gifts For Tween Girls')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          I was introduced to both the Paul Frank and Hello Kitty tech gadgets at a small blogger event with the owners of Sparklebee. They introduced us to the latest gadgets and they were not only adorable, but also very functional. I hope that the Paul Frank line grows so that boys that might not want Hello Kitty gadgets can still have some fun pop culture electronics of their own.
         </p>
         <h3>
          Here are some of my favorite Hello Kitty tech gadgets:
         </h3>
         <p>
          1.Hello Kitty  19 LCD Television/ PC Monitor with Remote  Available only atToys R Us. $299.99

          </b>
