          <p>
           <!-- BEGIN THEIA POST SLIDER -->
          </p>
          <p>
           First Essence trend edition for the month of July is finally here.
           <img alt=":happy:" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.parokeets.com/en/wp-content/images/smilies/sm23.gif" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
           I bought all eye souffls, nail polishes and blush souffl in
           <strong>
            Ballerina Backstage TE
           </strong>
           .
           <img alt=":blush:" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.parokeets.com/en/wp-content/images/smilies/1166268420.gif" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_23678" style="width: 460px">
           <a data-lightboxplus="lightbox[23676]" href="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE">
            <img alt="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE" class="size-medium wp-image-23678" height="341" src="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_parokeets-450x341.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE" width="450"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Essence Ballerina Backstage LE
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           I must say that I was pleasantly surprised by
           <strong>
            Essence Dance The Swan Lake
           </strong>
           nail polish. Im usually not huge fan of white shades (especially not those with visible nail line), but this one I actually liked  is summer heat getting to me?
           <img alt=":biggrin:" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.parokeets.com/en/wp-content/images/smilies/sm50.gif" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
           Application is not piece of cake, but if you use thicker layers 2 should be enough. I messed up and used to thin first layer, so I needed 3 coats. Pinkish shimmer is not really visible that much, but it ads something special to this shade.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_23679" style="width: 340px">
           <a data-lightboxplus="lightbox[23676]" href="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_Dance-The-Swan-Lake_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Dance The Swan Lake">
            <img alt="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Dance The Swan Lake swatch" class="size-full wp-image-23679" height="430" src="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_Dance-The-Swan-Lake_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Dance The Swan Lake" width="330"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Essence Ballerina Backstage LE  Dance The Swan Lake
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Wear Your Little Tutu
           </strong>
           is cute light pink shade with very similar shimmer as used in Dance The Swan Lake polish. Unfortunately this shade does nothing to accentuate my skin tone, so I wont be wearing it much. But it is perfect for those looking for neutral shade. Applied much like Dance The Swan Lake. 2 coats + top coat.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_23680" style="width: 340px">
           <a data-lightboxplus="lightbox[23676]" href="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_Wear-Your-Little-Tutu_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Wear Your Little Tutu">
            <img alt="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Wear Your Little Tutu swatch" class="size-full wp-image-23680" height="430" src="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_Wear-Your-Little-Tutu_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Wear Your Little Tutu" width="330"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Essence Ballerina Backstage LE  Wear Your Little Tutu
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           <strong>
            On Your Gracile Tiptoe
           </strong>
           is very lively summer shade. Polish is lovely orange-coral creme shade which applies beautifully (at least mine did, some had problems with theirs). Shade looks little less orange it real life. 2 coats + top coat.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_23683" style="width: 340px">
           <a data-lightboxplus="lightbox[23676]" href="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_On-Your-Gracile-Tiptoe_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - On Your Gracile Tiptoe">
            <img alt="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - On Your Gracile Tiptoe swatch" class="size-full wp-image-23683" height="430" src="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_On-Your-Gracile-Tiptoe_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - On Your Gracile Tiptoe" width="330"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Essence Ballerina Backstage LE  On Your Gracile Tiptoe
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Do A Floating Pirouette
           </strong>
           is a little bit too cool mix of pink and violet for me. It is pretty shade, but it doesnt look so hot on me. Formula is a little bit strange  cross between jelly and creme finish. But 2 layers are enough for even application.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_23682" style="width: 340px">
           <a data-lightboxplus="lightbox[23676]" href="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_Do-A-Floating-Pirouette_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Do A Floating Pirouette">
            <img alt="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Do A Floating Pirouette swatch" class="size-full wp-image-23682" height="430" src="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_Do-A-Floating-Pirouette_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Do A Floating Pirouette" width="330"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Essence Ballerina Backstage LE  Do A Floating Pirouette
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           The polish I was most looking forward from Ballerina Backstage collection is
           <strong>
            Grand-pli In Black
           </strong>
           . And it didnt disappoint me  deep black base loaded with copper glitter.
           <img alt=":wub:" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.parokeets.com/en/wp-content/images/smilies/wub.gif" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
           They could have added more glitter, but it looks lovely anyway. 2 layers + top coat.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_23681" style="width: 340px">
           <a data-lightboxplus="lightbox[23676]" href="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_Grand-plie-In-Black_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Grand-plie In Black">
            <img alt="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Grand-plie In Black swatch" class="size-full wp-image-23681" height="430" src="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_Grand-plie-In-Black_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Grand-plie In Black" width="330"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Essence Ballerina Backstage LE  Grand-plie In Black
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Now onto the products I was really looking forward to  souffls.
           <img alt=":love:" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.parokeets.com/en/wp-content/images/smilies/sm14.gif" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
           First of I swatched all three creamy eyeshadows on my hand: Dance The Swan Lake, Pas Des Copper and Grand-pli In Black and Prima Ballerina creamy blush.
          </p>
          <p>
           My first impressions:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Dance The Swan Lake
            </strong>
            : opaque matte light beige eyeshadow;
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Pas Des Copper
            </strong>
            : gorgeous copperish shimmery shade;
           </li>
           <li>
            <strong>
             Grand-pli In Black
            </strong>
            : glycerin like patchy sheer formula that is suppose to be black + it stains skin.
           </li>
          </ul>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_23685" style="width: 460px">
           <a data-lightboxplus="lightbox[23676]" href="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_eye_souffle_swatch_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Dance The Swan Lake, Pas Des Copper, Grand-plie In Black creamy eyeshadow ">
            <img alt="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Dance The Swan Lake, Pas Des Copper, Grand-plie In Black eye souffle swatch" class="size-medium wp-image-23685" height="341" src="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_eye_souffle_swatch_parokeets-450x341.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Dance The Swan Lake, Pas Des Copper, Grand-plie In Black creamy eyeshadow " width="450"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Essence Ballerina Backstage LE  Dance The Swan Lake, Pas Des Copper, Grand-plie In Black eye souffle (sun)
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_23686" style="width: 460px">
           <a data-lightboxplus="lightbox[23676]" href="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_eye_souffle_swatch_shade.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Dance The Swan Lake, Pas Des Copper, Grand-plie In Black creamy eyeshadow ">
            <img alt="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Dance The Swan Lake, Pas Des Copper, Grand-plie In Black eye souffle swatch" class="size-medium wp-image-23686" height="341" src="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_eye_souffle_swatch_shade-450x341.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Dance The Swan Lake, Pas Des Copper, Grand-plie In Black creamy eyeshadow " width="450"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Essence Ballerina Backstage LE  Dance The Swan Lake, Pas Des Copper, Grand-plie In Black eye souffle (shade)
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Then I tested it on my eyelids. By themselves eye souffls dont last long on my oily eyelids. If I set them with eyeshadow (I used Inglot 406 pearl), make up lasted for 4-6 hours before partially ending in the crease. The longest lasting was make up with
           <strong>
            Dance The Swan Lake
           </strong>
           as a base. But you get the least of wow effect with this very opaque beige shade (which leans a little bit to the pinkish side on my skin).
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Pas Des Copper
           </strong>
           acts as perfect base for eyeshadows  they stick to it like a glue but are still easy enough to blend. I cant use it close to my tear duct as it irritates my eyes. :hmm:
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Grand-plie In Black
           </strong>
           eye souffl looked really weird while applying  like I was applying something slimy with hint of black color. Texture reminded me of old black shoe polishes. But when you layer the eyeshadow over it 
           <img alt=":drop:" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.parokeets.com/en/wp-content/images/smilies/drop.gif" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
           I couldnt believe it how perfectly eye souffl grabbed eye shadow + blending it was so easy. Downside is that it wont stay in place as long as other two on oily eyelids without some kind of base under it and it irritated my eyes.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_23694" style="width: 460px">
           <a data-lightboxplus="lightbox[23676]" href="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Inglot_406P_over_Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_eye_souffle_parokeets.jpg" title="Inglot 406P over Essence Ballerina Backstage LE eye souffles">
            <img alt="Inglot 406P over Essence Ballerina Backstage LE eye souffles swatch" class="size-medium wp-image-23694" height="341" src="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Inglot_406P_over_Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_eye_souffle_parokeets-450x341.jpg" title="Inglot 406P over Essence Ballerina Backstage LE eye souffles" width="450"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Inglot 406P over Essence Ballerina Backstage LE eye souffles
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Prima Ballerina
           </strong>
           blush souffl is soft dirty pink cream blush with glycerin feel to it. I like the color and fine shimmer but not the formula. Staying power is not so great is you dont set it with some kind of powder and even then its not stellar. Few hours at most on my oily skin.
          </p>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_23684" style="width: 460px">
           <a data-lightboxplus="lightbox[23676]" href="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_Prima-Ballerina_creamy_blush_parokeets.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Prima-Ballerina creamy blush">
            <img alt="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Prima-Ballerina creamy blush swatch" class="size-medium wp-image-23684" height="341" src="http://www.parokeets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Essence_Ballerina-Backstage_LE_Prima-Ballerina_creamy_blush_parokeets-450x341.jpg" title="Essence Ballerina Backstage LE - Prima-Ballerina creamy blush" width="450"/>
           </a>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            Essence Ballerina Backstage LE  Prima-Ballerina blush souffle
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Prices:
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            10 ml (0,33 fl.oz.) nail polish: 1.75 EUR (2.5 USD);
           </li>
           <li>
            2,8g (0,09 oz.) eye souffl: 2.25 EUR ( 3.2 USD);
           </li>
           <li>
            6,1g (0,21 oz.) blush souffl: 2.75 EUR (4 USD).
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           What is your favorite from
           <strong>
            Essence Ballerina Backstage TE
           </strong>
           ?
          </p>
          <div class="mcePaste" id="_mcePaste" style="position: absolute; left: -10000px; top: 962px; width: 1px; height: 1px; overflow: hidden;">
           <strong>
            Dance The Swan Lake
           </strong>
