            </span>
            , Abigail will also be adding author to her already impressive resume with her publishing debut,
            <a href="http://www.harpercollins.com/childrens" target="_blank">
             <b>
              THIS MAY SOUND CRAZY.
             </b>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            Written with Abigails wit and honesty, THIS MAY SOUND CRAZYis full of cautionary tales and clever advice ranging from why you should stop stalking your ex to how to survive your very own
            <span class="xn-chron">
             Halloween
            </span>
            horror story. In this collection of nonfiction essays, Abigail brings her hilarious and heartfelt voice to the page as she explores the things nearest and dearest to every teenlove, loss, and Tumblr.
           </p>
           <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_12613" style="width: 460px">
            <a href="http://www.abeautyfeature.com/wordpress1/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/196316.jpg">
             <img alt="Actress Abigail Breslin signs publishing debut with HarperCollins " class="size-full wp-image-12613" height="675" src="http://www.abeautyfeature.com/wordpress1/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/196316.jpg" width="450"/>
            </a>
            <p class="wp-caption-text">
             Actress Abigail Breslin signs publishing debut with HarperCollins
            </p>
           </div>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            I started my tumblr Mixtapes and
            <span class="xn-person">
             Winter Coats
            </span>
            because I wanted to share my late night ramblings and feels, such as talking to my cat at2 AM, with all of you, says Breslin. That blog evolved into my book, THIS MAY SOUND CRAZY. I hope when you read it, youll see that you are not alone even when youre wondering when its ok to text your crush first, which is the most terrifying situation ever.
           </p>
           <h4 style="text-align: justify;">
            Actress Abigail Breslin signs publishing debut with HarperCollins
           </h4>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            Im so thrilled to be working with
            <span class="xn-person">
             Abigail Breslin
            </span>
            on
            <a href="http://www.harpercollins.com/childrens" target="_blank">
             THIS MAY SOUND CRAZY,
            </a>
             says
            <span class="xn-person">
             Dave Linker
            </span>
            , Executive Editor at HarperCollins. She channels the angst of her generation with a fearlessness thats at once hilarious and deeply moving. Anyone whos ever had a broken heart or felt lost or who just wished for a perfect
            <span class="xn-chron">
             Christmas Day
            </span>
            will feel instantly understood in these pages.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: justify;">
            World rights for THIS MAY SOUND CRAZY were sold to
            <span class="xn-person">
             David Linker
            </span>
            , Executive Editor
            <a href="http://www.harpercollins.com/childrens" target="_blank">
             HarperCollins US
            </a>
            from CAA and will be published in
            <span class="xn-chron">
             October 2015
            </span>
