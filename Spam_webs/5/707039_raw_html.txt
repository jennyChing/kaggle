    <h3 class="entry-header">
     <a href="http://basia.typepad.com/india_ink/2015/07/juneau-in-bloom.html">
      Juneau in Bloom
     </a>
    </h3>
    <div class="entry-content">
     <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
     <div class="entry-body">
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        Something I love about Juneau: the colorful gardens that pop up everywhere in the summer. I have yet to see anyone mowing a lawn in this town; instead of lawns there are just flowers galore.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Juneaugarden2015-06-4925.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        I LOVE this sweet little cottage. Baby blue. White picket fence. Surrounded by brilliant blooms. The only thing it needed was a little more space around it; it was a bit close to its neighbors. I wanted to pluck it and settle it down in the middle of a lush forest. Or maybe a tranquil seashore somewhere.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Juneaugarden2015-06-4933.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        It's not the state flower, but it should be. In June and July, brilliant fireweed is everywhere. It is so called because after a fire, it is the quickest plant to regenerate.
        <br/>
        <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Juneaugarden2015-06-4892.gif"/>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        Some people protect their heads from the rain; others their children or pets. For this gardener, it's the fragile peonies that are a priority. Adorbs.
       </span>
       <br/>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Juneaugarden2015-06-4931.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <br/>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/JuneaugardenIMG_1434.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        Peonies (some the size of basketballs) are especially popular.
        <em>
         Photo by Donna
        </em>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Juneaugarden2015-06-4891.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        In some gardens, the foxglove (Digitalis purpurea) is almost as tall as I am.
        <br/>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Juneaugarden2015-06-4936.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/>
      </p>
     </div>

  <div class="entry-category-plants entry-category-us_alaska entry-author-basia entry-type-post entry" id="entry-6a0105369f72c0970c01b7c7a80814970b">
   <div class="entry-inner">
    <h3 class="entry-header">
     <a href="http://basia.typepad.com/india_ink/2015/07/upside-down-flower-towers.html">
      Upside-Down Flower Towers
     </a>
    </h3>
    <div class="entry-content">
     <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
     <div class="entry-body">
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Garden2015-06-4834.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        Take a fallen spruce or hemlock tree and flip it upside down. Drive the top half of the trunk deep into the ground. On what was the bottom half (the root ball), plant a bed of brilliantly colored flowers. The result: the upside-down Flower Towers of
        <a href="http://www.glaciergardens.com/" target="_self">
         Glacier Gardens Rainforest Adventure
        </a>
        in Juneau. I've never seen anything like it!
        <br/>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Garden2015-06-4749.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Garden2015-06-4858.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        In keeping with the upside-down theme, scattered throughout the building are dozens of enormous (some almost 5-foot long) hanging petunia baskets.
       </span>
       <br/>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Garden2015-06-4816.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        The gardens are set in the Tongass National Forest, with a panoramic view of Juneau at the top.
       </span>
       <br/>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Garden2015-06-4762.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Garden2015-06-4744.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Garden2015-06-4796.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        Seeing how everything is upside in this garden, this collection of parasols hanging from the rafters fit right in.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-07/Garden2015-06-4843.gif"/>
      </p>

    <h3 class="entry-header">
     <a href="http://basia.typepad.com/india_ink/2015/06/sea-lions-whales-and-bears.html">
      Sea Lions, Whales and Bears
     </a>
    </h3>
    <div class="entry-content">
     <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
     <div class="entry-body">
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Wildlife2015-06-4607a.gif" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        I've been saying that I'm not leaving Alaska until I see a bear in the wild. I can go home now.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        This week's cruise to Tracy Arm fjord was a bear bonanza, with three separate black bear sightings, including a mama bear with her three cubs.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Wildlife2015-06-4385b.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Wildlife2015-06-4386.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        Also spotted - a rock full of raucous sea lions, all barking at each other.
       </span>
       <br/>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Wildlife2015-06-4656.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Wildlife2015-06-4662.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       Also making an appearance was a humpback whale (showing his characteristic hump below).
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Wildlife2015-06-4681.gif"/>

    <h3 class="entry-header">
     <a href="http://basia.typepad.com/india_ink/2015/06/glaciers-of-tracy-arm-fjord.html">
      Glaciers of Tracy Arm Fjord
     </a>
    </h3>
    <div class="entry-content">
     <input name="zemanta-related" type="hidden" val=""/>
     <div class="entry-body">
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Fjord2015-06-4496.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        If you have only one day in the Juneau area, take a cruise to Tracy Arm. Turquoise and emerald green waters. Glaciers that will take your breath away. Mile-high rock walls. Brilliant blue icebergs (chunks that break off from the glaciers) floating by.
        <br/>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        South Sawyer Glacier.
       </span>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Fjord2015-06-4426.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        North Sawyer Glacier
       </span>
       <br/>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Fjord2015-06-4486.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>

      </p>
      <p>

      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Fjord2015-06-4464.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Fjord2015-06-4466.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>

      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        Photo of Basia and Donna by Eva
       </span>
       <br/>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Tracy%20Arm.jpg"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Fjord2015-06-4588.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Fjord2015-06-4620.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Fjord2015-06-4647.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        The tour group we did it with was Adventure Bound, and I would highly recommend them. The group set off in two ships, each with about 30 people. The ship below was our sister vessel. It was a long, leisurely journey; we spent over an hour just standing in place watching ice calve (break) off the glacier.
        <br/>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Fjord2015-06-4549.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        Eva, Basia, and Donna on the Captain Cook.
       </span>
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="" border="1" src="http://basia.typepad.com/2015-06/Fjord2015-06-4689.gif"/>
      </p>
      <p style="text-align: center;">
       <span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 10pt;">
        <em>
         Where:
         <br/>
         Sawyer Glaciers, Tracy Arm Fjord, Alaska.
         <br/>
        </em>
       </span>
      </p>
