         <div class="page-header clearfix">
          <h1 class="h1__text" itemprop="headline">
           Chaos ensues in pileup at Macau tin-top race
          </h1>
         </div>
         <div class="video featured videoWrapper">
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="471" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/UzU3M-run0o" width="628">
          </iframe>
         </div>
         <div class="copy clearfix">
          <p class="byline pull-left">
           <span class="author hcard">
            <span class="author-headshot photo">
             <a class="url" href="/bloggers/noah-joseph/">
              <img class="img-responsive" onerror="this.onerror=null;this.src='http://o.aolcdn.com/os/wheels/img/profile_placeholder.png';" src="http://www.blogsmithmedia.com/www.autoblog.com/v/38aae4bef673b85facb94068826aea94849f8f44/img/editors/noah-joseph.jpg"/>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="btn-group">
             <a class="active btn btn-ultralight btn-xxs" href="https://plus.google.com/107002021296971448146">
              <span class="text-dark abicon abicon-google-plus">
              </span>
             </a>
             <a class="btn btn-ultralight btn-xxs" href="http://www.autoblog.com/bloggers/noah-joseph/rss.xml">

              </span>
             </a>
            </span>
            <span class="fn clearfix" itemprop="author">
             <a href="/bloggers/noah-joseph/">

             </a>
            </span>
           </span>
          </p>
          <div itemprop="articleBody">
           Touring car grids around the world have been growing at suitably rapid pace. The
           <a href="http://www.autoblog.com/tag/btcc/">
            British Touring Car Championship
           </a>
           has been fielding record numbers of entries,
           <a href="http://www.autoblog.com/tag/dtm/">
            DTM
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://www.autoblog.com/tag/v8+supercars/">
            V8 Supercars
           </a>
           both have more manufacturers participating than they have had in years, the
           <a href="http://www.autoblog.com/tag/stcc/">
            Scandinavian Touring Car Championship
           </a>
           has brought several national series together on one grid.... we could go on, but you get the point. And nothing illustrates that point quite like this pileup at this year's
           <a href="http://www.autoblog.com/tag/macau/">
            Macau Grand Prix
           </a>
           .
           <br/>
           The premier event in
           <a href="http://www.autoblog.com/tag/formula+3/">
            Formula 3
           </a>
           racing is held on the streets of China's version of Monte Carlo or Las Vegas and includes numerous support races. Among those are the
           <a href="http://www.autoblog.com/tag/wtcc/">
            World Touring Car Championship
           </a>
           , a motorcycle race and the Chinese Racing Cup. It's during that tin-top race  campaigned by a field of 26 identical BAIC Senova D70s  that this massive pileup ensued.
           <br/>
           <br/>
           We won't spoil it by giving you a play-by-play, but if you fast-forward to about the 45-second mark you can watch the nobody-got-hurt hilarity unfold before your very eyes. Be sure to add some mental Benny Hill Theme music for the full effect.
