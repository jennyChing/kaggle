            <p>
             <a href="http://popdust.com/tag/kathy-griffin" target="_blank">
              <strong>
               Kathy Griffin
              </strong>
             </a>
             has quit
             <em>
              E!
             </em>
             s
             <a href="http://popdust.com/tag/fashion-police" target="_blank">
              <em>
               Fashion Police
              </em>
             </a>
             because she thinks its too mean.
            </p>
            <p>
             In a surprising move, the comicwho was brought in
             <a href="http://popdust.com/2014/09/04/joan-rivers-dead-dies-obituary-young-photos-career/" target="_blank">
              following the death of
              <strong>
               Joan Rivers
              </strong>
             </a>
             released a statement via
             <a href="http://popdust.com/tag/twitter" target="_blank">
              <strong>
               Twitter
              </strong>
             </a>
             saying:
            </p>
            <blockquote>
             <p>
              I thought I could bringmy brand of humor to Fashion Police so that beautiful people in beautiful dresses could be teased when appropriate. My brand of humor, while unrepentant and unafraid, is all about CONTEXT. Theres plenty to make fun of in pop culture without bringing peoples bodies into it.
             </p>
            </blockquote>
            <p>
             She didnt mention the whole
             <a href="http://popdust.com/tag/zendaya" target="_blank">
              <strong>
               Zendaya
              </strong>
             </a>
             patchouli oilthing, which was
             <a href="http://popdust.com/2015/02/27/kelly-osbourne-quits-fashion-police/" target="_blank">
              rumored to have caused
              <strong>
               Kelly Osbourne
              </strong>
              to quit
             </a>
             , but she did add:
            </p>
            <blockquote>
             <p>
              I do not want to use my comedy to contribute to a culture of unattainable perfectionism and intolerance towards difference.
             </p>
            </blockquote>
            <p>
             Which to us speaks a thousand words.
            </p>
