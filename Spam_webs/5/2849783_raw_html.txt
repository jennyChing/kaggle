        <h1 class="post-title" itemprop="name">
         Should Husbands Buy Their Wives Mothers Day Gifts?
        </h1>
        <a class="comments" href="http://mochadad.com/2012/05/should-husbands-buy-their-wives-mothers-day-gifts/#comments">

        <div class="post-content-right">
         <div class="post-content description" itemprop="articleBody">
          <p>
           <a href="http://mochadad.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/Mothers-Day-graphic-photo-pic-graph.jpg">
            <img alt="" class="alignright wp-image-5805" height="311" src="http://mochadad.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/Mothers-Day-graphic-photo-pic-graph.jpg" title="Mothers Day graphic photo pic graph" width="415"/>
           </a>
           Mothers Day is an occasion when people around the world celebrate their moms. Buying your own mother a gift is a no-brainer, but things arent always so obvious when it comes to other moms in your life. Im speaking specifically of the mothers of our children.
          </p>
          <p>
           I asked people on
           <a href="www.facebook.com/pages/Mocha-Dad/32466428487">
            Facebook
           </a>
           and
           <a href="http://www.twitter.com/mochadad" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.twitter.com/mochadad', 'Twitter']);">
            Twitter
           </a>
           if they thought that husbands were obligated to buy their wives a gift on Mothers Day. Sixty-eight percent (68%) of respondents believe that husbands should buy a gift. In fact, some were quite vehement in their assertion.
          </p>
          <p>
           If she is a mother or if yall are trying to have kids, then absolutely, said Richard. I dont want the mother of my two beautiful boys upset with me on Mothers Day because I decided not to get her a gift because aint my momma. That would be dumb.
          </p>
          <p>
           Other comments such as Duh and Of course were expressed in the survey.
          </p>
          <p>
           When I broke down the data by gender, I discovered that 33% of men believe that they should not purchase their wives Mothers Day gifts compared to 31% of women.
          </p>
          <p>
           I think that this is a day that children should honor their mothers, said Broadus. Spouses have plenty of other opportunities.
          </p>
          <p>
           Ann, a mother of three agreed.
          </p>
          <p>
           I tell my husband Im not his mother and I dont expect a gift from him. I encourage him to buy for his own mother to set a good example for our kids.
          </p>
          <p>
           Despite the differing views about buying gifts, one thing was consistent: Mothers want to feel appreciated on Mothers Day.
          </p>
          <p>
           Gifts arent required, said Mary. But husbands must do something to recognize her role as the mother to his children.
          </p>
          <p>
           Men would do well to follow this simple bit of advice from LuCretia.
          </p>
          <p>
           Its not the size or cost of the gift that matters, she said. The fact that my husband thought of me is whats priceless.
          </p>
          <p>
           Stay Strong,
