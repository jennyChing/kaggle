       <p>
        <em>
         Note: this is a satirical post. I wrote it when I first launched Intergeek, but since this postI have been producing what is considered helpful content by at least one person other than myself. If youre looking for genuinely useful advice, youll want to check out my other posts. However, before that, put your feet up and enjoy this:
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Dont you just hate it when everyone keeps copying your style?
       </p>
       <p>
        You try and come out with something new and exciting and people
        <em>
         just dont get it.
        </em>
        Next thing you know, everyone is doing it.
       </p>
       <p>
        Hipsters were producing great content years ago; now everyone is jumping on the bandwagon and you know what?
       </p>
       <p>
        It isnt cool anymore.
       </p>
       <p>
        Content isnt king and if you want to be a hipster in the SEO world then youve got a lot to learn.
       </p>
       <p>
        Fortunately, weve put together this handy guide to educate you in the ways of hipster SEO, ensuring you can stay ahead of future trends.
       </p>
       <p>
        You
        <em>
         are
        </em>
        future trends. And you know whats
        <em>
         in
        </em>
        right now? Things that were cool in the past, obviously.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Hipster SEO Attire
       </h3>
       <p>
        First things first, why are you dressed like that?
       </p>
       <p>
        Youre gonna need a black hat around here; thats just our thing these days. Forget the
        <del>
         trampy
        </del>
        upcycled clothes from the thrift shop; ignore the long beards; the skinny jeans; the lack of socksall you need to be an SEO hipster is a black hat.Preferablyvintage.
       </p>
       <p>
        Dont worry what everyone is saying about your new hat, theres no need to conform. You arent a sheep, are you?
       </p>
       <p>
        Youre not going to be affected by negative opinions any more.
       </p>
       <p>
        Pretty soon, youre not even gonna be able to hear the haters because of the sound of all that money landing in your bank after you win at the search engines.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Hipster with coffee" class="aligncenter wp-image-379" height="839" src="http://intergeek.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/9428305104_a69ef586ce_b.jpg" width="650"/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Networking
       </h3>
       <p>
        OK, so, networking. Forget everything youve learned about it in the past few years; thats not cool now,
        <em>
         remember
        </em>
        ? You dont want to be hanging out and interacting with popular sites, popular
        <em>
         sucks
        </em>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        Youve got to know the right people if you want to find the good places to hang out at these days, and fortunately I am that people.
       </p>
       <p>
        Relationship building is a time consuming process, even to just get one measly link back at the end. Whats the point in that?!
       </p>
       <p>
        The best thing you can do is use software to generate thousands of links in minutes.
       </p>
       <p>
        This enables you to spend the rest ofyour daydrinking the finest roast of coffee and looking for new music by bands who dont even know
        <em>
         themselves
        </em>
        that theyve formed yet.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Where to Hang Out
       </h3>
       <p>
        I know a load of sweet, rundown link farms and thousands of niche directories that are super sick, you wont find those douches from
        <a href="http://moz.com/" target="_blank" title="Moz">
         Moz
        </a>
        or any other mainstream SEOs hanging out around there anymore. Just cool guys like me.
       </p>
       <p>
        Were kinda fussy with where we hang out.
       </p>
       <p>
        As we all know, the higher the PageRank of a site, the more exclusive and underground its comment section becomes.
       </p>
       <p>
        The trick when commenting is to make it abundantly clear you are, in fact, a robot. However, you still need to sweet talk the author in the distant hope that yourcomment may be published. Akismet knows what spammers are doing, so fool it by acting
        <em>
         just
        </em>
        like a spammer!Its both genius
        <em>
         and
        </em>
        foolproof.
       </p>
       <p>
        Something vague but overly complimentary, with the occasional typo, will usually suffice:
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Hey! I must say what a fantastitc job youve done with this piece. You really are a knowledgeable expert in this field. I thank you from the bottom of my heart for putting together this insightful article. Hopefully you can check out
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          <strong>
           my blog
          </strong>
         </span>
         sometime!
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Extra kudos if youve linked to a website that is selling viagra.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Press Releases
       </h3>
       <p>
        Have you launched a new page or product on your website that absolutely no-one will care about? Me too!
       </p>
       <p>
        We both know the links and shares will come flooding in from all the people that arent interested in your truly creativeattempt at news, but all we need to do is set the ball in motion through a press release or two hundred.
       </p>
       <p>
        Not to worry! I know a heapof press releases sites (all PR6+!) that you can write a short-but-sweet, meaningless article on with up to seventeen exact-match anchor text links in. And two more in your author bio.
       </p>
       <p>
        As well as appearing on these highly valuable websites, your article will be delivered tothe inbox of top journalists around the country who definitely wont click delete as soon as it arrives.
       </p>
       <p>
        Even if they dont share yourcontent(which they wont, stupid punks) at least your press release will be syndicated across a further fifty websites, all with links back of course.
       </p>
       <p>
        Soon enough, youll have so much link juice flowing to your website that it will get drunk and stagger its way right to the top of Google.
       </p>
       <p>
        All these sober, clean SEO websites wouldnt dare of climbing so high.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="Panda" class="aligncenter wp-image-380" height="502" src="http://intergeek.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/425893654_fd94682070_b.jpg" width="750"/>
       </p>
       <h3>
        Tripping Out
       </h3>
       <p>
        You need to be aware of the side effects that can be brought on by consuming allof that delicious link juice whilstsimultaneouslywinning the internet.
       </p>
       <p>
        This might sound crazy, I didnt believe the lads when they told me, but then I experienced it for myself.Weve all been attacked by penguins.
       </p>
       <p>
        I dont know where they came from, but they were ferocious and they killed all of the visitors that were previously arriving at my uber exclusiveniche websites.
       </p>
       <p>
        Thats not even the worst of it. The other day, I was attacked by a panda. An actual
        <em>
         panda
        </em>
        ! They might be so inbred that they are too dumb to reproduce, but theyre pretty scary yknow.
       </p>
       <p>
        This is all because Google just follows the crowd,agreeing with every opinion that the majority have. Thin isnt cool anymore, just ask
        <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7PCkvCPvDXk" target="_blank" title="All About That Bass">
         Meghan Trainor
        </a>
        .
       </p>
       <p>
        The public want models to have more weight on them and Google want their websites to be chubbier too. Theyre
        <em>
         weight-ist.
        </em>
       </p>
       <p>
        Fat is in, so we love thin.
       </p>
       <p>
        After some digging around in the templeof non-confirmist SEO,
        <a href="http://www.blackhatworld.com/" title="BlackHatWorld">
         BlackHatWorld
        </a>
        ,I found thatpandas hunt down your website when its underweight and vulnerable. Theyre vicious beasts that have a burning hatred for web traffic. They can consume thousands of visitors in a single sitting.
       </p>
       <p>
        They might be causing havoc butI love wildlife. Pandas and penguins are chill, man. White hat
        <em>
         sheeple
        </em>
        are all hating on these animals, well I think someone needs to stick up for them.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Forget Organic
       </h3>
       <p>
        We used to think thatorganic was the best thing since sliced bread, now us hipsters despise organic.
       </p>
       <p>
        Were not bothered in saving the planet anymore, or the SERPs for that matter. In fact were more than happy to contribute to negative climate change through the use of website churning-and-burning.
       </p>
       <p>
        Pandas are endangered creatures and with every low quality website we create, we help them populate.
       </p>
       <p>
        Think of it as charity workif charity work allowed you to have fat-stack-generating affiliate banners and Skimlinks.
       </p>
       <p>
        You watch, everyone will be following this guidein a couple of years. Just remember, we were black hat before it was cool
        <em>
         AND
        </em>
        after it was cool.
       </p>
       <h3>
        Mobile-Friendliness
       </h3>
       <p>
        Im just gonna come out and say this straight.
       </p>
       <p>
        Why the fuck would you want your site to be mobile-friendly?
       </p>
       <p>
        What an insane investment to make when you already know that anyone cool sends hand written letters, transported by carrier pigeon.
       </p>
       <p>
        You wont see a pigeon losing signal under a bridge or in a tunnel.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>

       </p>
