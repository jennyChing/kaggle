                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     Cranberry Recipes
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-5068678026921792247" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     When I saw this beautiful cranberry garland from
                     <a href="http://funkyjunkinteriors.blogspot.com/2010/12/when-non-crafting-junker-crafts-for.html">
                      Funky Junk Interior
                     </a>
                     I went looking for more blog posts about cranberries.
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_bieH-shASYc/TP3Zn1k4AeI/AAAAAAAAL2k/6AT-c8FcmeM/s1600/cranberry.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="300" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_bieH-shASYc/TP3Zn1k4AeI/AAAAAAAAL2k/6AT-c8FcmeM/s400/cranberry.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <div style="margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">
                      I was expecting to write a post about using them for Christmas decorations, but after getting hungry looking at all the great recipes I found, I figured I would share some of them with you.
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     <a href="http://pisforparty.blogspot.com/2010/12/recipes-cranberry-poppers.html">
                      P is for Party
                     </a>
                     has a recipe forcranberry poppers:
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_bieH-shASYc/TP3aC2gHWWI/AAAAAAAAL2o/CpEPqw4lVck/s1600/cranberry+poppers.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="274" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_bieH-shASYc/TP3aC2gHWWI/AAAAAAAAL2o/CpEPqw4lVck/s320/cranberry+poppers.png" width="320"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     This cranberry salsa from
                     <a href="http://www.yourhomebasedmom.com/cranberry-salsa/">
                      Your Homebased Mom
                     </a>
                     is going to make an appearance at our house before the holiday season is over:
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_bieH-shASYc/TP3bjgDmDtI/AAAAAAAAL2s/eqdJVqtQ_RM/s1600/cranberry+salsa.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="265" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_bieH-shASYc/TP3bjgDmDtI/AAAAAAAAL2s/eqdJVqtQ_RM/s400/cranberry+salsa.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     if you didn't get enough pumpkin during Thanksgiving you might want to dress it up for the holidays with this cranberry pecan pumpkin tart from
                     <a href="http://www.goodlifeeats.com/2010/12/cranberry-pecan-pumpkin-tart.html">
                      Good Life Eats
                     </a>
                     :
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_bieH-shASYc/TP3cC2dlYDI/AAAAAAAAL2w/0mA2pZOscyA/s1600/carnberry+tart.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="266" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/_bieH-shASYc/TP3cC2dlYDI/AAAAAAAAL2w/0mA2pZOscyA/s400/carnberry+tart.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     or if you are looking for something simpler you can try her popcorn trail mix (that has dried cranberries in it):
                     <br/>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_bieH-shASYc/TP3c4STN6WI/AAAAAAAAL20/BscoeE9itec/s1600/popcorn.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img border="0" height="285" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_bieH-shASYc/TP3c4STN6WI/AAAAAAAAL20/BscoeE9itec/s400/popcorn.jpg" width="400"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <br/>
                     What is your favorite cranberry recipe?
