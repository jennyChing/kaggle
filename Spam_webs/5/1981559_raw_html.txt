            <div class="excerpt">
             <p>
              Last night was the Hollywood Music in Media Awards, and it was AMAZING!!! For those of you who dont know, the 5th annual
              <a href="http://www.hmmawards.org/2014-hollywood-music-media-awards/" target="_blank" title="HMMAs">
               HMMAs
              </a>
              is one of the biggest award shows in Hollywood for recognizing the talents of those in music and the visual arts. It honors individuals who create and produce music from all over the world, in both the mainstream and independent sectors.
             </p>
             <p>
              <span style="line-height: 1.5em;">
               For that reason, we were so excited to be nominated in not just one category, but two!
