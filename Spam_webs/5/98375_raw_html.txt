        <h1 class="article-title" itemprop="headline">
         Five Things Successful Small Businesses Do
        </h1>
       </header>
       <!-- end article header -->
       <br class="clearfix"/>
       <div class="post-img">
        <img alt="15_00243-Lede-Blog02-800x600-2-Successful[8]" class="attachment-single-post-thumbnail wp-post-image" height="600" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15_00243-Lede-Blog02-800x600-2-Successful8.jpg" width="800"/>
       </div>
       <section class="article-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
        <p>
         What makes one business more successful than another? At TELUS, we wondered the same thing and decided to launch the
         <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cobTP1elLDU&amp;list=PLhSGs3IE5KDwZLsDNUhis-xy0Nyk0Xaql">
          #MyBusinessTip
         </a>
         video series to find out. Over the last two years, weve collected nearly fifty pieces of advice from Canadas top small business owners and entrepreneurs. These individuals have shared a number of lessons with us, including a few that you wont learn in business school.
        </p>
        <p>
         As the saying goes, you dont need to reinvent the wheel. Smart companies adopt and adapt strategies that other successful organizations have proven. Below are five tried-and-true tips that will help small businesses achieve long-lasting success, including a tip of my own.
        </p>
        <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-1.jpg">
         <img alt="15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-1" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-4941" height="250" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-1.jpg" width="700"/>
        </a>
        <p>
         As an entrepreneur, you might feel like you need to have all the answers, but its hard for a single person, or even a group of managers, to have the knowledge to consistently make the right business decisions. The most successful entrepreneurs seek advice from outside of their organization and arent afraid to reach out to their friends and colleagues for advice.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <br/>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/3ZCi7D9D3h0" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </center>
        <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-2.jpg">
         <img alt="15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-2" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-4942" height="250" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-2.jpg" width="700"/>
        </a>
        <p>
         Honesty is the best policy, especially in business. Show your customers exactly how far theirdollar will go. If you truly understand your clients needs, theyll see value in your offerings and will happily do business with you. The bottom line is: people want to do business with credible, trustworthy companies.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/UV21ZbM7pjg" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </center>
        <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-3.jpg">
         <img alt="15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-3" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-4943" height="250" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-3.jpg" width="700"/>
        </a>
        <p>
         Good customer service goes a long way for small businesses. Youve probably heard that positive experiences create loyal customers who are quick to make recommendations to friends and family. What you might not have considered is how beneficial this can be for small businesses with small marketing budgets. When cash flow is limited, word-of-mouth marketing is invaluable.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ksf79zJEA1g" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </center>
        <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-4.jpg">
         <img alt="15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-4" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-4944" height="250" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-4.jpg" width="700"/>
        </a>
        <p>
         Meaningful customer relationships are as important online as they are offline. Social media platforms like Facebook and Twitter provide inexpensive ways for companies to develop connections with their customers, as well as conduct market research and communicate brand values.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/UKV_mbdx7Cs" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </center>
        <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-5.jpg">
         <img alt="15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-5" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-4945" height="250" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/15_00243-SubHead-Blog02-700x250-5.jpg" width="700"/>
        </a>
        Small businesses face unique challenges when looking for financing because the funding options available to them can be limited. With a great deal of competition out there, its more important than ever for entrepreneurs to quickly and effectively capture the interest of potential investors. One way to communicate the value of your business opportunity is through a perfect pitch. Be sure to take the time to understand what the investor really wants and make it the focus of your presentation.
        <p>
        </p>
        <center>
         <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Uzo8YFPPKTw" width="560">
         </iframe>
        </center>
        Speaking of funding, heres a potential source 
        <a href="http://business.telus.com/en/campaigns/2015-small-business-challenge ?CMP=PR_sCPR_cChallenge5successful_cdPR article _ad">
         The Small Business Challenge
        </a>
         our annual contest with The Globe and Mail that helps Canadian small businesses overcome hurdles and reach their full potential. From now until June 1, were asking small businesses to submit their biggest challenge and explain how $100,000 would help them overcome it. Sound like a contest for you or someone you know?
        <a href="http://business.telus.com/en/campaigns/2015-small-business-challenge ?CMP=PR_sCPR_cChallenge5successful_cdPR article _ad">
         Click here
        </a>
        for more information.
        <a href="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/15_00188-01EN_20.png">
         <img alt="15_00188-01EN_20" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-4748" height="132" src="http://blog.telus.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/15_00188-01EN_20.png" width="953"/>
        </a>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
