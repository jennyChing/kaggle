      <article class="post-9313 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-home category-organize tag-decorating-tips tag-organization tag-spring-cleaning tag-tips-tricks" id="post-9313" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Article">
       <header class="wrapper entry-header page-header">
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="entry-category">
          <a href="http://julieblanner.com/category/home/">
           Home
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="http://julieblanner.com/category/organize/">
           Organize
          </a>
         </span>
         <time class="entry-date updated" datetime="2015-03-31T13:44:36+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">

         </time>

        <div class="title-with-sep single-title">
         <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
          An Organized Eye to Design
         </h1>
        </div>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-media">
       </div>
       <div class="wrapper">
        <div class="grids">
         <div class="grid-8 column-1">
          <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            Its officially spring &amp; while many may be thinking about cleaning, Im always trying to find ways to avoid it. I naturally give our home a little refresh in spring due to my newfound motivation that comes with the cool breeze &amp; sunlight, but with 3 little ones &amp; a busy schedule, Id rather spend my time enjoying our home than cleaning it.I believe an organized home leads to a cleanerhome, with minimal effort. I like to blend organizing &amp; decorating together for a home that is both functional &amp; beautiful. Step inside for organization ideas infused into decor
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://julieblanner.com/organization-ideas/how-to-organize/" rel="attachment wp-att-9322">
             <img alt="Beautiful organization ideas" class=" wp-image-9322 size-full aligncenter" height="1050" src="http://julieblanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/how-to-organize.jpg" width="700"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Living clutter free starts with organization. Having an organized mudroom helps us keep up with a fast pace, being able to grab what we need, when we need it &amp; having a place to put it when we return home so that its not abandoned in various rooms throughout our home. Over time, needs change. Rather than create built-ins for our mudroom, I purchased a beautiful marble top antique chest. It serves as a landing spot for our keys, cell phones, etc &amp; offers plenty of storage. As our lifestyle changes, we can adapt what we store in it or the piece itself.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://julieblanner.com/organization-ideas/cleaning-tips/" rel="attachment wp-att-9333">
             <img alt="How to maintain a clean &amp; organized home" class=" size-full wp-image-9333 aligncenter" height="1050" src="http://julieblanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/cleaning-tips.jpg" width="700"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            As youll see, I cant resist a tray. Theyre a great catch all &amp; you can easily move them along with thecontents, to wipe the surface clean.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://julieblanner.com/organization-ideas/living-room-organization/" rel="attachment wp-att-9330">
             <img alt="Easy organization ideas that blend with decor" class=" wp-image-9330 size-full aligncenter" height="1050" src="http://julieblanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/living-room-organization.jpg" width="700"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            I also have a tray in our living room  its been a staple for several years now because it has proven to be functional. It collects our magazines until we have time to peruse through them &amp; is a safe haven for our remote controls. In addition, it becomes a decorative element, while freeing the living room from clutter.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://julieblanner.com/organization-ideas/organization-ideas/" rel="attachment wp-att-9327">
             <img alt="Simple &amp; beautiful organization ideas" class=" size-full wp-image-9327 aligncenter" height="1017" src="http://julieblanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/organization-ideas.jpg" width="700"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            To promote picking up toys, we have baskets placed throughout our home that double as accents. In our family room, we have a coffee table that offers plenty of storage for their books. The girls call it their library! Its a library without afiling system, which makes it easy for them to access, enjoy &amp; clean up. Bonus? There are no bookcases to dust!
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://julieblanner.com/organization-ideas/living-room-storage-3/" rel="attachment wp-att-9326">
             <img alt="Easy storage solutions for your home" class=" size-full wp-image-9326 aligncenter" height="1051" src="http://julieblanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/living-room-storage.jpg" width="700"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Instead of creating a beautiful collection of accents to display in our china cabinet, I displayed our china in an artful way by thoughtfully stacking &amp; arranging pieces.Doing so reduces clutter &amp; allows me to use my china beyond the holidays.
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://julieblanner.com/organization-ideas/dining-room-decorating-ideas/" rel="attachment wp-att-9325">
             <img alt="Beautiful ways to decorate with function" class=" size-full wp-image-9325 aligncenter" height="467" src="http://julieblanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/dining-room-decorating-ideas.jpg" width="700"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://julieblanner.com/organization-ideas/dining-room-organization/" rel="attachment wp-att-9329">
             <img alt="An organized eye to decorating - simple ways to blend both beauty &amp; function" class=" size-full wp-image-9329 aligncenter" height="1051" src="http://julieblanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/dining-room-organization.jpg" width="700"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Even the
            <a href="http://julieblanner.com/laundry-room-storage-solutions/" target="_blank" title="Laundry Room Storage Solutions">
             laundry room
            </a>
            has been accessorized with functional decor! Its limited tocleaning caddies for convenience, a boxwood topiary &amp; pretty glass accent. I leave the surface of the washer &amp; dryer open to use as a work surface when checking for stains or folding. Its also the only surface that needs to be wiped down!
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://julieblanner.com/organization-ideas/pretty-organization-ideas/" rel="attachment wp-att-9323">
             <img alt="Easy storage solutions for your home" class=" size-full wp-image-9323 aligncenter" height="1050" src="http://julieblanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/pretty-organization-ideas.jpg" width="700"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Our guest bathroom isnt frequently used, which makes it susceptible to dust. To keep it tidy Ive limited the decor to another little brass tray, a natural sponge &amp; brush in addition to a jar filled with my favorite soaps for guests. Storing them in a jar makes them easily accessible to guests, keeps them dust free &amp; allows me to dedicate the cabinet to towel storage. Plus, soaps in a jar are just gorgeous, arent they?
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://julieblanner.com/organization-ideas/bathroom-organization/" rel="attachment wp-att-9328">
             <img alt="An organized eye to decorating - simple ways to blend both beauty &amp; function into your home" class=" size-full wp-image-9328 aligncenter" height="1050" src="http://julieblanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/bathroom-organization.jpg" width="700"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Are you ready to
            <a href="http://www.maids.com/blog/10-decluttering-tips-that-take-5-minutes-or-less/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             declutter
            </a>
            ? Heres a list of great
            <a href="http://www.maids.com/blog/10-decluttering-tips-that-take-5-minutes-or-less/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
             decluttering ideas that take 5 minutes or less
            </a>
            !If you incorporate any of these organization ideas into your home, take a photo &amp; share on Instagram with the hashtag #julieblanner  Id love to see!
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://julieblanner.com/organization-ideas/pretty-organizing-ideas/" rel="attachment wp-att-9324">
             <img alt="bedroom organization ideas" class=" size-full wp-image-9324 aligncenter" height="1050" src="http://julieblanner.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/pretty-organizing-ideas.jpg" width="700"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <em>

             </a>

            </em>
           </p>
