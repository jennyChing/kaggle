                    <h3 class="post-title entry-title" itemprop="name">
                     3 Ingredient Zucchini Brownies
                    </h3>
                    <div class="post-header">
                     <div class="post-header-line-1">
                     </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-1938376419955167618" itemprop="description articleBody">
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-rmSBMOYASFw/U9U_QbP64aI/AAAAAAAAUWY/zEFZ4t8J4sw/s1600/3IngredientZucchiniBrowniesTitled.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="3 Ingredient Zucchini Brownies via thefrugalfoodiemama.com- these incredibly moist brownies are so easy to make, plus bonus hidden veggies!" border="0" height="640" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-rmSBMOYASFw/U9U_QbP64aI/AAAAAAAAUWY/zEFZ4t8J4sw/s1600/3IngredientZucchiniBrowniesTitled.jpg" title="" width="480"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="javascript:void((function(){var%20e=document.createElement('script');e.setAttribute('type','text/javascript');e.setAttribute('charset','UTF-8');e.setAttribute('src','http://assets.pinterest.com/js/pinmarklet.js?r='+Math.random()*99999999);document.body.appendChild(e)})());" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="Pin It" class="aligncenter" src="http://www.clickinmoms.com/cmprodaily/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/pinit.jpg"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       A visit to any local farmers' market or perhaps even to your own backyard garden will reveal that one thing is certain- zucchini season is definitely here and they are plentiful! :)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       While I didn't plant any zucchini myself this year, I can attest that they can be pertained at both the farmers' markets and grocery stores for pretty darn cheap right now. And cheap is always good. ;)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       But let's be honest- you can only make
                       <i>
                        SO
                       </i>
                       many loaves of zucchini bread and
                       <a href="http://cupcakesandkalechips.com/2014/07/31/zucchini-noodles-zoodles-greek-salad/" target="_blank">
                        batches of zoodles
                       </a>
                       (have you all seen these??) before you are zucchini'ed out.
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       So let's make something chocolaty, fudgy, and deceptively decadent with them instead, shall we?
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <br/>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                       These super simple and easy zucchini brownies were inspired by my friend Jellibean Journals' recipe for
                       <a href="http://www.jellibeanjournals.com/skinny-avocado-brownies/" target="_blank">
                        Life-Changing Skinny Avocado Brownies
                       </a>
                       . Both recipes have just 3 simple ingredients, but have only one ingredient in common- the boxed brownie mix! But both variations are delicious, fudgy, and are a fun &amp; easy way to sneak more veggies into your family's diet... ;)
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
                      <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                      </span>
                     </div>
                     <a name="more">
                     </a>
                     <br/>
                     <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                      <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Zu-gwokJEew/U9U_YvlJRRI/AAAAAAAAUWg/0DdOAm_fKlI/s1600/3IngredientZucchiniBrownies1.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                       <img alt="3 Ingredient Zucchini Brownies via thefrugalfoodiemama.com- these incredibly moist brownies are so easy to make, plus bonus hidden veggies!" border="0" height="480" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Zu-gwokJEew/U9U_YvlJRRI/AAAAAAAAUWg/0DdOAm_fKlI/s1600/3IngredientZucchiniBrownies1.jpg" title="" width="640"/>
                      </a>
                     </div>
                     <div class="hrecipe f1">
                      <div class="fn single_recipe_header" style="color: #d61313; margin: 5px 5px 5px 0px; padding: 0px;">
                       <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                        3 ingredient Zucchini Brownies
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <img alt="" class="photo" height="200" src="http://www.recipage.com/images/user2205/1406904608/recipe_image.jpg" style="clear: both; float: left; padding: 0px 10px 10px 0px;" width="150"/>
                      <br/>
                      <div class="single_recipe_text" id="author" style="color: #120902; margin: 5px 5px 5px 0px; padding: 0px;">
                       <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                        by
                        <span class="author">
                         Frugal Foodie Mama
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="single_recipe_text" style="color: #120902; margin: 8px 4px 4px; padding: 0px;">
                       <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                        <span style="font-weight: bold;">
                         Prep Time:
                        </span>
                        <span class="preptime">
                         5 minutes
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="single_recipe_text" style="color: #120902; margin: 4px; padding: 0px;">
                       <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                        <span style="font-weight: bold;">
                         Cook Time:
                        </span>
                        <span class="cooktime">
                         per boxed mix instructions
                        </span>
                       </span>
                      </div>
                      <div class="single_recipe_text" style="color: #120902; margin: 4px; padding: 0px;">
                      </div>
                      <div id="get_media_div" style="clear: both; max-height: 100px;">
                       <div id="recipe_id_div" style="display: none;">
                        6065134
                       </div>
                       <script src="https://ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jquery/1.6.4/jquery.js" type="text/javascript">
                       </script>
                       <script src="http://www.recipage.com/new_scripts/get_html2.js" type="text/javascript">
                       </script>
                      </div>
                      <div id="ingredients">
                       <div class="single_recipe_header" id="ingr_header" style="clear: both; color: #d61313; padding: 0px; text-decoration: none;">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         Ingredients
                         <span class="single_recipe_text" style="color: #120902;">
                          (9-12 brownies)
                         </span>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <ul class="single_recipe_text" id="ingr" style="color: #120902;">
                        <li class="ingredient" style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                          1 box of your favorite brownie mix (I used an 18.9 oz box of Betty Crocker brownie mix)
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li class="ingredient" style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                          1 cup pureed zucchini chunks
                         </span>
                        </li>
                        <li class="ingredient" style="margin-bottom: 3px;">
                         <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                          1/3 cup coconut milk
                          <i>
                           (**You could use any milk in this or even water instead. I used coconut milk for it's creaminess.)
                          </i>
                         </span>
                        </li>
                       </ul>
                      </div>
                      <div id="instructions">
                       <div class="single_recipe_header" id="inst_header" style="color: #d61313; padding: 0px; text-decoration: none;">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         Instructions
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="instruction">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         Preheat your oven according to the brownie mix box's instructions.
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="instruction">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="instruction">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         In a large mixing bowl, combine the brownie mix, pureed zucchini, and coconut milk. Mix well.
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="instruction">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="instruction">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         Pour into a greased baking pan (I baked mine in a 9x9 pan.)
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="instruction">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         Bake according to the box's specifications.
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="instruction">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="instruction">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         Allow to cool completely and then cut into 9-12 squares, depending on how large of brownies you would like.
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="instruction">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         <br/>
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="instruction">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         Store any leftovers covered in the fridge for up to 5 days.
                        </span>
                       </div>
                       <div class="instruction">
                        <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">
                         Enjoy!
                        </span>
                       </div>
                      </div>
