        <h1 class="headline">
         Spurs vs FC Sheriff: Preview, Team News, Likely Line-Up, Key Man &amp; Predicted Score
        </h1>

        <div id="content-area">
         <p>
          <a href="http://football-talk.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/white-hart-lane-e1324215414846.jpg">
           <img alt="tottenham news" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-14465" height="362" src="http://football-talk.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/white-hart-lane-e1324215414846.jpg" width="475"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           Tottenham Hotspur welcome FC Sheriff to White Hart Lane tonight looking to cement their place in the knockout stages of the Europa League.
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Spurs have won all three fixtures in Group K so far and boast a five point lead over second placed Anzhi with Sheriff a further two points adrift, so all they need tonight is a draw to secure safe passage to the last 32 of the competition. For those who like to gamble, the home side are huge odd-on favourites with bookmakers such as
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <a href="http://www.sportsbettingonline.ag/" target="_blank">
            SportsBettingOnline.ag
           </a>
          </span>
          , so our tip would be to back Spurs at -2 to get any real value. Defoe to score at any time is also worth a look.
         </p>
         <div class="wpInsert wpInsertInPostAd wpInsertMiddle" style="margin-right: 10px;
float: left;">
          <!--/* FM AD: FTK-MPU-2 */-->
          <script type="text/javascript">
           if (!window.OX_ads) { OX_ads = []; }

          </script>
         </div>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Boss Andre Villas-Boas men have so far yet to concede a goal in any of their previous three games and beat the Moldovan champions 2-0 in the reverse fixture last month, so its going to be a tall order for the visitors to take anything away from the game tonight.
         </p>
         <h2>
          Team News
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          The latest team news from
          <a href="http://www.tottenhamhotspur.com/news/spurs-vs-fc-sheriff-team-news-061113/" target="_blank">
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            Tottenhams official website
           </span>
          </a>
          is that AVB will once again rotate his team in Europe with several changes expected from the side that drew 0-0 with Everton on Sunday.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Hugo Lloris has trained this week despite being knocked out at the weekend after a clash with Romelu Lukaku, but Brad Friedel is expected to start against Sheriff.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Michael Dawson, Jan Vertonghen, Paulinho, Andros Townsend and Roberto Soldado are expected to be rested, with the likes of Erik Lamela, Christian Eriksen, Aaron Lennon and Younes Kaboul coming in.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Etienne Capoue may make his first start in over two months in midfield with Sandro out after picking up a hamstring injury on Sunday.The Brazilian joins Danny Rose, Nacer Chadli and Zeko Fryers on the sidelines.
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          Jermaine Defoe should start up front as he looks to score one more goal to become Tottenhams
          <a href="http://www1.skysports.com/football/news/11675/9014339/jermain-defoe-credits-martin-chivers-after-breaking-tottenhams-european-goalscoring-record" target="_blank">
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            all-time leading goal-scorer in Europe
           </span>
          </a>
          .
         </p>
         <h2>
          Likely Line-Up
         </h2>
         <p>
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter" height="513" src="http://www.footballuser.com/formations/2013/11/868141_Tottenham_Hotspur.jpg" width="316"/>
         </p>
         <h2>
          Key Man
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           Jermaine Defoe:
          </strong>
          The striker has been deadly in the Europa League so far this season and will be well up for this game as he looks to become Tottenhams all-time leading scorer in Europe.
         </p>
         <h2 style="text-align: justify;">
          Predicted Score
         </h2>
         <p style="text-align: justify;">
          <strong>
           Tottenham 3-0 Sheriff:
          </strong>
          The north Londoners will have way too much for the Moldovan minnows to handle.
         </p>
