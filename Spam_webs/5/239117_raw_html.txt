         <div class="at-above-post-recommended addthis-toolbox at-wordpress-hide" data-title="Black Males Open Fire on 5 Year-Old White Girl. Wheres Sharpton and Jackson?" data-url="http://www.tpnn.com/2015/05/11/black-males-open-fire-on-5-year-old-white-girl-wheres-sharpton-and-jackson/">

         <div style="text-align: justify;">

          <p>
           <a href="http://www.tpnn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/ZLaylahSharpton.jpg">
            <img alt="ZLaylahSharpton" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-102734" height="358" src="http://www.tpnn.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/ZLaylahSharpton.jpg" width="562"/>

           A five year old girl who had done nothing wrong was wantonly murdered as she sat on the lap of her grandfather. You havent heard about it? Thats not surprising; while the Al Sharptons and the Jesse Jacksons of the world posture and bloviate about violence and a lack of respect for human life that permeates communities, they routinely keep quiet about attacks that happen every single day in America unless the victim was a person of color and there are television cameras pointed at them.
          </p>

         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          You see, the left isnt interested in telling the story of the murder of Laylah Petersen because Petersen doesnt fit the preferred narrative. What I mean is: Petersen was white.
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          While sitting on her grandfathers lap, a hail of bullets rang throughout the house in a mostly-black neighborhood in Milwaukee.
          <a href="http://conservativetribune.com/black-males-white-family/" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://conservativetribune.com/black-males-white-family/', 'Reports indicate that two black males');">
           Reports indicate that two black males
          </a>
          went to the door of the house and opened fire on the house that belonged to Laylahs grandparents.
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          The neighborhood in which Laylahs grandparents lived is 77% black and only 14% white.
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          Of course, nobody will ever knowthe truemotive behind this heinous crime; but then again, nobody bothered to ask about the motive before Sharpton, Jackson, Attorney General Eric Holder and the partisan media pounced on the shooting deaths of Trayvon Martin and Michael Brown.
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          All they knew is that a black person was killed by a white (or, in the case of Martin, a Hispanic) person. All other facts melted away in the early moments of rage and rhetoric and little Laylah Petersen lies dead and the race-hustlers and dividers dont give a darn.
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          Even if we account for the conflicting version of events surrounding the deaths of Martin and Brown, we can see that the issue is more-complicated than originally suspected. Brown had just robbed a store and allegedly attacked police officers. Martin had allegedly scuffled with George Zimmerman and went for Zimmermans gun.
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          What the hell could Laylah Petersen possibly have done? She was five years old and sitting on her grandfathers knee in the relative safety of her grandparents home. Was she a Crip from a rival set? Did she owe bookies money? Had she perpetrated a drive-by earlier in the day and this shooting was retaliation? All highly unlikely scenarios
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          So, I ask again: where are the supposed defenders of innocent life? Where are the race-hustlers demanding a full accounting of what is being done to see that justice is done?
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
         </div>
         <div style="text-align: justify;">
          Somehow, I cant help but believe that this would receive more attention if Laylah wasnt white.
