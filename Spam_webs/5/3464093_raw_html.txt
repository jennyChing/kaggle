        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Neptune Necklace
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <a class="entry-date" href="http://www.modabakeshop.com/2009/03/neptune-necklace.html" title="March 4 2009">
          <time datetime="2009-03-04T00:01:00+00:00" pubdate="">
           Mar
           <b>
            4
           </b>
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="entry-byline">
          by
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.modabakeshop.com/author/hhickman" title="View all posts by Holly Hickman">
            Holly Hickman
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>

       <div class="entry-content">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5309008787776287282" src="http://modafabrics.files.wordpress.com/2009/03/54417-finishednecklace.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://www.modabakeshop.com/2012/09/rotary-cutter-ratings.html" target="_blank">
          <img align="absBottom" alt="" border="0" height="52" src="http://www.modabakeshop.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/MBS-rotary-ratings-easy.jpg" width="250"/>
         </a>
         <br/>
         <span id="more-16776">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" src="http://www.unitednotions.com/title_ingredients.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <div>
         <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5309004313690713458" src="http://modafabrics.files.wordpress.com/2009/03/15218-supplies1.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <div>
         1 charm pack  featured Neptune by Tula Pink for Moda
        </div>
        <div>
         13  20 mm wood beads
        </div>
        <div>
         glue stick
        </div>
        <div>
         48- 3/8 grosgrain ribbon
        </div>
        <div>
         heavy duty needle with large eye
        </div>
        <div>
         Awl
        </div>
        <div>
         Scissors
        </div>
        <div>
         <img alt="" src="http://www.unitednotions.com/title_instructions.jpg"/>
        </div>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5309004032539683266" src="http://modafabrics.files.wordpress.com/2009/03/9f56c-cuttingcharms.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         Pick out 13 prints (small patterns work best).
         <br/>
         Cut into quarters.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5309004303116109314" src="http://modafabrics.files.wordpress.com/2009/03/422d6-glue.jpg"/>
         \
        </p>
        <p>
         Apply a
         <span class="blsp-spelling-corrected" id="SPELLING_ERROR_1">
          generous
         </span>
         amount of glue to the back of fabric.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5309004028591168802" src="http://modafabrics.files.wordpress.com/2009/03/87fe1-clipoffexcess.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         Roll bead in the middle of fabric.
         <br/>
         Cover entire bead with fabric.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5309011715854745170" src="http://modafabrics.files.wordpress.com/2009/03/84269-cutofexcess.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Clip off excess fabric from each end.
         <br/>
         Leave enough fabric to be tucked into hole of bead.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5309004308474966306" src="http://modafabrics.files.wordpress.com/2009/03/606f0-pokeholeinbead.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Smooth fabric up the bead and tuck into hole of bead.
         <br/>
         Use an awl (I used the end of a paint brush) to push fabric into hole and hollow out hole.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" border="0" id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5309004027792675986" src="http://modafabrics.files.wordpress.com/2009/03/31941-beadsonribbon.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Thread grosgrain ribbon on to heavy duty needle.
         <br/>
         Tie a double knot 12 from the end of ribbon.
         <br/>
         Thread bead onto ribbon.
         <br/>
         Next make a knot.
         <br/>
         Thread another bead on.
         <br/>
         Repeat steps until all beads have been threaded onto ribbon.
         <br/>
         Tie a double knot after the last bead.
        </p>
        <p>
         Your finished!
         <br/>
         <img alt="" src="http://www.unitednotions.com/title_yield.jpg"/>
         <br/>
         4 necklaces or 1 super long necklace.
        </p>

        </p>
