       <h1 style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         State Bank of India (SBI) Latest SBI Bank PO JobsRecruitment 2015 for Various 2096 Post @ www.sbi.co.in.
        </strong>
       </h1>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        If you are unemployed or want to change your job and willing to work in banking sector then its a good time to start, State Bank of India has published
        <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
         <strong>
          latest banking PO jobs hiringnotification 2015-2016
         </strong>
        </span>
        for
        <em>
         SBI Bank PO Jobs Recruitment 2015
        </em>
        and invite online applications for 2096 posts(2000 posts for Probationary Officers and 96 for Specialist Officers). SBI Bank is well known name in
        <strong>
         Indian Banking Sector Industry
        </strong>
        that is owned by government and have thousands of branches in every part of India and main headquarter setup in Mumbai, Maharashtra. State bank now recruiting 2096 officers all over india.For Probationary Officers, last date to apply online is 02 May 2015 and for Specialist Officers last date is 27 April 2015. If you are eligible according to job description(check below) and want to work in
        <em>
         banking sector
        </em>
        then go through the apply link from the post.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        SBI Bank PO JobsRecruitment 2015 Total Vacancy:
       </h2>
       <ol>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Probationary Officers (POs):
         </strong>
         2000 Posts
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Specialist Officers:
         </strong>
         96 Posts
        </li>
       </ol>
       <p>
        Posts are reserved for
        <strong>
         SC/ST/Person with Disability (PWD)
        </strong>
        category according to government criteria. Candidates applying for reserved postshave to submit their certificate along with the other essential details defined by
        <em>
         Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment
        </em>
        . Candidates applying under Person with Disability (PWD) category should have minimum 40 percent disability level. For more information on name and number of jobs for reserved category,visit State Bank of India website and check advertisement notification.
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="color: #ff0000;">
         <a href="http://recruitmentsandresults.com/latest-jobs-openings-recruitment-updates/" rel="nofollow" style="color: #ff0000;" target="_blank" title="Check Latest Jobs Openings 2015 Table">

           Check Latest Jobs Openings Table
          </strong>
         </a>
        </span>

       <h3>
        Essential Qualifications for SBI Bank PO JobsRecruitment 2015:
       </h3>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Probationary Officers Posts
         </span>
         :
        </strong>
        Candidates who hold graduation in any course/discipline are eligible for the above listed posts, and candidates who holdany qualification or authorized course that is equivalent to graduation can also apply for the listed vacancies. All the students who are pursuing in their final semester of graduation can apply too, but only passesgraduate studentsas on 01 Nov 2015 are applicable to join if they also clear the selection process.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          Specialist Officers Posts
         </span>
         :
        </strong>
        <strong>
         M.D/M.S/M.B.B.S
        </strong>
        passed candidates can apply for Chief Medical Officer post, Post
        <strong>
         Graduation in Economics/MBA/PGDBA/PGDM
        </strong>
        passed candidates are eligible for Chief Manager Post, who did Post-Graduation in any specialization or CA(Chartered Accountant) with minimum 3 yrs experience can apply for manager post.For more details on required qualification for various jobs post, visit the
        <em>
         State Bank of India website
        </em>
        i.e.
        <a href="http://www.statebankofindia.com" rel="nofollow" target="_blank" title="State Bank of India Jobs Opening 2015">
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          <strong>
           www.statebankofindia.com
          </strong>
         </span>
        </a>
        and click on 
        <em>
         career with us
        </em>
         and download official advertisement notification.

       <h3>
        Age Limit and Application Fees Details for SBI Bank Recruitment 2015:
       </h3>
       <p>
        For SBI Bank PO Jobs, candidate minimum age must be 21 years and maximum age can be 30 years as on April 01, 2015. For reserved category applicants age rules can be compensate according to government criteria.
       </p>
       <p>
        Applicantsapplying inreserved category have to deposit 100rs and for general and others application fees is 600rs, fees in non refundable once you have deposited it.
       </p>

       </p>
       <h3>
        State Bank of India Recruitment 2015 Selection Procedure:
       </h3>
       <p>
        Selection will be made in 3 phases. In Phase-1 candidates have to attend Preliminary Examination that will be online. In Phase-2, objective and descriptive test. Cutoff marks obtained candidates will be short list for phase-3 where shortlisted candidates have to face group discussion followed by interview.
       </p>
