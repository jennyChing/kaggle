         <p align="center">
          <i>
           Disclosure: This is a sponsored post written by me on behalf of the Institute of Vibrant Living, all opinions are 100% my own.

         <p>
          Happy Saturday everyone! Do you have any fun fall-ish plans for the weekend? I cant believe how chilly it already is, which makes me nervous about the weather next month.
         </p>
         <p>
          As you may already know we are just 3 weeks away from our wedding, which means it is time for me to kick it into high gear! You may have noticed a lack in posts this week, but I have decided that I need to shift around some priorities to make sure things get finished before we head up to North Carolina for our wedding. Planning a wedding in 6 months has not been an easy task and I hope you will understand :)
         </p>
         <p>
          Although I will continue to post every week, I wont be posting as often and may even show more photos of what I have worked on, and then post the tutorials after the wedding. So the next 3 weeks will be spent finishing up furniture projects, finalizing some wedding plans {&amp;working on as many projects as I can!!}, and getting our house in order before we leave for our wedding/honeymoon.
         </p>
         <p>
          I was also brought on board to help answer questions and book weddings at the
          <a href="http://upcycledtreasures.com/2013/04/rustic-chic-cabin-wedding-venue/" title="Our Rustic-Chic Cabin Wedding Venue">
           cabin venue
          </a>
          we are getting married at, which means my inbox stays pretty full, and I have A LOT of details to put together for the new website and brides-to-be.
         </p>
         <p>
          With that said, my plate is pretty full and its not always easy keeping up with it all. Thats why when the folks over at the Institute of Vibrant Living offered to send me samples of their All Day Energy Greens drink mixture, I was all for it! Lets face it Im a vegetarian, a bride, and pretty much always running around, so getting the nutrients I need is not always easy.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="all-day-energy-greens-product" class="aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/upcycledtreasures.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/all-day-energy-greens-product.jpg?resize=363%2C480"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          I recently received the ADEG samples and am pleasantly surprised with it! Today I am going to share with you what I think about this
          <a href="http://www.ivlproducts.com/Superfoods/All-Day-Energy-Greens-174---The-Best-Energy-Supplement.axd?utm_source=sverve&amp;utm_medium=link&amp;utm_content=upcycledtreasures&amp;utm_campaign=sverve-ADEG" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.ivlproducts.com/Superfoods/All-Day-Energy-Greens-174---The-Best-Energy-Supplement.axd?utm_source=sverve&amp;utm_medium=link&amp;utm_content=upcycledtreasures&amp;utm_campaign=sverve-ADEG', 'green drink']);">
           green drink
          </a>
          , as well as a delicious smoothie recipe that you can use.
         </p>
         <p>
          <b>
           A little info about All Day Energy Greens {ADEG}:
          </b>
         </p>
         <p>
          This Green drink is packed with vitamins and nutrients, 38 super foods to be exact! Its no wonder that your body feels full and your energy level naturally goes up.
         </p>
         <blockquote>
          <p>
           All Day Energy Greens (ADEG) by Institute of Vibrant Living, is a dietary supplement that exceeds the nutritional equivalent of five servings of fruits and vegetables. The ingredients make it the most potent, energizing, immune-enhancing green drink on the market. Best of all its been helping people lead healthier lives, have more energy, and lose weight.
          </p>
         </blockquote>
         <p>
          When I first received my sample packets (you can also order it by the canister}, I noticed that the directions say it can be added to water, juice, or your favorite smoothie. I added the mixture to a bottle of Aquafina, shook it up really good for about a minute and then drank some of it. I am the girl that orders wheat grass shots and actually enjoys the taste of healthy food. If you are like this than you would probably enjoy drinking the mixture with water alone.
         </p>
         <p>
          However, I will admit that even for me the mixture has a bit of a wheat smell and although it was good, I wanted to see if there was a less hardcore way to drink it.
          <b>
           Side Note:
          </b>
          My fiance really enjoyed drinking it this way and basically replaced his red bull addiction with this green drink and has nothing but positive things to say about it :)
         </p>
         <p>
          The next thing I tried was to mix the green drink with orange juice. I actually really liked it this way, and I didnt use my blender, just a fork to mix it all up. You would want to stir it for a good minute or so or even just throw it in your blender to make sure its all mixed up.This was quick, easy and delicious!
         </p>
         <p>
          The final way I tried this drink was as a smoothie and this is by far my favorite. Here is what I used:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           1 Cup Apple Juice
          </li>
          <li>
           1 Banana
          </li>
          <li>
           4 Strawberries
          </li>
          <li>
           1 Serving of All Day Energy Greens
          </li>
          <li>
           1 Cup Ice
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <img alt="all-day-energy-greens-smoothie" class="aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/upcycledtreasures.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/all-day-energy-greens-smoothie.png?resize=544%2C363"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          I blended everything together and voila, a delicious, nutritious smoothie that was super easy to make and is packed with wholesome goodness.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="green-drink-smoothie-recipe-1" class="aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/upcycledtreasures.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/green-drink-smoothie-recipe-1.png?resize=360%2C540"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="ADEG-smoothie-recipe" class="aligncenter" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/upcycledtreasures.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/ADEG-smoothie-recipe.png?resize=426%2C346"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          This smoothie is perfect for someone low on time, on the go, or looking for a boost of energy. This actually made enough for 2 full glasses which I would then split with Eric. However, I noticed that I was able to keep my energy up the best when I split the smoothie up into 3 small glasses throughout the day :)
         </p>
         <p>
          So that about sums up my experience with this
          <a href="http://www.ivlproducts.com/Superfoods/All-Day-Energy-Greens-174---The-Best-Energy-Supplement.axd?utm_source=sverve&amp;utm_medium=link&amp;utm_content=upcycledtreasures&amp;utm_campaign=sverve-ADEG" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.ivlproducts.com/Superfoods/All-Day-Energy-Greens-174---The-Best-Energy-Supplement.axd?utm_source=sverve&amp;utm_medium=link&amp;utm_content=upcycledtreasures&amp;utm_campaign=sverve-ADEG', 'green drink']);" target="_blank">
           green drink
