         <p>
          Crossword Puzzles are a great way to keep your guests entertained at a bridal shower or farewell brunch. You can have them customized to include several fun family facts or questions unique to the bride and groom. You can also have prizes (favors) for the table who is the first to complete the puzzle. This is a great way to get the families to interact and later, allow bragging rights as the winners for the next family function.
         </p>
