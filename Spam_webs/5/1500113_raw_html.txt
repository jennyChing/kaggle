        <p>
         <a href="http://play-activities.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Family-goals-and-priorities-Feb.jpg">
          <img alt="Family goals and priorities Feb" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6686 pin-it" height="460" src="http://play-activities.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/Family-goals-and-priorities-Feb.jpg" width="610"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Although I love making goals I need some accountability in reaching them.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im sure Im not alone there. Since Im a big believer in scheduling things to get it done Im also good at rescheduling and bumping myself of my own schedule. Just to be transparent. So in a bid to get more of the positive things I want and dream for the family started Ive joined a new linky where we share our monthly goals.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Started
         </strong>
         is a better way ( instead of word of the year) for me in 2015. Many times I flesh out an entire project or idea and something happens and I cant see how it can fit anymore. The whole thing gets benched. But if I get started then there is a chance to continue. When I stop its not a stop it is a pause. I can dive right back in. With this in mind, all the goals are things I want to do but most of all
         <strong>
          get started
         </strong>
         , keep going and be consistent. If I dont keep going I can circle back around and once I get past keeping going then the point is to remain consistent.
        </p>
        <p>
         I dont have a hard time with this system although it might seem like a get out of jail free card. Our home life ebbs and flows depends on work, health, life and the kids. While there are some rigid things theres also plenty of flexibility. The problem is keeping account of where you deduct you time from when youre flexible.
        </p>
        <p>
         Im going to share two areas; family goals and personal goals.
        </p>
        <p>
         Id designed the
         <a href="http://raisingplayfultots.com/sppp" target="_blank">
          Simple Parenting and Play Planner
         </a>
         out of the need to have more of our family dreams and ideas a chance to actually come to light. So Ill sometimes share what Im doing with my planner too.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <h2>
         Family goals
        </h2>
        <p>
         Here Ill talk about the children and as parents the parenting things well do.
        </p>
        <ul>
         <li>
          Find a
          <a href="http://amzn.to/1yBNzJC">
           just right pillar candle
          </a>
          for dinner. We had one before we moved but you cant take candles with you when you move overseas. I just noticed that its missing from our dinner ritual.
         </li>
         <li>
          Work with the kids for a better and more effective set up and clean up at dinner time. Its almost there but a few key parts are missed. Im really looking for pride in doing a good job because we stopped to observe that
          <em>
           it was complete
          </em>
          , not that it was done perfectly. Many times I call the boys back for something because they got distracted and didnt finish. When I call them back and say,  Can you see something thats not right? they can. I want to see fewer call backs and them saying a mantra or doing something to do that final look over. So when I ask them that question they can say no. Done not perfect is what I think.
         </li>
         <li>
          Read aloud before the bus in the morning:
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0544104854/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=9325&amp;creativeASIN=0544104854&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=playactiv-20&amp;linkId=W6AXALQSB3BJLBSX">

          <br/>
          <a href="http://amzn.to/1CWn1HU">
