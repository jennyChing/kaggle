        <p>
         I know I said I would stop with the red and green but apparently I was totally lying. Sorry.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/chocolate-ruffle-cake.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-39808" data-pin-description="Chocolate Curls Christmas Cake ~ http://iambaker.net" height="515" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/chocolate-ruffle-cake.jpg" title="Christmas Cake with Chocolate Ruffle Curls" width="420"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         I made a white cake, tinted some frosting red and green, then melted some chocolate. Have you ever made chocolate curls? I had tried once before. It was a
         <a href="http://iambaker.net/triple-chocolate-mousse-cake/" target="_blank">
          major fail
         </a>
         . I am pretty sure there were even some comments mocking my skills, and if they werent totally right I
         <em>
          might
         </em>
         have been upset.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/chocolate-ruffle-cake-1.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-39809" data-pin-description="Chocolate Curls Christmas Cake ~ http://iambaker.net" height="536" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/chocolate-ruffle-cake-1.jpg" title="Christmas Cake with Chocolate Ruffle Curls" width="420"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Ok, so looks like I still have not mastered this technique. I could not get one single perfect swirly curl.
        </p>
        <p>
         I blame the red candy melts I used. And the warm December weather. And that one boy in 2nd grade who called me 4-eyes. *cough*
        </p>
        <p>
         Clearly I have issues.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/chocolate-ruffle-cake-2.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-39810" data-pin-description="Chocolate Curls Christmas Cake ~ http://iambaker.net" height="479" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/chocolate-ruffle-cake-2.jpg" title="Chocolate Ruffles on a Christmas Inspired White Cake" width="420"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         In addition to sharing this sweet little Christmas cake with you, I also wanted toshare a couple other cakes that I think you would enjoy!
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <a href="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/chocolate-ruffle-cake-3.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter wp-image-39811" data-pin-description="Chocolate Curls Christmas Cake ~ http://iambaker.net" height="521" src="http://cf.iambaker.net/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/chocolate-ruffle-cake-3.jpg" title="The Perfect Slice of Christmas Cake" width="420"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">

        </p>
