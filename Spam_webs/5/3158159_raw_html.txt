         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full" height="500" src="http://hudabeauty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/b_20120327032325000000.jpg" title=" Eucerin Calming Creme Daily moisturizer" width="500"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://hudabeauty.com/2012/10/21/drugstore-product-of-the-week-71/&amp;media=http://hudabeauty.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/b_20120327032325000000.jpg&amp;description=Drugstore Product of the Week!')">
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        As much as I love my sweet scented moisturizers, there are days that I look for something without any scent but something still packed with super hydration! I recently started using
        <span id="more-19009">
        </span>
        the Eucerin Calming Creme Daily Moisturizer, and I found it particularly good for super dry areas! Its great because it has literally no scent and it makes your skin feel SUPER soft! I was actually recommended this by a friend who has eczema and she said this helped her dry skin dramatically!
        <strong>
         Its super gentle and you can literally apply this head to toe and feel great!
        </strong>
        I also liked it because most moisturizers and body washes break me out and this one only left me with beautiful soft skin! My only complaint is the container, not a fan of it! It seems more appropriate for a hand cream than body lotion, but it still works fabulously!
        <strong>
         If you have sensitive skin, meet your new BFF!
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <em>
         Available at most pharmacies and supermarkets
        </em>
