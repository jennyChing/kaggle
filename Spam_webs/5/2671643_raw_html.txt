               <p>
                Social media is an essential part of a successful digital marketing strategy. Salesforce came up with a helpful
                <a href="https://www.salesforce.com/ca/blog/2015/03/twitter-tips.html">
                 infographic
                </a>
                with tips to take your companys Twitter presence to the next level. This infographic shows you best practices for tweeting, how to optimize your Twitter profile (personal or business) and how to boost engagement among your followers.
               </p>
               <p>
                Here are some of the tips mentioned in the infographic:
               </p>
               <ul>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  The winning tweet structure:
                 </strong>
                 follow this structure to get more clicks, retweets and favorites on your tweet:
                 <strong>
                  [Key Message] - [Link] [#Hashtag]
                 </strong>
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Keep it short and sweet
                 </strong>
                  try to use about 100 characters in your tweet so followers can retweet and add their own thoughts.
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Dont keep it strictly business
                 </strong>
                  mix in some funny or interesting content to show off your brands personality.
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Dont just tweet headlines
                 </strong>
                  be more creative when sharing an article on Twitter by adding your own opinion, pulling a quote or asking a question to engage your followers.
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Be visual
                 </strong>
                  include an image or video in your tweet to increase retweets by up to 35%.
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Use hashtags sparingly
                 </strong>
                  hashtags are a great way to categorize your tweets and make them more visible in Twitter search results. But dont go overboard with them use 1-3 relevant hashtags per tweet.
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Engage!
                 </strong>
                  start interacting with your followers, people you follow, key influencers and thought leaders in your industry and they will engage back!
                </li>
                <li>
                 <strong>
                  Understand what works (and what doesnt) 
                 </strong>
                 use Twitter Analytics to see how many impressions, favorites, retweets, replies and link clicks your tweets got. Track the performance of your tweets to determine the best type of content and times of day to post.
                </li>
               </ul>
               <p>
                Check out the full infographic below for even more helpful Twitter hints:
               </p>
               <p>
                <a href="https://www.salesforce.com/ca/blog/2015/03/twitter-tips.html">

                </a>
               </p>
