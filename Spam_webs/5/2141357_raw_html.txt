       <h1 class="entry-title single-title">
        <span>
         #FADSWinterWarmer
        </span>
       </h1>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-media">
       <img alt="FADS Winter Warmer" class="attachment-big-size wp-post-image" height="522" src="http://blog.fads.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/fads-winter-warmer-2.jpg" width="918"/>
      </div>
      <div class="wrapper">
       <div class="grids">

         <div class="single-box clearfix entry-content">
          <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
          <p>
           To coin a phrase from Game of Thrones Winter is Coming. But this doesnt just mean frozen pipes, snowdrifts and having to de-ice the car every morning. Winter can be one of the cosiest and most enjoyable times of year, especially if youre snug and warm at home.
          </p>
          <p>
           Weve been thinking what makes us happy at home and remembering everything we do as the nights draw in and the temperature drops. The comfiest room in the house for us has to be the lounge; what better way to stave off the misery and cold than snuggling up on
           <a href="http://www.fads.co.uk/sofas/two-seater-sofas/fabric/forester-grey-fabric-2-seater-sofa-with-pocket-sprung-base/?r=15">
            this 2-seater sofa
           </a>
           with a loved one?
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://blog.fads.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/forester-grey-fabric-2-seater-sofa-with-pocket-sprung-base_1377182528.jpg">
            <img alt="forester-grey-fabric-2-seater-sofa-with-pocket-sprung-base_1377182528" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-140" src="http://blog.fads.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/forester-grey-fabric-2-seater-sofa-with-pocket-sprung-base_1377182528.jpg" style="width:100%;"/>
           </a>
           A roaring fire, mugs of hot chocolate and a softly lit room deters from the cold and probably awful weather outside your doors. Weve been so inspired we have put together our cosiest ideas onto our #FADSWinterWarmer board on Pinterest.
          </p>
          <p>
           Big cushions, draping curtains and warm oranges, golds and firey colours create that Summer feel wed be missing once Christmas starts to approach. But its a time for families to gather, children young and old to get excited and the big duvets to be brought out. Maybe youll be buying bigger dressing gowns and big fluffy slippers?
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://blog.fads.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/kelly-hoppen-vintage-flock-burnt-orange-patterned-damask-wallpaper_1320671431.jpg">
            <img alt="kelly-hoppen-vintage-flock-burnt-orange-patterned-damask-wallpaper_1320671431" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-141" src="http://blog.fads.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/kelly-hoppen-vintage-flock-burnt-orange-patterned-damask-wallpaper_1320671431.jpg" style="width:100%;"/>
           </a>
           We arent suggesting you have to redecorate your whole house to create a cosy room, but this fantastic
           <a href="http://www.fads.co.uk/wallpaper/pattern/kelly-hoppen-vintage-flock-burnt-orange-damask-wallpaper/?r=0">
            Kelly Hoppen wallpaper
           </a>
           would create a perfect base colour with which to have a warm room to hide away in.
          </p>
          <p>
           <b>
            Competition
           </b>
          </p>
          <p>
           We know the sudden change from Summer into Autumn and then Winter can be a huge shock to the system, so to ease the pain were giving away 100 to spend online on our website.
          </p>
          <p>
           Simply create your own #FADSWinterWarmer room on Pinterest as we have done
           <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/fadsuk/fadswinterwarmer/" target="_blank">
            here
           </a>
           using FADS products as the base of your room, then add your own little touches and pins to it; title the board #FADSWinterWarmer and share it with all your Pinterest followers!
          </p>
          <p>
           Your board needs to contain at least three items from the FADS.co.uk website, our competition pin and be titled #FADSWinterWarmer. Let us know about the great board youve created by commenting at the bottom of this post with your boards URL. Tag each pin with #FADSWinterWarmer &amp; #Winter so we can see all you boards growing and the fun youre having putting them together!
          </p>
          <p>
           If youre a blogger why not write about the inspiration into your room and inspire other people to share their ideas online. Weve built a lounge but you dont have to, how about a bedroom, study or even your kitchen; what better way to be cosy then by a warm oven? Happy pinning and good luck!
          </p>
