       <p class="main_image">
        <a href="/article/Design/Interiors/Eabarker92/QUEEN-BEE-HOME">

       <h2 class="margin-bottom" itemprop="summary">
        A quaint shop filled with wonderful things for your home...
       </h2>
       <p class="one_article" itemprop="description">
        Hidden down one of the many Roman side streets of Canterbury, this Aladdin's cave harbors gorgeous items for the inside of your abode.  

Teeming with distressed furniture bursting at the seams with character and charm, this shop is the perfect place to visit if you are looking for something unique and quirky for your home. 

One of my favourite items in there at the moment is a distressed teal coloured piece of furniture that could be described as a cabinet or wardrobe of some kind.Alas, I do believe it has been sold, and am slightly envious of the new owner. 

Amongst the beautifully restored furniture are little accessories such as scented candles, wonderful cushions with hares, stags and other woodland creatures on and art by the local artist Kate Knight. 

Knight's work incorporates creatures along with a sense of desire and contrasting elements, creating images that are in a sense; tragically beautiful. 

A pair of stag antlers attached to a plaque and painted in eggshell adorn a wall. Vision these on your wall as a jewellery holder for necklaces &amp; bracelets.

Unique and beautiful items fill this shop, creating probably the first cosy Alladin's cave.
       </p>
