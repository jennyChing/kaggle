Find out with the Heart Age Calculator on shoppersdrugmart.ca! #TreatYourHeart" width="580"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/2014/02/06/heart-age-calculator-shoppersdrugmart-ca/&amp;media=http://i1109.photobucket.com/albums/h430/OurFamilyWorld/Brands/SDM%20Heart%20Health/HeartAge_zps2c7e3348.jpg&amp;description=How Old is Your Heart Find out with the Heart Age Calculator on shoppersdrugmart.ca! #TreatYourHeart | http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/2014/02/06/heart-age-calculator-shoppersdrugmart-ca/')">
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        While you should be taking care of your heart all year round, February is a great month to really take a look at your overall heart health and see where you could use a little extra help. Im going to be completely honest here: I really dont pay enough attention to my heart health. Although my grandmother died from heart disease, it didnt happen until she was close to 80. At 38, I figure I have a lot of time to worry about it. Thats a horrible outlook, though, so Im vowing to start paying attention now! After all, heart disease remains one of the leading causes of death in Canada and the United States.
       </p>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <b>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          Get Heart-Smart with the Heart Age Calculator Provided by Shoppers Drug Mart
         </span>
         <br/>
        </b>
       </h3>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt=" Heart Age Calculator on shoppersdrugmart.ca! #TreatYourHeart" class="aligncenter" height="419" src="http://i1109.photobucket.com/albums/h430/OurFamilyWorld/Brands/SDM%20Heart%20Health/SS1_zps08fd8764.jpg" title=" Heart Age Calculator on shoppersdrugmart.ca! #TreatYourHeart" width="553"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/2014/02/06/heart-age-calculator-shoppersdrugmart-ca/&amp;media=http://i1109.photobucket.com/albums/h430/OurFamilyWorld/Brands/SDM%20Heart%20Health/SS1_zps08fd8764.jpg&amp;description=How Old is Your Heart Find out with the Heart Age Calculator on shoppersdrugmart.ca! #TreatYourHeart | http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/2014/02/06/heart-age-calculator-shoppersdrugmart-ca/')">
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Getting heart-smart starts with looking at the factors of your lifestyle that affect the age of your heart. As I said, Im 38 in chronological years. That doesnt mean I have the heart of a 38-year-old woman. Canadian Cardiovascular Society recommends that everyone know the age of their heart, that way you can see where you need improving. In fact, research shows that arming yourself with this knowledge may lead to making better choices and better managing your overall heart health.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        I took the
        <span style="color: #0000ff;">
         <a href="http://adserver.adtechus.com/?adlink|3.0|5282|2788943|1|16|AdId=5162565;BnId=1;" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           Heart Age Calculator
          </span>
         </a>
        </span>
        test provided by Shoppers Drug Mart to find out just how old my ticker really is. The Heart Age Calculator is an easy-to-use online tool that helps Canadians find out the true age of their heart. Just answer the questions on the screen-
        <b>
         honestly-
        </b>
        and youll discover your cardiovascular age as well as your risk of heart disease over the next 10 years. This tool is the
        <b>
         only
        </b>
        scientifically validated online tool of its kind that offers a personalized heart age and risk profile as recommended by the Canadian Cardiovascular Society Guidelines.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Wondering why I bolded the word honestly? Well, here some more honesty of my own: I lied throughout the entire test. I wanted to come across as this vision of health. After all, Im a medical writer and former nursing student. Heres a little secret, though: those of us who teach healthy practices are usually
        <b>
         the worst
        </b>
        at following our own advice.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt=" Heart Age Calculator on shoppersdrugmart.ca! #TreatYourHeart" class="aligncenter" height="375" src="http://i1109.photobucket.com/albums/h430/OurFamilyWorld/Brands/SDM%20Heart%20Health/Myresults_zps56af50e1.jpg" title=" Heart Age Calculator on shoppersdrugmart.ca! #TreatYourHeart" width="543"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/2014/02/06/heart-age-calculator-shoppersdrugmart-ca/&amp;media=http://i1109.photobucket.com/albums/h430/OurFamilyWorld/Brands/SDM%20Heart%20Health/Myresults_zps56af50e1.jpg&amp;description=How Old is Your Heart Find out with the Heart Age Calculator on shoppersdrugmart.ca! #TreatYourHeart | http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/2014/02/06/heart-age-calculator-shoppersdrugmart-ca/')">
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        With my lies, my heart age was 35.1. A full three years younger than my real age! Then I told the truth. I didnt fudge my weight, exercise levels or anything else. You know, it really wasnt that bad! My true heart age: 39.1.
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
         <img alt=" Heart Age Calculator on shoppersdrugmart.ca! #TreatYourHeart" class="aligncenter" height="375" src="http://i1109.photobucket.com/albums/h430/OurFamilyWorld/Brands/SDM%20Heart%20Health/TrueResults_zps9beb6e1a.jpg" title=" Heart Age Calculator on shoppersdrugmart.ca! #TreatYourHeart" width="564"/>
         <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/2014/02/06/heart-age-calculator-shoppersdrugmart-ca/&amp;media=http://i1109.photobucket.com/albums/h430/OurFamilyWorld/Brands/SDM%20Heart%20Health/TrueResults_zps9beb6e1a.jpg&amp;description=How Old is Your Heart Find out with the Heart Age Calculator on shoppersdrugmart.ca! #TreatYourHeart | http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/2014/02/06/heart-age-calculator-shoppersdrugmart-ca/')">
         </span>
        </span>
       </p>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Thats less than a year older than I am, since Ill be 39 in July. The moral of this story: dont lie because it does nothing to help you learn about your true heart age and, really, the truth may not be as bad as you think!
       </p>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <b>
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          Understanding your heart age score
         </span>
        </b>
       </h3>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        See, the reason my heart age didnt come out much higher than I thought it would is because I really dont have a lot of risk factors for heart disease. Granted, I dont eat as well as I should and I exercise far less than I should. On the other hand, my blood pressure pretty much rocks and my cholesterol levels are great. Your true heart age is determined by many factors, including: weight, waist size, activity levels, whether or not you smoke, prior history of heart disease, diabetes, and other factors. Some of those factors carry more weight than others.
       </p>
       <h3 style="text-align: justify;">
        <span style="color: #ff0000;">
         <b>
          Tips to improve and maintain your heart health
         </b>
        </span>
       </h3>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        Just because my heart is healthier than I thought it may be doesnt mean I can start slacking on its care! Here are a few tips to maintain your heart health.
       </p>
       <ul style="text-align: justify;">
        <li>
         If you smoke, stop!
        </li>
        <li>
         Monitor your blood pressure
        </li>
        <li>
         Live an active lifestyle. Start small if you have to, just increase your levels of activity until it becomes a part of your daily routine.
        </li>
        <li>
         Regulate your Cholesterol Levels
        </li>
        <li>
         Take Control: this means applying all the above tips, visiting your doctor with any concerns you have and getting proactive about your heart health.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p style="text-align: justify;">
        <strong>
         Its time to get Heart Healthy and encourage other Canadians to do the same. The first step: find out your hearts true age with the Heart Age Calculator. I did it, so can you! Come back and tell us how it went! Were you surprised by your results?
        </strong>
       </p>
