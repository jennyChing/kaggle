       <h2>
        Am I Dreaming?
       </h2>
       <div style="color: #000; line-height: 1.75;">
        <img alt="Knigssee, Bavaria" class="featurePhoto" data-inbucketlist="False" src="http://media.dreamplango.com/resources/dpg/Feature/1fdf4c2d-dfb7-4dff-be17-b76ca52f0ef4.jpg" title="Knigssee, Bavaria"/>
        <div class="photoCredit">
         Shutterstock
        </div>
        <b>
         Knigssee, Bavaria|
        </b>
        In the mountainous terrain of Southern Germanys Bavaria (Bayern) province, Knigssee has left generations of travelers, artists and writers spellbound. Though the lakes exceptional clarity is often noted, its really Knigssees setting  surrounded by rugged, snow-capped mountains and alpine forests  that makes it one of the most beautiful places in the world. The pilgrimage church of St. Bartholom, nestled into this stunning panorama, is only accessible by boat or via hiking trails around the lake. Knigssee is easily accessible from nearby Berchtesgaden, Germany, but is also a relatively short drive from Salzburg, Austria.
