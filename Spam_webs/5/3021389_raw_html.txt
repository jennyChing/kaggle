       <h1 class="title">
        A.C.E. Sports Pilot 250cc Off-Road Go Kart
       </h1>
       <p class="post-meta">
        <img alt="" src="http://www.geekalerts.com/wp-content/themes/geekalerts6/images/ico-time.png"/>
        November 23, 2007
        <span class="comments">
         <img alt="" src="http://www.geekalerts.com/wp-content/themes/geekalerts6/images/ico-comment.png"/>

       <div class="entry">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <img alt="A.C.E. Sports Pilot" height="362" src="http://www.geekalerts.com/u/ace-pilot.jpg" title="A.C.E. Sports Pilot" width="393"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Sometimes you really wish you were living a bit outside the city center, like when reading about cool toys on wheels such as this little beauty.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-948">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Pilot 250 is powered by a 250cc liquid-cooled engine that provides a top speed of 46 mph (74 kmh) and a consistent power and high torque, providing the perfect combination of high performance and outstanding features.
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          The fully independent front and rear dual a-arm suspension with sharp handling ensures a smooth comfortable ride.
         </p>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         <img alt="A.C.E. Sports Pilot" class="border" height="309" src="http://www.geekalerts.com/u/ace-pilot2.jpg" title="A.C.E. Sports Pilot" width="400"/>
        </p>
        <blockquote>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Features:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           Headlights with Hi-Lo beams
          </li>
          <li>
           Speedometer, Odometer and Tachometer
          </li>
          <li>
           Horn, 12v power plug
          </li>
          <li>
           Turn signals and Brake light
          </li>
          <li>
           Center rear view mirror
          </li>
          <li>
           Sealed battery with larger capacity
          </li>
          <li>
           Large 4-gallon gas tank with roll over protection
          </li>
          <li>
           All wheel fenders
          </li>
          <li>
           2 rear trailer hitch receiver
          </li>
          <li>
           Cast ductile iron rear knuckles with sealed bearings
          </li>
          <li>
           Bucket seats w/headrests
          </li>
          <li>
           Automotive grade paint finish &amp; UV protection
          </li>
          <li>
           Tool kit
          </li>
          <li>
           Dimensions: 90L x 62W x 63H
          </li>
          <li>
           Dry weight: Approximately 650 lbs.
          </li>
         </ul>
        </blockquote>
        <p>
         The
         <a href="http://www.costco.com/Browse/Product.aspx?Prodid=11247458">
          A.C.E. Sports Pilot 250cc Off-Road Performance Go Kart
         </a>
         is available at Costco and will set you back $4,200.
        </p>
