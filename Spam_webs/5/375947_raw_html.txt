         <a href="http://petapixel.com/2015/01/26/leatherman-tread-moves-multi-tool-camera-bag-onto-wrist/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to The Leatherman Tread Moves Your Multi-Tool Out of Your Camera Bag and Onto Your Wrist">
          The Leatherman Tread Moves Your Multi-Tool Out of Your Camera Bag and Onto Your Wrist
         </a>
        </h2>
        <div class="postmetadata addthis_native_toolbox visible-xs">
         <ul>
          <li class="author">
           Jan 26, 2015 
           <a href="http://petapixel.com/author/michaelczhang/">

          <li class="fblikebtn">

           </div>
          </li>
          <li class="tweetbtn">
           <a class="twitter-share-button" data-related="petapixel" data-text="The Leatherman Tread Moves Your Multi-Tool Out of Your Camera Bag and Onto Your Wrist" data-url="http://petapixel.com/2015/01/26/leatherman-tread-moves-multi-tool-camera-bag-onto-wrist/" href="https://twitter.com/share">
           </a>
          </li>
          <li class="hidden-xs">
           <div class="g-plusone" data-href="http://petapixel.com/2015/01/26/leatherman-tread-moves-multi-tool-camera-bag-onto-wrist/" data-size="medium">
           </div>

         <div class="clearer">
         </div>

        <div class="entry">
         <p>
          <img alt="bracelet" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-156698" height="457" src="http://petapixel.com/assets/uploads/2015/01/bracelet.jpg" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Depending on the type of photography youre involved in, a multi-tool may be one of the invaluable items found inside your camera bag. If so, Leatherman has a new product for you called the
          <a href="http://www.leatherman.com/on/demandware.store/Sites-leatherman-Site/en_US/Blog-ShowPost/?p=705" target="_blank" title="">
           Tread
          </a>
          . Upon first glance, the product may look like a sleek but rather ordinary metal bracelet, but look a little closer and youll see that its actually a fancy new multi-tool that puts 25 separate tools around your wrist.
          <br/>
          <span id="more-156697">
          </span>
          <br/>
          <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="360" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/gp8xR07rBH4?rel=0&amp;showinfo=0" width="640">
          </iframe>
         </p>
         <p>
          The bracelet comes in the form of separate stainless steel links that can be joined together in any order you choose. Each of the pieces has two to three functional tools built in.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="bracelet2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-156700" height="525" src="http://petapixel.com/assets/uploads/2015/01/bracelet2.jpg" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          The 25 tools include: screwdrivers, hex drives, box wrenches, a glass breaker, a #2 square drive, a cutting hook, and a bottle opener.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="pieces" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-156699" height="214" src="http://petapixel.com/assets/uploads/2015/01/pieces1.jpg" width="640"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          There will also be a timepiece component that turns the bracelet into a watch. Designed by Leatherman and manufactured in Switzerland, the watch will feature a quartz movement and scratch-resistant sapphire crystal glass:
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="watch" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-156701" height="640" src="http://petapixel.com/assets/uploads/2015/01/watch.jpg" width="514"/>
         </p>
         <p>
          Leatherman plans to launch the Tread in April or May 2015. The stainless steel and black carbon finished versions will cost $150 and $200, respectively. In watch form, the two finishes will cost $500 and $600, respectively.
         </p>
