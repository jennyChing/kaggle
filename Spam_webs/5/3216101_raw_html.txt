           <h1 class="title single-title">
            Wedding Trends 2015
           </h1>
           <span class="post-info single-postmeta">
            <a href="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/author/hazel/" rel="nofollow" title="Posts by Hazel">
             Hazel

          <div class="post-single-content box mark-links">
           <p>
            From sophisticated color palette to simplest wedding cakes these
            <em>
             wedding trends 2015
            </em>
            are sure to inspire you. Pantone has a whole surprising color palette for spring/summer weddings, off-shoulder wedding dresses are back and gold/platinum is replaced with soft rose jewelry. Check out these
            <strong>
             wedding trends 2015
            </strong>
            !
            <span id="more-330">
            </span>
           </p>
           <h2>
            Hot Wedding Trends
           </h2>
           <p>
            Pastel hues will reign over the wedding market. Pantone have announced the colors for next year and consist of surprising mix of Marsala and Classic Blue. Scuba Blue, playful and reminiscent of a tropical ocean; Lucite Green, cool and refreshing, yet light in tone; and Toasted Almond, representing warmth, are other colors that will be popular.
            <a href="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/10-hot-wedding-color-trends-2015/">
             Pic Courtesy
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Lavendar-Herb-Wedding-Color-Trend-300x300.jpg" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Lavendar-Herb-Wedding-Color-Trend-300x300" class="aligncenter wp-image-341" height="407" src="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Lavendar-Herb-Wedding-Color-Trend-300x300-300x300.jpg" title="wedding trends 2015" width="407"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            Wedding Trends 2015 fro flowers will be very feminine, soft and romantic. Romantic Luxe is the key, luxurious floral will see the presence of hydrangeas, hyacinths and orchids in creamy pastels.
            <a href="http://jamaicaweddingsblog.com/wedding-decor-ideas-for-your-wedding-in-jamaica/" rel="nofollow">
             Pic Courtesy
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <img alt="Decor Trends 2015" class="alignnone wp-image-333" height="303" src="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Decor-Trends-2015-300x223.jpg" title="wedding trends 2015" width="408"/>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            2015 weddings will feature off-the-shoulder tops, romantic and timeless look.Variations from one-shoulder to draped sleeves on both sides will inspire the soft bohemian look.
            <a href="http://sophiatolli.com/wedding-dresses/y11563/" rel="nofollow">
             Pic Courtesy
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Dress-Trend-2015.jpg" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Wedding Dress Trend 2015" class="alignnone wp-image-335" height="488" src="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Dress-Trend-2015-225x300.jpg" title="wedding trends 2015" width="366"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <h3 style="text-align: center;">
            Wedding Trends 2015
           </h3>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            Gold and platinum have been a popular choice for wedding jewelry, but 2015 will seeyellow gold and rose gold are creeping their way back into engagement ring stores.These hues are just as special and may suit every skin tone, Dont rule them out as of yet!
            <a href="http://hitchedinraglan.co.nz/?p=2753" rel="nofollow">
             Pic Courtesy
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Ring-Trend-2015.jpg" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Wedding Ring Trend 2015" class="alignnone wp-image-339" height="423" src="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Ring-Trend-2015-300x300.jpg" title="wedding trends 2015" width="423"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            There is a new metal in town  copper, from invitations to centerpieces it gives a vintage vibe creating a ultra glam mood. Vintage lace will adorn the table decor along with lush romantic garland runners.
            <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/yourweddingco/wedding-color-palette-ideas/" rel="nofollow">
             Pic Courtesy
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Decor-Trend-2015.jpg" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Wedding Decor Trend 2015" class="alignnone wp-image-334" height="531" src="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Decor-Trend-2015-179x300.jpg" title="wedding trends 2015" width="317"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="line-height: 17.15pt; background: white; margin: 12.0pt 0in 12.0pt 0in;">
            <span style="font-size: 11.0pt; font-family: 'Arial','sans-serif'; color: #252525;">
             Simple, plentiful and comfort food, rustic is still a big wedding trend that will continue and extend throughout the menu.
             <a href="http://www.weddingbells.ca/planning/reception-ideas/2015-wedding-trends-food/4/" rel="nofollow">
              Pic Courtesy
             </a>
            </span>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Food-Trend.jpg" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Wedding Food Trend" class="alignnone wp-image-337" height="271" src="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Food-Trend-300x200.jpg" title="wedding trends 2015" width="407"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            Dont forget to put the snacks and cupcake table as mid reception snacks table, not only it saves cost but keeps guests taste buds engaged.
            <a href="http://www.weddingbells.ca/planning/reception-ideas/2015-wedding-trends-food/5/" rel="nofollow">
             Pic Courtesy
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Food-Trend-1.jpg" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Wedding Food Trend 1" class="alignnone wp-image-336" height="408" src="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Food-Trend-1-300x300.jpg" title="wedding trends 2015" width="408"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            Fairy lights or soft romantic twinkle lights are to be right on trend, lighting is the one of the most important factors for a gorgeous wedding. It will enhance, create focus and set the mood for the wedding reception.
            <a href="http://www.quintessentiallyweddings.com/blog/wedding-trends-2015-let-there-be-light" rel="nofollow">
             Pic Courtesy
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
            <a href="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Light-Trends-2015.jpg" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="Wedding Light Trends 2015" class="alignnone wp-image-338" height="317" src="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Wedding-Light-Trends-2015-300x225.jpg" title="wedding trends 2015" width="423"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            Go way back to basics, revisit classic cake designs. They are never out of style, if you dont want all white, go for a subtle cream or ivory.Another wedding cake trend in 2015 will be to skip the elaborate dessert and sweet table and serve cake as your dessert.
            <a href="http://www.slice.ca/weddings/photos/2015-wedding-trends/#!5f302478563d8368f77abd0ce46f63ce" rel="nofollow">
             Pic Courtesy
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            <a href="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/2015-Cake-Trends.jpg" rel="nofollow">
             <img alt="2015 Cake Trends" class="alignnone wp-image-332 aligncenter" height="442" src="http://itsallaboutweddings.in/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/2015-Cake-Trends-287x300.jpg" title="wedding trends 2015" width="423"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
            There is a strong spirit of glam in the air this season,
            <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
             wedding trends 2015
            </span>
            will communicate what couples want. Elegance and individuality is the new trend. After All Its All About Weddings!!
           </p>
