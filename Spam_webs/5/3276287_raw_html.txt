      <p>
       Becoming homeless is one of the worse things that can happen in someones life.
      </p>
      <p>
       Few of us who are fortunate enough to have 4 walls and a roof over our heads can imagine how awful it must be to be homeless.
      </p>
      <p>
       There are as many reasons for being homeless as there are victims, but some Massachusetts artists decided to make the signs held by some of these people more personal.
      </p>
      <p>
       This will help those who are able to contribute see the homeless person as an individual and a human being.
      </p>
      <p>
       The project involves the artists meeting with a homeless person, listening to their tale, giving them some money for their crude sign, and then making a bright and beautiful new sign for them.
      </p>
      <p>
       Regardless of why the person is homeless, we should all remember, 
       <em>
        There but for the grace of God, go I.
       </em>
       
      </p>
