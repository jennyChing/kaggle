       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://adserver.adtechus.com/?adlink%7C3.0%7C5282%7C2788943%7C1%7C16%7CAdId=4424946;BnId=2" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
         <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">
          <img alt="Mass-Vacation-Logo" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-22099" height="131" src="http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Mass-Vacation-Logo.jpg?aace32" width="469"/>
          <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/2013/08/07/artistic-side-of-massachusetts/&amp;media=http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Mass-Vacation-Logo.jpg&amp;description=Explore the Artistic Side of Massachusetts #XploreMass | http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/2013/08/07/artistic-side-of-massachusetts/')">
          </span>
         </span>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Massachusetts for Music Lovers
        </b>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px">
        <img alt="Boston Symphony Orchestra " border="0" height="350" src="http://i1109.photobucket.com/albums/h430/OurFamilyWorld/How%20to%20plan%20vacations/Visit%20Mass/Music_zps31c74e31.jpg" style="border: 0px none;" title="Boston Symphony Orchestra " width="500"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Boston Symphony Orchestra violinist.
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        From the start of this campaign, I wondered how I could work my favorite Boston punk band into the mix! Well, heres how! If youre a fan of good, energetic Irish punk music, one show you cannot miss is the Dropkick Murphys St. Patricks Day show! This isnt a show for the kids, but its something that is high up on my bucket list. Ive seen the band live four times, but never in their hometown. I hear its quite an amazing show!
       </p>
       <p>
        Not a punk rock girl at heart? Well, youre still in luck, because Boston is home to a wide variety of music. If a relaxing symphony is more your tune, check out the Boston Symphony Orchestra at the historic symphony hall. Prefer something a little more modern? Peruse the lineup at the Bank of America Pavilion on the beautiful Boston Harbor to see who is playing while youre in town.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         A Theater Fans Dream
        </b>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 522px">
        <img alt="Romeo and Juliet" border="0" height="341" src="http://i1109.photobucket.com/albums/h430/OurFamilyWorld/How%20to%20plan%20vacations/Visit%20Mass/ShakespeareCreditKevinSprague_zpsfc2ecd62.jpg" style="border: 0px none;" title="Romeo and Juliet" width="512"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Romeo and Juliet at Shakespeare and Company
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        If theater is more up your alley, youll find plenty of that throughout Massachusetts. Shakespeare fans will love checking out the bards plays at Shakespeare and Company in Western Massachusetts. Comedy lovers can catch a show at Boston Universitys Huntington Theater. Want to be among the first to take a peek at the work of emerging playwrights? Head to the Gloucester Stage Company! The Cape Playhouse in Dennis is perfect if you want a good mix of styles, as they offer everything from mysteries to musicals.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Finest of Fine Arts
        </b>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 481px">
        <img alt="Norman Rockwell Museum in Massachusetts" border="0" height="350" src="http://i1109.photobucket.com/albums/h430/OurFamilyWorld/How%20to%20plan%20vacations/Visit%20Mass/Norman_zps767c2952.jpg" style="border: 0px none;" title="Norman Rockwell Museum in Massachusetts" width="471"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Norman Rockwell Museum in Massachusetts
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Do you love exploring art museums and discovering stunning new images to inspire your own creative side? Youll have a hard time choosing which of the many Massachusetts art galleries and museums to visit! Mass MoCA in North Adams is perfect for those who enjoy something a little on the modern, psychedelic side. Looking for more traditional arts? How about the Norman Rockwell Museum? It doesnt get more traditional than that! Kids will love exploring the Boston Childrens Museum and getting hands-on with everything from science to culture.
       </p>
       <p>
        <b>
         Get inspired by natural beauty
        </b>
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px">
        <img alt="Secret Garden Bench " border="0" height="281" src="http://i1109.photobucket.com/albums/h430/OurFamilyWorld/How%20to%20plan%20vacations/Visit%20Mass/SecretGardenBench_zps322b797a.jpg" style="border: 0px none;" title="Secret Garden Bench " width="500"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         The secret garden bench is a great place to relax and get inspired!
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        Llooking for inspiration for your next masterpiece painting? Need a quiet place to write the next bestselling novel? Massachusetts has plenty of natural art to inspire you! I cant think of a more serene place to brainstorm my next idea than on the Secret Garden Bench in the Tower Hill Botanic Garden. The wonderful thing about Massachusetts is that you never have to travel far to
        <span style="color: #ff0000;">
         <a href="http://www.ourfamilyworld.com/2013/08/02/outdoors-in-massachusetts/" target="_blank">
          <span style="color: #ff0000;">
           find a stunning view
          </span>
         </a>
        </span>
        !
       </p>
       <p>
        What is your favorite way to enjoy the fine arts? Do you prefer museum hopping? Love the sounds of a symphony orchestra? Or will I see you in the mosh pit one of these St. Patricks Days? Whatever makes your heart sing, youll find it in Massachusetts.
       </p>
       <h2 style="text-align: center;">
        <strong>
         Check out
         <span style="color: #ff0000;">
          <a href="http://adserver.adtechus.com/?adlink%7C3.0%7C5282%7C2788943%7C1%7C16%7CAdId=4424946;BnId=2" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           <span style="color: #ff0000;">
            MassVacation
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         .com to learn more about the artistic side of Massachusetts!
        </strong>
       </h2>
       <p>
        Theater Photo Credit:Kevin Sprague. All other Massachusetts Office of Travel &amp; Tourism.
       </p>
