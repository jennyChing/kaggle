       <div class="post-content">
        <h3>
         <a href="http://mochadad.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/mochadad-chocolate-rice-krispies-treats.jpg">
          <img alt="mochadad chocolate rice krispies treats" class="aligncenter wp-image-12344" height="419" src="http://mochadad.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/mochadad-chocolate-rice-krispies-treats-1024x768.jpg" width="558"/>
         </a>
        </h3>
        <h3>
         Ingredients
        </h3>
        <p>
         3 Tablespoons of Butter
        </p>
        <p>
         4 Cups of Miniature Marshmallows
        </p>
        <p>
         1 Cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips
        </p>
        <p>
         4 Cups of Kelloggs Rice Krispies cereal
        </p>
        <p>
         2 Cups of Kelloggs Krave cereal
        </p>
        <p>
         1 Cup of walnut pieces
        </p>
        <h3>
         Directions
        </h3>
        <p>
         1. In large saucepan, melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and chocolate chips and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
        </p>
        <p>
         2. A Kelloggs Rice Krispies cereal, Krave cereal, and walnut pieces. Stir until well coated.
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Using wax paper, evenly press mixture into 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan coated with cooking spray. Cool for at least 15 minutes and cut into 2-inch squares. Makes about 12 Chocolate Walnut Rice Krispies Treats.
        </p>
