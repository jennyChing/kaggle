       <h2>
        How This Came to Be
        <br/>
       </h2>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        I spent several years advising small business owners and building relationships with lenders and investors. I started to get frustrated because I was telling different people the same thing over and over. The information may have been new to them, but it was old to me! Three people told me to start writing it down so I did. This book is the result of that.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Small Business Financing
       </h2>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        What I realized was that there were several problems.
        <br/>
       </p>
       <ol>
        <li>
         Many business owners did not track their cash flow and so didn't realize they needed funding until they were in a money crunch.
         <p>
         </p>
        </li>
        <li>
         Business owners assumed that because they banked at a particular bank, this "relationship" constituted a banking relationship. So they were often very surprised and extremely disappointed when their bank did not give them a loan.
        </li>
        <p>
        </p>
        <li>
         Many business owners were simply unaware of all the various sources of financing that existed, how to approach them or how to best leverage them to meet their goals. For example, some companies used factoring but wondered why they had little cash. They didn't realize they were paying essentially a 24 - 68% interest rate. So they stayed on the factoring treadmill.
         <p>
         </p>
         <p>
         </p>
        </li>
        <li>
         Business owners thought of "venture capital" when they though of investors. They didn't realize there's a whole host of other investors who may be interested in their non-tech businesses. And owners didn't think of their business' from an investor perspective.
         <p>
          <img src="https://images.indiegogo.com/file_attachments/422901/files/20140309220905-iStock_Stock_Certificates_and_Cash.jpg?1394428145" style="width: 400.494px; height: 258px;max-width:580px;"/>
         </p>
         Most small
to medium businesses are completely unaware of the variety of financing sources
that exist for their business or they are unprepared to meet the requirements
from a financial packaging perspective!
