       <header class="entry-header">
        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Immerse Yourself in the World of Harry Potter
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.lifeinpleasantville.com/author/candace/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Candace Derickx
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.lifeinpleasantville.com/diagon-alley-universal/#comments">
           10 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         Less than a month after the new
         <em>
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.universalorlando.com/Theme-Parks/Wizarding-World-Of-Harry-Potter.aspx" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.universalorlando.com/Theme-Parks/Wizarding-World-Of-Harry-Potter.aspx', 'Diagon Alley']);" target="_blank">
            Diagon Alley
           </a>
          </strong>
         </em>
         and
         <em>
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.universalorlando.com/Theme-Parks/Wizarding-World-Of-Harry-Potter-Hogwarts-Express.aspx" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.universalorlando.com/Theme-Parks/Wizarding-World-Of-Harry-Potter-Hogwarts-Express.aspx', 'Hogswart Express']);" target="_blank">
            Hogswart Express
           </a>
          </strong>
         </em>
         opened in the summer of 2014, just over 1 million people made the trek to visit the crown jewel at
         <em>
          <strong>
           <a href="https://www.universalorlando.com/Home.aspx" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.universalorlando.com/Home.aspx', 'Universal Studios Orlando']);" target="_blank">
            Universal Studios Orlando
           </a>
          </strong>
         </em>
         . By the time we made it there in the fall, there were probably a million more, and its not hard to see why people will be flocking here for years and years to come.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="diagon alley universal orlando" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15138" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.lifeinpleasantville.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Kings-Cross-Station-Harry-Potter.jpg?fit=700%2C537"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Those not familiar with the books or movies may walk by this new attraction at Universal Studios with a bit of a yawn. While theres no doubt its an impressive replica of Charing Cross Road in London, it doesnt exactly scream excitement. In fact, its downright muggle-like. Harry Potter fans though must come upon this facade and feel their hearts skip a beat, because as all good witches and wizards know, the entrance to Diagon Alley is just behind the book store and the record shop.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="Diagon Alley Universal Studios" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15246" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.lifeinpleasantville.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Diagon-Alley-Universal-Studios.jpg?fit=700%2C427"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         With the completion of
         <em>
          <strong>
           Diagon Alley
          </strong>
         </em>
         and the
         <em>
          <strong>
           Hogswart Express
          </strong>
         </em>
         , the
         <em>
          <strong>
           Wizarding World of Harry Potter
          </strong>
         </em>
         has become a completely immersive experience. A quick train ride from Kings Cross to
         <em>
          <strong>
           Hogsmeade
          </strong>
         </em>
         keeps guests far from muggle distractions like The Simpsons or Transformers (if they so wish). Robes and wands are commonplace here and you may find yourself longing for a Nimbus 2000. After all, this is all completely real, right?
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="diagon alley universal, quidditch supplies" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15247" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.lifeinpleasantville.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/quidditch-supplies.jpg?fit=700%2C456"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Okay, fine its not, but dont let that discourage you from at least pretending it is. Its not hard to lose yourself in the fantasy. Upon entering
         <em>
          <strong>
           Diagon Alley
          </strong>
         </em>
         youll find Ollivanders, The Owl Post, The Daily Prophet, Madame Malkins Robes, and of course Gringotts Wizarding Bank. Its a literal feast for the eyes as guests explore the bricks and mortar version of their favourite story.
        </p>
        <p>
         Speaking of feasts, theres also one for your tastebuds. The Leaky Cauldron offers traditional English comfort foods plus Butter Beer of course. At Florean Fortescues Ice Cream Parlour youll find your favourite muggle flavours plus a few twists like Granny Smith, Peanut Butter and Strawberry, and Earl Grey and Lavender. Finally, your sweet tooth will find everything it needs at Weasleys Wizard Wheezes, like U-No-Poo and Fever Fudge. What? You dont find that appetizing? Hmmm, maybe only the youngest of wizards find it appealing.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="food at Diagon alley universal" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15249" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.lifeinpleasantville.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/food-at-Diagon-alley-.jpg?fit=700%2C1000"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Of course, what would a wizarding story be without a little death defying drama right? No need to worry, because youll find that on
         <a href="https://www.universalorlando.com/Rides/Universal-Studios-Florida/Harry-Potter-And-The-Escape-From-Gringotts.aspx" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.universalorlando.com/Rides/Universal-Studios-Florida/Harry-Potter-And-The-Escape-From-Gringotts.aspx', 'Harry Potter and the Escape from Gringotts']);" target="_blank">
          <em>
           <strong>
            Harry Potter and the Escape from Gringotts
           </strong>
          </em>
         </a>
         . You enter this ride beneath the dragon, the actual fire-breathing dragon if you please, and then proceed to pass by some seriously creepy goblins.
         <em>
          Does it feel like their eyes are following you? Why, as a matter of fact they are. GAH!
         </em>
         Once you made it past their piercing stares youre ready to enter the depths of the vaults. As with most rides at Universal, youll find it a thrilling combination between a traditional coaster and 3D technology. Trust me when I say that youll make it out of there.but barely. Or at least thats how it felt to me.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="diagon alley universal, Goblins at Gringotts" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15250" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.lifeinpleasantville.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Goblins-at-Gringotts.jpg?fit=700%2C467"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         You may need to take in a little entertainment at this point. Maybe a little Celestina Warbeck and the Banshees to calm your nerves? Relax as Celestina sings classics like You stole my cauldron, but you cant have my heart.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="diagon alley universal,celestina warbeck diagon alley" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15251" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.lifeinpleasantville.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/celestina-warbeck-diagon-alley.jpg?fit=700%2C467"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Finally, what trip to Diagon Alley would be complete without a trip down Knockturn Alley. Those with a penchant for the Dark Arts will find all they require at the uber creepy Borgin and Burkes. Those crazy Death Eaters, cant live with em.
        </p>
        <p>
         After youre done checking out every nook and cranny at Diagon Alley, its time to hop onboard the Hogswart Express to Hogsmeade.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="diagon alley universal,hogswart express" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15252" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.lifeinpleasantville.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/hogswart-express-.jpg?fit=700%2C1000"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Hogswart Express is not to be missed. The experience changes depending if youre traveling to Hogswart or to London. Make a point of taking it both ways while there, you wont regret it. The real journey between the two attractions is quite short but as with everything here, you get to suspend your disbelief and play along for what feels like a very real journey through London and the English Countryside.
        </p>
        <p>
         Once you disembark in Hogsmeade you are
         <a href="http://www.childmode.com/2014/02/19/finding-magic-harry-potter-universal-studios/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.childmode.com/2014/02/19/finding-magic-harry-potter-universal-studios/', 'in the heart of the original Harry Potter attraction']);" target="_blank">
          in the heart of the original Harry Potter attraction
         </a>
         which is no less amazing now that Diagon Alley has arrived. I do believe that the most avid Harry Potter fans could and probably should allow for a full day to explore Diagon Alley and Hogsmeade. If youre interested in the rest of Universal Orlando (its amazing), then I highly recommend purchasing a multi-day pass.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="diagon alley universal,Hogswart School of Witchcraft and Wizardry" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-15258" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.lifeinpleasantville.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/Hogswart-School-of-Witchcraft-and-Wizardry.jpg?fit=1280%2C750"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         Finally, there is another new addition that I think is absolutely delightful. You can now
         <a href="https://www.universalorlando.com/Shopping/Islands-of-Adventure/Ollivanders.aspx" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'https://www.universalorlando.com/Shopping/Islands-of-Adventure/Ollivanders.aspx', 'purchase wands that work within Diagon Alley and Hogsmeade']);" target="_blank">
          purchase wands that work within Diagon Alley and Hogsmeade
         </a>
         to make your sorcerers experience complete. Each wand is $45 and comes with a map to help guide you to places where you can practice your magic. I know I just heard a small gasp from budget-conscious mothers everywhere. I hear you when you say that $45 is a lot of money. Multiply that by a couple of kids and you might be thinking Im out of my mind. But can you say 
         <i>
          Wingardium Leviosa
         </i>
         ? I can tell you that when I return with my daughters I will be shelling out $90 for two wands and letting them perform magic for the day. Very rarely would I suggest a souvenir is worth every penny, but for this one, I believe it is. You may even want to get your own and join along in the fun.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: center;">
         <em>
          <strong>
           Many thanks to Universal Orlando for hosting Life in Pleasantville. You continue to surprise and delight us at every turn.
          </strong>
         </em>
        </p>
