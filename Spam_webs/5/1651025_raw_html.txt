       <div class="entry-summary">
        <p>
         <b>
          Cn g tuyt vi bng khi cui tun c thng thc nhng mn n ngon cng ngi yu, bn b hay ngi thn. Di y l mt vi gi  cho tic
          <a href="http://www.nhommua.com/ho-chi-minh/tim-kiem/buffet-chay.html">
           buffet chay
          </a>
          y lng mn &amp; sang trng ti nhng nh hng cao cp.
         </b>
        </p>
        <p>
         C ng  v tr c a v si ng ca TP HCM, nh hng buffet Hong Yn M Linh Point c khng gian bi tr theo phong cch ng i, sang trng. Tng mn n  nh hng Hong Yn m  du n thin nhin, hng ng c ni. Nhng mn n thun Vit hi t t mi min t quc c ch xut bi nhng u bp giu kinh nghim. Buffet cho 2 ngi  y gip bn chn ra c hng v qu nh qua nhng mn nh bn ch H Ni, bnh tm H Ty, b cun l lt, n cc loi bnh Hu. Dn ghin hi sn th tha sc chn lc nhng mn ngon m  v bin nh: nghu hp s, hn xc bnh a, c hp gng, c trng nng mui t, cua rang me
        </p>
        <p style="text-align:center">
         <img alt="Khng gian buffet cho 2 ngi lng mn ti nhng nh hng sang trng" src="http://phununews.vn/upload_images/images/2014/10/06/mon-chay-voi-mit2.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         K tip, tic buffet cho 2 ngi s c din ra trong khng kh m cng ca Legend Hotel Khch sn Lotte Legend Saigon l khch sn 5 sao vi 283 phng rng ri &amp; tin nghi, c to mu vi phong cch thm m cao cp &amp; khc thng, phc v chuyn nghip. Mt trong nhng li im na ca khch sn l khung cnh rt xinh xung dng sng Tp H Ch Minh un ln nh di la ng nh. V h thng nh hng ca Lotte Legend th trn c tuyt vi chuyn phc v tic buffet sng, tra chiu ti cho nhiu thc khch. c bit vi khng gian lng mn ni y chc chn s l im hn l tng cho tic buffet cho 2 ngi.  
         <a href="http://lozi.vn/nha-hang/buffet-chay-co-noi-61-63-hai-ba-trung-p-ben-nghe-quan-1-tp-hcm-469">
          buffet chay sai gon xua va nay
         </a>
         
        </p>
        <p>
         Khng gian buffet cho 2 ngi sang trng ti Khch sn New Word y sa hoa, sang trng. Thc n buffet gm nhiu mn n ho hng, th loi phong ph gm: tm hm, tm cng, tm s, hu, c hng, nghu, s lng, s huyt, c, sushi, sashimi, salad, bnh ngtcng ban nhc jazz phc v trong sut thi gian tic Buffet. Khng gian buffet rng ri, khu vc bn n l tng c th nhn ra bn ngoi qua khung knh. i ng nhn vin phc v chuyn nghip, thn trng, informal. trong khng gian buffet tra hin i, bn d dng la chn cho mnh nhng mn n yu thch m khng phi chen chc, chn rn. Khi  chn c cho mnh phn n thm ngon, hp dn, tin n khu vc bn n, bn c th mnh trn nhng chic gh m m  c cm gic thng thc mn n thoi mi, hng phn nht.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align:center">
         <img alt="Khng gian buffet cho 2 ngi lng mn ti nhng nh hng cao cp" src="http://tikicdn.com/media/catalog/product/cache/1/image/9df78eab33525d08d6e5fb8d27136e95/c/a/cac-mon-an-chay-1_copy_.jpg"/>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://vidieuphap.com/tin-tuc-su-kien/1500-am-thuc-buffet-chay-gay-quy-cong-duc-gop-phan-hun-phuoc-xay-dung-tinh-xa-phap-hue.html">
          Buffet Chay Nha Hang La Tia To
         </a>
         : c bit n l mt vo nhng nh hng buffet ln nht ti Si Gn, L hi Buffet (Ngc Thy c) l im n l tng khi bn mun thng thc nhng bui tic buffet trong khng gian sang trng vi thc n a dng, mn n thu ht. Vi tic buffet 2 ngi ti nh hng Ngy hi Buffet (Ngc Thy c) ln ny, bn s c tri nghim gn 90 mn n mang phong cch   u c sc. Nhng mn n c ch bin k cng, t m, pht biu bt mt &amp; c hng v thch hp vi khoi khu ca ngi Vit. Bn cnh cc mn chnh, thc n buffet cn bao gm nhiu mn trng ming thm ngon nh tri cy theo ma, cc loi ch, bnh ngt, mang n cho cc bn ba n trn vn, tha mn, va lng nht.
        </p>
