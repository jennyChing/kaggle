    <section class="cols2">
     <div class="unit col1">
      <article>
       <div class="story">
        <div class="content" id="article_body">
         <div class="group-container ">
          <div class="group" itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            Leisure-oriented Spirit Airlines is giving away more than 300 million frequent flyer miles during a road tour of the United States.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="group-container ">
          <div class="embed-container image" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/ImageObject">
           <img alt="Spirit Airlines" height="298" src="http://fm.cnbc.com/applications/cnbc.com/resources/img/editorial/2012/04/19/47101203-spirit-airlines-200.530x298.jpg?v=1339827872" width="530"/>
           <meta content="http://fm.cnbc.com/applications/cnbc.com/resources/img/editorial/2012/04/19/47101203-spirit-airlines-200.jpg?v=1339827872" itemprop="image"/>
           <meta content="width='530'" itemprop="width"/>
           <meta content="height='298'" itemprop="height"/>
           <meta content="2012-04-19T10:43:43-0400" itemprop="datePublished"/>
           <meta content="2012-04-19T10:43:43-0400" itemprop="dateCreated"/>
           <meta content="2012-09-16T01:16:24-0400" itemprop="dateModified"/>
           <meta content="2012-04-19T10:43:43-0400" itemprop="uploadDate"/>
           <meta content="2099-12-31" itemprop="expires"/>
           <!-- TODO: defaulting to 2099 until we get the expiry date from toolbelt -->
           <meta content="jpg" itemprop="encodingFormat"/>
           <meta content="Source: Spirit Airlines" itemprop="author"/>
           <meta content="CNBC" itemprop="publisher"/>
           <meta content="Spirit Airlines" itemprop="caption"/>

            Source: Spirit Airlines
           </div>
           <div class="caption">
            Spirit Airlines
           </div>
          </div>
          <div class="group" itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            The event got underway Wednesday in Dallas, Texas and stops are planned in 25 cities across the country. More than 15,000 people will receive 20,000 "Free Spirit" frequent flyer miles which can be redeemed for a free roundtrip award ticket (taxes of at least $5 per ticket apply). Anywhere from 250 to 1,000 certificates will be distributed at each venue.
           </p>
           <p>
            "We're looking forward to giving away 15,000 award tickets as we say hello and thank our loyal customers, as well as reach out to potential new customers in cities we serve across the United States," says Michael Pewther, Spirit's senior director of sales in a release.
           </p>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div class="group-container last">
          <div class="group" itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            <strong>
             Here's the fine print
            </strong>
            . Recipients of the bonus certificates have to register their claim on the airline's
            <a class="inline_asset" data-nodeid="4401834" href="http://marketing.spirit.com/2012/" target="_blank">
             website
            </a>
            within seven days, and miles will be deposited within 45 days of registration. Travel must then be booked within 30 days of the date the miles are posted into the certificate holder's account.
           </p>
           <p>
            The full list of locations, dates and times can be found by going
            <a class="inline_asset" data-nodeid="4401834" href="http://marketing.spirit.com/2012/" target="_blank">
             here
            </a>
            .
           </p>
