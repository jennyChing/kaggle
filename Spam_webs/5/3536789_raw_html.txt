        <h2 class="sectiontitle">
         The Purpose
        </h2>
        <div class="imgright" style="width: 154px;">
         <div class="imgmain">
          <a href="/img/1p8xe-53svon1_1h/clique">
           <img alt="clique" height="150" src="http://img.wikinut.com/img/1p8xe-53svon1_1h/jpeg/180x300/clique.jpeg" title="clique" width="150"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="imgtitle">
          <a href="/img/1p8xe-53svon1_1h/clique">
           clique
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         Even at the tender age of fourteen, Janelle knew the importance of
         <a href="http://humour.wikinut.com/How-To-Crash-A-Party/1fd_8nv5/" title="How To Crash A Party">
          Parties
         </a>
         .  Unlike those who think parties are to celebrate an event or create a joyous venue for friends to congregate, Janelle knew that the purpose of a Party was to
         <b>
          Not
         </b>
         invite people.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Nothing adds to cache of a
         <b>
          Party
         </b>
         as knowing how many people are being sent into the depths of despair knowing they were
         <b>
          not
         </b>
         Invited.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         This is why instead of holding her
         <a href="http://guides.wikinut.com/The-Pagan-Festival-Called-Christmas/1hl07e53/" title="The Pagan Festival Called Christmas">
          Christmas
         </a>
         Break party on the Saturday after the last Friday, she held it the week before.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         In this way she could insure that delightful event called
         <b>
          Morbid Monday.
         </b>
        </p>
        <a id="section-1" name="The-Monday">
        </a>
        <h2 class="sectiontitle">
         The Monday
        </h2>
        <div class="imgright" style="width: 184px;">
         <div class="imgmain">
          <a href="/img/eub-5_nyz0xa7yth/Monday-Morning">
           <img alt="Monday Morning" height="250" src="http://img.wikinut.com/img/eub-5_nyz0xa7yth/jpeg/180x300/Monday-Morning.jpeg" title="Monday Morning" width="180"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="imgtitle">
          <a href="/img/eub-5_nyz0xa7yth/Monday-Morning">
           Monday Morning
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         On Monday morning, all the girls who had been invited would be babbling loud and repetitively about what had transpired on Saturday night.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         They'd wax lyrical about the clothes, the food, the music, and go through intricacies of who was with whom, shoving and pushing their way through the crowd, finding someone who ought have been invited but was left off the list, to torment.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         "Oh Barbie! I didn't see you Saturday night!"
         <br/>
         <br/>
         "Hey Carl, did you think you'd be invited?"
         <br/>
         <br/>
         And they'd cackle like hens, passing all sorts of   words between them and making sure everyone at the school knew
         <b>
          they
         </b>
         had been at Janelle's Party who had
         <b>
          NOT
         </b>
         been.
        </p>
        <a id="section-2" name="Enter-the-Nerd">
        </a>
        <h2 class="sectiontitle">
         Enter the Nerd
        </h2>
        <div class="imgright" style="width: 86px;">
         <div class="imgmain">
          <a href="/img/11x1.nv_he96p6_2/nerd">
           <img alt="nerd" height="100" src="http://img.wikinut.com/img/11x1.nv_he96p6_2/jpeg/180x300/nerd.jpeg" title="nerd" width="82"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="imgtitle">
          <a href="/img/11x1.nv_he96p6_2/nerd">
           nerd
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         Way back in October I enter the story.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         I had been sitting in
         <i>
          Stink Central
         </i>
         l when one of the Class Queens came to call me.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Emitting the most significant words I had ever heard;
         <b>
          "Janelle wants to see you,"
         </b>
         as she invited me to
         <i>
          Heaven.
         </i>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         I rose, all eyes upon me, and moved from the place every meal had come to die to the table by the open window.   At that table sat the
         <b>
          School Goddess,
         </b>
         Janelle, surrounded by
         <b>
          Grade Empresses, Class Queens
         </b>
         , and their
         <b>
          Ladies in Waiting.
         </b>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         As I stood there, ignored, while Janelle discussed how she would do her hair, all eyes were on me. Hence, boldly I went where no girl would dare to go, I shoved onto the bench beside Janelle.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         So shocked was she by my behaviour she stopped talking, glared at me, as if to turn me to stone, then recalled she had sent for me.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <i>
          "I want the social studies homework,
         </i>
         " she decreed, adding how I would do an extra copy of my homework, put it into a magazine and hand it to one of the minor Ladies in Waiting.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         I refused.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <i>
          "My deal is with you, hence I will give it to you.
         </i>
         "  Said I.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         For about forty seconds she stared, long enough for her
         <a href="http://guides.wikinut.com/Irrelevance-of-I.Q./2s1q2xpg/" title="Irrelevance of I.Q. ">
          brain
         </a>
         to comprehend that either she is seen associating with me for about fifteen seconds every morning or fails social studies.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         It was a hard choice, but she decided I could breath her air.
        </p>
        <a id="section-3" name="The-Nerd-s-Revenge">
        </a>
        <h2 class="sectiontitle">
         The Nerd's Revenge
        </h2>
        <div class="imgright" style="width: 132px;">
         <div class="imgmain">
          <a href="/img/1i6r2sc2ja-i928z/class">
           <img alt="class" height="84" src="http://img.wikinut.com/img/1i6r2sc2ja-i928z/jpeg/180x300/class.jpeg" title="class" width="128"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="imgtitle">
          <a href="/img/1i6r2sc2ja-i928z/class">
           class
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         As I had been seen talking to Janelle I was suddenly cool.  I was no longer the Nerd, but someone of such importance that Janelle could
         <a href="http://humour.wikinut.com/How-to-Tell-Friends-from-Enemies/15.6ohrg/" title="How to Tell Friends from Enemies">
          speak
         </a>
         with me.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Kids were asking me all sorts of questions about style and music and I was winging it because my mother dressed me and the kind of music I listened to wouldn't be on the pop ten.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         It was due to this
         <i>
          'connection'
         </i>
         with Janelle I learned about the
         <b>
          Party.
         </b>
        </p>
        <a id="section-4" name="What-I-Told-my-Parents">
        </a>
        <h2 class="sectiontitle">
         What I Told my Parents
        </h2>
        <p>
         Although I was a
         <b>
          Nerd
         </b>
         I was an aware Nerd.  I knew that if I was
         <b>
          not
         </b>
         at that Party come
         <b>
          Morbid Monday
         </b>
         everyone would know that I had not been invited to Janelle's Party.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         That fact would race through the school like a
         <a href="http://guides.wikinut.com/Rumour/3hves.5h/" title="Rumour">
          virus
         </a>
         and by lunch period my status would be in the sub-basement.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         I had to go.
         <br/>
         I had to
         <a href="http://humour.wikinut.com/How-To-Crash-A-Party/1fd_8nv5/" title="How To Crash A Party">
          Crash
         </a>
         Janelle's Party.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         I cultivated a friendship with a
         <b>
          Lady in Waiting.
         </b>
         I told my parents I would go over to her house to study on Saturday and then we'd then go to the early movie, and my father could pick me up at ten p.m.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         This sounded good.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         A girl going to study with another girl in a house in a decent area who would have dinner with the family, go to an early movie and be under Daddy's control by ten o'clock.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         The address I gave for my friend was that of Janelle's house.
        </p>
        <a id="section-5" name="What-I-Actually-Did">
        </a>
        <h2 class="sectiontitle">
         What I Actually Did
        </h2>
        <div class="imgright" style="width: 184px;">
         <div class="imgmain">
          <a href="/img/njxa.8ndn0alyjax/party">
           <img alt="party" height="180" src="http://img.wikinut.com/img/njxa.8ndn0alyjax/jpeg/180x300/party.jpeg" title="party" width="180"/>
          </a>
         </div>
         <div class="imgtitle">
          <a href="/img/njxa.8ndn0alyjax/party">
           party
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <p>
         I arrived at the
         <b>
          Lady In Waiting's
         </b>
         house at about two o'clock. In my bag was a pretty little dress I could wear as a long blouse and a pair of strappy heels.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         I listened to her pointless babble until five o'clock when we began to get ready and then her father dropped us at Janelle's house at seven.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         As everyone knows if a Party is set for seven it doesn't start until the entrance of the hostess at eight.  This means that for one hour I could play official greeter for all the little wannabes and mini-friends of Janelle.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Everyone saw me as they entered, and clearly I must
         <b>
          BE SOMEBODY
         </b>
         to have been appointed to this important task.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         At eight, when Janelle entered the room, I was hiding in the side bathroom, (where I'd hidden my bag with my go home clothes).
         <br/>
         <br/>
         I gave Janelle twenty minutes to wander around the room before emerging, Keeping to the very edges of the room, as  I did not want to be found too quickly, I tried to be wherever Janelle wasn't.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         I got away with this until nearly nine fifteen.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         At that point one of
         <b>
          Class Queens
         </b>
         saw me, ran to tell Janelle, and I began making a circle saying good night to everyone as I moved to the side bathroom.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         I washed my face, pulled on my go home clothes, and it was ten to ten when I slipped out of the house to wait for my father on the road.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         As I saw the car coming I moved to it, jumped in. When my father asked me why I was out on the road I told him a  clever lie about a surprise party they tossed for my friend and I felt so embarrassed to be in these play clothes.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         I got over.
        </p>
        <a id="section-6" name="On-Monday">
        </a>
        <h2 class="sectiontitle">
         On Monday
        </h2>
        <p>
         As I reached the school I began telling everyone I saw about
         <b>
          Janelle's Party.
          <br/>
         </b>
         <br/>
         Janelle was waiting for me by the school door with her posse of Queens and Empresses, and Ladies in Waiting.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <i>
          "I want to talk to you!"
         </i>
         she began, in clear hearing of everyone.
         <i>
          "You have one nerve!"
         </i>
         She shouts as I move past her and the retinue.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         Everyone wanted to know what it was about, but as discretion is the better part of valor I said;
         <i>
          "It's private."
         </i>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         <a href="http://guides.wikinut.com/Rumour/3hves.5h/" title="Rumour">
          Rumours
         </a>
         abounded. From flirting with her boyfriend to making fun of her dress. I, of course, said nothing.
         <br/>
         <br/>
         I never did Janelle's homework again, of course. Never went to
         <i>
          Heaven
         </i>
         but stayed in
         <i>
          Stink Central,
         </i>
         <br/>
         <br/>
         No one knew why Janelle and I were no longer
         <a href="http://humour.wikinut.com/How-to-Tell-Friends-from-Enemies/15.6ohrg/" title="How to Tell Friends from Enemies">
          friends
         </a>
         , but everyone knew I had been.  Which was good enough to get me off the '
         <b>
          Nerd'
         </b>
         list.
         <br/>
         <br/>
        </p>
