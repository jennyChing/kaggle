The ecolodge has 12 cruiser bikes to take you to one of the most secluded virgin beaches in the Caribbean, just a 15 to 20 minute ride away.           <div class="collapse-body">
           Start your day with 8am breakfast, followed by a full-day tour of Rincon beach from 9:30 until 5:00. Drive along the coast to one of the top 5 beaches in the world. Enjoy a delicious lunch of chicken or fish cooked over an open wood bbq pit on the beach. Next to the restaurant is a gentle fresh water river that meanders into the ocean. After eating and relaxing, it's time to raise your adrenaline level with a cliff dive. It is amazing! There are outstanding views for those that dont want to jump and a thrill like no other for those brave enough to do it.
Next, stop off at another beach for some drinks and chill time. Finally, head to do a casa tipica (local home) where you'll learn about coffee, chocolate, rum, local life, and more. See how these things have been made for centuries and taste the difference of authentic preparation  for many guests, this is a highlight of the trip.

         <div class="collapse-collapse collapse" id="644823403-collapse" style="height: 0px">
          <div class="collapse-body">
           Rise for 7:30am breakfast and then journey to Los Haitises National Park for a full-day tour (8:30am until 4:00pm). More than just an excursion, today's activity is an adventure and a communion with nature! Depart from the private dock in the town of Samana on a comfortable, fast, and secure boat to Los Haitises National Park, designated a Biosphere Reserve by UNESCO. Start the tour in Cayo Los Pajaros (Bird Cay) and observe the nesting areas of indigenous birds. Depending on the time of year, you may get a glimpse of one of the newborns being fed by their parents. Board the boats again and take a short ride to a pristine island located a half mile off the coast near Samana. Locally this is known as Bacardi Island due to its history as the filming spot for Bacardi television commercials. This is a beautiful island that boasts turquoise blue water and miles of sandy white beaches. This is a day to relax and unwind under the warm Caribbean sun, or in the shade of a palm tree. The soothing light ocean breeze will lull you off to a dreamy place. Sip on cool margaritas by day, and return for dinner and another magical evening at the Tree House.

         <div class="collapse-collapse collapse" id="644947222-collapse" style="height: 0px">
          <div class="collapse-body">
           After breakfast at 8am, embark on an adventurous day of zip lining and exploring El Valle Beach. The visionary who built the impressive tree-top guest rooms in the jungle started with the dream of building a zip line in the mountains between Samana and the North Coast. He hired German engineers, brought in American builders and spared no expense in building what may be the single best zip line experience in all of the Caribbean.
When the company that rates the safety of zip lines around the world came to walk the plank for themselves, they said it was the safest zip line theyd ever seen. You could strap your car up there and ride across safely if you had a big enough strap! From what looks like a wrecked ship atop the tallest mountain in the area, visitors begin their flight-like voyage by walking the plank. Reaching speeds of up to 40mph, you criss-cross from platform to platform with a breathtaking view! On the final zip, you glide over a jungle river and waterfall. Once you reach the bottom, youre greeted next to the stunning waterfall and a glass of local rum and coke (complements of the friendly staff). It wont be long before the crystal clear waters beckon you to wade in for a refreshing dip. Life looks very different when youre looking out from behind a waterfall. It will help your perspective on everything! Just what the doctor ordered to boost your spirits. A massive rope swing also makes for a dramatic entrance and the locals (particularly the zip line staff) make the experience that much more entertaining! End your action-packed day with dinner at the lodge.
