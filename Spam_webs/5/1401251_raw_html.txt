           The JocksByRJ Rewards Program
          </h3>
          <div class="entry-content">
           <div class="entry-body">
            <p>
             <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.underwearfanatic.com/.a/6a00d8341bfb8453ef01b7c710cacd970b-pi" style="display: inline;">
              <img alt="Clip_image002" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d8341bfb8453ef01b7c710cacd970b img-responsive" src="http://www.underwearfanatic.com/.a/6a00d8341bfb8453ef01b7c710cacd970b-300wi" style="width: 268px; display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" title="Clip_image002"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.jocksbyrj.com/" target="_self">
              Jocks By RJ
             </a>
             continues to offer their customers a successful
             <a href="http://www.jocksbyrj.com/" target="_self">
              REWARDS PROGRAM
             </a>
             . They began doing this because so many of their sales are to loyal repeat customers. They wanted to reward their customers for their continued support and it is a great incentive to attract new customers as well.
            </p>
            <p>
             Now whenever a purchase is made at Jocks By RJ, customers are rewarded with a point for every dollar spent. In the future, customers can use those points and convert them into dollars. Ten points equals 1 dollar towards another purchase at JocksByRJ.com. Thats like getting 10% back on all of your purchases. You can check your rewards account balance whenever you want by logging in to JocksByRJ.com. Please check the rules and specifics for our rewards program on the site. Something to consider during the holidays when so many people are shopping online.
            </p>
            <p>
             Check out JocksByRJ.com because there has never been a better time to shop there.
