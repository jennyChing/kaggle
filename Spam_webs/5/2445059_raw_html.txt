            Crochet Lion Amigurumi
           </a>
          </h1>
          <p>
           <a class="external" href="http://www.artfire.com/ext/shop/product_view/4821549" target="_blank">
            <img alt="" class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-4706" height="300" src="http://itsbetterhandmade.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/rsz_lion_001-284x300.jpg" width="284"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Lion is crocheted by me. The body, head, tail,and limbs is made with 100% soft acrylic yarn in mustard color.
           <br/>
           The mane is made of 100% thick wool. The wool is carefully hand sewn into the head by me. I then tie a knot close to the head to make sure the mane is secure. It can not be pulled out of the head. The color of the mane on the lion pictured is called broken reds. But, can made with any choice of color.
          </p>
