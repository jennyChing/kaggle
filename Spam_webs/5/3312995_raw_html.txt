           <h1 class="post-title">
            Robin Williams Suicide Reasons
           </h1>
           <p class="post-byline">
            by
            <a href="http://www.joythedesigner.com/author/jshariarlive-com/" rel="author" title="Posts by Joy">
             Joy
            </a>
             August 12, 2014
           </p>
           <div class="clear">
           </div>
           <div class="entry">
            <div class="entry-inner">
             <p>
              Why did Robin Williams commit suicide?
              <span style="color: #232323;">
               investigators from the Marin County Sheriffs Office are responsible for revealing the details of the recent suicide of the Hollywood actor Robin Williams. You may know him from the movies: Mrs.Doubtfire, Good will hunting and not to mention Dead Poet Society. We have come to know that the actor has been battling depression lately which led to his shocking suicide inTiburon, Northern California. The autopsy revealed that he committed suicide by hanging himself in his home. Sources say he has been battling depression and even went to rehab last month, but other sources confirm that he went to rehab to battle his Sobriety issue but it was just too late.
              </span>
             </p>
             <blockquote>
              <p>
               <span style="color: #000000;">
                This morning, I lost my husband and my best friend, while the world lost one of its most beloved artists and beautiful human beings. I am utterly heartbroken. Susan Schneider,(Robins Wife)
               </span>
               <span style="color: #000000;">
                <br/>
               </span>
              </p>
             </blockquote>
             <p>
              <span style="color: #000000;">
               On August 11th 11:55 am someone called 911, caller said a suicide had taken place(she seemed distraught).When the authorities arrived at the premises,
               <span style="color: #232323;">
                he was found unresponsive in the main house (not in his garage like others mentioned).
               </span>
               We later learned the Robin Williams left no suicide note. The first responders noticed cuts in the inside of his wrist,although this isnotthe cause of death, it stillleads us to believe he was depressed.
               <br/>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p>
              Williams had struggled with cocaine and alcohol addiction in the 80s but sources say he has been sober for the past 20 years. Toxicology will be conducted to see if Williams had taken any drugs prior to the events.
             </p>
             <p>
              We have also learned that Williams and his wife wasnt sleeping in the same bedroom. Why? We dont know!
             </p>
             <p>
              Lt. Keith Boyd lets us know, that this is an active investigation so some information will be withheld from the public until later on, but he has confirmed that the preliminary cause of death was asphyxia due to hanging. He was suspended from a belt which was wedged between a door and the door frame in a seated position slightly above ground.
             </p>
             <p>
              We give our condolences to the entire Williams family.
             </p>
