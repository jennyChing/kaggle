          <h1 class="entry-title cb-entry-title cb-single-title" itemprop="headline">
           7 of Heath Ledgers Best Roles
          </h1>
          <div class="cb-byline">
           <div class="cb-author cb-byline-element">
            <i class="fa fa-user">
            </i>
            <a href="http://badwolf.media/author/david/">

          <section class="entry-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
           <p>
            Heath Ledgers best roles are a pretty difficult list to compile any time, but today is the 7th anniversary of the method actors tragic death. And so we decided that a fitting tribute to this great actor would be to showcase 7 of his best roles. Some wont come as any surprise, others might.
           </p>
           <div class="video">
           </div>
           <p>
            In his early career, Ledger, grew in fame as a bit of a teen heartthrob. But as his career developed, it became clear that beneath his rugged good looks there was an actor with supreme talent, twinned with a troubled personal life.
           </p>
           <p>
            When you look at the list of roles Heath Ledger has played, it certainly feels short for a man with such talent. Its hard to imagine what we would have seen from him over the last 7 years, but its not hard to imagine that the entertainment world would be better with him in it.
           </p>
           <p>
            Not much more needs to be said, and so lets enjoy the memories that the blonde haired chameleon gave us. Starting with:
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <p>
           </p>
           <h2>
            7. 10 Things About You
           </h2>
           <div class="arve-wrapper arve-normal-wrapper arve-youtube-wrapper " itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/VideoObject">
            <div class="arve-embed-container">
             <iframe allowfullscreen="" class="arve-inner" frameborder="0" mozallowfullscreen="" scrolling="no" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/w6XGUhzfutc?autoplay=0" webkitallowfullscreen="">
             </iframe>
            </div>
           </div>
           <p>
            <a class="arve-hidden" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w6XGUhzfutc">
             https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w6XGUhzfutc
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            It wouldve been negligible to talk about 10 things I hate about you and not showcase Ledgers little solo number. As mentioned above, the first of Heath Ledgers best roles is a teen heartthrob, as his early career began to take off. Fortunately he managed to break out of the typecast role of good looking love interest and managed to showcase his ranging talent.
           </p>
           <p>
            Still, 10 Things I Hate About You is a classic Rom-com that has stood the test of time and is still an entertaining watch now.
           </p>
           <p>
            Next up is a very unique role. Half good looking love interest, half lead actor.
           </p>
