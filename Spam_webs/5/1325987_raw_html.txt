        <div class="at-above-post-recommended addthis_default_style addthis_toolbox at-wordpress-hide">

        <div class="wp-caption alignleft" id="attachment_12342" style="width: 229px">
         <a data-slb-active="1" data-slb-group="12339" data-slb-internal="0" href="http://www.ncscooper.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Ben_Stiller_2010_Cropped.jpg">

        <p>
         <strong>
          Culver City, CA 
         </strong>
         The recent international hack into Sonys corporate database has grabbed the headlines and raised some concerns lately about cyber security and national espionage. But little attention has been given to the actual information uncovered by the
         <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Korea" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="North Korea">
          North Korean
         </a>
         hack of Sony.
        </p>
        <p>
         Recently The Scooper has managed to get our hands on the actual files that were released by
         <a href="http://www.ncscooper.com/north-korean-leader-to-vacation-in-nevada-county/" title="North Korean Leader to Vacation in Nevada County">
