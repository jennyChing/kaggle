     <p>
      Josh and Caleb love eating English Muffin Breakfast Pizzas in the morning. It is their thing. I usually eat my

     </p>
     <p>

       <img alt="English Muffin Breakfast Pizzas on twopeasandtheirpod.com These easy breakfast pizzas are great for breakfast or anytime of the day!" class="aligncenter wp-image-17582 size-full" height="720" src="http://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/English-Muffin-Breakfast-Pizzas-1.jpg" title="English Muffin Breakfast Pizzas Recipe" width="480"/>

     <p>
      English Muffin Pizzas are perfect for breakfast because they are so easy to make! You dont have to make pizza dough from scratch, you just use store bought English muffins for the crust.
     </p>
     <p>

      <span class="item">
       <h2 class="fn">
        English Muffin Breakfast Pizzas
       </h2>
      </span>
      <div class="time">
       <p>
        <strong>
         Yield:
        </strong>
        <span class="yield">
         Serves 2-4
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Prep Time:
        </strong>
        <span class="preptime">
         5 minutes
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Cook Time:
        </strong>
        <span class="cooktime">
         10 minutes
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Total Time:
        </strong>
        <span class="duration">
         15 minutes
        </span>
       </p>
      </div>
      <div style="clear:left">
      </div>
      <div class="summary">
       <p>
        These quick and easy breakfast pizzas are great for breakfast...or anytime of the day!
       </p>
      </div>
      <h3>
       Ingredients:
      </h3>
      <div class="ingredient">
       <p>
        4 large eggs
        <br/>
        2 English Muffins
        <br/>
        1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
        <br/>
        2 strips cooked Butterball turkey bacon, chopped
        <br/>
        1 green onion, chopped
        <br/>
        Salt and black pepper, to taste
       </p>
      </div>
      <h3>
       Directions:
      </h3>
      <div class="instructions">
       <p>
        1. In a small bowl, beat eggs with a fork until well mixed. Coat a small skillet with cooking spray and heat over medium heat. Pour egg mixture into hot skillet; cook and stir until eggs are set, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
       </p>
       <p>
        2. Toast the English muffins in a toaster. Evenly divide scrambled eggs onto English muffin halves. Top with shredded cheese and bacon.
       </p>
       <p>
        3. Preheat the broiler. Place the English muffins on a baking sheet and place under hot broiler for 3-4 minutes, or until cheese is melted. Remove from oven.
       </p>
       <p>
        4. Place breakfast pizzas on a plate and garnish with the green onion. Season with salt and black pepper, to taste. Serve immediately.
       </p>
      </div>
