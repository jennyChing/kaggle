         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           We all have had dreams at some point in our lives. Life has a way of wearing us down and many of us tend to give up on ourselves or listen to others who have given up on themselves and their dreams.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A64/105BD1/a8oNq59vQg0Q39e9lEZ8qQ/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-1.jpg">
           <img alt="7 People That Did Not Give Up On Their Dream 1" class="aligncenter wp-image-1189 size-medium" height="300" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A64/105BD3/Akv_-OfsA6g_NYAfTlkGTw/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-1-265x300.jpg" width="265"/>
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           Many of us have heard the story of
           <a href="http://www.celebritynetworth.com/richest-athletes/nba/michael-jordan-net-worth/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Michael Jordan
           </a>
           being cut from his high school basketball team.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A64/105BDD/nO8dAJD683_NIacWACkJxg/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-2.jpg">
           <img alt="7 People That Did Not Give Up On Their Dream 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1190" height="300" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A64/105BDD/nO8dAJD683_NIacWACkJxg/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-2.jpg" width="400"/>
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           We have all heard the famous story about Albert Einstein
           <a href="http://rarehistoricalphotos.com/albert-einsteins-matriculation-certificate-1896/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            failing a math test
           </a>
           when he was in elementary school. That story is actually false, but young Einstein only began speaking when he was 4, reading when he was 7, and once described by a teacher as 
           <a href="http://www.perceptualtalent.com/famous-dyslexics/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            mentally slow, unsociable, and adrift forever in his foolish dreams
           </a>
           .
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A64/105BE1/q0JAUKOvDkJIu1I0YGyxvg/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-3.jpg">
           <img alt="7 People That Did Not Give Up On Their Dream 3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1191" height="402" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A64/105BE1/q0JAUKOvDkJIu1I0YGyxvg/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-3.jpg" width="402"/>
          </a>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           <a href="http://www.biography.com/people/thomas-edison-9284349" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Thomas Edison
           </a>
           invented thousands of ways that a light bulb would not work before he found one that would. Thank goodness he did not give up when most people would have.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           Whether it was to entertain, invent, inspire, or simply push the boundaries of the human spirit, here are
           <strong>
            7 more people that did not give up on their dream
           </strong>
           .
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #000000">
            1. Fred Astaire
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C0A/ZLS_6lUGvcaXdWpeZIDfyA/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-4.jpg">
           <img alt="7 People That Did Not Give Up On Their Dream 4" class="aligncenter wp-image-1192 size-medium" height="300" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C0C/uEHl8e069rz-_hUMjISHYg/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-4-238x300.jpg" width="238"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           <a href="http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000001/bio" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Fred Astaire
           </a>
           was once told that he didnt have what it took to succeed in motion pictures. At his first screen test at MGM studios, the testing director wrote:
          </span>
         </p>
         <p style="text-align: center">
          <span style="color: #000000">
           
           <em>
            Cant act. Cant sing. Balding. Can dance a little.
           </em>
           
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #000000">
            2. Vincent Van Gogh
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C6B/s4b_SWLxMxRjmHY5SpL_Pg/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-5.jpg">
           <img alt="7 People That Did Not Give Up On Their Dream 5" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1201" height="176" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C6B/s4b_SWLxMxRjmHY5SpL_Pg/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-5.jpg" width="287"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vincent_van_Gogh" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Vincent van Gogh
           </a>
           was heavily criticized by his fellow artists who referred to his style as primitive.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C11/fh3GZzYgke1uKvIH9Ah1TQ/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-6.jpg">
           <img alt="7 People That Did Not Give Up On Their Dream 6" class="aligncenter wp-image-1194 size-medium" height="232" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C13/8_fKtZAww7_FGhIXi0eR5w/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-6-300x232.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           With 900 paintings and 1,100 sketches,
           <a href="http://www.theartstory.org/artist-van-gogh-vincent.htm" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            sold only one painting
           </a>
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Red_Vineyard" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Red Vineyard at Arles (The Vigne Rouge)
           </a>
           ,
          </span>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           while he was alive.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           Today, the hundreds of paintings he could not even give away are each worth millions of dollars.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           <strong>
            3. Margret Mitchell
           </strong>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C16/D5qNvzLRsms_mFLt3p97EA/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-7.jpg">
           <img alt="7 People That Did Not Give Up On Their Dream 7" class="aligncenter wp-image-1195 size-medium" height="168" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C18/feD6SIigCOxTS1TQk-duEg/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-7-300x168.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Margaret_Mitchell" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Margret Mitchell
           </a>
           only published one book in her whole life. It was
           <a href="http://www.literaryrejections.com/best-sellers-initially-rejected/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            turned down by 38 publishers
           </a>
           but Mitchell believed in herself and her book. Her book, Gone With The Wind, won the Pulitzer Prize in 1939, sold 30 million copies, and went on to be one of the most watched movies in history.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #000000">
            4. J.K. Rowling
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C1A/LEfEMTQ_hgmtO6Q6C8uwsA/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-8.jpg">
           <img alt="7 People That Did Not Give Up On Their Dream 8" class="aligncenter wp-image-1196 size-medium" height="157" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C1C/Ip1QTle0k168XvZnAitTtg/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-8-300x157.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           The same holds true for one of my favorite authors,
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/J._K._Rowling" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            J.K. Rowling
           </a>
           .
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           After being turned down by multiple publishers, and at the brink of financial ruin, finally found a publisher for her book,
           <a href="http://www.biography.com/news/jk-rowling-harry-potter-facts" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Harry Potter
           </a>
           .
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           J.K. Rowling is now one of the wealthiest women in the world, the book has sold over 400 million copies and the
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_highest-grossing_films#Highest-grossing_franchises_and_film_series" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            movie
           </a>
           has grossed almost 8 billion dollars.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #000000">
            5.Howard Schultz
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C1E/KiZEVzNW8j3u8We69lq42Q/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-9.jpg">
           <img alt="7 People That Did Not Give Up On Their Dream 9" class="aligncenter wp-image-1197 size-medium" height="300" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C20/mQC976oSBGjiUcGLtoTmrA/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-9-300x300.jpg" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           After being
          </span>
          <a href="http://www.neweramanagement.com/blog/what-we-can-learn-from-howard-schultz/?doing_wp_cron=1426446318.5448660850524902343750" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
           turned down by banks over 242 times
          </a>
          ,
          <span style="color: #000000">
           <a href="http://www.biography.com/people/howard-schultz-21166227" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Howard Schultz
           </a>
           refused to give up on his dream. There would be no problems talking to banks for Mr. Schultz today, founder of Starbucks Coffee.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #000000">
            6. Walt Disney
           </span>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C22/wVUhnQe7IoTNDOXCdfI7cA/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-10.jpg">
           <img alt="7 People That Did Not Give Up On Their Dream 10" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1198" height="147" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C22/wVUhnQe7IoTNDOXCdfI7cA/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-10.jpg" width="343"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           A young Walt Disney was once fired because he lacked creativity.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Disney
           </a>
           has 14 theme parks, owns and operates ABC Television and ESPN and is one of the most valuable companies in the world and worth
           <a href="http://ycharts.com/companies/DIS/enterprise_value" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            196 billion dollars
           </a>
           .
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           <strong>
            7.
           </strong>
           <strong>
            A young actor
           </strong>
           refused to sell his script to producers if he could not play the lead role in his own movie. Broke, virtually homeless, but not giving up on his dream, they finally gave in and took a chance on him even though he wasunknown and talked funny.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C26/oa2wlovUafAE5j-WUMnWOw/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-11.jpg">
           <img alt="7 People That Did Not Give Up On Their Dream 11" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1199" height="236" src="http://d1zlh37f1ep3tj.cloudfront.net/wp/wblob/54592E651337D2/A65/105C26/oa2wlovUafAE5j-WUMnWOw/7-People-That-Did-Not-Give-Up-On-Their-Dream-11.jpg" width="236"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           That movie was 
           <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/sylvester-stallone-made-rocky-against-all-odds-2014-4" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            Rocky
           </a>
            and the actor, writer, was of course
           <strong>
            Sylvester Stallone
           </strong>
           . The six part Rocky series has brought in over a billion dollars.
          </span>
         </p>
         <h3>
          <strong>
           <span style="color: #000000">
            Message from Les Brown
           </span>
          </strong>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <span style="color: #000000">
           It makes you wonder how many amazing people we have never heard of with amazing dreams, ideas, and talents because they stopped dreaming. I feel that people dont really give up on their dreams, they just give up on themselves.
          </span>
         </p>
