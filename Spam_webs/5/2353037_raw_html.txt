        <!--  displaying the post image should be the job of the theme! -->
        <div class="recipe-header has-image">
         <div class="recipe-header-image">
          <img class="hidden" itemprop="image" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/scotchpotatohero.jpg" title="Hash Brown Wrapped Eggs"/>

         <!-- DESCRIPTION -->
         <span class="rpr_recipe_notes" itemprop="post">
          It's been hard for me to switch gears from the whole "
          <a href="http://www.amazon.com/Stuffed-Ultimate-Cookbook-Different-Delicious/dp/1624140114/">
           Stuffed
          </a>
          " concept. It's too late to get this guy into the book, so here it is, Stuffed DVD special features! This is a play on a scotch egg, but also a Peruvian dish called
          <a href="https://www.google.com/search?q=papas+rellenas+recipe&amp;oq=papas+rellen">
           papas rellenas
          </a>
          . The key is perfectly soft boiling the eggs so that the yolk stays soft and comes running out when you cut into it.
          <div align="center">
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato01.jpg"/>
           Roast the potatoes until tender.
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato02.jpg"/>
           Mix with some flour, salt, herbs, and spices. Just enough flour to bring everything together.
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato03.jpg"/>
           Soft boil some eggs using
           <a href="http://www.macheesmo.com/2012/12/soft-boiled-eggs-a-giveaway/">
            this method
           </a>
           . Be really gentle while peeling and handling them!
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato04.jpg"/>
           The potato mixture is mashed and ready.
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato05.jpg"/>
           With well floured hands and surface, spread out some potato and roll it around your egg. You want this to be as thin as you can around the egg, but also as tight and secure as you can make it.
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato06.jpg"/>
           Perfect.
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato07.jpg"/>
           Fry em up until browned all around.
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato08.jpg"/>
           Just another potato...
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato09.jpg"/>
           With a suprise inside!
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato10.jpg"/>
           Wait for it...
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato12.jpg"/>
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato13.jpg"/>
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato14.jpg"/>
           <img alt="" border="0" src="http://www.thefoodinmybeard.com/content/scotchpotato/scotchpotato15.jpg"/>
           Pop a little sriracha on this thing and it will be the best breakfast you have had in months!
          </div>
         </span>

          <h3>
           Instructions
          </h3>
          <!-- INFO LINE (Times) -->
          <ol class="recipe-instructions">
           <li itemprop="recipeInstructions">
            <span class="recipe-instruction ">
             Roast the potatoes until tender.  Mix with some flour, salt, herbs, and spices. Just enough flour to bring everything together.  Get your soft boiled eggs (method linked above) and gently wrap a think layer of the potato mixture around the egg.  Fry until browned and serve.
