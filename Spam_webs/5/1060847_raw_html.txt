      <p>
       National Philanthropy Day  (NPD) will be celebrating 28 years in recognizing the great contributions of the non-profit community and the profound impact that philanthropy has on our society. NPD has a rich tradition and dates back to 1986 when President Ronald Reagan signed a proclamation officially recognizing November 15 as National Philanthropy Day .
      </p>
      <p>
       The Association of Fundraising Professionals Big Bend Chapter (Big Bend AFP) will celebrate National Philanthropy Day  two days early on November 13, 2014, from 11:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. at Capital City Country Club. During this event, Big Bend AFP will recognize the communities Heart of Gold recipients, Philanthropist of the Year and Community Partner of the Year.
      </p>
      <p>
       Big Bend AFP is excited to announce Founder of 4Generations Institute Jack Levine as the Keynote Speaker for this celebration. As an expert in developing and delivering messages to the media, public officials, and a diverse network of advocates on the value of preventive investments in members of our community, Jack will enlighten attendees on the subject of Bridging our Generations.
      </p>
      <p>
       Join us as we honor Philanthropist of the Year Dr. Charlotte Maguire, Mother of the Florida State Medical School and Community Partner of the Year Gulf Winds Track Club.
      </p>
      <p>
       Dr. Maguire was instrumental in creating the College of Medicine at Florida State University; hence, the fitting name, Mother. She has enjoyed a distinguished career and has spent a lifetime dedicated to caring for children and the underserved. Dr. Maguire has received numerous accolades including the prestigious Great Floridian award.
      </p>
      <p>
       Big Bend AFP is honored to recognize Dr. Charlotte Maguire as Philanthropist of the Year.
      </p>
      <p>
       Gulf Winds Track Club resulted from the idea of a grass roots community organization to conduct local running events. The Club was officially incorporated in 1977 with 1972 Olympic 10,000 meter runner Jeff Galloway as Club president.
      </p>
      <p>
       Today, the Club consists of a diverse group of members with a variety of interests and abilities. Our community is fortunate to have Gulf Winds Track Club and Big Bend AFP is honored to recognize them as Community Partner of the Year.
      </p>
      <p>
       For additional information or to purchase tickets visit www.afpbigbend.org. Tickets are $30 for Members and $50 for non-Members. Capital City Country Club is located at 1601 Golf Terrace Drive.
      </p>
