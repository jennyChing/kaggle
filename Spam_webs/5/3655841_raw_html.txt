         C7's latest entry in their
         <em class="bbc">
          World War Cthulhu
         </em>
         series.
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         <em class="bbc">
          World War Cthulhu: Cold War
         </em>
         is a 1970s Cold War espionage setting for the
         <em class="bbc">
          Call of Cthulhu
         </em>
         roleplaying game promising to
         <em class="bbc">
          "[throw] you into one of the most paranoid and chaotic conflicts of the 20th century. The tangled webs of the spymasters tear and rejoin, double and triple agents make their moves and counter-moves, and its hard to be sure which side youre really working for.
         </em>
         <em class="bbc">
          Nuclear destruction hangs over everyone, and a few people at the heart of western intelligence continue their personal battle against the insidious influence of a much older enemy
         </em>
         <em class="bbc">
          "
         </em>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         The Kickstarter is funded (already at more than twice the original goal, likely in no small part to Cubicle 7's reputation in this area) and runs until 21st July.
        </p>
