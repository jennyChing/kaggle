       <div class="content">
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.djbooth.net/index/artists/info/wax" title="Wax">
          Wax
         </a>
         and
         <strong>
          Herbal T
         </strong>
         : rap game Olsen twins. While you might think you are tripping or still drunk from New Years Eve, dont worry; that isnt really Booth favorite Wax, but rather his twin brother (hence the Olsen twin comparison). Not only do they look alike, but the brothers can both tear up the mic. On his Booth debut,
         <strong>
          <em>
           Thats That
          </em>
         </strong>
         , Herbal T selects the perfect beat for his dense mic style.
         <strong>
          Lack of Afro
         </strong>
         s funky, bass-driven boardwork is cool and relatively low-key, giving Herbal T ample room to perform his lyrical acrobatics. He delivers his tongue-twisting bars with confidence and ease, making this track feel effortless and hammering home its slick, funky vibe.
         <em>
          Thats That
         </em>
         is set to appear on a currently-untitled album, due out later this year.
        </p>
