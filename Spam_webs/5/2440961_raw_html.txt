        <div>
         Fiber one pancakes are our FAVORITE  when we make them 
        </div>
        <div>
         I dont have children yet, but for some reason it seems like my husband and I buy enough cereal to have at least 5. Just recently we purchased
         <a href="http://www.fiberone.com/product/cereals.aspx" target="blank">
          Fiber One with Honey Clusters
         </a>
         . Im not the type to eat cereal for breakfast everyday, Id rather eggs, oatmeal, or yogurt and fruit for breakfast. So in order to de-clutter our cereal stock I made pancakes! Who cant use a little extra fiber in their diet?
         <img alt=":-)" class="lazy lazy-hidden wp-smiley" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://www.courtneyssweets.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         <noscript>
          <img alt=":-)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.courtneyssweets.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
         </noscript>
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <span>
          <b>
           <i>
            Fiber One Chocolate Chip Pancakes
           </i>
          </b>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
           Makes 7-9 medium sized pancakes
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
           1 1/2 Cups Fiber One Cereal, Honey Clusters
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
           3/4 Cup All Purpose Flour
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
           3 1/2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
           1/2 Teaspoon Salt
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
           1 1/2 Cup Milk
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
           1 Teaspoon Vanilla Bean Paste
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
           1 Egg
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
           1-2 Tablespoons Butter
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
           1/2 Cup Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips (optional)
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/courtneyssweets/5583205234/" title="Fiber One Chocolate Chip Pancakes by Courtneys Sweets, on Flickr">
           <span>
            <img alt="Fiber One Chocolate Chip Pancakes" class="lazy lazy-hidden" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://www.courtneyssweets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/5583205234_49560bce3f.jpg" height="375" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="500"/>
            <noscript>
             <img alt="Fiber One Chocolate Chip Pancakes" height="375" src="http://www.courtneyssweets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/5583205234_49560bce3f.jpg" width="500"/>
            </noscript>
           </span>
          </a>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
          </span>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <div>
           <span>
            In a blender or food processor, crush the Fiber One Cereal. Once it is crumbly place the flour into the blender and pulse on low.
           </span>
          </div>
          <div>
           <span>
           </span>
          </div>
          <div>
           <span>

              <img alt="Fiber One Chocolate Chip Pancakes" height="375" src="http://www.courtneyssweets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/5582619561_afa15b3c6d.jpg" width="500"/>
             </noscript>
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <div>
          <span>
           <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/courtneyssweets/5582620115/" title="Fiber One Chocolate Chip Pancakes by Courtneys Sweets, on Flickr">

           <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/courtneyssweets/5583206914/" title="Fiber One Chocolate Chip Pancakes by Courtneys Sweets, on Flickr">
            <img alt="Fiber One Chocolate Chip Pancakes" class="lazy lazy-hidden" data-lazy-type="image" data-src="http://www.courtneyssweets.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/5583206914_71112c7a72_m.jpg" height="180" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" width="240"/>

         </div>
         <div>
          <div>
           <span>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div>
          <span>
           Place the cereal flour mixture in a bowl and add the remaining ingredients. (excluding butter and chocolate chips)Whisk together, meanwhile heat a nonstick pan on medium-low with butter. ( I used my
           <a href="http://www.manpans.com/" target="blank">
            Manpan
           </a>
           , so I had to coat in Canola Oil, then a tab of butter for flavor) Once the butter is melted, start cooking!
          </span>
         </div>
         <div>
          <span>
           I used a large cookie scoop for my pancakes, you could also use a 1/4 measuring cup. Place about 10 chocolate chips on the top of the pancake once its in the pan. Once you see tiny air bubbles you can flip your pancakes.
          </span>
