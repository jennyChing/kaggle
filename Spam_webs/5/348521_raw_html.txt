        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          I have a furry little garden thief. I expected it to be a bunny. But no. Its this little girl:
         </p>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_651" style="width: 410px">
          <a href="http://www.lifewithlevi.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_2727.jpg">
           <span class="pibfi_pinterest ">

            <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://www.lifewithlevi.com/i-have-a-garden-thief-and-its-not-who-i-expected/&amp;media=http://www.lifewithlevi.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/IMG_2727.jpg&amp;description=I Have A Garden Thief (and its not who I expected)')">
            </span>
           </span>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           That's my cucumber she's noshing on.
          </p>
         </div>
         <p>
          Turns out all three of my dogs really like cucumbers. Enough that Bailey, my chihuahua, swiped one from the garden. Whod have thought vegetables would be so popular with the canine crowd?
         </p>
