          <p align="justify">
           Hi, loves! Happy Monday!  Are you looking for a chic ring pillow for your wedding?  Want something thats chic, handmade and perfect for the spring or summer?  Weve found eight of em!  Recently I popped into
           <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/laurastark" target="_self">
            Laura Stark Jewelry &amp; Bridal Accessories Studio
           </a>
           and I love her collection of linen ring pillows.  These are ideal for your wedding and will add to the beautiful decor in your home, too!  Read on to see which eight linen ring pillows weve hand-picked to share with you today and tell us which one is your favorite!
          </p>
          <h1>
           Linen Ring Pillows
          </h1>
          <p>
           <strong>
            1. Something Blue
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           This light blue linen ring pillow has white embroidery lace.  It is just the perfect touch for your ceremony and adds something blue in an instant.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://emmalinebride.com/ceremony/linen-ring-pillows/" target="_self">
            <img alt="light blue lace ring pillow via 8 Chic Linen Ring Pillows" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-88318" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/emmalinebride.com/wp-content/uploads/light-blue-lace-ring-pillow.jpg?resize=570%2C443"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            2. Pink Pleats Ring Pillow
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           This pillow is pretty in pink: it pairs a romantic combination of white and pink pleats as trim across the center of the pillow.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/laurastark" target="_self">
            <img alt="pink pleats ring pillow via 8 Chic Linen Ring Pillows" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-88319" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/emmalinebride.com/wp-content/uploads/pink-pleats-ring-pillow.jpg?resize=570%2C560"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            3. Lace Chambery Linen Pillow
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           This pillow is named for Chambery, France, and features a hand-stitched applique across a linen ring pillow.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/laurastark" target="_self">
            <img alt="lace ring pillow chambery via 8 Chic Linen Ring Pillows" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-88320" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/emmalinebride.com/wp-content/uploads/lace-ring-pillow-chambery.jpg?resize=570%2C413"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            4. Green Celtic Knot
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           The green celtic knot linen ring pillow is perfect for celebrating your Irish heritage!  Dark green thread is hand-embroidered onto a white linen ring pillow.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/laurastark" target="_self">
            <img alt="green celtic knot ring pillow via 8 Chic Linen Ring Pillows" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-88322" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/emmalinebride.com/wp-content/uploads/green-celtic-knot-ring-pillow.jpg?resize=570%2C657"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            5. Mustard Yellow Ring Pillow
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           This ring pillow features a lovely line flower and white tulle atop the pillow.  Ribbons, made of lace, hold your rings to the pillow.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/laurastark" target="_self">
            <img alt="mustard yellow ring pillow" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-88323" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/emmalinebride.com/wp-content/uploads/mustard-yellow-ring-pillow.jpg?resize=570%2C380"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            6. Tulle Ring Pillow
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           This linen ring pillow features a luxurious piece of tulle across the center.  It also includes a pretty embroidered ribbon to hold your rings.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/laurastark" target="_self">
            <img alt="tulle ring pillow linen" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-88324" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/emmalinebride.com/wp-content/uploads/tulle-ring-pillow-linen.jpg?resize=570%2C481"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            7. Blue Rosette
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           This is another pillow with something blue, but in the form of a sweet and subtle blue rosette atop a bed of ivory tulle petals.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/laurastark" target="_self">
            <img alt="blue rosette ring pillow via 8 Chic Linen Ring Pillows" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-88325" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/emmalinebride.com/wp-content/uploads/blue-rosette-ring-pillow.jpg?resize=570%2C397"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            8. Lace Ring Pillow with Rhinestone Button
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p align="justify">
           This ring bearer pillow features lace which has been hand-sewn over a linen ring pillow.  It is finished with a rhinestone button.

           <a href="https://www.etsy.com/shop/laurastark" target="_self">
            <img alt="lace ring pillow toulon via 8 Chic Linen Ring Pillows" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-88326" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/emmalinebride.com/wp-content/uploads/lace-ring-pillow-toulon.jpg?resize=570%2C641"/>
