          <p>
           The cold is gone, the flowers have bloomed, the mornings are brighter, all marking the startof the new season, spring. The change of seasons calls for switching the fragrance we have been wearing all winter for something fresh and vibrant. Floral scents are no wonder are dominant during spring. Here are the best new and sexy perfumes for spring.
          </p>
          <h3>
           Prive Pivoine Suzhou by Giorgio Armani
          </h3>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://womenpulse.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/10-Best-Spring-Fragrances-2015-2.jpg">
            <img alt="10 Best Spring Fragrances 2015" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3909" height="604" src="http://womenpulse.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/10-Best-Spring-Fragrances-2015-2.jpg" width="461"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Notes:
           </strong>
           The perfume opens with notes of mandarin, pink pepper and raspberry. Peony and may rose absolute form the heart of the fragrance and are enhanced by the base notes of amber, musk and patchouli.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Perfume:
           </strong>
           The fragrance is inspired by the gardens of Asia and is the celebration of Chinas beloved flower, peony. The composition is based on harmony and elegance accentuated with the floral notes of peony and rose.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Price:
           </strong>
           $165
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Where to buy:
           </strong>
           Buy it at
           <a href="http://www.armanibeauty.com/fragrance/armani-prive/les-eaux/pivoine-suzhou.aspx" target="_blank">
            ArmaniBeauty.com
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3>
           La Panthre Eau De Parfum Lgre by Cartier
          </h3>
          <p>
           <a href="http://womenpulse.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/10-Best-Spring-Fragrances-2015-3.jpg">
            <img alt="10 Best Spring Fragrances 2015" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3910" height="391" src="http://womenpulse.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/10-Best-Spring-Fragrances-2015-3.jpg" width="444"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The Notes:
           </strong>
           Chypre, gardenia, musk, monoi, tiare flower, vanilla essence
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            The perfume:
           </strong>
           A floral fragrance born out of delicate gardenia and velvety notes of musk, the perfume reflects a liberated and passionate woman. Bright and musky, this fragrance is surely for the one with an expensive taste.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Price:
           </strong>
           $76
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Where to buy:
           </strong>
           Buy it at
           <a href="http://www.cartier.us/collections/fragrances/womens-fragrances/la-panthere/fs327037-la-panthere-eau-de-parfum" target="_blank">
            Cartier.com
           </a>
          </p>
          <h3>
           Brit Rhythm by Burberry
          </h3>
          <p>
