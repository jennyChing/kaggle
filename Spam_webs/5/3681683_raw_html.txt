     <h2 style="text-align:center; font-size:24px">
      <a href="http://storiesfromlastweekend.com/boom-goes-dynamite/">
       BOOM GOES THE DYNAMITE!
      </a>
     </h2>
     <h3 style="padding-top:5px; color:#000">
      April 7th, 2014
     </h3>
     <p>
      About 7 years ago, there was a guy who subbed in as a sportscaster for a day and all he would say is Boom goes the Dynamite! He was an internet sensation and Im sure you can find him on You Tube. We played basketball last weekend with a guy who watched that clip a few too many times!!
     </p>
     <p>
      I was playing basketball at a Rec Center in Minneapolis last week and I dont think Ive ever laughed so hard. I was there with a few friends and we pick up a 5th to run with. We win about 3 games in a row, but unfortunately the other guy we are playing with has to go and so we need to pick up a 5th. Some Rec Cens you can just pick up whoever you want if you are winning, but here they go by The Board.
     </p>
     <p>
      The next on the board is a guy named Kyle. He doesnt look very athletic at all, but Ive been fooled before so we are hoping for the best. Kyle didnt touch the ball about the first 4 possessions, because he was just sitting about 10 feet behind the three point line. Why would you pass a guy like that the ball??? On our 5th possession, I started feeling a little bad for the kid though, who obviously didnt know how to play basketball at all, so I passed him the ball. He was still 10 feet from behind the line when he launched a shot. As it left his hand, he yelled Boom goes the Dynamite as loud as anyone could possibly yell. The guy hadnt said one word since stepping on the court, so it took us all by surprise.
     </p>
     <p>
      I cant do justice to the shot itself. It was about 10 feet short and about 15 feet wide. A worse basketball shot, I have never seen! Two players on our team fell to the ground laughing uncontrollably. The people on the sidelines started busting out in laughter. Kyle seemed to take it in stride and jotted back down to go play defense.
     </p>
     <p>
      The game continued and it was pretty intense so we didnt pass Kyle the ball again. We were down 14-13 in a game to 15 (3s count as 2s) when we had a chance to win if we could hit a 3 pointer. For those of you who have seen Hoosiers, you are probably thinking about the lousy kid Ollie who hits the game winning shot. I wish that was what happened
      <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://storiesfromlastweekend.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      <a href="http://storiesfromlastweekend.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/draw7.jpg">
       <img alt="draw7" class="alignnone wp-image-335" height="496" src="http://storiesfromlastweekend.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/draw7-1024x885.jpg" width="574"/>
      </a>
     </p>
     <p>
      We pass the ball around a few times and a pass intended for me gets deflected to Kyle. Hes at his usual spot, 10 feet behind the 3 point line. He launches the shot, and even louder than the first time BOOM GOES THE DYNAMITE!! I can already hear the laughter before the shot even leaves his hand. The first shot looked like Jordan compared to this one. It started right and just kept drifting. If I didnt know better, Id think he was aiming for the corner of the gym. Probably 5 members of the two teams fell to the ground laughing. They didnt want to make fun of the kid, it was just that funny. The sidelines looked like a comedy show with everyone busting up.
     </p>
     <p>
      Things really couldnt get any funnier, but after everyone gets back up and we run back down on defense, Kyle says Looks like Im just a little bit off today. Yeah, just a little bit buddy!!!
     </p>
     <p>

      </em>
