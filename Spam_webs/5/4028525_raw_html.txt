       <span class="entry-title" style="display: none;">
        Travel Connection: My ultimate world domination partner in crime
       </span>
       <div class="post-content">
        <h2>
         <em>
          A travel connection unlike any other
         </em>
        </h2>
        <p>
         Ive definitely made some incredible connections with people in my life  but why is it that a travel connection seems so much deeper? Of my travel connections, there were some which were all but forgotten shortly after, like that guy who operates the Hookah shop in a back alley in Luxor. On the other side of the spectrum, there were other travel connections which will last a lifetime, like one made during my most recent travels through Thailand and Borneo with fellow
         <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #0000ff;">
          <a href="http://www.manvsclock.com" target="_blank">
           <span style="color: #0000ff; text-decoration: underline;">
            travel blogger Anthony Middleton
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         (pictured below with another great travel blogger and friend,
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          <span style="color: #0000ff;">
           <a href="http://www.nomadicnotes.com" target="_blank">
            <span style="color: #0000ff; text-decoration: underline;">
             James T. Clark
            </span>
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
         ).
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_945" style="width: 630px">
         <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Chiang-Mai-Songrkan-Ian-Anthony-James.jpg">
          <img alt="Chiang Mai, Thailand - Songrkan travel connections" class="size-large wp-image-945 [ftmt_id]" height="465" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Chiang-Mai-Songrkan-Ian-Anthony-James-1024x768.jpg" title="Chiang Mai - Songrkan - Ian Anthony James" width="620"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Travel Bloggers vs Chiang Mai, Thailands Songkran Festival
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_948" style="width: 630px">
         <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Kuching-Semenggoh-Ian-Anthony.jpg">
          <img alt="Kuching, Borneo - Semenggoh Rehabilitation Centre" class="size-large wp-image-948 [ftmt_id]" height="412" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Kuching-Semenggoh-Ian-Anthony-1024x682.jpg" title="Kuching - Semenggoh - Ian &amp; Anthony" width="620"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Checking out Orangutans in the wild with Anthony (of Man vs Clock) in Borneo
         </p>
        </div>
        However, without taking away from any of the other special and life changing travel connections Ive made, Id have to say my most meaningful travel connection was actually with someone I already knew. Having worked in the travel industry with an
        <em>
         adventure travel
        </em>
        tour operator for 6 years came with some perks  the best of which was the annual discounted tour, anywhere in the world they operated. In speaking with a work colleague, Miss Erin Schildermans, we both expressed an interest in travelling toAntarcticaaround the same time. We figured it would be nice to share such a unique experience with someone we know, and so why not try travelling together. We were good enoughacquaintancesat the time to be considered friends, though most of our associating happened down at the local watering hole and hadnt yet ventured much deeper than that.
        <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_935" style="width: 630px">
         <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Erin-Africa-1427.jpg">
          <img alt="Ngorogoro Crater, Tanzania - We made it!" class="size-large wp-image-935 [ftmt_id]" height="348" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Erin-Africa-1427-1024x576.jpg" title="Africa - Tanzania - Ngorogoro Crater - Erin and Ian" width="620"/>
         </a>
         <p class="wp-caption-text">
          Ngorogoro Crater, Tanzania  A wildlife lovers paradise!
         </p>
        </div>
        <div>
         Our dream trip was in the final stages of being approved and confirmed when a tragic accident actually caused the vessel to
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a1cDBsLZNAg" target="_blank" title="">
          sink after hitting an ice burg
         </a>
         . Once assessing the situation and knowing that all passengers were safe, it began to
         <em>
          sink in
         </em>
         that ourpossibilitiesof doing such a unique voyage lay 20 leagues below the sea along with the ship that was intended to take us there. In a frantic last minute scramble, we asked each other where the other had intended to travel next. We both then realized that travelling around eastern Africa was actually a common goal and desire. With some last minute arrangements, less than a month later we took off on what would be a life changing experience for both of us, making our travelconnectionthat much stronger.
        </div>
        <div>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_936" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Erin-Africa-1895.jpg">
           <img alt="Netherlands, Amsterdam - Cone Fries" class="size-large wp-image-936 [ftmt_id]" height="348" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Erin-Africa-1895-1024x576.jpg" title="Netherlands, Amsterdam - Cone Fries - Ian and Erin" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Eating Cone-Fries in Amsterdam!
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         Two long flights with8-12 hour layovers (at least they were inAmsterdamandJohannesburg), 90+ hours of driving 12 hour days, and 3 weeks in a tent will either make two people want to kill each other, or will make them better friends than ever before. Fortunately for Erin and myself, it was the latter. After surviving this crucial test, we became travelling buddies, and decided to take all of our annual trips together, in addition to several other independent ones as well.
        </div>
        <div>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_937" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_1100.jpg">
           <img alt="Peace from Takayama, Japan" class="size-large wp-image-937 [ftmt_id]" height="465" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_1100-1024x768.jpg" title="Takayama, Japan - Erin and Ian peace" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Peace from Takayama, Japan
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_938" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_1189.jpg">
           <img alt="Nikko, Japan - prayers in a samurai temple" class="size-large wp-image-938 [ftmt_id]" height="465" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_1189-1024x768.jpg" title="Nikko, Japan - prayers in a samurai temple" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Prayers in a samurai temple in Nikko, Japan
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_939" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_1464.jpg">
           <img alt="Kamakura Japan - Mystic temple Jump Shot" class="size-large wp-image-939 [ftmt_id]" height="465" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_1464-1024x768.jpg" title="Kamakura Japan - Mystic temple Jump Shot" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Mystic Temple (necessary) Jump Shot in Kamakura, Japan!
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         Together, weve now conquered 6 continents together, including Antarctica.Oceaniais our last stop on our world domination tour together, though weve both been there independently, thus knocking it down a few notches on our to-do lists.This is a feat Id say few have accomplished in their lifetime, let alone being able to do so with the same person.Its an incredibly powerful travel connection for both of us!
        </div>
        <div>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_940" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_1957.jpg">
           <img alt="Buenos Aires, Argentina - Taking it in" class="size-large wp-image-940 [ftmt_id]" height="930" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_1957-682x1024.jpg" title="Buenos Aires, Argentina - Taking it in - Ian and Erin" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Taking it all in  Buenos Aires, Argentina
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_947" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_2083.jpg">
           <img alt="Tierra del Fuego National Park, Argentina" class="size-large wp-image-947 [ftmt_id]" height="412" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_2083-1024x682.jpg" title="Tierra del Fuego, Argentina - Ian, Erin, Ben" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Three Amigos in the Southern most National Park in the World! Tierra del Fuego!
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         Having seen each other at our best and worst (oh the stories Im leaving out  maybe save them for another blog entry), weve been through a lot together. This of course means that all those other connections one thinks of with the act of travelling, weve both shared. Facebook would tell you we have the most Common Friends and travel connections out of any other  I could have told you that without a doubt in my mind. Perhaps unexpectedly at first, Erin has become
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iJPFSNu_QNs" target="_blank" title="">
          the
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iJPFSNu_QNs" target="_blank" title="">
          P
         </a>
         <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iJPFSNu_QNs" target="_blank" title="">
          inky to my Brain
         </a>
         (I know Ill get punched for that one  its probably the other way around), a best friend through thick and thin, and as close to a sister as Ill ever know.
        </div>
        <div>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_942" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_2336.jpg">
           <img alt="Antarctica - Erin and Ian" class="size-large wp-image-942 [ftmt_id]" height="412" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_2336-1024x682.jpg" title="Antarctica - Erin and Ian" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           WE MADE IT! Antarctica zodiac cruisin!!
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_943" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_2544.jpg">
           <img alt="Port Lockroy, Antarctica" class="size-large wp-image-943 [ftmt_id]" height="412" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_2544-1024x682.jpg" title="Port Lockroy, Antarctica - Ian, Erin, Ben" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           The Ice Pirates makin it to Port Lockroy, Antractica
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div>
         Im curious to think where our friendship would be now had we not taken the leap of faith in travelling together all those years ago  all that matters is that it did happen, and this travel connection was able to be made. For thatIm grateful.
        </div>
        <div>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_944" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_4006.jpg">
           <img alt="White Temple, Chiang Rai" class="size-large wp-image-944 [ftmt_id]" height="412" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/IMG_4006-1024x682.jpg" title="White Temple, Chiang Rai - Ian and Erin" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           The bizarre and incredible White Temple in Chiang Rai, Thailand
          </p>
         </div>
         <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_946" style="width: 630px">
          <a href="http://www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN0502.jpg">
           <img alt="Temples in Chiang Mai, Thailand" class="size-large wp-image-946 [ftmt_id]" height="826" src="//www.wheresidewalksend.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/DSCN0502-768x1024.jpg" title="Temples in Chiang Mai, Thailand - Ian and Erin" width="620"/>
          </a>
          <p class="wp-caption-text">
           Exploring the temples of Northern Thailand
          </p>
         </div>
        </div>
