                  Marriage marks a new beginning in the life of a groom and a bride. Certain traditions and ceremonies make it more memorable and special. One of such occasions is Engagement in which both bride and groom exchange rings with each other. A woman really appreciates it if her fiance gifts a diamond ring to her on the engagement. Diamonds are preferred by each and every lady because of its classy look that enhances their beauty. Buying an engagement ring is the most important decision for a man. Usually men consider this task as a bit scary and difficult as this decision is based on a major factor i.e. budget. As the prices of diamonds have an upward rising graph, it becomes a thoughtful thing for a man to buy a diamond ring that works well with his pre-determined budget. For women, diamond ring is a symbol of emotional bonding and happiness. Therefore, to make her fell special, a man should buy a diamond ring for her to make her feel special and on top of the world.

                  Now, the question arises that How to buy an inexpensive engagement ring that is of high quality and matches the persona of your woman as well. Here are few tips that you can keep in mind while looking for a cheap diamond ring:
                  <br/>
                  <br/>
                   You should keep in mind the 4 Cs that are cut, clarity, color and carat of a diamond.
                  <br/>
                   Usually a diamond that has a high carat value is much expensive than a diamond having low carat value.
                  <br/>
                   Rather than going for a colored diamond, you can go for the basic one as it would cost you much lesser than the colored ones.
                  <br/>
                   Clarity defines the amount of inclusions that a diamond has. Clarity enhanced diamonds cost a bit more than those diamonds that have minute natural inclusions in them.
                  <br/>
                   Since buying a diamond is a onetime investment, therefore, you should plan your budget accordingly.
                  <br/>
                   You should choose the metal of the ring as an inexpensive one such as yellow or white gold so that you can afford it comfortably.
                  <br/>
                   You should buy an engagement ring from a credible jeweler that provides you with
                  <a href="http://www.freddydiamonds.com/" target="_blank">
                   high quality and certified jewelry
                  </a>
                  .
                  <br/>
                   It should carry a certificate of authentication with it as a proof of purity.
                  <br/>
