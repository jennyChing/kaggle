          Feeling like you need to replenish your energy and uplift your mood? Try a few or all of the following energy-boosting yoga poses recommended by expert instructors.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           Padahastasana (Standing Forward Bend)
           <br/>
           <img alt="" src="http://cdn-jpg.theactivetimes.net/sites/default/files/u181360/embed_1_forward_bend_shutterstock_188291183.jpg" style="width: 460px; height: 305px;"/>

As well as being a solid grounding pose it also charges up the root giving vitality and a connection with the powerful energy of the earth.
