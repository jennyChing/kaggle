        <p>
         High-intensity discharge (HID) lamps have been all the rage in the car market for several years now, so it was only a matter of time until the tech trickled down to the personal lighting market. The
         <a href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B001U04MEY/uncrate-20" target="_blank">
          Stanley HID Spotlight
         </a>
         ($TBA) packs a 35 Watt HID bulb in its yellow, black, and silver chassis, along with an unbreakable polycarbonate lens, a metal rollbar for durability, a rechargeable battery with a 35 minute full power runtime, a 2-way dimmer for extending battery life, a CEC AC charger, and a carry lanyard.
        </p>
