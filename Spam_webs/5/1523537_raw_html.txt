     <h1 class="title">
      <a href="http://www.superadrianme.com/technology/casio-exilim-ex-10-camera-review/" rel="bookmark" title="Permalink to Casio EXILIM EX-10  The Only Camera You Need For Travel">
       Casio EXILIM EX-10  The Only Camera You Need For Travel
      </a>
     </h1>
     <div class="meta-author">
      Posted by
      <a href="http://www.superadrianme.com/author/kpat/" rel="author" title="Posts by Dennis">
       Dennis
      </a>
     </div>
     <div class="clearfix post-77073 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-camera-and-camcorder category-technology" id="post-77073">
      <div class="entry">
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_77075" style="width: 700px">
        <img alt="Casio EXILIM EX-10 - The Only Camera You Need For Travel" class="size-full wp-image-77075" height="388" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Fullscreen-capture-612014-10911-AM.jpg" width="690"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Casio EXILIM EX-10  The Only Camera You Need For Travel
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
        You want to travel light for your vacation, but still want to be able to take gorgeous selfies, capturing slow motion video of jumping into the pool or even record that mesmerizing sunset with a timelapse video. With the
        <strong>
         Casio EXILIM EX-10
        </strong>
        digital compact camera you can achieve all these without being a photography expert.
        <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
         The EX-10 is packed with all the essential features to grant you the freedom to creatively record your photo and video journal just the way you want it.
        </span>
       </p>
       <p>
        <span style="font-size: 14px; line-height: 1.5em;">
         Here are some of the cool &amp; useful features on Casio EXILIM EX-10:
        </span>
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Intelligent Bracketing Mode
         </strong>
          achieve more creativity and get your best shot with just one click. It sounds more complicated that it really is.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Makeup Mode
         </strong>
          get instantaneous radiant and smooth complexion for your selfies.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          High Speed Video Recording Mode
         </strong>
          from 120fps to 1,000fps you will be able to record the world in slow motion.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Motion Trigger Sensor
         </strong>
          no more remote control, just wave to the camera and get ready to shoot. This is really too easy though you may look like you at waving at someone everytime you want to take a photo.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Flip Stand
         </strong>
          forgot to bring your tripod? Dont worry because you can just use the built-in flip stand.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          Time-lapseVideo Mode
         </strong>
          comes with several preset settings based on scenarios like Clouds, Sunset, Night Scene and many more.
        </li>
        <li>
         <strong>
          WiFi Connectivity
         </strong>
          its easy to share your pictures and video with friends and family on social media.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Heres a look at how these modes on EX-10 creatively aid us in accomplishing our daily photo &amp; video log:
       </p>
       <h2>
        Motion Trigger Sensor with Flip Stand
       </h2>
       <p>
        It is so easy to take multiple group shots even when waiting for the train to arrive. Flip the stand and the display screen, put it on the floor or any flat surface, press the trigger and wave at the screen icon when ready. Trust me, once it starts, you cannot help but to take multiple candid group shots with it. The best way to use these shots are to make them into animated gifs using online freeware like
        <a href="https://imgflip.com/images-to-gif" target="_blank">
         imgflip
        </a>
        and share them on your social media.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_77635" style="width: 700px">
        <img alt="Easy to take group shot with EX-10" class="size-full wp-image-77635" height="518" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/CIMG3923.jpg" width="690"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Easy to take group shot with EX-10
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_77636" style="width: 370px">
        <img alt="Easy to take group shot with EX-10" class="size-full wp-image-77636" height="270" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/692ce.gif" width="360"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Take multiple shots and create Animated GIF with freeware online
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_76767" style="width: 700px">
        <img alt="Casio EX-10 on flip stand" class="size-full wp-image-76767" height="400" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/EX-10.jpg" width="690"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Casio EX-10 on flip stand
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Intelligent Bracketing Mode
       </h2>
       <p>
        This feature is unique to EX-10 and no other camera in the world has this cool feature yet. Sometimes if you only have five seconds to get your shot right, intelligent bracketing mode will come in very handy to accomplish your task in time, just like a pro. Even if you are not a good photographer, this mode will definitely help you be a better one, by giving you preset options suitable for different scenarios. Once a preset option is chosen, just hit the trigger and the camera will take multiple shots with different settings for each shot and you are bound to get a good shot in this mode. You no longer need to waste time scrambling through the settings, shoot then adjust settings to get the right shot.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_77637" style="width: 700px">
        <img alt="EX-10 Intelligent Bracketing mode" class="size-full wp-image-77637" height="488" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/EX-102.jpg" width="690"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         EX-10 Intelligent Bracketing mode
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_77638" style="width: 700px">
        <img alt="model: Moonberry from Moonberry.com" class="size-full wp-image-77638" height="518" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/CIMG3278o.jpg" width="690"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         model: Moonberry from Moonberry.com
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_77718" style="width: 700px">
        <img alt="Casio EX-10 Night Landscape" class="size-full wp-image-77718" height="488" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Collages3.jpg" width="690"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Casio EX-10 Night Landscape  Intelligent Bracketing mode and select the best out of the 9 shots
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
       </p>
       <p>
        Once you get the hang of choosing the right variables to use for your shots, its easy to achieve stunning pictures without much effort and in a snap. The variables include Shutter Speed, Aperture, White Balance, Exposure, Saturation, Contrast and even Focus Points.
       </p>
       <p>
        Check out what the professional photographers have to say about the Intelligent Bracketing mode:
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="388" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/bRrADco_Bsw" width="690">
        </iframe>
       </center>
       <h2>
        Time-Lapse Video
       </h2>
       <p>
        With the EX-10, it is so easy to create a timelapse video even if you are trying it for the first time. Conventionally, you need to trial and error on the settings to finally know what setting is best for what scenarios for timelapse videos. But with preset modes like clouds, sunset, nightscape and many more, a novice will be able to get it right at the first try.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>
       <center>
        <iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="388" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/CuTA0cWh6Us" width="690">
        </iframe>
       </center>
       <h2>
       </h2>
       <h2>
        Makeup Mode
       </h2>
       <p>
        Doesnt matter if you are a male or female, all genders will enjoy the makeup mode especially during days when you look tired and exhausted. This mode simply makes your skin glow and look radiant right after you shot. No need to waste time on post processing via your computer or smartphone and look your best instantaneously.
       </p>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_77719" style="width: 700px">
        <img alt="Casio EX-10 Makeup Mode" class="size-full wp-image-77719" height="518" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/CIMG3232.jpg" width="690"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         Casio EX-10 Makeup Mode  now every have flawless &amp; radiant skin effortlessly
        </p>
       </div>
       <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_70200" style="width: 700px">
        <img alt="From left to right: No Makeup Mode, Makeup Mode strength 6(half) and Makeup Mode strength 12(max)" class="size-full wp-image-70200" height="432" src="http://www.superadrianme.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/100CASIO.jpg" width="690"/>
        <p class="wp-caption-text">
         From left to right: No Makeup Mode, Makeup Mode strength 6(half) and Makeup Mode strength 12(max)
        </p>
       </div>
       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        WiFi Connectivity
       </h2>
       <p>
        Like most cameras these days, Casio EXILIM EX-10 comes with WiFi connectivity for you to either share your photos with your mobile devices or even lets you control the camera remotely. But unlike most cameras, EX-10 lets you share the video you have captured to your mobile device too. So you can instantly upload those cool slow motion video or time-lapse video on Instagram effortlessly.
       </p>
       <p>
       </p>

       <h2>
        The Verdict
       </h2>
       <p>
        There is no other camera that comes packed with so many features and you can be rest assured that all these features will make your daily photo or video log more creative and memorable. Although it is pricey, retailing at
        <strong>
         S$1,099,
        </strong>
        it is a worthy investment for an All-In-One Multi-purpose digital compact camera in one small package.
       </p>
