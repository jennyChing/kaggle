           <span class="xc_pin" onclick="pin_this(event, 'http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http://loveandmarriagecoach.com/how-to-deal-with-your-mother-in-law&amp;media=http://loveandmarriagecoach.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/dreamstime_xs_34027890.jpg&amp;description=How to Deal With Your Mother-in-Law')">
           </span>
          </span>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         It is well known that most often it is men who make mother-in-law jokes, but usually it is the women who are on the receiving end of a mother-in-laws interference or attempts at control. For simplicitys sake I am writing this article using this generality.
        </p>
        <p>
         Most mother-in-laws care about their daughter-in-laws, and want to see them happy. It is important to understand that most mother-in-laws give advice from a place of love. They do want their children to be happy and they think their advice is the gate to happiness.
        </p>
        <p>
         Regardless of a mother-in-laws motivations; criticisms and interference with no boundaries will ultimately be destructive to a marriage.
        </p>
        <p>
         It is crucial that when a wife has a problem with her mother-in-law, she needs to speak to her husband, and they must come to an understanding of how they will handle the problem together. It is very important to present a united front, and it is best for your husband to be the one to speak to his mother.
        </p>
        <p>
         Often times a mother-in-law will criticize her daughter-in-law to her son. It is very important for a son to tell his mother that he will not tolerate the criticism, and he must also defend his wife and speak well of her. When Mom realizes that each time she has something negative to say about her daughter-in-law, her son says something kind about her and defends her, she will stop criticizing. It may take a few times for Mom to realize that her criticism is not welcome, but eventually she will stop when she hears her son speaking up for his wife.
        </p>
        <p>
         When there is friction with a mother-in-law, the best way to handle visits is for the couple to visit the mother-in-law on her turf. This may sound counter-intuitive. Why go to your parents when they are the problem? SImple. When you visit your in-laws on their turf if there is a problem you can always leave. If you have your mother-in-law come to your house to visit and there is a problem it will be harder and more problematic to ask her to leave. If your husband wants to spend more time with his parents and you dont, you can opt out. There is no rule that you always have to visit your in-laws with your spouse. Take a day and spend it with your friends, or go to a movie or shopping.
        </p>
        <p>
         If your mother-in-law tends to come over too often, gives unsolicited advice, tells you how to raise your kids, etc., it is important to stop this interference from day one. If you dont set a precedent at the start, you are allowing a bad pattern to take hold. You should both take her gently aside. Be honest and explain what your boundaries are and stick to them. Always be respectful and show her that you appreciate her. If your husband prefers not to get involved, just take charge yourself.
        </p>
        <p>
         If you are a mother-in-law reading this and your daughter-in-law has requested that you not interfere, honor that. A mature mother understands that her son is an adult and even if she doesnt like his choice of spouse, she will honor it. It is better to have the attitude that if your daughter-in-law makes your son happy then you are happy. You can be a great mother-in-law. Let your children know that if they ever need you that you will be there for them.
        </p>
        <p>
         Questions? Comments?
        </p>
