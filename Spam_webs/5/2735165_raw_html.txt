          <h2>
           About Investment Banking and Capital Markets
          </h2>
          Global Banking and Markets represents the global corporate and investment banking, and capital markets businesses of Scotiabank , providing full service coverage in Canada and servicing selected niche markets globally.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Global Banking and Markets professionals provide a broad range of financial products, including debt and equity sales, trading, research, derivatives, structured finance and securitization, foreign exchange, money market securities and precious metals, as well as products that are relevant to the financing and strategic development needs of clients including: Corporate Banking, Equity Capital Markets, Debt and Equity, Mergers &amp; Acquisitions, Infrastructure Finance, Global Loan Syndications.
          <br/>
          <br/>
          Some Investment Banking / Capital Markets job opportunities include: Global Capital Markets Sales &amp; Trading Associate-in-Training; Investment Banking or Mergers and Acquisitions Analyst; Trade Floor Technology Consultant; Equity Administrator; Investment Banking Associate; M&amp;A Specialist; Sales and Trading, Foreign Exchange, Commodity Specialist; Futures / Options Trader; Derivatives Specialist and Equity Research Associates.
