             Eyjafjallajokull Eruption
            </h1>
            <p>
             by Orvaratli
            </p>
           </div>
           <div id="es_win_caption">
            Eyjafjallajokull, Iceland
I could not wait going out again photographing the volcanic Eruption in Eyjafjallajokull glacier. The weather has been really bad here in Iceland for the last few days. On this day it finally was good with clear skies so off I went with a few friends and family.
