     <p>
      <strong>
       Jim Rohn
      </strong>
      greatly influenced many of todays top personal development speakers,people such as
      <em>
       Tony Robbins, Darren Hardy, Les Brown, Brian Tracy
      </em>
      and manymore. Jim was an Idaho farm boy who became a legend in network marketing,and went on to become a multi millionaire and a much sought after seminarspeaker and successful author. Jims quotes are enjoyed and shared by millionsof people all over the world today because of his straight forward, easy tounderstand style.
     </p>
     <p>
      Jim based many of his teachings around the
      <strong>
       fundamentals for success
      </strong>
      or as healso called them The basics. You will find thatmany of Jims basics for success arecontained in these awesome picture quotes that I have compiled for you to enjoy.Jims easy to understand words of wisdom inspired me to start the
      <a href="https://www.facebook.com/jimrohndailyquote" target="_blank">
       Jim Rohndaily Quote
      </a>
      Facebook page, which in turn developed into Your Peak State.So pull up a seat and enjoy the timeless words of wisdom by the legend that is Mr
      <strong>
       Jim Rohn
      </strong>
      .
     </p>
     <p>
     </p>
     <blockquote>
      <p>
       If you don't design your own life plan, chances are you'll fall into someone else's plan. And guess what they have planned for you? Not much.
       <strong>
        - Jim Rohn
       </strong>
      </p>
     </blockquote>
     <p class="center">
      And Remember, all of our quotes are free to share
