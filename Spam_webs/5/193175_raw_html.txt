        <h1 style="text-align: left;">
         Winnipeg
         <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Traffic_collision" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Traffic collision">
          MOTOR VEHICLE COLLISION
         </a>
         INVOLVING A TRAIN  ARREST
        </h1>
        <figure class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_34692" style="width: 210px;">
         <a href="http://i0.wp.com/newscanada-plus.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Canada1333.jpg">
          <img alt="winnipeg news  -  Winnipeg  MOTOR VEHICLE COLLISION INVOLVING A TRAIN  ARREST " class="size-full wp-image-34692" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/newscanada-plus.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Canada1333.jpg?resize=210%2C212"/>
         </a>
         <figcaption class="wp-caption-text">
          Winnipeg MOTOR VEHICLE COLLISION INVOLVING A TRAIN  ARREST
         </figcaption>
        </figure>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         (
         <span style="font-family: 'arial black', 'avant garde';">
          <strong>
           <em>
            NEWSCANADA
           </em>
          </strong>
         </span>
         )
         <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
          PLUS WINNIPEG NEWS
         </span>
           On October 12, 2013 at approximately 9:50 p.m., members of the
         <a class="zem_slink" href="http://www.winnipeg.ca/police/" rel="homepage" target="_blank" title="Winnipeg Police Service">
          Winnipeg Police Service
         </a>
         and other emergency personnel responded to a collision involving a train in the area of Archibald Street and Fermor Avenue.
        </p>
        <p style="text-align: left;">
         It has been determined that the driver of a
         <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mazda3" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Mazda3">
          Mazda 3
         </a>
         had been travelling westbound on Fermor Avenue between
         <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_State_Route_19" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="California State Route 19">
          Lakewood Boulevard
         </a>
         and Archibald Street when he encountered signal lights and warning bells activated at a railway crossing.
        </p>
        <p>
         For unknown reasons, the driver ignored the signals and proceeded in attempt to beat the train. As a result, the Mazda 3 struck the second engine of the train causing significant damage. Despite the potential of this collision, no one was injured.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Adam NORTON, 29 years-old
         </strong>
         has been charged with the following offences due to his alleged involvement:
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          Dangerous Operation of a Motor Vehicle
         </em>
         <br/>
         <em>
          Fail to Comply with Conditions of an Undertaking
         </em>
         <br/>
         <em>
          (
          <a class="zem_slink" href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ms536471(VS.85).aspx" rel="homepage" target="_blank" title="HTML Application">
           HTA
          </a>
          ) Drive while Disqualified
         </em>
         <br/>
         <em>
          (HTA) Fail to Stop at a Railway Crossing at which a Traffic Control Device is Signalling the Proximity or Passing of a Train
         </em>
        </p>
        <p>
         He was detained.
        </p>

            </em>
           </span>
           )
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            PLUS
            <a class="zem_slink" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=49.8994444444,-97.1391666667&amp;spn=0.1,0.1&amp;q=49.8994444444,-97.1391666667 (Winnipeg)&amp;t=h" rel="geolocation" target="_blank" title="Winnipeg">
             WINNIPEG, MB
            </a>
            NEWS
           </span>
           (Marketwired  October 09, 2013) Sunwing Airlines will increase its flying schedule from Winnipeg by adding direct flights to
           <a href="http://ctt.marketwire.com/?release=11G008949-001&amp;id=3529087&amp;type=0&amp;url=http%3a%2f%2fwww.sunwing.ca%2fBahamas-Travel%2fgrand-bahama-vacation-packages.asp" rel="nofollow">
            Grand Bahama
           </a>
           this winter and further expanding its operation from the Winnipeg Richardson International Airport by opening a seasonal base. The opening of this base will result in recruitment of over 60 crew members from within the local community. The move clearly illustrates Sunwings continuous growth and success in the Winnipeg market.
          </p>
          <p>
           We are very committed to the ongoing development of our Winnipeg program, said Mark Williams, President of Sunwing Airlines. Due to the overwhelming success Sunwing has experienced in the Winnipeg market, we have decided to further expand our Winnipeg operations and are very excited about the upcoming season.
          </p>
          <p>
           Sunwing has been operating from Winnipeg since 2006 when it introduced direct flights to
           <a class="zem_slink" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=21.1605555556,-86.8475&amp;spn=0.1,0.1&amp;q=21.1605555556,-86.8475 (Canc%C3%BAn)&amp;t=h" rel="geolocation" target="_blank" title="Cancn">
            Cancun, Mexico
           </a>
           . Today, the airline takes Winnipeg travellers to several popular vacation hotspots in Cuba, Dominican Republic, Jamaica, Mexico and now Grand Bahama starting this winter.
          </p>
          <p>
           When airlines do well in our market its a win-win situation, said Barry Rempel, President &amp; CEO of Winnipeg Airports Authority. We are pleased to work with quality carriers in enhancing local employment and providing increased non-stop destinations for our region. Sunwings flight to Grand Bahama is a great addition to our line-up of winter getaway destinations.
          </p>
          <p>
           Sunwing Airlines will offer weekly flights to Grand Bahama starting January 18, 2014 until the end of March providing Winnipeg residents with an opportunity to experience Grand Bahamas stunning white sand beaches and turquoise waters. A one-week all inclusive vacation to Grand Bahama starts from just $1,395 + $350 taxes per person to Memories Grand Bahama, departing on February 22 or March 1, 2014.
          </p>
          <p>
           All
           <a href="http://ctt.marketwire.com/?release=11G008949-001&amp;id=3529090&amp;type=0&amp;url=http%3a%2f%2fwww.sunwing.ca%2f" rel="nofollow">
            Sunwing Vacations
           </a>
           packages include Sunwing Airlines Champagne Service, which features hot towel service, a choice of hot meals at lunch and dinner served with a choice of wine (hot snacks with soft drinks for flights to Grand Bahama), and complimentary in-flight entertainment, including first run movies. For just $40 more per flight segment, travellers can upgrade to Sunwings
           <a class="zem_slink" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elite_%28video_game%29" rel="wikipedia" target="_blank" title="Elite (video game)">
            Elite Plus
           </a>
           service, which features advance seat selection, separate check-in at a majority of airports, advance boarding, 30 kg baggage allowance, extra legroom seats, and priority baggage handling.
          </p>
          <p>
           For more information or to book your vacation, visit
           <a href="http://ctt.marketwire.com/?release=11G008949-001&amp;id=3529093&amp;type=0&amp;url=http%3a%2f%2fwww.sunwing.ca" rel="nofollow">
            www.sunwing.ca
           </a>
           or contact your local travel agent.
