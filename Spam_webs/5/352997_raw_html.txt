     <h2>
      How does this flu forecast map work?
     </h2>
     <p>
      By entering your zip code, you can find out what flu conditions may be like in your county in the weeks ahead.
     </p>
     <p>
      Our methodology takes into account current and historical CDC data, rising and falling interest in flu on social media and in online searches, and local and regional weather information. The flu map predicts flu severity county by county across the United States so you can plan ahead and take precautions to avoid the flu  both at home and in places where you plan to travel.
     </p>
