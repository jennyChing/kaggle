    <div class="g_txt_5">
     United Kingdom, December 05,2014/Free-Press-Release.com/ -- Whenever you look in your wardrobe you will see some clothes and realise that you may never wear them, or there are clothes lying there that have been forgotten. Studies show that on average 92% of people leave clothes forgotten in their wardrobe or drawers. Everyone likes to keep in with the current style which means that regularly lots of clothes will be thrown away with the rubbish or maybe donated to charity. This is the same with footwear as well as accessories.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     This is backed up by the fact around 30kg of clothes are thrown away by each person every year, it may not seem like much but given that the majority of clothes do not weigh much this is actually a large amount. The most popular method of getting rid of your unwanted clothes is by donating them to charity by putting them in charity bins or by leaving them outside a charity shop. However there is another option where you can exchange your clothes for cash.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Genie Recycling offer this service to everyone looking to throw away their clothes. They give everyone the opportunity to earn money for their old items while at the same time feeling good about giving to the people who need them and they will not just be throwing them away and adding to the waste that is being thrown away on a daily basis.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Director Richard Wood explains: With over 80 years of experience our family run company is best placed to help you in this industry. We allow people to earn money for their clothes which otherwise would be thrown away while at the same time be in the knowledge they will be put to good use and reducing their carbon footprint. We send you a 30kg sack and will pay you an industry leading 60p per kilo, so the more you donate the more you will earn.
     <br/>
     <br/>
     Unlike many of their competitors, Genie Recycling arranges a date and time to suit you to pick up your clothes. Within 24 hours of your clothes being received and evaluated you will be paid by a method which suits you. The added bonus of knowing that you are reducing the environmental impact by not throwing your unwanted clothes into a landfill site encourages people to use their services by adding to the fact they are helping to make the world a better place in which to live.
