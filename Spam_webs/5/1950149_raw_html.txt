     <h1 class="detail_title">
      Handmade Handcrafted Jewelry- How to Make Shell Earrings
     </h1>
     <p class="detail_subtitle">
      Summary: This handmade handcrafted jewelry aims to teach you a quick-to-learn way on to how to make shell earrings.
     </p>
     <div id="articleProductList">
     </div>
     <div class="art_detail_text">
      <div class="art_detail_content">
       <p>
        This simple handmade handcrafted jewelry was designed all by myself. I just chose some seldom-used items from my supplies that where at hand and racked my brain to invent a fancy idea for making DIY button earrings. Fortunately, my efforts were not in vain, and now Id like to share how to make shell earrings with you!
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/LearningCenterImg/Content/Handmade-handcrafted-jewelry--how-to-make-shell-earrings1.jpg" style="width:800px"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Materials and tools:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.pandahall.com/beads/wholesale-Tibetan-Style-Links/0-499-1.html">
         Tibetan Style Connectors
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Iron jewelry finding beads
       </p>
       <p>
        Flowery
        <a href="http://www.pandahall.com/beads/wholesale-Shell-Beads/0-234-1.html">
         shell beads
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Flowery acrylic beads blue
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.pandahall.com/beads/wholesale-Earring-Hooks/0-334-1.html">
         Ear hooks
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Round nose pliers
       </p>
       <p>
        Flat nose pliers
       </p>
       <p>
        Wire cutter
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/LearningCenterImg/Content/Handmade-handcrafted-jewelry--how-to-make-shell-earrings2.jpg" style="width:800px"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Instructions:
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Step 1: make shell dangles
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        1st, thread a head pin through a blue acrylic bead and a white shell bead;
       </p>
       <p>
        2nd, continue threading it through the center of a connector;
       </p>
       <p>
        3rd, pass the pin through the top hole of the connector with pliers and make a wire wrapped loop;
       </p>
       <p>
        4th, cut off the extra length.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/LearningCenterImg/Content/Handmade-handcrafted-jewelry--how-to-make-shell-earrings3.jpg" style="width:800px"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         Step 2: add iron bead and ear hook
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        1st, make a simple loop above iron bead;
       </p>
       <p>
        2nd, attach it to the shell dangle.
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/LearningCenterImg/Content/Handmade-handcrafted-jewelry--how-to-make-shell-earrings4.jpg" style="width:800px"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        The final product is as the picture shows:
       </p>
       <p>
        <img alt="" src="http://lc.pandahall.com/LearningCenterImg/Content/Handmade-handcrafted-jewelry--how-to-make-shell-earrings5.jpg" style="width:800px"/>
       </p>
       <p>
        Tada, the tutorial of how to make shell earrings is completed! Do you like this sort of handmade handcrafted jewelry? Always keeping mind open while searching and making jewelry, who know what you will be able to create!
       </p>
