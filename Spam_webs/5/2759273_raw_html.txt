             <section class="article-content">
              <p>
               <img alt="Mashable 10 Logo" class="alignleft size-full wp-image-442035" data-width="105" src="http://rack.2.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEyLzAyLzA3LzA4XzUwXzMwXzc3Nl9maWxlCnAJdGh1bWIJMTIwMHg5NjAwPg/87f20785" title="Mashable 10 Logo"/>
               <i>
                This post is part of the
                <a href="http://mashable.com/tag/mashable-10">
                 Mashable 10
                </a>
                , Mashable's gift guide of the 10 hottest gadgets that that our editors think should be on everyone's wish lists this holiday season. Read about all 10 gadgets
                <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2010/11/27/mashable-10-holiday-gift-guide/">
                 here
                </a>
                .
               </i>
              </p>
              <p>
               <img alt="gifts image" class="alignright size-full wp-image-143936" src="http://rack.3.mshcdn.com/media/ZgkyMDEyLzAyLzEyLzE1XzI0XzU0XzUwOV9maWxlCnAJdGh1bWIJMTIwMHg5NjAwPg/efc4ab31"/>
               After telling you what we think are the hottest tech items on the market this year in the
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/mashable-10/">
                Mashable 10
               </a>
               gift guide,
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2010/11/18/holiday-wishlist-open-thread/">
                we asked you
               </a>
               to tell us what's on
               <em>
                your
               </em>
               holiday gadget wish list.
              </p>
              <p>
               Some items were in line with our recommendations; others were close to making our list and a few we didn't know much about. Check out the 10 best answers below. You may find some more items for your own wish list or gift ideas for your loved ones.
              </p>
              <p>
               If you didn't get a chance to share your wish list items in the initial post, feel free to tell us about them in the comments here. Happy holidays!
              </p>
              <h3>
               1.
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2010/11/15/mashable-10-ipad/">
                iPad
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
               Some tablets in the market are just too small and less functional. Besides, the iPad will be a good addition to my iPhone and Macbook Pro. :D
               <strong>
                - Autumn Rain
               </strong>
              </p>
              <h3>
               2.
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2010/11/26/xbox-360-kinect-mashable-10/">
                Kinect
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
               I want to tear open the wrapping paper on a Kinect for Xbox. I tried out Kinect Sports: boxing at PAX and love the idea that I can KO people twice my size!
               <strong>
                - Jessica Otte
               </strong>
              </p>
              <h3>
               3.
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2010/09/02/samsung-galaxy-tab-2/">
                Samsung Galaxy Tablet
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
               I currently have an iPhone for developing purposes, so I am wanting something Android so I can start developing for both!
               <strong>
                - Eric Calisto
               </strong>
              </p>
              <h3>
               4. Canon PowerShot SD3500IS
              </h3>
              <p>
               I would absolutely love a Blackberry Playbook, but they aren't due to come out until early part of next year, so instead I say a new Canon PowerShot SD3500IS so I can give the boyfriend my old Canon Exilim.
               <strong>
                - MJ Schrader
               </strong>
              </p>
              <h3>
               5.
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2010/03/03/tivo-apps-premiere-dvrs/">
                TiVo Slide
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
               With all the developments in Internet TV technology over the past few months, why are we using the same standard, medieval remotes our parents did?  Remotes have been around since 1950 its time for a serious change. The TiVo Slide remote has a full QWERTY keyboard and cuts search time significantly. I hope Santa brings me one.
               <strong>
                - Lacey Haines
               </strong>
              </p>
              <h3>
               6. Eagle Eye Converter
              </h3>
              <p>
               It lets you play your favorite PS3 games using a keyboard and mouse!
               <strong>
                -James Little
               </strong>
              </p>
              <h3>
               7.
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2010/09/01/new-apple-tv/">
                Apple TV
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
               I would say iPad, but I'm going to wait for the next one with a front-facing camera, so I can have Facetime. So I'll get the new Apple TV. OnLives recently just announced Micro Console and an HDMI hub so I can use all those set-top boxes on my one TV.
               <strong>
                - Carson Gallo
               </strong>
              </p>
              <h3>
               8.
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2010/06/03/dell-streak-priced/">
                Dell Streak
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
               The thing is, I want a tablet and a good phone. I have a Jabra bT3030 Bluetooth headset, which [I] use as my headphones and I talk from it too, so I don't have to care what people think of me having [a] big phone. Plus I like to take pics and 5MP is good enough for me. Top that with Dell pushing Android 2.2 OTA this month.
               <strong>
                -Deep Soni
               </strong>
              </p>
              <h3>
               9.
               <a href="http://mashable.com/category/pc/">
                PC
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
               My main wish list item is an assembled PC because I haven't had a chance to buy any computer for four years. My decided specs: Intel Core i7 920 (Processor) and XFX Radeon 5850 (Graphics Card).
               <strong>
                - Jayant Raj
               </strong>
              </p>
              <h3>
               10.
               <a data-crackerjax="#post-slider" href="http://mashable.com/2010/11/22/digifit/">
                Digifit Connect &amp; App
               </a>
              </h3>
              <p>
               Since I've already got the Apple platforms I need, I'm looking to accessorize this holiday season. On the top of my list: Digifit Connect &amp; App- monitors my workouts and health to get me back in shape after the holiday meals.
               <strong>
                - Ben Wyche
               </strong>
              </p>
