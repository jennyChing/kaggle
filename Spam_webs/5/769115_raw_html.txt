        A
        <b>
         Google Bomb
        </b>
        or
        <b>
         Google washer
        </b>
        is a certain attempt to influence the ranking of a given page in results returned by the
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google" title="Google">
         Google
        </a>
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Search_engine" title="Search engine">
         search engine
        </a>
        , often with humorous or political intentions. Due to the way that Google's
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PageRank" title="PageRank">
         PageRank
        </a>
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Algorithm" title="Algorithm">
         algorithm
        </a>
        works, a page will be ranked higher if the sites that link to that page all use consistent
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anchor_text" title="Anchor text">
         anchor text
        </a>
        . A Google bomb is created if a large number of sites link to the page in this manner.
        <i>
         Google bomb
        </i>
        is used both as a
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Verb" title="Verb">
         verb
        </a>
        and a
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Noun" title="Noun">
         noun
        </a>
        .
