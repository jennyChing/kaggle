     <div class="justify summaryCopy bodyCopy" id="summary-text1">
      Just like the earthquake that devastated much of Haiti, the call came without warning. But within hours, a small AirForce team from the 23rd Special Tactics Squadron was on its way to Port-au-Prince where they discovered the air traffic control tower was out of commission.
     </div>
