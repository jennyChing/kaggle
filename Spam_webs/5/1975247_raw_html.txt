           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Last week, I told you about a top-secret, underground language of cat slang that our kitties use when we humans arent around. Our cats, in a further effort of claiming complete world domination, have taken to creating a language spoken only feline-to-feline. And Im not just talking about local felines  Fanglish has become a worldwide feline-code.
           </span>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Heres the thing: If they ever knew we were hip to their covert vocabulary, theyd definitely band together and then wed be under feline reign faster that you can say "clowder. Thankfully, my trusted source has enlightened me with a few more Fanglish cat-slang terms. I share these with you with extreme trepidation, but full confidence youll keep them far away from your cats ever-watchful eyes.
           </span>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            With that in mind, please give your cats some catnip and sneak behind closed doors before you reader any further. Here are 10 new Fanglish terms:
           </span>
           <br/>
           <br/>
           <strong style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Neverending journey
           </strong>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            A car ride while secured in a cat carrier.
           </span>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <em style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Tabby saw the carrier and hid behind the sofa, hoping to avoid a neverending journey.
           </em>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <strong style="font-size: 12pt;">
            <br/>
            Pariah Carry
           </strong>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            A cat, who returning from a vet visit, is smelled and shunned by the other cats after she exits the carrier.
           </span>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <em style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Leonard the kitten made his debut as Pariah Carry when he returned from his first vet visit.
           </em>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <strong style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Sass-quatch
           </strong>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            A sassy cat racing away with a stolen item, seen only as a blur by humans.
           </span>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <em style="font-size: 12pt;">
            <br/>
            The Lady had an unexpected sass-quatch sighting when Snowball moved through the living room with a piece of turkey in her mouth.
            <br/>
           </em>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <strong style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Company acquisition
           </strong>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Converting a human visitor into a cat-lover.
           </span>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <em style="font-size: 12pt;">
            The Ladys date said he didnt like cats, but Oreo made a company acquisition by the end of the night.
            <br/>
           </em>
           <strong style="font-size: 12pt;">
            <br/>
            Freeze warning
           </strong>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Body language from a cat wearing clothing, whos stiffened, fallen to the ground and looking annoyed.
           </span>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <em style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Mitzis freeze warning told The Man he should never again make her wear the hot-dog costume.
           </em>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <strong style="font-size: 12pt;">
            <br/>
            Strong under-toe
           </strong>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Fast-moving feet underneath a blanket.
           </span>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <em style="font-size: 12pt;">
            <br/>
            Fluffy started swatting when The Lady created a strong under-toe at the end of the bed.
           </em>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <strong style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Business plan
           </strong>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            A cats intention to use the litter box.
           </span>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <em style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Mitzi didnt care that The Lady was pouring new litter into the box, she continued on with her business plan.
            <br/>
           </em>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <strong style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Pillie Nelson
           </strong>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            A cat whos "on the road again when she knows its time to take her pill.
           </span>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <em style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Patches suddenly became Pillie Nelson and raced down the stairs when she saw The Lady grab the bottle of antibiotic pills.
            <br/>
           </em>
           <strong style="font-size: 12pt;">
            <br/>
            Basket weaving
           </strong>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            Weaving in and out around a humans legs while theyre carrying a full laundry basket.
           </span>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <em style="font-size: 12pt;">
            When she heard The Lady walk up the stairs from the laundry room, Butterscotch immediately took up basket-weaving.
           </em>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <br style="font-size: 12pt;"/>
           <span style="font-size: 12pt;">
            I hope youve enjoyed this weeks selections, and mentally filed them in a safe corner of your brain. If youre wise, youll quickly change your computers password and avoid suspicious activity that may alert the felines were onto their classified jargon. They may look like theyre napping, but they always have an ear cocked in our direction.
           </span>
