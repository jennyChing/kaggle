     <h2>
      Amanpours ridiculous racism
     </h2>
     <div class="entry">
      <p>
       I first heard about this while listening to
       <a href="http://medializzy.wordpress.com/" target="_blank">
        Lizzy Blakney
       </a>
       s
       <a href="http://www.blogtalkradio.com/MediaLizzy/va/2008/07/25/free-for-all-friday-culture-and-the-sexy-factor-in-2008" target="_blank">
        podcast
       </a>
       :
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/americas/7524660.stm" target="_blank">
        <img align="center" src="http://newsimg.bbc.co.uk/media/images/44864000/jpg/_44864314_waving_ap226b.jpg"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       During todays press conference in Paris, Christiane Amanpour asked Sarkozy if he feelt awkward praising black Obama when, only a few years ago, he was calling black French rioters scum.
      </p>
      <p>
       I kid you not.
      </p>
      <p>
       Watch:
       <br/>
       <object height="344" width="425">
        <param name="movie" value="http://www.youtube.com/v/IWwHc-dzU4I&amp;hl=en&amp;fs=1">
        </param>
        <param name="wmode" value="transparent">
        </param>
        <param name="allowFullScreen" value="true">
        </param>
        <embed allowfullscreen="true" height="344" src="http://www.youtube.com/v/IWwHc-dzU4I&amp;hl=en&amp;fs=1" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="425" wmode="transparent">
        </embed>
       </object>
      </p>
      <p>
       This woman equates French rioting delinquents with an American presidential candidate because of race.  And were supposed to respect her as a journalist?
      </p>
      <p>
       Sarkozy seized the opportunity to pour scorn and sarcasm, praising her exceptional knowledge of French political life.   And then he let it rip.
      </p>
      <p>
       Good for him.
      </p>
