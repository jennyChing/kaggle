          <h1>
           DIY INSTAGRAM BIRTHDAY COLLAGE
          </h1>
          <div class="metadata">
           <div class="published">
            Apr 10 2014 8:08 PM
           </div>
          </div>
         </header>
         <div class="sharebar-wrapper">
          <div class="sharebar">
           <!-- AddThis Button BEGIN -->
           <div addthis:title="DIY INSTAGRAM BIRTHDAY COLLAGE" addthis:url="http://www.blacks.ca/inspiration/DIY-Instagram-Birthday-Collage" class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style">
            <a class="addthis_button_facebook_like" fb:like:layout="button_count">
            </a>
            <a class="addthis_button_tweet">
            </a>
            <a class="addthis_counter addthis_pill_style">

           <!-- AddThis Button END -->
          </div>
         </div>
         <p class="taxonomy-field">
          <span class="name">
           Pick  Section:
          </span>
          <a href="/inspiration/section/everyday-moments">
           Everyday Moments
          </a>
          ,
          <a href="/inspiration/section/seasonal">
           Seasonal
          </a>
         </p>
         <div style="width: 100%; position: relative; float: left; margin: 0 0 1em;">
          <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/inspiration/hoopla">
           <img height="44" src="/inspiration/Media/Default/SmallBloggerImage/hoopla.jpg" style="float: left;" width="44"/>
          </a>
          <div style="float: left; padding-left: 0.8em; font-size: 1.077em;">
           <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/inspiration/hoopla">
            <span style="color: #333;">
             <span style="font-weight: bold;">
              Robin and Shannon,
             </span>
             Toronto, ON
            </span>
           </a>
           <br/>
           <span style="color: #a1a1a1;">
            <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/inspiration/hoopla">
             Blogger Since: March, 2014
            </a>
            |
            <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/inspiration/hoopla">
             View Robin and Shannon's Bio
            </a>
           </span>
          </div>
         </div>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          As event designers (or merry makers, as we like to call ourselves), we are always trying to use cheerful ways to decorate a party and make me people happy.
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span lang="EN-US">
           For our first BLACKS project, we thought we would utilize their
           <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/products/made_for_instagram">
            cool Instagram services
           </a>
           for a fun birthday DIY . Weve ordered prints made from our Instagram photos before, but it was never THIS easy. We wanted to try out BLACKS iPhone app, so we downloaded it, chose our 30 photos from our Instagram feed, sent them to our closest BLACKS location and they were DONE. Like, printed done. Within 15 minutes, we had received an email notification saying they were printed and ready to be picked up. It doesnt get much easier than that!
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span lang="EN-US">
           All of the
           <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/find_a_store">
            BLACKS locations
           </a>
           also come equipped with computers so if you happen to be close to a store and have your phone, you can print your Instagram photos right then and there. You just plug in your phone to the comptuer, choose your photos and send them to the printer. In an hour or less, BAM, the photos are done. Dont have your phone? Just log in to your Instagram account on the computer. Easy peasy.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span lang="EN-US">
           Now what did we do with our newly printed
           <a href="http://beta.blacks.ca/products/made_for_instagram">
            Instagram photos
           </a>
           ? Well, just wait and see
          </span>
          <span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Wingdings; mso-ascii-font-family: Cambria; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Cambria; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-char-type: symbol; mso-symbol-font-family: Wingdings;">
           J
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span lang="EN-US">
           Keaton, Shannons son, had a birthday coming up, so we thought it would be an excellent time to try out a fun birthday dcor idea for his party.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span lang="EN-US">
           We printed out all the photos of just him on our Instagram feed. We chose 5x5 prints, but you could also choose 4x4, 6x6 and 8x8 sizes as well.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span lang="EN-US">
           Now Keaton was turning five, but weve only had our Instagram account for a year and a half. We couldnt do a true from birth to now visual, but you could still see his growth. It would be especially awesome if you were creating one for a first birthday party and had all the photos from when your baby was born to twelve months. We will have to think of that for future babies
          </span>
          <span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: Wingdings; mso-ascii-font-family: Cambria; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Cambria; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-char-type: symbol; mso-symbol-font-family: Wingdings;">
           J
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span lang="EN-US">
           We then placed the photos in the shape of a five on the wall.
           <o:p>
           </o:p>
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span lang="EN-US">
           <img height="2816" src="/inspiration/Media/Default/imgresources/Keaton-five.jpg" width="1968"/>
          </span>
         </p>
         <!--more-->
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <img height="3888" src="/inspiration/Media/Default/imgresources/close-up-keaton-2.jpg" width="2592"/>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <img height="2592" src="/inspiration/Media/Default/imgresources/keaton-close-up-3.jpg" width="3888"/>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <img height="2592" src="/inspiration/Media/Default/imgresources/Close-Up-Keaton-.jpg" width="3888"/>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          And there you have it! A super simple birthday dcor project all thanks to BLACKS. Oh, and did we mention how inexpensive this idea is. Each 5x5 print was $0.35 and we had 30 prints, so it cost a total of $10.50. Not bad eh?
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span lang="EN-US">
           You could also customize this DIY, depending on the amount of Instagram photos you had. You could write out the childs name, a happy birthday message or just a monogram letter.
          </span>
         </p>
         <p class="MsoNormal">
          <span lang="EN-US">
           Happy 5
           <sup>
            th
           </sup>
           Birthday Keaton!
          </span>
         </p>
