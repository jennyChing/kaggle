        <p>
         If you are facing short of cash for starting your restaurant business, SBA loans can be of great help for you. You can avail various types of SBA (Small Business Administration) loans depending on the requirement of your restaurant business. One of the best funding options from SBA is the SBA 504 loan.
        </p>
        <p>
         Since, the SBA-504 loan is government-guaranteed, you need to submit collateral on the the property that you have purchased. You do not have to submit any additional collateral. However, closing costs that include environmental fees, appraisal fees and contingency fees can be included in the financing. This loan is particularly advantageous for growing restaurants.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          How to Qualify for SBA 504 Loans for Restaurants?
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         While securing a SBA 504 business loan, you should follow these basic steps.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Get All Your Financial Documents Ready:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         While planning to secure a SBA 504 restaurant loan, you should have all the relevant financial documents beforehand.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Ensure that You Have Relevant Business Experience:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         As per the eligibility criteria of
         <a href="http://www.headwayfinance.com/services/sba-restaurant-loans" target="_blank" title="SBA restaurant loan program">
          SBA restaurant loan program
         </a>
         , you need to have some relevant experience in the field of food industry. If you do not have direct relevant business experience, then you should gain some industry experience prior to starting your own food business.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Examine Your Credit History:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Generally speaking, you should have a credit score above 650. In some cases, your spouses credit history may also be taken into consideration. To avoid any hassle, check your credit score before you approach any lender for funding assistance.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Prepare a Comprehensive Business Plan
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Never forget that your business plan is the most important element while securing a SBA 504 loan for your restaurant business. Try to include all your business strategies and repayment procedure in your business plan.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Advantages of an SBA-504 Loan
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Lower Down Payment:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         The prime advantage of this loan is that you do not have to pay a high down payment amount. Since, these loans allow higher loan-to-value ratios, it is easy to qualify for these loans. Restaurateurs have the option to borrow up to 90 percent of their funding requirements and preserve their cash for other business purposes.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Longer Repayment Terms:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Another advantage is that you get a longer repayment term with this loan. SBA repayment periods are longer as compared to traditional bank loans. Hence, the monthly loan payment becomes more affordable for a restaurant-owner as well as the cash flow also improves.
        </p>
        <p>
         <strong>
          Projected Income Consideration:
         </strong>
        </p>
        <p>
         Unlike many lenders, most SBA lenders consider the projected income of a business. They do not take your cash flow history into account. This is especially advantageous if your restaurant business is in a growing phase.
        </p>
        <p>
         The SBA 504 loans are a smart and hassle free loan option for business owners to acquire commercial property without having any unpleasant experience. SBA 504 loans help restaurateurs in starting and expanding their food businesses. You can also use this
         <a href="http://www.headwayfinance.com/services/restaurant-equipment-loans" target="_blank" title="loan for buying or leasing restaurant equipment">
          loan for buying or leasing restaurant equipment
         </a>
         .
        </p>
