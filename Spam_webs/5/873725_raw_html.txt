Thus, heart disease is a fatal one and needs utmost care. With the advancement of modern science, heart diseases are easy to detect and there are ample treatments to recover from this. A periodic health check-up is very much required in such cases. People of any age can be a victim of heart attack. So. it is always better to remember the old proverb of Prevention is always better than cure.
