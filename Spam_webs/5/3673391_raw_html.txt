          <div class="post-title">
           <h5>
            The Master Console Race... Hint: Its not Xbox
           </h5>
          </div>
          <!--/ .post-title-->
          <br/>
          <br/>
          <p>
           Leeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeeets get reaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaady to ruuuuuuuuuuuuuuumble!
          </p>
          <p>
           We are inching closer to the global launch of Xbox One and PS4. The war will be great. Many shall perish. This battle is the gamer version of Bloods vs Crips. And its time to represent yo!
          </p>
          <p>
           There are a lot of articles, movie stars, hipsters hanging outside the local coffee shop pushing their top console pick at us. Theyve gone to
           <a href="http://killstreakmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Xbox-8.png">
            <img alt="Xbox 8" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-9140" height="225" src="http://killstreakmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Xbox-8-300x225.png" width="300"/>
           </a>
           events, researched, did pros and cons yadyaydaydaya thats not what this is about.
          </p>
          <p>
           I know games and YOU know games. Now is the time to show off your level of honor and allegiance to a console. Vote below in the comment section and pick a side my fellow gypsy gamers.
          </p>
          <p>
           Moving on
          </p>
          <p>
           This is about trashing all the info youve heard about these new consoles and following your heart to the motherland. Today we shall use democracy to pick the ultimate winner. We shall rise above race, creed, sex and united stand over the weaker console with the superior console raised high above our heads like Gods.
          </p>
          <p>
           Today, we take back the Game Verse. Are you with me?
          </p>
          <p>
           <em>
            Disclaimer:
           </em>
           <del>
            <em>
             You should know beforehand I am an Xbox guy but I think
            </em>
           </del>
           <em>
            Playstation
           </em>
           <em>
            has the upper hand
           </em>
           <em>
            .
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           Heres why --
           <em>
            note: these judgments are made on past attributes influencing my emotions (side note: Am I using the ellipsis right here? This console war has me all worked up to be sure):
           </em>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <a href="http://killstreakmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Xbox-5.png">
             <img alt="Xbox 5" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-9129" height="186" src="http://killstreakmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Xbox-5-300x186.png" width="300"/>
            </a>
            1. Free Online Multiplayer
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           For those of you out there who have siblings, friends and even the daredevil grandparent multiplayer doesnt have that much weight for you. For that I say, shame on you! There are gamers all over the world who dont have the luxury to play Resistance with someone. Its not right.
          </p>
          <p>
           Playstation made this free, which isnt a surprise if you think about it. America buys a lot of products from China and Japan  where Playstation was birthed. It stands to reason as an act of good faith they would give us a freebie aka global access to other gamers.
          </p>
          <p>
           Microsoft cant do this. But they gave us Bill Gates
           <a href="http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/09/26/bill-gates-control-alt-delete_n_3995179.html">
            Control-Alt-Delete design
           </a>
           . Which for some is more than enough. Just not Gates as he recently admitted. Sigh.
          </p>
          <p>
           Now before we throw stones at Gates be aware Playstation is joining the darkside. The buzz surrounding PS4 is much like Xbox you will need to pay to use online services. It was good while it lasted.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            2. Built in Blu Ray WTF!?
            <a href="http://killstreakmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/xbox-6.png">
             <img alt="xbox 6" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-9130" height="127" src="http://killstreakmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/xbox-6-300x127.png" width="300"/>
            </a>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           No its not Christmas already, Playstation really did feel like what was missing from their console was HD EVERYTHING to the nth degree. Yes, Xbox can play movies. But the picture quality isnt the same.
          </p>
          <p>
           I still remember the first thought I had when I heard this,
           <i>
            Jeez, heres a gaming company that really cares about the little things I need to get through the day.
           </i>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            3. Money, money, money. MOOOOONEY.
            <a href="http://killstreakmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Xbox-7.png">
             <img alt="Xbox 7" class="size-medium wp-image-9131 alignleft" height="300" src="http://killstreakmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Xbox-7-221x300.png" width="221"/>
            </a>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Playstation really low balled their console beating Xboxs price tag by nearly a Benjamin. Kind of cool if youre on a budget and need to get your gaming fix on.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            FINAL THOUGHTS
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           I am an Xbox guy.
           <a href="http://killstreakmedia.com/2013/10/03/how-do-you-get-rid-of-old-games/">
            I have every Xbox game I ever bought I cant get rid of them
           </a>
           . I think the games Xbox has to offer are far more entertaining than Playstation. As I once said,
           <a href="http://www.kirksbedroom.com/home/iloveyouimjustnotinlovewithyou">
            I love you, but I'm not in love with you.
           </a>
           From a gamer standpoint I cant deny the perks Playstation offers. Perks no doubt that will increase in PS4.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://killstreakmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Xbox-2.png">
            <img alt="Xbox 2" class="size-medium wp-image-9127 alignright" height="217" src="http://killstreakmedia.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Xbox-2-300x217.png" width="300"/>
           </a>
           Small side note, these consoles are racing to be released. Theyre forcing each other to meet the due dates.
          </p>
          <p>
           The sad truth, only ONE console will be the MASTER CONSOLE.
          </p>
          <p>
           Who do you think itll be?
          </p>
