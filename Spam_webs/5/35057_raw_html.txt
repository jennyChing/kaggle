       <h1 class="title">
        Iron Man Pizza Hand Pies
       </h1>
       <p class="byline">
        October 3rd, 2011 by justJENN
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/rolling-pin.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10661" height="469" src="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/rolling-pin.jpg" title="rolling pin" width="504"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Tony Stark is the man. No literally.
        <em>
         THE
        </em>
        man, Iron Man  and for some reason I can only envision little Iron Man hand pies filled withpizza. Oh, so delicious.
       </p>
       <p>
        I used the
        <a href="http://www.williams-sonoma.com/products/marvel-hero-cookie-cutter-set/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.williams-sonoma.com/products/marvel-hero-cookie-cutter-set/', 'Marvel cookie cutter set']);">
         Marvel cookie cutter set
        </a>
        from Williams Sonoma. And like my
        <a href="http://justjennrecipes.com/captain-america-apple-hand-pies/2011/08/22/">
         Captain America Apple hand pies
        </a>
        , these couldnt be easier. No need to measure, just fill em up and bake.
       </p>
       <p>
        <strong>
         ingredients:
        </strong>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://justjennrecipes.com/pastry-dough/2010/04/18/">
         pie dough
        </a>
        <br/>
        marinara sauce
        <br/>
        mozzarella cheese
        <br/>
        pepperoni, diced small
       </p>
       <p>
        egg wash (1 egg whisked with 1 Tablespoon water)
       </p>
       <p>
        Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Prep baking sheets with silpats.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/iron-man-faces.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10662" height="345" src="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/iron-man-faces.jpg" title="iron man faces" width="504"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Roll out the dough to about 1/4 inch thick. Make it thin but let it still have enough room to stretch. The Marvel cookie cutters are great because they make perfect impressions and generally dont stick to doughs. If the dough gets too soft, put it in the fridge for a few minutes.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/inggredients.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10664" height="419" src="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/inggredients.jpg" title="inggredients" width="504"/>
        </a>
        <br/>
        <a href="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/prep.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10663" height="309" src="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/prep.jpg" title="prep" width="504"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Half of the cut outs will be the back side. Flip them over and top with a spoonful of marinara, then cheese, then pepperoni. Remember not to overfill or they will seep out of the side, making for a not so attractive Iron Man face.
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/tony-hand-pie-crust2.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10665" height="261" src="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/tony-hand-pie-crust2.jpg" title="tony hand pie crust2" width="504"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        Now take the other Iron Man faces and place them carefully on top of the filling. Push the edges to close, no need for pinching, just press firmly.
       </p>
       <p>
        Place the pies on the baking sheet and brush the tops with the egg wash. Bake for 25 minutes, depending on your oven, until just browned. Let cool on a wire rack.
       </p>
       <p>
        Little Iron Man pizza pies, the snack of choice for all Avengers!
       </p>
       <p>
        <a href="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/iron-man-hand-pie-done.jpg">
         <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10659" height="432" src="http://justjennrecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/iron-man-hand-pie-done.jpg" title="iron man hand pie done" width="258"/>
        </a>
       </p>
