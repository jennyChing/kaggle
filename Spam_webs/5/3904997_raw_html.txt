         <h1>
          Remembering Veterans
         </h1>
         <p>
          Everyday our troops make sacrifices to help keep us and our country safe. From those who have served, to those currently serving 
    and even those who will serve in the future  its important to recognize the sacrifices that they have made. We even have a few 
    holidays dedicated to recognizing the troops, however, they are often confused for each other. Its important to understand the 
    difference between Memorial Day and Veterans Day and the purposes that they serve for our military. You can recognize the efforts 
    of the men and women of the military by showing your appreciation on those days, or everyday by having a flag in your yard, donating 
    to different non-profits, or even just thanking a service member. Remember, that without the sacrifices that are made by these men 
    and women, that our country would not be what it is today. Heres our look at how we can remember our military veterans.
