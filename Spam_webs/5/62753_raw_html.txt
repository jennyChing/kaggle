      <h2>
       $1000 Mesothelioma Nursing Scholarship
      </h2>
      <p>
       We support the next generation of Nurses who may provide support and assistance for mesothelioma cancer patients. We are pleased to offer our $1000 annual Mesothelioma Nursing Scholarship to help future nurses attend college and becomes leaders in the healthcare field.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Who Should Apply:
       </strong>
       Students enrolled in an undergraduate (ADN, BSN, ADN-to-BSN) or graduate nursing program.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Requirements:
       </strong>
       Send us proof of your school enrollment and write a 2 page (double spaced) essay about the importance of helping patients with mesothelioma cancer. Attach your proof of enrollment, essay, and your contact information (full name, city, state of birth, email address, phone number and address) and email it to
       <a href="mailto:scholarship@mesothelioma.net?Subject=Mesothelioma Nursing Scholarship">
        scholarship@mesothelioma.net
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       The due date is May 31st and we will choose a winner by June 15.
      </p>
      <p>
