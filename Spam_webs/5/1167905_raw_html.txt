           <p>
            Weve all seen our cats get up on their two back legs from time to time, but George the cat wants to STAY there! Trending all over social media at the moment is this sweet cat named George. He just wants to be a human it seems and stand up on two legs! And as with so many other trends, up-to-the-minute
            <a href="http://www.boredpanda.com/george-the-cat-standing-on-2-legs/" target="_blank" title="http://www.boredpanda.com/george-the-cat-standing-on-2-legs/">

           </p>
           <blockquote>
            <p>
             <span style="color: #6a6a6a;">
              People like to say awww, he thinks hes people when they see an animal standing on two legs, but sometimes, they may not know how close they are to the truth. George is a cat that prefers to stand on two legs and looks like he intends to one day become a real person and take over the world.
             </span>
