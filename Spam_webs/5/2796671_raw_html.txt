        <section class="entry-content single-content clearfix" itemprop="articleBody">
         <p>
          Impress your guests this year with some personalized monogram napkins. These napkins are so easy to make and will add that extra special touch to the table to make your guest feel right at home. Either sew your own napkins or dress up linens you already have or bought from the store.
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://d17h7uxf2ajg2e.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/1_monogram_napkins.jpg">
           <img alt="Printable Monogram Napkin | Oh Happy Day!" class="alignnone wp-image-28908 size-full" height="866" src="http://d17h7uxf2ajg2e.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/1_monogram_napkins.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
          <a href="http://d17h7uxf2ajg2e.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2_monogram_napkins.jpg">
           <img alt="Printable Monogram Napkin | Oh Happy Day!" class="alignnone wp-image-28909 size-full" height="446" src="http://d17h7uxf2ajg2e.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/2_monogram_napkins.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
          <a href="http://d17h7uxf2ajg2e.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/3_monogram_napkins.jpg">
           <img alt="Printable Monogram Napkin | Oh Happy Day!" class="alignnone wp-image-28910 size-full" height="401" src="http://d17h7uxf2ajg2e.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/3_monogram_napkins.jpg" width="600"/>
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span id="more-28907">
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          <span class="s1">
           Materials:
           <a href="http://d17h7uxf2ajg2e.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/printable_monogram_napkin.pdf">
            Printable Monogram Napkin
           </a>
           , 8.5 x 11 printable Iron-on transfer paper (I used
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00004TS2H/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B00004TS2H&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=ohhada-20&amp;linkId=24HVYSCQLNOLDDPL">
            Epson Iron-on Cool Peel paper
           </a>
           <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=ohhada-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B00004TS2H" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
           ),
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008R4GPGE/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B008R4GPGE&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=ohhada-20&amp;linkId=66S3FMIANN7Q75LJ">
            cotton napkins
           </a>
           <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=ohhada-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B008R4GPGE" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
           (purchase from the store or sew your own),
           <a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002VPQH0Y/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&amp;camp=1789&amp;creative=390957&amp;creativeASIN=B002VPQH0Y&amp;linkCode=as2&amp;tag=ohhada-20&amp;linkId=Z34EWVZPKQMNTWY4">
            scissors
           </a>
           <img alt="" border="0" height="1" src="http://ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=ohhada-20&amp;l=as2&amp;o=1&amp;a=B002VPQH0Y" style="border: none !important; margin: 0px !important;" width="1"/>
           , iron &amp; ironing board (not pictured)
          </span>
         </p>
         <p>
          Step 1: Print out the
          <a href="http://d17h7uxf2ajg2e.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/printable_monogram_napkin.pdf">
           Printable Monogram Napkin
          </a>
          on 8.5 x 11 iron-on transfer paper.
          <br/>
          Step 2: With scissors, cut out the letters you need getting as close to the artwork as you can.
          <br/>
          Step 3: Iron out the napkin if there are any wrinkles. Place the front letter face down lat the corner of the napkin. Iron the letter to the napkin (for best results follow instructions provided with the printable iron-on paper).
          <br/>
          Step 4: After the napkin &amp; letter has cooled, carefully peel the iron-on paper off of the napkin
          <br/>
          Step 5: Dress your table up with these cute napkins and have a happy Thanksgiving!
         </p>
         <p class="p4">
          <a href="http://d17h7uxf2ajg2e.cloudfront.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/4_monogram_napkins.jpg">

          </a>
         </p>
