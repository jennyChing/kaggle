        <div class="article-content clearfix">
         <p>
          Phoenix, Arizona resident Nikki Elise experienced a waking nightmare when she discovered a man robbing her home while she was playing
          <a href="http://blog.dota2.com/">
           Dota 2
          </a>
          on the video game platform
          <a href="http://www.twitch.tv/">
           Twitch.
          </a>
         </p>
         <p>
          Because Elise, known online as Sajedene, is a popular streamer, the robbery was broadcast in real time over the internet so that her fellow gamers could inadvertently
          <a href="http://www.dailydot.com/esports/dota-2-burgler-live-twitch/">
           experience the horror
          </a>
          of the robbery along with her. Elises global audience witnessed a harrowing incident where an armed man removed items from her room, including Elises own firearms, before disconnecting her webcam.
         </p>
         <div class="mainembed videoembed mainembed-full_width align-center no-image">
          <iframe allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" frameborder="0" height="" src="//www.youtube.com/embed/MSVyPq1b-qk" width="">
          </iframe>
         </div>
         <p>
          Thankfully, Matthew Bailey, a journalist who also knows the gamer personally, can confirm that Elise has not been harmed and is doing okay.
         </p>
         <blockquote class="twitter-tweet" lang="en">
          <p>
           I can confirm that
           <a href="https://twitter.com/Sajedene">
            @Sajedene
           </a>
           &amp;
           <a href="https://twitter.com/SUNSfanTV">
            @SUNSfanTV
           </a>
           are ok. Very shaken up but ok.
          </p>
          <p>
            Matthew Bailey (@Cyborgmatt)
           <a href="https://twitter.com/Cyborgmatt/statuses/475981656323809280">
            June 9, 2014
           </a>
          </p>
         </blockquote>
         <p>
          <script async="" charset="utf-8" src="//platform.twitter.com/widgets.js">
          </script>
         </p>
         <p>
          Local media reports that one of the alleged robbers has been
          <a href="http://www.azfamily.com/news/Search-on-for-2-armed-suspects-in-early-morning-home-invasion-262377261.html">
           taken into custody
          </a>
          while another remains at large. According to
          <a href="http://www.azfamily.com/news/Search-on-for-2-armed-suspects-in-early-morning-home-invasion-262377261.html">
           AZ Family
          </a>
          , police were alerted to the scene when one of Elises friends called 911 while watching the stream.
         </p>
         <p>
          Source:
          <a href="http://www.dailydot.com/esports/dota-2-burgler-live-twitch/">
           The Daily Dot
          </a>
