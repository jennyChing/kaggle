&lt;/div&gt;

&lt;div&gt;&lt;div class="wsite-image wsite-image-border-none " style="padding-top:10px;padding-bottom:10px;margin-left:0;margin-right:0;text-align:center"&gt;

&lt;img src="/uploads/1/7/1/8/17185292/2057266_orig.jpg" alt="Photo of very cluttered kitchen counter and caption: As the book title promised, the recipe took only 15 minutes, but how long to clean all this?  This undermines simple living." style="width:auto;max-width:100%" /&gt;

&lt;div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;"&gt;&lt;span style='text-decoration:none; font-style:normal; font-weight:400; color:rgb(0, 54, 48); '&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;Food provides nourishment and pleasure. But the shopping, preparation, and cleanup for daily meals can be a hassle, even for those of us who love to cook occasionally.&nbsp;Simple cooking can help simplify life.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;

&lt;div&gt;&lt;div id="838443291346852944" align="left" style="width: 100%; overflow-y: hidden;" class="wcustomhtml"&gt;&lt;h3&gt;  Tips for Simple Cooking   &lt;/h3&gt;&lt;/div&gt;



&lt;/div&gt;

&lt;div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;"&gt;&lt;span style='text-decoration:none; font-style:normal; font-weight:400; color:rgb(0, 54, 48); '&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;&lt;span style="color: rgb(0, 54, 48); font-style: normal; font-weight: 400; text-decoration: none;"&gt;1)&nbsp;&lt;strong&gt;Adopt a standard dinner menu for each of the five weekdays,&lt;/strong&gt; preparing something special only on the weekends. Deliberating about what to prepare, checking recipes, and shopping for unusual&nbsp;ingredients can be time-consuming and stressful, and we don't&nbsp;need unique menus every day of the month. Stock up on the non-perishables required for the weekday menus when they are on sale.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;2) &lt;strong&gt;Use simple recipes that call for a small number of easily available ingredients.&lt;/strong&gt; Running around town, or even throughout a super-sized grocery store, to find needed ingredients is not conducive to simple living. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;3) &lt;strong&gt;Use simple recipes that call for few steps and short preparation times.&lt;/strong&gt; Slicing, dicing, and stirring take time. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;4) &lt;strong&gt;Use simple recipes that call for few utensils, processors, mixing bowls, pots, and pans.&lt;/strong&gt; All that stuff needs to be washed and then put away. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;5) &lt;strong&gt;Cook enough so that leftovers can be used for a second meal.&lt;/strong&gt; Some people cook two days&rsquo; worth of the main course and then serve it the second day with different sides. Others eat the leftovers the next day for lunch. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;6) &lt;strong&gt;Assign each person in the household a distinctive glass or mug to use throughout the day.&lt;/strong&gt; Have them responsible for rinsing it out after each use. Run all through the dishwasher just once a day. &lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;7) &lt;strong&gt;Teach your kids to prepare food and&nbsp;do the cleanup&lt;/strong&gt; when they are between 4 and&nbsp;10 years old, and thereafter have them share those tasks with the adults. Young children want to engage in&nbsp;adult behavior and usually delight in learning to cook alongside their parents. (Even a three-year-old can help by scrubbing vegetables.) By the teen years, the attraction&nbsp;often wears off, but the routine and expectation will be established.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;8) &lt;strong&gt;Use a Pyrex pint or quart measuring pitcher for microwaving.&lt;/strong&gt; Round containers provide the most even heating/cooking, and the handle will stay cool, allowing removal without a hot pad. Use any plastic cover to prevent splatters.&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;9) &lt;strong&gt;Microwave ovens are a boon to simple food preparation when used properly. &lt;/strong&gt;Remember to pierce the skin of whole potatoes, tomatoes, apples, hot dogs, and sausage. It is easier to read the Instruction Guide than to&nbsp;deal with a fire or explosion. &nbsp;&lt;br /&gt;&lt;span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/div&gt;

&lt;div&gt;&lt;div id="564045434604003499" align="left" style="width: 100%; overflow-y: hidden;" class="wcustomhtml"&gt;    &lt;h3&gt;  Twofers: Radically Simple Recipes  &lt;/h3&gt;&lt;/div&gt;



&lt;/div&gt;
