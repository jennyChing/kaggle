       <a href="http://news.pickuptrucks.com/2014/08/build-your-own-paper-power-wagon-pickup-1.html">
        Build Your Own Paper Power Wagon Pickup
       </a>
      </h2>
      <div class="post-info">
       Posted by Mark Williams | August 20, 2014
      </div>
      <p>
       <a class="asset-img-link" href="http://blogs.cars.com/.a/6a00d83451b3c669e201a73e057733970d-pi" style="display: inline;">
        <img alt="Paper PW 1 II" border="0" class="asset asset-image at-xid-6a00d83451b3c669e201a73e057733970d image-full img-responsive" src="http://blogs.cars.com/.a/6a00d83451b3c669e201a73e057733970d-800wi" title="Paper PW 1 II"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       It's not every day we find out you can download instructions on how to build your very own classic 4x4 pickup truck from the internet, but that's exactly what we found out from our friends at
       <a href="http://www.wermopar.com/blog/power-wagon-paper-model/#.U_S4fZT8w0o" target="_self">
        WeRMopar.com
       </a>
       . In fact, with a few clicks of your computer mouse, you can download instructions from the article or you can
       <span class="asset asset-generic at-xid-6a00d83451b3c669e201a73e057e93970d img-responsive">
        <a href="http://blogs.cars.com/files/dodge-power-wagon-paper-model-wermopar.pdf">
         click here
        </a>
       </span>
       to get a free template for this Dodge Power Wagon. Weve also provided a how-to video below.
      </p>
      <p>
       For the best results, use a heavier stock of paper and a good color printer for the most realistic end product. Of course, if only the real thing will do, you can always try to find a fully restored classic Power Wagon or invest in a
       <a href="http://news.pickuptrucks.com/2012/11/sema-2012-day-3-truck-of-the-day.html" target="_self">
        very cool reproduction
       </a>
       , but both will be pretty pricey. You can't beat the price on this miniature paper Power Wagon.
      </p>
      <p>
       We hope to see more of these free or reasonably priced paper model kits. We'll let you know if Ford or GM websites respond with their own F-100 or Chevrolet 3100 paper pickups projects.
      </p>
      <p>
       <em>
        Photos from WeRMopar.com
       </em>
      </p>
