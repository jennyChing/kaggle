      <div class="text">
       <h1>
        Add Features Instantly
       </h1>
       <h2>
        Rapidly develop a web application online with Prelang.
       </h2>
       <p>
        Take the guesswork out of starting a Rails project by using Prelang to kickstart your web app.  Add dozens of features to your codebase such as user login, UI frameworks, analytics, and more with the click of a button. Getting a head start on your project has never been easier or faster.
       </p>
      </div>
     </article>
     <article class="pitch_section" id="pitch_section_1">
      <div class="text">
       <h1>
        Model Your Data Visually
       </h1>
       <h2>
        Configure your data with a GUI: visualize your schema, manage attributes easily, and add plug-in validations.
       </h2>
       <p>
        Prelang's interface allows you to model data graphically.  It gives you visibility and control over all your models, their attributes, and their validations in one dashboard.  You can even add custom validators such as email, URL, and phone. Modeling data doesn't have to be a pain; relax and build your schema with more intelligent tools.
       </p>
      </div>
     </article>
     <article class="pitch_section" id="pitch_section_2">
      <div class="text">
       <h1>
        Generate Powerful Code
       </h1>
       <h2>
        Clone your project from GitHub and continue developing on a robust foundation.
       </h2>
       <p>
        Pick up where Prelang left off by loading your project into your local environment.  You'll already have your pre-selected features ready to use, and your data will be modeled... all without touching a line of code. Prelang even makes git commits along the way to show you what has been added. Instead of starting off with an empty application, let Prelang take the first steps for you.
       </p>
      </div>
