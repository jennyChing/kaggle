							&lt;h1&gt;The Crazy Cuban, the Chance to win $25K with Arnold Bread and a Giveaway!&lt;/h1&gt;
						&lt;p class='post-meta'&gt;
				&lt;span class='post-date'&gt;August 11, 2014&lt;/span&gt; 
				Posted in&amp;nbsp&lt;a href="http://thepost-itplace.com/category/give-away/" rel="category tag"&gt;give-away&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://thepost-itplace.com/category/post-it/" rel="category tag"&gt;Post-It&lt;/a&gt;, &lt;a href="http://thepost-itplace.com/category/recipe/" rel="category tag"&gt;recipe&lt;/a&gt;				&lt;span class='post-comments'&gt;&lt;a href="http://thepost-itplace.com/2014/08/11/the-crazy-cuban-the-chance-to-win-25k-with-arnold-bread-and-a-giveaway/#comments" title="Comment on The Crazy Cuban, the Chance to win $25K with Arnold Bread and a Giveaway!"&gt;62 comments&lt;/a&gt;&lt;/span&gt;

		&lt;div class='post-body'&gt;
			
							
				&lt;p&gt;We eat a lot of sandwiches around here. Between being a quick meal on a really busy day, something we love and the fact that Bric and Erin both take their &#8220;lunch&#8221; with them to work there is always a sandwich being made. We also like to get creative. The traditional ham and cheese or peanut butter and jelly can get old fast and the amount of calories we consume in bread alone can be overwhelming.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;One of the things that we have learned that can help is&lt;a href="http://bit.ly/1xF3skw"&gt;Arnold&lt;/a&gt; Sandwich Thins. We LOVE them and keep them in the bread box and freezer at all times. While they may only have 100 calories per thin, you won&#8217;t miss out on any taste. I love that they are so soft and fresh! These are all Bric will eat.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="size-medium wp-image-13576 aligncenter" src="http://thepost-itplace.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/IMG_5550-300x225.jpg" alt="IMG_5550" width="300" height="225" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;I was recently presented with the opportunity to try out some of the newArnold breads AND enter the Arnold Bread Americas Better Sandwich Contest! And you can too!! Entrants will have the opportunity to win the $25,000 grand prize!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;All of Arnold Whole Grain Sliced Breads and Arnold Sandwich Thins Rolls contain 3 to 4 grams of fiber per slice or thin,&lt;br /&gt;
Zero grams of Trans Fat, No High Fructose Corn Syrup and are Cholesterol free.&lt;br /&gt;
Health authorities recommend munching on 25-38 grams of fiber[1] and 48 grams of whole grains per day[2], much of which may come from whole grain products like Arnold Whole Grain Sliced Breads or Arnold Sandwich Thins Rolls varieties. If youre not sure of how many grams of whole grains you are getting, look for the Whole Grain Stamp found on all of Arnold Whole Grain Sliced Breads and Arnold Sandwich Thins Rolls.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Are you ready to hear more about Americas Better Sandwich Contest ?&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: left;"&gt;The sandwich architect whose filling matches the goodness of Arnold Bread will win a grand prize of $25,000.&lt;br /&gt;
 Before entering a better sandwich in the Contest, the entrant should make sure it includes any of the Arnold Whole Grain Sliced Breads or Arnold Sandwich Thins Rolls varieties.&lt;br /&gt;
 There are four (4) sandwich categories in the Arnold Americas Better Sandwich Contest including: Creative Classic (a healthful twist on a sandwich that has been around for more than two generations, such as a Reuben, BLT or club sandwich), Stealthily Simple (maximum of five (5) ingredients), Fueling Good (ingredients that help boost your energy such as healthful fats, protein, fiber and iron), and Meatless Marvel (no animal products including land and sea animals may be included in the recipe).&lt;br /&gt;
 The recipe in each sandwich category with the highest score will receive a $5,000 category prize.&lt;br /&gt;
 Entries will be accepted July 23rd &#8211; August 17th, online consumer voting will take place September 22nd &#8211; October 17th, and the winners will be announced on National Sandwich Day- November 3rd.&lt;br /&gt;
 To submit a recipe, receive tips on creating a better sandwich or learn more about the Official Rules visit: &lt;a href="http://www.americasbettersandwich.com"&gt;www.americasbettersandwich.com&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;So, with a deadline of August 17th, you better get busy!! To check out more, make sure to follow along at the &lt;a href="http://on.fb.me/1hMBezz"&gt;Arnold Bread Facebook page&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Want a sneak peak at what I created? Check out a new twist on the beloved Cuban Sandwich. We call it the Crazy Cuban!&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;&lt;img class="size-full wp-image-13577 aligncenter" src="http://thepost-itplace.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/044.jpg" alt="044" width="375" height="500" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;And now you see my favorite! &lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;The Pocket Thins! &lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p style="text-align: center;"&gt;&lt;strong&gt;Still only 100 calories but they sure do make cozy little sandwiches!&lt;/strong&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;&lt;img class="size-full wp-image-13578 aligncenter" src="http://thepost-itplace.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/041.jpg" alt="041" width="375" height="500" /&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Lean pork loin slow cooked to perfection in a traditional Mojo marinade then shredded, topped with shredded cabbage a slice of tomato and a sauce made with low-fat mayonnaise, balsamic vinegar and spicy brown mustard.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;2 pound lean pork loin&lt;br /&gt;
2 cups Mojo marinade&lt;br /&gt;
1 onion, cut into strips&lt;br /&gt;
1 green bell pepper, cut into strips&lt;br /&gt;
1 pkg Arnold Multi-grain Pocket Thin or Sandwich Thins&lt;br /&gt;
1 cup shredded cabbage&lt;br /&gt;
1 tomato, sliced thin&lt;br /&gt;
1/2 cup low-fat mayonnaise&lt;br /&gt;
1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar&lt;br /&gt;
2 Tablespoons spicy brown mustard&lt;br /&gt;
1/2 tsp garlic powder&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Place the pork loin in slow cooker, top with onion and bell pepper. Pour marinade over top and cook on high 4-6 hours. Once fully cooked shred.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Mix mayonnaise, spicy mustard, balsamic vinegar and garlic powder in a small bowl.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Spread a little of the dressing on each side of the bread. On one side put a little of the meat, onions and bell peppers. Top with some shredded cabbage, a slice of tomato and other piece of bread. If using a pocket thin, stuff in same order.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Enjoy!&lt;/p&gt;
