     </h1>

     <h1>
      <span style="font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size: 11px;">
       <a href="../bti/our_team.html#jerald">
        <strong>
         By Jerald OKennard
        </strong>
       </a>
      </span>
     </h1>
     <p>
      Every one interested in drinks wants to have a great bar to enjoy with their guests. But how do you create one? Simply going out and buying the most expensive spirits isnt the answer because you dont always get what you pay for and unfortunately there are a lot of overpriced and overhyped brands out there. Obviously buying just the cheapest spirits isnt the solution either for anyone interested in taste. We take the guesswork out the process for you with our new BTI Virtual Bar. Weve selected some of the best tasting spirits discovered in our most recent
      <a href="/International_Review_of_Spirits.html">
       International Review of Spirits
      </a>
      and
      <a href="/BTIBestBuyBar.html">
       Best Buy Bar
      </a>
      competitions. These spirits will deliver incredible taste, and in many cases incredible value, for your Dream Bar or your Best Buy Bar.
     </p>
     <p>
      You will also find our recommendations for the tools of the trade (cocktail shakers, blenders, glassware, etc)
      <a href="../spirits/bar_tools.html">
       here
      </a>
      .
     </p>
