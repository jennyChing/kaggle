       <h1 class="entry-title">
        USA  Road Trip  Trinidad  24th September
       </h1>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <div class="topsy_widget_data topsy_theme_blue" style="float: right; background: url(data:,%7B%20%22url%22%3A%20%22http%253A%252F%252Fwww.notworkrelated.co.uk%252F2011%252F10%252Fusa-road-trip-trinidad-24th-sept%252F%22%2C%20%22style%22%3A%20%22big%22%2C%20%22title%22%3A%20%22USA%20%E2%80%93%20Road%20Trip%20%E2%80%93%20Trinidad%20%E2%80%93%2024th%20September%22%20%7D);">
        </div>
        <p>
         When we got up the following day the fog had cleared and we spent a few hours at the
         <a href="http://www.rogueguide.com/IndianCreek.htm" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.rogueguide.com']);" target="_blank">
          Indian Creek Cafe
         </a>
         topping up our writing for the blog. Its a popular place for those passing through on-route and we felt a little bad for taking up a table for just a coffee and an orange juice, friendly and funny staff though.
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-6908" height="188" src="http://www.notworkrelated.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/notworkrelated_usa_roadtrip_trinidad_07-188x188.jpg" title="notworkrelated_usa_roadtrip_trinidad_07" width="188"/>
         We drove in and out through the fog and stopped off at various places along the route. Its a rugged coastline with rocks jutting out all along the way, there are so many stopping points it takes ages to get anywhere. We drove through some of the well known Redwood Forests and State Parks but the weather was not so good so we decided not to camp there for the night. A little further down Highway 1 and we landed in Trinidad and decided that we werent going to camp, instead we wanted a rooma bed!! After searching and stopping off at a few B&amp;Bs we ended up at
         <a href="http://www.rvintheredwoods.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.rvintheredwoods.com']);" target="_blank">
          The Emerald Forest Resort
         </a>
         , where we got a log cabin for a AAA reduced rate because Helens aunty is a member! This was our first night in a bed in nearly 2 weeks and we were pretty excited!
        </p>
        <p>
         <img alt="" class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-6923" height="188" src="http://www.notworkrelated.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/notworkrelated_usa_roadtrip_trinidad_23-188x188.jpg" title="notworkrelated_usa_roadtrip_trinidad_23" width="188"/>
         We walked up to
         <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trinidad,_California" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://en.wikipedia.org']);" target="_blank">
          Trinidad Head
         </a>
         , which proved to be very rewarding, its a good little walk up to a point with more fantastic views. Trinidad is a nice little town but does seem to have most property on the holiday rental lists so its hard to find the community there. Back at our cabin we enjoyed food and Finding Nemo, oh how easy it is to get sucked into TV.
        </p>

         </p>
