             <h1 class="post-title entry-title">
              <a href="http://www.smashtrend.com/2014/10/bizarre-theories-about-pyramids-that.html" title=" Bizarre Theories About The Pyramids That will surprise you .">
               Bizarre Theories About The Pyramids That will surprise you .
              </a>
             </h1>
             <div itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting">
              <div class="post-body entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
               <div style="text-align: center;">
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                  1.) Medieval Europeans believed the pyramids were built to store grain because of the mentions of granaries in the Old Testament.
                 </span>
                </h3>
                <div>
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <div>
                 <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Ysb2o1ZFMrg/VFDyIlGpZ3I/AAAAAAAABf0/j8ZLMvDlzDE/s1600/7.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                  <img border="0" height="358" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-Ysb2o1ZFMrg/VFDyIlGpZ3I/AAAAAAAABf0/j8ZLMvDlzDE/s1600/7.png" width="640"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: center;">
                <br/>
                <a name="more">
                </a>
                <br/>
               </div>
               <div style="text-align: center;">
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                  2.) In 1845, M. Fialin de Persigngy offered up a theory that the pyramids were built to act as barriers to protect Egypt and Nubia from the wind and sand.
                 </span>
                </h3>
                <br/>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-CaKhw_TckMs/VFDyGKDoaUI/AAAAAAAABfU/UIg4b502sHg/s1600/2.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                  <img border="0" height="582" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-CaKhw_TckMs/VFDyGKDoaUI/AAAAAAAABfU/UIg4b502sHg/s1600/2.png" width="640"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-family: Oswald, sans-serif; font-size: 2rem; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <br/>
                </h3>
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                  3.) Ignatius Loyola Donnelly, a former Congressman from Minnesota, wrote in 1882 that residents of the fictional city of Atlantis built the pyramids during a period of colonization.
                 </span>
                </h3>
                <br/>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-5Ok12Gi_dfo/VFDyG17kK1I/AAAAAAAABfg/5hLN0mPcOWw/s1600/3.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                  <img border="0" height="640" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-5Ok12Gi_dfo/VFDyG17kK1I/AAAAAAAABfg/5hLN0mPcOWw/s1600/3.png" width="556"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                  4.) A Swedish philosopher from the 1800s theorized that the pyramids were actually filtering reservoirs for the murky Nile River.
                 </span>
                </h3>
                <br/>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-zjtZxTcTPX0/VFDyHny_toI/AAAAAAAABfk/Nl6Ud6cmsQA/s1600/4.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                  <img border="0" height="480" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-zjtZxTcTPX0/VFDyHny_toI/AAAAAAAABfk/Nl6Ud6cmsQA/s1600/4.png" width="640"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                  5.) In 2014, Egyptian researcher Amir Gamal told Elaph newspaper that he believes Israel is plotting to steal the pyramids.
                 </span>
                </h3>
                <br/>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-arn2jpYnciI/VFDyH17qNmI/AAAAAAAABfs/PG-nHV5hgIU/s1600/5.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                  <img border="0" height="428" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-arn2jpYnciI/VFDyH17qNmI/AAAAAAAABfs/PG-nHV5hgIU/s1600/5.png" width="640"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                  6.) Author of The Orion Mystery Robert Bauval claims the pyramids are aligned with the Orion constellation.
                 </span>
                </h3>
                <br/>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-BRf44pbIBMY/VFDyIh1q2pI/AAAAAAAABf4/lsccwRQLZkM/s1600/6.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                  <img border="0" height="424" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-BRf44pbIBMY/VFDyIh1q2pI/AAAAAAAABf4/lsccwRQLZkM/s1600/6.png" width="640"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-family: Oswald, sans-serif; font-size: 2rem; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <br/>
                </h3>
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                  7.) A leader of the early Jehovah's Witness movement, Joseph T. "Judge" Rutherford, wrote in 1928 that the pyramids were built by Satan
                 </span>
                </h3>
                <div>
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                  <br/>
                 </span>
                </div>
                <a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-M38Sp1onbxU/VFDyF0ddEXI/AAAAAAAABfQ/GinqWZKMgSs/s1600/1.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                 <img border="0" height="480" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-M38Sp1onbxU/VFDyF0ddEXI/AAAAAAAABfQ/GinqWZKMgSs/s1600/1.png" width="640"/>
                </a>
                <br/>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <br/>
                </div>
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                  8.) An 1884 scientific conference held in the city of Philadelphia featured a paper that theorized the pyramids were actually hills that were cut into pyramids.
                 </span>
                </h3>
                <br/>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-O0kjszOFQgw/VFDyJ0nhHpI/AAAAAAAABgI/mcnMsdQD_w8/s1600/8.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                  <img border="0" height="420" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-O0kjszOFQgw/VFDyJ0nhHpI/AAAAAAAABgI/mcnMsdQD_w8/s1600/8.png" width="640"/>
                 </a>
                </div>
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-family: Oswald, sans-serif; font-size: 2rem; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <br/>
                </h3>
                <h3 style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 400; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; text-align: left;">
                 <span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">
                  9.) Charles Piazzi Smyth, the Astronomer Royal for Scotland, published a book in 1864 that the Great Pyramid would foretell the end of the world, which he predicted to take place in 1881.
                 </span>
                </h3>
                <br/>
                <div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
                 <a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-3q3IjZIYJTc/VFDyKBJTZFI/AAAAAAAABgM/PUKZ5wrJEnc/s1600/9.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;">
                  <img border="0" height="470" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-3q3IjZIYJTc/VFDyKBJTZFI/AAAAAAAABgM/PUKZ5wrJEnc/s1600/9.png" width="640"/>
