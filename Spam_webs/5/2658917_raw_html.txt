        <header class="entry-header">
         <h1 class="entry-title">
          25 Things You Will Only Understand If You Have Had A Bad Haircut
         </h1>
         <ul class="post-details clearfix">
          <li>
           9 months ago by
           <a href="http://www.thegloss.com/author/lizlicata/" rel="author" title="Posts by Elizabeth Licata">
            Elizabeth Licata
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="http://www.thegloss.com/2014/10/23/beauty/hair-beauty/bad-haircut-annoying-things-reaction-gifs/#comments" title="Comment on 25 Things You Will Only Understand If You Have Had A Bad Haircut">
            <span class="dsq-postid" rel="443573 http://www.thegloss.com/?p=443573">
             4 Comments 
            </span>
           </a>
          </li>
          <li>
           <a href="#top" onclick="show_modal_window('share-tip-window');">
            Share a Tip
           </a>
          </li>
          <li class="last-item">
          </li>
         </ul>
        </header>
        <!-- .entry-header -->
        <div class="entry-content">
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-32.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443638" height="378" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-32.gif" title="giphy-3" width="500"/>
          </a>
          Guys, I have the worst haircut ever. Two years ago I moved to a bucolic German wonderland that looks just like the town Belle calls provincial in
          <em>
           Beauty and the Beast,
          </em>
          and I had no sense of where to go for a haircut. Whenever I saw someone with cool hair I would ask where she got it cut, and the answer was always, Paris. Le sigh.
         </p>
         <p>
          Finally though, I just needed the hair to go. I had about 10 of split ends so rough I could tie my hair in a knot and it would stay like that. My efforts to avoid a bad haircut had left me with a shaggy haircut worse than any awkward cut could be. So I went to get a haircut. How bad could it be, right?
         </p>
         <p>
          So bad, guys.
         </p>
         <p>
          If youve never had a bad haircut, heres how it goes down:
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           1. When you choose the salon on a wing and a prayer
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy3.gif">
           <img alt="giphy" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443641" height="288" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy3.gif" title="giphy" width="500"/>
          </a>
          They say dont judge a book by its cover, but it should be safer to judge a salon by its decor. I picked the fanciest looking one, figuring the poshest place must be the best. It had a slick, minimalist design, huge mirrors, and all white everything. It seemed comparatively pricey for a college town, too.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           2. When you get an immediate appointment as a walk-in
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-32.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443638" height="378" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-32.gif" title="giphy-3" width="500"/>
          </a>
          Should I be nervous or relieved that I dont have to make an appointment?
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           3. When you realize that the same posters are in every salon in town
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-113.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443640" height="364" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-113.gif" title="giphy-1" width="500"/>
          </a>
          Those people with wild 80s hair in the window are stock hair photo models. They are not examples of the salons work.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           4. When you get your consultation
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-271.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-27" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443615" height="256" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-271.gif" title="giphy-27" width="245"/>
          </a>
          I bring out a tablet and start googling pictures of celebrities with hair I like. The stylist starts going a little pale.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           5. When you have your oh crap moment
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-122.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-12" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443630" height="354" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-122.gif" title="giphy-12" width="500"/>
          </a>
          Theres always one moment when you realize, Shit, this could go really wrong. For me it was when the stylist said she needed to bring over her boss because her English wasnt good enough to communicate with me. So I chatted with the salon owner a bit and then he started talking to her in German. After a minute or two I realized he wasnt explaining the style I wanted to her, he was actually explaining to her what layers are and how to cut them.
         </p>
         <p>
          Oh crap, I thought.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           6. When theres still time to leave
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-82.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-8" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443634" height="339" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-82.gif" title="giphy-8" width="500"/>
          </a>
          I could do it. I could say I changed my mind. I could say the baby was crying. I could say I had to go to the bathroom. I could still get out of there 
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           7. When you cant deal with the awkwardness
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-172.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-17" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443625" height="138" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-172.gif" title="giphy-17" width="246"/>
          </a>
          But I couldnt leave, because that would be awkward. The stylist might be sad that I didnt trust her. The salon owner might think I was finicky and uptight.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           8. The moment of resignation
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-132.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-13" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443629" height="300" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-132.gif" title="giphy-13" width="392"/>
          </a>
          It was too awkward to leave, so I stayed. How bad could it be? I thought.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           9. Denial
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-72.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-7" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443635" height="215" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-72.gif" title="giphy-7" width="241"/>
          </a>
          Everything will be fine, I thought.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           10. Bargaining
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-102.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-10" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443632" height="362" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-102.gif" title="giphy-10" width="362"/>
          </a>
          If it gets messed up, at least I can write about it, right?
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           11.When the stylist asks if the length is OK
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-153.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-15" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443627" height="202" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-153.gif" title="giphy-15" width="250"/>
          </a>
          Is it too short? she asks. I wanted to know what she planned on doing if I said, Yes, it is definitely too short. Put it back on.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           12. When you realize the stylists cute haircut is not indicative of what you will be getting
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-251.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-25" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443617" height="180" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-251.gif" title="giphy-25" width="320"/>
          </a>
          She didnt cut her own hair.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           13. When the hair starts to pile up in your lap
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-28.gif">
            <img alt="giphy-2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443639" height="282" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-28.gif" title="giphy-2" width="500"/>
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Hair grows, you mutter to yourself. Hair grows.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           14. When something doesnt look right
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-182.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-18" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443624" height="162" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-182.gif" title="giphy-18" width="245"/>
          </a>
          Something about the way she is snipping the back doesnt look like the way Ive seen anyone else snip ever. But Im not a stylist and she is, so 
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           15. When you are 100% sure something has gone wrong
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-241.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-24" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443618" height="500" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-241.gif" title="giphy-24" width="399"/>
          </a>
          The stylist whipped out a diffuser and dried my hair super curly. Its almost impossible to assess the lines of a haircut when its curly. Im pretty sure shes hiding something.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           16. When you have another shot at awkwardness
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-211.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-21" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443621" height="290" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-211.gif" title="giphy-21" width="245"/>
          </a>
          Crap. Now that you know its bad, what do you do? Do you say something? Ask them to fix it? You know youre supposed to tell them to fix it. Damn your conflict-averse personality!
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           17. When you realize you are a giant coward
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-222.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-22" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443620" height="250" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-222.gif" title="giphy-22" width="500"/>
          </a>
          You didnt say something.
          <strong>
           Hillary Clinton
          </strong>
          would have said something.
          <strong>
           Helen Mirren
          </strong>
          would have said something.
          <strong>
           Claire Huxtable
          </strong>
          would have said something.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           18. When you first see yourself outside the salon
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-192.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-19" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443623" height="375" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-192.gif" title="giphy-19" width="500"/>
          </a>
          In the window of the real estate office around the corner, I spotted my new haircut. It wasnt layered. It was like a mutant mullet, and not one of those cute mullets that were so popular in Japan in 2004. My hair was cut in a shoulder-length bob all around, completely unlayered. Then, underneath all that, theres another section at the back that is five inches longer. They arent layered together or joined in any way, its basically like mom hair with a really thick rat tail attached. I just laughed and laughed.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           19. When you consider fixing it yourself
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-92.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-9" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443633" height="150" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-92.gif" title="giphy-9" width="245"/>
          </a>
          Im pretty sure I could just lop those bonus five inches right off the back and have a basic bob.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           20. When you consider going back to the salon
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-222.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-22" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443620" height="250" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-222.gif" title="giphy-22" width="500"/>
          </a>
          Is it worth it to go back? But so awkward! And I already invested an hour of my life into this and thats an hour too much. And having to go in and explain in German that I had a really weird-looking haircut would be difficult. Could I just point at my head and say, Fix?
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           21. But they were the salon that fucked it up in the first place
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-142.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-14" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443628" height="180" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-142.gif" title="giphy-14" width="320"/>
          </a>
          Can they really fix it? Or would that just be throwing good hair after bad?
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           22. When you decide to let it go
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-42.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-4" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443637" height="270" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-42.gif" title="giphy-4" width="540"/>
          </a>
          Like Elsa, who let it go when someone gave her those big 80s bangs.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           23. When you fantasize about having it fixed
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-231.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-23" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443619" height="263" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-231.gif" title="giphy-23" width="250"/>
          </a>
          I sat through a terrible haircut. Clearly that means I deserve to go to Paris and treat myself to a $260 haircut
          <a href="http://david-mallett.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://david-mallett.com']);" target="_blank">
           by the stylist who cuts Marion Cotillards hair
          </a>
          . Im sure wed be best friends. I am visualizing us laughing over my bad haircut together like old chums.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           24. When you look at your fancy shampoos and hair supplies
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-261.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-26" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443616" height="281" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-261.gif" title="giphy-26" width="500"/>
          </a>
          It just seems like kind of a waste now.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           25. When you put it in a ponytail
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          <a href="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-202.gif">
           <img alt="giphy-20" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-443622" height="220" src="http://cdn2.thegloss.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/10/giphy-202.gif" title="giphy-20" width="245"/>
          </a>
          Now its really over. Sometimes I even forget whats going on back there.
         </p>
         <p>
          <em>
           Photos: Giphy
          </em>
         </p>
