           <h1 class="post-title">
            Samsung Galaxy Note Edge vs. LG G Flex 2
           </h1>
           <p class="post-byline">
            by
            <a href="http://www.galaxynoteedge-accessories.com/author/admin/" rel="author" title="Posts by admin">
             admin

            <div class="entry-inner">
             <p>
              #SamsungGalaxyNoteEdge #LgGFlex2 #GalaxyNoteEdge
              <br/>
              <a href="http://goo.gl/kLoqv6">
               <img alt="Galaxy Note Edge vs. LG G Flex 2" class="aligncenter wp-image-37" height="264" src="http://www.galaxynoteedge-accessories.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/04/LG-G-Flex2-vs-Samsung-Galaxy-Note-Edge.jpg" width="500"/>
              </a>
              The Note Edge is a great phablet, in fact Samsung mobiles in general are considered to be the best not only because the support of the Samsung brand but also because their mobiles include special features and also because it seems like they have a mobile for everyone, depending on your needs.
             </p>
             <p>
              The
              <a href="http://goo.gl/kLoqv6" target="_blank" title="Get the cheapest Samsung Galaxy Note Edge">
               Samsung Galaxy Note Edge
              </a>
              has specific features and characteristics, for example, it features a 5.6  QHD screen, support SMS, MMS, Push Mail, IM and Email messaging, also includes Java, thanks to an MIDP emulator, also the battery charge at 60% in just 30 minutes, it has an advanced photo/video editor, it has a dedicated mic with an active noise cancellation, and the Dropbox of 50 GB cloud storage.
             </p>
             <div class="code-block code-block-2" style="margin:8px 0px;">
              <div>

             <p>
              But lets compare this amazing mobile with another one of another company, for example the LG G Flex 2, which is a good phone with special features as well, we are not going to talk about the similarities but well focus in the significant differences. In terms of camera, which is one of the features that people take into consideration when they are going to buy a mobile, the Note Edge has a camera of 16 MP  3456 x 4608 pixels, autofocus, LED flash, image stabilization, dual shot plus touch focus among other characteristics, however the LG G Flex 2 camera is good but not as much as the one of the Note Edge (it has only 13 MP  4128 x 3096 pixels).
             </p>
             <div class="code-block code-block-3" style="margin:8px 0px;">

             <p>
              Other differences are that the Note Edge is bigger and heavier than the Flex 2, but the difference is not really important, also the Note Edge has a resolution of 1600 x 2560 pixels and the LG G Flex 2 is just 1080 x 1920 pixels, which is a significant difference. In resume, both phones are really good, however if we focus on the main differences, you must choose the Note Edge.
