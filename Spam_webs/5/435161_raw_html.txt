      <h1 class="post-title">
       Take Back Your Beauty: Our Appearance Should Appeal to Ourselves
      </h1>
      <div id="tab-container">
       <ul>
        <li>
         <a href="#article-wrap">
          Article
         </a>
        </li>
        <li>
         <a href="#comment-wrap">
          Comments
         </a>
        </li>
       </ul>
       <div id="article-wrap">
        <div class="entry">
         <div class="shareaholic-canvas" data-app="share_buttons" data-app-id="6934065" data-link="http://www.projecteve.com/take-back-your-beauty-our-appearance-should-appeal-to-ourselves/" data-summary="" data-title="Take Back Your Beauty: Our Appearance Should Appeal to Ourselves">
         </div>
         <p>
          Many women make body and beauty choices because of the way men will perceive them, or the way their friends or competitors will regard and treat them.
         </p>
         <p>
          Often, especially in the workplace, women are either
          <a href="http://www.forbes.com/sites/evangelinegomez/2012/01/31/should-businesses-worry-about-appearance-based-discrimination-in-the-workplace/" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.forbes.com/sites/evangelinegomez/2012/01/31/should-businesses-worry-about-appearance-based-discrimination-in-the-workplace/', 'pressured to look a certain way']);">
           pressured to look a certain way
          </a>
          to keep their job and impress their coworkers, or they feel squelched in their self-expression for fear of looking too sexy or standing out among their already higher-paid male counterparts.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          In 2014, its okay to be a beautiful woman and feel empowered  not because the boss favors you, but because you deserve to feel confident in yourself.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          <strong>
           How to reclaim your beauty
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          Dont let your fear and lack of self-confidence hold you back from being the woman you were meant to be. You can start to appeal to your own vision of beauty, rather than someone elses, by following these tips:
         </p>
         <ul>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <p dir="ltr">
             Pamper yourself. Dont be afraid to relax and treat yourself well. Many professional women seem to feel that self-pampering is a sign of laziness or weakness, but its actually the opposite. When you get pampered, its a sign that you have worked hard and understand the value of rejuvenation. Examples of pampering include manicures and pedicures, massages, and salon sessions. In addition to helping you relax, a pampering session has enormous benefits for your physical appearance.
            </p>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </ul>
         <ul>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <p dir="ltr">
             Wear your favorite colors. Everyone has her favorite colors, but many people avoid wearing them for fear of appearing too vivacious or happy. If you really want to
             <a href="http://www.businessinsider.com/rules-of-appearance-in-the-modern-workplace-2013-11" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.businessinsider.com/rules-of-appearance-in-the-modern-workplace-2013-11', 'reclaim your beauty']);">
              reclaim your beauty
             </a>
             , you need to get over those irrational fears and start strutting what you like. Wearing your favorite colors allows you to feel more confident and poised.
            </p>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </ul>
         <ul>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <p dir="ltr">
             Choose fabrics that feel good. Much like wearing your favorite colors, its important to wear fabrics that you find comfortable. Its incredible how much difference comfortable clothing can have on your workplace confidence. Instead of wearing uncomfortable fabrics that are supposedly in style, opt for what you prefer.
            </p>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </ul>
         <ul>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <p dir="ltr">
             Ignore trends and social pressures. As a rule of thumb, go ahead and ignore trends you dont like. Often, people follow trends for the sake of doing whats popular. If you ever find yourself feeling pressured into wearing something you dont want to wear, stick with your personal preferences. Youll have much more respect for yourself and wont feel as if you gave in to pressure.
            </p>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </ul>
         <ul>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <p dir="ltr">
             Love your body. While its important to maintain a healthy body, you should also learn to love the body youre given. Once you learn to love the way you look, you can begin to accept yourself as worthy. You shouldnt have to live up to anyone elses expectations of what you should look like. On the other hand, if you arent entirely satisfied with your weight or shape, maybe you should consider working out and altering your diet. But remember, the important thing is for you to be happy, not everyone else.
            </p>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </ul>
         <ul>
          <ul>
           <li>
            <p dir="ltr">
             Take care of your hair and skin. One simple way to improve your own vision of beauty is to take care of your hair and skin in a healthy way. There are a number of ways to do this; you may consult hair-care and skin-care professionals, purchase in-home products, and change things up from time to time. Skin-care products are among the easiest fixes. With products like
             <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eIi-0CKY_K8" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eIi-0CKY_K8', 'Ldara']);">
              Ldara
             </a>
             , you can achieve healthy, younger-looking skin in just weeks. Some hair products take even less time to work.
            </p>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </ul>
         <p dir="ltr">
          <strong>
           Confident women
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          Women often get a bad rap for lacking self-confidence and strong self-esteem. Theres no reason that should be the case for you.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          While you may not lack confidence, society can often make you feel guilty for feeling good about yourself. Everywhere you look, articles and advertisements are telling you that women lack self-esteem.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          Dont go down that road! This
          <a href="http://www.psychologytoday.com/blog/the-power-women/201001/the-truth-about-women-and-self-esteem" onclick="_gaq.push(['_trackEvent', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.psychologytoday.com/blog/the-power-women/201001/the-truth-about-women-and-self-esteem', 'mischaracterization']);">
           mischaracterization
          </a>
          has many negative side effects.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          Its important to reclaim your beauty and confidence by ignoring what everyone else has to say. You are as beautiful as you want to be, and shouldnt let anyone else hold you back. By following some of these tips, you can start to regain your image and devalue the importance of other peoples opinion.
         </p>
         <p dir="ltr">
          Your mission is about productivity and efficiency, not about how you do or dont look. Start a trend in your workplace that says no to judgmental glances and yes to self-esteem and beauty.
         </p>
