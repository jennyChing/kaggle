        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         Chore Ribbon {Tutorial}
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2011-06-15T15:19:00+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          June 15, 2011
         </time>
         by
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://whipperberry.com/author/hthoming" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Heather
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://whipperberry.com/2011/06/chore-ribbon-tutorial.html#comments">
           7 Comments
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <a class="isly-pinterest-permalink" data-description="Whipperberry: Chore Ribbon {Tutorial}" href="http://whipperberry.com/2011/06/chore-ribbon-tutorial.html" style="display: none !important;">
        </a>
        <div class="pin-it-btn-wrapper">
         <a data-pin-config="beside" data-pin-do="buttonPin" href="//www.pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwhipperberry.com%2F2011%2F06%2Fchore-ribbon-tutorial.html&amp;media=http%3A%2F%2Flh3.ggpht.com%2F_-fnKHrxIOI8%2FTcsmV-ZYISI%2FAAAAAAAAGV4%2Fh8Tidt0yaDw%2FWhipperBerryLogo%255B5%255D.jpg%3Fimgmax%3D800&amp;description=Chore%20Ribbon%20%7BTutorial%7D" rel="nobox">
          <img data-pib-button="true" src="//assets.pinterest.com/images/pidgets/pin_it_button.png" title="Pin It"/>
         </a>
        </div>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
          <em>
           A few weeks ago we had the honor to guest post on the Silhouette blog {yea, just a little excited about that!} I thought that I would post the project that we posted over there today, just in case you missed it
          </em>
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
          One of our favorite products EVER is the Silhouette. We have been working with it for quite some time now and have really fallen in love. I have recently started working with the heat transfer material and my stars what a great design element.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
          With the summer approaching I decided to make a Chore Ribbon for my kiddos
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <a href="http://lh4.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmNBooAkI/AAAAAAAAGUo/VjJiy975uF4/s1600-h/WB%20043%20copy%5B6%5D.jpg">
          <img alt="WB 043 copy" border="0" height="799" src="//lh6.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmN-G_mqI/AAAAAAAAGUs/OyDZk5Ci2IM/WB%20043%20copy_thumb%5B3%5D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; margin: 0px auto; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="WB 043 copy" width="524"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
          This is kind of a non traditional approach to the chore chart. I used the silhouette vinyl for the hanger and then used the heat transfer material to place my girls names directly on the jute ribbon. Heres how I did it
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
          First, I created my girls names in the Silhouette software with the LD Charming Bold font that I purchased from the Silhouette store. Keep in mind when using the heat transfer material you need to create a mirror image to place on your fabric.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <a href="http://lh4.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmOLGY3CI/AAAAAAAAGUw/efkwJ6t88BQ/s1600-h/screenshot%5B3%5D.jpg">
          <img alt="screenshot" border="0" height="321" src="//lh3.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmOrXF5BI/AAAAAAAAGU0/oXA_WfzF2_0/screenshot_thumb%5B1%5D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="screenshot" width="533"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
          I then cut the heat transfer material according to the package instructions. Next, I carefully peeled the excess heat transfer material away from the plastic
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmPGjO1pI/AAAAAAAAGU4/ntcLVPiQ6yE/s1600-h/WB%20001%20copy%5B4%5D.jpg">
          <img alt="WB 001 copy" border="0" height="264" src="//lh6.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmPkIIxlI/AAAAAAAAGU8/nXmVCCVr1mc/WB%20001%20copy_thumb%5B1%5D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: block; float: none; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; margin-left: auto; border-left-width: 0px; margin-right: auto; padding-top: 0px" title="WB 001 copy" width="298"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
          I flipped the image over and placed it on the jute ribbon
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <a href="http://lh3.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmQJBbimI/AAAAAAAAGVA/B-MEkq0Jg6o/s1600-h/WB%20012%20copy%5B3%5D.jpg">
          <img alt="WB 012 copy" border="0" height="344" src="//lh5.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmQerwC2I/AAAAAAAAGVE/Tc6qxFdiBwE/WB%20012%20copy_thumb.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="WB 012 copy" width="516"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
           with the image right facing and the plastic sheet on top of the heat transfer material.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <a href="http://lh3.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmQlsVdcI/AAAAAAAAGVI/bo5NUq7BgfQ/s1600-h/WB%20014%20copy%5B4%5D.jpg">
          <img alt="WB 014 copy" border="0" height="467" src="//lh5.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmRH8tLqI/AAAAAAAAGVM/OCB2TSjmVcQ/WB%20014%20copy_thumb%5B1%5D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="WB 014 copy" width="512"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
          Next, I place a thin cloth over the ribbon and the image and ironed according to the package instructions. After it has cooled for a few minutes carefully remove the plastic covering and voila
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <a href="http://lh4.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmR7mtBfI/AAAAAAAAGVQ/NXuAIvpe1Y0/s1600-h/WB%20049%20copy%5B7%5D.jpg">
          <img alt="WB 049 copy" border="0" height="344" src="//lh5.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmScIEAVI/AAAAAAAAGVU/oLLwkCj9Lm0/WB%20049%20copy_thumb%5B1%5D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="WB 049 copy" width="516"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
          the heat transfer material in now firmly affixed to the jute ribbon. To finish the project I added vinyl cut with the Silhouette using the same font to the wooden hanger
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <a href="http://lh4.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmS4qRMpI/AAAAAAAAGVY/loW2B7QDzqs/s1600-h/WB%20033%20copy%5B3%5D.jpg">
          <img alt="WB 033 copy" border="0" height="344" src="//lh5.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmTKhlZ9I/AAAAAAAAGVc/FDJF6DPl4Fc/WB%20033%20copy_thumb.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="WB 033 copy" width="516"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
           now isnt that cheerier than a chore chart? There is a need to do side and a finished side to the ribbon with all of the jobs on labeled clothes pins. Place the clothes pins on the appropriate side of the ribbon with a reward section an the bottom to track their rewards.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <a href="http://lh5.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmTugTOpI/AAAAAAAAGVg/WyYVRemxETI/s1600-h/WB%20053%20copy%5B3%5D.jpg">
          <img alt="WB 053 copy" border="0" height="344" src="//lh6.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmUOjipCI/AAAAAAAAGVk/FkqtfLZ1z1g/WB%20053%20copy_thumb.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="WB 053 copy" width="516"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
          Hang from a convenient door and you and your kiddos have a fun way to track what they have accomplished for the day.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmUW65eoI/AAAAAAAAGVo/T8PaTfwtbyg/s1600-h/WB%20White%20copy%5B5%5D.jpg">
          <img alt="WB White copy" border="0" height="773" src="//lh4.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmU0MJa_I/AAAAAAAAGVs/FeLeB8iiSxw/WB%20White%20copy_thumb%5B2%5D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="WB White copy" width="514"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <font face="Century Gothic" size="2">
          Come on over to WhipperBerry we have a few more Silhouette inspired project that we think are quite dandy. Thanks Silhouette for this fun opportunity to share our Silhouette love with your fans!!
         </font>
        </p>
        <p align="justify">
         <a href="http://lh5.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmVDk141I/AAAAAAAAGVw/aHNcxCdGras/s1600-h/Signature_edited-1%5B4%5D.jpg">
          <img alt="Signature_edited-1" border="0" height="72" src="//lh3.ggpht.com/_-fnKHrxIOI8/TcsmVr89vuI/AAAAAAAAGV0/nPFSO7Srobc/Signature_edited-1_thumb%5B1%5D.jpg?imgmax=800" style="background-image: none; border-right-width: 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; display: inline; border-top-width: 0px; border-bottom-width: 0px; border-left-width: 0px; padding-top: 0px" title="Signature_edited-1" width="129"/>
         </a>
        </p>
