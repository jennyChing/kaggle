       <h1 class="entry-title">
        The Restoration Hardware look for LESS!
       </h1>
       <div class="post-info">
        <span class="date published time" title="2015-01-20T06:00:55+00:00">
         January 20, 2015
        </span>
        By
        <span class="author vcard">
         <span class="fn">
          <a href="http://www.prettyprovidence.com/author/kelsey/" rel="author" title="Kelsey">
           Kelsey
          </a>
         </span>
        </span>
        <span class="post-comments">
         <a href="http://www.prettyprovidence.com/restoration-hardware-look-less/#comments">
          <span class="dsq-postid" data-dsqidentifier="7671 http://prettyprovidence.com/?p=7671">
           8 Comments
          </span>
         </a>
        </span>
       </div>
       <div class="entry-content">
        <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
        <p>
         <a href="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_0096.jpg">
          <img alt="Okay this is AMAZING! I always wish I have the money to shop at places like Restoration Hardware, and now I know I don't even need to! SO great." class="alignnone wp-image-7681" height="585" src="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/IMG_0096.jpg" width="585"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         Hey guys! I am so excited to be back again talking interior design. Jess and Sar are all about saving money, and lets be honest, who isnt!? Today I want to show you how you can save money when designing your home, without sacrificing style. With a little research, you can often find much less expensive versions ofname-brand pieces you love. Todays post is all about getting a designer look for less. I picked one of my favorite stores, Restoration Hardware, and selected three pieces I have been swooning over. I then set off to find MUCH more affordable alternatives for you. These are so similar, its crazy! (For more awesome tips and tricks, follow along with me on
         <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/wellscollection/" target="_blank">
          Pinterest
         </a>
         and
         <a href="http://instagram.com/thewellscollection" target="_blank">
          Instagram
         </a>
         )
        </p>
        <p>
         1. I love this
         <a href="http://api.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=422175816&amp;pid=uid625-6589225-11" target="_blank">
          classic, winged, deep tufted linen headboard
         </a>
         . These are so romantic! The straight lines and wings add a masculinity that balances the soft texture perfectly.
         <span id="more-7671">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-10.23.53-PM.png">
          <img alt="Screen Shot 2015-01-19 at 10.23.53 PM" class="alignnone wp-image-7674" height="369" src="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-10.23.53-PM.png" width="585"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         What I dont love as much is the price tag. For a queen in the tan linen, the cost is $3,095.
        </p>
        <p>
         As an alternative I bring you
         <a href="http://api.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.overstock.com%2FHome-Garden%2FINSPIRE-Q-Naples-Wingback-Button-Tufted-Upholstered-Queen-Sized-Headboard%2F9477539%.h 2Fproduct tml&amp;pid=uid625-6589225-11" target="_blank">
          this baby
         </a>
         , which you can get in the beige linen for $374. Thats literally one TENTH of the price.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-10.01.28-PM.png">
          <img alt="Screen Shot 2015-01-19 at 10.01.28 PM" class="aligncenter wp-image-7673" height="566" src="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-10.01.28-PM.png" width="585"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
        </p>
        <p>
         2. I have been crushing hard on cowhide rugs lately. Not the cowboy style, but the patchwork kind. You can buy this
         <a href="http://api.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?id=448655723&amp;pid=uid625-6589225-11" target="_blank">
          gorgeous herringbone pattern one
         </a>
         in an 810 at restoration hardware for $2,635-
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-10.53.52-PM.png">
          <img alt="Screen Shot 2015-01-19 at 10.53.52 PM" class="alignnone wp-image-7675" height="729" src="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-10.53.52-PM.png" width="585"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         OR you can purchase
         <a href="http://www.rugsusa.com/rugsusa/rugs/rugs-usa-chevron/natural/200QALUM18A-76096.html" target="_blank">
          this one
         </a>
         at rugsusa.com for $733. Both hand tufted. Both %100 real cowhide.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-10.56.10-PM.png">
          <img alt="Screen Shot 2015-01-19 at 10.56.10 PM" class="aligncenter wp-image-7676" height="856" src="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-10.56.10-PM.png" width="585"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         3. Last but not least I bring you one of my
         <a href="http://www.restorationhardware.com/catalog/product/product.jsp?productId=prod691015&amp;categoryId=cat1701016%20 Screen Shot 2015-01-19 at 11.04.31 PM" target="_blank">
          very favorite chandeliers
         </a>
         . Restoration Hardwares version is a pretty penny at $2295.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-11.04.31-PM.png">
          <img alt="Screen Shot 2015-01-19 at 11.04.31 PM" class="alignnone wp-image-7677" height="569" src="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-11.04.31-PM.png" width="585"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://api.shopstyle.com/action/apiVisitRetailer?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.overstock.com%2FHome-Garden%2FEdwards-Antique-Bronze-32-inch-Chandelier%2F9478143%2Fproduct.html&amp;pid=uid625-6589225-11" target="_blank">
          This one
         </a>
         from Overstock.com is the exact same size (32 diameter), and will cost you $350.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-11.08.02-PM.png">
          <img alt="Screen Shot 2015-01-19 at 11.08.02 PM" class="aligncenter wp-image-7678" height="661" src="http://prettyprovidence.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/01/Screen-Shot-2015-01-19-at-11.08.02-PM.png" width="585"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <p>
         There you have it! to sum it up, you could purchase these three items from Restoration Hardware for $8,095, OR go with the alternatives for a total of $1,457. That brings you a savings of 82%. THAT is getting the Restoration Hardware look for less!
        </p>
        <p>
         Thanks for reading! Come follow along over at
         <a href="http://thewellscollection.com" target="_blank">
          The Wells Collection
         </a>
         for more design tips and tricks.
        </p>
        <p>
         Happy bargain hunting!
        </p>
        <p>
         Love,
        </p>
        <p>
         Kelsey
