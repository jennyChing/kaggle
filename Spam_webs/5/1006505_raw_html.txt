          <p style="text-align: center;">
           Theres no better feeling in a fashionistas mind than slipping into that pair of boots youve been just itching to bring out of storage for the fall time. With the fall season being in full swing, now is the perfect time to stock up on the hottest boots for the season. Need some help finding your perfect pair? Weve got the inside scoop on whats blazing hot this fall. Read on to see which made our list!
          </p>
          <p style="text-align: center;">
          </p>
          <div class="ezcol ezcol-one-half">
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: 18px;">
             <b>
              Over the Knee
             </b>
            </span>
           </span>
           <p style="text-align: left;">
            <a href="http://www.modvive.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/overknee.png">
             <img alt="over knee" class=" wp-image-25040 alignleft" height="300" src="http://www.modvive.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/overknee-198x300.png" width="160"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Whether it has a heel or not is up to you, but regardless, one thing is for sure: every girl needs at least one pair of over the knee boots. We love the classic, sexy look of a black leather over the knee boot, which is why the 
            <a href="http://shop.nordstrom.com/s/chinese-laundry-south-bay-over-the-knee-boot/3291333?cm_cat=datafeed&amp;cm_ite=chinese_laundry_'south_bay'_over_the_knee_boot:544408&amp;cm_pla=shoes:women:boots&amp;cm_ven=Google_Product_Ads&amp;mr:referralID=8ff67319-3692-11e3-a8bc-001b2166c62d " target="_blank">
             South Bay pair from Chinese Laundry
            </a>
            is high on our list of must-haves. Youll get a ton of wear out of these beauties during the fall time, as they can be worn with sweater dresses, leggings, and even a skirt with some tights. With their ability to instantly amp up an outfit, over the boots add a layer of sex appeal to your favorite fall ensemble.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="ezcol ezcol-one-half ezcol-last">
           <span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: 18px;">
            <b>
             <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
              Knee-Length
             </span>
            </b>
           </span>
           <p style="text-align: right;">
            <a href="http://www.modvive.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/kneelentgh.png">
             <img alt="knee length" class=" wp-image-25041 alignright" height="300" src="http://www.modvive.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/kneelentgh-193x300.png" width="160"/>
            </a>
           </p>
           <p>
            Knee length boots in a riding boot style is perfect for fall, as it exudes an air of playfulness while still maintaining functionality. The
            <a href="http://us.asos.com/ASOS-CASPER-Knee-High-Boots/ys30u/?iid=2251365&amp;cid=4172&amp;sh=0&amp;pge=0&amp;pgesize=9999&amp;sort=-1&amp;clr=Darkbrown+brown&amp;mporgp=L0FTT1MvQVNPUy1DQVNQRVItS25lZS1IaWdoLUJvb3RzL1Byb2Qv&amp;utm_source=google_product_search&amp;utm_medium=paid&amp;utm_campaign=google_product_search&amp;WT.tsrc=Google%20Product%20Search&amp;WT.srch=1&amp;affid=2365&amp;WT.srch=1&amp;utm_source=google&amp;utm_medium=ppc&amp;utm_term=%7Badwords_producttargetid%7D&amp;utm_content=&amp;utm_campaign=&amp;cvosrc=ppc.google.%7Badwords_producttargetid%7D&amp;network=%7Bnetwork%7D&amp;mobile=%7Bifmobile:1%7D&amp;search=%7Bifsearch:1%7D&amp;content=%7Bifcontent:1%7D&amp;creative=%7Bcreative%7D&amp;ptid=%7Badwords_producttargetid%7D&amp;adposition=%7Badposition%7D&amp;gclid=CO_Xp8SBnLoCFUlBQgodRlQAIw " target="_blank">
             Casper Knee High Boots by ASOS
            </a>
            is a definite fall essential, as it features an almond shaped toe and is in a color that can be styled easily. Pair this pair with some comfortable leggings and a chunky knit sweater and youre ready to rock this season in style.
           </p>
           <p style="text-align: center;">
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="ezcol-divider">
          </div>
          <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
           <span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: 18px;">
            <b>
             Mid-Length
            </b>
           </span>
          </span>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.modvive.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/mid-knee.png">
            <img alt="mid knee" class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-25047" height="300" src="http://www.modvive.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/mid-knee-200x300.png" width="200"/>
           </a>
           A truly classic style, mid-length boots hit somewhere between the ankle and the knee for a sophisticated look. Since wearing calf-length boots (as its sometimes called) can run the risk of cutting off the leg line, opt for heeled ones that make you appear taller and leaner, like this pair of
           <a href="http://www.mytheresa.com/en-us/travis-studded-suede-boots.html" target="_blank">
            Jimmy Choos
           </a>
           . Giving meaning to the saying go big or go home, these strikingly eye-catching boots feature a suede material and studs all around, giving your look a touch of edginess thatll have you swimming in compliments.
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <div class="ezcol ezcol-one-half">
           <span style="text-decoration: underline; color: #000000;">
            <span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: 18px;">
             Ankle Boot
            </span>
           </span>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.modvive.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/ankle.png">
             <img alt="ankle" class="alignleft wp-image-25048" height="300" src="http://www.modvive.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/ankle-337x300.png" width="190"/>
            </a>
            Ah, the classic ankle boots. Loved for its versatility and adored for its model-like style, ankle boots are perfect for wearing during the fall time, as theyll keep your precious feet warm while still looking fashionable. When it comes to choosing the perfect pair, this
            <a href="http://new.shoebuy.com/boutique-9-isoke/657803/1346734?cm_mmc=googleproductads_pla-_-none-_-none-_-%7Bkeyword%7D" target="_blank">
             Boutique 9 Isoke in light brown leather
            </a>
            looks pretty promising. Its camel-colored hue makes it a prime candidate for both summer and fall wearing while its ultra-comfortable wedge will ensure you can wear it for hours without hurting your feet. Plus, its semi-pointed toe gives it a dash of sophistication and chicness!
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="ezcol ezcol-one-half ezcol-last">
           <span style="text-decoration: underline;">
            <span style="font-family: 'trebuchet ms', geneva; font-size: 18px;">
             Booties
            </span>
           </span>
           <p>
            <a href="http://www.modvive.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Booties.jpg">
             <img alt="Booties" class="alignright wp-image-25049" height="300" src="http://www.modvive.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Booties-300x300.jpg" width="198"/>
            </a>
            Some people have a love-hate relationship with booties. Our take on this ultra-modern boot style? Its a true must-have! Booties look good with virtually anything you pair it with, from denim jeans to skirts with tights. Of course, no boot list would be complete without some fur. These
            <a href="http://www.barneys.com/on/demandware.store/Sites-BNY-Site/default/Product-Show?pid=502166363&amp;utm_source=J84DHJLQkR4&amp;utm_medium=affiliate&amp;siteID=J84DHJLQkR4-UFjgLCok5KxYNBL4AXaRSw " target="_blank">
             Nicholas Kirkwood Scalloped Ankle Boot
            </a>
            feature pony hair and a pointed toe for an ultra-glamorous, classy look. Its black and white contrast makes it easy to style, too.
           </p>
          </div>
          <div class="ezcol-divider">
          </div>
          <p>
           Whether youre a flats girl or a heels girl, boots are a vital part of your fall wardrobe. With their ability to be paired with anything from jeans and a tee to a more formal attire, its a good idea to keep boots in every length on hand for those fashion emergencies (trust us, theyll come in handy!)
          </p>
