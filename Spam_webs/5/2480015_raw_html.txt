        <h2 class="purple" itemprop="name">
         How To Make A Kiss Me Quick Juice
        </h2>
        <p class="wrapped_description" id="web_text_description" itemprop="description">
         Kiss Me Quick Juice. Ranish Jansari makes a Kiss Me Quick juice at Fushi in London. It's an anti-oxidant drink rich in Vitamin C. Experience our Kiss Me Quick Juice.
        </p>
        <meta content="PT2M16.005S" itemprop="duration"/>
        <div class="web-text" itemprop="transcript" role="article">
         <h3>
          Step 1: You will need
         </h3>
         <div id="resource_items">
          <ul>
           <li>
            <span>
             1 chopping board
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span>
             1 knife
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span>
             1 juicer
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span>
             1 spoon
            </span>
           </li>
           <li>
            <span>
             1 blender
            </span>
           </li>
          </ul>
         </div>
         <h3>
          Step 2: Prepare fruit
         </h3>
         <p>
          Peel and slice 3 oranges.  Chop the pear into chunks small enough to fit into your juicer.  Cut the passion fruit in half, and chop up 3 slices of pineapple.
         </p>
         <h3>
          Step 3: Juice
         </h3>
         <p>
          Add the 3 oranges and one pear to the juicer and juice.  Pour into the blender.
         </p>
         <h3>
          Step 4: Blend
         </h3>
         <p>
          Now scoop out half a passion fruit into the blender, and add 3 slices of pineapple. Blend on a medium speed for 20 seconds.
         </p>
         <h3>
          Step 5: Special ingredient
         </h3>
         <p>
          If you like, try adding a dash of a defence tincture blend to the glass before you pour.  This one contains astragals, echinacea, cleavers, licorice, siberian ginseng, ashwaganda, and ginger. This combination will support and strengthen your immune system.
         </p>
         <h3>
          Step 6: Pour
         </h3>
         <p>
          Pour into the glass, and serve.
         </p>
