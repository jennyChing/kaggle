          <p>
           <a href="http://www.beauty101blog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/shake-up-summer-.jpg">
            <img alt="Stuck in a rut and itching to break out? This post has 3 easy ways to shake up your summer and step out of your comfort zone! #ad " class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3366" height="1160" src="http://www.beauty101blog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/shake-up-summer-.jpg" width="660"/>

          </p>
          <p>
           Do you ever feel like youre stuck in a rut? I am a total creature of habit and Im tired of it! I think that Ive been playing it too safe for TOO LONG so Ive decided to shake things up this summer and its oddly thrilling. Im not brave enough to do anything super drastic like shave my head or bungee jump but Im a firm believer that even small lifestyle changes will pay off and give me a new perspective on things.
          </p>
          <p>
           Are you curious about how you can shake things up this summer and step out of your comfort zone? Check out these ideas that Ive been incorporating into my routine:
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.beauty101blog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/shake-up-your-summer-2.jpg">
            <img alt="shake up your summer 2" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3360" height="682" src="http://www.beauty101blog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/shake-up-your-summer-2.jpg" width="459"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Go To The Gym:
           </strong>
           I just recently started a new job that comes with some pretty fantastic perks, one of which being a membership to a really great gym here in New York City. The old Casey would be intimidated and nervous by going to the gym but Im making an effort to step out of my comfort zone and have been going regularly. I love it and Im feeling so much healthier! You know whats crazy? I had to buy a new pair of shoes for my workouts and I realized that I havent owned sneakers in over two years. Who does that?! Clearly I need to workout if its been that long, haha.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.beauty101blog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/shake-up-your-summer-3.jpg">
            <img alt="shake up your summer 3" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3361" height="440" src="http://www.beauty101blog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/shake-up-your-summer-3.jpg" width="660"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Try New Makeup Colors:
           </strong>
           Ive started to feel like Im in a makeup rut (neutral smoky eye, cat eye liner, nude lips) so in an attempt to shake things up Ive been incorporating new colors into my routine. Im NOTORIOUS for
           <span style="text-decoration: line-through;">
            hoarding
           </span>
           buying lip products only to continue to reach for the same 10 colors over and over again. Not cool! Every day Im making an effort to wear a color that Ive been neglecting and I have to say, Im definitely discovering some new favorites!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.beauty101blog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/shake-up-your-summer-1.jpg">
            <img alt="shake up your summer 1" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3359" height="440" src="http://www.beauty101blog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/shake-up-your-summer-1.jpg" width="660"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Order Something New:
           </strong>
           For the last almost TWO YEARS Ive ordered the same exact thing from Starbucks every single time I go (which is easily 5x a week): a Venti Very Berry Hibiscus Refresher. In an attempt to change my routine Ive started to order the
           <a href="http://pubads.g.doubleclick.net/gampad/clk?id=122332443&amp;iu=/5485/sugar.pop/track" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            TeavanaShaken Iced Peach Green Tea Lemonade
           </a>
           and I am completely obsessed! I actually fell in love with this drink last summer and anxiously awaited the day that it was re-released this year because it is phenomenal and insanely addicting. Maybe its the way the baristas ever-so-precicely shake the loose Teavana tea 10 times to mix and unlock the flavor or maybe its the fact that I consider peach to be one of the most refreshing flavors of all time, but whatever it is this tea is a welcomed addition to my summer routine. Good thing I work around the corner from Starbucks (PS  its treat receipt season. Im sorry + youre welcome) because I find myself craving this drinkregularly. You wouldnt think that something as simple as a Starbucks order could change things but for this creature of habit it works!
          </p>
          <p>
           If
           <a href="http://pubads.g.doubleclick.net/gampad/clk?id=122332443&amp;iu=/5485/sugar.pop/track" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            TeavanaShaken Iced Peach Green Tea Lemonade
           </a>
           isnt your cup of tea
           <span style="text-decoration: line-through;">
            we cant be friends
           </span>
           no worries! Starbucks also has a few other versions of their shaken tea drinks that are super delicious. Check out theTeavanaShaken Iced Blackberry Mojito Tea Lemonade or theTeavanaShaken Iced Black Tea Lemonade instead!
          </p>
          <p>
           Speaking of shaking up your summer, check out
           <a href="http://www.pinterest.com/beauty101blog/summer-shakeup-inspiration-sponsored/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">
            my newest Pinterest board
           </a>
           where Im sharing my inspiration and ideas for ways to shake up my summer and step out of my comfort zone!
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.beauty101blog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/summer-shakeup-pinterest.jpg">
            <img alt="summer shakeup pinterest" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-3362" height="351" src="http://www.beauty101blog.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/summer-shakeup-pinterest.jpg" width="660"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           How are you shaking up your summer? Have you incorporated any new habits into your routine? Comment below and let me know!
          </p>
