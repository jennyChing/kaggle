      </a>
     </h2>
     <small>
      <a href="http://antoniab.com/2009/11/opi-holiday-glow/">
       November 2, 2009
      </a>
      by Toni |

      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_654" style="width: 310px">
       <a href="http://antoniab.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/OPI-Holiday-Glow.JPG">
        <img alt="OPI Holiday Glow" class="size-medium wp-image-654" height="225" src="http://antoniab.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/OPI-Holiday-Glow-300x225.jpg" title="OPI Holiday Glow" width="300"/>

       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        OPI Holiday Glow
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       Goes on beautifully, of course. Takes 3 coats, definitely, to bring out the Glow in this holiday polish. Holiday Glow is a brown with small golden glitter particles that you can feela little rough like sandpaper not chunky, though. Once the topcoat is on, it feels smooth to me. Looks like embers in a fire. It is a little plummy, but definitely more brown to me. Ive seen other pictures in which this polish looks so incredible, and Im not sure if I got a bum bottle or what, but it definitely looks better in other peoples pictures.
      </p>

      </a>

     <div class="entry">
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_610" style="width: 310px">
       <a href="http://antoniab.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/Poshe-Seche-Vite-Matte-About-you-topcoats1.JPG">
        <img alt="Poshe, Seche Vite, Matte About You topcoats" class="size-medium wp-image-610" height="225" src="http://antoniab.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/Poshe-Seche-Vite-Matte-About-you-topcoats1-300x225.jpg" title="Poshe, Seche Vite, Matte About You topcoats" width="300"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Poshe, Seche Vite, Matte About You topcoats
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       Your topcoat protects your nails by locking out moisture. You need topcoats also for shine and protection for your nails and polish.Seche Vite and Poshe are both available at Sallys. Essie Matte About You is for a matte topcoat, making your polish matte. It works great. All of these dry fast. SV is a little on the thicker side, while Poshe is a thinner formula, but they dry equally fast.
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_608" style="width: 310px">
       <a href="http://antoniab.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/Nail-Tek-II-Intensive-Therapy.JPG">
        <img alt="Nail Tek II Intensive Therapy Basecoat" class="size-medium wp-image-608" height="225" src="http://antoniab.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/Nail-Tek-II-Intensive-Therapy-300x225.jpg" title="Nail Tek II Intensive Therapy Basecoat" width="300"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        Nail Tek II Intensive Therapy Basecoat
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       Nail Tek II is great for weak or peeling nails. I got mine at a small beauty supply shop. I use this under any basecoat I apply or as a clear coat. I believe the best way to use it is to leave nails unpolished, using only this polish for a week consecutively not removing the layer from the day before. Then take it all off and start over at the end of the week. Presumably, the more you use it the better it works.
      </p>
      <div class="wp-caption alignnone" id="attachment_605" style="width: 310px">
       <a href="http://antoniab.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/basecoats-CC-ChG.JPG">
        <img alt="CC and ChG Basecoats" class="size-medium wp-image-605" height="225" src="http://antoniab.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/10/basecoats-CC-ChG-300x225.jpg" title="CC and ChG Basecoats" width="300"/>
       </a>
       <p class="wp-caption-text">
        CC and ChG Basecoats
       </p>
      </div>
      <p>
       There are lots of basecoats out there, these are two of my favorite. The milky white is nice for french nails or a natural look, especially when your nails are stained like mine. Its a nice milky white that is still clear somehow! Im wondering if it might be good for syrup manis? Anyway. China Glaze Strong Adhesion is what I use when Im going to wear any color. It really helps the polish to hold on and Ive yet to get a chip when using this base. The base also locks moisture out, so its really important to anyone taking care of their nails.
      </p>
     </div>
