      <h2 class="posttitle">
       Kale Egg Salad Sandwiches
      </h2>
      <input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"/>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kale-egg-salad-sandwich-3.jpg">
        <img alt="Recipe - Kale Egg Salad Sandwiches #GreatDay" class="aligncenter wp-image-23627 size-full" height="717" src="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kale-egg-salad-sandwich-3.jpg" width="500"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       If you are looking for an easy recipe this summer, look no further than to this
       <a href="http://www.greatdayfarms.com/recipes/216/kale-egg-salad-sandwiches" target="_blank">
        Kale Egg Salad Sandwich
       </a>
       . These sandwiches are perfect for lunch or dinner, you choose!
      </p>
      <p>
       We had them for both at our house. I am sharing this easy recipe over on Great Day Farms. These are absolutely perfect paired with garden produce.
      </p>
      <p>
       <a href="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kale-egg-salad-sandwich-1.jpg">
        <img alt="Recipe - Kale Egg Salad Sandwiches #GreatDay" class="aligncenter wp-image-23628 size-full" height="403" src="http://www.mommyhatescooking.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/kale-egg-salad-sandwich-1.jpg" width="600"/>
       </a>
      </p>
      <p>
       I can hardly look at the screen now without craving one of these for lunch today. Be sure to head over to Great Day Farms for the
       <a href="http://www.greatdayfarms.com/recipes/216/kale-egg-salad-sandwiches" target="_blank">
        complete recipe
       </a>
       for
       <a href="http://www.greatdayfarms.com/recipes/216/kale-egg-salad-sandwiches" target="_blank">
        Kale Egg Salad Sandwiches
       </a>
       !
      </p>
      <p>
       Happy Egg Eating Day!
