      </h2>
      <h4 class="text-center">

         <h2 class="black">
          Unify disparate data into one centralized place
         </h2>
         <p>
          Skip the data preparation nightmare. Collecting and preparing your data is the starting point for all that is business intelligence, and a lot of effort is spent just on that. Sisense provides the end-to-end
          <a href="/technology/">
           technology
          </a>
          that allows non-techies to easily join large data sets from multiple sources, and create one neat and centralized repository of all datawith no scripting, no expensive hardware, and little to no help from IT. Say goodbye to the tedious work of standardizing data before you can even start to analyze it, and head straight to visualization and insights.
         </p>
        </div>
        <div class="span6 image">

         </h2>

          Use
          <a href="/connectors/" target="_blank" title="Built-in Connectors to Join Multiple Data Sources and Formats:">
           built-in connectors
          </a>
          to join multiple data sources and formats including: Excel files, Google Adwords and Analytics, information from a CRM, and all other organizational databases. Sisense even provides built-in integration with Cloud applications like Zendesk and Salesforce. While other BI tools require an IT department to prepare data by programming sources to speak the same language, Sisense is the only
          <a href="/" target="_blank">
           business intelligence tool
          </a>
          on the market that lets business users drag-and-drop data to easily combine large data sets.

Sisense ensures the numbers you get are 100% accurate by consolidating data correctly and taking all of your data into account, no matter how large and from all disparate sources. Everyone in your organization can then analyze the same numbers and rely on a single source of truth. Rest assured the trends you harvest are high-quality, and arm decision-makers with the most accurate information.
