         <h2>
          Refreshing Summer Bite
         </h2>
         <div style="color: #000000;">
          <p>
           We are fully submerged in the Summers heat here in South Florida! Once this heat sets in on us we go straight torefreshing summer bite.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div style="color: #000000;">
          <p>
           Watermelon Pie hits the spot!!
          </p>
         </div>
         <div style="color: #000000;">
          <p>
           On a beach date the other day I was told that we had an abundance of Watermelon. Watermelons are on sale here $3.99 for a whole Seedless Watermelon! You cant go wrong. (
           <em>
            except if there are only 2 people in your house like us
           </em>
           ). I needed something new to do with all of this watermelon.
          </p>
         </div>
         <div style="color: #000000;">
          <p>
           I made a wonderful Watermelon Smoothie that smarted me with *brain freeze*. This discouraged me from doing it again.
           <img alt=":)" class="wp-smiley" src="http://www.foodisloverecipes.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/simple-smile.png" style="height: 1em; max-height: 1em;"/>
           But the flavor of the smoothie was sooo good! I needed another way to do it without the *pain*.
          </p>
          <p>
           A walk through my local market brought me the 
           <i>
            lightbulb
           </i>
            moment!
          </p>
         </div>
         <div style="color: #000000;">
          <p>
           <b>
            <i>
             <span style="font-size: x-large;">
              Watermelon Pie
             </span>
            </i>
           </b>
          </p>
         </div>
         <div style="color: #000000;">
          <p>
           <a href="http://www.foodisloverecipes.com/recipe-items/watermelon-pie/watermelon-pie-b/" rel="attachment wp-att-3316">
            <img alt="Refreshing Summer Bite - Watermelon Pie" class="alignnone size-large wp-image-3316" height="550" src="http://www.foodisloverecipes.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/06/Watermelon-Pie-B-412x550.jpg" width="412"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Check out the Recipe for yourself! It will be a summer staple for sure!
          </p>
