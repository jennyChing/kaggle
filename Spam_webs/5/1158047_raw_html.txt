         <p>
          This week I've been experimenting with baking with yellow squash and I'm SO happy with the results! I've made some delicious baked goodies and treats that have been a huge hit with my little family. And the best part is, you really don't even know that they yellow squash is in the recipe. It's like a veggie bonus!
         </p>
         <p>
          This Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit recipe is one that has gone over really well. These make an excellent side dish for grilled meats or even a great side for a garden fresh chef salad. Or you can just munch on them for a late night snack if you have a cheese craving! No matter how to decide to enjoy these I hope you do it soon!
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40565" data-lazy-original="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0909.jpg" height="372" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="560"/>
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40565" height="372" src="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0909.jpg" width="560"/>
         </noscript>
         <h2>
          Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe
         </h2>
         <p>
          <strong>
           What You Need:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <ul>
          <li>
           2 cups Yellow Squash, shredded
          </li>
          <li>
           1 teaspoon Salt
          </li>
          <li>
           3 cups Flour (you can use
           <a href="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/gluten-free-all-purpose-flour.html" target="_blank" title="Gluten Free All Purpose Flour">
            gluten free flour
           </a>
           )
          </li>
          <li>
           1 Tablespoon Baking Powder
          </li>
          <li>
           2 teaspoons Baking Soda
          </li>
          <li>
           1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder
          </li>
          <li>
           4 Tablespoons Butter, melted
          </li>
          <li>
           1 cup Cheddar Cheese, shredded
          </li>
          <li>
           3/4 cup Milk
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40560" data-lazy-original="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0902.jpg" height="372" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="560"/>
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40560" height="372" src="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0902.jpg" width="560"/>
         </noscript>
         <p>
          <strong>
           What To Do:
          </strong>
         </p>
         <p>
          Start by gathering ingredients. I used fresh yellow squash from the garden but if you want to make this in the winter you can use
          <a href="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/zucchini-zucchini-and-more-zucchini.html" target="_blank" title="Zucchini, Zucchini, and More Zucchini">
           frozen, shredded squash
          </a>
          . You could also use zucchini if you don't have yellow squash.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40561" data-lazy-original="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0903.jpg" height="600" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="399"/>
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40561" height="600" src="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0903.jpg" width="399"/>
         </noscript>
         <p>
          Mix together all the dry ingredients. Add in the shredded squash and stir until coated with the dry ingredients.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40562" data-lazy-original="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0904.jpg" height="600" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="399"/>
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40562" height="600" src="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0904.jpg" width="399"/>
         </noscript>
         <p>
          Add in the remaining ingredients and mix together well. I used my hands to make sure I didn't leave any clumps in the dough.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40563" data-lazy-original="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0905.jpg" height="600" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="399"/>
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40563" height="600" src="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0905.jpg" width="399"/>
         </noscript>
         <p>
          Drop with a large cookie scoop into a lightly greased 9x13 baking dish or two 8x8 baking dishes. You should place the biscuits fairly close together.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40564" data-lazy-original="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0906.jpg" height="600" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="399"/>
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40564" height="600" src="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0906.jpg" width="399"/>
         </noscript>
         <p>
          Stick the biscuits in the oven at 400 degrees for about 15 minutes (20 minutes for gluten free). The biscuits will be brown on the top.
         </p>
         <p>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40566" data-lazy-original="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0911.jpg" height="600" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAAAAACH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAICRAEAOw==" width="399"/>
         </p>
         <noscript>
          <img alt="Yellow Squash Cheddar Biscuit Recipe" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-40566" height="600" src="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DSC_0911.jpg" width="399"/>
         </noscript>
         <p>
          Enjoy with a nice slice of butter spread over the top. YUM!
         </p>
         <p>
          If you are looking for other biscuit recipes you might want to check out these
          <a href="http://www.littlehouseliving.com/cream-biscuits.html" target="_blank" title="Easy Cream Biscuit Recipe">
           Cream Biscuits

          or these

           Baking Powder Biscuits
          </a>
          . Both are great!
         </p>
