        <article class="et_pb_post post-4162 post type-post status-publish format-standard has-post-thumbnail hentry category-entertainment tag-funny tag-road-signs tag-street-sign tag-warning" id="post-4162">
         <h1>
          Top 10 Funny Road Signs
         </h1>
         <p class="post-meta">
          by
          <a href="http://allyouneedislists.com/author/guest-author/" rel="author" title="Posts by List Contributor">
           List Contributor
          </a>
          | Sep 9, 2011 |
          <a href="http://allyouneedislists.com/category/entertainment/" rel="category tag">
           Entertainment
          </a>
          |
          <span class="comments-number">
           <a href="http://allyouneedislists.com/entertainment/top-10-funny-road-signs/#respond">
            0 comments
           </a>
          </span>
         </p>
         <div class="entry-content">
          <p>
           Sometimes you can be driving along, exploring somewhere new and just enjoying the journey when youll come across a road sign that doesnt make any sense at all, is open to wild misinterpretation, takes on an entirely new meaning thanks to careless placement or for some other unexpected reason is simply laugh-out-loud funny. Dont believe us? Well, heres our pick of the top 10 funny road signs that are out there to confuse and amuse you.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            <span class="drop-cap">
             10
            </span>
            Fasten Brastraps
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="funniest-road-signs-fasten-brastraps" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10323" height="350" src="http://allyouneedislists.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/funniest-road-signs-fasten-brastraps.jpg" width="488"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           On the road to the Kosi Bay Bush Camp, Mozambique, a sign advises motorists:
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <strong>
             WARNING: Fasten bra straps and remove dentures: very bumpy road!
            </strong>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            <span class="drop-cap">
             9
            </span>
            Ham Sandwich
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <img alt="funniest-road-signs-ham-sandwich-street" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-10324" height="435" src="http://allyouneedislists.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/funniest-road-signs-ham-sandwich-street-600x450.jpg" width="580"/>
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           The simplest things can make a road sign funny. English place names are a good source of the unintentional laugh, as demonstrated by the road sign in Kent, southeast England, which reads:
           <strong>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Ham  , Sandwich 3
            </strong>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            <span class="drop-cap">
             8
            </span>
            Road ends in water
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="funniest-road-signs-road-ends-in-water" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-10325" height="441" src="http://allyouneedislists.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/funniest-road-signs-road-ends-in-water-600x457.jpg" width="580"/>
          </p>
          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           Occasionally you know what the sign writer meant, but somehow it just didnt turn out that way. Take, for example, a road sign found in Pennsylvania, USA, which does not suggest that you need a submersible car, but actually leads to a boat launch:
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Road ends in water
            </strong>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            <span class="drop-cap">
             7
            </span>
            Hitchhikers may be escaping inmates
            <br/>
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10326" style="width: 590px">
           <img alt="flickr" class="size-large wp-image-10326" height="435" src="http://allyouneedislists.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/funniest-road-signs-hitchhikers-may-be-escaping-inmates-600x450.jpg" width="580"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/mikkorauhala/223848686/" target="_blank">
             flickr
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Some road signs have a serious message behind them, but lets be honest; would you really stop to pick up a hitchhiker in the vicinity of a prison in Texas or Oklahoma, USA? If you would, heed the ominous signs that say:
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Hitchhikers may be escaping inmates
            </strong>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            <span class="drop-cap">
             6
            </span>
            Hunting children
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="funniest-road-signs-slow-children-at-play-hunting-with-shotgun-only" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-10328" height="666" src="http://allyouneedislists.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/funniest-road-signs-slow-children-at-play-hunting-with-shotgun-only.jpg" width="549"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Mixed messages often arise as a result of two signs placed in close proximity. Just a little more thought might have prevented a few accidents in Maine, USA, where a post displays two signs. The upper sign says:
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <strong>
             SLOW: children at play
            </strong>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           Whilst the lower sign adds, rather disturbingly:
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <strong>
             Hunting with shotgun only
            </strong>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           <strong>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            <span class="drop-cap">
             5
            </span>
            Stop, wait 28 seconds
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10327" style="width: 543px">
           <img alt="railroadforums.com" class="size-full wp-image-10327" height="800" src="http://allyouneedislists.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/funniest-road-signs-stop-wait-28-seconds.jpg" width="533"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://www.railroadforums.com/photos/showphoto.php/photo/55374/title/stopwait-28-seconds-21/cat/500" target="_blank">
             railroadforums.com
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Some road signs seem simply baffling. What are the consequences of being one or two seconds out either way, when a sign in Anchorage, Alaska commands you to:
           <strong>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <strong>
             STOP Wait 28 seconds
            </strong>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           OK  we know its probably a timed traffic signal with a 28 second wait, but you can bet one or two motorists reach for their stopwatch
          </p>
          <h2>
           <strong>
            <span class="drop-cap">
             4
            </span>
            Road unsafe when underwater
           </strong>
          </h2>
          <p>
           <strong>
            <img alt="funniest-road-signs-road-unsafe-when-underwater" class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-10329" height="544" src="http://allyouneedislists.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/funniest-road-signs-road-unsafe-when-underwater-600x563.jpg" width="580"/>
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Road signs that unintentionally state the very obvious are just asking to be laughed at. A case in point being the Mississippi, USA, sign that cautions drivers:
           <strong>
            Road unsafe when underwater
           </strong>
          </p>
          <h2>
           <span class="drop-cap">
            3
           </span>
           Sand!
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10330" style="width: 483px">
           <img alt="allposters.com" class="size-full wp-image-10330" height="355" src="http://allyouneedislists.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/funniest-road-signs-warning-sand.jpg" width="473"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://www.allposters.com/-sp/Road-Sign-Warning-of-Sand-Swamopmund-Namibia-Africa-Posters_i3159559_.htm" target="_blank">
             allposters.com
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           And the same goes for the road sign helpfully posted at Swapokmund in the Namibian Desert, Africa, which warns simply:
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <strong>
             ! Sand
            </strong>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           Sand. In a desert. Who knew?
          </p>
          <h2>
           <span class="drop-cap">
            2
           </span>
           Bloody idiot
          </h2>
          <p>
           <img alt="funniest-road-signs-if-you-drink-then-drive-you're-a-bloody-idiot" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-10331" height="375" src="http://allyouneedislists.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/funniest-road-signs-if-you-drink-then-drive-youre-a-bloody-idiot.jpg" width="500"/>
          </p>
          <p>
           Australias Transport Accident Committee decided to take a direct route with its anti-drink driving road sign campaign. Whereas the UK opted for road signs bearing the slogan Dont drink and drive, Australia went with:
          </p>
          <blockquote>
           <p>
            <strong>
             If you drink, then drive, youre a bloody idiot.
            </strong>
           </p>
          </blockquote>
          <p>
           Its funny because its true.
          </p>
          <h2>
           <span class="drop-cap">
            1
           </span>
           Invisible cows
          </h2>
          <div class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_10332" style="width: 590px">
           <img alt="flickr" class="size-large wp-image-10332" height="385" src="http://allyouneedislists.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/funniest-road-signs-beware-of-invisible-cows-600x399.jpg" width="580"/>
           <p class="wp-caption-text">
            <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/arnog/65882724/" target="_blank">
             flickr
            </a>
           </p>
          </div>
          <p>
           Road signs relating to animals and wildlife are often funny both intentionally and unintentionally, but if there are any that will leave a driver doubting their own sanity, it is those in Mauna Kea, New Zealand, which begin:
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            Beware of invisible cows
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           Well you know what they mean!
          </p>
