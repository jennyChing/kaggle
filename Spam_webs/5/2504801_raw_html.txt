       <h1>
        10 Favorite Celebrity Vacation Spots
       </h1>
      </div>
      <div class="post-entry">
       <p>
        <strong>
         Celebrities live in the lap of luxury every day, but their vacation destinations are not always as exotic or surprising as you might think.
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        We will take you on a journey across the world, to some very famous but also some very secretive locations. Who knows, maybe next year you decide to have a vacation on the same place as your favorite Hollywood star.
        <strong>
         Dont believe us?
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Then check out this list weve put together of 10 favorite celebrity vacation spots.
       </p>
       <h3>
        1. Hawaii
       </h3>
       <p>
        While most Americans escape to Florida for some fun in the sun, celebrities like to jet off to Hawaii. Katy Perry loves Kuaui, Kimye cant get enough of Honolulu, and Britney Spears, Will Smith and Cameron Diaz all know how to hula.
       </p>
       <p>
        This almost magical place has always been a celeb hot spot, but lately it started to lose its clientle due to more exotic offers from far east. Still, if you want to visit places where some of the biggest stars have vacationed, Hawaii is the place to be!
       </p>
       <h3>
        2. Tokyo
       </h3>
       <p>
        Japan is a must-stop spot for celebs promoting films, and many stars have fallen in love with the island nation. Lady Gaga and Jessica Alba have both visited the Tokyo Disneyland Park, and we are sure that they had a great time.
       </p>
       <p>
        If youre looking for that weird place that will have something that cannot be found anywhere else, be sure to visit Tokyo.
       </p>
       <h2>
        3. Charleston, SC
       </h2>
       <div id="inarticle">
        <div>
         <noscript>
          <a href="http://media.fastclick.net/w/click.here?sid=89787&amp;m=6&amp;c=1" target="_blank">
           <img border="1" height="250" pagespeed_url_hash="2975164160" src="http://media.fastclick.net/w/get.media?sid=89787&amp;m=6&amp;tp=8&amp;d=s&amp;c=1&amp;vcm_acv=1.4" width="300"/>
          </a>
         </noscript>
         Surprisingly to many, Charleston, South Carolina, is consistently ranked one of Americas best cities for travelers. Maybe thats why both Taylor Swift and Ryan Reynolds have recently been seen strolling down King Street.
        </div>
       </div>
       <p>
        Just the mere thought that you could be just walking down the street and then see someone famous is exciting to most people. Plus Charleston is famous for its hospitality an great hotel infrastructure.
       </p>
       <h3>
        4. New Orleans, LA
       </h3>
       <p>
        Another southern city that celebrities love to vacation in is New Orleans, Louisiana. Nearly every celebrity you could name has stayed in the Windsor Court Hotel, and the Pitt-Jolies own a vacation home in the Big Easy.
       </p>
       <p>
        A town that every one of us should see,this living Sin City is something that you cannot miss, and when you add stars to that mix you just cant go wrong with New Orleans.
       </p>
       <h3>
        5. Punta de Mita, Mexico
       </h3>
       <p>
        Lady Gaga was the first famous vacationer to check out Punta de Mita, Mexico, in 2011. Since then, Kimye, Kirsten Dunst, and Charlie Sheen are just a few of the celebs that have followed her lead.
       </p>
       <p>
        Now were talking, this relatively secretive resort is pricey, but in return you get complete anonymity and beautiful beaches. And if you can fly there, well, we think that the decision has already been made about your next year vacation.
       </p>
       <h2>
        6. Toronto
       </h2>
       <div id="inarticle">
        <div>
         <noscript>
          <a href="http://media.fastclick.net/w/click.here?sid=89787&amp;m=6&amp;c=1" target="_blank">
           <img border="1" height="250" pagespeed_url_hash="2975164160" src="http://media.fastclick.net/w/get.media?sid=89787&amp;m=6&amp;tp=8&amp;d=s&amp;c=1&amp;vcm_acv=1.4" width="300"/>
          </a>
          With dozens of TV shows and movies filming in Toronto at any given time, its no surprise that the place is always crawling with visiting celebrities.
         </noscript>
        </div>
       </div>
       <p>
        Even if theyre not working on a project, many stars like to pop in to visit each other on the job, including Rachel McAdams and Ryan Gosling.
       </p>
       <p>
        Filled wiht historical monuments and great nightclubs, this Canadian party hub has drawn attention to itself in the late nineties. After that it was just a matter of time before its rise
       </p>
       <h3>
        7. Vancouver
       </h3>
       <p>
        Celebrities vacation in Vancouver for the same reasons as Toronto, and there are always productions filming in this lovely Canadian city.
       </p>
       <p>
        A bit more wild than Toronto, this ethnically very diverse city is the one you want to be if youre looking for that next big project.
       </p>
       <p>
        Final fact: Voted one of the top five cities in the world to live in. If this isnt enough for Hollywood stars then its time for us, regular folk, to take over this gem.
       </p>
       <h2>
        8. Bedford, New York
       </h2>
       <div id="inarticle">
        <div>
         <!-- BEGIN JS TAG -  ROS 300x250 &lt; - DO NOT MODIFY -->
         <!-- END TAG -->
         <noscript>
          <a href="http://media.fastclick.net/w/click.here?sid=89787&amp;m=6&amp;c=1" target="_blank">
           <img border="1" class="theiaPostSlider_nav _center _lower" height="250" pagespeed_url_hash="297516&lt;/noscript&gt;You wouldnt expect the tiny town of Bedford, New York, to be a celebrity vacation haven, but its a great escape for stars that need a break from NYCs madness.&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;In fact, Glenn Close, Martha Stewart and Blake Lively all own vacation homes in Bedford.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This time its more about the rest than the fun, so Bedford cannot be praised for its nature, architecture or history, but its definitely a place where Celebs like to crash.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;9. Cte dAzur, French Riviera&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;There has never been a shortage of famous figures strolling the French Rivieras beaches since Bridget Bardot put the region on the map back in the 1950s.Princess Diana and Dodi Fayed spent their last days together on his fathers yacht off Pampelonne Beach near Saint-Tropez.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;What to say about a place where ever since 18th century, wealthy people have vacationed. With crystal clear waters, exclusive parties and wild nightlife, Cte dAzur is a sure thing when it comes to celebrity vacation spots.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;10. Dubrovnik, Croatia&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;p&gt;Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes visited the island first, but Beyonce and Blue Ivy Carter were recently named honorary citizens of Dubrovnik, Croatia. How cool is that?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;This is also the location where most of the Game of Thrones series has been filmed. So whether you support the Starks or the Lannisters, Dubrovnik should be on your list.&lt;/p&gt;
