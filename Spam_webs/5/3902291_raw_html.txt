        <h1 class="entry-title">
         The Most Common Warning Signs Of Cheating
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://pleasurehertonight.com/the-most-common-warning-signs-of-cheating/" rel="bookmark" title="2:20 pm">
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2013-08-26T14:20:31+00:00" pubdate="">
           August 26, 2013
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://pleasurehertonight.com/author/olga/" rel="author" title="View all posts by Tom Peters">
            Tom Peters
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <a id="dd_start">
        </a>
        <p>
         <a href="http://pleasurehertonight.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/cheating.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignright size-full wp-image-827" height="261" src="http://pleasurehertonight.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/cheating.jpg" title="cheating" width="300"/>
         </a>
         There are no strict and definitive clues that can tell if your spouse or partner is cheating. However, noticing several of the common signs happening at the same time should be enough to stir your curiosity and make you become a little cautious. Although it is not healthy to doubt your partner, it will help for you to be on your toes if your guts tell you that something wrong is up.
         <span id="more-824">
         </span>
        </p>
        <p>
         If your instinct tells you that your partner might be seeing another, you might want to look into other possible signs. See if your husband or wife is showing some of these following signs.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Theyre Starting to Dress Up  Better Than Usual
        </h2>
        <p>
         If your partner dresses up better when they are scheduled to go out with you, then thats something you should appreciate. However, if there is a sudden change in styling and hygiene, even without an occasion to dress up for in the first place, you get the right to be wary. If the change doesnt happen along with an effort to be with you or please you, then he or she might be doing it for someone else. You can also watch out for a change in perfume or cologne. If they have been using the same scent for years and suddenly puts on another without saying anything about it, it must mean something that you dont want happening.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Always on the Guard of His or Her Phone and Social Media Accounts
        </h2>
        <p>
         If your partner suddenly wont let you touch their phone, turn it off or put it on silent mode when youre together, or take it wherever he goes, then it could be that they are hiding something. Same goes with their
         <a href="http://www.sexysocialmedia.com/cheating-online-inc/">
          social media accounts
         </a>
         . If they wont let you know of their new password, and the reason why they changed it, you can start putting the radar up and running.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Catching Secret Phone Conversations
        </h2>
        <p>
         You can imagine it: you walk into a room where your husband or wife is, and they suddenly drop the call, telling you its work-related. This ones a sure classic that you shouldnt miss or let pass.
        </p>
        <h2>
         He or She Starts Picking a Fight About Every Little Thing
        </h2>
        <p>
         If they begin arguing with you for no reasonable reason at all and blaming you for just about anything, they could be subconsciously covering something up. Furthermore, these fights will give them the excuse to stay away from you for quite some time, getting the chance to spend more time with the other man or woman.
        </p>
        <h2>
         Friends Acting Weird
        </h2>
        <p>
         If you have common friends and they suddenly act weird around you, then they might know something that you dont know. Whether theyre loyal to your partner enough to cover them up or they love you that much they dont want to hurt you, the bottom line is, they must be hiding something really fishy.
        </p>
        <h2>
         He or She Starts Accusing YOU of Sheating
        </h2>
        <p>
         This is probably the worst and most painful signal that your partner is cheating. If they begin accusing you of cheating when you are not actually doing it, it must be their guilty conscience speaking. Cheaters are often too sensitive and defensive and this can manifest through accusations and making hurtful remarks about you.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <a href="http://therapists.psychologytoday.com/rms/name/Savannah_Ellis_DBA,MBA,BBSc,MHC,Intern_Las+Vegas_Nevada_122021" target="_blank">

           affair recovery coaching
          </a>
          and sharing an advice for cheaters and their partners.
         </em>
        </p>
