          <p>
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.gamengadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Porsche-911-HLS-4.jpg">
            <img alt="Porsche 911 HLS (4)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11618" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.gamengadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Porsche-911-HLS-4.jpg?resize=620%2C413"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           The 911 HLS resurfaces after being forgotten for 40 years, It was found in a body shop where it was left for 40 years.
          </p>
          <p>
           The story of the forgotten Porsche began in 1964. Back then, this particularPorsche 911was happily running along in its standard configuration.Then, in 1966, it would be sent to the Automotive Engineering department at the University of Aachen.
          </p>
          <p>
           The mission: turn it into a racing coup with a folding roof. Thedesign study seems to take at least some inspiration from thePorsche 904race carand itsrear engine cover, which could be seen as a kind of retractable roof.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.gamengadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Porsche-911-HLS-2.jpg">
            <img alt="Porsche 911 HLS (2)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11620" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i1.wp.com/www.gamengadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Porsche-911-HLS-2.jpg?resize=620%2C413"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Technical drawings and a miniature model were devised before the in-house body department at the University of Aachen went to work on building a completely new body.To keep the weight low, many parts were fabricated in aluminium, including the characteristic rear louvres and the adjacent Targa rollover bar.
          </p>
          <p>
           The fact that this Porsche had genuine racing ambitions can also be seen from the arrangement of the instruments on the dashboard: they were subsequently added to the right-hand side.The highlight of the study was undoubtedly the hinged cockpit canopy, integrated in its closed state to flow in harmony with the body line.
          </p>
          <div class="ezAdsense adsense adsense-midtext" style="text-align:center;margin:12px;">
          </div>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.gamengadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Porsche-911-HLS-1.jpg">
            <img alt="Porsche 911 HLS (1)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11619" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.gamengadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Porsche-911-HLS-1.jpg?resize=620%2C413"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           Finally, the concept car, complete with its retractable roof, was sent to a paint shop in Wrselen, a German town in the Aachen district of North Rhine-Westphalia, where it was given its bright green color. Here, too, the model name Porsche HLS was added in black letters.
          </p>
          <p>
           What happened next explains why the retractable roof Porsche 911 has, untilnow, generated not a single entry onGoogle: it simply disappeared.Presumably, the eccentric study found no favor at Zuffenhausen, so it was simply parked at the body shop and left therefor40 years.
          </p>
          <p>
           It was not until 911 expert Manfred Hering ofEarly 911swas asked whether he could restore the long-forgotten concept that it once again saw the light of day.
          </p>
          <p>
           <a href="http://i2.wp.com/www.gamengadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Porsche-911-HLS-3.jpg">
            <img alt="Porsche 911 HLS (3)" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-11617" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i2.wp.com/www.gamengadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/Porsche-911-HLS-3.jpg?resize=620%2C310"/>
           </a>
          </p>
          <p>
           I was asked whether I could restore the Porsche for around 30,000 euros.Of course, I had to say no, but I then tried to buy this unique Porsche, says Hering.After three years of discussions and negotiations I stood in front of the handsome wreck that had been left exposed to the weather for the last four decades.The original engineand a spare engine were dismantled but came with itand had already served as a home for several families of mice.
          </p>
          <p>
           Hering has definite plans for the carsfuture of the car: In mid-April 2015, I will present the retractable roof Porsche at the Techno Classic for the first time.Then we will study it more closely and attempt to restore as many original parts as possible.
          </p>
