        <h1 class="entry-title" itemprop="headline">
         What to say when asked the are you sleeping around question
        </h1>
        <p class="entry-meta">
         <time class="entry-time" datetime="2013-11-14T21:52:12+00:00" itemprop="datePublished">
          November 14, 2013
         </time>
         By
         <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Person">
          <a class="entry-author-link" href="http://www.letsleepingblogslie.net/author/admin/" itemprop="url" rel="author">
           <span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">
            Shaz Fisher
           </span>
          </a>
         </span>
         <span class="entry-comments-link">
          <a href="http://www.letsleepingblogslie.net/what-to-say-when-asked-the-are-you-sleeping-around-question/#respond">
           Leave a Comment
          </a>
         </span>
        </p>
       </header>
       <div class="entry-content" itemprop="text">
        <p>
         <font size="3">
          <a href="http://i0.wp.com/www.letsleepingblogslie.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Depositphotos_2652956_xs.jpg">
           <img align="right" alt="Telling lies" border="0" data-recalc-dims="1" src="http://i0.wp.com/www.letsleepingblogslie.net/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Depositphotos_2652956_xs_thumb.jpg?resize=390%2C278" style="border-top: 0px; border-right: 0px; background-image: none; border-bottom: 0px; float: right; padding-top: 0px; padding-left: 0px; margin: 0px 0px 7px; border-left: 0px; display: inline; padding-right: 0px" title="Telling lies"/>
          </a>
          The question itself is offensive. If a person was asked and they hadnt been  would they then not wonder why they were being asked? Some people say the person who asks is said to be the guilty one???
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font size="3">
          If the person has not been honest to date  why would they risk being honest when asked?
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font size="3">
          I think when the evidence is clear then people back down and realise they have to make a choice.
         </font>
         <font size="3">
          Marital affairs can be a hurdle people get over, however, it must start with honesty at some point. Not everyone believes it is a deal breaker and many marriages survive affairs.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font size="3">
          However, it is a gamble during the whole journey and the outcome is unknown to all parties. Nobody wants to think their devoted husband or wife is having an affair  so this is why everyone thinks it is happening to someone else.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font size="3">
          Unfortunately, marital affairs have been happening since the beginning of time and I cant see a time when it wont be happening. Nevertheless, we also cant go around not trusting everyone because some people do it; suspicion ruins marriages because of the lack of trust.
          <strong>
           The number one ideal is to trust yourself and then you will be able to trust your instinct.
          </strong>
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font color="#ff0000" size="3">
          <strong>
           Affairs affect so many people and in different ways:
          </strong>
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font size="3">
          1. If you see your friends husband with another woman and it looks intimate  what do you do?
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font size="3">
          2. You find out your Dad is having an affair? Who tells Mum?
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font size="3">
          3. Everyone has to live with secrets and lies.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font size="3">
          4. You watch a happy marriage fall apart.
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font size="3">
          5. After being caught; everyone wonders  will they do it again?
         </font>
        </p>
        <p>
         <font size="3">
          It is one of those situations that, until you are in it, it is very hard to know. Life deals us amazing hurdles and often when we least expect it.
         </font>
        </p>
