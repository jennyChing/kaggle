            <h1>
             Islamic State Spokesman Abu Mosa was reportedly killed by a Syrian airstrike.
            </h1>
            <p>
             <a href="http://www.funker530.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/twit.jpg">
              <img alt="" class="alignnone wp-image-5087" height="342" src="http://www.funker530.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/twit.jpg" width="780"/>
             </a>
            </p>
            <h4>
             Mosa hadrecently gained public attention after
             <a href="http://youtu.be/bsCZzpmbEcs" target="_blank">
              <em>
               Vice News
              </em>
             </a>
             released their video series
             <em>
              The Spread Of the Caliphate: The Islamic State
             </em>
             in which Mosa threatened to raise the flag of Allah in the White House.
            </h4>
            <h4>
             In the same video, Mosa voiced his and the rest of the Islamic States fear and futility against drones and airstrikes. It seems that he had a pretty good idea how his fate would be sealed. Good riddance. The Vice video also shows Mosa explaininghow they (al Qaeda) humiliated the American soldiers in Iraq.
            </h4>
            <h4>
             Oddly, I cant remember being humiliated in Iraq, ever. Contrarily, I vividly remember raid after raid, night after night, dragging these soft-bodied scumbags out of bed and grinding their face into the floors of their own homes, while they cried like infants in front of their families. That had to have been humiliating.
            </h4>
            <h4>
             In light of this news, the world is a slightly better place today.
            </h4>
