          <p>
           EPDM rubber, or Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer rubber is a very commonly used form of synthetic rubber. It has a wide range of useful weathering properties and as a result is very well suited to use for outdoor purposes, as it has a strong resistance to UV and ozone, as well as resistance to a vast temperature range of between -40C and 130 C, depending on the grade. These qualities also mean that EPDM rubber is also highly suited for use in the automotive industry.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            So what are the main advantages of EPDM rubber?
           </strong>
          </p>
          <ul>
           <li>
            Very high resistance to UV rays and ozone
           </li>
           <li>
            Excellent resistance to most water-based and vegetable-based hydraulic oils  Qualities which are essential for outdoor and automotive use
           </li>
           <li>
            EPDM solid rubber is available in a range of shore hardnesses  from 40 IRHD to 80 IRHD, depending on the grade of the rubber
           </li>
           <li>
            EPDM rubber can be used in a large range of thicknesses
           </li>
           <li>
            In addition to its weather qualities, EPDM can be easily moulded or extruded, creating complex shapes for a number of uses
           </li>
          </ul>
          <p>
           <strong>
            What do we use EPDM rubber for at CB Frost?
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           At CB Frost, we can supply EPDM in a number of forms in order to best suit our customers requirements, including EPDM solid rubber sheeting, rubber strips, gaskets, washers, mouldings and extrusions.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            EPDM solid rubber sheeting
           </strong>
            We keep EPDM sheeting in stock for quick turnaround, however, we can also convert sheeting into strips, washers or gaskets by cutting it down to size, or produce moulded or extruded parts using our in-house manufacturing facilities.
          </p>
          <p>
           As standard, we supply EPDM rubber sheeting in thicknesses ranging from 1mm to 6mm, however we can supply thicker materials if our customers request this. If you require large amounts of EPDM sheeting and would prefer to cut it down yourself, we can supply in roll or sheet form to suit your needs.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            EPDM rubber strips
           </strong>
            We cut rubber strips from stock EPDM sheeting, to exact customer specifications, though we do cut to a minimum width of 4mm. In terms of length, at CB Frost we can supply EPDM rubber strips in a wide range of lengths.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            EPDM gaskets and washers
           </strong>
            At CB Frost, we can manufacture EPDM gaskets and washers from stock roll material in a range of thicknesses between 1mm and 12mm. For gaskets between 1 and 6mm thick (1 and 8mm for washers), we cut them to size using our in-house overhead, swing arm or kiss cutting presses, depending on the job in question. For thicker specifications, we use a water jet cutting technique. This technique makes sure that all edges of the gasket or washer are flat and accurate.
          </p>
          <p>
           We are equally happy to provide customised one of products or full volume productions; for higher volume production needs, we can supply kiss-cut washers and gaskets on rolls. Of course, this is limited dependent on the size and shape of your gasket and washer needs.
          </p>
          <p>
           For EPDM rubber sheets, strips, gaskets and washers, we can provide them both plain and adhesive back dependent on their intended use.
          </p>
          <p>
           <strong>
            EPDM rubber mouldings and extrusions
           </strong>
          </p>
          <p>
           We are able to manufacture EPDM rubber mouldings and extrusions in a wide range of densities and hardness. We can provide extrusion dies and compression and injection moulding tooling that is built specifically for your requirements.
          </p>
