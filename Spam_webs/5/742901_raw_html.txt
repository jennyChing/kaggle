            <p>
             <a href="http://lh6.ggpht.com/_Dwex9AQ8IuA/S-XXXQITvlI/AAAAAAAAVGg/EGdJRR9DHqU/tempeh-bacon-550.jpg">
              <img border="0" height="444" src="http://lh6.ggpht.com/_Dwex9AQ8IuA/S-XXXQITvlI/AAAAAAAAVGg/EGdJRR9DHqU/tempeh-bacon-550.jpg" title="vegan-bacon-facon" width="550"/>
             </a>
             <br/>
             I'm obsessed with
             <span style="font-weight:bold;">
              vegan tempeh bacon.
             </span>
             I adore the
             <span style="font-style:italic;">
              BLT
             </span>
             sandwich at
             <span style="font-style:italic;">
              Teany
             </span>
             in NYC,  featuring a smoky tempeh bacon.  So, I needed my own recipe. Especially since I wanted to make a vegan club sandwich. You can't have a club without bacon! My recipe is a bit
             <span style="font-weight:bold;">
              spicy
             </span>
             , a tad
             <span style="font-weight:bold;">
              sweet
             </span>
             and a little
             <span style="font-weight:bold;">
              smoky
             </span>
             .
             <span style="font-style:italic;">
              And a lot
              <span style="font-weight:bold;">
               yummy...
              </span>
             </span>
             <span class="fullpost">
              <br/>
              <span style="font-weight:bold;">
               <br/>
               Tempeh Bacon Fills the Void.
              </span>
              From sandwiches to tofu scrambles. Life calls for a little 'facon'
              <span style="font-style:italic;">
               (fake-in)
              </span>
              once in a while.  Thus vegan tempeh bacon fills the void. My recipe isn't as smoky as most I've seen. I'm not big on really smoky flavors.  I wanted a more barbecue-ish flavor. So here's the recipe...
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <span style="font-weight:bold;">
               Tempeh Bacon,
               <span style="font-style:italic;">
                Vegan Facon
               </span>
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="font-style:italic;">
               makes about 1/2 cup
              </span>
              <br/>
              <span style="font-style:italic;">
               *a nice stack for 1-2 sandwiches
              </span>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              5 ounces tempeh (2/3 a standard 8 ounce pack)
              <br/>
              1-2 Tbsp maple or agave syrup
              <br/>
              1 Tbsp olive oil
              <br/>
              1/2 tsp cumin
              <br/>
              a dash of cayenne
              <br/>
              1 tsp liquid smoke (opt'l)
              <br/>
              1 tsp soy sauce
              <br/>
              1/2 tsp thick black pepper
              <br/>
              for pan: 2 tsp olive oil
              <br/>
              <span style="font-style:italic;">
               *if you don't have liquid smoke use these BBQ spice combos:
              </span>
              <br/>
              (garlic, pepper, paprika and onion)
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <span style="font-weight:bold;">
               To make:
              </span>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              1. Very thinly slice the tempeh.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              2. As thin as you can slice it without it falling apart.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              3. Combine all ingredients in a shallow dish.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              4. Soak tempeh in marinade for 1-2 minutes.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              5. Turn saute pan on high,  add 1-2 tsp olive oil. Lay the tempeh flat on skillet. One layer only.  Drizzle a bit of excess marinade onto skillet-it should be sizzling a lot now.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              6. Allow to cook for 1 minute,  then flip. Allow to cook for another minute on other side-or until both sides are crisp and browned.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              7. Lay cooked tempeh on parchment paper to cool. Sprinkle with black pepper and thick sea salt to taste.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              8. For a crisper tempeh bacon,  do not pour excess marinade in skillet, and allow all liquid to steam off of pan while cooking.
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <span style="font-weight:bold;">
               Serve.
              </span>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              ...Marvel in your vegan culinary mastery.
              <span style="font-style:italic;">
               Move onto vegan bacon cupcakes perhaps...
              </span>
              <br/>
              <br/>
              <span style="font-weight:bold;">
               Tempeh Vegan Bacon Uses:
              </span>
              sandwiches, sides, sliced into salads, soups and heck even desserts - maybe.
