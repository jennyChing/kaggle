        <a href="https://www.change.org/p/the-walking-dead-bring-beth-back" target="_blank" title="petition">
         petition
        </a>
        to deliver to TWD Producers, which has already received over 50,000 signatures, demanding they resurrect the teenage girl. It appears the production team is listening, and has agreed to bring Beth Greene back to life.
       </p>
       <p>
        Viewers have cried in outrage about Beths death, saying that
        <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daryl_Dixon" target="_blank" title="Daryl Dixon">
         Daryl Dixon

        (Norman Reedus) could have, and should have, savedher. Yes, he shot Dawn in retaliation for killing Beth, but the fans say that is not enough. Not only have the petitionersgained impressive steam on the petition, but theyhave also been sending plastic spoons with their favorite Beth Quotes written on them, to the shows production offices, to drive the point.
