        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        Here are some things I felt you should consider more closely about this product:
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         I have to be honest  even though its very helpful, there could be more to The Study Method. Its a very light 90 or so pages. I know I likely wouldnt have picked it up if it were longer, but now that I know how good it is, I guess I wish there was a second book or more supplementary information.
        </li>
        <li>
         This is not a miracle product. You have to think about the book and put it to practice when you study and when you are in class to get the benefits out of it. I dont want you to think that you read the book and suddenly you are someone different. Its more like you read the book and suddenly you have a plan that
         <em>
          if you follow
         </em>
         will transform your grades like it did mine.
        </li>
        <li>
         This product is only available online. I guess Im a bit nostalgic, but I like my study guides where I can highlight and mark them up. That would have been a great feature to see here. Youll need to get used to using a PDF instead of a bound book. The information is great, but its hard to dog ear an iPad.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        <strong>
         In Conclusion
        </strong>
       </p>
       <p>
        If you are still reading this then you want to improve your grades. I know that not every product is for everyone, but if you are someone who needs to know how to study better, then I cant think of reasons to not tell you to give it a go. Itll only take a few hours to read, its very reasonably priced, it comes with a variety of techniques, and is completely risk free because of the money back guarantee.
       </p>
       <p>
        The only real question is whether or not youre ready to enjoy all the benefits that come with getting good grades and studying effectively. If the answer is yes, then its time to give yourself a leg up and order The Study Method. I wouldnt have written this review if I didnt think it could make a difference for other people like me. Theres no risk, so why not try?
