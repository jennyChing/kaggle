       <span class="st_facebook_hcount" displaytext="Facebook">
       </span>
       <span class="st_twitter_hcount" displaytext="Tweet">

       <p>
       </p>
       <h2>
        Content marketing is one of the best forms of marketing right now, but consumers often want live-person interaction before committing to a purchase. This article will explain how you can support your content marketing with a call center.
       </h2>
       <p>
        <a href="http://www.sound-tele.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DeathtoStock_Wired2.jpg">
         <img alt="This picture is of a hand holding a computer mouse to demonstrate how you can support your content marketing with a call center" class="wp-image-18121 alignleft" height="226" src="http://www.sound-tele.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/DeathtoStock_Wired2-1024x682.jpg" width="339"/>
        </a>
       </p>
       <p>
        As a telecommunication company, Sound Telecom does offer
        <a href="http://www.sound-tele.com/call-center-services/outbound-telemarketing/" title="Sound Telecom Outbound Telemarketing">
         outbound telemarketing services
        </a>
        . However, with the popularity of inbound and content marketing in todays business world, it would seem very astute for a company to prepare to support the interest garnered from these marketing tactics. We have an article about 
        <a href="http://www.sound-tele.com/blog/how-to-develop-inbound-customer-support" target="_blank" title="How to Develop a Customer Support Campaign">
         How to Develop an Inbound Customer Support Campaign
        </a>
        , which is geared towards people who are already your clients. However, this post will be geared towards supporting possible leads drummed up by content marketing with a call center.
       </p>
       <p>
        First of all, you may be wondering what constitutes good content marketing. Good content marketing creates something that is valuable and shareable on social media to a specific audience to encourage consumer behavior. While the good ol phone call may be useful in reaching out to older generations, people today (such as millennials) are far more reachable through online contact and social media. They are often interested by things they find of their own accord online and prefer to be in control of further contact.
       </p>
       <p>
        So lets say youve written some good blog posts with keywords, built out your Facebook and Twitter pages, and have created interest in your product or service through these channels. These interested people will want more information and assurance that there are real faces behind your companys web page. When these people reach out, you want to respond as quickly as possible in todays society of instant gratification.
       </p>
       <ul>
        <li>
         According to an
         <a href="http://blogs.salesforce.com/company/2013/08/customer-service-stats.html" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview', '/out/blogs.salesforce.com/company/2013/08/customer-service-stats.html']);" target="_blank" title="18 Interesting Stats to Get You Rethinking Your Customer Service Process">
          article by SalesForce
         </a>
         , 45% of US consumers will abandon an online transaction if their questions or concerns are not addressed quickly. Thats almost half your online leads!
        </li>
        <li>
         According to an
         <a href="https://econsultancy.com/blog/61991-83-of-online-shoppers-need-support-to-complete-a-purchase-stats#i.71n6zasuwcoj11" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackPageview', '/out/econsultancy.com/blog/61991-83-of-online-shoppers-need-support-to-complete-a-purchase-stats#i.71n6zasuwcoj11']);" target="_blank" title="83% of online shoppers need support to complete a purchase: stats">
          econsultancy article
         </a>
         , 83% of shoppers surveyed said they needed some form of support during their online journey. These were the most popular choices for customers who stated in what ways they preferred to be helped: 61% telephone number to call customer services, 60% email to customer services, and 57% Live Chat.
        </li>
       </ul>
       <p>
        There will be times when you cannot promptly respond to a customer, such as during downtime for your business when it closes at the end of the day and when your employees are busy handling other duties. You dont want to miss the opportunity to turn a lead into a customer, just because that person tried to contact your company once and then gave up. Having inbound call center representatives who are prepared to handle questions and provide further information in a timely manner is a great asset for a busy company. And to support those other clients who dont prefer to speak on the phone, a good contact center outsourcing partner like Sound Telecom can provide
        <a href="http://www.sound-tele.com/call-center-services/live-chat/" title="Sound Telecom Live Chat">
         Live Chat
        </a>
        and
        <a href="http://www.sound-tele.com/call-center-services/email-management-services/" title="Sound Telecom Email Management Services">
         email services
        </a>
        to reduce response times, as well!
       </p>
