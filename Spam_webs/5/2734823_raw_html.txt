        <h1 class="entry-title">
         Halloween Drink Recipes
        </h1>
        <div class="entry-meta">
         <span class="sep">
          Posted on
         </span>
         <a href="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/recipes/halloween-drink-recipes.html" rel="bookmark" title="2:53 pm">
          <time class="entry-date" datetime="2010-09-21T14:53:48+00:00" pubdate="">
           September 21, 2010
          </time>
         </a>
         <span class="by-author">
          <span class="sep">
           by
          </span>
          <span class="author vcard">
           <a class="url fn n" href="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/author/admin" rel="author" title="View all posts by Bed and Breakfast Network (www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com)">
            Bed and Breakfast Network (www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com)
           </a>
          </span>
         </span>
        </div>
        <!-- .entry-meta -->
       </header>
       <!-- .entry-header -->
       <div class="entry-content">
        <p style="text-align: justify;">
         <a href="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_01.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-854" height="90" src="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_01-150x150.jpg" title="halloween-drink-recipes_01" width="90"/>
         </a>
         Looking to spice up your next Halloween party with some tasty and delicious drinks? Tired of the same old beverages with no thrill or appeal? How about a Halloween themed Martini or a super scary punch of Sangria? We have chosen 5 spooky drink recipes that are guarantee to be a huge hit at your next party.
        </p>
        <p>
         <span id="more-845">
         </span>
        </p>
        <h5>
         Nightmare-Tini
        </h5>
        <ul>
         <li>
          1 1/2 oz. Gin
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 oz. Dry Vermouth
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 oz. Cheery Brandy
         </li>
         <li>
          1 oz. Orange Juice
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Fill a shaker with a cup of ice. Add the Gin, Brandy, OJ and Vermouth. Shake and pour in cocktail glass (chilled)
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_02.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-846" height="300" src="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_02.jpg" title="halloween-drink-recipes_02" width="270"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h5>
         Zombie recipe
        </h5>
        <ul>
         <li>
          1/2 oz Bacardi 151 rum
         </li>
         <li>
          1 oz pineapple juice
         </li>
         <li>
          1 oz orange juice
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 oz apricot brandy
         </li>
         <li>
          1 tsp sugar
         </li>
         <li>
          2 oz light rum
         </li>
         <li>
          1 oz dark rum
         </li>
         <li>
          1 oz lime juice
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Blend or pore all the ingredients minus the 151 rum(will add at the end) into a blender or shaker, mix and pore into Glass. *Optional Add Pineapple and cherries slices for extra effect.
        </p>
        <p>
         <em>
          <a href="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_03.jpg">
           <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-847" height="300" src="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_03-187x300.jpg" title="halloween-drink-recipes_03" width="187"/>
          </a>
          <br/>
         </em>
        </p>
        <h5>
         Blood Red Sangria
        </h5>
        <ul>
         <li>
          1 bottle (inexpensive) red wine
         </li>
         <li>
          12 oz. club soda
         </li>
         <li>
          2 oz. cognac
         </li>
         <li>
          3 tbs. warmed honey
         </li>
         <li>
          4 sliced figs
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 cup red or black grapes, halved
         </li>
         <li>
          1/2 cup pitted cherries (can use frozen if necessary)
         </li>
         <li>
          ice
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         In a large glass bowl add all the fruit, then add the cognac and honey the honey has been dissolved. Add red wine and stir. Put in refrigerator for at least two hours. When the party is ready add club soda to bowl and serve.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_04.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-848" height="300" src="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_04.jpg" title="halloween-drink-recipes_04" width="200"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h5>
         Halloween Pumpkin Margarita
        </h5>
        <ul>
         <li>
          2 oz. Tequila
         </li>
         <li>
          1  oz. pumpkin liqueur
         </li>
         <li>
           oz. Triple Sec
         </li>
         <li>
          2 cups ice
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Blend all together until smooth
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_05.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-849" height="250" src="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_05.jpg" title="halloween-drink-recipes_05" width="250"/>
         </a>
        </p>
        <h5>
         The Headless Horseman
        </h5>
        <ul>
         <li>
          2 oz. black vodka
         </li>
         <li>
          3 dashes bitters
         </li>
         <li>
          Ginger ale
         </li>
        </ul>
        <p>
         Pour the vodka and bitters into a Collins glass. Fill the glass with ice, then pour in ginger ale. Garnish with a slice of blood orange.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a href="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_06.jpg">
          <img alt="" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-850" height="240" src="http://www.bedandbreakfastnetwork.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/halloween-drink-recipes_06.jpg" title="halloween-drink-recipes_06" width="158"/>
         </a>
        </p>
