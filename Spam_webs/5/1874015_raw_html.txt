                 <br/>
                 Have you ever seen this? Nope we dont think so.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 Playbox is an 22 laptop that combines both Xbox and PlayStation 4 into one single machine.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 The builder/engineer of this mega machine is Ed Zarick,
                 <br/>
                 <a name="more">
                 </a>
                 who is a self-taught engineer who previously produced separate laptop editions of both console, has now came up with with this.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
                 "The PS4 sits on the left hand side of the unit, and the Xbox One is on the right side," Zarick said in his new
                 <a href="http://www.edsjunk.net/wordpress/?p=347" target="_blank">
                  blog post
                 </a>
                 . The whole laptop only uses one power cord, but only one console can be powered
                 <br/>
                 on at the same time.
                 <br/>
                 <br/>
