         <b>
          Vegetarian foods and verity with spices IT
         </b>
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         It is really to feel proud that in India there are a good no of availability of vegetarian recipes for juniors as well as for adults too. There we can prepare lots of vegetarian foods for Kids and vegetables are like cats and water. Like cats always avoid water, children run as far as they can from vegetables. If you are a parent or have a little one in your home, you are well aware of what I am saying. And we also know that nothing can replace or supplement the actual nutrients present in the vegetables  not even the health drinks. Vegetables are indispensible part of our diet. They are essential to growth, immunity and overall health of an individual. However this logic wont work on your children, so you need to be tactful and cook them some delicious vegetarian dishes that they cant say no to. It is not difficult or time consuming all you need is a little creativity.
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         <b>
          Some recipes of vegetarian dishes
         </b>
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         People of India are fond of a having some delicious vegetarian dishes. Here we can get some ideas:-
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         1. Instead of simply giving them chappatis and sabji add some spices to the sabji and cook them and put them in the chappati and roll them. Now it is a new item.
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         2. Decorate the rice and create funny faces with different vegetables. Remember decoration counts.
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         3. Cut the sandwiches with cookie cutter and give them different shapes.
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         4. Add some spices. Just because they are children, do not give them plain flavourless food. A little bit of spice wont harm them.
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         A little creative decoration and presentation is what you primarily need. And on a special day give them a bonus  maybe a vegetable cake. They cannot say no to Cakes either, so try putting all healthy ingredients in it while you can. Make them a fruit cake or a vegetable cake.
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         <b>
          Here is a recipe for all of you.
         </b>
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
        <div>
         CHOCOLATE BEETROOT CAKE - Disguise the nutrient rich beetroot in the irresistible flavour of chocolate.
        </div>
        <div>
         1. Prepare the oven to 180 degree Celsius.
        </div>
        <div>
         2. Grease 22cm round cake tin with oil/butter and put butter paper.
        </div>
        <div>
         3. Put the chocolate (250 gm) in a bowl and let it melt in simmering water.
        </div>
        <div>
         4. Place 3 eggs, 200 gm sugar, and 100ml sunflower oil in a bowl to whisk together.
        </div>
        <div>
         5. Add 1tsp of Vanilla extract, 100 gm flour,  tsp bicarbonate of soda,  tsp baking powder and 50gm ground almonds and stir together.
        </div>
        <div>
         6. Add 250 gm of peeled, grated and squeezed out beetroot in the chocolate mixture and mix them thoroughly.
        </div>
        <div>
         7. Pour this mixture in the tin and bake for one hour.
        </div>
        <div>
         8. Cool a little while in the tin then take it out and let it cool in the wire rack.
        </div>
        <div>
         9. Melt 150gm of plain chocolate. Let it cool and then add 100 gm of icing sugar and soured cream until it turns thick. Then spread this mixture on top and side of the cake as an icing.
        </div>
        <div>
         10. ENJOY!
        </div>
        <div>
         <br/>
        </div>
