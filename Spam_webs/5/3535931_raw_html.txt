        <section class="entry-content cf" itemprop="articleBody">
         <p>
          <img alt="Screen Shot 2014-05-06 at 11.00.41" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-341290" height="232" src="http://cdn.bleedingcool.net/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/Screen-Shot-2014-05-06-at-11.00.41-350x232.png" width="350"/>
          According to
          <a href="http://www.aintitcool.com/node/67205" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.aintitcool.com/node/67205', 'Harry Knowles');">
           Harry Knowles
          </a>
          , citing what he believes to be separate sources but, for all we know, could be the same prankster coming at him from all angles, the working title for
          <em>
           Star Wars Episode VII
          </em>
          is
          <em>
           The Ancient Fear
          </em>
          .
         </p>
         <p>
          This refers to
          <strong>
           Max von Sydow
          </strong>
          s character, he says.
         </p>
         <p>
          I get it.
          <em>
           Ancient
          </em>
          vs.
          <em>
           New
          </em>
          ,
          <em>
           Fear
          </em>
          vs.
          <em>
           Hope
          </em>
          . Even
          <em>
           Phantom Menace
          </em>
          chimes with
          <em>
           Ancient Fear
          </em>
          in some way. It ties all of the titles up in a well, maybe a bow, maybe a knot. I cant quite make my mind up right now. Maybe Im feeling unjustly grumpy because and just need a while to let it bed in.
         </p>
         <p>
          Just a rumour, but maybe one worth discussing.
         </p>
